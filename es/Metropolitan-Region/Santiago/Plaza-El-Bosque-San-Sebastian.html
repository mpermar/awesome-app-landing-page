Descripción

Plaza El Bosque San Sebastian Suites features elegant rugs and wall moldings in full 4-star style. Upscale Providencia neighborhood is 16 blocks away.

Air-conditioned rooms at Plaza El Bosque feature carpeted floors, full length curtains and a palette of warm shades. There are seating areas, cable TV and bathrooms with baths. Some rooms have 2 bathrooms and flat-screen TVs.

A full buffet breakfast with pastries and regional jams can be enjoyed daily. The restaurant features a charming courtyard area. Room service can be ordered.

Santa Lucia Hill is 3 miles away and Arturo Merino Benitez Airport is 17.2 miles away. Private parking is free.

Las Condes is a great choice for travelers interested in seafood, restaurants and food.
