Descripción

Las habitaciones del Blue Iguana Inn tienen alfombras tejidas, obras de arte enmarcadas y una decoración rústica. Están equipadas con una pequeña nevera y un baño privado. Algunas habitaciones incluyen suelos de madera y una chimenea antigua.

Por la mañana, los huéspedes pueden disfrutar de un desayuno continental con pasteles y zumo de fruta fresca. Hay una cafetería y varios restaurantes a menos de 5 minutos en coche.

El Blue Iguana Inn se encuentra a 40 minutos en coche de Santa Bárbara. Se ofrece aparcamiento público gratuito.

Experiencias

22/06/2017

Great place near Ojai

"This inn is a couple of miles outside of Ojai where most restaurants are. The inn is very interesting with a number of smaller separate buildings with lots of trees, bushes and flowers in between. You can't see much from the road, but inside the wall it is very nice.Our room had two queen beds that made it feel a bit cramped. One king would have been better. There was a desk for my laptop but no room at the desk for a chair. The bath was small but laid out very well with ample space for our stuff. The room had a fridge, coffee maker, excellent A/CBreakfast was very basic but OK. We did not use the pool but other families did and seemed to have a great time."
