Descripción

Todas las suites del Carmel Valley Ranch incluyen una chimenea y conexión inalámbrica a internet gratuita. Todas las suites cuentan con soporte para iPod, TV de pantalla plana de 42 pulgadas y refrigerador. Las suites también ofrecen una terraza privada y baños con duchas separadas y bañeras.

El Carmel Valley Resort ofrece un spa de servicio completo, así como 9 canchas de tenis, clases de golf y 2 piscinas al aire libre. También hay un gimnasio, bañeras de hidromasaje y rutas de senderismo panorámicas.

The good and the bad

"The location in the foothills was lovely and every day we drove up the winding road to our mountain top suite, we saw a family of dear on the hillside. The staff working at Sunnydale Ranch (the part of the ranch dedicated to families) were absolutely wonderful, especially Kayla who manages it and her helpful staff members who helped us to plan day-hikes and gave us interesting ideas for excursions of the beaten path. My kids took a swimming lesson at sunnydale and the teacher was great at getting my nervous son to loosen up and have fun. During the 5 days that we were at the ranch , the forest fire near Big Sir was raging and sunnydale offered free lodging (in the yoga studio) and dinner each night to firefighters that came from places as far away as San Francisco and beyond to help out. That made for a wonderful atmosphere at this family-designated area of the ranch. At Sunnydale ranch, there was a small cafe that offered sophisticated food and was a good alternative to eating lunch at the Lodge main area of the ranch) up the hill. The cafe at Sunnydale was open until 9pm along with the pool that was huge and deliciously warm. It was nice to sit outside by the fire pit in the early evening. There was also an Olympic size pool behind the yoga area for teenagers 16 yrs and up, as well as tennis courts. The amenities were outstanding. We didn't get to hike on the property nor visit the ranch's stable, but are sure both are great."
