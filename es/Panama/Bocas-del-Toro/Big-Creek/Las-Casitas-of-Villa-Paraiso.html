Descripción

The accommodations come with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with shower and free toiletries, while the kitchen features a microwave. Some units feature a seating area and/or a terrace.

Breakfast is available each morning, and includes à la carte and American options.

The resort village has an outdoor pool.

A grill is available on site and both cycling and canoeing can be enjoyed within close proximity of the property.

Bocas Town is 1.5 miles from Las Casitas of Villa Paraiso.
