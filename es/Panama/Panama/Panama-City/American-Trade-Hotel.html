Descripción

Each stylish air-conditioned room has a flat-screen satellite TV, an iPod dock and a minibar. Some rooms have a balcony overlooking the street. The bathrooms include a rain shower, hairdryer, bathrobe and slippers.

At the American Trade Hotel, there is indoor and outdoor banquet facilities, luggage storage and a dry cleaning service. The property provides free parking.

The hotel is in the Casco Viejo district, within a 5-minute walk of Plaza Herrera Square, San Francisco Church and the Presidential Palace. Albrook Airport is 2.5 miles away, and Tocumen International Airport is 16 miles away.
