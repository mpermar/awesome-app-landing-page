Descripción

All rooms at the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV and a kitchenette. Each room comes with air conditioning, and selected rooms also feature a balcony. The rooms will provide guests with a microwave.

A buffet breakfast is available each morning at The Suite Playa Blanca.

You can play tennis at this 4-star resort.

Speaking English and Spanish, staff are willing to help at any time of the day at the reception.

Penonomé is 18 miles from the accommodation.
