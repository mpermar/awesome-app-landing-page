Descripción

This hotel is located within the Multiplaza Shopping Center, which has entertainment venues, boutiques, restaurants, shops and bars.

All rooms at the Courtyard Panama Real Hotel have soundproof windows with a view of the city. Each room includes a large work desk, a refrigerator and a coffee maker.

On-site dining for all meals is available at the Pacific Grill, serving international cuisine. Guests can also enjoy a sandwich and a drink and watching the flat-screen TVs in the hotel’s Corner Bar. There is also a sushi bar and coffee shop.

Free access is granted to the Panama Courtyard Hotel’s fitness room, and the on-site business center is open 24/7. The outdoor pool is surrounded by a deck for relaxation. Guests will find Costa del Este Business Park within a 15-minute drive.

Torre Las Americas is located right next to the hotel, and the Panama Canal and the Tocumen International Airport are both a 15-minute drive away.
