Descripción

Radisson Decapolis Hotel Panama City has a chic modern design, including original Panamanian art. Each stylish, air-conditioned room comes with flat-screen cable TV, a coffee maker and minibar. Free breakfast is included with the rate.

The hotel’s main restaurant serves modern fusion cuisine and has views of the glass-bottom pool. The Martini Bar and Sushi Bar offer fresh sushi and cocktails. Room service is available 24 hours a day.

The Radisson Decapolis is next to Multicentro Shopping Center and the Majestic Casino, just a quarter mile from Panama Bay. Free private parking is available on site, and airport transportation can be arranged.
