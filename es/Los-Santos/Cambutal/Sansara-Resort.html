Descripción

At the hotel, every room is equipped with a desk. Featuring a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries, certain units at Sansara Resort also provide guests with a terrace. Guest rooms feature a seating area.

A continental or à la carte breakfast is available every morning at the property.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool. Activities such as snorkeling, cycling, canoeing can be enjoyed in the surrounding areas, and guests can relax along the beachfront.

Pedregal is 6 miles from Sansara Resort.
