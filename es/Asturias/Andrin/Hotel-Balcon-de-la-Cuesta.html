Descripción

All rooms feature views to Andrín Valley, a flat-screen TV, and heating. The bathroom comes with free Hermès toiletries, bath, and hairdryer.

Among other services and facilities you can find an excellent à la carte restaurant with several dining rooms and terraces, cafe and wine bar.

Guests have access to the spa for a surcharge. It includes facial and body massages with Natura Bisé products and spa treatments. Access to the spa is exclusive for the room that makes the reservation.

Cuesta’s golf course is just 500 metres away from the hotel and so is Ballota Beach, with its stunning viewing points from which you can admire miles and miles of our extraordinary coast.
