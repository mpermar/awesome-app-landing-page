Descripción

All rooms at Hotel Huayacán are equipped with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with a hair dryer and a bath. Guest rooms feature a closet.

Cuernavaca is 4.1 miles from Hotel Huayacán, while Tepoztlán is 9 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Benito Juarez International Airport, 39 miles from Hotel Huayacán.
