Experiencias

19/06/2018

A great place to stay!! Highly Recommend!!

"From the moment we checked in to the time we checked out, everything about this hotel was excellent! The staff was friendly and helpful. When we checked in, it was about 7:30 pm and we were hungry. We asked the front desk staff for a recommendation on where to eat. She said there was a good place across the street from the hotel called Landmark Inn 1850 that served a variety of food and drinks. It was a great recommendation. The room is a great size, with a nice sized "living room" and kitchen. The kitchen has a microwave, nearly full sized refrigerator, sink and a dishwasher. There's no plasticware or paper...real plates, bowls, glasses and silverware. The bed was so comfortable as well as the pillows! It was a great night sleep every single night. We were a little worried that we would hear the planes taking off and landing because it's so close to the airport, be we didn't! There was a pretty good thunderstorm during our stay and we did hear the rumbles, but it wasn't bad at all. We didn't use the pool, but in talking with a couple families who did, they said it was really nice. The pool is indoors and heated. The jacuzzi, according to one dad, was awesome! In the lobby, they have coffee and fruit (apples, bananas etc) for the taking. There is also a refrigerated area that is available 27/7 to purchase various frozen foods, water, soda, beer and wine. Breakfast is included from 6-10 am and offers a variety of breakfast foods...oatmeal, muffins, toast, bagels, mixed fruit, cereal and make your own waffles. There's also microwavable breakfast sandwiches and scrambles. Other than the oatmeal and waffles, nothing else is hot unless it's microwaved or toasted. The hotel is approximately 8 miles from downtown Milwaukee. At first, we were going to cancel and find a hotel closer to downtown. But we're so glad we didn't!! First of all, there is plenty of parking and it's free."
