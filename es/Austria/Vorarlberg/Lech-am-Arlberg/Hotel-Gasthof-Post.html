Descripción

Una gran e impresionante granja con paredes gruesas se ha convertido en un pequeño hotel de lujo, mientras que su encanto original se ha mantenido.

Las habitaciones en el Post son todas individuales y cada una tiene su propia historia.

El Post-Café sirve almuerzos, café de la tarde y los famosos strudels caseros. En el salón Zirbel puedes disfrutar de una sabrosa cena. El bar es un lugar de encuentro popular desde después del esquí hasta la medianoche.

Políticas de Alojamiento

Children and Extra Beds
All children are welcome.
One child from 0 to 2 years is charged EUR 40 per person per night in an extra bed.
One child from 3 to 5 years is charged EUR 80 per person per night in an extra bed.
One child from 6 to 10 years is charged EUR 120 per person per night in an extra bed.
One child from 11 to 14 years is charged EUR 180 per person per night in an extra bed.
One older child or adult is charged EUR 240 per person per night in an extra bed.
The maximum number of extra beds/cribs in a room is 1.
Any type of extra bed is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
