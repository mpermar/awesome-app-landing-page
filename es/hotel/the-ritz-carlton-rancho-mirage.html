Descripción

Las habitaciones del The Ritz-Carlton, Rancho Mirage cuentan con aire acondicionado, TV de pantalla plana vía satélite y reproductor de DVD. La zona de comedor incluye nevera y cafetera. El baño privado incluye bañera o ducha, secador de pelo y albornoz.

El restaurante State Fare se encuentra en The Rancho Mirage Ritz-Carlton y sirve desayunos, almuerzos y cenas.

Los huéspedes pueden disfrutar de una sesión de ejercicio en el gimnasio del The Ritz-Carlton. Otras instalaciones del hotel incluyen instalaciones para reuniones. Se pueden practicar diversas actividades en el establecimiento o en la zona, como senderismo.

El parque Century está a 10 km del hotel. El aeropuerto internacional de Palm Springs está a 8 km.

A Wonderful Way to Visit a Desert

"This place was a treat to myself and it was so worth it. I had never been to a desert and this was such a special way to see one. The staff was among the best I have ever dealt with. The place comes up with all sorts of little 'treats' during the day. They work hard to cater to both family, and adults. Everyday from 12-5 there is a 'candy bar' set out in the lobby with all sorts of goodies available- a lot kid oriented. At 5ish there are complimentary Manhattans in the lobby in honor of Old Blue Eyes himself. There is an adults only pool with a very active wait staff and bar. There is also a family pool, which is bigger, and holds all the children and families. The pools have a few floating things in them, so people could just float. The fitness area and spa are fabulous. I had my hair done at the Salon and he did an amazing job- just what I wanted. The room had an amazing view and I sat on the patio a lot. Room service was wonderful, and the food is delicious. There is staff all over asking what they can do for you, and if you have any questions they fall over backwards to help you. I had a wonderful nature walk of the property, and there is an early morning hike if one is so inclined. Every night they set up a telescope with an astronomer and I saw Jupiter and Saturn. There are lawn games set up so that people can take a break from swimming and play lawn games. The beauty of the place, the staff were all the highlights of the stay and it is hard to have a bad time with these so completely covered."
