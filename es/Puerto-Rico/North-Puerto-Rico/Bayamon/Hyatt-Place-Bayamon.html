Descripción

The rooms at the Hyatt Place Bayamon feature elegant contemporary décor, with functional wood furniture and a sofa. All rooms have ironing facilities and a modern bathroom with a hairdryer.

The Hyatt Place Bayamon has meeting facilities and a lounge area. On-site parking is available for an extra fee.

Fast food outlets, bars and restaurants can be found within a 5-minute drive of the Hyatt Place. Downtown San Juan is 12 miles away, and Cerro Gordo Beach and Luis Munoz Marin Airport are both within 19 miles.
