Descripción

The rooms at this property are air-conditioned and feature cable TV and a fridge. The bathrooms are private.

The on-site restaurant is specialized in seafood, steak-style dishes and Puerto Rican cuisine. Villa Cofresi also has a snack bar and extensive events facilities, suitable for meetings, conventions and weddings.

Popular activities in the surrounding area including scuba diving, parasailing and horseback riding. You can also go whale watching in season. Borinquen Airport in Aguadilla is a 30-minute drive away.
