Descripción

The bedrooms at Estancia La Jacama are air-conditioned, and feature a sitting area and flat-screen satellite TV. The bathrooms are private.

The snack bar at Estancia La Jacama serves light meals, local and international beers and rums and homemade sangria. There is also a free coffee service.

Estancia La Jacama has limited free private parking available on site, along with a tour desk.

Las Lajas Airpark is reachable in a 10-minute drive, and Guanicas Dry Forest is a 20-minute drive away. Cabo Rojo and Playa Sucia Beach are both a 25-minute drive from the property and to get to San Juan, guests will have to drive through highway 52 Luis A. Farré for 2 hours. The Mercedita Ponce National Airport is 40 miles away.
