Descripción

The hotel will provide guests with an oven, a coffee machine, a shower, free toiletries, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and DVD-Player. Villa El Oasis Guatape Lake has some accommodations with mountain views, and all rooms include a balcony. All rooms will provide guests with a microwave.

A continental breakfast is available every morning at the accommodation.

The area is popular for skiing, and ski equipment rental and bike rental are available at Villa El Oasis Guatape Lake.

Medellín is 27 miles from the hotel. The nearest airport is José María Córdova International Airport, 17 miles from the property.
