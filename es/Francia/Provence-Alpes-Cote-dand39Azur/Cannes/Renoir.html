Descripción

Las habitaciones y suites del Renoir están insonorizadas y tienen aire acondicionado. Todas las habitaciones están equipadas con minibar, TV de pantalla plana con canales vía satélite y baño privado completo. Algunas también tienen vistas al mar Mediterráneo.

Los huéspedes están invitados a relajarse con una copa en el bar de Renoir y pedir una comida a través del servicio de habitaciones 24 horas del hotel.

Hotel Renoir cuenta con servicios de secretaría y alquiler de autos. Hay conexión inalámbrica a internet y aparcamiento privado disponibles por un suplemento. Renoir está convenientemente ubicado a 150 metros de la estación de tren de Cannes y a 26 km del aeropuerto de Niza.

Experiencias

07/05/2018

Great location, very helpful and friendly staff. Very clean and spatious room.

"The staff were very helpful and friendly. We stayed in one of the suites on the top floor which was lovely, spacious and clean and had a view of the sea from the balcony. The hotel is a short walk (5mins) from the beach and the hotel even provided us with beach towels. There is no parking at the hotel, but their is a multi-storey which is only a 2 min walk and seemed very safe (albeit it is 16 euros per day). We have a disabled child, and despite the hotel being on a hill, the hotel entrance and access to rooms is fully disabled friendly as there is a wheelchair lift from the street to the lobby and then an internal lift serving all floor, although the lift is quite small, but was large enough for my son's wheelchair plus one adult with some space left over, so an adult wheelchair should also fit."

Políticas de Alojamiento

Children and Extra Beds
All children are welcome.
One child under 12 years is charged EUR 25 per night when using existing beds.
All children under 12 years are charged EUR 20 per night for extra beds.
Any additional older children or adults are charged EUR 45 per night for extra beds.
The maximum number of extra beds in a room is 1.
Any type of extra bed is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
