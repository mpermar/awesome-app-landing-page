Descripción

This stylish and elegant hotel offers a variety of room types, including ground floor suites with direct pool access, private gardens and other exclusive benefits. Some suites have wonderful views of the sea. A free bottle of water is provided in all rooms.

The Rooftop offers dazzling views, an infinity-style pool and Balinese beds, available only for adults. The ground floor presents an upbeat environment with daily DJs, Balinese beds and a poolside menu, also available only for adults. Younger guests can enjoy the children’s pool.

RADIO ROOFTOP BAR & ÁNGEL LEÓNNew concept with the 4 stars Michelin Angel León, the Chef of the Sea, National Award of Gastronomy, for his innovative culinary techniques and creations. highlight the use of Marine Plankton among its ingredients.-Salt & Sea -> now: "Bianco Mare"New concept with the Mediterranean essence, authentic Italian cuisine,

The property offers a fitness area and free private parking. Complimentary yoga sessions are offered daily, on request and subject to availability. There is a Kids' Club. Nikki Beach Ibiza is located beside the hotel.

Es Cana tourist resort is just 1.5 km away, and Ibiza town is 12 km from the property. Ibiza Airport is a 35-minute drive from the hotel. You can also hire a private boat to the beautiful neighbouring island of Formentera, on request and subject to availability.

The hotel offers free valet parking.
