Descripción

Las habitaciones del Hampton Inn & Suites Vineland están equipadas con microondas y nevera. Hay conexión Wi-Fi gratuita en todas las habitaciones y set de café.

Una tienda de conveniencia abierta las 24 horas está disponible en el Vineland Hampton Inn & Suites. Un centro de negocios y lavandería también están en el sitio para mayor comodidad.

El Hampton Inn & Suites Vineland está a 30 km de la Universidad de Rowan. El centro médico regional de South Jersey Healthcare se encuentra a 6,2 km.

Warm, comforting, enjoyable, and rememberable. 10/10 would stay again.

"There are multiple things that my family & I enjoyed during our stay, so I'll detail our visit in order of how the events occurred. Since we've never been to this area of New Jersey before we were delighted to see the wide variety of stores within a short driving distance of the hotel. We arrived in the evening (Which is typically when the parking lot is filled) and immediately found parking right outside the front door. I had an issue with my booking (Had absolutely nothing to do with the hotel, rather Booking.com not sending over my information) and the front desk receptionist immediately had the manager step out and resolve my problem.The main lobby is much nicer than I had anticipated, and there was someone using an available computer which is a great idea! There is an open seating area with a family-style table in the center...you don't see that too often in a hotel.Unfortunately we didn't have enough time to use the pool (These things take extra time when traveling with children) but we did look around and it seemed very clean and well taken care of. I was not sure what the golf-area outside was for, but since we were tight on time we wouldn't have been able to use it anyway.The rooms were absolutely spotless, there may have been a bulb out (Two switches at the door, but only 1 seemed to turn a light on) but there were 4 or 5 lamps in the room which gave off plenty of light. I struggled to close the curtains early in the morning...which I found out later that day that the curtains were actually behind another set of curtains.Overall we had an excellent time, and the breakfast was packed...they may need to open up an additional room, or add seating since we noticed someone having to eat standing. There's an area with a desk next to the coffee area which seems out of place, they could easily fit 2 tables there. The kids loved the breakfast and the DIY waffle maker process."
