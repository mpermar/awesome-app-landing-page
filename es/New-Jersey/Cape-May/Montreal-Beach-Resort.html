Descripción

Todas las habitaciones del Montreal Beach Resort incluyen TV por cable, cafetera y nevera. Algunas suites ofrecen un dormitorio separado y una pequeña cocina.

Los huéspedes pueden visitar los viñedos y la bodega Turdo o dar un paseo en carruaje, ambos a 8 km del New Jersey Montreal Beach Resort. Compras en el Washington Street Mall está a menos de una milla de distancia.

Experiencias

05/04/2018

My grandchildren and I lived it

"This place reminded me of an Atlantic City hotel room. The room was spacious and a little luxurious with the bathroom very modern. The beds were very comfortable. We were provided with in room coffee maker wine glasses. I just loved this place. Staff was helpful and courteous."

08/07/2017

Nice rooms, but needs improvement...pricey

"Hotel room was excellent, and the kids liked the large saltwater pool. I loved the slider to the balcony. We were very unhappy with our experience at Harry's restaurant though. We went for dinner and then we were having drinks and listening to the band when the waitor brought the check and stopped waiting on us. I had to get the manager in order to get another drink. For the price I paid for the room and the amount of money we spent at the restaurant (which is overpriced for their mediocre food) I would have expected much better service. My fish taco had some kind of nasty sweet and sour sauce on it, which totally ruined it. Our breakfast at Harry's was also bad. The food did not seem fresh, challah french toast had no flavor, home fries seemed like leftovers!! The front office staff was very friendly,but there were a lot of hotel workers loitering outside smoking, being loud and blocking areas where guests need to go. It didn't make for a welcoming environment. Overall, for the price I paid, I would expect better service and more professional hotel workers. This doesn't include the very friendly and helpful front desk staff, they were great!"
