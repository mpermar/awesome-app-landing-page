Descripción

Decoradas en relajantes colores neutros, todas las habitaciones del Windsong Resort tienen conexión inalámbrica a internet gratuita y TV de pantalla plana.

El Resort Windsong tiene una gran piscina al aire libre con vistas al océano. Los huéspedes pueden alquilar kayaks o botes de remos para usar cerca de la playa. También hay un spa en el lugar que ofrece masajes y tratamientos faciales.

El JoJo’s Café del hotel sirve platos saludables en un ambiente informal para el desayuno, el almuerzo y la cena.

El complejo deportivo Graceway está a 1,6 km del complejo. El aeropuerto internacional de Providenciales se encuentra a 6,4 km.

Experiencias

20/06/2018

Amazing weekend on a beautiful beach

"We travelled for the weekend of June 16-17 and never had an issue finding a spot for breakfast or on the beach. Overall the resort was quiet with only a few families at the small but accommodating pool (no swim up bar btw). Plenty of bicycles, kayaks, floating rings (can fit up to 6ppl I think) if you want them. The resort beach has acccess to a protected reef you can snorkel around, too! The beach here is AMAZING. Plenty of covered chairs, towel service, etc. Rooms: comfortable and only $5 for a huge bottle of Fiji. We brought filtered water bottles but didn’t need them. Food: we only ate breakfast here.There’s a great little bar right next door called Somewhere with descent food or you can venture farther down the left side to Gansevoort which has a fire pit, nice little bar and Stelle restaurant. We went to the right one night and walked 2mi to the Grace Bay Club for happy hour - which gets busy!Activities: we used the bikes to go to the Salt Millls. There was side walk almost the whole way so we felt safe. I needed an ice cream by the time we made it - that sun can be rough! We also did the 1/2 day snorkel tour with Caicos Dream tours - so worth it. Iguana Island has the most beautiful beaches I’ve ever seen."
