Descripción

Las habitaciones del Tokyo Park Hyatt cuentan con paneles de madera Hokkaido y sábanas de algodón egipcio. Los huéspedes pueden tomar una copa en el bar húmedo y relajarse en la bañera profunda, o relajarse en la bata de baño suave y esponjosa y ver una película de video a pedido. Se proporcionan artículos de aseo gratuitos, zapatillas y secador de pelo. Las habitaciones también cuentan con zona de estar, escritorio y conexión a internet por cable de alta velocidad gratuita, así como conexión WiFi.

El parque central de Shinjuku se encuentra a 3 minutos a pie del hotel. La estación de tren JR Shinjuku se encuentra a 15 minutos a pie y Shinjuku Gyoen está a 20 minutos a pie.

Los huéspedes pueden disfrutar del masaje o la sauna en el Club on the Park Spa o hacer ejercicio en el gimnasio, que cuenta con ventanas de piso a techo. La biblioteca del hotel ofrece una amplia selección y la recepción está abierta las 24 horas. El servicio de cambio de divisas está disponible en el hotel.

El New York Grill del piso 52 ofrece espectaculares vistas del horizonte. Los huéspedes pueden disfrutar de una bebida en The Peak Bar mientras observan la puesta de sol en el Skyline de Tokio. En la pastelería Boutique se pueden comprar suntuosos pasteles y dulces de temporada, mientras que en el establecimiento se ofrecen modernos platos japoneses, cocina francesa y una elegante cafetería.

Shinjuku Ward es una gran opción para los viajeros interesados en ir de compras, locales amigables y comida local.

Experiencias

22/05/2018

Luxury as expected with some lovely touches

"Upgraded with a suite with our son which was great for space. Also a great park (which is hard to find). in Tokyo across the road our 4 yr old loved. It is a fair walk to the station (and back) each day so get the shuttle at least one day ! Some 7/11 type shops around corner but otherwise away from the action a bit. If you want to get out and about, I suggest Golden Gai area (although you can't take children). Also note you can't take kids into New York bar, and then need to keep fairly quiet in pool area to respect people who are relaxing. I also liked the free evening stretch session they had on at 9pm one night. Keep an eye out for activities. !"

24/08/2017

Pleasant, comfortable, and dated.

"Good value for money, in terms of the more upgraded rooms. Comfortable beds, nice Aesop toiletries, soundproofing, and a convenient iPhone charger which also allowed for streaming video from the phone to the tv (great for the kids when they want to just chill with a film). Internet was fast, free, and reliable. Tea/coffee (Nespresso) in room was decent. The bar on the 52nd floor has a nice atmosphere and live music every night, a good assortment of (western) drinks and food, but it's extremely expensive. Room service provided ice in the rooms all the time, and the mini-bar is well stocked. Swimming pool is kid-friendly."

21/01/2016

It was an amazing stay for our entire family.

"It was an amazing stay for our entire family. My children even had bath robes to fit, for their size & age. Everything about the hotel is stunning, including the little cafe in the basement! The staff our so incredibly lovely, assisting our every need. Our children watched a movie in bed, while we had a drink in the rooftop bar. We shared 2 double beds in our room, which was more than comfortable for a family of 4."
