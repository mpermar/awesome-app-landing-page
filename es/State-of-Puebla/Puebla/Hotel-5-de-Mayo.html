Descripción

All rooms features air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a coffee machine, a bath or shower, free toiletries and a desk. Rooms have a private bathroom with a hair dryer. All guest rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area.

An American breakfast is available daily at Hotel 5 de Mayo. There's an on-site restaurant, which serves Mexican cuisine.

Speaking Spanish and English, staff are always on hand to help at the reception.

Acropolis Puebla is 2.3 miles from the accommodation, while Cuauhtemoc Stadium is 2.5 miles away. The nearest airport is Hermanos Serdán International Airport, 14 miles from Hotel 5 de Mayo.

Puebla Centro is a great choice for travelers interested in museums, architecture and old-town exploration.
