Descripción

Las habitaciones del Negril Treehouse Resort tienen vistas a los jardines y están equipadas con TV por cable. El aire acondicionado está incluido y hay conexión Wi-Fi gratuita en el vestíbulo del hotel.

El restaurante del Negril Treehouse sirve una mezcla distinta de platos jamaicanos e internacionales, que incluyen mariscos recién preparados y cabra al curry. También se sirven cerveza y pizzas Red Stripe.

Se ofrecen olas y esquí acuático en el mar. Una amplia piscina al aire libre ofrece una experiencia de agua más tranquila y se pueden organizar servicios de masajes a través de la tienda de regalos del complejo.

El Treehouse Resort en Negril está a 5 minutos del centro de Negril y a 13 minutos de Orange Bay.

20/05/2015

Had an amazing time at the Tree House!

"Had an amazing time at the Tree House! Would definitely recommend tofriends and return myself.- BEACH: One of the best I've experienced. Fine yellow sand, amazingly clear water, beautiful turquoise color. No rocks so it was very comfortable to walk in. You could go out a fair distance and still be only 5 feet deep, so food for children and non-swimmers. The water was generally pretty still, no huge waves at any point. We did see a couple of manta rays nearly every day swimming close to the shore for a bit - scary at first, but they were totally harmless.- HOTEL ROOMS: Awesomely priced, spacious rooms that get lots of light and have a small outdoor space. Feels like you're staying at a friend's house, not a hotel. They have AC but the ceiling fan will generally do. A number of American TV channels available for viewing. Free in-room wifi generally reliable though not fast. - HOTEL GROUNDS: Small, but in a quaint and charming way as opposed to cramped. No matter where your room is you are a 2 minute walk from the lobby and the beach. There's one bar with fair prices and generally good food. It's not a scene, but convenient when you want to relax and eat/drink. - STAFF: Incredibly helpful and attentive. Reliable beachside service. Friendly front desk folks who are great with recommendations. And of course I can't forget to mention the famous singing chef, who really makes you feel special and at home. - LOCATION: Things are fairly quiet in Negril, at least in the low season (which includes May when I visited). The staff said that earlier in the year, as well as in the late summer things pick up a ton and there are a lot more people. Even then I don't think I'd consider it a particularly busy party scene, though there are definitely late-night options if you're looking for fun."
