Descripción

Evergreen Laurel Hotel - Keelung se encuentra a 25 minutos en coche del aeropuerto de Taipei Songshan y a 70 minutos en coche del aeropuerto internacional de Taiwán Taoyuan. Conduciendo desde la propiedad, los huéspedes pueden llegar a Jinguashi en 30 minutos, Yehliu en 35 minutos y Jiufen en 40 minutos.

Todas las habitaciones tienen aire acondicionado, caja fuerte, minibar y nevera. El baño privado incluye bañera, artículos de aseo gratuitos y secador de pelo. Algunas habitaciones tienen vistas al puerto, la montaña o la ciudad.

El centro de negocios del hotel ofrece acceso a internet, computadora, fax y servicio de llamadas internacionales. El amable personal estará encantado de ayudar a los huéspedes con el almacenamiento de equipaje y el servicio de venta de entradas en la recepción las 24 horas. Los extras ofrecen servicios de limpieza en seco, planchado y lavandería. El atento servicio de transporte también es posible bajo petición.

Con una ventana del piso al techo, el bar del vestíbulo ofrece a los huéspedes una amplia variedad de postres, refrigerios y un ambiente acogedor para relajarse. Los salones de café 18 y 19 sirven platos de buffet y cuentan con vistas al mar y al puerto. Previa solicitud, los salones de baile funcionales están disponibles para que los huéspedes realicen conferencias y banquetes.

11/01/2017

Pleasent but may require a few adjusments.

"The staff was brilliant; friendly, helpful and the service was 5 star. We were upgraded and had the view of the port/ harbour. The complimentary spa facilities (sauna, steam room, jacuzzi and resting area) were great - clean and spacious. Note that the spa facilities for males and females are seperated, like most hotels in asia and you have to be nude to use the facilities, except the rest area. However, when my wife was using the female spa facilities, she had seen a lady with a towel wrapped around her in the sauna and no one complained in that situation, so i think it really depends if someone makes a complaint. The indoor pool was a good size with a balcony/ terrace. Swim cap is required, we didn't have any but the hotel provided us each with one free of charge for the duration of our stay, which was again a plus since some hotels charge. They had a children's play area with toys and game consoles such as WII and Xbox 1, great for kids and families. Our room was clean and a good size. The bed on our first night was a bit too firm for our liking, however, upon mentioning it to the staff the next day, they promptly changed the mattress to a softer one, we had no more issues after. The hotel is a short walk to the bus stop, which you can conveniently take bus 788 to places like Jiufen old street/ Jinguanszi old street (about 35 - 45 minutes away). The railway station is about 10 - 14 minutes away on foot. There were frequent local train service to Taipei, which is about 45 minutes away or less depending on which stop you alight. There were also plenty of food places and convenience stores nearby."
