Descripción

All guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. The rooms have a private bathroom and a bath, and selected rooms also feature a balcony. All rooms at Hotel Portal del Angel have air conditioning and a desk.

A continental breakfast is available every morning at the accommodation.

Hotel Portal del Angel has a terrace.
