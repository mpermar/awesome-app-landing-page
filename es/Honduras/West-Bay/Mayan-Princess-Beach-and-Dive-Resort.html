Descripción

Mayan Princess Beach and Dove Resort’s villas are elegantly furnished and feature a 32-inch LCD flat-screen satellite TV, sofa bed, furnished terrace, internet access, electronic safety deposit box, fully equipped kitchen with microwave and coffee machine, plus a private bathroom with shower.

The resort also offers beach volleyball courts, board games and a book exchange service. Massages, facials, and mani and pedi are some of the services offered in the spa facilities. Scuba diving lessons are also given on site. There is also a gift shop as well as a hair salon.

The Mayan Princess Beach and Dove Resort features 4 gourmet-style restaurants that serve à la carte or buffet services, vegetarian meals, low calorie or natural cuisine along with a wide variety of national and international selection of wines, liqueurs and beers. Live music and cultural shows are also available.

Juan Manuel Galvez International Airport can be reached in a 18-minute drive.
