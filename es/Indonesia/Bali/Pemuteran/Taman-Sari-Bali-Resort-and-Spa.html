Descripción

Las habitaciones están equipadas con aire acondicionado, cocina americana y una terraza privada. Se proporcionan camas de día.

Los huéspedes pueden bucear, hacer senderismo o relajarse en el spa de Taman Sari. Los servicios incluyen alquiler de bicicletas y un mostrador de información turística. Transporte al aeropuerto también se proporciona.

El Taman Sari Bali Spa and Resort se encuentra a 4 horas en coche del aeropuerto internacional Ngurah Rai de Denpasar.

Beautiful,family friendly resort right on the beach, great for relaxing and swimming/snorkelling

"Beautiful location right on the beach, lovely, friendly staff who went out of their way to help, spotlessly clean rooms, snorkelling right off the beach, even a very nice traditional Balinese dance and gamelan ensemble performing one night. We had a great stay at the Taman Sari, staying first in a very smart villa with private pool, then in a much smaller studio. Both were extremely clean. We ended up staying an extra night.Great chilled place, lovely place to relax. Very child friendly, nothing was too much trouble (they arranged a cake on our son's birthday). At night you can look up at the stars from the beach as the waves hit the shore. Nice sandy beach. Not the cheapest (in fact quite pricey!) but definitely worth it.Off the beach, though it's great to be able to snorkel right there, there is some coral bleaching happening and even the world famous dive site nearby (around 45 mins by boat) was not quite as spectacular as the guide books had suggested it would be (though still teeming with fish, and we saw a turtle). The views of the volcanoes on Java are amazing, on that trip, though. If you do go on a dive/snorkelling trip, make sure you ask the boat to drop you so you can snorkel with the current, rather than against it, which is hard work."
