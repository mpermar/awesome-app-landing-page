Descripción

Paradise View ofrece un servicio profesional con un toque personal. Construido en el estilo tradicional de la isla, este hotel está situado en Paradise Beach. Los huéspedes pueden relajarse en la hermosa terraza que rodea la piscina al aire libre. La ubicación del hotel ofrece hermosas vistas de la playa y el paisaje.

Paradise Beach ofrece una gran variedad de bares de playa, tiendas, bares, restaurantes y tabernas griegas. El aeropuerto está a solo 10 minutos en coche y el centro de Mykonos está a solo 15 minutos. Se puede acceder usando el autobús local, que se detiene frente al hotel. También hay acceso gratuito a internet inalámbrico en el hotel.

13/09/2017

Kindness and tranquility

"The staff at Paradise View were exceptionally kind, friendly and accommodating. They were fabulous with our children. We traveled in peak season with 3 small children. Mykonos isn't ideal for kids regardless of location but it's still a fab place to visit. The hotel location is near the 'party beach' Paradise Beach, which is beautiful but gets busy and wild later in the day as all the party-goers surface. It was fine in the morning but not sure how suitable it would be later for families with kids. We only stayed 1 night so cannot comment. Getting to the beach is easy, a short walk, but it's hilly so the walk back is tough after a day at the beach. The hotel itself met our needs - had everything we needed. It's basic for what we paid but that's because it was last minute and in peak season. The pool is beautiful and offers peace and tranquility on what can be a busy and lively island. A bonus is that transfers are free - a welcomed and much-appreciated surprise. Getting to Mykonos Town is a short bus or cab ride, but cabs are in short supply and it will cost you about 20E. George, thank you for making our 1 night in Mykonos great, for being so helpful and kind. One day we will return, without the kids!"
