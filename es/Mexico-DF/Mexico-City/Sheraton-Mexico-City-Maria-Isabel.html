Descripción

Each room here includes air conditioning, a minibar and cable channels. The beds have a custom-designed plush mattress, a comforter and plush pillows. The elegant and spacious bathrooms have either a bathtub or a shower. Extras include a desk, a safe and ironing facilities.

At Sheraton Maria Isabel Hotel Towers there is a tennis court and a fitness center. Jorongo, the on-site bar, features live mariachi music. Ristorante Amici, the award-winning restaurant at Sheraton Maria Isabel, specializes in Italian cuisine.

The hotel is a short 5-minute walk from the Mexican Stock Exchange Building and right next to the U.S. Embassy. The historic center is a 10-minute drive away.
