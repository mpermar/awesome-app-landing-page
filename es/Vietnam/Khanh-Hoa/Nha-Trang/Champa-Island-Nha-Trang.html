Descripción

Las habitaciones del complejo están equipadas con una tetera eléctrica. Algunas habitaciones del Champa Island Nha Trang cuentan con baño privado y vistas a la piscina. Las habitaciones incluyen TV de pantalla plana con canales vía satélite.

Todas las mañanas se ofrece un desayuno buffet y desayuno completo inglés / irlandés en el alojamiento. En Champa Island Nha Trang encontrarás un restaurante que sirve cocina de mariscos. También se puede solicitar una opción vegetariana.

El complejo cuenta con un parque infantil. Puedes jugar al billar en Champa Island Nha Trang.

La recepción puede ofrecer consejos útiles para moverse por la zona.

El mercado Dam se encuentra a 12 minutos a pie del alojamiento, mientras que el Museo Alexandre Yersin está a 1,5 km. El aeropuerto internacional de Cam Ranh se encuentra a 29 km.

Experiencias

15/02/2018

An excellent place with caring staff

"We fell in love with this place. Staff are wonderful, and they tried to provide a 5-star service experience every day of our stay, and we could see they cared. We stayed for 3 weeks and we were sorry to leave. The territory is large, and there was a lot of space for our 2 toddlers to run around and explore. A small cosy playground, safe swimming pools. I work remotely, so I truly appreciated wi-fi in every single corner of the island and their pretty business center. Breakfasts were great, and both people who eat fresh veggies/fruit and people who eat cooked food had a lot of options. The fitness center opens early, and it is outdoors - that's an excellent idea. We visited during Lunar New Year, so there were hundreds of tourists, and the staff stayed organised and handled everything really well. All in all, a truly wonderful experience. Thank you for everything!"
