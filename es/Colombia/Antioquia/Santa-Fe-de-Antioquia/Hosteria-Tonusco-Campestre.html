Descripción

Guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Rooms are equipped with a private bathroom, and some rooms include a balcony. The rooms have a closet.

A continental breakfast is served daily at the property.

Hosteria Tonusco Campestre has a playground. Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Santa Fe de Antioquia, like cycling.

Hosteria Tonusco Campestre can conveniently provide information at the reception to help guests to get around the area.

Olaya Herrera Airport is 29 miles away.
