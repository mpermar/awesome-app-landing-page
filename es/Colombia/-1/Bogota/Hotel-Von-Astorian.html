Descripción

At the hotel, the rooms come with a closet. Rooms are equipped with a private bathroom with free toiletries, while some have city views. All guest rooms in Hotel Von Astorian are equipped with a flat-screen TV and a hair dryer.

A à la carte or American breakfast is served at the property. Guests can enjoy a meal at the in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of international dishes.

At the accommodation guests are welcome to use a fitness room. Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near Hotel Von Astorian.

Guests can make sightseeing and ticketing arrangements at the tour desk, or conduct business at the business center.

Corferias International Exhibition Center is 1.4 miles from the hotel.

Teusaquillo is a great choice for travelers interested in business, shopping and city walks.
