Descripción

Hampton by Hilton Cartagena has rooms with air conditioning, cable TV and private bathrooms.

A daily breakfast is included.

Guests can use computers with Internet access at the lobby

The city’s convention centers as well as the historic circuit are 3 miles away. Rafael Nuñez Airport is 5.5 miles away.
