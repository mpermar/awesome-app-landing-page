Descripción

At San Rafael del Campo - Estación Turística, every room includes a patio. Rooms have a coffee machine, while some have a balcony. All guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

A continental breakfast is served daily at the property. Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant, which specializes in international cuisine.

San Rafael del Campo - Estación Turística has a playground. Guests at San Rafael del Campo - Estación Turística will be able to enjoy activities in and around Barichara, like cycling.

San Gil is 7 miles from San Rafael del Campo - Estación Turística. The nearest airport is Palonegro International Airport, 34 miles from the property.
