Descripción

The vacation home features 9 bedrooms, a living room, and a kitchen with a dining area and a dishwasher. A TV is offered.

The property has a hot tub and a sauna. Guests can enjoy the outdoor swimming pool and garden at the vacation home.

Pereira is 24 miles from La Herencia Hotel. The nearest airport is El Edén International Airport, 1.9 miles from the accommodation.
