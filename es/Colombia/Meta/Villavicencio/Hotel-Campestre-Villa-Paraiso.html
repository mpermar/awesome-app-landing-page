Descripción

At the hotel, every room comes with a closet. The private bathroom is equipped with a shower. At Hotel Campestre Villa Paraiso, every room includes air conditioning and a flat-screen TV.

A continental breakfast is available daily at the property. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of seafood, steakhouse and local dishes and also offers vegetarian and dairy-free options.

The wellness area at the accommodation is comprised of a hot tub, a sauna and a hammam. Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near Hotel Campestre Villa Paraiso.

The hotel also offers a car rental service and a business center. Staff are always available to help at the reception.

The nearest airport is La Vanguardia Airport, 0.6 miles from the property.
