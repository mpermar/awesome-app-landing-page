Descripción

All units come with a patio, a kitchen with a dishwasher, and a shared bathroom. There's also a microwave, fridge and a coffee machine.

The vacation home has an outdoor pool.

There's a a playground at this property and guests can go cycling nearby.

Palmira is 30 miles from Villa Corinigua, while La Cumbre is 20 miles away. Alfonso Bonilla Aragón International Airport is 27 miles from the property.
