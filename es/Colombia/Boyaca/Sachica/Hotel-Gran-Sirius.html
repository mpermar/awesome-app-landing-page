Descripción

At the hotel, every room comes with a closet and a flat-screen TV. With a private bathroom equipped with a shower and free toiletries, some units at Hotel Gran Sirius also offer a terrace.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at the property.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool. Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near Hotel Gran Sirius.

Villa de Leyva is 3.4 miles from the hotel, while Tunja is 13 miles away.
