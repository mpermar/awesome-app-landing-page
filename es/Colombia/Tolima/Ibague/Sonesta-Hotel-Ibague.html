Descripción

All guest rooms in Sonesta Hotel Ibagué are equipped with a TV. The rooms are equipped with a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries. All rooms at Sonesta Hotel Ibagué feature air conditioning and a desk.

An American breakfast is served at the property.

The wellness area at Sonesta Hotel Ibagué is comprised of a sauna and an indoor pool.

Sonesta Hotel Ibagué offers a business center for guest use.

Girardot is 29 miles from Sonesta Hotel Ibagué. The nearest airport is Perales Airport, 5 miles from the property.
