Descripción

Guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom, while some rooms at Hotel Chinauta Real also have a terrace.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy an American breakfast.

The property has a sauna. You can play ping pong at Hotel Chinauta Real, and the area is popular for hiking.

Melgar is 14 miles from the hotel, while Girardot is 23 miles away. El Dorado International Airport is 36 miles from the property.
