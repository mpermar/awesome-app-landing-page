Descripción

The vacation home has 5 bedrooms, a living room, a fully equipped kitchen with a dining area, and a bathroom with a hot tub. A flat-screen TV with satellite channels is provided.

The property has a hot tub and an indoor pool. A playground and a sun terrace are available for guests at the property to use.

Melgar is 14 miles from El Peñon. The nearest airport is Perales Airport, 23 miles from the property.
