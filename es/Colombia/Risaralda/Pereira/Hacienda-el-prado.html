Descripción

At the hostel, each room has a balcony. The private bathroom is equipped with a shower and a hair dryer. The rooms will provide guests with a microwave.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property.

The wellness area at Hacienda el prado is comprised of a hot tub and a sauna. You can play ping pong at the accommodation.

Staff speak English and Spanish at the 24-hour front desk.

Viaduct between Pereira and Dosquebradas is 3 miles from Hacienda el prado, while César Gaviria Trujillo Viaduct is 3.1 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Matecaña International, 6 miles from the hostel, and the property offers a free shuttle service.
