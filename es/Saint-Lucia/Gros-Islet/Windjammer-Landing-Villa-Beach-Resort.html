Descripción

Las habitaciones y villas del Windjammer disponen de aire acondicionado, conexión a internet por cable de alta velocidad gratuita, TV por cable, mininevera y set de té y café. Los baños están equipados con secador de pelo y artículos de tocador gratuitos. Algunas de las villas totalmente equipadas tienen una piscina privada.

El spa Serenity cuenta con un área de piscina privada, baño de vapor y sala de tratamientos con vista a jardines tropicales. Los tratamientos incluyen aromaterapia, masajes, envolturas corporales y tratamientos faciales. El complejo también cuenta con bañera de hidromasaje y gimnasio.

Cenas informales y formales están disponibles en esta villa de Santa Lucía. Hay 5 restaurantes y 3 bares para elegir.

El Jacquot Fun Club de Windjammer tiene actividades supervisadas y noches temáticas para niños de 4 a 12 años. Los adolescentes pueden disfrutar de noches de fogata, karaoke y deportes acuáticos en el Teen Club.

Experiencias

23/01/2018

Lovely week at the Windjammer. Breathtaking views, lovely people. Complete relaxation!

"We traveled as a family, but our children are 22 and 19. I think the bedroom setup for families with younger children who need quite a bit of their parents attention (preschool and elementary school aged kids) , would not be ideal. Kids were upstairs with their own porch, we were downstairs. Little ones I think would need more supervision or closer proximity to parents. The two bedroom villa was perfect for us with grown up children. We had the space we needed, as well as plenty of space to hang out together with a spectacular view. We didn't get the all inclusive plan because we didn't know it was available until after we got there. I think it would be worth it to look into the all inclusive plan if you are going. Food was mediocre...ok to good, not great, at all restaurants at the resort and very expensive. If you want to do any cooking or eating in the villa, suggested that we should have stopped at a grocery store prior to arriving at the resort. There is a small grocery store on resort, but it is quite expensive. However, a man in a boat comes to the beach twice a week to sell delicious tropical fruit, which was fun and the fruit was good. Main shared areas of villa were open air, bedrooms were air conditioned. Loved the private pool on our porch of the villa. Most of the staff were lovely. The gym was very nice. One note, there is a shuttle from villa to beach or main resort area and restaurants, but if you have any disability (or small kids) the hills are VERY steep. If you don't want to wait for the shuttle, you may find the hills challenging. It would not deter me personally, as the view from the mountain tops are beautiful, but it was challenging to walk up. Staff mentioned people can request lower level villas for those with disabilities.Consider the open jeep, land and sea tour! A great way to see the island in one day! So fun!"
