Descripción

All units feature air conditioning and a satellite flat-screen TV. Some units have a terrace and/or balcony.

A continental, à la carte or gluten-free breakfast is served at the property.

The condo hotel has an outdoor pool.

Fishing can be enjoyed nearby and a car rental service is available.

Colon Bus Station is one mile from Iguape Aparts.
