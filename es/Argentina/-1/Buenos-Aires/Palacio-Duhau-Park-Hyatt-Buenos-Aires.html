Descripción

Decorated with Belle Epoque furnishing and contemporary painting, rooms at Palacio Duhau are equipped with air conditioning, heating and plasma TVs. All of them offer panoramic views, and some have fireplaces and spa bathtubs.

A full buffet breakfast is served daily. Argentinean specialties can be ordered at Duhau Restaurante, the main property restaurant featuring a large wine cellar. Al Fresco dinners can be enjoyed at Gioia Restaurante & Terraces, which features a private terrace overlooking the palace and its gardens.

Drinks and snacks are served at the Oak Bar, which features a wood burning fireplace and garden views. Guests are welcome to have breakfast at the Salones de Piano Nobile lounge, which opens all day and offers individual choice menu service.

Guests can enjoy several beauty treatments at the spa, request a massage session or relax in the hot tub. A gift shop is featured and babysitting service is available upon request. The most exclusive boutiques can be found around the corner. Puerto Madero district and the Malba Museum are both 2 miles away.

Valet parking service is provided. Palacio Duhau - Park Hyatt Buenos Aires is 21 miles from Ezeiza International Airport and 4 miles from Jorge Newbery Domestic Airport. Palermo Soho district is 3 miles away.

Recoleta is a great choice for travelers interested in gourmet food, food and museums.
