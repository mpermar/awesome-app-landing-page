Descripción

Featuring pool views, the vacation home is composed of 2 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms with free toiletries. The kitchen comes with an oven and a microwave, as well as a coffee machine and an electric tea pot.

Around the clock guidance is available at the reception, where staff speak English and Spanish.

The vacation home has a grill. The property has a playground.

Salta is 7 miles from Tu Lugar en el Mundo. The nearest airport is Martin Miguel de Güemes International Airport, 12 miles from the accommodation.
