Descripción

At the hotel rooms are equipped with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a kitchenette, a dining area and a private bathroom with free toiletries, a bath and a hair dryer. The rooms come with a coffee machine, while some have a balcony. Guest rooms at AQ Tailored Suites feature air conditioning and a desk.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool.

Speaking English, Spanish, French and Portuguese at the reception, staff are always on hand to help.

Museo Bellas Artes Argentina is a 19-minute walk from AQ Tailored Suites, while Cabildo is 1.2 miles away.

Recoleta is a great choice for travelers interested in gourmet food, food and museums.
