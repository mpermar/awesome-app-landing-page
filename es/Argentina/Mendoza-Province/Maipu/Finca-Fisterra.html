Descripción

The air-conditioned units have a fully equipped kitchenette with dining area, an oven, electric tea pot, and a microwave. A patio with mountain views is offered in all units.

Guests at the condo hotel can enjoy a continental breakfast. The restaurant at Finca Fisterra serves international cuisine.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool.

A grill can be found at the property, along with a shared lounge.

La Rural Winery is 5 miles from Finca Fisterra. The nearest airport is Governor Francisco Gabrielli International Airport, 16 miles from the property.
