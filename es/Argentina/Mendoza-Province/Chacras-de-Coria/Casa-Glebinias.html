Descripción

Decorated with parquet floors and exposed-brick walls, rooms at Casa Glebinias have large windows overlooking the garden. They provide heating and air conditioning. All of them have kitchen facilities and private bathrooms.

A buffet breakfast is served daily. Guests can enjoy drinks and snacks at the bar, or a barbecue on the grounds. They can also sample one of the many wines from the property’s cellar.

The city of Mendoza is 9 miles away, and Vallecitos Ski Centre is 43 miles away. Casa Glebinias is 25 minutes from the bus terminal by car and 19 miles from El Plumerillo Airport.
