Descripción

Guest rooms at the hotel come with a flat-screen TV and a kitchenette. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with free toiletries, while certain rooms at Cabanas Los Troncos also have a balcony. All rooms will provide guests with a microwave.

Fishing is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.

Baradero is 16 miles from Cabanas Los Troncos.
