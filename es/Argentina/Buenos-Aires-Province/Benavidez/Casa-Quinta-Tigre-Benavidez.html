Descripción

The 4-bedroom vacation home features a living room with a TV with cable channels, a fully equipped kitchen, and a bathroom with bath. The vacation home also provides guests with a seating area and a bathroom.

Staff at the reception can assist guests at any time of the day.

The vacation home has an outdoor pool. A bicycle rental service is available at this property and skiing can be enjoyed nearby.

Buenos Aires is 22 miles from Casa Quinta Tigre Benavidez, while Tigre is 7 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Jorge Newbery Airfield Airport, 19 miles from the accommodation.
