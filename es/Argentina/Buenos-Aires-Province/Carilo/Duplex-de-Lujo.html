Descripción

The apartment has 2 bedrooms, a living room, and a kitchenette with a dining area. A flat-screen TV with satellite channels is available.

The apartment offers a sauna. An outdoor swimming pool is available for guests at Duplex de Lujo to use.

Villa Gesell is 7 miles from the accommodation, while Pinamar is 4.1 miles away. The nearest airport is Villa Gesell - Pinamar International Airport, 9 miles from the property.
