Descripción

All units feature a satellite flat-screen TV, a private bathroom and a fully equipped kitchen. A terrace with garden views is offered in all units.

A continental breakfast is served at the property.

Cabañas Hoja de la Tierra has a grill.

There's a kids' club offering various activities, as well as a playground.

The nearest airport is La Plata Airport, 40 miles from the property.

Políticas de Alojamiento

Children and Extra Beds
All children are welcome.
One child under 16 years is charged USD 80 per night when using existing beds.
All children under 2 years are charged USD 50 per night in a crib.
All children under 12 years are charged USD 80 per night for extra beds.
Any additional older children or adults are charged USD 100 per night for extra beds.
The maximum number of extra beds in a room is 1.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
