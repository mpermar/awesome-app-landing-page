Descripción

At the bed and breakfast, the rooms come with a closet, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Las Lechuzas provides some rooms that have a patio, and every room includes a electric tea pot. Guest rooms will provide guests with a microwave.

An American breakfast is served each morning at the property.

The accommodation has a playground. You can play ping pong and pool at Las Lechuzas.

Calvario Hill is 4.3 miles from the bed and breakfast, while Independence Park is 4.5 miles from the property.
