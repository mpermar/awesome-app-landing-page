Experiencias

08/04/2018

This was the perfect place to finish our two week trip to Nicaragua!

"The beds were really comfortable and the shower was the best one I used during our two week trip through Nicaragua. The hotel is right on the beach. Breakfasts were amazing - I especially enjoyed the option of the smoothie bowls after a lot of consecutive days eating eggs, rice and beans for breakfast. We stayed in the "family room" with our two kids, ages 4 and 8. It was great that it was on the main floor, so it was easy for us to put our kids to bed and then lounge with a book and a drink in the common area."
