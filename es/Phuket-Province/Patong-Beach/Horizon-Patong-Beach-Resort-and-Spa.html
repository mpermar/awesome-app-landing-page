Descripción

El Patong Horizon Beach Resort ofrece aparcamiento gratuito y servicio de traslado gratuito a la playa de Karon. El aeropuerto internacional de Phuket está a 40 km del complejo.

Las habitaciones del complejo presentan interiores tailandeses modernos y mucha luz natural. Las habitaciones están bien amuebladas y cuentan con TV y baño privado con ducha de agua caliente.

Los huéspedes pueden hacer ejercicio en el gimnasio o relajarse en las piscinas. Disfrutar de un masaje corporal relajante en el Morradok Spa es otra buena opción de recreación.

El galardonado restaurante Baluchi sirve platos indios, y el restaurante Borsalino sirve pizzas. Bebidas refrescantes están disponibles en Jimmy’s Corner Sports Bar.

Lovely atmosphere, great pools, perfect location, very helpful staff

"The location of this hotel is perfect for access to Patong Beach, the OTop markets and the Patong central area including Junceylon Shopping Precinct and Bangla rd. Plenty of eating places and convenience stores right next to the hotel. We stayed for 7 nights. I think this hotel is good for couples, families or groups.The hotel has two pools which are perfect for relaxation or having a chat with friends. Both pools have bars (the family pool is a swim up bar). We spent most of the time in the adult pool area and making use of the 3pm to 6pm happy hour (2 for the price of 1 cocktails). Very good value and very good cocktails. I'm not a big drinker but did indulge quite a few times. The breakfast selection is excellent. You could not ask for more selection. There are two cooks at breakfast who supply omelettes, scrambled eggs, fried eggs etc. to request (delicious). If you order poached eggs or fried eggs, just make sure you ask them to have them well done. The grounds and the rooms are very neat and quite spacious. The staff were very friendly and helpful. There is a free shuttle from the Horizon hotel that runs several times a day between Patong and the other Horizon Hotel at Karon Beach (worth checking out, just see reception to book it). There is a really good massage shop on the right hand side as your looking at the entrance to the hotel (Tanya Salon and Massage). Most of us in the group I was travelling with visited it them a number of times and thought they were excellent). The general ambiance of the property is that you are in Thailand. It is modern with its facilities and still has the Thai charm to make you feel you are really on holidays in Thailand. Overall just a great experience."
