Descripción

Con techos con vigas de madera y terrazas privadas, las amplias habitaciones se abren a vistas tropicales o de la piscina. Incluyen un televisor de pantalla plana de 32 pulgadas, un sofá y conexión inalámbrica a internet gratuita. Algunas habitaciones tienen piscinas privadas y pabellones.

Los huéspedes pueden disfrutar de relajantes masajes tailandeses o descansar junto a la gran piscina al aire libre, que cuenta con chorros de agua. El gimnasio, que cuenta con ventanas de piso a techo, está ubicado dentro de jardines tropicales.

Con comidas durante todo el día, el restaurante Himmphan es ideal para una comida informal de platos occidentales y asiáticos. También sirve platos de mariscos frescos, y el bar de la piscina sirve jugos y cócteles recién exprimidos.

En la tranquila zona de Kata Beach, el Metadee Resort está a solo 15 minutos en coche de las opciones de vida nocturna de Soi Bangla. Ofrece aparcamiento gratuito y un servicio de transporte gratuito al aeropuerto desde y hacia el aeropuerto internacional de Phuket, situado a 45 minutos en coche.

Experiencias

12/04/2017

A week in paradise

"Everything about our stay at the Metadee Resort and Villas was incredible. The staff were warm and inviting. Golf, Tar, Fern, Kluay, Ball, Song, Bow and Ass were among some of the super friendly and kind staff during our stay. The private pool villa was gorgeous and they provided everything we could have possibly needed, including a roll away bed to fit our youngest child. You get a free stocked mini bar (non-alcoholic) daily and tons of bottled water. Beach towels, flip flops and a beach bag are in your room. Room service and the hotel restaurant and bar have a great variety of Thai and Western food and drink. Breakfast buffet was great, had a variety of breads, danish, veg, fresh tropical fruit, yogurts, cereals, juices, and Thai food, as well as an omelet and egg station and coffee, tea and fresh juice station. The spa was beautiful and gave top notch service. And the prices were incredibly reasonable for all services, including in room babysitting, food, drink, and spa treatments. They have a free shuttle to the edge of the property or to nearby Karon Beach and a paid service to and from the airport at a very affordable price. And the POOL! The pool was just beautiful and huge. Surrounded by Plumeria trees with mountain views. This was the star of our stay with our three young boys. We spent most days lounging by the pool ordering drinks and food and swimming around the sizeable pool. I was sad to leave and want to come back next year!!"
