Descripción

Las habitaciones del hotel tienen balcones privados y aire acondicionado. Cuentan con una amplia sala de estar con TV e incluyen cocina americana con utensilios de cocina. Los baños privados ofrecen una selección de artículos de tocador.

En el restaurante del Kantary Bay Hotel se sirve cocina internacional y una variedad de platos tailandeses recién preparados.

El Cape Spa del hotel ofrece tratamientos de masajes tradicionales y un gimnasio con actividades físicas. Hay conexión Wi-Fi gratuita en las zonas comunes y aparcamiento privado gratuito en el establecimiento.

Ubicado en Cape Panwa, el Kantary Hotel es una escapada a las famosas atracciones de Phuket, incluida la playa de Kata Noi y el templo Wat Chalong.

20/03/2016

This was our 3rd visit to Cape Panwa.

"This was our 3rd visit to Cape Panwa. The first two trips we stayed in the Cape Panwa Hotel but that was pre-kids, so for the first trip with kids we decided to stay at the Kantary Bay as the rooms are apartments as opposed to hotel rooms. On our second trip to Cape Panwa we actually got married at the Cape Panwa Hotel and we had lots of friends and family staying in Kantary Bay and all gave it great reviews.Staying at Kantary was a much more affordable option and better set up for kids. The apartment we were given was separated from the other apartments and was more villa like and had a large terraced area out the front. The kitchen was fairly basic but had all the key things we needed for our stay including fridge freezer, microwave, kettle, toaster, washing machine. The only thing lacking was a plug for the kitchen sink which meant we had to do all our washing up in the bathroom sink.The other hotel facilities were good, the kids liked both pools, but mainly used the large one as the rooftop can get quite hot and is unshaded. The major benefit of Kantary is that you can use all the Cape Panwa Hotel facilities and sign all food, drinks, massages etc to your Kantary Bay room. I found all of the hotel staff to be very friendly and welcoming and this definitely adds to the overall appeal of the hotel.I would definitely recommend people to stay here, if you are looking for nice, value for money, comfortable apartment facilities, but if you are wanting a high end hotel experience I would recommend paying a bit extra to stay at the Cape Panwa Hotel."
