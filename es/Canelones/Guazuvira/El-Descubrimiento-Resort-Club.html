Descripción

With gabled roofs and exposed brick walls, the chalet at El Descubrimiento feature heating, air conditioning and plasma TVs. All of them have private gardens with BBQ facilities.

A breakfast buffet is served daily. Guests can relax on the lounge chairs available by the pools, or use the hot tub. Spa facilities include a sauna and a massage room. A tennis court, a soccer field and a volleyball court are also featured.

Free parking is available. El Descubrimiento Resort Club is 25 miles from Piriápolis and 9 miles from Atlántida. Carrasco International Airport is 25 miles away.
