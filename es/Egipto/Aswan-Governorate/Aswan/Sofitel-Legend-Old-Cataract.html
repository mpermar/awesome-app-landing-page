Descripción

Este edificio victoriano con una fachada de granito rosa tiene habitaciones modernas con baño privado. Cada una de las habitaciones con aire acondicionado está decorada con muebles de madera.

El Sofitel Legend Old Cataract tiene 4 restaurantes que sirven platos internacionales y orientales para el desayuno, el almuerzo y la cena. El restaurante Oriental Kebabgy tiene una terraza con vistas panorámicas al Nilo.

Los 4 bares del hotel ofrecen bebidas refrescantes. The Promenade tiene vistas a las Islas Elefantinas, mientras que The Bar es un salón de piano nocturno en un ambiente oriental clásico.

Los huéspedes pueden visitar el spa del hotel y relajarse en la sauna, el hammam o la bañera de hidromasaje. También pueden usar el gimnasio o disfrutar de tratamientos de masaje.

El aeropuerto internacional de Asuán está a 36 millas de distancia, y el hotel ofrece aparcamiento privado gratuito en el establecimiento.

I can only repeat every word of what Katiero wrote!

"I can only repeat every word of what Katiero wrote! I had dreamed of going to this hotel for almost 30 years, after seeing the film (4x so far) of Agatha Christie's "Death on the Nile". The real hotel far surpassed every one of my expectations -- A Dream Come True!I too was upgraded, at no extra cost, to a fantastic suite, overlooking the Nile. Above the Nile, in every direction from my balcony (which reminded me of a wealthy sheik's tent, so unusual), I could see the large temple excavations directly across the river, Aga Khan's splendid tomb, that looked like a sand-colored Taj Mahal up high in the sand dunes, and at night, the lit-up tombs of the nobles, while the prayers were sung from the nearby towers. Utter magic! I was given a tour through the splendid Winston Churchill suite and the Agatha Christie suite. I felt like a time traveller. After only seeing the Nile around Cairo, a filthy cesspool, I was so stunned at its wonderful blue color and clarity around Assuan, when I went sailing in a Feluca through the nearby islands and the First Cataract for 2 days.I could see the rocks clearly to the bottom, like the Canadian lakes of my childhood. With all the luxury of this hotel, meal prices were very reasonable, a nice surprise. Everyone in the hotel did everything they could to make my 10 days there so wonderful, esp. as I was on my own. As you may believe reading this,1 week later the magic has not worn off. In heart and spirit, I really am still there. So I MUST go back."
