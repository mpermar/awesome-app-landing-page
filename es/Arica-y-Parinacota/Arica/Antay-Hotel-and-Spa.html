Descripción

All rooms at the hotel come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a shower. Each room includes a coffee machine, while some have a terrace. The rooms include a desk.

A buffet or à la carte breakfast is served at the property. The restaurant at Antay Hotel & Spa specializes in local cuisine.

The accommodation offers a fitness center.

Arica Port is a 9-minute walk from Antay Hotel & Spa, while Station Arica-La Paz is half a mile away. The nearest airport is Chacalluta International Airport, 9 miles from the hotel.
