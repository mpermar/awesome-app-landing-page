Descripción

At the hotel, the rooms include a desk. All rooms come with a private bathroom. All rooms have a closet.

Breakfast is available daily, and includes continental and American options.

The property has a hammam. Hiking is among the activities that guests can enjoy near Finca El Refugio Hotel Spa.

Anapoima is 29 miles from the accommodation, while Villeta is 6 miles away. El Dorado International Airport is 25 miles from the property.
