Descripción

Rooms at Diplomatic Hotel have luxury French furniture, dark wood floors, white linens and panoramic views of the city or Andes Mountains. The marble bathrooms are stocked with bathroom amenities and a hairdryer. Each room also has a work desk and 32” LCD cable TV.

Guests can enjoy Pilates and massage sessions at the health club.

El Plumerillo Airport is a 10-minute drive. Free private parking is included and car rental can be arranged directly with the hotel.
