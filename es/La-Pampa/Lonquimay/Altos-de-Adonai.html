Descripción

At the hotel, rooms include a desk. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a bidet and a hair dryer, while certain rooms have a seating area. All rooms come with a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

An Italian breakfast is available each morning at Altos de Adonai.

Staff are always available to help at the reception.

The nearest airport is Santa Rosa Airport, 37 miles from the property.
