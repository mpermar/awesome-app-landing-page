Descripción

Todas las habitaciones del complejo están equipadas con TV de pantalla plana. El Lala Mukha Tented Resort Khao Yai ofrece ciertos alojamientos con vistas a la montaña, y cada habitación tiene una tetera eléctrica. Todas las habitaciones incluyen zona de estar.

El establecimiento sirve un desayuno continental o americano.

Lala Mukha Tented Resort Khao Yai tiene una piscina al aire libre.

Asistencia las 24 horas está disponible en la recepción.

La mansión Prasenchit, Villa Musée, se encuentra a 4,3 km del complejo, mientras que Khao Yai Panorama Farm está a 6,6 km.

"I have not heard of the word “glamping” until I came across the Lala Mukha Tented Resort. It turned to mean “glamorous camping”. We had a deluxe tent which comes with its own attached bathroom. Some other tents in the resort do not have its own bathroom and the residents will visit the communal bathroom. We took a peek at that and found that it is super clean as well. We swam in the infinity pool and felt like we were in the middle of nature, surrounded by countless trees and the endless blue sky. Breakfast was buffet style. The receptionist, Saranya, is super nice and helpful. She spoke good English as well. Thumbs up for her!"
