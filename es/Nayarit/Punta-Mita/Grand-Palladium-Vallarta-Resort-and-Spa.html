Descripción

The air-conditioned rooms at the Grand Palladium Vallarta Resort & Spa feature free Wi-Fi, satellite TV and a private balcony or terrace. Each one comes with a coffee maker, and the private bathroom includes a hairdryer.

The resort has 5 restaurants, ranging from Asian cuisine to steakhouse food. Open-air themed dinners are held regularly. There are also 7 bars, as well as a nightclub.

The Grand Palladium’s spa includes a hot tub, sauna and steam baths. The hotel also organizes daily entertainment and activities for both adults and children.

There is a shopping center and a movie theater within a 5 minute drive of the hotel. Puerto Vallarta Airport is 12 miles away, and a shuttle service is available upon request.
