Descripción

Hard Rock Hotel Vallarta - All Inclusive features 2 beachfront pools with bar service. Guests can explore in a kayak, participate in a game of beach soccer, or workout in the fully equipped fitness center. After discovering nearby sites on a guided tour, adults can relax with a massage in the full-service spa, while the kids have fun in the kid's club.

The guest rooms at the All Inclusive Hard Rock Hotel Vallarta offer a fully stocked minibar, 24 hour room service and double hot tub. Guests will enjoy extras services such as purified water, nightly turndown service and upscale bath amenities.
