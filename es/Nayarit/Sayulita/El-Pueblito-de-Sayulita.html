Descripción

All rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Selected rooms will provide you with a kitchenette with a microwave, a toaster and a fridge. All guest rooms will provide guests with a private bathroom.

à la carte and American breakfast options are available every morning at El Pueblito de Sayulita.

Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Sayulita, like cycling.

Rincon de Guayabitos is 15 miles from El Pueblito de Sayulita, while Puerto Vallarta is 23 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Lic. Gustavo Diaz Ordaz Airport, 18 miles from the property.
