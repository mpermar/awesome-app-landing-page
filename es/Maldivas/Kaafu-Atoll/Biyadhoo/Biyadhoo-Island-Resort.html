Descripción

Biyadhoo Resort ofrece un transporte de ida y vuelta en lancha rápida desde el aeropuerto internacional de Malé, ubicado a 40 minutos.

Las habitaciones están amuebladas de forma sencilla y cuentan con muebles clásicos de madera y vistas a la vegetación circundante. Cuentan con aire acondicionado, caja fuerte y menaje para preparar té y café.

Los huéspedes pueden disfrutar de las tardes de windsurf y snorkel en las aguas cristalinas o relajarse con un masaje relajante. Después de un día de actividad, se pueden pasar momentos tranquilos en la biblioteca.

El restaurante The Palm, que ofrece un ambiente informal al aire libre, ofrece una selección de mariscos frescos y platos internacionales. Después de las comidas se sirven bebidas en el Coconut Bar.

22/03/2017

Childhood dream came true

"My husband and I were searching for a hotel on an island with a pretty reef, because we planned to go snorkelling several times per day, and it was always one of my greatest wishes to see this wonderful underwater world. In addition, we wanted no restrictions when it comes to bikinis or a cocktail, and also searched for a hotel not too posh. We found all that, and more, on Biyadhoo island and in the hotel with the same name.First, as already mentioned by many others, the "house reef" is amazing! The amount of fish living already in the lagoon, and then outside on the reef edge, strikes you. We saw many, many baby sharks, once their mom / dad, twice a giant moray, many octopus, a small ray, a turtle, and fish of all colours. We can say, we found Nemo ;)Second, the island itself is a dream. The hotel is perfectly included into nature, so that on arrival, you see almost nothing but green trees, bushes, white sands and turquoise waters. No "water bungalow line" that's both ugly and harming the environment (weakening the normal currents around the island on one side, enforcing them on the other, so that the reef suffers). No, just "wildness", with the 2 storey room buildings included in the small forest. Wonderful!Third, the hotel was even better than we had imagined. We thought it might be a little old and maybe run down, but we found it all spotlessly clean and well looked after. Bathroom might be a bit dated, but bed / mattress very comfortable. Room service was super efficient, people on reception and in the restaurant always really friendly. You feel really welcomed on Biyadhoo island! Thank you very much :)"

14/02/2016

Our criteria were: close to Male (because we spent most of...

"Our criteria were: close to Male (because we spent most of our trip on a local island several hours away by speedboat), relatively cheap (we're budget travellers at heart), with great snorkelling (so we didn't need to pay extra for boat trips). We found all that at Biyadhoo. The speedboat takes 40 mins max. The price, after taking tax/transfer into account, was the cheapest of several "budget" resorts I researched in the area - plus it included three meals a day whereas others included only breakfast. The island is lovely - it's covered in trees, with little paths down to the beautiful beach which stretches around ~3/4 of the island. Despite there being six blocks of accommodation on a small island there always seemed to be a quiet corner to relax in. The reef is great and you'll see something new every time you snorkel. It's true that the rooms are functional rather than fancy (the bathroom especially was a little tired, and the balcony is too small to really relax on - plus the thick foliage means no sea view). However they're clean and the beds are comfortable. There's some strange food in the dining room, but many delicious things too. This isn't the place to come if your Maldives dream demands luxury furnishings, complete seclusion and staff waiting on you hand and foot. However, if you just want to experience the beauty of Maldives with comfortable surroundings, I would recommend it. Friends/couples/families of many nationalities and ages were there when we stayed."

Niños y camas extra

Los niños mayores o adultos adicionales pagan 60 USD por noche en camas supletorias.
El número máximo de camas supletorias en una habitación es 1.
Cualquier tipo de cama supletoria está disponible bajo petición y debe ser confirmada por la gerencia.
Las tarifas adicionales no se calculan automáticamente en el costo total y deberán pagarse por separado durante su estadía.
