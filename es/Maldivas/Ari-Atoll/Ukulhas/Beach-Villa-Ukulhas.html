Descripción

Las habitaciones están equipadas con escritorio, TV de pantalla plana y baño privado. Cada habitación está equipada con una tetera eléctrica, mientras que algunas habitaciones también cuentan con balcón y otras también tienen vistas al mar. Todas las habitaciones del Beach Villa Ukulhas cuentan con aire acondicionado y armario.

Todos los días se sirve un desayuno continental en el establecimiento.

El alojamiento tiene una terraza.

La asistencia continua está disponible en la recepción.

El aeropuerto de Male está a 46 millas de distancia.

20/06/2018

A true Maldivian experience made wonderful by the family that runs this villa.

"I brought my 7-member family, ranging from 4 to 70 years old to Maldives for the first time.Our stay in Beach Villa Ukulhas was once in a lifetime. The villas were hand-built and are run entirely by the family giving such a sincere and personal touch which you cannot find on any resort. Practically, accommodation itself was extremely clean and comfortable and had all necessities such as good aircon, cable TV and wi-fi. The garden is a lovely place to relax and eat in. The beach is just 10 steps away and have baby reef (non-dangerous), hermit crabs and wonderful shells. A more sandy tourist/bikiini beach is 5 min away. With kids, must go in the morning before the tide comes in. I came during monsoon season, I think the waters are calmer and clearer in Dec/Jan.The beach villa is not a resort, but what compensates for this are the many activities available - the visit to picnic island, fishing and swimming with the manta rays are a must do. We did the first two twice!! The trip prices are VERY reasonable and Yako will take you personally. He even pulled my son in the lagooon so he could be fast enough to swim with the manta rays, and Hakim is an excellent fishing teacher. You will not be able to do daily trips like this, at this price, on a resort.The food cooked by the sisters in the family is very clean and tasty with a large menu variety. The fresh juices are an absolute must try! There are other restaurants on the island, but in the end, we ate mainly at the villa.Ukulhas is a beautiful island. The local people are very dedicated to the growth of their island and are very polite, and welcoming to tourists even though we can be very different. Everyone knows everyone - and if you need something like last minute souvenirs - they will call the shop owner to open up, and he will be there in 5 min! =)As we went there during Ramadan and Eid, we learnt a bit about Islam and were even given some celebration treats (very yummy)."
