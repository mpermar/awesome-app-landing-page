Descripción

Todas las habitaciones cuentan con minibar, caja fuerte, canales de alta definición, aire acondicionado y zona de estar. También hay un hervidor eléctrico con set de té y café. El baño privado incluye ducha de agua dulce fría / caliente, secador de pelo y artículos de aseo gratuitos.

El equipo de snorkel y la bicicleta están disponibles de forma gratuita. En Nala Veli Villa encontrará acceso gratuito a la zona de playa bikini con sombrillas y tumbonas. El establecimiento también ofrece instalaciones para practicar deportes acuáticos, visitas culturales gratuitas y un salón compartido. Se puede disfrutar de una colección de actividades en el lugar o en los alrededores, como barbacoa, pesca y buceo.

El Nala Veli Villa está a 71 km del aeropuerto internacional de Malé. Se puede acceder en un paseo en lancha rápida de 60 minutos.

Experiencias

08/01/2018

The stay was AMAZING.

"We have stayed at the Nala Veli Villa from 22nd till 31st of December 2017 and we had the most pleasant stay of all of our holiday's! The people of the guest house really made us feel at home and we still had all the privacy we wanted/needed. We could ask for anything we needed (extra towels, Snorkeling Gear, anti mosquito spray, beach beds, an umbrella and more) and were always helped with sincere kindness. Something you don't come across a lot anymore these days!The guest house and its rooms are new and and include air conditioning, ceiling fan, quality bed/mattress, spacious shower and Wi-Fi. We could get our breakfast when we woke up: different kind of Maldivian dishes or more Western breakfasts. I recommend the devilled sausages! You could have dinner in the garden or the air-conditioned restaurant room of Nala Veli. Or the people of the guest house would be so kind to show you the other 4 restaurants of Ukulhas, if you would like to. When we got out of the airplane at Male, we were welcomed by one of the Nala Veli Villa staff members and taken to the harbor. There is a 90 minutes speedboat going twice a day to Ukulhas. The island has some beautiful nature (palm, banana and Banyan trees) and a very nice beach with a lot of fish (butterfly, parrot, unicorn and puffer fish, starfish, lionfish, dolphins, manta rays and little baby black-tip sharks). The weather at the Maldives is around 30°C (86°F) and has a very nice tropical humidity. We had a delicious barbecue on the beach and a lovely time with the kind people of the Nala Veli Villa. We hope to come back to them one day! Thank you very much for making our holiday even more special!"
