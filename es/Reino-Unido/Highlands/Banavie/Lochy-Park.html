Brilliant location and stunning views of Ben Nevis

"This was a 3 bed log cabin which was large enough for 2 x couples and 2 children or a combination of. The only downside was the signal for the TV and the wifi were weak and often lost the signal. Not a major disappointment though as the scenery and walks around the park were stunning."
