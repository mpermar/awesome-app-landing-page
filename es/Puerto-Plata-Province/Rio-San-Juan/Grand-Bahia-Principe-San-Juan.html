Descripción

All guest rooms at the hotel come with a seating area and a flat-screen TV. Rooms contain a private bathroom with a shower.

A buffet breakfast is available every morning at Grand Bahia Principe San Juan.

Guests can use the hot tub and fitness center. A variety of popular activities are available in the area around the accommodation, including snorkeling, windsurfing and canoeing.

Gregorio Luperón International Airport is 29 miles from the property.
