Descripción

The rooms at the resort come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a bath. Some rooms come with a kitchen with a dishwasher. All guest rooms have a desk.

Ocean Village Deluxe Resort & Spa offers a continental or à la carte breakfast.

The accommodation has a playground.

Cabarete is 6 miles from Ocean Village Deluxe Resort & Spa, while Río San Juan is 29 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Gregorio Luperón International Airport, 4.3 miles from the property.
