Descripción

Todos los alojamientos cuentan con zona de estar, TV de pantalla plana y cocina totalmente equipada. Todos los alojamientos disponen de balcón con vistas al jardín.

El complejo turístico tiene un parque infantil.

Si desea descubrir la zona, puede practicar ciclismo, pesca y senderismo de inmediato.

Mount Gambier se encuentra a 43 km del parque de caravanas Millicent Hillview, mientras que Penola está a 48 km. El aeropuerto más cercano es el aeropuerto Mount Gambier, a 39 km del establecimiento.

Children and Extra Beds
All children are welcome.
One child under 6 years is charged AUD 15 per night when using existing beds.
Free!All children under 2 years stay free of charge in a crib.
All children under 6 years are charged AUD 15 per night for extra beds.
Any additional older children or adults are charged AUD 15 per night for extra beds.
The maximum number of extra beds in a room is 4.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
