Descripción

Todas las unidades cuentan con aire acondicionado y TV. También se ofrece un horno, un microondas y una tostadora, así como una tetera eléctrica.

Se puede practicar ciclismo en las inmediaciones.

El centro deportivo Kingborough se encuentra a 3,2 km del complejo turístico. El aeropuerto más cercano es el aeropuerto de Hobart, a 19 km del establecimiento.

17/02/2018

Recommended!

"Although the place was initially a little tricky to find, the fact that it was located close to a main highway meant that it was easy to get around into the city of Hobart, and also down to Bruny Island. We could hear a small amount of highway noise, but it is still a bush setting, with hills on most sides, and wallabies venturing close to the cabins at times. The cabins are fairly close to each other, but we didn't really hear noise from other occupants.The cabin is fairly compact, but we had six altogether (2 adults, 4 children), and it could house us fairly comfortably, with one room with a double bed, and another room with two bunk beds (a bit cosy, but it worked fine). The main bedroom has some space for storage like suitcases. Besides that there's a living/dining/kitchen area - all in one space, including a small round table (6 chairs can fit around it), a sink/cooking facilities, and a small 2 seater couch. The toilet and shower area is combined as one. The laundry is a separate building with washer/drier that is shared by all the cabins. Everything was neat and clean, and looked fairly modern. There was even an air conditioner and heater, plus a small TV with built-in DVD player.The location is convenient, with the highway nearby, and less than 10 minutes drive to grocery shops and more in Kingston. Overall a positive stay."
