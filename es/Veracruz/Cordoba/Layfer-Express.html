Descripción

Guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a coffee machine. At Layfer Express rooms contain a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

There's an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of Mexican dishes.

The property has a fitness center. You can play pool at the accommodation, and bike rental and car rental are available.

A business center and dry cleaning facilities are available at Layfer Express.

Orizaba is 9 miles from the hotel. The nearest airport is General Heriberto Jara Airport, 55 miles from Layfer Express.
