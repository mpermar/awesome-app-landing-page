Descripción

Este hotel boutique fue construido alrededor de una elegante villa reformada del siglo XIX. Se enfrenta al Palacio Presidencial y está al lado del hermoso Jardín Botánico de San Antón. Rodeado de hermosos jardines, el Corinthia Palace es un lugar tranquilo para un descanso relajante y uno de los principales hoteles de 5 estrellas de la isla.

Elija entre más de 50 tratamientos diferentes en el Atheneum Spa o simplemente relájese en la piscina. Comer aquí también es un verdadero placer. Disfrute de una amplia gama de cocina, desde italiana hasta oriental. Elija entre un servicio a la carta o un refrigerio ligero en el bar.

El Corinthia Palace Hotel & Spa cuenta con amplias habitaciones, todas con balcón privado y todas las comodidades modernas.

El equipo profesional de personal siempre está disponible y estará encantado de servirle.

Estás bien ubicado para explorar Malta. La ciudad amurallada de Medina está a solo 4 km del hotel, mientras que la popular localidad de St Julians está a 7 km.

El Corinthia Palace Hotel & Spa ofrece un servicio de transporte nocturno gratuito a San Julián, que le permite disfrutar de la vida nocturna allí y regresar a su alojamiento tranquilo con facilidad.

Rest and Relaxation in Spades

"Our room was quiet with crisp clean bed linen and towels. The bed was very very comfortable and the pillows excellent. As I have shoulder and neck problems I always dread hotel pillows and these were 10 out of 10. Breakfast was excellent and nothing too much trouble. My husband wanted kippers and he got kippers. I asked for Qmarat, a local sweet date pastry and they were made for me. Service with a smile.The hotel was quiet and surrounded by lovely gardens, not only their own but the botanical gardens of San Anton were just a two minute walk from the gate with free entry with unusual and interesting plants and trees to see. The gardens of Villa Bologna, which are being lovingly restored, are five minutes up the hill. There is an interesting pottery attached to the villa and entry is by donation.The villages of Attard and Balsan were within ten minutes walking distance and the buses to Valletta or Mdina were two minutes away. Although we did not use it the hotel runs a complimentary shuttle service into Valletta each day.There were plenty of seats and loungers by the pools, but don't be fooled by the azure pools, the outdoor ones are cold! but the spa was relaxing and the indoor pool inviting.I would thoroughly recommend The Corinthia Palace and we had a wonderful few days of rest and relaxation before we joined our children and grandchildren in Sliema!!!! shold have been the other way round! As we visit Malta frequently, our city is twinned with Valletta, we would return here."
