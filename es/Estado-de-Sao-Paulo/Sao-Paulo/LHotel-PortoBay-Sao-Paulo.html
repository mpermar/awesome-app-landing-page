Descripción

Las habitaciones de L'Hotel PortoBay São Paulo son elegantes e incluyen WiFi gratuita, TV por cable, escritorio y baño privado con artículos de aseo Malin + Goetz. Algunas tienen una zona de estar amplia.

El PortoBay alberga el restaurante Trebbiano, que ofrece especialidades italianas y mediterráneas. Además, Il Piano Bar sirve cócteles y aperitivos. El spa dispone de piscina cubierta y servicio de masajes.

L'Hotel PortoBay se encuentra a media manzana de la Avenida Paulista y cerca de restaurantes, bares, museos y tiendas. También se halla a 2,5 km de Liberdade y a 10 km del aeropuerto de Congonhas.

Experiencias

13/03/2017

Perfect stay!

"It really doesn't get much better than this! We loved this hotel. Firstly, it has a really good, but quiet, location, just off Avenida Paulista. The airport shuttle bus stops 50m away, just round the corner (outside the Maksoud Hotel) and there are two underground stations close by - Trianon/Masp and Brigadeiro (Green line 2). The room was lovely and large, with all the facilities you could require (minibar, safe, etc.), a very comfortable bed with the most wonderfully smooth sheets ever, and a decent sized bathroom with a marble bath and nice toiletries. Breakfast was fantastic, with numerous different choices, including a delicious fruit selection and egg dishes made to order, and you could eat outside on the terrace in the sun. Staff were, without exception, very friendly and helpful. The gym was small, but adequate, and the sauna and wooden Jacuzzis perfect, although the tiny pool was more for children to play in then for any serious swimming. Finally, there were all the nice little touches, like being able to check in early, finding little chocolates in the room, and being given water on arrival and departure. Would definitely stay there again."
