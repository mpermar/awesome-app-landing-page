Experiencias

31/12/2017

Monkey Bungalows are a nice little collection of bamboo & concrete bungalows next to the beach.

"The location was great - right on Khlong Kong beach , which was full of restaurants and bars, but not too crowded in the evenings. There's a few other restos, convenience stores, travel agents on the main road next to the bungalows. Quiet at night, even if close to the beach bar, it's not that loud. Not a party place at all, friendly for travellers and families."
