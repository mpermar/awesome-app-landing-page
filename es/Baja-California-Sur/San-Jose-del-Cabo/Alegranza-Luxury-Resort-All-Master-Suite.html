Descripción

Each suite and apartment has a full kitchen, balcony, and flat-screen TV. Select apartments have a hot tub on a rooftop terrace.

Restaurant Casianos is available on site with one of the best chefs in town for breakfast, lunch or dinner. This San José del Cabo Alegranza Luxury Resort also offers the Palapa Bar with outdoor barbecue facilities.

A tennis court and underground parking are available. Tour information is available as well.

Guests are a a 15-minute drive from the Los Cabos International Airport. The property is just under 1 miles from San Jose Estuary, about a mile from Club Campestre, and 28 miles from Cabo Pulmo National Park.
