Descripción

Each spacious accommodations will provide a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kitchenette area, sitting area, and a balcony. Free toiletries will be available in the bathroom with shower.

Hacienda del March Vacation Club boasts an on-site restaurant. The property also has a fitness center for guests to use.

Guests can practice golf at the surrounding course, or they could ask the property’s friendly staff for information about sports fishing and horseback riding.

Los Cabos International Airport is located a 30-minute drive from Hacienda del March, and Cabo San Lucas is a 15-minute drive away.
