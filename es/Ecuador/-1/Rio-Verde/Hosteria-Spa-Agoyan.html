Descripción

At the hotel, every room comes with a closet. Hosteria Spa Agoyan has certain accommodations with mountain views, and rooms are equipped with a patio. All rooms feature a TV with satellite channels.

Continental and American breakfast options are available each morning at the accommodation. There's an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of local dishes and also offers vegetarian options.

The property has a hot tub and a sauna. You can play ping pong at Hosteria Spa Agoyan, and the area is popular for hiking.

The hotel can conveniently provide information at the reception to help guests to get around the area.

Baños is 9 miles from Hosteria Spa Agoyan.
