Descripción

At the hotel, all rooms have a closet. With a private bathroom equipped with a bath, rooms at Playa Aventura Hotel also offer free WiFi, while some rooms are equipped with a garden view. At the accommodation rooms contain air conditioning and a flat-screen TV.

A à la carte breakfast is served each morning at the property.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental is available at Playa Aventura Hotel.

Staff at the reception can assist guests at any time of the day.

Salinas is 22 miles from the hotel, while Montañita is 10 miles from the property.
