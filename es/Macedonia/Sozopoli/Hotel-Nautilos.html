Descripción

Todos los estudios y apartamentos de Nautilos se abren a un balcón o patio con aire acondicionado y calefacción. Cada uno está equipado con una cocina americana con nevera o cocina totalmente equipada, teléfono y TV de pantalla plana. El baño privado incluye ducha y artículos de aseo gratuitos.

Los servicios del hotel incluyen una recepción de 16 horas, asistencia telefónica las 24 horas y transporte de equipaje. Rieles de cama de protección infantil se pueden proporcionar a petición.

Los huéspedes encontrarán restaurantes, bares y supermercados a pocos pasos del Hotel Nautilos. El centro de Salónica se encuentra a 50 km, mientras que el aeropuerto internacional de Salónica está a 39 km. El establecimiento dispone de aparcamiento público gratuito.

The place - Sozopoli is a village in Halkidiki, very closed...

"The place - Sozopoli is a village in Halkidiki, very closed to Thessalonik (47 km), Nea Moudania (14km), and many other sea villages (eg. Nea Plagia, Nea Flogita, etc). Sozopoli is a traditional greek village, beautiful, quiet, with olives trees around, with Sunday morning church ceremony, with many mobile marketers, who sell figs, apples, plums, watermelones (all of them very fresh and tasty).The beach and the sea - the beach is generous, long, with sand and small and coloured stones, with umbrellas and sunbeds in front of Nautilos hotel. The sea is very beautiful, very clean, and, even there is a small line with stones, (but without see plants), after 5-6 meters has only sand, is not so deep, you can stand, play with children or swim until buoy. On the beach you can order different kind of coffee, juice, cocktails and something to eat at nirmal price and important, for Nautilos hotel guests it is not mandatory to order something if you want to use the sunbeds and umbrellas.The hotel - Nautilos hotel is located on the beach. Very easy to find it (there are severals street signs with the hotel names). The hotel has spacious rooms for smaller or larger families, with balcony, with sea side view and room facilities for self catering (fridge, stove, glasses, cups, plates, etc). The rooms have AC (each room has AC), TV, there are towels in the rooms and very clean bed' sheets (there are locations in Greece (I had this bad chance) without towels or with very bad sheets). And there is a swimming poll! (Unusual for Greek hotels located on the beach).The hotel has a taverna where you can find a lot of very tasty food and, very closed to hotel is a market, a bakery and a butchery with fresh products.The owner/s of the hotel is/are very friendly, try to solve any possible problem/issue you have (we had a problem with an light bulb and Roula, the owner/manager of the hotel, help us imediately), we offered us free deserts at every our meal at taverna."
