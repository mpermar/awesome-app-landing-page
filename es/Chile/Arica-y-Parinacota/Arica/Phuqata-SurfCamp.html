Descripción

At the bed and breakfast, each room comes with a closet. Every room includes a shared bathroom, while some feature a sea view.

The daily breakfast offers continental and buffet options. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the in-house restaurant, which serves Peruvian cuisine and also offers vegetarian options.

Phuqata SurfCamp has a year-round outdoor pool. You can play ping pong at the accommodation, and the area is popular for cycling.

The reception at Phuqata SurfCamp can provide tips on the area.

Culture House of Arica is 6 miles from the bed and breakfast. Chacalluta International Airport is 3.7 miles from the property.
