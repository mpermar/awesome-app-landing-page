Descripción

This apartment includes 1 bedroom, a living room and a flat-screen TV, an equipped kitchen with a dining area, and a bathroom with a bath.

When guests need guidance on where to visit, the reception will be happy to provide advice.

The apartment has an outdoor pool.

Station Arica-La Paz is half a mile from Departamento Arica City Center. Chacalluta International Airport is 9 miles away.
