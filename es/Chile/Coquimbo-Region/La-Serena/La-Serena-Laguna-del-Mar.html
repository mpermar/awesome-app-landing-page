Descripción

The apartment features 2 bedrooms, a living room, a dining area and a well-appointed kitchen. Featuring a bath and a washing machine, the bathroom also comes with free toiletries and a hair dryer

Around-the-clock advice is available at the reception, where staff speak Spanish and English.

The apartment has an outdoor pool. A grill and a playground are available for guests at La Serena Laguna del Mar to use.

Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Japanese GPark Kokoro No Niwa, Gabriela Mistral Building and President Gabriel González Videla Regional History Museum. The nearest airport is La Florida Airport, 4.3 miles from the property.
