Descripción

The Ecoreserva Quelhue Lodge y Cabañas is 3 miles from Caburgua Beach. Bike rental, mountain bike, hiking and fishing trips can be arranged through the tour desk.

The cabins at Quelhue have kitchen facilities, seating and dining areas. All of them have spacious balconies and some of them have views of the organic garden, the mountains and the volcano.

The cabins are 6 miles from Pucon’s bus station and 4 miles from Pucon’s air field. The nearest ski center is 6 miles away. Private parking is free.
