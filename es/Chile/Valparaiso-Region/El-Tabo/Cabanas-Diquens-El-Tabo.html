Descripción

Fitted with a terrace, the units feature a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with shower. Some units feature a dining area and/or a patio.

Cabañas Diquen's El Tabo has a grill.

Guests can relax in the garden at the property.

Viña del Mar is 31 miles from Cabañas Diquen's El Tabo, while Valparaíso is 29 miles away. The nearest airport is Arturo Merino Benitez Airport, 50 miles from Cabañas Diquen's El Tabo.
