Descripción

Los estudios del Montauk Blue Hotel disponen de conexión Wi-Fi gratuita, TV por cable, microondas y nevera. Los huéspedes pueden relajarse en la sala de estar con un sofá cama o pasar tiempo en el patio privado.

Se proporcionan instalaciones de barbacoa para los huéspedes que deseen cocinar al lado de la playa. Las máquinas expendedoras también están disponibles para aperitivos y bebidas.

Montauk Downs y el aeropuerto de Montauk están a menos de 8 km del Montauk Blue Hotel. El faro y museo de Montauk Point se encuentra a 7 minutos en coche.

Montauk Blue-a good place for families at a great location

"The Montauk Blue is right on the ocean. Easter weekend was a full house. We were on an anniversary get-away and checked in on Wednesday and left on Saturday. It is an older wooden type motel that stretches along the beach front with a wooden decking on the first floor and a balcony for the 2nd floor rooms. We requested and received a 2nd floor room right at the end of the main building. The decor was shades of blue and white and the bedding was soft and the king bed very comfortable! Considering it was a full house with lots of family groups, it didn't feel too noisy or crowded. It is a great place to go with young kids especially if you have a first floor room-kids were having a good time playing in the sand when it was warm enough. The deck was wider in front of the smaller section of the motel and had nice lounge chairs we used in the afternoon to read and relax. There was a pool in a separate glass enclosure and that was very active with lots of kids-we didn't use the pool so can't comment on that. There were vending machines for drinks and an ice machine outside the pool area with a few lounge chairs and umbrella tables in a very protected area that as long as the sun was out would be pretty warm.Back to the room-It was great to leave the slider door open to hear the waves and get the salt breeze. There was a Keurig coffee machine with 2 capsules and 2 Styrafoam cups and there was a microwave and a very small fridge inside a cabinet. (There was coffee in the office if you wanted more.) Our shower had good water pressure and the water was nice and hot."
