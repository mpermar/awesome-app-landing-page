Descripción

Fully carpeted, the rooms at Los Pinos Resort feature air conditioning, a fan and heating. All of them have garden views and marble bathrooms.

All meals and beverages are included in the rates. A buffet breakfast with pastries, natural juice, scramble eggs and cold meats is served daily. There are 2 restaurants that serve regional and international dishes. They are open from 1 pm to 3 pm and from 9 pm to 11 pm. Snacks and drinks can be enjoyed at the 2 bars.

Guests can enjoy beauty treatments at the spa, or relax on the lounge chairs by the pool. They can also engage in hydro-gym and yoga lessons, or play football, tennis and bocce. Spa facilities include Turkish baths and sauna. Hiking and biking tours are arranged on a regular basis.

Free parking is available. Los Pinos Resort & Spa Termal is just over a quarter mile from the bus terminal and 50 miles from Tucuman Airport. Transportation is provided to and from Termas de Rio Hondo bus station and Teniente General Benjamín Matienzo International Airport, in San Miguel de Tucumán. An airport shuttle to Vicecomodoro Ángel de la Paz Aragonés Airport in Santiago del Estero can be arranged for a fee.
