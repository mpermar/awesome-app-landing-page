Descripción

Todas las habitaciones del Burghotel Nürnberg, de gestión privada, incluyen baño privado y algunas ofrecen conexión inalámbrica a internet (de pago).

Además de la piscina cubierta, el Burghotel Nürnberg ofrece sauna y solárium.

Se puede llegar al Messe (centro de exposiciones) en unos 20 minutos en transporte público. Las paradas de autobús más cercanas se encuentran en la plaza Hauptmarkt (a 400 metros).

Mitte es una gran opción para los viajeros interesados en museos, paseos por la ciudad y arquitectura.

02/01/2017

perfect location in the old town

"The location of this hotel was superb - it is located in the old town, close to the city walls, and just a short distance to the main old square. We were here for the Christmas Markets, and it was just a 5 minute walk from the hotel to everything in town. We found a perfect little restaurant just around the corner. We were in town for 3 nights and after discovering it the first night, couldn't find anywhere else in the local vicinity that offered the same selection (with great prices and portion size!) - so ended up going back every day! We didn't try the hotel breakfast so can't comment on that. We found a supermarket (a bit like an Aldi) in the market square, and bought foods to have in our room - very handy that the room came with a mini fridge (stocked bar).The Sauna was a great relief from the cold - and had decent opening hours that actually fitted customer needs (unlike some other hotels that close early!). I don't think the pool was warm enough for a dip/lounge about, and not really big enough to do lengths to work up a bit of a sweat - but if you have kids this would be perfect. I stuck to the sauna. I just wish there was a jacuzzi/spa too!"
