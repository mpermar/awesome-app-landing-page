Descripción

Las habitaciones están equipadas con TV de pantalla plana con canales por cable, tetera eléctrica, bidé, artículos de aseo gratuitos y armario. Cada habitación cuenta con baño privado con secador de pelo, mientras que algunas habitaciones tienen terraza.

Todos los días se sirve un desayuno continental o a la carta en el establecimiento. El restaurante del hotel ofrece varias opciones gastronómicas, como cocina francesa y asiática.

El hotel tiene una piscina al aire libre. El ciclismo es una de las actividades que los huéspedes pueden disfrutar cerca de Airport Eco Chalets.

El personal está disponible para ayudar en la recepción las 24 horas.

Colombo se encuentra a 17 km del alojamiento, mientras que Negombo está a 16 km. El aeropuerto más cercano es el aeropuerto internacional de Bandaranaike, a 9,6 km del Airport Eco Chalets.

Excellent

"Yoho Eco resort is the perfect place if you had an overnight flight to Sri lanka. It is just a few kms away from the airport so if you have plans to go to places like Nuwara Eliya or Kandy later, you can stay for a day here before proceeding further. Colombo is a bit far though and has traffic issues so i suggest you enjoy your stay at Eco resort and then proceed to other places.Mr Mahesh is very accommodative and he even arranged a van for us (choose a van over a car if you have kids) to go to NE, Kandy and Bentota. The driver Kasun was a great guy and drove very safely over the next seven days of our journey. The cost that Mr Mahesh provided was also very reasonable. The only caution to travelers though is that Srilankan roads are narrow and they have made sure not to let urbanisation destroy their environment so if google says 3 hours to reach a destination, add two more hours to it and plan accordingly since the terrain is quite hilly overall.Even during our return journey from Bentota, since we had a few hours before our flight he was kind enough to let us stay for a couple of hours at his place.The only suggestion is to improve the breakfast options, and tie up with a few restaurants if possible to ease breakfast accessibility. The property is new so Mr Mahesh is very welcoming to feedback aimed at improving the customer experience in whatever way possible. Mr Mahesh went out of his way to providing us Indian food but we suggest they have a tie up so that it is easy to manage."
