Descripción

En la cama y desayuno, todas las habitaciones incluyen un escritorio. El baño privado está equipado con bidet, artículos de aseo gratuitos y secador de pelo. Las habitaciones de la Villa Elite cuentan con aire acondicionado y armario.

Todas las mañanas se sirve un desayuno bufé en el establecimiento.

Agrigento se encuentra a 6 km del alojamiento, mientras que Caltanissetta está a 48 km.

Políticas de Alojamiento

Children and Extra Beds
All children are welcome.
Up to two children under 12 years are charged EUR 15 per night when using existing beds.
Free!All children under 2 years stay free of charge in a crib.
All children under 12 years are charged EUR 15 per night for extra beds.
Any additional older children or adults are charged EUR 20 per night for extra beds.
The maximum number of extra beds in a room is 2.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
