Descripción

El hotel de 4 estrellas se compone de 3 villas, cada una diseñada para ofrecer entradas independientes, privacidad y un alto nivel de confort. Una unión armoniosa entre tradición e innovación, toda la estructura está hecha de madera, piedras, granito y cerámica para reflejar las tradiciones locales.

Situado estratégicamente cerca de muchas carreteras principales, el hotel es la base ideal para explorar esta maravillosa región; Está a una milla de la ciudad de Arzachena, a 3 millas del mar en Cannigione, a 9 millas de la famosa localidad turística de Porto Cervo y a solo 15 minutos en coche de las hermosas playas de la Costa Smeralda.

Experiencias

23/08/2017

Relaxing, fabulous staff and a cracking pool.

"The various reviews we'd read have the Hotel Parco Degli Ulivi spot on - the staff at the Ulivi can't do enough to make your stay perfect. The team at the office will give you a great map and point out the best nearby beaches. They arranged a skippered sailing trip to the nearby islands and will happily book you on similar day trips or book you your own powered boat / rib. We even had a braces emergency with our 11 year old and they booked us into an orthodental hospital down the road within minutes. It's a beautiful space. The rooms are arranged in villas around the park with their own outside space. The cleaning staff tackle them every day as if it the final clean for a long term villa rental. Spotless is an understatement.The pool is in a great setting. There were always enough loungers / umbrellas despite the hotel being full and it being high season - unlike other hotels in Sardinia which we stayed at on this trip there was no culture of nabbing the loungers early and the hotel supplies the free beach towels. There is a shallow pool for little kids / the very lazy, a jacuzzi pool and a lovely deep swimming pool. We were there in the middle of August during the heatwave - the gentle breeze coming up the valley to the pool area was a godsend. We'd taken the half board option - our 14 and 11 year old enjoyed taking on new food combinations - we were there for over a week and we saw no repeats on the menu. A perfect place for either a short relaxing stay or a longer one as a base for exploring the north of the island. We will definitely be going back."

18/07/2017

Serenity

"The property is amazing in the middle of the nature nearby Arzachena. We have traveled with a 5 years boy and we had a suite. Nothing to complain about the suite, very nice, the bed was comfy, both rooms of the suite quite big and designed traditionally. The suite also has two terraces. The restaurant is good, breakfast of good quality, serving very good food and good coffee, espresso or cappuccino at the table on request. The pool area is very nice and relaxing. They also have a small pool for kids and a jacuzzi. The entire property is a beautiful garden with old olive trees, aromatic herbs, oleanders, flowers. The nearest beaches are at 10 minutes drive in LaConnia or in Cannigione. For a peaceful vacation I would definitely recommend the property. It is a small piece of heaven."
