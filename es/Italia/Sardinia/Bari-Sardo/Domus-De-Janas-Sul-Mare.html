Descripción

Las habitaciones van desde apartamentos hasta habitaciones en el último piso con excelentes vistas. También hay disponibles habitaciones dobles estándar más grandes y apartamentos tipo estudio.

El restaurante del hotel ofrece cocina sarda e internacional. El desayuno es estilo buffet.

El puerto de Arbatax se encuentra a 18 km.

Lovely unique hotel with great in-house restaurant and beach across the (quiet) street.

"Cute hotel with convenient in-house dining. The rooms are really cool - with all kinds of twists and turns (appreciated the fridge!), and balcony and rooftop seating from which the beach and the nearby medieval tower can be seen. I loved the breakfast and the in-house restaurant, not only was it convenient, the food was very tasty and the service was too. The receptionist checked a few times that my connection was sufficient, and after the desk closed (my meeting was scheduled for US time) - she quietly closed up and I finished our online meeting. Folks at this hotel seemed to really enjoy it as well - I saw quite a few families and larger groups. It's a quiet place in a fairly undisturbed lovely area of Sardinia."

Políticas de Alojamiento

Children and Extra Beds
All children are welcome.
One child under 6 years is charged EUR 7 per night when using existing beds.
All children under 2 years are charged EUR 20 per night in a crib.
All children under 12 years are charged EUR 23 per night for extra beds.
Any additional older children or adults are charged EUR 23 per night for extra beds.
The maximum number of extra beds in a room is 1.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
