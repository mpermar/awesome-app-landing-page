Descripción

El Hotel Hilburger alberga un restaurante que sirve cocina regional y nacional elaborada con productos locales frescos. El desayuno bufé incluye zumo de manzana casero.

Las habitaciones del establecimiento cuentan con TV de pantalla plana vía satélite y baño privado. Hay WiFi gratuita en todo el establecimiento.

El Hilburger está a solo 35 minutos en coche de Bolzano y a 10 minutos en coche de Merano.

Políticas de Alojamiento

Children and Extra Beds
All children are welcome.
All children under 2 years are charged EUR 10 per night in a crib.
All children under 16 years are charged EUR 35 per night for extra beds.
Any additional older children or adults are charged EUR 35 per night for extra beds.
The maximum number of extra beds in a room is 1.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
