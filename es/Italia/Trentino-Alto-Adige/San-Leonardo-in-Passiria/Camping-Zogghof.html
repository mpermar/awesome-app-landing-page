Descripción

Todos los alojamientos disponen de terraza con vistas a la montaña, cocina con nevera y fogones y baño privado.

Todas las mañanas se sirve un desayuno continental en el establecimiento.

Camping Zögghof tiene un parque infantil.

Los huéspedes también pueden relajarse en el salón compartido.

Merano se encuentra a 18 km del Camping Zögghof, mientras que Bolzano está a 37 km.

29/01/2018

Lovely clean fresh apartment with great view

"Perfect accommodation for a short stay in Winter (trocken Raum available) or a longer summer stay. Bedrooms are not very spacious but will do. If you can live outside, like in Summer time, it will feel better I reckon. Appartement was super clean and fresh. With more then enough towels. Bed is relatively hard, but very proper. Not like your cheap camping mattress but a proper thick one. Extra facilities are available like washing room, breakfast Services (good Italian coffee and Austrian style bread, so more then just a cracker and jam but croissants, ‘semmel brötchen’ and more) dinner in the nearby (walking distance) village is cheap and enough choice. Heating can be adjusted in every room and the blinds make the room properly dark. Dogs are allowed (when well trained and house trained, behaving well, so no barking, no biting of improper behaviour towards other guests, no marking inside the apartment etc!)Bunk bed fits adults as well as children. Bathroom is clean and has enough space in the shower. Owner is very friendly and helpful. And took his time to explain all we wanted to know. Especially for the price this is really, really good."
