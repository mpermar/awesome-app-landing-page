Descripción

Puede caminar hasta Tremezzo en unos 15 minutos o tomar el autobús de enlace programado del hotel. El Hotel La Perla ofrece aparcamiento gratuito.

La mayoría de las habitaciones de La Perla tienen su propio balcón y excelentes vistas. Todas tienen TV de pantalla plana vía satélite y aire acondicionado.

Este tranquilo hotel es de gestión familiar y tiene un servicio amable. Hay una sala de televisión, un punto de internet y un bar con terraza.

12/09/2017

This is a family-run hotel with 20 rooms, outdoor swimming...

"This is a family-run hotel with 20 rooms, outdoor swimming pool, terrace with lake view and small well-groomed territory with olive trees. Five family members (two twin sisters with husbands and a daughter of one of them) work together on a daily basis serving breakfast, providing any info needed at the reception desk, paying attention to your requests. The way they do it is impressive: at least one sister we had a conversation with spoke Italian, French, German, English!!! That was impressive! They try to speak your native language accompanying the conversation with “Perfecto! Prego! Grazie!” in such a natural way that it makes you feel extremely welcome at this hotel from the first minutes you arrived. The view from the terrace overlooking the lake is fantastic! The breakfast is Italian (croissant, coffee, fruits, yogurts, cheese, ham) and everything was fresh and tasty. You can also order the dinner in advance (salads, pasta, etc). Unfortunately, there was a rainy weather during our stay, which did not allow us to swim in the pool and have a long walks around, nevertheless, we just relaxed in our cozy room or on the terrace. We have perfect memories and definitely would recommend this hotel!One more point to mention, the hotel is situated on the hill, therefore it might be difficult to go up with the luggage or children, therefore plan/discuss your arrival with the owners, who can transfer you (usually it is 2 times per day) from the boat station in Tremezzo to the hotel. It helped us a lot, taking into consideration that we came during the pouring rain."
