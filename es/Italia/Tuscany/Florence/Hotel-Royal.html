Descripción

Esta espléndida villa florentina fue construida en 1700. Fue una residencia familiar hasta 1950, cuando se convirtió en un elegante hotel. Se encuentra en un barrio verde de Florencia, al lado del centro de exposiciones Fortezza da Basso y su parque.

Cada habitación del Royal tiene aire acondicionado. Las habitaciones y las zonas comunes están decoradas con buen gusto con muebles antiguos.

Este hotel de gestión familiar garantiza un ambiente agradable y relajado y un servicio atento. La piscina es ideal para darse un refrescante chapuzón después de un día de turismo por Florencia.

El estacionamiento y la piscina están disponibles por un costo adicional.

Lovely hotel. Time travel included.

"I don't usually write reviews, but I wanted to clarify a few things. Some of the things I read in other reviews are simply not true:- WiFi is free (I read in reviews it was not)- Use of the pool is €3 per day and kids are free (I read €8 in reviews here)- The staff are very helpful and kindThis is a hotel the way hotels used to be decades ago. No keycards, but a board of dangling large keys behind the front desk. Your file and invoice are not kept in a computer, but in a giant book that sits on the front desk. If you want to go for a swim, the staff write a hand-written note and put the note in the giant book with the rest of your information. The hotel itself also seems like something from a different time and place. The gravel courtyard in front makes it look more like a place you'd find in the countryside rather than right in the middle of one of the biggest tourist hotspots in Europe. The decor is whatever the opposite is of minimalistic. The cleaning ladies wear old fashioned maid costumes. The back courtyard is a beautiful spot to relax after a busy day in the city, as is the pool. The room that we stayed in was on the 2nd floor and not the most modern affair, but everything worked and was comfortable. Due to the comfy bed we overslept and missed breakfast (ended at 10). When I was checking out and asked if there was a place for me to buy a coffee, the guy arranged that we could still have breakfast, even though they had already started to clear the tables and everything. It was a nice breakfast as well, with lots of cakes, yoghurt, fruit, and good coffee. If you're looking for sleek and modern, go elsewhere, if you want old fashioned (but slightly dated) charm, this is a good place for you."
