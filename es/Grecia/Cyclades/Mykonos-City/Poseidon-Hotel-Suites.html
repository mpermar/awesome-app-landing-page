Descripción

Cada una de las habitaciones de las Cícladas se abre a un balcón privado con vistas a la ciudad de Mykonos, el mar y Delos. Una nevera, TV vía satélite y caja fuerte son estándar.

Se ofrece servicio de transporte gratuito de ida y vuelta desde el aeropuerto o el puerto. Poseidon ofrece conexión inalámbrica a internet gratuita en las zonas públicas, y el aparcamiento también es gratuito. Los huéspedes pueden alquilar el yate a motor de Poseidón y explorar las magníficas playas de Mykonos.

En la sala de desayunos y su patio fresco, los huéspedes pueden degustar delicias locales caseras, fruta fresca y café de buena calidad. El Alley Bay sirve cócteles exclusivos, a pocos pasos del Poseidón.

El Poseidón está a solo 200 metros de los famosos molinos de viento de Mykonos. Justo al lado del hotel, los huéspedes encontrarán pequeñas calas de arena y un camino que conduce a la pintoresca capilla de Agios Charalambis.

Experiencias

19/10/2017

A great location which I'd recommend.

"Amazing staff, always polite and efficient. Great hotel, just two minutes walk into the town centre. the beauty is, you can't hear any noise from the town when in the hotel. The pool is large with plenty of sun loungers. The poolside bar is managed by Thatos, who adds an extra vibe to the venue. The poolside bar provides great food at good prices. Restaurant food is extremely expensive and you can expect to pay at least £60 for a meal and house wine. More if you add starters and better quality wines. There are places that serve giros which are like wraps with meat and salad; we didn't try them but these establishments were always busy. The breakfast in the hotel was superb. Such a range of items to select from; more than enough to suit every taste. Our room was huge and had a large balcony overlooking the sea (room 7). From here, we were treated to amazing sun sets almost every night. Quite spectacular. I would recommend this hotel for couples or groups as it's not child friendly. Lots of steep stairs throughout the property. I would definitely stay again if Mykonos was not so expensive!"

19/05/2017

Lovely hotel in great location

"We were upgraded to a two bed suite on the second floor which was spacious and well equipped with great views over a sandy, sheltered, beach. The hotel has sun beds on its terrace just next to the beach and they serve drinks and cocktails all day. Breakfasts were OK, choice a bit limited and the same each day. The beds were extremely comfortable, bathrooms modern and fresh and the aircon worked well. The walk into the main town took around 15 minutes. Overall a great location for couples or families."
