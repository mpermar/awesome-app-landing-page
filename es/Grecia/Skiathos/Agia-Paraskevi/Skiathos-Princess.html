Descripción

Las elegantes habitaciones incluyen un balcón con impresionantes vistas al mar o al jardín. Las comodidades privadas incluyen artículos de tocador Apivita, albornoces, zapatillas y secador de pelo. Servicio de habitaciones disponible las 24 horas.

El Harmony Club Spa ofrece tratamientos naturales y diversas instalaciones, incluido un baño de lluvia tropical. Los más pequeños de Paraskevi pueden disfrutar en el parque infantil o en el club infantil.

El restaurante principal sirve ricos buffets para el desayuno y la cena. Hay 3 restaurantes temáticos individuales con cocina en vivo en el lugar, así como 3 bares.

El Skiathos Princess Hotel, de 5 estrellas, se encuentra a 6,4 km del puerto y el aeropuerto de Skiathos. Aparcamiento gratuito disponible en el sitio.

Ammos taverna offered a wide selection of meals and drinks. We didn't try the dinner buffet so can't comment on that. Breakfast was an 11/10 with pretty much anything you could want. Crepes, waffles, omelette made to order and your standard cereals, eggs, cold meats and fruits.The best part of this hotel is the staff (especially after the horrible experience we had at the hotel we had come from). Special shout out also to Eva (hostess) and Dimitri (barman) are the pool bar who were excellent with the kids - poor Dimitri was worn out with all the high 5s he had to give our very demanding 1.5 year old, Also Nadia the spa manager who was very attentive and friendly. There is a supermarker located directly across the road from the hotel for bits and pieces. There is also a small περίπτερο on the Agia Paraskevi beach.
