Descripción

All rooms in the hotel are equipped with a TV with satellite channels. The rooms come with air conditioning, and certain units at Hotel Club Acuario have a balcony. Guest rooms have a private bathroom and a hair dryer.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning at the property.

The nearest airport is José Martí International Airport, 9 miles from the property.
