Descripción

At the hotel, every room includes a desk. Each room is equipped with a private bathroom with free toiletries, while some have pool views. Guest rooms include a closet.

An American breakfast is served every morning at the property. There's an in-house restaurant, which serves international cuisine and also offers vegetarian and dairy-free options.

Hotel Boutique Casa Farallones offers a business center for guest use. Speaking English, Spanish and Portuguese at the reception, staff are ready to help around the clock.

Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Jorge Isaacs Theater, Saint's Peter Cathedral and La Ermita Church. The nearest airport is Alfonso Bonilla Aragón International Airport, 12 miles from the property.
