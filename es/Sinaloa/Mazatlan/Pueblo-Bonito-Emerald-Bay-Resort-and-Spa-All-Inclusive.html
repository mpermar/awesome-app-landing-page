Descripción

Pueblo Bonito Emerald Bay features a kitchenette and spacious bathroom in every spacious suite. Guests can also enjoy the private balcony.

Recreational facilities at the Emerald Bay Pueblo Bonito include hot tubs, a gym and children's pool area. Free shuttle service is also available to Pueblo Bonito Mazatlán. Complete concierge service is available.

La Cordelier restaurant has elegant dining and theme nights. The Pueblo Bonito also has the bistro-style Emerald Grill. Two swim-up bars and 2 lounges are also available.
