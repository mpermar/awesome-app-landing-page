Descripción

Sunscape Dorado Pacífico Beach Resort has 6 restaurants, serving a range of food including Italian, Mexican and International food. There are 4 bars, one of which is a swim-up bar.

The hotel’s spa features a fitness center and a range of treatments. There is also a tennis court and a table tennis table. The hotel provides an entertainment program for children.

The rooms at the Sunscape Dorado Pacífico feature tiled floors and blue and white décor. Each one comes with flat-screen satellite TV and a coffee-maker.
