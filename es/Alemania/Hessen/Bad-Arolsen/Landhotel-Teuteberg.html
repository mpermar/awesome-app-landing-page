Descripción

Todas las habitaciones del Landhotel, de gestión familiar, tienen canales de TV vía satélite y baño privado.

La zona de spa del Landhotel incluye sauna finlandesa, cabina de infrarrojos, baño turco y solárium.

En el restaurante tradicional del Landhotel se sirve comida alemana y regional. Todas las mañanas se sirve un desayuno bufé.

Otras instalaciones que ofrece el Landhotel Teuteberg incluyen plazas de aparcamiento gratuitas y un parque infantil.

Children and Extra Beds
All children are welcome.
Free!All children under 3 years stay free of charge when using existing beds.
Free!All children under 2 years stay free of charge in a crib.
All older children or adults are charged EUR 20 per night for extra beds.
The maximum number of extra beds in a room is 1.
The maximum number of cribs in a room is 2.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
