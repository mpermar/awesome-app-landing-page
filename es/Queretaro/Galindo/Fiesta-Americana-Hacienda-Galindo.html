Descripción

Fiesta Americana Hacienda Galindo features a large outdoor pool with a children's wading pool. Go for a run on the jogging track or relax with a massage, while the kids have fun at the Fiesta Kids Club.

After a day of playing tennis, volleyball and mini-golf, enjoy international and regional cuisine at Hacienda Fiesta Americana's on-site restaurant. After dinner, relax with a drink at the hotel's lounge.
