Descripción

At the hotel, every room is equipped with a desk. With a private bathroom equipped with a shower and free toiletries, rooms at La Casa De Los Dos Leones also have free WiFi. At the accommodation, rooms have air conditioning and a flat-screen TV.

A continental or à la carte breakfast is served at the property.

La Casa De Los Dos Leones has an outdoor pool.

Guests can make sightseeing and ticketing arrangements at the tour desk, or conduct business at the business center.

Queretaro Congress Centre is 2.6 miles from the hotel, while Uptown Center Queretaro is 7 miles from the property. Quétaro Intercontinental Airport is 13 miles away.
