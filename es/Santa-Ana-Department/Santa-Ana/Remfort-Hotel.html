Descripción

At the hotel, rooms are equipped with a patio. With a private bathroom, some units at Remfort Hotel also offer a balcony. Guest rooms feature a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

A continental breakfast is served daily at the property.

The nearest airport is Monseñor Oscar Arnulfo Romero International Airport, 50 miles from the property.
