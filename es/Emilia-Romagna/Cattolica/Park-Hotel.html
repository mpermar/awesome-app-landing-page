Descripción

Todas las habitaciones del Park Hotel, de 4 estrellas, cuentan con conexión inalámbrica a internet gratuita y TV de pantalla plana con canales vía satélite. Los balcones ofrecen vistas frontales o laterales al mar. Se aplican descuentos en la playa privada.

El restaurante del hotel sirve cocina tradicional italiana y platos de Emilia Romagna y las comidas se pueden disfrutar en el comedor con vistas al mar.

El Hotel Park está a 10 minutos en coche de la salida de la autopista A14. La estación de Cattolica se encuentra a 15 minutos a pie.

Políticas de Alojamiento

Children and Extra Beds
All children are welcome.
Free!All children under 2 years stay free of charge in a crib.
All children from 3 to 8 year are charged 25 % of the room stay per night per person for extra beds.
All children from 9 to 12 year are charged 40 % of the room stay per night per person for extra beds.
All older children or adults are charged 60 % of the room stay per night per person for extra beds.
There is no capacity for extra beds in the room.
The maximum number of cribs in a room is 1.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
