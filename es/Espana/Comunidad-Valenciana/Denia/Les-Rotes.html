Descripción

Las habitaciones de Les Rotes cuentan con TV y minibar. Algunas tienen vistas al mar.

El restaurante de Les Rotes, el Punta Negra, ofrece desayunos y platos mediterráneos de temporada. Además, el establecimiento alberga un bar junto al solárium, que cuenta con tumbonas y maravillosas vistas.

La bahía de Punta Negra se encuentra a solo 5 minutos a pie. Les Rotes ofrece aparcamiento gratuito y un servicio de alquiler de bicicletas y quads.

Awesome place.

"Awesome place. Great location near Les Rotes nature reserve and seaside promenade. If you look for quiet place to relax it's perfect. Very relaxing place with nice garden (tropical ambient). You need a car to get to Denia city center and it is short drive to Javea as well. We had superior apartment with little private garden with sun lounges (it was great for our child, those superior apartments have no sea view - you can only get sea view room in main building). There was good size swimming pool. Good restaurant in the hotel. There is no sandy beach near the hotel within walking distance (but plenty of great places for snorkelling - it was not suitable for our small child though). We were driving to the beach to Denia or Javea (3-4km). Very nice staff. I really recommend this place."
