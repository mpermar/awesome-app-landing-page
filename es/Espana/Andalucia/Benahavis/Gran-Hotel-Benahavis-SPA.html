Descripción

El spa cuenta con piscina de hidromasaje climatizada, bañeras de hidromasaje, una sauna y un baño turco. Se ofrece gran variedad de masajes y de tratamientos de belleza. Asimismo, los huéspedes del Benahavis Spa Hotel cuentan con acceso gratuito al gimnasio y con 1 hora de acceso al spa por persona y por noche.

Las habitaciones del hotel son luminosas y espaciosas, con aire acondicionado y calefacción central, minibar, caja fuerte y TV vía satélite.

Benahavís es una localidad conocida por su precioso entorno natural y por su excelente gastronomía. Las playas de la Costa del Sol se encuentran a poca distancia en coche, mientras que el club de golf de Marbella se halla a menos de 2 km del hotel.

El Gran Hotel Benahavís alberga un bar y un restaurante elegante. La recepción permanece abierta 24 horas y tiene un mostrador de información turística que cuenta con servicio de alquiler de coches. El establecimiento dispone de aparcamiento público gratuito.

"Benahavis is a very pretty village set on a hillside, and the hotel is set below the main shopping and restaurant area, so the route there involves a serious uphill walk even though it is only about 400m. That having been said, it is delightful once you get there and the restaurants excellent. The hotel facilities are very good and the staff helpful, there are 2 lovely outdoor pools with a lifeguard (including children' s pool area) and associated bars and a covered terrace for lunch or supper, as well as a dining room and terrace and a breakfast room and terrace.We attended a wedding reception whilst there in the specially designed separate building, which was a great success - food, drinks, service all excellent.We enjoyed our stay there and would return in the future."

19/09/2016

A really nice hotel set on the edge of a very pretty little town.....

"The Hotel is fairly large and spread out and there were lifts at the end of each corridor. There were numerous exterior areas for sitting and relaxing in as well as indoor and exterior dining. There were two large beautifully maintained swimming pools and a small children's pool. The hotel has a Spa. My bedroom was in the standard category and was large and spotlessly clean. The bathroom was okay but could have benefitted from a little updating.Breakfast was good with a reasonable selection of hot and cold dishes but there was a note stating that the juices were "not fresh". I did not try the Bar food by the pool. The hotel staff without exception were welcoming , attentive and extremely helpful.As a footnote, the little town of Benahavis is delightful and is accessible on foot, but some of the streets are very steep and this might cause difficulties if one has mobility problems. A car for getting around in would be a bonus."
