Descripción

El Parador de Mojácar dispone de habitaciones elegantes y luminosas decoradas en tonos neutros. Están equipadas con WiFi gratuita, TV de pantalla plana, baño privado, minibar y caja fuerte.

El restaurante del Parador Mojácar está especializado en pescado fresco y arroces, y dispone de terraza.

La piscina, rodeada por un solárium y palmeras, goza de vistas excelentes. En verano se organiza un club infantil gratuito y clases de natación gratuitas. Además, se ofrecen masajes bajo petición y aparcamiento gratuito. También hay instalaciones para cargar coches eléctricos de forma gratuita.

El hotel está a menos de 2 km del centro de Mojácar, a 20 minutos en coche del parque natural del Cabo de Gata y a unos 80 km del aeropuerto de Almería.

Perfect hotel in a great location with friendly staff and great wifi!

"Perfect hotel in a lovely central spot on Mojacar playa. Our 7th visit to Mojcar but our first to the Parador and we will definitely return. Clean, spacious rooms and public areas, comfy beds and attentive staff, gorgeous place, well worth a visit. Oh and the wifi connection inside and out was the best we've ever had! The pool isn't massive but it's ample and there's a smaller children's pool next to it. Lots of sunbathing areas and loads of sunloungers, you can also get a beach towel from the lifeguard which I'll remember for future visits. We didn't use the restaurant but the poolside cafe bar was lovely for snacks and drinks which are all charged to the room and paid for on check out which makes life easy. Great location in the centre of the playa for a browse around the little shops, a walk along the seafront or to hop on a bus up to Mojacar Pueblo. Definitely a hotel worth visiting!"
