Descripción

The simple rooms at RVHotels Condes del Pallars include a private bathroom and cable TV. They are also equipped with heating and a work desk.

The hotel has a heated indoor pool and sauna. There is an on-site gym and massage services are available.

There is a restaurant and guests can also enjoy drinks in the bar. The hotel has an on-site discoteque. During some periods of the year, there are activities and events organised for free in the swimming pool by Rosa dels Vents.

RVHotels Condes del Pallars offers mini golf, tennis courts and table tennis facilities. The tour desk can organise activities and excursions, such as horse-riding and canoeing. The picturesque village of Sort is just 4 km away.

Port Aine Ski Station is 19 km away while Espot Esquí is 40 km from the hotel.
