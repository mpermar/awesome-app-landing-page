Descripción

Las instalaciones incluyen un gran terreno con una zona privada con aves protegidas. Los apartamentos tienen una zona de cocina totalmente equipada con microondas y lavadora. Además, se proporcionan toallas y ropa de cama.

El establecimiento cuenta con parque infantil y mesas de ping pong y de futbolín. En la zona se pueden practicar varias actividades, como ciclismo, buceo y windsurf.

A 400 metros del establecimiento hay una parada del autobús que conecta con Palma de Mallorca en unos 90 minutos. El aeropuerto de Palma de Mallorca es el más cercano y queda a 49 km.

11/06/2018

Amazing accommodation for families in a safe and quiet area only 8 mins from the beach.

"Just back from a week at these apartments with our kids, 7 and 3, and we couldn't have picked a better location. It is a very short walk to a lovely part of the beach (slightly quieter than nearer the marina) past some useful shops for water, lilos and buckets and spades. Maria had no problem whatsoever in staying up very late to show us around when we arrived late after a delayed flight and with two tired children, and we had the most lovely welcome from her. Our apartment had two bedrooms and was very clean and pretty much everything we needed. There was air conditioning in the lounge area and the master bedroom and a couple of big fans, but we found that having the windows open was enough to keep the place cool. There is a shared swimming pool which was spotless and a fenced off bit for toddlers, and the kids left their lilos, inflatables and water guns for everyone to enjoy throughout the day. The whole garden is enclosed and very far from the road, so it felt completely safe, and there was enough room to run about and play football (or water fights). There were loungers enough for everyone, with shades and a climbing frame, play houses, a tightrope(!) and bikes for the kids to use, plus table football and ping pong near the car parking area. We couldn't believe what brilliant accommodation it was and we will definitely be returning. Book with confidence if you have a family who don't just 'chill' (but perhaps not in school hols if you are a couple), this was easiest one of the best holidays we have had as a family."
