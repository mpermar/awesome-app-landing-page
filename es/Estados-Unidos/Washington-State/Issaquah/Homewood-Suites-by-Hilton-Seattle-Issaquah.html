Experiencias

02/04/2018

Great place to stay with kids!

"The property was great. It's in a nice location with mountain views. The staff was friendly. Breakfast was really good. We had waffles every day but they also had scrambled eggs with different hot sides daily, i.e., potatoes or biscuits & gravy. They also had some healthy options like boiled eggs, oatmeal, fresh fruit and yogurt. There is an activities room with an arcade, pool table, and xboxes, so my 12 year old loved going there. The pool and hot tub looked awesome eventhough we did not use them. We did take advantage of the laundry facility which was very convenient. Overall, we loved our stay and would definitely come again!"
