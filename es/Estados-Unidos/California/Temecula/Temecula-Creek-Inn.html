Descripción

Todas las habitaciones incluyen TV de pantalla plana, escritorio y hervidor de agua. Las unidades seleccionadas cuentan con un área de descanso para su conveniencia. Todas las habitaciones están equipadas con baño privado. Para su comodidad, encontrará albornoces y baño privado con artículos de aseo gratuitos y secador de pelo.

Corcho | Fire Kitchen, el restaurante del hotel, ofrece cocina fresca con ingredientes de granjas locales. Temecula Creek Inn también tiene una parrilla, el CFK Grille & Tap.

La recepción está abierta las 24 horas. Un gimnasio está disponible en el sitio, y el complejo ofrece tratamientos de masaje en la habitación.

San Clemente se encuentra a 45 km del Temecula Creek Inn, mientras que Vista está a 32 km. El aeropuerto internacional de Palm Springs está a 70 km.

Experiencias

18/04/2017

Beautiful property with spacious rooms and gorgeous views!

"We had a dining reservation on Easter Sunday for brunch @ 10:45am at the Cork Fire restaurant. The restaurant was not full at all and when I requested a booth near the window I was told by a hostess that we couldn't sit there because "The seating has already been prearranged and those window seats are unavailable like for children" I was certainly puzzled because the restaurant was not that busy and all the window booths were unoccupied with the exception of one. My husband urged me to ask the manager if we could be moved so I did and after a few minutes the manager returned and with a great attitude and a big smile said "We can absolutely seat you by the window". So we moved by the window with a beautiful view of the golf course and waterfalls and enjoyed our meal. An hour later all the "unavailable" tables by the window were still unoccupied. The food and service was great just unsure why the hostess was unwilling to seat us by a window in the first place. We were also staying there for one night and were able to check in early. The room was very clean, spacious and we had a gorgeous view. I would defenitly stay there again. Also location is great if you plan on visiting Pechanga Casino."
