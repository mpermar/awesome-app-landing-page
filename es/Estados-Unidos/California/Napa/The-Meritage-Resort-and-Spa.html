Descripción

Las amplias habitaciones de The Meritage están equipadas con aire acondicionado, reproductor de CD / DVD, caja fuerte, secador de pelo, plancha y cafetera. Cada uno tiene un escritorio y un baño privado de mármol.

El Meritage Resort Napa se encuentra a 8 km del aeropuerto del condado de Napa y a 5 km del club de campo Chardonnay.

El Spa Terra cuenta con 12 habitaciones y ofrece tratamientos de salud especializados. Los huéspedes de The Meritage Napa pueden cenar en el restaurante Siena y disfrutar de una copa en el bar del vestíbulo.

El hotel admite mascotas y ofrece aparcamiento privado gratuito en el establecimiento.

Beautiful Sunset Destination under the Winemaker Statue near NAPA

"My first time visiting as a guest I was surprised that the resort was larger then expected. I decided to reserve two nights because I was made to feel I received a deep room discount (after taxes ended up being over $500 a night for the two nights… this is more then staying a night in Paris at decent hotel). It was stated as being in the heart of NAPA, but it was not as close as expected. The sunset view from the giant winemaker statue on the hill behind vineyard was amazing and an unexpected treasure. The room was beautifully appointed and cool enough on one of the hottest weekends in history. The coffee shop named "Blend" made an exceptional Ham and Cheese sandwich and was very convenient before we ventured more then 40+ minutes north to most of NAPA, St Helena and Calistoga. Eric in the tasting room was a fun and proved to be a loyal host and representative for both the winery and the Meritage property. Eric is enthusiastic and even more knowledgable, he is filled with lots of history, and pride for both the wines and the NAPA valley region. Eric was able to assist many visitors at once while remaining calm and responsive. He provided a great atmosphere for us to meet many other guests and helped us all start lasting conversations. The tasting room and the wines are refreshing and seem to be marketed as a feature of the hotel. I think the focus should be on the wines alone. The reception staff are very attentive and professional. The short summary.... The hotel is not as close NAPA as marketing sells it. On Father's Day weekend the hallways seemed to always have dirty food trays, strollers, and kids who are bored scooting around on two wheeled scooters. The pool was also dirty, slightly cloudy filled with particulate as if not being filtered clearly. The Meritage was more like a time share property or Holiday Inn for medical business meetings and weddings not an exclusive expensive NAPA Spa Resort."
