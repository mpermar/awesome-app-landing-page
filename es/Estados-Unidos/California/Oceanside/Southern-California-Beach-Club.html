Descripción

Los condominios de un dormitorio en el complejo ofrecen conexión inalámbrica a internet gratuita y TV por cable. Proporcionan zonas de comedor y zonas de estar con sofás cama. Las cocinas americanas incluyen lavaplatos, microondas y refrigeradores. Los ventiladores de techo se proporcionan en el dormitorio y la cocina.

Southern California Beach Club ofrece a sus huéspedes una sala de juegos y zona de barbacoa. También cuenta con lavandería.

El complejo se encuentra a 11 km de Carlsbad Strawberry Company y Legoland California. Está a 11 millas de Sky’s the Limit Ballooning Adventures y a 13 millas del Jardín Botánico de San Diego.

"Hotel location, cleanliness"

19/08/2016

The Southern California Beach Club is a great location on the beach.

"The view was great, we stayed on the 4th floor which was an excellent view of the beach. Our room had a patio with a table, chairs and umbrella plus the deck had lounge chairs to relax in. The room was comfy and the kitchen was well equipped. Beds were comfy and going to sleep with the sound of the ocean was a plus. Enjoyed my morning coffee upstairs watching the surf and a glass of wine at night was great too. Close to pier, we walked every day and very pleasant area. We reserved an underground parking spot, which fit our Toyota Sienna mini van perfectly. This helps because street parking is limited and if you move your car you lose the spot. The hotel is on the beach so no need to drive around finding a spot for the beach. They have umbrellas, boogie boards, sand toys, and bikes to rent for guests for free. There are bbq grills on the 4th floor if you chose to cook. Front desk and staff are very pleasant and helpful, they even let us keep our car in parking spot on last day when we checked out so that we could enjoy the beach for a few more hours before leaving. This was our second visit here, and would recommend the 2 bedroom for larger families, the 1 bedroom we stayed in last year was excellent as well, but the view is better on the 4th floor. Will come back again next year and every summer is great at oceanside, and especially at the The Southern California Beach Club."
