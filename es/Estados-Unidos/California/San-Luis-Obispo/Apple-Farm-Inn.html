Descripción

Las habitaciones del Apple Farm Inn cuentan con TV por cable, soporte para iPod, baño privado, set de té y café. Todas las habitaciones están cómodamente amuebladas con escritorio y sala de estar.

Ofreciendo una cena informal, el restaurante Apple Farm Inn sirve platos tradicionales estadounidenses como pollo y albóndigas, pastel de pavo y galletas con salsa. Todos los huéspedes reciben un vale para la panadería del hotel.

La cata de vinos está disponible en el viñedo de Edna Valley, a 15 minutos en coche de este hotel. El Museo de los Niños de San Luis Obispo está a 3,2 km.

Experiencias

10/07/2018

The Apple Farm was one of our favorite hotels EVER!

"We LOVED our stay at Apple Farm! After a long 4-hour journey, we received Chardonnay and chocolate chip cookies upon arrival. We stayed in a newly remodeled room which was beautiful and so clean! Our room had a sitting area where we played cards with the kids in the evening. We used the gorgeous pool both days because it was very hot and they had pool noodles for the kids. The DETAILS in the room were lovely: night light, clothes line in the shower, used towel basket, lots of plugs for our phones, very comfortable beds, large mirror and flattering lighting. The gift shop was one of the best I've seen! Also the restaurant had amazing breakfasts - we went both days. The Apple Farm is close to the downtown area. We walked to dinner - about a mile, but we are all healthy and fit. Cal Poly is super close. We loved our stay and can't wait to go back!"
