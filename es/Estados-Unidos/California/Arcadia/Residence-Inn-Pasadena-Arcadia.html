Descripción

Las amplias suites ofrecen televisores de pantalla plana de 32 pulgadas con HBO y áreas de comedor y sala de estar. Cuentan con sofá cama y escritorio, y cuentan con utensilios de planchado.

Los huéspedes del Pasadena Arcadia Residence Inn tienen acceso a una piscina climatizada al aire libre, un gimnasio y canchas de voleibol y tenis. El hotel tiene una sala de reuniones y área de picnic. Sirve un desayuno bufé y bebidas alcohólicas.

El Residence Inn se encuentra a 3,2 km de la pista de Santa Anita Rack. Está a 13 km del Museo Norton Simon y Old Pasadena y a 24 km del zoológico y jardín botánico de Los Ángeles.

Our stay was relaxing and thoroughly enjoyable.

"This is a great place to stay for families with young kids and pets. The rooms are spacious and comfortable with a picnic table right outside each door that would be great for eating takeout with the kids. Little paths lead to the parking lot, a grassy dog walk, a sports court, and a pool - all with a gorgeous view of the mountains. The complementary buffet breakfast has a large variety of offerings and lots of seating. They also have complementary food a couple nights each week, but there are lots of restaurants and dining choices right next door and across the street. The staff is friendly and helpful. We couldn’t have found a better place for a family of 5 (including a grandma, 2 young kids, 2 dogs, and a cat) to stay for 4 nights while our house was being tented for termites. Even though the parents were going to work and the dogs to daycare, our stay felt like a vacation in the city where we actually live. A great place for all ages to visit in Arcadia is the L.A. County Arboretum where peacocks, ducks, and geese wander through the gardens. Kids can run on the large grassy areas and through jungle like paths and adults can enjoy the diversity of plants like those in the desert garden."
