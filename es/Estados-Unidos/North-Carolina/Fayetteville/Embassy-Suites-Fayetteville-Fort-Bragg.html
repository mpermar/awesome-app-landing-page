Descripción

Las habitaciones del Embassy Suites Fayetteville / Fort Bragg disponen de conexión inalámbrica a internet gratuita y TV de pantalla plana por cable. Cada habitación también viene equipada con una pequeña nevera y microondas.

Además, el Fort Bragg / Fayetteville Embassy Suites cuenta con piscina cubierta, gimnasio y servicio de lavandería. También hay una recepción de gerentes que se celebra todas las noches.

El restaurante Flying Spoons está en el hotel y abre para el desayuno y la cena. El servicio de habitaciones también está disponible para el servicio de cena.

El campo de golf Ryder se encuentra a 15 minutos en coche del hotel. El jardín botánico Cape Fear se encuentra a menos de 16 km.

Unbeatable and Phenomenal

"Outstanding. In all ways. I have to travel this way out for business and this will be where I always stay at. My husband and 6 year old child came with me and there was plenty for them to do at the hotel while I worked. Every experience was superb. It made my work easy, since everything was luxurious and taken care of. There's a starbucks in the hotel, a pool with waterfall and hot tub, great work out center, many areas to sit and converse, inside and outside, the whole place was quiet, even in the lobby area. The shower by itself is worth the stay. Fluffy towels, great bed, incredible pillows. The breakfast was extensive. You can get omelets made to order, and bacon, sausage, breakfast potatoes, but there was also southern flair such as grits, and many other options such as fresh oatmeal, bagels, yogurt, juice, biscuits and gravy, literally everything. There's other people coming and going, but it is so spacious and quiet, you almost feel you have it all to yourselves, there's just no feeling of crowding. The hotel room itself is incredibly spacious. You have a couch, plus large desk and work area, and several other chairs on top of the couch, plus a desk chair. The room had a mini fridge AND a mini freezer, which was great cause we were able to put our ice packs from our travel cooler in the freezer and reset our cooler easily when we left. The hotel sits just down the block from the mall and a long road full of places to eat. Right near the highway, but the way it was situated, you feel like you somehow stepped on to a resort and everything is spacious. I had no issues finding a place to park, there were plenty of spaces.
