Descripción

Las habitaciones del Omni Grove Park Inn, amuebladas con piezas de roble macizo, también cuentan con TV de pantalla plana, cafetera y nevera. Las habitaciones del piso Club incluyen una ducha de hidromasaje a ras de suelo tipo spa y acceso al salón solo para adultos.

El spa subterráneo cuenta con piscinas minerales, sauna y sala de vapor por un cargo adicional. Un complejo deportivo y un campo de golf diseñado por Donald Ross están en el sitio.

Sunset Terrace sirve platos clásicos de carne y mariscos. El comedor Blue Ridge tiene un menú de estilo sureño. EDISON, cerveza artesanal + cocina, sirve comida casera sureña y cerveza artesanal local. VUE 1913 ofrece comida francesa.

El Biltmore Estate está a 10 minutos en coche del hotel. Los huéspedes estarán a 3,5 km de la Universidad de Carolina del Norte Asheville, a 20 km del Arboreto de Carolina del Norte y a 15 km del Blue Ridge Parkway.

Experiencias

12/06/2017

I upgraded to the Club Floor.

"I upgraded to the Club Floor. You have access to the spa and concierge services with valet or garage parking included. They also provide key card access to this floor. It allows no children and is very quiet and private. The concierge staff are very professional and courteous."

11/05/2016

Wonderful, historic hotel.

"Wonderful, historic hotel. The scenery is breathtaking, and we loved the great hall where we could sit by a fireplace at night with a hot cocoa or wine and listen to live music. Our room in the Sammons wing was nice, but a basic contemporary hotel room you would find anywhere, the real draw is the scenery and main building. The restaurants were good, and the Marketplace was really a fun place to shop and pick up a light breakfast or coffee. Sitting at the Edison outside for dinner was magical. I hiked the short Sunset Trail with children and it was not strenuous at all. The herb water set out for guests all day in the Hall was a treat."
