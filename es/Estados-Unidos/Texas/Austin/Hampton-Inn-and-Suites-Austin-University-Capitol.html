Descripción

Las modernas y luminosas habitaciones del Hampton Inn and Suites Austin University Capitol están equipadas con un escritorio y una silla ergonómica. Los huéspedes pueden disfrutar de canales de películas gratuitos en el televisor de pantalla plana en cada habitación. Una cafetera, nevera y microondas también se proporcionan.

Los huéspedes pueden energizarse en el moderno gimnasio o imprimir documentos de viaje en el centro de negocios del hotel. Una lavandería de autoservicio también es convenientemente accesible en el hotel.

Todas las mañanas se sirve un desayuno bufé caliente que incluye gofres recién hechos, avena, jugo y café en el Austin Hampton Inn.

El edificio del capitolio del estado de Texas y el distrito de entretenimiento Sixth Street se encuentran a una milla del hotel.

We were greeted with a smile and host facilitated our check...

"We were greeted with a smile and host facilitated our check in quickly. It was a weekend so parked in front at meter for no charge after 6pm and free all day Sunday. Easy to get in and out for dining and sightseeing. The hotels in downtown Austin charge up to $47/day! That’s crazy to me. We enjoyed pool and area surrounding nice especially since rooftop. I did see speakers but there was no music playing. We ended up using our own speaker just to make it a bit more lively. It was a hot day yet the position of pool it almost felt cold. In a couple of more weeks that for sure will not be a problem. Breakfast Sunday was awesome! With 3 kids under 18 (3,14 & 16) who wants to deal with everyone agreeing on one place to eat? I highly recommend this hotel and location for a family. If it was all adults being walking distance to bars and restaurants would have been a priority but in my case this was perfect!"
