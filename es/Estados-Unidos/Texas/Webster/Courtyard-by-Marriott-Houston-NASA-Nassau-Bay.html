Descripción

El Courtyard by Marriott Houston NASA Nassau Bay ofrece habitaciones con TV de pantalla plana y canales por cable. Las habitaciones disponen de nevera pequeña, microondas y set de café.

Un gimnasio ofrece equipo cardiovascular y pesas libres para los huéspedes que quieran hacer ejercicio durante su estadía. El Courtyard Houston también cuenta con instalaciones de lavandería y un centro de negocios. The Marketplace ofrece bocadillos, refrescos y artículos de tocador para comprar.

The Bistro, el restaurante del hotel, sirve desayunos y cenas, un bar nocturno y café Starbucks. Las opciones de desayuno incluyen avena, yogurt, cereales, comidas calientes y pasteles. La cena incluye sándwiches, pizzas y hamburguesas. El paseo marítimo de Kemah está a 11,2 km del hotel. El aeropuerto Hobby está a menos de 30 minutos en coche.

Experiencias

08/05/2018

Overall very good

"The bed was nice, the area around the hotel seems to be clean and crime free. The Police station is in the same parking lot as the hotel. Somebody lit off one of the sirens in a parked Police car (not Police bashing-at all-so stop) early in the morning but in hindsight that struck us as pretty funny thinking about how hard that somebody was probably trying to find the off switch.The neat thing about this place is it's proximity to the space center so many that stay here seem to be here for that attraction. There were a LOT of well-behaved ROTC kids in the place and it was fun watching their excitement about something such as this.The Houston Space center is across the street so it was nice to go all day not have to drive far to the hotel. The other area dining and amusements are within 10 miles so we had plenty to do."
