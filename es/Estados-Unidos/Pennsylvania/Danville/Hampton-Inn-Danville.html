Descripción

Todas las habitaciones del Hampton Inn Danville cuentan con escritorio, microondas y cafetera. También se proporcionan artículos de aseo gratuitos en cada habitación.

Este hotel ofrece una bañera de hidromasaje y un desayuno bufé diario. Los huéspedes también pueden disfrutar de la comodidad de un centro de negocios en el lugar.

Los restaurantes cercanos incluyen Cracker Barrel, a solo 14 km de este Hampton Inn. El aeropuerto de Danville está a 7 km y el bosque estatal Bald Eagle se encuentra a 30 minutos en coche.

07/07/2017

Comfortable stay, happy bellies, friendly staff

"The photos and reviews for this property were accurate. We booked it a day prior to arriving on July 3rd for a nearby amusement park experience and were happy to discover a great value here. In room 304, the beds were comfortable, the linens clean, and room layout thoughtful. The complimentary breakfast had something for everyone in our party - from a delicious Western omelet to make-your-own waffles, from fresh fruit to yummy pastries (chocolate and blueberry muffins were very good :)). We just missed the evening cookies, since we got back from dinner later, but we were able to enjoy hot tea with some night-time TV. The pool area was clean, well-stocked with towels, and sunny - lots of windows to the outdoors. The children truly enjoyed the pool... and therefore, so did I. Parking was very convenient and the location was easily accessible but also tucked away at the same time. The staff was friendly, even as the lobby area bustled with guests during the latter part of breakfast. Thank you for a comfortable stay and happy bellies!"

"We stayed here after a day trip to knoebels. A friend had recommended the Hampton Inn to us.
