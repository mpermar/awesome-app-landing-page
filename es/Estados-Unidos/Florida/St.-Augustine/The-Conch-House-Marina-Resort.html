Descripción

Las habitaciones del The Conch House Marina Resort disponen de microondas, nevera y cafetera. Los huéspedes también pueden disfrutar de TV por cable en sus habitaciones.

Una tienda de souvenirs está en el sitio en este complejo para no fumadores. El restaurante Conch House está en el lugar y ofrece mariscos para el almuerzo y la cena.

El anfiteatro de San Agustín se encuentra a 2,4 km del establecimiento, mientras que el parque estatal Anastasia está a 2,8 km. Los huéspedes estarán a 3 minutos en coche del parque zoológico de Saint Augustine Alligator Farm.

Experiencias

05/03/2018

The room didn't feel like a crammed hotel room.

"The room didn't feel like a crammed hotel room. It feel like a little version of home. Living room, bedroom, kitchen (no Stove) and bathroom. The room was just off of the pool. We will return and bring the kids next time."

21/12/2016

Fun place near the water and out of the Old Town's congestion

"It's a cute little family-run place right near the water -- not a resort, but a quaint motel. The restaurant on the property was cute (though we didn't eat there because it was mostly seafood) and is raising a few baby alligators that were fun to see. We had fun exploring the marina and watching pelican families. For us the location was great because it had lots of parking and wasn't located in the noisy, congested downtown area. We had a car, but the lighthouse is just a few blocks away and you could walk across the bridge into the old town in about 20 minutes, which is nice because parking downtown was hard to find and sometimes expensive."

15/02/2017

Resort mit eigenem Charakter

"Das Appartment hatte zwei kleine separate Schlafzimmer und ein Wohnzimmer (wir sind mit 2 Kindern verreist). Die Lage ist ok, wenn auch nicht berauschend, da man vor Ort praktisch nicht spazieren gehen kann. Die Innenstadt von St Augustine ist gut erreichbar und auch der Aligator Park ist in der Nähe. Das Personal an der Rezeption war sehr freundlich und hilfsbereit. Ansonsten hat die Anlage eher Motel- als Resortcharakter."
