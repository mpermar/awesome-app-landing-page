Descripción

Las habitaciones de Regal Palms están equipadas con muchas comodidades modernas, como cocinas totalmente equipadas, lavadoras y secadoras. Los huéspedes también disfrutarán de televisores de pantalla plana, terrazas al aire libre y acceso gratuito a internet inalámbrico.

Los huéspedes que se alojen en Villas at Regal Palms pueden disfrutar de la casa club, que cuenta con una sala de juegos, áreas de barbacoa e instalaciones para hacer ejercicio. El establecimiento ofrece actividades infantiles supervisadas en el lugar, así como un bar y una tienda de conveniencia.

SeaWorld Orlando se encuentra a 30 minutos en coche de las Villas at Regal Palms Resort & Spa. El Orlando Vineland Premium Outlets está a 28 km.

20/03/2016

Our family of 7 was pleased with the accommodations 145...

"Our family of 7 was pleased with the accommodations 145 Bergamo. Rooms were clean and had all the amenities that we required. Wifi was excellent. The distance from the clubhouse made it near impossible to walk. All beds were upstairs in the condo we rented and had two pullout couches on the lower level - so it was challenging for my mother-in-law to manage the stairs all week (thus another reason distance to the pool was an issue). Pool and lazy river were crowded (March Break so expected it to be busy) but almost impossible to get a chair by the pool. Many of the resort condos were filled with students playing baseball or lacrosse so a younger crowd at the pool at night (not always the best for a family). Lazy river was great (need to purchase your own floatie) however fast moving so younger or poor swimmers will need to be supervised. Pool and lazy river not quite big enough to accommodate the size of the resort. More seating around the pool especially with some shade or in view of the water would be helpful. Pool and lazy river water was very cloudy.There were no extra activities for kids to do (other resorts we have stayed at had much more to offer). No additional costs for use of pool. Restaurant understaffed but pizza was good. For the cost not a bad deal especially if going to theme parks, however if staying for a week to be entertained at the resort it doesn't have enough to keep kids occupied (4 kids age 12-5)."
