Descripción

Todas las habitaciones cuentan con TV por cable, nevera pequeña y set de café. Este Birmingham Westin ofrece salida de video en la habitación para mayor comodidad. Una cama para perros está disponible para mascotas con mascotas.

Los huéspedes del Westin Birmingham serán recibidos en la recepción las 24 horas. Los huéspedes tienen a su disposición un gimnasio y un centro de negocios durante toda su estadía. También hay disponibles amplias instalaciones para reuniones de 7,000 pies cuadrados.

Octane Coffee ofrece comidas ligeras con café recién hecho en el lobby. La cocina clásica americana se sirve en Todd English P.U.B. en un entorno exclusivo y moderno. El Lobby Bar ofrece un extenso menú de vinos, cervezas y licores.

El Museo de Arte de Birmingham está a 1,6 km. El zoológico de Birmingham y los jardines botánicos de Birmingham se encuentran a 8 km del hotel.

Experiencias

31/07/2017

Housekeeping staff was excellent!

"Housekeeping staff was excellent! Very accommodating and friendly! I mentioned that we loved the body lotion and she left us a couple extra bottles! The room was spacious and comfortable, we loved the rainfall shower. It was very quiet and got super dark at night. The AC worked super well and the unit was outside so you didn't hear it when it kicked on. Everything was very clean and well maintained. We were super into the bathroom mirror which was essentially a giant makeup mirror that helped you get your selfie game on POINT! :))). The area seemed nice! The Westin was in a little complex of restaurants and bars right outside the door. We didn't go to any of these but they appeared busy and fun! Outside of this little area there isn't much in walking distance. We took an uber to some other bars just a few minutes away so the location was perfect.There was a HUGE church group staying in the hotel at the same time as us (we're talking about 10 school buses when they left!), and we had NO IDEA they were even there till the buses showed up to take them home. I think it speaks measures about a hotel when literally hundreds of kids are staying there and you have no clue. Now that I think about it, that may be what was being handled by the front desk staff when checked in (I supposed we should give them a pass :) )."
