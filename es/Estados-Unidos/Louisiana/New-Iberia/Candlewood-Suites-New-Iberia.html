Descripción

El Candlewood Suites New Iberia cuenta con un área de cocina totalmente equipada con pisos de madera. Incluye TV de pantalla plana por cable, cafetera y escritorio.

Una tienda de conveniencia en el lugar ofrece bocadillos, bebidas y artículos personales durante todo el día. Las instalaciones de barbacoa se encuentran debajo del pabellón para comidas estilo picnic.

Los huéspedes del New Iberia Candlewood pueden disfrutar de la piscina al aire libre o ocuparse del trabajo de última hora en el centro de negocios.

El New Iberia Candlewood Suites está a 12 minutos del zoológico de Acadiana y a menos de 4,8 km de Shadows, en la histórica mansión Teche.

Experiencias

17/02/2015

I checked into this hotel to surprise for my husband on...

"I checked into this hotel to surprise for my husband on valentines night. I checked in while he was at work at 3 PM to set up the room with chocolates and more. I made sure I brought all he would need for a night at a hotel. Checking in was a breeze. Needless to say my husband was very happy to have a hotel to ourselves for Valentines night. A place we could relax watch movies and more ..wink wink... without the interruption of our three children. The hotel staff was amazing. But what I found most convenient was the little store they have by the front desk. That little store had little packets of aspirin bottles of soda and water and juices little breakfast ideas and antacid eye drops and anything that you might have overlooked or not even known you needed. So much better than vending machines that rely on the fact that you're carrying around wads of singles. My husband and I were actually very grateful it was there because while he was showering he got soap in his eye and we made a special trip down to grab some Visine. They also had a little area where you could borrow small appliances such as crockpots, staplers, tape, highlighters (which I would imagine would be very helpful with the traveling businessman and buisnesswoman). They also had movies and games. The hotel room itself was nice and clean the shower was excellent the water pressure was great. We really had everything we needed for quiet relaxing room to ourselves. One of my favorite things about this hotel and to be honest was the reason I chose it was they have a 12 o'clock check out time which means that we could sleep in together. something with three kids were not normally able to do. Everybody was very friendly checkout was very easy and I honestly can't wait to stay again. It was perfect for a couple just looking to get away for the night. But I imagine it would be very suitable for vacationing couple and business mean and woman alike. Thanks forms great stay!"
