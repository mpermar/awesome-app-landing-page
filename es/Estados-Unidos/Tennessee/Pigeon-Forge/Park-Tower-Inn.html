Descripción

Las habitaciones del hotel están equipadas con armario y TV de pantalla plana. Todas las habitaciones incluyen aire acondicionado y algunas habitaciones tienen balcón. Todas las habitaciones disponen de microondas.

El desayuno diario ofrece opciones continentales y americanas.

El establecimiento cuenta con bañera de hidromasaje.

El teatro Country Tonite se encuentra a 2,1 km del Park Tower Inn. El aeropuerto más cercano es el aeropuerto McGhee Tyson, a 39 km del alojamiento.

The Park Tower Inn is a great value hotel and I highly recommend it.

"This is a great value hotel in Pigeon Forge, especially with kids in your party. Again, this is a VALUE hotel. This means that it is neither the fanciest nor most luxurious hotel you will ever stay at. My writing on the other hand, as I proof-read this, likely sounds way too professional for a hotel review. That is because I teach English and now my writing sounds way too formal due to constant essay writing. Don't judge me. Anyway, PTI is a great value for little money. Here is how our stay was. Take from it what you will.We never tried the outdoor pool or even looked at it because the indoor pool and hot tub were nice enough and never too crowded. The lazy river pool they have was where we spent most of our hotel leisure time though. The track is long enough for it not to become monotonous and my kids switched back and forth between lazily inner-tubing around and swimming after each other around the track...when it wouldn't bother anyone else.The game room is small but nice. We never used it but we gave it a look. With so many arcades in the area small but nice is kind of meh. I never saw anyone in the fitness room and since I was swimming a ton I saw no reason to use it myself. There is a stair-climber, exercise bike, and treadmill. It's not going to wow you but if you're not into swimming but do want to be fit it would get the job done.The breakfast was what I would call continental plus. Cereals, beverages, breads, and fruits were on offer like any value hotel but there were also two waffle-makers, one of those automated pancake makers, and gravy for the biscuits. There was nothing that had to be cooked by staff which is why it's still just a continental breakfast. That means no eggs, bacon, sausage, etc. We never missed any of those things. Everything that was available was fresh, good, and filling."
