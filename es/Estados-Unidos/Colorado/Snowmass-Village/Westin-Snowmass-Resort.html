Descripción

Este complejo de ski-in / ski-ski se encuentra junto a la góndola Sky Cab y ofrece un servicio de valet privado de esquí. Hay un spa y 2 restaurantes disponibles. Se ofrece un servicio de traslado gratuito al aeropuerto del condado de Aspen-Pitkin.

Las habitaciones modernas del Westin Snowmass Resort tienen vistas al complejo desde un balcón privado y están equipadas con TV de pantalla plana. Diseñados con sábanas blancas crujientes, los huéspedes pueden disfrutar de artículos de baño gratuitos con bañera de hidromasaje en el baño privado.

Snowmass Kitchen sirve un desayuno bufé, pizza y pasta para el almuerzo y un menú temático del norte de Italia para la cena en el Westin Snowmass Resort. El Vue Lounge ofrece cerveza y cócteles a diario. Starbucks se presenta en el sitio. El servicio de habitaciones está disponible las 24 horas.

Después de un día de esquí, los huéspedes pueden aprovechar un menú completo de tratamientos de spa en Snowmass Spa.

Este complejo cuenta con acceso directo a las pistas, una escuela de esquí y un punto de venta de forfaits. También se pueden alquilar equipos de esquí. La zona es popular para esquiar y jugar al golf. El aeropuerto del condado de Aspen-Pitkin se encuentra a 8 km.

Experiencias

21/02/2018

Great hotel, great location! Staff were friendly. They have a great kids club!

"Staff were great, however when talking with the 'tourist/booking' desk, they could not help us with booking ski school or tickets as they can do this prior to arrival, but not after. I thought this very interesting."

08/01/2018

Small room but unbeatable location/convenience

"Location is fantastic. We had a room that was truly ski in ski out and beside the heated pool & jacuzzi which the kids LOVED. I was underwhelmed by the size of the rooms because of the daily rate but I suppose the cost comes down to the hotel's unbeatable location. I just couldn't get over how small the rooms felt, especially with so much ski gear (2 adults and 2 young kids) and barely anywhere to set things down. I was a bit disappointed that the kid's club was charging for access as some other hotels don't, but I think they had to limit the numbers as there were just too many families in the hotel. The breakfast buffet was disappointing in quality and variety. The best thing the hotel has going, is definitely it's location and convenience to the gondola, ski rental, ski lift, shops, F&B, which will probably still make it a very desirable option for most."
