Descripción

At the hotel, rooms come with a patio. All rooms have a flat-screen TV, and some accommodations at Sumaj Casa Hotel have a terrace. All guest rooms are equipped with a private bathroom equipped with a shower.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental breakfast.

The nearest airport is Cadete FAP Guillermo del Castillo Paredes, 3.1 miles from Sumaj Casa Hotel, and the property offers a free airport shuttle service.
