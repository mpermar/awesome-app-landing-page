Descripción

At the resort, rooms include a desk and a TV. Guest rooms at Bella Terra Laguna Azul feature air conditioning and a closet.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy an American breakfast. There's an on-site restaurant, which serves local cuisine.

Guests at Bella Terra Laguna Azul Resort & Spa will be able to enjoy activities in and around Sauce, like fishing.

Tarapoto is 18 miles from the resort. The nearest airport is Cadete FAP Guillermo del Castillo Paredes Airport, 17 miles from Bella Terra Laguna Azul.
