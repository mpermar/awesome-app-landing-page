Descripción

Rooms at Aranwa Sacred Valley Hotel & Wellness have impeccable 5- star hotel décor with a colonial style. They all offer a jacuzzi with a heated towel rack, LCD TV and free Wi-Fi.

The spa center provides a full spectrum of services ranging from essential oil treatments with native products, such as quinoa, to hydrotherapy and aromatherapy sessions.

Aranwa offers 3 different dining options that include Japanese-Peruvian fusion cuisine, native organic products and a chef's menu. The cellar carries an exclusive collection of wines.

The hotel is by the Vilcanota River, and Cuzco is 1.5 hours away. Airport shuttles can be arranged and there is free private parking available.
