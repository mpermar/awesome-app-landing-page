Descripción

The Suites at Radisson San Isidro Hotel & Suites feature stylish décor, a large sitting area with LCD cable TV and a coffee machine. Some suites also have a private spa tub.

The hotel´s facilities include a dry sauna. Guests can work out in the Radisson´s gym or explore Lima during one of the city excursions that can be arranged at the tour desk.

Guests can dine in the innovative Zendo Restaurant which serves an eclectic mix of salads as well as seafood, in a modern setting. Guests are also invited to a daily extensive buffet breakfast. Vouchers for drinks are provided per room per night to use at the bar.

Hotel Radisson San Isidro is just a 25-minute drive from Jorge Chavez International Airport. The Hotel provides a shuttle service upon request.

San Isidro is a great choice for travelers interested in gourmet food, restaurants and food.
