Descripción

The rooms in the hotel are equipped with a coffee machine. Featuring a private bathroom with a hot tub, rooms at Hotel El Reith Lake also offer free WiFi, while certain rooms are contain a mountain view. All rooms feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Continental and Full English/Irish breakfast options are available every morning at the accommodation. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of international and Grill/bbq dishes.

Hotel El Reith Lake has a terrace. The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental is available at the hotel.

Languages spoken at the 24-hour front desk include English and Spanish.

Granada is 4 miles from Hotel El Reith Lake, while Managua is 30 miles away. The nearest airport is Augusto C. Sandino Airport, 24 miles from the accommodation.
