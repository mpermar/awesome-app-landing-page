Descripción

At the hotel, all rooms include a desk and a flat-screen TV. Each room is equipped with a private bathroom with free toiletries. Guest rooms at Hotel Jardin de Granada feature air conditioning and a closet.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental breakfast.

Hotel Jardin de Granada has a terrace.

Augusto C. Sandino Airport is 20 miles from the property.
