Descripción

Las habitaciones del Barong Resort and Spa presentan una decoración tradicional balinesa. Incluye TV por cable, reproductor de CD y zona de estar al aire libre. Las villas cuentan con piscinas privadas.

Barong Resort and Spa organiza diariamente actividades de trekking por la mañana hacia el valle de Ubud y los arrozales cercanos. Un tour nocturno a Petulu Village también está disponible.

Los huéspedes pueden disfrutar de relajantes servicios de masajes tradicionales en el spa o disfrutar de un libro en la biblioteca de Barong. Se proporcionan bicicletas gratuitas para que los huéspedes exploren la zona cuando lo deseen.

Samanera Bowl sirve una selección de auténticos platos locales y favoritos asiáticos. Todos los días se sirve té gratuito por la tarde y pasteles balineses.

El Barong Resort and Spa se encuentra a 3,3 km de Campuhan Ridge y a 4,3 km de Goa Gajah. Está a una hora en coche del aeropuerto internacional Ngurah Rai.

Experiencias

20/06/2018

Private escape perfect for all even families!

"We stayed at Barong resort and spa as a young family. The King size bed was more than ample for my husband, two year old and myself and the staff put a beautiful cot complete with netting in the room for our three month old at no extra cost. The breakfast was delicious with fresh pineapple and pawpaw jam making it extra special. The private courtyards allow you to relax outdoors unwatched. The pool was delightful to swim in and there was always some shade provided by the lush garden for those wanting to be sunsmart. The staff never made us feel pressured to use their services (we did use their spa, restaurant and drivers during our stay - all of which were enjoyable experiences) which is a relief following the constant "come into my restaurant / shop" etc that you face on the street. This relaxed approach does them credit and I'm sure wins them many return guests."
