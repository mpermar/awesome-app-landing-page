Descripción

Las habitaciones de la Villa Coral tienen aire acondicionado, balcón privado y minibar. Algunas habitaciones cuentan con bañeras y tienen vistas a los jardines, a la piscina o al océano.

Los huéspedes pueden pasear por la playa o los jardines tropicales. Actividades acuáticas como buceo y snorkel están disponibles en Villa Coral View. Los jóvenes invitados también pueden jugar a los dardos y otros juegos de mesa en la sala de juegos.

El Villa Coral View ofrece servicios de lavandería y cambio de divisas. También cuenta con un mostrador de información turística que organiza actividades diurnas. Los autos también se pueden alquilar por el día.

Los huéspedes pueden disfrutar de una experiencia gastronómica al aire libre en el restaurante del hotel, que sirve comida europea y balinesa. El restaurante frente al mar sirve mariscos frescos y una buena selección de vinos. Se ofrece servicio de habitaciones las 24 horas.

18/04/2018

All in all, a great place!

"All in all, a great place! You can have a huge beach umbrella for two, snorkling equipment and a guy with glass bottom canoe at the beach (this one very useful for kids affraid of snorkling). Great snorkling experience, with a sunk boat in front of the resort and a japanese ship 3 km afar. You can see Mount Agung from the beach and arrange to climb it if you have a spare day. The food in the restaurant is delicious, the bungalows are comfy and nicely arranged with spacious bathrooms."

"The resort is wonderful, near the beach and the coral view area. We had all we needed regarding the facilities and our needs. Great place for snorkeling

"Snorkeling possible directly in front of our villa at the beach though the beach itself is packed with boats. 17/06/2015

We stayed 2 nights in a two story villa looking at the beach.

"We stayed 2 nights in a two story villa looking at the beach. The view was amazing and the balcony was prefect for viewing the sunrise. Room was nice with traditional Balinese features. The outdoor bathroom was stunning with a big stone bath. Pool area was nice and peaceful. Breakfast was okay but limited. The restaurant was otherwise quite good and reasonably priced. The hawkers on the beach weren't too pushy and I found them actually quite helpful.Only cons would be no TV in the room (although that didn't bother me too much) and the bathroom door wouldn't close properly so had to lock from the top to stop mosquitos which meant our 5 year old couldn't go to toilet at night without waking us up to unlock the door.Overall I would definitely recommend this hotel. Especially good fro families. The photos don't do it justice. It is such a scenic spot."
