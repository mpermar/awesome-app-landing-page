Descripción

Todos los alojamientos disponen de terraza con vistas al jardín.

Todas las mañanas se sirve un desayuno continental en el establecimiento.

El complejo turístico cuenta con una piscina al aire libre.

Tanto el servicio de alquiler de bicicletas como el de alquiler de coches están disponibles en Seaside Huts Lembongan.

Blue Corner se encuentra a 2,2 km del alojamiento. El aeropuerto más cercano es el aeropuerto internacional Ngurah Rai, a 30 km del establecimiento.

Experiencias

07/03/2018

Close to the beach, helpful staff, lovely villas, dive centre nearby, views of Mt Agung

"My stay at Seaside Huts Lembongan was nothing short of fantastic. The huts, aren't huts all at all, but gorgeous little villas - with access to a beautiful pool that has views of Mt Agung when the weather is right. Putu (the owner) went out of his way to help, and kindly took me around the island, and across to Cennigan one afternoon for some exploring. Nothing is too much trouble. The chefs of Seaside Huts Lembongan, Katut and Wayan - also make some beautiful authentic Indo cuisine, Katut's Nasi Goreng is a must.. There are many options to eat out, but it's as good eating in - with the quality. If you are a diver, Seaside Huts, is a stone's throw away from a fantastic dive centre, Tamarind Divers, who I also couldn't recommend more highly. I was a solo female traveler, and found Seaside to be very accomodating, but I think it would be even more enjoyable for couples, or families."

19/09/2017

Delightful

"Beautiful little cabins, powerful aircon.There are now 7 cabins, the 3 in front of the water and another 4 set 50 meters back called garden view rooms.Bathroom is really lovely, yes water is very slightly salty but you probably shouldn't be drinking it anyway.Hit water was indeed warm but not really hot.Staff are very helpful and friendly, breakfast now has more than 3 options and comes with coffee or tea plus juice and fruit.We stayed for 8 night's and were very happy here.I don't know why people are calling it isolated. If you walk 8 minutes in one direction you are in the tourist hotel area if mushroom bay, walk 5 minutes along the coast in the other direction and you can paddle out to the surf break playgrounds, walk another 10 minutes past there and you are in the main local village. Hire a scooter and you can be anywhere on the island in minutes.Just a couple of very minor suggestions for the owners though. A. Bedside tables, somewhere to rest your charging devicesB. Drying racks for wet clothes outside the room to save hanging them on the railing C. A water refill station, I wouldn't mind having to pay to refill a bottle, it would be much better than contributing to the massive plastic bottle problem on the island."
