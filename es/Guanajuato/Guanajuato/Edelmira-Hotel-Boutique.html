Descripción

This boutique hotel features a bar and a restaurant, and features a colonial-style structure with archways and a domed central patio.

Featuring a modern décor in turquoise and brown tones, Edelmira’s air-conditioned rooms have parquet floors and stone walls. Each comes furnished with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with shower.

Edelmira Hotel Boutique is a few feet from the Union Garden, and is within walking distance of main colonial buildings. The famous Mummies of Guanajuato Museum is a 6 minute drive.
