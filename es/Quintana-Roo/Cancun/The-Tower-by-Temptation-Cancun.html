Descripción

At the resort, each room is equipped with a balcony. The rooms come with a flat-screen TV, and certain rooms at The Tower by Temptation Cancun Reso have a terrace. At the accommodation every room comes with a closet and a private bathroom.

A buffet breakfast is served each morning at the property. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the in-house restaurant, which serves international cuisine.

The Tower by Temptation Cancun has an outdoor pool.

Beto Avila Stadium is 3.4 miles from the resort, while Cancun Convention Center is 2.5 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Cancun Airport, 9 miles from The Tower by Temptation Cancun Reso.
