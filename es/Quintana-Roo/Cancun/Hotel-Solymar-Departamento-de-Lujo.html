Descripción

Guest rooms at the hotel come with a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a kitchenette. Each room includes a private bathroom with a bath and a hair dryer, while certain rooms are equipped with a balcony. All guest rooms have a seating area.

Cancun Government Palace is 8 miles from Hotel Solymar Departamento de Lujo. The nearest airport is Cancun Airport, 6 miles from the property.
