Descripción

In the Cancun Hotel Zone, the Royal Islander offers a free shuttle between several sister resorts. The private Royal Marina offers free use of boats and sailing classes on the lagoon.

The elegant, air-conditioned rooms come equipped with cable TV, a coffee maker and fridge. Suites also have a large terrace, full kitchen and lounge. Bathrooms have a hairdryer.

international cuisine and seafood are served in the Cayo Largo restaurant, while Paco’s Tacos serves Tex-Mex dishes. There is also a pizza delivery service and grocery store.
