Descripción

Each spacious room at Sandos Playacar Beach Resort is decorated in tropical colors and features a plasma TV, well-stocked minibar and a private balcony. It also offers a work desk and a safe in every room.

Sandos Playacar Beach Resort offers 2 lighted tennis courts, a fully equipped gym and free bike rentals with organized tours. Water activities include snorkeling and water basketball.

Guests can enjoy both casual and fine dining at Sandos Playacar Beach Resort. An Italian buffet, gourmet steak house and Mexican specialties are just a few of the various options. A poolside snack bar is also featured.

The archaeological sites of Tulúm and Cobá are within an hours’ drive of this Sandos Playacar Beach Resort.
