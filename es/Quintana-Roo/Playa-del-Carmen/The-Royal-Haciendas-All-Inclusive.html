Descripción

All accommodations have free Wi-Fi, and many suites have a sitting area with a flat-screen cable TV and DVD player. Some also have wonderful ocean views.

The Royal Haciendas complex has a spa and health club with Turkish baths. There is also a fitness center. The beach next to the complex has lounge chairs and sun umbrellas.

The Hacienda complex features activities including diving. Nearby Playa del Carmen there is a good range of shops, bars and restaurants, as well as beautiful natural surroundings. Cancun International Airport is a 30-minute drive away.
