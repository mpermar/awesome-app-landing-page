Descripción

Riviera Del Sol is in the lively city of Playa del Carmen, just around the corner of the popular 5th Avenue, which is surrounded by unique bars and nightclubs.

The hotel’s restaurant, Luna Brasserie, serves Mexican cuisine which can be enjoyed in the gardens or in the pool area. Room service is available. There is also a bar that is specialized on flavored margaritas.

The air-conditioned rooms have a minibar and a cable TV, as well as a balcony or a patio that overlook the grounds. Each comes equipped with a private bathroom with shower and free toiletries.

Playa Del Carmen Airport is one mile away, while Cancun is around a one hour drive.
