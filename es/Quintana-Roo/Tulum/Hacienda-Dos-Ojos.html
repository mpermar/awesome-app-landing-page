Descripción

The rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. The rooms have a private bathroom, while some have a pool view. Guest rooms will provide guests with a closet and a coffee machine.

Guests at Hacienda Dos Ojos can enjoy a à la carte breakfast. The on-site restaurant specializes in Mexican and international cuisine.

The accommodation offers a hot tub.

Speaking English and Spanish, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical guidance on the area at the 24-hour front desk.

Bus station Tulum Ruins is 8 miles from Hacienda Dos Ojos, while Tulum Bus station is 10 miles from the property. Cozumel Airport is 32 miles away, and the property offers a free airport shuttle service.
