Descripción

Rooms at Las Estrellas are in traditional thatched cabins, with hardwood floors and mosquito nets. Each elegant room has a laptop safe, a fan and a private bathroom with free toiletries.

Villas Las Estrellas has a seafront restaurant, which serves a fusion of Mexican and Italian cuisine. You can also relax with a cocktail on the beach.

The hotel can arrange bike rental, diving and snorkeling trips and visits to the nearby Sian Ka’an Biosphere Reserve. Cancun Airport is a 90 minute drive away, and airport transportation can be organized upon request.
