Descripción

Ixchel Beach Hotel features an outdoor pool with the Kalak snack bar and grill. Guests can spend the day at the beach, then relax with a massage.

Activities such as deep sea fishing and scuba diving are available only moments from the Ixchel Beach. Guests can also go swimming with whale sharks or spend the day at the nearby Garrafon Nature Reserve.
