Descripción

The elegant and lavish suites are fully appointed and feature a DVD and CD library, personal concierge services from 08:00 until 17:00, a pillow menu and aromatherapy scents delivered in your room. The bright bedrooms offer mosquito nets over the canopy beds. The elegant bathrooms have double sinks and a shower. Some units have an outdoor shower or a hot tub.

El Dorado Casitas Royal has an extensive selection of different appealing cuisines from around the world including 10 different restaurants offering world cuisine, contemporary Italian menus, a flavor from the Pacific, a Mediterranean fusion, Caribbean style, and Mexican cuisine. Pizzas and authentic Italian cooking are also offered. There are also 10 swim-up bars, 1 beach bar and 4 lounge bars available.

Naay Spa is dividen into the Spa Royale and the Spa Casitas. Spa Royale is in the center of the property, while Spa Casitas is a 20,000 sq-ft facility offering an extensive menu of traditional and alternative spa treatments.

The Bonanza Ranch is the property's own stables with riding horses. There is a guided horseback tour through the jungle so guests can experience the true Riviera Maya beauty.
