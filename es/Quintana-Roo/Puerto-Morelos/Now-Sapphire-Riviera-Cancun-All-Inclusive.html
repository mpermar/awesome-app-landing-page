Descripción

The spa at Now Sapphire Riviera Cancun includes a Temazcal steam bath, private oceanfront treatment cabins and a fitness center.

The luxurious rooms have a private balcony or terrace with views of the Caribbean Sea or the tropical garden. The mini bar including soft drinks, bottled water and beer is replenished daily. 24-hour room service is also available.

Guests can choose from a range of cuisines at one of the resort’s 6 restaurants, including Mexican, French, Pan-Asian, Mediterranean, Seafood and fine steaks or an international buffet. There are also several outdoor bars and lounges, and live entertainment.

Now Sapphire offers a variety of outdoor activities, including beach and pool aerobics, tennis, eurobungee or watersports. A free green fee is available at a nearby golf course.

Cancún International Airport is 14 mi away.
