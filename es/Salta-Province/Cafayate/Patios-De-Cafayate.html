Descripción

All of the spacious guest rooms feature antique furniture, ceramic floors and artisan carpets. The rooms all have air conditioning and cable TV, and offer views of the mountains or landscaped grounds.

The Patios De Cafayate restaurant serves quality cuisine prepared using fresh local ingredients.

There is also an outdoor pool and terrace, and the largest golf course in South America is a 5-minute drive away. Guests can relax in the shaded galleries or in the property's courtyard.

Patios De Cafayate is just over 1 miles from Cafayate’s airport. Free on-site parking is available.
