Descripción

Units are equipped with a kitchen with a dining area, and a private bathroom with a free toiletries and shower. Some units include a seating area and/or a balcony.

A continental breakfast is served at the property.

The condo hotel has an outdoor pool.

A terrace and water sports facilities can be found at Coson Bay, along with a shared lounge.
