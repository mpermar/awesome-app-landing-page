Descripción

Las habitaciones están equipadas con aire acondicionado, TV vía satélite, reproductor de DVD, minibar y caja fuerte. Se proporcionan balcones privados y vestidores.

Sirarun Resort ofrece una variedad de instalaciones, que incluyen un gimnasio, karaoke y servicios de cuidado de niños. Los huéspedes pueden alquilar bicicletas o practicar deportes acuáticos como buceo y snorkel.

El restaurante del complejo sirve especialidades tailandesas.

El Sirarun Resort se encuentra a 2 horas en coche del aeropuerto internacional de Hua Hin.

08/01/2018

To sum up, couple of drawbacks, overall experience was absolutely positive!

"The location is perfect for people trying to escape crazy life of a megapolis - far away from any cities, quiet and secluded. That’s exactly what we’d been looking for and happy 100% with our choice. The bungalow was cosy, bright and spacious - living room with couches, table, kettle and tv; bedroom with kingsize bed; toilet with separate shower and an additional one in a small yard outside. There was more than enough room and we’re just happy that we decided to look through hotels only with bungalows (villas), not with a unit block.The best thing is that everything you need is within 1 min walk - your house, beach, pool, restaurant, gym - and the place itself is begging you to relax and forget about busy weekdays. The food was delicious, although the variety of dishes is somewhat modest (they can email you the menu if you ask them). The nearest village is 6 km to the south, there are couple of supermarkets and cafes there. You can get there in 30 min by bike, which hotel offers free of charge, or they can take you there by car for $10 one way. Overall there are something like 16 bungalows in a vast area, so privacy is guarantied. Some days we barely even met other guests at all. Personnel is kind and friendly always ready to help you out with everything. The sea is shallow. Child friendly.Make sure you download some stargazing apps - the view is breathtaking. If you’re an AVgeek, some planespotting apps too - there are some airways intersecting just over the place."
