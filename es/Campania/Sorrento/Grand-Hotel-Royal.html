Descripción

Las habitaciones ofrecen vistas al mar, a los jardines o al pueblo. Decorado de forma clásica con muebles de madera sorrentina, la mayoría incluye un baño de mármol y un balcón.

Durante la temporada alta, los huéspedes reciben acceso gratuito a la playa y uso gratuito de sombrillas, tumbonas y toallas de playa. En el interior, los salones cuentan con muebles antiguos, escaleras de mármol y lámparas de araña de cristal.

El Grand Hotel Royal tiene una experiencia gourmet completa. Incluye diferentes restaurantes y bares que sirven cocina regional y clásica italiana. Muchos ofrecen vistas panorámicas del paisaje circundante.

El hotel se encuentra a 5 minutos a pie de la estación de tren Circumvesuviana, que conecta Sorrento con Nápoles, a 48 km. Pompeya y el parque nacional del Vesubio se encuentran a 40 minutos en coche.

Experiencias

03/11/2017

An exceptional hotel that deserves the name 'Grand' with friendly and welcoming staff.

"The location of the hotel is exceptional on the cliff with views directly over the sea and Bay of Naples. It is extremely close to the old centre of Sorrento, the station for the Circumvesuviana railway and a short walk (downhill - public lift available for return) to the port for boats to Capri, Naples and elsewhere. The hotel has beautiful gardens in front, with ample seating on a couple of levels to enjoy the views overlooking the bay. There is a pleasant, though unheated, swimming pool and a deck beach below the cliff accessed via a lift (closed for swimming by the time time we stayed in late October). The hotel itself is a grand hotel with a handsome main building. The interiors match the exterior, the decor is opulent but in very good taste. All the staff were exceptionally friendly, helpful and courteous. Our two rooms were interconnecting in a small modern extension with our balcony overlooking the pool and the sea, accessed either through the garden or along internal corridors and stairs to the main building. It was perfect arrangement with grandparents in the 'superior' room and granddaughters in a 'standard' room (slightly smaller with view to the side). The rooms were well appointed, clean with air conditioning if needed and adequate storage space. The breakfast buffet was perfect. We had two evening meals in the hotel. The food was enjoyable if somewhat expensive compared with prices in restaurants in the centre of Sorrento and the menu not quite as child friendly. However, the ambience and views from the dining room justified the cost. The only complaint from our granddaughters was that the pool was cold (October) and unheated - though this did not deter them from swimming. Apart from this we all agreed that this hotel is ideal In its class and we'd love to stay here again."
