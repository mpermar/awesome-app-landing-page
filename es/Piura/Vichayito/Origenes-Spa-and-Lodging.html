Descripción

All rooms in the resort are equipped with a TV. A seating area is provided in all guest rooms, while certain rooms include a DVD player. Guest rooms will provide guests with an oven.

A continental breakfast is available every morning at Origenes Spa & Lodging.

Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.

Máncora is 5 miles from Origenes Spa & Lodging. Talara Airport is 32 miles away.
