Descripción

Todas las habitaciones de la Villa de Pico son amplias, luminosas y decoradas con detalles de estilo local. Todas están equipadas con baño privado, balcón con vistas al océano y TV de pantalla plana.

El desayuno se sirve todas las mañanas en el restaurante del hotel, especializado en platos locales e internacionales. Los huéspedes pueden disfrutar de un cóctel y una vista al mar en el bar bien surtido del hotel.

El personal de recepción está a su disposición las 24 horas y ofrece servicio de lavandería, paquetes turísticos y servicios de transporte. También hay una tienda de recuerdos en el establecimiento y hay aparcamiento gratuito disponible.

Villa de Pico Highland Beach Resort se encuentra a 20 minutos en coche de la ciudad de Puerto Galera, donde se encuentran bares, tiendas y tiendas de buceo.

24/02/2017

Peaceful Hillside Stay.

"There are really two places here: the best value is a row of fan rooms in Philippines style at backpacker rates--all very clean and rustic. Most with adjoining doors that would suit young families. The rest are standard rooms in thatched buildings some with air conditioning--a little expensive. The lounge and dining area are very good and open to the breeze. The Villa is on a hillside overlooking the ocean and the breeze keeps the rooms cool and the mosquitos away. The included breakfasts are simple and vary each day--eggs and toast or omelettes and toast, or pancakes. There is a small pool and steep steps to a stoney beach. It is a 15 minute walk to Puerto Galera and a tricycle or Jitney ride away from Sabang, and you can catch them right at the front door. Unless you are into rather crowded diving or the grotty night life, you wouldn't want to stay at Sabang, but the Puerto is interesting to visit, with several quite good restaurants. There is a rather ordinary Philippine lunch and dinner menu at the Villa, but large portions are served, and the cook kindly made several vegetarian meals to order for me in the evening. There was a nice family feel staying here, and the pleasure of daily conversations with the key staff, Paul, Rachael, and Julieta and her little daughter Sophia."
