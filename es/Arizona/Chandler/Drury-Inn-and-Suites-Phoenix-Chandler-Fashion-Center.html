Experiencias

20/05/2018

Happy hour not adult time

"A lot of families with children- more than 10 in the happy hour room and at breakfast.The kids were loud and messy, parents not caring to clean up. Nice hotel but because of the freebies- families end up there. I’ve raised my children and was looking forward to adult time during happy hour- we ended up leaving for a quieter place and forfeited our remaining drinks."

09/10/2017

Clean comfortable and modern hotel & friendly staff

"Very quiet floor, even with lots of kids and their parents in town for a large youth soccer tournament, the hotel asks you to sign sign an agreement of sorts to honor their “quiet after 10PM policy” that was really appreciated. The breakfast was fresh and tasty and really enjoyed their nightly guest & family “happy hour” with yummy snacks and assorted soft drink vending machine, coffee bar and for the adults, a wine bar. Thoughtful touches to have after a long day of sightseeing. Our room was average sized, clean fresh linens, bathroom was average sized with plenty of fresh towels. A mini fridge and microwave and coffee maker. Clear tv and cable reception, though can’t comment on WiFi as we didn’t use it."
