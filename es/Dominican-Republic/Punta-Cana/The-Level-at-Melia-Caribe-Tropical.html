Descripción

Each room is fully furnished and includes a flat-screen cable TV, air conditioning and a patio. There is also a coffee machine. The private bathroom includes bathrobes and slippers. Some unites have either a spa tub or a hot tub. Extras include a terrace, a minibar and a sitting area.

At The Level at Melia Caribe Tropical there is a restaurant, a tennis court and a fitness center. Other facilities at the property include water sports facilities, meeting facilities and a tour desk. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including golf, canoeing and mini golf. The property provides free parking.

Punta Cana International Airport is a 20-minute drive away.

Bavaro is a great choice for travelers interested in excursions, nightlife and water sports.
