Descripción

Guests can enjoy rooms with air conditioning, ceiling fan, safe, satellite TV, minibar and balconies overlooking the pool or garden. All bathrooms have a bathtub or shower, and housekeeping is available daily.

The 3 restaurants at Tropical Princess Beach Resort & Spa have a main buffet and Dominican and Brazilian cuisine; and the 7 bars serve national and international drinks. There is also a snack bar and a creperie.

The Areíto Discotheque provides free admission and free national drinks until midnight. This complex also features a tennis court, bicycles and table games. Non-motorized water sports and archery can be arranged.

Clubs for kids and teenagers are available. Internal transport and a free shuttle to Princess Tower Casino are also provided. Free Wi-Fi is available in the lobby.

Punta Cana International Airport is a 30-minute drive away.

Bavaro is a great choice for travelers interested in excursions, nightlife and water sports.
