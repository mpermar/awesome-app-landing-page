Descripción

The rooms have air conditioning, a flat-screen cable TV, minibar and a balcony with sea views. The bathrooms are private and include a rain shower.

Westin Puntacana Resort & Club has 8 restaurants offering international food and there is a pool-side barbecue. A lobby bar and Don Quicos hand-rolled cigar bar serving wine, cocktails and local rum specialties are featured as well.

Activities such as diving, fishing, paddle surfing, kite surfing and snorkeling can be arranged. This property features also a children’s playground, kid’s club and a souvenir shop.

Guests can enjoy access to Six Senses Spa and 45 holes of ocean-view, championship golf courses, including Corales and La Cana golf courses.

The complex is a 40-minute drive from the center of La Romana and a 5-minute drive from Punta Cana International Airport.
