Descripción

At the Paradisus Resort, there are activities for everyone. From kayaking and sailing, to dance classes and sushi-making, the range of activities within the resort ensures that there is never a dull moment.

All rooms are spacious, modern and include a flat-screen TV and private balcony. Every room features a 2-person spa tub.

Bavaro is a great choice for travelers interested in excursions, nightlife and water sports.
