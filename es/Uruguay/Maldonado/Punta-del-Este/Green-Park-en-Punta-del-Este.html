Descripción

Units include a balcony with garden views, a fully equipped kitchen and a private bathroom with bidet. There's also a oven, microwave and a coffee machine.

There's an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of Mediterranean and international dishes.

The apartment offers a hot tub.

Green Park en Punta del Este has a garden to relax in after a busy day, along with a a private beach area.

The Fingers beach is 9 miles from the accommodation, while Gorriti island is 7 miles away. The nearest airport is Capitán de Corbeta Carlos A. Curbelo International Airport, 3.1 miles from the property.

Solanas is a great choice for travelers interested in nature, outdoor activities and sandy beaches.
