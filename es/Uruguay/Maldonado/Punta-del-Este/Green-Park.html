Descripción

The apartment comes with 1 separate bedroom and includes a kitchen with an oven, microwave and dining area. A flat-screen TV is available.

Non-stop advice is available at the reception, where staff speak English and Spanish.

The wellness area at the apartment is comprised of a hot tub, a fitness center and an indoor pool. In addition to a year-round outdoor pool, Green Park also provides a children's playground and shared lounge.

Gorriti island is 7 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Capitán de Corbeta Carlos A. Curbelo International Airport, 3.1 miles from the property.

Solanas is a great choice for travelers interested in nature, outdoor activities and sandy beaches.
