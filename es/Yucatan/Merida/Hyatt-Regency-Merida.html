Descripción

The Hyatt Regency Merida offers contemporary rooms with a marble bath and plush robes. Satellite TV, an in-room coffee maker and a minibar are included.

Peregrine Bistro serves local and international cuisine while Amuza Social Food bar-restaurant serves creative Latin American dishes which are ideal for sharing. The lobby lounge, Cielito Lindo, offers specialty cocktails.

The Merida Hyatt Regency’s spacious pool facility includes a whirlpool and snack bar. Guests may also enjoy the fitness center and tennis courts.

The Merida Hyatt is less than 6.2 miles from the Lic M. Crecencio Rejon International Airport and 0.6 miles from the Anthropology Museum.
