Descripción

At the hotel, every room comes with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Selected rooms also feature a kitchenette with a microwave. The rooms at Mulen Hotel Tandil have air conditioning and a closet.

The accommodation offers a continental or buffet breakfast. There's an in-house restaurant, which specializes in international cuisine.

The property has a sauna and an indoor pool.

There's an on-site bar and guests can also use the business area.

Del Libertador Hill is a 10-minute walk from Mulen Hotel Tandil.
