Descripción

At the hotel, each room includes a closet and a flat-screen TV. Every room includes a private bathroom, while some feature a pool view. At Terrazas Club Hotel, all rooms have a seating area.

An American breakfast is available each morning at the accommodation.

Pinamar is 11 miles from Terrazas Club Hotel, while Mar de las Pampas is 6 miles away. Villa Gesell - Pinamar International Airport is 3.7 miles from the property.
