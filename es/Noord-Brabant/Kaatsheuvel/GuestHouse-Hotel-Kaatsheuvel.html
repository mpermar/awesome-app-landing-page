Descripción

Todas las habitaciones del hotel están equipadas con TV de pantalla plana. Las habitaciones del GuestHouse Hotel Kaatsheuvel cuentan con baño privado y conexión WiFi gratuita. Todas las habitaciones disponen de zona de estar.

El desayuno está disponible todas las mañanas e incluye opciones continentales, buffet y vegetarianas.

GuestHouse Hotel Kaatsheuvel dispone de un parque infantil. Los huéspedes del hotel podrán disfrutar de actividades en Kaatsheuvel y sus alrededores, como ciclismo.

El aeropuerto más cercano es el de Eindhoven, ubicado a 34 km.

Super funky modern hotel with good location to Efteling and local facilities.

"Beautiful clearly new built hotel, brilliantly decorated and kitted out with some seriously funky but functional furniture. The beds were comfortable but there was still enough space for all our luggage and our baby's cot. The room was air conditioned which was so welcome after a long hot day. The ensuite wet room is just that - don't leave anything on the floor it will float anyway! The external area looks great and full of games older children might enjoy. The free parking outside is ideal and is gated after 11pm which gives peace of mind. If you're using this hotel to go to Efteling I'd suggest parking up here and walking the 500m to the gates - it'll save you €10 on parking at Efteling park itself. There's a supermarket around the back of the hotel so great if you want to pick up fresh food to take as a picnic with you. For evening meals the hotel does have a restaurant downstairs but we went wandering and found Grill-IG a steakhouse about 15 minutes walk away and had ourselves very reasonably priced Dutch bittenballen and cold beers. It was a nice way to round the day up and enjoy the cooler night air. Again the hotel does offer breakfast at £10 per person, we decided to utilise the supermarket (around the back of the hotel) and the bakery across the street to set ourselves up for another day of adventures."
