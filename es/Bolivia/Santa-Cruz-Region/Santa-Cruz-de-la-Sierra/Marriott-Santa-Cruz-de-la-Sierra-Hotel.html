Descripción

The rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Every room includes a private bathroom with free toiletries. All guest rooms at Marriott Santa Cruz de la Sierra Hotel feature air conditioning and a desk.

A buffet breakfast is available each morning at the accommodation.

Guests can use the hot tub.

Zoo Fauna Southamerica is 1.1 miles from Marriott Santa Cruz de la Sierra Hotel. Viru Viru International Airport is 9 miles from the property.
