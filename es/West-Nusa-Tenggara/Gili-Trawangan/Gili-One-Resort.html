Descripción

Los alojamientos disponen de TV de pantalla plana, caja fuerte, terraza y baño privado con ducha. Se proporcionan sábanas y toallas.

Todas las mañanas se sirve un desayuno continental en el establecimiento. El restaurante del hotel sirve cocina indonesia. En los alrededores se pueden encontrar otras opciones gastronómicas.

Se puede practicar ciclismo y snorkel en las inmediaciones del establecimiento.

Los puntos de interés populares cerca del Gili One Resort incluyen el mercado de arte de Gili Trawangan, el puerto de Gili Trawangan y Sunset Point.

Experiencias

27/09/2017

Never again (until somebody professionally manage the property)...

"It was nice that they did not charge for childs breakfast, this was the norm in all other hotels we stayed in Gili T though. Sufficient air conditioning. Free bicycle rental. They serve afternoon munchies to your room (if you happen to be at the resort- but no table at the patio so you have to use patio fence as a table). Then again, although they knew we were staying as 3 persons, one day they served for 2, the other day for 3 next day for 2 portions. Good location which is away from hustle bustle of Gili T. They have a pool table which you can play (free of charge) but only if you can find a proper pool cue. There were 8-9 cues with only one usable, expected from an properly maintained resort only. The best for this resort is, there is another resort next to it just 30 meters away with awesome manager, very good food (we dined in 3 nights there) with very well trained personnel. (We tried to switch to that resort but unfortunately no room was available)"
