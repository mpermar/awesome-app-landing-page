Descripción

La famosa ciudad medieval amurallada de Dubrovnik se puede ver desde algunas terrazas de los hoteles. El hotel está a solo 3 millas del aeropuerto internacional de Dubrovnik, por lo que sus vacaciones pueden comenzar inmediatamente a su llegada.

A solo 11 millas de Dubrovnik, el Hotel Croacia es un punto de partida perfecto para descubrir la costa sur de Dalmacia.

Relájese en habitaciones completamente renovadas, la mayoría con impresionantes vistas al mar, habitaciones comunicadas, varios restaurantes y un asador.

Tome el sol en 2 playas o en las 2 piscinas. Relájese en el centro de spa y descubra senderos a lo largo de la costa.

One of many good hotels in this great location

"Bed very comfortable. View incredible if you get sea view. We didn't but was fine to back onto trees and a bit of sun first thing. I love that this is like a cliff for all the swifts and housemartins that fly around the building. Swimming pool is sea water and therefore can be colder than the sea, indoors and out. Nice facility but definitely go for sea swimming! Stunning location especially for sunsets. Saunas and steam room clean and worth a visit. Saunas have lovely sea views. Hotel rooms comparable with a Hilton in terms of layout and facilities. Cavtat is pefect for families or couples or singles. Very laid back and plenty of places to walk to to eat in the pretty harbour. Taverna Galija highly recommended for dinner, near the monastery on the harbour side. Carpaccio starters great, fish and meat varieties. Main fish dishes very good and the grilled chops are wonderful. The hotel is quite resorty and basically full of English and a few French and other nationalities. It's really close to the airport in a useful way. You could walk it but best to take a taxi. Fares are fixed at the airport at about £12-15 or 120 kuna. A bit of aircraft noise but only from the odd tourist flights coming and going. Easy to reach Dubrovnik from here. Much cheaper to use the taxi driver you came with rather than booking a transfer from the hotel which is likely to be significantly marked up. That said, it can be a bit expensive if you're going in regularly. A cheaper option is to take the number 10 bus from the bus station behind the harbour. It costs 25 kuna each way, so about three pounds. It does get very full and hot and it takes more than half an hour so it's not the most pleasant in the heat of the summer and not a great idea if you are carrying luggage. Go for the taxi in that case! The Atlas bus from the airport is a good and reliable option to get into Dubrovnik with luggage. I would definitely recommend the Hotel Croatia as a good five star hotel option."
