Descripción

Algunas unidades también tienen una cocina equipada con horno.

En el complejo turístico encontrará un restaurante que sirve cocina mediterránea, parrilla / barbacoa y mariscos.

Holiday Park Mimoza tiene un parque infantil.

El establecimiento dispone de una terraza y se puede practicar snorkel y windsurf en las inmediaciones del establecimiento.

Trieste se encuentra a 27 km del alojamiento, mientras que Portorož está a 8 km. El aeropuerto más cercano es el aeropuerto de Pula, a 72 km del Holiday Park Mimoza.

Experiencias

29/08/2017

A real rest

"This place was AMAZING, we loved every minute of our stay. Parking was few steps away from our house, so very convenient, and it was easy to come and go as you please (you get a remote key for the gate). There was a small playground our 8 year old loved, and the whole place was very family friendly and safe. The property is very close to the beach. Even though the beach is really nothing special look wise, we got used to it very quickly, and ended up liking all those unusual "features" we disliked at the beginning. The owner was very friendly, and happy to help. The house is equipped really well. There is an on site restaurant with reasonable prices, and great service, and you can take away the food to eat at the house. Really convenient on the days you don't fancy cooking or, as our kids (8 & 14) wanted pancakes one evening, it was great being able to let them walk over to the restaurant without any worries... We would recommend this place, and will definitely be back!"
