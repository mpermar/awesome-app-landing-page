Descripción

Hilton Lima Miraflores has chic rooms with air conditioning, luxury linens, flat-screen TVs and private bathrooms with showers. Some of them feature executive lounge access and elegant Peruvian-colonial-inspired décor.

Guests can treat themselves to Peruvian-American dishes or relax with a drink in the outdoor terrace.

Jorge Chavez International Airport is a 40-minute drive away and there is free private parking on site.

Miraflores is a great choice for travelers interested in gourmet food, friendly locals and traditional food.
