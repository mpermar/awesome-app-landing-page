Descripción

The rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kitchen, a dining area and a private bathroom with a bath or shower. All guest rooms have a closet.

When guests need guidance on where to visit, the reception will be happy to provide advice.

Santa Cruz Bay is 2,100 feet from Hotel Boutique Casa Blanca Del Sol. The nearest airport is Huatulco International Airport, 9 miles from the property.
