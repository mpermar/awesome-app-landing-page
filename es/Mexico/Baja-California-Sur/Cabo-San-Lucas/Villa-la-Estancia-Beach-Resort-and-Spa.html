Descripción

The Villa la Estanica Cabo San Lucas features villas that include satellite TVs and sitting areas on their spacious balconies. Some villas have full kitchens and dining areas.

Guests at the Villa la Estancia can relax in the hot tub or swim in the outdoor pool. La Casona restaurant is located next to the pool and serves drinks.

The Cabo San Lucas Villa guests can shop for groceries and other necessities at the on-site store. The resort also has a billiards table.
