Descripción

At the hotel, rooms include a desk and a flat-screen TV. The private bathroom is equipped with a shower. Guest rooms at Hotel HBlue have air conditioning and a closet.

A buffet or American breakfast is served at the property.

The accommodation has a terrace.

The nearest airport is Manuel Marquez de Leon International Airport, 6 miles from Hotel HBlue.
