Descripción

The rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a coffee machine, a shower, free toiletries and a closet. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom, while some accommodations at the hotel also feature a balcony. Guest rooms at Grand Gardenia come with a seating area.

A buffet breakfast is served daily at the property.

Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Atlixco, like cycling.

The nearest airport is Hermanos Serdán International Airport, 19 miles from the property.
