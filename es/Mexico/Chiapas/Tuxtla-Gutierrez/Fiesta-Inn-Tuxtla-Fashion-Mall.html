Descripción

All guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Every room has a private bathroom. All rooms at Fiesta Inn Tuxtla Fashion Mall have air conditioning and a desk.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool.

Fiesta Inn Tuxtla Fashion Mall offers a business center for guest use.

San Marcos Cathedral is 2.8 miles from the hotel. The nearest airport is Ángel Albino Corzo International Airport, 18 miles from Fiesta Inn Tuxtla Fashion Mall.
