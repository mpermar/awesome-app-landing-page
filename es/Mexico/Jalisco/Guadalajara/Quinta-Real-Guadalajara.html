Descripción

Quinta Real features antique-style furniture, original works of art, and ivy-covered, pink stone walls. Relax in the outdoor swimming pool in the hot afternoon sun, then enjoy a delicious meal in the gourmet restaurant.

The accommodations offer marble bathrooms and bathtubs, his and hers bathrobes and slippers, pillow menu and Hermès bath accessories to complete the Quinta Real experience.
