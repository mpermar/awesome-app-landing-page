Descripción

Costa Sur Resort & Spa features access to non-motorized water sports. There is a fitness center and spa services at the hotel. Concierge services are available as well.

Guest suites at the Costa Sur come with cable TV and sofa beds. Ocean views are also offered in all suites.

Mediterranean cuisine is served on the hotel's Sunset Terrace. La Cascada Restaurant serves international cuisine. Guests can have a drink in the hotel's Barefoot Bar.
