Descripción

The Westin Resort and Spa Puerto Vallarta has everything needed for an unforgettable stay. Relax with a beachside massage, browse for gifts at the souvenir shop, plan the next's day's activities with concierge desk or enjoy a gourmet meal at one of the on-site restaurants.

There are a variety of activities and attractions surrounding the Westin Puerto Vallarta. Explore the charming Marina Vallarta waterfront, filled with art galleries, shops and entertainment, view exciting wildlife or participate in a number of water sports.

Marina Puerto Vallarta is a great choice for travelers interested in relaxation, restaurants and friendly locals.
