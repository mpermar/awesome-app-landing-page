Descripción

There is always something to do when staying at the Crown Paradise Vallarta. With endless water activities only steps away, daily activities including dance lessons and nightly entertainment, there is something for everyone. The property also has outdoor walkways.

La Palapa is the buffet restaurant featured at the Crown Paradise Golden. Bar services are provided for adult guests in the pool and hot tub area.

The Crown Paradise Vallarta resort features a variety of modern amenities and facilities. The on-site travel agency, beauty salon and gift shop provide guests with all of the comforts of home and more. The meeting rooms all offer ocean views.
