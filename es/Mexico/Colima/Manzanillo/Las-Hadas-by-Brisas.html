Descripción

Las Hadas is in one of the most beautiful spots on Mexico's Pacific Coast, Manzanillo Bay. You'll feel like you're living a dream come true as you walk through its streets and Arabian architecture. Las Hadas has been ranked among the best 100 Golf Resorts in the world and is proudly a AAA 4-diamond hotel.
