Descripción

Guest rooms come with a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a coffee machine, a bath, free toiletries and a desk. Rooms come with a electric tea pot, while some have a balcony. The rooms include a seating area.

The hotel offers a buffet or à la carte breakfast.

A variety of popular activities are available in the area around Fairmont Mayakoba Riviera Maya All Inclusive, including cycling and canoeing.

Speaking English, Spanish and French, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical advice on the area at the reception.

ADO International Bus Station is 6 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Cozumel Airport, 14 miles from Fairmont Mayakoba Riviera Maya All Inclusive.
