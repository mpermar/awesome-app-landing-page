Descripción

The rooms at BlueBay Grand Esmeralda overlook the sea, and include satellite TV. They come with a minibar and safe.

Guests can enjoy water sports such as snorkeling and windsurfing at this Playa del Carmen resort. A gym and sauna are on the property. A game room and kids club are available.

The shops and restaurants of Playa del Carmen’s Fifth Avenue are only 15 minutes from BlueBay Grand Esmeralda. Cancun is less than an hour from this resort.
