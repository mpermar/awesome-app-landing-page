Descripción

Guest suites at the Zoetry Paraiso de la Bonita - Endless Privileges include a mini-bar and cable TV with a DVD player. The suites feature a daily fruit basket and bottle of champagne. A handmade beach bag and bottle of tequila are also included.

AAA Five Diamond Award winner Zoetry Paraiso de la Bonita - Endless Privileges also offers daily excursions and trips. The resort can arrange tours to the Sian Ka’an biosphere. Nature hikes, cooking classes and wine appreciation classes are also offered at the resort.

The Zoetry Paraiso de la Bonita - Endless Privileges is 7 minutes by car from the world’s largest reef where guests can go snorkeling. The resort offers a 4-person boat for sailing. Grand Coral Riviera Maya Golf Course is also nearby and has free green fees for guests.
