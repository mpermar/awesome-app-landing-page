Descripción

Each suite at the Hotel Marina El Cid Spa & Beach Resort Cancun Riviera Maya is furnished with a minibar, coffee maker and provides satellite TV. Guests will also find a hairdryer, ironing facilities and a work desk in each suite.

Four restaurants are available at the Hotel Marina El Cid Spa & Beach Resort. Mediterranean, casual international, Mexican and seafood dishes are offered at the hotel.

The Hotel Marina Beach Resort contains a convention center and a gym. The outdoor pool features water slides and rock formations. An adults-only pool is also on site.

El Cid Spa Hotel Marina & Beach Resort is less than one mile from the charming, bohemian fishing village of Puerto Morelos. It is 30 minutes from Playa del Carmen.
