Descripción

The rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Selected rooms also feature a kitchen with a microwave. Guest rooms feature a closet.

Hotel & Suites Oasis Bacalar has a terrace.

Chetumal is 14 miles from the accommodation, while Corozal is 20 miles away. The nearest airport is Chetumal International Airport, 13 miles from the property.
