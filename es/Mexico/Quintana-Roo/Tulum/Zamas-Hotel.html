Descripción

Each bright cabin has stylish décor with charming rustic touches. Mosquito nets and fans are provided, and all cabins have a private bathroom. You can relax in a hammock on the balcony.

Massages and beauty treatments can be booked at Zama Hotel’s front desk. The staff can also arrange local excursions or airport transportation for an extra fee.

The Mayan ruins of Tulum are just 3 miles from Hotel Zamas, and the Sian Ka’an Biosphere Reserve is 3.5 miles away. Cancun Airport is a 90-minute drive from the hotel.
