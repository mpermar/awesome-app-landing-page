Descripción

GR Solaris Cancun All Inclusive features a spa, fully equipped gym, and day and night activity programs for all ages.

GR Solaris Cancun is considered the luxury version of Hoteles Solaris Resorts, featuring 300 deluxe rooms.

A variety of dining options are available at the 5 restaurants serving 10 different types of cuisine per week, and room service is provided 24/7. There are bars which serve unlimited alcoholic and non alcoholic selected beverages.
