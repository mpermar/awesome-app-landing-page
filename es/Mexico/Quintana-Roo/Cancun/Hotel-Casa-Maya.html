Descripción

Hotel Casa Maya offers spacious, air-conditioned Junior Suites with terraces overlooking the sea. They all include cable TV, a private bathroom and a kitchenette. The hotel also offers studios with cable TV, a private bathroom and a small kitchenette.

The hotel has 2 restaurants, La Veranda, which serves a delicious international buffet, and Las Brisas Restaurant, offering a variety of Mexican food, seafood dishes as well as international cuisine.

Other facilities at this hotel include a snack bar, a 24-hour front desk and a fitness center.

Hotel Casa Maya is only a 5 minute drive from Pok-ta-pok golf course, shopping malls, restaurants and night clubs.

Cancun International Airport is a 25 minute drive from the hotel.

Good beach location before the crowded Hotel Zone

"The hotel is in a good location if you want to be on the beach but away from all the commotion of the Hotel Zone. Th hotel is at the very beginning of the Hotel Zone and there is much less traffic noise. The hotel seems to cater mainly to Mexican tourists. We had a junior suite on the third floor. Our corner suite had a view to the beach/pool and the lagoon. There were two balconies one off the living room and the other off the bedroom. The suite is very big and had a kitchen, living room, dining area, bedroom with a king bed and a bathroom. There was plenty of lighting in all the rooms and several outlets in the kitchen to charge electronics and cameras.The room staff was very efficient and brought extra towels almost immediately upon request. There is no valet parking but the free parking lot is just next to the hotel entrance and secure.There are two swimming pools. The main pool is always crowded and filled with kids. We read on Booking.com that there is another smaller pool next to the exercise room. This was perfect for sunning and relaxing. It even has a big palapa with four hammocks. Unfortunately you have to get and return pool towels at the main pool. There are lots of lounge chairs with palapas on the beach. The sand is sugar powder white. There are two restaurants one in the hotel and an open sided one next to the beach/pool. We had to ask which one was open since only one seems to be open at a time. I would recommend the cheeseburger at the beach side restaurant. The buffet was only OK. There is also a bar on the beach.Overall, the hotel is ideally located on the beach with a good selection of food, quiet rooms, good free wifi in the rooms, free self parking. It's also easy to drive to the Cancun airport, especially if you have a GPS to take you on the side roads."
