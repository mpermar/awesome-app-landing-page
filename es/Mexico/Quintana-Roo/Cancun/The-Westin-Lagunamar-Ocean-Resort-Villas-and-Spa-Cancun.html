Descripción

The Westin Lagunamar Ocean features 2 spacious outdoor pools and a state-of-the-art fitness center. Guests can spend the day at the hotel's private beach taking part in a variety of the hotel's various activities or exploring the area on a guided tour. After a busy day, a massage at the Heavenly Spa by Westin to relax.

The guest accommodations at the Lagunamar Ocean Westin Resort offer views of the Caribbean Sea or Nichupte Lagoon. Guests will enjoy satellite TV and high-speed wireless internet access as well as plush bathrobes and the luxurious Westin Heavenly Bed.
