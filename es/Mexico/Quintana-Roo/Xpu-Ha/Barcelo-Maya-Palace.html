Descripción

On-site at the Barcelo Maya Palace guests can discover a large variety of amenities, including 7 different restaurants, a full-service spa and a nightclub. The resort also features several spacious swimming pools as well as hot tubs, and a children's play area.

The Maya Palace also features several on-site tennis courts along with free snorkeling equipment hire, kayaks, and water aerobics classes. Guests will also enjoy the resort's mini-golf course and live nightly entertainment.

Cancun International Airport is 49 mi away.
