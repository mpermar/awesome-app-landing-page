Descripción

Guests staying at the Barcelo Maya Beach can enjoy dining at 5 different restaurants, with both buffet and menu options. The resort also features 2 large swimming pools along with a playground, souvenir shops and boutique stores.

The Barcelo Maya Beach also has a recently renovated main lobby, lobby bar, club premium lounge and guest service area. It's new restaurant "Mexico Lindo" serves delicious local dishes.

A large variety of recreational activities are also available at the Maya Beach Barcelo. With a basketball court, free diving lessons, water aerobics and pool tables, there is something for everyone.

- 26/12/2017 -

A good trip but am not sure we'd go back because of the noise factor.

"On the positive side, the Barcelo is a huge resort that has been nicely designed - especially the public/collective areas. There are about 8 'hotels' each with different names and their own lobbies, but they are all connected within the complex. It is well staffed, well organized, and well maintained. We found the staff to be helpful and nice. The resort is on the Mayan Riviera near Playa del Carmen, so about an hour drive away from Cancun. The beach was clean and well equipped with lots of beach chairs. There are many food options all day within the complex. The nicest in our opinion was the outdoor, roofed "snack bar", which is where we ate most of our meals. Despite the name you could eat an enormous, varied meal there if you chose to, with plenty of options (i.e. my partner is a meat-lover and I am a vegetarian who prefers to avoid wheat/bread - we were both happy with what we could eat there). But there is no shortage of restaurants where you can book dinner, and also a huge indoor buffet for breakfast, lunch, and dinner if you don't want to sit outside. Beyond these options, it's also easy to get coffee, cocktails, drinks, etc all day long if that's what you are after. We never sat through an entire evening show but the nightly entertainment was good. These were professional performers - they even staged a truncated version of the Nutcracker ballet, on point, while we were there. We used the gym about four times during our week stay and really liked it. The extensive pools were a welcome option given that the beach was (for us - we are not strong swimmers) too rocky and the sea too rough for safe swimming. We saw families, couples, gay couples and groups of friends and the place seemed very open to and safe for all kinds of visitors, of all ages - this was a nice aspect of the trip."
