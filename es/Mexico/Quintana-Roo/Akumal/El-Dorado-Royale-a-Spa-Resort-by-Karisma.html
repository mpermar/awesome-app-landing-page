Descripción

The elegant suites and rooms at El Dorado Royal offer an indoor hot tub, bathrobes, cable TV, a furnished terrace with a small table, 2 chairs and a hammock upon request, plus a private bathroom including a hairdryer. The minibar is daily stocked with beer, soft drinks and water, plus there is a coffee maker available.

The Gourmet Inclusive Experience includes flavors from the international cuisine, contemporary Italian dishes, Pacific flavors, Caribbean selection, authentic Mexican delights, traditional Italian and gourmet pizzas, among others. There are 11 different places from which guests can choose to dine, plus 5 lounge bars and 3 swim-up bars.

Naay Spa is divided into Spa Royale and Spa Casitas. Both feature an extensive menu of traditional and alternative spa treatments. Other activities that guests can enjoy at this resort include diving lessons and the rental of the equipment, a gift shop on site, Wi-Fi access, tennis lessons and wave runners to cruise the sea.
