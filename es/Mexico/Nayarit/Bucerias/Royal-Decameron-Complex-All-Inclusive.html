Descripción

The air-conditioned rooms at the Royal Decameron Puerto Vallarta feature colonial-inspired design and bright colors. Each one comes with a TV and a private balcony with ocean or garden views.

The Royal Decameron has 7 restaurants, ranging from Japanese food to Italian food. A snack bar is also on site.

The hotel provides scuba diving lessons in the pool, and you can hire non-motorized water sports equipment for free. There are also nightly shows, as well as children’s activity program.

The hotel is about a short drive from the Flamingos Golf Club and a 20-minute drive from Puerto Vallarta International Airport. Mezcales city center is 3.5 miles away.
