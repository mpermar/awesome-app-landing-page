Descripción

Emporio Ixtapa rooms include a private bathroom and air conditioning. The hotel offers 24-hour room service to guests.

There are 2 buffet restaurants at the Emporio. The Condimento Restaurant features Mexican and International buffets. The hotel has a pool bar, which features snacks, drinks and beach-side service.

The Ixtapa Emporio features activities for children and adults, including bike tours, tennis and volleyball.

The Emporio hotel is within a 5 minute drive of Zihuatanejo, which features shopping. Guests can find dining across the street from the hotel.
