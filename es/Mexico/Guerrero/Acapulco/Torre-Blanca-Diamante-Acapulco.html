Descripción

This air-conditioned 2-bedroom apartment comes with a seating area, a cable flat-screen TV and a kitchen with an oven.

The apartment offers a range of wellness facilities including a hot tub and a fitness center. A car rental service is available at Torre Blanca Diamante Acapulco.

Acapulco Navy Historic Museum is 4 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is General Juan N Alvarez Airport, 6 miles from Torre Blanca Diamante Acapulco.
