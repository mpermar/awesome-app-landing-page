Descripción

All rooms in the hotel are equipped with a coffee machine. Every room is equipped with a flat-screen TV, and certain units at Hotel Villas Layfer have a garden view. All guest rooms include bed linen.

The accommodation provides a laundry service, as well as business facilities like fax and photocopying.

Orizaba is 10 miles from Hotel Villas Layfer. The nearest airport is General Heriberto Jara Airport, 54 miles from the property.
