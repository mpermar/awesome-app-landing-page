Descripción

At the hotel, rooms have a patio. The rooms include a private bathroom and a shower, while some rooms have a terrace. All rooms will provide guests with a desk and a coffee machine.

A continental, à la carte or American breakfast is served at the property. At Hotel Layfer Centro you'll find a restaurant serving international cuisine.

You can play ping pong and pool at the accommodation, and car rental is available.

Guests have access to the on-site business center where they can check the newspapers or use the fax machine and photocopier.

Orizaba is 11 miles from Hotel Layfer Centro, while Nogales is 16 miles from the property. The nearest airport is General Heriberto Jara Airport, 52 miles from the hotel.
