Descripción

The rooms at Gran Hotel Diligencias include cable TV and free Wi-Fi. A safety deposit box is provided.

A buffet breakfast is served daily at this Veracruz hotel. A 24-hour gym and sauna are located on-site. The hotel hosts a restaurant and bar specializing in seafood.

Veracruz General Heriberto Jara International Airport is 15 minutes from Gran Hotel Diligencias. The San Juan de Ulúa Museum is also 15 minutes away from the hotel.
