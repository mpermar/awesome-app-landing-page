Descripción

Suites have contemporary décor and include flat-screen cable TV and an iPod dock. They all have a balcony, some with city views. The kitchenette has a microwave and a Nespresso coffee machine.

The Las Suites complex features a gym and a spa area with a hot tub and steam bath. A range of relaxing massages is available upon request from the 24-hour front desk.

The National Auditorium is a 10-minute walk from the hotel, and you can walk to Chapultepec Park in around 20 minutes. Just over a quarter mile away, Presidente Mazarik Street is full of stylish shops.

Las Suites is a 20-minute walk from Auditorio Metro Station. Free private parking is available on site.
