Descripción

Hotel Casa San Angel rooms include a private bathroom, filtered water and traditional hammocks. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning and free Wi-Fi.

The Casa San Angel was originally the home of Graciano Ricalde, mathematician and scientist. Guests at the hotel can shop for traditional Mexican crafts in the on-site boutiques.

The Casa San Angel features the Tala Bistro, which offers Mexican cuisine and homemade desserts.

Hotel Casa San Angel is within a mile of the Plaza Grande. The Parque de Centenario Zoologico (The Centennial Park Zoo) is within a 10-minute walk of the hotel.

"The hotel is lovely. The staff was extremely friendly and helpful. The public areas of the hotel are welcoming and comfortable.
