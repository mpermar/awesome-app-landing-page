Descripción

All guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Every room has a private bathroom with a shower, free toiletries and a hair dryer. All rooms feature a closet.

An American breakfast is served at the property.

Casa 1810 Hotel Boutique provides an ironing service, as well as business facilities like fax and photocopying. Staff at the 24-hour front desk can give tips on the area.

Public library is 600 feet from the accommodation. Quétaro Intercontinental Airport is 41 miles away.
