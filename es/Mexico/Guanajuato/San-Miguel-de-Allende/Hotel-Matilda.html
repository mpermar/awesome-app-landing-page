Descripción

The rooms and suites are decorated with modern Mexican photography. The large bathrooms have double sinks.

The spa offers a range of massages and treatments, and includes a Turkish bath. It also has a fitness center.

The Matilda’s restaurant offers a range of Mexican food by the well-known chef Enrique Olvera. The bar offers cocktails which can also be enjoyed on the shaded terrace.

San Miguel de Allende is a UNESCO World Heritage Site in the central highlands of Mexico. The property offers free on-site parking. Airport shuttle service can be arranged upon request. The property offers bike rental.
