Descripción

At the bed and breakfast, rooms include a closet. All rooms come with a private bathroom with a bath and selected rooms contain a terrace.

An American breakfast is served daily at the property. There's an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of local dishes and also offers dairy-free and gluten-free options.

Tu Casa En El Parque has a playground. You can play ping pong at the accommodation, and bike rental and car rental are available.

Staff at the reception can provide information on what to do in the area.

Popular points of interest near Tu Casa En El Parque include Benito Juarez Park, The view point and La Casona. The nearest airport is Quétaro Intercontinental Airport, 41 miles from the property.
