Descripción

At the hotel, rooms contain a desk and a flat-screen TV. All rooms include a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hair dryer.

The daily breakfast offers continental, buffet or American options. There's an on-site restaurant, which serves international cuisine.

There's an in-house bar and guests can also use the business area.

Expo Forum Convention Centre is 1.2 miles from Courtyard by Marriott Hermosillo, while Heroe de Nacozari Stadium is 6 miles from the property. The nearest airport is General Ignacio P Garcia Airport, 7 miles from the accommodation.
