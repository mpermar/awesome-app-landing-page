Descripción

Esta casa señorial del siglo XIII fue el hogar del duque de Lucca. Las habitaciones incluyen aire acondicionado, conexión inalámbrica a internet y minibar, mientras que algunas tienen techos con vigas de madera y lujosos papeles pintados.

El atento personal ofrece un desayuno bufé internacional cada mañana, que se sirve en la terraza cuando hace buen tiempo. Los huéspedes pueden disfrutar de descuentos en los restaurantes asociados cercanos.

La Principessa se encuentra a 4,8 km del centro de Lucca. Pisa se encuentra a 20 minutos en coche y el hotel dispone de aparcamiento gratuito.

"We are returning guest from NYC/Budapest. It is an enchanted and charmingly beautiful place. This villa/hotel is a real classy retreat. It is far from the noisy beach crowd of the Versilia beaches but close enough, 19 km plus, to hop in your car and get there in a short time. Couple of minutes from Lucca.The building is situated in a huge garden, with a nice pool with bar service, etc. The rooms and the entire building has a smell of the early XVIII century aristocracy, high ceilings, antique walltapestry, fireplace, paintings, etc. so if you are looking for sonething modern and high tech comfy, this is not your place.Not sure this is the right place for families with young children. Kids might find it boring and stuffy. Which it is not really. So if you are looking for a nice relaxing place in a beautiful building, in a huge wonderful garden with startling vegetation, with a very helpful, smiling, and polite staff, and feel like an Aristocrat in the 18th century, I would recommend this villa. (In fact I made a mistake with booking, arrived one day too early, and the reception lady went out of her way to find the best solution and best room). The extra charm and proof of quality is that you meet people, very international, from previous years."
