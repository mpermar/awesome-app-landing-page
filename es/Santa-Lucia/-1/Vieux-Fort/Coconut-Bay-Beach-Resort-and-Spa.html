- 16/02/2018 -

Relaxing weekend. I think I gained 10lbs! Lol

"Great facilities for families. Kids enjoyed the slides immensely especially as they couldn’t use the beach due to the rough waters. The food options were wide and delicious. From our experience the only restaurant that did not meet our expectations was Silk which served Asian cuisine. Though the staff went out of their way to fry wantons for my son (this was not on the menu), the food here was maybe a 7/10. Many families complained though that by the time they got to the resort, restaurants were already booked up and they were forced to eat all their meals at the main restaurant as this one did not require reservations. Perhaps a thought might be to call ahead of your arrival to make restaurant reservations especially if traveling with little kids so that you can secure the seating time that serves you best. The food at the Jerk Treehouse is highly recommended. Simple but tasty offerings. All staff were friendly, courteous and accommodating to the best of their ability. The staff at the pool grill though did not seem as welcoming. Perhaps their long hours and constant busy atmosphere took a toll on their friendliness. The concierge staff were all awesome. From the moment we arrived we were met by Alden who took care of our every need during our stay. He and his colleagues at that desk do a fabulous job. A true asset to this establishment."
