Descripción

Hotel Quadrifolio rooms have high ceilings, and large windows that overlook a lush garden. Rooms come equipped with a flat-screen TV, minibar, and iPod docking station.

Guests of the Quadrifolio can relax on the roof-top terrace in the hydro-massage tub and enjoy city views. Hotel Quadrifolio also has a speedboat that can be rented for day excursions.

The Caribbean Sea is only 500 feet from Hotel Quadrifolio. Rafael Núñez International Airport is a 20 minute ride away.

Centro is a great choice for travelers interested in architecture, food and history.
