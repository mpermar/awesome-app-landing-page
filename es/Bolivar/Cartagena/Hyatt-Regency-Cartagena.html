Descripción

At the hotel, rooms contain a desk and a flat-screen TV. With a private bathroom, certain rooms at Hyatt Regency Cartagena also feature a sea view. Guest rooms at the accommodation feature air conditioning and a closet.

A buffet breakfast is available every morning at Hyatt Regency Cartagena. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of international dishes.

The hotel has an outdoor pool.

San Felipe de Barajas Castle is 1.2 miles from Hyatt Regency Cartagena, while Cartagena's Walls is 1.4 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Rafael Núñez International Airport, 3.1 miles from the property.
