Descripción

Guest rooms in Hotel Monaguillo de Getsemaní are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Every room includes a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries. All rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

An American breakfast is served daily at the property.

Hotel Monaguillo de Getsemaní offers 5-star accommodations with a hot tub.

With staff speaking Spanish and English, round-the-clock advice is available at the reception.

Palace of the Inquisition is an 8-minute walk from Hotel Monaguillo de Getsemaní, while San Felipe de Barajas Castle is a 10-minute walk away. The nearest airport is Rafael Núñez International Airport, 2.5 miles from the property.

Getsemani is a great choice for travelers interested in culture, architecture and city walks.
