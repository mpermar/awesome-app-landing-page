Descripción

Each air-conditioned room comes with a fully equipped marble bathroom. There are tea and coffee amenities in every room along with a minibar.

The choice of restaurants and bars includes Las Chivas Restaurant, which overlooks the sea and serves regional and international cuisine. The beach-side Tibabuyes Kiosk offers grilled meats, ceviche and light snacks in a laid-back atmosphere.

Hilton Cartagena has 3 tennis courts. For relaxation, guests are welcome to visit the on-site spa center offering sauna, Turkish bath and massage treatments.

Hilton Cartagena Hotel is a 10 minute drive from the historic center of the city.

Laguito is a great choice for travelers interested in beaches, ancient landmarks and city walks.
