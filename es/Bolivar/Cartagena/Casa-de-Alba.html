Descripción

Guest rooms at the hotel come with a flat-screen TV and a kitchen. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, and some rooms at Casa de Alba have a balcony. The rooms will provide guests with a private bathroom.

A continental breakfast is served at the property.

Staff at reception can lend a hand around the clock with guidance on the area.

Cartagena's Walls is 0.7 miles from the accommodation, while San Felipe de Barajas Castle is a 19-minute walk from the property. The nearest airport is Rafael Núñez International Airport, 3.1 miles from the property.

Centro is a great choice for travelers interested in architecture, food and history.
