Descripción

All rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. With a private bathroom fitted with a bath, rooms at Now Amber Puerto Vallarta MX also boast free WiFi, while some rooms also feature a terrace. The rooms feature a desk.

A buffet breakfast can be enjoyed at the property. Guests can dine in the in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of Grill/bbq dishes.

Guests can make use of the hot tub.

For guests' convenience, the accommodation has a business centre. Staff at the 24-hour front desk can help guests with any queries that they may have.

Family Friendly hotel, please see fine print for additional information.

The nearest airport is Lic. Gustavo Diaz Ordaz Airport, 3.1 miles from Now Amber Puerto Vallarta MX.
