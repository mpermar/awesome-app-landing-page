Descripción

Guest suites at Sunset Plaza Beach Resort & Spa feature satellite TV and an iPod docking station in every air-conditioned guest room. A balcony is also provided.

Recreational facilities at the Sunset Plaza Beach include a hot tub, tennis courts and a gym. Guests can also enjoy water sports, including snorkeling, on site.
