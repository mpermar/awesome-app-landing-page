Descripción

All suites have a flat-screen TV with cable channels, tea and coffee-making facilities and a safe. The bathrooms include free toiletries and a hairdryer.

The on-site Flor de Aguacate restaurant specializes in exquisite regional and international cuisine. Continental and buffet breakfasts are available for an extra fee.

Expo Guadalajara and the World Trade Center are a 5-minute drive from Áurea Hotel and Suites, and Guadalajara Airport is a 40-minute drive away. Shuttle services can be arranged by the hotel for an extra fee.
