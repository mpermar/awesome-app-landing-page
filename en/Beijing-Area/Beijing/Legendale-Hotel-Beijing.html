Description

Legendale Hotel is a 5-minute walk from Dengshikou Subway Station, a 15-minute drive from Forbidden City and Tiananmen Square. Beijing International Airport is a 30-minute drive away.

Classy European charm revives in all units, manifested in the antique furniture, patterned carpeting and delicate chandeliers. With stylish modern decoration, the spacious rooms feature air conditioning with shower facilities and a bathtub. They are also equipped with a flat-screen TV, DVD player and mini-bar.

Guests of Legendale can go for a relaxing massage in the hotel. To keep fit, a refreshing swim or workout sessions in the gym are nice options.

The hotel’s 3 restaurants serve a variety of Macanese, Portuguese and French dishes. Guests can enjoy light snacks at the L Café and the lobby lounge.

Dongcheng is a great choice for travelers interested in monuments, sightseeing and temples.
