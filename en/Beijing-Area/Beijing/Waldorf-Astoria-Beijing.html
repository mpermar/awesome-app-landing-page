Description

Expansive and contemporary with a Chinese flavor, the rooms with free Wi-Fi are fully controlled by a Samsung Tablet and are also equipped with a flat-screen HDTV and Apple TV. The marble bathrooms include double vanities, bathtub and Ferragamo toiletries. Guests also enjoy True Waldorf Service, a personal concierge service that can take care of every need and request, at any point in time.

The Waldorf Astoria Spa offers a range of signature treatments and features 7 private spa suites. Guests can also refresh themselves with a workout at the fitness center or relax at the heated indoor swimming pool, which also has a whirlpool.

Zijin Mansion features Cantonese and Beijing specialties, and Waldorf signature dishes can be savored at Brasserie 1893, which features an impressive show kitchen. An apertif or nightcap can be enjoyed at the sophisticated Peacock Alley, which serves an impressive menu of cocktails.

Waldorf Astoria Beijing is a short 5-minute walk from the Wangfujing Pedestrian Street, a 10-minute drive from Tian'anmen Square and the Forbidden City. Dengshikou subway station (Line 5) is around 700 feet from the hotel, and Dongdan subway station (Line 1) is just over half a mile away, which provides easy access to many popular attractions.

Dongcheng is a great choice for travelers interested in monuments, sightseeing and temples.
