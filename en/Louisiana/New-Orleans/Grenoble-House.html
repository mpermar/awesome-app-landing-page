"When we booked the two bedroom suite for a week, we had no idea they did not allow children (lots of stairs and a pool without a gate Vic explained upon arrival). We were warmly welcomed despite this and very pleased with our stay. The staff here is the very best. I've stayed at several hotels in the French Quarter over the years. Everyone is kind and helpful. They had excellent dinner suggestions (which was helpful since we had to forgo all fine dining). Mark was my favorite staff member, although everyone is amazing. The suite itself was beautiful, spacious, clean, with a full kitchen. We had more than enough space and were very comfortable. The beds were great and the noise level was surprisingly low, even on busier nights. I look forward to another great stay here in the future sans kiddos."
