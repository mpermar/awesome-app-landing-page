Description

Each unit at Drury Inn & Suites LaFayette has a flat-screen TV with premium movie channels for in-room entertainment. All guest rooms have a microwave, a refrigerator and coffee-making facilities.

LaFayette Drury Inn provides 24-hour access to its business and fitness centers. Guests can swim in the seasonal outdoor pool or take a relaxing soak in the hot tub.

Drury Inn hosts a cocktail hour in the lobby every evening and keeps a fresh pot of coffee available in the lobby.

Acadian Village is 9 miles from the Drury Inn. The University of Louisiana-Lafayette is within about a 5-minute drive of the property.
