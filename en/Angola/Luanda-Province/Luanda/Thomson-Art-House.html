Description

Located in Luanda, 1. 5 km from Monumento à Mulher Peixeira, Thomson House Hotel features free WiFi access and free private parking.

There is a shared kitchen at the property. Guests can enjoy a drink at the rooftop bar.

The onsite restaurant serves dinner. This hotel has water sports facilities and bike hire is available.

Car hire is available at this hotel and the area is popular for windsurfing. Naval Club of Luanda is 2.
