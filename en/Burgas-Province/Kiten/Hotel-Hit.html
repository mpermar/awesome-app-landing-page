Description

Hotel Hit provides a secured parking lot, a swimming pool and a garden with a children's playground.

The restaurant serves Bulgarian cuisine accompanied by live music in the evenings.

The hotel also features a 24-hour mini market and it is close to diverse shops and restaurants.

Hotel Hit offers shuttle service to and from Burgas Airport for an extra fee.

Experiences

08/25/2015

The owners are great.

"The owners are great. They were friendly, going out of their way to accommodate, had valuable suggestions on where to go and what to see, and made a huge positive difference regarding our stay. The rest of the staff was also very helpful. The facilities are very good, rooms and the hotel as a whole are kept clean all the time. Our room had a nice view towards the main street, was spacious and the air conditioning and TV worked perfectly. The hotel is in a central location, while close to the Atliman beach, good value for money, has a clean swimming pool with sections for children and adults, and a store which works almost non-stop. We would consider returning another year to stay in this hotel and could very much recommend to others."
