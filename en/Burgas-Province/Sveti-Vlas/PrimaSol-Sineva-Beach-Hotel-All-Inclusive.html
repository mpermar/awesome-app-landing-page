Description

Almost all rooms in the PrimaSol Sineva Beach Hotel offer panoramic views of the sea, the Sunny Beach resort and of Nessebar.

A wide range of dishes of the Bulgarian and international cuisine is served in the restaurant or on the terrace.

Tennis courts, a mini-golf course, a fitness and wellness center and an open-air theater for entertainment programs can be found on the grounds of the PrimaSol Sineva Beach Hotel.

The center of Sunny Beach is 2.5 miles away and it's just 6 miles to the ancient city of Nessebar. You can park your car for free on site.
