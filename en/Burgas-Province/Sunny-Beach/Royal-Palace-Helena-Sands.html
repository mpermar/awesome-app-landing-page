Description

Set near a natural creek with picturesque bridges in lush surroundings, the property offers spacious, air-conditioned rooms with satellite TV and mostly with sea views. There is an entertainment area for children.

Royal Palace' main restaurant serves a delicious buffet breakfast and a warm buffet-style dinner, with courses prepared on the spot. The Dolphin, a fish restaurant, offers a menu with a variety of seafood.

Spicy and aromatic, grilled dishes are served at the Blue Lagoon barbecue restaurant.
