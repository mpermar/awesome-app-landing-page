Description

All rooms in the resort are equipped with a electric tea pot. With a shared bathroom, certain units at Songjeong Marina Resort also offer a sea view. The rooms at the accommodation include air conditioning and a closet.

Guests at Songjeong Marina Resort can enjoy a buffet breakfast. At the resort you'll find a restaurant serving Korean cuisine.

Languages spoken at the reception include English, Japanese, Korean and Chinese, and guests are invited to request advice on the area when needed.

Haeundae Beach is 2.3 miles from Songjeong Marina Resort.
