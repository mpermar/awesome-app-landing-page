Description

Providing free WiFi in all rooms, Jeju Shinhwa World Marriott Resort features indoor and outdoor pools, spa facilities, a casino and a business centre.

Each room is fitted with a safety deposit box, minibar, flat-screen TV and desk. Private bathroom includes a bath, bathrobe, hairdryer and bath amenities by Thann.

Guests can store luggage at the 24-hour front desk or enjoy a drink at The Islet Lounge.

Including an electric vehicle charging station, private parking is provided on site.

Breakfast is offered at Sky On Five Dining Restaurant from 06:30 to 10:30. All-day-dining buffet is available from 6:30 until 22:30 at Café The Islet, while Chinese dishes prepared by chef Alan Chen are served at Le Chinois.

Jeju Shinhwa World Marriott Resort is a 40-minute drive from Jeju International Airport.

Alive Museum Jeju and Hyeopjae Beach are both less than 15 km away from the 5-star hotel.
