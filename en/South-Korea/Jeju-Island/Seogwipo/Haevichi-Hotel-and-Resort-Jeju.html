Description

Only 200 metres from Jeju Folk Village, Haevichi Hotel features a luxurious getaway with both seasonal indoor and outdoor pools, relaxing spa treatments and beautiful views of Halla Mountain and the Pacific Ocean.

Free Wi-Fi and free wired internet are both available. Haevichi Hotel is 500 metres from Pyoseon Beach and a 30-minute drive from scenic Seopjicoji.

Jeju International Airport is an hour's drive away. Boasting ocean views, all the modern guestrooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV and DVD player.

The large en suite bathroom has a separate bathtub and rainshower. Summora Restaurant features all-day casual dining overlooking the sparkling sea.

A buffet breakfast is offered there daily. For recreation, guests can exercise at the fitness centre, lounge in the sauna, or visit the games room.
