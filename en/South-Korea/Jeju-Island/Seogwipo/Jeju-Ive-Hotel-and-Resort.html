Description

Situated on the southern edge of the scenic Halla Mountain, Jeju I’ve Hotel & Resort offers free-standing log cabins and an outdoor pool.

Property-wide WiFi and on-site parking are available free of charge. Each cabin is equipped with a full kitchen and a dining area.

Guests can rent barbecue facilities to use at the outdoor dining area. The private bathroom comes with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

There is an on-site mini-market at the resort. An airport shuttle service, luggage storage and banquet facilities can be arranged.

From the resort, Jeju Convention Centre is a 20-minute drive and Cheonjiyeon Waterfall is a 30-minute drive.
