Description

All rooms in the resort are equipped with a TV with cable channels. Rooms have a private bathroom and a shower, and certain accommodations at Grim Resort have a terrace. Guest rooms will provide guests with a microwave.

Jeju Paradise Casino is 2.4 miles from the accommodation, while Jeju National Museum is 2.6 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Jeju Airport, 0.6 miles from the property.

Jeju City is a great choice for travelers interested in friendly locals, mountains and sightseeing.
