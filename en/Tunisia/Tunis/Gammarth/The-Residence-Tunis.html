Description

The spacious rooms at Résidence Tunis all have a sitting area with an LCD TV and a DVD player. Individual balconies offer views of the sea or the lush garden. All rooms are air-conditioned and have a private bathroom with a bathtub.

The Résidence Tunis caters to a range of palates from light, healthy salads and fresh seafood to home-style Tunisian dishes. The hotel’s restaurants offer cuisine from around the world including Chinese and Mediterranean specialties.

Guests at the Residence receive complimentary access to a 37,600-ft² luxurious Thalasso-Spa and fitness center. Hotel staff is available 24 hours to assist guests with treatment and leisure bookings.

This hotel is 9 miles from Tunis-Carthage International Airport and 3 miles from both Carthage and Sidi Bou Said.
