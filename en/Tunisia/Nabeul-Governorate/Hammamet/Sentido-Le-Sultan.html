Description

Providing direct access to Hammamet Beach, this 4-star hotel offers 2 swimming pools.

It is located 3 km from the centre of Hammamet. The air-conditioned guest rooms at Le Sultan have a private balcony.

Each classic room is equipped with a minibar and a TV with satellite channels. The marble bathrooms feature a glass wall.

Restaurant Les Voiliers serves Mediterranean cuisine on the terrace, Sakura restaurant offers fusion cuisine and there also is a Moorish café.

A buffet breakfast is served each morning at Le Serail Restaurant. Hotel Sultan has 5 bars including the Sherazade piano bar.

With a spa on site, the hotel has internet access in all areas. A free shuttle service is provided to Yasmine Golf Course and the PADI scuba diving centre.

With free private parking on site, the hotel is 8 km from Bir Bourekba Train Station.
