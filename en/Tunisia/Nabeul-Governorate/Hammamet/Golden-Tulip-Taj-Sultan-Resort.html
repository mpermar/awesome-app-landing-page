Description

Golden Tulip Taj Sultan is situated on Yasmine Hammamet beach, 15 minutes from the centre of Hammamet.

It offers en suite accommodation with 2 swimming pools and a spa and wellness centre.

The spacious guest rooms are air conditioned and equipped with satellite TV. Each room has a balcony overlooking the sea or the gardens.

Breakfast is served every day. The restaurant offers à la carte and buffet dining options serving a range of Tunisian and international dishes.

Golden Tulip Taj Sultan’s spa includes a hamman, and massage rooms. Guests also have free use of a fitness centre, tennis court and billiards tables.

Free Wi-Fi access is available in all areas. Carthage Airport is 61 km away. Airport shuttles are available upon request and at an extra charge.
