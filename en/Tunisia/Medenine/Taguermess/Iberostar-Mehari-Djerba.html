Description

Iberostar Mehari Djerba offers all-inclusive accommodations equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV and a balcony or terrace with views of the grounds, sea, or pool.

The hotel’s restaurant, La Guellala, serves Tunisian specialties and international dishes. There is also a buffet-style restaurant. Guests can enjoy a drink and snacks at the poolside and beach bars.

Guests have free use of the fitness room, heated swimming pools and Jacuzzi. The hotel’s Spa & Wellness Center provides a Turkish bath, mini spa and massage service.

Many on-site activities can be enjoyed, such as aerobics, basketball, beach volleyball and water polo. There is also a kids' club.

Djerba International Airport is 9 miles away and Midoun tourist area is 4 miles from the hotel. The Iberostar Mehari Djerba has free on-site private parking.
