Description

Situated in Djerba, 6 km from Midoun this luxurious, Robinson Club Djerba Bahiya sits on the beach.

It offers a swimming pool, tennis court and fitness centre. All the en suite guestrooms overlook the ocean, gardens or pool.

Modern facilities include air conditioning, a mini bar and satellite TV. Guests can choose from a selection of bars and restaurants serving Mediterranean, Tunisian and seafood cuisine.

The relaxation centre offers massage treatments, a hammam and a hot tub. In the evenings guests can relax with a drink at the various bars.
