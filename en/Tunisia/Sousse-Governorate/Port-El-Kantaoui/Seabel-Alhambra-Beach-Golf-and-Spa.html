Description

The all-inclusive Seabel Alhambra Beach Golf & Spa is located on the seafront, in an 11-hectare park in North East Tunisia.

All of the luxurious guestrooms are spacious and equipped with modern en suite facilities and have a private balcony and air conditioning.

The Seabel Alhambra is located by its own private beach and has an indoor and outdoor pool with poolside bar and tennis courts.

The hotel has a number of other attractions and activities on offer including the full service spa centre, the hotel restaurant, a games room and a specialized children’s club.

The Seabel Alhambra is located 27 kilometres from the international Skanes-Monastir airport and within walking distance from El Kantaoui port and golf club.
