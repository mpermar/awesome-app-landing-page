Description

This hotel with 2 swimming pools, a 2-minute walk away. It offers a spa including massage treatments, a thalassotherapy centre and a show twice a week.

Each en suite room includes a balcony with a view of the garden or the sea. All the air-conditioned rooms at Hotel Marhaba Beach have satellite TV and a fridge.

Each morning a buffet breakfast is available. The hotel offers a choice of 3 restaurants, snack bars and 5 bars, including a bar on the beach.

Other services include a fitness centre, kids club and a beauty centre. Located 3 km from central Sousse and its train station, this hotel is a 30-minute drive from Monastir–Habib Bourguiba International Airport.
