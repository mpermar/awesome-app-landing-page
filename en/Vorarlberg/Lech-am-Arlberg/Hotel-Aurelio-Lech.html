Description

Enjoy all the sophistication and comfort of a world-class hotel combined with the atmosphere and elegance of a private residence.

Lech enjoys a worldwide reputation as a top-class ski area, with slopes reaching the hotel’s doors. Famous for its 5-month, snow-sure winter season, its high-quality skiing experience and its glamorous atmosphere, Lech became the home of the Aurelio Lech luxury hotel in December 2008.

Located directly at the world-famous slopes and ski lifts of the Arlberg ski region, it offers luxurious, spacious and exclusively equipped rooms. All rooms are elegantly and individually furnished and feature balconies with a view of the snow-covered mountains.

The Aurelio Spa offers relaxation at the highest level, with an indoor pool and exclusive treatments in a tranquil atmosphere.
