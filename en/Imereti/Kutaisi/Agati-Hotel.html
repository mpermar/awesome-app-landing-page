Description

At the hotel, every room has a closet. All rooms have a private bathroom with free toiletries, while certain rooms here will provide you with a balcony.

A buffet breakfast is available each morning at the property.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental is available at Agati Hotel.

The accommodation provides a dry cleaning service, as well as business facilities like fax and photocopying. Speaking English and Russian at the 24-hour front desk, staff are willing to help at any time of the day.

The nearest airport is Kopitnari Airport, 16 miles from Agati Hotel.
