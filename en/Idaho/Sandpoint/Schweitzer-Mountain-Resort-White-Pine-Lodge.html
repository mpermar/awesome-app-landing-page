Description

Villas in White Pine Lodge have full kitchens and sitting areas with a flat-screen TV. Guests have access to a fitness center; an outdoor, heated pool, 2 hot tubs; and a theater. Covered parking, as well as ski, snowboard, and bike storage is available. White Pine Lodge has two stores: Stiles Mountain Wear, and an artist studio featuring paintings, pottery, and jewelry.

Schweitzer Mountain has acres of slopes and trails perfect for skiing, tubing, snowboarding, snowshoeing, and snowmobiling. Activities in warmer months include tennis, biking, and hiking. No matter the season, the Solstice Spa offers a variety of soothing massages and beauty treatments.

The closest town to Schweitzer Mountain Resort is Sandpoint, Idaho. The town, and the Sandpoint Airport, are 12 miles away.
