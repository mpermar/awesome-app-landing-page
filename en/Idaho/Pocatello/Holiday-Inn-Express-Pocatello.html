Description

The rooms here have a cable flat-screen TV, air conditioning, and a sitting area. A coffee machine and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries can be found in each room.

A business center, fitness center, meeting and banquet facilities, and a 24-hour front desk are all available’ convenience. The daily breakfast consists of eggs, hot and cold cereals, fruit, yogurt, baked goods, coffee, tea, and juices.

Idaho State University is just over 1 miles away, the Idaho Museum of Natural History is 2 miles from the property, and the Pocatello Zoo is 4.5 miles from the Holiday Inn Express Pocatello.
