Description

A private balcony with views and a full kitchen are found in each lodge-style room of Stoneridge Resorts. A flat-screen cable TV, a DVD player and a coffee maker are also furnished.

Basketball, volleyball and pickleball courts are available. A playground is also available. A fitness center and steam room are onsite.

Downtown Spirit Lake is 9 miles from the resort. Spokane, Coeur d'Alene, Priest Lake and Sandpoint are within an hour drive.
