Description

Suites at Homewood Suites Boise are spacious and furnished with a work desk and a seating area. Cable TV, free Wi-Fi and an MP3 docking station are all included.

Boise Hilton Homewood Suites offers a 24-hour gym and a business center. Laundry facilities are available for added convenience.

Guests are provided a hot breakfast every morning and a light reception on select evenings. A convenience store is also on site.

The Wahooz Family Fun Zone is a 10 minute-drive from the Homewood Suites. The Boise Center is also 10 minutes away and Boise State University is a 15-minute drive from the hotel.
