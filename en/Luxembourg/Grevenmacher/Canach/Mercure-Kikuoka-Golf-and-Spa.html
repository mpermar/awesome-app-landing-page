Description

The hotel rooms are generous in size, are decorated with light pastel tones and offer air conditioning. They have a TV, minibar and lounge corner. Each room also has a private bathroom with a bathtub.

A traditional restaurant and a bar with a summer terrace are available for drinks and meals. Furthermore the hotel has free Wi-Fi in public areas and a fitness center.

This Mercure is in quiet surroundings, at the edge of a forest. Parking is free of charge.
