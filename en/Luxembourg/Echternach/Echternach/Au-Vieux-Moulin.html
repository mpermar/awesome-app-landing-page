Description

Located in the heart of a rural and mountainous environment, the Aux Vieux Moulin offers a garden and terrace where you can enjoy the beautiful landscape, and free WiFi access.

A breakfast buffet is served in the morning and for dinner you can enjoy the tasty cuisine in the gastronomic à-la-carte restaurant.

Many sightseeing trips can be arranged to various destinations such as Petite Suisse and the historic village of Echternach.

Close to the hotel you can also see the stream which used to activate the watermill.

The old town of Echternach is worth visiting, as is the Lac d’Echternach et la Sûre.
