Description

At the hotel, rooms have a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Free WiFi is accessible to all guests, while some rooms contain a balcony. All guest rooms have a closet.

The property has a sauna, a fitness center and a hammam.

Staff at the reception can provide guidance on what to do in the area.

Merano is 16 miles from Hotel Waldhof, while Bolzano is 5 miles away.
