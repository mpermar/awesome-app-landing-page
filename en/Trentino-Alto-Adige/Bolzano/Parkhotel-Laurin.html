Description

This elegant hotel dates back to 1910 and is built in the Art Nouveau style. Each room features designer furniture and original artwork by contemporary artists.

Parkhotel Laurin effortlessly combines old-world charm with all the latest technologies. It features a fully equipped conference center for up to 160 people. The banquet rooms and open spaces are perfect for weddings and exhibitions.

You can enjoy a varied mix of regional and Italian cuisine at the restaurant, which opens out into the garden during the summer. The Laurin Bar has original Art Nouveau frescoes and a relaxing atmosphere, ideal for a quiet drink.

Parkhotel Laurin is just 650 feet from Bolzano Station and is close to the main square and all the city’s attractions.
