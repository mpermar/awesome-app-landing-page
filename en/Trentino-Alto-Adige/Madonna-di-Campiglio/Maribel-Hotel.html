Description

The cableway connecting to Grosté and Pradalago is just 2,000 feet away. The Maribel provides a storage room for skiing equipment.

Rooms at Hotel Maribel are equipped with satellite TV and free wired internet access. Wi-Fi is free in public areas.

The hotel also has an on-site restaurant, a bar and a terrace.

The Grosté 1 Express and Predalgo Express chairlifts are less than one mile away.

Discounts are available for stays longer than 4 nights.
