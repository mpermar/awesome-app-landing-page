Description

This four-star superior hotel features the Cristal Energy Spa, a large fitness and spa center with indoor swimming pool.

Hotel Cristal Palace has comfortable guest rooms with valley or mountain views. Each room has Wi-Fi access, minibar and an LCD TV with satellite channels.

There is a heated storage area for your ski equipment and a shelter for bicycles. There is also a free shuttle service to and from the ski slopes, upon request.

The Cristal Palace organizes free guided tours throughout the summer for exploring the Adamello-Brenta Park. At the hotel, there is a mini club and a cinema room with surround sound system.
