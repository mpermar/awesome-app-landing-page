Description

At the hotel, rooms contain a desk. Rooms are equipped with a private bathroom equipped with a shower and a hair dryer, while some rooms will provide you with a kitchenette. All rooms at Zunnelerhof come with a seating area.

A continental breakfast is served at the property.

You can play ping pong at the accommodation.

Taser Seilbahn is 1.1 miles from Zunnelerhof, while Verdinser Seilbahn is 2.4 miles away.
