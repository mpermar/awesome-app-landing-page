Description

The restaurant at the Silence & Schlosshotel Mirabell serves Tyrolean and classic Italian cooking. Breakfast is buffet style.

The Mirabell Silence & Schlosshotel has 2 swimming pools, and the spa center includes a sauna, and Turkish bath. Massages and beauty treatments are available and cost extra.

The cable car to the Siusi Alps skiing area is half a mile away, and the Mirabell is 15 miles from Bolzano.
