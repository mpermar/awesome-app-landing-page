Description

Alpholiday Dolomiti Wellness & Fun Hotel offers rooms with balcony, minibar and satellite TV. Wi-Fi internet access is also available.

The restaurant at the Alpholiday Dolomiti Hotel serves both Trentino and classic Italian cuisine.

The Alpholiday Hotel offers a health club with sauna, Turkish bath, jacuzzi, gym and beauty treatments.

The hotel is 2 miles from the ski lifts for the Skirama Dolomiti Adamello Brenta, which has 236 miles of trails.
