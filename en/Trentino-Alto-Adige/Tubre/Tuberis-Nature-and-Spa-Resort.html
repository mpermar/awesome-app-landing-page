Description

At the resort, each room includes a desk. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with free toiletries, while some units at Tuberis Nature & Spa Resort also provide guests with a balcony.

Buffet and vegetarian breakfast options are available every morning at the accommodation. At Tuberis Nature & Spa Resort you'll find a restaurant serving French cuisine. Vegetarian, Vegan and Dairy-free options can also be requested.

Spa and wellness facilities including a hot tub, a sauna and a fitness center are at guests' disposal during their stay at the resort. Guests can enjoy a number of activities in the surrounding areas, including skiing, cycling and hiking.
