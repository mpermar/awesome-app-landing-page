Description

The hotel's extra-large terrace offers unbeatable view of the Dolomites and the slopes. Guests have a free heated ski deposit on site, plus a ski rental service and ski school next door.

Sit back and relax in Alpina's contemporary spa with a number of saunas and relaxation areas, plus a gym and beauty farm. Breakfast is buffet style, while the gourmet restaurant serves a mixture of South Tyrol and Mediterranean cuisine, with both a buffet and a varied dining menu.

The spacious rooms come with warm color schemes and hardwood floors. They feature a flat-screen TV, minibar and a bathroom with separate shower and bathtub plus a separate toilet. All have a balcony or terrace.

You will have free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel and free parking in the garage. You enjoy easy access to highway A22 to Bolzano 19 miles away.
