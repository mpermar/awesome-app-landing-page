Description

Set in a Belle Epoque building from the early 20th-century, all rooms at Lido Palace come with high design black and gray décor, along with a wall-mounted 42" LCD TV. You also have free WiFi, an extra-large bed and a modern bathroom with underfloor heating and exclusive toiletry set.

The only luxury 5-star hotel in the Trentino region, the Lido Palace features a wellness and spa center complete with a sauna, Turkish bath and a fully equipped fitness center.

The main restaurant has a Michelin-awarded chef and serves traditional Mediterranean cuisine. Another, more intimate one, offers à la carte menus based on fish specialties.

Offering free parking, the hotel is a 15-minute drive from the A22 motorway. Trento can be reached by car in 40 minutes.
