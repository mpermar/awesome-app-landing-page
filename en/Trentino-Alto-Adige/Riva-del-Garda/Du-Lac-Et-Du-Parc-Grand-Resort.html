Description

Rooms here come with air conditioning, satellite TV, and a balcony.

The Grand Resorts outdoor swimming pool has beautiful mountain views which you can enjoy from the sun terrace with loungers. Bike rental is available so you can explore the surrounding area. Charging stations for electric vehicles are also available.

The hotel has a number of dining options including an la carte restaurant serving local and national specialties, a poolside caf and a snack bar. There is also a roof garden for seasonal outdoor dining.

The beauty spa offers a variety of relaxing treatments, massage and a sauna. The indoor swimming pool offers year-round relaxation and water aerobics classes.
