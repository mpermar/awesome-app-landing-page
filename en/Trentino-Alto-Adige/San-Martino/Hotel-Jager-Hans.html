Description

At the hotel, each room has a balcony with a mountain view. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a shower, while certain accommodations at Hotel Jager Hans also feature a seating area. At the accommodation rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

A continental breakfast is served at the property.

Spa and wellness facilities including a hot tub, a sauna and a hammam are at guests' disposal during their stay at Hotel Jager Hans. A variety of activities are offered in the area, such as skiing and cycling.

Merano is 8 miles from the hotel.

Policies

Children and Extra Beds
All children are welcome.
One child under 3 years is charged EUR 5 per person per night in a crib.
One child from 3 to 9 years is charged 40 % of the room stay per night and person in an extra bed.
One child from 10 to 14 years is charged 70 % of the room stay per night and person in an extra bed.
One older child or adult is charged 90 % of the room stay per night and person in an extra bed.
The maximum number of extra beds/cribs in a room is 1.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
