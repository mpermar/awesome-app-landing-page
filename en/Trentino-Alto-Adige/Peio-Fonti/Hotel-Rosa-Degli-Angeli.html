Description

Everything you need for a perfect vacation is close by, including ski resorts, Peios thermal spa center, where you will receive discounts, and a sports center where you can play anything from tennis to basketball or soccer. The hotel also has its own spa and relaxation center, with swimming pools and a range of different treatments available. Children are also well catered for, with staff on hand to keep them busy and safe.

Close to Hotel Rosa degli Angeli you will find shopping, restaurants and bars and the cable car station which will take you up the mountains is also not far away. The area is wonderful for walks, hikes and bike rides and other sports such as rafting can also be tried here.

This hotel has been innovatively built to reduce its effect on the environment, for example using solar panels to heat the building and water.
