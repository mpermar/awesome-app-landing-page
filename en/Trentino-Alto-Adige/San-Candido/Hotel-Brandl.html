Description

At the hotel, the rooms include a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. All rooms feature a closet.

The daily breakfast offers continental and buffet options. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the on-site restaurant, which specializes in local cuisine and also offers gluten-free and vegetarian options.

The property has an indoor pool. A range of activities can be enjoyed on site or nearby, including skiing, cycling and hiking.

Haunold Chairlift is half a mile from Hotel Brandl, while Doris is 0.6 miles away.
