Description

At the hotel, all rooms come with a desk. Featuring a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries, rooms at Hotel Patrizia Wellness & SPA also feature free WiFi, while some have a pool view. At the accommodation rooms contain a closet and a flat-screen TV.

Continental and buffet breakfast options are available each morning at Hotel Patrizia Wellness & SPA. The restaurant at the hotel specializes in local and Italian cuisine.

Spa and wellness facilities including a sauna, a hammam and an indoor pool are at guests' disposal during their stay at Hotel Patrizia Wellness & SPA. You can play ping pong at the accommodation, and the area is popular for cycling.

With staff speaking English and Italian, information is available at the reception.
