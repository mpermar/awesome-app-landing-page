Description

Featuring a private beach area, Familien-Sporthotel Brennseehof is located in Feld am See. This 4-star hotel features free WiFi and water sports facilities. Guests can have a drink at the bar.

The rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Sporthotel Brennseehof has certain units that have mountain views, and the rooms are equipped with a private bathroom and a desk. All guest rooms include a closet.

A buffet breakfast is served at the property. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of International, Italian and Austrian dishes and also offers gluten-free, vegetarian and dairy-free options.

At the accommodation guests are welcome to use a hot tub, a sauna and a fitness room. Guests can take part in various activities in the surrounding areas, including skiing and cycling.

Bad Kleinkirchheim is 8 miles from Familien-Sporthotel Brennseehof. The nearest airport is Klagenfurt Airport, 39 miles from the hotel.
