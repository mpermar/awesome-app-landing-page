Description

The Seehotel offers a private bathing lake shore with sun umbrellas and lounge chairs. Pedal and row boats as well as water bicycles are provided free of charge. There is also an indoor swimming pool with saunas.

The restaurant at Seehotel Steiner serves international and Austrian cuisine as well as typical local specialties.
