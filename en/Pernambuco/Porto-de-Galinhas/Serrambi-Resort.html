Description

All rooms are spacious and feature cable TV, a minibar, air conditioning and a private bathroom with a hot shower.

The property offers a large outdoor pool, fitness center, diving facilities, tennis courts, beach volleyball courts and soccer field. Boat trips to Ilha de Santo Aleix and other nearby places can be organized. In addition, exquisite meals and drinks are served in the hotel restaurant and bar.

Resort Serrambi is 4 km from Maracaipe Beach and 7 km from Merepe Beach. Recife International Airport is 60 km away.
