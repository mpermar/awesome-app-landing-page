Description

The rooms are decorated in a contemporary style and feature a balcony with sea views, air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, a minibar and a private bathroom with a hot shower and toiletries. The deluxe rooms have a hot tub on the balcony.

Due to its proximity to the sea, the hotel offers numerous options to relax on the beach, such as sun loungers and bar service.

Hotel Vivá Porto de Galinhas has a spa, a fitness center, a bar and a sports court. In addition, there are several leisure options for children, including a children's cinema.

Hotel Vivá Porto de Galinhas is 400 meters from Cupe Beach, 5 km from the city center and 47 km from Guararapes International Airport.
