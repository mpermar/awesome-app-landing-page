Description

The spacious and bright rooms and bungalows at Hotel Village Porto de Galinhas have a balcony, air conditioning, cable TV and a minibar. They also have a private bathroom with a hot shower and a hairdryer.

Village Porto De Galinhas Hotel offers a beach bar, tennis court and disco. A free breakfast is served daily and guests can enjoy regional dishes and fresh fruit.

The hotel is a short drive from the center of Ipojuca, 3 km from Merepe beach and 60 km from Guararapes-Gilbertto Freyre airport in Recife.
