Description

All rooms at the hotel are spacious and feature a balcony with sea or garden views, air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a safe, a minibar and a private bathroom with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries. Some units also have a wooden terrace with a bathtub and a seating area, while others have a hot tub.

Praia Hotel's restaurant serves breakfast, lunch and dinner, and guests can choose between buffet and à la carte menu. The restaurant also has a wood-fired pizza oven and barbecue.

Guests can book a variety of activities and services at the reception, such as massages, scuba diving lessons and childcare. In addition, the reception is available 24 hours.

The nearest airport is Recife International Airport, located 48 km from Porto de Galinhas Praia Hotel.
