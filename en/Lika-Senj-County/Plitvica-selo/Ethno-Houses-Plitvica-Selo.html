Description

Made of dark wood, all rooms are elegantly decorated and equipped with a king bed, a desk and a sitting area. Each unit has a private bathroom with shower.

Guests can play ping-pong and rent bikes on site. Restaurants and souvenir shops are part of the national park, all within walking distance. An adrenalin park is located across from the complex.

Free private parking is available. A1 Highway has access to Zagreb, and Western Europe can be reached 34 miles from Ethno Houses Plitvica Selo.
