Description

Eurostars Berlin provides stylish rooms and suites with flat-screen TV, tea/coffee maker, and elegant wooden flooring. All bathrooms feature select bathroom products.

A modern gym and sauna can be found in the Eurostars’ spa area. Guests are welcome to use the indoor pool throughout their stay.

Creative, international cuisine is served in the Berlin Eurostars’ à la carte restaurant. A full buffet breakfast starts each day.

Friedrichstraße Underground and S-Bahn Train Station is just 800 feet from the Eurostars. Guests can use the Eurostars Hotel's underground garage on request.

Mitte is a great choice for travelers interested in history, museums and architecture.
