Description

The Hotel Rheinsberg am See’s rooms include a private bathroom, a work desk and cable TV channels.

A large breakfast buffet is available each morning in the Rheinsberg’s restaurant. In the evenings, German and international food is served here.

Guests can use the Rheinsberg am See’s spa facilities for free. These include an indoor swimming pool, a Finnish sauna and a hot tub. Fitness classes are offered in the pool on Tuesdays and Thursdays.

The nearby Wittenau Underground Station provides a direct link to the famous Alexanderplatz Square in around 25 minutes.

Reinickendorf is a great choice for travelers interested in city trips, city walks and convenient public transportation.
