Description

The air-conditioned rooms of the Crowne Plaza Berlin City Center include tea/coffee facilities and a safety deposit box. Guests can use the hotel’s wellness facilities, including a modern gym, free sauna facilities and massage service.

The Crowne Plaza’s varied breakfast buffets feature lactose-free and gluten-free food. Wilson’s – The Prime Rib Restaurant serves original US prime rib, a specialty from the USA, carved directly from the rib from a live carving station.

A smaller breakfast is served until 14:00 in the Lobby Lounge, where light Californian cuisine is also served. Guests can also enjoy drinks and snacks in Kemmons Bar.

The Crowne Plaza Berlin is a 5-minute walk from the KaDeWe department store, Kaiser Wilhelm Memorial Church, and Wittenbergplatz Underground Station.

Tempelhof-Schöneberg is a great choice for travelers interested in nightlife, shopping and convenient public transportation.
