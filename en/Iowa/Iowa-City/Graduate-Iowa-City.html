Description

The elegant rooms at the hotel are styled with wood furniture and warm colors. Each includes a coffee maker with Starbucks coffee, a work desk and lounge chair.

Guests have free access to the indoor pool and state-of-the-art gym. A full-service business center with free WiFi and a 24-hour front desk are available.

Share Wine Lounge and Small Plate Bistro are located on-site. The restaurant offers an extensive wine selection and American cuisine.

Englert Civic Theater and the University of Iowa Museum of Natural History are just a 10-minute walk from this hotel. Coral Ridge Mall is 10 minutes away by car.
