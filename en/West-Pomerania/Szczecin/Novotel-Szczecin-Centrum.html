Description

The rooms of the Novotel Szczecin feature a classic interior design. Each is bright and has a work desk. For guests’ convenience an electric kettle is included.

The Novotel Szczecin has an indoor swimming pool, games room and a children’s playground. Guests can also relax in the hot tub or work out in the fitness center.

Located in an area that features numerous shops and restaurant, the hotel is only 1,800 feet from Szczecin Główny Railway Station. Piastowski Boulevard on the Oder River is within 1,650 feet. The Old Town is 1,150 feet away.

The hotel’s restaurant serves Polish and international dishes. In the morning a breakfast buffet is available. There also is a bar on site.

Srodmiescie is a great choice for travelers interested in food, shopping and restaurants.
