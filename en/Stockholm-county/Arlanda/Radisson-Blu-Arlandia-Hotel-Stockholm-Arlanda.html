Description

Cable TV and tea/coffee-making facilities are featured in each air-conditioned and soundproof Radisson Blu Arlandia Hotel, Stockholm-Arlanda guest room.

Radisson Blu Arlandia’s restaurant serves a large lunch buffet, as well as a dining menu of international dishes. Guests can choose from a selection of fine wines.

Other facilities include a bar, pool table and children’s playroom.

The free airport bus departs every 15 minutes from 5 am until midnight. A car rental company is located right across from the hotel. The center of Stockholm is a 30-minute drive away.
