Description

Featuring an on-site restaurant and wellness facilities, Mlyn Penzion & Wellness is set in Radvaň nad Dunajom.

Its modern-style accommodation features a patio with a view to Danube River. Free Wi-Fi is provided.

All spacious and elegant rooms come with hardwood floors and feature a flat-screen satellite TV, air conditioning and a private bathroom.

Apartments also comprise a living room and a fully equipped kitchen. The Mlyn Penzion’s restaurant features a fireplace and a terrace.

Its guests can enjoy Slovak and Hungarian cuisine, as well as fish and grill specialities.

At a bar, selection of hot and cold drinks is available. In addition to gym, sun beds and various massage treatments, hairdresser services, cosmetics treatments, manicure and pedicure are provided.

Wellness facilities include Finnish sauna and infra sauna, a hot tub and a salt bath.

A summer gazebo with barbecue facilities and a garden with a children’s playground are at guests’ disposal.

Biking opportunities can be found in the vicinity and bike rental can be arranged on site.

Patince Thermal Pool is 6 km away, while Sturovo Vadas Thermal Pool can be reached within 30 km.

The town of Komarno and a border with Hungary are at a distance of 18 km. Free private parking is possible on site.
