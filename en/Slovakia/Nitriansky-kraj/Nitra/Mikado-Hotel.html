Description

Rooms here come with a flat-screen TV with 60 cable channels. Each room has free internet access, a minibar and water boiler. Bedding and pillows are anti-allergic, and bathrobes and slippers are provided in every room.

Slovakian and international dishes are served at the 2 on-site restaurants, each with their own cocktail bar. Guests can also visit the lobby bar, with its comfortable leather armchairs and library corner.

A golf simulator can be found in the spa area, which includes a sauna, a fitness studio and a massage room. For a snack break, guests can visit the wellness bar, which serves fresh fruit and drinks.

The lobby is open 24 hours at the Mikado, and free Wi-Fi is available in all public areas. Nitra Train Station is one mile away.
