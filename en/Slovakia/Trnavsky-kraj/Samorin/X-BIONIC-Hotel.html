Description

Offering a spa centre and hot tub, X-BIONIC Hotel is located in Šamorín. There is a water park on site and guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant and bar.

Free WiFi is offered throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Each room at this resort is air conditioned and has a flat-screen TV. A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms.

Extras include free toiletries and a hairdryer. You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property.

You can play billiards at this resort, and the area is popular for golfing. The resort also offers bike hire and car hire.
