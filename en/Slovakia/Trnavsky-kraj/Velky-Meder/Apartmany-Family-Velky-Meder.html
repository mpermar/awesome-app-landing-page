Description

Apartmány Family Veľký Meder is located just 200 metres from the Termálpark Veľký Meder and offers a self-catered accommodation, a 24-hour front desk, a garden with barbecue facilities, a terrace and a playground for children.

Free WiFi is available in all areas and free private parking is possible on site.

Each unit at Apartmány Family Veľký Meder comes with a terrace or a balcony, a TV with satellite channels, a well-fitted kitchenette and a private bathroom with a shower.

Meal plans can be arranged at the Hotel Orchidea situated just steps away and the nearest grocery store can be reached on foot in 5 minutes.
