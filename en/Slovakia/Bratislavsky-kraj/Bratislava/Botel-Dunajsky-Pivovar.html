Description

Set on the Danube River, between the SNP Bridge and Old Bridge, Botel Dunajský Pivovar offers a unique accommodation on a boat with a private brewery.

Guests can enjoyed a glass of Patrón beer in the onsite restaurant or a bar with nice views of Bratislava Castle and city.

Each cabin at Dunajský Pivovar Botel comes with modern furnishings, air-conditioning, a flat-screen TV, a minibar, a safety deposit box and a private bathroom.

Free WiFi is available in public areas and free parking is possible on site. If you feel like visiting surrounding check out the Main Square, St.

Martin's Cathedral, St. Michael's Gate or Bratislava Castle all reachable within 1 km from the property.

Aupark Shopping Center is located only 600 metres from the property and you get here through Sad Janka Kráľa Park.
