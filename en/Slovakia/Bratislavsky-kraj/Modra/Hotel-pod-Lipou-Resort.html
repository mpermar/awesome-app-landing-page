Description

Situated in the recreational area Harmonia located only 2 km from the town of Modra and surrounded by the Small Carpathians Mountains, Hotel pod Lipou offers accommodation in modern rooms, a restaurant, a bar, a conference centre, an outdoor summer swimming pool and kids pool, and many other sports facilities.

Free WiFi is available in all areas and free parking is possible on site. Each unit at Hotel pod Lipou is air-conditioned and comes with a minibar, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, a desk, a seating area and a private bathroom.

Meals can be enjoyed in the onsite restaurant Harmonia with capacity of 60 seats.

Here you can take advantage of rich breakfast buffet with high quality Slovak products and organic fair.
