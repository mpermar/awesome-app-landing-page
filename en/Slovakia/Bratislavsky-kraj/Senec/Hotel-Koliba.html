Description

Built in the traditional style of a Slovak shepherd's hut, Hotel Koliba is located 150 metres from the lakeshore and just a 30-minute drive from Bratislava's centre.

It offers typical Slovak cuisine and a free outdoor pool and fitness centre. Each room has a private bathroom and is equipped with a minibar and an LCD TV.

Free Wi-Fi is available in the entire building. Traditional Slovak dishes and grilled specialities are served in the rustic-style restaurant of the Koliba Hotel.

Breakfast is available every morning. 3-course menus are also offered and on weekends you can enjoy live gypsy music.

Guests are also offered sun umbrellas, deck chairs and beach towels. Massages can be arranged and an infrared sauna can be used.

You can enjoy bathing and water sports at the Slnečné Jazerá Lakes or take a 20-minute walk to the centre of Senec.

Horseback riding is possible in Hruba Borsa and Blatne, which are a 3 and 5 km away respectively.
