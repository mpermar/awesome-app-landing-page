Description

Surrounded by a pine forest, Wellness Hotel Spark enjoys a quiet location 2 km from the city of Malacky.

It features a spa and wellness area with an indoor pool, a hot tub and a sauna. Free Wi-Fi is available.

The air conditioned rooms with carpeted floors feature a TV with satellite channels.

There is a private bathroom with a shower, bathrobes and free toiletries. Spark Wellness Hotel offers buffet breakfast.

The restaurant with a terrace offers lake views and serves international cuisine.

Massage services are available on request. Free private parking is possible on site.

You can reach the hotel via the D2 highway, 6 km from the exit. The White Eurovalley Golf Park in Malacky is 2 km from the property.
