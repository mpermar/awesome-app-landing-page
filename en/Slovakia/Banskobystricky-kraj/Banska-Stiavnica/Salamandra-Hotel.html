Description

Located just a couple of steps from a ski slope, Salamandra Hotel features a Wellness area with a swimming pool and an on-site restaurant and bar.

Various sports facilities are also available, as well as free Wi-Fi. All accommodation units feature modern furnishings, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with shower.

The restaurants serves a variety of Slovak and international dishes, as well as a rich breakfast buffet.

Wellness facilities include a sauna, Turkish bath, hot tub and massages, while sports facilities include squash, cycling and skiing.

Salamandra Ski Slope can be reached in just 100 metres, while the town of Hondrusa Hamre is 4 km away.
