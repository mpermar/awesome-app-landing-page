Description

Set on the outskirts of Hodruša Hámre, 3 km from the Salamandra Ski Resort, Apartmány Weissov Dom offer a garden with a children’s playground and barbecue facilitates.

Free Wi-Fi is provided in the entire building and free private parking is possible on site.

The apartments include a private bathroom, a fully equipped kitchen and a living room with a flat-screen TV.

The property is 14 km from Vodny Raj Vyhne Aqua Park and free private parking is available.
