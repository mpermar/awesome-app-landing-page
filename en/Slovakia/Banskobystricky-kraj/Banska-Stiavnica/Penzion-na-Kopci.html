Description

Enjoying a hilltop position, Penzion na Kopci offers an on-site restaurant with unique views on the town of Banská Štiavnica.

It features rooms decorated with wood-panelled ceilings. Free Wi-Fi is provided. All rooms come with private entrance.

They feature a seating area, satellite TV. an electric kettle and a private bathroom.

Free private parking is possible on site. The Penzion na Kopci’s restaurant has a large terrace and serves international cuisine.

A children’s corner and a playground are also available. Football field is just 50 metres away.

A swimming pool can be found 300 metres from the Penzion na Kopci. A local bus stop is 500 away.

The historic centre of Banská Štiavnica can be reached within 800 metres. A train station is 1.
