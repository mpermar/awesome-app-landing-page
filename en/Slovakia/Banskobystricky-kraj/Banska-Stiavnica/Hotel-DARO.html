Description

Surrounded by the Štiavnické Vrchy Mountains and set in the village of Hodruša-Hámre, Hotel DARO offers accommodation in modern rooms and studios, spa and wellness facilities, a restaurant, a bar, a 24-hours front desk, a bike rental and a children´s playground.

Free WiFi is available in all areas and free private parking is possible on site.

The UNESCO-protected town of Banská Štiavnica can be reached within 13 km. Each unit at Daro Hotel comes with mountain views, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a telephone and a private bathroom with a shower or a bath tub.

A shared lounge is also available for all guests. Meals can be enjoyed in the on site restaurant and the nearest grocery store can be found within 300 metres from the hotel.

Wellness area features facilities like a sauna, a hot tub and a hammam, available free of charge.

Ski storage, barbecue facilities, a balcony, a terrace, newspapers, a safety deposit box, air-conditioning and a daily maid service are provided free of charge, while business facilities and billiards are offered for a fee.

The area is popular for skiing and cycling. The artificial water reservoirs, also called the Tajch, can be found in a radius of 30 km from the property and the Salamandra Ski Area is reachable within a 10 km.
