Description

Surrounded by the forest and set 1. 5 km from the village of Kováčová, Hotel Tatran offers accommodation with a restaurant, a garden, a table tennis and free parking possible on site.

Free WiFi is available in all areas. Each room at Hotel Tatran features internet connection, a TV with satellite channels, a telephone and a private bathroom with a shower or a bath tub and a hairdryer.

Breakfast can be enjoyed on site. If you feel like visiting surroundings, check out Holiday Park Kováčová set only 300 metres away or Tri Duby Golf Course reachable within 7 km.
