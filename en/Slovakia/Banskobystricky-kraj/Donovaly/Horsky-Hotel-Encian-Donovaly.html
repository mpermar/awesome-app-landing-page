Description

Located in the Low Tatras National Park, Horsky Hotel Encian offers a restaurant, and free-of-charge access to wellness area with a hot tub, a sauna and massages.

Free WiFi is provided in the entire building and free private parking is possible on site.

The restaurant serves international and traditional Slovak cuisine. It comes with a winter terrace and a lobby bar with a fireplace.

The rooms include a private bathroom, a TV and a refrigerator. They offer views of the mountains.

As the Horsky Hotel Encian is located in the Donovaly Ski Area, other ski slopes can be found within a 15-minute drive.
