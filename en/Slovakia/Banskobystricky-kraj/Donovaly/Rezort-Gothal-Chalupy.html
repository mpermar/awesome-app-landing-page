Description

Gothal Chalupy offers accommodation in a multi-functional areal with various sport and relaxation activities available the neighboring water-wold.

The property is surrounded by the Veľká Fatra and Low Tatras Mountains and set 12 km from Donovaly Ski Area.

All chalets have a private bathroom with a shower, a furnished kitchen, a TV with cable channels, WiFi and a safety deposit box.

The water-world offers various pools, wellness with saunas, restaurants, conference rooms, floating, badminton courts , golf simulator , bowling , fitness center with a climbing wall , foosball , pool and table tennis , workout playground , children’s playground and other facilities.

The complex includes 2 types of restaurant. The nearest airport is Poprad-Tatry Airport, 72 km from the property.
