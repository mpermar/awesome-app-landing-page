Description

100 metres from the nearest ski slopes, Residence Hotel & Club can be found amid the Donovaly Ski Area in the Low Tatras Mountains and offers you rooms and apartments with balcony and an Ayurveda spa centre.

The spacious rooms are equipped with a minibar and a flat-screen TV and the elegant apartments come with a fully equipped kitchenette.

Free WiFi is available in the entire hotel. The Grand Restaurant and the Diamond Hill Restaurant serve fine Slovak and international cuisine.

The Caf√© Residence has an inviting summer terrace. The on-site Ayur Ganesh spa centre offers you Indian Ayurveda massages, an Ayurveda sauna, a Finnish sauna and a steam bath.

Fitness facilities are available as well. Children can have fun in the kids' area and you can also make use of the beach volleyball court of the Residence Hotel & Club.

Tennis courts and a swimming pool are located 200 metres away. At the bowling and billiard centre 30 metres away you can take advantage of discounted prices as a hotel guest.
