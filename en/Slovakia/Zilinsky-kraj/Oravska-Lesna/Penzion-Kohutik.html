Description

Situated directly at the Orava Snow Ski Area and set in the Oravská Lesná, Penzión Kohútik offers accommodation in rooms, a restaurant, a bar, a games, room a terrace, a 24-hour front desk and a garden with barbecue facilities.

Free WiFi is available in all areas and free private parking is possible on site.

Each unit at Penzión Kohútik comes with mountain views, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, an electric kettle and a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries.

Other facilities offered at the property include ski storage, a daily made service and a kids' club.

A sauna, a hot tub, a ski school and massages are provided for a surcharge. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including skiing, cycling and hiking.
