Description

Offering a ski-to-door access and moutain view, Horský hotel Hájenka is located 10 km from Makov, 55km from Žilina.

The hotel has a barbecue and children's playground, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Free private parking is available on site. All rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV.

All rooms are equipped with a private bathroom fitted with a shower. Horský Hotel Hájenka features free WiFi throughout the property.

The hotel's restaurant serves an à la carte menu. There are conference room on site.

Guests can also enjoy a Sauna area which is available for an extra charge. You will find a 24-hour front desk and gift shop at the property.

Ski rental is available off site. Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including skiing, cycling and hiking.

Ostrava is 80 km from Horský hotel Hájenka Bratislava, while 210 km from the property.
