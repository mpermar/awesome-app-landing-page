Description

Hotel Port has a restaurant, bar, and shared lounge and water sports facilities in Trstená.

Among the facilities at this property are a 24-hour front desk and room service, along with free WiFi throughout the property.

Private parking can be arranged at an extra charge. At the hotel, each room is equipped with a wardrobe.

At Hotel Prístav the rooms are fitted with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

Continental and buffet breakfast options are available each morning at the accommodation.

Hotel Prístav offers a children's playground. Guests at the hotel will be able to enjoy activities in and around Trstená, like hiking, skiing and snorkelling.

Zakopane is 43 km from Hotel Pristav, while Kościelisko is 39 km from the property.
