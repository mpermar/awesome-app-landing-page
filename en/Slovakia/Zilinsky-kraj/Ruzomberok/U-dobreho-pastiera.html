Description

Located in Ružomberok, Koliba u Dobry shepherd offers a restaurant and a garden.

This self-catering accommodation features free WiFi and public parking on site. All rooms offer views of the surrounding mountains and feature a desk and a TV with satellite channels.

The bathrooms are fitted with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries. Meals can be enjoyed at the onsite restaurant.

At Koliba u dobrého pastiera guests can use barbecue facilities and relax on a terrace.

Guests can also rent a bike and cycle in the surroundings. The property is located 7 km away from Ruzomberok - Malino Brdo, 10 km away from Vlkolinec Village Attraction park and 16 km away from Gino Paradise Bešeňová.
