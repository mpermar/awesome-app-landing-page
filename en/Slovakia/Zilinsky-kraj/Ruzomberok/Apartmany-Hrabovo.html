Description

Nestled in the Veľka Fatra National Park, you can find Rezort Apartmany Hrabovo right on the shore of the Hrabovo Lake, 50 metres from the Skipark-Ruzomberok Ski Centre.

It offers self-catering apartments with a balcony and scenic mountain views. The modern apartments are fitted with a fully equipped kitchen, a living room with a seating area and a flat-screen satellite TV and a private bathroom with floor heating.

Video monitored private parking is provided for all apartments. An open-air hot tub and a sauna as well as massage services are available on request for a surcharge.

Guests receive a discount on ski passes. The centre of Ruzomberok is 4 km from the Apartmany Hrabovo.

The closest restaurant is 50 metres away, while the grocery store is 2 km from the property.
