Description

Located in Ružomberok and only 50 metres from the Malino-Brdo Ski Area. Studio Helena 5 - Apartment Hrabovo offers a self-catered accommodation with free WiFi.

The property is 3. 6 km from Vlkolinec Village. Accommodation will provide you with a TV and cable channels.

Complete with a microwave, the dining area also has a refrigerator and kitchenware.

Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with slippers. Extras include bed linen.

At Studio Helena 5 - Apartment Hrabovo you will find a garden, barbecue facilities and a terrace.

Other facilities like water sports facilities, ski equipment hire and ski pass vendor are offered.

Activities in the surrounding area include skiing, cycling and fishing. The property offers free parking.
