Description

Surrounded by the Western Tatras and set in the village of Žiar, Penzión pod Barancom offers accommodation in rooms, billiards, bowling, a garden and a terrace.

Each unit at Penzión pod Barancom comes with a private bathroom with a shower. A shared lounge area and a common well-fitted kitchen are available for all guests.

Some rooms are situated in the dependence building. Breakfast can be arranged upon a prior request and the nearest restaurant can be found 3 km away.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including hiking and bowling.

The property offers free parking. The Dolinky Ski slope can be reached within 4 km from the property.
