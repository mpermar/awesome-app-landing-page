Description

Hotel Boboty is located in Stefanova and offers transport to the Vratna Dolina ski centre.

It provides en suite rooms with satellite TV. The restaurant serves Slovak and international dishes.

Guests can make use of the ski and bicycle storage and relax at the café and the terrace.

The night bar has a DJ on request. The Boboty has an indoor swimming pool, sauna and billiards.

Sports equipment can be rented upon request. The property also offers discount for ski-passes to Vratna Mala Fatra.

The village of Terchová with the statue of Slovakia's national hero, Juraj Janosik, is 5 km away.
