Description

Hotel SOREA MÁJ is surrounded by the Low Tatras National Park, at the entrance to the Jánska Valley and at the end of the historic village of Liptovský Ján.

The Javorovica Ski Area can be reached within a 5-minutes walk. The hotel offers accommodation in single and double rooms, family rooms, suites and room for disabled clients.

Rooms are equipped with a bathroom, a toilet, satellite TV, a telephone, a safe and a balcony.

Room for disabled clients is situated on the ground floor without a balcony). WiFi connection is provided and parking is possible next to the hotel.

A varied offer of dishes are served in the hotel restaurant with views of the West Tatras.

Breakfast and dinner are served in a buffet style manner with hot and cold dishes, lunches are served a la carte.

A wide range of cocktails can be enjoyed in the BISTRO café or in the cocktail bar.

Additional services include an indoor swimming pool with thermal water with beneficial effects on the respiratory system, muscle system and skin problems a wellness zone with a sauna, massages, relaxation baths, sea baths and a fitness centre, a big conference room with capacity 150 persons and 2 small conference rooms with capacity 30 persons.

The guests can also enjoy billiards, a table tennis, board games and a kid's corner.
