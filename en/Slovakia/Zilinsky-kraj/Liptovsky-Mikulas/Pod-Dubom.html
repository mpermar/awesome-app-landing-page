Description

Set in the Demanova, 3 km from Liptovsky Mikulas and at the entrance to the Demanovska Dolina valley, Pod Dubom offers self-catered apartment with a private terrace, free parking and free WiFi.

The accommodation unit consists of 3 bedrooms, each featuring a TV. There are also a functionally equipped kitchenette and a bathroom with a shower.

Guests can also use the shared garden with a gazebo and barbecue facilities. Upon request, breakfast can be served.

The closest convenient store and restaurant are within 500 metres. The ski centres of Ziarce in Pavcina Lehota are reachable in 3 km.

Ski areas of Jasna Zahradky and Chopo Sever are further away, 12 km from the property.

The Aquapark Tatralandia water park is located at the distance of 5 km from the Pod Dubom apartment.
