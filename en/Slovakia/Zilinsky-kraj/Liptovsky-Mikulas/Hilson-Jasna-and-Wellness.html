Description

Offering views of the surrounding mountains, Hilson Jasna & Wellness features modern self-catering apartments in the outskirts of Liptovský Mikuláš.

Sauna facilities, a relaxation room and a hot tub are available on site at a surcharge.

Designed with natural materials, all of the Hilson Jasna’s accommodation units come with an LCD TV with satellite channels, separate bedroom and living areas and a well equipped kitchen with a dining area.

The bathrooms have a shower and free toiletries. Each apartment enjoys a balcony or a terrace and studios have a french window.

You can sample various beverages and choose from the selected wine list at the lobby bar.

A common terrace, kids’ playground and barbecue facilities are available on the premises.

The closest restaurant is right outside the Hilson Jasna. A grocery shop and a bus stop can be found in a 3-minute walk away.

Free wireless internet connection and free secured parking are provided. The water fun park Tatralandia Aquapark is 3 km away.
