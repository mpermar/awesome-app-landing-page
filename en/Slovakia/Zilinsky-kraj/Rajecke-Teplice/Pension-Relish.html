Description

Guest rooms in the guesthouse are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Rooms include a private bathroom with free toiletries, while some feature mountain views. All guest rooms at Pension Relish include air conditioning and a closet.

The accommodation offers a continental or buffet breakfast. The restaurant at Pension Relish serves European and Asian cuisine.

The guesthouse has a playground. Guests can take part in various activities in the surrounding areas, including skiing and hiking.

Lietava Castle is 2.9 miles from Pension Relish. Piesťany Airport is 52 miles away.
