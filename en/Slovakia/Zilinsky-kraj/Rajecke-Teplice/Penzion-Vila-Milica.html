Description

Enjoying a quiet location, Penzion Vila Milica is located directly in the centre of Rajecké Teplice.

It offers an on-site gym, rooms and self-catering apartments with satellite TV. Free Wi-Fi is provided in public areas.

All rooms and apartments comprise a private bathroom. Apartments also feature a kitchen and a living area.

Some units are located in the attic and are decorated with exposed wooden beams. A common living room is at guests’ disposal.

Children’s playground can be found right next to the property. Free private parking is possible on site.

Guests can relax in the Aphrodite Spa Centre, which is 100 metres away. The nearest restaurant is 100 metres away.

A bus stop and a train station are both at distance of 400 metres. Hiking trails start in Lúčanská Malá Fatra, 2 km away.

Fishing is possible in Tomato River, also 2 km away. Rajecka Lesna Ski Resort is 11 km from Penzion Vila Milica.

Lietavský Castle is 4 km away. Rajec Golf Resort is at a distance of 12 km and the town of Žilina can be reached within 15 km.
