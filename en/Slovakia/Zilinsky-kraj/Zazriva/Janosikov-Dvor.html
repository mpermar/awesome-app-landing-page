Description

Located in the Mala Fatra National Park, Janosikov Dvor consists of traditional Slovak cottages, mainly with wooden furniture.

It offers free Wi-Fi in the restaurant, free parking and a sauna. All rooms provide a private bathroom, a toilet and heating.

There is a sauna available in one of the cottages and there is also a cottage with a shared kitchen and one with a stylish restaurant.

The Jánošík’s Tavern serves traditional Slovak cuisine. Guests can benefit of the sheltered barbecue facilities in front of the Janosikov Dvor cottages, as well as of the little pool.

A large playground is also available. The property features a hot tub for up to 5 people and there is a minigolf course.
