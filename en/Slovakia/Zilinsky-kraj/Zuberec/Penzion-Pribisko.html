Description

Penzion Pribisko is 2 km away from the Milotin and Janovky ski centres in the High Tatras, and 8 km to the Zverovka-Spalena Ski Area, where a free ski bus operates every morning.

It features a bowling alley, table tennis, squash and mini golf facilities. The guest house has a private fish pond in the garden, where a children’s playground and barbecue facilities can be found as well.

The Pribisko also houses a sauna with a cooling pool. The restaurant, located in the main building, serves a variety of Slovak specialities, and buffet breakfast is served every morning.

Free WiFi is provided in public areas and free parking is available. All accommodation units come with free wired internet, satellite TV and a private bathroom with shower.

Some units also have flat-screen TV, a fridge or a balcony. The village centre of Zuberec is 1.

5 km away. Guests can also visit the geothermal spa in the Meander Park in Oravice, 12 km away, or the historical, 19th-century outdoor Museum, 3 km away.
