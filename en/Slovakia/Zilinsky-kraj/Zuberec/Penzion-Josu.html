Description

Built in traditional northern Oravan style, Penzion Josu is situated only 150 metres from the centre of Zuberec.

The resort features a restaurant, bowling alley, tennis court, wellness facilities, massages and a summer terrace.

Free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV with satellite channel and seating area is provided in all rooms at Penzion Josu.

Private bathrooms come with a shower and some rooms have a balcony. With traditional décor, the restaurant offers hearty Slovak food.

Barbecue facilities and a salaš gazebo can be used at the resort. The wellness facilities include a Finnish sauna, infrared cabin, steam bath, relaxing room and massages.

Guests are given a 15% discount from the Thermal Park in Besenova. The Milotin Ski area can be reached in 5 minutes by car while Zverovka Ski Area is a 10-minute drive.

10% discount applies to the ski lifts in Spalena dolina. Aquapark Oravice is is a 15-minute drive from the property.
