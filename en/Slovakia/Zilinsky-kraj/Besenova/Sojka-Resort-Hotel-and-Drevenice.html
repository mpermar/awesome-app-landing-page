Description

Sojka Resort - Hotel & Drevenice is situated in the middle of Liptov region between the High and the Low Tatras, in the peaceful village of Malatíny.

It offers accommodation in various buildings built in a traditional Slovak style and placed in a spacious garden.

The onsite wellness centre features a pool, a kids pool, a Finnish sauna, an infra sauna, a steam sauna, a herbal sauna and a relaxation room.

The guests can also enjoy massages, various procedures as well as a wellness bar.

Wellness is only available for an extra charge if staying in a chalet or a cottage.

In the surrounding gardens you can also find children´s playground, sand pits, a table tennis, a minigolf, a small statue gallery, a multi-functional playground or an outdoor buffet.

The rooms come with free WiFi, a TV with satellite channels, a safety deposit box and a bathroom with a shower and free toiletries.

Other facilities include a fitness centre and a retro bar. The on-site restaurant serves delicious Slovak and international dishes.

A wide range of Slovakian wines, as well as local and Belgian beers, can be enjoyed at the bar.

Sojka resort is located near the thermal spring of Besenova and the large Aquapark Tatralandia.

It is a stone's throw from the beautiful caves of Demänovská dolina, the wooden church of Svätý Kríž and the open-air museum of Pribylina.
