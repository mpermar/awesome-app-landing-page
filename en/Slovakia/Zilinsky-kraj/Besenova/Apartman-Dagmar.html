Description

Apartmán Dagmar is located on the second floor of the Wellness Hotel Luka amid the Thermal Park Gino Paradise Bešeňová with pools, saunas, 7 restaurants and fitness facilities.

Guest of the Dagmar apartment get a 10% discount on the entrance fee. A fully equipped kitchenette, a private bathroom with bathrobe and hairdryer and flat-screen TV with satellite channels are features of the apartment.

Free WiFi internet access is available as well. There are 7 restaurants, bars and fast food stores within in the Thermal Park Bešeňová.

You can park your car free of charge on site. The Kalameny Thermal Spring is 6. 4 km, the Lucky Thermal Spa is 7 km and the Aquapark Tatralandia is 17 km away from Apartmán Dagmar.
