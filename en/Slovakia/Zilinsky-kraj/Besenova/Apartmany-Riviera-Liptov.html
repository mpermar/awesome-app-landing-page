Description

Apartments Riviera - Liptov are located in the Slovakian Liptov region, in the village of Liptovské Sliače.

The location is situated between Chočské vrchy and the High and the Low Tatras. The accommodation offers a free car park and a high-speed free WiFi connection in both the apartments and the garden.

You can enjoy a large garden, a wooden gazebo, a fully equipped kitchen, a large lounge, cozy rooms and a bathroom.

In summer, a large garden can be used, where sandpit, swings and a large trampoline are also available for children.

It is also possible to play darts or petanque. Tennis courts can be found within 2 km from the property.

The surrounding area offers various attraction including thermal baths, ski resorts, hiking trails and cycling paths.

The nearest airport is Poprad-Tatry Airport, 65 km from. Fun and relaxation can be found in the water parks Aquapark Gino Paradise Bešeňová 8 km.

Aquapark Tatralandia 12 km, or Vitalpark Lúčky 8 km. If you like winter sports, you can visit the winter ski resorts of Skipark Donovaly reachable within 25 minute drive.

Skipark Jasná cab be reached within a 25 minute drive or Skipark Ružomberok is set within a 10 minute drive.
