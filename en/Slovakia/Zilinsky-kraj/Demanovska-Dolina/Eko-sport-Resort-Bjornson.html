Description

Situated in Demanovska Valley and only 100 metres from the Jasna Ski Area, Eko-šport Resort Björnson offers accommodation furnished in the chalet-style and with views of the surrounding countryside.

Guests can enjoy accommodation in the rooms situated in the main building or in the villa and the hostel rooms situated on the Björnsonka grounds alongside the main hotel.

each unit features a TV and free WiFi. Meals can be enjoyed in the onsite restaurant serving traditional Slovak dishes as well as selected international specialties.

It also includes a terrace with panoramic views. Various facilities provided by the property include the Hriatô outdoor relaxation area with wooden swimming pools and a relaxation room, barbecue facilities, a children´s playground, a pétanque court, basketball court, sports equipment for hire and an activity room in the main building.

Eko-šport Resort Björnson also features free parking, a 24-hours reception and daily cleaning.

You can engage in various activities, such as skiing and cycling. Chopok mountain is 2.

8 km from Eko-šport hotel Björnson, while Demanovská Ice Cave is 5 km away. The nearest airport is Poprad-Tatry Airport, 49 km from the property.
