Description

Surrounded by the Low Tatras National Park and located at the very beginning of Demänovska Valley, Apartment house Vila Vista is just a few minutes ride from Aquapark Tatralandia, Gino Paradise Besenova and the Jasna Nizke Tatry Ski Area.

Breakfast can be served in the restaurant located 50 metres from the property. It offers a free infrared cabin, accommodation with a flat-screen satellite TV, and free WiFi.

Guests can make use of a gazebo with barbecue facilities, a trampoline and table tennis.

A hot tub in the garden can be used at a surcharge. The private parking is guarded by a camera system.

All accommodation units comprise a private bathroom with shower. Apartments also feature a fully equipped kitchenette with a dining area and a sofa.

Some units also come with a balcony. Within walking distance from the Apartmanovy Dom Vila Vista there are several restaurants.

Free on-site parking is provided and the property can also arranged an airport transfer for a surcharge.

It is a good starting point for various outdoor sport activities like skiing and hiking.

Jasna Ski Center is an 11-minute drive away. The Demanovska Cave of Liberty Cave is 4 km away.

Guests of Apartmanovy Dom Vila Vista receive free Liptov Region Card, which provides various discounts in the regional ski centres as well as a free ski bus to the Jasna Ski Centre.
