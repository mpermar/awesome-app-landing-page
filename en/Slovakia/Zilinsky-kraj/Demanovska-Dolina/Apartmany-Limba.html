Description

Located in Demänovská Dolina, Apartmány Limba is housed in the alpine-style building.

It offers self-catering studios and apartments only 6 km from Jasná Ski Resort. Free Wi-Fi is provided.

Built in 2012, all apartments and studios comprise a fully equipped kitchenette, a living area and a flat-screen cable TV.

They come either with a furnished patio or a balcony with a mountain view. A bar with a summer terrace is next to the Limba.

The nearest restaurant is 100 metres from the property and the nearest grocery store can be reached within 500 metres.

Breakfast can be arranged on site. A children's playground, table tennis facilities, basketball and badminton courts are 20 metres away.

Bike rental can be arranged 100 metres away. Tatralandia Aquapark is at a distance of 10 km.

Bešeňová Thermal Park is 25 km from the Limba Apartmány. Free private parking is possible on site.
