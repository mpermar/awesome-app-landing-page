Description

Hotel Mikulášska Chata, located in the Low Tatras, offers modern rooms with a balcony and free Wi-Fi in a restored cottage.

It features 2 bowling alleys and extensive spa facilities available for a surcharge.

All of the rooms at Hotel Mikulášska feature satellite TV and a work desk. The wellness area features a sauna, a hot tub and many other spa facilities including a roman bath, ice well and herbal steam room.

Other activities available nearby include skiing and hiking. Mikulášska Chata is a 30-minute drive from the centre of Liptovský Mikulás.

The Liptovská Mara reservoir offers a variety of leisure facilities. The hotel restaurant serves Slovakian cuisine and International dishes.
