Description

Hotel Čingov Slovenský raj is 6 km from Spisska Nova Ves and the High Tatras National Park.

Free WiFi is available and free parking is possible on site. The hotel offers comfortable accommodation in an exceptionally beautiful natural landscape.

Poprad and the Poprad-Tatry Airport is approximately 20 km away. During your stay you can enjoy swimming pool with 31 degrees temperature, a wellness centre with 5 types of sauna, a hot tub, a cooling pool and massages.

During summer months you can also relax in a garden and enjoy a live music during dinner time.
