Description

Stay in the heart of Kosice, in one of the charming rooms and suites of the City Residence apartment hotel in the Old Town, within walking distance of all important sights.

Bicycles with bike-maps are available at the property for free. Free private parking is possible on site and shuttle service can be arranged.

Each unit is air-conditioned and features a flat-screen TV, a sofa, a seating area, a minibar, a kitchen or a kitchenette, a dining area and a bathroom with free toiletries.

The restaurant offers a wide choice of meals and beverages, plus a daily lunch menu.

During the warmer months you can also enjoy the tasty food in the charming inner courtyard.

Complimentary breakfast is served to your room. Walking south, you reach the main street, Hlavna, with intriguing sights like the Immaculata statue, the State Theatre and the beautiful St.

Elisabeth Cathedral, built in Gothic style. Around the city centre you will find many other architectural sights plus many shops and places to eat and drink.
