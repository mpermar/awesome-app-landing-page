Description

Winter&Summer Resort is quietly set in Zdiar, in the Belianske Tatras. Featuring free Wi-Fi and free parking, it offers a restaurant, with a children’s corner, serving Slovak and international dishes.

The property offers elegant suite and rooms. All accommodation units provide a blend of traditional and modern.

They comprise a flat-screen TV, a seating area and a private bathroom. Guests can relax in the garden with a terrace and a playground.

The entire area is filled with historical and cultural monuments and thermal pools.

Hiking, skiing and rafting on the Dunajec River are popular activities in this region.

A grocery shop and a bus stop are 30 metres away. Zdiar Strednica Ski Area is within 3 km.

Bachledová Bikepark is at a distance of 5 km. Tatranska Kotlina Train Station is within 15 km.

Vrbov Thermal Park is 30 km, while Aquacity Poprad is 34 km from the Resort Winter&Summer.
