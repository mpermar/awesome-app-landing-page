Description

Situated in the center of the Zdiar Ski Area and close to many ski resorts and hiking attractions, Penzion Bayer offers en-suite accommodation, discounts for the nearby ski resorts and aquaparks, a bar, a restaurant, a terrace and free WiFi in all areas.

All units in Penzion Bayer come with a TV with satellite channels and some rooms feature a balcony.

A shared lounge area can be enjoyed by all guests. The meals are served in the on-site dining room.

The nearest grocery store and 3 restaurants can be found within 300 metres from the property.

Free on-site parking, ski storage, luggage storage, a garden with barbecue facilities, darts and a children’s playground are available for all guests.

A seasonal outdoor hot tub is offered for a surcharge. The Strachan Skicentrum, the Strednica Skicentrum and the Bachledova Dolina Ski Area are reachable within 3.

5 km. Belianska Cave located in the Tatranska Kotlina is situated 8 km away and the Aquapark Vrbov is set within 30 km.
