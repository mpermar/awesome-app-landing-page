Description

Apartment Aqua Tatry is situated 1 km from Poprad city centre and 500 metres from the Aquacity Poprad, and offers a furnished terrace with access to the garden, a 15% discount to the Aquacity Poprad and free WiFi.

This spacious unit is spread over 2 floors. A living room with a seating area, cable TV and wood-burning stove, as well as a kitchen, is on the ground level.

The 2 bedrooms are on the upper floor, with 2 bathrooms included in the apartment.

The nearest restaurant is only 10 metres away and the grocery store is within a 5-minute walk.

Public parking is possible on site, free of charge. Aquacity Poprad is reachable within a 10-minute walk.
