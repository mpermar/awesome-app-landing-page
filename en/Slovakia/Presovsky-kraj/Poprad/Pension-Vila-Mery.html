Description

Vila Mery is situated in central Poprad, near High Tatras Mountains, and benefits from views of the river bank.

There is a garden and WiFi is available free throughout the hotel. All of the spacious apartments at Vila Mery feature satellite TV, an en-suite bathroom and a minibar.

Some of the apartments also have balconies, offering charming views along the Poprad riverfront.

Breakfast is served in the dining room, and guests can also eat at the outdoor terrace and grill.

There are also several restaurants within a few minutes’ walk of the hotel. The High and Low Tatras mountains provide a range of nearby outdoor activities, including hiking, cycling and fishing.

National Park Slovak Paradise is a 30-minute drive from Vila Mery. Station Poprad-Spišská is just 200 metres from the hotel.
