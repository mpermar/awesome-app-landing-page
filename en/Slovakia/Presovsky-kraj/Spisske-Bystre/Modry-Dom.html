Description

In the small town of Spišské Bystré, Modrý Dom is a 5-minute drive from Kubasok Ski Centre, and offers stylishly designed accommodation units with satellite TV and free Wi-Fi.

All accommodation units come with a private bathroom with a shower. The apartments comprise a terrace and come with a functionally equipped kitchen.

For guests staying in the rooms, there is a shared kitchen available. Modrý Dom is within 25 km from 3 national parks - Low Tatras, High Tatras and Slovak Paradise.

Liptovska Teplicka Ski Resort is 13 km away, while Spa Aquacity is reachable in 10 km.

A grocery store, restaurants and a bus stop are all in town, a 5-minute walk away.
