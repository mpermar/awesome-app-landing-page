Description

Located in the historical centre of Presov, next to the PKO cultural centre and 50 metres from the main square, A 7 - Avenue 7 Penzion is a 3-star accommodation with a flat-screen satellite TV, an on-site restaurant, and Wi-Fi for free.

All spacious rooms contain a private bathroom, provided with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

The large apartments additionally comprise a living room with a seating area and a fully-equipped kitchenette, and a bathroom fitted with a corner bathtub.

The restaurant with a winter garden serves small snacks and a breakfast can be enjoyed every morning at additional cost.

Children can spend their time in the park and playground 300 metres from the A 7 - Avenue 7 Penzion.

St. Nicholas Church is 200 metres away from the property. Free camera-secured private parking is possible on site.
