Description

Guest-house Pavilon D is situated in a picturesque mountain surroundings in the heart of the High Tatras Mountains, in the Novy Smokovec Ski Area and offers fully equipped studios with WiFi and free parking space.

The property is within a walking distance from the shops and amenities of Stary Smokovec.

All studios at Pavilon D guest house come with WiFi connection a kitchen with all necessary appliances and a bathroom with shower.

Guests will enjoy the central location, with groceries and restaurants within 200 metres from the house.

Bike rental or ski equipment rental is available 200 metres away. A lockable storage space for ski-equipment is also provided.

The Sibir Tram Stop is set 100 metres away and the Roman-Catholic Church can be found within 300 metres.

The cable car to Hrebienok is reachable within 1 km, while the distance to the Jakubkova Ski Resort is just 200 metres.

The Tatranska Lomnica Ski Area is situated 7 km away and the Strbske Pleso Ski Area is located within 15 km.
