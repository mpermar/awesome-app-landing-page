Description

Situated in Domaša Dobrá, 46 km from Košice, Hotel Zelená Lagúna features a children's playground and sun terrace.

The hotel has a free spa centre and hot tub, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

Free WiFi is offered throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Rooms come with a flat-screen TV. Some units feature a seating area where you can relax.

Views of the lake or pool are featured in certain rooms. Each room comes with a private bathroom.

Other facilities feature a kid´s corner, a wine tavern, a common lounge and a library.

There is a 24-hour front desk, a shared lounge and gift shop at the property. Guests can also enjoy on-site swimming pool free of charge.

This hotel has a private beach area and bike hire is available. You can engage in various activities, such as cycling and fishing.

Prešov is 33 km from Hotel Zelená Lagúna, while Michalovce is 33 km from the property.
