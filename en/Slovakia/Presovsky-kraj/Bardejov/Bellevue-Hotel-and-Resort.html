Description

Bellevue Hotel and Resort is located on a hill high above the historic town of Bardejov in Northeastern Slovakia, offering impressive views of the valley below.

This old town is important for its well-preserved medieval centre that has been listed under UNESCO World Heritage Sites since year 2000.

The spacious rooms and the suites with their marble-lined bathrooms provide a high degree of comfort and privacy.

The comfortable bedding was especially designed and produced for the hotel by a major American bedding manufacturer.

The property also offers an indoor children playing area and 3 small private lounges, which can be used for private events or dinning.

Banquet and wedding facilities are also provided. Bellevue Hotel and Resort offers a massage room, hiking in the oxygen trail around hotel, as well as tennis courts and a play area for children.
