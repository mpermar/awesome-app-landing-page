Description

Featuring a home cinema and a games room with table tennis and billiards, the family-run Penzión Biela Voda offers functionally decorated rooms with mountain view.

Located in Veľká Lomnica Village, this holiday home is 7 km from Tatranská Lomnica Ski Resort.

All rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV, an electric kettle and a fridge. The private bathroom comes with shower and a hairdryer.

Local and international drinks can be sampled at the bar. A large garden suitable for ball games is available, as well as a summer terrace with a barbecue.

Guests can also play table football and darts in the guest house. The nearest bus stop can be found at a distance of 700 metres from Biela Voda Penzión.

The Studený Potok Train Station is 1. 2 km away. Poprad Airport and Aquacity, as well as Vrbov Thermal Park, are within a radius of 6 km from Biela Voda Guest House.
