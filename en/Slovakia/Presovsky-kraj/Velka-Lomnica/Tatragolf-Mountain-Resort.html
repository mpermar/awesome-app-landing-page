Description

Tatragolf Resort in Velka Lomnica is just a 5-minute walk from the 27-hole Black Stork Golf Club and offers accommodation with panoramic views of the High Tatras.

This modern complex consists of 11 identical buildings and offers spacious studios, one and two-bedrooms apartments.

Each of them features a well-fitted kitchenette, a flat-screen TV and free WiFi. A 24-hours reception, a kid´s corner and an outdoor playground are also available.

Each apartment has either a balcony or a terrace and most of them provide High Tatras views.

Onsite parking is possible free of charge and a private garage parking is provided for an additional cost.

Meals can be enjoyed in the on-site restaurant which serves traditional Slovak and international dishes.

A private VIP wellness with a sauna, a hot tub and massages is available for a surcharge.

The property also offers various discounts up to 25% to the water-parks in the area.

An on-site water-park is opened in July and August. The Tatranska Lomnica Ski Area and the Vrbov Thermal Park are both 6 km away.

Strbske Pleso can be reached by car in 30 minutes and Stary Smokovec in 15 minutes.
