Description

Right at the banks of the Strbske Pleso lake, the Hotel Solisko is situated in the High Tatras Mountains, 1351 metres above sea level.

It has its own spa and fitness centre and a lakeside restaurant. Rooms are equipped with elegant, modern furniture, a flat-screen cable TV and a minibar.

Free Wi-Fi is available in the entire Solisko hotel. You have free access to the spa and fitness centre.

Spa facilities include a hot tub, a sauna, a cold-water pool and various massages.

There are also private rooms with spa baths. Traditional Tatra cuisine and specialities of the Italian cuisine are served in the lakeside restaurant.

The cigar bar offers a wide selection of fines cognac and cigars and kids can have fun in the children's corner.

The next lift of the Strbske Pleso Ski Area is 800 metres away. Numerous hiking and cycling paths can be found in the surroundings.

Skis and bikes can be rented on site and you can also try paragliding, bungee jumping and many more activities nearby.
