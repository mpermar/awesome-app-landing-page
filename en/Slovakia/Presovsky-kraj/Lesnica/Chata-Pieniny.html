Description

Featuring free WiFi and a restaurant, Chata Pieniny offers pet-friendly accommodation in Lesnica, a 30-minute walk from Szczawnica.

The guest house has a ski-bus, a ski school and guests can enjoy meals at the restaurant.

Free private parking is available on site. Each room has a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Certain rooms have a seating area to relax in after a busy day. All rooms are fitted with a private bathroom.

You will find a 24-hour front desk, a shared lounge, gift shop and shops at the property.

This guest house has ski storage space and ski equipment hire is available. You can play tennis, table tennis, and billiards at the guest house.

The guest house also offers bike hire. Białka Tatrzanska is 25 km from Chata Pieniny, while Bukowina Tatrzańska is 27 km from the property.
