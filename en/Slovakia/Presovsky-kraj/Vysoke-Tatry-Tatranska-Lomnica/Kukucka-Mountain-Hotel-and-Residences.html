Description

Featuring a spa area, Kukučka Mountain Hotel and Residences is centrally located in Tatranská Lomnica Ski Resort, 200 metres from one of the steepest cable cars in Europe, going to Lomnický Peak.

It offers modern-style accommodation with free Wi-Fi and a ski-to-door access. All apartments are decorated in mountain style design featuring wooden furnishings as well as stone walls.

They come with a seating area and bedrooms, each equipped with a flat-screen TV. Kukučka’s restaurant serves traditional Slovak cuisine and a great variety of international dishes.

A cigar lounge is also available. Guests can relax in the wellness area available free of charge and comprising a Finnish sauna, steam bath and a hot tub for 5 persons.

For the more active ones, Kukučka Hotel and Residences can be a great starting point for hiking in the High Tatras.

A children’s corner is also provided. The nearest grocery shop, as well as the Main Bus and Train Station, are 500 metres away.

A golf course can be reached within 8 km. Free private parking is available on site.
