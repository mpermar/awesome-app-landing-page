Description

Guest rooms at the hotel come with a seating area. Rooms contain a private bathroom, while selected rooms also feature a balcony. The rooms have a desk.

A continental breakfast is available daily at Las Perlitas Playa Samara.

A variety of popular activities are available in the area around the accommodation, including cycling and hiking.

Santa Teresa Beach is 30 miles from Las Perlitas Playa Samara, while Nosara is 10 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Tamarindo Airport, 35 miles from the property.
