Description

At the hotel, every room has a balcony with a sea view. With a private bathroom, certain accommodations at Hotel Peace & Lodge also feature a patio. At the accommodation, each room is equipped with a seating area.

A continental breakfast is served at the property. At Hotel Peace & Lodge you'll find a restaurant serving French cuisine. A Vegetarian option can also be requested.

Santa Teresa Beach is 27 miles from the hotel. The nearest airport is Tambor Airport, 32 miles from the property.
