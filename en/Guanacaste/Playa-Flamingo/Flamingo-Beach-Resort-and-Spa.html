Description

The air-conditioned rooms and studios at Flamingo Beach Resort & Spa feature free Wi-Fi, flat-screen cable TV and a coffee maker. Each one comes with a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The Flamingo Beach has a fitness center, tennis courts and a range of classes including cocktail making, salsa dancing and water aerobics. There is a games center with free billiards, ping-pong and a mini-movie theater.

Flamingo Beach Resortâ€™s Las Arenas Restaurant serves a wide range of international food and offers nightly themed buffet dinners. Bar Magallanes offers poolside dining and a swim-up bar, and Sunset Lounge serves cocktails with amazing views. There is also an on-site casino.

Playa Flamingo is a 10 minute drive from Reserva Conchal Golf Course and 15 minute drive from Las Baulas National Marine Park, where leatherback turtles nest. In 30 minutes you can drive to the Congo Trail zip-line course. Daniel Oduber Airport is a 45 minute drive away.
