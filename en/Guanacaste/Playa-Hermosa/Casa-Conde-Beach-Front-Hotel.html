Description

The air-conditioned rooms at the Casa Conde Beach Front Hotel all feature free Wi-Fi, cable TV and a coffee maker. Each room has a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The hotel’s Mare Calmo restaurant serves a range of international cuisine. There is also a poolside bar.

The Casa Conde has an on-site gym and a hot tub, and massages are available. The hotel also arranges trips to the national parks in the area, as well as to Tamarindo, Conchal and Flamingo Beaches, among others.

Within a 20-minute drive you can find shopping centers, supermarkets, bars and casinos. Daniel Oduber Airport is also under a 20-minute drive away.
