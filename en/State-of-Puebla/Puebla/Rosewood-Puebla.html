Description

All guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Featuring a private bathroom, certain accommodations at Rosewood Puebla also offer a terrace. Guest rooms include a closet.

An American breakfast is served every morning at the property.

The reception can offer helpful tips for getting around the area.

Cuauhtemoc Stadium is 2.8 miles from the accommodation, while Estrella de Puebla is 2.9 miles away. Hermanos Serdán International Airport is 14 miles from the property.

Puebla Centro is a great choice for travelers interested in museums, architecture and old-town exploration.
