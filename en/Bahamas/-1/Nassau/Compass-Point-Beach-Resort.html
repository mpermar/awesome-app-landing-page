Description

The air-conditioned rooms have a light decoration with colorful details, flat-screen cable TV and an iPod docking station. The private bathroom with shower offers hairdryers and brand toiletries for free. The rooms also feature a safe.

Some activities that can be performed at the property are snorkeling, diving and touring among the islands. Compass Point also offers sun terraces, an outdoor swimming pool and spa services. There is also an on-site restaurant.

The Lyndon Pindling International Airport can be reached in 7 minutes by car.
