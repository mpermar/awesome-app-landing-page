Description

Free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen cable TV, and coffee-making facilities are featured in all rooms at this Ridgeland Drury Inn and Suites Jackson. A desk is also included.

Guests can stay active in the fitness center and fuel their workout with a few snacks from the guest pantry. A business center and on-site laundromat are available at the Drury Inn and Suites Jackson Ridgeland as well.

Guests can savor a rotating menu of hot food items with soft drinks during a nightly reception. Cold beverages such as beer, wine, and mixed drinks are also available.

The property is 4 miles from Belhaven College and 10 miles from the Mississippi Braves Stadium, home of the Braves major league baseball team.
