Description

The rooms are equipped with a toaster, stovetop, a electric tea pot, a shower, free toiletries and a closet. Rooms are complete with a shared bathroom equipped with a hair dryer, while selected rooms will provide you with a balcony. All guest rooms have a seating area.

A continental, à la carte or Asian breakfast is served at the property.

A variety of popular activities are available in the area around the property, including fishing and hiking.

Baku is 25 miles from the guesthouse.
