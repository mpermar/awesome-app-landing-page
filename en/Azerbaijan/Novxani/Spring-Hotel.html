Description

The rooms come with a flat-screen TV with cable channels, fridge, a electric tea pot, a bath and a closet. The rooms include air conditioning, and certain units at the resort have a balcony.

A continental or buffet breakfast is served at the property. There's an on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of European dishes.

Spring Hotel has a playground. You can play ping pong at the accommodation, and car rental is available.

A business center and newspapers are available on site. Speaking Azerbaijani and English, staff are always on hand to help at the reception.
