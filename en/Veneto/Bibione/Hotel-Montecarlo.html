Description

Hotel Montecarlo is in the heart of Bibione, 1,700 feet from the town's thermal spa. Golf Club Lignano is 6 miles away, while Lignano Sabbiadoro is a 20 minute drive away.

Rooms are air conditioned and come with wooden or carpeted floors, a minibar, and a TV with satellite channels. Free Wi-Fi is available in the business center.

Breakfast is buffet style and both the breakfast room and restaurant offer panoramic views out over the Adriatic Sea. Gluten-free and children's menus are available.

Bikes can be rented at the front desk, with concierge service until 10 pm. Parking is free.
