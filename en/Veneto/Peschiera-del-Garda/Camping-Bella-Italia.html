Description

Sports facilities at the campsite include ping-pong and a multi-purpose sports field for soccer, volleyball and basketball. There are tournaments, kids clubs and a full timetable of daytime and evening entertainment.

All air conditioned, the bungalows and apartments come with a kitchenette and comfortable living area. Most have a patio.

There are a total of 3 restaurants at Bella Italia Camping, including a traditional pizzeria. You also have a mini market, ice cream parlor and a bar with terrace overlooking the lake.

Free parking is available and the campsite is easily reached from highway A4. The peninsula of Sirmione is a 15 minute drive away.
