Description

The air-conditioned rooms at the Enjoy include satellite LCD TV, hardwood floors and a minibar. Wi-Fi is free throughout the hotel.

Served on the grounds when the weather's nice, the breakfast here includes bacon and eggs, juices and pastries. The restaurant has a buffet lunch and serves an Italian menu for dinner.

The hotel is 950 feet from Lake Garda and a 5-minute walk from Peschiera del Garda Train Station. The spa town of Lazise is 2.5 miles away.
