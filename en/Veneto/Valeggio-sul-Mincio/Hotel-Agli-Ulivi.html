Description

The hotel features a garden, where children can play, and a sun terrace with lounge chairs. The restaurant serves specialties from Valeggio, including the famous homemade tortellini.

The hotel is the ideal starting point to visit famous amusement parks like Gardaland, the Thermal Park of the Garda and Park Zoo Safari. Verona and Mantova are also close by. The surrounding countryside is ideal for enjoy long walks and biking.
