Description

Hotel Eliseo Terme includes indoor and outdoor pools, both at 35° C, and open daily from 7 am until 7 pm. Hot tub, sauna and water aerobics courses are also available.

Rooms are elegant and include a satellite flat-screen TV, parquet floors and a small fridge. Each has a private bathroom with soft bathrobes and slippers.

The Eliseo's restaurant overlooks the park, and in summer meals are served on the terrace. Breakfast is an American buffet.

The hotel is set on the main shopping street of Montegrotto. The bus to Padua stops just outside the door.
