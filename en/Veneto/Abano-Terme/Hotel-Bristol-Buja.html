Description

Bristol Buja has been in the family for 3 generations. It has an excellent team of helpful and professional staff.

In the grounds you will find a tennis court, mini golf course, and a bowling green. Indoors there is a reading room and a game room. The spa center offers a wide range of thermal treatments and massages.

As a guest at the Bristol Buja Hotel you can enjoy a special discount on your green fee at nearby golf courses.

The restaurant serves Italian and international dishes. In warm weather, breakfast is served outside by the swimming pool.
