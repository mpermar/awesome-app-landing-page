Description

All rooms and suites at Casa Country are air-conditioned and equipped with flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, and a balcony.

Villa Casa's private park also features a little lake and horse stables.

Villa Casa Country is within easy reach of Venice, Padua and Terme Euganee. The nearest train station is in Montegrotto Terme.
