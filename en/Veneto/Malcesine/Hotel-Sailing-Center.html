Description

Completely renovated, Sailing's bright rooms and junior suites all have a private bathroom, satellite TV, and centralized heating and air conditioning. Some rooms overlook the garden.

Hotel Sailing Center features a fitness room. The spa area includes a free sauna and Turkish bath. You can also rent mountain bikes on site.

Sailing Center Hotel faces the Bay of Campagnola and is near a sailing and windsurfing school. The hotel's storage room is suitable for windsurfing equipment.
