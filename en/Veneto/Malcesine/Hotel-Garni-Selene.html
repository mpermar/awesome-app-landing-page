Description

All guest rooms at the family-run Hotel Garni Selene offer a balcony overlooking the lake. Each room has simple furnishings and a private bathroom.

A varied breakfast buffet is served daily in the dining room, which has patio windows overlooking the garden, or on the terrace during the summer months. The hotel bar is open for light refreshments throughout the day.

Boats to Limone Sul Garda leave from Malcesine pier, 3.1 mi away. The Rovereto exit of the A22 Motorway can be reached in 35 minutes by car.
