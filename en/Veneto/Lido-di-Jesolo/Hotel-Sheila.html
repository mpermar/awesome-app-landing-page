Description

Accommodations here are bright and colorful. All air conditioned, they come with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and either a balcony or terrace.

Breakfast is buffet style and drinks are available throughout the day at the bar. You can reserve lounge chairs at the beach in front of the Sheila Hotel.

Guests have a sports center with mini golf and tennis courts 1,000 feet away. The hotel offers a free outdoor parking lot and garage parking for an extra daily fee, both subject to availability. There is a bus stop right outside.

Children and Extra Beds
All children are welcome.
All children under 2 years are charged EUR 10 per night in a crib.
All children under 12 years are charged EUR 40 per night for extra beds.
Any additional older children or adults are charged EUR 50 per night for extra beds.
The maximum number of extra beds in a room is 1.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
