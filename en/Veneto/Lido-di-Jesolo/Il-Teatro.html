Description

Rooms and suites include a fully equipped kitchenette with dishwasher. Decorated in a modern-style, they also provide air conditioning and flat-screen satellite TV. A hairdryer is available in the bathroom.

During the summer, the hotel offers a sun umbrella, sun bed and deck chair for free on the private beach. Pizzeria Il Teatro, adjacent to the property, is a popular restaurant serving traditional pizzas.

The Teatro hotel is 1,700 feet from the Aqualandia water park. Golf Club Jesolo is one mile away. Ferries for Venice depart from Punta Sabbioni, a 30-minute drive away.
