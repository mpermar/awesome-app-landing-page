Description

Favouring fun and relaxation, Hotel Stockholm has a swimming pool, a guest lounge and a full bar. On the beach, you can enjoy complimentary deck chairs and sun umbrellas.

The Stockholm’s own restaurant serves a breakfast buffet and a wide choice of menus including vegetarian options. Open for lunch and dinner, the restaurant offers a cozy atmosphere.

Guest rooms feature elegant contemporary furniture, all modern conveniences and balconies. For families and groups, connecting rooms are available.

Policies

Children and Extra Beds
All children are welcome.
One child under 1 year is charged EUR 10 per person per night when using existing beds.
One child under 1 year is charged EUR 10 per person per night in a crib.
One child from 1 to 3 years is charged 50 % of the room stay per night and person in an extra bed.
One child from 4 to 12 years is charged 70 % of the room stay per night and person in an extra bed.
The maximum number of extra beds/cribs in a room is 2.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
