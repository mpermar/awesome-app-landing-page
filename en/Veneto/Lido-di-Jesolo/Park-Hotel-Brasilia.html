Description

With classic décor, rooms provide satellite TV with Sky satellite channels channels. The bathroom is complete with hairdryer and toiletries, while the private terrace comes with tables and chairs.

A breakfast buffet is served every morning in the dining room that overlooks the grounds and the swimming pool. The Ipanema restaurant features a sea-view porch and offers fish specialties.

The friendly staff at the 24-hour front desk can provide tourist information. During the summer, guests enjoy 1 sun umbrella and 2 lounge chairs per room on the private beach.
