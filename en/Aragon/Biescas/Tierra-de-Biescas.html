Description

It offers an impressive mountain location, so the hotel is bright and sunny throughout the year. The elegant and light interiors feature light wood to create a version of the traditional mountain shelter of the 21st century.

The rooms at Tierra de Biescas have a flat-screen TV, heating, air conditioning and views. There is a bathroom with free toiletries, bath or shower and hairdryer.

Guests have access to the avant-garde restaurant, the traditional grill and the cider house.

Skiing and hiking are very popular in the surroundings. The Land of Biescas offers an ideal base to explore Candanchú, Astún and the impressive Ordesa National Park. It is ideal for skiers heading to the tourist centers of Panticosa and Formigal.
