Description

This design hotel has a 24-hour free gym, free Wi-Fi and a bar. The Tryp Zaragoza Hotel is located in front of the Delicias train station in Zaragoza.

The Tryp Zaragoza Hotel offers rooms with a modern design, air conditioning, satellite TV and a minibar.

Tryp Zaragoza serves a buffet breakfast and offers local specialties. Cocktails are prepared in the lobby bar.

Hotel Tryp Zaragoza is next to the Water Park and a 7-minute walk from the Aljafería Palace. Zaragoza Airport is a 20-minute drive away.
