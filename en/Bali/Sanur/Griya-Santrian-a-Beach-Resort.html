Description

Griya Santrian, a Beach Resort is a 15-minute drive from Sanur Town and a 20-minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport. Ubud is a 35-minute drive from the hotel.

The spacious rooms at the hotel let in plenty of natural light. A minibar and TV with cable channels are provided in each room. Select bathrooms feature a bathtub.

At the spa, guests can enjoy beauty and massage treatments with natural fruit and flower extracts. The resort also organizes yoga classes, cycling tours and beach barbecues.

Wantilan Restaurant serves Asian and Western dishes with live entertainment and views of the Beach. Italian and Japanese food are available at The Griya Beach Corner.
