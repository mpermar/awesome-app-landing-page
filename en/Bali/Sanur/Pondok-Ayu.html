Description

Guest rooms has air conditioning, a TV with satellite channels, a fridge, a electric tea pot, a bath and a desk. All rooms are equipped with a private bathroom, while some rooms also feature a balcony. At the hotel each room has a seating area.

Pondok Ayu offers a à la carte or American breakfast.

Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Sanur, like cycling.

The reception can provide advice on the area in order to help guests plan their day.

MakBeng's Warung is a 19-minute walk from Pondok Ayu. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 7 miles from the hotel.
