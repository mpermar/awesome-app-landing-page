Description

At the hotel, every room is equipped with a desk and a flat-screen TV. The private bathroom is equipped with free toiletries. At The Alantara Sanur every room comes with a seating area.

A continental or à la carte breakfast is served at the property.

The accommodation has a terrace.

Kopi Bali House is half a mile from The Alantara Sanur, while Batu Jimbar Café is 0.7 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 7 miles from the property.

Experiences

12/01/2017

Fantastic service and welcome to Sanur

"Beautiful hotel and villas, the pool area is stunning with plenty of space, really nice landscaping and it’s nice to see a hotel like this honor it’s Balinese heritage and take these aspects through the design and presentation of everything - room was spotless and cleaned daily, all the amenities that you could need, beds were super comfy. Fantastic breakfast selection in the morning and the food overall was really good. Delightful staff that would go out of their way to help in any way they can and are all very genuinely warm and welcoming. The fitness centre wasn’t opened when I visited as the grand opening is at the end of the month but it didn’t cause a problem as I was only there for a few days. The spa was temporarily set up in one of the ground floor rooms with a bath and private outdoor shower, which was beautiful the range of treatments on offer is fantastic and great value, I had the 3hour package with coffee scrub and mask and it was such a nice, relaxing experience. Ideal for couples and families that want to relax!"
