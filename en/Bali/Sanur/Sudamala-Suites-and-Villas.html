Description

Sudamala Suites and Villas is located a 30-minute drive from Kuta and Ngurah Rai Airport.

Each spacious suite features a private balcony or terrace. A flat-screen satellite TV, minibar and tea/coffee-making facilities are provided. Some suites feature an outdoor bathroom.

Guests can rent a bike for up to 2 hours to explore the surroundings. A library and business center are available. Laundry and currency exchange services are offered. The hotel can set up lounge chairs and umbrellas on the beach upon request. It also provides complimentary yoga class based on schedule.

A selection of traditional Balinese cuisine, as well as barbecued delights, can be enjoyed at Ares Resto and Lounge. Room service is also provided.

From the beginning to the end a wonderfully relaxing and...

"From the beginning to the end a wonderfully relaxing and comfortable stay. All staff members are unbelievably helpful, kind and go well beyond what would otherwise be expected of them.We had booked an airport pick-up and drop-off service from the hotel, which awaited us at the airport with a cool bottle of water and an iced towel for refreshment in the car. So lovely after our seven-hour flight!The hotel itself is located just at the end of Sanur's main street, on which all the restaurants and shops are located. This location makes for significantly lower traffic and street noise than anywhere along that street but still keeps are very good selection of restaurants and shops in easy walking distance.Different from all other upmarket Sanur hotels, the Sudamala's front terrace borders onto the footpath of the road, but at an elevated level. This gives the hotel a very open and inviting flair. Every time we returned to the hotel we could feel this feeling of being invited back into the embrace of the hotel. Once inside the hotel, though, there is a very distinct feeling of a secluded enclave where you can relax and enjoy the well-groomed gardens.The rooms are spacious, with beautifully carved doors and hand-made furniture from solid wood. Everything is well-maintained, immaculately clean with maid service twice daily. There is even a daily story, a cookie and mozzie coil as part of the evening make-up service.Relaxing at the pool the whole day, with occasional trips to the pool bar and grill, and the hotel's spa could hardly be more pleasant. The hotel is clearly aimed couples seeking some peace and quiet, and our five-year old son was the only child to be seen in the hotel.One of the highlights of our stay however occurred on our departure day. Booked on an evening flight, we had left our luggage with the hotel after checking out in the morning and spent the day out in the heat."
