Description

With shades of bright orange and green, the spacious air conditioned rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV and iPod dock. Each also comes with a private balcony and sitting area.

All Seasons Legian Bali is about one mile from Kuta and Legian Areas, famous for their nightspots and dining options. Guests who are driving can enjoy free on-site parking.

Guests may relax by the lounge chairs around the landscaped pool, or enjoy a massage at the spa. The resort also provides a tour desk and 24-hour room service.

Offering free Wi-Fi, the 24-hour Colors Café serves Asian and international dishes. Drinks can be enjoyed in the comfortable lobby lounge or at the poolside bar.
