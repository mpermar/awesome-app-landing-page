Description

At the hotel, all rooms include a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Each room includes air conditioning, and certain rooms include a balcony. All guest rooms are equipped with a seating area.

A buffet breakfast is available each morning at the property.

Fairfield by Marriott Bali Legian has an outdoor pool.

Round-the-clock assistance is available at the reception, where staff speak Indonesian and English.

Sky Garden is 1.2 miles from the accommodation, while Bounty Night Club is 1.2 miles away. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 3.1 miles from Fairfield by Marriott Bali Legian.
