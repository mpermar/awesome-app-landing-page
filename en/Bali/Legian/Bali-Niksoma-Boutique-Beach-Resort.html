Description

Air conditioned rooms at Bali Niksoma Boutique Beach Resort come with modern décor. Each elegant room has a DVD player and flat-screen TV with cable channels. Complete with a balcony, safety deposit box and a minibar. A bathtub and shower is in the private bathroom.

Guests of the resort can work out at the fitness centre or enjoy pampering treatments at Visala Spa. The resort also has a boutique gallery that showcases local artwork and handicraft.

The beachfront Hitana Restaurant and Bar serves a range of Asian & Indonesian specialties and international dishes.. Refreshing cocktails and tropical juices are available. Alternatively, guests can sample a fine selection of Asian and Italian cuisine at Mozzarella by the Sea located just right next to the resort.

Bali Niksoma Boutique Beach Resort is 0.9 miles from Kuta Beach and 1.2 miles from Seminyak Beach. Kuta Square and Kuta Art Market are 1.6 miles from the resort.
