Description

Legian Beach Hotel is a stroll from the famous entertainment and dining spots of Legian, as well as the Legian Art Market. Ku De Ta is about 0.7 mi away. Sahid Beach is a 7-minute walk away.

Rooms are fitted with an elegant décor and various facilities like a mini-bar, a TV and air conditioning. They all have private bathrooms equipped with a bathtub and necessary toiletries.

Legian Beach Hotels’s Indonesian restaurant provides a great setting for a relaxing meal, while delicious Mediterranean flavors are offered at the alfresco Gino's Pasta and Pizza Corner. Enjoy upbeat lounge music with light snacks at Ole Ocean View Patio located by the pool and overlooking Legian Beach.

Water activities like surfing are provided at the hotel. Alternatively, indulge in a relaxing Balinese traditional massage at Usada Spa.

Speaking German, English and Indonesian at the reception, staff are ready to help around the clock.

Popular points of interest near Legian Beach Hotel include Sunset Star and Double Six Beach. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 2.5 mi from the property.
