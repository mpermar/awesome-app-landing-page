Description

Steps from Seminyak’s famous “Eat Street”, the Amadea Resort & Villas Seminyak Bali is a 25 minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport. It offers airport shuttle service and free parking.

Rooms at Amadea Resort & Villas Seminyak feature modern décor and glass-walled bathrooms. They provide tea and coffee-making facilities and a safe.

Soothing massages can be enjoyed at Amadea’s rooftop spa, which features a pavilion in a tropical garden. For convenience, childcare and travel services are available.

Bistro Batu Kali offers casual dining for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Guests can enjoy the bustling atmosphere along the lively street. Room service is available 24 hours.
