Description

Seminyak’s lively restaurants, bars and boutiques surround the resort. Ku De Ta is 2,650 feet from the property, while Ngurah Rai Airport is a 15-minute drive away. Free parking is available.

Rooms have private balconies or ground-floor terraces which overlook the gardens or the pool. Each room is equipped with a mini-bar, cotton kimonos and a daily hospitality tray with tea and coffee. A 24-hour room service is provided.

Using traditional Balinese healing methods, the spa provides relaxing massage and therapies. Guests can also exercise at the gym, practice yoga in the gardens or join cooking classes.

The Breezes Bali Resort & Spa can conveniently provide information at the reception to help guests to get around the area.

The poolside restaurant serves local Indonesian specialties and Western food. Drinks and snacks can be enjoyed at the Lobby Lounge and the swim-up Pool Bar.
