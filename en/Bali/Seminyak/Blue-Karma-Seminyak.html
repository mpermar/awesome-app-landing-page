Description

Blue Karma Hotel is a 5 minute drive to Ku De Ta beach club and Seminyak Square Shopping Center. It is a 20 minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport.

Light wood furnishings and marble floors give each spacious villa an elegant yet tropical feel. All come furnished with a flat-screen TV, DVD player and sitting area. Private bathrooms have hot-water showers and toiletries.

Day trips and cooking classes can be arranged at the tour desk. Leisurely afternoons can be spent playing chess, enjoying a massage or visiting the library to borrow a DVD.

The World Around The Corner restaurant serves Indonesian and international cuisines. A daily breakfast is also available there.
