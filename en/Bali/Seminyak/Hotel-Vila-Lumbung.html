Description

Providing tranquility amid lush tropical garden, this resort is only 650 feet from Seminyak Square Shopping Mall and its lively neighborhood. Kuta and Legian areas are both 15 minutes' drive away. Ngurah Rai International Airport is 4.7 mi from the resort.

The well-designed rooms at Hotel Vila Lumbung are flooded with natural light. They all come with a flat-screen TV, a DVD player, and a personal safe. Mini-bar and tea/coffee making facilities are included. Some rooms offer a private balcony. Guests enjoy private bathrooms with a bath or a shower as well as free toiletries.

Guests can pamper themselves with a relaxing massage at the spa, or work out at the fitness center. Day tours and car or motorbike rentals can be arranged at the tour desk.

Featuring an adjacent pool bar and garden terraces, the open-air Lumbung Restaurant serves a variety of Asian and Western food, which also can be ordered through room service.
