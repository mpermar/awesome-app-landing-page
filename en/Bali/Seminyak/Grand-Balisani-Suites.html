Description

Grand Balisani Suites is about 8 miles from Ngurah Rai International Airport. Seminyak Beach and Canggu Beach are both within 1.5 miles from the hotel. Tanah Lot Temple is 16 miles away.

Featuring locally produced fabrics and classical decor, rooms come with a cable TV, DVD player and minibar. A personal safe and hot water showers are also included.

Overlooking Batu Belig Beach, the hotel offers traditional massage services and a playground. Arrangements for day trips can also be made at the tour desk.

Guests can enjoy the magnificent sunset view of the sunken pool bar while dining at Kamboja Restaurant. A daily buffet breakfast is also available.
