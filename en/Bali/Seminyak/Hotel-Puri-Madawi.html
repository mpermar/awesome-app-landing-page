Description

Rooms at Madawi Puri Hotel come with traditional Balinese décor with marble flooring. They are equipped with a safety deposit box and mini-bar. A hairdryer and bathrobes are also available.

Recreational activities include taking a dip in the swimming pool. An on-site tour desk can help with travel and sightseeing arrangements. Free parking is provided.

Guests staying at the Puri Madawi can enjoy a free 24-hour coffee and tea station and complimentary bottled water in the rooms. Meals can be enjoyed at the restaurant, which serves a variety of local and international dishes.

Front desk can give assistance to arrange airport transfer service, tour bookings, scooter rentals and massage service at additional charges.

Puri Madawi Hotel is a 25-minute drive Ngurah Rai International Airport.
