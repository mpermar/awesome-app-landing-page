Description

Luxurious suites feature traditional Balinese design, a marble terrace and unique outdoor shower. They are equipped with air conditioning, mini-bar, tea/coffee maker and a private bathroom with both bathtub and shower.

Matahari Beach Resort's award-winning spa offers a variety of traditional massages and treatments. Private yoga classes and cooking lessons can be arranged. Free sports facilities include badminton, tennis and table tennis courts.

Food served at Matahari Beach Resort & Spa are all grown on organic farms and plantations. Restaurant Dewi Ramona and Leon Beach Bistro offer local and international specialties. Vegetarian meals are available at Restaurant Dewi. The WAYANG Bar offers an impressive drink list with tantalizing coctails and mocktails.

Matahari Beach Resort & Spa is a 3-hour drive from Bali International Airport. Airport transfers are provided upon request.
