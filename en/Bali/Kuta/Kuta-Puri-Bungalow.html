Description

The hotel is a 20-minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport and 30 minutes from Nusa Dua. It takes 5 minutes on foot from the hotel to Kuta Beach and 15 minutes by car to Seminyak. An airport shuttle service is available on request.

All rooms at Kuta Puri Hotel are air conditioned and well equipped with a TV, a mini-bar and a private patio. Free toiletries and views of the garden are enjoyed from all rooms.

The hotel provides the services of a tour desk which helps guests arrange excursions to tourist attractions. There is also a 24-hour front desk.

Kuta Puri restaurant provides Indonesian and Western food. Room service is also available.
