Description

Bungalows at Uyah Amed & Spa Resort come with traditional Balinese décor and dark wood furnishings. Each well-furnished bungalow is fitted with a private balcony and private bathroom with a hot shower.

Guests can enjoy an Indonesian massage at the spa, or play a game of ping-pong. The hotel provides bike and car rental services. The staff at the tour desk can also assist with sightseeing activities.

Cafe Garam serves a variety of Western and Asian dishes. Traditional Balinese performances are available on Wednesdays and Saturdays.

Resort Uyah Amed is a 20-minute drive from the Water Palace and a 30-minute drive from Bali’s traditional market.
