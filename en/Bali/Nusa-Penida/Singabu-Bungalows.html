Description

At the property, each room is equipped with a wardrobe and a flat-screen TV. The rooms have a terrace with views of the pool or the garden. The private bathroom comes with a shower.

Daily breakfast can be enjoyed at the property. Coco Leaf serves a variety of international dishes. Room service is also available.

Singabu Bungalows can conveniently provide information at the reception to help guests to get around the area. Airport transfer can be arranged at a surcharge.

Sampalan Harbor is an 8-minute walk from the accommodations, while Goa Giri Putri Temple is 3.1 mi away. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 27 mi from Singabu Bungalows.
