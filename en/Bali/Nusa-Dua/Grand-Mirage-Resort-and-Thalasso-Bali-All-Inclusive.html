Description

It takes about a 6-minute drive from the resort to Bali Collection Shopping Center in Nusa Dua, and around a 10-minute drive to Bali National Golf Club. Benoa Harbour is reachable within a 16-minute drive and getting to Ngurah Rai Inernational Airport will take about 20 minutes, both via the toll road.

The beautifully furnished rooms at Grand Mirage Resort & Thalasso Bali are all air-conditioned and fitted with a flat-screen satellite TV, sitting area, a safe, a minibar, and a private bathroom featuring bathtub, shower, free toiletries, slippers, and bathrobes. There is also a balcony offering either a view of the grounds or an ocean view.

Facilities like the fitness center, kid's club, karaoke, shuttle service, tennis court, ping-pong, and billiards, are all provided for free at this property. 24-hour front desk, a concierge service, currency exchange service, and a tour desk are also available. Others like laundry and dry cleaning, babysitting service, meeting/banquet facilities, and BBQ facility are also available for a fee.

As for dining options, Grand Mirage has both sit-down and buffet restaurants, a bar, and snack bar. Guests can also explore the surrounding area for more dining options on Pratama Street where it is lined with restaurants and bars just about a 10-minute walk away.
