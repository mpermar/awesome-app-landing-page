Description

Rooms at Grand Mirage offer beautiful ocean views. Each room comes with an LCD TV, safe and tea/coffee maker. Bathrooms have a separate bathtub and shower, with shutters opening into the bedroom.

Grand Mirage Resort & Thalasso provides a large variety of on-site activities for guests, such as Balinese cooking lessons and guided bike tours. Guests can also make use of water sports equipment. Cultural evening programs feature bonfires, traditional dances, games and themed buffets.

The award-winning La Cascata Restaurant serves Italian and Mediterranean specialties. Open 24 hours, The Grand Café offers an Asian and international menu as well as dinner buffets. Fresh seafood and barbecue dishes can be sampled at Jukung Grill, on the beach.

Grand Mirage Resort & Thalasso Bali is a 20 minute drive from Benoa Harbour. It is a 15 minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport. Kuta Beach is a 15 minute drive away.
