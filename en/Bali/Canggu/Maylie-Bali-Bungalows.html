Description

A terrace with pool views is offered in all units.

The daily breakfast offers continental and à la carte options. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of international, European and French dishes.

If you would like to discover the area, cycling is possible nearby and a car rental service can be arranged.

Vue Beach Club is 2.7 miles from the resort village, while La Laguna Bali is 2.9 miles away. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 8 miles from the property.
