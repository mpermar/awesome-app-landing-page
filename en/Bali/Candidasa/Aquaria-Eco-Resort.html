Experiences

10/15/2017

Beautiful family beachfront villa, exceptional service, & delicious organic food in perfect location

"The food, the spectacular view, the massages at the onsite Spa, the caring staff, the beautiful pool, the location.... pretty much everything about this place was perfect!Glyn and his partner Jeni have created a truly beautiful place to relax and recharge your batteries right on the beachfront in Candidasa. We drifted off to sleep and awoke every day to the sound of waves breaking on the reef right in front of the property. We had the beachfront Family Villa and it was perfect for two adults and one child with huge comfy beds and lovely decor... but the best part of it all was the view! We spent hours relaxing on the daybed in the front of the villa and enjoying breakfast on the terrace looking out to sea and across to Nusa Penida, or watching the locals taking a swim late in the afternoon. The pool was a big hit with our 8 year old and it was beautifully maintained, no horrible chlorinated water here! The rooms were supplied with daily water direct from their own filtering system which was a welcome change to the bottled fare we usually have to drink in Indonesia. The location was perfect, a short walk to restaurants and some will even come and pick you up then drop you off after dinner. The reception staff were amazingly helpful and happy to call a local restaurant for us to arrange our free pick up and drop off on our behalf. We only ate offsite once, seriously the food they serve in the onsite restaurant is amazing! By far and away the best Indonesian Magibung we've ever eaten! Across the board, the food is fantastic! Fresh and beautifully prepared, we loved the daily breakfasts and we ate lunch and dinner there most days. Every meal was delicious and beautifully presented. The onsite Spa is definitely worth a visit and my husband and I enjoyed daily massages with Kadek who worked wonders on our stressed out, tired bodies. Our son loved his foot and leg massage too! This really was a little piece of Heaven and a wonderful end to our holiday!"
