Description

Spacious, air-conditioned bungalows at Candidasa Sea Breeze come with granite floors and natural stone walls. Each has a flat-screen TV and DVD player. Views of the Indian Ocean can be enjoyed.

Guests can take cooking and painting classes, or relax with a meditation session. A fitness center and spa services on-demand are offered. Watersports activities including diving and snorkeling can be enjoyed by the beach.
