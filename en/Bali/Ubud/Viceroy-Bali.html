Description

The spacious villas have a flat-screen TV, iPod dock and an Illy espresso machine. The private marble bathroom has a bathtub.

Viceroy Bali is a one hour drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport. Alternatively, guests can opt for a 12-minute helicopter ride from the property's private helipad.

Lembah Spa provides a variety body treatments. The gym provides private yoga classes and free energy drinks. The library is stocked with books and DVDs.

The award-winning CasCades Restaurant serves French specialties with Asian influences. CasCades also offers an extensive wine list, and cocktails are available at Viceroy Bar.
