Description

Guest rooms in the resort are equipped with a electric tea pot. Rooms contain a flat-screen TV, and selected rooms are equipped with a pool view. All guest rooms have a desk.

A continental breakfast is served at the property. There's an in-house restaurant, which serves international cuisine.

Ubud Valley Boutique Resort has a terrace. The area is popular for cycling, and car rental is available at the accommodation.

Ubud Valley Boutique Resort can conveniently provide information at the reception to help guests to get around the area.

Elephant Cave is 3 miles from the resort, while Ubud Monkey Forest is 3.7 miles away. Ngurah Rai International Airport is 21 miles from the property.
