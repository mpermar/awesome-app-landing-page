Description

The rooms comes with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a electric tea pot, a shower, free toiletries and a desk. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a hair dryer, while certain rooms will provide you with a balcony. At the resort each room is equipped with a seating area.

SereS Springs Resort offers a continental or buffet breakfast. The restaurant at the accommodation serves international cuisine.

SereS Springs Resort has an outdoor pool. You can play minigolf at the resort, and car rental is available.

Staff are available to help at the 24-hour front desk.

Arma Museum is 1.4 miles from SereS Springs Resort, while Rudana Museum is 1.4 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 16 miles from the property.
