Description

In the courtyard or within tropical gardens, each room comes with beautifully designed interiors. They are spacious and well-equipped with air conditioning, a luxurious bathroom and a minibar. All rooms feature fine artwork from local artists.

Guests at The Mansion Bali can enjoy fine dining French cuisine at Indochine or sample delicious dishes from an international menu at Caf La Terrace. It also houses the hotels private art collection. Other options include Jasmine Tea Lounge and Wantilan Bar for drinks.

The resort offers a variety of beauty treatments like rejuvenation and spa sessions. To compliment the experience, there are a series of yoga classes and therapeutic massages at the resort. Guests can also play a game of pool.

Just a few minutes' drive from the popular Kuta, Seminyak or Sanur area, Mansion Hotel & Spa is also a 40-minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport. Ubud Market is less than 2 miles away.
