Description

The suites at Warwick Ibah are decorated in a traditional Balinese style and feature fantastic views of the surrounding natural scenery. Each suite includes a separate living area and a marble bathroom. Room amenities include cable TV, a DVD player and a fridge.

Warwick Ibah’s outdoor saltwater pool is set in a stone garden, with relaxation corners and day beds built into a large stone wall. The spa provides soothing massages, while the reading lounge offers a quiet spot to enjoy a book or magazine.

Surrounded by gardens and fishponds, the restaurant serves Indonesian favorites and international specialties, as well as vegetarian choices and a wine selection. Guests can also have their meals by the pool or the waterfall, or on the grounds.

Warwick Ibah Luxury Villas & Spa is one mile from Ubud Royal Palace. Ngurah Rai International Airport is a 45-minute drive away.
