Description

At the hotel, rooms come with a desk and a flat-screen TV. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a bidet and free toiletries, while certain rooms will provide you with a seating area. Guest rooms at Korurua Villa include air conditioning and a closet.

A continental breakfast is served each morning at the property. At the accommodation you'll find a restaurant serving Asian cuisine. Vegetarian and halal options can also be requested.

Korurua Villa has an outdoor pool.

Staff at the hotel are available to give advice at the 24-hour front desk.

Elephant Cave is 2.9 miles from Korurua Villa, while Botanic Garden Ubud is a 6-minute walk from the property. Ngurah Rai International Airport is 19 miles away.
