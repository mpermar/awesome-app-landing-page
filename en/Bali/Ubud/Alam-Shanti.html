Description

Guest rooms at the resort come with a seating area. Rooms have a private bathroom with a bath, while some rooms are equipped with a terrace and others also offer pool views. All rooms at Alam Shanti include air conditioning and a desk.

An Asian breakfast is served at the property.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool. The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental and car rental are available at Alam Shanti.

When guests need guidance on where to visit, the reception will be happy to provide advice.

Elephant Cave is 1.9 miles from the resort. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 17 miles from the property.
