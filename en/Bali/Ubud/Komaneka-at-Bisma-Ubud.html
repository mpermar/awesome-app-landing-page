Description

Rooms at Komaneka come with private balconies, where guests can enjoy the natural scenery from the lounge chairs provided. Each room includes a large flat-screen TV and a DVD player. Spacious bathrooms have a bathtub and a separate glass-enclosed shower.

Guests can exercise at the fitness center or enjoy some quiet reading at the library area. Wi-Fi is free throughout the resort, which also provides free private parking.

Indonesian specialties are served at the hotel’s fine dining restaurant.

Komaneka at Bisma is around 19 miles from Ngurah Rai International Airport.
