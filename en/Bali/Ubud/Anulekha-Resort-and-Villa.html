Description

The accommodations includes a flat-screen satellite TV with DVD player, minibar and electric kettle. Featuring a shower, the private bathrooms also have a bathtub and free toiletries. You can enjoy a pool view and a view of the grounds from all the rooms.

The resort is a 10-minute drive from Ubud Monkey Forest as well as Batubulan, a 15-minute drive from Ubud and a 1.5-hour drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport.

At Anulekha Resort & Villa, there are facilities at the property including ticket services, a tour desk and luggage storage. The property provides free parking. Airport transportation is available for a fee.

The Silungan Restaurant offers Indonesian, Balinese and western dishes at the resort surrounded by palm trees.
