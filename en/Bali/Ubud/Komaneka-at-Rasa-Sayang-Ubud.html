Description

Free wood carving and Balinese dance lessons are held twice a week. Guests can enjoy some quiet reading at the library, or join a Neka Art Museum tour.

Each room at Komaneka Rasa Sayang features a private balcony with a day bed. A TV and a minibar are provided. Attached bathrooms provide shower facilities.

Komaneka’s restaurant serves Indonesian and international dishes. Free afternoon tea is offered daily, while room service is available 24 hours.

Komaneka at Rasa Sayang is a one-hour drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport.
