Description

Rooms at Uluwatu Breeze Village are fitted with air conditioning, mosquito nets and a personal safe. Every room has a terrace with views of the landscaped garden and a private bathroom with free toiletries.

A continental, à la carte or Full English/Irish breakfast can be enjoyed at the property. At the vacation park you will find a restaurant serving European cuisine. Gluten-free, Halal and Vegetarian options can also be requested.

A bicycle rental service is available at the accommodations. The staff can also arrange walking tours and cultural classes.

Dreamland Beach is 1.9 mi from Uluwatu Breeze Village. Ngurah Rai International Airport is 6.8 mi away.

"The breakfast was one of the best I have had in Indonesia. There were a decent number of options, all served with served with a selection of vibrant exotic fruits, crusty brown bread and freshly squeezed juice. Within a 5 - 10 minute walk, you can reach several really nice restaurants which are opposite the turning to thomas beach (another 5-10 minutes to climb down the steps to the shore). It's also walking distance to Single Fins beach bar, which is probably a 15 minute walk. If you get a moped or car you will unlimited access to even more hidden beaches that are nearby."
