Description

Fitted with air conditioning, all rooms come with a flat-screen TV and personal safe. Views of the garden can be enjoyed from a private terrace. Private bathrooms have a shower and free toiletries.

Komune Resort & Beach Club Bali is a 3-minute drive from Bali Safari Marine Park. It is a 25-minute drive from Ubud and the surf spots at Sanur. Ngurah Rai International Airport is a 1-hour, 30-minute drive away.

Day trips and car rentals can be arranged at the tour desk. The resort also provides laundry and babysitting services.
