Description

Monte Chalaça is a rural property in Alentejo, 5 km from Ferreira do Alentejo. It has an area of 3 hectares with farm animals, orchard and an outdoor swimming pool.

Free WiFi is available. The air-conditioned accommodation range from double rooms to 2-bedroom houses.

Double rooms have private and shared bathroom facilities, while the houses are self-catering and feature barbecue facilities.

Guests are welcomed with a seasonal fruit basket and water on arrival, and meals can be requested.

The outdoor swimming pool offers moments of relaxation after a long walk through the property.

For the colder days, Monte Chalaça provides a living room with a fireplace and TV.

Here, guests can also enjoy a game of snooker, chess, poker and karaoke. Monte da Chalaça can organise wine tasting sessions and guests can feed the farm animals, like the ducks, geese and the pony.

Roxo Dam and Odivelas Dam are 17 km and 25 km away, respectively. Monte da Chalaça is 150 km from Lisbon.
