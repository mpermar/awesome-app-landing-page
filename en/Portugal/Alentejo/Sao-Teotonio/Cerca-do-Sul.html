Description

Offering views of Monchique Mountains, Cerca do Sul is an 8-hectare property located 4 km from Carvalhal Beach, in São Teotónio.

This B&B offers a typical Alentejo architecture near Vicentine Coast. Featuring exposed wooden beams and sloped ceilings, each uniquely designed room has a bright décor.

They offer views of the garden, tea making facilities and a flat-screen cable TV.

The private bathroom comes with a shower and free toiletries. A daily breakfast buffet is served daily.

It includes fresh juices, yoghurts, homemade granola, local bread, eggs with bacon or tomato, local cheeses and ham, homemade cake and crepes or scones.

In the afternoon, staff can provide a light snack, a bottle of wine and a cold beer, and occasionally dinners are cooked and served under the porch or in the dining rooms.

Within 1 km there are typical restaurants where guests can taste fresh fish and meat dishes.

Cerca do Sul offers a common kitchenette where guests can prepare light meals. The poolside sun loungers and parasols offer a relaxing setting after a dip in the pool.

The more energetic guests can use the rental bicycles available to explore further this countryside location.

Cerca do Sul can organise hiking, horse riding and surfing activities, as well as a massage or a yoga class.

Zambujeira do Mar is 18 km, and Odeceixe Beach is 9 km away. Sagres with its fortress is located a 55-minute drive away.
