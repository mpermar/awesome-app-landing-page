Description

Featuring free WiFi, an outdoor pool and a barbecue, Quinta da Saudade offers accommodations in houses and studios, 6.8 mi from Ponte de Sor. The property is at a walking distance from a river beach. Free private parking is available on site.

Each room is air conditioned and comes with a flat-screen TV. Some units have a seating area where you can relax. Enjoy a cup of coffee while looking out at the lake or pool. All rooms have a private bathroom with hairdryer and free toiletries.

You can engage in various activities, such as fishing and hiking. Ponte de Sor Airfield is 2.5 mi away and Montargil is 9.3 mi from Quinta da Saudade. Golegã is 47 mi from the property, while Crato is at a 35 mi distance.
