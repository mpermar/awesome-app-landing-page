Description

Terra do Sempre offers accommodation in Grândola. The farm stay has an outdoor pool, year-round outdoor pool and barbecue, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the snack bar.

Some units include a seating area to relax in after a busy day. Some rooms have views of the pool or garden.

Every room comes with a private bathroom. Dinners are served upon previous request.

Bike hire and car hire are available at this farm stay and the area is popular for cycling.

Sines is 35 km from Terra do Sempre, while Porto Covo is 38 km away. The nearest airport is Portela Airport, 87 km from Terra do Sempre.
