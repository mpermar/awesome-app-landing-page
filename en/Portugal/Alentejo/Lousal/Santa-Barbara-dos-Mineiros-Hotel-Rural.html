Description

Located in Minas do Lousal and surrounded by Alentejo countryside, Santa Barbara Dos Mineiros offers an outdoor pool.

The historical building dates to the 1940s, and has preserved many of its original features.

Overlooking the Minas do Lousal village, all contemporary rooms are equipped with air conditioning and satellite TV.

Each has a minibar, work desk and a safety deposit box. Some rooms include large terraces.

The hotel includes a restaurant where lunch and dinner are served. The common areas of the hotel include a living room with a fireplace and a bar.

Guests can also enjoy drinks at the Garden Bar. Santa Barbara Dos Mineiros organises hiking tours.

The Lousal Mines, the Mine Museum and the dam are accessible by bike, while the beaches of Alentejo coast can be reached within an hour by car.
