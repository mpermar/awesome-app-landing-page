Description

Located in the Guadiana Valley Natural Park, 4.7 mi from the picturesque town of Mértola, Horta da Quinta offers an outdoor swimming pool, a gym and a hot tub. Free WiFi is available.

This eco-friendly unconventional country house features a unique tranquil setting with stunning countryside views and a decoration inspired in local traditions. Rooms are equipped with air conditioning and access to guests with reduced mobility.

Breakfast is included in the rate and original produce from property is available. A variety of restaurants serving local cuisine may be found in Mértola.

Horta da Quinta offers a variety of experience packages which include mountain biking, paintball, canoeing, 4x4 tours, as well as bird and star watching. In addition, painting, tile, ceramic, sculpture and bread workshops are available.

In Mértola guests can visit the Medieval Castle and a variety of museums about Roman and Moorish cultures. São Domingos Mine is 9.3 mi away and features a fluvial beach as well as a unique abandoned mine museum. Serpa is 34 mi away and the city of Beja is 50 mi away. Faro International Airport is 78 mi away.
