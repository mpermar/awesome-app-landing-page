Description

The rooms at the farm stay are equipped with a seating area. Each room includes a private bathroom and a shower, while selected rooms have a patio. Guest rooms at Herdade Vale de Cabras have air conditioning and a closet.

The accommodation offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

Herdade Vale de Cabras has a terrace. A selection of activities can be enjoyed on site or nearby, including diving, cycling and fishing.

Évora is 21 miles from the farm stay, while Beja is 23 miles from the property.
