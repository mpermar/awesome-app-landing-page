Description

Located in Peroguarda in the interior region of Alentejo, Sesmarias Turismo Rural & SPA offers an outdoor pool.

WiFi is available in common areas. The rooms are bright with simple decoration providing a feeling of peace and relaxation.

Each includes a private bathroom with direct view for the room. They are equipped with air conditioning and a flat-screen cable TV.

Guests have a restaurant at their disposal with a varied menu. After a dip in the pool, guest can relax in the sun loungers and enjoy a fresh drink from the pool bar.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling, fishing, hiking and bird watching.

The property offers free parking. Beja is 37 km away and Lisbon International Airport is 170 km away.
