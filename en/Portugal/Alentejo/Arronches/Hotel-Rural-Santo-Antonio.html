Description

Located in the town of Arronches, the Hotel Rural Santo António offers rooms with a flat-screen TV and a private balcony.

It features an outdoor pool and a Jacuzzi® with uninterrupted views of the Alentejo region.

The air-conditioned rooms are decorated with natural colours, tiled floors, and floor-to-ceiling windows.

All rooms have a work desk, and an en suite bathroom. The Santo António Restaurant is open daily and serves traditional dishes from the Alentejo.

It is possible to dine outside on the terrace. Free WiFi is available in all Hotel Rural Santo António.

The hotel also offers a 24-hour reception and bicycles to rent. Hotel Rural Santo Antonio is 23 km from Portalegre and Campo Maior and 33 km from Elvas, a World Heritage Site by UNESCO.
