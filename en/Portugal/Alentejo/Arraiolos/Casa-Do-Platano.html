Description

Casa Do Platano is situated in Arraiolos, in Portugal’s Alentejo region. It features modern, colourful rooms with a flat-screen TV.

Facilities include an outdoor swimming pool. Casa Do Platano embraces the principles of Eastern philosophy.

Each room has a different colour, and each colour represents a different energy centre (chakra).

All rooms feature air conditioning, wooden floors, and a private bathroom. Guests can relax and meditate in the garden, or practice yoga on the sun terrace.

Free Wi-Fi is offered in Casa Do Platano’s public areas. The hotel’s staff is available 24/7.

Every morning, breakfast is served in the dining room. It includes fresh orange juice, a variety of teas and coffee, and homemade sweets and cakes.

Arraiolos Castle is just 500 metres from Casa Do Platano. Free parking is possible on site.
