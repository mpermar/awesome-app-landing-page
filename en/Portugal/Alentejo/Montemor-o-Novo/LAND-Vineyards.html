Description

L'AND offers award-winning accommodations among vineyards in Alentejo’s scenic countryside. It is set in an architectural gem featuring suites with opening ceilings to sleep under the stars.

The 22 state-of-the-art suites at L'AND Vineyards are built in natural stone and local wood. They are all fitted with the help of interior designers and open out onto panoramic patios with outdoor fireplaces. Some have a plunge pool.

A culinary journey is possible at the on-site restaurant awarded with one Michelin Star, serving dishes prepared with locally-sourced ingredients. Food is matched by fully flavored wines, produced at the renowned in-house vinery.

Grape-infused treatments and massages are available at the Caudalie Spa. An outdoor swimming pool, an indoor heated pool, a sauna and tennis courts complement the offer. L'AND Vineyards also includes a bike rental to discover the nearby town of Montemor-o-Novo.
