Description

One of our top picks in Montemor-o-Novo – and a guest favorite.

Featuring an outdoor pool, Royal Cocoa Company Palace offers accommodations in Montemor-o-Novo. The unit is in a chocolate factory, integrated into a palatial estate. Guests can enjoy a massage chocotherapy, subject to hotel availability and at an extra fee.

Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and is equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Free WiFi is available. Some units include a seating area where guests can relax. Views of the pool, garden or city are featured in certain rooms. Rooms come with a private bathroom with a bath or shower, with bath robes and slippers provided. Extras include free toiletries and a hair dryer. A TV is featured.

There is a shared lounge at the Palacete da Real Companhia do Cacau - Royal Cocoa Company Palace. At the end of the day, guests are offered chocolates and can enjoy a cup of hot cocoa. Visits to the chocolate factory are offered, upon request.

Évora is 17 mi from Royal Cocoa Company Palace, while Montargil is 30 mi from the property. The nearest airport is Portela Airport, 50 mi from Palacete da Real Companhia do Cacau.
