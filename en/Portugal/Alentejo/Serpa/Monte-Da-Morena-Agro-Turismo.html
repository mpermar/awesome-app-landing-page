Description

Located in the Serpa district of Beja, Monte da Morena is a rural accommodation featuring a spacious terrace and an outdoor pool.

It offers free Wi-Fi and on-site parking. The guest rooms are air-conditioned and equipped with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

All the apartments have a fully equipped kitchen and a seating area. Guests can start their day with a breakfast, including fresh juices, coffee and fruits.

They can also enjoy a massage, relax in the garden or visit the games room. Hotel Monte da Morena also organizes several activities such as boat trips, wine tasting, and visits to nearby towns.

Monte Da Morena Agro-Turismo is situated less than 1 km away from the town of Serpa, where guests can visit the church of Santa Maria.
