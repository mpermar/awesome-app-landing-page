Description

Nestled high in the mountains of Serra da Estrela Natural Park, this guest accommodation features panoramic views of valleys and lakes.

It offers an on-site restaurant. Rooms at Albergaria Senhora do Espinheiro have simple wooden furnishings and private balconies.

They come equipped with satellite TV and a minibar. The private bathrooms have a hairdryer.

In the morning the Espinheiro serves a buffet breakfast with fresh fruit. The hotel’s restaurant overlooks the countryside and serves regional cuisine.

Albergaria guests can enjoy a game of billiards, or take a walk through the park which is filled with black oak trees.

Nearby leisure activities include skiing and hiking. The Albergaria Senhora do Espinheiro is a 20-minute drive from the Vodafone Ski Resort.

The guest accommodation offers 24-hour front desk service and free public parking is possible on site.
