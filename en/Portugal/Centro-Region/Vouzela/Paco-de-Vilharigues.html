Description

Set in a renovated 17th century manor house combined with modern architectural details, Paço de Vilharigues is located in a rural setting, 4 km from Vouzela.

The property’s building is attached to the Medieval Tower of Vilharigues. Each room at Paço de Vilharigues has air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom.

Free WiFi access is offered throughout the property. Guests can enjoy the on-site bar or relax in the shared lounge.

Billiards, football table and other games are available for guests’ convenience. Free use of bicycles is also available at the property and the area is popular for cycling.

Guests benefit from free private parking on site. The A25 motorway is 3 km from the Paço de Vilharigues.

Termas de Sao Pedro do Sul is 10 km away while Viseu is 19 km from the property. Porto Airport is 74 km away.
