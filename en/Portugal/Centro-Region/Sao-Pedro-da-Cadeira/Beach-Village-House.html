Description

The air-conditioned vacation home consists of 4 separate bedrooms, 3 bathrooms with a hair dryer and a living room. There's a seating area, a dining area and a kitchen equipped with a dishwasher.

The vacation home offers a hot tub. A terrace is available on site and skiing can be enjoyed within close proximity of Ericeira Beach Village House.

Lisbon is 28 miles from the accommodation, while Sintra is 18 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Humberto Delgado Airport, 25 miles from the property.
