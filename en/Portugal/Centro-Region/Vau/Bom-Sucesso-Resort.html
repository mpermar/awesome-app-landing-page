Description

Located on the banks of the bidos Lagoon, this 5-star resort offers uniquely designed villas, just a 15-minute drive from the medieval town of bidos.

It features an outdoor pool, and an 18-hole golf course. Designed by famous architects, all villas and houses of Bom Sucesso have a private furnished patio that leads to the garden.

All elegant and sophisticated accommodation features a kitchen and common areas decorated in a contemporary style.

Daily cleaning is included. The Guardian Bom Sucesso Golf, a golf course with a PGA rating, occupies an area exceeding 60 hectares and has 18 holes, a driving range and a golf academy.

Bom Sucesso Holiday Design Villas offers 4 tennis courts, 2 paddle tennis courts, football pitch and a kids club.

Bom Sucessos restaurant and bars offer international cuisine and Mediterranean dishes with a Portuguese touch.

Breakfast can be served at the golf club house. During the summer, a shuttle to transport guests to the beach and bidos is available.
