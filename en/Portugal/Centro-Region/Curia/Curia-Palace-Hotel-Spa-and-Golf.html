Description

Located halfway between Lisbon and Oporto, this beautifully restored palace is set in the scenery of Bairrada vineyards and private gardens.

It features elegant interiors with numerous antiques and high ceilings. Curia Palace, Hotel Spa & Golf offers spacious Art Deco rooms with marble bathrooms.

Special attention is given to the use of warm colours and natural fabrics. Free WiFi is available in the public areas of the hotel.

The wellness area includes a large outdoor pool and complete spa facilities. Guests can enjoy typical Portuguese cuisine at the restaurant with a terrace facing the landscaped gardens.

A glass of renowned Buçaco wine, produced on palace grounds, will complement the meal.

The hotel is located 15 km from the Bussaco National Forest and 37 km from Conimbriga Roman ruins.
