Description

Set in an 11-hectare estate, Quinta Raposeiros offers an outdoor pool and self-catering apartments with Atlantic Ocean views.

São Lourenço Beach is a 20-minute walk past orchards, palms and pine trees. Featuring a balcony, each 1-bedroom apartment has central heating and a living room with 2 sofa beds.

All have tiled floors and a private bathroom (hair dryers are available, upon request), and some have wooden ceilings.

The Raposeiros also features Premium Double Rooms, each with a double bed, a single bed, bathroom with bath, a living area, LCD TV, central heating and a private balcony with views of São Lourenço Beach.

All Premium Double Rooms have free WiFi. Breakfast can be served in the apartments upon request and there is a barbecue in the gardens.

The apartment kitchenettes include a fridge, stove and microwave, and a supermarket and a few restaurants are within 10 minutes’ drive.

The fishing town of Ericeira is 7 km away and its coastline has some of Europe’s best surfing beaches.

Surf boards and bicycles can be rented from the hotel. Quinta Raposeiros also has a skate park.

Sintra is 40 minutes by car and Lisbon is 5 minutes further. Óbidos is 60 km away and Alcobaça is 98 km away.
