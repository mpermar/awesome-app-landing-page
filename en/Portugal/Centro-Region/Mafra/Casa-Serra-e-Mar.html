Description

The vacation home has 3 separate bedrooms, 4 bathrooms, a fully equipped kitchen with a dining area and dishwasher, and a living room. A flat-screen TV with cable channels is available.

Languages spoken at the reception include Spanish and French.

The property has a hot tub. The vacation home features a grill, garden and sun terrace, which can be enjoyed by guests if the weather permits.

Lisbon is 17 miles from Casa Serra e Mar, while Sintra is 8 miles away. The nearest airport is Humberto Delgado Airport, 15 miles from the property.
