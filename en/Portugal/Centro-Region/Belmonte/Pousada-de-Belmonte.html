Description

The elegant restaurant offers local dishes with a contemporary touch, and a seasonal mushroom menu. The chapel has been converted into a lounge and bar, yet keeps many original features.

All the rooms of Pousada de Belmonte have a balcony overlooking the Estrela Mountains Nature Reserve. They feature antique furniture, wrought-iron bed frames, and are decorated with stonework and relaxing sand colors.

Pousada de Belmonte has a large terrace with a swimming pool and lounge chairs. Guests can enjoy horseback riding, fishing and hiking in the local area.

The town of Belmonte is half a mile from the Pousada de Belmonte.
