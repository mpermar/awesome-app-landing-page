Description

Offering an outdoor pool and children's playground, Colmeal Countryside Hotel is located in Figueira de Castelo Rodrigo in the Centro Region, 50 km from Ciudad-Rodrigo.

The hotel has a sun terrace and sauna. Certain rooms have a seating area for your convenience.

Some units feature views of the mountains or pool. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hair dryer.

A flat-screen TV is available. Free WiFi is provided and free private parking is available on site.

You will find room service at the property. There is a restaurant onsite, which is only available upon advance booking.

The area is popular for cycling and hiking. The hotel also offers bike hire. Comfortable pet shelters are available, upon an extra charge and upon availability.
