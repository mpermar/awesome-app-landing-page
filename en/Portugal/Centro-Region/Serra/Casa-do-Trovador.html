Description

Only 1 km from Serra Village, the Casa do Trovador is set in Aldeia da Serra on a hill overlooking the lake Castelo de Bode Dam, Ilha do Lombo and surrounding mountains.

It has a terraced garden with a hydro massage pool and a sheltered relaxation corner.

All rooms at the Trovador feature wooden floors and local art on the walls. They have a private bathroom with a hairdryer and bathrobe.

In addition, each room has free Wi-Fi. A breakfast buffet is served each morning.

The bar features a varied menu of drinks, and full meals can be served upon request.

There is also a restaurant in Serra Village, just 1 km away. The Casa Trovador features a mini soccer field and a sheltered garden area with hammocks.

Guests can prepare their own meals in the shed facilities for grilling at the pool.

The city of Tomar, with its UNESCO-listed Convent of Christ and castle of the Knights Templar, is 13 km away.

Boat trips and water sports are available at the lakeside, 1 km from the Trovador.
