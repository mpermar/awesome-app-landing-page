Description

The 3-star Hotel Louro is a 5-minute walk from the medieval village of Óbidos. The traditionally-styled hotel has an outdoor swimming pool and is a 10-minute drive from Praia d'el Rey Country Club.

Hotel Louro features rooms with cable TV and a work desk. Some rooms provide a seating area and a balcony with a scenic view of the gardens and the historic village.

Massage services are available and need to be booked 72 hours in advance. Breakfast is included in the room rate and served each morning at the hotel's meal area.

The nearby Óbidos centre has a wide range of traditional Portuguese restaurants and eateries for guests to discover.

Complimentary internet access and free private parking is available. Hotel Louro offers picturesque landscaped gardens where guests can enjoy a relaxing stroll.

Historical Óbidos is renowned for its fortified medieval walls, traditional cobbled streets and variety of tourist-friendly shops.

The centre of Leiria is a 38-minute drive away. Lisbon International Airport is a 49-minute drive from the Hotel Louro.
