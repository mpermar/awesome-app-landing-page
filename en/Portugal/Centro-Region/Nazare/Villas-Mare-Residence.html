Description

Located in the heart of Nazaré, Villas Mare Residence offers modern apartments with a complete kitchen and a furnished patio or balcony overlooking the seasonal pool.

The beach is a 10-minute walk away. The brightly decorated apartments feature a fully equipped kitchen with an oven, dishwasher and a fridge.

There is a separate dining and seating area with a sofa and a flat-screen cable TV.

Each apartment has a stylish private bathroom with white décor and a bath or shower.

Several cafés, bars and restaurants serving regional and national Portuguese cuisine are within 5 minutes’ walking distance.

There are on site barbecue facilities, and local shops and grocery stores are located in the town centre, a 10 minute walk away.

Guests can sunbathe poolside on the sun terrace or enjoy local activities such as sailing, surf lessons and horseback riding.

Bicycle rental is available. Peniche and São Pedro de Moel are within a 1-hour drive away.
