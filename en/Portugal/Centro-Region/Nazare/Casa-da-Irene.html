Description

One of our best sellers in Nazaré!

This property is a 3-minute walk from the beach. Located in the heart of Nazaré, Casa da Irene features two large 2-bedroom apartments and two studio apartments, with free WiFi in all areas.The apartments are at a 2-minute walk from the beach.

The apartments can accommodate up to 6 people and include 2 bedrooms and a living room, while the studios accommodate 2 adults and 2 children. Each unit is equipped with a flat-screen cable TV and a DVD player, as well as a private bathroom with a bath, a shower, a bidet and a hairdryer.

Guests staying at Casa da Irene will be able to prepare their own meals since each apartment features a fully equipped kitchen that open to the living room and dining area. In addition guests can taste the fish and seafood specialties served by the local restaurants within a few minutes’ walk.

Most services like supermarkets, banks and grocery shops are within a 2-minute walk since the property is next to Nazaré's main commercial street. Guests can enjoy the panoramic views of the Atlantic Ocean and the town at the Sítio using the Nazaré Funicular 1,300 feet away. Lisbon International Airport is 73 mi away.
