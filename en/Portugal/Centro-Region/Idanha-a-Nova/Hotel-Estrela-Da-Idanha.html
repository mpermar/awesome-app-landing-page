Description

Located in Idanha-a-Nova, a historic town over 800 years old set near the Spanish border, this hotel offers modern rooms. In the center of Beira Baixa region, it features 2 swimming pools and spa facilities.

Estrela Da Idanha Hotel’s rooms are decorated in neutral colors and have wooden furniture. Each of the air-conditioned rooms has a private bathroom with a hairdryer. Some rooms have views of the historic village of Monsanto.

A breakfast is served at the property, including seasonal fruits, eggs, a variety of breads, cereals and sweets, natural juices, coffee, and different types of tea. Guests can relax on the sun loungers by the outdoor pool, or use the sauna and hammam. Massage treatments are also available.

Featuring a skating track and a minigolf course, Hotel Estrela Da Idanha provides many sports opportunities.

The Tagus International Nature Park is 5 minutes by car and the hotel is a 25-minute drive away from Penha Garcia, a rustic village featuring cultural fairs and markets. Castelo Branco is 35 minutes’ drive away.
