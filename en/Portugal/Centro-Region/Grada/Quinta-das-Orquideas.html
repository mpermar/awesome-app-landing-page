Description

At Quinta das Orquídeas, the rooms are equipped with a terrace. Every room comes with a coffee machine, while some have a balcony. At Quinta das Orquídeas, every room includes a seating area.

A continental breakfast is served each morning at the property. At Quinta das Orquídeas you'll find a restaurant serving seafood and sushi cuisine. A Gluten-free option can also be requested.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental is available at Quinta das Orquídeas.

Speaking English, French and Italian, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical advice on the area at the 24-hour front desk.

Aveiro is 21 miles from Quinta das Orquídeas, while Coimbra is 13 miles from the property.
