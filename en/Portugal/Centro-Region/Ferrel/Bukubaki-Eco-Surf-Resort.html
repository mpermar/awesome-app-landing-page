Description

At the resort, the rooms have a closet. Certain rooms come with a kitchenette with a microwave. The rooms at Bukubaki Eco Surf Resort are equipped with a seating area.

A continental breakfast is served at the property.

Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.

Nazaré is 21 miles from Bukubaki Eco Surf Resort, while Óbidos is 7 miles from the property. Humberto Delgado Airport is 41 miles away.
