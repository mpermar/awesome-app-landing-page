Description

One of our top picks in Ferreira do Zêzere.

These spacious apartments feature a big balcony with views of the Blue Lake. Apartamentos do Lago has an outdoor swimming pool, and free Wi-Fi access is available in all public areas.

All apartments at the Aparthotel Varandas do Lago include a kitchen that comes with a refrigerator and a stove. All have an open plan design with a dining area and a seating area with a TV. Each has a private bathroom with a bath and a shower.

The accommodations is all self-catering, but there are also a number of restaurants just a 5-minute drive away.

There are a range of services offered by the Varandas, including bike rental.
