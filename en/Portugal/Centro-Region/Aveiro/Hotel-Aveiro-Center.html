Description

Each room includes a flat-screen TV, cable TV, free coffee and tea-making facilities, a desk, hairdryer and amenities.

Experience the quietness of the countryside in the heart of the city, known as the Portuguese Venice. Guests can explore the area after enjoying a buffet breakfast in the breakfast room or in the comfort of their own room.

Guests can sample the local nightlife and shopping that are within a 5-minute walk of the Hotel Aveiro Center.
