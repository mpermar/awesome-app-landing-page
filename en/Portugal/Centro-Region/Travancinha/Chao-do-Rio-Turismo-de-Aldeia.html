Description

Located in Seia, Chao do Rio - Turismo de Aldeia features rustic homes, as well as an outdoor biologic swimming pool and free bicycles.

The traditional stone-built units are inserted in natural surroundings which include a garden and a flock of sheep.

The pet-friendly units all have a living area with a sofa, flat-screen TV and a CD player.

All come with an equipped kitchenette, dining area and a private bathroom. The units feature a balcony and a terrace.

When guests arrive at the property, their kitchenette is already filled with breakfast items for their stay.

Fresh bread is delivered each day at 17:00 and guests may cook their own meals in the comfort of their own accommodation.

In addition, meals can be served upon previous request. These meals are cooked at a restaurant in the vicinity.

Other facilities are provided, like a children's playground, and laundry facilities.

Communal barbecue facilities are also available in this 19-acre property, as well as an outdoor wood burning oven, an outdoor fireplace, a vegetable garden and a picnic area and farm animals.

The Seia centre is 12 km from the unit. The green areas of the Serra da Estrela Natural Park are 15 km away.

The Nelas Train Station is 20 km away while Porto International Airport is at a 173 km distance from the Chao do Rio.
