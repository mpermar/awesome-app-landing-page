Description

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool and a tennis court, Villa de Paco is located in Sever do Vouga.

The unit features rustic holiday homes, with exposed stone walls and modern furnishings.

The accommodation’s homes will provide guests with air conditioning and a balcony.

Each has a full kitchenette with a microwave and a refrigerator. The bathroom also comes with free toiletries and the houses enjoy mountain views.

Breakfast is available and served each morning at the property. It can be taken in the terrace, overlooking the tranquil surroundings.

The Sever do Vouga centre is 8. 7 km away and features various Portuguese restaurants for guests to enjoy.

At Villa de Paco guests will find a hot tub, a garden and barbecue facilities. Other facilities offered at the property include grocery deliveries, meeting facilities and a shared lounge.

An array of activities can be enjoyed in the surroundings, including cycling and hiking.

The green areas of Cabreia Park are 14 km away from the unit. São João da Madeira is 40 km away.
