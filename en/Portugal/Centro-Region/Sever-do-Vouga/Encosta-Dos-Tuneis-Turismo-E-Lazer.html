Description

One of our top picks in Sever do Vouga – and a guest favorite.

This property is a 6-minute walk from the beach. Encosta Dos Túneis - Turismo E Lazer is located in Sever do Vouga, 16 mi from Aveiro and 24 mi from Viseu. Guests can enjoy the on-site bar.

Each room at this guest house is air conditioned and is equipped with a flat-screen TV. Some rooms have a seating area to relax in after a busy day. Certain units feature views of the mountain or pool. All rooms come with a private bathroom. Extras include free toiletries and a hairdryer.

There is a shared lounge at the property.

This guest house has water sports facilities. The Vale do Vouga bicycle trail passes through the property and bike hire is available. Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including horse riding, cycling and fishing. Termas de Sao Pedro do Sul is 14 mi from Encosta Dos Túneis - Turismo E Lazer, while Lagoa is 27 mi away. The nearest airport is Porto Airport, 40 mi from the property.
