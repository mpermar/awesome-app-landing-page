Description

Set in a carefully restored manor, this guest house offers luxurious accommodations with views of Viseu’s Cathedral and the historic center. It is fitted with elegant antique furnishings which can be purchased upon request.

Named after famous personalities of the city, rooms at Casa da Sé are all air conditioned and include free Wi-Fi. Some of the deluxe private bathrooms feature 17th-century azulejos tiles.

Guests can start the day with a buffet breakfast. This is served in the lower part of the building, where the original walls include hand-paintings.

Located along the cobbled streets of Viseu's town center, Casa da Sé is a 5-minute walk away from vintage shops and restaurants. The Grão Vasco Museum and Viseu Cathedral are just a 2-minute walk away. Free public parking is available at a nearby location.
