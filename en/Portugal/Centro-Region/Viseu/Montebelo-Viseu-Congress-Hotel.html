Description

Just a 20-minute walk from Viseu's centre, this 5-star hotel features an extensive spa, indoor and outdoor pools and spacious rooms decorated with warm, earthy colours.

Free parking is available. Each room of Montebelo Viseu Hotel & Spa features modern furnishings, air conditioning and a minibar.

Some also have a seating area and a dining area. Fine dining can be enjoyed at Montebelo Viseu Hotel & Spa’s restaurant where national and international cuisine is served.

Breakfast can be sampled in the comfort of the guest room. At the spa and wellness centre, guests can unwind with a professional massage or in the hot tub.

Montebelo Viseu Hotel also offers a state-of-the-art fitness centre and a Turkish bath.
