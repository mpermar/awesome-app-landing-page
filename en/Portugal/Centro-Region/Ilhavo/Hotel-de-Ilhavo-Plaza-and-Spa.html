Description

Situated in Ílhavo, Hotel De Ílhavo is a 4-minute drive from the center of Aveiro.

It has an outdoor pool with sun-lounger terrace and offers air-conditioned guestrooms with furnished balconies.

Hotel De Ílhavo rooms have parquet floors and modern furnishings. They include a work desk and a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

The private bathrooms include marble counter tops and a bath. In the morning the hotel serves a buffet breakfast with fresh fruit.

Restaurant Casa Velha overlooks the pool and serves international cuisine and traditional Portuguese dishes.

Ílhavo's guests can relax in the sun and enjoy a cocktail, or visit the hotel’s spa which includes a heated indoor pool, a hot tub and a Turkish bath.

There is also a state-of-art gym. Hotel De Ílhavo is located 10 km from the beach and is a 7-minute drive from Aveiro River.

It offers 24-hour front desk service and free private on-site parking upon availability.
