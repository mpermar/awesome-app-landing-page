Description

Pousada da Ria is located close to the resort town of Torreira, overlooking the River Aveiro.

At 42 km's driving distance from the centre of Aveiro, this property has a restaurant and bar, and a swimming pool.

Rooms are decorated with bright, warm colours, and all include a balcony. They are also equipped with TVs, a bath and shower and air conditioning.

Traditional Portuguese cuisine is served in the restaurant. The bar offers floor-to-ceiling windows and refreshing drinks.

The Pousada da Ria is ideally located for enjoying the region’s outdoor activities such as canoeing, fishing, horse riding and hiking.

The property is 8 km from the harbour with ferry connections with Aveiro. Sao Jacinto is a 15-minute drive away.
