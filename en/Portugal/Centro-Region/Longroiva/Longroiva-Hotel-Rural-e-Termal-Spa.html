Description

Longroiva Hotel Rural e Termal Spa offers free WiFi throughout the property and rooms with air conditioning in Longroiva.

Featuring a 24-hour front desk, this property also provides guests with a year-round outdoor pool.

Guests can enjoy a selection of Portuguese dishes in the on-site restaurant. At the hotel the rooms are fitted with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

All units at Longroiva Hotel Rural e Termal Spa are equipped with a seating area.

A continental breakfast is served daily at the property. The accommodation offers a terrace.

Hiking is among the activities that guests can enjoy near Longroiva Hotel Rural e Termal Spa.
