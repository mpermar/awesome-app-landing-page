Description

Situated on the shores of Pateira of Fermentelos, this guesthouse has a welcoming atmosphere and a unique panoramic sight.

The natural surroundings of Estalagem da Pateira are coupled with the property’s large garden and indoor and outdoor pools.

The Estalagem da Pateira offers bright and spacious rooms with balconies facing the lake, air conditioning, private bathroom with hairdryer, TV and free Internet access.

Guests can access the restaurant, bar and snack-bar for light meals. The property also includes a living-room, conference room and private parking.

Besides the pool areas, the guesthouse provides guests with a number of activity options, such as sauna, cycling, pedal boats, table tennis, table football, billiards and walking circuits.

The Porto International Airport is 76 km away. Located near national highways, the guesthouse is a 17-minute drive to Águeda, a 22-minute drive to Aveiro and a 40-minute drive to Coimbra.
