Description

At the guest house, every room is equipped with a wardrobe. With a private bathroom, rooms at Vila Louro also boast a garden view. The units at the accommodations are fitted with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Continental and buffet breakfast options are available every morning at Vila Louro.

You can play billiards at the guest house, and the area is popular for cycling.

Lisbon is 30 mi from Vila Louro, while Sintra is 22 mi from the property. The nearest airport is Humberto Delgado Airport, 27 mi from the property.
