Description

Located within Geopark Naturtejo, Quinta dos Trevos offers a natural setting a 2-minute drive from Ladoeiro village, 25 km from Castelo Branco city.

It offers a 24-hour reception. All units at Trevos include unique pieces made of wrought iron, wood and weaving, made on site.

Each unit comes with a TV and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

A breakfast buffet is served daily. Guests can participate in handicraft workshops held at the property.

Activities available in Geopark include bird watching, boat tours, BTT, and horse rides amongst others.

Quinta dos Trevos offers bicycles which guests can use for free to explore the natural landscape.

Massages can be arranged on request, as well as laundry services. Monsanto, known as the most Portuguese village of Portugal, is 30 km away, and the historical Idanha-a-Velha village is 17.
