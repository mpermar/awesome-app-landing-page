Description

Situated in the countryside of the Centro Region, this boutique hotel features a vineyard and a farm.

It has a swimming pool, and offers guestrooms and apartments with free Wi-Fi. The air-conditioned rooms at Quinta De Santa Maria D' Arruda have hardwood floors and elegant furnishings.

They include a private bathroom with luxury amenities. The apartments have fully-equipped kitchenettes.

Quinta De Santa Maria serves a buffet breakfast in the restaurant. The garden area is ideal for guests to relax and enjoy nature and the farm animals.

The Quinta De Santa Maria is located 25 minutes’ driving from Lisbon and 15 minutes from Portela International Airport.
