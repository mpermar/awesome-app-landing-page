Description

Located near Serra da Estrela, this 250-year old manor house has historic furnishings and artistic accessories. Each themed room is a reminder of older way of life.

The sunny rooms of Casa Das Obras also have modern comforts such as a TV and central heating.

Guests can relax and unwind at the Casa Das Obras’s outdoor swimming pool. A wholesome breakfast consisting of local cuisine is also offered here.

Casa Das Obras is within driving distance of the natural springs of Caldas de Manteigas. The beautiful Poço do Inferno Waterfall is also a short drive away.
