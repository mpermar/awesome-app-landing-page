Description

Located in the historical town of Sertã, Hotel Larverde offers air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi, a balcony and private bathroom.

The hotel has an outdoor pool and a sun terrace. Each room features a seating area with a flat-screen TV and a sofa.

The private bathroom comes with a bath and hairdryer. A buffet breakfast is served daily in the dining room.

Guests can relax on the sun terrace overlooking the town and enjoy hot and cold drinks from the bar.

There are a restaurants within a 2-minute walk. The outdoor pool area at Hotel Larverde has sun loungers and a sun-bathing area.

Indoors, guests can play snooker or relax in the lounge with its flat-screen TV and magazines.

The Roman Bridge is a 5-minute walk away. Lisbon International Airport is 185 km away and free private parking is available on site.
