Description

Inserted in natural and tranquil surroundings, this property features modern amenities such as an outdoor swimming pool, sauna and hot tub.

Vale d'Azenha Hotel Rural & Residences is located at 7 km from the famed Alcobaça Monastery and 10 km from Nazaré Beach.

The property features air-conditioned rooms, with free WiFi and a private bathroom.

All rooms come with a balcony. There is also the option of an ample and pet-friendly villa, with a full kitchen.

Guests can enjoy a daily buffet breakfast at the Vale d'Azenha Hotel Rural & Residences.

Traditional Portuguese meals are available for savouring at the property’s à la carte restaurant.

There is an on site bar, serving refreshing drinks and cocktails. The property is 15 km from the green areas of Serras de Aire and Candeeiros Natural Park.

Several activities such as hiking and cycling are possible in the area. São Martinho do Porto is 12.
