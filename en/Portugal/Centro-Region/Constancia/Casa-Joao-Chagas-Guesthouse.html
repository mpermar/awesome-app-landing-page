Description

Casa Joao Chagas Guesthouse is set in 2 historic buildings of the 19th century and is situated in Constancia Main Square.

The hotel is uphill from a beach situated where the Zêzere and Tagus rivers. Parking is free.

The air-conditioned guestrooms at Casa Joao Chagas Guesthouse are fitted with extra-long beds and a desk, a wardrobe and PVC windows.

There are a number of radio and cable TV channels and private bathrooms with selected toiletries.

Free WiFi is available throughout the hotel. Guests can start the day with a breakfast buffet prepared with local specialities at the on-site bar.

Staff can also advise typical restaurants, serving authentic food of the Central Region of Portugal.

Activities like hiking, horseback riding and canoeing are available nearby, as well as some river beaches.

The Live Science Centre of Constancia is just a 20-minute walk away. The Almourol Castle is 6 km from Casa Joao Chagas Guesthouse and the Torres Novas Castle is 20 km away.

The Environmental Park of Santa Margarida is about 7 km from the property, while the Convent of Christ in Tomar is 20 km away.
