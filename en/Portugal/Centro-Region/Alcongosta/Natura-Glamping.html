Description

Offering a restaurant, Natura Glamping is located in Alcongosta, 7. 5 km from Fundão.

Free WiFi access is available in this tented camp. The accommodation will provide you with a balcony and a terrace.

Each luxurious tent offers mountain views and a private bathroom with hairdryer and free toiletries.

You can enjoy mountain view from the room. Extras include a seating area. At Natura Glamping you will find a 24-hour front desk, a garden and a terrace.

Other facilities offered at the property include a shared lounge, a games room and a tour desk.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling and hiking.
