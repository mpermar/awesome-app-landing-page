Description

Just 2 km from Serra da Boa Viagem Mountain, Malibu Foz Hotel offers large pool in a residential area of Figueira Da Foz.

The city centre is a 15-minute walk away. The rooms and suites of Malibu Foz Hotel have a modern décor and include views of the surrounding area.

Each comes with air conditioning and a balcony. Guests can enjoy a variety of facilities that include a cinema room, a kids' club and a fitness centre.

The surrounding area is ideal for hiking, cycling, diving, or fishing activities.

The Mensa Restaurant features panoramic views of the swimming pool and serves Italian cuisine.

There is a pool bar serving lunch and drinks. The nearest beach is 300 metres away.
