Description

Atlântida Sol features beachfront accommodation with views over the Atlantic. The hotel features 2 hot tubs and massage facilities together with an indoor and an outdoor pool.

The spacious guestrooms include private bathrooms, air conditioning, satellite TV and a radio.

Some have a bathtub and balcony. Guests can enjoy a menu of fresh, local specialities at the restaurant.

Drinks and cocktails are provided at the roof-top bar of the Hotel Atlântida Sol.

The hotel has an on-site tennis court and hiking trails around the property. Wi-Fi internet connection is free in the hotel’s public areas.

Atlântida Sol is 30 km from Coimbra and 2 km from Figueira da Foz, which offers plenty of entertainment, including a casino.
