Description

Featuring a large seaside terrace and free WiFi, Promar - Eco Beach & Spa Hotel is an award-winning ecological hotel in Porto Novo.

All rooms have frontal Atlantic Ocean views and the property is a 1-minute walk from the beach.

Each room has a private bathroom with a hairdryerAll rooms include a 32" flat-screen satellite TV, a mini fridge, safety deposit box, work desk, free WiFi and panoramic sea views Offering terraces with wind shields and parasols, the hotel's 2 restaurants serve fish and traditional pizzas, and snacks can be taken on the private beach 10 metres away.

After a swim in the sea, or in the salty water of outdoor pool, guests can enjoy the complimentary access to the gym and the Spa and relax in the heated hydrotherapy pool, Turkish bath and sauna.

Massages and Vichy shower treatments are available at an extra charge. Promar - Eco Beach & Spa Hotel is situated 2 km from the thermal springs of Vimeiro and 5 km from Santa Cruz with its bars and nightlife.
