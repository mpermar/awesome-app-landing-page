Description

Completely renovated in July of 2018, the Well Hotel & Spa offers a privileged view over the Atlantic Ocean and is a 30-minute drive from Lisbon.

Free WiFi access is available throughout the building. The hotel has a panoramic lift, 2 heated swimming pools and free access to the gym, sauna, Turkish Bath and JacuzziÂ®.

Suites, double rooms and family rooms all feature a flat-screen TV, heating or air conditioning.

All have a fridge, as well as a private bathroom with hairdryer. Some offer views of the sea and a balcony or terrace, others have views of the Alcabrichel River and the golf course.

Well Hotel & Spa features a cosy living area with a fireplace, plenty of books and a flat-screen TV with a great variety of channels.

Children can play in their own swimming pool, while adults enjoy the adults pool.

The beach, 10 metres away, invites guests to practice water sports, from surfing to fishing.

Other activities in the area include para-sailing, golfing or hiking. Guests may explore the surroundings and find the thermal baths at Vimeiro, 3 km away.

Massage services are available upon request. The Well Hotel & Spa is 3 km from Santa Cruz where guests will find plenty of entertainment.

Lisbon International Airport is a 40-minute drive away and a shuttle service can be provided upon request for an extra charge.

The A8 motorway is 10 km away from Well Hotel & Spa and provides access to Porto and Lisbon.
