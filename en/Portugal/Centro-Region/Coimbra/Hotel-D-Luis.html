Description

With panoramic views over the city of Coimbra, the Hotel D. Lus is located just a few minutes from the city centre.

The hotels guest rooms feature seating areas where guests can relax and watch satellite TV.

Each is fitted with a mini fridge. Hotel D. Lus restaurant offers picturesque views and an extensive range of traditional, regional and international cuisine.

After dinner, guests can unwind on the terrace with a drink from the bar. Portugal dos Pequenitos is less than a 5-minute drive away.

A car rental service is provided by Hotel D. Lus and free private parking is available on site.
