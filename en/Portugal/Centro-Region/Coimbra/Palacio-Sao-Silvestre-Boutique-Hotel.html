Description

Situated in Coimbra, 12 km from Portugal dos Pequenitos, Palacio São Silvestre-Boutique Hotel features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a fitness centre and a bar.

With a shared lounge, the 4-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom.

The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, room service and luggage storage for guests.

At the hotel, rooms come with a wardrobe and a flat-screen TV. Palacio São Silvestre-Boutique Hotel offers a continental or buffet breakfast.
