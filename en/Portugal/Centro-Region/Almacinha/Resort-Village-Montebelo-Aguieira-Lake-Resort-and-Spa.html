Description

With a unique architecture blending with the surrounding landscape, this 5-star resort is set in the picturesque Aguieira Reservoir. Montebelo Aguieira Spa provides a variety of treatments and massages for a surcharge and upon reservation.

Flat-screen TV and reservoir views are standard in the apartments of Montebelo Aguieira Lake Resort & Spa. They have elegant decor in gray hues, with parquet floors and modern furniture. A fully equipped kitchen and a dining table are provided.

Exclusive wines from the Dão region can be enjoyed at the resort’s restaurant along with home-made traditional cuisine. The poolside bar provides drinks during the day.

Montebelo Aguieira’s private marina offers a relaxing spot for sailing, rowing or motor-cruising. There is a tennis court, a panoramic outdoor swimming pool and a jogging trail. The resort’s natural wood provides bird watching.

The 27-hole Montebelo Golf is a 30-minute drive from Aguieira Lake Resort & Spa.
