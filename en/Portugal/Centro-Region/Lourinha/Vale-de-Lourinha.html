Description

Boasting a swimming pool, a shared lounge and a garden and views of the pool, Vale de Lourinha is situated in Lourinhã and provides accommodation with free WiFi.

Featuring a patio, every unit is air conditioned and features a dining area and a seating area with a cable flat-screen TV.

There is a private bathroom with shower and a hairdryer in each unit, along with free toiletries.

Guests at the aparthotel can enjoy a continental breakfast. Vale de Lourinha offers a hot tub.

A children's playground is also available at the accommodation, while guests can also relax on the sun terrace.

Lourinhã Museum is 1. 5 km from Vale de Lourinha, while Dino Park Lourinha is 7 km from the property.
