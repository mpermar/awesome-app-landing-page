Description

Figueiredos Hotel is situated in Lourinha less than a 3-minute drive from the Areia Branca beach.

At a 12-minute drive from Dino Park Lourinhã, this hotel offers air-conditioned rooms and includes a daily breakfast buffet.

Each room at Hotel Figueiredos has a private bathroom, a flat-screen TV and free WiFi.

Guests can enjoy a swim in the the pool, which can be indoor or outdoor, or relax in a sun lounger.

For children, the hotel offers a playground. The hotel is located 30 km from the medieval castle of Óbidos and 12 km from Peniche.

Porto de Barcas beach is a 10-minute drive away. The Figueiredos also has a large garden and 24-hour front desk service.
