Description

Located only a short walk from the beach, this unique 4-star resort features a seasonal water park and provides guests with a Spa. In the evening, guests can enjoy a delectable meal at the restaurant.

Hotel Cristal is located within close proximity to Fatima, Leiria and Marinha Grande where beautiful beaches, medieval monastries and castles can be found.
