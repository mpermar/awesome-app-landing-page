Description

Hotel Cristal Vieira Praia & Spa features a seasonal water park and is situated in front of a sandy beach.

It features modern rooms with panoramic Atlantic Ocean views. The modern rooms are elegantly decorated in light tones.

Featuring air conditioning, the rooms have a minibar and a private bathroom. Guests can enjoy a varied menu of Portuguese specialities in the panoramic restaurant, which includes sea views.

Praia guests can relax in a poolside sun lounger, or visit the wellness centre which has a Turkish bath and offers massages.

Marinha Grande, the glass-making capital of Portugal, is just a 10-minute drive from Hotel Cristal Vieira Praia & Spa.

Leiria is within a 25-minute drive. The hotel is located next to Leiria's pine forest and guests may also enjoy cycling or hiking in the surroundings.
