Description

The Beachfront - Praia D'El Rey Golf & Beach Resort offers accommodations overlooking the Atlantic Ocean.

It offers direct access to the beach, an outdoor swimming pool with sun loungers and access to a recently-opened fitness centre.

Providing sea views from the balcony or terrace, all Beachfront‘s townhouses are modern in style.

The elegant living room with sofas, flat-screen TV and DVD player includes a dining area.

The furnished balcony allows guests to enjoy meals while gazing at the sea and the Berlengas, a natural reserve.

They all have a fully equipped kitchen with dishwasher and washer/dryer, plus all the necessary appliances to prepare meals.

At 3 km, guests can enjoy the facilities of Hotel Marriott at an extra fee, which include the Kalyan Spa, Kalyan Fitness, Contato Lobby Bar, 2 restaurants and a Kids Club.

After relaxing under a poolside parasol, guests can head to the tennis court and spend some energy.

Guests wishing to practice their golf swing are only a few steps from a championship course.

The newly-opened West Cliffs Golf Course is a 10-minute drive away. A pro golf shop is also on the grounds.
