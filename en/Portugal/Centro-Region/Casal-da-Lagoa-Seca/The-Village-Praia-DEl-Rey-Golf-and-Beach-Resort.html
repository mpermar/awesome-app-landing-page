Description

Located next to the Atlantic Coast, The Village - Praia D’El Rey Golf & Beach Resort offers spacious self-catering apartments, townhouses and villas.

The accommodation is divided by properties "By the Garden", which are located next to the golf course, and by properties "By the Ocean", which are next to the resort's northern beach.

Accommodation is done in a modern decor. It includes a kitchen with kitchenware, a refrigerator, as well as a microwave and dishwasher.

A dining area is provided. Satellite TV with a DVD player is also present. The apartments and townhouses have access to a condominium swimming pool, while the villas each enjoy their own private swimming pool.

For a special rate, guests have access to the Hotel Marriot's facilities, which include a spa, fitness centre, bar, 2 restaurants and a championship golf course.

These facilities are within 1 to 3 km, depending on the booked accommodation. Other activities, such as scuba diving, are organized within the resort.

The resort is 20 km from the medieval town of Óbidos. The fishing village of Peniche is 19 km away.

Lisbon Airport is 75 km away. Guests may rent a car at the resort. Free parking is possible on-site.
