Description

Surrounded by gardens only 200 metres from the Spanish border, this Monfortinho hotel is a 5-minute drive from thermal spas.

It offers air-conditioned guestrooms and a bicycle rental service. Free Wi-Fi available.

Hotel Boavista rooms have parquet floors and antique wood furnishings. They include cable TV and come equipped with a private bathroom.

In the morning, guests can enjoy breakfast on the garden terrace. The hotel has a rustic bar and a restaurant that is open for lunch and dinner.

Hotel Boavista is a 35-minute drive from Monsanto. Nearby leisure activities include hiking and karaoke.
