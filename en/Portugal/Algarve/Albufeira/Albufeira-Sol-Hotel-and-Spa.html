Description

Albufeira Sol Hotel & Spa is a modern 4-star hotel with bright rooms featuring balconies.

Situated in Areias de São João, it offers an outdoor pool, restaurant and is a 10-minute walk from the beach.

The rooms at Albufeira Sol Hotel & Spa are decorated with neutral colours and wooden furniture.

All rooms have a full bathroom equipped with hairdryer and amenities, air conditioning, telephone, LCD satellite TV with 30 channels, safe, tea facilities, fridge, microwave and balcony.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. The Hotel's restaurant serves local Portuguese dishes as well as international cuisine.

Throughout the day, in addition to the main meals there are snacks available at the Pool Bar.

Guests can enjoy the occasional evening entertainment, such as live music, dancing and karaoke, at the bar.

The new SPA area is available at an extra fee and has a contemporary design. It includes a sauna, hot tub, Turkish bath, gym, hairdresser, massage room, relaxation and beautician.

Popular local beaches of Oura and Santa Eulália are just 1 km from the Albufeira Sol Hotel & Spa, and Albufeira’s city centre is 3 km away.

The unit is a 5-minute drive from the main entertainment area of Albufeira, the lively Strip with plenty of bars, clubs, restaurants and shops.
