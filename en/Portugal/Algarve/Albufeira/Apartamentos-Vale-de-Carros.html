Description

Situated in a green hilly area near Albufeira, Vale de Carros is 2. 5 km from world-renowned Falesia Beach.

The complex offers self-contained accommodation around gardens, 2 pools and a tennis court.

Sun-drenched apartments and studios at Apartamentos Turísticos Vale de Carros are pleasantly located in rustic terraced houses.

Each includes a kitchenette with dining area, a TV set and en suite bathroom with bathtub.

Guests can enjoy lively karaoke and entertainment evenings at the hotel bar. Plenty of bars and cafes dot Açoteias town centre, 600 meters away from Vale de Carros resort.

A bicycle rental service is available on site and a shuttle service can be organised upon request.
