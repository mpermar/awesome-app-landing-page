Description

Offering 2-storey villas overlooking the Atlantic Ocean, Pinheiros da Balaia presents landscaped gardens, 2.

2 kilometers from Santa Eulália Beach. It offers an outdoor swimming pool and a bar.

Each has a modern décor and includes a living/dining area which opens to the garden area.

Outside, there is a furnished patio, where guests can enjoy the meals prepared in their own fully equipped kitchen.

Pinheiros da Balaia villas have 2 twin rooms and 2 individual sofa-beds in the living area to accommodate 2 guests.

Guests can enjoy a drink from the bar in one of the poolside sun loungers, while children have fun in the playground.

There is a golf course within 1 km. Faro International Airport is 30 km away, and guests can book a shuttle service in the 24-hour reception.

Albufeira’s Strip, with its bars, restaurants and clubs, is less than a 5-minute drive.
