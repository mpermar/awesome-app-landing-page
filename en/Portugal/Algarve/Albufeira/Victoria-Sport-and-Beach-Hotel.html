Description

Situated close to Falésia beach in Albufeira, Victoria Sport & Beach Hotel boasts an outdoor pool.

The hotel has a children's playground and sun terrace, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and is equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Certain units feature a seating area where you can relax. Every room is equipped with a private bathroom.

For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer. Victoria Sport & Beach Hotel features free WiFi throughout the property.

A running track, tennis court and 2 paddle tennis courts are offered. High jump, pole vaulting, long jump and shot put facilities are provided as well.

The property offers direct access to the Açoteias international cross-country trail.

The area is popular for diving and cycling. The nearest airport is Faro Airport, 30 km from Victoria Sport & Beach Hotel.
