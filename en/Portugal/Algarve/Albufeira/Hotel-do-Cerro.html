Description

With panoramic views of Albufeira Bay, Hotel do Cerro offers comfortable rooms and facilities such as indoor and outdoor pools, a fitness room and a spa.

The contemporary rooms of Hotel do Cerro are well-equipped with air conditioning and satellite TV.

All rooms have either balconies or terraces, where guests can enjoy the picturesque views.

Guests can visit the sauna and Turkish bath. There is free Wi-Fi in public areas for quick access to the latest news and emails.

Hotel do Cerro offers several dining possibilities, from à la carte to buffet-style.

You can enjoy a drink and evening entertainment at the hotel bar. It is only a short walk from Fisherman’s Beach, and several natural parks are in the vicinity.
