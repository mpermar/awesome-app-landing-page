Description

The property has large relaxing rooms and an outdoor pool. Bask by the pool on one of the lounge chairs.

The tea room offers a variety of traditional products, most of them produced on site. Breakfast is served with fresh traditional products in a lounge with a magnificent panoramic view of the surrounding countryside.

In addition to the nice beaches nearby, from Quinta do Mel it is also possible to get to all the recreational facilities around the area of Albufeira and Vilamoura, including restaurants, theme parks and stores.
