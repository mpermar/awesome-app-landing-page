Description

Overlooking the stunning São Rafael Beach, the luxurious 5-star Sao Rafael Suites is a 5-minute drive from Albufeira.

It features fantastic sea and garden views and offers free parking. The modern and stylish rooms and suites at Sao Rafael Suites come with a balcony and offer land or sea views.

Each features modern facilities and is equipped with a minibar, a private bathroom with free toiletries and a seating area with a satellite TV.

Sao Rafael Suites serves an elegant buffet for breakfast. The hotel’s 3 restaurants also serve delicious international and Mediterranean dishes for lunch and dinner.

Drinks are served on the hotel's terrace, which boasts fantastic ocean views. Guests can lounge at the hotel’s swimming pools or get a massage at the wellness centre.
