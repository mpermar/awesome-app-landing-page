Description

The rooms at EPIC SANA Algarve Hotel are spacious and have contemporary design, complemented by white marble bathrooms with rain showers and bathtubs. All rooms also include balconies with partial ocean or resort views. Free Wi-Fi access is available throughout the resort.

Breakfast and all-day dining is available at the main restaurant, both indoor and outdoor. A poolside restaurant provides the opportunity of relaxing with a drink or a meal while enjoying the sun. In the evening, a gourmet restaurant and 2 bars are also available.

Recreational facilities include a multi-sports court, a fitness center and an extensive spa. For families with children, a kids’ club provides supervised activities and care, so that parents can take a break and relax.

The EPIC SANA Algarve Hotel features a conference center for business and social events. Faro International Airport is 19 miles away and the center of Albufeira is a 15-minute drive away. Free on-site parking is available. Oura Beach is a 10-minute drive away.
