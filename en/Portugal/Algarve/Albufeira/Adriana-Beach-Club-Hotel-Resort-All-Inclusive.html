Description

Surrounded by gardens, all-inclusive Adriana Beach Club is 300 metres from Falesia Beach with free sun loungers and umbrellas for guests.

It has 2 adult and 2 childrens outdoor pools, sports facilities and a spa with massage facilities.

All air-conditioned rooms feature a furnished balcony or terrace. The brightly decorated rooms include a satellite TV, fridge and private bathroom.

Breakfast is served daily and Adriana's 3 spacious restaurants offer a range of international and Portuguese cuisine.

The 2 open-air bars have table and chairs on the sun terrace overlooking the pool.

Restaurants and bars are open depending on the season. Facilities include minigolf and tennis, football and beach volleyball courts.

A sauna, hot tub and gym are available and bicycles are available to rent. Entertainment, including live music, shows and a kids club, are provided daily.

Vilamoura is 3 km from the Adriana Beach Club Hotel via the beach and a free shuttle is available to Albufeira, 10 km away.

Faro Airport is a 45-minute drive away. The Ocenico Laguna and Victoria golf courses are 12 km away.
