Description

The traditional-style apartments at Albufeira Clube all have private balconies and cable TV. Every apartment has self-catering facilities, a separate dining area and a private bathroom.

The O Pavilhão and Casa Azul restaurants serve both local and international cuisine. Light snacks and drinks are available in the hotel's bars, A Pergola and A Cascata.

Recreational activities available nearby including snorkeling, water skiing and wind surfing. Bicycles can be rented for exploring the locate area. There is a separate children’s swimming pool, and a children’s play area.

Clube Albufeira is less than 5 minutes by car from Albufeira city center which has attractions including the Modelo Shopping Center. It is 30 minutes’ drive to Faro Airport.
