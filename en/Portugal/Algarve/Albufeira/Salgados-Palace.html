Description

The luxurious 5-star Salgados Palace is next to the Salgados Golf and features scenic ocean views.

The hotel features 3 outdoor swimming pools, a SPA & Wellness Centre as well as restaurants and bars with panoramic views.

The modern rooms at the Salgados Palace feature a private entrance, bathroom, flat-screen cable TV and a minibar.

Some rooms also feature a balcony and views over the ocean and the golf course. Guests can enjoy local and international cuisine in one of the property’s à la carte restaurants.

The restaurants feature plenty of natural light and offer views over the property, the golf course and the ocean.

Breakfast is served each morning in the bright dining rooms. A snack bar is available, for lighter meals.

Also featured are lounge bars, where refreshing drinks are served. The outdoor swimming pools feature sun loungers and a terrace, where guests can quietly relax following a swim.

The Spa & Wellness centre is accessible for a surcharge, available only to guests with 16 or older, and features a heated indoor swimming pool, Turkish bath and a sauna.

Massages are also available, at a surcharge. The hotel also features a state-of-the-art business centre.

Free WiFi access is available in public areas. It is a 2-minute walk from the famed Salgados Golf, with its green 18-hole courses.

Salgados Beach is a 5-minute drive away. The Albufeira city centre is a 16-minute drive and the Salgados Palace is a 29-minute drive from the famous cliff-surrounded Falésia Beach.

The Faro International Airport is a 40-minute drive away from the Salgados Palace.
