Description

All of the Alfagar Apartments have a private balcony with ocean or garden views. In addition, every apartment offers kitchenette facilities and a separate dining area.

Guests of the Alfagar Resort can dine in the restaurant, which serves an international menu throughout the day. There is also a pool bar serving drink and snacks, and a mini-market for self-catering supplies.

Enjoy swimming in one of the pools or sunbathing in the garden. Children have their own pool and an exciting adventure playground. The Balaia Golf Course is 0.6 mi away.

Alfagar Resort is a 5-minute drive from the lively shopping streets of Areias de São João. Faro Airport is 21 mi away and on-site car parking is free.
