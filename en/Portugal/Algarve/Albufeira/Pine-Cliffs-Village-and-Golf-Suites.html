Description

Situated on a cliff-top surrounded by pine trees. It features pools, a tennis courts, a spa and a golf course.

The bright suites and villas of the Pine Cliffs Village & Golf Suites are decorated with tiled bathrooms.

They are all equipped with satellite TV and air conditioning and include terraces; some have a private swimming pool.

During school holidays, an extensive range of activities is organized by experienced staff at the Porto Pirata Children’s Club.

A well-equipped gym with a personal trainer can help guests stay fit. Located within the Aldeia Das Acoteias Natural Reserve, Pine Cliffs Village & Golf Suites is a 15-minute drive from central Albufeira.
