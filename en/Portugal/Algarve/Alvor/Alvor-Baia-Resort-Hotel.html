Description

Located in the Algarve village of Alvor, the Alvor Baia Resort Hotel is 1. 5 km from its centre and provides a free shuttle to the beach, a 5-minute drive away.

Surrounded by a large green area, it offers swimming pool with a children’s section.

Each of the 402 air-conditioned suites comes with a furnished balcony, free WiFi, 2 flat-screen cable TVs, a telephone, a microwave, a refrigerator and bathroom with free toiletries, a bath and a shower.

In addition there is a living room with a sofa bed and a dining area. Alvor Baia Resort Hotel features restaurants, bars, a conference room and a Spa area with a heated hydrotherapy pool, Turkish bath, sauna and massage services for an extra charge.

In addition there are 2 padel courts and a children’s playground. Garage parking is available.

Golf enthusiasts will find the Alto Golf Course and the Penina Golf Course within 3 km.

The city of Portimão is a 4-minute drive away and Lagos is a 24-minute drive. Faro International Airport is 74 km away.
