Description

Set on a clifftop overlooking the Burgau fishing village and the Atlantic Ocean, this eco-friendly hotel features an outdoor swimming pool.

It is 70 metres from the 17th century Burgau Fort and a 2-minute walk from the beach.

All rooms are neatly decorated with wooden furniture and they all have private bathrooms with a bath or shower, plus a small refrigerator.

Some rooms have balconies with sea views. Free WiFi is available throughout the hotel.

Set on the border of the Vicentine Natural Park, Hotel Burgau offers a daily breakfast buffet and is within 100 metres from local restaurants serving Algarve’s finest fish and seafood delicacies.

Surrounded by a rich cultural heritage, guests of the Hotel Burgau may visit the dinosaurs’ footprints in Salema’s Beach and the Vale de Boi’s Palaeolithic archaeological village, both within 6 km.

In addition, guests can explore the ancient shipwrecks and the Roman artefacts in the Boca do Rio area, 5 km way.

Focused on biodiversity, Hotel Burgau’s trained staff will gladly provide educational workshops and activities regarding endemic flora and fauna.

Alternatively guests are welcome to engage in activities like surf, diving, fishing, cycling, mountain biking and golf.
