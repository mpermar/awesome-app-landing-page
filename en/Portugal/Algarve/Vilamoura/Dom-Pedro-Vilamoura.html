Description

At just a 2-minute walk from Vilamoura Beach, this recently renovated 4-star hotel is 500 metres away from Vilamoura’s lively marina.

Dom Pedro Vilamoura has a spa, restaurant and indoor and outdoor swimming pools. The property comes with elegant rooms, each with a flat-screen TV and a minibar.

All rooms include a private bathroom, safety deposit box and air conditioning. Some rooms include a balcony and/or views over the Atlantic Ocean.

Guests can relax in Dom Pedro’s Spa Aquae, which features an indoor swimming pool, a Jacuzzi ® and cardiovascular equipment in the fitness centre.

A daily buffet breakfast is served at the property. Restaurant Oliva serves a buffet with Mediterranean influence.

Show cooking is also available at the restaurant, upon request. Vegetarian and Vegan options are also available.

An animation programme during summer and facilities such as a playground and kids pool are offered.

Dom Pedro Vilamoura also features a games room. A beach bar with sun loungers is also available during the summer season.

The Vilamoura Casino is just a 2-minute walk from the Dom Pedro. Golf enthusiasts will find some of the most renowned courses of the region within 3 km.

The renowned Falésia Beach is within a 6-minute drive. Faro International Airport is 20 km away from Dom Pedro Vilamoura.
