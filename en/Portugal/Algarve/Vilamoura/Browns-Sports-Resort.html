Description

Set among some of the finest golf courses in the Algarve, Browns is renowned for its sporting and leisure facilities, situated in a relaxing environment 4 km away from the Vilamoura Marina and Falésia Beach.

Accommodation is comprised of 1-bedroom, 2-bedroom and 3-bedroom units, with each one having a private outside area overlooking the gardens.

Free WiFi is available in the rooms as well as at the clubhouse. Browns' guests can enjoy a wide array of drinks and meals while sitting out on the terrace or while relaxing in the indoor space, where you can watch your favourite sports on TV, play pool or darts and admire all the displayed memorabilia from sporting teams who have stayed at Browns in the past.

Situated in the main building, guests will find the Health Club which is comprised of a gym, squash court, tennis court, sauna, steam room, indoor and outdoor pool.
