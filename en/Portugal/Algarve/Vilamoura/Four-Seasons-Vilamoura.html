Description

Featuring a lagoon-style outdoor pool and a spa, this Vilamoura resort offers bright and spacious apartments with balconies overlooking the palm garden.

Airport transfers to Faro Airport can be arranged. Four Seasons Vilamoura overlooks the Pinhal, an 18-hole championship golf course with a putting green, driving range and Club House.

The resort provides a private entrance to the course. Free Wi-Fi is available in the bar and lounge.

With a complete kitchenette, all studios and apartments at Four Seasons Vilamoura are decorated in a modern style with soft colours.

Each includes a private bathroom with a bathtub and shower. The on-site restaurants serve open buffets and √† la carte dishes indoors or on the poolside terraces.

The restaurant A Cascata offers Brazilian, Italian & Portuguese theme nights, and freshly caught fish at lunch.

The Brasserie offers a sophisticated menu in a comfortable atmosphere. Live entertainment is organised on a regular basis.

The pool bar provides refreshing drinks, and a take-away service for in-room meals is available.

Leisure facilities include a heated indoor and outdoor pool, a hot tub, and a sauna.

Various beauty treatments can be enjoyed at the spa, and guests can work out at the gym.
