Description

Surrounded by manicured gardens and 3 swimming pools, Old Village & Prestige is located in Vilamoura, a 5-minute drive from Falésia beach.

It offers self-contained apartments with free access to an outdoor swimming pool.

Each apartment is well-equipped and includes a furnished balcony or terrace with parasols.

Some of the air-conditioned apartments include a living room, and all offer private bathroom with free toiletries.

Guests can prepare their own meals in the fully equipped kitchen and enjoy them in the privacy of the balconies or terraces.

Restaurants and bars are within 1 km. The 3 outdoor swimming pools provide a relaxing setting to sunbathe and take a refreshing dip.

Younger guests can have fun at the playground. Oceânico Pinhal Golf Course is next to the property.

Free parking is available on site and Faro International Airport is 24 km away. Vilamoura Marina is a 15-minute walk away.
