Description

In the air conditioned rooms, guests can relax on the balcony or watch some satellite TV. Each has a kitchenette with a microwave and a refrigerator.

For guests who like to keep active, Hotel Apartamento Do Golfe has a tennis/racquetball court and can help organize diving, hiking and horseback riding trips. It also offers a 24-hour front desk and a car and bike rental service.

At Apartamento Do Golfe’s restaurant, international and traditional, Portuguese cuisine can be savored with a fine wine from the cellar. Refreshments and light snacks are offered at the bar and drinks can be enjoyed on the terrace.

Free private parking is available on site. Many golf courses are nearby including The Old Course - Vilamoura and Pinhal Golf Course – Vilamoura, both a 10-minute walk from the hotel.
