Description

Mouraliz offers self-catering apartments with free WiFi in Vilamoura centre. Falésia Beach is 15 minutes’ walking, while the trendy Vilamoura Marina is just 80 metres away.

Each air-conditioned apartment features a fully equipped kitchen with oven, electric hob and refrigerator.

They all have the necessary equipment to prepare a homemade meal, including an electric kettle.

The living area includes a flat-screen LCD TV and the ground floor apartments include a private patio with a small garden, where guests can relax after a day of sightseeing.

Golf enthusiasts can enjoy 6 golf courses in a 4 km radius. Mouraliz can organise a shuttle service to Faro International Airport, located around 25 km away.

Bicycle, boat and car rentals can also be arranged, helping guests to explore the Algarve at their own pace.
