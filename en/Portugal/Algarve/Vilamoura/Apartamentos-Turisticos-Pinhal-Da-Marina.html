Description

Apartamentos Turísticos Pinhal Da Marina is located just half a mile from Vilamoura’s center and a 20-minute walk from the Marina and sandy beach. The Oceânico Pinhal Golf Course is less than a 10 minute walk away.

All apartments are south-facing and overlook the grounds or the pool area. Each has a kitchenette with a microwave.

Pinhal Da Marina‘s restaurant offers a dining menu of traditional Portuguese and international dishes, accompanied by a selection Portuguese wines. A kids menu is also available.
