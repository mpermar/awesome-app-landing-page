Description

Just a few metres away from Marina Beach and the Vilamoura Marina, the Vila Gale Ampalius is a 4-star hotel in sunny Algarve.

Facilities include an outdoor pool, a health club, and a tennis court. The rooms of Vila Galé Ampalius are decorated with warm colours and wooden furniture.

All rooms have a work desk, cable TV, a minibar, and a private bathroom. Some rooms have a balcony with panoramic pool or sea views.

The Versátil Restaurant offers traditional Portuguese dishes and international cuisine.

The pool bar serves cocktails prepared on the spot with fresh fruit. The health club offers a variety of health and beauty treatments, as well as an indoor pool, a Turkish bath, and a sauna.

Massages are also available, at an extra fee. Guests can explore the beauty of the region with a rental car or by cycling.

Children can have fun at Vila Galé's Nep Kids Club. The Ampalius is close to golf courses, the Casino of Vilamoura, Falésia Beach and the Roman Ruins of Cerro da Vila.
