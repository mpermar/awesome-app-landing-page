Description

Set 5 km from Salir, 6 km from Salir Castle Ruins, Casa da Tita features a year-round outdoor pool and views of the mountain.

Free WiFi is available. The pet-friendly accommodation is air conditioned and features a satellite flat-screen TV.

Some units feature a terrace and/or patio with pool or garden views. The junior suite and the double rooms have access to a shared kitchen and terrace.

Each unit is equipped with a private bathroom with bathrobes and slippers. Towels are offered.

Casa da Tita also includes a barbecue. Free use of bicycles is available at the property and the area is popular for cycling and hiking.

The property provides a shuttle to Salir for a surcharge to the guests hiking in the Via Algarviana.

The nearest airport is Faro Airport, 24 km from the property. Free private parking is available on site.
