Description

Located 500 metres from the famous Praia da Rocha beach in Portimão, Clube Vilarosa offers fully equipped apartments surrounded by 5000 m² of gardens.

It features 2 swimming pools for adults, 1 for children and 2 tennis courts. Each apartment is carefully decorated with quality furniture.

Living areas feature a sofa, a dining table and a TV. Superior accommodation includes air conditioning.

Guests may prepare their own meals using the fully equipped kitchen or kitchenette.

Alternatively guests may enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant and relax in the evening at one of the pool bars.

In addition, a few minutes’ walk away guests can visit the bustling bars and restaurants of the Praia da Rocha beach.

Golf enthusiasts will find the Pestana Golf Academy within 3. 5 km and families may visit the Slide & Splash Water Park 10 km away.

Supermarkets and shopping centres are available within a 5-minute walk plus the International Algarve Racetrack is 13 km away.
