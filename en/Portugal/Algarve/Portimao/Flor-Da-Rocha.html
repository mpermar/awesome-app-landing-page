Description

Located 600 metres from the Praia da Rocha beach, the Flor da Rocha offers spacious apartments with kitchenetes and balconies.

The Flor Da Rocha Apartments are situated 3 km from Portimão’s city centre. There are many restaurants, minimarkets and shops within a 5-minute walk from the property.

The apartments have private bathrooms, satellite TVs and safes. Each is equipped with air conditioning in the living room.

There is a 24-hour reception. Guests can enjoy the outdoor swimming pool or play a game of tennis on the tennis courts.

Children will enjoy the children’s pool and the ice creams available at the pool bar.

The Amarilis bus stop is situated just around the corner of Flor Da Rocha. The Portimão Train Station is a 10-minute drive away and offers connections to Faro in 1 hour and 20 minutes.
