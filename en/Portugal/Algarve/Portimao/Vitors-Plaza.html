Description

Located 1. 5 km from Vau Beach, the recently renovated Vitor’s Plaza offers an indoor and outdoor pools, tennis and volleyball courts and a football field.

The spacious apartments feature a balcony and free WiFi. All apartments at Vitor's Plaza have a kitchenette fitted with a microwave, a toaster and a stovetop.

There is a flat-screen TV, a safety deposit box and air conditioning in the units.

A hairdryer is provided. Guests can lounge by the outdoor pool under the shade of the parasols and the palm trees.

Vitor's Plaza also offers a hot tub, a gym, table tennis and mini golf. For younger guests, the hotel features a playground and a games room.

The Three Brothers, the on-site restaurant, offers a range of Portuguese and International dishes.

Refreshing drinks and cocktails as well as light meals are served at the poolside bar.

There is a mini-market at the property with daily fresh bread. Vitor hotel provides a car and bicycle rental service for those who wish to explore the area.

The nearest golf course is located at an 8-minute walk. Free public parking is available on site.
