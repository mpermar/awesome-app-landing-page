Description

Oasis Parque Country Club is located a 10-minute drive from the quaint fishing village of Alvor and 4 km from Portimão.

It features tennis courts, Jacuzzi ®, and 2 pools surrounded by large terraces. The self-catering properties are elegantly decorated with modern furnishings.

All units have a TV, a balcony and a fully equipped kitchen with dishwasher and washing machine.

Oasis Parque Country Club includes a poolside coffee bar/restaurant which serves traditional and English homemade dishes in a friendly cosy atmosphere.

Local supermarkets, restaurants and bars are within walking distance. This country club features a leisure area with a large outdoor swimming pool, an indoor pool and 2 tennis courts.

It also has children’s pools with a play area and sun terraces. There is a computer in the lobby area with Internet access, which guests can use at an additional cost.

Several golf courses are available within a 5-minute drive. The surrounding area has a selection of shops, including supermarkets.

Faro Airport is 45 minutes away and a shuttle service can be arranged at the hotel.
