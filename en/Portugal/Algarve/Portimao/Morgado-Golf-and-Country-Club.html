Description

The Morgado Golf & Country Club is surrounded by golf courses and overlooks the Monchique Mountain.

This 4-star hotel has 4 outdoor swimming pools, a restaurant and includes a buffet breakfast.

Free WiFi is available in all areas. All rooms feature a flat-screen cable TV, private bathroom and sweeping views over the surroundings.

Guests can try several local and international dishes in the varied buffet restaurant of the hotel.

A rich breakfast buffet is served each morning in the property. The hotel is surrounded by two 18-hole golf courses and is ideal for golfing enthusiasts.

The Golf Academy and Club House feature golfing equipment rental and also provide individual or group golf lessons.

There are 2 outdoor pools with an adjacent sun deck and 2 shallow pools for children.

There is also a comfortable sun terrace and sun loungers in front of each one of the refreshing swimming pools.

The centre of Portimão is a 20-minute drive and features plenty of restaurants, cafés and bars.

The famed Rocha Beach is a 23-minute drive away and is a popular tourist spot in the Algarve.

Alvor is a 23-minute drive away while the Portimão Marina ares is 14 km away. Albufeira features plenty of well-known beaches and is a 35-minute drive away.

Morgado Golf & Country Club features an entertaining program for children at the Kids Club and a variety of shows at night.

The Faro International Airport is a 51-minute drive away from the Morgado Golf & Country Club.
