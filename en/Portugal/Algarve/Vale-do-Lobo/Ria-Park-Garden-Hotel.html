Description

Located within Ria Formosa Natural Park, the Ria Park Garden features tropical gardens and a pool enclosed by flowers and plants.

The Vale de Garrão Beach is 800 metres away. With its many verandas and arches, the Arab-inspired Ria Park Garden Hotel offers spacious rooms and suites with private terraces and satellite TVs.

Guests can relax at the hotel's private beach area at Garrao Beach or visit the spa with its hot tub, Turkish bath and sauna.

There are also 2 tennis courts and an official-size football field. Ria Park’s restaurant serves fresh fish, salads and international meals.

The pool bar and lobby bar provide refreshing drinks and light meals during the day and evening.

Golf enthusiasts will find some of the most renowned golf courses within 3 km including Vale do Lobo, Quinta do Lago and Oceanico.

Vilamoura is 12 km away. The hotel is situated in the Vale do Lobo Valley, 20 km from Faro International Airport and 2 hours from Lisbon and Seville.
