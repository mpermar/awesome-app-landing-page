Description

Set in Vale do Lobo in the Algarve Region, 18 km from Albufeira, the beachfront Dunas Douradas Beach Club features an 3 outdoor pools, one of them for children, as well as an indoor pool, a sauna and a hot tub.

The resort has a terrace and views of the sea, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

The rooms include a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Certain units feature a seating area where you can relax.

You will find a coffee machine in the room. Every room is equipped with a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower.

Extras include bath robes, free toiletries and a hair dryer. Breakfast is available upon request at the Dunas Douradas Beach Club.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. This resort has a private beach area and bike hire is available.

The area is popular for golfing and horse riding. Faro is 11 km from Dunas Douradas Beach Club, while Vilamoura is 7 km away.
