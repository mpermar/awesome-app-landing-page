Description

Located in the countryside of Algarve in São Bartolomeu de Messines, Bartholomeu Guesthouse offers air-conditioned rooms with a mix of modern and old in an 1893 recently renovated building.

Free WiFi is available. The rooms at Bartholomeu Guesthouse include private bathrooms and are equipped with a flat-screen TV.

Beds in a dormitory room are also available. Bartholomeu Guesthouse features an à la carte restaurant on site serving traditional Portuguese dishes as well as international cuisine.

Guests can enjoy their meals with the best variety of wines available. In addition there is a snack bar and room service at guests' disposal.

With a 24-hour front desk, Bartholomeu Guesthouse includes a shared lounge area and shared kitchen facilities where guests can prepare their own meals.

Centrally located, guests can access most services in the town of Silves after a few minutes’ walk.

Guests can enjoy the best that Algarve has to offer including the beaches of Albufeira 25 km away and the golf courses and the casino of Vilamoura 30 km away.
