Description

Offering a tranquil location in the countryside and an outdoor swimming pool, Monte Joao Roupeiro - Turismo Rural is located 2.

6 km from Odeceixe. It is within Sudoeste Alentejano e Costa Vicentina Natural Park.

This farm stay provides double rooms and self-catering one-bedroom houses. The layout of each accommodation is based on Alentejo architecture.

The Monte Joao Roupeiro - Turismo Rural provides free WiFi access. Guests can head to Odeceixe and enjoy the selection of restaurants available here.

Guests can enjoy the sun by the pool and in their own private terrace. The beautiful Arrifana Beach is 21 km from Monte João Roupeiro.
