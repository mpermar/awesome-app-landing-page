Description

This old traditional farm in Algarve features 12 ha of organic Mediterranean orchards.

The self-catering units come with private patios and fireplaces. The Praia Verde Beach is only a 5-minute drive away.

Quinta da Fornalha offers studios and apartments equipped with full kitchens. Free WiFi is available in all areas.

In the summer months, guests can refresh in the outdoor pool or enjoy bird watching at the Castro Marim Nature Reserve.

Most Algarves cities and villages can be reached by train, only 500 metres away.
