Description

Modern rooms and holiday homes are offered by One2Seven AL in Ferragudo, located on the opposite bank of Portimo along the Arade River.

On-site leisure facilities include an outdoor pool and a gym. Free WiFi is available throughout.

Wooden floors and large windows define all accommodations at the One2Seven AL. The spacious houses provide a fully equipped kitchen with dishwasher, and a private roof terrace.

One of the property's houses has been reconverted to a guest house and has accommodation available in a suite and a twin room.

A continental breakfast is available in the morning. Afterwards, guests can relax on the common lounge terraces and garden by the pool.

The property is situated 300 metres from the Fort of So Joo do Arade. An airport shuttle can be arranged to Faro Airport, 55 km away.

While Ferragudo boasts many eateries and bars, guests can also cross the river by taxi-boat to Portimo and Rocha Beach.
