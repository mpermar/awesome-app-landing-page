Description

The Eurotel Altura Hotel is located on Altura’s white sandy beach. All rooms have balconies and sea views.

Buffet breakfast is included and free parking is available on site. The hotel has an outdoor swimming pool and a wellness spa.

The spa has an animated hydrotherapy area, a Turkish bath and a themed shower. A fitness centre and 3 massage rooms are also available.

Guests have direct access to the beach through the hotel's garden. The hotel has a restaurant with views for the pool, a bar and a café.

In the hotel you can also find special areas for children and a Kids Club with activities programs.

The Eurotel Altura is a 15-minute drive from several of the Algarve’s best golf courses like the Monte Rei, Castro Marim, Quinta do Vale and Quinta da Ria Cima.
