Description

Located in Porches, Algarve, this spa resort features panoramic Atlantic Ocean views and has direct beach access.

It also has 2 restaurants, outdoor and indoor pools and a gym. The hotel’s luxurious spa offers massage services, a Turkish hammam and a hot tub.

There is also an indoor pool and an outdoor pool surrounded by an exotic garden with palm trees.

The gardens have direct access to the beach. The rooms at the Pestana Viking Beach & Golf Resort have a balcony, satellite TV and a kitchenette.

Some rooms offer garden views or uninterrupted views of the Atlantic Ocean. In the evenings guests can choose from the resort’s Valhalla à la carte restaurant and the Pool Bistro, which serves light meals, from June to September.
