Description

Be Live Family Palmeiras Village All-Inclusive is located in Porches, at just 300 metres from the beach.

The recently-renovated property includes new adult and kids' outdoor swimming pools, a kids club and a fitness centre.

The air-conditioned rooms all feature a satellite TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

Some rooms include a lateral sea view. The Be Live Family Palmeiras All-Inclusive features 3 on-site restaurants with Japanese, Brazilian and Mediterranean cuisine.

For more options, local restaurants are available within 400 metres from the Be Live Family Palmeiras.

The hotel has a pool bar, where refreshing drinks and cocktails are served until the late hours of the night.

Evening entertainment is also provided at the bar. Guests can lounge in the sun terrace, following a dip in the outdoor swimming pool.

A fitness centre is also available, for guests wishing to include a workout in their vacation time.

For golfing enthusiasts, there are several quality golf courses to choose from within a 10-minute drive from the property.

Albufeira, with beaches such as the famed Falésia Beach, is a 30-minute drive. The Faro International Airport is a 45-minute drive from the Be Live Family Palmeiras Village All-Inclusive.
