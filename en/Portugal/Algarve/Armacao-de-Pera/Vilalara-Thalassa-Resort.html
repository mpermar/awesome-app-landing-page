Description

The air-conditioned guest rooms have private balconies overlooking the gardens, the sea or the pool. Free Wi-Fi, satellite TV and a large sitting area are in all rooms.

Guests can enjoy thalassotherapy treatments and hot stone massages at the spa. A sauna and a Turkish bath are featured. Vilalara Thalassa offers a fitness center and yoga classes.

The B&G Restaurant is a low-calorie restaurant offering gourmet Portuguese cuisine and a buffet breakfast. The Terrace Grill is a seasonal restaurant providing seafood and summer barbecues. Tonic Lounge Bar has sea views from its poolside location.

Vilalara Thalassa Resort has direct access to the Praia das Gaivotas beach and a car and bike rental service is provided by the resort. Aeroporto Internacional de Faro is 35 miles away and free private parking is available on site.
