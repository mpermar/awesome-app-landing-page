Description

The apartment has a terrace, 1 bedroom, a living room and a well-equipped kitchen. The apartment also provides a flat-screen TV, washing machine and a bathroom with a bath, a bidet and free toiletries.

The apartment has a grill. Guests can go cycling or relax in the garden.

Fisherman's Beach Armacao de Pera is 1.6 miles from Apartment Quinta das Palmeiras. The nearest airport is Faro Airport, 24 miles from the accommodation.
