Description

Located between Olhão and Tavira, the Colina Verde Golf & Sports Resort features a communal outdoor swimming pool, 9-hole golf course and a spa.

The spacious apartments at Colina Verde come equipped with a complete kitchenette and air conditioning.

Each private bathroom has electric under floor heating. Various local restaurants, cafés and bars are available within a 10-minute drive from the property.

Tennis and a gym offer guests the possibility to enjoy some exercise. The golf course is suitable for beginners and advanced players, and the spa includes a hot tub, sauna and Turkish bath.

Fuseta Beach is 4 km away, and guests can enjoy bird watching in the surrounding Ria Formosa Natural Park.

Olhão is 13 km away and features a waterfront area with various fresh fish and seafood restaurants.

Luz beach is 8 km away. The Colina Verde Golf & Sports Resort is 26. 3 km from Faro International Airport.
