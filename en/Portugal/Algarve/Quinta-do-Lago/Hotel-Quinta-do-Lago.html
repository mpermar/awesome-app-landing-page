Description

The spacious and elegantly decorated rooms, with marble bathrooms, have private balconies from which guests can enjoy views of the Ria Formosa’s lagoon, the sea or the hotel's gardens. Each room features a flat-screen satellite TV and air conditioning.

In the Brisa do Mar restaurant, guests can enjoy traditional Algarvian cuisine on an open-air terrace with panoramic views. The Ca’ d'Oro serves fine Venetian cuisine in an intimate atmosphere. The Laguna Piano Bar is the ideal spot to enjoy champagne or a cocktail and overlooking the surrounding scenery.

The hotel has outdoor and indoor heated pools, and a spa featuring a choice of many rejuvenating treatments. Just a few steps away from the property, there are Quinta do Lago golf courses, South, North and Laranjal.
