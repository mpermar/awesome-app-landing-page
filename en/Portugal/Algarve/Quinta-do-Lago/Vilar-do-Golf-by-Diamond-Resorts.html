Description

This resort is situated on the 2,000-acre Quinta do Lago Estate, 4 km from Almancil's centre.

It offers an indoor swimming pool, a children’s pool, a gym and championship golf courses.

Free WiFi is available throughout the resort. The 4-star Vilar do Golf provides stylish low rise pink and white villas with fully equipped kitchens and spacious living area with cable TV.

Each apartment has a terrace overlooking sub-tropical gardens. Apartments are cleaned depending on the length of the stay.

The IlVero Restaurant features views of Quinta do Lago Golf Course and serves international dishes for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

The restaurant uses fresh local ingredients and has a varied menu. The Vilar’s lounge bar offers refreshing cocktails, while the café serves aromatic coffees and teas.

Guests can work out with cardiovascular equipment at the gym. The hotel also offers a sauna and a large indoor pool with sun decks.

Access ramps for wheelchairs and baby buggies are available throughout the resort including at the pool, reception, supermarket and lounge area.

During the summer months there is a daily children's activity club. There is also a supermarket and medical centre.
