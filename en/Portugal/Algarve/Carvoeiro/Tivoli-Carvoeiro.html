Description

Situated in the picturesque town of Carvoeiro, overlooking Vale Covo, the renovated 5-star Tivoli Carvoeiro offers high standards of quality, ideal for family holidays and memorable events.

The refurbished rooms are spacious allowing superior comfort and convenience. With its elegant contemporary design, rooms are equipped with air conditioning, WiFi, a minibar, a TV, a desk and a bathroom with a shower.

The Med Food & Wine serves fish and seafood in dishes inspired by the distinct flavours of the Atlantic, accompanied by delicious wines and a stunning view of the sea.

At The One Gourmet guests may experience the soul of the Portuguese cuisine through a bespoke affair.

The Mare Bistro, offers the freshest daily catches and produce. At the Tivoli Spa guests can enjoy a variety of treatments and find perfect balance in a memorable experience with its indoor pool, steam room and sauna.

Tivoli Active is a fitness centre with advanced training equipment where guests can work out.

Tivoli Carvoeiro’s facilities include 6 meeting rooms, most of them with direct access to the green, and capacity for 900 guests.

In addition, a wide range of services are available for children including activity plans overseen by professional monitors.

The hotel is less than 5 minutes’ drive from Vale de Milho Golf Course and Slide and Splash is 5 km away.

Tivoli Carvoeiro is a 40-minute drive from Faro International Airport. Free on-site car parking is available.
