Description

Situated on a plateau with ocean views, this property offers fully furnished townhouses.

It has an outdoor pool surrounded by a leafy garden and a restaurant. Pestana Palm Gardens’ bedrooms are decorated in warm, sunny colours and feature glass sliding doors leading onto the garden and the furnished patio.

Some rooms have panoramic sea views. Each unit has a country-style lounge fitted with a plush sofa and ornate wooden furniture.

At Pestana Palm, guests can enjoy a game of tennis on the sports court or make use of the cardio equipment in the gym.

The property also offers a sauna and massage services. A pool bar is open during Summer months.
