Description

Set in the rural Algarve landscape, Vale d'El Rei Resort is surrounded by vineyards and only 3 km from the sandy Marinha Beach.

It has 2 pools, a stream, and lake. Decorated in warm chocolate and earthy tones, all spacious rooms feature wicker and suede furnishings.

Fitted with terracotta flooring, each has a satellite TV. The Pool Terrace serves a buffet breakfast and light meals.

Mediterranean cuisine can be enjoyed at the Serenata restaurant while the Calabash Café offers a nice setting for cocktails.

Relaxation facilities at Vale d'El Rei Resort include a day spa with sauna, hot tub and Turkish baths as well a lit tennis court.

The hotel activities team can arrange horse riding, mountain biking and fishing excursions.

Vale d'El Rei Resort provides guests with a free shuttle service to Carvoeiro Beach.
