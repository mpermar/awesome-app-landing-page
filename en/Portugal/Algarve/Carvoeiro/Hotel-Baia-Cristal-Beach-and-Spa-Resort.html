Description

Hotel Baia Cristal Beach & Spa Resort is located on a cliff-top overlooking the Atlantic Ocean, just 300 metres from Vale de Centeanes Beach.

It offers an indoor and an outdoor pool, a spa and a tennis court. The rooms at the Hotel Baia Cristal provide modern amenities, including a balcony with panoramic sea views and satellite TV.

Each has its own private bathroom with bath, shower, bidet, hairdryer and free toiletries.

The Restaurant Gaivota serves rich local cuisine, while Bar Albatroz offers a relaxing atmosphere and live music.

Guests can savour exotic cocktails and snacks while relaxing on the spacious terrace.

The pool bar, which is open during the summer time, serves fresh fruit juices and light meals.

Full-body massages are on offer at the spa, which also features a hammam. More active guests can also enjoy a game at the tennis court.

Within 2 km golf enthusiasts will find the Vale de Milho Golf Course. Hotel Baia Cristal is 100 metres from bus stops and 8 km from the Estombar Railway Station.

The hotel provides a free shuttle bus to Carvoeiro village centre, 1. 3 km away. Portimo is 13 km away and Armao de Pra is a 17 km away.
