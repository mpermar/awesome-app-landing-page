Description

Beautiful cliff-top, Suites Alba offers direct access to the remote Albandeira Beach.

It has an infinity pool with Atlantic Ocean views, a peaceful garden and an elegant restaurant.

All rooms and suites at the Suites have polished wooden floors and garden or sea views.

Rooms have LCD TVs with DVD players and spacious en suite bathrooms. The Morgadinho à la carte restaurant has panoramic ocean views, and Lounge Bar is a chill-out space serving drinks and snacks.

Catedral de Vinhos serves candle-lit dinners and wine from the well-stocked cellar.

Guests can enjoy golf or horse riding nearby. Alba also offers diverse treatments and therapies for those preferring to completely unwind on holiday.

The Suites Alba is 45 minutes’ drive from Faro International Airport, and on-site car parking is free.
