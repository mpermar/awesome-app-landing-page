Description

One of our top picks in Carvoeiro.This 5-star resort has a 6 outdoor swimming pool and offers spa facilities. Located 0.6 mi from Carvoeiro Beach, it offers luxurious suites and linked villas with private balconies and panoramic views.

All suites and townhouses at Monte Santo Resort are decorated in soft, beige colors and come equipped with air conditioning and LCD TV. They include a modern kitchen and a spacious living area. Some units have a spa bath and views of the garden or ocean.

Guests can benefit from a large main swimming pool and children’s pool next to the Club House. Four additional pools are located in the resort’s most secluded area, adjacent to the linked villas. Featuring private security, the resort also has a Kid's Club.

The Alma Spa is complemented by a Wellness Center with an indoor heated pool, sauna, Turkish bath, and a gym free of charge. Treatments and massages are available at the Spa, for an extra fee.

The Aroma Restaurant has an outdoor terrace and serves local and international delicacies. There is also a lounge bar that offers a selection of 30 original cocktails.

Resort Monte Santo is 0.9 mi away from Vale do Milho Golf. It is a 10-minute drive from Slide and Splash Water Park. Faro Airport is just 40 mi away. A free shuttle service is provided for the beach, to Carvoeiro Village and the golf courses.
