Description

One of our top picks in Quarteira.Located beside the Aquashow Outdoor Water Park, this 4-star hotel offers rooms with a private balcony. It features indoor and outdoor pools, a Jacuzzi速, and a fitness centre. Free WiFi is available.

The air-conditioned rooms at Aquashow Park Hotel feature a modern interior with parquet floors. All rooms have a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a work desk and a minibar.

The hotel offers free access to the Aquashow Outdoor Water Park, from 1 May to 30 September, which offers a water rollercoaster, a large wave pool, and countless water slides.

Guests can enjoy local Portuguese dishes as well international cuisine at Restaurant Charme. The 2 bars offer a wide variety of snacks and drinks, including exotic cocktails.

Aquashow Park Hotel is only a 5-minute drive from the blue-flag Quarteira Beach and the renowned golf courses of Vilamoura. It is possible to rent a car or bicycle at the hotel. Free parking is available on site.
