Description

Costa d'Oiro Ambiance Village offers the Mediterranean architecture of villas, gardens, terraces and swimming pools spread throughout.

It is 300 metres from Lagos’ Dona Ana Beach. Each room is uniquely decorated with a different style, according to a Mediterranean theme.

All open out to a private balcony or terrace, and some offer views of the swimming pool.

The Costa d'Oiro breakfast experience - choose from the extensive and varied breakfast menu, the perfect start for a day by the pool, or exploring the coast.

There is a wide selection on offer, including the hot and cold buffet, as well as the Portuguese corner, offering exciting local delicacies from the Algarve.

Themed nights are held 2 nights per week, one with Fado music, and the other with a barbecue and folklore.

The Restaurante do Village serves breakfast, snacks and dinner, always with Portuguese recipes in mind.

The helpful and friendly staff at Costa d'Oiro Ambiance Village can help you organize activities such as windsurfing, canoeing, dolphin safaris and surfing.

Ponta da Piedade Lighthouse with its fabulous caves and beaches is 5 minutes’ walking from the property.
