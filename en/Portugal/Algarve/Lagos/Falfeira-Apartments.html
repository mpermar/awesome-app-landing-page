Description

Set in a quiet rural location near Lagos, Falfeira Apartments are a 5 minutes’ drive from the marina and Atlantic beaches.

With sea views from a distance, this property features an outdoor swimming pool. Falfeira’s apartments are fitted with wooden furnishings, creating a rustic and homely style.

All have views from the pool, and some come with a furnished balcony. Falfeira’s apartments include a fully equipped kitchen and barbecue facilities.

Within 2 km guests can find numerous restaurants and bars at the marina. The outdoor swimming pool is surrounded by sun loungers and a wide garden area, providing relaxation moments.

Guests practice golf within 3 km, and enjoy diving and snorkelling in the immediate surroundings.

Faro International Airport is 1 hour-drive away and there is free parking on site.

Lagos’ city centre is less than 2. 5 km away, and Portimão is 22 km. Sagres’ Fortress is a 30 minutes’ drive away.
