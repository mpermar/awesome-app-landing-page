Description

The guest rooms at Solar de Mós are decorated in a rustic style and include an LCD TV, air conditioning and a sitting area. Each private bathroom offers a bathrobe and a hairdryer.

Guests can relax on a lounge chair by the pool or book a massage for a relaxing afternoon. Children can enjoy the kid’s pool, play darts or relax in the lobby with its old clocks and mosaic tiles.

The restaurant with its fireplace offers a classic setting to taste some garlic shrimp or traditionally cooked lamb. The adjoining terrace overlooks the well-maintained garden and pool area.

Solar de Mós is one mile from the historic quarter of Lagos, and half a mile from the Porto de Mós beach. The Boavista and Palmares golf courses are less than 4 miles away.

The 24-hour front desk staff at Solar de Mós can arrange car and bike rentals. Free parking is available.
