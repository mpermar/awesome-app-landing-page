Description

Situated in Lagos in the Algarve Region, 6 km from Meia Praia Beach Lagos, Aldeia Azul features an outdoor pool, barbecue and children's playground.

The resort has a sun terrace and sauna, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

Free WiFi is offered throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Every house at this resort is air conditioned and comes with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and DVD player.

All units feature a living area to relax after a busy day. You will find a coffee machine and a kettle in the room.

All units include a private bathroom. You will find a shared lounge at the property.

You can play tennis at the resort. There is also a football field and the option to play volleyball.

The resort also offers car hire. Lagos Marina is 4. 3 km from Aldeia Azul, while Lagos Live Science Center is 4.

3 km from the property. The Boavista Golf Course is 3 km away and the nearest airport is Faro Airport, 89 km from Aldeia Azul.
