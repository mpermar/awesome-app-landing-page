Description

Each unit comes with a balcony, a fully equipped kitchen with an oven, a seating area with a sofa, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with bath and a hair dryer. A toaster, a fridge and stovetop are also featured, as well as a coffee machine and a electric tea pot.

Cycling and hiking can be enjoyed nearby.

Meia Praia Beach Lagos is a 12-minute walk from the apartment, while Lagos Marina is half a mile from the property. The nearest airport is Faro Airport, 39 miles from Ferienwohnung mit Pool.
