Description

Set within an ecological landscape on a cliff overlooking the Atlantic, this holiday village features 2 outdoor pools, in separate areas.

The units have a private terrace with views of the garden or Luz Bay. Belver Porto Dona Maria offers townhouses and villas equipped with dishwashers and cable TV.

Private bathrooms with bathtubs are also included. The on-site grocery store sells local specialities and light meals.

The Cabana do Pescador coffee shop offers drinks and sandwiches, while the L’Escargot Restaurant serves Mediterranean cuisine.

Guests can relax by the pool or book an unwinding massage. It is also possible to practice your golf skills at the Dona Maria Golf Academy, which has its own driving range and putting green.

Scuba diving and kite surfing are available as well. The reception staff can arrange car rentals and excursions.
