Description

Offering a sun terrace, Quinta da Falcoeira is situated in Luz, 10 km from Lagos.

Free private parking is available on site. Some rooms include views of the mountain or garden.

For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer. Quinta da Falcoeira features free WiFi.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including golfing, horse riding and diving.

The guest house also offers bike hire. Lagos Live Science Center is 9. 5 km from Quinta da Falcoeira, while Lagos Marina is 8.
