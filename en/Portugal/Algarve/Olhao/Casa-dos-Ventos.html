Description

Located 4 km from Olhão in the Algarve region, Casa dos Ventos is a B&B that offers stunning views of the sea and the mountains and an outdoor swimming pool.

Free WiFi is available. Surrounded by a 2 hectare property with fruit trees and gardens, Casa dos Ventos’ rooms are furnished with a typical Portuguese style and include their own private bathroom and access to a living room with fireplace, an ample dining area and a shared kitchen.

Besides the kitchen, guests may use the outdoor barbecue facilities to prepare their own meals and enjoy them outdoors.

In Olhão, guests will find a great variety of restaurants serving traditional local cuisine which includes fish and seafood specialities.

The picturesque city of Tavira is 25 km away and Faro with its Old Town is a 20-minute drive.
