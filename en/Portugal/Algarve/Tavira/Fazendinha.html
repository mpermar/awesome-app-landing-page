Description

Set in the calm Sequa River valley, just 5 minutes’ driving from Tavira city, this 150-year old converted guest house offers simple, tasteful and individually styled rooms.

It features an outdoor swimming pool, a garden and barbecue facilities. All rooms have private terraces and en-suite bathrooms.

Air conditioning, under floor heating and WiFi access are also featured. Some have a seating area with a sofa.

Upon request, breakfast can be served either as a continental option or as a hearty breakfast with more products.

Guests can use the shared kitchen facilities to prepare meals, as well as the barbecue facilities.

Pego do Inferno Waterfall is less than 10 minutes’ driving, and guests will find Faro International Airport 43 km away.
