Description

Herdade Da Corte is spread over 2 separate hills, 15 km from Tavira. The main country house has a large garden with outdoor pool and sun terrace, while the house with junior suites is surrounded by nature.

Overlooking the mountains, the air-conditioned rooms and suites have rustic décor and some include a sloped, wooden ceiling.

Each private bathroom provides free toiletries. Junior suites and Family Suites have a separate seating area and are set 800 metres from the main property.

Breakfast is served daily, and the lounge bar playing jazz and blues offers tapas and.

There is an honesty bar, and several restaurants can be found within a 20-minute drive.

Children can enjoy the playground whilst adults relax with a massage. The Herdade Da Corte has bicycles for rent and the property has hiking trails, as well as a tennis court.

Free WiFi is available in the public areas. On-site private parking is free and car hire can be arranged upon request.
