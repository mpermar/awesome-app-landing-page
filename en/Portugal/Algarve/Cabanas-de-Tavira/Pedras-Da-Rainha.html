Description

This resort is located near Cabanas, a fishing village on the border of Ria Formosa Natural Reserve.

It offers 2 pools, both a tennis and padel tennis court, a bowling alley and massage services.

The air-conditioned studios and apartments at the Pedras Da Rainha have a terrace or balcony, a kitchenette and a spacious living area.

Pedras Da Rainha has a restaurant serving local and international dishes. There is also a supermarket and kiosk available on site.

Guests can relax at the outdoor swimming pool, which is surrounded by a lush garden.

The resort also has a games room with darts, a ping-pong table and a children’s playground.

One of Algarve's top golf courses is situated just 1 km from the Pedras da Rainha hotel.
