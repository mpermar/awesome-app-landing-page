Description

One of our top picks in Funchal.

Offering an outdoor pool and spa center, Castanheiro Boutique Hotel is set in Funchal, 1,650 feet from Marina do Funchal. The hotel has a year-round outdoor pool and hot tub, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

Each room is equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels. A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms. The rooms include a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find bath robes, slippers and free toiletries.

Castanheiro Boutique Hotel features a SPA with hydrotherapy pool, sauna, turkish bath,massage rooms and a relaxing area. You will also find a hairdresser's at the property.

The hotel also offers bike hire and car hire. CIFEC - Madeira Congress Center is 1.2 mi from Castanheiro Boutique Hotel, while Parque Ecologico do Funchal is 3.7 mi away. Madeira Airport is 8.1 mi from the property.

Experiences

06/04/2017

A Lovely Place in Funchal to stay for a week!

"We were there at the time of the Flower Festival and the hotel was perfectly located for viewing and visiting all the events. The children's Wall of Hope was at the square a few steps away. Our tickets to the parade gave us seats within a 10 minute walk of the hotel and the carpets of flowers were less than that. It was great for the flower festival.The breakfast was a complete buffet with lots of offerings. There was wonderful fruits, and the varieties were different every day. We ate in the restaurant for one dinner, but it is so centrally located that we had so many restaurants close at hand that we wanted to try.I had just had emergency surgery, my walking was a little limited and I could not leave Madeira for 7 days. The staff helped us to find things we could do close by or we could get a ride to. They were always helpful and friendly.Last of all it is in a lovely area with the Jesuit College directly across the street. All in all it is a lovely centrally located hotel with a good restaurant and lovely staff. It is a long distance from Atlanta, GA, but we would definitely go again."
