Description

Overlooking the Bay of Funchal, this well designed hotel provides luxury accommodation within Santa Caterina Park.

There is a spa with pools for relaxation and a casino and discotheque for entertainment.

Featuring private balconies, all rooms at the Pestana Casino Park Hotel & Casino come with seating areas and satellite LCD TVs.

Many guest rooms provide a panoramic view of the Atlantic Ocean. Pestana’s 6 dining venues include the Panoramic Restaurant with far-reaching views of the Bay and pool area.

The Aqua overlooks the hotel’s gardens and casino, while the sunset Restaurant offers a varied menu and a cocktail bar.

Guests can relax in the indoor or outdoor pools, exercise in the fitness centre or enjoy one of the quality treatments available from the Pestana Spa.

The hotel arranges diverse recreational activities including hiking and diving. Pestana Casino Park is just a 5-minute walk from the town centre and 30 minutes’ drive from Madeira Airport.
