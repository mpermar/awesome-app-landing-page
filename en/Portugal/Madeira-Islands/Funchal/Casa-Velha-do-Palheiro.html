Description

Dating back to 1804, Casa Velha do Palheiro consists of 3 buildings with all rooms individually decorated with chic and elegant furnishings. Free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs with a wide choice of international channels are available.

A variety of eating options include the Classical dining room with international haute cuisine, the more relaxed Palheiro Golf clubhouse restaurant with its panoramic views of Funchal bay, the tea house in the Palheiro gardens or healthy snacks at the Spa.

Guests can relax by the outdoor pool in the gardens or use the free facilities of the Spa at Casa Velha featuring a heated indoor pool as well as sauna and steam room. Highly qualified therapists offer an array of Pevonia SPA treatments for holistic relaxation.

The hotel organizes deep-sea fishing on its own motor yacht, Balancal, as well as nature experiences with whale and dolphin-watching. On site there is a tennis court, badminton, croquet as well as the property's own championship golf course, Palheiro Golf. Casa Velha do Palheiro is 4 miles from central Funchal and offers direct access to the award-winning Palheiro Gardens. Free private parking is available as well as a daily free shuttle service to the center of town.

Sao Goncalo is a great choice for travelers interested in hiking, walking and scenery.
