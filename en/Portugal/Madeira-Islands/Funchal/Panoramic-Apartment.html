Description

The apartment comes with 2 bedrooms, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a fully equipped kitchen that provides guests with a dishwasher, an oven, a washing machine, a microwave and a toaster. The apartment also has a bathroom with a hot tub.

The apartment has a terrace. If you would like to discover the area, hiking is possible nearby and Panoramic Apartment can arrange a bicycle rental service.

Gomes' Pools is one mile from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Cristiano Ronaldo Madeira International Airport, 10 miles from Panoramic Apartment.

Sao Martinho is a great choice for travelers interested in relaxation, nature and nature walks.
