Description

A luxurious 5-star design hotel sitting on Calheta Beach, between Madeira Mountains and the Atlantic Ocean, Savoy Saccharum Resort & Spa features a rooftop infinity pool and conference rooms.

Each room has the design of a local artist, based on the theme of the sugar cane.

Guests can also enjoy the comfort of a self-catering apartment. The Savoy Saccharum Resort & Spa features a buffet restaurant, an Ã  la carte restaurant and summer terraces, in addition to barbecue facilities.

Four bars complete the offer. Guests can spend their day on site, using the squash court, a playroom for children and the heated indoor pool.

For warmer days, 2 outdoor swimming pools are available with large sun terraces. Calheta Marina is 20 metres from the Savoy Saccharum Resort & Spa, and Funchal city centre is 34 km away.
