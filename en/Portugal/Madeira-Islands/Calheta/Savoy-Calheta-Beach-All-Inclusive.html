Description

One of our top picks in Calheta.

This property is 1 minute walk from the beach. Overlooking the Atlantic Ocean, this hotel is located on the sandy Calheta Beach. It features indoor and outdoor pools, a spa and a gym. Each room has a private balcony.

All rooms and apartments at Savoy Calheta Beach - All Inclusive offer mountain or sea views. Each is equipped with satellite TV, mini-bar, and a work desk.

Local specialties and international dishes are served at Calheta and Onda Azul Restaurants. Rabaçal Bar features live music, while the Pôr do Sol Bar offers cocktails and overlooks the ocean.

Savoy Calheta Beach features a spa with a Turkish bath and sauna. It also offers full-body massages, hot stone-massages and a range of therapies based on marine algae.

More active guests can go for a swim in the outdoor pool, which is surrounded by palm trees. Guests who wish to keep up with their fitness routine can work out with cardiovascular equipment at the gym.

Savoy Calheta Beach is a 25-minute drive from Funchal. An airport shuttle is available. A supermarket is available next to the hotel.
