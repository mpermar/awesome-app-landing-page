Description

This property is 1 minute walk from the beach. With a private stretch of sandy beach along the Porto Santo shore, this 5-star resort in Southern Madeira features 2 natural-style pool areas surrounded by 98 ft² of gardens.

A private balcony and wall-mounted flat-screen TVs are standard in Pestana Porto Santo Beach Resort & SPA - All Inclusive’s rooms. They are elegantly decorated in the blue and green shades of the ocean.

Pestana Porto Santo Beach Resort & SPA features 4 restaurants and 3 bars, offering a selection of Italian and Mediterranean cuisine. Views of the Atlantic Ocean can be enjoyed at the Sunset Bar.

The spa area includes a heated pool with water jets, wet and dry saunas and an aromatic steam bath. Guests can train in the gym, or play a game of tennis on the multi-purpose court.

Porto Santo Airport is about 2.5 mi away, while Madeira Island Airport is 34 mi of the Pestana Porto Santo.
