Description

Located on the idyllic island Madeira, Vila Bela is just 100 meters away from beach Praia da Alagoa.

Overlooking the Atlantic Ocean, this hotel has access to a communal outdoor pool adjacent to the Porto da Cruz promenade.

The air-conditioned rooms of Vila Bela offer a bathroom with bath, a flat-screen satellite TV and a balcony.

Some of the rooms feature sea view, while other rooms overlook the island’s mountains.

With views of the Atlantic Ocean, the restaurant of this hotel serves an a la carte menu of local cuisine.

A range of snacks and local drinks are offered in the hotel’s bar. Madeira International Airport is just 15 minutes away by airport shuttle and Machico is a 10-minute drive.

Golf club Santo da Serra Golf Club can be found within 13 km from hotel Vila Bela.

Praia da Alagoa is the best place to have a surf experience. Surfing lessons can be arranged at the Calhau Surf School, property of the Hotel Vila Bela.
