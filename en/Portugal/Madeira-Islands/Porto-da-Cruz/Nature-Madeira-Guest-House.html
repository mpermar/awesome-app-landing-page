Description

The homestay offers a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with free toiletries, a hair dryer and shower. There's also a kitchen in some of the units equipped with a dishwasher and a microwave.

Guests at Nature Madeira Guest House can enjoy a continental breakfast.

The accommodation has a playground.

A bicycle rental service is available at Nature Madeira Guest House, while cycling can be enjoyed nearby.

Funchal is 10 miles from the homestay. The nearest airport is Cristiano Ronaldo Madeira International Airport, 6 miles from Nature Madeira Guest House.
