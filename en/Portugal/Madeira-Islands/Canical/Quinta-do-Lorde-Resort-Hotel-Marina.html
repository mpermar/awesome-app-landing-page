Description

Located in Ponta de Sao Lourenco, Madeira Island, Quinta do Lorde Resort - Hotel - Marina is a 5-star resort offering luxurious accommodation and thoughtful services.

It features its own marina and a private beach area. Resembling a typical Madeiran village, units are spread through 5 buildings, connected by green areas and cobbled paths.

They all feature air conditioning, a flat-screen cable TV and a balcony with sea or resort views.

There are 2 restaurants and a snack-bar on site. Flôr de Sal restaurant combines local products with international cuisine, and the seasonal Lord’s Club offers a privileged view of the Atlantic Ocean.

For a drink, guests can chose between the cocktail, beach and pool bar. After exploring Funchal city, located a 30-minute drive, guests can return to their accommodation and enjoy a relaxing massage, a tennis match or a session at the gym.

Children are looked after at the kids club. Quinta do Lorde Resort - Hotel – Marina can arrange a shuttle service to Madeira International Airport, located 15 minutes’ driving from the resort.
