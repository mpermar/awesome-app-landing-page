Description

Guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. All rooms have a private bathroom with a bath, free toiletries and a hair dryer. All guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

A continental breakfast is available daily at Hotel Solar Bom Jesus.

Funchal is 7 miles from the accommodation, while São Vicente is 17 miles from the property. Cristiano Ronaldo Madeira International Airport is 1.2 miles away.
