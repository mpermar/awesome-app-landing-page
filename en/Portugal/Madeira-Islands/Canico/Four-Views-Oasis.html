Description

Rooms at the Four Views Oasis have modern wood furnishings and a sitting area with cable TV. They come with tea-and coffee-making facilities and a minibar. Some rooms offer panoramic sea views.

In the morning the Restaurant Aquamarina offers a buffet breakfast. The Oasis has a restaurant, a cocktail lounge with live entertainment, and a poolside bar that serves beverages and light snacks.

Guests can relax in the heated indoor pool or visit the fitness center. There is a playground for children and a reading room with a computer and free wired internet.

The Four Views Oasis is 6 miles from Funchal. The hotel has 24-hour front desk service and free on-site private parking. An airport shuttle service is available upon request.
