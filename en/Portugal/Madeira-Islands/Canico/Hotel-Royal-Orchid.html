Description

Overlooking the Atlantic, this beachfront hotel offers private access to the Ocean, 2 pools and a wellness centre.

Guests can benefit from a free shuttle service into Funchal 3 times a day. The rooms, studios and suites at Hotel Royal Orchid are modern in style and include an LCD TV and air conditioning.

Each accommodation has a kitchenette and a private bathroom with hairdryer. Royal Orchid’s restaurant provides lots of daylight and serves regional as well as international cuisine, including freshly caught fish and tender beef.

Madeira wine is available at the Aloé Bar. Besides the heated indoor pool and outdoor pool, guests can enjoy massages or visit the sauna and steam bath in the Nautilus Health Club.

Sun beds, umbrellas and showers are available at the sun terrace, which overlooks Caniço Bay.

Hotel Royal Orchid is just a 20 minutes’ drive away from Funchal, Palheiro Golf Course and the Santo da Serra Golf Course.

The hotel offers free parking as well as a car-rental and bike-rental service. All services and facilities from the neighbouring Hotel Roca Mar are available to the Royal Orchid guests during their stay.
