Description

This beach resort in Caniço, Madeira offers a gym, massage services and beauty treatments.

It has 4 restaurants, 3 pools and panoramic sea views. Funchal’s centre is a 20-minute drive away.

The rooms at the 4-star Galo Resort Hotel Galosol have a balcony with panoramic sea views.

They also have air conditioning, satellite TV and a private bathroom with toiletries.

All rooms have free WiFi access. Guests can enjoy a range of relaxation treatments at the spa, including stone massages, pedicures and manicures.

They can also attend aerobics or yoga classes and anti-stress programs. A diving centre is also available.

The Hotel Galosol also features a restaurant which serves local and international cuisine which can be enjoyed on the terrace overlooking the ocean.

The Galo Resort is a 20-minute drive from Madeira Airport and offers free parking onsite.
