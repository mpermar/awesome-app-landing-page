Description

Accommodations are elegantly decorated and includes tea-and-coffee making facilities, a mini-bar, and a balcony with sea views. Some have a sitting area with a sofa.

The 24-hour Aqua Natura Madeira has a panoramic restaurant on site serving local cuisine along with national and regional wines. Breakfast buffets and menu options are served for lunch and dinner.

Guests can use the hot tub and relaxing with the Atlantic Ocean views. Massage services and a gym are available. For the sports fans, the Aqua Natura features a diving center on site. The natural swimming pools are a few steps away.

Madeira International Airport is 37 miles away, and an airport shuttle is available upon request.
