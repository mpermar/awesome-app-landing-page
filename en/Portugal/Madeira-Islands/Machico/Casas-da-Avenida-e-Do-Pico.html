Description

One of our top picks in Machico.

This property is a 3-minute walk from the beach. Located in the town of Machico, Casas da Avenida e Do Pico are within walking distance from most attractions and the beach. They offer free WiFi in all areas and private parking on site.

Set on a quiet area the apartments include a bathroom each and a living room equipped with flat-screen cable TV, a dining area, sofas and a sound system.

Guests can prepare their own meals using the fully equipped kitchen available in each apartment. Alternatively guests can taste the local gastronomy by visiting the restaurants within 650 feet. In addition guests will find supermarkets and cafés at a few minutes’ walk.

The bus stop that takes guests to Funchal is a 5-minute walk away. After a 30-minute trip guests will reach Funchal and will be able to visit most attractions in the city including museums and galleries.

Guests can enjoy panoramic views of Machico in the Queimada viewpoint 1.9 mi away. Madeira International Airport is 3.1 mi away.
