Description

The sea view rooms at Hotel Da Vila feature all-white décor and natural wood accents. Each is equipped with cable TV, air conditioning and a mini-fridge.

Breakfast or evening cocktails can be enjoyed at Da Vila’s restaurant and bar. Bicycle and car rental can be arranged right at the hotel.

Guests have free use of the facilities at Hotel Da Vila’s sister resort, situated 650 feet away. The sister resort offers an sea-view outdoor swimming pool, a jacuzzi, steam bath, sauna, and spa.

Funchal Ecological Park is a 40 minute drive away. Madeira Airport is 18.5 miles from Hotel Da Vila.

A dream break for couples.

"The staff were amazingly welcoming and helpful at this hotel. When booking for a week they offer free airport shuttles. On arrival at the airport we were welcomed by the driver and then whisked in air conditioned comfort to the hotel in a Mercedes Mini-bus. It takes 30mins on excellent roads. The rooms are amazing - very chic and modern looking, and all of them have a glorious view out over the ocean. You can lie in bed listening to the waves. It is not a 'no children' hotel but there are no real facilities for them and some strict rules about peace and quiet around key areas like the pool. As such it was incredibly peaceful up there. The views up there are tremendous and the restaurants in both hotels are very good. The village itself has a great selection of bars and restaurants, including a couple of really cool little wine bars just behind the hotel itself. The hotel arranged free shuttles to the start/from the end of several good levada walks, which we took advantage of. All in all a tremendous place to stay and very good value too!"
