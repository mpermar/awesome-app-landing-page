Description

Perched on a mountain top with panoramic views, Delfim Douro offers luxury accommodations with private balconies overlooking the Douro River. It features a rooftop terrace, an outdoor pool, a restaurant and massage facilities.

The stylish, air-conditioned rooms and suites have a seating area with a flat-screen satellite TV, mini-bar and free WiFi. Each has a private bathroom with free toiletries. An in room breakfast is available.

A buffet breakfast is served daily. Guests can wine and dine in the restaurant serving a variety of national and international cuisines and wines with picturesque views of the surrounding vineyards.

Guests at Delfim Douro can sunbathe poolside, relax on the rooftop terrace or in the garden. The tour desk can organize day excursions, wine tasting and activities such as canoeing, cycling and boat tours in the Douro River. Free parking is available at a nearby location.

The hotel is 4.8 mi from Peso da Régua and 8.1 mi from Lamego. Vila Real Airdrome is a 30-minute drive away by car and the nearest airport is Porto International Airport, 65 mi from the property. Régua train station is 3.7 mi from Delfim Douro Hotel and offers train connection between Porto and Régua.
