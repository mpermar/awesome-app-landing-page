Description

Just a short drive from the city of Braga, this hotel is located in the Portuguese countryside and has been built and decorated in harmony with its surroundings.

Boasting local stone and beautiful wooden floors, Hotel Rural Alves presents guests with a welcome retreat from everyday life.

Enjoy a swim in the outdoor pool, make use of the games room or explore the region on rental bike.

Hotel Alves features only a small number of guest rooms, making it the ideal romantic getaway or family retreat.

Treat yourself to a relaxing bath in your modern bathroom or stay in contact with friends and family using free internet access.

After a day spent outdoors, the restaurant beckons with delicious regional specialities.
