Description

Surrounded by vineyard and forests, this charming, rustic guest house offers chic accommodations in a peaceful, rural setting. The nearest beaches are just 10 minutes away.

The Quinta da Bouça D'Arques is housed in converted farm buildings on a historic, 6-hectare estate. It blends original, traditional features with stylish modern design, creating a unique venue for your stay in northern Portugal.

Enjoy rides along the nearby scenic cycle paths and riverside walks. Or simply relax in the tranquil grounds of the Quinta da Bouça D'Arques with a dip in the outdoor swimming pool.

The nearest town to the hotel is Neves, a mile away, and the Spanish border is just a 30-minute drive away.
