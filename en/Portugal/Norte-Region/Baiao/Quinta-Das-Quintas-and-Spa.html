Description

Featuring a spacious terrace and a pool overlooking the Douro River, this guest house is surrounded by Baião’s countryside.

It offers air-conditioned accommodation and free parking on site. There is a Spa available.

A kitchenette with a dishwasher and a microwave is in Quinta’s apartments. Some rooms feature a traditional décor, while others are decorated with modern wooden furniture and bold colours.

A continental breakfast is served outside on the terrace daily. In the evening, guests can relax in the bar with a glass of homemade wine.

Das Quintãs also has a barbecue in the garden, and all-day room service is available.

Mirão train station is less than a 2-minute walk from Quinta Das Quintãs and links to the rest of Douro Valley.
