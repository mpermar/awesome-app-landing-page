Description

Situated in Vila Nova de Gaia, Novotel Porto Gaia offers rooms with free Wi-Fi and a flat-screen cable TV, just a 10-minute drive from Porto’s city centre.

Facilities include an outdoor pool. The air-conditioned rooms at Porto Gaia Novotel feature a modern interior.

All rooms have a minibar, a work desk, and tea and coffee making facilities. Guests can enjoy street food-inspired modern cuisine at the Côté Jardin Restaurant.

The bar serves a variety of drinks and refreshments, and during summertime by-the-pool barbecues are hosted.

Children can have fun at Novotel Porto Gaia’s video game area and the indoor and outdoor playgrounds.

The hotel also offers a 24-hour reception, laundry facilities, and car rental. Novotel Porto Gaia is close to many renowned wine cellars.

The Oporto Cathedral is 3,5 km from the hotel, and the National Museum Soares Dos Reis is 3 km away.
