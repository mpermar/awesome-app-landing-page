Description

Vale do Rio Hotel Rural offers modern accommodations surrounded by the rolling hills of Palmaz in the Aveiro Province. Its concept is inspired by the 4 elements, and facilities include a spa and a gym. Within a rural setting, this is an eco-friendly property just 3.1 mi from the center of Oliveira de Azeméis.

Fitted with designer furnishings, rooms at Hotel Rural Vale Do Rio all open out onto a private balcony. They are equipped with a flat-screen TV and a lounge with couch and work desk.

The on-site health club at Vale do Rio includes an indoor heated pool and Turkish steam bath. Massage treatments are available as well, and there is an on-site library with many Portuguese novels.

Guests can enjoy traditional specialties from Northern Portugal at the HC Restaurant. Meals can also be served on the outdoor terrace or in the privacy of your room.

This hotel produces electricity through a hydroelectric power station on the adjacent Caima River. Water and solar energy sources are also used by the hotel.

Located at 6.2 mi from São João da Madeira, the unit is 11 mi away from Santa Maria da Feira. Hotel Rural Vale Do Rio is 23 mi from Aveiro. Porto International Airport is a 45-minute drive from the hotel.
