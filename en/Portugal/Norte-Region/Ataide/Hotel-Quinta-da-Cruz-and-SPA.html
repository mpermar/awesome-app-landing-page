Description

Hotel Quinta da Cruz & SPA offers air-conditioned rooms with a private bathroom. It features an outdoor pool, sun terrace, a garden, and a bar.

The individually decorated en suite rooms are equipped with a TV, a desk and some rooms have a terrace.

Free wired internet is available. A continental breakfast buffet is served daily in the breakfast room or in the bedroom upon request.

Guests can exercise in the fitness centre, sunbathe poolside or relax on the terrace overlooking the garden and pool.

Hotel Quinta da Cruz has a spa & wellness centre and a billiards table. Indoors, there is a spacious lounge area with a piano and a TV.

Vila-Meã Railway station is a 10-minute walk away. The hotel's 24-hour front desk can help with suggestions on day trips, excursions and car hire.
