Description

One of our top picks in Melgaço.The Monte Prado Hotel & Spa is nestled between the River Minho and the Gerês and Galiz mountain ranges. It features air-conditioned guestrooms with furnished balconies and panoramic views of the countryside.

Prado Hotel rooms have parquet floors and contemporary designer furnishings. They come equipped with a TV and mini-bar. Some rooms include a separate living area with a sofa.

The hotel has an elegant restaurant with floor-to-ceiling windows that offers a buffet breakfast and serves international cuisine and traditional Portuguese dishes made from fresh locally-sourced ingredients.

Monte Prado guests can relax on a sofa by the fireplace, or enjoy a swim in the heated indoor pool. The spa offers massage and body treatments, while also featuring a hot tub.

Monte Prado Hotel & Spa is 1.9 mi from the Cinema Museum of Melgaço. It offers 24-hour front desk service and free public parking is possible on site.
