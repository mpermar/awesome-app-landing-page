Description

Located 3 Km from the centre of Vieira do Minho, Quinta da Mouta offers modern accommodations that blend with the natural landscape of the Peneda-Gerês Natural Park.

Free WiFi is available in all areas. Quinta da Mouta consists of 3 houses with 8 bedrooms.

The main house includes 3 bedrooms, a living room with TV and a dining room. The other rooms have private entrances and are distributed through the 2 remaining houses with granite walls.

Each room includes a private bathroom. Guests can enjoy a breakfast that includes regional bread, natural juices, diverse fruits and homemade cake.

In addition guests can visit the traditional Portuguese restaurants located in Vieira do Minho.

Quinta da Mouta features a seasonal outdoor pool with panoramic views of the mountains.

All rooms include air conditioning and daily maid service. Surrounded by garden this property is prepared to accommodate disabled guests and pets are allowed on request.

The Peneda-Gerês Natural Park is 15 Km away and the Ermal Dam is 6 Km away. The Caniçada Dam is 12 Km away and Porto International Airport is 89 Km away.
