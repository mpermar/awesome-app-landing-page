Description

Quinta Da Penela offers rooms with traditional wood furnishings and modern bathrooms with heated towel rack. Wooden shutters ensure a good night’s rest.

Free bicycles are available to venture into the hotel’s private grounds, hosting farm animals and archeological remains. Afterwards, guests can cool off in the pool while admiring the green hills.

A daily breakfast buffet is served in the Quinta’s rustic breakfast room or to the guest rooms. The lounge room with wooden ceilings is the perfect place to spend an evening by the open fireplace.

Quinta Da Penela is just one mile from the center of Vieira do Minho. It offers free private parking on site.
