Description

At the guesthouse, all rooms include a desk. Every room comes with air conditioning, and selected rooms include a balcony. The rooms have a closet.

A continental or buffet breakfast is served at the property.

Guests at Quinta do Bento will be able to enjoy activities in and around Vieira do Minho, like hiking.

Speaking Spanish, English and Portuguese at the 24-hour front desk, staff are always at hand to help.

Tahiti Waterfall is 6 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Francisco Sá Carneiro Airport, 38 miles from the property.
