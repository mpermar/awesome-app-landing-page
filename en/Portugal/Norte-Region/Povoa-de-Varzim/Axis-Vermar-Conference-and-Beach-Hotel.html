Description

The Axis Vermar is situated on the beach in Povoa de Varzim. It has an outdoor pool with a sun-lounger terrace and offers air-conditioned rooms with panoramic views of the North Atlantic Ocean.

Rooms at the Axis Vermar Conference & Beach Hotel have simple furnishings and a private bathroom with luxury amenities.

They come equipped with a TV and a minibar. The rooms with sea views feature a private balcony with sweeping views.

The Jardim Restaurant boasts sea views and serves international cuisine and traditional Portuguese dishes.

In the morning a buffet breakfast with fresh fruit and pastries is served. The hotel has a tennis court and a playground for children.

Nearby leisure activities include mountain biking and hiking. Spa treatments are available by appointment.

Axis Vermar Conference & Beach Hotel is 20 km from the airport and is 1. 5 km from the Casino of Povoa de Varzim.

The hotel offers 24-hour front desk service and free on-site private parking. Axis is taking part in an initiative of corporate social responsibility, integrating into its team people with intellectual disabilities.
