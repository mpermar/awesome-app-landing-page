Description

Situated in Viana do Castelo, 1. 4 km from Shipyards of Viana do Castelo, Hotel FeelViana boasts a seasonal outdoor pool and water sports facilities.

The hotel has a sun terrace and spa centre, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Free WiFi is featured throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and has a flat-screen TV. Certain units have a seating area for your convenience.

Certain rooms feature views of the sea or garden. All rooms come with a private bathroom.

For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property.

A selection of activities are offered in the area, such as windsurfing, cycling and hiking.

The hotel also offers bike hire. Tourism Office - Viana Welcome Center is 4 km from the property.
