Description

Set high on the Santa Luzia Hill, Viana do Castelo Inn overlooks the town of Viana do Castelo.

Rooms have panoramic views over the ocean or lush landscapes. Pousada de Viana do Castelo’s rooms are furnished with air conditioning, cable TV, and a minibar.

The grounds of Pousada de Viana do Castelo feature an outdoor swimming pool, tennis court and many terraces from which guests can admire the views.

Viana do Castelo's restaurant offers local and international gourmet cuisine.

Guests can also visit Viana do Castelo and its restaurants by using the Panoramic Lift of Santa Luzia.
