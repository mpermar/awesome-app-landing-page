Description

Elegantly inserted into the local Alto Minho landscape, Hotel Minho features a modern spa and wellness centre.

The property also includes indoor and outdoor swimming pools and a business centre.

Hotel Minho’s simply decorated air-conditioned rooms include a private bathroom, flat-screen cable TV, work desk.

The rooms also feature a balcony and views of the mountain, garden and pool area.

Breakfast is served in the dining room each morning. The hotel’s restaurant also prepares meals based upon the rich local cuisine.

The historical centre of Vila Nova de Cerveira is a 10-minute drive and has several restaurant options, surrounded by typical Minho homes from the 17th and 18th centuries.

The hotel’s state-of-the-art spa facilities include a Vichy Shower room, spoon bath, hot tub, treatment room, oriental treatment room, swimming pool, sauna and a Turkish bath.

Hotel Minho also includes a Business Centre, which operates in an autonomous floor.

The centre has a versatile conference room and a meeting room which boasts a view of the pool area.

The Wine Bar serves refreshing drinks and quality wines from the property’s vast wine cellar, including the local Vinho Verde.

The property features indoor and outdoor swimming pools. Guests can also take in a tennis match in the hotel’s outside court.

An airport shuttle service is available at an extra fee. The Porto International Airport is a 65-minute drive from the Hotel Minho.
