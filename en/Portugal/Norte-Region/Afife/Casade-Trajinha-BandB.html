Description

At the bed and breakfast, rooms include a closet. With a private bathroom equipped with free toiletries, rooms at Casade Trajinha B&B also feature free WiFi, while selected rooms come with a balcony. All guest rooms will provide guests with an oven.

A continental or buffet breakfast is served at the property.

Cycling and hiking are among the activities that guests of the accommodation can find nearby.

Braga is 27 miles from Casade Trajinha B&B, while Viana do Castelo is 6 miles away. The nearest airport is Francisco Sá Carneiro Airport, 39 miles from the bed and breakfast.
