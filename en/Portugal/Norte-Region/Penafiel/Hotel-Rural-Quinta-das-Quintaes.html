Description

At Hotel Rural Quinta das Quintães, the rooms are equipped with a closet.

A continental or buffet breakfast is served at the property.

Hotel Rural Quinta das Quintães has a terrace. Guests at Hotel Rural Quinta das Quintães will be able to enjoy activities in and around Penafiel, like cycling and hiking.

Guests can use the business center to work or head for a sightseeing trip organized by the tour desk.

Porto is 16 miles from Hotel Rural Quinta das Quintães. The nearest airport is Francisco Sa Carneiro Airport, 21 miles from the property.
