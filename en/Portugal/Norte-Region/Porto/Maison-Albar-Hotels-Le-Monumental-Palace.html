Description

Located a 3-minute walk from Paris Galleries Street, Maison Albar Hotels Le Monumental Palace offers 5-star accommodations in Porto and is located in a renovated historic building from 1923.

The property has 2 restaurants, a bar, indoor swimming pool and a Spa & Wellness Centre.

The hotel is close to several noted attractions, around 400 metres from City Market Bolhao and around 400 metres from Clerigos Tower.

Free WiFi is available. The property features rooms and suites, all equipped with a coffee machine and a private bathroom with a shower.

All units are equipped with a seating area. Buffet breakfast is served every morning at the breakfast room.

Le Monumental Café features fish and seafood options, while Le Monument is a gourmet restaurant.

There is also the Bar Americain, which also serves snacks. Maison Albar Hotels Le Monumental Palace offers a range of wellness facilities including a sauna and an indoor pool, hammam and a fitness centre.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can provide tips on the area. Le Monumental features a library lounge and a comfortable TV room.

Popular points of interest near Maison Albar Hotels Le Monumental Palace include Oporto Coliseum, Ferreira Borges Market and Soares dos Reis National Museum.

The nearest airport is Francisco Sá Carneiro Airport, 11 km from Maison Albar Hotels Le Monumental Palace.
