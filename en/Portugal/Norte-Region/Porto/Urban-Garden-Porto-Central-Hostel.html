Description

Urban Garden Porto Central Hostel is located in the Santo Ildefonso district, in Porto, 300 metres from City Market Bolhao and 500 metres from Oporto Coliseum.

The property features a 200 m2 garden and also has traditional 19th century stone walls Every room includes a private bathroom equipped with a shower.

Towels, toiletries and a hairdryer are provided. Urban Garden Porto Central Hostel features free WiFi throughout the property.

Breakfast is served every morning and free tea/coffee is available all day. The hostel also offers car hire.
