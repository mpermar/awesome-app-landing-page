Description

Guest rooms comes with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a coffee machine, a shower, free toiletries and a desk. With a private bathroom, some accommodations at the hotel also have a balcony. The rooms feature a closet.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at Torel Avantgarde.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool.

When guests need guidance on where to visit, the reception will be happy to provide advice.

Cristal Palace Gardens is a 4-minute walk from Torel Avantgarde. Francisco Sá Carneiro Airport is 7 miles away.

União de Freguesias do Centro is a great choice for travelers interested in architecture, city walks and wine.
