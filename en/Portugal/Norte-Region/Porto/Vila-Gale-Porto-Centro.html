Description

The spacious rooms and suites at Vila Gale Porto are air-conditioned and feature bright décor. All have a flat-screen cable TV, minibar and a private bathroom, including a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Vila Gale Porto’s Paris-Texas Restaurant serves Portuguese cuisine and pasta. Room service is available 24/7, for an extra fee. Guests can enjoy wine from the hotel’s vineyard Casa de Santa Vitória, at one of the 2 bars. A billiards room is also available on site.

Vila Gale’s Health Club features an indoor pool, sauna, hot tub, turkish bath, tanning bed, vichy shower, massage treatments and fitness center with views of Porto city. Wi-Fi is available in all areas of the hotel.

Central Aliados Avenue is just under 1 milesss away, and the Clérigos Tower just under 1 mile away. Dom Luis I Bridge is just under 1 mile away and is next to Ribeira and the Port Wine Cellars. Galerias de Paris, Porto's main bar area, are a 10-minute drive from the property.

Campanhã Train Station is just over half a mile away. The hotel is located 9 miles from Francisco Sá Carneiro Airport, and has direct access to the Porto Metro system.

Bonfim is a great choice for travelers interested in monuments, city trips and sightseeing.
