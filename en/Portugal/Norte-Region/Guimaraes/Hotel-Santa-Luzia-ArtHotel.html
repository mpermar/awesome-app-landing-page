Description

Located 1,650 feet from Guimarães historic city center, considered World Heritage Site since 2001, Santa Luzia ArtHotel welcomes guests with a restaurant and bar. The hotel has a seasonal outdoor pool and sun terrace, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Some rooms have a seating area to relax in after a busy day. All rooms are equipped with a private bathroom. Santa Luzia ArtHotel features free WiFi throughout the property. A flat-screen TV is provided.

Santa Luzia Spa features an indoor pool with water jets, hot tub, sauna, Turkish bath, and relaxation room. The spa also includes 2 treatment rooms, Teatro and Cinema, and a state-of-the-art gym.

Church of S. Miguel do Castelo is 1,650 feet from Santa Luzia ArtHotel, while Dom Afonso Henriques Stadium is 1,300 feet from the property. The nearest airport is Porto Airport, 24 mi from Santa Luzia ArtHotel.
