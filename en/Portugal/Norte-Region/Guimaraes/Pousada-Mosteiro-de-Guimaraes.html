Description

Situated in a 12th century Augustin Convent, the Pousada Guimaraes Monastery overlooks Guimarães.

Rooms are equipped with modern amenities including air conditioning and cable TV.

Each of the individually decorated rooms are located in what used to be cloisters.

All rooms of Pousada Guimaraes Monastery offer an en suite bathroom. The Pousada Guimaraes Monastery is within walking distance of the center of Guimaraes, just 2 km from Guimaraes' shopping district.

Guests can enjoy Pousada Mosteiro de Guimaraes's outdoor pool or a stroll around the landscaped gardens.

The Pousada offers free private parking on site. Guimarães Sports Pavilion is 3 km away.
