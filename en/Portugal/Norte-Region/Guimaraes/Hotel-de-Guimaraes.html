Description

Located in the heart of historical Guimarães, Hotel de Guimarães features a spacious indoor pool and offers rooms with cable TV.

Palace of the Dukes of Bragança, the city walls and the Salado Monument are within a 15-minute walk.

The air-conditioned accommodation at Hotel de Guimarães feature contemporary furnishings and a LCD TV.

They come equipped with a safety deposit box and a minibar. The restaurant D. Maria is decorated in warm colours and offers panoramic city views.

It serves a buffet breakfast and seasonal Mediterranean cuisine. The Piano Bar serves a selection of beers, wines and refreshments.

Guests of the Hotel de Guimarães can relax in the sauna, and enjoy a massage or body treatment.

The hotel also a boasts a large fitness centre. Hotel de Guimarães offers free on-site public parking.
