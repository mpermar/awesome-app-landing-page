Description

Surrounded by mountains and lakes, Águas Do Gerês Hotel offers a seasonal outdoor pool in the lush Peneda-Gerês National Park.

Rooms have air conditioning and a private bathroom. The hotel's restaurant offers regional dishes in the dining room.

International and traditional Portuguese drinks are served at the bar. Águas Do Gerês' wellness centre is located in front of the hotel and features floor-to-ceiling windows.

It offers a range of health and beauty treatments. Guests can enjoy a professional massage after a swim or relax in the hot tub.
