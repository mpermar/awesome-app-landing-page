Description

Located in Caminha and just 1. 5 km from the beach, Design & Wine Hotel combines contemporary and historical 18th-century design.

It offers traditional Portuguese wines and features a restaurant and a spa. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout.

Design & Wine Hotel has modern rooms with views of Minho River and Santa Tecla Mountain.

The high-tech building revolves by 35 degrees twice a day providing guests with the best views possible of the river.

Each of the air-conditioned rooms has a flat-screen cable TV, games console, an iPad, and a minibar, available upon request and at an extra fee.

A breakfast buffet is served daily, while the hotel’s restaurant offers local dishes prepared with regional products.

Enoteca’s space is ideal for tasting the best wines, as well as traditional snacks.

Guests can choose from a variety of activities in the surrounding area, including cycling, fishing, diving, and hiking.

Matriz Church and the Clock Tower are within walking distance from the property. The hotel offers a reading room, and a communal lounge area.

The spa features a sauna, Turkish bath, gym, and a hot tub. Due to the hotel's ecological nature, the indoor pool is heated by solar panels and therefore its temperature might vary.

Vigo-Peinador Airport is 43 km away and Oporto International Airport is 90 Km away.
