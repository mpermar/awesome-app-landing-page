Description

Located in the famous Douro Valley, a region known for its rural charm and exquisite wines, this hotel offers stunning views of the surrounding hills, the vineyards, and the Pinhão River.

Facilities include an outdoor pool. The air-conditioned rooms at Hotel Rural da Quinta do Silval are traditionally decorated.

All rooms have free Wi-Fi, satellite TV, a work desk, and an en suite bathroom. Quinta do Silval’s organizes wine tasting and guided visits to the vineyards, where it is possible to have a close up to the Douro and Port wines.

Children can have fun at the playground. At the restaurant, guests can enjoy authentic, home-made Portuguese meals made with local produce.

The wine shop offers a wide selection of the best wines of the region. Hotel Rural da Quinta do Silval is only 6 km from the shops, restaurants and bars of Pinhão.
