Description

Located in the heart of the historic center of Santa Maria da Feira, Plaza Hostel offers accommodation 700 meters from the Castle of Santa Maria da Feira.

Free WiFi access is provided throughout the property. The property offers air-conditioned dormitories and double rooms, some boasting garden or city views.

The double rooms include a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and an en suite bathroom fitted with a hairdryer.

Linen are included. Guests have access to a shared kitchen, where they can prepare their meals.

There is also an events room at the hostel and catering service available. Electric bikes can be rented at the property.

Europarque Congress Centre is 5 km from the property, while São Jorge Hot Springs are 9.

7 km away. Porto is 33 km from Hostel da Praça and the nearest airport is Porto International Airport, a 35-minute drive away.
