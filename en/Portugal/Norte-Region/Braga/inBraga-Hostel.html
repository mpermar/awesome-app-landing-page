Description

Set in a renovated historical building in the center of Braga, inBraga Hostel is located within the Santiago de Compostela pilgrimage route. The hostel is just a 2-minute walk from the Braga Train Station and 1,000 feet from the Braga Sé Cathedral.

The unit offers accommodations in dormitories and has free WiFi in all areas. Each dormitory includes access to shared bathroom facilities and comes equipped with bunk beds, heating and lockers. Featuring views for the historic streets of Braga, guests have linen and towels provided.

Featuring a 24-hour front desk, the inBraga Hostel offers a shared kitchen where guests can prepare their own meals. Barbecue facilities are available as well. Alternatively, guests can visit the various dining options available within walking range and try the local gastronomy.

The main bus station is a 5-minute walk away and has connections with the Porto Airport. The hostel is 0.7 mi from Braga Municipal Stadium and 1,950 feet from BragaShopping. Porto Airport is 25 mi away.
