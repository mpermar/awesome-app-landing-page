Description

Guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Each room includes a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hair dryer. All rooms at Vila Gale Collection Braga include air conditioning and a closet.

The accommodation offers a buffet or American breakfast.

At Vila Gale Collection Braga guests are welcome to use an indoor swimming pool.

University of Minho - Braga Campus is 1.6 miles from the hotel, while Biscainhos Museum is 1,500 feet from the property. The nearest airport is Francisco Sá Carneiro Airport, 25 miles from Vila Gale Collection Braga.
