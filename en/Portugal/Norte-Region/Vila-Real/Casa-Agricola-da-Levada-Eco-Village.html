Description

Located next to the Corgo River, this large eco-friendly and family-run B&B in the Douro Region comprises 6 stone cottages and 3 guest rooms in the main house.

The green gardens feature an outdoor pool and plenty of sun terraces. Decorated in either a historic, romantic or modern style, the accommodations at Casa Agricola da Levada come with heating and a private bathroom.

The studios and apartments also include cooking facilities. A self-service washing machine is available in the common area.

Breakfast is made of the property's biological products and local producers. Guests can relax by the outdoor pool and use the available BBQ facilities for an afternoon lunch or summer evening.

Free private parking is provided. The property is located 3 km from the centre of Vila Real and a 15-minute drive from the heart of the Douro Valley.
