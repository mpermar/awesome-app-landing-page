Description

At the bed and breakfast, the rooms have a desk and a flat-screen TV. With a private bathroom equipped with a shower and free toiletries, rooms at Borralha Guest House also feature a garden view. At the accommodation, all rooms include a seating area.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property.

Borralha Guest House has an outdoor pool. Guests at the bed and breakfast will be able to enjoy activities in and around Vila Real, like cycling.

Francisco Sá Carneiro Airport is 49 miles away.
