Description

S. Martinho Farmhouse is a typical, rustic house of the Tras os Montes countryside.

It offers free Wi-fi, a garden pool, and is 200 metres from Mateus Palace. Built with 17th century granite, the inn features a garden with an outdoor pool and barbecue area.

Guests can select from rooms and apartments, two of which face the pool and a barbecue area.

All units include traditional furnishing, satellite TV, and tea and coffee making facilities.

There is a central living room with a fireplace, a library, a dining room for all guests.

For drinks, Casa Da Quinta offers a wine bar where Port wine and other drinks are served.
