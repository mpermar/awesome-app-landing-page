Description

Celorico Palace Hotel & Spa is a 4-star hotel located in Celorico de Basto, 25 km from Guimaraes.

It features an outdoor and indoor pool, a spa, gym and a restaurant on site. The elegant rooms are decorated in soft shades and include a mini-bar, air conditioning, and a flat-screen cable TV.

The modern bathrooms have a walk-in shower and free toiletries. A breakfast buffet is available each morning and includes fruit, fresh and crispy bread.

Varanda das Camélias restaurant serves regional delicacies and the best wines from Basto.

Guests can enjoy a drink from the bar on one of the sun loungers surrounding the swimming pool.

After a workout at the gym, guest can relax in the spa with a Hamman, sauna, Jacuzzi and indoor pool.

Take one of the rental bicycles and explore the well-known Ecopista do Tâmega Eco-track, located a few metres from Celorico Palace Hotel & Spa.

Peneda Geres National Park is 40 km from the hotel, and Porto International Airport is 58 km away.
