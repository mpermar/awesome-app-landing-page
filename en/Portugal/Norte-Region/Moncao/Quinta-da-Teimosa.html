Description

Offering an outdoor swimming pool and a tennis court, the scenic Quinta da Teimosa is located 4.

5 km from Monção. The Alvarinho wine-producing property offers local jams to guests, along with a wine tasting session.

Each modern apartment features plenty of natural light and has a living area with sofas and a flat-screen satellite TV.

The comfortable apartments have contemporary furnishings and feature exposed wooden beams.

The whole house features areas with exposed stone walls. Free WiFi access is available.

Breakfast is served each morning in each of the apartments. Guests are welcome to cook their own meals in the kitchenette, enjoying them in the dining area.

The Monção centre is 5 km away and features various regional restaurants. The unit has an ample lawn, where guests may enjoy sporting activities, such as tennis.

An array of activities can be enjoyed in the surroundings, including hiking or cycling with the free bikes provided at the property.

After a refreshing dip in the pool, guests may lounge in the lawn area and enjoy the sun, as well as the tranquil surroundings.

The Minho riverside area is just 5. 5 km away. Valença do Minho is 20 km away, while Caminha is within 47 km.
