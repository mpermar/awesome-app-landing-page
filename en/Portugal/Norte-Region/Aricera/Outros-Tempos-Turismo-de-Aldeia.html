Description

Outros Tempos consists of 4 granite rural houses from the 17th and 18th centuries which are located in the Douro Valley, village of Arícera.

It features a swimming pool and free WiFi access. Accommodation at Outros Tempos is charming and fitted with antique furnishings.

Rooms combine authentic rural décor with modern amenities. They include air conditioning, satellite TV and en suite bathrooms with slippers and bathrobes.

Guests have the possibility to book rooms or independent self-catering houses. The breakfast is included in the rate and features local products like bread, ham, jams, homemade cakes and cheese.

Meals can be arranged upon request and include appetizers, traditional desserts, regional meats, codfish and some of the best wines of the region.

This unique experience is complete with poetry and popular songs during the dinner.

The programs for groups and families which the friendly staff at Outros Tempos can organize include boat and historic train tours through the Douro River, various rural experiences like grape harvesting, cherry and apple picking, hiking, road journeys through viewpoints, wine-growing villages and wine estates where guests can taste their produce.

Providing free public and private parking, Outros Tempos also offers billiards, free mountain bikes and a living room with a fireplace.

The Régua train station is 19 km away and Porto International Airport is 118 km away.
