Description

Quinta da Mata is a Manor house that is rurally located, just 3 km from the town of Chaves.

It is surrounded by the Brunheiro Mountains. Free internet is available. The rustic rooms are well equipped with an en-suite bathroom and TV.

Guests can relax by the outdoor swimming pool or use the tennis courts. To further unwind, you can get a full massage or use Quinta da Mata’s sauna.

Every morning a breakfast is served in the restaurant with views across the valley.

The restaurant also serves various homemade dishes and local wines. Free parking is available.
