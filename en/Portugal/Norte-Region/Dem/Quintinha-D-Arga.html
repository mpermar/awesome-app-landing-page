Description

Maintaining a rustic, yet elegant feel, the Quintinha D`Arga is a 25-minute drive from Ponte de Lima and a 15-minute drive from the Spanish border.

It features an outdoor swimming pool and horse riding is possible, at an extra cost.

The double rooms feature a private bathroom and views over the pool, garden and mountain area.

WiFi access is available in bottom floor rooms and terrace. A rich and varied continental breakfast is included in the rate and served each morning in the dining area.

The property features an à la carte restaurant, where guests can enjoy traditionally-cooked Portuguese meals or international dishes.

Meals can be taken indoor on in the outdoor dining area, which overlooks the stables.

A snack bar is also available, for lighter meals and snacks. Free bicycles are available for guests wishing to leisurely explore the region.

The Quintinha features an on site bar, where guests can get refreshing drinks and cocktails to enjoy either in the communal lounge or in the pool area.

The garden-surrounded pool also provides a relaxing setting for guests to enjoy. The historical town of Viana do Castelo is a 20-minute drive away.

Valencia is a 30-minute drive away and Vila Nova de Cerveira is a 15-minute drive away.

The beaches of Vila Praia de Âncora and Moledo are 8 km away. Just at 10 km away is the medieval town of Caminha.
