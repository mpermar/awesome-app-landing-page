Description

Each air-conditioned accommodations comes with a private bathroom, a living area and a flat-screen TV. Some feature an equipped kitchenette. A balcony with garden and mountain views are also included.

Guests may enjoy the á la carte menu of the Pausa Restaurant which can deliver meals to the accommodations upon request. Within a 5-minute drive guests will find additional restaurants and supermarkets.

The Spa offers a variety of relaxing treatments and massages. It also includes an indoor swimming pool with jets, a sauna, a Turkish bath, halotherapy, a cardio area and a tennis court. Guests can enjoy a billiards match at the Lounge Bar, while children play with the game console.

Vigo is 24 mi from Nature's Pleasure Resort & Spa, while Braga is 30 mi from the property. Francisco Sa Carneiro Airport is 46 mi away.
