Description

Offering a biological outdoor pool, Parque Biológico de Vinhais is located in Vinhais, within Montesinho Natural Park. Free WiFi access is available in the public areas of this camping. This pet-friendly property offers free parking on site.

The accommodations will provide guests with a flat-screen TV and a balcony. Complete with a microwave, the dining area also has a refrigerator. Featuring a shower, the private bathrooms also come with towels. You can enjoy a mountain view and a garden view from all the rooms.

At Parque Biológico de Vinhais you will find a garden, barbecue facilities and a terrace. Other facilities offered at the property include entertainment staff, a ticket service and a tour desk. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including horse riding, cycling and hiking.

Inside the property guests will find an equestrian center, an interpretation center of indigenous breeds and a mycological center. Bragança is 20 mi away, and Porto International Airport is 133 mi away.
