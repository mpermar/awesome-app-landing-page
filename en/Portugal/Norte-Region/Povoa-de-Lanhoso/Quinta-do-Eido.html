Description

At the guesthouse, every room includes a balcony. With a private bathroom, rooms at Quinta do Eido also feature a mountain view. At the accommodation each room is equipped with a desk and a flat-screen TV.

Guests at Quinta do Eido will be able to enjoy activities in and around Póvoa de Lanhoso, like hiking.

Braga is 9 miles from the guesthouse, while Guimarães is 11 miles away. The nearest airport is Francisco Sá Carneiro Airport, 33 miles from Quinta do Eido.
