Description

The rooms at Hotel Rural de Charme Maria da Fonte are decorated with warm colors, wooden furniture, and parquet floors. All rooms have a private bathroom.

Guests can get a horse-drawn carriage ride at the horseback riding center. Maria da Fonte also offers a multi-purpose sports field, a game room, and a tennis court.

For total relaxation, guests can use the hotel’s spa facilities, including an indoor pool, a gym, a sauna, and a Turkish bath.

The restaurant offers local Portuguese dishes as well as international cuisine, made with organic produce from the hotel’s garden. The traditional wine cellar offers fine regional wines.

Free parking is available on site. Gerês National Park is 9 miles from Hotel Rural de Charme Maria da Fonte, and Braga is 12 miles away.
