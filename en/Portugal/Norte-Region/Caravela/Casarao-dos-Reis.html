Description

Casarão dos Reis is located in the village of Caravela, 15 km from Bragança. Free WiFi access is available in this farm stay which includes farm animals and traditional activities.

Each accommodation will provide guests with a TV and air conditioning. Featuring a shower, the private bathrooms also come with a bath or a shower.

Guests can enjoy a garden view from the rooms. There is a shared kitchen available where guests can prepare their meals.

An additional private kitchen is available for a surcharge. Both kitchens are fully equipped and include a flat-screen TV and a large dining area.

Guests are welcome to pick vegetables from the on-site garden and use them to cook their meals for free.

Guests have at their disposal a shared lounge and on-site there is a smokehouse where guests can taste and purchase regional products.

Each morning a continental breakfast is served and is included in the rate. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling and hiking.
