Description

Offering an outdoor pool and an à la carte restaurant, Casa Grande do Seixo is located 9 km from Vidago.

Free WiFi access is available. Featuring a traditional architecture with granite walls and exposed wooden beams, Casa Grande do Seixo offers heated rooms with a flat-screen TV and access to a private bathroom with a shower and a bidet.

Guests can enjoy a mountain view and a pool view from all the rooms. At Casa Grande do Seixo you will find buffet restaurant.

Other facilities offered include a shared lounge. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including hiking.

The property offers free parking. Casa Grande do Seixo features in its centre a chapel with Baroque Architecture elements.

In addition guests will find extensive vineyards in the property which offer a high quality white made of the finest selection of grapes.

The property is 20 km from Pedras Salgadas and 11 km from Golf Course Vidago Palace.

The City of Chaves is a 15-minute drive away. Porto International Airport is 143 km away.
