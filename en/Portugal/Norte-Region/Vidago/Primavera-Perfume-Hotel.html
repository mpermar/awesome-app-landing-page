Description

The rooms at Primavera Perfume are modern and have hand-painted walls and hardwood floors. They include a flat-screen satellite TV and a private bathroom.

Therapeutic massages can be scheduled at the spa. Guests can also enjoy the heated indoor pool or swim in the outdoor pool, which is surrounded by sun-lounge chairs.

The Perfume Hotel is just a 5-minute drive from the center of Vidago and 10 miles from Chaves. Porto International Airport is 1.5-hour drive from the hotel, and a shuttle is available upon request.
