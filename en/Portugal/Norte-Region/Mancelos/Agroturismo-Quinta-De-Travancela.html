Description

All units are air-conditioned and some of them feature a seating area with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen with a dining area. Some units have a terrace and/or patio.

The resort village has a seasonal outdoor pool.

There's a a grill at this property and guests can go cycling nearby.

Porto is 24 miles from Agroturismo Quinta De Travancela, while Braga is 24 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Francisco Sá Carneiro Airport, 26 miles from the property.
