Description

All rooms offered by the Hotel Meira are elegantly furnished and feature hardwood floors. Each comes with a safe and satellite TV.

Treat yourself to breakfast in your room or enjoy the rich breakfast buffet. Meira’s restaurant will provide you with the finest Portuguese cuisine prepared from fresh, local produce.

Guests can spend the day exploring the region or simply soak up the sun on the hotel’s terrace. Hotel Meira’s swimming pool offers perfect refreshment on hot summer days.

The Meira Hotel is located only 650 feet from Praia das Crianças and 8 miles from Viana do Castelo. Porto city center can be found at the distance of 50 miles.
