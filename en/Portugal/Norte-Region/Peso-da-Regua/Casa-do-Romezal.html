Description

Casa do Romezal is a characteristic Douro Valley house, surrounded by vineyards and providing stunning views over the surroundings. The rustic home was renovated and features a west-facing outdoor swimming pool.

Located in Peso da Régua, the unit features comfortable rooms, which blend contemporary details and furnishings, with rustic stone walls. Each air-conditioned room includes a flat-screen satellite TV, free WiFi, a desk and a window view to the surrounding landscape. The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries.

A daily breakfast is served in the Romezal, which can be taken on the terrace facing the pool and features fresh local products. If guests wish to try out the local flavors, then various restaurants are available within 2.5 mi.

The house’s shared lounge area has a hearth, sofas, a flat-screen satellite TV, board games, vintage vinyl records and player, as well as a variety of books in a library. Guests may also enjoy a refreshing dip in the swimming pool, which has a terrace with views over the valley.

An array of activities can be enjoyed in the surroundings, including hiking. The Peso da Régua center is 4.6 mi from the house, as is the Douro Museum. The bank of the Douro River is within 3.1 mi. Tanha Train Station is just 2 mi away. Porto International Airport is a 90-minute drive from the Casa do Romezal.
