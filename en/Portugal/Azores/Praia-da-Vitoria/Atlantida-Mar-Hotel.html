Description

Atlantida Mar is a contemporary hotel featuring designer guestrooms with panoramic views.

It is located on Terceira Island, with direct access to the Atlantic Ocean Promenade, and offers an outdoor swimming pool.

Rooms have been carefully designed and are fitted with minimal furnishings. They all open out onto a balcony, include a seating area with LCD cable TVs and some have hi-tech cooking facilities.

Every morning the Mar Hotel serves a buffet breakfast. Guests can relax in the sauna or plunge in the hot tub, or even work out in the gym.

Situated in Praia da Vitoria, Atlantida Mar Hotel is less than 4 miles from Lajes International Airport.
