Description

Offering 123 rooms with stunning views of the sea, Azor Hotel is a vibrant and cosmopolitan hotel with an outdoor swimming pool located in Ponta Delgada, São Miguel Island of the Azores Archipelago.

Each modern room is equipped with air conditioning, soundproofing, a minibar and a flat-screen cable TV.

Private bathrooms come with a bath or shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

Guest may enjoy a revitalising breakfast every morning and the À Terra restaurant serves a varied à la carte menu with typical delicacies.

In the evening guests may relax at the on-site bar. The Hotel features Spa and Wellness facilities as well as a conference.

Taking advantage of the natural surroundings of the São Miguel Island, a great variety of outdoor adventures and local experiences may be arranged.

Within a 15-minute walk, guests will find landmarks like São Brás Fort and São Sebastião Mother Church.
