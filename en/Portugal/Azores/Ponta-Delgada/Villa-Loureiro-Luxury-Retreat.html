Description

One of our top picks in Ponta Delgada.

This property is a 12-minute walk from the beach. Located a 5-minute drive from Ponta Delgada old town, the modern Villa Loureiro-Luxury Retreat offers an outdoor pool. The property is 17 mi from Furnas and free private parking is offered. Free WiFi is featured throughout the property.

The villa is composed of 5 bedrooms and 4 bathrooms. Some bathrooms are fitted with a hot tub. The kitchen has a dishwasher and an oven. A flat-screen TV and DVD player, as well as an iPod docking station and a CD player are available.

Bike hire is available at the property and the area is popular for golfing.
