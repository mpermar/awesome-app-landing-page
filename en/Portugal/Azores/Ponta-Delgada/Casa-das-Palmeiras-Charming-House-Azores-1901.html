Description

Set in Ponta Delgada, within a 5-minute walk of Portas da Cidade and 1,300 feet of Santuario Nossa Senhora da Esperanca, Casa das Palmeiras Charming House - Azores 1901 offers free WiFi.

All rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV with cable channels. The property features a coffee and tea station.

Accessible via stairs only, rooms have a private bathroom, while some rooms have a balcony city view.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property.
