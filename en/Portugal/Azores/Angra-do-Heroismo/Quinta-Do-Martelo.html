Description

Located in the Terceira Island, Azores, Quinta do Martelo is 4.3 mi from Angra do Heroísmo and offers an outdoor pool surrounded by a lawn and free WiFi in all areas.

Quinta do Martelo offers houses and rooms to its guests. The houses are completely renovated keeping their main characteristics from the first settlers. They include private bathrooms and fully equipped kitchens.

Quinta do Martelo includes an á la carte restaurant and offers a breakfast with biological ingredients and regional products like honey, fruits, bread and cheese.

Lajes International Airport is located 16 mi away and the parish of Biscoitos with its natural pools and protected wine production zones are a 27-minute drive. Praia da Vitória is 15 mi away and the Ilha Terceira Golf Club is at a 23-minute drive.
