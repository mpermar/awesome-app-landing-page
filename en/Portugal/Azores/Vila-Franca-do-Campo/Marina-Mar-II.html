Description

One of our top picks in Vila Franca do Campo.

This property is 2 minutes walk from the beach. Located in a new complex, Marina Mar II provides modern accommodations with access to an outdoor swimming pool. The unit is in So Miguel Island and is 500 feet from Vinha da Areia Beach.

The apartments and the holiday home all feature an equipped kitchen and a private bathroom. The living room area has sofas and a cable TV. All units are located near the pool.

Local restaurants are available within 500 feet from the property. Guests of the Marina Mar II may also choose to prepare their own meals in the fully-equipped kitchen at their disposal.

For golfing enthusiasts, there are 2 nearby golf courses, one at 6.2 mi and another at a 19 mi distance. The Ponta Delgada center is 16 mi away.

The nearest bus station is 650 feet away. Ponta Delgada Airport is a 28-minute drive from Marina Mar II.
