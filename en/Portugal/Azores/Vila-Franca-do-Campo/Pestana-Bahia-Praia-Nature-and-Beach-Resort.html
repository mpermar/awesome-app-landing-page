Description

This property is 2 minutes walk from the beach. Featuring direct access to Baia de Água d’Alto Beach, this luxurious resort is located on the island of São Miguel and overlooks the Atlantic Ocean. Leisure facilities include a large fresh-water outdoor pool.

Rooms at the Pestana Bahia Praia Nature & Beach Resort are spacious and have modern furnishings. They come equipped with a mini-bar and cable TV. Some suites include a seating area and a furnished balcony with panoramic sea views.

Guests can choose between 2 restaurants that serve international cuisine and traditional Azorean dishes. There is also a bar near the pool that offers live music and entertainment.

The resort has 2 tennis courts and a large garden with a small 17th-century chapel. There is a games room with billiards and table tennis. Nearby leisure activities include golfing, hiking and cycling.

Pestana Bahia Praia Nature & Beach Resort is a 10-minute drive from the town of Vila Franca do Campo. Ponta Delgada Airport is 12 mi away. The hotel offers 24-hour front desk service and free on-site private parking.
