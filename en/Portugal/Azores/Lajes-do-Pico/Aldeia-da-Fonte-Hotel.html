Description

Located on Pico Island in the Azores, this eco-sustainable resort looks like a village of stone houses and local plants. Set between the Atlantic Ocean and the Pico Mountain, it offers a whale-watching tower, yoga sessions, a Tai Chi Garden, direct ocean access, a sea lounge and an Atlantic Swimming Pool.

The famous Fonte Cuisine restaurant serves vegetarian and regional dishes. It is well-known for its Chinese food, its outdoor dining terrace and its dishes served on ceramic replicas of whaling boats.

The hotel features a fitness area with large windows opening onto the garden. Guests can relax in the free infrared sauna. The nearby coastline offers sweeping Ocean views and is ideal for a relaxing stroll.

All units at Aldeia da Fonte are decorated in wooden furnishings, a flat-screen TV and mini-bar.All suites and studios have a bar area. All suites feature a fireplace while all the guest rooms include free WiFi access.

Lajes do Pico is a 5-minute drive away. Pico Airport is just a 40-minute drive away.
