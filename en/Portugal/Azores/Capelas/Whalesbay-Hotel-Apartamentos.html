Description

Whales Bay Hotel Apartments is located in the town of Capelas, São Miguel within a 10-minute drive from Ponta Delgada.

The property is 12 km from the airport and 300 meters from natural pools. The property has a total of 15 apartments, all with air conditioning, safe, telephone , balcony with sea view or garden view, and bathroom with bath or shower.

Each comes with a dining area, living room and bedrooms with a flat-screen TV. Free WiFi is available throughout.

Guests are welcome to cook their own meals in the full kitchen at their disposal.

A 5-minute drive away are restaurants, bars, supermarkets and banks. The apartment complex includes a convenience store, games room with children's play area, gym, sauna, Turkish bath and a hot tub.

It also includes a salt water pool, green area, a patio and Mini Golf. Whalesbay Hotel Apartamentos is 11 km from Ponta Delgada, 100 metres from the sea and 6 km from Pico do Carvão.

It is located 5 minutes by car from the Batalha Golf Course. Sete Cidades Lagoon is 6.
