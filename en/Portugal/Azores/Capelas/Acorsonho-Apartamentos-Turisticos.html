Description

Acorsonho Apartamentos Turisticos is located in the Azorean São Miguel Island. The unit features access to outdoor and indoor swimming pools, a tennis court, games room with billiards and a mini golf course.

The apartments feature a TV, air conditioning and a balcony. There is a full kitchenette with a microwave and a refrigerator.

Featuring a bath, the private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Mountain and pool views can be enjoyed from the apartments. The unit’s complex features a restaurant, with both à la carte and buffet services.

There is also a bar, where refreshing drinks and cocktails are served. Room service is available, at an extra fee.

Guest may also choose to cook their own meals in the kitchenette. Guests can visit the spa and wellness centre and enjoy a massage, available at a surcharge.

Also available is a sauna and a hammam. Acorsonho Apartments is 800 metres from the coast and 5 minutes' drive from the center of Capelas.

The ApartHotel is 15 km from Ponta Delgada Airport and 21 km from Sete Cidades. On site there is free private parking and a car rental.
