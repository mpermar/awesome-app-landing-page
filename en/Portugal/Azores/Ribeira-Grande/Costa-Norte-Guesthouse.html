Description

This property is a 8-minute walk from the beach. Costa Norte Guest house is located in Ribeira Grande, São Miguel Island of the Azores archipelago and offers comfortable triple rooms. Free WiFi access is available.

Each room includes 3 single beds or a bunk bed and access to shared bathroom facilities. They are equipped with parquet floors, a chair, wardrobes and features views for the streets.

In the shared lounge area guests can enjoy a daily continental breakfast which is included in the rate and relax in the sofa and watch TV. In the vicinity guests can visit the restaurants and taste the local gastronomy.

Set in the historical center of Ribeira Grande, the hosts will gladly guide guests throughout the most interesting places to visit of the São Miguel Island. The famous Sete Cidades Lagoon is 28 mi away and the stunning Fire Lagoon is just 6.8 mi away. Ponta Delgada and its Airport are within 14 mi.
