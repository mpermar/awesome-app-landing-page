Description

Just 150 metres from the Lake of Furnas, these modern, 4-star apartments come with free WiFi in all areas and views of the lush green fields.

It has an outdoor pool with sun-lounger terrace. The colourful apartments at Furnas include a modern kitchen with a stove and a microwave.

Each has a living room with a fireplace and a satellite TV with a DVD player. A freshly prepared breakfast including honey, yoghurt, and cheeses from the Azores can be delivered to each morning.

Bike and car rentals are available, and the village of Furnas is just 3. 5 km from the apartments.
