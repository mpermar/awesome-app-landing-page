Description

At the resort, the rooms are equipped with a desk. All rooms have a coffee machine, while some rooms will provide you with a kitchen with a dishwasher. At Aldeia das Adegas each room comes with a seating area.

Pontas Negras is 11 miles from the accommodation, while São Mateus is 9 miles from the property.
