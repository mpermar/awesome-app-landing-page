The air-conditioned apartment consists of 3 bedrooms, a living room, a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher, and a bathroom with a bath and free toiletries. A flat-screen TV is provided.

The apartment has a grill. A sun terrace is available for guests to use at Surf and Family Beach House.
