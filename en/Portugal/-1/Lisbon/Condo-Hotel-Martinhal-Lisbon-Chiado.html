Description

Located in the heart of Lisbon, Martinhal Lisbon Chiado offers accommodations in the Chiado district, a 6-minute walk from the Cais do Sodré metro and train Station. Free WiFi is provided and a kids club is available at the property free of charge.

Composed of 37 air-conditioned apartments, accommodations at Martinhal Lisbon Chiado range from studios to 2-bedroom apartments, all featuring wooden floors, a seating area with a sofa, a dining area and a private bathroom. Linen and towels are provided.

Kids are central to the property’s philosophy and there is a kids club open day and night, covering the ages of 6 months to early teens, where kids can be looked after by a caring and responsible staff in a safe and secure space, while parents enjoy their time in Lisbon.

In each apartment guests will find an equipped kitchen where meals can be prepared, fitted with an oven, a dishwasher, a refrigerator, a microwave, an electric kettle and a coffee machine. Alternatively, there is a wide range of restaurants and cafés in the vicinity.

The lively Bairro Alto, offering various bars and eateries, is a 6-minute walk from the property, while Alfama, known for its Fado Houses and narrow streets, is 0.9 mi away and the iconic São Jorge Castle is 1.1 mi away. Lisbon International Airport is 6.2 mi from Martinhal Lisbon Chiado.

Misericordia is a great choice for travelers interested in ambiance, restaurants and friendly locals.
