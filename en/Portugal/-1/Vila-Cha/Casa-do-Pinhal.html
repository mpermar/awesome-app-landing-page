Description

At the bed and breakfast, rooms are equipped with a closet and a flat-screen TV. With a private bathroom, rooms at Casa do Pinhal also have free WiFi, while some rooms are equipped with a garden view.

A continental or buffet breakfast is served at the property.

You can play pool at the accommodation, and bike rental and car rental are available.

Casa do Pinhal can conveniently provide information at the reception to help guests to get around the area.
