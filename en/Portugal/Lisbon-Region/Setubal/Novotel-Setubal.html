Description

Rooms at the Novotel Setubal have modern furnishings. They include a sitting area with satellite TV and come equipped with a minibar and work desk.

The restaurant has a dining menu and serves regional cuisine indoors or on the terrace overlooking the pool. Room service is available.

Setubal guests can play a game on the outdoor tennis court. The hotel has a children’s playground and a video games area. The Tróia Peninsula and Atlantic beaches are a ferry boat ride away.

The Novotel Setubal is 3 miles from the Luisa Todi Theatre. Nearby recreational activities include bowling, golf, and horseback riding. The hotel has a 24-hour front desk service and free public parking on site.
