Description

The tranquil bedrooms feature strong-colored walls contrasting with the light-colored fabrics. They all include sophisticated and functional furniture plus objects from the 1950's. Each private bathroom comes with a bathrobe and slippers. Guests of the suites will find a Delta coffee machine in their room.

The on-site Sitio Valverde Restaurant and Patio offers a daily menu inspired in traditional Portuguese cuisine. Themed dinners are available, with live Fado music on Tuesdays and Saturdays, while Thursdays feature jazz and boss nova. In addition, guests can enjoy an afternoon tea or a cocktail in the intimate ambiance of the Patio. Breakfast is served daily between 07:00 and 11:00, and a brunch is available on Sundays, from 12:00 to 16:00 and accompanied by live jazz music.

The outdoor pool features jets and heated water plus background music carefully selected by a DJ or live music. The 24-hour reception can organize all tours and required visits to the city and other places. Transport services, car rental and purchase of tickets for shows or theaters are also available.

Rossio Square is a 10-minute walk, as well as D. Maria II National Theater. Lisbon International Airport is 5.1 mi away.

Santo Antonio is a great choice for travelers interested in monuments, friendly locals and architecture.
