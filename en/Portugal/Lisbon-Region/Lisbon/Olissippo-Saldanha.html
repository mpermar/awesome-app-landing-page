Description

Rooms at Olissippo are air-conditioned and include a flat-screen cable TV. All rooms have elevator access and feature a private bathroom, and some have a sitting area with a plush sofa.

A breakfast buffet is served daily, including hot and cold items, and can also be served in the guests’ rooms. A bar is available for those wishing to relax with a drink and a chat. The restaurants next door serve international cuisine.

To explore further Lisbon and its surroundings there is a car rental service available, as well as currency exchange services. The business center and meeting facilities on site cover guests’ business needs.

Lisbon International Airport is 3 miles from Olissippo Saldanha and is easily reached by public transports. The lively Bairro Alto district is 2 miles away, and the business and shopping district of Parque das Nações is 5 miles away.
