Description

This property is a 12-minute walk from the beach. Located 1.2 mi from Cascais village center and 1,650 feet from the ocean, Casa Da Luísa features an outdoor swimming pool surrounded by garden areas and a pleasant terrace with a barbecue. Free WiFi access is available in all areas.

This pet-friendly property offers large rooms with a flat-screen cable TV and a private bathroom. Some rooms have a balcony.

Guests can enjoy a breakfast buffet in the dining room opening to the swimming pool, the shared lounge area, and the library corner. Barbecue facilities are available for guests’ use. A communal kitchen is available, at an extra cost.

The seaside is within a 5-minute walk, as well as Guia Lighthouse, and Cascais Marina can be reached in a 7-minute drive. Lisbon is 16 mi from Casa Da Luísa, and the scenic Sintra village is within a 20-minute drive.

Casa Da Luísa can organize a shuttle service to Lisbon International Airport, located a 30-minute drive away. Cascais –Guincho bus station is 650 feet away.
