Description

Rooms and suites at Vila Galé Sintra are carefully decorated and provide maximum comfort. Each accommodation features a balcony and is equipped with air conditioning, minibar, safety deposit box, flat-screen cable TV and free WiFi. The bathroom features a walk-in shower, hairdryer and free toiletries.

Both restaurants of the Vila Galé Sintra offer either an à la carte light gourmet or low-calorie buffet options together with far-reaching views of the Várzea de Sintra and the Pena Palace. In addition, healthcare services are available providing options for a healthy and balanced diet.

The Revival Medical Spa features a store, medical appointments, nursing and clinical analysis. In addition, the Spa Satsanga includes a panoramic heated indoor pool, a sauna, a hot tub, a Turkish bath, a gym, a fitness area and a variety of therapies and rituals.

Other facilities include a beauty salon, laundry services, parking garage, adult and children outdoor swimming pools, a library, a children’s carrousel, NEP Kids Club with trampoline, 24-hour reception, 6 meeting rooms and a spacious hall for events.

Vila Galé Sintra is 20.5 miles from Humberto Delgado International Airport and conveniently within a 25-minute drive from beaches like Praia das Maçãs, Adraga and Azenhas do Mar. Landmarks like Quinta da Regaleira, Pena Palace and Moors Castle are within 6.2 miles.
