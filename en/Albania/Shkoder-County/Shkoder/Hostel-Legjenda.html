Description

Free WiFi is accessible to all guests, while selected rooms here will provide you with a patio.

The hostel offers a continental or American breakfast.

Hostel Legjenda has a playground. Bike rental and car rental are available at this hostel and the area is popular for cycling.

Staff at the accommodation are available to give advice at the 24-hour front desk.

Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport is 45 miles away.
