Description

Guest rooms at the hotel come with a seating area and a flat-screen TV. The private bathroom is equipped with a bath, free toiletries and a hair dryer. All guest rooms have a desk.

A Full English/Irish breakfast is available each morning at Buna Park Hotel. There's an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of Greek, Turkish and Italian dishes.

The accommodation has a playground. A range of activities can be enjoyed on site or nearby, including fishing and water park.

Lake Skadar is 1.2 miles from Buna Park Hotel. The nearest airport is Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, 45 miles from the property.
