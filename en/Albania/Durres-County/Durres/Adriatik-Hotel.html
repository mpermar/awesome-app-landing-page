Description

Completely renovated in 2017, Adriatik Hotel is located just a few steps from the beach and 5 km from the centre of Durres, it offers spa centre, a bar and a restaurant with a spacious terrace, an outdoor and indoor swimming pool and various sports facilities.

Free Wi-Fi and parking are provided. All rooms are air-conditioned and feature a desk, a TV, a phone and a minibar.

Each bathroom provides a bath. The on-site restaurant serves traditional Albanian specialties and Italian food.

A grocery store can be found 50 metres from the hotel. Local buses also stop 50 metres away.

Various facial and body treatments can be arranged in the hotel spa. There is a private beach with parasols and sun loungers available for all guests.

Tirana International Airport can be reached in 30 km, while a Ferry Port is available 4 km from Hotel Adriatik.
