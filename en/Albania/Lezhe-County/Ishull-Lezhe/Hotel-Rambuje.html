Description

Hotel Rambuje has a restaurant, seasonal outdoor swimming pool, a bar and garden in Ishull-Lezhë.

Boasting family rooms, this property also provides guests with a children's playground.

The accommodation features room service, a kids' club and currency exchange for guests.

All rooms are fitted with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a fridge, a kettle, a shower, a hairdryer and a desk.

At the hotel rooms are equipped with a wardrobe and a private bathroom. A à la carte breakfast is available each morning at Hotel Rambuje.

You can play table tennis and darts at the accommodation, and bike hire is available.

The nearest airport is Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, 46 km from Hotel Rambuje.
