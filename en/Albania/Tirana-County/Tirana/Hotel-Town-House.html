Description

Set within 1 km from the central Skanderbeg Square, Hotel Town House has a restaurant and features air-conditioned rooms with 32 inch flat-screen TVs and free Wi-Fi.

Renovated in 2013, the hotel features a common area with a fireplace. Public buses stop right in front.

All rooms come with a desk, wardrobe, fridge, iron and a laptop-sized safety box.

The private bathroom includes a large shower cabin, hairdryer and Italian toiletries.

Reception operates 24 hours a day and offers currency exchange and free laundry and ironing services.

The vibrant Blloku District of Tirana with several shops, bars and restaurants is 1.

3 km from the hotel, while the large Lake Park is 2 km away. Dajti Express Cable Car leading up to the Dajti Mountain National Park is at a distance of 4 km.
