Description

Hotel Flower & SPA features free WiFi throughout the property and views of sea in Golem.

This 5-star hotel offers spa facilities and a 24-hour front desk. Guests can relax in the hot tub, or enjoy garden views.

All rooms at the hotel come with a seating area and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

The rooms include air conditioning, and selected rooms have a balcony. The units feature a desk.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at Hotel Flower & SPA. Tirana is 43 km from Hotel Flower & SPA, while Durrës is 16 km from the property.
