Description

Featuring a restaurant and a private beach area, Hotel Klajdi is located in Durrës.

It offers modernly decorated rooms with air conditioning. Free WiFi access is available throughout the property.

Each room here provides a flat-screen cable TV and a minibar. Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also comes with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Some rooms come with a balcony, while suites also provide a seating area. The reception desk at Klajdi Hotel stays open 24 hours a day, while the property itself includes a garden with a children's playground.

After a day on the beach, you can relax with a drink at the on-site bar. The hotel is 13 km from Port of Durrës and 15 km from Durrës Amphiteatre.
