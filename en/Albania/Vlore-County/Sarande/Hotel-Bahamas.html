Description

Hotel Bahamas is located right on the private pebbly beach near the town of Sarandë.

It offers an outdoor pool with sun loungers and parasols, a restaurant and free Wi-Fi.

A games room and a children’s playground are also available. All rooms are air-conditioned and offer a cable TV, a fridge and a balcony.

Private bathroom is fitted with a shower. The on-site bar and restaurant serve traditional Albanian food.

There is also a beach volley court on site. Free parking is provided. The centre of Sarandë can be reached in 4.

5 km. Sarandë Old Town is 14 km away, while the coastal town of Ksamil is 12 km away.
