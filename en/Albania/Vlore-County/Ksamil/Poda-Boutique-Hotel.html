Description

Located in Ksamil, 3. 1 km from Butrint National Park, Poda Boutique Hotel provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a seasonal outdoor swimming pool and a bar.

With free WiFi, this 4-star hotel has a garden and a terrace. The accommodation offers room service, luggage storage space and currency exchange for guests.

All units are equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fridge, a kettle, a shower, a hairdryer and a desk.

At the hotel every room is equipped with a wardrobe and a private bathroom. Guests at Poda Boutique Hotel can enjoy a continental breakfast.
