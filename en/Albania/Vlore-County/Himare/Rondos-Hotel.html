Description

Rondos Hotel is set right on a private pebbly beach equipped with free-to-use sun loungers and parasols.

It offers an on-site restaurant and bar with a terrace, free parking and free Wi-Fi.

The centre of Himara is 800 metres away. All rooms are air-conditioned and offer a balcony, a flat-screen TV, a wardrobe and a private bathroom with a shower.

Some offer direct sea views and some side views of the sea. The hotel's restaurant serves sea food and international specialties.

Guests can find various bars and clubs in the centre of Himara. Daily trips and excursions to Saranda and Vlora can be arranged by the hotel and the excursion bus stops right outside.

Local buses with lines to Himara stop 200 metres away, while the Main Bus Station is 800 metres away.
