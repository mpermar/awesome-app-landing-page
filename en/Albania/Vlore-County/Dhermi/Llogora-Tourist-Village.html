Description

Located within thee Llogora National Park, Llogora Tourist Village provides an indoor pool, as well as an on-site bar and restaurant that offers traditional Albanian dishes.

Saunas and a Turkish bath can be used free of charge. Free WiFi is available in the lobby.

All accommodation units provide a seating area and either a TV or a flat-screen TV.

Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with free toiletries and slippers.

You can enjoy a mountain view from all the rooms. Tourist Village Llogora runs a 24-hour front desk.

Other facilities offered at the property include a shared lounge, a games room and a children's playground.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including mini golf, billiards and table tennis.

The property offers free parking. A sandy beach is available in the town of Dhermi, 15 km away.
