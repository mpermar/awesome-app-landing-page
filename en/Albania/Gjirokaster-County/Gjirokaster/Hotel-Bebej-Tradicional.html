Description

The hotel comes with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a kitchenette, a dining area and a private bathroom with a bidet. Hotel Bebej Tradicional has some rooms that feature a balcony, and the rooms come with a coffee machine. The rooms have a desk.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property.

Sarandë is 15 miles from the accommodation.
