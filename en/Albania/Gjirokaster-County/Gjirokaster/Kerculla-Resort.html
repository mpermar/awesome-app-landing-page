Description

Offering an outdoor pool, barbecue and children's playground, Kerculla Resort is located in Gjirokastër, 62 km from Sarandë.

Free WiFi is offered and free private parking is available on site. Rooms feature a flat-screen TV.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. Guests can enjoy the on-site bar.
