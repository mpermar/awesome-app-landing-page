Description

Featuring free WiFi throughout the property, Alvero Hotel offers accommodation in Përmet.

The hotel has a children's playground and views of the mountain, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Rooms come with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Certain units feature a seating area for your convenience.

Views of the river, garden or city are featured in certain rooms. Each room is equipped with a private bathroom with a bath or shower and bidet, with slippers and a hairdryer provided.

You will find a gift shop at the property. Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including cycling, fishing, rafting and canoeing.
