Description

Jurmala Golf Club&Hotel is located in Piņķi. Property offers free WiFi and air-conditioned rooms.

This elegant and welcoming hotel also provides guests with a restaurant and a 24-hour front desk.

Rooms in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV and a balcony. Varied buffet breakfast, as well as delicious dishes, are available at the Club House restaurant.

It is also possible to refresh and relax in the bar. The complex is located just 17km from Riga, and 13km from Jurmala.
