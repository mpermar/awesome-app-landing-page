Description

The Jurmala Spa is a modern spa resort and conference centre, only a short walk away from the beach.

It offers free WiFi in public areas and free parking. The hotel’s spa and health centre spreads over 2 floors and offers various massages and water therapies.

Three pools with different temperatures, children's pool with slide, hot tubs, 5 different saunas, children's sauna, a solarium, contrast shower, Russian shower, cascade, massage room and a bar can be found in the spa area.

There is also a modern gym and a wide range of beauty treatments available at Hotel Jurmala Spa.

Some treatments are offered at an additional cost. The on-site restaurant serves Latvian and international cuisine and a wide selection of cocktails and wines.

It has a dance floor with live music on certain days. The Seaside Bar offers panoramic views over Jurmala and the Gulf of Riga.
