Description

Located just 2 km from the centre of Sigulda, this romantic, alpine-style guesthouse offers a quiet, relaxing accommodation in a beautiful natural environment.

The Kakitis offers a sauna complex with several saunas, a hot tub and a sauna bar, all available at a surcharge.

The nearby adventure park on Kakitis Hill provides skiing fun in winter and a variety of activities in summer.

The pub serves a wide range of tasty meals in every season. From the wide terrace you can enjoy beautiful views of the surrounding area in the warm season, while you can sit by the cosy fireplace in winter.

Ski and snowboard rental, as well as a playroom for children, are also available at the Kakitis.

The beautiful Gauja Valley and the famous bobsleigh and toboggan run of Sigulda are nearby.
