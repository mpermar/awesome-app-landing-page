Description

Opened in 2007, the Augustine hotel is a small, cosy and family-owned hotel in a renovated historical wooden building, just a short tram ride away from Old Riga.

Parking area is provided for your convenience and the hotel's garden is a great place to relax in the warmer months.

A Finnish sauna is available upon request in the cellar of the hotel. A buffet breakfast is served from 08:00 to 10:00 every morning and a self-catering kitchen with all utensils, an electric cooker and a refrigerator can be used upon request without any extra costs.

Augustine Hotel is located 2-minutes away from Centre Sports Quarter - an outdoor playground with basketball, tennis, football, beach-volleyball courts, skatepark and BMX track.

An airport shuttle service is available upon request at an additional cost. Riga International Airport is located 11 km away.
