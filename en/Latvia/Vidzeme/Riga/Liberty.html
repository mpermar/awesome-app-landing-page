Description

Liberty is located on the main pedestrian street of the Old Town and is 100 metres from the Monument of Freedom.

The hotel offers accommodation on the top floor, accessible by lift. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

There is 24/7 video surveillance in common areas and electronic passage control by programmable room keys.

There is hard flooring and hypoallergenic environment throughout the building. Guests receive breakfast discounts to restaurants nearby.

Guests have access to a TV lounge with a kitchenette. The hotel is next to Bastejkalns park, 200 metres from the Latvian National Opera, 300 metres from the University of Latvia and 400 metres from the Arsenals Exhibition Hall.
