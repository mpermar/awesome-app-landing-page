Description

The 4-star Superior Bellevue Park Hotel Riga is located next to the picturesque Victory Park, 1.

7 km from the city’s Old Town. It offers spa facilities, a fitness room and free WiFi, and free on-site parking.

All air-conditioned rooms at Bellevue Park Hotel Riga are bright and furnished in wooden furniture.

Each comes with a minibar, work space and a flat-screen, cable TV. All have a bathroom with a bath or shower and a hairdryer.

There is a possibility for hypo-allergic floors. Ironing facilities are provided for guests upon request.

Located on the 10th floor, restaurant Le Sommet offers Mediterranean cuisine and panoramic views of Riga.

Park Restaurant is located in the hotel lobby. Both restaurants offer outdoor seating in summer.

Guests may relax in a steam bath, exercise in the fitness centre or try a selection of massage treatments.

There is also a beauty salon on site. Front desk staff is available 24 hours a day and can arrange for concierge and room service.

Riga Passenger Terminal, Central Train and Bus stations are all located within 3 km from Bellevue Park Hotel.
