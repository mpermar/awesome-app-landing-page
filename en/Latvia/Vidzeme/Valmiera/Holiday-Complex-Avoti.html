Description

Holiday Complex Avoti is located a 6-minute drive from the centre of Valmiera. It offers apartments with free Wi-Fi, as well a camping site, with a mini golf course and a sauna.

The Avoti offers homely accommodation combining modern conveniences with the proximity of nature.

The apartments are air-conditioned and include satellite TV. There is a football pitch and a tennis court on the site, as well as a playground for children.

Holiday Complex Avoti features a BMX training track. The area offers many hiking and cycling possibilities The complex is situated by the A3 route and offers free private parking.
