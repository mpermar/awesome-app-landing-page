Description

Located within the Gauja National Park, the largest in Latvia, the stylish, family-run Estere offers self-catering accommodation with free wired internet and private parking.

Cēsis Castle is 1. 6 km away. The cottage at Estere, surrounded by greenery, is spacious, air-conditioned and decorated in a modern style featuring rustic elements.

There is a flat-screen TV with a DVD player and a bathroom is equipped with a shower.

Guests have access to a sauna at a surcharge. Guests have use of a kitchenette which comes with a refrigerator, a stove and a microwave and a dining area is also provided.

Barbecue facilities are available in the garden. Cēsis Train Station is 2 km away and Žagarkalns and Ozolkalns ski centres are only 800 metres away.
