Description

Rakari is a recreation complex on the seaside of Vidzeme. A sauna and indoor pool is available at surcharge at the property.

The property features a guesthouse with individual rooms as well as cottages. Each room in the guesthouse features a private bathroom with a shower, and a TV.

Every cottage consists of a bedroom and a private bathroom with a shower, a kitchenette and a dining area.

Each cottage features an outdoor terrace. A pub is featured at the property. Guests can play sports at the property, barbecue facilities are available.

The is a children's playground at Rakari. The Via Baltica motorway is 800 metres away, while Riga is 82 km away.
