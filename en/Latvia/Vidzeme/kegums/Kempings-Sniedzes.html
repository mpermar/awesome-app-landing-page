Description

Kempings Sniedzes is a camping site by the Daugava River, with water sports facilities and a sauna.

It offers wooden cottages and the Internet connection is provided on the site. Each cottage at Sniedzes is equipped with a fridge and a traditional stove for heating.

There are wooden bunk beds. Shared bathroom facilities are situated in the main building.

The camping area offers many sport possibilities including volleyball and croquet courts.

Guests can also rent water bikes, surfboards or boats. There also is a sauna to relax in, at surcharge.

Kempings Sniedzes is located in a quiet area, a 25-minute drive from the town Ogre.
