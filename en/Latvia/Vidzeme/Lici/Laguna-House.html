Description

Located in the middle of the forest and on the shore on the Sunīšu Lake, Laguna House hotel offers rooms with free Wi-Fi.

It has been designed by a well known Latvian architect, Janis Vizins. All rooms in Laguna House are classically designed and painted in warm colours.

They feature a TV with cable channels and a bathroom with free toiletries and a shower.

Guests of Laguna House can enjoy a private, sandy beach or sunbathe on a sun terrace.

Children will enjoy a playground. Guests can also fish in the lake or rent a boat.

Breakfast is served in a restaurant, and in the summer, Laguna beach bar serves barbecue dishes.

Hotel Laguna House is located 200 metres from A4/A1 highway between Vilnius, Riga and Tallinn.
