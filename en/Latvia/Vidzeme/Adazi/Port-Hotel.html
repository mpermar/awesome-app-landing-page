Description

Surrounded by a beautiful pine forest, yet only 300 metres off E67, Via Baltica motorway, Port Hotel offers accommodation with free private parking and spa and wellness facilities.

Free Wi-Fi is also available. Featuring elegant wooden furniture inspired by a nautical style, the rooms at Port Hotel come with a TV, a telephone and a desk.

A private bathroom is equipped with a hairdryer and toiletries. A breakfast buffet is offered every morning at the restaurant which specialises in international cuisine and also boasts a fine wine list.

Guests can enjoy their meals on the terrace in the summer. An indoor swimming pool is provided for free from 7:00 till 11:00.

There is a sauna, a steam bath and massage services at an additional surcharge. Children can have fun in the play room where toys and colouring books.
