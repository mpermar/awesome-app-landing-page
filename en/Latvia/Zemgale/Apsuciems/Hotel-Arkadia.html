Description

The new Hotel Arkadia is located in the village of Apsuciems, 60 km west of Riga and about 500 metres from the shores of the Baltic Sea.

Enjoying a picturesque location right by the meandering Lacupite River, this comfortable hotel features a modern spa centre offering professional massages and beauty treatments.

The Hotel Arkadia also offers a big restaurant, a bistro, conference facilities, a children’s playroom, a billiard room and a gym.
