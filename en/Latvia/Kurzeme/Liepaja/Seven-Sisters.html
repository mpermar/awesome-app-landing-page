Description

Just a 10-minute walk from Liepaja city centre, Seven Sisters offers free Wi-Fi and bright rooms with a flat-screen TV.

A pretty Baltic Sea beach is just 2 km from the property. A hearty breakfast buffet is available each morning at the Seven Sisters.

Guests are also welcome to use the shared barbeque facilities in the hotel’s large garden.

Each of the simply furnished rooms at the Seven Sisters hotel come equipped with cable TV channels and a seating area.

A shower and hairdryer can be found in the private bathrooms. Liepaja Airport is just 5 km away from the hotel and the Liepaja Lake can be reached in just 5 minutes on foot.
