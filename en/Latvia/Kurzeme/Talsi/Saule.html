Description

Located only 65 metres from Lake Talsi, Saule offers homelike accommodation in a renovated house that is over 100 years old.

Rooms feature with free Wi-Fi. All the rooms in Saule feature renovated, century old wooden floors and are decorated in classical style, with light pine wood furniture.

They all have a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a DVD player, as well as an electric kettle and a bathroom with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Saule is surrounded by a green garden with a rock garden area, as well as barbecue facilities and a private pond.

At an extra charge, guests can fish, ride a horse or enjoy a boat trip, as well as shuttle services.

Baron Fircks Manor, where Talsi District Museum is located, is only a 3-minute walk away.
