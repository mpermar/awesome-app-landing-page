Description

Karlamuiza Country Hotel is arranged in a building of the old manor. Today, spacious suites with separate kitchens and bedrooms equipped with up-to-date appliances are located there.

Every suite is equipped with basic kitchen amenities as well as a bathroom and all common facilities.

The breakfast at the Karlamuiza Country Hotel is served as a buffet. Newly-baked breads, squeezed juices, fresh fruit, tea and coffee can be tasted in the dining room with a fireplace.

It reveals a splendid view of the garden. Karlamuiza is located in the Cesis district, Karli, Gauja National Park territory.
