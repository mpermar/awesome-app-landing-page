Description

Surrounded by a forest park with a pond, this restored historic manor from the mid-18th century enjoys a scenic location about a 1-hour drive northeast of Riga.

Annas Hotel - Design Hotel Spa Restaurant features individually decorated rooms, a meeting room with a fireplace, and a restaurant and bar with a summer terrace.

The restaurant in the manor’s former refectory (dining hall) offers a menu consisting of natural, locally grown products prepared according to the philosophy of Slow Food.

Free Wi-Fi access is available in the entire Annas Hotel. The spa features a marble swimming pool, a hot tub, a sauna, a steam bath, rain showers, and much more.

Massages are also available. Herbal tea is served in the spa area. The forest park surrounding Annas Hotel features a hot spring, where you can take a bath, and offers a wide range of sports and leisure options.

These include fishing, boating, and hiking, as well as cross-country skiing and ice-skating.
