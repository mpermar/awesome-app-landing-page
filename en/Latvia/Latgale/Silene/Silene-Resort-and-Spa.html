Description

Nested in the middle of a pine forest and on the banks of the lake Sila, Silene Resort & Spa offers an accommodation in various holiday homes and rooms.

Spacious holiday homes are equipped with a fireplace, free WiFi and TVs with cable channels.

Guests have an access to to outdoor swimming pool, hot tub and a sauna. The fully-equipped kitchen includes a stove, a refrigerator and a microwave.

Guests can barbecue and enjoy their meals on the wooden terrace. Silene Resort & Spa has a wide offer of various recreational facilities, such as a sand box and a playground for children, a volleyball, basketball, paintball and football pitches, a basketball court and rope park.

Guests can rent out boats, bikes, pedalos, electric mobiles, electric scooters and and catamarans.
