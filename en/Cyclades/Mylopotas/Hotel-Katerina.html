Description

Hotel Katerina is built in traditional island fashion. Its stylish and spacious rooms offer modern amenities and comforts during your stay.

The quality of Hotel Katerina's service, the friendly staff and the excellent attention to guests are appreciated by the many regular guests who return every year.

Enjoy your breakfast or drink at the poolside, along with a wonderful panoramic view of Mylopotas beach. The beach is 0.6 mi long and claims to be one of the best in Greece.

Shops, clubs, restaurants and water sport facilities are just few of the amenities within easy access of Hotel Katerina. Enough parking space is available for hotel guests. All levels are accessible by vehicle.
