Description

This charming hotel offers sea and caldera views from its terrace. The family owners at Kokkinos Villas are available 24 hours a day to help with travel arrangements or provide personal recommendations.

On the beach, there is a scuba diving school for the more adventurous. For those who would like to relax, traditional Greek and local specialties are available at Remezzo tavern.

Akrotiri is a small traditional village located at the southern part of Santorini, known for its light-house, its old castle and the unique ancient ruins of 1500 B.C.

"Wonderful experience! I cannot say enough positive things about my families stay here! The owner, staff, family all were great! This was our time in Santorini and I almost booked us in Fira and boy am I glad I did not. If you like extreme crowds, noise and being on top of everybody and awful traffic then Fira and Oia is for you maybe. We rented a car.. it’s cheap there 28 to 32$ a day.. a lot of people do vespas or atvs.. you will spend 30-40euros one way in cab fair to and from the airport.. consider that into your cost. We went to the black beach two of our four days.. it great.. hot sand though. If you buy a smoothie or a bite to eat you can sit in very comfortable lounge chairs with palm umbrellas that the restaurant or bars have out along the villas give you beach towels to take to the beach. We did the 6 mile hike from Fira to Oia, it was spectacular! Parking is free everywhere on the island. Be sure to eat at the “Family bakery” not too far from the villas.. look up Santorini Dave on the web.. lots of good information there! The view is incredible from the villas.. there is a little market store to buy water and essentials less than 5 minutes from the villas.. I cannot thank the owner and staff enough for all they did to make our stay so great! We have traveled all over the world and this trip to Santorini has probably been our favorite.. it’s gonna be hard to beat!"
