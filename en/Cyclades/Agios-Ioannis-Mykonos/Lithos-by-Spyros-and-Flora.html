Description

Studios and apartments at Lithos are minimally furnished and decorated in soft tones. They all include a kitchenette and a living area with a satellite LCD TV. The spacious bathroom is stocked with free toiletries, hairdryer and slippers. Some units enjoy views of the pool and Agios Ioannis Bay.

Guests can enjoy refreshing drinks, fresh juices and snacks at a shaded area by the pool or have an evening cocktail at the lobby bar. Continental breakfast is served in the morning.

The picturesque and lively center of Mykonos Town is 2.5 miles away and Mykonos Airport is 3 miles away. The sandy beach of Ornos one mile from the property. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the property and free on-site parking is available.
