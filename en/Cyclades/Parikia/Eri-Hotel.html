Description

On attractive grounds, Eri rooms and apartments are traditionally decorated and have a balcony. They come with air conditioning, a satellite TV and a mini fridge. 24-hour room service is also available.

Guests can start their day with a rich buffet breakfast. Later they can enjoy a refreshing drink or cocktail by the pool overlooking Parikia town. Greek and international cuisine is served at the hotel’s restaurant.

The nearest beach is 1,000 feet from Eri. Paros Airport is a 20-minute drive away. Private on-site parking is provided free of charge.
