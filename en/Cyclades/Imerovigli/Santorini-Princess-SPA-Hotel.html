Description

The units are modernly decorated and preserve the Cycladic-style. They have a 26” LCD satellite TV, a laptop, a safe and a minibar. The bathroom includes a hairdryer, bathrobes and toiletries.

A breakfast buffet is served daily in the sit-down restaurant or in the privacy of your room. The outdoor restaurant prepares Mediterranean and Greek cuisine. The pool bar looks out onto the pool and sea. Room service is also available.

The facilities include a spa and a health club which has a sauna, a hot tub and treatment rooms. The outdoor pool is heated during April, May and October. Information on local attractions and beaches can be provided by the on-site tour desk.

The Santorini Princess Spa Hotel is less than 2.5 miles from the lively town of Fira. Santorini Airport is 6 miles away. Shuttle services to and from the airport and around the island can be arranged upon request for an extra fee. Free private parking is available on site.
