Description

Faros offers air conditioned rooms with a fridge, and apartments with an equipped kitchenette. Most units have private balcony or porch with views of the sea and the sunset. Wi-Fi is free in public hotel areas.

Guests can wake up to in-room Continental breakfast. Greek dishes and local specialties are served at the tavern by the beach. The all-day pool bar serves coffee, milkshakes, snacks and ice cream, and offers sea and sunset views.

Guests can take advantage of the variety of activities offered in the area, such as water sports, cycling and horseback riding.

The friendly family-run hotel can offer tips on walks and sights, and assist guests with car rental, and bike rental. Naxos Town is 11 miles away, and transportation can be arranged with the hotel’s mini bus.
