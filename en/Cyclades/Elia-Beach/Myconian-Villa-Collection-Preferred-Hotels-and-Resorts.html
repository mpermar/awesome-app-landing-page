Description

Combining stylish interiors with hand crafted details and soft hues, Myconian rooms and villas enjoy unlimited sea views. They include spacious living areas and bathrooms with rain shower. They are all equipped with a 3D flat-screen TV and DVD/MP3 player.

You can start your day with a full American breakfast in buffet style. The all-day pool bar serves exotic cocktails and light snacks, while Cabbanes a la carte restaurant prepares Mediterranean and local dishes, which can be enjoyed under the sea-view pergola.

In Thalasso Spa Centre, guests can choose among a wide variety of spa treatments, such as color hydrobath, Pantai treatments and massages. The centre also includes an indoor pool with lounge chairs, where you can relax and enjoy fresh fruits and refreshments.

The cosmopolitan beach of Psarrou is 8.7 miles away, while Platys Gialos is at 6.2 miles. The charming centre of Mykonos’ main town can also be reached at 7.5 miles. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the resort and free private parking is possible.
