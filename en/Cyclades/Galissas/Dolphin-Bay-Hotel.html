Description

Built in the traditional Cycladic architectural style, the hotel offers bright and airy rooms, suites and apartments with private terraces or balconies with sea, or garden and village views.

Continental breakfast is served each morning, and guests can enjoy drinks and snacks on the poolside. Guests enjoy the Dolphin Bay’s traditional tavern, piano bar, and hotel restaurant.

A children’s pool and play area are provided. There is free Wi-Fi in the entire hotel. The facilities also include water slides for the younger guests.

The hotel is within easy walking distance of picturesque seaside villages, the ancient ruins of Galissas, and the Cave of Saint Stefanos.
