Description

Each with a private balcony, the air-conditioned studios and apartments at Tropicana are simply, yet tastefully furnished. They have a well-equipped kitchenette, satellite LCD TV and a private bathroom with shower.

Guests can enjoy Greek and Italian cuisine at the on-site restaurant. Drinks and exotic cocktails are served at the bar, which also organizes regular beach parties.

The picturesque main town and port of Mykonos is 3 miles from Tropicana. Mykonos Airport is at 2 miles. Free parking can be found near the premises.
