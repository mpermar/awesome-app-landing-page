Description

All air conditioned rooms at Anatoli Hotel have a furnished balcony, most with views of the eastern part of the island and the Aegean Sea. Each unit is equipped with TV and fridge.

There are plenty of free lounge chairs and sun umbrellas around the pool and children’s pool, as well as a bar that serves coffee and drinks. Family-run Anatoli’s restaurant offers Greek cuisine dishes during lunch and dinner.

Guests of Anatoli Hotel are offered free Wi-Fi access in all public hotel areas. There is also free private parking space on site.

The staff at the 24-hour front desk can organize tours to Santorini's sights such as the Akrotiri archeological site. The Anatoli is a 10-minute walk from the bus stop with routes to most beaches. Santorini International Airport is 3 miles away.
