Description

The hotel offers facilities with great consideration for its guests. The units provide all modern comforts, for dream-like holidays. All rooms have the traditional island style. They feature a balcony or terrace with an impressive view of the sea and the island.

Guest can relax by the swimming pool and enjoy the sea and the great views. Thira hotel is the ideal place to discover the island, with the volcano and the unique beaches.

Downstairs has a large room with double bed and 2 smaller beds in the open area which are really only suitable for children up to teenage years. Upstairs was another large room with double bed and a large sofa bed which is OK for adults. 2 separate shower rooms with toilets. So really comfortable and spacious for 4/5 adults and 2 children.The people managing the property were fantastic and they helped us to transfer our luggage to and from the property as it cannot by accessed directly with a car. As most properties in Santorini it is accessed via an alley way; the car can get most of the way there apart from the last 20 meters.It is also served well by some very good restaurants nearby and small convenience shops; but also you can be looking over the Caldera within 10 minutes - and that's is walking with young kids.
