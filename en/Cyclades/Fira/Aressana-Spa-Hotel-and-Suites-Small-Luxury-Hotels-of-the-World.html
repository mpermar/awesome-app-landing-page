Description

The elegant and fresh guest rooms are decorated in warm earthy tones. Guests are welcomed with in-room complimentary homemade sweets and local spirits.

Guests can indulge in beauty and relaxation treatments at the hotel’s Tefsion Kallos Spa. There is also a well-appointed fitness center on the site. Aressana Spa Hotel has many little hideaways where guests can read or just relax.

A certified Greek Breakfast buffet with Santorinian specialties and traditional delicacies is served adjacent to the swimming pool between 08:00 and 11:00. The poolside snack bar provides a refreshing break from the Greek sun, serving light Santorinian and International meals, cool drinks and cocktails.

Aressana Hotel’s peaceful location offers magnificent sunrise views. Santorini's many local taverns, shops and restaurants are just steps away from the hotel.

The hotel is well-serviced by taxi and a bus station, located just 350 feet away. There is also a bank and post office close by. Santorini Airport (JTR) is 3.7 mi away and Athinios Port is 6.2 mi.
