Description

The 6 luxurious suites, situated on the caldera, are in a magnificent and quiet location, only steps from the nightlife, and provide guests with simple yet exquisite Cycladic luxury.

All suites are fully equipped with minibar, fridge, air condition, satellite TV, CD and DVD player, laptop, free wireless internet access, pool towels and natural bath amenities by Korres.

Aria Suites provide guests with 24 hour front desk service with concierge assistance. Massage and beauty treatments as well as special therapies or candlelit dinners can be arranged in the privacy of the suite, upon request.
