Description

Located on the banks of the Sabie River and surrounded by lush gardens, Laughing Waters Guest House in Hazyview offers Tuscan ambience and an outdoor pool.

Through the landscaped courtyard, most of the rooms offer views of the surrounding.

An English breakfast is served on the veranda, overlooking the garden. Dinner can be arranged upon advance request for a surcharge.

Guests can take a swim in the outdoor pool or relax in the lounge area, which offers a selection of books.

Laughing Waters Guest House is 17 km from Kruger National Park and close to the Panorama Route.
