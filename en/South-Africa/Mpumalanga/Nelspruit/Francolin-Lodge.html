Description

This modern, 5-star guest house offers stylish open plan rooms with private balconies in the city centre of Mbombela.

Francolin Lodge includes an outdoor swimming pool with a deck, shaded seating area and a designated out-door smoking area.

All of the luxurious rooms at Francolin Lodge have a LCD TV, kitchenette and en-suite bathroom.

Lodge Francolin is a 15-minute drive from Lowveld National Botanical Gardens and only 2 km from Nelspruit Golf Club.

The Numbi Gate of Kruger National Park is 63 km away. Orange Restaurant serves modern South African cuisine and guests can have a drink on the terrace whilst enjoying panoramic views over Nelspruit.

Breakfast and lunch packs are available upon advance request. The guesthouse offers free private parking on site and an airport shuttle service is available upon request.
