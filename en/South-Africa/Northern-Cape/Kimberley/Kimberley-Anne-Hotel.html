Description

Guest rooms has air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fridge, a coffee machine, a shower, free toiletries and a desk. With a private bathroom, some rooms at the hotel also offer a pool view. All rooms have a closet.

Kimberley Anne Hotel offers a continental or Full English/Irish breakfast. The restaurant at the accommodation serves international cuisine.

With staff speaking Xhosa and Zulu, around the clock advice is available at the reception.

Benfontein Nature Reserve is 8 miles from Kimberley Anne Hotel, while Hoffe Park Stadium is 0.7 miles away. The nearest airport is Kimberley Airport, 2.5 miles from the property.
