Description

All guest rooms in the resort are equipped with a electric tea pot. Marepe Country Lodge has some units with mountain views, and the rooms come with a balcony. The rooms feature a private bathroom.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a Full English/Irish breakfast. At Marepe Country Lodge you'll find a restaurant serving Grill/bbq cuisine. Gluten-free and kosher options can also be requested.

Guests can take part in various activities in the surrounding areas, including cycling and hiking.

There's an in-house bar and guests can also use the business area.

Jessica the Hippo is 10 miles from the resort, while Blyde Canyon Adventure Centre is 4 miles away. Air Force Base Hoedspruit Airport is 16 miles from the property.
