Description

Set 1 km from Kruger National Park, Sefapane Lodge in Phalaborwa offers an outdoor pool, spa and game drives.

The thatched accommodation is spread across the tropical gardens which include 4 lakes.

Featuring a private veranda overlooking the grounds, the brightly decorated rondavels have thatched walls and ceilings.

The 3-bedroom safari houses include a furnished deck, spacious living room and a kitchen.

Some of the rooms are located at the Sefapane River Lodge, which is set on the banks of the Olifants River in a private game reserve.

Breakfast is available and there is an à la carte restaurant at the lodge which serves African dishes with a Western twist.

There is a bar by the pool and evening safaris, complete with a bush braai barbecue, are also available.

Sefapane Lodge offers different safaris and activities,such as full days safaris, morning safaris, evening safaris, river safaris and even cycling trips and bushwalks in the Kruger National Park.

Cultural Village tours and day excursions to the Blyde River Canyon and Moholoholo animal Rehab center can also be arranged.
