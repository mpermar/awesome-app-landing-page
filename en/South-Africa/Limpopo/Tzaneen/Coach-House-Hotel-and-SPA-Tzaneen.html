Description

Located 17 km from Tzaneen, this classic country hotel features stunning views of the Wolkberg Mountains.

It offers indoor and outdoor pools, a spa and a restaurant. Free parking is available.

The well appointed rooms at the Coach House Hotel & Spa are air-conditioned and each features an en suite bathroom and a private veranda with panoramic mountain views.

All the room are equipped with TV with selected satellite channels, a minibar and a safe.

The Zeederberg Restaurant offers fine dining and local wines. Meals can be enjoyed indoors or in the lush garden, next to the swimming pool.

Guests can enjoy facial and body massages and a range of other relaxation treatments at Agatha Spa.
