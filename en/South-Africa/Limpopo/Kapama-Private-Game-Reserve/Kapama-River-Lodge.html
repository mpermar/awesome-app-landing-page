Description

Part of the Greater National Kruger Park, Kapama River Lodge is situated in the expansive Kapama Game Reserve.

Enjoy a luxurious stay in modern accommodations, while surrounded by the African bush and wildlife at the bend of the seasonal Kapama River.

Kapama is the largest luxury game lodge in the Greater Kruger National Park area, including 64 air-conditioned suites all stylishly furnished and set against the backdrop of the Drakensberg Mountains.

Each suite has a private bathroom fitted with a bath and shower, and complete with complimentary amenities.

Each is also equipped with a minibar and coffee machine. The lodge features a swimming pool and spa.

Kapama River Lodge offers free WiFi and guests enjoy a variety of activities game drives and bush walks.

All drinks and buffet meals are served at the on-site bar and restaurant, and special diet menus are catered on request.
