Description

Located in Polokwane, this hotel has 2 restaurants, a tennis court and 4 swimming pools surrounded by landscaped gardens.

The Ranch can arrange safaris and game viewing. Free private parking is available.

The bright rooms at the 4-star Protea Hotel The Ranch have satellite TV, a spacious seating area with a work desk, a tea/coffee maker and a private bathroom.

Guests can enjoy gourmet cuisine in elegant surroundings at Basil's Fine Dining Restaurant.

The hotel also features The Buttery Family Restaurant which offers a rich breakfast prepared with seasonal ingredients.

The Protea Hotel’s outdoor swimming pool is surrounded by sun loungers, where guests can relax while enjoying a cocktail from the pool bar.

Relaxing steam rooms are also available. There are a variety of activities that can be arranged at The Ranch, including Game drives, horseback safaris and golf.

Protea Hotel is located within a 1,000 hectare ‘malaria free’ nature conservancy, just off the N1 Freeway.
