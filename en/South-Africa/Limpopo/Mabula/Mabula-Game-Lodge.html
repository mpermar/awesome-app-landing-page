Description

Located in a private game reserve, against the backdrop of the lush green Waterberg Mountains, Mabula Game Lodge offers luxurious rooms.

It features an outdoor pool and spa facilities. Only two hours North of Johannesburg, this 12000 hectare reserve is home to an abundance of animal and bird life.

Mabula Game Lodge has spacious rooms with twin or double beds. All rooms are elegantly decorated in neutral colours and some rooms offer a seating area.

Each of the air-conditioned rooms has a flat-screen satellite TV and tea-and-coffee-making facilities.

The hotelâ€™s restaurant serves a large buffet or local and international dishes, while the elegant bar offers refreshing drinks, as well as a whiskey and gin collection.

Guests can choose from a variety of facilities, including spa treatments at the Mabula Spa, a tennis court, and a Cubz Club for younger guests featuring activities and games.

Two daily safaris in an open safari vehicle are included in the rates at Mabula Game Lodge.

Parking is possible on site and an airport shuttle can be arranged at a charge upon request.
