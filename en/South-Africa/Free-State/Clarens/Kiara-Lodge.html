Description

Located a 14-minute drive from Clarens, Kiara Lodge is set amongst the Maluti Mountains and offers a heated indoor pool, a spa and wellness centre and a children’s playground.

The property is just next to the Golden Gate Highlands National Park. The apartments feature a balcony with BBQ facilities and a living room with a TV.

There are 2 or 3 bedrooms, of which one has an en suite bathroom and the main bathroom includes a bathtub.

The kitchen is equipped with an oven, microwave and stovetop. Groceries can be purchased in the on-site mini-market and there is also a bar where guests can relax with a drink.

Additional features include a tennis court, billiards, table tennis and mini golf.

Free private parking is possible at the property. Phuthaditjhaba is a 30-minute drive away.
