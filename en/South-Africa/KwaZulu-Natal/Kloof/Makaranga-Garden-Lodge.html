Description

This lodge lies within 30 acres of indigenous and exotic botanical gardens, 28 km outside of Durban.

It features a swimming pool, a wellness centre, and an art gallery on site. All of the accommodation at the Makaranga Garden Lodge is very spacious, and each room and suite features a balcony with views of the surrounding gardens.

Every room has a flat-screen TV and an en suite with a bath or shower. The lodge offers a choice of two restaurants, as well as the stylish Library Bar.

Guests can relax on the sunny terrace. A range of activities are offered for children.

Attractions in the area include the Kranzkloof Nature Reserve just 5 km away or visit Shongweni Dam less than 19km away.

Other nearby options include horse riding and polo. Free private parking is also available on site.
