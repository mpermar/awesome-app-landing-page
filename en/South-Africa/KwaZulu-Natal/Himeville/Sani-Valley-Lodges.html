Description

Located around a privately owned lake with views of the Drakensberg mountain range, Sani Valley Nature Lodges offers secluded chalet accommodation in a tranquil setting with game viewing opportunities.

Offering privacy, all chalets are individually decorated and offer views of the lake and the mountains.

Each chalet has a fully equipped kitchen, and a seating area with fireplace. Large glass sliding doors lead onto a wooden deck or patio.

Some of the units have spa bath. The property has a Clubhouse where meals can be arranged with prior notice at a surcharge.

Sani Valley Nature Lodges offers water sport facilities, a gift shop and a children's playground.

Massages and beauty treatments can be arranged. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling, hiking and canoeing.
