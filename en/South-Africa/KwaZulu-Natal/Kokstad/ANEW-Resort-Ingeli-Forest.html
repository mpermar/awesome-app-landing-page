Description

Situated midway between Harding and Kokstad, Anew Hotel Ingeli Forest and Spa is surrounded by a forest.

It offers several hiking and mountain biking trails. Also featured are a swimming pool and tennis court.

The rooms and cottages all come with satellite TV and tea-and-coffee making facilities.

The private bathrooms are equipped with a bath or shower. A hot buffet breakfast is served at Eagles Nest Restaurant, and Forrester’s Bar offers a variety of drinks.

Guests can enjoy hiking and mountain biking through the surrounding forest. Bicycles are available for rent.

Anew Hotel Ingeli Forest resort is located 25 km from Harding and 33 km from Kokstad.

Mount Currie Nature Reserve is 40 km away, and Budget Margate Airport is 115 km from the resort.
