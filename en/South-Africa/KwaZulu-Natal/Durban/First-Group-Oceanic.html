Description

Featuring a rooftop pool and restaurant, Oceanic offers self-catering accommodation in Durban.

The apartment is 1. 4 km from Durban ICC and uShaka Marine World is 3. 1 km away.

There is a games room on site and guests can enjoy drinks at the on-site bar. Each apartment has a lounge with satellite TV and a dining area.

The fully equipped kitchen includes a microwave, refrigerator and tea-and-coffee-making facilities.

Extras include free toiletries and a hair dryer. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property.

You can play pool at the resort. Marine Parade is 1. 3 km from Oceanic, while Moses Mabhida Stadium is 1.
