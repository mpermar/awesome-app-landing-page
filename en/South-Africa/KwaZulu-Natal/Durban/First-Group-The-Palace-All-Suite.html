Description

The Palace All-Suite is situated along Durban’s North Beach. This art deco styled resort features a rooftop pool and terrace.

uShaka Marine World is 2. 9 km from the property while the Durban ICC is a 15-minute walk away.

The modern and air-conditioned units come complete with satellite TV, as well as a kitchen.

The rooms offer a view and the private bathrooms have a bath or shower. At The Palace All-Suite, the restaurant and cocktail bar offers panoramic views of the Indian Ocean and Marine Parade, and offers an à la carte menu featuring a variety of Italian dishes.

Guests can relax in the spa which offers an array of treatments. The Durban ICC Arena is 1 km away and King Shaka International Airport is within 33 km.
