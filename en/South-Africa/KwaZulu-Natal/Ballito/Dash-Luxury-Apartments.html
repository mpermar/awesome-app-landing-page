Description

Situated on a secure Eco Estate, Dash Luxury Apartments features a heated swimming pool, sauna and steam room, and a fitness centre.

It offers access to the private Dunkirk Club House at Salt Rock Beach, a 5-minute drive away.

The air-conditioned apartments come with contemporary décor and terraces overlooking the Indian Ocean.

They also have free WiFi. The living space includes a lounge with satellite TV, a study area, dining table, and fully-equipped kitchen.

The apartments are self-catering but guests can also enjoy meals at Ray's Kitchen.

Beauty Therapy and tennis courts are also offered at Dash Luxury Apartments. Flag Animal Farm is 5.

7 km from the apartments, and the Simbithi Golf Course is 5 km away. King Shaka International Airport is located 25 km away.
