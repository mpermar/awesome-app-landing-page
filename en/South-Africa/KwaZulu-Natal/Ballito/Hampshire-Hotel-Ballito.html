Description

The Hampshire Hotel Ballito offers accommodation located on the famous Dolphin Coast, 1.

2 km from Ballito Junction Regional Mall and 19 km from King Shaka International Airport Hampshire Hotel Ballito offers tastefully decorated rooms which are fitted with custom duvets.

All rooms include flat-screen TVs and free Wi-Fi, and most rooms offer views of the outdoor pool.

A variety of South African and international dishes are served in the hotel lounge for breakfast.

Additional facilities include a well-equipped fitness centre, a spa, a swimming pool and hot tub.

Free guarded private parking is also available. It is a 10-minute drive from Hampshire Hotel Ballito to Umhlali Beach and Zimbali Country Club is 8.
