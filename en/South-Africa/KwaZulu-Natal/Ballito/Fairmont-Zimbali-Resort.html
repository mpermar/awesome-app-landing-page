Description

Located within the exclusive Zimbali Coastal Resort eco-estate, situated 50 km north of Durban, the Fairmont Zimbali Resort is a tranquil retreat offering the utmost in stylish comfort and excellent service.

Consisting 154 rooms and suites combine cosmopolitan flair with contemporary amenities.

Every room features Wi-Fi internet and a wide selection of TV channels. The large bathrooms provide complimentary Le Labo products and oversize dressing gowns.

The hotel features a luxurious spa, 3 outdoor swimming pools as well as 8 restaurants and bars.

Set inside a gated estate the hotel offers great security to its guests, while surrounded by lush flora, pristine forest and glistening sea.

Fairmont Zimbali Resort offers a range of dining opportunities such as casual poolside dining at Ayoba, Indian Fusion cuisine from OSA and the international flavours of Coral Tree Restaurant.

There is also a lobby bar and tea lounge where guests can relax in a luxurious atmosphere.

The hotel features 3 outdoor swimming pools, with serviced loungers, for your relaxation.

The Willow Stream Spa features 9 private treatment rooms, steam room and fitness centre.

Fairmont Zimbali Resort is conveniently located to Durban and Umhlanga, and Ballito is a 5 minute' drive away.
