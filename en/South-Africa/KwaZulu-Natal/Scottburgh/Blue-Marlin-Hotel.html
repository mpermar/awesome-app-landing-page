Description

The Blue Marlin Hotel is situated on KwaZulu-Natal’s popular South Coast, just 300 metres from the beach.

Set in sub-tropical gardens with an outdoor pool, this hotel offers panoramic ocean views.

All of the rooms at the Blue Marlin have a fan, TV and telephone. Some of the rooms offer views of the Indian Ocean.

The hotel’s facilities include both a swimming pool and a children’s pool. There’s also a children’s play area with a trampoline, swings and jungle gym.
