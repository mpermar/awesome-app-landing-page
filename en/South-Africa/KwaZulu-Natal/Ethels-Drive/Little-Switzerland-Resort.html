Description

Offering magnificent views of the Drakensberg mountain range, Little Switzerland Resort is located a 30-minute drive from Bergville.

The resort offers rooms and self-catering chalets. The accommodation will provide you with a seating area, heating, satellite TV and tea-and-coffee making facilities.

The chalets also offer a kitchen. At Little Switzerland Resort you will find an outdoor pool and a restaurant.

Other facilities offered at the property include a tennis court meeting facilities and games room.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including golfing, bird watching, cycling and horse riding.
