Description

Situated along the beach, Margate Sands is located in Margate and features an outdoor swimming pool, restaurant and a kids’ club.

Featuring sea views, the apartment offers 3 bedrooms, an open plan lounge and dining area, as well as a kitchen.

Extras include a satellite TV, DVD player and a fan. The private bathroom has a bath or shower.

At Margate Sands you will find barbecue facilities and the Rondevoux Shopping Mall is a 2-minute walk away.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including mini golf, walking along the beach and fishing.

The Wild Coast Casino is 35 km away and the King Shaka International Airport can be reached in 169 km.
