Description

Set in Margate in South Africa's KwaZulu-Natal Region, Guest House features an outdoor pool and views of the sea.

The guest house has a barbecue and hot tub, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Every room has a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Some units have a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

You will find a kettle in the room. All rooms are equipped with a private bathroom or an en suite bathroom.

For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer. You will find a shared lounge at the property.

Limited free WiFi is featured and free private parking is available on site. Hibiscus Mall Centre is 1 km away and Port Shepstone is within 30 minutes' drive.

Durban is 140 km from Albatros Guest House and the nearest Airport is King Shaka International Airport, 174 km away.
