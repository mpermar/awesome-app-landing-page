Description

All guest rooms come with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, fridge, a coffee machine, a bath, free toiletries and a desk. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a shower and a hair dryer, while certain rooms have a seating area. All rooms at the hotel feature air conditioning and a closet.

A Full English/Irish breakfast is served each morning at the property.

Spa and wellness facilities including a sauna and an indoor pool are at guests' disposal during their stay at Hotel Fordoun.

Tourist Information Centre (Nottingham) is 2.7 miles from the accommodation.
