Description

Nottingham Road Hotel is a historical landmark known as Notties by many generations of locals.

It is situated in the village of Nottingham Road, in the heart of the Midlands Meander.

Each classic room comes equipped with a satellite TV and tea-and-coffee-making facilities.

The private bathroom has a bath or shower and free toiletries are provided. There is an à la carte restaurant at Nottingham Road, and guests can enjoy a pub meal while they watch sports events on the big screen in Notties pub.

Picnic baskets are available on request. The staff at the hotel can arrange for guests to take part in activities such as fly-fishing, golf, 4x4 trails and forest walks in the area.

Tours of the Battlefields and the Sani Pass can also be arranged. Free Wi-Fi and and private parking is available.

The King Shaka International Airport is 162 km from the hotel and Durban is 137 km away.
