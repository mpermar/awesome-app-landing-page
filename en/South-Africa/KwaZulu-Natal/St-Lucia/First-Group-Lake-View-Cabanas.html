Description

Just off the mouth of the St Lucia Estuary and offering an outdoor pool and a restaurant, Lake View Cabanas is located in St Lucia.

The resort offers a children's playground and bicycle rental. The beach is 3 km away.

The apartments at Lake View Cabanas will provide you with a fully equipped kitchen, a seating area and a patio.

They are also equipped with a ceiling fan, satellite TV and DVD player. Featuring a bath or shower, private bathrooms also come with a hairdryer.

The property has a games room and 1 parking bay is available per unit. Activities in the surrounding area include nature trails, fishing, lake cruises and bird watching.

The Hluhluwe Umfolozi Reserve is 1 hour away by car, while Richards Bay is 78 km from the property.
