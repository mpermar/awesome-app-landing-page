Description

Located in St. Lucia within the iSimangaliso Wetland Park, Marlin Lodge St Lucia boasts an outdoor swimming pool, terrace area and barbecue facilities.

It features an entertainment area, where guest can relax and watch satellite TV. All rooms offer a patio which leads onto the garden area.

Each room is fitted with tiled floors, a fan and wardrobe. Some rooms come with air-conditioning.

Marlin Lodge St Lucia serves breakfast daily and it can be enjoyed outdoors on the terrace area or in the dining room.

A number of restaurants serving a range of cuisines can be found within 1 km. Hluhluwe-Imfolozi Game Reserve is 55 km away, whilst Cape Vidal is 34 km from the property.

Wi-Fi is available at a fee in all rooms and free private parking is available on site.
