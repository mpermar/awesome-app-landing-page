Description

iGwalagwala Guest House, situated in the village of St Lucia, is located in the iSimangaliso Wetland Park at the mouth of the St Lucia Estuary.

It features a swimming pool. Each guest room at iGwalagwala is air-conditioned and individually decorated.

Equipped with tea-and-coffee-making facilities, the rooms also come with a hairdryer.

The private bathrooms are each fitted with a shower. A light buffet breakfast or a full English breakfast is available at iGwalagwala.

Guests can relax in the lounge/TV room or have one of the staff members book an activity in the area such as Hippo and Crocodile Estuary Tours or horse riding and safaris.

Free WiFi and private parking is available. The King Shaka International Airport is 212 km away.
