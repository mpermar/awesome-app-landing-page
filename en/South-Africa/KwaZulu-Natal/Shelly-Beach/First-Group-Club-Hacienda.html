Description

Club Hacienda offers well-appointed, self-catering accommodation in Shelly Beach.

This beachfront accommodation has a terrace with BBQ facilities. There is also a swimming pool and a large garden where guests can relax and take in the surroundings.

The apartments at Club Hacienda are air-conditioned and include a lounge with satellite TV.

Enjoy a cup of tea from your terrace or patio, and for your convenience there are free toiletries and a hairdryer.

The kitchen is fully equipped and there is also a dining area. The resort offers mini-golf and a children's playground to keep young guests entertained.

Daily housekeeping is included. Club Hacienda is 2. 4km from Margate Country Club.
