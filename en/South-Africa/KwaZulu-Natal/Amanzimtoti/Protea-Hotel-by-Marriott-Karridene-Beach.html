Description

Located 20 minutes south of Durban, between the lagoon and the beach at Illovo, this hotel offers spacious rooms with free WiFi.

Protea Hotel by Marriott Karridene Beach features an outdoor pool and a spa. Each of the air-conditioned rooms is decorated with modern wooden furniture and carpeted floors.

All rooms are equipped with a flat-screen satellite TV, a work desk and an electric kettle.

Protea Hotel’s Oakwoods Restaurant is housed in a glass structure, overlooking the Umzimbazi lagoon and the lush Natal vegetation surrounding it.

A rich breakfast buffet, à la carte lunches and buffet style dinners are served daily.

Guests can choose from a variety of sports, including squash, mini golf, table tennis and volleyball.

Younger guests can use the small pool for kids. Spa treatments are available at SPA @ Karridene.

Aliwal Diving Centre and Galleria Shopping Mall are 10 km away. Ushaka marine World is a 30-minute drive away by car, and the hotel offers free private parking on site.
