Description

Guest rooms in the farm stay are equipped with a electric tea pot. Selected rooms contain a kitchen with an oven, a microwave and a toaster.

A Full English/Irish breakfast is available daily at the property.

Dalmore Guest Farm has an outdoor pool. Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Bergville, like cycling.

Information Bergville is 6 miles from Dalmore Guest Farm.
