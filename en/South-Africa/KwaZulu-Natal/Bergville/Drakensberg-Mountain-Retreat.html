Description

Set on a ridge overlooking the northern and central Drakensberg Mountains, Drakensberg Mountain Retreat features an indoor swimming pool, library, shared lounge area and conference facilities.

Each room at Drakensberg Mountain Retreat has an en suite bathroom, and complimentary toiletries are provided for your comfort.

Guests can enjoy home-cooked meals in the dining room. They can also relax by the swimming pool or in the garden.

The games room offers darts and a billiards table. Free Wi-Fi access is available in public areas.

Drakensberg Mountain Retreat is located 30 km outside of Bergville and is 35 km from the Amphitheatre Golf Club.

Harrismith is 40 km away and Royal Natal National Park is 50 km from the property.
