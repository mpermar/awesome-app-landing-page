Description

Featuring a 9-hole golf course, Gooderson Monks Cowl Golf Resort also offers an outdoor swimming pool, tennis courts, and a restaurant and pub.

It is situated in the Central Drakensberg region. The spacious rooms all come with satellite TV and tea-and-coffee making facilities.

Each en suite bathroom is equipped with a bath, shower and complimentary toiletries.

Guests are free to use the swimming pool and tennis courts. They can also enjoy meals in the restaurant and pub, which features a log fireplace in the winter.

A function room is also available for conferences and events. Activities such as river rafting, quad biking and horse riding can be found within 10 km of the golf resort.

Gooderson Monks Cowl is located 25 km outside of Winterton. The Winterton Train Station is 28 km away and Thaba-Tseka Airport is 30 km away.
