Description

Offering an outdoor pool, Shakaland Hotel & Zulu Cultural Village is located in Eshowe, where the TV mini-series Shaka Zulu is set.

Free WiFi access is available. The rooms are set in Zulu bee-hive huts and feature a private bathroom with a bath or shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

You can enjoy a garden view. Breakfast is included in the rate at Shakaland. Further meals can be enjoyed in the restaurant, and barbeque facilities are available.

At Shakaland Hotel & Zulu Cultural Village you will find a garden, a terrace and a bar.

Other facilities offered at the property include entertainment staff, a tour desk and luggage storage.
