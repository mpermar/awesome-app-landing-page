Description

Situated within 12 km from Mooi River, surrounded by meadows, Midlands Saddle and Trout offers an array of activities from fishing, mini golf and horse riding.

The resort features a restaurant, spa and children’s playground. At Midlands Saddle and Trout, the spacious apartments offer views of the mountain and come complete with an open plan lounge, dining area and fully equipped kitchen which include a dishwasher.

Extras include a satellite TV, DVD player and linen. The Dancing Bushman is the resort’s cosy restaurant which offers tasty dishes.

Guests can relax at the poolside or enjoy a refreshing swim in the large pool. The spa features a range of treatments.
