Description

Located within 8 km from the Robberg Nature Reserve and Plettenberg Bay, T'Niqua Stable Inn features country-style accommodation.

It features a garden, sea views and horse riding. The individually decorated rooms and suites are fitted with a flat-screen TV and tea-and-coffee making facilities.

The family stable suites have an upstairs loft area for children. Guests can relax and admire the sea view from the Western Saloon, a chique breakfast and dining area with a cozy log fire in winter.

There is also a boma with a big log fire in the evenings. The surrounding area boasts scenic views and activities range from hiking, bird watching and cycling.

The property has percheron horses and offers coach rides. The property can host weddings, small functions and team buildings.

Free private parking is available on site. Plettenberg Bay Country Club is within 15 minutes drive.
