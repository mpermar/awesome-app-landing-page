Description

Bella Manga Country Escape is surrounded by the Tsitsikamma Mountains. It features 2 large outdoor pools.

Plettenberg Bay with its beaches is a 20-minute drive away. Rooms and self-catering cottages are available as well Bella Manga Country Escape traditional, round Rondavel houses.

The forest lodges are set apart for privacy. All units offer a small fridge and some of them have a TV.

Guests can enjoy a drink from the honesty bar and relax in the Old Dairy lounge and TV room.

The Fire Pit in the garden also offers a unique setting for enjoying aperitifs and a traditional braai barbecue.

Walking can be enjoyed on the property and small game and birds can be often be found.

Additional facilities include a business centre, a guest lounge with flat-screen satellite TV, and secure private parking.

Free WiFi is available in the rooms and public areas of the main guest house. Bella Manga also offers a function and wedding venue.
