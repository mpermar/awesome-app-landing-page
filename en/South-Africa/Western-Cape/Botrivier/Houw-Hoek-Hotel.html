Description

Surrounded by mountain vistas, rolling lawns and meandering streams, country-style Houw Hoek Hotel is located between the towns of Grabouw and Botrivier.

The well-appointed rooms at Houw Hoek offer satellite TV, a seating area and an en suite bathroom.

Tea-and-coffee making facilities are available in each room. The main restaurant serves breakfast, à la carte lunches and dinners.

Guest can enjoy meals or a drink on the terrace with views of the magnificent surrounding mountains.

The hotel has 2 outdoor pools, a wedding venue and conference facilities for up to 250 delegates.
