Description

Offering an authentic African haven, Protea Hotel by Marriott Oudtshoorn Riempie Estate is a privately owned hotel surrounded by greenery, right in the heart of Klein Karoo, Oudtshoorn.

Protea Hotel by Marriott Oudtshoorn Riempie Estate offers the chance to live in chalets and rondavels with individual private entrances.

All living units are spacious, charmingly decorated to reflect the local culture and contain en suites, air conditioning, a satellite TV, a bath and/or shower, and tea/coffee making facilities.

There is an outdoor pool and a large terrace as well as a children's playground. Packed lunches can be prepared upon request and guests enjoy personalized service that include babysitting and room service.

Traditional South African dishes are served in the charming restaurant and authentic South African BBQ can be arranged for groups larger than 15 guests.

Riempie has a quiet and relaxed atmosphere, perfect for a relaxed holiday. Guests can visit the Cango Wildlife Ranch and Valley of Ancients, numerous Ostrich Show Farms and Cango Caves.
