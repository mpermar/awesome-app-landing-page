Description

Set 16 km from Oudtshoorn, Berluda offers charming accommodation in country-style cottages located in the scenic Schoemanshoek Valley, 15 km from the Cango Caves.

It features 2 swimming pools. Cottages and rooms at Berluda Farmhouse and Cottages open out onto private balconies or panoramic patios.

Some have well-equipped kitchens. The restaurant offers breakfast and dinner in the old tobacco barn.

Guests have the opportunity to experience the farming activities of a working ostrich farm at Berluda.

Guests at Berluda can enjoy activities such as sunset drives on the farm and the Meerkat Tour, offering guests a close encounter with the interesting creatures.
