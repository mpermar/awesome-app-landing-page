Description

Located with the backdrop of the Swartberg Mountains, Oude Meul is en route to the Cango Caves.

The property features 2 pools among extensive native gardens. Apartments at De Oude Meul Country Lodge open on to furnished verandas overlooking the natural surroundings.

They are all air conditioned, provide free Wi-Fi access and private entrance. Guests will enjoy the hotel’s restaurant, set on the banks of the Grobbelaars River and serving local specialities, including a wide variety of venison.

BBQ facilities are also located outdoor on verandas and in the gardens. Staff can advise on nearby bird watching oasis and naturalistic tours on 4x4 available on prior request.

The property has a seasonal ostrich farm. Central Oudtshoorn is a 10-minute drive from De Oude Meul and free parking is available on site.
