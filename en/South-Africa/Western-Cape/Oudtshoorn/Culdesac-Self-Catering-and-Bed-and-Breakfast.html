Description

Located in the heart of Klein Karoo, Oudtshoorn, this guest house offers elegant accommodation with free Wi-Fi.

Nested between the breathtaking Swartberg and Outeniqua mountains, it features an outdoor pool and barbecue facilities.

Culdesac Self Catering and Bed & Breakfast has traditionally decorated rooms with carpeted floors.

Each of the air-conditioned rooms has a private entrance, a satellite TV and a patio.

Guests can start their day with a farm style breakfast served in the privacy of their room, including home made bread.

Traditional Klein Karoo meals can also be enjoyed by prior arrangement. With a stretch of agricultural land bordering on the guest house, you can view the ostriches from your room.

You can also relax on the sun loungers, by the pool, while younger guests are having fun in the children’s playground.

George Airport is 50 km away, and free private parking is available on site Culdesac.
