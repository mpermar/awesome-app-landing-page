Description

Set in a forest and nestled among the trees, Moon Shine on Whiskey Creek offers self-catering accommodation in wooden cabins, just toff the N2 national road and 15 km away from Plettenberg Bay.

The units are equipped with kitchen facilities, fireplace and seating area. Some of the bathrooms are fitted with a mosaic shower.

Barbecue facilities are available. Moon Shine on Whiskey Creek offers a swimming pool and a children's playground.

A 30-minute forest hike takes you to a mountain pool. Other activities offered on site or in the surrounding area include bird watching, canoeing and horse riding.

Free WiFi is available in all rooms. Birds of Eden and Monkeyland are both within 10-minutes' drive.
