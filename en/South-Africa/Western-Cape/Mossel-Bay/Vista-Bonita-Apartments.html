Description

Situated in Mossel Bay, Vista Bonita Apartments offer a balcony and sea view. The Diaz Waterpark is a short walk from the property.

Free private parking is available on site. Each unit features a dining area and a kitchen, equipped with an oven.

A microwave and kettle are also available. Bed linen is offered. A restaurant is situated on the ground floor and Langeberg Mall is within 5 minutes' drive.
