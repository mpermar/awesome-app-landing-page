Description

Located in central Franschhoek, The Corner House offers en-suite rooms and a wrap-around verandah overlooking the garden and sparkling pool.

The historical Huguenot Monument is only 5 minutes’ walk from the guest house. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

The rooms each offer a ceiling fan, tea-and-coffee-making facilities and en-suite bathroom with shower.

All of the rooms have a TV. Breakfast is served in the breakfast room or outside on the verandah.

A variety of fine dining restaurants are situated along the Main Road, in walking distance from The Corner House.

The property has a library which can also be used as a conference room for 8 people.

Activities in the area include wine tasting, golf, hiking and horse riding. La Petite Ferme Wine Estate is 1.

3 km and Pearl Valley Golf Estate about 18 km away. Graham Beck Winery is a 12-minute drive from The Corner House and the Mont Rochelle Nature Reserve is 7 km away.
