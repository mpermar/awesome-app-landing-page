Description

Housed in a typical Dutch-style working farm dating back from 1698, this luxurious guest house, enclosed by vineyards and fruit trees, welcomes guests to experience genuine hospitality in a tranquil setting.

A 5-minute drive from Paarl town centre, De Leeuwenhof Estate consists of an historic main building, a beautifully restored farm building and 12 hectares of land.

Start the day with optional breakfast on the terrace and marvel at stunning views of the area.

During the summer months De Leeuwenhof Estate Restaurant offers a set 3-course menu.

Your hosts are happy to arrange a private tour of the farm, wine-tasting or traditional braai for you.

Afterwards, you can enjoy a swim in the pool or relax in the comfort of your beautifully furnished guest room or apartment.
