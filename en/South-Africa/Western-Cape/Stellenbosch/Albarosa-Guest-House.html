Description

Located in the heart of Stellenbosch, this guest house is situated a 5 minutesâ€™ drive from the Jonkershoek Nature Reserve.

It offers a plunge pool and stylish rooms with LCD TVs and free WiFi. Individually designed and fitted with wooden floors and tasteful furnishings, all rooms at Albarosa Guest House feature tea-and-coffee making facilities.

A healthy breakfast is served each morning in the elegant dining room. When weather is nice, guests can relax on the sunny patio.

For chilly days there is a lounge with fireplace and a library. Albarosa Guest House is within walking distance to town centre with its many restaurants, cafes and museums.

Wine tasting can be enjoyed in the surrounding areas and Cape Town International Airport is 40 km away.
