Description

Located beside the Eerste River, in the heart of the Stellenbosch wine-growing region, this 4-star hotel offers spacious rooms with a shaded outdoor patio.

Facilities include an outdoor pool. The rooms at Spier Hotel are situated in village-style buildings, grouped around 6 private courtyards.

All rooms have satellite TV, and a bathroom with a bath and separate shower. Each room is decorated with contemporary artworks from the Spier Collection.

The on-site travel desk can help arrange a selection of activities and tours. The Spa at Spier offers a variety of health and beauty treatments.

Restaurant Eight offers gourmet cuisine made with fresh, regional produce. At the popular wine bar and terrace, guests can enjoy award-winning wines, and healthy light lunches and bar snacks.

Wine tastings are offered at the Wine Shop, which is housed in an original Cape Dutch gabled barn dating back to 1750.

Guests can also purchase Spier’s own wines here. Cape Town International Airport is a 20-minute drive from Spier Hotel, and the beaches of Gordon's Bay and the Strand are a 15-minute drive away.
