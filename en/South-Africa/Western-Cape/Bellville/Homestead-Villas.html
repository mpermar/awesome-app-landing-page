Description

Nestled next to Tygerberg Nature Reserve against the slopes of Tygerberg Hills, Homestead Villas offers modern suites with LCD TV and features an outdoor swimming pool and lounge.

Each of the 10 suites has air conditioning, LCD TV with selected satellite channels, DVD player, Wi-Fi access and hair dryer.

The bathrooms feature under-floor heating and heated towel rails. Homestead Villas features views of the Boland Mountains.

The lounge offers a relaxed setting for socialising and meetings. It features comfortable sofas and a wood-burning fireplace.

There is also a stylish bar with French oak furnishings. Homestead’s clubhouse offers a billiard room, a library, and an equipped meeting and conference venue.

Homestead Villas is located in the Northern suburbs of Cape Town. It is just 25 minutes from Cape Town’s central business district.
