Description

Carmel Guest Farm offers accommodation in George. The hotel has a year-round outdoor pool and views of the sea, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Some rooms feature a seating area where you can relax. A terrace or patio are featured in certain rooms.

Each room is fitted with a private or shared bathroom. There is a shared lounge at the property.
