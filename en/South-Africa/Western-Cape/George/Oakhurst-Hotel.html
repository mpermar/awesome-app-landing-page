Description

Centrally situated in the Garden Route and George's business centre, Oakhurst Hotel offers comfortable accommodation that are ideal for travellers wishing to combine business with pleasure.

WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge. Oakhurst provide charmingly decorated en suite rooms, featuring vaulted ceilings, bright fabrics, hand painted ceramics and original oil paintings.

All units include a TV, tea/coffee making facilities, and same day laundry & dry cleaning service.

The Oakhurst Hotel has an intimate, home-away-from-home atmosphere in its comfortable interiors that are full of country charm, which is emphasized by the personal and efficient service of a friendly staff.

Guests can make use of the outdoor swimming pool or simply enjoy a drink at the bar while drinking from a wide range of wines.

There is a full-service restaurant that serves a wide variety of culinary treats for candlelight dinners and breakfast.

The hotel is conveniently located just 10 km from the George Airport, as well as within walking distance of shops, banks and restaurants.
