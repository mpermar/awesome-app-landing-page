Description

Just 50 metres from the beach, Paternoster Manor features elegantly decorated rooms.

Free WiFi access is available. Each room here will provide you with a satellite TV, tea-and-coffee making facilities and a private bathroom with complimentary toiletries.

Other facilities onsite include a hydrotherapy tub, internal courtyard, library room and fitness room.

An array of activities can be enjoyed in the surrounding area, including cycling, fishing, hiking and bird watching.
