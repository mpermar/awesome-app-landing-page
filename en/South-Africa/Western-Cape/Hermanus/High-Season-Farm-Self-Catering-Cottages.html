Description

High Season Farm is located in Hermanus. Free WiFi access is available in this farm stay.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including hiking.

The accommodation will provide you with a TV and a balcony. Featuring a bath, private bathroom also comes with a shower and a hairdryer.

You can enjoy mountain view and garden view from the room. There is a full kitchen with a dishwasher and a microwave.

At High Season Farm you will find a garden and barbecue facilities. The property offers free parking There is limited cellphone reception on the farm.

Free WiFi access is available in all cottages. If you feel like visiting the surroundings, check out La Vierge Winery (700 metres) and Hamilton Russel Vineyards (1.
