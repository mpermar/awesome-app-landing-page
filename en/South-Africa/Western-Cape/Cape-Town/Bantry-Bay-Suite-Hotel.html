Description

Situated between the V&A Waterfront and Camps Bay, Bantry Bay Suite Hotel offers self-catering suites with air conditioning.

It features an outdoor pool, a restaurant, and a 24-hour reception. The Bantry Bay's spacious suites include a kitchenette with a microwave and tea/coffee maker.

Each suite also has a seating area with a flat-screen satellite TV. Floor-to-ceiling windows and wooden furnishings are part of the modern classic d√©cor.

A traditional South African breakfast is served in the Atlantic Restaurant. Light lunches, dinners and freshly made cocktails are available at Freddie's Bar & Lounge.

Bike and car rentals are offered by Bantry Bay Suite Hotel. Guests can hike or cycle along Table Mountain paths.

They can also relax on a lounger by the pool. Saunders Rock, Queens, Sunset, Broken Path and Milton beaches are situated in front of the Bantry Bay Suite Hotel.

Clifton is a 2-minute drive away and the V&A Waterfront and the Capetown Stadium are a 5-minute drive away.
