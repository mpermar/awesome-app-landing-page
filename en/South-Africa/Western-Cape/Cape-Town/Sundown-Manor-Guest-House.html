Description

This guesthouse is located in Fresnaye beneath the Lions Head Mountain and just 200 metres from Sea Point promenade.

It offers modern accommodation in relaxing surroundings. Sundown Manor is a 10-minute drive from V & A Waterfront and the convention centre.

It is just 5 minutes' drive from the famous Camps Bay and Clifton Beaches. All of Sundown Manors rooms feature king-size beds or twin beds and en suite bathrooms are fitted with spacious showers.

Free Wi-Fi is available in every room. Sundown Manor has a rooftop terrace, and a patio garden with an outdoor pool and sun loungers.
