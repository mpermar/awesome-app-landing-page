Description

Situated in Green Point, close to Cape Town's famous V&A Waterfront is this 5-star aparthotel offering guests the convenience of homely comfort coupled with excellent service.

The modern and sophisticated apartments include a kitchen, full en-suite bathrooms, spacious living rooms and outdoor balconies with panoramic views.

Wireless Internet access is available. Romney Park offers a club room serving breakfast.

Enjoy a relaxing swim in the stunning outdoor pool or soak up the sun on a deck lounger with a refreshing drink.

Venture out to the near by golf course or enjoy delightful dishes at one of the outstanding restaurants.

Alternatively, enjoy Green Point's lively pubs, clubs and cabaret restaurants. For your convenience, Romney Park has 24 hour, 7 days a week assistance.
