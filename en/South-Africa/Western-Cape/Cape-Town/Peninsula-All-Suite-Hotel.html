Description

Overlooking the bay, Peninsula All Suite Hotel is a short walk from the beach and a 10-minute drive from Camps Bay.

If offers free WiFi throughout and boasts 2 pools, a garden and a bar. Featuring a balcony with a sea view, all accommodation at Peninsula All Suite is air-conditioned and furnished with a modern décor.

Each suite includes an open-plan seating area with a flat-screen TV, a kitchenette and a private bathroom.

You can dine at the alfresco Sunset Restaurant or have a snack or beverage at the Sunset Deck.

Breakfast can be served in the rooms, and a 24-hour room service is available. The hotel also has a gym and a sauna.

Peninsula All Suite Hotel provides a free shuttle service to V&A Waterfront, City Centre, Table Mountain, Clifton and Camps Bay.
