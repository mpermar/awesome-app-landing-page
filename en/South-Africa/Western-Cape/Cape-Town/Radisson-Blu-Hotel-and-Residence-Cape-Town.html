Description

Located in Cape Town's city centre, Radisson Blu Hotel & Residence offers accommodation within a 10-minute walk of the Cape Town International Convention Centre.

Free high-speed WiFi is available. Radisson Blu Hotel & Residence, Cape Town offers rooms equipped with a smart TV, a mini fridge and a butler station.

Each room is air-conditioned and has a bathroom with a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Some rooms offer access to the Business Class Lounge. Guests at Radisson Blu can enjoy a light lunch or a gourmet pizza at Ghibli Bar and Pool Terrace, while The Sea Street Bar and Lounge is a good place to get a morning coffee or evening cocktail.

24-hour room service is also available. The hotel also boasts a swimming pool, terrace and fitness centre.

There is a 24-hour concierge desk, as well as complimentary shuttle services to the V&A Waterfront, 2.

5 km from Radisson Blu. Table Mountain is a 15-minute drive from the hotel, while a number of bars and restaurants are within walking distance.
