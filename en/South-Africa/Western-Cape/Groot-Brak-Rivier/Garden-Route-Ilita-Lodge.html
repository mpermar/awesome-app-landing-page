Description

Ilita Lodge offers panoramic Southern Ocean views along the Garden Route on a hill overlooking Great Brak River.

Rooms have private balconies with sea views and there are self-catering apartments.

There is an outdoor pool. All air-conditioned rooms feature a furnished seating area with satellite TV.

Each has a minibar and a private bathroom. Apartments have a kitchen and dining area.

A full English breakfast or continental option is available in the dining room. There is a bar and executive lounge area for drinks.

A sun terrace with sun-loungers surrounds the pool and offers garden and sea views.

Ilita also features a fitness centre. George Airport is located 22 km away by car.
