Description

This guest house occupies a secluded location on the Garden Route. It provides far-reaching views across Knysna Bay and the beach is just a few minutes’ walk away.

Free WiFi is available. All rooms at Augusta Bay Guest House feature large living spaces and some offer views across the estuary towards the Outeniqua Mountains.

Each room is individually decorated and equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV and an en suite bathroom.

Guests of Augusta Bay have the choice of English or Continental breakfast. There are many recreation activities available locally, including golf, abseiling, hiking and white water rafting, and the guest house staff can help arrange trips.

Alternatively guests can discover many local boutiques, markets and art galleries in the village centre, 5 minutes’ drive away.
