Description

The rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Certain units have a sitting area to relax in after a busy day. Views of the sea, mountains or pool are featured in certain rooms. Each room is equipped with a private bathroom. Extras include slippers and free toiletries.

There is free shuttle service at the property.

The hotel also provides car rental. Playpark is 0.8 miles from Waterstone Lodge, and Methodist Church is 0.9 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Cape Town International Airport, 16 miles from Waterstone Lodge.
