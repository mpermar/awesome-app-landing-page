Description

Hermitage Huisies offers accommodation within a 5-minute drive of the centre of Swellendam.

This property boasts expansive gardens and mountain views. Free WiFi is available.

Each unit at Hermitage Huisies has a kitchen, a dining area, heating panels, fans, and private BBQ facilities.

The beds are fitted with luxury, pocket-spring mattresses, and linen and towels are provided.

Some units offer a TV. Guests can enjoy the many horses on the farm, as well as a dam, play area, a trickling stream and green pastures.

Various activities in the surroundings include golf, hiking, cycling, berry picking, honey tasting and horse riding.
