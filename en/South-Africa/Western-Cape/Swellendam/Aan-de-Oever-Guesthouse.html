Description

Nestled between oak trees at the foot of the Langeberg Mountains, Aan de Oever Guesthouse offers accommodation in Swellendam.

Offering a private entrance, each room at this Cape Dutch-style guesthouse is elegantly furnished and has an en suite bathroom.

Guests can start their day with breakfast and end off with a sundowner from the honesty bar.

Aan de Oever features a swimming pool, deck and BBQ area. The guesthouse is within walking distance of the Drostdy Museum, restaurants and shops.
