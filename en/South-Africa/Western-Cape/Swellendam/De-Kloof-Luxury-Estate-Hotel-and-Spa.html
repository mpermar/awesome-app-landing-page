Description

De Kloof Luxury Estate, this refurbished 5-star boutique hotel and Cape Dutch Landmark, is just a few minutes walk into the heart of Swellendam.

It has a 12-metre outdoor pool and spa with panoramic mountain views. The modern and spacious garden suites offer designer bathrooms, free WiFi and air conditioning.

Some of the suites have waterbeds, a spa bath and a fireplace. Restaurant 18 Zero One serves global fusion gourmet meals using local ingredients from the estate’s organic gardens.

Guests can enjoy a glass of award winning wine at the large pool area with panoramic mountain views or book a relaxing massage.

De Kloof Luxury Estate has a koi pond, and a dam with geese and ducks. The Bontebok National Park is 7 km away and Swellendam Golf Course can be reached within 5 minutes by car.
