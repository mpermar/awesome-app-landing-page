Description

Set within the Noordhoek Farm Village shopping and dining complex, De Noordhoek Lifestyle Hotel features views of the surrounding mountains.

It has an outdoor pool, spacious rooms and sushi bar. Overlooking the garden or Noordhoek Mountains, the air-conditioned rooms provide free Wi-Fi.

Each includes a satellite TV, extra long beds and a modern, private bathroom. Some are interconnecting, while others offer disability access.

A hot and cold buffet breakfast is served in the à la carte restaurant overlooking the courtyard.

Room service and packed lunches can be arranged. The hotel also has a bar with a wood-beamed ceiling.

There are sunloungers and a children’s playground in the garden, and the indoor lounge has an open fireplace.

The 24-hour reception staff can arrange massages. De Noordhoek Hotel is a 6-minute drive from Noordhoek Beach and within 10 minutes’ drive of some Cape Point vineyards.
