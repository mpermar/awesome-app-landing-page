Description

Lanseria Country Estate offers country-style accommodation and is located 6 km from Lanseria International Airport.

It features a terrace overlooking the dam and garden, an outdoor swimming pool and barbecue facilities.

The modern furnished units feature a satellite TV, tea-and-coffee-making facilities and kitchen or kitchenette.

The bathroom includes free toiletries and some units come with a seating area. Free WiFi is available in the rooms.

Lanseria Country Estate serves breakfast in the dining area or on the deck which overlooks the dam.

Lunch and dinner can be enjoyed at the on-site restaurant, @ThePlace2B. Guests can enjoy a refreshing swim in the pool or play a game of golf at the nearby Blair Atholl Golf Estate which is 15 minutes drive away.

Activities such as quad biking and river rafting are in close proximity and can be arranged.

Sterkfontein Caves and the Rhino and Lion Nature Reserve is within 26 km. The OR Tambo International Airport is 56 km away.

Free airport transfers between the property and HLA Lanseria International Airport are available.
