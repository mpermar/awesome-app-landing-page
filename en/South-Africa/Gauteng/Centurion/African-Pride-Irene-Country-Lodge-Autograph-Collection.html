Description

In its rustic setting in Irene, a 30-minute drive from Pretoria, this lodge offers charming rooms facing a lake and 24-hour room service.

There is an outdoor pool with a hot tub. The spacious en-suite rooms at Irene Country Lodge are decorated in an English-country style with wooden furniture.

The rooms include a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private terrace.

Guests can enjoy their meals at the restaurant, which is available at least 16 hours per day for snacks, drinks and complete meals.

The 24-hour front desk can help with the booking of massages. Guests of the Irene Country Lodge can admire the many species of birds around the property or explore the surroundings by train or car.
