Description

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool and BBQ area, Glen Marion Guest House is conveniently located just 1.

6 km from Menlyn Park Shopping Centre. It also offers free Wi-Fi in all areas. The rooms come with a balcony, modern décor and seating area with satellite TV.

They also include a minibar and electric kettle. A hairdryer and complimentary toiletries are also available.

Guests can relax by the pool or in one of the four lounge areas. They can also get drinks at the honesty bar.

Game Drives to Kruger Park and Pilanesberg can be arranged upon request. Glen Marion Guest House is located 10 km from the Pretoria Botanical Garden, and 15 km from downtown Pretoria.

Sun Time Square Casino and Sun Arena is 800 meter away, while Menlyn Maine is 1. 1 km away.

The Union Buildings are also 15 km away and the Wonderboom Aerodrome is 30 km away.
