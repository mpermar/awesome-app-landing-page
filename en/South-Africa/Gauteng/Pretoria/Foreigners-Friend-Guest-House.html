Description

Offering an outdoor pool in a garden with rich birdlife, Foreigners Friend is a thatched cottage in a private Pretoria suburb.

With free Wi-Fi, the spacious rooms have extra-long beds and wooden or thatched ceilings.

Overlooking the garden, each air-conditioned room has a satellite TV and kitchenette with microwave.

Some include a hot tub in the private bathroom, and a large seating and dining area.

Breakfast is served daily on the open-air terrace or in the breakfast room. There is a communal braai barbecue, and dinner and packed lunches can be arranged.

A fireplace features in the lounge. Bicycle rental and darts are available, and Silver Lakes Golf Course and Pretoria Country Club are within a 15-minute drive.

The University of Pretoria, CSIR and Loftus Stadium are within a 10-minute drive, while the city centre is an additional 5 minutes from Foreigners Friend Guest House.

There is free parking and a shuttle is available to Johannesburg, Lanseria and Pretoria airports.
