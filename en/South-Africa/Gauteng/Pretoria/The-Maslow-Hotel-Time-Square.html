Description

Set in Menlyn and within 300 metres of Menlyn Maine Central Square, The Maslow Hotel, Time Square features a casino, non-smoking rooms, and free WiFi throughout the property.

Among the various facilities are a garden, numerous bars, as well as various restaurants.

The hotel features an outdoor swimming pool and a 24-hour front desk. At the hotel all rooms are fitted with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kitchenette, a dining area and a private bathroom with free toiletries, a shower and a hairdryer.

Guest rooms feature a desk. Guests at The Maslow Time Square can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

3 km away. The nearest airport is O. R. Tambo International Airport, 43 km from the property.
