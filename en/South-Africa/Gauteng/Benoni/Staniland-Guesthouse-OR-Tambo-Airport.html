Description

The rooms in the guesthouse are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. The rooms come with a private bathroom and free toiletries, and selected rooms also feature a balcony. Guest rooms will provide guests with a desk and a electric tea pot.

A continental breakfast is available daily at Staniland Guesthouse.

The accommodation has a playground. Guests at Staniland Guesthouse - OR Tambo Airport will be able to enjoy activities in and around Benoni, like cycling.

Ski World is 2.9 miles from the guesthouse, while Glynnwood Hospital is 3.1 miles away. O.R. Tambo International Airport is 7 miles from the property.
