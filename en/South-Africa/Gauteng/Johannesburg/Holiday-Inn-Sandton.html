Description

Situated in Johannesburg, Holiday Inn Sandton is less than 1 km from Sandton Convention Centre and just a 5-minute walk from the Sandton Gautrain Station.

It has a rooftop pool with panoramic views, and offers air-conditioned guestrooms with flat-screen TVs.

Rooms at the Holiday Inn Sandton have modern wood furnishings and include blackout curtains.

They come equipped with a work desk and a coffee maker. The private bathrooms have granite countertops and a hairdryer.

The Lettuce & Lime restaurant serves a buffet breakfast and a la carte lunch and dinners.

Relax in the cafe-style Chairman's Bar or enjoy a sundowner at the popular Skye Bar, offering sweeping city views.

Sandton guests can relax in a sun-lounger poolside, or visit the fitness centre which includes free weights and cardiovascular equipment.

The Holiday Inn tour desk provides information on local attractions. The Holiday Inn Sandton is located within a 10-minute walk from the famous Nelson Mandela Square, a unique shopping plaza with a range of restaurants, shops and entertainment venues.
