Description

Guest rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a electric tea pot, a shower, free toiletries and a desk. All rooms include a private bathroom with a hair dryer.

The hotel offers a continental or buffet breakfast. There's an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of local dishes.

The Capital on the Park has a playground.

The accommodation offers a business center for guest use. Around-the-clock assistance is available at the reception.

Gold Reef City & Casino is 10 miles from The Capital on the Park. The nearest airport is O.R. Tambo International Airport, 11 miles from the property.
