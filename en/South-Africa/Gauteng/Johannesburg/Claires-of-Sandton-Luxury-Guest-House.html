Description

This secure boutique guest house is a 5-minute drive from Sandton and features a garden with a pool and veranda.

There is free WiFi available on site. All rooms are decorated in warm colours at Claires of Sandton come with elegant furniture and overlooks the pool or garden.

Each air-conditioned room has a LCD TV, tea/coffee maker, microwave and kitchenware to prepare snacks or small meals.

A freshly prepared breakfast is served daily in the dining room or on the covered veranda.

A variety of restaurants are situated in Sandton’s direct surroundings. With free secured parking, guests can drive up to Nelson Mandela Square (3 km) or Montecasino (8 km).
