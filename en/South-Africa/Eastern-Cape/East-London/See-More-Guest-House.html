Description

Set in tropical gardens with rich birdlife, See More offers rooms with views towards the Indian Ocean.

A sun terrace surrounds the pool and there is a games room with dart board. Free Wi-Fi and parking are available.

A variety of bed and breakfast or self-catering accommodation options are on offer.

They all benefit from homely furnishings, a TV with DVD player and an en-suite bathroom.

Some have a balcony or terrace. Breakfast is served every morning, while lunch, dinner and packed lunches can be arranged.

A snack bar and barbecue facilities are featured and a few restaurants can be found within a 10-minute drive.
