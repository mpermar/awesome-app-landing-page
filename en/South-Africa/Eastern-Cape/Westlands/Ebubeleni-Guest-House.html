Description

Guest rooms include a seating area, a TV with satellite channels, a kitchenette, a dining area and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a shower. Rooms contain a electric tea pot, while some have a balcony. All guest rooms feature a closet.

Guests at the guesthouse can enjoy a continental or a Full English/Irish breakfast.

The property has a hot tub. You can play ping pong, pool, and darts at Ebubeleni Guest House.

The nearest airport is Port Elizabeth International Airport, 13 miles from the property.
