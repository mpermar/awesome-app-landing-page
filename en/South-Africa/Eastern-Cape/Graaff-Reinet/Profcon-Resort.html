Description

The rooms has air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a microwave, a electric tea pot, a shower and a closet. Some rooms also feature a kitchenette with a fridge. All rooms are equipped with a seating area.

Guests at the resort can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

Profcon Resort has a playground. You can play ping pong at the accommodation.

Popular points of interest near Profcon Resort include Assemblies of God, Union High School and Historical Church.
