Description

Situated along the coastline of the Eastern Cape, Hole in the Wall is situated in Coffee Bay and the resort overlooks the iconic landmark of Hole in the Wall.

The self-catering, thatched roof apartments come fitted an open plan, well-equipped kitchen and a lounge area with a double sofa sleeper couch.

Extras include fan, towels, bed linen and free toiletries. The restaurant at Hole in the Wall offers an la carte menu and the bar serves a variety of drinks.

The resort features an outdoor swimming pool, a childrens playground and BBQ facilities.

The spa has a selection of treatments. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including fishing and hiking.
