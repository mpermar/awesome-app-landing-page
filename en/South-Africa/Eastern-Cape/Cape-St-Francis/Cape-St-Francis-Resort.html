Description

Located in Francis Bay, Cape St Francis Resort offers spacious apartments with balconies overlooking the garden or the outdoor pool.

The resort features an open plan lounge and braai facilities. Cape St Francis Resort offers spacious one or two roomed apartments with balconies overlooking the garden or outdoor pool, family cottages in a garden setting as well as luxury beach villas.

Cape St Francis Resort’s apartments have a private entrance, a seating area and a fully equipped kitchen.

Each room is decorated with wooden furniture in neutral tones and tiled floors. The apartments are close to the restaurant and bar.

Special diet menus and packed lunches are also available. You can choose from a variety of sports, including diving, fishing, hiking and windsurfing.

Cape St Francis beach is only a short walk away from the apartments. Port Elizabeth Airport is 112 km away, and the resort offers an airport shuttle and free private parking on site.
