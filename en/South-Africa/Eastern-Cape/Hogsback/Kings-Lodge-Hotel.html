Description

Located in Hogsback Nature Reserve, Kings Lodge Hotel offers individually furnished accommodations.

Its outdoor pool is surrounded by landscaped gardens. There is a barbecue patio area, a pub and restaurant.

The bright rooms and cottages at Kings Lodge are decorated in warm tones and overlook the gardens.

Each has heating and a TV. The cottages also have a fully equipped kitchen and a separate lounge with a fireplace.

Country-style meals are provided at the restaurant. There is also a buffet breakfast available.

Traditional pub lunches are served at the bar, which features flat-screen TVs and a fireplace.

Guests can watch TV or read a book in the lounge. There is a games room and a playground for younger guests.
