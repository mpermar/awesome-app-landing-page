Description

Onse Khaya Lodging and Conferencing in Summerstrand is located just 250 metres from the beach.

The guest house offers modern rooms, an outdoor pool and BBQ facilities. The well-appointed rooms are equipped with a patio or balcony.

They have satellite TV, a small kitchenette and an en-suite bathroom. Onse Khaya Lodging and Conferencing offers free parking and WiFi is available in public areas.

The guest house is 5 minutes' walk from The Boardwalk and 4 km away from Port Elizabeth Airport.
