Description

Featuring an outdoor pool, terrace and secluded garden, First Avenue Guesthouse is situated 100 metres away from the beach in Port Elizabeth.

It offers free WiFi and on-site parking. Each room includes a flat screen TV, safe, fan, heater and kitchenette featuring a microwave and electric kettle.

The en-suite bathroom comes with a bath and/or shower. The Boardwalk Casino & Entertainment Complex is less than 200 metres away.

Walmer Park and Greenacres shopping centres are 20 km from First Avenue Guesthouse.
