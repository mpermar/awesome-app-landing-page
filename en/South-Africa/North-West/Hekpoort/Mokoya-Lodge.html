Description

Mokoya Lodge is located in Hekpoort on the banks of the Magalies River and 30 minutes' drive from the Cradle of Humankind.

Surrounded by landscaped gardens, this serene country lodge has a terrace with outdoor pool.

The lodge offers en suite rooms with tea-and-coffee making facilities and fan. There is a private bathroom with a bath or shower and free toiletries in each unit.

Towels and bed linen are available. Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant, followed by a drink at the bar.

You can play darts at the property, and the area is popular for cycling. Lesedi Cultural Village and Hartbeespoort Dam are both within 30 minutes' drive and the nearest airport is Lanseria Airport, 50 km from the property.
