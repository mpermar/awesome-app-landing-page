Description

Surrounded by nature on the banks of the Magalies River, Valley Lodge & Spa features individually decorated rooms, an indoor pool, a floodlit tennis court and hiking trails.

Lavishly decorated, the rooms offer a patio, a seating area, TV and a bathroom with bath and shower.

You can enjoy garden and river views. The hotelâ€™s main restaurant has a buffet section and an adjoining wine cellar.

The pub-style main bar has a billiard table and dart board. Private dining facilities are also available.

The spa area offers an indoor heated mineral pool, a hot tub, steam rooms and 6 treatment rooms.

Guests can also work out in the fully equipped gym, overlooking the garden. Other facilities at Valley Lodge & Spa include an outdoor pool, a chapel and meeting and banquet facilities.

Laundry and ironing services as well as babysitting are offered. Located close to the Cradle of Human Kind, the Sterkfontein Caves can be reached within 20 minutes by car.
