Description

Located at the top of Magaliesburg, African Pride Mount Grace Country House & Spa offers panoramic views of Magaliesburg Mountains.

This country style hotel features an outdoor pool and lush gardens. The rooms reflect modern and contemporary decor.

Each is equipped with a flat-screen satellite TV, air conditioning and en-suite bathroom with heated towel rails.

A full buffet breakfast is served daily at Twist Restaurant. Ã€ la carte specialities prepared with organic vegetables and free-range meat are served in the evening at Rambling Vine.

For mountain bike and trail running enthusiasts, The Cog & Sprocket, is the hotel's mountain bike activity centre, offering a access to tracks and a wide range of facilities.

For relaxation, The Mount Grace Spa offers a floatation pool, steam room, outdoor hot tub and reflexology walkway.

Massage treatments are offered and should be booked at least 48 hours in advance.

The hotel also has a kids club, catering for all ages over weekends, long weekends and school holidays.

Johannesburg and Pretoria are within a 1-hour drive. Free private parking is available at African Pride Mount Grace Country House & Spa.
