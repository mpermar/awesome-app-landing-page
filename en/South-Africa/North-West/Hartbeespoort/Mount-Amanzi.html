Description

All guest rooms in the resort are equipped with a TV with satellite channels. Every room includes a private bathroom with a bath or shower. Guest rooms will provide guests with an oven.

Mount Amanzi has a playground. Hiking is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.

Aerial Cableway Hartbeespoort is 3.4 miles from Mount Amanzi, while Blue Ivy Art Gallery is 5 miles away. Lanseria International Airport is 16 miles from the property.
