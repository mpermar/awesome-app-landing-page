Description

The bright, spacious guest rooms feature floor-to-ceiling windows which lead onto balconies. Each room has white linens and a living area.

The property features two traditional bomas, wooden enclosures where drinks can be enjoyed by the fire. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served in The Kwa Maritane Restaurant, overlooking the waterhole and the parkâ€™s natural granite rock formations.

Sun lounge chairs can be found among the palm trees on the grounds, by the pool. Kwa Maritane also has a playground.

Kwa Maritane Lodge is just under 1 miles from Lanseria Airport. Sun City is a 10-minute drive away. Complimentary private parking is on site and a shuttle service to Sun City is available for a fee.
