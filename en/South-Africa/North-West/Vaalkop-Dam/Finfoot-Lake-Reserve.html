Description

Situated on a 3,500 hectare reserve, Finfoot Lake Reserve is situated 49 km from Sun City and offers free WiFi.

This resort boasts a beautiful lakeside setting. Finfoot Lake Reserve offers units, chalets and villas.

The air-conditioned, furnished chalets are private and secluded and feature an en-suite bathroom fitted with a bath and shower.

They are are surrounded by natural flora and are all equipped with a mini fridge, tea-and-coffee-making facilities, a hairdryer and a private patio.

The family villas offer two en-suite bathrooms, the first fitted with a shower and the second offering a bath.

The resort also has a self-catering villa that boasts a view offers a loft area, private plunge pool, BBQ facilities and a fully-equipped kitchen.

The resort has an on-site restaurant offering set menus for breakfast and dinner and an a la carte menu for lunch.

There s a bar where guests can enjoy cocktails. There are 2 large swimming pools as well as a games room with a TV, billards table and board games.

Featuring 8 km of water frontage, the reserve is home to game, bird and plant life.

Activities include game drives, canoeing, fishing, barge cruises, and Segway game excursions.

Guided bush walks are also offered. A spa and wellness centre is available on site and the property also has two fully equipped conference centers seating up to 100 delegates as well as breakaway options.

Pilanesberg National Park is 75 km from Finfoot Lake Reserve, while the Johannesburg city center is 168 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Lanseria (Johannesburg) Airport, 125 km from the property.
