Description

Featuring a peaceful location in Muldersdrift and surrounded by the greenery, Riverstone Lodge offers a restaurant, outdoor pool and spacious, luxuriously furnished rooms.

This property includes a shared garden and lounge area. Each room is air conditioned, and comes with lots of wood details, a kettle and flat-screen satellite TV.

All rooms have a fully fitted private bathroom with a self-standing bath, while 1 room also has a small private pool.

At Riverstone Lodge you can enjoy relaxing moments out in the garden or on the shared terrace while sipping a drink from the bar.

Riverstone Lodge is a 30-minute drive from Johannesburg and the Rhino and Lion Nature Reserve is only 10 km away.
