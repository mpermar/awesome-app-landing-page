Description

Kloofzicht Lodge & Spa offers 5-star accommodation on a nature reserve filled with animals.

Situated in the Cradle of Humankind, this lodge includes an infinity pool, outdoor showers, and many outdoor activities.

Kloofzicht Lodge & Spa provides stylish interiors with hand-cut sandstone tiles, mahogany wood and thatched roofs.

All suites are tastefully furnished, spacious, and have private patios with views overlooking the Zwartkops mountain range and fly fishing dams.

Bathrooms include a separate bath tub and shower. Buffet breakfast and lunch are served daily.

For dinner, guests can choose from an à la carte dinner menu and a variety of traditional dishes.

The Little Foot Bar is open daily and serve cocktails on spacious decks. The lodge is surrounded by animals such as Zebras, Kudus, Impalas, and Blue Wildebeests.

Recreational facilities and activities include an outdoor hot tub, hiking, as well as hot air balloon rides.

Kloofzicht Lodge & Spa is just a 40-minute drive from Johannesburg and Pretoria. Guests can visit the Rhino and Lion Reserve or explore the nearby Wonder Caves or Sterkfontein Caves.
