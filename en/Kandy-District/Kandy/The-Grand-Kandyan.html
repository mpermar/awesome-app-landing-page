Description

If you feel like exploring the surrounding area, check out Kandy Museum situated just under 1 mile away and Bogambara Stadium, located just over 1 mile away. Bandaranaike International Airport is 53 miles from the hotel.

Rooms are elegantly furnished and fully air-conditioned. Amenities include a private balcony, a minibar and a sofa sitting area. Private bathrooms have free toiletries and a bathtub.

Refreshing workouts can be enjoyed at the fitness center. Guests staying at The Grand Kandyan can utilize the meeting facilities and free parking spaces. It also offers soothing spa treatments and traditional massages.

Sample tasty local and international meals at the buffet restaurant. Light snacks and a variety of alcoholic beverages are served at the bar.
