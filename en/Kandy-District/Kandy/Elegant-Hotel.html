Description

All rooms feature air conditioning, a fridge, a electric tea pot, a bath, free toiletries, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, Blu-ray player and DVD-Player. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a bidet and a hair dryer, while certain rooms are contain a seating area. The rooms have a desk.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning at the property. There's an on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of Asian dishes and also offers halal options.

The hotel has an outdoor pool. You can play darts at this 4-star hotel, and the area is popular for cycling.

For any tips on how to get around or what to do in the area, guests can ask at the reception.

Sri Dalada Maligawa is 6 miles from Elegant Hotel. Bandaranaike International Airport is 48 miles away.
