Description

All guest rooms comes with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a electric tea pot, a shower, free toiletries and a desk. Each room is equipped with a private bathroom, and some rooms also feature a terrace. The rooms have a closet.

A continental or Full English/Irish breakfast is served at the property. Guests can dine in the on-site restaurant, which specializes in German cuisine.

The hotel offers a hot tub. You can play ping pong at AENON SUITES OSHOGBHO.

Speaking English, French and Hausa at the reception, staff are willing to help at any time of the day.

Osun-Osogbo Grove is 2.4 miles from the accommodation, while Obaluaye Shrine is 3 miles away.
