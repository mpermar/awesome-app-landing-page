Description

Offering a seasonal outdoor pool, children's playground and sun terrace, Verholy Relax Park is situated in Poltava.

Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. This hotel features both free WiFi and free private parking.

Each room at this hotel is air conditioned and comes with a flat-screen TV. Certain rooms feature a seating area for your convenience.

A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms. For your comfort, you will find bath robes, slippers and free toiletries.

There is a sauna at the property. Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including horse riding and cycling.

The hotel also offers bike hire. Poltava-Yuzhnaya Train Station is 8 km from Verholy Relax Park.
