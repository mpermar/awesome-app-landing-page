Description

All rooms at the inn come with a TV and a kitchen. Eco Resort Nad Karpatamy provides some rooms with mountain views, and each room includes a coffee machine. Guest rooms will provide guests with a dishwasher.

The daily breakfast offers continental and à la carte options. There's an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of European dishes.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool. A variety of popular activities are available in the area around Eco Resort Nad Karpatamy, including skiing and golfing.

Speaking Belarusian, Polish, Russian and Ukrainian, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical advice on the area at the reception.

Vorokhta is 29 miles from the inn, while Migovo is 19 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Chernovtsy Airport, 47 miles from the property.
