Description

Offering free WiFi throughout the property, a sun terrace with a swimming pool, free bikes and garden, Острівки is set in Svityazʼ.

Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a children's playground.

The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.

At the resort, all rooms come with a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

The units will provide guests with a fridge. Острівки offers a barbecue. You can play table tennis at the accommodation, and the area is popular for cycling.
