Description

This Lutsk hotel is only 2 km from Lubart's Castle. Free Wi-Fi, an indoor pool and sauna are on site.

All rooms at Sribni Leleky Recreation Complex are air-conditioned and come with a flat-screen TV and minibar.

Every room includes a private bathroom with free toiletries. Guests can enjoy European cuisine at Sribni Leleky Restaurant.

The hotel also features a café. Chornovola Street bus stop is 100 metres from Sribni Leleky Recreation Complex.
