Description

Located in the ecological environment of Transcarpathian region, Voevodyno Resort features an outdoor swimming pool, wellness center and sauna.

This property offers horse riding lessons, BBQ and terrace. The classic-style rooms feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, minibar and a wardrobe.

The private bathroom comes with a shower and hairdryer. Guests can try Ukrainian cuisine in one of the restaurants on site and have a drink at the bar.

Both Uzhgorog Airport and Svalyava Train Station are 40 km away. Turiya-Paseka Bus Stop is 5 km away.
