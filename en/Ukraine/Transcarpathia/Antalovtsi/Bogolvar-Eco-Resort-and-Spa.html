Description

Featuring free Wi-Fi, an outdoor seasonal pool and a spa centre, this hotel is located in Antalovtsi village, 30 km from Uzhgorod.

It also offers a steam bath and fishing facilities. Each room at Hotel&Spa Complex Bogolvar comes with a classical design and a minibar.

A hairdryer is provided in the bathrooms. Bogolvar’s elegant restaurant serves Hungarian, Ukrainian and European cuisines.

Al fresco dining is available on the summer terrace. Guests of the hotel can visit the spa centre, which includes a range of beauty treatments, a massage and hammam.

A hot tub and a sauna can also be found on site. Uzhgorod Castle is 35 minutes’ drive from Hotel&Spa Comlex Bogolvar, while Uzhgorod Airport is 40 minutes by car away.
