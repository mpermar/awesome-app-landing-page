Description

Set 500 metres from Richkas ski slopes, Villa Richka has a beautiful mountain setting, 100 metres from the river.

Shuttle service to Pylypets and Izk ski lifts are available. This charming property offers rooms with lovely views, a seasonal outdoor pool and a sauna.

Each of the rustic rooms at Villa Richka has lightly coloured dcor. All rooms have a flat-screen TV and private bathrooms.

Guests can relax on the porch, which looks onto the gardens. There is also a barbecue here.

Food can be prepared upon request. Guests can order delicious meals at the restaurant on site.

You can relax on the porch, which overlooks the Carpathian forest. The surrounding area is ideal for hiking, fishing, cycling and horse riding.

Ski storage is provided onsite. Shepit Waterfall is 10 km away from the property and Synevir Lake can be reached within 35 km from Villa Richka.

Volovec Railway Station can be reached in 30 minutes by car. Uzhgorod Airport is just over 2 hours' drive away.
