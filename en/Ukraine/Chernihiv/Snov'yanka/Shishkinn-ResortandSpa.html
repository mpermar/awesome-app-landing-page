Description

Surrounded by pine forests and located in the eco-friendly area, this hotel is set on the lake in the village of Snovyanka.

A Russian steam bath is offered at Shishkinn Resort&Spa Hotel. The hotel’s elegant, air-conditioned rooms feature comfortable accommodation and warm-coloured interiors.

Each room includes a flat-screen TV, a minibar and a private bathroom, and most rooms overlook the picturesque lake.

The Grand Café Restaurant serves a daily buffet breakfast, and Shkatulka Restaurant offers Ukrainian and European cuisine.

Fishing, hiking, cycling and quad bike riding are among the outdoor activities offered at the hotel.

A children’s playground is available for young guests, and adults can relax playing billiards.

The Snov River is just 150 metres from the hotel. Snovyanka bus stop is 1. 5 km from Shishkinn Resort&Spa Hotel.

Chernihiv Train Station is 20 km away, while Boryspil International Airport is 165 km from the hotel.
