Description

Free Wi-Fi and soundproofed rooms with flat-screen TVs are features of this hotel.

The picturesque banks of Ukraine’s Desna River are in walking distance, just 500 meters away.

The simply-furnished rooms of the RiverSide- Restaurant, Hotel, Beach include a minibar and complimentary toiletries.

All rooms offer air conditioning and room service. River Side Restaurant is set on the Desna River and includes a summer terrace, a seasonal swimming pool and a beach.

European and chef's cuisine is served here. A shuttle service and free on-site private parking is available.
