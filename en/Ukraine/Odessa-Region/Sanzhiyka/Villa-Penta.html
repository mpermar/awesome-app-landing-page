Description

Located in Sanzhiyka, 30 km from Odessa, Villa Penta features a restaurant, bar and free WiFi throughout the property.

The resort has a seasonal outdoor pool and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Free private parking is available on site. Rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Some units have views of the sea or pool. Every room comes with a private bathroom.

For your comfort, you will find bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries. You will find dry cleaning services at the property.

Bike hire is available at this resort and the area is popular for hiking. Odessa International Airport is 22 km from the property.
