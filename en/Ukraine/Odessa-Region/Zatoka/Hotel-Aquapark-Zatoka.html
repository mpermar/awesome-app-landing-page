Description

At the resort, every room comes with a desk and a flat-screen TV. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a hair dryer, while some accommodations at Hotel Aquapark Zatoka also feature a terrace. Guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

A buffet breakfast is served at the property.

Odessa is 26 miles from the accommodation. Central Airport is 21 miles away.
