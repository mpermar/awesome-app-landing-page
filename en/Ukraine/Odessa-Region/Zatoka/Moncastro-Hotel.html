Description

All rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels, fridge, a electric tea pot, a shower and a closet. The rooms come with air conditioning, and some accommodations at the hotel have a balcony.

Guests at Moncastro Hotel can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

At the accommodation guests are welcome to use a hot tub.

The nearest airport is Central Airport, 27 miles from the property.
