Description

Outdoor and indoor swimming pools, a sauna and gym are featured at this hotel, set in Zatoka, just 100 metres from the Black Sea coast.

Edem Hotel offers a private beach. The bright, air-conditioned rooms have warm-coloured interiors and classic-style décor.

Each room includes a TV, a refrigerator and a private bathroom. A buffet breakfast and Ukrainian dishes is served in the on-site restaurant.

A summer terrace and barbecue facilities are available at the restaurant. Guests can visit the hotel’s spa centre and relax in the aroma room or a salt room, take a pearl bath or have a massage.

A children’s cinema and a playground with a small water park are offered for young guests.

Odessa Central Train Station is 58 km from Edem Hotel, and Odessa Airport is 60 km away.
