Description

This modern hotel in Odessa features air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, spa facilities and a sun terrace with panoramic views of the Black Sea.

It is nearby Ukraine’s famous Arcadia Beach, a yacht club and a modern water park.

Guests at Stella Residence Club are welcomed by a grand, marble hall. Rooms include cable TV, minibar, DVD player and bathrobe.

Some also offer a private terrace. A rich, fresh breakfast can be brought up to the Stella Residence’s guest rooms every morning.

A 24-hour restaurant serves typical Ukrainian and European cuisine that can be enjoyed on the outside terrace with sea view.

Guests at the Stella Residence Club enjoy free use of the gym, swimming pool, sauna, spa bath and Turkish hammam.

A children’s games room is also available on site. The M05 motorway is easily reachable from Stella Residence Club, offering good connections to Kiev and other cities of Ukraine.

Free private parking is available. Odessa International Airport is 9 km from Stella Residence Club.
