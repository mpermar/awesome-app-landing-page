Description

Located in Odessa, within 12 km of Odessa Archaeological Museum and 13 km of Odessa Theatre of Opera and Ballet, Redling Hotel features accommodation with a seasonal outdoor swimming pool.

Zolotoy bereg beach is a 10-minute walk from the property. With free WiFi and parking, this 4-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk and a concierge service.

Some rooms come with a balcony with city views, kitchenette and a dressing room. At the hotel, air-conditioned rooms include a desk and a flat-screen TV.

Each room is fitted with a private bathroom fitted with a shower, towels, bathrobe, slippers and free toiletries.

All guest rooms have a wardrobe. A buffet breakfast is served daily at the property.

Guests can dine in the on-site restaurant, which serves European cuisine. Redling Hotel offers a children's playground.

Duke de Richelieu Monument is 13 km from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Odessa International Airport, 10 km from the property.
