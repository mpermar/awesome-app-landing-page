Description

Just 5 minutes’ walk from the sandy Malibu Beach in Odessa, this guest house features air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi.

A summer garden terrace is available for guests’ enjoyment. A cable TV is available in each individually designed room.

Vilari Guest House offers classic-style rooms and suites, some with garden views.

Each one comes with its own bathroom with shower. A hot breakfast is served daily in the dining area or visitors are welcome to use Vilari’s fully equipped shared kitchen.

Drinks can be enjoyed on the garden terrace or at the bar. Barbecue facilities are also available.

Vilari’s 24/7 reception staff can organise transfers to Odessa Central Station which is a 15-minute drive from the hotel.

Odessa city centre and the large Poseidon Aqua Park are both a 22-minute drive from the guest house.
