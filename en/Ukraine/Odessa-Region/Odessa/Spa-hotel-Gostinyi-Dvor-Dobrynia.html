Description

Set in Odessa, 2 km from the sea coast, Spa-hotel Gostinyi Dvor Dobrynia features a year-round outdoor pool, seasonal outdoor pool, a Russian steam bath, massage services and sun terrace.

The mini-hotel has a spa centre with a limited free access. Free private parking is available on site.

A flat-screen TV is offered. All rooms include a private bathroom, fitted with bathrobes and slippers.

A terrace or patio are featured in certain rooms. Extras include free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Spa-hotel Gostinyi Dvor Dobrynia features free WiFi throughout the property. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant. Chernomorets Stadium and Deribasovskaya Street are 9 km from Spa-hotel Gostinyi Dvor Dobrynia.

The Opera and Ballet Theatre is 10 km away. The train station is 7 km from the mini-hotel.
