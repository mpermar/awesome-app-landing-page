Description

Located 5 minutes walk from a private beach and the Black Sea, this Odessa resort features free WiFi in public areas, on-site restaurant, a seasonal outdoor pool and 24-hour security on site.

Odessa city centre is 17 km away. The property's territory is under 24-hour security surveillance.

All rooms feature a flat-screen cable TV, small refrigerator, and electric kettle as well as an en suite bathroom.

Bungalows and cottages at Sovinyon Resort feature a kitchenette and separate seating area with sofa bed.

A childrens playground, business centre, and free parking are provided at this Odessa Sovinyon Resort.

Spa facilities and bowling area are available at an additional cost. Restaurant Zhemchuzhyna serves European and traditional Urkainian cuisines for lunch and dinner.

Airport Odessa is 8 km away. The resort is 15 km from the Odessa bus and railway station.
