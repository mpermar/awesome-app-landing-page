Description

Resort & SPA Hotel NEMO is set in the historical centre of Odessa, on Lanzheron Beach.

It offers 9 heated sea-water swimming pools, fitness & spa zone, a 24-hour room service and free WiFi.

All rooms are air-conditioned and come with a balcony, flat-screen TV, a safety deposit box and minibar.

All suites feature a spa bath. Guests can enjoy Ukrainian, European and Japanese cuisine in the hotel's restaurant, or have a drink at the bar.

Odessa city centre is a 5-minute drive from Resort & Spa Hotel Nemo. Central Train Station is a 10-minute drive from the resort.
