Description

Boutique Hotel Portofino is located on the Black Sea coast, first line of beach Arcadia.

It features a seasonal outdoor swimming pool, private beach area and a restaurant.

It is 5 km from Odessa city centre. Each room at Portofino Beach Resort includes a flat-screen TV, free WiFi, safety deposit box and minibar.

Bathrooms are fitted with a shower, hairdryer, bathrobes and slippers. European cuisine is served in the Portofino’s trendy restaurant, and meals can be enjoyed on the beach terrace with a view of the sea.

Golf cars rental is provided on site. Guests can relax in the wood-fired sauna, with direct access to the sea or in the restaurant with panoramic sea views.

A shuttle service to Odessa city centre (7 km) and the Train Station (5. 5 km) is provided at surcharge at Portofino Beach Resort.
