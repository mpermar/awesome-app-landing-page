Description

Hotel Vele Rosse is located a 5-minute walk from the Black Sea sandy beach. It offers a fitness centre, a sauna, billiards and classically decorated rooms and luxury suites, all including free WiFi and a private bathroom with shower.

Each room at the Vele Rosse is air-conditioned and features a TV. There is also a minibar and a balcony.

The bathroom includes a shower cabin and free toiletries. The hotel features a restaurant serving a wide variety of Ukrainian and European dishes à-la-carte.

It is furnished with flowery, cushioned chairs and is decorated with unique murals.

There is also a bar were hot and cold drinks can be enjoyed. Hotel Vele Rosse provides a 24-hour front desk service and offers valet parking.

The 12-stantsiya Fontana bus stop is 10 metres from the building and offers easy access around Odessa.
