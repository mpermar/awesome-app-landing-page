Description

Shalanda Hotel is located in the centre of Odessa, right on the Black Sea beach. Free Wi-Fi is provided.

Shalanda Restaurant offers European and Ukrainian cuisine. It is possible to order breakfast in room, or to have a drink in the bar.

All classic-style, air-conditioned rooms come with a flat-screen TV, and have a sea view.

Free toiletries, slippers and a bathrobe are provided in a bathroom. Guests can go fishing or cycling, and it is also possible to order a massage service.

The hotel offers an on-site children’s playground for the youngest guests. Odessa Opera Theatre is 1 km from Shalanda Hotel, and the distance to the famous Privoz Market and Deribasovskaya Street is 2 km.

Park Shevchenko bus stop is 800 metres from the hotel, and the trip to Odessa Central Station takes 10 minutes.

Odessa International Airport is 8 km away, and a shuttle service is provided at surcharge.
