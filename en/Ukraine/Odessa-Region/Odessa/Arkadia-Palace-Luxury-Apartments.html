Description

Arkadia Palace Luxury Apartments are located only 200 metres from the famous Arkadia Beach.

They feature designer interiors, and free Wi-Fi is provided. All luxury apartments come with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, washing machine and coffee maker.

Bathrooms are equipped with a spa bath and a hairdryer. Various cafes, bars and restaurants can be found within a 5-minute walk from the property.

Also it is possible to cook in a modern kitchen or to order meals in the room. Popular nightclubs are just 100 metres away the Arkadia Palace Luxury Apartments, and the distance to the historic centre is 5 km.

Arkadia bus stop is 200 metres from the properties, and Odessa Central Station is 5 km away.

The distance to Odessa International Airport is 10 km, and a shuttle service can be arranged at surcharge.
