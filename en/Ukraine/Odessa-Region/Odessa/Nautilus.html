Description

Set in Odessa, 18 km from Odessa Archaeological Museum, Nautilus offers accommodation with a seasonal outdoor swimming pool, private parking, a shared lounge and a garden.

Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a barbecue. The accommodation features a shared kitchen and free WiFi throughout the property.

The units in the hotel are equipped with a TV with satellite channels. All rooms will provide guests with a wardrobe and a kettle.

Guests at Nautilus can enjoy a continental breakfast. The accommodation offers a terrace.

Odessa Theatre of Opera and Ballet is 18 km from Nautilus, while Duke de Richelieu Monument is 19 km away.

The nearest airport is Odessa International, 13 km from the hotel, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
