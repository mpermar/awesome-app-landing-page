Description

Located in Kanev city, this hotel is 2 minutes’ walk from the Dnieper River. It features free Wi-Fi and a children’s playground.

Tarasova or Chernecha Hora is a 15-minute walk away. Decorated in classic style, all rooms at Knyazha Hora Hotel are equipped with satellite TV.

Private bathrooms come with a hairdryer and free toiletries. Guests can enjoy European and Ukrainian cuisine in Knyazha Hora Restaurant, or relax with a cocktail at the bar.

Mironovka Train Station is a 45 km from Knyazha Hora Hotel. A shuttle to and from Boryspil International Airport (120 km) is available on request.
