Description

Situated next to the Dnepr River, this quietly located hotel features 3 restaurants and a wine cellar.

It offers bicycle and boat rental, as well as saunas and various sports facilities.

The air-conditioned rooms at Hotel Selena each come with a TV and a minibar. The bathroom comes with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

The restaurants offer a selection of Georgian and European cuisine, and guests can also dine on the sunny terrace.

During the summer the beach bar serves drinks and snacks. Active guests will enjoy Hotel Selenas choice of activities, with a yacht club and number of tennis courts available.

All public areas have free Wi-Fi. Dakhnovka train station is a 30-minute drive from the hotel, and free private parking is available.
