Description

Featuring a spa and wellness centre, TAOR Carpathians located in Lastivka, 9 km from Skhidnitsa, offers free Wi-Fi and free private parking.

There is an on-site restaurant serving European and Ukrainian cuisine. Accommodations at the property feature a fully equipped kitchen, a dining area, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom.

Other facilities at TAOR Carpathians include a sauna, Turkish bath, and a number of outdoor activities such as ATV racing and RC racing.

Truskavets Train Station is 30 km from the property, and Lviv Airport is 120 km away.
