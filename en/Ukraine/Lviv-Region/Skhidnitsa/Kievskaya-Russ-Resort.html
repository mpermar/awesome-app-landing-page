Description

This elegant health resort in Shidnytsia features a free indoor pool, sauna and outdoor pool.

Surrounded by the beautiful north-eastern Carpathian Mountains, guests enjoy free Wi-Fi and complimentary full-board.

The Kievskaya Russ Resort welcomes guests with stylish rooms, offering a variety of bright colours and classic-style furniture.

All feature a minibar, safe and balcony. "Kyivska Russ" restaurant offers buffet breakfast and dinner as well as lunch upon request.

A selection of Ukrainian and European dishes can be enjoyed here all day. The restaurant has a log fire.

You can order your favourite drink at the bar. Guests can relax in the Kievskaya’s beauty salon or watch a film in the cinema.

The SPAcentre and children's playroom are free. Rental bicycles are available at the Kievskaya Russ for exploring the Carpathian hills and mountains.
