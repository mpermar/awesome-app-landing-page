Description

A sauna with an indoor pool and spacious rooms with free Wi-Fi are offered by Kopa Hotel.

Quietly located on the outskirts of Lviv , it stands just 4 km from Lviv Arena. Decorated in neutral colours, solid wooden furniture and classic-style interiors feature throughout.

Each air-conditioned room is complete with a cable TV and a modern bathroom. Traditional Ukrainian and European cuisine can be enjoyed in Kopa Hotel’s stylish restaurant with black and white décor and large windows.

Breakfast is provided and can be served in the rooms on request. Guests will find King Cross Leopolis Shopping and Entertainment Complex in just a 7-minute drive from the hotel.

The beautiful churches and palaces in Lviv’s historic Old Town are only 13 km away.

Lviv International Airport can be reached within a 20-minute drive from Kopa Hotel.
