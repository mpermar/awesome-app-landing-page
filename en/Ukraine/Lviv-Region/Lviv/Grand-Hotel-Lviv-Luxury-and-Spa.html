Description

Situated in the heart of historic Lviv, the Grand Hotel combines high-end modern comfort with a refined, classical ambiance.

There is free Wi-Fi available throughout the hotel. All guest rooms in the Grand Hotel feature air conditioning, a minibar and satellite TV.

A full range of concierge and room services is available. Tea and coffee making facilities, as well as an iron is offered to guests.

A daily buffet breakfast is served in the hotel and is included in the room rate.

The hotel’s GrandSoLie restaurant serves a full menu throughout the rest of the day.

Grand Hotel's own Spa & Fitness centre offers saunas, swimming pool, hot tub, massage service and gym.
