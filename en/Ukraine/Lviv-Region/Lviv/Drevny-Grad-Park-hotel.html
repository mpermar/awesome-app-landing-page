Description

Drevny Grad Hotel is situated in Lviv’s forests, just a 15-minute drive from the town centre.

It offers rooms with rustic furniture, free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel and a daily buffet breakfast.

Rooms at Drevny Grad feature wood furniture and an outdoor balcony. Each includes a TV with satellite channels and a work desk.

Traditional Ukrainian dishes and European cuisine are served at Drevny Grad Hotel’s restaurant.

Meat and fresh fish specials are prepared daily and guests can dine by the fireplace.

Guests can walk along the stone pathways in the hotel’s alpine garden. Staff at the 24-hour reception desk can arrange area tours.

Lviv International Airport is a 25 minutes’ drive from the hotel. An airport shuttle is available at an additional cost.
