Description

Offering a seasonal outdoor pool, barbecue and children's playground, Villa Alpiyka is located in Slavske, 150 metres from the ski complex and Vysoky Verkh mountain slope.

Free WiFi is featured and free private parking is available on site. Each room includes a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Certain units include a seating area to relax in after a busy day and a fully equipped kitchen.

You will find a kettle in the room. This guest house has ski storage space and ski equipment hire is available.

A number of activities are offered in the area, such as skiing, horse riding and cycling.
