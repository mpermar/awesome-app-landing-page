Description

Set on a large property with 3 lakes, this non-smoking resort offers a private beach, a riding stable, and spa facilities.

Lviv city centre is 12 km away. The family-friendly Buhta Vikingov has soundproofed rooms, suites, and apartments with balcony and TV.

All include a modern bathroom. Tennis, fishing, and skiing are only some of the leisure activities offered by the Buhta Vikingov.

Children can have fun in the playground and kids club. Guests are welcome to relax in the sauna and massage lounge.

The hotel also has a gym and bicycle rental service. Continental breakfast is available in at the Vikingov in the mornings.
