Description

Featuring free WiFi and a spa centre, Wish Family Space offers accommodation in Vishenki, 9 km from Kiev.

The hotel offers spa facilities, including 5 swimming pools and gym. Free private parking is available on site.

Guests can enjoy a meal at WISH Rest Restaurant or WISH Grill Restaurant located on site.

An open terrace with a lake view and gazebos are also featured. Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and comes with a flat-screen TV.

All rooms have a minibar, safety deposit box and private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find bathrobes and slippers.

Beach towels are also available. There is babysitting service at the property. Kid's club as well as children's playground are at guests' disposal.
