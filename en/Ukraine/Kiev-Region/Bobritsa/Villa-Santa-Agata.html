Description

The villa with a terrace and pool views has a living room, a flat-screen TV, an equipped kitchen, and a bathroom with a hot tub and a bath.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can help guests with any questions that they might have.

At the villa guests are welcome to use a hot tub, a sauna and a fitness room. An outdoor swimming pool and a private beach area can be found at Villa Santa Agata, along with a playground.

Kiev is 16 miles from the accommodation. Igor Sikorsky Kyiv International Airport is 12 miles from the property.
