Description

Quietly located on the outskirts of Kiev, the Terra Nova offers free Wi-Fi and free private parking.

The property has a business centre and equipped conference rooms. Guests can enjoy swimming pools, a water bar, fitness room, SPA lounge, and massage room at the sports and fitness centre.

Modern air-conditioned rooms come with a flat-screen TV, minibar, a safety deposit box and private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Ukranian and European cuisine is served in the Terra Nova’s spacious restaurant, which also features a summer terrace, a bar and a lounge area.

Terra Nova is 7 km from the Expocenter of Ukraine exhibition grounds, while Kiev Central Station is 16 km away.

With a forest located just 200 metres away, this hotel is also ideally located for hiking.

Kiev city centre is a 15-minute drive. Boryspil International Airport is 42 km from the property.
