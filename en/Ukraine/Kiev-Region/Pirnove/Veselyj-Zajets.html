Description

Veselyj Zajets features a children's playground and a small zoo. Free Wi-Fi and air-conditioned rooms are offered at this hotel, located in Pirnovo village, and a bus stop providing a direct link to Kiev is just opposite the hotel.

Each room at Veselyj Zajets Hotel has a minibar, as well as a balcony. Bathrooms include complimentary slippers.
