Description

This complex that located on 1. 5 hectares of parkland in the historic Koncha-Zaspa district of Kiev.

Offering an elegant spa with a 2 pools, restaurant European and Middle Eastern cuisine, and a big garden.

Free WiFi is featured. Just a 20-minute drive from central Kiev, KNZS Hotel - restaurant Complex Central Park has soundproofed rooms and suites in 2 different styles - French Quarter and Russian Village Town, with a flat-screen TV, and climate control system.

The spacious spa at the KNZS offers a variety of saunas, including infrared, Russian, Finnish, Japanese and Hamam.

There is also a tennis court and billiards room. Gourmet dishes are served in the hotel’s traditional-style restaurant or on the garden terrace.

Special diet menus are provided on request. Parking is free at KNZS Hotel - restaurant Complex Central Park.
