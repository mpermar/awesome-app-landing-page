Description

This 4-star hotel is located in Koncha Zaspa, the town of Kozyn, a 30-minute drive south of Kiev city centre.

It offers a large spa and stylish rooms with free WiFi internet. All rooms at the Seasons SPA Boutique Hotel feature air conditioning, a balcony, TV and a private bathroom with bath.

There is an outdoor swimming pool at Seasons Boutique Kozyn. Spa facilities also include a sauna, steam room, hot tub and gym.

A range of body and facial treatments are available. Breakfast is served each morning at the Seasons Hotel Kozyn.
