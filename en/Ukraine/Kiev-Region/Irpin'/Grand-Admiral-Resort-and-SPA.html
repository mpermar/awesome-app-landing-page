Description

Set in several acres of forests, this resort features a spa and wellness centre with 3 swimming pools, saunas and fitness centre, tennis courts and various entertainment programmes for children and adults.

It is quietly located in Irpin, a 30-minute drive from Kiev city centre. The spacious rooms at Grand Admiral Club feature free Wi-Fi and a minibar, and traditional wooden cottages are also available.

Bathrobes and a hairdryer are provided in the elegant bathrooms. A varied breakfast buffet is provided every morning at the Grand Admiral Hotel.

Dishes of Ukrainian and European cuisine are served in the large restaurant, which has several separate dining halls.

Irpin town centre is a 10-minute walk from the Admiral Club, and Irpin Main Station is a 5-minute drive away.
