Description

Located in Kamyanets-Podilsky’s historical and cultural centre, this hotel is 2 km from the Central Train Station.

Guests can use the fitness centre, pool, Finnish sauna and Turkish steam bath. All colourful, stylish accommodation at the 7 Days Hotel Kamyanets-Podilsky comes with a cable TV and air conditioning.

Apartments have a flat-screen TV, 4 balconies and large living room with bar table.

As well as breakfast, Ukrainian, European, Japanese and Thai cuisine are served in the hotel's 2 restaurants.

Guests can relax on the sun terrace of the Coffee Studio, or in the lounge with a hookah pipe.

At the weekend, discos and parties are held for guests. Kamyanets-Podilsky’s Old Town is 2.

5 km away. The historic Old Fortress, Dominican Monastery and St. Peter and Paul's Cathedral are within a 15-minute walk.
