Description

Located in Polska Brama historic district, on the riverside of the Smotrich River, this hotel features free Wi-Fi.

Aleksandra Nevskogo Cathedral is 10 minutes’ walk away. Rooms feature a TV and DVD player.

Private bathrooms are fitted with a shower or bath. A hairdryer, iron, kettle and fridge are provided upon request.

Breakfast is served in the on-site café, home cuisine dishes are provided upon request.

Zarlanskaya Street bus stop is 100 metres from Bilya Richky Hotel. Airport Chernivtsi is 200 km away.
