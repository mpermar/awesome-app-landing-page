Description

Located 20 metres from the Black Sea sandy beach in Zalizniy Port village, this resort features free Wi-Fi and an outdoor swimming pool.

It offers a terrace and table tennis facilities. There is also a spa area for therapeutic mud treatments.

All the simply decorated rooms at Dolce Vita Resort Hotel include a work desk. Bathrooms come with a shower and free toiletries.

Local cuisine is served in the Dolce Vita’s bright café, while refreshing cocktails can be enjoyed at the poolside bar.

Barbecue facilities can also be found on site. Relaxation at the hotel includes a sun terrace with loungers and the outdoor swimming pool.

Table tennis is available, and younger guests can have fun on the children’s playground.

A water park is 500 metres from Dolce Vita Resort Hotel, and a bus stop offering a link to Kherson city is 5 minutes’ walk away.
