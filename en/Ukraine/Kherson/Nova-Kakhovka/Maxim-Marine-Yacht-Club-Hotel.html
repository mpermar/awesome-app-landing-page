Description

Riverside tennis courts, 2 swimming pools and a natural spring are featured at this hotel with free WiFi.

It lies on the Dnieper River, just 2 km from Nova Kakhovka’s centre. Elegant, air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi and satellite TV are offered at the Maxim Marine Yacht Club Hotel.

Bathrobes and toiletries are featured in the private bathrooms. Guests can relax in the sauna next to the spring.

Boats and jet skis can be hired at the Maxim. European cuisine is served in the stylish Café del River restaurant.

Guests can enjoy fresh meat and fish dishes and a range of wines on the terrace. Free private parking is provided at the hotel.
