Description

This hotel boasts a private stretch of beach along the shores of Lozoven’kovskoe Lake.

It features a traditional Russian sauna and a restaurant serving European specialities.

All rooms at the Baden-Baden Hotel combine modern comforts with elegant décor. Free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and minibars blend with dark wood furniture, thick carpets and Art-Deco lamps.

The restaurant's wine selection ranges from renowned Italian and French to more unusual Georgian and Chilean varieties.

The Baden-Baden offers 3 bars, including a beach bar and a 24-hour lobby café. Krakhov’s city centre is about a 20 minutes’ drive away.
