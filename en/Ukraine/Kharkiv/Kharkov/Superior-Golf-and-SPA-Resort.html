Description

This 5-star hotel is located at the Superior Golf and SPA Resort, a 15-minute drive from Kharkov city centre.

On-site spa facilities include indoor and outdoor swimming pools, steam rooms, a gym and massages.

The spacious rooms at the Superior Golf and SPA Resort feature bright, contemporary décor.

All rooms are air-conditioned and include a flat-screen satellite TV and a luxury bathroom with bathrobe and slippers.

European dishes are served in the Albatross restaurant, which features chic white interiors and a beautiful terrace.

Guests can relax in the lobby bar, and there is also a golf museum on site. Private parking is free at the resort, and the M27 motorway is a 5-minute drive away.
