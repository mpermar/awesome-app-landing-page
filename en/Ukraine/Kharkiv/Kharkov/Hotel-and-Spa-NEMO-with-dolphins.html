Description

This hotel is set in the heart of Kharkiv, a 5-minute walk from the Freedom Square.

A seasonal outdoor pool, indoor pool with sea water, spa area, fitness centre and gym are available.

Free WiFi is provided. The bright, air-conditioned rooms of Nemo Hotel offer warm-coloured interiors and contemporary-style décor.

Each room comes with a flat-screen TV, a minibar and a private bathroom with bathrobes, slippers and a hairdryer.

The Terrace Restaurant serves European and Italian cuisine. Guests can also dine at the Burger Club café.

Room service is available 24/7. Universitet Metro Station is a 5-minute walk from Nemo Hotel.

Kharkov Central Train Station is 3 km away, and Kharkov International Airport is 10 km from the hotel.
