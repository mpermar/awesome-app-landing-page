Description

Etoile is located in the green Novaya Bavaria district of Kharkov, just a 10-minute drive from the city centre.

All bright rooms have a flat-screen TV. Guests are welcome to use the leisure facilities at Etoile, which include a modern fitness centre with indoor pool.

There is also a Russian steam room. A buffet breakfast is available each morning at Etoile, and there are also Italian restaurant on site.

All of the classically furnished rooms and suites at Etoile come equipped with dark wooden furniture, satellite TV and air conditioning.

A new children's amusement park with a babut arena, a children's restaurant and other children's entertainment has been built on the territory of the hotel.
