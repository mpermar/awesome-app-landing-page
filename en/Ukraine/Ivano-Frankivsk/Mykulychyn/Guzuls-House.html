Description

Guzul's House in Mykulychyn features spacious rooms with free WiFi, an indoor swimming pool and a sauna, within 20 km from Bukovel Ski and Spa Resort.

Guests can use the free barbecue facilities. All rooms offer a balcony, some of which overlook the mountains.

Each room includes a seating area with cable TV and a private bathroom. For your meals, you can turn to Guzul's House’s restaurant, which serves Ukrainian and European dishes.

Mykulychyn Waterfalls are 6 km away; Yaremcha Waterfalls are 10 km from Guzul’s House.

The hotel is 2 km from Mykulychyn Bus Station. Ivano-Frankivsk International Airport is located at 70 km.
