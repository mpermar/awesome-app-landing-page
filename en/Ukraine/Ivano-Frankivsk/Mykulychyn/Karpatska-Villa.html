Description

Karpatska Villa is located in Mykulychyn. Bukovel Ski Resort is 22 km from the property.

The accommodation will provide you with a flat-screen TV. Complete with a refrigerator, the dining area also has an electric kettle.

Featuring a hairdryer, private bathrooms come with bathrobes and free toiletries.

Extras include a safety deposit box. At Karpatska Villa you will find a children's playground, a trampoline, barbecue facilities and a terrace.

Other facilities offered at the property include a tour desk, luggage storage and ski storage.

There is a natural lake with cascade falls on site. Yaremcha souvenir market and Probiy waterfall are 10 km from Karpatska Villa.

The distance to Mykulychyn Train Station is 3 km, and Ivano-frankivsk airport is 60 km away.
