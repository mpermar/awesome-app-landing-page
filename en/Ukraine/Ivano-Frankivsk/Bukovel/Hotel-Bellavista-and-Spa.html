Description

Located in the ecological environment of Polyanytsya, just 2 km from Bukovel Ski Resort, Bellavista hotel features a sauna and free WiFi.

This property offers a restaurant and a heated outdoor swimming pool with sunbeds.

Overlooking the mountains, the classic-style rooms feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and private bathroom with a shower.

Smaller guests can play in children's playground and there is a barbeque on the premises that guests can use free of charge.

Guests can relax in an outdoor and indoor heated pools, as well as in a hot tub. There are Roman and Finnish saunas and guests can use bathrobes and slippers.

Restaurant offers its guests to enjoy local and Ukrainian meals. Hotel Bellavista is 22 km from Mykulychyn Train Station and 89 km from Ivano-Frankivsk Airport.
