Description

Featuring a sauna, a heated swimming pool and free Wi-Fi, this design hotel is located 2 km from ski lifts of Bukovel Ski Resort.

It offers a childrens playground and rooms with a flat-screen TV. Every room at History Hotel Bukovel includes a traditional Carpathian interior and a balcony overlooking the Carpathian Mountains.

Bathrooms come with a shower. Guests can enjoy local dishes, as well as Ukrainian cuisine, in the on-site restaurant, while drinks are offered at the bar.

Relaxation at History Hotel is provided by the sauna, and ski storage is also available.

Palyanytsya bus stop is 400 metres from History Hotel Bukovel, offering access to Tatariv Train Station.
