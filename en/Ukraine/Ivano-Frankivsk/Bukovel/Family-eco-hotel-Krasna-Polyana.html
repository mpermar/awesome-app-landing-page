Description

This hotel is located in the village of Polyanytsya, a 10-minute drive from the Bukovel ski resort.

Family eco-hotel Krasna Polyana offers an outdoor heated pool, spa bath, sauna and great views of the Carpathian Mountains.

The family apartments and cottages at the Family eco-hotel Krasna Polyana feature traditional interiors with wood panelling and floors.

All include a TV and a private bathroom. Some rooms come with a fireplace. A selection of Ukrainian and Gutsul specialties as well as barbecue dishes are served at the traditional-style restaurant.

Kid's menu is available. A welcome drink is offered. Other facilities include a children's playground, kids' room with animation and barbecue facilities.

On-site parking is free, and bus stop is 200 metres away. A shuttle can be arranged to the Bukovel ski resort.
