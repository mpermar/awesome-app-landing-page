Description

Set among the picturesque Carpathian Mountains, this hotel is located in the village of Polyanitsa.

Free Wi-Fi and a children’s playroom are featured at Hotel Plus. The bright hotel rooms offer warm-coloured décor and wooden furnishings.

Each rustic-style room comes complete with a flat-screen TV, a desk, a balcony and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The on-site restaurant serves Ukrainian and European cuisines and offers a selection of drinks.

Barbecue facilities are available for guests. Hunting, horse riding, quad bike and snowmobile riding can be organized upon request.

Ski storage is also provided for guests. Bukovel Bus Station is 1. 3 km from Hotel Plus.

Yaremche Train Station is 35 km away, and Ivano-Frankivsk International Airport is 90 km from the hotel.
