Description

Offering ski storage and equipment, Villa Leku is located in the Carpathian Mountains in Polyanytsya village.

Bukovel Ski Resort is 1. 5 km away. Free Wi-Fi and parking are available. The comfortable rooms feature rustic décor and come with a flat-screen satellite TV.

The private bathrooms come with a shower. The restaurant at Villa Leku serves Ukrainian cuisine.

Yaremcha souvenir market is 30 km away. Bukovel Bus Station is 1. 5 km from the property, and Ivano-Frankivsk Airport is 110 km away.
