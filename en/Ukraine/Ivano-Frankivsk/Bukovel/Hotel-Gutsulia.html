Description

Located just 2 km from the ski lifts in Bukovel, Hotel Gutsulia offers a ski storage.

Polyanytsya is only a 5-minute drive away. WiFi is offered. All rooms feature a balcony, mountain views and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

The en suite bathroom includes a hairdryer. There is also a 5-bedroom cottage with a terrace and fireplace.

Guests can dine on site in the hotel’s restaurant, that serves local dishes. Grocery shops and bars can be reached within walking distance.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including hiking and skiing.

Ivano-Frankivsk Airport is 62 km away and free private parking is possible on site.
