Description

Located in Bukovel in the Ivano-Frankivsk Region, 100 metres from Vityag 5, Chevalier Hotel features a barbecue and views of the mountain.

All rooms come with a flat-screen TV. Some rooms have a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

You will find a kettle in the room. Extras include bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries.

There is a 24-hour front desk, concierge service and a terrace at the property. The hotel also offers bike hire.

Vityag 2 and Vityag R2 are 200 metres from Chevalier Hotel. Ivano-Frankivsk Airport is 91 km away.
