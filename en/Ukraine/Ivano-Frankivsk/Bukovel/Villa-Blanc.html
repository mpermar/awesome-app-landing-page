Description

Located 2 km from the several Bukovel Ski Lifts, Villa Blanc offers bright and heated rooms with parquet floor.

Free ski storage, free WiFi and free private parking are offered and available on site.

There is a plunge pool on site. Each room at Blanc comes with a private bathroom with shower and hairdryer.

There is a flat-screen satellite TV and some rooms have a living area with a sofa and a table up to 4 people.

A breakfast is served every morning, and national dishes are prepared for lunch or dinner.

Special diet menus and room services are available on prior request. Plenty of outdoor activities can be arranged in the surrounding countryside, mountains and lake.
