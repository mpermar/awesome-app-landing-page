Description

Located 5 minutes’ drive from Bukovel Ski Resort, this design hotel in Polyanitsa village features a Russian steam bath and free Wi-Fi.

It also features eco-friendly rooms and a restaurant. For families with children, the hotel runs a free creative workshop.

Each room and cottage at Hotel Stara Pravda is decorated in a rustic style with wooden furnishings.

All bathrooms come with a hairdryer. Stara Pravda’s restaurant with exposed wooden beams serves Ukrainian and European cuisines.

A barbecue can be set up in the courtyard. Guests of the hotel can relax in the steam bath and cool off in the plunge pool.

Table tennis and children playground can be found on site, while ski storage is also available.

A shuttle service to Bukovel’s ski slopes is available on request, and Guk waterfall in Yaremche village is 30 minutes’ drive away.
