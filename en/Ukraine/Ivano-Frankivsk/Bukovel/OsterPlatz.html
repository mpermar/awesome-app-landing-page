Description

Featuring free Wi-Fi and a steam bath, this hotel is located in Palyanitsya village, 7 minutes’ walk from Bukovel Ski Resort.

It offers rooms with eco-friendly décor and a kettle. Each room at OsterPlatz includes country-style décor and wooden furnishings.

All rooms and cottages have a flat-screen TV, and bathrooms are fitted with a hairdryer.

Homemade breakfast can be served in the guests’ rooms, while cottages and apartments come with a kitchen.

Guests can relax in the steam bath with a plunge pool, and a massage service is offered on request.

Ski equipment hire is also available on site. The H-09 motorway offering a link to Ivano-Frankivsk Airport is 7 km from Osterplatz.

Yaremche Train Station is 35 km away, and a shuttle service is provided on request.
