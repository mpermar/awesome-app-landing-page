Description

Located in the Carpathian Mountains, 4 km from Tatariv village, this hotel offers free Wi-Fi and a sauna.

There is also a traditional restaurant and rooms with a flat-screen TV. Each room at Hotel Dykyi Med features wooden interior and a balcony.

Bathrooms come with slippers and bathrobes. Kolyba Restaurant, decorated in a Ukrainian style, serves national dishes, which can also be enjoyed on the terrace.

Barbecue facilities can be found on site. Relaxation at Dykyi Med includes a massage service and the sauna with a plunge pool.

Guests can also play table tennis or sing karaoke. Hiking routes to Khomyak Mountain start 5 minutes walk from Hotel Dykyi Med, and Bukovel Ski Resort is 15 km away.

Mykulychin Train Station is 7 km from the hotel, offering a link to Ivano-Frankivsk.
