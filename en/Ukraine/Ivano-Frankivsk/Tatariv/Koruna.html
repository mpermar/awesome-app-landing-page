Description

Surrounded by the picturesque Carpathian Mountains, this resort is located in the village of Tatariv.

A heated outdoor pool, a fitness centre, a sauna and billiards are featured at Koruna Resort.

The bright rooms offer warm-coloured interiors and rustic-style, traditional Gutsul décor.

Each room comes complete with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. The on-site restaurant serves Gutsul and European cuisine, and a variety of drinks is offered at the bar.

Bukovel Ski Resort is 12 km from Koruna. Vorokhta bus and train stations are 5 km away, and Ivano-Frankivsk International Airport is 80 km from the resort.
