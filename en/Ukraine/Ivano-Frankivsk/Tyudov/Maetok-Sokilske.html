Description

All rooms in the hotel are equipped with a TV. The rooms come with a private bathroom and a shower, and some rooms come with a balcony. Guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

The daily breakfast offers buffet and à la carte options. Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant, which specializes in European cuisine.

You can play ping pong at this 3-star hotel, and ski equipment rental is available.

Yaremche is 30 miles from Maetok Sokils'ke, while Vorokhta is 26 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Chernovtsy Airport, 39 miles from the property.
