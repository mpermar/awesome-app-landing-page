Description

Surrounded by the Carpathian Mountains, this hotel is located in the scenic town of Yaremcha.

Free Wi-Fi, as well as indoor and outdoor swimming pools, is featured at Romantik Spa Hotel.

The elegant, classic-style rooms of the hotel offer carpeted floors and warm-coloured interiors.

Each room comes complete with a flat-screen TV, a minibar and a balcony. Romantik Spa Restaurant serves Ukrainian and European cuisine.

Guests can also enjoy drinks at the lobby bar. Yaremcha Souvenir Market is 300 metres from the hotel, and the Probiy Waterfall is 500 metres away.

Bukovel Ski Resort is 30 km from the hotel. Yaremcha Bus Station and Yaremcha Train Station are 2 km from Romantik Spa Hotel.
