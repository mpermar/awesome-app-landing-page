Description

Offering a sauna and water sports facilities, Yaremche Premium is located in Yaremche in the Ivano-Frankivsk Region, 30 km from Bukovel.

The aparthotel has a seasonal outdoor pool and hammam, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

Free WiFi is provided and free private parking is available on site. Rooms come with a flat-screen TV.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. You can engage in various activities, such as skiing and hiking.

Vorokhta is 18 km from Yaremche Premium, while Verkhovyna is 38 km from the property.
