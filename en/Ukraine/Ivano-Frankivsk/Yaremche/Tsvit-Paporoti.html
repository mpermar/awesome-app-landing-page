Description

This hotel is located 5 minutes’ walk from the centre of Yaremche, near the Prut River with a beautiful view of the Elephant rock and Makovitsya Mountain.

It features an indoor swimming pool. All rooms and apartments at Tsvit Paporoti Hotel feature classic-style decor, cable TV and a balcony overlooking the Carpathian Mountains.

Bathrooms come with a shower. Tsvit’s restaurant serves Ukrainian and European cuisine, as well as Hutsul specialities.

All dishes are cooked from local products. Guests of Tsvit Paporoti Hotel can relax in the infrared sauna and order a massage service.

Billiards and a children’s playground can also be found on site. Tsvit Paporoti is 5 minutes’ walk from Yaremche train and bus stations, providing access to Ivano-Frankivsk.

A transfer service to Bukovel Ski Resort is offered, and Ivano-Frankivsk Airport is 1 hour’s drive from the hotel.
