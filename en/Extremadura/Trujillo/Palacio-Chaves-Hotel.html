Description

The spacious rooms of the Palacio Chaves Hotel overlook Trujillo. They feature a flat-screen TV, air conditioning and a private bathroom.

The Chaves Palace has an impressive library with high ceilings and wooden beams. Guests can relax in the bar area or in one of the beautiful lounges.

This impressive hotel is located in a Renaissance building, just 300 meters from the Plaza Mayor de Trujillo. The natural park of Monfragüe is about 25 km away and offers a spectacular natural landscape.
