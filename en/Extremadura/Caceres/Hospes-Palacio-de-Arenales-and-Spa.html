Description

The hotel has a shared pool, garden, terrace and restaurant. It also offers air-conditioned rooms with flat-screen TVs, minibars and private bathrooms with bathrobes and free toiletries.

The hotel has a well-equipped spa and wellness center with a state-of-the-art hydrothermal circuit. In addition, it offers several health and beauty treatments.

Hospes Palacio de Arenales is located just 3 km from the historic center of Cáceres, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, a 15-minute drive from the Norba Golf Club and 7 km from the Llanos de Cáceres Nature Reserve and Sierra de Fuentes.
