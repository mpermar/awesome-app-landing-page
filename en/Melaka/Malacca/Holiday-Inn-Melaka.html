Description

Right next to Mahkota Parade Shopping Mall, Melaka Holiday Inn provides free shuttle services to nearby attractions. It is a 3 hour drive from Singapore and a 2 hour drive from Kuala Lumpur.

Decorated with Malaysian-style fabrics, the air-conditioned rooms offer the comfort of classic furnishings and ample workspace. They include a safe and a private bathroom with a bathtub.

Guests can exercise in the fitness center, or relax in the sauna. Tour and business services are provided.

Wood-fired pizzas are served at Sirocco Italian Restaurant, while hearty buffet spreads with international dishes are offered at es.sence Kitchen. Herron's Kopi Counter and Sirocco Bar are ideal for light snacks.
