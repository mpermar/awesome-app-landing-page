Description

The spacious rooms at Casa del Rio Melaka come with modern décor and hardwood floors. All rooms are well equipped with an iPod docking station and TV with cable channels.

Guests can work out at the fitness center, or enjoy a relaxing massage at the spa. The hotel provides laundry and ironing services. The staff at the 24-hour front desk can assist with guests’ requests.

A buffet breakfast is available at The River Grill. Bar Rio features Strait of Malacca views, and serves light snacks and refreshing beverages.

Melaka Casa del Rio is a short walk from Jonker Street and a 6-minute drive from Melaka Sultanate Palace. Free parking is available.
