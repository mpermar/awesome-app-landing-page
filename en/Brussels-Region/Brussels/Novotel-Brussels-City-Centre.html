Description

Novotel Brussels Centre Tour Noire has modern, spacious rooms with an LCD TV. Other amenities include a work desk with ergonomic chair and a tea/coffee maker. Some rooms feature a bathrobe and slippers.

Guests can work out at the extensive facilities at the InBalance Novotel Wellness area, which includes an indoor jet-stream pool, a hammam, a relaxing area, infrared showers and a fitness center.

Breakfast includes a selection of hot and cold dishes. The Foodsquare brasserie has a large outdoor terrace and serves an international cuisine menu. The bar has a wide range of foreign beers.

The Rue Neuve shopping street is a 5-minute walk from the Novotel Brussels Centre Tour Noire. The Manneken Pis Statue is 10 minutes away by foot. Brussels-Central Train Station is half a mile from the hotel.
