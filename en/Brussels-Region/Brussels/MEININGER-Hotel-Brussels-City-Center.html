Description

Each modern room is decorated with light gray colored walls and a parquet floor. A flat-screen TV and private bathroom with shower are featured as well in the room.

The restaurant and bar are decorated in the style of the factory with large white pillars, the original concrete ceiling and metal factory lamps above the tables.

A 24-hour front desk provides guests service whenever needed. Bicycle rental is available at Meininger Hotel as well as a game room.

The Stock Exchange is one mile from the hotel. Metro station Graaf van Vlaanderen is a 6 minute walk.Brussels Central Station is one mile away. Brussels highway R0 is 8 minutes by car.

Sint-Jans-Molenbeek / Molenbeek-Saint-Jean is a great choice for travelers interested in waffles, walking and beer.
