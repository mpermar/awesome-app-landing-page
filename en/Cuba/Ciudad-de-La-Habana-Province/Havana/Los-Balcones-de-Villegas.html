Description

Units come with a flat-screen TV and DVD player, a kitchenette with a fridge, and a private bathroom. A terrace with mountain views is offered in all units.

A continental, buffet or American breakfast is served at the property.

A babysitting service is also available for guests at the property.

Old Square is an 8-minute walk from the homestay, while Castillo de San Salvador de la Punta is half a mile away. The nearest airport is José Martí International Airport, 11 miles from the property.

Old Havana is a great choice for travelers interested in atmosphere, live music and street musicians.
