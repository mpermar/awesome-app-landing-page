Description

At the hostel, all rooms include a closet. Certain rooms include a kitchenette with a dishwasher, an oven and a microwave. Guest rooms at Hostel Casa de Ania are equipped with a seating area.

A continental, à la carte or Full English/Irish breakfast is available every morning at the property.

Speaking English and Spanish, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical guidance on the area at the reception.

Old Square is 1.7 miles from the accommodation. José Martí International Airport is 10 miles away.
