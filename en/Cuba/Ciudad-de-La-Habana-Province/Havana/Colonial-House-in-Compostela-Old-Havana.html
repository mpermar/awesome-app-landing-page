Description

The rooms at the guesthouse are equipped with a seating area. Every room has a private bathroom and a bath, and certain rooms have a balcony. All rooms have a desk.

The daily breakfast offers continental and la carte options.

Colonial House in Compostela has a playground. Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Havana, like cycling.

A business center is at guests' disposal at Colonial House in Compostela - Old Havana. Speaking Spanish and English at the reception, staff are ready to help around the clock.

Old Square is a 6-minute walk from the guesthouse, while Castillo de San Salvador de la Punta is 0.6 miles away. The nearest airport is Jos Mart International Airport, 11 miles from Colonial House in Compostela.

Old Havana is a great choice for travelers interested in atmosphere, live music and street musicians.
