Description

All air-conditioned units at the love hotel come with a hot tub, free toiletries, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, Blu-ray player and DVD-Player. Every room has a coffee machine and a private bathroom, while some rooms also feature a balcony. The rooms include a seating area.

Suite Plaza Vieja, "Luxury Holiday in Old Havana" offers a continental or à la carte breakfast.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental and car rental are available at the accommodation.

La Bodeguita del Medio is a 7-minute walk from Suite Plaza Vieja, "Luxury Holiday in Old Havana", while Castillo de San Salvador de la Punta is 0.9 miles from the property. José Martí International Airport is 10 miles away.

Old Havana is a great choice for travelers interested in atmosphere, live music and street musicians.
