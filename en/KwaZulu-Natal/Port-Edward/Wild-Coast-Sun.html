Description

Each air-conditioned room at the Wild Coast Sun has a private bathroom, satellite TV and tea/coffee-making facilities. Breakfast is served daily in the hotel's restaurant.

The hotel is located only a mile south of KwaZulu-Natal. The property has a casino and also has a collection of shops. Guests can also enjoy a game of golf at the Wild Coast Country Club.
