Description

At Hotel y Restaurante Rincón Familiar, the rooms come with a patio with a pool view. The rooms have a private bathroom, while some feature a garden view. All guest rooms feature a seating area.

A continental breakfast is available daily at Hotel y Restaurante Rincón Familiar.

Hotel and Restaurant Rincón Familiar has a playground.

San Salvador is 16 miles from Hotel y Restaurante Rincón Familiar, while La Herradura is 26 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Monseñor Oscar Arnulfo Romero International Airport, 17 miles from the property.
