Description

This beach front property is located on a private beach in La Libertad, Hotel Los Farallones offers tropical gardens, a private pool and a sun terrace with hammocks and sea views.

T he air-conditioned rooms feature flat-screen cable TV. All rooms offer a DVD player and a safe, while the private bathrooms come with toiletries.

Los Farallones has its own bar and restaurant. You can also find a range of restaurants within a 15-minute walk.

The popular surfing spot of El Sunzal is just a 5-minute drive from the hotel. El Salvador International Airport is a 30-minute drive away, while San Salvador is 40 minutes away.
