Description

Offering an outdoor swimming pool and a restaurant, Atami Escape Resort is located in La Libertad.

Free WiFi access and free private parking available. Each room here will provide you with a cable TV, air conditioning and a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries.

You can enjoy pool view and garden view from the room. Extras include bed linen. At Atami Escape Resort you will find a private beach area and surf lessons.
