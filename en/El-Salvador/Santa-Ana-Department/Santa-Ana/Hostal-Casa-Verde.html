Description

Offering an outdoor pool and free bicycle rentals, Hostal Casa Verde is located in Santa Ana.

Free WiFi access is available throughout. Shared and private rooms are available at Hostal Casa Verde.

They are all equipped with a flat-screen TV. Some have a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

You can enjoy mountain view and city view from the room. Towels and linens are available with surcharge.

Facilities at Hostal Casa Verde include a 24-hour front desk, a communal terrace and a shared kitchen for the preparation of meals.

Lockers, on-site laundry facilities, a lounge area and a games room are also provided.

The town square is within 12 minutes' walk, while the nearest bus station is an 8 minutes' walk.
