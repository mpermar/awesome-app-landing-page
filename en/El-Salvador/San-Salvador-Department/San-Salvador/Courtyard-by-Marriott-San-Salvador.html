Description

Air-conditioned rooms have flat-screen cable TV, a CD player and laptop safe. There is a minibar, work desk and coffee maker. The marble bathroom comes equipped with a bathtub, shower and hairdryer.

The hotel’s La Puerta del Sol Restaurant serves a buffet breakfast. Guests can enjoy a drink in the Lobby Bar. There is also shopping, cafés and restaurants within a short walk of the hotel.
