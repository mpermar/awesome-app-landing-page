Description

All units come with a dishwasher, oven, a coffee machine, a microwave and electric tea pot. There's a seating and/or dining area in some units.

Guests at the resort village can enjoy a buffet breakfast. The in-house restaurant specializes in French cuisine.

VVF Villages Bleriot Plage has a playground.

Guests can enjoy the indoor pool at the accommodation.

Calais is 2.2 miles from VVF Villages Bleriot Plage. The nearest airport is Le Touquet Airport, 32 miles from the resort village.
