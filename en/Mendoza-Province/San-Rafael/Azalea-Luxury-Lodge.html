Description

Opening to a terrace with pool or garden views, all units at the resort village are fitted with a kitchen with an oven and a microwave. A fridge and coffee machine are also featured.

A à la carte breakfast is served at the property. There's an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of local dishes.

Guests can use the hot tub.

An outdoor swimming pool can be found at the property, along with a grill.

Hipolito Yrigoyen park is 2.5 miles from Azalea Luxury Lodge. The nearest airport is San Rafael Airport, 1.2 miles from the property.
