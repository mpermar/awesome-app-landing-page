Description

This hotel complex is set in the Trivale Park, just a 5-minute drive from Pitesti city centre.

Hornul Vanatorului offers free WiFi and 7 restaurants. Rooms at the Tourist Complex The Hunting Horn feature classic d√©cor.

All rooms are air-conditioned and include a flat-screen TV and private bathroom. A continental breakfast is provided each morning in the restaurant with its stylish white design and wooden floorboards.

The Hornul Vanatorului is 1 km from Pitesti Zoo and 2 km from Pitesti Train Station.
