Description

Situated near Ploieşti, Sky Blue Hotel & Spa offers a free, seasonal outdoor pool, an extensive spa and wellness centre and buffet breakfast each morning.

Free WiFi internet is available throughout the hotel. Each elegantly furnished room is air-conditioned and has a balcony overlooking the pool area.

The bathrooms are fitted with a bathtub and equipped with a hairdryer, free toiletries and slippers.

The suites are additionally providing a separate seating area. Room service is also available.

Guests of the Sky Blue Hotel & Spa have free access to the wellness area, including a hammam, a Finnish sauna and a steam sauna, a hot tub and a salt room.

Other on-site facilities comprise a fitness centre with free access, a tennis court, a playground for children, a bar and a bowling alley.

Massages are available at a surcharge. Ploiești Shopping City is 4. 7 km away, while Ploieşti North Train Station is 9 km from the Sky Blue Hotel.

Bucharest's Henri Coandă International Airport is located 60 km away. Shuttle service is available at an additional cost.
