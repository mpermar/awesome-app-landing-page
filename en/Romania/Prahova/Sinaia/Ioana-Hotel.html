Description

The boutique-style Ioana Hotel can be found in quiet surroundings in the Bucegi mountains above Sinaia.

It offers panoramic views, a spa centre and easy access to the ski slopes. All rooms at the Hotel Ioana are tastefully styled in pastel colours and feature high-quality beds and a balcony, as well as individual decors.

Each of them comes equipped with a flat-screen TV. Bathrobes and slippers can be found in the elegant bathroom.

Spa facilities include a sauna, a hot tub with free access for guests, and a wide range of massages upon request.

The little ones spend their time in the children's playroom. The Forest Restaurant serves international cuisine and local specialties to guests who dine surrounded by growing fir trees.

You can also relax at the Focus Bar and Lounge, or sip a glass of wine recommended by the winebar's sommelier.

There is a cable car only 300 metres away from the Ioana Hotel, and the Peles and Pelisor Castles can be reached on foot within 30 minutes.

A minibus stops right in front of the hotel but the centre of Sinaia is also reachable on foot within 40 minutes.
