Description

Located in Sinaia, 1 km from the Sinaia Ski Slope, New Aosta Garden offers a terrace and a garden with barbecue facilities and a childrens playground.

Free Wi-Fi is provided in the entire building. The rooms of New Aosta Garden include a private bathroom with free toiletries, and a TV.

They offer views of the mountains. The guest house features 2 refrigerators, in the shared living room, for guests.

The town centre can be reached in a 10-minute walk. The Kalinderu Ski Slope is 4 km away and Peles Castle is 1.
