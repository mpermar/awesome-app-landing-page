Description

The hotel is only 20 minutes from Henri Coanda International Airport, and it offers luxurious and well-equipped rooms with free Wi-Fi and many superb facilities.

The Radisson Blu houses Romania's biggest casino – the Platinum Casino as well as several exclusive shops and the World Class Health Academy.

The Academy is a spa center where you can unwind at indoor and outdoor pools and enjoy the exotic Bali Spa, which recreates the atmosphere and spirit of Asia with state-of-the-art facilities and a wide range of treatments.

The Radisson offers a selection of restaurants and bars with diverse international food which will ensure culinary delights.

Sector 1 is a great choice for travelers interested in museums, architecture and food.
