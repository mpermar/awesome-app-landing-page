Description

Guests at the Epoque enjoy free access to a spa center with a Finnish sauna, a steam room, a hot tub, an indoor pool and a fitness center. Various massages are also available, at an extra fee.

With interior of neutral colors and with attentively chosen design touches inspired by the Neo-Romanian architectural style, some units have a french balcony and views of the grounds.

A pillow menu is available, and luxury toiletries are provided in the private bathroom. Each guest room has a well-lit work desk as well as a comfortable living area fitted with an overstuffed sofa, and all suites have 2 flat-screen TVs.

Guests can taste the refined French Haute Cuisine with a contemporary touch in L'atelier - l'art culinaire restaurant led by a French resident Executive Chef. A variety of vegetarian dishes is also available. An apéritif can also be enjoyed in its atrium.

Hotel Epoque is just over half a mile from the Parliament Palace, the Romanian National Opera and the National Museum of Art. It is also in a close proximity of Bucharest's business and bank district. Izvor Metro Station is approximately a 5-minute walk away.

Sector 1 is a great choice for travelers interested in museums, architecture and food.
