Description

Harmonia Mundi has a restaurant, bar, a shared lounge and garden in Vlaha. Boasting a shared kitchen, this property also provides guests with a barbecue.

There is a children's playground and guests can make use of free WiFi and free private parking.

All guest rooms at the guest house come with a seating area. At Harmonia Mundi the rooms come with a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, and a patio with a garden view.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet breakfast. Harmonia Mundi offers 4-star accommodation with a sauna and sun terrace.

You can play table tennis and darts at the guest house, and the area is popular for cycling.

Cluj-Napoca is 19 km from Harmonia Mundi. The nearest airport is Cluj Avram Iancu International, 27 km from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
