Description

Surrounded by a broadleaf forest and set on a hill, 30 km from Buzău, Cetatuia offers accommodation with free WiFi, an à la carte restaurant, and a terrace.

Rooms overlook the forest, and are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

They are fitted with a private bathroom equipped with a shower. For your comfort, you will find slippers and free toiletries.

Among the activities available at this property, you can play table tennis, try archery or take a ride with a mountain bike.

There is also a meeting room with a projector and a flip chart, which can be used for free by the guests.

Free private parking is available on site. The restaurant offers local and international dishes, and during the warm seasons, you can take your meals on the outdoor terrace.

Guests can also relax in the garden, or in the gazebo. Ciolanu Monastery is 3 km from this property.

The Mud Volcanoes are at 30 km, Siriu is 57 km from Cetatuia, while Sărata-Monteoru can be reached within 33 km.
