Description

The Septimia Resort - Hotel, Wellness & Spa in Odorheiu Secuiesc offers a wide range of wellness and sports facilities, including indoor and outdoor pools, free safe parking and free WiFi.

The wellness facilities are including pools, saunas and hot tubs, some with salty water, and they can be used at a surcharge.

Sporty guests can benefit from the on-site football, tennis, volleyball and squash courts.

Table tennis can also be enjoyed at a surcharge. A bowling alley and a bar are available as well.

All rooms in the Septimia Resort - Hotel, Wellness & Spa are air-conditioned and feature a satellite TV and bathrobes.

Fine Romanian and international cuisine is served in the à-la-carte restaurant. There is also a bar and a café.
