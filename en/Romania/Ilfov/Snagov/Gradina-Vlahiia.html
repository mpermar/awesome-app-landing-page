Description

Guest rooms at the resort are equipped with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a bath. All guest rooms at Gradina Vlahiia include air conditioning and a desk.

A à la carte breakfast is available every morning at the accommodation.

Bucharest is 20 miles from Gradina Vlahiia. The nearest airport is Bucharest Henri Coandă International Airport, 11 miles from the property.
