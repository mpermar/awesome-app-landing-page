Description

Set in Otopeni, Bucharest Airport Suites & Villas, offers private accommodation,1 km away from Henri Coandă International Airport.

Paid airport pick-up and drop-off are provided. Free WiFi throughout the property is available.

The air-conditioned apartments include a fully-equipped kitchen and a flat-screen TV.

Other facilities include a barbecue. some units include a private pool. The property can arrange a car rental for you upon request.

Guests can play table tennis at the property, and the area is popular for cycling.
