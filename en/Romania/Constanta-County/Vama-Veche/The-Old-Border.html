Description

The Old Border is a marine-themed boutique hotel in Vama Veche. With barbecue facilities, the property also features a garden.

It offers rooms with air conditioning, private bathrooms, and a mini-fridge. Complimentary coffee, tea and fruits are offered.

The property features sea views, a children's playground, and free WiFi is available throughout the property.

The reception can provide advice on the area in order to help guests plan their day.

The nearest airport is Mihail Kogălniceanu International Airport, 69 km from The Old Border.
