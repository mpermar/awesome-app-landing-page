Description

Zev Mamaia offers air-conditioned units in a quiet area of ​​Mamaia, next to Lake Siutghiol.

Free Wi-Fi is available in the entire property and free private parking is also provided.

The bright rooms at Zev Mamaia have unique, modernly decorated and personalized interiors.

Some rooms offer lake views, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

At the ground floor, you will find the hotel's restaurant, Zev Sushi & Lounge, with lakeside terrace, where you can enjoy Japanese-style cuisine.

The property features 24 hour reception. Guests benefit from easy access in a wide variety of restaurants and summer clubs.

H2O Beach is 1. 4 km from Zev Mamaia, and the Mamaia Casino area is about 3 km from the property.

The Aqua Magic Park is about 4. 6 km away. Mihail Kogalniceanu Airport is 36. 5 km away.
