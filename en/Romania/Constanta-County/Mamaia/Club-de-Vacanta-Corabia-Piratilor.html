Description

Holiday Club Corabia Piratilor is situated 300 meters from the beach in Mamaia Nord.

It offers a restaurant inside a replica ship and 2 seasonal swimming pools. Wi-Fi is free.

Free private parking is possible on site. The air-conditioned apartments come with spacious living rooms and flat-screen TVs.

Each has a kitchenette and a private terrace with a grass lawn and barbecue. Located on a quiet street, Vacanta Corabia Piratilor is 2 km from Mamaia and 7 km from Constanta.

There is a Bus Stop right in front of the hotel, providing links to both resorts.
