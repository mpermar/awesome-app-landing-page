Description

Located in the heart of the Mamaia resort, on the Siutghiol Lake's shore, Imago Boutique Hotel offers you lake-view rooms with free WiFi.

Free bicycles are offered for guests and parking is free of charge. From the Imago Boutique Hotel's lounge bar you can see the Siutghiol lake as well.

A Swedish buffet breakfast is served every morning, and you can sip your coffee on a sunbed, on the dock.

You will be greeted with a free welcome drink upon arrival. Guests can go with a kayak on the lake, or go fishing with the gear provided by the property.

The sandy beach by the Black Sea can be reached in less than 10 minutes on foot. There is a hypermarket Mega Image 350 metres away from the property.
