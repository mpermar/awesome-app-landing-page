Description

Nayino Resort Hotel is located in Mamaia Nord resort, 100 metres from NƒÉvodari beach, one of the top-rated beaches in Romania.

The property offers an outdoor pool, a rooftop lounge, bike rental and free WiFi.

The rooms and suites offer views of the Black Sea, or of the colourful garden by the pool.

All the units are furnished in light and natural colours that reflect the sea and the tropical forest, and come with a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

The rooms in the lodges by the pool have access to a private grass terrace. Guests can rent bikes on site and begin exploring the surroundings.

The outdoor pool includes a separate pool area for children. You can savour Mediterranean-inspired cuisine at Nayino Restaurant.

Drinks and appetizers can be found at the 2 on-site bars, one of which is a rooftop bar.

Free private parking is available on site. At Nayino Resort Hotel you will be close to windsurfing, kite surfing and other water sports' facilities.
