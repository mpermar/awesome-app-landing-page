Description

The G. G. Gociman hotel in the Mamaia resort between the Black Sea coast and Lake Siutghiol offers air-conditioned units and free WiFi, as well as free access to a heated swimming pool with a constant temperature of 26 °C.

Romanian and international cuisine can be enjoyed in the restaurant or on the charming lakeside terrace during the warm season.

You can park your car for free on site. The AquaMagic water park and the Casino can be found close to the G.
