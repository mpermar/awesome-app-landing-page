Description

Just 30 metres from Lake Bellona and 50 metres from the sandy Eforie Nord Beach on the Black Sea Coast, this hotel offers a children’s playground and free private parking.

The air-conditioned rooms at Hotel Anca include free WiFi, a flat-screen TV and minibar, as well as a safety deposit box.

The en suite bathrooms include luxury toiletries and a hairdryer. Guests can play tennis table and billiar outside on the hotel terrace.

Breakfast is served each morning in the on-site restaurant, while other dining options can be found within walking distance.

Eforie Nord Train Station is a 10-minute walk from the hotel, offering direct connections to Constanta in just 10 minutes.
