Description

All rooms have views of the sea or the lake, and are fitted with a flat-screen cable TV, and a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries.

Guests can fish in the Belona Lake or have mud treatments at the nearby Techirghiol Lake. Hotel Vis also includes a children’s playground and free private parking.

The closest restaurant is 350 feet away, and the promenade area next to the sea, with many shops and bars, is also within walking distance.

The Eforie Nord Train Station is 500 feet away, and the nearest bus stop can be reached within 950 feet.

08/13/2016

A room with a view

"We arrived early Monday morning and were pleasantly surprised to find out that the room was ready, 'cause the other guests left really early. It was great not to have to wait until check-in time at 14:00, as we were there with our 19 months old baby girl. The room is really big, all the rooms face the lake and the sea shore. The furniture is simple, but elegant. It has air-conditioning and a small lcd tv. we had a double room, but found out on our last day there that the armchair could easily be made into a bed. There is a clothes wire for hanging up to dry the wet things you bring back from the beach. The bathroom is quite small, but there is no bad smell anywhere (some seaside hotels smell like they're moldy, this was not the case).There is a dining place down stairs where they serve breakfast between 8:00 and 10:00 am. The food is the same everyday, but there are plenty of things to choose from, so you won',t get bored. They have there a special chair for babies, which was very useful, as our daughter tends to run around from place to place if I don't strap her in the chair during feeding times. There is also a play area for children in the lobby and outside. There are plenty of couches in the lobby and tables on the terrace for relaxation and to enjoy a cup of coffee or a glass of juice.Overall, enjoy your stay at this hotel is a nice place. We were lucky that the circus was near by (20 meters), because there is not much else to do at night in this town."
