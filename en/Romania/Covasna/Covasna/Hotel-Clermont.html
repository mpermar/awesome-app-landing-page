Description

Hotel Clermont enjoys a quiet location, 2. 5 km from the small town Covasna. It offers a modern indoor pool and a tennis court.

A bowling alley is also available. All elegant rooms are air-conditioned and have a balcony.

Satellite flat-screen TVs, free access to Wi-Fi and a work desk are at guests’ disposal.

The modern bathrooms are all equipped with toiletries, bathrobes and slippers. The Clermont’s spa area features a hot tub and a sauna.

Beauty and body treatments, as well as health treatments are offered. The spacious fitness centre is at guests’ disposal.

Guests can enjoy the restaurant’s international cuisine. Meals are also served on the terrace in nice weather.

Room service is provided on request. A climbing wall and a zip line course is available for guests at an extra cost.

Also, a billiards and a ping-pong table can be found on site. The children’s playground is set in the garden.

Clermont Hotel provides free private parking on site with video surveillance. The property can also arrange for you paid trips around the region and transfers.
