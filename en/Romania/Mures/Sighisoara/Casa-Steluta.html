Description

Casa Steluta is situated in Sighişoara, within a 7-minute walk from the Medieval Citadel.

Free WiFi is offered throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

The apartments have a flat-screen TV, and a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

Each unit has a fully-equipped kitchen and a private bathroom with free toiletries.

You can relax in the private furnished garden, where you will find a barbecue and barbecue wood.

There is also a children playground. Târgu-Mureş is 54. 2 km from Casa Steluta, while Praid is 74 km away.

The nearest airport is Târgu Mureş Airport, 60 km from the property. Free private parking is available.
