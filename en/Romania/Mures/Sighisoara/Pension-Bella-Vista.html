Description

Offering panoramic views over Sighisoara, Pension Bella Vista enjoys a quiet location next to the forest and features rooms with free WiFi.

The city centre is 2 km away. The rooms at the Bella Vista guest house feature wooden floors, a TV and a bathroom.

Some units come with a balcony. The on-site restaurant serves a selection of simple dishes, while an à la carte restaurant can be found within 400 metres.

Guests can enjoy playing table tennis. On request, bicycles can also be borrowed at Bella Vista free of charge.

Free private parking is available on the premises. Sighisoara Train Station is 1.

2 km away. Airport transfers from Tirgu Mures, Sibiu, and Cluj-Napoca are available on request.
