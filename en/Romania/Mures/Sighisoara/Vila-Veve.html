Description

Vila Veve provides accommodation in Sighișoara. Free WiFi is provided and free private parking is available on site.

Fitted with a terrace, the units feature a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with bath.

Some units include a balcony and/or a patio with garden views. The aparthotel offers a barbecue.

A children's playground is also available for guests at the property. Sovata is 45 km from Vila Veve.
