Description

An entirely renovated 17th-century building, the Pension Castel Haller enjoys a quiet location in Targu Mures, 200 metres from the Mures River.

It features a restaurant serving international cuisine. The air-conditioned rooms are equipped with period-style furniture and many have historic parquet floors and vaulted ceilings.

Free Wi-Fi is available in the entire hotel. Free private parking is available on site.

Situated off the number 15 road, Pension Castel Haller is only a 15-minute drive away from Targu Mures Airport.
