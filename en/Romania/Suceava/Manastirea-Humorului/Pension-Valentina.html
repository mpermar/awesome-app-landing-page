Description

Pension Valentina enjoys a quiet location in Minastirea Humorului, in northern Romania.

It offers comfortable rooms with balconies and flat-screen cable TVs. Free Wi-Fi is available in the lobby.

The Valentina's restaurant is set in a spacious dining room with large windows, overlooking the garden.

Romanian and regional dishes are served in the restaurant. A bar and barbecue facilities are also on site.

A common living room with a bar is at guests’ disposal. A children’s playground is available.

A selling exhibition of Romanian Orthodox icons and traditional Bucovina decorations is available on site.

Slopes and cable cars can be reached within a 10-minute drive from Pension Valentina.

The center of Gura Humorului is 5 km away. Suceava Fortress is a 35-minute drive away.
