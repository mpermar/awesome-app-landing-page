Description

Set in the historical region of Bucovina, Pensiunea Vranis is within 15 km from the painted monasteries Voronet and Humor, and features free WiFi and an à la carte restaurant with a terrace.

All units in the guest house have mountain views and are equipped with a flat-screen cable TV and a private bathroom with a shower, a bidet, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Some also have a balcony. The restaurant serves both traditional Romanian and international cuisine.

Guests at Pensiunea Vranis also have access to a bar on site. Free private parking is available at the guest house, while the nearest train station is 100 metres away.

The Wood Art Museum is 4 km away and the Pietrele Doamnei National Reserve is 15 km from the property.
