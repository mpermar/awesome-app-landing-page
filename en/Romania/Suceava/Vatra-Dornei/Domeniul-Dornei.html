Description

Boasting a modern spa area, to which guests have free access, Domeniul Dornei is amongst the Bistrita Mountains in Dirna Arini, a 10-minute drive from Vatra Dornei.

WiFi is free and free parking is under surveillance. Elegant rooms feature touches of Bucovina and Maramures elements and views of the surroundings from the balcony.

An LED cable TV and minibar are provided. Bathrooms have a shower or bath and free toiletries.

Spa facilities include dry and wet saunas, an indoor hot tub, fitness centre and indoor pool.

A wide variety of massages are available. The in-house restaurant serves traditional dishes with modern flare using ingredients grown on site.

The private lake and sun terrace are great places to relax and enjoy the surroundings.

Barbecue facilities and the gazebo can also be used. Children are kept entertained in the play room and it is also possible to arrange sleigh and carriage rides.

Laundry services are on site. Nearby activities include rafting, horse riding and skiing, all within 5 km.
