Description

Located on the main road passing through Gura Humorului, in Suceava county, Casa Nobilis features a children's playground and views of the garden.

Free private parking is available on site. Every room comes with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

You will find a kettle in the room. Each room has a private bathroom. Extras include free toiletries and a hair dryer.

Casa Nobilis features free WiFi throughout the property. There is also a shared, fully-equipped kitchen.

Free use of bicycles is available at this guest house and the area is popular for cycling.

There is a gas station just across the road from the property. Suceava is 36 km from Casa Nobilis, while Câmpulung Moldovenesc is 32 km away.
