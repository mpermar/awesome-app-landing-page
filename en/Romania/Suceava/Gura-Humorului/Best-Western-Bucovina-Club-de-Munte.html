Description

Located in the centre of Gura Humorului, Best Western Bucovina Club de Munte offers 2 restaurants, a terrace, a spa area, and free WiFi.

Şoimul Ski Area, Ariniş Park and Aventura Park leisure centres are within a 10-minute walking distance.

All rooms at Best Western Bucovina Club de Munte come with a LCD TV with cable channels, a minibar, and a private bathroom with free toiletries.

The on-site Vitality Spa features a sauna, a hammam, and a hot tub. Various types of relaxing massages and therapies are available on request.

The hotel features a kids' club with an indoor playground, air hockey, fussball and Xbox.

The hotel’s 2 restaurants serve traditional Romanian cuisine, international dishes, and a selection of vegetarian meals.

A large selection of drinks, including cocktails and coffees, is offered in the Lobby Bar.

The hotel’s private car park can be used free of charge and is under constant video surveillance.
