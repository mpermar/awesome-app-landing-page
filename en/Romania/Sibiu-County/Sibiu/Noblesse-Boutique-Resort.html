Description

Located within less than 1 km from the historical centre of Sibiu and 500 metres from the famous Council Tower of Sibiu, Noblesse Boutique Resort offers a place where the citys charm is very present.

The property is decorated with exquisite paintings and provides free access to the fitness centre.

Featuring comfortable air-conditioned rooms with black-out curtains and feather pillows, each one has a different style.

All are made to assure a perfect rest and recharge. The rooms and suites come with a minibar, a flat-screen TV and a modern bathroom with bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries.

The property welcomes guests with an on-site restaurant and bar. The breakfast features a buffet with a variety of continental and local traditional options, with the restaurant offering an international menu.

Moreover the accommodation includes a fully equipped conference room and a dedicated meeting lounge for different events.

You will find a 24-hour front desk service and a gift shop at the property. The accommodation offers bike hire, as well as car hire for guests.

The surrounding area is popular for cycling. The popular Great Square and Albert Huet Square are only 600 metres from Noblesse Boutique Resort.
