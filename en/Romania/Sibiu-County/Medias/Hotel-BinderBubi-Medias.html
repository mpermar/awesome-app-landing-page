Description

Located in a green and peaceful area, 2 km from the centre of Medias, Hotel BinderBubi features a state-of-the-art spa centre and a heated, indoor pool.

Free WiFi is offered The rooms and suites have minibars, and cable TV. Air conditioning comes as a standard amenity.

Guests can benefit from the bicycle rental service, and they can play paint ball in the vicinity.

Guided excursions to medieval towns such as Medias, Sighisoara or the Saxon fortified churches of Biertan and Mosna can be organised by the BinderBubi Medias Hotel.
