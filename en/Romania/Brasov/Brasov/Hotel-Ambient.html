Description

Ideally located in the centre of Brasov, a few minutes away from the historic monuments, elegant bars and glamorous shops, this hotel is a reference point for luxury accommodation.

Enjoy superb views of the city, perfect service and culinary delights in the hotel's restaurant.

Utmost attention is paid to every single detail, ensuring that you spend an unforgettable stay at the Ambient hotel.

Guests can enjoy their meals at the hotel's restaurant, as well as festive dinners on holiday dates, for an extra charge.

Don't miss to explore the picturesque Old Town of Brasov with its mansions of the late Middle Ages and elegant 19th-century buildings.

Guests of this property benefit from discounts and preferential prices to several restaurants, pubs, as well as cultural and touristic sites.
