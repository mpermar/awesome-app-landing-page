Description

Enjoying an elevated position 10 minutes' walk from the centre of Brasov, Hotel Esprit offers panoramic views over the entire city.

An indoor pool is available. Esprit Hotel features a panorama elevator and modern rooms with individually adjustable air-conditioning, massage showers and flat-screen cable TVs.

Guests benefit from complimentary access to an indoor pool and sauna, as well as a fitness room.

Massage services are at guests' disposal at a surcharge. Private parking and internet access are available for free.

Every morning a full English breakfast is served, which also includes Romanian specialities.

The Poiana Brasov ski resort is only a 15-minute drive away from the Hotel Esprit.
