Description

Featuring rooms with free WiFi, Casa Corona is located in Braşov, 300 metres from the Blacksmiths' Bastion.

Guests can relax in the common TV lounge, and free private parking is possible at the property.

All rooms are fitted with wooden floors and feature a flat-screen cable TV. Guests can enjoy views of the surrounding mountains in each room and there’s a bathroom with a shower or a bathtub provided in all of Casa Corona’s units.

A ski storage room can be used on site and airport shuttle services can be requested.

Braşov Old Town is 300 metres away and the closest restaurant is within the same distance.
