Description

Set in Rîşnov, Maggie's Ranch features a garden and a terrace. Located around 8 km from Dino Parc, the guest house is also 7 km away from Rasnov Fortress.

The property features mountain views, an outdoor pool, and free WiFi is available throughout.

At the guest house each room is fitted with a terrace. With a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower and free toiletries, some rooms at Maggie's Ranch also provide guests with pool view.

The units feature a wardrobe. The accommodation offers a children's playground. The area is popular for cycling, and bike hire is available at Maggie's Ranch.

The guest house can conveniently provide information at the reception to help guests to get around the area.
