Description

Located in the Poiana Brasov Ski Resort, 1 km from the ski lift, Royal Boutique Hotel offers free access to a sauna, as well as an on-site bar serving a wide array of drinks.

Free WiFi is available and free private parking is possible on site. Rooms of Royal Boutique Hotel are located in one of the two buildings.

They come with a cable TV, a refrigerator, a wardrobe and a private bathroom with a bath or a shower and free toiletries.

In most rooms, you will also find a balcony with mountain views. Some units also feature a sofa.

Further facilities at Royal Boutique Hotel include a children's playground and a terrace.

Billiards and darts are available at a surcharge. The front desk is open 24-hours.

You can have breakfast on-site. This hotel has ski storage space and bike hire is available.

Braşov is 12 km from Royal Boutique Hotel, while Rasnov Fortress can be reached in 10 km.
