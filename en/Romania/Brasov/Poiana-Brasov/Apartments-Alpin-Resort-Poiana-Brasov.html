Description

The private Apartments Alpin Poiana Brasov offer accommodation within Aparthotel Alpin in the centre of Brasov, and provides free access to the resort's facilities, which include a semi-Olympic pool.

Free WiFi is featured throughout the property. The Apartments Alpin Poiana Brasov offer flat-screen satellite TV in the living room and in both bedrooms.

They have fully-equipped kitchenettes with full dinnerware sets and cutlery, a refrigerator, a coffee machine, and a microwave.

A full stocked minibar is provided in each bedroom. From the terrace, guests can enjoy mountain views.

Guests of this accommodation have access to the Alpin Resort Hotel's facilities, including the spa centre, a hot tub, a gym, a dry and wet sauna.

There are 2 children's playgrounds and a children's pool, shops, 4 restaurants with international cuisine, 2 terraces and summer gardens, bars, a nightclub, You will also find in the resort meeting rooms that can accommodate up to 700 people.

A free shuttle to the ski slopes departs every 5 minutes from the Alpin Resort Hotel in the winter.

At the Alpin Resort, guests can find a ski school and ski storage in winter, a bicycle school and bike rental in summer, and hiking and trips activities throughout the year.

Apartments Alpin Poiana Brasov are 12 km from the Old Town of Brasov. The nearest airport is at Otopeni, 147 km from here, and an airport transfer service is available for a surcharge.
