Description

Quietly set in the picturesque area of Bran, 4 km from the Bran Castle, Pensiunea Cerbul Carpatin features accommodation with cable TV, as well as a shared living room with a fireplace and WiFi access.

All functionally-furnished units are equipped with a cable TV and a private bathroom with free toiletries.

Some rooms also have a balcony with mountain views. Guests at Pensiunea Cerbul Carpatin have access to a shared kitchen, barbecue facilities and an outdoor terrace.

Breakfast can be served upon request, while the closest restaurant is 500 metres away.

Free private parking is available on site and a bus stop is 200 metres from the property.

The Rasnov Citadel is 10 km away and the city of Brasov can be reached within 25 km.
