Description

Situated in Bran, Şimon in Brasov Region only 1. 5 km from Dracula Castle, Casa Mon Reve features ski storage space.

Free WiFi is featured throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Certain units have a seating area for your convenience.

Certain rooms feature views of the mountain or garden. Rooms have a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower.

There is a shared lounge at the property. A range of activities are offered in the area, such as skiing, horse riding and cycling.

Braşov is 24 km from Casa Mon Reve, while Poiana Brasov is 19 km from the property.
