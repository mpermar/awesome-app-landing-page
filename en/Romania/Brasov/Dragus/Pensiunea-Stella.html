Description

Situated in Dragus, Pensiunea Stella boasts views of the mountain, a children's playground and a terrace.

Free WiFi is offered and free private parking is available on site. Every room has a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Certain rooms feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day. Rooms include a private bathroom.

Extras include free toiletries and a hairdryer. There is a shared lounge at the property.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant. You can engage in various activities, such as skiing and hiking.

Brancoveanu Monastery is 2. 5 km away. Sîmbăta de Sus is 10 km from the property.
