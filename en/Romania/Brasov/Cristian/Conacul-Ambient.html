Description

Offering an indoor pool, a sauna and a restaurant, Conacul Ambient is located in Cristian.

Free WiFi access is available in this resort. The property offers free parking. All elegantly furnished and soundproofing rooms at Conacul Ambient will provide you with a flat-screen cable TV.

Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer, free toiletries and bathrobes.

A tumble dryer is also provided. You can enjoy mountain view from the room. At Conacul Ambient you will find a multisport field, a snooker table and table tennis.

Other facilities offered at the property include meeting facilities, a shared lounge and a games room.

For little guests there is a children´s playground. Sport activities that can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, include cycling, horse-back riding and hiking.

Guests of this property benefit from discounts and preferential prices to several restaurants, pubs, as well as cultural and touristic sites.

The closest ski slope is reachable within a 10-minute drive as well as the Brasov Train Station.
