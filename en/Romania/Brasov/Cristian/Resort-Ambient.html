Description

Offering a hot tub, a heated indoor pool and ski storage, Resort Ambient is situated in Cristian, 7 km from Braşov.

The resort has a seasonal outdoor pool and children's playground. The rooms of Resort Ambient come with beamed ceilings and traditional furniture in pastel colours.

The amenities include a flat-screen TV with cable channels, bathrobes and slippers, as well as free toiletries.

Certain units feature a seating area for your convenience. You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property.

You can play table tennis and billiards here, while children have a playground at their disposal.

Guests can savour their meal in the stylish Edelweiss restaurant, or relax with a drink from the bar.

The restaurant serves international and local dishes. The resort also offers car hire so you can explore the surroundings, such as Râșnov Citadel, a 15-minute drive away.
