Description

Lebada Luxury Resort & Spa features a restaurant, bar, a shared lounge and garden in Crisan.

Among the facilities at this property are room service and a kids' club, along with free WiFi throughout the property.

The hotel has an indoor pool, fitness centre, evening entertainment and a 24-hour front desk.

Guests at the hotel can enjoy a continental or a buffet breakfast. Lebada Luxury Resort & Spa offers 5-star accommodation with a sauna and children's playground.

You can play tennis at the accommodation. Tulcea is 41 km from Lebada Luxury Resort & Spa.
