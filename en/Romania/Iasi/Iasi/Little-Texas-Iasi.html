Description

Located in Iasi, 4 km from the International Airport and 3 km from the city centre, Little Texas Iasi offers free WiFi, a 24-hour front desk and a garden with a terrace.

Free parking spaces are available on site. The air-conditioned rooms at Little Texas come with a private bathroom with a shower, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a balcony.

They also feature a minibar and a seating area. Guests can enjoy a daily breakfast at the hotel and dine at the on-site à-la-carte restaurant.

In the summer, breakfast can also be served in the garden. Several shops and bars can be found in the city centre, only a 3-minute drive away.

Furthermore, Little Texas Iasi provides free luggage storage. Shuttle services to and from the airport are provided upon request and at a surcharge.
