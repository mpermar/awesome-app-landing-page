Description

At the resort, rooms have a balcony. Featuring a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries, some rooms at Club Hamak also feature a lake view. The rooms at the accommodation have air conditioning and a closet.

Club Hamak has a playground. A variety of popular activities are available in the area around the property, including fishing and hiking.
