Description

Located in Orăştie, Arsenal Park Transilvania offers accommodation in a military-themed park covering 88 ha of land.

It also offers an open-air military museum, an adventure park and an aqua park. The units of Arsenal Park Transilvania feature free WiFi, a cable TV, and heating.

Private bathrooms are also included. At the on-site restaurant, themed like a mess hall, guests can choose local and international dishes.

During evenings, you can relax by the camp fire, accompanied by music. The Dacian Village features 3 cabins that can host indoor parties with traditional food.

The property provides free access to the aqua park once per day. An extra charge applies only when guests exit the park and want to enter again.

The aqua park features three relaxation areas. Guests can also choose to get an adrenaline rush by choosing from the array of on-site activities, available at an extra cost: military drills, airsoft, archery, paintball, treasure hunts, rides with crawler carriers.

You can use the on-site sports ground, the outdoor fitness area, the mini-golf grounds, the skate park, the skating rink or the petanque grounds.

In the games room you can choose to play billiards, darts, poker, or table tennis.

The adventure park awaits guests with rope courses, zip lines, PowerFan jumps. Deva is 21 km from this property, while Sibiu is 99 km away.
