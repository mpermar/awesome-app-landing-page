Description

Situated in Săpînţa, 200 metres from Merry Cemetery, La Conacul Vesel features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a garden and barbecue facilities.

Boasting family rooms, this property also provides guests with a children's playground.

The air-conditioned rooms provide a mountain view and come with a desk and free WiFi.

All rooms at the guest house are fitted with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and a shower.

All guest rooms have a wardrobe. Guests at La Conacul Vesel can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

The accommodation offers a terrace. Săpânţa-Peri Monastery is 2. 1 km from La Conacul Vesel.

The nearest airport is Satu Mare International, 97 km from the guest house, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
