Description

Eurohotel is located less than 2 km from the centre of Baia Mare and it offers rooms with free WiFi.

The air-conditioned rooms of Eurohotel have a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a minibar and a refrigerator.

The bathrooms are fitted with a hairdryer. Guests can make use of the hairdressing salon at the hotel.

You can access the indoor pool, the sauna and the gym free of charge. The two-level restaurant offers traditional Romanian cuisine and a range of international dishes, and can accommodated up to 170 people.

There is also a conference centre with 2 equipped meeting rooms. The Baia Mare Train Station is 1 km, and Baia Mare Airport is 6 km away from the hotel.

The nearest ski slopes can be reached within a 30-minute drive. The Sapanta Merry Cemetery is 55 km away.
