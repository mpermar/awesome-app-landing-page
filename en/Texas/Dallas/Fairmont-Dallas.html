Description

Modern décor and neutral colors are showcased in rooms at Fairmont Dallas. Guests can enjoy watching movies with a flat-screen TV and state-of-the-art sound system. A coffee machine and minibar are provided for in-room refreshments.

Dallas Fairmont has a contemporary fitness center featuring cardio and weight machines, as well as free weights. Guests can also enjoy massage services or take a swim in the outdoor pool on the terrace level. Floral and gift shops are available for purchasing souvenirs.

Pyramid Restaurant & Bar offers fine American dining in an elegant setting. The restaurant features local ingredients, including herbs and vegetables from the hotel’s rooftop garden. A Starbucks and full bar is also available on-site.

Victory Park is less than one mile away. The Conspiracy Museum is a 5 minute drive away.

Arts District is a great choice for travelers interested in museums, friendly locals and restaurants.

Experiences

Beyond Expectation

"Everything. It was beautiful. I was almost intimidated by how fancy it looked and afraid that everyone might carry an air of superiority but thank God I was wrong. Absolutely everyone we encountered on the staff was super friendly and accommodating and more than willing to offer advice. We were travelling with very small children and the bellhop had a wagon of toys for them to choose something upon arrival and then went a step further and treated them to complimentary Shirley temples. There was construction going on that we could see of our lovely view of the city but we could hear not a peep until we went outside. Their room service was great (you have to have the warm cookies and milk). The pool was a really great size and beautiful. There were too many great things to remember all of it to list. We will definitely be staying again if we ever need to stay in Dallas."
