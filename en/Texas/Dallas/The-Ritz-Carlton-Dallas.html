Description

The luxurious rooms at the Ritz-Carlton, Dallas have a flat-screen TV, a work desk and free coffee and tea.

Guests can relax at the Ritz-Carlton Spa, Dallas, which features relaxation therapies such as the 8-hand massage, facials, pedicures and body scrubs.

The Fearing Restaurant serves delicious international dishes which can be enjoyed outdoors. The hotel also serves light meals at the spa and outdoor swimming pool. For fine chocolates and Italian ice cream, guests can visit the Indulge Boutique.

Uptown Dallas is a great choice for travelers interested in fine dining, restaurants and nightlife.
