Description

At the hotel, each room is equipped with a seating area, a flat-screen TV and desk. Guest rooms at Hotel Alessandra feature bathrobes and luxury toiletries. An in-room tablet allows guests to order room service and schedule spa services. Each room is equipped with an espresso maker and mini-bar.

The on-site restaurant, Lucienne, offers French cuisine, while the Bar Bardot offers craft cocktails and light American fare.

Discovery Green Park is 1,950 feet from Hotel Alessandra, while George R. Brown Convention Center is 2,300 feet away. The nearest airport is William P. Hobby Airport, 9.3 mi from the property.

Downtown Houston is a great choice for travelers interested in sports, city trips and museums.
