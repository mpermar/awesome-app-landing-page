Description

The rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Some rooms also feature a kitchen with a microwave and a fridge. All rooms feature a desk.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning at the property.

The property has a hot tub.

Speaking both English and Spanish, staff at the reception can help you plan your stay.

The Galleria is 16 miles from Home2 Suites by Hilton Houston Willowbrook, while Water Wall is 17 miles away. The nearest airport is George Bush Houston Airport, 12 miles from the property.
