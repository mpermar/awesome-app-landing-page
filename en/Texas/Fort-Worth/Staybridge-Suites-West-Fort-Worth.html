Description

All suites at Staybridge Suites West Fort Worth contain a kitchenette with a refrigerator and dishwasher. A flat-screen cable TV, DVD player and an in-room coffee maker are included.

Guests of the Fort Worth Staybridge Suites have access to an on-site fitness center with cardio equipment, weights and a TV. Books and games are also available for borrowing.

A continental breakfast is served each morning and an evening reception with beer and wine is held every Tuesday-Thursday. A 24-hour convenience store offers drinks and snacks throughout the day.

The West Fort Worth Staybridge is 9 miles from the museums of Fort Worth’s Cultural District.
