Description

Showcasing high ceilings and marble finishes, the elegant guest rooms at Mokara Hotel also have a spa bathtub. Complimentary wireless internet and a flat-screen TV are featured in each room. Food and coffee service are available for delivery 24 hours.

Guests can maintain their fitness during their stay with an on-site gym and a heated rooftop swimming pool. Laundry and shoe shine service are provided on location. The full-service concierge is available to make reservations and give recommendations.

Fine seafood and an oyster bar are on the menu at Ostra. Guests can also enjoy lighter cuisine and cocktails at Rooftop Cafe.

Henry B. Gonzalez Convention Center is within less than one mile.

A great place to stay!

"We were traveling from Houston with car sick kids (2 yo twins and a 5 yo) it was a mess but the valet and concierge were super nice and helped my mom take the kids up to the room w the bags.
