Description

All rooms at Pear Tree Inn San Antonio Airport have a TV with premium movie channels for in-room entertainment. Each room has a microwave, a refrigerator and coffee-making facilities.

San Antonio Pear Tree Inn has 24-hour access to its business and fitness centers. An outdoor swimming pool and a guest laundromat are also on site. Pear Tree Inn serves beer, wine and soda in the lobby every evening as well as popcorn and soft drinks throughout the day.

Henry B. Gonzales Convention Center and AT&T Center are each within about a 15-minute drive of the hotel. San Antonio International Airport is 1 miles away, and a complimentary transportation service is available.
