Description

Apartments are uniquely decorated and include a seating area with cable TV. The apartments at Island House Condominiums also have fully equipped kitchens with microwaves and refrigerators.

Guests can swim in the outdoor pool or cook-out at the barbecue pit and have a picnic.

The Island House is within a 20-minute of La Palmera Mall. The Texas State Aquarium is 18 miles from the property.
