Description

Guest rooms come with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, microwave, a coffee machine, a bath or shower and a desk. Each room comes with a private bathroom with a hair dryer. All rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

Breakfast is available each morning, and includes continental and buffet options.

For guests' convenience, the hotel has a business center. Staff at the reception can assist guests at any time of the day.

Reynosa is 9 miles from La Quinta Inn & Suites McAllen Conv, while Mission is 4 miles from the property. McAllen-Miller International Airport is 2.5 miles away.
