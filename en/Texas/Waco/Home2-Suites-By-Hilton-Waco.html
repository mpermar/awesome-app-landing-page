Description

At the hotel, rooms contain a desk and a TV. All rooms are equipped with a coffee machine, while some rooms also feature a kitchenette with a dishwasher and a microwave. Guest rooms have a closet.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property.

For guests' convenience, Home2 Suites By Hilton Waco has a business center. Non-stop assistance is available at the reception.

The nearest airport is Killeen-Fort Hood Regional (Robert Gray Army Airfield) Airport, 52 miles from the accommodation.
