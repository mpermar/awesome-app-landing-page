Description

A sitting area with a sofa bed and flat-screen TV is provided in each suite at Home2 Suites Dallas-Frisco. Each suite has a kitchenette equipped with a microwave, fridge, dishwasher, and kitchenware for preparing private meals.

This Dallas Frisco Home2 Suites boasts an outdoor pool and walking trail. A Spin2Cycle center, with a laundromat and fitness center, is offered as well. Free limited local area shuttle service is available.

The Home2 Market offers toiletries, snacks, and other sundry items. Dallas Home2 Suites provides a complimentary breakfast including cereal, yogurt, pastries, and hot breakfast sandwiches.

Frisco Lakes Golf Club is less than a 20 minute drive away. Lewisville Lake is within 16 miles.
