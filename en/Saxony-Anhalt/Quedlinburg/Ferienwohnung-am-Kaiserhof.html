Description

Guest rooms feature a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a kitchen, a dining area and a private bathroom with free toiletries, a shower and a hair dryer. All rooms include a coffee machine and a shared bathroom, while certain rooms will provide you with a balcony. The rooms have a closet.

Collegiate Church St. Servatius is a 9-minute walk from the hotel, while Quedlinburg Christmas Market is a 3-minute walk away. The nearest airport is Leipzig/Halle Airport, 53 miles from Ferienwohnung am Kaiserhof.
