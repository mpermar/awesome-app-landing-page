Description

Well-designed, the bright air-conditioned rooms are all equipped with a satellite TV, mini-bar and personal safe. Views of the garden and sea can be enjoyed from the balcony. Bathrooms come with a shower and hairdryer.

Cidade De Goa is a 10-minute drive from Panjim City and a 25-minute drive from Karmali Railway Station in Old Goa. It is a 30-minute drive from Mangueshi Temple. Dabolim Airport and Margao Railway Station are within a 45-minute drive away.

At Clube Saude, guests can exercise with a personal trainer, practice yoga or relax in the steam room. The hotel also provides a tour desk and free parking.

Fine dining and candlelight meals can be enjoyed at Alfama, The Chef's Specialty Restaurant. Other dining options include international meals at Café Azul, buffets at Laranja and cakes at the 24-hour Doçaria.
