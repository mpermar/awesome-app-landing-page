Description

Each elegant guest room comes with a private balcony overlooking the pool or garden. Featuring a seating area, they are also fitted with a 32-inch flat-screen TV, electric kettle and mini-bar. The private bathroom offers a shower and free toiletries. A hairdryer and 24-hour room service is available.

Guests can work-out at he Zuri White Sands, Resort & Casino's gym or relax at the hot tub and sauna. The swimming pool features a waterfall, swim-up bar and a separate pool for children. Other facilities include badminton courts and cycling trails.

Live Teppanyaki can be enjoyed at Sake, while Waterfalls Cafe is a 24-hour multi-cuisine coffee shop. Fins & Rambooze is a seasonal beach restaurant and bar serving local delicacies from November to April. Alcoholic beverages are served at N.U.T.S bar and terrace, as well as Tangerine, which offers live entertainment.

It is a 20-minute drive to Madgaon Railway Station, as well as Colva and Benalium beaches from The Zuri White Sands, Resort & Casino. Dabolim Airport is a 45-minute drive away. Free private parking is available.
