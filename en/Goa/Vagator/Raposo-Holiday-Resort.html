At the resort, rooms have a closet. Complete with a private bathroom equipped with a bidet and free toiletries, the rooms at Raposo Holiday Resort have a flat-screen TV and air conditioning, and some rooms also feature a seating area. Guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

The daily breakfast offers continental and buffet options.

The nearest airport is Dabolim Airport, 16 miles from the resort.
