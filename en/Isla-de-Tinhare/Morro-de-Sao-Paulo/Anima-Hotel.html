Description

The bungalows of the Anima Hotel are distributed in 6 hectares of tropical forest. All of them have satellite TV with CD and DVD player and a terrace with shower and hammock. Some include an outdoor hot tub.

The restaurant serves a homemade buffet breakfast and exquisite cuisine. Most dishes are made with organic vegetables and local seafood. The Anima Hotel also has a lounge with WiFi and a library with a large collection of movies and music. In the immediate vicinity of the accommodation you can perform various activities, such as horse riding, snorkeling or diving.

The hotel enjoys a quiet location on the island of Tinharé, south of the Cuarta de Morro beach in São Paulo. A shuttle service is offered between the Anima and the city center that operates several times a day. In addition, the Anima Hotel offers a bicycle rental service. Saudade Island is 2.7 km from Anima Hotel, while First Beach is 3 km away. Luis Eduardo Magalhães Airport is 83 km away.
