Description

Most of the guest accommodations come with a private balcony where you can relax after a busy day exploring Victoria. You are close to the bus station and the main shopping street. The historic Citadel and the gardens of Villa Rundle are also nearby.

Back at Hotel Downtown you can enjoy drinks and snacks from the bar; open from 7:00 a.m. until late. At the restaurant choose from the menu and pizza selection. A rich carvery buffet is available Saturday at dinner and for Sunday lunch.
