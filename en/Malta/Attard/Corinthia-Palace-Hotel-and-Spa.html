Description

This boutique hotel was built around an elegant, converted villa from the 19th century. It faces the Presidential Palace and is next to the beautiful San Anton Botanical Gardens. Surrounded by its beautifully landscaped gardens, the Corinthia Palace is a peaceful setting for a relaxing break and one of the island's leading 5-star hotels.

Choose from over 50 different treatments at the Atheneum Spa or just relax by the pool. Dining here is also a real treat. Enjoy a wide range of cuisine, from Italian to Oriental. Choose from an à la carte service or a light snack at the bar.

Corinthia Palace Hotel & Spa features spacious guest rooms, all complete with a private balcony and all modern comforts.

The professional team of staff are always on hand and will be delighted to be of service to you.

You are well located for exploring Malta. The walled city of Medina is just 2.5 mi from the hotel, while the popular resort of St Julians is 4.3 mi away.

The Corinthia Palace Hotel & Spa offers a free evening shuttle service to St. Julian's, enabling you to enjoy the night life there, and return to your quiet accommodations with ease.

Rest and Relaxation in Spades

"Our room was quiet with crisp clean bed linen and towels. The bed was very very comfortable and the pillows excellent. As I have shoulder and neck problems I always dread hotel pillows and these were 10 out of 10. Breakfast was excellent and nothing too much trouble. My husband wanted kippers and he got kippers. I asked for Qmarat, a local sweet date pastry and they were made for me. Service with a smile.The hotel was quiet and surrounded by lovely gardens, not only their own but the botanical gardens of San Anton were just a two minute walk from the gate with free entry with unusual and interesting plants and trees to see. The gardens of Villa Bologna, which are being lovingly restored, are five minutes up the hill. There is an interesting pottery attached to the villa and entry is by donation.The villages of Attard and Balsan were within ten minutes walking distance and the buses to Valletta or Mdina were two minutes away. Although we did not use it the hotel runs a complimentary shuttle service into Valletta each day.There were plenty of seats and loungers by the pools, but don't be fooled by the azure pools, the outdoor ones are cold! but the spa was relaxing and the indoor pool inviting.I would thoroughly recommend The Corinthia Palace and we had a wonderful few days of rest and relaxation before we joined our children and grandchildren in Sliema!!!! shold have been the other way round!
