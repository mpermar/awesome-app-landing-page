Description

Modern rooms at the San Antonio Hotel & Spa are air-conditioned, and include a balcony and a 32" LCD satellite TV. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the property.

Massages and treatments at the beauty salon are available for an additional fee at the San Antonio Hotel & Spa. Guests can enjoy discounts at the nearest private sandy beach, just 500 feet away.

Start your day with a rich and varied breakfast buffet. For lunch and dinner you have a choice of cuisine in a variety of restaurants and cafes.

San Antonio Hotel & Spa has a fun variety of multilingual entertainment the whole year round. A local bus service stops just 350 feet away and takes you to the rest of Malta.
