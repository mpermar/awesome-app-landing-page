Description

All air-conditioned rooms at the Labranda Riviera Premium Resort & Spa have a TV with international channels, mini-bar, safe and tea/coffee maker. The private bathroom comes with a bath or shower and hairdryer.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning. The property also offers to guests an all-inclusive option and has several restaurants serving traditional Maltese, Italian or international dishes. Guests can enjoy cocktails, hot and cold drinks and snacks from one of the bars opened 24 hours a day.

Diving packages and courses can be organized on site. Guests can book a variety of massages and beauty treatments at the wellness center or enjoy the daily entertainment offered. Spa facilities include a hydropool, heated indoor sea-water pool, sauna and steam room.

Labranda Riviera Resort & Spa is located 0.9 mi from Cirkewwa Ferry Terminal, while Mellieha beach is located 1.9 mi away. A bus stop 350 feet away offers links to Valletta and Malta Airport, which is a 30-minute drive away.
