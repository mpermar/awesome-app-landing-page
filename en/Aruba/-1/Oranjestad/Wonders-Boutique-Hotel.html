Description

The spacious rooms at Wonders Boutique Hotel Aruba have a flat-screen TV and fridge. A minibar containing wine, beer and soft drinks is restocked daily.

Breakfast is served at Wonders Boutique each morning using local ingredients and herbs from the hotel’s own garden. The breakfast area acts as a reading corner through the rest of the day.

Eagle Beach is less than 10 minutes from the hotel, and Bubali Bird Sanctuary is less than 5 miles away. The hotel has a free shuttle to Eagle Beach and Palm Beach each morning.
