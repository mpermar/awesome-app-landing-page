Description

The rooms at La Cabana Beach & Racquet Club feature contemporary décor and have air conditioning, a balcony and a private bathroom with bathtub or shower and hairdryer.

Guests can enjoy tapas and grill specialties at Las Ramblas restaurant or at the pool-side Islander Grill. There is also an on-site mini market.

La Cabana Beach & Racquet Club also features a state-of-the-art fitness center.

The Queen Beatrix International Airport is a 10-minute drive away.
