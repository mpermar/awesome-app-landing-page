Description

An endless variety of recreational activities are available at the Divi Village Golf and Beach Resort. From on-site tennis courts and 2 swimming pools, to snorkeling and a beachfront rock-climbing wall, there is something for everyone. Guests at the resort also have full access to all facilities at the 5 other nearby Divi Aruba Resorts.

Guests at the Divi Village Golf Resort can also enjoy easy access to the nearby Alhambra Casino, or take advantage of on-site car rental services and explore the surrounding area. The resort also features a variety of organized excursions and tours as well as a children's program.
