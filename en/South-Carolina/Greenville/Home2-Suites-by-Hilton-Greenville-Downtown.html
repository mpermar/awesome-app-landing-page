05/08/2017

Modern, clean and super convenient

"It was one of the largest hotel rooms I have ever stayed in. Really spacious with two beds and a sitting room area which was separated by a curtain. The kitchen area was very well appointed. The hotel is brand new and spotless. Pillows were nice and sheets were soft. I liked the waffles with Nutella for breakfast. We had them every morning. I have stayed in my share of noisy hotels. This hotel was really quiet. I didn't feel like I needed to keep my voice down in the am, so my neighbors don't hear my conversation. The location was amazing. Especially considering the nightly room rate. A walking distance to a small park with a playground (3 blocks) and restaurants, shopping (1 block), and the first mouse of the fun scavenger hunt was just a block away. If traveling with kids, throw a golf club in your car, the small park has a mini golf course. We used the gym every day and were the only ones there. There was a good mix of families with kids, business people and travels."
