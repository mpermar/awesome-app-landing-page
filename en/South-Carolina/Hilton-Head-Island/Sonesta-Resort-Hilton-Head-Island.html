Description

All spacious rooms have either private balconies or patios with views of the resort or Atlantic Ocean. A flat-screen cable TV and small refrigerator are also provided.

Hotel guests can appreciate a 24-hour business center and fitness center. Sonesta Resort guests have access to 2 swimming pools and a full service spa. A shuttle through Shipyard Plantation is provided.

Light, seasonal lunch fare is served poolside at Sweet Cane Bar and Grille. Heyward's restaurant serves breakfast and dinner. Bayley's Bar and Terrace features a full bar.

Van Der Meer Tennis and Shipyard Golf Course are adjacent to this Hilton Head Island hotel. Coligny Plaza and Sea Pines Forest Preserve are 2 miles away.
