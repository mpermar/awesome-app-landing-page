Description

Rooms at the Myrtle Beach Hampton Inn are equipped with flat-screen cable TVs. Guests will enjoy the convenience of a microwave, small refrigerator, and coffee maker. A work desk with an ergonomic chair is also provided.

Hampton Inn & Suites Myrtle Beach Oceanfront provides a 24-hour airport shuttle. Guests can take advantage of the onsite fitness center and business center. A children's playground is also available.

Ripley's Aquarium is 10 minutes' from the Oceanfront Hampton Inn in Myrtle Beach. The Market Commons shopping and dining area is 2 mi away, while Tanger Outlet Myrtle Beach is 7.8 mi from the hotel. Myrtle Beach International Airport is 1.7 mi away.
