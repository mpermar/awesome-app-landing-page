Description

Free WiFi, a refrigerator, and a cable TV are standard in each Compass Cove room. Some rooms include an oceanfront view, full kitchen, and separate living area.

The Compass Rose serves a daily traditional southern breakfast buffet. The Tiki Hut is located near the pools and serves iced drinks. Guests can also enjoy an oceanfront meal at The Crow's Nest Restaurant.

Guests at the Compass can relax at the full-service spa, get a souvenir at the gift shop or play in the game room. The hotel features a shuffle board court and billiards.

The Cove is within 5 minutes' drive of Myrtle Beach State Park, with access to the beach. The Family Kingdom Amusement Park is 1 mi from the property.
