Description

A children's pool, heated indoor pool, and indoor lazy river are on site as well as an additional hot tub. Avista Resort guests can also enjoy a 24-hour reception and a fitness center.

A fully equipped kitchen, private balcony, and separate sleeping and living area are standard in each suite. Extras include a cable TV, DVD player, and free unlimited DVD rentals.

Just Off Main serves American fare all day. The Art Deco-style Tree Top Lounge offers daily drink specials and pool tables while seasonal Poolside Grill Sip-N-Dip has sandwiches and cocktails.

House of Blues Myrtle Beach is 5 mi from Avista Resort, while Main Street shops and restaurants are 5 minutes' walk away. Possum Trot Golf Club is 8 minutes' drive away.

North Myrtle Beach is a great choice for travelers interested in sandy beaches, restaurants and the oceanside.
