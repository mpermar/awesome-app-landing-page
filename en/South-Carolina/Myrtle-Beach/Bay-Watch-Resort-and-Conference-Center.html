Description

Guests can work out in the modern fitness center or use the business center at Bay Watch Resort & Conference Center. A convenience store is also on site.

A microwave, fridge and coffee-making facilities are included in each studio and suite. There is a safety deposit box and seating area. The suites offer ocean views and fully equipped kitchens.

The Blue Room Café at Bay Watch Resort North Myrtle Beach seasonally serves breakfast and dinner. Sandtrap Sports Bar & Grill serves lunch and dinner and Fishtails Beach Bar offers cocktail dining.

Alligator Adventure, Alabama Theater and Beachwood Golf Club are 2 mi from this resort. Guests can also drive 10 minutes south to Myrtle Beach Mall and Tanger Outlet Myrtle Beach.

North Myrtle Beach is a great choice for travelers interested in sandy beaches, restaurants and the oceanside.
