Description

A furnished balcony and a sitting area are standard in each Island Vista Resort apartment. Extras include a dining area, safe, and towels.

A spa and health club and a ticket service to area attractions are available to all guests throughout their stay. The entertainment staff offers a kids’ club on site as well.

The Cypress Room offers an ocean view with an American Southern-style breakfasts and gourmet dinners. Tiki Hut offers more casual fare, and home-style pizza delivery is available for a fee.

The hotel is half a mile from Skywheel Myrtle Beach and less than 3 miles from both Carolina Opry Theater and Palace Theater Myrtle Beach. Myrtle Beach Airport is 7 miles away.
