Description

Guests staying at the Kingston Plantation Condos can take advantage of the complex's 9 swimming pools, on-site water park and top-rated golf course. The hotel also offers daily children and adult activities as well as gourmet dining at the neighboring Embassy Suites and Hilton properties.

The Kingston Plantation Condos place guests close to the House of Blues and Tanger Outlet Mall. Broadway at the Beach, a large complex filled with shops, restaurants and entertainment options is also located nearby.
