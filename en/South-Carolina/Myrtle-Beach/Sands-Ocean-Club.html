Description

A balcony, kitchenette, and a private bathroom with bathtub are standard in each room. Sands Ocean Club extras include towels and linens.

Atlantis Spa features massage, beauty, and body scrub services as well as customized experiences upon request. A kids club, fitness center, and seasonal water park are available.

Ocean Annie’s Beach Bar serves American cuisine such as hamburgers and salads alongside specialty cocktails. Live entertainment is periodically featured.

Carolina Opry Theater is just under 1 miles away. The resort is also 3 miles from the Skywheel Myrtle Beach and a 20-minute drive from Myrtle Beach Airport.
