Description

There are several metro and bus lines that lead directly to the Puerta del Sol, in the city center. The Juan Carlos I fairgrounds are 4 km away and can be easily accessed via the nearby M30 motorway.

The Claravía restaurant serves Mediterranean cuisine made with fresh produce. There is also a bar.

Up to 2 children (under 16 years old) can stay for free if they share a room with their parents. The hotel has a playground, Play Station 3 and Xbox 360 consoles and various games for children. In addition, each child will receive a gift.

Linear City is a great choice for travelers interested in monuments, sightseeing and parks.
