Description

The rooms at the Novotel Madrid Center are modern and feature a 37 or 50-inch flat-screen TV, a safe, a private bathroom with a rain bath or shower, wooden floors and an electric kettle. Superior and executive rooms include a Nespresso® coffee maker.

The Novotel Madrid Center houses 20 conference rooms with cutting-edge technology. Guests also have access to Mac® computers and a press service. Also, the hotel restaurant, the Eat Bar, prepares traditional dishes. It is modern and has a video screen where you can enjoy the latest sporting events.

The nearest metro station is O'Donnell, 450 meters away. In addition, there is a selection of shops, bars, banks and restaurants about 5 minutes walk. Adolfo Suarez Madrid-Barajas Airport is a 10-minute drive away.

Salamanca is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, culture and food.
