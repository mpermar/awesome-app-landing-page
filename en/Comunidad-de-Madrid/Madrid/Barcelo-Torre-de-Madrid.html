Description

Barceló Torre Madrid is located in the Plaza de España in Madrid and offers elegant rooms with free Wi-Fi, as well as an indoor pool and restaurant. The Plaza de España metro station is next to the hotel.

The rooms are spacious and modern and feature a king-size bed, air conditioning, a 42-inch TV and a laptop safe. The bathroom is private and includes a rain shower and a hairdryer.

This hotel also provides an indoor pool, fitness center, sauna and massage service.

The Barceló Torre Madrid's restaurant also serves à la carte dishes. There is also a bar with DJ.

The hotel has meeting rooms and the reception is open 24 hours a day.

The hotel is next to the Gran Vía in Madrid, near many of the city's theaters. The Royal Palace is a 10-minute walk away, while Puerta del Sol and Plaza Mayor are 15 minutes away on foot.

Centro de Madrid is a great choice for travelers interested in eating tapas, architecture and parks.

Experiences

04/22/2017

Modern, stylish, amazing place at the center of Madrid!

"Absolutely everything! Amazing, sunny and colorful place. Modern concept and stylish design. It is actually fantastic to be in the middle of the historic part of Madrid and enjoy super-modern facilities. We travelled with 2 teenager kids and booked 2 rooms. Since interconnecting rooms were not available for the type of rooms we booked, the management upgraded us for free for another type of room just to make sure we have rooms located next to each other and in a private , enclosed space. Breakfast is superb - plenty of choice , including healthy choices, very tasty. There are also plenty of tapas bars and restaurants around the hotel - so if you wake up late (like teens normally do!) - it is easy to find good food nearby. All major sightseeing places are accessible either by metro or by walking (we preferred walking - great way to get to know the city!). The location is very central, yet it is about 5 mins away from most crowded central parts, so we actually liked less noise at night. People are incredibly friendly, service is superb and I think the most precious thing about this hotel is its staff. Our special gratitude goes to Patricia Lara and Pablo, both of them made out stay unforgettable and pleasant. We will definitely return and stay again at Barceló Torre de Madrid!"
