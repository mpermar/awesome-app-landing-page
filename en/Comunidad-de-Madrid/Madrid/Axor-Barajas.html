Description

The Axor Hotel has a modern fitness room and a cozy spa with sauna, hot tub and steam room. In addition, massage service is provided.

The rooms at Axor Barajas are spacious and bright, soundproofed and feature wooden floors, air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, heating, safes, minibars, and desks.

The Axor Barajas has a bar and the Mirage restaurant, with Mediterranean cuisine. There is a cafeteria and room service. The hotel has a garden and several themed rooms, such as a Wii room and a chill-out area.

The hotel provides laundry, ironing and dry cleaning services. In addition, meeting and conference rooms are available for a fee.

Axor Barajas is 6.9 km from Wanda Metropolitano Stadium and 5 km from Faunia Zoo. The nearest airport is Adolfo Suarez Madrid-Barajas Airport, 4 km from Axor Barajas.

San Blas is a great choice for travelers interested in monuments, sightseeing and shopping.
