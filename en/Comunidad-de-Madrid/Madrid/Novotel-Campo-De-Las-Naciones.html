Description

The Novotel Campo de Naciones has an outdoor pool and a terrace. Free access to a nearby gym is also provided.

The rooms are spacious, soundproofed and include a safe and tea / coffee making facilities.

The Novotel Campo de Naciones is close to the M-11 and M-40 highways, well connected to the airport and the city center. The Feria de Madrid metro station is 100 meters away. The airport shuttle service operates every 30 or 35 minutes, from 06:00 to 23:00.

In addition, the Novotel Campo de Naciones serves Mediterranean dishes. The à la carte service is available from 12:00 to 23:00. There is also private parking for a fee.

Barajas is a great choice for travelers interested in history, architecture and urban getaways.
