Description

The rooms are elegantly decorated reminiscent of their original Art Deco style and are equipped with a seating area and flat-screen TV.

Guests can enjoy an à la carte buffet breakfast with hot and cold dishes.

Lobo 8, chef Willy Moya's restaurant, offers a modern version of Mediterranean cuisine. Guests can also enjoy their meals at the LobByto cocktail bar located in the hotel lobby.

The Gran Hotel Inglés also offers business facilities, such as conference and meeting rooms. In addition, various services are offered at the Egoïste wellness center.

The Royal Palace is a 20-minute walk from the accommodation and Retiro Park is just 1 km away.The Adolfo Suarez Madrid-Barajas Airport is the closest and is 13 km away.

Centro de Madrid is a great choice for travelers interested in eating tapas, architecture and parks.
