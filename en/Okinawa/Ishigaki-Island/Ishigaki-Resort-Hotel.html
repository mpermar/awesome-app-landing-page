Description

The rooms in the resort are equipped with a electric tea pot. Certain rooms include a kitchen with a dishwasher, an oven and a microwave. All guest rooms feature a seating area.

A buffet or American breakfast is available daily at the property.

Ishigaki Resort Hotel offers a fitness center. You can play ping pong at the accommodation.

Ishigaki Resort Hotel provides a laundry service, as well as business facilities like fax and photocopying.

Yaeyama Museum is 3.8 miles from the resort. The nearest airport is New Ishigaki Airport, 3.1 miles from the property.
