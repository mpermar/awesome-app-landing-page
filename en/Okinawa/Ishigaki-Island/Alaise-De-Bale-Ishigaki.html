Description

All rooms in the hotel are equipped with a electric tea pot and a CD player. With a private bathroom, certain units at Alaise De Bale Ishigaki also feature a mountain view. The rooms will provide guests with bed linen and towels.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at the accommodation.

Alaise De Bale Ishigaki has an outdoor pool. Guests at the hotel will be able to enjoy activities in and around Ishigaki Island, like hiking.

Tamatorizaki Observation Point is 3.4 miles from Alaise De Bale Ishigaki, while Kabira Bay is 6 miles from the property. New Ishigaki Airport is 6 miles away.
