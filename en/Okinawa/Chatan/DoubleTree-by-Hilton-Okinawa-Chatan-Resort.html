Description

The rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, fridge, a electric tea pot, a hot tub and a desk. The hotel has certain accommodations with sea views, and all rooms include a private bathroom. At DoubleTree by Hilton Okinawa Chatan, each room is equipped with a seating area.

A buffet breakfast is available every morning at the accommodation.

DoubleTree by Hilton Okinawa Chatan Resort has a terrace.

Camp Foster is 2 miles from the hotel. The nearest airport is Naha Airport, 11 miles from DoubleTree by Hilton Okinawa Chatan.
