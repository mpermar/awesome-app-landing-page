Description

Featuring free Wi-Fi throughout, the studios, apartments and cottages open out onto a patio. Simply furnished, each comes equipped with a cable TV and a dining table. Toiletries and a hairdryer are available in the private bathroom.

The seasonal Cavendish Lodge and Cottages is just over half a mile from Eagles Glenn Golf Course. The Shining Waters Family Fun Park is conveniently located less than half a mile from the non-smoking premises.
