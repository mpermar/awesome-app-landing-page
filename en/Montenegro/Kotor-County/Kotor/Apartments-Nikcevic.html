Description

Located in Dobrota, 5 km from the historic, UNESCO-protected town of Kotor, Apartments Nikčević features an outdoor swimming pool, a shared garden and terrace with a barbecue and free parking.

Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the property. All studios at Nikčević Apartments feature air-conditioning, a desk and a TV.

A fully equipped kitchenette with a dining area is available, while the private bathroom provides a shower and a hairdryer.

A grocery shop and a bakery can be found just 100 meters from the apartments, while the nearest restaurant is 100 metres away.

The centre of Kotor can be reached in 5 km, while a sandy and rocky beach called Premijer is 50 metres away.

Boat excursions from Kotor through the bay and to the Perast Islands can be arranged in the centre.

The city museum is 5 km away. Nearest bus stop is located 100 metres away, while the bus station can be found in 5 km.

Tivat Airport is 15 km away and shuttle service can be arranged for an additional cost.
