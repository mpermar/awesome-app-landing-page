Description

Hotel Vizantija is located on the sea coast near Tivat. A restaurant serving local and international specialties with a spacious outdoor terrace is located on site.

Rooms at the Vizantija offer free Wi-Fi and air-conditioning. Seating area provides satellite TV and a safe.

Private balconies overlook the garden or the sea. Bathroom provides a shower and a hairdryer.

The nearest beach is just 10 metres from the hotel. The hotel organises fishing trips and diving excursions.

Guests of the Vizantija get free sun loungers at the beach. They can also enjoy refreshments at the beach bar.

The centre of Tivat can be reached in 8 km. The nearest shop is 1. 5 km away. Restaurants and bars can be found within a 1 km radius.

Bus Station in Tivat is 5 km away, while the Ferry Port can be reached within 8 km.
