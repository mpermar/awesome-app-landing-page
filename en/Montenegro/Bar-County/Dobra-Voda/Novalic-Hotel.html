Description

Featuring an indoor pool with sun loungers, Novalic Hotel is situated 30 metres from the beach in a peaceful part of Jankovii, 9 km from Bar.

Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. Free WiFi is available and free private parking is provided on site.

Every unit is air-conditioned and features a flat-screen TV. Every room is equipped with a private bathroom with a shower.

Every room includes a fridge. Some units feature a balcony. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property.

Airport shuttle service can be arranged at an additional cost. Ulcinj is 18. 6 km from Novalic Hotel, while Budva is 46 km away.
