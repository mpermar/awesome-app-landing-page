Description

Overlooking the Old Town of Ulcinj and the Adriatic Sea, Apartments Tati offers air-conditioned accommodation with free Wi-Fi and LCD satellite TV.

The property enjoys a peaceful location near an olive grove, within 600 metres from town centre and a sandy beach.

The nearest grocery shop is 50 metres away. Tennis courts and 2 rocky beaches can be found within a radius of 500 metres.

Owners of Tati Apartments can arrange boat or car rental services, as well as help with organising boat trips.

The Ada Bojana Island, created by the delta of River Bojana, features a 3 km long sandy beach and a naturist area.
