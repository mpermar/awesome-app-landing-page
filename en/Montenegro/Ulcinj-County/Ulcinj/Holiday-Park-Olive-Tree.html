Description

Some units feature a dining area and/or balcony. There is also a kitchen, fitted with a fridge. Each unit is equipped with a private bathroom with a shower. The holiday park features free WiFi.

Holiday Park Olive Tree also includes a barbecue.

Bike hire and car hire are available at the property and the area is popular for horse riding. Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including snorkelling, windsurfing and diving. Long beach is 3.7 miles from Holiday Park Olive Tree. The nearest airport is Podgorica Airport, 29.8 miles from the property.
