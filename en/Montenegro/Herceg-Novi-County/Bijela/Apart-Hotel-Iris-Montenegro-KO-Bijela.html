Description

Fitted with a terrace, the units offer air conditioning and feature a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with bath and free toiletries. A balcony with mountain views is offered in all units.

Guests at the condo hotel can enjoy a continental or a buffet breakfast.

Apart Hotel Iris Montenegro KO Bij has a seasonal outdoor pool.

A children's playground is also available for guests at the accommodation.

Dubrovnik is 31 miles from Apart Hotel Iris Montenegro KO Bijela, while Budva is 15 miles away. The nearest airport is Tivat Airport, 5 miles from the condo hotel.
