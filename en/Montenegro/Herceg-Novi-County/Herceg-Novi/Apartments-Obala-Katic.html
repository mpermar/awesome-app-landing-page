Description

Located right by the sandy beach in Igalo, Apartments Obala includes an outdoor pool with hot tub and free sun loungers.

Its air-conditioned rooms and studios come with free Wi-Fi and cable TV. Free shaded parking is provided.

A furnished terrace, barbecue and a shared kitchen can be used on the ground floor.

You can easily go fishing from the terrace right below the house. Grocery shops, restaurants and bars can all be reached within walking distance.

There is a nudist beach just 100 metres away. The centre of Igalo, featuring various sports and rehabilitation facilities, is about 950 metres away, while the historic core of Herceg Novi, its fortresses and promenade are 3 km from Obala Apartments.

International airports at Dubrovnik and Tivat are within a 25 km radius from the property.
