Description

Hotel Xanadu is located in the small town Kumbor, at the Bay of Kotor. It is 70 metres away from the beach and offers air-conditioned accommodation, free WiFi and 2 outdoor pools.

Each room and apartment has satellite TV and a private bathroom. Bathrobes, slippers and toiletries are provided.

Some rooms come with a balcony. The in-house restaurant serves excellent local and international cuisine.

A rustic tavern and a bar are also on site. Special diet menus are prepared on request, while both breakfast and dinner are served buffet-style.

Guest can relax on the sunny terrace. Herceg Novi is 5 km away. Tivat Airport and Dubrovnik Airport can both be reached within 25 km.

Free secured, private parking is offered at the hotel. Car rental and airport transfer services can also be arranged.
