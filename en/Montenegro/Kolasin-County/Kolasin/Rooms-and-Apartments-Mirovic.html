Description

Enjoying a peaceful location next to the River Kolašinska, Rooms and Apartments Mirović is located on the foot of Bjelasica and Sinjajevina Mountains and 1 km from the centre of Kolašin.

Its accommodation features a flat-screen satellite TV and free Wi-Fi. On-site restaurant serves local dishes.

Decorated with hardwood furniture, all suites and rooms feature a bathroom with shower, while some include a seating area and a terrace overlooking the surrounding nature.

A shared garden with barbecue and a common kitchen are at guests disposal. There is a grocery store 100 metres from the property.

The area is known for its skiing opportunities. Guests can also experience hiking, cycling and fishing in the vicinity or visit Biogradska Gora National Park, 20 km away.

Bus Station and Railway Station, with lines to Belgrade and Bar, are about 1. 5 km from the property.

Podgorica Airport is at a distance of 80 km, and shuttle service is possible at a surcharge.
