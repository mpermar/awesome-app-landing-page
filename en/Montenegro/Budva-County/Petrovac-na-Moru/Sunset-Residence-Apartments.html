Description

Sunset Residence Apartments is surrounded by beautiful Montenegrin beaches. Beaches Rezevici and Porat are 250 metres away.

The property offers luxuriously decorated apartments. A summer terrace with deckchairs and parasols is at guests’ disposal.

The air-conditioned apartments offer a furnished terrace with Adriatic Sea views.

An LCD TV and DVD player are available. Some apartments come with a fitness bike and a spa bath.

A spacious garden with barbecue facilities is available to all guests. Sunset Residence Apartments offers a children's playground.

Sveti Stefan is 3 km away from the Sunset Residence Apartments, while the town of Petrovac can be reached within 5 km.

The Old Town of Budva is 12 km away. Tivat Airport is 29 km away, and Dubrovnik Airport can be reached within 117 km.
