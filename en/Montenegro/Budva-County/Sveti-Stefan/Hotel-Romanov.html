Description

Located on the Montenegro coastline, the property is 80 metres from Sveti Stefan Beach.

Each room has a Mediterranean interior which includes Italian furniture and luxury bedding.

Every guest room has 2 comfortable armchairs in its seating area and is fitted with a flat-screen TV.

Guests of Hotel Romanov can use the spa facilities next door at Hotel Azimut. Diverse types of international and Mediterranean cuisines are served in the hotel’s restaurant.

It features a summer bar, serving light snacks and apéritifs. Hotel Romanov is 5.

6 km away from Bečići. Budva’s seaside cafés and beaches are less than 10 km away.
