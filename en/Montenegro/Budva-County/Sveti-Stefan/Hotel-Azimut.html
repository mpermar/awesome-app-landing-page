Description

Hotel Azimut is a 4-star superior hotel which views the Adriatic Sea. It is set close to the beach and offers an on-site restaurant, as well as a spa and wellness centre.

The chic accommodation is decorated with elegant, ornate furnishings. Guests can relax in the spa bath fitted in several of Azimut’s rooms.

A balcony is featured in all accommodation and many rooms have a furnished terrace.

Cable TV is offered in each room while wall-mounted flat-screen TVs can be found in some.

Luxurious amenities featured at Azimut include a well-appointed spa & wellness centre with a sauna, steam bath and massage treatments.

A fitness centre is provided for those who like to keep fit. Mediterranean cuisine is served daily at Hotel Azimut’s restaurant.

For in-room dining, room service is offered. Packed lunches can also be prepared upon request.
