Description

Located just 200 metres from the centre of Budva, Marinero Apartments feature a lush garden surrounded by palm trees, lemon trees and kiwi and orange trees.

Free Wi-Fi and free parking are provided. All units are air-conditioned and uniquely decorated.

The colourfully furnished apartments feature a seating area, cable TV and a fully equipped kitchenette.

Private bathroom provides a shower, while the terrace provides a seating area. A sandy beach is just 200 metres from the property, while Budva Old Town can be reached in a 10-minute walk.

A restaurant and a grocery shop can be found 100 metres away. The popular Mogren Beach is 500 metres from Apartments Marinero.

Local buses stop just 500 metres away, while Tivat Airport can be reached in 35 km.
