Description

Opened in 2016, ApartHotel Belvedere Residence is located in Bečići, about 3 km from Budva.

The property offers an outdoor pool and offers sea views. Free WiFi access is available throughout the property and private parking is available on site.

The comfortable apartments and studios are decorated in a modern style. Each one is air-conditioned and fitted with a satellite flat-screen TV.

Kitchen facilities with a dining area and a fridge are provided, and some units come with a terrace.

Car hire is available at Belvedere Residence. Bečići Beach is located within 350 metres, and the nearest airport is Tivat Airport, 28 km away.
