Description

Surrounded by lushly landscaped gardens, the elegant 5-star Dukley Hotel & Resort in Budva boasts a private beach area and offers views of a crystal-clear sea.

Guests can enjoy snacks and relax on the sun loungers next to the rooftop infinity.

Each apartment comes with a furnished terrace, air conditioning and free WiFi. All units are uniquely decorated and equipped with modern amenities for a relaxing and comfortable holiday.

There is a fully-equipped kitchen with a dining area as well as a seating area with a cable flat-screen TV.

Featuring a spacious private bathroom, every unit offers bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries.

Fine dining with fresh food concept is at guests' disposal in Dukley Beach Lounge.

A fine selection of various specialities is offered at the 3 on-site restaurants set on 3 different beaches in the resort.

Mot and Chandon Beach & Bar offers refreshing drinks and snacks, exotic-style Key West Beach & Restaurant offers Caribbean dishes and bites while family-oriented Laguna Beach & Restaurant offers Mediterranean dishes.

To get around the area with ease guests have golf carts at their disposal. Free on-site parking is provided and a boat transfer to the Budva Old Town is available.
