Description

Apartments Captain's Villa is located in Budva, 800 metres from Mogren Beach and Budva Old Town and is surrounded by a fragrant Mediterranean garden.

Free WiFi is featured throughout the property. The stylishly appointed accommodation is air conditioned and features a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

All units have a dining area and include a fully equipped kitchen. Each unit has a private bathroom with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Towels and bed linen are offered. A grocery shop is located near the property, while various restaurants and shops are located within walking distance.

A sandy beach can be reached in 600 metres. Sveti Stefan is 6 km from Apartments Captain's Villa.

Tivat Airport is 16 km away, while Podgorica Airport is at a distance of 80 km. Dubrovnik Airport can be reached in 70 km.
