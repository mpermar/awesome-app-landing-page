Description

Located in one of the oldest settlements on the Adriatic Riviera, Becici Budva, Hotel Tara is 80 metres from a marvellous private sandy beach offering up-to-date, attractive accommodation.

The rooms are equipped with international cable TV, in-room safes and wireless internet access.

Two deck chairs and a parasol, which can be used on the private beach, are included in the room rate.

Many rooms also feature balconies, some of them boast superb views of the Adriatic.

The outdoor swimming pool has a sun deck as well as a bar which serves drinks and snacks.

For extra comfort, guests will have 2 sun loungers and a parasol at their disposal free of charge.

The private beach is fully serviced, featuring a bar. Hotel Tara has an aperitif bar which is open daily as well as a restaurant where delicious buffet breakfasts and dinners are served.

Guests can experience the amazing cultural heritage, interesting architecture and a vibrant night life in Budva.

The various sandy beaches, coves and small islands around the town are popular with swimmers, sunbathers and boaters.
