Description

Designed with warm-colored fabrics and textures, open and bright rooms overlook the beautiful grounds and water features. Each comes with a flat-screen TV, minibar and private balcony or porch.

Taj West End is just across the road from Bangalore Turf Club and about 1.5 miles from the center of the city. It is 21 miles from Bangalore Domestic and International Airports.

Guests can exercise at the fitness center, or arrange day trips and car rentals at the tour desk. The 24-hour front desk provides helpful services like daily newspapers and baggage storage.

Mynt restaurant offers international all-day dining, while Blue Ginger serves modern Asian cuisine. Blue Bar provides a relaxed country club atmosphere to enjoy drinks in.
