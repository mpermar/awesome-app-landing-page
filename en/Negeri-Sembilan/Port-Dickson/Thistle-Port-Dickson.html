Description

The units are decorated with warm colors and air conditioning. It features a flat-screen TV and ironing facilities. Selected units have a private balcony while some have a bath. The private bathroom comes with a hairdryer and free toiletries. Guests enjoy garden, pool or golf course view from the room.

Chinoise serves modern Chinese dishes, while Fresh offers a buffet spread of international and local favorites. Glass features Italian delights and a choice of indoor or outdoor dining. Cumulus and Steps also serve snacks and drinks.

Guests can relax by the poolside or workout at the gym. Alternatively, you can play a game of golf or rent a bike to explore the area. Water sports activities can be arranged at the reception and children can play in the kids' club. Other services offered include a spa and laundry.

Thistle Port Dickson is 1.6 mi from Ostrich Farm and is 4.1 mi from Port Dickson Golf & Country Club. Kuala Lumpur International Airport is 35 mi from the resort.
