Description

Coral Beach rooms have modern furnishings. They come equipped with satellite TV and a minibar. Some rooms feature views of the Gulf.

The Waves Restaurant overlooks the beach and has a dining menu. Casa Samak serves seafood and offers views of the Gulf. The signature Al Dente features Italian specialties. Coral Beach has a coffee shop and a bistro that serves freshly baked pastries and refreshing beverages

Resort guests can enjoy a relaxing massage, or play a game of tennis. Younger guests can visit the children’s pool with water slides and shaded playground. There is a well equipped gym as well as banquet and meeting facilities.

The Coral Beach Resort is 7.5 miles from the Sharjah Airport and a 30 minute-drive from Dubai Airport. It has a 24-hour front desk service and free on-site private parking.
