Description

It is at the heart of Sands® Cotai Central, Macao's integrated resort featuring a variety of leisure, shopping and entertainment options.

Bright, air-conditioned rooms with its floor-to-ceiling windows come equipped with a flat-screen TV and desk. The private bathroom has a shower, bathtub and free toiletries.

The health club is fully equipped with a fitness center, hot tub and sauna. Guests can also enjoy yoga and Pilates lessons. Activities for children are also available.

The Pool Cafe overlooks the Cotai Strip from its terrace seating and has a selection of beverages and an international menu. Afternoon tea and refreshments are served at the Lobby Lounge.

Macau International Airport is a 5-minute drive away, while Macau Taipa Ferry Terminal is less than a 10-minute drive from the hotel.
