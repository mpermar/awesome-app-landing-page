Description

The modern rooms at the Macau Grand Hyatt come with city or river views. They include a 42-inch flat-screen TV and spacious twin basin bathrooms with a rainshower.

Guests can walk in the outdoor garden, enjoy a treatment at Isala Spa, or relax in the steam room or bubble bath. The Grand Hyatt offers private pool-side huts.

Grand Macau Hyatt features 5 restaurants, a bar and a lounge. The Beijing Kitchen serves Northern Chinese specialties, while Mezza9 offers Macanese dishes.

The Grand Hyatt Macau is a 5 minute drive from the Taipa Ferry Terminals. The free City of Dreams shuttle service drives between the airport, the ferry terminals and the hotel.
