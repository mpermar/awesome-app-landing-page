Description

Priory Lodge Hotel has been family-run since 1979, and is a short walk from the center of town. The sandy beaches are around a 5 minute walk away.

The rooms are spacious, and all have a TV and tea and coffee-making facilities. Guests can use free Wi-Fi in public areas, and there is space for free parking on site.

The Olde Worlde bar offers a range of beers, wines and spirits. There is also a restaurant with views of the coastline, and all dietary needs can be catered for.
