Description

All guest rooms at the hotel come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV and a safety deposit box. The private bathroom is equipped with a bath and bathrobes. The rooms will provide guests with bed linen and towels.

A Full English/Irish breakfast is available each morning at the property.

Treglos Hotel offers a sauna. You can play ping pong and pool at the accommodation.

St. Ives is 30 miles from Treglos Hotel. The nearest airport is Newquay Airport, 6 miles from the hotel.

Beach hotel with quality service

"- The staff were very friendly, organized and attentive at all times (i.e. arrival, breakfast, dinner).- The room had gorgeous views with large windows, the bed was huge and soft, the bathroom was modest but well stocked. - The pool was lovely and the Jacuzzi was nice too.- Dinner was of a good quality and again the staff were very courteous ( I travelled with a vegan and the chef made up something especially for her after giving advance notice).- The hotel is 5 minutes from Constantine beach. Having lived in Cornwall throughout my childhood I know how beautiful the beaches on the North coast can be, even so this one was breathtaking. Rock pools, golden sands and crystal clear water with a view of the rocky outcrops just off shore. Not massive waves, but big enough to surf. The golf course behind the dunes appears to have prevented the area from being developed too much and the whole area is charming. - Lovely breakfast with a lot of options (amazing coffee).
