Description

At the hotel, rooms contain a desk and a flat-screen TV. The rooms are equipped with a private bathroom with a shower. Guest rooms include a closet.

A continental breakfast is served each morning at the property. The restaurant at The Mayfield Seamer serves Grill/bbq and British cuisine.

The accommodation has a playground. Hiking is among the activities that guests can enjoy near The Mayfield Seamer.

The reception can provide advice on the area in order to help guests plan their day.

Scarborough General Hospital is 3 miles from the hotel, while Scarborough Magistrates is 3.3 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Humberside Airport, 45 miles from The Mayfield Seamer.
