Description

Each room at the Best Western Crown Hotel has a TV, telephone, ironing facilities and free tea/coffee. Rooms also feature a private bathroom with toiletries and a hairdryer.

Guests have full use of the on-site leisure center which includes a steam room, sauna and spa bathtub. Personal trainers are also available in the gym.

Made using local produce, classic British cuisine is served in The Crown Grill. Stables Bar serves real ales and has traditional exposed beams and an open fireplace.

With free secure parking provided, Harrogate is just 10 miles’ drive away. The scenery of the North York Moors National Park is less than 30 minutes away by car.
