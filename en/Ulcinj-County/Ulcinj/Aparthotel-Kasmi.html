Description

All guest rooms comes with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fridge, a coffee machine, a bidet and a desk. Each room comes with a private bathroom, and some rooms have a balcony. The rooms will provide guests with a closet and a electric tea pot.

Guests at the hotel can enjoy a buffet or a halal breakfast. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of Mediterranean and European dishes and also offers kosher and vegetarian options.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can help guests with any questions that they might have.

Long beach is 2.5 miles from Aparthotel Kasmi. The nearest airport is Podgorica Airport, 30 miles from the property.
