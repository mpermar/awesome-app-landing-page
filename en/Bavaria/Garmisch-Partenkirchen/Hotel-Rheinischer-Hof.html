Description

All rooms at Hotel Rheinischer Hof include a flat-screen TV.

The Fontana spa has a sauna, solarium and massage room.

Guests can enjoy Bavarian specialties and international dishes in the stylish restaurant. Drinks are available at the bar.

The Rheinischer Hof is at the foot of the Zugspitze Mountain, and near the Austrian border. The surrounding countryside of the Bavarian Alps is ideal for hiking, biking, and skiing.

The Rheinischer Hof is half a mile from Garmisch town center and 2.4 miles from Garmisch-Partenkirchen Train Station.
