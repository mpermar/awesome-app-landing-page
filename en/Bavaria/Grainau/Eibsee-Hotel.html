Description

The non-smoking rooms of the Eibsee Hotel feature balconies with panoramic views and satellite TV. Fax and modem connections are available upon request.

The Eibsee’s spa area includes a sauna, pool, and fitness room. Massages are also provided.

Bavarian and international food is served in the Eibsee’s restaurant with lakeside terrace. There is also a bar with fireplace.

The Eibsee Hotel is a good base for activities including golf, hiking, and swimming.

"Views, Views, Views. If you are looking for serenity and nostalgia of Christmastime, this is it. The hotel was a upscale cabin meets luxury with excellent service. Breakfast and dinner were excellent and included in the price, minus drinks. Family of 6, two rooms, parents with 3 young kids, age 5, 6 and baby and grandma. beautiful location right on the edge of Eibsee lake which...

"beautiful location right on the edge of Eibsee lake which although is a tourist hot spot is very quiet and peaceful and the hotel has its own private jetty and beach making it feel even more remote. The hotel is in an excellant location being a 2 minute walk from the cable car that takes you to the of Zugspiste the tallest mountain in Germany, definitely worth a visit but go early to avoid long queues and the hotel will organise your tickets to save additional queues. The hotel is also a few minutes walk from the train and bus stop that takes you all the way to Garmisch and stops at a few different cable cars on route all of which have beautiful walks and some easy family friendly routes too, you will be given a card that allows you to use this facility for free, well worth it to save you taking the car out. The food in the hotel is not amazing but it is good and the half board menu is excellant especially for the price. The extras in the hotel like the mini bar and babysitting are all really reasonable and not tourist rip offs like you so often find. The hotel staff are excellant, extremely efficient and very friendly nothing was to much and they are very accommodating and friendly to children. We will definatley be going back and I would reccomend to anyone looking for a holiday in Bavaria."
