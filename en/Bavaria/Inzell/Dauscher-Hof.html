Description

Each unit comes with a sofa, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a well-fitted kitchen and a private bathroom with bath or shower. A dishwasher and oven are also available, as well as a coffee machine.

A buffet breakfast is available each morning at the apartment.

Dauscher Hof offers a sauna.

Guests can swim in the indoor pool, go skiing or cycling or relax in the garden.
