Description

Every room at the Holiday Inn München Süd includes a flat-screen TV, tea/coffee facilities and a private balcony or terrace.

The Restaurant Kupferkessel serves German and international food. Colorful cocktails and German beers can be enjoyed in the lobby bar or on the hotel's terrace.

Guests can use the swimming pool and sauna at the Holiday Inn München Süd for free.

Machtlfingerstraße Underground Station is a quarter mile away, providing services to the Marienplatz Square in 15 minutes.

Thalkirchen - Obersendling - Forstenried - Fürstenried - Solln is a great choice for travelers interested in convenient public transportation, atmosphere and city trips.
