Description

The Hotel Schnitzer has quiet, country-style rooms with balconies, satellite TV, and private bathrooms. Free Wi-Fi is available in all public areas.

Guests are welcome to relax in the Schnitzer’s spacious garden or in the indoor pool. Other wellness facilities at the Schnitzer include different saunas, a Kneipp area, and massage lounge.

Popular dishes from the Tegernsee area, homemade cakes, and international dishes are served in the Schnitzer’s traditional-style restaurant.
