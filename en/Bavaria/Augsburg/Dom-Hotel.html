Description

Traditional rooms with wooden beams are featured at Dom Hotel some rooms have a terrace with views of Augsburg Cathedral.

Guests at the Dom Hotel receive complimentary minibar drinks in the rooms and free coffee in the lobby.

Augsburg town hall, the pedestrian zone with its many shops and all other attractions, from the marionette theater museum to the Fuggerei - can be reached within a 10-minute walk of Hotel Dom.

The main train station and a tram stop lie just a 5-minute walk away.
