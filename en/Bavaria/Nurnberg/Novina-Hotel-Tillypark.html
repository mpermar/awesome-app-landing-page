Description

Novina Hotel Tillypark has bright, modern rooms with soundproofed windows and air conditioning. Comforts include a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with hairdryer.

Regional specialties and a generous breakfast buffet are offered in the Novina Hotel’s restaurant. Guests are free to relax with cocktails, spirits and hot drinks in the cozy bar and lounge

Reception is open 24 hours a day at the Tillypark. Guests can make free use of 2 internet terminals in the lobby.

Gustav-Adolf-Str. Underground Station is 350 feet away, providing quick connections to the Central Station and Nuremberg Opera House. The A73 motorway is just 5 minutes away.

Weststadt is a great choice for travelers interested in Christmas markets, shopping and old-town exploration.
