Description

At the resort, all rooms are equipped with a desk. Each room is equipped with a private bathroom, and certain rooms have a balcony. The rooms at Reflections Retreat include air conditioning and a closet.

A à la carte breakfast is served at the property.

The accommodation has a grill. Guests at Reflections Retreat will be able to enjoy activities in and around Port Vila, like snorkeling.

Konanda Reef is 9 miles from the resort.
