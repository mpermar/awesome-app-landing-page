Description

Guests have a choice of beachfront and overwater bungalows, or suites and villas with a private pool on the beach front. Each includes an in-room safe, satellite TV, iPod docking station and a minibar.

Snorkeling equipment, kayaks, outrigger canoes and pedal boats are available free of charge. The day spa provides a range of body treatments and massages.

Le Tipanie restaurant overlooks the lagoon and serves Polynesian, Asian and Western cuisine. Le Te Ava restaurant allows guests to dine with their feet in the sand. Miki Miki Bar overlooks the beach.

Motu Mute Airport is 5.5 miles away.
