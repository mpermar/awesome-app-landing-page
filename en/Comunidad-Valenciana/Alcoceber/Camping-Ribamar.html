Description

The beautiful wooden bungalows of the Ribamar have a porch and a small garden area. All rooms have a living room with a TV and a kitchenette with a dining area. All areas are accessible through a fixed ramp. In addition, there is 1 bungalow adapted for people with reduced mobility.

The complex has a grocery and drinks shop, a bar and a business center that offers WiFi for a fee.

The Ribamar has a multipurpose sports field that can be used as a tennis court. Various activities, such as cycling, can be practiced in Alcossebre or in the surrounding area.

Castellón Airport is the closest and is 22 km away.
