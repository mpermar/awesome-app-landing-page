The Kitsume restaurant at SH Villa Gadea serves Japanese dishes and the Miramar restaurant serves local specialties. There is also a buffet restaurant, a lobby bar with live music and a summer terrace with barbecue facilities and sea views. Half board packages include breakfast and dinner.
