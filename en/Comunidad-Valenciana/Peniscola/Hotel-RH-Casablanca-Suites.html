Description

The bedrooms at Casablanca Suites are beautifully decorated and feature air conditioning, tiled floors, a safe and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The Casablanca terrace has sun loungers and 2 hot tubs. The hotel also houses a sauna and gym. In addition, in summer, it offers a varied entertainment program for children and adults.

This hotel has a buffet restaurant that offers an international menu and show cooking. It also has a cafeteria with terrace.

You can reach the center of Peñíscola, located 3 km from the Casablanca Suites, by a regular bus service every 30 minutes. From the AP-7 motorway, which runs along the Mediterranean coast of Spain, you can easily access the hotel.
