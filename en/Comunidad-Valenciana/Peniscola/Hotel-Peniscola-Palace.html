Description

The hotel spa includes a sauna, Turkish bath and ice fountain. There are also ping pong and pool tables.

Rooms at the Peñíscola Palace hotel are equipped with tiled floors and wooden furniture. They also have an LCD TV, a safe and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The buffet restaurant offers show cooking and serves Mediterranean dishes. There is also a cafe overlooking the sea.

The Peñíscola Palace hotel features a free Wi-Fi zone and a tour desk. It is a 20-minute walk from the picturesque historic center and the castle of Papa Luna. The hotel is well connected to the AP7 motorway and is a 20-minute drive from the Sierra de Irta Natural Park.
