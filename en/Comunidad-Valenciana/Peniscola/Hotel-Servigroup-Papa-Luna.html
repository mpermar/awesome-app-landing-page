Description

The wellness center comes at an extra cost and features a sauna, hot tub, Turkish bath, contrast showers and a variety of massages and beauty treatments. There is also a small gym.

Papa Luna's buffet restaurant has show cooking and prepares buffet dishes. The poolside bar serves drinks.

The hotel is a 10-minute walk from the historic center and the Peñíscola Marina, where you will find a variety of shops, bars and cafés. The Serra d'Irta Natural Park is less than 3 km away.
