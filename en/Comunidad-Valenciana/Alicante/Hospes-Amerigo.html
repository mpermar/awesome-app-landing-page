Description

This stylish design hotel is located in a 19th-century Dominican convent, in the historic center of Alicante, and 300 meters from Postiguet Beach. Hospes Amerigo is located 50 meters from the cathedral and the town hall of Alicante.

The rooms at Hospes Amerigo are elegant and feature high ceilings, large windows and a DVD player. Some have abstract silk paintings.

The Bistro of Amérigo is a bar located in the lobby and offers tapas and light ducks. In summer, guests can enjoy the views from the rooftop lounge bar, located next to the outdoor pool.
