Description

The beach of San Juan, with a length of 6 km, is the longest on the Costa Blanca. The hotel is 10 minutes by car from the center of Alicante and 20 minutes from the airport. It has free parking and is 100 meters from the tram stop that goes to Alicante.

The rooms at the Almirante hotel are modern and include air conditioning, flat-screen satellite TV, a safe and a private bathroom with toiletries.

In addition, the Almirante hotel serves a buffet breakfast at the Pocardy restaurant, which also offers Mediterranean dishes for lunch and dinner, including rice and fish dishes. Guests can eat on the outdoor terrace and enjoy music in the evenings. There is also a bar.
