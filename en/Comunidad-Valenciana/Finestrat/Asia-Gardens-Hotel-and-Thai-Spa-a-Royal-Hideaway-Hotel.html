Description

Asia Gardens Hotel & Thai Spa, a Royal Hideaway Hotel offers elegant rooms with free Wi-Fi. Its design is Balinese style and kimonos and slippers are provided.

The splendid gardens house several Asian-style fountains, infinity-edge pools and traditional palafitos, where Thai massages are offered. The spa has a heated indoor pool and hot tub and there is also a fitness center and a children's pirate club.

The hotel has 4 restaurants, including the Udaipur buffet restaurant and an à la carte Asian restaurant. There is also a restaurant with Mediterranean and international cuisine and 3 exclusive bars.

The hotel is a 30-minute drive from Alicante airport and 2 km from Levante beach.
