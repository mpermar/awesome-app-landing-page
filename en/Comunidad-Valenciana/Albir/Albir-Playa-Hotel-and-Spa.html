Description

Arroceria La Palapa is an elegant restaurant that serves lunch in the tropical gardens of Albir Playa Hotel & Spa. The restaurant specializes in rice, meat, fish and fresh seafood, as well as homemade desserts. In addition, the restaurant offers bar service from 11:00 to 19:00, also available by the pool.

The 1,000 m² spa at Hotel Albir Playa is available for a fee. It includes sauna, whirlpools and ice fountain. In addition, different types of treatments are offered.

The Albir Playa Hotel has a games room and children's play area. The hotel also offers a bicycle loan service. The reception is open 24 hours a day.

Benidorm is just 10 minutes drive from the Hotel Albir Playa.
