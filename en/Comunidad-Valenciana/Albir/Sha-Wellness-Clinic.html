Description

The SHA has 80 rooms for anti-aging, slimming, beauty and purification treatments, and combines oriental and western relaxation methods.

The Wellness Clinic area offers more than 6,000 m² of facilities designed to improve health and well-being, and is perfectly designed and equipped to enjoy the wide variety of treatments and services of the SHA. There is a large fitness room, and the cabins are specially designed to offer the exclusive treatments of SHA, medical consultations, beauty services, spa circuits and rooms for physical activities.

The spacious and elegant suites at SHA Wellness Clinic have minimalist black and white décor, and include a private terrace, free internet connection and a TV with DVD player.

The hotel's SHAMADI restaurant serves healthy food and teaches cooking classes. There is a piano bar and a terrace overlooking the bay of Altea. The property offers translation and wake up calls.

The SHA is easily reached from the AP7 motorway, which runs along the Spanish Mediterranean coast. Horse riding and water sports are common activities in the area, and the property is 1.5 hours 'drive from Valencia Airport and 40 minutes' drive from Alicante Airport.
