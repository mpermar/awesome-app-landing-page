Description

The rooms have modern décor with wooden floors, flat-screen TVs, air conditioning, heating, safe and minibar. The marble bathroom includes a bathtub. Room service is also available.

The Cullera Holiday has swimming pools for adults and children, a small fitness center, sauna, as well as a bar and a restaurant serving buffet or à la carte meals.

Full-board and half-board rates include buffet-style food or menus.

Cullera Holiday is located 2 km from the center of Cullera and an 8-minute drive from Albufera Natural Park. Buses that go to the center of Cullera stop in front of the establishment. The train connects with Valencia in 30 minutes.
