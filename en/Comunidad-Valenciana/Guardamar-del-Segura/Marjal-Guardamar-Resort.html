Description

The bungalows at Marjal Guardamar Resort feature parquet floors, a private porch and a kitchenette with a hob and microwave.

There is a large lagoon-style pool with 3 hot tub and waterfall areas. The establishment also has a fitness center, spa and entertainment program for adults and children.

The restaurant serves Mediterranean and international food and specializes in paellas. In addition, there is a cafeteria with terrace and a poolside bar.

The Marjal campsite is well connected to the AP7 motorway and is 30 minutes from Alicante airport. Elche palm grove, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is also 30 minutes away by car.
