Description

Melia Villaitana is designed in the style of a traditional Mediterranean town. All rooms are large and have air conditioning, private balcony or terrace, satellite TV and private bathroom with hairdryer.

The Villaitana dedicates some of its areas to the exclusive rooms and services of The Level. The Level guests have private access to The Level lounge, which provides a private buffet and open bar, daily cocktails, press and concierge services, and pre-arrival services. The rooms include a pillow menu, superior toiletries, a sound bar, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a Nespresso coffee machine and a coverage service, among other services. The Level rooms also offer some privileged views of the area.

The Villaitana's wellness center consists of a free gym, sauna and water circuit with thermal baths. Guests can enjoy a large selection of beauty and relaxation treatments. The complex has tennis and paddle courts, a children's playground for children over 5 years old and a disco.

The Villaitana Hotel has a cafeteria and several restaurants. Hotel Villaitana is well connected to the AP-7 motorway and allows easy road access to the Terra Mítica theme park and the Terra Natura nature reserve. The property is a 20-minute drive from Alicante, 50 km from Alicante Airport and 134 km from Valencia Airport.
