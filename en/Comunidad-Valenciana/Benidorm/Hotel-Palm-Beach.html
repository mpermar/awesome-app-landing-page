Description

Levante Beach is a 10-minute walk away and Aqualandia and Mundomar theme parks are a 15-minute walk away.

The establishment has an all-year indoor swimming pool, gym, sauna and children's play area with a small soccer field. During the summer months, a children's club is organized with scheduled activities. The WiFi connection is free in all facilities.

The Palm Beach offers a wide variety of entertainment activities at night. It also houses the disco El Templo.

The buffet restaurant serves Spanish and international dishes during breakfast, lunch and dinner. The cafeteria prepares snacks.
