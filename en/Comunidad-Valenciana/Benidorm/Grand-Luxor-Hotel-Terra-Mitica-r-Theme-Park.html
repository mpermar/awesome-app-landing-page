Description

The rooms at the Grand Luxor Hotel are spacious and have air conditioning and a balcony or terrace. They also have a flat-screen TV, tea and coffee making facilities, a safe, a minibar and a sofa bed. All include a private bathroom with free toiletries. Some enjoy views of the sea and others, the theme park.

The hotel has a restaurant with show cooking. It also has a bar, 24-hour reception, meeting facilities and conference rooms with the latest technology.

The Grand Luxor Hotel is very well located, close to the AP-7 motorway and just 15 minutes drive from Aqualandia. Alicante is 30 minutes away by car and Alicante Airport is 53 km away. Valencia Airport is 150 km away.
