Description

The hotel has a spa with thermal pools and treatment rooms. There is also gym, 2 tennis courts and 6 paddle tennis courts.

The Level at Meliá Villaitana dedicates some of its areas to The Level exclusive rooms and services, where The Level guests have private access to a private reception area, an Open Bar and Aperitive Time and to an external heated swimming pool exclusive for these guests. Guests can also enjoy golf and exclusive animation activities.

Rooms include pillow menu, superior amenities and toiletries, sound bar, flat-screen TV with satellite channels, Nespresso coffee machine and turndown service, among other services. The Level rooms also offer some privileged views over the area.

Spacious rooms feature a walk-in wardrobe and private balcony or terrace. They have a flat-screen satellite TV, safe and minibar. There is a seating area with a sofa. The private bathroom includes a hairdryer, bathrobes and free toiletries.

The Level at Meliá Villaitana offers a large number of restaurants, bars and cafés. Guests can enjoy barbecues by the pool or dine on the terrace. The Indigo Restaurant offers gastronomic and avant garde food with Mediterranean touches. Casa Nostra Restaurant features modern Italian food. Guests can also enjoy Restaurante Trasluz, a gastro-design restaurant.

The hotel is 4 km from Benidorm and there is easy road access to Terra Mítica and Terra Natura Theme Parks. Alicante Airport is 50 km away, and Valencia Airport is 134 km away.
