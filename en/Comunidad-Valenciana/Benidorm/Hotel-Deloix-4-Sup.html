Description

Aqua Center Hotel is just 800 meters from Aqualandia and Mundomar water parks and 1.5 km from Benidorm's center and the popular Levante Beach. In addition, there is a regular local bus line that communicates with the city and beaches.

The wellness center at Deloix Aqua has an indoor pool, hot tub, beauty treatments and thermal circuit.
