Description

All rooms have modern amenities such as individually controlled air conditioning, LED TV, telephone, safe and minibar. The bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries.

The property has a large garden area and an outdoor pool with a children's play area.

There is a solarium, a chill out area with a bar and sun loungers and a sports area. In addition, the hotel is soundproofed.

The entertainment staff of Port Benidorm organizes a wide variety of daily activities for children and adults.
