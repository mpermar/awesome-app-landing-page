Description

Hotel Rosa is set in beautiful gardens and has an Andalusian-style terrace next to the pool. The rooms and bungalows include satellite TV and a private bathroom.

The restaurant offers à la carte dishes and traditional Valencian cuisine. There is also a cafeteria, and drinks can be served on the terrace. Hotel Rosa has air conditioning and free Wi-Fi.

There are supermarkets and shops 200 meters away, while the nearest bus stop is only 150 meters away. The port and restaurant area known as Raset is 1 km away and the city center of Dénia is 2 km away.
