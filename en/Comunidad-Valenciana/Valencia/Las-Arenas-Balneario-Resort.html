Description

All rooms at this design hotel offer elegant decoration in neutral tones. They include free Wi-Fi and a 32-inch flat-screen TV.

The Sorolla restaurant serves traditional cuisine with a creative twist. Guests can enjoy a buffet breakfast and light snacks at the poolside bar.

The Las Arenas Balneario Resort's spa has a heated indoor pool overlooking the gardens. Massages and beauty treatments are offered and there is also a gym.

The Palacio de la Música and the Copa América port are a short walk from the hotel. There are 2 metro stations and many bus stops nearby.

Maritime towns is a great choice for travelers interested in the beach, sunny days and warm weather.
