Description

The wooden bungalows at Marjal Costa Blanca Resort are beautiful, surrounded by tropical gardens and modernly decorated. They have a living room with a sofa bed and a flat-screen TV, and a kitchen with a microwave, fridge and coffee maker, as well as a dining area.

The Marjal has a restaurant with Mediterranean cuisine, a bar, a barbecue area and a small supermarket.

Guests enjoy 1 free access for 1 hour per stay to the Marjal Costa fitness and spa center, where there is a sauna, Turkish baths, 2 indoor and 3 outdoor pools and various facial and beauty treatments.

The complex is about 10 minutes drive from Crevillente and Elche and less than 24 km from the beaches of Guardamar.
