Description

This accommodation is a 4-minute walk from the beach. This elegant hotel offers a lagoon-style pool, a spa and direct access to Oliva Nova beach. It is part of the Oliva Nova golf course, designed by Seve Ballesteros.

Rooms at Oliva Nova Golf Beach & Golf Hotel are spacious and elegant and feature a seating area, flat-screen TV and free Wi-Fi. All have a covered terrace overlooking the complex, the nearby dunes or Montgó.

The El Olivo restaurant of the Oliva Nova Hotel produces typical rice dishes from Valencia. There is also a restaurant with a terrace next to the pool, an Irish pub and a beach bar. Spa facilities are available for a fee.

Hotel Oliva Nova, located between Gandía and Dénia, is a 45-minute drive from Valencia. It offers easy access to Valencia and Alicante through the N-332 and AP7 motorways.
