Description

In the morning a rich buffet breakfast is served. The restaurant offers a wide selection of hot and cold dishes for lunch and dinner.

Guests can relax on the sun terrace and in the outdoor pool.

This place is perfect to enjoy the golden sand beach and the warm waters of the Mediterranean.

The establishment has a games room and a bar, where entertainment activities are offered at night. There is also a TV lounge.
