Description

All bungalows feature air conditioning, wooden floors, free Wi-Fi, a private terrace and a lounge with a sofa and flat-screen TV. The kitchenette has a fridge, microwave and coffee maker.

The complex is very extensive and houses a barbecue area, a restaurant and several shops. There is a playground and a games room with billiards and ping pong.

The spa at the La Marina International Resort includes an indoor pool, hot tub and sauna. It also offers massage service.

Free public parking is available nearby. The property is very well connected to the main road N332 and is 30 km from Alicante and an 8-minute drive from the Salinas de Santa Pola Natural Park.
