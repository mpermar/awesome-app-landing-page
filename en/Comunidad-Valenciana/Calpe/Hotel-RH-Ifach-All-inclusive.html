Description

The rooms at the Hotel RH Ifach All are spacious and have a private balcony, satellite TV and a private bathroom. Free WiFi is provided.

The hotel has a buffet restaurant and an outdoor restaurant bar, La Palapa, located next to the pool. Both places serve traditional homemade dishes.

The establishment has a fully equipped gym. Reception staff can provide information on tourist sites and nearby activities such as hiking trails, water sports, excursions, golf, diving and gastronomic visits.
