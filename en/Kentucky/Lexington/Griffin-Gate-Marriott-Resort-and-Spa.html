Description

All of the spacious rooms are air conditioned and have tea and coffee making facilities. For in-room dining, room service is available.

Guests can relax in Griffin Gate Marriott Resort’s spa with a massage treatment or in the hot tub. Free Wi-Fi access is also offered in public areas.

Griffin Gate Marriott Resort’s restaurant, JW’s Steakhouse, is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Starbucks is on site to provide refreshing beverages and snacks.

Kentucky Horse Park is 5 miles from the hotel.
