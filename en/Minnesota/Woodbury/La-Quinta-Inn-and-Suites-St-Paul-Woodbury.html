Description

All rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Featuring a private bathroom with a bath or shower and free toiletries, rooms at La Quinta Inn & Suites St. Paul-Woodbury also have free WiFi. The rooms will provide guests with a microwave.

A continental breakfast is available daily at the accommodation.

A business center is at guests' disposal at La Quinta Inn & Suites St. Paul-Woodbury.

Minneapolis is 15 miles from the hotel. Minneapolis-Saint Paul International Airport is 13 miles from the property.
