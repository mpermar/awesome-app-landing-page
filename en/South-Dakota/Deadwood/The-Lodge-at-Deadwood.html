Description

A flat-screen TV and an iPod docking station are included in every guest room at the Lodge at Deadwood. The rooms also have a refrigerator and are decorated in rich browns.

Oggie’s Sports Bar & Emporium serves traditional food in a casual environment with darts. The Deadwood Grills provides a fine dining experience.

Guests can shop for souvenirs at the Essential Gift Shop or work out in the fitness center. The Deadwood Lodge also provides an airport shuttle service and a cash machine.

The Adams Museum & House is 1 miles from the lodge. Historic Mount Moriah Cemetery, burial place of many icons of the American West, is less than 2 miles away.
