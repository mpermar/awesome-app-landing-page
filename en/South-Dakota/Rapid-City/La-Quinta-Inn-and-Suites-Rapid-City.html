Description

Free Wi-Fi is provided in every room at La Quinta Inn & Suites Rapid City. For added convenience, tea and coffee-making facilities are also included.

A daily continental breakfast with waffles, fruit, and more is served at Rapid City La Quinta Inn & Suites. Guests can take advantage of the fitness center and launderette facilities located on site.

Shopping at the Rushmore Mall is 8 minutes’ drive from this hotel. Mount Rushmore National Monument is 30 miles away.

All older children or adults are charged USD 10 per night when using existing beds.
One older child or adult is charged USD 10 per night in an extra bed.
Free!One child under 2 years stays free of charge in a crib.
The maximum number of extra beds/cribs in a room is 1.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
