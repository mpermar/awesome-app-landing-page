Description

Free WiFi, a flat-screen cable TV with premium channels and a coffee machine are included in every room at Grand Gateway Hotel. Each air-conditioned room also includes a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

Guests can work out in the on-site fitness center or take advantage of the fax and photocopying services in the business center at Grand Gateway Hotel Rapid City. The reception desk is available 24-hours a day.

Guests can enjoy a meal 24 hours a day at Perkins Restaurant & Bakery, or visit the Cheers Lounge and Casino, as well pay a game of billiards, table tennis and air hockey at Grand Gateway Hotel.

Conveniently located off Interstate 90, this hotel is 15 minutes’ drive from Ellsworth Air Force Base. Central Rapid City is less than 4 miles away.
