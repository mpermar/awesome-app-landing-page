Description

The hotel offers very comfortable and fully-equipped rooms; some even have their own private balcony or a lounge. There are family rooms as well.

You can relax at the spa by the heated indoor swimming pool, hot tub and sauna, after a game of golf, tennis or billiards. The restaurant serves fine Aubrac cuisine, and the bar is open from 7:30 am to 10:30 pm.

The dedicated staff can also help you organize seminars, banquet, and cocktails for 10 to 250 people as well as tours to explore the amazing natural heritage of the Midi-Pyrénées region.
