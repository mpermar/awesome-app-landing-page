Description

In a 7-acre park, the Domaine includes a four-star hotel with guest rooms and a castle, open to visitors. There is also a spa center on site where massages are available.

Guests can relax with a drink at the hotel's bar. The restaurant serves traditional French cuisine, which can be enjoyed on the hotel’s gravel terrace.

The Domaine de Château de Garrevaques is a 45-minute drive from Carcasonne, Toulouse and Albi. Guests can enjoy hiking a cycling in the surrounding countryside.
