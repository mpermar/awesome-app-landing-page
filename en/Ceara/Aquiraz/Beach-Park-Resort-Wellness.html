Description

Wellness suites feature cheerful décor, balcony, air conditioning, TV and kitchenette with microwave and refrigerator. Some include access to the water park.

The Wellness features the Azul do Mar restaurant, bar area, a lobby bar, children's club, outdoor gym area and games room.

The Beach Park complex is next to the beach of Porto das Dunas and has coastal facilities, such as a well-known water park, 3 resorts and an area with beach services. The property is 26 km from Fortaleza and Pinto Martins International Airport.
