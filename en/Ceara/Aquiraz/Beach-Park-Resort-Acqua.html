Description

All rooms at Acqua have modern décor, air conditioning, TV and minibar. There is access to the water park from some of them.

The Acqua houses the Aquiraz restaurant and the Toaçu bar area. There is also a children's club, tennis court and fitness center.

The Beach Park complex is next to the beach of Porto das Dunas, and has its famous water park, 3 resorts, a hotel and a beach area. Fortaleza and Pinto Martins International Airport are 26 km away.
