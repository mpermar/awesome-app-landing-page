Description

All accommodation at this hotel has air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, bright décor and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

The Gran Mareiro Hotel also features 2 bars, a sauna, a fitness center, a games room, a playground and a children's club, a cafeteria, a crepe area and a business center.

The accommodation is just 9 km from Iracema Beach and 19 km from Beach Park. The nearest Pinto Martins Airport is 16 km away.

Praia do Futuro I is a great choice for travelers interested in sandy beaches, sunny days and meeting friendly people.
