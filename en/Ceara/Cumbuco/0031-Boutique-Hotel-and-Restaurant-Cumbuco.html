Description

The rooms at 0031 are spacious, have views of the sea or the pool and are decorated in bright Mediterranean colors and equipped with a large balcony with hammocks, air conditioning, flat screen TV, sofa bed, minibar and private bathroom with hot shower, hairdryer and free toiletries.

Guests of the 0031 Hotel & Restaurant can book excursions to the Serra de Baturité nature reserve or to the panoramic beach of Jericoacoara. You can also have an exotic cocktail in the solarium, in the shade of palm trees.

A breakfast with fresh bread, muffins and eggs is served every morning on the porch. The Castnah restaurant serves contemporary meat and seafood dishes, while the cafeteria prepares coffee and snacks. The lounge restaurant at 0031 serves lunch from 12:00, and in the afternoon offers cocktails and snacks.

0031 Cumbuco is a 35-minute drive from Fortaleza International Airport. Airport shuttle service is provided.
