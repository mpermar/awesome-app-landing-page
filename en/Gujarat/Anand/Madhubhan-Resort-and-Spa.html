Description

This luxurious property is located 4 miles from Anand Train Station and 25 miles from Vadodra Airport with shuttle services available for a fee.

Featuring traditional wood furniture, elegant rooms are fitted with a sitting area, a balcony and flat-screen TV. The private bathroom includes a bathtub and a shower.

Soothe tense muscles with yoga classes or indulge in various spa treatments. Madhubhan Resort also features a game room. Other facilities include a 24-hour front desk, car rental services and a business center.

The Banyan Tree Restaurant, will serve authentic Indian cuisines and other oriental dishes. Guests can also choose from 4 other cafes including Bake-A-Cake confectionery store. In-room dining options are available as well.
