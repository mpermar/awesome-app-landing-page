Description

Enjoy the beauty and natural environment of the island and relax on the well-kept lawns, surrounded by the scents of the sea and the sound of the waves.

Unwind in the lounge chairs or under lush olive trees, surrounded with lavender and Mediterranean green vegetation.

The large swimming pool provides activities like counter-current swimming, geysers and waterfalls and is sure to satisfy even the most demanding guests.
