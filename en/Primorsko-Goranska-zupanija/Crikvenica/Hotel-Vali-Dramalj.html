Description

After the delicious breakfast buffet in the morning, you can head out to the beach, which is just 50 feet from your room.

Alternatively, the surrounding region has plenty to offer for active and adventurous guests. If you need to relax after that, why not treat yourself to a massage or a manicure.

Your evening starts with some typical Croatian cuisine in Hotel Vali's romantic restaurant, followed by freshly prepared cocktails out on the terrace.
