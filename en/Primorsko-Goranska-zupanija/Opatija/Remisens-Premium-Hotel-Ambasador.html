Description

The large La Fourchette D'Or restaurant serves a buffet breakfast and dinner, as well as various specialties from the menu. Themed or gala dinners accompanied by live music are organized occasionally.

The Hortenzija restaurant is in the summer garden, and the Palma Café has enough space for dancing. Guests can also relax at the Manhattan Cocktail Bar.

Renovated in 2013, the rooms of the Remisens Premium Hotel Ambasador have private balconies.

Opatija Riviera is in the Gulf of Kvarner, on the eastern shore of the Istrian Peninsula, where the sea makes its deepest cut into the mainland.
