Description

Free access to the seasonal outdoor pool and the indoor gym is provided to all guests of the Hampton Inn and Suites Knoxville-Turkey Creek. Parking is also free on site.

A coffee maker and free Wi-Fi are standard in every modern room at the Turkey Creek Hampton Inn. All rooms include a seating area and a work desk.

Downtown Knoxville is a 20-minute drive from the hotel. Concord Park is 5 miles away.
