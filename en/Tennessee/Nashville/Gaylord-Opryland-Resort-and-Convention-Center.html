Description

A flat-screen cable TV and coffee maker are standard in every room. All rooms are warmly decorated with wood furniture and include a safety deposit box and work desk. Some rooms offer a balcony with seating areas and views the interior landscape.

International cuisine is represented through the various restaurants at Opryland Gaylord resort, including Revello’s southern Italian and Solario’s Mexican dishes. Guests have their choice of swimming in 2 outdoor pools or an indoor pool.

Massage and body treatments are offered at Relache Spa and access to the fitness center and sauna is provided to all hotel guests. WiFi is included in the resort fee.

Nashville International Airport is 9.9 mi from the hotel. Shuttle services are available to the airport and local attractions including Gaylord Springs Golf Course, just a 9-minute drive away.
