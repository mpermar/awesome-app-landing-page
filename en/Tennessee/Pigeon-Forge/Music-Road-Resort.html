Description

Guest rooms provide free Wi-Fi, microwaves and refrigerators. They are also furnished with coffee-making facilities and hairdryers.

A hot tub, fitness center and game room with pool tables are available to Music Road Hotel guests. The hotel provides meeting facilities and has a daily continental breakfast. It provides concierge and dry cleaning services and has a gift shop.
