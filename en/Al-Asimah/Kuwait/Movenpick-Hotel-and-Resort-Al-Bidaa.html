Description

Movenpick Hotel Kuwait's modern suites and apartments are in low-rise buildings and surrounded by the resort’s gardens. Most units offer views of the garden, pool or over the sea.

Movenpick’s main restaurant serves an extensive oriental and international buffet alongside an international menu. The hotel also has a specialty seafood restaurant. Guests can enjoy dining on outdoor terraces in both restaurants.

Recreational facilities include a well-equipped gym and a spa with 12 treatment rooms and a variety of massages. There is a children’s playground and kids club with a wide range of activities.

The Movenpick Resort Al Bida’a is a 15-minute drive from Kuwait International Airport. For a fee, airport pick-ups and drop-offs can be arranged by the hotel.

Hawally is a great choice for travelers interested in beach walks, clothes shopping and museums.
