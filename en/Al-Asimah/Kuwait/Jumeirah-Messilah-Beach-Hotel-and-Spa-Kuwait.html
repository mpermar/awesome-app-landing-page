Description

With contemporary décor and electric curtains, all air-conditioned units come equipped with a flat-screen TV, minibar, tea/coffee making facilities, in-room safe and private bathroom. The apartments have a full kitchen and a living room.

An international buffet is served at Garden Café, and Italian cuisine is available at Olio. You can grab a healthy bite at Mint, savor a juicy steak at Pepper or enjoy seafood by Salt’s beachfront.

After a game of tennis or an energizing workout at the gym, you can unwind at Talise Spa where a range of treatments are available. You can take a dip in the swimming pool or soak up the sun at Messilah Beach.

Jumeirah Messilah Beach Hotel & Spa Kuwait features state-of-the-art conference and banquet facilities for business travelers. Sinbad’s Kids Club and The Scene Teens Club provide entertainment for children and teens.
