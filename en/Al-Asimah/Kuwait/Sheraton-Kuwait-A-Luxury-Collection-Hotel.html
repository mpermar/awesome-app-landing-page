Description

The 5-star accommodations at Sheraton Kuwait feature chic, modern furnishings and flat-screen TVs. Each is spacious and includes luxury bathrooms and free Wi-Fi.

Guests can enjoy Iranian cuisine at the Shahrayar Restaurant, Lebanese specialties at Tarbouche and Indian fare at Bukhara. The popular English Tea Lounge offers traditional English-style afternoon tea. Italian delicacies can also be enjoyed at Riccardo fine dining restaurant.

Fitness fans will have the well-equipped gym. To relax, the Sheraton has a sauna and a steam bath.

The Sheraton Kuwait Hotel & Towers has complimentary airport pick-up and drop-off to all its guests. Kuwait Airport is 6 miles away. Free parking is available on site.

Kuwait City District is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, markets and souks.
