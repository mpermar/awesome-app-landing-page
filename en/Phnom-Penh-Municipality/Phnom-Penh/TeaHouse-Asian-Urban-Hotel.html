Description

It is 2,150 feet to Independence Monument, while the Royal Palace and Chaktomouk Conference Hall 2 are 0.9 mi from TeaHouseAsian Urban Resort. The nearest airport, Phnom Penh International Airport is 6.5 mi from the property. Airport transfers are available at additional charges.

All rooms come with a flat-screen cable TV, mini-bar and refrigerator. Room service is available.

Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for currency exchange, tour arrangements, ticketing and concierge arrangements.

The on-site restaurant serves a variety of Cambodian and western dishes. Cocktails, wines and local beverages can be enjoyed at the pool bar.

Daun Penh is a great choice for travelers interested in museums, temples and local food.
