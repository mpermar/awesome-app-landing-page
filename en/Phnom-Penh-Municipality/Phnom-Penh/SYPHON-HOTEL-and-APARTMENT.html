Description

The rooms at the hotel are equipped with an oven, a microwave, a electric tea pot, a hot tub, free toiletries, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and DVD-Player. Each room is equipped with a shared bathroom with a hair dryer, while selected rooms will provide you with a balcony and others also offer city views. All guest rooms have a seating area.

A buffet breakfast is available daily at SYPHON HOTEL & APARTMENT. Guests can dine in the in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of American and Asian dishes.

The accommodation offers a range of wellness facilities including a hot tub and a sauna.

Guests can use the business center or rent a car to explore the area. When guests need guidance on where to visit, the reception will be happy to provide advice.

Aeon Mall is one mile from SYPHON HOTEL & APARTMENT, while Tuol Sleng Genocide Museum is 1.1 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Pochentong Airport, 6 miles from the hotel.

Chamkar Mon is a great choice for travelers interested in restaurants, food and culture.
