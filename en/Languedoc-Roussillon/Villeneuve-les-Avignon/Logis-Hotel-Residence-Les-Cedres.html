Description

Some of the rooms are located in the main building and are decorated with antique furniture. Other rooms have a private terrace and have air conditioning. All rooms have a private bathroom and Wi-Fi connection.

The residence Les Cèdres has a restaurant serving meals made from fresh local produce, a swimming pool, terraces and private parking. Guests can take the public bus into the center of the city.

This is the ideal starting point for various excursions to Nimes, Arles, Orange, Pont du Gard, Les Baux de Provence, Alpilles, the Luberon, the Camargue and the Mont Ventoux.
