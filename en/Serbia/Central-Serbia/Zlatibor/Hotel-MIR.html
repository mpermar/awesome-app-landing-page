Description

Located in a quiet area of Zlatibor, Hotel MIR features an à-la-carte restaurant and bar with a terrace, a Spa Area and a fitness room.

Free Wi-Fi and parking are provided. All rooms are modernly furnished and feature seating area, a desk, a minibar and a flat-screen TV.

Private bathroom provides a shower, hairdryer and free toiletries. Rooms also feature a balcony.

Hotel MIR’s luxuriously decorated restaurant serves international specialties and traditional cuisine.

Breakfast buffet is served at the restaurant each morning. The spa area features a sauna and a hot tub.

A children’s playground is located in front of the hotel, while numerous bike paths are reachable in just a few steps.

There is also a shopping mall, 500 metres away. Local buses stop 500 metres away, while Belgrade is 230 km away.
