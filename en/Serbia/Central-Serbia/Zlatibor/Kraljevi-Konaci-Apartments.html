Description

Located 100 metres from the centre of Zlatibor and Zlatibor Lake, Kraljevi Konaci Apartments offers an on-site restaurant that serves domestic cuisine.

This self-catering accommodation features free Wi-Fi. The small ski slope of Obudevica is 1 km away.

All apartments come with a living room comprising a seating area and a flat-screen TV.

Each one has cooking facilities with a dining area and a private bathroom fitted with a shower that also includes a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Some apartments overlook the lake. The nearest bar is 50 metres away and a grocery shop is located at a distance of 100 metres.

The local marketplace is 400 metres from the property. Kraljevi Konaci Apartments also offers a garden and a shared terrace.

A seasonal outdoor swimming pool is 50 metres away. The ski slopes of Tornik are located at a distance of 7 km.
