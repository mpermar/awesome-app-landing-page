Description

Villa Bozovic is located 700 metres from the centre of Zlatibor and surrounded by a lush garden with a seating area and a children’s playground.

Guests can use the shared barbecue and enjoy on the terrace with parasols. Free Wi-Fi is available.

All units at the Bozovic feature a seating area, cable flat-screen TV and a fully equipped kitchenette.

Private bathroom provides a shower. Free parking is provided for all guests. Obudojevica Ski Slopes can be reached in just 900 metres, while Tornik Ski Centre is 7 km away.
