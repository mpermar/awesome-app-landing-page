Description

Featuring a large outdoor pool surrounded by sun loungers and a bar with terrace, Villa Romantika offers studios and apartments with free Wi-Fi and free video-surveilled parking.

Each unit comes with a kitchen and cable TV. There is a private bathroom in all apartments, while kitchen facilities include a fridge, stove and dining table.

Guests can shop for groceries and dine at the nearest restaurant within 150 metres from the Romantika.

Ribničko Lake is at a distance of 6 km, while the Tornik Ski Centre is 7 km away.
