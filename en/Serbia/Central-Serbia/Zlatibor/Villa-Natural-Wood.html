Description

Villa Natural Wood enjoys an idyllic location amongst lush green meadows, 1. 5 km from the centre of Zlatibor.

It offers accommodation in beautiful, wooden cottages with free access to Wi-Fi and free bike rental.

Free private parking is available on site. Villa Natural Wood consists of the main apartment log cabin with uniquely designed apartments featuring wood and stone wall elements.

There are also 2 separate single unit cottages. Each apartment and cottage has its own furnished balcony or terrace.

The living rooms boast modern flat-screen cable TVs and comfortable sofas. Modern, fully equipped kitchenettes with kitchenware, dining area, a microwave and a refrigerator are provided.

Natural Wood Villa has a garden with a children’s playground and barbecue facilities which are at guests’ disposal.

Motor sledges can be hired during winter months and a small mountain quad is available during summer.

Guests can also use the spa centre with a sauna and a hot tub, or enjoy a warm drink in the on-site coffee bar.
