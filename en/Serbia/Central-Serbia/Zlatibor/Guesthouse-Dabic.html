Description

Offering a furnished garden and a sun terrace, Guesthouse Dabić is located in quiet mountainous surroundings in Zlatibor.

Free Wi-Fi access is provided. Rooms here provide cable TV and a minibar. Some units come with a kitchen or kitchenette and a dining table.

The private bathrooms are fitted with a bathtub or a shower and offer free toiletries.

A games room and a library can be found at Dabić Guesthouse. Various skiing opportunities are available nearby.

An outdoor pool can be used at an additional cost. A bus station is located 500 metres from the guesthouse.
