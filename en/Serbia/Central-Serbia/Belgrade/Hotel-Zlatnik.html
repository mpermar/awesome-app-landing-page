Description

Featuring free WiFi throughout the property, Hotel Zlatnik is located in a quiet residential area of Zemun, a 10-minute drive from Belgrade's city centre.

The well-known Zlatnik Restaurant offers traditional Serbian and international cuisine, including kosher and halal food, as well as a rich selection of wines.

All rooms are decorated with natural materials and warm colors. Amenities include a flat-screen satellite TV and a DVD player.

A private bathroom with a bidet and shower is featured in each unit. Free toiletries, bathrobes and slippers are also provided.

Zemun's sights can be easily reached on foot as well as Danube Promenade with restaurants, bars and cafés.

Tower Gardoš is a short walk away. Belgrade Arena and Sava Congress Centre are within 5 km, while Belgrade Airport is 13 km from Hotel Zlatnik.
