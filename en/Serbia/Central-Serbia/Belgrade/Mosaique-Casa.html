Description

Located 13 km from Republic Square Belgrade, Mosaique Casa provides accommodation with free bikes, an outdoor swimming pool and a 24-hour front desk for your convenience.

The apartment offers a seating area with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and shower.

Guests at Mosaique Casa can enjoy a continental or a à la carte breakfast. The accommodation offers a children's playground.

Barbecue facilities are available and guests can also relax in the garden or in the shared lounge area.

St. Sava Temple is 14 km from Mosaique Casa, while Splavovi is 16 km away. The nearest airport is Belgrade Nikola Tesla, 32 km from the apartment, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
