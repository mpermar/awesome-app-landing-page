Description

1000 Ruza is set in a green environment at the foot of Mount Avala, easily connected to Belgrade's centre by the E70 motorway and a new bypass road.

It offers air-conditioned rooms with cable TV, minibars and free Wi-Fi. The city centre and Belgrade Fair can be reached within a 15-minute drive.

The accommodation provides a free private parking lot. 1000 Ruza (1000 Roses) has a spacious restaurant which serves national Serbian cuisine and features a garden with a playground for children.
