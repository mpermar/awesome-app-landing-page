Description

Set just a 5-minute drive from Nikola Tesla Airport in Belgrade, and about 9 km from Belgrade Centre, Hotel Hollywoodland Wellness & Aquapark offers a water park with indoor and outdoor pools, and a spa centre.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant or have a drink at the bar. Free WiFi is available and free parking is provided on site.

Every unit is air conditioned and has a flat-screen TV. Every room includes a private bathroom with a bath or a shower.

Some units include a seating area. For your comfort, you will find a minibar and free toiletries.

Hotel Hollywoodland Wellness & Aquapark includes a kids’ pool with water slides. Wellness and spa facilities include saunas, spa bath, salt room, steam bath, various massages and beauty treatments.
