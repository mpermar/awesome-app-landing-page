Description

The property boasts modern architecture and a minimalist design with calming colors, custom-made furnishings and decorations by the sculptor Gabriel Glid.

The rooms are equipped with an espresso machine, Bang & Olufsen A/V equipment and wireless internet access.

The Townhouse 27 has a conference room and a business center as well as a cafe and a bar. Buffet breakfast is served from 6:30 a.m. to 11 a.m. at the cozy and stylish cafe, where you can also relax, work or chat with friends.

Stari Grad is a great choice for travelers interested in nightlife, food and history.
