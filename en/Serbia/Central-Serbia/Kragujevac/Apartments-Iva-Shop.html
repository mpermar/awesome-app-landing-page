Description

Located in the pedestrian zone in the centre of Kragujevac, Apartments Iva Shop features self-catering accommodation with free Wi-Fi and cable TV.

There is an Italian restaurant on the property's ground floor. Free private parking is available.

All units are air conditioned and include a kitchenette with a dining area. They also comprise a bathroom with shower.

A grocery store can be found in the vicinity of the Iva Shop Apartments. Various restaurants and a green market can be reached within 150 metres.

The Bubanj Lake with biking trails is 1. 4 km away. A local bus stop is right next to the property, while the Main Bus and Tram Stations are 1.

5 km from the Apartments Iva Shop. Belgrade Airport is 140 km away and shuttle service is available at a surcharge.
