Description

Peacefully located next to Bor Lake, the Jezero Hotel offers air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi, a balcony, minibar and LCD cable TV.

It has an indoor pool, its own restaurant and a bar with terrace, as well as a bowling alley, table tennis and billiards.

Each room also includes a safety box and a bathroom with shower. The hotel's spa and wellness facilities can be used at a surcharge.

Front desk is attended 24 hours a day. There is a playground in the hotel's quiet and green surroundings, where you can also find terrains for basketball and football.

The nearest beach is 300 metres away, while the local bus station is 500 metres from Hotel Jezero.
