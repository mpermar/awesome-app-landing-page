Description

Set at 1,770 metres above sea level in the very centre of Kopaonik, the modern and comfortable Grand Hotel & Spa offers amazing views of Kopaonik National Park, just steps away from the main ski lift.

The hotel features elegant, spacious rooms. All rooms comprise a private bathroom equipped with a hairdryer and a bathrobe.

Amenities include an LCD TV, a telephone, a DVD player, a minibar and a safe. The 1,800 m big Grand Oaza spa centre offers a sauna, Turkish bath, salty ice cave and an outdoor hot tub, as well as a relaxation area overlooking the mountainous landscape.

Various massage and beauty treatments can be organised. The restaurants and bars at Hotel Grand serve a selection of hot and cold drinks, as well as samples of Serbian and international cuisine.

In addition to a modern business centre, kids' corner and a fitness area, Grand Hotel features a multi-purpose sports hall with basketball, handball and volleyball courts.
