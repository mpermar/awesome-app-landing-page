Description

Located 18 km from Kopaonik Ski Centre and covering 8 hectares, Junior Hotel offers a spa and numerous sports facilities at the foot of the Kopaonik Mountain.

There is 1 large restaurant, 3 outdoor dining options, a ski school and a private beginners ski slope.

All rooms and apartments at Hotel Junior come with a private bathroom with a shower as well as a fridge and satellite TV.

Some suites also have a kitchenette, a hot tub, and a sauna. The décor is elegant and cosy.

In the spacious hotel lobby there is an open fireplace and a bar. There is also a disco, a pool hall, and a shop.

The à la carte restaurant serves local delicacies, while the 3 smaller ones offer café and bar menus.

Guests can work out in the gym or play football, basketball, and volleyball on-site.
