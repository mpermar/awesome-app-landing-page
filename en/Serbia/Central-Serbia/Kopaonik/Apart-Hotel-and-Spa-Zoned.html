Description

Apart Hotel & Spa Zoned offers peaceful surroundings at the entrance to the Kopaonik National Park.

Opened in 2011, the hotel features a spa and wellness area, 2 restaurants and offers unique architecture and interior design with futuristic décor.

Accommodation units at Apart Hotel & Spa Zoned are lavishly decorated combing modern and traditional style and offer a comfortable seating area with an LCD TV.

Private bathrooms come with a shower and a hairdryer. Spa and wellness facilities include a sauna, Turkish bath and hot tubs, and can be used at a surcharge.

Massage treatments and a fitness centre are available. Guests can also relax in the lounge bar while using free WiFi.

Pančić's Peak, the highest point of Kopaonik Mountain, is a 15-minute drive away, while the nearest town of Raška can be reached by car in 40 minutes.
