Description

Set in Vrnjačka Banja, 450 metres from Bridge of Love, Hotel Tonanti features a garden, a bar and a shared lounge, as well as free WiFi.

Offering a restaurant, the property also has a seasonal outdoor swimming pool, as well as an indoor pool and a fitness centre.

The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, room service and luggage storage for guests.

The units come with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kettle, a shower, a hairdryer and a desk.

At the hotel all rooms are fitted with a wardrobe and a private bathroom. Guests at Hotel Tonanti can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

Guests staying at the accommodation have access to the on-site spa and wellness centre which includes a hot tub and a sauna.

Skiing and cycling are among the activities that guests of Hotel Tonanti can find nearby.
