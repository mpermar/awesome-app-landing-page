Description

Situated in the peaceful village of Bela Zemlja 15 km from Zlatibor, this modern complex features free Wi-Fi access and free private parking.

Surrounded by a garden, Hotel Zlatiborska Noc features playgrounds for children and outdoor terraces.

The property is close to the Mokra Gora mountain with its famous Sargan Eight Railway, which goes through the steep landscape.

Guests can go hiking and exploring the mountainous region. The Zlatiborska Noc features a large outdoor swimming pool and a gym.
