Description

HOTEL EMROVIC RAJ has a restaurant, bar, a shared lounge and garden in Novi Pazar.

Among the facilities at this property are a 24-hour front desk and room service, along with free WiFi throughout the property.

The hotel has newspapers and a fax machine and photocopier that guests can use. At the hotel the rooms come with a terrace, a private bathroom and a flat-screen TV.

Guests at HOTEL EMROVIC RAJ can enjoy a continental breakfast. The accommodation offers a children's playground.
