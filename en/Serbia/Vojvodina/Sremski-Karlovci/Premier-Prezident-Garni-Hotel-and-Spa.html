Description

All accommodations units comprise free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, an LCD cable TV and a DVD player. Each unit includes a private bathroom with a shower, hairdryer and free toiletries.

The spa and health club consists of an indoor pool, a sauna, hot tub and a fitness center. A hairdresser and various massage treatments are available for a fee. Guests can also relax at the Louis XVI Lounge Bar.

The Sremski Karlovci Train Station is 650 feet away, and the main bus station is a 5-minute walk away. National Park Fruška Gora, with its numerous Orthodox monasteries, is 3 miles from the Prezident Premier.
