Description

Located in the thermal settlement of Vrdnik, Hotel Premier Aqua is set halfway between Belgrade and Novi Sad.

This modern five-star hotel offers a spa centre, an indoor and outdoor pool and an on-site restaurant.

Free Wi-Fi is provided throughout the property. All accommodation units at the Premier Aqua offer a flat-screen TV, a phone, a minibar and a safe.

Private bathroom provides a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer. Each unit overlooks the mountains and the pool and features hypoallergenic linen.

Hotel facilities include a business centre and various services such as car rental, laundry and dry cleaning service, room service and free parking for all guests.

The hotel also houses the Aqua Medica specialist clinic for physical medicine and rehabilitation.
