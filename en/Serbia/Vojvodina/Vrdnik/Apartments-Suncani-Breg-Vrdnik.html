Description

Offering a balcony in each unit, Apartments Sunčani Breg Vrdnik is set in peaceful countryside surroundings in Vrdnik and provides air-conditioned accommodation with free WiFi access.

A spacious patio and garden are available, as is free bike rental. Units here come with a flat-screen cable TV, some also featuring a seating area with a sofa.

Apartments also have an equipped kitchenette with a dining area. The private bathrooms are fitted with a shower, some also providing a hairdryer.

Free toiletries are provided. Sunčani Breg Vrdnik Apartments provides guests with communal barbecue facilities.

Grocery deliveries can be arranged as well as massages. Hiking and cycling opportunities can be found in the property's surroundings, as can a pool and a children's playground.

The Fruška Gora National Park is located at a distance of 15 km. Free private parking is provided on site.
