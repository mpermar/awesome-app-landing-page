Description

Rooms at the Regina Hotel feature an LCD TV and classic-style furniture. Most have a private balcony with a beautiful view of the lake. The hotel has a fitness room and bike storage facility. Guests can also rent a bike from the front desk.

Hotel Regina is committed to saving energy and installed 753 ft² of solar panels. Nothing is too much trouble for the friendly staff who are always on hand.

Here guests can relax on the terrace and sunbathe on the private beach area. The bar provides drinks and snacks throughout the day. Take a walk into the ancient village of Gravedona where there is wide choice of restaurants for an evening meal.

A nice hotel with an absolutely stunning view

"The hotel is in a fabulous location right by the lake with a stunning view of the mountains, Gravedona and the lake itself. Much quieter than other villages on the lake which suited us perfectly. The hotel does a good continental breakfast but doesn't serve food the rest of the day. It does have a bar though. Quiet, a nice pool, very few children and stunning views. We'd happily go back! Awesome!"
