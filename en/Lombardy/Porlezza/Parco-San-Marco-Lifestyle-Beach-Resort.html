Description

Parco San Marco Lifestyle Beach Resort provides spacious suites and studios, each with flat-screen TV, air conditioning, and views of the lake. The suites also feature a balcony or direct garden access.

Free Wi-Fi is available at reception, both pool areas and the beach.

Ristorante San Marco is the main restaurant on site and serves 4-course menus for dinner, and a rich breakfast buffet in the morning. Other dining options are also available in this large resort.

In the small village of Porlezza, the property is only 9 miles from the center of the city of Lugano in Switzerland, and a 15-minute drive from the Menaggio & Cadennabbia Golf Club.
