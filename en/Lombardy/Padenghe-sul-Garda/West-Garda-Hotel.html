Description

Surrounded by 10 acres, the Hotel West Garda is in a quiet area. It is just a short walk from the harbor and Lake Garda.

All the guest rooms at the West Garda Hotel have recently been refurbished and include all modern facilities.

Enjoy typical Mediterranean cuisine and both fish and meat specialties at the hotel restaurant or enjoy a drink at the poolside bar.
