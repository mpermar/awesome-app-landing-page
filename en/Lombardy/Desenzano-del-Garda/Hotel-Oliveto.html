Description

One of the biggest lakes in Italy, Lake Garda is set among the provinces of Trento, Verona and Brescia. Citrus fruits and olive trees grow on its shores, creating an enchanting Mediterranean garden among the peaks of the Alps.

When you are not exploring the hotel’s spectacular surroundings you can take advantage of the facilities, such as meeting rooms, an indoor and outdoor swimming pool and a solarium.

The Oliveto Restaurant offers local dishes and wines. An American Bar, a children's playground and a fitness center are also available.

Desenzano Del Garda Train Station is 1.6 mi from Hotel Oliveto, while Sirmione is a 20-minute drive from the property.
