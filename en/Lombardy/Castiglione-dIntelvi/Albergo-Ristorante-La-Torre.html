Description

La Torre offers guest rooms with satellite TV, and self- catering apartments. You will receive a remote control to open the parking's gate at all hours.

Free wired internet is available in all La Torre's accommodations. Free Wi-Fi is available in common areas.

Albergo Ristorante La Torre serves freshly prepared Italian meals. Breakfast is buffet style.

The historic center of Valle d’Intelvi is just 3 miles from Albergo La Torre.
