Description

Guest rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a electric tea pot, a bidet, free toiletries and a desk. The private bathroom is equipped with a hair dryer. All rooms at the hotel feature air conditioning and a closet.

The daily breakfast offers continental and buffet options.

Lugano is 24 miles from Hotel Ponti&Ponti. The nearest airport is Malpensa Airport, 12 miles from the property.
