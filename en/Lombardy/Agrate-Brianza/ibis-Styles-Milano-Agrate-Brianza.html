Description

The ibis Styles Milano Agrate Brianza provides free access to the on-site gym. Guests can also enjoy a bar and a restaurant serving a mix of international and Italian cuisine.

From Monday to Friday, a free shuttle bus provides services to Milan's Cologno Nord and Cascina Gobba metro stations. The hotel is a 15-minute drive from the center of Monza and Golf Club Milano. Milan's Linate Airport is 12 miles away.
