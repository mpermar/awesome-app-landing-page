Description

All rooms at the Lefay Resort and Spa Lago Di Garda are spacious, and provide a satellite TV. Each room has a lake-view balcony or terrace.

The property has free access to the spacious Mondo Lefay Spa, which also features indoor and outdoor pools.

A generous breakfast buffet is served in the panoramic on-site restaurant, overlooking the lake.
