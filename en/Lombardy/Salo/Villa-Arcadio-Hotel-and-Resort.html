Description

Beautifully-restored, Hotel & Resort Villa Arcadio features charming interiors. Rooms are spacious, and come with free Wi-Fi, and an LCD TV with Sky channels. They offer views of the lake or the surrounding countryside.

Drinks are available on the terrace, where breakfast is also served during pleasant weather. The restaurant with lounge bar serves tasty, elegantly presented meals. Breakfast is buffet style.

The hotel features a Finnish sauna. Yoga lessons and a range of Oriental massages, beauty and Ayurveda treatments can be booked. There are 17 outdoor fitness areas located along a 1.9 miles circuit that runs around the property.
