Description

Guest rooms come with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, minibar, a electric tea pot, a bidet, bathrobes and a desk. Every room comes with a private bathroom and a shower, and certain accommodations at the hotel have a balcony. All guest rooms include a safety deposit box.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at the property. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the on-site restaurant, which specializes in Italian and international cuisine.

The property has a hammam. The area is popular for skiing, and ski equipment rental is available at Hotel Sporting Family Hospitality.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can give tips on the area.

Pian della Volpe is 1.2 miles from the accommodation.
