Description

Rooms at the Capriolo Hotel are spacious and comfortable. They are decorated in an Alpine style and feature satellite TV and Wi-Fi access. Most rooms have balconies with mountain views.

Capriolo's restaurant is open early for a buffet breakfast, and later serves Italian cuisine at lunch and dinner. The bar with fireplace offers a cozy setting for an evening drink.

The hotel is surrounded by shops, bars, and ski schools. It is close to some of the most popular ski slopes along the Italian Alps. An on-site shop selling and renting sports equipment is also available.
