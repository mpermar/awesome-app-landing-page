Description

Rooms at the Bivio Hotel have free Wi-Fi, TV and minibar. For extra comfort, bathrobes and slippers are provided.

There are 2 restaurants, an informal bistro serving light meals, and the Gourmet Restaurant open for dinner. Parking is free.

Hotel Bivio is on the edge of Stelvio National Park. Popular local activities include hiking and mountain biking and skiing.
