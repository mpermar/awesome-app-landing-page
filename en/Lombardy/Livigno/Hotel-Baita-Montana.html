Description

Your room at Baita Montana comes with free Wi-Fi and satellite TV. Each room has a balcony with a view of Livigno, the forest, or the ski slopes.

The free, covered parking lot has an elevator that goes straight to the hotel lobby. There is also a children's mini club, a playroom, and a lawn with lounge chairs.

Baita Montana's spa center is also free, while beauty treatments are available for an additional fee. There is a heated storage room for ski equipment and a bike storage area with a small workshop.

Children and Extra Beds
All children are welcome.
Free!One child under 2 years stays free of charge in a crib.
One child from 3 to 6 years is charged 50 % of the room stay per night and person in an extra bed.
One child from 7 to 12 years is charged 70 % of the room stay per night and person in an extra bed.
One older child or adult is charged 90 % of the room stay per night and person in an extra bed.
The maximum number of extra beds/cribs in a room is 1.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
