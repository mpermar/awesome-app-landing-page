Description

The LING BAO hotel features original Chinese furnishings to create an authentic Asian atmosphere.

Sample the finest of Asian cuisine, mixed with European influences in the LING BAO’s restaurants.

Visit the MANDALA SPA and go for a swim in the indoor and outdoor pools. Treat yourself to a wide variety of Chinese, Thai and Balinese treatments. Linger for a while in the beautiful oriental garden.

The town of Brühl lies near to Cologne and Bonn, making this a good location from which to explore the region and easily access the exhibition centers.
