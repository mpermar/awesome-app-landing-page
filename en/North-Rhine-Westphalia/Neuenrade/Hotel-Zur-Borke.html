Description

At the hotel, every room has a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. All guest rooms have a closet.

Guests at Hotel Zur-Borke can enjoy a continental breakfast.

The area is popular for skiing and cycling.

Winterberg is 31 miles from the accommodation, while Dortmund is 23 miles away. The nearest airport is Dortmund Airport, 19 miles from the property.
