Description

With its long history of hospitality and stately architecture, the hotel building makes a grand backdrop for vacations and celebrations. It is also an ideal base for exploring the Eggegebirge Nature Park and the Southern Teutoburg Forest, with their hilly landscapes, dense woodland and lush meadows.

Make use of the hotel’s extensive spa facilities and outdoor pool. Fine cuisine is offered in the hotel’s 2 restaurants, and your elegant room promises a peaceful night’s rest.

Experiences

09/24/2015

I cannot begin to explain how amazing this hotel was.

"I cannot begin to explain how amazing this hotel was. The staff were welcoming and very helpful. No matter what the request, they were happy to accommodate. The rooms were stunning. Decor was beautiful, quality of furniture was high. Beds were top rate comfortable and the bathrooms were extremely clean and beautifully decorated. Each room had a bathrobe and slippers and the toiletries were local made produce which when used were top quality! The breakfast was an experience like no other! Food was perfectly displayed and no matter what you wanted, they had it! Quality was superior and service was always with a smile. The other restaurants on the grounds were amazing and we ate in them every night! This place also has a spa which was a world of relaxation and free if you booked in to the hotel. Have you ever swam in a heated pool filled with mineral water?? Well this is what they provide! We had some treatments too and they were all wonderful. My husband and I had a mud bath and my children a massage and milk bath. Highly recommend all! The hotel is in a park which you can walk around in. The grounds are stunning and there is this huge lake with ducks and geese that are so tame they walk right up to you. There are beautiful sculptures dotted around the park too. People going to the hotel have a huge respect for each other and the premises. So if you want to come here and be loud and dive bomb into the pool this is not the place for you. The ethos of the hotel is to revive mind, body and soul and it does these with ease. It is a place to be quiet, be healthy and be whole. We absolutely loved every minute of our stay and highly recommend it to everyone."
