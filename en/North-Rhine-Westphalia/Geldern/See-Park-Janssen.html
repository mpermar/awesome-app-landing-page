Description

The See Park Janssen provides elegantly furnished, en-suite rooms in the idyllic countryside surrounding the German-Dutch border.

Relax in the panoramic pool and enjoy the views of the lake and 18-hole golf course. Work out in the modern gym, and pamper yourself in the large sauna area.

Bowling, biking, horseback riding, and Nordic walking are only some of the activities you can enjoy at your doorstep. Personal trainers can be booked for a surcharge.

Treat yourself to freshly prepared, healthy meals in the See Park Terrassen restaurant or the conservatory, which offers wonderful views of the lake. When the weather's nice, you can dine on the attractive terrace.
