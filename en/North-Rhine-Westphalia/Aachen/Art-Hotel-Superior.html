Description

The Art Hotel Superior provides modern rooms with flat-screen TVs, sitting areas, and spacious bathrooms.

The Art Hotel's Roman-style spa area includes a swimming pool and a selection of saunas. Massages and beauty treatments are also available upon request.

Original dishes from around the world are provided in the spacious Il Mercato restaurant. Other facilities include the Tavernetta wine lounge and Boulevard café.

Aachen's old quarter is a 10-minute drive from the Art Hotel Superior. It also provides good access to highways in Germany, Belgium and the Netherlands.
