Description

The Scandinavian-style rooms at DGI-byens Hotel have hardwood floors and light, spacious bathrooms with hairdryers. Room furnishings include a work desk and an armchair.

The restaurant serves healthy and tasty food, including a rich Scandinavian breakfast buffet. Guests can enjoy dining at the outdoor terrace during the summer months.

Tivoli Gardens and Copenhagen Central Train Station are a 4-minute walk from the hotel. Copenhagen International Airport is 20 minutes from the hotel by train.

Vesterbro is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, museums and sightseeing.
