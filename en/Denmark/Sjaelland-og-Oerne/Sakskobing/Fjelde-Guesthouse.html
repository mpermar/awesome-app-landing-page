Description

Situated in Sakskøbing in the Lolland Region, 35 km from Rødbyhavn ferry terminal, Fjelde Guesthouse features a barbecue and views of the garden.

Free private parking is available on site. Highway E45 is 7 km away. Certain units have a seating area where you can relax.

A terrace or patio are featured in certain rooms. Fjelde Guesthouse features free WiFi throughout the property.

There is a shared lounge and gift shop at the property. Guests can also relax in the garden and children can enjoy the playground.

Knuthenborg Safaripark is 17 km from the property. Maribo is 11 km from Fjelde Guesthouse, while Nykøbing Falster is 20 km away.
