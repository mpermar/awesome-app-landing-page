Description

This B&B is set on a 19th-century estate, 3. 5 km outside Nykøbing Falster. WiFi, parking and tea/coffee are free.

Guests with electric vehicles can charge them for free. Knuthenborg Safari Park is within 35 minutes’ drive.

Ny Kirstineberg Gods Bed & Breakfast’s rooms feature individual decor and a TV. Bathroom facilities are either private or shared.

A communal kitchen, lounge and patio are available at Ny Kirstineberg Gods Bed & Breakfast.

The property also has a large garden. Lalandia Water Park is a 30-minute drive away.
