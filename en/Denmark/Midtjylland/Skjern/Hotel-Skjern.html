Description

Hotel Skjern is located on a quiet pedestrian street in the town of Skjern, just opposite the train station.

It offers modern rooms with free WiFi and free parking. All rooms feature a work desk, seating area and cable TV.

An electric kettle is provided in each room. General facilities at Skjern Hotel include a children’s playroom and a cosy lounge with newspapers.

Golf, fishing and hiking are popular activities in the area around Hotel Skjern. River Skjern is only 600 metres away.
