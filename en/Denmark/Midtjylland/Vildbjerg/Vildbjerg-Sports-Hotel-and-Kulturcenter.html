Description

Located in central Vildbjerg, Vildbjerg Sports Hotel offers free gym and water park access, free Wi-Fi and bright rooms with a flat-screen TV.

Vildbjerg Train Station is 350 metres away. All accommodation at Vildbjerg Sports Hotel & Kulturcenter has a work desk and private bathroom facilities.

On-site leisure activities include a tennis court, table tennis and pool table. Relaxation options include a heated indoor swimming pool and a hot tub.

Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served as a buffet every day. Trehøje Golf Club is 6 km away.
