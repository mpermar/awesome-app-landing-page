Description

Set on a working farm, Vestervang Bed & Breakfast is 3 km from Vildbjerg. It provides free Wi-Fi, a fully equipped guest kitchen and modern rooms with a flat-screen TV and private bathroom.

Renovated in 2012, guest rooms have wooden floors, a desk and a dining area. All rooms open onto the communal terrace.

Vestervang B&B’s daily breakfast is based on organic ingredients, including homemade bread.

In summer, guests can use the BBQ grill. Children are welcome to pet the farm’s animals, and a playground is available.
