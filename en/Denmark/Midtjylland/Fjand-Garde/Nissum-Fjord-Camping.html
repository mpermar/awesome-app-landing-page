Description

There's also a kitchen in some of the units equipped with an oven.

A à la carte breakfast is served at the property.

The campground has a seasonal outdoor pool.

Guests can go cycling or hiking or relax in the garden.

Holstebro is 18 miles from Nissum Fjord Camping, while Ringkøbing is 16 miles from the property. Karup Airport is 37 miles away.
