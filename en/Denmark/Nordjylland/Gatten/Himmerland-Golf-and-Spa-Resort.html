Description

With a peaceful location in the North Jutland Region of Denmark, this resort features a professional golf course and 3 restaurants.

It offers a spa, sports activities and free Wi-Fi. The rooms of Himmerland Golf & Spa Resort have a flat-screen TV, a seating area and a fresh bathroom.

Bathrobes are added in each room for extra comfort. Gastronomien Restaurant offers gourmet cuisine and a selection of quality wines, while tasty salads and other light dishes are served at Restaurant P.

O. L. O. Pizzas, burgers and steaks are also available. For those who enjoy sport activities, Himmerland has a tennis court, a bowling alley and a pool table.

For relaxation, the spa provides rejuvenating face and body treatments. Guests wishing to explore surroundings can rent a bike at the reception.

The town of Farsø is 16 km from the property. On-site parking is free for guests.
