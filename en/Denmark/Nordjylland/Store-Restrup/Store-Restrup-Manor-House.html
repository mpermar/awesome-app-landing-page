Description

Situated 13 km from Aalborg, this historic manor house was built in 1723, It is surrounded by lush green forest in an area that has been inhabited since the Stone Age.

It offers free WiFi, as well as a restaurant, bar and surrounding garden with a terrace.

Each individually and elegantly decorated room at Store Restrup Manor House features a TV and private bathroom.

Guests can enjoy the 17th-century Baroque-style halls, as well as the Tapestry Room, which is decorated with French tapestry from 1815.

The à la carte restaurant at Store Restrup Manor House, serves traditional cuisine, made with seasonal and fresh ingredients.

Lunch is available on request. Fishing, hiking and golfing are common area activities.
