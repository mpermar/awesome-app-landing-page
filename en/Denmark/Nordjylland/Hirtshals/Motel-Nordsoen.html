Description

The family-run Motel Nordsøen is located in Hirtshals, 50 metres from Nordsøen Oceanarium.

It offers brightly decorated guest rooms with a private bathroom and free internet access.

A buffet breakfast is served every day at Nordsøen Motel. In the summer, guests can enjoy it on the terrace overlooking the nearby woodlands.

A small self-service kitchen is also available for guests’ use. Children will enjoy the on-site playroom with a TV.

Guests can relax in the garden. The staff can help arrange green fees at Hirtshals Golf Club, a 6-minute drive away.

Lilleheden Train Station is located 100 metres away. The large Kjul Beach, which has been awarded the Blue Flag, is located 500 metres from the motel.
