Description

Set in the countryside on a working farm, this B&B is 4 km from the town of Nykøbing Mors.

It provides free WiFi, brightly decorated rooms and a communal TV lounge. A table and set of chairs are found in each Agerdal Bed & Breakfast guest room.

Bathroom facilities are shared or private. Guests can enjoy free coffee and tea, as well as use a shared microwave.

Agerdal B&B also has a large garden and terrace with swings and a camp fire. Sheep, geese, chickens, along with cats and a dog are found on site.

Jesperhus Flower Park is a 12-minute drive away, while fishing and swimming in the Limfjord is 6 km from the property.
