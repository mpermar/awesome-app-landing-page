Description

Featuring a spacious children's playroom with a ball pit and an outdoor playground, Hestkær Family Rooms offers pet-friendly accommodation in Grindsted.

Legoland and Lalandia are only a 20-minute drive away. Every room is equipped with a flat-screen TV, Playstation 3 console and video games.

Some units include a seating area to relax in after a busy day. There are 2 guest kitchens at the property and an outdoor barbecue area.

Breakfast baskets can be booked on site. Guests can relax in the outdoor seating areas, while children can enjoy outdoor activities in the garden surroundings at Hestkær Family Rooms.

You can also play table tennis and billiards at this hostel. Herning is 49 km from Hestkær Family Rooms, while Kolding is 47 km from the property.
