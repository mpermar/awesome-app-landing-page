Description

This property features a large garden with children’s playground, ponies and bicycle rental, just 3.

5 km from Legoland Theme Park and 25 km from Givskud Zoo. Gregersminde’s rooms feature a flat-screen TV and seating area.

Some rooms have a private kitchen, dining area and bathroom. Other rooms share a TV-lounge, kitchen and bathroom.

Wi-Fi is free. Guests at Gregersminde enjoy free parking. Billund Airport is 10 minutes’ drive away.
