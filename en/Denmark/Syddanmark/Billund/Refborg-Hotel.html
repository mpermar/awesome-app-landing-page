Description

Refborg Hotel is located in central Billund, only 5 minutes’ drive from Legoland Amusement Park.

Local wine, food specialities and crafts can be discovered at the on-site gourmet boutique.

Light meals and refreshments can also be purchased from the boutique's cafe. All rooms at Refborg Hotel have a flat-screen TV, work desk and private bathroom with a shower.

A selection of international dishes and traditional Danish specialities are offered at Restaurant Spiseriet.

A continental breakfast is also offered. The staff can help arrange golf trips to Gyttegård Golf Club, 5 km from the property.
