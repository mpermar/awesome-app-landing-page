Description

Hotel Propellen is only 300 metres from Legoland Amusement Park and 3 km from Billund International Airport.

Wi-Fi internet is free, and all rooms have a flat-screen TV. The hotel’s bright, fresh rooms have carpeted floors, satellite TV and a minibar.

Guests can use the sauna, exercise room or indoor swimming pool. Children can play in the kiddie pool and heart-shaped whirlpool.

Indoor and outdoor playgrounds are also found on site. Propellen has 3 dining options: Cockpit, Restaurant Ludwig and Restaurant Pejsestuen.

They offer everything from quick, light dishes to gourmet cuisine by cosy fireplace settings.
