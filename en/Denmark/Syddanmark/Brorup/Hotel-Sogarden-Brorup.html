Description

Only 300 metres from Brørup Station, this hotel offers a bowling alley and rooms with private balconies, free parking and free WiFi.

Legoland Theme Park is 30 km away. Hotel Søgården Brørup’s rooms have a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with shower.

The in-house à la carte restaurant has a play area for children and serves Danish as well as international dishes.

A bar and a café are also available. Guests can relax on the garden terrace. Hotel Søgården's leisure facilities include a minigolf course and air-hockey and pool tables.

Wi-Fi is free in public areas. Søgården is a short stroll from several tennis courts, and 2 golf courses are found within 10 km of the hotel.
