Description

Set in central Jutland’s countryside, this B&B is 6 km from the town of Ansager and within 30 minutes' drive of Legoland Theme Park.

It offers free in-room Wi-Fi and a quiet location in beautiful surroundings. Guests at Pension Lærkelill can choose rooms with either private or shared bathrooms.

All rooms have a TV, seating area and work desk, as well as free coffee and tea. During nice weather, guests can sit outside on Lærkelill’s garden furniture or on the communal terrace in front of the guest rooms.

The neighbouring forest has a petanque pitch. The town of Grindsted is 15 km from the B&B.
