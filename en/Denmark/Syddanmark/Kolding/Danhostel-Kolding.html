Description

Just 10 minutes’ walk from central Kolding, this hostel offers views of Kolding Fjord and the harbour.

Facilities include a communal kitchen and TV lounge. WiFi is free. Danhostel Kolding’s bright, simple rooms have either a private bathroom or shared bathroom facilities.

Some rooms have a view of Koldinghus Castle. Relaxation and leisure options include a garden and terrace as well as table tennis and barbecue equipment.

The 11th-century Koldinghus Castle is less than a 20-minute walk from Danhostel Kolding.
