Description

Surrounded by forest, Hotel Koldingfjord is set in an impressive 1911 building, just a 12-minute drive from central Kolding.

It offers rooms with flat-screen satellite TVs and free WiFi. All rooms at Koldingfjord Hotel have a work desk and minibar.

Some rooms offer views of the Kolding Fjord, while others are located in an annex building.

Guests can stay active in the fitness area or relax in the sauna or indoor swimming pool.

Hiking is available on the trails around the hotel. A terrace is also available, featuring views of the surrounding nature.

The à la carte Koldingfjord Restaurant serves international dishes. Café Louise has a summer terrace, and offers light snacks and drinks.

Drinks are also available at Frederik VIII Bar. Hotel Koldingfjord is 1. 5 km from the Trapholt Museum of Modern Art.
