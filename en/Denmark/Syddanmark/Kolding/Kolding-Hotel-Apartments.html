Description

This property is located in Kolding’s Old Town, on the shore of Lake Slotssøen. It offers free internet access.

Koldinghus Castle is a 2-minute walk away, while Kolding Station is 700 metres away.

A kitchen with a dishwasher, as well as a seating area is standard at Kolding Hotel Apartments.

Each also features designer furniture and cable TV. Some apartments are located in triangular, circular, square-shaped or star-shaped towers.

Kolding Hotel Apartments offer views of the lake, street, or courtyard. Discounted admission is offered to the nearby Slotssøbadet Water Park and Fitness Center.

Legoland Amusement Park and the Givskud Zoo are both 40 minutes' drive from the apartments.
