Description

Located on Rømø Island, 10 minutes’ walk from Rømø’s harbour, this resort offers modern apartments.

On-site activities include a wellness center, golf course and bowling alley. Free WiFi is available.

Enjoy Resorts Rømø offers apartments with a fully equipped kitchen as well as a washing machine.

The apartments also have a fireplace and flat-screen TV. Guests can enjoy free access to the 2,600 m² spa and wellness centre.

It features a gym, sauna and steam room, in addition to bookable beauty treatments.

There are also a restaurant and bar on site. Bicycles and golf equipment are available for rent.

Rømø’s popular wide beaches are only 2 km away, while Østerby’s town centre is a 15-minute walk away.
