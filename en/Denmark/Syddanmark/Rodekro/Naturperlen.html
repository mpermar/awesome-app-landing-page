Description

Offering a barbecue and water sports facilities, Naturperlen is situated in Rødekro in the Syddanmark Region.

Flensburg is 37 km away. Free WiFi is offered throughout the property. The accommodation is fitted with a flat-screen TV.

There is a seating and/or dining area in some units. A refrigerator and kettle are also featured.

There is also a kitchen in some of the units, equipped with a dishwasher, an oven, and a microwave.

Every unit comes with a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries. Free private parking is available at the aparthotel.

Naturperlen also includes a terrace. Bike hire is available at the property and the area is popular for windsurfing.

A range of activities are offered in the area, such as cycling and fishing. Kolding is 41 km from Naturperlen.
