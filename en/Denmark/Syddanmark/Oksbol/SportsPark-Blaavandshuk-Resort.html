Description

Featuring free WiFi, a spa centre and a barbecue, SportsPark Blaavandshuk Resort offers accommodation in Oksbøl, 21 km from Esbjerg.

Free private parking is available on site. Certain rooms include a seating area for your convenience.

There are shops at the property. Guests can play golf for free on some of SportsPark Blaavandshuk Golfcenter's par 3 and par 4 holes, 12 km from the property.

Guests at the resort have free access to a fitness centre and a waterpark. There is also table tennis, football and badminton opportunities.

Ribe is 45 km from SportsPark Blaavandshuk Resort, while Ho is 9 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Billund Airport, 56 km from SportsPark Blaavandshuk Resort.
