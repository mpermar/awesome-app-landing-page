Description

These farmhouse apartments are surrounded by forests and greenery in Give, 10 km from Legoland Amusement Park.

It offers free horse riding, free private parking and free Wi-Fi access. Stutteri Skandihest’s apartments have a TV, a DVD player and a patio.

All have private bathroom and kitchen facilities. Stutteri has a large garden, terraces and a children’s playground.

Facilities at Stutteri Skandihest include a fully equipped BBQ area and a putting green.
