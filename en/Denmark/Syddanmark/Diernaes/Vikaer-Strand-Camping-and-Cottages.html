Description

Overlooking the Diernæs Bay, Vikær Strand Camping & Cottages offers a private child-friendly beach and cottages with a private terrace and WiFi access.

Haderslev town centre is a 10-minute drive away. A fully equipped kitchen with a stove, fridge and coffee maker is featured in all cottages at Vikær Strand.

Most feature sea views. Cottages have either a private bathroom or access to shared facilities.

Freshly baked bread is available all days during high season and on weekends in low season.

Basic groceries are available at the on-site kiosk. Restaurants are found in the surrounding area.

Leisure facilities include a mini golf course and a beach with beach volleyball nets and fishing opportunities.

Children will enjoy the 2 on-site playgrounds with bouncy castles. The property also offers an indoor TV lounge with a games room and a play area.

Fishing is also possible at Lake Slivsø, 3 km away. Kolding city centre is a 25-minute drive from the property.
