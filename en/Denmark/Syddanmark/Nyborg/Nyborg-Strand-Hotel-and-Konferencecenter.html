Description

Overlooking the Great Belt strait, Nyborg Strand is a beachfront property just 2 km from Nyborg’s Old Town.

Odense city centre is within 30 minutes' drive. The on-site spa offers a sauna, hot tubs and a rain shower area.

A cold water basin, relax lounge and foot baths are also featured. All rooms at Nyborg Strand include a TV, a work desk and a private bathroom.

Tea/coffee facilities are also available in the rooms. Restaurant Panorama serves seasonal Danish and French cuisine, plus panoramic views of the sea.

Grilled dishes are available at Bistroen, which features an outdoor terrace. Stjernebaren serves chilled beers and drinks in a relaxing atmosphere.

Guests can exercise in the well-equipped gym, and play billiards or boules on site.

The garden features a putting green, and a play area is available for children during summer months.

Parking and WiFi are free. St Knud's Golf Club is 3 km from the hotel. Nyborg Fortress is a 5-minute drive away.
