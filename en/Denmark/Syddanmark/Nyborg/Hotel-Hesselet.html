Description

Stay active on the hotel's tennis court or have a swim in the pool. Treat yourself to a relaxing massage or jacuzzi. Take time out for a sauna or steam bath before sampling the gourmet food in Hesselet's own restaurant.

All of Hotel Hesselet's guest accommodations have a private bathroom with a double sink and a separate bathtub and shower.

Guests are sure to enjoy the view from their comfortable rooms. On one side lies the beach-view of the Great Belt (Denmark's biggest sea strait); on the other is a scene of green forest.
