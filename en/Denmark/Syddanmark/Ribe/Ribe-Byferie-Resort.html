Description

Ribe Byferie Resort is an 8-minute walk from the medieval town of Ribe, 1 km from the Viking Museum.

It offers free parking and rooms with terraces or balconies and free Wi-Fi. All rooms and apartments at Ribe Byferie have a seating area and a refrigerator.

All apartments have a kitchen with a dishwasher and an electric kettle. Guests enjoy free access to swimming pools, hot tubs, water slides and saunas at 2 nearby centres (Esbjerg 30 km).

On-site facilities include a playground, large outdoor chess game and BBQ area. Free tea/coffee is available at reception.

The Wadden Sea Centre is a 15-minute drive away, while Legoland Theme Park is 60 km from the property.
