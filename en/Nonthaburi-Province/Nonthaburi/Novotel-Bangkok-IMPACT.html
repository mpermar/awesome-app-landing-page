Description

Novotel Bangkok IMPACT is approximately a 20-minute ride from downtown Bangkok.

The spacious rooms at Novotel Bangkok IMPACT feature wood-on-white decor with a glass-wall bathroom. Each non-smoking unit includes a sofa and fridge with minibar. Guests can slip on the soft bathrobe and enjoy watching a flat-screen TV.

Both a fitness center and business center are available.

Prego Restaurant serves international dishes for lunch and dinner. The Square is open for all meals and features a buffet spread of Thai cuisine and Western foods. A lobby lounge is also housed at the hotel.
