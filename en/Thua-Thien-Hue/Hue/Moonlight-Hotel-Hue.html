Description

Conveniently located just a 20-minute drive to Thuan An beach, the property is also a just a 3-minute walk to Huong River and a 25-minute drive to Phu Bai International Airport.

Rooms at the Moonlight Hotel Hue feature air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a minibar and a telephone. The private bathrooms include hot/cold shower facilities or a bathtub and a hairdryer.

Guests can enjoy a relaxing traditional massage or treat themselves at the spa, and a 24-hour front desk can assist with ironing/laundry services, car or bike rentals and room service. For convenience, airport shuttle services are available for a fee.

The Moonlight Hotel has an in-house restaurant that serves local and international cuisines and 2 bars.
