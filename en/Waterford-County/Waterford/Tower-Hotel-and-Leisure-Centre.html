Description

Located directly opposite Reginald's Tower on the Mall, 350 feet from the House of Waterford Crystal and Waterford Museum of Treasures. The hotel is also just a short stroll from countless shops and other local attractions.

With a great location for touring South East Ireland, the Waterford Tower Hotel & Leisure Center is just a little over 75 minutes drive from Dublin and 45 minutes' from Rosslare Harbor.

The hotel is close to some of the finest golf courses in region, as well as the charming fishing villages of Dunmore East, Passage East and Cheekpoint.

The leisure and fitness center has a well-equipped gym, 50 feet deck level pool which has a separate kiddieâ€™s pool.

It also features a sauna, hot tub, steam room and plunge pool.
