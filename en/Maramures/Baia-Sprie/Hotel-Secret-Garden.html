Description

All of the rooms have cable TV, a minibar and a robe and slippers. Each room also has air-conditioning.

Guests at Secret Garden Hotel can also make use of the 3 outdoor pools and the indoor swimming pool, gym and sauna. The hotel also offers a range of massage treatments including a hot volcanic stone massage.

Hotel Secret Garden is a 15-minute drive from Baia Mara which offers sights including the Art Gallery, Baia Mara Ethnographic Museum and the Planetarium.

Recreational activities available in the local area include fishing and hiking in the surrounding mountains. Secret Garden has tennis courts on site as well as a game room with darts and ping-pong facilities.

In winter, guests can enjoy skiing on the Cavnic and Suior ski resorts, which are both 9 miles from the hotel.

The hotel restaurant serves a range of international dishes both inside and on the terrace.
