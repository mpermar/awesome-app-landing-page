Description

At the hotel, each room includes a terrace with a garden view. Rooms include a closet and a flat-screen TV, and selected rooms also feature a patio.

Breakfast is available daily, and includes continental, buffet and Full English/Irish options.

De Opstal Country Lodge has an outdoor pool. You can play darts at the accommodation, and the area is popular for hiking.

Safari Ostrich Show Farm is 11 miles from De Opstal Country Lodge, while Swartberg Pass is 15 miles away. The nearest airport is George Airport, 37 miles from the hotel.
