Description

The apartments are in different buildings, with either a harbor or pool view. Each brightly decorated unit features a satellite TV, kitchen facilities, a private bathroom and a balcony.

Guests can relax in Club Mykonos with the sauna, massages and a hot tub. Other sporting options include mini golf, ping-pong and boats. Food and beverages are provided at its bars and restaurants.

Free private parking is available on site. West Coast National Park and West Coast Fossil Park are a 20-minute drive away.
