Description

Styled in tan hues and elegant upholstery, the hotel boasts 5-star décor. Rayentray Puerto Madryn’s beach front location ensures scenic views from every angle.

All rooms have free Wi-Fi, flat-screens with cable TVs and DVD. They are equipped with floor to ceiling windows and sofas. There is 24-hour room service.

The restaurant serves a full buffet breakfast with croissants, homemade pastries and regional jams. There is also a coffee area equipped in a literary-cafe style.

Rayentray hotel is only 6 miles from El Tehuelche Airport and a 15-minute drive from downtown Puerto Madryn.

The hotel offers its guests 24-hour assistance.
