Description

All guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a TV. With a private bathroom, some units at Hotel Delfa also provide guests with a balcony. The rooms at the accommodation have air conditioning and a desk.

Guests at Hotel Delfa can enjoy a continental breakfast. There's an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of Italian dishes and also offers Gluten-free and Dairy-free options.

The hotel has a playground.

Salerno is 20 miles from Hotel Delfa, while Positano is 29 miles away. The nearest airport is Capodichino Airport, 47 miles from the accommodation.
