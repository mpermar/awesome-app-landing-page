Description

Boasting spectacular views of the sea and Mount Vesuvius, a scenic garden, and a variety of panoramic hiking trails, the hotel offers elegant accommodations in which to relax amidst stunning natural surroundings.

With an ample parking lot, free shuttle service to the center of Sorrento and a comprehensive host of tourist services, Hotel Prestige is committed to making your vacation hassle-free.

Tours with English speaking guides can be arranged to points of interest in the region, including Naples and Pompeii, as well as shuttle service to the airport and the train station.
