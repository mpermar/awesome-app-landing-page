Description

Rooms at La Badia Hotel feature a satellite flat-screen TV, safe and desk. The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries. Some rooms have a balcony with views of the Tyrrhenian Sea and Mount Vesuvius.

An Italian buffet breakfast is served daily. The restaurant specializes in Campania cuisine, and guests can sample Limoncello, Sorrento's famous lemon liqueur, at the bar.

Free WiFi is available in public areas. Sorrento Harbor, where guests can catch ferries to Capri, is a 25-minute drive from the property, while Sorrento Train Station is 1.9 mi away.
