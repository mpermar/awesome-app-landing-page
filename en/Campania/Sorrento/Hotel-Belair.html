Description

Air-conditioned rooms at the 4-star Belair Hotel feature a satellite flat-screen TV, minibar and safe. The private bathroom comes with a hairdryer.

A continental breakfast is provided daily and includes both hot and cold items. All guests have access to the property's lounge where free drinks and snacks are provided daily, from 12:00 until 19:00. The restaurant specialises in Campania cuisine, and is served on one of the panoramic terraces with views of the Bay of Naples.

Guests can enjoy the fitness centre and relax on the terrace with sun loungers. Club lounge access with free drinks and snacks from 12:00 to 19:00 is also offered.

The property provides free private parking on site. Sorrento Train Station, where guests can catch trains to Naples, is 1.2 miles away. Positano is a 30-minute drive away.
