Description

Enjoy a day trip with the suggestions provided by the staff and come back to relax in an elegant and serene atmosphere. The hotel rooms are elegantly furnished and equipped with all amenities, and the whole facility presents a charming décor. The restaurant, overlooking the thermal springs, offers traditional Mediterranean cuisine, prepared with organic products from the region.

A conference room, fitted with audio and video equipment and internet connection, is also available for meetings and presentations.
