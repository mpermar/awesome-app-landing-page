Description

This pretty hotel is surrounded by its own private gardens and features a large swimming pool complete with sun loungers. Take a stroll down to the romantic beach of Faraglioni or simply relax at the hotel outside by the pool or in the comfortable lounge area.

The Canasta Hotel is a modern establishment which has recently been renovated. All of its guest rooms are tastefully furnished and contain modern amenities.
