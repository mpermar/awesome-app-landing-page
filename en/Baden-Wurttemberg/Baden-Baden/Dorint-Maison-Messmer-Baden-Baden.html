Description

The soundproofed rooms and suites at the Dorint Maison Messmer Baden-Baden feature a flat-screen TV, mini-bar, and stylish bathroom. WiFi is free in all areas for guests.

The "Royal Spa" features a 800 m² spa area and includes a striking indoor pool, a beauty salon with different treatment rooms, various saunas and a modern gym. Scenic jogging routes can be found in the surrounding area.

Fine specialties are served in the Dorint’s Salon J.B. Messmer restaurant, while the Theaterkeller restaurant serves food from the Baden-Alsace region. The Kaminbar bar features a cozy fireplace, where you can enjoy a drink.

The famous Baden-Baden Casino is only a 2-minute walk from the Maison Messmer. The Caracalla Spa is a 10-minute walk away.
