Description

Elegant, individually styled rooms with high quality furnishings are provided at Europäischer Hof Heidelberg. Bathrobes and slippers are provided in the modern bathrooms.

All guests can use the well equipped gym for free. Single travelers enjoy free use of the Panorama Spa Club, and this includes a pool, saunas and a steam room.

The roof terrace with lounge chairs features excellent views of the city and countryside. The sophisticated Kurfürstenstube restaurant offers gourmet international cuisine, and fine wines can be enjoyed in the Europa Bar. A rich breakfast buffet can be booked each morning.

The Philosophenweg Path is just under 1 miles from Europäischer Hof Heidelberg, and overlooks the scenic Neckar River.

Altstadt is a great choice for travelers interested in restaurants, history and culture.
