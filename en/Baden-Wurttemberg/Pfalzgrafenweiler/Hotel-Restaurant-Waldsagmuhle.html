Description

An ideal base in all seasons, the Hotel Restaurant Waldsägmühle provides classically furnished, comfortable rooms with free wireless internet access.

Wake up to a delicious, complimentary breakfast before setting out on an adventure through the surrounding nature trails by foot, by bike or on skis, depending on the weather.

On your return, why not relax in the spa area, featuring a sauna, solarium, beauty treatments and an indoor pool, as well as the Waldsägmühle’s new Zinsbach Therme thermal bath.

Excellent regional and international cuisine is available every day in the restaurant, which you can enjoy alongside a fine glass of wine and a relaxed atmosphere.

Popular day trip destinations include Freudenstadt with its large marketplace, and Lake Constance.
