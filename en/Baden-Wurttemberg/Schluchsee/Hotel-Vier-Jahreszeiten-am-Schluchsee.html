Description

Spacious and modern rooms and apartments at the Hotel Vier Jahreszeiten am Schluchsee include a satellite TV, telephone with answering machine, and balcony.

A buffet breakfast is offered each morning, and a selection of drinks can be enjoyed in the Karussellbar. Cafe Gugelhupf offers coffee, cakes and snacks. A half board option with a 6-course dinner or themed buffet is also offered. In the evenings, Club Vulcano opens with a live DJ.

As well as use of the indoor and outdoor pools, guests enjoy a steam room, hot tub and massage and beauty treatments. Other leisure facilities include table tennis and a children’s playground.

With around 99 mi of mapped nature paths, the surrounding countryside is perfect for cycling and hiking. A free shuttle service from and to Schluchsee Train Station is offered, just 0.9 mi away.
