Description

Rooms and suites are available at the Villa Hammerschmiede. These are bright rooms individually decorated with a mix of modern and antique furniture. All feature views of the surrounding gardens.

The peaceful surroundings include the Hammerschmiede’s extensive private park grounds. At night, these are lit up, and can be enjoyed from the English-style glass pavilion.

The restaurant offers a varied menu, including regional specialties and fine dining dishes.
