Description

Rooms at d'Aragona come with a mini-bar and a TV. Most have a balcony overlooking the pool or gardens.

Guests can enjoy drinks at the bar and specialties from the Puglia region at the hotel restaurant. Breakfast is buffet style and includes sweet and savory items.

Conversano is well placed for exploring Puglia. The seaside town of Polignano a Mare is 6.2 mi away, while Bari center and Bari Airport are around 40 minutes away by car.
