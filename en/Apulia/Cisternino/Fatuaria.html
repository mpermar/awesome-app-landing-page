Description

At the resort, rooms have a patio. Complete with a private bathroom equipped with free toiletries, all guest rooms at Fatuaria have a flat-screen TV and air conditioning, and certain rooms are contain a seating area.

A buffet, à la carte or Italian breakfast is served at the property.

Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.

Speaking Italian and English at the 24-hour front desk, staff are always on hand to help.

Alberobello is 13 miles from Fatuaria, while Taranto is 19 miles away. Casale Airport is 26 miles from the property.
