Description

The accommodations come with a fully equipped kitchen with an oven, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bidet. Some units have a balcony and/or patio.

Guests can grab a bite to eat in the on-site restaurant, which serves Italian cuisine.

The resort village has a playground.

Snorkeling and windsurfing are possible within the area, and Victor Village offers a private beach area.

Gallipoli is 15 miles from the accommodation, while Otranto is 26 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Casale Airport, 55 miles from Victor Village.
