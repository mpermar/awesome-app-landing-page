Description

Guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Villa Donna Lisa Wine Hotel has some accommodations that have garden views, and the rooms are equipped with a private bathroom and a desk. The rooms feature a closet.

The daily breakfast offers continental and buffet options.

Bike rental and car rental are available at this hotel and the area is popular for cycling.

Lecce is 11 miles from the accommodation, while Gallipoli is 23 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Casale Airport, 19 miles from Villa Donna Lisa Wine Hotel.
