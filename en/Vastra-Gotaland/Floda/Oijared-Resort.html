Description

All rooms at Öijared Resort come with a seating area and a flat-screen TV. Rooms come with a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries, while certain rooms here will provide you with a patio. Guest rooms feature a desk.

A buffet breakfast is available daily at the property.

Öijared Resort offers an indoor pool. A variety of activities are offered in the area, such as cycling and hiking.

The reception can provide advice on the area in order to help guests plan their day.

Gothenburg Landvetter Airport is 14 miles from the property.
