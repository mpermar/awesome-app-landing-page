Description

Sea Sand Resort is a 15-minute boat ride from Ao Nang Beach and a 45-minute boat ride from Krabi Town.

The bungalows and suites at the resort are well-equipped with a minibar, refrigerator and TV with cable channels. Hot shower facilities and toiletries are provided in each private bathroom.

Guests can go on a snorkeling and fishing trip, or enjoy a relaxing Thai massage. The resort provides car rental services and sightseeing and travel arrangements can be made at the tour desk.

Sand Sea Restaurant features beachfront dining, and serves a variety of local and international dishes.
