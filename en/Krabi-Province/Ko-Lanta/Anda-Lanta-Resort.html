Description

Rooms feature air conditioning, a private balcony and a private bathroom. Wi-Fi is free.

Guests can take a dip in Anda Lanta’s outdoor pool, or have local and Western meals at Pink Pearl Restaurant. They can also sip drinks at the beach front bar.

Resort facilities include a library, laundry facilities and a tour desk. Kayak rentals are available.

Anda Lanta Resort is 2 miles from Koh Lanta National Marine Park.
