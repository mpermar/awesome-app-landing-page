Description

Rooms at Anyavee Tubkaek are decorated in a modern Asian design. Offering an LCD TV, they also have a coffee/tea maker, minibar and large bathroom with hairdryer.

Guests can have a refreshing swim in the pool, which also has an attached children's pool. To relax, massage services are available.

Anyavee Tubkaek Beach Resort is a 40-minute drive from Krabi International Airport. The tourist center of Ao Nang Beach is just 20 minute drive away.

Half and full day tour arrangements can be made at Anyavee, along with car rentals. Free private parking is offered.
