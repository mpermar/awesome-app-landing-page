Description

Guest rooms at the resort are equipped with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kitchen, a dining area and a private bathroom. Rooms come with a coffee machine, while some have a terrace. All guest rooms at AliSea Pool Villas have air conditioning and a desk.

Breakfast is available every morning, and includes Full English/Irish and Asian options. At the accommodation you'll find a restaurant serving Asian cuisine. Halal and vegetarian options can also be requested.

Krua Thara Seafood Restaurant is 0.9 miles from AliSea Pool Villas, while Aonang Mosque is 2.9 miles from the property. Krabi International Airport is 13 miles away.
