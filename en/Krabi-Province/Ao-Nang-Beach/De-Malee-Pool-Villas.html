Description

Guest rooms features air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fridge, a coffee machine, a shower and a desk. All rooms include a private bathroom with free toiletries, while some have pool views. At the resort rooms include a seating area.

A continental or buffet breakfast is served at the property. Guests can enjoy a meal at the in-house restaurant, which serves international cuisine.

Guests can rent a car or take advantage of the business center.

Krua Thara Seafood Restaurant is 1.6 miles from De Malee Pool Villas, while Aonang Mosque is 1.7 miles away. The nearest airport is Krabi International Airport, 11 miles from the accommodation.
