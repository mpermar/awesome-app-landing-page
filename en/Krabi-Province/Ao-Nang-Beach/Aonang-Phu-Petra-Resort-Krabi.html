Description

Aonang Phu Petra Resort, Krabi, provides beach and airport transportation. Krabi Airport is a 30-minute drive away and Aonang Beach is 10 minutes away by car. A free scheduled shuttle service from the property to Ao Nang City Centre is available.

Modern rooms feature classic dark wood furnishings and spacious balconies. Each has a cable/satellite TV, DVD player and coffee-making facilities. Bathrobes, slippers and a hairdryer are provided.

Massage services are available at the resort and a library corner allows for quiet reading. Other facilities include a tour desk and children’s playground. Motorbikes can be rented for convenience.

Sila Petra Restaurant serves local and international dishes. Enjoy mountain and pool views and sipping cocktails at Phu Anda Lounge.
