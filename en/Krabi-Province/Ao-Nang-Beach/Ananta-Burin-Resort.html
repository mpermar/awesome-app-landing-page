Description

All rooms at Ananta Burin Resort comes with a 36" LCD flat-screen TV, a safety deposit box and a mini-bar. Guests can enjoy pool and garden views of the balcony's sitting area. The private bathroom has a bathtub and separate shower.

The resort offers car rental and a tour desk for guests who wish to go sightseeing or excursions to nearby islands. Luggage storage is provided at the 24-hour front desk. Laundry and dry cleaning services are also available.

American breakfast is served daily at the resort. Thai and international dishes can also be enjoyed at the restaurant.
