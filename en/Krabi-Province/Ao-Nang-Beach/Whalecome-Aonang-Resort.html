Description

All rooms at the resort come with a seating area. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a bidet, while some units at Whalecome Aonang Resort also offer a balcony. At the accommodation, every room includes air conditioning and a flat-screen TV.

The daily breakfast offers continental, buffet or American options. Guests can enjoy a meal at the in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of American dishes and also offers kosher and vegetarian options.

Languages spoken at the 24-hour front desk include English and Thai.

McDonald, Aonang is a 1-minute walk from Whalecome Aonang Resort. Krabi International Airport is 12 miles from the property.
