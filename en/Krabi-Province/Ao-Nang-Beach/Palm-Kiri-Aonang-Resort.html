Description

Guest rooms has air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a electric tea pot, a shower, free toiletries and a desk. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a hair dryer, while some rooms include a terrace. At the resort the rooms include a seating area.

A continental or halal breakfast is available each morning at the property. At Palm Kiri Aonang Resort you'll find a restaurant serving local cuisine.

Dragon Crest Mountain is 5 miles from the accommodation. Krabi International Airport is 11 miles from the property, and the property offers a free shuttle service.
