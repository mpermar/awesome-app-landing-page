Description

Rooms are decorated with wooden furnishings and feature a large balcony to kick-back and enjoy the scenery. Amenities offered in each room include air conditioning, free bottled water daily and room service.

Cool off with a dip in the swimming pool and pamper yourself a traditional Thai massage. Wireless internet access is also available.

To explore the island, rent a car or bike or just inquire with the helpful staff at Krabi Tipa Resort. Other facilities include separate children's pool and playground.

The restaurant offers a delicious selection of Thai favorites and international fare.
