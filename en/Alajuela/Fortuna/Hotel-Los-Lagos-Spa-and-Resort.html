Description

One of our top picks in Fortuna.This tropical hotel is next to Costa Rica's Arenal Volcano, 3.5 miles from La Fortuna. It features a natural hot spring, outdoor adventure activities, a luxurious spa and scenic volcano views.

The air-conditioned rooms and villa sat Hotel Los Lagos Spa & Resort are spacious and comfortable and feature scenic views. All have cable TV, a minibar and safe. Villas have a kitchenette and lounge. The standard and superior rooms are in different areas of the property.

The hotel’s The Las Palmas Restaurant features gourmet, tropical dishes made with local produce. You can also enjoy a cocktail at the relaxing swim-up Lemnos Wet Bar by the seasonal outdoor pool.

The hotel organizes many activities, from canopy tours on wire cables in the trees, to visits to frog, butterfly and crocodile farms. A 2 miles walk takes you to a breathtaking viewpoint of the volcano.

Los Lagos is less than 6 miles from Arenal Lake. Juan Santmaria International Airport is just over a 2-hour drive away.
