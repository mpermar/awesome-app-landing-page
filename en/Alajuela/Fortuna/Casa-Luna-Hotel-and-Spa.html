Description

Each air-conditioned room at Casa Luna Hotel & Spa has a balcony, cable TV and free Wi-Fi. All rooms include a minibar and coffee maker, and bathrooms feature a hairdryer and a safety-deposit box.

The rustic-style Luna Grill restaurant serves a varied menu of steaks and pasta dishes, as well as fresh juices, cocktails and international wines. It offers fantastic views of the Arenal Volcano.

Casa Luna Hotel & Spa is an ideal spot for bird watching and enjoying nature. The Rio Fortuna Waterfall is just under 1 miles away, and the Arenal Volcano and Lake are 9 miles away.

The property also has laundry services, as well as free Wi-Fi, a tour desk and private parking.
