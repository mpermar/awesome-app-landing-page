Description

The air conditioned guest rooms at hotel Tildi feature a TV with satellite channels. Free Wi-Fi is available.

Tildi Hotel Agadir has 2 restaurants serving international and Moroccan cuisine. The bar has a large terrace overlooking the ocean and mountains.

Free private parking is provided on site for guests arriving by car. Al Massira Airport is 12 miles away.
