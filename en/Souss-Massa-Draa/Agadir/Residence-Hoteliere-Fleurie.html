Description

All air-conditioned apartment features a balcony, and is equipped with Satellite TV and free Wi-Fi access. Kitchenettes are equipped with a refrigerator.

The residence’s cafeteria serves a range of Moroccan specialties for breakfast or evening snacks.

Residence Fleurie has a 24-hour reception and offers room service.

The Golf Club Med les Dunes golf course is 3.1 mi from the hotel and the Al Massira Airport is 12 mi away.
