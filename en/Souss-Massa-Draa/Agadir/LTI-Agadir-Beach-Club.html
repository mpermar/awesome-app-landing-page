Description

Views of the gardens, swimming pool or sea are offered in the rooms at Agadir Beach Club. All rooms are air-conditioned and also feature satellite TV. Some rooms have a private balcony.

Dining options at Beach Club include a buffet breakfast, Italian cuisine, Moroccan specialties, fish dishes and international food. Guests can also enjoy a drink in the various bars, nightclub or piano bar.

Free Wi-Fi in public areas, tennis courts and souvenir shops are just some of the other facilities available. Private parking is free on site and the hotel is just just over half a mile from the N1, which leads directly to the Grand Port.
