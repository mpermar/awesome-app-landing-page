Description

Situated on 35 acres of land, of which 5 km are pristine and secluded beaches, Villa Montaña Beach Resort is a northwest Puerto Rico resort offering two swimming pools, two restaurants and spa services.

Guests can enjoy activities like diving, surfing and snorkeling during their stay.

Free WiFi access is available in all rooms. The exotic guestrooms' decor at Villa Montana Beach Resort feature is inspired by the Caribbean Plantation style, complete with earth tones and terracotta tile flooring.

Rooms provide either garden or sea views. The room is fitted with a flat-screen TV and a refrigerator.

Free parking is available on site. Eclipse Restaurant serves meals on a covered terrace with views of the sea.

The ‘O’ is an Asian inspired restaurant featuring sushi. Picnics on the beach can also be arranged.

Villa Montaña Beach Resort also features a tennis court, a fitness centre and a children's playground.

Complimentary beach cruiser bikes, snorkel gear, and horseback riding are also available upon request (reservation needed).
