Description

The rooms have a satellite flat-screen TV, air conditioning and a dining area with coffee machine and fridge. Featuring a shower, the private bathrooms also include a hairdryer. Extras include sofa, a safe and ironing facilities.

At Hyatt Place Manati, there is a fitness center. The property provides free parking.

This property is 1.5 miles from Manati town center and a 40-minute drive from Old San Juan City Centre. Luis Munoz Marin International Airport is 35 miles away. Airport transportation can be arranged upon request.
