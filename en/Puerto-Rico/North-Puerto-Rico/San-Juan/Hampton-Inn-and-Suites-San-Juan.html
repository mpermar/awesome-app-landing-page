Description

Nestled in the Isla Verde area of San Juan, Puerto Rico, the Hampton Inn is 1 km from Luis Muñoz Marín International Airport.

It offers free WiFi and an outdoor pool with a swim-up bar. Each classic guest room at Hampton Inn & Suites San Juan includes a 37-inch flat-screen TV and a hardwood desk.

They are furnished with a refrigerator and coffee maker. Guacamayo Pool Bar & Grill, located on the property, serves American cuisine for lunch and dinner.

The hotel serves a hot breakfast in the mornings. The San Juan Hampton Inn’s on-site gym has floor-to-ceiling mirrors and a mounted TV.

A business center is located off the lobby and dry cleaning services are available.

The colonial buildings of Old San Juan are 11. 6 km from Hampton Inn & Suites San Juan.
