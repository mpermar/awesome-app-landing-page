Description

This ocean-front hotel in Dorado, Puerto Rico is steps from the beach. Features include a free made-to-order breakfast, outdoor pool, and on-site restaurant.

Guests can use Precor® exercise equipment in the the state-of-the-art fitness centre.

A tour desk, concierge service are available as well. The 2-room suites feature 2 TVs with HBO® and pay-per-view films.

Embassy Suites Dorado Beach Resort suites boast extras like a microwave, refrigerator, and coffee facilities.

All reservations include complimentary drinks daily from 17:30 to 19:30 and made-to-order breakfast.

The Blue Seahorse Pool Bar & Grill serves lunch and beverages by the pool, while the Paradise Cafe serves international and Caribbean cuisine for breakfast and dinner.

E'terie specializes in coffee, sandwiches, wraps, salads among other items to go.

Golf Range is 4. 7 km away. This resort is also 1. 8 km from Bosque de Pterocarpus de Dorado and 29 km from Luis Munoz Marin International Airport.
