Description

A 29-inch satellite TV and mini refrigerator are standard in each air-conditioned room at this beachfront hotel. Hair dryers and a safety deposit box are also provided.

The Royal Palm Cafe is located next to Rincon of the Seas' gardens. It serves Caribbean dishes and features an a la carte menu alongside buffets and live cooking during the weekends. The pool bar offers exotic drinks and champagne.

Rincon of the Seas Grand Caribbean Hotel is 3.7 miles from Domes Beach and the Punta Higueras Lighthouse. Mayaguez and Aguadilla are both a 30-minute drive away.
