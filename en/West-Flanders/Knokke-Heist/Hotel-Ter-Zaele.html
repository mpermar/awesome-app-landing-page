Description

Each of the rooms at Ter Zaele has a TV and a private bathroom with a bathtub. Some of the rooms include a south-facing balcony. A buffet breakfast is served daily.

The hotel also has includes a spa corner with a sauna and solarium. Ter Zaele Hotel includes a cozy library and grounds with a terrace overlooking the countryside.

Bruges, which offers sights including the Beguinage and Grote Markt, is a 20-minute car ride away. The Dutch border is just over a 5 minute drive from the hotel. Free bike storage is available on site.

There is a coastal tram service going from Knokke to other seaside cities such as Blankenberge and Duinberge.
