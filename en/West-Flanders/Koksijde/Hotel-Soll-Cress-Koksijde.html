Description

The rooms at Hotel Soll Cress Koksijde are simply furnished and come equipped with a TV and telephone. They also each have their own private bathroom.

Hotel Soll Cress includes spa facilities including a fitness center, sauna and spa tub. Guests can also enjoy a relaxing massage. All of these spa services are available for an extra charge.

The restaurant features a terrace and serves daily-changing dishes with fresh ingredients from the local market. The hotel also has packed lunches for guests wishing to explore the local area.

Casino Koksijde is a 15-minute walk away. De Panne, featuring Plopsaland, is 3 miles from the hotel and accessible via the tram stop in front of the hotel. Dunkerque, over the French border, is a 30-minute drive away. Free public parking is available on site.

Any type of extra bed is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
