Description

The beautiful hotel offers refined guest rooms which have a warm and friendly atmosphere. In addition to the listed facilities, all rooms have a bathtub and shower and a desk.

From the hotel, there are plenty of possibilities for a trip. Watch the sun set while walking down the beach or go to the center of town. The forest is a nice place to take a walk and enjoy the quiet surroundings.

When weather is nice, take a swim in the outside swimming pool and relax in the charming garden.

There is a coastal tram service providing easy services between De Haan and other seaside cities such as Oostende and Blankenberge.
