Description

At the hotel, all rooms come with a desk. The private bathroom is equipped with free toiletries. Guest rooms feature a closet.

A continental breakfast is available daily at the property.

Guests at Hotel Hof Ter Lepelem will be able to enjoy activities in and around De Haan, like cycling.

Bruges is 7 miles from the accommodation. Ostend-Bruges Airport is 10 miles from the property.
