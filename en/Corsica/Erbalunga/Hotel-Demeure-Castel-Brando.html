Description

The family-owned Castel Brando presents a peaceful backdrop to your Corsican vacation. The rooms all have private bathrooms and are bright and spacious living spaces.

The Castel Brando offers a range of teas, homemade pastries and delicacies. You can enjoy those in the library lounge or, in the summertime, on the shady patio or park planted with 100-year old palm trees and lime trees.

There is no restaurant on-site, but you will find 9 restaurants in Erbalunga.
