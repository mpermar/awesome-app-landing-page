Description

Free Wi-Fi is available in each room with a bathroom. All rooms feature extras long beds, a balcony and ironing facilities.

Breakfast is provided in the form of a buffet each morning. For other meals guests can sample regional food in the restaurant while enjoying views of Ajaccio Bay. Afterwards guests can relax in the hotel bar.

Located 3 miles from Porticcio and its beach, the Radisson Blu Resort & Spa is 12 miles from Ajaccio town center. Services include a laundry, business center and organization of excursions.
