Description

At the hotel, rooms have a terrace. Every room has a private bathroom with free toiletries, while some rooms come with a balcony and others also offer pool views. All guest rooms include a desk.

Continental and Asian breakfast options are available every morning at Relax Garden Boutique Villa Hoi An.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool.

Assembly Hall of Chaozhou Chinese Congregation is a 20-minute walk from Relax Garden Boutique Villa Hoi An. The nearest airport is Da Nang International Airport, 15 miles from the property.

Cam Chau is a great choice for travelers interested in beaches, relaxation and shopping.
