Description

Spacious guestrooms at the resort are decorated with Vietnamese style, and have large windows offering plenty of natural light. Each well-appointed room is fitted with a cable TV and mini-bar for your convenience. Private bathroom is equipped with shower facility, a hairdryer and slippers.

Guests can head to the gym to have a good workout, relax with soothing massages or enjoy a sauna session at the spa. Staff at the 24-hour reception can assist with currency exchange, luggage storage and airport transfer upon request. Other recreation choices include attending a cooking class.

Hoi An Ancient Town is 1,800 feet from Vinh Hung Riverside Resort & Spa, while Kim Bong Carpentry Village is 0.7 mi away. The nearest airport is Danang International Airport, 14 mi away.

Red Tile Restaurant serves a fine selection of local and international dishes, while light snacks are available at Red Brick Bar.

Minh An is a great choice for travelers interested in history, ambiance and old-town exploration.
