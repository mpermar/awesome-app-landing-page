Description

All rooms at the hotel come with a seating area. Silver Fern Beach Retreat provides certain rooms with sea views, and the rooms include a electric tea pot. Guest rooms feature a desk.

The accommodation offers a continental or Full English/Irish breakfast.

Silver Fern Beach Retreat has a terrace. The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental is available at the hotel.

Gili Trawangan is 28 miles from Silver Fern Beach Retreat, while Tanjung is 28 miles from the property.
