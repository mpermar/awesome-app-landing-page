Description

The units come with tiled floors and feature a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, and a private bathroom with bidet and free toiletries. A terrace with pool views is offered in each unit.

The daily breakfast offers continental and Asian options. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the on-site restaurant, which specializes in European cuisine and also offers halal options.

The resort village has an outdoor pool.

A bicycle rental service is available at this property.

Sunset Point is 0.7 miles from Villa Pine Tree, while Gili Trawangan Art Market is a 16-minute walk from the property.
