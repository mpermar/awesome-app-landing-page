Description

All air-conditioned units have a private patio. Amenities include a personal safe, fridge and a flat-screen TV with DVD player. Private bathrooms include a shower and free toiletries.

The staff can assist with laundry requests and airport transportation at additional fees. Meals can be ordered through room service.

The island itself is reachable by a 1.5-hour boat ride from Padang Bai Harbour in Bali. It is a 15-minute horse carriage ride to reach the property from Gili Trawangan Harbour.
