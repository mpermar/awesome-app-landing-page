Description

All guest rooms at the hotel come with a seating area and a flat-screen TV. Each room comes with a private bathroom with a shower, while some have pool views. All rooms will provide guests with a desk and a coffee machine.

An American breakfast is served each morning at the property.

Staff are available to help at the 24-hour front desk.

The nearest airport is Fayetteville Regional (Grannis Field) Airport, 7 miles from Homewood Suites Fayetteville.
