Description

At the resort, the rooms are equipped with a closet and a flat-screen TV. Rumbling Bald Resort provides some units that have a patio, and every room has a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries. All guest rooms at the accommodation are equipped with a seating area.

Rumbling Bald Resort offers a range of wellness facilities including a sauna and a fitness center. A variety of popular activities are available in the area around the property, including cycling and hiking.

Asheville is 22 miles from the resort, while Black Mountain is 13 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Asheville Regional Airport, 20 miles from the property.
