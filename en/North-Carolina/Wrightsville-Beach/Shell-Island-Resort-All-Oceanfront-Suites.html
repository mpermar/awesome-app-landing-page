Description

An oceanfront terrace and a living room with a sofa bed are provided in each suite at Shell Island Resort. Suites feature 2 flat-screen TVs, a Blu Ray player, and toiletries from Bath & Body Works.

Guests can enjoy a workout at the fitness center or use the business center at Shell Island Resort - All Oceanfront Suites. A children’s playground and a sand volleyball court are also available. A gift and convenience shop is on-site for guests to purchase food to cook in their suites.

Shell Island Resort - All Oceanfront Suites offers a full breakfast buffet at North End Bistro.

This resort is within 15 minutes’ drive of Airlie Gardens and shopping at Mayfaire Town Center. Wilmington city center is about 20 minutes’ drive away.
