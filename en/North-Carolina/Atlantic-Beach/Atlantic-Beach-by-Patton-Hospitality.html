Description

Each cozy villa at Peppertree Atlantic Beach by Patton Hospitality includes a kitchen complete with microwave, dishwasher, and oven. Guests can enjoy the view from the private balcony or surf the web with free Wi-Fi.

An on-site gym and children’s playground are part of this Atlantic Beach hotel. Tennis, volleyball, and shuffleboard courts are also on site.

Reel Screamin' Fishing Charters is less than 1 miles from the Atlantic Beach Peppertree. The North Carolina Maritime Museum is less than a 15-minute drive away.

Great family resort packed with "old school" fun

"We had a range of ages in our family group (young teens, twenty-somethings, and us old folks) and there was something for everyone here! We were in a building that was the farthest from the beach access and it was still a quick, easy walk. The kids played tennis and used the indoor pool (which is open until midnight). The "game room" (not an arcade as I had assumed) has a wide array of board games and outdoor equipment to borrow (we used their tennis racquets and poker chips). There are two outdoor pools, a little putt putt area, small basketball court, and more. At the same time, the property was not too large and we were comfortable letting the young teens explore on their own. The rooms were large and spacious and our 2-bedroom unit was well-equipped with everything we needed for putting together simple meals. The staff were very friendly and helpful. Great place for families and some "old school" outdoor fun!"
