Description

A microwave, small refrigerator, and coffee-making facilities are provided in the rooms at Proximity Hotel. A flat-screen cable TV and a sitting area are featured as well as a desk.

Print Works Bistro, which sources its food from local vendors, serves European-influenced cuisine. The restaurant is open daily for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. A wine menu and an outdoor terrace are available for guests to enjoy.

A fitness center, with bikes for guests to explore the area, is open 24 hours a day. Guests can relax with an in-room massage. An airport shuttle and a local transportation service are available to guests free of charge.

Wesley Long Hospital is just under 1 miles from the hotel. University of North Carolina – Greensboro is a 5-minute drive from Hotel Proximity. Piedmont Triad International Airport is an 11-minute drive away.
