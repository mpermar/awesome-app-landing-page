Description

Adorned in warm shades with tasteful furniture, all air-conditioned units have a fan-cooled option, personal safe and seating area with sofa. A private balcony and cable flat-screen TV are offered. Views of the garden can be seen from selected units. Private bathroom is equipped with shower facility and a hairdryer. Extras include free toiletries, slippers and bathrobes.

World-class amenities await at Seahorse Resort & Spa, including water sport facilities and tennis court. Guests can go an day trips or jeep excursions, which can be arranged at the tour desk.

Stunning views of the ocean and sunsets accommpany a wide variety of international dishes and fresh seafood at Hippocampe Restaurant. Seahorse Bistro serves delectable local delights, while light refreshments are available at the Non La Pool Bar.

The 4-star Seahorse Resort & Spa is 6.8 mi from Phan Thiet city and a 4-hour drive from the vibrant Ho Chi Minh city.
