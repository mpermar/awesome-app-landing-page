Description

The tastefully furnished rooms at the Mia Mui Ne are fitted with a sitting area, a TV with cable channels and a minibar. The attached bathrooms include free bath amenities and fluffy bathrobes.

The resort’s 24-hour front desk will be happy to assist with laundry/ironing services and make travel arrangements at the tour desk. Other facilities include spa and massage treatments and airport shuttle services are available for a fee.

Offering in-room dining options, the in-house restaurant will serve local Vietnamese and international dishes. Guests can also enjoy a drink by the beach-side bar.

Mia Mui Ne Resort is located within 7.5 miles to Phan Thiet City and 10.5 miles from the popular Red Sand Dunes.
