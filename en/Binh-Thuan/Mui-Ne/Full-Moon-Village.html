Description

A 2-minute walk from the beach, Full Moon Village is 12 miles from Mui Ne City Centre. Attractions like Lotus Lake and Red Sand Dune are within a 15-minute drive from the resort.

Decorated with Vietnamese fabrics and furnishings, the air-conditioned villas come furnished with a dining area, kitchenette and sitting area. Each comes equipped with a 42-inch flat-screen TV, DVD player and personal safe.

Guests may visit the Beach Club to enjoy water sports activities, or arrange a day trip at the tour desk. The resort also provides car rentals and laundry services.

Beach Club Restaurant serves a selection of local and Western food. A daily buffet breakfast is also available there.
