Description

D'Acosta Hotel Sochagota has nicely decorated rooms with private balconies and lake views. There are minibars, cable TV and stylish furniture.

Bungalows have private swimming pools and fireplaces. All rooms have private bathrooms and laundry services are available.

A full buffet breakfast with tropical fruit and jams can be enjoyed daily. Balcón de las Garzas Restaurant offers regional specialties and international cuisine.

Boyacá Bridge is 30 miles from Sochagota. Car and bike rental is available. Guests can also relax with a ping pong match.

The airport is a 45-minute drive and private parking is free upon reservation.
