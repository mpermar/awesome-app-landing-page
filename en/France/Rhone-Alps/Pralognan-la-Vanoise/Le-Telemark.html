Description

Le Télémark is a 3-star hotel situated just 10 minutes’ walk from the centre of the picturesque alpine village Pralognan-la-Vanoise.

It offers a sauna, a guest lounge with a fireplace and en suite rooms with a balcony.

All rooms at the Telemark are heated and each has a bathroom with a bathtub. Each room also has a TV and a balcony offering views of the heated outdoor pool or the mountains.

The residence’s on-site restaurant serves specialities of the Savoyard region. There is also a bar and guest lounge with a fireplace where guests can enjoy a drink or use the free WiFi.

Le Télémark provides numerous leisure facilities including tennis and badminton courts, a fitness room and a library.

During French school holidays, the hotel organises activities such as art classes, guided hikes and daytime and evening entertainment.
