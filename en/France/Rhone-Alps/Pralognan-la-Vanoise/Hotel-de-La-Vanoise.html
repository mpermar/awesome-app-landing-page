Description

Ideally located in the heart of the village, just 50 metres from the ski lifts and the shops, the Hotel de la Vanoise offers a sauna and guest rooms with a terrace and a flat-screen TV.

Each room has a telephone and a private bathroom. Family rooms come with mezzanine.

Local dishes are served in the hotel’s restaurant. The Hotel de la Vanoise also has a bar, the perfect place to enjoy a hot chocolate or a glass of wine at the end of the day.

This property has a library with a fireplace and guests can play billiards in the games room.
