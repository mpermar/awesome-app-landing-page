Description

Offering ski-to-door access, Hotel Beauregard is a traditional Savoyard chalet-style hotel, located at the foot of the ski slopes, close to the village La Clusaz.

It offers a sauna, a hammam, a hot tub and a heated indoor pool. Free WiFi and a 24-hour reception are available.

At the hotel, all rooms come with a private bathroom fitted with a bath or shower, bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries.

They are equipped with a desk and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Each room has views over the resort of La Clusaz or over the Aravis mountain range.

Selected rooms also feature a balcony. A continental breakfast is served each morning at the property.

The onsite restaurant, Le Symphonie, serves dishes inspired by traditional cuisine.

Afterwards with a piano and fireplace, the bar and large lounge offers an ideal place to relax and enjoy the speciality cocktails.

A pool table and a foosball are also available for guests’ enjoyment. Many shops ans restaurants are located within walking distance.

The nearest airport, Geneva Airport is 70 km away. Onsite parking is available upon reservation and depending on availability.
