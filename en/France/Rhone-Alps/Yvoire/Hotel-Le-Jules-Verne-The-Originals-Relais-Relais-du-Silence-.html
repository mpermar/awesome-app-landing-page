Description

Hôtel Le Jules Verne, The Originals Relais (Relais du Silence) is located in the heart of the medieval town of Yvoire, opposite the marina.

It has a bar and includes free WiFi internet access. The guest rooms are decorated in a contemporary style and include an LCD TV with satellite channels.

They also provide air conditioning and views of the lake. Some rooms are located on the ground floor.

A hammam and hot tub are located at the property and can be used for an additional fee.

The on-site restaurant serves breakfast and gastronomic-style regional cuisine on a shaded terrace overlooking the lake.

You can also enjoy dinner cruises with an advance reservation. Guests can access Geneva in 25-minutes and Lausanne in 40-minutes when taking the boat shuttle and then a train from Nyon.

Hôtel Le Jules Verne is 16 km from the Thonon-les-Bains Train Station. Geneva International Airport is 33 km away.
