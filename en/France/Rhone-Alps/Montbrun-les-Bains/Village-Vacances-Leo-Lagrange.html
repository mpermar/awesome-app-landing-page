Description

Offering a seasonal outdoor pool and mountain and city views, Village Vacances Leo Lagrange is located in Montbrun-les-Bains.

Apt is 45 km away. Free WiFi is provided. All units feature a terrace and a kitchenette equipped with a dishwasher, microwave, fridge and oven, as well as coffee machine.

A microwave, a fridge and stovetop are also offered, as well as a coffee machine.

Free private parking is also available at this holiday park. Village Vacances Leo Lagrange also includes a barbecue.

Sports equipment such as tennis and ping- pong rackets, balls and board games are available at the reception.

Bike hire is available on site. You can play tennis at the property, and the area is popular for cycling and hiking.

Isle-sur-la-Sorgue is 42 km from Leo Lagrange Vacation Village. The nearest airport is Avignon-Provence Airport, 52 km from Leo Lagrange Vacation Village.
