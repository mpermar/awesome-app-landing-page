Description

Situated in a 7000m² garden in Salavas village close to the Gorges de l'Ardèche, Les Cigalous offers self-catering cottages and apartments with an outdoor heated swimming pool and free Wi-Fi.

The large-capacity apartments and cottages are equipped with a kitchenette that has a microwave and a fridge.

Individual bathrooms are provided with a shower and each room has its own balcony with views.

Les Cigalous has a beach volleyball court as well as areas for playing table tennis and petanque.

There is also a play area for young children. Guests can swim in the hotel’s pool or walk 300 metres to the local river.

The village Vallon-Pont-d'Arc is less than 2 km from the property and the Pont d'Arc,a natural stone bridge on the Ardèche river, is 10-minutes away by car.

Aven d'Orgnac, a limestone cave, is 10 km away. Free parking is available on site.
