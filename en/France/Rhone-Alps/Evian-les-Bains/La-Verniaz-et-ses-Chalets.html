Description

Surrounded by private gardens, La Verniaz et ses Chalets is a 17th-century former farmhouse in the heights of Evian with a seasonal private heated pool and tennis court.

It is a 5-minute drive from Lake Geneva and a 10-minute walk from Evian Golf Course.

Each accommodation features a welcome tray with Evian water, tea and a coffee machine.

There is also a minibar, a TV and free WiFi access. Most of the individually styled rooms, apartments and chalets at La Verniaz et ses Chalets have their own private balcony.

La Verniaz also features a bar and a traditional restaurant, which is open every day for both lunch and dinner from April to October.

Meals can be enjoyed on the flower-filled terrace overlooking the garden. Snacks are available from 18:00 until 21:00 daily.

Services include currency exchange, concierge and room service. A free cable car that will take you to the city centre can be found 300 metres from the property.

Bernex and Thollon Ski Resorts are 20 minutes' drive away, and Les Portes du Soleil Ski Resort is 40 minutes' drive away.
