Description

Located in Portes du Mont Blanc ski station and featuring views of Mont Blanc, this hotel is a 5-minute walk from the centre of Combloux village.

The property features table tennis facilities and a boules court. There is also a terrace with tables, chairs and parasols in the summer months and guests have use of free WiFi and free on-site parking.

All the heated rooms at Hôtel Les Granits feature a desk, a wardrobe and a flat-screen TV.

They have wooden floors and a private bathroom with a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Some rooms offer mountain views. A continental or buffet breakfast can be enjoyed every morning in the restaurant or in the comfort of the guest rooms.

Regional dishes are served for dinner in the restaurant, which offers panoramic mountain views.

This hotel is 500 metres from a leisure resort that has an ecological swimming pool.

Skiing is a popular activity in the area and there is a bus stop in front of the property where guests can take the free ski shuttle that runs twice a day.
