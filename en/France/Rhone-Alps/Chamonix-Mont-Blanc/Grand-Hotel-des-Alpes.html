Description

The friendly and attentive staff welcome you in an elegant and familial atmosphere. Enjoy its stylish woodwork, comfortable lounge with a view of the Massif de Mont Blanc or relax at the bar next to the fireplace.

Guest rooms are warm and welcoming and furnished with style. They are all equipped with modern private bathrooms, satellite TV and free Wi-Fi internet access.

This lovely location allows guests the opportunity to appreciate the typical charm of the center of the city and the enchanting surrounding area all year round.

The property is a ski pass vendor and also provides free shuttle services to the Chamonix ski slopes upon request in winter.

Grand Hotel des Alpes is just under half a mile from the AIguille du Midi cable car and guests are able to book guided mountain excursions or ski instructor sessions directly with the hotel.

Chamonix City Centre is a great choice for travelers interested in downhill skiing, outdoor activities and nature.
