Description

Located at the foot of the lifts in Grands Montets, the Hôtel Montana offers modern, chalet-style rooms with free Wi-Fi and balconies facing Mont Blanc.

The hotel is situated just 5 km from Chamonix town centre. Guests are invited to have a drink at the hotel's bar.

After a busy day skiing or hiking you can relax by the fireplace in the hotel's lounge.

During winter, guests can enjoy access to the hotel's hot tub, which has magnificent views of Mont Blanc.

The hotel offers an indoor and heated outdoor swimming pool, massages, sauna and hammam, upon prior reservation and at an extra fee.

Free private parking is provided at the Hôtel Montana, making it easy to explore the Haute-Savoie region by car.
