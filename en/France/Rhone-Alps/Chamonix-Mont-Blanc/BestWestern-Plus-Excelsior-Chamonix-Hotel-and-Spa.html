Description

Offering panoramic views of the Mont Blanc mountain range, Hotel Excelsior is located 2 km from La Flégère ski slopes.

It features an outdoor heated pool during the summer. The Pure Altitude Spa includes a hot tub, sauna and hammam, as well as massages and beauty treatments.

The modern guestrooms feature free WiFi, satellite TV and tea and coffee making facilities.

The private bathroom has a bath or shower and offers some free Pure Altitude Spa toiletries.

Some rooms have views of the Mont Blanc mountain range. Breakfast is served every morning in the restaurant.

Guests can have a drink at the bar or on the terrace by the outdoor pool, facing the mountains.

An on-site ski shop and kids' games room are available. The property is 10 km from Chamonix Train Station.

Charging stations for electric cars are available at this property. A private car park is available upon reservation and for an additional fee.
