Description

Novotel Lyon Gerland Musée des Confluences is located in Lyon, on the banks of Rhône River, 50 metres from the Halle Tony Garnier concert venue, a 5-minute walk from the Musée des Confluences and a 6-minute tram ride from the shopping centre.

It offers an outdoor pool with terrace and free WiFi. Its 17 meeting rooms were renovated in August 2016.

The air-conditioned guestrooms at the Novotel Lyon Gerland have a modern décor and feature a safe, a minibar and a flat-screen TV with free Canal+ and beIN Sport channels.

All rooms have a kettle while the suites also have a Nespresso coffee machine. A courtesy tray and free bottle of water are also included.

The Novotel Café provides a daily buffet breakfast in its dining area, or a continental breakfast in guests’ rooms.

The hotel restaurant serves a range of French dishes and overlooks the pool and garden, and guests can also enjoy a variety of snacks at the bar.

Halle Tony Garnier Tram Station is a 5-minute walk from the hotel providing direct access to Lyon Part-Dieu train station, and Perrache train and metro station.

Old Lyon is 15 minutes' drive from the hotel and the Gerland Stadium is 1. 5 km away.

The hotel is a 2-minute drive to A7 highway. A secured and covered private parking is available on site, at an extra cost.
