Description

Each air-conditioned guest room at Villa Florentine has an elegant decor and is equipped with a flat-screen TV with Canal + and beIn Sports channels and a mini-bar and some rooms have a seating area. All rooms have a modern, private bathroom with a bathtub or shower and some rooms offer views of Old Lyon.

Guests can have breakfast prepared with fresh and local produce in Villa Florentine’s breakfast room or in their room, on the terrace with views of the city or in the bar upon request. “Les Terrasses de Lyon”, Villa Florentine’s gastronomic restaurant, has an outdoor terrace and serves traditional French cuisine and regional specialties.

Additional facilities available include a fitness center with sauna, steam room, a cardio training room and a small beauty treatment area. Guests also have access to a heated swimming pool and hot tub.

For guests with cars, private parking is possible on site for a supplement. The hotel is located 2.8 mi from Lyon Part-Dieu Train Station. Place Bellecour is 0.7 mi away.
