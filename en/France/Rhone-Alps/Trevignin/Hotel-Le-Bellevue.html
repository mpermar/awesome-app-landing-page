Description

Hotel Le Bellevue features a restaurant, seasonal outdoor swimming pool, a bar and garden in Trévignin.

There is a terrace and guests can make use of free WiFi and free private parking.

The units come with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kettle, a shower, a hairdryer and a desk.

At the hotel all rooms are equipped with a wardrobe and a private bathroom. Hotel Le Bellevue offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

You can play darts at the accommodation. Annecy is 36 km from Hotel Le Bellevue, while Aix-les-Bains is 5 km away.
