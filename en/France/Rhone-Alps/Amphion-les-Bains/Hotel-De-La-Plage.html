Description

Set in Amphion les Bains, on the edge of the Lake Geneva, Hôtel De La Plage offers free WiFi.

There is a restaurant on stilts, a garden and a seasonal outdoor swimming pool. All guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV.

Every room is fitted with a safety deposit box and some rooms here will provide you with mountain views.

The units feature a desk. The "Le Radeau d'Alexis" gourmet restaurant features large windows overlooking the Lake Geneva.

The hotel features a games room and lounges, table tennis, a children's playground, a large shady park, mooring pontoon for boats and tennis court.

Geneva is 41 km from the accommodation, while Lausanne is 69 km from the property.
