Description

Residence Hôtelière La Renardiere is located in Samoens, just 800 metres from the cable car that goes to the Grand Massif Ski Region.

It offers 2 swimming pools, a hot tub, hammam and sauna, and 2 massage rooms. A free shuttle bus to the ski slopes running every 20 minutes can be found 50 metres away.

Free WiFi is provided. Accommodation at La Renardiere includes apartments decorated in a typical Savoyard style.

They all have a private balcony, a kitchenette and a flat-screen TV. Additional facilities include a lounge bar with a fireplace and a billiard room.

Breakfast, bakery and catering service is available upon request. Free private parking is provided on site, and the A40 motorway is 20 km from the property.

Local activities also include hiking and cycling during the summer months, or walking the 300 metres to the historical market town.
