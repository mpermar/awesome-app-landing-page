Description

Hôtel Domaine de l'Eau Vive is located in southern Ardèche in the Rhone-Alpes, a 40-minute drive from Caverne du Pont-d'Arc and an hour's drive from the Gorges de l'Ardèche.

It offers private access to a nearby river, as well as a large onsite garden, a children's playground and an outdoor swimming pool.

Each guest room at the L’Eau Vive Residence has an individual decor and satellite TV.

All suites have a private terrace. The Domaine de L’Eau Vive’s restaurant serves regional cuisine prepared with home-grown vegetables whenever possible.

Breakfast, made from fresh organic produce, is served daily. Guests can also enjoy a drink at the bar.

Massages are available onsite, upon reservation and for an extra charge. The villages of Balazuc, Labeaume and Vogüé are all less than 18 km away.

Free WiFi is available in the public areas and the hotel also provides free private parking.
