Description

Casa mARTa: Suites, terraces and panoramic views are situated in Tournon-sur-Rhône, 15 km from Valencia.

The guest house has a barbecue, gardens and sun terrace. Guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Free private parking is available on site. Certain rooms include a seating area where you can relax.

A terrace or balcony and a TV are featured in certain rooms. Casa mARTa: Suites, terrasses et vue panoramique features free WiFi.

The property provides homemade meals prepared onsite, featuring a daily menu with a selection of locally sourced food.

There is a 24-hour front desk, a shared lounge with a TV, babysitting service and gift shop at the property.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including cycling and canoeing.

The guest house also offers bike hire. Romans-sur-Isère is 17 km from Casa mARTa: Suites, terraces and panoramic views, while Saint-Bonnet-le-Froid is 32 km away.

The nearest airport is Grenoble - Isère Airport, 51 km from Casa mARTa: Suites, terraces and panoramic views.
