Description

Offering a heated outdoor pool and a furnished pool house, Ecully Loft is located in Écully.

Free WiFi access is available in the rooms. The property is 9 km from Lyon's city centre.

The accommodations will provide you with a flat-screen TV, and a kitchenette with a microwave and refrigerator.

Some of them feature a terrace or a balcony. At Ecully Loft you will find a garden.

Other facilities offered at the property include grocery deliveries, meeting facilities and a ticket service.

The hotel is 1 km from EM Lyon Business School. Lyon - Saint Exupéry Airport is 35 km away.
