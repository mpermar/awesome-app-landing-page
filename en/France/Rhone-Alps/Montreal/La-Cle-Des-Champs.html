Description

All units are equipped with a oven, microwave, a coffee machine, a toaster and kettle. A terrace with mountain views is offered in each unit.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of French dishes and also offers Vegetarian options.

The vacation home offers an outdoor pool. Entertainment activities for children can be organized in July and August.

Guests can also relax in the garden or on the shared lounge area.

Montélimar is 22 mi from La Cle Des Champs, while Alès is 29 mi from the property. Vallont-Pont-d'Arc is 12 mi away. The nearest airport is Avignon-Provence Airport, 51 mi from the property.
