Description

Offering an outdoor pool, L'Estapade des Tourelons is a B&B located in Saint-Jean-en-Royans.

Free WiFi access is available. The property is a former farmhouse dating from the 17th century, where you can enjoy views of the Vercors Regional Natural Park.

Each room here features air conditioning, a seating area and a wardrobe. Featuring a shower, the private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

In the morning, a continental breakfast is served. A restaurant can be found 800 metres away.

Guests can have access to the dining area, living room, fridge, microwave, 3700 m² garden, outdoor furniture and covered terrace equipped with stone oven and BBQ facilities.

Kitchenware is available. Other facilities offered at the property include ski storage and a children's playground.

An array of activities can be enjoyed in the surroundings, including skiing, cycling and hiking.
