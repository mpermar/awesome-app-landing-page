Description

The ideal place for a business trip or for a vacation, the Best Western St. Etienne Aeroport features numerous meeting rooms for your workshop or seminar sessions of up to 100 people.

The hotel's restaurant, the 1827, serves traditional dishes from the region. The hotel is in the heart of the Loire Forez business park and has free secure private parking.

The hotel is accessible via Saint-Étienne-Châteaucreux Train Station, which is a 15-minute bus ride away. The buses stop next to this Best Western every 30 minutes during weekdays.
