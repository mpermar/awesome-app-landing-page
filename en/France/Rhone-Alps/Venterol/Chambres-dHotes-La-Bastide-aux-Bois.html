Description

At Chambres d'Hôtes La Bastide aux Bois, every room is equipped with a closet and a flat-screen TV. Rooms come complete with a private bathroom equipped with a shower and a hair dryer, while some accommodations at Chambres d'Hôtes La Bastide aux Bois also offer a seating area.

Guests at Bed and Breakfast La Bastide aux Bois can enjoy a continental breakfast.

Bed and Breakfast La Bastide aux Bois has a playground. You can play ping pong at La Bastide aux Bois, and bike rental is available.

Orange is 22 miles from La Bastide aux Bois, while Montélimar is 21 miles away. Caumont Airport is 34 miles from the property.
