Description

Located in Le Crozet, Relais Crozati features ski-to-door access, ski equipment hire and ski pass vending facilities.

Its terrace offers views of the Alps and Geneva, just 18 km away from Lake Geneva.

With free Wi-Fi and views of the mountain and the lake, the en suite rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

There is a hairdryer and free toiletries. Some rooms have a sofa. Breakfast is continental and packed lunches can be prepared upon request.

Guests can also sample typical, traditional mountain dishes based on regional products in the hotel’s à la carte restaurant.

Free secure parking, a tour desk and a children’s playground are available on site together with ski school, at a surcharge.

Ski equipment and passes can be rented nearby. The hotel is located 16 km from Divonne-les-Bains and 15 km from the centre of Geneva.
