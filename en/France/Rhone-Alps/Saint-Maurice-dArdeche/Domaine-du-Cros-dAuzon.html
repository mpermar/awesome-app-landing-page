Description

Located in Vogüé in the Ardèche, Domaine du Cros d'Auzon features an outdoor swimming pool, water slides and an indoor swimming pool.

There is also a children’s club, with entertainment provided during the day and some evenings.

Guestrooms are air-conditioned and include a TV. The private bathrooms offer a shower and toilet.

A breakfast buffet is served every morning and a bakery, supermarket and restaurant can be found within walking distance.

The hotel is situated 10 km from a shopping centre. Local activities include kayaking in the Ardèche Gorges, hiking and playing tennis or mini-golf on site.
