Description

The Best Western Alexander Park provides en suite accommodation 5 km from the centre of Chambery.

It has an outdoor swimming pool, offers free Wi-Fi internet access and is certified as ecologically friendly.

Alexander Park rooms are air-conditioned and soundproofed. Each one is equipped with a flat-screen TV, a safety deposit box and private bathroom.

The rooms are accessible by lift and reception is open 24 hours for guests’ convenience.

Alexander Park’s restaurant serves specialities a la plancha. Guests are invited to relax in the hotel’s bar.

Breakfast is free for children aged 12 years old or younger if their parents take breakfast as well.

The Best Western Alexander Park is easily reached from the E70 and A43 motorways.
