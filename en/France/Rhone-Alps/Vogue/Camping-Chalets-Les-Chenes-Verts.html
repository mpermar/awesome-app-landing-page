Description

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool, a children's playground and a paddling pool, Camping les Chênes Verts is located in Vogüé.

WiFi access is available free of charge. The chalets offer a terrace, a seating area and a full kitchen with a refrigerator and kitchenware.

The private bathroom offers a bath or shower and toilet. Camping les Chênes Verts features barbecue facilities, a terrace and table tennis.

In July and August, guests can use the on site shared lounge and a snack bar. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling, fishing and hiking.
