Description

Located in Saint-Jean-de-Sixt in the Haute-Savoie region, AEC Vacances - Forgeassoud is a holiday park with panoramic views of the Aravis mountains.

Free Wi-Fi and free private parking are provided. The rooms are equipped with a desk, a wardrobe and a private bathroom with shower, and free toiletries.

They all have a flat-screen TV. Every morning, guests can enjoy a breakfast buffet.

Packed lunches can be prepared on site upon request or guests can have lunch in restaurants on the ski slopes.

They can go alpine skiing, cross-country skiing or hiking in the area. There are also paragliding, via ferrata and canoeing facilities.

La Clusaz Ski Resort is just 4 km away and guests can drive 17 km to Annecy and its lake.
