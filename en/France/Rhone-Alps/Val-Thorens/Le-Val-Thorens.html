Description

Offering ski-to-door access, Hotel Le Val Thorens is located at the heart of the 3 Vallées ski resort.

It boasts a heated indoor pool, a sauna and a steam room, as well as 2 restaurants.

Decorated in a contemporary style with wooden furniture, the guest rooms feature a flat-screen TV, free WiFi access and a balcony with views.

The en suite bathrooms include a hairdryer. A buffet breakfast is prepared every morning at this hotel.

In the evening, you can enjoy a drink in the bar lounge. A ski shop is available at the property and guests can rent ski equipment on site.
