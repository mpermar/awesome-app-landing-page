Description

Stay in one of the hotel's 36 spacious rooms, all accessible with elevator and equipped with all the modern amenities you may need during your stay: free wireless internet, private bathroom, etc.

Get off to a good start and enjoy a copious and healthy buffet before taking a refreshing dip in the hotel's outdoor pool. Located close to the ski slopes and the ski school, Hôtel Le Christania is the perfect place for your next winter vacation.
