Description

Hotel Residence Loisirs Les Cotes is located 500 meters from the village center of Morzine Avoriaz and 700 meters from Morzine Ski Lift, giving access to the Portes Du Soleil Ski Area.

It has an indoor swimming pool, a sauna and hammam, and a fitness centre with a tennis court.

Each room at the Les Côtes is equipped with a private bathroom, a work desk and a satellite TV, as well as a balcony.

Free WiFi access is available throughout the hotel. Breakfast is served daily in the guest room or in the restaurant.

Guests can enjoy regional cuisine at Les Côtes Restaurant and a drink at the bar.

Guests of Les Côtes have access to a hot tub. In the summer, there is a garden with a children’s playground and a boules field.

The Super-Morzine cable car is just 500 metres away and offers access to the Avoriaz ski resort.
