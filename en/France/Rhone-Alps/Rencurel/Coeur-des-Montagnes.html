Description

In the Vercors Natural Regional Park, Coeur des Montagnes offers a terrace with sun loungers, a lounge with fireplace, ski-to-door access and ski storage.

Free private parking is provided. The property offers guest rooms with a balcony with mountain views and a bathroom with shower.

Guests can also stay in a 4-bed dormitory room with mountain views and access to a shared bathroom and toilet.

Every morning, a continental breakfast is served with homemade bread, jam and honey.

The property also has a restaurant and a snack-bar. Local products can be bought in the on-site shop.

Guests can buy ski pass and hire ski equipment nearby. Entertainment is organised during French school holidays including a visit from Santa Claus and snowshoeing excursions.

Children’s games are also available. Via ferrata facilities can be found 15 km away and canyoning facilities are 2 km away.

There is a public swimming pool 10 km away. Guests can go dog sledding, cross-country skiing or snowshoeing in winter.

Grenoble Train Station is 35 km away, while access to A49 Motorway is possible 15 km away.
