Description

Featuring a private bathroom, some rooms at the hotel also offer a balcony.

A continental breakfast is served at the property.

At Hotel du Golf guests are welcome to use a hot tub and a sauna. Skiing is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.

Rambins 1 is 900 feet from Hotel du Golf, while Correncon en Vercors Ski School is a 14-minute walk away. The nearest airport is Saint Geoirs Airport, 25 miles from the hotel.
