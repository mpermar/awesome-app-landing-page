Description

Set in grounds of 3,500m2, the Villa offers rooms and suites with extensive views of the surrounding valley. The uniquely decorated rooms all have a private bathroom and all have free WiFi access.

Villa Aimée provides a daily breakfast of homemade and local produce. In fine weather it is served in the garden underneath the century-old chestnut tree overlooking Vals-les-Bains.

Guests have free use of the outdoor swimming pool, table tennis and badminton facilities. The Villa can also arrange beauty treatments and massages at the nearby thermal spa.

Montelimar TGV high-speed train station is 23 mi away and free on-site parking is available for guests exploring the Ardeche region by car.
