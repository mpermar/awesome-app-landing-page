Description

Sport' Aparthotel de Milan offers a fitness centre, as well as accommodation with a kitchen in the centre of Le Bourg-d’Oisans.

Complimentary WiFi is featured and free, gated parking is available on site. All units at the aparthotel are equipped with an oven, a coffee machine, a microwave and kettle.

A balcony with mountain views is offered in some units. A common washing machine can be used for free.

In the wellness area you will find a sauna, sports facilities and onsite sports massages.

The property also has secure bike storage and a well-equipped terrace with chairs, tables, picnic area and a BBQ beside the river.

There is also an outside sauna. Les Deux Alpes is 18 km from Sport' Aparthotel de Milan, while Grenoble is 51 km away.

Grenoble - Isère Airport is 91 km from the property, and the property offers a free shuttle service.
