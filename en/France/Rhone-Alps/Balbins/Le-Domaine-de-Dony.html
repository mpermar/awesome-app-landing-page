Description

At the guesthouse, each room includes a desk and a flat-screen TV. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a shower, while some accommodations at Le Domaine de Dony also offer a balcony. The rooms feature a closet.

A continental breakfast is served at the property.

Guests can take part in various activities in the surrounding areas, including skiing, cycling and hiking.

Grenoble is 29 miles from the accommodation, while Villard-de-Lans is 28 miles away. The nearest airport is Saint Geoirs Airport, 6 miles from Le Domaine de Dony.
