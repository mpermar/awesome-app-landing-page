Description

Featuring free WiFi, a children's playground and a terrace, Les Galapons is a pet-friendly B&B in Trept, 40 km from Lyon.

Free private parking is available on site. The rooms have a flat-screen TV. Certain rooms include a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

You will find a kettle in the room. Every room includes a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Homemade evening meals can be enjoyed if reserved 48 hours in advance. You will find luggage storage space at the property.

You can play table tennis at this bed and breakfast, and the area is popular for fishing and hiking.

Aix-les-Bains is 67 km from Les Galapons, while Chambéry is 62 km away. Lyon - Saint Exupery Airport is 32 km from the property.
