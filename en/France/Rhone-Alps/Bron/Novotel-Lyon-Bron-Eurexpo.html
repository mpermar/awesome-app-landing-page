Description

Novotel Lyon Bron Eurexpo is located 10 km from Lyon city centre and 1. 7 km the A43 motorway.

Eurexpo exhibition centre is 3 km from the property while Parc Olympique Lyonnais Stadium is a 15-minute drive away.

The hotel features an outdoor pool, a terrace with a bar, and a fitness room. Free WiFi is available throughout.

The air-conditioned rooms at Bron Eurexpo feature a minibar and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

The private bathrooms have a bath or shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries. Breakfast is served every morning at the hotel.

The Novotel Café serves contemporary French cuisine throughout the day, and meals can be enjoyed in the dining area or on the terrace by the swimming pool, weather permitting.

This hotel is 20 minutes' drive from Saint-Exupéry Airport and 10 km from Lyon Part-Dieu Train Station.
