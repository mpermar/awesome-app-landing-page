Description

ibis Valence Sud is a hotel located 3 km from the centre of Valence, close to the Drôme Provençale, Ardeche and Rhone regions.

Its outdoor swimming pool is surrounded by a furnished sun deck. Free WiFi is provided.

All of the rooms are air conditioned and have a private bathroom with a shower. They also have flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

A buffet breakfast composed of sweet and savoury dishes such as eggs, fruit salad, yogurts and juices is served every day.

Pastries baked on site and fresh French Madeleine cakes are also on offer, as well as a hot beverage and a piece of fruit to take away.

Outside of regular breakfast hours guests can also enjoy a lighter option, available from 04:00.

Its restaurant has a terrace and serves traditional cuisine and the bar offers 24-hour snack service.

This hotel is accessible from the A7 motorway and is 16 km from the Valence TGV Train Station.

ibis Valence Sud is 6 km from the Valence Exhibition Centre. Free private parking is available on site.
