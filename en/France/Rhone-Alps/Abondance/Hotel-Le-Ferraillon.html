Description

With ski storage and a children’s playground, Hotel Le Ferraillon is 3 km from the centre of Abondance and 8 km from Chatel ski slopes.

It offers free Wi-Fi in public areas and free access to the indoor swimming pool and hot tub.

All the heated rooms feature mountain views, wooden floors and a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

The private bathroom includes free toiletries and a bath or shower. A buffet breakfast can be enjoyed every morning at Hotel Le Ferraillon.

Specialities from the Savoy region are served in the hotel’s restaurant. Packed lunches can also be requested.

The hotel is Eco-friendly, and has 16 solar panels to heat the hotel and 8 solar panels to heat the swimming pool.

Guests can go mountain biking, skiing and paragliding in the area. There is a fitness room at the property and Évian-les-Bains is 37 km away.
