Description

Hotel Valery is located in Montélier in a wooden building. It offers en suite rooms with free Wi-Fi access.

Hotel Valery’s rooms are air-conditioned and equipped with a TV and private bathroom with shower.

Each room has a balcony with views of the Vercors and fields. Breakfast is served daily in the breakfast room.

Valery Hotel is at the foot of the Vercors. It is a 5-minute drive from Valence Chabeuil Airport and the centre of Valence can be reached in 10 minutes by car.
