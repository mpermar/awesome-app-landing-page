Description

Les Acacias features accommodation with a garden and free WiFi. All units include a fully equipped kitchen, allowing guests to prepare their own meals.

There is a seating and/or dining area in some units. A buffet breakfast can be enjoyed at the property.

The restaurant at the campsite serves French cuisine. Les Acacias offers a children's playground.

After a day of cycling or fishing, guests can relax in the shared lounge area. Villard-de-Lans is 81 km from the accommodation, while Valence is 52 km away.
