Description

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool and a tennis court, Le Saint Marc is located in Mollans-sur-Ouvèze, on the foothills of Mont Ventoux.

Free Wi-Fi access is available. All rooms offer a flat-screen TV and a complete, private bathroom.

Some rooms also feature air conditioning, a kitchenette or a view. Le Saint Marc has an à la carte restaurant and a bar.

Breakfast and packed lunches are available at an extra charge. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling and hiking.

The property is 13. 5 km from Vaison-la-Romaine, 20 km from Nyons and offers free on-site parking.
