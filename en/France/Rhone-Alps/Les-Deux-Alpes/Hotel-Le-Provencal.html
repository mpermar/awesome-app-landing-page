Description

Set in Les Deux Alpes, 60 metres from Super Venosc Ski Lift, Hotel Le Provencal features a sun terrace and ski-to-door access.

Free WiFi is offered throughout the property. All rooms feature a private bathroom and a balcony with mountain views.

Breakfast is available daily at a supplement, while guests can also enjoy the on-site restaurant.

A range of activities are offered in the area, such as skiing, cycling, hiking, canoeing and paragliding.

Diable Ski Lift is 350 metres from Le Provencal, while Grand Viking Ski Lift is 290 metres away.
