Description

Set in the quiet countryside of the Rhône-Alpes, Moulin de la Brevette is surrounded by 4 hectares of parkland including hundred-year-old chestnut trees.

It is near a water mill from the 18th century, a pond and a wooden bridge. The sunny, calm and large rooms are tastefully decorated and are equipped with shower, toilets, TV and phone.

They can accommodate up to 5 people. A buffet breakfast is available for an extra fee.

Guests can also request an evening catering service upon prior reservation. For a sporting, relaxed or tourist weekend, the hotel is very well situated in the middle of the country, between Bourgogne and Bresse.

You can relax on the terrace and there is a children's playground in the garden. Moulin de la Brevette is located a 10-minute drive from the motorway A6 and a free parking is available nearby.
