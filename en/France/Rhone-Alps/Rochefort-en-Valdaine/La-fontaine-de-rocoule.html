Description

Offering a sun terrace, mountain views and a heated indoor pool, La fontaine de rocoule is located in Rochefort-en-Valdaine in the Rhône-Alps Region, 42 km from Orange.

Free WiFi is offered throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Each room has a flat-screen TV. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hair dryer.

Guests have free access to the hot tub. You will find a shared lounge at the property.

Homemade meals are available upon prior request. Valence is 47 km from La fontaine de rocoule, while Montélimar is 10 km from the property.
