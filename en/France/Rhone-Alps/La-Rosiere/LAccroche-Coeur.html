Description

Situated on a hillside in the Tarentaise Valley, this hotel is just 23 km from the Bourg Saint-Maurice Train Station.

It offers a sauna, a restaurant and heated, en suite rooms with free Wi-Fi access.

The guest rooms each have a balcony and light wooden paneling. All rooms are accessible by lift and have a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

A buffet breakfast featuring homemade pastries is available daily. Après ski snacks can be arranged and guests can sample dishes from the Savoy region in the evenings.
