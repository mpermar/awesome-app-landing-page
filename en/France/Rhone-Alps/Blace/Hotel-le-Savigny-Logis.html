Description

The residence is located in a former winery and is composed of several stone buildings. The spacious rooms are traditionally furnished and include private bathrooms with TV.

Hotel Le Savigny is 6 miles from the A6/E15 highway and free private parking is available arriving by car. The Lyon-Saint Exupery Airport is 29 miles from the hotel.
