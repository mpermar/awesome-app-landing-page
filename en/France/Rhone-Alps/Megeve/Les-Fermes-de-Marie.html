Description

Featuring a fitness centre and indoor pool, Les Fermes de Marie is just a 10-minute walk from the centre of Megève.

Free WiFi is available. A free shuttle service is available to Megève and the ski lifts.

Rooms at Les Fermes de Marie are decorated in typical Haute-Savoie style and each one is equipped with satellite TV.

The Pure Altitude Spa offers rejuvenating and relaxing treatments. Guests can enjoy a drink by the fireplace while deciding where to dine.

The property can also organise taxis for you. The Fermes de Marie’s Traditional Restaurant offers tasty seasonal dishes and in the winter season the Alpin Restaurant serves regional cheese specialities and rotisserie dishes.
