Description

Located in the heart of the village, hotel M de Megève welcomes guests in a cosy atmosphere just 100 metres from the Chamois ski lifts.

Guests can enjoy the on-site spa, bar and restaurant. Rooms and suites at M de Megève are decorated in a style combining local and contemporary spirit.

Each are equipped with a coffee maker, electric kettle and minibar. Free Wi-Fi access is provided throughout the property.

The Cinq Mondes spa invites guests to relax and offers massages, sports wellness and pampering treatments.

The bar, situated in front of a traditional fireplace, welcomes guests throughout the day.

For transportation, Sallanches TGV Station is 12 km from Megève. Geneva International Airport is 83 km away.

Transfer can be organized by the hotel with advance reservation and a private parking garage is available on site.
