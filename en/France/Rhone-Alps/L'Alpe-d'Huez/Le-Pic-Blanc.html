Description

All rooms at Le Pic Blanc are equipped with a satellite TV and a desk. The private bathroom is fitted with a bath or shower.

A buffet breakfast is served daily. Le Pic Blanc offers a bar, L’Herpie, and guests may enjoy local, traditional cuisine at the property’s restaurant, Le Rif Briant. Other facilities include a terrace and a lounge bar with a fireplace.

Activities in the local area include sledging and cross-country skiing in winter, and hiking and cycling in summer. Grenoble Airport is 1.5 hours’ drive from Le Pic Blanc, while Grenoble Train Station is 1 hour away by car. Charging stations for electric cars are available at this property.
