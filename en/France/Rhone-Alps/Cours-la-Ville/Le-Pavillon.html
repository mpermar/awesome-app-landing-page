Description

Featuring a Spa and BBQ, Hotel Restaurant Le Pavillon is set in a pine forest located in Le Col du Pavillion.

It offers rooms with a private balcony or terrace and free Wi-Fi internet access.

A fitness centre is available. Each guest room at Le Pavillon has a private entrance and are also equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

All the bathrooms were renovated in 2017 and include a hairdryer. Towels are provided.

Breakfast is served daily in the guest rooms or on the garden terrace. The Pavillon’s restaurant offers regional cuisine prepared with seasonal and local produce.

There is a children's playground and relaxation area on site. Guests can play petanque and rent quad bikes on site.

Activities can be enjoyed nearby, including hiking and fishing. Hotel Le Pavillon provides free private parking.
