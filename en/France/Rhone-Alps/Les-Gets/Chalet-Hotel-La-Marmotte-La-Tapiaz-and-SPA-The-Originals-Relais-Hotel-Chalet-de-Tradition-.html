Description

Chalet-Hôtel La Marmotte, La Tapiaz & SPA, The Originals Relais (Hotel-Chalet de Tradition) is located in the village of Les Gets, opposite the ski slopes and ski lifts of the Portes du Soleil.

It features a spa with an heated indoor swimming pool, a hot tub and a sauna. Each room at Chalet-Hôtel La Marmotte is equipped with en-suite facilities, a TV and free Wi-Fi internet.

Rooms also have a private balcony. The Tapiaz rooms are located in the same building as the spa and pool, accessed via an underground passage directly from the main hotel.

During the ski season, every room has access to a secured ski storage locker. You will also have free access to the hydro-relaxation area, which features a sauna, hammam, a sensory shower and massage rooms.

This Hôtels-Chalets de Tradition resort is 10 km from Morzine-Avoriaz. Les Gets Golf Club is 3 km away.
