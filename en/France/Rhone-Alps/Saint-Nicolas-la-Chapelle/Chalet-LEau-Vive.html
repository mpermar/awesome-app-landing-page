Description

Set in Val d'Arly, this hotel offers a free parking garage in the village of Saint-Nicolas-La-Chapelle.

It features a private garden with a shaded terrace offering mountain views. Free Wi-Fi access is provided in the guest rooms, which have a chalet-style décor.

The en suite bathrooms include toiletries. The restaurant at Chalet Hotel l'Eau Vive features dishes made with homemade products only.

It offers a daily menu and some regional specialities are available upon reservation.

A buffet breakfast is prepared every morning and you can enjoy a drink in the bar.

There is also a shared living room with books. Espace Diamant ski resort is 10 km away and there is a free shuttle service to Flumet ski resort, during the winter.
