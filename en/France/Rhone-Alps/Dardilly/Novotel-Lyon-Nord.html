Description

Located in the centre of the Dardilly business district, a 15-minute drive from central Lyon and a 20-minute drive from Beaujolais, Novotel Lyon Nord offers comfortable and well-equipped rooms.

Novotel Lyon Nord features its own garden and an outdoor swimming pool. Free private parking is provided at the Novotel Lyon Nord, and pets are allowed upon request for an additional charge.

The friendly and attentive staff at the Novotel Lyon Nord will be happy to help you organise your stay 24 hours a day.

The hotel is located at the exit of A6 and A89 motorways. Up to 2 children (15 years and under) stay free when sharing with parents.
