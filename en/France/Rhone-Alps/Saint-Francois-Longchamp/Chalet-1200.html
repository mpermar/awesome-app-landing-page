Description

Located in Saint-François-Longchamp, Chalet 1200 is a hotel set in a typical chalet-style building.

You can relax with a drink from the on-site bar and dine at the restaurant. A free shuttle to Saint-François-Longchamp ski lift is provided.

Offering a mountain view, the rooms at Chalet 1200 have wooden furniture and a flat-screen TV.

Each room features a private bathroom with a bathtub or a shower. Breakfast is available each morning in the communal lounge, with an additional cost.

It includes pastries, bread, butter, jams, hot drinks and fruit juices. The hotel’s restaurant serves traditional dishes for dinner and different options are available.

Several activities are available in the area, such as skiing, hiking, cycling and tree climbing.

Ski passes can be bought at the hotel, which also features a sun terrace, a garden and free Wi-Fi access.
