Description

The Originals City, Hôtel Hélios, Roanne Nord (Inter-Hotel) is 4 km from Roanne centre, train station, and events hall.

La Table des Tuilereries restaurant proposes local cuisine. There is also a swimming pool with sun terrace on site.

The air-conditioned rooms come with an Italians shower, hairdryer, flat-screen TV and free WiFi.

You can start your day with buffet breakfast with fruit, pastries, cheese, sausage and deli meats.

During the day, relax with a drink at the lounge or terrace, or enjoy local cuisine at the restaurant.

Hôtel Hélios is on RN7 Paris-Lyon route and is near the SNCF train station and a shopping centre.
