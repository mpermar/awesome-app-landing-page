Description

Located in Roussillon, L'Oree du Château is surrounded by parkland with century-old trees and bamboo plants.

It offers individually-decorated rooms with free Wi-Fi and views of the park or shaded terrace.

Air-conditioned guest rooms at L’Oree du Chateau are equipped with an 80-cm flat-screen TV and free Wi-Fi.

They each have a private bathroom and a work desk. The Chateau’s restaurant serves regional cuisine, prepared with seasonal products.

Guests can enjoy their meal on the terrace, which opens onto the park and the children’s playground.

L’Oree du Château provides a 24-hour reception and free private parking. It is close to local hiking trails, and a 30-minute drive south of Lyon.
