Description

Each guest room at the Château de Mazan is equipped with air conditioning and satellite TV. Rooms also have a spacious private bathroom.

Château de Mazan features a gastronomic restaurant, L'Ingénue, which serves traditional cuisine. There is also a bar and a 24-hour reception with free Wi-Fi access.

Free parking is available on site and the Château de Mazan is located 19 mi east of Avignon, in the south of France. It is an ideal base for exploring the region.
