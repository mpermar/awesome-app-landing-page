Description

With modern-style decor, all the air-conditioned rooms are serviced by a elevator and feature a flat-screen TV and a telephone. The private bathrooms are complete with a bath, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning at the hotel, free for children under 12 years of age. L’Angelus, the hotel’s restaurant, serves traditional cuisine.

Guests can enjoy the indoor spa which includes a swimming pool, a hot tub, a sauna, a hammam, a fitness room, a relaxation area and a herbal tea room. The property includes a 24-hour front desk and private car park. A shuttle service to and from Antibes Train Station is also available at an extra cost.

Guests on their Honey Moon can enjoy a VIP welcome. Please precise upon reservation.
