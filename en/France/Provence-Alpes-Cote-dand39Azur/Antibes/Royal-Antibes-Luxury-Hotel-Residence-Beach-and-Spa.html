Description

This property is composed of suites, apartments and other rooms with a city view. An LCD TV and a mini-bar feature in every type of accommodations at Royal Antibes. All accommodations is contemporary in style and some suites have magnificent sea views.

The Italian-inspired Café Royal serves meals throughout the day. During the high season, Royal Beach features a terrace with sea views and offers Mediterranean cuisine.

During the summer, a private beach is available for guests' use with an extra cost. The 24-hour front desk can advise guests on Cannes, just a 20-minute drive down the coast. Free Wi-Fi access is available throughout.
