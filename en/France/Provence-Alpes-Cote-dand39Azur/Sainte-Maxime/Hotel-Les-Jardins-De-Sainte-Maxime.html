Description

All air-conditioned rooms are decorated in the typical south of France style. Most of the rooms have a balcony or patio and provide an exceptional view of the surrounding area.

The restaurant at Jardins de Sainte-Maxime serves a buffet breakfast and a range of cuisines from Mediterranean to a large choice of regional specialties. Dinner can be served in the dining room or on the terrace.

Guests can enjoy a drink on the terrace of the hotel’s bar. There is a games area with billiards, Foosball and ping-pong and free Wi-Fi is available on site.

The hotel is a 15 minute walk from the center of the city of Sainte-Maxime, and just 1,000 feet from the beach.
