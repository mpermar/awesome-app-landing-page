Description

The air-conditioned rooms at Domaine du Calidianus include private bathrooms, satellite TV and a minibar. They overlook the swimming pool or the garden.

Breakfast is served daily in the dining area and drinks from the bar can be enjoyed on the grounds. The hotel’s restaurant serves regional and traditional French cuisine.
