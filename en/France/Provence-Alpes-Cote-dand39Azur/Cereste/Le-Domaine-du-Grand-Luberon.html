Description

At the resort, every room includes a balcony. All rooms come with a private bathroom with a shower. All guest rooms have a desk.

A buffet breakfast is served each morning at the property.

Le Domaine du Grand Luberon has an outdoor pool. You can play ping pong at this 3-star resort, and the area is popular for cycling.

Aix-en-Provence is 24 miles from the accommodation, while Apt is 9 miles away. The nearest airport is Provence Airport, 34 miles from the property.
