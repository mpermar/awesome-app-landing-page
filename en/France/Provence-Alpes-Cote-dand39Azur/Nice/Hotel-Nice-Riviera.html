Description

Guest rooms at Nice Riviera Hotel & Spa feature environmentally friendly, bamboo parquet flooring and colorful cushions and bed throws. Each room has an private bathroom with a bathrobe, slippers and toiletries.

A buffet breakfast is served daily in the hotel’s luminous breakfast room or in the comfort of guest rooms. The hotel's lounge bar, Riviera Café invites guests to use the free Wi-Fi or enjoy a drink by the fireplace.

Guests have free access to the hotel's on-site wellness center. Nice Riviera Hotel & Spa is located 0.6 mi from Nice Train Station and a 15-minute drive from Nice Airport. Place Massena and Vieux Nice are 1,650 feet away.
