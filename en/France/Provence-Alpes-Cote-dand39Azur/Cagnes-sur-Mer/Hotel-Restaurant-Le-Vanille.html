Description

Guest rooms at Hôtel Restaurant le Vanillé are comfortable and modern. They are equipped with private bathrooms, a TV and a fan. The sea view room includes a private balcony.

Hôtel Restaurant le Vanillé also provides daily continental breakfast, free private on-site parking.

Hôtel Restaurant le Vanillé is well-placed to explore the French Riviera. It is 10 minutes from Nice Airport, 20 minutes from Monaco and 25 minutes from the famous Croisette in Cannes.
