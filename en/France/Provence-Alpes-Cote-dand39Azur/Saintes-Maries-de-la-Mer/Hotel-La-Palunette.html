Description

Each guest room at the Palunette also has a TV and a private bathroom. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel.

Guests can borrow a book from the hotel library and relax with a beer or a cocktail in the bar.

Montpellier is 34 miles from the hotel and Arles can be reached in 30 minutes by car.
