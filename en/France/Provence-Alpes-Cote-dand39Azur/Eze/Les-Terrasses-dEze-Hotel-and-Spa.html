Description

Just beyond the infinity pool are the majestic hills rolling in to the sea that are enjoyed from the terrace. Also, the spacious guestrooms are equipped with modern private bathrooms and free Wi-Fi internet access.

Enjoy regional cuisine in the hotels restaurant, Le Tillac, and take a relaxing moment with a drink or an aperitif in the cozy lounge bar.

The entire team at Les Terrasses d'Eze warmly welcomes you to this fairytale setting and will do what it takes to make your stay as pleasant as possible.
