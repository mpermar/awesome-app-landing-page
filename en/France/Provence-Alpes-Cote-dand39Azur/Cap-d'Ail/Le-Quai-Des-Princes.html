Description

The hotel features a seasonal outdoor pool with panoramic sea views and a fitness center. It offers a 24-hour reception.

It offers 32 air-conditioned and soundproofed suites/studios/apartments with a satellite TV and yacht inspired decor. Each accommodations has a fully equipped kitchenette, a private balcony with views of the port or the sea and free WiFi.

Guests are invited to relax with a drink on the terrace or in the hotel’s bar. A buffet breakfast is served daily in the chic dining area.

The hotel is 1.9 mi from Monte Carlo City Center and a 30-minute drive from Nice and Nice-Cote d’Azur Airport.
