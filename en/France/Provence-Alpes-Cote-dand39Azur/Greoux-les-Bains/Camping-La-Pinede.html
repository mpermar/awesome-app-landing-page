Description

All units comprise a seating area with a sofa, a dining area, and a fully equipped kitchenette with various cooking facilities, including a microwave, a fridge and a stovetop.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the on-site restaurant, which serves Mediterranean cuisine.

The campground has a playground.

Guests can also relax in the shared lounge area.

Aix-en-Provence is 27 miles from Camping La Pinede, while Apt is 26 miles away. Provence Airport is 39 miles from the property, and the property offers a free shuttle service.
