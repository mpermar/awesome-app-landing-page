Description

Each unit has with a terrace, a satellite flat-screen TV, a seating area, a well-fitted kitchen and a private bathroom with shower and a hair dryer. A balcony with pool views is offered in every unit.

A continental breakfast is served daily at the property.

The condo hotel has a seasonal outdoor pool.

Guests can relax in the garden at the property.

Nice is 13 miles from Eden Azur, while Cannes is 3.4 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Nice Côte d'Azur Airport, 9 miles from the property.
