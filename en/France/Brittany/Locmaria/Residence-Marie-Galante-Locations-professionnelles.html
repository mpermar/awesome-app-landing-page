Description

Offering a garden, Résidence Marie-Galante offers pet-friendly accommodation in Locmaria.

Vannes is 47 km from the property. Free WiFi is provided. Featuring a terrace, the accommodation is fitted with a seating and dining area.

There is also a kitchen, fitted with a dishwasher. A microwave, a toaster and refrigerator are also featured, as well as a coffee machine and a kettle.

Free private parking is also available at this holiday park. Résidence Marie-Galante also includes a seasonal outdoor pool.

Bike hire and car hire are available at the property and the area is popular for cycling.
