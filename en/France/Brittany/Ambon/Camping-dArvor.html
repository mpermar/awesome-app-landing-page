Description

Located at the edge of Morbihan Gulf and a 5-minute drive from the beach, Camping d’Arvor features 2 swimming pools.

A children’s playground and billiards are also provided. The accommodations at Camping d’Arvor have a fully equipped kitchenette and a private bathroom.

Some also feature a private and furnished terrace and WiFi access is available at surcharge.

The equipped kitchenette has a stove, a refrigerator and a dining area. You will enjoy the restaurant room of the camp park decorated in a nautical style.

A kids' club is available in July and August. The camp park is situated 25 km from the city of Vannes and 70 km from Saint-Pierre de Quiberon.
