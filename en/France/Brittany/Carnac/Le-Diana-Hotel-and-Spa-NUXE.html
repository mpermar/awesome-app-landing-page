Description

Located on the south coast of Brittany, the 4-star H么tel Le Diana is opposite Carnac beach.

Guests have free access to the heated outdoor swimming pool, sauna and hammam. All rooms at Le Diana have a balcony, satellite TV, free WiFi and a hairdryer.

Some rooms have panoramic views of the sea. A breakfast buffet is served in the restaurant or on the outdoor terrace.

The hotels gastronomic Le Diana restaurant is open for dinner on Friday and Saturday and for lunch on Sunday and offers panoramic views of the bay of Quiberon.

Guests are invited to enjoy a drink at the hotel's bar, which has an outdoor terrace and a large selection of champagne.

A private boat ride, a picnic and a visit to the nearby islands can be enjoyed at an extra cost.
