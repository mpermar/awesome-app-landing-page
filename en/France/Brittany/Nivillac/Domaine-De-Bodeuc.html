Description

All of the guest rooms are serviced by an elevator and some have jet showers.

At the Domaine de Bodeuc fine local Bretonne specialties are served. A varied wine list is also available. A continental breakfast is served daily.

The Domaine de Bodeuc is just 20 minutes from the beaches and a golf course.
