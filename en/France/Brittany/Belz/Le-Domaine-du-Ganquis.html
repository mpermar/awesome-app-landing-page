Description

Set on a 4-hectare park, this property is 1. 6 km from the sea and a 12-minute walk from Belz.

It offers a heated outdoor swimming pool, a sauna and a hot tub. Free WiFi is available in each unit.

All the heated cottages at Le Domaine du Ganquis offer tiled floors and a seating area and a terrace.

They also include a private bathroom with a bath or shower. Guests can prepare their own meals at Le Domaine du Ganquis.

Each cottage has a kitchen equipped with an oven and a refrigerator. A microwave-oven, toaster, kettle and a coffee machine are also provided.

Guests can play table tennis at the property and they have access to laundry facilities, with a washing machine and tumble dryer.
