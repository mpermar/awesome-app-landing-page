Description

The vacation home features 3 bedrooms, a kitchenette with a dishwasher, and a bathroom. A flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a Blu-ray player are offered.

Cycling and hiking can be enjoyed nearby.

Saint Malo is 26 miles from the vacation home. The nearest airport is Dinard Bretagne Airport, 27 miles from Gite Du Marais.
