Description

Offering an outdoor swimming pool and an indoor swimming pool, as well as a fitness room, sauna, hammam and hot tube, Résidence Néméa Le Domaine de la Baie offers holiday houses just 1 km from the beach.

Free Wi-Fi is available in the public areas. Each house has a fully equipped kitchen with a dining area, a bathroom with shower, a seating area with sofa and a balcony or terrace.

In the morning at Le Domaine de la Baie, a continental breakfast can be served upon request and at an extra cost.

In July and August, there is entertainment staff and animations for kids between 4 and 12 years old.

We have 6-acre park, Residence Nemea The Domaine de la Baie is 35 km from Quimper and its train station.
