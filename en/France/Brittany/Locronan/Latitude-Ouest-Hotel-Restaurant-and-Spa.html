Description

Set on a 3-hectare park, this hotel is 800 metres from the centre of Locronan and 4 km from the sea.

It offers free Wi-Fi access, 2 terraces and a wellness area with hot tub, infrared sauna and massage room at an extra cost.

All the heated rooms at Le Latitude Ouest Hotel & Restaurant offer garden views and a flat-screen TV.

A private bathroom with free toiletries and a bath or shower is provided. A daily buffet breakfast is served at the property.

In the evenings, guests are able to try the traditional dishes proposed by the hotel’s restaurant.

A bar is also available. Le Latitude Ouest Hotel & Restaurant is a 10-minute drive from the centre of Douamenez and 12 km from Quimper.
