Description

Hotel Breiz Armor has a prime beachfront location with a view of the Bay d'Audierne between Pointe du Raz and Penmarc'h.

It offers a fitness centre with a hot tub. Apartments at the Breiz Armor feature a balcony and have direct access to the sea front terrace.

They have a kitchenette fully equipped with a refrigerator, and a coffee maker. All rooms are soundproofed and are equipped with a flat-screen TV and free Wi-Fi access.

Every morning a continental breakfast is served. The restaurant serves gourmet cuisine and can be enjoyed on the spacious terrace overlooking the ocean.

After dinner, guests can relax with a drink at Le Breiz bar. Breiz Armor hotel is ideally situated for seaside trips since it is only 20 metres from Penhors beach.

Quimper Airport is 12 km away, and Quimper Train Station is 20 km from the hotel.
