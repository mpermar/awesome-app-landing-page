Description

The Hotel Ker Vennaïk is located in the seaside resort of Bénodet, opposite the lighthouse and 150 metres from the harbour.

It offers free Wi-Fi and rooms with an LCD TV. The functional rooms also include a minibar.

Free toiletries and a hairdryer are provided in the en suite bathroom. Some rooms also have views of the Odet Gardens.

Additional facilities at this hotel include a buffet breakfast served from 08:00 to 12:00, bicycle rental and an airport shuttle service, available with an extra cost.
