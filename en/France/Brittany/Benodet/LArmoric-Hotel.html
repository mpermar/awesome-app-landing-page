Description

L'Armoric Hotel sits just 500 metres from the charming harbour and beach in Benodet.

Its individually decorated rooms are en suite with satellite TV and free WiFi internet access.

Some of the more spacious rooms at L'Armoric Hotel also have a sofa bed and a mini bar.

All of the rooms are serviced by a lift. The restaurant at L'Armoric Hotel served regional and local specialties made with fresh local products.

A breakfast buffet is also served daily. There is also a lounge bar for an evening aperitif.

L'Armoric Hotel also features a relaxing private garden, a terrace and a heated outdoor swimming pool with a retractable roof, open from April to November.
