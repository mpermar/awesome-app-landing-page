Description

Located on the west coast of France, Les Jardins d'Arvor is just 150 metres from Benodet beach.

Guests have access to a heated pool (29 degree water), sauna and hammam. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

The Jardins d’Arvor offers apartments, each with a private balcony or terrace and TV.

All of the apartments have a seating area and a kitchenette with a dining table. Bed linen and towels are provided in the accommodation.

The property is pet-friendly. A breakfast buffet is served daily from 8:00 to 10:00.

Free parking and a free bike shed are available on site at Les Jardins d’Arvor. It is situated 17 km from Quimper SNCF Train Station.
