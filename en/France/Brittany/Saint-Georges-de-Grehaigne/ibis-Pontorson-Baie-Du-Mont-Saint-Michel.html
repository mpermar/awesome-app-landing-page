Description

Situated in Saint-Georges-de-Gréhaigne, 11km from Mont Saint Michel, ibis Pontorson Mont Saint Michel Bay features a terrace, bar, and restaurant.

This property also provides guests with free WiFi. At the hotel, all rooms include a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a wardrobe.

The rooms come with a private bathroom with a shower and a hair dryer. Guests at ibis Pontorson can enjoy a continental buffet breakfast, including sweet and savoury products.

Gluten free items are also available. Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.

Cancale is 39 km from ibis Pontorson Baie Du Mont Saint Michel, while Dinan is 48.
