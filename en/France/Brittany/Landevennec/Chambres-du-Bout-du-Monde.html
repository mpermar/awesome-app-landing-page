Description

Chambres du Bout du Monde offers guest rooms and a self-catering cottage located in the Armorica Natural Regional Park, just 500 metres from the coast.

Free Wi-Fi access is provided in the whole property. Rooms have a modern design and include a TV, a wardrobe and a private bathroom with a walk-in shower.

When rented as a whole, the cottage also includes a terrace, a lounge area and an equipped kitchen.

Free private parking is possible on site. The property is located on the GR34 hiking path, and a 10-minute walk from the harbour in Landévennec.
