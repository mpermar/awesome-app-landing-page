Description

Guest rooms at Castel Clara have refined décor and include satellite TV and a free Wi-Fi connection. All rooms feature a private bathroom, and some rooms offer sea views.

Castel Clara Hotel & Thalassotherapy has 2 restaurants. Bleu Maniere Verte serves French gourmet and regional cuisine. Café Clara is a seafood buffet restaurant.

Guests can dine in one of the dining rooms or on the panoramic terrace facing the sea.

Massage and beauty treatments are available upon request. There is also a sauna and a hot tub.
