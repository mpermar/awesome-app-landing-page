Description

Set on the Nantes-Brest Canal, in the heart of Brittany, Au Relais de l'Oust is a hotel just 3 km from Josselin Castle.

You can relax with a drink from the bar, enjoy the park or rent the bicycles to discover the area.

Free Wi-fi access is available. Accessed by a lift, the rooms feature a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with bathtub.

Some of the rooms have a terrace facing the canal. A traditional French breakfast can be enjoyed and served each morning in the dining room, in the comfort of your room or on the terrace when the weather is fine.
