Description

Facing the sea, Spa Marin is set on Pléneuf Beach in the Val André resort. It offers a Spa and Wellness Center with free access from 9:00 to 20:30 and has a large terrace overlooking the sea.

The soundproofed guest rooms are equipped with air conditioning, Canal + channels and free Wi-Fi internet access. Rooms are available with garden or sea views and guests receive a welcome tray on arrival (with teas and coffee).

A buffet breakfast is available for an extra charge at Spa Marin Du Val André. Upon reservation guests can enjoy lunch or dinner at the restaurant which serves refined French cuisine. During fine weather it is possible to eat on the terrace, which features sea views.

The Spa at this Thalasso resort includes a heated, salted pool, a steam bath and a fitness room. It is advised to reserve your treatments in advance.

Saint-Brieuc Train Station is 17 mi away and Dinard-Pleurtuit Airport is an hour’s drive from the resort. The accommodations is 9.3 mi from Lamballe Train Station.
