Description

Located in Paimpol, 400 metres from Abbaye de Beauport, Le Clos de la Baie offers guest rooms with garden or sea views, just 300 metres from Cruckin Beach.

You can enjoy the garden and surf the internet using the free Wi-Fi connection. All of the rooms at Le Clos de la Baie have a wooden floor and a TV.

The private bathroom is fitted with a shower. Some rooms have a partial sea view.

A continental breakfast is included and served each morning in the dining room. Several restaurants can be found 1.

8 km from the the guest house. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the property. Free private parking is also possible on site and Paimpol Train Station is 1.
