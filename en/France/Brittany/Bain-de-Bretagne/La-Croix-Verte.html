Description

Set in a 16th-century building in Bain-de-Bretagne, La Croix Verte offers guestrooms and self-catering studios.

Guests can enjoy free access to the on-site wine cellar and relax on the terrace.

The accommodations at La Croix Verte offer free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bath or shower.

Some accommodations offer a kitchenette and others are accessible for persons with reduced mobility.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning in the dining room, and guests can choose to have breakfast in their room.

Guests can enjoy local products at the on-site restaurant and have a drink at the bar.

The Rennes Train Station is 30 minutes away and the Le Bois Glaume Castle is 8 km away.

Free private parking is available at the property, and guests can visit the Bain Lake just 700 meters away.
