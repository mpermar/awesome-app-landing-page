Description

The Hôtel Le Cobh is located in the Brittany town of Ploërmel near the historic Paimpont forest.

Guestrooms have satellite TV and free Wi-Fi. Le Cobh’s restaurant serves gourmet international cuisine.

Guests can also enjoy the hotel’s reading room, garden and games area for children.

The design of the Hôtel Le Cobh was inspired by the Arthurian legends of the area.

Guests can easily drive to the castle at Josselin 10 kilometres away. The ancient Broceliande and Paimpont forests can be reached in 15 minutes.
