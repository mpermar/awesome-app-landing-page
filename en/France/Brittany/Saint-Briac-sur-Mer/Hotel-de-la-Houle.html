Description

Located in Saint-Briac-sur-Mer, in Brittany, this hotel features en suite rooms decorated with warm colours, free Wi-Fi internet access in the public areas and free private parking on site.

Dinard is 7. 5 km away. With a refined décor, each room at Hôtel de la Houle includes a private bathroom with shower and a wardrobe.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning in the dining room. The port is 300 metres away, Dinar Golf Club is 800 metres away and Saint-Malo is a 30-minute drive away.

Dinard-Pleurtuit-Saint-Malo Airport is 9 km away and Saint-Malo Train Station is 20 km from Hotel de la Houle.
