Description

The ibis Quiberon Plage, a lovely leisure establishment, is situated in Quiberon, just 250 metres from the Thalassotherapy centre and the beach.

It features a relaxation area with an indoor heated swimming pool, a solarium, sauna, hamman, spa and a children's playground.

The ibis Quiberon Plage offers fully renovated rooms, including duplex family rooms.

They are spacious and equipped with modern en suite facilities. WiFi internet access is free of charge throughout the hotel, including the guestrooms.

A buffet breakfast composed of sweet and savoury dishes such as eggs, fruit salad, yogurts and juices is served every day.

Pastries baked on site and fresh French Madeleine cakes are also on offer, as well as a hot beverage and a piece of fruit to take away.

Outside of regular breakfast hours guests can also enjoy a lighter option, available from 04:00.

The hotel offers a privileged location from which to discover the spectacular natural coast of Quiberon.

It is also close to the landing pier, where boats depart to Belle-Île, Houat and Hoëdic.
