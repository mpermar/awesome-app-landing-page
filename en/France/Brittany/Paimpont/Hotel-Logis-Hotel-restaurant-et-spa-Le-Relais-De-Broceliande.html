Description

This Logis hotel is located in the Brittany village of Paimpont, next to the lake. The spa features a sauna, a hammam and an outdoor hot tub, and beauty treatments are available upon request.

The spacious guest rooms include satellite TV and free Wi-Fi. Each room has a contemporary decoration and an private bathroom.

Guests of the Logis Hotel Le Relais De Broceliande can enjoy seasonal market produce in the restaurant. Guests can also enjoy a drink in the bar, or on the terrace.

The Logis Hotel Le Relais De Broceliande is surrounded by the Forêt de Paimpont. Guests can enjoy hiking and cycling in the scenic surroundings.

The hotel is 4.3 mi from the N24 motorway and free private parking is provided.
