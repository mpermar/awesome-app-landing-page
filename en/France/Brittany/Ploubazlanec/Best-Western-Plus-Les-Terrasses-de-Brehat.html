Description

This 4-star hotel is set directly on the beach and a 10-minute drive to Paimpol. Best Western Plus Les Terrasses de Bréhat features free Wi-Fi access, an indoor swimming pool and a spa and fitness centre.

Each room features a view of the sea or garden and is equipped with a flat-screen TV, a minibar and a kettle.

There is a private bathroom with a bath or shower and free toiletries. Buffet breakfast is served daily.

The restaurant offers a bistro style lunch and fine cuisine for dinner. Guests can make use of one of the three hot tub for free.

Books, magazines and board games are available at the reception. There is also a washing machine and tumble dryer at guest's disposal at the hotel.

Local activities include hiking and paragliding. Launay sandy beach is a 20-minute walk away and Saint-Brieuc is 50 km away.
