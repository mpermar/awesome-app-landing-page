Description

The rooms in the bed and breakfast are equipped with a coffee machine. All rooms include a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries. All guest rooms feature a desk.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning at the property.

Capitainerie Clos Morin offers a sauna. You can play ping pong at the accommodation, and bike rental is available.

Solidor Tower is 1.4 miles from Capitainerie Clos Morin. The nearest airport is Dinard Bretagne Airport, 3.7 miles from the property.
