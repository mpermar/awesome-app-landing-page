Description

Situated in Saint-Benoît-des-Ondes in the Brittany Region, 12 km from Saint Malo, La Grande Mare boasts an outdoor pool and sun terrace.

Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. Free private parking is available on site.

La Grande Mare features free WiFi. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property.

You can play table tennis at this guest house, and the area is popular for hiking.

Le Mont Saint Michel is 25 km from La Grande Mare, while Cancale is 6 km from the property.
