Description

Chateau d’Apigné is set in a beautiful 25-hectare park, 8 km from Rennes. It features spacious and luxury rooms in a classic 19-centurary décor and large beds.

The rooms are situated in the castle or in the Pavilion Elizabeth appendix. All rooms are soundproofed and have free access to Wi-Fi, sports channels.

Each room has a private bathroom either with bathtub or walk-in shower. Les Tourelles restaurant serves traditional cuisine in a gourmet style along with a daily breakfast.

Chateau d’Apigné has a 24-hour reception, an elevator that takes guests to their rooms and free private parking is provided.
