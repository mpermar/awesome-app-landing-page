Description

All units comprise a seating area, and a fully equipped kitchenette with various cooking facilities, including a microwave, a toaster and a fridge. A terrace with garden views is offered in every unit.

A continental breakfast is served at the property. There's an in-house restaurant, which specializes in local and international cuisine.

The resort village has a playground.

Hiking can be enjoyed nearby.

La Baule is 29 miles from Village Club Kerlannic.
