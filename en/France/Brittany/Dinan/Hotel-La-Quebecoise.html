Description

Hotel La Québécoise is located 3 km from the train station and old town at Dinan.

This former farmhouse offers comfortable rooms and free parking. Rooms are fully equipped and hairdryers, irons and kettles are available on request.

There is a communal fridge and microwave for use by guests. The large car park can accommodate lorries at an extra cost and upon availability, and there is space in the garage for motorbikes and bicycles.

Hotel La Québécoise makes a great base for exploring Brittany. The popular town of Saint-Malo is easily accessible by car.
