Description

Hotel La Chapelle Saint Martin is located in a large park and welcomes you in an elegant country house. It offers an outdoor swimming pool and free Wi-Fi access.

La Chapelle Saint Martin offers 4-star rooms featuring period paintings and traditional decor. Each room offers satellite TV. Some of them are air-conditioned.

The hotel has a gourmet restaurant serving local specialties prepared with fresh produce.

The center of Limoges is 4.3 mi from this hotel and Limoges Bellegarde Airport is only 2.5 mi away. The hotel is accessible via the A20 motorway, 7.5 mi away.
