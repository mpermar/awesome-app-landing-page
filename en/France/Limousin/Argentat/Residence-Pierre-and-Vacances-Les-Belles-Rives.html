Description

In a picturesque location on the left bank of the Dordogne overlooking the river, the Pierre & Vacances Les Belles Rives residence offers comfortable, self-catering accommodation and an outdoor swimming pool.

The property offers a basic WiFi connection for free and higher speed internet is available at an extra charge.

The apartments provide a well-appointed living space for 4 or 5 guests. They feature modern en suite facilities, a TV, seating area and a full equipped kitchen area, including a dishwasher.

Relax on your private balcony and enjoy the pleasant view over the Dordogne River.

Children will be kept entertained in the outdoor playground. During your stay, make the most of the activities on offer, including fishing, cycling and hiking.
