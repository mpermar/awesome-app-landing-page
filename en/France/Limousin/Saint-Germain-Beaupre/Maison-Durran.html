Description

Maison Durran is set on a plot of 500m2 with a garden at the rear. It is English owned and is set in the small village of Saint Germain Beaupre.

Guests can enjoy free Wi-Fi in public areas and in some guestrooms. During the months of July and August, guests can enjoy an above-ground swimming pool.

Maison Durran offers free tea, coffee, cordial or water on arrival. The rooms at Maison Durran are individually decorated in a classic style and feature parquet floors.

Each room offers tea/coffee making facilities, linen and towels, ironing facilities, hairdryer and alarm clock.

There is also bottled water, mini fridges with drinks and chocolate, and Android tablets with free Netflix, Youtube and Spotify music access.

each room has a private bathroom with bathrobes and slippers. Two lounges are available for guests.

The rooms have a TV, DVDs, board games and books to enjoy. There is also a small gift shop and a bar.

Every morning an extensive continental breakfast is served that includes tea, coffee, hot chocolate, fruit juices, cold meat, cheeses, jams, honey, cereals, yogurts, fruit, compotes, bread and cakes.

The hosts can provide a full English cooked breakfast at an additional cost but this must be ordered the night before.

With at least 48 hours of advance notice, the hosts can also prepare evening meals for any type of dietary requirement, served between 18:30-20:30.

Guests can also find a restaurant 100 metres away. The dining room at Maison Durran is equipped with a counter top oven, a microwave and a coffee machine.

The nearest town is La Souterraine, less than 15 minutes away by car. This Medieval town with its ancient church square and snaking streets offers a delightful choice of shopping.

There are numerous other places of interest to visit and many activities. Please ask the hosts for their advice.
