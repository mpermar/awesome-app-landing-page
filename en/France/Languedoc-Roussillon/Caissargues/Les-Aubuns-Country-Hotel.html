Description

This country-side hotel is located in Garons, in close proximity to the Nimes-Arles-Camargue Airport and a few kilometres from Nimes.

It offers an outdoor pool and free Wi-Fi access. Each guest room at Les Aubuns Country Hotel has a garden view and are individually decorated and have cotton bedding.

Each has a private bathroom and is equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, and most are air-conditioned.

Les Aubuns has a restaurant, which serves regional cuisine prepared with seasonal produce.

Guests can relax with a glass of wine from the local vineyards on the pool-side terrace.
