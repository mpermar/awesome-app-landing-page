Description

Surrounded by gardens, this hotel features a seasonal swimming pool and is located 200 metres from the entrance to the site of the Roman aqueduct, Le Pont du Gard.

Guests are invited to dine 'al fresco' on the terrace. A flat-screen TV, air conditioning and en suite bathroom facilities are provided in each of the rooms at Le Colombier.

Some rooms feature free Wi-Fi access and open up onto a terrace. Breakfast is served each morning inside or on the terrace.

The hotel has a bar, and the restaurant proposes traditional meals prepared with products from the region.

Two free, private car parks are available on site and the Remoulins Train Station is 2.

5 km from the hotel. Avignon is reached by car in 30 minutes and the city of Nimes is 30 km away.
