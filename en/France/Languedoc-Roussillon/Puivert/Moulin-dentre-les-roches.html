Description

At the bed and breakfast, the rooms have a closet and a flat-screen TV. With a private bathroom equipped with a shower and free toiletries, rooms at Moulin d'entre les roches also provide guests with free WiFi. Guest rooms include a seating area.

The accommodation offers a continental or Full English/Irish breakfast.

Skiing, cycling and fishing are among the activities that guests of Moulin d'entre les roches can find nearby.

The bed and breakfast provides a laundry service, as well as business facilities like fax and photocopying.

Carcassone is 25 miles from Moulin d'entre les roches, while Pas de la Casa is 31 miles away. Salvaza Airport is 24 miles from the property.
