Description

Park & Suites Village Gorges de l’Hérault-Cévennes is set at the foot of the Cévennes mountain range, 6 km from the medieval village of Brissac.

It offers views of the countryside, an outdoor swimming pool, children’s playground and go-karting on site.

Montpellier is 40 km away. All accommodation is air-conditioned and features a private terrace, a living area with a flat-screen TV, soundproofing and a bathroom.

The kitchen includes an oven, dishwasher and coffee machine. A continental breakfast is available each morning.

Additional features include a terrace with panoramic views, pétanque playing facilities and free Wi-Fi access.

Cévennes National Park is only a 15-minute drive away and Notre-Dame-de-Suc Abbey is 6 km from the holiday park.
