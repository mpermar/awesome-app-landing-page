Description

Located in Vers-Pont-du-Gard, La Bégude Saint Pierre sits in a 17th-century building with a 14-hectare domain with olive trees.

It features an outdoor swimming pool, a lounge bar and a restaurant. The air-conditioned guest rooms offer a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a Nespresso coffee machine and a minibar.

Some rooms have a private terrace with views of the pool. The bathroom features a bathtub or shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries from Durance.

A range of pastries and hot drinks is served for breakfast as a buffet or to be enjoyed in the comfort of guest rooms.

Guests can enjoy the inventive cuisine served at the restaurant offering views of the pool and the garden.

Pont du Gard is 2 km away, Uzès is 10 km away and Nîmes is 22 km from the property.

Avignon Train Station is a 30-minute drive away. There is access to A9 Motorway, just a 5-minute drive away.
