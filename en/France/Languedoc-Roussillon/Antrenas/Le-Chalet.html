Description

Set on the edge of the Aubrac Plateau, this hotel is just a 5-minute drive from Parc des Loups du Gévaudan animal park.

It offers a large terrace with panoramic views of the mountains. Free WiFi is available.

The accommodation at Le Chalet is individually decorated and includes a private bathroom with a shower.

Rooms on the south side feature a TV and some accommodations have a balcony or views of the countryside.

A continental breakfast is served every morning in the dining room or on the panoramic terrace.

You can also relax with a drink from the bar. Free private parking and a playground are available at the hotel.

Other local activities include visiting the Millau Viaduct, a 15-minute drive away, or the European Bison Reserve, 35 km away.
