Description

Offering an outdoor pool and terrace, Le Prose Hôtel is situated in La Grande-Motte in the Languedoc-Roussillon Region.

Guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Free WiFi and free private parking are available.

Each room at this hotel is air conditioned and is fitted with a flat-screen TV. You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property and a restaurant that serves Mediterranean specialities.

A brunch with seasonal products is served on Sundays. Evening entertainment such as DJ sets can be enjoyed on Saturday night.

Seminar rooms are available at the property. On Saturdays and Sundays in July and August, various activities including brunches, themed evenings and pool parties are hosted onsite.

Montpellier is 25 km from Le Prose Hôtel, while Nîmes is 48 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Montpellier - Mediterranee Airport, 15 km from Le Prose Hôtel.
