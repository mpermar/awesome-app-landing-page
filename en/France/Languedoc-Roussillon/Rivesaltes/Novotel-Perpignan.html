Description

This hotel is located 10 minutes from Perpignan and the beaches. Novotel Perpignan offers 56 air-conditioned rooms as well as the Novotel Caf bar restaurant with meals served 24 hours a day.

A number of meeting rooms are available for holding your functions and conferences.

With our outdoor swimming pool and pine forest gardens, we invite you to come and visit Perpignan for a weekend or for a few days on a family holiday.

Secure private parking is also available to guests. Up to 2 children (15 years and under) stay free when sharing with parents.
