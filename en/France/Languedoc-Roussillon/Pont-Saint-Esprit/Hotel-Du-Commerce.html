Description

Nestled in the heart of the historic quarter of Pont-Saint-Esprit, Hotel Du Commerce is set in a traditional house innorthern Provence.

Free WiFi is provided. The 10 rooms are all equipped with double-glazed windows and a private bathroom.

Guests can benefit from discounts on several attractions such as the Côtes du Rhône wine caves or boats trips along the Ardèche Gorges.

After an active day, relax with a book or among friends in the hotel's lounge. Guests have access to a secured garage where bicycles and motorbikes can be parked upon request.
