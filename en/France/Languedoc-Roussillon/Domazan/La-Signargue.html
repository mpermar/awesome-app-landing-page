Description

At the guesthouse, every room has a patio. Rooms contain air conditioning and a flat-screen TV, and some units at La Signargue have a pool view. All rooms are equipped with a seating area.

The accommodation has a playground. You can play ping pong at La Signargue.

Avignon is 8 miles from the guesthouse. The nearest airport is Caumont Airport, 13 miles from the property.
