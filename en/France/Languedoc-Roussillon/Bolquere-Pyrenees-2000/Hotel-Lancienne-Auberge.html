Description

This Logis hotel provides en suite accommodation in the heart of the Pyrenees Catalanes Regional Natural Park.

It has a restaurant, free Wi-Fi and free parking. L'Ancienne Auberge rooms are equipped with a TV, telephone and private bathroom with shower.

Some rooms have a bathtub and balcony. L'Ancienne Auberge serves traditional Catalan specialities made from local ingredients.

The meals are complemented by a selection of regional wines. Guests can relax with an aperitif on the bar’s terrace.

There are a number of activities and sites near Hotel L'Ancienne Auberge including fishing, hiking and museums.
