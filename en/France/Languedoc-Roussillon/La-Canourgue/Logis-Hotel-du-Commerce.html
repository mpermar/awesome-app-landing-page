Description

Logis Hotel du Commerce is located in the heart of the village of La Canourgue. It offers free parking and well-equipped and comfortable rooms with free WiFi.

Guests can enjoy typical regional specialties in the relaxed atmosphere of the hotel restaurant, and continental breakfast is served daily.

Logis Hotel du Commerce makes an excellent base for discovering the Lozère region.

It is situated only 30 minutes by car from the Viaduct de Millau, Aubrac, and Tarn Gorge.
