Description

At the hotel, the rooms are equipped with a desk. All rooms feature a closet.

A continental or buffet breakfast is available daily at the property. At Hotel De La Paix you'll find a restaurant serving French cuisine.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool.

Montpellier is 29 miles from Hotel De La Paix, while Sète is 29 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Beziers Cap d'Agde Airport, 28 miles from the property.
