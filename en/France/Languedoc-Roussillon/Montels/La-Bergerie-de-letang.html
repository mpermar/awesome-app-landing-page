Description

Located in the countryside, this former farmhouse overlooks the Capestang Pond. It features an outdoor swimming pool and modern guest rooms, some with free Wi-Fi access.

The air-conditioned guest rooms and apartments offer a balcony with panoramic views over the landscape.

Some apartments include a kitchenette with a fridge and all guests have access to a washing machine.

A continental breakfast is served on the terrace or in the lounge every morning at La Bergerie de l'etang.

There is also a selection restaurants just 4 km from the guest house. Guests can relax in the lounge at La Bergerie de l'etang, which features a sculpted fireplace.

With free public parking on site, the guesthouse is 8 km from Narbonnes City Centre.
