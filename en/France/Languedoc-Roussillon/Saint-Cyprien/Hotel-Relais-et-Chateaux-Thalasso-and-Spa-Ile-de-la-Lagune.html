Description

One of our top picks in Saint-Cyprien.This hotel is located on a private island, 0.9 mi from Saint-Cyprien-Plage. It provides luxury accommodations with a gastronomic restaurant and a heated, outdoor pool on the rooftop.

Each of the spacious suites is equipped with air conditioning, free WiFi and a marble bathroom with Carita free toiletries, bathrobes and slippers. They also have a terrace overlooking the lagoon and Mediterranean gardens.

The one-Michelin starred restaurant, L’Almandin, serves refined dishes, mixing modern and regional influences. Guests can dine on the terrace with views of the lagoon and gardens.

A hot tub features in the spa center, and you can also request a massage.

Free on-site parking is provided for guests exploring the Catalan region by car. The Saint Cyprien Golf Course is 3.7 mi away and the historic quarter of Perpignan is 9.3 mi from the hotel. Perpignan–Rivesaltes Airport is a 35-minute drive away.
