Description

ibis Styles Carcassonne La Cité is located just 600 metres from the medieval town of Carcassone.

It features a restaurant, an outdoor swimming pool, a fitness room and free private parking.

Free WiFi is included. The rooms and studios at ibis Styles Carcassonne La Cité are all air-conditioned and equipped with a TV and a private bathroom with a shower or a bath.

They are all accessible by lift and some are adapted for disabled guests. A daily breakfast is served in the dining room, which offers views of the swimming pool and the city's roof tops.

Tea and coffee can be enjoyed anytime during the day in the hotel hall. ibis Styles Carcassonne La Cité is a 15-minute walk from the Basilica of Saint-Nazaire and Saint Celse.
