Description

Located in Carcassonne, Campanile Carcassonne East - The City is a 20-minute walk from the medieval city.

It offers a restaurant, bar and free WiFi throughout the hotel. Carcassonne city centre and train station are 3 km away.

Guest rooms at Campanile Carcassonne Est-La Cite are decorated in a contemporary style.

They are air-conditioned and feature a flat-screen TV and tea and coffee making facilities.

A buffet breakfast is served each morning and the hotel’s restaurant offers traditional French cuisine, as well as a starter and dessert buffet.

The hotel’s reception desk is open 24-hours a day. During their stay, guests can use the hotel’s lounge or enjoy a drink in the bar or on the terrace with a pergola.

There is an outdoor swimming pool with a dedicated lane fenced off for swimming training.

The Campanile Carcassonne offers free private parking. It is 4 km from the A61 which provides access to Toulouse and Narbonne.
