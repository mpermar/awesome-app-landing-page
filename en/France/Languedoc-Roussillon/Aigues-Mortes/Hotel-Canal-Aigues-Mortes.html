Description

Hotel Canal Aigues Mortes is a 5-minute walk to the fortified city, on the banks of the canal, visible from its panoramic terrace and swimming pool.

It offers air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi. Private parking is available for an extra fee.

All of the rooms in the Hotel Canal Aigues Mortes are soundproofed and have flat-screen TV with French and foreign channels.

In the warmer months the buffet breakfast can be enjoyed on the outdoor terrace. Hotel Canal Aigues Mortes is a 10-minute drive from Grau-du-Roi beach and a 20-minute drive from Montpellier-Méditerranée Airport.
