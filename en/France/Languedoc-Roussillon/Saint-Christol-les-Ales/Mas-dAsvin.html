Description

All rooms in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. All rooms include a private bathroom with a shower. At Mas d'Asvin each room includes a seating area.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet breakfast. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the in-house restaurant, which specializes in local cuisine and also offers Vegan options.

Spa and wellness facilities including a sauna and a hammam are at guests' disposal during their stay at Mas d'Asvin. A variety of popular activities are available in the area around the property, including cycling and fishing.

Nîmes is 21 miles from the resort. The nearest airport is Garons Airport, 27 miles from Mas d'Asvin.
