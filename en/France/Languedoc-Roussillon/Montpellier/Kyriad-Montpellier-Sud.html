Description

This contemporary design hotel is located on the motorway A709, exit 31 Croix D'argent in Lattes, less than a 15-minute drive to Montpellier city centre and 10 minutes from the Mediterranean Sea.

It offers free WiFi. Each soundproofed and air-conditioned room at the Kyriad Montpellier Sud A709 has modern furniture and is equipped with a cable flat-screen TV including foreign channels, Canal +, Canal Sat, beIN sports, RMC Sport and Euronews channels.

Extras include tea and coffee making facilities. It also features a private bathroom.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning. Guests can dine in the onsite HEY HEY restaurant, which services cuisine using fresh produce.

Kyriad Montpellier Sud A709 has a 24-hour front desk service. Free parking is provided and guests can travel from the hotel directly to Montpellier Saint-Roch train station by bus or tram.

It is a 15-minute drive from Montpellier - Méditerranée Airport and less than a 20-minute drive from Place de la Comédie.
