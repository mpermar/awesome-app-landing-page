Description

All of the 4-star rooms have a refined decoration and have a flat-screen TV. Some of the rooms also have a seating area and coffee and tea facilities. The marble bathrooms are fitted with a walk-in shower and they include a hairdryer.

A buffet breakfast is available. The restaurant, La Closerie, serves traditional and regional cuisine from Monday to Friday. The hotel’s bar, Le Club, is open daily and room service is available.

A business center with computers, printers and free WiFi connection is also available for guests throughout the hotel.
