Description

Lagrange Grand Bleu Vacances - Résidence La Grenadine is just 50 meters from Mediterranean Beaches and 8 km from Cap d'Agde.

Built in 2012, it offers an outdoor seasonal swimming pool and a fitness centre with a sauna and a hammam.

The villas at Lagrange Grand Bleu Vacances - Residence La Grenadine are set within 2 hectares of private grounds.

All accommodations have their own modern bathroom with shower. Villas for guests with limited mobility are also available upon request and subject to availability.

Each villa is air conditioned and has a kitchenette with a dishwasher, a stovetop, a microwave and a fridge.

Guests can dine indoors or on the terrace. On-site activities include table tennis, petanque and children’s games.

Free private parking is provided, Sete is 17 km away and Beziers is 30 km from Lagrange Grand Bleu Vacances – Résidence La Grenadine.
