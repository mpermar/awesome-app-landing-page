Description

Guest rooms in the guesthouse are equipped with a flat-screen TV. The rooms have a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries. The rooms include a closet.

A continental breakfast is served at the property.

Domaine De Bailheron has a playground. The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental and car rental are available at the accommodation.

The nearest airport is Beziers Cap d'Agde Airport, 6 miles from the property.
