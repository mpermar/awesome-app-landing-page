Description

The Domaine de Fraisse is located in Leuc, 10 km south of Carcassonne. It features an outdoor swimming pool and offers en-suite accommodation with a garden terrace.

Breakfast is served daily and guests can enjoy organic cuisine at the Domaine de Fraisse’s restaurant, La Centaurée.

The residence offers free on-site parking. It is easily accessible by the A61 motorway and local leisure activities include hiking and golf.
