Description

Located in the seaside resort of Cap d’Agde, this hotel features an outdoor, heated swimming pool and a 5000 m² Mediterranean garden.

Guests have free access to the fitness centre. It is situated 2 km from the beach and 1 km from Cap d’Agde Golf Club.

The air-conditioned rooms feature satellite TV with international channels and free WiFi access.

Some of the modern rooms include a minibar or views of the garden or pool. Rooms with private terraces can accommodate pets.

Guests can enjoy a drink in the snack bar or on the terrace. A continental, buffet breakfast is prepared every morning and can be enjoyed in the guestrooms for an extra charge.

A 24-hour reception is also at your disposal. Free private parking is available. Béziers Cap d'Agde Airport is a 15-minute drive away.
