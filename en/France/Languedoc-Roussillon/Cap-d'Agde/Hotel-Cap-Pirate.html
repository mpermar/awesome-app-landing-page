Description

Hôtel Cap Pirate is a 4-star luxury 'hotel museum' offering a unique pirate-themed experience.

This 2-hectare property is located in Cap d'Agde, a 5-minute walk from the beach.

Guests have free unlimited access to the onsite 'La Baie des Pirates' aquatic park and can benefit from VIP service including free lockers, showers, sun loungers and access to the park one hour before it's opened to the general public and one hour after closing to the general public.

Two free surf simulator sessions will also be offered per stay. Each suite has unique pirate decor and offers free WiFi, air-conditioning, a flat-screen satellite TV and a kitchen equipped with a dishwasher, microwave, toaster and kettle.

Upon arrival, guests will find bottled water and a Nespresso coffee machine with coffee capsules in their suite.

The private bathroom has a walk-in shower. There is either a furnished terrace or a garden with a canopy bed, outdoor shower and hammock.

Each suite offers a stone barbecue. An outdoor private hot tub is available in some suites.

The onsite O'Pirata restaurant offers an all-you-can-eat buffet with seasonal local produce, as well as a continental or American breakfast in the mornings.

The Le Poséidon lounge bar has a tapas menu, in addition to signature sushi specialities, cocktails, a wine cellar and a rum cellar.

Guests are invited to discover the 30,000 pirate artifacts and replicas displayed around the property.

Pirate items and other miscellaneous objects can be purchased from the gift shop.

Meeting facilities and 2 escape rooms will open from June 2018. Free pirate-themed live entertainment is also offered and is suitable for all age groups.

Free beach shuttle service is also available all-year-round, from 08:00 to 20:00.

Guests also benefit from unlimited free access to the swimming facilities at Archipel la Cité de l'Eau aquatic centre located 1 km from the property, and can get discounted access to the centre's spa facilities.

Beziers Cap d'Agde Airport is 11 km away. Free private parking is available on site.
