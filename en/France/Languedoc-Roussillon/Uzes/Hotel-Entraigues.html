Description

Set in Uzès in the Languedoc-Roussillon Region, 32 km from Avignon, Hôtel Entraigues features an outdoor pool.

The hotel has a sun terrace and views of the city, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Free WiFi is provided throughout the property and private parking is available on site for an extra charge, subject to availability.

The rooms have air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and tea and coffee making facilities.

Some rooms have a seating area to relax in after a busy day, a terrace or a patio.

You will find a babysitting service at the property. A variety of other restaurants are available within a 2-minute walk.

An array of activities are offered in the area, such as golfing and cycling. Nîmes is 20 km from Hôtel Entraigues, while Arles is 41 km away.
