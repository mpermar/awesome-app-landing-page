Description

Located 19 km from the Chauvet Cave, Hotel Le Mas de Rivet is located in Barjac. This hotel is set in a traditional-style property, originally built in the 18th century.

It offers a sun terrace and a seasonal swimming pool. Featuring a view of the pool, the air-conditioned rooms here will provide you with free toiletries.

Free WiFi access is available. A buffet breakfast is also available and you can request a packed lunch on site.

Other facilities offered include meeting facilities, shared lounge with a TV and a games room.

Guests can also read a book from the library and request a shuttle service. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including horse riding, cycling and hiking.

The Gorges de l'Ardeche are a 45-minute drive from the property. Hotel Le Mas de Rivet offers free parking.
