Description

The rooms of this boutique hotel are individually decorated in an Art Deco style based on different colors, artwork and personality. Each has a private, a bathroom with a bathtub and slippers and amenities are provided. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the property.

Breakfast can be enjoyed in the restaurant, on the terrace or in the guest rooms. The restaurant serves refined cuisine using local produce, and has an extensive wine collection. Tables are set up for open-air dining on the terrace overlooking the pool and garden.

In the heart of the Pyrénées-Orientales region, the Villa Duflot is just 2 miles from the A9 highway and a 30-minute drive from the Spanish border.
