Description

Located in a large park and surrounded by olive trees and vineyards, La Villa Vicha, The Originals Relais offers air-conditioned chalets with private terraces.

They are situated between Monpellier and Nîmes. Guests have access to an outdoor swimming pool and a private tennis court.

La Villa Vicha, The Originals Relais also has a spa centre, which includes a sauna, hot tub and hammam.

The rooms are all equipped with free WiFi access. Each comes with a TV and a minibar, and has a private bathroom with bath.

A continental breakfast is served on the large terrace during summer. Breakfast is available upon prior reservation during the low season and there is also a bar and a children’s play area.

In the heart of Provence, La Villa Vicha, The Originals Relais is located in Aubais.

It is a 30-minute drive from the Mediterranean Coast and a 35-minute drive from Arles.
