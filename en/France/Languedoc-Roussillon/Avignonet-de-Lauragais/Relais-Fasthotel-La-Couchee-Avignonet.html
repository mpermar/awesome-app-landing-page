Description

Located in Avignonet-Lauragais, Relais Fasthotel "La Couchée" Avignonet is set in the rest area of Port Lauragais, on A61 Motorway.

Surrounded by the Canal du Midi, it features a garden and a 24-hour reception. All of the rooms have a desk, a carpeted floor and a telephone.

Each room also include a private bathroom, which is fitted a hairdryer and a bath or a shower.

Rooms for guests with reduced mobility are also available. Breakfast is served every morning in the communal lounge.

The on-site restaurant offers traditional dishes from the region for lunch and dinner.

Free Wi-Fi is provided throughout the property. Free private parking is possible on site and the hotel is a 20-minute drive to both Toulouse and Carcassonne.
