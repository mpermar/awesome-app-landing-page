Description

Village club de Bois d'Amont features a restaurant, bar, a shared lounge and ski-to-door access in Bois-dʼAmont.

Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a children's playground.

The hotel offers an indoor pool, hammam, entertainment staff and a kids' club. All units in the hotel are fitted with a flat-screen TV.

The units at Village club de Bois d'Amont come with a private bathroom equipped with a shower.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental breakfast. The wellness area at Village club de Bois d'Amont is comprised of a hot tub and a sauna.

You can play billiards and table tennis at the hotel, and the area is popular for hiking and skiing.

Les Rousses is 9 km from Bois d'Amont Village Club, while Divonne-les-Bains is 40 km away.
