Description

Located directly opposite Charles-De-Gaulle Airport Terminal 3 and 5 km from Paris Nord Villepinte Exhibition Centre, ibis Styles Paris Charles de Gaulle Airport features free WiFi, an on-site bar and a sun terrace.

The rooms feature air-conditioning and a flat-screen TV with cable channels. The Suites have a seating area where you can relax.

Breakfast is served daily from 04:30. Guests can enjoy fresh produce at the Foody'S Planet restaurant.

The free airport shuttle service can be found opposite the hotel. Aéroport Roissy-Charles de Gaulle RER Train Station is a 2-minute walk from the property and provides direct access to the city of Paris.
