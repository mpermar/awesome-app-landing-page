Description

Each air-conditioned room at the Premiere Classe Marne la Vallée - Bussy St Georges has simple décor and comes with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom. All rooms are accessible with an elevator.

A continental breakfast is served in the colorful breakfast room or on the terrace. The hotel also has vending machines and free daily newspapers in the 24-hour front desk.

The hotel is less than half a mile from Bussy St Georges RER station and a 3 minute drive from Ferrières-en-Brie Castle. You may also choose to walk the 10 minutes to Bussy Guermantes Golf Course.
