Description

Located 20 mi from Paris and 3.7 mi from Disneyland® Paris, Villages Nature® Paris is an atypical holiday resort where guests can enjoy 5 themed areas including the Aqualagon, one of Europe’s largest water parks, which is geothermically heated to 30°c year-round and features 7 slides, a wavepool and an outdoor pool with views of the resort's lake.

Guests can choose from 3 different accommodations styles- Cocoon VIP, Country Premium or Clan Comfort. Available as apartments or cottages, each accommodations has a bathroom, a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher and a spacious living room. During their stay, guests can use the free shuttle service to travel around the resort.

Villages Nature Paris at Disneyland® Paris offers a variety of restaurants serving fresh and local produce including a brasserie, an Italian restaurant and a wine bar. There is an on-site grocery store and a bakery which offers a breakfast delivery service.

During their stay, guests can relax and learn about nature in the Extraordinary Gardens. Children can enjoy the playgrounds in the Forest of Legends and the Lait’s Play area in Bellevie Farm, where guests can also have a hands-on farm experience and take part in beekeeping and bread making classes for an extra fee. The Lakeside Promenade offers family entertainment including boat rental, bowling and a children's club. Other activities throughout the resort include segway rental and a selection of escape games and fitness trails. Guests can enjoy massages and treatment in the Deep Nature Spa.

The resort is a 10-minute drive from Marne-la-Vallée-Chessy Train Station which provides direct access to Paris and Charles de Gaulle Airport. For guests arriving by car, 1 or 2 free parking spaces are available, depending on accommodations types. On the day of check-out, guests can make use of the resort's activities until 23:00.
