Description

The Radisson Blu Hotel Paris, Marne-la-Vallée sits on the Disneyland Paris Golf Course, just a 10-minute drive from Disneyland and 4 km from Val d'Europe RER Station and shopping centre.

It offers contemporary rooms with views of the surroundings, a wellness centre and 2 restaurants.

Guest rooms at the Radisson have flat-screen TV, tea and coffee facilities and free high-speed internet access.

These stylish rooms and suites offer views of the golf course, while some also have a separate living room.

Dining in the Radisson Blu Hotel Paris, Marne-la-Vallée can begin with a copious buffet breakfast at the Restaurant Birdie.

The Pamplemousse restaurant offers fine dining and specialises in French cuisine, while the Chardon Bar serves light snacks and sandwiches.

During the summer season, the shisha bar is open until 01:00. A free shuttle service will take guests from the hotel to the Marne la Vallee-Chessy Train Station and the Disneyland Resort Paris.

The wellness centre features a fitness area, sauna, hammam and indoor swimming pool.
