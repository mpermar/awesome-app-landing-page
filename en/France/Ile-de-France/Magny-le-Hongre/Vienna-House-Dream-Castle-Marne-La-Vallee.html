Description

Offering a free shuttle service to Disneyland Paris, Vienna House Dream Castle Marne La Vallee is surrounded by a lake and gardens and its facilities include an indoor pool, and a Spa.

Decorated in the style of Musketeer and Potsdam Castle, the guest rooms and suites are equipped with a flat-screen TV, free WiFi access, a minibar, tea and coffee making facilities and air conditioning.

The hotel restaurant's, The Kitchen, serves a full American buffet breakfast offering a wide choice of hot and cold dishes and pastries.

The Market’s buffet-style restaurant is open every evening serving a selection of French and European dishes.

Snacks are available in the bar from 11:00 until 23:00. Guests are invited to relax in the Dream Castle Spa that features a heated indoor swimming pool, sauna, hot tub, fitness room, massage therapy and a variety of beauty treatments.

You can buy Disneyland tickets at the hotel and the free shuttle service also stops at Marne-la-Vallée-Chessy RER station and TGV - Thalys - Eurostar terminal.

The property is a 35-minute drive from Paris and 4. 5 km from Val d'Europe RER Station and shopping centre.
