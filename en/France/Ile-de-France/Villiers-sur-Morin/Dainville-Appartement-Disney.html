Description

Offering a garden, Dainville Apartment offers accommodation in Villiers-sur-Morin, 9 km from Disneyland Paris.

Free private parking is available on site. The accommodation comes with a seating area with a flat-screen TV, CD player, and Blu-ray player.

Some units include a dining area and/or terrace. All units have a kitchen equipped with a dishwasher and oven.

A microwave, a coffee machine and a fridge are also featured. Each unit is equipped with a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Towels and bed linen are offered. You can play table tennis at the property, and the area is popular for golfing.

A number of activities are offered in the area, such as horse riding, fishing and canoeing.

Paris is 45 km from the property. The nearest airport is Paris - Charles de Gaulle Airport, 41 km from the property.
