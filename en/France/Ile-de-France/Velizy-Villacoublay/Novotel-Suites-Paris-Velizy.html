Description

Situated 10 km south of Paris, across the street from the Velizy 2 shopping centre, the Novotel Suites Paris Velizy offers air-conditioned suites.

It is 6 km from the Château de Versailles and 16 km from Le Golf National. All suites at the Novotel Suites Paris Velizy offer air conditioning and a private bathroom with a bath and a shower.

Free WiFi, cable TV and a Nespresso coffee machine are also available. Canal + and Bein Sports channels are also provided.

The Novotel Suites Paris Velizy offers 24-hour services including the Boutique Gourmande gourmet food counter and a 24-hour fitness room.

A bar is also available and breakfast can be enjoyed in the comfort of your room.

Guests can find several restaurants a 5-minute walk away. For added comfort, the hotel also features a free private, covered car park.

The property is a 5-minute walk from the T6 tramway, which provides access to Paris in less than 30 minutes.
