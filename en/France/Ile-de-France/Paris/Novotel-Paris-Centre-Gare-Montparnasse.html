Description

The Novotel Paris Centre Gare Montparnasse is ideally located in the heart of Paris, within easy reach of many of the capitals famous sights.

It is just 400 metres from both Pasteur and Montparnasse-Bienvene Metro Stations, which give direct access to the Eiffel Tower, the Marais, the Champs-Elyses, Montmartre and Saint-Germain-des-Prs.

Decorated in a modern style, the rooms are air-conditioned and feature a flat-screen HD TV with satellite channels.

There is also an electric kettle, minibar and free bottle of water. The Executive rooms come with free drinks and snacks, a Nespresso coffee machine, a bathrobe and slippers.

All private bathrooms have a bath or shower. The hotels NCaf is a restaurant and there is a bar with a shaded terrace, open every day until midnight.

The bar offers a large choice of drinks and regular entertainment is provided. Guests can also enjoy sports events on the large TV screen.

The 24-hour reception also sells a selection of snacks. The buffet breakfast can be served in the restaurant or on the sun terrace and is composed of pastries, fruit, eggs, cold meats and hot items.

An express breakfast option is also available at the bar. In the French school holidays, childrens entertainment is available during breakfast hours.

There is also private, covered parking, a ticket service, free newspapers and 2 iMac computers in the lobby.
