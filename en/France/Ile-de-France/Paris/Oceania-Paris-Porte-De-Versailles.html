Description

Each air-conditioned and soundproof room at the Oceania Paris Porte De Versailles is elegantly decorated. They have a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and free Wi-Fi.

The Oceania swimming pool comes with hydro jets and a counter-current swimming area, and guests have access to the spa with hot tub and a fitness center.

A buffet breakfast is served in the spacious dining room or on the outdoor terrace overlooking the garden. Guests can enjoy a drink at the hotel’s bar.

Metro Porte de Versailles is 1,000 feet from the Oceania Paris Porte De Versailles, allowing guests to explore Paris.

15th arr. is a great choice for travelers interested in romance, tourist attractions and ancient landmarks.
