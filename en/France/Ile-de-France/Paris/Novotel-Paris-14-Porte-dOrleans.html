Description

Located in south Paris, this 4-star Novotel is just 2 minutes’ walk to Porte d’Orleans Metro Station (Line 4) and Tramway (Line 3), giving direct access to the centre of Paris in just 15 minutes.

It offers soundproofed and air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi and is 12 km from Orly airport.

The spacious guest rooms at Novotel Paris 14 Porte d'Orléans are equipped with satellite TV and tea and coffee making facilities.

All rooms have a private bathroom and most include a bathtub. Up to 2 children (16 years and under) stay free when sharing the room with their parents.

Open for lunch and dinner, the Novotel Cafe Restaurant serves healthy, French dishes prepared with seasonal produce.

Meals can be eaten in the restaurant, bar or in guests’ rooms. Snacks can be enjoyed all day long at the bar.

Staff are available 24 hours at the Novotel Paris 14 Porte d'Orléans. Private parking is available with an extra cost.

From the metro station Porte d'Orléans, you will directly access the Latin Quarter, Notre Dame Saint-Michel and the Luxembourg Gardens.

You can get to the Eiffel Tower in 20 minutes by metro. The Parc des Expositions Porte de Versailles is only a few tram stations away.
