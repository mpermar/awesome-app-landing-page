Description

Situated opposite Gare de Lyon Train and Metro Station in the heart of Paris, Novotel Paris Gare De Lyon offers a fitness centre and a 24-hour heated indoor swimming pool.

The air-conditioned rooms are equipped with free WiFi internet, a 42-inch flat-screen TV and desk.

Novotel Paris Gare De Lyon provides a 24-hour à la carte service and guests can enjoy breakfast in their rooms.

A variety of restaurants can be reached within walking distance. The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day and guests have access to a luggage storage facility and a private car park.

On weekends and during school holidays children's entertainment is provided. The Louvre Museum is 14 minutes away by metro from Novotel Paris Gare De Lyon and the Orsay Museum is 25 minutes by RER C.
