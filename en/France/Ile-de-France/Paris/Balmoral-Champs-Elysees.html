Description

Hotel Balmoral is located 100 meters from Champs-Elysees and Charles de Gaulle Etoile Metro Station.

You can borrow one of the free iPads, which feature free international press, music and videos.

Rooms at Balmoral are decorated in a contemporary style. These air-conditioned rooms have an LCD TV and a minibar, while some of the private bathrooms include luxury bathrobes.

Some rooms feature a private balcony and a coffee machine. You can enjoy produces from the buffet breakfast in your room or on the small garden terrace.

The bar also serves a range of aperitifs and provides a relaxing spot for reading one of the free newspapers.

Guests can enjoy complementary soft drinks each afternoon in the hotel's bar. High-speed, fibre-optic internet access and WiFi access are available for free.

Bicycles are available and concierge service is also provided at Balmoral. Air France bus terminal is 50 metres away.

The airport shuttle service can be organised at the 24-hour reception, for a EUR 80 fee.
