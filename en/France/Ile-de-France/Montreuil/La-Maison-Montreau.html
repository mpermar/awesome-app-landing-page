Description

La Maison Montreau set 2 km from the center of Montreuil, 7 km from La Villette Park and a 30-minute drive from Gare de Lyon TGV Train Station.

It provides accommodation with a garden, a terrace and a shared lounge. The hotel provides a self service restaurant serving french dishes and free WiFi.

At the hotel, every room comes with a wardrobe and garden views. Guests will have access to shared bathroom facilities in front of their rooms.

Breakfast is served every morning and includes coffee, fresh bread, fresh seasonal fruits, handmade butter and jam as well as organic tea and orange juice.

Light meals for lunch are also offered on weekdays. Hiking is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.

The Maison Montreau is located 20 metres from the Parc Montreau bus stop, which provides access to the RER train station A - Val de Fontenay within 15 minutes.

The nearest airport is Paris Charles de Gaulle Airport, 22 km from La Maison Montreau.
