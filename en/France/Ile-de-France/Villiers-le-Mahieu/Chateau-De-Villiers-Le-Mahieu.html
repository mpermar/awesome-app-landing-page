Description

Château De Villiers-Le-Mahieu has been converted into a hotel and all-inclusive rates with free access to a swimming pool, 2 tennis courts and a spa.

It is located in Villiers-Le-Mahieu surrounded by forest. The 93 rooms at the Château De Villiers-Le-Mahieu have flat-screen TVs and each room offers a view of the property and the park.

Some rooms are in the main property building and others are in annexe buildings. The Spa centre with its fitness centre, sauna and heated outdoor swimming pool is exclusively for guests of the Chateau.

Guests enjoy free access to the golf practice range and can also enjoy free activities such as volleyball, cinema or cooking classes.

Meals at the Château De Villiers-Le-Mahieu are included in the rate. The restaurant offers traditional cuisine made using fresh seasonal produce.

Bars are open all day and offer an open bar service. Free private parking and 24 hours services are available at the Château De Villiers-Le-Mahieu.
