Description

Situated 10 km from the centre of Lille, Holiday Inn Lille Ouest Englos offers accommodation with free WiFi, a garden and an outdoor seasonal swimming pool.

The property is just 14 minutes' drive from Pierre Mauroy Stadium. Holiday Inn Lille Ouest Englos offers 121 rooms, 30 of which are family rooms.

Most of the rooms are non-smoking and 3 have been adapted for people with disabilities.

Each room is fitted with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a minibar. The private bathrooms include free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Guests can enjoy breakfast in their rooms or a meal in the hotel's restaurant. A snack bar is available for guests' convenience and guests can enjoy refreshments on the terrace by the swimming pool.

Meeting rooms are available for business customers. Additional facilities include a fitness centre, games room, children's playground and a laundry service.

The nearest station is Lille Europe SNCF and TGV Train Station, which gives direct access to Paris, Marseille, Brussels and London.
