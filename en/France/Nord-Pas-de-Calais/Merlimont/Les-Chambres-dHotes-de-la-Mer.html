Description

All guest rooms are equipped with an oven, microwave, a electric tea pot, a shower, a hair dryer and a closet. Rooms include a private bathroom, while some have a sea view. Guest rooms will provide guests with a toaster.

A continental breakfast is served daily at the property.

Windsurfing is among the activities that guests can enjoy near Les Chambres d'Hôtes de la Mer.

Le Touquet-Paris-Plage is 4.1 miles from Les Chambres d'Hotes de la Mer, while Boulogne-sur-Mer is 18 miles away. Le Touquet Airport is 4.3 miles from the property.
