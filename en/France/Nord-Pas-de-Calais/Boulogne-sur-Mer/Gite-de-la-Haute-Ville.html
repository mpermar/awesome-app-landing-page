Description

Gîte de la Haute Ville is located in Boulogne-sur-Mer, just 3 km from the coast. It offers a restaurant with a terrace and self-catering accommodation with free WiFi access.

The studio and the apartment feature a DVD player and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

They also have a bathroom with a hairdryer and a shower. Each accommodation type includes a kitchen with kitchenware, a microwave and a fridge.

An oven, a stove and a toaster are also provided. Gîte de la Haute Ville is 500 metres from Boulogne-sur-Mer Tintelleries Train Station and 1.
