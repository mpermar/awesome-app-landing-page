Description

Located 19 km from the Opal Coast in northern France, Hotel du Moulin aux Draps offers free private parking.

WiFi access is provided in each of the bright guest rooms. A flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bath and shower are provided in all rooms.

They all have a mini fridge. A view of the garden or the river, forest and the mill is offered from each room.

The hotel sits on 6 hectares of grassland, which opens to a clearing leading to the Desvres Forest.

The sun terrace is next to the river and provides a panoramic view of the surrounding area.

Breakfast is available upon request with an extra charge. Guests can enjoy local products with a 3 course dinner at the hotel’s restaurant upon prioir request.
