Description

Najeti Hôtel Du Parc features modern rooms with a view. The rooms are bright and spacious, with a stylish and creative decor. Some feature a balcony or terrace opening onto the park or forest. Free WiFi is available in all areas.

Breakfast buffet is served every morning at a surcharge. The restaurant and lounge offer fine dining using traditional French recipes. The piano bar, Le Saint Laurent, is open every day and you can choose a cocktail from the selection on offer from the barman.

Free private parking is provided on site. Boulogne-sur-Mer is 8.7 mi from Najeti Hôtel Du Parc, while le Touquet Paris Plage is 13 mi from the hotel.
