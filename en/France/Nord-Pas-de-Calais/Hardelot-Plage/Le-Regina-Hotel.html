Description

Situated in the pine tree forest of Hardelot, the Régina welcomes you in a verdant and peaceful setting near one of the many beaches of the Côte d’Opale.

Le Régina Hotel features 42 comfortable, spacious and well-appointed rooms, a lift and a private car park.

The restaurant, Le Woods, presents an extensive menu and welcomes you in a cosy atmosphere.
