Description

One of our top picks in Le Touquet-Paris-Plage.Hôtel Barrière Le Westminster is a 1930’s luxury hotel in the heart of Le Touquet. It is just 1,650 feet from the beach and offers spacious, Art Deco guestrooms. Guests have free access to the heated indoor swimming pool, the sauna and the hot tub and can relax at the spa and wellness center, both accessible at an extra cost.

Hôtel Barrière Le Westminster features 115 Art Deco rooms with an elegant interior design and a view of the forest or lighthouse. All rooms are nonsmoking. WiFi access is available in all rooms and public areas.

Guests are invited to enjoy fine cuisine in the gastronomic Michelin-starred restaurant Le Pavillon or the brasserie, Les Cimaises.

The Nuxe Spa has 5 treatment rooms, one of which is for 2 people. It offers a range of treatments and massages for the body and the face.

Private parking is possible on site at an extra cost per night and charging stations for electric cars are available at this property. Casino de la Forêt is 1,500 feet away.
