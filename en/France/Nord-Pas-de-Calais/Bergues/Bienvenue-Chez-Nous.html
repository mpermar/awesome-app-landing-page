Description

Located 200 metres from the Belfry in the town of Bergues, Bienvenue Chez Nous is also only 3 km from Dunkirk Golf Club and 20 km from Cassel.

A buffet breakfast with fresh pastries and fruit juices is served every morning. Guest room feature an ornamental fireplace, parquet flooring and antique furniture.

Each has an LCD TV and free Wi-Fi access, and some of the en suite rooms also offer a view of the Belfry.

Breakfast is served in the dining room, which is decorated with a chandelier. You can enjoy traditional French dishes including foie gras in the on-site restaurant, which is open daily.

Bergues Train Station and the Museum of Mont de Piété are both only 500 metres from the property.
