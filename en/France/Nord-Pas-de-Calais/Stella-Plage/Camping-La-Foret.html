Description

Camping La Foret is ideally located just 1 km from a sandy beach and only 500 metres from the shops.

A local market is open daily during the high season. Le Touquet Paris-Plage is only 7 km away.

Each unit is fully equipped with a TV, bathroom and terrace with garden chairs. The kitchenette is equipped with a microwave.

Each plot has a private parking space. La Foret offers a bowls ground, a children’s play area and a games room with a pool table, table tennis and table football.

Bike hire and Nordic walking poles are available. WiFi connection is provided throughout the camp site, for an extra fee.

A bakery service is available as well as a selection of regional produce. Guests can also benefit from reduced-priced tickets for attraction parks nearby.

Within 500 metres of La Foret guests can find a sauna and squash and tennis courts.
