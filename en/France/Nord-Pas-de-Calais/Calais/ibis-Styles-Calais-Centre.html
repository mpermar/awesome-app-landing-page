Description

The ibis Styles Calais Centre is situated in the centre of Calais, just 500 metres from Calais-Ville Train Station and 10.

5 km from the Channel Tunnel. The hotel offers a 24-hour reception and rooms with free Wi-Fi.

Private parking is available on-site at an extra cost. A buffet-style breakfast is served every morning in the breakfast room under the glass roof.

It is served from 06:00 until 10:00 from Monday to Friday and from 06:00 to 11:00 during the weekend.

Meeting facilities are available on site. The hotel is a 10-minute drive from Cap Blanc Nez beaches and 5 km from the Car Ferry Terminal.
