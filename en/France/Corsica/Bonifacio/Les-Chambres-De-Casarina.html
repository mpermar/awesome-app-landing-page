Description

Located in Casarina Village holiday park, 6 km from Bonifacio city centre, Les Chambres De Casarina offers a seasonal heated outdoor pool and views of the sea.

Former Chapel of the Trinity is 7 km from the property and local beaches are 15 minutes' drive away.

Set over 2 buildings, the rooms include a flat-screen TV, minibar, coffee machine and terrace.

For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer. A continental breakfast of hot drinks, fruit juice, bread, mini pastries, jam and yoghurt is served directly in the guest's room and there is babysitting service at the property.

Guests also have access to a boules pitch. You can play tennis near the guest house.

Lavezzi Islands is 8 km from Les Chambres De Casarina, while King Aragon Steps is 5 km away.

The nearest airport is Figari-Sud Corse Airport, 24 km from Les Chambres De Casarina.
