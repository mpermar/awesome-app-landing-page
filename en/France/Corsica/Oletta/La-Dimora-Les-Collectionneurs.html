Description

Situated in a former farmhouse 5. 8 km from Saint-Florent, this hotel features a spa, an outdoor swimming pool and a luxurious garden.

Free Wi-Fi, free private parking, a sauna and a hammam are available on site. Individually decorated with elegant furniture, each of the air-conditioned rooms at La Dimora includes a private bathroom with bathrobe, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

A minibar and an LCD TV with satellite channels are provided. A buffet breakfast is served daily and a bar offers snacks and drinks.

Set in the former sheepfold, the restaurant serves meals every day for lunch and dinner during the high season.

You are invited to relax in the spa, which includes a Turkish bath, spa bath and body treatments, or read a book in the shaded poolside lounge area.

Désert des Agriates beaches and Port Saint Florent are a 7-minute drive away while Bastia is a 34-minute drive away.
