Description

Hôtel Marinca & Spa is located in southern Corsica, a 13-minute drive from Propriano.

This 5-star property is situated near local beaches and overlooks the Valinco Gulf of the Mediterranean Sea.

Guests can swim in the 4 onsite pools and sunbathe on the private beach. This 5-star hotel offers individually decorated rooms and suites.

Each has free WiFi, a flat-screen TV, air-conditioning and a bathroom with bathtub and shower.

All rooms or suites have a private balcony with a sea view. La Verrière restaurant serves a gourmet cuisine, prepared by the awarded chef, and offers set menus.

From June to September the Paillote beach restaurant is open every lunch time and offers grilled specialties.

Guests can relax in the spa and wellness centre with a steam room. Massage and wellness treatments are also available.
