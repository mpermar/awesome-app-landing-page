Description

Located in the south-west of Corsica, this 3-star resort is in the heart of Valinco Gulf, a 5-minute drive from the nearest beach.

The park features 2 outdoor pools, a larger non-heated pool and a smaller pool which is heated during the low season.

Guests can enjoy sea views from both pools. The modern chalets feature a dining area and a furnished terrace with barbecue facilities.

Each chalet has a fully equipped kitchen with a microwave, fridge and a coffee maker.

Sheets and towels are available at a surcharge. During July and August, Vigna Maggiore features a snack bar as well as a restaurant, open for dinner every night and for brunch on Sunday, serving pizzas and light Mediterranean cuisine using vegetables from the property's garden.

There is also an on-site grocer and boutique open during this period. Guests will also find a children's playground, table tennis and outdoor fitness facilities.

WiFi access is available for free in public areas. In 2019, an adults-only wellness space, a meeting room, and a new boutique food shop will open.

This property is accessible via the T40 and the D157 road. It is less than a 10-minute drive from Propriano.

The Propriano ferry terminal is 4 km away and both Ajaccio and Figari airports are 60 km from the resort.
