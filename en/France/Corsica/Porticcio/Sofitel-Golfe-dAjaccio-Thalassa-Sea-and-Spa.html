Description

Stunningly situated facing Ajaccio Bay and the Sanguinaires Islands in Corsica, the Sofitel Golfe d'Ajaccio Thalassa Sea & Spa features a heated outdoor seawater pool open all year round.

With its unrestricted view of the sea and rocks, the hotel boasts a small private beach, where guests can sunbathe.

The hotel has charming, sunlit rooms decorated with wood. The air-conditioned rooms are equipped with Wi-Fi access, satellite TV and they have a private bathroom.

La Carte Postale restaurant offers a variety of delicious and healthy options. The chef has created a gourmet menu, full of varied international and local flavours.
