Description

Best Western Ajaccio Amirauté features sea and port views and is close to the town centre and the Ajaccio Airport.

Fully renovated in April 2015, it features an exterior swimming pool, heated from Easter to All Saints Day, a Swimspa, heated to 32°C all year round, and a sun terrace.

The guest rooms feature modern decor, a safe, tea/coffee facilities, air conditioning and a flat-screen TV.

Certain rooms offer views of the sea. Facilities/services offered by the hotel include a bar, a restaurant, free and unlimited WiFi, a secure and private garage, bicycle racks, a motorbike tool kit, and business and meeting facilities.

Upon reservation, a baby cot, a push chair and a bottle warmer are available. Breakfast is available on site, serving Corsican specialties.

The hotel prides itself on high quality service. It is also suitably equipped to accommodate guests with reduced mobility.

Calvi – Sainte-Catherine Airport is 154 km from Best Western Plus Ajaccio Amirauté and Figari-Sud Corse Airport is 120 km away.
