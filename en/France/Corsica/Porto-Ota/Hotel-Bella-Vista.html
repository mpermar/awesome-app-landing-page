Description

Situated midway between Calvi and Ajaccio, this charming hotel is located in Porto, a city classified as a World Heritage site by UNESCO.

It features a bar and a garden. Tucked away from the centre of the resort, Hôtel Bella Vista features comfortable guest rooms, all equipped with a television and air conditioning.

Free Wi-Fi connection is available. Ideally situated between Scandola Gulf and the Orto Capo 1294 metres high, Porto offers numerous outdoor and water leisure activities.

Hôtel Bella Vista is located a one and a half-hour drive from Sainte Catherine Airport.
