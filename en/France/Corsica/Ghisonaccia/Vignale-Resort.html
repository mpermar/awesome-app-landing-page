Description

Offering an outdoor pool and a seasonal restaurant and bar on the beachfront, Vignale Resort is a holiday park located on the beachfront in Ghisonaccia.

It features a heated outdoor pool. WiFi access is available at an extra cost. The accommodation will provide you with a furnished terrace and a seating area with a TV.

There is a fully equipped kitchen with a microwave and a refrigerator, and the private bathroom has a bath or shower.

At Vignale Resort you will find a seasonal spa, a garden and BBQ facilities. Evening entertainment is offered at the property and meeting facilities are available for guests.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling and hiking.

A multi-sports ground and cross fit are at guests' disposal throughout the year. This holiday park is 90 minutes' drive from Bastia - Figari Aiport and offers free parking.
