Description

Offering an outdoor pool and a restaurant, U SORTIPIANI is located in Corte. Free WiFi access is available in this hotel.

The private bathrooms come with a shower. You can enjoy mountain views from the rooms.

Meals can be taken indoors or outdoors at the hotel’s restaurant. At U SORTIPIANI you will find a garden and barbecue facilities.

Farm animals can be seen on site. Other facilities offered at the property include a shared lounge and a games room.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including fishing and hiking.
