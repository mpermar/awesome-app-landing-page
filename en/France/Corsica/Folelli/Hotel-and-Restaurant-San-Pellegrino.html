Description

Located on the beach, Hotel San Pellegrino offers 2 tennis courts, volleyball and a games room.

Bastia is a 43-minute drive away and Folelli is 3 km from the hotel. All units are air-conditioned and features a terrace, a safety deposit box, a sofa and a wardrobe.

The en suite bathrooms include a hairdryer and free toiletries and some pavilions offer an equipped kitchen.

Beach towels, beach umbrellas and sun loungers are available at extra cost. A buffet breakfast is available each morning at the hotel and guests can enjoy a quick snack from the beach bar.

Traditional cuisine is served in the restaurant each day and the dinner menu includes a starter, dish, cheese and dessert.

A vending machine with hot drinks can also be found on site. Casamozza is a 20-minute drive away and Poretta Airport is only 18 km from the hotel.

Additional features include massage treatments on site, table tennis, beach games and laundry facilities.
