Description

La Bergerie is a hotel converted from a 20th century farm, situated on the peaks of Ile-Rousse in Corsica.

It boasts a panoramic view of the town, and an outdoor swimming pool. Free WiFi is included.

The rooms offer both heating and air-conditioning and a private bathroom with bath or shower.

All have a view of either the garden, pool or mountains. Guests can watch TV in the shared lounge.

The daily breakfast includes croissants, breads, jam, hot beverages and juices. The onsite seasonal restaurant serves Mediterranean dishes and seafood.

With the sea just 800 m away, La Bergerie is ideally situated for the beaches. The train station of Ile-Rousse is 1 km away.
