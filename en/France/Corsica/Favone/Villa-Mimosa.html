Description

At the bed and breakfast, rooms are equipped with a closet. Each room comes with air conditioning, and some rooms have a terrace. All guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

Guests at Villa Mimosa can enjoy a continental breakfast.

Snorkeling, cycling and fishing are among the activities that guests of the accommodation can find nearby.

Porto-Vecchio is 14 miles from Villa Mimosa. The nearest airport is Sud Corse Airport, 25 miles from the bed and breakfast.
