Description

This seafront residence is located in Coti Chiavari in South Corsica, 800 metres from la Plage d'Argent beach and 200 metres from a a small creek.

It offers an outdoor pool with a fully furnished terrace, and free Wi-Fi. Each air-conditioned apartment and studio at the Résidence Aigue Marine features a fully equipped kitchenette, a flat-screen TV and a lounge area.

All apartments have a private balcony with views of the mountains. There is a large garden for guests to relax in and a children’s playground.

Activities and sights in the nearby area, include hiking and water sports and the residence is 300 metres from the shopping street.

The Résidence Aigue Marine is 12 km from Ajaccio and Ajaccio-Napoleon Bonaparte airport, and 17km from Ajaccio harbour.
