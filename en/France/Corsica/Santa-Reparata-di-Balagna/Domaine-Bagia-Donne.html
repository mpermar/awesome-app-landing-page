Description

Located 3 km from the centre of Ile Rousse, Domaine Bagia Donne features panoramic views of the sea and the surrounding village.

This resort has WiFi access in the reception area. The property provides self-catering studios, apartments and villas, all of them with air conditioning.

Equipped with towels and linen, each accommodation has a fully equipped kitchen, a living room and a bathroom.

Guests staying in an apartment also have access to a shared outdoor pool. The villas will provide you with a garden, a swimming pool, BBQ facilities and a terrace.

Bars, restaurants, night clubs and supermarkets can be found in the centre of Ile Rousse.

Water sports, hiking and trekking can be done in the surrounding area. Calvi is 20 km away, Bastia is 80 km away and Ajaccio is 100 km away.
