Description

Hotel Paesotel is set in Venaco, just a 15-minute drive south of Corte. It offers an outdoor swimming pool, a tennis court and a spa.

Views of the garden, mountains or the Vecchio River are included in all of the rooms at Hotel Paesotel.

They are decorated in a contemporary style and also provide an LCD TV with satellite channels.

Breakfast is served in the comfort of guest rooms, in the dining room or on the terrace.

You can also relax in the rustic-style bar before sampling traditional cuisine in the on-site restaurant.

Guests can use the on site hot tub and sauna for an extra cost. Tennis rackets are available to rent.

Free Wi-Fi is provided in public areas and free private parking is possible on site.

Other local activities include driving the 20 minutes to Restonica Valley or going canoeing on the nearby Vecchio River.
