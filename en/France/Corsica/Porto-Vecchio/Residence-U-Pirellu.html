Description

Résidence U Pirellu is located 7 km south of Porto Vecchio in Corsica. It is a 5-minute drive from Palombaggia beach and offers a hot tub and an outdoor swimming pool, which is heated during off-season.

The air-conditioned villas and studios at Résidence U Pirellu have a living room and free Wi-Fi access.

The fully equipped kitchen includes a washing machine, dishwasher, fridge and freezer.

Each also has a private terrace with barbeque facilities and sea views. A continental breakfast is served every morning.

Résidence U Pirellu offers a mini golf course and a children’s playground. The residence is a 30-minute drive from Porto Vecchio Forest and offers free on-site parking.
