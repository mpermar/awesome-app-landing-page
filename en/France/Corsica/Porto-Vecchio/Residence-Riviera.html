Description

An outdoor swimming pool and a terrace with deck chairs and views of the mountains are offered at this residence.

Free WiFi access is available throughout the property. The property is set within a park in Lecci, a 15-minute drive from Porto-Vecchio and the beach.

Residence Riviera proposes air-conditioned villas with a private garden, an en suite bathroom and a TV with a DVD player.

They each include a washing machine and ironing facilities. Each villa also features a kitchen area including hot plates, microwave, refrigerator and freezer.

BBQ facilities are at guests' disposal in each private garden. A washing machine is available.

Free private parking is provided on site. The residence is 32 km from Figari, 35.
