Description

Offering an outdoor pool and a children’s playground, Campo Di Mare - Corsicatours is located in a natural park in Porto-Vecchio.

Free WiFi access is available in the public areas. The air-conditioned mobile homes feature a seating area and a terrace.

There is a fully equipped kitchen with a microwave and a refrigerator. The private bathroom has a shower.

At Campo Di Mare you will find a garden, a boules pitch and a table tennis table.

Other facilities offered at the property include a shared lounge. If you feel like visiting the surroundings, check out Palombaggia Beach (7 km).

This chalet is 143 km from Ajaccio – Napoléon Bonaparte Airport. The property offers free parking at the entrance of the park, which cannot be accessed by car.
