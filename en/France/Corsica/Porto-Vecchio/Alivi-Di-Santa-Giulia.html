Description

Alivi Di Santa Giulia is a boutique hotel offering panoramic views of the Bay of Santa Giulia.

It has a spacious outdoor swimming pool and guest rooms with terraces or private gardens.

Free WiFi is available throughout and there is a courtesy shower and living room that can be used in case of early arrival or late departure.

Each of the air-conditioned guest rooms at the Alivi Di Santa Giulia has a view of the sea or the surrounding scrubland, and is equipped with satellite TV and a minibar.

Guests can enjoy gourmet cuisine at the restaurant U Santa Marina located 800 metres away, in the dining room or on one of the outdoor terraces, overlooking the Mediterranean sea.

Breakfast is served every morning from 08. 00 - 11. 00 and can be enjoyed either in your room or by the swimming pool in the warmer months.

The hotel is located in the south of Corsica, 8 km from Porto Vecchio Bay and a 20-minute drive from Bonifacio.

Figari-Sud Airport is a 30-minute drive away. Free private parking is available on site.
