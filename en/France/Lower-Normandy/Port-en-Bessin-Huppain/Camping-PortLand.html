Description

Located in Port-en-Bessin-Huppain, Camping Port-Land offers an indoor and outdoor swimming pool, a garden with 4 fishing ponds and a sports ground.

Omaha Landing Beach and the American Cemetery are both 6 km away. All mobile homes feature a terrace, seating area with a sofa and a bathroom with a shower, while some also have a TV and fishing pond views.

The kitchen includes a stove, microwave and refrigerator. A continental breakfast is available each morning and is served in the bar or restaurant.

Guests can also enjoy a quick bite or pizza from the snack stand and order fresh bread the evening before.

Additional features include a games room, free Wi-Fi access in the public areas and free private parking on site.

Bayeux is just 10 km away and Vierville-sur-Mer is a 15-minute drive from the camp site.
