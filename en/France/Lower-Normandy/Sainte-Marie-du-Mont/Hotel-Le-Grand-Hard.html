Description

Located in Sainte-Marie-du-Mont, Le Grand Hard is 3.1 mi from Utah Beach and 25 mi from Omaha Beach. It offers free Wi-Fi and a shared lounge with a fireplace.

All the heated rooms feature a wardrobe, a desk and satellite TV. The private bathroom is complete with free toiletries, a bath and a hairdryer. Rooms are suitable for guests with reduced mobility.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at Le Grand Hard. The family room includes a kitchenette, and a bar is available at the property.

This hotel is 5.6 mi from Sainte-Mère-Église and 30 mi from Saint-Lô. Guests can bring their own horses to this property. Free private parking and bike rental are both available on site.
