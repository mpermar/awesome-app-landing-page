Description

Located in the heart of Normandy, just a 20-minute drive from Mont-Saint-Michel, this B&B is housed in an early 19th-century building and is surrounded by a 1-hectare park.

Guest rooms offer a view of the garden and are decorated in a classic style with antique furniture.

Each room has a wardrobe and an en suite bathroom offering a hairdryer and complimentary toiletries upon arrival.

A continental breakfast is served each morning at the B&B. Upon request, guests can sample typical French dishes in the evening, prepared by their hosts.

Guests can relax in the B&B’s lounge where free Wi-Fi internet access is available.
