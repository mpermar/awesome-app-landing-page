Description

Set in a stone building dating from the 19th century, Les Villas d'Arromanches, The Originals Relais is located 100 metres from Arromanches in the Normandy region.

It is just 200 metres from the D-day beaches. It offers free parking and free internet access.

Guest rooms at Les Villas d'Arromanches, The Originals Relais have a TV, work desk and en suite facilities.

A buffet breakfast is served daily at the hotel and you can relax at the lounge bar.

Les Villas d'Arromanches, The Originals Relais is also 10 km from the Bayeux Tapestry and Port-en-Bessin golf course.
