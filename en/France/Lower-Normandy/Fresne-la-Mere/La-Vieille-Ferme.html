Description

Set in a 18th-century farm house, this bed and breakfast is 5 km from Falaise. It offers free Wi-Fi access in public areas and free hot beverages in the lounge, just a 40-minute drive from Caen.

A seating area and views of the garden and courtyard are offered in the rooms at La Vieille Ferme.

All have an en suite bathroom and some rooms have original wooden beams. Continental breakfast is provided every morning in the dining room.

The hosts can also prepare local dishes from home-grown produce and children’s meals upon request.

You can watch French and English TV or DVDs in the lounge or play on the trampoline in the children's garden.

If you have a car, private parking is free on site, Camembert is a 20-minute drive away and Argentan Train Station is 26 km away.
