Description

Located between Saint Malo and Granville, this hotel is just 4. 8 km from the centre of Avranches.

It offers en suite rooms with satellite TV and free Wi-Fi internet access. Each room at ibis Avranches Baie du Mont St Michel has a large desk and a private bathroom.

Rooms for guests with reduced mobility are also available. A buffet breakfast composed of sweet and savoury dishes such as eggs, fruit salad, yogurts and juices is served every day.

Pastries baked on site and fresh French Madeleine cakes are also on offer, as well as a hot beverage and a piece of fruit to take away.

Outside of regular breakfast hours guests can also enjoy a lighter option, available from 04:00.

Room service is also available so that guests can enjoy dinner in the comfort of their room.

Free private parking is provided at the Hotel ibis Avranches Mont St Michel. The hotel is 17 km from Mont Saint-Michel.
