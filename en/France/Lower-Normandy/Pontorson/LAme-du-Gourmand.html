Description

Offering a seasonal heated outdoor pool, L'Âme du Gourmand is located in Pontorson, 9 km from Mont-Saint-Michel.

This traditional-style guest house features a private garden and a terrace. Bikes are available on site and free Wi-Fi access is available.

Located on the upper floors, the uniquely decorated rooms here will provide you with a flat-screen TV.

Featuring a hairdryer, private bathrooms also come with towels. A continental breakfast is provided daily.

Other facilities offered include a shared lounge with coffee facilities. The property is 20 km from Dol-de-Bretagne and 45 km from Saint Malo and a free car park can be found at a location nearby.
