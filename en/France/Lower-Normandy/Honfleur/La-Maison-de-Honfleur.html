Description

Set in a renovated house from the 18th century, this B&B is located in Honfleur. It features free Wi-Fi access, free bicycles and you can also watch DVDs for free.

Accessible via a steep staircase and with a parquet floor, all the rooms at La Maison de Honfleur are decorated in an elegant style with oak furniture and have a queen-sized bed, flat-screen TV, DVD player and tea and coffee making facilities.

Each room also includes a private bathroom with a bath and free toiletries. Breakfast are served in the guest rooms.

Every morning, outside your door, you will find a basket with French pastries and fresh bread.

The fridge is provided with fruit juice, water, butter, organic yogurt, cheese, milk and homemade jam.

The room also includes an electric kettle and different types of tea, and a coffee machine.

A shared kitchen is also available to guests. There are many shops near the B&B. Guests can relax in the living room by the fireplace with a complimentary tea or coffee at any time of the day.
