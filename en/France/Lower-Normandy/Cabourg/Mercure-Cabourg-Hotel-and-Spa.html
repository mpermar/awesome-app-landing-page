Description

Located behind Cabourg Racecourse, Mercure Cabourg Hôtel & Spa is 1 km from the centre of Cabourg and a 20-minute walk from the beach and casino.

It offers a fitness room, a swimming pool and a sauna. Free Wi-Fi is provided. All the air-conditioned rooms feature a flat-screen TV, a telephone and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

Some of them are suitable for guests with reduced mobility. A daily breakfast can be enjoyed at an extra cost and the on-site bar is open from 21:00 to 01:00.

With a 24-hour front desk, this hotel has 1 meeting rooms. Free private parking is provided.
