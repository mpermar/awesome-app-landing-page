Description

Located in Cabourg with direct access to the beach, Village Vacances Sweet Home offers a seasonal outdoor swimming pool, mini golf and a solarium.

The city centre is only a 15-minute walk away and Houlgate is 10 km from the holiday park.

Rooms feature free WiFi, TV, wardrobe, desk and some have views of the park or the beach The en suite bathroom includes a hairdryer, while the cottages also have a fully equipped kitchen and private terrace.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at Village Vacances Sweet Home.

Guests can also relax with a drink in the bar and sample the local cuisine in the restaurant.

Additional features include table tennis, a children’s playground, a library and football pitch.

Deauville is a 30-minute drive away and Golf de Cabourg is only 1. 3 km from the property.
