Description

Hotel Le Trouville is located in Trouville town centre, 200 metres from the beach.

It offers a welcome lobby. The Trouville Barrière Casino is 500 metres away and Trouville-Deauville Train Station is 1.

4 km away. Free WiFi is provided. All rooms come with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

Guests can start the day with a choice of express or continental breakfast. Hotel Le Trouville is 15 kilometres from the medieval town of Honfleur.

For guests coming by car, a private garage is available at an extra charge and upon availability (reservation is needed).
