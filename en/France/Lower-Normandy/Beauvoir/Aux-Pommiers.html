Description

Offering an outdoor swimming pool, Aux Pommiers is located in Beauvoir, just 4 km from Mont Saint Michel Abbey.

Provided with free Wi-Fi, this camping provides a mini golf course, a billiard table and a tennis table.

Each room will provide you with a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom with hairdryer and free toiletries and a terrace.

Extras include a desk and outdoor furniture. At Aux Pommiers you will find a bar and a snack bar.

Other facilities offered at the property include entertainment staff, a shared lounge and a games room.
