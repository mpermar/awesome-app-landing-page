Description

Located in the centre of Cherbourg, Appart Titanic offers 4 modern, self-catering apartments with a Titanic theme just 500 metres from La Cit de la Mer, the Pont Tournant and the pedestrian streets.

Free Wi-Fi access is provided. The apartments feature a living area with a flat-screen TV and DVD player and a bedroom with a bathroom.

Garden views are offered from the bedroom. Satin cotton sheets and organic cotton towels are provided, and beds are made prior to arrival.

Free toiletries and a hairdryer are also supplied. The kitchen includes an oven, stove, fridge and dishwasher and there is a wardrobe in the corridor.

Luggage storage service is available upon prior request, for an extra charge. Grocery shops, a butchers and a bakery can be found only 100 metres away and Collignon Beach is 4 km away.

Cherbourg Train Station is 1 km away and free public parking is possible at a location nearby.
