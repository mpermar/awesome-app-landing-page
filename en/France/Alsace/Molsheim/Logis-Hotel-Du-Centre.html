Description

Situated in the historical centre of Molsheim on the Alsace wine trail, this charming hotel features a floral garden with a patio.

Most of the guest rooms at Logis Hotel Du Centre are soundproofed and decorated with neutral and bright colours.

They all come with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Guests have free WiFi access throughout the hotel.

Every morning a buffet breakfast is served and includes gluten and lactose-free produce.

Guests can enjoy this in the garden on the terrace. There are many hiking trails surrounding the hotel.

Logis Hotel Du Centre is 15 km from Mont Sainte-Odile and 25 km from Strasbourg and its Christmas Market.
