Description

Le Relais du Ried is located in the Alsatian village of Bischwihr, a 10-minute drive from the centre of Colmar.

It is surrounded by a floral garden and guests have free access to the sauna, hammam, trampoline, swings and garden furniture.

Traditional cuisine and regional gourmet specialties are served in the hotel’s restaurant.

The hotel also features a bar, which is open in the evenings. At Le Relais du Ried, there is a games room with table tennis.

The relaxation area is open from 16:00 to 22:00, massages are available upon request, and children can enjoy the hotel’s playground.

Guests can enjoy visits to the nearby Alsatian vineyards and wine cellars. Other leisure activities in the area include hiking and cycling.
