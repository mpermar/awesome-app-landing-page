Description

Situated in Le Bonhomme in north-eastern France, this Logis Hotel is surrounded by fir trees and hiking paths.

It features an indoor pool and sauna. Lac Blanc is 13 km from the property. Guest rooms at the Logis Hotel de la Poste are equipped with satellite TV and free Wi-Fi access.

They are serviced by a lift and have a private bathroom. The Poste Hotel's restaurant serves traditional cuisine with dishes and wines from the Alsatian region.

Guests can relax on the terrace which is furnised with lounge chairs and leads onto the garden.
