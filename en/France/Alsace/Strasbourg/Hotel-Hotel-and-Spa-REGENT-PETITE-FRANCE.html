Description

Hôtel Régent Petite France & Spa is located in the center of Strasbourg, in the heart of the Petite France district of the city. It is set in a converted 17th-century mill and offers an on-site restaurant and a wellness center with a sauna, hammam, hot tub and massage treatments. A WiFi internet connection is available in entire hotel and private parking is available upon reservation.

Each of the rooms with attached bathroom at Hôtel Régent Petite France & Spa offer a view on the picturesque neighborhood of La Petite France, either overlooking the river Ill or the traditional half-timbered houses of Alsace. Rooms are air-conditioned and equipped with a private bathroom, satellite TV and a mini-bar.

Guests have a choice of a buffet breakfast served in the lounge or a continental breakfast served in their room. The hotel’s restaurant “Le Pont Tourant” serves innovative cuisine and guests can also enjoy a drink in the “Champagne Bar” offering over 20 types of champagne and a snack menu for those looking for a quick bite to eat.

Hôtel Régent Petite France & Spa is 0.6 mi from Strasbourg Central Train Station. Strasbourg Cathedral and the The Alsatian Museum are a 10-minute walk from the hotel.
