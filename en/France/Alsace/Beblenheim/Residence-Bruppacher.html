Description

Featuring free WiFi and a seasonal outdoor pool, Résidence Bruppacher offers pet-friendly accommodation in Beblenheim.

Private parking is available on site for an extra charge. All heated rooms at this bed and breakfast come with a TV and en suite facilities.

Certain units feature a seating area for your convenience. You can play table tennis at this bed and breakfast, and the area is popular for hiking.

Europa Park features 72 attractions and is just 46 km away. Colmar is 9 km from Résidence Bruppacher, while Freiburg im Breisgau is 43 km away.

Riquewihr is a 6-minute drive from the property and Ribeauville is a 10-minute drive away.

The nearest airport is Strasbourg International Airport, 48 km from Résidence Bruppacher.
