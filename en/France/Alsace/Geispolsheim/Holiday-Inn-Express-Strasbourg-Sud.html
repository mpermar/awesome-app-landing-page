Description

Located in green surroundings, this hotel is a 20-minute drive from the centre of Strasbourg and its European institutions.

The hotel welcomes you a 10-minute drive from the German border and offers an on-site outdoor swimming pool.

All rooms feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, free WiFi access and tea and coffee making facilities.

The private bathroom includes a shower and hairdryer. Free breakfast is served every morning to all of the guests.

Guests can also dine in the hotel lounge bar. Additional features include a secure private car park large enough for coaches, a sunny terrace and a business centre.

Holiday Inn Express Strasbourg Sud is only a 15-minute drive from the Wine Route and a 35-minute drive from Europa Park.

The hotel sales entrance tickets for Europapark at the reception desk. It is a 10-minute drive from Strasbourg Airport and only 500 metres from the N83.
