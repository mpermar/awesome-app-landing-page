Description

Ibis Styles Colmar Nord is located north of Colmar, overlooking the Vosges Mountains.

The hotel has an outdoor swimming pool and air-conditioned guestrooms with LCD satellite TV and Wi-Fi.

The hotel also has a playground. The cafe serves modern gourmet cuisine in a contemporary setting, with terrace overlooking the Vosges Mountains.

Ibis Styles Colmar Nord is 5 kilometres north of Colmar’s old town, near the A35 motorway.

The hotel is a short walk from the Aéroport Colmar Houssen and the business park.
