Description

Located in Saverne, this 3-star hotel is a 5-minute drive from the city centre and a 10-minute walk from Chateau de Rohan.

Relaxation and fitness facilities include an outdoor swimming pool, a hot tub, a sauna and a massage room.

The rooms at Villa Katz are elegant in style. Each includes a seating area featuring an LCD TV and free toiletries in the en suite bathroom.

Some of the rooms have a private balcony with views of the garden. The continental breakfast is served in the comfort of guest rooms or on the terrace, which leads onto the garden.

You can also enjoy a drink in the bar and dine at the on-site restaurant. You can relax in the lounge and read from the library.

Free Wi-Fi access is available in public areas and private parking is also free on site.
