Description

Set in a property originally built in 1845, Maison d'hôte Aux Deux Cigognes is a typically Alsatian house located in Gries, 27 km from Strasbourg and 8 km from Haguenau.

It offers free WiFi, a terrace and an outside relaxation area with a slide. This property provides an apartment as well as suites.

Each suite has its own private bathroom. The apartment features a living room, two bedrooms and a bathroom.

It also comes with an kitchen equipped with a dishwasher, oven, coffee machine. Towels and bed linen are offered in the apartment and in the rooms.

Breakfast is delivered to your door on a tray every morning. Guests can benefit from Table d'hôtes on site and enjoy fresh and homemade products.

Baden-Baden is 31 km from Aux Deux Cigognes, while Baiersbronn is 47 km away.

Kurtzenhouse Train Station is 1 km from the property and the nearest airport is Entzheim Airport, 27 km away.
