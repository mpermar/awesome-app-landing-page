Description

This half-timbered building provides en suite accommodation on Alsace’s famous wine route.

It also features a regional restaurant. The soundproofed rooms at Hôtel-restaurant Au Soleil are simply decorated and have views of the garden.

Free Wi-Fi access and a private bathroom with shower are provided in each room. A buffet breakfast is served every morning.

Alsatian specialities are featured on the restaurant’s menu. Obernai Train Station is 6 km from Hôtel-restaurant Au Soleil.

Château du Haut Koenigsbourg is 37 km away and Europapark amusement park is 40 km away.
