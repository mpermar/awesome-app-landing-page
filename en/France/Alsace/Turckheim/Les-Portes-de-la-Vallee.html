Description

Located along the Alsace Wine Route, a 10-minute walk from the centre of Turckheim, this hotel offers free Wi-Fi and an on-site restaurant and bar.

Guests can borrow bicycles for free during their stay. The guest rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a work desk.

Each room offers an en suite bathroom with a hairdryer and complimentary toiletries.

Breakfast is served each morning at the hotel and can be taken in guests’ rooms upon request.

The restaurant offers traditional Alsatian cuisine and wines. With free private parking on site the hotel is located 1.

4 km from Wintzenheim and 3. 2 km from Ingersheim. Colmar is a 15-minute drive from the hotel.
