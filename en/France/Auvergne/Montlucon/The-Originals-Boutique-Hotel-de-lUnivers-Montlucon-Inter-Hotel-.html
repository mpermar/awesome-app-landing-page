Description

Located in a building dating from 1900, The Originals Boutique, Hôtel de l'Univers, Montluçon (Inter-Hotel) is located in Montluçon city centre on the famous Marx Dormoy street.

Canal+ and free WiFi are provided in the rooms. Air conditioning is available throughout the property.

Rooms at The Originals Boutique, Hôtel de l'Univers, Montluçon (Inter-Hotel) have an LCD TV with French and international channels, a telephone and a desk.

The bathroom is equipped with a hairdryer, shower or bath and family rooms are also available.

A continental breakfast is served every morning, which needs to be reserved at reception, and can be enjoyed in the guest's room for an extra cost.

Staff will be pleased to give suggestions about restaurants in Montluçon. Bikes can be rented on site and secure bike parking is available for free.

Wilson Gardens are a 5-minute walk from The Originals Boutique, Hôtel de l'Univers, Montluçon (Inter-Hotel) and the Medieval citadel is within walking distance from the property.
