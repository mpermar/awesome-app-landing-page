Description

Situated in a 15-hectare park, the Chateau de Codignat overlooks the village of Bort L’Etang.

It features a heated outdoor swimming pool. The air-conditioned guest rooms decorated in opulent 16th-century style.

Each spacious room features unique, antique furnishings such as chandeliers and four-poster beds.

Modern comforts including flat-screen TV, free Wi-Fi access and a whirlpool bath are also provided.

All rooms have views of the mature garden. A continental breakfast is available daily at Chateau Codignat.

Gastronomic cuisine and wines from the Auvergne region are served in the hotel’s richly decorated restaurant.

Guests are invited to explore the park on bicycles provided by the chateau. There is also a tennis court and an open-air fitness trail.

Livradois-Forez Regional Park is 10 km from the hotel. Clermont-Ferrand-Aulnat airport is 20 km away.
