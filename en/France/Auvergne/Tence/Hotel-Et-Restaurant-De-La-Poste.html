Description

Offering a children's playground and views of the garden, Hotel Et Restaurant De La Poste is located in Tence in the Auvergne Region, 37 km from Saint-Étienne.

The hotel has a sun terrace and views of the city, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Free WiFi is provided throughout the property. Each room comes with a flat-screen TV.

Some units feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day. There is a shared lounge at the property.

You can play table tennis at the hotel. The hotel also offers bike hire. Le Puy en Velay is 33 km from Hotel Et Restaurant De La Poste, while Saint-Bonnet-le-Froid is 12 km away.
