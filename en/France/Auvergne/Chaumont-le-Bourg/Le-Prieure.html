Description

Le Prieuré is a renovated monastery dating from the 10th century with a 1. 7-hectare park, set in Livradois Forez Regional Park.

It offers free access to the seasonal outdoor pool, and a homemade breakfast is prepared daily.

Featuring antique furniture, the guest rooms are individually decorated and each has a flat-screen TV.

The en suite bathrooms include a hairdryer and some also have a corner bathtub. Seasonal fruit, homemade jams and local cheeses are served for breakfast at this hotel.

Traditional French cuisine is prepared for dinner in the on-site restaurant. Additional facilities include the swimming pool that is heated by solar panels and the sun terrace overlooking the garden.

You can also play table tennis and mini-golf for free. Moulin Richard de Bas Museum and the town of Ambert are both 12 km from the hotel.
