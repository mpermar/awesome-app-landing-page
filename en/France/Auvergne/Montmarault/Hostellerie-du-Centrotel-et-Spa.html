Description

Featuring a sun terrace, Hostellerie du Centrotel et Spa is located in Montmarault.

Exit 11 of Motorway A71 is just 800 metres from the hotel and Montluçon is 35 km away.

The rooms feature air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a desk and a wardrobe.

The private bathroom has a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer. A buffet breakfast with local produce is served every morning.

The hotel’s restaurant offers traditional, French cuisine for dinner and boasts exposed wooden beams.

An extensive wine list is available. Guests have access to a wellness centre with a sauna and hammam for an additional charge.

Meeting facilities and free WiFi are available at Centrotel. Free private parking is provided and covered parking is also possible with an extra cost.
