Description

The hotel is ideally situated in the heart of the Auvergne region, the countryside between Clermont Ferrand and Le Puy en Velay.

La Crèche offers a warm and friendly welcome. Guests can access the fitness centre and the covered swimming pool free of charge.

La Crèche offers Spacious and comfortable rooms with a simple decoration. Start the day with a hearty breakfast that will provide you the required energy.

Breakfast can be served either in guests rooms or as a buffet in the hotel restaurant.

For your comfort the hotel has a cozy, shared living room, a bar and a large garden.

Guests also have use of a pool table and a foosball table. Savour typical specialities in the relaxed atmosphere of the hotels restaurant.

The area offers many activities such as horse riding, hiking, mountain biking, water sports and excursions.
