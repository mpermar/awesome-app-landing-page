Description

At A l'Orée du Parc, every room is equipped with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Every room comes with a electric tea pot, while certain rooms include a terrace and others also feature garden views. Guest rooms feature a closet.

Breakfast is available each morning, and includes continental, buffet and vegetarian options.

A l'Orée du Parc has a playground. Guests at A l'Orée du Parc will be able to enjoy activities in and around Saint-Priest-des-Champs, like cycling and hiking.

Clermont-Ferrand is 21 miles from A l'Orée du Parc. Auvergne Airport is 24 miles from the property.
