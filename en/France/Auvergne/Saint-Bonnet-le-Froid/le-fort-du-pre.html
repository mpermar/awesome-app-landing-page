Description

Logis Le Fort Du Pré features bright and colorful rooms equipped with a TV and phone. Each room has a private bathroom with a walk-in shower and hairdryer. The hotel offers free Wi-Fi.

Gastronomic cuisine is served in the hotel restaurant or on the terrace during fair weather. A buffet breakfast can be enjoyed every morning . Guests can also enjoy a drink in the bar.

Le Fort Du Pré is located between the Haute-Loire and Ardeche regions and offers free on-site parking. The hotel is 19 mi from Annonay.
