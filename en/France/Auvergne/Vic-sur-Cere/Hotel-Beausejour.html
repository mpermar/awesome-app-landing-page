Description

Situated in the centre of Vic-sur-Cère, 20 minutes from Aurillac, the Beauséjour offers an exceptional panoramic view over the Cantal Mountains and features a gorgeous outdoor swimming pool.

Housed in a 19th-century building, the rooms and suites at the Beauséjour have been designed for ultimate comfort.

The hotel restaurant overlooks the Beauséjour's 12000m² park. It serves traditional cuisine made using local produce.

The Beauséjour is located a few metres from the tourist office, by the start of numerous hiking trails.

Free parking is provided at the Beauséjour, making it easy to explore the Auvergne region by car.
