Description

Offering an outdoor pool and a restaurant, Bel Horizon is located in Le Chambon-sur-Lignon.

Free Wi-Fi access, a fitness room and a wellness centre with a hot tub and sauna are available on site.

The guest rooms at Bel Horizon include a TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom.

Some of the rooms are set in separate cottages and feature air conditioning and a coffee machine.

Traditional cuisine prepared with local products can be sampled in the on-site restaurant and a buffet breakfast is served daily.

Many activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling, hiking and table tennis.

Free parking is provided on site and Le Puy is 40 km away. The property is 90 km from Tence, 57 km from Saint-Etienne and 70 km from Valence.
