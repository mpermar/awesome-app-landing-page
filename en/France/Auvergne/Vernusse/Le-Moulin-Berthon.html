Description

At the bed and breakfast, rooms contain a desk. Featuring a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries, rooms at Le Moulin Berthon also provide guests with free WiFi, while certain rooms have a terrace. All rooms have a closet.

Breakfast is available daily, and includes continental and buffet options.

The accommodation has a grill. Guests at Le Moulin Berthon will be able to enjoy activities in and around Vernusse, like cycling and fishing.

Vichy is 22 miles from the bed and breakfast, while Moulins is 28 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Auvergne Airport, 33 miles from the property.
