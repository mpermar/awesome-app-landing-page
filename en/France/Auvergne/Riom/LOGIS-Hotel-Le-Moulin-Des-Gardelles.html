Description

Situated on the motorway exit for Volvic from Riom, 15 minutes from Clermont-Ferrand, Le Moulin des Gardelles is a family-run Logis de France hotel.

Guestrooms have views over the surrounding countryside. They feature an en suite bathroom, flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a work desk.

There is WiFi internet access and free tea and coffee is available in all rooms. A buffet breakfast is served every morning.

Gardelles' gourmet restaurant serves regional dishes, prepared with only the fresh local ingredients.

The Moulin offers a library and a garden. A charging station for electric cars is available at this property.

The towns of Volvic, famous for its mineral water and Riom, well-known for its art are located nearby.
