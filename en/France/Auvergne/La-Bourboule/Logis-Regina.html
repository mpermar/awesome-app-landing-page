Description

Ideally located in a peaceful setting at the border of Dordogne, in La Bourboule, the hotel is also in close proximity to the city centre.

Free Wi-Fi access and free on-site parking are provided. The rooms at Logis de France Regina are spacious and equipped with flat-screen TV that have TNT and 4 cable channels.

Each has a private bathroom with a hairdryer. The surrounding area offers a variety of cultural sights and recreational activities to suit all tastes.

The hotel boasts excellent facilities such as an indoor swimming pool, hot tub, hammam and fitness centre.

Guests can relax in the on-site bar or lounge followed by a gourmet meal in the hotel restaurant.
