Description

Logis Du Puy Ferrand offers a variety of rooms, all comfortably furnished and decorated.

Some offer a balcony with views of the Massif du Sancy. The bright and spacious rooms also have private bathrooms with bath or shower and a hair dryer, satellite TV and free Wi-Fi internet access.

The restaurant with its outdoor, panoramic terrace serves traditional and seasonal cuisine made with fresh local products.

The dining room is brightly decorated, complimented by its fireplace. Located near the Dordogne River at the foot of Puy de Sancy, Logis Du Puy Ferrand is just 5 minutes from Mont-Dore, a large spa resort in the Auvergne region.
