Description

Lac des Graves boasts a location in Cantal. It is set in a 40-acre park surrounded by mountains and bordered by the Lac des Graves.

On site, there is a seasonal heated outdoor pool, open from June 2016. The village of Lascelle is 32 km from Salers.

Lac des Graves features well-equipped rooms with a TV, en suite bathroom with a bath and shower, a telephone and free Wi-Fi access.

Enjoy a splendid panoramic view of the lake from every room. The restaurant and terrace overlook both the lake.

A daily continental breakfast is provided and can be enjoyed in the dining room or on the terrace.

Regional dishes with local wines are available for lunch and dinner, depending on the season.

Many activities are offered on-site and everything contributes to making an unforgettable stay, dedicated to relaxation, in the Auvergne region.
