Description

Featuring countryside views from each of its guest rooms, La Claie des Champs is situated in the Auvergne region.

The hotel also offers 24-hour reception, an on-site restaurant and free Wi-Fi access throughout.

The spacious guest rooms have polished timber floors and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Each has facilities for ironing and making hot drinks and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

Guests can choose a continental or buffet breakfast every morning. The ground floor restaurant serves traditional dishes from the region.

It has a stone feature wall and terrace dining during warmer months. Free private parking is available on site.

The 14th-century Château de Pionsat is 11 km from La Claie des Champs and Montluçon is 23 km away.
