Description

Situated between Nancy and Epinal, Relais de Vincey features a tennis court, fitness centre and indoor and outdoor swimming pools, surrounded by countryside in the Vosges region.

Guest rooms at Relais de Vincey are modern in style with panoramic views over the fields.

They are equipped with a flat-screen TV and free Wi-Fi access. The restaurant serves regional cuisine with fine local wines.

Guests can enjoy meals in the restaurant decorated with wooden furnishings, or on the terrace during the warmer months.

Activities and sights nearby include Place Stanislas in the heart of the Lorraine region, hiking and cycling.
