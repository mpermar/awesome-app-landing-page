Description

Located 300 metres from the A33 motorway's exit and 1 km from the Exhibition Centre, ibis Styles Nancy Sud is a 10-minute drive from Nancy city centre.

With a a 24-hour reception, it offers an outdoor swimming pool, children's playground and meeting rooms.

With free Wi-Fi, the air-conditioned guest rooms have a desk, a satellite TV and a telephone.

The bathroom is provided with a hairdryer. The ibis styles Cafe serves modern cuisine that you can enjoy on the terrace or by the pool.

Metz is a 45-minute drive away while Paris city centre is 3 hours and a half by car from ibis styles Nancy Sud.
