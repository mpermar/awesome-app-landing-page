Description

Set within a 435-hectare forest, Center Parcs Les Trois Forêts is located in the Moselle region and has an on-site water park, Aqua Mundo, which features a wave pool, hot tubs and water slides. WiFi is available in all areas at an additional cost.

The cottage features a private terrace with garden furniture, a living room with a fireplace and flat-screen TV. There is a bathroom with a spa bath and a fully equipped kitchen. Bed linen and towels are provided.

A selection of bars and restaurants is available at Center Parcs Les Trois Forêts and there is also a bakery and supermarket.

The park's 5249 ft² Deep Nature® spa offers face and body massages, as well as energizing, reviatlising and relaxing treatments in single or double cabins.

For a fee, guests can enjoy body-boarding, adventure trails and paint-balling on-site. Segway hire, archery and pedalo hire are also available. There is also an animal farm for children.

Free private parking is available on site.
