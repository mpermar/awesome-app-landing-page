Description

Bed and Breakfast La Fraiseraie is a renovated farmhouse dating from 1800 in Tellancourt, a 10-minute drive from Château de Cons-la-Grandville.

A homemade breakfast is included and a BBQ is available. Decorated with original stone walls and parquet flooring, the rooms all offer a garden view.

Each has free Wi-Fi access and a TV, and the en suite bathrooms are fitted with a shower.

Breakfast can be taken in the dining room, which features exposed wooden beams or on the terrace during summer.

The hosts can prepare meals made with produce from the Lorraine region upon request.

Free tea and coffee is available throughout the day. You will also have access to the shared kitchen, which includes a microwave and an oven.

With free private parking on site, the B&B is 35 km from Luxembourg and a 15-minute drive from the International Golf Club in Longwy.
