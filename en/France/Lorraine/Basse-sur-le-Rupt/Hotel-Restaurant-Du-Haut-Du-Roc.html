Description

Situated near the ski resorts of Gerardmer and La Bresse, the Hotel Restaurant Haut du Roc offers functional rooms and all equipped with private bathroom and free Wi-Fi access.

The Auberge du Haut du Roc has a restaurant with Vosges specialties, a banquet independent, a bar with a pool billiard and terrace with barbecue and games for children.

A buffet breakfast is served daily. Near the ski slopes and alpine skiing, it is also possible to enjoy activities such as mountain bike trekking, snowshoeing and hiking.
