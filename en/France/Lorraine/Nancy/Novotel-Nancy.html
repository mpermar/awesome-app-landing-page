Description

Novotel Nancy is located 5 km from the centre of Nancy close to the major highways to Lyon, Paris, Metz and Strasbourg.

It features comfortable rooms in leafy surroundings as well as an outdoor swimming pool and 400 m² of fully equipped meeting rooms.

The Novotel Café invites you to taste contemporary French cuisine or to enjoy a cocktail.

The café offers guests à la carte service 24/7. Enjoy a meal on the terrace when the weather is nice.

Families are welcome and the Novotel Nancy boasts an indoor and outdoor play area for children.
