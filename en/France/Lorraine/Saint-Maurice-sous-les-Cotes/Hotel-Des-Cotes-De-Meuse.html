Description

The Hôtel des Cotes de Meuse is located in Saint-Maurice-sous-les-Côtes, in Lorraine.

It has 9 spacious guest rooms, each with a private bathroom, TV and free Wi-Fi. The Hotel des Cotes de Meuse has a restaurant which offers traditionally inspired regional cuisine.

L'Hôtel des Cotes de Meuse offers free private parking and the hotel is situated 15 km from the A4/E50 motorway between Metz and Châlons-en-Champagne.
