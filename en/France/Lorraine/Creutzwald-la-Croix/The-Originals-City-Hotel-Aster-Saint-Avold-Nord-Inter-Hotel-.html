Description

The Originals City, Hôtel Aster, Saint-Avold Nord is located in a quiet and green setting, on the edge of a lake.

It offers spacious rooms with flat-screen TVs and free WiFi access. Guests rooms at The Originals City, Hôtel Aster, Saint-Avold Nord are air-conditioned and equipped with satellite TV.

Each has a private bathroom with both shower and bathtub, and toiletries are provided.

The Originals City, Hôtel Aster, Saint-Avold Nord has a restaurant, which serves traditional cuisine, with buffet options.

Guests can enjoy an evening drink in the bar. Free private parking is provided at The Originals City, Hôtel Aster, Saint-Avold Nord.

The hotel is just a few kilometres from the German border and 5 minutes from the A4 motorway.
