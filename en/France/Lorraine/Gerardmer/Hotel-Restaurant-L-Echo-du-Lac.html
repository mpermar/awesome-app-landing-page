Description

Located on the edge of Gérardmer Lake and 2 km from the city centre, L'Echo du Lac offers a traditional-style restaurant overlooking the lake, a bar and en suite rooms.

You can relax on the terrace. The contemporary rooms at L'Echo du Lac have a flat-screen TV, a telephone and a private bathroom with hairdryer and free toiletries.

Some have a view of the forest or the lake. A continental buffet breakfast is available daily and guests can enjoy an afternoon tea or coffee at L'Echo du Lac.

After breakfast, you can use free Wi-Fi access, available throughout. The hotel is situated 400 metres from Gérardmer Nautical Club where you can canoe, fish and sail.

It is also 4 km from the ski slopes and 400 metres from the casino. Several hiking paths are located nearby.
