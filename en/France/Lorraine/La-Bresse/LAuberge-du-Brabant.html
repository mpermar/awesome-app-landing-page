Description

Located next to La Bresse Brabant ski resort, this eco-friendly hotel features mountain-style rooms with free Wi-Fi and a restaurant with a panoramic terrace.

Products used in the hotel are organic and sourced locally. With a view of the Vosges Mountains, each room at L'Auberge du Brabant includes an LCD TV, a safety deposit box and a telephone.

A private bathroom, a wardrobe and a desk are also available. Spring water is provided through the taps.

L'Auberge du Brabant serves a buffet breakfast and local specialties are proposed at the restaurant.

Guests can have a drink while relaxing on the terrace, which is in front of the slopes.

Themed nights, excursions and guided tours are proposed by the staff. Guests can also enjoy homemade cuisine, prepared with local products upon request.

Skiing, cross-country skiing and hiking are some of the activities that can be done nearby.

La Bresse city centre is an 8-minute drive away and the Corbeaux Lake is 4 km away.
