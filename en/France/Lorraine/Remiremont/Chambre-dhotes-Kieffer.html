Description

Set in a restored 18th century farmhouse, this B&B is located 2 km from central Remiremont and features a 5-hectares garden.

It offers a garden terrace with views of the surrounding landscape. Relaxing massages and ortho-bionomy therapy sessions are available upon prior reservation.

The guest rooms at Chambre d’Hôtes Kieffer are simply decorated and include a private bathroom, a work desk and a wardrobe.

All rooms offer views of the garden. The B&B’s communal kitchenette features a refrigerator and a microwave, and breakfast can be enjoyed by the fireplace in the dining area.

Barbecue facilities are available in the garden. Guests are invited to play table tennis and table football in the games room, or watch TV in the lounge.

Nearby activities include horse riding, hiking and tennis. Free parking is possible on site and Saint-Nabord is 7 km away.
