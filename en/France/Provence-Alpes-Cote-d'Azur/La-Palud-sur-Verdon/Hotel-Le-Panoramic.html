Description

Set in a 4-hectare estate in the heart of Verdon Regional Natural Park, Hotel Le Panoramic offers guestrooms 800 metres out of La Palud.

The hotel features a seasonal outdoor pool, several terraces, a children's playground and a lounge with billiards, table tennis and table football.

Free WiFi is available in public areas. The rooms come with a flat screen TV, a private bathroom with a bath or shower, a toilet and free toiletries.

Some rooms offer garden views and pool, while others have mountain views. The children's playground features football goals, volleyball court and badminton set.

Guests can have a drink at the on-site bar In the surroundings you can explore the Verdon Gorge on a pedalo, electric boat or kayak, as well as the Lake Sainte-Croix, which is 15 km away.

Hiking, climbing, bungee jumping, tree climbing, rafting and canyoning can be enjoyed in the area.

Free parking is available on site. The nearest airport is Nice Côte d'Azur Airport, 124 km from Hotel Le Panoramic.
