Description

Hotel & Spa des Gorges du Verdon is located in La Palud in the heart of the Verdon Grand Canyon.

Panoramic views can be enjoyed from the terraces, and a covered, heated outdoor swimming pool is available.

Free WiFi access is offered. The guest rooms and suites features a flat-screen satellite TV and a safety deposit box.

Most rooms also have a private balcony. The bathrooms have a bath or shower and a hairdryer.

Spa Cinq Mondes, opened in 2015, offers a hammam, a sauna, a hot tub and aquagym bikes.

Guests can also play tennis, boules and basketball on site, and there is a fitness room equiped with Technogym material.
