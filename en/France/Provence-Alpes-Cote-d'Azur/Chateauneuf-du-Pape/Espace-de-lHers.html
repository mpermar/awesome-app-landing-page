Description

Offering an outdoor pool, Espace de l'Hers is nestled in the heart of Châteauneuf-du-Pape’s vineyard.

The guest house offers a garden with a terrace, as well as air-conditioned accommodation with free WiFi access.

The rooms and apartment at Espace de l'Hers feature a terrace, a seating area and a TV.

There is a private bathroom with a walk-in shower and a hairdryer. The apartment includes an equipped kitchen with a dishwasher and a microwave.

A breakfast buffet of sweet and savory treats is available. To dine on site, packed lunches of local cheeses and charcuterie can be ordered 24 hours in advance.

There is a shared lounge where guests can relax, as well as a children's playground.

The owners can advise guests on visiting the region. The property offers free parking and is a 35-minute drive from Avignon TGV Train Station.
