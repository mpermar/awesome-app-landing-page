Description

Set in the Camargue Regional Park, this B&B is located in Paty de la Trinité. You can relax on the furnished terrace, by the garden and enjoy the outdoor swimming pool.

There are also a tennis table and a football table. Mas de Bouvet offers air-conditioned rooms with a contemporary décor.

Each room features a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with free toiletries. A continental breakfast is included and served every morning in the dining room.

Free Wi-Fi is provided throughout the property. Free private parking is possible on site and Arles is 15 km from the B&B.
