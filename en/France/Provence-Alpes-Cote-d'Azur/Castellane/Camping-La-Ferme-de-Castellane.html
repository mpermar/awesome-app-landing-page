Description

This campsite is set on the river bank, located in the village of Castellane, within a 5-minute drive of the Verdon Gorge.

Free WiFi is provided in public areas and the property has a table tennis table as well as a boules pitch.

A TV with cable channels and a private terrace feature in all the chalets and mobile homes, which have a simple dÃ©cor with wooden furnishings.

The en suite bathrooms are fitted with a shower. Located at the edge of the forest, all accommodation types offer mountain views.

A microwave, fridge and hotplates are included in each kitchenette at Camping La Ferme de Castellane.

A continental breakfast is prepared every morning and there are barbecue facilities.

Additional services include a laundrette and there is a childrenâs playground. You can watch Canal+ channels in the TV room, which also has a library or play table football and board games.

The centre of Castellane is 2. 3 km from the campsite and the city of Grasse is 65 km away.
