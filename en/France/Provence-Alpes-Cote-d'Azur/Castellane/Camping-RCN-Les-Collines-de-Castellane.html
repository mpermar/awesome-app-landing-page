Description

Camping Les Collines de Castellane is 20 km from Lac de Castillon and 6. 5 km from Castellane, the gateway to the Gorges du Verdon region.

It offers a seasonal outdoor swimming pool with slides and massage jets, a restaurant, a pizzeria and a snack bar.

The mobile homes, chalets and luxury tents at Les Collines have an equipped kitchen, a bathroom with a shower and bedrooms equipped with pillows and covers.

Each unit has a furnished terrace. Guests can play boules, basketball, football and volleyball at Camping Les Collines de Castellane, and there are outdoor fitness facilities.

For an extra fee, guests can experience the on site Adventure Park and Zip line, or play tennis.
