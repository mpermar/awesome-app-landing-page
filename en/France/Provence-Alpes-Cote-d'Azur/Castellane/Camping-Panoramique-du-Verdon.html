Description

Camping Panoramique du Verdon is located in the heart of the Verdon Gorges, just 1 km from Castellane.

It offers 1 heated swimming pool for children, a heated infinity pool and a volleyball pitch.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. The mobile homes at Camping Panoramique du Verdon feature a private terrace with panoramic views of Castellane.

Each one also includes a kitchenette, which is complete with a stove and a fridge-freezer.

Bed linen and towels are provided. You can order French pastries and fresh bread 24 hours in advance.

Local shops, bakeries and restaurants can all be found in the centre of Castellane.

Free private parking is provided on site. You may also choose to buy local honey, spices and crafts from the weekly market in the town centre.
