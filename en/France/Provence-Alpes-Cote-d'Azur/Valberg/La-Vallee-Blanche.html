Description

Set a 1-hour drive from Nice, La Vallée Blanche is located in the village of Valberg.

On the edge of Mercantour National Parc, it offers a spa, a sauna and a Nordic bath on the panoramic terrace.

All of the heated guest rooms have free Wi-Fi, a private bathroom with free toiletries, flat-screen TV and DVD player.

Some feature balconies with views of the mountains and ski slopes. An on-site restaurant seating up to 150 people offers traditional alpine cuisine.

Cocktails are available from the bar and can be enjoyed either on an open or a covered terrace.

Only 1. 4 km from the Valberg village center, La Vallée Blanche offers ski storage and provides equipment for snowshoeing and luge, making it easy to explore the surrounding ski slopes, forests and mountains.
