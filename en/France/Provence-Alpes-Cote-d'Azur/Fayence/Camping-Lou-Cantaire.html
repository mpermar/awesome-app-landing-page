Description

Camping Lou Cantaire is surrounded by woodland and is situated in the heart of the Provence, a 15-minute drive from Lake Saint-Cassien.

It offers mobile homes each with a private, furnished garden area. Each mobile home has a living area with an equipped kitchenette and a sofa.

Each also has a private bathroom with a shower. There is free Wi-Fi access by the reception area.

There are 2 outdoor swimming pools; 1 for adults and 1 for children. There is also a tennis court, pétanque area and a variety of organised activities.
