Description

This countryside hotel is located 3. 5 km from the village of Villecroze, in the heart of Provence.

It features an outdoor pool, a spa with a hot tub and rooms with private terraces.

Au Bien Etre is set in the middle of a large park. Each room has a private bathroom and a TV, and is air conditioned.

A continental breakfast is served every morning. The restaurant serves regional cuisine in the dining room or on the outdoor terrace.

Guests can also enjoy a drink in the bar. Additional facilities at Au Bien Etre hotel include a business centre with free internet and children’s playground.
