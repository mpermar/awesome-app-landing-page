Description

Located at the foothills of the Luberon Mountains, this hotel features an outdoor swimming pool, a terrace and a garden.

Free WiFi is provided throughout the property. At the hotel, all rooms have been renovated at the beginning of 2018.

They are all equipped with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Some rooms offer pool views.

A lounge bar is located on the ground floor and gives access to the terrace and swimming pool.

Buffet breakfast is served every morning on the terrace and you can enjoy snacks and refreshments by the pool.

A meeting room is available onsite. Free parking is provided. Marseille Provence Airport is located 51 km from Le Mas Du Colombier.
