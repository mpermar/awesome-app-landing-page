Description

Gîte Le Pas de l'Ane is located in Villar-d’Arène. This hotel features a restaurant, a terrace and a bar.

Guests can enjoy mountain views from the property. All rooms include a private bathroom with a shower.

Skiing is a popular activity in the area and Les Deux Alpes ski resort is just a 30-minute drive from the property.

Ski storage facilities are available on site. Grenoble - Isère Airport is 126 km away.
