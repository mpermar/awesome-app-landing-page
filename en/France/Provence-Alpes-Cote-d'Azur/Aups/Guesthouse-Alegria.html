Description

Surrounded by olive trees, this boutique guest house in the heart of the Provence Region is a short walk from the village of Aups. It offers individually decorated rooms and an outdoor pool with a fully furnished terrace. A bakery can be found a 5-minute walk away.

Alegria rooms and suites are decorated in a contemporary style. Each one features its own terrace, as well as free Wi-Fi and an iPod docking station. Each room or suite is also equipped with a custom-made kitchen island with a fridge, toaster, kettle, dishwasher and Nespresso coffee machine.
