Description

Located between Avignon and Carpentras in an exceptional setting, this former 18th century Post office relay, is a perfect place for your business or leisure trip in Vaucluse.

In the summer you will benefit from the shaded park and from its swimming-pool, and in the winter, lounges will welcome you in a warm atmosphere.

At your disposal is a free, closed car park and adjacent restaurant, The Mulberry. The Originals City, Hotel du Parc, Avignon East is 13 km from Chateauneuf-du-Pape, 19 km from L'Isle-sur-la-Sorgue and 16 km from Avignon.
