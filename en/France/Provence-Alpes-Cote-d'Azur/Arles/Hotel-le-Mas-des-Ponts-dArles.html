Description

Just 2. 7 km from Arles at the tip of the Camargue, Hôtel le Mas des Ponts d'Arles features comfortable accommodation for a stay with family and friends.

Guestrooms are located on the swimming pool side or the park side of the residence.

They offer a calm and relaxing setting for a pleasant stay. WiFi internet access is available and is free of charge.

Hôtel le Mas des Ponts d'Arles also features a lounge bar that serves a variety of drinks and is open daily until 20:00.

Breakfast is served daily from 07:30 to 10:00. Guests will also benefit from the use of the outdoor swimming pool and lawn chairs set in the hotels raised park.
