Description

Located in the perched village of Saint-Auban, La Pinatelle offers self-catering tents and mobile homes surrounded by woods and mountains.

An outdoor, heated swimming pool, a tennis, volley-ball and pétanque courts are some of the on-site facilities.

Made of thick, polyester canvas, the tents are equipped with a microwave, hot plates and fridge/freezer.

The self-catering mobile homes include a bathroom with shower. All accommodations include a cupboard with crockery, a table and chairs.

The on-site restaurant serves traditional cuisine as well as pizzas and offers a range of free kids’ meals for children under 2.

Vegetarian options are also available. Guests may also choose to have take-away picnic hampers.

Karaoke dinners and themed parties are organised at La Pinatelle during high season, and a secured playground is available for younger guests.
