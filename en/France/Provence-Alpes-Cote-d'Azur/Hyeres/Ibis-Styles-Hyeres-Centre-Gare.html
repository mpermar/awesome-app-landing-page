Description

Ibis Styles Hyeres Centre Gare is located in Hyeres, directly in front of Hyeres Train Station and just a 10-minute walk from the city centre.

It offers free WiFi access and a panoramic furnished terrace. A sauna and a hot tub are also available.

Each air-conditioned and soundproofed room at the Ibis Styles Hyeres Centre Gare has stylish décor and is equipped with a flat-screen TV with Canal+ and a private bathroom.

All rooms are accessible by a lift. A buffet breakfast is served daily and can be enjoyed in the dining room or on the terrace.

Guests are invited to have a drink at the hotel's bar. Free public parking is provided nearby.

Ibis Styles Hyeres Centre Gare is just a 10-minute drive from the port and beaches.

There is also a bus stop 30 metres from the hotel, which gives direct access to the beaches of Hyeres and the island of Porquerolles.
