Description

Pullman Marseille Provence Aéroport offers spacious rooms, a tennis court and a large outdoor swimming pool.

The Blue Coast is a 15-minute drive away, where the Camargue meets the Alpilles and Luberon Mountains.

Air-conditioned guest rooms feature a TV with satellite channels, free WiFi and a safety deposit box.

The private bathrooms offer a bath, bathrobes, free toiletries and slippers. Still and sparkling water as well as a selection of tea and coffee are offered in every room.

The Pullman Marseille Provence features a restaurant and lounge bar, D. llis. The chef creates innovative dishes taking inspiration from Mediterranean cuisine.

You can dine on one of its two terraces overlooking the hotel’s park. Guests at the Pullman benefit from room service 24 hours a day.

Guests can also enjoy the fitness centre, hammam and sauna. The hotel is a 10-minute drive from the Aix-en-Provence TGV Train Station.

The hotel offers a free 24-hour shuttle service that can take you to the hotel from Marseille Provence Airport in a few minutes.

The hotel is a 3-minute drive from the airport and a 25-minute drive from Velodrome Stadium.
