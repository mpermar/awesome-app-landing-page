Description

La Garance is located in Sainte-Colombe, in the heart of Provence, just 4 km from Bédoin.

The hotel is set in a restored farmhouse surrounded by vineyards and olive trees and has a view on Mont Ventoux.

It offers an outdoor swimming pool and free Wi-Fi access. The guest rooms at La Garance include a flat-screen TV, a work desk and a wardrobe.

Each room also includes a private bathroom fitted with a bath or a shower and free toiletries.

A buffet breakfast with mainly organic products can be enjoyed in the breakfast room or on the terrace on sunny mornings.

Provencal cuisine is served in the restaurant La Colombe next to the hotel. Free private parking is available on site and the Dentelles de Montmirail are 12 km away.

The property is a 20-minute drive from Carpentras and a 45-minute drive from the centres of both Orange and Avignon.
