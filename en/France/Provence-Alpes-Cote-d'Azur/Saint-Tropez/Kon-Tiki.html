Description

Set directly on Pampelonne Beach in Ramatuelle, this resort is just 4 km from Saint-Tropez.

It offers a spa with hot tub and sauna, and also has a beauty salon. Each bungalow at the Kon Tiki features a private terrace with garden furniture.

The fully equipped kitchen includes a fridge and coffee maker. All of the stylish bungalows have a private bathroom and air conditioning.

Both the Tiki Beach restaurant and the Tiki Club offers panoramic sea views from the outdoor terraces.

There is also a supermarket and the Tiki Snack bar offers takeaway meals. During July and August, Tiki Thai serves sushi.

Guests can practice archery and scuba dive on site. Other activities include yoga, pilates and volleyball.

A kids and teenagers club is available in July and August and the property has a bouncy castle for children.

Classes are available in the seasonal outdoor pool, and it is open to all guests to use from 12:00 to 15:00 daily.

Free private parking is possible on site and Gassin Golf Country Club is 5 km away.

An airport shuttle is available upon request. Charging stations for electric cars are available at this property.
