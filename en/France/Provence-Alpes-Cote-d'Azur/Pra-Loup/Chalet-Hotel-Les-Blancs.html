Description

The Chalet Hôtel Les Blancs is located just a few metres from the ski lifts of the Molanes (Pra-Loup 1500).

It offers a sauna and a heated indoor swimming pool. The comfortable guest rooms at Chalet Hôtel Les Blancs have a bathroom and TV.

Guests can access a fitness room, a bar, and free parking. The chalet’s restaurant serves traditional cuisine.
