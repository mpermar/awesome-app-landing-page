Description

Located a 5-minute walk from the centre of Vence on the French Riviera, Le Floréal provides rooms with free WiFi and features an outdoor pool and terrace.

The rooms at Le Floréal have a flat-screen TV with international channels and a bathroom including a walk-in shower and separate toilet.

Most rooms have a balcony with garden views. A buffet breakfast is served, which can be eaten in the restaurant or bedroom, and there is a bar open all year.

The restaurant offers a selection of simple, traditional dishes inspired by Provencal cuisine, which can be served under the olive trees or on the patio, weather permitting.

The hotel is just 20 minutes from Nice. Guests can easily access Nice Airport, Nice city centre and Cannes from the bus which stops at a few minutes' walk from the hotel.
