Description

Hotel Mas des Lys is situated in Saintes-Maries-de-la-Mer in the Camargues region, a 30-minute drive from Arles.

It offers an outdoor pool and air-conditioned guest rooms with a private terrace.

The guest rooms at Hotel Mas des Lys include a flat-screen TV, a telephone and a wardrobe.

They each feature a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries. A buffet-style breakfast is served every morning and drinks can be enjoyed in the hotel bar.

Restaurants are found within 2 km of the hotel and free Wi-Fi is provided in public areas.

Free private parking is provided and guests can rent bicycles and go horseback riding on site.
