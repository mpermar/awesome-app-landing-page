Description

Featuring a garden and a terrace with views of the glaciers, Auberge de Violaine is located in Le Monêtier-les-Bains.

The village centre is 300 metres away. All non-smoking rooms feature a desk, a flat-screen TV and free WiFi access, while some also offer a balcony.

The private bathroom offers a bath or shower and free toiletries. A continental breakfast can be served every morning at an additional charge.

The property offers an on-site bar, a restaurant and billiards. Auberge de Violaine can be accessed on the road located between Grenoble and Le Monêtier-les-Bains and is 800 metres from ski slopes and Valvital thermal centre.

Briançon is 14 km away and the property offers free private parking on site. Guest can access the ski slopes with the free city shuttle, only available during winter season (the shuttle stop is in front of the hotel).
