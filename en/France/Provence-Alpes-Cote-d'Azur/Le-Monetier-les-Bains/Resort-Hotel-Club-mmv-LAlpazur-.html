Description

This Hotel Club MMV features a spa center and a seasonal, outdoor pool with a sun terrace in summer, which overlooks the Hautes-Alps. It is located 1,950 feet from the Serre Chevalier's ski elevator.

Guest rooms at the Hotel Club MMV L’alpazur are furnished with a flat-screen TV and a work desk. They each have a private bathroom and WiFi access.

L’alpazur’s restaurant serves an all-you-can-eat buffet breakfast with unlimited hot and cold drinks, pastries, crêpes, dairy produce, cold meats, cheese and fruit. Lunch and dinner are also served, offering a wide choice of unlimited hot and cold dishes, wines and fruit juices. The restaurant opens onto an outdoor terrace and guests can also enjoy a drink in the bar.

MMV L’alpazur organizes evening entertainment and a children’s club. The spa and wellness center includes a sauna, hot tub, hammam, hydromassage shower and massages and treatments at a supplementary cost.
