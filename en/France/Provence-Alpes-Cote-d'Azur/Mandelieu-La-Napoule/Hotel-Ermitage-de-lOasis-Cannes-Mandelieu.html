Description

One of our top picks in Mandelieu-La Napoule.Ermitage de l’Oasis is a hotel set in the Bay of Cannes, overlooking the Port of Napoule and the Lérins Islands. Guests can enjoy the heated pool and views of the Mediterranean Sea or the Cannes Mandelieu golf course.

The rooms at the Ermitage l'Oasis (ex Ermitage du Riou)- Cannes Mandelieu are equipped with a satellite TV, a private bathroom and free WiFi access.

The hotel is in close proximity to the Mimosa Forest and the red rocks of l'Esterel. Private parking is available on-site at an extra cost.
