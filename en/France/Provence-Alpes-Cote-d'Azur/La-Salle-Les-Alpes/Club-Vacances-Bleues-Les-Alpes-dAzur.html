Description

Les Alpes d’Azur is located in Le Grand Serre Chevalier ski resort and offers rooms with a balcony in a property with a large garden, terrace, a heated indoor swimming pool, a hammam and a sauna.

The slopes are 800 metres away and a free shuttle is provided. Each room includes a private bathroom with a bath or shower and free toiletries.

A buffet breakfast is available daily. There is a bar on site and you will find shops situated 800 metres from the property.

Free WiFi access is available in all areas. A seasonal restaurant is available and open for lunch or dinner, subject to weather conditions.

Guests can have lunch in the park. Ski storage is provided and ski rental is possible on site.

Guests are entitled to a free aqua-gym and low-impact workout session. Water sports facilities, including kayaking, rafting and canoeing are available 2 km away.

Other activities in the surroundings include mountain biking, rock climbing and via ferrata.

Les Grands Bains du Monétier Spa, with its thermal baths and selection of beauty and well-being treatments as well as Briançon fortified town, are just a 10-minute drive away.
