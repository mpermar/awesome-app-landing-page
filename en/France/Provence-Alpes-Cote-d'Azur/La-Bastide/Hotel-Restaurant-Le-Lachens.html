Description

Hôtel Restaurant Le Lachens is located in La Bastide in the Var department, 3 km from La Roque-Escaplon and 8 km from Bargème.

It offers free Wi-Fi access, a large garden and a terrace. The guest rooms at Le Lachens include a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

They each have a wardrobe and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

A continental breakfast can be enjoyed every morning at Le Lachens, and other meals are proposed in the on-site restaurant.

A bar and a grocery store are fond within 100 metres of the hotel. Free private parking is available on site and many hiking paths are found nearby.

The property is 7 km from Château de Taulane Golf Club, 49 km from Grasse and 42 km from Draguignan.
