Description

Hôtel Belesso is located in Fontvieille, in the Chaîne des Alpilles. It has a terrace with panoramic mountain views and an outdoor swimming pool surrounded by an olive and cypress garden.

Rooms at Hôtel Belesso are air-conditioned with a dressing room, satellite TV and free Wi-Fi.

Hôtel Belesso's restaurant and bar serves fresh Provencal specialties in the courtyard and garden.

Guests can relax in the Hôtel Belesso’s walled garden, by the swimming pool, or enjoy hiking and cycling in the surrounding area.
