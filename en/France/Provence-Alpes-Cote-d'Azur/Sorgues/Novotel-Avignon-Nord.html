Description

Offering free WiFi, Novotel Avignon Nord is located close to the A7 motorway, 10 km away from the centre of Avignon.

A swimming pool, a terrace and 2 tennis courts are available. Novotel Avignon Nord offers 100 air-conditioned rooms with a flat-screen TV.

The hotel's restaurant, Confidentiel, serves dishes made with local products. A bar and 11 conference rooms with natural light for your meetings and functions (capacity 200 people) are available.

Private parking is available. A golf course and a shopping centre can be found nearby.

Avignon TGV railway station is 13. 2 km away and Avignon Airpot is 15 km from Novotel Avignon Nord.
