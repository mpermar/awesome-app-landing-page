Description

Offering a restaurant, Hotel Les Flots Bleus is located in Agay on the beachfront.

Free WiFi access is available. Each room is decorated in a contemporary style and features a balcony, flat-screen TV, safety deposit box, desk and air conditioning.

Featuring a bath or shower, private bathrooms also come with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

A continental breakfast is served daily. At Les Flots Bleus you will also find a bar, sun terrace and a snack bar.
