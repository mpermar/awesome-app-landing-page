Description

Mas De Peycavier is located in Callas and offers B&B accommodation. This property features a garden, an outdoor pool and BBQ facilities.

Free WiFi access is available and there is on-site parking for guests arriving by car.

Guests have free access to the shared spa baths. Guests have access to an oven and fridge in the common areas of the house.

All rooms have a private bathroom with a toilet. Cycling is a popular activity in the area and guests are able to borrow bikes from the property free of charge.
