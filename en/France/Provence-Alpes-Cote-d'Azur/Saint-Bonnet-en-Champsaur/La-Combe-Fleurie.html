Description

B & B La Combe Fleurie is situated in Saint-Bonnet-en-Champsaur, 8 km from Chaillol and Laye ski resorts.

Set within a garden, the property offers free Wi-Fi and a terrace with a panoramic view on the Alps.

The guest rooms are individually decorated and feature a flat-screen TV and parquet flooring.

They each include a private bathroom with a shower, and some rooms open onto a terrace.

A continental breakfast with homemade pancakes, pastries and jam is served every morning.

Traditional evening menus can be prepared upon request, and board games are available in the lounge area.

Free private parking is available on site and possible nearby activities include hiking, golf and biking.
