Description

Hotel Le Connetable, set in the heart of Saint-Bonnet-en-Champsaur at the foot of the Ecrins National Park, features a terrace with panoramic views.

The hotel offers a lounge area with library. All guest rooms are modern and decorated using bright colours.

Each one has a view of the courtyard and is equipped with a flat-screen TV and free Wi-Fi access.

The private bathrooms have tiled floors. A continental buffet breakfast is served every morning in the spacious breakfast room, or can be enjoyed on the terrace on sunny days.

Hotel Le Connetable also features a bar and restaurant. This hotel is situated 17 km from Gap in the French Alps and less than 10km from Gap Bayard golf course.

Col de Manse, Col du Noyer and Col de la Moissière cycling mountain passes can be found nearby.
