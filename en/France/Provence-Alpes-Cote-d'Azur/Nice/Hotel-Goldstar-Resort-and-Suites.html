Description

One of our top picks in Nice.Offering suites in the center of Nice, just 800 feet from the beach, Goldstar Resort & Suites features a heated rooftop pool with a terrace. Free high-speed WiFi access is available throughout the entire hotel.

The air-conditioned suites feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a kitchenette with a microwave and a dishwasher. Most suites have a separate bedroom and a living area with a sofa bed. Bathrobes are available only on request.

Guests at Goldstar have free access to the heated swimming pool (30 feet x 10 feet) and solarium on the rooftop from 09:00 to 19:00 daily. Access to the sauna and hammam can be reserved at no extra cost, both are accessible from 09:00 to 19:00 daily. Drinks can also be enjoyed on the rooftop terrace, which features a panoramic view of Nice.

Nice's central train station is a 10-minute walk from Goldstar Resort & Suites. A shuttle service is available at an additional cost to Nice Côte d’Azur Airport, 3.5 mi away. Private secure parking is available on site for an extra charge.
