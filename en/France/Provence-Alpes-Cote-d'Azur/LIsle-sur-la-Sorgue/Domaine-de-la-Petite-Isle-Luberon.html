Description

Set in a peaceful, 1000 m² park, the Domaine de la Petite Isle features 2 swimming pools, friendly staff and a calm environment on the banks of the Sorgue River.

The air-conditioned guest rooms are equipped with en suite facilities, a TV, telephone, tea and coffee maker and work desk.

The Superior and Privilege rooms have a terrace or balcony. Start the day with the buffet breakfast.

Later, enjoy a meal in the hotel's restaurant. Guests can choose from a large selection of innovative and traditional specialties, prepared with fresh, local ingredients.

Eat on the shady terrace when the weather is nice. During your stay, enjoy the peace and quiet and the lovely surrounding countryside.
