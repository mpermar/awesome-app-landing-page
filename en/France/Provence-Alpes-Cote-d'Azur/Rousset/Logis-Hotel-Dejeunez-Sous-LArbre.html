Description

In the heart of Provence, Logis Hôtel Déjeunez Sous L'arbre offers comfortable accommodation, a sauna, children’s playground and a delightful outdoor swimming pool in a quiet and peaceful village location.

Guest rooms are individually decorated in a rustic style. They feature a flat-screen TV, minibar, private bathroom, work desk and tea and coffee making facilities.

At The Lunch Under The Tree, enjoy views of the Mountain Victory Mountain, which features in several Cezanne paintings.

Stay in touch using the free Wi-Fi. Start the day with the buffet breakfast. Later, enjoy a meal in the restaurant.

A wide variety of seasonal menus are served using fresh, local ingredients. In the surrounding area you will find golf, horse riding and mountain walks.

Aix-en-Provence is just 10 minutes away. For your convenience, there is free on-site parking at the Logis.
