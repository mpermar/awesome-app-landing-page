Description

La Villa Mauresque is a hotel located right on the seafront with far-reaching views of the Gulf of St Tropez.

Guests can enjoy free access to the hotel’s swimming pool, seafront spa with hot tub, sauna, beach and boathouse with sailing equipment.

Free WiFi access is provided in all guest rooms. Most of the rooms at La Villa Mauresque have a view of the sea and some have swimming pool or garden views.

The bathrooms have a shower and/or spa bath. Each is equipped with a Nespresso machine, a fruit bowl and free Hermès toiletries.

The hotel serves a daily breakfast, which can be taken on the private terrace, by the sea or near the pool.

The on-site restaurant, Bougainvillier, is managed by a Michelin-starred chef, who offers fixed menus, à la carte dining and daily suggestions.

Guests can enjoy many activities thanks to its free canoes, kayaks, snorkelling material, table tennis and children's toys.

Massages, beauty treatments, babysitting, fitness and yacht hire services are available.
