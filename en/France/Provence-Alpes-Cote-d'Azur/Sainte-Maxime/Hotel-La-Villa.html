Description

Hôtel La Villa is located on the beachfront opposite Garonnette Beach, an 8-minute drive from the centre of Sainte-Maxime and 150 metres from Issambres Port, where guests can take the ferry to Saint-Tropez in summer.

A park with picnic tables can be accessed free of charge next to the hotel. It offers air-conditioned and soundproofed guestrooms and suites with free WiFi, a minibar and satellite TV.

The soundproofed apartments have a living room and some offer a view of the Mediterranean Sea.

For stays of 3 nights or more, guests have access to a private kitchenette equipped with a fridge, dishwasher and microwave for an extra fee.

A breakfast can be served on the terrace under the olive trees in the summer months.

The on-site bar is open all day and serves a variety of drinks and ice creams. Guests can also order a light meal of local tapas-style ingredients onsite, or can eat at the onsite restaurant upon reservation.

An array of activities are offered in the area, such as windsurfing, scuba diving, fishing and balneotherapy.
