Description

Refuge de l'Eterlou is located at the base of ski runs in the Joue du Loup ski station, 46 km north of Gap.

Free public Wi-Fi access and a sauna are available from this chalet-style hotel. Views of Dévoluy Massif are available from each soundproofed room.

All rooms have their own bathroom with bath or shower. A continental breakfast is available every morning at Refuge de l'Eterlou.

Guests can also relax with a drink from the bar while enjoying the views from the terrace.

Free private parking is provided on site. The hotel is a 20-minute drive from restaurants and shops in Veynes.
