Description

Hôtel Club Vacanciel Les Issambres is situated in a building from 1931 on the Gulf of Saint Tropez.

It is located between Saint Maxime and Saint Raphael and stands in a 4-hectare park with an outdoor swimming pool, private beach access, tennis courts and mini-golf.

The hotel offers air-conditioned rooms with a flat-screen TV. Some rooms have a terrace, and all rooms have an en suite bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer.

The Pavillon rooms are located in an annex building A free baby kit is available upon request.

Bed linen, towels and end of stay cleaning are provided. At the on-site restaurant, Le Rigalou, guests can enjoy Provençal seasonal and regional specialties while admiring the views of the gardens from the terrace or solarium.

The on-site bar, 1931, overlooks the gardens and offers champagne and cocktails. Kids clubs include day and evening activities such as sports tournaments, games, bingo and karaoke.

Free WiFi access is available in public areas of the hotel and free private parking is available on site.
