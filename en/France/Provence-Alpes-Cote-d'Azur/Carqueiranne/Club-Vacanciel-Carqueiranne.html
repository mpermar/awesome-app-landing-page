Description

Featuring a seasonal outdoor pool, hydro-massage pool and hot tub, Club Vacanciel Carqueiranne is set in Carqueiranne.

This 3-star hotel offers a tennis court, garden, table tennis table, children's playground and on-site mini golf course.

The guest rooms come with a desk, wardrobe, flat-screen cable TV and private bathroom, while certain rooms come with a terrace.

The villas include a kitchenette with a microwave, as well as a dining table and furnished terrace.

The daily breakfast offers continental and buffet options. The on-site restaurant features a terrace overlooking the sea and pool.

Further activities at the hotel include beach volleyball, archery and table football.

Watersports can be enjoyed nearby and several classes are available on site including aquagym and Zumba.

Music groups regularly perform at the hotel. Saint-Tropez is 56 km from the hotel, while Cassis is 55 km from the property.
