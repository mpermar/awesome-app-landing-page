Description

Offering an outdoor pool, 2 tennis courts, a volleyball pitch, a boules pitch and table tennis tables, Domaine Le Clos des Oliviers is located in Vidauban.

The property also features a football pitch and a basketball field. Free WiFi is provided.

Each accommodation will provide you with a TV with French channels and a terrace with a barbecue and outdoor furniture.

There is a full kitchen with a dishwasher and a microwave. Private bathrooms have a bath or shower.

Superior villas are air-conditioned and have ironing facilities and a flat-screen TV.

At Domaine Le Clos des Oliviers you will find a bar and snack bar open from June to mid-September.

There is also a children's playground and a bouncy castle. In July and August, there is entertainment for children, teenagers and adults and sport activities are also organised.

This holiday park is 30 km from both Frejus and Saint-Raphael. Arcs - Draguignan Train Station is 7 km away.
