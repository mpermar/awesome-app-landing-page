Description

La Villa Douce is a 4-star hotel located in Le Rayol-Canadel-sur-Mer, 1.5 km from the beach and 13 km from Le Lavandou.

The hotel has a heated, seasonal outdoor pool and views of the sea, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or in the wine and tapas bar.

Free WiFi is offered throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and features a flat-screen TV. The Suites feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

A terrace or balcony with sea views are featured in all the rooms. Extras include bathrobes, slippers and Christian Lacroix toiletries.

2 of the superior rooms are adapted for wheelchair users. An organic breakfast is served every day and includes sweet and savoury items such as cakes, muffins, eggs and jam.

Homemade cakes as well as gluten-free and organic produce are also provided. From 16:00 to 18:00, sweet treats, juice and alcohol-free drinks are available to guests for free.

The restaurants has terraces with views of the Mediterranean Sea and offers dishes inspired by the local cuisine.

During the high season, you will find free shuttle service at the property, available for your journeys in Rayol Canadel.

Electric bike rental, massages and beauty treatments are available at this hotel.

The area is popular for snorkelling and hiking. Saint-Tropez is 25 km away. The nearest airport is Toulon - Hyeres Airport, 37 km from the property.
