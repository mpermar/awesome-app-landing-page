Description

For a traditional Provençal experience, Le Moulin dAure provides a peaceful, charming haven and a warm friendly welcome.

From the shady olive groves providing a respite from the Provençal sun, to the rustic-style furniture, to the cool and inviting outdoor swimming pool and extensive gardens, Le Moulin is a perfect blend of Provence and Italy.

Read a book, soak up the sun or enjoy a drink on the poolside terrace. The individually decorated rooms are all air conditioned and equipped with a hydro-jet bath or shower.

Wi-Fi internet is also available and free of charge. Enjoy a meal in the hotel restaurant for a true gastronomic experience.

The Mediterranean-influenced cuisine is made using only the freshest ingredients fom the local market, and olive oil produced from the hotels own olive grove.

Le Moulin d'Aure is the perfect base from which to discover the delights of the region.

Explore the quaint villages in the Valley of Les Baux de Provence, Avignon, Arles and St.

Remy and the beautiful landscapes of the Luberon mountains, the Alpilles and the Camargue.

So for a taste of Provençal life, look no further than Le Moulin dAure where you are sure to enjoy your stay.
