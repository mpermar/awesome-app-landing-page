Description

La Bastide des Arts offers rooms set in a house dating from 1930, located 3 km from L’Isle sur la Sorgue and a 30-minute drive from Avignon.

It features free Wi-Fi access and has a garden with an outdoor swimming pool. Rooms have a mix of modern and barque décor, and all include a private bathroom with a bath or shower and a hairdryer.

You can relax in the shared living room or on the veranda. A homemade continental breakfast is served daily, and a savoury breakfast is possible on request.
