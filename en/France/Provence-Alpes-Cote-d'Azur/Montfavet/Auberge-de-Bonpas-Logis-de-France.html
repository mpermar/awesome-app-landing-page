Description

Auberge de Bonpas is 12 km from the centre of Avignon and 2 km from Avignon - Caumont Airport.

It offers an outdoor swimming pool, free Wi-Fi and a restaurant with a terrace. Parties are organised on Friday evenings.

Rooms and apartments at Auberge de Bonpas are soundproofed and feature air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and a wardrobe.

A private bathroom with free toiletries, a hairdryer and a bath or shower is provided.

Guests can enjoy a daily buffet-style breakfast at the property and Provencal specialities can be sampled every evening at the hotel’s restaurant.

There is also a bar on site. This hotel is 500 metres from Chartreuse de Bonpas and Avignon Cathedral is an 18-minute drive away.
