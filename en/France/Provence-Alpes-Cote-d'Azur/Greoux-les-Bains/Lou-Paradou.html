Description

The Lou Paradou hotel is located in Greoux-les-Bains, by the Verdon River, next to the lavender fields in the heart of the Provence.

It is set within a large park and offers a heated, seasonal and heated outdoor pool and an infrared sauna and steam bath.

The Lou Paradou offers air-conditioned studios equipped with a kitchenette, free Wi-Fi access in the rooms and a private balcony or terrace.

Some studios have a view of the park and air conditioning. A breakfast serving jams and fruit syrups both homemade is offered.

The hotel is just 400 metres from Greoux village pedestrian centre and there is free on-site parking.

Guests can visit the Verdon Gorge, the largest canyon in Europe, the Luberon villages and Haute-Provence hilltop villages.

Local leisure activities around Gréoux include hiking, mountain-bike, white-water sports, gliding and rock climbing.

Marseille Provence Airport is situated 80 km from the Hotel and Aix-en-Provence TGV Train Station is 70 km away.
