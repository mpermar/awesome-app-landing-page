Description

Directly on the sea shore, nhow Marseille at Palm Beach is in Prado Bay, 2.5 mi from Marseille center. The hotel is ideally located for exploring Marseille and the cultural attractions the city has to offer, while allowing guests to relax at the seaside. It offers an outdoor swimming pool, a spa center for an additional charge and panoramic sea and mountain views.

This design hotel has modern air-conditioned rooms equipped with free WiFi access and an LCD satellite TV. Many feature a marine-themed décor and some have a balcony or views of the sea.

Guests of nhow Marseille at Palm Beach can enjoy breakfast on the terrace overlooking the Bay. There is a restaurant and 3 bars site where guests can relax.

Additional facilities at the hotel include a fitness center. Guests may also order a massage or relax in the hammam or hot tub. There is 24-hour reception at the hotel.

La Canebiere and Vieux Port are 2.5 mi away and the Orange Velodrome Stadium is 1.9 mi away. The hotel’s valet service can park vehicles in the outdoor or indoor car park.

La Corniche is a great choice for travelers interested in beaches, museums and scenery.
