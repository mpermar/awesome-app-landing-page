Description

Belambra Clubs La Colle-sur-Loup - Les Terrasses De Saint-Paul De Vence is situated in 20 hectares of pine forest and just a 20-minute drive from Nice.

It offers self-catering apartments, an outdoor swimming pool and a range of leisure activities for adults and children.

Each air-conditioned apartment is brightly decorated and has a living area with satellite TV.

Most have access to a terrace with outdoor furniture. A kitchenette with electric hotplates, a microwave and a dishwasher is provided in the apartment.

The on-site restaurant serves seasonal dishes on the shaded terrace. The holiday park hosts a children’s club and also has 2 playgrounds and a mini-golf course.

Adults can enjoy 2 tennis courts and volleyball and basketball courts on site. WiFi is available for an extra charge.

Free private parking is provided at the entrance to this resort. Antibes TGV Station is 15 km from Belambra Clubs La Colle-sur-Loup - Les Terrasses De Saint-Paul De Vence.
