Description

Set on the hills of Mougins, once home to Picasso, Le Manoir de L'Etang is a beautiful manor house from the 19th-century situated in a park overlooking a small lake covered with Lotus flowers.

The guest rooms of the hotel are air-conditioned and spacious. Most of them overlook the lake, and all of them boast view of the nearby hills.

The beds have refined Egyptian cotton sheets and bathroom linen is of the finest quality.

A charming outdoor swimming pool is available on-site, as well as large landscaped gardens.

The hotel can provide fitness trainings, massages and beauty treatments to meet all your needs.

The restaurant offers a regularly changing menu including homemade beef, fish and vegetable ravioli to be be enjoyed in the manor house or on one of the terraces.

The gourmet chef Mathieu Soler has conceived the menu, including tempura and macarons.

The Manoir enjoys ideal location for a restful break on the French Riviera. Cannes, with its extensive shopping and great beaches, is set few kilometres away.

In the hills nearby you also will discover many picturesque villages such as Mougins, Valbonne and Biot.
