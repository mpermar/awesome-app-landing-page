Description

Chateau des Magnans contains 3 buildings and a castle, located in Jausiers in La Vallée de l'Ubaye.

It offers an indoor pool and a furnished terrace and guests benefit from free access to a wellness centre with a sauna, hammam and hot tub.

Each apartment or studio is soundproof and some have a terrace or balcony with views over the valley.

They feature a fully equipped kitchenette, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

1 of the buildings offers lift access, and the 3 others have access to the accommodation via a staircase.

For an extra charge, a continental breakfast is served in the property's dining room.

Guests can use the free WiFi in common areas. Guests can use the laundry service for an additional charge.

There are ranges of outdoors activities available nearby the Chateau des Magnans include horse riding, rafting and hiking in the Mercantour National Park.

The hotel is located at the start of the Col de Bonette, a popular route for cyclists.

It is 6 km from Barcelonette and a 20-minute drive from the Italian border. Guests can park free of charge in the car park or garage and a free shuttle service to nearby villages and ski stations is available.
