Description

Offering free WiFi and a garden, Village Vacances Oustal Del Mar is set in Le Lavandou.

There is a games room on site and guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Saint Clair Beach is 1.

9 km from the property. Free private parking is available on site. There is a private bathroom with a shower in every unit.

Towels are available. Oustal Del Mar also includes a children's playground. Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant.
