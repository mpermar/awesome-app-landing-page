Description

The Logis is located a 10-minutes drive from Aix-en-Provence and Aubagne. It has an outdoor swimming pool and en-suite guestrooms with TV, air conditioning and free Wi-Fi.

The Fenière's restaurant offers traditional dishes made with fresh produce, served on a shaded terrace.

Breakfast can be enjoyed in your room, upon request and at a supplement. Guests of the Logis Hôtel La Fenière can relax in the hotel's garden and enjoy hiking and cycling in the surrounding countryside.
