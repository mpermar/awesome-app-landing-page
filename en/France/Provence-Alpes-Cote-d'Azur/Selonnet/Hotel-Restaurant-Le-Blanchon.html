Description

Hotel - Restaurant Le Blanchon is located in Chabanon, 6 km from Selonnet and at the bottom of the ski slopes.

The property is a 3-minute walk from the ski lifts and offers views on the surrounding mountains.

The rooms at Hotel Le Blanchon come with a wardrobe and a flat-screen TV. Some of them also feature a balcony.

A continental breakfast and traditional dishes are available in the hotel's restaurant.

Packed lunch can be provided for a surcharge. Additional facilities include meeting and banquet facilities.

The property can organise musical evenings upon request for special events such as birthdays, weddings and seminars.

It is situated 40 km from Barcelonnette and 45 km from the city of Gap. A public outdoor pool is available an 8-minute walk away and is at an extra charge.
