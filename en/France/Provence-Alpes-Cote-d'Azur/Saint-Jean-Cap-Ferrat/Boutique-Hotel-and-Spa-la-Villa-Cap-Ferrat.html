Description

Located on Saint-Jean-Cap-Ferrat peninsula, Hotel & Spa la Villa Cap Ferrat is a 5-minute walk from the sea and the harbour.

Located 12 km from Monaco, this boutique hotel offers an outdoor swimming pool and spectacular views of the sea.

All rooms are air-conditioned and feature a private bathroom, flat-screen TV, a Nespresso coffee machine and a mini-fridge.

Each room also has free WiFi access and some offer a view of the sea, pool or garden.

A buffet breakfast is served daily and can be enjoyed on the terrace next to the swimming pool.

Tea and coffee making facilities are provided in the rooms. Local activities nearby include cycling and hiking, and guests can borrow bicycles for free.

A sauna and jacuzzi can be enjoyed for free on site and Rothschild Villa is 350 metres away.

For your comfort, the hotel holds a limited, private car park. The bus stop for buses to Nice is 100 metres from the hotel.

Nice-Ville Train Station is 10 km from the hotel and Nice Côte d'Azur Airport is 17 km away.
