Description

Domaine de Fontenille is located in Lauris, 5 km from Lourmarin. Surrounded by the Luberon hills, the Montagne Sainte Victoire and trees, the property is ideally situated for exploring the Luberon Regional Park and for visiting Provencal villages.

The 18 rooms and suites and the apartment are bright and spacious and offer an elegant and contemporary décor.

Amenities include air conditioning, soundproofing, a flat-screen TV, an iPod docking station and a safe.

Free WiFi is provided. The park includes a 20-metre long outdoor seasonally heated swimming pool, hammam, fitness centre, pathways, boules court and massage cabin.

There is also an on-site farm with donkeys, sheep and hens. The gourmet restaurant Le Champs des Lunes, and the La Cuisine d'Amélie bistrot restaurant are led by chef Jérôme Faure.

Guests can taste original meals prepared with local seasonal produce. In the summer, you can have meals on the terrace, enjoying the views of the park.

A library and various lounge areas are available for guests to relax and the reception is open 24 hours a day.

Activities in the surroundings include hiking, horse riding and cycling with the hotel also offering bike hire.

In the summer, guests can participate in on-site events such as music nights and outdoor cinema.

The domain's wine cellar and tasting shop are open to visitors all year round. An art gallery is also available.

Domaine de Fontenille is 40 km from Aix-en-Provence and 64 km from Avignon TGV Train Station.

The nearest airport is Marseille Provence Airport, a 50-minute drive away. Free private parking is available on site.
