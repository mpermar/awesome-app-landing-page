Description

Located in Saint-Véran, the highest village in Europe, just 58 km away from Briançon, Hôtel & Spa L'Alta Peyra offers ski-to-door access to the ski slopes.

Guests can enjoy both the indoor and outdoor swimming pool, spa and fitness centre.

Some rooms at Hôtel & Spa L'Alta Peyra offer a balcony with mountain views and free WiFi.

Some rooms have a seating area with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bathrobe, slippers and a bath or shower.

All rooms at the hotel are decorated in soft, natural colours. Every morning a buffet breakfast is served.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the brasserie and grill or at the gastronomic-style restaurant, and guests can relax with a drink at the bar.

The Briançon Train Station is 58 km away and the Italian border is 16 km away. Private parking is available at the hotel at an extra cost.
