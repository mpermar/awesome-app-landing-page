Description

Offering a restaurant, Le Bacchu Ber is located in Briançon. Set in a property originally built in the 18th century, this guest house offers a communal lounge with fireplace and a view of the mountains or the village.

Rooms here will provide you with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and free Wi-Fi access.

Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

The continental breakfast on site includes local products and homemade jams. The property offers free parking.

The guest house is 2 km from Serre Chevalier and 300 metres from Briancon Train Station.
