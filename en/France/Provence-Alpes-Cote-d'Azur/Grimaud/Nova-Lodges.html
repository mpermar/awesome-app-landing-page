Description

Nova Lodges is located on the sea front in Les Prairies de la Mer resort in Port Grimaud.

It features a private beach with restaurants and bars, water sports facilities, a seasonal outdoor pool and a wellness centre.

The property is 7 km from Sainte-Maxime and Saint-Tropez. Free WiFi access is available.

The air-conditioned bungalows feature a terrace with a garden view. There is a fully equipped kitchen with a microwave and a refrigerator.

The private bathroom has a shower. A continental breakfast can be enjoyed at the restaurants on the sea front.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site such as tennis and fitness. Gymnastics classes or sport tournaments can be organised.

A spa including a hammam, sauna and hot tub is available for a surcharge, and massages are also available upon request.

This holiday park is 25 km from Saint-Raphael Train Station and 80 km from Nice Côte d'Azur Airport.
