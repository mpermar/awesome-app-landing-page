Description

Mobil Homes Vacances is a tour operator located in Les Prairies de la Mer Campsite in Port Grimaud, just 6 km away from Saint Tropez and Saint Maxime.

Guests can have a drink at the bar or enjoy a meal at one of the on-site restaurants.

The accommodations at Mobil Homes Vacances are air-conditioned and offer a seating area, a kitchen and a private bathroom.

Everyday guests can enjoy lunch and dinner at the restaurant, pizzeria or the fast food restaurant.

A mini supermarket can be found on site. The Saint Raphael Train Station is 25 km away and the Nice Airport is 80 km away.
