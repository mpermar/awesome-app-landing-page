Description

Featuring a seasonal outdoor heated swimming pool and set in a former mansion house, Hotel Villa des Anges is located 2.

3 km from Sainte-Maxime. Free WiFi is available throughout and Saint-Tropez is 13 km away.

The hotel features individually designed rooms with a flat-screen TV offering Canal+ and Bein Sports channels.

Some rooms offer a view of the bay of Saint Tropez and the swimming pool. The private bathroom includes free toiletries and a hairdryer.

A buffet breakfast is available, which can be enjoyed in the dining-room or on the terrace, where you can enjoy a panoramic view of the sea.

In the summer, the hotel offers a snack service by the swimming pool. Facilities nearby include a tennis court and Beauvallon Golf Club, which is 1.

9 km from the property. Massage is available on site for a surcharge. Port Grimaud is an 8-minute drive away.
