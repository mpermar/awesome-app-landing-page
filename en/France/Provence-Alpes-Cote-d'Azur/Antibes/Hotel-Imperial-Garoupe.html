Description

Situated on Cap d'Antibes, the Mediterranean-style Hotel Imperial Garoupe features an outdoor swimming pool.

This 5-star hotel offers rooms and suites that open to a balcony or terrace with garden views.

Free WiFi is available throughout. Guests benefit from reduced price access to the property's private beach.

All modern accommodation units come with air conditioning, a satellite flat-screen TV and a DVD player.

They also have a minibar, safe and a bathroom with a bath, free toiletries and hairdryer.

Guests can start their day with the continental breakfast served at the propertyâs dining area.

Open for dinner, the Pavillon restaurant serves gastronomic cuisine with new menu every day and guests can also eat at the Pavillon Beach restaurant, which offers a set menu.

The city centre of Cannes is 13 km away and Antibes train station is 4 km away. Nice International Airport is 25 km from Hotel Imperial Garoupe.

Free parking is possible on site and a charging station for electric cars is available.
