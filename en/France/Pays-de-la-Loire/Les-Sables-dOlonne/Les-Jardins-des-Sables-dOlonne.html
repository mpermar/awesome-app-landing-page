Description

Les Jardins du Château d'Olonne is composed of independent and self-catering villas, most of which have a private swimming pool.

They sit just 800 meters from the coast in Château-d’Olonne. The spacious villas at Les Jardins du Château d'Olonne each have a large kitchen with hot plates, a refrigerator, an oven, a dishwasher and a kettle and coffee maker.

The private bathrooms have a bath or shower. You will find a flat-screen TV, a DVD player and a radio with a CD player in the living room.

A large private terrace with sun loungers also feature in the villas. On site there is also a mini golf, a tennis court, table tennis and a children’s playground.

Guests also have access to the heated, residence's pool, which is open and heated from 15 June to 15 September.

The bar, le Petit Rotterdam, is open from 08:00 to 22:00 and serves breakfast and light snacks during the summer season.

Freshly baked bread and pastries can be bought on site. Free WiFi access is available in every room and free private parking is provided on site.
