Description

Set on the Wild Coast sea front in Les Sables-d'Olonne, La Vigie Vacances is just steps from the beach.

You can admire the sea views and Armandèche Lighthouse from the bar’s terrace. The studios and cottages include a living room with an LCD TV, which leads onto a furnished terrace.

Each one also provides a fully equipped kitchenette. A continental breakfast is served every morning at La Vigie Vacances, which can be enjoyed in the comfort of guest accommodation.

You can also try ice cream at the bar. Free WiFi is available in the entire property and free private parking is possible on site.
