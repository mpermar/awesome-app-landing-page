Description

Situated in Angers, 650 metres from Terra Botanica Park, Domitys Rosa Gallica provides self-catering accommodation in a senior residence.

Terra Botanica Tram stop is only 160 metres away, providing access to Angers city centre.

All apartments feature a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom with shower and a fully equipped kitchenette.

Some units have a seating area and a terrace or balcony. A continental or à la carte breakfast is served every morning at the property's bar.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant, which is open every day of the year and serves a variety of French dishes.

Guests have free access to the indoor pool, sauna and fitness room at Domitys Rosa Gallica.

Complimentary WiFi is offered throughout the property and free private parking is also provided.

The A11 motorway is right next to the property. Faculty of Medicine Angers is 2. 5 km from Domitys and Angers Conference centre is 4 km away.
