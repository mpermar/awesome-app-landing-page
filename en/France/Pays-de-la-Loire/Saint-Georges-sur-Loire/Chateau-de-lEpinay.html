Description

Set in an 18th-century château, this elegant hotel is surrounded by a 25-hectare park with a swimming pool and offers free Wi-Fi.

Guests are free to relax by the fireplace, watch a film in the cinema room and play billiards, table tennis or piano.

The guest rooms at Château de l'Epinay are individually decorated and include a fireplace and a TV.

Each room has a view of the garden, a seating area and an en suite bathroom with bathrobes.

Breakfast is served every morning and includes fresh pastries, homemade jam and fresh eggs from the property’s hens.

The traditional-style restaurant serves organic and seasonal produce in a room with exposed beams, lusters and stone walls or on the terrace when the weather is fine.

A fitness room with the latest Technogym equipment is at guest’s disposal. Guests can enjoy a wellness centre with a hot tub and a hammam.

Beauty care and massages can be requested on site thanks to their partnership with the Cinq Mondes and Carita brands.

Free private parking is provided on site and the banks of the Loire River are 1. 5 km away.

The B&B is 7 km from Château de Serrant and a 15-minute drive from the centre of Angers.
