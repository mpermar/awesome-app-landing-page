Description

The Atlantic Gardens is a 3-star hotel set between a pine forest and a marina, 14 kilometers from Les Sables d'Olonne.

Choose among 135 high-quality rooms opening onto balconies with sea or forest views.

Take a dip in the heated indoor 26-metre pool, with integrated spa, or in the heated outdoor summer pool, unless you prefer to relax in the hotel's sauna and hammam.

Fitness enthusiasts will have the opportunity to do gymnastics, aqua gym or weightlifting, and even to play tennis or volleyball.

Other activities such as billiards and bowling are also available with a fee. Les Jardins de l'Atlantique also houses a bar with dance floor and a bowling alley, lounges and 15 meeting rooms for your seminars.

Gourmet will have the choice between two rooms in the restaurant renowned for its seafood buffets and many other delicacies.
