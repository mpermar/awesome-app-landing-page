Description

Set in Vendée, this pedestrian village is divided into 5 districts. It features charming apartments and houses, as well as a big lake, two 18-hole golf courses and 4 outdoor pools. Veillon beach is 1.6 mi away.

Enjoy the quietness of this 90-hectare estate and the privileged location within easy reach of the beach. Bicycle enthusiasts can take advantage of the bicycle paths.

The property includes a water park with a kid's pool and a water slide, and a club for teenagers.

Guests have free access to the swimming pools, playgrounds and sport complexes. Daytime and evening entertainment is also offered, and a kids' club and a club for teenagers are available.
