Description

Situated next to Le Mans train station, a 9-minute walk from Culture and Convention Centre of Le Mans, Campanile Le Mans Centre - Gare in Le Mans features a shared lounge and bar.

Featuring on-site dining, the 3-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with a private bathroom.

All guest rooms have a desk. A buffet breakfast is served each morning at the property.

Languages spoken at the reception include English and French. Attractions in the area include the Circuit24 du Mans, situated a 15-minute drive away, and Saint Julien's Cathedral, 2 km away.

The Cité Plantagenêt Old Town of Le Mans is a 20-minute walk away and the MMA headquarters are 1 km away.

Le Mans Town Hall is 2 km from Campanile Le Mans Centre - Gare. The nearest airport is Le Mans Arnage Airport, 7 km away.

Tours Loire Valley Airport is 94 km from the property and Rennes Airport is 162 km away.

Private parking is available on request subject to availability, and the hotel also has a partnership with a nearby car park.
