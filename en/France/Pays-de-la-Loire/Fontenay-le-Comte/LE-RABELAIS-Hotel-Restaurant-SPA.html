Description

Logis Le Rabelais sits in a quiet park with a seasonal outdoor heated pool, terrace and flowered garden.

There is also a sauna, fitness room and massage treatments upon request. Decorated with warm, calm colours, all rooms at Logis Le Rabelais features a private bathroom, a TV with cable channels and a desk with internet access.

Some of them have a view of the park. The restaurant serves daily buffet breakfast, either in the dining room or in the guest rooms.

Guests can also sample inventive and regional cuisine, prepared by the chef, in the dining room or on the terrace.

Located between La Rochelle and the Puy du Fou themed park, discover Fontenay le Comte historic town and its rich Middle Age and Renaissance heritage.
