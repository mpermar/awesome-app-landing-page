Description

Manoir De Ponsay is located in the countryside in Chantonnay, just 30 minutes from the Puy du Fou theme park.

It features an outdoor swimming pool. Guest rooms at Manoir De Ponsay are spacious and decorated with wooden antique furnishings.

Some of them have four-poster beds. Every morning a traditional daily breakfast is served.

In the evenings, dinner can be served upon request 24 hours in advance. The Manoir is close to La Venise Verte and Nieul sur l’Autise Abbey.

There are opportunities for hiking, cycling and horse riding in the surrounding region.
