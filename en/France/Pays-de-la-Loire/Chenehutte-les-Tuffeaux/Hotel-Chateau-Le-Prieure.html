Description

Chateau Le Prieure is offering accommodations in Chènehutte-les-Tuffeaux, 13 mi from Doue la Fontaine Zoo. Boasting a gastronomic restaurant and a terrace with panoramic views of the Loire River, this property also provides guests with a bar and meeting rooms.

All rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels, desk and mini-bar. The private bathroom has a bath, free toiletries and hairdryer.

Guests can have a meal at the property's gastronomic restaurant, le Castellane.

Guests at the accommodations will be able to enjoy activities in and around Saint-Martin-de-la-Place, like hiking.

Angers is 22 mi from Chateau Le Prieure, while Saumur is 5 mi away. The nearest airport is Tours Loire Valley Airport, 42 mi from the property.
