Description

Situated in Saumur, in the Maine-et-Loire region, Belambra Clubs Saumur - Le Domaine Du Golf features an indoor swimming pool in a beautiful 4 acre park.

WiFi access is available throughout the property at an extra charge. Guest rooms at Belambra Clubs Saumur - Le Domaine Du Golf all have a terrace and are decorated in a contemporary style.

They are equipped with a flat-screen TV, fully equipped kitchenette and barbeque facilities.

Onsite facilities include a children’s playground and bicycle rental. There is also a golf course, only 150 metres away from the site.

Belambra Clubs Saumur - The Domaine Du Golf is only 5km from Saumur train station.
