Description

Located a 10-minute drive from the centre of Angers, within the Arville commune, Logis Du Cavier is a 5-minute drive from the Terra Botanica Park.

It is also a 2-minute drive from Atoll Shopping Centre and a 5-minute drive from Aquavita Aquatic Centre.

The hotel is located on the site of an old windmill and guests of the Logis Du Cavier can enjoy their breakfast on the patio by the swimming pool during nice weather.

There are 2 restaurants on site, including Le Croix Cadeau, which has a large terrace overlooking the windmill and serves meals such as fish, meat.

pizza and pancakes. Le Cavier serves local produce and is set in the cellar of the functioning windmill.

There are 3 function rooms available for your events and there is also a children’s playground.

Guests of the Logis Du Cavier can enjoy hiking and cycling in the surrounding Loire countryside.

The hotel is situated 8 km from the A11/E60 motorway and free private parking is provided.
