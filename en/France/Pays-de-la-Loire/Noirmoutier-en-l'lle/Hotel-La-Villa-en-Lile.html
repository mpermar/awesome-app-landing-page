Description

Located in Noirmoutier-en-l'ile, La Villa en L'île Hotel is located at the edge of the Bois de la Chaise forest, just a 10-minute walk from Plage des Dames Beach and 800 meters from Noirmoutier Castle.

It offers a seasonal outdoor heated swimming pool, an indoor heated swimming pool and a Spa accessible upon prior request.

All of the rooms and suites at Hôtel La Villa en L'île feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a desk and a private bathroom with hairdryer and free toiletries.

Some of the rooms also have a terrace. A continental breakfast is served as a buffet each morning in the breakfast room or on the terrace.

There is a bar on site and restaurants can be found 300 metres from the hotel. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout at Hôtel La Villa en L'île.

Free private parking is possible at the hotel and the salt marches are 2 km away.
