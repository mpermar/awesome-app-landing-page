Description

Located 800 metres from Noirmoutier en l’Ile Harbour and Plage des Dames, La Chaize offers a heated indoor pool and a spa.

Free Wi-Fi access is provided throughout. Guest rooms at La Chaize feature a flat-screen TV and a telephone.

Some of them also have a terrace. They are uniquely-decorated and have a private bathroom with free toiletries and a bathtub or a shower.

A buffet breakfast is available every morning and includes homemade pastries such as sablés bretons or moelleux aux noisettes.

Activities nearby include nautical activities, hiking on the island and horse riding at Centre Equestre de l’Herbaudière.
