Description

Situated in L'Île-d'Olonne, 37 km from Saint-Jean-de-Monts, Camping Ile aux Oiseaux features a seasonal outdoor swimming pool and free WiFi.

Each unit has a terrace, a fully equipped kitchen with a microwave, a seating area with a sofa, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with shower.

For added convenience, the property can provide towels and bed linen for an extra charge.

The camping offers a children's playground. Guests can also relax in the garden, beside the indoor pool, or in the shared lounge area.

Les Sables-d'Olonne is 10 km from Camping l'Ile aux Oiseaux while Saint-Gilles-Croix-de-Vie is 20 km from the property.
