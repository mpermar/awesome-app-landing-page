Description

At Au Val des Cèdres, rooms come with a desk. Rooms have a flat-screen TV, and some rooms also offer a balcony. All guest rooms will provide guests with a microwave.

At Val des Cedres offers a continental or à la carte breakfast.

Au Val des Cèdres has a playground. Guests can enjoy a selection of activities in the surrounding areas, including cycling, fishing and hiking.

Angers is 11 miles from Au Val des Cedres, while The Arrow is 21 miles away.
