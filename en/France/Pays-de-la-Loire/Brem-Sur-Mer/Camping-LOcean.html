Description

Only 600 metres from beaches, Camping L'Ocean is located in Brem-sur-Mer. There is a large, aquatic leisure centre with waterslides, hot tub and different swimming pools for children and adults.

The mobile homes all include a furnished terrace, a bathroom and a kitchen with a microwave, fridge and coffee machine.

Some are air-conditioned. TVs can be rented on site, and washing facilities are available.

A seasonal snack bar and a grocery shop can be found at Camping L'Ocean. Guests can also rent barbecue facilities from the property.

Only 13 km from Sables dOlonne Train Station, Camping L'Ocean offers free, private parking on site and bike rental.
