Description

Among the facilities of this property are a shared lounge and luggage storage space, along with free WiFi.

The hotel has a garden and offers a terrace. At Hôtel le Corbusson, the rooms come with a desk, a TV and a private bathroom.

All guest rooms will provide guests with a wardrobe and a kettle. Continental and buffet breakfast options are available every morning at the hotel.

Evening meals are available upon prior reservation only. Guests at Hôtel le Corbusson will be able to enjoy activities in and around Saint-Berthevin, like hiking and fishing.

Laval is 8 km from the accommodation, while Vitré is 29 km from the property. The nearest airport is Rennes Airport, 72 km from Hôtel le Corbusson.
