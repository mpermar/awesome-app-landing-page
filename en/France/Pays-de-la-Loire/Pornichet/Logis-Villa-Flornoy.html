Description

Located in central Pornichet, only 300 metres from a sandy beach, Villa Flornoy features a heated, covered swimming pool set in a landscaped garden and an on-site restaurant.

Free Wi-Fi access is provided throughout the property. The soundproofed guest rooms are individually decorated and include a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

Additional facilities include a safety deposit box and a TV with cable and satellite channels.

A breakfast made from locally grown, seasonal products is available daily and may be enjoyed in the rooms or in Villa Flornoy’s green patio.

For evening meals, the restaurant offers a set menu that varies every day. Guests can book a massage on site or rent a bike from the property.

Free, public parking is available nearby. Popular outdoor activities in the area include water sports and hiking.
