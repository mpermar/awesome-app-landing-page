Description

Set in the heart of the Jura vineyards, Hôtel Auberge de Chavannes is located in Chavanne and provides soundproofed accommodations.

You can relax on the furnished terrace, by the garden. With a view of the park, all of the air-conditioned rooms are elegantly decorated and have wooden flooring.

They are all equipped with a desk, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom with a bath or a shower.

One of the rooms also includes a private terrace. A buffet breakfast is served daily in the communal lounge or on the terrace.

A restaurant is available on site and offers organic meals and wines. Hôtel Auberge de Chavannes features free Wi-Fi.

Free private parking is also possible on site and Lons-le-Saunier thermal baths are 10 km from the hotel.
