Description

At Maison d'hôtes & SPA La Scierie, each room comes with a closet and a flat-screen TV. The rooms come with a private bathroom, while some rooms come with a balcony. At Maison d'hôtes & SPA La Scierie, every room is equipped with a seating area.

Bed & Breakfast La Scierie offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

The property has a hot tub and a sauna. You can play pool at Maison d'hôtes & SPA La Scierie.

Besançon is 22 miles from Maison d'hôtes & SPA La Scierie. The nearest airport is Tavaux Airport, 24 miles from the property.
