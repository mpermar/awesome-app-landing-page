Description

This beautiful 18th-century manor sits in a large garden with furnished terrace, in the heart of Saint-Martin-de-Ré. It has a spa and elegant guest rooms, each with a personalized decor.

All of the rooms’ names at La Baronnie - Hôtel & Spa are inspired by the island's prestigious past. Most of them overlook the garden or the town's rooftops.

A full buffet breakfast is served every morning in the country-style dining room, on the terrace or in the garden. Many restaurants are located near La Baronnie. Guests will find a bar, between the living room and the garden, to enjoy a drink.

Accessible at an extra cost, the spa facilities include a counter-current swimming pool, sauna, whirlpool and a treatment room. Guests can enjoy a private use of the sauna and hot tub provided additional charges.

From La Baronnie - Hôtel & Spa, you have an easy access to the port. It is 6.8 mi from the bridge leading to La Rochelle. Private parking is possible on site.
