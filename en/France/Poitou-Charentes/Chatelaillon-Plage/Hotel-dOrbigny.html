Description

Set in a garden with outdoor pool, this hotel is 200 metres from the city centre and 50 metres from Châtelaillon Beach.

It offers en suite rooms with a flat-screen TV and Wi-Fi access. A buffet breakfast is provided every morning at the d’Orbigny, and it can be enjoyed on the terrace beside the swimming pool.

You can enjoy a drink at the bar, and daily newspapers are available. Guests can borrow beach towels and parasols.

A security deposit is required. Board games and books are also available. Hotel d’Orbigny is 15 km from La Rochelle, where guests can take a ferry boat to the Oleron Island.

Private parking and bike rental are possible upon reservation on site and at an extra cost.
