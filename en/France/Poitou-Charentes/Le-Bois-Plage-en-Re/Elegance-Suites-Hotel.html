Description

Just 150 metres from Atlantic beaches, this hotel is 650 metres from the centre of Bois Plage-en-Ré.

Elégance Suites Hôtel offers a heated outdoor pool and spacious guest rooms with air conditioning and free WiFi access.

With individual terraces, the suites are decorated in warm, neutral tones and equipped with a whirlpool bath.

Each includes a seating area, a minibar and a kettle for making hot drinks. A continental breakfast is served every morning at Elégance Suites Hôtel.

There is also a restaurant and bar on site offering gourmet local cuisine. In summer, dinner can be served on the terrace along the pool.

The property offers free access to the spa center with sauna, steam room, hot tub, massage and beauty center located 50 metres away.

Guests can also relax in the air-conditioned lounge. To explore the island, bicycle rental and motorboat tours are available at the reception.

Free private parking is available at the hotel. La Rochelle-Ile-de-Ré Airport is a 20-minute drive away.
