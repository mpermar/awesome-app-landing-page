Description

Hotel Les Cleunes is located in Saint-Trojan-les-Bains, on the Ile d'Oleron.

It has an outdoor pool and a sunbathing terrace. There is also an indoor spa area including a spa pool with massage jets, a sauna, a hammam and light therapy.

Each of the Cleunes’ guest rooms has a private bathroom, TV and free WiFi access.

Some rooms have a balcony or sea views. Guests can enjoy a drink in Albizia, the hotel’s bar or on the terrace overlooking the sea.

A buffet breakfast is served daily. A tennis court and a pool table are also featured on site.

Hotel Les Cleunes is set a 40-minute drive from Rochefort. Guests can enjoy modeling massage therapy in the hotel and water sports nearby.
