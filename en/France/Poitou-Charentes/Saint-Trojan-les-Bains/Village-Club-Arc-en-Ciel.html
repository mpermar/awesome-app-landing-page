Description

Located in Ile d'Oléron, Village Club Arc en Ciel is 800 metres from Saint-Trojan-les-Bains and 2 km from the nearest beaches.

It offers a heated swimming pool, a tennis court and free WiFi in public areas. Decorated in bright colours, the rooms are complete with a balcony or terrace and refrigerator.

All rooms have a private bathroom, while some also include views of the planted park.

All rooms offer free WiFi access and a TV. Dining options are available and a buffet breakfast is also served, where you can try local honey with fresh bread.

Guests are invited to watch the flat-screen TV in the lounge area. Numerous activities available at the village include volleyball, badminton, petanque facilities and table tennis.

A sauna and bike rental are available at an extra cost. Rochefort is located a 40-minute drive away and free private parking is provided on site.
