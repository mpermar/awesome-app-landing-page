Description

Hotel de l’Europe is located 400 metres from the thermal spa centres in La Roche-Posay.

It offers rooms with free Wi-Fi access, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. The hotel serves a buffet breakfast every morning.

The Europe hotel also has a garden with a children’s playground and table tennis.

The bedrooms are accessible to disabled guests. On the third floor, there is a guest lounge with a library, hospitality tray and ironing facilities.

Free private parking is available on site and the Hotel de l’Europe is just 1 kilometre from Connetable Golf Course.
