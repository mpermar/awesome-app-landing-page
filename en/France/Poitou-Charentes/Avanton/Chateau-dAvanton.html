Description

This charming guest house is set in a 16th-century chateau, just 4 km from Futuroscope and 9 km from Poitiers.

It offers a garden with swings and a swimming pool. All the studios and apartments are individually decorated with wooden furnishings, inspired by the history of France.

Some of the rooms are located in the attic with exposed wooden beams. Each has a private bathroom and a fully equipped kitchen.

Modern facilities include a TV and free Wi-Fi access. Every morning a continental breakfast is served at Château D'avanton.

Guests can enjoy views over the surrounding countryside and of the outdoor swimming pool.

Situated in the heart of the Poitou-Charentes region, this property is ideally situated for exploring the nearby medieval castles and villages.

With free private parking on site, Poitiers is just 9 km away and Chauvigny 37 km away.
