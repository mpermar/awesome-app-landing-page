Description

This hotel is situated in its own Mediterranean-style gardens on Oleron Island. It offers a heated swimming pool and is just 900 metres from beaches on the French Atlantic coast.

Each room at the Hôtel Le Vert Bois has a TV and its own private bathroom supplied with bathroom products.

Free Wi-Fi access is also provided. Every morning, a buffet breakfast is available in the hotel’s dining room.

Guests can eat in their room, on the shaded terrace or in the sunny breakfast room.

The hotel has some bikes that can be rented. Guests can take bike trails from right beside the hotel to the forest and beach.

Other popular outdoor activities include nautical sports and hiking. There is also a games room with table tennis on site.

Le Vert Bois hotel is 2 km from Dolus d'Oléron city centre, 41 km from Rochefort and its train station and 71 km from La Rochelle airport.
