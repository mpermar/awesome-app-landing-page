Description

This entirely air-conditioned hotel is located next to a peaceful park. It offers free Wi-Fi, a heated outdoor swimming pool accessible from mid June until mid September.

The property is easily accessible from the A10 and N10 roads. The Best Western Le Bois de la Marche provides free on-site parking and is a good base for visiting the area.

It is just 15 minutes from Parc de Futuroscope, 3 km from Poitiers and close to a wildlife park.

Guest rooms at the Best Western Le Bois de la Marche include a flat-screen TV, a CD player and a telephone.

Buffet breakfast can be enjoyed every day at Le Bois de la Marche. The restaurant serves traditional French cuisine.

During the summer season and depending on the weather, each Thursday and Friday evenings, afterworks including music, plancha and drinks are organised in the property's park.
