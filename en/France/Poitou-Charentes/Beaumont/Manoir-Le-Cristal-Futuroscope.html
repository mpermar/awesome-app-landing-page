Description

In Vienna B & B, Manoir Le Cristal (Futuroscope) is located at 20-minute drive from Poitiers.

It is set in an 17th-century mansion and surrounded by a garden with an outdoor swimming pool.

Manoir Le Cristal's rooms have free WiFi access. They offer a private bathroom with a bathrobe and a hairdryer.

Some rooms offer views of the lake view or the pool. Guests can enjoy a continental breakfast including homemade bread and jams, fruit and yogurts.

A restaurant serving traditional cuisine is located just 1 km away. Futuroscope Theme Park is 7 km away and the property offers 30% discount on park's entries.

Golf can be enjoyed just 3 km away and Chasseneuil-du-Poitou Train Station is a 14-minute drive from this property, where free parking is possible.
