Description

Set in a large park, this 19th-century mansion is located on the seafront with direct access to Nauzan beach.

It offers an outdoor swimming pool with a terrace. The different room types at the Hotel Residence De Rohan are decorated in a stylish manner, featuring parquet flooring.

All rooms are equipped with free WiFi, a flat-screen TV with Canal+ channels, and have a private bathroom with free toiletries.

A hearty buffet breakfast is served every morning, in the living room, on the terrace or in your bedroom.

There are several restaurants located at the foot of Rohan Residence. The Hotel Residence De Rohan is located only 3 kilometres from the popular seaside town of Royan and 2.
