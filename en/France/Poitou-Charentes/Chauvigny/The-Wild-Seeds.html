Description

The rooms at the bed and breakfast are equipped with a seating area. Rooms include a private bathroom with a bath and a hair dryer. All guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

A continental breakfast is served daily at the property.

Guests at The Wild Seeds will be able to enjoy activities in and around Chauvigny, like cycling.

Poitiers is 13 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Poitiers-Biard Airport, 15 miles from The Wild Seeds.

Policies

Children and Extra Beds
All children are welcome.
Up to two children under 12 years are charged EUR 15 per night when using existing beds.
All children under 2 years are charged EUR 10 per night in a crib.
All children under 12 years are charged EUR 15 per night for extra beds.
Any additional older children or adults are charged EUR 20 per night for extra beds.
The maximum number of extra beds in a room is 1.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
