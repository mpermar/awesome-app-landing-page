Description

Located in the heart of Saint-Palais-sur-Mer, Hôtel de la Plage dates back to 1870 and features air conditioning and an outdoor swimming pool, just 30 metres from Bureau Beach.

There is a terrace and free Wi-Fi. The rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV and a wardrobe.

They have a private bathroom with free toiletries, shower and hairdryer. Every morning, breakfast buffet is served.

The hotel’s restaurant serves seafood and traditional food. Newspapers are available in the à-la-carte restaurant.

A fitness centre, sauna and massages are available at a surcharge. Cordouan lighthouse is a 2-km drive away while Palmyre Zoological garden is 8 km away and the golf is 10 km away.
