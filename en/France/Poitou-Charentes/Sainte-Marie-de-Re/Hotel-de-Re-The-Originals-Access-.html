Description

A 10-minute walk from the centre of Sainte-Marie-de-Ré, this hotel features an outdoor, heated swimming pool.

Set in a garden, it offers a terrace, a bar and a paid private parking. Free WiFi is accessible in each room at The Originals Access, Hôtel de Ré (P'tit Dej-Hotel).

All are soundproofed, have a flat-screen TV and feature views of pool or the garden.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning before taking a 5-minute walk to the beaches.

A snack corner is available in the Suites, at an extra cost of EUR 15 per day. If you decide to cycle in the area, the hotel offers bike hire.

Other amenities include a children’s playground. La Rochelle TGV Train Station is 25 km from the hotel and La Rochelle-Île-de-Ré Airport is 11 km away.
