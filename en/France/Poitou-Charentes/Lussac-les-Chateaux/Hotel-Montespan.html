Description

Located in Lussac-les-Châteaux, Hôtel Montespan offers simple accommodation with free Wi-Fi, a 1000 m² garden with plants and furnished terraces, and a restaurant.

Both Lussac Train Station and the Museum of Prehistory are 500 metres from the hotel.

All of the rooms at Hôtel Montespan feature a TV, a wardrobe and a private bathroom with a bath or a shower.

Some of the rooms are suitable for guests with reduced mobility. A buffet breakfast is served each morning in the communal lounge.

Free Wi-Fi connection is available throughout the property. Free private parking is also possible on site and Poitiers is 35 km away.
