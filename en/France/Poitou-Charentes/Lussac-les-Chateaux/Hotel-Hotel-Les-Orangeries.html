Description

In the center of Lussac-les-Chateaux, Les Orangeries is a characteristic country-style house with an outdoor swimming pool and free WiFi. It has been awarded the European Ecolabel.

The hotel's rooms at the Orangeries are individually decorated, and some feature four-poster beds. Each is equipped with a TV and a private bathroom, and some rooms feature a balcony.

There is a restaurant on site serving meals prepared with local produce. Guests can relax in Les Orangeries’ garden, and there is also a children’s playground. Free private parking is also available.

The hotel is located in the Poitou-Charentes region. It is a 35-minute drive from Poitiers and 45 minutes from the Futuroscope Theme Park. A charging station for electric cars is available at this property.
