Description

Located in the heart of Cognac, Domaine Quai des Pontis is on the banks of the Charente River.

It offers bedrooms, suites, air-conditioned lodges, wooden caravans and cabins with a private balcony or terrace.

Each accommodation type at the Quai des Pontis has a flat-screen TV. They also have a refrigerator and a microwave.

Free Wi-Fi access is available in the entire property. A dinner tray can be prepared upon request.

The residence also provides free and secure on-site private parking as well as a swimming pool.

Bicycle rental is available and the property is just a 5-minute walk from the centre of Cognac.
