Description

The Originals City, Hôtel Alteora, Poitiers Site du Futuroscope is located just 500 metres from the Futuroscope theme park and 700 metres from the Convention Centre.

It offers an outdoor swimming pool and free WiFi access. All rooms at The Originals City, Hôtel Alteora, Poitiers Site du Futuroscope are air-conditioned and have a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom, including family rooms.

A buffet breakfast is available each morning, and children aged less than 5 can have breakfast for free.

The hotels has 2 restaurants. La Parenthèse, serves traditional cuisine and Pizzeria Pizz'Alté, a family-friendly restaurant, offers food in a buffet style and a pizzeria.

Guests are invited to relax in the bar or on the outdoor terrace, or enjoy free mini-golf and free cardio training room, as well as the games room.

The Originals City, Hôtel Alteora, Poitiers Site du Futuroscope has a 24-hour front desk and free parking on site.

Guests can purchase Futuroscope tickets at the hotel and Futuroscope Train Station is just 2 km away.
