Description

Located in the countryside, 15 km from Etretat, Le Clos Marie offers B&B accommodation in a typical Norman building.

The rooms are located in a renovated sheep barn, near to the owner’s house. The independent rooms are decorated in neutral tones and features a private terrace with a view of the garden, apple orchard and the property’s 2 horses.

There is also WiFi access, a TV, air conditioning, a desk and a private bathroom.

Guests have accessed to a shared kitchen, which is fully equipped with an oven and dining area.

Breakfast is prepared by the hosts with homemade and local products each morning at Le Clos Marie.

There is a children's playground at the property, complete with a swing set, rackets and balls.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including golf, horse riding, bowling, fishing and hiking.

Private parking is possible on site and cover is available for bicycles and motorcycles.

Fécamp is 14 km away from le Clos Marie. The A29 motorway is 12 km away providing easy access to le Havre and Honfleur.
