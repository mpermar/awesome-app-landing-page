Description

In the morning, enjoy a leisurely breakfast in the lounge overlooking the swimming pool. In the evening, relax with a drink on one of the bar’s comfortable armchairs.

Rooms at Le Clos Des Fontaines are neatly decorated in a marine style. The suites offer spacious interiors decorated in an elegant 19th-century style. All rooms offer free Wi-Fi internet access

From May to September, as you swim in the outdoor heated swimming pool, you can admire the view of Jumièges Abbey and the Seine River.
