Description

The rooms at this hotel are accessible via stairs, decorated in a contemporary style and feature a spa bath or a jet shower in the private bathroom. You will have free use of the hotel's computer and free DVD rental facilities.

Domaine Saint Clair includes a heated outdoor swimming pool open seasonally, 4 guest lounges, a library and a covered patio with a wood burner. You can request a massage or beauty treatments on site.

Specializing in fish and seafood, the restaurant serves meals prepared with fresh, homegrown ingredients, which are produced in the garden from March til October. You can enjoy meals on the terrace and enjoy the views of Etretat and the cliffs.

Close to the estate, the famous Etretat Golf Club stands on the lower cliffs, opposite the estate, 0.6 mi away. The property also has 2 free car parks.
