Description

Just 2. 9 km from the centre of Dieppe and the beach, this hotel offers a flowered garden where you can relax with a drink from the bar.

Arques Forest is 6 km from the property. The rooms at Hôtel Le Clos Robinson are accessed through a private entrance and are decorated according to individual themes.

Each one provides an LCD TV and free Wi-Fi access. A continental breakfast is served in the dining room or on the terrace, when the weather is nice.

Free private parking is available on site and Dieppe Train Station is 3 km away. Other local activities include visiting Dieppe Pourville Golf Club, just 5 km from the property.
