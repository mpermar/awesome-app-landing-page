Description

Offering a barbecue and children's playground, VVF Villages Forges Les Eaux is set in Forges-les-Eaux in the Upper Normandy Region.

There is a casino on site and guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Rouen is 36 km while Paris is 120 km away from the property.

There is also a kitchenette, equipped with a microwave. A toaster is also featured, as well as a coffee machine and a kettle.

Beds are made prior to arrival. Towels are included. VVF Villages Forges Les Eaux also includes a terrace.

You can play table tennis at the property, and bike hire is available. A public swimming pool nearby the property is free of charge.

Dieppe is 48 km from VVF Villages Forges Les Eaux, while Beauvais is 44 km away. The nearest airport is Beauvais Tille Airport, 45 km from VVF Villages Forges Les Eaux.
