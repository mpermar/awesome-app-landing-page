Description

Set in Grèges, in the middle of a large park, Les Chambres De Grèges is located 4.

7 km from Dieppe and the sea. Facilities include an indoor swimming pool, free private parking and a garden with terrace.

All the guest rooms at Les Chambres De Grèges are very bright and spacious. They have a refined decor and include an LCD TV and a private bathroom.

You can enjoy a continental breakfast each morning or try seafood specialties in the restaurant that you can find 5 km away.

Children’s games are available on site. Guests are invited to visit the farm located a 15-minute drive away.

Dieppe Train Station is 5 km away and Rouen Airport is 70 km from Les Chambres De Grèges.
