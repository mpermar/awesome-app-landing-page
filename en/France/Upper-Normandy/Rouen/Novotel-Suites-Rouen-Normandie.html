Description

Located on the banks of the Seine River and 10 minutes from Rouen downtown, this hotel offers free WiFi access.

The Kindarena Stadium is just 2 km away. The Novotel Suites air conditioned modular suites measure 30m² and feature a living room with a flat-screen TV with English and American channels.

They have a kitchenette equipped with a microwave, a mini-fridge and tea and coffee making facilities.

They also include a private bathroom fitted with a bath or a shower. The Novotel Suites Rouen Normandie provides services 24/7 including a Boutique Gourmande offering snacks, bar service, a fitness room with free access, a business corner (equipped with Internet access, a webcam, printer and fax).

There is also a secure underground car park. For maximum relaxation during your stay, a 10-minute ‘seated amma’ sessions is held every Thursday.

Do not forget to enjoy the Good Morning breakfast, a balanced way to start the day.
