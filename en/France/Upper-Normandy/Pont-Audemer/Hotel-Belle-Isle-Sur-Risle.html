Description

The stylish rooms and suites have a classic décor and each has a private bathroom and cable TV. Some of the rooms also feature a private terrace or a balcony, as well as a marble bathroom.

The Hotel Belle Isle’s restaurant serves French specialties and is set in a Victorian rotunda shaped restaurant, which offers views of the park.

A spa with massages is just one of the other facilities at this hotel. Trouville and Deauville are less than 30 minutes by car and Honfleur is a 20-minute drive away. Deauville Saint-Gatien Airport is 40 minutes away by car.
