Description

Auberge de La Chaloire is located in La Petite Verrière in the heart of the Morvan Natural Regional Park.

This family-run property offers a heated swimming pool in the garden and a terrace with views of the hills.

Guest rooms at Auberge De La Chaloire are equipped with free WiFi access and an en suite bathroom.

The restaurant serves homemade dishes prepared with fresh local products. Meals can be accompanied by French and international wines from the wine cellar.

Guests can explore the surrounding Morvan countryside and discover the numerous Gallo-Roman sites.
