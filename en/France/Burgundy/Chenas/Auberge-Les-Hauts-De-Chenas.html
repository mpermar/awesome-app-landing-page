Description

Surrounded by vineyards and woodland, this hotel is located in the South of Bourgogne between Lyon and Macon.

It offers an on-site wine museum with wines from the hotel’s estate. Guests can enjoy free red wine tasting upon arrival.

The property also boasts panoramic views across the countryside. Free WiFi internet access and a TV are provided in each of the bright rooms.

All guest rooms offer air conditioning and a modern en suite bathroom. A continental breakfast is served each morning at the hotel.

Free private parking is available on site. There is a garden with a terrace where guests can relax during the summer months.

Open during the summer, the restaurant offers local dishes. Guests can go hiking and cycling in the area.

There are also facilities for kids, including free mini golf and other leisure activities and equipment.
