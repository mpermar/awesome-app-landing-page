Description

All units come with a microwave, toaster, a coffee machine, a fridge and electric tea pot. There's a fully equipped private bathroom with shower and a hair dryer.

Guests at the resort village can enjoy a continental breakfast.

Moulins is 20 miles from Le Petit Robinson, while Nevers is 19 miles away.
