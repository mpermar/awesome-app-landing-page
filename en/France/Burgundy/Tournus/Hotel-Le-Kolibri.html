Description

Situated just outside the centre of Tournus, the Hôtel le Kolibri welcomes you in a lovely green setting.

The hotel is equipped with 30 spacious and soundproofed rooms. All rooms at the Hôtel le Kolibri are equipped with free WiFi internet access and a flat-screen TV with Canal+ and beIN Sports.

Guests can enjoy the local cuisine in the hotel's restaurant, La Table du Kolibri.

The hotel is located near exit 27 of the A6 motorway and free parking is provided, making it easy to explore the Bourgogne region by car.
