Description

The Delfotel is nestled in a lush and peaceful area 2 km away from the centre of Paray-le-Monial, right in front of the famous Canal du Centre.

It features a heated swimming pool. Rooms include air-conditioning, satellite tv and wireless Internet access both unlimited and free.

The Delfotel is the ideal place to stay whether you are just stopping by or enjoying a long holiday.

The hotel restaurant offers 4 different menus according to the seasons. The hotel is a 20-minute walk from the basilica and 10 km from Charolles.
