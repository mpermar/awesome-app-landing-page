Description

Sitting in the middle of a mature garden with centenary trees, this B&B is located in the countryside of Laives.

Set in an 18th-century coaching inn, it offers a tennis table, board games and free bicycles to discover the area.

All of the rooms at L’Atelier du Relais are uniquely decorated in a contemporary style.

They all feature a private bathroom with a shower. There are also rooms suitable for guests with reduced mobility.

A continental breakfast with jams is served every morning in the library room, in your room or on one of the terraces.

Free private parking is possible on site and the B&B is just a 10-minute drive from A6 Motorway Exit.
