Description

Chateau De Vault De Lugny is a chateau dating from the 13th and 16th centuries near Vezelay, Chablis and the Morvan Regional Park.

It offers luxurious rooms in a peaceful garden setting. The chateau and its spacious rooms and suites are all non-smoking, and they include a free minibar.

They all offer a garden view and some rooms have an open log fire and four-poster bed.

A flat-screen TV with Canal+ and Bein Sport channels is available in each room. The gastronomic restaurant is open daily upon reservation and serves dishes made with vegetable produce from the garden.

It offers a wide choice of the best Burgundy wines. Guests can enjoy the award winning pool, tennis court, hot tub, wine tasting and much more.
