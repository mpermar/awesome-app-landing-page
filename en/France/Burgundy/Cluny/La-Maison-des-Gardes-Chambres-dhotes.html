Description

Set in an 18th-century town house in the heart of Cluny, La Maison des Gardes offers B&B accommodation, a 3500-m² garden and a terrace.

Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas and in most of the rooms. The property features 5 spacious rooms, 3 in the main building and 2 in the annexe.

They feature a flat-screen TV and the private bathrooms are complete with a shower or bath tub.

A breakfast of homemade jams and French pastries is included and served daily. A fully-equipped kitchen including a microwave, refrigerator, stovetop, oven and kitchenware is at guests’ disposal on request with a minimum stay of 3 nights.

Maison des Gardes is 550 meters from Cluny Abbey, Macon is 25 km from the property.
