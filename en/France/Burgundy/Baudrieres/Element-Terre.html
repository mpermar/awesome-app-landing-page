Description

Set in Baudrières, 18 km from Tournus and 20 km from both Chalon-sur-Saône and Louhans, Element Terre bed and breakfast features free WiFi access and free private parking.

Every room includes a private bathroom fitted with a bath or shower. Extras include free toiletries and a hairdryer.

There is a shared lounge at the property and guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant upon prior reservation.

You can engage in various activities, such as cycling and fishing. you can rent electric bicycles on site.

The bed and breakfast also offers free use of bicycles. Mâcon is 43 km from Element Terre.
