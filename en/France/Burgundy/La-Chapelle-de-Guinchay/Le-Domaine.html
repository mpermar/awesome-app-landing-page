Description

Offering free WiFi throughout the property, a sun terrace with a swimming pool, shared lounge and garden, Le Domaine is set in La Chapelle-de-Guinchay.

Private parking can be arranged at an extra charge. All guest rooms at the guest house come with a seating area and a flat-screen TV.

Featuring a private bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer, rooms at Le Domaine also have pool view.

The accommodation offers a continental or gluten-free breakfast. You can play darts at Le Domaine.

Speaking English, Spanish, French and Italian at the reception, staff are always on hand to help.

Mâcon is 12 km from the guest house, while Bourg-en-Bresse is 46 km from the property.
