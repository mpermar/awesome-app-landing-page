Description

Renovated in 2018, this 16th century hotel is located in Beaune city centre, in front of the Hospices de Beaune.

Hôtel Central offers family rooms and free WiFi. Featuring a contemporary decor, each room offers a wardrobe, a desk and a private bathroom with bath or shower and hairdryer.

A continental breakfast, including local produces, is served every morning at an extra cost.

Guests may also taste local wines at the on site bar. Dame Tartine by Edgar, the hotel's restaurant, is located 50 meters away, in front of the Hospices de Beaune.

It serves traditional, regional cuisine and toasts prepared with organic bread from the bakery.

Guets may eat in the dining room, on the terrace on in the property's courtyard. Other onsite services include a a shared kitchen area, a lounge and a games room.

From the Hôtel Central, guests will have easy access to the Burgundy Wine Route. Guests can also easily visit the town's wine cellars, shops and art galleries.
