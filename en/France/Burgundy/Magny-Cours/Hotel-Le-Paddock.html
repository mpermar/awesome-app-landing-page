Description

Set in the countryside of Magny-Cours, Hotel Le Paddock offers air-conditioned and spacious rooms just 2 km from Magny-Cours Formula One Racetrack.

With free high-speed WiFi, it also offers an outdoor swimming pool, a wellness centre with sauna and hamman and a tennis court.

The guest rooms here offer a view of the countryside and golf course. A flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a telephone and desk are provided, and facilities for making tea and coffee are also included in the en suite rooms.

A buffet breakfast is served daily in the hotel's restaurant, which also serves local dishes from the region for lunch and dinner.

The property has a bar, Le Green, with a terrace where you can enjoy a refreshing drink.

The hotel is only 100 metres from Nivernais Golf Course and it is 15 km away from Nevers and its historic monuments.
