Description

Situated in Flammerans in the Burgundy Region, 33 km from Dijon, Chateau de Flammerans features an outdoor pool and views of the pool.

The hotel has a seasonal outdoor pool and sun terrace, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar around the pool table.

Rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV. Some rooms feature a seating area where you can relax.

You will find a kettle in the room. The rooms are fitted with a private bathroom.

For your comfort, you will find bathrobes and slippers. The restaurant is open on Thursdays, Fridays and Saturdays from 19:30 to 21:30 and Sundays from 12:00 to 13:30.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. A fitness room, a hot tub and a sauna are at guests' disposal.

A selection of activities are offered in the area, such as golfing and horse riding.

Besançon is 43 km from Chateau de Flammerans, while Dole is 16 km away. The nearest airport is Dole - Jura Airport, 21 km from Chateau de Flammerans.
