Description

All of the individually decorated rooms have a TV and a private bathroom with a shower or a bath. Several of them have 4-poster canopy beds.

At the Château D'igé guests are invited to relax in the winter garden or out on the terrace. In the warmer months, the terrace is a lovely place to dine. A charging station for electric cars is available at this property.

The restaurant uses local products to prepare fine traditional and regional cuisine.

Mâconnais is a region covered with rolling hills, vineyards, woods and meadowlands. Cluny Abbey is 7.5 mi from Château D'igé.
