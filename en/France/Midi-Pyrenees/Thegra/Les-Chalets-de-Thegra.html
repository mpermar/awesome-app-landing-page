Description

Each unit features a living room with a TV and a kitchenette fitted with a dishwasher and a microwave. A fridge and coffee machine are also offered.

The resort village has an outdoor pool.

A terrace is available on site and cycling can be enjoyed within close proximity of the property.

Sarlat-la-Canéda is 27 miles from Les Chalets de Thegra, while Brive-la-Gaillarde is 25 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Brive-La Roche Airport, 27 miles from the property.
