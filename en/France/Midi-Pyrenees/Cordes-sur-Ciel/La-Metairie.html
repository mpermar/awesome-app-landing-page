Description

Set among a park of cedar trees, La Métairie hotel offers a quiet and peaceful location with views over the village.

It features a fitness centre available all day long and an indoor pool. All rooms have free WiFi access and tea and coffee making facilities.

A buffet breakfast is served daily with homemade and local products. Albi town centre is 20 minutes away and free parking is available on site.
