Description

All rooms are equipped with a balcony, a kitchen with a microwave, a dining area and a flat-screen TV, while the private bathroom includes a shower. There's also a fridge, stovetop and a coffee machine.

A continental breakfast is available daily at the property.

The resort village has a sun terrace.

A ski storage space is available on site and skiing can be enjoyed within close proximity of ULVF les Ramondies.

Saint-Lary-Soulan Ski School is a 6-minute walk from the accommodation, while Pic Lumiere Gondola is a 7-minute walk away. The nearest airport is Tarbes Lourdes Pyrénées Airport, 30 miles from the property.
