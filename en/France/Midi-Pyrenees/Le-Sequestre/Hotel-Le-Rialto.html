Description

Set near the exhibition centre and the car racing circuit, just 5 minutes from the city centre, this charming hotel welcomes you during your stay in the Tarn area.

Le Rialto features 38 guest rooms, including 2 rooms for disabled guests. Each room comes with a private bathroom, LCD TV and free Wi-Fi access.

Local, traditional cuisine is served at the hotel's restaurant, and tasty buffet breakfast allows you to get off to a good start each morning.

Take a drink at the bar, on the beautiful veranda or the terrace when the weather is fine.
