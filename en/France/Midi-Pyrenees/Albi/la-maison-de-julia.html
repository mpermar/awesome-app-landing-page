Description

At the bed and breakfast, every room comes with a terrace. All rooms have a private bathroom and free toiletries, and certain accommodations at la maison de julia have a patio. Guest rooms will provide guests with a closet and a coffee machine.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property.

The accommodation has a seasonal outdoor pool.

The nearest airport is Castres-Mazamet Airport, 27 miles from the property.
