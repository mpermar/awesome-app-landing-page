Description

At the bed and breakfast, each room comes with a closet. Each room includes a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hair dryer. All guest rooms will provide guests with a microwave.

A continental or vegetarian breakfast is served at the property.

Agen is 18 miles from Le Farat Bed & Breakfast, while Montauban is 23 miles from the property. Agen - La Garenne Aerodrome Airport is 18 miles away.
