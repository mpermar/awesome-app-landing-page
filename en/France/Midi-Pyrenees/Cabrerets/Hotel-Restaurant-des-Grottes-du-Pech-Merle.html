Description

Located 1 km from Pech Merle prehistoric cave and 9 km from Saint-Cirq Lapopie, Hôtel Restaurant des Grottes du Pech Merle offers views of the Célé River, a seasonal outdoor swimming pool, a flat-screen TV in the lobby and a bar.

The rooms at Hôtel Restaurant des Grottes du Pech Merle all have a wardrobe, a desk, private shower room and toilets.

Free WiFi is available. In the morning, guests can enjoy a breakfast buffet with a large selection of local, organic and farm produce on the property's terrace.

Cahors Railway Station and the A20 Highway are 25 km from the hotel. Guests can go horse riding 2 km away and canoeing 4 km away and there is free parking at the property.
