Description

Offering a gastronomic restaurant and a bar, Hotel Le Viscos is located in Saint-Savin, a 5-minute drive from Argelès-Gazost. Free WiFi access is available.

Each room here will provide you with a TV, a desk, an electric kettle and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries. Some rooms feature air conditioning. You can enjoy a mountain view from some rooms depending on availability.

In the morning, breakfast consists of local produce. The restaurant serves regional produce and booking is needed.

At Hotel Le Viscos you will also find a terrace and a luggage storage. An array of activities can be enjoyed in the surroundings, including cycling, hiking, fishing horse riding and rafting.

Cauterets is 9.3 mi from the hotel and Lourdes is 8.7 mi away. The property offers free parking and charging stations for electric cars are available at this property.
