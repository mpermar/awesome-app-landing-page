Description

This 2-star, air-conditioned hotel is located in Villefranche-de-Lauragais, just 2 km from A61 Motorway.

With an on-site bar and restaurant, it is 35 km from Toulouse and guests can enjoy its furnished terrace.

All the rooms at Hôtel Restaurant du Lauragais are air-conditioned and serviced by a lift.

They all feature a parquet floor and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Each also includes a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning at Hôtel Restaurant du Lauragais. The hotel’s restaurant offers regional dishes for dinner and guests can relax in the 18th-century communal lounge, by the fireplace with a drink from the bar.

The hotel provides free Wi-Fi and free newspapers. A private parking is also available at an extra cost and Montgiscard is 15 km away.
