Description

Bed and Breakfast Cougousse is set in Salles-la-Source, 17 km from Conques and 14 km from Rodez.

It offers a garden with outdoor furniture for guests to relax during the summer months.

Free private parking is available on site and Marcillac is 2 km away. Every room is equipped with a flat-screen TV.

You will find a kettle in the room. Every room is fitted with a private bathroom fitted with a shower.

Chambres d'hôtes Cougousse features free WiFi. There is a shared welcome lounge at the property with a microwave at guests' disposal.

The area is popular for cycling and fishing. Figeac and Laguiole are 39 km from Chambres d'hôtes Cougousse.
