Description

Arrieulat Auberges Des Pyrénées is 13 km south of Lourdes, at the crossroads of the Tourmalet, Gavarnie and Aubisque valleys.

It offers free Wi-Fi in public areas, a games room and a spa with a sauna and a heated, outdoor hot tub.

The hotel façade, with its painted wooden facade and balconies, evokes a traditional Pyrenean atmosphere.

Each en suite guest room has a flat-screen TV and free WiFi access. The back of the hotel is surrounded by a tree-shaded garden with a private car park on the side and a large terrace in front.

Guests also have access to a library and a fitness room with a rowing machine. On the road to the Aubisque, Soulor and Tourmalet passes, Arrieulat, Auberges Des Pyrénées is popular with skiers and hikers.
