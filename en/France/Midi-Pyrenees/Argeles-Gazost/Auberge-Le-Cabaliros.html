Description

Auberge Le Cabaliros is situated in Argelès-Gazost, 2 km from Argelès-Gazost Thermal Baths.

The hotel has a children's playground and sun terrace, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Free private parking is available on site. Each room is equipped with a flat-screen TV.

Some units include views of the mountain or garden. The rooms include a private bathroom fitted with a bath or shower.

For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer. Auberge Le Cabaliros features free WiFi throughout the property.

There is a shared lounge at the property. A number of activities are offered in the area, such as skiing and horse riding.

The nearest airport is Tarbes - Lourdes Airport, 23 km from Auberge Le Cabaliros.
