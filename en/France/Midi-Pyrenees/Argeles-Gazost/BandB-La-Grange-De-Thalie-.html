Description

At the bed and breakfast, every room is equipped with a closet. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with free toiletries, while certain rooms have a seating area.

Breakfast is available each morning, and includes continental and gluten-free options.

Skiing is among the activities that guests can enjoy near B La Grange De Thalie..

The nearest airport is Tarbes Lourdes Pyrénées Airport, 13 miles from the accommodation.
