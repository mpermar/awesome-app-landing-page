Description

Auberge Du Relais D'auniac is located 5 kilometres from Gourdon and 17 km from Souillac, between Rocamadour et Sarlat.

This charming inn offers 7 guestrooms and an outdoor pool in a private garden. The hotel restaurant offers a taste of the region by serving traditional local dishes made with fresh local products.

Auberge Du Relais D'auniac is a great base for exploring nearby towns such as Périgord and Sarlat.

You can also enjoy sports activities on the River Dordogne, as well as hiking, cycling and horse riding in the surrounding countryside.
