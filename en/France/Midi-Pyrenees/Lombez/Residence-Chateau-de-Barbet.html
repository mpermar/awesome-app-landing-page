Description

Residence Chateau de Barbet is a holiday park located in Lombez. It offers villas with a private garden and free WiFi access.

Guests will find outdoor swimming pools and sun terraces. All villas at Residence Chateau de Barbet have a terrace and a seating area with a TV.

The private bathroom comes with a bathtub. There is a fully equipped kitchen and a dining room.

On site, guests can also enjoy a restaurant. A tennis court, a fitness centre, entertainment staff, and a kids' club are available in this holiday park.
