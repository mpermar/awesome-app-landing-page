Description

Located next to the Compans-Caffarelli Park, this hotel is a 10-minute walk from the centre of Toulouse and 4 km from Toulouse Stadium.

It offers an outdoor swimming pool and free Wi-Fi access is provided in the modern rooms.

A minibar and satellite TV feature in the air-conditioned guest rooms. Some of them feature a balcony, and each bathroom is equipped with a bathtub and a hairdryer.

A buffet breakfast is served every day and can be taken in your room. Room service is available 24/7 at Novotel Toulouse Centre.

You can enjoy a drink, tapas or local cuisine at the The Gourmet Bar or on the terrace.

With a 24-hour front desk, the hotel also provides a games console in the children’s play area.

Compans-Caffarelli Metro Station is 700 metres from this hotel, and Matabiau Train Station is 2.

5 km away. Limited parking is available on site at an extra cost. Public parking is also available nearby.
