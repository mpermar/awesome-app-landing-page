Description

Set in a 19th-century building, this hotel is located in the heart of Toulouse, opposite the SNCF train station and the airport shuttle stop.

It offers spacious accommodation with free WiFi access. The modern guest rooms are equipped with en suite facilities and satellite TV (Canal+).

The private bathrooms have been renovated in April 2019. All of the rooms are serviced by a lift.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning at ibis Style Toulouse Centre Gare. There is also a bar area and TV lounge where guests can relax.

The hotel also features an interior courtyard. The city’s many shops, sites and restaurants are easily accessible by foot, including the Capitole de Toulouse which houses the Hotel de Ville, opera house and dungeons.
