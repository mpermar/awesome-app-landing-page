Description

All rooms come with a microwave, toaster, a coffee machine, a shower, free toiletries and a closet. Rooms come with a private bathroom with a hair dryer, while some have mountain views. The rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

The guesthouse offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

Chateau de La Lanette has a terrace. Bike rental and car rental are available at the accommodation and the area is popular for skiing and cycling.

Baqueira-Beret is 24 miles from Chateau de La Lanette, while Saint-Lary-Soulan is 19 miles from the property. Tarbes Lourdes Pyrénées Airport is 36 miles away.
