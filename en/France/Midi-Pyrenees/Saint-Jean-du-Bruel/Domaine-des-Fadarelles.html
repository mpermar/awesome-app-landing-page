Description

All units have a kitchen, a private bathroom and a terrace with views of mountain. A microwave is also available, as well as a coffee machine and a electric tea pot.

Cycling can be enjoyed nearby and a bicycle rental service is available.

Millau is 16 miles from the resort village, while Anduze is 30 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Mediterranee Airport, 42 miles from Domaine des Fadarelles.
