Description

Offering a seasonal outdoor pool and sun terrace, Villa Toulousaine is situated in Grisolles, a 16-minute train ride from Toulouse Matabiau Train Station.

Free private parking is available on site. All units have a seating area for your convenience.

Every room comes with a private bathroom. Villa Toulousaine features free WiFi throughout the property.

There is babysitting service at the property. Bike hire is available and you can play darts at the bed and breakfast.

Montauban is 21 km from Villa Toulousaine, while Toulouse-Blagnac Airport, 23 km from Villa Toulousaine.
