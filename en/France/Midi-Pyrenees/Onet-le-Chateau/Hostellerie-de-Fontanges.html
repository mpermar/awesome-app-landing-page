Description

Hostellerie de Fontanges is an environmentally friendly venue that combines tradition and modernity.

Enjoy its natural setting, inventive regional cuisine and fireside lounge. All of the rooms are individually decorated and furnished and have TV, minibar and spacious en suite bathrooms.

They are all located on the 18-hole golf course in Rodez. Inside, you will find its fitness centre and sauna, which are free for guests.

The outdoor swimming pool is open in the warmer months and the terrace overlooks the Hostellerie’s mature garden.

Last but not least, the restaurant at Hostellerie de Fontanges serves inventive cuisine inspired by the traditions of Aveyron.

Select wines are available and aperitifs and after-dinner drinks can be had in the bar.

Free Wi-Fi internet access is available throughout the Hostellerie and private parking is also free of charge.

Hostellerie de Fontanges is just 2 km from the train station and 10 km from the airport in Rodez.
