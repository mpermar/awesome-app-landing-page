Description

Chateau de Labro is 5 minutes from Grand Rodez Golf Club. It offers a swimming pool in the orchard, a vineyard and a traditional kitchen.

All of the elegantly decorated rooms are fitted with classic furniture. They all have private bathroom.

Some are fitted with a Balneo bathtub. Chateau de Labro also offers a tree-house hut finely decorated and furnished.

It looks out over the orchard and has en suite facilities. Guests can relax in the Chateau's spa centre, which includes a hot tub and sauna, and offers a range of body and beauty treatments.

The daily breakfast is served in a fully restored barn furnished with antiques. It can be enjoyed fireside or in the interior garden.
