Description

All rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area. Relais de Mirepoix has some accommodations that feature pool views, and each room is equipped with a private bathroom with a bath. Guest rooms at the accommodation have air conditioning and a desk.

Guests can grab a bite to eat in the on-site restaurant, which specializes in local cuisine and also offers Vegan, Vegetarian and Dairy-free options.

Carcassone is 25 miles from Relais de Mirepoix, while Ax-les-Thermes is 26 miles away. The nearest airport is Salvaza Airport, 24 miles from the property.
