Description

The House of Hoursentut is located between St. Mary of Campan and La Mongie, 7 km from La Mongie Ski Resort.

It features a French restaurant that serves meals made with local products, a bar and a terrace.

The elegant and simply decorated accommodations at La Maison d’Hoursentut have a private bathroom, a TV with satellite channels and a wooden floor.

The breakfast is served in the dining room that includes a fireplace. You can also request a packed lunch.

You can leave your ski or your bike in the ski storage and then rest in the living room using the free WiFi access which is available in public areas.

Bagnères-de-Bigorre is 15 km away and the hotel is a 10-minute drive from Pic du Midi de Bigorre.
