Description

Offering a free seasonal outdoor pool, Le Domaine de Mazières is located in Montcabrier.

Free WiFi access is available in this holiday park. Each accommodation type has a private bathroom with a shower.

They come with bed linen and garden views. Guests can watch TV in the shared sitting room.

Other facilities offered at the property include meeting facilities. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling and hiking.
