Description

Located 5 minutes' walk from the Sanctuaries Notre-Dame in Lourdes, Hotel Paradis features a view of the Pyrenées Mountains.

It offers a large outdoor terrace and elegant rooms. Guest rooms at the Paradis are air conditioned and equipped with satellite TV.

Each has high-speed fiber-optic WiFi access and a private bathroom. The superior rooms also boast a welcoming tray, a safety deposit box and a mini fridge.

The hotel provides a computer area and and a restaurant. Guests can relax with a drink in the bar.

Hotel Paradis is in central Lourdes, opposite the Gave de Pau River. The Château Fort de Lourdes is just 10 minutes' walk away.
