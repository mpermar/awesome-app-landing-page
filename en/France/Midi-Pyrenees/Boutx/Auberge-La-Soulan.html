Description

A hotel located at Menté Pass just 800 metres from Mourtis ski resort, Auberge La Soulan offers a shared lounge with a fireplace, a restaurant and free WiFi access throughout the property.

At Auberge La Soulan, all rooms include a wardrobe and some have a private bathroom.

The hotel also has a sauna, ski storage facilities and a games room. There is a restaurant on site serving traditional cuisine composed with local produce.

Upon request, packed lunches can be prepared. An array of activities can be enjoyed in the surroundings, including skiing, cycling and fishing.

Montréjeau Train Station is a 40- minute drive away. The property offers free public parking.
