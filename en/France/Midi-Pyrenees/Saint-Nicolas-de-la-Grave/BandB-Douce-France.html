Description

Douce France offers rooms in a country property with a terrace and garden, located less than 1 hour’s drive from Toulouse and 10 km from Moissac.

It features free Wi-Fi access in the entire property and a covered heated outdoor swimming pool.

Each room has a private entrance and includes an en suite bathroom with a bath or shower and free toiletries.

A continental breakfast is available daily. You can enjoy traditional, regional cuisine for evening meals prepared by the hosts on request.

Douce France is a 10-minute drive from the open-air and water-sports centre. Free private parking is available at the property.
