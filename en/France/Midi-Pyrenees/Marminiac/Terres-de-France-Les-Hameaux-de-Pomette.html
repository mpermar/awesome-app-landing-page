Description

Located in the heart of the countryside, 2 km from the town of Cazals, this resort offers a swimming pool.

Free parking and a children’s playground are available on site. The resort offers accommodation in wooden chalets, which each have a patio with garden furniture.

Chalets feature a kitchenette with a microwave, fridge and hotplates, as well as a seating area equipped with a flat-screen TV.

Free Wi-Fi internet access is available in public areas of the resort. Additional facilities available for a supplement include a laundry service and a room offering a pool table and board games.
