Description

Located in Langres, in the north-eastern France, Camping de la Croix d'Arles offers self-catering mobile homes and yurts.

A swimming pool and a sauna feature on site, and Langres Train Station is 10 km away.

Some of the guest accommodations include a kitchen with a microwave and a fridge.

The mobile homes feature a private bathroom and seating area with sofa, while the yurts have a shared bathroom.

A breakfast is available with an extra cost, and the on-site restaurant is open for lunch and dinner.

A grocery shop can be found on site, and there is a supermarket 2 km away. Camping de la Croix d'Arles is only 12 km from the Lac de Liez.

A cycling and hiking path that leads to Langrès can be found in the area. Guests can also do adventure tree climbing 300 metres from the property, and free, private parking is possible on site.
