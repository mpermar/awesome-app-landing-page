Description

Maison De Marie-Claire is a B & B located in Doncourt-sur-Meuse, 28 km from Neufchateau.

Offering free bikes, it also features a shared lounge with a fireplace and a room with books and games.

Provided with free Wi-Fi, guest rooms include a seating area and a private bathroom with hairdryer.

Ironing facilities are at your disposal in each room. A continental breakfast is proposed daily.

Traditional meals and snacks can be requested at an additional cost. BBQ can be organised in the garden, which is furnished with a table and chairs.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling, hiking and table tennis.

Vittel Golf Club is 39 km away, Liez Lake is 44 km away and the A31 motorway is 9 km away.
