Description

Located a 10-minute walk from Blois city centre, this 4-star hotel is an ideal base to explore the Châteaux of the Loire Valley.

Renovated in 2018, each room at Novotel Blois Centre Val De Loire has air conditioning and is equipped with a safe and a welcome tray.

A private bathroom with hairdryer and free toiletries is also available. A buffet breakfast is served daily.

The Le Restaurant Gourmet Bar is open for lunch and dinner from Monday to Friday.

Guests can enjoy their cocktails inside or outside on the terrace, which was added to the property in 2019.

There is a 24-hour reception and guests can make use of the 2 onsite meeting rooms, which were also renovated in 2018.

This hotel offers charging stations for electric cars and paid private parking. Blois Castle is 1 km away and the Museum of Magic is 1.
