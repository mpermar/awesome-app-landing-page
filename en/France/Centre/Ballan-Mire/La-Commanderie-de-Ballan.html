Description

La Commanderie de Ballan is an ancient estate dating from the 12th century, located 3 km from Touraine Golf Club.

Guests have free access to the seasonal outdoor pool in the 7-hectare park. Set in the former stables, the guest rooms are all decorated in a traditional style and offer a garden view.

Most room also feature a terrace and each has a bathroom with a bath or a shower.

A homemade breakfast is prepared every morning in the dining room, which has exposed wooden beams and opens onto the garden.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling and table tennis.

The property offers free private parking and Tours Val de Loire Airport is 12 km away.
