Description

La Grange De Léonie is situated in Méry-sur-Cher in a wooded park. Among the various facilities are a seasonal outdoor swimming pool and a garden.

Facilities include a terrace and free WiFi is available. At the guest house, all rooms have a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

All rooms are equipped with a coffee machine, while some have a terrace. All units will provide guests with a dishwasher.

A continental breakfast is served daily at the property. An array of activities are offered in the area, such as cycling and hiking.

A bicycle garage is available and guests can recharge their electric bike. Bourges is 37 km from La Grange De Léonie.

The nearest airport is Chateauroux-Centre Airport, 47 km from the property. Free private parking is available.
