Description

Logis Auberge Du Centre is located in the small Loire valley town of Chitenay, just 34 km from Beauval Zoo.

The hotel offers free access to the seasonal pool in the garden and the rooms include Canal satellite channels and free Wi-Fi.

The hotel has a lounge with an open fireplace, air conditioning and free parking.

Logis Auberge Du Centre also provides bike tours of the surrounding countryside and famous castles.

Breakfast is served daily and children can enjoy it at the reduced price. The restaurant serves modern themed cuisine in either the rustic kitchen with open fire, the new dining room or on the terrace and garden.

Guests can enjoy a number of walks and excursions from the Logis Auberge. The historic city of Blois, set on the banks of the Loire, is just 15 km away.
