Description

Located 1. 5 km from the city of Lignières, this resort offers accommodation in the form of traditional wooden caravans and provides free Wi-Fi access.

Guests are free to use the BBQ facilities in the garden. With parquet flooring and heating, each caravan has a private bathroom and opens up to a small terrace with a view of the garden.

A kitchenette with hot plates and a refrigerator are featured. A buffet-style breakfast is served every morning in the breakfast room.

A snack bar proposing simple meals is available at resort Les Roulottes du Berry.

There is a common area with a children’s playground and the on-site lounge area is equipped with a TV and a pool table.

Donkey rides and excursions are arranged upon demand. Free private parking is possible and Bourges Train Station is 50 km away.
