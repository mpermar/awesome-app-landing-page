Description

Built in the 1830's, La Dixmeresse is located in Restigné and provides free WiFi access and free parking.

It also features a private garden, terrace and a shared kitchen. Each room here will provide you with a small seating area with a TV, a DVD player and CD player.

They have a private bathroom with free toiletries. You can enjoy a garden or village view from the room.

Breakfast is served daily. Regional dishes prepared with fresh produce can be sampled for dinner in the renovated, 15th-century barn, upon prior reservation.

Other facilities offered at the property include a shared lounge and luggage storage.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling and hiking.
