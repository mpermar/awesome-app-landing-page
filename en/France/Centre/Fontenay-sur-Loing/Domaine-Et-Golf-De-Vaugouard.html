Description

This 18th-century castle offers a beautiful setting in the historic Centre region.

Domaine Et Golf De Vaugouard has a golf course, a tennis court and an outdoor pool.

The property provides elegant guest rooms, some with a four-poster bed. They are equipped with a minibar, a telephone and a TV and each has a private bathroom.

La Brasserie restaurant provides daily buffet breakfast and snacks for lunch. In the evening, the Domaine De Vaugouard’s gourmet restaurant serves refined French cuisine with a large selection of fine wines.

Guests can enjoy a cocktail at the bar, or on the outdoor panoramic terrace overlooking the golf course.

There is also a pool-side bar, open when the weather allows it. The pool features a sun terrace with deckchairs.

Guests have access to the common living rooms, the pool table and the fitness centre.
