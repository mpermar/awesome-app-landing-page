Description

Situated in Sepmes, 44 km from Tours, Chateau Laroche-Ploquin boasts a restaurant and free WiFi.

The hotel has a sun terrace and views of the garden and free private parking is available on site.

All rooms include a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Some rooms feature a seating area for your convenience.

Rooms have with a private bathroom and extras include bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries.

Guests can enjoy a drink at the bar and dine at the on-site restaurant. You can engage in various activities, such as horse riding and fishing.
