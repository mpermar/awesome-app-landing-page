Description

Domaine des Thômeaux, The Originals Relais (Relais du Silence) is located in Mosnes, 1 km from the banks of the Loire.

Guests aged over 16 have free access to the spa with a hammam, sauna and heated indoor pool.

The air-conditioned guest rooms at Domaine des Thômeaux, The Originals Relais (Relais du Silence) come with a private bathroom and a TV.

The annex rooms are located in a separate building outside of the estate and also have a private bathroom.

The Spa offers various massage and relaxation therapies by appointment, and guests can exercise in the fitness room.

Open to non-residents as well as guests, Domaine des Thômeaux, The Originals Relais (Relais du Silence) restaurant offers a variety of dishes, which can be enjoyed on the hotel terrace.

Domaine des Thômeaux, The Relais Originals (Relais du Silence) is located in the Valley of the Kings, a 10-minute drive from the famous Chaumont-sur-Loire castle and gardens and the Chateau d'Amboise.
