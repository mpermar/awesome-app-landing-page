Description

The hotel also has a large outdoor swimming pool and terrace, and guests have free access to laundry facilities.

The Chateau des Arpentis is close to a number of tourist sites including the Pagode de Chanteloup, Chenonceaux Castle and the miniature castles park. It is 10 minutes by car to the center of Amboise.
