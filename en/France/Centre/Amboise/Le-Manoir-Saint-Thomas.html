Description

This manor is located near the Château d'Amboise. It offers a large outdoor swimming pool with a terrace and sun beds, and a free bottle of Liqueur de Fraises.

The large rooms and suites are air-conditioned and feature a traditional decoration.

Each has satellite TV, a minibar and a telephone, and the bathrooms are complete with a hairdryer and bathrobes.

Free Wi-Fi access is available on site. The Saint Thomas is 30 minutes by from Tours and 45 minutes from Blois.

Free private parking is provided at Le Manoir Saint Thomas, making it easy to discover the Loire Valley by car.
