Description

Novotel Amboise is located among the chateaux of the Loire Valley, the hotel offers a terrace, 2 tennis courts and swimming pool with a panoramic view of Amboise Château.

The air-conditioned rooms all include a minibar and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Each room has a private bathroom with free toiletries supplied. You can enjoy traditional French cuisine in the restaurant, and additional facilities include a bar and a children's play area.
