Description

Set in a 19th-century house, Bed and Breakfast Cerviña is located in Germigny-des-Prés, 15 minutes away from the Sully-sur-Loire Chateau.

Guests can enjoy the garden and relax on the terrace. The guestrooms at Cerviña are individually decorated, and feature free WiFi and a TV.

Each room has a private bathroom with a walk-shower, a hairdryer and a separate toilet.

Guests can relax in the property's games and library room. A continental breakfast is served every morning.

Evening meals featuring traditional French cuisine and local products can be provided upon prior request during the reservation, as well as picnic lunches.

The owners will be happy to share their local knowledge with guests. Orléans is a 30-minute drive away and Montargis is a 45-minute drive from the property.

Free private parking for cars and bikes is available at the property. The local Tourism Office is a 5-minute walk away.
