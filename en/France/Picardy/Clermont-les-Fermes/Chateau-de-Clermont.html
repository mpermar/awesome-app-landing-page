Description

Located in a 1. 5-hectare park in the village of Clermont-les-Fermes, this B&B is set in an 18th-century building.

It features a heated indoor pool, a games room and provides free Wi-Fi access. A seating area and views of the garden feature in the guest rooms.

Each room includes an electric kettle and a private bathroom with bathrobes. The cottage has a patio and a four-poster bed.

In the morning, guests who are staying in the guest rooms have a choice of a continental or full English breakfast.

Guests are invited to enjoy home-made meals in the dining room at Château de Clermont.

Private parking is free on site and Laon Train Station is 25 km away. The bed and breakfast is accessible via the A26 motorway.
