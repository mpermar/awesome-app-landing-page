Description

Le Bien-Venant is a guest house located in Domvast, 7 km from Saint-Riquier. This guest house is set in a traditional-style house and features a garden and a playground, as well as a shaded, furnished terrace.

Guests can play board games or read a book from the library. Different accommodation type are featured at Le Bien-Venant: a holiday home, two studios and five rooms.

Each accommodation has a private bathroom with a hairdryer. All rooms include a desk, a wardrobe and free Wi-Fi access is available.

Guests staying in the rooms will enjoy a continental breakfast daily. You can also request a homemade, traditional meal on site.

The property is situated 25 km from the Somme Bay and 15 km from The Valloires Garden.
