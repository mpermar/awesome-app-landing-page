Description

This stylish hotel is located just 200 metres from the beach in Cayeux-sur-Mer. Regional dishes with organic produce are served in the bistro, where jazz concerts are regularly organised.

Saint-Valry-sur-Somme is 11 km away from the property. An LCD TV and free Wi-Fi are provided in the guest rooms.

The decoration is inspired by the local area and some of the rooms offer a view of the village.

Fresh seafood and Belgian beers are available downstairs in the soundproofed bistro, Au Petit Caf.

You can enjoy a continental breakfast every morning at Hidden Bay Hotel. Deck chairs and beach towels are provided by the hotel, and you can also borrow binoculars and bicycles.

Noyelle-sur-Mer Train Station is 19 km away and free parking is possible nearby. Beach cabins can be rented on site from 01 May to 15 September.
