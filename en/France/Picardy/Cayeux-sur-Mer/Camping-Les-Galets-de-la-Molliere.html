Description

Camping Les Galets de la Molliere is situated 800 meters from Galets shingle beach and 4 km from the center of Cayeux-sur-Mer.

An outdoor swimming pool, a tennis courts and a children’s playground are featured at the property.

The 3-bedroom bungalows at Camping Les Galets de la Molliere are self-catering. Each has a private bathroom and an individual terrace with outdoor furniture.

During the summer, Camping Les Galets de la Molliere operates a snack bar and a grocery store on site.

Guests have access to a games room with billiards, table soccer and pinball. You can also play petanque and table tennis outside.

Cayeux-sur-Mer casino is 3. 6 km from Camping Les Galets de la Molliere. Abbeville Train Station is 27.

2 km away, while Saint-Valery-sur-Somme is a 13-minute drive from the property. Free private parking is provided on site.
