Description

This residence is located in a small village, 10 minutes from Laon in the Picardie region.

It offers soundproofed rooms, a spa centre and a heated indoor pool. Rooms at the Ferme are individually decorated, has free Wi-Fi access and each have a private bathroom.

A breakfast buffet is served every morning at the Ferme du Chateau. Meals are proposed in the restaurant upon reservation.

Guests can relax in the Chateau’s lounge bar equipped with a TV. The spa centre has a hot tub available at an extra charge.

Free evening entertainment is available in the resort during some evenings from July to August and a kids' club is provided on site.

Free private parking is available on site at the Ferme du Chateau. Lake Monampteuil and the Laon Museum of Art and Archaeology are just short drive away.
