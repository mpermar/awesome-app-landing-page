Description

Nestled in the valley of the Nonet in the charming village of St. Leonard Avilly, bed and breakfast Le Brame offers a garden and carefully furnished rooms with countryside views and free Wi-Fi.

It is just 3 minutes’ drive from Senlis and 5 minutes’ drive from Chantilly. Surrounded by water and forests, rooms offer a flat-screen TV and a terrace.

There is also an electric kettle, a desk and outdoor furniture. Within a 4-km distance you will find a variety of restaurants, and bicycles can be borrowed on site.

The breakfast room has a microwave and is available to guests all day long. Charles De Gaulle Airport is 20 km away.

The property offers free parking. Nearby points of interest include Parc Asterix, a 10-minute drive away and Golf courses of Apremont at 7 km.
