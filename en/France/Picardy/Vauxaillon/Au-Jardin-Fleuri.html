Description

This guest house is set in a renovated farm, located 5 km from the medieval town of Coucy le Chateau.

Views of the park are provided in the stylish guest rooms. Each room offers an individual decoration with parquet flooring.

The en suite bathrooms are fitted with a walk-in shower and a bathtub. Some rooms feature a lounge.

Home-made jams and local produce are served for breakfast every morning, and can be taken on the terrace.

A microwave oven and a fridge are available for guests’ use at Au Jardin Fleuri. Free Wi-Fi access is provided in public areas and there is a children’s play area in the garden.

You can also relax in the communal lounge and watch DVDs on the LCD TV. Soissons, with its cathedral and abbey, is 18 km from this guest house and free private parking is possible on site.
