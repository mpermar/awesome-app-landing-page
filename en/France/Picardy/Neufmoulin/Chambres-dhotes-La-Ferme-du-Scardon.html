Description

Located on a typical farm in Picardy, just 8 km from Abbeville, La Ferme du Scardon offers a garden, terrace, children’s play area and farm animals.

It is a 35-minute drive from the Baie de la Somme. All rooms at the bed and breakfast feature a view of the garden, a wardrobe and desk.

The en suite bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries. They both have access to a kitchenette or kitchen and the comfort room has access to the TV lounge.

A continental breakfast serving homemade marmalade and yogurt, fresh bread and fruit salad is provided each morning.

Additional features include a small fishing pond, free Wi-Fi access throughout and free private parking on site.

Le Crotoy is a 30-minute drive away and Amiens is 47 km from the bed and breakfast.
