Description

Hotel Du Champ De Foire has a restaurant, bar, a shared lounge and garden in Saint-Aulaye.

Boasting room service, this property also provides guests with a water park. The hotel has newspapers and an ATM machine that guests can use.

The hotel offers a children's playground. You can play table tennis and darts at Hotel Du Champ De Foire, and the area is popular for hiking and cycling.

Libourne is 49 km from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Bergerac Airport, 60 km from Hotel Du Champ De Foire.
