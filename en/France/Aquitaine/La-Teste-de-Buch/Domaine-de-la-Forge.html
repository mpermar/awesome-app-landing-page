Description

One of our top picks in La Teste-de-Buch.

Set in La Teste-de-Buch, Domaine de la Forge offers a garden and a seasonal outdoor pool. There is a water park on site and guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Bordeaux is 30 mi from the property.

Featuring a terrace, the accommodations comes with a seating and dining area. All units include a kitchenette fitted with a microwave and fridge. A stovetop and coffee machine are also featured. There is a private bathroom with a bath or shower in every unit.

Guests can enjoy a children's playground and a restaurant on site. The property also offers mini-market.

Bike hire is available at the property and the area is popular for golfing. You can engage in various activities, such as horse riding and diving. Arcachon is 5 mi from Domaine de la Forge, while Cap-Ferret is 7.5 mi away. The nearest airport is Mérignac Airport, 25 mi from the property.
