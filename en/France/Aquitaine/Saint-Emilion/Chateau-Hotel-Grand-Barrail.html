Description

Each guest room at the Grand Barrail Chateau includes air conditioning and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Some rooms also have a view of the surrounding vineyards.

The Grand Barrail Chateau Spa features a gourmet restaurant that serves regional cuisine.

Guests have access to the Grand Barrail’s spa, which features a sauna, hammam and 4 beauty cabins.

Free private parking is available on site, and the Chateau provides a 24-hour front desk and a bike rental service. Libourne is a 5-minute drive away.
