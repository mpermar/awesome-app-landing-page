Description

This Pierre & Vacances residence is located in Uhart-Cize, in southern Aquitane. It has a heated outdoor swimming pool and a garden with views of the town and Pyrénées landscape.

Each studio or apartment at Le Parc D’Arradoy has a TV, a fully equipped kitchen and a balcony or terrace with direct access to the garden.

The Résidence Pierre & Vacances Le Parc D’Arradoy also has a smaller pool. Books and board games can be borrowed on site for free.
