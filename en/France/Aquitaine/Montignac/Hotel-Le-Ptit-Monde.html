Description

Hôtel Le P'tit Monde is ideally situated in the black Périgord region, in Vézère Valley.

The property is just 2 km from Lascaux Caves and 25 km from the medieval city of Sarlat-la-Canéda.

Le P'tit Monde offers comfortable rooms with a decoration in warm tones. They all have a desk and a TV with satellite channels as well as a bathroom with a shower and free toiletries.

Savour typical specialties in the relaxed atmosphere of the hotel's restaurant or the terrace.

You will also start the day with a hearty breakfast that will provide you the required energy.

The area offers many activities such as discovering Perigord's most famous cities.

Hiking and bicycling can be enjoyed in the surroundings. Sarlat-la-Canéda Train Station is a 25-minute drive from the property.
