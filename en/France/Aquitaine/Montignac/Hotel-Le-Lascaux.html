Description

Hotel Le Lascaux is 300 metres from the centre of Montignac and 1 km from the famous Lascaux Caves.

It features a private garden, air conditioning in the rooms and common areas, as well as offers en suite rooms with free Wi-Fi access.

A balcony, seating area and LCD TV feature in the elegantly decorated guest rooms.

Each also has air conditioning, a minibar and a coffee maker. The private bathrooms are fitted with a shower or a bath.

Le Lascaux’s restaurant serves traditional French cuisine that guests can enjoy on the terrace.

A buffet breakfast is prepared every morning. You can borrow a portable tablet and games from reception.

There is also a playground for children on site. Free parking is provided at the Le Lascaux Hotel, making it easy to explore the Périgord Noir region by car.
