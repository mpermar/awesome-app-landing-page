Description

Located on a hill facing the Bazas fortifications, in a 10-acre park, this hotel features an indoor heated pool, an outdoor pool and a hammam.

Free WiFi and free parking are available. Rooms at Domaine de Fompeyre are equipped with satellite TV, a telephone and private bathroom.

In addition to a relaxation area, the hotel features a solarium and games room. The area surrounding Domaine de Fompeyre is ideal for mountain biking.

Domaine de Fompeyre is 12 km from Landes Forest and 20 km from Sauternes. Bordeaux is a 45-minute drive away.
