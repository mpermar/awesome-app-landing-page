Description

Located a 12 minutes drive from Sarlat-la-Canéda and set on the edge of the river of Dordogne, Hôtel Restaurant Plaisance offers a regional restaurant.

Guests can enjoy a heated, seasonal swimming pool in a landscaped garden situated just 200 metres away.

The air-conditioned rooms at Hôtel Restaurant Plaisance include a flat-screen TV, a safety deposit box and a hairdryer.

Free Wi-Fi access is provided. A buffet breakfast is served every morning. You can sample the regional meals such as foie gras, cabécou cheese and duck on the shaded terrace.

Other facilities are included such as a tennis court, table tennis and mini golf.

Guests can fish, canoe and horse ride in the surroundings. Kayaking excursions are possible at a location nearby.

The property is located 11 km from Beynac-et-Cazenac Castle and 6 km from La Roque-Gageac.
