Description

Beaumanoir is located in the Winter Park district, 25 minutes' walk to the Atlantic beaches in Biarritz.

This ancient manor combines tradition with modernity, and has a heated swimming pool.

Guest rooms have been decorated in an elegant Baroque style fitted with classical furnishings and large marble bathrooms.

All rooms feature a bed, a sofa and a minibar. Free Wi-Fi is also available in public areas.

If you are travelling with friends or family, try one of the hotels suites or apartments with separate lounge.

All guests are welcome to enjoy a drink in front of the fireplace in the Champagne lounge bar.

The immediate vicinity of the Hotel Le Beaumanoir provides many leisure activities such as golf, tennis, spa facilities, cinemas and a casino.

You can also make an appointment for a massage. The hotel offers transfers to and from the airport in a Rolls Royce at an extra cost.
