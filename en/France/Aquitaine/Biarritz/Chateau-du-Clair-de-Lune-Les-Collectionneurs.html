Description

Chateau Du Clair De Lune is in a 19th-century manor house located 5 km from the centre of Biarritz.

Free Wi-Fi is available. The rooms are split between the manor house and the hunting lodge.

Each room has a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, bathtub, hairdryer and minibar.

Rooms in the hunting lodge have a private terrace with garden or park view. The Chateau’s manor house has a lounge and library.

Guests are invited to enjoy Chateau Du Clair De Lune’s park, which is full of roses, magnolia and rhododendron.

Arosta is an independent restaurant on the hotel’s estate. It is housed in an 18th-century farm and serves traditional cuisine.
