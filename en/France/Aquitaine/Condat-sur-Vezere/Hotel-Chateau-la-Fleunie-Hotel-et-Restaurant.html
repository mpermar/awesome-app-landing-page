Description

This hotel is housed in a castle on 106-hectare grounds, which include a swimming pool, 2 tennis courts and an animal park. It is located in the Dordogne countryside and also has a sauna and a fitness center.

The Château de la Fleunie provides guests with a gastronomic restaurant, a pool-side grill and 2 bars. The buffet breakfast features hot beverages, fruit juice, homemade jams, ham, cheese, fruit, cereals and yogurts.

Guest rooms at the Fleunie come with private bathrooms and free WiFi access. Some also feature a flat-screen TV, spa bath or balcony.

Château de la Fleunie is located on the banks of the Vézère River. It is 19 mi from Sarlat-la-Canéda, and guests can also visit the Lascaux Cave in Montignac, which is 5 mi away.
