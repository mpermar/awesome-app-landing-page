Description

The family-run Prince Noir is located in the small village of Sérignac-sur-Garonne, in eastern Aquitaine.

It has an outdoor swimming pool, a shaded courtyard and terrace, and a tennis court.

Each of the Prince Noir’s guest rooms has a private bathroom, satellite TV with Canal+ and free Wi-Fi.

This Logis restaurant serves traditional cuisine for lunch and dinner. A buffet breakfast is also available and meals can be enjoyed on the hotel terrace.

This 17th century building has a garden and scenic surroundings. Guests can visit the historic fortified village and medieval Church.

Agen is situated 12 km from the Prince Noir. A charging station for electric cars is available at this property.
