Description

Les Sources de Caudalie is located in the Château Smith Haut Lafitte vineyards, less than a 20-minute drive from Bordeaux historic district. It features a luxury spa center, tennis courts and an outdoor swimming pool.

This 5-star hotel offers air-conditioned guestrooms and suites with free WiFi, cable TV and DVD players. Rooms have a private terrace or balcony with views across the lake or vineyards.

Les Sources de Caudalie’s Spa offers wine and grape-based treatments, a hot tub and hammam.

The hotel has 1 gourmet restaurants and a cooking school. La Grand Vigne is a Michelin starred restaurant.

Les Sources De Caudalie also offers wine-tasting evenings. A charging station for electric cars is available at this property.
