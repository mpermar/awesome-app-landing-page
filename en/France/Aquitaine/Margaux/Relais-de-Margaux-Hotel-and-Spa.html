Description

Relais de Margaux is located in the heart of the prestigious Margaux vineyards in Médoc, on a 55-hectare estate.

This 19th-century wine chateau is set on the banks of the Gironde Estuary and offers an outdoor pool.

The 88 air-conditioned rooms at this 4-star property feature free WiFi, a cable TV, a safety deposit box and a minibar.

They are all individually decorated and offer views of the golf course and the gardens.

Guests can enjoy a glass of wine at the bar. There are also 2 restaurants: a gourmet restaurant, L'Ile Vincent and the Brasserie du Lac which offers a panoramic view of the golf course.

The wellness centre, Les Bains de Margaux, features a sauna, a hammam and a heated indoor pool.

A fitness center is also available and guests can request massages at an extra fee.

24-hour reception is available on site, as well as free bikes and as a tennis court.

The Relais is a 40-minute drive from Bordeaux and Bordeaux-Mérignac Airport, and 75 km by car from Blaye.
