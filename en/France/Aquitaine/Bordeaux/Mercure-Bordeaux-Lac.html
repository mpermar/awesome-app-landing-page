Description

Located on the outskirts of Bordeaux, just 1 km from Nouveau Stade Bordeaux and Bordeaux Exhibition Centre, this Mercure hotel features an outdoor swimming pool and a terrace.

It offers air-conditioned rooms with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Each guest room also has a private bathroom with a shower.

Free Wi-Fi access is provided in the entire Mercure Bordeaux Le Lac. Brasserie au 33 restaurant serves traditional French cuisine and a wide range of wines.

Breakfast is prepared every morning. Bordeaux Casino is just a 2-minute walk from The Mercure Bordeaux Lac and Parc des Expositions Line C tram stop is a 5-minute walk away.
