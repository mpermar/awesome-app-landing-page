Description

Résidence Pierre & Vacances Les Dunes du Médoc is a resort featuring a garden and a solarium with an outdoor swimming pool.

It is located in the city of Soulac-sur-Mer, in the Aquitaine region. The spacious guest rooms at the Résidence Pierre & Vacances Les Dunes du Médoc are modern and brightly decorated.

All rooms feature a kitchenette fully-equipped with a dishwasher, microwave and a refrigerator, and also feature a balcony.

Activities available on site include cycling, and there is also a children’s playground.

Résidence Pierre & Vacances Les Dunes du Médoc is ideally situated 300 metres from the beach and 1 km from the town centre.

The famous vineyards of Medoc and Margaux are also nearby. Free parking is provided on site for guests arriving by car, subject to availability.
