Description

Hotel Les Goelands is located in the coastal town of Saint Jean de Luz. This eco-friendly hotel offers rooms with satellite TV and views on the sea or garden.

Open from end of March to the end of October, the restaurant at Les Goelands serves traditional family dishes.

The menu changes daily and the cuisine is made with fresh local products. Hotel Les Goelands is completely non-smoking and public parking is available onsite and free of charge.
