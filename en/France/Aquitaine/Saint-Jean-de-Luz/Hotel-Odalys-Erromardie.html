Description

Hôtel Odalys Erromardie is located 400 metres from Erromardie Beach in Saint-Jean-de-Luz.

It offers a heated, outdoor swimming pool and a tennis court. The 29 rooms of Hôtel Odalys Erromardie come with a private bathroom and a TV, and some of them feature a balcony or terrace.

Some rooms are overlooking the sea and the others face the mountain. The hotel offers a restaurant with a terrace and a large choice of leisure activities.
