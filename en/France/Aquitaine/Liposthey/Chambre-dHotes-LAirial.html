Description

A 19th-century stone house located 15 km from Parentis-en-Born, Chambre d'hôtes L'Airial sits in a landscaped garden.

It offers free WiFi throughout and the continental breakfast is served daily. With a view of the garden or Landes Forest, the air-conditioned rooms feature tea and coffee making facilities, and an en suite bathroom with a hairdryer.

The continental breakfast includes homemade products is served in the conservatory with garden views.

A shared, fully equipped kitchen is available to guests and restaurants serving traditional French cuisine can be found within 1 km of the property.

A bicycling path to Biscarrosse or Pissos is located just 800 metres from the property.

Secure private parking is free on site as well as a bicycle and motorbike shed. The guesthouse is 1 km from exit 17 on the A63 Motorway.
