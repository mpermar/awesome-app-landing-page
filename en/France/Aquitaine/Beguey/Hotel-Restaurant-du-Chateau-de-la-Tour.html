Description

Set in the green and tranquil environment of a 7-acre park, this hotel boasts an outdoor swimming pool.

Enjoy innovative gourmet cuisine in the elegant restaurant or on the lovely garden’s terrace.

Cadillac and the area surrounding the Château De La Tour are full of historical castles and fortified villages to visit.

The Château De La Tour’s location in the heart of the vineyards between Sauternes and St Emilion means there is plenty of excellent wine and gastronomy to be explored too.
