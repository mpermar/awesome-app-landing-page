Description

Located in the heart of the Perigord Noir, Hotel Bien Etre aux Cypres de Marquay (ex Mounea) is in the Dordogne region, 11 km from Sarlat-la-Canéda.

It offers a heated outdoor swimming pool and rooms with free WiFi access. Guest rooms at the Hotel Bien Etre aux Cypres de Marquay (ex Mounea) are equipped with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

Breakfast is served daily and guests can enjoy their meal on the terrace, with a panoramic view of the Perigord countryside.

Sports and well-being massages are available onsite, as well as make-up tutorials.

This hotel's location makes it an excellent base to discover the Vezere Valley as well as Lascaux Cave located 18 km away.

Hiking, fishing and canoeing can also be enjoyed in the surroundings. Free private parking is available on site.
