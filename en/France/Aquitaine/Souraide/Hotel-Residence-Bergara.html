Description

The Hotel Residence Bergara is located in the village of Sourraide in the heart of the Basque country.

It features a garden and a bar-restaurant with a terrace. The Bergara hotel offers comfortable rooms and studios with a modern décor.

Rooms are serviced by a lift and free Wi-Fi access is available. The traditional hotel restaurant serves regional specialties.

A continental breakfast is served every morning. Sauna is also available at an extra cost.

With free private parking on site, Bergara residence is located 20 kilometres from Biarritz and just 2 kilometres from Espelette.
