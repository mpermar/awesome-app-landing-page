Description

The coast of the Landes is renowned for its large pine-trees forest, its sandy beaches bordering the Atlantic Ocean and its numerous lakes, which are authentic havens ideal for outdoor activities.

The 3-star residence Les Cottages du Lac welcomes you for pleasant and warm holidays on the shores of Parentis Lake.

With WiFi access, the residence features a heated swimming pool, a sauna and a fitness room.

During summer, a snack-bar is available on site. The cottages and the apartments of the residence harmoniously blend with the environment, on which they offer wonderful views.

They each feature a furnished balcony or terrace. Bed linen and towels are provided for free.
