Description

Au Comté d'Ornon is a 15-minute drive from Bordeaux city centre and just 400 metres from the Bayonne/Arcachon/Espagne exit of the A63 highway.

The property features a spa and offers a range of beauty treatments and massages.

It offers air-conditioned rooms with contemporary bathrooms, free high-speed WiFi access and a flat-screen TV.

The hotel restaurant has a garden terrace and serves inventive regional cuisine. A buffet breakfast is served on the terrace or in the breakfast room.

Au Comté d'Ornon is conveniently located 2 km from Pessac and 4 km from Talence. It is located 2 minutes from the A63 motorway.
