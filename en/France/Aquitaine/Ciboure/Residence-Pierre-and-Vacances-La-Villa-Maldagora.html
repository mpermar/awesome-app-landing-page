Description

Pierre & Vacances La Villa Maldagora is located 3 km from the beach. Apartments range from studios, 1 bedroom or 1/2 bedrooms and can accommodate up to 7 people.

All are fitted with fully-equipped kitchens, bathrooms with bath and shower. The seasonal outdoor swimming pool is open from May to September.

Board games can be borrowed on site. This resort is situated between the Atlantic Ocean and the foothills of the Pyrenees Mountains at the gateway to Spain.
