Description

Au Feu De Bois is located between Dax and Pau, in the Landes region of southern France.

The hotel has an outdoor swimming pool and offers free private parking and free Wi-Fi.

Guest rooms at Au Feu de Bois have a flat screen TV and en suite bathroom with a shower and hairdryer.

A breakfast is served daily and guests can enjoy a traditional French meal in the hotel’s restaurant.

The hotel also has a garden with a large terrace and a separate children’s paddling pool.

The hotel is 180 kilometres south of Bordeaux and a 30 minute drive from the town of Dax, with its thermal baths.
