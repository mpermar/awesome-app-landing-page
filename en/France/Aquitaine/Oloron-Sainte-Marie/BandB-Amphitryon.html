Description

Located in Oloron-Sainte-Marie, Amphitryon is in the heart of the medieval district and a 15-minute walk from Cathédrale Sainte-Marie.

The property offers soundproofed rooms, a terrace and a garden. Free Wi-Fi access is available.

With views of Place Saint-Pierre, all rooms feature wooden floors and a work desk.

A private bathroom with a shower is also provided and guests have access to a communal TV lounge.

Guests are invited to enjoy a continental breakfast every morning at Amphitryon. They can also sample traditional dishes for dinner if they reserve before arrival.

This property has a communal living room with a TV and a fireplace. A games room with a piano is also available.
