Description

This charming country hotel, a former 17th-century farm was converted into a lovely hotel in 1995.

Since then, it has provided guests with comfortable accommodation and a family atmosphere.

An outdoor infinity pool is available on site. Over the years it has become a much-appreciated stopover for people visiting the Périgord Noir region.

Guestrooms here are bright, spacious and warmly decorated. Guests can have dinner on site upon request and availability or the hosts can book them a table at a nearby restaurant.

Relais du Silence Hôtel de la Ferme Lamy offers an authentic family atmosphere and stays that follow different themes such as hot-air balloons, truffles, and getaway weekends.

Also included are free Wi-Fi internet access and a free private car park for guests traveling to the area by car.
