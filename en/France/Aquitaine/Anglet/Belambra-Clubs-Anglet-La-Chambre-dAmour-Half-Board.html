Description

Set on the seafront in Anglet, Belambra Clubs Anglet - La Chambre d'Amour - Half Board is an 8-minute drive from Biarritz and its famous beaches.

It offers 2 outdoor pools and a bar with panoramic views. The modern guest rooms and apartments are decorated with bright colours and feature sea views.

Each room is equipped with a flat-screen TV and a fridge. All accommodations feature a private bathroom.

Belambra Clubs Anglet - La Chambre d'Amour - Half Board features a buffet restaurant with a terrace, serving regional specialities.

Breakfast is available every morning. Guests can choose to relax in the lounge, in the garden during the summer or in the games room featuring table tennis.

WiFi access is available at an extra charge. With free private parking on site, Belambra Clubs Anglet - La Chambre d'Amour - Half Board is just 6 km from Biarritz-Anglet-Bayonne Airport.
