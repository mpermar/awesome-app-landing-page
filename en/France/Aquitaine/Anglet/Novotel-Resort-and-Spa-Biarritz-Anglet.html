Description

This Novotel hotel provides 4-star accommodation 1 km from Biarritz-Anglet-Bayonne Airport.

The rooms have an en suite bathroom, TV and work area. Free W-Fi is available throughout the hotel.

Novotel Resort & Spa Biarritz Anglet is set in a 3-hectare park and has a variety of leisure activities including an exterior heated swimming pool.

Novotel’s restaurant Ardoise Gourmande serves traditional and local cuisine and has views of the park and swimming pool.

During nice weather, guests can dine on the terrace. Guests are invited to enjoy a drink at Au Comptoir wine bar open until midnight.

The hotel also provides rooms service for guests’ convenience. Novotel Resort & Spa Biarritz Anglet is a good base to explore the Basque Country.

It is 500 meters from the town centre and 4 km from beaches. San Sebastian is a 30-minute drive away.
