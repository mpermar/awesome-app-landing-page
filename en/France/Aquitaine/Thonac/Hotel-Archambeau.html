Description

This family hotel composed set in several houses is situated in the village of Thonac, in the heart of the Dordogne region.

It has a swimming pool, a private pond and a restaurant. The guest rooms at the Hotel Archambeau are equipped with flat-screen TVs and have private bathrooms with showers.

Some rooms have air conditioning as well as a private terrace. The Archambeau’s restaurant serves regional specialities and features an outdoor terrace.

Guests can relax on the sun terrace by the pool, or enjoy a drink in the bar. Hotel Archambeau is located 20 km from the medieval town of Sarlat-la-Canéda and 5 km from Lascaux Caves.

Guests can enjoy fishing in the hotel’s pond, or horse-riding, canoeing and hiking in the surrounding area.
