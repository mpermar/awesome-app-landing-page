Description

Offering a seasonal heated outdoor pool and views of the pond, Naturéo is situated in Seignosse in the Aquitaine Region, 26 km from Biarritz.

Saint-Jean-de-Luz is 39 km from the property. There is a water park less than 3 km away.

All units include a seating and dining area. There is also a kitchen, fitted with a microwave and refrigerator.

A stovetop and coffee machine are also offered. There is a private bathroom with a shower.

Naturéo also includes a children's playground. Guests can enjoy a drink at the on-site snack bar.

You can play table tennis and mini-golf at the property, and bike hire is available.

Bayonne is 24 km from Naturéo, while Hendaye is 45 km away. At an extra cost, a shuttle to and from Bordeaux and Biarritz airports is available.
