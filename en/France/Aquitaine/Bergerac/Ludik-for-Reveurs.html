Description

Situated in a green environment, Ludik for Rêveurs is located a 5-minute drive from Bergerac city centre and Bergerac Airport.

It has outdoor swimming pool, terrace and guest rooms with free WiFi and LCD cable TV.

Guests of Ludik for Rêveurs can enjoy a drink in the hotel’s bar and can play 300 board games with a friendly team.

The entire hotel is air-conditioned. Guests staying at this hotel will enjoy a personalized welcome.

The hotel serves a buffet breakfast, which can be taken on the outside terrace in the warmer months.

There is also a brasserie on site where guests can dine all year long. At Ludik for Rêveurs, meeting rooms are available to host events up to 120 seats.

Ludik for Rêveurs is easily accessible from the Motorway. Guests can also visit the nearby Château de Monbazillac located just 5 km from the hotel.
