Description

Philippe Starck’s new project, the hotel Ha(a)ïtza is located next to the Dune of Pilat and the famous hotel La Co(o)rniche.

Mixing the light wood and warm-white tones with fine touches of steel, the room decoration follows perfectly the Philippe Starck’s best codes, for a refined and unpretentious style. The glazed bathroom opens on the gentle and welcoming bedroom.

Ha(a)ïtza also includes a one-Michelin-starred gastronomic restaurant run by Chef Stéphane Carrade, a cocktail bar, a cake shop and a tea house providing a selection of infusions and organic teas.

The hotel features a covered swimming pool under an opening glass roof as well as a Ern spa, a famous Parisian hairdresser's salon, and a Techno-gym fitness room. The spacious reception hall, with a view over the swimming pool, can host weddings for up to 200 people. Guests will also find a boutique, and a conference room with a 50-person capacity.

A charging station for electric cars is available at this property.
