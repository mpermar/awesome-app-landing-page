Description

The Comfort Hotel Régina Périgueux is located in central Perigueux, opposite the SNCF train station.

It offers a free airport shuttle bus, a tennis court and an on-site bar. Comfort Hotel Régina Périgueux' air-conditioned rooms are all equipped with flat-screen cable TV and free WiFi internet access.

Some have a balcony. Breakfast is served daily and is composed of cold meats, cheese, yogurts, compotes, dry and fresh fruit, cereals, French pastries, cakes and biscuits.

The hotel is just a 5-minute drive from Saint-Front Cathedral. Private parking is available on site.
