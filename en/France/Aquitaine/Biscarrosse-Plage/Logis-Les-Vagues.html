Description

It offers en-suite and air conditioned rooms with flat-screen TV, some featuring a terrace or a balcony. Bathrooms offers massaging jet showers.

Les Vagues’s restaurant "Les Vagues" serves gastronomic cuisine and regional dishes. Meals can be enjoyed on the 2 outdoor terraces during fine weather. Guests can also enjoy a drink at the bar.

There is a playground for children on site, a mini golf and a ping pong table. With the free private parking and electric vehicle charging station, guests can easily explore the Landes region.
