Description

Offering a hot tub, a children's play area and an outdoor pool, Hotel du Porge is located just 9 km from Porge's beaches and 10 km from Arcachon Bay.

It offers air-conditioned accommodation, a large garden and a terrace with an outdoor swimming pool and views of the woods.

A private balcony and free WiFi access are featured in all of the rooms at Hotel du Porge.

They are decorated in a contemporary style and also include a TV. Hotel du Porge serves a buffet breakfast, which can be enjoyed in the breakfast room or on the terrace.

Other local activities include driving the 20 km to Lacanau Lake and Méjanne Golf Course.

Free private parking is available. It is closed at night and has a video surveillance.
