Description

VVF Villages Lège-Cap-Ferret is located in the heart of Arcachon Bay, 800 meters from the beach and 7 km from the ocean.

Free WiFi access is available in this holiday park and a snack bar is available in the summer.

There is also an outdoor heated pool and a paddling pool. The holiday homes have a terrace, a TV, a kitchen with a dishwasher and a refrigerator.

The private bathrooms feature showers. At VVF Villages Lège-Cap-Ferret you will find barbecue facilities and a children's playground.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling.
