Description

Located 1 km from the medieval town of Sarlat, Les Chalets d'Argentouleau offers a panoramic view of the town.

It features an outdoor swimming pool with a paddling pool and snack bar selling ice creams.

With a furnished terrace, the accommodations all have BBQ facilities and a dining area.

The private bathrooms have a shower, and the kitchens include a mini-oven, stove and fridge.

Leisure activities include table tennis, a pétanque area and a children’s playground.

Free Wi-Fi access is available in public areas and you can also use the fitness equipment provided.

The Causses du Quercy Regional Park is a 45-minute drive from this property and you can visit the picturesque town of Sarlat.
