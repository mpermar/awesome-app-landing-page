Description

Offering an outdoor, covered swimming pool, and a bar, Les Chambres Du Club Vert is located in Sarlat-la-Canéda.

Free WiFi access is available. At Les Chambres Du Club Vert, each accommodation has a terrace and a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries.

Other facilities offered at the property include a children's playground. The hotel is 1.

8 km from Sarlat-la-Canéda Train Station. Bergerac-Roumanière Airport is 57 km away.
