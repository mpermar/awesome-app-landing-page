Description

Best Western Le Renoir features an outdoor swimming pool, free WiFi and comfortable, elegant accommodation situated just 450 metres from the historic centre of the picturesque town of Sarlat, in the heart of Périgord.

The spacious guest rooms are equipped with modern facilities, including satellite TV, a minibar and an en suite bathroom.

Some have air conditioning, and rooms for families are available. In the warmer months, enjoy a refreshing drink by the swimming pool or lie in the sun on a relaxing sun lounger.

The Best Western Le Renoir is ideally situated to enjoy the best parts of town. Stroll through beautiful cobbled streets, eat a gourmet meal in a local restaurant and look around the lovely shops.
