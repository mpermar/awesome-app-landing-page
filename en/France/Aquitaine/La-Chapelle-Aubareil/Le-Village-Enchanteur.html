Description

Offering a seasonal salted outdoor swimming pool, Le Village Enchanteur is a holiday park with chalets located in La Chapelle-Aubareil, at the heart of a forest, a 5-minute drive from Lascaux Caves.

Each chalet has a furnished terrace, a seating area, a kitchen with a dishwasher and a private bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling, fishing and hiking.

There is a supermarket 10 km from the property and a traditional restaurant 1 km away.

This holiday park is 15 minutes drive from Sarlat-La-Canda and there is free parking on site.
