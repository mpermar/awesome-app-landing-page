Description

The Hotel Restaurant Chilo is situated in the heart of the mountains, in the village of Barcus.

The hotel has a tree-lined garden and a swimming pool. La maison Chilo is a charming hotel with a lot of character, offering an excellent cuisine.

You will be able to enjoy wonderful excursions to the mountains. Breakfast is prepared every morning and packed lunches can be prepared upon request.

You can also enjoy a drink from the bar while relaxing on the terrace. Free public parking is available on site.
