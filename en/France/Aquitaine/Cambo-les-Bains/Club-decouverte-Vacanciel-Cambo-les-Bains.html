Description

Set in a 2-hectare park opposite the Pyrennees mountains, this residence provides en suite rooms with TV.

It also offers an outdoor pool and a tennis court and daily entertainment. A full buffet breakfast is provided every morning at Club découverte Vacanciel Cambo les Bains.

The restaurant, Au Mai Nuna, serves a buffet-style menu every day. Cambo-Les-Bains’s spa, just 2.

5 km away, is equipped with a hammam, a hot tub and multi-jet showers. Guests can also have a drink at the bar or relax on the pool terrace.

Additional facilities include tour desk service and free WiFi access in public areas, including the common TV room.

Club discovery Vacanciel Cambo les Bains is 13 km from Spain and offers free private parking.
