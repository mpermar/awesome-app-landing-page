Description

Offering a year-round outdoor pool and views of the pool, Le Domaine du Cerneau is situated in Nailhac, 39 km from Sarlat-la-Canéda.

Free private parking is available on site. Some rooms feature a seating area for your convenience.

You will find a kettle in the room. Every room has a private bathroom. You can play table tennis at the bed and breakfast.

Brive-la-Gaillarde is 30 km from Le Domaine du Cerneau, while Périgueux is 35 km away.
