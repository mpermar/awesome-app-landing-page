Description

Golden Bay provides spacious rooms and suites with balconies and views of the Mediterranean Sea. Each room has air conditioning and is equipped with satellite TV and a minibar.

The Golden Bay Beach Hotel provides several dining options. Guests can swim up to the pool bar to order a refreshing drink, or visit the English pub.

The hotel is surrounded by landscaped grounds. Guests can enjoy a relaxing massage or beauty treatment at the spa. Hotel Golden Bay also has a game room, a playground and provides childcare services.

Larnaca International Airport is 8 miles from the Golden Bay Hotel. The center of Larnaca is a 15-minute drive away.
