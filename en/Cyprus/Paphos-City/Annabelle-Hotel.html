Description

The rooms at the Annabelle Hotel include elegant marble features, and deluxe amenities including bathrobes and slippers. All rooms come with mini-bars, satellite TVs, and free Wi-Fi.

Guests of the Annabelle are greeted with fresh juice and a refreshing cold towel upon arrival.

Guests can stroll through the landscaped gardens and admire the waterfalls. For a relaxing experience, guests can spend time in the sauna or use the spa center provided at the sister property next door.

The on-site Mediterraneo Restaurant serves traditional Cypriot, Greek and international cuisine in a charming setting with views of the sea. Fontana, the Annabelle’s main restaurant serves buffet-style meals with a children’s menu also available. Certified Cypriot breakfast is served in the morning.

The Annabelle is surrounded by the local shops, tavernas and bars of Paphos. Guests can enjoy sightseeing in the town, or a number of water sports and activities in the area. The Paphos Airport is 9.9 mi away. Free parking is available.

Kato Paphos is a great choice for travelers interested in ancient landmarks, harbors and history.
