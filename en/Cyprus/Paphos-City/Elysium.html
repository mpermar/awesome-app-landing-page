Description

All accommodations include a balcony or terrace with picturesque views of the lush grounds or the Mediterranean Sea. Every room has a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, and an private bathroom.

Elysium’s range of restaurants allows guests to enjoy Italian, Japanese or other international cuisine. Traditional Cypriot breakfast is served every morning. Outdoor dining provides views of the gardens and the sea.

The Elysium features a stunning multi-level outdoor pool, including a hydro-massage area. Guests can enjoy the adults-only Royal Pool, or relax with a drink at the swim-up bar.

The Opium Health Spa provides a wide choice of treatments with elements of aromatherapy and hydrotherapy. Elysium has 2 tennis courts, a scuba diving center and a gym.

Elysium is 30 minutes’ drive from Paphos Airport. Free on-site car parking is available.
