Description

Every air-conditioned room at the Cavo Maris Beach Hotel has a satellite TV and radio. All rooms include a seating area and a work desk.

The Alkion Restaurant offers international cuisine served on the hotel terrace with sea views. Guests can enjoy cocktails at the Coral bar and snacks and drinks at the Sirena Pool Bar.

Table tennis and billiards are available. There is a children’s pool and an on-site kid’s club that entertains younger guests. An indoor pool, hot tub and gym are provided for guests to use free of charge. Massage and beauty treatments can be arranged on request and extra charge.

Shops, bars and restaurants can be found within 350 feet of the hotel. A bus to central Protaras and Agia Napa stops within a 3-minute walk. Free private parking is offered at the hotel.
