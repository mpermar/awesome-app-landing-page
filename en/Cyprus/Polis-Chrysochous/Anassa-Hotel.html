Description

The Anassa Hotel combines tasteful decoration with elegant furnishings for a truly beautiful village-style setting. Luxurious rooms and suites are furnished with all modern comforts, including free wireless internet access. They also offer views of Anassa's beautiful garden.

The picturesque garden and pool areas are the ideal place for rest and relaxation. Indoor and outdoor pools ensure your relaxing retreat in every season. Anassa’s attractive spa center offers a range of treatments and massages for your health and well being.

Children can use their own pool and entertaining mini club. Direct access to the secluded, sandy beach allows you to easily enjoy a variety of water sports. Anassa Hotel can also arrange sailing lessons and cruises during your stay.

Guests can start their day with a buffet breakfast, and the Anassa offers a variety of fine dining restaurants featuring a number of gourmet delights for dinner. Afterwards, relax on Anassa’s intimate candlelit terrace and enjoy a drink while taking in the sunset.
