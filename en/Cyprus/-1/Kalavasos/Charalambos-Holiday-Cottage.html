Description

Each of the traditional apartments includes a fully equipped kitchen and a bathroom. Custom-made iron beds, Ralph Lauren bath towels and bedding, free WiFi and an LCD TV with local and international channels are standard. All rooms are equipped with ceiling fans and air conditioning.

Guests are greeted with a seasonal, own-produced fruit basket or fruit sweets, own-produced olive oil, bottle of homemade wine, coffee/tea, soft drinks and bottled water upon check in. Charalambos Holiday Cottage is within walking distance to a local market and taverns serving traditional Cypriot food.

Public parking is available in less than 350 feet from the house. Limassol City is around 18.6 miles away, while Larnaca International Airport is at a distance of 24.9 miles. The owners, over a welcome drink, will advise guests on places to visit along with local activities like horseback riding and hiking or local attractions, like the neolithic settlements of Tenta and Chirokitia.

Charalambos Cottage also includes a seasonal outdoor pool.

Bike rental is available at the property and the area is popular for biking and hiking. Limassol is 16 miles from Charalambos Holiday Cottage, and Nicosia is 28 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Larnaca International Airport, 20 miles from Charalambos Holiday Cottage.
