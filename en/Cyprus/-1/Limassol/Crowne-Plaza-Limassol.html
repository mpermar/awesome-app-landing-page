Description

The tastefully decorated rooms of Crowne Plaza Limassol have wooden floors and well-chosen furnishings. Each air conditioned unit comes equipped with an LCD satellite TV, a coffee maker and a mini-bar. Bathrobes, slippers and Sleep Advantage Kits are also available.

The Haven Restaurant and Terrace offers international cuisine and features panoramic views of the hotel's own harbor and Limassol Bay. Guests can also enjoy Italian fusion cuisine at the sea-view La Brezza restaurant. A variety of drinks and coffees are available at Med Pool Bar and the seasonal Lime beach bar, while The Lounge Bar provides cocktails and live entertainment and free Wi-Fi. 24-hour room service is available.

Guests can visit the fitness center or enjoy a massage. Other hotel facilities include a sauna, a steam room, table tennis and billiards.

A shopping district can be found 1.9 mi away. Kolossi Castle is 8.7 mi from the Crowne Plaza. Cyprus University of Technology is 1.7 mi away. The hotel offers free on-site private parking.
