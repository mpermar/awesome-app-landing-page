Experiences

07/31/2017

A clean well equipped modern villa with good sized pool to the North of Coral Bay.

"The villa was spacious and clean with modern fixtures and fittings. its appearance was true to the images on the website. The pool was a good size and the children had great fun in it. It was kept clean The garden had some fantastic fruit trees: passion fruit and pomegranites and there will be ripe oranges later in the year. Well equipped with cots and high chairs if you have a young family.The air conditioning worked well and we needed it when we first arrived. The owners agent was friendly and discrete ( he came early to clean the pool but we would have been unaware but for the freshly cleaned pool!). the villa is located just to the north of Coral Bay. It is close to the tourist restaurants but not on top of them. it is also close to several beaches within walking distance. A hire car is essential if you want to make your own way about and explore further afield but there seemed to be a reliable bus service taking people into Paphos and the sites to be seen there."
