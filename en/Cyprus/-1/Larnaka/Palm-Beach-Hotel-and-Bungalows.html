Description

All rooms and bungalows at Palm Beach Hotel are decorated in contemporary style with Mediterranean colors. All have balconies with either a garden or a sea view, and a bathroom with a shower and bathtub.

Palm Beach Hotel and Bungalows features 5 restaurants and 5 bars that serve a range of international cuisine. Breakfast buffet is provided in the coffee shop or on the outdoor patio.

The Palm Beach has an outdoor and indoor heated swimming pool, as well as a lagoon-style pool. A range of body and facial treatments are available at its spa center. It also has a sauna, hot tub and a steam bath.
