Description

All rooms and suites at the Lordos Beach Hotel have air conditioning, mini fridge, and satellite TV. Some rooms have private grounds and a wooden deck, which leads down to shared hot bathtubs. A minibar is included in some units or can be requested.

The Lordos Beach Hotel includes a large range of on-site water sports, including scuba diving, windsurfing, and water skiing. The hotel also has an indoor pool, a tennis court, and a kids club. Free Wi-Fi is available in the bar and the lobby.

Guests can enjoy traditional Cypriot, Mediterranean or international dishes in the 2 restaurants. The 3 bars serve a wide variety of drinks, including local wines and exotic cocktails.

The Lordos Beach Hotel is a 15-minute drive from the center of Larnaca, where guests can visit the Larnaca Fort and the ruins of ancient Kitium.
