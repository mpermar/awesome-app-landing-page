Description

All guest rooms have satellite TV, telephone, minibar and hairdryer. Each has a work desk and private bathroom with bathrobe and slippers.

Guests can use a free shuttle service to Cape Greco National Park, 3 miles from the hotel. The small picturesque harbor of Ayia Napa is only a 10-minute walk away.

Grecian Bay Spa Centre offers specialized massage treatments and aerobic sessions. Guests can also relax in the hot tub and have the hair salon.
