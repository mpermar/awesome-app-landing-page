Description

The recently refurbished suites at Mon Repos are spacious and include a sitting area with modern furniture. Each well-stocked kitchenette comes equipped with a refrigerator, a microwave and a coffee maker.

Local and international meals are served at the hotel’s restaurant. Guests can get drinks and refreshments from hotel Mon Repos' bar.

Free private parking is available at Mon Repos, and the hotel is a 5-minute drive from many of Ayia Napa’s bars and cafés.

Water World is a 10-minute walk from Mon Repos.
