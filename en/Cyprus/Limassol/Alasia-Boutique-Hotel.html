Description

Featuring wooden flooring, double glazed windows and air conditioning, all accommodations types at the Alasia have an LCD satellite TV and seating area. Each comes with a mini-bar, electric kettle and free toiletries. Some units feature a spa bath and/or outdoor hot tub.

The stylish Polo Bar serves cocktails, champagne and a selection of light food. Italian cuisine and selected wines are offered at the restaurant. Guests can also enjoy breakfast at the lounge and patio. Sunday brunch is served on the terrace.

Several rejuvenating treatments, sauna, steam bath, hot tub and a state-of-the-art fitness center are among the wellness options provided.

The nearest beach is a 10-minute drive away. The Limassol shopping center is at 2,650 feet. The old and new Limassol ports and the Limassol marina are within 0.6 mi. The Cyprus University of Technology is 0.9 mi away. Paphos Airport is a 35-minute drive away. Free private parking is possible on site, while the property can arrange taxi transfer upon request.
