Description

The spacious rooms at GrandResort Limassol are equipped with free WiFi, a LED TV, laptop safes and a mini-bar. Bathrooms include bathrobes, slippers and amenities. All bedrooms have a balcony, with most of them overlooking the Mediterranean Sea.

The 6 restaurants provide guests with a choice of cuisines. Restaurant Anthea serves an international menu and Kohili serves fresh fish on the beachfront. Authentic Asian dishes and sake are prepared at the open-air Kyoshi. The elegant Tycoons Bar overlooks the sea and serves coffee and drinks.

Guests can relax in the wellness center, which features a spa and a fitness club. There is also an indoor swimming pool and a games room with a billiards table. Diving courses can be arranged by the hotel's staff, while a kid’s club is provided for younger guests.

The hotel is 35 mi from Larnaca Airport and 43 mi from Paphos Airport. Limassol is 6.8 mi away. Free on-site car parking is available.
