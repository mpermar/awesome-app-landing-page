Description

The modern Scandic Segevång Hotel is just 12 minutes from Malmo city centre and borders the Malmö Burlöv Golf Club where guests have free green fees.

Guests are welcome to use Segevång’s 8-metre heated indoor pool. All rooms have a seating area and TV: The private bathroom has a shower.

The modern hotel restaurant features large panoramic windows that look out towards the pool.

Swedish and international cuisine, from light dishes to 3 course dinners are offered.

A children’s menu is also available. Guests can relax in the bar or borrow bikes upon request and rent e-bikes to explore the surrounding area.
