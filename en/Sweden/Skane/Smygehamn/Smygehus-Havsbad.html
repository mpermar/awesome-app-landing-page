Description

Offering a seasonal outdoor pool and a children's playground, Smygehus Havsbad is situated in Smygehamn.

The hotel has a terrace and sauna, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

Every room is equipped with a flat-screen TV. Some rooms have a seating area where you can relax.

A terrace or patio are featured in certain rooms. The rooms are equipped with a private bathroom.

You can play tennis at this hotel, and the area is popular for golfing. The hotel also offers bike hire.

Malmö is 37 km from Smygehus Havsbad, while Lund is 41 km away. The nearest airport is Sturup Airport, 21 km from Smygehus Havsbad.
