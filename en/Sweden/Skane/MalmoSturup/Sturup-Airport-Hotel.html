Description

Just a few minutes' walk from Malmö Airport, this hotel is less than 900 metres from Sturup Park Golf Club.

It offers free Wi-Fi, an outdoor swimming pool and modern rooms. Cable TV, soundproofed windows and a private bathroom with a shower are standard in all Sturup Airport Hotel rooms.

Free use of a sauna, small fitness centre, along with a tennis court and billiards can be enjoyed on site.

Sturup Hotel also has a bar and restaurant that serves an early breakfast buffet, weekday lunch and dinner in the evenings.

Short-term private parking is free on site, while long-term parking can be arranged.
