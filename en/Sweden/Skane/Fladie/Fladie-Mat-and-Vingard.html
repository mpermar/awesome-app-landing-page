Description

Flädie Mat & Vingård is a vineyard located in Flädie, 32 km from Copenhagen and 14 km from Malmö.

In the summer, guests can stroll through the vineyard or take a dip in the outdoor pool.

Free private parking and free WiFi are available. The rooms feature picturesque details such as visible beams and sloping ceilings.

Some units have views of the pool or garden. All rooms include a private bathroom.

For your comfort, you will find bathrobes and a hairdryer. You will find a shared lounge at the property.

The hotel also offers bike hire and car hire. Helsingborg is 43 km from Flädie Mat & Vingård, while Lund is 8 km away.
