Description

Situated on Djurö Island of Stockholm’s archipelago, this hotel with spa offers free access to an indoor pool, sauna and gym.

It has an on-site restaurant, terrace and individually decorated rooms. Views of the surrounding nature can be enjoyed from each Djurönäset Hotell & Konferens room.

All include a desk and bathroom with a shower and hairdryer. Free use of bicycles and free wired internet access are provided on site.

During the summer, Djurönäset Hotell staff will help to arrange canoe and kayak rental, as well as sailing tours and historical seaside walks.

Within a 5-minute walk is Djurönäset Marina and beach with a jetty, while Fågelbro Golf Club is 9 km away.
