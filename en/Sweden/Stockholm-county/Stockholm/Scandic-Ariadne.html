Description

A flat-screen TV with satellite channels, as well as a desk are standard to each guest room at Scandic Ariadne. Some also have a seating area.

The Sky Bar overlooks the sea, while the Mistral restaurant features an outdoor terrace with harbor views. It serves an organic buffet breakfast, as well as a Swedish dinner menu with international influences.

All younger guests receive a welcome present. In addition, Ariadne has a children’s playroom with toys and films. Scandic’s mini-shop sells drinks, snacks and toiletries.

Gärdet Metro Station is about 10 minutes’ walk from Scandic Ariadne. The metro ride to Stockholm Central Station takes just 6 minutes.

Scandic Ariadne is certified as an environmentally friendly hotel.

Östermalm is a great choice for travelers interested in food, shopping and museums.
