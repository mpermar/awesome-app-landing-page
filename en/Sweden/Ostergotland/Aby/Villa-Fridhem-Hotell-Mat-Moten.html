Description

Overlooking Bråviken Bay, this eco-friendly hotel is within 20 minutes’ drive of Norrköping and Kolmården Wildlife Park.

Set in a 1909 manor house, it has a restaurant, 2 bars, relaxation area and a large park.

A TV, desk and seating area are standard facilities in Villa Fridhem’s rooms. Some rooms feature a furnished terrace.

The hotel restaurant serves various meals and drinks, home made pastries, and an extensive breakfast buffet.

Guest can benefit an indoor pool, separate saunas for men and women and relaxation area.

Private spa baths, massage and other treatments can be arranged at a surcharge The manor park has many green spaces, ideal for croquet, boule, football and other activities.

The surrounding area includes several running and hiking trails. Guest can borrow basic training equipment.

Indoor leisure options include darts, billiards, and a children's playroom. Ingelsta Golf Club is 15 minutes’ drive from Villa Fridhem Hotell & Konferens.

The Östgötaleden hiking trail runs right by the property, and Skiren Beach is 2 km away.
