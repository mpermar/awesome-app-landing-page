Description

This family-friendly farm stay is located 12 minutes’ drive from central Linköping.

It offers rooms with a seating area, flat-screen TV and free Wi-Fi access. The Göta Canal is 3 km away.

Rooms at Kullagården have either private or shared bathrooms, as well as access to a communal, fully equipped kitchen.

Some rooms have wooden floors. Children can enjoy pony riding on site and take part in the daily work on the farm.

Guests have access to a terrace, garden and 2 communal living rooms. Breakfast is served each morning at Kullagården.

The E4 Highway is an 8-minute drive away. The Bergs Slussar locks are 10 km from the farm.
