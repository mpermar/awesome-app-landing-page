Description

Peacefully located by Lake Siljan, this Rättvik property features a private beach and waterfront terrace with panoramic lake views in a non alcoholic environment.

It offers a daily breakfast buffet, along with free sauna and gym access. The bright guest rooms at Stiftsgården i Rättvik are decorated with light wooden furnishings.

Each includes a flat-screen TV, work desk and garden views. Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas of the property.

Stiftsgården’s restaurant serves buffets made with Swedish specialities and locally produced ingredients.

Lake views can be enjoyed while dining. In summer, the large garden is an ideal location to relax and enjoy a cup of coffee.

Leisure options at Stiftsgården i Rättvik include giant chess and table tennis. Guests wishing to explore the surroundings can rent a bicycle on site and visit the 15th-century Rättvik Church, just 150 metres away.
