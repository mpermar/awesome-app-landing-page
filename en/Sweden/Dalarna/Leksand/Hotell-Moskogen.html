Description

Located 2 km from Leksand’s city centre, amongst Dalarna’s green surroundings, this property offers cottage accommodation.

Access is free to the tennis court and on-site sauna, hot tub and outdoor pool. All cottages at Hotell Moskogen feature a furnished patio and private bathroom.

Each has a separate dining area and a kitchenette with a fridge and stove. Cable TV and free wired internet are also found in each cottage.

Moskogen’s restaurant serves traditional Swedish cuisine for lunch. It features themed buffets in the summer.

During warm weather, guests can have their meals on the large terrace. They can also use BBQ facilities for free.

Leksand Golf Club is 7 km away from Hotell Moskogen, while Leksands Sommarland Amusement and Water Park is 5 km away.
