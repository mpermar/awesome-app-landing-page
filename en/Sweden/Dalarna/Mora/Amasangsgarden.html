Description

This property, 3 km from the Mora’s town centre, is 150 metres from Lake Siljan. It offers cosy cottages with a private patio.

Both private parking and WiFi are free. Each cottage at Åmåsängsgården features a TV and kitchen, including a fridge and microwave.

All have a private toilet, while some have shared or private shower facilities. Åmåsängsgården’s restaurant serves a seasonal menu.

During warm months meals can be served on the terrace. BBQ facilities are also offered.

The on-site sauna is bookable. Other on-site activities include minigolf and a boules court.

A sandy beach, ideal for swimming, is 150 metres away from Åmåsängsgården. Gesundaberget Ski Resort is a 30-minute drive away.
