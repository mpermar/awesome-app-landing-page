Description

This hotel is on Älvdalen’s main street, Dalgatan. It offers a restaurant and air-conditioned rooms with a TV.

Guests can enjoy free Wi-Fi, sauna and hot tub access and free private parking. A private bathroom with shower is standard at Hotell Älvdalen.

The restaurant serves breakfast, lunch and dinner. A bar is also available. The Österdalälven River, 1 km from the hotel, is popular for fishing, and guests can buy fishing permits at reception.
