Description

Located in the peaceful Dalarna countryside village of Tällberg, this hotel offers fantastic views over Lake Siljan from its hilltop setting, 10 km from Leksand town centre.

Guests can enjoy free hot tub, sauna and pool access. The quiet, simple rooms at Dalecarlia Hotel & Spa have a TV and a private bathroom.

Free WiFi internet is available in public areas of the hotel. Dalecarlia Spa has a poolside bar and large windows offering panoramic lake views.

Active guests can try a water aerobics class or work out in the gym. Massages, beauty treatments and aromatherapy sessions can also be booked.

Dalecarlia’s restaurant serves traditional Swedish dishes and international cuisine made from fresh, seasonal ingredients.

The piano bar provides a relaxing place for an after-dinner drink. 10 km from Dalecarlia Hotel & Spa is Dalhalla, a former limestone quarry that is now home to a unique amphitheatre.
