Description

Located by Lake Vidöstern in the Småland countryside, this 19th-century manor offers classic Swedish and international food.

Wi-Fi is free, and all rooms have an LED TV and private bathroom. Toftaholm Herrgård’s bright rooms feature colourful décor and a seating area.

Relaxation options include a sauna, hot tub and terrace. Guests can rent boats and bicycles or play boules in the garden.

Toftaholm offers free private parking, just 5 minutes’ drive from the E4 road. It is situated midway between Värnamo and Ljungby.
