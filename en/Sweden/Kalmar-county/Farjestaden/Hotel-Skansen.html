Description

Hotel Skansen is located in central Färjestaden on the island of Öland. This historic, family-run hotel is 3 km away from Öland Bridge.

It offers the world's largest whiskey selection, a swimming pool and free public parking.

The modern guest rooms at Hotel Skansen feature free WiFi and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Some also have a seating area and work desk. The on-site restaurant serves Nordic specialities, often prepared with seasonal, local ingredients.

During the summer, Skansen Hotel’s large terrace is an ideal spot to enjoy a meal or a drink.

Leisure options include a bookable spa with a sauna, a hot tub and massage treatments.

Guests also have complimentary access to the fitness centre located next door to Hotel Skansen.

Hotel Skansen is 300 metres away from a beach along Kalmar Strait. Other area activities include golf, such as at Saxnäs Golf Club, a 10-minute drive away.
