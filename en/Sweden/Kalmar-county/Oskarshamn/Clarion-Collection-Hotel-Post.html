Description

Housed in an old post office, this central Oskarshamn hotel is located 900 metres from the Oskarshamn Ferry Terminal.

It offers free WiFi and free access to the top-floor sauna. The guest rooms in Clarion Collection Hotel Post’s main building have high ceilings and charming décor.

More modern guest rooms with a flat-screen TV can be found in the hotel’s wing. A free light evening buffet is served between 18:00 and 21:00 and an ecological breakfast are offered daily in the elegant dining room.

Free coffee, tea and fruit are available all day long at Clarion Collection Post.

Hotel staff can recommend area restaurants, shops and attractions, several within walking distance.
