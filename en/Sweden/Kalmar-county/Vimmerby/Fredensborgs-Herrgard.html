Description

This 1870s mansion is situated beside Tobo Golf Course, 15 minutes’ drive from Astrid Lindgren’s World.

Boat and canoe rentals are free for guests. Free WiFi is available. Fredensborgs Herrgård’s bright, simple rooms have wooden floors, cable TV and a seating area.

Relaxation options include a lakeside sauna, a hot tub and a terrace. More active types might try minigolf, tennis or billiards.

An on-site playground and playroom are available for children. Fredensborgs Herrgård Hotel’s restaurant uses fresh, seasonal ingredients and lots of locally sourced products.
