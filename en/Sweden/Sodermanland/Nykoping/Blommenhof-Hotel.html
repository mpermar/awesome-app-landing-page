Description

Located only 5 km from Skavsta Airport, Blommenhof Hotel offers a range of relaxation options and Swedish-style rooms with free WiFi.

Nyköping city centre is a 7-minute drive away. All rooms at Blommenhof Hotel include a work desk and cable TV.

Some rooms include a bath. Swedish dishes with contemporary international influences are served at the in-house restaurant, overlooking the garden.

A buffet breakfast is offered every morning. Drinks are available in the cosy hotel bar.

On-site relaxation options include a sauna and an indoor pool. Guests wishing to explore the surroundings can borrow bikes from the hotel reception.

Nyköping Central Station is a 5-minute drive away. Ärila Golf Club is located 8. 5 km from this manor-style hotel.
