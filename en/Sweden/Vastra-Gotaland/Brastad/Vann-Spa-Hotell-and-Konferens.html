Description

Surrounded by nature, this eco-friendly hotel is situated on the Gullmar Fjord. It provides free Wi-Fi, a private beach and large spa.

Uddevalla and Lysekil are within 30 minutes’ drive. Guest rooms at the Vann Spa Hotell & Konferens feature modern décor and Nordic design.

Each room has satellite TV and a seating area. The spa has 6 pools and 3 varieties of saunas.

A relaxation room, a large selection of exercise equipment and treatments are also available.

On site facilities include a seasonal outdoor pool, mini golf, tennis, along with bicycle rental.

Vann's light-filled restaurant offers scenic fjord views and uses locally produced ingredients.

The hotel bakery supplies fresh bread and pastries daily. Guests can enjoy their meals on the large terrace during warm weather.
