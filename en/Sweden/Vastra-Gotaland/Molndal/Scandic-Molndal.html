Description

Situated less than 5 minutes’ walk from Mölndal Centrum Tram Stop, guests can reach Gothenburg’s city centre in 15 minutes by car or tram.

This hotel offers free WiFi. Classic Scandinavian design with wooden floors and floor-to-ceiling windows are featured in each Scandic Mölndal guest room.

Each bright room also has a TV, arm chair and work desk. The glass atrium provides a light and spacious lobby and dining area.

Seasonal Swedish cuisine is served in Mölndal Scandic’s contemporary restaurant. Guests can enjoy a stylish bar indoors and an attractive terrace outdoors.

Leisure facilities include free access to a gym and relaxation area with a sauna and hot tub.

An indoor playroom has toys and games for children. Hotel staff will gladly recommend area activities and attractions, such as Gothenburg’s beautiful archipelago.
