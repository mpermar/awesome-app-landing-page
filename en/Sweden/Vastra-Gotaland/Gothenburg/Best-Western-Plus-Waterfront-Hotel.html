Description

Situated in a former brewery at the entrance of Gothenburg Harbour, this hotel offers free WiFi, rooms with a flat-screen TV and in front of the hotel you can find an outdoor parking available at a small fee.

Gothenburg city centre is located 4 km away. All the brightly decorated rooms at Hotel Waterfront Göteborg have a seating area and in-room tea and coffee facilities.

Some rooms offer views of the Göta River. International dishes are available at Waterfront Restaurant, featuring magnificent views of the river and the Älvsborgs Brigde.

A wide selection of chilled beers and drinks can be enjoyed in the hotel bar. Guests can work out with exercise machines in the on-site InBalance mini gym.

Relaxation options include a sauna. Bicycles are available for rent at the hotel.

A bus stop with connections to central Gothenburg is 100 metres away. Slottsparken Park is located 2 km from Best Western Plus Hotel Waterfront Göteborg.
