Description

Located on the grounds of Nordens Ark Zoo, this hotel is 150 metres from the Bohuslän archipelago’s Åby Fjord.

It provides free Wi-Fi and free admission to the zoo during the stay. Each modern and bright room at Nordens Ark Hotell has a TV, electric kettle and bathroom with a shower and hairdryer.

Traditional Swedish cuisine can be enjoyed at the in-house restaurant which serves a breakfast buffet and lunch meals.

Nordens Ark has a café and restaurant with varied opening hours, as well as a souvenir shop and children’s playground.

Central Uddevalla is 55 km away, while a local bus stops just in front of the property.
