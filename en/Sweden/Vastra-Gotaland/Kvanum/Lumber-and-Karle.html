Description

This hotel lies in Kvänum Village, 25 km south of Lidköping and Lake Vänern. It has a bowling alley and a small gym and spa free for guests.

Free Wi-Fi and sauna access are also included. Each guest room at Lumber & Karle includes a seating area, a minibar and cable TV.

All rooms have private bathrooms with showers. Lumber & Karle’s facilities include a restaurant and a bar.

Meals can be taken out onto the terrace. Free private parking spaces are found next to the hotel.

The bowling alley has a shop and a café bar. The area around Lumber & Karle offers activities such as hiking, fishing and hang gliding.
