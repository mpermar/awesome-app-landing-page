Description

Situated along the Viskan River, this hotel is a 5-minute walk from Borås Central Station.

Quality Hotel Grand features an on-site restaurant with international cuisine, a popular nightclub and a cocktail bar.

Free WiFi is available. All rooms at Quality Hotel Grand, Borås have cable TV, seating area and desk.

All have views over Borås’s centre or Stadsparken, the city’s park. Some rooms feature a minibar.

A nearby gym offers discounted rates for Quality Hotel's guests. A selection of snacks, soft drinks and light meals can be purchased around the clock in the lobby.

On Saturdays, a tasty brunch is offered in the restaurant. The popular Borås Zoo is 2.

6 km from Quality Hotel Grand, Borås, while Navet Science Centre is only 3 minutes’ drive away.
