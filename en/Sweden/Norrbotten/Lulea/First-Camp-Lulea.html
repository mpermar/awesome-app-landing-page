Description

Beautifully located in Karlsvik, across the river from central Luleå, this comprehensive year-round camping and vacation village offers comfortable, fully equipped cabins with private parking and wired internet.

First Camp Luleå is surrounded by a unique, beautiful landscape of tall pines, bushes and inviting sand beaches.

The cabins have high-standard furnishings and décor in a contemporary Scandinavian style.

Prepare your own meals in the cabin’s fully modern kitchen. Buy groceries, snacks and other supplies at the convenience store by the reception.

During high season, the store offers freshly baked bread each morning. Guests at First Camp Luleå enjoy discounted rates at the nearby Arcus Bath, a large, popular outdoor water park, which is owned and run by First Camp.

Arcus Bath is open during the summer season. The city centre and all its attractions are only 10-15 minutes’ drive away.
