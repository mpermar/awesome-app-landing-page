Description

This 1908 property is set in Mullsjö, 20 minutes’ drive from Jönköping. It offers free hot tub and sauna access, Segway rentals and a lounge with fireplace.

WiFi and parking are free. Flat-screen TVs, private bathrooms and minibars are standard at Hotell Björkhaga.

The décor is bright and modern, with wooden floors in all rooms. Dining options include lunch buffets and set evening menus with Swedish cuisine.

Guests can enjoy a game of darts in the evening. Staff can book canoe rentals, boat trips, fishing licences, as well as suggest nearby walking trails.

The nearest fishing lake, Stråken, is 2 km away. Mullsjö Alpine Ski Center is within 15 minutes' drive.
