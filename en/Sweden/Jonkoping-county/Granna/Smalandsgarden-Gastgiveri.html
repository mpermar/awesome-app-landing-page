Description

This property lies by Lake Örensjön, 8 km from Gränna and Lake Vättern. Guests can enjoy free WiFi and free private parking.

Jönköping is 40 km away. Brahehus Fortress is 15 km away. Guest rooms are spread over several buildings, each with different decor and furnishings.

All rooms have a TV and a private bathroom with a shower. Some feature lake views.

Dinner can be enjoyed with a view of Lake Ören at the hotel restaurant. Free coffee is served in the afternoon.

Smålandsgården Gästgiveri has a rural setting, with lakes, forests and hiking trails found nearby.

Guests even have access to their own private beach during their stay at Smålandsgården.
