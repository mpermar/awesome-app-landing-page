Description

This hotel is found in a charming countryside setting by Lake Fryken, 7. 5 km from Sunne train station.

Guests can enjoy lake views and sauna sessions. All guest rooms at Hotel Frykenstrand have a TV, desk and a bathroom with a shower.

A communal living room is available for guests’ use, and there is a sauna by the water's edge.

Guests can admire views of Lake Fryken while dining at Frykenstrand’s restaurant.
