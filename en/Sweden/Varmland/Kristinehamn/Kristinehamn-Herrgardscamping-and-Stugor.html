Description

Set in Kristinehamn in the Värmland Region, 43 km from Karlstad, Kristinehamn Manor Camping & Cottages boasts a private beach area by Lake Vänern.

Karlskoga is 26 km from the property. Some units are air conditioned and include a seating and/or dining area.

There is also a kitchen in some of the units, equipped with a dishwasher. Kristinehamn Herrgårdscamping also includes 2 children's playgrounds.

During summer, guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant Gretas Café & Restaurang.

The property also offers mini-market. The property has water sports facilities and bike hire is available.

You can play tennis and mini-golf at the property, and the area is popular for cycling.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including fishing and canoeing.

The nearest airport is Orebro Airport, 56 km from Kristinehamn Herrgårdscamping & Stugor.
