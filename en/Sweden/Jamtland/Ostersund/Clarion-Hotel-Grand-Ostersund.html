Description

Centrally located in Östersund, this hotel is 350 metres from the Östersund Västra Train Station.

It offers free WiFi, free pool and sauna access and rooms with modern décor. A flat-screen TV, work desk and seating area are standard at Clarion Hotel Grand Östersund.

Kitchen & Table is a restaurant concept by renowned Swedish chef Marcus Samuelsson.

It combines the best of local, Scandinavian ingredients with Manhattan’s unique mix of international flavours.

The Lobby Bar serves cocktails, wines, locally brewed beers and a variety of snacks.

Active guests can take advantage of the hotel’s fitness centre and on-site bicycle rentals.

Clarion staff can recommend several restaurants, cafés and shops within walking distance.

The hotel is right around the corner from Östersund’s Stortorget square. Lake Storsjön is only 400 metres away.
