Description

The rooms in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. The rooms come with a private bathroom with a shower and a hair dryer. Guest rooms have a desk.

Guests at Lannalodge can enjoy a buffet breakfast. There's an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of Italian dishes.

Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Vintrosa, like skiing, cycling and hiking.

Örebro is 11 miles from Lannalodge. Orebro Airport is 5 miles away.
