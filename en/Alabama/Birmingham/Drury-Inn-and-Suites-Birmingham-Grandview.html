Description

A flat-screen cable TV, microwave, and small refrigerator are featured in all rooms. A sitting area with a sofa bed is included in select suites at this Southeast Birmingham Drury Inn and Suites.

The 24-hour fitness center allows guests to keep their own fitness schedule. A hot tub is on site. A laundromat and same-day dry cleaning services are available as well at Drury Inn and Suites Birmingham Southeast.

A rotating menu of items like macaroni and cheese, hot dogs, and broccoli cheddar soup are available during a nightly reception. Beer, wine, mixed drinks, and soft drinks are also available.

Birmingham International Airport is 13 miles away. The property is also 5 miles from Samford University and 15 miles from Oak Mountain State Park.
