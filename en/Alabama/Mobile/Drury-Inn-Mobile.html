Description

A refrigerator, microwave and coffee-making facilities are included in each room at the Mobile Drury Inn. A flat-screen TV and ironing facilities are included as well.

A fitness center and business center are available for guest use at Drury Inn Mobile. Laundry facilities are also included. Hot food and beverages are provided nightly.

Downtown Mobile is a 14-minute drive from this hotel. Guests at the property are 3 miles from Springhill College and 4 miles from the University of South Alabama.
