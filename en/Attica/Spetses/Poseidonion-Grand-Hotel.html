Description

Allocated in its originally built and newly added wing, guest rooms are luxuriously decorated with elegant furnishings and wooden floors. They feature a flat-screen satellite TV, mini-bar and safe. Their balconies offer sea or garden views.

At the swimming pool of Poseidonion Grand, surrounded by palm trees, guests can find sun beds and umbrellas, which they can use free of charge. Asian Spa therapists offer their rejuvenating services at the hotel’s spa.

On the a la carte Veranda Restaurant, guests may enjoy Greek fusion cuisine made with fresh ingredients from the hotel’s biological garden. Library Brasserie serves contemporary Greek and European a la carte cuisine, while Poseidonion Bar prepares light meals and cocktails.

Conveniently located in Dapia, the port of Spetses, Poseidonion Grand Hotel is within proximity to many bars, restaurants and shops. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel.
