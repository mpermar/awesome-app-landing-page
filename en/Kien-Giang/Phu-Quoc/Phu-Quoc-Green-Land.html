Description

All guest rooms has air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a electric tea pot, a bidet, free toiletries and a desk. The rooms are equipped with a private bathroom, while some rooms are equipped with a kitchen. Guest rooms feature a closet.

A buffet breakfast is available every morning at the resort. The restaurant at Phu Quoc Green Land specializes in European and Vietnamese cuisine.

At the accommodation guests are welcome to use a fitness room. Guests at Phu Quoc Green Land will be able to enjoy activities in and around Phu Quoc, like cycling.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can give tips on the area.

Fish Sauce Factory is 2.5 miles from the resort. The nearest airport is Phu Quoc International Airport, 7 miles from the property.

Ong Lang is a great choice for travelers interested in tropical weather, swimming pools and traditional food.
