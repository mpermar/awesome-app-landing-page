Description

Chen Sea Resort & Spa Phu Quoc's design is inspired by the calm environment. All bungalows and villas enjoy a calm setting and include verandas with sea views. All units feature air conditioning, a wardrobe and dressing room. A seating area, personal safe and desk are included. Private bathroom is equipped with a bath/shower facility, free toiletries and slippers.

Chen Sea Resort’s spa center offers a mixture of traditional and modern treatments to relax and revitalize. Guests can enjoy different water sports activities such as sailing, windsurfing, kayak rides and beach volleyball.

Fish Sauce Factory is 2.7 mi from Chen Sea Resort & Spa Phu Quoc, while Phu Quoc Night Market is 3.2 mi away. The nearest airport is Phu Quoc International Airport, a 35-minute drive away.

Il Ristorante restaurant is perfectly located on the sea front and serves Mediterranean specialties and local as well as international cuisine.

Ong Lang is a great choice for travelers interested in tropical weather, swimming pools and traditional food.

Experiences

01/04/2018

Excellent. Would love to return!

"The property is exceptional. We booked a beachfront apartment which is spacious, has outdoor rain shower and tropical bath on the patio where you can take a bath while observing sea. I loved the bath toiletries provided by property - Thann cosmetics got beautiful scent and felt luxurious on the skin. The room is clean, the bed is huge and comfortable, the view from the window is straight to the sea. We have been given fresh fruits everyday, at night the anti-mosquito candles are lighten up in the bedroom. The surrounding area is beautiful - the beach is sandy and clean, feel private and peaceful, perfect for couples and families. The infinity pool looked great, we did not use it though. The food in restaurant is good, lot of choice (western and local) and I like that it was mostly homemade - the jam, the honey, the juices etc The staff is excellent. Very professional, thoughtful and proactive. They did us a favor and allowed us to check-in early and checkout late which was very appreciated. The resort is about 30 min drive from airport, it is pretty much secluded which was a plus for us. In order to eat outside of resort, you need to take 15-20 min taxi to the centre or rent a bike/motorbike and explore area yourself. Either way, it's worth it!"
