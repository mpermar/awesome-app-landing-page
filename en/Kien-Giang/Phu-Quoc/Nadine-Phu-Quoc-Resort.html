Description

All rooms in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Rooms are equipped with a electric tea pot, while some rooms also offer a terrace and others also offer garden views. Guest rooms have a desk.

Nadine Phu Quoc Resort offers a continental or buffet breakfast. Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of European, Vietnamese and Asian dishes and also offers vegan and vegetarian options.

The accommodation has a playground. Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near Nadine Phu Quoc Resort.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can give tips on the area.

Phu Quoc Pearl Farm is 2.1 miles from the resort, while Phu Quoc Night Market is 2.1 miles away. Phu Quoc International Airport is 1.9 miles from the property.
