Description

Spacious air-conditioned rooms at Resort Phu Quoc feature classic wood furnishings and private balconies with sweeping views of the sea. Surrounded by tropical greenery, rooms come equipped with flat-screen cable TVs and an electric teakettle.

Located along the west coast, Resort Phu Quoc Saigon is a 10 minute drive from Phu Quoc Airport, which is a 50 minute domestic flight from Ho Chi Minh City and a 2 hour domestic flight from Hanoi. The resort is a 20 minute drive from Bai Vong Ferry Terminal.

Relaxing massages and body treatments await guests at Phu Quoc’s spa. For leisure, water sports like windsurfing, canoeing and diving are available. The resort also provides karaoke and barbecue facilities. Free parking is available.

Daily buffet breakfast is served at Saigon Restaurant, while The Spices Garden offers fresh seafood dishes. Light refreshments can be enjoyed at the Pool Bar and Sunset Bar.

Duong Dong is a great choice for travelers interested in food, local food and beach walks.
