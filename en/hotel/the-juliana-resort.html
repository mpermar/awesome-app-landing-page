Description

Each accommodations at The Juliana Resort features free Wi-Fi access, a TV, and a refrigerator. Select cottages and suites include a kitchen or a sofa bed. They are decorated in rich colors and wood furnishings.

Guests can enjoy the on-site games room with billiards. Fishing and free kayaks are available at the Juliana Resort. Barbecue facilities and a childrens playground are also offered.

Six Flags Great Escape & Splashwater Kingdom Amusement Park is 15 minutes drive from the property. Hiking at the Adirondack Mountain Club is 8 miles away.
