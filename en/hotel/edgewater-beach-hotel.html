Description

All rooms have a kitchenette, balcony, and sitting area with a flat-screen TV. Some Edgewater Beach Hotel rooms include a water or pool view. Extras include an iPod dock, sitting area, and CD player.

When not diving into the Gulf of Mexico, guests can use the fitness center or buy essentials in the on-site shops. This Florida Edgewater Beach Hotel provides free parking as well.

Coast offers global cuisine with beachfront views. Indoor and outdoor seating is available during breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

The property is 1 mile from Naples Zoo at Caribbean Gardens and 4 miles from the Naples Museum of Art. Collier-Seminole State Park is 20 miles away.
