Description

Guests can enjoy complimentary use of bicycles, kayaks, and canoes on site. A luxurious day spa with massage and body treatment services, and a state-of-the-art fitness center, complete with cardiovascular machines, weights, and personal fitness coaches are available at WaterColor Resort.

A balcony and a living area with a sofa bed, flat-screen cable TV, and DVD player are featured in the spacious guest rooms. A refrigerator is provided as well as an private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer. Extras include a desk and ironing facilities.

When away from the resort, visit Deer Lake State Park, a pristine conservation area located 4.7 mi from the WaterColor Inn, and explore the pathways that showcase several species of indigenous plants. Access to the private Sharks Tooth and Camp Creek Golf Clubs is granted for this resort's guests.
