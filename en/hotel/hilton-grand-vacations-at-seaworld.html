Description

Each room is fitted with a flat-screen TV and DVD player. Some accommodations feature a sitting area where you can relax. A balcony or patio are featured in certain rooms. Every room includes a private bathroom. Extras include free toiletries and a hairdryer.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property.

You can play tennis at the resort. Ripley's Believe it or Not! is 2.7 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Orlando International Airport, 10 miles from the property.
