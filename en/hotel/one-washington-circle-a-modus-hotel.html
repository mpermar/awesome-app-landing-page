Description

Each suite at One Washington Circle features a balcony with views of the city, a flat-screen TV and bathrobes.

An on-site fitness centre and seasonal outdoor pool are part of this Washington, D.C. hotel. Concierge services and valet garage parking are available.

The Circle Bistro, located in the hotel, serves French cuisine and boasts an outdoor patio. It has a wine list and full-service bar.

The Washington National Monument in the National Mall is 1.4 miles from One Washington Circle. The campus of George Washington University is 1,500 feet from the hotel.

Northwest is a great choice for travelers interested in city walks, culture and walking.
