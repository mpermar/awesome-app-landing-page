Description

Kitchens are equipped with dishwashers and dining areas in the Lighthouse Oceanfront Resort apartments. The units also feature a nautical décor and large windows.

Guests can play on indoor tennis court or swim in the indoor pool. The Lighthouse Resort also has a state-of-the-art fitness center and hot tub.

The Lighthouse Oceanfront Resort is 3 miles from the World Kite Museum & Hall-Fame. Cape Disappointment State Park is within a 20-minute drive of the property.
