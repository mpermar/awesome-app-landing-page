Description

A variety of recreational facilities are available on-site at the Crowne Plaza Hotel Lake Placid-Golf Club, including tennis courts, a putting green and an indoor pool. Guests will also appreciate free wireless internet access and a fitness center.

While staying at the Lake Placid Crowne Plaza, guests can enjoy on-site dining at MacKenzie's Restaurant, which serves Italian and American cuisine for breakfast and dinner.

Olympic Center, Whiteface Mountain and the Adirondack Mountains are only moments from the pet-friendly Lake Placid-Golf Club Crowne Plaza. The Olympic Training Center as well as shopping centres and local restaurants are also nearby.
