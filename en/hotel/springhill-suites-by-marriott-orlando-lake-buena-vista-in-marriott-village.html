Description

A mini-refrigerator and a microwave are provided in every guest suite at the Lake Buena Vista SpringHill Suites. A cable TV and separate sitting areas are also included in each spacious suite.

SpringHill Suites Orlando Lake Buena Vista in Marriott Village features an outdoor pool with a children's play area and hot tub. A fitness center and food court serving ice cream, pizza and sandwiches are also available on site.

Universal Studios (9.9 mi) and SeaWorld (4.3 mi) are within a short drive of the Orlando SpringHill Suites. The Orange County Convention Center is also 6.8 mi away.

Lake Buena Vista is a great choice for travelers interested in Disneyland, family-friendly trips and theme parks.
