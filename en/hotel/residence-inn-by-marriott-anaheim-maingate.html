Description

A full kitchen and dining area are featured in all rooms at the Residence Inn Anaheim Maingate. Free WiFi and satellite TV is included. Select suites boast a fireplace. Free parking and concierge services are included at this Residence Inn.

The hot breakfast buffet offered at Maingate Anaheim Residence Inn includes fresh fruits, pastries, waffles, eggs and breakfast meats. Guests can enjoy free grocery shopping services and a BBQ area.

Anaheim Resort Transit shuttle tickets can be purchased at the front desk. A shuttle service is available to drop guests off at Disneyland Park.

The Anaheim Convention Center is 5 minutes’ drive from the hotel. Knott’s Berry Farms is 15 minutes’ drive away.
