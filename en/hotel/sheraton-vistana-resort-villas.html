Description

Each villa provides separate sleeping and living areas at the Sheraton Vistana Resort Villas. They include a dining area, washer/dryer and are decorated in a casual Florida style. Guests can also relax and watch cable TV with pay-per-view movies.

The resort includes 3 game rooms, 9 lit tennis courts and shuffleboard courts. Guests can also play basketball or volleyball. An 18-hole miniature golf course is also on site.

Casual dining with traditional American meals can be found at Zimmie’s or Cascades Bar and Grill. Vistana Resort Villas Sheraton also includes the Market Place where light snacks and sandwiches are available.

SeaWorld Orlando is 10 minutes' drive from the resort. The resort is located off Interstate 4 and is 16 mi from Orlando International Airport.

Lake Buena Vista is a great choice for travelers interested in Disneyland, family-friendly trips and theme parks.
