Description

The spacious guest suites provide a sitting area with a sofa bed and 42-inch flat-screen cable TV. They also have CD players and safes. A separate dining area is also provided. Wi-Fi is available.

A 100% non-smoking facility, the Platinum Hotel and Spa provides a gym, business center and meeting rooms. The hotel features an on-site bar and restaurant that serves American cuisine. Spa services include massages and a sauna.

The centrally-located Platinum Hotel is less than one mile from The University of Nevada, Las Vegas. It is just 3 miles from McCarran International Airport.

Las Vegas Strip is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, nightlife and entertainment.
