Description

Guests are welcomed with a gourmet snack basket, fruit bowl, fresh roses, and a complimentary "wine grotto" minibar with Bernardus wines in their rooms. A flat-screen cable TV, iPod docking station and coffee machine are also provided.

Lucia Restaurant & Bar boasts an extensive wine list and a menu that focuses on organic, local ingredients. The bar and lounge area provides a more casual menu and experience.

The Spa at Bernardus includes marine-inspired massage services and body treatments in a spacious facility that features locker rooms, sauna rooms, a fitness room and a juice bar. A warming room with fireplace, a meditation garden, a dipping pool and a reflecting pool are available for guest relaxation. A full-service beauty salon is also available.

Two tennis courts are on site. A croquet lawn and bocce court are provided for guest use.

Garland Ranch Regional Park is just under 1 miles away. The Carmel Mission Basilica and Museum are 9.5 miles away.
