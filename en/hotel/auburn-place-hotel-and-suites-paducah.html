Description

A microwave, fridge and coffee maker are provided in every room at Auburn Place Hotels and Suites. The simply styled rooms include cable TV with pay-per-view movies and a work desk.

The Auburn Place Hotels offers fitness and business facilities on site for guest convenience. Guest laundry services are also available.

The National Quilt Museum is a 15-minute drive from this hotel. Harrah’s Riverboat Casino is 9.4 miles away.
