Description

A flat-screen cable TV, a sitting area and a private balcony with views are provided in each luxurious suite at Montage Kapalua Bay. The private bathrooms include a relaxing spa tub and guest slippers. For convenience, a washing machine and tumble dryer are included in each suite.

Guests at Montage Kapalua Bay can work out in the fitness center, enjoy a game of tennis or rent bicycles at the property. Other facilities include an entertainment staff, grocery deliveries and meeting facilities. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the area, including golf, hiking and windsurfing.

For the golf enthusiast, Kapalua Golf Club Bay Course is just a 7-minute walk away, and the state-of-the-art Kapalua Golf Academy is just under 1 miles away. Kaanapali Beach is 5.5 miles away. Kahului Airport is 17 miles away.
