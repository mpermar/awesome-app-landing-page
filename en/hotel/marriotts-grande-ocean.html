Description

Each accommodations features a fully equipped kitchen, complete with an oven, stove top, and a dishwasher.. Flat-screen TVs, separate dining areas, and private balconies are also available in rooms at Marriott’s Grande Ocean hotel.

A spa, fitness center and business center are all available on-site at Marriott’s Grande Ocean. Tennis and golf facilities are also offered.

The Marketplace Deli, located on-site, is open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. The seasonal pool bar is open for lunch while The Dolphin Grille offers casual dining poolside.

Hilton Head Island Airport is 10 miles from this hotel. Guests will also be 21 minutes’ drive from Pickney Island National Wildlife Refuge.
