Description

The Greensprings Vacation Resort boasts a state-of-the-art fitness center and outdoor swimming pool. Guests will also appreciate fully-equipped kitchens, warm fireplaces and washing machines and dryers.

Only a short distance from the Greensprings are many of the city's historic sites and attractions, including the Green Springs National Historic Landmark District. Top-rated Williamsburg National Golf Course is also nearby.
