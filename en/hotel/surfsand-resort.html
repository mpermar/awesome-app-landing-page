Description

Rooms at Surfsand have contemporary themes featuring iPod docking stations and free Wi-Fi. A TV with DVD player and free access to a movie library is also provided. Some rooms include a private patio or balcony as well as a soaking tub.

Guests may enjoy the heated indoor swimming pool and hot tub. An on-site fitness center includes workout equipment and saunas. Surfsand Kids club allows children to experience a number of crafts and activities.

The Wayfarer Restaurant & Lounge, connected to the resort, offers fresh seafood dishes daily.

Surfsand Resort offers a driver to surrounding attractions. Haystack Rock is less than 1 miles down the beach. The Columbia Maritime Museum is 25 miles away.
