Description

Villas feature seating areas with fireplaces and private decks overlooking the surrounding forest at the Mirror Lake and Tamarack. The villas have country décor and large windows.

Guests can swim in the seasonal outdoor pool or the indoor pool. The property also has a volleyball and tennis court.

The Mirror Lake and Tamarack is within 10-minute drive of the Kalahari Waterpark. Ripley’s Believe It or Not Museum is 5 miles from the property.
