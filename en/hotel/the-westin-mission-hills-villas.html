Description

The full-service, all-villa Westin Mission Hills Villa at Rancho Mirage, California, combines the beauty of the area with pampering to elevate your senses and refresh you spirit. Enjoy the beauty, the extraordinary service, the sense of belonging and the rewards of resort living. All that you need has been assembled in gracious villa accommodations and services that far exceed the limits of even the most generous hotel room.

Two 18-hole signature championship golf courses beckon and challenge you to tee-up. The desert offers bike riding, horseback riding and the subtle shadings of Joshua Tree National Park.
