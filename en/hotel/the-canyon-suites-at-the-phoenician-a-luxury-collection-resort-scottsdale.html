Description

All rooms come with free Wi-Fi, a terrace and a sitting area with a 42-inch flat-screen cable TV at Canyon Suites at The Phoenician Scottsdale. A private bathroom with a hairdryer and bathrobes is also provided.

Il Terrazzo serves Italian and American cuisine daily for breakfast, lunch and dinner at Scottsdale Canyon Suites at The Phoenician. J&G Steakhouse serves dinner each evening. Guests can enjoy casual dining for lunch and dinner at Relish Burger Bistro. Lunch and cocktails are served poolside at Canyon Grill.

Message therapies and spa treatments are available on site. A year-round outdoor swimming pool and a tennis court is provided.

Old Town Scottsdale is a 5-minute drive from the resort. Fashion Square Mall is 1 miles from Canyon Suites at The Phoenician Scottsdale.
