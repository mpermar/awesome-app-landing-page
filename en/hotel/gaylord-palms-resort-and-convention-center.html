Description

Guest rooms at the Gaylord Palms Resort and Convention Center provide coffee facilities and a refrigerator stocked daily with bottled water. Each includes cable TV, a CD player and free Wi-Fi. Some rooms are located just steps from the convention center.

The Gaylord Palms offers live nightly entertainment, a waterslide and separate children's and adults swimming pools. The hotel also features a sand beach play area, waterfalls and on-site boutique shops.

Fine cuisine is available at the Old Hickory Steakhouse and casual meals at Villa del Flora.

Located off Interstate 4, the Gaylord Palms provides easy access to many of the area's top sites, including SeaWorld, 6.6 mi away. Universal Studios is 11 mi away, and Orlando International Airport is within 20 minutes' drive.

Celebration is a great choice for travelers interested in Disneyland, tourist attractions and parks.
