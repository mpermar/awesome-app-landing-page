Description

A microwave, refrigerator and stove come standard in each room at Island Links Resort. Guests also receive access to cable TV and DVD player.

Bike trails are on site at Island Links Resort. Guests also have access to the resort clubhouse which includes shuffle board facilities and a hot tub.

Adventure Cove is just a 2-minute drive from Island Links Resort. Guests at the property are 3.4 miles from the Arts Center of Coastal Carolina and a 9-minute drive from Pickney Island National Wildlife Refuge.
