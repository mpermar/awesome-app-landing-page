Description

The spacious one to two-bedroom suites provide dining areas and free Wi-Fi. The seating rooms are equipped with cable TVs, DVD players and sofa beds.

Blue Tree Resort offers guests a fitness center and miniature golf course along with a games room. Additional on-site recreational activities include beach volleyball, tennis and basketball. The resort provides free shuttle service to Walt Disney World, Universal Studios, and SeaWorld. A business center and convenience store are part of the facilities and the front desk is open 24 hours a day. Attraction tickets and packages are available from the concierge staff.

SeaWorld is 3 mi from Blue Tree Resort. The resort is 7 mi from Universal Studios Orlando and 15 mi from Orlando International Airport.

Lake Buena Vista is a great choice for travelers interested in Disneyland, family-friendly trips and theme parks.
