Description

The accommodation includes a TV and air conditioning. There is also a refrigerator. Private bathroom also comes with a bathtub or shower. Extras include a desk.

At Scotty's Lakeside Resort there is a 24-hour front desk, a bar and a snack bar. Other facilities offered at the property include a vending machine. The property provides free parking.

If you feel like exploring the surrounding area, check out Fort William Henry and Lake George, just under 1 miles away. This resort is 47 miles from Albany International Airport.
