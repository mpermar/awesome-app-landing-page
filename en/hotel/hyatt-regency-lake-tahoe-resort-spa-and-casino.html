Description

Each air-conditioned room has a flat-screen cable TV with pay-per-view channels, an iPod docking station and coffee-making facilities at Hyatt Regency Lake Tahoe Resort, Spa & Casino. A private bathroom with a hairdryer is provided.

A year-round outdoor heated pool and 2 hot tubs are available at Hyatt Regency Resort, Spa & Casino Lake Tahoe. A spa and a fitness center are also featured.

Lone Eagle Grille serves classic American cuisine daily, Tahoe Provisions has a gourmet deli and a coffee bar, Sierra Café provides an American-style buffet and Cutthroats’ Saloon serves dinner daily and lunch and dinner on Saturday and Sunday. Guests can enjoy cocktails in 6 different bars on site.

Incline Golf Course is a 5-minute drive from Hyatt Regency Lake Tahoe Resort, Spa & Casino. Diamond Peak Ski Resort is 2 miles from the resort.
