Description

The accommodations at this resort will provide you with a private bathroom with a bathtub or shower, TV and air conditioning. Some rooms have a mountain view.

At the Mountain View Grand Resort & Spa, there are meeting facilities, a game room and a kids' club. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including hiking.

The resort is 8 miles from Santa's Village, 23 miles from Cannon mountain and 21 miles from the Mt. Washington Cog Railroad.
