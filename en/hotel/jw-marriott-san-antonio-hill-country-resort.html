Description

JW Marriott San Antonio Hill Country Resort rooms include a flat-screen TV and refrigerator. The rooms are equipped with a coffee maker. Wi-Fi is available.

Guests at the JW Marriott San Antonio Resort can dine in one of 7 restaurants, which serve American and Southwestern food. The hotel features a Starbucks and the Crooked Branch Bar, which serves cocktails and Tex-Mex food.

JW Marriott guests can enjoy the on-site water park featuring water slides and a lazy river. Adults can relax at the spa, which features a private swimming pool. The children's club features daily activities and child care.

This San Antonio Hill Country JW Marriott Resort is 19 mi from Six Flags Fiesta Texas amusement park.
