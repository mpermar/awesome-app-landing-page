Description

A balcony or patio offers views from each lakehouse-styled room of The Abbey Resort. A flat-screen TV, coffee maker and fridge are also included.

The Abbey Resort offers a variety of dining options. Overlooking the harbor, 240° West serves steaks, seafood and small plates with complimenting wine pairings. The Waterfront serves appetizers, sandwiches and BBQ along with a variety of beer and cocktails. Café Latte, Bar West and the Gazebo Grill are also open for dining.

Guests of Abbey Resort can enjoy an adults-only pool facility, an arcade and a state-of-the-art fitness center. Fireside s’mores are made outside at any time of the year. Ice skating is offered to guests in the winter.

The Geneva Lake Museum is 20 minutes from the resort. The Black Point Historic Preserve is 6 miles away.
