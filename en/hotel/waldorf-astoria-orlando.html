Description

A 42-inch flat-screen TV comes standard in each room at the Orlando Waldorf Astoria. Other amenities include a marble soaking tub, separate shower, and free toiletries. A work desk and safe are included.

Waldorf Astoria Orlando features a kids' club, 2 swimming pools, a lazy river, and private cabanas. The Waldorf Astoria Spa offers massages, facials, and salon services. The on-site golf club features 18 custom-designed holes.

Dining is available at 12 on-site restaurants, and the hotel has in-room dining. Options include a sushi lounge and an award-winning steakhouse.

Universal Studios and SeaWorld theme parks are within a 20-minute drive of the Waldorf Astoria in Orlando. Orlando Premium Outlets are 7 miles away, and the Orange County Convention Center is 10.5 miles away.

Lake Buena Vista is a great choice for travelers interested in Disneyland, family-friendly trips and theme parks.
