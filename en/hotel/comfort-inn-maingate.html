Description

Rooms at the Comfort Inn Maingate offer free Wi-Fi, flat-screen cable TVs and ironing facilities. Rooms are also equipped with a microwave, refrigerator and coffee-making facilities.

Guests can also enjoy a nightly buffet at Lighthouse Lobster Feast, the hotel's on-site restaurant.

The Maingate Comfort Inn is within a 30-minute drive from downtown Orlando. The hotel is 24 mi from the Orlando International Airport.

Celebration is a great choice for travelers interested in Disneyland, tourist attractions and parks.
