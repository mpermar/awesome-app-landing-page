Description

Cable TV, full kitchen, and sitting area with a sofa are included in all villas at Reunion Resort, A Salamander Golf & Spa Resort. Extras include a washing machine and a tumble dryer.

Guests can expect 3 on-site golf courses and a golf academy at this Kissimmee Reunion Resort, A Salamander Golf & Spa Resort. A tennis court, fitness center, and concierge are available as well.

The 7 on-site restaurants include Eleven steakhouse, Forte Italian restaurant, and Grand Lobby and Sushi Bar. Full-service dining is also available poolside at The Grill at Water Park.

Located 5 minutes’ drive from Interstate 4 Exit 60, guests will be 20 minutes’ drive from Aquatica, SeaWold’s water park, and the Orlando Premium Outlets. The Universal Studios Resort is 21 miles away.
