Description

All rooms in the resort are equipped with a electric tea pot. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom, while some rooms at Nanuya Island Resort also offer a balcony. The rooms have a closet.

A continental breakfast is available every morning at the accommodation.

A number of activities are offered in the area, such as snorkeling and canoeing.

Experiences

09/26/2017

You wont be dissapointed

"This is our third visit and since our last visit there has been a change in ownership. The staff are absolutely amazing, friendly and so helpful. 20% tax makes restaurant seem expensive at Fiji$50, 60 and 70 for a main meal but meals were really lovely and menu changes every day. Resort is not crowded with only about 14 Bure's. Swimming good at low and high tide, reef fish and coral amazing. The one criticism we have this year is we were bothered by mosquitos and flies and although owner told us they still spray pyrethrum every day I do not believe this as we never saw this happening once and we always did on our last two visits. Don't get me wrong, only a mild criticism because the mozzie and fly problem does not detract from the beauty and every bure is totally protected with screens for night time. New air-con in units now too. The included breakfast is lovely. You won't be disappointed with your stay here. Right in front of resort you can swim and feed the little fish. We saw two iguana really up close while we were there. No children under 7 but we have not ever seen any children staying there. Take the awesome adventure ferry out and pay the wee bit extra for captains cabin."
