Description

Situated on Fiji's main island at Viti Levu's most northern point, Wananavu Beach Resort boasts a swimming pool, tennis court and a private beach surrounded by coral reefs.

The activity center and tour desk can arrange daily excursions or snorkeling and scuba diving trips. Wai Spa offers a selection of massages, body therapies and beauty treatments.

The restaurant offers international dishes and Fijian influenced delights. The Sunset Bar overlooks the beach and serves drinks until late.

All accommodations at Wananavu Beach Resort offer air conditioning and ceiling fans. Some villas are located right on the beachfront and some feature their own private plunge pool.
