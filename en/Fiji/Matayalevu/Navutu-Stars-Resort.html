Description

Situated just 60 nautical miles from Nadi International Airport, the Navutu Stars Fiji Resort is a luxurious getaway in the archipelago of Yasawa, 40 nautical miles from Nadi.

Free activities include snorkeling, fishing, kayaking and cultural activities. To help you relax, there is a swimming pool surrounded by lounge chairs, as well as hammocks on the beachfront.

Guests can relax with a massage or beauty treatment at the day spa. You can dine at the restaurant’s main porch under the stars by candlelight, or at a beautifully set table on your own private terrace.

The resort has spacious thatched-roof bungalows that combine traditional Fijian architecture with modern luxury. Each includes a minibar, ceiling fans and a radio. Most bungalows have ocean views.
