Description

The Best Western Lamplighter Inn provides guests of all ages with a variety of thoughtful amenities and services. Guests can relax near the indoor waterfall and fish ponds or enjoy an energizing workout in the modern fitness center. The hotel also showcases an indoor swimming pool with a water slide, along with a game room.

Located close to area highways, the Lamplighter Inn provides easy access to London International Airport as well as nearby golf courses and parks. The hotel also provides guests with free shuttle service to a number of nearby hospitals.
