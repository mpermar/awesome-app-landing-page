Description

Each guest room features free WiFi and a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Select rooms include a whirlpool tub. Complimentary toiletries are provided.

A breakfast buffet is available each morning. It offers a selection of pastries, bagels, hot and cold cereals, coffee, teas, juice and two daily featured Hot Items (pancakes, french toast, sausages, omelets, waffles).

Clifton Victoria Inn at the Falls also has a business center, equipped with office supplies and a printer, available 24 hours a day.

This hotel is less than 5 minutes' walk from the attractions of Clifton Hill. The Falls lookout area is just 10 minutes' walk away.
