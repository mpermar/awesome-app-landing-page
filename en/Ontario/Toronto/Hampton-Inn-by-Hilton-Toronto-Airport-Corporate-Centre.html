"This was a very nice hotel in walking distance to a few restaurants. The staff was friendly and helpful, and the breakfast was wonderful. When the dining area filled, they opened a second room. We enjoyed the ballpark, the Aquarium, and the Zoo, as well as the CN tower. Tip for the zoo- pay the $8 for the train to get around quickly. Also, it is worth the walk (if you are physically fit) into the Canada exhibit to see the bears at feeding time. Amazing! Super long wait to see the pandas- Don't bother if you have little kids. We waited an hour and a half, and they are cute but were sleeping. Tip for CN tower - Book the dinner ahead of time (call and make a reservation). It is worth the cost because you get dinner AND the view but you don't have to pay a separate admission and you don't have to wait in line for the two hours it takes! Don't wait with little kids. It's just not worth the crying for two hours in line! CitiPass saved us money and time at the Zoo and Aquarium but the line for the CN tower is the same. One more thing - the EdgeWalk at CN tower also gets you to the head of the line instantly, but it costs a lot more."
