Description

A flat-screen TV, free Wi-Fi and an iPod dock are standard in each luxurious room at The Adelaide Hotel Toronto. The spacious bathroom has stone-topped vanities and an in-mirror TV. Free bottled water and tea and coffee-making facilities are included.

The Spa at The Adelaide Hotel Toronto is a 2-level facility providing a variety of body treatments and therapies. A juice bar, indoor pool and fully-equipped fitness center are part of the wellness center.

The Restaurant at The Adelaide Hotel offers fine dining options daily. Cocktails and wine are served in the evening and guests can enjoy the restaurant’s panoramic city views. Located on the lobby level, the stylish BAR ADELAIDE offers a variety of drinks and American-inspired snacks.

Many central Toronto attractions are less than 5 minutes’ drive from the hotel, including the Rogers Center, the Air Canada Center and the Metro Toronto Convention Center.
