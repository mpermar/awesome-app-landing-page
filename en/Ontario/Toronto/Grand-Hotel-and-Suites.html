Description

The Grand Hotel & Suites has modern, elegant rooms with hardwood floors and a marble bathroom. A kitchenette, flat-screen cable TV and a coffee maker are included.

The Grand Spa offers relaxing massage and beauty treatments. Guests can also enjoy the on-site fitness center and beautiful indoor pool facility. A gift shop is open at all times.

Citrus restaurant at the Grand Hotel serves a unique, European menu with fresh, local ingredients. The lounge area serves cocktails and afternoon tea.

The Grand Hotel & Suites is just over a quarter mile from Allan Gardens and just under half a mile from the Eaton Centre, filled with various shops. Parking is available on site upon request and for a fee.
