Description

A flat-screen TV with cable is offered in all suites at Cartier Place Hotel. Each air-conditioned suite includes a seating area and desk. They also open out onto a balcony.

Café Mezzaluna features Italian cuisine in an authentic setting. Vending machines offering snacks and drinks are located on-site.

A fitness center and sauna are provided at Hotel Cartier Place. Guests can enjoy the indoor swimming pool and hot tub. A games room and children’s playground are provided for entertainment.

Byward Market is 8 minutes' drive away. Rideau Canal is 1,950 feet away. Parking is available with surcharge.
