Description

Timberline Condominiums features free shuttle service to and from the Aspen Airport and destinations in Snowmass Village. Ski rental and storage are also available.

Guests can enjoy the Timberline's spacious outdoor hot tub and seasonal pool or the gym with a steam room and showers. The hotel's sundeck offers mountain views, gas barbecue grills and a picnic area.

The property offers a casual on-site restaurant that serves Alpine-inspired cuisine.

Snowmass connects to over 40 miles of hiking and biking trails. Activities include fishing, rock climbing, dog sledding and snowmobiling.
