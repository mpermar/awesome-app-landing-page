Description

Free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV and coffee-making facilities are featured in all rooms at Beaver Run Resort Breckenridge. A private bathroom with a hairdryer is also included.

At Breckenridge Beaver Run Resort a restaurant, a spa and health club and a fitness center are available. An array of seasonal activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including skiing, biking and fishing.

Beaver Run SuperChair is 350 feet from the resort. BreckConnect Gondola is less than a 5-minute drive from Beaver Run Resort.
