Description

Featuring a balcony, all apartments also include a sitting area with a fireplace, a flat-screen cable TV and a washing machine and tumble dryer at One Ski Hill RockResort Breckenridge. A full kitchen with a dishwasher, a refrigerator and an oven is also available.

The Living Room Restaurant and Bar offers casual dining for breakfast, lunch and dinner every day at Breckenridge RockResort One Ski Hill. The Ski Hill Grill serves cafeteria-style fare daily and guests can enjoy beer and cocktails at The T-Bar.

An indoor pool and a hot tub are available for guest relaxation. A fitness center and a business center are available.

The town center of Breckenridge is just over half a mile from the hotel. BreckConnect Gondola is adjacent to One Ski Hill, A RockResort.
