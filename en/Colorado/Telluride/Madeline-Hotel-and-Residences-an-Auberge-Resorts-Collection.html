Description

Each room provides a seating area at the Madeline Hotel and Residences. They include flat-screen TVs, iPod docking stations and free WiFi.

Activities for children are available at 20 Below Hangout. The M Club and Black Iron Kitchen and Bar offers food daily.

Guests can also take advantage of spa services at M Spa, including yoga and pilates, as well as massages, aroma therapy, herbal treatments and mud baths.

Madeline Hotel and Residences provides easy access to 84 ski and snowmobiling trails in the winter, and fly fishing and rock climbing in the summer.

Sheridan Opera House is a 20-minute drive from the property. Montrose Regional Airport is 68 mi away.
