Description

Styled in white plush linens and modern décor, each elegant room provides bathrobes. Free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen cable TV, iPod docking station are included. Select rooms feature mountain views at Park Hyatt Beaver Creek Resort.

After a day of activity, guests can enjoy the sauna and hot tub at Park Hyatt Resort Beaver Creek. Allegria Spa offers spa services onsite. Ski hire, concierge services and baby-sitting services are available.

In-room breakfast is available. The 8100 Bar and Grill offers American cuisine. Antler Hall offers cocktails and a light bar menu. Conveniently, The Café features deli sandwiches to go.

Eagle County Regional Airport is 35 minutes' drive away from Park Hyatt Beaver Creek Resort, while Vail Ski area is 20 minutes' drive away.
