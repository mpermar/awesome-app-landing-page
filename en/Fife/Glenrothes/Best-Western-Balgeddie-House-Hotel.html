Description

The Balgeddie House Hotel looks out over its own large gardens and the Fife countryside. It was built in 1923 and has now been extended and refurbished.

The rooms have private bathrooms and TVs with Freeview digital channels.

There is a relaxed cocktail lounge and an oak-paneled cocktail bar, with a fine range of malt whiskeys.

The Brasserie offers fresh Scottish cuisine, and looks out over the gardens. A full cooked Scottish breakfast is on the breakfast menu in the Brasserie.
