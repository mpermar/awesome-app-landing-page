Description

The rooms at the hotel are fitted with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a safety deposit box and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hair dryer. Oasia Hotel Downtown, Singapore by Far East Hospitality features certain units with city views, and every room is fitted with a kettle. Guest rooms have a desk.

An Asian breakfast is available daily at the accommodation. The restaurant at Oasia Hotel Downtown, Singapore by Far East Hospitality serves international cuisine.

Sri Mariamman Temple is 0.5 miles from the hotel, while Chinatown Heritage Centre is a 10-minute walk away.

Chinatown is a great choice for travelers interested in culturally diverse food, city trips and food.
