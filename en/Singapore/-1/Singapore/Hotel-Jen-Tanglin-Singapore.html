Description

The rooms in the hotel are fitted with a TV. Featuring a private bathroom, rooms at Hotel Jen Tanglin Singapore also offer free WiFi, while certain rooms are fitted with pool view. All units will provide guests with a desk and a kettle.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at the property. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the on-site restaurant, which serves Asian, Cantonese and European cuisine and also offers vegetarian options.

Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Singapore, like cycling.

Hotel Jen Tanglin Singapore also offers a bicycle rental service and a business centre. Staff at the 24-hour front desk can provide tips on the area.

Cineleisure Orchard is a 17-minute walk from the hotel, while National Orchid Garden is an 18-minute walk from the property.

Orchard is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, food and cleanliness.
