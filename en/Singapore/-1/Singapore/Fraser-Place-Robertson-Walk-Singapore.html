Description

Spacious and fitted with warm lighting, all the air-conditioned apartments come with a kitchen, cable TV and a DVD player. Some apartments have a washing machine and clothes dryer. Private bathrooms come with a bathtub and free toiletries.

Fraser Place Robertson Walk Singapore is a leisurely 10-minute walk from popular nightlife options and restaurants at Clarke Quay. It is a 30-minute drive from Changi International Airport. One free parking space is provided per apartment booked.

Guests can also relax in the steam room and sauna after a good workout. A children's playroom and barbecue facilities are available.

The Hub restaurant serves Asian and Western dishes, with the option of outdoor seating. In-room dining is also possible with room service. All-day dining serves no pork and no lard.
