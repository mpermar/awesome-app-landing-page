Description

Guest rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area and a flat-screen TV. Every room is fitted with a private bathroom. The rooms at Fullerton Bay Singapore have air conditioning and a desk.

A continental, buffet or Full English/Irish breakfast is available each morning at the property. Guests can enjoy a meal at the in-house restaurant, which specialises in international cuisine.

Languages spoken at the reception include English, Japanese, Korean and Malay, and guests are invited to request guidance on the area when needed.

Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Lau Pa Sat, Asian Civilisations Museum and Sands Expo and Convention Centre.

Marina Bay is a great choice for travelers interested in city trips, cleanliness and sightseeing.
