Description

The units are fitted with a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a kettle, a shower, free toiletries and a desk. The private bathroom is equipped with a hair dryer.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning at the property. There is an in-house restaurant, which specialises in Singaporean, local and international cuisine.

A business centre and newspapers are available on site.

Marine Life Park Singapore is a third of a mile from the hotel, while Adventure Cove Waterpark is a third of a mile from the property.
