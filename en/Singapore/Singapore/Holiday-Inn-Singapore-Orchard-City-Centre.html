Description

All guest rooms at the hotel come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a safety deposit box and a private bathroom with bathrobes and slippers. Guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

Guests at Holiday Inn Orchard can enjoy a continental breakfast.

The accommodation offers 4-star accommodation with a hot tub.

Holiday Inn Singapore Orchard City Centre also provides a business center and guests can check the newspapers or use the fax machine and photocopier at the hotel. Around the clock assistance is available at the reception.

Cineleisure Orchard is a 6-minute walk from Holiday Inn Orchard.

Orchard is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, food and cleanliness.
