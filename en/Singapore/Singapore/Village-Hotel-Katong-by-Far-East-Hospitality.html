Description

The rooms have air conditioning, a TV with cable channels, a kettle, a shower, free toiletries and a wardrobe. Every room is equipped with a private bathroom.

An American breakfast is served every morning at the property.

The hotel offers an outdoor pool.

Languages spoken at the 24-hour front desk include English and Malay.
