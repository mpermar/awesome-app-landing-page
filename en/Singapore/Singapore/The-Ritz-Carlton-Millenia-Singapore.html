Description

The rooms at the hotel are equipped with a bath, free toiletries, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and dvd player. All guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

Guests at Ritz-Carlton Millenia Singapore can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

A business center and newspapers are available on site. Around the clock guidance is available at the reception. Where staff speak English, French and Indonesian.

Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Singapore Flyer, ArtScience Museum and Esplanade Theatres on the Bay.

Marina Bay is a great choice for travelers interested in city trips, cleanliness and sightseeing.
