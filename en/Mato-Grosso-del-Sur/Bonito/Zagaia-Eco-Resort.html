Description

The rooms at the Zagaia Eco Resort feature air conditioning, a minibar and a telephone. Some also have a seating area and a balcony.

There is a children's playground, a tennis court and a soccer field. Guests can also go jogging and relax later in the sauna.

The Zagaia has a 24-hour reception, free parking and a tour desk, where visits to places of interest such as the Anhumas Abyss are organized.

The hotel restaurant serves typical dishes from various parts of Brazil. Guests can enjoy regional cheese bread, grilled fish, barbecues and desserts made with local fruits.

Zagaia Eco Resort is 1.5 km from Bonito city center, 2.5 km from the bus station and 20 km from the airport.
