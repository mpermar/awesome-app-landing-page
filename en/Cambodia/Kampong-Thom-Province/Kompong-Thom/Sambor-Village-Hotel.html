Description

Overlooking the scenic Stung Seng River, Sambor Village Hotel is located 600 metres from central Kompong Thom.

With traditional Cambodian architecture, it also offers an outdoor pool and free Wi-Fi.

Rooms at Sambor Village Hotel come with a spacious verandah and outdoor seating area.

Fully air-conditioned, each room also enjoys the comforts of a cable TV, personal safe and minibar.

Guests may use the free bicycles to explore the village at leisure. The hotel also provides free parking.

Only the freshest ingredients are used to prepare the local dishes served at Sambor Village.

Refreshing cocktails can be enjoyed by the poolside bar. Sambor Village Hotel is a 40-minute drive from Sambor Preh Kukh.
