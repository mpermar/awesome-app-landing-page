Description

This elegant property is located within a 10-minute walk to Central Market and Wat Phnom. It is 1.1 mi to Chaktomouk Conference Hall 2. The nearest airport, Phnom Penh International Airport is 6.8 mi away. Airport transfers and shuttle services are offered at additional charges.

Air-conditioned rooms at Patio Hotel & Urban Resort have a seating area furnished with a flat-screen cable TV and a desk. Some units offer a private balcony. The private bathrooms include shower facilities, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for currency exchange, tour arrangements, ticketing and concierge services. Guests can visit the internet cafe for a fresh brew or utilize the complimentary use of computers available. Alternatively, traditional massages can be enjoyed in-house.

Offering sweeping sea views from the 7th floor, the in-house restaurant will serve a variety of Asian and Western flavors with 24-hour in-room dining options. A selection of cocktails and wines can be enjoyed on the rooftop bar.

Chamkar Mon is a great choice for travelers interested in restaurants, food and culture.
