Description

Sokha Phnom Penh Hotel offers accommodation away from the hustle and bustle of Phnom Penh.

It features an outdoor swimming pool and guests enjoy complimentary use of spa facilities at Jasmine Spa.

The property boasts 8 in-house dining options for guests to choose from, including a bar.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. Staff here are able to converse in Chinese, Thai, Khmer and English.

It is 5 km to Wat Phnom and Phnom Penh Exhibition Hall. Old Market and Central Market are both 5.

6 km from the property. The nearest airport, Phnom Penh International Airport is 13.

6 km away. The property offers airport transfers at additional charges while shuttle services to the city centre are complimentary.

Each air-conditioned room and suite is soundproof. The seating area is equipped with an electric kettle, tea/coffee maker, minibar, flat-screen cable TV and safe.

Private bathrooms include a bathtub, hairdryer, toothbrushes, slippers and free toiletries.

Ironing facilities are also available. Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for currency exchange, tour arrangements, ticketing and concierge services.

The business centre has a in-house cafe where meeting facilities and banquet services can be arranged.
