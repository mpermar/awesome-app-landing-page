Description

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool, Samsara Villa is located in the city centre of Phnom Penh, with a convenient 10-minute drive to the famous River Front and Royal Palace.

Free Wi-Fi is available in the entire property. The property is only 1 km to the Independence Monument, while Phnom Penh International Airport is situated 12 km away.

Airport transfer and shuttle services can be arranged at additional charges. Air-conditioned rooms are furnished with a wardrobe, flat-screen cable TV and a seating area.

An electric kettle, minibar and personal safe are also included. En suite bathrooms offer a hairdryer, shower facilities and free toiletries.

Samsara Villa operates a 24-hour front desk that can assist with luggage storage, laundry and babysitting services.

Guests can rent a bicycle or car to explore the area and visit nearby attractions.

Alternatively, they can relax at the sun terrace or request for massage services.

The poolside bar offers a range of cocktails and cold drinks, while the restaurant serves a variety of Cambodian and Western cuisines.
