Description

Centrally located right on the lively Sisowath Quay Riverside strip in Daun Penh, TAO Riverside Residence offers uniquely designed, riverfront accommodations with sweeping views of the Tonle Sap and Mekong Rivers.

Each spacious apartment-style unit has wooden floors, a 50-inch flat-screen satellite TV and air conditioning.

There is a kitchenette, minibar, a seating area as well as a balcony overlooking river views.

The en suite bathroom is fitted with rain shower, bathtub, bathrobe and slippers.

Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for taxi, luggage storage, currency exchange and tour arrangements.

Daily breakfast is served at the iconic La Croisette Restaurant, just across the street or in your room upon request.

Situated close to several attractions, Royal Palace is just 800 metres away, while the National Museum is 650 metres from the property.

S21 Toul Sleng Museum and AEON Mall are a 15-minute drive, while the Night Market is a 10-minute walk away.

Phnom Penh International Airport is a 40-minute drive from TAO Riverside Residence.
