Description

Nestled within a tropical garden, The Kabiki offers acommodation in Phnom Penh's historical district.

It features an outdoor swimming pool and guests can enjoy meals at the in-house restaurant or have a drink at the poolside bar.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. It is 1 km to Chaktomouk Conference Hall 2, while Royal Palace, National Museum, Independence Monument and Riverside can be reached within a walking distance.

The nearest airport, Phnom Penh International Airport is 11. 1 km away. Airport transfers and shuttle services are available at additional charges.

Vibrantly-decorated air-conditioned rooms open up to views of the garden. Rooms are fitted with a flat-screen cable TV, refrigerator and a desk.

The en suite bathrooms are equipped with solar panels and provide hot shower facilities throughout the day.

Staff at the property can assist with currency exchange, tour arrangements and ticketing services.

A computer centre is available for use. Shoeshine services are offered. At the Kabiki Restaurant, guests can choose to dine al-fresco on the yard or by the pool.

The in-house restaurant serves a selection of Western and Cambodian dishes from locally-sourced produce.
