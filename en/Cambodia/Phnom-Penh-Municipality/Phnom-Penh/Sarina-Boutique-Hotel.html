Description

At the hotel, each room includes a closet. All rooms come with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV, and certain rooms include a balcony. At Sarina Boutique Hotel every room has a seating area.

The daily breakfast offers buffet and Asian options. The restaurant at the accommodation serves Asian and American cuisine.

Sarina Boutique Hotel offers 4-star accommodations with a hot spring bath and hot tub.

The hotel also provides a business center and guests can use the on-site ATM machine at Sarina Boutique Hotel. Around the clock assistance is available at the reception, where staff speak English and Chinese.

Sisowath Quay is 3.7 miles from the accommodation, while Royal Palace Phnom Penh is 3.8 miles away. Pochentong Airport is 2.5 miles from the property, and the property offers a free airport shuttle service.

Sen Sok is a great choice for travelers interested in history, friendly locals and markets.
