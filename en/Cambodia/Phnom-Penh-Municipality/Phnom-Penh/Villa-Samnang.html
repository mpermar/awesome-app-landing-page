Description

Featuring contemporary-style decor and nestled within private gardens, Villa Samnang is a boutique hotel offering accommodation in the Beung Keng Kang 1 district of Phnom Penh.

Villa Samnang features an outdoor chlorine-free saltwater swimming pool. Guests can enjoy meals at the poolside bar and bistro.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. It is a 5-minute walk from The Independence Monument and Samdech Hun Sen Park, and a 10-minute drive from the Royal Palace and lively Riverfront.

Phnom Penh International Airport is 12 km away, while S21 Toul Sleng Museum is 2 km away.

All rooms at Samnang Villa are spacious and individually designed. Air-conditioned rooms are equipped with a minibar, a safe and a flat-screen TV.

The en suite bathrooms include free toiletries and a hairdryer. Some rooms provide a private balcony with an outdoor seating area.

Tea/coffee facilities and bottled water are also provided. Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for tour arrangements, ticketing and concierge services.

Laundry and luggage storage services are available on request. The poolside bar and bistro serves up all-day dining that includes local dishes and international cuisine, light refreshments and a variety of cocktails.
