Description

Nestled within private gardens, Le Cocon Boutique Hotel offers accommodation in the Chamkar Mon district in Phnom Penh.

Guests enjoy access to the outdoor swimming pool and can have a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

It is located 500 metres away from Beung Kang Kong Market and 800 metres from Tuol Sleng Genocide Museum.

Independence Monument is 1. 1 km from the property while the Royal Palace and National Museum sits about 2.

9 km from the property. The nearest airport, Phnom Penh is 11. 7 km away. The property offers airport transfer services at additional charges.

Every room at this hotel is air-conditioned and is equipped with a flat-screen TV.

Certain units feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day. Each room is equipped with a private bathroom and some units are fitted with a bathtub.

For your comfort, you will find bath robes, slippers and free toiletries. Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for currency exchange, tour arrangements, ticketing and concierge services.

Complimentary use of bicycles are offered for guests who wish to explore the surrounding area.
