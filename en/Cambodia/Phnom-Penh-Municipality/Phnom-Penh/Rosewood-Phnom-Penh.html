Description

At the hotel, each room is equipped with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Rosewood Phnom Penh has some accommodations with river views, and rooms have a coffee machine. Guest rooms at the accommodation feature air conditioning and a closet.

Continental and à la carte breakfast options are available daily at Rosewood Phnom Penh. There's an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of Japanese dishes.

Guests can use the indoor pool.

Sisowath Quay is a 17-minute walk from the hotel. The nearest airport is Pochentong Airport, 5 miles from Rosewood Phnom Penh.

Daun Penh is a great choice for travelers interested in museums, temples and local food.
