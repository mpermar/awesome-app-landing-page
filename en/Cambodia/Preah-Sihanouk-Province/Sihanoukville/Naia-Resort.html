Description

Naia Resort is a beachfront property offering accommodation in Sihanoukville in the Preah Sihanouk Province Region, 6 km from Occheuteal Beach.

The resort boasts terraces where guests can enjoy beach views and an outdoor pool is available all year round.

It also features an in-house restaurant. A lift is available for access to the higher floors.

Serendipity Beach is 7 km from Naia Resort, while Samudera Supermarket is 8 km from the property.

Airport transfers and shuttle services are available at an additional cost. The air-conditioned rooms include a flat-screen TV with cable channels and come with a fan option.

Certain units feature a seating area that are fitted with desk. Certain rooms include views of the sea or pool.

Rooms have a private bathroom fitted with a shower. Extras include bath robes, free toiletries and a hair dryer.

Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for luggage storage, ticketing and tour services.

There is also an on-site gift shop. The resort's in-house restaurant offers packed lunches and caters to special dietary needs upon request.
