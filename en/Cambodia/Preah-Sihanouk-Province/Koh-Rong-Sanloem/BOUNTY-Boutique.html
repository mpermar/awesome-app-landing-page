Description

At the hotel, all rooms are equipped with a closet. All rooms include air conditioning and a TV, and some units at BOUNTY Boutique have a sea view. All guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

The accommodation offers a continental or à la carte breakfast.

Sihanoukville is 15 miles from BOUNTY Boutique, while Song Saa Private Island is 13 miles away. The nearest airport is Sihanoukville International Airport, 22 miles from the hotel.

"The set up of this place is amazing. The view is outstanding: 7 contemporain bungalows facing the beach as well as the restaurant and the swimming pool.
