Description

Offering an outdoor pool and a spa, The Moon Residence & Spa is set conveniently in Siem Reap, just 700 metres from the famous Pub Street.

Guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

It is 800 metres to the vibrant Artisans D'Angkor, Pub Street and Old Market, while Angkor Wat is 9.

8 km away. The nearest airport, Siem Reap - Angkor International Airport is 9. 3 km from the property.

The property offers complimentary two-way airport transfers. Each room at this hotel is air conditioned and features a flat-screen TV.

Some rooms offer a seating area where you can relax. A terrace or balcony are available in certain rooms.

Rooms have a private bathroom fitted with a bath. For your comfort, you will find bath robes, slippers and free toiletries.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. Complimentary airport shuttle service is provided.
