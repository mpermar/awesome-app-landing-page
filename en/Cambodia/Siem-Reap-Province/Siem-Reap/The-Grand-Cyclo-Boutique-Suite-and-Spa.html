Description

Located along the Charles de Gaulle district, The Grand Cyclo Boutique Suite offers accommodation in central Siem Reap.

It features a rooftop swimming pool and guests can enjoy meals at the in-house restaurant or have a drink at the bar.

Free WiFi is available in all rooms. It is 2. 4 km to the vibrant Pub Street and Old Market.

Angkor Wat is 7 km from The Grand Cyclo Boutique Suite, while Angkor National Museum is 650 metres from the property.

The nearest airport is Siem Reap - Angkor International Airport, 6. 8 km from The Grand Cyclo Boutique Suite.

The property offers complimentary pickup services from the airport. Featuring traditional Cambodian-style decor, air-conditioned rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV and a small refrigerator.

En suite bathrooms come with shower facilities and a hairdryer. Tea/coffee making amenities are also provided.

Guests can 24-hour front desk for currency exchange, tour arrangements, ticketing and concierge services.

A spa centre is available for guests who wish to indulge in massage treatments. The in-house restaurant serves up a variety of authentic Cambodian dishes while a selection of alcoholic beverages can be enjoyed at the rooftop bar.
