Description

Located across the Cambodian Cultural Village, Empress Angkor Hotel has an salt water infinity pool and a spa.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. It offers 3 dining options and provides free return transfers from Siem Reap International Airport, located 3 km away.

Featuring wooden flooring and furnishings, the air-conditioned rooms at Empress Angkor have a flat-screen cable TV, minibar and personal safe.

Some rooms have a separate living area. The private bathrooms are fitted with a bathtub and hot shower.

Guests can exercise in the fitness centre, or relax in the sauna. The hotel provides a free shuttle to the Central Market, the convenience of a 24-hour reception and a tour desk.

Free parking is provided on site. Buffet breakfast is served daily. Crown Café Restaurant serves traditional Khmer cuisine and Western dishes, as well as homemade ice cream.

Light snacks and drinks are available at Royal Pool Bar and The Sky Island Bar. Empress Angkor Hotel is a 10-minute drive to the historic temples of Angkor.
