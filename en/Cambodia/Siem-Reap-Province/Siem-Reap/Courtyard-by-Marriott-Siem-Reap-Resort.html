Description

Featuring Khmer architecture and sleek decor throughout, Courtyard by Marriott Siem Reap Resort welcomes guests with it's modern rooms located close to the city's ancient sites.

The lounge area greats guests with it's sweet and savoury delights, soothing tea and refreshing cocktails.

Alternatively guests can also enjoy stunning views of the city and Wat Angkor using the binoculars available at the observation area.

For all-day dining, international dishes are available at Lok Lak restaurant. The stylish rooms and suites come with their own balcony, a Bluetooth speaker, complimentary WiFi and a 43-inch flat-screen TV.

For recreational activities, during the stay at Courtyard by Marriott, guests can enjoy a swim at the saltwater pool and work out at the fitness centre.

For those looking for a more relaxing time, head over to the Quan Spa where pampering traditional Cambodian treatment awaits.

Golfers can easily access the three Siem Reap golf courses, located nearby the property.

On-site facilities that the resort offers include cooking classes, a kid's club and over 4400 square-feet of event space.
