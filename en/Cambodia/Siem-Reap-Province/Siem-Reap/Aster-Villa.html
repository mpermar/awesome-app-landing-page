Description

Located in the centre of Siem Reap, Aster Vila offers air-conditioned accommodation equipped with a LED TV and free WiFi.

This family-run hotel is a 15-minute walk from Pub Street and a 8-minute drive from Angkor Night Market.

The villa is equipped with a refrigerator, coffee facilities, bottled water and linens.

An activity in the surrounding area includes biking and the property offers free bike rental service for guests.

Angkor Wat is a 20-minute drive from the villa. Siem Reap - Angkor International Airport is a 25-minute drive from the property.
