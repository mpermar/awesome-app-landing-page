Description

Nestled within private tropical gardens, La Palmeraie d’Angkor offers accommodation with private balconies and features an outdoor swimming pool.

Free WiFi is also available and guests can enjoy meals at the in-house restaurant.

La Palmeraie is a 15-minute drive from Angkor Temple Complex and 5 minutes from Angkor Golf course.

Siem Reap Airport is 3. 5 km away. Air-conditioned rooms include private bathroom facilities.

All rooms have a flat-screen TV, a minibar and a safe. Tea/coffee making facilities are available.

Guests can relax on the sun loungers located around the swimming pool. The hotel’s tour desk can organise excursions to local attractions.

The Restaurant offers Continental, French and American breakfasts daily as well as authentic Khmer cuisine.
