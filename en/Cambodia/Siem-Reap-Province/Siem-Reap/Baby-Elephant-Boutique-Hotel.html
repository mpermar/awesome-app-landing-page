Description

Just a 10-minute walk from the various dining and retail options in downtown Siem Reap, this boutique hotel features contemporary rooms, an outdoor swimming pool and free WiFi access.

Baby Elephant Boutique Hotel offers air-conditioned rooms equipped with a flat-screen cable TV, a minibar and tea/coffee maker.

The black and white tiled bathrooms come with a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Guests can enjoy views of the pool and landscaped garden on the terrace or sunbathe by the pool.

The hotel also has a 24-hour front desk, a travel desk and luggage storage. Baby Elephant Boutique Hotel offers a free pick up service from Siem Reap International Airport, Borei Seang Nam Bus Station and Tonle Sap Ferry Dock.

It is just 8 km away from Angkor Wat Temple and the airport. It is 10 km to Tonle Sap Lake.

The property offers complimentary pickup services from the airport or bus station.
