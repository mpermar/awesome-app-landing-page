Description

Angkor Holiday Hotel is a 10-minute drive from both Angkor Wat and Siem Reap International Airport.

This 4-storey hotel features a spa, an outdoor pool with hot tub, and a restaurant.

The air-conditioned rooms at Angkor Holiday are equipped with a flat-screen satellite TV, safety deposit box and tea/coffee making facilities.

The private bathroom has a bathtub and shower. Bathrobes and slippers are also provided.

Guests can relax in the sauna, or exercise in the gym while enjoying pool views. Karaoke facilities and cooking or massage classes are also offered.

For guests’ convenience, the hotel has a 24-hour reception and a tour desk. Buffet breakfast is served at Pavilion Restaurant, which also offers an international menu for lunch and dinner.

Drinks can be enjoyed at the lobby bar. Room service is available 24 hours daily.

Angkor Holiday Hotel is less than 5 minutes’ walk from the Royal Gardens and Siem Reap River.
