Description

All guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a electric tea pot. Every room has a private bathroom and free toiletries, and some accommodations at Hello Cambodia Boutique have a balcony. Guest rooms come with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

A buffet breakfast is available each morning at the property. At the accommodation you'll find a restaurant serving American, Australian and Asian cuisine.

Guests can use the business center or relax in the bar. With staff speaking Khmer and English, around the clock guidance is available at the reception.

Kravan Temple is a 5-minute walk from Hello Cambodia Boutique, while Lucky Mall Siem Reap is 0.6 miles away. Angkor International Airport is 3.7 miles from the property.
