Featuring Khmer architecture and d√©cor, Residence Wat Damnak is just a 2-minute walk to the famous Pub Street and Old Market.

It provides an outdoor swimming pool, a 24-hour front desk and offers free WiFi access in the entire property.

The hotel is a 5-minute walk to the Crocodile Farm, while the notable Royal Palace and the Angkor National Museum is a 10-minute walk away.

Siem Reap International Airport and the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Angkor Wat Temple are conveniently 8 km away.

Fitted with tiled flooring, air-conditioned rooms are equipped with a personal safe, a satellite TV and seating area.

En suite bathrooms come with a shower facility and free toiletries. Rooms also offer views of the garden or pool.

Residence Wat Damnak has a business centre and a tour desk that can assist with travel arrangements.

Luggage storage, laundry and massage services are available on request. Guests may also rent a bicycle/car to explore the area, or arrange for shuttle services at a charge.

The in-house restaurant serves delectable Khmer cuisine, while the bar offers a selection of cocktails and beers.
