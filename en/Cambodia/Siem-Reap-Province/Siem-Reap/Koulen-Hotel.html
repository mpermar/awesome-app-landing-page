Description

Offering an outdoor swimming pool and barbecue facilities, Koulen Hotel is located in the heart of Siem Reap, not far from Pub Street and King's Road Angkor.

Free WiFi is featured throughout the property. The rooms have a fully equipped kitchenette with a microwave and a fridge.

All rooms are air-conditioned and include a terrace and/or a balcony. The private bathroom comes with a shower, slippers, bathrobe and free toiletries.

Flat screen TV, tea/coffee making facilities and in-room dining services are available in the units.

There is also a dining table in the living room. A buffet breakfast is available each morning at the property.

Consisting of a spacious poolside dining area, the in-house restaurant serves a variety of Khmer cuisine and international dishes.

The aparthotel provides an ironing service, as well as business facilities like fax and photocopying.

Guests can relax in the garden at the property. The in-house spa offers guests traditional back rub and other special spa treatments.

Angkor Wat is 6 km from Koulen Hotel. Siem Reap - Angkor International Airport is 7 km away.
