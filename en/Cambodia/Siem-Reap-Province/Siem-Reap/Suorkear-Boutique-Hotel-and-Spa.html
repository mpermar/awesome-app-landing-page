Description

Nestled within private gardens, Suorkear Boutique Hotel & Spa offers accommodation away from the hustle and bustle of Siem Reap.

Featuring an outdoor pool, the hotel offers an la carte restaurant, rooftop bar and free WiFi throughout the property.

The hotel sits 1 km away from the lively Pub Street and Old Market. The UNESCO World Heritage Site of Angkor Wat Temple is within 7 km away.

Located 8 km away is the nearest airport, Siem Reap International Airport. The property offers complimentary two-way airport transfers and shuttle services to the city centre.

With garden or pool views, the elegantly-furnished air-conditioned rooms all feature a private balcony.

Seating areas are fitted with a cable TV. Private bathrooms equipped with free toiletries and bathrobes.

Guests can relax in the garden or on the sun terrace, or read newspapers in the lobby.

Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for car rentals, spa sessions and tour and ticketing services.

Free usage of children's play ground is offered. The hotel's in-house restaurant serves up authentic Khmer-style cuisine and popular Western delights.
