Description

Located along the Charles de Gaulle road towards the UNESCO World Heritage-listed Angkor Wat, The Cyclo dAngkor Boutique Hotel offers accommodation in central Siem Reap.

This boutique hotel features an outdoor pool, free WiFi access and free bicycle rentals.

The property is 600 metres from Angkor National Museum and 1 km from Apsara Theatre.

Cultural Village is 6. 2 km away while Siem Reap-Angkor International Airport is 7 km from the property.

The property offers complimentary pickup services from the airport or bus station.

Featuring traditional Cambodian-style dcor with modern furnishings, each room comes with a flat-screen TV, a small fridge and tea/coffee making amenities.

There is also an attached bathroom with bath/shower facilities and a hairdryer. Guests staying at the hotel can enjoy traditional massages.

Facilities include an on-site gift-shop, a business centre and a 24-hour reception.

Complimentary use of bicycles are available for guests who wish to explore the surrounding area.

Dine at the in-house restaurant and sample authentic Cambodian-style meals. A selection of cocktails, wines and beers can be enjoyed at the bar.
