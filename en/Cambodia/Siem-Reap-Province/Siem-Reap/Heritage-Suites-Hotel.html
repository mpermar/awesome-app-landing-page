Description

Set just 8 km away from Siem Reap International Airport with free one-way airport pick up by Vintage Mercedes, Heritage Suites Hotel offers an outdoor saltwater pool and free WiFi access throughout the property.

Heritage Suites Hotel is located a 3-minute walk to Royal Palace and Angkor National Museum.

The historical Angkor Wat Temple is just 8 km away. The nearest airport, Siem Reap International Airport is 8.

6 km from Heritage Suites Hotel. Encompassing a blend of traditional furnishings with modern amenities, air-conditioned rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV with cable channels, tea/coffee making facilities and a minibar.

Private bathrooms include shower facilities and free toiletries. At Heritage Suites guests can enjoy a rejuvenating spa treatment, an outdoor swimming pool and an appetizing food selection of local Khmer cuisine and continental dishes.

A 24-hour front desk can assist with travel arrangements, car rentals and room service.

A business centre is also available. Guests can have lunch or dinner at Heritage Suites Restaurant which serves Khmer and Western cuisines.

The weekly Apsara Dance performance is available every Tuesday evening from 19:30 - 20:30 hrs between November and March.
