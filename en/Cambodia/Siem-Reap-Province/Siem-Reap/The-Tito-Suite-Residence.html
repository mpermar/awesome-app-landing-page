Description

Featuring free WiFi throughout the property, The Tito Suite Residence is set in Siem Reap, 500 metres from Pub Street.

The hotel has a year-round outdoor pool and views of the pool, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

Angkor Wat is 7 km from The Tito Suite Residence, while Artisans D'Angkor is 200 metres from the property.

The nearest airport is Siem Reap - Angkor International Airport, 8 km from The Tito Suite Residence.

The property offers complimentary two-way airport transfers. Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and is fitted with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Some units feature a seating area for your convenience. Some rooms have views of the garden or city.

Every room is fitted with a private bathroom fitted with a bath or shower and bidet.

Extras include bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries. There is a hairdresser's at the property.

Free use of bicycles, bike hire and car hire are available at this hotel and the area is popular for cycling.
