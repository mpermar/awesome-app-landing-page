Description

Featuring Khmer-style wooden houses on stilts, Malu Khmer Villa offers accommodations away from the hustle and bustle of Siem Reap.

It boasts a private pool fitted with a balcony overlooking views of the garden and another outdoor swimming pool.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the in-house restaurant. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

Pub Street, Old Market and Angkor Night Market are 1. 6 km from Malu Khmer Villa, while Angkor Wat is 8 km away.

The Royal Residence and Angkor National Museum are 3 km from the property. The nearest airport is Siem Reap - Angkor International Airport, 10 km from the property.

Individually-designed with historic significance, each wooden house features local artworks and carefully-restored furnishings.

Rooms have a private balcony and are fitted with a TV, minibar and a safe. En suite bathrooms include a bathtub, separate shower facilities, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for currency exchange, tour arrangements, ticketing and concierge services.
