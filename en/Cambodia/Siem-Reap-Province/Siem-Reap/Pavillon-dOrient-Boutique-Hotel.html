Description

A 15-minute drive from Angkor Wat, this hotel is housed in a colonial-style mansion.

It offers an outdoor saltwater pool and rooms with garden or pool views. Free WiFi is available in all rooms.

Fitted with wooden floors, rooms at Pavillon d'Orient Boutique-Hotel feature a blend of Asian and Art Deco furnishings.

Offering a private balcony, each room comes with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The hotel is within a 10-minute drive from The Old Market and 20-minute drive from Angkor International Airport.

Guests can relax with a massage treatment at Pavillon d'Orient’s spa. The tour desk can arrange for hot air balloon tours, cultural shows or day trips to Siem Reap City’s attractions.

Le Planteur Restaurant serves Cambodian and European dishes in an open-air setting.

Snacks are available by the poolside. In-room dining is possible. Upon arrival, guests enjoy welcome drink and fruit, cold towel as well as free gift.
