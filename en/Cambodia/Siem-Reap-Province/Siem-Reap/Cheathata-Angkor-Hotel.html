Description

Cheathata Angkor Hotel is a unique property located within the city center, a minute's walk to the popular Pub Street and Night Market.

Guests have access to the property's rooftop pool overlooking panoramic sunset views and can enjoy meals at the in-house restaurant.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. Cheathata Angkor Hotel is 7 km from Siem Reap International Airport 9 km from Tonle Sap Lake.

The property offers complimentary pickup services from the airport or bus station.

The air-conditioned rooms at Cheathata Angkor Hotel feature private balconies, polished wooden flooring and tasteful furnishings.

Each comes with a flat-screen satellite TV and minibar. Private bathrooms are equipped with bathtubs and hot shower facilities.

Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for currency exchange, tour arrangements, ticketing and concierge services.

Pampering massage services are also available for a lazy afternoon. Cheathata provides free parking.

The Krawan Dining Room serves a wide selection of Khmer and international dishes.
