Description

Khmer Mansion Residence has a restaurant, outdoor swimming pool, a fitness centre and bar in Siem Reap.

Each accommodation at the 4-star hotel has pool views, and guests can enjoy access to a garden and to a terrace.

The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.

The rooms at the hotel have air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, DVD Player, and a private bathroom with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

All guest rooms will provide guests with a desk and a kettle. Khmer Mansion Residence offers a buffet or à la carte breakfast.

There is a tour desk and bike rentals are available, while the business centre has a fax machine and photocopier.

Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Pub Street, The Happy Ranch Horse Farm and Angkor Night Market.

The nearest airport is Siem Reap - Angkor International, 12 km from Khmer Mansion Residence, and the property offers a free airport shuttle service.
