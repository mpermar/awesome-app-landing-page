Description

Naga Gate Boutique Hotel greets guests with a welcome drink, cold towel, and fresh fruit skewer.

It features an outdoor swimming pool, a bar, and also offers on-site cooking classes at the all-day dining restaurant.

Added convenience include a range of complimentary services, such as use of bicycles to explore the surrounding areas, daily shuttle service to the city, roundtrip airport transfer, and hi-speed WiFi access throughout the property.

All rooms come with a seating area with a flat-screen TV and satellite channels where guests can relax.

The bathroom comes with free toiletries, a hairdryer, bathrobes and slippers. Selected rooms also has a spa bath and views of the pool.

Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk to arrange tours, process currency exchange, ticketing and concierge services.

In-house massage treatments are also available for an additional charge. The in-house open-air restaurant serves up local Cambodian cuisine and international dishes.

Naga Gate Boutique Hotel is located 2. 5 km from Wat Damnak and Siem Reap Pub Street.

The Old and Central Markets are about 3 km from the property while the famous Angkor Temple is 8 km away.
