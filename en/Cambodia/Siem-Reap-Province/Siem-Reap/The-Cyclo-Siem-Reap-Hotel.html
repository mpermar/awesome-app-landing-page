Description

All guest rooms has air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a fridge, a electric tea pot, a bath and a desk. The private bathroom is equipped with a bidet and free toiletries. At the hotel each room includes a seating area.

A continental breakfast is served daily at the property.

Bike rental and car rental are available at this hotel and the area is popular for cycling.

Speaking English and Khmer at the reception, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical guidance on the area.

Angkor Wat is 3.6 miles from The Cyclo Siem Reap Hotel. Angkor International Airport is 5 miles away.
