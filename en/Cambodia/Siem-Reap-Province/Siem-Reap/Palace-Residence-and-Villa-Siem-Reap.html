Description

Featuring 5-star villas, the Palace Residence boasts tennis courts, an outdoor pool and spa.

Offering free WiFi in rooms, villas feature a daily breakfast and a free minibar.

Palace Residence & Villa Siem Reap is a 15-minute drive from Siem Reap International Airport.

It is a 10-minute drive from the world-class 18-hole golf course at Angkor Golf Resort.

Situated in garden landscapes, luxurious modern Cambodian-style villas feature spacious layouts and fine wood furnishings.

Comfortable seating areas have sofa sets and cable TV. Villas have patios as well as indoor and outdoor bathing areas.

Bedroom slippers are available for your convenience. The resort offers free airport transfers and a free shuttle service to downtown every evening from 19:00 - 23:00.

A free daily chauffeur service is also available. Parking and bicycle rental are provided for free at Palace Residence.

Relaxing massages and body treatments can be enjoyed in the spa pavilions. Regular workouts can be maintained at the fitness centre and there is a tennis court available.
