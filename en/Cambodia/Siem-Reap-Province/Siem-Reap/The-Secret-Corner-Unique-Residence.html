Description

Nestled within private gardens in the corner of Svay Dangkum village, The Green Corner Unique Residence offers accommodation away from the hustle and bustle of Siem Reap.

It features an outdoor swimming pool and guests can enjoy meals at the in-house restaurant or have a drink at the bar.

Free WiFi is available in all guest rooms. Staff here are able to converse in Chinese, Khmer, Japanese, French and English.

Pub Street is 900 metres from The Green Corner Unique Residence, while Angkor Wat is 7 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Siem Reap - Angkor International Airport, 7 km from The Green Corner Unique Residence.

The property offers complimentary airport transfers and shuttle services to the city centre.

All air-conditioned rooms are individually-designed with post-modern Khmer furnishings and antique pieces.

A flat-screen TV with cable channels, as well as a laptop are featured. Some rooms include a seating area where you can relax.

A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms and open up to views. Rooms come with a private bathroom.

You will find a kettle in the rooms. Extras include bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries.

Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for tour arrangements, currency exchange, ticketing and concierge services.

Bike hire is available at this hotel and the area is popular for cycling. The in-house restaurant serves up a variety of Khmer dishes and international dishes from 07:00 to 21:00.

Guests can sip on cocktails at Sunset Bar while enjoying panoramic views overlooking the village.
