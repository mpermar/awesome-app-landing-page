Description

Located in Siem Reap, HARI Residence & Spa features a spa, an outdoor pool and a fitness centre.

Just a 5-minute walk from the Night Market and the Old Market, guests can enjoy free WiFi in all areas.

Hari Residence & Spa is only a 10-minute drive from Royal Residence and Park and a 15-minute drive from the world famous Angkor Wat Temple.

Siem Reap International Airport is just 8 km away. The property offers complimentary airport pickup services.

Overlooking views of the garden and the city, all air-conditioned rooms are fitted with a flat-screen cable TV, laptop safe and tea/coffee making facilities.

All units are equipped with a private bathroom fitted with a shower. Extras include bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries.

Guests can enjoy a relaxing massage or make use of a sauna at the property. Sightseeing tours can be arranged at the tour desk.

The on-site Cafe House serves a selection of Asian, European and local Khmer dishes.

Snacks and drinks are served at Lobby Lounge and Poolside Bar. A 24-hour room service is available.
