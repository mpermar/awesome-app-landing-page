Description

Set in Siem Reap, Hillocks Hotel & Spa boasts an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre.

The hotel has a barbecue and guests can enjoy a meal at the in-house restaurant or a drink at the bar.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. It is a 6-minute drive from the vibrant Pub Street, Old Market and Angkor Night Market.

Angkor Wat is also a 20-minute drive away from Hillocks Hotel & Spa, while Angkor Trade Centre is a 5-minute drive away.

Siem Reap - Angkor International Airport is a 21-minute drive from the property. The hotel offers complimentary pickups from the airport or bus station and shuttle services to the city centre.

Air-conditioned rooms include a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and an electric kettle.

Some units include a seating area where you can relax. A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms.

Every room has a private bathroom equipped with a bath and bidet. For your comfort, you will find bathrobes, toothbrushes and slippers.

Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for currency exchange, tour arrangements and luggage storage.

A selection of activities are offered in the area, such as golfing, horse riding and cycling.

Union Pay is accepted at the property. The property's in-house restaurant, Green Hill serves up a variety of local Khmer cuisine as well as Asian and Western dishes.
