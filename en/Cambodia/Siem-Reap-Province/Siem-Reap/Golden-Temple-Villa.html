Description

The rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. All rooms come with a private bathroom with a shower, free toiletries and a hair dryer. All rooms will provide guests with a desk and a electric tea pot.

Breakfast is available every morning, and includes buffet, American and Asian options. There's an in-house restaurant, which specializes in American cuisine.

Golden Temple Villa has a terrace.

Ironing facilities, and a business center are available, as well as a 24-hour front desk.

Pub Street is an 8-minute walk from the accommodation, while King's Road Angkor is 0.6 miles away. Angkor International Airport is 4.3 miles from the property.
