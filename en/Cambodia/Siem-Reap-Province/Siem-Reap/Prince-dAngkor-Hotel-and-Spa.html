Description

Prince D'Angkor Hotel & Spa sits in Siem Reap’s Old French Quarter, a 10-minute walk from Pub Street, Old Market and the Angkor Night Market.

Featuring traditional Cambodian décor, guests enjoy access to the outdoor saltwater pool, spa baths, sauna/steam room and fitness centre.

Free WiFi is offered in all of the resort's rooms. Prince D'Angkor Hotel & Spa is less than 5 minutes’ walk to shops, restaurants and bars.

Siem Reap Angkor International Airport is around 15 minutes’ drive away. The property offers complimentary one-way transfer services from the airport or bus station.

Featuring wooden flooring and Khmer-style furnishings, the air-conditioned rooms at Prince D'Angkor Hotel & Spa are fitted with an electric kettle, slippers and dental kits.

Bathrooms are equipped with a bathtub and a separate shower. Some rooms offer a private balcony while all rooms feature a choice of either views of the pool or city.

Rooms feature a flat-screen satellite TV with international channels. Guests can enjoy a massage at Opal Health Club & Spa, which also offers a sauna, steam room and gym.

The hotel offers the convenience of a 24-hour front desk providing tour arrangements, luggage storage and currency exchange.

Staff are able to converse in English, French, Japanese and Chinese. The property's in-house restaurant, Blue Diamond serves up a selection of Khmer dishes and feature an international buffet, while Emerald Grill Room offers fine dining.

Afternoon tea can be enjoyed at the Sapphire Piano Lounge, with live music in the evenings.
