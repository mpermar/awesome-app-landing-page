Description

Les Bambous Luxury Hotel is a family-run hotel that is a 10-minute drive from Siem Reap International Airport, it is nestled within a 2000m² tropical garden and features a restaurant and air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi.

Guests enjoy access to the outdoor swimming pool, the spa tub and sauna. Les Bambous Luxury Hotel is a 5-minute drive to the Old Market and Pub Street.

It takes 15 minutes by car from Les Bambous Luxury Hotel to Angkor Wat Temple. The hotel offers complimentary two-way airport transfers and shuttle services to the city centre.

The ceilings of spacious rooms are decorated with hand-painted art pieces and equipped with a cable TV and minibar.

An en suite bathroom with hot water shower or brass bathtub and hairdryer are included.

Some rooms include a separate seating area and a private sauna. Views of the garden or the pool can be enjoyed from all rooms.

Free bottled water, tea and coffee and toiletries are provided. Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for tour arrangements, currency exchange, ticketing and concierge services.

Car and bicycle rentals are also available to explore the region. Alternatively, enjoy a massage in the room or by the pool.

The property offers use of local mobile phones for your convenience. Situated in the garden, KodeLock Restaurant serves menu of Khmer, Asian and Western dishes.

The restaurant also offers Boodle Fight, a unique way of eating in Cambodia using bare hands.

A variety of beers, spirits, cocktails and wine are served at bar and private rooftop bar.
