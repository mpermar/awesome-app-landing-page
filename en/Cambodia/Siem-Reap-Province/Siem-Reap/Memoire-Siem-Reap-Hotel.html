Description

Situated only a minute's walk from the Angkor Market and a 3-minute drive by Tuk Tuk to the vibrant Pub Street, Memoire Siem Reap Hotel features an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre.

Guests can enjoy meals at the in-house restaurant or have a drink at the Artist Lounge.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property and there is free parking on-site.

Memoire Siem Reap Hotel is a short 10-minute drive from Siem Reap-Angkor International Airport and the famous Angkor Wat.

The property offers complimentary airport transfer services. Fitted with tiled flooring and traditional Khmer decor, rooms are painted with colour choices infusing the local charm.

Every room is equipped with a satellite TV and a minibar. Some rooms come with a private balcony overlooking the pool.

Memoire Siem Reap Hotel's restaurant serves a selection of local and Western dishes.

For those who would like to dine in, room service is available. Transport rentals are provided at the property.

For convenience, the front desk opens 24 hours. Memoire Siem Reap Hotel's tour desk offers assistance with visits to local attractions.

Guests may make use of the business centre, or relax with a massage. Alternatively, they can visit the souvenir shop to buy gifts for their loved ones.
