Description

Centrally located in the Old French Quarter, Chateau d'Angkor La Residence offers accommodation in Siem Reap.

It features an outdoor saltwater swimming pool and guests can enjoy meals at the in-house restaurant.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property and complimentary private parking is provided.

The residence is a 10-minute walk to the famous Old Market (Phsar Chas) and the lively Pub Street.

It is 200 metres to the Royal Residence, while the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Angkor Wat Temple is 6 km away.

Siem Reap International Airport is 7 km from the property. Complimentary pickup services from the airport or bus station are available.

Featuring a balcony with views of the city, air-conditioned suites include a separate seating area with sofa and flat-screen satellite TV, a dining area, and a kitchen fully equipped with a fridge and kitchenware.

For guests' convenience, an electric kettle, slippers and dental kit are provided in the room.

En suite bathrooms come with a hairdryer and free toiletries. A washing machine is available.

Chateau d'Angkor La Residence operates a 24-hour front desk that provides tour arrangements, luggage storage and a business centre.

Guests can also indulge in massage treatments. Union Pay is accepted at the property.

The in-house restaurant serves a selection of Cambodian cuisine and Western dishes.
