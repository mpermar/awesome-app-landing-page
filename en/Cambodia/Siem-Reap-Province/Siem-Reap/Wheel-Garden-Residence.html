Description

The rooms has air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a electric tea pot, a bidet, free toiletries and a desk. Rooms come with a private bathroom. All rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

Continental and à la carte breakfast options are available daily at the hotel.

Wheel Garden Residence has a terrace. Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Siem Reap, like cycling.

Non-stop guidance is available at the reception, where staff speak Khmer and English.

Popular points of interest near Wheel Garden Residence include Angkor National Museum, Apsara Theatre and Kravan Temple. The nearest airport is Angkor International Airport, 3.7 miles from the property.
