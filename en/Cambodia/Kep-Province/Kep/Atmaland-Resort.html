Description

Nestled away amidst the lush greeneries of Kep National Park Hillside, Atmaland Resort is just 1 km away from the clear waters of Kep Beach.

It offers modern rooms and houses a restaurant. Featuring its own private terrace with seating area, air-conditioned rooms are fitted with a wardrobe.

Attached bathrooms include hot shower facilities and free toiletries. Atmaland Resort’s front desk can assist with travel arrangements, laundry services, luggage storage and room service.

Breakfast can also be served in the room upon request. Meeting facilities are also provided.

Guests can dine in the in-house restaurant which serves an array of Cambodian cuisine.

The property is just a 10-minute walk to the popular Phsar Kdam, (Crab Market) and a 150 km away to Phnom Penh International Airport.
