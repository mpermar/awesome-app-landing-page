Description

Enjoying beautiful sea views, Samanea Beach Resort & Spa provides charming accommodation in Kep.

It offers an outdoor pool, a private beach area and free internet access. The villas are spacious and elegantly furnished featuring classic Khmer d√©cor.

Each is fitted with a private terrace, air conditioning and a separate dining area.

They all have comfortable sofas and tea/coffee making facilities. Stylish bathrooms feature an outdoor bathtub and premium toiletries.

Staff at the tour desk can arrange for various activities including a cooking class with a professional chef, sightseeing trips with a local guide including visits to Bokor National Park.

The property houses a business centre and a souvenir shop. Both local Khmer-style and Western cuisines will be served at the restaurant.

Private dinners can be arranged by the beach accompanied by sea views or enjoyed in the villas.

Samanea Beach Resort & Spa is placed 72 km from Sihanoukville International Airport.
