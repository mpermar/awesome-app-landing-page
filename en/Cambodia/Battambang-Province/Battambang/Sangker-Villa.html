Description

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool and free WiFi access, Sangker Villa offers accommodation in Battambang, just a 2-minute walk from Sangker River.

It offers a complimentary pick-up service from the bus station at Nat Market, situated 200 metres away.

Sangker Villa is a 3-minute drive from Boeung Chhouk Market and an hours drive from Sampov Mountain.

Rooms are air-conditioned, and feature a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a minibar, an electric kettle with tea/coffee facilities and an en suite bathroom with toiletries and hot and cold shower facilities.

Sangker Villa houses a bar serving a wide selection of drinks and beverages and an in-house French restaurant.

Travel and ticketing arrangements can be made at the tour desk. The 24-hour reception staff can assist with luggage storage and free private parking services.
