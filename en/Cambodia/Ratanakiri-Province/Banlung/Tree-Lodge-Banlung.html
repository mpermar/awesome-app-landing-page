Description

Featuring views of the mountain, Tree lodge offers wooden and bamboo bungalows located approximately 1 km from the centre of Banlung.

Guests can enjoy meals at the on-site restaurant. Free private parking is provided on site.

All units include a seating area for guests to relax after a busy day. Rooms at Tree lodge come with a fan and a private bathroom.

A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms. You will find a 24-hour front desk at Tree lodge.

The property offers complimentary pickup services from the bus station. The area is popular for hiking.
