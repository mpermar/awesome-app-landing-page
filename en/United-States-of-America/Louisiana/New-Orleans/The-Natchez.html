Description

The rooms come with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a coffee machine, a shower, free toiletries and a closet. The rooms come with a private bathroom, and certain rooms will provide you with a balcony. Guest rooms are equipped with a seating area.

Guests can use the hot tub.

Lafayette Cemetery is 1.7 miles from the hotel. The nearest airport is Louis Armstrong New Orleans International Airport, 12 miles from the property.

Downtown New Orleans is a great choice for travelers interested in seafood, city trips and cafes.
