Description

A seating area with a sofa bed and a flat-screen TV is furnished in all studios at SpringHill Suites by Marriott Lafayette South at River Ranch. The modern suites feature warm colors; a microwave, mini-fridge and coffee maker are included for convenience.

The River Ranch SpringHill Suites offers an on-site business center and a fitness room for guest use. Meeting facilities are also available.

This hotel is 3.5 miles from The Mall of Acadiana, and 6.5 miles from Oakbourne Country Club. The Zoo of Acadiana is an 18-minute drive away.
