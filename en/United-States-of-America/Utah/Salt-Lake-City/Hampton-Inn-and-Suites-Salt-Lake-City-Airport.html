Description

Area attractions, including Brigham Young Historic Park can be found only a short distance from the Hampton Inn and Suites Salt Lake City Airport. A number of top-rated ski resorts, including Snowbird and Deer Valley are also within driving distance.

Guests staying at the Salt Lake City Airport Hampton Inn and Suites can enjoy 24-hour access to the on-site fitness center as well as a seasonal heated outdoor swimming pool. The hotel also features a free daily hot breakfast along with free high-speed internet access.
