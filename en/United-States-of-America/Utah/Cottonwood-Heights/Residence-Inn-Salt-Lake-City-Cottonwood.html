Description

The spacious suites at Residence Inn Salt Lake City Cottonwood offer free Wi-Fi and a 37-inch flat-screen cable TV with HBO. They are furnished with separate living and dining areas.

Cottonwood Residence Inn Salt Lake City includes a gym and a business center. Guests can use the barbecue facilities and laundry facilities.

A daily buffet breakfast and an evening reception with beer and wine are provided by the hotel for all guests.

Old Mill Golf Course is 1.4 miles from the Salt Lake City Residence Inn Cottonwood. The hotel is located outside the Wasatch National Forest.
