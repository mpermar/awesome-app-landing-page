Description

Located in St. George, Utah, this hotel features an indoor/outdoor pool, a hot tub, a fitness centre and a games room.

Tuacahn Center For the Arts is 14 km away. A free hot breakfast is offered daily.

Rooms at Red Lion Hotel St. George feature free WiFi and a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Each room comes with a private bathroom with a free toiletries and a hairdryer. Green Iguana serves Mexican cuisine Monday through Saturday.

Red Lion Hotel St. George has laundry facilities on site. Dixie Center Convention Center is 3 km from this hotel.

The Shoppes at Zion are 5 minutes’ drive away. Snow Canyon State Park is 16 km away.
