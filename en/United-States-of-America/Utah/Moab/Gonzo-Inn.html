Description

A private balcony or patio is featured in each decorative room of the Gonzo Inn. Cable TV, a microwave and a fridge are also included.

Breakfast at the Gonzo includes fresh fruit, oatmeal and cinnamon swirl bread. An espresso bar is also open with a variety of drinks.

Bikes can be stored at the inn, with a wash and repair station in the parking lot. A guest laundromat is also available.

The entrance to Arches National Park is 5 miles from the Gonzo Inn. The Moab Adventure Center is just a 3 minute walk away.
