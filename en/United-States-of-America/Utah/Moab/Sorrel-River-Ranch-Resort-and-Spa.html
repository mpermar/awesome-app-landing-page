Description

Featuring 160 acres of land, Sorrel River Ranch Resort & Spa is located along the Colorado River. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

Offering mountain or river views, each studio and suite has a private patio or balcony, as well as a kitchenette, dining area and a satellite TV. Bathrooms feature bathrobes and signature bath products. Select rooms have a fireplace and a spa bath.

Guests can relax in a seasonal outdoor pool and hot tub, enjoy a massage at the on-site spa or take advantage of the fitness center. Other activities include lawn games, tennis, basketball and daily hikes. There are is also a petting zoo, live music and nightly bonfires.

An on-site adventure center is available to assist guests with booking horseback riding, river rafting and more.

The River Grill serves farm-to-table cuisine with both indoor and outdoor dining options. The Epic Bar is open for evening drinks.

Arches National Park, as well as the town of Moab are both 30 minutes’ drive from the property and Canyonlands National Park is 55 minutes’ drive away.
