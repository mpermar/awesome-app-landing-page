Description

Rooms at Montage Deer Valley provide a patio or balcony, a seating area with a fireplace, a flat-screen cable TV with pay-per-view channels. Each private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer. Select rooms have mountain or garden views.

Apex features fine Continental cuisine for breakfast, lunch and dinner at Montage Deer Valley Park City. Daly’s Pub & Rec offers pub fare and cocktails for lunch and dinner and guests can enjoy an espresso bar at Buzz Coffee Shop. Asian cuisine is served at Yama Sushi and Vista Lounge offers alpine cuisine and live entertainment. Burgers and Bourbon serves American cuisine and cocktails.

Guests can take advantage of the spa treatments offered at Spa Montage, including massages, manicures and pedicures. A transfer service within Park City is also available at Park City Montage Deer Valley.

Ruby Express ski elevator is 650 feet and Empire Express ski elevator is 650 feet from the resort. Salt Lake City International Airport is 27 mi from Montage Deer Valley.

Deer Valley is a great choice for travelers interested in family-friendly trips, food and mountains.
