Description

Guests at the Holiday Inn Express West Valley City can begin each day with a free breakfast buffet or with a cup of coffee from in-room coffeemakers. A modern business center, complete with free wireless internet access is also available on-site.

Area attractions, including the Valley Fair Mall and Temple Square can be found a short distance from the West Valley City Holiday Inn Express. The Salt Palace Convention Center and the Hale Center Theatre are also nearby.
