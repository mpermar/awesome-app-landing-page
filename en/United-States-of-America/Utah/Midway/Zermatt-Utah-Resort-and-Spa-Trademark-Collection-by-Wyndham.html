Description

A flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom are available in each room. Select rooms include a sitting area to relax in after a busy day. Extras include free toiletries and a hairdryer.

There is free shuttle service and gift shop at the property.

This resort has a ski pass sales point and ski equipment and bike rental are available. There are lots of activities in the area, such as skiing and biking. Salt Lake City is 46.2 miles from Zermatt Utah Resort and Spa, and Sandy is 48 miles from the property. Salt Lake City International Airport is 51.3 miles away.
