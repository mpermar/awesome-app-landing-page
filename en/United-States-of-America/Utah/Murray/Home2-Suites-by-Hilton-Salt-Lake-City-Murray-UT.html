Description

Located directly across the street from Intermountain Medical Center, Home2 Suites by Hilton Salt Lake City-Murray, UT features an outdoor pool.

Free WiFi access is available in all guest rooms. A flat-screen cable TV, a work desk and a seating area are included in each air-conditioned room at this hotel.

Complete with a microwave, the dining area has a microwave, a refrigerator and a coffee machine.

The private bathrooms come with a hairdryer and free toiletries. Guests can work out in the fitness centre at Salt Lake City-Murray, UT Home2 Suites by Hilton.
