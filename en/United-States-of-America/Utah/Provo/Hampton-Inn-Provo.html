Description

Guest rooms provide desks and seating areas. They have microwaves and refrigerators and also include coffee makers and hairdryers. Rooms with sofa beds are available.

Provo Hampton Inn guests can access an on-site gym and business center. The hotel serves breakfast and has a hot tub and meeting facilities.

Provo Bay is within one mile of Hampton Inn. The hotel is 3 miles from Brigham Young University and 19 miles from Timpanogos Cave National Monument.
