Description

Offering free WiFi, Four Seasons Resort Oahu at Ko Olina features guest rooms with a 65-inch flat-screen HDTV. All rooms include a Four Seasons Bed with your choice of plush, signature or firm mattress topper, twice daily housekeeping, a private balcony, a deep soaking tub and a walk-in glass shower.

Guests can enjoy 24-hour in-room dining at Four Seasons Resort Oahu at Ko Olina. A fitness center is also available.

Ko Olina Marina and Ko Olina Golf Club are just 5 minutes’ drive from Four Seasons Resort Oahu at Ko Olina. The Arizona and Battleship Missouri Memorial at Pearl Harbor are 27 minutes’ drive away from this resort.

Ko'Olina Resort is a great choice for travelers interested in swimming pools, scenery and sunsets.
