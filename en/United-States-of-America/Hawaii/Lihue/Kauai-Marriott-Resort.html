Description

Guest rooms include a TV with cable channels, a coffee machine, and a refrigerator. The rooms provide a view of the ocean or the hotel's gardens. Select rooms offer a balcony.

Guests can enjoy the hot tub or the well-equipped fitness center. A full-service business center is also available.

Kukui's on Kalapaki Beach at the Marriott Kauai serves international cuisine for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Live music is available on Friday and Saturday nights. Guests can also enjoy sushi at Toro Tei or fresh seafood and ocean views at the casual Duke's restaurant.
