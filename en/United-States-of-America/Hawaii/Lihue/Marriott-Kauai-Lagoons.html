Description

Each spacious villa at the Marriott Kauai Lagoons is furnished with a full kitchen and multiple bathrooms. All villas are decorated with island-styles and include multiple flat-screen TVs, free Wi-Fi and an in-unite washer/dryer. A separate dining area and living room with sofa bed are also included.

Free access to the outdoor pools and jacuzzi is provided to all guests of the Kauai Lagoons Marriott. Barbecue facilities and free on-site parking are also available.

Free shuttle services are provided to Kauai Island Airport, just 7 minutes away. Huleia national Wildlife Refuge is 4.3 miles from the resort.
