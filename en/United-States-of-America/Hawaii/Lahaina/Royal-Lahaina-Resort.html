Description

Free WiFi and a TV with cable channels are featured in each guest room at Royal Lahaina Resort. A coffee machine is provided in all of the air-conditioned accommodations. All rooms with attached bathroom include toiletries.

Guests at Royal Lahaina Resort can enjoy dining at the full-service restaurant, an ice cream parlor, and a coffee shop. The resort offers flower lei and puka shell making classes and features Hawaiian and Polynesian arts and crafts. Two bars are also available to guests.

Historic Old Town Lahaina and Lahaina Harbor are 3 mi away from Lahaina Resort. Visit nearby Haleakala Crater and see the sunrise from 1.9 mi above sea level. A free guest shuttle is offered to shops and restaurants at Whaler’s Village.

05/23/2017

Royal Lahaina Resort Get Away

"The property was perfect for a relaxing vacation. The pool wasn't fancy but it was nice and quiet and there were ample chairs out for pool users. Also, it was located right next to the restaurant. You could stroll over and get something to eat or drink and still keep an eye on your children if there were playing in the pool. The beach was fantastic. The water crystal clear and the sand was clean. There was a lot of room both on the shore and in the ocean. We had variable winds during our stay which meant there was hardly any wind on the beach.There is only one restaurant on property. They serve breakfast through dinner. We had all our meals there. My favorite was the BBQ Pork Ribs with Sweet Potato Fries--it was delicious, sweet yet tangy and fall of the bone tenderness! The drinks were also good--the Bloody Mary came with both olives and celery. Most bars just give you olives--but the celery makes the drink IMHO. The pina colada was delicious and had just the right amount of alcohol.If you are afraid of birds, then sit inside the restaurant and not on the lanai. There are birds flying around everywhere looking for table scraps. We saw sparrows, mynah birds, turtle doves, mountain doves, and there were also a few birds which look like cardinals but only their heads were red. The rest of their bodies were gray.I almost forgot about the Royal Scoop. It's an ice cream/coffee/snack shop. The ice cream was delicious. My favorite was the Kona Mud Pie. Kona coffee ice cream with pieces of chocolate mixed in.Housekeeping was friendly and our room was perfect when we checked in. We stayed in a hotel in San Jose and the housekeeping staff couldn't get the number of towels we needed right. The view of the ocean and beach were spectacular. Like most hotels, the noise from the hallway easily made its way into the room. Also, we could hear the sound of the waves crashing onto the shore at night."
