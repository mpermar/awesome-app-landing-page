Description

All rooms include a refrigerator and coffee machine. The rooms also include a private balcony with views of the grounds or ocean.

Guests at the Westin Maui can relax in the full-service spa or swim in one of the 5 outdoor pool, including an adults-only area and large waterslide. The property also has a children’s club.

The Spa and Resort Westin Maui features 4 restaurants and bars which serve traditional Hawaii cuisine. The restaurants range from the Tropica, which serves gourmet food to the Colonnade Café, which offers snacks and breakfast.

The Maui Westin is adjacent to Whalers’ Village and 10 minutes from the Ka’anapali Golf Resort and Ka’anapali Golf Course South. A free shuttle service is available to these locations and to downtown Lahaina, which is 3 mi from the property.
