Description

Bright, spacious rooms are equipped with TV with cable channels and a PlayStation 3. Select rooms feature a private balcony with views of the Pacific Ocean or gardens.

Guests are welcome to enjoy the sauna, hot tub and fitness center. The Puamana Day Spa, on the Ka’anapali coast, offers spa rituals honoring the island’s natural splendor. A salon, tennis courts, and a children’s activities program are also available. The property features a garden pool that lights up in the evening.Aston Kaanapali Shores also offers free weekly ukulele classes.

The Beach Club Restaurant serves American and local cuisine throughout the day in a casual setting. Live entertainment is held every Wednesday.

The Aston Kaanapali Shores is less than 0.6 mi from Honokowai Beach Park and 1.6 mi from Kapalua-West Maui Airport.
