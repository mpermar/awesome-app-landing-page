Description

Tropically decorated, the rooms and suites at Napili Kai Beach Resort provide a TV with cable channels, a CD player, and a DVD player. A refrigerator, a microwave, and a coffee machine are included. The private bathrooms include a hairdryer and free toiletries.

The Sea House Restaurant at the Beach Resort Napili Kai is an open-air Maui restaurant. It is open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. The Makai Hale offers snacks and lunch adjacent to the main pool. Coffee is available in the morning, and afternoon tea is available in the lobby to guests. There is also a weekly mai tai party, as well as barbecue facilities available for guest use.

Massages are available at the resort. Guests can exercise in the fitness center or take advantage of the tour desk. Two shuffleboard courts and a boccie ball court are also on site.

Kapalua is a great choice for travelers interested in snorkeling, scenery and sandy beaches.
