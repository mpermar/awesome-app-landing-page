Description

Spacious suites provide a private balcony with ocean or surrounding area views. Ka’anapali Beach Club provides each suite with a partial kitchen and a cable TV. Suites are decorated in classic design.

On-site activities include scuba diving and yoga. Guests can also fish and use the on-site BBQ facilities.

A café and restaurant are available at the resort. A snack bar is on-site as well.

Hawaii Ka’anapali Beach Club is 5 minutes' drive away from West Maui Airport. The Farmers Market Maui is 10 minutes' walk away.
