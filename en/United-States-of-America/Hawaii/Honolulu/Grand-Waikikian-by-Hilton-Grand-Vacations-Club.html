Description

The suites offer fully-equipped kitchens and spacious living and dining areas. Guests can relax in the soaking tub or watch one of the 2 flat-screen TVs. A washer, dryer and balcony are also included in each suite.

Guests of the Grand Waikikian by Hilton Grand Vacations Club have access to 4 outdoor pools, including a saltwater lagoon. Guests can also take advantage of the state-of-the-art fitness center and the supervised children's programs.

Guests at the Hilton Grand Waikikian can enjoy a fresh breakfast at the Rainbow Lanai or a romantic dinner at the Bali by the Sea restaurant.

Waikiki is a great choice for travelers interested in beach walks, surfing and shopping.
