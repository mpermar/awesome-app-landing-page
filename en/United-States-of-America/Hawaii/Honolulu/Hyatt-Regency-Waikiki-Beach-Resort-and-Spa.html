Description

Each room also includes a satellite TV, a desk, an iPod docking station and Na Ho'ola Spa toiletries in the bathroom at this Honolulu resort. Styled in dark wood furnishings, white colored linens are offered.

Enjoy a breakfast buffet at Shor Restaurant which later provides Waikiki's newest and largest dinner buffets, "The Buffet at Hyatt" with fresh seafood, made to order poke, steamed crab, sushi, prime rib along with local favorites like Kalua Pork and Short Ribs. Guests can enjoy indoor or outdoor seating.

Guests may also visit SWIM to enjoy creative cocktails and a menu featuring island pupus, grass fed burgers, fun family fare and "Aloha Waikiki" hula show on Wednesday nights.

The resort's new cultural center, Ho'okela, will provide guests with the unique experience of Hawaii in learning to dance the hula, play the ukulele, make a lei and other arts & crafts. Over 60 unique shops and boutiques are featured at Hyatt Regency Waikiki. Concierge services are offered. A 24-hour fitness center is onsite.

The award winning, Na Ho'ola Spa is onsite offering rejuvenating treatments reflecting the Hawaiian cultural art of healing.

Waikiki is a great choice for travelers interested in beach walks, surfing and shopping.
