Description

This property is a 8-minute walk from the beach. Nestled on 20 acres on Kaua'i's southern shoreline, Sheraton Kauai Resort offers 2 outdoor pools. This Poipu Beach resort is enhanced by Kaua‘i’s natural beauty, site seeing attractions and an array of nearby activities.

Each guest room at Sheraton Kauai Resort features amenities such as flat-screen TVs, in-room refrigerators and Shine by Bliss bath products.

A beach activities center is offered to guests of this resort. Cultural activities, Hawai'i-rooted cuisine, spa services, romantic wedding locations and a host of spacious meeting spaces are available.

Explore and enjoy the many enticing water front dining venues at the Sheraton Kauai Resort, which feature stunning views of the Pacific Ocean and Poipu Beach.

Poipu is a great choice for travelers interested in sunsets, nature and hiking.
