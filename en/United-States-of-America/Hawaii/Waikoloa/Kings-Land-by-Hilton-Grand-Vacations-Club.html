Description

All suites offer a fully equipped kitchen, a private balcony and a flat-screen TV. An en suite bathroom and a washer and dryer are also provided.

The Kings’ Land Bistro & Lounge, open from 08:00 until 19:00, serves an assortment of breakfast breads and pastries. The daily dining menu features salads, sandwiches, burgers and pizza. The Kings’ Land Pool Bar is open daily and offers 60 plasma TVs.

A fitness centre and activity centre with game tables, video game systems and movies are available for guest use. Golf opportunities are located nearby at championship golf courses. WiFi is complimentary throughout the common areas, pools and the Bistro Lounge.

Hilo International Airport is located 69 miles away from this resort. Holualoa Kona Coffee Plantation is a 45-minute drive away. Puako Petroglyph Park is a 10-minute drive away from Kings' Land by Hilton Grand Vacations Club.
