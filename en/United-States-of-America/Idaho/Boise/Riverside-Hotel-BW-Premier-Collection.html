Description

Featuring a flat-screen TV with extended cable, each spacious room at The Riverside Hotel includes tea and coffee making facilities. Comfortably furnished, all rooms provide a work desk and seating area.

Located on site The Riverside Hotel, the Riverside Grill offers a breakfast buffet, salad bar and full dinner menu. A variety of sweet treats are offered including drunken fruit tart, harvest moon carrot cake and ice cream baked potato. A complimentary breakfast buffet is included in qualifying room rates at the hotel. Options include coffee, tea, cereals, granola, yogurt, and fruit.

Showcasing lush greenery and walking trails, the Boise River Greenbelt is across the street from this hotel. The Boise Towne Square Mall is 10 minutes’ drive away.
