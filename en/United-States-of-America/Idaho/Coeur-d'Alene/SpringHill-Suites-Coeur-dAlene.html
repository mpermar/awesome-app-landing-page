Description

Featuring free Wi-Fi, each modern room provides 42" cable TV and a private bathroom at SpringHill Suites Coeur d'Alene. Coffee-making facilities, a microwave and a small fridge are also featured. Rooms are styled in modern white linens, wood furnishing and refreshing aqua blue accents.

Cereals, breads, juice and coffee are just some of the breakfast items provided daily. A patio with a fire pit and lounge seating is featured. A three hole putting green is available. A fitness center and business center are provided. Dry cleaning services are available near the 24-hour front desk at Coeur d'Alene SpringHill Suites.
