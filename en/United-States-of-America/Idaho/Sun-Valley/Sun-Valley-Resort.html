Description

Traditionally designed, each colorful guest room features a flat-screen cable TV with pay-per-view channels, a refrigerator, and air conditioning. The private bathrooms feature a marble sink and free toiletries.

Guests have several on-site dining options here, such as the Ram restaurant which serves gourmet cuisine, as well as Bald Mountain Pizza and Pasta which serves family-style cuisine. A fitness center, a business center, and a tennis court can all be found at the Sun Valley Inn.

Free Wifi access and free parking are available at the resort. Sun Valley Golf Course is 3 minutes’ drive from the property, and skiing at Bald Mountain is just 8 minutes’ drive away.
