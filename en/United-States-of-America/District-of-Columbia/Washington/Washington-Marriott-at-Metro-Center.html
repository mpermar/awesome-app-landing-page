Description

The Washington Marriott offers spacious accommodations featuring flat-screen TVs with internet access and a tea/coffee maker.

Guests can work out at the hotel’s fitness center that has cardiovascular equipment, treadmills and stair masters. Other services include laundry facilities.

Breakfast is served at Fire & Sage, which offers American dishes in the evening. The complex also houses a Starbucks.

East Potomac Park Golf Course and Washington Convention Center is less than 1.2 mi from the hotel.

Northwest is a great choice for travelers interested in city walks, culture and museums.
