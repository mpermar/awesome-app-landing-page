Description

A flat-screen TV with cable channels along with a work station are offered in each contemporary room at Avenue Suites. Suites feature a full kitchen.

Guests can workout in the fitness center or use the business center that offers fax and photocopying services. Laundry facilities are also provided.

Breakfast can be purchased a la carte each morning in the lobby area.

The Avenue Suites Washington, D.C., is a half mile from George Washington University. The White House is one mile away and Georgetown University is a 5 minute drive.

Northwest is a great choice for travelers interested in city walks, culture and walking.
