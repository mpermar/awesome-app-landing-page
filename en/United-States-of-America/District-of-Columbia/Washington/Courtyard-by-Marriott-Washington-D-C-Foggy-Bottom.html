Description

On-site dining for breakfast and lunch is available at The Bistro. Guests staying at the Courtyard by Marriott Washington, D.C./Foggy Bottom can also enjoy an indoor pool and fitness center.

Each room at the Foggy Bottom Courtyard by Marriott includes a flat-screen cable TV and city views of Washington, D.C. A refrigerator is also provided.

The Foggy Bottom underground train station is a 10-minute walk from the hotel.

Northwest is a great choice for travelers interested in city walks, culture and walking.
