Description

This property is a 14-minute walk from the beach. Holly Tree Resort features indoor and outdoor pools and a well-equipped gym. Just a 6-minute drive from Sea Gull Beach, it offers spacious studios with a fully equipped kitchenette.

Each of the warm colored studios provides a DVD player. A sofa bed is also offered.

The resort offers a DVD library and video arcade. There are basketball and racquetball courts along with a picnic area and charcoal grills.

Holly Tree Resort is less than 1 miles from the Atlantic Ocean. It is within 3 miles of the JFK Museum, Nantucket ferry and Bayberry Hill Golf Course.
