Description

The Hampton Inn and Suites Berkshires-Lenox is located close to historical sites, museums and many recreational activities. Jiminy Peak Ski Resort, the Tanglewood Music Center and the Colonial Theatre can all be found nearby.

Guests at the Berkshires-Lenox Hampton Inn and Suites can start each day with a free hot breakfast after a restful night of sleep on signature Cloud Nine beds. On-site, guests can also enjoy a modern fitness center, an indoor swimming pool and a cozy lobby featuring a fireplace.
