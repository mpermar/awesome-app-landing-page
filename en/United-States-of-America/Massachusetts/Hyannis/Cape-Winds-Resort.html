Description

A microwave and a fridge are included in each light and airy, fully-fitted kitchen. A TV features in the spacious, open-plan dining and living area.

Bedrooms have a neutral decor and a selection of toiletries is included in each private bathroom.

A fireplace features in the Clubhouse and there is also a games room and a fitness center at the Cape Winds Resort. BBQ facilities are also available.

Cape Cod Carousel & Funhouse Arcade is just 1.5 miles from the Cape Winds Resort. Hyannis Yacht Club is just over 2 miles away and Hyannis Whale Watcher Cruises are within 4 miles of the resort.
