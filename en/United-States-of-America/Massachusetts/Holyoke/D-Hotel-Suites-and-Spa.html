Description

Located near Interstate 91, this Holyoke hotel offers free WiFi and rooms equipped with cable TV.

Holyoke Country Club is adjacent to the property and Smith College is 6. 5 km away.

Plush bedding is offered in each traditionally decorated room at D. Hotel Suites & Spa.

Tea and coffee-making facilities are also included. Full hot breakfast is served each morning and it features hot coffee or tea along with fresh fruit and pastries.

A business centre that offers fax and photocopying services is provided for guests.

Laundry facilities are also included. D. Hotel Suites & Spa is 15. 3 km from Mount Holyoke College and Skinner Museum.
