Description

Nestled on Hancock, Massachusetts’ ski slopes, Jiminy Peak Mountain Resort offers a restaurant, adventure parks and ski-to-door access.

It spacious suites and apartments feature a flat-screen TV and DVD player. Each suite features a sofa bed and fully equipped kitchenette.

The one to two-bedroom apartments feature a fully equipped kitchen and 2 bathrooms.

Guests can swim in the outdoor pool or relax in the indoor hot tub. They can also workout in the gym or enjoy the tennis courts.

The hotel offers a ski shop, ski lessons and ski storage. On-site dining is available at John Harvard's Restaurant & Brewery and Christiansen’s Tavern.

Jiminy Peak Mountain Adventure Resort is within 8 miles of Balance Rock State Park and Pontoosuc Lake.

It is 11 miles from Berkshire Museum. The resort is powered by 100 percent renewable energy.

A wind turbine, solar field and co-generation facility all located on the western Massachusetts property makes the resort energy self-sufficient.
