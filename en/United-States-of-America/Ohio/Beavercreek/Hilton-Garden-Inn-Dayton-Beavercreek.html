Description

Every guest room at the Hilton Garden Inn Dayton/Beavercreek comes with a 32-inch flat-screen TV. A microwave, refrigerator and coffee maker are also provided.

Guests can enjoy the indoor swimming pool and hot tub at the Beavercreek Hilton Garden Inn. A free modern gym and free 24-hour business center with remote printing service are available.

The full-service Great American Grill at the Hilton Garden Inn Beavercreek serves breakfast, lunch and dinner. Evening room service, 24-hour convenience store and a lounge are available.
