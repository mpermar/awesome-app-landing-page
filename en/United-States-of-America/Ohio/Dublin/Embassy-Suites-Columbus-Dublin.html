Description

Guests at the Embassy Suites Columbus Dublin can start the morning with a free cooked-to-order breakfast or enjoy a cup of coffee from in-room coffeemakers. The hotel also features a free evening reception along with a fitness center and indoor swimming pool.

Popular points of interest, including the Columbus Zoo, the Ohio Expo Center and Ohio State University are only a short drive from the Columbus Dublin Embassy Suites. Many shopping centers and local restaurants are also nearby.
