Description

Only moments from the Hampton Inn and Suites Denver Tech Center, guests can find the offices of IBM, Pepsi and McDonald's. The nearby Greenwood Village Park offers numerous shops and gourmet restaurants. The Red Rocks Amphitheater as well as a number of scenic parks are also easily accessible.

With modern amenities, including free high-speed internet access and an on-site fitness center, every stay at the Denver Tech Center Hampton Inn is sure to be enjoyable. Guests can also take advantage of the free daily breakfast bar as well as the free weekday evening cocktail hour.
