Description

A flat-screen cable TV is offered in each room at Radisson Hotel Colorado Springs. A microwave and refrigerator are included. All rooms provide a sitting area.

Bistro Colorado, the hotel restaurant, serves sophisticated fine cuisine in a casual setting. The lounge offers a wide range of beers, wines and premium liquor.

A heated indoor pool, hot tub and terrace are provided for guest relaxation. A business center and fitness center are on site. A gift store and game room are also available at Colorado Springs Radisson Hotel.

Peterson Air Force Base is a 10 minute drive away. Seven Falls is a 20 minute drive away. Garden of the Gods is a 25 minute drive from Radisson Hotel Colorado Springs.
