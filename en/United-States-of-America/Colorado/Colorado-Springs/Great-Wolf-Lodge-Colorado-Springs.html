Description

Great Wolf Lodge Colorado Springs is a family-friendly resort that features a full on-site water park.

Guests can work up a sweat in the fitness centre and then grab a bite to eat at one of several on-site restaurants.

Shopping choices are aplenty at the Great Wolf Lodge Colorado Springs. The nearest airport is Colorado Springs Airport, 29 km from the property.

Complimentary WiFi is provided for all guests. Rooms feature a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

A refrigerator, coffee maker and microwave are provided as well. Nature-themed kid's suites offer bunk beds.

Select rooms feature upgraded amenities such as additional living space, a balcony and mountain views.

The on-site water park features 13 water slides and a 79,000 sq ft swimming pool.

Kids will enjoy activities such as a story time and games. Dining options include 3 full-service restaurants and several quick-service eateries.

Great Wolf Lodge Colorado Springs is 15 km from the United States Air Force Academy.
