Description

The rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom, while certain units at Hyatt House Denver Lakewood Belmar also feature a seating area. All rooms will provide guests with a closet and a coffee machine.

A buffet breakfast is served daily at the property.

Denver is 5 miles from the accommodation, while Meridian is 16 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Denver International Airport, 24 miles from Hyatt House Denver Lakewood Belmar.
