Description

Snow Flower Condos by Wyndham Vacation Rentals has apartments furnished with a gas fireplace, cable TV and balcony. Each kitchen includes a microwave, full refrigerator and stove. Providing the comforts of home, all apartments have a living room and dining area.

After a day of skiing or shopping, guests of the Snow Flower by Wyndham Vacation Rentals can relax in the outdoor hot tub or use the barbecue equipment. The resort has laundry facilities on site and free parking.

Downtown Steamboat Springs and The Tread of Pioneers Museum are both 3.5 miles from Snow Flower Resort. Haymaker Golf Course is a 5-minute drive away.
