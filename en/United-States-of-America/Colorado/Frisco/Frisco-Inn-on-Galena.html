Description

Guest rooms at the inn come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a bath or shower. Some rooms also feature a kitchen with a dishwasher. The rooms have a desk.

Frisco Inn on Galena offers a Full English/Irish or American breakfast.

The accommodation has a sun terrace. Guests can enjoy a variety of activities in the surrounding areas, including skiing, cycling and fishing.

Other services and facilities offered at Frisco Inn on Galena include a business center, newspapers and a fax machine and photocopier.

Ten Mile Recreation Pathway is half a mile from the inn. Eagle County Regional Airport is 43 miles away, and the property offers a free shuttle service.
