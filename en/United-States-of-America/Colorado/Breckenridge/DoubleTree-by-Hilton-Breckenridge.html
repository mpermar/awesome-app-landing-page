Description

A flat-screen TV, a mini-refrigerator and a work desk are included in all of the contemporary guest rooms at the DoubleTree by Hilton Breckenridge. Select rooms feature views of the Rocky Mountains.

A flat-screen TV with cable channels, as well as an iPod docking station are offered. Certain units feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day. DoubleTree by Hilton Breckenridge features free WiFi.

Traditional American cuisine is offered at the 9600 Grill, which servers breakfast, lunch and dinner. During the summer months guests can enjoy a signature cocktail on the restaurant’s outdoor deck.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including skiing and golfing. The hotel also offers ski equipment hire. Beaver Run SuperChair is 700 feet from DoubleTree by Hilton Breckenridge, while QuickSilver Super6 is 700 feet away. Eagle County Regional Airport is 48 miles from the property.
