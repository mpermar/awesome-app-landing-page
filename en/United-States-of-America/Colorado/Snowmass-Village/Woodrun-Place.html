Description

Located on Fanny Hill, Woodrun Place features one to four-bedroom villas with fully equipped kitchens, fireplaces and cable TVs. Woodrun Place offers guests ski-to-door access and a heated outdoor pool.

The villas provide washing machines and dryers. They also include free Wi-Fi, seating areas with sofa beds and dining areas.

Guests at Woodrun Place are offered a hot tub and sauna. It has on-site ski rentals and ski storage and also provides a gym and meeting rooms and 1 assigned parking space.

Snowmass Village’s Woodrun Place is one mile from Ajax Whitewater Inflatable Kayaks. It is 7 miles from Maroon Creek Club and 10 miles from Aspen Art Museum and Colorado Mountain College.
