Description

Offering mountain views, each suite also provides a seating area with a wood-burning fireplace and sofa bed, 2 flat-screen TVs and an private bathroom at Christie Lodge Avon. A kitchenette with a dishwasher, refrigerator and coffee-making facilities is also available.

A free transfer service to the base of the ski gondola is offered during ski season and to Beaver Creek during non-ski seasons at Avon Christie Lodge. There are 2 restaurants and a sporting goods store on site. Free parking is provided.

Vail Mountain is 9.3 mi from the lodge. Eagle Vail Golf Course is 10 minutes’ drive from Christie Lodge.
