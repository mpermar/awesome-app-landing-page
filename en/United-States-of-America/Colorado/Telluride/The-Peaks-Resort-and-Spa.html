Description

The accommodations at The Peaks are equipped with cable TV and free WiFi. They are furnished with large picture windows and glass-enclosed showers. An in-room safe and is also provided.

A variety of dining choices are available at this Telluride resort including Altezza Restaurant, serving cuisine made with local and organic ingredients. The Great Room & Deck offers a lounge atmosphere with live music and Bean Cafe is an on-site espresso coffee and pastry shop. Guests can enjoy a drink or an appetizer poolside at the Deep End Pool Bar during the summer months.

A full-service salon, oxygen room, and tanning booth are part of The Peaks Resort and Spa. Concierge services and valet parking are available.

Telluride Regional Airport is 6.8 mi from The Peaks Resort and Spa. Downtown Telluride is a 20-minute drive from the property.
