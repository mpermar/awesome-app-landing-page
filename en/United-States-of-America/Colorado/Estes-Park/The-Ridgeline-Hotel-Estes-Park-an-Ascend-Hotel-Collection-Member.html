Description

Surrounded by Rocky Mountain National Park and adjacent to Lake Estes, this Estes Park, Colorado hotel features an on-site restaurant and heated indoor pool.

Every room offers a 50-inch flat-screen cable TV. Downtown Estes Park is a 15 minutes' walk from the property, or a 5 minutes' drive.

Guests at The Ridgeline Hotel, an Ascend Hotel Collection Member can work out in the fitness centre.

They can also relax in the hot tub. Free high speed WiFi and a coffee maker are provided in the contemporary rooms.

Each room at The Ridgeline Hotel, an Ascend Hotel Collection Member also has a desk.

Poolside rooms with a balcony are available. Latitude 105 Alehouse, open for lunch and dinner and also offers daily happy hour specials.

There is a full service conference centre and banquet facilities. Guests can enjoy a game room with billiards, foosball and video games.

The entrance to Rocky Mountain National Park is 10 minutes' drive from the property and is less than 5 minute's drive to Estes Park Visitor Center.
