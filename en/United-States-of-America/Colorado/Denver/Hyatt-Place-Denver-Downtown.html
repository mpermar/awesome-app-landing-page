Description

The rooms at the hotel come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a bath or shower. All units at Hyatt Place Denver Downtown include air conditioning and a desk.

An American breakfast is available every morning at the property.

A business center and a fitness center are at guests' disposal at the accommodations. When guests need guidance on where to visit, the reception will be happy to provide advice.

Denver Performing Arts Complex is 1,950 feet from Hyatt Place Denver Downtown. The nearest airport is Denver International Airport, 19 mi from the hotel.
