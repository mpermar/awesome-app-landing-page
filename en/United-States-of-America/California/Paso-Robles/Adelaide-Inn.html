Description

Free Wi-Fi is available in all rooms at the Adelaide Inn, and a work desk is also furnished. A coffee machine, microwave, and small fridge are also available.

A fitness room, outdoor pool, and hot tub are all available for guest use at the Paso Robles Adelaide Inn. Laundry services and dry cleaning services are available.

Pianetta Winery, Anglim Winery, Parrish Family Vineyard and other Paso Robles wineries are within a 5 minute drive of the Adelaide Inn. The Downtown City Park is also a 5 minute drive away.
