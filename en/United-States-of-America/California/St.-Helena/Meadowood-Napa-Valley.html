Description

Meadowood Napa Valley offers an elegant on-site restaurant, wine tastings, and tennis lessons. It is 5 minutes' drive from downtown St Helena and the Meadowood Golf Club is on site.

A wood-burning fireplace and a balcony or patio are featured in each room. The marble bathrooms are furnished with a shower, and most rooms have a soaking tub.

Natural treatments and products are available at the spa. Guests can work out at the on-site fitness center or attend a fitness class. Other activities available for guests include croquet, tennis, golf, bicycling, hiking, cooking classes, and wine experiences.

Gourmet California cuisine is served for dinner at The Restaurant at Meadowood. The Restaurant Bar & Terrace features a full-service bar, fireplace, and outdoor seating.

Bothe-Napa Valley State Park is 5 mi from Meadowood Resort and Spa Napa Valley. Downtown Napa is less than 30 minutes' drive from the property.
