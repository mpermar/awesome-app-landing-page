Description

Comfort Inn & Suites Galt-Lodi North offers rooms with free WiFi. More than 50 wineries are within 15 minutes' drive of this hotel, including Woodbridge, Oak Ridge, Michael-David and others.

The hotel offers furnished guest rooms and suites. All spacious rooms come equipped with refrigerators, microwaves, coffee makers, hair dryers, irons, ironing boards and cable television.

In addition, some rooms have sofa sleepers. Some rooms feature whirlpool bathtubs.

Hotel amenities and features at this non-smoking hotel include free coffee, tea and hot chocolate, free local calls and free USA Today.

The breakfast includes eggs, meat, yogurt, fresh fruit, cereal and more. Guests Comfort Inn & Suites Galt-Lodi North will enjoy relaxing in the indoor heated pool and hot tub.

A fitness centre is also available. Business travelers will appreciate conveniences like the public computer with Internet access and a printer, along with access to copy and fax services.

Laundry facilities are located on the property. Dry cleaning service is available upon request.

Located 30 minutes to the east are the Jackson and Plymouth "Gold Country" historical areas and wineries.

The Sacramento Convention Center is just 20 minutes north, ARCO Arena sports, entertainment and special events venue is 25 minutes' away, and Elk Grove and Lodi are within 10 minutes from this California hotel.
