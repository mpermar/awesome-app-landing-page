Description

The Anaheim Marriott offers bright and stylishly decorated suites, all with a large desk and 2 cable TVs. Each unit comes with a mini-fridge, coffee maker and ironing facilities.

The on-site SunSpot restaurant serves American cuisine in a relaxed atmosphere. Guests can also indulge in the simple luxuries of the hotel’s own Starbucks café, located just off the lobby.

Anaheim Marriott Suites is situated 5 miles from Anaheim Hills Golf Course and 9.2 miles from Knotts Berry Farm. The interstate 5 is just 2 miles away.

Garden Grove is a great choice for travelers interested in beaches, shopping and relaxation.
