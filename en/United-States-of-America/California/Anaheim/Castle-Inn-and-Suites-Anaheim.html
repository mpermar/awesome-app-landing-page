Description

A flat-screen cable TV, a small refrigerator, and a microwave are available in all traditionally furnished rooms at the Castle Inn and Suites Anaheim. A coffee maker and work desk are provided.

Free parking is available for 1 vehicle per room. Laundry facilities are available at this Anaheim hotel and guests can pick up items at the gift shop.

The Castle Inn and Suites is 2 miles from the Angel Stadium of Anaheim. Huntington Beach is less than 20 miles away.
