Description

Each room is equipped with a refrigerator and coffee maker. All modern rooms are warmly decorated with dark wood furniture and include a work desk and safety deposit box.

Free access to the gym, hot tub and outdoor pool is provided to all guests of Anaheim Majestic Garden Hotel. The hotel has a games room with billiards and arcade games and a business center offering free WiFi.

The Garden Court Bistro and Bar at the Anaheim Majestic Garden Hotel serves American cuisine and cocktails. California Deli offers sandwiches and salads.

HG Miller Golf Course is just 4 miles from the hotel. Bowers Museum of Cultural Art is 5.5 miles away.
