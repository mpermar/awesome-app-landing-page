Description

Desert Palms Hotel & Suites Anaheim Resort features a refrigerator and microwave in every guest room. Cable TV and free Wi-Fi are also included in each room. Suites offer separate sitting areas.

Guests can use the gym or the 24-hour business center at the Anaheim Desert Palms. Disneyland tickets are available from the front desk. On-site dining options include the Oasis Kitchen and Bar.

The Anaheim Convention Center is across the street from the Desert Palms in Anaheim. The Honda Center entertainment and event venue is 10 minutes away.
