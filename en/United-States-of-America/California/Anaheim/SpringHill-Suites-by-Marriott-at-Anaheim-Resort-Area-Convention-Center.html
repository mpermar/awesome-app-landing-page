Description

A flat-screen TV with cable is provided in each air-conditioned suite at SpringHill Suites by Marriott at Anaheim Resort Area/Convention Center. A microwave and small refrigerator are included.

A 24-hour front desk is available at SpringHill Suites Anaheim. Snacks and beverages are available at the snack bar.

The Anaheim Convention Center is a 5-minute walk from the property. Los Angeles International Airport is a 40-minute drive from SpringHill Suites by Marriott at Anaheim Resort Area/Convention Center. Disney California Adventure Park is a 6-minute drive away.
