Description

Rooms at the Ojai Valley Inn are decorated in Spanish Colonial style and feature in-room coffee machines. Spa Ojai bathroom amenities, a flat-screen TV and a radio with iPod connection are also included. Some rooms offer a fireplace.

Spa Ojai welcomes guests with warm, exotic rooms featuring a variety of massages and treatments. A long list of mind and body fitness classes is also available. In addition to the two pools at the spa, the resort offers a large, outdoor family swimming pool with food and beverage service.

The Ojai tennis program can offer coaching advice or simply provide the facilities for some fun. Teen and childrenâ€™s programs are also open in a variety of activities.

The Inn at Ojai Valley is 7 miles from Lake Casitas and 17 miles from Pierpoint Bay.
