Description

Casa Ojai Inn feature tasteful and functional plantation-style decor. The spacious guest rooms boast large armoires containing 27-inch (68 cm) TVs with expanded cable services. Enjoy the desk space and individual climate controls.

Explore the Ojai area stress free with the Ojai Trolley Service. With a stop right outside the Casa, guests can visit the attractions of Ojai, Meiners Oaks and Mira Monte within minutes.
