Description

Cable TV, a small fridge and wine glasses are provided in each room at Roman Spa Resort. Cherry wood furnishings and original artwork are featured throughout the rooms. Free Wi-Fi is also included.

Guests can enjoy any of the outdoor mineral pools or indoor pools. Massages and body treatments are offered at Roman Spa, as well as 2 Finnish saunas.

Outdoor patios and picnic areas are set among the gardens of Roman Resort. Gas and charcoal grills are available for guests’ use.

A public computer area is located in the lobby and a library is also on site.

The Sharpsteen Museum is 200 feet away and the Old Faithful Geyser of California is 5 minutes’ drive away.

04/10/2017

Great geothermal pools, but no kids under 4 allowed

"We were a party of three adults and three kids (grandma came along) and booked two rooms. The geothermal pools were really great, but what was the highlight of the trip were two hanging hummingbird feeders that attracted so many hummingbirds all day long. I couldn't peel myself from them as you can see the birds so close that sometimes I think the flutter of their wings could touch my nose. The rooms were standard but very clean. The property was overall pleasant and enjoyable. There were a number of good restaurants within a three minute walk and all were decent. If you have a car and can drive a bit south to Rutherford there were a number of excellent, upscale but affordable (for upscale food) restaurants worth trying. Since we had little kids we didn't do wine tours but we did visit petrified forest and Bothe park. Both were good, but petrified forest cost almost $50 for all of us. You can actually see some of the petrified wood right by the entrance of the hotel if you are curious but want to save the money towards booze. Since we went in April the weather was perfect. Apparently in the summer it gets really really hot, like upwards of 90 f. Overall we enjoyed our stay and would come back."
