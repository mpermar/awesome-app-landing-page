Description

Set in Calistoga in the California Region, 1.4 mi from Sterling Vineyards, Indian Springs Resort & Spa features an outdoor pool and views of the garden. The resort has a spa center and hot tub, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Every room is fitted with a TV. Rooms are equipped with a private bathroom fitted with a bath or shower. Indian Springs Resort & Spa features free WiFi .

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property.

You can play tennis at this resort, and the area is popular for golfing. Castello di Amoros is 2.4 mi from Indian Springs Resort & Spa, while Indian Springs Mud Baths is 650 feet from the property. The nearest airport is Sacramento Airport, 54 mi from the property.
