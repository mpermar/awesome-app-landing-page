Description

Each rustic accommodations at this resort includes a fireplace and a balcony or patio. The kitchen is complete with an oven, a refrigerator, microwave, a coffee machine, and a dishwasher. A TV with satellite channels and a DVD player are available, as well as free WiFi.

Tennis courts, 2 outdoor pools, and free barbecue facilities are located on site for seasonal use. The gym has a variety of cardio machines and a mounted TV. Guests have access to free parking. Guests also benefit from direct shuttles to ski elevators as well as downtown Tahoe. During the summer months, the Kids Camp is free to guests.

A complimentary continental breakfast is available during the winter months.

The Aston Lakeland Village Beach and Mountain Resort is 1.2 mi from Harrah's Casino and 1 mi from Heavenly Mountain Ski Area.
