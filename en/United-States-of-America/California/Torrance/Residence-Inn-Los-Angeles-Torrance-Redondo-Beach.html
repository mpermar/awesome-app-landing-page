Description

Residence Inn Los Angeles Torrance/ Redondo Beach suites include a dining and seating area, with free Wi-Fi. The kitchen has a microwave and dishwasher.

The Residence Inn Torrance provides guest with a breakfast buffet and the hotel hosts an evening reception Tuesday, Wednesday and every other Thursday. Guests can also use the barbecue facilities to cook out. A free grocery shopping service is available.

The Residence Inn is 8.7 mi from the Redondo Beach Boardwalk. The town of Redondo Beach is 2.9 mi from the hotel.
