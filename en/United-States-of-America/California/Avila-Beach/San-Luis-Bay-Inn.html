Description

All guest rooms in the resort are equipped with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a kitchen, a dining area and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a bath. Every room includes air conditioning, and some rooms also feature a balcony. The rooms will provide guests with a dishwasher.

Guests can use the hot tub.

A business center and vending machines with drinks are available on site at San Luis Bay Inn.

Pismo Beach is 6 miles from the accommodation, while Morro Bay is 14 miles from the property.
