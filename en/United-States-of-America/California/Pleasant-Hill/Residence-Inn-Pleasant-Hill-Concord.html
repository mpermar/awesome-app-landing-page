Description

Suites at the Residence Inn Pleasant Hill Concord have a sitting area and an LCD TV with cable channels. The kitchens include a microwave and dishwasher.

Guests can relax in the heated outdoor pool at Residence Inn or work out in the fitness center featuring cardio equipment and a sports court.

BBQ facilities are available on-site at Concord Pleasant Hill Residence Inn. The Market provides a variety of food and drinks 24-hours a day. An evening social with beverages and light food is provided. This hotel also has a free grocery shopping service.

This hotel is 20 miles from Six Flags Discovery Kingdom, and 1 miles from the Sun Valley Shopping Center. Free public parking is available.
