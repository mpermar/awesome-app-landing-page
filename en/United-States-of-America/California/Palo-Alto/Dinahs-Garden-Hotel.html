Description

At Dinah’s Garden Hotel, guest rooms include a refrigerator and a coffee maker. A safety deposit box is available in every room.

A gym with indoor and outdoor facilities is located at the hotel. Complimentary self-parking is also available.

Dinah's Poolside Restaurant is open for made-to-order breakfast, lunch and dinner daily featuring a full bar and California craft beers on tap.

THE SEA by Alexander's Steakhouse serves fresh and local sustainably harvested seafood. Dinah's Poolside Restaurant is open for made-to-order breakfast, lunch and dinner daily.

Dinah’s Hotel is 14 miles from San Jose International Airport. Stanford University is 4.5 miles away.

Experiences

02/25/2018

Dinah's Oasis

"Dinah's is a one-of-a-kind hotel, with gorgeous well-kept grounds. There were lemon trees by the pool and huge exotic palms surrounding the koi ponds. Our teenagers loved it after being in the fast-paced SF area. It was unusually cold but our kids had the cute pool to themselves. We enjoyed the sun and it felt a bit like we were at one of the timeshares in HI. This hotel was really close to Stanford, the reason for our visit. (The guided tour there was fantastic, if you are interested.) We have a wonderful breakfast and dinner in the restaurant. Just a little pricey but everything was top-notch with the exception of the salmon, note that we are from Seattle so have probably have unrealistic expectations. The portions were huge; we should have split. Even the sourdough toast was so good that it's worth a mention. The staff from the front desk, to the grounds keepers, to the wait staff were friendly and lovely."
