Description

A flat-screen TV with cable channels is provided in each air-conditioned room at Quail Lodge & Golf Club. For convenience, all of the air-conditioned guest rooms include an iPod docking station, a work desk, a refrigerator and a coffee machine. Additional room amenities include ironing facilities and a hairdryer.

Set in the clubhouse of Quail Lodge & Golf Club, Edgar’s restaurant offers indoor or outdoor fireside dining with views of the golf course and the Santa Lucia Mountains. All menu items feature fresh local organic ingredients. Guests can also enjoy classic cocktails, California wines and micro-brews in the Lobby Bar, which also offers a light menu. The Covey Breakfast Restaurant, Bar & Deck offers a variety of breakfast options.

A 24-hour reception greets guests of Quail Lodge. Guests can work out in the fitness center. A business center with fax and photocopying services is available.

Carmel River Beach is 5 miles away from this resort. Monterey Bay Aquarium is 20 minutes’ drive away.
