Description

Along with a fireplace and a slate spa bathtub, each luxury suite features a terrace and sitting area at CordeValle, a Rosewood Resort. Free Wi-Fi, Italian linens, a wet bar and a flat-screen cable TV are also provided.

After a day on the 7,500 feet championship golf course, guests can enjoy the modern fitness center on site or relax on plush poolside lounge chairs. A concierge and tour desk is available to help schedule area wine and hiking adventures at a Rosewood Resort, CordeValle.

Featuring local produce, Il Vigneto offers classic Italian cuisine for dinner daily. On a terrace, guests can enjoy a drink before sunset at Lions Peak Grill. Overlooking the Cordevalle hills, One Iron Bar offers contemporary California cuisine for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

CordeValle is a 45 minute drive north of the Monterey Peninsula and 30 minute drive south of San Jose, California. San Jose International Airport is just 30 miles away.
