Description

Guest rooms at the Hampton Inn & Suites Ridgecrest are equipped with a 32-inch flat-screen TV and an alarm clock with MP3 connectivity. A coffee maker is also provided.

The completely non-smoking Ridgecrest Hampton Inn features a gym and a business center with fax and printing services. A 24-hour convenience store and laundry facilities are also available.

Red Rock Canyon Park is 32 miles from the Hampton Inn Ridgecrest. The Sierra Vista Shopping Center is one mile away.
