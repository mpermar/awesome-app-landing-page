Description

Guests can choose a LEGO themed room at the resort. All of the air-conditioned rooms come with a seating area and a balcony with pool view. Select rooms offer a relaxing patio.

Open daily for breakfast, Bricks Family Restaurant serves freshly prepared dishes and homemade soups. Children can interact with LEGOLAND heroes as they dine.

The resort features an outdoor swimming pool and a 24-hour front desk. A fitness centre is also available.

Carlsbad Village is a 15-minute drive away from LEGOLAND California Resort. San Diego International Airport is 30 miles away from the resort.
