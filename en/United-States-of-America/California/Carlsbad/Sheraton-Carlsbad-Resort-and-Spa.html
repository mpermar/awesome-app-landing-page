Description

A flat-screen TV, a desk and a refrigerator are included in all of the spacious rooms at Sheraton Carlsbad Resort & Spa. The rooms have air conditioning and also include balconies or patios and select rooms offer ocean views.

On-site at Sheraton Resort & Spa Carlsbad, Twenty/20 Grill & Wine Bar serves signature entrees prepared using locally-grown fresh ingredients and open-air dining. The restaurant features panoramic ocean views and an extensive list of wines from around the world.

Guests at Sheraton Carlsbad can book a spa treatment at Ocean Pearl Spa. The spa specializes in advance and organic skin care, massage, and body treatments for men and women. The resort also features a 24-hour fitness center and a business center with fax and photocopying services.

The Crossings at Carlsbad Golf Course is less than 1 mile from this resort. Camp Pendleton Marine Corps Base is 6 miles away. The San Diego Zoo and San Diego International Airport are both a 40-minute drive away.
