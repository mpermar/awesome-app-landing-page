Description

The world class accommodations at Omni La Costa include luxurious bedding, a coffee machine with premium coffee, plush robes and a marble bath.

Omni La Costa Resort & Spa features 17 tennis courts and a 9,100 foot state-of-the-art fitness center. Guests can relax with a massage in the spa or attend a lifestyle workshop in the Chopra Center. Children can enjoy two 100 foot water slides, while adults can enjoy the adults-only Edge Pool. The on-site shops feature designer brands.

Located on-site, Bob's Steak & Chop House is a traditional American prime steak house, offering a combination of simple elegance, lively atmosphere, drinks, fine wines, and large cuts of the finest prime steaks, chops and seafood. VUE serves a variety of cuisine for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Guests can also enjoy cocktails and light bites at Diversions Sport Lounge. Marketplace, open 24 hours, offers an assortment of grab-and-go food, sweet treats, ice cream, Peet's coffee and gifts.

Batquitos Lagoon is less than 10 minutes’ drive from Omni La Costa Resort & Spa.
