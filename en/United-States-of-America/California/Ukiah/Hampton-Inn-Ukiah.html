Description

The Hampton Inn Ukiah houses a colorful garden courtyard where the pool and a koi pond are located. Guests may feed the fish with food provided at the front desk. A fitness room is also available on-site.

Free Wi-Fi is available in all rooms. TV and a coffee maker are included. Bathrooms feature WaterPik shower heads and a curved shower rod allowing more space.

Breakfast is served each morning. Bagged breakfast options are available.

The Ukiah Hampton Inn is less than 1.5 miles from the Grace Hudson Museum & Sun House and less than 20 minutes from Lake Mendocino.
