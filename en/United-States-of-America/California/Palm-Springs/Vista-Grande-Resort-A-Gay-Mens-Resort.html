Description

One Bedroom Suites and Studios at Vista Grande Resort - A Gay Men's Resort feature fully equipped kitchens where guests can cook a gourmet meal. Suites include kitchenware, a dining area and living room and private entrances. All accommodations at this resort include a cable TV, a microwave, a refrigerator and an private bathroom with free toiletries and daily housekeeping.

A steam room, a waterfall lagoon and a fire pit are available to guests of this clothing optional resort.

A daily continental breakfast is served at Vista Grande Resort. Vending machines featuring drinks are located on site.

Palm Springs International Airport is 3 miles from this resort. Palm Springs Aerial Tramway is 15 minutes’ drive away. Hiking and cycling opportunities are available in the area around this resort. Free guest parking is available. Free WiFi is available throughout the entire resort.
