Description

All guestrooms at the Colony Palms boast pillow-top mattresses and Italian linens. Guests will also enjoy HD TVs and iPod docking stations. After a busy day, guests can use the C.O. Bigelow bath products or refresh with a drink from the well-stocked mini-bar. Some rooms have fireplaces.

The Colony Palms Hotel features an on-site fitness center. Guests can relax in one of the hotel's comfortable courtyards or use the free wireless internet access. For breakfast, lunch and dinner, guests can visit the hotel's seasonal Purple Palm Restaurant & Bar.

The Spa at Colony Palms Hotel offers massages, facials, and other body treatments. The property also offers complimentary bike hire for guests to explore downtown Palm Springs and other surrounding neighborhoods,
