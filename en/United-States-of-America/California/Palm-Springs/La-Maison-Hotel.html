Description

Styled with dark wood furnishings, each room provides free Wi-Fi and free toiletries in the private bathroom at La Maison Hotel. Luxury comforters and linens are featured.

A poolside hot tub and barbecue facilities are featured at Palm Springs La Maison Hotel. Hiking and biking opportunities at Indian Canyon Hiking Trails is just 5 miles away. Free cruiser bikes rentals and free parking is available.

Mesquite Golf & Country Club is 1 miles from La Maison Hotel. Joshua Tree National Park is a 1-hour drive away. Palm Springs Air Museum is 5 miles away.
