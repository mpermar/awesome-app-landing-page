Description

Offering an outdoor pool and a fitness center, this Redondo Beach hotel features a free expansive daily hot breakfast buffet. All guest rooms include a fully-equipped modern kitchen.

Free Wi-Fi and a separate seating area with flat-screen TV is included in each air-conditioned studio or suite at Residence Inn by Marriott Los Angeles Redondo Beach. An private bathroom with hairdryer is provided.

At Residence Inn by Marriott Los Angeles Redondo Beach you will find barbecue facilities and a mini-market on site. Other facilities offered include a free grocery delivery service. An evening reception with appetizers is provided.

Manhattan Beach Pier is 2.5 mi away from the property. Los Angeles International Airport is 3.7 mi away.
