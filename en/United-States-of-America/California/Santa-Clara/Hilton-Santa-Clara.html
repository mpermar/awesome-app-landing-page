Description

Hilton Santa Clara provides guests with a variety of recreational facilities. Guests can swim in the outdoor pool or work out in the well-equipped fitness center. The hotel's 24-hour business center is sure to meet all business needs.

The guestrooms and suites at the Santa Clara Hilton feature luxurious beds with pillow-top mattresses, down duvets and 250 thread-count linens. Guests will enjoy 37-inch high-definition TVs and clock radios with MP3 connectivity. The granite bathrooms boast Peter Thomas Roth bath products.

Right next to Levi Stadium and Great America

"The location is great if you're planning to visit Levi Stadium or Great America. You just have to cross the parking lot to get there, probably a 10-minute walk (less if you don't have easily distracted kids who find a crack in the sidewalk fascinating) to the entrance gate of Great America and less to the stadium. Staff were awesome--so friendly, courteous, and accommodating. Our room faced Great America, and we were there over July 4, so we were able to watch fireworks from our room. We opted to stay an extra night and they were able to accommodate us. We were upgraded to the Concierge level, which got us breakfast and evening reception. Breakfast was good--standard breakfast fare--scrambled eggs, sausage, cereal, oatmeal, yogurt, juice, coffee--but the evening reception was amazing. It was like having a light dinner. They had a couple of pasta dishes, crudités, wine, and other light fare. Would definitely stay here again!!"
