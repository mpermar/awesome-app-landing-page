Description

A terrace with barbecue facilities is featured in all cottages. Featuring a river view, each cottage provides free WiFi, a seating area, cable TV and an private bathroom. Natural wood accents are featured in all rooms at Fern River Resort. Select cabins offer a fully equipped kitchen or a fireplace.

Surrounded by a majestic forest, this resort offers barbecue facilities with picnic tables. A game room is also offered at Felton Fern River Resort.

Guests can take advantage of the cabins to host weddings, private parties, and corporate events.

Monterey Bay Aquarium is 1 hours drive away. Mineta San Jose International Airport is 35 miles away from Fern River Resort.
