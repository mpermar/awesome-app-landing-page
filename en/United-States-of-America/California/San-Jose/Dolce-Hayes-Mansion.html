Description

The air-conditioned rooms at this hotel are furnished with a satellite TV, a work desk and a refrigerator.

Guests at the Dolce Hayes can also enjoy a variety of on-site dining options, including Silver Creek Dining Room which offers a casual atmosphere for breakfast and lunch.

Only a short distance from the Dolce Mansion, guests can discover top-rated golf courses, local wineries and an exciting nightlife. Mineta San Jose International Airport is 20 minutes’ drive away from the property. Shopping and dining at opportunities at the popular Santana Row shopping center are 9.9 mi away.
