Description

Relax in the Hadsten House Inn and Spa's uniquely furnished guest rooms, which feature decorative fireplaces, flat-screen TVs and lavish linens. Start the day with a free full hot breakfast including eggs, potatoes, bacon, homemade oatmeal, homemade granola, juice, coffee, tea, toast, and more.

Spend the afternoon at the full service spa or strolling through the on-site mini-vineyard. Enjoy free wine and cheese in the afternoon or take advantage of the indoor swimming pool.

The Hadsten House Inn has easy access to Santa Barbara as well as Santa Ynez Valley. The Scenic Hans Christian Andersen Park, art galleries and wine tours are also only minutes away.
