Description

Every room comes with a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Some units include a seating area where you can relax. You will find a coffee machine in the room. Sky Valley Resort features free WiFi .

Tennis, table tennis, and billiards are available at the resort. Hot tubs are also provided at the clubhouses.

Palm Springs is 11 mi from Sky Valley Resort, while La Quinta is 17 mi from the property. The nearest airport is Palm Springs International Airport, 9.9 mi from the property.
