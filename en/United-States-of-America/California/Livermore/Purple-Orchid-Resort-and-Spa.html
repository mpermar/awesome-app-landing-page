Description

Offering an outdoor pool and a spa and wellness center, the adults’ only Purple Orchid Resort & Spa is located in Livermore. Free WiFi access is available at this tranquil resort.

The air-conditioned accommodations at this resort include a satellite TV and a seating area with an inviting fireplace. All of the private bathrooms come with a relaxing spa bath, guest robes, a hairdryer and free toiletries. Select suites offer pool or garden views.

At Purple Orchid Resort & Spa guests can unwind in the hot tubs or relax in the gardens. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including golfing, cycling and hiking. The property offers free parking.

Poppy Ridge Golf Course is 0.6 mi away. The resort is 39 mi from San Francisco International Airport.
