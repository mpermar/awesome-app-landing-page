Description

A cable TV and a DVD player are featured in each suite at Desert Breezes Resort. All air-conditioned suites include a telephone and a seating area.

Guests of Desert Breezes Resort California have access to the on-site tennis courts and putting green. A gym is located on site. Gas BBQ facilities are available for outdoor entertainment options.

Indian Wells Golf Course is 3 mi from this resort. Featuring an up-scale mall and boutique shops, El Paseo Shopping District is just 3 minutes’ drive away. Fantasy Springs Casino is 16 minutes’ drive away.
