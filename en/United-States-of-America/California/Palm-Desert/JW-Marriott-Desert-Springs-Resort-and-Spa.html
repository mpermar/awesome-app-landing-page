Description

All of the rooms at the JW Marriott Desert Springs Resort offer views of the hotel’s pools and gardens. Guests can take gondolas tours of the waterways from the hotel’s gondola deck.

Guests can play tennis on the hotel’s clay and grass courts. There’s also fitness center with state-of-the-art. The hotel’s extensive spa offers a wide range of treatments.

JW Marriott Desert Springs Resort offers Californian cuisine at the Rockwood Grill, and Japanese dishes created by ‘Samurai chefs’ at the Mikado. The hotel’s lobby bar offers freshly prepared sushi. Room service is also available.

The JW Marriott Desert Springs Resort & Spa is 4.5 miles from the El Paseo shopping district, 5 miles from the The Living Desert Zoo & Botanical Gardens and 9.7 miles from the Oasis Waterpark.
