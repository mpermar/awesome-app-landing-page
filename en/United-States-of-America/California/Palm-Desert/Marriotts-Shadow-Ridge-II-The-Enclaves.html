Description

Each room is air-conditioned and equipped with a minibar, cable TV and a safe. It includes a well-equipped kitchen featuring oven, fridge and kitchen utensils. A dishwasher and washing machine are also provided.

Various salads, light snacks and pizzas are served at the Chuckwalla Pool Bar and Grille. Hearty dishes and alcoholic beverages are available at Nick Faldo's Bar and Grill.

Marriott's Shadow Ridge II - The Enclaves is 8 miles from Knott's Soak City Waterpark. It is a 15-minute drive from the Living Desert.
