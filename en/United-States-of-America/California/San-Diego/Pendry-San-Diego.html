Description

All guest rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area. Every room includes a private bathroom with a hair dryer. At Pendry San Diego each room has a desk and a flat-screen TV.

An American breakfast is served each morning at the property.

Guests can use the hot tub. You can play darts at the accommodation.

Staff at the reception can help guests with any questions that they might have.

Maritime Museum of San Diego is 1.1 miles from Pendry San Diego, while Balboa Park is 1.7 miles away. The nearest airport is San Diego International Airport, 2.5 miles from the property.

Downtown San Diego is a great choice for travelers interested in restaurants, harbors and food.
