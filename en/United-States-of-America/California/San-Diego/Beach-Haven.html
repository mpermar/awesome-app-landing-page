Description

Beach Haven Inn features an outdoor pool and whirlpool tub in the center courtyard. Guests can cook a meal in the outdoor barbecue area or in the full kitchen in their spacious studio or suite.

Guests at the Beach Haven can walk along the beach or explore the Crystal Pier, which is only 4 blocks from the motel. The nearby Pacific Beach boardwalk features 3 miles of restaurants, shops and nightclubs.

Pacific Beach is a great choice for travelers interested in beaches, sandy beaches and beach walks.
