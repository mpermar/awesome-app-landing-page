Description

A 42-inch flat-screen TV with HBO movie channels is provided in each air-conditioned room at Hilton San Diego Bayfront. Each warm-colored, contemporary room also includes a coffee machine, a work desk and a safe.

Guests at the Hilton San Diego Bayfront can relax by the outdoor pool and enjoy poolside dining at the open-air bar and lounge, The Pool Club, serving California coastal-inspired cuisine. The hotel also offers two hot bathtubs and a Kid’s Splash Zone.

Vela, Hilton San Diego Bayfront’s signature restaurant, serves international cuisine and fine wines. Guests can savor in handcrafted cocktails, eclectic wine, local craft beers and tasty bites at Odysea Lounge or enjoy a casual meal at Fox Sports Grill.

The hotel is just 3 miles from the San Diego International Airport. Fifth Avenue Pier is just a 2-minute walk away and San Diego’s vibrant Gaslamp Quarter is within a 9-minute walk away.
