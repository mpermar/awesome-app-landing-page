Description

Adorned with hand-crafted European furniture, The Westgate Hotel offers rooms with a 49-inch LED TV and a CD/DVD player. Each room includes a safety deposit box. Guests can print documents from their room to a main printer, then the hotel staff will personally deliver printed documents back to guests.

The Westgate Room serves Continental-California cuisine. Afternoon Tea, with live harp music, is available on select days. Live piano entertainment can be heard at the Plaza Bar.

Hotel Westgate provides a business center and laundry services. Ticket services are also available. An outdoor heated lap pool, a hot tub and a unique running track are all provided to guests.

The historic Gaslamp Quarter is less than 1 mi from The Westgate Hotel. SeaWorld San Diego is 5.3 mi away from the hotel. Guests are 3 mi from the San Diego Zoo.

Downtown San Diego is a great choice for travelers interested in restaurants, harbors and food.
