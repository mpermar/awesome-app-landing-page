Description

A spacious patio, hardwood floors and a marble bathroom make up each exotic room at Paradise Point Resort & Spa. A flat-screen TV and ironing facilities are also included.

Five pools, a fitness center and sports courts are open for guests at the Paradise Point. Relaxing nature tours, beach bonfires and poolside movie nights are also featured.

TIdal restaurant serves fine seafood and features craft beverages. Bars and grills are also open next to the resort’s beach and pools.

The San Diego Zoo is less than 9 miles from Paradise Point Resort. The San Diego International Airport is a 10-minute drive away.

Mission Bay is a great choice for travelers interested in beach walks, sandy beaches and family-friendly trips.
