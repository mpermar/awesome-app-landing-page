Description

A flat-screen cable TV with extended satellite channels is featured in each of the air-conditioned guest room at The Lodge at Torrey Pines. Each luxurious room includes a granite and marble bathroom with a large bathtub, a custom stereo system, guest robes and slippers, a minibar and a work desk. Select rooms offer botanical garden or golf course views.

A.R. Valentien restaurant is located at Torrey Pines Lodge. Taking its name from an early-1900s California artist, the restaurant serves fresh regional cuisine. It is in an elegant timbered room overlooking the 18th hole of Torrey Pines Golf Course and includes an exhibit of original Valentien artwork.

A 24-hour front desk greets guests at The Lodge. Guest can enjoy a game of tennis on the on-site courts or relax in the hot tub or sauna.

Torrey Pines State Park is 2 miles from this hotel. Downtown La Jolla is a 12-minute drive away. San Diego International Airport is 15 miles away.

La Jolla is a great choice for travelers interested in sunsets, beach walks and sandy beaches.
