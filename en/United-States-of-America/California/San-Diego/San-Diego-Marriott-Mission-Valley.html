Description

Situated in California, this Marriott is a 16-minute drive from San Diego International Airport.

The outdoor pool features a waterfall and private cabanas. Bright guest rooms provide a private balcony.

Decorated in soothing colors with beige accents, each room includes cable TV with the HBO movie channel.

Coffee facilities are standard in all San Diego Marriott Mission Valley rooms. On-site dining at the DEN Restaurant offers American and Spanish cuisine for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Guests can relax around the outside fire pit at the hotel. A hot tub is also available on the pool deck at Mission Valley San Diego Marriott.

The San Diego Zoo is 3. 6 miles from this Marriott hotel. The University of San Diego is 5.
