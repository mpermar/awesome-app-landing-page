Description

Each bright and contemporary guest room at The Westin San Diego Gaslamp Quarter is furnished with a refrigerator and tea and coffee-making facilities. A laptop safe and work desk are provided and Wi-Fi is available.

Pinzimini Restaurant, Bar at Pinzimini and Ingredients Grab ’n’ Go are located on-site at The Westin San Diego Gaslamp Quarter. Pinzimin Restaurant serves modern, Tuscan-style cuisine, while Bar at Pinzimini offers signature cocktails and snacks. Ingredients Grab ’n’ Go offers pre-made sandwiches, coffee and juices. Breakfast is served starting at 6:30 am to 11:00 am, lunch from 11:00 am to 1:30 pm, and dinner from 5:00 pm to 11:00 pm. The bar is open until 1:00 am.

Guests can relax in the outdoor pool and hot tub, or work out in the on-site gym. A business center is located off of the lobby and concierge services are available.

Balboa Park and the San Diego Zoo are 10 minutes’ drive from The Westin San Diego Gaslamp Quarter. Petco Baseball Park, home of the San Diego Padres, is 1 miles from the hotel.

Downtown San Diego is a great choice for travelers interested in restaurants, harbors and food.
