Description

A natural stone bathroom is featured in every guest room at Estancia La Jolla Hotel & Spa. Each luxurious room includes plush guest bathrobes, an in-room safe, a cable TV and a work desk with a high back reading chair.

Estancia La Jolla Hotel provides guests with a variety of dining options. On site are The Adobe El Restaurante, Mustangs & Burros and the Trinitas Cellars Wine Bar & Private Dining Room. Each restaurant features innovative cuisine and indoor or outdoor dining.

Guests of La Jolla Estancia can relax and enjoy spa and wellness services at The Spa at Estancia. The full-service spa offers massage therapies, facials and manicures and pedicures. Guests can also work out in the on-site fitness center.

Torrey Pines Municipal Golf Course is 2 miles from this hotel. Del Mar Racetrack is a 15- minute drive from the property. San Diego International Airport is a 20-minutes drive from the hotel.

La Jolla is a great choice for travelers interested in sunsets, beach walks and sandy beaches.
