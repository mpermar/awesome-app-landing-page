Description

A 55-inch cable satellite TV with per-per-view channels is provided in each air-conditioned guest room at Hyatt Regency La Jolla. Guest rooms include a seating area, a coffee machine and an private bathroom with free toiletries.

Located in the lower lobby of La Jolla Hyatt Regency, Barcino Grill features a full American breakfast buffet 7 days a week as well as breakfast specialty items. DRIFT offers flat screen TVs, several fire pits and bar. Guests can enjoy a full selection of wine, beer or cocktails and a happy hour menu 7 days a week.

Guests of La Jolla Hyatt have access to 2 outdoor fully lit tennis courts. If they prefer, guests can work out in the fitness center. For convenience, a 24-hour reception is available.

UCSD is within 3.1 mi from the property. La Jolla Shores is 10 minutes’ drive away from this hotel. The Scripps Research Institute and Torrey Pines Golf Course are within 3 miles away. SeaWorld San Diego is 15 minutes’ drive away.

La Jolla is a great choice for travelers interested in sunsets, beach walks and sandy beaches.
