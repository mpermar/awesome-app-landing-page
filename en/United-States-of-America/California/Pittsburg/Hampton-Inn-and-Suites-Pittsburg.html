Description

Hampton Inn & Suites Pittsburg features an outdoor swimming pool and modern fitness center. Guests will also appreciate in-room amenities such as coffeemakers and cable TV at this hotel.

While staying at the Pittsburg Hampton Inn & Suites, guests can play a game of golf at the Delta View Golf Course or visit the Pittsburg Marina. Guests can also spend the day at Waterworld amusement park or discover Mount Diablo State Park, which are both within driving distance.
