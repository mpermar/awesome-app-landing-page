Description

All SpringHill studios have separate areas for working, relaxing and sleeping. Each has a large desk and a pantry with a mini-fridge, coffee maker and microwave.

Start your day with a hot and healthy breakfast offered daily in the hotel’s inviting dining room. The Black Bear Diner serves American cuisine for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Guests at SpringHill Suites Madera can work out in the modern fitness center with cardio equipment, free weights and complementary fitness classes. For more relaxation, there is a heated pool and a whirlpool.

The hotel is located near Yosemite National Park, close to theme parks and Fresno.
