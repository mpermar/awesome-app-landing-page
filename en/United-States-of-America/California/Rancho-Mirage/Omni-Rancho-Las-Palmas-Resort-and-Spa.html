Description

Multiple water slides, splash zones and pools are available at this Palm Springs resort. Ranchos Las Palmas Country Club is onsite, rooms feature free WiFi and a flat-screen cable TV. Five restaurants are available on-site.

A seating area and a work desk are featured in every room at Omni Rancho Las Palmas Resort & Spa. Air-conditioning and a coffee machine are available for guest comfort. Most rooms boast a pool or fairway view.

With 5 restaurants on site at Omni Rancho Las Palmas Resort & Spa Palm Springs, guests can have their pick of cuisine and ambiance. Bluember features indoor or outdoor dining on the plaza and offers local ingredients and an extensive local wine list. R Bar features a sports-bar feel offering evening cocktails. Palms Café is the hotel’s coffee bar, offering a relaxing environment for quick breakfast options.

Located at Omni Rancho Las Palmas Resort, Splashtopia Waterpark, a 2 acre water playground, features a 450-foot lazy river, 2 100-foot water slides, a cliff-side hot tub, a sandy beach and fountains and sprinklers to give families and children endless amounts of fun.

An adults only Azure pool, a 20,000-square-foot European spa, 25 tennis courts and a 27-hole championship golf course are featured at this resort. A fitness center, sauna, and Turkish steam bath are all available for guest use. A business center is offered and a concierge service is provided to assist guests with area attractions and reservations.

Palm Springs International Airport is 20 minutes’ drive from Omni Rancho Las Palmas Resort & Spa. El Paseo Shopping District is 1 miles from the resort.
