Description

Offering free WiFi, all rooms and suites offer a step-out balcony or a furnished patio - many with commanding views of the Pacific Ocean and Newport Harbor. Features include floor-to-ceiling windows, LCD TVs and Bose audio systems. Marble bathrooms offer over-sized bath towels, fine linens and goose down duvets.

Oak Grill is a light-filled restaurant that features a spacious outdoor deck for al fresco dining, along with an indoor bar with multiple TVs. Aqua Lounge serves cocktails and small bites by day and spirited socializing by night. Market Place, open 24-hours, offers coffee, fresh pastries and on-the-go lunches.

Guests of Fashion Island Hotel can relax at the resort-style pool area, which features a contemporary poolside bar, private cabanas, a fireplace and modern furnishings. Adjacent to the pool, the fitness centre is outfitted with state-of-the-art equipment. The Spa at Fashion Island Hotel features a relaxation lounge, a eucalyptus steam room and 9 treatment rooms for massages and treatments.

Experiences

06/04/2018

Great hotel in excellent location.

"This was a great hotel. The location is perfect. Walking distance to shopping, restaurants, beaches. With the destination fee, you have access to cruiser bicycles, which were really fun to take out. The facility has a great pool area, fitness center, and activites like ping pong, corn hole, giant Connect 4 and giant Jenga. Kids would love it. The staff was super friendly and helpful and the hotel was very clean and well kept. We were there when it was apparently National Donut Day, so they even had free mini donuts in the lobby, which was a nice touch. I would definitely stay here again!"
