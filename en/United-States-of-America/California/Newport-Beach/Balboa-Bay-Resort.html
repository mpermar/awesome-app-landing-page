Description

The Balboa Bay Resort offers accommodations equipped with a DVD player and a flat-screen TV. The granite and marble bathrooms are furnished with soaking tubs. A refrigerator and in-room laptop safe are provided.

This resort features 3 on-property restaurants. Waterline Newport Beach, a waterfront water-to-table chef-driven restaurant, is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner. A&O Kitchen+Bar, a waterfront gastropub and bar, is open late nights and serves lunch and dinner. Blend, an Italian-inspired coffee shop, is open daily from 06:00 to 19:00 hours.

Disneyland is just 20 minutes from the Balboa Bay Resort. Crystal Cove State Park is 10 minutes from the resort.
