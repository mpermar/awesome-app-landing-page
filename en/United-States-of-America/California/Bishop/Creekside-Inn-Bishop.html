Description

Free Wi-Fi is offered in all rooms at the Creekside Inn. The temperature-controlled rooms feature cherry wood work desks and chairs. A flat-screen TV, refrigerator and coffee-making facilities are standard in all guest rooms.

The Creekside Inn Bishop offers guests a creek-side patio with views of the Eastern Sierra Nevada Mountains. A 24-hour front desk and laundry facilities are offered on-site. The property also offers guests homemade soups in the winter and fresh baked cookies year round.

Guests can enjoy a complimentary daily hot breakfast. In the evening the hotel offers guests homemade soups and fresh-baked cookies.

A golf course at Bishop Country Club is 1 miles away and the Tri County Fairground is 5 minutes’ drive from the hotel.

Experiences

01/07/2018

Big comfortable rooms, great location, and fresh, hot breakfast

"We decided to stay in Bishop and drive to Mammoth Mountain each day. The Creekside Inn had everything to make our stay comfortable. Check in was fast and the staff are genuinely nice. We used a luggage cart and took the elevator to our room. There are ice makers and soft drink vending machines on each floor. There is plenty of free self parking. The rooms are large with small balcony. We had a room with two queen beds on the second floor. Beds were high quality and just the right amount of soft. The lighting in the rooms were nice including charging outlets on the lamps. TV had full cable (not basic), but no DVD player. Wifi was free and strong signal even though our room was the furthest from the main lobby. Mini fridge was a good size, but no freezer section. Microwave was a plus for warming up drinks and food. Keurig coffeemaker provided. Bathroom had large shower stall with two sinks (one inside the bathroom and one just outside), which is convenient for multiple people getting ready. There is also a very nice laundry room for guest use on the second floor, but we did not use it. Every morning we had complimentary breakfast. It includes scrambled eggs, home fries, sausage, biscuits, gravy. Plus oatmeal, bagels, toast (nice multigrain), scones, cereal, yogurt. The coffee was good quality. (No espresso drinks.) The eggs were really delicious much better than the high end hotels I have stayed at. Each evening we stopped in for soup at 5p and cookies at 7p. We would sit by the large fireplace and play chess or chat with other families and guests. We used the hot tub, which was fabulous (as it was winter the swimming pool was not heated, but it was large and well maintained). There are nice outdoor seating areas with gas fire pits along the creek. The Inn does not serve lunch or dinner (and it does not serve alcohol). We had lunch and bought bread at the famous Schat’s Bakery, which is literally next door."
