Description

The rooms include a flat-screen TV. Some units have a seating area for your convenience. You will find a coffee machine in the room. All rooms are fitted with a private bathroom. Extras include free toiletries and a hair dryer.

Guests of Bear Creek Resort are offered free coffee and muffins for breakfast every day.

Alpine Slide at Magic Mountain is 0.7 mi from Bear Creek Resort, while Aspen Glen Picnic Area is 1,650 feet away. The nearest airport is Palm Springs International Airport, 37 mi from Bear Creek Resort.
