Description

Guest rooms at Hampton Inn & Suites San Clemente come with a refrigerator and microwave. A recliner or lounge chair is provided. Enjoy restful nights on the hotel's signature Cloud Nine beds.

Hampton Inn and Suites San Clemente is 24 miles from John Wayne Airport. Guests can also enjoy the beautiful weather at nearby Trestles Beach or explore antique shops on Del March Avenue. Camp Pendleton, a Marine Corps base, is also only a short drive away.

The hotel provides a gym and business facilities. Luggage storage and newspapers are available at the 24-hour front desk. A guest laundromat is on site.
