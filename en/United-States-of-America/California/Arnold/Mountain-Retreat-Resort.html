Description

Located on the western slope of the Sierra Nevada Mountains, this accommodations features rooms with free Wi-Fi. Calaveras Big Trees State Park is 3 miles away.

This 2-bedroom duplex-style guest accommodations offers rooms with generous wood furnishings and includes a full kitchen complete with a stove, a microwave and a refrigerator. Both bedrooms and the spacious living room at Mountain Retreat Resort offer cable TVs. A dining area and a washer and tumble dryer are included in this guest accommodations.

Guests of Resort Mountain Retreat have access to an on-site clubhouse which includes a pool, spa, sauna, business center, fitness center, billiards table, foosball table, a movie theater and gaming options. BBQ facilities are offered on the property.

Mercer Caverns are 30 minutes' drive from Retreat Mountain Resort. New Melones Lake is 28 miles away. Hiking, fishing, mountain climbing and kayaking opportunities are all nearby this quiet mountain retreat.

Experiences

08/11/2018

A nice place to stay for families close to Calaveras Big Trees Park

"Our initial plan was to go to the Yosemite Park but we could not go because of the Ferguson fire. So we decided to go further North and visit Calaveras Big Trees. We found this place to stay at the last minute and were very pleased. It is a nice family resort, with a small indoor pool but also access to a very big external pool across the road. The wooden bungalow was very spacious with 2 bedrooms upstairs with one double bed in each and a big open plan kitchen / dining / lounge downstairs with 2 sofa beds. The staff was extremely friendly and there were free activities organised occasionally, eg bingo night, ice cream night, .... The residence is located 5/10 mns drive from Calaveras Big Trees so it is easy to go and visit this beautiful sequoia national forest. We would say that the bungalows could benefit from being refreshed a bit, with carpets to be replaced but that did not have an impact on our stay and we enjoyed this family resort."
