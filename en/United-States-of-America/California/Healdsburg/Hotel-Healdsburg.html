Description

Each spacious and warm-colored room at Hotel Healdsburg has free Wi-Fi, an iPod docking station, and a flat-screen TV with DVD player. Guests can relax with a free DVD rental or enjoy the view from the balcony.

French cuisine is served at Charlie Palmer’s Dry Creek Kitchen, open for lunch and dinner. Coffee and snacks are available at the Healdsburg’s Café Newsstand and the Grand Fireplace Lounge features cocktails and a screened porch.

A full-service spa and an on-site gym are part of the Healdsburg Hotel. Business services and a 24-hour front desk are available. Complimentary valet parking services are provided.

Downtown Santa Rosa is a 15-minute drive from Hotel Healdsburg. The Armstrong Redwoods Trail is 20 miles away.
