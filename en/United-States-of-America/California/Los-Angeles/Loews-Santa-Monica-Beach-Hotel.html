Description

The contemporary guest rooms at Loews Santa Monica Beach Hotel are furnished with a flat-screen TV and carpeted floors. Guests can relax on the small step-out balcony with a drink from the mini-bar. Room service is available 24 hours a day.

Located at Loews Santa Monica Beach Hotel, Ocean and Vine Restaurant offers contemporary California cuisine prepared with only the freshest farm-grown ingredients.

Guests can relax in the hotel’s 4th floor elevated pool, which offers remarkable views of the Pacific Ocean and Santa Monica Pier. The pool deck includes 13 fire pits featuring a state-of-the-art sound system, as well as a poolside ice cream shop. Personal trainers and outdoor fitness classes are offered at the hotel. Bicycle and rollerblade rentals are available.

Venice Beach is 2 miles from Loews Santa Monica Beach Hotel. Los Angeles International Airport is 15 minutes' drive from the property.

Santa Monica is a great choice for travelers interested in the oceanside, road trips and skyline views.
