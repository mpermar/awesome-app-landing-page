Description

All rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Rooms contain a private bathroom, while some feature a city view. All guest rooms at Hotel Indigo - Los Angeles Downtown feature air conditioning and a desk.

Breakfast is available every morning, and includes continental and American options. At the accommodation you'll find a restaurant serving American cuisine. Dairy-free and vegetarian options can also be requested.

Hotel Indigo - Los Angeles Downtown has a sun terrace. You can play pool at the hotel, and bike rental is available.

A business center and vending machines with drinks are available on site at Hotel Indigo - Los Angeles Downtown. Staff at the accommodation are available to give information at the 24-hour front desk.

Staples Center is 1,200 feet from Hotel Indigo - Los Angeles Downtown. Los Angeles International Airport is 11 miles away.

Downtown LA is a great choice for travelers interested in city walks, food and entertainment.
