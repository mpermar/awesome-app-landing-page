Description

Santa Monica’s first new luxury lifestyle hotel in over a decade, Santa Monica Proper has seamlessly married the character of a 1928 landmark building with contemporary architecture.

Each room comes equipped with the Proper amenities including Fili D’oro Fine Linens, Bellino Italian comforter and pillows, Kelly Wearstler custom throws, Kelly Wearstler parachute home custom bathrobes, and Aesop bathroom amenities.

Proper boasts three restaurant-bar destinations including Onda, a collaboration between chefs Jessica Koslow of Sqirl and Gabriela Cámara of Mexico City’s Contramar.

The property also features a rooftop pool, deck and restaurant-bar. Additionally, a 3,000 square-foot Proper x Surya Spa provides a flagship collaboration offering Ayurvedic treatments, fitness center, and yoga terrace.

Original works by emerging L. A. artists and 24,000 square feet of meeting and event spaces complete this Westside hotel.
