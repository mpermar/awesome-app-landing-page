Description

All rooms feature original artwork from a Paris-based design studio and are dressed in custom-designed furnishings and luxury linens. Many include standalone pedestal bathtubs and separate rooms for wash closets. Rooms also include desks, a fully stocked minibar, toiletries, and bathrobes.

Guests can utilise the 24-hour fitness or lounge in the rooftop pool and bar. From the rooftop, guests can take in panoramic views of downtown Los Angeles or enjoy a cup of coffee from the outdoor cafe. There is a 24-hour front desk, valet parking and shops at the property.

Other dining options include the lobby lounge, which is also home to an on-site library and is open throughout the day and into the evening. The Giannini Bar offers a bar outside of the main lobby, and the Mezzanine offers the most formal dining option at the NoMad Hotel Los Angeles.

The Grove is 6 miles from the hotel, while TCL Chinese Theatre is 6.2 miles away. Walt Disney Concert Hall is 0.5 miles away. The nearest airport is Los Angeles International Airport, 11.2 miles from the property.

Downtown LA is a great choice for travelers interested in city walks, entertainment and food.
