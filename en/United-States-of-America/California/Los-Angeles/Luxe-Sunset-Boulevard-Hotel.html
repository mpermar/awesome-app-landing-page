Description

A flat-screen TV is provided in all spacious rooms at Luxe Hotel Sunset Boulevard. Rooms feature warm colors and offer a fully stocked mini-bar and bathrobes. Most rooms include a private patio.

Fitness facilities are also available. Creative dishes can be found at On Sunset. The restaurant offers a Sunday jazz brunch and a full bar. It offers both indoor and poolside dining.

The University of California Los Angeles and the Getty Museum are both less than 10 minutes' drive from the hotel. Beverly Hills and Rodeo Drive are both less than 15 minutes' drive away.
