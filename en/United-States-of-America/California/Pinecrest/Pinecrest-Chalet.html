Description

Free Wi-Fi, cable TV and DVD players and tea and coffee-making facilities are featured in all of cottages at Pinecrest Chalet. There is plenty of space for dining and relaxing. Select cottages have cathedral knotty pine ceilings. All cottages have forest views.

The Pinecrest Chalet offers guests volleyball and basketball courts. BBQ facilities and horseshoe pits are also available on-site and seasonal outdoor activities such as fishing and hiking and skiing and snowboarding are nearby.

Dodge Ridge Ski Resort is a 5 minute drive away, while Kennedy Meadows is 30 miles from Pinecrest Chalet.
