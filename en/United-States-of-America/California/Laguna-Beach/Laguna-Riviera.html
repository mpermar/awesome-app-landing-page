Description

Featuring a blend of contemporary and traditional decor, all rooms at Laguna Riviera include a flat-screen TV with cable. All comfortably furnished rooms provide tea and coffee making facilities, a microwave and fridge.

A 24-hour front desk is offered at Laguna Riviera. A business center with fax and photocopying services is available.

Knott’s Berry Farm is 40 miles from the hotel. The Laguna Beach Art Gallery is a 15-minute walk away.
