Description

Select suites feature plush cream sofas or an ocean view at Montage Laguna Beach. Each luxurious room provides plush bathrobes, free spa toiletries and a dual vanity mirror in the private bathroom. Equipped with free Wi-Fi, every room also has a flat-screen cable TV and an iPod docking station.

Studio Restaurant features a chef table that can seat twelve in a private dining room. Showcasing outdoor dining, The Loft Restaurant serves breakfast, lunch and dinner. Poolside dining and cocktails are featured at Mosaic Bar & Grille. Serving-up nightly entertainment as well as cocktails, The Lobby Lounge is also available for guests' enjoyment.

Spa Montage offers a variety of spa services, and poolside cabanas are provided at Laguna Beach Montage. Boutique shops and art galleries are also featured at the resort.

Dana Point is 5 miles from Montage Laguna Beach. Los Angeles International Airport is a one- hour drive away, and John Wayne Airport is 20 miles from the property.
