Description

Rooms at the Courtyard Santa Clarita Valencia have a modern décor and are equipped with free Wi-Fi and a 32" flat-screen TV. Some rooms also include a well-equipped kitchenette and cooking facilities.

Guests can enjoy the on-site Courtyard Café, serving American food daily for breakfast and dinner. An atmospheric lobby bar serves fresh drinks and local spirits until late in the evenings.

Popular nearby attractions include Six Flags Magic Mountain, less than 3 miles away, and the natural landmarks of Placerita Canyon, a 20-minute drive from the Courtyard Santa Clarita hotel.
