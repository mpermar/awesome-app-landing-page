Description

A 42-inch flat-screen TV with in-room movies is featured in all rooms at Westin Pasadena. A refrigerator, coffee maker and 24-hour room service are also provided.

Recreational facilities at the Pasadena Westin include a hot tub and a fully equipped gym. Guests can also use one of the hotel's running maps with 3 and 5 miles suggested runs.

Ventanas Restaurant & Bar serves award-winning California cuisine at The Pasadena Westin. Guests can read a book and enjoy a cocktail at the Living Room Lobby & Bar. Baked goods are available at The Store.

The Rose Bowl Stadium is 1.7 mi from the property, while Kidspace Children's Museum is 2.2 mi away. The nearest airport is Los Angeles International Airport is 29 mi away.
