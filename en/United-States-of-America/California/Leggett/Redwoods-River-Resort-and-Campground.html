Description

Redwoods River Resort & Campground features a restaurant, seasonal outdoor swimming pool, a bar and shared lounge in Leggett.

All rooms feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom.

The resort has family rooms. At the resort, each room is fitted with a patio with a mountain view.

Guest rooms will provide guests with a microwave. Redwoods River Resort & Campground offers a children's playground.

You can play billiards and table tennis at the accommodation, and the area is popular for hiking and cycling.
