Description

The air-conditioned guest rooms at this hotel include an LCD TV, a mini-refrigerator and a microwave. Rooms include a work desk and a coffee machine.

Guests of Hampton Inn & Suites Temecula can start the morning with a free hot breakfast.

An on-site fitness center and a business center are available at this hotel.

Hampton Inn & Suites Temecula is located only a short drive from California's Wine Country, where guests can enjoy tours of the vineyards and wine tastings. San Diego International Airport is a 55 minute drive away.

Experiences

07/06/2017

Hampton's never let's us down...

"Friendly staff, very clean facility, comfy, updated, clean rooms. Impressive decor. We stayed here for 3 nights while in town for a wedding. My favorite part about the stay was everything really. The staff is super friendly and helpful. Breakfast consists of real protein and it was different daily. It's not amazing breakfast but it's impressive enough. The rooms are super clean and comfy. I LOVED the pool! It is salt water and very clean. Not many children around either, which was nice. The staff puts fresh Otis Spunkmeyer baked cookies out daily! Such a sweet treat! We had a standard king room and it was plenty spacious for my husband and I. Lot's of mirrors to get ready. My only complaint about this place would be they do not have room service, like most other Hampton's. Which is fine because there is tons of restaurants near by. I'd stay here again! Highly recommend for couples. We drove in to San Diego for our last night and stayed at the Best Western. Huge disappointment compared to this place. Thank you, Hampton for rocking! As usual!"
