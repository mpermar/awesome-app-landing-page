Description

This ocean front property features direct access to the beach and rooms with sweeping ocean views. An on site restaurant is also available. Lincoln City is 15 minutes’ drive away.

A flat-screen TV with a DVD player and a semi-private balcony are available in all rooms at the Surfrider Resort. A microwave, small fridge, and coffee and tea-making facilities are provided.

Guests can relax in the indoor swimming pool and hot tub at the Resort Surfrider. A sauna is also available. Laundry facilities is provided for convenience.

The Surfrider Restaurant and Lounge offers guests a casual dining experience with an ocean view. Open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, guests can enjoy seafood favorites like fish and chips and oysters, or diner specialties in a relaxed atmosphere.

Depoe Bay is less than 10 minutes’ drive from the resort. Oregon Coast Aquarium is 30 minutes’ drive from the property. Salishan Golf Course is less than 4 miles away.
