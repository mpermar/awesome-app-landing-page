Description

Each fully-equipped tiny home features a flat-screen TV, a fully-stocked kitchen, and a shower.

Additional facilities available on site include a disc golf course, shared lounge area with a fireplace and barbecue facilities. A number of outdoor activities can be enjoyed in the area, such as hiking and canoeing.

Tillamook Forest Center is 26 mi away, the Tillamook Cheese Factory is 3.1 mi, and the Garibaldi Maritime Museum is 5 mi from Sheltered Nook On Tillamook Bay.
