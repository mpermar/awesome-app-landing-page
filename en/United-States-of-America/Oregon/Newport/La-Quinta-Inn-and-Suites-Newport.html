Description

A flat-screen cable TV is included in all guest rooms at the La Quinta Inn and Suites Newport. A microwave, small fridge, and coffee-making facilities are provided.

An indoor pool and hot tub are available for guest relaxation at the Newport La Quinta Inn and Suites. A business center and fitness center are featured.

The Hatfield Marine Science Center is just 1 miles from the hotel. The South Beach State Park is 1.5 miles away.
