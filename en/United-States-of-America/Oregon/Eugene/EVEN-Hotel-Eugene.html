Description

Each room features in-room fitness equipment in addition to a fitness center located on-site. Certain rooms feature stationary bicycles. Rooms also come with a flat-screen TV. Private bathrooms feature spa showers.

A complimentary shuttle to and from Eugene airport is offered. The on-site market and bar, Cork & Kale, features heart healthy and gluten-free meal options.

EVEN Hotel Eugene is within minutes' of downtown Eugene, Alton Baker Park, the Willamette River, and the University of Oregon. Mahlon Sweet Field Airport is 8.1 mi from the property.
