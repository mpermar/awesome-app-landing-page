Description

A microwave and fridge furnish every spacious room at Inn at Wecoma. The beach-themed rooms include a DVD player and free Wi-Fi.

Inn at Wecoma provides a deluxe continental breakfast each morning. A hot tub and indoor pool are featured for guest relaxation. A business center and fitness center are also available.

Devils Lake Golf Course and Tanger Outlet Center are each a 5-minute drive from this hotel. Devils Lake State Park is 1.5 miles away.
