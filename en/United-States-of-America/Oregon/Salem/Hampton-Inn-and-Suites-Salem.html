Description

A flat-screen TV with cable is featured in each room at Hampton Inn and Suites Salem. Comfortably furnished, all guest rooms come equipped with coffee-making facilities.

Salem Hampton Inn and Suites offers a variety of services including a 24-hour front desk and dry cleaning. A convenience store is on site. Guests have access to the fitness center.

Salem's Riverfront Carousel is 4 miles from this hotel. A C Gilbert's Discovery Village is an 11 minute drive away.
