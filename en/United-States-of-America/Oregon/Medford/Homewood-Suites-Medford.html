Description

A sitting area with a sofa bed and a flat-screen TV are furnished in all traditional rooms at Homewood Suites Medford. A work desk, clock radio and hairdryer are also included.

Free breakfast is provided daily at the Medford Homewood Suites. In addition, guests can enjoy a free manager’s reception Monday through Thursday with a light meal and beverage selection.

Fitness and business facilities are located on-site. A convenience store is also available.

Centennial Golf Course and Roxy Ann Winery are within an 8-minute drive of this hotel. Craterian Ginger Rogers Theater is just 2 miles away.
