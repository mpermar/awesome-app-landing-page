Description

Each guest room at the Hampton Inn & Suites Bend comes with a cable flat-screen TV, air conditioning, and a coffee maker. A private bathroom with a bathtub or shower and free toiletries is included.

Guests can relax after a long day with a dip in the hot tub, or exercise in the fitness center. A mini-market, business center, and laundry facilities can be found on site for added convenience.

Bend Hampton Inn & Suites is a 2-minute drive from the Les Schwab Amphitheater and a 7-minute walk from the Deschutes Brewery. Mt. Bachelor Ski Resort is 20 miles away.
