Description

The Residence Inn by Marriott Portland Airport at Cascade Station has large suites with separate sleeping and living areas. The kitchen is equipped with stove, dishwasher and conventional oven. The 32 inch flat-screen TV has cable channels.

Guests can go for a swim in the indoor saltwater pool or relax in the hot tub. The on-site fitness center comes with cardio equipment and free weights.

The hotel serves a extended hot breakfast buffet every morning. Guests can mix in with other guests at the social evenings, which are organised 3 times a week.

Colwood Golf Course is less than a mile away from the hotel.

Portland Airport Area is a great choice for travelers interested in scenery, sightseeing and shopping.
