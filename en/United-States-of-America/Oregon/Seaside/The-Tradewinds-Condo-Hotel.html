Description

Each unit here offers a well-equipped kitchenette or kitchen with a microwave and a refrigerator. A seating area, a flat-screen TV with a DVD player, and a private bathroom with free toiletries are included.

Barbecue facilities, a daily maid service, and a shuttle service are available on site at The Tradewinds Condo/Hotel. Free parking is offered. The property offers guest passes for use of a local sports club, featuring a swimming pool, hot tub, fitness center, and fitness classes.

The center of Seaside, with its many shopping and dining options, is just 10 minutes walk away. Seaside Golf Course is 2.3 mi from the aparthotel.
