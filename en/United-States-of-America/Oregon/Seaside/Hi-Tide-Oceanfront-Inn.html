Description

This property is 2 minutes walk from the beach. This Seaside, Oregon resort offers guests an indoor heated pool and a hot tub. Located off Highway 101, Lewis and Clark National Historical Park is 20-minutes’ drive away.

Free Wi-Fi are featured in all rooms at the Hi Tide Resort. Rooms also include a fireplace, a cable TV and DVD player and ample seating area. Select rooms have balconies with ocean views and kitchenettes.

Seaside city center is within walking distance of the Seaside Hi Tide Resort. Seaside Aquarium is a 10-minute walk from the resort and Seaside Municipal Airport is 5-minutes drive away.

A daily continental breakfast and free parking are offered to guests at the resort.
