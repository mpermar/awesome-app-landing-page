Description

Guest rooms provide desks and seating areas. They also include ironing facilities, hairdryers and coffee makers.

Chattanooga/Downtown Hampton Inn and Suites serves a breakfast buffet and offers guests a 24-hour business center and meeting facilities.

The hotel is within a half mile of Creative Discovery Museum, Chattanooga Riverwalk and Chattanooga Ghost Tours. Hampton Inn Chattanooga is one mile from the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga.
