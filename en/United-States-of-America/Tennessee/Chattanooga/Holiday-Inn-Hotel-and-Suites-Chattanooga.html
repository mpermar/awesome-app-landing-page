Description

Rodizio Brazilian Steakhouse serves churrascaria-style grilled meats for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. A gourmet salad bar, traditional Brazilian side items, and daily fish selection are also offered.

Guests can enjoy the city view from the seasonal rooftop pool or use the free city-wide shuttle, which stops outside the hotel. A fitness center and meeting facilities (1700 square feet) are on site.

A flat-screen TV, coffee machine, and en suite are standard in each room. Extras include a contemporary-style desk with an ergonomic chair, microwave, and refrigerator as well as room service (surcharge).

The Tennessee River is 2,650 feet from this Chattanooga Holiday Inn Hotel & Suites, while Lookout Mountain is 3.1 mi away. The nearest airport is Lovell Field Airport, 6.2 mi from the hotel.
