Description

Featuring a seasonal outdoor swimming pool, The Smoke House Lodge is located in Monteagle.

Providing a restaurant, the property also has a garden. The accommodation features free WiFi throughout the property.

At the hotel the rooms are fitted with a desk, bed linen and a terrace with a lake view.

Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a hot tub and free toiletries, while some rooms include a balcony.

All rooms feature a wardrobe. Breakfast is available daily, and includes American, Full English/Irish and vegetarian options.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike hire is available at this 3-star hotel.

For any tips on how to get around or what to do in the area, guests can ask at the reception.
