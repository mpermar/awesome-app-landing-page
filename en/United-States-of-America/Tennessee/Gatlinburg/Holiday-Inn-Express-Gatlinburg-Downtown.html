Description

One of our top picks in Gatlinburg.

Holiday Inn Express Gatlinburg Downtown is nestled in the foothills of the The Great Smoky Mountains. A complimentary cold and hot breakfast is served daily in the lobby. Guests can enjoy the hotel's water slides, heated indoor pool, and children's splash zone.

Free WiFi and parking are offered on site. A 24-hour front desk and fitness center are available for added convenience.

Each room has a flat-screen TV, microwave, small refrigerator, and Keurig® coffee machine. The private bathroom has a hairdryer.

In the surroundings, Ripley's Aquarium of the Smokies is 0.7 mi away, and the Gatlinburg Space Needle is 4 minutes' walk from the hotel. Gatlinburg Convention Center is only 2 minutes' walk away from the Holiday Inn Express.
