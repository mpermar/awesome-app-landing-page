Description

Guest accommodations at the Holiday Inn Club Vacations-Smoky Mountain Resort are furnished with all the comforts of home, including DVD players, dishware, stoves and ovens. The hotel also offers high-speed internet access.

While staying at the Smoky Mountain Resort Holiday Inn Club Vacations, guests can enjoy relaxing in the indoor or outdoor swimming pool, browsing for souvenirs in the gift shop, or playing in the game room.

Ober Gatlinburg, a year-round amusement park complete with a ski resort can be found only minutes from the Smoky Mountain Holiday Inn Club Vacations. The Hard Rock Cafe, Ripley's Aquarium and downtown Gatlinburg are also nearby.
