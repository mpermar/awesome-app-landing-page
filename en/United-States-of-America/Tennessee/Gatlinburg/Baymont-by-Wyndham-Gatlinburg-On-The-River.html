Description

A cable TV and work desk are provided in each warmly decorated room at Baymont by Wyndham Gatlinburg On The River. A microwave, refrigerator, and coffee machine are included.

A year-round, indoor pool, game room, and fitness center are available on site. Guests can also request fax and photocopy services.

Ober Gatlinburg Ski Resort and the Gatlinburg Convention Center are both just 2 minutes’ drive from Baymont by Wyndham Gatlinburg On The River. Dollywood is 11 minutes’ drive away.
