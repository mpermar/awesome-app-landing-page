Description

All guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a TV with cable channels. All rooms come with a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries. All rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

A continental breakfast is served each morning at the property.

A business center with a fax machine and photocopier is at guests' disposal at La Quinta Inn & Suites Cleveland. Speaking English and Spanish at the 24-hour front desk, staff are ready to help around the clock.
