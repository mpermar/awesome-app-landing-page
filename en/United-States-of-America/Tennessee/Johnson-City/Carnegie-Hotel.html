Description

A flat-screen TV and a mini-fridge are equipped in all rooms at Carnegie Hotel. The classic rooms are filled with traditional décor and light colors; they include a laptop safe box and a work desk. Most offer a seating area with a sofa bed.

Hotel Carnegie offers on-site dining at Wellington’s. The restaurant serves modern southern cuisine in a formal dining atmosphere. A variety of liquor and wine selections are available, and the restaurant offers occasional live entertainment.

A gym and a business center are offered on site for guest use. Meeting rooms, laundry facilities and dry cleaning services are also available.

This hotel is less than a 10-minute drive from the entrance of Roan Mountain State Park and Pine Oaks Golf Club.
