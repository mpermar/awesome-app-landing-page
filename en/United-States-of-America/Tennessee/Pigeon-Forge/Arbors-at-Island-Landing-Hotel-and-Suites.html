Description

A small refrigerator, cable TV, and a microwave are available in every room of this Pigeon Forge hotel. A desk and flat-screen TV are included. Select rooms offer a balcony with mountain views, spa bath and sofa beds.

The pancake breakfast includes coffee and is served across the street at Regan's House of Pancakes. A full breakfast buffet is offered for an additional fee. Doughnuts, coffee, and tea with hot cider are available every day in the lobby.

Guests can enjoy roasting marshmallows by the fire pit, games and puzzles by the lobby fireplace, and fishing at the Arbors at island Landing Hotel. Sport balls, horseshoes, and frisbees can be signed out at the front desk.

There on-site pool and hot tub are open 24-hours. Guests can make use of the barbecue facilities.

The hotel is just 1.9 mi from the Dixie Stampede Dinner Show, 2.5 mi from Dollywood, and a 2-minute walk from the Comedy Barn Theater. The Fast Tracks Amusement Park is a 5-minute drive away. McGhee Tyson Airport is 24 mi from the property.
