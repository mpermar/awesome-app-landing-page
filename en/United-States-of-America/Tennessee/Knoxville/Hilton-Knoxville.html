Description

Guestrooms are furnished with luxurious linens, flat-screen TVs and MP3 compatible radios.

Onsite at the Knoxville Hilton, guests will find a modern fitness center, complete with an outdoor pool and sundeck. The hotel also features an onsite restaurant and a lounge. An outdoor bar and patio with a fire pit are also available.

The Knoxville Zoo is 6 mi from the property. Dollywood is 35 mi drive away.
