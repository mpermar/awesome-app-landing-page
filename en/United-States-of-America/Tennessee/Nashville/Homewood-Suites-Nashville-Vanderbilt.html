Description

A cable TV and full kitchen are included in all rooms at this Nashville Vanderbilt Homewood Suites by Hilton. An iPod docking station, desk, and separate sitting area are provided.

Guests can check email in the business center or break a sweat in the fitness center. A laundromat and a snack shop are available to all Homewood Suites by Hilton Nashville Vanderbilt guests as well.

A replica of the Greek Parthenon, built for the 1867 Centennial Exposition, is 1 mile away. The property is also 9 miles from the Nashville Zoo at Grassmere.
