Description

The apartment features air conditioning, a seating area, and a patio/terrace. The dining area includes a microwave, a refrigerator, and kitchenware. A hairdryer and a shower are featured in the private bathroom. You can enjoy mountain view and garden view from the room.

Guests have access to a shared hot tub and barbecue facilities. Meeting facilities, a games room, and a children's playground are available and activities in the surrounding area include cycling and hiking. The property offers free parking.

The property is 13 miles' drive from Nissan Stadium and 17 miles' drive from The District, Nashville's city center. Nashville International Airport is located 28 minutes' drive from the property.
