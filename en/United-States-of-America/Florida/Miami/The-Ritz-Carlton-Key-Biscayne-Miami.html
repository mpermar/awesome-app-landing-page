Description

The 25,000-square-foot spa encompasses saunas, hot tubs, and a eucalyptus steam room. Spa showers come infused with Vitamin C. A full service hair salon, fitness center, and movement studio help complete your spa experience.

Suites include a separate living room while all accommodations include full or half balconies. Marble bathrooms feature a separate bath and shower.

Lightkeepers offers oceanfront dining for breakfast, lunch, and dinner and serves modern-coastal dishes with a local edge. Looking for Old Havana glamor? Cuban-inspired Rumbar serves more than 52 rums, piscos, and cacha.

Indulge in a luxury shopping experience at Brickell City Center, located 8.5 mi drive from the accommodations. Key Biscayne Yacht Club and Miami Marine Stadium are within a 10-minute drive of The Ritz-Carlton Key Biscayne, Miami.
