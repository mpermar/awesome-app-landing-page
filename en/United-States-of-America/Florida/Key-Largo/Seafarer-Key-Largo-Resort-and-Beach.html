Description

The breakfast includes a selection of fruit, yogurt, and pastries. Fresh coffee, tea, and juice are also served.

All rooms are air conditioned and feature a refrigerator, microwave, and flat-screen cable TV. Private bathrooms are also provided. Villas and apartments at this Key Largo Seafarer Resort include a fully equipped kitchen and separate seating area.

Guests can recline on beach hammocks and enjoy the breeze or explore Florida waters on a free kayak rental. Free on-site parking and barbecue facilities are also offered at Seafarer Key Largo Resort and Beach.

The property is 5 miles from both the John Pennekamp Coral Reef State Park and the Tavernier Towne II Shopping Center.
