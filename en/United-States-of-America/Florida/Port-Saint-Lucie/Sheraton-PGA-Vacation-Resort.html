Description

A kitchenette, separate living room, and dining room are provided in all Sheraton PGA Vacation Resort villas. Select villas have a fully equipped kitchen and a golf course view.

The climate-controlled pool is available to all guests throughout their stay at this Port Saint Lucie Sheraton PGA Vacation Resort property. A children's pool and children's playground are also available.

Jensen Beach Park is 31 minutes' drive away. The property is also 7 minutes' drive from Cashmere Corners shopping center and 11 mi from Savannas Preserve State Park.
