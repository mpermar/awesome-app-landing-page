Description

Homewood Suites Port Saint Lucie-Tradition suites include a 32-inch flat-screen TV and free Wi-Fi. The suites are also equipped with a coffee maker and a microwave.

Port Saint Lucie Homewood Suites provides guests with a daily breakfast buffet. The hotel also offers a Monday through Thursday evening reception.

Guests at the Homewood Suites can swim in the pool or work out in the gym.

Homewood Suites Tradition is within a 5-minute drive of the PGA Village, which features 3 golf courses. Jensen Beach is 15 mi from the hotel.
