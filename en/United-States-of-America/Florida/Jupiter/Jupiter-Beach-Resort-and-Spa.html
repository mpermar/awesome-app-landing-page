Description

Jupiter Beach features a state-of-the art 698 square yard spa that offers a variety of pampering services. Relax with specialized treatments such as the SweThai massage or a custom facials that will leave guests feeling radiant both inside and out.

Enjoy several water activities on the Jupiter Beach Resort's 1,0 feet of private beach. Guests can also take a swim in the ocean-side pool or sip a drink and listen to live music at the poolside bar.
