Description

A private balcony with a waterfront view of the Atlantic Ocean or Intracoastal waterway is featured in each room. A flat-screen TV, coffee machine, and private bathroom with a walk-in rain shower, bathrobes, free toiletries, and a hairdryer are included.

Eight restaurants are located on site at Margaritaville Hollywood Beach Resort. A surfing simulator and fitness center are also featured on site. Private parking is available for guests' convenience.

Over 30 live bands are featured at Margaritaville Hollywood Beach Resort. The 11th-floor offers an adult-only pool, complete with views of the Atlantic Ocean and access to License to Chill Bar.

Oceanwalk Mall is only 2,150 feet away and Dania Beach Marina is 2.7 mi from Margaritaville Resort. The nearest airport is Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport, which is 5.9 mi from the property.
