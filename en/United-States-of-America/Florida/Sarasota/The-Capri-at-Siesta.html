Description

Each air-conditioned villa at The Capri is furnished with a flat-screen cable TV, dining area, and free Wi-Fi. The kitchen includes a microwave, refrigerator, stove, and dishwasher. A sofa bed is in the living area.

Guests of Capri at Siesta can relax in the courtyard garden and use the barbecue equipment. Beach chairs and cabanas are available to rent and parking is free.

Downtown Siesta Key is 3 miles from The Capri at Siesta and offers a variety of shopping and dining options. Selby Botanical Gardens and Stoneybrook Golf Club are both a 12-minute drive from the hotel.
