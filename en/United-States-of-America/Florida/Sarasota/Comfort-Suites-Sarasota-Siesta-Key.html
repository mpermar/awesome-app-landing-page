Description

A flat-screen cable TV and a seating area are featured in each accommodations at the nonsmoking Sarasota Comfort Suites. All suites also include a small refrigerator, coffee machine, microwave, and private bathroom with a hairdryer.

A hot tub, 24-hour front desk, and mini-market are available at the property as well as luggage storage and laundry facilities. Comfort Suites Sarasota offers free parking and free daily newspapers for guests' convenience.

Myakka River State Park is 14 minutes' drive from the hotel, and Marie Selby Botanical Gardens are 17 minutes from the property. The John and Mable Ringling Museum of Art and St. Armand's Circle are each 23 minutes' drive away.
