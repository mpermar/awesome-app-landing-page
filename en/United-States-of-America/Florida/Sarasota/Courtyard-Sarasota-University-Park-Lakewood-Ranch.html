Description

At Courtyard University Park, guest suites are furnished with a microwave and a refrigerator. They also include cable TV and coffee facilities.

The lobby offers a 52-inch flat-screen display with weather and flight notifications. Guests can relax on the lakeside patio with a fire pit. A 24-hour business center is on site with 980-square-feet of meeting space.

The Bistro serves healthy morning food and beverage options with Starbucks coffee. Evening dining options are available with cocktails.

The Mall at UTC is 13 minutes' walk away. Courtyard by Marriott Sarasota is 14 mi from Mote Marine Lab & Aquarium. Interstate 75 is less than 1 mi away.
