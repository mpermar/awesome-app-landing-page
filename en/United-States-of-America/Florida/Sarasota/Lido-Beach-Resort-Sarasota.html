Description

A microwave, refrigerator and coffee machine are standard in every guest room at the Resort Lido Beach. Most rooms provide a fully equipped kitchenette, and some suites have a beach view.

Two beachfront pools are available; 1 family pool and 1 adults-only pool. The Lido Beach Resort also has 3 hot tubs, a fitness center, and the Beachside Tiki Bar.

Central Sarasota and Sarasota Jungle Gardens are less than 5.6 mi from the resort. The Mote Marine Laboratory is 2.1 mi from Lido Beach Resort Sarasota.
