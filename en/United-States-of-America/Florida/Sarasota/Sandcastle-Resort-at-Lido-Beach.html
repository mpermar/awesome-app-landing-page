Description

A refrigerator and coffee maker are provided in the guest rooms at Sandcastle Resort at Lido Beach.

Gourmet dining is offered at the Candlelight Restaurant and Lounge, specializing in steaks and seafood. The Poolside Bar serves cocktails and snacks while breakfast and lunch are available at the casual Sandcastle Coffee Shop.

The Sandcastle Resort features an on-site gym, a volleyball court, and table tennis. Guests can rent bicycles to explore the island.

The Mote Marine Aquarium is a 10-minute drive from Sandcastle Helmsley. The Sarasota Jungle Gardens are 6 miles from the property.
