Description

Residence Inn Gainesville I-75 provides accommodations with areas for working, eating and relaxing. Kitchens include a microwave, coffee maker and fridge. Each suite has a large desk with task lighting.

Guests can enjoy hot breakfast in the morning, with grocery shopping services available throughout the day. Residence Inn Gainesville has a patio with a fire pit and barbecue area for outdoor dining.

The hotel is located 5 miles from Ben Hill Griffin Stadium and it is a 10 minute drive from North Florida Regional Medical Center.
