Description

Residence Inn Sandestin offers suites and studios with living and dining areas. Kitchens come with stove-tops and refrigerators. Tea- and coffee-making facilities are also provided.

Guests at this Grand Boulevard hotel can work out at the fitness center which features cardio equipment and a weight machine. A sports court is also on site. A social hour with snacks is offered on select weeknights.

The Northwest Florida Regional Airport is 28 mi away. Shopping and dining at Boulevard 10 is a 7 minutes' walk away.
