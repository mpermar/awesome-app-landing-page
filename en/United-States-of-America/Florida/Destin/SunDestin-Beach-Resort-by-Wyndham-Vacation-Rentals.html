Description

A kitchen with a dishwasher, microwave, and stove is in SunDestins air-conditioned apartments. Each comes with a bright dcor and a living room including a sofa bed and cable TV with DVD player. All come with a bathroom and a guest toilet.

SunDestin Beach has a spa on site. Guests can also work out in the fitness center. A coin-operated laundry facility is available on each floor.

Silver Sands Factory Stores is 4 miles from the resort, and Destin - Fort Walton Beach Airport is 30 minutes' drive away. Free parking is available at SunDestin Beach.
