Description

All apartments feature a microwave, small refrigerator, and cable TV. Some villas at Topsl Beach and Racquet Club by Wyndham Vacation Rentals also include a spa tub and private balcony.

Wyndham Vacation Rentals' Topsl Beach and Racquet Club provides guests a chance to relax in the sauna or massage facilities from a long day on the beach. Video rentals are available as well for added convenience.

Guests are 5 miles from the Silver Sands Factory Stores and 9 miles from Big Kahuna Water and Adventure Park. The Destin History and Fishing Museum is 12 miles away.
