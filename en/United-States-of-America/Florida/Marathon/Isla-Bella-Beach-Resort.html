Description

Situated in Marathon, within 7 km of Conch-Air Florida Keys Scenic Flights and 10 km of Florida Keys Aquarium Encounters, Isla Bella Beach Resort features a private beach area.

Located around 6 km from Seven Mile Bridge, the resort is also 10 km away from Captain Hook s Marina Dive Center.

A Deep Blue Dive Center is 11 km away. At the resort, rooms have a terrace. Rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV, and certain units at Isla Bella Beach Resort have a balcony.

The rooms feature a private bathroom with a bath. The property has a full breakfast menu to choose from.

There is an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of Italian dishes. The wellness area at the accommodation is comprised of a hot tub and a fitness centre.

Guests at Isla Bella Beach Resort will be able to enjoy activities in and around Marathon, like cycling and fishing.

The resort also provides a business centre and guests can use the on-site ATM machine, check the newspapers or use the fax machine and photocopier at Isla Bella Beach Resort.

Round-the-clock assistance is available at the reception. Dolphin Research Center is 19 km from the accommodation.

The nearest airport is Florida Keys/Marathon International Airport, 8. 1 km from Isla Bella Beach Resort.
