Description

There is a dining area and a kitchen as well as a private bathroom. Towels and bed linen are featured in this self-catering accommodations. Other facilities at Luxury Pool Homes @ Solterra include a year-round outdoor pool and a hot tub.

Guests can enjoy a volleyball court, tennis court, and picnic area. A children's playground and business center are also available, as is a community pool.

Kissimmee is 12 mi from Luxury Pool Homes @ Solterra, while Lakeland is 25 mi away. The nearest airport is Orlando International Airport, 22 mi from Luxury Pool Homes @ Solterra.
