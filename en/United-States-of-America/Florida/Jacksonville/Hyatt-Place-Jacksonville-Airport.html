Description

Free Wi-Fi is included in each room at this Jacksonville Airport hotel and all rooms have a sofa bed. A small refrigerator and a flat-screen TV are also provided.

A 24-hour fitness center is available at the Jacksonville Airport Hyatt Place along with an outdoor pool. A laundromat is available as well as a business center.

This hotel is just off Interstate 95, less than a 15 minute walk from the River City Marketplace. Downtown Jacksonville is 12 miles away.
