Description

All guest rooms feature a microwave, small refrigerator, and coffee-making facilities. Select suites at the Resort and Club at Little Harbor offer a fully-equipped kitchenette, washer with tumble dryer, and water view.

Guests can get work done in the business center or break a sweat in fitness center. The Resort and Club at Little Harbor offers a massage service, 24-hour reception, and on-site shops. A 6,000-square foot meeting facility is available.

Sunset Grill serves grilled American Caribbean cuisine for breakfast, lunch, and dinner with views of Tampa Bay. Sunset Lounge features indoor and outdoor seating with live entertainment, while the beachside Tiki Bar offers light fare and drinks.

Manatees are 10 miles away at the Manatee Viewing Center. The Florida Aquarium and Channelside Bay Plaza are 24 miles away.
