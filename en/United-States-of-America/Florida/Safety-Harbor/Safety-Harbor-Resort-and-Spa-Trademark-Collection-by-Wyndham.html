Description

The Safety Harbor Resort provides guests with a variety of luxurious amenities and facilities. With 3 swimming pools, a spacious full-service spa and a state-of-the-art fitness center, every stay is sure to be enjoyable. Guests can also stop by the front desk for special discounts on spa packages.

The area surrounding the Safety Harbor is filled with beautiful natural scenery, picturesque views and a variety of activities. Guests can find numerous water sports, entertainment and unique shops minutes from the hotel.
