Description

Every room at the Holiday Inn Express & Suites Panama City Beach is oceanfront and offers panoramic views of the Gulf of Mexico. A coffee maker, seating area and hair dryer are provided along with cable TV and free WiFi.

Holiday Inn Express & Suites Panama City Beach has an outdoor pool and chairs. Guests can use the business center and laundry facilities.

Shipwreck Island WaterPark, Wonderworks interactive museum and Gulf World Marine Park are all within a 5-minute drive away.
