Description

Rooms at Eden Roc Miami Beach offer a flat-screen TV and an iPod docking station in each media center. Select suites include balconies and panoramic views. An en suite safe and small refrigerator are provided.

Schedule a private yacht charter at Eden Roc’s marina or enjoy jet skiing nearby. Indoor and outdoor event and meeting spaces are available by reservation. The 24-hour fitness center offers additional services including personal trainers and fitness classes.

Guests can enjoy organic farm-to-table dining at Malibu Farm restaurant. Nobu Restaurant serves Japanese-fusion cuisine among modern Japanese-inspired decor. The poolside restaurant is also available for quick dining in between swimming and sunbathing.

Bass Museum of Art is 7 minutes’ drive while Lincoln Road Mall is 11 minutes’ drive from the hotel. Miami International Airport is 11 mi away.
