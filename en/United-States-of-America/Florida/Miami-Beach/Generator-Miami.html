Description

Generator Miami has a restaurant, free bikes, a bar and shared lounge in Miami Beach.

Featuring a garden, the 3-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom.

The accommodation offers nightclub and a 24-hour front desk. The hotel offers a sun terrace.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike hire and car hire are available at Generator Miami.

Holocaust Memorial is 2. 4 km from the accommodation, while Miami Beach Convention Center is 2.

4 km from the property. The nearest airport is Miami International Airport, 18 km from Generator Miami.
