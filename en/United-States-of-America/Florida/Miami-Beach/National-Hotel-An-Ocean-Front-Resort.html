Description

Boasting Miami Beach’s longest outdoor lap pool at 200 feet long, this hotel is just 4 blocks from the Ocean Drive boardwalk. It offers modern rooms and a poolside cabana spa.

A cable TV and a mini-bar are standard in all rooms at The National Hotel. Rooms feature light colors and city or ocean views. A bathrobe and slippers are provided for guest convenience.

The restaurant at Hotel National serves small dishes in an intimate atmosphere for breakfast, lunch and dinner. It also provides a full bar and offers dining under the restaurant’s mosaic ceiling or on the poolside patio.

Fitness and business facilities are located on-site. A concierge and a tour desk with ticket services are also available, as is a second, heated pool.

Miami Beach Golf Club is a 5-minute drive, and Miami Beach Convention Center is a 3-minute drive from the hotel. Jungle Island is 4.7 miles away.
