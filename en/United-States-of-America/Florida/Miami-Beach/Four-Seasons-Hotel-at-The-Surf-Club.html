Description

Oceanfront dining is possible at the Amalfi Coast-inspired Le Sirenuse Miami and Champagne Bar & Lounge, which is a sister restaurant to the Michelin-starred Le Sirenuse's La Sponda in Positano, Italy.

The property features architecture by Pritzker-prize winning architect, Richard Meier, and interior designer Joseph Dirandan. A holistic spa, fitness center, and kid's club are offered. The front desk is open 24 hours.

Custom furnishings, a flat-screen TV, and floor-to-ceiling windows in addition to a marble bathroom are standard in every Four Seasons Hotel at The Surf Club room. Some rooms have a balcony with an ocean or city view, and 5 unique Cabana Studios are offered as well.

Bal Harbor Shops is 16 minutes' walk away, while the Miami Beach Convention Center is 5.6 mi from Four Seasons Hotel at The Surf Club. Lincoln Road is 6.2 mi from the property, and Miami International Airport is 15 mi away.
