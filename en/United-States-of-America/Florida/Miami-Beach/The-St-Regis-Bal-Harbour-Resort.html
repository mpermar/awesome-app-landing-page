Description

Rooms feature a flat-screen TV, iPod docking station, and modern décor curated by Yabu Pushelberg. Select suites include a kitchenette, complete with stovetop and kitchenware, and spacious balconies with views of the ocean.

The resort includes 11,400 square feet of meeting and event space. St Regis Athletic provides an on-site fitness center with ocean views and group classes that include yoga, cardio, and Pilates. Free internet access is included and butler service is available.

Atlantikós, the indoor-outdoor Greek restaurant, features both lounge and bar seating, while BH Burger Bar serves family-friendly meals, allowing guests to build their own burgers. St Regis Bar & Sushi Lounge features live music, vintages wines, and Asian-inspired dishes.

The resort is 8.7 mi drive from the sights of South Beach and 25 minutes' drive from Brickell, Miami's financial center. Miami International Airport is 15 mi drive from St Regis Bal Harbor Resort.

Bal Harbour is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, relaxation and restaurants.
