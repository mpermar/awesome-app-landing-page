Description

Hampton Inn Port Charlotte features an outdoor pool and on-site fitness center. Guests will also enjoy the business center with copy and fax services as well as in-room coffeemakers and cable TV.

The area surrounding the Port Charlotte Hampton Inn is filled with a variety of activities and attractions. Guests can kayak down the nearby Peace River or explore the shopping and dining opportunities at Fisherman's Village, which is just a short drive away.
