Description

Floor-to-ceiling windows and wooden tile floors decorate the modern rooms of the Wyndham Deerfield Beach Resort. Each features a private balcony, 37-inch flat-screen TV and a refrigerator.

Three oceanfront restaurants are featured on site, including Cafe Med, an authentic Italian restaurant serving breakfast and dinner daily, and Burger Craze, an award-winning restaurant featuring American cuisine. Patio Bar & Grille offers a third dining option and Vaniglia E Cioccolato serves authentic gelato.

Poolside drink and towel services are offered to all guests. Free WiFi, a fitness center and daily newspapers are also available.

The Deerfield Beach Wyndham Resort is 7 miles from the Pompano Beach Amphitheatre. The Children’s Museum is 10 minutes away.
