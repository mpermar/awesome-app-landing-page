Description

Every accommodations features a 47-inch flat-screen TV, small refrigerator, and tea and coffee-making facilities. A private balcony or patio is also provided.

The resort features a 100-foot water slide, golf course, and full-service spa. A sauna and hot tub are available, as are a games room and 15 tennis courts. Paddleboards and kayaks can be hired on site.

Naples Grande Beach Resort also features 5 restaurants and lounges as well as in-room dining. The beachfront restaurant, Rhode’s End, serves Mediterranean cuisine with Gulf of Mexico views.

Surrounded by 200 acres of mangrove estuary, this Naples resort is set 1.2 mi from Waterside Shops, a luxury shopping mall which also hosts restaurants and galleries.

Pelican Bay is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, relaxation and fine dining.
