Description

Nestled among 125 acres of tropical surroundings, this beachfront hotel and spa is located in Naples, Florida and features 2 beachside pools.

There is also a Jack Nicklaus designed 18-hole golf course on site. Unwind at the on-site spa, which offers massages, salon services, hydrotherapy, and more.

Yoga classes are available in the fitness centre and 6 tennis courts are located amid plush landscaping.

Children between the ages of 5 and 12 can enjoy a Kids Club. En suite amenities include a flat-screen TV, refrigerator, and coffee-making facilities.

Private bathrooms house a robe and hairdryer. Most rooms feature a patio or balcony.

This Naples Beach resort features 5 dining options, including HB’s On the Gulf, a beachfront restaurant that serves fresh seafood.

Free valet and self-parking is available. Tea and cookies are provided each afternoon.

Waterside Shops is 11 minutes’ drive from the property. Guests will be 14 minutes' drive from Naples Botanical Garden and 8 km from Naples Municipal Airport.
