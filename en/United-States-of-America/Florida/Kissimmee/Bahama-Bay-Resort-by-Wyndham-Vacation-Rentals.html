Description

Villas feature a full kitchen and cable TV with DVD player. A balcony is also included.

An on-site game room is offered at Bahama Bay Resort. A transfer service to and from Orlando theme parks is available for a charge as well.

Mambos, an on-site restaurant, offers casual poolside American dining. Tradewinds features a more elegant atmosphere with views of Lake Davenport and offers daily breakfast buffets, casual lunches, and a full dinner menu.

Universal Orlando Resort and SeaWorld Orlando are each a 25-minute drive from this resort. Old Town theme park is 11 miles away. Guests will be 23 minutes’ drive from the shopping venues at Orlando Premium Outlets - Vineland Avenue.
