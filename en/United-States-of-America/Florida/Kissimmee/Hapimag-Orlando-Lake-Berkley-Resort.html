Description

Hapimag Lake Berkley Resort, located in Kissimmee, is 1 miles from Medieval Times Dinner and Tournament and 10 miles from SeaWorld Orlando.

All units are 2 levels with bedrooms on 2nd level and feature a fully equipped kitchen, washer, and tumble dryer. A cable TV and telephone with voicemail are also included.

Guests staying at Lake Berkley Hapimag Resort can access a business center. The resort has a lake and 24-hour security and offers tennis courts and beach volleyball.
