Description

The full kitchen features sleek cabinetry, a dishwasher, and an oven. Modern rooms include a flat-screen TV, a game console, and a DVD player. The private bathroom houses a bath or shower. A patio is also included.

The Fountains at ChampionsGate offers a number of facilities, including a barbecue, a fitness center, and a children's playground. All town-homes include one parking space in the garage and another one outside the garage.

Guests are only a 12 minutes' drive from The Walt Disney World Resort and a 16 minutes' drive from the shops of Orlando Premium Outlets - Vineland. Orlando International Airport is 26 minutes' drive.
