Description

A separate sitting area with a sofa bed and flat-screen TV is provided in every suite at Star Island Resort and Club. Rooms feature light colors and offer a laptop safe box, DVD player and clock radio.

Tennis, basketball or volleyball courts, and mini golf facilities are just part of the many activities Star Island Resort offers on property. Later, relax in the sauna or beauty salon. Extensive fitness facilities are also available. There is a convenience store fully stocked with sandwiches, ice cream, and a variety of travel essentials on site at Star Island Resort and Club.

SeaWorld Orlando is 8.1 mi from this resort. Lake Buena Vista Factory Stores are just a 6-minute drive away.
