Description

Located 10 minutes' drive from Disney World, this Orlando resort features an over-sized movie theater and apartments include kitchens and WiFi.

The Tuscana Resort boasts apartments with a screened in private porch, some with golf course views.

They are furnished with a dining area and a separate living room. Washers and dryers are equipped in each unit.

A fully equipped fitness centre and a business centre are available at the property.

Resort Tuscana also features a large outdoor heated pool with cabanas. There is also a childrens pool with a fountain.

SeaWorld Orlando is 24. 3 km from the resort. Guests are also 31. 2 km from Universal Studios.
