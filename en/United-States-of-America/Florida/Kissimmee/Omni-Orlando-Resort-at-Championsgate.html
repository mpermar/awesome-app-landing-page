Description

Located 12. 7 km from Walt Disney World, Omni Orlando Resort features 3 year-round outdoor pools, including a lazy river, family pool with water slide, wave pool and adult pool with cabanas.

The resort has a signature Mokara Spa and 9 restaurants. A flat-screen cable TV, small refrigerator, safety deposit box, and 2 telephones are provided in each of the 862 rooms, suites and villas at Omni Orlando Resort at Championsgate.

A hairdryer is included in each private bathroom. Surrounded by 36 holes of championship golf, the resort also features WiFi access, an on-site gift shop, and a 24-hour reception.

A children's playground, fitness centre, and courts for tennis and basketball. The property also features an 18 hole miniature golf course, lighted 9-hole, par 3 golf experience.

Tickets to theme parks and area attractions are available for purchase at the Omni Orlando Resort concierge desk.

Complimentary scheduled transfer service to Walt Disney World theme parks is offered.

Universal Studios Orlando is 21 minutes' drive from this resort. Guests will be 20 minutes' drive from shopping at Orlando Vineland Premium Outlets, and 30 minutes' drive from Orlando International Airport.
