Description

All guest rooms at the resort come with a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a kitchen. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a bath, while some rooms also offer a terrace. The rooms will provide guests with a dishwasher.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the in-house restaurant, which serves American cuisine.

Encantada Resort offers a fitness center. You can play tennis at the accommodation, and the area is popular for fishing.

Fun 'N' Wheels Fun Park is 12 miles from Encantada Resort. The nearest airport is Orlando International Airport, 21 miles from the property.
