Description

A living area with a cable TV is included in all units at Pines & Palms Resort. The traditionally styled units feature light colors and island decor. Most offer a private terrace with ocean views.

Guests at Resort Palms & Pines have access to kayak and bike rentals, private boat docks and premium sport fishing areas. A covered cabana with a barbecue grill, fridge and sink is available for guest use.

This resort is less than 1 mi from Miss Islamorada Fishing Charters. The entrance to John Pennekamp Coral Reef State Park is less than 30 minutes drive away.
