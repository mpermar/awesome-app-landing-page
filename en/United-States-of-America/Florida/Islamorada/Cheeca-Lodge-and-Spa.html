Description

Positioned on 27 acres of lush gardens and 1,000 feet of private waterfront, Cheeca Lodge & Spa is a luxury resort in Islamorada, Florida. It features a 9-hole par 3 golf course, on-site spa, and 525-foot fishing pier.

Each guestroom includes dark hardwood furniture and a balcony with views of the resort. Guests can watch movies on the 42-inch flat-screen TV with DVD player or browse the internet with free WiFi. An in-room safe and coffee maker are also provided.

Adult and family-style swimming pools can be enjoyed at the resort. Additionally, a fully equipped fitness center, 6 tennis courts, and a full-service watersports facility are open to guests.

Three restaurants and a tiki bar are located on-site at Cheeca Lodge & Spa. Atlantic’s Edge offers tropically-inspired cuisine amid a classic décor while Nikai Sushi Bar & Restaurant serves nigiri, sashimi, and more.

Downtown Key Largo is a 20-minute drive from Cheeca Lodge & Spa. Long Key State Park is 13.7 miles from the resort.
