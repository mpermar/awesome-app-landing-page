Description

Located on the waters of the Florida Bay, the Islander Bayside Resort is less than 5 minutes’ walk from the beach. This resort features a kitchen in every townhouse and an outdoor pool.

A flat-screen TV is included in every guest townhouse of this Islamorada resort and all units have free WiFi and laundry facilities. Kitchens offer a microwave, refrigerator and an oven.

Bicycle rental is possible on site and various water sport activities are located nearby.

There are various dining options less than 1 mi from this resort and Windley Key Fossil Reef Park is 5 minutes’ drive away. Long Key is 15 minutes’ drive from the property.
