Description

Situated on a half-mile of private beach, The Breakers Palm Beach offers luxury accommodation amid carefully landscaped grounds.

The resort features an on-site spa and 2 championship golf courses. Every accommodation includes a mini bar, an electronic safe, and plush bathrobes and slippers.

WiFi is complimentary, and many suites feature living areas and private balconies.

The resort houses 4 heated oceanfront pools and 5 hot tubs as well as premium poolside bungalows available for daily hire.

Ten Har-Tru tennis courts and private fitness and wellness coaching are also offered.

The free fitness offers group classes. The Breakers Palm Beach features 9 on-site restaurants, which offer everything from fine dining to late-night cocktails and desserts.

A children's playground is on site. In addition to 11 on-site boutiques, Worth Avenue is located 1.

5 km from the resort and provides an upscale shopping experience. Palm Beach International Airport is 7 km from the resort.
