Description

Guest rooms at the Four Seasons Resort Palm Beach are equipped with a flat-screen TV and private balcony. The marble bathroom features a soaking tub and separate glass-enclosed shower. Premier Rooms offer direct ocean views.

The oceanfront outdoor pool at the Palm Beach Four Seasons is surrounded by tropical gardens. Pool attendants offer towels, water and fresh-cut fruit. Poolside cabanas are available upon request.

Guests can enjoy 2 tennis courts at the Four Seasons Palm Beach or access to the nearby Atlantis Golf Club. Water activities including windsurfing, kayaking and sailing are available on site.

Jove Kitchen and Bar at the Four Seasons serves Italian cuisine with local influences. The hotel also offers worldly fares at AB&G and fresh breakfast, lunches, and Sunday brunches at Graze.
