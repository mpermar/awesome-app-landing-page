Description

Guest rooms at the Fairfield Inn and Suites Melbourne Palm Bay/Viera have a flat-screen TV and a coffee maker. A mini-refrigerator and a work desk are also included in each room.

An outdoor pool and a fitness center are on-site at the Melbourne Palm Bay/Viera Fairfield Inn and Suites. The hotel has free coffee in the lobby and on-site laundry facilities.

Brevard Zoo, Melbourne Beach, and King Center for the Performing Arts are within a 20-minute drive of this hotel.
