Description

Residence Inn by Marriott St. Petersburg Treasure Island boasts a beachfront location.

Guests can start their day with a complimentary buffet breakfast and then take a dip in the oceanfront pool.

Complimentary WiFi is provided for all guests. Guestrooms feature a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

A refrigerator, coffee maker, and microwave are provided as well. Select rooms feature a full kitchen and a separate living area.

A balcony is offered in all rooms. Residence Inn by Marriott St. Petersburg Treasure Island features a fitness centre.

Free on-site parking is also available. Guests can have a drink at the evening weekday reception.

A 24-hour convenience store is also on-site. John's Pass Boardwalk is 2 km from the hotel.
