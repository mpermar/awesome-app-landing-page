Description

DoubleTree Beach Resort by Hilton Tampa Bay – North Redington Beach is located directly on the beach and offers semi-private beach access.

The hotel features on-site dining and easy access to recreational activities. Rooms at the hotel include a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Every room comes with a private bathroom and the convenience of a small refrigerator.

Several rooms feature a balcony with views of the water. Guests can start the day with a cup of coffee from the in-room coffeemakers, and then relax in the outdoor swimming pool.

Business guests can take advantage of the 24-hour business centre. Enjoy casual cuisine and fresh seafood for breakfast, lunch and dinner at Mangos Veranda restaurant, located on-site.

The Dali Museum is 25. 3 km from the property. St. Pete–Clearwater International Airport is a 29 minutes' drive away.
