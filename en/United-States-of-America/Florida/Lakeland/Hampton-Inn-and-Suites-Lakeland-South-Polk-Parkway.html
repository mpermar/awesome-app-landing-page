Description

Hampton Inn & Suites Lakeland-South Polk Parkway features cable TV and a work desk in every guest room. Rooms are also equipped with a coffee maker and ironing facilities.

Guests at the Lakeland Hampton Inn can enjoy the gym. A business center with copy and fax services is also available.
