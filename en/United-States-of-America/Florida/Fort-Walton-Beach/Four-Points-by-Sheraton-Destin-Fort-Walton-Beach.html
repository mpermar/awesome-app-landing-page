Description

A microwave, small refrigerator, and 1 bottle of water are provided with every room at this Destin Four Points by Sheraton. Extras include free beach towels. Some rooms have views of the courtyard. Oceanfront rooms have a microwave and refrigerator.

The front desk is open 24 hours and provides express check-in and check-out. Access to a 24-hour business center is available for all guests throughout their stay. Water sports facilities are available on site for a fee.

Four Points by Sheraton Fort Walton Beach features dining at Dempsey’s Lounge and Restaurant, Paradise Cove, and KiWi's beach-front hut and café. Seasonal summer live entertainment is featured as well.

The property is next to the Gulfarium and the Boardwalk Entertainment Complex. Guests at the property are 4 miles from Elgin Air Force Base and a 15-minute drive from the Northwest Florida Regional Airport.
