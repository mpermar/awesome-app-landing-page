Description

A lake view, full kitchen with a microwave and a refrigerator, and cable TV are provided in all units. Lake Ida Beach Resort extras include an en suite, bed linen, and towels.

The reception is open 24 hours for added convenience. Barbecue facilities, terrace, and laundry facilities are also on site.

Historical Swanns Railroad Station is 6 mi away. The holiday park is also 7.1 mi from Legoland Florida and 12 minutes’ drive from Interstate 4 Exit 48.
