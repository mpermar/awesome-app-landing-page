Description

Hilton Garden Inn Pensacola Airport/Medical Center features a microwave, refrigerator and coffee maker in every guest room. A 37-inch flat-screen TV and mp3 docking station are also included.

Guests can enjoy the free 24-hour business center or the well-equipped gym.

The Great American Grill is open daily for breakfast and dinner. Guests can enjoy a snack in the 24-hour convenience store.

Downtown Pensacola and the Cordova Mall are within 5 miles of the Hilton Garden Inn Medical Center.
