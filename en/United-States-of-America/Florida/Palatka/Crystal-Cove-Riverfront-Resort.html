Description

Located along the Saint Johns River, Crystal Cove Riverfront Resort features boat rentals, boat docking facilities and a waterfront swimming pool. Free WiFi and a daily continental breakfast are available.

A microwave, refrigerator and coffee-making facilities are offered in each room at Crystal Cove Riverfront Resort. A cable TV is included.

Meeting and banquet facilities are on site at Crystal Cove Riverfront Resort. Guests can also enjoy access to onsite River Deck Cafe, which is open for breakfast and lunch.

Camp Blanding is 29.2 miles from Crystal Cove. Guests will be 31.7 miles from Saint Augustine city centre.
