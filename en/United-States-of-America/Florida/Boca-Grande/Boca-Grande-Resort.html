Description

Boca Grande Resort is located just a 7 minutes’ drive from beach access and shopping at the Historic Village of Boca Grande.

The resort features an on site marina, Uncle Henry’s Marina. Free WiFi is offered throughout the property.

A 42-inch flat-screen cable TV, microwave and refrigerator are included in each of the air-conditioned rooms at this resort.

A radio alarm clock and wake up service are available for added convenience. Feel pampered at the on-site spa and salon before relaxing at the outdoor pool.

Boca Grande Resort facilities and amenities include golf carts, bicycles, and a grocery store.

Wedding services are offered at the property as well. Gasparilla State Park is just 10 minutes’ drive from the property.
