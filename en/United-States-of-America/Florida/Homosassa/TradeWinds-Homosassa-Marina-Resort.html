Description

A resort clubhouse with an outdoor pool and a tennis court are on site. Popular area activities include biking, boating, fishing as well as golfing. Each villa also has a 2-car garage at this TradeWinds Homosassa Marina Resort.

Free WiFi, a separate living area with a flat-screen TV, and dining area are featured in all villas. Extras include a gas barbecue grill, a private terrace or patio, and and multiple private bathrooms.

Homosassa Springs Wildlife State Park manatee observation area is 1.2 mi from TradeWinds Homosassa Marina Resort, while Sugarmill Woods Country Club is 12 minutes' drive away. Monkey Island is 1.3 mi away.
