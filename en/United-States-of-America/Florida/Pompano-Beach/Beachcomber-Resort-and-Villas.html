Description

A coffee machine and work desk come in each room at the all non-smoking Beachcomber Resort and. A cable TV is also included.

The Beachcomber Resort has the Ocean Grill Restaurant, which serves a variety of meals including seafood. The poolside Tiki Bar provides tropical cocktails and light snacks.

The Port Everglades are a 15-minute drive away. The Pompano Beach Municipal Golf Course is 4 miles from the hotel.
