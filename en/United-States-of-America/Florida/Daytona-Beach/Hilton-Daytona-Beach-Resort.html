Description

On-site restaurants include Clocktower Lounge with American fare with a view of Daytona Beach and the historic Clocktower. Hyde Park Prime Steakhouse serves aged steaks, full bar, and a terrace menu. Atlantic Deli offers quick, fresh pastries and Starbucks Coffee.

You will find a 24-hour front desk, gift shop, and shops at Hilton Daytona Beach Resort. A children's activity club, car hire, and table tennis are available at the resort.

A flat-screen cable TV, as well as an MP3-compatible alarm clock radio are offered in every room. A terrace or patio are featured in certain rooms. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer. Cabana suites offers a separate seating area and 2 televisions.

Daytona International Speedway is 5 mi from Hilton Daytona Beach Resort, while Ponce de Leon Inlet Lighthouse Museum is 6.2 mi from the property. Daytona Beach Airport is 4.3 mi away.
