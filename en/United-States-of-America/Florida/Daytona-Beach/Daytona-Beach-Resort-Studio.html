Description

At the resort, rooms include a balcony. The rooms are equipped with a coffee machine, while selected rooms also offer a kitchenette with a microwave, a toaster and a fridge. All rooms at Daytona Beach Resort Studio have air conditioning and a closet.

At the accommodation guests are welcome to use a hot tub.

Daytona Beach Surfing School is a 5-minute walk from Daytona Beach Resort Studio, while Ocean Center is 2.7 miles away. The nearest airport is Daytona Beach International Airport, 6 miles from the property.
