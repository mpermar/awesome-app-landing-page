Description

Located directly on Daytona Beach, this hotel offers heated indoor and outdoor pools and a 60-foot waterslide.

Ocean Walk Village, offering shopping and entertainment, is 3 miles away. The brightly colored guest rooms at the Sun Viking Lodge feature cable TV, a microwave and fridge.

Some rooms include a full kitchen with full oven, stove and kitchenware. Select rooms feature a balcony.

Additional recreational facilities at the Sun Viking include an oceanfront hot tub, children's pool, gym and sauna.

Guests can also enjoy gas barbecue grills and a children's playground. Both parking and Wi-Fi are free.

Daytona International Speedway and Daytona Beach International Airport was within 8 miles of the hotel.
