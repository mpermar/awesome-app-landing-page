Description

Guest rooms at Perry’s Ocean Edge Resort all have a kitchen and a sitting area. The spacious rooms include cable TV and some rooms have a private balcony and free Wi-Fi.

Perry’s Ocean Edge Resort features a 10,000-ft² atrium, including an indoor heated pool and hot tub surrounded by tropical landscaping. A fitness center and kid’s program are also provided. During Bike Week and Biketoberfest, motorcycle parking is available for a minimal fee.

Coffee, juice and donuts are served daily at the Ocean Edge Resort. Drinks are available at the Tiki bar.

Daytona USA and the Museum of Arts and Sciences are a 10-minute drive from Perry’s Ocean Edge Resort. The Ponce de Leon Inlet Lighthouse is a 20-minute drive from the hotel.
