Description

A kitchenette comes standard in each room at Daytona Beach Resort and Conference Center. Select accommodations feature a full kitchen. Guests can also make use of a flat-screen cable TV and free toiletries.

A fitness center is on site at Daytona Beach Resort. A 24-hour business center is available. Meeting facilities and are offered for added guest convenience.

Daytona Beach Resort and Conference Center is located 40 feet from the Ocean Center Convention Complex. Daytona 500 Experience is 8 mi away.

Reflections restaurant presents a casually elegant atmosphere with a pristine ocean view. Guests can enjoy Florida-Caribbean fusion cuisine here, while tropical drinks, wine and beer are served at the oceanfront pool bar.
