Description

A refrigerator and coffee machine are standard in each room at Aloft Tallahassee Downtown. A flat-screen cable TV is included. Each private bathroom provides towels, a hairdryer, and free toiletries.

A free shuttle service is available at this Tallahassee Aloft hotel. Other facilities include a business center, a 24-hour front desk, and an on-site mini market. Daily maid service is available.

The Museum of Florida History is half a mile from this hotel. Guests are a 6-minute drive from shopping at Tallahassee Mall.
