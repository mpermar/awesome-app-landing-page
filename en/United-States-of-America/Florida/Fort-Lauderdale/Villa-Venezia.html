Description

Guest rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a coffee machine, a bath or shower, free toiletries and a closet. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a hair dryer, while some accommodations at the hotel also offer a terrace. All rooms will provide guests with a microwave.

Popular points of interest near Villa Venezia include The Galleria at Fort Lauderdale Shopping Center, Las Olas Boulevard and International Swimming Hall of Fame. The nearest airport is Fort Lauderdale Hollywood Airport, 4.3 miles from the property.
