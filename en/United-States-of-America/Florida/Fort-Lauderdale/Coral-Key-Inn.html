Description

Free Wi-Fi and cable TV are featured in each suite and studio of this Lauderdale hotel. Guests will enjoy the added convenience of a microwave in the kitchens and kitchenware is also provided.

A laundromat is available at the Coral Key Inn and free parking is available on site. Luggage storage is available as well as barbecue facilities.

This hotel is 5 miles from Fort Lauderdale Stadium, home of the Baltimore Oriole’s spring training. Pompano Park Racing is less than a 20 minute drive away.
