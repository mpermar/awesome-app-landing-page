Description

A kitchen with an oven, stove, microwave, and refrigerator is included in each Casa Del Sol apartment. A CD player and a DVD player are also provided. International calling plans are available for a surcharge.

Barbecue facilities are on site at Casa Del Sol Waterfront Resort and Marina. Laundry facilities are offered for guests’ convenience. For enjoyment, guests can ride bicycles, which are available for free.

Hugh Taylor Birch State Park, a beachfront park, is 1.2 mi from the property. Guests can go shopping at The Galleria, which is 2 minutes’ drive away. The Bonnet House Museum and Garden is 1.4 mi from Casa Del Sol.
