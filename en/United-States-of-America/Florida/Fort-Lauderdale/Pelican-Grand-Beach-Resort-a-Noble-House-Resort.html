Description

Beach-inspired guestrooms feature a small refrigerator and 42-inch flat-screen TV. In-room movies, games, and music are provided, as is a coffee maker.

Boasting unobstructed views of the Atlantic Ocean, the on-site fitness center and Pure Spa offers Swedish massages, cleansing facials, and other spa services.

Oceanfront dining and a poolside cocktail are available at Ocean 2000. The Emporium gift shop is an old-fashioned ice parlor that also serves bakery and coffee treats.

Enjoy an upscale shopping experience at The Galleria at Fort Lauderdale, just 6 minutes’ drive from the property. Broward County Convention Center is 10 minutes' drive.
