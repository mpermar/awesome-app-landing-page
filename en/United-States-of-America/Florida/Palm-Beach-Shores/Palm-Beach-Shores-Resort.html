Description

An endless number of activities are available on-site at the Palm Beach Shores Resort. From area walking tours and yoga classes to volleyball games and bingo, there is something for everyone. A variety of massage services as well as an outdoor pool are also available.

Guests at the Palm Beach Shores can also enjoy modern guestrooms furnished with private balconies, DVD players and free high-speed internet access. Toasters, refrigerators and ovens are also provided.

Popular area attractions, including the Flagler Museum and the South Florida Science Museum are only a short drive from the Palm Beach Shores Resort. The Palm Beach Zoo at Dreher Park and the Lion Country Safari are also easily accessible.
