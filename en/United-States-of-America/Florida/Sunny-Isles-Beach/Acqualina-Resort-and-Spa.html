Description

Every stay is sure to be unforgettable at the Acqualina Resort and Spa on the Beach with exceptional amenities such as in-room flat-screen TVs and iPod docking stations. Guests can also enjoy on-site boutique shops as well as 3 different dining options, including traditional Japanese sushi.

The Acqualina also provides easy access to a variety of recreational activities, including on-site kayak and jet ski rentals. A state-of-the-art fitness center along with 3 outdoor swimming pools is also available on-site.

Only 10 minutes' drive from the Acqualina Resort are the luxury shops and restaurants of Bal Harbor. The vibrant South Beach area as well as the legendary Art Deco district is also within driving distance.
