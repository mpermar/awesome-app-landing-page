Description

A full kitchen, flat-screen cable TV, and 2-sofa seating area as well as a 6-person dining area are provided. Hardwood furnishings are featured in each Villa Eagle Orlando room as well.

Guests can play with inflatable pool toys and relax on the screened-in sun terrace. The community clubhouse has a fitness center, shared lounge, and games room.

The house is 11 mi from Legends Golf & Country Club and 15 mi from the Orlando Premium Outlets Vineland Avenue. Disney's Winter Summerland Miniature Golf is 16 minutes’ drive away.
