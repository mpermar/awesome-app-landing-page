Description

All rooms feature a flat-screen TV, microwave, refrigerator, and coffee machine. The private bathroom includes a shower and a hair dryer. Certain rooms are fitted with a balcony.

Enjoy amenities such as the fitness center, garden, terrace, and bar. A business center and vending machines with drinks are also available on site at the resort.

Located on site, Jimmy Guana’s Restaurant serves American cuisine.

Sunsets at Pier 60 is 6.8 mi from Harbor Club Condos while Sand Key Park is 5.6 mi away. St. Pete-Clearwater International Airport is 9.9 mi away.

Indian Rocks Beach is a great choice for travelers interested in kid-friendly beaches, warm weather and seafood.

- 04/06/2018 -

Pretty cool place!

"So I read a lot of the complaints when I scheduled my booking. I usually research every aspect of a property before making a decision, especially since it’s very pricey. Check in is not as difficult as these reviews make it. If you read and followed instructions then you’ll find the building just fine considering it’s literally right across from the condo. They don’t have luggage carts so be prepared to transport your items to your room. We used our fold up wagon so it was no big deal. Parking can be tricky but really it’s to be expected on the beach. It’s busy. The waterpark is separate from the condo even though it’s attached. (Different owners) For 2 bedroom apartments like I got you’ll get 4 free passes everyday after check in and smaller rooms you get 2 passes. Additional passes are $16.95 each. Senior passes are cheaper at the waterpark entrance ($11) so don’t pay the $16.95 with the condo. They will try to get you to do a presentation and offer you extra free waterpark passes and a gift card to Jimmy Iguanas. They say it’s not a timeshare sale but it’s just like it so if you have a few hours to waste it’s worth it. Otherwise just say no and enjoy your vacation. Guests staying at the condo have access to the pool in the waterpark from 8-10am & 6-10pm. From 10-6pm you will need the waterpark passes and can enjoy the entire park. It’s pretty cool. The lazy river was the most popular spot. My kids ages 12, 10, 6 & 6 months had a blast. The tiki bar drinks were amazing and staff was wonderful. They only offer maid service if your stay is 2 weeks or longer but I didn’t mind. We stayed 4 nights and I really didn’t want people in my room. I know how to use a broom. The beach is less than a 5 minute walk so that will save you some money. All in all I would stay here again."
