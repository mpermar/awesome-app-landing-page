Description

Italian-inspired fare is offered at the on-site restaurant, Sea-Guini, which has coastal hues in the decor and sea-to-table dishes. The Tiki bar is Gulf-front and serves small plates alongside cocktails, beer, and wine.

A zero-entry outdoor pool, sun terrace, and firepits are available to all guests throughout their stays. Guests can also expect 2,322 square yards of indoor and outdoor event, wedding, and meeting spaces.

A Gulf-front balcony is standard with each room and suite. A flat-screen TV, desk, refrigerator, coffee machine, and private bathroom are provided

Guests can visit the dolphins, Winter and Hope, at the Clearwater Marine Aquarium, which is 7 minutes' drive away by car. Clearwater city center is 5.6 mi away, while the nearest airport is St. Petersburg-Clearwater Airport, 9.3 mi from the property.
