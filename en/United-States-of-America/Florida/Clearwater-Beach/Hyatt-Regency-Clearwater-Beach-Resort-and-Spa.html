Description

Guest rooms and suites boast a private balcony, a kitchenette or kitchen, and a flat-screen cable TV with yoga workout program. An iPod® docking station along with deluxe bedding and views of the Gulf of Mexico are available.

Swim Bar & Grill offers Gulf-front views, a Pan Latino menu, and happy hour specials, or guests can enjoy American fare at SHOR for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Breakfast and snacks are served at Skimmer’s Coffee Bar.

Poolside food and beverage service is available when booking a climate-controlled cabana. Spa treatments include private suites, detoxifying facials, and massages. A kid's club is also available.

Jolly Trolley stops nearby the hotel and travels the beach up to St. Petersburg. Pier 60 is 1,150 feet away, and Clearwater Marine Aquarium is 1.4 mi from the resort. Sand Key Park is 1.9 mi away, and Caladesi Island State Park is 2.6 mi from this Hyatt Regency.
