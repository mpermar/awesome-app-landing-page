Description

A 50-inch flat-screen TV is provided in each room at Wyndham Grand Clearwater Beach Hotel. Coffee-making facilities, a refrigerator, and complimentary bottled water are also provided. A balcony or terrace are available with each accommodations.

A spa and fitness center are open to guests of the Clearwater Beach Wyndham Grand. There is also a 24-hour reception at the property.

St Petersburg Clearwater International Airport is 14 mi from this hotel. Guests will be 17 minutes' drive from baseball games at Bright House Field.
