Description

A living room with a sofa bed and a flat-screen TV are provided in every suite at the Four Points by Sheraton Jacksonville Beachfront. The suites are brightly decorated with orange color accents. They also include a microwave, fridge and minibar. A laptop safe is also available.

Four Points by Sheraton Jacksonville Beachfront has a fitness center and a business center on site for guest convenience. Bike rentals and a local shuttle service are also available.

There is an on-site bar and restaurant featuring a hot breakfast daily. A snack bar is also on site.

Shopping at Middle Beach Commons is just a 7-minute walk from this hotel. Jacksonville Beach Golf Club is a 5-minute drive from the property, and Adventure Landing is a 3-minute drive away.
