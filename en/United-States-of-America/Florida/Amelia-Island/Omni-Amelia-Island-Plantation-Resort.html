Description

This property is a 8-minute walk from the beach. Located on the sparkling beaches of Amelia Island, the Omni Amelia Island Plantation Resort features a full-service luxury spa, indoor and outdoor pools, and 36-holes of championship golf. Guests can enjoy a meal at one of the 8 onsite dining options. The Nature Center offers kayaking, paddleboarding, Segway, cycling, and walking tours. Complimentary WiFi is provided.

Rooms each feature a flat-screen cable TV, work desk, and furnished balcony or patio. The air-conditioned accommodations also have Egyptian cotton linens and a private bathroom. Select rooms feature upgraded amenities such as additional living space, spa baths, and kitchens,

A fully equipped fitness center with 50 feet indoor lap pool, and complimentary self-parking are offered for guests' convenience. Meeting space and business facilities are provided onsite. The property offers a variety of activities including nature tours, fitness classes, and a kids club.

Bob's Steak and Chop House features a fine dining atmosphere and offers prime steaks, chops, and seafood. Guests looking for a refined seafood dining experience will enjoy Veranda. Falcon's Nest features a military-style theme and provides a family-friendly atmosphere. Marsh View Bar & Grill offers Southern-inspired, casual fare in a venue overlooking the marsh area.

Located 3.1 mi from the resort, Amelia Island State Park offers fishing, hiking, and horseback riding on over 200 acres of beaches, salt marshes, and coastal maritime forests. Fort Clinch State Park with wildlife viewing, historic attractions, and Civil War reenactments is 8.8 mi away.
