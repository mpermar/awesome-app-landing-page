Description

Located 24. 8 km from RSW International Airport, this Bonita Springs resort features 5 on-site restaurants, a full-service spa, and an 18-hole golf course.

Hyatt Regency Coconut Point Resort and Spa offers air-conditioned rooms with a 65-inch TV with streaming capability and views of Estero Bay.

A coffee maker, DVD player, and mini refrigerator are provided. Rental DVDs are available at Cool Beans Marketplace.

A variety of dining options at Coconut Point Resort include seafood at Tarpon Bay and international cuisine at Tanglewood.

Corkscrew Bar and Grill serves lunch and cocktails, and Cool Beans Marketplace serves Starbucks coffee.

Guests can enjoy 4 pools, 5 waterslides, and a lazy river pool or relax at Stillwater Spa which offers massages, facials, and body treatments.

For exercise, Hyatt Regency has a fitness centre. Beach shuttle services can be arranged to Big Hickory Island Beach, within 4 km of the Hyatt Coconut Point Resort.
