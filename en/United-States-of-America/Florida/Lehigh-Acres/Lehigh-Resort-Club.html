Description

A small seating area, cable TV, and DVD player are provided in each apartment at this Florida Lehigh Resort Club. Some apartments have a patio.

An outdoor pool, hot tub, and tennis court are available at the Lehigh Resort Club clubhouse. An indoor shuffleboard, library, and mini-golf course are on site as well.

Fort Myers Beach is 30 mi away. This resort also within 20 minutes’ drive from Westminster Golf Club and Interstate 75.
