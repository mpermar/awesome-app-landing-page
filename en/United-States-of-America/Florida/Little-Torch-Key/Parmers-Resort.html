Description

All rooms in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV. The rooms have a private bathroom with a bath or shower, while selected rooms also feature a kitchen with an oven. The rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

A continental or buffet breakfast is available each morning at the property.

Parmer's Resort has a terrace. Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Little Torch Key, like fishing.

The nearest airport is Key West International Airport, 25 miles from Parmer's Resort.
