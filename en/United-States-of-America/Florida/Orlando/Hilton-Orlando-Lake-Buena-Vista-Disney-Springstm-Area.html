Description

Guests of Hilton Orlando Lake Buena Vista are able to enjoy Disney's Extra Magic Hours Benefit and extended 60 day Disney FastPass+ booking window.

A small refrigerator, flat-screen cable TV, and coffee-making facilities are provided in each room at Hilton Orlando Lake Buena Vista. A spacious seating area is also included. Children can enjoy the on-site children's pool. In addition, this Hilton Orlando Lake Buena Vista offers a fitness center, hot tub, and jogging trail.

On-site dining options include classic American, Japanese, and Italian cuisines. Covington Mill features a fresh breakfast buffet with appearances by Disney characters on Sunday mornings.

Disney Springs is a 2 minute walk away. Typhoon Lagoon water park is 5 minutes' drive from the property.

Lake Buena Vista is a great choice for travelers interested in Disneyland, family-friendly trips and theme parks.
