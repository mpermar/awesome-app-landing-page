Description

Free Wi-Fi, a full kitchen, and a flat-screen cable TV are provided in all units at Orlando Resorts Rental at Vista Cay Resort. Select units include 2 or 3 full bathrooms, a washing machine, and a tumble dryer.

A hot tub, game room, and small cinema are available to all guests in the Vista Cay Resort clubhouse. A lakeside fitness trail, basketball court, and children’s water playground are also on site.

An internet café with public use computers is open to all guests. Concierge services, outdoor grills, and vending machines are also available.

SeaWorld is 3 miles from the property, and Walt Disney World is 8 miles away. The Orlando International Airport is a 15-minute drive from the property.
