Description

This Orlando resort features 3 heated outdoor pools, 3 hot tubs, and a game room.

Guests will enjoy free transport service the Walt Disney World Resort and the Universal Orlando Resort, which are within 14 minutes’ drive away.

A fully equipped kitchen, spa bath, and separate seating area with a sofa bed are featured in the one and two-bedroom villas at Westgate Towers.

The studio villa includes a small refrigerator and microwave. A cable TV with a DVD player is also included.

Guests can take advantage of free bicycle, paddle boat, and sports equipment hires at this Central Florida Westgate Towers.

A 24-hour reception is available for added convenience. Just 5 minutes' drive away, guests can enjoy Drafts Sports Bar & Grille, Sid's Bistro, Hersey's Ice Cream Shop at Westgate Vacation Villas and Westgate Town Center Resort & Spa.
