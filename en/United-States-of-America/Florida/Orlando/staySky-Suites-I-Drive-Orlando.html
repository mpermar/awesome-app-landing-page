Description

Each room and suite has a cable TV and pay-per-view movies at staySky Suites I-Drive. The suites include kitchen facilities with a dining area and a separate sleeping area.

Guests can play basketball or visit the game room at the hotel. This Orlando staySky Suites I-Drive also provides an on-site convenience store, barbecue facilities and lounge bar. Ticket services are available.

The hotel is less than a mile from Interstate 4 and 2 miles from Orange County Convention Center. Orlando International Airport is a 19-minute drive away.
