Description

A satellite 37-inch flat-screen cable TV, free bottled water, and iPod docking station are included in all guest rooms at the Ritz-Carlton Orlando Grand Lakes. Each marble bathroom is stocked with bathrobe and slippers. Select rooms feature a private furnished balcony.

Guests can take advantage of the bike hire or walk along on-site hiking trails. Kayaks, tennis courts, and a large, heated outdoor pool are available as well. The Ritz Kids Club is available for younger guests.

Dining options include a steakhouse, international restaurant and spa cafÃ© serving salads and smoothies. The Lobby Lounge offers afternoon tea and evening cocktails accompanied by sunset views.

The hotel is 3 mi from SeaWorld and 7 mi from the Universal Orlando Resort. The Orlando International Airport is 9.9 mi away. Shopping at the Mall at Millenia and Orlando Premium Outlets is within 6.6 mi.
