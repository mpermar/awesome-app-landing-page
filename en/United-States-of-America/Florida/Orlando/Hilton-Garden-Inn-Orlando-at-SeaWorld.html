Description

Rooms at the Hilton Garden Inn Orlando at SeaWorld feature a microwave and refrigerator. Each room comes with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom that includes a hairdryer and complimentary toiletries at this Hilton Garden Inn Orlando at SeaWorld.

Guests can enjoy the on-site Garden Grill and Bar, which serves both full American and continental breakfasts as well as dinner. Sting Ray’s Lounge serves additional drinks in the Pavilion Lounge.

The 24-hour Pavilion Pantry offers prepackaged take-away meals, drinks, and snacks. A business center and meeting rooms are available for business travellers.

Universal Orlando Resort is 6.2 miles from the Hilton Garden Inn. Walt Disney World Resort is 4.5 miles away. The Orange County Convention Center is 2.3 miles from the property.
