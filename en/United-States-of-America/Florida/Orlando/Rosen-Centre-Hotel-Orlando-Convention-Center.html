Description

The rooms have a flat-screen TV. The rooms come with a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer.

There is a 24-hour front desk, concierge services and a hairdresser's at the property.

The hotel also provides car rental. Ripley's Believe it or Not! is 1.6 miles away. The nearest airport is Orlando International Airport, 9 miles from the property.
