Description

Margaritaville Resort Orlando offers Key West-themed accommodations in the heart of Orlando.

The resort provides amenities such as a sand-covered beach and zero-entry pool. Dining and shopping options are available onsite as well.

Walt Disney World resort is 9 km from the resort. Rooms are decorated in a beach theme and include a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

A coffee maker is provided as well. Suites feature additional seating space and upgraded amenities.

Select rooms and suites feature a pool view. Guests can get a quick workout in the onsite fitness centre.

Kids will have a blast at the Parakeets Kids Club. A wedding pavilion and meeting space are provided.

Concierge service is offered as well. Universal Orlando Resort is 27 km from the property.

SeaWorld Orlando is 19. 8 km from the Margaritaville Resort Orlando. Orlando International Airport is 32 minutes away.
