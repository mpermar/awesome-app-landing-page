Description

Rosen Shingle Creek furnishes every room with a 40-inch flat-screen TV. These spacious rooms are outfitted with a refrigerator and coffee facilities. The elegant bathrooms provide bathrobes and deluxe amenities.

Four outdoor pools are available for guests use. Rosen Shingle Creek includes scenic walking trails and a golf academy. The spa area features a hot tub and a sauna.

The completely non-smoking Rosen Shingle boasts the legendary steakhouse, A Land Remembered. Guests can have Italian food at Cala Bella, deli-style meals at 18 Monroe Street Market as well as coffee and pastries at Java Coffee Bar.

The resort is 3.5 mi from SeaWorld Orlando. Orlando International Airport is 11.1 mi away while Disney World is 12.4 mi from the resort.
