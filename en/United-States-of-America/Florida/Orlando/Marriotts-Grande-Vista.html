Description

Situated 6. 4 km of the Walt Disney World Resort, this Florida hotel offers 4 heated swimming pools, on-site golf course, full-service spa, and 4 restaurants.

SeaWorld is also 4. 8 km away. A balcony, seating area, and sofa bed are included in all Marriott’s Grande Vista rooms and villas.

Select villas include a refrigerator, microwave, and kitchenware. Guests can schedule a massage or body wrap at The Spa at Grande Vista.

This Florida Marriott’s Grande Vista offers personal golf instruction as well. The Grill serves American cuisine with golf course views for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

Guests can enjoy poolside dining and cocktails at Copa Loca Bar and Grill. An on-site Market Place is available.

The Wizarding World of Harry Potter at the Universal Orlando Resort is 9. 6 km away.
