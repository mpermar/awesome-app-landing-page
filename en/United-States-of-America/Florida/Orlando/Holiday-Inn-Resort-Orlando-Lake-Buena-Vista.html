Description

Top area attractions, including Universal Studios, SeaWorld and a number of shopping centers are easily accessible with the Holiday Inn Resort Orlando - Lake Buena Vista's free shuttle service. Proximity to I-4 also puts Busch Gardens in Tampa within driving distance.

While staying at the Orlando Lake Buena Vista Resort Holiday Inn, guests can enjoy 24-hour fitness facilities, a large outdoor pool and 3 on-site dining options. The hotel also features an on-site movie theater playing family-friendly movies all day.

Lake Buena Vista is a great choice for travelers interested in Disneyland, family-friendly trips and theme parks.
