Description

Enjoy a cup of coffee or relax in front of the flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Every room comes with a private bathroom. Some units include a seating area and 2nd and 3rd floor rooms offer a balcony.

There is a gift shop at the property. Fitness facilities, a games room, and a volleyball court are also provided. Boating, hot air ballooning, and spa services are available nearby.

Prime Outlets Orlando is 0.7 mi from Cypress Pointe Diamond. Orlando International Airport is 12 mi away.

Lake Buena Vista is a great choice for travelers interested in Disneyland, family-friendly trips and theme parks.
