Description

This all-suite hotel provides furnished accommodations with fully equipped kitchenettes. A TV with cable channels is also offered.

The Homewood Suites Lake Buena Vista provides a daily hot breakfast and a reception on select evenings. Guests will also enjoy free high-speed internet access and a swimming pool.

Popular Orlando attractions, including SeaWorld and Universal Studios are only a short drive from the Lake Buena Vista Homewood Suites. With nearby access to Interstate 4, guests can also easily explore the surrounding area.
