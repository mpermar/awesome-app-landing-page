Description

A cable TV is featured in every bedroom of the WorldQuest Orlando Resort. Guests will also enjoy a full kitchen in all suites to prepare their own meals. A DVD player is also provided in each suite.

A 24-hour convenience store is available at the WorldQuest Orlando Resort. A hot tub and a seasonal outdoor tiki bar are also offered. Guests will have access to laundry facilities for clean clothes on the go.

This hotel is less than 15 minutes’ drive from the Orlando Premium Outlets. SeaWorld Orlando is 7.3 mi from the property. Universal Orlando Resort is 11 mi away.

Lake Buena Vista is a great choice for travelers interested in Disneyland, family-friendly trips and theme parks.
