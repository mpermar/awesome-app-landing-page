Description

A living room with a sofa bed and a flat-screen cable TV is provided in all units at Mystic Dunes Resort & Golf Club. The traditionally styled units also include a fully equipped kitchen or kitchenette, laundry facilities and a spa bath.

Guests at Mystic Dunes can enjoy playing at the resort’s tennis courts or the 18-hole mini golf course. In addition to the outdoor pools, a 2-story water slide is also on site. Bike rentals are also available to explore the area.

Dining is offered at Kenzie’s Restaurant. It features panoramic views of the Mystic Dunes Golf Course and serves American cuisine. Room service and grocery delivery services can also be arranged.

A concierge and dry cleaning services are offered at the resort for guest convenience. Free on-site parking is also available.

Resort & Golf Club Mystic Dunes is within 1 miles of Interstate 4. A World of Orchids is a 3-minute walk away, and Discovery Cove is a 15-minute drive away.

Celebration is a great choice for travelers interested in Disneyland, tourist attractions and parks.
