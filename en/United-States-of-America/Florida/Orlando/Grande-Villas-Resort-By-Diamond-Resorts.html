Description

The villas offer a kitchen or kitchenette and a dining area. A DVD player, satellite TV and iPod docking station are also included. Select villas offer garden baths.

Grande Villas Resort features an outdoor pool with a separate children's area and water slide. A gym and children's playground are also available at the property.

SeaWorld and Universal Studios are within a 15-minute drive from Grande Villas Resort. Orlando International Airport is 20 minutes away.

Lake Buena Vista is a great choice for travelers interested in Disneyland, family-friendly trips and theme parks.
