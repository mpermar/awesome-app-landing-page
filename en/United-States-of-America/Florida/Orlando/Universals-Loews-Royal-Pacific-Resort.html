Description

A flat-screen TV and Keurig single-serving coffee cups are provided in all rooms of the Loews Royal Pacific Resort along with an iPod docking station. Club level rooms offer upgrades, including snacks and cocktails in the Royal Club Lounge.

A Hertz Rental Car desk and business center are available. The Gymnasium fitness center (surcharge) is included for Club Level guests. A volleyball court, 2 hot tubs, and transfer service to Universal Studios theme parks are also offered.

Guests can choose from 6 restaurants located on the property, such as Jake’s American Bar, which serves casual fare. 24-hour room service is available.

Orlando International Airport is located 20 minutes’ drive from Loews Royal Pacific Resort. Universal City Walk offers shopping, dining, and nightlife just 10 minutes’ walk away.
