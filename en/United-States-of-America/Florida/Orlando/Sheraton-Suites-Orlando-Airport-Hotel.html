Description

The Sheraton Suites Orlando Airport Hotel provides all the amenities needed for a relaxing stay, including signature Sweet Sleeper beds, flat-screen TVs and in-room microwaves and mini-refrigerators.

Additional amenities at the Orlando Airport Sheraton Suites include both an indoor and outdoor pool as well as a modern fitness center.

Guests can also enjoy on-site dining at the Sky Grill and Sky Bar, serving American cuisine for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

SeaWorld is 12 mi from the property. Walt Disney World is 17 mi from the Sheraton Suites at the Orlando Airport. The numerous shops at the Florida Mall are 6.8 mi away.
