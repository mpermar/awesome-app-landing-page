Description

A flat-screen cable TV and coffee-making facilities come standard in each room at the Orlando Four Seasons Resort at Walt Disney Resort. A furnished balcony is included. Select rooms feature theme park views.

A fitness centre with golfing and tennis, business centre, and theme park ticket service as well as kids and teen amenities are on site.

A Disney Character Breakfast is available Thursday and Saturday. PB&G offers barbecue al fresco, while Capa rooftop Spanish steakhouse features craft cocktails and fresh Florida seafood.

Guests will be a 28 minutes’ drive from Orlando International Airport.
