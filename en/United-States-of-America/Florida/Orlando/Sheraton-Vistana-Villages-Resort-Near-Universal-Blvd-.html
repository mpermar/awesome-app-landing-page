Description

The spacious apartments at the Sheraton Vistana Village Resort include a full kitchen or kitchenette. A living room with a sofa bed and an in-villa laundromat are also provided.

The fitness center provides free weights and cardio machines. Guests can relax on the terrace and have a drink at the poolside bar.

Flagler Station Bar & Grill, on site, serves American dishes for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Mallory Square Market has coffee and pastries.

The Sheraton Vistana Villages Resort Villas are 3 miles from SeaWorld. Orlando International Airport is within a 25-minute drive of the property.
