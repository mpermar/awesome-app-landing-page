Description

A flat-screen TV and a small refrigerator are found in each bright and colorful room of the Walt Disney World Dolphin. Some rooms also feature a private balcony and beautiful views of the resort.

A total of 17 restaurants and lounges, including Shula’s Steak House and Todd English's bluezoo restaurant are open to guests of the Dolphin. Dining with Disney characters is offered at the Garden Grove.

Disney Extra Magic Hours and FastPass+ experience are provided to all Walt Disney World Dolphin guests. Disney tickets and advanced Disney golf course tee times are available for a fee.

The entertainment and dining of the Disney BoardWalk are 7 minutes' walk from the hotel. Guests are only 15 minutes' walk away from the Epcot theme park.

Lake Buena Vista is a great choice for travelers interested in Disneyland, family-friendly trips and theme parks.
