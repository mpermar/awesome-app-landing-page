Description

Guests at The Fountains will enjoy views of Lake Eve from their private balcony. All suites also offer washers and dryers. Full kitchens are provided.

The resort-style indoor/outdoor has 2 water slides. Guests can fish or paddleboat on Lake Eve, then enjoy a drink and an appetizer at the H2O lounge. Mug o' Joe's serves gourmet coffee drinks and fresh breakfast items.

SeaWorld Orlando is 2.8 mi away. The Orange County Convention Center is 3.9 mi from the property.
