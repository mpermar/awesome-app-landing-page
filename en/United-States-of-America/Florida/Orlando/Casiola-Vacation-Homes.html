Description

Free WiFi and a private patio or balcony are included in the fully furnished apartments at this Orlando Casiola Vacation Homes at Vista Cay Resort. Each includes a washer, tumbler dryer, and 2 or more bathrooms.

Casiola Vacation Homes provides an internet café and basketball court. A cinema and state-of-the-art fitness center are available as well.

Orlando International Airport and Walt Disney World are within 20 minutes' drive from the resort. Universal Studios is 4 mi away. Guests are also near a walking and jogging trail that goes around Lake Cay.
