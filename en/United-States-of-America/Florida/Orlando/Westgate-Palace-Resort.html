Description

Westgate Palace features an outdoor pool and a children's pool. A fitness center and game room are also available. Guests can also use the barbecue facilities or basketball courts.

Each 2-bedroom suite at the Westgate Palace Orlando is nonsmoking and offers 3 cable TVs. Washers and dryers are also included in each suite.

The Orange County Convention Center is 2.4 mi from the property. Orlando International Airport is 14 mi away.
