Description

All rooms come with a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Some rooms include a sitting area for your convenience. You will find a coffee machine in the room. Every room is fitted with a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer.

You will find free shuttle service at the property.

Ripley's Believe it or Not! is 1.9 miles away. The nearest airport is Orlando International Airport, 9 miles from Best Western Orlando Convention Center Hotel.

This is likely the highest score I will give to a hotel in a...

"This is likely the highest score I will give to a hotel in a decade. That is not to say that this is the "best" hotel on an absolute scale -- I have stayed at much more luxurious hotels by, obviously paying a lot more money. This hotel gets the highest score because at slightly less than 100 dollars per night, it made a difficult plan work out perfectly.I was with my children for a trip dedicated to disney, universal and sea world. It is understandable why I did not also want to deal with a car. The fact that we were here for more than just disney was significant, because that is why we do not want to stay at a disney resort which makes going to other places intentionally difficult (and I am a budget traveler too). A stroke of good fortune, my wife (who could not be with us on the trip) found this hotel that provides free shuttle to all the parks we want to go to.Having the commodity is a very different thing from the commodity actually working the way you expect. But the shuttles, not without their limitations -- but reasonable limitations (running on limited, inflexible schedules that are not always inclusive of the entire park operating hours), worked, very well. With some mindful planning and some luck, we were able to watch the Magic Kingdom fireworks, stay until closing of Epcot, did all we want with Sea World and Universal, and never had to call the taxi card. On our very first night (inexperienced), I was even able to call the shuttle as we were running 5 minutes late, and the shuttle actually waited for us, a very nice touch that was not to be assumed (evidenced by fellow travelers that were trying to emulate, unsuccessfully). Even though the bus had a big sign asking for tips, the drivers themselves never said a single word about tips. I think we should tip them, but it can make the 'complimentary shuttle' false advertisement on the hotel's part if the drivers were behaving differently."
