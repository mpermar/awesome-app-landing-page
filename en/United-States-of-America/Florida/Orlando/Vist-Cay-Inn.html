Description

Multiple bathrooms and cable TVs are provided in the apartments and townhouses at Vista Cay Inn. Guests also have a large living and dining area, and most units also feature a sofa bed. A laundry unit is also included.

Playing pool in the game room or watching a movie of choice in the resort theater are just 2 activities available at Inn Vista Cay. A basketball court is also on site.

A health club is available along with fitness and business facilities. Tour services and a gift shop are on site for guest convenience.

This resort is next to the Orange County Convention Center and 6 miles from Disney World. The popular shops and restaurants of International Drive are a 5-minute drive from the property.
