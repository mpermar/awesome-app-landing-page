Description

A fully equipped kitchen and a flat-screen TV with cable are featured in every accommodations at this Florida resort. Each room comes with a private bathroom.

This resort has water sports facilities and bike hire is available. Guests can enjoy various nearby activities, including cycling and fishing. Free private parking is available on site.

Okeeheelee Park is 8.1 mi from Palm Beach Waterfront Suites, while Lake Worth Municipal Park is 2,650 feet from the property. The nearest airport is Palm Beach International Airport, 6.2 mi from Palm Beach Waterfront Suites.
