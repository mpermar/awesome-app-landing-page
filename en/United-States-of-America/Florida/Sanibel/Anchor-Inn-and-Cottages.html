Description

Each beach-themed accommodation at the Sanibel Anchor Inn has a private entrance and a small patio. They are equipped with a refrigerator, microwave and coffee maker. Guests can relax in the air conditioning and watch cable TV.

Bicycle rentals are available at this pet-friendly hotel, and 30 miles of bicycle paths are accessible from the property. Picnic areas, shuffleboard tables, and horseshoe pits are located on site.

The Beachview Golf Course is less than a mile from the Anchor Inn and Cottages. Fort Myers Beach is 25 minutes away.
