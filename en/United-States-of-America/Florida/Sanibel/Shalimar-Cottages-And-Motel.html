Description

The studios and cottages include full kitchen facilities at Shalimar Cottages And Motel. Bright colors and light wood furniture are featured in each accommodation.

Guests can rent bicycles and kayaks from the property. A shuffleboard, basketball court and outdoor heated pool are also available.

Motel Shalimar Cottages is 3 miles from the J.N. Ding Darling National Wildlife Refuge. Guided nature tours can be found at the Tarpon Bay Explorers, a 5 minute drive away.
