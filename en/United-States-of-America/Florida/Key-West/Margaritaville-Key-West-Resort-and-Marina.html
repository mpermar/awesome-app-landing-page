Description

A cable TV comes standard in each room at the Margaritaville Key West Resort & Marina. A work desk, hairdryer and ironing facilities are also included.

A business center and fitness center are available on site at Key West’s Margaritaville Key West Resort & Marina . Guest can also enjoy access on-site shops and water-sports facilities.

Bistro 245 features American fare for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Castaways Pool Bar offers light poolside dining options.

Historic Duval Street is just 6 minutes’ walk from this hotel. Guests will be opposite Mel Fisher Maritime Heritage Society & Museum.
