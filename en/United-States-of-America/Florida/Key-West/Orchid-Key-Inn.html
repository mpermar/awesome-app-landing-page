Description

A 42-inch flat-screen cable TV, microwave, and small refrigerator as well as an iPod dock are standard in each room. Extras include a Keurig® coffee machine, and some suites have a spa bath and a sofa bed.

A complimentary daily happy hour with wine, beverages, and snacks is offered to all guests throughout their stay.

The Orchid Bar serves specialty cocktails made with fresh herbs and infused spirits from early afternoon to late evening. Guests will receive 1 complimentary beverage per day.

The inn is 3 minutes’ walk from the Historic Lighthouse and 7 minutes’ walk from the Southernmost Point. Key West city center is 1 mi away.
