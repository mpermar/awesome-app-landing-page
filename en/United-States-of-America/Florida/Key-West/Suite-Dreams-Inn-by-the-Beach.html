Description

The elegant and contemporary guest suites at the Suite Dreams Inn include a flat-screen TV and free Wi-Fi. They are furnished with beautiful modern artwork. The deluxe suite has a private pool and a full kitchen.

Laundry facilities are available at this Key West hotel. Free parking is on site.

The famous restaurants and bars of Duval Street are a 20 minute walk from the Key West Dreams Suite Inn. The ferry to Dry Tortugas National Park is 1.5 miles away.
