Description

In the area surrounding the DiamondHead Beach Resort, guests can discover sailboat rentals, deep-sea fishing charters, a fishing pier and top-rated golf courses. Many local restaurants and shopping centers are also nearby. Spa services are available.

DiamondHead Resort also provides many on-site activities and amenities, including a fitness center, film rentals and a beachfront swimming pool. Guests can also enjoy fresh seafood at the on-site beachfront terrace at Chloës restaurant. Cabañas Beach Bar & Grille offers poolside service and seating on the sand.

Guest accommodations at the DiamondHead are furnished with many thoughtful amenities, including private screened balconies. Guests can also enjoy wireless internet access as well as a dining area with table and chairs.
