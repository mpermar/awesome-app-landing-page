Description

Guest rooms at The Breakers on the Ocean include a living room with a sofa bed. A full kitchen features a microwave, fridge, stove, and oven. Rooms offer ocean views.

A putting green, heated pool, and BBQ facilities are available for outdoor recreation. The Breakers also has a game room featuring a pool table, board games, and puzzles. A roundabout shuttle is available for downtown Delray Beach.

Morkiam Museum and Japanese Garden is a 20 minute drive from the hotel. Delray Beach Golf Club is 4 miles away.
