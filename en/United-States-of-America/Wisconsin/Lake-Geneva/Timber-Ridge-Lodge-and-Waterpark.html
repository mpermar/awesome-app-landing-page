Description

Suites at Timber Ridge Lodge and Waterpark come furnished with fireplaces, spa bathtubs and balconies. Each suite comes with flat-screen TVs and free wired internet access. All suites have separate living and dining rooms.

Guests of Timber Ridge Lodge get passes to the Moose Mountain Falls water park, presenting a 35-foot slide. As part of the Grand Geneva Resort, Timber Ridge provides guests with access to 2 golf courses, a ski run and WELL Spa and Salon.

Among the 8 dining options at the resort, Timber Ridge Lodge hosts Smokey’s Bar-B-Que House, serving southern American barbecue.

Timber Ridge Lodge and Waterpark offers horseback riding, tennis courts and a gym.
