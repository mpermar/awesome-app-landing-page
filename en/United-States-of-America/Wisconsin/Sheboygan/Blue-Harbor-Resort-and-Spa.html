Description

A 42-inch flat-screen cable TV, microwave, and fridge are provided in every suite at Blue Harbor Resort & Spa. Featuring a bath or shower, private bathrooms also come with a hairdryer. All rooms include free waterpark passes, and select rooms feature balconies with lake views.

Reflections Spa offers body wraps, facials, massages, and more. Other facilities offered at Resort & Spa Blue Harbor include on-site shops, a laundry and fitness center. The property offers free parking.

There is a coffee shop, snack bar, and lounge on site.

The Children's Museum is 5 minutes’ drive away. This resort is 56 mi from General Mitchell Airport.
