Description

A 42-inch flat-screen cable TV is included in every room at Hyatt Place Milwaukee Airport, along with a refrigerator and sofa bed. For convenience, a coffeemaker and hairdryer are also provided.

A fitness center is available at Milwaukee Airport Hyatt Place. A free transfer service is offered to the airport.

A hot breakfast is served daily, or guests can purchase snacks and light meals from the Gallery Menu.

Miller Park, home of the Brewers, is 11 mi away. The Milwaukee city center can be reached in 15 minutes’ drive.
