Description

Views of the bay or woods are offered in every air-conditioned room or townhouse at The Shallows Resort. A microwave and fridge are provided, along with satellite cable TV.

Fishing and biking is possible on site at The Shallows Resort Egg Harbor. Guests can enjoy tennis, a swim in the heated pool, or just relax on the deck.

Peninsula State Park can be reached in a 17-minute drive. Downtown Green Bay is 59 miles away.
