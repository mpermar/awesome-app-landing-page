Description

Free Wi-Fi access and cable TV are included in all rooms at Holiday Inn Hotel & Suites Green Bay Stadium. They come equipped with a refrigerator and a microwave and have a work desk.

The on-site restaurant, the Golden Greats Bar and Grill, serves American favorites for breakfast and dinner.

A free airport shuttle is available. Guests can relax in the hotel’s hot tub, or work out in the fitness center. There is also a convenience store and an ATM on site.

The Bay Park Square Mall is 1 miles from the hotel. Downtown Green Bay is a 9-minute drive away.
