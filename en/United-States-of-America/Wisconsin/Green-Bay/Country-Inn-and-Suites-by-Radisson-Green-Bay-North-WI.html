Description

Complimentary Wi-Fi access and cable TV are part of every room at Country Inn and Suites Green Bay. They come with a microwave and a refrigerator.

Guests can relax in the hot tub or work out in the fitness center. The reception is staffed 24 hours a day and there is a business center on site as well.

The Green Bay Country Inn and Suites is 7 miles from the New Zoo. Oneida Casino is 10 miles away.
