Description

Guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Each room comes with a private bathroom. At Home2 Suites Green Bay, every room is equipped with a seating area.

The daily breakfast offers continental and buffet options.

Other services and facilities offered at the accommodation include a business center, newspapers and a fax machine and photocopier.

Lambeau Field is an 11-minute walk from Home2 Suites Green Bay, while Resch Center is 0.4 miles away. The nearest airport is Austin Straubel International Airport, 3.7 miles from the hotel.
