Description

In the area surrounding the Hampton Inn and Suites Madison-West is the Monona Terrace and Convention Center, the State Capitol Building and Vilas Park Zoo. The campus of the University of Wisconsin-Madison along with a variety of shopping centers and restaurants are also nearby.

After a restful night's sleep on signature Cloud Nine beds, guests at the Madison-West Hampton Inn and Suites can enjoy a free hot daily breakfast. The hotel also provides an on-site fitness center, as well as free wireless internet access.
