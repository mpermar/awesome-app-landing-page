Description

A large flat-screen TV with cable channels is included in every room at The Madison Concourse Hotel. A refrigerator and coffee-making facilities are provided for additional convenience.

The hotel's restaurant, CIRC, serves contemporary American cuisine with global influences for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Guests can enjoy a cocktail or appetizers at The Bar, an on-site lounge. Starbucks is conveniently located next to the on-site restaurant and offers a wide variety of beverages and food options.

An indoor pool and sauna are offered at Concourse Hotel Madison. A business center is available so guests can take care of work during their stay.

The Wisconsin State Capitol Tour can be reached in less than 1 minute by foot. A variety of shopping and dining options are available on State Street, just 2 minutes' walk from the property. Free transfer service is provided to Dane County Regional Airport, which is 5 mi away.

Experiences

03/19/2018

I would highly recommend this hotel and would gladly stay again!

"My husband, myself and our 10 yr. old daughter came into town for a concert at the Orpheum, and wanted to stay somewhere within walking distance. We stayed on the top floor, which I highly recommend because it’s part of the Governor’s Club! Between the wonderful breakfast in the morning and the drinks and hors d’oeuvres in the evening, it was well worth it. Just note, children are not allowed in the lounge after 4:30, but there is a very nice seating area just outside. We were all able to sit and enjoy our drinks and hors d’oeuvres out there. We ordered up a meal from the Circ Restaurant one of the evenings and were pleasantly surprised. After reading some of the reviews we weren’t sure what to expect. The Indian Farms mushroom and carrot ginger soups were very good, as were the hand breaded cheese curds and pretzel bites. Room was clean and spacious, bed was very comfortable. Staff was amazing, even offering to arrange a shuttle to/from the zoo! (Another place I highly recommend.) I want to give a special shout out to WHIT, at the front desk, who answered questions and always had a “Hello.” as we walked in and MOHAMMAD, who kept our room spotless with cold water in the fridge!"
