Description

A 32-inch flat-screen TV is included in each suite at Staybridge Suites Madison East. The kitchenette features a stovetop, full-size refrigerator, and a dishwasher.

A hot breakfast buffet is served every morning at this Madison hotel. Barbecue facilities are located on the property.

The business center is open 24 hours a day. A library of books and board games is available.

The Madison International Speedway is a 30-minute drive from the East Madison Staybridge Suites. The East Towne Mall is just 5 minutes away by car.
