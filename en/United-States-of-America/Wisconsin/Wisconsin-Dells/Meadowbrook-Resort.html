Description

Log-wood handcrafted furniture is featured throughout the resort and is balanced by modern amenities like a 42-inch flat-screen TV, luxury bedding, and free WiFi in rooms. Select suites at The Meadowbrook Resort provide a full kitchen, whirlpool, and fireplace.

The Timber Camp Water Adventure Playland is a fun and interactive indoor/outdoor pool area. The Sawdust Springs are a jetted indoor area, where adults can relax while supervising the 3 kid-friendly indoor sections of the Timber Camp Water Adventure Play Land. Seasonal outdoor pools feature a kid-friendly interactive pool, alongside a full-size pool for all to enjoy. Nightly campfires are available for all guests to join.

Barbecue facilities and picnic tables are located near all rooms at the Wisconsin Dells Meadowbrook Resort. Guests can work out in the on-site fitness center or play a game of table tennis. Outdoor activities like horse riding and skiing can be enjoyed nearby.
