Description

Luxury shopping boutiques, a golf course and several sports courts are featured at this Texan resort.

The property is a 30-minute drive from George Bush International Airport. A flat-screen TV, microwave and compact refrigerator are offered in the spacious Woodland Resort rooms.

Select rooms overlook the pool or golf course. Ironing facilities and a safety deposit box are also included.

Guests can play a game of tennis or go for a walk and have dinner on the property.

The Resort also features the Forest Oasis Waterscape, which offers 2 outdoor pools, a waterslide and lazy river.

A full-service spa is available to guests. The hotel's conference centre has over 60,000 square-feet of event space as well as event staffing.

The property is 8. 1 km from the Woodlands Mall, Market Street Shopping Center and Cynthia Woods Mitchell Pavillion.
