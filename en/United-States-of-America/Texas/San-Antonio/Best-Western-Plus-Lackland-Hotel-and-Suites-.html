Description

Each room has a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Some rooms have a seating area to relax in after a busy day. You will find a coffee machine in the room. Every room has a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer. Plus Atrea Hotel & Suites features free WiFi throughout the property.

Enjoy cocktails in the stylish lounge at the Best Western Plus Atrea Hotel & Suites. A buffet breakfast is served every morning.

There is a 24-hour front desk and gift shop at the property.

Henry B Gonzalez Convention Center is 9.9 mi from Best Western Plus Atrea Hotel and Suites, while The Alamo is 9.9 mi from the property. The nearest airport is San Antonio International Airport, 14 mi from the property.
