Description

A flat-screen TV and an iPod dock are provided in each room at Home2 Suites by Hilton San Antonio Airport. The rooms are also equipped with a kitchenette, which includes a dishwasher, microwave, refrigerator and kitchenware.

The front desk is open 24 hours a day for your convenience. A snack bar is also available at San Antonio Airport Home2 Suites by Hilton. Other facilities include meeting facilities.

The restaurants and entertainment on the San Antonio River Walk are a 10-minute drive from the property. The AT&T Center is 10.5 miles away.
