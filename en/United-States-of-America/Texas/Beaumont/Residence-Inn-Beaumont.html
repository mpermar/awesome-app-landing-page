Description

Kitchen facilities include a stovetop and a dishwasher. A sitting area and tea and coffee making facilities are also provided in each of Residence Inn Beaumont’s rooms.

Guests can relax in the jacuzzi after a workout in Residence Inn Beaumont’s gym. Cardiovascular equipment and free weights are offered.

Free public parking is available at Residence Inn Beaumont. The hotel is 5 miles from Beaumont Municipal Airport.
