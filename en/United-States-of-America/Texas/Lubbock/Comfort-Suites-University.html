Description

At the hotel, every room comes with a desk. At Comfort Suites - University rooms come with a seating area.

Other services and facilities offered at the hotel include a business center, newspapers and a fax machine and photocopier. Staff at Comfort Suites - University are always available to provide advice at the reception.
