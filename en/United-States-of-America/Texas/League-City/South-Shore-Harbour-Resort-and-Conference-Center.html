Description

Spacious rooms include floor-to-ceiling windows with views of Clear Lake at the South Shore Harbour Resort and Conference Center. The rooms also have cable TV and free Wi-Fi.

Guests can work out on the tennis court or relax with a massage during their stay.

Dining at the South Shore Harbour includes a seasonal swim-up bar at the outdoor pool. The resort also has a restaurant and lounge with views of Clear Lake.

The resort is a 35 minute drive from Galveston, which features dining and entertainment. Downtown League City is 3 miles from the property.
