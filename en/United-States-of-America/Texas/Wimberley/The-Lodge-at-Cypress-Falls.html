Description

The Lodge at Cypress Falls has an outdoor swimming pool, tennis court and BBQ facilities in Wimberley.

This inn offers a 24-hour front desk and a shared lounge. The rooms in the inn are equipped with a TV.

Each room comes with a private bathroom with free toiletries. All units at The Lodge at Cypress Falls have air conditioning and a wardrobe.

An onsite food truck is available. Guests can enjoy a meal at the onsite restaurant, which serves a variety of American dishes.

In addition, guests can enjoy a cocktail and enteratainment at the Tavern at Cypress Falls.

Guests can enjoy an array of activities in the surroundings, including fishing, canoeing and hiking.

Austin is 45 km from the accommodation, while San Marcos is 23 km away. Austin-Bergstrom International Airport is 49 km from the property.
