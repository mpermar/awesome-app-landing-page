Description

A microwave and fridge are available in every room at Hilton Garden Inn Houston NW America Plaza. Guests can enjoy the included tea and coffee-making facilities, as well.

The Great American Grill and Bar is on the property and serves American cuisine for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. The Pavilion Lounge features cocktails from the full bar.

A fitness center is available at Houston NW America Plaza Hilton Garden Inn Houston. Guests can take advantage of the business center near the lobby.

The Energy Corridor District and the Galleria are 13 miles from this hotel, and can be reached in a 15-minute drive.
