Description

Each guest suite at the Homewood Suites by Hilton Fort Worth Medical Center has a sitting area with a sofa and a flat-screen TV. Each kitchenette has a full-size refrigerator, a microwave, and a coffee machine.

The Homewood Suites provides travelers with a refreshing outdoor swimming pool with a shaded patio. A well-equipped fitness center and a computer workstation are also available on site.

Texas Christian University and the center of Fort Worth are each within less than a 10-minute drive from the hotel.
