Description

The hotel is close to many educational institutions and attractions in downtown Austin and the surrounding area. Guests will enjoy having easy access to the 6th Street arts and entertainment district, Lake Austin, Texas Capitol building, the Austin Convention Center, Frank Erwin Center and the Dell Diamond, home of the Round Rock Express minor league baseball team.

Southwestern University and St. Edward's University are within 20 miles. Many restaurants and cocktail lounges are located near the hotel.

Guests will enjoy many full-service amenities including free high-speed Internet access, free newspapers during the week, free local calls and a fitness center.

Your Morning Breakfast is full of hot options, making breakfast at the Comfort Suites NW Lakeline the perfect way to start your day. Enjoy a free hot breakfast featuring eggs, meat, yogurt, fresh fruit, cereal and more, including your choice of hot waffle flavors. If you're leaving early, a Your Suite Success Grab & Go bag is available for the two hours prior to breakfast.

Be sure to enjoy the outdoor pool and hot tub.

Business travelers will appreciate a fully equipped business center, dual telephone lines, voice mail and a meeting room.

All guest rooms have refrigerators, microwaves, irons, ironing boards and cable TVs. Keep your electronic devices charged with the recharge device including AC and USB outlets provided in all guest rooms. Rooms with whirlpool bathtubs are available.

The hotel also has an on-site guest laundry facility.
