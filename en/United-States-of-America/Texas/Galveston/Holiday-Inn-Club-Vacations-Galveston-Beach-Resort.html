Description

A kitchenette or full kitchen is included in every self-catering suite at Holiday Inn Club Vacations Galveston Beach Resort. All suites have a living room with a sofa bed, TV and DVD player. Select rooms offer a balcony with views of the Gulf of Mexico.

Free access to the on-site gym and hot tub is provided to all guests of Galveston Beach Resort.

Central Houston is a 1 hour drive northwest of the hotel. William P. Hobby Airport is 45 miles away.
