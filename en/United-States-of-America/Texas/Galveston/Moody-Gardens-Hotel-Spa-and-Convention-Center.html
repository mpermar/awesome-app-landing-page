Description

A flat-screen TV and free WiFi are provided in each of the rooms at Moody Gardens Hotel. Guests are offered bathrobes and complimentary toiletries in addition to a coffee maker and a refrigerator.

Fresh seafood dishes are served in a casual atmosphere at Café in the Park. Fine dining is available at Shearn’s Restaurant. The Lobby Bar serves cocktails in a tropical setting.

The Spa At Moody Gardens offers skin and nail treatments, hair and makeup, and a variety of massages. Business and banquet facilities are available on site.

Free transfer service around Moody Gardens, Strand Shopping District and Scholes International Airport is provided. Transportation service is available to and from the cruise terminal for a fee.
