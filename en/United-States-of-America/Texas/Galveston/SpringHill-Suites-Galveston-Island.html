Description

Rooms at SpringHill Suites Galveston Island come with free Wi-Fi, TV and minibar. Decorated with warm colors, they all include a sitting area with a couch and a private bathroom with hairdryer.

Guests can enjoy the central location 3 miles from Galveston city center and discover many local eateries. There are also on-site vending machines, providing drinks and snacks 24/7.

Popular nearby attractions include the Rainforest Pyramid at Moody Gardens and Ashton Villa, both a 5-minute drive from the SpringHill Suites hotel. Free parking is available on site.

07/03/2018

Great quiet hotel for families

"Right off the highway so it can get a little loud but the AC works great and it almost sounds like a fan and drowns out most of the noise. The pool and hot tub are nice although smaller than it looks in the picture. Beds and bedding are comfortable. The pull out couch mattress wasn't great but worked fine. The living and bedroom areas were separated by a wall and had a tv on the dresser that you could see from either side. The kitchen area was great with it's own counter and sink unlike most hotels you have to use the bathroom sink. It had a microwave, mini fridge and cabinets to store your stuff. The closet was small and the door didn't open correctly but we just used it to hang our wet stuff. The cleaning staff was amazing and did a great job. Breakfast was pretty standard. Waffles, eggs, sausage, muffins, bread, cereal and juice/milk. It was super crowded the entire time and there was limited seating but it was not bad. There is only 1 elevator and it's super slow so we took the stairs most of the time. Parking lot is standard although the street to get in is only one way but there is a back way you can take. It is only a short drive to the seawall so distance to the beach is nothing but it's a lot quieter when you want to relax away from the hustle."
