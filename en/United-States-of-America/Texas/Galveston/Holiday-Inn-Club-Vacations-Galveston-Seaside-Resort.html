Description

The rooms at the hotel come with air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a kitchen, a dining area and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a bath. All guest rooms will provide guests with a dishwasher.

There's an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of American dishes.

Holiday Inn VacationsSeaside Resort has an outdoor pool. You can play ping pong and pool at the 3-star hotel.

Pleasure Pier is 16 miles from the accommodation, while Moody Mansion Museum is 16 miles from the property. William P. Hobby Airport is 37 miles away.
