Description

The rooms at the resort come with a seating area and a flat-screen TV. Selected rooms are equipped with a kitchen with an oven, a microwave and a fridge. All guest rooms will provide guests with a stovetop.

You can play ping pong and pool at Vacation Villages at Lake Travis.

Austin is 19 miles from the accommodation, while Round Rock is 21 miles away. The nearest airport is Austin-Bergstrom International Airport, 25 miles from the property.
