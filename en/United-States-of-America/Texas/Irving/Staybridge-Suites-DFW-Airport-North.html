Description

Guests can use the modern fitness center and Sports Court. Staybridge Suites DFW Airport North also offers an outdoor hot tub.

A flat-screen cable TV, DVD player and free Wi-Fi are provided in the contemporary suites. There is also a comfortable seating area and a desk.

Staybridge Suites Irving serves a hot breakfast buffet and offers a reception with free snacks and cocktails Tuesday through Thursday evenings. It also offers barbecue grills and a 24-hour convenience store.

Dallas/Fort Worth International Airport is 7 miles from the hotel. Six Flags Over Texas amusement park is 16 miles away.
