Description

Hampton Inn & Suites Dumas features an indoor pool and a fitness center. An on-site business center and sauna are also available.

Guest rooms at the Dumas Hampton Inn are equipped with 32-inch LCD cable TVs. Coffeemakers and work desks are also included.

Lake Meredith National Recreation Area is within a short drive of the Hampton Inn in Dumas. The American Quarter Horse Association Hall of Fame & Museum is also nearby.
