Description

At the hotel all rooms have a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a kitchen, a dining area and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a bath. Free WiFi is accessible to all guests, while some rooms also feature a balcony. All guest rooms include a closet.

An American breakfast is available daily at the property.

Guests can use the fitness center. You can play tennis and ping pong at Holiday Inn Club Vacations - Villages Resort.

The accommodation provides a laundry service, as well as business facilities like fax and photocopying. Staff are always available to help at the reception.

Tyler is 13 miles from Holiday Inn Club Vacations - Villages Resort.
