Description

Overlooking Lake Travis, this resort offers a full-service marina and spa. It features 4 private golf courses, a 3 tier pool with swim-up bar and rooms include free Wi-Fi.

The guest rooms at the Lakeway Resort and Spa offer floor-to-ceiling windows with views of the rolling hills or the lake.

Flat-screen TVs and bathrooms are provided in every room. The Lakeway Resort boasts a gym with personal training and nutritional assessments available.

A children’s pool with a waterslide and a business center are at the resort as well.

Travis Restaurant offers upscale dining in a rustic atmosphere with views of Lake Travis.

The restaurant serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner. In the evening, guests can watch the sunset while having drinks at the Back Porch.

Downtown Austin is 23 miles from the Lakeway Spa and Resort. Flintrock Falls golf course, created by Jack and Jackie Nicklaus, is located on the property.
