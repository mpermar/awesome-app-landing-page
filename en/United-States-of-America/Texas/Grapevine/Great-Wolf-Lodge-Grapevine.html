Description

Every room at this resort is air conditioned and has a TV with cable channels. A microwave and refrigerator are also included. Some rooms feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day. You will find a coffee machine in the room. Every room includes a private bathroom.

There is a 24-hour front desk, a cash machine and gift shop at the property.

Legoland Discovery Center Dallas Fort Worth is 1.6 mi from Great Wolf Lodge Grapevine, while Great Wolf Lodge Dallas is 1,300 feet from the property. Dallas-Fort Worth International Airport is 3.7 mi away.
