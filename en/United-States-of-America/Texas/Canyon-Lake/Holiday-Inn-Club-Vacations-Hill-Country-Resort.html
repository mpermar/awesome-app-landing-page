Description

All guest rooms at the hotel have a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, DVD-Player, and a private bathroom a bath and free toiletries. Selected rooms are equipped with a kitchen with a dishwasher. At Holiday Inn Hill Country Resort, all rooms are equipped with a seating area.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool. You can play pool at Holiday Inn Club Vacations - Hill Country Resort.

The hotel provides a laundry service, as well as business facilities like fax and photocopying. Staff at the 24-hour front desk can lend a hand with advice about the area.

San Marcos is 16 miles from Holiday Inn Hill Country Resort, while New Braunfels is 15 miles away. San Antonio International Airport is 30 miles from the property.
