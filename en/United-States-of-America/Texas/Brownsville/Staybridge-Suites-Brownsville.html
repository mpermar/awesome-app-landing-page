Description

Every suite at the Staybridge Suites Brownsville comes with a full kitchen and a full refrigerator. Each suite has cable TV with in-room movies, video games and a CD/DVD player.

A 24-hour fitness center and an outdoor pool are on-site at the Brownsville Staybridge Suites. The hotel features sports courts, barbecue grills and a convenience store on-site as well. Free laundry facilities are also available on-site.

Boca Chica State Park on the Gulf of Mexico is 25 miles from the hotel. The Brownville/South Padre Island International Airport is 7 miles from the hotel.
