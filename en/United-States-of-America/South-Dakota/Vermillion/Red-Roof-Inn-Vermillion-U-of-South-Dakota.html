Description

Less than a 10 minutes’ walk from The University of South Dakota, this hotel offers rooms equipped with free WiFi.

All rooms feature a 32-inch flat-screen TV and granite counter tops. Guests can enjoy a swim in the heated indoor pool at Red Roof Inn Vermillion.

The 24-hour reception desk offers guests fax and photocopying services and daily newspapers.

The National Music Museum is less than 1 mile from this hotel. Harold Davidson Field is 8 minutes’ drive away.
