Description

This Deadwood hotel is minutes from the Black Hills Mining Museum, the Homestake Gold Mine, Deer Mountain ski area and Terry Peak ski area.

Black Hills State University is about 20 minutes away. The entire city of Deadwood is a national historic landmark.

Ponder the truth at the foot of Wild Bill's grave in Mount Moriah Cemetery, tour the Adams Museum & House, pan for gold in the Broken Boot Gold Mine or take a self-guided walking tour of this one-of-a-kind Wild West town.

A variety of restaurants are located in the surrounding area. There is a seasonal snack bar available at Gulches of Fun.

Guests can enjoy many full-service amenities and features, including free high speed Internet access, free local calls, free weekday newspaper and free coffee.

Enjoy our free hot breakfast featuring eggs, meat, yogurt, fresh fruit, cereal and more.

In addition to the on-site arcade, guests of this Deadwood hotel can enjoy the indoor heated pool, hot tub and exercise room.

Corporate travelers can appreciate conveniences like the on-site business center with access to copy and fax services.

The banquet and meeting rooms accommodate up to 175 people for most events and business functions.

A gift shop, laundry facilities and an evening cocktail hour are provided for added guest enjoyment.

Guests can also enjoy the hotel's jogging track, nature area and playground. All guest rooms have coffee makers, hair dryers, irons, ironing boards, voice mail, multiple telephone lines and cable television with HBO.

Some rooms come equipped with refrigerators and microwaves. Be sure to ask about the large business and family suites.
