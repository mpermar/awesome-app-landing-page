Description

A microwave, a small refrigerator and one-cup coffee-making facilities are included in every guest room at SpringHill Suites by Marriott Deadwood. Featuring modern furnishings, each guest room also has ironing facilities, a hairdryer, and an in-room safe.

Guests can enjoy a swim in the indoor infinity pool or relax in the hot tub at Deadwood SpringHill Suites by Marriott. A fitness center is provided on the property. Guests can purchase snacks and drinks from the gift shop on site.

Brown Rock Sport's Café serves American fare for breakfast, lunch and dinner each day.

Next to Cadillac Jack's Casino, this hotel is across the street from the Days of ’76 Museum. Rapid City and Mount Rushmore National Memorial are within 50 miles of the hotel.
