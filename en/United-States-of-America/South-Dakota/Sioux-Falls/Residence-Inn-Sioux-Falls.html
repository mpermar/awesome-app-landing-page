Description

The spacious suites at Residence Inn Sioux Falls include fully equipped kitchens with fridges, microwaves and coffee makers. Each boasts luxury bedding and individually controlled air conditioning.

Guests can relax in the whirlpool or exercise with cardiovascular equipment. The indoor pool offers a towel service.
