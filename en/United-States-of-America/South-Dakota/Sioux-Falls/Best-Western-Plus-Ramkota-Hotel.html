Description

A cable TV and a work desk are provided for each room at Best Western Plus Ramkota Hotel. The traditionally styled rooms also include coffee-making facilities for convenience.

Ramkota Best Western Plus offers guests access to the on-site fitness room and a business center. A small gift shop is also available.

This hotel is less than a 15 minute drive from Sioux Falls city center and Great Plains Zoo. Royal River Casino is about a 45 minute drive away.
