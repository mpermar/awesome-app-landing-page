Description

Set among the scenic Black Hills of South Dakota, this Holiday Inn features a spacious indoor pool facility and an on-site restaurant.

Free WiFi is available throughout the hotel. Rooms at the Holiday Inn Spearfish-Convention Center are equipped with a flat-screen cable TV.

A refrigerator, microwave, and in-room coffee maker are included amenities. An iron and ironing board are also available.

Lucky’s 13 Pub serves a menu of American favorites as well as a signature burger and tater tots.

The pub has a family-friendly restaurant area and a sports lounge. The Spearfish Holiday Inn’s recreation facility includes a spacious pool and two enclosed whirlpools.

A games room offers an air hockey table and video games and a fitness center is also available.

The Holiday Inn Spearfish is less than a half mile from I-90 and less than 13 miles from historic Deadwood.
