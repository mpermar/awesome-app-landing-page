Description

Located on the Tulsa Expo Square and State Fairgrounds, this hotel is 4 minutes’ walk of Big Splash Water Park.

A daily continental breakfast is served, and free WiFi is offered in every room. A flat-screen cable TV is provided in every room at Expo Inn.

For added convenience, a microwave and refrigerator are also included. The 24-hour reception desk at Expo Inn offers fax and photocopying services to all guests.

Free parking is also available. The University of Tulsa is just 2 miles away. BOK Center and central Tulsa can be reached in 10 minutes’ drive.
