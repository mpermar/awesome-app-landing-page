Description

Guests can enjoy Leapin’ Louie’s Lagoon, the indoor children’s water playland at the Wyndham Tulsa. A fitness room and 24-hour business center are also on-site.

The hotel’s Beechwood’s Restaurant features American cuisine and is open for breakfast and lunch. Evening cocktails and a late meal are available at the on-site Mulligan’s Sports and Spirits.

A spacious work desk is provided in all rooms at the Tulsa Wyndham. Each room also includes a coffee maker.

Shopping at Woodland Hills mall is just 4.8 miles from the hotel, and the Tulsa Zoo is 9.7 miles away. The Tulsa Air & Space Museum and Planetarium is a 15-minute drive from the hotel.
