Description

A cable TV is included in each room at the Hilton Garden Inn Oklahoma City/Bricktown. Along with a microwave, the rooms also have a refrigerator and a coffee machine.

At the Hilton Garden Inn Oklahoma City/Bricktown, there is a 24-hour front desk and an on-site mini-mart. Meeting facilities are also available.

Will Rogers World Airport is 7 miles from the hotel. Chesapeake Energy Arena is less than a 5-minute drive away.
