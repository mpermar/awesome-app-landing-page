Description

Begin the day at the Embassy Suites Oklahoma City Airport with a free cooked-to-order breakfast or take advantage of in-room coffee makers. Relax in the indoor swimming pool or enjoy a work out in the modern fitness center before taking the hotel's free shuttle to Will Rogers World Airport.

The Oklahoma City Airport Embassy Suites is only a 30-minute drive from the University of Oklahoma as well as the Oklahoma City National Memorial. Easy access to I-40 and I-35 place the Oklahoma City Zoo and Tinker Air Force Base only minutes away.
