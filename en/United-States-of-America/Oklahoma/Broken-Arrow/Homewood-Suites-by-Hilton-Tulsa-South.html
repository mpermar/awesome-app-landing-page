Description

Each spacious suite offered at Homewood Suites by Hilton Tulsa-South is furnished with 2 flat-screen cable TVs and a sofa bed. Decorated in warm colors, all suites include an MP3 connection and free Wi-Fi.

Snacks and beverages can be purchased 24/7 at the Pavilion Pantry Snack Shop on-site. Each weeknight the Homewood Suites Tulsa provides a light meal and free drinks for every guest.

Free access to the hotel gym and seasonal outdoor pool is provided to all guests of the Tulsa South Homewood Suites. Laundry facilities and free parking are also available.

Battle Creek Golf Club is 4.3 miles from the Homewood Suite. Big Splash Water Park and Bell’s Amusement Park are both 15 minutes away.
