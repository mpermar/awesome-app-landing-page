Description

Featuring a seasonal outdoor pool with cabanas, swim-up bars, fire pits and 6 hot tubs, Choctaw Casino Resort - Durant is located in Durant, 16 mi from Denison. This resort offers free WiFi, onsite dining and an extensive casino with over 4,000 slot machines, table games, poker and more.

Each room at this resort is air conditioned and is fitted with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Each room includes a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower.

Guests can enjoy a variety of onsite activities at The District movie theater complex, which includes a bowling alley, arcade, cinema, and laser tag. A 24-hour fitness center and luxury spa are also featured.

Several restaurants, cafes and lounges are available throughout Durant Choctaw Casino Resort.
