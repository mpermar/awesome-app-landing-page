Description

Each modern studio boasts a flat-screen cable TV, a seating area and an iPod docking station. A full kitchen with a dishwasher and microwave is provided. The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries.

At HYATT house, 24-hour reception, a safety deposit box and free parking is available. Meeting rooms, dry cleaning and a self-serve laundry facility is also included.

An on-site heated seasonal outdoor pool with a hot tub and fire pit is offered. A business centre with fax and copy services is available.

Short Pump Town Center and Funny Bone Comedy Club is a short walk away.
