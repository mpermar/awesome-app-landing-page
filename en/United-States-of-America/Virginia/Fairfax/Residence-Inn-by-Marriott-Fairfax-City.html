Description

A flat-screen TV, a kitchenette and an iPod docking station are found in each modern room at Residence Inn by Marriott Fairfax City. A work desk and coffee-making facilities are included.

Guests of the Fairfax City Residence Inn can enjoy the on-site fitness room. A business center and a laundromat are also available.

Van Dyck Park is 2 miles from the hotel. The National Firearms Museum is less than a 10 minute drive away.
