Description

The Hampton Roads Embassy Suites provides accommodations equipped with cable TV and free Wi-Fi. They are furnished with a sofa bed, microwave, and refrigerator. A laptop safe box is provided.

This Hampton hotel hosts an on-site restaurant and bar. A gym and 24-hour business center are also part of its facilities. A breakfast buffet is served every morning.

The Virginia Air and Space Center is 10 minutes from Embassy Suites Hampton Roads. Busch Gardens Amusement Park is 30 minutes from the hotel.
