Description

Westin Washington Dulles Airport features a 24-hour, state-of-the-art fitness center and an indoor pool. Guests can also take advantage of the nearby jogging path, then relax with an in-room massage.

All guest rooms at the Washington Dulles Airport Westin boast amenities such as flat-screen TVs and iPod docking stations. Guests will also enjoy floor-to-ceiling windows and ergonomic work spaces at this completely smoke-free hotel.

Padilla's restaurant and lounge at the Washington Westin Dulles Airport serves Italian cuisine for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Guests can dine outdoors and enjoy the view of the duck pond and stone bridge.
