Description

Each studio at the TownePlace Suites Falls Church includes cable TV and free internet access. The studios are furnished with a sofa bed and the kitchens include a stove top and oven.

A gym is available at the hotel along with barbecue facilities. Falls Church TownePlace Suites also has laundry facilities for added convenience.

TownePlace Suites Falls Church is 7.2 miles from Arlington National Cemetery. Smithsonian Institution is 9.7 miles from the hotel.
