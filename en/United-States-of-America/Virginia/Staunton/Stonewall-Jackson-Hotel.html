Description

The rooms here have a private bathroom with a shower or bathtubb, cable TV and air conditioning. Extras include a desk, hairdryer, free toiletries and ironing facilities.

At the Stonewall Jackson Hotel, you will find a 24-hour front desk. The property has a grand historic lobby.

Guests can enjoy comfort foods and seasonal specialties for dinner on site.

Stonewall Jackson is just under a quarter of a mile from Woodrow Wilson Presidential Library and 200 feet from Blackfriars Playhouse.
