Description

Situated in historic Alexandria, Virginia, this hotel is 11. 4 km from Washington, D.

C. It provides free shuttles to Reagan National Airport, free local shuttles and on-site dining options.

Free WiFi is offered. All guest rooms feature a mini-refrigerator, a coffee maker and a flat-screen cable TV.

While staying at the pet-friendly Holiday Inn & Suites Hotel Alexandria - Old Town, guests can enjoy the on-site fitness centre and indoor pool.

The hotel also features on-site dining at Bistro on First for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

The Holiday Inn & Suites Hotel Alexandria - Old Town is located only moments from the Potomac River and the numerous shops, restaurants and entertainment of King Street.

The White House is 11. 2 km from the property while Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport is 4 km away.
