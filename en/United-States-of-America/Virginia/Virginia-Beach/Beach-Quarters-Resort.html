Description

The classically decorated rooms at Beach Quarters Resort contain a kitchenette with a microwave and a refrigerator. Cable TV and a dining area are included.

Beach Quarters guests can work out in the athletic club or use the on-site sauna and hot tub. Tennis and shuffleboard are played on the rooftop court and children can play in their own activities center.

The Sandbar poolside tiki bar and café serves light items and beverages on the deck.

Beach Quarters Resort is less than 1 miles from the Virginia Beach Amusement Park and less than 2 miles from the Contemporary Arts Center of Virginia.
