Description

Cable TV, free WiFi access, and a coffee machine are included in each room at the Water’s Edge Resort & Spa. They are decorated in soothing tones and have an iPod docking station.

Visitors can relax in the indoor and outdoor swimming pools at the Westbrook Water’s Edge. Tennis courts and a fitness center are available for use.

The Water’s Edge Restaurant serves breakfast, lunch, dinner, and brunch while overlooking the water. Seaview Bistro and Martini Bar offers a variety of drinks and hosts live entertainment some weekend nights.

Hammonasset Beach State Park is 9.9 mi away. The Clinton Country Club is 4 mi away.
