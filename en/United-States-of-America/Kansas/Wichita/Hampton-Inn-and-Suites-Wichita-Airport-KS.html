Description

All guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. The rooms have a seating area.

An American breakfast is served at the property.

Guests have access to the on-site business center where they can use the on-site ATM machine. The reception can provide advice on the area in order to help guests plan their day.

The nearest airport is Wichita Mid-Continent Airport, 0.6 miles from Hampton Hilton Wichita Airport.
