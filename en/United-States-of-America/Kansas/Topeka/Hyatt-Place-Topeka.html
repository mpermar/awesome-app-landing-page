Description

The modern guest rooms at Hyatt Place Topeka are equipped with a 42-inch flat-screen TV and an iPod docking station. A refrigerator and coffee maker are also provided.

Cereal, fruit, and yogurt are part of the continental breakfast served at this Topeka hotel. The Bakery Café serves Starbucks coffee along with wine and beer.

Guests can work out in the 24-hour on-site gym or swim in the seasonal outdoor pool. A computer and printer are available in the business center.

The Combat Air Museum at Forbes Field is a 20-minute drive from the Topeka Hyatt Place. The Topeka Golf Course is 3.3 miles from the hotel.
