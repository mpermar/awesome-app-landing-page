Description

All rooms provide desks and seating areas. They are also equipped with coffee makers. The spacious studios feature sofa beds.

Guests at Dunn Hampton Inn can enjoy a daily breakfast buffet. The hotel offers a gym and business center. For added convenience, meeting facilities are available.

General William C. Lee Museum is one mile from Hampton Inn. The hotel is 10 miles from Campbell University and 20 miles from Raven Rock State Park.
