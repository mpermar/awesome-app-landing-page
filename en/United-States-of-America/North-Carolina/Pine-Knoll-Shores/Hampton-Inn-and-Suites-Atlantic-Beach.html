Description

Guest rooms at the Hampton Inn and Suites Atlantic Beach are furnished with a microwave, a refrigerator and coffee facilities. Each room and studio also provides a 32-inch flat-screen cable TV with a seating area.

Hampton Inn and Suites Pine Knoll Shores includes a business center. For added convenience, laundry facilities and a snack shop are available at the hotel.

Fort Macon State Park is 5.6 miles from Atlantic Beach Hampton Inn and Suites. Downtown Atlantic Beach is 2.8 miles away.
