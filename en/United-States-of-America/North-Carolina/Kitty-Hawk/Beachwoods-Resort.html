Description

Beachwoods Resort is nestled in a natural setting surrounded by woodlands and lakes. The property is adjacent to the 700-acre Kitty Hawk Wood Coastal Reserve. Guests can take a refreshing plunge in the indoor heated pool or go fishing on-site.

Room feature a flat-screen TVs with cable channels. A full kitchen is provided as well. Guests will enjoy the comfort of a furnished seating area with a sofa bed. A washing machine and dryer are provided.

Beachwoods Resort offers various facilities including a tennis court, fitness center, and barbecue facilities. Guests can enjoy the on-site family water park.

Nags Head is 11 mi from the accommodations, while Duck is 5.6 mi from the property.
