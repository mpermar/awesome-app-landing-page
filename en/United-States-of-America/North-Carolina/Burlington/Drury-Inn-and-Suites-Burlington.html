Description

The rooms comes with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a coffee machine, a bath, a hair dryer and a desk. The private bathroom is equipped with a shower. All guest rooms will provide guests with a microwave.

A buffet breakfast is available each morning at the hotel.

Guests can use the hot tub.

Guests can swim in the outdoor swimming pool or use the business area.

Greensboro is 15 miles from Drury Inn & Suites Burlington, while High Point is 28 miles away. The nearest airport is Piedmont Triad Airport, 24 miles from the accommodation.
