Description

With the Homewood Suites Raleigh/Crabtree Valley's free shuttle service, the Museum of Art, the state fairgrounds and Carter Finley Stadium are easily accessible within a 3 miles radius. North Carolina State University, Duke University and the Time Warner Cable Music Pavilion are also only a short drive away.

Guests staying at the Raleigh/Crabtree Valley Homewood Suites can enjoy starting the morning with a free breakfast and finishing the day with a free weekday evening reception. The hotel also features an on-site convenience store, modern fitness facilities and an outdoor swimming pool.
