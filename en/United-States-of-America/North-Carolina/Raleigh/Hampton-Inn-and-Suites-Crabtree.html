Description

Free Wi-Fi and a flat-screen cable TV are featured in each room of this Raleigh hotel. Rooms feature contemporary décor and each one includes a work desk.

A fitness center is available at the Hampton Inn and Suites Crabtree and guests can also use the hotel’s business center. Laundry facilities is also on site for added convenience.

This hotel is just 0.5 miles from the shopping and dining options of the Crabtree Mall. North Carolina State University Centennial Campus is 15 minutes’ drive away.
