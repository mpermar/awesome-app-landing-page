Description

Many golf courses, parks, outdoor recreation areas and movie theaters can be found in the surrounding area. A number of specialty stores, retailers and restaurants are close to the hotel and some can be found within walking distance.

Hotel features include free wired and wireless high-speed Internet access, free coffee in the lobby, free daily newspapers, free local calls and free hot breakfast. If guest leave early, there is a breakfast bag available during the two hours before breakfast starts.

Guests can also enjoy the indoor heated pool and exercise room.

Corporate travelers can enjoy additional conveniences in the on-site business center like the public computer with Internet access, and copy and fax services. A meeting room is on the premises and can accommodate up to 10 people.

All guest rooms are suites furnished with flat-screen TVs, microwaves, refrigerators, coffee makers, curved shower rods, hair dryers, desks, irons, ironing boards, sofa sleepers and cable TV. Keep your electronic devices charged with the recharge device including AC and USB outlets provided in all guest rooms. Connecting rooms can be requested.

For added convenience, this North Carolina hotel has a sundry shop and laundry facilities. There is lots of free parking provided.
