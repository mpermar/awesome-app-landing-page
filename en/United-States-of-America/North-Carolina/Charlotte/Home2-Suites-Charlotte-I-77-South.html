Description

All suites at this Charlotte Home2 Suites are tailored for extended stays and feature a microwave, small refrigerator, and dishwasher. A flat-screen TV and sofa bed are also included.

A business center and fitness center are on site. Guests can stay connected around the property through free Wi-Fi. A market, Home2 MKT, is available for convenient, last-minute purchases.

Home2 Suites is 7 miles from Charlotte Douglas International Airport and 4 miles from SouthPark Mall. Guests will also be 5 miles from downtown Charlotte.
