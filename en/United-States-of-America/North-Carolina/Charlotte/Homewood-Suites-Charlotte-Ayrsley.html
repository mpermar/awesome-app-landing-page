Description

Free Wi-Fi and a flat-screen TV with DVD player are provided in all suites at Homewood Suites Charlotte Ayrsley. Suites feature warm colors and traditional décor. A sitting area with a sofa bed and a work desk are included.

Free light meals and beverages are offered at a nightly manager’s reception Mondays-Thursdays. Charlotte Ayrsley Homewood Suites also has barbecue grills which are available for guest use.

Charlotte Douglas International Airport and Discovery Place are each a 20-minute drive from this hotel. The NASCAR Hall of Fame is 9.5 miles away.
