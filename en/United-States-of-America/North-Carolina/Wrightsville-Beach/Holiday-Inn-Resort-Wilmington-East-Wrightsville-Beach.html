Description

Guests can enjoy southern cuisine and seafood at the on-site Oceans Restaurant. Lazy Daze Poolside Bar & Grill serves lunch and tropical drinks, and Gabby’s Lounge has drinks and live entertainment on weekends.

Holiday Inn Resort Wrightsville Beach-Wilmington has an on-site playground. A concierge staff is ready to help guests plan their visit. There is an on-site gift shop, an ATM, and a business center. Bicycles, umbrellas, and beach chairs are available for rent.

Rooms at Holiday Inn Resort Wilmington E-Wrightsville Beach are furnished with a microwave and a refrigerator. They include a 32-inch flat-screen TV and free Wi-Fi.

The USS NC Battleship is almost 12 miles from the Wilmington – Wrightsville Beach Holiday Inn Resort. The Cape Fear Museum and the Cameron Art Museum are both 9 miles away.

Experiences

07/10/2017

We will be back!

"The location of this hotel is beautiful with a very clean, family friendly beach. My mother who has trouble getting around was staying with us and the elevator was steps from our room, the lobby, the hotel restaurant, gift shop, the pool and the parking lot. The shower was large and easy for her to get in and out of. Throughout the hotel the staff was very friendly and always willing to help. Getting extra blankets or towels for the room and beach was easy and quick. We stayed on a busy weekend and we never felt over crowded at the pool or beach or while waiting for an elevator like some beach resorts. My teenage children liked that we were close to paddle board and kayak rentals. The beach town is lovely with shops and restaurants."
