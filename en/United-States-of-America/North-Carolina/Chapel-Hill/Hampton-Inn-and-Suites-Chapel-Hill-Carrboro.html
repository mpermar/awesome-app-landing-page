Description

A flat-screen cable TV is included in every room at this Chapel Hill hotel. Spacious suites are available and each bathroom has a hairdryer for added convenience.

A fitness center and a business center are on site at the Chapel Hill Hampton Inn and Suites Carrboro. A pool and a laundromat are also available.

This hotel is less than 1 mile from the center of the city of Chapel Hill. The UNC Basketball Museum is located 3.5 miles away.
