Description

This lodge is 5 minutes’ walk from the Whitefish Lake Golf Club. A free shuttle to downtown Whitefish, the Amtrak station, and the airport is available. Rooms feature a small fridge and coffee-making facilities.

Guest rooms feature log cabin inspired décor at the Grouse Mountain Lodge. A TV, a small refrigerator and coffee-making facilities are provided. Free Wi-Fi is available.

An outdoor firepit and seating area is available for a relaxing afternoon, in addition to an outdoor hot tub. An indoor pool and dry sauna are also provided.

Logan’s Bar and Grill offers on-site dining 7 days a week. The Lobby Espresso Bar opens at 7:00 am for guest convenience.

The entrance to Glacier National Park is 25 minutes’ drive from Grouse Mountain Lodge. Blanchard Lake is less than 10 minutes’ drive from the property.
