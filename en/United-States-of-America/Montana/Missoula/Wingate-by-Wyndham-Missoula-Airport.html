Description

Each of the bright and spacious rooms at the Missoula Airport Wingate by Wyndham comes with free Wi-Fi. Guests can prepare or store snacks with the in-room microwave and refrigerator, or purchase them at the Sundry Shop, which also offers toiletries, medicine, and more. Room service is available and provided from the Montana Club Restaurant.

This hotel offers free 24-hour shuttle service to Montana and Missoula International Airport, just a 5-minute drive away, and limited shuttle service to North Reserve restaurants and shopping areas from 8 am to 10 pm. The University of Montana is 7 miles from Wingate by Wyndham Missoula Airport. The Missoula Art Museum is a 10-minute drive from the hotel.
