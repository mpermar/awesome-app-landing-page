Description

This slope-side ski accommodations is less than 5 minutes' walk from the mountain village and several ski slopes. Spacious apartments feature full kitchens for convenience.

A large living room with a TV is available in each fully furnished apartment at Shoshone. A balcony with mountain views is also provided.

Solace Spa and Salon is on site for guest relaxation. A heated indoor/outdoor pool, hot tub, fitness center, and tennis courts are all included. Babysitting/childcare is available from the Shoshone and ski equipment hire is offered. The Yellowstone Conference Center is attached to the property.

Yellowstone National Park is 1 hour and 30 minutes' drive away. Big Ski Skiing is 4.5 miles from the property. Gallatin River, with blue ribbon fly fishing, is just a 15 minutes' drive from Shoshone.
