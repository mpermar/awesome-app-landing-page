Description

Each suite at Huntsville Homewood Suites features a spacious living area and kitchenette including a dishwasher, 2-burner stove and full refrigerator. A 32-inch flat-screen TV, CD/DVD player and an iPod docking station are provided.

Homewood Suites by Hilton Huntsville-Village of Providence offers a gym and full-service business center.

A hot breakfast buffet with pastries, fruit, waffles and quesadillas is served every morning at the Village of Providence Homewood Suites. Snacks are available in the convenience store.

Sci-Quest Museum is a 10-minute drive from this Hilton Homewood Suites hotel and the Huntsville Botanical Garden is a 15-minute drive away. Joe Davis Stadium is 20 minutes away by car.
