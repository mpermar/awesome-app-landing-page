Description

Dining includes Low Tides Poolside Bar, High Tides Lobby Bar, and the Grab 'n' Go Deli. The daily breakfast features eggs, breakfast meats, and waffles as well as pancakes. Guests can also have their pick of pastries, bread, and bagels.

A beachfront pool deck, adult and children’s activities, and barbecue grill are available to Best Western Premier The Tides guests throughout their stay. Free parking is offered as well.

A 42-inch flat-screen TV with media chargers and Bluetooth, free WiFi, and Keurig coffee machine are standard in every room. A microwave and refrigerator are provided. Kid's bunk bed suites include a gaming system.

The Wharf shopping area is 4.8 mi from Best Western Premier The Tides, while Adventure Island water park is 3 minutes’ drive by car. A walk through nature is only 2,150 feet away along the Cotton Bayou Trail into Gulf State Park.
