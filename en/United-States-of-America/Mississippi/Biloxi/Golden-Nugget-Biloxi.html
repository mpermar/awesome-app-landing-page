Description

A 42-inch flat-screen TV and iPod docking station are provided in all Golden Nugget rooms. Ironing facilities, a coffee machine, and an private bathroom with a hairdryer are also included.

Four restaurants, room service, and a bar are available at Golden Nugget Biloxi. A 24-hour front desk, shops, and a daily maid service are also offered. Guests can also enjoy lounging on the sun terrace.

Gulf Marine State Park is 900 feet away from the resort, Biloxi city center is located 1.7 mi from Golden Nugget Biloxi, and the Biloxi Lighthouse is 3.7 mi from the casino.
