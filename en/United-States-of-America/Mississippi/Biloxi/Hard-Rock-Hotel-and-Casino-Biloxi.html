Description

A mini bar and bathrobes are provided in all the rooms of the Hard Rock Hotel and Casino Biloxi. Rooms feature modern decor and are equipped with a flat-screen cable TV, a work desk and tea- and coffee-making facilities.

Concierge services and a fitness center are available to the guests of the Biloxi Hard Rock Hotel and Casino. Guest can also enjoy Tequila's on the Gulf, which features the Sound Bar, private cabanas and Tequila's on the Gulf Restaurant.

The 10 restaurants at the Hard Rock cater to all tastes and palates. The fine dining restaurant offers steak and seafood dishes. Casual dining and snack options are found at the other on-site restaurants along with lunch and dinner buffet options.

Central Biloxi is 1.2 mi from the hotel. Ship Island Excursions offers passenger ferry services and is located a 15-minute drive away.
