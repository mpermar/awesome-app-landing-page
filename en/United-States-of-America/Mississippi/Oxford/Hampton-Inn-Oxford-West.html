Description

All rooms at the Hampton Inn Oxford/West offer a coffee maker and clock radio. For guest convenience, a hairdryer and free bathroom amenities are also provided.

This hotel offers a relaxing indoor jacuzzi, an on-site gym and a business center. A daily breakfast is served each morning.

The Oxford Hampton Inn is just a 10-minute drive from Ole Miss Golf Course and University-Oxford Airport is a 7-minute drive. Rowan Oak, home of the famous author William Faulkner, is just 3 miles away.
