Description

At the Homewood Suites by Hilton York, guest suites provide a fully equipped kitchen and dining area. An iPod docking station is also available in every suite. Select suites offer a separate living area and a sofa bed.

The York Homewood Suites by Hilton feature an indoor pool and a fitness room. An on-site convenience store and laundry facilities are available at the hotel.

Hilton York Homewood Suites is a short drive from the York Memorial Ice Rink. The Strand-Capital Performing Arts Center is also within driving distance of the hotel.
