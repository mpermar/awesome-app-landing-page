Description

The Lodges at Gettysburg provide air-conditioned studios and suites with a fireplace, hardwood floor and vaulted beam ceilings. All lodges include a flat-screen TV, bathroom and private porch.

The Gettysburg Lodges are an ideal base for viewing the battlefields, or exploring the 37 acres of mature woods on a walking or cycling tour. Fishing is popular in the 3-acre lake.

The Lodges at Gettysburg are 5 miles from the town of Gettysburg. They are 6.5 miles from the Gettysburg Historic Military Park and the Eisenhower National Historic Site.
