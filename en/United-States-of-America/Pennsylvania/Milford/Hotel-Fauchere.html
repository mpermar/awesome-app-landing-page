Description

A TV is provided in each room at Hotel Fauchere. Guests can also have an in-room coffee machine and a desk. Private bathrooms have a bathtub or shower.

A 24-hour front desk welcomes guests to Hotel Fauchere, which provides luggage storage facilities for guests. Dry cleaning and ironing services are also available at the hotel.

Guests can dine on-site in style at the Delmonico Room restaurant or get a drink and enjoy live music at the Bar Louis brasserie. Priority reservations at both establishments are available of the Hotel Fauchere.

Delaware Water Gap National Recreation Area and Grey Towers National Historic Site are both within 1 miles of Hotel Fauchere. Forest Hall Antiques is 1,050 feet away.
