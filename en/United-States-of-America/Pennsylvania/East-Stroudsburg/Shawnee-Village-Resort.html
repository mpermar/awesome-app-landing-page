Description

The resort offers spacious villas that are fully furnished and offer two bedrooms and two bathrooms. The property is equipped with a full kitchen, a spacious living room and a private patio.

Guests have access to the fitness facilities, an indoor swimming pool and an outdoor swimming pool. Shawnee Mountain Ski Area is 3 mi away from this property.
