Description

DoubleTree Resort by Hilton Lancaster rooms include a 32-inch flat-screen TV and a MP3 docking station. The rooms are equipped with free Wi-Fi and a refrigerator.

The Lancaster Doubletree Resort has a putting range, basketball and tennis courts. Cafe 24 Hundred features casual American fare, full service lounge, and seasonal outdoor seating.

The Doubletree Resort is within a 15-minute drive of the Tanger Outlet Mall and The Amish Farm and House. Herseypark is within a 40-minute drive of the hotel.
