Description

The recreational offer of the hotel includes an indoor swimming pool and a fitness center, equipped with the latest cardio machines. After a workout session, guests can unwind around the outdoor open fire.

A buffet breakfast is served daily, and includes eggs and sausage, waffles, and juice. Free coffee and tea are available 24/7 in the lobby area.

The Springhill Suites Erie is located just 6 miles from Erie International Airport and close to the major corporations. Erie Zoo are within 3 miles of the hotel. Millcreek Mall is across from the property.
