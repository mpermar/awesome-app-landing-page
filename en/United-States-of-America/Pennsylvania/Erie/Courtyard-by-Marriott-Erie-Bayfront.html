Description

At the hotel, rooms are equipped with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Every room is equipped with air conditioning, and some rooms include a balcony. The rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

The daily breakfast offers à la carte and American options.

Courtyard by Marriott Erie Bayfront offers a range of wellness facilities including a hot tub and an indoor pool.

Port Erie Bicentennial Tower is a 5-minute walk from the accommodation.
