Description

Rooms at the Best Western Premier, the Central Hotel and Conference Center all feature free Wi-Fi and a flat-screen cable TV. They are decorated in modern furnishings and include a microwave, refrigerator, and coffee making facilities.

O’Reilly’s Tap Room has an extensive beer selection and serves comfort food and Irish cuisine for lunch and dinner.

The hotel has an on-site fitness centre and business centre. Guests can take advantage of laundry facilities and free shuttle services.

The Hollywood Casino at Penn National Race Course is 14.9 miles from the hotel. Harrisburg City Island is 15 minutes’ drive away.
