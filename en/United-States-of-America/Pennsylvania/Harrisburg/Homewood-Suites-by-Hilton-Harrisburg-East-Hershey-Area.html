Description

Begin the day at the Homewood Suites Harrisburg East-Hershey Area with a free hot breakfast buffet or with an energizing workout in the on-site fitness center. Finish some work in the 24-hour business center, relax in the outdoor gazebo or practice on the hotel's putting green. Experiences

05/07/2018

Great stay!

"Pros- For the price, the stay was excellent. It was close to Hershey, the pool was great and open until 12 at night, the breakfast was great and offered a lot of options, and the staff was very nice. We had a king suite which was offered some privacy from our child and for getting ready in the mornings. Coffee was offered at all hours of the day which was a bonus! The weekdays offer free dinners which we missed but on Friday night they left cookies out all night which were delicious and on the weekends they had bananas and apples available for free throughout the day! Definitely worth the money if you like to save on food! The basketball court was also fun and my child spent an hour outside playing- they offer balls at the front desk. The room was very clean and so was the rest of the hotel. The location of our room was on the first floor and it was convenient as we were close to the pool and breakfast area. The noise level was great- I couldn't hear anyone else and slept well. Cons- The sofa bed wasn't comfortable and you could feel every spring. The air conditioning seemed to be much stronger in the room with the sofa bed so my child was freezing at night. Also, the breakfast area was awkward because you share seating with other people but we got used to it. They did open a separate area to eat on busy mornings which was nice. Also AVOID the ruby Tuesday's restaurant next door- we thought it was great to have a restaurant next door but the service was horrible. Even with the cons, we thought the stay was amazing and the value and staff were great!"
