Description

This Cody, Wyoming bed and breakfast is surrounded by mountains and was once a working cattle ranch. It features trick horses and a Western breakfast cooked over an open fire each morning.

The Sheepherder's Wagon is an authentic 1897 wagon, renovated to hold a bed and boast heat and air conditioning. Another accommodations at K3 Guest Ranch is a rustic tent house. Both share a modern bathhouse.

A great room stocked with a DVD player and a selection of movies is available to guests at K3 Guest Ranch. The tepee patio is a great place to relax outdoors and watch wildlife.

Buffalo Bill State Park is 5.9 miles from K3 Guest Ranch. Downtown Cody, Wyoming, is a 10-minute drive away.
