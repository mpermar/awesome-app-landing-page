Description

A flat-screen cable TV and a work desk are offered in all rooms at Arbuckle Lodge Gillette. A refrigerator and microwave are provided for convenience. The spacious rooms are styled in wood furnishings and warm-colored linens.

A daily breakfast buffet is served in the lobby and includes waffles, breakfast burritos, biscuits and gravy, egg dishes, French toast, fresh fruit and other breakfast items. Guests can enjoy a manager’s reception featuring light appetizers Monday through Thursday evenings.

A heated indoor pool and hot tub are provided for guest relaxation. A fitness center and business center are offered. BBQ facilities are available at Gillette Arbuckle Lodge.

Gillette Thunder Speedway and Dalbey Memorial Park are within 2.2 mi of Arbuckle Lodge Gillette. The Rockpile Museum is 5 minutes’ drive away.
