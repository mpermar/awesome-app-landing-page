Description

All rooms have free Wi-Fi, desks and sitting areas. They also have coffee makers and hairdryers. Spacious suites with sofa beds are available.

Rawlins Hampton Inn serves a breakfast buffet and has a snack shop. Guests can access the gym. Additional on-site facilities include a business center and meeting rooms.

Rodeo Park and several restaurants are within one mile of Hampton Inn. Wyoming Frontier Prison Museum and Carbon County Museum are 2 miles away.
