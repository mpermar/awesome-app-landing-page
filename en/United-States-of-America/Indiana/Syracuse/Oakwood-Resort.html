Description

A flat-screen TV is featured in each colorful room at the Oakwood Resort. Tea and coffee-making facilities are also provided in every guest room.

Guests can enjoy a variety of outdoor activities at Oakwood Resort, including swimming, kayaking, fishing and boating. A fitness center, 2 tennis courts and a basketball court are also available on site.

The on-site Back Porch at the Pier serves seafood, pastas and steak. It is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner daily.

The Syracuse-Wawasee Historical Museum, Maxwelton Golf Club and Syracuse Golf Club are within 2 miles of this resort. Fort Wayne and South Bend, Indiana are within a 55-minute drive of the property.
