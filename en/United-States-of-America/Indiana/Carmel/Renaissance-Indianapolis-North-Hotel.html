Description

Rooms at the Renaissance Indianapolis North Hotel are spacious and include luxury bedding. They come equipped with a coffee maker and ironing facilities. The private bathrooms include Aveda amenities.

At Grille 39 Indianapolis North Hotel guests can enjoy Starbucks coffee, smoothies, and American cuisine. The hotel also has vending machines and newspapers in the lobby.

Brookshire Golf Club is just over 5 miles from the Renaissance Indianapolis North Hotel. The Indianapolis Zoo is 15.5 miles away.
