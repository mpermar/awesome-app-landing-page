Description

Located across the street from the famous Shipshewana Auction & Flea Market, Farmstead Inn and Conference Center is located in the heart of Shipshewana Amish Country and features an indoor pool, indoor basketball court and ping-pong room and an outdoor gazebo with a playground. Free WiFi is available.

Every room at Farmstead Inn and Conference Center provides a flat-screen HDTV with premium cable channels, a microwave and a small refrigerator. A sofa bed, spa bath, kitchenette and fireplace can be enjoyed in select rooms.

Guests can start the day with complimentary hot breakfast, work out at the on-site fitness center or relax by the fireplace at the hotel’s 3-story atrium. The hotel also offers spacious meeting and event space.

Fort Wayne Airport is 52 mi away from the property.
