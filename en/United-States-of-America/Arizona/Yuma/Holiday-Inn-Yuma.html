Description

A microwave, small fridge and tea and coffee-making facilities are featured in each guest room at Holiday Inn Yuma. Cable TV is also provided.

Kem’s Comfort Food is open daily for breakfast and dinner. Traditional American fare as well as specialty hamburgers are featured.

A 24-hour front desk welcomes guests to Yuma Holiday Inn. A fitness center and business center are available for guest use.

Yuma city center is 3 miles away. Imperial Sand Dunes is 11 miles from the hotel.
