Description

All air-conditioned rooms include a work desk, a small refrigerator and coffee-making facilities at Hyatt Regency Scottsdale Resort and Spa. An private bathroom with a hairdryer and a bathrobe are also offered.

SWB serves Southwestern cuisine for breakfast, lunch and dinner at Scottsdale Hyatt Regency Resort and Spa. Noh offers Japanese cuisine for dinner and Alto Ristorante e Bar offers Italian fare for dinner. Guests can also enjoy cocktails at Cantina Due, Center Stage Bar and The Grotto.

There are 10 outdoor swimming pools and a waterslide at Hyatt Regency Scottsdale Resort and Spa. 4 separate tennis courts are also featured.

Camelback Mountain is 15 minutes’ drive from Hyatt Regency Scottsdale Resort and Spa. Old Town Scottsdale is 6.6 mi from the hotel.
