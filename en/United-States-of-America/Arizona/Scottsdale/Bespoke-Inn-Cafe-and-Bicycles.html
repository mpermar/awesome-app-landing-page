Description

At the inn, every room includes a closet, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Each room includes air conditioning, and certain rooms at Bespoke Inn, Cafe & Bicycles have a terrace. All rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

A à la carte, American or Italian breakfast is available daily at the property.

The area is popular for cycling and hiking.

Old Town Scottsdale is a 6-minute walk from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Phoenix Sky Harbor International Airport, 6 miles from the property.
