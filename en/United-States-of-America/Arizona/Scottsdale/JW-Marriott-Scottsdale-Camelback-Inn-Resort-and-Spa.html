Description

Guests can relax in a sun lounger by the pool, or rent a private, poolside cabana. JW Marriott Scottsdale Camelback Inn Resort & Spa offers hot stone massages and body wraps. The resort also has a fitness center offering Pilates classes.

Rooms at JW Marriott Scottsdale Camelback Inn Resort & Spa each have a kitchenette equipped with a fridge and microwave.

Dining options include a steakhouse and Rita’s Kitchen, which serves Southwestern cuisine. Sprout’s, located in the JW Marriott Scottsdale Camelback Inn Resort & Spa, offers healthy cuisine including salads and smoothies.

The Phenix Mountains Preserve is 2 miles from JW Marriott Scottsdale Camelback Inn Resort & Spa. Guests can reach Phenix Sky Harbor International Airport with a 7 miles drive. Resort parking is free.
