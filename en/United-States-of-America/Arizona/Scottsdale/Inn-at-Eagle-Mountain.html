Description

All suites at Inn at Eagle Mountain feature a sofa bed. Boasting South-Western inspired décor, all rooms also include a cable TV, tea and coffee making facilities and a refrigerator.

Overlooking the golf course, Inn at Eagle Mountain’s restaurant serves breakfast, lunch and afternoon appetizers.

Scottsdale Museum of Contemporary Art is 17 mi from this hotel. Frank Lloyd Wright’s Taliesin West is 6.9 mi away.
