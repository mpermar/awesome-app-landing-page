Description

A flat-screen cable TV, a microwave and a refrigerator are included in all guest rooms at Courtyard by Marriott Scottsdale Salt River. The air conditioned rooms include tea and coffee-making facilities.

Serving breakfast and dinner in a casual atmosphere, the Bistro is on site at Salt River Courtyard by Marriott. Guest can enjoy a selection of beer and wine in the evening at the restaurant.

A business center with fax and photocopying services is available on site at Courtyard by Marriott. Guests also have access to the hotel fitness center.

The Desert Botanical Gardens are a 14 minute drive from this hotel. University of Phoenix Stadium is 34 miles away.
