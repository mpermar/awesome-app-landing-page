Description

The Ridge on Sedona Golf Course provides guests with a variety of on-site amenities and facilities. With in-room full kitchens, outdoor barbecue facilities and a modern fitness center, every stay is sure to be enjoyable. Guests can also enjoy contemporary accommodations featuring private balconies or patios.

The area surrounding The Ridge is filled with a variety of recreational activities. Guests can enjoy scenic hiking trails through Oak Creek Canyon or a hot-air balloon ride over the desert. A number of shopping centers and restaurants are also easily accessible from the hotel.
