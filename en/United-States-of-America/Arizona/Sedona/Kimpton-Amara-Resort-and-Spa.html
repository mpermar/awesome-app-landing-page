Description

A 40-inch flat-screen TV and Italian linens are found in each room of the Amara Resort & Spa. A private bathroom is also included.

SaltRock Kitchen & Cocktails offers Southwestern cuisine for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Catering for private events, weddings and meetings is also available.

Guests of Amara can take free morning yoga classes or take a free ride to town. A fitness center, hot tub and concierge services are also available. A hosted evening social reception from 1700 to 1800 is provided. A full service award-winning spa also provides massages and treatments.

Slide Rock State Park is 6.8 mi away, while Cathedral Rock Trail is 3.3 mi away. The Grand Canyon is 108 mi away.

Uptown is a great choice for travelers interested in friendly locals, shopping and restaurants.
