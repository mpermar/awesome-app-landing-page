Description

A patio with pool views, a seating area with flat-screen TV with premium cable channels and a kitchen with a dishwasher, a refrigerator and a dining area are featured in rooms at Sedona Pines Resort. An private bathroom equipped with a hairdryer is also included.

Bella Vita offers Italian cuisine every night. Guests can enjoy a miniature golf course. A 24-hour reception is available at Pines Resort Sedona.

Sedona Airport is 6.6 mi from the resort. Slide Rock Market is 14 mi from Sedona Pines Resort.
