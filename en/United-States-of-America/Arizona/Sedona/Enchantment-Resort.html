Description

Explore out at the renowned Enchantment Resort, offering an unparalleled experience set within the spectacular vistas of Boynton Canyon.

From inventive culinary and world-class mountain biking, hiking and golf to exclusive access to Mii amo, an award-winning destination spa, the opportunities for adventure and relaxation are endless.

Enjoy well-appointed guestrooms, suites and villas inclusive of private balconies boasting views beyond compare.

Enchantment Resort’s striking red rock views are complimented by bespoke service, exquisite amenities and tailored preferences to create an unforgettable stay.

While in residence embark on an epicurean adventure as you explore Enchantment Resort’s four signature dining experiences featuring a diverse selection of innovative cuisine, unrivaled ambiance and handcrafted cocktails and spirits.

From sunrise to sunset (and even once the stars appear) Enchantment Resort offers a variety of activities for every age and interest making it the ultimate Sedona destination.
