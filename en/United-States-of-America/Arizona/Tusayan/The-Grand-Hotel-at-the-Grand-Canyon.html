Description

The Grand Hotel rooms include a coffee maker and cable TV. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning.

The Grand Hotel offers a lobby with a stone fireplace, seating and a grand piano. Guests can use free Wi-Fi in the lobby or get a cup of coffee at The Grand Café. The hotel also features the Canyon Star Restaurant and Saloon, which features western cuisine.

The Grand guests can swim in the indoor pool or work out in the gym. The hotel has a gift shop features Grand Canyon themed gifts.
