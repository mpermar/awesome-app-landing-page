Description

Rooms at Days Inn by Wyndham Williams include a coffee maker, cable TV and a hair dryer. Some rooms offer a refrigerator and a seating area.

Days Inn by Wyndham Williams offers laundry facilities and a business center with fax and photocopy services. A continental breakfast is served every morning.

Downtown Williams and the Grand Canyon Railway are within a 5-minute drive of the hotel. The Grand Canyon is just an hour’s drive away.
