Description

All rooms have a flat-screen cable TV, a microwave, a small refrigerator and coffee-making facilities at Drury Inn & Suites Phoenix Airport. A private bathroom equipped with a hairdryer is also provided.

Guests can relax in a hot tub at Phoenix Airport Drury Inn & Suites. A fitness center and a business center are available. Free hot snacks and cold beverages are served in the evening.

Arizona State University is a 10-minute drive from Drury Inn & Suites Phoenix Airport. Camelback Mountain is 10 miles from the hotel.

South Mountain is a great choice for travelers interested in scenery, family-friendly trips and warm weather.
