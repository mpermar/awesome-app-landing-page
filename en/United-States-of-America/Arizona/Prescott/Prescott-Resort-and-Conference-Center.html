Description

Prescott Resort & Conference Center features a full-service health club and salon with a salt-water swimming pool, sauna, spa services and more. The resort's helpful staff can arrange tee times at the nearby Prescott Country Club, horseback riding or adventurous jeep tours. At the on-site casino, guests can choose from 300 slots or play a game of poker.

The guest rooms at the Prescott Conference Center are decorated with artwork inspired by the Yavapai Native American Tribe and the area's beautiful scenery. Guests will feel pampered with amenities such as premium Dolcè Vitaô bedding, down comforters and Judith Jackson bath products. After a busy day, guests can relax while watching a movie on the flat-screen TV or with an in-room massage.
