Description

Free Wi-Fi, a cable TV with extended movie channels, a refrigerator and tea and coffee-making facilities are included in all of the comfortable rooms at Forest Villas Hotel. The rooms have balconies with panoramic mountain views and select rooms include spa bathtubs.

The Prescott Forest Villas Hotel serves a large selection of beer, wine and champagne. Guests can order a beverage from the front desk and have it delivered to their room or enjoy a drink in the hotel lobby, which is decorated with crystal chandeliers and European tapestries.

A fitness center and a business center with fax and photocopying services are available. A 24-hour front desk and a laundromat are also available at the hotel.

Stoneridge Golf Course is a 10-minute drive from this hotel. Prescott Regional Airport is 8 miles away. Prescott National Forest is a 25-minute drive from the property.
