Description

Featuring a year round outdoor swimming pool, this hotel is just 10 minutes’ drive from Blythe Speedway.

All rooms include free WiFi. A breakfast buffet is offered to guests daily. Each air-conditioned room provides a seating area with a work desk, a 40-inch flat screen cable TV with satellite channels and an en suite bathroom with a hairdryer at Best Western Desert Oasis Ehrenberg.

A microwave, a small refrigerator and coffee-making facilities are also featured.
