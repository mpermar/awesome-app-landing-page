Description

A microwave and a refrigerator are furnished in every studio and suite at Red Lion Inn and Suites Tucson Foothills North. Tea and coffee-making facilities are available and select suites include a sitting area.

Tucson Red Lion Inn and Suites includes a free membership to Gold's Gym, located next door. For guests’ convenience, a 24-hour front desk is available.

Tucson International Airport is 19 miles from the Red Lion Inn and Suites Tucson Foothills North. Mount Lemmon Ski Valley is 46 miles away.
