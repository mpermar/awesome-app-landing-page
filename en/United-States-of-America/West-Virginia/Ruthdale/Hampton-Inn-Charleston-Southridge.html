Description

Guests at the Charleston Southridge Hampton Inn can start the morning with a free continental breakfast or with an energizing workout in the modern fitness center. The hotel also features an indoor pool as well as an outdoor patio area, complete with a children's play area.

The Hampton Inn Charleston Southridge is in a great location only 4 miles from Kanawha State Forest, offering scenic hiking trails, horseback riding and top-rated white water rafting. The downtown area, including the East End historic district, is also easily accessible.
