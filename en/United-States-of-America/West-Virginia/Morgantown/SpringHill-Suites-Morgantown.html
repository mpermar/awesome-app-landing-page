Description

Rooms at SpringHill Suites Morgantown each include a separate sitting area with pull-out sofa bed. A well-lit desk with ergonomic chair, mini-fridge and microwave are also provided.

Guests have free access to Morgantown Springhill Suites’ fitness center and indoor pool area with whirlpool. A business center and laundry facilities are also available.

Buffet breakfast is available each morning. The SpringHill Suites Morgantown’s lobby offers free coffee, plenty of seating and a flat-screen TV.

Downtown Morgantown is a 2 miles drive. The Highpoint Raceway and Pennsylvania border are within 10 miles from the SpringHill Suites Morgantown.
