Description

Decorated in warm desert colors and cherry wood furniture, each room at Aquarius Casino Resort includes coffee facilities. The bathrooms feature granite countertops and stone tiled floors.

An outdoor pool and a gym are available at the hotel. Tennis courts and a business center are also offered.

Laughlin Ranch Golf Course is 5.3 miles from Aquarius Resort. Big Bend of the Colorado State Recreation Area is 6.7 miles away.
