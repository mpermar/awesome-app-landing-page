Description

This Nevada resort is centrally located in downtown Reno. It offers a free airport shuttle service to Reno-Tahoe International Airport, 8 km away.

Circus Circus Reno features a casino, a variety of restaurants, daily shows and entertainment.

Free WiFi is provided in each guest room. The modern non smoking guest rooms at Circus Circus Reno are equipped with a 50-inch HDTV and a coffee machine.

Each private bathroom has a hairdryer. Ironing facilities are available in every room.

Circus Circus Reno features a conference and banquet centre and is part of the Tri-Properties Resort Complex – THE ROW - that connects the Silver Legacy and Eldorado via a large indoor walkway.

Guests can stroll along the Circus Circus Carnival Midway which offers free circus acts and family-oriented fun.

Guests of Circus Circus Reno can enjoy Mexican cuisine and drinks into the late night at El Jefe’s Cantina.

Fresh sushi and sake is served at Kanpai Sushi. For a scrumptious breakfast, guests can stop by Madame Butterwork’s Curious Café, which features specialties such as Dutch baby pancakes, waffles and French toast.

A fitness facility and on-site shopping opportunities are available to guests of Circus Circus Reno.

Valet parking is also provided. Circus Circus Reno is 5 minutes’ walk from the Reno Events Center and the Reno Ballroom.

The property is 1. 6 km away from the University of Nevada. Virginia City and Lake Tahoe are within 30 km of the property.

Offering restaurants, bars, shops and a white water park, Truckee River Walk is 10 minutes’ walk away.
