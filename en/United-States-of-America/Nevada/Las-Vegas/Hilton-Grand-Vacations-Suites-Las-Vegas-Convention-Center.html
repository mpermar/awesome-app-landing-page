Description

Cable TV with HBO and free Wi-Fi are included in the suites at Hilton Grand Vacations Suites - Las Vegas. Each provides a microwave, refrigerator and kitchenware. A seating area with sofa bed is also featured.

Restaurant and show reservations are available at the tour desk. Guests can access a game room and a business center.

Dining options at Las Vegas Hilton Grand Vacations include The Marketplace and outdoor barbecue grills are also on site.

The Las Vegas Monorail is only a 5-minute walk from the Las Vegas Hilton Grand Vacation Suites hotel. McCarran International Airport is a 12-minute drive away.
