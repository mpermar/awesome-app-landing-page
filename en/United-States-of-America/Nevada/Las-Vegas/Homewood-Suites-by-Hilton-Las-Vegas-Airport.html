Description

Begin the day at the Homewood Suites Las Vegas Airport with a free hot breakfast before taking the free shuttle service to the Las Vegas Strip. After a long day, relax in the pool and take advantage of the hotel's free grocery-shopping service.

Minutes from the Las Vegas Airport Homewood Suites, there are the excitement of the Las Vegas Strip, including Mandalay Bay. The hotel is also close to several area highways, providing easy access to nearby sites such as Red Rock National Park.
