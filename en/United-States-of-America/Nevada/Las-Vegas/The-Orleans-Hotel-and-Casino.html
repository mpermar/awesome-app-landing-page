Description

A separate seating area is available in all spacious guest rooms at The Orleans Hotel and Casino. Coffee-making facilities are provided.

With 14 dining options, spanning from casual to fine dining, guests' at the Hotel and Casino Orleans have plenty of choices. Alder & Birch features gourmet cuisine, The Prime Rib Loft offers steak and seafood, and guests can enjoy Asian fare at Ondori's.

A spa offering massage, a salon, and hot towel shaves is available at the hotel and casino. A pool, fitness center, and video game arcade are also included.
