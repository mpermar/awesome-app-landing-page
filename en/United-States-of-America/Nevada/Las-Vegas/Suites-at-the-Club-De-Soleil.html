Description

All units have a kitchen, a private bathroom and a terrace with views of pool. A dishwasher and oven are also available, as well as a coffee machine.

Guests staying at the apartment have access to the on-site spa and wellness center which includes a hot spring bath and a hot tub.

A garden and an outdoor swimming pool are featured at Suites at the Club De Soleil.

Crystals Shopping Center is 2.5 miles from the accommodation. McCarran International Airport is 3.7 miles away, and the property offers a free airport shuttle service.
