Description

Offering a patio and mountain views, the vacation home includes 4 bedrooms, a living room, satellite flat-screen TV, an equipped kitchen, and a bathroom with a bath and a shower. The air-conditioned vacation home also has a seating area, washing machine and a bathroom with free toiletries.

The Winner’s Retreat has a grill. If you would like to discover the area, skiing and cycling are possible nearby and The Winner’s Retreat can arrange a car rental service.

Bellagio Fountains is 11 miles from The Winner’s Retreat, while Crystals Shopping Center is 11 miles away. The nearest airport is McCarran International Airport, 12 miles from the property.
