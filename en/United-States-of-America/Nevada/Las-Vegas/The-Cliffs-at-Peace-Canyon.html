Description

The Cliffs at Peace Canyon features one and 2 bedroom accommodations with fully equipped kitchens and separate living areas. After some area sightseeing or shopping, take a swim in the pool or relax with the spa services.

Enjoy the outdoor fireplace and entertainment area and meet some new friends. The great location plus the comfort and independence of the guestrooms make the Cliffs an ideal place for a Las Vegas getaway.
