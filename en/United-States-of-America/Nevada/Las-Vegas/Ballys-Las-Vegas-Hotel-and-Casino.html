Description

A safe is available in all rooms at Bally’s Las Vegas Hotel and Casino. Ironing facilities and a work desk are also included.

Featuring nationally recognized chefs, Bally’s Steakhouse is on site at Las Vegas Bally’s Hotel and Casino. Tequila Taqueria features classic Mexican food, and Ichiban Sushi serves nigiri and various specialty rolls. Guests can also visit the all day buffet at Buca Cafe Italiano which serves a selection of traditional Italian dishes.

An extensive casino is available for guest entertainment and Jubilee!, featuring classic Vegas showgirls, is located at the property. An outdoor pool, and spa offering a variety of treatments, dry saunas, steam rooms, and hot bathtubs are featured at Bally’s.

Sands Expo and Convention Center is 5 minutes’ drive from the hotel. McCarran International Airport is 3.5 miles away. The Wynn Golf course is less than 10 minutes’ drive from the property.

Las Vegas Strip is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, nightlife and entertainment.
