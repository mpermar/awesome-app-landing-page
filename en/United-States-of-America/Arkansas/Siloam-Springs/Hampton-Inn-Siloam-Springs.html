Description

A seating area and spacious work desk are provided in all warmly decorated rooms at the Hampton Inn Siloam Springs. Each room includes a coffee maker and a clock radio.

Free access to the on-site fitness room is offered to all guests. The hotel’s business center offers a computer with free Wi-Fi, and meeting space is available.

A daily hot breakfast is offered at the Siloam Springs Hampton Inn.

The Siloam Springs Museum and Cherokee Casino are both within a 5-minute drive of this hotel. Natural Falls State Park offers a variety of recreational activities and is 10.1 miles away.
