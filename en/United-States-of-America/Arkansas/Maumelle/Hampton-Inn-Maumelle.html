Description

Free Wi-Fi access and a flat-screen TV are included in every room at Hampton Inn Maumelle. They are equipped with a refrigerator, microwave, and coffee maker.

Guests at the Maumelle Hampton Inn can exercise in the gym or work in the business center. There are guest laundry facilities and a snack shop on site as well.

The Little Rock Zoo and Pinnacle Mountain State Park are both 12 miles from the hotel.
