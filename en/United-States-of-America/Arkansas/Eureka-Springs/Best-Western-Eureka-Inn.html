Description

All rooms at Best Western Eureka Inn are 100% non-smoking. Free Wi-Fi and a 37-inch flat-screen TV with cable channels are featured in each traditional guest room. A coffee machine and ironing facilities are also provided.

At Eureka Inn, guests can go for a refreshing swim in the seasonal heated outdoor pool or enjoy a relaxing soak in the hot tub. Guests can start the day with a challenging workout in the hotel’s fitness center. Meeting rooms are available for conferences and parties.

Eureka Best Western is just 3 miles from The Great Passion Play and less than 1 miless from Ozark Mountain Ziplines. Pine Mountain Jamboree music theater is 1 mile away and Turpentine Creek Wildlife Refuge is within a 15-minute drive of the property.
