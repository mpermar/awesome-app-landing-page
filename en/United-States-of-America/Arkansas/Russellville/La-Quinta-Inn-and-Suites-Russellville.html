Description

LaQuinta Inn and Suites Russellville features rooms with a cable TV. A coffee machine, microwave, and fridge are provided for in-room convenience.

A fitness center with exercise equipment and flat-screen TV is accessible for guests of Russellville LaQuinta Inn. A laundry facility provides a coin-operated washing machine and dryer.

A manager’s reception where beer, wine, and snacks are served is held Monday through Thursday evenings. A continental breakfast of cereal, muffins, fruit, and waffles is served daily. A convenience store sells drinks, snacks and toiletry items.

Lake Dardanelle State Park is within 2 miles of the hotel. Holla Bend National Wildlife Refuge is 15 miles away.
