Description

Embassy Suites Omaha-La Vista/ Hotel & Conference Center suites include a separate living area equipped with satellite TV and video games. The suites also have a microwave and refrigerator.

Guests at the Embassy Suites La Vista are provided with a daily breakfast and a weekday evening reception. Guests can also dine at La Vista Nines with casual dining for lunch and dinner.

The La Vista Embassy Suites Omaha has an indoor pool and hot tub. The hotel also features a gym and business center.

The Embassy Suites Hotel & Conference Center is within a 45-minute drive of downtown Lincoln. The hotel is within a 6-minute drive of the Oak View Mall, which features shopping and dining.
