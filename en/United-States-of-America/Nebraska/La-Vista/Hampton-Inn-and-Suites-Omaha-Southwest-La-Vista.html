Description

Free access to the hotel gym and a hot tub is provided to all guests at the the Hampton Inn and Suites Omaha Southwest-La Vista. A daily buffet breakfast is served in the morning and the Pavilion Pantry convenience store is open 24/7.

Each air-conditioned room at the La Vista-Omaha Hampton Inn is furnished with a flat-screen cable TV and a coffee maker. All rooms include a work desk, hairdryer and ironing facilities.

Downtown La Vista is 4.5 miles from the Hampton Inn. Attractions including the Omaha Zoo and Joslyn Art Museum are 13 miles away.
