Description

This property is a 10-minute walk from the beach. Featuring an on-site restaurant and an outdoor swimming pool, Cape Arundel Inn and Resort is located in Kennebunkport. The property features a Main House and a Clubhouse located 10 minutes' walk away. Free WiFi access is available and yoga is offered seasonally.

A flat-screen cable TV is provided in each elegantly appointed guest room at the resort’s two buildings. The private bathrooms are equipped with free toiletries. Nightly turndown service is available upon request. Private In-Room Dining is available for Rockbound Bungalows and Ocean Suites at an additional cost.

A shared lounge and games room are available at Kennebunkport Cape Arundel Inn and Resort. Meeting facilities are provided. An array of activities can be enjoyed in the surroundings, including golfing. Golf cart rental is available and daily shuttle service is offered throughout the day. There are lawn games and movies at the Clubhouse.

Ocean restaurant is located on-site and offers fine dining influenced by southern French and Mediterranean cuisine. Pool menu at the Clubhouse including a full bar is available seasonally from 11:30 to 19:00. Daily Wine & Cheese hour is offered seasonally as well as weekly wine tastings. Starting in May, mixology class is offered once a week. Daily happy hour is also available to guests.

If you feel like visiting the surroundings, Webhannet Golf Club is 12 minutes’ drive.
