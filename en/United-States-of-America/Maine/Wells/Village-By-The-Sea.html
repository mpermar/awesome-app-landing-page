Description

A balcony or patio is included with each unit at the property. The dining area has a microwave and refrigerator. Apartments also have a sitting area and cable TV.

There is a 24-hour front desk at Wells Village by the Sea. Meeting facilities and laundry facilities are available. The property provides free parking.

Ogunquit Beach and Kennebunkport are both a 13-minute drive from Village by the Sea.
