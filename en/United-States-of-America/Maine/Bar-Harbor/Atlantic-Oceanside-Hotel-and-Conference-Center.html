Description

Cable TV and free WiFi are included in each room at the Atlantic Oceanside Hotel & Conference Center. The warmly decorated rooms have a refrigerator, coffee machine, and work desk.

A daily continental breakfast is served at the Bar Harbor Atlantic Oceanside Hotel. Guests can visit the fitness centre or relax in the hot tub. A free seasonal shuttle to Acadia National Park and Bar Harbor town centre is available from mid-June until Columbus day.

Cadillac Mountain is 8.5 miles away. Bar Harbor Whale Watch is 1.4 miles from the hotel.
