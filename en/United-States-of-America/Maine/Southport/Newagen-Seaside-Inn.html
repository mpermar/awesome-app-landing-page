Description

Located on 20 acres of oceanfront property, Newagen Seaside Inn is located on Southport Island, just 15 minutes' drive from Boothbay Harbor. Free breakfast, free parking, and free WiFi are provided for all guests.

Featuring a restaurant, an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness center, the inn offers a tennis court, free bike and row boat rentals, a games room and a children's playground. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including fishing, hiking and bowling.

Portland International Jetport Airport is 69 mi away from Newagen Seaside Inn.
