Description

The spacious guest rooms at Inn By the Sea are warmly decorated with soft colors and local artwork. All accommodations feature flat-screen TVs and coffee makers.

Guests can enjoy open-air dining at the Sea Glass restaurant. There is also an intimate bar and lounge for drinks.

The inn is 7 miles from downtown Portland. The L.L. Bean in Freeport is a 32-minute drive from the inn.
