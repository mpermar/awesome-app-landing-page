Description

Boasting an indoor heated pool and an outdoor pool, this resort is located across 11 acres.

Free WiFi access is available. The property also has 2 fire pits and a bocci court.

A balcony and seating area are included in each guest room at Seafarer Condominium Resort.

Select rooms are equipped with a kitchen or kitchenette. Air conditioning/heat, free toiletries, a coffee machine, hairdryer and cable TV are also provided.

A garden and BBQ facilities are located on the grounds at the resort. Refreshments are available from the on-site vending machine.

If you feel like visiting the surroundings, Ogunquit Beach is 1. 5 km away from the Seafarer Resort Condominiums.

Ogunquit and downtown Ogunquit is 950 metres away. Playhouse is steps from the property.
