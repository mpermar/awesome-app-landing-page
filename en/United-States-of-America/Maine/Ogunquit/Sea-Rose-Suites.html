Description

Accessible by only stairs, Sea Rose Suites feature a private balcony, free Wi-Fi and a kitchenette. A flat-screen cable TV and a coffee maker are also included.

Guests of the Sea Rose are welcome to enjoy the on-site fitness center and BBQ facilities. Laundry facilities and newspapers are also offered.

The Ogunquit Playhouse is 2,650 feet from the suites. Perkins Cove is an 8-minute walk.
