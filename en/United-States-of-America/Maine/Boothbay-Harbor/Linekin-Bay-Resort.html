Description

Free WiFi is available throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Views of the sea, pool or garden and flat-screen TVs are featured in certain rooms.

There is free shuttle service, a shared lounge and gift shop at the property.

Free use of bicycles is available at this resort and the area is popular for fishing and canoeing. Portland International Jetport Airport is 62 mi away.
