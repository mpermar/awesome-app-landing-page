Description

Enjoy a tranquil retreat all year-round at the Shoreline Inn and Conference Center, an Ascend Collection hotel located in the lakefront resort community of Muskegon, MI.

This hotel is near the Culinary Institute of Michigan and Pere Marquette Lighthouse.

Other nearby attractions include: Frauenthal Theater, Lakeshore Museum Center, Muskegon Museum of Art and L.

C. Walker Arena. Guests of the hotel will enjoy the indoor and seasonal outdoor pool, two indoor spa tubs, an exercise facility, an on-site restaurant, room service and access to rooms with waterfront views upon request.

Business travelers will appreciate the on-site Business Center, which offers access to a computer with Internet and copy and fax services.

Seven meeting rooms are available, with the hotel able to accommodate 300 for banquets and 250 for conferences.

Other amenities include: Free wireless high-speed Internet access and free newspaper are available.

The hotel is 100 percent non-smoking. All well-appointed guest rooms offer cable television, a refrigerator, microwave, hair dryer, alarm clock, coffee maker, iron and ironing board.
