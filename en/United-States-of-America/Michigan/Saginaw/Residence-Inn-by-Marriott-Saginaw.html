Description

A seating area with a flat-screen cable TV and a sofa bed is furnished in all suites at Residence Inn Saginaw. The traditionally styled rooms also include a work desk and bathroom amenities.

The Saginaw Residence Inn serves a daily breakfast buffet. The hotel also offers a local restaurant dinner delivery service as well as free coffee and newspapers in the lobby.

This hotel is within 1.5 miles of Kokomo’s Family Fun Center and Stardust Lanes. MBS International Airport is a 20-minute drive away.
