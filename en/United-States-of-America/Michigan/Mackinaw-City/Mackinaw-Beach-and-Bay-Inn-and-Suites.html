Description

Every suite at the Mackinaw Beach and Bay All Suites Resort includes a refrigerator, a microwave and a coffee maker. Two flat-screen HDTVs and a sofa bed are also available in the suites. The suites feature separate living and sleeping areas.

The Mackinaw Bay All Suites Resort include an on-site hot tub. A fitness center is also on site.

Mackinaw Island is a short ferry ride from the Mackinaw Beach All Suites Resort. The historic district includes Fort Mackinac and offers tours of the many lighthouses along the coast.
