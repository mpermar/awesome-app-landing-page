Description

Each comfortably furnished room at Comfort Inn Ludington features cable TV. Tea and coffee making facilities are provided in each bright en suit accommodations. A seating area is included.

A 24-hour reception is offered at Ludington Comfort Inn. A business center complete with fax and photocopying services is available. Laundry facilities is located on site.

Big Sable Point Lighthouse is 5 minutes’ drive from this hotel. Serving traditional fare such as roasted chicken and cottage pie, Jamesport Brewing Company is 2 miles away.
