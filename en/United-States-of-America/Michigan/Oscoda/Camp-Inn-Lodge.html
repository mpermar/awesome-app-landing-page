Description

A flat-screen cable TV is provided in all air-conditioned accommodations at Camp Inn Lodge. All rooms include a microwave, fridge, and coffeemaker. The villas feature a full kitchen and views of the lake.

The Camp Inn Lodge Restaurant has a daily continental breakfast. Guests can enjoy char-grilled burgers and other American classics for lunch and dinner for an additional fee.

Guests at Lodge Camp Inn will have access to a private beach area and a sauna. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the area, including golf and cycling. The property provides free parking.

The Au Sable River is just over half a mile away. Oscada-Wurtsmith Airport can be reached in a 10-minute drive.
