Description

A dining area and a 32-inch, flat-screen cable TV is included with all accommodation of TownePlace Suites Joliet South. The fully-equipped kitchens offer a stovetop, a dishwasher and cutlery.

A freshly-prepared continental breakfast is served daily. For light snacks and refreshments, TownePlace Suites Joliet South provides a snack bar and vending machines.

Chicagoland Speedway is a 15-minute drive from the hotel. Joliet South TownePlace Suites offers free public parking.
