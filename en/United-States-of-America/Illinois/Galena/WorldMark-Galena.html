Description

A balcony and dining area are provided in every studio and apartment at WorldMark Galena. For added convenience, a microwave, fridge, and tea and coffee-making facilities are also included.

An on-site fitness center and sauna are available at Galena WorldMark. Guests can enjoy the resort’s game room or views of the countryside while making use of the outdoor BBQ facilities.

The Shenandoah Horseback Riding Center is a 5 minute drive from this resort. The Ulysses S. Grant Home and Historic Site is 7 miles away.
