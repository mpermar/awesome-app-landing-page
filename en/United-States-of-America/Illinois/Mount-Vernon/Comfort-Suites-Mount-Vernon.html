Description

The Comfort Suites Mount Vernon is situated in close proximity to several popular points of interest, including the Cherry Creek Miniature Golf Course, the Mitchell Art Museum and a number shopping centers.

Guests at the pet-friendly Mount Vernon Comfort Suites will appreciate the hotel's complimentary hot breakfast, in-room microwaves and refrigerators and 24-hour heated indoor swimming pool.
