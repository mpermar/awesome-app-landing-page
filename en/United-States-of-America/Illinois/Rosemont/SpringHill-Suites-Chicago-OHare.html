Description

Guest rooms at the Chicago SpringHill Suites come with mini-refrigerators and microwave. Guests can also use the in-room free Wi-Fi or the coffee maker.

An indoor pool and fitness center are featured at SpringHill Suites Chicago O'Hare. Laundry facilities and a 24-hour front desk are also included at this completely non-smoking hotel.

Chicago city center is a 20-minute drive from the SpringHill Suites near O'Hare. The Donald E. Stevens Convention Center is a 5-minute drive away, and guests will be 2 miles from Rivers Casino.
