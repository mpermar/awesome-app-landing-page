Description

Styled in warm colors and dark wood furniture, the modern suites offer a private bedroom with a spacious living room. A small refrigerator, microwave and an iPod docking station are provided.

Osteria Via Stato features a traditional 3-course Italian meal. Embassy Suites Chicago–Downtown offers a daily continental breakfast and a nightly manager’s reception with free snacks and beverages.

Guests at Chicago Embassy Suites can workout in the well-equipped gym or visit the gift shop. A concierge desk and a business center are available.

The Magnificent Mile shopping area is just 3 blocks from this hotel and the Water Tower Place is a 15-minute walk away. Soldier Field and Shedd Aquarium are 3 miles away.

River North is a great choice for travelers interested in restaurants, shopping and food.
