Description

A 42-inch flat-screen TV and free Wi-Fi are featured in every room at the Chicago Peninsula. Each room includes a temperature and lighting control console and soaking tub in the private bathroom.

The Peninsula Chicago features a half-Olympic indoor swimming pool enclosed by floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the cityscape. The modern gym provides fitness classes, including yoga. The hotel also has an outdoor terrace with city views.

The hotel has a renowned afternoon tea serving champagne and chocolate. The hotel's award-winning restaurants include The Lobby, which serves contemporary American cuisine surrounded by elegant decor. There is also an on-site bar.

The historic Water Tower Place is across the street from the hotel. Navy Pier is just a 15-minute walk away.

River North is a great choice for travelers interested in restaurants, shopping and food.
