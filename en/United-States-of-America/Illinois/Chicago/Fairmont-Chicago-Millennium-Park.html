Description

Just 1 block away from Millennium Park and Navy Pier, this downtown Chicago hotel offers on-site gourmet dining options, 24-hour fitness centre and full-service spa.

Free WiFi is available in the hotel lobby. Every modern room at Fairmont Chicago Millennium Park features a 42-inch flat-screen TV and a large marble bathroom with a rain shower, separate bath tub and Le Labo bath amenities.

Guests can enjoy beverages from the minibar or Starbucks brewed coffee from the coffee maker.

In-room dining services are also offered. Columbus Tap offers a tap house experience with a Midwest-focused menu.

Signature craft cocktails and extensive wine choice are paired with small plates in THE BAR.

The Field Museum and Shedd Aquarium are both 2 miles away from the Fairmont. This hotel is 3 block away from Chicago’s Magnificent Mile, which features shopping, dining and entertainment.
