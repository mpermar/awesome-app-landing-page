Description

Homewood Suites by Hilton Louisville-East features suites with full kitchens and 24-hour housekeeping services. Guests will also enjoy in-suite amenities such as free high-speed internet access and flat-panel TVs with cable.

Guests staying at the Louisville-East Homewood Suites will start the day with a free hot breakfast. On evenings from Monday through Thursday, guests can relax at the Welcome Home reception with a complimentary light meal and beverages.
