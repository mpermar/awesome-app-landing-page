Description

All rooms come with a microwave, fridge, a coffee machine, a shower, free toiletries and a desk. The rooms at the hotel have air conditioning and a closet.

An American breakfast is served each morning at the property.

At Aloft Newport on the Levee guests are welcome to use an indoor swimming pool. You can play pool at the accommodation, and the area is popular for cycling.

Other services and facilities offered at Aloft Newport on the Levee include a business center, an ATM machine and newspapers.

Highland Hills Park is 2.1 miles from the hotel. The nearest airport is Cincinnati/Northern Kentucky International Airport, 9 miles from Aloft Newport on the Levee.
