Description

Guests have free access to Residence Inn Paducah’s fitness center and indoor pool. An outdoor barbecue area with patio is also available, and Wi-Fi access is free throughout the hotel.

Paducah Residence Inn’s rooms include a separate living area, and kitchen with fridge, microwave and dishwasher. In-room entertainment includes cable TV with premium channels.

The Residence Inn Paducah’s buffet breakfast includes biscuits and gravy, croissant sandwiches and other hot items. Evening social hours with free drinks occur weekly.

The hotel is next to Interstate 24 and less than 3 miles from Fort Massac State Park and the Illinois-Kentucky border.
