Description

Each cottage or condominium at the Paradise Resort features a full kitchen, a sitting area with a sofa or sofa bed, and cable TV. Most cottages offer waterfront or water views from a private deck.

Guests at the Murray Paradise Resort can relax in the outdoor pool or enjoy a game of volleyball or tennis. They also have a private sandy beach area, boat rentals, and barbecue facilities available.

Miller Memorial Golf Course is 10 miles from the accommodation. The Golden Pond Planetarium and Observatory in the Land Between the Lakes National Recreation Area is 18 miles away.
