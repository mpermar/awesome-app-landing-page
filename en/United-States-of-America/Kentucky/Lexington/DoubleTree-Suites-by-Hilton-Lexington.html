Description

Popular area attractions, including the University of Kentucky, Red Mile Harness Track and the historic Mary Todd Lincoln house are moments from the Doubletree Guest Suites Lexington.

The Lexington Doubletree Guest Suites provides guests with comfortable Sweet Dreams beds, and in-room MP3 compatible radios. Guests can also enjoy on-site dining at the hotel's restaurant, working out in the modern fitness center, or buy snacks at the on-site convenience shop.

The rooms at this non-smoking property feature sitting areas and HD TVs.
