Description

With stair access only, the Inn Building is located across the street from the private beach and the amenities building. This building is surrounded by an all-purpose field and adjacent to the outdoor pool

The Lakefront Building is connected to the main amenities building by an underground walkway leading to the front desk, indoor pool, health club and Blackstone's Restaurant where breakfast is served daily.

Cable TV and free WiFi are included in each traditionally styled guest rooms. Gourmet coffee-making facilities and a seating area are provided.

A continental breakfast is provided during the winter months and a hot breakfast buffet is provided during the summer months. The waterside Gazebo Beach Bar offers lunch, dinner, cocktails, and a 42" plasma TV.

The shops at the Tanger Outlet Center are 13 mi from The Margate Laconia Resort. The Gunstock Mountain Resort is 6.8 mi away.
