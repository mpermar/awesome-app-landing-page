Description

The accommodations have a TV, air conditioning and a patio with BBQ facilities. There is also a full kitchen equipped with a microwave and a refrigerator.

At Cathedral Ledge Resort, there are tennis courts and a fitness center. Additional facilities at the property include a game room, a playground and a vending machine. Free Wi-Fi access is available athe this resort.

Merriman State Forest and Story Land amusement park are each a 6-minute drive from the resort. The property has free parking.
