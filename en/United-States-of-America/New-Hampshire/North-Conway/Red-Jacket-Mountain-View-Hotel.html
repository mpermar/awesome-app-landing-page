Description

Nestled on 40 acres, overlooking the Moat Mountain Range and the Saco River Valley, this resort in North Conway, New Hampshire offers an indoor water park at a surcharge.

Red Jacket Mountain View Hotel is home to Kahuna Laguna, a 4,716 sq metre water park, which boasts a wave pool, waterslides and more.

Guests can also go for a swim in the seasonal outdoor pool, play a game in the game room, or work out in the well-equipped fitness centre.

Champney's Restaurant at the Mountain View Red Jacket Hotel serves New England style cuisine.

For light fair and delicious specialties, guests can visit the resort's Palmer's Red Ridge Grille.
