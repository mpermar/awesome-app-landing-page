Description

The Attitash Mountain Village provides easy access to top-rated ski trails, scenic hiking trails and canoeing and kayaking locations. Guests can also discover numerous nearby shopping centers, including outlet stores and antique shops.

In addition to 60 acres of tranquil landscape, the Attitash Mountain Village features indoor and outdoor pools, tennis courts and hot tubs. Guests will also enjoy free Wi-Fi internet access as well as modern on-site fitness facilities.

"The room was the perfect size for a weekend away with my husband. The room was rustic themed with lots of little added personality throughout. Plenty of seating in the room including an adorable rustic rocking chair. Just the right size kitchen with a fridge microwave and all the dishes/utensils you could need. There was also a small dining table and chairs perfect for a little snack or a full meal. The bed was soft with plenty of pillows. There were also extra pillows and blankets in the closet.The bathroom was a typical bathroom with a tub/shower combo. Added bonus was the heat light perfect for warming up when you get out of the shower. The room was also full of rustic charm.
