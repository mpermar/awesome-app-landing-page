Description

A flat-screen TV is offered in the living area of Valley Brook Cottages and Cabins. Bedrooms feature wood furnishing and comfortable bedding.

One-room cottages and cabins have a kitchenette with a gas stove. The one-bedroom cottages and cabins have two-burner hot plates. Gas and charcoal barbecues are also provided.

Guests can swim in the outdoor heated seasonal pool between 28 May and 20 September, relax in the hot tub or enjoy a game of tennis at the property.

Loon Mountain is 9 miles away from Valley Brook Cottages and Cabins. Whales Tale Waterpark is 15 minutes drive.
