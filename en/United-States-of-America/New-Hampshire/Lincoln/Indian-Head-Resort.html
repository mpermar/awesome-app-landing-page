Description

Offering a relaxed, family-friendly atmosphere in the heart of Lincoln, New Hampshire's scenic White Mountains, this resort offers many on-site activities along with easy access to surrounding area attractions.

The Indian Head Resort is situated near a variety of leisure activities, including skiing, biking and fishing.

Guests can also easily discover local shopping centres as well as beautiful natural surroundings.

Voted the 2009 Best Family-Friendly Resort in New Hampshire, the Indian Head Resort features live nightly entertainment at the on-site lounge, seasonal supervised children's programmes and a large gift shop.

The resort also features an indoor and outdoor swimming pool as well as an on-site lake, offering fishing and boating.

Loon Mountain is 13. 9 km from Indian Head Resort and Ski New Hampshire is 7. 3 km away.

Alpine Adventures is 5. 9 km away and White Mountains Visitor Centre is 5. 7 km away.
