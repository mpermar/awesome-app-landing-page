Description

Fairfield Inn & Suites by Marriott - Cumberland have air-conditioned rooms with a flat-screen cable TV, Pay TV, a work desk and a mini-fridge.

Guests can enjoy a hot breakfast daily which features Jimmy Dean sandwiches, cereal and other healthy ingredients. Complimentary coffee is available in the lobby.

Fairfield Inn and Suites Cumberland is 1,500 feet from Allegany County Museum. Frostburg State University is 12 mi from the hotel.
