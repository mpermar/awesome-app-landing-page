Description

All guest bedrooms at Bowie’s Hampton Inn are equipped with an LCD TV with cable channels and video games. Every room has hot drink facilities and an en suite bathroom with a hairdryer.

Breakfast is served in the spacious dining area, and breakfast bags are available for those in a hurry. Restaurants within 5 minutes’ walk include Jarry’s Seafood and TGI Friday’s.

Hampton Inn Bowie is 7 miles from Goddard Space Flight Center and 4 miles from Bowie Golf and Country Club. Free on-site car parking is available.
