Description

City views can be enjoyed from each guest room. A flat-screen cable TV and a seating area with a sofa are provided. The private bathrooms have a hairdryer and free toiletries.

A fitness center is available at the Baltimore Inner Harbor Hyatt Place. Meeting facilities and a shared lounge are also offered for guests’ convenience.

Maryland Science Center is 1.2 mi from the hotel. Oriole Park at Camden Yards, home of the Baltimore Orioles baseball team, is also 5 mi from the property.

Little Italy is a great choice for travelers interested in bars, food and restaurants.
