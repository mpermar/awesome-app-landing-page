Description

The Hampton Inn Waldorf places guests only moments from scenic hiking trails and excellent bike paths. One of the area's largest shopping centers is also located across from the hotel. Downtown Washington, D.C. is also within driving distance, and is ideal for a day trip.

Guests at the Waldorf Hampton Inn are provided with a variety of thoughtful amenities, including a free hot breakfast. The hotel also offers comfortable Cloud Nine beds as well as free high-speed internet access.
