Description

Hampton Inn Hagerstown-Maugansville features guest rooms with coffee makers and cable TV. Microwaves and mini-refrigerators are also included in every room.

Guests at the Hagerstown Hampton Inn can go for a swim in the indoor pool or workout in the fitness center. A business center is also available on-site.

Hagerstown Speedway and the Hagerstown City Park are only minutes from this Hampton Inn in Hagerstown. Penn State University is also within driving distance.
