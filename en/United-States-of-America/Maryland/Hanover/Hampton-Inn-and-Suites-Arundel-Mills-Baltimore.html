Description

The Hampton Inn and Suites Arundel Mills/Baltimore is conveniently situated close to Highway 295, which allows easy access to downtown Baltimore as well as Washington, D.C. Monuments, memorials and museums along with historic Annapolis and Baltimore's Inner Harbor are all nearby.

Guests at the Arundel Mills/Baltimore Hampton Inn can relax in the seasonal outdoor pool, enjoy a free hot breakfast and browse the internet with free high-speed access. The hotel also features a 24-hour modern fitness center.
