Description

A microwave, refrigerator and coffee-making facilities come standard in every room at this hotel. Guests at Drury Inn and Suites Atlanta Airport also include a hairdryer and ironing facilities.

Business guests can keep up with work in the on-site business center at this Drury Inn and Suites. A fitness center and laundry facilities are also available.

Georgia International Convention Center is just 2.5 miles from this hotel. Guests are a 10-minute drive from Turner Field, home to the Atlanta Braves.
