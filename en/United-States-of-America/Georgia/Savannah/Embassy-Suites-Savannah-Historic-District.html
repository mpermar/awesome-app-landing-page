Description

Free Wi-Fi, a 42-inch flat-screen cable TV, and a microwave are featured in all suites. A refrigerator and additional counter space with a sink are provided by Embassy Suites Savannah Historic District as well.

Concierge services, a tour desk, and a business center are available to all guests throughout their stay at this Savannah Embassy Suites. A laundromat, valet, and self-parking are available as well.

The evening reception is a complimentary 2-hour event, which includes a full bar. Talmadge Grill serves traditional American Southern cuisine, and Market 405 includes a coffee shop with sandwiches and salads.

The hotel is just under 1 miles from Savannah College of Art and Design and a 10-minute drive from Savannah Hilton Head International Airport. Tybee Island Beach is 17 miles away.
