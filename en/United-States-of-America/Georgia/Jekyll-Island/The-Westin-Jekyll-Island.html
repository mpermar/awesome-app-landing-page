Description

The Westin Jekyll Island is a dog-friendly resort located along the Georgia Atlantic Coast and offers direct beach access, an outdoor pool, and on-site restaurants. The property has a LEED Silver Certification. The Georgia Sea Turtle Center is 1.2 mi away.

The banquet and meeting rooms offer a total 520 square yards of available function space. Plus, the Jekyll Island Convention Center is adjacent to the resort.

Salty's Oceanside is a tropical-style poolside bar and lunch spot with an ocean view. The Reserve serves modern American cuisine with a focus on fresh, local ingredients indoors or al fresco.

Every room at this resort is air conditioned and is fitted with a flat-screen cable TV and small refrigerator. A coffee machine and a rainforest-style shower is also included.

The Jekyll Island Beach Village shopping, dining, and oceanfront promenade are located around the property. Sea Island is 8.7 mi from The Westin.
