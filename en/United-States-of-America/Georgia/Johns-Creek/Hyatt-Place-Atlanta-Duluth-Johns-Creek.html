Description

All rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels. All rooms come with a private bathroom, while some have a lake view. At Hyatt Place Duluth, rooms include a seating area.

The accommodation offers a continental or buffet breakfast. The in-house restaurant serves American cuisine.
