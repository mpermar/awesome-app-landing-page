Description

Featuring rooms with panoramic river and garden views, 506 On the River Inn is located in Woodstock, Vermont. The property offers an indoor pool and sauna, gym and kids game rooms, a restaurant and bar.

Each air conditioned room includes a seating area and a balcony with a view. The decor style is influenced by New England. The private bathrooms are equipped with free toiletries and towels. Some rooms feature a river view.

Guests at Woodstockâ€™s 506 On the River Inn can enjoy an array of activities on site and in the surrounding area, including horseback riding, hiking, biking and skiing. WiFi access is free for all our guests.

506 Bistro & Bar offers a menu of seasonal dishes as well as a selection of local beers, wines and cocktails. A hot breakfast buffet is also included. A 24-hour front desk is available. Free parking is provided.

The inn is located 2.1 mi to Woodstock town and hiking trails, 9.3 mi to VINS Nature Center and the Ottauquechee gorge, and 18 mi to Killington ski resort.
