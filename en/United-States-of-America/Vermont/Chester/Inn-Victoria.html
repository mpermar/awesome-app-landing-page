Description

Inn Victoria features rooms decorated in antique furnishings and plush bedding. They all include free Wi-Fi and a flat-screen cable TV. Select rooms have a fireplace, a spa tub, or private deck with a hot tub.

Concierge services are available at the Victoria Inn. Guests can use the computer as well as copy and fax services.

A 3-course breakfast, including homemade baked goods, fruit, and a hot entree, is served daily in the dining room. Afternoon tea and snacks are also provided.

Taterhill Golf Club is 10 miles from the bed and breakfast. Enjoy a winter sleigh ride at Taylor Farm, a 20 minute drive away.
