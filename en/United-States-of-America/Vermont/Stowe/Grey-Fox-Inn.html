Description

This Stowe, Vermont inn is located 14. 8 km from Mount Mansfield. Guests can relax in the sauna after a day of skiing or hiking.

Each guest room at the Grey Fox Inn features cable TV and an en suite bathroom. Some rooms have a fireplace and/or kitchen facilities, while some have mountain views.

Visit the indoor and outdoor pools, as well as the fitness centre and hot tub at the Stowe Grey Fox Inn.

Walk, jog, or ride your bicycle on the nearby Stowe Recreation Path. Stowe Golf Course is 1.

8 km from the inn while Smugglers Notch State Park is 9. 2 km away. Spruce Peak Performing Arts Center is 10.
