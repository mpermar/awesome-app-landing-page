Description

The Gray Ghost Inn features a private bathroom with a hairdryer in every guest room. Select suites include additional amenities like a TV, a refrigerator, and living area.

Guests at the West Dover Gray Ghost Inn can relax by the fireplace in the lounge area, or play a variety of games, including billiards and ping-pong. Ski storage is also available.

A cook-to-order breakfast is served every morning in the dining room.

The Mount Snow Golf Course is a 5-minute drive from the inn. Hiking trails, available during warm weather months, are within 1 mile of the property.
