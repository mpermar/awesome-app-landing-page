Description

The traditionally furnished rooms at the Essex Culinary Resort and Spa include cable TV and free WiFi. Some feature a kitchenette or gas fireplace.

A golf course, gym, and 2 swimming pools are part of this Essex resort. Hot air balloon rides and fly fishing river tours are available.

A shuttle service to Burlington International Airport, 20 minutes' drive away, and the Essex Junction railway station are available for a fee.
