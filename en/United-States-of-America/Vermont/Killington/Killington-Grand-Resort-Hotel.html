Description

Offering an outdoor pool and a spa and wellness center, Killington Grand Resort Hotel is located in Killington, Vermont. Free Wi-Fi access is available in this resort.

The accommodations will provide you with a TV, air conditioning and a DVD player. There is also a coffee machine. Private bathrooms are equipped with a hairdryer.

At Killington Grand Resort Hotel you will find a restaurant and a fitness center. A seasonal breakfast is offered for the number of guests in your room. Other facilities offered at the property include a games room and ski storage. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including skiing and hiking.

If you feel like visiting the surroundings, Killington Peak is 1.2 mi away, Killington Golf Course is 200 feet from the property and the Killington Ski Area is 1.1 mi away. Pico Mountain is 6 mi away.The property offers free parking.
