Description

Located in Brownsville, Vermont, this resort offers access to a variety of year-round recreational activities, and on-site dining.

On-site laundry facilities are available to guests. Holiday Inn Club Vacations Mount Ascutney Resort features tennis and racquetball and basketball courts.

Guests can also enjoy nearby activities like backpacking, mountain biking and more.

The resort also offers indoor/outdoor pools, a fitness centre and an arcade for children.

The Maple Kitchen at the Holiday Inn Club Vacations Mount Ascutney Resort offers farm to table dining, hand-selected wines and desserts.
