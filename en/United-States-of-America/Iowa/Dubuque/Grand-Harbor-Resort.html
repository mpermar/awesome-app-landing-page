Description

A microwave, refrigerator and tea and coffee-making facilities are included in all rooms at Grand Harbor Resort. Each air-conditioned room also features an iron and a hairdryer.

Dubuque Grand Harbor Resort features a large Mississippi-themed indoor/outdoor water park with water slides, activity pools, hot bathtubs and a lazy river. A fitness center and complimentary transportation service are provided for guest convenience.

Tony Roma’s Restaurant and Lounge, on site, provides guest with a chance to relax in a casual atmosphere. Known for their selection of ribs and seafood, guests can enjoy the flat-screen TVs, live entertainment and views of the Mississippi River.

Next to the Mississippi Riverwalk, this hotel is less than 1 miles from downtown Dubuque. The National Mississippi River Museum & Aquarium is a 4-minute walk from the property.
