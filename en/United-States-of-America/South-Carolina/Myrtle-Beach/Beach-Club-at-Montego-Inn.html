Description

Free Wi-Fi is included in each studio and apartment at these Myrtle Beach vacation rentals. A TV is also provided in every unit and guests can relax on a private balcony.

An outdoor pool can be accessed by guests of the Montego Inn Beach Club and barbecue facilities are also available. A community room is also available to rent books, DVDs, and games.

This property is 2.5 miles from the Whispering Pines Gold Course and one mile from the Family Kingdom Amusement Park. Myrtle Beach State Park Pier is 4 miles away.
