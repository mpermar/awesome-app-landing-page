Description

An outdoor pool with a lazy river, surrounding sun terrace, and loungers are on site. An indoor pool and shared seating area with a fireplace are also available to all guests.

A kitchenette, living area with a flat-screen TV, and dining table are standard in each 1 and 2-bedroom holiday villa apartments. Extras include a sofa bed, washing machine, and tumble dryer. Some villas have a full kitchen.

Sunny's Poolside Bar and Grill is open seasonally during warmer months and offers cocktails, sandwiches, and salads. The restaurant is open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

The beach is 1.2 mi from the resort, while WonderWorks Myrtle Beach is 1.5 mi away. Family Kingdom Amusement Park is 5.5. km from Sheraton Broadway Plantation.
