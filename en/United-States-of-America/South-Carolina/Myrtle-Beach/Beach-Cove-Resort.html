Description

Offering an outdoor pool and indoor pool, this beach-front North Myrtle Beach resort is only 5 minutes’ drive from Barefoot Resort and Golf.

Guests can enjoy 3 outdoor pools, a childrens' pool, a 106-metre lazy river, and sauna.

A balcony with an ocean view, cable TV, and microwave are provided in each suite.

Apartments have a full kitchen, separate seating area, and sofa at this South Carolina Beach Cove Resort.

Free WiFi is provided. Beach Cove Resort guests can use the indoor hot tub and fitness centre.

A business centre is also provided, and a launderette is available. The seasonal Breeze’s Beach Bar and Grill offers a place for guests to lounge with a cocktail and have lunch or dinner, while the Town Center boasts oceanfront dining, a games room, gift shop, and a bar and grill.

This resort is 3 minutes’ drive from the Alligator Adventure theme park and 8 minutes’ drive from the Carolina Opry.
