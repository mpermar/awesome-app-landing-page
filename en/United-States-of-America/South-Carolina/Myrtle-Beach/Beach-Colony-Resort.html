Description

A cable TV and sitting area are included in all Beach Colony Resort rooms. Guests can enjoy a balcony with an ocean view. A kitchen with a microwave, a refrigerator, a stove, and coffee-making facilities are also available.

A spa and health club, a restaurant, a business center, and a fitness center are on site at the Resort Beach Colony. In addition, a game room, a sauna, a hot tub, and laundry facilities are provided for guests to enjoy.

Myrtle Waves Water Park is 4 miles from the Beach Colony. Medieval Times Dinner and Tournament is 7.5 miles from the resort. Guests can also go shopping at Tanger Outlets, which are a 10-minute drive away.
