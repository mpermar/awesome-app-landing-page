Description

At the hotel, every room comes with a balcony with a city, coastline, or Atlantic Ocean view. Every accommodations has a private bathroom with a bath and free toiletries. The rooms come with a 49-inch flat-screen TV with cable channels. Suites include a kitchenette.

A buffet breakfast is available every morning, and the hotel can conveniently provide information at the 24-hour front desk to help guests to get around the area. A sun deck is at guests' disposal at SOUTH BAY INN & SUITES.

The property offers 4-star accommodations with a hot tub and a waterpark with water slides. You can play table tennis and billiards at the accommodations.

Broadway at the Beach is 1.9 mi from SOUTH BAY INN & SUITES. The nearest airport is Myrtle Beach Airport, 2.5 mi from the property.
