Description

A small refrigerator is provided in all rooms and suites at the Grande Shores. Select suites include a full kitchen, dining area, and private balcony. Extras include cable TV, DVD player, and free, unlimited DVD rentals.

Grande Shores guests can relax in their choice of 5 hot bathtubs. Access to free Wi-Fi, a business center, and fitness center are provided. Meeting facilities are available.

The Shoreline Café and Lounge serves American cuisine and seafood for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. The pool area features a bar and grill.

Guests can interact with exotic animals at T.I.G.E.R.S. Preservation Station 6 miles away. The hotel is also 3 miles from Pine Lakes Country Club.
