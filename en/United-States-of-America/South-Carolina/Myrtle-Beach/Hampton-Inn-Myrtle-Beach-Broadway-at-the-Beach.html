Description

Only steps from the Hampton Inn Myrtle Beach-Broadway at the Beach's front doors, guests will find the legendary Hard Rock Cafe, Ripley's Aquarium and the IMAX Theater. Myrtle Beach International Airport is also easily accessible with the hotel's free airport shuttle service.

Before a day of excitement, activity or relaxation, guests at the Broadway at the Beach Hampton Inn can enjoy a free daily continental breakfast. Guests can also take advantage of the hotel's indoor and outdoor swimming pools.
