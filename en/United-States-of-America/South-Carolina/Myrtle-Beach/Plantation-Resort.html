Description

A fully-equipped kitchen and laundry facilities are included in most united at Plantation Resort. Guests will enjoy free WiFi and a TV with cable and a DVD player in all holiday rentals.

A fitness center and an outdoor pool are on site at the Plantation Resort. Guests will also have access to an indoor pool and a hot tub as well as a games room.

Mother Earth Day Spa is located inside the health and swim club and offers services by appointment including hot stone massages, reflexology, and facials. Spray tanning and couples massages are available as well.

Broadway at the Beach is 9.9 mi from this property. Myrtle Beach International Airport is 15 minutes’ drive away the Tanger Outlets are 12 minutes’ drive from the resort.
