Description

A microwave and refrigerator are included in every room at Holiday Inn Club Vacations South Beach Resort. A cable TV and coffee-making facilities are also provided.

A seasonal outdoor pool and year-round indoor pool are available to all guests at this Holiday Inn Club Vacations South Beach Resort in Myrtle Beach. Guests can also sip on a cocktail beside the pool at the seasonal Captain Cliff's Lagoon Bar.

The Market Place at the Myrtle Beach Holiday Inn Club Vacations South Beach Resort offers breakfast items and casual foods.
