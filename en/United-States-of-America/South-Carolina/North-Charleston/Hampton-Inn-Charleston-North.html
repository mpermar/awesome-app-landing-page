Description

Hampton Inn Charleston North features an outdoor swimming pool and on-site fitness center. After an afternoon swim, guests can relax and watch their in-room flat-screen TV. Guestrooms also boast microwaves and refrigerators.

The area surrounding the Charleston North Hampton Inn is filled with a variety of attractions. Guests can visit the North Charleston Coliseum or explore the historic Fort Sumter. After discovering the Charleston Museum, the oldest museum in the United States, visitors can go to the South Carolina Aquarium.
