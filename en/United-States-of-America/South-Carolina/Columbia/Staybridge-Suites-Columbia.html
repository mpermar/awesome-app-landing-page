Description

Each of the spacious suites at the Columbia Staybridge Suites includes hardwood furniture and a flat-screen TV. Every kitchen is complete with an oven, full-size refrigerator, and a dishwasher. Cookware and dishware are provided.

A gym and convenience store are part of this Columbia hotel. The business center is located off of the lobby and a breakfast buffet with fresh fruit is served every morning.

The Columbia Metropolitan Convention Center is a 5-minute drive from Staybridge Suites Columbia and Columbia Metropolitan Airport is 11 miles from the hotel.
