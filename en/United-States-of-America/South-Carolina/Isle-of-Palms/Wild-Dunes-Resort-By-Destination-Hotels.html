Description

Cable TV and a coffee maker are provided in all elegantly furnished rooms of Wild Dunes. Villas feature a fully equipped kitchen and laundry facilities.

Tennis courts, a fitness center and bicycle rentals are available to all guests of the Dunes. Local tours, boat rides and children’s activities can also be arranged.

Both fine dining and casual meals are served at the resort’s restaurants, including Hudson’s market and The Lettered Olive.

Central Mt. Pleasant is less than 10 miles from Wild Dunes. Historic Charleston city center is 25 minutes' drive away.
