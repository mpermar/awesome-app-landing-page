Description

Free Wi-Fi, a cable TV, a microwave and a refrigerator are included in the comfortable guest rooms at Liberty Inn. The spacious rooms offer cherrywood furnishings with writing desks, sitting area and a coffee maker.

Liberty Inn Dupont offers an on site business center with fax and photocopying services. Guests can also enjoy a workout in the fitness center and for convenience a laundromat is also available.

The Home Golf Course is 2 miles away. Nisqually National Wildlife Refuge is a 10 minute drive from Liberty Inn. Mount Rainier National Park is 60 miles southeast of the inn.
