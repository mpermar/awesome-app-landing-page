Description

A cable TV and a work desk are provided in each room at Mirabeau Park Hotel. The traditionally styled rooms also offer tea and coffee-making facilities. Some offer a private terrace.

Hotel Mirabeau offers Max at Mirabeau Restaurant, serving American and international dishes often made with local ingredients. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are available as well as some room service.

Guests can work out in the 24-hour fitness room or work in the business center. Free on-site parking is also available.

This hotel is less than a 10 minute drive from HUB Sports Center, and is about a 15 minute drive from downtown Spokane. Spokane International Airport is 18 miles away.
