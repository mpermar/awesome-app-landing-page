Description

All rooms at Holiday Inn Bellingham have a microwave, a coffee maker, and a refrigerator. Cable TV and a daily newspaper are also provided.

Holiday Inn Express Bellingham serves hot dishes, including a variety of eggs and bacon for its buffet breakfast. A business center with fax, copy, and printing services is available.

Holiday Inn Express provides a complimentary shuttle service from Bellingham International Airport and the Amtrak train station, both of which are less than 10 miles away. Whatcom Community College is less than a mile from the hotel.
