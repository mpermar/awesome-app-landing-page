Description

The rooms in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV. With a private bathroom, some accommodations at Suncadia Resort also feature a river view. Guest rooms have a desk.

The accommodation offers an American or vegetarian breakfast.

At Suncadia Resort guests are welcome to use a hot tub. Guests at the resort will be able to enjoy activities in and around Cle Elum, like cycling and hiking.

The reception at Suncadia Resort can provide tips on the area.

Ellensburg is 27 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Pangborn Memorial Airport, 40 miles from Suncadia Resort.
