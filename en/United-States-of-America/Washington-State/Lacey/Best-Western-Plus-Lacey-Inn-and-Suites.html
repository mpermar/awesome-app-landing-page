Description

A flat-screen satellite TV and a work desk are available in all rooms at the Best Western PLUS Lacey Inn and Suites. A microwave, small fridge, and coffee-making essentials are also included.

Breakfast at the Lacey Inn and Suites Best Western PLUS includes a choice of bread, cereal, eggs, and breakfast meat. Fruit, juice, and coffee are also offered.

A fitness center and business center are both available for guest use. Laundry and dry cleaning services are available.

The Nisqually National Wildlife Refuge is a 10 minute drive from the hotel. Fort Lewis is 10 miles away and the Centralia Outlets are a 30 minute drive from the property.
