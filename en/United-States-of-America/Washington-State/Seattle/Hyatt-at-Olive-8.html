Description

Styled in a soothing color scheme, each modern room provides an iPod docking station, a flat-screen cable TV and a sitting area. LEED Certified fixtures and amenities are featured in all rooms at Hyatt at Olive 8.

After a day in downtown Seattle, guests can enjoy a sauna and a fitness center at Olive 8 Hyatt. A 24-hour front desk is offered. Free Wi-Fi access is available throughout the hotel.

Guests can enjoy coffee and lite bites at Urbane Market on site. Urbane, the on-site restaurant, features a farm-to-table dining experience from produce of the Northwest.

Pike Place Fish Market and the Space Needle are within a 20 minutes’ walk of Hyatt at Olive 8. Seattle-Tacoma International Airport is 20 minutes’ drive from Hyatt at Olive 8.

Seattle Central Business District is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, seafood and food.
