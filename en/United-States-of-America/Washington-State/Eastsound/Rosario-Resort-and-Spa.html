Description

Each room here includes a private balcony with a sea view, a flat-screen TV, a desk, and free Wi-Fi access. A microwave, refrigerator, and a private bathroom with free toiletries are included.

Guests here can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant, and relax in the evening in the hot tub or sauna. For added convenience, a business center, meeting or banquet facilities, and a mini-market are all available at this resort.

Rosario Resort & Spa is a 12-minute drive from Orcas Island Airport and a 25-minute drive from Orcas Island Ferry Terminal.
