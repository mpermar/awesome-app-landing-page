Description

Free Wi-Fi is available in all rooms at the Holiday Inn Express Pullman. Each room includes a flat-screen cable TV with pay-per-view movies and video games. A microwave, fridge and coffee maker are included.

A gym and a business center are on site at the Pullman Holiday Inn Express. Free parking is possible at the hotel.

A hot breakfast bar is offered at the hotel. It features cinnamon rolls, bacon, eggs and fruit. Guests can also use the outdoor barbecue facilities.
