Description

Select rooms feature an ocean view and/or a balcony. Each room provides a flat-screen cable TV and free environmentally-friendly toiletries in the private bathroom at Semiahmoo Resort and Spa. All rooms are decorated with white linens and elegant wood furnishings.

Packers Oyster Bar features casual dining for lunch and dinner. Featuring a snack bar, Loomis Trail Bar and Grill is on site. With fine dining, Pierside Restaurant serves breakfast, lunch and dinner at Semiahmoo Resort.

A poolside hot tub is provided. A fitness center is also available.

Canadian Museum of Flight is 19 miles away. Langley Regional Airport is a 40-minute drive from Semiahmoo Resort and Spa.
