Description

Guests at the Hampton Inn and Suites Burlington can easily visit the San Juan Islands, British Columbia and North Cascades National Park. Beautiful gardens, excellent golf courses and top-rated ski trails are also nearby.

With amenities including in-room microwaves and refrigerators as well as free high-speed internet access, every stay at the Burlington Hampton Inn and Suites is sure to be pleasant. The hotel also features a free daily hot breakfast buffet.
