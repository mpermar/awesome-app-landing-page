Description

This property is 1 minute walk from the beach. With a seasonal outdoor pool and a sun terrace, Icona Golden Inn is located in Avalon and a short distance from the beach. Cape May is 17 mi away.

Every room at this resort is air conditioned and is fitted with a flat-screen TV. Certain units feature a seating area as well. Bathrobes and free toiletries are provided.

Icona Golden Inn has an onsite gift shop, a spa center and a hot tub. Guests can also enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar. Free WiFi and private parking is available as well.

The nearest airport is Atlantic City International Airport, 35 mi from Icona Golden Inn.
