Description

Golden Nugget Hotel & Casino offers classic games and live performance shows. Guests can dance at Live Bar nightclub or relax at the outdoor pool. A fitness center with state-of-the art equipment is also provided.

Dine at the Golden Nugget’s Chart House or Vic and Anthony’s, each serving a variety of steak and seafood dishes. Michael Patrick's Brasserie is open 24 hours, and a poolside cafe is available.

A sitting area with a work desk is provided in all rooms at this hotel. A hairdryer is included for guest convenience. Some rooms feature a bay view.

Atlantic City Outlets and the Atlantic City Convention Center are each a 7 minute drive away.
