Description

Lotus Inn is a non-smoking property located in Wildwood Crest. This property features an outdoor heated salt water pool.

The accommodation features a concierge service and ticket service for guests. All units at the hotel are equipped with a seating area.

Rooms include a coffee machine and a private bathroom with free toiletries, while selected rooms also boast a kitchenette fitted with a microwave.

At Lotus Inn, rooms are fitted with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV. A complimentary continental breakfast is served from mid June to Labor day.

The accommodation offers a sun terrace. Guests at Lotus Inn will be able to enjoy activities in and around Wildwood Crest, like cycling.

Bike racks are available at the property. Wildwoods Convention Center is 1. 9 km away, while Morey's Piers is 2.
