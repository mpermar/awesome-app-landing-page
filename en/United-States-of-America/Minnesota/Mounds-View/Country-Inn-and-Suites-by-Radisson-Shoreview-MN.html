Description

Each room at the Shoreview Country Inn is furnished with a work desk and lounge chair. The spacious rooms include a microwave, refrigerator and cable TV.

Guests at the Country Inn Shoreview are provided a free deluxe continental breakfast. A 24-hour convenience store and business center are available for added convenience. A library is also featured on site.

Mall of America located within a 30-minute of the Country Inn and Suites Shoreview. The Science Museum and University of Minnesota are only 20 minutes from the hotel.
