Description

All guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Every room includes a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hair dryer. At Hampton Inn Roseville every room has bed linen and towels.

A continental or buffet breakfast is available each morning at the property.

When guests need guidance on where to visit, the reception will be happy to provide advice.

Minneapolis is 5 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Minneapolis-Saint Paul International Airport, 10 miles from Hampton Inn Minneapolis-Roseville, MN.
