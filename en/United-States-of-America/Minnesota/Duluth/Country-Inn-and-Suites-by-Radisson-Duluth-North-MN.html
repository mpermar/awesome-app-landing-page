Description

A microwave and a refrigerator are included in every room at Country Inn & Suites Duluth North. Ironing facilities and a hairdryer are also provided in each guest room.

Guests can enjoy a workout in the fitness center or relax in the hot tub at Duluth North Country Inn and Suites. The front desk is available 24 hours a day.

A complimentary hot breakfast is served each morning.

The Duluth Entertainment Convention Center and Great Lakes Aquarium are within 8 miles of this hotel. Lake Superior Maritime Museum is a 15-minute drive away.
