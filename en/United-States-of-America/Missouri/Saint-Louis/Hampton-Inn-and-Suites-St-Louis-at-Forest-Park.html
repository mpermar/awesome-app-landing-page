Description

Hampton Inn & Suites St. Louis at Forest Park features an indoor pool and fitness center. After a busy day exploring downtown St. Louis, guests can relax in their room and watch cable TV.

Attractions such as the St. Louis Zoo and the St. Louis Art Museum are only minutes from the St. Louis Hampton Inn. Guests can also explore the city's botanical gardens or see a show at The Muny, one of the country's oldest and largest outdoor theaters.
