Description

Cable TV and a coffee maker are found in each wood-furnished room of the Holiday Inn At Six Flags St. Louis. A work desk and iron are also included.

The Haymarket Restaurant serves American meals in an elegant country setting. Drinks and light food can also be enjoyed at the Pool Terrace Bar.

A whirlpool, sauna and arcade games are all open for guests’ enjoyment inside the Six Flags St. Louis Holiday Inn. A fitness center, putting green and shuffleboard court are also available.

Grant’s Farm is 21 miles from the Holiday Inn. Downtown St. Louis is 30 miles away.
