Description

All guest rooms in the resort are equipped with a TV. All rooms are equipped with a coffee machine, while some have a terrace. Guest rooms include a closet.

An American breakfast is served every morning at the property. At Pointe Royal Condo you'll find a restaurant serving American cuisine.

Spa and wellness facilities including a hot tub and a fitness center are at guests' disposal during their stay at the accommodation. Fishing is among the activities that guests can enjoy near Pointe Royale Condo Vacation and Golf Resort.

There's a business center and guests can use the laundry service.

Titanic Museum is 2.7 miles from the resort. Springfield-Branson Airport is 45 miles from the property.
