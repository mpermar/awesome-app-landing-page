Description

Camden Hotel and Conference Center guest rooms include cable TV. Free Wi-Fi is also available in all rooms.

Free 24-hour coffee and cappuccino is available in the lobby. A deluxe breakfast is available each morning.

The hotel features a 6,000-square-foot convention center with 400 seats available.

Silver Dollar City, an amusement park, is 7 miles from Camden Hotel. It is a short walk to Ride the Ducks. College of the Ozarks is a 10 minute drive from the hotel and the Titanic Museum Attraction is one mile away.

Branson Theatre District is a great choice for travelers interested in food, live music and country music.
