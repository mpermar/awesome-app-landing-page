Description

Living and dining areas are provided in every apartment at Surreys’ Carriage Place. Guests can enjoy the convenience of a washing machine and clothes dryer or relax by the cable TV.

A tennis court is available to all guests of Surreys’ Carriage Place Branson. A game room and hot tub are included for family fun, or a fitness center is provided for guest convenience.

Shopping at 90 Outlets is 1 mile from this non-smoking property. Lake access in Indian Point can be reached in a 20-minute drive.
