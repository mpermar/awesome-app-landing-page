Description

The rooms at this Kansas City Fairfield Inn and Suites include a refrigerator, microwave and free Wi-Fi. A work desk and coffee maker are also provided.

Kansas City Airport Fairfield Inn and Suites hosts an on-site fitness center and indoor pool. A continental breakfast is served daily. Laundry and dry cleaning services are available.

Worlds of Fun Amusement Park is 20 minutes from Fairfield Inn & Suites Kansas City Airport. The Harry S. Truman Library & Museum is 30 minutes from the hotel.
