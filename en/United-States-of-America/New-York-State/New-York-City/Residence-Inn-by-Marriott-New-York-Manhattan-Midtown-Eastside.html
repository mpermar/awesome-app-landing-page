Description

The Residence Inn New York Manhattan/Midtown East has a flat-screen cable TV with HBO channels in every guest room. Decorated in bright colors, the rooms offer a work desk and sitting area. Select rooms have a balcony and sofa bed.

Guests can work out in the fitness center at the Midtown East Residence Inn. Newspapers are available in the lobby and on-site laundry facilities are provided for convenience. Luggage storage services are also offered.

Times Square and Central Park are both a 15-minute walk from the hotel. Rockefeller Center is a 10-minute walk away.

Midtown East is a great choice for travelers interested in food, walking and restaurants.
