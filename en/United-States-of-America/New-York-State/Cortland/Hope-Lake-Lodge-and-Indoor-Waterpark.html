Description

A fireplace, dining area and fully-equipped kitchen are standard in all rooms at the Hope Lake Lodge. Each room includes at least 1 sofa bed.

The resort offers plenty of on-site entertainment, including Cascades Indoor Water park and the outdoor Adventure Center, which offers ziplines and ropes courses. The Waterfalls Spa features a variety of relaxing treatments.

The Lodge Hope Lake offers traditional American cuisine for all meals at the Acorn Grill, and the Edgewater Café is the resort’s convenient snack bar. Drinks and light appetizers are available at the Bobcat Lounge.

Willowbrook Golf Club is a 5.8 mi drive from this resort. The Dowd Art Gallery is 8.1 mi away.
