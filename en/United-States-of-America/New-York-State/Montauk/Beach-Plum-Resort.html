Great Value for High Season in Montauk

"This is a great option for an inexpensive apartment style stay across from the beach and a quick walk into town. The rooms are dated, no headboards and small beds, wall air units but it definitely is a plus to have a room upstairs with a deck overlooking the ocean. You also see the parking lot and highway but it's still nice to see the water. Hotels in Montauk are very expensive in the summer so this is definitely a good value at less than $300 a night for 2 people and a water view this close to town. It is also great for families."
