Description

Each guest room at Driftwood on the Ocean features a patio, cable TV and private bathroom. They are decorated in bright colors and beach décor. Select rooms offer ocean or sand dune views.

Tennis courts and BBQ facilities are available for guests to use at the Driftwood Resort on the Ocean. A games room is also located on site.

Montauk Downs State Park Golf Course is 15 minutes’ drive from the hotel. Hiking and fishing at Camp Hero State Park is 9.3 mi away.
