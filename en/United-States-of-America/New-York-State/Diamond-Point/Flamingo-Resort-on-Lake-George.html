Description

A flat-screen cable TV is featured in each room at Flamingo Resort on Lake George. A refrigerator is provided as well.

Guests can enjoy numerous activities on site, including table tennis, volleyball and badminton. A fishing charter service also departs from the resort. Barbecue facilities, a games room and a children’s playground are available as well.

Lake George Beach State Park is 10 minutes’ drive from Flamingo Resort on Lake George. Six Flags Great Escape indoor waterpark is 14 minutes’ drive.
