Description

A sofa and a TV are featured in each room at Sea Crest. Guests are also provided with a kitchenette, a balcony and a dining area with dining table. The private bathroom includes a hairdryer.

Guests at the resort can use the on-site tennis court for exercise and entertainment, or purchase refreshments from the vending machines. Laundry facilities are available on site.

East Hampton is a 15-minute drive from Sea Crest. Hither Hills is 1.5 miles away.
