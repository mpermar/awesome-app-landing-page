Description

The rooms at the Woodcliff Hotel and Spa contain a flat-screen TV with premium cable. A refrigerator and a minibar are also provided.

On-site dining at the Woodcliff Hotel includes Horizons Restaurant for creative cuisine. Horizons Lounge is where guests can receive a 4-star meal and classic American cuisine with weekend entertainment. Room service is also available at the hotel.

Woodcliff Spa features an indoor and outdoor pool. A gym with equipment including cardio and free weights is also on site. Guests can play golf on the regulation 9-hole golf course located at the hotel.

Seneca Park Zoo is 18 miles from the Woodcliff Spa and Hotel. Geva Theatre Center is 13 miles away.
