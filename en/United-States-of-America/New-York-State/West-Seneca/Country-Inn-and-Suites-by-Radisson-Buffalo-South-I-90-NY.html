Description

A flat-screen TV and a sitting area are featured in the traditional rooms at West Seneca County Inn & Suites. Tea and coffee-making facilities are also included.

Breakfast is served each morning and it features hot coffee or tea along with fresh fruit and pastries.

Guests can work out in the fitness center, relax in the hot tub, or use the business center that offers fax and photocopying services. Laundry facilities are also provided.

Country Inn & Suites – West Seneca is a 15-minute drive from Central Buffalo. Lake Erie is 3 miles away and McKinley Mall is 5 miles away.
