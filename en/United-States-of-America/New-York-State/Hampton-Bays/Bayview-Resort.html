Description

The accommodations will provide you with air conditioning and a seating area. There is a full a kitchenette with a refrigerator. Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with a bath or shower and a hairdryer.

At Bayview Resort, guests will find barbecue facilities, a fire pit, a picnic area and dock. Other facilities offered at the property include grocery deliveries, meeting facilities and a shared lounge. The property offers free parking. A daily continental breakfast is served in July and August.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including golfing, fishing and canoeing. Kayaks, canoes and small row boats are available.

Southampton is 10 mi away from the property, while Tanger Outlet Riverhead is 12 mi away. Ponquogue Beach is 4.7 mi away.
