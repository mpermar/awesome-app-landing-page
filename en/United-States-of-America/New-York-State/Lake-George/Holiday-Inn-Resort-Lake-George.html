Description

Situated off Interstate 87 and set in the heart of the Adirondack Park Preserve, this Holiday Inn Resort Lake George offers indoor and outdoor pools and an on-site restaurant and lounge.

Six Flags Great Escape Amusement Park is 5 miles away. Free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV and a work desk with ample seating area are standard in all the spacious guest accommodations at the Holiday Inn Resort Lake George.

With outdoor basketball courts, shuffleboard, a putting green and a giant chessboard, there is something for everyone at the Holiday Inn Resort Lake George.

The resort features a children's swimming pool and playground, seasonal outdoor big screen movies, and an on-site fitness centre.

Lake George Beach State Park and the Lake George Steamboat Company are 15 minutes’ walk from the hotel.
