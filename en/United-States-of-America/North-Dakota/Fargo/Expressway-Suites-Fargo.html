Description

Free Wi-Fi access and a flat-screen TV with cable are available in all rooms at Expressway Suites Fargo. Rooms are also equipped with a microwave and a refrigerator.

Guests at the Fargo Expressway Suites can relax in the hot tub or work out in the fitness center. The hotel has a movie library, as well as a book and game corner. The hotel offers a light dinner and cocktails on weekday nights.

The West Acres Mall is just a 2 minute drive from Expressway Suites Fargo. The Red River Zoo is 1.5 miles away.
