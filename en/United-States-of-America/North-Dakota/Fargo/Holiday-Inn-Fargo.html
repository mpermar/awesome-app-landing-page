Description

Tea and coffee-making facilities are included in all rooms at the Holiday Inn Fargo. A work desk is also provided in each spacious guest room. Every room includes a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The on-site Shipwreck Bay Waterpark features a large swimming pool and water slides. A fitness center and laundromat are also available on site.

Gallery Restaurant and Café offers a variety of dining options for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Hector International Airport is 5 miles from this hotel. The Fargodome, North Dakota State University and the Fargo Air Museum are within a 10-minute drive from the property.
