Description

A 37-inch flat-screen cable TV is provided in every room at Best Western Plus Kelly Inn and Suites. For convenience, a microwave, fridge, and coffeemaker are also included.

A fitness center is available at Kelly Inn and Suites Best Western Plus. Along with an airport shuttle service, a 24-hour front desk and business center are provided on site.

A daily hot breakfast is served at this hotel, and a manager’s reception includes cocktails and appetizers in the evenings from Monday through Thursday.

Shopping at West Acres Mall is a 10-minute walk away. Hector International Airport can be reached in a 12-minute drive.
