Description

Staybridge Suites Fargo offers rooms with a fully equipped kitchen and free Wi-Fi. A flat-screen cable TV, work desk and coffee maker are included.

Guests of the Fargo Staybridge Suites are welcome to enjoy the indoor pool, sauna and fitness room. A selection of books, movies and games can also be borrowed at the hotel.

A breakfast buffet is served each morning and the on-site Bridgemart offers snacks, drinks and personal items. BBQ facilities are also available at the hotel.

The Staybridge Fargo is 5 minutes from Thunder Road Fun Park and less than 6.5 miles from the Fargo Air Museum.
