Description

Spacious and luxurious, the guest rooms enjoy views of either the mountains or the ocean. A 40-inch flat-screen TV and sofa are included. Private bathrooms have either a shower or bathtub or sunken bath. Room service is available. Free WiFi is provided for 2 hours per stay.

Auberge Discovery Bay Hong Kong is a 15-minute drive from Discovery Bay Golf Club, Sunny Bay Station / Tung Chung Station and Ngong Ping 360. A bus service is provided to and from Hong Kong International Airport, about 25 minutes away by bus.

Guests can arrange day trips with the concierge team, check email at the business center, or rent a meeting room. The front desk operates around the clock.

The on-site Café bord de Mer & Lounge serves an international buffet and a la carte items, and drinks are provided at The Bounty Entertainment & Sports Bar.
