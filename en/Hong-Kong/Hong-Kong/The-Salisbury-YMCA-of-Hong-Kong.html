Description

The Salisbury-YMCA of Hong Kong is conveniently situated within the prime shopping and entertainment district of Tsim Sha Tsui. A 40-minute drive from Hong Kong International Airport, it is also a walk from Nathan Road, the Waterfront Promenade and the historic Clock Tower.

Rooms are bright with large windows. Select rooms offer spectacular views of Victoria Harbour and Hong Kong Island's skyline.

The Salisbury Dining Room serves delicious international buffets, while The Mall Cafe offers a variety of oriental and western dishes. Room service is available.

Guests can use the racquetball courts and the well-equipped fitness center. There are also dance studios and a playground for younger guests. If you're looking to relax, The Salisbury YMCA’s hair salon and hot tub are great options.

Tsim Sha Tsui is a great choice for travelers interested in luxury brand shopping, clothes shopping and shopping.
