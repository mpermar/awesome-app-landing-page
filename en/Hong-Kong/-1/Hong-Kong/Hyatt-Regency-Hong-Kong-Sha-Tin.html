Description

Located next to University MTR subway station, air-conditioned rooms with earth tone furnishings and carpeted floors feature a 42-inch LCD TV and minibar. The private bathroom has a shower, bathrobes and slippers.

Guests can enjoy a range of therapies at the spa or stay in shape with a game of tennis or exercising at the fitness center. Other facilities include a sauna, tour desk and bike rental. Supervised activities for young children are also available at Camp Hyatt. A free shuttle bus service to Hong Kong Science Park, Sha Tin and Tsim Sha Tsui is available.

Dongguan cuisine and Peking Duck can be savored at Sha Tin 18, which features an outdoor terrace. All-day dining is available at Cafe, which serves international and local fare. Other dining options include cocktails at Tin Tin Bar, homemade pastries at Patisserie and snacks at Pool Bar.

Shatin is a great choice for travelers interested in gourmet food, local food and shopping.
