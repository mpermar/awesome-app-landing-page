Description

Mandarin Oriental Hong Kong is in the heart of Central, a major business and shopping district. It is a 40-minute drive from the airport.

Spacious and elegant, rooms at Mandarin Oriental Hong Kong feature clear floor-to-ceiling windows with panoramic city and harbor views. Modern comforts like an iPod dock, flat-screen TV and minibar are included.

Guests can use the steam room, jacuzzi and sauna at the spa. It offers traditional Chinese and Ayurvedic treatments. The fitness center has an indoor heated swimming pool.

Mandarin Grill and Bar serves fresh seafood and western dishes. Cantonese specialties can be sampled at Man Wah. All-day dining is available at Cafe Causette. Mandarin Oriental Hotel also has 2 bars and a cake shop.

Central District is a great choice for travelers interested in nightlife, restaurants and gourmet food.
