Description

The Spiwak Chipichape Cali-Un Hotel Preferred is just a few steps from Chipichape Mall and less than a quarter mile from the Batacian Nature Reserve.

All rooms at the Spiwak have chic décor, air conditioning and a flat-screen cable TV. They have fully-furnished kitchen facilities and some of them include marble bathrooms with spa tubs. Local phone calls are free and there are 10 minutes of free national and international calls.

Guests can enjoy breakfast with world-famous Colombian coffee at the poolside bar and treat themselves to Mediterranean dishes at La ZarZuela Restaurant.
