Description

The Ascot has a hot tub, sauna and a heated indoor swimming pool available for use by all guests. There are also conference facilities. A free shuttle service is provided into the center of town.

Emberz at Ascot restaurant serves gourmet cuisine, and Emberz Bar features a range of beer and wine. Guests can also relax at the Sportmans bar which has a large TV screen.

Ascot Park Hotel supports sustainability, with environmentally-friendly policies around the hotel such as recycling in the rooms, public and staff areas, along with chemical-free cleaning in the hotel rooms.
