Description

Villa Gemella is a large building with 2 wings, with a saltwater swimming pool in the center. The pool has a whirlpool area and a shallow end for children.

Hotel Villa Gemella is in Baia Sardinia, just over a quarter mile from the center of the city, and less than 950 feet from the beach of Porto Sole. Guests receive discounted rates on local restaurants.

All the rooms in Villa Gemella have private bathrooms and are provided with modern comforts. Some of the rooms feature a balcony with incredible sea views.

Park your car in the reserved parking by the English garden, and get ready for a relaxing stay. Start by sipping a drink on the terrace, before finding out about the many excursions and tours that the staff can arrange for you.
