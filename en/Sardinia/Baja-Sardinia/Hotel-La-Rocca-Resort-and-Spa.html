Description

All rooms at the Hotel La Rocca Resort & Spa are air-conditioned and feature a minibar, TV, and a balcony or terrace overlooking the gardens or La Maddalena Archipelago. Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas.

A large buffet breakfast is available at the hotel's elegant restaurant, serving Sardinian, Italian and international specialties for dinner. There is also a beach-side restaurant.

La Rocca is surrounded by olive groves and Mediterranean greenery, and features both indoor and outdoor swimming pools. The free spa is complete with a sauna, Turkish bath, Kneipp path and sensory showers. A relaxation area and a hot bench are also available.

The private beach has free lounge chairs, sun umbrellas, and beach towels. The shuttle also services to the center of Baja Sardinia. The popular seaside town of Porto Cervo is just a 10-minute drive away.
