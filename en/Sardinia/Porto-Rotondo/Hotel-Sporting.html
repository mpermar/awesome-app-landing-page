Description

All rooms feature exposed wood-beamed ceilings, simple, elegant furnishings and a balcony with a view of the grounds, harbor or sea. The bathrooms are in Orosei marble or Sardinian wax. Modern amenities include free Wi-Fi and a satellite TV in every room.

Hotel Sporting is part of Prestige Hotels of the World. There is free internet terminals and babysitting services. The friendly staff can assist you with tourist information and organizing day trips.

The hotel's famous restaurant serves regional specialties and international cooking. The restaurant features a romantic environment with a view of the harbor and the swimming pool.

The historic center of Porto Rotondo is just a 10-minute walk away. Guests at the property are a 30-minute drive from Olbia Airport.
