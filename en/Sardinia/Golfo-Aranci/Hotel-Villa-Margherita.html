Description

Decorated with creamy tones and wicker furnishings, rooms at Hotel Villa Margherita are also equipped with a TV and free Wi-Fi. They are all air-conditioned and include private bathrooms with courtesy bathrobes and slippers.

Fresh seafood and regional Sardinian specialties are served at the hotel restaurant. Guests can also enjoy open-air dining, possible on the outdoor terrace among palm trees and hibiscus flowers.

The hotel also features a well-equipped gym and on-site relaxation facilities, including a hammam and massage treatments. The on-site bike rental service provides guests with the opportunity to discover the fishing port and various white sand beaches.
