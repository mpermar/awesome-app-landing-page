Description

This charming hotel features precious, Mediterranean ceramics and typical Sardinian furnishings. Relax at the outdoor swimming pool, and walk down to the beautiful beach.

The friendly staff at Fiore di Maggio will be delighted to take you on one of their motorboat excursions around the islands of the Parco Geomarino.

Enjoy traditional, local cuisine at lunch and dinner, accompanied by a glass of fine wine. End your meal in style with a special, Sardinian dessert.
