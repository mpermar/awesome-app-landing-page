Description

Each guest room is bright and spacious and features a sunny balcony or porch. Take advantage of the free parking lot and free Wi-Fi at the Sa Muvara Hotel.

You must try the award-winning restaurant for a taste of traditional, regional cuisine. Choose from a mix of meat and fish specialties, hot and cold starters, and a range of Italian and international dishes. The team of chefs use a lot of organic ingredients.

Enjoy your meal on the porch overlooking the private gardens, with a glass of selected local wine.

Explore the surrounding area by foot, mountain bike or with a Jeep excursion organized by the hotel. Canoeing and kayaking on the nearby lakes are also very popular. Nearby you will find tennis and basketball courts and soccer pitches.
