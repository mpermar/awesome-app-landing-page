Description

The panoramic swimming pool and the various shaded spots provide moments of total relaxation. Free access to the open-air fitness center is provided. The architectural features of the Gallura region are reflected in the lay-out of the hotel rooms. Some rooms offer spectacular views of the lake.

Furnishings are in the authentic Sardinian style. Particular care has been paid to the choice of wood and iron work for the furniture and fixtures, textiles and ceramics, all crafted by the finest Sardinian artisans.

Both the restaurant and the rooms are set among the local shrub and flowers, carefully trimmed and illuminated at night to create a beautiful atmosphere.

The Aldiola restaurant provides the best choice of regional and international cuisine, with 2 types of menus that change daily. The finest traditional dishes are enhanced by local ingredients and flavors. The house wine, from the family vineyard, is a fine, fresh red wine. The restaurant also stocks the best known Sardinian labels.

The surrounding area is rich in extraordinary sites, both natural and archaeological. A short distance away, on the shores of the lake, are numerous 1000-year-old olive groves and amazing granite rocks. You can also admire monuments from the Nuragic age.

Aldiola Country Resort is also the ideal starting point for nature walks and more challenging trekking excursions.
