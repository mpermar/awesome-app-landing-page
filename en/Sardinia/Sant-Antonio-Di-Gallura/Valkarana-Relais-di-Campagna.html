Description

Surrounded by the Mediterranean vegetation and wild olive groves, the hotel has its own lush grounds. It features an outdoor swimming pool for adults and children, and private lake access.

Hotel Restaurant Valkarana offers a range of Sardinian specialties, including local meats and excellent wines. Meals are served on panoramic dining rooms and terraces overlooking the lake.

Rooms are spacious and simple, all having independent access and great views. They are decorated with traditional materials such as wood, brick, granite, wrought iron and Sardinian ceramics.

Located in the Gallura region, the hotel is 19 miles from Olbia's airport and harbor. The village of Sant'Antonio di Gallura is a 5-minute drive away, while Arzachena is 9 miles away.

06/11/2017

Great retreat kind of place

"The place is super picturesque. Very beautiful estate by the mountain with sweeping views of the lake. Getting there is a fun drive and there is plenty of uncovered parking. Dinner was great and cost about 20 euros for a main, wine and dessert. The included breakfast was Ok, not much variety but fresh coffee from the espresso machine (although its supposed to be limited to 1). The room was huge and with aircon. There are 2 pools, one big and one for kids and there are sunbathing chairs with a special cover that you can use to protect your face from the sun. The staff provided good service. Overall a very pleasant stay, perfect for disconnecting from the world and just enjoying the views, pool, lake and food while reading books or spending time with a loved one."
