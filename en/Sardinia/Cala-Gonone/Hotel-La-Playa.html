Description

All rooms are decorated in a classic style with wood furniture. Each room has a TV and a work desk. Some rooms have a balcony. The private bathrooms provide free toiletries.

Hotel La Playa serves a buffet breakfast with both sweet and savory items. The hotel’s restaurant specializes in traditional Sardinian recipes. Vegetarian and gluten-free options are available upon request.

This family-run hotel is air-conditioned and provides free Wi-Fi in all public areas of the property. On request, it also organizes various activities, including hiking, snorkeling and horseback riding. There is a diving center at the hotel which offers diving courses for all levels of experience.

Olbia Costa Smeralda Airport is within a 1.5-hour drive of the hotel. The city of Nuoro is reachable in 45 minutes by car.
