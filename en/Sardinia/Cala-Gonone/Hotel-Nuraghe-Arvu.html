Description

In whitewashed buildings with arches decorated with flowers, the air-conditioned, rustic-style rooms have a private entrance from the garden. Superior rooms have wood ceilings and the suites include a separate lounge.

Serving Sardinian cuisine, the restaurant has exposed wood features and opens onto a covered terrace overlooking the pool. A bar, room service and packed lunches are also available.

The multilingual 24-hour front desk can provide tourist information, bike rental and car rental. There is a children’s playground, game room and free Wi-Fi in public areas.

Nuraghe Arvu Hotel is a 45-minute drive from the walk to the Bronze Age nuraghe settlement at Tiscali. There is free parking on site and a shuttle is available to Olbia Airport, one mile away.
