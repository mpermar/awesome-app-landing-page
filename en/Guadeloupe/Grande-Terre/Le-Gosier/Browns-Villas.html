Description

All units include a private bathroom and have air conditioning, a TV and a microwave. A fridge and coffee machine are also featured.

The resort village has a grill.

Guests can also relax in the garden or on the sun terrace.

Sainte-Anne is 5 miles from Brown's Villas. The nearest airport is Pointe-à-Pitre Le Raizet Airport, 6 miles from the accommodation.
