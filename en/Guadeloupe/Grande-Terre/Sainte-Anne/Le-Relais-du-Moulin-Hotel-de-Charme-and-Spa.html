Description

Offering a year-round outdoor pool and views of the garden, Le Relais du Moulin is set in Sainte-Anne.

Guests can enjoy the on-site bar and free private parking is available on site. Certain units include a seating area to relax in after a busy day, a terrace or balcony.

Rooms come with a private bathroom, free toiletries and a hair dryer. A flat-screen TV is featured.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. Le Relais du Moulin features free WiFi throughout the property.

You can play tennis and table tennis at this hotel, and bike hire is available. The hotel also offers car hire.

Le Gosier is 17 km from Le Relais du Moulin, while Saint-François is 7 km from the property.
