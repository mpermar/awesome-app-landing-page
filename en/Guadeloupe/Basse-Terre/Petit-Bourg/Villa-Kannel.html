Description

The rooms come with a dishwasher, oven, a coffee machine, a shower, a hair dryer and a desk. Every room is equipped with a electric tea pot and a private bathroom, while some rooms come with a kitchen. Guest rooms will provide guests with a microwave.

A continental breakfast is served daily at the property.

Le Gosier is 11 miles from the guesthouse, while Sainte-Anne is 18 miles from the property. Pointe-à-Pitre Le Raizet Airport is 10 miles away.
