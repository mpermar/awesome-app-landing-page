Description

This tropical hotel offers units with a private patio or balcony overlooking the gardens.

The hotel is 5 minutes’ walk from the Grande Anse Beach. Studios are uniquely decorated with tropical decor and are equipped with a small kitchenette at the Habitation Grande Anse.

The studios also have a microwave and refrigerator. A daily breakfast is available all year round, and the on-site restaurant opens for lunch and dinner from October to May.

The hotel also has an outdoor pool. Habitation Grande is 11 km from the Botanical Garden.
