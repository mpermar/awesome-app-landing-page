Description

The resort village offers a seating area with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a hair dryer and shower. A microwave, a toaster and fridge are also provided, as well as a coffee machine and a electric tea pot.

Le Bougainvillier Blanc has a year-round outdoor pool.

A grill is available for guests at the accommodation to use.

Le Gosier is 20 miles from Le Bougainvillier Blanc, while Sainte-Anne is 27 miles away. The nearest airport is Pointe-à-Pitre Le Raizet Airport, 17 miles from the resort village.
