Description

The hotel offers luxurious designer rooms with trendy décor and private bathrooms with a bathtub/shower. Free tea and coffee facilities are provided in each room. Bicycle rental is also offered.

The WestCord Fashion features an indoor swimming pool and a modern gym equipped with cardio equipment and weights. These facilities are free of charge for guests at the the hotel. There's also a relaxing sauna available for an extra charge.

Fashion’s Restaurant and bar serves delicious gourmet specialties or light snacks for dinner. The chic SKYY Bar offers panoramic city views and serves a range of wines and exotic cocktails.

A 10-minute tram ride will take guests to the center of the city. The RAI convention center and Schiphol Airport are a 10-minute car drive away.

12/16/2017

The WestCord Fashion Hotel looks like many of the...

"The WestCord Fashion Hotel looks like many of the surrounding modern, office-style buildings surrounding it, but inside, it is clearly an upmarket hotel, and extremely well presented. The staff were extremely courteous and friendly, and always helpful. The food was excellent, and among the best I had whilst on my stay in Amsterdam. The breakfast options were considerable, and unlike many buffets, always piping hot and warm. The mushroom risotto, with chanterelles was truly excellent. I can also heartily recommend the black chicken. Both the bar and restaurant served fresh, local, seasonal food. The double room I had was nothing less than sumptuous. The bed was huge, and the en suite with both bath and shower was exquisite. I made use of the safe, and was always happy to return to the room to read, work, or just relax. I did make use of the pool and spa, which I found very pleasant. Children's swimming lessons are given in the pool, but can be avoided by taking note of the times. Otherwise, I often had the pool to myself and enjoyed it immensely. I also worked in the bar and lounge area regularly, and made full use of the free Wi-Fi. Overall, a great hotel. Perhaps more suited to corporate stays, but it has enough personality and facilities to more than cater for tourists too. Tram and transport options are also close by, with a 10-minute tram ride putting you in the centre of Amsterdam."
