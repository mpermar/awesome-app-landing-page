Description

Sofitel Legend The Grand Amsterdam offers 177 well-kept rooms and suites with modern bathrooms and a rain shower. The 52 luxury suites include butler service. Dressing gowns and slippers are provided for extra comfort. Free WiFi is available, there is also high speed wireless internet available for an extra charge.

Their Michelin star Bridges restaurant focuses on seafood created by executive Chef Andrès Delpeut. You can complement each dish with quality wines, carefully selected by Wine Director Lotte Wolf. For an all-day menu with worldly influences you can visit Bridges BarBistro. Additionally, you can enjoy a coffee or a cocktail at the lively bar in the middle of Bridges BarBistro or at the Dutch brown café The Flying Dutchman. In Library ‘Or’ you can enjoy The Grand Afternoon Tea.

Besides the excellent food & beverage facilities, The Grand holds several historical meeting- and event facilities and an unwinding SoSPA with heated indoor pool, sauna, hammam and fitness area in which after a busy day you can regain energy. There is also a charming garden and terrace for sunny days.

From The Grand most important landmarks are within walking distance, including the Van Gogh Museum, P.C Hooftstraat, the Anne Frank House and Amsterdam Central Station. Trams stop at Dam Square and offer easy access to the entire city.

Amsterdam City Center is a great choice for travelers interested in nightlife, architecture and walking.
