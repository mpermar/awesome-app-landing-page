Description

Each room is decorated with warm colors, modern furnishings and a work desk. They have a private bathroom with free toiletries.

Guests can choose from various activities such as bowling, tennis or a work-out in the fitness room. There is an extensive playground available for children as well.

Hotel Zuiderduin also houses a restaurant which serves international cuisine for breakfast, lunch and dinner. The Time Out bar offers drinks and light snacks through the day.

From Zuiderduin Hotel is only 950 feet from Egmond aan Zee’s shopping street. The Sluispolder golf courses are 6 miles away. Bicycle rental is also available at the hotel.
