Description

Styled in keeping with the local architecture, Korina Gallery Hotel’s rooms feature carefully chosen furniture by famous Italian designers and Lay-Z-Boy mattresses. All rooms are decorated with paintings from famous European artists. Free WiFi and LCD satellite TV are standard.

A combination of Greek and Continental breakfast is served in the elegant dining room, offering view to Vathi port. Korina Gallery features a stone-built bar.

Korina Gallery Hotel’s guests enjoy flexible timetable for breakfast which consists of locally produced pies, jam and cake. The hotel’s personalized services include recommendations for restaurants and boat excursion arrangements.

The center of Vathi, featuring restaurants and bars, the marina and museums, is 5 minutes’ walk from the Korina Gallery. Tour services can be provided upon charge. The nearest blue-flag beach is at 1.2 mi distance.
