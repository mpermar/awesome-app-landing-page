Description

The spacious rooms and suites at Anastazia Hotel have sea-view balconies and tasteful décor. Each features a living room and a bathroom with hairdryer. A safe and satellite TV are also included.

Breakfast is served each morning. Drinks and snacks can be ordered at the main bar.
