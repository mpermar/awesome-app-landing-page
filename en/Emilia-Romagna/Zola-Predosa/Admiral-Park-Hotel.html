Description

The Admiral is approximately 15 minute drive from either the center of the city, the exhibition district, the central train station and Bologna International Airport.

The hotel offers a conference center with different types of rooms (capacity up to 300 people) all air-conditioned and equipped with modern technologies.

Free shuttle bus service to and from the fair district in Bologna during major exhibitions.
