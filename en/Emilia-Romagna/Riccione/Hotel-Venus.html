Description

At the hotel, every room comes with a balcony.

A continental or buffet breakfast is available daily at the property. There's an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of Italian dishes.

Hotel Venus has a playground. You can play ping pong at the accommodation.

Staff at reception can provide assistance around the clock with advice on the area.
