Description

An LCD TV with satellite channels comes standard in the Fantasy’s rooms. All have Adriatic-Sea or garden views.

A daily buffet breakfast is provided. The restaurant serves local cuisine for lunch and dinner.

Free bikes are available. Guests receive a discount on admission at a nearby private beach.

Riccione Station is just under 1 miles from the hotel. The Aquafan theme park is a 10-minute drive away.
