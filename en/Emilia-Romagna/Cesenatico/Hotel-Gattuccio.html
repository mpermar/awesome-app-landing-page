Description

At the hotel, rooms are equipped with a balcony. All rooms come with a private bathroom with a bidet and free toiletries. All guest rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Breakfast is available every morning, and includes buffet and Italian options. There's an in-house restaurant, which specializes in Italian cuisine.

Hotel Gattuccio has an outdoor pool. You can play ping pong at the accommodation.

Pantani Space is 2.1 miles from Hotel Gattuccio. The nearest airport is Federico Fellini Airport, 15 miles from the hotel.
