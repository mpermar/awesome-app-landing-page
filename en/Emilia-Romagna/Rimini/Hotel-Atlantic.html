Description

At the hotel, rooms contain a desk. With a private bathroom equipped with a bidet and a hair dryer, certain rooms at Hotel Atlantic also feature a sea view. At the accommodation, all rooms are equipped with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV.

A buffet breakfast is available daily at Hotel Atlantic.

The hotel has a terrace. Guests at Hotel Atlantic will be able to enjoy activities in and around Rimini, like cycling.

Rimini Stadium is 3.6 miles from the accommodation. Federico Fellini Airport is 7 miles away.

Viserbella is a great choice for travelers interested in kid-friendly beaches, beach walks and the oceanside.
