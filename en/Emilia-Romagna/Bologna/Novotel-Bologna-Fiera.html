Description

Rooms at Novotel come with air conditioning, a coffee machine, and flat-screen TV. All local phone calls are free.

Brunello Restaurant offers a healthy, well-balanced menu, as well as a great selection of wines. It is open daily until midnight. Room service is available from 06:30 to midnight.

This 4-star hotel is set in a business district 1.2 mi from the historic city center, and less than a 10-minute drive from FICO Eataly World.
