Description

Guests at Kings Manor can use the Fountain Spa Leisure Club, which has a sauna, a steam room and a beauty therapy suite. There is also a spa bathtub and a tennis court to enjoy.

Best Western Kings Manor has spacious rooms with private bathrooms, featuring satellite TV and 24-hour room service. Rooms also have a hospitality tray for tea and coffee.

Kings Manor is dedicated to running an environmentally responsible business and promotes eco-friendly tourism.

Lauders Restaurant has a traditional menu, and there is a bar and an informal bistro. In the morning, both full English and continental breakfasts are available.

With free parking on site, the hotel is a 15 minute drive from the city center. There is also good access to the city center from the bus stop outside the hotel.

Portobello is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, scenery and sightseeing.
