Description

All guest rooms in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV. The Key Highland Izu provides some accommodations that have a balcony, and every room is equipped with a electric tea pot. Guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

An Asian breakfast is served every morning at the property.

The accommodation has a terrace.

Speaking English and Japanese at the 24-hour front desk, staff are always at hand to help.

Izu Shaboten Park is 0.6 miles from The Key Highland Izu, while Akazawa Onsen Day Spa is 2.9 miles from the property. Shizuoka Airport is 53 miles away, and the property offers a free shuttle service.
