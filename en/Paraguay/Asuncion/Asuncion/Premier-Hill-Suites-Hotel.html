Description

Situated on one of the Seven Hills of Asuncion, the Premier Hill Hotel offers stylish accommodation in the heart of the city.

There is free Wi-Fi and a garden pool. Guest rooms are elegantly furnished in precious woods and are equipped with kitchenette, iron, hairdryer, and safe box.

Some rooms have balconies with views across Asuncion. A breakfast is available each morning, and the hotel’s La Colina restaurant is one of the most elegant dining destinations in the whole city.

Premier Hill Suites Hotel features a Spa & Wellness Centre, a swimming pool and a hammam.

Guests can also tour the magnificent wine cellars which are only open to those staying in the hotel.

Located close to both the commercial and cultural centers, it is an ideal hotel for business people and tourists alike.
