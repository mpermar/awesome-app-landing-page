Description

With its European Style, Hotel Westfalenhaus offers comfortable facilities in Asunción.

Guests staying here can enoy a swimming pool, spa, restaurant and meeting facilities.

The property features views of the garden and free WiFi access. All air-conditioned rooms have cable TV, private bathroom facilities and a hairdryer.

Some feature a kitchenette and a balcony as well. The in-house Piroschka restaurant serves local and international dishes.

Snacks, soft drinks and spirits are available at the bar. Guests can use a gym and a sauna.

For sightseeing and short trips, the hotel has a tour desk. Free parking is also provided.

Westfalenhaus is close to a golf course and hiking routes. Other points of interest nearby include the Maká Indian Reservation, Conmebol Museum and the Zoo.

Located 8 km from Silvio Pettirossi Airport, the hotel is less than 10 km from central Asunción.
