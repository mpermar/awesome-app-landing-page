Description

This 4-star hotel features 29 carefully restored guest rooms with antique furniture and spa bathtubs. All units have satellite TVs, free internet connection and safes.

A full English breakfast is served in the pretty breakfast room.

There are numerous bars, restaurants and shops within easy reach of the hotel. The Venetian port and castle, as well as the sandy beach are just a short walk away.
