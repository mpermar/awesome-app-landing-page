Description

The accommodation features views of the sea, the garden or the pool and is equipped with air conditioning, a minibar, a flat-screen cable TV, a seating area and a desk. Some units also include a balcony and all have a private bathroom with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

The establishment offers 3 restaurants with regional and international menus and 3 bars where drinks and snacks are served. The room rates include meals, snacks and drinks with and without alcohol.

The complex has entertainment staff, room service, free private parking and playground. In addition, water sports, volleyball and adventure activities can be practiced. The reception is open 24 hours a day.

The nearest airport is Maceio / Zumbi dos Palmares International Airport, 133 km from the complex.
