Description

Rooms at the Best Western Premier Maceió are modern in style and feature 40-inch LED TVs with cable channels, minibars, and air conditioning. Some have a large balcony with panoramic sea views.

The Best Western Premier Maceió serves a rich buffet breakfast. The hotel houses the restaurant Le Premier, which prepares delicious international dishes with fresh ingredients from the area.

The Best Western Premier Maceió is a short distance from the center of Maceió. It offers parking for a fee, depending on availability.
