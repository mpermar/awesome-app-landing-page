Description

The Ritz Lagoa Da Anta offers spacious rooms with a thematic decoration with tiled floors. All rooms have a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a minibar and a private bathroom with a hairdryer. Some rooms have four-poster beds, and others have sea views.

The new Le Sururu is now the main restaurant of the Ritz Lagoa da Anta Hotel & SPA and serves French and Brazilian specialties. The dinner menu offers a variety of appetizers, main courses and desserts. The Solarium Deck Bar serves exotic cocktails and offers ocean views. Damascus restaurant offers Arabic specialties.

The Ritz Spa provides a wide variety of treatments, including Ayurvedic Indian massages. The Ritz Lagoa also has a large lagoon pool surrounded by sun loungers and a volleyball court. The hotel staff can also help guests rent cars and bicycles.

Zumbi dos Palmares International Airport is a 30-minute drive from Ritz Lagoa Da Anta.
