Description

The rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Each room comes with a electric tea pot, while selected rooms also feature a kitchenette with a microwave. All guest rooms have a desk.

A buffet or Italian breakfast is served at the property.

Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near Hotel Palmensis.

San Benedetto del Tronto is 14 miles from the accommodation, while Ascoli Piceno is 24 miles from the property. Falconara Airport is 39 miles away.
