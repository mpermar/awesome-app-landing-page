Description

Park Plaza guest rooms are spacious and feature a sitting area, cable TV with a DVD player and free Wi-Fi. Most rooms also have a gas fireplace.

Resort Park Plaza features an indoor pool with hot tub, a gym and a sauna. Guests can use the game room which includes a pool table and arcade games as well as the outdoor BBQ facilities.

Historic Main Street and Olympic Park are a 5 minute drive from Park Plaza Resort. Outdoor activities include skiing and dog sledding in the winter and hiking and kayaking in the summer.
