Description

Each room at the Holiday Inn Express Hotel & Suites Moab features a flat-screen TV, a desk and coffee making facilities. Select suites have a microwave, a refrigerator, and a sofa bed.

Guests can use the copy and fax services at the on-site business centre. The Moab Holiday Inn Express has a 24-hour front desk and offers laundry facilities. An outdoor swimming pool with 2 hot tubs, a fire pit and a waterfall are also provided.

A full hot breakfast is served to guests daily.

White-water rafting tours along the Colorado River are 2 miles away. Moab town centre is 5 minutes’ drive from the hotel. It is 5 minutes’ drive from Arches National Park.
