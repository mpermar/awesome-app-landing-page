Description

Tea and coffee-making facilities are featured in all rooms at Saint George Inn and Suites. A fridge and microwave are included. Guests can relax in the comfortably furnished rooms complete with cable TV.

A 24-hour front desk is offered at Inn and Suites Saint George. A business center complete with fax and photocopying services is available. A laundromat and vending machines are on site.

Saint George Motocross Track is a 6 minute drive from this hotel. Zion National Park is 43 miles away.

Policies

Children and Extra Beds
All children are welcome.
Free!Up to two children under 17 years stay free of charge when using existing beds.
Any additional older children or adults are charged USD 5 per person per night when using existing beds.
One older child or adult is charged USD 10 per person per night in an extra bed.
Free!One child under 2 years stays free of charge in a crib.
The maximum number of extra beds/cribs in a room is 1.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
