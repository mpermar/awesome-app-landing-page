Description

Facilities include an indoor pool, a tennis court and a fitness center. Hotel Villa Michelon's restaurant serves regional dishes. There are also meeting facilities, entertainment staff and shared lounge.

The rooms feature traditional and cozy décor, TV, minibar, private bathroom with shower, hairdryer and free toiletries, plus CD player and satellite channels.

The hotel is 15 km from the Fundaparque convention center.
