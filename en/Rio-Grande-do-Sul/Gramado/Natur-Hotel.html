Description

The Natur Hotel features a balcony or patio with views of the garden, TV, telephone, safe and modern bathroom with glass shower cubicle and granite countertop.

The famous St. Peter's Church and the Rua Coberta are 700 meters away and the Gramado bus station is 1.5 km from the Natur Hotel.
