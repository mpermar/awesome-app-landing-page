Description

The rooms at the Hotel Recanto da Serra are spacious and have wooden floors. They also have a minibar, telephone and satellite TV. In addition, there is air conditioning and heating.

The hotel serves a delicious buffet breakfast that includes fresh fruit and artisan pastries, as well as a variety of cheeses and sausages. For its part, the Queen's pub offers snacks, exotic drinks and wines.

This hotel is located 400 meters from the Palácio dos Festivais Convention Center and just a 2-minute drive from the Gramado bus station.

The property offers a games room, a playground and a bicycle rental service (depending on availability). Santa Claus Village, a replica of the town of Santa Claus, is located 400 meters away. In addition, it is close to the Joaquina Rita Bier lake and the popular Coberta street.
