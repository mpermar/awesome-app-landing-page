Description

At the Four Seasons Resort Nevis, the rooms and houses are air-conditioned and have TVs with DVD players and telephone lines. The private bathrooms are marbled and include a hairdryer. Some of the villas and homes feature a private pool, plus views of the sea or mountains.

The Four Seasons has 4 on-site restaurants that offer everything from breakfast buffets to Caribbean and Mediterranean cuisine. There is also a library bar. Other types of cuisines available include seafood, West Indian fusion and steakhouse. This property has earned the AAA Five Diamond Award.

Spa services include massages and rejuvenating body treatments. There are also salon services available. Other activities available at the Four Seasons are sailing courses, hiking and diving.

Charlestown is 2 miles from the Four Seasons Resort Nevis, and the Nevis Vance Amory International Airport is 5 miles away.
