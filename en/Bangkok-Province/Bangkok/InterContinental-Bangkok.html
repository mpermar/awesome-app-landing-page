Description

InterContinental Bangkok is a short 5 minute walk from shopping at Central World Plaza and Gaysorn Plaza. Suvarnabhumi Airport is 14 miles from the hotel.

Stylishly decorated, the spacious air-conditioned rooms at InterContinental have large double-glazed windows overlooking the city. Each comes with a flat-screen cable/satellite TV and DVD player.

Offering a bird’s eye view of Bangkok, the 24-hour Infinity Fitness features a well-equipped gym and yoga classes. After a refreshing workout, guests can relax with a massage or facial at Spa InterContinental.

The hotel’s restaurants serve a delicious selection of international, Western, Italian and Chinese dishes. Champagne and cigars can be enjoyed at the hotel’s 2 bars.

Pathumwan is a great choice for travelers interested in clothes shopping, shopping and food.
