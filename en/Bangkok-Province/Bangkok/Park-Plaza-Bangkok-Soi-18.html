Description

The elegant rooms at Park Plaza Sukhumvit 18 come with modern décor. Each well-appointed room is fitted with a DVD player and mini-bar. Hot shower facilities are in the private bathroom.

Guests can work out at the rooftop fitness center and bask in the warmth of the sunshine on one of the many poolside sun loungers. The hotel provides car rental services and free parking. Laundry services are also available upon request.

Mezza Restaurant serves Thai and international dishes. Light snacks and refreshing beverages can be enjoyed by the pool at the rooftop lounge outlet, Sway.

Park Plaza Bangkok Sukhumvit 18 is a 5-minute walk from Sukhumvit MRT Station and Asok BTS Station and a 10-minute walk from Terminal 21 shopping mall. Suvarnabhumi International Airport is 22 mi from the hotel.

Khlong Toei is a great choice for travelers interested in nightlife, clothes shopping and shopping.
