Description

Sivatel Bangkok is a 5-minute walk from Ploenchit BTS Skytrain Station and less than just over half a mile from shopping at Central World Plaza and Pratunam Market. Suvarnabhumi International Airport is 25 miles from the hotel.

The modern rooms include a 40-inch flat-screen TV, iPod dock and Apple TV systems. A minibar and personal safe are also included. Spacious bathrooms feature a bathtub and floor-to-ceiling windows with beautiful city views.

The Porch offers international cuisine and fine wine in an elegant setting. Other dining options include coffee at Cafe Jardin.

Recreation choices include working out at the fitness center, or enjoying a massage at the spa. The hotel also provides a business center, tour desk and concierge services.

Pathumwan is a great choice for travelers interested in clothes shopping, shopping and food.
