Description

Decorated in warm colors and hardwood floors, the modern suites at Urbana Sathorn, Bangkok feature full-length glass windows overlooking Bangkok City. Each comes with a flat-screen TV, DVD player and personal safe.

The hotel is a 10 minute drive from the scenic Chao Phraya River and about 14 miles from Suvarnabhumi Airport.

Guests can enjoy a refreshing workout at the gym. Urbana Sathorn, Bangkok also houses a business center and provides babysitting services.

Amontre Restaurant is available for all-day dining, and serves a wide spread of Western and Asia dishes. Outdoor and indoor dining options are available.

Sathorn is a great choice for travelers interested in temples, culture and sightseeing.
