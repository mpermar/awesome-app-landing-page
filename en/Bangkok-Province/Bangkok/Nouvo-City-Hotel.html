Description

Decorated in warm colors and featuring modern artwork, the air-conditioned rooms come with glass-walled bathrooms and wooden flooring. Each room is equipped with a flat-screen TV, mini-bar and tea/coffee making facilities. Selected room types come with views of the canal.

The halal Nouvo City Hotel is a 15-minute walk from popular Khaosan Road and heritage sites like the Grand Palace and Wat Phra Kaew. Suvarnabhumi Airport is about 17 mi from the hotel.

Guests may relax with a massage at Sala Spa, or exercise at the fitness center. Day trips around Thailand can be easily arranged at the tour desk, which also offers ticketing services.

Sara Restaurant opens daily from 06:00 - 22:00 hrs and provides an a la carte menu, while Canal Restaurant offers a breakfast and lunch buffet selection. Room service is also available 24-hours.
