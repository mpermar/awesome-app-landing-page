Description

Guest rooms at Sukosol Hotel are elegantly furnished with Thai décor accents and Art Nouveau-style teakwood. They all include a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a seating area and private bathrooms.

An array of diverse gourmet cuisines is on offer thanks to the 5 restaurants of the hotel. The restaurants, the lobby and the public areas are all fitted with antiques, artifacts and curios from around Asia and across the globe.

The hotel offers free drop-off service to Phaya Thai Airport Rail Link Station, providing quick links to Suvarnabhumi Airport. The lively Pratunam Market is 1 block away. Free private parking is available on site.

Pathumwan is a great choice for travelers interested in clothes shopping, shopping and food.

Hotel is not known to most taxi that we asked, the room was lovely and spacious.

"Friendly staff, we received free presidential suite upgrade because we had 3 kids. Kids stayed free, with free breakfast. Hotel staff kindly asked how many beds we would like in our room to accommodate our three kids and they rearranged the room accordingly. Room is very nice and spacious despite the extra beds. Location is 5 - 10 min walk to a small shopping centre (Century Mall), the ground floor has an super cheap food court serving local cuisines. Then there is a few nice restaurants to choose from on the other upper floors. Along the side street of Century Mall is also a few local restaurants worth trying. Pool area was clean and kids had a great time there. The hotel itself is not very well known to the locals. A lot of taxi would not take us as they had no idea where it was, so make sure you take their business card with their address with you. A chukchuk from the Grand Palace to the hotel is much more expensive than taking a taxi (double the price)."
