Description

Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and is equipped with a TV. Certain rooms feature a sitting area for your convenience. Enjoy a cup of tea from your terrace or balcony. For your comfort, you will find slippers, free toiletries and a hairdryer. Superior rooms have a spa tub or a hot tub.
