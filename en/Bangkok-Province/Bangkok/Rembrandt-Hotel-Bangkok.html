Description

Rembrandt Hotel Bangkok is a 5-minute drive from Queen Sirikit National Convention Center and a 45-minute drive from Suvarnabhumi International Airport.

Boasting stunning city views from floor-to-ceiling windows, modern rooms come with a 32-inch flat-screen cable TV and a laptop safe. Spacious bathrooms have all-natural toiletries and a hairdryer.

Guests can indulge with body treatments at the spa or maintain workouts at the well-equipped fitness center. Staff at Rembrandt Bangkok can help with travel arrangements and provide concierge service.

Dining highlights include hearty buffet breakfasts at The Flavors and fine Indian dining at award-winning Rang Mahal. Other dining options include Thai, Mexican and Italian fare.

Khlong Toei is a great choice for travelers interested in nightlife, clothes shopping and shopping.
