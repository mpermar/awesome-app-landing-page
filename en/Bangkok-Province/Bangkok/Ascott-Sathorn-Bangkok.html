Description

Tastefully furnished, the spacious apartments at Ascott feature full kitchen facilities and a comfortable living room. Each is equipped with a washing machine, satellite/cable TV and DVD player.

Guests can exercise at the fitness center or enjoy a relaxing massage at the hotel’s spa. The hotel also provides car rentals and laundry services.

Ascott Sathorn’s restaurant serves a variety of local and international dishes. The hotel’s bar provides a casual setting to enjoy light snacks and cocktails. For guests who drive, parking is provided for free.

Ascott Sathorn Bangkok is a 45-minute drive from Suvarnabhumi Airport. Attractions like Bangkok Chinatown and MBK Shopping Mall are less than 1.9 mi from the hotel. BNH Hospital is also a short drive from the property.

Sathorn is a great choice for travelers interested in temples, culture and sightseeing.
