Description

Mercure Bangkok Siam is within a 5 minute walk from MBK and Siam Shopping Centres as well as Bangkok Art and Culture Center. Its is a 20 minute drive from Hua Lamphong Train Station and a 45 minute ride from Suvarnabhumi International Airport.

Each non-smoking room is tastefully decorated and fitted with a minibar, a safe and ironing facilities. There is also a fridge and a cable flat-screen TV. Private bathrooms come stocked with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

With a 24-hour front desk, additional services and facilities available include a tour desk, luggage storage and room service. Facilities for disabled guests are available as well.

All day dining restaurant features an extensive menu of local and international cuisines. On the 9th floor, Mercure Lobby Bar features a cozy lounge and a terrace overlooking the vibrant city of Bangkok. Guests can enjoy a selection of international wine, tea and coffee menus as well as light snacks here.

Phaya Thai is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, clothes shopping and food.
