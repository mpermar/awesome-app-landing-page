Description

Located not far from a vibrant nightlife of Bangkok, Radisson Blu Plaza Bangkok is 2.2 mi from Patpong and 4.8 mi from Khaosan Road. The property is 13 mi from Suvarnabhumi Airport.

Featuring air-conditioning, each unit offers a seating area with a safety deposit box. The property provides an array of entertainment including a flat-screen cable TV and an iPod docking station. Its private bathroom is equipped with a bathtub, a shower and a hairdryer. Free toiletries and bathrobes are offered in each unit.

With its modern designs and welcoming atmosphere, Twenty-Seven Bites Brasserie offers fresh buffet, serving Western and Eastern cuisine. Located on the 28th floor with spectacular views of Bangkok skyline, Attico restaurant provides all guests with authentic and delectable Italian dishes. Guests can also indulge in Chinese cuisine, ranging from bite-size options to à la carte dishes, at China Table restaurant.

Convenient with a 24-hr front desk and room service, the property offers free luggage and storage service. Meeting facilities are offered at the property along with a valet parking service. A laundry and babysitting are available at the hotel.

Wattana is a great choice for travelers interested in nightlife, clothes shopping and shopping.
