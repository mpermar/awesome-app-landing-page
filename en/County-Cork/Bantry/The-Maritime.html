Description

On the water’s edge, The Maritime Hotel enjoys uninterrupted sea views out over Bantry Bay. At the Maritime Hotel, everything you desire for a short break or extended stay awaits you.

You can enjoy a pampering, relaxing vacation or host an elegant event. The facilities available at the Club Maritime leisure center are chic by any standards. Club Maritime features a large swimming pool and a children's pool, sauna, steam room, hot tub and gym.
