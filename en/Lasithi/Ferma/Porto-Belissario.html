Description

All of the guest rooms in Hotel Porto Belissario have air conditioning, telephone and satellite TV. The rooms are equipped with traditional Cretan furniture and each has its own private bathroom. Some rooms include access to a private yard.

The hotel’s restaurant serves a full menu of traditional Cretan cuisine. You can have a drink in the bar or relax in the landscaped grounds.

Guests can sit by the pool area which overlooks the Lybean Sea, and the beach is just a short walk away.

Hotel Belissario is just 6 miles from the ancient town of Ierapetra and less than 50 miles from Heraklion International Airport. Free parking is available on site.
