Description

With wooden furniture and classic décor, rooms at Villa Balbi have an elegant feel. Some overlook the grounds, others the historic center of town, and some offer sea views. They provide free Wi-Fi.

Public spaces feature antique furnishings, marble and elegant chandeliers. Grand Hotel Villa Balbi includes a 17th-century villa that was home to aristocratic families.

Regional cooking, seafood specialties and homemade desserts are served at Il Parco restaurant. During the summer months, it is possible to dine on the grounds.

The hotel is in the center of Sestri Levante. Genoa and La Spezia are about a 45 minute drive away.
