Description

This 3-star hotel has been completely renovated and offers elegant rooms that come with refined hardwood floors and furniture. Each one is air-conditioned and features free Wi-Fi, tea and coffee-making facilities, plus a 32” LCD TV with satellite and Mediaset Premium channels.

The Hotel Metropoli features a welcoming lobby with an interesting collection of contemporary Italian art and a free internet point. The staff is available 24 hours a day and can recommend sights and restaurants nearby. It serves a rich breakfast buffet including homemade jams and pastries.

The hotel is just under a quarter of a mile from De Ferrari Metro Station and from the Doge’s Palace. Brignole Station is just over half a mile away.

Genoa Historical Centre is a great choice for travelers interested in museums, food and history.
