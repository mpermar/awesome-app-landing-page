Description

Guests can practice rock climbing. The hotel also has a spa with massage service, beauty salon, boutique and concierge service.

The rooms at La Torre are colorful and include air conditioning, a balcony, cable TV, a telephone, a minibar and a private bathroom with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

The Nativa Clube Praia kiosk, located in front of the beach, offers sun loungers, umbrellas and sun beds, among other beach services.

La Torre is 15 km from Porto Seguro Airport and 13.6 km from the city center. It also provides car rental, free parking and sun loungers and beach umbrellas.

Praia do Muta is a great choice for travelers interested in nature, sandy beaches and relaxation.
