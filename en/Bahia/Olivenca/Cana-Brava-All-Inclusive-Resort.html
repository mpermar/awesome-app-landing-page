Description

The rooms at the Cana Brava Resort Hotel feature brightly colored interiors and are air conditioned. All rooms are equipped with cable TV, minibar and free Wi-Fi. Some rooms overlook the lake or the sea.

Cana Brava Resort's restaurant serves traditional local dishes as well as international cuisine. The bar serves a large selection of drinks, such as tropical cocktails.

Guests can rent surf equipment in the Cana Brava. It also includes a gym, games room and a cinema. Trips to the historic center of Ilhéus, cocoa plantations, Tijuípe waterfall and Una ecological park can be booked at the Cana Brava Resort tour desk.

Jorge Amado Airport is 24 km away.
