Description

This complex is ideal for the whole family and allows you to rest, do leisure activities and be in touch with nature.

The Vila Galé Marés has a giant outdoor pool, the Mega Kids Club, several dining places and a spa, the Satsanga.

It also offers a gym, bicycles, water activities, tennis courts and other sports, a hairdresser, shops and a travel agency.

Luís Eduardo Magalhães Airport, located 43 km away, is the closest.
