Description

The rooms at Iberostar Praia do Forte include air conditioning, a minibar, satellite TV and a seating area. They also have a large balcony with hammocks and views of the garden or the South Atlantic Ocean. Room service is provided every day, 24 hours a day.

The Iberostar has a playground with a pool table, a library and a large children's play area. It also has entertainment staff and organizes live shows in the evenings.

The complex serves a rich buffet breakfast and houses 6 restaurants, where you can taste regional and oriental proposals, as well as à la carte dishes of international cuisine. The resort's bars serve local and international drinks. The spacious spa at the Iberostar Praia do Forte has steam baths and offers 20 different treatments, including facials and massages.

The complex is only 9 km from the Tamar Project and the Baleia Jubarte Institute. Salvador de Bahia Airport is 57 km away. Free private parking is provided.
