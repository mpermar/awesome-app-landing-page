Description

The houses have been decorated in a rustic style, with wooden details and vivid and natural colors. All have many places to sit, a seating area with a flat-screen cable TV, and a fully equipped kitchen. The accommodation offers a spa and gym, for a fee.

The establishment has a lounge area in Praia dos Nativos, is located next to the historic Quadrado square and is within walking distance of the craft fairs in the area. In the surroundings you can go hiking and canoeing.

UXUA Casa Hotel & Spa is located 7 km from Terravista Airport and 38 km from the local bus station and Porto Seguro Airport. Shuttle service is available for a fee. The property has free parking.
