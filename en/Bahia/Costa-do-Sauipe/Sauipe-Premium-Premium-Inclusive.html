Description

Its rooms feature air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, a balcony, a seating area with a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a minibar and a private bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer.

The Stella Artois lobby bar enjoys panoramic views of the pool, while the Casa Grande restaurant features an extensive wine list for a perfect pairing with its dishes. All restaurants and the Sauipe Premium bar allow you to enjoy an exclusive menu for a fee.

The Sauipe also houses a children's club with exclusive entertainment staff. In addition, guests can practice activities such as fishing, zip line, kayak and paddle surf.

This complex is 76 km from Luis Eduardo Magalhães Airport and free public parking is available.
