Description

The rooms at the Arraial D'ajuda Eco Resort are 5-star and include free Wi-Fi, cable TV, air conditioning and a minibar. In addition, they feature an exclusive lovely decoration. Some units offer sea views or have a balcony with a hammock and river views.

The tourist information desk at Arraial D'ajuda organizes ecological excursions. The complex also has a diving center, tennis court and games room. There is a golf course 25 minutes away by car.

Arraial d’Ajuda Eco Resort's restaurants serve fresh fish, traditional Bahia dishes and international cuisine.

The exclusivity of the complex lies in its ideal location and its private beach and jetty, as well as its own cinema and water park, the Arraial D'ajuda Eco Park. The water park is available at certain times and opening days.

The complex is within walking distance of the ferry that connects with Porto Seguro. In addition, it has a shuttle service to the town and its own ferry to cross to Porto Seguro, both services available at a time indicated at the reception.
