Description

The Txai has spacious suites and private bungalows built on raised wooden platforms and equipped with four-poster king-size beds. All are tastefully decorated and have air conditioning, while some have a private balcony with panoramic sea views or views of the Atlantic forest.

The Txai is in a coconut plantation and allows you to enjoy the unique experience of approaching the beach during the turtle nesting season. Guests of the Txai Resort can take a walk along Itacarezinho Beach, climb the hill, go through Havaizinho Beach and reach Engenhoca Beach.

This complex has 3 restaurants. One serves a combination of organic specialties typical of Bahia and contemporary cuisine, while the pool bar offers exotic cocktails and fine wines from the winery.

At the Txai Resort there is a helipad and free parking. Txai Resort is 11 km from Costa Beach and Tiririca Beach. The nearest Ilhéus / Bahia-Jorge Amado Airport is 49 km from Txai Resort.
