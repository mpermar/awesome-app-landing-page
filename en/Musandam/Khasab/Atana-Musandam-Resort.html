Description

The suites includes a private balcony or terrace, a flat-screen TV, a dining area and a kitchen. Featuring a shower, the private bathroom also has a bathtub or shower and a hairdryer.

Local and international delights are served in the room or at the restaurant. Room service is available 24 hours.

Other facilities at the property include water sports facilities, meeting facilities and a game room. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the area, including fishing, diving and snorkeling. The property provides free parking on site.
