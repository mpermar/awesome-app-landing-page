Description

At the hotel, each room comes with a balcony. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a bath, while certain rooms have a seating area. At Onederz Khmer House the rooms are equipped with a desk and a flat-screen TV.

A à la carte or American breakfast is available each morning at the property. At the accommodation you'll find a restaurant serving Asian cuisine.

Onederz Khmer House offers a business center for guest use. Languages spoken at the reception include Japanese, English and Khmer.

Angkor Wat is 5 miles from the hotel. The nearest airport is Angkor International Airport, 6 miles from the property.
