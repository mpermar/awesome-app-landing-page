Description

At the hotel, every room is equipped with a desk. Rooms come complete with a private bathroom equipped with a bath and free toiletries, while certain accommodations at Neat Pearly Angkor Hotel also have a seating area. Guest rooms at the accommodation feature air conditioning and a closet.

The daily breakfast offers continental, American or Asian options. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the on-site restaurant, which serves American cuisine.

Languages spoken at the reception include Korean and English, and guests are invited to request advice on the area when needed.

Kravan Temple is an 11-minute walk from Neat Pearly Angkor Hotel. The nearest airport is Angkor International Airport, 3.7 miles from the property.
