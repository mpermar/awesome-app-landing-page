Description

The property is just a 10-minute drive from Siem Reap International Airport, and a 15-minute drive from the renowned Angkor Wat Temple. The famous Pub Street and Angkor Night Market are accessible within a 5-minute Tuk tuk ride.

Featuring a balcony, air-conditioned rooms and suites come equipped with a wardrobe, a personal safe, sofa sitting area and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. A fridge, minibar and electric kettle are also included. The private bathroom has a shower, bathrobes, hairdryer and free bath amenities.

The Privilege Floor @Lotus Blanc offers a business center and meeting/banquet facilities. Guests may rent a bike/car to explore the area, or enjoy a relaxing massage at the spa. Laundry service and luggage storage is available, and tours and airport transportation can be arranged.

The in-house restaurant serves a delectable spread of local and international dishes. Special diet meals can also be prepared upon request. A wide range of after-meal drinks are available at the bar.
