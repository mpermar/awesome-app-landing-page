Description

Guest rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, fridge, a electric tea pot, a bath, free toiletries and a desk. The hotel provides some accommodations with pool views, and every room is equipped with a balcony. All guest rooms feature a closet.

The daily breakfast offers continental, à la carte or American options. At Vihara Residence you'll find a restaurant serving Cambodian and American cuisine.

Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Siem Reap, like cycling.

Guests can also take advantage of the business center and newspapers. For any tips on how to get around or what to do in the area, guests can ask at the reception.

Popular points of interest near Vihara Residence include Angkor Trade Centre, Old Market and Platinum Cineplex Siem Reap. Angkor International Airport is 6 miles away.
