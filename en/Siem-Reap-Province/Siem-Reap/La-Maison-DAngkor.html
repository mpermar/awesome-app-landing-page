Experiences

12/30/2016

A little run down but still very charming

"I first stayed here in 2008 and then again in December 2016. La Maison D'Angkor was a wonderful find. It's a quaint little oasis with beautiful gardens, and an awesome swimming pool. The rooms are a good size and comfortable.Although the staff speak very little english they were all very pleasant and helpful.We had 3 young children with us and this was the perfect place to get back to where there was plenty of space for them to run around.If you're looking for something other than a hotel, and the focus is on green space, a lovely pool and a very chilled atmosphere, this would be a top choice."
