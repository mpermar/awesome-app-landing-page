Description

Each bright, air-conditioned room at Coyaba Beach Resort has Caribbean-style décor. All rooms come equipped with satellite TV, free Wi-Fi and a coffee maker.

Guests can enjoy a range of Grenadan cuisine in the Coyaba’s buffet restaurant, which overlooks the sea.

The center of St Georges Bay is within a 10 minute walk of the resort. Grand Etang Forest Reserve is 6 miles away.
