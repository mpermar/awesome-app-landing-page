Description

Each room here includes a sitting area and a work desk. Featuring a bathtub or shower, the private bathrooms also have a hairdryer and bathrobes.

At the Bellmoor Inn and Spa, there is a 24-hour front desk, an outdoor pool and a fitness center. Other amenities at the property include a business center and laundry facilities.

An breakfast is served each morning, and afternoon refreshments are also offered.

Several shopping and dining options are located within a 5-minute walk. The inn is half a mile from Rehoboth Beach, 2 miles from the Tanger Outlet Center - Rehoboth Beach and 9.3.5 miles from Delaware Seashore State Park. Cambridge-Dorchester Airport is 53 miles away.
