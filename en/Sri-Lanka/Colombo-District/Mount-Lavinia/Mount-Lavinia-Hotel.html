Description

Boasting a private beach area and a terrace with views of the bay and Colombo’s skyline, Mount Lavinia Hotel offers a relaxing getaway.

This colonial-style hotel features a spa and 3 dining options. Located on Mount Lavinia Beach, the hotel is 20-minute drive from the National Zoological Garden in Dehiwala.

Driving from Bandaranaike International Airport takes an hour and 15 minutes. Fitted with dark wood furnishings, air-conditioned rooms have satellite TV and tea/coffee making facilities.

The private bathrooms offer a hot shower and Ayurvedic toiletries. A dental kit is provided on request.

Guests can visit the beachfront CocoSpa for a massage, or relax in the outdoor pool while younger guests play in the children’s pool.

A gym and game room are available. Other facilities include 24-hour travel desk, a shopping arcade, and a business centre.

Fresh seafood can be enjoyed at the beachfront restaurant. International buffets are served at The Governors restaurant, while The Terrace offers local and international dishes.
