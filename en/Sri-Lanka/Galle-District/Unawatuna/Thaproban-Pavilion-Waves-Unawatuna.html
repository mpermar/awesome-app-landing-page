Description

The rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. With a private bathroom, some units at Thaproban Pavilion Waves Unawatuna also feature a sea view. Guest rooms have a seating area.

An American breakfast is available every morning at the property. The restaurant at the accommodation specializes in European, French and pizza cuisine.

Thaproban Pavilion Waves Unawatuna has an outdoor pool. Guests can take part in various activities in the surrounding areas, including cycling and hiking.

Guests can enjoy playing pool, or take advantage of the business center. For any tips on how to get around or what to do in the area, guests can ask at the reception.

Galle Dutch Hospital is 1.8 miles from the hotel.
