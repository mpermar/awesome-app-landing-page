Description

Adjacent to Unawatuna Beach, Sergeant House is set in a boutique villa showcasing colonial architecture and originating in the 1800s.

It offers free access to an outdoor swimming pool, 24-hour front desk and a fitness centre.

It also comprises a restaurant and a garden with a children’s playground and barbecue facilities.

All the rooms are air-conditioned and feature a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a balcony with garden view.

They come equipped with free WiFi and a bathroom with a bath or shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

The units offer a pool or Courtyard with aquarium. At Sergeant House you will find a restaurant serving Sri Lankan, continental, Italian and Thai dishes.

For an additional charge, guests can relax in the wellness centre with one of the many massage treatments.

The property offers free parking. Supermarkets and eating facilities are 500 metres away.

Koggala Lake is a 10-minute drive. Galle’s Bus and Railway Station are 5. 8 km from Sergeant House.
