Description

Offering a restaurant and a public beach area, Surasa Beach Resort is located in Weligama.

Free WiFi access is available in this resort. The accommodation will provide you with a work desk.

There is also an electric kettle. Featuring a shower, private bathroom comes with a bidet.

You can enjoy sea view from the balcony. A common kitchen, a common area with a flat screen TV(with satellite connection) and a refrigerator are available at the resort.

Other facilities offered at the property include a tour desk, luggage storage and an ironing service.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including fishing and diving.

The property offers free parking. This resort is 84 km from the Mattala Rajapaksa International Airport.
