Description

Set in beautiful landscaped grounds, Haus Berlin offers relaxing stays in spacious rooms.

Featuring an outdoor pool, it offers bike rental and free WiFi in public areas for free.

The property provides Ayurveda treatments. A 5-minute drive from Bentota’s sandy beaches, 8-minute walk from Lunuganga Lake and Haus Berlin is a 2 and a half hour drive from Bandaranaike International Airport.

Free parking is available. Well-ventilated rooms at Haus Berlin Bentota open out to views of tropical greenery.

They come with air conditioning and a private bathroom with heated shower facilities.

Guests can enjoy leisurely afternoons at the seating areas dotting the property. Other facilities include a restaurant that serves local, Chinese and international dishes.
