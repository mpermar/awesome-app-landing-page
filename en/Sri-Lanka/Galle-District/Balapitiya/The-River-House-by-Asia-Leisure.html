Description

Offering views of the grounds or the Madhu River, rooms at River House are inspired by South Asian architecture. Each room has a flat-screen TV, DVD player and safe.

Guests can enjoy an in-room massage, or go for a relaxing swim in the outdoor pool that’s surrounded by greenery. Canoes are available for a quiet cruise down the river. Free Wi-Fi and free parking are also provided.

International dishes are served at the 24-hour restaurant. Views of the grounds and greenery accompany meals in an open-air setting.

The River House is a 3.5-hour drive from Bandaranaike International Airport and a 3-hour drive from Colombo.
