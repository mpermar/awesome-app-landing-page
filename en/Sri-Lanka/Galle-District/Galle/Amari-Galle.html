Description

Located in the historic fort city of Galle on the southern coast of Sri Lanka, Amari Galle is a luxury resort featuring rooms and suites, an outdoor pool with a pool bar, spa and fitness centre.

All rooms boast of a direct view of the Indian Ocean, carefully designed to offer living spaces with a 40-inch LED TV, complimentary Wi-Fi, work desk, mini bar, tea and coffee making and ironing facilities.

The bathrooms offer a rain shower, complimentary toiletries, bathrobe and bath slippers and hair dryers.

AHARA Gourmet Gallery is Amari Galle’s signature all-day dining restaurant serving a buffet breakfast.

A range of Asian and International cuisine is prepared from live cooking stations using only fresh and hand-picked produce, and is available for lunch and dinner.

BOMMU Rooftop Bar offers a panoramic view of the surroundings along with cocktails and finger foods.

The Voyager Lounge is Amari Galle’s unique offering to its guests who are looking to rest and relax during an early arrival or late check-out.

The Lounge offers complimentary luggage-size lockers, showers, a sitting area and Wi-Fi.

A Food and Beverage outlet is also available for guest convenience. The Resort also offers a dedicated Kids Club with a variety of supervised activities to keep our young guests occupied.
