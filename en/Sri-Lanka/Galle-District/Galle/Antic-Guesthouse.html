Description

Featuring free WiFi, a restaurant and a terrace, Antic Guesthouse offers accommodation in Galle.

Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurants. Every room at this guest house is air conditioned and has a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Certain rooms include a seating area for your convenience. A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms.

Every room includes a private bathroom fitted with a bath or shower. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including snorkelling and diving.

The guest house also offers bike hire. Galle Light house is 100 metres from Antic Guesthouse, while Galle Fort is 300 metres from the property.
