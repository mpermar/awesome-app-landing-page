Description

200 metres from Dutch Church Galle, Prince of Galle is situated within the fortress walls of Galle Fort and features free WiFi, express check-in and check-out and concierge services.

Boasting several terraces, all compact yet well appointed rooms have private bathrooms and desks and complimentary snacks and non alcoholic mini bar.

All units at the hotel are equipped with a seating area. The private bathroom is fitted with a bidet.

All guest rooms include a desk. The daily breakfast offers continental and à la carte options.

Guests can indulge in live music and happy hours in the evening. Free cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy.

The reception can provide advice on the area in order to help guests plan their day.

The cafe serves their signature Breagustation (5-course breakfast) and Hoppas lunch.

Guests can enjoy live piano music every afternoon in the common lounge on the 1st floor.

Galle International Cricket Stadium is 500 metres from the accommodation, while Galle Light house is 500 metres from the property.
