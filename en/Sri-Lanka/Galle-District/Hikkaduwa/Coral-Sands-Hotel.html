Description

Air-conditioned guest rooms include an attached balcony. The private bathroom has toiletries and hot shower facilities. Some rooms also have a safe and a minibar.

Diving and snorkeling facilities are available on site. Guests can enjoy a massage at the Ayurveda Massage Centre. Laundry and ironing facilities are available.

The on-site restaurant serves a variety of international dishes.

Hotel Coral Sands is a 3.5 hour drive from Colombo International Airport. An airport shuttle service is provided upon request.
