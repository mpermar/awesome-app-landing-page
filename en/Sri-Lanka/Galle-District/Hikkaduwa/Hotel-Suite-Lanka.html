Description

Hotel Suite Lanka sits on the sands of Hikkaduwa Beach, one of the widest beaches on Sri Lanka’s west coast.

Offering spacious accommodation, it has an outdoor pool and restaurant. Boasting views of the Indian Ocean, rooms at Suite Lanka feature Ceylonese décor and wooden furnishings.

Each air-conditioned room has a fan, fridge with minibar, and ample seating areas.

Private bathrooms provide a hot and cold shower. Guests can enjoy the peaceful beach area, or have a relaxing massage.

The library offers space for quiet reading. Bicycle rental, currency exchange and laundry services are available at the hotel.

American breakfast is served daily. Guests can also order food from the kitchen and enjoy their meals anywhere on the property, such as at their room’s private terrace or on the beach.

Hotel Suite Lanka is 128 km from Sri Lanka's Bandaranaike International Airport and 98 km from Colombo.
