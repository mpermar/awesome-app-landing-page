Description

Situated in Ahangama Village just a 5-minute drive from popular whale and dolphin watching spots, Insight Resort features an outdoor swimming pool and complimentary Wi-Fi access at all public areas.

Located just a few steps away from the beach, the resort is also a 20-minute drive to Hikkaduwa City from the southern expressway and Bandaranaike International Airport is approximately a 3-hour drive from the resort.

Modern air-conditioned rooms at Insight Resort feature large windows which offer ample natural light.

They are tastefully furnished with a wardrobe and seating area. The en suite bathrooms include free toiletries.

A 24-hour front desk will be happy to assist guests with laundry services and barbeque facilities.

The hotel can also arrange for traditional massages and windsurfing activities. Authentic Sri Lankan cuisines infused with Western delights can be enjoyed at the in-house restaurant.
