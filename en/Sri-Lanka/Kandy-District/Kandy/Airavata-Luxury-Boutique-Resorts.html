Description

All rooms at the resort come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a bidet. Every room includes air conditioning, and certain rooms at Airavata Luxury Boutique Resorts have a balcony. The rooms have a desk.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental breakfast.

Airavata Luxury Boutique Resorts has a playground.

With staff speaking English, Hindi and Arabic, round-the-clock guidance is available at the reception.

Kandy City Center Shopping Mall is 2.4 miles from the resort, while Bogambara Stadium is 2.5 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Bandaranaike International Airport, 54 miles from the property.
