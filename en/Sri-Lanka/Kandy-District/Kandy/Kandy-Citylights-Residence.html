Description

All guest rooms at the guesthouse come with a TV and a kitchen. Kandy Citylights Residence provides certain units that include a balcony, and each room comes with a electric tea pot. At the accommodation, each room has a seating area.

A Full English/Irish, Asian or halal breakfast is served at the property.

Kandy Museum is one mile from Kandy Citylights Residence, while Lakeside Adventist Hospital is 1.3 miles away. The nearest airport is Bandaranaike International Airport, 52 miles from the guesthouse.
