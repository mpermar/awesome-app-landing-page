Description

There's a fully equipped private bathroom with shower and free toiletries.

Breakfast is available daily, and includes continental and Full English/Irish options.

The homestay has a terrace.

Cycling can be enjoyed nearby and a bicycle rental service is available.

Sri Dalada Maligawa is 1.7 miles from THE PEPPERMINT, while Kandy Museum is 1.7 miles from the property. Bandaranaike International Airport is 51 miles away.
