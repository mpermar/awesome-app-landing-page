Description

Air-conditioned rooms at Amaya Hills include private balconies with mountain or valley views. Each room provides a flat-screen TV and tea/coffee-making facilities. The attached bathroom has a bathtub and shower.

Guests can exercise in the gym, or enjoy a game of racquetball or billiards. The tour desk can arrange activities like bird watching excursions, jungle treks and night safaris. Amaya Kandy also features a nightclub called Le Garage.

Buffet breakfast is served at the hotel. Local and international dishes are offered at Rasawasala Restaurant, which has both indoor and outdoor seating. Drinks are available at the Tea Room and Dumbara Bar.

Amaya Hills is 4 miles from the heart of Kandy. Sri Lanka Airport is 73 miles away.
