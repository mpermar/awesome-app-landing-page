Description

Each room at this resort is air-conditioned and has a flat-screen TV. All units have a sitting area to relax in after a busy day. For your comfort, you'll find free toiletries, kettle, and a hairdryer.

Room service is available at the property. Car rental is also available.

There are lots of activities in the area, such as golfing and horseback riding. Nuwara Eliya is 47 miles from Serendib Signature Resort.
