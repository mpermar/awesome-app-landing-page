Description

Guest rooms at the resort come with a seating area. The private bathroom is equipped with a bidet and free toiletries. The rooms feature a desk.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property.

Greenwood Udawalawa Safari Resort has a playground. Cycling and fishing are among the activities that guests of the accommodation can find nearby.
