Description

Spacious and well-designed, the air-conditioned rooms all feature a Balinese four-post bed and a flat-screen TV. A personal safe and tea/coffee making facilities are included. Bathrooms have relaxing rainshowers.

The Wallawwa is a 30 minute drive from the beaches and fish markets of Negombo. It is also a 30 minute drive from Muthurajawela Wetlands. Colombo City is a one hour drive away. Free parking is available.

Z Spa provides a selection of massage treatments and other beauty therapies. Guests can read a book in the library, or make use of the iMac computer in the hotel's study. Room service is provided 24 hours.

Nestled within the gardens, The Verandah serves Asian fusion cuisine prepared with fresh local ingredients. Vegetarian options can also be arranged.
