Description

The rooms at the resort come with a seating area. Airport Eco Resort provides certain units with pool views, and each room has a terrace. At the accommodation each room includes a private bathroom.

Breakfast is available each morning, and includes continental and buffet options.

A variety of activities can be enjoyed on site or nearby, including cycling and water park.

A business center and a bicycle rental service are also offered by Airport Eco Resort. Non-stop assistance is available at the reception.

Maris Stella College is 4.2 miles from the resort, while Dutch Fort is 5 miles from the property. Bandaranaike International Airport is 1.2 miles away, and the property offers a free airport shuttle service.
