Description

At the hotel, rooms come with a closet and a flat-screen TV. Featuring a private bathroom with a bidet and free toiletries, certain accommodations at Hotel Sico also feature a city view.

Breakfast is available every morning, and includes continental, à la carte and American options.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool.

Maris Stella College is 1.7 miles from Hotel Sico, while Dutch Fort is 2.2 miles away. Bandaranaike International Airport is 5 miles from the property.
