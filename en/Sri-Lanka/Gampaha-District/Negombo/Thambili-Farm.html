Description

Each room has a private bathroom, and some rooms also feature a patio.

Guests at the resort can enjoy a continental breakfast.

Thambili Farm has a sun terrace. Guests can take part in various activities in the surrounding areas, including cycling and canoeing.

St Anthony's Church is 17 miles from the accommodation. Bandaranaike International Airport is 19 miles from the property.
