Description

At the guesthouse, all rooms come with a desk. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a bidet, while certain rooms also offer a terrace. At 9 Arch View Point rooms include a seating area.

A continental or buffet breakfast is served at the property.

A variety of activities are offered in the area, such as cycling, fishing and hiking.

Guests can rent a car or take advantage of the business center. For any tips on how to get around or what to do in the area, guests can ask at the reception.
