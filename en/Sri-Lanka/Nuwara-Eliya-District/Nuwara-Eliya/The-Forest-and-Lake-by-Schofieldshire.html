Description

Guest rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, microwave, a coffee machine, a shower and a desk. All rooms have a electric tea pot and a private bathroom, while selected rooms here will provide you with a kitchen. All guest rooms at the resort come with a seating area.

A continental breakfast is served daily at the property.

You can play darts at The Forest and Lake by Schofieldshire, and the area is popular for cycling.

Hakgala Botanical Garden is 3.4 miles from the accommodation, while Ambewela Farms is 1.4 miles from the property.
