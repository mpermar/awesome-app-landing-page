Description

Spacious suites have elegant hardwood floors and furnishings. They provide a day bed, ample seating space and private bathroom facilities featuring both an open-air bathtub and shower. Suites offer sweeping views of the ocean.

Massages are available at Villa Saman. The hotel also provides a sauna and a hot tub. Other facilities include a book and CD library.

Saman’s main restaurant offers an international menu and local specialties for dinner, while the terrace restaurant provides breakfast and lunch. Guests can also enjoy stunning views of the ocean with their drinks at the bar.

Villa Saman is surrounded by tropical gardens and coconut plantations. It is on the southwest coast of Sri Lanka, about 40 miles from Colombo. The International Airport is about 57 miles away.
