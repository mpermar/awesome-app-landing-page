Description

The sea-facing rooms are air-conditioned and include a minibar, a safe and satellite TV. The private bathrooms have a shower and a hairdryer.

Bandaranaike International Airport is approximately 34 miles from Cinnamon Bey.

Guests have access to the fitness studio and spa, which includes a sauna and a steam room. Laundry, ironing and airport shuttle services are also available.

A range of mezze and grills are served at the Arabic Mezz2 restaurant, and handmade ice cream, sorbets and frozen yogurts are available at I.C.E Ice Cream Emporium.
