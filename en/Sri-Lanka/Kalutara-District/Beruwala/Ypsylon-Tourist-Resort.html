Description

Guest rooms at Ypsylon are equipped with a ceiling fan and mosquito net. The private bathroom comes with bathroom amenities and hot/cold shower facilities.

Guests can enjoy relaxing massages at the Ayurvedic Center, or explore the spacious grounds. Sports facilities at the hotel include ping-pong and beach volleyball. Laundry and dry cleaning services are offered.

Local and international dishes are served at the restaurant, with a choice of indoor seating or beachfront dining.

The Ypsylon is located 59 miles from the Colombo Bandaranaike International Airport.
