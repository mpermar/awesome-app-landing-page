Description

All rooms at the resort are equipped with a seating area. Rooms are equipped with a private bathroom, while some rooms contain a terrace and others also feature a garden view. The rooms feature a desk.

An Asian or vegetarian breakfast is available every morning at the property. Guests can dine in the on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of Indian dishes.

Green House Resort offers a spa center. The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental is available at the accommodation.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can help with guidance about the area.
