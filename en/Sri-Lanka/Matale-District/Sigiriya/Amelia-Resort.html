Description

At the hotel, rooms include a balcony with a mountain view. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a bidet, while some rooms contain a patio. At Amelia Resort every room includes a closet and a flat-screen TV.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at the property. The restaurant at the accommodation serves American and Italian cuisine.

You can play darts at Amelia Resort, and the area is popular for cycling and hiking.

Sigiriya Rock is 2.3 miles from the hotel, while Sigiriya Museum is 2.4 miles away.
