Description

At the guesthouse, rooms are equipped with a balcony. With a private bathroom, rooms at Sevonrich Holiday Resort also have a pool view. All rooms feature a seating area and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

The accommodation offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental and car rental are available at Sevonrich Holiday Resort.

Staff at the reception can assist guests at any time of the day.

Rangiri Dambulla International Stadium is 1.4 miles from the guesthouse.
