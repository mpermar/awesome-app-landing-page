Description

At the hotel, all rooms are equipped with a terrace. The rooms have a coffee machine and a private bathroom with a hot tub and free toiletries, while selected rooms will provide you with a kitchen equipped with a dishwasher. At LakeSide Cabana Tissamaharama the rooms come with a seating area.

A buffet, Full English/Irish or American breakfast is available daily at the property.

The accommodation has a playground. Cycling and fishing are among the activities that guests of LakeSide Cabana Tissamaharama can find nearby.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can give guidance on what to do in the area.

If you want a truly authentic experience then Stay here and you won’t be disappointed

"Everything! The hosts are a really lovely family and everyone gets involved - even Grandpa was hooking the coconuts down from the tree for drinks from the minute we arrived. Nothing was too much trouble and done with an enormous smile. Stunning location right on the edge of a lake - (no mossies when we were there) we stayed in two treehouses (without being in trees ) - each one slept 4 in 2 doubles. We are a family of 5 so the children (16,17,11) slept in one and us in the other. They are only 20 yards apart. Ask for the right hand one facing the lake if you want the stunning view. We booked our safari trip through them which was excellent value - an early start but worth it and a sandwich and fruit packed breakfast included. We saw a leopard, sloth bear, elephants, a mongoose, warthogs, deer and numerous birds including Cranes and pelicans. I really admire this family’s entrepreneurial spirit and absolutely recommend it. Oh and they made us an enormous meal in the evening which was delicious."
