Description

All rooms at the resort come with a seating area. With a private bathroom equipped with a bath and free toiletries, rooms at Saman Resort also provide guests with a garden view. All guest rooms at the accommodation have air conditioning and a desk.

A continental or buffet breakfast is available every morning at the property. There's an on-site restaurant, which serves Asian cuisine.

Saman Resort has a playground. Guests at the resort will be able to enjoy activities in and around Tissamaharama, like cycling.

Languages spoken at the reception include German and English.

Ranminitenna Tele Cinema Village is 2.2 miles from Saman Resort.
