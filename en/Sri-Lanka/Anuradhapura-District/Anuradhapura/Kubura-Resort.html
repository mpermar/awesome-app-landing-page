Description

All rooms at the resort come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a bidet. Every room includes air conditioning, and selected rooms also offer a balcony. The rooms feature a desk.

Breakfast is available daily, and includes buffet and Full English/Irish options.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental is available at Kubura Resort.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can give tips on the area.
