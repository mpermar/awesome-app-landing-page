Description

At the resort, every room includes a closet. The rooms include a private bathroom with a shower. All rooms are equipped with a seating area.

A continental or à la carte breakfast is served at the property. The restaurant at Waasala Leisure specializes in American cuisine.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool. Bike rental and car rental are available at this resort and the area is popular for cycling.

When guests need guidance on where to visit, the reception will be happy to provide advice.

Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi is 1.6 miles from Waasala Leisure, while Kuttam Pokuna, Twin Ponds is 2.9 miles away.
