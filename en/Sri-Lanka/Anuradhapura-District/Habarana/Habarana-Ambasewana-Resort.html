Description

At the resort, the rooms come with a desk. Each room comes with a private bathroom with a bidet and selected rooms are equipped with a terrace. Guest rooms at Habarana Ambasewana Resort feature air conditioning and a closet.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy an Asian breakfast.

Habarana Ambasewana Resort has a playground. Guests can enjoy a variety of activities in the surrounding areas, including cycling, fishing and canoeing.

Round-the-clock assistance is available at the reception.
