Description

At the bed and breakfast, each room includes a desk. Guest rooms will provide guests with an oven.

The daily breakfast offers Full English/Irish, American or Asian options.

Tharurashmi Holiday Resort has a playground. Bike rental and car rental are available at this bed and breakfast and the area is popular for cycling.

Tangalle is 7 miles from the accommodation, while Mirissa is 18 miles from the property.
