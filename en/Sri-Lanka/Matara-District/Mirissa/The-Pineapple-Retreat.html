Description

At the resort, every room is equipped with a closet. All rooms have a private bathroom and free toiletries, and some units at The Pineapple Retreat have a terrace.

The daily breakfast offers continental and à la carte options.

The accommodation has a grill. The area is popular for cycling and hiking.

Whale Watching Mirissa is a 6-minute walk from The Pineapple Retreat.
