Description

All guest rooms in the resort are equipped with a electric tea pot. The rooms come with a private bathroom with a hot tub, while some feature sea views. At On the beach resort each room comes with a seating area.

Breakfast is available every morning, and includes buffet, American and Asian options.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental and car rental are available at the accommodation.

Whale Watching Mirissa is an 11-minute walk from On the beach resort.
