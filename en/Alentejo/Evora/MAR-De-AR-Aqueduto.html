Description

A creative blend of Alentejo and Mediterranean flavors is served at Restaurant Degust’ AR, which is open for lunch and dinner. M'AR De AR Aqueduto hotel also offers a bar with a wine cellar.

The elegantly designed rooms include a work desk and a comfortable sitting area. All bathrooms enjoy natural light and are separated from the sleeping area by a big glass window.

Hotel Aqueduto’s spa offers a broad range of treatments and massages including aromatherapy and mud treatment. Guests can also unwind in the steam bath or sauna.

M'AR De AR Aqueduto is located 1,950 feet from the Temple of Diana, Cathedral of Evora Se and Praca do Giraldo. The Chapel of Bones Capela dos Ossos is half a mile away.
