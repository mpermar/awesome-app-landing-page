Description

The spa at the Troia Design spans 12900 ft² and offers over 70 different relaxation treatments. There is also an outdoor infinity pool as well as a children’s pool.

The luxurious rooms at the Troia Design Hotel are spacious and have a modern design. Each room has a balcony, a flat-screen TV and a sitting area. Some rooms have a kitchenette.

Guests can enjoy delicious local and international cuisine at the restaurant. There is also a bar serving refreshing drinks and light snacks.

The Design Hotel has a kids’ club as well as babysitting services. Other convenient facilities include an airport shuttle and car rental services.
