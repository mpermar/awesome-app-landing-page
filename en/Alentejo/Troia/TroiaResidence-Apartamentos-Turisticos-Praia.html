Description

Each apartment’s well-equipped kitchen has a ceramic stove, an oven and a 4-seater dining table. The apartments feature a living room with a sofa and fireplace, as well as an equipped kitchen. Some apartments include access to a private pool.

Spread across 3 different areas of the resort, some apartments offer stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean and are within walking distance of the beach. Others offer panoramic views out over the marina and Sado Estuary.

For hot, sunny days, the resort features an outdoor pool. Guests may also head to the Aqualuz spa center for a massage or beauty treatment.

The resort is located between Arrabida Natural Park and the Sado Estuary. Free and private parking is available on site.
