Description

Rooms, chalets and apartments at Résidence La Combeauté have cable TV and private bathrooms. Apartments also have a kitchen equipped with a fridge and microwave.

The Résidence La Combeauté has a game room and a reading lounge with Wi-Fi access. There is also a children’s playground, and barbecue facilities are available for the summertime.

Guests can enjoy the sauna and the hydro-massage shower in the spa center.

The Résidence La Combeauté offers a bike rental service and is an ideal base for hiking and mountain biking.
