Description

Guest rooms are spacious and equipped with an private bathroom, a flat-screen TV and air conditioning. They offer a cozy living area for your stay.

The Novotel Café serves contemporary cuisine and offers breakfast, lunch and dinner. The café is open daily and room service is also available throughout the day and night.

The Novotel Metz also features fully equipped meeting rooms and can cater for your seminars or banquets. Optic fiber internet access is available for free throughout the property.

Up to 2 children (15 years and under) stay free when sharing with parents.
