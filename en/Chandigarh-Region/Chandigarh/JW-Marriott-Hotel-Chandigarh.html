Description

The spacious guest rooms are fitted with an iPod dock, tea/coffee-making facilities and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. The private bathroom has a bathtub.

The Chandigarh JW Marriott is located 3.5 miles from the Capitol Complex and 4.5 miles from Sukhna Lake. It is 2 miles from the Rose Garden and 2 miles from the Bird Sanctuary. Free parking is available on-site.

The renowned Quan Spa offers a variety of massage services. Other recreational facilities include a fitness center and children’s pool. The tour desk can assist guests with making travel arrangements.

Saffron restaurant serves a variety of Indian dishes, and Cafe@JW prepares an international menu. Italian Cuisine can be enjoyed at the Oregano Restaurant and Bar.
