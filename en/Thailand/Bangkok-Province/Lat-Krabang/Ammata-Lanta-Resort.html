Description

Featuring free roundtrip airport transfers, WiFi and a spa centre, Ammata Lanta Resort offers pet-friendly accommodation in Lat Krabang, 1.

9 km from The Paseo Mall, Lat Krabang. The resort has an outdoor pool and children's playground, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Free private parking is available on site. All rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV and DVD player.

Certain units have a seating area to relax in after a busy day. Some units feature views of the lake or pool.

Rooms have a private bathroom equipped with a bath. Extras include bath robes, free toiletries and a hair dryer.

Car hire is available at this resort and the area is popular for golfing. King Mongkut's Institute of Technology Lat Krabang is 4.

1 km from Ammata Lanta Resort, while Lat Krabang Bangkok Hospital is 4. 4 km away.
