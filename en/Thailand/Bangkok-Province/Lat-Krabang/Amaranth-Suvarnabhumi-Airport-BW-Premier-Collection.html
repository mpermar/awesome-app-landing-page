Description

Amaranth Suvarnabhumi Airport, BW Premier Collection by Best Western is located near the Skytrain station, which provides easy access to the entertainment and shopping areas of central Bangkok.

Decorated in a contemporary style, rooms at Amaranth Suvarnabhumi Airport, BW Premier Collection by Best Western offer several facilities to make your stay pleasant. It includes air conditioning, ample workspace and free Wi-Fi internet access.

Lounge by the swimming pool with a refreshing drink or cool off with a swim. If you're looking to indulge yourself, head over to Amaranth Suvarnabhumi Airport's spa, which provides a wide range of relaxing treatments and massages. Other facilities include a well-equipped fitness center and tour desk.

Dine at Bua Chompp Restaurant, which is open 24-hours daily, providing a delicious buffet spread and a la carte menu of international and Thai favourites. To unwind, The Red Brown Lounge provides a cosy atmosphere to socialise with a cocktail and live music.

Amaranth Suvarnabhumi Airport, BW Premier Collection by Best Western provides a 2-way shuttle mini-bus service from the airport to the hotel. The shuttle leaves every 30 minute from 05:00-02:00. Reservations are essential.
