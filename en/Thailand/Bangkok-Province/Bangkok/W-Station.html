Description

Situated in Bangkok, just a 5-minute walk from BTS Wongwian Yai Skytrain Station, W Station offers easy access to both Bangkok Old Town Area and City centres.

Guests can enjoy at stylish restaurant and free WiFi. Attractions such as The Grand Palace and Bangkok City Pillar are within a 20-minute drive from the property.

Featuring Thai modern art design, rooms at W Station are fitted with a TV with cable channels.

Certain units feature a seating area where you can relax. All rooms comes with a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower.

Extras include free toiletries and a hairdryer. A Thai herb welcome drink is served upon your arrival.

Free private parking is available on site. Wat Arun is 3. 8 km from W Station, while Temple of the Golden Mount is 4.

8 km away. Asiatique The Riverfront is a 10-minute drive from the property. Suvarnabhumi International Airport is 37 km from W Station.
