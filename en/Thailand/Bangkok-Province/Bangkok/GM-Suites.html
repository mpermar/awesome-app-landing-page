Description

Centrally located, GM Suites is a 5-minute walk to Nana BTS Skytrain Station. Boasting an outdoor pool, the hotel offers free Wi-Fi throughout the property.

Free public parking is available on site. The hotel is a 5-minute walk to Bumrungrad Hospital.

Terminal 21 shopping centre is a 10-minute walk away. Each air-conditioned room features a flat-screen TV, fridge and minibar.

It also comes with a safe and microwave. Shower facilities and free toiletries are included in an en suite bathroom.

For extra convenience, there is a 24-hour front desk. Laundry services are available.

Rooms are inclusive of a breakfast or meal served from 07:00-11:30 hrs daily. Local restaurants can be reached within walking distance.
