Description

Close to the commercial district on Sukhumvit Road, Conrad Bangkok is within easy reach of the mass transit system, which makes for effortless exploration of the city. The luxurious hotel is also near Lumpini Park.

Rooms at Conrad Bangkok are decorated in a contemporary style with a Thai touch that includes silk and natural wood. They have a relaxing environment with luxurious bedding and a pillow menu that will ensure a good night's rest. It also features high-speed broadband internet access, data ports and room service.

Indulge yourself with a massage or body treatment at Season Spa or workout at the fully equipped fitness center. Take a refreshing dip in the outdoor swimming pool or play a game of tennis. It also comes with a sauna and steam room.

Conrad Bangkok features 5 dining outlets. Guests can experience authentic Chinese cuisine at Liu and Thai and French dishes can be savored at Café@2. Other dining options include Kisara, which serves Japanese cuisine. Delicious cocktails and non-alcoholic beverages are served at Diplomat Bar.

Pathumwan is a great choice for travelers interested in clothes shopping, shopping and food.
