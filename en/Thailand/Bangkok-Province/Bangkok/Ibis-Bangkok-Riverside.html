Description

Located along the Chao Phraya River, Ibis Bangkok Riverside offers bright, modern rooms with flat-screen TVs.

It offers an outdoor pool and a free shuttle to Krungthonburi BTS Skytrain Station.

Rooms feature wooden flooring and brightly coloured walls. Each room comes with satellite TV and tea/coffee making facilities.

A fridge and a work desk are also provided. Ibis Bangkok Riverside is a 30-minute drive from Suvarnabhumi International Airport.

The hotel is 4 km from the city centre and 6. 5 km from Wat Pho. The hotel offers a tour desk and a self-service business centre.

Laundry, dry cleaning and ironing services are available. Guests can enjoy the stunning riverfront views while pampering at Sala Massage.

The “It's all about TASTE” restaurant serves local and international dishes, while drinks and light snacks can be enjoyed at the Lobby Bar.
