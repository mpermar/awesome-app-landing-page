Description

A minutes walk from Ratchadamri Skytrain Station, Anantara Siam Bangkok Hotel is a touch of Thai luxury in the city.

The hotel features a spa, an outdoor pool and free parking. Rooms feature elegant touches of Thai culture, offering dcor made of teak and Thai silk.

A flat-screen TV and DVD player are provided. Grand, en suite bathrooms showcase elegantly carved wood furnishings.

Guests enjoy free use of a smartphone with free international calls and internet.

Anantara Siam Bangkok Hotel is a 10-minute walk from Central World Plaza and Lumpini Park.

Bumrungrad International Hospital is a 12-minute drive away, while Suvarnabhumi International Airport is 25 km away.

The Spa by MSpa provides the option of a spa suite with a private plunge pool and complimentary herbal tea and cookies.

Guests can head for the health club which has a steam room and offers fitness classes.

Younger guests can look forward to treats such as bedtime milk and cookies and a range of childrens toiletries.

The hotel provides 9 restaurants and lounges including Italian cuisine and Japanese creations.

Local Thai delights are featured at Spice Market while Mocha and Muffins serves a selection of cold cuts, sandwiches and fine wines.
