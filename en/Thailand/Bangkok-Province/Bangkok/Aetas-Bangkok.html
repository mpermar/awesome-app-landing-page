Description

Centrally located along Sukhumvit Road, Aetas Bangkok provides free shuttles to Phloen Chit BTS Skytrain Station.

It offers an outdoor pool and accommodation with panoramic views of Bangkok. This hotel is about 20 minutes by BTS from Siam Paragon and The EmQuartier shopping centres.

The stylish, spacious rooms at Aetas come with a sitting area and a flat-screen TV.

Each room has tea/coffee making facilities and ironing amenities. Featuring a glass wall, the bathroom has a bathtub and rain shower.

Guests can visit the spa for a massage, or exercise in the fitness centre. Services provided at the 24-hour business include flight bookings and mobile phone rental.

The hotel offers limousine services with English-speaking drivers. Buffet breakfast is served daily.

Moments Restaurant offers an international menu and wine list. Snacks, cocktails and soft drinks can be enjoyed at Pause lobby bar and Time Out pool bar.

Aetas Bangkok is a 5-minute walk from Phloen Chit BTS Skytrain Station. Suvarnabhumi Airport is a 40-minute ride away.
