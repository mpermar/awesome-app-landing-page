Description

Comfortable stays await at Pantip Suites' spacious accommodation, with its kitchenette and friendly staff.

Featuring an outdoor pool, it provides free Wi-Fi access in all areas. Located in the business district of Sathon, Pantip Suites is a 10-minute drive from Silom Market.

The property is a 15-minute drive to Chulalongkorn Hospital as well as to BHN Hospital.

Shopping malls like Siam Paragon and Siam Centre is a 20-minute drive away. It is a 45-minute drive from Suvarnabhumi International Airport.

Featuring a private balcony, well-furnished suites at Pantip Suites feature a comfortable living area with a sofa set and DVD player.

They are fitted with a safe and ironing facilities. Take in views of tropical landscapes while lounging by the outdoor pool.

Alternatively, guests can relax in the sauna or with a soothing massage after an active workout at the gym.

Café Pantip serves delicious Thai dishes, a full international menu and all-day buffet.
