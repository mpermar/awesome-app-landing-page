Description

Set in a peaceful area of bustling Sukhumvit, Fraser Suites offers spacious rooms with a complete range of services and facilities.

The outdoor pool is designed for leisurely swimming and relaxation. Free WiFi is accessible throughout the property and free parking is available on site.

At Fraser Suites, a buffet breakfast is served daily with options of Halal, vegetarian and international dishes.

Charcoal Tandoor Grill & Mixology offers fresh Indian cuisine and fun dining atmosphere, while Above 11 serves not only delectable Japanese fusion food, but also stunning views of Bangkok's night cityscape.

Boasting city views, all rooms at this property provide guests with more space and comfort.

Each room is equipped with a washing machine, a dish washer and a microwave for guests' convenience.

The fully-fitted kitchen features cooking utensils and cutlery. Flat-screen TVs with international channels and a DVD player are provided in both bedroom and living room.

Most of the en suite bathroom comes with a bathtub and free premium amenities. Guests can indulge in the sauna or workout at the fitness centre.

Kids can also enjoy their time at the kids club. Cleaning service is provided daily.

Complimentary shuttle service will take guests to anywhere nearby such as Nana BTS Station and Bumrungrad International Hospital.

Massage shops and several places to eat are reachable within a walking distance. Arab Street is a 5-minute walk from the property, while Terminal 21 shopping centre is 10-minute drive away.
