Description

Offering free Wi-Fi and free shuttle buses to Chidlom BTS Skytrain Station, Oriental Residence Bangkok combines Eastern designs and modern comforts.

It features an indoor pool and a lounge with floor-to-ceiling glass walls. Featuring large windows, the bright rooms come with kitchens.

Amenities include an iPod dock, a flat-screen TV, and a DVD player. Each room has a marble bathroom with a bathtub and separate shower.

Guests can exercise at the fitness centre, or arrange day trips at the tour desk.
