Description

Less than a 3-minute walk from Phloen Chit BTS Skytrain Station, The Athenee Hotel, A Luxury Collection Hotel, Bangkok is a luxurious 5-star hotel with a tropical lagoon-style pool, 8 options for food and drinks and free WiFi access throughout.

It is centrally located within the embassy district and Pathumwan district, hidden away from the bustling streets.

Featuring Thai colonial designs, rooms and suites are equipped with a 48-inch LED TV with international channels, a stocked minibar and a sofa seating area.

The en suite bathroom includes free toiletries, along with a separate bath and shower.

Guests can enjoy pampering treatments at Spa Athne or stay fit with energising workouts at the 24-hour fitness centre.

Other facilities and services here include a well-equipped business centre, steam rooms, shuttle services and laundry.

A mouth-watering spread of international, French, Chinese, Thai and Japanese dishes can be sampled at the 5 dining venues.

Highlights include buffets and a signature breakfast at The Rain Tree Caf, traditional Thai dishes at Smooth Curry and modern French cuisine at The Reflexions.

The hotel also has 2 bars and a coffee shop. The Athenee Hotel, A Luxury Collection Hotel, Bangkok is within a short walk of the citys premier shopping malls and restaurants, including Central World Plaza and Central Embassy.

The famous Erawan Shrine is also a 1 km walk away. The hotel is approximately a 40-minute drive from Suvarnabhumi International Airport.
