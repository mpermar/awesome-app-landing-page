Description

Situated within a prestigious residential area, Marriott Executive Apartments Mayfair Bangkok provides 5-star residences with full kitchen facilities and home entertainment systems.

A rooftop salt-water pool and a 24-hour gym are available. Free Wi-Fi is available in the public area.

Choose from 1-3 bedroom apartments, all equipped with a flat-screen TV, DVD player and dedicated work spaces.

In-room comforts include a bathtub, bathrobes and fine linen. Each room comes with different layout and furniture.

Marriott Executive Apartments Mayfair Bangkok is less than a 10-minute walk from Chitlom BTS Skytrain Station and Ratchadamri BTS Skytrain Station.

Shopping at Central World Plaza and Central Chidlom are a 10-minute walk away. Parking is free.

Guests can enjoy a massage at the spa, have a relaxing dip in the hot tub or lounge in the sauna.

There is also a children's play area and laundry services. All-day dining with international dishes is available at Bistro M, while Le Boeuf serves steaks and French cuisine.
