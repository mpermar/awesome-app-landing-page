Description

Guest rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with bathrobes and a bidet. Amara Bangkok Hotel has certain rooms with city views, and every room has a electric tea pot. The rooms have a desk.

Breakfast is available each morning, and includes continental and buffet options. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the on-site restaurant, which specializes in Asian cuisine.

Speaking English, Japanese and Thai at the reception, staff are always at hand to help.

MBK Shopping Mall is 1.1 miles from the accommodation. Don Muang Airport is 14 miles away.

Bang Rak is a great choice for travelers interested in friendly locals, convenient public transportation and street food.
