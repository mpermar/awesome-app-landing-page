Description

Bangkok Marriott Hotel Sukhumvit is located 100 metres away from Thonglor BTS Skytrain Station.

It offers modern air-conditioned rooms with a bathtub. Guests can indulge in deep relaxation at the spa or lounge by the outdoor pool.

It features a rooftop bar with panoramic views of Bangkok, as well as restaurants.

Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas. The hotel is a 10-minute drive to Emporium Department Store.

Suvarnabhumi Airport is located 29 km away. Rooms at Marriott Sukhumvit are stylishly furnished with contemporary dcor.

Each room is fitted with a flat-screen cable TV, a minibar and a safety deposit box.

It also comes with ironing facilities. Shower facilities are included in an en suite bathroom.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can assist guests with currency exchange and luggage storage.

The hotel is also equipped with a fitness centre, a business centre and meeting facilities.

Shuttle service can be arranged upon request. Guests can enjoy international and local Thai dishes all day at 57th Street.

The District serves grilled specialities during dinner hours. Drinks are offered at Lobby Lounge and Octave Bar and Lounge.

For pastries and coffee, guests can head to Chocolate Cake Company. Room service is available.
