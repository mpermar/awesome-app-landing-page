Description

Bliston Suwan Park View hotel is a 30-minute drive from Suvarnabhumi International Airport. Popular shopping areas like Central World Plaza and the Pratunam Wholesale Market are about just over half a mile away.

Spacious with hardwood floors, the air-conditioned apartments have a large sitting area. They have well-equipped kitchens and modern home entertainment systems with cable TV. Bathrooms include a bathtub.

Relaxation options at the Bliston Suwan Hotel include a rooftop swimming pool surrounded by lounge chairs and a steam bath. The fitness center provides professional trainers to assist guests. Spa@Tonson specializes in Thai massage.

Providing a fine dining experience, Artur Restaurant features prime steaks, as well as, fresh seafood. The restaurant is well-known for its Caesar salad and extensive martini menu.

The hotel has room service, babysitting and laundry services. Car rentals are also provided for added convenience.

Pathumwan is a great choice for travelers interested in clothes shopping, shopping and food.
