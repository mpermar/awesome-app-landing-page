Description

W Bangkok Hotel is approximately a 10 minute ride to Silom Road and a 40 minute drive from Suvarnabhumi International Airport. The property is located 3 miles from Siam Paragon Shopping Mall and 6 miles from Emerald Buddha Temple and Grand Palace.

Stylish rooms come with a cable flat-screen TV, an iPod docking station and a refrigerator. Private bathrooms come equipped with free toiletries, a bathtub and shower facilities.

Guests can enjoy rejuvenating massage treatments offered at the spa or relax by taking a dip in the pool. Other facilities and services provided are a 24-hour front desk, laundry/dry cleaning and luggage storage.

Restaurant: The Kitchen Table, an all-day dining restaurant serves a variety of international cuisines. Refreshing beverages can be enjoyed at WOOBAR.

Bang Rak is a great choice for travelers interested in friendly locals, convenient public transportation and street food.
