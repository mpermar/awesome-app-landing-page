Description

Located in Bangkok’s business centre, In Residence Bangkok Sukhumvit is 400 metres from Nana BTS Skytrain Station.

This non-smoking hotel features free internet, a rooftop pool and spacious suites with a kitchenette.

Suites at In Residence come with elegant modern interiors and bright wall colours.

Each well-appointed suite has a living area, dining area and en suite bathroom with a bathtub.

Guests can work out at the fitness centre or enjoy a traditional body massage. Staff at the 24-hour front desk can provide car rental and airport shuttle services.

Fax/photocopying services are available in the business centre. The stylish Mauve restaurant serves a spread of international dishes.

Other dining options include refreshing beverages at Asqu bar and fresh pastries at Aromas.

Bangkok In Residence provides free parking, and is a 45-minute drive from Suvarnabhumi International Airport.
