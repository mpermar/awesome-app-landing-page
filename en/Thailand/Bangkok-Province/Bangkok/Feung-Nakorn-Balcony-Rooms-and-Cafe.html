Description

In a peaceful heritage district, Feung Nakorn Balcony Rooms and Caf is a 10-minute walk from Wat Pho. The Grand Palace and Flower Market are just 15 minutes away by foot.

Stylish non-smoking rooms feature classic wood furnishings with antique fittings in some. Select units have a flat-screen TV, personal safe and private bathrooms with toiletries and heated showers.

The open-air New Road Caf is set within a landscaped garden and serves delicious Thai food and freshly brewed Lavazza coffee. At night, it is transformed into a lively bar.

Attentive staff provide a 24-hour front desk and round-the-clock security, and can also offer information on nearby attractions.
