Description

Leave the city behind you and step into the tranquil Nishaville Resort with its luxurious villas and a palm-lined beach at its doorstep.

Set in Huay Yang fishing village, this beachfront resort is only 30 km from Prachuap Khiri Khan city and an hour drive from Hua Hin.

The resort boasts a restaurant. All well-appointed rooms at Nishaville Resort feature a spacious living and dining area, cosy bedrooms, as well as all modern amenities that you would expect from a 5-star resort.

Take a refreshing dip in a swimming pool, unwind in the spa pool with a drink or work out at a fitness centre.

This coastal resort also features gardens and an organic farm, For leisure, there are a number of attractions available in the area, that range from the Wanakorn National Park, to Koh Talu, where you can sign up for snorkelling, scuba-diving and a variety of water-sports.
