Description

Situated in Hua Hin, just a 4-minute walk from Cicada Market, My resort Huahin by D511 offers accommodation with free WiFi.

Guests staying at this apartment have access to a terrace. This air-conditioned apartment is equipped with 2 bedrooms, a cable flat-screen TV, a dining area, and a kitchenette with a microwave and a toaster.

The apartment also has a bathroom. The apartment offers a fitness centre. Guests can spend time in the water park or enjoy the outdoor swimming pool and garden at My resort Huahin by D511.

True Arena Hua Hin is 1. 2 km from the accommodation, while Khao Takiap Temple is 2 km away.
