Description

All rooms in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Rooms include a private bathroom and a bath, while certain rooms come with a patio. All guest rooms will provide guests with a microwave.

Baan Talay51 has a year-round outdoor pool.

The accommodation provides a laundry service, as well as business facilities like fax and photocopying. With staff speaking English and French, guidance is available at the reception.

Hua Hin Clock Tower is a 13-minute walk from Baan Talay51.
