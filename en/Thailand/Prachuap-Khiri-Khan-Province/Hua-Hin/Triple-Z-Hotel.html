Description

All guest rooms at the hotel come with a seating area and a flat-screen TV. Triple Z Hotel has some units that feature a terrace, and rooms have a electric tea pot. The rooms have a desk.

Breakfast is available each morning, and includes buffet, à la carte and American options. Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of Thai dishes.

The property has a fitness center.

With staff speaking English and Thai, advice is available at the reception.

Black Mountain Water Park is 3 miles from the accommodation, while Hua Hin Clock Tower is 3.1 miles away.
