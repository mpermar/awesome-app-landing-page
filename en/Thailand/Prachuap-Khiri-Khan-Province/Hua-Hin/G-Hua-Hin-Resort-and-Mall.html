Description

Just 200 metres from Hua Hin Beach, G Hua Hin Resort & Mall offers spacious air-conditioned rooms with a private balcony.

This resort features an outdoor swimming pool, restaurant and free Wi-Fi. G Hua Hin Resort & Mall is 50 metres from Bangkok Hospital and 250 metres from Hua Hin Market Village.

BlúPort Hua Hin Resort Mall is 750 metres away. It is 1 km from Cicada Market and Khao Takiab.

Hua Hin Railway Station is 2 km away. Parking is free. Cosy rooms come with elegant modern interiors and neutral wall colours.

Each well-appointed room is equipped with a cable TV and seating area. A shower, hairdryer and toiletries are in the en suite bathroom.

Guests can use the facilities at the business centre. Concierge and laundry services are also provided.

Available for all-day dining, Pier 94th serves Thai and European cuisines. In-room dining options are available.
