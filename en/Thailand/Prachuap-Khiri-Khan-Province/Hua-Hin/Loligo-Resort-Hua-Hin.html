Description

Loligo Resort Hua Hin is a modern Thai-style boutique property with tropical surroundings.

It features a outdoor swimming pool and a swim-thru pool bar. Throughout the stay, guests can take advantage of a sun terrace, on-site cafe and free WiFi.

Located near Khao Takieb, the resort is 100-metres from the beach. A few minutes' drive away is Hua Hin town and the famous Night Market.

Each spacious room features a balcony, satellite TV and DVD player. Traditional massage and hot herbal compresses can be arranged at Blue Coral Spa.

Guests can seek assistance from staff at the 24-hour front desk or enjoy working out at the fitness centre.

Meeting facilities are available at this resort as well. Refreshing drinks, sandwiches and light snacks are available all-day at A Caravan.

The Captain, the all-day-dining restaurant, has a wide range of Thai and international food.
