Description

The rooms at the resort are equipped with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a shower. Resort de Paskani has certain rooms with sea views, and the rooms have a electric tea pot. All guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

A buffet, à la carte or American breakfast is available each morning at the property.

You can play darts at this 3-star resort.

For any tips on how to get around or what to do in the area, guests can ask at the reception.

True Arena Hua Hin is 1.4 miles from the accommodation, while Hua Hin Bus Station is 1.9 miles from the property.

Khao Takiab is a great choice for travelers interested in swimming pools, family-friendly trips and kid-friendly beaches.
