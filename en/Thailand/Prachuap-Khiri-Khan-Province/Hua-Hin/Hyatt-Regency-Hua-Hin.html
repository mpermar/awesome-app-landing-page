Description

This luxurious 5-star beachfront property boasts a large lagoon pool set in 12. 5 acres of tropical landscapes.

Stretching across 315 metres of white sandy beach, it houses the award-winning The Barai Spa.

Opening out to tropical or sea views, spacious rooms feature a 32-inch flat-screen TV and iPod dock.

They offer a seating area and a bathroom with a window and bathtub. Guests can lounge by the outdoor pool which boasts a water slide or enjoy workouts at the gym.

Added conveniences include 24-hour concierge service, free Wi-Fi and free parking.

Pet-friendly rooms are also available for an additional charge. Those staying in Club rooms or suites will enjoy extensive facilities and club benefits, including daily continental breakfast, all-day refreshment and evening cocktails in the club lounge.

Housed in a restored 19th century Pavilion, McFarland House offers fine dining and modern European food.

Other dining options include Figs Italian Restaurant and Talay Thai. Just 3 minutes’ walk from Cicada Market, Hyatt Regency Hua Hin is a 5-minute drive from Hua Hin Night Market and Railway Station.
