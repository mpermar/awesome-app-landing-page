Description

Located 400 metres from a beach, Amari Hua Hin offers elegant air-conditioned rooms with a private balcony and free Wi-Fi.

Guests can unwind by the pool or relax with spa treatments. Those who want to stay active can workout at the gym.

Staff are able to converse in English, Thai and Chinese. The hotel is a 10-minute drive from Hua Hin City and Hua Hin Night Market.

It is a 2. 5 hr drive from Bangkok. The property offers airport pick-up and drop-off services at an additional charge.

Rooms at Amari Hua Hin are tastefully furnished with contemporary decor. Each well-appointed room comes with a flat-screen satellite TV with international channels and a DVD player, a minibar and a safety deposit box.

Slippers and an electric kettle are also available. Free toiletries, a hairdryer and a bathrobe are included in an en suite bathroom.

The hotel is equipped with a business centre, meeting facilities and a tour desk.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can assist guests with laundry and shuttle service.

A baby sitting service is also available. The hotel also provides free shuttle service to the beach.

Union Pay is accepted at the property. The lobby offers an array of international newspapers for your convenience.

Breakfast can be enjoyed at Mosaic while seafood specialities are served at Shoreline Beach Club.
