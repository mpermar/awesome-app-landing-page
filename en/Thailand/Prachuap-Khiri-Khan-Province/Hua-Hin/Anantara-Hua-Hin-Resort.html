Description

Luxurious yet relaxing, Anantara Hua Hin Resort offers a beachside escape with 2 outdoor pools and a spa.

Boasting 7 dining options, it also has a tennis court and 24-hour reception. Set amidst 14 acres of landscaped gardens, Anantara Hua Hin is a 15-minute drive from Hua Hin Town and Night Market.

A limousine transfer to Bangkok, which is 3 hours away, can be arranged by the resort.

Blending traditional Thai artwork with modern design, the spacious rooms feature a private balcony or terrace with views of the garden, sea or lagoon.

Each is equipped with a flat-screen TV, DVD player and tea/coffee making facilities.

Guests may start the day early with a yoga class, take a fruit carving or Thai cooking lesson, and end it with a relaxing herbal massage at the spa.

Staff at Anantara's tour desk can also arrange day trips to the Elephant Safari and nearby golf courses.

Issara Cafe serves a full range of international breakfast favourites while Baan Thalia serves modern Italian dishes set in a cosy yet elegant setting.

Grilled specialties featuring fresh seafood along with an extensive list of fine wine can be found at Sai Thong.

For savoury authentic Thai dishes, guests can head over to Rim Nam that overlooks the pool and tropical garden.

Light meals and refreshing drinks can be found at the 2 pool bars and at Sala Siam.
