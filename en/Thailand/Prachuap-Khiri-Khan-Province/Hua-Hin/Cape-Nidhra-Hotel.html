Description

Hotel Cape Nidhra is a 15-minute walk from Chatchai, the Hua Hin night markets. Both Hua Hin Temple and Villa Market are a short walk from the hotel.

Each modern room at Cape Nidhra comes with its own private pool. A 32-inch LCD TV and a minibar are provided in all rooms.

Guests can enjoy traditional Thai massages and spa treatments at the Cape Spa. Free parking and free Wi-Fi are provided at Cape Nidhra Hotel.

The hotel’s restaurant serves local cuisine and seafood. For drinks and remarkable views of Gulf of Thailand, visit On the Rocks Bar.
