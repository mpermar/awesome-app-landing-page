Description

At the hotel, the rooms are equipped with a terrace. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a shower, while certain units at Asira Boutique HuaHin also have a seating area. Guest rooms have a desk.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

Klai Kangwon Palace is a 12-minute walk from Asira Boutique HuaHin, while Hua Hin Clock Tower is a 12-minute walk from the property.
