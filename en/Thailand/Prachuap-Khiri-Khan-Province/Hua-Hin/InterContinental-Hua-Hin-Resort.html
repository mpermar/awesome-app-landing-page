Description

Located along Hua Hin Beach, InterContinental Hua Hin Resort is a luxury resort with a personalized butler service, a state-of-the-art gym, and spa with massage treatments.

It also features a large outdoor pool and 3 dining options. Staff are able to converse in Chinese, Thai, German, French and English.

InterContinental Hua Hin Resort is a 2. 5-hour drive or 35-minute private flight from Bangkok.

Rooms at Intercontinental feature free WiFi, a smart TV, BOSE sound systems, iPod docks and slippers.

Each has a balcony, with select rooms offering breathtaking views of the sea, pool or garden.

Accommodation is split into 2 separate wings, with rooms located in the Beach Wing and in the BluPort Wing, which is located opposite to the Beach Wing area.

The resort has a newsstand, florist and gift shop for guests’ convenience, and a library for a quiet read.

Chinese and Hong Kong newspapers are also available at the lobby. All guests staying the resort are provided with complimentary unlimited access to Vana Nava Water park with transportation provided as well as free unlimited access to Le Colonial Beach Club and Vana Nava Sky.

Traditional Thai cuisine is served at Pirom Restaurant while Azure Restaurant serves Mediterranean seafood with a concept of "farm or sea-to-table".
