Description

Nestled in popular Hua Hin, the oceanfront Mövenpick Asara Resort & Spa Hua Hin offers a memorable getaway with its luxurious accommodation and views of the ocean.

It boasts spectacular landscaped pools within tropical gardens. Mövenpick Asara Resort & Spa Hua Hin is less than 1 km from Hua Hin Airport and a 10-minute drive from Hua Hin's town centre.

The famous Plearnwan shopping area is within 2 km from the hotel. Featuring high ceilings and bay windows, suites at Mövenpick Asara Resort & Spa Hua Hin provide a satellite TV and DVD player, a pillow menu and en-suite bathroom with indoor and outdoor rain forest showers.

Villas feature a private plunge pool with sun deck and private garden. Enjoy a refreshing dip in the resort's beautiful swimming pools, which have a splash zone for children, or in the tropical lagoons.

Guests can also work out at the fitness centre or indulge in a variety of massage therapies at Asara Spa.

Guests can dine at the on-site restaurants offering a delicious buffet spread, breakfast and grilled favourites within tropical gardens.
