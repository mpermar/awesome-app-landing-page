Description

At the resort, every room comes with a desk. Every room includes air conditioning, and certain units at The Yana Villas Hua Hin have a terrace. Guest rooms in the accommodation are equipped with a flat-screen TV and free toiletries.

A continental breakfast is served at the property. Guests can enjoy a meal at the in-house restaurant, which specializes in Italian, seafood and Japanese cuisine.

Speaking Thai and English at the reception, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical guidance on the area.

Hua Hin - Pattaya Ferry is 0.7 miles from The Yana Villas Hua Hin, while Cicada Market is a 20-minute walk from the property.

Khao Takiab is a great choice for travelers interested in swimming pools, family-friendly trips and kid-friendly beaches.
