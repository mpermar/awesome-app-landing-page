Description

Guest rooms at the resort come with a seating area. Each room is equipped with a electric tea pot, while selected rooms also offer a terrace and others also feature sea views. All rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

Ban Krut Resort has an outdoor pool.

Bang Saphan is 12 miles from the accommodation.
