Description

All rooms in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Rooms include a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hair dryer. Guest rooms will provide guests with a shared bathroom with a shower.

A à la carte breakfast is served daily at the property. The restaurant at Cordelia Resort Samroiyod specializes in Thai cuisine.

The accommodation has a playground. Guests at Cordelia Resort Samroiyod will be able to enjoy activities in and around Sam Roi Yot, like cycling.

Khao Daeng View Point is 8 miles from the resort.
