Description

Bungalows at Thai Garden feature their own balcony. Fitted with local rattan and bamboo furnishings, each bungalow is equipped with a private bathroom and a mini-bar.

Overlooking the mountains and greenery, the resort’s restaurant and bar serves à la carte local and international dishes, as well as drinks and snacks.

Aside from a tour desk, Thai Garden Inn offers car rentals and an airport shuttle service. Parking is free.
