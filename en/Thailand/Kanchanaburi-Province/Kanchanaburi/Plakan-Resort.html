Description

All guest rooms at the resort are equipped with a seating area. With a private bathroom equipped with a shower and free toiletries, rooms at Plakan Resort also feature a mountain view. The rooms will provide guests with a closet and a electric tea pot.

A buffet breakfast is available daily at the property.

The accommodation offers a fitness center.
