Description

At the hotel, the rooms include a balcony with a mountain view. The private bathroom is equipped with free toiletries. All guest rooms have a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

A continental, buffet or Asian breakfast is available every morning at the property. Guests can dine in the in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of Thai and American dishes.

The wellness area is comprised of a hot tub and a fitness center. Guests at Natee The Riverfront Hotel Kanchanaburi will be able to enjoy activities in and around Kanchanaburi City, like cycling.

The reception can provide advice on the area in order to help guests plan their day.

Death Railway Museum is 2,100 feet from the accommodation.
