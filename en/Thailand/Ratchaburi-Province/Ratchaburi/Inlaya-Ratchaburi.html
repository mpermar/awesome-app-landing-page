Description

At the resort, every room is equipped with a closet and a flat-screen TV. Featuring a private bathroom, rooms at Inlaya Ratchaburi also feature free WiFi. Guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

An American breakfast is served at the property. Various dining options including Asian and American cuisine are available at the on-site restaurant.

The accommodation has a playground. Cycling and canoeing are among the activities that guests of Inlaya Ratchaburi can find nearby.

Wat Mahathat is 6 miles from the resort, while Ratchaburi National Museum is 6 miles from the property.
