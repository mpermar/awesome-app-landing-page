Description

At the resort, the rooms include a desk. Rooms have a flat-screen TV, and some rooms also feature a pool view. All rooms are equipped with a seating area.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property.

Sawasdee Sukhothai Resort has a playground. The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental is available at the accommodation.

Sukhothai Airport is 17 miles from the property.
