Description

Ndol Streamside Villas is about 16 miles from Khao Yai National Park. Coming down from Bangkok, it will take a 1-hour 30-minute drive.

Furnished with antiques and local artwork, rooms at Ndol are uniquely designed with colorful fabrics and classic hardwood floors. A flat-screen TV and sofa sitting area can be enjoyed in all rooms. A minibar and personal safe are included.

Lazy afternoons can be spent sunbathing by the outdoor pool or relaxing with a massage at Ndol Spa. Quiet time can be spent at the library. The hotel also offers free parking and a tour desk.

Amber Café & Curry serves the hotel’s signature soy-bean ice cream and fruit sorbet. Authentic Thai dishes can be enjoyed at the open-air dining room.
