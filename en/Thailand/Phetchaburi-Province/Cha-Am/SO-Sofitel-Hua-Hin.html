Description

Located in the coastal town of Cha-Am, SO Sofitel Hua Hin is a luxurious beachside resort offering 5-star facilities including a pool with sea views and rooms designed to be personal retreats.

Free WiFi is available. Situated a 30 km drive from Hua Hin's centre, the resort is less than a 15-minute drive Santorini Park and a 7-minute drive from Cha-Am's centre.

Bangkok is approximately a 2. 5 hour drive north of the property. SO Sofitel Hua Hin features stylish Thai furnishings and spacious interiors.

Each room features a home theatre system and a 42-inch Plasma TV. The en suite bathroom comes with luxurious toiletries.

Guests can relax at the resort's So Pool next to the beachfront, while adults can enjoy privacy and tranquillity at the Solarium Pool.

Alternatively, pampering treatments are available at SoSpa. The 24-hour fitness centre, SoFit, also has personalised programs.

18 hole golf course is available at SO Wonderland. A 2. 2 km bicycle track with bicycle rental is also offered.

Choose from 3 signature restaurants at the resort, with each offering different experiences.

White Oven serves an international breakfast buffets, along with authentic Thai cuisine.

Sea views can be enjoyed from the panoramic rooftop bar at HI-SO. Guests can also sip on cocktails created by mixologists at Beach Society.
