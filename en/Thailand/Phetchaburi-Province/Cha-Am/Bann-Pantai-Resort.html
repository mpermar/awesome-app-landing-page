Description

Rooms offer spacious and stylish interiors and come with large windows that allow the fresh ocean breeze in. Each room has a flat-screen cable TV, minibar and a safe. Bathrobes and slippers are provided.

Resort Bannpantai provides a restaurant which serves local specialties. Meals can be enjoyed in the privacy of guests’ rooms.

The resort boasts a well-equipped fitness center. Other recreational activities at the resort include diving and horseback riding. Guests can also make use of the bike rental to explore the area.

The Bannpantai is surrounded by tropical grounds and is a 20-minute drive from Hua-Hin and the airport. Free parking is also available.
