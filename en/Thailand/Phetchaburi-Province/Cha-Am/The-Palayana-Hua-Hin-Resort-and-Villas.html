Description

Located along the Cha-am coast, The Palayana Hua Hin is a tropical getaway just steps from a private beach area.

The resort features an outdoor infinity pool with sunken bar. Decorated with wooden furnishings and local fabrics, rooms feature a private balcony with a daybed.

Room amenities include a flat-screen TV and DVD player. Beach accessories are provided along with an eco-friendly tote bag.

Guests can enjoy a traditional Thai massage at the spa, or relax on the sun loungers provided on the beach.

The library offers a venue for quiet reading, while the art gallery displays artwork by local artists.

Yoga classes and cooking lessons are available. The hotel provides a shuttle service to the city centre for an additional charge.

ThaiPas Restaurant offers outdoor Mediterranean dining accompanied by pool and garden views.

Snacks and refreshments are available at KangSadan Bar & Lounge, the pool bar, or the deli shop.

The Palayana Hua Hin is around 2 hours and 30 minutes’ drive from Bangkok. Transfers from Bangkok’s Suvarnabhumi International Airport can be arranged at an extra charge.
