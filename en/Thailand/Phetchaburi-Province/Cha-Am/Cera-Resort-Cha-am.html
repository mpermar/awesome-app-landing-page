Description

Located 50 metres from the beachfront, Cera Resort @ Cha-am boasts bright air-conditioned rooms with a private balcony and a flat-screen TV.

It also has a large outdoor pool and free Wi-Fi. On-site parking is available. Spacious and inspired by modern art, tastefully decorated rooms are equipped with a personal safe and free tea/coffee making facilities.

Private bathrooms have a shower and a separate Thai-style ceramic bathtub. Cera Resort @ Cha-am is 5 km from Marukkatayawan Summer Palace and 10 km from Cha-am Beach.

It is 15 km from Spring Field Golf Club and 20 km from Hua Hin Town. Bangkok is a 2-hour drive away.

The White House Restaurant serves Thai and international cuisine, with the option of outdoor seating.

Cocktails and fresh fruit juice, as well as light meals, can be ordered from the Pool Bar.

The resort provides meeting rooms, karaoke facilities and a children's play area.
