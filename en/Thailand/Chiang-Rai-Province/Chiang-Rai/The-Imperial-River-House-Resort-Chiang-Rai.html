Description

Rooms at River House Resort provide stylish furniture, fresh white walls and hardwood floors. Each room provides a 25” flat-screen TV with cable and satellite channels. Rooms are also equipped with tea and coffee makers and bathroom toiletries.

As well as relaxing in the large outdoor swimming pool that overlooks the river, guests can enjoy the hot tub and well-equipped fitness center. At the spa and health club, a range of beauty treatments are offered, as well as relaxing massage therapies.

River House Resort and Spa is just a 15-minute drive from Chiang Rai Airport. For convenience, a free airport shuttle is provided.
