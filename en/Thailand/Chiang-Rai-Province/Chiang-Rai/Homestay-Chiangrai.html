Description

With North Thailand’s hills and mountains as a backdrop, Homestay Chiangrai offers relaxing stays in homely Thai-style rooms.

Featuring tropical gardens, it has an outdoor pool, free Wi-Fi and shuttle service to the city centre.

Boasting scenic views of tropical landscapes, rooms come with wood furnishings and have air conditioning and a ceiling fan.

A TV with international channels is provided. Chiangrai Homestay is situated just a 10-minute drive from Chiang Rai’s city centre and a 15-minute drive from Chiang Rai International Airport.

Free parking is available. For leisure, guests can relax with a soothing massage or take in stunning mountain views at the rooftop terrace.

Alternatively, biking and walking tours can be arranged. Featuring a covered veranda, the resort has a kitchen serves up Thai specialities and Western dishes.
