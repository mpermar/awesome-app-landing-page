Description

Every unit features a private bathroom and bidet, air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and a fridge. Some units include a terrace and/or a balcony with mountain views.

A continental breakfast is available every morning at the property. The restaurant at the homestay serves Asian cuisine.

Na Na Doo Homestay has an outdoor pool.

Guests can also relax in the shared lounge area.

Chiang Rai Saturday Night Walking Street is 2.1 miles from the accommodation, while Clock Tower Chiang Rai is 2.2 miles away. The nearest airport is Chiang Rai International Airport, 4.3 miles from Na Na Doo Homestay.
