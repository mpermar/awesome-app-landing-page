Description

Guest rooms in the resort are equipped with a TV. All rooms have a private bathroom with free toiletries, while some feature mountain views. The rooms at Sanikul resort come with a seating area.

An Asian breakfast is available each morning at the property.

The accommodation has a terrace. Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near Sanikul resort.

Statue of King Mengrai is 22 miles from the resort, while Chiang Rai Saturday Night Walking Street is 23 miles from the property. Chiang Rai International Airport is 19 miles away.
