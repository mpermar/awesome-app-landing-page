Description

Guest rooms at the resort are equipped with a seating area. Each room is equipped with a private bathroom with a bath and free toiletries. The rooms include a TV with cable channels.

A continental or à la carte breakfast is available every morning at the property.

Mae Yao Resort has a terrace. Bike rental and car rental are available at the accommodation and the area is popular for cycling and fishing.

Clock Tower Chiang Rai is 4.1 miles from Mae Yao Resort, while Chiang Rai Saturday Night Walking Street is 4.1 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Chiang Rai International Airport, 6 miles from the property.
