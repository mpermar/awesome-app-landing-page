Description

The air conditioned rooms at Ville Flora come with large windows that offer plenty of natural light. Each room is equipped with a refrigerator and private bathroom with hot shower facilities.

Recreation choices include relaxing in the sauna. The hotel provides airport shuttle services while day trips and sightseeing arrangements can be made at the tour desk.

Guests can enjoy Thai dishes at the restaurant. A wide variety of local snacks and refreshing beverages are also offered.

The Flora is a 30-minute drive from Central Festival Phuket and a 45-minute drive from Phuket International Airport.
