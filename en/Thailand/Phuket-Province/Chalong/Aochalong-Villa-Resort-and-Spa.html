Description

Located along a quiet beach on Chalong Bay, Aochalong Villa Resort and Spa features Asian-inspired rooms with free Wi-Fi and garden, pool or sea views.

It offers a large infinity pool overlooking the sea. Traditional Thai massage services are available.

Aochalong Resort is a 3-minute drive from Ao Chalong Circle and Chalong Pier, which connects to Koh Khai, Coral Island and Phang Nga Bay.

Phuket Town is a 10-minute drive away. Rooms and villas each have a balcony and feature classic wooden furnishings.

A safety deposit box, minibar and fridge are provided. Villas have a separate living and dining area from the bedroom.

The property provides a 24-hour golf cart service from the resort to the main road.

Sightseeing arrangements, car and bicycle rentals can be made at the tour desk. Barbecue facilities are available.

Seafood, Thai and Western dishes can be enjoyed at the beach-side restaurant. Room service is also available 24 hours daily.
