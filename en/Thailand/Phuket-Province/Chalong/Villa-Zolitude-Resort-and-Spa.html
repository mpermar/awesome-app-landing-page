Description

Villa Zolitude Resort & Spa is a 40-minute drive from Phuket International Airport. Wat Chalong Temple is just around 650 feet from the resort. A free shuttle goes to Nai Harn Beach and Central Festival Phuket Department Store.

Featuring a private pool with a spacious deck, each villa at Zolitude has a living room and pantry. Amenities include a flat-screen TV, a free minibar and free Wi-Fi. The bathrooms come with a bathtub and an outdoor shower.

Guests can visit Zense Spa for massages or facial and body treatments. The library offers a collection of DVDs, CDs, reading material and board games.

Drinks and snacks are served at the Coffee Corner. The restaurant features an international, Thai and seafood menu, along with live music.
