Description

Located at the tip of Cape Yamu overlooking the Andaman Sea and Phang Nga Bay, COMO Point Yamu, Phuket features accommodation with interiors designed by an Italian designer Paola Navone.

Offering an outdoor pool, it also houses a spa and wellness centre. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the property.

COMO Point Yamu, Phuket is a 20-minute ride from Bangtao Beach. Phuket International Airport is 27 km away.

Guests who drive can enjoy free on-site parking. Fitted with floor-to-ceiling glass doors, each well-designed unit features air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and a refrigerator.

Most rooms have private terraces with sea or mountain views, while the suites boast a private pool.

Their en suite bathroom comes with shower facilities and a hairdryer. The residence boasts treatment rooms, Thai massage alcoves, plus separate male and female steam rooms, a spa pool and a fitness centre.

Other activities include yoga lessons. Guests can visit nearby attractions with help from the tour desk.

Restaurants here offer a choice of Italian, Thai or healthy cuisines. They also serve local seafood.
