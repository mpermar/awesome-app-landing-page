Description

Overlooking Chalong Bay, Cloud19 is just a 10-minute drive to Phuket Town and offers rooms with a balcony or terrace.

The resort features 2 swimming pools and a spa. Cloud19's air-conditioned rooms offer a cable TV with DVD player, minibar, coffee/tea maker and private bathroom.

Guests can choose to have a swim at the pool with attached hot tub, located near the beach or at the upper level pool with beautiful views of Chalong Bay.

Traditional Thai or oil massage services are available. Activities such as kayaking, snorkelling and mountain biking can also be arranged by Cloud19 Beach Retreat Hotel.

Lainapa Restaurant offers Thai and international dishes. The Sunset Bar offers a variety of beverages.
