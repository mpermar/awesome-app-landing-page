Description

Set in a tropical landscape, the elegant rooms at Panwa Cape hotel have spacious balconies and lounge chairs. A flat-screen TV and DVD player are also provided.

Guests can take a dip in two outdoor swimming pools, or go on a snorkeling trip. Day trips and travel arrangements can be made at the on-site tour desk.

Café Andaman serves Thai and international dishes and fresh seafood is available at Top of the Reef. Light snacks and refreshing beverages can be enjoyed at the beach-side Bamboo Bar.

Hotel Cape Panwa is a 10-minute drive from central Phuket and a 50-minute drive from Phuket International Airport.
