Description

A few steps from Bangtao Beach, Moevenpick Residences Phuket boasts an infinity pool with sunken spa pool and a health club.

Its luxurious accommodation features a kitchen and a balcony. Equipped with living and dining areas, spacious suites at the resort feature garden or lagoon views.

The fully equipped kitchen comes with a microwave oven and a dishwasher, along with a fridge.

Free Wi-Fi access is provided. Moevenpick Residences Bangtao Beach Phuket is a 20-minute drive from Phuket International Airport.

Patong Beach is a 30-minute drive away. Suites also offer a flat-screen TV with DVD player and Bose surround sound system.

Some suites have a private pool or a spa bath. Guests can relax at Blossom Spa or head to the health club, which includes a steam room, sauna and gym.

In addition to a tour desk and a business centre, a games room is available. The Palm Cuisine and Terrace serves Thai and international dishes and seafood specialities.
