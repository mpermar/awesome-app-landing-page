Description

Located 180 m from the centre of Bang Tao Beach, Casuarina Shores offers 24-hour security services, 2 large outdoor pools and 2 children pools.

Guests can sunbathe on sunbeds or party at the on-site bar. Free WiFi and free bicycle rental are also available.

The property is 6. 6 km from Wat Prathong and 8 km from Khao Phra Thaeo National Park.

Phuket Airport is located 13 km away. Comfortable units are fitted with a living room, a fully-equipped kitchen and dining area.

There are flat-screen cable TVs, safety deposit box and washing machine. Shower facilities and free toiletries are included in en suite bathrooms.

The property features a free parking, a sauna and a fitness centre. Guests are offered with unlimited bottled water and beach towels.

Landline telephone (free Thailand calls), choice of mattresses and pillows including memory foam.
