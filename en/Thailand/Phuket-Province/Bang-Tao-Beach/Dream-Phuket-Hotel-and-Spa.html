Description

Featuring a tropical outdoor pool, Dream Phuket Hotel & Spa is located in Bang Tao, and is a 4 km drive from the beach.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. Free scheduled shuttles to Dream Beach Club are also provided.

It is a short drive from restaurants and Laguna Phuket Golf Club. Phuket International Airport is a 25-minute drive away.

All modern rooms are air-conditioned and feature a flat-screen TV. The en suite bathroom has a bath or shower.

Private villas are also available. Guests staying at Dream Phuket Hotel & Spa can make use of the fitness room, order dishes at the restaurant or enjoy a pampering treatment at the spa.

Laundry, transfer and tour services are available. There is free private parking on-site.
