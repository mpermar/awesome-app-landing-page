Description

Offering an outdoor pool and views of the sea, Cassia Phuket is located in Bang Tao Beach, 1 km from Xana Beach Club.

The hotel has a sun terrace, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar. Free WiFi is available throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

All suites come with a separate bedroom, living area and has a private balcony. The kitchen is fully equipped with all the utensils, perfect for indoor cooking.

Hot shower facilities are included in the private bathroom. Housekeeping services are provided once every two days.

There is also free shuttle service within the area and a 24-hour front desk service.

An all day dining venue with an awesome view, Vista Gastro - Pub, serves coffee, drinks, snacks and other meals.

A buffet breakfast is available at the restaurant. For more grocery shopping and dining options, guests can also head over to Villa Market and Boat Avenue, located approximately 1 km from the property.
