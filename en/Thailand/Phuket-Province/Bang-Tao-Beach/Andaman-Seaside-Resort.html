Description

Located just 100 metres from Bangtao Beach, Andaman Seaside Resort offers free Wi-Fi, free parking, a swimming pool and traditional Thai massage.

Rooms feature a private balcony, while villas offer direct access to the pool. They are fitted with a minibar, coffee/tea maker, cable TV and safe.

Room service is provided. Andaman Seaside Resort has a tour desk and courts for volleyball and badminton.

Water sports such as snorkelling and sailing are available by the beach. Fusion Food Restaurant serves a range of international and local dishes.

Andaman Seaside Resort is within walking distance to a variety of restaurants. Patong is a 20-minute drive away.
