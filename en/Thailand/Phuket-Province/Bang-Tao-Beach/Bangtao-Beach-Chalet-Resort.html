Description

Chalets at Bangtao Beach Resort come with traditional Thai décor and have plenty of natural light. Each chalet is well equipped with a TV, refrigerator and private bathroom with bathtub.

Guests can enjoy a Thai massage or relax by the pool. Taking a walk on the grounds is another good recreational choice. For convenience, the hotel provides laundry services upon request.

Dirham Halal Restaurant serves a spread of Thai and international dishes. Light snacks and refreshing beverages are also offered.

Resort Bangtao is 8 miles from Patong Beach and Phuket. Phuket Airport is 16 miles from the resort.
