Description

Phuket Town is approximately a 20-minute drive from The Naka Phuket. A 40-minute drive takes guests to Phuket International Airport.

Stylish villas are tastefully decorated and offer modern facilities such as a flat-screen cable TV, a refrigerator and a safety box. Private bathrooms come with a bathtub and shower facilities.

Guests of The Naka Phuket can laze by the pool or enjoy pampering massage treatments at the spa. For convenience, the residence offers a 24-hour front desk, a tour desk and meeting facilities.

For meals, sample a variety of both Thai and international cuisines served at The Wiwa, an all-day dining restaurant located just 5 steps away from the beach. With particular views of the sea and the sunset, The Nana offers refreshing beverages until late night. The Meka provides guests with a wide range of healthy dishes and drinks.
