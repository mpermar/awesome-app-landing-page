Description

Paresa Resort offers luxury and elegantly appointed villas and suites with private infinity pools and views. Suites feature large bathrooms, luxury linens, iPod stations, and LCD TVs and villas feature private bars with espresso machines.

Guests can enjoy Italian dishes at Diavolo or head to Talung Thai for traditional Thai dishes.

Paresa offers a Thai spa with a variety of massage treatments and Thai cooking classes. Outdoor pools include Infinity Pools and Energy Pools. Other facilities include a well-equipped gym, a library and private gallery.
