Description

All guest rooms at the resort come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a kitchenette, a dining area and a private bathroom with free toiletries, a bidet and a hair dryer. Each room is equipped with air conditioning, and selected rooms here will provide you with a balcony. The rooms feature a desk.

A continental breakfast is served at the property. There's an in-house restaurant, which specializes in American cuisine.

Old Phuket Town is 8 miles from The Woods Grand Natural Park Phuket, while Premium Outlet Phuket is 6 miles away. Phuket Airport is 11 miles from the property.
