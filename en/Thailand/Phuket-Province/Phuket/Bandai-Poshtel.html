Description

Free WiFi is available to all guests, while some rooms also feature a balcony.

A continental, buffet or Asian breakfast is available daily at the property.

Bike rental and car rental are available at this hostel and the area is popular for cycling.

Thai Hua Museum is 0.4 miles from the hostel. The nearest airport is Phuket Airport, 17 miles from the property.
