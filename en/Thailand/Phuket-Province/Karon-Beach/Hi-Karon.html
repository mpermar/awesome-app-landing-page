Description

Hi Karon features a restaurant, bar, a shared lounge and garden in Karon Beach. Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a terrace.

The accommodation offers evening entertainment and room service. The units in the hotel are equipped with a kettle.

At Hi Karon all rooms are equipped with a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

The accommodation offers a continental or à la carte breakfast. Starbucks Kata Beach is 1.

2 km from Hi Karon, while Karon Night Market is 1. 3 km away. The nearest airport is Phuket International, 48 km from the hotel, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
