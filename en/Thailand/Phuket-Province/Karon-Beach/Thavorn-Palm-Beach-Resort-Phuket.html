Description

Enjoying a beachfront location on Karon Beach, Thavorn Resort offers rooms with private balconies on its 25 acres of landscaped grounds.

It provides 4 swimming pools, a fitness centre and 3 dining options. Free WiFi access is available in all areas.

Thavorn Palm Beach Resort is a 10-minute drive from Patong Beachs popular entertainment hub.

It takes 30 minutes by car to Phuket Town and around 40 minutes from Phuket International Airport.

The charming rooms are spacious and feature Thai-style dcor and furnishings. Nestled among tropical gardens, rooms offer plenty of natural light and great views.

Each room comes en suite bathroom. Guests can enjoy views of the sea, steak and Italian dishes at Ciao Bistro Italian Restaurant.

Located next to a lotus pond, Windsong Caf is the perfect spot for a tranquil breakfast.

The resort offers invigorating Thai massage services by the pool and a state-of-the-art fitness centre.
