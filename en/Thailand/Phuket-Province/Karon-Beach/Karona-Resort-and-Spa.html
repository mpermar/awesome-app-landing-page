Description

Located on Karon Beach is beautiful Karona Resort and Spa. It provides a 2-level free-form pool and tropical-themed rooms with private balconies and views of the ocean or landscaped garden.

The entire resort is decorated with traditional ceramic art pieces. Rooms are spacious and have colourful walls and warm lighting.

They all come with private bathroom facilities and a seating area. A minibar is provided.

Following the rainforest theme, the swimming pool’s waterfall pours onto tropical plants located on the second pool level.

The Karona Resort and Spa also provides luxurious massages and body treatments like aromatherapy.

CHILIES Restaurant specialises in fresh seafood dishes. It features a local and international menu.

Guests can also make use of the barbecue facilities located next to the pool. The Resort is a 5-minute walk from Karon Beach.

It takes 20 minutes by car to reach downtown Phuket, while Phuket Airport is a 50-minute drive away.
