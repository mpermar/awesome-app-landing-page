Description

The luxurious 5-star Mövenpick Resort and Spa Karon Beach is located in South-West Phuket.

This tropical sanctuary offers 4 outdoor pools, 7 restaurants and bars and a well equipped Spa.

Karon Mövenpick Resort is just 15 minutes by car from popular Patong Beach and Jungceylon Shopping Centre.

Phuket International Airport is 30 km away. The spacious units at Mövenpick Resort Phuket feature modern Thai décor and beautiful views of the sea or garden.

A satellite TV, minibar, and a safety deposit box are included. Rooms also come with air conditioning and a refrigerator.

The Spa at Mövenpick features relaxing massage treatments. Entertainment options like a DVD lounge and Playstations await at The Play Zone.

The Game Lounge offers snooker and table tennis tables. All-day dining and a breakfast buffet can be enjoyed at Pacifica Restaurant.

The beachfront Café Studio serves gourmet desserts and coffee. Other cuisines available at El Gaucho and Orient Asia include Brazilian, Thai and Indian cuisines.
