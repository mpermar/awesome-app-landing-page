Description

Spacious rooms feature modern Asian décor and balconies with garden, pool or sea views. A DVD player, safe and tea and coffee-making facilities are standard in all rooms. Free Wi-Fi access is available throughout the property.

The resort provides a free scheduled shuttle bus service to Patong Beach, 15 minutes away. Horizon Karon Beach Resort and Spa is 12 miles from Phuket Town.

Guests can stay active at the fitness center, grab a snack at the mini-mart or dine at one of the two restaurants. A variety of European and Asian dishes are featured at Border Town Restaurant. The award-winning Baluchi Restaurant serves Indian food.

Relaxing massages are provided by Sontaya Spa. Other recreation options include a game room, library and DVD rentals.
