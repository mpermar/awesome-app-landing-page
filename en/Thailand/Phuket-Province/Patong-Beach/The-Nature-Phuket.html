Description

Boasting an outdoor swimming pool, fitness centre, garden and free WiFi, The Nature Phuket is located 2 minutes' drive from Patong Beach, 3.

3 km from Jungceylon Shopping Center and 3. 3 km from Patong Boxing Stadium. In a prime location in the Kalim Beach district, this hotel provides a terrace and a restaurant.

The hotel has a sauna and free shuttle service. At the hotel, rooms include a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

The Nature Phuket features certain units with sea views, and rooms come with a balcony.

The units will provide guests with a fridge. The accommodation offers a buffet or American breakfast.

Guests can make sightseeing and ticketing arrangements at the tour desk, or conduct business at the business centre.

Around-the-clock assistance is available at the reception. Phuket Simon Cabaret is 5 km from The Nature Phuket, while Bangla Road is 5 km away.
