Description

Novotel Phuket Resort is located along Kalim Bay, a 10-minute walk from the shopping and nightlife at Patong. It is less than 1.2 mi from popular Soi Bangla and Jungceylon Shopping Center.

Decorated with a mix of contemporary design and traditional Thai decor, spacious air-conditioned rooms feature a private balcony and 24-hour room service. Each is equipped with a flat-screen TV, mini-bar and personal safe.

Guests may relax with a massage at the spa, exercise at the fitness center or enjoy a game of tennis. The resort also provides a business center and laundry services.

All day breakfast with Asian and Western specialties can be found at Coffee House while fine Thai cuisine is served at Ruen Thong. Cocktails and live music are available at The Hourglass and Le Mirage Pool Bar.
