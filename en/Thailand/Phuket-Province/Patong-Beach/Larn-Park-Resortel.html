Description

Larn Park Resortel offers modern accommodation that is a 5-minute walk from Patong Beach.

It offers affordable rooms with an outdoor pool, free parking and free internet at the lobby.

Air-conditioned guestrooms at Larn Park are equipped with cable TV, a fridge and tea/coffee making facilities.

Each room has a private bathroom with shower and bathroom amenities. Resortel Larn Park provides safety deposit services at the 24-hour front desk.

Airport transfer is available upon request. To relax, guests can arrange for massages.

Larn Park Resortel is a 2-minute walk from Jungcylon Shopping Centre and a 3-minute walk from the Boxing Stadium.
