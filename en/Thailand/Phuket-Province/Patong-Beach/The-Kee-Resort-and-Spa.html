Description

Located a 5-minute walk from Patong Beach, Kee Resort is 12 mi to Phuket Town and 28 mi to Phuket International Airport.

Fitted with tiled flooring, modern rooms at Kee Resort feature warm lighting. Equipped with air conditioning and a large private bathroom, each room also includes a daybed and a mini-bar. Room service is provided.

Serving both local and international dishes, Kee Kitchen opens all day and comes with a fully stocked bar and an extensive wine list. Delicious cocktails and snacks can be enjoyed with a stunning sunset view in the background at the rooftop Kee Sky Lounge. For a more relaxed and casual setting, Kee Sino Lounge offers light snacks and a variety of specialty coffees and teas.

Guests at the resort can exercise at the fitness center or relax at the spa. Alternatively, they can enjoy a quiet read at the library.

Kee Resort also features a tour desk, souvenir shop and a games room. Free parking is provided. Wi-Fi is free.
