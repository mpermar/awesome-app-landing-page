Description

Grand Mercure Phuket Patong is 600 metres from Bangla Road. It offers air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi.

The property features a pool and a fitness centre. The hotel is 300 metres from Patong Beach and Phuket International Airport is 40 km away.

Rooms come with a flat-screen cable TV and refrigerator. Shower facilities are included in an en suite bathroom.

Free toiletries and hairdryer are provided. Some room types also have a balcony. Staff at the 24-hour front desk can assist guests with laundry and luggage storage.

Sightseeing trips can be arranged at the tour desk. Guests can enjoy Thai and European cuisines at the restaurant.
