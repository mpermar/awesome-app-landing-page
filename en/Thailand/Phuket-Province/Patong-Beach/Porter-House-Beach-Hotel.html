Description

Just 30 metres from Patong Beach, Porter House Hotel features cosy rooms with modern Lanna décor.

This property has an outdoor swimming pool and restaurant. Wi-Fi and parking are free.

Porter House Hotel is 1 km from Jungceylon Shopping Centre and a 30-minute drive from Phuket Town.

Phuket International Airport is a 45-minute drive away. Tastefully decorated, the beautiful guest rooms are bathed in natural sunlight.

Each unit is equipped with a cable TV, fridge and private balcony. Shower facilities are in the attached bathroom.

Available for all-day dining, the restaurant offers a selection of Thai and European specialities.

Room service is available upon request. Staff at the tour desk can make arrangements for day trips and travel bookings.
