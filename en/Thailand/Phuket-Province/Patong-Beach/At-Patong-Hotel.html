Description

Located in Patong Beach, At Patong Hotel offers convenient accommodation with free WiFi.

Guests can enjoy the restaurant on site. Jungceylon Shopping Center is a 10-minute walk away.

Other facilities include an outdoor pool and a fitness centre. Each room at this property features a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

The private bathroom comes with a shower Staff are available for 24 hour at the front desk.

Car rental service is also provided at this property. Bangla Road is 700 metres from At Patong Hotel, while Patong Boxing Stadium is 700 metres away.
