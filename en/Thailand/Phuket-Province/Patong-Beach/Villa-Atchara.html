Description

Housed in a classic white mansion, Villa Atchara offers well-furnished Thai-style rooms with free Wi-Fi and daily breakfast.

It features a landscaped pool and is within an easy 3-minute stroll to Patong Beach.

The air-conditioned rooms feature warm colour schemes and classic wood furnishings.

They are provided with a cable TV, minibar and a safe. A heated shower and hairdryer are available.

Leisurely afternoons can be spent at the garden or on a day trip arranged by the tour desk.

Laundry and shuttle services can be provided. Hearty Thai and international meals can be served in the restaurant or by the pool.

There is also a bar and room service. Located on Phukets lively West Coast, Atchara Villa is a 15-minute walk from the lively Bangla Road and a 10-minute drive from Karon Beach.
