Description

Set in Patong Beach, 1. 3 km from Patong Boxing Stadium, The Villa Residences Resort offers accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre.

Each accommodation at the 3-star hotel has garden views, and guests can enjoy access to a bar and to a garden.

The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and free WiFi throughout the property.

All guest rooms come with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a fridge, a kettle, a shower, a hairdryer and a wardrobe.

At the hotel, the rooms are equipped with a private bathroom with free toiletries.

Guests at The Villa Resedences Resort can enjoy a continental breakfast. The accommodation offers 3-star accommodation with a sauna and barbecue.

You can play billiards at The Villa Residences Resort, and the area is popular for hiking.

The hotel provides an ironing service, as well as business facilities like fax and photocopying.

Popular points of interest near The Villa Resedences Resort include Jungceylon Shopping Center, Phuket Simon Cabaret and Banzaan Fresh Market.

The nearest airport is Phuket International Airport, 40 km from the accommodation.
