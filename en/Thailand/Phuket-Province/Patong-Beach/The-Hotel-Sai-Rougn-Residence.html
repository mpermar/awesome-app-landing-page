Description

Overlooking Patong Bay, Sai Roung Residence is located on Phuket Island. It features an outdoor swimming pool, 2 dining options and air-conditioned rooms with a private balcony.

Free Wi-Fi and parking are provided. Guests enjoy free shuttle service around Patong Beach.

Residence Sai Roung is 250 metres from Simon Cabaret and 1 km from Patong Beach. Phuket International Airport is a 45-minute drive from the hotel.

Set amidst a tropical landscape, rooms at Sai Roung are fitted with wooden furnishings.

Each is equipped with cable TV, refrigerator and private bathroom with shower facilities.

Guests can enjoy a relaxing body massage or play a game of pool. The hotel provides shuttle services to Patong Beach.

Travel arrangements can also be made at the tour desk. The open-air restaurant serves a variety of western dishes.
