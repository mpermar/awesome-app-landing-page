Description

Air-conditioned rooms at Duangjitt Resort feature private balconies and private bathrooms. Each room comes with satellite TV, a safe, and tea/coffee-making facilities. Some rooms have a flat-screen TV or a bathtub.

Occupying 51,600 ft², Wimarn Spa features a spa bathtub, a sauna, and spa villas. Garden views accompany workouts in the fitness center. The resort provides tour arrangements, currency exchange, and laundry services.

Buffet breakfast is served daily. Both Banburee Restaurant and Thomthong Restaurant offer Thai, Asian and international dishes. Drinks and live entertainment are available at the Lobby Lounge.

Duangjitt Resort and Spa is a 25 minute drive from Phuket Town. Phuket International Airport is a 45 minute drive away.
