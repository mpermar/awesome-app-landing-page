Description

Set on the outskirts of Patong and less than 3 km from the city centre, Lets Phuket @ Absolute Twin Sands Resort & Spa is a short walk from Tri Trang Beach.

The resort features an outdoor pool, spa and sauna. Guests can also enjoy drinks at the bar and sample Mediterranean and Asian flavours at the on-site restaurant.

Every room at this resort is air conditioned and is equipped with a flat-screen TV, blu-ray player, and DVD player.

Some units include a seating area where you can relax. Enjoy a cup of tea from your terrace or balcony.

Extras include free toiletries and a hair dryer. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property.

Bangla Road is 2. 7 km from Lets Phuket @ Absolute Twin Sands Resort & Spa, while Jungceylon Shopping Centre is 2.

8 km from the property. The nearest airport is Phuket Airport, 25 km from Lets Phuket @ Absolute Twin Sands Resort & Spa.
