Description

Offering a restaurant and a fitness centre, The Beach Heights Resort is only a 6-minute walk from Kata Beach.

Free WiFi access is available with rooms coming in 4 styles that represent different Thai regions.

Rooms here will provide you with a flat-screen TV, air conditioning and a balcony.

Complete with a refrigerator, the dining area also has an electric kettle. Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Extras include a minibar, a seating area and satellite channels. At The Beach Heights Resort you will find a 24-hour front desk and a garden.

Other facilities offered include an outdoor pool and a tour desk. The property offers free parking.

The hotel is 3 km from Kata View Point and 1. 5 km from The Big Buddha. Phuket International Airport is 32 km away.
