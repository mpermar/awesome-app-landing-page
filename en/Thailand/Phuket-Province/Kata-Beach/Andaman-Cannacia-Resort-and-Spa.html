Description

Sitting atop a hill overlooking the Andaman Sea, Andaman Cannacia Resort provides a free shuttle service to nearby Kata Beach.

This resort has a beautiful outdoor pool and pampering spa services. Fully air-conditioned, spacious rooms feature modern Thai decor and a private balcony or terrace.

Each room has a satellite TV, fully stocked minibar and a personal safe. Selected rooms have outdoor hot tubs and bathrobes.

Andaman Cannacia Resort & Spa is a 20-minute drive from Patong Beach and a 30-minute drive from Phuket Town.

Phuket International Airport is a 45-minute drive from the resort. Free parking is available.

Santi Spa provides traditional Thai massage, floral baths and aloe vera body wraps.

Guests can also practise yoga in the landscaped gardens, or exercise at the gym. Le CoCo Restaurant and Sunset Terrace both serve Thai and international dishes.
