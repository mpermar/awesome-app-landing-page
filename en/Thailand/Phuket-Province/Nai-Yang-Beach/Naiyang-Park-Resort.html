Description

All guest rooms at the resort are equipped with a seating area and a flat-screen TV. The private bathroom is equipped with free toiletries. The rooms at Naiyang Park Resort have air conditioning and a desk.

An American or Asian breakfast is available daily at the property.

The nearest airport is Phuket Airport, 1.2 miles from the property.
