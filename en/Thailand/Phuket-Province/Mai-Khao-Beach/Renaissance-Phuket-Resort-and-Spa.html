Description

Renaissance Phuket Resort & Spa offers luxurious accommodation on the white sands of Mai Khao Beach.

It features a large outdoor pool, jogging and biking trails, and 5 restaurants. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

Floor-to-ceiling window allows plenty of natural daylight. The luxury bathroom features a separate bathtub and walk-in shower with premium bath amenities.

Pool Villas have a private swimming pool for utmost privacy. A range of recreation activities are available for all ages including yoga classes, pilates, aqua aerobics, Thai boxing and bike tours.

Loca Vore Restaurant serves breakfast and an a-la-carte menu. Guest can also enjoy delicious Thai fare at Takieng Restaurant.

Renaissance Phuket Resort & Spa is 19 km from Phuket International Airport. Shuttle services to Patong Beach and Phuket Town can be arranged at an additional charge.
