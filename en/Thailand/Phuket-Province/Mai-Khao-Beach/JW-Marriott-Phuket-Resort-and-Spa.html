Description

Providing a secluded getaway, JW Marriott Phuket Resort and Spa offers direct access to the unspoiled beach area of Mai Khao Beach.

It features 3 outdoor pools and 11 dining options. Free parking is provided. Marriott Phuket Resort is 17 km from Phuket International Airport.

Shuttle services to Patong Beach and Phuket Town can be arranged at an extra charge.

Soundproofed rooms at Marriott Resort have private balconies/terraces with views of the garden, pool or ocean.

Each is equipped with a flat-screen cable TV and DVD player. A minibar and tea/coffee maker are also provided.

For guests' convenience, there is a 24-hour room service. Guests can choose from a wide range of recreational activities, such as yoga and cooking classes and Muay Thai.

Guests can also enjoy a visit to the on-site turtle shelter, an award-winning spa as well as a 24-hour gym and games room.

Buffet breakfast is served at Marriott Caf√©, which also has an all-day international menu.

Gourmet sandwiches and pastries can be enjoyed at Siam Deli. Other restaurants include Thai, Japanese, Italian and a steakhouse.
