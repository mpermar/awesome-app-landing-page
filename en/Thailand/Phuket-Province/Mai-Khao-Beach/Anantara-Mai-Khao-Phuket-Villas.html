Description

Located on Mai Khao Beach and partially bordered by Sirinath National Park, Anantara Mai Khao Phuket Villas boasts a beachfront infinity pool, 5 dining options, and villas with private pools and sundecks.

Anantara Mai Khao Phuket Villas is a 15-minute drive from Phuket International Airport.

Patong Beach and Phuket Town are both 30 minutes’ drive away. Each villa features an espresso machine and a personal wine cellar.

A flat-screen TV, DVD player, and BOSE sound system are provided for guests’ entertainment.

The bathroom provides a terrazzo bathtub big enough for two. Guests can relax on the private beach, or enjoy a massage in the spa’s private treatment suites.

Resort activities include yoga, aerobics and Thai cooking classes. Water sports facilities are also provided.

A breakfast buffet with live cooking stations is offered at La Sala restaurant, which also serves international buffets.

The beachside Sea. Fire. Salt. specialises in grilled seafood, while cocktails are available at the Tree House.
