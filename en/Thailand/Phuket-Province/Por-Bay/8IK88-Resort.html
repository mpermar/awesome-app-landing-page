Description

All guest rooms at the resort are equipped with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a bath. All rooms include air conditioning, and certain rooms at 8IK88 Resort have a terrace. Guest rooms have a desk.

A à la carte breakfast is available every morning at the accommodation. The restaurant at 8IK88 Resort serves local cuisine.

The resort has an outdoor pool.

The nearest airport is Phuket Airport, 9 miles from the property.
