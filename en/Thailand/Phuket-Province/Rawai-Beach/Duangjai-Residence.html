Description

Situated only a 10-minute drive from the white and sandy Nai Harn Beach, Duangjai Residence offers a year-round outdoor pool and modern one-bedroom apartments.

Free WiFi is offered all throughout the property. The fully furnished apartments all comes with a separate bedroom and a living/dining area equipped with a Western kitchen, a dining table and a comfortable sofa.

Shower facilities, a hairdryer and free toiletries are included in the private bathroom.

Fitted with a private balcony with pool views, some units also features views of the mountain and the famous Big Buddha.

The helpful staff can assist guests with arrangements for shuttle services to the nearby attractions, laundry services and a relaxing Thai massage, all for an extra charge.

For those who want to explore the area by themselves, both car and motorbike rentals are also available.

At Duangjai Residence, guests can enjoy both Thai and international dishes at the on-site restaurant.

The pool bar serves refreshments and light snacks. Room service is also available.

The property is a 10-minute drive from Chalong Pier and 30-minutes away from Patong Beach.

Convenience stores can be found nearby. Phuket International Airport is approximately 1-hour away.
