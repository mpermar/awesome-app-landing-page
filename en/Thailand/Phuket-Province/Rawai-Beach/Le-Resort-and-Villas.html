Description

Located in Rawai Beach, 2. 9 km from Nai Harn Beach, Le Resort and Villas provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, free bikes and an outdoor swimming pool.

Among the various facilities are a fitness centre, a bar, as well as a garden. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and free WiFi throughout the property.

At the hotel, the rooms have a patio with a garden view. At Le Resort and Villas each room comes with a seating area.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental or a buffet breakfast. Le Resort and Villas offers a range of wellness facilities including a sauna and a hot tub.

The area is popular for cycling, and car hire is available at the hotel. Windmill Viewpoint is 3.

6 km from Le Resort and Villas, while Promthep Cape is 5 km away. The nearest airport is Phuket International Airport, 47 km from the accommodation.
