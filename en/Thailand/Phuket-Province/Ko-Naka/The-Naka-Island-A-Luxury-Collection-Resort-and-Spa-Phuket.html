Description

The Naka Island, A Luxury Collection Resort & Spa, Phuket is located on an exotic island of the Andaman Sea.

Nestled in a tropical landscape, the resort is just 10 minutes away by speedboat from Ao Por Grand Marina.

It offers luxurious villas with a private pool. Equipped with a private beach, a gym and a world-class spa centre, guests can enjoy free round-trip boat transfer.

Ao Por Grand Marina is a 25-minute drive from Phuket International Airport. It is 20 km from Heroines Monument and 25 km from Phuket Town.

Patong Beach is 45 km away. Elegantly furnished with contemporary decor, stylish air-conditioned villas at The Naka Island Resort & Spa feature an outdoor pavilion with day bed.

Rooms come with a 32-inch flat-screen cable TV and a DVD player. An open-air bathroom has a separate shower and outdoor soaking tub.

Free newspaper and bottled water are provided. Staff at the concierge service can arrange various recreational activities for guests such as snorkelling, windsurfing and scuba diving.

Other activities are cooking classes, fitness classes and culture tour. The property also has a freshwater pool, library and free rental bicycles.

My Grill offers the freshest seafood and juiciest steaks with a local twist. Tonsai serves scrumptious dishes of Thai and international cuisines.

An extensive list of cocktails can be enjoyed at Z Bar. Room service is available 24 hours.
