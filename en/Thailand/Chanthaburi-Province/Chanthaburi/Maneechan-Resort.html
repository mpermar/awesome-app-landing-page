Description

Located in the town of Chanthaburi, Maneechan Resort offers affordable accommodation with free Wi-Fi.

It also features a large outdoor swimming pool and a sports club with a fitness room.

Rooms are spacious and feature a balcony with a view of the garden. They are equipped with air conditioning, tea/coffee maker, minibar and attached bathroom with amenities and hairdryer.

Maneechan Resort's sports club also features a spa bath, sauna and spa. Besides that it also has a golf putting green and has a Playzone for children.

Guests can enjoy a wide variety of Thai dishes at Maneechan Resort's restaurant. It also has a beer garden with live entertainment.

Maneechan Resort is near various tourist attractions, including Wat Pai Lom Temple, King Taksin Shrine and the Cathedral of Immaculate Conception.
