Description

Guest rooms in the resort are equipped with a electric tea pot. All rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV, and some accommodations at Doi Inthanon Riverside resort have a terrace. All guest rooms have a private bathroom.

Buffet and à la carte breakfast options are available each morning at the accommodation.

Doi Inthanon Riverside resort has a year-round outdoor pool.

The nearest airport is Chiang Mai Airport, 30 miles from the property.
