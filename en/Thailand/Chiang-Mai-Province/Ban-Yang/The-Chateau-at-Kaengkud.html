Description

At the resort, each room has a terrace with a mountain view. Every room has a coffee machine, while certain rooms come with a kitchen with a toaster and a fridge. All rooms include a desk.

The daily breakfast offers buffet and à la carte options.

Cycling and hiking are among the activities that guests of The Chateau at Kaengkud can find nearby.

Pai is 28 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Chiang Mai Airport, 32 miles from the property.
