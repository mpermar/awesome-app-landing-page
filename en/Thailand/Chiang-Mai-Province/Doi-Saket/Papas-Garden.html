Description

All guest rooms in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV. With a private bathroom, some accommodations at Papa's Garden also feature a terrace. Guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

A à la carte breakfast is available each morning at the property. At the accommodation you'll find a restaurant serving Asian cuisine.

Papa's Garden has an outdoor pool.

Chiang Mai is 11 miles from the resort, while Mae Rim is 13 miles away. The nearest airport is Chiang Mai Airport, 13 miles from Papa's Garden.
