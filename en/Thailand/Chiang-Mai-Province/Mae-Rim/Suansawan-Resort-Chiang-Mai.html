Description

Located in Mae Rim Valley, Suansawan Resort Chiang Mai is a 40-minute drive from downtown Chiang Mai and the Night Bazaar.

It offers rooms with private terraces and views of the mountains. Spacious rooms feature Balinese décor.

They are equipped with air conditioning, attached bathroom and free WiFi. Satellite TV, DVD player and a minibar are provided.

Guests of Suansawan Resort can have local or Western meals at the resort’s restaurant and bar, which offers indoor and outdoor dining with garden views.

The resort offers 2 outdoor pools, a fitness centre with sauna and a tour desk. Alternatively, guests can also take a stroll at the lake or have a quiet read in the library.

For the adventurous, mountain bikes are available for rent. The property is also capable of catering for disabled guests with disabled-friendly rooms, equipped transfer vehicles and on-site nursing staff.

Suansawan Resort Chiang Mai is a 30-minute drive from Chiang Mai International Airport.
