Description

Located in Chiang Mai, Baan Suan Residence offers an outdoor pool and bicycle rental.

It provides rooms and villas with free WiFi. The property also has a library. Surrounded by nature, all villas and rooms come with a balcony, refrigerator and cable flat-screen TV.

Bathrooms are fitted with a bathtub. Some villas have a private pool. For guests’ convenience, the property provides laundry and daily maid service.

Free shuttle services for the city centre and airport are available. Guests can enjoy Thai dishes at the restaurant.

Room service can be arranged upon request. The city centre is 8 km away, while Chiang Mai International Airport is a 20-minute drive from the property.
