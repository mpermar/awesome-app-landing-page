Description

All rooms in the resort are equipped with a electric tea pot. Phutarntree Resort provides certain accommodations that have mountain views, and all rooms are equipped with a private bathroom and a closet. At the accommodation, rooms have air conditioning and a flat-screen TV.

Breakfast is available daily, and includes continental, à la carte and American options.

Phutarntree Resort has a playground. You can play ping pong at the resort.

Chiang Mai Zoo is 7 miles from Phutarntree Resort, while Ton Payom Market is 7 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Chiang Mai Airport, 7 miles from the property.
