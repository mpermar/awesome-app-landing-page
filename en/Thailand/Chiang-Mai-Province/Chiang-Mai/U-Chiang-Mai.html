Description

The stylish rooms feature rich traditional design elements, a private balcony and a day bed. All rooms come with a flat-screen TV, DVD player and minibar. Attached bathrooms have a luxurious rainshower.

U Chiang Mai is just steps from the famous Sunday Walking Street and Tha Phae Road. Attractions like Wat Chedi Luang and the Three Kings Monument are a 5-minute walk away. It is a 20-minute drive from the Chiang Mai International Airport.

Relaxing afternoons can be spent at The Reading Room, which features an extensive collection of books on Chiang Mai. The resort also features a 24-hour fitness center and free bike rentals.

Eat Restaurant serves Thai and international dishes, while drinks can be enjoyed at the open-air bar. In-room dining is also possible with room service.
