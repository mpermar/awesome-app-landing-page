Description

Located in downtown Chiang Mai, Palm Springs Lodge The Thamna offers an accommodation in Mediterranean style buildings.

It features a swimming pool, free WiFi in all areas and free parking. The property along with its garden covers an area of 8000 square meters.

Featuring warm earth tones, each room here will provide you with air conditioning, a minibar and a balcony with lovely garden views.

Complete with a refrigerator, the dining area also has an electric kettle. The en suite bathroom comes with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries made from natural sources.

At Palm Springs Lodge you will find a 24-hour front desk, a garden and a terrace.

Other facilities offered at the property include a shared lounge, a ticket service and a tour desk.

There is a playground as well as a kid's corner where children and parents can enjoy a cultural activities together as a family.

Using fresh seasonal fruit, vegetables and organic local ingredients, guests can enjoy a healthy and delicious meal at the on-site restaurant set amidst flowers and greenery.

Baby food is available upon request. The hotel offers shuttle bus service to the old city once a day at 10:30 hrs.

Free airport pick-up service is also available. Palm Springs Lodge is only 400 meters distance from convenience store.

It is about 5-minute drive to Central festival, 10-minute drive to city centre and 15-minute drive to Sunday night market.
