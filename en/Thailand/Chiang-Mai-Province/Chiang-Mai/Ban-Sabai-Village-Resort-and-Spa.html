Description

Tastefully decorated, each room has spacious interiors and private balconies, and private villas feature open-air bathrooms with spa bathtubs and steam rooms. Rustic in style, but well-furnished with a bathtub, a satellite TV and a minibar, the rooms provide comfort.

Ban Sanbai is located within 3 miles from various attractions like Sunday Walking Street, Warorat Market and Wat Gate. The resort has bike and car rental facilities.

For a truly relaxing getaway, the Ban Sabai Resort features two garden spa pavilions which provide traditional massages and beauty treatments. There is also a meditation area in the middle of a lotus pond. Other facilities include an outdoor swimming pool, a gym and a library.

The Wild Orchid Restaurant serves both Asian and international dishes daily. Private dinners on the grounds pavilion can also be arranged upon request.
