Description

The air-conditioned accommodations will provide guests with rooms offering a closet, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a shower. A seating area is available in guest rooms, while selected rooms will provide you with a DVD player. At the resort all rooms have bed linen and towels.

A Full English/Irish breakfast is served at the property.

Baan Saen Fang Chiang Mai has a terrace.

Wat Gate is 2,100 feet from the accommodation, while Naowarat Bridge is 0.4 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Chiang Mai Airport, 2.5 miles from Baan Saen Fang Chiang Mai.

Chang Moi is a great choice for travelers interested in street food, sightseeing and local food.
