Description

This intimate boutique property offers restored Thai-style suites with colonial influences and attentive service.

Nestled in tropical greenery, it features 2 outdoor pools, a day spa and free WiFi throughout.

Boasting a spa bath and fine teak fittings, tastefully furnished suites are surrounded by tropical greenery and landscaped gardens.

They come with a flat-screen TV and private balcony. Shewe Wana Suite Resort is only a 10-minute walk from the local night bazaar and shops along the Ping River.

It is a short walk from San Pa Khoi Market where northern Thai food and fresh fruit can be sampled.

It is a 20-minute walk from Chiang Mai Old City and a 20-minute drive from Chiang Mai International Airport.

Comfort at its finest, guests can enjoy yoga classes and relaxing massages at the spa.

The resort also provides a tour desk, laundry services and free parking. Shewe Wana also offers complimentary Tuk-Tuk shuttle service to the night bazaar.

Aside from a daily breakfast, the 2 on-site restaurants serve a variety of vegetarian, Thai and European dishes.

The restaurants also feature lovely garden views and personalised menus including gluten-free dishes, which can be prepared upon request.
