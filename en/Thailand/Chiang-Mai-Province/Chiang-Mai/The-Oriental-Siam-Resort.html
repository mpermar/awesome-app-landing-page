Description

Air-conditioned villas have access to a private garden and are decorated with elegant teak furnishings. A flat-screen TV, DVD player and private bathrooms with shower and bathtub are included in all rooms. Guests enjoy a free mini-bar and welcome drink.

Oriental Siam is located next to a temple with views of Doi Suthep Mountain. It is a 10-minute drive to Chiangmai Airport and local arts and handicraft villages. A free shuttle service to Promenada Resort Mall Chiang Mai is also available.

Guests can enjoy traditional Thai massage nearby, or exercise at the gym. Other services include a tour desk and car rental.

Local Thai dishes are served at the property’s on-site restaurant or served in the villa.
