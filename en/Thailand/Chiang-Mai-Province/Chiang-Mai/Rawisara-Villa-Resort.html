Description

Located in Chiang Mai, Rawisara Villa Resort features comfortable accommodation with modern facilities.

It boasts an outdoor pool and on-site parking facilities. With free WiFi access available, a free shuttle is also provided to the night bazaar and Promenada Resort Mall.

Rawisara Villa Resort is just a 15-minute drive from Chiang Mai International Airport.

Bo Sang Village and San Kamphaeng District are a 10-minute ride awy, while Chiang Mai Night Safari is a 20-minute ride from the property.

Surrounded by gardens, all units come with a private balcony and air conditioning.

They are provided with a TV, a minibar and a refrigerator. Shower facilities are included in the en suite bathrooms.

For convenience, the resort provides laundry and ironing services. Staff at the front desk can help guests with sightseeing arrangements and shuttle services.

Guests can enjoy a variety of Thai and international favourites served at Rawisara Restaurant.
