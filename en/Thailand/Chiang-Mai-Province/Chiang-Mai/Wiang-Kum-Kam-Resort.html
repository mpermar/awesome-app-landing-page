Description

Wiang Kum Kam Resort in Saraphi boasts accommodation with free bikes, an outdoor swimming pool and a garden.

With free WiFi, this hotel offers a tour desk and luggage storage space. Guest rooms at the hotel come with a TV, a seating area and a kitchenette.

Each room includes a private bathroom. The units will provide guests with a microwave.

Car hire is available at this hotel and the area is popular for cycling. Chiang Mai Night Safari is 13 km from Wiang Kum Kam Resort, while Wat Phrathat Doi Suthep is about 30 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Chiang Mai International Airport, 7 km from the accommodation.
