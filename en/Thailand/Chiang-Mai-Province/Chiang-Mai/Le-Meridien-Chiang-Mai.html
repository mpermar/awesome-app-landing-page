Description

Featuring views of Doi Suthep mountain or the city, the elegant rooms all include a flat-screen TV, minibar and a personal safe. The large private bathrooms each have a separate bathtub and hot-water shower. 24-hour room service is also available.

The 5-star Le Meridien Chiang Mai is in the center of the famous Chiang Mai Night Bazaar. It is 4 miles from Chiang Mai International Airport and 16 miles from Doi Suthep Temple. Free parking is available.

The Spa features a relaxing menu of body massage and herbal treatments. Guests can also exercise at the gym or arrange a day trip at the tour desk. There is also an on-site shopping arcade.

Favola serves popular Italian dishes with a modern spin, and Latest Recipe provides international buffets from its interactive open kitchen. Snacks and cocktails can be enjoyed at Latitude 18 or the poolside Plunge terrace.

Chang Khlan is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, night markets and street markets.
