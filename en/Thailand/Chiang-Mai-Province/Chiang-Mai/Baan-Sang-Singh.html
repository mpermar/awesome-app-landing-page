Description

Offering an outdoor pool, Baan Sang Singh is situated in Chiang Mai, less than a 10-minute walk from Chiang Mai Gate.

Free WiFi is featured throughout the property. On Saturday and Sundays, a free shuttle is offered for guests to visit the famous Walking Street.

The accommodation is air-conditioned and has a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

A fridge and kettle are also available. There is a private bathroom with a shower, hairdryer and free toiletries in every unit.

Complimentary tea, coffee and cookies are available at the lobby during for a limited time in the morning hours.

Guests staying at Baan Sang Singh can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant or a drink at the bar.

Bicycles can be rented to explore the old town and attractions. Free shuttles to Chiang Mai Night Bazaar is also available.

The popular Saturday Walking Street is less than 1 km away and Chiang Mai International Airport is a 10-minute drive away.
