Description

Nestled amidst tropical gardens, Papai Country Lodge boasts an outdoor pool and a restaurant.

Featuring comfortable villas, the property offers on-site parking and free Wi-Fi access.

Guests enjoy free bicycle rental. Papai Country Lodge is 6 km from San Kamphaeng Hot Springs and 12 km from Mae On Cave.

Chiang Mai International Airport is 24 km away. The villas are provided with air conditioning, a seating area and a cable TV.

They also have a refrigerator and a safety box. Free toiletries are included in an en suite bathroom.

For leisure, enjoy relaxing massages or arrange sightseeing tours at the on-site tour desk.

A laundry service is also available. Guests can dine at Tara Restaurant, which serves authentic Thai cuisine.
