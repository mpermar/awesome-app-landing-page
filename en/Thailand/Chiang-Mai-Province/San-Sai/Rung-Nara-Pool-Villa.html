Description

Guest rooms at the resort come with a seating area. The rooms include air conditioning, and some units at Rung Nara Pool Villa have a terrace. All guest rooms will provide guests with a toaster.

Breakfast is available every morning, and includes à la carte, American and Asian options. The restaurant at the accommodation serves Thai cuisine.

Rung Nara Pool Villa has a playground. Guests at the resort will be able to enjoy activities in and around San Sai, like cycling.

Chiang Mai is 11 miles from Rung Nara Pool Villa. Chiang Mai Airport is 12 miles away.
