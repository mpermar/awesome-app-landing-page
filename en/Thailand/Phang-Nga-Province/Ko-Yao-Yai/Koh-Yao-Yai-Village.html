Description

Each air-conditioned bungalow at the Koh Yao Yai Village comes decorated in local hand-crafted artwork and furnishings. Featuring a semi-open concept, the spacious bungalows provide outdoor rainshower facilities, a private terrace with deck chairs and umbrella. Dreamy views of the ocean or rainforest can be enjoyed from the rooms.

Overlooking the white sandy beach and nearby islands, the pool provides a beautiful spot for sun tanning, and the library provides internet access and a peaceful spot indoors. Gifts and souvenirs are available at a gift shop and an Activity Centre helps guests with their sightseeing plans.

Savor local and international cuisine at the Khayee Restaurant, which features outdoor and open air dining. Alternatively, Krachang Bar & Grill serves fresh seafood dishes. For drinks and light snacks, head to the pool bar.

Koh Yao Yai Village is a 30-minute speed boat ride from Phuket (Ao Po Grand Marina Pier) or Krabi (Ao Nang Pier) and connecting transportation via both places are available. The property is a 20-minute drive from Klong Hai Pier.
