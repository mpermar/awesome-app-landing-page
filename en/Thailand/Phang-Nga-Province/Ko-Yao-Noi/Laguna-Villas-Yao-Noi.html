Description

Located on Koh Yao Noi, Laguna Villas Yao Noi is conveniently located only 20 metres from the beautiful Pa Sai Beach.

Offering free WiFi in all luxurious rooms, guests can enjoy a relaxing afternoon by taking a dip into the infinity pool or getting massages.

Other activities provided by the property are Thai cooking classes, diving and kayaking.

Fitted with contemporary furnishings, rooms at Laguna Villas Yao Noi are stylish and comfortable.

Each Deluxe Beach Suite consists of a bedroom, an en suite bathroom and a spacious living area with a sofa, a table and a set of chairs.

The air-conditioned room comes with a private balcony, a seating area and a flat-screen TV.

The kitchen area is equipped with cutlery, an electric kettle, microwave oven and a refrigerator.

Staff at the front desk can assist guests with tour arrangements and motorbike and bicycle rentals.

For convenience, the property offers babysitting services and a chargeable shuttle service.

The property is a 20-minute drive to a local market and Manoh Pier. Krabi is a 30-minute ride away from Phuket via speedboat.
