Description

Surrounded by tropical forests, rice paddies and an unspoilt beach, Koyao Bay Pavilions offers luxurious resort-style accommodation with patios.

A 10-minute drive from Ta Manoh Pier, it features a private beach area and a restaurant.

Koyao Bay Pavilions is an hour's boat and car journey from Krabi and Phuket Airports.

Choose from beach front suites or cottages and villas with pools. Each room has a private bathroom with a shower.

They are equipped with a fan or air-conditioning, or both. Enjoy massage services or pick a book from the library.

Activities including snorkelling, fishing and diving can be arranged at the tour desk.

Sports equipment, motorcycles and mountain bikes are available. Thai dishes, fresh seafood and international cuisine is served at the Koyao Bay Pavilions restaurant.
