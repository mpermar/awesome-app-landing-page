Description

Situated along a peaceful beachfront on Ko Yao Noi, Koh Yao Seaview Bungalow offers a garden and accommodation with sea views.

Free WiFi is available. The air-conditioned bungalows provide a cable TV, a refrigerator and a mosquito net.

The private bathroom has a shower. At Koh Yao Seaview Bungalow you will find a bar and a restaurant.

Other facilities offered at the property include bicycle rental and a tour desk. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including canoeing, hiking and snorkelling.
