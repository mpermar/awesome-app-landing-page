Description

Adorned with contemporary decor, rooms at The Sands Khao Lak include a flat-screen cable TV, a DVD player and an iPod docking station. Free toiletries and a hairdryer are included in a private bathroom.

Luggage storage is available at the 24-hour front desk. Free private parking is available on site.

Guests can enjoy a great selection of Thai, western and seafood dishes at the restaurant.

Nang Thong Beach is a great choice for travelers interested in beach walks, restaurants and sunsets.
