Description

Mukdara Resort is a one hour drive from the Phuket International Airport. Taplamu Pier is 5 minutes by car from the resort, and short ferry rides to Similan Island can be found there.

All rooms have a private terrace presenting grounds or pool views. Rooms are also equipped with satellite TV and a safe.

Mukdara Beach Villa offers sea sports equipment and transportation to local attractions. It has a tennis court and 3 outdoor swimming pools. The Mukdara Spa offers massage treatments.

The restaurants serve Western, authentic Thai and seafood dishes. Dining at Touch Beach also gives you a view of the ocean.

Bang Niang Beach is a great choice for travelers interested in markets, restaurants and friendly locals.
