Description

Boasting a large and well-designed outdoor pool with views of the mountain ranges and the sea, La Vela Khao Lak offers modern rooms with complimentary WiFi.

Sea breeze and the sunlight can be enjoyed at the beachfront deck. Spa and wellness centre invites all guests to indulge in various refreshing treatments and therapies.

Every room comes with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Some units feature a seating area for your convenience.

All rooms have an en suite bathroom fitted with a bathtub. For your comfort, you will find bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. A range of activities are offered in the area, such as snorkelling, cycling and fishing.

The resort also offers bike hire. Tsunami Memorial - Rue Tor 813 is 900 metres from La Vela Khao Lak, while Tublamu Pier is 11 km from the property.
