Description

Located in Khao Lak, 8 km from Tublamu Pier, Kalima Resort and Villas Khao Lak provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, free bikes and an outdoor swimming pool.

With a bar, the property also has a shared lounge, as well as a garden. The property features a fitness centre, evening entertainment and a 24-hour front desk.

All units are equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fridge, a kettle, a bidet, a hairdryer and a desk.

With a private bathroom equipped with a shower and free toiletries, rooms at the resort also offer free WiFi.

At Kalima Resort and Villas Khao Lak, each room includes a seating area. A buffet breakfast is available daily at the accommodation.

Kalima Resort and Villas Khao Lak offers a children's playground. Guests at the resort will be able to enjoy activities in and around Khao Lak, like cycling.

There is also a business centre and a kids' club on site. Tsunami Memorial - Rue Tor 813 is 10 km from Kalima Resort and Villas Khao Lak, while Khaolak Tourist Information Centre is 1.

7 km away. The nearest airport is Phuket International Airport, 70 km from the accommodation.
