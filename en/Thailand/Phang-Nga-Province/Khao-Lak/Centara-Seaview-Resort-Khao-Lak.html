Description

Set on Nang Thong Beach, Centara Seaview Resort Khao Lak is a 1-hr drive from Phuket Airport.

Offering air-conditioned rooms, the property is equipped with a spa, 4 swimming pools with a fantastic slide for the kids, and a tennis court.

Free WiFi is accessible. Each room comes with a satellite TV, a safety deposit box and a bathtub.

Slippers and bathrobes are provided. Some rooms have a private pool, a minibar and a balcony.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can assist guests with shuttle service, car rental and laundry.

Other facilities include meeting rooms and a fitness centre. Thai and international dishes can be sampled at the resort’s restaurant.
