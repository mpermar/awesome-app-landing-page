Description

Boasting a private beachfront location along coast of Khao Lak, Manathai Khao Lak features an infinity pool that looks out towards the sea.

With free WiFi access, guests can enjoy pampering massages, dine on delicious dishes at one of the on-site restaurants or enjoy a water-based activity.

All rooms provide guests with a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a safety deposit box and a balcony.

Each room features a garden view. Some rooms provide an outdoor dining area. Featuring a shower, the private bathroom includes free toiletries, a hairdryer and bathrobes.

A full range of services and facilities are available at Manathai Khao Lak, including a tour desk and laundry services.

Drivers enjoy free parking and car rental can be arranged. The resort is just over an hours' drive from Phuket International Airport and 11.
