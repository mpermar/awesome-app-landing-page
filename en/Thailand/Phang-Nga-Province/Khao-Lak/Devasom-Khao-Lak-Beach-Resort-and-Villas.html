Description

Located directly on the Khao Lak’s sunset beach sided by a natural lagoon, Devasom Khao Lak Beach Resort & Villas is a resort found amid tropical garden and coconut groves.

Guests can enjoy free WiFi access, resort activities and a welcome fruit platter on arrival.

Offering luxurious guest rooms, pool suites and beachfront villas, each unit boasts breathtaking sea view and is decorated with ancient architecture of Southeast Asia.

Rooms and villas are equipped with an LED TV with satellite channels, bathroom amenities, and a large private balcony or a deck with seating area.

Complimentary in-room minibar is replenished daily. Guests can indulge in a range of spa treatments at Devasom Spa and Wellness or exercise at the 24-hour fitness centre.

Kids club is available at the property. Takola Restaurant and Wine Bar serves up a fine selection of authentic Thai and western cuisines while Devasom Beach Grill and Bar features a cocktail bar and Mediterranean fusion menu with an open grill and a wood-fired pizza oven.

For a memorable experience, a private dining at the observation tower and the beachfront area can also be arranged.

Devasom Khao Lak Beach Resort & Villas is located a 10-minute drive from Khao Lak Town.

Guests can make use of the scheduled daily shuttle transfers to/from Khao Lak Town.

Phuket International Airport can be reached within a 75-minute drive from the property.

Nearby attractions include Phang Nga Bay and many scenic national parks and waterfalls.

Similan Islands can be reached with ease by speedboat and guests can explore the surrounding waterways by kayak or on a bicycle tour.
