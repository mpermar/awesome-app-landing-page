Description

At the resort, rooms have a desk. The rooms come with air conditioning, and some rooms come with a terrace. Guest rooms include a closet.

Tublamu Pier is 7 miles from Light Khao Lak Resort, while Bangnieng Afternoon Market is 0.7 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Phuket Airport, 39 miles from the accommodation.

Bang Niang Beach is a great choice for travelers interested in markets, restaurants and friendly locals.
