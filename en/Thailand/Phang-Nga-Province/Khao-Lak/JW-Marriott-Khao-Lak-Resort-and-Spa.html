Description

Situated on the Andaman Sea’s white-sand Khao Lak Beach, this Thai-style resort offers bright rooms with free WiFi access.

Five restaurants and three bars are located on site. The resort boasts a 3. 2 km lagoon pool.

The JW Marriott Khao Lak Resort’s spacious guest rooms feature large windows, a separate sitting area and a private balcony.

Each is equipped with an LCD TV. Guests can relax in a sun lounger by Southeast Asia's largest lagoon-inspired swimming pool while enjoying a tropical drink.

Quan Spa offers a variety of treatments including traditional Thai massage. The resort also provides a lounge for guests to relax in before check-in and after check-out.

Ta-Krai serves traditional Thai cuisine. Teppanyaki and Japanese dishes are offered at Sakura.

Waterfront, which offers al fresco dining overlooking the Andaman Sea, serves a variety of international dishes.

JW Marriott Khao Lak Resort is located a 15-minute drive from Khao Lak Town. Guests can reach Phuket International Airport with a 1.
