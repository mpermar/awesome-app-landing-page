Description

Offering comfort with well-designed rooms and attentive service, Ramada Resort by Wyndham Khao Lak is located on the peaceful Bang Niang Beach.

It boasts an outdoor pool, spa and beachfront bar and restaurant. Spacious rooms with modern furnishings are housed in low-rise buildings set in tropical landscapes.

They come with a 32-inch flat-screen cable TV, balcony and a bathtub. Leisurely afternoons can be spent in the library or with a variety of water sports.

There is also a fitness centre, children’s playroom and a business centre. Hearty buffet breakfasts are served at The Kitchen while Sassi’s Beach Club offers beachfront grills and refreshing cocktails.

Mellow Bean provides internet facilities and light refreshments. Ramada Resort by Wyndham Khao Lak is an hour-long drive from Phuket International Airport.
