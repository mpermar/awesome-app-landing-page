Description

At the resort, each room comes with a desk and a flat-screen TV. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a bath and free toiletries, while selected rooms here will provide you with a seating area. Guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

The daily breakfast offers continental and à la carte options.

Guests at Thap Pala Cottage will be able to enjoy activities in and around Songkhla, like cycling, fishing and canoeing.

Thaksin University is 2.1 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Hat Yai Airport, 21 miles from the property.
