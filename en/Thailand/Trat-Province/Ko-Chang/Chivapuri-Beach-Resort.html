Description

Chivapuri Beach Resort is next to the Khlong Kroi Canal and is about a 35-minute drive from the Centrepoint Pier.

The spacious Thai-style rooms feature hardwood floors and private balconies with views of the sea and tropical greenery. They are fitted with a DVD player, safe and cable TV. The private bathrooms have a bathtub with separate rain shower area.

Guests can relax with massages by the beach or with island trips arranged by the tour desk. Chivapuri also provides a ticket service and car rental.

Enjoy a spread of international dishes with sunset views at the beachfront restaurant. Cocktails and liquor are available at Sunset Bar.

Khong Koi Beach is a great choice for travelers interested in friendly locals, tranquillity and nature.
