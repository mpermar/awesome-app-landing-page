Description

Featuring free bikes, Feel@Chill Resort Koh Chang offers rooms in Ko Chang, 500 metres from Khlong Son Beach.

Boasting an outdoor swimming pool, this property is set a short distance 2 km from Ao Sapparot Pier.

In addition to free WiFi, the property also features a terrace as well as a fitness centre.

Guest rooms at the resort are equipped with a seating area. Featuring a private bathroom, rooms at Feel@Chill Resort Koh Chang also offer pool view.

At the accommodation, every room includes air conditioning and a flat-screen TV. An American breakfast is available each morning at the property.

The area is popular for cycling, and car hire is available at Feel@Chill Resort Koh Chang.

Koh Chang National Park is 12 km from the resort, while International Clinic, Ko Chang is 4.
