Description

At the resort, the rooms come with a balcony. The private bathroom is equipped with a shower and free toiletries.

A Full English/Irish breakfast is served every morning at the property.

Yuyugoldenbeach has a sun terrace.

Khlong Son Temple is 11 miles from the accommodation, while Ao Sapparot Pier is 12 miles from the property.

Khong Koi Beach is a great choice for travelers interested in friendly locals, tranquillity and nature.
