Description

Offering an outdoor pool and a restaurant, Pajamas Koh Chang is just a 2-minute walk from Klong Prao Beach.

It offers both private and dormitory rooms with air conditioning. Free WiFi access is available in all areas.

The hostel is 5. 2 km from Kai Bae Beach, 5. 5 km from White Sand Beach and 5. 7 km from Koh Chang National Park.

Guests who drive enjoy free parking. Boasting mountain views, each room here comes a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Private rooms have an en suite bathroom, while shared bathroom facilities are available for dormitory rooms.

At Koh Chang Pajamas you will find a bar and a tour desk. Other facilities offered include a shared lounge and a ticket service.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling.
