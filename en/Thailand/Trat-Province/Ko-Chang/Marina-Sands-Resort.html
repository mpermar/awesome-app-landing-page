Description

Located at Siam Royal Yacht Marina, a direct access to coral reef snorkelling, 3 different pool areas, restaurants, bars and lounges, spa and wellness and kids club await guests at Marina Sands Resort.

Just a few steps away from a sandy beach, guests can enjoy free WiFi throughout the property.

All rooms at Marina Sands Resort are air conditioning and well equipped with a flat-screen Smart TV with satellite channels.

Guests can grab a drink or snack from the minibar and sit out on the balcony. The private bathroom comes with bathrobes, rainshower and free toiletries.

Airport transfers, free in-resort shuttle services, excursion and soothing massages can be arranged upon request at the front desk.

Free parking and motorbike rental are also available. Guests can enjoy a meal at the Harbour, an over-water restaurant which specialises in Thai and international cuisines.

The Flybridge rooftop pool bar and lounge also serves tropical drinks and cocktails.

Other on-site facilities offered at Marina Sands Resort include room service, a children's playground, and a water sports centre.

Chang Noi Beach is 450 m from the property, while Khlong Son Temple and Ao Sapparot Pier are 2 km away.
