Description

This resort in Koh Chang, Trat is situated on Klong Prao Beach. It offers massage rooms, a beachfront restaurant and 2 outdoor swimming pools.

Free private parking is available. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout. Rooms at the Koh Chang Tropicana Resort & Spa have Thai-style décor and wooden furniture.

They overlook a tropical garden and include a private balcony, a satellite TV, a tea/coffee maker and a work desk.

Thai, Italian and western cuisine can be enjoyed at the Sea Breeze Restaurant, which overlooks the ocean.

Guests can also enjoy drinks next to the outdoor swimming pools, or relax at the spa pool.
