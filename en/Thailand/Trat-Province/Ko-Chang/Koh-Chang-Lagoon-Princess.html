Description

Located on White Sand Beach, Koh Chang Lagoon Princess is a 20-minute drive from Ao Sapparot Pier and Klong Prao Beach.

It features free WiFi, free parking and air-conditioned rooms with a private balcony.

Set amidst a tropical landscape, rooms and bungalows come with modern interiors. Each is equipped with a cable TV, minibar and an en suite bathroom with shower facilities.

Guests can enjoy a relaxing body massage or go on a snorkelling trip. With a 24-hour front desk, the resort provides car rental and ticketing services.

Travel arrangements can be made at the tour desk. Available for all-day dining, Lagoon Restaurant serves Thai, international and seafood dishes.

Light snacks and refreshing beverages can be enjoyed at the coffee shop. Koh Chang Lagoon Princess is a 20-minute drive from Kai Bae Beach and a 45-minute drive from Ao Thammachat Pier.
