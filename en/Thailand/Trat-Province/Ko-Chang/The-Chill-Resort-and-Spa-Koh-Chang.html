Description

Just 8 km from Laem Ngop Pier, The Chill Resort and Spa, Koh Chang is a boutique resort located at Kai Bae Beach.

Rooms feature a private terrace and there is free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel. The rooms at The Chill Resort and Spa, Koh Chang offer stylish furnishings and modern decor.

Each air-conditioned room is equipped with a flat-screen TV with a DVD and Blu-Ray player, a tea and coffee maker and bathrobes.

Guests of the The Chill Resort and Spa, Koh Chang can enjoy fusion cuisine and all-day breakfast, along with the sunset view at the beachfront Chill-Out Cafe from 07:00-23:00 hrs.

The Chill Spa offers traditional body and foot massages and skin treatments. There is also a refreshing outdoor pool and Chill fitness centre.
