Description

Boasting a private beach area called Ao Ta Pao in Ko Kood of Trat Province, Medee Resort offers cosy accommodation surrounded by luscious greenery, a restaurant, various massage treatments, and free WiFi.

Fully air-conditioned, all stylish bungalows are complete with a TV, a seating area, a private bathroom, and a balcony overlooking the well-landscaped garden or the glittering sea.

A minibar is also available in all units. Ko Mak is 20 km from Medee Resort, while Koh Kong is 49 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Trat Airport, which is situated 73 km from the property. Outdoor enthusiastic guests of Resort Medee can enjoy acclivities like diving, snorkelling and canoeing.

The resort also provided bicycle rentals, car hiring and laundry services. The on-site restaurant serves fresh sea food as well as delectable dishes for all meals.
