Description

Guest rooms at the resort are equipped with a seating area and a TV with satellite channels. Each room is equipped with a private bathroom, and some accommodations at Lamphurai Riverside have a terrace. All rooms will provide guests with a shared bathroom with a bath or shower.

à la carte and American breakfast options are available each morning at the accommodation.

Guests at Lamphurai Riverside will be able to enjoy activities in and around Trat, like cycling.

Ko Chang is 22 miles from the resort, while Chanthaburi is 31 miles away. The nearest airport is Trat Airport, 11 miles from the property.
