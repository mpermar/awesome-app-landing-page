Description

At the guesthouse, rooms are equipped with a balcony. Phuttal Residence provides some accommodations with garden views, and rooms have a private bathroom. Guest rooms come with a seating area.

A continental, Asian or halal breakfast is available each morning at the property.

The accommodation has a playground. Guests at Phuttal Residence will be able to enjoy activities in and around Phra Nakhon Si Ayutthaya, like cycling.

Wat Mahathat is 1.2 miles from the guesthouse. The nearest airport is Don Muang Airport, 30 miles from the property.
