Description

All rooms at the resort are equipped with a seating area. Each room includes a flat-screen TV, and certain rooms will provide you with a pool view. The rooms at Menam Resort have air conditioning and a desk.

Breakfast is available each morning, and includes à la carte, American and Asian options.

The accommodation has a playground. Fishing is among the activities that guests can enjoy near Menam Resort.

Bangkok is 23 miles from the resort. The nearest airport is Don Muang Airport, 27 miles from Menam Resort.
