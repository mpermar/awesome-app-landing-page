Description

Spacious rooms at Bari Lamai feature an outdoor terrace with lounge chairs, separate living area and satellite TV. They are equipped with a refrigerator and minibar. Complimentary fruits are offered upon arrival. Rooms are also equipped with Wi-Fi access.

The resort has an internet room with ample work space. Meeting and banquet facilities are also available.

Bari Bar and Restaurant offers seafood dishes and beverages.

Bari Lamai Resort is 2.5 hours by car from Bangkok.
