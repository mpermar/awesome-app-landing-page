Description

Centara Q Resort Rayong is located on Laem Mae Phim Beach. It has a restaurant and bar, as well as a relaxing outdoor swimming pool.

Featuring a private balcony with sea views, rooms are equipped with air-conditioning, minibar and a flat-screen TV.

They also come with a safety deposit box and an outdoor seating area. A bathtub or shower can be found in the en suite bathroom, along with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Besides heading down to the Laem Mae Phim Beach, guests can unwind with a pampering treatment at the on-site Cense by SPA Cenvaree or work out at the fitness centre.

At Qzeen Restaurant, both local and international dishes are served. Drinks and cocktails can be enjoyed at Quattro Rooftop Lounge or at the pool bar.

Centara Q Resort Rayong is a 3-hour drive from Bangkok city or 45-minute drive from Pattaya.
