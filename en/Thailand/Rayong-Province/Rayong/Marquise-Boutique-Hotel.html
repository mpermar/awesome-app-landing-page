Description

All guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a coffee machine. The rooms include a private bathroom with free toiletries. The rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

The daily breakfast offers continental, American or Asian options. The restaurant at Marquise Boutique Hotel specializes in American cuisine.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool.

The reception can provide advice on the area in order to help guests plan their day.

Yomjinda Walking Street is 1.2 miles from Marquise Boutique Hotel, while Ko Kloy Floating Market is 1.2 miles away. U-Tapao Rayong-Pattaya International Airport is 18 miles from the property.
