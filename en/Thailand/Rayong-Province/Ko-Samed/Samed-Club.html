Description

Samed Club is located on the quieter part of Koh Samet within Khao Laem Ya National Park, providing a peaceful retreat away from the city. Koh Samet is accessible via a 30-minute boat ride from Ao Prao Ban Phe Pier located on the mainland town of Rayong.

Bungalows and cottages at the Samed Club feature simple yet contemporary designs. Each air-conditioned room comes with its own balcony, satellite TV, mini-bar and fridge.

Besides the outdoor pool, other water activities available include sailing, scuba diving, windsurfing, canoeing and snorkeling.

Opened daily is the beachside restaurant, Rice, which serves a variety of authentic Thai dishes alongside international favorites.
