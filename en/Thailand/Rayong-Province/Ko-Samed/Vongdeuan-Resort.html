Description

Vongdeuan Resort is a 7-minute drive from Ao Phai Beach. It offers modern accommodation with free Wi-Fi.

Guests enjoy free round-trip shuttle boat service from the hotel to Ban Phe. Canoeing and snorkelling equipment can be arranged upon request.

The resort is a 10-minute drive to Sai Keaw Beach, Ao Prao Beach and Nah Dan Pier.

It is a 1. 5-hr drive from Pattaya and 2. 5-hr drive from Suvarnabhumi Airport. Bangkok is a 3-hr drive away.

Each unit at Vongdeuan Resort comes with a satellite TV, minibar and electric kettle.

It also has a seating area and hairdryer. Free toiletries are included in an en suite bathroom.

Guests can cool off in the outdoor swimming pool. Other services include laundry and safety deposit boxes.

A variety of international dishes is served at Vongdeuan Restaurant. Beverages and light snacks are offered at Tempo Coffee Shop.
