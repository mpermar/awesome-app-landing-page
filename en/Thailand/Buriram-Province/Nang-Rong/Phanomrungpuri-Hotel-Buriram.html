Description

With an outdoor pool, Phanomrungpuri hotel is located in the historic district of Nang Rongdistrict, Buriram.

It features a coffee shop with karaoke and offers traditional Thai massage. The Khmer-style rooms are air conditioned and equipped with en suite bathrooms.

Each comes with a minibar and satellite TV. Free Wi-Fi is available in the entire building.

American breakfast can be enjoyed every morning at the Barai Restaurant, which also serves Thai and international cuisine throughout the day.
