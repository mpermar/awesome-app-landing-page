Description

At the resort, each room comes with a balcony. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a bidet, while certain rooms have a seating area. Guest rooms at Eastin Thana City Golf Resort Bangk come with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

The nearest airport is Suvarnabhumi Airport, 3.7 miles from the accommodation.
