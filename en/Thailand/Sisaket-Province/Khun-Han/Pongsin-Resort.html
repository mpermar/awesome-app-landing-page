Description

At the resort, rooms have a desk and a flat-screen TV. Pongsin Resort has certain units that have garden views, and the rooms have a private bathroom with a shower. All rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

A continental breakfast is served daily at the property. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the on-site restaurant, which serves Chinese cuisine.

The accommodation has a playground. You can play ping pong at Pongsin Resort.

Guests can rent a car or take advantage of the business center.

The nearest airport is Ubon Ratchathani Airport, 50 miles from the resort.
