Description

At the guesthouse, all rooms include a closet. Each room comes with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV, and selected rooms will provide you with a balcony. All guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

A continental breakfast is served each morning at the property.

Hard Rock Cafe Pattaya is an 18-minute walk from Bua Khao Paradise, while King Power Pattaya Complex is 1.2 miles from the property. The nearest airport is U-Tapao Rayong-Pattaya International Airport, 19 miles from the property.
