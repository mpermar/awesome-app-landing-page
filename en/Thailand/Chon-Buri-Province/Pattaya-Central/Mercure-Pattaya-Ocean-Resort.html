Description

The hotel is 650 feet from Art In Paradise and 2,950 feet from Hard Rock Cafe. Suvarnabhumi Airport is 52 mi away. Chargeable airport transfer services can be arranged. On-site parking is possible.

Air-conditioned rooms and suites here will provide you with a flat-screen TV with cable/satellite chanels, a refrigerator and a mini-bar. There are also tea and coffee facilities and an electric kettle offered. Shower facilities and a hairdryer are in an private bathroom. Select units feature ocean views.

Other facilities offered include a tour desk, luggage storage and a kids' club. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including a water park. Union Pay is accepted at the property. International newspaper is also available at the hotel.

For meals, enjoy a variety of local, Asian and international dishes served at the on-site restaurant.
