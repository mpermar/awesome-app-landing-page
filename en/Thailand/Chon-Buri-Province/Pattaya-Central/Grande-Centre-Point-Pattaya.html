Description

Set within 3. 5 km of King Power Pattaya Complex and 3. 2 km of Alcazar Cabaret, Grande Centre Point Pattaya features rooms with air conditioning and a private bathroom in Pattaya Central.

This 5-star hotel offers an outdoor swimming pool. Guests can enjoy a drink at the bar or a meal in the restaurant.

At the hotel, every room is fitted with a flat-screen TV. Each room includes a kettle, while certain rooms are fitted with a terrace and others also provide guests with sea views.

All guest rooms include a wardrobe. A buffet breakfast is served daily at the property.

The property has a fitness centre. You can play billiards at Grande Centre Point Pattaya.

Guests can access the free WiFi or use the business centre. Staff at the 24-hour front desk can provide tips on the area.

Art In Paradise is 3. 5 km from the accommodation. The nearest airport is U-Tapao Rayong-Pattaya International Airport, 50 km from Grande Centre Point Pattaya.
