Description

Fifth Jomtien is located next to the Pattaya Immigration Offices and is a 2-hour drive from Suvarnabhumi International Airport. It is a 5-minute walk from the dining options surrounding Jomtien Beach.

Featuring a private balcony, air conditioned studios at Fifth Jomtien offer the comfort of spacious layouts and fine wood furnishings and floors. A flat-screen TV, safe and fridge are provided.

Lazy afternoons can be spent lounging at the sun terrace or on the hotel’s grounds. Round-the-clock security is provided.

Guests can dine at Fifth Jomtien’s restaurant, which offers a variety of Thai and Western dishes.
