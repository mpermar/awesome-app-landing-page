Description

Opening to a balcony with sea, pool or city views, all units are fitted with a kitchenette with a microwave and a fridge. There's a fully equipped private bathroom with bidet and a hair dryer.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of European, pizza and Italian dishes.

At the apartment guests are welcome to use a fitness room.

Cycling can be enjoyed nearby and a car rental service is available.

Mimosa Pattaya is 4.1 miles from Jomtien beach Condominium S2, while Nong Nooch Tropical Botanical Garden is 9 miles from the property. The nearest airport is U-Tapao Rayong-Pattaya International Airport, 17 miles from the accommodation.
