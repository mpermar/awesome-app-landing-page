Description

Centra by Centara Maris Resort Jomtien, located in southern Jomtien, boasts a lagoon-shaped swimming pool with a waterfall feature, tropical gardens and a hobbit-themed children's club.

The resort offers a range of accommodation types, all with free WiFi access and light wood furnishings.

Featuring views of the garden or pool, rooms and suites are all air-conditioned and provide a flat-screen TV with international channels.

Amenities include a one-touch control panel, a stocked minibar and tea/coffee making facilities.

Some units also include a living area and a furnished balcony. The en suite bathroom comes with free toiletries and a shower.

After a day of lounging by the pool, guests can indulge in pampering treatments at Cense by SPA Cenvaree or work out at the fitness centre.

The young ones will enjoy use of the children's pool, complete with waterslides, as well as a tech-friendly E-Zone entertainment area with video games.

Other facilities and services include laundry services, a free daily maid service and free parking.

For meals and drinks, guests staying at Centra by Centara Maris Resort Jomtien can sample both Thai and international dishes at one of the on-site restaurants.

Drinks can also be ordered at the pool bar and room service is available. A short drive from the beach, Centara Maris Resort Jomtien is less than a 30-minute drive from various attractions in Pattaya, including Central Festival Shopping Centre and the popular nightlife on Walking Street.

Utapao International Airport is a 50-minute drive away, while Suvarnabhumi International Airport and Bangkok can be reached within a 2-hour drive.
