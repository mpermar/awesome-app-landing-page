Description

All rooms in the resort are equipped with a electric tea pot. Every room includes air conditioning, and certain rooms at Bang Sarey Nordic Resort have a terrace. The rooms are equipped with a seating area.

You can play ping pong and pool at the accommodation.

The nearest airport is U-Tapao Rayong-Pattaya International Airport, 9 miles from the property.
