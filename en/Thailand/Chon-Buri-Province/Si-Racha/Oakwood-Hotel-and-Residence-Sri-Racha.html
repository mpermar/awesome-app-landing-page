Description

The rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. The rooms are equipped with a private bathroom. All rooms at Oakwood Hotel feature air conditioning and a desk.

A continental breakfast is served at the property.

The accommodation has a playground. You can play pool at this 5-star hotel.

Speaking English and Hindi at the 24-hour front desk, staff are ready to help around the clock.

Flight of The Gibbon is 10 miles from Oakwood Hotel and Residence Sri Racha. The nearest airport is U-Tapao Rayong-Pattaya International Airport, 34 miles from the hotel.
