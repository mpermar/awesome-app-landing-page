Description

Located on a private beach, Serene Sands Health Resort is within 15 minutes from downtown Pattaya.

The resort provides two-way scheduled transportation from Pattaya. Free Wi-Fi and parking are available.

Comfortable rooms at Serene Sands Health Resort feature a TV, DVD player and fridge.

Each room also has a safety deposit box and an electric kettle. At Serene Sands Health Resort guests can enjoy a dip in the 25 sqm salt water pool or relax in the spa bath.

Other facilities include a gym, as well as pool table and table tennis table. Tibetan bowl sound therapy, Shiatsu healing massage, Tai-Chi and Chi-kung lessons are also available.
