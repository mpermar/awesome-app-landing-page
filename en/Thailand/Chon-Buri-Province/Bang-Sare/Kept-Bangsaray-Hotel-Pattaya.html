Description

Situated in Bang Sare, Kept Bangsaray Hotel Pattaya has free bikes, bar, on-site dining, and free WiFi throughout the property.

The property is around 4 km from Cartoon Network Amazone Water Park and 8 km from RamaYana Water Park.

All rooms come with a balcony with views of the sea. At the resort, all rooms include a desk.

With a private bathroom, some rooms at Kept Bangsaray Hotel Pattaya also offer pool view.

All rooms at the accommodation have air conditioning and a wardrobe. A continental breakfast can be enjoyed at the property.

Kept Bangsaray Hotel Pattaya offers an outdoor pool. Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the resort.

The property also provides free uses of bicycles for guests during their stay. With staff speaking English and Thai, guidance is available at the reception.

The nearest airport is Utapao-Rayong-Pataya International, 15 km from Kept Bangsaray Hotel Pattaya, and the property offers a free airport shuttle service.
