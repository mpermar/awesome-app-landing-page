Description

Sunset Villa is a 25-minute drive from Pattaya City. It is located beside the sea and features an outdoor pool.

Guests can enjoy beverages and cocktails served at the poolside. Free WiFi is available in the lobby area.

Nong Nooch Tropical Garden is only a 5-minute walk from Sunset Villa. Silverlake Vineyard is a 10-minute ride away.

All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, a refrigerator and a minibar. The rooms offer an en suite bathroom with shower facilities.

Guests can relax with massage services offered at the resort. There is a tour desk to help guests with sightseeing arrangements.

The Sea Terrace Restaurant offers selection of fresh seafood, Thai and International cusines.
