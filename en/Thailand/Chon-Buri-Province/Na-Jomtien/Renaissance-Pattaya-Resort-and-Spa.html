Description

Five-star service, several dining options and modern facilities await guests at Renaissance Pattaya Resort & Spa.

The hotel offers guests a relaxing time with two outdoor swimming pools, kids' pool and kids' club.

Complimentary WiFi is available in all public areas and guest rooms. Each room, pool villa and family suite is equipped with modern decor and luxurious amenities.

It comes with air conditioning and is fitted with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Certain units have a seating area for your convenience. Views of the pool, garden or city are featured in certain rooms.

The rooms include a private bathroom. Extras include bathrobes and free toiletries.

Guests can make use of spacious venues, as well as on-site catering, audiovisual and event planning services.

Guests can work out at the fitness center accompanied by panoramic sea views or relax at at the full-service spa centre.

609 Kitchen serves international cuisine around the clock, while Pebbles Bar and Grill features tasty cocktails for lunch and dinner.

R Bar is where guests can meet up and unwind at the lobby area. They can also enjoy their favorite drinks whilst soaking up the sun at the Pool Bar along the infinity pool.

Situated in a vibrant area of Pattaya, Jomtien Beach is 15 km from Renaissance Pattaya Resort & Spa, while Ko Larn is 19 km from the property.

The nearest airport is U-Tapao Rayong-Pattaya International Airport, 25 km from the property, while Suvarnabhumi Airport is only a 90-minute drive away.
