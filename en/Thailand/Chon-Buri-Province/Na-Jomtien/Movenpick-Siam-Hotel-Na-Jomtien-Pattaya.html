Description

Offering a pristine and private beachfront area along Na Jomtien, Mövenpick Siam Hotel Na Jomtien Pattaya boasts a large lagoon swimming pool and panoramic sea views from all hotel rooms.

Various facilities are available at this 5-star property, as well as free WiFi access in public areas.

A short walk from Ocean Marina Yacht Club, Mövenpick Siam Hotel Na Jomtien Pattaya is a 15 km drive from Pattaya, where guests can find lively nightlife, shopping and a wide variety of restaurants.

Siam Country Club is a 15-minute drive away. Suvarnabhumi International Airport can be reached within a 2-hour drive.

Spacious rooms and suites at this hotel are equipped with air-conditioning, a flat-screen satellite TV and a minibar.

They have an indoor seating area and a furnished balcony overlooking the Gulf of Thailand.

En suite bathrooms come with a shower and free toiletries. Guests looking to relax can enjoy a pampering treatment at the spa.

The 24-hour front desk can assist with guests' needs and the concierge can help arrange transportation.

Free parking is available. Both Thai and international dishes are available at the on-site dining options.
