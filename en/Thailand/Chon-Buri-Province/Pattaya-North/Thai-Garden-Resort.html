Description

Just 1 km from Pattaya Bay, the 4-star Thai Garden Resort offers luxurious rooms with free Wi-Fi.

Nestled within palm trees, the large lagoon swimming pool is bordered by sun loungers.

Airport shuttles are available at an extra charge. Each of the resort’s spacious rooms is equipped with a flat-screen cable TV, a seating area and air conditioning.

They are fitted with crisp white linens, dark wood furniture and a modern bathroom with large mirrors.

The resort provides traditional Thai massages and foot reflexology sessions, which can be enjoyed in rooms or in the resort’s gardens.

Breakfast is available at Lanna Coffee Shop. Wong Amat Beach is located 2. 5 km from Thai Garden Resort, while the popular Walking Street Pattaya is about 3 km away.
