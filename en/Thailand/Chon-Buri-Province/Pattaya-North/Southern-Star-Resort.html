Description

Resort Southern Star is within a 10-minute drive of Pattaya’s walking street and the premier shopping mall, Central Festival Pattaya. It offers free parking and is a 1.5-hour drive from Bangkok.

Featuring a private balcony, air conditioned rooms are equipped with classic wood furnishings and have a minibar and safe. Heated showers are available in the private bathroom.

Guests can enjoy refreshing drinks at the poolside bar or lounge by the outdoor pool. The hotel also provides bike rental, massage and laundry services.

Naklua Beach is a great choice for travelers interested in tranquillity, swimming pools and seafood.
