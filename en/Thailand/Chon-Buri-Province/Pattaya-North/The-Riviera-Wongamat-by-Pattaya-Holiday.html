Description

Situated within 1. 8 km of The Sanctuary of Truth and 3. 3 km of Tiffany Show in Pattaya North, The Riviera Wongamat by Pattaya Holiday provides accommodation with a kitchen.

Complimentary WiFi is featured. Fitted with a terrace, the units feature a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with shower.

Some units have a balcony and/or patio. Guests can make use of the hot tub, sauna and indoor pool.

An outdoor swimming pool can be found at the property, along with a garden. Healthland Spa and Massage is 3.

4 km from the apartment, while Mini Siam is 5 km from the property. The nearest airport is U-Tapao Rayong-Pattaya International Airport, 50 km from The Riviera Wongamat by Pattaya Holiday.
