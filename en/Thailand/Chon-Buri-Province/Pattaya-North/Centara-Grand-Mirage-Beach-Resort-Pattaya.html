Description

Exciting beach holidays await at Centara Grand Mirage with a beachfront water theme park and direct beach access.

Featuring stunning ocean views, it has fitness facilities and a range of dining options.

Located along the Wong Amat and Naklua Beaches, Centara Grand Mirage Beach Resort Pattaya is 3 km from Pattaya Train Station.

Offering free parking, it is a 2-hour drive from Suvarnabhumi International Airport.

Boasting sea views from private balconies, modern Thai-style rooms offer the 5-star comforts of a flat-screen TV and rain shower.

They feature pleasant shades of green and comfortable seating areas. Guests will be spoilt for choice with extensive leisure facilities including large lagoon pools, tennis courts and a first-rate spa.

Enjoy a range of activities from snorkeling to rock climbing at Centara Pattaya. Start the day with a hearty buffet breakfast at Oasis.
