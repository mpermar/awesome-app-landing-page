Description

A beachfront property in Pattaya, Grand Heritage Beach Resort & Spa is a 10-minute drive from the center of the city.

The spacious rooms have hardwood floors and modern Thai décor. Each room has a flat-screen TV, a DVD player, and tea/coffee making facilities. The private bathroom has a bathtub and shower.

Guests can enjoy a massage in the spa, or exercise in the gym. For younger guests, there is a playroom and a children’s pool with water slides. Activities on the beach include snorkeling and windsurfing.

On a terrace with pool and sea views, the restaurant serves both local and international dishes. Refreshing drinks are available at the bar.
