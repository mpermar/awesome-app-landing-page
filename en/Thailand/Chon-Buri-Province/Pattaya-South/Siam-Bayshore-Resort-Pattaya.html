Description

In Pattaya’s Cholburi area, Siam Bayshore Resort & Spa is just over a quarter mile from Khao Phra Bat Temple. Bali-Hai Pier is about half a mile from the property.

The rooms at Siam Bayshore feature rattan, bamboo or teak wood furnishings. In addition to cable TV, a tea and coffee maker and a minibar are included. The private bathrooms have a bathtub and a hairdryer.

The 24-hour Sunrise Sunset Restaurant serves international dishes and buffet breakfast. Other dining options include Siam Elephant Thai Restaurant, Bali-Hai Chinese & Seafood Restaurant and Seafood BBQ Terrace. Drinks are available at the hotel’s 4 bars.

The Lotus Spa of Siam features deluxe spa services among the tropical landscaped grounds. Guests can lounge by the outdoor spa tub or exercise at the fitness center. Parking is free.
