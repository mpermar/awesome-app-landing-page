Description

Located in the South Pattaya, FX Hotel Pattaya is just a 5-minute drive from Pattaya Park Water World and a 10-minute walk to Pattaya Night Market.

This hotel features an outdoor pool and fitness facilities. Guests can access to free WiFi in all area.

Restaurant and free parking are available on site. All rooms here are air conditioned and equipped with a cable flat-screen TV.

Guests can enjoy either mountain or pool views from their room. Every room comes with an en suite bathroom, while some also have a seating area for relaxation.

At this hotel, front desk is available 24 hours. Bali Hai Pier is 1. 6 km from FX Hotel Pattaya.

The Outlet Mall Pattaya is 5. 7 km from the property. The nearest airport is Utapao-Rayong-Pataya International Airport, 39 km away.
