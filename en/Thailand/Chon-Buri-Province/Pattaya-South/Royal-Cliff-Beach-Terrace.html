Description

Offering an infinity outdoor pool overlooking the sea and a private beach area, Royal Cliff Beach Terrace is located in Pattaya South.

It is just 800 metres from Bali Hai Pier and 1. 5 km from Walking Street Pattaya.

During the stay, guests can take advantage of a fitness centre, massage service and a specialized bar and restaurant.

This property provides free parking space and free WiFi. Elegantly decorated with modern amenities, each stylish room comes with flat-screen cable TV, a DVD player and a sofa set.

A safety deposit box, ironing facilities and a minibar are standard in all units.

Guests can enjoy sea views from the room. Complete with a shower, en suite bathroom are supplied with a bathtub and free toiletries.

Guests’ facilities at Terrace Royal Cliff Beach include a 24-hour front desk, luggage storage and laundry service.

Meeting facilities, a business centre and Kid’s club are available. For sporty guests, they can enjoy tennis, table tennis and squash on-site.

The hotel also arrange shuttle service to and from designated spots at additional costs.

Under a journey of 4 km, guests can find Alcazar Cabaret and Outlet Mall Pattaya; whilst Jomtien Beach and Mini Siam can be reached within 6 km.

Suvarnabhumi Airport is situated 86 km from Royal Cliff Beach Terrace. Open for all-day dining, the in-house restaurant serves tasty Thai and international dishes.
