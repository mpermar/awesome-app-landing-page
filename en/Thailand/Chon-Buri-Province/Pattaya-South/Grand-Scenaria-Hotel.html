Description

Grand Scenaria Hotel is a 10-minute drive from Pattaya Walking Street. It offers air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi.

The hotel features a pool, fitness and tour desk. Free public parking is possible on site.

The hotel is a 7-minute drive from Bali Hai Pier and Pattaya Beach. Rooms are fitted with a flat-screen cable TV, minibar and electric kettle.

They also come with a seating area and safety deposit box. Shower facilities are included in an en suite bathroom.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can help guests arrange the shuttle service. Dining options at this hotel include LIQUID, which houses a separate BBQ bar, a poolside bar and a rooftop lounge.

Guests can enjoy refreshing beverages including local and international beers. An extensive buffet breakfast and a selection of a la carte international dishes, as well as Russian cuisine can be sampled at Palate Restaurant.
