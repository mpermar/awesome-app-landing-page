Description

Brave Lion is 100 metres from Dong Tan Beach. It offers modern air-conditioned rooms with a private balcony and a pantry area.

Providing free Wi-Fi in public areas, the hotel also features luggage storage and laundry services.

The property is a 5-minute drive to Phatamnak Mountain and a 10-minute drive to Jomtien Beach.

It is a 15-minute drive to Central Festival Department Store. Suvarnabhumi Airport is a 1 hr drive away.

Rooms at Brave Lion are equipped with a flat-screen TV, a DVD player and a seating area.

Hot shower facilities are included in an en suite bathroom. Guests can enjoy a variety of international dishes and refreshing beverages at the hotel’s restaurant.
