Description

Located in Suratthani, 2. 7 km from Bandon, Chang Bed And Bar Hostel provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a bar and a shared lounge.

Featuring room service, this property also provides guests with a barbecue. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, a shared kitchen and currency exchange for guests.

At the hostel each room is equipped with air conditioning and a shared bathroom. Guests at Chang Bed And Bar Hostel can enjoy a à la carte breakfast.

The accommodation offers a terrace. The area is popular for cycling, and car hire is available at Chang Bed And Bar Hostel.

The nearest airport is Surat Thani, 30 km from the hostel, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
