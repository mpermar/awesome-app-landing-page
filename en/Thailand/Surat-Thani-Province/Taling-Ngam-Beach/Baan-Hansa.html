Description

With direct access to a terrace, the villa consists of 5 bedrooms. A flat-screen TV is provided.

A continental or Asian breakfast is available every morning at the property.

The reception can offer helpful tips for getting around the area.

Spa and wellness facilities including a hot tub, a sauna and a fitness center are at guests' disposal during their stay at the villa. If you would like to discover the area, canoeing is possible nearby and a car rental service can be arranged.

The nearest airport is Samui International Airport, 11 miles from the property.
