Description

This tropical Thai-style beach resort features an outdoor pool and a private beach.

It offers a range of bungalows with free Wi-Fi and beautiful views of the garden or sea.

Relaxing Thai massage is also available. Fully air-conditioned, bungalows at Am Samui Resort all come with a satellite TV, minibar and personal safe.

A private balcony and private bathroom are provided. Am Samui Resort is a 45-minute drive from Chaweng Beach.

Transfers to the airport are available at an extra charge. Guests can arrange day trips at the tour desk, or request for room service.

The resort also provides laundry and ironing services. Thai cuisine and fresh seafood are served at the resort's restaurant.

Refreshing cocktails accompanied by colourful sunset views can be enjoyed at the bar.
