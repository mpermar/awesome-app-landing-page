Description

Situated only a short walk from the beach in Bophut, The shore samui offers comfortable rooms with sea views and free WiFi access in all areas.

It is located just 2. 3 km away from the famous Big Buddha Temple and only 1. 8 km from Bo Phut Pier.

Complete with a TV, all the air-conditioned room also comes with a refrigerator. A sofa and a telephone is also available in some rooms.

The private bathroom features shower facilities, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

For the guests' convenience, The shore samui provides car and bicycle rental services as well as laundry facilities.

For drivers, the inn also provides free private parking. With a short drive, guests can find many of Koh Samui's attractions including Samui Crocodile Farm (800 metres), Fisherman Village (2.

1 km) and Chaweng Walking Street (8. 1 km). Samui Airport is located 2. 8 km away.
