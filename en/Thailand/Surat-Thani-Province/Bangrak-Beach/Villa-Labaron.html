Description

Located 1. 8 km from Big Buddha, Villa Labaron boasts a beach front accommodation with a communal outdoor pool.

This villa provides free WiFi access in all areas. Villa Labaron is 600 metres from Samui Crocodile Farm.

A 3-minute drive will bring guests to Fisherman Village. Samui Airport is 1 km away.

Featuring a terrace with an outdoor seating area, this air-conditioned villa offers a living area, a sofa set and a flat-screen TV.

It also has a microwave, a fridge and a dining area. Shower facilities, free toiletries and towels are included in an en suite bathroom.

At Villa Labaron, guests will find sun loungers and airport shuttle service. Bangrak Pier, from which guests can commute to Koh Pangan, is 1 km away.
