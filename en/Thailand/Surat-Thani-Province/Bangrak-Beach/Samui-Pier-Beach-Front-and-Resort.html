Description

Samui Pier Beach Front & Resort is just 2 minutes’ walk from Bangrak Beach. It features an outdoor pool and rooms with sea views.

Wi-Fi is free in public areas. Offering views of Koh Phangan, the air-conditioned rooms have cable TV, a safety deposit box, and tea/coffee making facilities.

Each room has a private bathroom with a shower. Samui Pier Beach Front & Resort is about 5 minutes’ drive from Samui International Airport and Big Buddha Temple.

Koh Samui is an 80-minute flight from Bangkok’s Suvarnabhumi International Airport.

Guests can have a relaxing massage, or enjoy water activities like diving and fishing.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can arrange tour bookings, shuttle services, and laundry.

Car rentals and luggage storage are also available. Thai and European dishes are served at the restaurant.
