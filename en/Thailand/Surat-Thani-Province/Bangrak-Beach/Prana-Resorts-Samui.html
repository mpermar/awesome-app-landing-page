Description

Prana Resort Samui boats an outdoor swimming pool, a fitness centre as well as a spa.

It also features a 24-hour reception with concierge services. Free WiFi access is available for all guests.

Prana Resorts Samui is a 5-minute drive from Samui Airport. It is a 3-minute drive from Fisherman's Village and the Big Buddha.

Chaweng Beach and Central Festival Samui are both a short drive away. Koh Phangan, where the Full Moon Party is held every month, is just 30 minutes away by ferry.

Stylish and spacious rooms offer air conditioning and a private balcony or patio with seating.

All well-appointed rooms are equipped with a 42-inch flat-screen TV, tea/coffee making facilities and a safety deposit box.

En suite bathrooms are fitted with shower facilities and a hairdryer. Suites include a separate living area and sea views, while some rooms feature a pool access.

Additional services and facilities include a kid's club, meeting facilities and an outdoor spa bath.

The property also offers a chargeable shuttle to the city centre and Fisherman's village.

Free on-site parking is provided. A variety of western cuisines are also offered a rooftop terrace restaurant called Zama.
