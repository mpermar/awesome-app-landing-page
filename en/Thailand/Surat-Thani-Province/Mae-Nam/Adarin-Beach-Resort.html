Description

Standing along the shores of Koh Samui, Adarin Beach Resort enjoys scenic views of Koh Phangan and offers guests spacious suites and villas.

Adarin Beach Resort is located on Koh Samui’s Mae Nam Beach, a stretch of unspoiled white, sandy beach that is almost six kilometres long.

The crystal clear water next to Adarin Beach Resort is perfect for snorkelling, diving and fishing.

A cool breeze can be enjoyed on the beach all day long. Decorated with Thai-style furnishings, each suite and villa is spacious, bright and airy.

Some villas are located on the beachfront, with the sea right at their doorsteps.

Other suites and villas are set in a rich, tropical garden, offering you a natural and peaceful setting.

From Thai dishes and seafood specialities to Mediterranean and European creations, Adarin Beach Resort’s restaurant offers a wide range cuisine.

Dine on the beach under the blue sky or enjoy a romantic candlelight dinner under the stars.
