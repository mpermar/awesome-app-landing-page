Description

Ampha Place is located in Koh Samui, a 3-minute drive from Mae Nam Beach. It features a restaurant, an outdoor swimming pool and air-conditioned rooms with a balcony.

Free WiFi and parking are provided. Rooms at Ampha come with modern interiors and plenty of natural light.

All rooms are equipped with a cable TV, refrigerator and an en suite bathroom with shower facilities.

Available for all-day dining, Ampha restaurant serves a fine selection of Thai dishes.

Wine and cocktails are also offered. Homemade jams are also available during breakfast time.

Guests can take a stroll in the garden or relax on the terrace. The staff can also assist guests with car and motorbike hire.

Darts, boules, games, books and beach towels are also available for guests to use and enjoy, free of charge.

Place Ampha is a 10-minute walk from the lively centre of Mae Nam Village and a 20-minute drive from Samui International Airport.
