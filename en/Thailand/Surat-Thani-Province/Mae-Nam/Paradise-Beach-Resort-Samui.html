Description

Set in lush tropical gardens on Koh Samui's tranquil Maenam Beach, Paradise Beach Resort is a perfect place for relaxation.

It offers two swimming pools, large and modern furnished rooms, a sophisticated restaurant, a bar and an attractive choice of activities.

Paradise Beach Resort is located on the shores of the tranquil Maenam beach and is just about 500 metres from the centre of the village.

Complimentary round trip shuttle services to/from Fisherman's Village and Chaweng's city centre are provided.

Furnished in modern Thai style, the intimate resort has 77 grand deluxe rooms, 6 suites, 9 villas, 6 Maenam suites and 2 beach front villas.

Each accommodation comes with a private balcony or verandah. There is air conditioning and a private bathroom with shower facilities.
