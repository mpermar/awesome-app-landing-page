Description

Offering a choice of bungalows to cozy guestrooms in the main building, all rooms are air-conditioned and comes with views of the sea or garden. The private bathroom is fitted with a hand/rain shower and complimentary toiletries. Other amenities include a flat-screen TV, a fridge and a safety deposit box.

Activities including sunset tours, island day trips and fishing can all be arranged for an additional charge. Other services such as motorbike/car hire & transportation are all available. Complete the day after a long day out with a traditional Thai massage and relaxing treatments, which can be booked at the front desk.

Open from 07:00 - 22:00 hrs, Castaway Restaurant & Bar offers a wide selected of Thai and international dishes. Guests can sit back by the pool while enjoying refreshing drinks and cocktails from the bar.

It is 100 feet to Mae Nam Beach. Samui Airport is 5 mi from the resort. Guests can visit nearby attractions in Bophut including the Fisher Man Village (1.2 mi) and Wat Phra Yai (4.3 mi).
