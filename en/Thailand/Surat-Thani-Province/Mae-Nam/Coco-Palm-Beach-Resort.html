Description

Set on peaceful Maenam Beach, Coco Palm Beach Resort boasts beautifully landscaped grounds away from Samuis bustle.

Offering free WiFi and parking, it boasts a beachfront outdoor pool and beachside restaurant.

Coco Palm Beach Resort is about 10 km from Samui Airport and Nathorn Pier. It is 5 km from lively Bophut and is a 1-minute walk from Lomprayah Pier.

Featuring Thai-style designs and wood furnishings, air-conditioned bungalows are set amidst tropical greenery.

They come with satellite TV, heated showers and bathrobes. Leisure afternoons can be spent with a Thai massage or relaxing stroll in gardens.

Added conveniences include a business centre, tour desk and water sports facilities.

A delicious selection of Thai, Asian and international dishes can be enjoyed at Palm Terrace Restaurant while Thai Lantern offers panoramic views of the gulf.
