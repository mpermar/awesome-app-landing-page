Description

Shangrilah Bungalow is located within a 1-minute walk to the beautiful Maenam Beach of Koh Samui.

Equipped with free public parking, the guest house offers modern air-conditioned rooms overlooking the green garden.

The property is a 5-minute drive to Lomprayah Pier connecting to Koh Phangan and Koh Tao.

It is a 15-minute drive to Fisherman’s Village and Big Buddha. Samui Airport is a 20-minute drive away.

Simply furnished, each bungalow features a TV and a seating area. A refrigerator, a wardrobe and a safety deposit box are provided.

Shower facilities are included in an en suite bathroom. For convenience, the hotel has an on-site tour desk where staff can help guests organise sightseeing tours.

A shuttle service is available upon request with extra charges. Shangrilah Restaurant serves an amazing selection of Thai and European dishes.
