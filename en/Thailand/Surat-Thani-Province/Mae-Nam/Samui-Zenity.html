Description

Samui Zenity is set a 5-minute bicycle ride away from Mae Nam Beach in Koh Samui.

The hotel offers free shuttle services to and from Mae Nam Beach as well as free bike rentals.

Scooter rentals are offered. Available facilities include an outdoor pool with a water slide, bar, sun terrace and views of the mountain.

Free private parking is available. Each room at this hotel is air-conditioned and is equipped with a flat-screen TV.

All rooms have a seating area where you can relax. Enjoy a cup of tea from your terrace or balcony.

All rooms are fitted with a private bathroom equipped with a shower. Samui Zenity features free WiFi throughout the property.

Local foods can be found nearby. There is ticket service at the property. Samui Zenity also provides daily shuttle services to Maenam Pier at an additional charge.
