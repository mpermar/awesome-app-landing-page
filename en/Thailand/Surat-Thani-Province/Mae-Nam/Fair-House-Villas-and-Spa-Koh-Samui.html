Description

A tropical and relaxing getaway along Mae Nam Beach can be enjoyed at Fair House Villas & Spa, Koh Samui.

Aside from a private beach, it has an outdoor pool and free Wi-Fi throughout the property.

A chargeable airport shuttle is also available upon request. Fair House Villas & Spa is 10 km from Koh Samui’s city centre and Chaweng Beach.

Samui Airport is 15 km from the resort. Spacious and elegant, rooms feature classic wooden furnishings and floor-to-ceiling windows which let in natural light.

A cable TV and minibar are included in all rooms. Some come with ocean views. Lazy afternoons can be spent at Pandanus Spa with its relaxing massage therapies.

With panoramic views of the ocean, the fitness centre provides a refreshing setting to exercise.

Delicious Thai and European dishes are served at the resort’s restaurant. Every night, a special barbecue dinner with fresh seafood is available.
