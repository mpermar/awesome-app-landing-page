Description

Featuring spacious interiors, the air-conditioned rooms are tastefully furnished with dark wood fittings and local fabrics. They open out to tropical views and feature bathrooms with shower facilities.

The Chaweng Garden Beach Resort is just a short walk from the restaurant and bars lining Beach Road. It is a 15-minute drive from Koh Samui Airport and provides free parking.

Leisurely afternoons can be spent with a massage by the outdoor pool. Other conveniences include water sports facilities, a tour desk and free Wi-Fi in public areas.

Shaded by palm trees, the Sukulaya Restaurant features fresh seafood grilled specialties and international dishes by the seaside.
