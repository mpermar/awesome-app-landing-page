Description

Chaweng Cove Beach Resort is around a 20-minute drive from Samui International Airport. It is a 15-minute walk from Chawengs town center.

Featuring wood furnishings, the air-conditioned rooms all have a flat-screen TV and a DVD player. A minibar and tea/coffee-making facilities are provided. Some rooms have a private balcony with sitting area.

Guests can relax on the lounge chairs provided on the beach. Samui Wellness Spa offers massages, facials and body treatments. The resort has a small fitness room, a tour desk, and a library area.

Fresh seafood, Thai and international dishes are served at the restaurant. Cocktails and traditional Thai refreshments are available at Sea Almond Beach Bar.
