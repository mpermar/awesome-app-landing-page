Description

Banana Fan Sea Resort is located on the tranquil southern tip of Chaweng Beach, within a stroll of its entertainment and shopping spots.

The resort provides well-appointed rooms, an outdoor pool with hot tub and free Wi-Fi.

Each air-conditioned room comes with satellite TV, a mini bar and coffee/tea making facilities.

They have an en suite bathroom fitted with a bathtub, large mirrors and toiletries.

Guests can enjoy a traditional Thai massage outdoors, by the pool and on the beach, or in the comfort of their rooms.

For an exciting day outdoors, visit the many sports centre offering diving and boating trips, located along the beach.

Banana Fans beachfront Baitong Restaurant offers an open-air dining concept and serves both local and international dishes.

Room service is available. Banana Fan Sea Resort is a 15-minute drive from Samui Airport.
