Description

Buri Rasa's spacious rooms are of a blend of traditional Thai decor with modern facilities. It provides a private bathroom, four-poster bed and cable TV with DVD player. Tea/coffee maker and a minibar are provided.

Guests can enjoy a beach side dining at the Beach Club @ Buri Rasa. The lobby and beach bars serve drinks.

For relaxation, guests can have traditional Thai herbal spa and therapy or soak up the sun at the outdoor pool or Chaweng Beach. Buri Rasa Village offers airport shuttle and car rental services. Parking is free.

Buri Rasa Village is a 15 minute drive from Samui Airport.
