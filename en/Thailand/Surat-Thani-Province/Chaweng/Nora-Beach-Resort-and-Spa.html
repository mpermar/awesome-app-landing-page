Description

Classical Thai-style rooms are built atop a slope overlooking a secluded bay. Modern luxuries like a flat-screen satellite TV and minibar are available in all rooms. Private bathrooms have a bathtub.

Nora Beach Resort & Spa is a 10 minute drive from Samui International Airport. It is within 3 miles of Fisherman's Village and Bo Phut Pier. Free on-site parking is provided.

Anodas Spa features rejuvenating massage treatments, while the fitness center provides a full body workout. Other facilities include a tour desk and laundry services.

Pra-Suthon Restaurant serves authentic Thai cuisine, Western dishes and fresh barbecued seafood. A daily buffet breakfast is also available there.
