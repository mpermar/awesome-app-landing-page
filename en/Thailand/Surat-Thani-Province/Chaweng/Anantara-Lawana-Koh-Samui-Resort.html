Description

Anantara Lawana Koh Samui Resort offers 5-star luxury with modern Thai-style suites and spacious villas.

Featuring a first-rate spa, it has restaurants boasting stunning views and an outdoor infinity pool overlooking the ocean.

Featuring private balconies with outdoor seating, tastefully furnished suites and villas are fitted with free WiFi, a cable TV and DVD player.

Spacious bathrooms have a bathtub and hairdryer. Steps from a peaceful beachfront, Anantara Lawana is a 10-minute walk from Chaweng Beach and its nightlife options.

It is a 10-minute drive from Samui Airport. Guests are spoilt for choice with a wide range of water sports, activities and trips organised by the tour desk.

Other recreation options include a library and fitness centre. Panoramic ocean views accompany hearty international buffets at Ocean Kiss Restaurant.

Tree Tops Restaurant offers a unique dining experience high amidst the tall trees.
