Description

Located on Lamai Beach, Beach Republic offers luxurious 4-star accommodation with free WiFi and modern furnishings.

It features an outdoor pool overlooking the sea, spa as well as fitness and wellness centre.

Rooms at Beach Republic are spacious enough to accommodate seating area and an en suite bathroom with amenities and bathrobe.

A in-room safe, minibar and tea/coffee maker are provided. Guests can enjoy relaxing massage treatments at Asian Fusion Spa or make use of the resort’s spa bath.

A variety of wellness programs are available at Health Republic. Staff at Beach Republic can help guests with trip planning and tour booking.

Vehicle rental, airport shuttle and laundry services are provided. Free private parking is available.

Guests enjoy fine dining and superb wines at Ocean Club & Restaurant which also serves the famous weekly Sunday Sessions Brunch while offering views of Lamai Bay.

Beach Republic is about a 30-minute drive from Samui Airport. Lamai Town is a 10-minute drive from the property.
