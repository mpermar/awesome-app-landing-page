Description

Featuring modern Thai decor, the air-conditioned rooms feature gorgeous sea views. All come equipped with a flat-screen TV, a refrigerator and personal safe. Private bathrooms have a rain shower.

Beluga Boutique Hotel is a 10-minute walk from nightlife of Lamai and a 30-minute drive from Samui International Airport. Airport shuttles can be arranged for an extra fee.

Day trips and car rentals can be arranged at the tour desk. Water sports facilities like diving and snorkeling are also available. The hotel also provides laundry services.

Thai and European dishes are served at the restaurant. In-room dining is possible with room service.
