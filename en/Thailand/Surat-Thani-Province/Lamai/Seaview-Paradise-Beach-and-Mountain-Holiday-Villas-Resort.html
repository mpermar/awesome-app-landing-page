Description

Situated on the quiet end of Lamai Beach, Seaview Paradise Holiday Resort offers private beachfront villas and rooms with stunning sea views.

It also has accommodation in its Mountain Estate, which features an infinity edge pool.

Beachfront villas and rooms are just steps from the Gulf of Thailand, while Mountain Villas are perched 150 metres above beautiful Lamai.

The spacious air-conditioned villas come with a balcony, living room and kitchen.

They are furnished with satellite TV, a DVD player and a minibar. Seaview Paradise Holiday Villas Resort is situated close to restaurants, on a less than 10-minute drive from Lamai Temple.

Samui Airport is a 30-minute drive away. Guests can arrange a day trip at the tour desk, or arrange babysitting services at the concierge.

The hotel also provides an airport shuttle, laundry services and relaxing massage treatments.
