Description

Set upon a hill in Koh Samui, Silavadee Pool Spa Resort enjoys a private beach and an outdoor infinity pool.

Aside from free WiFi, it also has a free DVD library. Overlooking the Gulf of Siam, Silavadee Resort offers free shuttle services to Lamai and Chaweng.

Samui Airport is about 9 km from the resort. Spacious and decked with luxurious teak wood furnishings, the Thai-style rooms feature modern amenities like a flat-screen TV, iPod dock and minibar.

En suite bathrooms are equipped with bathtubs and hairdryers. The peaceful Silavadee Wellness Spa has a relaxing menu of massage treatments and beauty therapies.

The resort also arranges a business centre and laundry services. Fresh seafood, traditional Thai and international dishes are served at The Height Restaurant, which has open-air seating overlooking stunning views of the Gulf of Thailand.

The pool has a bar offering cocktails and fruit juices. The Moon Restaurant provides all guests with all day dining, serving varieties of International and Mediterranean dinner a la carte.
