Description

The rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a electric tea pot, a shower, free toiletries and a closet. With a private bathroom, rooms at the resort also offer a mountain view. All rooms at Phannarai House come with a seating area.

The accommodation has a sun terrace.

Grandfather and Grandmother stone is 1.4 miles from Phannarai House, while Samui Aquarium is 3.4 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Samui International Airport, 6 miles from the property.
