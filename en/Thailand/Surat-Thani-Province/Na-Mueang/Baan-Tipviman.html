Description

All rooms in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Baan Tipviman provides certain rooms with pool views, and rooms have a electric tea pot. All guest rooms at the accommodation have air conditioning and a closet.

Guests can use the hot tub. You can play pool at Baan Tipviman, and car rental is available.

Chaweng is 8 miles from the resort, while Lamai is 5 miles from the property. Samui International Airport is 9 miles away, and the property offers a free airport shuttle service.
