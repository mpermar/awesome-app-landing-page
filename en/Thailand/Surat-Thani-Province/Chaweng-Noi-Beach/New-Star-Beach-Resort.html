Description

Situated on Chaweng Beach in Koh Samui, New Star Beach Resort offers luxurious beachfront accommodation.

It features an infinity-edge pool and 2 dining options. Free parking is provided.

Rooms and villas are decorated in a tropical style with wooden furnishings. Each room has a private balcony, TV and minibar.

Treat yourself to a massage at Akiira Spa, or go on a dive trip or an elephant trek.

Other facilities include laundry service and car rental. Breakfast buffet is served at Lime Restaurant, which offers indoor and al fresco seating.

Sip afternoon tea at the Lobby Lounge or arrange for a meal by the pool. New Star Beach Resort is a 15-minute drive from Samui Airport.
