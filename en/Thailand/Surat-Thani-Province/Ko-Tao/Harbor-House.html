Description

Situated in Ko Tao, 400 metres from Mae Haad Pier, Harbor House features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a bar and a shared lounge.

All rooms boast a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom. The hotel has family rooms.

The rooms at the hotel have air conditioning and a wardrobe. A continental breakfast is available each morning at Harbor House.

The accommodation offers a sun terrace. The area is popular for cycling, and bike hire is available at Harbor House.

Nangyuan Island is 3. 9 km from the hotel, while Sunken Ship is 4. 1 km from the property.
