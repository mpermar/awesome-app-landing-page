Description

Khao Sok Nature Place features bright and airy rooms with air conditioning. Located 700 metre away from Khao Sok National Park, it offers on-site parking and a tour desk.

Free Wi-Fi is available in all areas. Khao Sok Nature Place is situated 60 km from Ratchaprapa Dam and 105 km from Surat Thani Airport.

Comfortable rooms feature a private balcony and a seating area. They also have a flat-screen cable TV and a refrigerator.

Free toiletries are provided in an en suite bathroom. A range of restaurants can be found at a distance of 5 km from the property.
