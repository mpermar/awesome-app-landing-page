Description

Our Jungle Camp - Eco Resort is located in Khao Sok National Park in the Surat Thani Province.

The restaurant serves dishes made from fresh ingredients from the on-site farm. Part of the proceeds from stayed guests will also be sponsored towards a nature camp for Thai children to learn about nature.

Varying from a choice of tree houses, bungalows and bamboo huts, all rooms come with a balcony with views of the river or nearby jungle.

Each unit has its own private bathroom. Activities that guests can enjoy around the property include full day and overnight tour to the Cheow Lan Lake, full day and overnight hikes, tubing and cooking classes.
