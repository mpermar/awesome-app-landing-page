Description

Santhiya Koh Phangan Resort and Spa is just 500 feet from the popular Thong Nai Pan Noi Beach. The resort is a 40 minute speed boat ride from Koh Samui.

The stylish air-conditioned rooms feature elegant teakwood furnishings and classic Thai decor. Each comes with a cable TV, DVD player and minibar. Selected rooms have whirlpool bathtub.

Santhiya's Ayuravana Spa features a relaxing menu of facials and body massage. There is also an on-site water sports center offering activities like canoeing, kayaking and windsurfing.

Chantara Restaurant serves Thai cuisine, with the option of open-air dining. A daily breakfast buffet is also available there every morning.
