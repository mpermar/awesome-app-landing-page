Description

A dreamy retreat hidden amongst tropical greenery, Panviman Koh Phangan overlooks Thong Nai Pan and boasts a private beach access.

The infinity pool and hilltop spa are excellent spots to catch spectacular sunrises.

Panviman Koh Phangan is a 30-minute boat ride from Koh Samui. Than Sadet Waterfall is about 4 km from the resort.

Island transfers and shuttles can also be arranged for an additional charge. All rooms are luxurious with fine beddings and furnishings.

They are designed to provide all the comfort of home and provide beautiful landscape views.

They have large private balconies and lavish bathrooms with an indoor or outdoor bathtub.

Guests also enjoy uses of a smartphone with free data and call services in all room types.

Guests can enjoy local and international cuisine at Panviman’s two beachfront restaurants, Pan Sea and Stone Beach restaurants.

The Pan Sea offers a 360 degree view of the Gulf of Siam. Activities include a variety of water sports like diving, snorkelling and kayaking, or land activities like trekking or excursions in jeeps.
