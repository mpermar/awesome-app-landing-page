Description

Rooms and villas at Mai Samui Beach Resort & Spa are decorated in a contemporary style with a Thai touch, using local woods and fabrics. Offering stunning sea view, accommodations at Mai Samui are equipped with a variety of amenities for your comfort. It includes high-speed internet access and a private pool for villas.

Mai Samui Beach Resort & Spa offers a range of facilities that will help you relax and recharge. Set amidst a lush landscaped garden, soak up the sun by the large swimming pool or take a refreshing dip. For a pampering experience, head to the spa where a menu of body treatments and massage therapies await you.

For those looking to maintain their regular work-out, there is a well-equipped fitness center and racquetball courts available. Other facilities include a game room and kids club, as well as 3 different restaurants where you can enjoy a delicious selection of food that will satisfy your appetite.

For convenience, Mai Samui Beach Resort & Spa offers a shuttle bus service to Chaweng, 3 times, daily.
