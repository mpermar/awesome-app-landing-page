Description

All rooms features air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fridge, a electric tea pot, a shower and a closet. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with free toiletries, while certain rooms include a terrace. At the resort the rooms include a seating area.

The daily breakfast offers American and Asian options.

Canoeing is among the activities that guests can enjoy near THE MUD.

The nearest airport is Samui International Airport, 9 miles from the accommodation.
