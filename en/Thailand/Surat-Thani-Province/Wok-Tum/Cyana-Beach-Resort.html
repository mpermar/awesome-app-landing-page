Description

The Cyana Resort is at Woktum Bay, a 1-hour drive from Koh Samui. The resort provides car rental facilities for guests who would like to explore the island.

All rooms at Cyana Resort have great views of the ocean or tropical grounds. They are well-furnished with a safe, a minibar and a flat-screen TV with DVD player.

To make the most of the tropical climate, enjoy water activities such as fishing, and snorkeling.

The Beach Restaurant provides a Thai and international menu. It also offers fresh seafood dishes.
