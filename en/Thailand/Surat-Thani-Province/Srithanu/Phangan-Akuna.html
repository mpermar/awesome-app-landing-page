Description

Located in Sri Thanu on Koh Phangan, Phangan Akuna boasts a year-round outdoor pool and accommodation surrounded by coconut plantations and lush evergreen trees.

For guests to stay connected, free WiFi is provided in-rooms and all throughout the public areas.

Every unit opens up to a large balcony and is fitted with air conditioning, LED TV with cable satellite channels and a safety deposit box.

Additional room amenities include a minibar, complimentary coffee and tea as well as a beach bag with umbrellas and slippers.

The private bathroom includes a bath or shower and a hairdryer. A daily breakfast can be enjoyed at the property.
