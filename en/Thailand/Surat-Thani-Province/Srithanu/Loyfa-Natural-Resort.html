Description

Located on the secluded Sri Thanu peninsula on the west coast of Koh Phangan, Loyfa Natural Resort features 2 outdoor pools among natural surroundings and their private beach front.

Free WiFi access is available. All of the accommodation at Loyfa Natural Resort have a satellite TV, air conditioning and a minibar.

Rooms, stretching from the shoreline to the crest of hilltop, have sea views or are situated on the beach front.

Shower facilities can be found in the private bathroom. A short walk from the village of Sri Thanu, it is located within a 5-minute walk from convenient stores and is a 15-minute drive from Thongsala Pier and Haad Yao.

With a touch of traditional Thai and local spirit, Loyfa Natural Resort offers treatments for the retreat of your body & mind.

The resort serves a variety of European and Thai cuisines at Loyfa Restaurant, which overlooks the beach.
