Description

Benjamin's Hut is within a 5-minute walk from the local shops and a minimart. It is a 10-minute drive from Thongsala Pier. Traveling to Koh Samui is a 1.5 hour ride by both land and ferry.

Simply furnished, each hut is equipped with a fan and private bathrooms with shower facilities. Each hut also has a private balcony with outdoor seating.

Guests can use free Wi-Fi in the hotel's public areas, arrange diving and snorkeling trips at the tour desk, or request for a Thai massage. The hotel also provides laundry services and free parking.

The hotel's restaurant serves a selection of Thai and European dishes. Drinks can be enjoyed at the bar.
