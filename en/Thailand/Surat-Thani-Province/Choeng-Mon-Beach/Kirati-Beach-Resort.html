Description

Kirati Beach Resort is situated in the centre of Chengmon Beach, a 5-minute drive from Chaweng Beach and a 15-minute drive from Maenam beach.

It offers massage services, a tour desk, and a 24-hour reception. Throughout the stay, guests can enjoy free WiFi.

Surrounded by greenery, each air-conditioned room at Kirati Beach Resort comes with a private balcony.

Room amenities include a TV, a fridge, and a safety deposit box. Kirati Beach Resort offers laundry services and car rental.

Guests can enjoy quiet moments in the garden or on the terrace. The seaside restaurant serves fresh seafood dishes as well as Thai and international specialties.

Alternatively, a range of dining outlets can be found within a 5-minute drive from this property.

Kirati Beach Resort is a 10-minute drive from Samui Airport. Transfers can be arranged.
