Description

Overlooking the Gulf of Siam, Boat Lagoon Samui is easily accessible from the village of Cheong Mon. It is a 15 minute drive from both the International Airport and the nightlife of Chaweng.

Surrounded by tropical landscapes, modern Thai-style villas offer privacy and convenience with ample lounge areas and well-furnished kitchenettes. They feature a patio with lounge chairs.

Samui Boat Lagoon also provides free parking on site. For leisure, enjoy soothing massages or arrange sightseeing tours and water sports activities with help from the tour desk.

Samui Boat Lagoon is within walking distance of various beachfront bars and restaurants.
