Description

Idyllic Samui Resort at Samrong Bay is an ocean front resort, with direct access to the beach.

Located on a peaceful area of Koh Samui, vibrant Chaweng Beach is accessible within a short 15-minute drive away with shuttle bus and taxi service provided by the property.

Free WiFi is available. All villas have access to a private pool, and most villas have a children pool.

All villas come with a private parking and tropical garden. Guests staying in the apartment and studio types can make use of a shared swimming pool.

All room types come with an en suite kitchen, separate seating area and a dressing room.

Cable TV, a DVD player and BBQ facilities are also available. Guests staying in the villas of Idyllic Samui Resort have access to barbecue facilities.

Guests can request to have their massages in the villa or in front of the sea. Airport shuttle is provided.

Idyllic Samui is 10 minutes by car from Fisherman Village where there are many restaurants and small shops.
