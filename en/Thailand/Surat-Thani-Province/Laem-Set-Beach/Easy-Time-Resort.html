Description

Easy Time Resort is located along Laem Sor Beach in Ko Samui, a 2-minute walk to the beachfront.

The hotel offers free Wi-Fi, an outdoor pool and a restaurant. The air-conditioned rooms at Resort Easy Time come with separate living rooms and a minibar.

En suite bathrooms are equipped with hot shower facilities. Sun decks and massage services are provided by the pool.

Other activities include snorkelling, fishing and diving by the beach. Guests can enjoy Italian and Thai dishes at the hotel’s on-site restaurant.

Easy Time Resort is a 40-minute drive from Samui International Airport. The hotel provides an airport shuttle upon request.
