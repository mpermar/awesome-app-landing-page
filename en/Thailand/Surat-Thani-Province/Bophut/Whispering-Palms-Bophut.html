Description

Dark wood accents are used throughout the Whispering Palms’ modern villas. Each includes a large separate living and dining area, balcony and LCD TV.

Guests can relax on the resort’s outdoor pool deck or use the on site sauna and steam bath. Free Wi-Fi is available in the public areas of the Whispering Palm Bophut.

The Whispering Palms is a 15 minute walk from the stores and restaurants of Fisherman’s Village. Big Buddha Monastery and the Samui Airport are each a 25-minute drive away.
