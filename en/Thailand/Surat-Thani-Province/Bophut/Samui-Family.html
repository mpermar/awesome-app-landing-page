Description

Guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a electric tea pot. Samui Family provides certain rooms that feature mountain views, and the rooms include a private bathroom with a shower. The rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

Breakfast is available each morning, and includes continental and vegetarian options.

The nearest airport is Samui International Airport, 1.9 miles from the accommodation.

The big swimming pool is fantastic, clean, and big enough for a good round of 1000m swim, I think it's at least 25 m long. The staff is very nice, welcoming and helpful, they offer motorbike rental (which is not our style at all), but also a car with a driver with VERY decent prices for taxi and especially for touring the island. The Italian owner invited us for dinner and it was great! We ate breakfast only on the first day when we arrived, it was good and good priced as well, taking into account the level of prices in Ko Samui (much higher than Bangkok or Chiang Mai). Then we preferred to prepare it ourselves, buying food from the supermarket in Chaweng (very good variety of food), because in fact we have a protein/vegetable rich diet, which is very rarely offered in hotels anywhere. The accomodation is situated very near to The Fisherman's Village, where we found the restaurants and shops to be pretty expensive, but we were used to Bangkok and other places in Thailand and neighbouring countries... The beach at Fisherman's Village has not very fine sand, but it is much cleaner than Chaweng, and not that populated, at least during day time.
