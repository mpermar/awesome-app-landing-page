Description

Facing the sea, Lipa Lodge Beach Resort provides beach-front accommodation with free Wi-Fi, an outdoor pool and spa treatments.

Surrounded by beautiful gardens, it also houses the award-winning Restaurant Lipa Lodge.

All air-conditioned rooms feature a balcony with views of the sea or beach. All come with a flat-screen TV, DVD player and minibar.

En suite bathrooms come with a shower and free toiletries. Lipa Lodge Beach Resort is a 10-minute drive from Raja Pier.

It is a 45-minute drive from Samui International Airport and Big Buddha Beach. Recommended by many guide books, the on-site restaurant serves Thai and international dishes.

It enjoys spectacular sea views and is lit up beautifully at night. Day trips and area shuttles can be arranged at the tour desk.

The resort also provides laundry and ironing services. Luggage can be stored at the front desk.
