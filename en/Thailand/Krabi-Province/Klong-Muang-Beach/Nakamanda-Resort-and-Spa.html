Description

Surrounded by mangrove forests and plantations, villas are decorated in a tropical Thai style. Along with a balcony or terrace, all include a day bed, satellite TV and tea/coffee maker. Large bathrooms have a plunge bathtub and separate showers.

Nakamanda Resort is a 20-minute drive from Ao Nang Beach and a 40-minute drive from Krabi International Airport. By speedboat, it is 15 minutes from Hong Island and 1 hour, 45 minutes from Phi Phi Island.

The Naga Spa has beautiful views of the Andaman Sea, and provides rejuvenating beauty treatments. Guests can read a book in the library, arrange day trips at the tour desk, or enjoy scenic strolls by the beach.

The beachfront Kitchen Restaurant serves Thai and Western dishes. English high tea and refreshing cocktails are available at The Living Bar.
