Description

Located in Ao Nang Beach, just a 10-minute walk from Ao Nang Walking Street, Glow Ao Nang Krabi features accommodation where guests have easy access to local restaurants, food stalls and seaside shops.

The hotel has an outdoor swimming pool and 2 fitness centres. Guests can enjoy meals at the on-site restaurant.

Free WiFi is offered in all guestrooms and free private parking is available on site.

Each room at this hotel is air conditioning and comes with Glow's signature bedding, a 40-inch flat-screen TV, a mini fridge, tea/coffee maker and a safe.

Overlooking views of the pool, the mountain or the city, there is a balcony or a terrace featured in all rooms.

For your comfort, you will find slippers and a hairdryer. An en suite bathroom features rain shower and free toiletries.

Guests can make use of a 24-hour front desk, kid's club and an internet lounge during their stay at the property.

Nopparat Thara Beach is a 5-minute walk from Glow Ao Nang Krabi, while Krabi Town is 16 km from the property.
