Description

Located on Ao Nang Beach, Krabi Resort provides direct beach access and charming rooms with private terraces.

It has a large outdoor pool and a restaurant offering beautiful sea views. Conveniently situated near Krabi’s spectacular diving sites and limestone cliffs, The Krabi Resort is also just 4 km from Phra Nang Cave.

It takes 30 minutes by car from the hotel to Krabi International Airport. Fitted with fine furnishings, rooms present a traditional Thai decor or contemporary designs.

Each room has a cable TV, a minibar and ample seating space. Most rooms come with a luxurious bathtub.

Recreation facilities include rock climbing, scuba diving and snorkelling. The resort also provides tour services, airport transfers and wireless internet access for a fee.

The Boat, the resort’s all-day dining restaurant, serves Thai and international cuisine.

Fresh seafood barbeque dishes are also available. Guests can enjoy refreshing beverages at the Beach and Pool Bar.
