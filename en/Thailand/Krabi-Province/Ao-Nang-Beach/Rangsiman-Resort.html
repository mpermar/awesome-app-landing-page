Description

Rangsiman Resort is a 10-minute drive from Aonang Beach. It offers bungalows with air-conditioning and terrace.

The resort is equipped with a pool and tour desk. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the area.

The resort is a 15-minute drive to Nopparat Thara Beach and a 25-minute drive to Krabi Downtown.

It is a 40-minute drive from Krabi International Airport. Phuket International Airport is a 2-hr drive away.

Bungalows are fitted with a cable TV, refrigerator and safety deposit box. Shower facilities are included in a private bathroom.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can assist guests with laundry services. Free private parking is possible on site.
