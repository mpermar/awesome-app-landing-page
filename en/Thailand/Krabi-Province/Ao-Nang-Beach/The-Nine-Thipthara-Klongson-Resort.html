Description

All guest rooms in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Every room comes with a private bathroom with a shower and a hair dryer. All rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

Guests at The Nine Thipthara Klongson Resort can enjoy a continental breakfast. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the on-site restaurant, which specializes in international cuisine.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool.

The reception can provide advice on the area in order to help guests plan their day.

Aonang Mosque is 3.4 miles from The Nine Thipthara Klongson Resort. Krabi International Airport is 12 miles from the property.
