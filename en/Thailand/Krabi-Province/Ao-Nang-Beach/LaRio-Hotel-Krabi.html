Description

At the hotel, each room has a balcony with a mountain view. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom, while some accommodations at LaRio Hotel Krabi also provide guests with a seating area. At the accommodation, every room comes with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

A buffet breakfast is served at the property.

LaRio Hotel Krabi offers 3-star accommodations with a hammam. A variety of popular activities are available in the area around the hotel, including cycling and hiking.

Non-stop assistance is available at the reception, where staff speak English, French and Thai.

Krua Thara Seafood Restaurant is 2.4 miles from LaRio Hotel Krabi. The nearest airport is Krabi International Airport, 11 miles from the property.
