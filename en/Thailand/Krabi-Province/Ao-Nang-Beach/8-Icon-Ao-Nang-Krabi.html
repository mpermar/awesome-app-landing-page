Description

Close to Ao Nang and Nopparattara Beach by 10-15 mins walk, 8Icon Ao Nang Krabi features an outdoor pool with poolside bars and views of the mountain.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant. Free WiFi access and free private parking are available on site.

Decorated in Thai Southern style, rooms come with a 32" LED flat-screen TV with satellite channels and USB media play.

Certain units feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day. A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms.

All rooms include a private bathroom equipped with a rain shower. For your comfort, you will find bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries.

There are a 24-hour front desk, a shared lounge, a dry cleaning service and shops available at the property.

The hotel also offers car hire and travel package to the island. The property area is close to several famous islands: Koh Poda, Koh Hong or Maya Bay.
