Description

Spacious rooms feature modern amenities such as satellite TV, Wi-Fi access and a DVD player. The private bathroom has a separate bathtub and rain shower. A tea/coffee maker, refrigerator and hairdryer are provided as well.

Guests can lounge by the pool and enjoy the tropical landscapes or get refreshing traditional Thai massages. Other on-site activities include billiards and karaoke. Barbecue facilities are also available for use. Inquiries about day trips and excursions can be made at the front desk. The resort provides airport transfer.

Cha Wan Terrace offers Thai and international dishes with pool views. A range of drinks and cocktails are available. Guests also have the option of dining in their rooms.

Cha Wan Resort is a 30-minute drive from Krabi Airport.
