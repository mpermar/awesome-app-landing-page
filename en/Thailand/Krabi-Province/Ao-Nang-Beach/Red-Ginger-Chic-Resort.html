Description

The resort is 12 miles from the center of the city. It is a 40-minute drive from Krabi Airport. Railay Beach is 15 minutes away by longtail boat.

Red Ginger's air-conditioned rooms have a tea/coffee maker, TV with DVD player and minibar. The private bathrooms include a bathtub and shower.

Guests can work-out at the fitness center or make arrangements for trips and activities at the tour desk.

Red Ginger Chic Resort's restaurant serves Thai and international dishes. In-room dining is also available.
