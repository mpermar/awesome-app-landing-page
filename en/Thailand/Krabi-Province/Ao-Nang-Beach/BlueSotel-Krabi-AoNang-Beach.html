Description

Featuring free WiFi, a spa centre and an outdoor pool, BlueSotel Krabi is located in Aonang, about 2.

3 km from Nopparat Thara Beach. Rooms boast stunning views of the surrounding limestone hills.

Appointed with wooden furniture, cosy rooms at Krabi BlueSotel come with a flat-screen TV, a safety deposit box and a minibar.

Some units also have a seating area or a balcony. All rooms have a private bathroom with a bathtub and free toiletries.

During the stay, guests can take advantage of a 24-hour front desk, a business centre, room service, and free parking space.

Staff at the on-site tour desk can take care of travel arrangements along with shuttle services.

Ao Nang Krabi Boxing Stadium is 2. 1 km from BlueSotel Krabi, while Nopparat Thara Beach is 2.

3 km away. Krabi Airport can be reached within 19 km of the property. Featuring 2 spacious dining outlets, guests can enjoy delectable Thai and international dishes and night entertainment.

Additionally, refreshing drinks and light meals are served at its pool bar as well as at the lobby bar.
