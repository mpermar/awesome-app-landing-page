Description

At the resort, every room includes a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Aonang Cliff Mountain New Resort provides some rooms that feature a balcony, and every room comes with a electric tea pot. The rooms come with a seating area.

A buffet breakfast is available daily at the accommodation.

Ao Nang Krabi Boxing Stadium is 1.1 miles from Aonang Cliff Mountain New Resort, while Dragon Crest Mountain is 3.8 miles away. The nearest airport is Krabi International Airport, 11 miles from the property.

12/27/2017

Nice, secluded hotel with friendly and helpful staff. Would recommend for families.

"Friendly and helpful staff. Location a bit far from beach, but we knew that when booking. Slightly secluded, hill side location better suited to family than along Aonang beach front, in my humble opinion. Got a few free trips (including airport pick up) by hotel car to beach and town anyway. Tuk tuk ride from hotel to Aonang is about 15-20mins, costs 200 baht (maybe less if you’re prepared to haggle harder!) and fun for kids, so no real issue.Nice pool and attractively laid out setting, with chalet type rooms on hill side.Buffet breakfast very nice and mostly local style, though with some ‘foreign’ offerings for those who need/want it. Refreshing not to be faced with the usual British and American breakfast that is so often served in modern hotels wherever you travel in the world!Wifi signal good in open lobby area and restaurant. A fair bit weaker in room, but generally no problem. We didn’t choose the place for its wifi strength in any case!Nice view from room of spectacular mountains and greenery. Especially so in the early morning."
