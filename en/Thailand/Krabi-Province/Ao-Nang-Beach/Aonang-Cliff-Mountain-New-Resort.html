Description

At the resort, every room includes a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Aonang Cliff Mountain New Resort provides some rooms that feature a balcony, and every room comes with a electric tea pot. The rooms come with a seating area.

A buffet breakfast is available daily at the accommodation.

Ao Nang Krabi Boxing Stadium is 1.1 miles from Aonang Cliff Mountain New Resort, while Dragon Crest Mountain is 3.8 miles away. The nearest airport is Krabi International Airport, 11 miles from the property.
