Description

Stretching across the white sands of Railay Beach, Railay Resort & Spa offers spacious cottages set in tropical landscapes.

It features free WiFi throughout, a beachfront outdoor pool and a restaurant. Offering free parking on the mainland at Ao Nam Mao Pier, Railay Bay Resort & Spa is a 20-minute boat trip from Ao Nam Mao Pier or Ao Nang Beach to Railay Pier.

Featuring private balconies, tropical-style cottages at Resort Railay feature casual cane and wood furnishings.

In-room comforts include DVD player, a safe and a spacious bathroom with a bathtub.

For leisure, guests can relax with soothing massage at Railay Spa or try activities like paddle canoeing and rock climbing.

A tour desk offers help with trip planning. Cool sea breezes accompany seafood and pizza specialities at Railay’s restaurant.
