Description

Phutawan Railay Resort is a 15-minute speedboat ride from popular Ao Nang Beach. Diving and snorkeling trips to Ko Lanta and the Phi Phi Islands are just a short speedboat ride away.

Rooms at Railay Phutawan are tastefully decorated with rustic wood furnishings and Thai accents. Each has a minibar and fridge. Bathrooms feature hot and cold water showers.

Guests may arrange an exciting day trip at the tour desk. The resort also provides a terrace and scenic landscaped grounds, ideal locations for an evening walk.
