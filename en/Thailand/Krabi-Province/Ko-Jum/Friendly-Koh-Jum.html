Description

At the resort, rooms come with a balcony. Free WiFi is accessible to all guests, while certain rooms come with a patio. All rooms at Friendly Koh Jum are equipped with a seating area.

The accommodation offers a continental or American breakfast.

Guests at Friendly Koh Jum will be able to enjoy activities in and around Ko Jum, like cycling.

Phi Phi Don is 14 miles from the resort, while Ao Nang Beach is 19 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Krabi International Airport, 21 miles from Friendly Koh Jum.
