Description

Tucked away on Koh Lanta's west coast and inspired by its natural surroundings, on the scenic Klongnin Beach, SriLanta is a boutique resort offering charming villas set amidst a tranquil tropical landscape, just across the road from the beach.

It showcases different styles of accommodations including rustic Thai-style Veranda villas, Plantation Villas, deluxe suites, Mandara rooms as well as the Beach House.

Guests can relax at a large beachside pool and a spa. SriLanta's rustic thatched villas sit on the hillside, offering garden or mountain views.

They are designed to reflect traditional Thai living with its local furnishings. They are all equipped with air conditioning and a safe.

Indulge with a relaxing massage at the spa or recharge with Yoga and meditation sessions.

The hotel has plenty of lounge chairs, perfect for enjoying a drink or reading in between swims.

If you're looking for adventure, arrange with the helpful staff for activities that range from snorkelling and jungle trekking to island-hopping and diving.

Take pleasure in outdoor dining at Surya Chandra Restaurant, which serves traditional Thai cuisine or head over to Chedi Beach Front Restaurant where seafood BBQ and a range of international dishes can be sampled.

To unwind and enjoy the beautiful sunset, head to the Ten Face Beach Bar, which offers drinks and light refreshment by the beachfront.
