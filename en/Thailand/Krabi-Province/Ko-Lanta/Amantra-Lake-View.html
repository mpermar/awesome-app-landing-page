Description

All guest rooms at the resort come with a seating area and a flat-screen TV. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom, all rooms at Amantra Lake View are equipped with air conditioning, and certain rooms also offer a terrace.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental breakfast.

You can play ping pong and minigolf at Amantra Lake View, and free use of bicycles is available.

Had Hin Ngam is 1.3 miles from the resort, while Ta Noad Beach is 1.8 miles away.
