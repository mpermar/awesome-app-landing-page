Description

At the resort, each room comes with a balcony with a garden view. Free WiFi is available to all guests, while selected rooms include a patio.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at the property. There's an on-site restaurant, which serves international, European and Thai cuisine and also offers halal and vegetarian options.

Saladan Pier is 5 miles from Lanta A&J Klong khong Beach, while Saladan School is 6 miles away.
