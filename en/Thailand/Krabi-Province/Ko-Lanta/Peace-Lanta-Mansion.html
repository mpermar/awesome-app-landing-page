Description

Offering accommodation with air conditioning and a private balcony, Peace Lanta Mansion is set in Ko Lanta's Klong Khong area.

The beach is a 5-minute walk away. Free WiFi is featured throughout and free private parking is available on site.

All units have a satellite TV, a refrigerator, and a private bathroom with a hot shower and free toiletries.

Pra Ae Beach is 1. 7 km from Peace Lanta Mansion, while Long Beach is nearby. There is a 24-hour convenience store a short stroll away.
