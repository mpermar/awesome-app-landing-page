Description

Lanta Pearl Beach Resort is located on Long Beach and offers free WiFi. Set amongst tropical greenery, it features bungalows with private balconies.

Each bungalow at Lanta Pearl Beach Resort comes with traditional Thai décor and hardwood furnishings.

Their balconies overlook the garden and are equipped with a hammock. Private bathrooms are fitted with shower facilities.

Guests can arrange for fishing and snorkelling activities, or enjoy a barbecue by the beach.

The hotel provides car and motorbike rental services. Travel and sightseeing arrangements can be made at the tour desk.

The Pearl Restaurant serves a variety of traditional Thai and fresh seafood dishes.

Other dining options include light snacks and beverages at The Pearl Bar. Lanta Pearl Beach Resort is approximately 55 km from Krabi Airport and 65 km from Trang Airport.
