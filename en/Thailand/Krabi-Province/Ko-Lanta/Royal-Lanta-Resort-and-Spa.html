Description

Fitted with classic wood furnishings, modern Thai-style rooms at Royal Lanta Resort come with a flat-screen TV and a private balcony with outdoor seating. In-room comforts include a fridge and a spacious bathroom.

Spend lazy afternoons on the white sands of Klong Dao Bay or head into the ocean for a dive or fishing. After a day of activity, relaxing massages can be enjoyed at the spa.

Freshly brewed coffee and fine pastries are served by the beach at Sea Cow Bakery. Resort Royal Lanta can also provide an intimate dining experience under the stars at the beachfront terrace.
