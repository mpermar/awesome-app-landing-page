Description

Located along Long Beach, Lanta Sand Resort & Spa offers beachfront accommodation with direct access to the beach.

The hotel features free Wi-Fi and 3 outdoor swimming pools. Every room comes with air condition with cable TV, minibar, tea/coffee making facilities.

Private balconies and a work desk are provided. Lanta Sand Resort and Spa offers a tour desk while massage and beauty services are available at the spa or on the beach.

Guest can dine at Rabeang Lanta Restaurant for romantic sunset or night time. For night life, the resort's Beach Bar is at your service from 11.
