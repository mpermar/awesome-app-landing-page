Description

All rooms comes with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fridge, a electric tea pot, a bidet and a desk. The rooms include a private bathroom. At the hotel, the rooms are equipped with a seating area.

Family Tree Hotel offers a continental or à la carte breakfast.

Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Chaloem Rattanankosin Stadium, Thara Park and Weekend Night Market Krabi town. The nearest airport is Krabi International Airport, 5 miles from Family Tree Hotel.
