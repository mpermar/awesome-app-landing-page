Description

Offering a private beachfront, Mama Beach Residence is located on Phi Phi Island.

Rooms feature flat-screen TVs and balconies with views of the ocean or beach. The air-conditioned rooms at Mama Beach come with modern décor and have plenty of natural light.

Each room has an en suite open-air bathroom with hot shower facilities. Guests can go on snorkelling and diving trips.

Sightseeing arrangements can be made at the tour desk. Mama Restaurant serves Thai and French dishes, and offers sweeping views of the ocean.

Vegan options are also available for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Mama Beach Residence is a 10-minute walk from Phi Phi Island’s main pier and a 15-minute walk from the village.

Upon arrival at the pier, staff are waiting to assist guest with carrying luggage to the property.
