Description

Beautifully situated on the banks of Mun River in Ubon Ratchathani, Mini-golf ubon & Resort offers comfortable bungalows with free WiFi.

Overlooking Had Ku Dua, the property is equipped with a convenient store and free private parking.

Mini-golf ubon & Resort is a 15-minute drive from the city centre and Central Plaza Ubon.

A 30-minute drive brings guests to Ubon Ratchatani Airport. Adorned with modern décor, each air-conditioned unit is fitted with tiled flooring and a roof ventilator.

They feature a flat-screen satellite TV with over 100 channels and a minibar with complimentary drinking water.

An en suite bathroom comes with hot shower facilities and free toiletries. Activities that can be enjoyed on-site include a 18-hole mini golf course and an outdoor pool.

For families with young children, the resort also has plenty of child facilities including a trampoline, swing set, a baby pool and a sand pit.

The spacious garden area also comes with seating areas and barbecue facilities for guests to enjoy on-site.

The staff speaks fluent French, German, Italian, Spanish, Thai and English. Guests can enjoy a great variety of French, Italian and vegetarian menus while admiring sweeping views of the river at the panoramic restaurant.

For cocktails and refreshing drinks, head over to the 80's theme bar by the pool.

It features darts, card games, books and a pool table. Other local dining options can be reached within 2 km.
