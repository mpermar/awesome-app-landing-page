Description

All guest rooms at the resort are equipped with a seating area and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Paan Yong Villa has certain accommodations that have garden views, and every room comes with a private bathroom with a shower. The rooms will provide guests with a closet and a electric tea pot.

A continental breakfast is served each morning at the property.

The accommodation has a sun terrace. The area is popular for cycling, and car rental is available at this 3-star resort.

Chiang Mai is 12 miles from Paan Yong Villa. The nearest airport is Chiang Mai Airport, 12 miles from the resort.
