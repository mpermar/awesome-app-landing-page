Description

Guest rooms comes with air conditioning, a TV with cable channels, a electric tea pot, a shower, free toiletries and a closet. Each room has a private bathroom with a hair dryer, while selected rooms here will provide you with a balcony.

A buffet breakfast is served at the property. Guests can enjoy a meal at the in-house restaurant, which serves Asian cuisine.

The resort has a playground. Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near Baan Amphawa Resort & Spa.

The accommodation can conveniently provide information at the reception to help guests to get around the area.

Amphawa Floating Market is 1.2 miles from Baan Amphawa Resort & Spa. The nearest airport is Don Muang Airport, 55 miles from the property.
