Description

All rooms in the resort are equipped with a electric tea pot. The rooms include a private bathroom, and certain rooms include a terrace. The rooms at Thai Break Resort feature air conditioning and a desk.

Continental and buffet breakfast options are available daily at the accommodation.

Guests can use the hot tub. A variety of popular activities are available in the area around Thai Break Resort, including cycling and hiking.

Ratsadanupradit Mahitsaraphakdi Park is 9 miles from the resort. Trang Airport is 7 miles away.
