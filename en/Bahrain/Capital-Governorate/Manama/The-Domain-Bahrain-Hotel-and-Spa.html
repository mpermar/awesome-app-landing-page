Description

All air-conditioned suites are serviced by floor butlers. Each one has a private entrance, a living room with a flat-screen TV and a minibar. The bathroom has a shower or bathtub and complimentary toiletries.

Guests can enjoy a daily breakfast at the hotel’s restaurant. Room service is available 24 hours upon request.

The Marina is 3 miles away and Bahrain National Museum is 10 minutes away by car. Bahrain International Airport is a 15-minute drive from The Domain Bahrain.
