Description

This 5-star hotel is directly attached to City Centre, the largest mall in Bahrain.

A host of complimentary facilities such as swimming pool, infinity pool, fitness and spa facilities are available free of charge.

Spa treatments can be booked at an additional charge. Free WiFi is available and each room features a flat-screen TV, air conditioning and a bathroom with complimentary toiletries.

For dining, guests can choose from dining options at both the hotel and its sister hotel, Le Meridien Bahrain City Centre.

Bahrain International Airport is a 15-minute drive away and Bahrain International Exhibition Centre 1.
