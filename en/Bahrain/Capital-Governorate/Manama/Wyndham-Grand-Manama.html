Description

Featuring breathtaking views of the Manama skyline and Arabian Gulf, the 5-star Wyndham Grand Manama offers guests to relax in three pools and a sauna, and indulge in gourmet onsite dining.

Guests can enjoy Free WiFi throughout the property. The units features a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a bath, free toiletries and a desk.

With a private bathroom equipped with a bidet and a hairdryer, rooms at the hotel also provide guests with free WiFi.

Guest rooms at Wyndham Grand Manama include air conditioning and a wardrobe. Breakfast is available every morning, and includes halal and buffet options.

With staff speaking Afrikaans, Arabic, German and English, around the clock guidance is available at the reception.

Bahrain City Centre Mall is 2. 9 km from Wyndham Grand Manama, while Bahrain International Exhibition & Convention Centre is 3.

9 km from the property. The nearest airport is Bahrain International Airport, 6 km from the hotel.
