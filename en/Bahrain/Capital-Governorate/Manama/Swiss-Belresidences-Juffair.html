Description

Swiss-Belresidences Juffair is located in Manama and offers accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool and free WiFi, 5 km from Bahrain National Museum and 9 km from Bahrain City Centre Mall.

The accommodation is air conditioned and has a hot tub. All units come with a living room, a seating area with a sofa, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fully equipped kitchen and a private bathroom.

An oven and kettle are also provided. A buffet breakfast is available every morning at the Swiss-Cafe Restaurant.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the in-house restaurant, which specialises in International cuisine.

At Swiss-Belresidences Juffair guests are welcome to take advantage of a hot tub.

The accommodation also offers a car rental service and secure parking is available.

This property features a gym, business centre and terrace, as well as a 24-hour reception and concierge service.

There is a games room equipped with pool and ping pong tables as well as a playstation.

Wahooo! Waterpark is 9 km from Swiss-Belresidences Juffair, while Bahrain International Exhibition & Convention Centre is 9 km from the property.
