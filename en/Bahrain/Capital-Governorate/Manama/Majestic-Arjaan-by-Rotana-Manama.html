Description

Majestic Arjaan by Rotana consists of five modern state of the art buildings and is located in Busaiteen district, in Manama, the region of Muhafazat al-Asimah, opposite King Hamad University Hospital, just 40.

2 km from Al Khobar and 48. 3km from Dhahran Depending on the room type, there are flat screen TVs, a sitting & dining room, a kitchenette fitted with a dishwasher and stove & oven, a microwave, and a kettle.

Each unit contains a private bathroom with bathrobes and slippers. Guests are able to enjoy on-site facilities such as an outdoor year-round swimming pool, sauna and fitness center.

The in-house restaurant serves a buffet or set menu of international cuisine. The nearest airport is Bahrain International Airport, situated 1.

9 km from the property. The renowned Avenues shopping center is 4 km away allowing for convenient access to coffee shops, cinemas and stores.

You can drive to Avenue in 5 minutes, or shop at Bahrain City Centre, which is a 10-minute drive from Majestic Arjaan.
