Description

Perched on the pristine beaches of Zallaq overlooking the Arabian Gulf on Bahrain’s west coast, this 5-star luxury hotel in Bahrain boasts the only Thalassa Sea and Spa in the GCC countries.

This is spectacular seawater spa hotel and resort blends French art de vivre with traditional Bahraini elegance.

The hotel has 262 elegantly appointed rooms and suites, each with private balcony, latest technology, and chic furnishings.

This luxury beachfront resort offers unparalleled view of the Arabian sea from all of its room categories.

Bahrain’s Pearl, the thalassotherapy spa complex offers a vast array of seawater-based treatments, including hydrotherapy, massage and wellness.

Restore your inner energy with individual thalassotherapy treatments, wraps, massage, and relaxing or stimulating sessions in the indoor heated sea water pool.

Fine dine on international cuisine in Sofitel Bahrain with five restaurants serving flavors from across the globe.

Choose from the award-winning seafood, Italian, Southeast Asian or Lebanese restaurants and lap up the luxury of seasonal menus, al fresco and all-day dining.

Whether you find yourself at Sofitel Bahrain Zallaq on business or pleasure you can linger in the three bars and lobby lounge, each perfect for a nightcap, business meeting or rendezvous with friends.

Set admits a manicured garden with swaying palm trees, the inviting outdoor pool draws you to its shimmering blue waters, and you can swim to the pool bar for a refreshing cocktail or spend the afternoon in laying under the shade.

There's also a beach club with fitness facility, steam rooms, tennis and squash courts as well as Golf sessions for the avid Golfers with the first Eco-Friendly Golf ball in Bahrain that dissolves into water becoming fish food! Enjoy 5-star service amenities such as concierge porter, free WiFi, babysitting and a Kids Club that will keep your little ones engaged with its exciting kids’ activities.

Bahrain International Airport and Manama city are just a 30-minute drive from Sofitel Bahrain and the hotel is just 5 km away from the Bahrain International Circuit.
