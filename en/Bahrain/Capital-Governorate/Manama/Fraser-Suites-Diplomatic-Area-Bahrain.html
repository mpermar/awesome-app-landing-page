Description

Located in Manama, Fraser Suites Diplomatic Area features modern and elegant accommodations.

It offers free WiFi in all areas, an indoor pool, a spa and a fitness centre. The property is 1 km from Bahrain World Trade Center (BWTC) and 1 km from The Avenues Mall, a waterfront shopping center along Bahrain Bay.

Each air-conditioned unit here will provide you with a living area with a flat-screen TV, an iPod dock and a dining area.

There is a full kitchen with a microwave and an oven. Featuring a bath, private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and bathrobes.

Other facilities offered at the property include luggage storage and a kids' club.

The hotel is 3. 9 km from Wahooo! Waterpark and 7. 1 km from Bahrain Fort. Bahrain International Airport is 5 km away.
