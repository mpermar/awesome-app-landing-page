Description

The rooms at the Ramada Hotel Bahrain are all modern, with air conditioning and satellite TVs. The beds feature comfortable comforters. All rooms include free tea/coffee making facilities and bottled water.

A deluxe breakfast is served daily.

The city's shopping malls, the diplomatic areas, business areas, and the Bahrain International Airport are all just a short drive away.
