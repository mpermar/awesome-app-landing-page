Description

Just 500 metres from Wahooo! Waterpark and 600 metres from Bahrain City Centre Mall, The Grand Apartment is an apartment in Manama with access to a an outdoor swimming pool.

The property was built in 2017, and features air-conditioned accommodation with free WiFi.

This apartment comes with 1 bedroom, a kitchen, a flat-screen TV, a seating area and 2 bathrooms.

Guests can enjoy the complimentary mini bar. Guests can make use of the hot tub. Guests can also relax on the sun terrace.

Bahrain International Exhibition & Convention Centre is a 19-minute walk from the apartment, while Seef Mall is 1.

9 km away. The nearest airport is Bahrain International Airport, 8 km from The Grand Apartment.
