Description

With 200 metres stretch of sandy beach descending into the Arabian Gulf, this 5-star resort is located in Ajman.

Fairmont Ajman boasts an expansive infinity pool, and a spa with a range of signature treatments.

Guests can enjoy free WiFi in the hotel as well as in the rooms. All guestrooms and suites are highlighted by far-reaching views of the sea.

Each is luxuriously furnished with plush bedding and state-of-the-art in-room technology.

The sumptuous bathroom comes with separate rain shower, bathtub and Le Labo bath amenities from New York.

Fairmont Ajman offers eight restaurants and lounges. The Turkish restaurant serves Turkish favorites in a traditional Ottoman setting.

Spectrum features open show kitchens and international cuisine. Guests can enjoy shisha at the outdoor lounge overlooking the sea.

Dubai International Airport is 31 km away. Corniche Avenue is a 5-minute walk from Fairmont Ajman.

The neighboring Dubai emirate, famous for its world-class attractions, is a 30-minute drive away.
