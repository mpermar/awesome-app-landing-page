Description

All rooms at the resort come with a seating area, a TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a bath. The rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

Sahara Beach Resort & Spa offers a continental or buffet breakfast. There's an on-site restaurant, which specializes in seafood cuisine.

Spa and wellness facilities including a hot tub and a fitness center are at guests' disposal during their stay at the accommodation.

Speaking English, Hindi, Russian and Filipino at the 24-hour front desk, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical guidance on the area.

Khalid Sea Port is 0.6 miles from Sahara Beach Resort & Spa, while Al Jazeera Park is 0.8 miles away. Dubai Airport is 7 miles from the property.
