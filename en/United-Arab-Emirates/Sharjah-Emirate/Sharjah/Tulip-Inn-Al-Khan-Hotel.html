Description

At the hotel, each room includes a desk. The private bathroom is equipped with a hot tub. All guest rooms at Tulip Inn Al Khan Hotel feature air conditioning and a closet.

A continental breakfast is served each morning at the property. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the on-site restaurant, which serves international cuisine.

Spa and wellness facilities including a hot tub and a sauna are at guests' disposal during their stay at the accommodation.

Speaking Russian, Filipino and Arabic at the 24-hour front desk, staff are always at hand to help.

Sahara Center is 1.5 miles from Tulip Inn Al Khan Hotel, while Sharjah Aquarium is 1.7 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Dubai Airport, 5 miles from the hotel.

Al Majaz is a great choice for travelers interested in beach walks, cleanliness and family-friendly trips.
