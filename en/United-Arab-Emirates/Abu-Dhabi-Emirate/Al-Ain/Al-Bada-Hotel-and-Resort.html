Description

All the chalets at Al Bada feature elegant décor. Each air-conditioned chalet provides a spacious sitting area with a dining table and a flat-screen TV. The well-equipped kitchen has an oven, a stove and a fridge.

The open-air restaurant hosts live music entertainment over the weekend. Shisha tobacco water pipes are also available.

Guests can enjoy an array of activities on site such as: horseback riding, paintball and motorcycle riding. Younger adults can visit the game zone that includes electronic games and pool.

Foah Shopping Mall is a 3 miles drive from Al Bada Hotel and Resort, while Hili Park is 5 miles away. Al Ain International Airport is 10 minutes by car. An airport shuttle service is available upon request.
