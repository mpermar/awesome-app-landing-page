Description

All guest rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area and a flat-screen TV. Rooms are equipped with a private bathroom, a shower and free toiletries, and some rooms include a safety deposit box. Guest rooms at Western Hotel - Madinat Zayed include air conditioning and a desk.

The daily breakfast offers continental, buffet or Italian options. There's an on-site restaurant, which specializes in Indian cuisine and also offers Vegan and Halal options.

The accommodation has a playground.
