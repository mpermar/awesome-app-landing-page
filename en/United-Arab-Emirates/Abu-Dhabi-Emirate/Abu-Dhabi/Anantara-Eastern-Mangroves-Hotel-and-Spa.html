Description

The spacious rooms have carpeted floors and feature balconies. They include sofas with flat-screen TVs, tea/coffee-making facilities, and safes.

Guests can enjoy Thai cuisine at Pachaylen restaurant, or choose Ingredients restaurant for all-day international cuisine. Guests can experience some nightlife in the rooftop lounge, which includes sofas and a view of the Abu Dhabi skyline.

The Abu Dhabi National Exhibitions Company is less than 15 minutes away by car. Abu Dhabi International Airport is 16 miles from the Eastern Mangroves Hotel & Spa Abu Dhabi. An airport shuttle can be provided by the hotel.
