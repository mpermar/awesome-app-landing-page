Description

Set amidst the landscaped gardens with its private beach overlooking the Grand Canal, Ritz-Carlton Abu Dhabi is inspired by renaissance architecture.

It offers an outdoor pool, a gym and 8 restaurants. All rooms open onto lush gardens with direct views of the pool, gardens or Grand Canal.

Each room offers a satellite TV, a Nespresso machine, a minibar and ironing facilities.

The villa includes a terrace and a spacious living room complete with dining area.

Guests can enjoy an eclectic dining experience at Ritz-Carlton with the availability of 8 restaurants offering from Middle Eastern, Mediterranean to Pan-Asian cuisines.

Room service is available 24 hours a day. Espa Spa features a steam room, a Hammam, a hair and beauty salon and 15 treatment rooms offering relaxing massages.

The health club is fitted with a range of cardiovascular and weight equipment. Yas Island is a 20-minute drive away from the Ritz-Carlton Abu Dhabi.

The holy Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque is a 5-minute walk away. Free private parking is possible on site.
