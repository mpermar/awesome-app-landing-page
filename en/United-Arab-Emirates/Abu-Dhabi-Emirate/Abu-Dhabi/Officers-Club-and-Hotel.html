Description

This luxurious 5-star hotel in Abu Dhabi has a gym, an expansive spa and swimming pools.

It also features a range of stylish à la carte restaurants and bars. Each elegant room at the Officers Club & Hotel has a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a tea/coffee maker and a bathroom with luxurious amenities.

Some rooms include a kitchenette and a seating area, while others have a balcony.

Upon arrival, guests can enjoy a fruit basket and Arabic coffee, fresh juice and dates.

For lunch and dinner, the Al Bathna restaurant serves fresh seafood, salads, premium steaks, and freshly-pressed pasta.

The Officers Club features an Olympic-size swimming pool (Ladies timings during Monday, Wednesday and Saturday).

There are also squash courts, basketball courts and a bowling alley. Complimentary shuttle service to selected tourist destinations is available.
