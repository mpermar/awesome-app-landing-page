Description

With a panoramic view of the Corniche coastline, this resort hotel is connected to Al Raha Mall and is close to the business district.

It features free Wi-Fi in all areas, swimming pools and a luxurious spa. Al Raha Beach Hotel is located 10 minutes drive away from the Yas Marina Circuit where the F1 race will take place.

Each room has a large marble bathroom and is luxuriously decorated. All rooms at the Al Raha Beach Hotel have LCD TVs and some have Gulf views.

The Al Raha has both indoor and outdoor swimming pools, a separate children’s water playground and a range of water sports.

The spa offers a choice of body treatments, as well as sauna, steam bath and hot tub.

There are several dining and entertainment options, including a beachside restaurant and cafe.

McGettigan's, an Irish themed bar and restaurant, provides a unique evening entertainment experience with signature menu options and beverages.

The hotel is a 10-minute drive from Abu Dhabi National Exhibition Centre and Abu Dhabi International Airport.
