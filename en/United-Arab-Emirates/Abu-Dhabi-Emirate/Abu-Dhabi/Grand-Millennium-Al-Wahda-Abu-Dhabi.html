Description

Grand Millennium Al Wahda the 5-star hotel in Abu Dhabi is located in the heart of the city also offering direct access to Al Wahda Mall.

This 5-star hotel offers free WiFi in the lobby bar and rooms with panoramic views over the city.

Swim above Abu Dhabi’s skyline in the rooftop swimming pool. Featuring stylish décor, the spacious rooms include a LCD SMART TV, a private bathroom with bathtub and separate walk in shower.

Indulge with a relaxing massage and beauty treatment at Zayna Spa. Get fit and feel good at Al Wahda health club offering fully equipped cardiovascular and weight equipment with a separate gym for ladies.

Grand Millennium Al Wahda features eight restaurants and bars with a wide range of international cuisines.

In close proximity, the beach front is 7 km, Abu Dhabi City Golf Club is 10 km, Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque is 14 km.

Ferrari World Theme Park, Yas Waterworld and Abu Dhabi International Airport are 30 minutes drive.
