Description

Novel Hotel City Center is located the centre of Abu Dhabi, close to the Corniche waterfront.

It features an outdoor pool and a stylish bar and lounge area. Guest rooms and suites at Novel Hotel City Center have contemporary furnishings and are fitted with cable flat-screen TV.

Guests can enjoy free WiFi in the rooms. Novel Hotel City Center offers a choice of 4 restaurants including Chinese and French specialties.

There are also 3 bars, including the international pub and club. There is an outdoor swimming pool and well-equipped fitness centre.

The nearest airport is Abu Dhabi International Airport, 30 km from Novel Hotel City Center.
