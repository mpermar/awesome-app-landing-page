Description

This 5-star hotel in Abu Dhabi city centre is located on a private beach within walking distance of business and shopping districts.

It has outdoor pools, a gym and 12 food outlets. The Sheraton Abu Dhabi Hotel & Resort offers luxurious guest rooms.

Each room has a marble bathroom and a private balcony with views over the city or the Gulf.

The Abu Dhabi Hotel serves a range of cuisine prepared with fresh ingredients, including Italian, Mexican, Spanish and local seafood.

Meals are served both indoors and al fresco, overlooking the ocean. Guests can relax at the outdoor pools, which are surrounded by palm trees.

There is also a sauna, a steam room and hot tub for relaxation. The well-equipped gym features modern cardiovascular equipment and weights.

The Sheraton Abu Dhabi Hotel & Resort is within walking distance of the newly developed Corniche Park, and provides easy access to Sheikh Zayed Tunnel.
