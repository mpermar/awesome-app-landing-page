Description

Ramada Downtown Abu Dhabi hotel is in the heart of Abu Dhabi, conveniently located within a 5-minutes walking distance from Abu Dhabi Shopping Mall, the city’s business district and 25 minutes’ drive from Abu Dhabi International Airport.

Yas Water world and Ferrari World are 25 minutes away by car. The guest rooms are spacious in size, classy and modernly furnished.

All rooms are air-conditioned and fitted with satellite TV and a private bathroom.

Free WiFi is available throughout the hotel. Start your day with our tasty morning breakfast in Sura restaurant, where you can find also a selection of fine dishes for lunch and dinner.

We offer 24 hours room service. The Blendz Coffee Shop located in the hotel lobby is the perfect spot to meet and relax at any time of the day.

In the evenings visit one of the cities hot spot, the Vertical Bar on our roof top.

Enjoy taking a dip in our swimming pool, unwind with refreshing health and beauty treatments in the Ramada Spa on-site, and along with The Figure 8 fitness center the ideal place for your workout needs.

There is as well a ladies beauty salon. The hotel’s 24-hour front desk is happy to provide sightseeing advice and help guests with travel, tour and airport pick up & drop off arrangements.
