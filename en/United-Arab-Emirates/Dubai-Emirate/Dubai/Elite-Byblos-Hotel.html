Description

Located in the heart of Al Barsha, this hotel is just a 5-minute walk to Mall of the Emirates.

It features an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre. WiFi is free in all rooms and public areas.

The spacious, air-conditioned rooms and suites at the Elite Byblos Hotel have a seating area with a flat-screen TV.

They include deluxe linens and pillows, and the bathrooms have bathrobe and a hairdryer.

Al Nafoora Restaurant offers a range of International and Arabic dishes with both la carte and buffet options, while Bazerkan Cafe offers light meals and pastries.

A 24-hour room service is also available. Shisha lounge offers a variety of shisha flavors.

Tour information is available from the 24-hour front desk, which also offers luggage storage.

Free valet parking is offered on site. Elite Byblos Hotel is a 10-minute drive from Wild Wadi Water Park and 15 km from the famous Dubai Mall.
