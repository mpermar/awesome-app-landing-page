Description

Situated in Dubai, 8 km from Dubai Autodrome, Five Jumeirah Village features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre.

With a bar, the 5-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom.

The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, room service and organising tours for guests.

At the hotel, rooms include a wardrobe and a flat-screen TV. Five Jumeirah Village offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

The wellness area at the accommodation is comprised of a hot tub and a sauna. Mall of the Emirates is 10 km from Five Jumeirah Village, while Burj Al Arab Tower is 14 km from the property.
