Description

Rising from Dubai’s Creek and Yacht Club in Port Saeed, Emirates Concorde welcomes guests in its striking hi-tech foyer.

It features a pool, nearby golfing facilities and a hammam with massage treatments.

Guests can enjoy free WiFi in the rooms for a maximum of 3 devices. Spacious and air conditioned, rooms at Emirates Concorde Hotel are equipped with extra long beds.

Each room includes a lounge with seating area, a work desk and satellite channels reception.

The hotel also offers spacious suites with a fully equipped kitchen and balcony. A fusion of continental food and traditional Middle Eastern specialities are served daily at the in-house restaurant.

The hotel also boasts a shopping arcade, a dental clinic, a barber shop and a beautician.

Located in the central District of Dubai, hotel Emirates Concorde is in close proximity to Deira City Centre Mall and Al Ghurair Mall.

Alrigga Metro station is 900m away from the property, while Union Metro is 1km away from the property.
