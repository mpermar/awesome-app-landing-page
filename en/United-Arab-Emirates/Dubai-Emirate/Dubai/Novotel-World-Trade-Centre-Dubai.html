Description

Novotel World Trade Centre is a 4-star luxury hotel less than 5 minutes' walk from World Trade Centre Metro Station (red line).

Situated in Sheihk Zayed Road, with 5 minutes to Burj Khalifa (world's tallest building), Dubai Frame, Dubai Mall (with Ski Dubai and shopping), Dubai Outlet Mall, Dubai Financial Centre and a few minutes to Jumeriah Beach and the Gold Souq.

Free shuttle service is provided to many attractions. The hotel is connected to the Dubai World Trade Centre.

Guests can enjoy an outdoor temperature controlled swimming pool, sun lounges, free WiFi throughout the property, spa and fully equipped gym.

Guests can also relax in the steam room and sauna. The spacious, modern rooms include a flat-screen satellite TV, tea/coffee facilities replenished daily and private bathroom with luxury toiletries.

The hotel provides daily housekeeping. Children below 16 years of age stay free at the hotel when sharing parent's room.

The hotel also has a dedicated play area for the kids with play station, books and toys.

International cuisine is served in Entre Nous Restaurant. It also has daily buffets ranging from Chinese, Italian, Asian and Japanese.

An extensive menu is available on 24/7 in-room service. Snacks and healthy options are on offer at Café Cream and Chill’s Pool bar.

Live bands perform at the award winning Blue Bar every evening. There is a business center, meeting rooms, 24-hour reception and a concierge service which can arrange car hire and tours.
