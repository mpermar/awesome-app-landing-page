Description

Boasting a 1200-metre sandy beach, Le Meridien Mina Seyahi is located in Jumeirah.

This 5-star luxury resort has several swimming pools situated amidst lush gardens and 5 dining and entertainment venues.

Featuring a classic chic décor, Le Meridien rooms are air-conditioned. Each room offers free WiFi, a Flat-screen TV and a safety deposit box.

They all offer panoramic views of the Arabian Gulf or Dubai's skyline. Leisure facilities at the Mina Seyahi include a water sports centre and Club Mina fitness centre.

Younger guests can enjoy Le Meridien Family Kids Club which offers activities programs, beach games as well as 2 separate kids’ pools.

Guests can enjoy a wide range of international classic and hearty dishes at the Latest Recipe.

Horizon offers casual snacks and cocktails by the pool. For more upbeat evening, Barasti Bar has live entertainment.

Le Meridien Mina Seyahi Beach Resort is a 10-minute drive from Emirates Golf Course.

Mall of The Emirates is 8 km from the resort and Dubai International Airport is 31 km away.
