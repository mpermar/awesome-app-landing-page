Description

Steigenberger Hotel Business Bay is a cosmopolitan 5-star hotel located in Business Bay district in close proximity to the Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall and the Dubai Downtown area, with convenient access to and from Sheikh Zayed Road and Al Khail Road.

Guests can choose from a variety of unique rooms and suites, spread across 19 floors, offering views of the Dubai Canal, the city skyline or the iconic Burj Khalifa.

All rooms offer a spacious working desk with wired or WiFi internet. The marble bathrooms come with both a tub and a shower.

With its elegant design, relaxed seating and lounge area to dine in, meet, or simply relax, the Club Lounge is available complimentary for all our suite guests, or for an additional surcharge.

It offers panoramic views of the Dubai Water Canal, refreshments throughout the day and exclusive benefits.

A wide variety of dining options are available in the 6 in-house venues from Brothaus Bakery – Bistro a ‘must-try’ destination for breakfast lovers; The Backyard, the city’s most unique street-art inspired garden terrace; Swim & Tonic pool bar and lounge with breath-taking Burj Khalifa views; Bayside Restaurant & Terrace, serving freshly prepared international dishes.

Active guests can enjoy the spa, gym, squash court and the outdoor swimming pool with view an outdoor Jacuzzi and kids pool, all with a stunning view of the Burj Khalifa.

The nearest airport is Dubai International Airport, 11 km or 15 minutes from Steigenberger Hotel - Business Bay.
