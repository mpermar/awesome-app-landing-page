Description

All rooms and suites at Four Seasons Resort Dubai are decorated in a modern style with a touch of Arabic elegance. Each suite includes a living area with a flat-screen TV and a dining area. PlayStation/Xbox are available upon request. The gleaming bathroom has custom Murano glass pendant and features a deep soaking tub, mirror with built-in TV and a separate rain shower.

For an eclectic dining experience head to SUQ with its dynamic market style or relax at Mercury lounge on the sixth floor overlooking Burj Khalifa. Hendricks bar, overlooking the sea, serves cigars and cocktails in a gentlemen’s club inspired atmosphere. In-room dining is also available upon request.

The hotel has 2 tennis courts and a jogging track by the beach. There are special programs for younger guests designed to engage their minds in a casual, fun environment. In-house guests can enjoy the exclusivity and privacy of the cabanas and day villas.

The Four Seasons Resort is 12 miles from Palm Jumeirah and Dubai International Airport. Mall of the Emirates is 20 minutes away by car. Gold Souk is 6 miles from the property.

Jumeirah is a great choice for travelers interested in beaches, food and relaxation.
