Description

JA Palm Tree Court features an 800m private beach and is surrounded by lush gardens, peaceful water features and birdlife.

This 5-star hotel is home to 4 temperature-controlled swimming pools, a spa, leisure facilities, 3 kids clubs and 16 dining outlets.

All 208 spacious suites enjoy views over the gardens or the beach from the balconies or private terraces.

The suites have a separate walk-in shower, bathtub, LCD TV, DVD player and minibar.

The luxurious suites of The Residence come with additional benefits that include private butler service and free babysitting.

These guests also enjoy exclusive access to The Signatures Saltwater Pool and lounge.

With 16 restaurants and bars, dining is a pleasure. At La Fontana, international fare and themed nights can be enjoyed with views over the swimming pool, while White Orchid serves Asian Fusion specialties, and Shooters crafts tex-mex specialties overlooking the shooting range.

Guests can book decadent Elemis massages, facials, body wraps and hammam rituals at the beautiful spa, which features a Jacuzzi, steam room and sauna or enjoy hair and nail treatments in the salon.

A variety of sports and leisure facilities make this hotel the perfect destination for an exciting Dubai holiday.

JA Palm Tree Court is 20 minutes from Dubai Marina and 15 minutes from Dubai World Central Airport and offers daily shuttle transfers to major shopping malls.

It is also one of the closest hotels to Dubai Parks & Resorts, home of three world-class theme parks and one water park.
