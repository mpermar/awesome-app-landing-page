Description

Featuring a rooftop pool and bar with panoramic views of Dubai Creek, city and Arabian Sea, the 5-star Hilton Dubai Creek is located on the busy Dubai Creek.

Its a 10 minute drive to many of Dubais attractions such as Gold & textile market as well as to Dubai International Airport.

Hilton Dubai Creek's rooms feature contemporary decor and marble bathrooms. All rooms are equipped with a Hilton Serenity Bed and deluxe bedding.

The rooms are modern with flat screen TV and offer creek or city skyline views. Hilton Dubai Creek offers a complimentary shuttle service to its sister hotel on Jumeirah Beach, where guests have access to the hotel’s beachside facilities.

Guests can relax in the rooftop swimming pool while enjoying views of the city, or exercise in the fully-equipped fitness centre.

Guests can enjoy dining on modern classics at Glasshouse Brasserie, international cuisine at the casual-chic bistro Table 9, a hearty American fare alongside sports at the famous O'Learys or poolside barbecue at The Edge Pool &amp; Bar.

Business guests can make use of the meeting spaces with room for 20 delegates or stay connected in the fully serviced business centre.

Dubai International Airport is 5 kms away and can be reached easily either by metro or taxi.

An airport shuttle is available upon request. Complimentary valet parking is available at the property.
