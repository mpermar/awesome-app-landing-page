Description

JA Beach Hotel features an 800m private beach, 2 temperature-controlled swimming pools, a children’s pool, 3 kids’ clubs and babysitting services.

The 231 rooms and suites enjoy free WiFi and stunning views over the lush gardens, marina or Arabian Gulf.

Family rooms consist of 2 interconnecting rooms, ideal for larger families. Room amenities include a DVD player, LCD TV and minibar.

This beachfront hotel includes 12 restaurants and bars including La Traviata, an Italian restaurant, and Ibn Majed, an all-day dining restaurant with themed culinary nights.

Guests can also enjoy tex-mex specialties overlooking the shooting range at Shooters.

Sport and leisure facilities include a 9-hole golf course and academy, wide range of water sports, tennis, squash, horse riding, table tennis and mini-golf.

The private 85-berth full service marina offers fishing trips, boat trips and seaplane flights.

Younger guests will be entertained at the Crèche which caters for babies from 4 months to toddlers under 4 years and CoolZone kids club offers daily activities for children aged between 4 to 12 years, while ChillZone is dedicated to teenagers from 13 -16 years.

The hotel’s spa features an impressive selection of Elemis body treatments, facials and massages, as well as an extensive wet area with a Jacuzzi, steam room and sauna.

JA Shooting Club is a short drive from the hotel and offers pistol and clay pigeon shooting, while the adjacent JA Centre of Excellence features several flood-lit pitches, perfect for football, rugby and cricket.

JA Beach Hotel is 20 minutes from Dubai Marina and 15 minutes from Dubai World Central Airport and offers daily shuttle transfers to major shopping malls.

It is also one of the closest hotels to Dubai Parks & Resorts, home of three world-class theme parks and one water park.
