Description

This hotel offers a rooftop pool with views of Dubai cityline and non-smoking rooms with an interactive flat-screen TV and designer bathrooms.

It is located across from Burjuman Shopping Centre. Park Regis Kris Kin Hotel’s rooms have a bedside panel featuring controls for lighting and air conditioning.

It features a large work space with an ergonomic chair and plug-points at desk-level.

International beers and grilled food are available at Grandstand Bar. The 19th floor Restaurant and Wine Bar offers panoramic views of Dubai combined with authentic Asian dishes and fine wines.

Guests can relax in the hot tub or stretch their muscles in the well-equipped fitness centre.

The hotel also offers a sauna and a steam room. The World Trade Exhibition Centre is just a 10-minute drive from the hotel.

The hotel provides airport shuttle to Dubai International Airport for an additional charge.
