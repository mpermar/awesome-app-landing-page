Description

The suites of Kempinski Hotel & Residences Palm Jumeirah include a dining/living area, a full kitchen and private balcony. DVD player, 40" flat-screen TV and sound system sound system are featured in each suite.

Authentic Mediterranean cuisine is served at the elegant Brunello Restaurant. Succulent steaks and seafood can be enjoyed at the beach side barbecue area. The K-West Bar offers cocktails and cigars.

Guests can relax under the palms that surround the large seafront pool, or train at the state-of-the-art gym. The experienced staff at the spa provides relaxing body wraps, manicures and several beauty treatments.
