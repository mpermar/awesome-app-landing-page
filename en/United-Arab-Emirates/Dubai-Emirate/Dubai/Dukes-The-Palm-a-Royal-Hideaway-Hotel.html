Description

Situated on Palm Jumeirah, Dukes The Palm, a Royal Hideaway Hotel brings a world of British charm with cosmopolitan luxury.

Guests can lounge around in the lazy river or enjoy an array of water sports on the beach front.

Dukes The Palm, a Royal Hideaway Hotel boasts 279 luxuriously appointed rooms and 227 tastefully furnished serviced apartments with spectacular views of the Arabian Gulf, framed by the iconic Marina skyline.

All rooms and suites have a flat screen satellite TV, coffee and tea making facilities, a spacious wardrobe and an in room safe.

You will also find bathrobes, slippers and complimentary Floris toiletries. Dukes The Palm, a Royal Hideaway Hotel offer the absolute best of British to the table, the Great British Restaurant (GBR) serves up a fabulous choice of dishes with a British twist.

Guests can dine at GBR for an all-day dining experience with views of the glistening Dubai Marina skyline, or choose to enjoy a wonderful dining experience at our West 14th Beach front bar restaurant.

Dukes Bar concocts some of the world’s best Martinis. The property offers a state-of-the-art gym with advanced equipment to cater to guests fitness regime.

Situated on the 14th floor, guests can work out to magnificent views of Dubai Marina Skyline through floor to ceiling windows.

There is also a highly reputable hair salon at the property for guests who want to pamper themselves.

Dukes The Palm, a Royal Hideaway Hotel is easily accessible from Dubai International Airport (DXB) and Al Maktoum International Airport (DWC), which are both just 30 minutes’ drive away.

Mall of Emirates is a 20 minute drive away, whilst Dubai Mall can be reached in 30 minutes.
