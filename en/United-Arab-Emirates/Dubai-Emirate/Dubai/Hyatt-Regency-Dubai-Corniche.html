Description

Hyatt Regency Dubai is located in the heritage district, within a 5-minute walk from the popular gold, spice and textile souks.

This luxury hotel offers a revolving restaurant, a private ice skating rink and free WiFi.

In the luxurious guest rooms, elegant furnishings and scenic views are standard. A seating area is featured in some rooms where guests can relax with a drink from the minibar while watching satellite TV.

Hyatt Regency’s Club Olympus Spa and Fitness has a modern gym and tennis courts. Spa services are offered with a wide range of relaxing massages.

From Al Dawaar, Dubai’s only rooftop, revolving restaurant to genuine Japanese cuisine at Miyako or international cuisine at The Kitchen, unique dining options are offered at the hotel.

Unwind at a Karaoke lounge, watch major sporting events live at a sports bar, enjoy a fine selection of beverages, or party in style with legendary nightlife.

Hyatt Regency Dubai - Corniche is 8 km from Dubai International Airport. Palm Deira Metro Station 2 can be reached in 7 minutes by feet or in a few minutes by complimentary shuttle service upon availability.
