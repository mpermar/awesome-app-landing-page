Description

Nestled in the heart of Dubai, Swissôtel Living Al Ghurair is a deluxe hotel apartment property connected to the city’s business and tourist destinations through the Dubai Metro rail network.

It is a walking distance from the Union Metro station (central hub to all metro stations).

It is 4 stations away from the Dubai International Airport, 3 stations away from Dubai World Trade Centre, and 3 stations from the Health Care City It offers 192 home apartments that include well-designed one, two and three bedrooms.

Each room has a private balcony with views of Dubai, a beautiful complex gardens and swimming pools.

A fully equipped kitchen with a fridge, glass-ceramic cooktop panel, oven (in three-bedrooms only), microwave and dish washer makes a comfortable living for both long & short-stay guests.

The best choice for a family oriented hotel apartment destination in Dubai. It is connected to Al Ghurair Center, a shopping destination with a wide range of restaurants featuring various cuisines.

Swissôtel Living Al Ghurair offers a complimentary shuttle service to La Mer Beach in Jumeriah.
