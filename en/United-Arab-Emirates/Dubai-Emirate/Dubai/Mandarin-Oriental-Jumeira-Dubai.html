Description

Boasting 5 outdoor swimming pools, fitness centre and shared lounge, Mandarin Oriental Jumeira, Dubai is located in Dubai, with direct access to the beach.

Conveniently located in the Jumeirah district, the property is situated 250 metres from Mercato Mall, 2.

6 km from The Village Mall and 3. 1 km from City Walk Mall. The property features a 24-hour front desk.

All rooms in the hotel are equipped with a coffee machine. All rooms are fitted with a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hair dryer.

The units are equipped with a seating area. A buffet breakfast is available each morning at the property.

The Spa at Mandarin Oriental provides extensive range of beauty and wellness therapies and is connected to a comprehensive fitness studio.

Mandarin Oriental Jumeira, Dubai also provides a business centre and guests can use the on-site ATM machine or check the newspapers at the accommodation.

Dubai International Financial Centre is only 10 minutes from Mandarin Oriental Jumeira, Dubai.
