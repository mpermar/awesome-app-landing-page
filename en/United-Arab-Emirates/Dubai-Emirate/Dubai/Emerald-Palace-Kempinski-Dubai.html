Description

Situated on the western crescent of the man-made island, Palm Jumeirah, the Emerald Palace Kempinski Dubai boasts a 500-metre private white sand beach, an outdoor swimming pool offering views of the city, and 8 culinary experiences including the miX by 3-Michelin star chef, Alain Ducasse.

The elegant rooms are air-conditioned and come complete with a flat-screen satellite TV, a seating area and minibar.

At Emerald Palace Kempinski Dubai guests can indulge in an array of cuisines comprising of Italian, Venetian, Asian and Mediterranean.

A scrumptious buffet breakfast is served daily in the Le Jardin restaurant. The 3000 square-metre Cinq Mondes Spa features 23 treatment rooms, offering guests a haven to relax and unwind.

Here guests can also find a modern state-of-the-art fitness centre and a large indoor lap pool.

Free WiFi is available in public areas and the concierge service may offer guests assistance in booking tours, excursions, as well as special requests.

Emerald Palace Kempinski Dubai is 4 km from Aquaventure Waterpark, 18 km from Mall of the Emirates and 30 km from Burj Khalifa.
