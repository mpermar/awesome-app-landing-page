Description

Situated on Dubai’s famous The Walk on Jumeirah Beach Residence just steps away from Jumeirah Beach and offering endless shopping, dining and leisure facilities.

JA Ocean View Hotel is ideally located whether you’re travelling on business or leisure, with its scenic Arabian Gulf views, 6 restaurants and bars, infinity swimming pool and free WiFi throughout the property.

All rooms and suites at JA Ocean View Hotel feature sea views and a private balcony.

Additionally, Club Rooms and Suites offer free benefits, snacks and access to Coral Lounge on the 24th floor.

Up to 2 adults and 2 children can stay in a room, though larger Family Rooms are also available.

Enjoy tea, coffee and light snacks at Caffé Via, or savour gourmet buffets and theme nights in modern brasserie style at Le Rivage.

Enjoy pizza at Pizzeria Motorino, a new generation pizza concept from Brooklyn New York, or drinks at Offside, the new sports bar.

The Deck serves cocktails and light snacks by the poolside during daytime. At night, the venue transforms to Sehr Al Bahar, an Arabian venue with shisha, belly dancing and dinner.

Relax in the outdoor swimming pool or pick up fresh towels on your way to the public beach.

During the week, guests can also take the free shuttle to the private resort beach at JA Jebel Ali Golf Resort.

Kids can look forward to a play area and children’s pool. The gym is well equipped and for an extra fee.

Calm Spa & Salon provides massages and other treatments for an additional charge, while the Health Club offers access to a sauna, hot tub and steam room.

A complimentary shuttle to shopping malls and sightseeing landmarks is available for guests, as well as free valet parking.

Local excursions can be arranged at the hotel’s tour desk. Dubai Marina Mall, Ibn Battuta Mall and Emirates Golf Club are all within 10 minutes’ drive from JA Ocean View Hotel.

Legoland and other theme parks can be reached in 25 minutes. Both Dubai International Airport and Al Maktoum International Airport are 30 minutes away by car.
