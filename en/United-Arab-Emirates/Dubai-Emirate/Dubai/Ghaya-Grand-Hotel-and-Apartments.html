Description

Ghaya Grand Hotel is located in the International Media Production Zone. Guests can enjoy free WiFi throughout the property.

It offers a swimming pool and 5 restaurants. All modern rooms are furnished with LED TVs with satellite channels, individual air conditioning, a sitting area, complimentary tea and coffee making facilities and a minibar.

The bathrooms feature a large shower cabinet and a bathtub. Dining is a pleasure at Ghaya Grand Hotel.

Guests can enjoy a wide array of cuisines in our two main restaurants, Red Diamond and Fusion.

Shisha is served at Al Mawal. A 24 hour lobby lounge and a pool bar will keep you refreshed throughout the day.

At Ghaya Grand you will also find a tennis court, a gym, a spa where you can enjoy rejuvenating treatment and a kids playground.

The property can assist in the planning of large or small meetings, seminars or private celebrations along with any audio-visual equipment you may require for the Ghaya Ball Room or any of the 9 meeting rooms.

The Ghaya Grand Hotel is only a 10-minute drive from JBR and Mall of the Emirates, and a 25-minute drive to Dubai International Airport.

It is a 5-minute walk from Me`aisem City Center. It offers free shuttle buses to Mall of the Emirates, JBR and Ibn Battuta Mall on a daily basis.
