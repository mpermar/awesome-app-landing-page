Description

Mövenpick Bur Dubai is a modern hotel offering 312 rooms and apartments. The apartments are situated next to the hotel.

Upscale facilities include a well-equipped gym and an outdoor swimming pool. For full relaxation, guests can treat themselves to a rejuvenating massage treatment at a surcharge.

Dubai International Airport is 10 minutes away Rooms are elegantly furnished with a seating area and a work desk.

The floor-to-ceiling windows allow plenty of daylight to enter each room. A coffeemaker and minibar provide are also available.

The apartments are fully equipped with Kitchenette complete with a refrigerator and a washing machine.

High speed WiFi is available for free. Enjoy unique dining experience from a choice of International, Arabian and Indian cuisine in 7 restaurants and bars.

For those seeking entertainment, Pulse nightclub is a popular place to be in Bur Dubai.

Free private parking is available. Situated opposite the American Hospital, Mövenpick Bur Dubai is just off Sheikh Zayed Road.

Dubai Healthcare City, Wafi and Lamcy shopping malls can all be reached in 5 minutes.

Dubai Mall, Burj Khalifa and World Trade Centre are 10 minutes by car. Expo 2020 grounds is 32 km away.
