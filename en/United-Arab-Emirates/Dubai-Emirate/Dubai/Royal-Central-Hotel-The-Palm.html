Description

Featuring a private beach area, Royal Central Hotel The Palm is situated in Dubai.

Boasting a 24-hour front desk, this property also provides guests with a terrace.

The in-house restaurant serves Mediterranean cuisine. All units are fitted with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, fridge, a kettle, a bath and a wardrobe.

All rooms have a private bathroom with free toiletries. The hotel offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

Guests can make sightseeing and ticketing arrangements at the tour desk, or conduct business at the business centre.

Mall of the Emirates is 5 km from Royal Central Hotel The Palm. The nearest airport is Dubai International Airport, 25 km from the property.
