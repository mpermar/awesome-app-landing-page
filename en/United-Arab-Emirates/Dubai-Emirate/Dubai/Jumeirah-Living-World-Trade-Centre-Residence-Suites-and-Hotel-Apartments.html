Description

Situated at the World Trade Centre & Exhibition Center, this Dubai hotel offers a rooftop pool, a well-equipped gym and free Wi-Fi.

The luxurious apartments include private spa baths, kitchens and satellite TV. All Jumeirah Living World Trade Centre Residence, Suites and Hotel Apartments offer views of the Burj Khalifa, Zabeel Palace and Sheikh Zayed Road.

The bathrooms have monsoon showers, and guests can listen to their own music with an iPod docking station.

Recreational facilities at Jumeirah Living include a rooftop swimming pool, a children's pool and a large gym with modern equipment.

Situated on the 30th floor of the World Trade Centre, The Club restaurant offers panoramic views across the city.

It serves a selection of international food. Al Jafiliya Metro Station and Dubai International Convention Centre are both 5 minutes’ walk from Jumeirah Living Residence.

World Trade Centre Metro Station within is a short 250 meters away. Free parking is available on site.
