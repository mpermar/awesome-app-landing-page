Description

Located on the world famous Palm Jumeirah, Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah features luxury rooms and suites with panoramic views of the Arabian Gulf.

This elegant beach resort offers a 200-metre private beach, 2 temperature-controlled swimming pools, water sports activities, tennis courts, Kids Club and a Waldorf Astoria Spa.

Guests can enjoy free WiFi acess in the whole property. All spacious rooms are decorated with a contemporary design and features the signature Waldorf Astoria beds.

The rooms offer a large balcony with stunning sea views of the Palm Jumeirah or the Dubai skyline.

Each room has a comfortable seating area, workstation and a 42 inch flat-screen TV.

The white marble bathrooms are fitted with a spacious bath with TV, a walk-in shower and luxury amenities.

Guests can choose from 6 restaurants and bars for a diverse dining experience with Arabic-inspired cuisine at Mezzerie, Southeast Asian flavours at LAO or fine-casual dining at Social by Michelin chef Heinz Beck.

Palm Avenue restaurant and lounge features a terrace with private cabanas where cocktails and international dishes can be enjoyed.

Serafina Bar offers a signature cocktail list and live entertainment in the evenings.

Dubai Marina is a 10-minute drive from Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah. Burj Khalifa and the Dubai Mall are 30 minutes away by car.
