Description

Marjan Island Resort & Spa is situated on the first man-made island in Ras Al Khaimah, inside the clear waters of the Arabian Gulf.

Located 45 minutes from Dubai, this alcohol free resort is devoted to serenity and well-being.

It offers spacious rooms and suites with breath-taking views, a private beach, a one of a kind spa and various leisure activities.

All elegantly designed rooms and suites are equipped with interactive flat-screen TVs and offer complementary WiFi to ensure utmost comfort.

The resort offers eclectic dining options to cater for all tastes. Savour international food in Al Liwan, experience traditional Lebanese cuisines or other cooking style in Zaitouna House and enjoy an Al-fresco dining in the Mediterranean Al Forno restaurant with stunning sea view.

Various children activities like the Pirates club, equipped with the newest arcade games, the annexed safe and fun playground gardens or riding bicycles and carts on the corniche will ensure the little ones are enjoying their time.

Lamar Spa offers a health club including steams, saunas, hot tubs, gyms and indoor pools.

A private health club for ladies only is available. Shopping can be enjoyed at Al Hamra Mall which is a 5-minute drive away from Marjan Island Resort & Spa.

The popular Ice land Water Park is 10 minutes by car. Free private parking is possible on site.
