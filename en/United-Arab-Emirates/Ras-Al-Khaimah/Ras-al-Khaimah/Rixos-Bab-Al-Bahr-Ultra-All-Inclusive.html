Description

This all-inclusive resort in Ras Al Khaimah features unlimited food and beverage offerings from 14 dining options along with daily entertainment.

It is located on the pristine beaches of the man-made Al Marjan Island. All the rooms and suites offer a flat-screen TV, a minibar and an electric kettle.

Some suites include a balcony with a panoramic sea view. Rixos Bab Al Bahr offers 1 global cuisine buffet restaurant, 6 a la carte restaurants, 6 bars and lounges and 1 night club.

Room service is available. Guests can take a dip in the outdoor pool and soak up the sun at the resort's beach.

To enjoy an energising workout, there is a fully equipped fitness centre. The resort also boasts a spa, a kids' club and a teens’ club.

Ras Al Khaimah City Centre is a 25-minute drive from Rixos Bab Al Bahr. Rixos Bab al Bahr is a 60-minute drive from Dubai International Airport.
