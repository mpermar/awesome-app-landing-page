Description

Hilton Garden Inn Ras Al Khaimah is located in the Al Nakheel area by the creek, just steps from the Free Trade Zone and RAK Exhibition Centre.

A walking distance to two major shopping malls, the hotel is also a stone's throw away from the souks and historical sites of the Emirates’ old city.

Hilton Garden Inn Ras Al Khaimah's rooms feature modern décor and a spacious seating area.

All rooms are equipped with ergonomic chairs and plush beds to ensure comfort at all times.

After a good night sleep, start the day with a delicious breakfast – choose from the extensive buffet and cooked-to-order eggs to accompany homemade American waffles.

Perfect for a relaxing meal, the Garden Grille and Bar also serves internationally inspired cuisine for lunch and dinner.

For those seeking solace after a long day, unwind with cocktails at our Lounge or pick up snacks, drinks, and travel essentials at The Shop.

The 24 hours room service is ideal for those feeling peckish in the late evenings.

The Hilton Garden Inn Ras Al Khaimah is located 25 minutes’ drive from Ras Al Khaimah International Airport while Dubai International Airport is 70 minutes away.
