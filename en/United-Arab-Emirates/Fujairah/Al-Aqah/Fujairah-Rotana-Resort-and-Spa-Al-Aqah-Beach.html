Description

Nestled between the Hajar Mountains and the Indian Ocean, this beachside resort offers luxury spa treatments and floodlit sports courts.

The non-smoking rooms have a balcony with panoramic views. All rooms feature a colonial-style interior and are fitted with deluxe bedding.

Each one has a well-stocked minibar and also offers complimentary tea and coffee facilities.

At Fujairah Rotana Resort, guests can relax on the private beach or go for a refreshing swim in the temperature-controlled swimming pool.

Younger guests at Fujairah Rotana can make use of the hotels club for kids or enjoy a game in the outdoor play area.

There is also a separate childrens pool. Mozaique serves a variety of international dishes and also includes regular themed dinner nights.

Well-known cocktails, a variety of beers and Cuban cigars are available at Bar Muda.
