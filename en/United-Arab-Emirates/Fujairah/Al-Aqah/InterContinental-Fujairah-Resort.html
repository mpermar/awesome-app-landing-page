Description

The InterContinental Fujairah Resort is located on the shoreline of the exquisite Al Aqah Beach and beneath the spectacular backdrop of the Hajjar Mountains.

Spread over 69 acres of land, the resort features 190 stylish guest rooms and suites with expansive balconies offering uninterrupted views across the ocean.

There are various lunch and dining options, from a sumptuous international buffet served in the all-day-dining restaurant, to flame-grilled delicacies and Worldly Classics cocktails prepared by the beach, through to a fun poolside food truck, each outlet providing guests with a truly unique dining experience.

For something a little different, three beach fire pits add a unique touch to family and friend gatherings or corporate events.

Designed to enhance the stay of discerning guests, Club InterContinental offers a range of exclusive benefits, including bespoke menus in the Club Lounge and exclusive use of a temperature-controlled swimming pool.

Take time to unwind and indulge in the resort's wonderfully pampering O Spa by L'Occitane, including six treatment rooms, two couples’ suites and a Turkish hammam.

Sport enthusiasts of all ages can get active through numerous recreational activities and a 24/7 gym.

The resort's Kids & Teens Club provides junior guests aged 4 to 16 years old with unforgettable fun moments during their stay.

Dubai International Airport is less than a two-hour drive from InterContinental Fujairah Resort.
