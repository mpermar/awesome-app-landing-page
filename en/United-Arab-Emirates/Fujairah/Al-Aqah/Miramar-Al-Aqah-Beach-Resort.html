Description

Nestled between the foothills of the Hajar Mountains and the glistering Indian Ocean, this 5-star Moroccan style resort offers luxurious rooms with a private balcony.

Facilities include a spa, a diving centre, outdoor and indoor pools, and tennis courts.

All rooms of Miramar Al Aqah Beach Resort Fujairah are equipped with air conditioning, a minibar, and satellite TV.

The private balconies offer views of the pools, the Hajjar Mountains, or the Indian Ocean.

Miramar Al Aqah Beach Resort’s entertainment team organises live music with dancing shows and folklore throughout the day.

The spa features 2 saunas, 2 steam baths, and a hot tub. Guests can indulge in a variety of water sports including windsurfing, diving, snorkeling and kayaking.

Miramar Al Aqah Beach Resort also offers 24 hour room service, laundry service and wireless Internet access all over the resort.

Safari trips inland can be arranged or guests can stay within the complex and dance their cares away at the Discothèque.

The hotel also provides a Kid’s Club (ages 4 – 12) that will keep youngsters safely occupied with a children's pool and activities, playroom and special menus.

A selection of shops, a health club and gym, temperature controlled outdoor and indoor pools are also readily available Miramar Al Aqah Resort offers a variety of dining options, including traditional Middle Eastern specialities, Italian cuisine, and Asian dishes.

The resort is located on the East Coast of the United Arab Emirates, 140 km from Dubai and 45 km away from Fujairah National Airport.

Free shuttle buses are provided twice a week to malls in Dubai and Fujairah City.
