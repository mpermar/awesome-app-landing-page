Description

Nour Arjaan by Rotana is in Fujairah, 2.5 miles from the Gulf of Oman and about one mile from Fujairah Airport. Free parking is available on site.

All Rotana’s spacious accommodations come equipped with satellite TV and high-speed internet. The kitchenettes include a microwave and a fridge, and the private bathrooms have a hairdryer.

Nour Arjaan by Rotana – Fujairah has a restaurant with modern décor that serves international and regional cuisine. Aquarius Café has casual meals, refreshing cocktails and light snacks by the pool.

Bodylines fitness center features a fully equipped gym with group lessons, massage rooms and dry and wet saunas. Desert safaris, fishing and golf trips can be arranged through the concierge service.
