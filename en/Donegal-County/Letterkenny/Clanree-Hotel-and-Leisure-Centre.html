Description

Rooms at The Clanree all include private bathrooms, satellite TV and tea/coffee-making facilities. Free Wi-Fi is available in the public areas of the hotel.

The leisure club includes a relaxing sauna, a steam room, and a children’s pool. Fuchsia Beauty Salon provides a range of beauty treatments.

Aileach Restaurant serves a traditional roast meal lunch, and early bird menu. McGettigan's Bar features an extensive bar food menu and live entertainment on most weekends.

In the heart of Donegal, the Clanree Hotel is a 25-minute drive from Glenveigh National Park and the historic monuments of Grianan of Aileach. Derry City is 30 miles away.
