Description

Rooms are provided with a TV, a mini-bar and a seating area. Private bathrooms come with a shower and extras include a safety deposit box.

At a surcharge, guests also have access to a hot tub and can use the barbecue facilities. Hotel Ondrášův dvůr also features a restaurant with a terrace, serving Czech and Moravian cuisine.

The property is 1,000 feet from the Bílá – Mezivodí Bus Stop and 6.2 mi from the Šance Reservoir.
