Description

The rooms at the resort come with a dishwasher, an oven, a electric tea pot, a bath, free toiletries, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and Blu-ray player. The private bathroom is equipped with a hair dryer. All rooms include a closet.

Avoca Palms Resort offers 4-star accommodations with a sauna. You can play ping pong at the accommodation.

Kingsford Smith Airport is 35 miles away.
