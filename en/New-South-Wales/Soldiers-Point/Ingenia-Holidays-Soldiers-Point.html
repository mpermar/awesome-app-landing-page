Description

All of the units feature a private bathroom, flat-screen TV and fully-equipped kitchen. A microwave and toaster are also available, as well as a electric tea pot.

The property has an indoor pool.

A water park can be found at the resort village, along with a garden.

Newcastle is 22 miles from Ingenia Holidays Soldiers Point, while Nelson Bay is 4.3 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Williamtown Airport, 15 miles from the property.
