Description

Each refurbished modern room includes an LCD TV with select cable channels and movies-on-demand, air conditioning, heating, an ergonomically designed work space and a luxurious private bathroom. Many rooms offer views of Terrigal Beach.

Guests can indulge in fresh seafood and local produce at Seasalt restaurant, relax with a cocktail, wine or beer in the Lord Ashley Lounge, enjoy vibrant, open-air dining at the Florida Beach Bar, or sample the in-room dining options.

Guests can stay active at the on-site fitness center with an outdoor heated pool, or relax with a massage and treatment at the Endota Day Spa. A kid’s club is available during the school holidays.

Crowne Plaza Hotel Terrigal is just a short 2-minute walk from Terrigal’s best shopping and restaurants. Guests can discover an idyllic lifestyle and world-class amenities, just a 90-minute drive from Sydney.
