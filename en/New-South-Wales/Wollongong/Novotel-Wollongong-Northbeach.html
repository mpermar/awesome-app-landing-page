Description

Novotel Hotel Wollongong Northbeach caters to both leisure and business travelers, offering facilities and services such as a swimming pool, fitness center, day spa, restaurant and 2 bars.

All rooms are beautifully styled with modern furnishings, crisp white bed linens, comfortable beds, and spacious bathrooms with over-sized bath towels.

Windjammers Restaurant features panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean, an innovative and contemporary menu, and an extensive wine list. The intimate Lobby Bar is the ideal for pre-dinner drinks.

Just a 1.5-hour drive south of Sydney in the beautiful Illawarra, Novotel Wollongong Northbeach is within easy walking distance of central Wollongong.
