Description

The Castle of Comfort Country House is just off the A39, less than 10 miles from Bridgwater. The 5-star house has a Gold Award from VisitBritain.

The house was a travelers inn during the 17th century and still has many original features, such as wooden beams.

The Castle of Comfort is now a small country house providing 5-star guest accommodations. The rooms with private bathrooms have TVs, telephones, hairdryers, tea/coffee and free sherry.

There are large gardens, a lounge for drinks and a restaurant that is open every evening except Sundays.
