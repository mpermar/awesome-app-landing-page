Description

Rooms have a plantation-style décor, king beds and private bathrooms with showers. They also include a TV, a coffee maker, and a safe. Some units have views of the gardens or the seafront.

East Winds Inn’s restaurant serves international cuisine and provides afternoon tea services.

East Winds Inn also has a lounge area with live music, massages and a bar. Recommended activities include yoga lessons, ping-pong, and guided tours.

George F.L. Charles Airport can be reached in a 10-minute drive from the inn, and Pigeon Island National Park is 7.5 miles from the property.
