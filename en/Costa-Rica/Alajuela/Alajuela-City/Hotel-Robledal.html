Description

Within 6 km of San José Airport, Hotel Robledal is a 30-minute drive from San José city centre.

Set in gardens, it features rooms with balconies or terraces, and an outdoor swimming pool.

Hotel Robledal’s bright, air-conditioned rooms overlook the hotel grounds. All rooms come with cable TV and a private bathroom.

Guests can enjoy a varied breakfast including fresh fruit and Costa Rican coffee.

A range of local grilled cuisine is on offer for dinner. The hotel is within 30 km of Poás Volcano Park and Braulio Carrillo National Park.

San José's butterfly garden is also nearby. The Pan American Highway is just 5 minutes’ drive away.
