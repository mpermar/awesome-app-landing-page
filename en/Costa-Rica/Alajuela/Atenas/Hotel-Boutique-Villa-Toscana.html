Description

Guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Every room includes a coffee machine and a private bathroom, while selected rooms here will provide you with a balcony and some have mountain views. The rooms at Hotel Boutique Villa Toscana feature air conditioning and a closet.

The daily breakfast offers continental and American options.

Jacó is 30 miles from the accommodation, while San José is 21 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Juan Santamaría International Airport, 12 miles from Hotel Boutique Villa Toscana.
