Description

Offering outdoor pool and a hot tub, Hotel Roca Negra Del Arenal is located in the suburbs of Fortuna.

Free Wi-Fi access is available. Rooms here will provide you with cable TV, air conditioning and a terrace.

Complete with a refrigerator, the dining area also has a coffee machine. Private bathrooms also come with a shower.

Guests will be able to find several restaurants available within 100 metres, like Vagabundo and La Pradera.

At Hotel Roca Negra Del Arenal you will find a garden and a tour desk. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including hiking, visiting a bird sanctuary, or enjoy the tropical gardens.

Juan Santamaría International Airport is 73 km away, while Arenal Volcano National Park is 15.
