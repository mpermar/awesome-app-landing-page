Description

Offering an outdoor pool and a restaurant, Hotel del Sur is located in Isidro del General.

At Hotel del Sur you will find a tennis court and a fitness centre. Other facilities offered at the property include a tour desk and luggage storage.

The property offers free parking. Juan Santamaria International Airport is 155 km away.
