Description

Located in the stylish Cariari district of San José, this charming home-style boutique hotel offers an outdoor pool and terraces.

The spacious, air-conditioned rooms open onto the patio and gardens. The elegant rooms offer a home atmosphere and feature cable TV and free WiFi and wired internet access.

The private bathrooms include a shower and free toiletries. Staff can arrange excursions and airport transfers.

Juan Santamaria International Airport is a 15-minute drive from Casa Primo, while central San José is just a 20-minute drive away.

Free private parking is available onsite and the Wyndham Herradura Convention Centre is 5 minutes' walk away.
