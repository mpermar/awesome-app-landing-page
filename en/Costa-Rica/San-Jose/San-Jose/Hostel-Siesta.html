Description

Boasting a garden, bar, shared lounge and free WiFi, Hostel Siesta is situated in San José, 2.

1 km from National Theatre of Costa Rica and 4 km from La Sabana Metropolitan Park.

Featuring room service, this property also provides guests with a barbecue. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, a shared kitchen and currency exchange for guests.

The inn offers a continental or buffet breakfast. Hostel Siesta offers a sun terrace.

Estadio Nacional de Costa Rica is 6 km from the accommodation, while Parque Diversiones is 10 km from the property.
