Description

Located 5 minutes’ drive from central San Jose, this comfortable hotel offers free breakfast, an outdoor pool and tennis courts.

Juan Santamaria International Airport is just 18 km away. The elegantly decorated rooms at Best Western Irazú Hotel & Studios have air-conditioning and feature cable TV, tea and coffee facilities and en-suite bathroom.

Free local calls and Wi-Fi are included. Denny’s Restaurant is open 24 hours and serves breakfast, lunch and dinner, while Sangria Tapas Bar and Restaurant serves cocktails.

Room service is also available. Best Western Irazú Hotel & Studios has a gym. Guests can also enjoy night entertainment at the Casino Concorde.

The hotel is ideally located for many local attractions including the Costa Rican Art Museum, 3 km away, and the National theatre, 7 km away.
