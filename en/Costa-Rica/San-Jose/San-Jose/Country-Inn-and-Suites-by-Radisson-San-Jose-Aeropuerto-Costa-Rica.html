Description

The rooms in Country Inns & Suites by Carlson feature a private bathroom, air conditioning, and cable TV. Room service and laundry services are available.

Guests at in Country Inns & Suites by Carlson can enjoy on-site dining at Mambi Restaurant. This eatery overlooks the hotel's lush gardens and lovely outdoor pool, creating the perfect dining atmosphere.

Pueblo Antiguo and Café Britt coffee plantation are a 10-minute drive from the hotel, and the Poas Volcano is a 40-minute drive away. Guests can enjoy tours to the rainforest or relax at the beaches only a short drive away. Juan Santamaria Airport is 5 mi away.
