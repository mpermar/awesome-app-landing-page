Description

All guest rooms in BornToBeWild EcoResort are equipped with a electric tea pot. Featuring a private bathroom, certain rooms at the accommodation also offer a terrace. At BornToBeWild EcoResort all rooms are equipped with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV.

Breakfast is available every morning, and includes continental and buffet options.

Hiking is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the resort.

Tamarindo is 17 miles from BornToBeWild EcoResort, while Playa Conchal is 10 miles away. Liberia Airport is 12 miles from the property.
