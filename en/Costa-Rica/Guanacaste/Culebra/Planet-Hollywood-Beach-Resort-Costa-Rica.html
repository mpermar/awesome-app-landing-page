Description

Planet Hollywood Beach Resort Costa Rica offers accommodation in Guanacaste. Featuring a 24-hour front desk, this property also provides guests with 7 different restaurants.

The tour desk is available to assist guests in planning their days out. Each suite also features: Light PHantasticTM program for the coolest lighting everywhere 24-hour room service Private balcony Plush bathrobes & slippers Liquor dispenser Your SoundtrackTM, program to pick your music theme during your stay in paradise Plugged InTM, all guests can enjoy a PHTV in each suite, high-speed Wi-Fi throughout the resort, and can download the PH Mobile App to enhance their vacation experience Buffet breakfast is available each morning at the property.

The accommodation offers a fitness centre. You can play tennis at this 5-star hotel.

Tamarindo is 80 km from Planet Hollywood Beach Resort Costa Rica, while Playa Conchal is 60 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Daniel Oduber Quirs International Airport, 30 km from the property.
