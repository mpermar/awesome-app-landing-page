Description

Villas Sol Costa Rica’s double rooms feature 2 double beds or one extra large double bed. They have a flat-screen cable TV, an 16-inch digital safe and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

All villas are air-conditioned and have a spacious living-dining room and a full kitchen including kitchen utensils, a toaster and a fridge.

La Bahía restaurant serves a continental and buffet breakfast, and is open for lunch and dinner. The restaurant offers themed dinner nights, including Costa Rican, Mexican, Italian or international food.

The staff can give you more information about sights and activities in the surrounding area.

Daniel Oduber International Airport is just a 20 minute drive away.
