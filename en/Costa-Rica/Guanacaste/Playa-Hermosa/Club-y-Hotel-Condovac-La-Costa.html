Description

Featuring free WiFi, a spa centre, an infiniti outdoor pool and hot tub, Club y Hotel Condovac La Costa offers accommodation in Playa Hermosa.

The hotel has a year-round outdoor pool and sauna, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Free private parking is available on site. Every room is fitted with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Certain rooms include a seating area to relax in after a busy day. You will find a coffee machine in the room.

All rooms include a private bathroom. There is a 24-hour front desk and hairdresser's at the property.

Facilities at the property include a Restaurant, an infinity pool with a wet bar, a children's pool, fitness centre, Hot tub, conference room, spa, tennis and basketball courts, pool tables, and terraces overlooking the sea.

A variety of activities such as Hiking, beach volleyball, recreational activities and entertainment for the whole family and nightly shows are also offered.

You can also find internal transportation, car hire and an on-site supermarket. Free Kayak gear is available.

Tamarindo is 36 km from Club y Hotel Condovac La Costa, while Playa Conchal is 25 km from the property.
