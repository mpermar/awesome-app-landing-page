Description

All rooms at the hotel come with a seating area. Rooms are equipped with a private bathroom with free toiletries. The rooms will provide guests with an oven.

A continental breakfast is available daily at Flor y Bambu.

Tamarindo is 3.4 miles from the accommodation, while Playa Conchal is 4.4 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Tamarindo Airport, 3.1 miles from the property.
