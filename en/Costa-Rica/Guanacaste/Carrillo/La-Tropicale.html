Description

La Tropicale Beach Lodge offers free continental breakfast. Situated in Carrillo Beach, this property features 2 outdoor pools and massages for a fee.

The bungalows are fully equipped with private bathroom with toiletries, kitchen, dining area and a terrace offering panoramic views.

La Tropicale Beach Lodge has an on-site seafood restaurant and a bar. Guests will also find a supermarket within 350 metres and an Argentinian restaurant, El Colobrí, 300 metres away.

Guests will be helped by La Tropicale Beach Lodge staff to contact a local agency and arrange visits to the beach, the zoo, snorkelling trips, among other activities.

The property also features BBQ facilities and a pool table. The Arenal Volcano site is 3 hours’ drive from the property and Daniel Oduber Quirós International Airport can be reached in 1 hour and 45 minutes by car.
