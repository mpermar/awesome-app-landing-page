Description

Offering spectacular views of Lake Arenal and Arenal Volcano, Mystica has an outdoor pool, yoga platform and free WiFi in the restaurant and lobby.

Rooms have access to a terrace which overlooks the tropical gardens and lake. Mystica’s rooms are individually decorated and named after different local animals.

Each airy room includes Egyptian cotton sheets, a ceiling fan and screened windows.

Rooms also have a private bathroom. You can enjoy pizzas, Italian dishes and international desserts in Mystica’s restaurant.

There is also a lounge with lake views and a fireplace. Free private parking is available at the complex.

Staff at reception are happy to arrange car hire or excursions in the local area.
