Description

All units come with a patio, a kitchen with an oven and a microwave, and a shared bathroom with shower. A fridge and coffee machine are also featured.

The lodge offers a à la carte or American breakfast. Guests can dine in the on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of American dishes and also offers Vegan and Dairy-free options.

Barba Negra Eco-surflodge has a playground.

Both a bicycle rental service and a car rental service are available at the accommodation, while cycling can be enjoyed nearby.

Tamarindo is 8 miles from Barba Negra Eco-surflodge. Tamarindo Airport is 9 miles away.

All children under 6 years are charged USD 10 per night for extra beds.

Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
