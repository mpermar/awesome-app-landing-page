Description

At the hotel, each room comes with a terrace. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with free toiletries, while selected rooms are equipped with a patio. All rooms feature a seating area.

Continental and buffet breakfast options are available every morning at Golfo Dulce Retreat.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool. A variety of popular activities are available in the area around the property, including canoeing and hiking.

Golfito is 6 miles from Golfo Dulce Retreat. The nearest airport is Golfito, 5 miles from the hotel, and the property offers a free airport shuttle service.
