Description

This pleasant apartment complex is set 4 km from the beautiful Manuel Antonio National Park which is open to the general public from Tuesdays through Sundays.

Set in tropical gardens, it offers an outdoor pool, free Wi-Fi zone and rooms with a terrace and scenic views.

The air-conditioned Condiminium Villas Mymosa are spacious and feature cream and light wood décor.

All include a lounge area with TV, a fully equipped kitchenette and a large bathroom.

A safe can also be rented. Mymositos Restaurant, open during the morning, offers an American breakfast made with local produce.

The Mymosa villas are set 2 km from Playitas and Espadilla Beaches and 5 minutes’ drive from Puerto Quepos.

The area is popular for water sports and the complex organises activities including safaris, cruises, hiking and diving.

Condiminium Villas Mymosa offers free parking and is 2 hours’ drive from San Jose.
