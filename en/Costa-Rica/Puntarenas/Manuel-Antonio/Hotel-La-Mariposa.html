Description

The rooms offer modern décor, air conditioning, a desk, fridge, telephone and flat-screen cable TV. The terraces have ocean views and the bathrooms have a shower, hairdryer and free toiletries.

Le Papillon restaurant serves French cuisine; the Sunset Bar & Lounge has international drinks and there is also a pizza place and a pool bar for guests to enjoy. Room service is available.

Activities like canopy tours, fishing, rafting and horseback riding can be arranged at Hotel La Mariposa.

This property is a 10 minute drive from Quepos Village and a 5 minute drive from Manuel Antonio Beach. Juan Santamaria International Airport is a 2 hour drive away.
