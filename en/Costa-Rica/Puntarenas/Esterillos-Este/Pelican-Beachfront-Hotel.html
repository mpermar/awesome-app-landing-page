Description

Located along the Central Pacific Coast of Costa Rica, the Pelican Beachfront Hotel has lush gardens, outdoor pool, restaurant on site and terraces with BBQ facilities.

Pets are allowed on request. The functional rooms are air conditioned and have ceiling fans.

The bathrooms come with shower and provide free toiletries. The restaurant serves international and local Costa Rican dishes.

Surfing lessons, yoga classes and a pool table are some of the activities that can be performed at the Pelican Beachfront Hotel.

Horse rides, mountain tours and hikes are also available. The Manuel Antonio National Park is 44 km from the Pelican, and Jacó Beach is 27 km away.

Tobias Bolaños International Airport can be reached in 1 hour and 45 minutes by car.
