Description

Guest rooms come with a fridge, stovetop, a coffee machine, a shower, free toiletries and a closet. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom, while certain units at the resort also feature a seating area.

Guests at Tambor Tropical Beach Resort can enjoy a continental or a à la carte breakfast.

Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Tambor, like cycling.

Jacó is 27 miles from Tambor Tropical Beach Resort. The nearest airport is Tambor Airport, 1.2 miles from the property.
