Description

Hotel Club del Mar is nestled between the rainforest and the Pacific Ocean, on the south end of Jaco Beach.

Surrounded by tropical gardens, the resort offers an oceanfront pool and a spa. Each air-conditioned room and apartment at Club del Mar has a balcony with rainforest and ocean views.

All accommodation includes cable TV, a coffee maker and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.
