Description

Located in the heart of the coastal town of Jaco, this comfortable hotel features free Wi-Fi, on-site private parking and free daily a la carte breakfast.

It is surrounded by gardens. The rooms at Hotel Mar de Luz are cleaned daily and come equipped with a cable TV, safety-deposit box and air conditioning.

They also boast a private bathroom with hot water, in addition to a kitchenette. Fast-food establishments and local restaurants, can be found within 300 metres of the property.

Adults can relax at Hotel Mar de Luz’s hot tub, while children enjoy a playground and a game room.

The tour desk can help arrange activities like surfing, hiking and guided tours. Jaco Beach is just 150 metres from the property, while Puntarenas Airport is a 1.
