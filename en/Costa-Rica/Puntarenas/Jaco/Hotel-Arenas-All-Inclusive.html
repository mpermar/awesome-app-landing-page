Description

The all inclusive Hotel Arenas is located in Punta Leona, Central Pacific, a small community committed to environmental protection.

The hotel is located in the middle of the dry tropical forest and just 400 metres from Manta Beach.

Offering 135 standard rooms, 16 of which under the 7600 Act and 18 superior. Each room here is air-conditioned and includes cable TV and a seating area.

Many rooms overlook the gardens an have a private balcony. Surrounded by tropical gardens you can find two restaurants, a pizzeria, ice cream parlor, swimming pool with snack bar, spa, gym, lounge bar , three meeting rooms for special events with capacities for 500, 250 and 50 people.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including horse riding, fishing and hiking.

Juan Santamaria International Airport in San José can be reached in 1. 5 hours by car.
