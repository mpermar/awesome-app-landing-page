Description

Featuring its own rainforest canopy tour and zip line, La Foresta Nature Resort has a beautiful setting outside Quepos.

It offers bungalows, suites and villas, as well as an outdoor swimming pool and attractive terrace.

All charming accommodation at La Foresta Nature Resort offers garden views and a terrace, and has Costa Rican-style décor with cooling tiled floors and wooden furnishings.

There is air conditioning, cable TV, coffee machine, and fridge. Bungalows and villas include a kitchenette.

You can enjoy creative cuisine prepared with local produce in La Foresta’s restaurant.

There is also a lounge and bar. The resort is hike through our Trials is something visitors can take advantage , hotel is located in Naranjos Natural Corridor which Manuel Antonio National Park also belongs so you can enjoy wildlife including monkeys, sloth, birds and more.

The Foresta arranges various activities, such as hiking in the rainforest, horse riding, cycling, surfing and kayaking.

Free shuttle from/to Manuel Antonio Beach, please ask for the schedule at the front desk.

Manuel Antonio National Park and the beaches at Playa Espadilla can be reached in 15 minutes by car.
