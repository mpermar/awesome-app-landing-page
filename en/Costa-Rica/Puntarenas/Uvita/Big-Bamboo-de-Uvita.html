Description

Just 2 km from Marino Ballena National Park beach and reef, hotel Big Bamboo de Uvita offers free Wi-Fi connection in all its rooms and has a full restaurant and bar.

The front desk operates 24 hours a day and public parking is available on site. The rooms are air conditioned and feature views of the garden.

They also have a wardrobe and ironing facilities. Free toiletries are available in the private bathroom.

The Big Bamboo Restaurant serves seafood meals and typical Costarican dishes. It operates during breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Guest may also order food to take out. The Marino Ballena restaurant is 800 metres away.

Some other services that Big Bamboo offers are babysitting, laundry and ironing. The front desk staff may help with information of the attractions that are worth visiting.

Some tours that the hotel organize are headed towards the Nauyaca Waterfalls and, when season, whalewatching.

Uvita town is 3 hours and 30 minutes’ drive from San José city. Juan Santamaría International Airport is 180 km away.
