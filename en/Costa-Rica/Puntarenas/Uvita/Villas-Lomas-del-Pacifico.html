Description

Located in Uvita, Villas Lomas del Pacífico features a garden, outdoor pool, and year-round outdoor pool.

Free WiFi is available. Bike hire and car hire are available at the property and the area is popular for snorkelling.

The area is popular for cycling and fishing. Parque Nacional Marino Ballena is 800 metres away, where guests can find waterfalls and parks.
