Description

Offering a restaurant and Bar with pool table , Tucan Hotel is located in Uvita, a 3-minute drive from Marino Ballena National Park.

It features on-site yoga classes and gardens with hammocks. Four types of rooms are available: 4-beds dorms, rustic cabins, tree house, and private room with air conditioning, bathroom and hot shower.

Tucan Hotel you will a find a two fruit gardens. Restaurants, shops and bars are available within a 3-minute walk.

The property has a tour desk that can arrange outings to local sights, with the popular Uvita waterfall and swimming spot just 1 km away.
