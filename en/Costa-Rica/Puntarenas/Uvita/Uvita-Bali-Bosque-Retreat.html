Description

Guest rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area. The rooms are equipped with a coffee machine and a private bathroom with a shower, while selected rooms come with a kitchen equipped with a dishwasher. The rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

Uvita Bali Bosque Retreat has a terrace. The area is popular for cycling and hiking, and car rental is available at the accommodation.

Playa Hermosa is 12 miles from Uvita Bali Bosque Retreat, while Santa Elena is 14 miles away. The nearest airport is Palmar Sur Airport, 24 miles from the property.
