Description

Set in lush gardens, GoldenEye features private beaches, hidden coves and a saltwater lagoon.

This tropical property was Ian Fleming’s Jamaican retreat, and all 14 James Bond novels were written here.

At GoldenEye you’ll find 3 outdoor pools, and full spa. You can choose from a range of villas, beach huts and cottages, including beachfront and lagoon-front properties.

All the villas and cottages come with kitchen facilities. You can also eat out in GoldenEye’s beachfront Bizot Bar bistro or enjoy fine dining in The Gazebo.

GoldenEye is located a 20-minute drive east of Ocho Rios. It is a 90-minute drive from Montego Bay International Airport and an 8-minute drive from Ian Fleming Private Airport.
