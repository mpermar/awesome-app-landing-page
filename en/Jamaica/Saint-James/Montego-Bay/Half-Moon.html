Description

This 400-acre resort has 2 miles of private white sand beach on the Caribbean Sea.

It features 3 restaurants and 5 bars. Half Moon Resort includes an 18-hole golf course and a 68,000-square foot spa available with an extra cost.

Sports fans will enjoy the basketball, volleyball, 4 squash courts and 11 tennis courts.

An Equestrian center and fitness center are also on site. For guest safety and comfort, a 24-hour medical center is located near to the Half Moon Resort.

Half Moon Resort has 3 restaurants with varying cuisine, including Italian and Jamaican.

The Seagrape Terrace offers alfresco dining. The Hibiscus Swim-up Pool Bar makes it possible to enjoy a drink without leaving the pool.

Rooms, suites, or villas at Half Moon all have minibars and safety deposit boxes and free WiFi.

Montego Bay and its nightlife and stores are a 15 minutes' walk from Half Moon Resort.
