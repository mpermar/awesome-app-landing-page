Description

The all-inclusive Holiday Inn Resort Montego Bay All Inclusive offers a private beach, 2 outdoor pools, a spa and casino.

Each air-conditioned room has a balcony with views of the sea or tropical gardens.

Rooms at the Holiday Inn Resort Montego Bay All Inclusive have tropical décor and include a coffee maker and a seating area.

The private bathrooms include toiletries and a hairdryer. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout.

You can enjoy free meals and drinks in the resort’s 4 bars and 4 restaurants and grills.

A wide variety of Caribbean and international cuisine is available. At an extra cost, you can also enjoy Japanese teppanyaki cuisine in the stylish Kenko restaurant.

The resort offers on-site facilities for tennis, mini-golf and snorkelling. Staff can also help arrange activities, excursions and island tours.

The centre of Montego Bay is 10 km from the resort, while Sangster International Airport is just 8 km away.
