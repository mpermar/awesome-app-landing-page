Description

Set in Montego Bay in the Saint James Region, 47 km from Runaway Bay, Iberostar Rose Hall Beach features a year-round outdoor pool, hot tub, a private beach area and a spa and wellness centre.

Car hire is available at the property. The rooms come with a flat-screen TV, minibar and coffee maker.

All rooms are fitted with iPod dock and a private bathroom and a shower. The on-site restaurant offers buffet, à la carte menu and also special diet menus upon request.

Breakfast in room is also available. Children Policy From 0 - 2 years old : Infants free of charge From 3 to 12 year old : children paying children rate From 13 Years old: paying adult rate Request child rates information at hotel reservation department.

You will find a 24-hour front desk, table tennis and tennis court at the resort. Guests can request massage service and enjoy sauna and hammam.

Other activities offered on site are nightclub, kid's club and entertainment staff.

The nearest airport is Montego Bay Airport Airport, 16 km from Iberostar Rose Hall Beach.
