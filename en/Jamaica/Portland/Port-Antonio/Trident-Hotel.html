Description

Most of Hotel Trident's stylish air-conditioned villas feature a private plunge pool and bathtub. All villas have a lounge with flat-screen cable TV and free Wi-Fi. A fridge and kettle are also provided.

Trident Hotel's restaurant serves à la carte breakfast and lunch, and you can enjoy dinner with live jazz music at Mike’s Supper Club. Local restaurants and bars can also be found in Port Antonio, a 7-minute drive away.

Water sports guides can accompany guests who want to snorkel, take a boat ride or go kayaking. You can go rafting on the Rio Grande River, a 30-minute drive from the hotel. Navy Island is 3.5 miles away.
