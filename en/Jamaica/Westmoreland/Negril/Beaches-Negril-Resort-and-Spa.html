Description

At the resort, rooms are equipped with a balcony. The private bathroom is equipped with a shower. At Beaches Negril Resort and Spa all rooms include air conditioning and a flat-screen TV.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental or a buffet breakfast.

Beaches Negril Resort and Spa offers 4-star accommodations with a fitness center. A range of activities are offered in the area, such as snorkeling and canoeing.

Montego Bay is 29 miles from the resort, while Alma is 9 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Sangster International Airport, 30 miles from Beaches Negril Resort and Spa.
