Description

Just 100 metres from Negril’s 7-Mile Beach, Rayon Hotel offers air-conditioned accommodation with private balconies and a swimming pool surrounded by tropical vegetation.

Negril town is 10 minutes’ drive away. Rooms have classic décor and wooden ceilings, and include a fan, wardrobe and TV.

All accommodation offers pool views, a fridge and a private bathroom. The apartments also have a fully equipped kitchen.

The on-site restaurant serves international and local dishes for breakfast, lunch and dinner, and there is also a poolside bar.

Guests at Rayon Hotel can find a variety of restaurants within 5 minutes’ walk. There is an on-site games room with table tennis and a pool table.

Bicycle rental and activities such as hiking are also available. The property is 10 minutes’ drive from West End, while Montego Bay and the Sangster International Airport are a 1-hour drive away.
