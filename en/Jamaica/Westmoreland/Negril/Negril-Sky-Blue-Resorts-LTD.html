Description

At the resort, the rooms come with a closet, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Negril Sky Blue Resorts LTD provides some accommodations with sea views, and every room has a coffee machine. Guest rooms are equipped with a seating area.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental or a à la carte breakfast. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the on-site restaurant, which specializes in American cuisine.

Guests can use the hot tub. You can play ping pong at Negril Sky Blue Resorts LTD, and bike rental and car rental are available.

Alma is 9 miles from the resort. The nearest airport is Sangster International Airport, 32 miles from Negril Sky Blue Resorts LTD.
