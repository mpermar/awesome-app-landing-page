Description

Set on Negril's 7-Mile Beach, Travellers Beach Resort features a gym and an outdoor swimming pool.

It offers rooms with free WiFi and balconies Set in a bright building, Travellers Beach Resort offers accommodation with simple décor.

All rooms have air conditioning and cable TV, except for the bungalows which are fan cooled.

Guests can enjoy a daily continental breakfast in the Palm Restaurant, which serves Jamaican and American dishes for dinner.

Cocktails are served on the beachfront terrace. Staff at the Beach Resort’s reception can arrange scuba diving, jet skiing and paragliding excursions.

Glass-bottom boat trips are also available. The resort is within 15 minutes’ drive of Orange Bay and West End.
