Description

The accommodations at Coco La Palm feature simple décor and are air-conditioned. Cable TV and a telephone line are at hand, plus a coffee maker with premium Jamaican coffee beans. The bathroom with shower is private.

Coco La Palm has an on-site restaurant located seaside that serves international-style dishes with pork, chicken, seafood, pasta and salads, plus drinks and cocktails. The property also has The Beach Bar and Grill with American-style food and drinks.

The hotel has a large hot tub, an adult pool where parties can be held, and a family pool surrounded by lush gardens.The property provides beach and pool side umbrellas and has a beach lounge.

Negril Airport is half a mile from Coco La Palm.
