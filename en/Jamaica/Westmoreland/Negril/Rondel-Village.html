Description

Rooms at Rondel Village have views of the grounds. They come equipped with a refrigerator and include cable TV. Wi-Fi access is also available.

The on-site Rondel Restaurant and Bar specializes in seafood and vegetarian dishes. Room service is available. The resort also has guest laundry, a tour desk, and a currency exchange.

Experiences

03/30/2018

A great choice for budget-conscious families or couples without feeling like you're on a budget.

"2 adjacent rooms meant we had the whole top floor of a 2-story building to ourselves. Rooms are simple and comfortable, each with private covered balconies and a mini fridge. Staff was polite and friendly. Service at the restaurant and bar were good; not as slow as many of the other nearby establishments. Location is nearby a great selection of restaurants you can walk to, and has a lovely beach, raked to remove tree litter daily. Availability of some floaty toys was a plus. Live reggae music played next door at the Boat Bar in the afternoon 2 of the 4 days we were there. It was great that we could enjoy it at just the right volume with a Red Stripe while lounging on the beach at Rondel. We could hear late-night music farther down the beach, but it was never enough to keep us light-sleepers awake. The best part is that I left my shoes in the room all week - a short walk on a paved path between buildings (< 1 minute walk) and then beach the rest of the way since we stayed on the beach side of the property. We did not visit the other side of the property across the street, but were very happy that we did not need to cross the street to get to the beach."
