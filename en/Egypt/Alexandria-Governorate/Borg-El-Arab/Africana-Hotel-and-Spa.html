Description

Africana Hotel is located in Borg Al Arab and features 3 outdoor pools and a spa.

It offers rooms with views of the pool, the lake and the garden. It includes several bars and a restaurant.

Each air-conditioned unit at Africana features a balcony and has a flat-screen with cable TV.

The suites have a living room whilst the rooms have a seating area. Free wired internet is available in the rooms and free Wi-Fi is provided in public areas.

A complementary buffet breakfast is served every day at the hotel. Guests can enjoy the open buffet restaurant, offering each day a different kind of cuisine, like Italian, Oriental or Chinese.

The 3 bars also offer light snacks and drinks. Guests can relax at the sauna or have a massage.

The hotel also offers a programme of night shows. For guests wanting to practice sports, there is a fitness centre, a tennis court and a football field.

Free private parking is also available at the Africana. Borg El Arab International Airport is a 5-minute drive away from the hotel.

Alexandria’s city centre is 25 minutes by car. Borg El Arab International Stadium is 10 km away.
