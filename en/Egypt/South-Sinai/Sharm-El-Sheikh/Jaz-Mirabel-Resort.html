Description

Jaz Mirabel Beach offers a peaceful location in Nabq Bay with regular shuttle buses to Naama Bay.

It offers 6 swimming pools, a water park and impressive spa facilities. The air-conditioned rooms are decorated with neutral colours and filled with natural light.

Each opens onto a balcony with a view of the Gulf of Aqaba or the pool. Guests can feel light and beautiful with the spa, which includes offers reviving and reinvigorating treatments, massage, sand relaxation programs and also Beauty services at an extra charge.

Alternatively, you can enjoy the water sport facilities, windsurfing, diving and Snorkeling at an extra charge.

You can also get babysitting service at an extra charge to enjoy your time. Guests can make use of Jaz Mirabel's tennis court.

The hotel also has a shopping center on site. The restaurants serve la carte and buffet-style cuisine with a local and international flavour.

Snacks, drinks and ice cream are offered at the beach bars. There is also a poolside bar.

Shurouk Lobby Bar, part of the all inclusive package, is an adult only bar serving a wide variety of drinks.

Free private parking facilities are available by reservation. Sharm el-Sheikh International is a 10-minute drive from the hotel.
