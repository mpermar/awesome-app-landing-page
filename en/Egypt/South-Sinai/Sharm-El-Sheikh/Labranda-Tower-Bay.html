Description

Overlooking Labranda Tower Bay in Sharm, this 4-star hotel offers panoramic views of the turquoise Red Sea and the Sinai Mountains.

Facilities include a 700-metre beach, an outdoor pool, and a gym. The architectural style of Labranda Tower Bay resembles a traditional Egyptian village.

All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, cable TV, and a fridge. Some feature a balcony and a bathtub.

Guests can enjoy traditional Egyptian, as well as international dishes at the restaurant.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning. Labranda Tower Bay offers opportunities to go snorkelling and diving at Labranda Tower Bay.

The hotel also has a tennis court and a spa with hot tub, Turkish bath and massage facilities.

Children can play in an equipped playground. The resort is just a 10-minute drive from Naama Bay and a 15-minute drive from central Sharm el-Sheikh.
