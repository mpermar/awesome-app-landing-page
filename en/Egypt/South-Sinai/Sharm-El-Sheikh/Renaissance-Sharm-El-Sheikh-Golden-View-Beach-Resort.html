Description

Renaissance Sharm El Sheikh is a luxury resort with 350 meters beachfront. It offers the chance of discovering the Red Sea underwater world.

Free WiFi service is available in all public areas. The resort comprises 384 smoke-free guest rooms and suites fully equipped with means of comfort like 42” flat-screen TV with comprehensive IPTV system, tea & coffee making facilities, minibar for a fee, iron & iron board, desk and chair, high speed internet for a fee.

Guests can request interconnecting rooms and suites. Resort’s Outlets: Coast main buffet restaurant, Calamari Seafood Market Restaurant, Italian restaurant Ristorante La Casa Pizza & Pasta and 4 bars.

The View Beach Bar offers dancing floor & DJ. Entertainment program by the night.

The resort presents many facilities & activities: 4 swimming pools (1 heated in winter).

Onsite diving center, Gym room, Spa, Kids playground & kids club. Stretch out exercises by the beach.

MICE facilities. An Airport shuttle bus is available for an additional fee. Sharm El Sheikh International Airport is a 22-minute drive from the property.
