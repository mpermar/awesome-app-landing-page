Description

This 5-star all-inclusive resort is situated in the centre of Soho Square, it offers access to the private sands of White Knight Beach.

Sierra Sharm El Sheikh features 7 swimming pools and water sports centre providing gear for parasailing, windsurfing and water-skiing.

There is also a gym and tennis courts. Rooms come with balconies or patios overlooking the garden or pool.

Each has satellite TV and a minibar. Bathrooms include make-up mirrors and hairdryers.

Sierra Sharm El Sheikh’s spa offers a broad variety of body treatments, reflexology and massages.

Guests can relax in the steam bath, sauna or hot tub, and there is a rooftop rest area with parasols.

Guests can enjoy an all-inclusive open buffet at Mountain View. Akuna Matata offers a variety of Mexican, Moroccan, Chinese, Egyptian and Seafood cuisines.
