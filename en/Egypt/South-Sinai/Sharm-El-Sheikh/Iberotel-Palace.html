Description

Iberotel Palace is a white-washed complex featuring 2 freshwater outdoor pools and rooms with balconies overlooking Sharm El Maya Bay.

The property offers a shuttle boat service to a coral beach. All rooms feature a private furnished balcony or terrace with views of the pool, sea side or the garden.

Rooms with inter-connecting doors and accessible rooms designed for the guests are available upon request.

International cuisine and night entertainment are provided at the 8 restaurants and bars.

Guests can choose between seafood, Italian specialities and meat dishes, and sip an exotic cocktail at the poolside bar.

Feel light and beautiful with our spa, which includes offers reviving and reinvigorating treatments, massage, relaxation programs, sauna, steam and also beauty services at an extra charge.

For some outdoor activity, there are 2 tennis courts and water sports facilities for an added charge.

Located 10 km from Naama Bay and 20 km from Sharm el-Sheikh International Airport, Iberotel Palace offers Airport transfer service for an additional cost and free private parking on site.
