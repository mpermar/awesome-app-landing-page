Description

Sensatori Sharm El Sheikh Resort is located in Sharm El Sheikh. It offers an outdoor and indoor pool, a fitness centre and a private beach.

Free WiFi access is available in public areas. Each room will provide you with a flat-screen TV and air conditioning.

Featuring a spa bath, the private bathroom also comes with a shower and a bath. You can enjoy sea and pool views from the room.

Extras include a minibar, an iPod dock and a seating area. At Sensatori Sharm El Sheikh Resort you will find a restaurant and a tennis court.

Other facilities offered at the property include entertainment staff, luggage storage and a kids' club.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including diving and snorkelling.
