Description

Located in the Om El Seid cliff area in the centre of Sharm El Sheikh, this hotel features a private beach, 2 outdoor pools with sun-lounger terrace.

Naama Bay is a 10-minute drive and Il Mercato Mall is a short walk from the hotel.

Rooms at Sharm Reef Hotel include a balcony or terrace with views of the garden or pool and air conditioning.

Some rooms include a kitchen with a dining area. In the morning, the hotel serves a buffet breakfast.

Local and international meals are served at Hotel Sharm Reef’s restaurant. The hotel’s bar provides drinks and snacks.

Nearby leisure activities include diving and snorkelling. Meeting facilities are available upon request.

The Sharm Reef Hotel offers an airport shuttle to Sharm El Sheikh International Airport, 15 km away.

The hotel also offers 24-hour front desk service and free private parking is possible on site.
