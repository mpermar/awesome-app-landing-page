Description

All rooms in the resort are equipped with a electric tea pot. With a private bathroom, rooms at TUI Magic Life Sharm El Sheikh also feature free WiFi, while some rooms include a sea view. All guest rooms at the accommodation are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning at the property.

The property has a sauna and a fitness center. You can play tennis, ping pong, and darts at TUI Magic Life Sharm El Sheikh.

Nabq Bay is 1.8 miles from the resort, while Ghibli Raceway is 10 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Sharm el-Sheikh International Airport, 5 miles from TUI Magic Life Sharm El Sheikh.
