Description

Situated in Sharm El-Sheikh, this hotel is located within Naama Bay. It has a private beach, 5 swimming pools, and offers air-conditioned rooms with satellite TV.

Rooms at the Novotel Sharm El-Sheikh have tiled floors and elegant furnishings. They come equipped with a minibar, as well as tea and coffee making facilities.

Some rooms feature balconies with panoramic views. The Novotel has 3 restaurants that serve a variety of regional and international cuisine.

Al Dente offers Italian fare and views of the Red Sea. The Bedu Bar provides live entertainment.

Guests can enjoy a relaxing massage, or visit the fitness centre. The hotel also has a games room with table tennis, and a children’s playground.

Novotel Sharm El-Sheikh is less than 10 km from Sharm el-Sheikh International Airport.
