Description

Jaz Fanara Resort is located in a prime position on the cliff of Ras Om El Sid with a breathtaking view of the Red Sea and the national park of Ras Mohamed and only minutes away from Naama bay.

Rooms at Jaz Fanara are air-conditioned and equipped with a satellite TV. Some offer spacious living room areas and sea views from private balconies.

This 4 star hotel is especially suitable for couples although there are well appointed family rooms available.

Guests can choose among indoor and outdoor dining possibilities at El Medina and Temple Pool Restaurant, serving traditional Arabic cuisine.

Jaz Fanara also offers a beach bar and the lively La Loca disco. Guests can feel light and beautiful with the spa, which includes offers reviving and reinvigorating treatments, massage, sand relaxation programs and beauty services at an extra charge.

Alternatively, you can enjoy playing tennis, snorkeling free of charge or diving with an added charge.

Guests can get babysitting service at an extra charge to enjoy their time. The Jaz Fanara Resort is only 2 km from Sharm city centre and 22 km from Sharm El Sheikh International Airport.
