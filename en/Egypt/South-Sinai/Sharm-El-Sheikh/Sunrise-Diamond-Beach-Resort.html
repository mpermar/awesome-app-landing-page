Description

Situated on Om El Seid Plateau, Sunrise Diamond Beach Resort has a private beach.

It features 4 restaurants, 3 swimming pools, including an Aqua park Pool and a diving school.

It offers 2 à-la-carte restaurants and free WiFi. Guests can choose between wide varieties of rooms.

A special benefit for families is the Family Room concept, which offers separate rooms for parents and children.

All guest rooms at Sunrise Sunrise Diamond Beach Resort are equipped with satellite TV, an en suite bathroom and a mini-refrigerator.

Every room has a balcony, providing views of the Red Sea or pool area. Guests can enjoy a variety of dining venues, including the Oriental Restaurant located on the beach, and Italian restaurant and the stylish Kandahar Indian restaurant.

There are also 6 bars, providing a choice of relaxing spots in which to enjoy a drink.

The resort offers a spa and fitness center and an international diving center, where you can explore the colorful reefs and exotic fish of the Red Sea.

Sunrise Diamond Beach Resort is 20 km from Sharm el Sheikh International Airport.
