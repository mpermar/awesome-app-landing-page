Description

Situated on a beach overlooking Temple Reef, this all-inclusive resort has spacious rooms with a balcony.

It features outdoor pools, a wellness centre, and panoramic views of Sinai Mountains and Tiran Island.

This 5-star hotel includes a diving pool, 3 heated pools, and 2 pools for children.

There is an Aqua Park with 14 pools. Snorkelling and diving is possible on the beach.

It has 9 restaurants and 15 bars. The air-conditioned rooms at Reef Oasis are contemporary decorated with dark wooden furnishing.

Each room comes with a TV and a minibar. Some rooms have a flat-screen TV and a separate seating area.

The hotel’s restaurant serves local dishes, while the bar offers refreshing drinks.

The wellness centre provides a range of beauty treatments and massages. Guests can relax in the hot tub or stroll through the lush gardens.

Sharm el-Sheikh International Airport is 16 km away, and the hotel offers free public parking on site.
