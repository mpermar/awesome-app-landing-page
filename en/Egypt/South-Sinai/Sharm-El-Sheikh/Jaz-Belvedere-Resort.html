Description

Located right on Montaza Beach, this 5 star resort combines an impressive range of features and facilities.

It features a private beach, 2 pools, a kids' pool and club. Jaz Belvedere is 6 KM away from SOHO Square and 13 KM away from the International Congress Center.

Styled with touches of traditional Arabian-Moroccan design, the hotel is one of the finest in Sharm El Sheikh.

At Belvedere, you can enjoy something very special: warm, individual service, high quality entertainment programs, and unforgettable opportunities for bathing and diving at one of the most beautiful underwater spots in the world.

An array of activities can be enjoyed at the resort that includes beach tennis,volleyball court and a multi-purpose court.

Guests can enjoy water sport facilities, windsurfing, diving, snorkeling, and canoeing with an added charge.

Feel light and beautiful with our spa, which includes offers reviving and reinvigorating treatments, foot-bath, massages, relaxation programs, and beauty services with an added charge.

Jaz Belvedere Resort is just 10 km from Sharm El Sheikh International Airport and 20 km from Sharm El Sheikh town centre.
