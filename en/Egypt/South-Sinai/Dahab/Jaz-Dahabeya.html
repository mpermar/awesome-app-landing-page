Description

This luxurious, 4-star hotel offers stunning views of the Red Sea and the Sinai Mountains.

It features a lagoon-style pool, massage treatments and a sauna. Set in a picturesque garden with palm trees, Jaz Dahabeya offers elegant rooms with satellite TV and a balcony.

Some overlook the pool area and the garden. For those who like to keep fit, Jaz Dahabeya features a well-equipped gym.

Water-based activities available include windsurfing, diving and snorkeling at an extra charge.

Feel light and beautiful with the spa, which includes reviving and reinvigorating treatments, massage, relaxation programs, sauna and beauty services at an extra charge.

Guest can relax, while children are playing at Dahabeya children’s playground. Le Palmier Restaurant serves Mediterranean and international cuisine.

Regional specialities can be enjoyed on the terrace at Sea Pearl Restaurant. The centre of Dahab is 3 km away.

Abu Gallom National Park is a 10-minute drive away. Guests can benefit from the free private parking offered at the hotel.
