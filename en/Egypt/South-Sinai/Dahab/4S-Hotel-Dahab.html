Description

4S Hotel Dahab is a 10-minute walk from the centre of Dahab and is only 600 metres from the Sinai Peninsula promenade.

It has an outdoor pool with sun-lounger terrace and offers air-conditioned accommodations.

Suites and studios at 4S Hotel Dahab include modern furnishings and a balcony that overlooks the mountains, the sea or the swimming pool.

They come equipped with free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, and a fully equipped kitchenette.

In the morning the hotel serves a buffet breakfast on the sun terrace. There is also a bar, and a rooftop restaurant that features traditional cuisine and Bedouin seating.

The rooftop cafe serves cocktails and refreshing tea and coffee. Guests can enjoy a relaxing massage, or rent a bicycle and explore the countryside.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk provide luggage storage and can arrange safari and snorkelling trips.

4S Hotel Dahab is 150 km from the St. Katherine Nature Reserve and a 6-minute drive from the beach and Blue Hole.
