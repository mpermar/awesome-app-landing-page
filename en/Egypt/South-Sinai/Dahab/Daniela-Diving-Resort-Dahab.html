Description

All rooms boast a traditional interior which includes locally made tapestries and rugs. Each air-conditioned room has a private balcony equipped with cozy rattan furniture.

Daniela Diving Resort offers guided diving excursions catering to beginner and experienced divers. Guests can also join in snorkeling activities or take part in camel rides or jeep safaris offered by the hotel.

Modern European and Arabic cuisine are served at the hotel’s restaurant. Regional specialties such as Arabic tea and shisha pipes are available at the bar.

Daniela Resort is located 15 minutes’ walk from Dahab’s popular snorkeling and diving sites. It is 2 miles from Dahab’s Bedouin village, a cultural attraction featuring traditional lifestyles.
