Description

The air-conditioned accommodations at Mousa Coast Resort have a flat-screen satellite TV and a balcony with a view of the sea or the garden. A hairdryer and free toiletries are included in the private bathroom.

Mangiamo Restaurant serves a buffet and a menu with international, Middle Eastern and local cuisines. Other dining outlets include Red Fish Restaurant which includes fresh seafood, and La Pergola serves a variety of grilled meats.

You can enjoy pool tables, tennis table or volleyball for a fee. Horseback riding activities and training can be arranged upon request. The tour desk provides local excursions. Mousa Coast is 93 miles from Cairo.
