Description

Located in New Administrative Capital, 59 km from City Stars, ALMASA Royal Palace-New Capital features an artificial beach area and free bikes.

This 5-star hotel offers free WiFi. The hotel has a children's playground and a spa centre.

All guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a kettle. ALMASA Royal Palace offers certain units that include lake and pool views, and every room has a private bathroom and a desk.

The units at the accommodation feature air conditioning and a wardrobe. Dining is available at one of the hotel's 4 restaurants and guests can grab coffee at the coffee shop/cafes.

Various dining options including Italian, Middle Eastern and International cuisine are available at the in-house restaurant.

At ALMASA Royal Palace - New Capital guests are welcome to take advantage of a hot tub, a sauna and a fitness room.

Guests at the hotel will be able to enjoy activities like cycling. Staff at reception can assist around the clock with guidance on the area.

Cairo Intl Conference Centre is 66 km from ALMASA Royal Palace. The nearest airport is Cairo International, 50 km from the accommodation, and the property offers a free airport pickup service.
