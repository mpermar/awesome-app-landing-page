Description

Located in the heart of Heliopolis, the hotel has 132 guest rooms and suites, an outdoor pool and barbecue facilities.

The hotel also has a children's playground and sun terrace. Guests can enjoy meals in various restaurants and private parking is available on site.

All rooms have a flat-screen TV and some units have views of the pool or garden. Rooms have a private bathroom with a bathtub.

The Hotel Gare features free Wi-Fi throughout the residence. The residence offers a 24-hour front desk.

Saudi German Hospital, 10 Kilometer from The Guard Hotel. The mosque is located inside the hotel while the Roxy Cinema is 5 km from the residence.

Cairo International Airport is the nearest airport to the Hotel Gare, 4. 1 km away.
