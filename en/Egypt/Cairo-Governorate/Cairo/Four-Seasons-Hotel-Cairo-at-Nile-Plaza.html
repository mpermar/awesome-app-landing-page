Description

Featuring panoramic views of the Nile, this 5-star hotel offers air-conditioned rooms with a flat-screen satellite TV and a marble bathroom.

It has luxury spa facilities, and outdoor landscaped pool area. The well-appointed rooms at Four Seasons overlook Cairo.

A bathtub, separate glass-enclosed shower, and 2 sinks are in each bathroom. Some rooms have a sitting area with sofas and others have a private terrace where meals can be enjoyed.

The Nile Plaza’s spa includes a gym, salon, and an indoor pool. Guests can relax in the hot tub or unwind with rejuvenating massage.

Car rental can be arranged by the front desk. They also provide a laundry service, and 24-hour room service is available.

A freshly prepared, rich breakfast buffet is served every morning. Guests can sample traditional meals from the region at Four Seasons Hotel Cairo at Nile Plaza’s restaurant.

The middle of the city is a 10-minute drive from the hotel. Cairo International Airport is 20 km from Four Seasons Hotel Cairo at Nile Plaza.
