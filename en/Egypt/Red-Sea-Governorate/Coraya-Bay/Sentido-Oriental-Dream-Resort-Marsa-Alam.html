Description

The 5-star resort offers a luxury spa, a large outdoor pool and a private beach in Marsa Alam, by the Red Sea.

The air-conditioned rooms open onto a balcony with a garden or sea view. This property offers 2 à-la-carte restaurants where guests can choose from.

Guests can relax with a Thai massage treatment or in the sauna at the spa centre.

Kids can have fun at the playground or at the children’s club, Nemo. There is also a spa bath and a terrace with sun loungers.

Sentido Oriental Dream Resort provides rooms with satellite TV and a minibar. Each is air conditioned and spacious.

Complimentary WiFi is available in the terrace area. The resort offers an Italian, à la carte restaurant and a seafood restaurant.

Guests can enjoy refreshing drinks at the bar or a water pipe at the Sultana Café.

The Sentido Resort is situated 40 km from RMF - Marsa Alam International Airport.
