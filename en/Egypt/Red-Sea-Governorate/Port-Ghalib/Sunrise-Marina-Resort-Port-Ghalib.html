Description

The five stars Arabian style modern designed SUNRISE Marina Resort is located in Port Ghalib City and provide direct access to the Marina promenade and its variety of outlets, coffee shops & restaurants.

For a perfect Holiday experience, the hotel sandy beach prime location at the entrance of Port Ghalib Marina where guests can discover the red sea underwater world.

Guests can enjoy free WiFi throughout the property. Certain units feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

Some units have views of the pool or garden. Rooms have a private bathroom. SUNRISE Marina Resort features free WiFi.

A flat-screen TV is provided. The resort offers a diversity of facilities & activities for all ages, 2 swimming pools (one is heated), 4 slides heated Aqua park & heated mini kids aqua, animation supervised signature Kids club (Kids Planet), kids games center, diving center, tennis court, Gym, specially designed SPA Health Center, magnificent club (Red Club) and state of the art (Amuse Bowling center), all accompanied with soft animation concept, daily evening entertainment and weekly scheduled live singing program.

SUNRISE Marina Resort also features a carefully selected blend of food and beverage outlets including two main restaurant venues of different styles (Heliodoro main restaurant), two specialty restaurants offering the Egyptian flavour in (Gamila Oriental Cuisine) and the Mediterranean international tastes in (Elia) together with resort spread bars and snacks outlets introducing (Sunray) Bar located by the hotel lobby, (Chill Out) food court by the Aqua park, (Chiringuito) Beach bar and the resort pool bars (Splash) & (Aqua).
