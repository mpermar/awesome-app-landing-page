Description

Mövenpick Resort’s rooms feature large windows and the bedrooms open onto a private balcony equipped with patio furniture. Each one has satellite TV, sitting area and well-stocked minibar.

Palavrion Restaurant features a variety of Egyptian delicacies and also has a live cooking presentation near the buffet table. Bua Khao restaurant serves Thai delicacies in a themed environment.

At Mövenpick Resort & Spa El Gouna, guests can work out in well-equipped gym or enjoy a game of tennis in the sports court. There are diving and snorkeling activities in the surrounding area.

Mövenpick Resort & Spa El Gouna is a 4-hour drive from Luxor. Free private parking is available on site.

El Gouna is a great choice for travelers interested in water sports, relaxation and beaches.
