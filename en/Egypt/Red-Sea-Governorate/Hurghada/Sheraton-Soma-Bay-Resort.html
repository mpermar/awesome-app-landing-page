Description

This hotel is situated on a beach in Soma Bay, 30 km from Hurghada. It features a large outdoor pool, 18-hole golf course designed by Gary Player, and rooms with balconies and sea views.

Rooms at the Sheraton Soma Bay Resort have air conditioning and modern oak furnishings.

They come equipped with satellite TV and a minibar. Most rooms overlook the Red Sea Mountains.

Guests can dine outdoors on the sprawling terrace of the International Restaurant, overlooking the sea or try the cozy Italian Restaurant and watch the chefs prepare authentic Italian fare.

Copper Crescent is a popular lunch spot with cool breezes and exotic aromas in the open air.

Sheraton guests can relax on a sun-lounger, or play a game of beach volleyball. Guests can pamper themselves at Les Thermes Marina des Cascades, a comprehensive facility for thalassotherapy.

Nearby leisure activities include snorkeling and scuba diving. The Hurghada International Airport is 20 km from the Sheraton Soma Bay Resort.

An airport transfer shuttle is available upon request. Private on-site parking is available.
