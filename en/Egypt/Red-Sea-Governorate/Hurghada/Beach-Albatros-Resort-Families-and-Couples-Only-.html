Description

Located directly on the beach, this 4-star resort features an Aqua Park with 3 outdoor swimming pools and spacious rooms with a balcony.

It includes a spa and a diving center. Overlooking the Red Sea or the landscaped gardens, Beach Albatros air-conditioned rooms have a satellite TV and a minibar.

Each comes with a private bathroom with toiletries and a hairdryer. Beach Resort’s main restaurant offers Egyptian and international buffet specialities.

It also has a themed restaurant and a snack bar. Drinks are available at the resort's 5 in-house bars.

Snorkeling and kayaking are possible at the beach. It also has a volleyball court.

Beach Albatros offers a tennis court and a mini-golf course. Guests can play billiards or darts in the games room.

In the evening, guests can enjoy karaoke or dance in the nightclub. Hurghada Airport is only 9 km from the hotel, and the city centre is a 20 minutes' drive away.
