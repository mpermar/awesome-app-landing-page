Description

Located at the 4 km long Makadi Bay, the SUNRISE Royal Makadi Aqua Resort has an oriental flair.

The unique architecture and lush gardens are enriched with spectacular pool areas, where the guests can enjoyaqua park facilities.

This 5-star resort in Makadi Bay offers direct access to the beach and 2 outdoor pool areas.

It houses a spa providing an extensive range of massages and beauty treatments. This SUNRISE Royal Makadi Aqua Resort offers 6 à-la-carte restaurants.

The dining options include Italian, Mexican, Mediterranean, Greek and Asian cuisine.

24-hour room service is also available. The resort offers 7 different theme bars, such as a typical English pub, two pool bars and the Oriental-style Sultana Café.

All accommodations at SUNRISE Royal Makadi Aqua Resort come with a minibar and a terrace or balcony.

Some units are located right on the beach and feature outdoor seating areas with Red Sea views.

The extensive pool area features 5 swimming pools, including 2 heated ones for winter months and a children’s pool with multiple slides.

Guests can exercise on the 6 km jogging track or at the well-equipped fitness center.

The wonderful views of the Red Sea which can be enjoyed are an invitation to rest and relaxation.

This region is as well known for some of the best snorkel and diving areas. The resort is just 30-minute drive from Hurghada International Airport.
