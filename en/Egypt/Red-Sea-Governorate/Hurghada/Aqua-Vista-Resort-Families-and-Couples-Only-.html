Description

This modern 4-star all-inclusive Hurghada resort is just 250 metres from the Red Sea.

It offers a lagoon-style outdoor pool, 5 buffet restaurants and a disco. All rooms have a balcony or terrace.

Guests can enjoy free access to Aqua park for both Albatros Garden and children Aqua Park at Sea World Resort.

Aqua Vista Resort has 4 pools, including a wave pool. Guests have free use of the sun loungers which surround the pool and beach areas.

Spa facilities include a sauna, Moroccan hamman, beauty salon and massage rooms. All of Aqua Vista Resort’s rooms are air-conditioned and have satellite TV.

Twenty-four hour room service is available. Four buffet restaurants are available and serve a variety of international cuisine including Thai, Italian and seafood specialities.

Guests can choose from 5 bars, including a poolside bar. Hurghada International Airport is a 7-km drive from the Aqua Vista Resort.

Downtown Hurghada is 15 km away. Guests can also enjoy beach access at the nearby Albatros Resort by shuttle bus.
