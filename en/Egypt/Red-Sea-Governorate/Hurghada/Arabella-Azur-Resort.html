Description

Featuring a lagoon-style pool, a private beach area and a spa centre, this property is a 4-star luxury resort.

The bright, spacious rooms open onto a balcony overlooking Hurghada or the Red Sea.

A flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a minibar are featured in all air- conditioned rooms at the Arabella Azur.

The private bathroom offers shampoos, soaps and a hairdryer. At the spa, guests can enjoy a relaxing massage treatment or unwind in the sauna.

Diving and snorkelling can be organised by staff at the 24-hour front desk. For children, Arabella Azur Resort offers a playground.

The Grill Restaurant offers a buffet of international and Asian cuisines. Guests can also enjoy meals on the terrace.
