Description

Jaz Almaza Bay resort is located In Almaza Bay. It features a white sandy beach, 9 swimming pools and 6 swimming pool for the kids, 4 à-la-carte restaurants and 15 bars.

All rooms at Jaz Almaza Bay come with a balcony or terrace overlooking the Mediterranean Sea.

Each room comes with a flat-screen TV, a minibar, a wardrobe and a telephone. Some rooms include a spacious seating area and connected rooms.

Guests can enjoy a broad assortment of international dining options to choose from including 3 fine dining a la carte restaurants.

Morgana serves Mediterranean fair in the open air. Corallo offers seafood à la carte dining options while overlooking the Mediterranean.

Sofra features authentic Lebanese favorites. The Jaz Almaza Bay features spa with range of relaxation options such as: sauna, steam room and Jacuzzi.

There is also a fully equipped gym, and beauty salon with treatment rooms against charge.

The property offers beach lodges where guests can enjoy a quiet time with food and beverages services by the private butlers.

Reservation is needed and extra charges apply. A range of sport activities are available on site such as table tennis, billiard, beach volley ball, water ball, aqua gym.

Other activities include kite surfing, water pedal and kayak are against charge. Children can enjoy daily activities at Kids club.
