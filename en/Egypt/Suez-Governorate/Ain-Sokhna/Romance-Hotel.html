Description

The spacious rooms are decorated with muted colors. Each room has a sofa bed and a satellite TV. A minibar and tea/coffee-making facilities are provided. A hairdryer is available in the bathroom.

Guests can play a game of volleyball at the beach or ping-pong in the game room. The Romance Hotel also has a modern gym. There is a playground and a pool for younger guests.

A limo transportation service to Zafarana City and Ain Sokhna Port can be arranged. Cairo Airport is 112 miles away.
