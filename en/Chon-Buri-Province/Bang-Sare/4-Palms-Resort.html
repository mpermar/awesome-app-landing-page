Description

At the resort, all rooms are equipped with a balcony. Rooms have a TV, and some rooms include a mountain view. All guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

The daily breakfast offers Full English/Irish and Asian options.

4 Palms Resort offers a hot tub. Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.

U-Tapao Rayong-Pattaya International Airport is 9 miles from the property.
