Description

The country-style rooms at Hotel Schwarz Alm are equipped with a cable TV and a minibar, and all rooms have a private balcony. Luxury toiletries, bathrobes and slippers are provided.

Elegant relaxation rooms, a well-equipped gym and several saunas and steam baths are offered at the Schwarz Alm spa. Massages and beauty treatments are available.

Austrian specialties are served in the restaurant, and a rich breakfast is served every morning. Guests can dine and enjoy drinks on the panorama terrace.
