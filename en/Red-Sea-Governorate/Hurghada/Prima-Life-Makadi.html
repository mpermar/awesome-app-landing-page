Description

At the resort, the rooms include a balcony. Rooms include a private bathroom and free toiletries, and some rooms at Prima Life Makadi have a terrace. At the accommodation, every room has a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Guests at Prima Life Makadi can enjoy a continental breakfast. Guests can dine in the on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of local and Italian dishes and also offers Vegan options.

The resort offers 5-star accommodations with a fitness center. You can play ping pong and darts at Prima Life Makadi, and car rental is available.

Giftun Island is 17 miles from the accommodation, while Sharm El Naga is 8 miles from the property. Hurghada International Airport is 14 miles away.

Makadi Bay is a great choice for travelers interested in water sports, swimming pools and family-friendly trips.
