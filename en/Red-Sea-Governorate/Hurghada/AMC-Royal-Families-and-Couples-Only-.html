Description

All rooms and units feature a balcony with views of the Red Sea. Each one offers a flat-screen TV, a mini-bar and an electric kettle. The suite includes a seating area complete with a dining table. A shower is fitted in the bathroom.

Guests have a variety of dining options at AMC Royal Families & Couples Hotel; Blue Lagoon Restaurant serves selection of International buffets for the 3 meals. The Italian restaurant serves authentic pastas and pizzas & pastas . Palms Island restaurant serves Oriental food. and Kona Kai Sushi Bar serves Tapenyaki. Drinks can be enjoyed at the Shots Fun Pub.

For an energizing workout, the hotel features a fully equipped health club including gymnasium, sauna, and steam and massage rooms. Other activities on site include table tennis, beach volleyball and darts.

Hurghada Center, with its markets and promenade, is only 6.8 mi away from AMC Royal Families & Couples Hotel. Free private parking is possible on site.
