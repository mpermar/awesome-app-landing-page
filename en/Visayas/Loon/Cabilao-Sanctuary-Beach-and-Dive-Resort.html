Description

The rooms at the resort come with air conditioning, a seating area, and a balcony or terrace. Some units at Cabilao Sanctuary Beach & Dove Resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV and a wardrobe. Guest rooms include a private bathroom with a shower and toilet.

A continental and à la carte breakfast options are available each morning at the property. The restaurant at Cabilao Sanctuary Beach & Dove Resort specializes in international and local cuisine.

You can play table tennis at the resort, and bike hire is available while waters sports facilities are also available. Diving may also be arranged for guests at an additional charge.

Tagbilaran City is 18 mi from Cabilao Sanctuary Beach & Dove Resort, while Bohol is 19 mi away. The nearest airport is Tagbilaran Airport, accessed by land and boat transfers.

03/18/2018

Greetings from paradise

"It's been a few weeks since we stayed there, but my thoughts are still in this beautiful, relaxing, tranquil place. We are a family of four, children are 5 and 8. We truly had the time of our life. We enjoyed the swimming pool, went for a walk along the very quiet and beautiful beach, rented kayak, my husband and I had a massage, I also did the diving course, which was amazing. The food is delicious and the views are amazing. The manager, who is Italian, is a very warm person, truly welcoming host and the staff members are very friendly and helpful. 02/23/2018

Amazing stay

"Design, Atmosphere, Cleanliness, Nature, Room, Staff, Swimming pool, Snorkeling, Spa.We spent 5 days enjoying nature in complete relax. It took time to reach Cabilao from our starting point (Mactan Airport in Cebu), but the place definitely deserved the trip. It was the perfect break from city life, without losing the chance to treat ourselves with attentive service and comfort.It is a small and super-clean boutique resort immerse in the nature, nicely decorated, with private beach, and a stylish lounge area facing the sea. Although it was supposed to be designed for divers, surprisingly it turned out to be child-friendly as well and our 15 months old boy loved the swimming pool, the huge number of starfish just few steps from the beach, walking around barefoot and yes… the food.The place is new and you can feel it. Room was large, well decorated and aircon was working smoothly.No fridge tough, but we did not really care since the bar and the water station to refill the complimentary travel mug (nice welcome gift) was few meters close. We appreciated the cleanliness of the common areas, the room with balcony and the attention we had from the staff with a particular mention to Antonia who took care of us as we were her family (thank you Antonia and see you next year!) Recommended: Bring your water shoes (help entering water when low-tide) and snorkel right in front of the resort."

02/22/2018

Paradise found!!

"Paradise! The resort is incredibly well-managed, beautiful and so relaxing. Their attention to detail is outstanding. The staff were constantly attending to the grounds and ensuring everything was sparkling clean. The sitting area overlooking the water was SO comfortable and the snorkeling, with a dramatic coral drop just 200 meters off-shore was fantastic. (Shallow rocky way out to the drop, but the little store had swimming shoes to buy or rent from the dive store.) And they had a lovely pool and sandy beach for kids. Food was limited to the resort itself, but all excellent, generous portions, and very reasonable prices given how remote it felt. The cottages aren't overlooking the water, but they were super comfortable , peaceful, and clean. They provided lovely towels, beach bags and umbrellas. All very well thought out. I especially liked the resorts attention to the environment - providing refillable water bottles, which you could keep as a souvenir, and at one point I noticed the dive instructors gently asking guests not to take (the abundant) starfish out of the sea. I would have stayed longer if I could have!"
