Description

Modern décor and large windows feature throughout the spacious guest rooms at the Marco Polo Plaza. Each non-smoking room lets in plenty of natural light, and is fitted with a 32-inch flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Guests can enjoy body treatments and massages at the Wellness Zone. The hotel provides car rental services and free parking. Local souvenirs can be purchased at the gift shop.

Offering sweeping views of Cebu City, the Blu Bar & Grill serves a fine selection of grilled dishes. Other dining choices include Asian food at Café Marco and refreshing cocktails at El Viento.

Cebu Marco Polo is 10.5 miles from Mactan International Airport and 2 miles from Magellan's Cross.

Lahug is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, food and relaxation.
