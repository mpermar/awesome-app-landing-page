Description

Featuring antique European pieces, The Peacock Garden’s rooms combine marble and wood furniture. Each room has a flat-screen TV, DVD player and safe. The bathrooms have a shower, slippers, and terry cloth bathrobes.

The Roman-inspired Fontana Aurelia Spa has a variety of massages and body treatments. Other spa facilities include a fitness center and a spa bathtub. Sun lounge chairs are provided on the terrace for guests to enjoy the beautiful views.

The 4 dining options include a cigar lounge and a wine cellar. Decorated with vintage furnishings, The Old Heidelberg Restaurant serves international dishes. Guests can enjoy music and dancing at Pim’s pub.

The Peacock Garden is 6 miles from Tagbilaran Airport. It is just a few minutes’ drive from historic Baclayon Church.
