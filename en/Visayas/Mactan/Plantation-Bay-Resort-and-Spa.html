Description

Featuring spacious layouts and classic-wood furnishings, air-conditioned rooms include cable TV and a safe. Private bathrooms have marble fittings and a bathtub. Internet is free in the entire hotel.

Recreational facilities include water slides in the freshwater pool, wall-climbing and archery amenities. For those looking to exercise, there are tennis courts and a fitness center. Hiking and diving trips can be arranged by Plantation Bay staff.

Offering a range of cuisines, 4 award-winning dining options at Resort Plantation Bay include Palermo Café Bar, Kilimanjaro Kafe, Savannah Grill and Fiji Restaurant.

Plantation Bay Resort and Spa is a 15 minute drive from Lapu Lapu Shrine and Gaisano Mall. It is a 20 minute drive from Mactan Airport.
