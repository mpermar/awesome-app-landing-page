Description

The air-conditioned rooms at the Pipers Cove feature a private balcony, a dining and sitting area. The kitchens have refrigerator, stove and cooking utensils.

Activities in the surrounding of the property include snorkeling, scuba diving and water skiing.

Runaway Bay Golf Course is 1.5 miles from the resort. The Seven Oaks Sanctuary for Wildlife can be reached within a 5-minute drive. Dunns River Falls can be reached in 20 minutes by car, and Ocho Rios is 30 minutes by car from the Pipers Cove.
