Description

The homey rooms and suites at Victor's Seehotel Weingärtnerall feature a flat-screen TV with Sky satellite channels. Some of the rooms also have their own balcony.

Start your day with the delicious breakfast buffet, before relaxing by the lake or hiking or biking through the surrounding countryside.

Guests at the Seehotel Weingärtner are welcome to take a dip in the pool or unwind in the sauna, hot tub and steam bath.

Victor's restaurant serves regional specialties and international favorites in the evening. After dinner, guests can enjoy a drink in the inviting pub. On sunny days enjoy your meals and refreshments outside on the terrace.

The Bostalsee is ideal for fishing and all sorts of water sports, and golf enthusiasts can visit the nearby Heidehof golf course.

"We stayed here one night en-route and it was a wonderful night, we would have loved to stay longer. The staff were welcoming and spoke excellent English. We ate our evening meal at the hotel, trying a recommended local wine. The wine and food were wonderful. Portions were on the large side. There is a wait as food is cooked to order, but worth the wait. They offered a children’s menu and high chairs were available. Breakfast was also fantastic lots of choice including omelettes cooked in front of you and cocoa for the children. The pool and jacuzzi was a nice way to unwind after a long drive.
