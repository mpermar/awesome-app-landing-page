Description

Located in the heart of Jerusalem and within walking distance of the Old City, the Dan Panorama is a hotel offering air-conditioned accommodation and a great range of facilities.

Free Wi-Fi is provided. All of the Dan Panorama Jerusalem Hotel's rooms are spacious and elegant.

They come with cable TV, while most have views across Jerusalem and some overlook the Old City.

At the Dan Panorama Jerusalem Hotel you can enjoy the rooftop pool and sun terrace in summer.

The hotel also has a free gym, a synagogue and shops. The Panorama's restaurant serves many Israeli specialities alongside international dishes.

The children's club Daniland is open during August. The opening date is subject to change.
