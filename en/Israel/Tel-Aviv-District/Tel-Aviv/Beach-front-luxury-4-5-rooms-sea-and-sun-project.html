Description

This air-conditioned 3-bedroom apartment comes with a kitchen, a seating area, and a satellite flat-screen TV and DVD player. The apartment also provides washing machine and a bathroom with a hot tub and a bath.

Guests can use the spa center. Guests can swim in the indoor pool, or go cycling.
