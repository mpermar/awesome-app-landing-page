Description

The apartment features 2 bedrooms, a kitchen with an oven, a washing machine, and a bathroom with a hair dryer. A flat-screen TV with satellite channels is offered.

Spa and wellness facilities including a spa center, a sauna and a fitness center are at guests' disposal during their stay at Sea View Suite. A bicycle rental service is available at this property.

Haifa is 27 miles from the accommodation, while Netanya is 8 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Ben Gurion Airport, 30 miles from Sea View Suite.
