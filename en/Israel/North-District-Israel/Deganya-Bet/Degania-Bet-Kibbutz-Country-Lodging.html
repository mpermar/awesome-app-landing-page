Description

Degania Bet Kibbutz Country Lodging is a short walk from the Sea of Galilee. It offers lots of leisure facilities, including a swimming pool and football and indoor basketball courts.

All rooms at Degania Bet Kibbutz offer air conditioning, cable TV, and a small kitchenette with coffee maker.

Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas. Parking is free at the Kibbutz Country Lodging.

Guests can use the barbecue facilities in the garden. The hotel is 12 km from the historic city of Tiberias.
