Description

Offering a summer swimming pool and mini golf course, Parod Country Inn is set in a Kibbutz in the Upper Galilee area.

It has a peaceful setting 20 minutes' drive from Rosh Pina and the Sea of Galilee.

Rooms feature a kitchenette with microwave and kettle. They are air conditioned and include a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

An Israeli buffet breakfast can be enjoyed in the dining room or in the comfort of your room.

Parod Country Inn provides free Wi-Fi in communal areas and there is a pub in the Kibbutz that has a wide collection of whiskies.

A car is the best way of getting around and free parking is provided. Acre is 30 minutes' drive away.
