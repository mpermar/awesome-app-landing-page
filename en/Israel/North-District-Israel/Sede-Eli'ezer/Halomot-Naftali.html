Description

Offering modernly designed suites with free Wi-Fi, Halomot Naftali features a heated swimming pool and 2 treatment rooms.

It is located in Sde Eliezer, near the Yesud HaMa'ala Junction. The garden boasts picturesque streams, a small orchard and romantic trails.

Some of the suites come with a private pool. Free parking is provided at the Halomot Naftali.
