Description

Nir David Country Lodge offers rustic wood cabins in a kibbutz situated along the Asi River, outside the city of Beit She'an.

Each unit features a hydromassage bath and a patio. All accommodation at the Country Lodge offers an LCD TV, self-catering facilities, and air conditioning.

Breakfast is served in a terrace overlooking the river. Country Lodge Nir David features an indoor heated pool, and the staff can arrange canoeing excursions along the river.
