Description

Located in the Artists' Colony quarter of Safed, The Way Inn - Boutique Hotel boasts a sun terrace and views of the Galilee Hills.

Rooms are set in 200-year-old restored stone villas, decorated by local artists. Certain units include views of the garden or city.

Each room has a private bathroom. The Way Inn - Boutique Hotel features free WiFi throughout the property.

The daily Israeli breakfast includes pastries, bread, eggs and salad, together with hot and cold drinks.

Rosh Pinna is 11 km from The Way Inn - Boutique Hotel, while Israel Bible Museum is 1.
