Description

Combining traditional style and modern amenities, Artist Quarter Guesthouse B&B is located in Safed close to numerous art galleries and ancient synagogues.

It offers self-catering accommodation with free WiFi and a Kosher breakfast. Set in a 300 years old building, all accommodation units are decorated with stone wall elements.

Each is equipped with air conditioning and a kitchen or a kitchenette. Dining tables for guests to relax are available in the shared courtyard.

The nearest grocery store is 100 metres away. In the vicinity guests can also enjoy numerous restaurants.

A local bus station is located 300 meters away from the Artist Quarter Guesthouse B&B.

Rosh Pinna is 10 km away, the Sea of Galilee is at a distance of 24 km and Tiberias can be found 36 km away.
