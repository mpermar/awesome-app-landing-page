Description

Set on the eastern shore of the Sea of Galilee, Kinar Galilee Hotel is a kosher property located 4 km from Moshav Ramot.

It offers a gym and a Kosher restaurant serving meat and fish specialities. The Kinar Galilee is around 30 minutes' drive from Tiberias on the other side of the Sea of Galilee and the Holy City of Safed.

The nearby beach has separate sections for men and women. All air-conditioned, rooms here have views of the surrounding greenery.

You will also find a fridge, kettle and a TV. Each private bathroom comes with a hairdryer and toiletries.

Breakfast is buffet style. Guests also have an outdoor swimming pool, tennis court and basketball court.

Indoors, you will find a sauna and hot tub. The hotel even has its own synagogue, Mikvehs and a large lobby with views of the gardens.
