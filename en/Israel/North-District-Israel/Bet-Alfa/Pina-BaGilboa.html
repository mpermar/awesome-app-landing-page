Description

The country house features 1 bedroom, a living room, and a kitchen with an oven. The country house also provides guests with a bathroom.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property.

The country house has a playground. Guests can also relax in the garden or on the sun terrace.

Tiberias is 20 miles from Pina BaGilboa, while Nazareth is 15 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Ben Gurion Airport, 48 miles from the accommodation.
