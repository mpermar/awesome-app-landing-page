Description

Set in the heart of the Galilee, Kibbutz Inbar Country Lodge is located 30 km from Akko and Tiberias.

This typical guesthouse offers rooms with free Wi-Fi, and a healthy vegetarian breakfast.

The functional, air-conditioned rooms at Kibbutz Inbar Country Lodge are simply furnished.

Offering views on the surrounding countryside, they include a private bathroom, a microwave, and a fridge.

Guests can enjoy a buffet breakfast in the kibbutz’s cosy dining room, and relax in the property’s furnished garden.

The property is a 10-minute drive from a free seasonal swimming pool and 12 km from Karmiel shopping centre.
