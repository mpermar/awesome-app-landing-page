Description

Set in the Moshav of Beit Hilel, Villa Vitrage is surrounded by nature. It offers a spa with thermal swimming pool and ultra-modern villas, each with a balcony, patio and private hot tub.

There are a total of 7 villas, all featuring free Wi-Fi, air conditioning and a flat-screen TV with DVD player.

The kitchenette includes a microwave and kettle. A varied Israeli breakfast is served buffet style each morning.

Guests have discounts at various local restaurants and there is a supermarket in the Moshav.

Wellness facilities at Villa Vitrage include a sauna and hot tub. This property is ideal for exploring the Hahula Valley, Dan Nature Reserve and the Banias Waterfalls.
