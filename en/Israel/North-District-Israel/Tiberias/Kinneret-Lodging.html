Description

At the resort, the rooms include a closet, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Kinneret Lodging has certain rooms with sea views, and the rooms are equipped with a terrace. At the accommodation all rooms come with a seating area.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning at the property. Guests can dine in the in-house restaurant, which serves Moroccan and Mediterranean cuisine and also offers dairy-free and kosher options.

The property has a hot tub. Skiing and cycling are among the activities that guests of Kinneret Lodging can find nearby.

St. Peter's Church is a few steps from the resort, while Casa Dona Gracia is a 6-minute walk away.
