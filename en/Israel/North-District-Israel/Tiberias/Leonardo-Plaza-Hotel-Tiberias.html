Description

Leonardo Plaza Hotel Tiberias is set on the promenade overlooking the Sea of Galilee.

It has a spa with a gym, and includes seasonal facilities, such as a pool. All rooms are air conditioned and feature cable TV and a minibar.

Some have a balcony with a view of the Sea of Galilee. Free international calls to 60 countries worldwide can be made from the rooms.

Leonardo Plaza's wellness centre includes a sauna, hot tub, and a range of massages.

Children have their own mini club and playground. Hotel Leonardo Plaza is on the north side of the city.

Tiberias' Old City is only 10 minutes' walk away. Tiberias' shopping centre is also nearby.
