Description

The Isrotel Riviera Club Hotel offers self-catering apartments with free WiFi and a kitchenette.

A mini-market and an activity club for children with instructors are featured on site.

This hotel has an outdoor swimming pool and a children's playground. Pleasant and soothing music is played in the evening.
