Description

Unique, luxurious, and impressive, the Queen of Sheba is one of Eilat's grandest hotels, offering a superb location overlooking the Red Sea.

You can enjoy the hotel's own shopping mall and swimming pool overlooking the sea.

Eilat Queen of Sheba presents a long list of top-quality facilities. Guests can do business in the Executive Lounge or enjoy fine dining in one of the 4 restaurants.

Visit the Sheba Spa for sauna, steam room, hot tub and massages, or stay fit in the well-equipped gym.

The hotel is located in the centre of the North Shore hotel area, right by the promenade, and only a 2-minute walk from the beach.

Ramon airport is 20 mins' drive from the hotel. The hotel and the surrounding area offer lots of activities for the whole family, including exciting water sports.
