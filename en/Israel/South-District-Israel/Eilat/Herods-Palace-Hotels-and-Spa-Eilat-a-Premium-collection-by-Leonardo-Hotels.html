Description

Rooms at the Herods Palace Hotels & Spa have balconies and LCD TV with cable and satellite channels. There is also a mini-bar and kettle for guests’ comfort.

The 5-star superior hotel serves a daily Israeli breakfast, and guests can also enjoy snacks and drinks in the coffee shop or poolside bar. The restaurant serves both Middle Eastern and international cuisine.

The wellness center includes a gym, sauna and Turkish bath. The private beach is directly in front of the hotel.
