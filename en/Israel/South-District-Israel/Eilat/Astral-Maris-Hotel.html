Description

Located right on the waterfront and opposite the northern beach of Eilat, the Astral Maris Hotel features a sea-view swimming pool, a children's pool and mini club.

All rooms offer air conditioning and a plasma TV. Free WiFi is available in the lobby.

The rooms feature a seating area and a minibar, and the private bathrooms are complete with hairdryer.

Most rooms have a balcony with views of the airport and the mountains of Eilat. Other facilities at the Astral include a concert hall and a lobby bar.
