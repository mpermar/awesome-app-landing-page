Description

Located in the Lagoon area of Eilat’s North Beach, the Royal Garden is set among palm-filled gardens and 5 free-form pools.

It has a gourmet restaurant and spacious suites with fully-fitted kitchenettes. A balcony, air conditioning and free WiFi access come as standard in the Isrotel Royal Garden’s suites.

Each is decorated with wooden furniture and has a dining and seating area. The main restaurant serves an Israeli breakfast and buffets at lunch and dinner.

The Café Boulevard has freshly brewed coffee and snacks, while the Ranch House specialises in steak, grills and vegetarian dishes.

During the day the gym, spa and play area will keep both adults and children occupied.

In the evening Las Vegas-style shows are performed in the theatre. The Underwater Observatory is a 10-minute drive from the hotel.
