Description

Featuring an indoor and outdoor pool, Eilot Kibbutz Country Lodging is 6 km north of Eilat and its beach.

It offers spacious rooms, and a garden with free BBQ facilities. Modern, air-conditioned rooms at the Eilot Kibbutz come with a balcony, seating area, and refrigerator.

Some also include a microwave. Park Timna, famous for its King Solomon Pillars, is 30 km away.
