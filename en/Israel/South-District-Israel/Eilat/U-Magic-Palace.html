Description

Right by the northern beach, U Magic Palace is overlooking the lagoon and Eilat's famous marina.

It is located in the hotel area, close to Eilat's attractions. Free WiFi is available throughout.

U Magic Palace offers large and comfortable rooms, some with pool view and balconies.

The hotel is perfect for a family vacation, and offers great facilities such as a large swimming pool and spa services (fitness, hot tub and saunas for additional fees).

Entertainment and activities for both adults and children are offered. For those wanting to enjoy their time off and wake up late in the morning, breakfast is served every day until 12:00.
