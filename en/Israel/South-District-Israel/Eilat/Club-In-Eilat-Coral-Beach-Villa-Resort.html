Description

Located in a valley with views of the desert, Club In Eilat is a resort with spacious accommodation units just 300 metres from Almog Beach.

Eilat city centre is 7 km from this property offering free WiFi throughout. Club In Eilat offers air-conditioned rooms with kitchen, dining area, and lounge.

Most have a balcony, while some have a garden view. The outdoor swimming pool is open all year round and is surrounded by sun beds and parasols.

Sports facilities include tennis courts, basketball court, table tennis and ping pong tables, as well as fitness equipment.

Shared barbecue facilities are available for guests to use. The Club In restaurant serves Israeli and international cuisine.

Breakfast is buffet style, and includes cheese, salad, pastries, eggs as well as fruit.
