Description

Just 5 minutes' drive from the Ramon Crater, iBike Hotel is modern boutique hostel in Mitzpe Ramon in the Negev Desert.

It features a café and social areas with a TV and DVD player. Hotel iBike has a bright and colourful design throughout.

Guests can relax out in the garden or in the comfortable lounge with plenty of sofas.

Cyclists and hikers are welcome and bikes are available to rent from reception. Accommodation here is air-conditioned and has a private bathroom with slippers and a hairdryer.

The apartments also include a kitchenette. Breakfast is available each morning and the café serves light meals at lunch and dinner.

Guests have free Wi-Fi and free parking, plus lots of local recommendations from the staff.
