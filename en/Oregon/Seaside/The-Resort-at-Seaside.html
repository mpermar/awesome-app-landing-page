Description

At the resort all rooms are equipped with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a kitchen, a dining area and a private bathroom with a hair dryer and a bath. Every room has a coffee machine, while some have a balcony. All rooms will provide guests with a dishwasher.

At The Resort at Seaside guests are welcome to use a hot tub and a fitness room. You can play ping pong at the accommodation.

The Resort at Seaside offers a business center for guest use. Staff speak English and Spanish at the reception.

Elmer Feldenheimer State Park is 2.2 miles from the resort. The nearest airport is Charlotte Amalie Harbor Seaplane Base (St. Thomas) Airport, 53 miles from the property.
