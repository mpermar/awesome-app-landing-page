Description

Each of the contemporary rooms has a flat-screen TV, a mini-bar and facilities for making tea and coffee. The private bathrooms include a hairdryer and free toiletries.

You can enjoy French cuisine in the hotel’s restaurant and the bar serves a variety of drinks. A buffet breakfast is prepared every morning and it can be delivered to your room upon request.

The hotel is only 3.7 mi from Versailles and 20 minutes from Paris. Le Golf National is 8.7 mi from the hotel. Public parking is available on site.
