Description

All of the contemporary rooms offer an LCD TV with satellite channels, a private bathroom with a bathtub, safety deposit box and free Wi-Fi access. Each has a kitchenette complete with a microwave and refrigerator. Kitchenware can be rented with an extra cost.

A buffet breakfast is served daily and guests can enjoy a brunch every Sunday from 11:00 until 15:00 at an extra cost (brunch is free for children aged 5 years old or less). L'Origano restaurant proposes Italian dishes and is open every day. In the evening, Le Crystal lounge bar serves aperitifs and cocktails.

Marne la Vallée Chessy TGV station is 10 minutes away from Paxton Resort & Spa. The hotel offers a large conference center and the Val d'Europe shopping complex is directly accessed by the 22 bus, located nearby. The property offers a shuttle to Bussy Saint Georges RER Train Station. The RER will take the guests directly to Disneyland Paris Parc.
