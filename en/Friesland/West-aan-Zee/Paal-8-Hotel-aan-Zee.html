Description

This unique location not only offers a splendid view of the North Sea and surrounding nature, it also allows you to completely escape the hectic city life. Relax in the sociable lounge, unwind with drinks in the bar and dine in the attractive restaurant – popular for its quality of food and pleasing atmosphere.

If relaxing on the beach or enjoying hours of undisturbed walking isn’t enough, the hotel also offers an indoor swimming pool, sauna and solarium. From families to solo travelers, this charming hotel caters to the needs of all guests and the friendly staff is pleased to provide you with that extra bit of personal attention.
