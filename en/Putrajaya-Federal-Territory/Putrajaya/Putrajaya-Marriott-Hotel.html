Description

Putrajaya Marriott Hotel is only a 5-minute drive from the Putrajaya Express Rail Link Station. Kuala Lumpur city center and Kuala Lumpur International Airport are a 25-minute drive away.

Elegantly decorated, each room comes with cable TV, and bathroom with both a shower and a bathtub.

Guests can work out at the fitness center, soak up the sun by the pool or enjoy a massage at the Dewi Teratai Spa. Other recreation options include shopping at the IOI City Mall or golf at the 18-hole Palm Garden Golf Club, located within IOI Resort City.

The Zest Restaurant serves a daily international buffet breakfast and dinner. Other dining options include Midori Japanese Restaurant and Summer Palace Chinese Restaurant. Tuscany Restaurant and Pizza Bar serves Italian-American specialties.
