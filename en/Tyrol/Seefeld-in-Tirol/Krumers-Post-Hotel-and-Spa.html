Description

The rooms at Krumer's Spa Hotel are renovated in 2016 and offer a balcony or a terrace as well as views of the Tyrolean mountains.

The 2.500 m² spa area is spread includes a steam bath, an infrared cabin, a ladies' sauna, and a solarium. The relaxation area with waterbeds provides for absolute relaxation, while the Leisure Lounge features Swiss stone pine wood, loungers and a 36°C warm pool with underwater massage loungers and panoramic mountain views. The spa area is only available for guests of age 16 years and older.

Half board includes a buffet breakfast, an afternoon snack, a 5-course dinner, as well as juices, tea and fresh fruit all day long in the spa area. Guest can enjoy dishes prepared by Chef Julius Polak, who is awarded with 1 toque.

The golf course is only a 2-minute walk away from Krumers Post Hotel. Hotel guests get a 35% discount on green fees. You will also get a 20% discount on ski rental.
