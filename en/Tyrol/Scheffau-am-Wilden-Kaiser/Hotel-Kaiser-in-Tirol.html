Description

Enjoy an extended half-board in winter, with drinks for dinner and coffee, tea and snacks in the afternoon.

The slope right next to the hotel leads to the valley station of the ski elevator taking you into the Wilder Kaiser-Brixental ski area. The Kaiser Spa and the indoor pool can be used free of charge.

In summer Hotel Kaiser in Tirol offers an all-inclusive arrangement, with a program of activities for children and adults. There is a natural swimming pond in the big garden. You can also find a volleyball court, a football pitch, a climbing area and much more.
