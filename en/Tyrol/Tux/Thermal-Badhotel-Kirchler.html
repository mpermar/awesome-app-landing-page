Description

Thermal-Badhotel Kirchler features a thermal indoor pool, a Finnish sauna, a Turkish steam bath, and a herbal sauna. The private thermal spring, which only supplies the hotel, is the highest in Europe.

The Hintertux Glacier offers ideal conditions for skiing and snowboarding. The downhill run ends right behind Thermal-Badhotel Kirchler.
