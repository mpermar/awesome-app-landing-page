Description

Enjoy the ultimate skiing pleasure - you can ski directly from the hotel to the Gaisberg and Festkogel valley stations. From there you can easily access of the most beautiful and convenient ski areas in the Alps.

In summer many hiking and mountain trails start in the immediate vicinity of the hotel.

The Hotel Alpenland has been built in a typical Tyrolean style, with great attention to detail. Masterful woodwork and discrete elegance combine to create a harmonic whole and give the hotel its cozy atmosphere amidst a beautiful mountain panorama.
