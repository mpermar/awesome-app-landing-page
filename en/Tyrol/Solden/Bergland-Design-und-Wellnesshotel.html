Description

Modern design and natural materials characterize the rooms at Hotel Bergland. All have a balcony, a mini-bar, and satellite TV. The bathrooms have a glass wall. Free WiFi is available.

The Bergland’s restaurant serves dishes made from healthy, regional ingredients, as well as a wide selection of fine wines. A cigar lounge is available after dinner. Half-board includes a rich breakfast buffet with regional and organic products, and a 5 or 6-course dinner with a healthy option.

The 1,700 m² Sky Spa over the roofs of Sölden features an indoor pool, an outdoor hot tub, a bio sauna, a Finnish sauna, various relaxation areas, and an aerobics and fitness room. Massages and beauty treatments are also offered. Guests booking via this website get a discount on wellness services.

Hotel Bergland offers a wide range of sports activities, including guided ski tours and ice-climbing. There is also a sports shop at the hotel.

Parking is available free of charge. Hotel Bergland also has an underground car park.

The Ötztal Premium Card is included in all rates from June to mid-October and offers free rides on the cable cars, elevators and buses, free guided hiking tours, free access to public baths and lakes and many more benefits.
