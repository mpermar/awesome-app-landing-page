Description

You will feel relaxed and at home in the rustically furnished reception areas with their cozy corners, with a glass of wine at the open fireplace, in the restaurant with its culinary delights, at the bar or in the comfortably furnished bedrooms.

An ample breakfast buffet is available every morning and includes a wide variety of coffee specialties, hot chocolate, a broad choice of teas, and several egg dishes.

Located close to the center of Fulpmes, the Alpenhotel Tirolerhof is an ideal starting point to enjoy the region’s many sports and leisure options in every season.
