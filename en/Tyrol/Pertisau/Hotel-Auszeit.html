Description

All guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a TV. Hotel Auszeit has some units that feature a balcony, and all rooms have a private bathroom with a bath or shower and free toiletries. The rooms include a closet.

A buffet breakfast is served daily at the property.

The wellness area at the accommodation is comprised of a sauna and a hammam. Skiing and cycling are among the activities that guests of Hotel Auszeit can find nearby.

Jochlift is 0.9 miles from the hotel, while Zwölferkopflift is a 19-minute walk away. The nearest airport is Kranebitten Airport, 21 miles from Hotel Auszeit.
