Description

A flat-screen TV with cable channels is featured in every guest room at the Drury Inn & Suites Independence Kansas City. A microwave and a refrigerator are also provided in all rooms.

Guests can enjoy a swim in the indoor/outdoor pool or relax in the hot tub at Kansas City Independence Drury Inn and Suites. The on-site fitness center and business center are available 24-hours a day.

A daily hot breakfast with fresh fruit, bagels, pastries and cereals is provided to all guests. Free snacks and beverages are served nightly.

Downtown Kansas City is 15 miles from this hotel. Kauffman Stadium and Arrowhead Stadium are within a 10-minute drive of the property.
