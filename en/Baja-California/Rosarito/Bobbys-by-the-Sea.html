Description

Each Bobby’s villa has free Wi-Fi and a sitting area with a TV. The private bathrooms have a shower and hairdryer, and the full kitchens include a microwave and an oven.

At Bobby´s by the Sea, there is a restaurant, a game room and laundry facilities. Live entertainment is provided on Fridays, Saturdays and Sundays.

The property provides free parking. Downtown Tijuana and Tijuana International Airport are within a 30-minute drive.
