Description

This hotel is set on Lake Schuchye, among the pine forest of the National Park. A spa centre and free Wi-Fi are featured at Rixos Borovoe Hotel.

The spacious, air-conditioned rooms of this hotel offer warm-coloured decor and overlook the lake.

Each elegant room includes a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with heated floors.

Masala Restaurant serves Indian and Thai cuisine. L’Olivo Ristorante overlooks the lake and offers Italian dishes.

Guests can work out in the gym and relax in the Turkish steam bath or the Finnish sauna with a hot tub.

A personal butler service is provided. Borovoe Village and Borovoe Train Station are 15 km from Rixos Borovoe Hotel.
