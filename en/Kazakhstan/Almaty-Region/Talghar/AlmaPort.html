Description

All rooms in the inn are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. AlmaPort has some rooms with mountain views, and the rooms are equipped with a patio. All guest rooms include a desk.

A continental breakfast is served daily at the property.

The accommodation has a playground. Guests at AlmaPort will be able to enjoy activities in and around Talghar, like hiking.

With staff speaking English and Russian, guidance is available at the reception.

Almaty is 14 miles from the inn. Almaty International Airport is 11 miles from the property.
