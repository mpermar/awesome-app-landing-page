Description

Offering ski-to-door access and a ski school, S. N. e. G is set in Besqaynar, 21 km from Almaty.

Kombi-1 Ski Lift is located nearby. The hotel has a sun terrace and views of the mountain, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Every room is fitted with a flat-screen TV. Some rooms have a seating area for your convenience.

All rooms are equipped with a private bathroom fitted with a shower. Extras include bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries.

S. N. e. G features free WiFi throughout the property. You will find a tour desk at the property.

You can play darts at this hotel, Xbox 360 and game console and the area is popular for skiing and hiking.
