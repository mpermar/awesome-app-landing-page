Description

Offering a spacious spa centre with a sauna, Turkish hammam and a snow room, Rixos Khadisha Shymkent is located in a business area of Shymkent.

Guests can have a swim in the indoor pool and work out in the fitness centre on site.

The elegant suites and rooms feature traditional neoclassic decor and marble bathrooms.

They are air conditioned and offer a flat-screen TV and a coffee machine. Kazakhasia restaurant serves a variety of dishes of Asian cuisine, and Olivia restaurant offers Italian cuisine.

Guests of Rixos Khadisha Shymkent can have a drink at Atrium Café or at the English bar White Horse.

Shymkent Train Station is 3 km away, while Shymkent Airport is 10 km from Rixos Khadisha Shymkent.
