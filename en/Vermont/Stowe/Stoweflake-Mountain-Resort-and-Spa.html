Description

Stoweflake Mountain Resort features luxury guest rooms with free Wi-Fi, a refrigerator and coffee maker. Each room is spacious and individually decorated and offers spa bathrobes and toiletries.

Stoweflake Mountain Resort and Spa’s Winfield's Bistro is a 4-star restaurant that serves gourmet American cuisine. Charlie B's Pub offers traditional pub dishes and both restaurants offer an extensive wine selection from the resort’s wine cellar.

Spa at Stoweflake is on 50,000 square feet, which includes 30 treatment rooms and a fully equipped gym. This luxury spa also features indoor and outdoor pools, a 12-foot high massaging waterfall and a meditating garden.

Other amenities at the Stoweflake Resort include a private hot air balloon during the summer months, tennis courts and a children’s program.

02/21/2017

Pleasant but overpriced on holiday weekend

"Nice big room with a timer-operated gas fireplace, but it made the room very hot. Liked the fire in the lobby although the seating around it was small. Kids loved apres ski tea, with cookies and cocoa. Meant to try the spa but never got a chance. Outdoor pool was a draw for us when booking. It is heated but still cold unless the air temp is VERY cold for contrast, and only open 3-7. Indoor pool is small but good for kids. Outdoor jacuzzi was nice but small, more than 6 adults felt awkwardly snug. Snowshoe trail is basically a walking path around the hotel building and pool but there are nice trails nearby. Close driving distance to restaurants, town, and Stowe mountain, and you can get the shuttle from the hotel to the lift."
