Description

KC Grande Resort is about 2 hours by car and ferry from Trat Airport. Ferry rides are available from the Centre Point, Kromaluang Chumporn and Ao Thammachat Piers.

Spacious rooms at KC Grande feature wood furnishings and a private terrace with views of the ocean or tropical gardens. They come equipped with cable TV, safe and a fridge.

Guests can relax at the large outdoor hot tub or with massages and sauna at the spa. Activities such as canoeing, snorkeling and beach volleyball are available. Airport shuttle service and car rental are available.

A range of Thai and European dishes prepared with fresh seafood is available at The Beach Restaurant. Cocktails with live music are available at the beachfront KC Pub.

White Sands Beach is a great choice for travelers interested in sandy beaches, beaches and beach walks.
