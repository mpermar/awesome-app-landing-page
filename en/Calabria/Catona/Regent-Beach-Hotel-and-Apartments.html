Description

The rooms at the Hotel Regent have contemporary design with original works of art. They are all air-conditioned and include a balcony and TV with satellite channels.

Guests can enjoy Calabrian dishes and Italian classics at Regent's restaurant. Breakfast is an American buffet, and meals can be enjoyed on the sea-view terrace in the summer.

Guests can rent 1 sun umbrella and 1 lounge chair at a special daily rate on the private beach.

The Museo Nazionale della Magna Grecia museum, with its famous Riace Bronzes, is a 10-minute drive away. Tito Menniti Airport is 9 miles from the hotel.
