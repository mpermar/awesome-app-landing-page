Description

All guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. The rooms include a desk.

Breakfast is available every morning, and includes continental, buffet and à la carte options. There's an on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of seafood, Italian and Grill/bbq dishes.

DOMUS Grand Hotel has a playground.

The nearest airport is Lamezia Terme Airport, 30 miles from the accommodation.
