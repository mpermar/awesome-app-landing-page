Description

At the hotel, all rooms include a closet. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a shower, while certain accommodations at Hotel Agua Boutique also feature a balcony. All rooms have a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

A à la carte breakfast is served daily at the property.

Staff at the reception can assist guests at any time of the day.

Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Cartagena's Walls, San Felipe de Barajas Castle and Cartagena's Modern Art Museum. The nearest airport is Rafael Núñez International Airport, 3.1 miles from the property.
