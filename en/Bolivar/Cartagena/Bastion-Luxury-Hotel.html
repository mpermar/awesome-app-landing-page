Description

The property has luxury rooms and suites with a contemporary décor that evokes it's colonial past. They feature a flat-screen TV, a large wardrobe and a private bathroom with L’Occitane toiletries.

The on-site restaurant is categorized under the kitchen by Rausch program of the well-known Rausch brothers. Other options are available within just over a quarter mile of the property.

Santo Toribio church is located next to the hotel, and Rafael Núñez International Airport can be reached in 15 minutes by car.

Centro is a great choice for travelers interested in architecture, food and history.
