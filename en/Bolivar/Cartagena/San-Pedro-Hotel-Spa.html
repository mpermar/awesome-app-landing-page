Description

San Pedro Hotel Spa welcomes guests with a welcome massage session and San Pedro lemonade. Guests can relax in the hot tub or in the spa.

Rooms are styled with Colonial-style artwork, decorated tiles and ornamented mirror frames. They include air conditioning, DVD players and minibars. Some of them have flat-screen TVs, and one of the suites features a spa tub.

Bocagrande Beach is a 10-minute drive and car rental is available.

Breakfast includes organic jams, different varieties of bread, eggs, tropical fruit and world-famous Colombian coffee or tea and hot chocolate.

San Diego is a great choice for travelers interested in architecture, food and history.
