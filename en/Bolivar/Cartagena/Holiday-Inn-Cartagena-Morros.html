Description

The Holiday Inn Cartagena Morros has elegant rooms with air conditioning, flat-screen cable TVs and work desks. All of them have safes, coffee makers and private bathrooms with toiletries.

Guests wake up to a full breakfast buffet with tropical fruits and world-famous Colombian coffee. Mediterranean and International dishes can be enjoyed at Blue Restaurant. Bar lounge 992 serves tropical cocktails and fine wines with light snacks.

The Cartagena Morros is in upscale northern Cartagena, right in front of the finest beaches. Car rental is available and the tour desk is always ready to provide tips.
