Description

At the Hampton Inn Holland hotel, every room comes with a coffee maker. A work desk is included in every guestroom. Other amenities in each room include cable TV.

On-site at the Holland Hampton Inn hotel are a basketball court and a fitness room. An indoor pool and a tennis table are also on-site. The hotel offers a video arcade and a pool table.

Windmill Island and the Tulip Gardens are minutes from the Holland, Michigan Hampton Inn hotel. Gentex Corporation - Corporate Headquarters is a short drive from the hotel.
