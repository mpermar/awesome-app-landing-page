Description

Holiday Inn Midland features an indoor pool and indoor/outdoor hot tub. Guests can also take advantage of the 24-hour fitness center or the well-equipped business center. The hotel's Sports Bar and Grill serves American cuisine for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Guestrooms at the Midland Holiday Inn offer 32-inch flat-screen TVs. Guests will also enjoy in-room microwaves, mini-refrigerators and coffeemakers.
