Description

The hotel's AguaMarinaSpa has a hydrotherapy pool, Scottish shower and hammam. There is also an indoor pool with waterfall and for children there is a mini club.

Rooms at the Gran Hotel Liber & Spa Playa Golf feature air conditioning, a private balcony, a flat-screen TV and a fridge. They include a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The La Marea restaurant serves a daily buffet breakfast and offers international food for lunch and dinner. Pleamar Café serves drinks and light meals throughout the day.

The Cabárceno Natural Park, which is an outdoor zoo, is about 45 minutes away by car. The hotel is easily accessible from the A8 motorway.
