Description

The rooms at Hotel Bahía are spacious and feature satellite plasma TV, a minibar, views of the sea or Santander Cathedral and a marble bathroom with toiletries and a hairdryer.

A varied buffet breakfast, à la carte dishes and the daily menu are served in the Bahia restaurant. The hotel cafeteria overlooks the bay.

The hotel is a 10-minute walk from the city's main shops and a good selection of restaurants, and 1.5 km from the Santander Convention Center.
