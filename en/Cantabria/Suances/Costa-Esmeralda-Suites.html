Description

The property occupies a traditional stone building with a French-style country décor. The rooms have a private bathroom with a hairdryer, free toiletries, bath and shower. The hotel also has an outdoor pool and outdoor hot tub that is open from June to October.

This area of Spain, known for its pleasant climate and lush vegetation, is in the narrow strip between the Picos de Europa and the Bay of Biscay. In the town of Suances you can find several bars, shops and a fishing port.
