Description

The rooms at Castilla Termal Balneario de Solares are comfortable and modern and have natural light and large windows overlooking the park or the garden. They are equipped with air conditioning, minibar, TV and bathroom with bathtub, hairdryer and free toiletries.

In the hotel there is a spa, well-equipped fitness center, indoor pool and outdoor pool.

The restaurant at Castilla Termal Balneario de Solares serves dinner based on traditional regional specialties and international dishes.
