Description

The Thermas Hotel & Resort has spacious and bright rooms with air conditioning, 32-inch LCD TV and minibar. It also has a balcony, a safe and a bathroom with a hairdryer.

The establishment offers a swimming pool, a running track, facilities for playing squash, tennis and soccer, a children's club and a small farm. In addition, the price of the stay includes admission to the water park.

Planeta Água is located between the pools and offers refreshments and an underwater bar. The Moinhos serves Brazilian and international haute cuisine. There is also a sushi bar and Japanese cuisine.

The Thermas Hotel & Resort is a 10-minute drive from the center of Mossoró. It also has free parking.
