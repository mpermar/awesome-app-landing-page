Description

Paua Hotel's rooms feature a balcony with garden views, air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen cable TV, a fan, a minibar and a private bathroom with a shower and towels.

The property is 100 km from São Gonçalo do Amarante International Airport, 600 meters from the center of Pipa and 400 meters from bars and restaurants.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning, including various fresh fruits, breads and cold meats. In addition, regional specialties and a selection of drinks can be enjoyed in the hotel restaurant bar.
