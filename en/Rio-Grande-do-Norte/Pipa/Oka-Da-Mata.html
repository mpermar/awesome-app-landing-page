Description

The rooms at Oka Da Mata feature satellite TV, air conditioning and a large balcony overlooking the garden or the ocean.

The Oka serves a rich buffet breakfast and allows you to enjoy drinks and light snacks on its terrace. The Oka also houses a spacious lounge with a library and a TV room with a selection of DVDs. Private parking is free.

This property is 1 km from Pipa city center and organizes a free shuttle service to all Pipa beaches and the city center.

Mejoró expectativas
