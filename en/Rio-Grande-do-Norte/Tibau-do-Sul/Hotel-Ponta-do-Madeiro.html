Description

All hotel rooms have air conditioning, cable TV, minibar, safe and balcony. The bathroom is private and includes a shower, hairdryer and free toiletries. Some rooms have a hot tub.

This hotel has a restaurant and a shared TV room, as well as a 24-hour reception.

Hotel Ponta do Madeiro is 2.5 km from Dolphins Bay Beach, while the center of the town of Pipa is 4 km away. The nearest airport is Natal International Airport, 90 km away.
