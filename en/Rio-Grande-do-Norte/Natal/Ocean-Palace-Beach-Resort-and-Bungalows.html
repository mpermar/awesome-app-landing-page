Description

The rooms are spacious and bright and feature a balcony, colorful décor and local artwork. They are equipped with air conditioning, flat-screen cable TV and a minibar. Some offer views of the sea and others, of the dunes.

The Ocean Palace Bungalows & Beach Resort has a hot tub surrounded by tropical plants, sauna and massage service. You can attend Pilates classes. There is a kids club.

The complex houses a Brazilian restaurant specializing in the typical feijoada. Le Solei serves contemporary cuisine, while Sunrise prepares a rich breakfast buffet. There is also a sushi bar and a pizzeria.

The complex has free private parking. It is located in front of the Natal Convention Center, 3 km from the Morro do Careca and 38 km from the São Gonçalo do Amarante International Airport.
