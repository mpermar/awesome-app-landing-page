Description

Rooms at the Serhs Natal Grand Hotel feature a balcony with sea views, air conditioning, a minibar and a TV. The bathroom is private and includes a shower and a hairdryer. Room service is available 24 hours a day.

The Grand Hotel Serhs Natal has a sports center, fitness center, spa, games room and children's club.

The hotel is 4 km from Arena das Dunas, 9 km from the beach of the Artist. São Gonçalo do Amarante International Airport is 44 km away.
