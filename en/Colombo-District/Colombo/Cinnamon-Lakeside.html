Description

Guests can swim in the outdoor pool, or enjoy a massage at Azmaara Spa. The hotel also has tennis and racquetball courts and a gym. Exclusive to guests, The Library is a lounge by day and a club by night.

Stylish rooms have a flat-screen TV, safe and tea and coffee-making facilities. All rooms have a marble bathroom. Some rooms include a separate living area, executive privileges and a balcony.

Offering both indoor seating and outdoor lakeside dining, 7° North serves Mediterranean dishes, cocktails and wines. Other restaurants include Thai, Singaporean and Japanese options.

Cinnamon Lakeside Colombo is a 45 minute drive from Bandaranaike International Airport. Odel Department Store is one mile away.

Fort is a great choice for travelers interested in seafood, relaxation and sightseeing.
