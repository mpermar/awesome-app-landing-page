Description

Located less than 2. 5 km west of the Brandon city center, this hotel features an indoor pool with a two-story spiral waterslide.

Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the inn. Rooms at the Clarion Hotel & Suites are equipped with a cable TV and a DVD player.

A free selection of movies is available for guests’ viewing. A microwave, refrigerator and in-room coffee maker are also included.

Echo Restaurant and Wine Bar, located within the Clarion Hotel & Suites, serves meals throughout the day and offers more than 50 varieties of wine.

Houston’s Night Club is also open on the premises. The pool area includes a whirlpool and a children’s splash pool.

A fitness room is connected to the pool area and a mini-arcade is also located on-site.

Clarion Hotel & Suites is less than 2 km from Brandon University and less than 11 km from Brandon Airport.
