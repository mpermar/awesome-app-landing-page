Description

This Winnipeg, Manitoba hotel features an on-site bowling alley, restaurant and rooms with a microwave and refrigerator.

Canwest Park baseball stadium is only a 10-minute drive away. The Travelodge Winnipeg’s guest rooms include cable TV and free Wi-Fi.

The spacious rooms also offer a coffee maker and a work desk. Winnipeg East Travelodge features an indoor tropical courtyard with a pool, hot tub and sauna.

Doubles Fun Club is a large game room with pool tables, flat-screen TVs and video games.

Marquee Restaurant and Lounge is open all day and serves a variety of dishes. Room service is also available.

The Manitoba Children's Museum and Winnipeg Convention Center are a 10-minute drive away.

Assiniboine Park, which houses the Assinibone Park Zoo and Leo Mol Sculpture Garden, is a 20-minute drive away.
