Description

An iPod docking station and a CD player are provided in the rooms at Fairfield Inn and Suites by Marriott Winnipeg Airport. Each warmly decorated room includes a microwave, coffee-making facilities and a desk with an ergonomic chair.

Each morning a continental breakfast is served, which can also be enjoyed in the privacy of your room.

A fitness center is available in the hotel along with a business center. Guests also have access to the on-site laundromat. A 24-hour mini market can also be found on the premise.

Polo Park, Winnipeg's largest shopping center, is either a 20-minute walk or a 5-minute drive from Fairfield Inn and Suites by Marriott Winnipeg Airport. MTS Centre is a 10-minute drive away, and Western Canada Aviation Museum is 1 miles from the property.
