Description

Featuring an indoor saltwater pool, the Holiday Inn Winnipeg – Airport West is located 9 km from the Winnipeg Art Gallery.

Daily breakfast and on-site restaurants are available. Free WiFi is provided Flat-screen cable TVs with pay-per-view channels and video games are offered in each guest room at the Winnipeg – Airport West Holiday Inn.

A small refrigerator and a coffee maker are available. A small table with chairs is provided.

The on-site Holidome Recreational Facility features a fitness centre, children’s pool and play area.

A games room has air hockey tables and arcade games. A 24 hour business centre is available.

The S. O. S. Lounge features specialty coffees. Bell Bottoms Restaurant is open all day and serves contemporary cuisine, including a soup bar with 3 homemade soups.

The Winnipeg Convention Center is 12 minutes’ drive from this hotel. The historic Forks is 10 km away.
