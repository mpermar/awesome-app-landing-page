Description

Featuring a themed indoor pool with water slides, this Winnipeg hotel offers an on-site restaurant.

Guests can enjoy the gym or the free 24-hour business center. Free WiFi is provided throughout the property.

Victoria Inn Hotel and Convention Center Winnipeg features a 37" LCD digital TV and a coffee maker in every air-conditioned guest room.

A work desk and ironing facilities are also provided. Chicago Joe's Restaurant at the Victoria Inn Winnipeg is open daily for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

A sports bar and room service are also available. This hotel features a 24-hour complimentary shuttle to and from Winnipeg James Armstrong Richardson International Airport, just 500 metres away.

Assiniboine Zoo is only 15 minutes' drive away. Assiniboine Golf Club is 3. 5 km from the property.
