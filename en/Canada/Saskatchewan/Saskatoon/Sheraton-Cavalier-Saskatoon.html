Description

This hotel is next to the South Saskatchewan River, in Saskatoon's Central Business District.

The hotel offers an indoor water park, on-site dining and free WiFi. Sheraton Cavalier Saskatoon features completely non-smoking guest rooms with flat-screen TVs and Starbucks coffee.

Designer bath amenities are also included. The Saskatoon Sheraton Cavalier's River Country Water Park offers 1 water slide, a large main pool and a children's pool.

Guests can use the 2 hot tubs or the 24-hour gym. Carver's Steakhouse at the Sheraton in Saskatoon is open every evening for dinner.

Guests can have a cup of coffee or a light meal at the hotel's Windows and The Express.
