Description

Located in the centre of Silver Star Mountain Village, this resort features a ski school and ski-to-door access.

Vernon is 24 km away. The Vance Creek Hotel & Conference Centre features a flat-screen TV and free WiFi in all accommodation.

The bright, open rooms and suites feature a wood dining table and country furnishings.

Guests can relax in the rooftop hot tub at this hotel. Boutique shops, heated ski lockers and a launderette are available on site.

The Bakery Cafe and 1609 Restaurant offer on-site dining at the Vance Creek Hotel.

The hotel is located on the grounds of Silver Star Provincial Park. Spallumcheen Golf Course & Country Club is 45 minutes’ drive from this property.
