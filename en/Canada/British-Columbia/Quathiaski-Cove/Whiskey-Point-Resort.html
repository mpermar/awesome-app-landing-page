Description

Along with a bay window with harbor views, each room at Whiskey Point Resort features a flat-screen TV with cable. All rooms with private bathrooms include tea and coffee-making facilities.

Offering views of Discovery Passage, a seaside gazebo is on site. Four covered decks complete with a sitting area and BBQ facilities provide spacious outdoor entertainment space.

Whiskey Point Resort Quadra Island is 1.5 miles from Ferry Terminal. Campbell River Airport is 7 miles away.
