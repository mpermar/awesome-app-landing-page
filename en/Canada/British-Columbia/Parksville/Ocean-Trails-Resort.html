Description

Situated on 7 acres, this Parksville property overlooks Rathrtrevor Beach. A seasonal outdoor pool and a hot tub are provided.

A balcony is offered in each unit. Brigadoon Golf Course is 3 km away. Free WiFi and a flat-screen TV are provided in all rooms at Ocean Trails Resort.

A full kitchen is included. An en suite bathroom offers free toiletries for added convenience.

A guest launderette is available to guests of Ocean Trails Parksville Resort. A tennis court and a children’s playground offer outdoor recreation.

Guests can enjoy free passes to Jim’s Gym fitness centre. Englishman River Falls Provincial Park is 25 minutes’ drive away.
