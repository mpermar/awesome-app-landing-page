Description

The Beach Club Resort features a private balcony and flat-screen TV in every studio and suite. A DVD player, small refrigerator and coffee maker are also provided in each non-smoking accommodation. One-bedroom suites come with a full kitchen.

Guests can use the swimming pool and the hot tub at the Beach Club. A gym and business center are available. The hotel also offers free underground parking.

Pacific Prime Steak and Chop Restaurant & Lounge at The Beach Club offers a beachfront patio next to the Parksville Beach Boardwalk. The restaurant offers ocean and mountain views.
