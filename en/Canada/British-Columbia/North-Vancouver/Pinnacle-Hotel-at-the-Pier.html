Description

Flat-screen TVs and spacious work desks feature in each guest room at Pinnacle Hotel at the Pier. Mini-refrigerators, microwaves and a free tea and coffee service are offered. Vancouver city skyline views are offered from select rooms.

In the evening, guests can enjoy fresh seafood and a variety of regional product at the hotel's restaurant.

Pinnacle at the Pier Hotel features a fitness center with a yoga studio and wellness center. Guests can relax in the sauna or the steam room, and then enjoy the outdoor deck. Free bikes are available to explore the surroundings.

Grouse Mountain and Capilano Suspension Bridge Park are both within 15 minutes’ drive from this hotel. Lonsdale Quay is 1,300 feet away.
