A flat-screen TV with cable is featured in all rooms at Best Western Cranbrook Hotel. Each air-conditioned room offers tea and coffee-making facilities. Select rooms include a kitchenette.

An indoor swimming pool is featured at Cranbrook Hotel Best Western. Guests can enjoy breakfast and lunch at True Root restaurant. A business center is available. Guests can relax on the sun terrace and garden.
