Description

Summerland Waterfront Resort features a seasonal outdoor pool and year-round hot tub. Guests can also take advantage of the fitness facilities and the full-service spa.

Water sport rentals are available only steps from the Summerland Resort. Guests can play a game of golf or take a winery tour minutes from the hotel. Skiing is also available nearby.
