Description

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool and a hot tub, each apartment at this Whistler Village North property includes a kitchen.

Free WiFi is provided. Whistler Olympic Plaza is 4 minutes' walk away. A flat-screen TV and a fireplace are offered in the lounge of each unit at Twin Peaks by Whistler Vacation Club.

A washing machine and dryer are included in each unit. Some units have more than one bathroom.

Underground parking allows guest direct access to their apartment. Ski equipment is available for hire on site.

Guests also have access to a barbecue. Blackcomb Excalibur Gondola is 1 km away. Squamish Lil'wat Cultural Centre is 10 minutes’ walk away from this property.
