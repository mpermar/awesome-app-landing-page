Description

A 37" flat-screen cable TV is offered in each guest room at Hilton Whistler Resort & Spa. A work desk and coffee- and tea-making facilities are provided. An private bathroom features bathrobes and slippers.

The Whistler Hilton features on-site ski and bike rental services as well as a modern fitness center. Guests can also enjoy on-site dining at the Cinnamon Bear Bar and Grille.

The Blackcomb Excalibur Gondola is just 6 minutes' walk away. The Whistler Conference Center is 650 feet away.
