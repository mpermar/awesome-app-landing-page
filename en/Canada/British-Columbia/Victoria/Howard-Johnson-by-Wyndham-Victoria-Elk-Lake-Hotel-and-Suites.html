Description

Located just off Highway 17 and less than 10 km from the Victoria city center, this Howard Johnson by Wyndham Victoria Elk Lake Hotel & Suites features a seasonal outdoor heated pool and an on-site restaurant.

Featuring a 42" flat-screen TV with HBO and Movie Central, rooms at theHoward Johnson by Wyndham Victoria Elk Lake Hotel & Suites are equipped with free high speed WiFi and include a microwave and a refrigerator.

An in-room coffee maker and a work desk are also available. The Lakes Grillhouse n’ Bar serves a variety of full and light meals throughout the day.

Guests can relax in the restaurant’s lounge area. The Howard Johnson by Wyndham Victoria Elk Lake Hotel & Suites features an indoor hot tub looking out toward the pool.

The front desk is open 24 hours to assist guests with any needs. The Howard Johnson by Wyndham Victoria Elk Lake Hotel & Suites is less than 15 minutes from the historic Butchart Gardens and less than 10 minutes from Cordova Bay Golf Course.
