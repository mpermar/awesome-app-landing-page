Description

A 42-inch satellite TV, a microwave and a fridge are offered in all of the modern rooms at Best Western Plus Revelstoke. Each room includes a desk and tea and coffee-making facilities.

A complimentary breakfast features items like fresh waffles, eggs, bacon ham or sausage, cereal, muffins, oatmeal, toast, selection of juices, tea and fresh brewed local coffee.

A well equipped modern fitness center that offers massage services is located on site at Revelstoke Best Western Plus. A business center with fax and photocopying services is available. A 24-hour reception is offered and features complimentary newspapers.

Revelstoke Mountain Resort is 5 mi away from this hotel. Revelstoke Golf Club is 10 minutes’ drive away.
