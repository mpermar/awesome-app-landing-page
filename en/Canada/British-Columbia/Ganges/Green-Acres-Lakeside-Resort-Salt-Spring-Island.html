Description

Located on the shores of St Mary Lake, this 7 acre resort is located on Salt Spring Island.

Free Wi-Fi and a kitchen are featured in each accommodation. Ganges is 10 minutes' drive away.

Each accommodation at Green Acres Lakeside Resort Salt Spring Island offers a private deck.

A comfortably furnished lounge offers a fireplace and cable TV. Lakeside Resort Green Acres offers beach volleyball, a covered recreation deck, and a children’s jungle gym.

A boat dock is also on-site. Ferries in Long Harbour are a 20-minute drive from the resort and can take guests to several islands nearby.
