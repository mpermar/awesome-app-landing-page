Description

Situated on Upper Arrow Lake, this Nakusp resort offers panoramic views. A wellness spa and 3 hot springs pools are on site.

A balcony is included in all rooms. Nakusp Golf Club is 40 km away. A flat-screen cable TV is provided in units at Halcyon Hot Springs Village and Spa.

Free Wi-Fi and a seating area are offered. A refrigerator is included. Select units feature a full kitchen.

Boasting fresh, local ingredients, King Fisher Restaurant is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Menu items include seafood, pasta, and meat dishes. Guests can also enjoy a drink in the fireside lounge.

Featuring a spacious private beach, Halcyon Hot Springs features activities like kayaking and canoeing.

After enjoying the hot springs, guests can swim in the freshwater pool. A guest terrace is also offered.

McDonald Creek Provincial Park is 40 minutes’ drive away. Revelstoke Mountain is 77 km from Halcyon Hot Springs Village and Spa.
