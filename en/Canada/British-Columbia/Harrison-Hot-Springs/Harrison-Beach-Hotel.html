Description

This Harrison Hot Springs hotel is located across the road from Harrison Lake. The hotel offers a gym and rooms with free WiFi.

Harrison Beach Hotel rooms and pet-friendly suites offer a cooking area with kitchenware.

The rooms are also equipped with air conditioning. Guests at the Harrison Hotel can swim in the indoor pool or use the fitness centre or outdoor hot tub.

The hotel offers a seasonal restaurant. Beach Hotel Harrison is within a 30-minute drive of Sasquatch Provincial Park.

The Agassiz Speedway is 19 km from the hotel, while Sandpipper Golf Resort is 22 km away.
