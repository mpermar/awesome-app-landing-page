Description

Placed at the foot of Mt. Currie, 28 minutes north of Whistler Ski Resort, this all-suite hotel in Pemberton Valley offers accommodations with kitchens and free WiFi.

An outdoor pool and a hot tub are featured on site. Pemberton Valley Lodge features one-bedroom suites and studios with stunning mountain views.

Guests will enjoy 32" TVs, DVD players and free wireless high-speed internet access.

Select suites and studios boast fireplaces. Free local calling is included. A free shuttle to Whistler Ski Resort is provided, leaving in the morning and returning in the evening.

Mountain bikes are available for guest use in the summer. Snowshoes are available for guests in the winter.

Free luggage storage, safety deposit boxes and underground parking are included. Nairn Falls Provincial Park is only 5 minutes' drive and Joffre Lakes Provincial Park is only 30 minutes' drive away.
