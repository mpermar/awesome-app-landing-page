Description

A flat-screen cable TV is offered in all rooms at Comfort Suites Kelowna. A work desk and a coffee maker are provided. A recharge station includes 2 USB ports and 3 electric outlets.

Daily breakfast at Kelowna Comfort Suites includes a waffle station. Guests can take advantage of the fitness center at this family-friendly hotel.

Gyro Beach Park on Okanagan Lake is only 5 mi from the property. Kelowna Golf & Country Club is only 8 minutes' drive away.

Any additional older children or adults are charged CAD 20 per person per night when using existing beds.
One child under 2 years is charged CAD 10 per night in a crib.
The maximum number of cribs in a room is 1.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
