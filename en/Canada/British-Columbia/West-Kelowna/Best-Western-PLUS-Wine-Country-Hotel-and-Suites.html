Description

Next door to 3 Eagles Golf Club, this hotel in West Kelowna, British Columbia offers an indoor pool with a 12 metre waterslide.

Mission Hill Family Estate Winery is 4 km away. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

A 32" flat-screen TV is featured in each guestroom at the Wine Country Best Western.

A microwave and refrigerator are also provided. Free toiletries are included in the spacious private bathroom.

Best Western Wine Country is a completely non-smoking hotel with a fitness center and 24-hour business center.

Guests will also appreciate the free continental breakfast and newspaper every morning.

Shannon Lake Golf Club is 8 minutes' drive from this hotel. Penticton is 50 km away.
