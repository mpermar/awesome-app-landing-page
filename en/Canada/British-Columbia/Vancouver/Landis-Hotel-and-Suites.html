Description

A flat-screen cable TV and a DVD player are provided in suites at Landis Hotel & Suites. A seating area and a work desk are included. An private bathroom offers free toiletries for added convenience.

A 24-hour concierge and a tour desk are offered at Hotel & Suites Landis. Guests can visit a variety of city center attractions via the free shuttle.

Canada Place is 6 minutes' drive away. Vancouver Art Gallery and shopping on Robson Street are 2,450 feet from Landis Hotel & Suites.
