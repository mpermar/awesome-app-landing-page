Description

Penticton Regional Airport is just a 6-minute drive from this 100% non-smoking hotel, which features indoor and outdoor pools, a hot tub and playground.

Its spacious guest rooms offer free Wi-Fi. A microwave, fridge and coffee maker are provided in the contemporary, warm-colored rooms at Best Western Inn at Penticton.

Each room has a flat-screen TV, ironing facilities, and a bathroom with a hairdryer.

Guests can enjoy a full breakfast that includes waffles, muffins and eggs. They can also relax by the barbecue pit.

Skaha Lake Park is a 10-minute walk from Best Western Inn at Penticton. Apex Mountain Ski Resort is a 30-minute drive away.
