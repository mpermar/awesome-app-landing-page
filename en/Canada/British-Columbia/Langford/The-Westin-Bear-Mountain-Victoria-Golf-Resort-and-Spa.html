Description

The Westin Bear Mountain Victoria Golf Resort & Spa has a relaxing natural environment, only steps from scenic hiking trails, exciting wildlife and breathtaking views. The center of Victoria, including the Royal BC Museum, is also just a short drive from the resort.

While staying at the Westin Bear Mountain Golf Resort and Spa, guests can enjoy state-of-the-art fitness facilities, an outdoor heated pool and a variety of dining options, including sushi, pub-fare and west coast classics.

Contemporary accommodations at the Westin Bear Mountain Resort feature kitchenettes or kitchens, private balconies and signature Heavenly beds. Guests will also enjoy in-room DVD players, as well as high-speed internet access.
