Description

Each modern suite at the Timberline features cable TV. A locker for storing sporting equipment is also included. Free WiFi is available.

Guests can relax in the sauna or work out in the gym. The King Fir spa offers massages and other spa treatments. Complimentary DVD rentals are also available. A games room and communal BBQs are provided. A free ski shuttle service is available for guests.

In the winter, guests can enjoy dog sledding, snowshoeing and sleigh rides. Mountain biking, golf and whitewater rafting are popular in the summer months.

Fernie city center is only 10 minutes' drive away.
