Description

Located off Highway 40, this hotel is near Galeries de la Capitale and 10 km from Old Québec.

A gym and an indoor pool with water playground are featured. Guest rooms include a flat-screen cable TV.

The rooms at Hôtel & Suites Le Dauphin Québec are furnished with a fridge, microwave and coffee maker.

Every room includes a DVD player and a computer equipped with internet access. A work desk is also provided.

A healthy breakfast buffet is offered at The Dauphin Quebec Hotel & Suites. Laval University is 8.
