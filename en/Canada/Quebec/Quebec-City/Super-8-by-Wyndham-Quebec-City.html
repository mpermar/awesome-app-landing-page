Description

Super 8 by Wyndham Quebec City is minutes from Jean Lesage International Airport and Highway 138.

This non-smoking hotel offers an indoor pool with an 80-foot water slide. Super 8 by Wyndham Quebec City features rooms with cable TV and a work desk.

Rooms are also equipped with microwaves, mini-refrigerators and coffee makers. This property provides a free continental breakfast each morning, featuring cold breakfast items.

Laundry facilities and free parking are available at the Super 8 by Wyndham Quebec City.

Parc Aquarium de Quebec and the Quebec City Convention Center are within driving distance of the hotel.
