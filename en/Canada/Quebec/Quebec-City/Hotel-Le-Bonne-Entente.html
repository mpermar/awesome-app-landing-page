Description

Featuring a seasonal outdoor pool, a restaurant and a bar, Hôtel Le Bonne Entente is 6 km from Laval University.

Each room comes with a flat-screen cable TV and a work desk. A coffee machine and safety deposit box are included at each room at Le Bonne Entente Hotel.

The bathroom includes a bathrobe, free toiletries and a hair dryer. Guests can also enjoy room service.

The hotel provides a 24-hour front desk, tour desk, concierge service and laundry services.

A variety of children's activities are offered at the Fun Club. The on-site steakhouse restaurant features gastronomic meals.

The spa and wellness centre features a gym, hot tub and sauna. Massage services are also available.

The hotel is 7 km from Quebec City Jean Lesage International Airport. It is also 4 km from Aquarium du Québec and 14 km from Old Quebec.
