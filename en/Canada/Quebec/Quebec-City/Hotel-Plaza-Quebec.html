Description

Offering a spa centre and hot tub, Hotel Plaza Quebec is located in Quebec City, 200 metres from Parc Aquarium du Quebec.

The hotel has a children's playground and sun terrace, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. Grande Allee is 6 km from Hotel Plaza Quebec, while Battlefields Park Parc des Champs de Bataille is 7 km away.
