Description

Situated in Sutton, 39 km from Magog-Orford, Huttopia Sutton. The main chalet has a restaurant, lounge, games for children, an outdoor pool and an indoor heated pool.

Free private parking is available on site. There is a seating and/or dining area in some units.

There is also a kitchenette/dining area, equipped with a fridge and select rooms offer a wood stove.

Towels and bed linen are offered. Guests can make a bonfire. Guests can enjoy the on-site bar and restaurant.

A number of activities are offered in the area, such as hiking and biking. Bromont is 24 km from Huttopia Sutton, while Jay Peak is 48 km from the property.
