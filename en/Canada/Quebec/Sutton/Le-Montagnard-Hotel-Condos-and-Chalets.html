Description

Hôtel & Condos Le Montagnard is a mountain resort located at the foot of Mount Sutton, offering hotel rooms, studios, apartments and chalets.

An outdoor pool and year-round hot tub are featured on site. The Hotel & Condos Le Montagnard offers spacious units with a wood fireplace and a flat-screen cable TV.

All include a refrigerator and coffee maker. A bar with a billiards table is open for Le Montagnard guests’ enjoyment.

Meeting and reception facilities are available on site. A volleyball and badminton court, children’s playground and fire pit are among additional on-site facilities.
