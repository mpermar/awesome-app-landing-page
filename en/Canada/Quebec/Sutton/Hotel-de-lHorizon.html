Description

Offering a sun terrace, indoor pool and a ski pass sales point, Hotel de l'Horizon is set in Sutton.

Guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Free WiFi and free private parking are available on site.

Cabins are equipped with a wood stove. The rooms come with a private bathroom, free toiletries and a hair dryer.

A range of activities are offered in the area, such as skiing and hiking. Magog is 39 km from Hotel de l'Horizon, while Granby is 33 km from the property.
