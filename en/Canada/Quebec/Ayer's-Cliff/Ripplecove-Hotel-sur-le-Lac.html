Description

Situated 75 minutes from Montreal and 15 minutes from Vermont, Ripplecove Hotel offers an ideal location with its 12-acre land directly on Lake Massawippi, Quebec.

This 5-star hotel boasts a full-service spa and a gourmet restaurant. Each unique room features views of the lake or the hotel’s landscaped gardens.

An iPod docking station, cable TV and a seating area are all provided. Rooms come with free WiFi.

Ripplecove’s on-site restaurant "Le Riverain" features innovative regional and international cuisine, an extensive wine list and live piano music.

Ripplecove's Arboressence Spa provides various therapies and beauty treatments, as well as an outdoor hot tub overlooking the lake.

Guests can also use the tennis courts and heated outdoor swimming pool. This luxury hotel offers various activities on site, including tennis, boating, swimming, cycling, snow-shoeing, skating and ice fishing.
