Description

Offering an outdoor pool and views of the mountain, Hôtel Valcartier is set in Saint-Gabriel-De-Valcartier.

The resort has an outdoor pool and terrace, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

Free private parking is available on site. Certain rooms have a private bathroom with a spa bath, while others have bathrobes and slippers.

Certain units feature a seating area where you can relax. Enjoy a cup of coffee from your terrace or balcony.

Hôtel Valcartier features free WiFi throughout the property. A flat-screen TV with cable channels is offered.

There is a 24-hour front desk, a cash machine and gift shop at the property. You can play billiards at the resort.

Quebec City is 22 km from Hôtel Valcartier, while Stoneham is 11 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Québec City Jean Lesage International Airport, 18 km from Hôtel Valcartier.
