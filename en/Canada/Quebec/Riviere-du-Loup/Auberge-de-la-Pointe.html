Description

Each guest room at Auberge de la Pointe includes a coffee maker and refrigerator. Guests can watch cable TV or enjoy the view of either the river or the city.

An indoor pool, hot tub, and gym are part of this pet-friendly hotel. Concierge services and free bike rentals are available.

The L’Espadon Dining Room specializes in seafood and has a large fireplace and views of the river.

Pointe-de-Rivière -du-Loup and the ferry to Saint-Simon are about one mile from Rivière -du-Loup Auberge de la Pointe.
