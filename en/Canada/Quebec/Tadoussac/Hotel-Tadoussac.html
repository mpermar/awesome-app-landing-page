Description

Overlooking Tadoussac Bay and the Saguenay Fjord, this beautiful historic hotel features a wellness area offering massages and body treatments.

Free WiFi and a choice of 2 restaurants are also available. A flat-screen cable TV and a coffee maker are found in each room at Hotel Tadoussac.

A work desk and iron facilities are also provided. Guests can reserve a massage, enjoy the seasonal outdoor pool or play a game of tennis at the Tadoussac Hotel.

A games room, billiards and mini-golf are also offered. Dinner can be enjoyed at Le William Restaurant and a la carte items are served in Le Coverdale Dining Room.

There is also an on-site bar, Le Coquart. Activities such as hiking, biking, sea kayaking and whale watching are located nearby.

The Tadoussac Tourism Office is 1 minute from the hotel. Free shuttle service is offered to local cruises.
