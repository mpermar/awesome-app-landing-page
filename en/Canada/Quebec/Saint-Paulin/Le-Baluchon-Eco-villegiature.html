Description

Auberge Le Baluchon is located in Saint-Paulin. The resort offers a variety of outdoor activities including tennis, skiing, cycling, horse riding and nature walks.

Rooms include a refrigerator. Auberge Le Baluchon rooms include cozy bathrobes and a seating area.

The rooms are also equipped with free Wi-Fi. The Le Baluchon is a group of several different lodges, each with a lounge area, hot tub, and sauna.

Guests at the Auberge can relax at the spa or swim in the seasonal outdoor pool. The hotel also offers barbecue facilities.
