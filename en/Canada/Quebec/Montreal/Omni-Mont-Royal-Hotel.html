Description

An iHome clock radio and docking station is provided in each elegant room at Omni Mont-Royal Hotel. Select rooms include a city or Mont-Royal Park view. Suites come complete with a separate living area.

Featuring floor-to-ceiling windows, on-site Alice Bar serves a selection of cocktails and light snacks. Le Petit Opus Café showcases French bistro-style cuisine.

A fitness center is also available at Hotel Omni Mont-Royal.

McGill University is just a 2-minute walk away and Montreal Museum of Fine Arts is a 6-minute walk. Within a 10-minute walk, guests can also find a number of art galleries and restaurants.

Perfect location, excellent Omni property, friendly staff

"We enjoyed our 2 days spent at the Omni in Montreal for many reasons but the top being the excellent location (we could easily walk or drive to a lot sights and neighborhoods). The valet staff was very friendly, as was the concierge staff - because of them, we discovered the best bagels (in the world), the most lovely children's park in Mont Royal, and the easiest way to get to the freeway vs. how our phone app was routing us. The hotel overall was a standard Omni experience - well decorated, professional staff, clean rooms, and nice bonuses such as their pool and hot tub. We were traveling with our 7 month old baby and our room was the perfect size for the addition of a crib -- it didn't feel cramped at all. Our bathroom was generously sized. We stayed in a second hotel in Montreal later on our Canada trip (Courtyard Marriott Downtown) and we definitely wished we had booked the Omni again instead."
