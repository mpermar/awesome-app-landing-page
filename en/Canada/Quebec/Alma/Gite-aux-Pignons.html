Description

All rooms at the bed and breakfast are equipped with a dishwasher, a coffee machine, a hot tub, free toiletries, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and Blu-ray player. Rooms are complete with a shared bathroom equipped with a bath, while certain rooms will provide you with a balcony. The rooms include a desk.

Breakfast is available every morning, and includes American and vegetarian options.

The property has a hot tub. Guests at Gite aux Pignons will be able to enjoy activities in and around Alma, like cycling, canoeing and hiking.

Chicoutimi is 30 miles from the accommodation.
