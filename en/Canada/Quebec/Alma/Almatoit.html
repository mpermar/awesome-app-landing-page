Description

The cozy rooms at Almatoit feature rustic décor. Comfortable bath towels are provided each day and a telephone is included for guest convenience.

Almatoit guests can cycle at Veloroute des Bleuets (Blueberries Bike Route) 2.2 miles from the property, or shop at Le Centre Alma (Alma Shopping Center) 0.8 miles from the property. Lac Saint-Jean (Lake Saint-Jean) is a 34-minute drive away.
