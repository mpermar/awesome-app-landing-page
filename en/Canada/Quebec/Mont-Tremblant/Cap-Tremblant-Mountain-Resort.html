Description

The spacious vacation homes of Cap Tremblant Mountain Resort feature a jet tub and a large, private balcony. A full-size kitchen, cable TV and free Wi-Fi are also included.

The Ocean Pavilion Spa at Cap Tremblant provides a variety of massage treatments and whirlpools.

A 60-foot water slide, 3 tennis courts and 2 golf simulators can be enjoyed at the resort. Kids can also play at the amusement park or watch for deer that visit the area.

The il Pinnacolo restaurant serves fine Italian cuisine in a greenhouse dining room. Guests can also sit at the solid wood bar or in front of the natural stone fireplace.
