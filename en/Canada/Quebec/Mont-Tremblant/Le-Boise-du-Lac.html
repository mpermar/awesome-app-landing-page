Description

Nestled among lush forests on the picturesque shore of Lake Duhamel, Le Boisé du Lac is 5 minutes’ drive from downtown Mont-Tremblant.

A continental breakfast is served every morning. Free WiFi is offered on-site at the hotel.

The property also features a private beach with canoes, pedal boats, kayaks and rowing boats, all provided to guests for free.

Barbecue pits, picnic areas and a children’s playground are featured behind the hotel.

A communal kitchen gives guests the option to prepare their own meals. The dining room opens up to an outdoor deck, which overlooks the lake and mountains.

Activities in the hotel include fishing, swimming, volleyball, table tennis, basketball and soccer.
