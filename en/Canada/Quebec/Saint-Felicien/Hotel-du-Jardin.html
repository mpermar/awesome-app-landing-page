Description

Located in the heart of Saint-Félicien, Hôtel du Jardin features an on-site restaurant, indoor pool and fitness centre.

Free in-room Wi-Fi is included. The Saint-Félicien Zoo is 15 km away. A 27-inch TV with cable is standard in all rooms at Hôtel du Jardin Saint-Félicien.

A coffee maker, work desk and free local calls are included. The on-site Oasis Restaurant serves a variety of food, including seafood and regional cuisine.

Featuring a pub-style ambience, Bistro-Bar l’Éden is equipped with a flat-screen TV and fireplace.

After a workout at the fitness centre, guests can relax in the hot tub. Massage services are also available on site.

Arbre en Arbre zip lining, a zoo and other activities are just 10 minutes’ drive from Hôtel du Jardin.
