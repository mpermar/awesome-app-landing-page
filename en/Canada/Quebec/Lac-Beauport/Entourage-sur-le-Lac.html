Description

Set in Lac-Beauport, 1. 4 km from Le Relais, Surround-on-Lake features a private beach area.

This 4-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk, a shared lounge and free WiFi. There is a restaurant.

The rooms at the hotel are fitted with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a shower.

Rooms come with a coffee machine, while some have a balcony. The units will provide guests with a microwave.

A Continental, Vegetarian or American breakfast can be enjoyed in the breakfast area.

At Entourage sur-le-Lac you will find a restaurant serving local cuisine. Guests must reserve their table ahead of arrival.

The accommodation offers 4-star accommodation with a fitness centre and seasonal outdoor pool.

You can play billiards at Entourage sur-le-Lac. There is also a business centre and a babysitting service on site.
