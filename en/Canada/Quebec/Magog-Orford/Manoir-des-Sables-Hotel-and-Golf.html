Description

This Orford, Quebec hotel overlooks Lake Ecluse. The hotel has a golf course located on the property and a full-service spa.

Manoir des Sables rooms include free Wi-Fi and a seating area. The rooms also have a work desk.

Guests at the Manoir can swim in the indoor pool and then relax in the sauna and outdoor jacuzzi.

The hotel also has a tennis court and gym. The hotel offers snowshoeing and cross-country ski trails on-site.

Les Jardins offers seasonal dining on the terrace overlooking Mount Orford. The Manior des Sables has two bars the Albatros Bar, near the golf course and Le Pub, which offers a casual meeting place.

The Manoir is 3 miles from Mont Orford Skiing. Mont-Orford National Park is 1. 5 miles from the hotel.
