Description

Located in Magog, Condos Memphrémagog offers apartments located along Lake Memphrémagog.

An indoor and outdoor pool are available for guest use. A fully equipped kitchen, linen and towels are standard in all apartments at Condos Memphrémagog.

Cable TV and a DVD player are included. A fireplace is featured in select units. A private beach on Lake Memphrémagog is accessible to all guests.

A sauna and hot tub offer relaxation options. Badminton and volleyball are among additional activities that are available on site.
