Description

This hotel in Orford is located directly at the base of Mount Orford and is less than 10 minutes’ drive from Magog.

The hotel features the gourmet restaurant Bistro 4 Saisons, the Burger Pub and the Dôme de Morphée Esthetic Spa.

A coffee maker, cable TV, refrigerator and free WiFi are included in all rooms at Espace 4 Saisons.

All rooms are appointed with simple designs, dark wood furniture and large windows.

A fireplace and/or spa bath are featured in select rooms. Most rooms also offer a kitchenette or fully equipped kitchen.

Guests also have free access to the hotel’s fitness room, swimming pool and hot tub, as well as the private home theatre, games room and lounge.

Free outdoor parking available, and indoor heated parking is also available with extra fees.

Mount Orford National Park is located directly next to Espace 4 Saisons. Lake Orford is 5 minutes’ drive away.
