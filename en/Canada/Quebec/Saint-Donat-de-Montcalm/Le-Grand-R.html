Description

Le Grand R offers accommodation in Saint-Donat-de-Montcalm. There is a restaurant and bar and guests can have fun at the games room.

Mont-Tremblant National Park is 26 km away. Free WiFi is offered throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

All units include a satellite flat-screen TV. Some units are air conditioned and include a seating area with a TV and DVD player.

There is also a kitchen, fitted with a dishwasher. An oven, a microwave and toaster are also available, as well as a coffee machine.

There is a private bathroom with free toiletries in each unit. Towels are provided.

Le Grand R also includes an indoor pool. Guests can enjoy a drink at the on-site snack bar, while special diet menus and packed lunches are available on request.

The property has a private beach area and ski equipment hire is available. The area is popular for skiing and cycling.

The area is popular for canoeing and hiking. Mont Tremblant International Airport is 49 km from the property.
