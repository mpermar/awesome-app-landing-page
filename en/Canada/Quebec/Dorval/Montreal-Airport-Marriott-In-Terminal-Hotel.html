Description

A flat-screen TV and a mini-fridge are standard in all modern rooms at the Montreal Airport Marriott In-Terminal Hotel. The rooms also include an in-room safe and a coffee maker with free coffee and tea.

The Montreal Airport Marriott In-Terminal Hotel offers spa services. Fitness and business facilities are on-site for guest convenience.

Dine at the Bijou Restaurant and Bar, serving international food made with fresh, local ingredients. Illy Cafe is also on site.

Morgan Arboretum and the Cosmodome are within a 25-minute drive of this hotel. Dorval Municipal Golf Club is just 1 miles away.
