Description

Located on the shore of Lake Morency, this hotel is 15 minutes' drive from the Mont Olympia Ski Area.

It features an on-site spa, canoe rentals, and rooms with therapeutic tubs. The accommodations at Auberge du Lac Morency are spacious and include cable TV and free WiFi.

A refrigerator and coffee maker are provided. Chez Hippolyte, the elegant on-site restaurant, offers gourmet dishes and views of Lake Morency.

The wine cellar stores more than 500 varieties of wine. ATV and snowmobile rentals are available during the winter months at this Saint Hyppolite hotel.

Tennis courts, hiking trails, and indoor and outdoor pools are located on the property.

Free parking is also available. The Shawbridge Golf Club is 9 kms and the Piedmont Golf Club is 15 km from Auberge du Lac Morency.
