Description

Each of the traditional guest rooms at Auberge du Lac-à-l'Eau-Claire is equipped with a TV and a coffee maker. They are air-conditioned and include a hair dryer and ironing facilities.

An on-site gym, hammam, and large indoor lap pool are available at this resort’s wellness center. Spa treatments and massages are also available.

The Lac-à-l'Eau-Claire Auberge Fine Dining Room has a lake view and is open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. The bar, located next to the dining room, boasts a billiard table and a variety of cocktails.

Downtown Saint-Alexis-des-Monts is a 25-minute drive away.
