Description

Hotel Bromont is just 2 km from Ski Bromont and Golf Bromont and near other major ski hills and golf courses.

An on-site indoor pool and hot tub are available all year round. The contemporary rooms feature views and free WiFi.

They have a fridge and coffee-making facilities. Many rooms offer a balcony, and rooms with a spa bath are available.

Guests can enjoy a continental breakfast that includes muffins, bagels and fresh fruits.

During the summer, they can dine on the terrace. Vignoble La Mission is 15 minutes’ drive from Bromont Hotel.
