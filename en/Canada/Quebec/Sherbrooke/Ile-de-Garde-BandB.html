Description

Nestled in Sherbrooke’s historic neighbourhood, this European-style B&B showcases local art and is 7 minutes’ walk from the Sherbrooke Museum of Fine Arts.

All guest rooms include a private bathroom. Featuring hardwood floors and colourful accents, each room at Île de Garde is equipped with free Wi-Fi.

A hairdryer and toiletries are provided with all rooms. Appointed with several arched windows, Île de Garde B&B features lots of natural light.

In warm months, guests can relax on the terrace. A children’s playground is also available on site.

The Granada Theatre is 15 minutes’ walk from this B&B. The Orford Express, a tourist train with scenic views of the Eastern Townships, is 20 minutes’ walk away.
