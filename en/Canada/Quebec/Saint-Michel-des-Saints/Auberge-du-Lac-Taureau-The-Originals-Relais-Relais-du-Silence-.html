Description

Boasting spa services, fine dining, and a variety of summer and winter activities, this waterfront resort in Saint-Michel-des-Saints, Quebec features views of Lake Taureau.

The rustic guest rooms at Auberge du Lac Taureau, The Originals Relais (Relais du Silence) include stained hardwood flooring and a marble bathroom.

A cable TV, free WiFi and a coffee maker are provided in each room. During their stay, guests can relax at the indoor pool, hot tub, or sauna.

Guests can also go canoeing, cycling, bear watching, and dogsledding. In the evenings, gourmet cuisine is served in the dining room.

The on-site bistro offers salads, soups, and sandwiches starting at noon. The bistro's bar has a full-service bar and billiard tables.
