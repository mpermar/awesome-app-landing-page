Description

All rooms in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV. The rooms are equipped with a private bathroom and coffee machine, while certain rooms will provide you with a balcony and others also feature pool views. All guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

Breakfast is available daily, and includes à la carte, American and vegetarian options.

A variety of popular activities are available in the area around Rawley Resort, Spa & Marina, including skiing and cycling.

Barrie is 29 miles from the accommodation, while Orillia is 20 miles from the property.
