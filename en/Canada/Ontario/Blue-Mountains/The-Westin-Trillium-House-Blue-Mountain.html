Description

Situated in the heart of Blue Mountain Village at the base of Blue Mountain Ski Resort, this hotel features a heated outdoor pool.

The shores of Georgian Bay can be found within 5 minutes' drive. A French or full balcony showcasing mountain or village views is available in each spacious room.

Additional amenities include a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a comfortable seating area and a fireplace.

All units boast a kitchenette with granite countertops. 2 hot tubs offer a great place to relax at The Westin Trillium House.

In-room spa treatments can be arranged in advance. The hotel can arrange a variety of on-site and off-site activities for guests.

These vary according to season and include hiking, rock climbing, skiing and snow boarding.

Located on site, Oliver and Bonacini Café Grill is a modern restaurant that serves American cuisine and room service.

Guests can enjoy drink at the Oliver and Bonacini Bar. The Westin Trillium House is 4 km from Scandanave Spa and 9 km from the town of Collingwood.
