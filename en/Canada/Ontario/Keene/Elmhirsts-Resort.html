Description

Each rustic cottage at Elmhirst’s Resort is furnished with a flat-screen TV and DVD player. Guests can relax on the private outdoor deck or in the whirlpool tub. The kitchenette is equipped with a stovetop, full-size refrigerator, and dishwasher.

Guided ATV and snowmobile tours are offered at Elmhirst’s. Kayak and boat rentals are also available. Biking, hiking, and ski trails wind through the property.

Fresh-picked organic vegetables are used in the meals at the Hearthside Dining Room and The Wild Blue Yonder Pub boasts casual dining, a full-service bar, and a big screen TV.

Downtown Peterbough is 15.5 miles from Elmhirst Resort. Bellmere Winds Golf Club is a 15-minute drive away.
