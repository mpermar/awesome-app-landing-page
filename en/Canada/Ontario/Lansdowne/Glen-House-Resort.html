Description

A coffee maker is included in each room at Glen House Resort. Cable TV and a sofa bed are provided.

A tennis court, volleyball court, and gym are offered to guests at this Lansdowne resort. Bicycle rentals are available.

Pub-style food is served at the Club House Grill, part of Glen House. BBQ Express provides American fast food.

The 1000 Islands Charity Casino is within a 15-minute drive of the Glen House Resort. Saint Lawrence Islands National Park is 6.8 miles from the property.
