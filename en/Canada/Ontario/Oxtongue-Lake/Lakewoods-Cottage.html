Description

Overlooking Oxtongue Lake, this cottage resort is 10 km from Algonquin Park. It offers on-site access to a sandy beach and a fully equipped kitchen in each cosy cottage.

A TV is included in all waterfront cottages at Lakewoods Cottage Resort. Gas barbecue facilities and a picnic table are also provided.

Select cottages feature a gas fireplace. Guests can enjoy free use of canoes, kayaks and paddle boats.

Paddling the Oxtongue River to Ragged Falls is a popular activity. A children’s playground, volleyball court and horseshoe pits are also available.

The town of Huntsville is 30 km from Cottage Resort Lakewoods. Dwight Beach is 10 km away.
