Description

Situated close to Pearson International Airport and boasting one of the area's longest indoor waterslides, this family-friendly resort features comfortable accommodations and state-of-the-art amenities.

The Hilton Mississauga/Meadowvale is conveniently located close to Highway 401, which allows guests to easily explore nearby attractions.

A number of major business corporations are located nearby. The sites and attractions of downtown Toronto are also only 25 minutes' drive away.

Guests at the Hilton Mississauga/Meadowvale can enjoy dining at the on-site restaurant.
