Description

Each room at Fairfield Inn & Suites Sudbury is furnished with a sitting area and a work desk. The TVs come with channels such as HBO, ESPN and pay-per-view movies. Other amenities include ironing facilities and microwave/oven.

Guests can relax at the poolside hot tub. The fitness center has cardio equipment and free weights. The hotel also offers laundry and dry cleaning facilities.

A complimentary breakfast is served with fresh juice and fruit at the hotel’s lounge every day. Guests can use the barbecue facilities to grill snacks and meat.

New Sudbury Shopping Center is less than one mile away. The Timberwolf Golf Club is 2 miles driving by car. Free private parking is available on site.
