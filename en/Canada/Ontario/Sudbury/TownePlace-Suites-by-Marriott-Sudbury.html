Description

TownePlace Sudbury provides spacious studios with fully equipped kitchens. Each unit comes with a well designed desk and work area.

Guests at the Sudbury TownePlace can enjoy a buffet breakfast every morning. The hotel’s lounge serves light meals and beverages during the day, and there is coffee available in the lobby.

Enjoy a swim in TownePlace pool with a water slide or take advantage of the hotel’s cardio room. Other nearby activities include mini golf, jogging and bowling.

TownePlace Suites Sudbury is located minutes from Science North and Dynamic Earth. Complimentary on-site parking is possible.
