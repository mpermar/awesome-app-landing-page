Description

This property has two different resorts, Carriage Ridge & Carriage Hills which are a 2 minutes’ drive from each other.

Carriage Hills Resort has a year-round heated indoor-outdoor pool, an indoor sauna, and outdoor hot tub.

Carriage Ridge Resort has an indoor heated pool and hot tub. Both resorts are within a 5 minute drive to Horseshoe Ski Hills.

Both resorts have similar suite/studio layouts. Guest studios include kitchenettes with a sink, toaster, coffee-maker, under mount bar fridge and microwave.

Guest suites include a full kitchen with a full fridge, stove, dishwasher and microwave.

All suites also include a king bed, sofa bed and private balcony. Mount St. Louis Moonstone Ski Area is 12 km from Carriage Ridge Resort & Carriage Hills Resort.
