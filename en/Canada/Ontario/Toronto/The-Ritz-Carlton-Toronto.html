Description

All of the rooms and suites include WiFi, 42-inch LCD flat-screen HD TV, deep soaker tubs and floor-to-ceiling windows with heated perimeter flooring. Valet parking service is also available at this property.

The hotel has an on-site spa, Spa My Blend by Clarins. Guests can enjoy the complimentary indoor salt water pool, hot tub and sauna. The space also has a 24-hour fitness center and personal training sessions upon request.

The property has 3 different dining and bar outlets for guests and locals to enjoy. TOCA restaurant serves Italian inspired, handcrafted cuisine with many Canadian local ingredients. TOCA also has a Cheese Cave located in the space. Ritz Bar operates as a café in the mornings and as a cocktail bar in the evening. The Bar has a Peruvian street-food menu. DEQ Lounge has an extensive cocktail list and offers a Canadian comfort food menu.

During their stay, guests can also opt to have Club Lounge access for an additional daily cost. Located on the hotel’s 20th floor, the Club Lounge has a panoramic view of Toronto. With access they can enjoy daily breakfast, lunch, dinner and snack service – which includes premium alcohol and wine after 11:00.

The Ritz-Carlton, Toronto property is located in a key location with walkability to the iconic CN Tower, Ripley’s Aquarium, The Hockey Hall of Fame, Rogers Center, Air Canada Center and The Princess of Wales Theater.
