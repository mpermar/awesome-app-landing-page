Description

A large work desk and flat-screen TV are included to all rooms at Radisson Admiral Toronto Harbourfront. A pool, harbor or city view may also be featured in these rooms.

Showcasing regional ingredients, Watermark Restaurant specializes in Canadian cuisine. Light snacks are served at Radisson Admiral’s Watermark Lounge, along with local beers and wines.

A fitness center is among recreational facilities available at Toronto Harbourfront Radisson Admiral. Serenity Spa & Salon offers a range of body treatments and massages.

Union Station is about a 15-minute walk from this hotel. Several attractions, including the CN Tower, Hockey Hall of Fame and lively Chinatown, are also within a 15-minute walk of the property. Billy Bishop Toronto City Airport is just over 1 miles away.
