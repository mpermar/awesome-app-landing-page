Description

Spacious guest rooms at Hockley Valley Resort provide guests with a seating area and a work desk. Tea and coffee-making facilities and free upscale toiletries are available.

An outdoor & indoor pool is available for guests at the Resort Hockley Valley. A fitness center and 18 hole golf course are also on site. Guests can enjoy the peaceful outdoors with a guided snowshoe hike in the winter, and a ski hill with 15 runs. Ski and snowboard equipment are available for hire on site.

This culinary destination resort features Babbo, a lively lobby bar with an extensive wine list and dining options. Cabin, the resort's signature restaurant, embodies the farm to table philosophy with products from regional farmers. Guests can also enjoy ingredient's from the resort's fruit and vegetable garden.

Hockley Valley Provincial Nature Reserve is 15 minutes’ drive from the Hockley Valley Resort. Orangeville is less than 15 minutes’ drive away, and Luther Marsh Conservation area is 1 hours’ drive from the property.
