Description

A microwave, refrigerator, and coffee maker are included in each guest room at Embassy Suites Niagara Falls Fallsview. Cable TV with pay-per-view movies is provided.

The Keg Steakhouse & Bar offers views of the Falls and casual dining for lunch and dinner. Free snacks and beverages are available at the manager’s reception, along with coffee at the on-site Starbucks.

Guests can work out in the Embassy Suites’ on-site gym or relax in the hot tub.

Casino Niagara is less than one mile from the Fallsview Niagara Falls Embassy Suites. The Skylon Observation Tower is a 10 minute walk from the hotel.
