Description

Located in Collingwood, Ontario, this resort is nearby the Blue Mountains and the Georgian Bay.

This 750-acre resort features a full-service marina and spa along with an 18-hole golf course.

At the Living Stone Golf Resort, guest rooms include free Wi-Fi, a coffee maker and cable TV.

Select condos provide full kitchens and spa bathtubs. The Living Stone Golf Resort offers an adult pool and a family pool.

There are on-site tennis courts, and a children’s camp. Winter activities include cross-country skiing, snowshoeing and snowmobiling.

Memories Restaurant & Bar offers fine Italian cuisine at the Living Stone Golf Resort.
