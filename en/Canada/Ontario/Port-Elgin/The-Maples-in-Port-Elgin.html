Great little cottage in a friendly town close to all amenities.

"This property was amazing. The owners are very friendly. Everything is provided for your use during your stay so you really only need to bring food. There is a Walmart and Independent Grocery Store, Tim Hortons and Canadian Tire within two Kms of the property. You will also have access to a full kitchen, cable tv and wifi access which comes in handy if you have kids who are frequently bored or can't live without their iPads. The cottage also comes equipped with a BBQ and fire pit area. Firewood is included if you're willing to put in the work to chop it up for yourself. If you are into biking, this cottage is conveniently located close to many family friendly trails. For the price you pay, the accommodations are excellent. I will definitely book here again in the future."
