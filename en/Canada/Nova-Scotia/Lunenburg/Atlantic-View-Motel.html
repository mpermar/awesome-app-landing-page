Description

Offering a sea view, all rooms includes cable TV and a private bathroom at Motel Atlantic View. Tea and coffee-making facilities are provided.

Guests can enjoy a terrace at Atlantic View Motel. A laundromat is also on site.

Kejimkujik National Park and National Historic Site is 53 miles away. Golfing opportunities at Bluenose Golf Club are less than a 5-minute drive away.
