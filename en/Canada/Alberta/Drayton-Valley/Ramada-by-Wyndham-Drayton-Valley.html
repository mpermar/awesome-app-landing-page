Description

A DVD player and free Wi-Fi are included in each room at Ramada Drayton Valley. Guests can also use an in-room microwave and fridge.

Drayton Valley Ramada serves continental breakfast daily. Guests can then work out in the on-site fitness center or relax in the hot tub.

A business center and laundry facilities are also available at this hotel.

Nearby activities include golf at Drayton Valley Golf Club 2 miles away. Drayton Valley Indoor Rodeo is just under 1 miles from this hotel.
