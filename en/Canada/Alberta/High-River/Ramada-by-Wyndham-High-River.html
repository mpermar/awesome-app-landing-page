Description

Centrally located, this hotel is about 3 km from High River city centre. Guests can enjoy an in-room flat-screen TV.

Each modern room at Ramada High River is equipped with free Wi-Fi. A microwave and fridge are also available for use.

High River Ramada serves a hot breakfast daily. Guests can then work out in the on-site fitness centre or relax in the hot tub.

A business centre and laundry facilities are also provided for guests’ convenience at this hotel.

3 minutes’ drive away is Bob Snodgrass Recreation Complex. Calgary is about 45 minutes’ drive away and accessible via Highway 2.
