Description

Modernly furnished rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Other amenities include a microwave, a fridge and a coffee machine.

Guests can enjoy a delicious meal at Smitty's Family Restaurant, located at Travelodge by Wyndham Edmonton West. A free continental breakfast is served daily. The on-site business center is available 24 hours a day. Free on-site parking is provided.

Telus World of Science is 3.7 mi from this property. West Edmonton Mall is a 7 minute drive.
