Description

Featuring a PlayStation 3 gaming system in each room, Home Inn Medicine Hat features a complimentary breakfast with eggs, bacon, sausage, toast, fruit, muffins and waffles.

The motel offers a 24-hour front desk and is 2 miles from Medicine Hat College. Each room offers a 32" flat-screen TV and free WiFi.

A mini-refrigerator, microwave, and Keurig coffee maker are also included. A 24-hour coffee bar is available in the lobby of Home Inn Medicine Hat, as well as games and movies for rent.

A guest laundry facility is located on-site. Home Inn Medicine Hat is within one kilometre of Medicine Hat Mall, Connaught Golf Club and Medicine Hat Lodge Resort Casino & Spa.
