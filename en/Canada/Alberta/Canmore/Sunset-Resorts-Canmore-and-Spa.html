Description

This resort is located in the Canadian Rocky Mountains in Canmore. The resort features a hot tub.

A full kitchen is offered in the guest apartments. Silvertip Golf Course is 3 km away.

At Sunset Resorts Canmore, each apartment provides guests with a fireplace. A dining area, a stovetop oven and a coffee maker are available in every apartment.

Guests of the Canmore Sunset Resorts are issued a discount card for local area merchants.

The resort also includes a gym and barbecue facilities. Property offers access to the on-site swimming pool, water slide and hot tub in the Grande Rockies building.

Horseback riding at Cross Zee Ranch is 3 km away. The Trans-Canada Highway is 3 minutes' drive from Sunset Resorts Canmore.
