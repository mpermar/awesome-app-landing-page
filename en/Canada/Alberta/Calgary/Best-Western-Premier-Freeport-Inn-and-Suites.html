Description

The stylish rooms are equipped with a refrigerator, microwave and safe. Free Wi-Fi and an iPod docking station are provided and suites feature a fireplace and bathrobes.

Best Western PREMIER Freeport offers a modern gym. Guests can use the full-service business center or visit the gift shop.

A full breakfast is served daily at Best Western PREMIER Freeport Inn & Suites. The lobby lounge features pub-style food and a full bar.

The University of Calgary is a 20-minute drive from this hotel. The Aero Space Museum is 4 miles away and the Calgary Zoo is a 15 minute drive away.
