Description

A mini-fridge and tea and coffee-making facilities are included in each room at Aloft Calgary University. A work desk and an ergonomic chair are provided. Private bathrooms include walk-in showers.

Le Soleil Spa is available of Calgary University Aloft. A shared TV lounge and a game room are provided. Guests can enjoy grab-and-go pantry items at Re:fuel by Aloft or have a drink at W XYZ Bar is on site.

McMahon stadium is located within 800 feet of the property. Canada Olympic Park is located 6 miles from the hotel. Calgary Stampede grounds are a 13-minute drive away.
