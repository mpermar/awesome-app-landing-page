Description

Hotel Blackfoot offers rooms with coffee-making facilities and high-speed internet access. The rooms have the city, mountains or outdoor pool view.

A 24-hour fitness room and a seasonal heated outdoor pool are on-site at the Hotel Blackfoot. A hot tub and a sauna are also on site.

The Calgary Stampede Park is a 3.7 mi drive from this Calgary hotel. Calgary Zoo is a 15 minute drive away.
