Description

Nestled on New London Bay, this guest accommodation offers on-site access to a sandy beach.

It features a seasonal outdoor swimming pool and waterfront cottages with a fully equipped kitchen.

Satellite TV and free Wi-Fi are available in each cottage at Beach & Breeze Cottages Stanley Bridge.

All cottages come complete with a balcony with view of New London Bay. Gas barbecue facilities provide outdoor cooking options for guests.

A playground and sandbox offer on-site recreation for children. Swimming is possible on site at the beach or swimming pool.

Towels are supplied. In the evening, guests at Beach & Breeze Cottages can enjoy an on-site campfire.

Canoes, parasails and kayaks are available for hire within 500 metres of these cottages.
