Description

Surrounded by maple trees, this property in the centre of Cavendish offers a seasonal outdoor pool and hot tub.

All cottages are equipped with a full kitchen. Featuring pine interiors, all cottages at Cavendish Maples Cottages are appointed with a living area.

Select units have sofa beds. Cable TV and a DVD player offer indoor entertainment in all units.

3 playgrounds are available on site. A volleyball court and horseshoe pits are among additional activities at Maples Cottages Cavendish.

Guests can also enjoy outdoor fire pits. Shining Waters Family Fun Park is 100 metres away.
