Description

At the resort, rooms come with a closet. All rooms are equipped with a shared bathroom with a hair dryer, while certain rooms come with a kitchen with an oven. The rooms feature a seating area.

Guests can use the hot tub. The area is popular for cycling, and car rental is available at Blue Crest Cottages.

Rustico is 1.9 miles from the accommodation, while Charlottetown is 17 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Charlottetown Airport, 14 miles from Blue Crest Cottages.
