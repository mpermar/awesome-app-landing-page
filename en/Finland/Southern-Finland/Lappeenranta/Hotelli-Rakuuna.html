Description

Located in Lappeenranta, 1. 7 km from South Karelian Art Museum, Hotelli Rakuuna provides accommodation with a shared lounge, free private parking and a garden.

Among the facilities at this property are a shared kitchen and room service, along with free WiFi throughout the property.

The hotel features family rooms. At the hotel, every room is equipped with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

All units include a wardrobe. A buffet breakfast is available daily at Hotelli Rakuuna.

The accommodation offers 4-star accommodation with a sauna and children's playground.

You can play billiards at Hotelli Rakuuna, and the area is popular for cycling. Lappeenranta Fortress is 1.

8 km from the hotel, while South Karelian Museum is 1. 9 km away. The nearest airport is Lappeenranta Airport, 7 km from Hotelli Rakuuna.
