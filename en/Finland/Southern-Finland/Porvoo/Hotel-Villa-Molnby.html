Description

Located in Porvoo, 12 km from Porvoo Bus Station, Hotel Villa Molnby provides accommodation with a shared lounge, free private parking, a garden and a terrace.

This 3-star inn offers a ski pass sales point and a concierge service. The accommodation offers a sauna, free WiFi and family rooms.

A continental breakfast is available daily at the inn. Hotel Villa Molnby offers a children's playground.

Ski equipment hire, bike hire and car hire are available at the accommodation and the area is popular for hiking and skiing.

The nearest airport is Helsinki-Vantaa, 60 km from Hotel Villa Molnby, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
