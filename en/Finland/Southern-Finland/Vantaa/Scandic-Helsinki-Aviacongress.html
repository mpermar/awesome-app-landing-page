Description

This hotel is 2 km from Helsinki-Vantaa Airport and 1 km from the Finnish Aviation Museum.

It offers free sauna and pool access, free airport transfer and a popular breakfast served from 04:00.

Guests can also request a takeaway breakfast bag from the reception at any time. Scandic Helsinki Aviacongress’s air-conditioned guest rooms have a cable TV and a work desk.

Free 1 GB internet is included. The hotel has a restaurant and a bar. Guests are offered free gym access to a sister property 50 metres away from Scandic Helsinki Aviacongress.
