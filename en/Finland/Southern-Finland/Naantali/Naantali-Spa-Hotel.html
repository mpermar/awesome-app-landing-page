Description

Naantali Spa Hotel is situated by the waterfront in the Finnish Archipelago, less than 1 km from Naantali town centre.

It offers free WiFi, a gym and a variety of spa treatments. All rooms at Hotel Naantali Spa include a seating area and flat-screen TV.

Some rooms include a private balcony with views of the archipelago. Guests can relax in the Roman bath under the turquoise cupola or enjoy a swim in the 5 pools.

The hotel offers a spa salon with body and facial treatments as well as relaxing saunas and hammams.

Meals and drinks of all kinds, ranging from Thai food to traditional Finnish dishes, can be enjoyed in no less than 8 restaurants, cafés and bars on site.
