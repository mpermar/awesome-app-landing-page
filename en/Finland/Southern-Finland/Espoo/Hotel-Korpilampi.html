Description

Situated amongst Espoo’s nature and Korpilampi pond, this hotel is 400 metres from Serena Water Park.

It offers 3 saunas and rooms with a flat-screen TV. Both Wi-Fi and parking are free.

Modern décor, a work desk and private bathroom with shower are standard at Hotel Korpilampi.

Some rooms feature wooden floors. The on-site à la carte restaurant offers international cuisine and views of the pond.

Guests can also relax with a drink or snack in the lobby bar or on the terrace during warm months.

Leisure activities at Korpilampi Hotel include 2 indoor swimming pools, a hot tub and a fitness centre.

Guests can enjoy free use of walking sticks to explore the hiking path around the pond.

Helsinki-Vantaa Airport is 20 minutes’ drive away. A bus that takes guests to central Helsinki in 40 minutes stops in front of the hotel.
