Description

Overlooking Lake Saimaa, these short-term rental cottages are just 900 metres from Holiday Club Saimaa Spa.

All feature a private pier, sauna and free WiFi access. Central Imatra is 12 minutes’ drive away.

A fully equipped kitchen features in all Saimaa Resort Sauna Cottages. Each has a terrace with lake views and a living room with a fireplace, seating area and flat-screen TV.

Some have a bathroom with a massage shower. Saimaa Resort Sauna Cottages has a private beach by the lake.

The nearest restaurant is 1 km away. Shopping Centre Capri and the Angry Birds Theme Park are just 900 metres from the villas.
