Description

Situated by Lake Kolkonjärvi, Kuus-Hukkala offers rooms and cottages with a TV. Guests can enjoy a private beach and free access to a lakeside sauna.

Rantasalmi village centre is a 20-minute drive away. Free WiFi is available in the restaurant and reception area.

Kuus-Hukkala Holiday Village offers accommodation options with a private bathroom and also cottages with shared bathroom facilities.

Other facilities include a children’s playground and a barbecue area. International dishes like pizza is served at Kuus-Hukkala Restaurant.

During summer, meals can be served at the terrace. Traditional dance evenings and other events are arranged regularly.

Kuus-Hukkala Holiday Village provides organised activities such as jeep safaris and fishing trips.
