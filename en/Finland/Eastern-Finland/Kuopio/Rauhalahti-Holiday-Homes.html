Description

These villas, situated in Rauhalahti Holiday Centre, are less than 50 metres from the shore of Lake Kallavesi.

Rauhalahti Holiday Centre Villas offers accommodation with a fully equipped kitchen, private sauna and terrace.

Some villas have lake views. Guests can enjoy drinks, snacks and light dishes at the summer restaurant, which has a spacious terrace.

Other facilities include a playground and barbecue grills. A sandy beach is just a few minutes’ walk from the most of the villas, and hiking, cycling and cross-country ski trails are found nearby.

Rauhalahti Spa Centre, 10 minutes’ walk away, has several restaurants and bars. A grocery shop, open during summer, is 500 metres from the property.
