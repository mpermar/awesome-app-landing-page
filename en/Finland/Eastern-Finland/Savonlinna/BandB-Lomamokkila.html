Description

This property is set on a family farm, 12 km outside Savonlinna and 10 minutes’ walk from Lake Pellosjärvi.

It offers a private beach, lakeside sauna and homemade Finnish food. Lomamokkila’s accommodation options have wooden floors and either private or shared bathroom and kitchen facilities.

Some include flat-screen TVs, while free WiFi is available throughout the property.

Guests can book either a traditional or smoke sauna on site. They can also enjoy free use of rowing boats on the lake.

Volleyball, badminton and tennis are popular summer activities. The farm’s own products are used in the breakfast as well as in the summer restaurant.

During winter, guests can cross-country ski on the trails that surround the farm.
