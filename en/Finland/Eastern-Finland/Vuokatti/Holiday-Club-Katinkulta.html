Description

Located in the popular ski resort of Vuokatti, Holiday Club Katinkulta is 200 metres from Lake Nuasjrvi.

It offers a spa with wellness centre, the on-site Nuas 18-hole golf course and free Wi-Fi.

Holiday Club Katinkulta Hotel offers rooms with a TV, bathrobes and free wired internet access.

Most rooms have balconies with views of the Vuokatti Hills and the golf course. Restaurant Easy Kitchen offers a varied la carte menu and a childrens playroom, while fine dining can be enjoyed at Maisemaravintola, open on request.

There is also Classic Pizza Restaurant and O'Learys Sports Bar. Karaoke nights are often arranged on site.

The spa and wellness centre has Finnish and traditional saunas, a steam cave and a Roman bath.

Katinkulta also has a gym as well as several indoor and outdoor swimming pools. Vuokatti town centre is 2 km away.
