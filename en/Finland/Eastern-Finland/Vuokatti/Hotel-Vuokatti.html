Description

Peacefully located on Lake Nuasjärvi, this hotel offers a private beach with a jetty.

Free access to the gym and communal sauna are included. Downhill skiing is possible within 700 metres, while cross-country tracks are just 50 metres away.

All rooms at Hotel Vuokatti have a flat-screen TV, free WiFi and a modern bathroom with a shower.

Each room also features a minibar, hairdryer, safe and a small fridge. There is a lounge area, where guests can unwind with a glass of wine or a cup of coffee.

Next to the sauna is an outdoor pool, where guests can take a cooling dip even in the winter time.

Additional free facilities include bicycles, tennis courts, an 18-hole mini golf course and a court where guests can play badminton, basketball and floorball.

Nordic walking poles can be borrowed on site and during summer bicycles are available for hire.

Staff can help arrange other area activities, such as swimming, hiking, skiing and golfing.
