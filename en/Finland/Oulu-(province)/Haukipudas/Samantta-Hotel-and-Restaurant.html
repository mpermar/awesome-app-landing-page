Description

This hotel is located 100 metres away from the Kiiminki River in Haukipudas, 20 km north of Oulu.

It offers free Wi-Fi, free private parking and free tea/coffee in the lobby. All guest rooms at Samantta Hotel & Restaurant are decorated in an elegant, traditional style.

Each has a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with a shower.

Samantta Hotel & Restaurant restaurant offers an early breakfast and a lunch buffet.

During weekends, it hosts dancing and live music. There is also a popular night club on site.

Guests can also enjoy a drink or snack on the hotel's furnished terrace. Guests can book the hotel sauna and order snacks from a special menu.
