Description

Offering free spa and pool access, this hotel is situated in western Finland's town of Kalajoki along a sandy beach.

It offers impressive Bay of Bothnia views and a variety of on-site dining and leisure options.

WiFi is free. A minibar, TV and parquet floors are found in all rooms at Santa's Resort & Spa Hotel Sani.

Some feature a windowed balcony with views over the bay, while others have a French balcony.

Guests can enjoy a drink on the terrace, as well as 2 bars, a spa cafe and bistro that uses locally grown and organic produce.

The spa at Santa's Resort & Spa Hotel Sani includes swimming pools with a slide, a children's pool, hot tubs, a Finnish sauna and Turkish steam bath.

From Wednesday to Friday, guests can enjoy a morning swim or sauna. Massage treatments can also be booked.

A children's playroom, bowling, mini golf and a fitness centre are also available.
