Description

Hotel Lokkilinna is located right by Hiekkasärkät Beach in Kalajoki, on Finland’s Gulf of Bothnia coast.

It offers free Wi-Fi, sea views and rooms with a private balcony. Guests can relax with a free evening sauna before dinner.

Lokkilinna’s restaurant serves traditional Finnish dishes, and its glass terrace offers panoramic sea views.

Boat tours of the Rahja archipelago depart regularly from Kalajoki. Golf, windsurfing and hiking are also popular in the area.
