Description

Tea/coffee facilities, a minibar and cable TV are standard at Radisson Blu Hotel Oulu. Air conditioning and memory foam mattresses add extra comfort. Some rooms also feature a sitting area.

A generous breakfast buffet is served at Restaurant Toivo, and Restaurant Sassi provides Italian-style cuisine and Scandinavian specialties. Typical Finnish food such as reindeer or traditional Oulu sausage is also available. Bar Sassi and its terrace are popular for casual fun and good company.

In addition to leisure and fitness facilities, bicycles can be borrowed for free on site.

Radisson Blu Oulu is just over a quarter mile from Oulu Castle and just over half a mile from Oulu Train Station.
