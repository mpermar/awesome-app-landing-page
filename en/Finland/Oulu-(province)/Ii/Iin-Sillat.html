Description

Offering a garden, Iin Sillat is located in Ii. Oulu is 35 km from the property. All units feature a TV.

Some units have a seating area and/or balcony. A fridge and coffee machine are also featured.

There is also a kitchenette in some of the units, fitted with a microwave and stovetop.

There is a private bathroom with a bidet and a hairdryer in each unit. Towels are available.

Iin Sillat facilities includes a children's playground. In summer, guest can enjoy fishing ans swimming in the river.

A sauna and grill is also offered. Iin Sillat is located 500 metres from Highway 4.

The nearest bus stop is a 7 minutes walk, the bus to Oulu departs here. The nearest airport is Oulu Airport, 44 km from the property.
