Description

This eco-friendly, stylish hotel, 50 metres from Vaasa Train Station, is a 10-minute walk from Bothnian Bay.

It features an indoor rooftop pool and saunas with panoramic city and water views.

There is free WiFi throughout the property. All rooms have a TV with satellite channels, as well as coffee/tea making facilities.

Some also include air conditioning. Hotel Royal Vaasa has 2 restaurants and a pub on site, featuring different dining experiences.

Restaurant Frans & Nicole specialises in French-inspired cuisine, while Restaurant Wazaca offers Mexican flavours.

The hotel also features an EasyFit sports center. Meeting and banquet events are bookable as well.

Vaasa Airport is just 10 km away. Hotel staff can help arrange boat and fishing trips in the Vaasa archipelago.
