Description

This hotel, located in scenic natural surroundings, is 5 minutes’ drive from Jyväskylä’s town centre.

It features a bowling alley and a large spa centre with views of Lake Tuomiojärvi.

Each guest room at Scandic Laajavuori has a work desk and TV with satellite and pay-per-view channels.

Some also offer a balcony and a minibar. Free 1 GB WiFi connection is included. Guests can enjoy free access to Scandic Laajavuori’s fitness centre and 800 m² spa facility with several pools, hot tubs, saunas and wellness services.

It also has a cosy fireplace lounge. In addition to the daily breakfast buffet, guests have the option to request a takeaway breakfast bag from the reception at any time.

Alvar Aalto Museum, dedicated to the Finnish architect, is 10 minutes’ drive away from the hotel.
