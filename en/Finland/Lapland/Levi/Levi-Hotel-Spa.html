Description

Just 5 minutes’ walk from Levi Fell’s ski slopes, this hotel features Spa Water World which includes pools, saunas and a gym.

It has a log cabin restaurant, 8-lane bowling alley and nightly live music. Levi Hotel Spa’s tastefully decorated rooms feature flat-screen TVs, tea/coffee making facilities and modern bathrooms.

Free WiFi is available in all. Various saunas, 17 indoor and outdoor pools and a water slide are found at the spa and water park.

Guest can also book massages and beauty treatments at Levi Hotel Spa. A bouncy castle and toys are found in the children's playroom.

Restaurant Ahku is set in a traditional Lapland log building and offers both traditional Lappish cuisine and international dishes.

There is also a pizza restaurant on site, as well as a burger restaurant. Ski storage is available at the hotel.

Levi Tourist Centre, restaurants, bars and boutiques are within walking distance of Levi Hotel Spa.
