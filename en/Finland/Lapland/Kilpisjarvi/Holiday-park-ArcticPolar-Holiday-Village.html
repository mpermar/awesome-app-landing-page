Description

Offering a restaurant, ArcticPolar Lomakylä is located in Kilpisjärvi. Free WiFi access is available in the reception area of this property.

The accommodation will provide you with a TV and a terrace.There is a full kitchen with a dishwasher and a microwave. Featuring a shower, the private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer.

At ArcticPolar Lomakylä you will find a sauna and a garden. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including skiing, fishing and hiking. The property offers free parking.

If you feel like visiting the surroundings, check out Ovre Dividal National Park (30.9 miles).
