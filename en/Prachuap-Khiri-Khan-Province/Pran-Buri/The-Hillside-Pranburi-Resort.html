Description

Guest rooms at the resort are equipped with a seating area. Rooms are equipped with a private bathroom and free toiletries, and certain accommodations at The Hillside Pranburi Resort have a balcony. All guest rooms will provide guests with a desk and a electric tea pot.

Buffet and American breakfast options are available each morning at the accommodation.

Khao Kalok Beach is 2.3 miles from The Hillside Pranburi Resort.
