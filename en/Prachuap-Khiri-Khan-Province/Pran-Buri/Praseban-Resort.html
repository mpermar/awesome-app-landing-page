Description

Rooms and villa's feature a private balcony with a view of the sea. Rooms feature air conditioning, satellite TV, minibar and large bathroom with hairdryer.

Praseban Resort offers a sunbathing deck, laundry service and room service.

Kanang Sari Restaurant has outdoor seating and serves Thai and international dishes. Praseban Seafood Terrace is an open-air restaurant offering fresh seafood.

Praseban Resort is 18.5 miles from Hua Hin and is about a 3-hour drive form Bangkok.
