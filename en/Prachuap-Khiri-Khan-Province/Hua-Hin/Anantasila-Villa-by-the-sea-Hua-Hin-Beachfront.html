Description

Decorated with Thai-style furnishings and beddings, Anantasila’s rooms feature floor-to-ceiling windows with views of surrounding greenery. Each room comes with cable TV, a DVD player and a safety deposit box. Private bathrooms have a bathtub and shower.

Guests can play a round of mini golf, or relax on the sun loungers provided on the beach. Massages can be enjoyed on the beach, by the pool, or in the privacy of guests’ rooms.

Beach views accompany meals at the restaurant, which serves Thai, Western and Italian specialties. Cocktails are available at the beach bar.

Anantasila Villa is a 15-minute drive from Hua Hin’s town center. It provides a free shuttle to a bus stop where guests can catch a minibus to the town center.

Khao Takiab is a great choice for travelers interested in swimming pools, family-friendly trips and kid-friendly beaches.
