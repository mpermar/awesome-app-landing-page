Description

MGM Grand Sanya is a 10-minute drive from Yalong Bay Tropic Paradise Forest Park and a 20-minute drive from Sanya city center. It is a 20-minute drive from Yalong Bay HSR Station while the Phenix International Airport can be reached by a 40-minute drive.

Modern rooms are soundproofed and fitted with a flat-screen cable TV, mini-bar and tea/coffee-making facilities. Private bathrooms feature a bathtub and separate shower cubicle. A bathrobe, hairdryer and slippers are provided.

Guests can work out in the fitness center or enjoy a game of tennis. Ticketing and currency exchange services are available. Other recreational options include Papillon nightclub and AOC bar.

Silk Restaurant and Pin Ju Restaurant feature a selection of Cantonese cuisine. Japanese dishes are offered in Neera Restaurant while an international buffet can be enjoyed in Hai Tang. Other options include Poolside Café and Lobby Lounge.
