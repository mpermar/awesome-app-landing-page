Description

All guest rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area and a flat-screen TV. Sofitel Sanya Leeman Resort provides some rooms with sea views, and rooms include a terrace. The rooms have a desk.

A buffet breakfast is served at the property. Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant, which serves Chinese cuisine.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool. The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental and car rental are available at Sofitel Sanya Leeman Resort.

Tianya Haijiao Tourist Area is 25 miles from the hotel. The nearest airport is Sanya Phoenix International Airport, 21 miles from Sofitel Sanya Leeman Resort.
