Description

Tastefully decorated, all of the rooms at Best Western Plus include a 40-inch LED TV, microwave and mini fridge. An iron, ironing board, coffee-tea-facilities and desk. A hairdryer can be found in the bathroom.

Other hotel facilities includes a state-of-the-art fitness center and a front desk, conveniently open 24 hours a day.

Best Western Plus Winnipeg West is 3 miles Glendale Golf & Country Club. Both the MTS Centre and Winnipeg International Airport are 8.5 miles away. Parking is available on site for free.
