Description

Located in Raerd in the province of Friesland, B & B Zazza offers a family room with free Wi-Fi access and free parking is available.

Leeuwarden can be reached within 18 minutes by car. The family room, including a terrace, comes with a kitchenette equipped with a stove, refrigerator and coffee machine.

The bathroom is fitted with a shower and free toiletries are provided. B & B Zazza can serve Dutch lunch and a Dutch or Egyptian dinner at a surcharge upon request.

At B & B Zazza you will find a garden. Other facilities offered at the property include shared lounge, a tour desk and luggage storage.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling and fishing.
