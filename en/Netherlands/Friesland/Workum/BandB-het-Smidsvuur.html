Description

This bed and breakfast is ideally situated along the water on the edge of the village of Workum.

B&B het Smidsvuur offers Scandinavian-style rooms with shared bathroom facilities and free Wi-Fi.

Guests can rent bicycles on site. Nearby boat rental is also available. B&B het Smidsvuur is 4 km from Workum Railway Station.

The edge of the scenic IJsselmeer can be reached in 5 minutes by car. Sneek is 20 minutes drive away.

Free public parking is available nearby. B&B het Smidsvuur has simply-furnished rooms with wood paneled walls and access to a shared bathroom with a shower and toilet.

Guests can make use of the communal living room or prepare a meal in the shared kitchen.

The bed and breakfast also has a games room and a waterside terrace which can also be used for barbecues.
