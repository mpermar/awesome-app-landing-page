Description

The restaurant serves a variety of meals. When weather is nice, guests can have a refreshing drink on the waterside terrace and watch boats sail by.

The sauna and swimming pool offer a relaxing setting after a busy day. Kids can have hours of fun with the ping-pong table.

Hotel Galamadammen is in between the Frisian lakes, 2 miles from the Gaasterland Forests. Villages like Stavoren, Workum and Hindeloopen are a 15 minute drive away.
