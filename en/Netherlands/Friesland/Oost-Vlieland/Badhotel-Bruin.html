Description

Badhotel Bruin, nestled between the dunes and the mudflats on Vlieland, offers Boutique-style rooms with private bathrooms.

This hotel dates from 1896 and features free WiFi in the public areas. The hotel is a 5-minute walk from the ferry terminal.

Badhotel is situated next to Podium Vlieland and opposite of Tromp’s Huys Museum.

In summer the island of Texel is 30 minutes away by boat. Pets can be accommodated in the apartments only upon request for a surcharge.

Guests can make use of the sauna, solarium and the beauty salon Bruisend, which is working with Aveda.

Bruin Badhotel also features a library and a children room's. Some of the many activities available nearby include cycling, fishing and horse riding.

The restaurant serves light Mediterranean cuisine prepared with local ingredients and complemented by a select wine menu.
