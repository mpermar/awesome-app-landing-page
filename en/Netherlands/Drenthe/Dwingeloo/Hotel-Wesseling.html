Description

For generations the hotel is well known as an authentic family hotel where personal hospitality is the root for sincere cosiness.

Hotel Wesseling is a traditional family hotel, which offers its guest all modern comfort.

The hotel provides 33 guest rooms in 2 different buildings which are across the road from each other.

All the rooms are easy reachable by elevator or they are situated on the ground floor.

We also offer room for our disabled guests. From our sun lounge you can enjoy the breathtaking view over the St.

Nicolaas church and the Siepel tower. The restaurant also provides a sun lounge, which has a view over the Brink, and here you can enjoy an exclusive à la carte dinner.

Golf court Havelte is a 15 minute drive. Amsterdam is at 2 hours by car, Groningen and Zwolle at 40 minutes and Assen at 25 minutes.
