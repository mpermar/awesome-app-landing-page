Description

This holiday park is located in the natural area of Dwingelderveld. RCN Vakantiepark de Noordster offers guest rooms and self-catering bungalows.

There is also a restaurant on site which offer a variety of meals. Each guest room has a private bathroom with a shower.

Each bungalow features a furnished terrace, seating area, TV and kitchen. Its bathroom is fitted with a shower.

RCN Vakantiepark de Noordster has an la carte restaurant on site that also serves a special childrens menu.

There is a variety of activities on site including a tennis court, mini golf and a childrens playground.

The surroundings of RCN Vakantiepark de Noordster are ideal for hiking and cycling.

Bicycle rental and free private parking are also offered on site. Sights near the property are: Radio Telescope Camras Dwingeloo, Shakespeare Theater Diever, Bird Park Ruinen, Museum Vledder, Orvelte, Wildlands Emmen, Prison Museum Veenhuizen, Memorial Camp Westerbork.
