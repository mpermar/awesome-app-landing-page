Description

All units here feature a flat-screen TV, a living room with a sofa, a well-equipped kitchen with a dining area, and a private bathroom with shower, free toiletries and a hair dryer. A microwave is also provided, as well as a coffee machine and a electric tea pot.

The chalet has a playground.

A bicycle rental service is available at Chalet Drenthe, while cycling and fishing can be enjoyed nearby.

Groningen is 22 miles from the accommodation. Groningen Eelde Airport is 16 miles away.
