Description

Fruitpark Hotel & Spa has a restaurant, fitness centre, a bar and shared lounge in Ochten.

Among the facilities at this property are room service and a concierge service, along with free WiFi throughout the property.

The property offers an indoor pool, a garden and rooms with garden views. All guest rooms come with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fridge, a kettle, a shower, a hairdryer and a desk.

At the hotel all rooms include a wardrobe and a private bathroom. Fruitpark Hotel & Spa offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

The accommodation offers 4-star accommodation with a spa centre and children's playground.

Bike hire and car hire are available at Fruitpark Hotel & Spa and the area is popular for cycling and fishing.

Arnhem is 31 km from the hotel, while Nijmegen is 26 km from the property. The nearest airport is Eindhoven Airport, 78 km from Fruitpark Hotel & Spa.
