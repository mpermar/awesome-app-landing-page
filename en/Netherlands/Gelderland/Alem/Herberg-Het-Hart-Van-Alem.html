Description

Herberg Het Hart Van Alem is located in Alem in a monumental building of 225 years old.

Free WiFi access is available and The property offers free parking. Each room comes with a flat-screen TV and private bathroom fitted with a bath or shower.

Free toiletries are provided. At Herberg Het Hart Van Alem each room features tea/coffee making facilities, and there is a restaurant within walking distance.

Breakfast is available each morning in the breakfast room. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling and walking.

Guests can use the covered bicycle storage with electrical charging points. Jachthaven de Maas is 220 metres away where guests can also rent a small boat.

Zaltbommel is 6 km away and the centre of 's-Hertogenbosch can be reached within 20 minutes by car.
