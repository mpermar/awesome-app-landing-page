Description

All units are equipped with a microwave, toaster, a coffee machine, a fridge and electric tea pot. Some units also have a kitchen equipped with an oven and a stovetop.

The resort village has a playground.

Cycling can be enjoyed nearby and a bicycle rental service is available.

Arnhem is 23 miles from Park Nieuwgrapendaal, while Zwolle is 14 miles away. Schiphol Airport is 56 miles from the property.
