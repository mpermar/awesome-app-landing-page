Description

This century-old former farmhouse is situated on the edge of the Veluwe woodland region and offers rooms set in spacious gardens.

Vierhouten is family-run and features bicycle rental and free Wi-Fi. All of the rooms at Hotel Pension Vierhouten are individually decorated with a nature theme and have a flat-screen cable TV.

Each room also has a private bathroom with a shower and toiletries. Pension Vierhouten is a 5-minute walk from the Centrum Bus Stop.

The key sights of Apeldoorn, including Apenheul Monkey Park and Paleis Het Loo, are a 30-minute drive from the hotel.

The library at Hotel Vierhouten features fiction books and tourist information including walking routes and cycle maps.

The hotel also has table tennis facilities and a communal lounge with games and a TV.

Guests can enjoy a high tea with home-made scones on the sun terrace or have a drink in the self-service bar.

The restaurant serves a traditional Dutch 3-course set menu in the dining room or conservatory.
