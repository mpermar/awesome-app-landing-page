Description

All guest rooms in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Every room comes with a coffee machine, while some rooms have a kitchen with a dishwasher and an oven. Guest rooms have a closet.

You can play ping pong at Landgoed De Ijsvogel, and bike rental is available.

Utrecht is 24 miles from the accommodation, while Arnhem is 18 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Schiphol Airport, 39 miles from Landgoed De Ijsvogel.
