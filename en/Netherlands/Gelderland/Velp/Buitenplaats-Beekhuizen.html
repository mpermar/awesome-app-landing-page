Description

Set in Velp, in the middle of National Park Veluwezoom, Buitenplaats Beekhuizen provides accommodation with immediate access to the forest.

Both WiFi and private parking are available on site free of charge. All units come with a terrace, a kitchenette with a microwave, and a private bathroom with shower.

A fridge is also available, as well as a coffee machine and a kettle. Buitenplaats Beekhuizen offers a children's playground, restaurant WOODZ, an outdoor jacuzzi and wood packages for a camp fire.

The accommodation features a terrace. Arnhem is 6 km from Buitenplaats Beekhuizen.

The nearest airport is Eindhoven Airport, 74 km from the property. The nearest train station can be found within a 30-minute walk.
