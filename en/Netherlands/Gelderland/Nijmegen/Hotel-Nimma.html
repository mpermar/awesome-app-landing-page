Description

Located in Nijmegen, Hotel Nimma features a sun terrace. Guests can enjoy a drink at the on-site bar and free parking is available.

Grote Markt is 2 km from the hotel. Each room at Hotel Nimma features a private bathroom with a shower and toilet.

There is a desk and a flat-screen cable TV. Free Wi-Fi is included. The apartments include a kitchen and a seating area.

Guests can enjoy breakfast, lunch and dinner at the hotel restaurant. De Waal river is 500 metres from Hotel Nimma and former factory Honigcomplex is a 9-minute walk.

Art and cultural meeting point Vasim is 550 metres away. Bike hire is available at this hotel and the area is popular for cycling.
