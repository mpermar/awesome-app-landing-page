Description

Hotel Het Landhuis is a family-run hotel, set on the edge of Oldenzaal. It features a wellness area including a spa bath, a whirlpool with steam, steam cabin, sauna facilities and a sunbed (for an extra charge).

Free Wi-Fi is available throughout. In summer, special activities such as plays and cabaret are organised for guests aged 65+.

The rooms are classically furnished and come with cable TV. They have private bathroom facilities with free toiletries.

Het Landhuis Hotel has a restaurant and a bar which has a snack menu including a dish of the day and daily 3-course menu.

Guests can also benefit from the small fitness centre. Bike and hiking routes are available at the hotel and there is a charging point for electrical bikes.

Oldenzaal’s old city center is just 1 km away from Het Landhuis. Golf course ‘t Kruisselt is just 1.
