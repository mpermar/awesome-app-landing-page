Description

Wildthout Hotel en Restaurant is situated in a former mayor's house in Ommen. There is a large, sunny conservatory with sliding doors that lead into the garden.

All rooms offer a view of the green surroundings. For families there is a family unit with separate rooms for parents and children.

Free WiFi is available throughout. When weather is nice, breakfast and dinner can be served on the garden terrace.

The restaurant offers Ã  la carte dishes and a daily 3-course menu. Logis Wildthout is ideally situated along several well-known hiking and cycling routes, such as the Pieterpad.

From Wildthout, guests can also take a boat trip on the Vecht River. Bikes can be rented through the hotel.

There is a bike shed and several free parking places. Hellendoorn is a 15-minute journey from the hotel by car.
