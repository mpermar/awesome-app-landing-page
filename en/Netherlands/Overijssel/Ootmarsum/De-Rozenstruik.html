Description

De Rozenstruik is an Intimate family hotel near the centre of Ootmarsum. Enjoy the comfortable rooms and the pretty garden.

All rooms are equipped with a TV, radio, telephone, a seating area, and a private bathroom.

Some rooms have a private balcony. You can enjoy a flavoursome meal in the evening before going to sleep.

The hotel has a cosy living room with a chimney and a sitting area. You can also take a walk in the royal garden with terraces and a pond.

The hotel has a cycle shed so you can store your bike safely. There is also a charging opportunity for electric cars.

This village offers shops, galleries and various museums. The variable landscape of Twente is excellent for walking- and cycling tours.
