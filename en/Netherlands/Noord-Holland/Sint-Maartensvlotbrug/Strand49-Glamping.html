Description

Units are equipped with a kitchen with a dining area, and a private bathroom with a a hair dryer and shower. A dishwasher, a microwave and toaster are also offered, as well as a coffee machine and a electric tea pot.

The resort village has a playground.

A terrace can be found at Strand49 Glamping, along with a garden.

Amsterdam is 29 miles from the accommodation, while Haarlem is 28 miles from the property. Schiphol Airport is 33 miles away.
