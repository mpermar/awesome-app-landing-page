Description

Van der Valk Volendam is a 4-star hotel situated in a peaceful rural area, just over 15 km from Amsterdam.

It benefits from an indoor pool and a spacious terrace overlooking a small lake. Free WiFi access is available throughout the hotel.

The nearest bus stop is located at a distance of 200 metres. Each room at Van der Valk Hotel Volendam includes a flat-screen TV, a telephone, a safety deposit box, a refrigerator and a work desk.

All rooms also include a seating corner, wake-up service, coffee machine and conditioning.

The private bathroom is equipped with a bath or shower, a toilet and a hair dryer.

Restaurant Martinus at Van der Valk serves a traditional Dutch for lunch. International dishes are offered in the evening.

The bar menu features a selection of finger foods and a range of wines. The hotel also features a health club including a gym and sauna for an extra charge.

Bicycles and scooters are available for rental. The centre of Amsterdam, featuring the Van Gogh Museum and Dam Square, is just over a 15-minute car journey from the hotel.
