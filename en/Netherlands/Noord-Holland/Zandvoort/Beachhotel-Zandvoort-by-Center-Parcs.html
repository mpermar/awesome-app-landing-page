Description

Beachhotel Zandvoort offers rooms with views of the sea and beach in the centre of Zandvoort with free access to the water park Aqua Romana.

The resort benefits from a supermarket, bakery and many leisure activities. Free WiFi is available in all areas.

Each of Center Parcs Hotel Zandvoort's rooms feature box-spring beds, a work desk and seating area with a TV.

Also included is a electric kettle and Nespresso Coffee machine. Some of the leisure facilities available at the main resort park include bowling, mini golf, an arcade and more.

Guests can also make use of the spa bath, sauna and solarium. Bicycles are available for rent from the reception.

There is Grand Café restaurant on site, whilst Il Giardino serves Mediterranean cuisine.

Zandvoort aan Zee Railway Station is a 10-minute walk from Beachhotel Zandvoort. The Kennemer Golf & Country Club is less than a 10-minute drive from the hotel.

The centre of Amsterdam is 30 km away. Zandvoort Circuit is a 4-minute drive away.
