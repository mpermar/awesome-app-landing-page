Description

This holiday park offers accommodation with free Wi-Fi 1 km from De Cocksdorp. The park includes 3 restaurants, a supermarket and both indoor and outdoor swimming pools with water slides.

All of the bungalows and chalets at Vakantiepark De Krim Texel have a modern interior with a private terrace and a fully-equipped kitchen including a dishwasher.

Extras include bed linen. Kids can enjoy the indoor and outdoor playgrounds as well as the entertainment programmes during the school holidays.

The park also features bowling facilities indoor minigolf, indoor laser tag arena and bicycle rental.

De Krim Texel is located next to Golf Course De Texelse. You can reach the ferry service to the mainland within a 20 to 30-minute car ride.
