Description

Amsterdam Marriott Hotel is a 5-star accommodation offering luxurious rooms and facilities in the city’s heart, across from the Leidse Square.

The stylish hotel rooms are elegantly decorated with a cozy seating area and a work desk.

Each room features a flat-screen TV with cable channels. A sauna is available, as is a gym for working out 24 hours a day.

Our Executive rooms also include access to the Executive Lounge, which offers a complimentary breakfast, drinks and hors d'oeuvres during the day.

The highly rated steakhouse Midtown Grill Restaurant overlooks the Leidse Square and offers a wide choice of USDA qualified meat.

Sorel's, the hotel's vibrant bar for local crafts, Bourbon and cocktails, is a place to unwind.

Sorel’s also serves lunch and dinner. From Amsterdam Marriott Hotel, guests can easily visit famous locations such as the Van Gogh Museum & Rijksmuseum or walk around in the beautiful Vondelpark.
