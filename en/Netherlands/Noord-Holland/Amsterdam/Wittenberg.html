Description

Wittenberg is a monumental short stay building, offering modern accommodation in Amsterdam's residential area.

It features a Smart HD TV. Carre Theatre and the Dutch National Opera & Ballet are just 500 metres from the property.

All other city highlights are easily accessible by pubic transport. All apartments are fully equipped, including flat-screen TVs, dishwasher, oven, and microwave.

Each has its own bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer. For your comfort, towels, bed linen and a weekly housekeeping are provided.

The apartments also feature a washing machine. The Hermitage Museum is just 650 metres away, while Artis Zoo is only 400 metres from Wittenberg.
