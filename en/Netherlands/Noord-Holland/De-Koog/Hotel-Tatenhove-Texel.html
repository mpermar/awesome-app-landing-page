Description

Close to the beach, Hotel Tatenhove Texel features free Wi-Fi throughout and is situated in peaceful natural surroundings.

De Duinen Van Texel National Park is only 400 metres away. The hotel features a restaurant, and a spa and wellness centre with a sauna and hot tub.

It is a 5-minute walk to the centre of De Koog, where you will find shops, bars and restaurants.

A 15-minute walk will take you to the forest or the beach. There is free car parking and an e-bike charging point on-site.

All the comfortable rooms at this pleasant family hotel have cable TV, telephone and a bathroom with a shower or a bathtub.

At the hotel there is a comfortable lounge with a cosy ambiance, a fireplace and billiards.
