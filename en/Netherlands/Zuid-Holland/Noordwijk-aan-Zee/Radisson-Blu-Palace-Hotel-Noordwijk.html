Description

Featuring extensive spa and fitness facilities, the impressive Radisson Blu Palace Hotel Noordwijk is set along the Dutch coast in charming Noordwijk aan Zee.

It offers spacious guest rooms and 1 fine dining restaurant. Guests can enjoy free access to the Vitality fitness centre and swimming pool.

Afterwards they can relax in the spa area with turkish steam bath and sauna. Extensive massage and body treatments are available.

Radisson Blu Palace Hotel Noordwijk has recently been awarded with a 4-Star Superior status and the hotel provides spacious guest rooms, decorated with bright colours and large windows.

They come with climate control, free wired internet, a seating corner and a large private bathroom.

At Brasserie & Terrace Circles guests can enjoy a buffet breakfast, lunch, dinner or high tea.

The new Dunes Lounge & Bar is open for drinks and bites. On sunny days, guests can enjoy the sun at Circles's large terrace.

Paul's Bar is available for groups. The direct dune and forest area of the hotel is ideal for long walks and cycling.

The Noordwijkse Golf Club is 10 minutes away by car. The world famous Keukenhof flower exhibition is within 20-minutes’ driving.
