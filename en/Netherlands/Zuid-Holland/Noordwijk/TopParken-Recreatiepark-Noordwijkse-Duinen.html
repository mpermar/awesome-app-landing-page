Description

The chalets at Noordwijkse Duinen include a sitting area with a TV. Each is individually decorated and fitted with heating and a bathroom with a shower.

Guests can prepare meals in the kitchen, which includes a dishwasher, microwave, and a fridge. There's an on-site restaurant, and guests can enjoy a drink on the shaded terrace.

Bike rentals are available, and guests can cycle to Noordwijk’s beach, just 1.5 miles away. During warmer months, an outdoor pool is available at Recreatiepark Noordwijkse Duinen.
