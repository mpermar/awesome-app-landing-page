Description

Offering an outdoor pool and an indoor pool, Molecaten Park Noordduinen is located in Katwijk aan Zee.

Paid Wi-Fi access is available in this holiday park. The property offers free parking.

The accommodation will provide you with a tv and a terrace. There is a full kitchen with a dishwasher and a microwave.

Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with towels. At Molecaten Park Noordduinen you will find a restaurant.

Other facilities offered at the property include a kids' club and a children's playground.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling and table tennis.

This accommodation is most suitable for leisure guests. If you feel like visiting the surroundings, check out Space Expo Noordwijk (900 m).
