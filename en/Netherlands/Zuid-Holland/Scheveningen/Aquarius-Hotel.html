Description

Situated right on the boulevard, this comfortable budget hotel offers guests a homey atmosphere in a quiet place in lively Scheveningen.

Free WiFi access is available. The Aquarius Hotel rooms are of a decent size and attractively decorated.

They are equipped with coffee and tea making facilities. While some rooms have unique views on the beach, others have nice balconies to breathe in some fresh sea air.

Have your favourite drink in the cosy bar or on the outside terrace. The restaurant serves a set menu in the dining room.

The most attractive locations of Scheveningen are within walking distance of Aquarius Hotel.

At a few minutes walking distance, you can find the terminus of the most important bus and tram connections to The Hague.
