Description

Hidden in the royal woods of Lage Vuursche, just outside Utrecht and 35 km away from Amsterdam, lies a stunning hotel in a tranquil environment.

Everything about this hotel breaths relaxation and hospitality. The friendly hotel staff, beautiful natural surroundings and extended hotel facilities all add to a pleasant stay.

The hotel restaurant serves a variety of tasty dishes, all proudly prepared with an eye for detail.

On Sunday evenings guests can enjoy a selection of light meals instead. This former coach house, dating from 1880, combines an authentic atmosphere with modern facilities and comfort.

Pleasantly removed from the hustle and bustle yet conveniently close to main cities, this truly is a distinguished location for your business meetings, events or weekend stay.

Activities offered at Ernst Sillem Hoeve Hotel include table tennis and boules.
