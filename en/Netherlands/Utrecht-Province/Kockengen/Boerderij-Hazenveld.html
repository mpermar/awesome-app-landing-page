Description

Hazenveld farm is located in the countryside of Kockengen and only 5 km away from the Haarrijnse Plas.

It offers spacious non-smoking rooms with free parking on site. Each room at the dairy farm features a private bathroom with a shower, hairdryer and toilet.

Tea/coffee making facilities are available. Free Wi-Fi is also included. Breakfast is available every morning upon request.

Guests can also make use of the shared kitchen facilities. Several restaurants can be found within 3 km from Boerderij Hazenveld.

The lake Loosdrecht Lakes is located 10 kilometers away, while Kasteel de Haar is 5 minutes by car.
