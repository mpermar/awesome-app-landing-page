Description

Lentemaheerd is located in Uithuizen, 750 m from motorway N363. It offers non-smoking rooms with free WiFi access and free private parking on site.

Each room here will provide you with a seating area and a TV. The private bathroom is fitted with a shower and free toiletries.

At Lentemaheerd you will find a garden, barbecue facilities and a terrace. Other facilities offered at the property include meeting facilities and a shared lounge.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including billiards.

Roodeschool train station is 1 km away from Lentemaheerd. Groningen is 29 km. The Wadden sea, which is a protected nature area without beach, is 8 km away from the accommodation.
