Description

Hotel Marenland Winsum is located in Winsum, only 1 km from the train station. It offers guest rooms with free WiFi.

Each room here will provide you with cable TV, a CD player and a seating area. There is also an electric kettle.

The private bathroom comes with a shower. At Hotel Marenland Winsum you will find a garden, barbecue facilities and a terrace.

Other facilities offered at the property include water sports facilities, a tennis court, meeting facilities and luggage storage.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling, fishing and canoeing.

The property offers free parking. Groningen is 14 km from the hotel, while Lauwersmeer is 19.
