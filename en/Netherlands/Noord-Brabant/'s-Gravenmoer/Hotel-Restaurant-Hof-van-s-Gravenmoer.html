Description

The family-run Hotel Restaurant Hof van 's Gravenmoer is set in the quiet ‘s Gravenmoer countryside and overlooks an orchard.

It has a terrace and spacious rooms with free wired internet. Rooms come with a flat-screen TV and a desk.

All include a private bathroom, and some have a glass-walled shower. Some open onto a terrace.

The breakfast room at Hotel Hof serves home-made breakfast and has a fireplace. Guests can also relax in the bar, which features wooden-beamed ceilings and a rustic décor.

The hotel is equipped with a private restaurant, where guests can enjoy an à la carte dinner.

Hiking and cycling routes are available at the on-site tourist information point.

Guests can cycle to Brabant Golf, just 8 km away. The Efteling amusement park is a 10-minute drive, and the Beekse Bergen Zoo is 30 minutes’ drive from ‘ s Gravenmoer.
