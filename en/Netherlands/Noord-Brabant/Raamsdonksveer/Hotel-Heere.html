Description

Featuring a restaurant, Hotel Heere is located in the North Brabant region, 15-minutes by car from theme park the Efteling and nature park De Biesbosh.

You can discover the surroundings by bike, foot, or canoe. Free WiFi is available.

Make use of free on-site parking and rent a bicycle or electric boat to explore the area.

The Hotel Heere is a small and intimate hotel, located in the heart of the charming village of Raamsdonksveer.

Wake up each morning with a free breakfast buffet and plan your day at leisure. Benefit from the free internet connection, both Wi-Fi and wired.

The bar and outside terrace are great places to relax with a drink and enjoy the warm atmosphere.

The traditional feel of the hotel will add to your pleasant stay. The restaurant serves delicious 3-course meals for a delightful dinner.
