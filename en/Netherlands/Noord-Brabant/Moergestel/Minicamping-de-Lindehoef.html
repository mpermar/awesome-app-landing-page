Description

The apartment has a cable flat-screen TV and 2 bedrooms. It also has a seating area, a fully equipped kitchen with a microwave, and a bathroom with a shower.

If you would like to discover the area, cycling is possible nearby and the apartment can arrange a bicycle rental service.

Eindhoven is 15 miles from Minicamping de Lindehoef, while Den Bosch is 11 miles from the property. Eindhoven Airport is 11 miles away.
