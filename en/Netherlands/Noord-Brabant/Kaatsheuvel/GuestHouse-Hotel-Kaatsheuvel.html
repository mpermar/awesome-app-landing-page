Description

All guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Featuring a private bathroom, rooms at GuestHouse Hotel Kaatsheuvel also feature free WiFi. All rooms at the accommodation come with a seating area.

Breakfast is available every morning, and includes continental, buffet and vegetarian options.

GuestHouse Hotel Kaatsheuvel has a playground. Guests at the hotel will be able to enjoy activities in and around Kaatsheuvel, like cycling.

The nearest airport is Eindhoven Airport, 21 miles from the property.
