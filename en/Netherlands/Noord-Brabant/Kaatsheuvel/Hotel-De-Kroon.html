Description

At only 5 minutes' walk from the Efteling theme park, Hotel de Kroon offers an ideal base for families.

Park your car nearby for free. The 24h reception can be found next door. The spacious and modern rooms have black-out curtains for a good nights rest.

Start the day with a breakfast buffet. The lounge area offers coffee after a busy day.

The special amusement area for children makes sure you can recover from the day while the kids are busy playing.

Hotel de Kroon is located near the Beekse Bergen zoo. If you want to discover the area, you can explore the beautiful natural surroundings by bike or on foot.
