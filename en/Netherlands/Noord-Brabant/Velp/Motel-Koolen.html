Description

Motel Koolen is situated in Velp Noord-Brabant along a wood-like park, surrounded by beautiful gardens in a quiet area.

Benefit from free parking facilities at the hotel, as well as free WiFi and enjoy the friendly and welcoming atmosphere.

The hotel has comfortable double rooms that are well-kept. Each room has a private bathroom and TV, as well as free tea/coffee making facilities.

All the rooms are situated on the ground floor. You can enjoy a free breakfast in the garden when weather is nice.

The restaurant serves tasty meals for your evening dinner, upon request. The friendly staff will be happy to help you find the best cycling routes or provide you with any other information about the area.
