Description

Situated on the edge of Eindhoven’s grand park, Hampshire Hotel Parkzicht Eindhoven offers a garden, a terrace and free Wi-Fi access.

The elegant rooms offer a TV, coffee- and tea facilities and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The hotel's restaurant is open 7 days per week. Here, guests can enjoy lunch and dinner.

During the Summer months, dinner can also be served on the terrace. This hotel has an excellent location situated right across the green Stadswandelpark and the Parktheatre.

Hampshire Hotel Parkzicht Eindhoven provides a convenient base in this green area.

In front of the hotel free parking is provided for hotel guests. The city centre of Eindhoven can be reached by foot within 15 minutes.
