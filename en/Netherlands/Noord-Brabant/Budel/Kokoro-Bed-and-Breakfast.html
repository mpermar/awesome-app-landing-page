Description

Kokoro Bed and Breakfast is located in Budel. Free Wi-Fi access is available. Rooms here will provide you with a TV and a seating area.

Tea and coffee making facilities are included. The private bathroom is fitted with a bath or shower.

At Kokoro Bed and Breakfast you will find a terrace and a shared kitchen. Other facilities offered include a shared lounge, a games room and a tour desk.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling, bowling and fishing.

Eindhoven is 22 km away. Bus stop The Deken van Baarsstraat is 180 m away and offers direct access to Weert.
