Description

Van der Valk Hotel 's-Hertogenbosch - Vught is located in Vught and easily accessible from the A2 and N65.

There is free parking, and the centre of Den Bosch is a 5-minute car ride away. There is a bus stop across the road.

All rooms have air conditioning, a balcony and a private bathroom with a separate bathtub and walk-in shower.

There is a restaurant, a lounge and a piano bar. The terrace is nice during summer days and at a surcharge you can make use of the fitness room and sauna next door.

The Efteling is a 20-minute car ride from Van der Valk 's Hertogenbosch - Vught.

There are various possibilities to walk and cycle in the surrounding area. The hotel also offers a taxi service that can bring guests to the city centre of 's-Hertogenbosch for EUR 5.
