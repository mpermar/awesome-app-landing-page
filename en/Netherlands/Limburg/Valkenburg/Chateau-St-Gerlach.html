Description

Set in between extensive parks, a vineyard, herb gardens, apiaries, an orchard, a rose garden and a church with 18th century frescoes, Château St.

Gerlach offers attractive accommodations in the beautiful Geul Valley, near Maastricht.

Admire the baroque gardens and unwind in the wellness centre. The characteristic rooms have pretty decorations and provide space and comfort for a relaxing stay.

An extensive breakfast is served in the garden pavilion, with views on the spice garden.

Each room features free WiFi access. The hotel has a cosy lounge and bar where you can have a drink.

Do not miss out on the wellness centre, with its pretty indoor Roman swimming pool, sauna and Turkish steam bath.

Dine in the French-orientated à la carte restaurant and have wonderful wines complement your dishes.

Enjoy locally inspired cuisine in the gastro-pub setting of Bistrot de Liège or order artisan coffee and light bites at Burgemeester Quicx, Coffee & More.

Château St. Gerlach offers free parking. You can walk on the estate or read a book in the rose garden.
