Description

Boshotel Vlodrop is situated in the middle of the forests of the National Nature Park De Meinweg, near Roermond.

The hotel has its own restaurant and accommodation with forest views. Boshotel Vlodrop has 80 rooms with a bathroom.

The large suites have a private terrace, a bath and views of the surrounding scenery.

The hotel highly recommends the wellness centre Vita Allegra with swimming pool, cardio fitness area, sauna facilities with steam bath, infrared and sauna cabins.

There is also a beauty centre with 7 salons, solariums, relax area and much more.

The fitness room, the swimming pool with jet stream, spa bath and the swimming pool sauna can be used free of charge.

Guests can buy a day ticket for the sauna landscape which includes a Finnish sauna, Turkish steam bath, infra red cabin and more.

For beauty treatments, reservations are recommended. Golf course 'De Herkenbosche' is 7 km from the hotel.
