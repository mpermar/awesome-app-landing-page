Description

Bed & Breakfast Angelbeeck is a family-owned accommodation located in the Maasduinen in Afferden and offers 4 guest rooms and 1 cottage with free WiFi access, as well as free private parking.

Each room features a garden view. It will provide you with a private bathroom that is fitted with a shower and towels.

At Bed & Breakfast Angelbeeck you will find a children playground and a terrace. benefit from free coffee and tea.

Meeting facilities are available. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including horse riding, hiking and cycling.

Guests. The secured bicycle shed has charging points for electric bicycles. Bed & Breakfast Angelbeeck is a pet-friendly accommodation, also horses can be accommodated National park Maasduinen is located next to Bed & Breakfast Angelbeeck.

The Hertog Jan Brewery is at 20 km, the thermal baths are at 22 km and Nijmegen is 30 km away.
