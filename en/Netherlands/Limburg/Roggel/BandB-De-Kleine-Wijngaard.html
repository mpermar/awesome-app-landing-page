Description

Located in Roggel in peaceful, rural surroundings at 1 km from the natural area Leudal, B&B De kleine Wijngaard offers accommodation with free WiFi access.

Each unit comes with a TV and private bathroom that is fitted with a bath and shower.

During the morning breakfast is served in the breakfast room by De Kleine Wijngaard.

River Maas is 6 km from the accommodation, while recreation park De Leistert is 1.

4 km away. For shopping guests can go to the designer outlet in Roermoend, which is 20 minutes by car.
