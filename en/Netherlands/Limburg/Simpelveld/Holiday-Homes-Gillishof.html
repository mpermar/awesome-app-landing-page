Description

Holiday Homes Gillishof is located in Simpelveld and offers self-catering accommodations with free WiFi access.

Each unit will provide you with a TV and a terrace. There is a well-equipped kitchen with a refrigerator and kitchenware.

The private bathroom is fitted with a shower. At Holiday Homes Gillishof you will find a garden and barbecue facilities.

Other facilities offered at the property include a tour desk, luggage storage and a children's playground.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling.
