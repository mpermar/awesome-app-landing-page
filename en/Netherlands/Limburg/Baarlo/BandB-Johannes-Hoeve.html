Description

Set in Baarlo, 49 km from Dsseldorf, B&B Johannes-Hoeve features free WiFi access and free private parking.

Rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV. All rooms have a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

You will find a kettle in the room. All rooms are fitted with a private bathroom.

Extras include free toiletries and a hair dryer. There is a shared lounge at the property.

A number of activities are offered in the area, such as golfing and cycling. The bed and breakfast also offers bike hire.

Eindhoven is 45 km from B&B Johannes-Hoeve, while Duisburg is 47 km from the property.
