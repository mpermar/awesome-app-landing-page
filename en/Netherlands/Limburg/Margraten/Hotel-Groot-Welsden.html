Description

Resting in a peaceful area with a lovely garden that overlooks the Limburg hillside, this hotel is renowned for its friendly service, cuisine and English style decoration.

The front desk staff will be happy to advise you about golf courses, hiking, cycling and day trips to the Limburg hillside or the Ardennen in Belgium.
