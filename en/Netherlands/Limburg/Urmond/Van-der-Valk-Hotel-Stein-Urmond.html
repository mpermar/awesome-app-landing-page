Description

This Green Key awarded hotel offers rooms with a seating area and balcony, only 20 minutes’ drive from Maastricht.

Hotel Stein includes a health club, solarium and bar with fireplace. Free WiFi access is also available here.

Each of the rooms at this hotel benefits from a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with a bath and separate shower.

Guests can enjoy seasonal cuisine and dishes with organic ingredients in the restaurant at Van der Valk Hotel Stein Urmond.

The bar serves a range of beers, whiskey and cocktails inside and on the terrace.

The Hoge Kempen National Park is just over a 15-minute drive from Van der Valk Hotel Stein.

Aachen, just over the German border, is 30 minutes’ away by car. Free public parking is available on site.
