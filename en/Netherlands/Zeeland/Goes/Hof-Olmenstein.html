Description

Offering free Wi-Fi access, Hof Olmenstein is located on the edge of Goes. The accommodation will provide you with a TV and a living room.

There is a fully-equipped kitchen with a microwave and oven. The bathroom comes with a bath or shower and free toiletries.

At Hof Olmenstein you will find a garden, barbecue facilities and a terrace. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including horse riding, cycling and table tennis.

The property offers free parking. If you feel like visiting the surroundings, check out Goes Station (3.

5 km) and Art Gallery De Kaai (4. 1 km). This holiday home is 26 km from Middelburg.
