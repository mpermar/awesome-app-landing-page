Description

Located on the Noord-Beveland part of Zeeland, this hotel features rustic surroundings and cosy accommodation with free Wi-Fi.

Apart Hotel Het Veerse Meer has an in-house bar and outdoor terrace for guests to relax.

It serves a buffet breakfast in the cozy breakfast room. The bedrooms feature parquet floors and bright colours.

Guests benefit from extra long beds and private bathrooms. The nearby area is ideal for hiking and cycling trips.

Apart Hotel Het Veerse Meer is set within 500 meters or Kortgene’s restaurants and cafés.
