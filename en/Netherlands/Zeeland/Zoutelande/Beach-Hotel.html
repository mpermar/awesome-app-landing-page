Description

Located directly at the dunes, the Beach Hotel offers accommodation in Zoutelande.

The beach and Nort Sea are 100 metres away, while the centre of Zoutelande is 500 metres away.

It benefits from an on-site restaurant, terrace and wellness facilities. Free WiFi and parking are provided.

All rooms feature a private terrace, balcony or French balcony. The private bathroom boasts a shower, toilet and washstand.

Each room is also equipped with Auping or Eastborn bedding, air conditioning and a 42-inch LED TV.

Restaurant Brasserie De Walvis serves á la carte lunch and dinner, or a daily menu is offered.

Extras include wellness facilities with qualified masseuses. Free coffee and tea can be found on each floor.

The nearest airport is Antwerpen, which is 100 km away. Middelburg is 10 km away.
