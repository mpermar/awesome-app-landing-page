Description

All of the rooms at Hotel Restaurant 't Klokje have a TV, minibar and modern bathroom facilities. Every morning a buffet breakfast is served including fresh bread, cheeses, cold cuts and cereals. Fresh juice and freshly brewed tea and coffee are also available.

Restaurant 't Klokje serves international cuisine in the bright dining room which has floor-to-ceiling windows offering views of the grounds and pond.

The hotel is 1.5 miles from the center of Renesse on the Schouwen-Duiveland part of Zeeland. Burgh is just over a 10-minute car ride away. Guests can rent a bike at the hotel to explore the local area.
