Description

Situated only 250 metres from the beach, Hotel De Zeeuwse Kust is located in Renesse and offers 2 restaurants.

Children can enjoy the water playground and spray park. Free WiFi access is available.

Each room here will provide you with air conditioning, a balcony or a terrace. There is a well-equipped kitchen with a microwave and a refrigerator.

Featuring a shower, the private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer. Beach caf DOK and restaurant La Costa Pizza & Pasta both offer a roof terrace and views over the park.

At Hotel De Zeeuwse Kust you will find a tennis court. Other facilities offered at the property include entertainment staff, a kids' club and a children's playground.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling and mini golf.

The hotel is 2. 5 km from Slot Moermond. Rotterdam The Hague Airport is 71. 3 km away.
