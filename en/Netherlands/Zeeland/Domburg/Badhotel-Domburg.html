Description

Offering a spa, Badhotel Domburg is a hotel within walking distance (800 meters) from the sea.

Enjoy free access to the swimming pool. All rooms are spacious and functional with natural materials and sunny colours.

The rooms and modern bathrooms are fully-equipped. A large breakfast buffet awaits you in the morning.

For dinner, Restaurant Zee & Land warmly welcomes you with a selection of international and regional dishes.

The Grand Café 1866 is a stylish place for a snack or a refreshing drink whenever you like.

SPA Dombug offers multiple saunas, relax areas and a water square. The spa is decorated with materials offered by the region Zeeland, like a wall of mussels and oysters.

For your comfort, the hotel also boasts a library. During the school holidays and weekends, a playroom is specially set up for the children.

Domburg has nice shops and many different restaurants and terraces. The nearest city is Middelburg, with its historic city centre and medieval abbey.

Bicycles are available for rent for those who wish to explore the surrounding area.
