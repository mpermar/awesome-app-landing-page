Description

Strandhotel Dennenbos is located in a historical building, at a distance of 500 metres from the beach and 1 km from the center of Oostkapelle.

The forest and dunes are located within walking distance. All rooms have been recently renovated and feature a box spring bed, a flat screen TV, a rain shower, a toilet and a sink.

Three of the rooms feature a small balcony. The Family Room has a connecting door and can accommodate 4 guests.

Guests can enjoy a breakfast buffet on request. Guests of Strandhotel Dennenbos can make use of all facilities of Vakantiepark Dennenbos, located across the road.

At this park, guests can find a seasonal heated outdoor-pool with a seasonal toddler bath, a childrens' playground, bike rental and a summer supermarket.

Guests can enjoy several activities in the nearby surroundings, including cycling, walking, water sports and golfing.

Domburg is located at a distance of 5 km, while Veere can be found at a distance of 12 km and Middelburg is 13 km away.
