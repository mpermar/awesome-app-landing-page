Description

With a tranquil location between the Zeeland village of Oostkapelle and the beach, Hotel Irene offers proximity to nature and a range of leisure activities.

Free WiFi and free parking are also available to all guests. Some rooms of Hotel Irene are located on the ground floor and have a private veranda.

There are also rooms available on a higher floor with a balcony with a rural view or a view of the forest.

A wholesome breakfast buffet is served every morning, while the restaurant is open upon request.

On a nice day, you can enjoy a drink on one of the terraces. Hotel Irene has a games room, go-karts (upon request) and bicycles for rent.

A swimming pool and a wellness centre with sauna are located within 5 km. The town Oostkapelle and the beach are both 1 km from the hotel.

Domburg is 3 km from the hotel. Larger cities such as Middelburg 10 km, Vlissingen 20 km, and Goes 35 km are some of the places in the area that are definitely worth a visit.
