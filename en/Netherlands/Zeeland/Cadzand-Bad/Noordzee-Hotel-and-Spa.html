Description

Noordzee Hotel & Spa is a comfortable accommodation situated on top of the dunes of Cadzand.

This luxurious 4-star hotel offers a wellness centre, free Wi-Fi, free parking and a lift.

Pamper yourself with a visit to the wellness area. Free of charge, you can use the sauna, the bio-sauna or the steam bath with its relaxation room.

You can even get different massages and body treatments. Rest in 1 of the reclining chairs by the indoor swimming pool after a refreshing swim.

Relax in your room which is nicely decorated and has a private bathroom. Each room is decorated in a unique style and has different decorations.

Start your day with an extensive breakfast buffet at the coffee bar. You can also go here for a cup of tea or for an evening drink.

The hotel has a charming terrace on the south, where you can sit in the sun and experience the quiet atmosphere.

The greenhouse restaurant offers tasty fish dishes in a charming setting. Watch the stars while making your evening dinner a romantic experience.
