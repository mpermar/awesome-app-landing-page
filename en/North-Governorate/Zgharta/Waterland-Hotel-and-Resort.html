Description

Guest rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a electric tea pot, a shower, free toiletries and a desk. Every room includes a private bathroom, while some have a mountain view. The rooms include a seating area.

A à la carte breakfast is available every morning at the property. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of American dishes and also offers Halal and Vegan options.

The resort has a terrace.

Jbeil is 25 miles from Waterland Hotel and Resort. The nearest airport is Beirut Rafic Hariri International Airport, 47 miles from the accommodation.
