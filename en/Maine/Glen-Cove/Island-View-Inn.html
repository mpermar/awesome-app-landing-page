Description

Guest rooms at the Island View Inn feature a balcony, cable TV and a refrigerator. They are brightly decorated with beach-theme décor and have a private bathroom. Rooms come equipped with binoculars.

Visitors can relax poolside at the Rockport Island View Inn. Free parking is also available to all guests.

Several dining options are within easy driving distance of the property.

Windjammers American Eagle boat cruise is a 5-minute drive away. The Farnsworth Art Museum is 3 miles away.
