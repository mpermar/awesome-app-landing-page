Description

Located on Southport Island, the Ocean Gate Resort is surrounded on 3 sides by a tidal inlet. The resort’s accommodations are nestled amongst the tall pines and perched on the rocky coastline overlooking the water.

With a wide range of activities offered, guests can make the most of the resort's facilities on a complimentary basis. These include kayaks, and canoes, fishing, tennis, miniature golf, basketball courts, outdoor pool and much more.
