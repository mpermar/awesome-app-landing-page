Description

The air-conditioned units include a kitchen with a microwave, a toaster and dining area. A fridge and stovetop are also offered, as well as a electric tea pot.

Guests can dine in the on-site restaurant, which specializes in local cuisine and also offers Vegetarian and Gluten-free options.

The resort village has a grill.

A private beach area is available on site.

Agnes Water is 2.2 miles from Captain Cook Holiday Village 1770, while Long Island is 25 miles away. Gladstone Airport is 47 miles from the property.
