Description

Luxurious 5-star water-front resort with direct beach access and a horizon pool just 15 minutes' drive from Cairns International Airport.

Vue Apartments offer extra spacious and stylish self-contained units, all with private balconies offering stunning views of the Coral Sea.

The apartments include a flat-screen TV, internet connection, CD and DVD player. Each of the units has a bath and is equipped with a generously appointed kitchen with granite bench, oven and microwave.

They offer a contemporary decor with designer linens. Guests can eat at the Trinity Beach Hotel, right next to the Vue Apartments, which features a restaurant and a bar.

Barbeque pavilions can be found in the pool area. Vue Apartments Trinity Beach is located 20 minutes north of Cairns.

The resort provides a car rental and a tour desk to explore world-famous Great Barrier Reef and Daintree Rainforest.
