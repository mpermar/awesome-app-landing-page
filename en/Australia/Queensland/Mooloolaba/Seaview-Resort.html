Description

Centrally located on the Sunshine Coast, the 12-storey high-rise Seaview Resort Mooloolaba features apartments with free WiFi and a balcony offering views over Mooloolaba Beach.

Seaview Resort is within walking distance of fine restaurants and shops. The Seaview Resort has a heated swimming pool, a small tennis court, BBQ facilities and sauna rooms.

The spacious apartments at Seaview Resort feature well-equipped kitchens, large windows and a sliding door that leads out to a private balcony.

Each apartment offers a fully equipped kitchen. Seaview Resort Mooloolaba is a 15-minute drive from the Sunshine Coast Airport.
