Description

Nautilus is located in the heart of Mooloolaba just steps away from year-round patrolled Mooloolaba Beach.

Nautilus Mooloolaba is located across the road from The Wharf, Underwater World and the Mooloolaba River.

It is a short walk to The Esplanade. All of the apartments are self-contained with air conditioning and free cable TV.

Most have a spa bath. Resort facilities include a heated resort pool with a waterfall, children's facilities, heated spa, fitness room and barbecue area.

There are many popular restaurants, cafes and shops within walking distance. Guests can enjoy a tour of the Hinterland and vineyards or visit attractions like Aussie World, 13 km from the property.

Whale watching exhibitions are available nearby. Maroochydore Airport is 20 minutes away.
