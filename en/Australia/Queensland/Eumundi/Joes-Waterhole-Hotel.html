Description

Guests at Joe's Waterhole Hotel enjoy access to free WiFi, a bar and a bistro. Located opposite the popular Eumundi Markets, it offers air-conditioned accommodation with a flat-screen TV.

After a long day you can have a cold beer or a glass of wine with friends at the bar.

The bistro offers a variety of tasty dishes, including steak and pasta. Each room has a fan, ironing facilities, a refrigerator and an electric kettle.

Most offer access to shared bathroom facilities. Apartments feature a fully equipped kitchen and a DVD player.

Joe's Waterhole is 20 minutes’ drive from Noosa. Coolum Beach and Sunshine Coast Airport are both 25 minutes’ drive away.
