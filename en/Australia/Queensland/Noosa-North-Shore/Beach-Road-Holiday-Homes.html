Description

Offering luxurious eco-friendly holiday homes, surrounded by native trees and pristine coastline, Beach Road Holiday Homes are the ideal place to relax and unwind.

Guests can laze in and around the swimming pools or hot tub. Beach Road Holiday Homes offer an abundance of air, light and space.

Each offers large entertaining areas, open-plan living spaces and a large screened patio with BBQ facilities.

The leisure centre features 2 swimming pools, a hot tub and a tennis court. There is a games room with billiards tables and gaming consoles.

Younger guests can enjoy the children’s playroom. Hastings Street is only 20 minutes’ drive from Beach Road Holiday Homes.
