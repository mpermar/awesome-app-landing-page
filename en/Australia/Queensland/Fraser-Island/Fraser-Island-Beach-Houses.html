Description

Guest rooms at the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV and a kitchen. Fraser Island Beach Houses provides some accommodations with sea views, and rooms have a coffee machine. All rooms will provide guests with a dishwasher.

Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Fraser Island, like fishing and hiking.

Hervey Bay is 22 miles from Fraser Island Beach Houses. The nearest airport is Hervey Bay Airport, 20 miles from the property.
