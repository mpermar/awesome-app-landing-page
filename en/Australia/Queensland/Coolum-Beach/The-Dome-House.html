Description

With its unique timber structure and boutique resort feel, The Dome House provides guests with an outdoor swimming pool, tropical garden, free WiFi and on-site parking.

The Dome House is located a 10-minute drive from natural beauty of Mt Coolum National Park.

The property is located 6 km from Sunshine Coast Maroochydore Airport and 7 km south of Noosa.

Guests can enjoy relaxing on the lounges beside the pool, or watch a movie on the flat-screen TV in the shared living area.

A fully equipped communal kitchen featuring an oven, stovetop, and a dishwasher is provided.

Each bright and modern room features a fan, bed linen and towels. Some rooms offer a balcony with outdoor furniture and garden or mountain views.

All rooms include a private bathroom with a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Some bathrooms include a bath. The Dome House offers 24-hour front desk service, guest laundry and luggage storage.
