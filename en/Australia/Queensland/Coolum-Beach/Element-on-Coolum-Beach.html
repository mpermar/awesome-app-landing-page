Description

Element on Coolum Beach is surrounded by beautiful landscaped gardens with cascading water.

The resort features on-site shops, cafes, restaurants and a heated outdoor pool with sun loungers.

The resort is located just across the road from Coolum Beach’s fully patrolled surf beach.

Free WiFi is available at the property. Element on Coolum Beach offers apartments and suites.

The apartments have a spacious private balcony, laundry and well-equipped kitchen facilities and a large living area with DVD player.

Secure covered car parking and elevator access are provided. BBQ facilities are available.

On-site restaurants include Coffee Club cafe, Coolum Thai Spice Restaurant and Mr Mancino Restaurant which serves Italian dishes.

Element Day Spa offers facials and massages. The apartments are a 15-minute drive away from Sunshine Coast Airport and a 20-minute drive from Underwater World.
