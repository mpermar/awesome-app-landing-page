Description

Overlooking the Coral Sea at Point Arkwright, The Point Coolum features self-contained villas with private balconies and ocean views.

All villas have a CD/DVD player and a washing machine. The Point Coolum Hotel is a 15-minute drive from Sunshine Coast Airport and a 25-minute drive from Noosa.

The hotel is within easy reach of golf courses, shopping and dining. Guests can choose between 2 tropical swimming pools.

Some villas have a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher. Villas with beautiful ocean views are also available.

The hotel is a short walk from secluded bays and a 5-minute drive from Coolum Beach.
