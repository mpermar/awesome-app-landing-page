Description

Clubb Coolum Beach Resort offers self-catering holiday apartments directly opposite Coolum Beach.

Guests can relax in the swimming pool with heated spa pool, or play on the full-size tennis court.

Each apartment at Clubb Coolum Resort includes 2 hours of free Wi-Fi per stay, a full kitchen, laundry facilities and large living/dining area with satellite TV.

All apartments have a private balcony with ocean views. Barbecue facilities are located amongst the peaceful gardens.

The on-site restaurant, Bombay Bliss, specialises in Thai cuisine. There are many dining options within 5 minutes' walk.

Facilities include a games room, golf putting green, and air-conditioned fitness centre.

Free secure underground car parking is provided. The tour desk can arrange trips to Fraser Island.

Coolum Palmer Golf course is 5 minutes' drive away and Sunshine Coast Airport is only 11 km from the resort.
