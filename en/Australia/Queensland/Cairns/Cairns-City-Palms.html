Description

Cairns City Palms self-contained suites and apartments are located 20 minutes' walk from the centre of Cairns, and a 10-minute drive from Cairns Airport.

5-minute walk away you will find supermarkets, restaurants, bars, bottle shops, a fitness centre and other take away food outlets.

Free WiFi is available throughout the motel. The Palms Private Lounge gives guests access to a TV, computer terminal with free printing facilities along with a shower and toilet.

Free tea, coffee, juice and water are provided throughout the day. From 7:00 until 10:00 each morning, free homemade scones with cream and a selection of spreads are offered.

Guest facilities include a tropical outdoor swimming pool with a Balinese style hut, pool side tables with chairs and sun lounges for guests to relax and enjoy the year-round warm weather.

Escape the sun in the Palms Garden Hut which features barbecue facilities with table and chairs, a TV and refrigerator.

Guest laundry, onsite parking (subject to availability) and luggage storage is also available.

If you are visiting the Cairns region to enjoy the outdoor activities, the tour desk can assist.

Guests receive 10% off all tour bookings. Other close by attractions include the Cairns Central Shopping Center, Cairns Civic Theater, Cairns Convention Center, Cruise Ship Terminal, Cairns Museum, botanical gardens, Cairns Marina, Reef Casino, night markets and Munro Martin Parklands.

Cairns City Palms is located in the beautiful Tropical North Queensland city of Cairns.

Cairns is Australia's gateway to the Great Barrier Reef and is surrounded by rainforest covered mountains.
