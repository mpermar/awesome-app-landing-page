Description

Featuring 2 outdoor pools set in lovely gardens, Toowong Villas offers Apartment style accommodation with free WiFi, a courtyard or balcony, a fully equipped kitchen or kitchenette with most apartments having full laundry facilities.

Free covered parking is available on site for 1 car per apartment. Toowong Villas Brisbane is 3 minutes' walk from Toowong Shopping Centre and 5 minutes' walk from Brisbane River.

It is 10 minutes’ drive from South Bank and Brisbane Airport is a 20-minute drive away.

Accessed via stairs only, all air-conditioned apartments have flat-screen satellite TV and a DVD player.

Each apartment features exposed brick walls. Some apartments are exclusively pet friendly but you need to advise the property of this requirement prior to arrival and are subject to availability.

Guests can enjoy a casual meal in the barbecue area. The property also offers a children’s playground and luggage storage facilities.
