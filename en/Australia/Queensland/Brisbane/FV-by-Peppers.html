Description

Offering 1-bedroom and 2-bedroom apartments, each room features air-conditioning as well as a TV. All rooms include a fully enclosed balcony as well as a private bathroom with a bath or shower. There is a well equipped kitchen featuring European appliances.

Guests can enjoy the moonlight cinema or wind down at the yoga retreat or tropical outdoor lounge area. FV by Peppers features an innovative bronze mesh skin allowing the buildings to breathe, with its clever control of light and airflow. There is valet parking at an additional charge, express checkout as well as a 24-hour reception.

Story Bridge is 0.7 miles away while Queen Street Mall is 1.4 miles away from FV by Peppers. The nearest airport is Brisbane airport, which is 7.5 miles from the property.

Fortitude Valley is a great choice for travelers interested in nightlife, restaurants and entertainment.
