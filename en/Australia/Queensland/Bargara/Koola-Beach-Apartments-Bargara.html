Description

Featuring free Wi-Fi access, these air-conditioned apartments include a full kitchen, 42-inch LCD cable TV and private laundry.

Each apartment has a balcony with views and outdoor seating area. Koola Beach Apartments Bargara is located in a modern 5-storey building and features an outdoor swimming pool with spa pool, barbecue area and free secure undercover car parking.

The complex is surrounded by tranquil parklands and is just a 400 metre stroll to Neilson Park Beach, which features picnic areas, a children's playground and an oceanfront walkway.

The tour desk can arrange day trips to Lady Elliot Island and Lady Musgrave Island.

Koola Beach Apartments are less than 10 minutes walk from the shops, cafes and restaurants in Bargara.
