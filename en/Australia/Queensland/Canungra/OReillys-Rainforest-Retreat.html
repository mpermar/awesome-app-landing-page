Description

Set in the World Heritage-listed Lamington National Park, O'Reilly's offers accommodation overlooking the rainforest and mountains.

Complimentary guided bird walks are offered daily and the iconic Tree Top Walk is free of charge.

Guests can enjoy a swim in the outdoor infinity pool, or relax in the hot tub. O'Reilly's Retreat also offers a library with a fireplace and a kids room.

All Retreat rooms have a refrigerator and tea/coffee making facilities. There are no TV's or telephones in the rooms.

The Villas are air-conditioned and include a fully equipped kitchen, flat-screen satellite TV, spa bat and a BBQ on the deck overlooking the mountains or the rainforest.

O'Reilly's is 90 minutes’ drive from Coolangatta Airport. Brisbane’s city centre is 2 hours from O'Reilly's and the Gold Coast is a 90-minute drive.

O'Reilly's Dining Room specialises in hearty meals, including lamb shanks and pork belly.
