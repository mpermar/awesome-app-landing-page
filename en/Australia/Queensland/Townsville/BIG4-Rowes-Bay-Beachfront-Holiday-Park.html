Description

Boasting a beachfront location just 10 minutes’ drive from Townsville city centre, BIG4 Rowes Bay Beachfront Holiday Park offers spectacular views to Magnetic Island.

Guests can have a hit of tennis, a swim in the outdoor pool, or prepare a meal in the BBQ area.

Free parking is available on-site. Positioned on Cleveland Bay Beach, the property is a short 6-minute drive to The Strand which features a water park.

It is a 10-minute drive from Breakwater Ferry Terminal, which provides ferries to Magnetic Island.

Townsville Airport is also within a 10-minute drive away. At BIG4 Rowes Bay Beachfront Holiday Park, you can enjoy a range of on-site facilities including a children’s playground, beautiful gardens and a shared kitchen.

A Mini Market is available for small necessities. Each air-conditioned cabin or villa here offers a kitchen or kitchenette featuring a microwave and a refrigerator.

All include a flat-screen TV, linen and towels. A private bathroom or access to shared bathroom facilities is provided.
