Description

Boutique Apartments Beach Location 2 Bedroom 2 Bathroom are only 20 metres from The Strand, Townsville.

The beach, BBQ areas, an Olympic-sized swimming pool, a fitness centre, water-park plus a variety of additional attractions are 2 minutes' walk from the property.

Free unlimited WiFi is available in all units. All superior apartments have 2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, air-conditioning, balconies and both seating and dining areas.

There are also kitchens fitted with a dishwasher, large refrigerator, oven and a microwave.

A toaster, kettle and condiments are also available. Every unit has 2 private bathrooms with a shower and free toiletries as well as a full laundry.

Towels, bed linen and beach towels are provided. Boutique Apartments Location 2 Bedroom 2 Bathroom is within a 10-minute walk to over 50 cafes, restaurants and entertainment venues.

Free unlimited WiFi is available in all units. Tour information and extensive local knowledge is available to all guests.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including horse-riding, snorkelling, windsurfing and trips to Magnetic Island.

Townsville 400 Racetrack Start/Finish line is 2. 2 km from the property. Townsville Airport is 6 km away.
