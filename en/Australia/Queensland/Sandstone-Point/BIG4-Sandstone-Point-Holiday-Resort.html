Description

Boasting a fitness centre, garden, bar and free WiFi, BIG4 Sandstone Point Holiday Resort is set in Sandstone Point, a 10-minute walk from Spinnaker Sound Marina and 2.

8 km from Pacific Harbour Marina. Each room comes with a patio. All units at the resort come with a seating area and a flat-screen TV.

Each room has a private bathroom, while certain rooms come with a kitchen with a microwave.

All guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge. The Sandstone Point hotel is a 2-minute walk from the BIG4 Sandstone Point Holiday Resort.
