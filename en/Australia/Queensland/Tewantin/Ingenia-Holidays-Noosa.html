Description

Offering an outdoor pool with a toddlers pool, a giant outdoor chess board and a jumping pillow, Ingenia Holidays Noosa is located in Tewantin.

All accommodation features cooking facilities and a patio or balcony. Ingenia Holidays Noosa is 5 minutes' drive from Noosa Marina and Doonella Lake.

It is 15 minutes' drive from Noosa River. It is 45 minutes' drive from Steve Irwin's Australia Zoo.

Accommodation options include self-contained bungalows and villas. All include a TV, a dining area and outdoor furnishings.

Some accommodation offers garden views. At Ingenia Holidays Noosa you will find a garden, BBQ facilities and a shared kitchen.
