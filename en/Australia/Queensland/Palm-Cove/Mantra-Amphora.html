Description

The hotel rooms and self-contained apartments at the Mantra Amphora have private balconies with either garden, pool or ocean views. Most also include a spa tub.

Guests can relax by the lagoon-style pool and the tour desk can arrange trips to local attractions. Free covered parking is provided.

Located at the front of the resort, Vivo Bar and Grill serves modern Italian cuisine and features a relaxed vibe and uninterrupted ocean views. A large selection of fine wines and cocktails are also available.

In one of tropical North Queensland’s most sought-after destinations, Mantra Amphora is within walking distance of a range of restaurants, bars and boutique shops.
