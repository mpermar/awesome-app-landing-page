Description

This 4-star hotel is adjacent to the Great Barrier Reef, next to restaurants, shops and galleries.

It is a 2-minute walk to the Palm Cove Esplanade and a 10-minute drive to the Skyrail Rainforest Cableway.

Free parking and free WiFi are provided. The apartments at Oasis Palm Cove have island-style d√©cor and feature a full kitchen and lounge/dining areas with front and rear balconies.

Guests at the Oasis Palm can enjoy its lagoon-style pool and spa. The hotel also has a BBQ area and full-size tennis court.

It is a 20-minute drive from Cairns International Airport. Guests receive discounts for tours and restaurants when staying at the Oasis at Palm Cove.
