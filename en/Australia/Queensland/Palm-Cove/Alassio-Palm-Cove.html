Description

Situated on the beach in picturesque Palm Cove, Alassio is an apartment complex just 35 minutes’ drive from the Cairns city centre and 25 minutes’ drive from Cairns Airport.

Alassio offers studios and apartments, all overlooking the hotel’s tropical gardens or the Coral Sea.

Alassio on the Beach is located at the northern end of Palm Cove and is a 5-minute walk to the resort centre with its many shops, bistros and restaurants.
