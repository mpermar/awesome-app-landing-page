Description

Rainbow Beach Holiday Park offers accommodation just a 3-minute drive from the town centre of Rainbow Beach.

It’s positioned on a tidal inlet of the Great Sandy Strait and has a boat ramp right at it’s doorstep.

Free private parking is available on site. Guests can enjoy water activities such as boating, fishing and kayaking at the holiday park.

The nearest airport is Hervey Bay Airport, 67 km from Rainbow Beach Holiday Park.
