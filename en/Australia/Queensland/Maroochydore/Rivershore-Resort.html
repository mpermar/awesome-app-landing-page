Description

At the resort, all rooms are equipped with a balcony. Selected rooms here will provide you with a kitchenette with a microwave and a fridge. All rooms in Rivershore Resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV and free toiletries.

A à la carte or Full English/Irish breakfast is served at the property. At the accommodation you'll find a restaurant serving Australian cuisine. Gluten-free, Vegan and Vegetarian options can also be requested.

Rivershore Resort has a playground. A variety of popular activities are available in the area around the resort, including cycling and fishing.

The nearest airport is Sunshine Coast Airport, 3.7 miles from the property.
