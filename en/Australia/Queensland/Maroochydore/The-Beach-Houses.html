Description

Just 100 metres from Maroochydore Beach, The Beach Houses offer a private balcony.

The property features an outdoor pool and a barbecue area with outdoor seating. Free parking is available on-site and free WiFi is available in all apartments.

Laundry facilities and a full kitchen with a dishwasher and microwave are standard in all apartments.

Each air-conditioned apartment has a spacious seating area with cable TV. The Beach Houses Maroochydore are 5 minutes’ drive from Underwater World and the popular Sunshine Plaza Shopping Centre.

Australia Zoo is a 30-minute drive, and Sunshine Coast Airport is 11 km away. Guests can enjoy a meal in the barbecue area, or swim in the pool.
