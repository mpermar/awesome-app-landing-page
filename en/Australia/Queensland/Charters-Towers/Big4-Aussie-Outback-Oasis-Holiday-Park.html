Description

Featuring a 21-metre resort style pool, Big4 Aussie Outback Oasis Holiday Park offers self-contained accommodation located in Charters Towers.

Fun filled activities offered on-site include a jumping pillow and a children’s playground.

Free WiFi access is provided. Surrounded by landscaped gardens, Big4 Aussie Outback Oasis Holiday Park is located a 5-minute walk from the Charters Towers Racecourse.

A range of dining options on Gill and Mosman Street are a 6-minute drive from the property.

The Charters Towers Golf Course is also a 6-minute drive away. A range of additional amenities are available at Big4 Aussie Outback Oasis Holiday Park, including a guest laundry, free parking and an outdoor BBQ area.

Each air-conditioned cabin provides a private bathroom and a seating area including a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

You can prepare a light meal in the kitchenette which offers kitchenware and a microwave.

After a day of action, take time to relax on the patio and soak in the garden views.
