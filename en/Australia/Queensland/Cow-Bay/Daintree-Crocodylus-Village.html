Description

Daintree Crocodylus Village offers environmentally friendly accommodation, with the sights, sounds and smells of the Daintree Rainforest at your door.

Facilities include a caf, bar and outdoor pool. Nestled deep within 21 acres of private rainforest, Daintree Crocodylus Village Resort is just 10 minutes drive north of the Daintree River Ferry Crossing.

Cow Bay Beach is only 3 km away. The safari-tent style rooms are built from wood and canvas, with high ceilings, fans and mosquito net.

Some have a private bathroom. All have a private or shared veranda with chairs and rainforest views.

The lodge has a cafe and bar serving breakfast, dinner and drinks or you can self cater in the fully equipped self-catering kitchen.

Self-service laundry and ironing facilities are available. Guests have access to a games room, pool table and bicycle hire.
