Description

Offering a beachfront location and two swimming pools, Comfort Resort Blue Pacific is a 15-minute drive from Mackay city centre, Mackay Harbour and Blue Water Trail.

It is set on a long stretch of beach in Mackay with approximately 6 km of golden sand.

Guests enjoy free WiFi, beachfront BBQ facilities and a half tennis court. Mackay Airport is a 30-minute drive away.

All accommodation at Comfort Resort Blue Pacific Mackay offers air-conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, DVD's as well as a microwave, fridge and stove.

Most units have a fully equipped kitchen and laundry facilities. Guests can enjoy a game of table tennis or billiards in the games room and arrange activities at the tour desk.

There are a range of dining options and a convenience store within walking distance.
