Description

Featuring an outdoor pool and a restaurant and bar, Seabreeze Hotel is located just 2 minutes' walk from Lamberts Beach.

This hotel also offers meeting facilities and a children's playground. The semi self-contained and pet-friendly rooms all include air-conditioning, a seating area with local TV, a dining table, and a kitchenette.

Some rooms also come with a balcony or garden views. Guests can dine at the restaurant and enjoy drinks at the bar.

Packed lunches are available upon request. There is free private parking on site.

Seabreeze Hotel is situated a 10-minute drive from Mackay's city centre and a 25-minute drive from Mackay Airport.

Transfers to and from Mackay Airport are available for guests, with bookings essential and confirmed 48 hours before your arrival.
