Description

Offering a saltwater outdoor pool, a BBQ area and a jumping pillow, BIG4 Mackay Marine Tourist Park is located just a 5-minute drive from Mackay's Harbour Beach.

The self-catering accommodation comes air conditioning and a private kitchen or kitchenette.

It also includes a sofa with satellite TV and a dining area. Fitted with a shower, the private bathrooms also come with towels.

Guests can relax by the pool or enjoy the BBQ facilities. There is also a fully-equipped communal kitchen area and an internet kiosk.

Free parking can be found on site. BIG4 Mackay Marine Tourist Park is situated only a 5-minute drive from the Mackay town centre, and 10 minutes' drive from the Bluewater Trail.
