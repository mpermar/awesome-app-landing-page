Description

Villa Mar Colina offer Mediterranean-style villas with views of the Keppel Bay Islands.

Located 150 metres from the restaurants and shops in Yeppoon’s town centre, it includes free satellite TV.

All accommodation at Villa Mar Colina features modern kitchens with refrigerator, dishwasher and microwave.

Each has a private balcony or terrace, and some also feature private barbecue facilities.

Guests can relax by the tropical swimming pool. Other facilities include an outdoor entertainment area and a tour desk.

A private garage with electronic remote control for one car is provided for each villa.
