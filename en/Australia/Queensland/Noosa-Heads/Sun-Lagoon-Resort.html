Description

Boasting a waterfront location overlooking Noosa River, Sun Lagoon Resort features a private beach and a private boat jetty.

It offers apartments with a private balcony and lagoon, river or pool views. You can enjoy a game of tennis, or relax in the spa pool.

Sun Lagoon Apartments also offer an outdoor swimming pool, a sauna and a games room with billiards.

Paddle boards and kayaks are available for hire. All apartments have laundry facilities and a full kitchen with a dishwasher and an oven.

Each apartment has a separate living room with flat-screen cable TV and a DVD player.

The property is just 2 km from Hastings Street and Noosa’s Main Beach. Noosa National Park is a 10-minute drive, and Sunshine Coast Airport is a 35-minute drive away.
