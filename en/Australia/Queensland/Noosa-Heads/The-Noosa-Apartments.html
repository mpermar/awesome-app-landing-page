Description

Located opposite the Noosa River, The Noosa Apartments offer self-contained accommodation with a private balcony or patio.

Guests have access to an outdoor pool, a hot tub and a sunny rooftop terrace. The Noosa Apartments are just 5 minutes’ walk from Noosa Main Beach, and 15 minutes’ drive from Noosa National Park.

Sunshine Coast Airport is a 35-minute drive away. The air-conditioned apartments have laundry facilities and a full kitchen with a dishwasher and an oven.

Each apartment has 2 bathrooms and a spacious living room with flat-screen cable TV and a DVD player.

You can enjoy a meal in the barbecue area located on the rooftop terrace, or in the garden.

The tour desk can book fishing charters and day trips to Fraser Island. Free parking is available on site.
