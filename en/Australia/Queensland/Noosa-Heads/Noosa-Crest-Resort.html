Description

Noosa Crest Resort is situated on a hilltop near Noosa Heads with views over Laguna Bay and the Noosa River.

Facilities include 2 heated swimming pools, 2 spa pools and 2 saunas. Guests can enjoy the 2 rooftop BBQ areas, or make use of the tennis court.

Wireless internet access is available at an additional charge. Each apartment has a private balcony and many have water or parkland views.

All have a kitchen with dishwasher, seating area and in-room safe. The lounge area comes with a TV, DVD and CD player.

With private access to the Noosa River, Noosa Crest Resort are only a 5-minute walk via private boardwalk to Main Beach and the famous Hastings Street, with a variety of shops and restaurants.
