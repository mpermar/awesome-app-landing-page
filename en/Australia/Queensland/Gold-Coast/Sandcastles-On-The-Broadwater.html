Description

The spacious apartments at Sandcastles on the Broadwater come with full kitchen and laundry facilities. All air conditioned units have a private balcony and cable TV.

Guests can enjoy a meal in the barbecue area, or unwind in the spa bathtub. The tour desk can assist guests with travel arrangements and tickets to theme parks and cruises. Baggage storage is also available.

Sandcastles Gold Coast is only a 15 minute drive from major theme parks, including Dreamworld and Wet ‘n’ Wild Water World. A number of shopping outlets are just a 5-minute drive away.
