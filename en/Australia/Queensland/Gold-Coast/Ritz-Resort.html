Description

Just 75 metres from Kurrawa Beach, this resort offers free Wi-Fi, a heated swimming pool and a rooftop terrace.

Situated in the heart of Broadbeach, these air-conditioned apartments each boast a balcony and a fully equipped kitchen.

Jupiters Casino and the Gold Coast Convention Centre are both only 10 minutes’ walk from The Ritz Resort Gold Coast.

The restaurants and shops of Broadbeach are just a 2-minute stroll away. Guests can relax in the landscaped gardens or share a BBQ on the rooftop terrace.

There is also a fitness centre with a sauna. Free secure car parking is provided.

All apartments offer sea, pool or city views. Each has a lounge area featuring a sofa and a flat-screen TV with cable channels.
