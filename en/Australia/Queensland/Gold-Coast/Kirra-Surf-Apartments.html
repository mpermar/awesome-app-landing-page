Description

Relax in your spacious apartment at Kirrasurf and enjoy the chic and minimalist design. Start each day with breakfast on your private balcony and admire the stunning sea views. Watch satellite TV in the comfort of your lounge.

The inviting outdoor pool is the ideal place for a cooling afternoon swim. A fully equipped gym and sauna are also available for your use.

Just a short walk from Kirrasurf Apartments, explore the expanding restaurant and cafe precinct. If you prefer open-air dining closer to home, barbecue facilities are also available at Kirrasurf.

Coolangatta is a great choice for travelers interested in surfing, relaxation and beaches.
