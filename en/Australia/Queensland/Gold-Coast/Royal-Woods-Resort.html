Description

This world-class resort features unique natural tropical surroundings, exquisite rooms and breathtaking views, with spacious villas set amongst trees and plants providing abundant privacy and peace.

Each villa is self-contained with kitchen, air conditioning, spa bath, laundry and garage.

Some select villas offer balcony views of the scenic hinterland. Royal Woods is ideal for the ultimate family holiday.

Royal Woods Resort offers a range of 1 and 2-bedroom suites or 3-bedroom villas, providing the freedom and comfort of a home-away-from-home.

The Gold Coast's dazzling nightlife offers a choice of entertainment and the theme parks provide unbeatable action.
