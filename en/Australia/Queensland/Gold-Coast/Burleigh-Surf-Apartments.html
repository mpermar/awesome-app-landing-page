Description

Boasting an indoor heated pool, a spa pool and a tennis court, Burleigh Surf Beachfront Apartments are situated opposite the patrolled North Burleigh Beach.

All apartments offer a spa bath and a large balcony offering superb ocean views. Guests have access to a steam room, a fitness centre and a games room.

Set amongst landscaped gardens, the complex also has an internet kiosk and 3 covered BBQ areas.

Each of the apartments offer air-conditioned living areas and a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher.

For your convenience, a one-off starter pack of consumable items is provided upon arrival.

Burleigh Surf Apartments Gold Coast is less than 30 minutes’ drive from Wet n Wild, Movie World, Seaworld and the Australian Outback Spectacular.
