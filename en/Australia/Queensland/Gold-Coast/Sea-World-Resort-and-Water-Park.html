Description

Located on Broadwater, linked by monorail to Sea World and only 15 minutes' walk from the popular Surfers Paradise and Marina Mirage Shopping area, this 4.

5-star hotel boasts a 50-metre lagoon pool and waterfall as well as an aquatic playground and 12-seater hot tub.

Rooms at Sea World Resort are spacious and boast a contemporary décor. Each room is fitted with a balcony or patio which offers views of either the broadwater, pool or surrounding gardens.

Guests enjoy free WiFi in all areas of the resort, as well as free wired internet in their room.

At the resort's award-winning restaurant, Hatsuhana, guests can enjoy Japanese cuisine cooked Tepinyaka-style from fresh seafood and ingredients.

The Lobby Lounge offers a variety of meals, from light snacks to delicious steaks.

Buffet breakfast and dinner is available daily at the Shoreline Restaurant. Guests at Sea World Resort have unlimited access to the Water Play aqua playground in the main pool area.

Other activities include the SpongeBob SplashBash and evening light show. The resort is surrounded by beaches and public beaches are accessible within a 2-minute walk.
