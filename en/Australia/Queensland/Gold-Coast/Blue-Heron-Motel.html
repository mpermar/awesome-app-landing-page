Description

Blue Heron Motel is located just 2 minutes' walk from Mermaid Beach. The family-owned, boutique style motel was renovated in 2018 and features a garden and BBQ area created by an award-winning landscape designer.

Blue Heron Motel offers free WiFi, a large swimming pool and free parking. All rooms feature a flat-screen Smart TV and are located on the ground floor.

Blue Heron Motel is 5 minutes' walk from local stores and cafes. There are also restaurants and shopping centres within 5 minutes' drive.

Surfers Paradise is a 10-minute drive away, while Cbus Super Stadium and Metricon Stadium are both within 15 minutes' drive.

All rooms offer air conditioning and heating with en suite bathrooms that include a shower, hairdryer and guest toiletries.

Deluxe rooms include a Nespresso Coffee Pod Machine, refrigerator, microwave, kettle, crockery and cutlery, in-room safe.

Communal facilities include a guest laundry. Blue Heron guests can relax in the landscaped garden area located next to the pool or enjoy using the communal BBQ facilities.

There is free bicycle hire and free beach towels for your convenience. The bus stop is close by, providing easy access to all of the Gold Coast’s attractions.

The tour desk can arrange tickets and transfers to theme parks such as Dreamworld, Sea World and Wet 'n' Wild.
