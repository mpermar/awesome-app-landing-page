Description

Relax by the heated outdoor pool and spa pool at Pelican Cove, only 15 minutes’ drive from Surfers Paradise.

Each spacious apartment has a private balcony offering garden or pool views. Guests enjoy free WiFi.

Pelican Cove Apartments Gold Coast are a 15-minute drive from Seaworld, Wet ‘n’ Wild, Movie World and Dreamworld.

Gold Coast Coolangatta Airport is only 45 minutes' drive away. Each apartment includes a fully equipped kitchen with an oven, a stove, a dishwasher and a microwave.

A separate dining area and a lounge room with air conditioning and satellite TV are provided.

Laundry facilities are provided. Free secure covered car parking is included. The large gardens include a BBQ area and tennis half-court.

Guests can hire DVD players and DVD movies from the reception desk, as well as purchase theme park tickets, day tours and dinner cruises.
