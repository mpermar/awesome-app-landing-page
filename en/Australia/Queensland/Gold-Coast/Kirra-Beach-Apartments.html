Description

Set among landscaped grounds opposite the main beach, Kirra Beach Apartments offers accommodation with a private balcony, most with ocean views.

It features an outdoor pool, spa and limited Wi-Fi access. The modern apartments of the Kirra Beach are fitted with a fully equipped kitchen and washing machine.

There is a seating area offering cable TV and a DVD player. All the units have air conditioning.

Property facilities include a covered BBQ area and free underground parking. The tour desk at Kirra Beach Apartments help you discover local attractions such as the Currumbin Wildlife Sanctuary and the numerous theme parks on the Gold Coast.
