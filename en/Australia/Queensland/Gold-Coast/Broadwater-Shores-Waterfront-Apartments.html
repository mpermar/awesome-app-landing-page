Description

Just 3 minutes walk from The Broadwater, Broadwater Shores Waterfront Apartments offer apartments with a wrap around balcony overlooking The Broadwater or the hotel's tropical gardens.

The property features 2 outdoor pools and a tennis court. Free WiFi is provided in the ground floor common area.

Laundry facilities and a full kitchen with a dishwasher and microwave are standard in all apartments.

Each apartment has a spacious seating area with cable TV. Broadwater Shores Waterfont Apartments is 10 minutes drive from Surfers Paradise, and 20 minutes drive from Jupiters Casino.

Gold Coast Airport is a 40-minute drive away. Guests can enjoy a meal in the barbecue area, or relax in the sauna.

The tour desk can book tickets to the Gold Coast theme parks, including Dreamworld and Movieworld.
