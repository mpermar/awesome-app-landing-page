Description

Just 300 metres from Main Beach, Chidori Court features spacious, self-contained 2 bedroom, 2 bathroom apartments with a private balcony or patio.

Set in lush tropical gardens, the property boasts a heated pool, a hot tub and an entertainment area with BBQ facilities.

Chidori Court Apartments include laundry facilities and a full kitchen with a dishwasher and microwave.

Each apartment has a living room with a TV. Guests can request a DVD player from reception.

When you are ready to go out, you can make use of the tours and tickets service, or visit the nearby golf course.

Buses to Jupiters Casino, Pacific Fair and the Broadbeach shops are just minutes away.
