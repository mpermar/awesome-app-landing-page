Description

Circle Luxury Holidays Apartments are located in the centre of Cavill Avenue, a short walk from the mall, restaurants, clubs and the beach.

Free private parking is available on site. Guests have access to the buildings lap pool, swimming pool, heated pool, spa, sauna and steam room, as well as a fully equipped fitness centre.

Circle Luxury Holidays features a balcony with stunning views of Surfers Paradise Beach or river and hinterland views.

The air-conditioned, private apartment has a fully equipped kitchen and laundry facilities.

The kids will be entertained with access to the buildings games room and an outdoor play area right near the BBQ, TV viewing platform and gardens.

Gold Coast Convention and Exhibition Centre is 3. 1 km away, while The Star Gold Coast Casino is 3.

4 km away. Marina Mirage Shopping Centre is 3. 6 km from Circle Luxury Holidays, while Australia Fair Shopping Centre is 3.

6 km from the privately owned and managed apartment. Gold Coast Airport is 20 km away.
