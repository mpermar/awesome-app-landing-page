Description

Situated 3 km from Gold Coast Convention and Exhibition Centre, Cavill Avenue Luxury Private Apartments offers an outdoor swimming pool, a fitness centre and accommodation with a balcony.

The accommodation features a spa bath. The units all have a seating area with a sofa, a flat-screen TV, DVD player, a fully equipped kitchen and a private bathroom with free toiletries.

A dishwasher, an oven and microwave are also available, as well as a kettle. At the apartment you will find a restaurant serving Asian cuisine.

Cavill Avenue Luxury Private Apartments offers a range of wellness facilities including a hot tub and a sauna.

The accommodation has a barbecue and a garden. The Star Gold Coast is 3. 4 km from Cavill Avenue Luxury Private Apartments, while Pacific Fair Shopping Centre is 3.

7 km from the property. The nearest airport is Gold Coast Airport, 20 km from the apartment.
