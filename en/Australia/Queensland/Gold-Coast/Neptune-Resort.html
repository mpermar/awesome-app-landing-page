Description

Featuring a hot tub, sauna, swimming pool and tennis court, Neptune Resort is located in the heart of Broadbeach, just 5 minutes’ walk from Kurrawa Beach.

A private balcony overlooking the Gold Coast is standard in all apartments. Guests enjoy free on-site parking.

Neptune Resort Apartments is just 10 minutes’ walk from The Star Casino and the Gold Coast Convention Centre.

It is 5 minutes' drive from Surfers Paradise. Coolangatta Airport is a 25-minute drive away.

All air-conditioned apartments feature laundry facilities and a kitchen with a dishwasher and microwave.

Each bright and airy apartment features wall art and a seating area with a TV. High speed WiFi is available in each room, and fees may apply.

The tour desk can book tickets to Gold Coast theme parks, including Dreamworld and Wet ‘n’ Wild Water World.

Hard Grind Cafe, refurbished in October 2015, serves breakfast and lunch daily, while the BBQ area is perfect for sharing a casual outdoor meal.
