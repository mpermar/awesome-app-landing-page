Description

Located on the Nerang River, Surfers Hawaiian Apartments offer scenic riverfront views, a swimming pool and well-equipped gymnasium.

Gold Coast Airport is a 25-minute drive away. Spacious and fitted with modern furnishings, each apartment has a Smart TV, a lounge area and well-equipped kitchen fitted with storage space.

Each one has a large balcony with a seating area. Surfers Hawaiian has a large swimming pool and plunge pool, fitted with shaded seating.

Facilities also include a marina and boat ramp. Gold Coast Convention and Exhibition Centre is less than 2 km away from the apartments.
