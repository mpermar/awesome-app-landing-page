Description

Wyndham Surfers Paradise Resort offers self-catering apartments with an LCD TV, an iPod docking station, a home theatre system and a laundry.

Free WiFi and free private parking are provided. All spacious apartments include full kitchen facilities, balconies, air conditioning.

and free movies to watch. Each apartment has double-glazed windows, and most have ocean, river or hinterland views.

Built in 2009, the 17-storey hotel has a recreation deck as well as a spa pool, outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre.

Guests can make use of the barbecue facilities and outdoor seating area. Refreshments are available from the coffee shop and wine bar.

Wyndham Surfers Paradise is located in the heart of the Gold Coast. Restaurants and shops are on the hotel’s doorstep.
