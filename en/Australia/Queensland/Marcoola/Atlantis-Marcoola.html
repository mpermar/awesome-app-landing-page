Description

Just 2 minutes walk from Marcoola Beach, this resort offers a heated lagoon-style pool, a hot tub and half-size tennis and basketball courts.

All accommodation at Atlantis Marcoola features a balcony and cooking facilities.

Guests can enjoy a fitness centre, jumping pillow, childrens playground and a covered BBQ area.

There is a games room with billiards, an air hockey table and car racing machines.

Atlantis Marcoola Beachfront Resort is a 5-minute drive from both Sunshine Coast Airport and Novotel Twin Waters Golf Course.

Maroochydore is 10 km away and Mooloolaba is 15 km away. All accommodation is air conditioned and offers a stove, microwave and refrigerator.

Each apartment or studio has a dining area and a sofa. Some apartments offer a hot tub or ocean views.
