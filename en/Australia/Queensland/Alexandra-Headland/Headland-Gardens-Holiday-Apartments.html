Description

Situated just 1 minutes' walk from the Alexandra Headland surf beach, Headland Gardens offers self-contained apartments surrounding a lagoon-style heated pool.

All apartments offer a balcony or courtyard with pool or garden views. Some apartments on higher floors offer ocean views.

Each apartment features a fully equipped kitchen, laundry facilities and a TV with satellite channels.

Guest facilities includes a sauna, steam room and spa pool. The grounds include a half-size tennis court and a games room with table tennis, billiards and computer games.

Being locals to the area for several years, the on-site managers can provide advice on activities and attractions in the area.

They can assist with booking Hinterland and Eumundi Market tours and tickets to Steve Irwin's Australia Zoo, Underwater World or Aussie World.

Mooloolaba’s dining and nightlife is only 10 minutes’ walk away. Free secure undercover parking is available.
