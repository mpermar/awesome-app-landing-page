Description

BreakFree Alexandra Beach is situated in Queensland’s Alexandra Headlands area directly opposite Alexandra Beach.

It offers self-contained apartments with a balcony or terrace and free private parking.

All rooms at BreakFree Alexandra Hotel are air-conditioned and offer laundry facilities.

Some have a kitchenette, others offer a full-sized kitchen. Tour desk staff can help plan outings to local attractions including the Hinterland Mountains.

It is a 1-hour drive from Brisbane Airport. The property offers a 130 meter long lagoon Pool with waterslides and water falls, a swimming pool and 2 heated spas, all surrounded by tropical gardens where guests can relax.
