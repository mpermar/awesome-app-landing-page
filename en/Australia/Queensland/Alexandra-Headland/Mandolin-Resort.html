Description

These stylish apartments, located opposite Alexandra Headland Beach, feature private balconies with Pacific Ocean views.

Facilities include a sauna, table tennis and free cable TV. Each spacious apartment at Mandolin Resort includes a fully equipped kitchen with refrigerator, oven, microwave and dishwasher.

Air conditioning, laundry facilities and a DVD player are provided. Mandolin Holiday Apartments are a 15-minute drive from Sunshine Coast Airport.

Mooloolaba Beach is a 20-minute walk away. Children can play in the games room or outdoor playground while you prepare a BBQ.

Free secure underground car parking is provided. Free internet access is available in the apartments.
