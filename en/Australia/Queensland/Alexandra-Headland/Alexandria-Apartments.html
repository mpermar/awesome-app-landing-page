Description

Just 1 minute's walk from Alex Beach, Alexandria Apartments offer a private balcony with beautiful ocean views.

Guests can enjoy a heated swimming pool, a sauna and a free mini golf course. The spacious air-conditioned apartments include a kitchen with a microwave, oven, hotplate and cooking utensils.

Each apartment has laundry facilities and a living room with flat-screen cable TV.

Wi-Fi access is available in all apartments. You can play a game of table tennis in the games room, or enjoy a meal in the barbecue area.

The tour desk can book whale watching on Hervey Bay and Fraser Island adventure tours.

Alexandria Apartments are located 2km from the Sunshine Plaza Shopping Centre and a 10-minute walk to Mooloolaba's many restaurants.

Maroochydore Airport is a 12-minute drive, and Steve Irwin's Australia Zoo is a 30-minute drive away.
