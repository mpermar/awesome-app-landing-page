Description

Just 5 minutes’ walk from the beautiful Alexandra Headland Beach, clubs, cafes and restaurant dining, this renovated holiday park, with a range of villas and cabins, offers a heated swimming pool and a children’s playground.

All accommodation offers a balcony or patio and a flat-screen LED TV. The Two-Bedroom Holiday Home and Villa's include cable channels.

Free WiFi is available. There is a large covered communal BBQ area with 4 BBQs, pizza oven, large group tables, refrigerator, and a sink, for guests to use.

You can also enjoy a game of billiards in the games room. All air-conditioned rooms feature fully equipped kitchens and dining settings.

Some include a separate lounge area and large individual parking bays to accommodate high vehicles.

The standard cabins have shared bathroom facilities. Alex Beach Cabins and Tourist Park is located in the heart of the Sunshine Coast beside major access roads and public transport.

The property is within 10 minutes’ walk of several shops and restaurants. Steve Irwin’s Australia Zoo is 30 minutes’ drive away.
