Description

Ideally located just 300 meters from the beach in Hervey Bay on the Fraser Coast, Whalecove Apartments offers contemporary, air-conditioned accommodation.

The fully landscaped gardens wrap around a 25-metre lagoon pool and heated spa. The apartments feature a separate dining area and living room with a TV.

Each has fully equipped laundry and kitchen facilities and a private bathroom. There is a recreation room for your enjoyment where you can relax on the lounges or enjoy a game of ping pong.

There are BBQ facilities at either end of the pool with ample outdoor furniture for your use.

Breakfast can be enjoyed at Smokey Joe's Cafe, located a short walk away from Whalecove Apartments.

Whalecove Apartments are just 5 km from Hervey Bay Airport and guests are provided with free private parking on site.
