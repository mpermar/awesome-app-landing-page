Description

Located on the Esplanade alongside the Urangan Harbour, Ramada by Wyndham Hervey Bay boasts an outdoor pool with a spa pool, a restaurant and free WiFi.

There are a range of executive studios and luxurious self-contained suites. The luxurious studio rooms are ideal for business travellers or couples on a romantic escape.

The self-contained, one-bedroom suites are perfect for a longer getaway. Smokey Joe’s Café Bar & Grill is open daily for breakfast and dinner with an enticing menu and live entertainment on Friday and Saturday nights.

The reception desk can provide friendly advice on the best Fraser Coast tours, including trips to Fraser Island and whale watching.
