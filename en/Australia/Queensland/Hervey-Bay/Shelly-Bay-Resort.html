Description

Opposite a family friendly beach, Shelly Bay Resort provides 4-star fully self-contained apartments, with views of Hervey Bay from every apartment.

It features a lagoon-style swimming pool and free WiFi is provided. All apartments at Shelly Bay Resort are air-conditioned and feature ceiling fans, elevator access and LCD TV with satellite channels.

All apartments also have a fully equipped kitchen. The outdoor pool is surrounded by tropical gardens with a barbecue and entertainment area.

Guests have access to a half-size tennis court and children's playground. The resort’s restaurant features an al fresco dining area.

Shelly Bay Resort is the ideal gateway to Fraser Island and The Great Barrier Reef.

The tour desk can provide travel advice and book seasonal whale-watching trips and tours to Lady Elliot Island and Fraser Island.
