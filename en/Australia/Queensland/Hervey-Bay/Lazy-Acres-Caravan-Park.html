Description

Offering a patio, specific units are air-conditioned and feature a dining area and a seating area with a flat-screen TV. A microwave and electric tea pot are also available.

The resort village has an outdoor pool.

A playground can be found at Lazy Acres Caravan Park, along with a shared lounge.

Hervey Bay Botanic Gardens is 1.6 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Hervey Bay Airport, 1.9 miles from the property.

Torquay is a great choice for travelers interested in beaches, beach walks and warm weather.
