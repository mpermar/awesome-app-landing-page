Description

Lychee Tree Holiday Apartments is a resort complex which offers unique 1 and 2 bedroom self-catering apartments.

A swimming pool and waterfall with lounge chairs to relax on are featured, as well as a BBQ area with a large dining table to enjoy a meal.

Free high-speed WiFi is available throughout the property and free parking is offered adjacent to each apartment.

The air-conditioned apartments have individual interiors and offer lounges as well as a dining area, kitchen, bathroom and laundry facilities.

Lychee Tree Holiday Apartments are within walking distance of the Port Douglas Village centre, where there is a huge variety of cosmopolitan cafs and restaurants and many boutique shops.

Four Mile Beach is a 7-minute walk from the property. The Lychee Tree Holiday Apartments are a perfect base for exploring the surrounding World Heritage sites of the Great Barrier Reef, Mossman Gorge and The Daintree Rainforest.

The tour desk at the property offers advice and can book all your tours and experiences to best cater to your needs.
