Description

Outrigger Apartments Port Douglas offers a heated saltwater swimming pool, a tropical garden and free Wi-Fi.

Just 400 metres from the heart of Port Douglas Village and the Marina, it features modern apartments that are fully self-contained.

The spacious apartments offer a full kitchen with a microwave, an oven and a stove.

Each has laundry facilities, a DVD player and a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Guests can enjoy views of the garden from their balcony. Port Douglas Outrigger is a 5-minute walk from Four Mile Beach and 2.

3 km from Port Douglas Golf Course. Cairns International Airport is a 1-hour drive away.
