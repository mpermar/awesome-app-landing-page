Description

The rooms at the resort are equipped with a fridge, a electric tea pot, a hot tub, free toiletries, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and DVD-Player. Some rooms will provide you with a kitchen with a dishwasher. At Sea Temple Port Douglas all rooms include a private bathroom.

The accommodation has a grill. Guests at Sea Temple Port Douglas will be able to enjoy activities in and around Port Douglas, like fishing.

Macrossan Street is 2.9 miles from the resort. The nearest airport is Cairns Airport, 30 miles from Sea Temple Port Douglas.
