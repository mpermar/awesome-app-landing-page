Description

The Mantra Heritage Port Douglas is a 3-minute walk from the cafes, bars and restaurants along Macrossan Street. It is a 15-minute walk to Four Mile Beach.

All rooms and suites feature ceiling fans, tea/coffee-making facilities and cable TV. Suites with kitchenette and laundry facilities are available. Free parking is provided.
