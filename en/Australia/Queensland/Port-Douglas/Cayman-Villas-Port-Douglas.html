Description

Situated just 500 metres from the famous Four Mile Beach, Cayman Villas Port Douglas offers spacious self-catering villas, a refreshing outdoor swimming pool and a relaxing spa pool.

Each of these modern villas has a large living area and spacious private balcony.

The kitchens have granite bench-tops and European appliances. The Cayman Villas provides poolside gazebos and barbecues for casual outdoor dining.

Surrounded by tropical gardens, it is an ideal setting for relaxing after a day at the beach.
