Description

Situated 1 km north of the popular Steve Irwin’s Australia Zoo, Beerwah Motor Lodge features rooms with free WiFi, a flat-screen TV and a DVD player.

Guests can have a swim in the outdoor pool or prepare a meal in the BBQ area. Breakfast is available daily for an additional cost.

You can visit reception to book tickets to Australia Zoo. There is an on-site children’s playground for the younger guests to enjoy.

Beerwah Motor Lodge is 15 minutes’ drive from the Glass House Mountains National Park.

The Ginger Factory and Sunshine Coast Airport are both a 30-minute drive away. All air-conditioned accommodation is equipped with a microwave, a refrigerator, a toaster and an electric kettle.
