Description

Set in a pretty Tuscan inspired garden, the 4-star Toscana Village Resort offers accommodation with free WiFi and panoramic views of the village, Pioneer Bay and the Whitsunday Islands.

Guests enjoy free private parking, 4 outdoor swimming pools. Conveniently located within 5 minutes' walk from the heart of the village, Toscana Village Resort is 200 metres from Airlie Beach.

Conway State Forest is a 15-minute drive from Toscana Village Resort. Each spacious apartment here features a private balcony with views of the ocean.

All include a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge, microwave and a dishwasher. Providing a range of options for the whole family, the property includes a toddlers wading pool, cascading waterfall, and small plunge pool.
