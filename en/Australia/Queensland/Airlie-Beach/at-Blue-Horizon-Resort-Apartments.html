Description

Featuring an outdoor pool and hot tub, Blue Horizon offers 1, 2, 3 and 4 bedroom luxury apartments with spacious balconies and Coral Sea views.

Ideally located directly opposite the Port of Airlie, it is a short walk from the Airlie Beach Village dining and shopping precinct.

Guests enjoy free WiFi in the hotel lobby. The modern, Balinese-inspired apartments are air conditioned and offer satellite TV, a microwave, a refrigerator and laundry facilities.

Some apartments feature a spa bath or a hot tub on the balcony. Relax on the sun deck or prepare a meal in the BBQ area.

The tour desk can help you arrange trips to The Great Barrier Reef, world class Whitehaven Beach or one of the 74 Whitsunday Islands.

There is a fitness centre and free undercover parking on site. Blue Horizon Airlie Beach apartments are a 2-minute drive from Airlie Beach.

Funnel Bay is 6 minutes’ drive and Proserpine Whitsunday Coast Airport is 40 minutes’ drive away.
