Description

Located opposite Golden Beach, the Belvedere Apartments each feature a balcony or patio, a fully equipped kitchen and free cable TV.

Guests also enjoy a swimming pool, BBQ facilities and free secure parking on site.

All apartments have a lounge area offering a sofa, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a DVD player.

Each also includes laundry facilities, a dining setting and outdoor furniture. Most apartments offer panoramic or partial water views overlooking the Pumicestone Passage and Bribie Island.

The Noughty Corner was opened in October 2017 next to the BBQ area where guests can place a game of noughts and crosses.

Caloundra Belvedere Apartments is across the road from a children's playground, picnic and BBQ area.

It is 10 minutes’ drive from Pelican Waters Golf Course. Steve Irwin’s Australia Zoo is 30 minutes’ drive away.
