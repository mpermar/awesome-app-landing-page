Description

Watermark Caloundra offers absolute waterfront and non-smoking accommodation with free unlimited WiFi.

Guests enjoy 3 outdoor heated swimming pools including 1 heated year-round, a hot tub, a BBQ area and a private boardwalk for fishing and other water activities.

This accommodation options include air-conditioned self-contained apartments and private motel-style rooms.

Each apartment features a balcony offering water views, a fully equipped kitchen and laundry facilities.

Motel rooms include tea/coffee making facilities and garden (but not water) views.

All accommodation offers a flat-screen satellite TV and a DVD player. Each floor is accessible via elevator.

Free domestic phone calls are included. Located in the heart of the city centre, Watermark Caloundra is 10 minutes walk from Bulcock Beach.

Steve Irwins Australia Zoo and Sunshine Coast Airport are each a 30-minute drive away.

Guests can relax in the BBQ area, or seek travel advice at the tour desk. Caloundra Watermark also offers free on-site parking and luggage storage facilities.
