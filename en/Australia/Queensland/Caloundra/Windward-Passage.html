Description

Just steps away from Golden Beach, Windward Passage is an absolute waterfront property with an outdoor pool, BBQ facilities and a tour desk.

Most accommodation boasts a spa bath and a private balcony with sea views. The tour desk can book hinterland tours, whale watching cruises, trips to Fraser Island and car rentals.

An airport shuttle can also be arranged and charges apply. Each self-contained apartment has a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, and a fully equipped kitchen with an oven, a stove and a microwave.
