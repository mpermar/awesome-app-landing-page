Description

Located in Pumicestone Passage along the Golden Coast, Ramada Resort by Wyndham Golden Beach 4-star resort features a heated lagoon-style swimming pool, a spa pool and a full-size tennis court.

Guests also enjoy a restaurant, a bar and a games room. All accommodation at Ramada Resort Golden Beach features a private balcony, some offering lovely city, ocean and beach views.

Each apartment offers a kitchenette, a TV and a DVD player. Guests can enjoy an outdoor meal in the BBQ area, work-out in the fitness centre or just relax on the sun loungers by the pool.

Surrounded by tropical gardens, Ramada Resort Golden Beach is just 10 minutes' walk from Golden Beach.

There is an array of arts and crafts shops, cafes and dining options within 15 minutes' walk.
