Description

Featuring a private balcony with partial ocean views, Merrima Court is just 100 metres from Kings Beach.

It offers an outdoor pool, a garden area and free undercover parking. Portable BBQs are provided for guests to enjoy on their balconies.

Merrima Court Holidays are only 10 minutes’ walk from Caloundra Surf Club and Caloundra Bus Station.

Caloundra Golf Club is a 5-minute drive away. A laundry and a full kitchen with a dishwasher and microwave are standard in all apartments.

The apartment has a spacious seating area with flat-screen cable TV, a DVD player and a CD player.

Guests can relax on the sunny terrace, or enjoy the ocean views from the poolside deck.
