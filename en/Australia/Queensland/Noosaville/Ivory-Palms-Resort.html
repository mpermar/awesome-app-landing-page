Description

This 3. 5-star resort offers free parking and spacious accommodation, just a short stroll from Noosa River and 6 km from Noosa's main surf beaches.

It features 3 swimming pool areas (one heated), a spa, sauna, barbecue area, and 2 flood-lit tennis courts.

Ivory Palms Resort provides fully-contained 1, 2, 3 bedroom apartments, villas, and townhouses.

Guests enjoy 500MB of free WiFi or 7 days access, whichever comes first. Additional data is available for purchase.

Guests can enjoy walks around the landscaped lagoons. The tour desk can arrange airport transfers, hire cars, day tours and local activities.

The property also offers a supervised Kids Club, which includes activities such as tennis, air hockey, walks face painting, play stations and movies.

Ivory Palms Resort is located close to Noosaville Restaurants, where guests can enjoy great dining experiences in Noosa.
