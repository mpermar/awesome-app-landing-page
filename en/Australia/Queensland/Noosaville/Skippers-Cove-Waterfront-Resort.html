Description

Set on the Noosa River, this waterfront property offers waterfront or poolside apartments with a private balcony or patio.

Guests enjoy access to 2 lagoon style swimming pools, half-size tennis court and access to a private beach.

Free WiFi is available in the rooms. Skippers Cove Waterfront Resort is 20 minutes’ walk from Noosa’s main beach and the popular Hastings Street.

Gympie Terrace, offering an abundance of shopping and dining experiences is also close by.

It is 7 km from Sunshine Beach and 12 km from Noosa National Park. All apartments feature a fully equipped kitchen, laundry facilities and outdoor furniture.

Each has a spacious living and dining area offering sofas, a TV with cable channels and a DVD/CD player.

Kayaking gear is available for free and the private beach offers small wharves, great for fishing.

The property also has a spa, on-site bicycle hire and paddle board hire. All local tours and activities can be booked at reception.
