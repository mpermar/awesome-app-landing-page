Description

Just 150 metres from the Noosa River with its range of restaurants, cafes and boutique shops, Verano Resort offers apartments with a private balcony overlooking the pool and tropical gardens.

Guests can enjoy the outdoor pool, hot tub, children's pool and barbecue area. Free Wi-Fi is included and there is free secure parking available.

Verano Resort Noosa is 2 km from Noosa Main Beach and the popular shopping and dining options on Hastings Street.

Sunshine Coast Airport is a 30-minute drive away. All air-conditioned and spacious apartments have a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a DVD player.

Each apartment has laundry facilities and a full kitchen with a dishwasher and microwave as well as a large living and dining area.

The tour desk can book airport transfers, tours to the Everglades and trips to Fraser Island.

The resort also offers bicycle rental and a sunny poolside terrace. You can catch the ferry to Noosa and enjoy its popular shopping and restaurants then catch the ferry back to Verano or continue on to the Marina.
