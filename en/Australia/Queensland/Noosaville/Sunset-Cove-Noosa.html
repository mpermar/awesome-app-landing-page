Description

Surrounded by beautiful tropical gardens, Sunset Cove Noosa offers spacious self-contained, boutique apartments around a heated lagoon-style pool.

Facilities include a spa pool, sauna and covered barbecue facilities. All apartments are air-conditioned and have a fully equipped kitchen with dishwasher, microwave and full-size refrigerator.

Each apartment contains a flat-screen TV with DVD player. There is an additional flat-screen digital TV in each master bedroom.

Laundry facilities are included. Guests can relax on the sun lounges around the pool or enjoy a casual get together in the barbecue area.

Noosa River, with its pretty boardwalks and parks, is just 50 metres away. The popular shopping and dining precinct of Noosaville is within a 5-minute walk from Sunset Cove Noosa.

The friendly on-site managers can organise airport transfers, car hire and tours.

Noosa Main Beach and the famous Hastings Street are 5 minutes by car or a leisurely 20-minute ferry ride along the Noosa River.
