Description

Set on 5 acres of tropical gardens, The Islander Noosa Resort offers self-contained villas located across from the Noosa River foreshore.

Facilities include 7 restaurants, 3 swimming pools and a fitness room. Each villa has a private garden courtyard, balcony overlooking the tennis courts, or views of the pool.

All are air-conditioned and feature cable TV, DVD player, and full kitchen and laundry facilities.

Guests enjoy 2GB of free WiFi. Guests can relax in the sauna or spa pool. The games room includes an internet kiosk.

Kids clubs facilities are available during school holidays. Free on-site covered parking and free luggage storage are available.

The Islander Resort Noosa is right in the heart of Noosaville, with shops and cafes on the doorstep.
