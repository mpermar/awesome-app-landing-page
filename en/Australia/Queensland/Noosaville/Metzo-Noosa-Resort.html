Description

Metzo Noosa Resort is located just a short walk from the Noosa River and its parkland foreshore as well as the cafe and restaurant precinct of Gympie Terrace.

Directly opposite the resort is the picturesque Keyser Island and Lake Weyba. The property is located 2 km from the famous Hastings Street, which has a variety of restaurants, cafes and shops.

Here you will also find the sparkling waters of Laguna Bay. Each apartment has a private balcony with water views.

Free WiFi, free parking and access to a barbecue area and a heated swimming pool, surrounded by tropical gardens are provided.
