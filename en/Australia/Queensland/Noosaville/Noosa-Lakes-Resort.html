Description

Surrounded by beautiful wilderness, Noosa Lakes is located on 7 landscaped acres by the shores of Lake Doonella and opposite the Noosa River.

Noosa Lakes offers air conditioned studio rooms, 1-bedroom apartments and 2-bedroom townhouses.

All accommodation offers a TV with cable channels, a DVD player and laundry facilities.

There is a large range of restaurants and cafes right opposite the resort at the Noosa Marina.

It is a 10-minute drive from Hastings Street, Noosa Heads Beach, Noosa National Park and other leisure activities such as golf, deep sea fishing and river cruises.

The complex is located 90 minutes north of Brisbane Airport and just 30 minutes from Sunshine Coast Airport (Maroochydore).
