Description

In addition to providing direct access to a beach and fishing jetty, Eastcoaster Tasmania boasts an indoor swimming pool, a hot tub, a tennis court and a basketball court.

Guests also enjoy an on-site restaurant and bar. Eastcoaster Tasmania is 8 minutes’ drive from Orford Golf Club and Prosser River.

Rheban Beach is 20 minutes’ drive and Maria Island is a 30-minute boat ride away.

All suites and cottage come with a flat-screen TV, a microwave and tea/coffee making facilities.

Some feature a fully equipped kitchen or a private patio with outdoor seating. The restaurant is open for lunch and dinner daily.

Other on-site leisure facilities include a squash court, a sauna and a children’s playground.
