Description

The Lighthouse Hotel is located in Ulverstone Town Centre, a 4-minute walk from Leven River and a 15-minute drive from Devonport Airport.

It offers a gym, sauna facilities, free on-site parking and free WiFi. There is an outdoor spa area with a TV and seating.

Accessed via stairs only, all rooms are located on the 1st floor and include tea/coffee making facilities and a flat-screen TV.

The en suite bathroom comes with shower and a hairdryer. The property has a lounge area in the mezzanine level for guests’ use.

There is also a children’s playhouse with a jungle maze. Lighthouse Hotel Ulverstone is a 20-minute drive from Gunns Plains Caves and a 30-minute drive from Leven Canyon.
