Description

Offering free on-site parking, Mole Creek Cabins is located in Mole Creek. This property offers a variety of accommodation, all with cooking facilities and free on-site parking.

Featuring stunning views of the Great Western Tiers, Mole Creek Cabins is an hours' drive from the Devonport Ferry Terminal.

The air-conditioned accommodation options include chalets, studios and holiday homes.

All include a TV, a dining setting, a refrigerator and tea/coffee making facilities.
