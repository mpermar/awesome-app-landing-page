Description

This award-winning boutique wilderness retreat features an on-site restaurant and is situated amongst peaceful rainforest surroundings.

Cradle Mountain - Lake St Clair National Park is only 40 minutes away. Free WiFi and free parking are available.

Lemonthyme Wilderness Retreat offers spacious self-contained cabins, and luxury suites with spa bath and mountain views.

Whispering Woods restaurant is open every day for breakfast, lunch and dinner. The wine list showcases wines from across Tasmania.

The guest lounge features an open log fireplace. There are many walking trails through the forest surrounding the lodge.

Cradle Mountain National Park is a 35-minute drive from the property, while Launceston is a 2 hours' drive away.
