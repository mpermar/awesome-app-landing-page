Description

Featuring free WiFi, Beachend Bicheno offers accommodation in Bicheno. Coles Bay is 29 km away.

Parking is free and private. The accommodation is fitted with a TV, DVD player, and iPod docking station.

Some units include a seating area and/or balcony. An oven, a kettle, and a rice cooker are also featured.

Some units also have a kitchen or a kitchenette, fitted with a microwave. Every unit comes with a private bathroom with free toiletries.

Towels and bed linen are free. Beachend Bicheno also includes a barbecue. A range of activities are offered in the area, such as golfing, fishing and hiking.
