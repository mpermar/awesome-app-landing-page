Description

AAA Granary Accommodation offers self-contained studios and cottages with full kitchen facilities and mountain views.

Guests have free access to a hot tub, sauna and a games room with a PS4 - Playstation.

Nightly rates are offered, with stays of 3 nights or more being at a discounted rate.

All accommodation at AAA Granary Tasmania features heating, electric blankets and laundry facilities.

The spacious lounge area has a TV with DVD/CD player. The balcony provides views towards Mt Roland.

The property features large landscaped gardens and an adventure playground for children.

There is also a fitness room and sports grounds including a basketball court, a cricket pitch and a soccer field.

AAA Granary Accommodation is located next door to Tasmazia Family Village and is a 30-minute drive from Cradle Mountain National Park.
