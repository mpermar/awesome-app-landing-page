Description

Just a 5-minute walk from Scamander Beach, Blue Seas Holiday Villas boasts a heated indoor swimming pool and a games room.

Guests can enjoy self-contained accommodation. Upstairs units offer a balcony while all rooms offer sea views.

Blue Seas Villas is a 2-minute drive from Scamander Sports Complex, offering a golf course, tennis courts and a bar and restaurant.

It is within a 45-minute drive of both the Bay of Fires and Bicheno famous for fairy penguins.

All accommodation features a fully equipped kitchen or kitchenette and a living and dining area with a flat-screen TV.

All accommodation includes ironing facilities, a bathroom and outdoor furniture. Located close to Scamander River, guests can enjoy fishing at their door step.
