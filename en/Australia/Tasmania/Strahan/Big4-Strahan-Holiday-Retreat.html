Description

Located in the heart of Strahan, 2 minutes' walk from West Strahan swimming beach and adventure playground, Strahan Holiday Retreat offers a range of quality accommodation plus a shop and cafe on site.

WiFi is available. Guests staying at Stahan Holiday Retreat can enjoy a cup of coffee at Molly's Cafe, located on-site and open every day.

The cafe also stocks supermarket items. For your convenience there is a bottle-shop located next to the reception, otherwise there are a variety of local bars and restaurants 5 minutes' walk away.

Set on 11-acres of mixed bushland and open spaces, all cabins, studios, apartments and cottages offer TVs, fans, heating, dining areas, kitchens and access to laundry facilities.

At dusk, wild Platypus feature in the natural stream winding through the 8 acre property.
