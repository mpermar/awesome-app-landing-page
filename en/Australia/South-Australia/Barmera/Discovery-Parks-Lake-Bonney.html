Description

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool, a 'water trampoline' and a children’s playground with jumping pillow, Discovery Parks – Lake Bonney offers direct beach access with swimming, kayaking and fishing on offer.

It has a range of cabins with cooking facilities. Situated in the town of Barmera, Discovery Parks – Lake Bonney is a 15-minute drive from Berri and 30 minutes from the towns of Renmark and Loxton.

Banrock Wine and Wetlands Centre is 12 km away. Kayaks, bicycles and paddle boats are available to hire.

Each holiday cabin features air conditioning, tea/coffee making facilities and a refrigerator.

Most have an en suite bathroom. Superior chalet with balcony, outdoor seating and park views are available.

Guests have access to a BBQ area and self-service laundry facilities, with coin-operated washing machines and clothes dryers.
