Description

Victor Harbor Beachfront Holiday Park is located right on the beachfront, with views to Encounter Bay.

Guests can take a short 5-minute walk to the town centre, which offers many cafes, restaurants and shops.

Free wireless internet access is available. Positioned a 1-hours drive from Adelaide on the Fleurieu Peninsula,Victor Harbor Beachfront Holiday Park is a 7-minute drive from Granite Island, where you can visit the Fairy Penguins.

Victor Harbor is an excellent base for visiting the Murray River, the wineries at McLaren Vale and Kangaroo Island.

Accommodation available at the holiday park includes a range of villas and cabins including 2 bedroom, studio and deluxe.

The park facilities include a children’s jumping pillow and playground, barbeque area, camp kitchen and bicycle hire.
