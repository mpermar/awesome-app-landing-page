Description

Discovery Parks - Port Augusta is situated right at the foothills of the Flinders Ranges, where the desert meets the sea.

Guests can enjoy free barbecues, a swimming pool, a toddler’s pool and a camp kitchen.

This 4-star property is a leading holiday park located on 4. 5 hectares. Surrounded by shady trees it is the perfect place to relax.

There is a recreation and TV room for your enjoyment. Wireless internet and a shop offering ice and LPG gas refills are also available.

The property offers comfortably furnished, self-contained cabin accommodation. Port Augusta is the 'back door' to the great Australian outback.
