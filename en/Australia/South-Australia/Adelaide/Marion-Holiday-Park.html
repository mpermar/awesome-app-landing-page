Description

Offering an outdoor pool, an indoor pool and a jumping pillow, Marion Holiday Park is located in Adelaide.

Accommodation options include self-contained cottages, villas and studios. All include cooking facilities and free on-site parking.

Marion Holiday Park is 10 minutes' walk from Tonsley Railway Station. Adelaide Convention Centre and Adelaide Oval are both within 20 minutes' drive.

All air-conditioned accommodation features a lounge area with a sofa, a flat-screen TV and a DVD player.

Some accommodation offers a spa bath or a patio. Guests can play a game of tennis or work out in the fitness centre.
