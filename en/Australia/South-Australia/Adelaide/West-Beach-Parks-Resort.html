Description

Set on the stunning coastline and just a short drive from the Adelaide CBD (Central Business District), West Beach Parks Resort offers direct beach access and a beautiful natural environment with spacious, lush grounds.

There are a range of accommodation options, including deluxe self-contained units, villas and bungalows.

The resort offers a broad range of facilities for guests, including a tennis court, barbecues, jumping pillow, playground, go kart hire and free WiFi.

The AquaCove is open for guests to enjoy, complete with 2 water slides, 5 poolside cabanas and 15 sun loungers.

West Beach Parks Resort also has a central Garden View function room, which is suitable to host meetings, conferences or celebrations, plus ample on-site parking.

The property is 5 minutes' drive from several restaurants and shops. Glenelg, Henley Beach and Harbourtown are within 15 minutes' drive.
