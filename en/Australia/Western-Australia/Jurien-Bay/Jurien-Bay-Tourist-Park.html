Description

The Jurien Bay Tourist Park is a 4-minute drive from Jurien Bay Country Golf Club, and a 30-minute drive from the The Pinnacles in Nambung National Park. Perth is a 2.5-hour drive away.

The air-conditioned cottages have a kitchenette with a stovetop, a toaster, a microwave and a refrigerator. Each cottage has ironing facilities and a private bathroom.

The tour desk can arrange fishing charters, skydiving, sea lion tours and seasonal whale watching. The property also features 2 guest kitchens, weekly entertainment and guest laundry facilities.

The Jetty Café is next to the property and serves breakfast, lunch and snacks. There are a number of dining options within a 2-minute walk of the property.
