Description

Discovery Parks - Port Hedland is located on a water-front location in Port Hedland.

It offers direct beach access and is close to Port Hedland Golf Course. There are a variety of simply furnished units that are surrounded by a green park.

Facilities include a kid’s playground, a pool table, a games room, an outdoor swimming pool, barbecue facilities, a camp kitchen, and a guest laundry.

WiFi access is available. Discovery Parks - Port Hedland has direct access to Pretty Pool Inlet.

Guests can enjoy easy explorations of the Pilbara Region of Western Australia's rugged North West.
