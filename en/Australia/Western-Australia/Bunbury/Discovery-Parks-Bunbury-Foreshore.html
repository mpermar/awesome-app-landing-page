Description

Featuring a tennis court and outdoor swimming pool, Discovery Parks – Koombana Bay offers a range of cabins, villas and chalets with LCD TV, refrigerator, air conditioning and a private bathroom.

Discovery Parks – Koombana Bay is located in Bunbury, midway between Perth and the famous Margaret River Wine Region.

The coastal town of Busselton is less than 1 hour’s drive away. Facilities include a tennis court, large children’s jumping pillow, BBQ area with outdoor seating and an on-site function room.

Pedal buggies are available for hire. All accommodation includes a microwave and tea/coffee making facilities.
