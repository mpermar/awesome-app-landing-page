Description

Situated in Australia's beautiful South West, on the gateway to the Margaret River wine region, Amalfi Resort offers affordable family holiday accommodation a short stroll from the beach.

Guests enjoy free Wi-Fi and a swimming pool. Accommodation options include fully self-contained villas with 2 and 3 bedrooms.

All include a patio, a fully equipped kitchen and a TV with cable channels. Guests can enjoy a leisurely game of tennis, soak in the spa pool or enjoy a BBQ.

The nearby foreshore is perfect for cycling or walking along the sandy beaches of Geographe Bay.
