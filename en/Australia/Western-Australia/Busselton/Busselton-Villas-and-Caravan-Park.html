Description

At the resort, all rooms include a closet. Rooms have air conditioning, and certain units at Busselton Villas and Caravan Park have a balcony. Guest rooms in the accommodation are equipped with a flat-screen TV and free toiletries.

Busselton Villas and Caravan Park has a grill. You can play ping pong at the resort.
