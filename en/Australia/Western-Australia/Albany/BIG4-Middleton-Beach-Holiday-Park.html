Description

Offering free Wi-Fi, a solar-heated swimming pool and a hot tub, BIG4 Middleton Beach Holiday Park is located alongside 500 metres of Middleton Beach.

All accommodation features a fully equipped kitchen and a patio or balcony. Set beside the calm waters of King George Sound, BIG4 Holiday Park Middleton Beach is just 3.

2 km from Albany's city centre. It is 13 km from Albany Airport. There are several walkways, bicycle paths, restaurants, cafes and bar within 10 minutes' walk.

Accommodation options include villas, bungalows and chalets. Each offers a dining setting, outdoor furniture and a lounge area with a sofa, a flat-screen TV and a DVD player.

Some accommodation offers sea views or a spa bath. You can play billiards or table tennis in the games room and there is a kids' club for the younger guests.

A small purpose-built theatre screens films and there is a recreation area with BBQ facilities.
