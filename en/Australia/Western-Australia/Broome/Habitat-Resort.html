Description

This award-winning resort offers 4-star accommodation set amongst 8 acres of tropical gardens.

Located next door to Roebuck Bay Golf Club, it features 2 swimming pools and free in-house movies and free Wi-Fi access in all rooms.

Guests at Habitat Resort Broome have free access to the adjacent golf course. The tour desk can arrange day tours and airport transfers.

Broome town centre is a 5-minute drive away. Each apartment and bungalow includes a full kitchen, laundry facilities, air conditioning and ceiling fans.

Guests can relax on their private verandah with outdoor seating. One of the outdoor swimming pools is surrounded by an outdoor gazebo, sun loungers and free barbecue facilities.

The other pool has a waterfall feature, spa pool and children’s waterslide. Continental breakfast is available and can be delivered to the rooms.
