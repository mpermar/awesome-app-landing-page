Description

The Billi Cable Beach offers holiday villas with fully equipped kitchen, outdoor shower and private patio with barbecue.

All have access to the gardens and swimming pool. Each of the 2-bedroom villas is air-conditioned and includes a large living area with TV and DVD player.

Every villa has an indoor bathroom and free Wi-Fi access. The Luxury Eco Tents feature en suite bathrooms, kitchenettes, outdoor seating areas and BBQ facilities.

Facilities include a guest laundry, outdoor swimming pool and large outdoor terrace.

The tour desk can arrange car and scooter hire, camel riding, 4-wheel-drive tours and scenic flights.

The beach is under 10 minutes’ walk and Broome town centre is a 10-minute drive from The Billi Private Villas.

There are cafes, restaurants and bars within a 3-minute drive. Broome Airport is 6 km away.
