Description

Just 5 minutes’ walk from Preston Beach, Footprints boasts 2 heated swimming pools, a tennis court and a pool bar.

It offers self-contained accommodation with barbecue facilities and a private balcony or patio.

Footprints Preston Beach is 400 metres from the entrance to Preston Beach Golf Club and 3 km from the entrance of Yalgorup National Park and Lake Preston.

The air-conditioned apartments have laundry facilities and a kitchen with microwave, stove and barbecue facilities.

Each apartment has a flat-screen TV and a DVD player. The Resort has a modern bar and restaurant with casual indoor and alfresco dining.
