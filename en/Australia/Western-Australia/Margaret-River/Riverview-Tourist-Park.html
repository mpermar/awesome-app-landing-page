Description

Guests can enjoy a BBQ while gazing at the Margaret River or rent a canoe and explore the area.

Each chalet and cottage includes a flat-screen TV, air conditioning and tea/coffee-making facilities. Most have a kitchenette or a full kitchen with a microwave, stove and kitchenware. Some have a private balcony with river views.

Riverview Chalet Park is a 10-minute drive from the Margaret River Golf Club. It is a 15-minute drive from Prevelly Beach and the Leeuwin Estate Winery. The Margaret River Chocolate Company is a 22-minute drive away.
