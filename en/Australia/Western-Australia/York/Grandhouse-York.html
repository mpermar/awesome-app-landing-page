Description

Situated in a quiet street, Grandhouse York offers free Wi-Fi and free breakfast.

All rooms boast garden views and a flat-screen TV. Guests enjoy a communal lounge area and free use of bicycles.

Located in Western Australia's oldest inland town, Grandhouse York B&B is perfectly situated for visiting historical buildings such as St.

Patrick's Church, built in 1886. The property is 10 minutes' walk from Avon Park and the Avon River.

The air-conditioned rooms include a refrigerator, a DVD player and tea/coffee making facilities.

All offer a private bathroom with a shower, bathrobes and a hairdryer. Some rooms offer a sofa suite.
