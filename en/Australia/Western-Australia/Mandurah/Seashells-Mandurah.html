Description

The bright and airy apartments include full kitchen and laundry facilities and a spacious sitting area with flat-screen satellite TV and a CD player. Most apartments also feature a spa bathtub and stunning ocean views.

Seashells Resort Mandurah is just 1,300 feet from the famous Mandurah Ocean Marina. Mandurah Train Station is a 10-minute drive from the property, and Perth is a 1-hour drive away.

Guests can enjoy a game of tennis, or relax on the sunny poolside terrace. The resort also offers airport transportation and baggage storage facilities.
