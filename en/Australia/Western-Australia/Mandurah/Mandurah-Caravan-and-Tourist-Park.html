Description

Mandurah Caravan and Tourist Park provides a range of self-contained cabins and chalets. All include heating, air conditioning and a kitchenette. Most have a private bathroom and an outside deck area.

Guests can cook and eat outdoors at the BBQ area, with Balinese-style gazebos for shelter and shade. Facilities include an internet kiosk and coin-operated laundry.

Mandurah Tourist Park is a 15-minute drive from Mandurah Ocean Marina, which features a large selection of specialty shops, restaurants, cafes and market stalls. Peel Zoo is 8 miles away.
