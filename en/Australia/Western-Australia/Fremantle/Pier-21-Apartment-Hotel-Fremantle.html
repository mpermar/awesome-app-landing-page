Description

Pier 21 is ideally situated beside the Swan River. Port Beach is less than 20 minutes’ walk away.

All of the modern apartments have balconies. This apartment hotel benefits from an indoor pool with spa, as well as an outdoor swimming pool.

Guests can make use of the communal barbecue facilities. All rooms have been refurbished and feature a 40-inch flat-screen TV and Biology guest amenities.

Pier 21 was awarded Best Apartment/Suite Accommodation at the 2015 Australian Hotels Association Industry Awards.

The property is located just 3 km from the centre of Fremantle home to attractions such as Fremantle Prison and Fremantle Markets.

Fishing Boat Harbour is just a 10-minute drive, and Perth CBD is a 30-minute drive away.
