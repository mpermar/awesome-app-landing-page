Description

Boasting a solar-heated pool and barbecue facilities set in tropical gardens, Kalbarri Edge Resort is just 5 minutes’ walk from the Murchison River.

All accommodation includes free WiFi and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Kalbarri Edge Resort is 5 minutes’ walk from the Kalbarri Family Entertainment Centre, and 4 minutes’ drive from the Kalbarri Golf Club.

Geraldton Airport is a 2-hour drive away. A kitchenette or kitchen with a microwave and refrigerator is standard in all rooms.

Most room types also have a spa bath or a private balcony. You can relax on the sunny terrace, or enjoy a game of chess on the giant chessboard.

The tour desk can arrange horse riding, skydiving and trips to Wagoe Beach. Edge Restaurant features a seasonal menu including Australian favourites such as chicken, steak and seafood.
