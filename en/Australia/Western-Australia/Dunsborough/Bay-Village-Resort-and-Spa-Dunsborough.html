Description

All units at Bay Village Resort Dunsborough include cable TV with free in-room movies. A safe and ironing facilities are provided for guests' convenience. Most rooms also have a full kitchen.

Guests at the Bay Village Resort can unwind at the Day Spa with a massage, or choose from a range of facial and body treatments. The resort also features a barbecue area where guests can relax with friends.

Bay Village Resort and Spa Dunsborough is 2.5 hours south of Perth. The Dunsborough Lakes Golf Course is only a 3-minute walk away. Many of the wineries in the Margaret River Wine Region are less than 3 miles away.
