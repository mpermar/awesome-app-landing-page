Description

Just a 2-minute walk from the sandy shores of Portsea Beach, guests enjoy heated indoor and outdoor swimming pools and a tennis court at Portsea Village Resort.

It also features a fitness centre, sauna, squash courts and free WiFi. Some apartments offer a balcony with sea views.

This resort offers 1 or 2 bedroom apartments with a fully equipped kitchen and indoor/outdoor dining areas.

The lounge area offers a satellite flat-screen TV and a DVD player. Full laundry facilities are available in each apartment.

The on-site restaurant is open on the weekends and offers local Mornington Peninsula wines and produce.

Portsea Village Resort hosts weddings conferences, group functions and special events.

Portsea Village Resort is within a 5-minute drive of Sorrento Beach. Melbourne CBD is a 1.
