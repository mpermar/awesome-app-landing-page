Description

Situated along the famous Great Ocean Road, this holiday park features a swimming pool, hot tub, children’s playgrounds and toddler’s playroom.

All units include air conditioning and private bathrooms. Anglesea Family Caravan Park is only a 3-minute walk from the calm waters of Anglesea Beach and a 10-minute walk from downtown Anglesea.

It is 20 minutes’ drive to Torquay Beach. Each cottage and cabin offers self-catering facilities, including a stove, microwave and tea/coffee making facilities.

Most offer flat-screen TVs with cable channels and private balconies. Some cottages also feature spa baths.

Leisure facilities include mini golf, table tennis, games room, internet kiosk and an indoor heated hot tub.

The outdoor heated swimming pool includes a wading pool. Guest laundry and covered BBQ facilities are available.
