Description

Located opposite the beach, Cumberland Lorne Resort offers free WiFi, free secure parking and spacious modern apartments in the heart of Lorne town centre.

Facilities include a swimming pool, fitness centre and squash and tennis courts. Each apartment at Cumberland Resort Lorne features a private balcony with outdoor furniture, a corner spa bath and free cable TV.

Fully equipped kitchen and laundry facilities are also included. Guests have access to a sauna, BBQ facilities and a hot tub.

Cumberland Lorne Resort is a 5-minute drive from Lorne Country Club and 15 minutes from Erskine Falls.
