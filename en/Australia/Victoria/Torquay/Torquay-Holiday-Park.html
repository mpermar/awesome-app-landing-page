Description

Just 1 km from Torquay Main Beach, Torquay Holiday Park offers a solar-heated outdoor pool, a spa and a large variety of sports facilities.

All self-contained cottages include a TV, microwave, stove and tea/coffee making facilities.

Some have a fully equipped kitchen, a DVD player or a private patio. On-site sports facilities include tennis courts, a mini-golf course, cricket net and beach volleyball.

There are undercover BBQ facilities where you can prepare a fresh-cooked meal. The games room offers table tennis, table soccer and arcade games.

Torquay Holiday Park is 5 minutes’ walk from Torquay town centre. It is 8 minutes’ drive from The Torquay Sands Golf Course and 22 minutes’ drive from Geelong.
