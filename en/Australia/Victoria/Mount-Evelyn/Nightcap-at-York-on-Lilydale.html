Description

Boasting a restaurant, bars and a lovely outdoor swimming pool, York On Lilydale is surrounded by peaceful bushland, manicured gardens and a rustic lake.

Each air-conditioned room features a private en suite bathroom, bar fridge, electric kettle, flat-screen cable TV and a balcony or a courtyard.

The Family Bistro offers delicious menu options including daily specials. There is also an indoor and outdoor children's playground.

The on-site sports bar is the perfect place to meet friends for a refreshing drink.

Function facilities are also available. For guests wanting to stay active, there is a tennis court to be enjoyed.

York On Lilydale Mount Evelyn is a 10-minute drive from Dorset Golf Course and a 25-minute drive from Healesville Sanctuary.

The Yarra Valley wine-growing district is a 30-minute drive away, and Melbourne is a 45-minute drive.
