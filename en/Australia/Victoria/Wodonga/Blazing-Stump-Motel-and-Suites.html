Description

Featuring free WiFi and free parking, Blazing Stump Motel & Suites offers 2 seasonal swimming pool, a gym, a sauna, tennis court and basketball court.

It is a 3 minutes’ drive from Wodonga’s town centre. BBQ facilities and laundry facilities are also provided.

Blazing Stump Motel & Suites is 6 minutes' drive from Albury’s town centre. It is 8 minutes' drive from Albury Airport.

The Rutherglen wineries are within 45 minutes’ drive. The Lake Hume is a 7-minute drive.

All air-conditioned accommodation features a kitchen or kitchenette. The apartments and suites each have a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a DVD player.

Free selected Foxtel channels and in-house movie channels are available. Some accommodation also includes a spa bath or fireplace.

The adjacent Blazing Stump Hotel Bar & Bistro is open for lunch and dinner, offering steaks, salads, seafood and pasta.
