Description

Featuring a water park and outdoor pool, BIG4 Swan hill offers self contained villas and apartments.

This holiday park is a 4-minute drive from Murray River and a 5-minute drive from both Swan Hill Racecourse Bowls Club and Swan Hill Train Station.

Guests can relax indoors in their air-conditioned accommodation or outside on their private balcony.

All rooms also include a fully equipped kitchen, flat-screen TV and a DVD player.

Linen is also provided. For children and guests young at heart, a jumping pillow, mini golf and a race track with pedal kart hire is offered.
