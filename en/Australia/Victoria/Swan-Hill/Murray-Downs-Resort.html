Description

Located next to the Murray Downs Golf & Country Club, boasts a large swimming pool, a tennis court and a children’s playground.

It offers air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi. Murray Downs Resort Swan Hill is a 10-minute drive from Swan Hill Train Station.

Lake Boga is a 20-minute drive away. All rooms have a electric kettle, fridge &amp; a flat-screen TV.

Some rooms also have a spa bath or a fully equipped kitchen. You can enjoy a dip in the outdoor pool, or play a game of tennis.
