Description

Nestled on 25 acres amongst the Otway Ranges, BIG4 Wye River Holiday Park is located opposite the beach.

Guests enjoy free WiFi and self-contained accommodation with a living area and kitchen.

BIG4 Wye River is a 20-minute drive from Lorne and a 25-minute drive from Apollo Bay.

All air-conditioned accommodation features a kitchen with a stovetop and fridge. It offers a dining and living area with a flat-screen TV and DVD player.

Some have a balcony or an oven. Guests can enjoy a range of facilities including an inflatable jumping pillow, a children’s playground and a games room with table tennis.

There is also BBQ facilities and a shared lounge area. There are plenty of nearby activities to enjoy including fishing, surfing and nature watching.
