Description

Situated in a spacious bush setting, only 4 km from Cowes and close to the Penguin Parade, Seal Rocks and the Grand Prix Circuit, Amaze N Things Holiday Park & Motel is just across the road from the Koala Conservation Centre.

Stunning beaches where you can enjoy swimming or surfing are less than 5 minutes’ drive away.

All chalets offer a patio. Free parking is available. Each self-contained chalet offers a private entrance and a TV at Amaze N Things Holiday Park & Motel.

A microwave, refrigerator, stove top and electric kettle are also offered. Select chalets provide a TV with a DVD player and fully equipped kitchen.

The studio motel rooms are all on the ground floor and have an ensuite, kettle, toaster and bar fridge.

Guests can enjoy billiards and table tennis at the on-site game room at Amaze N Things Holiday Park & Motel.

BBQ facilities and a children’s playground are featured. A launderette is also available.

Phillip Island Wildlife Park is 3 kilometres from Amaze N Things Holiday Park & Motel.
