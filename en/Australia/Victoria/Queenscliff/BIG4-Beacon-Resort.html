Description

Offering free Wi-Fi, an indoor pool, a tennis court and a day spa, BIG4 Beacon Resort is located in Queenscliff.

Free on-site parking is provided and there are free children's activities every day.

All accommodation feature cooking facilities. Mud Day Spa offers massages, facials and beauty therapies featuring LI'TYA and Pevonia products.

Accommodation features a kitchenette or fully equipped kitchen, a dining setting and a flat-screen TV.

Each bungalow and room includes a bathroom with a shower. Other facilities offered at the property include grocery deliveries, meeting facilities and a shared lounge.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling, billiards and table tennis.

BIG4 Beacon is 3 minutes' drive from Point Lonsdale Beach. It is 10 minutes' drive from the wineries and gourmet food producers of the Bellarine Taste Trail.
