Description

Situated on 68 acres of land, Merool Holiday Park offers self-contained accommodation located a 5-minute drive from Port of Echuca.

This 4-star property offers an outdoor pool, tennis courts and free-standing private cabins with kitchen facilities.

Each room is equipped with a flat-screen TV and a DVD player. Kitchen facilities include a fridge, tea/coffee maker and a microwave.

All rooms also come with a lounge and dining area. Located next to the Morrisons Winery, Echuca Moama Merool Holiday Park is a 5-minute drive from the Oz Maze Mini Golf.

It is a 10-minute drive from Chanter Estate Winery & Military Museum. The property has a 4-lane boat ramp.
