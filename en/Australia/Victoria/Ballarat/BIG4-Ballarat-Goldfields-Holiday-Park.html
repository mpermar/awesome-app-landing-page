Description

Boasting an outdoor pool, and a tennis court, BIG4 Ballarat Goldfields Holiday Park has everything you need for a memorable stay.

It is 5 minutes' drive from Ballarat Wildlife Park. Guests enjoy up to 300MB of free WiFi per day.

Enjoy a game of billiards or table tennis with friends. BBQ facilities are available if you wish to create an evening meal.

The on-site children's playground is sure to keep the younger guests busy. BIG4 Ballarat Holiday Park is 5 minutes' drive from Bridge Mall Shopping Centre.

Ballarat Botanical Gardens and the Ballarat Aquatic & Lifestyle Centre are both a 10-minute drive away.

All air-conditioned accommodation has kitchen facilities, including a microwave, stovetop and refrigerator.
