Description

Each room at the Begonia City Motor Inn Ballarat includes heating, air conditioning and electric blankets. Ironing facilities, a microwave, tea/coffee-making supplies, a toaster and hairdryer are also provided.

Begonia City Motor Inn is 3.5 miles from Sovereign Hill and the popular 'Blood on the Southern Cross' attraction. Sebastopol Bowling Club, which serves bistro meals and features gaming facilities, is just a 3-minute walk from the motel. The Royal Mail Hotel is within a 5-minute walk.
