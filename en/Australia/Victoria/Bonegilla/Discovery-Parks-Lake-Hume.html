Description

Discovery Parks – Lake Hume is situated on the shores of Lake Hume. Various styles of fully self-contained 2-Bedroom villas, cabins as well as powered and non-powered sites for caravans are provided.

Accommodation at Discovery Parks – Lake Hume contain facilities such as air conditioning, TV, DVD players, as well as kitchens or kitchenettes.

Facilities include a resort-style swimming pool, trampolines, a children's playground and a games room.

Discovery Parks – Lake Hume is ideally situated for easy day trips to Bright, Mt.

Beauty, the historic Beechworth, and the Rail Trail and the wineries of Rutherglen.
