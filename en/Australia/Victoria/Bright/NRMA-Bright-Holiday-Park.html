Description

Bright Holiday Park is located just 10 minutes' walk from the town centre, where guests can find a variety of shops and restaurants.

Amenities include free WiFi access, free parking, a hot tub, a garden and BBQ facilities.

Younger visitors can enjoy the children's playground. Bright Holiday Park is a 5-minute drive to the winding Ovens River, which is known for kayaking and fishing.

The stunning Mt Buffalo National Park is a 20-minute drive away. Popular activities in and around Bright include wine tasting, hiking and cycling.

Each cabin, villa or lodge at the holiday park offers air conditioning, a microwave, refrigerator and a flat-screen TV.

Extras include an outdoor seating area, bed linen, towels and ironing facilities.
