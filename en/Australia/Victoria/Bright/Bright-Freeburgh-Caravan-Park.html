Description

Just a 5-minute drive from the heart of Bright and set on 12 acres on the banks of Ovens River, Bright Freeburgh Caravan Park boasts views of Mount Feathertop.

It is ideally located for visits to the beautiful Mount Hotham. Falls Creek and Mount Buffalo, which are are just over a 1-hour drive from Bright Freeburgh Caravan Park.

The property features an outdoor heated pool and tennis courts. All accommodation features air-conditioning, heating and a balcony with mountain views.

Your fully equipped kitchen includes a coffee machine with coffee pods and hot chocolate pods.

The lounge area offers a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a DVD player.

Some rooms boast a fireplace or spa bath. Guests can enjoy activities such as hiking, fishing and table tennis.

There is also BBQ area, basketball courts, shared laundry and a children’s playground with a jumping pillow.

Trikes are available for hire. Bright Freeburgh Caravan Park is a 15-minute drive from Boynton’s Winery.
