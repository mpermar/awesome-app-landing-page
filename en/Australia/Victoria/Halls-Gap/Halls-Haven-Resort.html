Description

Situated on 50 acres, Halls Haven Resort boasts a swimming pool, tennis court and free bike hire.

All rooms feature cooking facilities and a private patio. Halls Gap township is a 5-minute drive away.

The resort offers free BBQ facilities for you to create a casual meal. The younger guests will enjoy the on-site children’s playground.

Halls Haven Resort Halls Gap is a 5-minute drive from Halls Gap Zoo and the Grampians National Park.

You can visit the MacKenzie Falls and Reed Lookout, both less than 30 minutes’ drive away.

All rooms offer an oven, a stove, a refrigerator and a microwave. The en suite bathroom includes free toiletries, bathrobes and a hairdryer.
