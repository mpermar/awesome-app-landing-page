Description

Offering an outdoor pool, a tennis court and a games room, BIG4 Grampians Parkgate Resort is located in Halls Gap.

All accommodation features a kitchen or kitchenette, a flat-screen TV and mountain views.

The Halls Gap BIG4 Grampians Holiday Park is 2 minutes drive from Grampians Tourist Information Centre.

It is 5 minutes drive from both Halls Gap Zoo and The Gap Vineyard. Recreation facilities include a billiards table, table tennis and a jumping pillow.

Younger guests can enjoy a childrens playground. The air-conditioned accommodation options include studios and self-contained apartments and villas.

All features a dining area, a DVD player and private bathroom facilities. Some accommodation offers a patio or spa bath.
