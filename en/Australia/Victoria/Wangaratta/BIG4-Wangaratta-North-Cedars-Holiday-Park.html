Description

Offering a seasonal heated outdoor pool and views of the garden, BIG4 Wangaratta North Cedars Holiday Park is situated in Wangaratta in the Victoria Region, 33 km from Beechworth.

There is a games room on site and small shop. Yarrawonga is 47 km from the property.

All units are air conditioned and have a flat-screen TV with DVD player, a seating area and/or balcony, a microwave and kettle.

Each unit has a kitchen, equipped with a small oven, a toaster, and a refrigerator.

The private bathroom has a shower. Bed linen is provided. BIG4 Wangaratta Holiday Park also has a fully equipped camp kitchen featuring 2 barbecues, a full sized oven and stove, a mini fridge and 2 x 6 seater table and chairs for guests to enjoy all year round.
