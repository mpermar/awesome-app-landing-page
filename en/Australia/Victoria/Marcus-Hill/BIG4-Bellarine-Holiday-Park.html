Description

Fitted with a balcony, the units feature a TV and a private bathroom with shower and a hair dryer. A microwave and toaster are also featured, as well as a electric tea pot.

Guests can use the fitness center.

A grill can be found at the property, along with a shared lounge.

Geelong is 13 miles from the resort village, while Sorrento is 11 miles from the property. Avalon Airport is 15 miles away.
