Description

Surrounded by 6 acres of Gippsland Lakes wetlands filled with birdlife, McMillans of Metung Resort features a secure private boat jetty and relaxation center with a range of massages and facial treatments.

Guests have access to a tennis court, game room, children’s playground and laundry facilities. The solar-heated outdoor pool with a shallow children’s pool is surrounded by a large deck with lounge chairs.

Each cottage and villa features air conditioning, a CD player, hairdryer and private BBQ facilities. Some also have a spa tub. The full kitchens include a dishwasher, gas stove and electric oven.

The modern restaurants and boutique shops in Metung village are a 5-minute drive away. It is just over a quarter mile to Legend Rock, an important Aboriginal site. Bairnsdale Train Station is 19 miles from the property.
