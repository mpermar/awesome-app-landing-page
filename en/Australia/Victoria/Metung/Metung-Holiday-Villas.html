Description

Boasting a heated swimming pool and a children's playground surrounded by lovely gardens, Metung Holiday Villas are just 15 minutes' walk from Metung's restaurants, shops and waterfront.

Each villa features a patio and cooking facilities. Located in the East Gippsland region, Metung Holiday Villas are 3 minutes' drive from Metung Marina and Bancroft Bay.

It is 20 minutes' drive from Lakes Entrance. The air-conditioned villas each offer a kitchenette or fully equipped kitchen.

All include an outdoor dining setting and a lounge area with a sofa and flat-screen TV.
