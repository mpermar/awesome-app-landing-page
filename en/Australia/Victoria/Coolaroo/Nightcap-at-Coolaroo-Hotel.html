Description

Coolaroo Hotel boasts a bistro, and an indoor children’s playground. It is a 5-minute drive from Broadmeadows Shopping Centre and features rooms with a TV.

You can enjoy a casual meal at The Coolaroo Bistro, which serves tasty dishes including chicken parmigiana, roast of the day and fisherman's basket.

There is also a bar offering refreshing beverages. Coolaroo Hotel is less than 10 minutes’ drive from The Meadows Melbourne Greyhound and Racing Association, as well as the Broadmeadows Motor Cycle Park.

Melbourne Airport is 15 minutes’ drive away. Each room is equipped with a work desk, ironing facilities, a telephone and a refrigerator.

They have a private bathroom with a shower and toilet. Hairdryers and a DVD player are available from reception upon request.
