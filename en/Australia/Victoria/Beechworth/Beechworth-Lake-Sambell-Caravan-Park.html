Description

Situated on the banks of Lake Sambell, this holiday park offers self-contained cabins with kitchenette facilities and an outdoor dining area.

Activities include a playground, free mini golf, badminton and volleyball. Beechworth Lake Sambell Caravan Park is only 500 metres from the start of the Murray to Mountains Rail Trail and a 15-minute walk from Beechworth town centre.

It is a 2-minute walk to the nereast supermarket. Guests at Lake Sambell Caravan Park Beechworth have access to laundry facilities, covered BBQ areas, and walking tracks to Lake Kerford and around Lake Sambell.

Bicycles, canoes and kayaks are available for hire. Each holiday cabin includes a microwave, refrigerator, stove and kitchenware.

Air conditioning and heating are provided. All cabins have a flat-screen TV with DVD player.
