Description

Located in the heart of Falls Creek alpine village, this hotel offers free WiFi, a bar and a restaurant.

All rooms feature a balcony offering mountain views. Guests also enjoy a hot tub and a sauna.

There are ski lifts just metres from the hotel. Each room offers a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a refrigerator and tea/coffee making facilities.

All rooms include a private bathroom with a shower. Bed linen and towels are provided.

You can relax with a swim in the heated swimming pool, or just unwind by the fireplace in the guest lounge.
