Description

Set in Toora, Toora Tourist Park offers a garden and indoor pool. Wilsons Promontory National Park is only a 30-minute drive away.

Free private parking is available on site. All units include a seating area with a flat-screen TV and DVD player.

Some units have a dining area and/or balcony. There is also a kitchen, fitted with an oven, microwave and toaster.

A refrigerator is also offered, as well as a coffee machine and a kettle. All units have a private bathroom, some with a shower, and some with a spa bath.

The holiday park features free WiFi throughout the property. Toora Tourist Park features free BBQs available for use, as well as a year round heated indoor pool and spa open from 09:00-18:00.

You can play tennis and table tennis at the property, and the area is popular for cycling.
