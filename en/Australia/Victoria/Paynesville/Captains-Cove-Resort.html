Description

Set on the Paynesville canals on the Gippsland Lakes, Captains Cove Resort offers an indoor heated pool, a tennis court, private jetties and water activities.

It is just 5 minutes’ walk from to the Raymond Island ferry and the Esplanade, where you will find waterfront restaurants, wine bar, cafes and walking tracks.

Guests can choose from a waterfront apartment or a Cove apartment, with each offering a fully-equipped kitchen, BBQ, laundry, split system heating/cooling and a TV in the lounge area and master bedroom.

All include a covered car parking space and access to a private boat jetty. Guests can stroll along the boardwalk and the peaceful gardens.

Local Gippsland Lakes activities include boating, sailing, fishing, visiting 90 mile beach, bike riding and water skiing.

Guests can hire kayaks, stand up paddle boards and bikes for all ages. Paynesville Captains Cove Resort is just 1 km from Raymond Island, where you can go hiking through the bush and also visit the Koala colony.
