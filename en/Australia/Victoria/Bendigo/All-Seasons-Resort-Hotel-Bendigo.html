Description

Featuring an indoor pool and a spa, All Seasons Resort Hotel Bendigo offers relaxing stays in elegant rooms with flat-screen TVs.

It provides free parking and has a bistro and barbecue facilities. Fitted with classic wood furnishings, well-appointed rooms have air conditioning, a sofa bed and cable TV.

A fridge and hairdryer are provided. All rooms offer premium pillow-top mattresses.

All Seasons Resort Hotel Bendigo is a short drive to the CBD and a 10-minute drive from White Hills Botanical Gardens and Quarry Hill Golf Course.

It is a 6-minute drive from the Bendigo Racecourse. Guests can relax in the indoor pool, spa and sauna area after an active workout at the fitness room.

Hearty home-style pub fare is served at Seasons Bistro, which offers a variety of local wines.
