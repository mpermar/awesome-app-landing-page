Description

Offering a solar-heated, saltwater swimming pool, 2 tennis courts and a games room, High Country Holiday Park is 4 minutes’ walk from Mansfield’s shops and restaurants.

All accommodation features a kitchenette and free parking. Mansfield High Country Holiday Park is 10 minutes’ drive from Mansfield Zoo.

The popular Mansfield Armchair Cinema is 3 minutes’ drive away. It is 40 minutes’ drive from Mt.

Buller. Each bungalow and cottage offers a dining area, a sofa and a private bathroom with a shower.

Toiletries and towels are standard in all accommodation. Some bungalows feature a patio or balcony.

Facilities include a large communal kitchen and an outdoor dining area with BBQ facilities.

You can play a game of table tennis, while younger guests enjoy the children's playground.
