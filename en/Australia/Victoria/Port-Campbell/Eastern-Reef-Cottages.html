Description

The Eastern Reef Cottages Port Campbell are a 10-minute drive from the Loch Ard shipwreck and Marine Park. The famous Twelve Apostles are a 15-minute drive away.

A kitchenette with a microwave, refrigerator and tea/coffee maker is standard in all cottages. Each air-conditioned cottage has a living room with a TV and DVD player.
