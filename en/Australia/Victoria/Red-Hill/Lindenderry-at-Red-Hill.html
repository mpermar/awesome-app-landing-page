Description

This 4-star luxury guest house is set on a 30 acre estate and offers secluded, countryside accommodation in the heart of Victorias Mornington Peninsula.

It is situated between Safety Beach and Merricks Beach. Lindenderry at Red Hill provides a variety of spacious rooms with country style décor.

Some rooms include panoramic views of the gardens and lake. Breakfast, lunch, and dinner are served indoors in the large restaurant or outdoors on the terrace.

Lindenderry has a winery, gardens, lawns, and private vineyards. Guests can enjoy discovering the sights of Australia’s most highly regarded wine and food regions.
