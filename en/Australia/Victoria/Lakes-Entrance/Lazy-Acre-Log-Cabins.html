Description

Lazy Acre Log Cabins is a 5-minute walk from Ocean Beach, restaurants and shops. It features a solar-heated swimming pool, a BBQ area and a children’s playground.

Each detached cottage at the property offers air-conditioning and a heater, a fully equipped kitchen, a dining setting and private bathroom and laundry facilities.

There is also a living area with a TV and a DVD player. Lazy Acre Log Cabins is set among gardens and offers boat parking at 4 of the cottages.

A hot tub and dryer are available at an additional charge. The property is an ideal base for a fishing holiday, with the host happy to offer free fishing advice.

Lazy Acre Log Cabins are 5 minutes’ drive from Lakes Entrance Golf Course and the boat ramp and an 8-minute walk from the Lake Cruise Terminal and hire boats.

Raymond Island, home to koalas and the famous Buchan Caves is a 45-minute drive away.

The tour desk can provide sightseeing advice and help guests with travel arrangements.

Guests are not permitted to cook crabs in the cottages, or anywhere on the property.
