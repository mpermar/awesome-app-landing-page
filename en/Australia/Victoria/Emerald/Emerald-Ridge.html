Description

Located on a 10 acre property, nestled in the green rolling hills of Emerald, guests staying at this property are welcomed by a grand stone entrance and a tree-lined driveway.

There are 2 accommodation options at Emerald Ridge. There is a guest room or a 2-bedroom self-contained cottage.

Wine and chocolates are provided on arrival as well as breakfast provisions. At Emerald Ridge, you can enjoy views of the property and surrounds.

There are bathrobes, slippers, toiletries, verandahs, tea/coffee making facilities, flat-screen TVs, Netflix and free WiFi on offer.

There is also a playground and farm animals for children to feed and play with. Emerald Ridge is set in the Dandenong Ranges, less than 5 minutes away from cafes, restaurants and shopping choices.

Puffing Billy and Emerald Lake are located within 5 minutes of the property. It is less than a 20-minute drive from wineries, wedding venues such as Avalon Castle and the famous 1000 steps walk.

Melbourne is 45 km from Emerald Ridge Bed & Breakfast, while Olinda is 13 km away.
