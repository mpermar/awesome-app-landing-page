Description

Only a 5-minute drive from Geelong city, Barwon Valley Lodge is set on 8 acres of relaxing parkland alongside the Barwon River.

The city centre is about a short 5-minute drive away. Free Wi-Fi is available in the apartments.

Barwon Valley Lodge features an outdoor swimming pool and a gas heated spa upon request.

There is also a full-size tennis court for entertainment. An island adventure playground is available for kids to play.

Guests can enjoy a picnic at the undercover area with the barbeque facilities provided by the property.

Located on the ground floor, each apartment has a fully equipped kitchen and a washing machine with a dryer.

A private patio, a flat Screen TV with DVD/CD player and an en suite bathroom are included.

There are also a spacious lounge and a dining area. Free private parking is available in front of each apartment.

A 15-minute walk from Belmont Shopping Centre, it only takes 7 minutes by car from Barwon Valley Lodge to Geelong Racecourse.

Torquay and the famous Great Ocean Road are about 20 minutes’ drive away. It is also a 3-minute drive from Geelong Train Station and a 1-hour drive from Melbourne International Airport.
