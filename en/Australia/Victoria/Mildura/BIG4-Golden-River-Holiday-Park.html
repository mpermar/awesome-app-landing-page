Description

Located in Mildura, Golden River Holiday Park features a heated outdoor swimming pool with 2 water slides and splash pad.

Guests at this tranquil retreat can enjoy a scenic along the banks of Murray River, just 100 metres from the property.

A variety of children’s activities are available including an in-ground bouncing pillow, a kangaroo jumper and an adventure playground.

The property also features a games room, 3 level playground, a shared kitchen and a communal lounge area.

All accommodation is air-conditioned and offers a flat-screen TV. Kitchen facilities include a microwave, refrigerator, tea and coffee making facilities.

There is a private bathroom with a shower. Towels and linen are provided. Golden River Holiday Park is 5 minutes’ drive from Mildura Golf Course and 15 minutes’ drive from Mildura Airport.
