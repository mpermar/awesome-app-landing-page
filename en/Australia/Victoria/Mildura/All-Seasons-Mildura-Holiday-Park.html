Description

Accommodations are fitted with air conditioning, a fully equipped kitchen with a dining area, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with shower. There's also a microwave, toaster and a electric tea pot.

The resort village has an outdoor pool.

A grill can be found at All Seasons Mildura Holiday Park, along with a shared lounge.

Paddlesteamers Melbourne & Rothbury is 2.5 miles from the accommodation. Mildura Airport is 3.7 miles away.
