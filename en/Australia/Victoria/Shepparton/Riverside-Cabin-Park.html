Description

Providing a balcony, particular units are air-conditioned and have a dining area and a seating area with a flat-screen TV. Some units also have a kitchen equipped with a microwave, a toaster, and a fridge.

The resort village has a seasonal outdoor pool.

A grill is available for guests to use at Riverside Cabin Park.
