Description

Located in Melbourne CBD, this award-winning, eco-friendly boutique hotel features free Wi-Fi and relaxing massage chairs in the cosy lounge.

It offers air-conditioned accommodation with an LED flat-screen TV, over 100 cable channels and an iPod dock.

Guests enjoy free access to a fitness centre just 3 minutes' walk away. Alto Hotel on Bourke is less than 5 minutes’ walk from Southern Cross Station and 2 tram stops.

Etihad Stadium and Melbourne Aquarium are each a 10-minute walk away. You can read a book or browse the internet over a free cup of espresso coffee or herbal tea in the colourful lounge.

Free parking is available for guests in hybrid or electric vehicles. The Alto Hotel rooms are elegantly decorated, and have a granite bathroom with guest toiletries.

Each room has a refrigerator, and most rooms also include a well-equipped kitchenette or kitchen.
