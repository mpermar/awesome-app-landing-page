Description

Located in a historic 19th century landmark building in the heart of Melbourne's CBD, the boutique Treasury on Collins offers self-contained accommodation, well-positioned for trams, buses and taxis.

Guests enjoy free Netflix, free WiFi and free access to the Treasured Guest Mezzanine Lounge from 10:00 to 19:00.

Treasury on Collins is an ideal base to explore Melbournes best attractions. It is a 5-minute walk from Etihad Stadium and 1 km from Crown Casino.

Flinders Street Station is a 10-minute walk away. There are several shops, restaurants and cafes within 5 minutes' walk.

Airport shuttles can be arranged on request. The air-conditioned suites and apartments are stylishly decorated with a blend of elegance and contemporary furniture.

All include a 40-inch flat-screen TV, kitchenette, coffee machine, washing machine and dryer.

The Heritage Balcony Suites offer views. Relax with a book, enjoy a snack or browse the internet over a cup of espresso coffee or herbal tea in the Treasured Guest Mezzanine Lounge.

Guests also receive free access to the 24-hour onsite fitness centre. Treasury on Collins is a modern apartment hotel within the historic Bank of Australasia building.

Only 8 storeys high, it is well-known for its architectural beauty and central location.
