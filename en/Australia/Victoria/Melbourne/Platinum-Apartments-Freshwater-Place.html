Description

Located in the heart of Melbourne CBD, Platinum Apartments at Freshwater Place offers modern self-contained accommodation.

Each apartment provides access to an indoor pool, spa and wellness centre, and free WiFi.

All apartments have prime landmark views. Guests of Platinum Apartments can take a leisurely 5-minute stroll down the Southbank Promenade or to Crown Casino, where a variety of shopping and dining options are available.

Melbourne Arts Centre and Flinders Street Station are both a 10-minute walk away.

Offering sweeping views of the city, the Freshwater Place building offers a shared rooftop garden terrace, BBQ facilities and 24-hour reception service.

You can enjoy a hot sauna or spend time in the on-site fitness centre. The luxurious air-conditioned apartments will provide you with a spacious lounge, flat-screen TV with cable channels, DVD player and an iPod dock.

You can prepare a meal in the fully equipped kitchen, which includes a microwave, dishwasher and a stovetop.

Bathrooms include a shower and a bath, hairdryer and free toiletries. Some apartments have a balcony and Yarra River views.
