Description

Situated next to Flemington Racecourse and the Melbourne Showgrounds, Quest Flemington Apartment Hotels offers well-appointed accommodation with free WiFi and full kitchen and laundry facilities.

Guests enjoy an outdoor pool, tennis courts and free onsite parking. Quest Flemington Apartment Hotels just 3.

5 km from central Melbourne and a 15-minute drive from Tullamarine Airport. It is a 7-minute drive from the Melbourne Zoo and the State Hockey Netball Centre.

Featuring a private balcony, the spacious air-conditioned apartments have a large flat-screen TV and DVD player.

Ironing facilities, a bathtub and a hairdryer are provided. For leisure, guests can enjoy relaxing massages or make sightseeing arrangements at the tour desk.
