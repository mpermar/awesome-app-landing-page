Description

All rooms come with a toaster, fridge, a electric tea pot, a bath or shower, a hair dryer and a closet. The resort provides certain rooms with garden views, and the rooms include a private bathroom. All guest rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Boambee Bay Resort offers 3-star accommodations with a sauna and playground. Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Bonville, like canoeing.

Coffs Harbour is 3.9 miles from Boambee Bay Resort. The nearest airport is Coffs Harbour Airport, 2.5 miles from the resort.
