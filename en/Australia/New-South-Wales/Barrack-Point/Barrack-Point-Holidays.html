Description

Set in Barrack Point, Barrack Point Holidays provides barbecue facilities. Every room is equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

All units in the resort are equipped with a kettle. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom fitted with a hairdryer, while some rooms also boast a seating area.

The rooms at Barrack Point Holidays have air conditioning and a wardrobe. The accommodation offers a children's playground.

Wollongong is 16 km from Barrack Point Holidays, while Kiama is 12 km away. The nearest airport is Illawarra Regional Airport, 7 km from the property.
