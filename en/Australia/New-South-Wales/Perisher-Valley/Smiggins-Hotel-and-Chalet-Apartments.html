Description

Located at the base of Smiggins Holes ski lifts, Smiggins Hotel & Chalet Apartments boasts ski-to-door access and an onsite bar/restaurant.

Guests enjoy a sauna, a shared lounge area and a kids club each evening. Smiggins Hotel is located at the base of the Link T-Bar which provides direct access to Perisher, Blue Cow and Guthega ski areas.

It is a 25-minute drive from Jindabyne and a 1-hour drive from the Snowy Mountain Airport.

The hotel offers a range of accommodation types including rooms, suites and apartments.

All accommodation features heating, a flat-screen TV and a fridge. They have a private bathroom with a shower.

Some rooms offer mountain views or a kitchenette. Guests enjoy access to ski school, ski hire, ski pass vending and free ski storage.

Massages are available for an additional charge. Other facilities include a free shuttle bus, weekly magicians, kid’s karaoke, free WiFi and live music.

The restaurant serves a range of dishes including soups, salads, pasta, steaks and pizza.
