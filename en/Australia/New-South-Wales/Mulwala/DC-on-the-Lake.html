Description

Located on the banks of Lake Mulwala, DC on the Lake guests enjoy a tennis court, free watersports and fishing.

All cottages boast stunning lake views, and a balcony with an outdoor dining area.

DC on Lake Mulwala is a 5-minute drive from central Mulwala and a 10-minute drive from Mulwala State Forest.

Albury is a 1-hour and 10-minute drive away. All air-conditioned cottages feature a fully equipped kitchen and a dining area.

You can relax in the lounge area complete with a flat-screen TV. The cottages have ironing facilities and a bathroom with free toiletries.

Towels and linen are provided. Guests can share a meal using the BBQ facilities. Also on offer is a free shuttle bus.
