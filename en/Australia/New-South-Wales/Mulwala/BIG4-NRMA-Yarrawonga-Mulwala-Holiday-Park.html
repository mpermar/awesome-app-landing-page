Description

Set on the shores of the beautiful Lake Mulwala, the park offers self-contained accommodation.

Guests can enjoy a swimming pool, tennis court and bouncing pillow. NRMA Yarrawonga Mulwala Holiday Park is just 5 minutes' drive from both Linley Park Animal Farm and Gardens and Yarrawonga Mulwala Golf Club, with Murray Estate Wines a 10-minute drive away.

All accommodation is air-conditioned and includes a includes a kitchen with a stove top, microwave and fridge.

It offers a dining and lounge area with a TV and a DVD player. Some rooms have a balcony, oven or BBQ.

Guests enjoy a range of facilities including a children’s playground, BBQ facilities and a games room.
