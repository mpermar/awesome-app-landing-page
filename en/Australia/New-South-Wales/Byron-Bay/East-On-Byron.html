Description

Situated amongst the peaceful surrounds of Belongil Beach, East On Byron is a 15-minute walk from the heart of Byron Bay.

The landscaped gardens feature a lagoon-style pool and hot tub. Each of the 26 luxury villas at East On Byron are individually furnished with all the comforts of home, polished timber floors and open-plan living and dining areas.

Facilities include heating, air conditioning, flat-screen TVs and laundry facilities.

There is also an outdoor barbecue and dining area, plus a fully-equipped modern kitchen, in every villa.

Located only 20 metres from the beautiful white sands of Belongil Beach, East On Byron is only a short 800-metre stroll along the beach into central Byron Bay.
