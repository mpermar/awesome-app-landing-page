Description

Offering an outdoor swimming pool, free WiFi and a children's playground with a jumping pillow, Eden Gateway Holiday Park is set on 20 acres, 3 minutes' drive from Eden Town Centre and just a 15-minute walk from Aslings Beach.

All villas include cooking facilities and a balcony with outdoor furniture. Some villas have a spa bath and a washing machine.

Located on the Sapphire Coast, Eden Holiday Park is 5 minutes' drive from the Eden Killer Whale Museum and Lake Curalo.

The property is home to an abundance of native birds, including bellbirds. Each air-conditioned villa offers a living area with a dining setting, a sofa and a TV.

All include a bathroom with a shower, towels and free toiletries provided. All villas feature a fully equipped kitchen or a kitchenette.

Guests enjoy biscuits on arrival. Guest can share a meal using the communal kitchen, dining area and BBQ facilities.

There is also a shared guest lounge and kid's activities available during peak seasons.
