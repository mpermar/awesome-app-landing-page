Description

Offering direct access to the beach, Ballina Beach Village features self-contained cabins,2 swimming pools and a café.

All accommodation at Beach Village Ballina features a kitchenette, air conditioning and a TV.

Nature walks, bird watching and fishing. There is also a children’s playground, a barbecue area and laundry facilities.

Access to the 4WD beach with no permit required. Mermaids Café & Kiosk with basic groceries is open for breakfast and lunch extended trading for Dinner Friday and Saturday nights.

Ballina Beach Village is one hour’s drive from Byron Bay. The shops, restaurants and nightclubs of Ballina are 9 km away via Burns Point ferry.
