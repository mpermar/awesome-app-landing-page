Description

Relax on your private balcony and gaze out at the Pacific Ocean, you may even spot a whale! The Observatory Holiday Apartments offer self-contained accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool, BBQ facilities and free WiFi.

The Observatory Holiday Apartments are conveniently located just 10-minutes’ walk from Coffs Harbour Jetty and Marina, close to a variety of shopping and dining options.

It is just 15-minutes' walk from Pet Porpoise Pool and 6-minutes' drive from Coffs Harbour Airport.

These air-conditioned apartments are styled with contemporary furnishings and feature a fully equipped kitchen.

The lounge area has a sofa and a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Some apartments also feature a spa bath.
