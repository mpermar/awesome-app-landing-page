Description

Offering self-contained accommodation, Aqua Villa Holiday Apartments has a solar-heated swimming pool and landscaped gardens.

Located across from Coffs Harbour Marketplace, it is 3 km away from Coffs Harbour Jetty.

The resort offers contemporary villas and town houses decorated with simplistic furnishings.

Each one has a well-equipped kitchen, spacious lounge and dining area as well as in-house movies.

Aqua Villa Holiday Apartments features a large swimming pool fitted with sun loungers.

Free internet is also available in public areas. Big Banana is a 10 minute drive away from Aqua Villa Holiday Apartments.
