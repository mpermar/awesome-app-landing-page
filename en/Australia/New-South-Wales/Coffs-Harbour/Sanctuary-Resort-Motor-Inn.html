Description

Featuring a restaurant, swimming pool and tennis court, Sanctuary Resort Motor Inn is located just 2.

5 km from Coffs Harbour city centre. Free Wi-Fi and free car parking are included.

All rooms at Sanctuary Resort Coffs Harbour feature air conditioning and satellite TV.

Some rooms have a spa bath or kitchen facilities. Children will enjoy the waterslide and outdoor swimming pool.

Sanctuary Resort Motor Inn is a 15-minute drive from The Big Banana and the Pet Porpoise Pool.

Treehouse Restaurant & Bar (open Monday-Saturday, closed Sundays and Public Holidays) is surrounded by native trees and offers la carte dining in a relaxed atmosphere.

It serves a full buffet breakfast, and provides in-room dining services for dinner only.
