Description

Set in Emerald Beach, Discovery Parks – Emerald Beach features a garden, outdoor pool, and barbecue.

Coffs Harbour is 16 km from the property. Free WiFi is available and free private parking is available on site.

A flat-screen TV with cable channels and DVD player are available in some units. There is also a kitchen in some of the units, equipped with an oven and microwave.

You can play tennis at the property, and bike hire is available. Sawtell is 24 km from Discovery Parks – Emerald Beach.

The nearest airport is Coffs Harbour Airport, 18 km from Discovery Parks – Emerald Beach.
