Description

Accommodations at the Durras Lake North includes traditional cottages and self-contained cabins. The cabins feature a kitchenette with a refrigerator. The cottages have a full kitchen and a patio. Lightweight blankets and pillows are provided. However, linens and towels are not provided, but can be rented.

Facilities include a barbecue area and guest laundry. Local activities include swimming, fishing and hiking. Surfboards and kayaks are available for rent.

The Durras Lake North Holiday Park is located within Batemans Marine Park. Pebbly Beach is just a 10-minute drive away and Batemans Bay is a 20-minute drive from the property.
