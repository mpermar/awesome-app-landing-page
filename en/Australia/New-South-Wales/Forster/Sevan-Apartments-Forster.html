Description

Located within 5 minutes’ walk from Forster’s main beaches, 4. 5-star Sevan Apartments showcases a 17 metre outdoor pool and a terrace with shared BBQ facilities.

All apartments offer free WiFi access and feature a large balcony with outdoor furniture.

Sevan Apartments Forster is 5 minutes’ walk from both Main Beach and Pebbly Beach, and a variety of local restaurants and shops.

The Stockland Shopping Centre is 5 minutes’ drive away. The town of Forster is located 90 minutes’ drive from Newcastle and 3 hours’ drive from Sydney.

Guests at Sevan Apartments Forster can relax in the spacious shared lounge area, which features a flat-screen TV and DVD player.

All air-conditioned apartments offer a unique design with spacious living and dining areas and a fully equipped kitchen, laundry, TV with satellite channels and a DVD player.

Apartments on the higher floors offer beach or lake views, and each includes an en suite bathroom.

Free underground secure parking is provided for one vehicle per apartment. Sevan Apartments is a wheelchair friendly property.
