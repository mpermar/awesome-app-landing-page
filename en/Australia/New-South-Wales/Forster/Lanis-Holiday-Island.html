Description

Located on the Lake Wallis waterfront, Lani's Holiday Island features an outdoor pool, a sunny terrace and a barbecue area with outdoor seating.

A private patio and flat-screen cable TV are standard in all rooms. Free parking is available on site.

Lani's Holiday Island Forster is 5 minutes’ drive from Forster-Tuncurry Golf Course and Foster Main Beach.

Booti Booti National Park is a 10-minute drive, and Taree Airport is a 1-hour drive away.

All cottages have a kitchenette or kitchen with a refrigerator, tea and coffee maker and kitchenware.

Each air-conditioned cottage has a dining area and a work desk. The tour desk can book dolphin watching cruises, fishing trips and surfing lessons.

The property also features a children's playground, a games room and a communal kitchen.
