Description

Best Western Plus Albury Hovell Tree Inn is within a 15-minute walk of Albury City Regional Art Gallery. Albury Airport is a 5-minute drive away.

Equipped with air conditioning and heating facilities, each room has a flat-screen TV with cable and movie channels. Tea/coffee making facilities and a minibar are included. Some rooms have a spa tub.

Hovell Bar and Grill is open daily for breakfast and dinner. In-room dining is possible and barbecue facilities are available.

Albury Hovell Tree Inn Motel has self-service laundry facilities and a 24-hour front desk that provides fax/photocopying services. Free parking is provided.
