Description

Crowne Plaza Hawkesbury Valley offers accommodation with free Wi-Fi and views to the Blue Mountains.

It boasts the largest day spa in Sydney, plus an indoor heated swimming pool and sauna.

All rooms at Crowne Plaza Hawkesbury Valley include en suite bathroom, cable TV and heating/air conditioning.

Some rooms feature a fireplace, while others include a spa bath. Set within 8 hectares of landscaped gardens overlooking the Hawkesbury River, the property features a fitness centre, tennis courts and 2 golf courses.

Guests can relax with a drink at the Barracks Bar and enjoy modern Australian cuisine at The Harvest Restaurant.

Located in the historic town of Windsor, the resort is a 45 minutes’ drive from central Sydney and 50 minutes’ drive from Sydney Airport.
