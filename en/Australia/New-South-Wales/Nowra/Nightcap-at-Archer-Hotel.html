Description

Nightcap at Archer Hotel features an on-site restaurant, tennis court, and 20-metre swimming pool.

WiFi access is available. Guests enjoy free continental buffet breakfast, daily. Nightcap at Archer Hotel is situated on the Princes Highway, about halfway between Sydney and Batemans Bay.

It is a 30-minute drive to Jervis Bay and 90-minute drive to Mogo Zoo. Each room features a flat-screen TV, refrigerator, desk, heating, air-conditioning and tea/coffee making facilities.

Facilities include a pool, luggage storage and conference facilities. The bar and restaurant offers bistro-style meals, pool tables, a VIP lounge and ATM cash machines.
