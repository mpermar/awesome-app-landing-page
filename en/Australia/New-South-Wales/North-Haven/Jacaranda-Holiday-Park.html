Description

All units comprise a dining area, seating area with a sofa and a fully equipped kitchenette with various cooking facilities, including a microwave, a toaster and a fridge. Some units include a balcony and/or a patio with pool views.

A shared kitchen and shared laundry are available for use.

The holiday park offers an outdoor pool. You can enjoy a game of badminton, volleyball or basketball.

Jacaranda Holiday Park offers a barbecue and a children's playground.

Port Macquarie is 14.9 miles from the accommodation, while Taree is 28.6 miles away.
