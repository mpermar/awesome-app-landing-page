Description

Featuring an indoor/outdoor heated pool with spa pool, an indoor recreation area with table tennis, and an outdoor children’s playground, this holiday park is set on 16 acres of peaceful natural surroundings.

Sapphire Valley Tourist and Holiday Park is just 600 metres from the shops, cafes and restaurants of Merimbula town centre.

Merimbula Lake is just an 10-minute walk from the property and the main beaches are 1.

5km away. All accommodation includes a TV, table and chairs, and a private bathroom with shower.

Cooking facilities include an electric kettle, kitchenware, microwave, refrigerator, toaster and stove.

Guests have access to indoor and outdoor electric barbecue facilities. The central amenities block offers dish washing facilities, as well as full laundry, drying and ironing facilities.

Sapphire Valley Caravan Park is only 5 minutes’ drive from popular fishing spots and golf courses.
