Description

Just 5 minutes’ walk from Lake Merimbula, Anchorbell offers self-contained apartments with flat-screen satellite TV and DVD players.

It features a swimming pool, a tennis court and barbecue facilities. Free WiFi access is available.

All air-conditioned apartments come with laundry facilities and a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher and microwave.

Each apartment includes spacious living and dining areas. Anchorbell Apartments offers free airport transfers.

Local activities include fishing, whale watching and horse riding. Anchorbell Holiday Apartments are located just 5 minutes’ walk from the shops, restaurants and bars in Merimbula.
