Description

Beach Cabins Merimbula offers self-catering accommodation, just 100 metres from Short Point Beach.

Facilities include a heated swimming pool, children’s playground, barbecue area and guest laundry.

Each studio and cottage at Merimbula Beach Cabins includes a refrigerator, microwave, stove, electric kettle and toaster.

Some also have a full kitchen with oven. All accommodation has a TV, DVD player, private bathroom and patio with outdoor furniture.

Free car parking is provided. Wi-Fi access is available. Merimbula town centre is a 2-minute drive away.

It is a 10-minute drive to Merimbula Lake, ideal for canoeing and fishing. Pambula Merimbula Golf Course is 7 km away.

The tour desk can arrange whale watching cruises. Volleyball and badminton courts are located on site.
