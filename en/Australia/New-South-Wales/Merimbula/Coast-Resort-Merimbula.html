Description

Situated on 2 hectares of land, stretching from Merimbula Lake to Merimbula Beach, Coast Resort Merimbula offers a stunning selection of apartments, townhouses and penthouses.

Coast Resort offers accommodation with private balconies and flat-screen satellite TVs.

It features 2 swimming pools, a tennis court and barbecue facilities. Coast Apartments are located a 15-minute walk from the heart of Merimbula.

Magic Mountain Theme Park and Merimbula Airport are each less than 5 minutes drive away.

All apartments include laundry facilities and a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher, refrigerator and microwave.

Each apartment has beautiful views of the lake, garden or pool. Guests can enjoy the sun on the terrace, or play an invigorating game of tennis.

Local activities include surfing, swimming, boating, fishing, golfing, shopping, dining and whale watching.
