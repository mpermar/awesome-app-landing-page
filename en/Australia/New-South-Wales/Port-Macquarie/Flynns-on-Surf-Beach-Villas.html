Description

Just a 2-minute walk from Flynns Beach, these spacious villas each offer free Wi-Fi, a balcony with private BBQ, and a lock-up garage.

There is a heated pool and a patio with private BBQ facilities. Flynns on Surf Beach Villas are less than 3 km from Port Macquarie’s town centre.

It is a 5-minute walk from both the Macquarie Nature Reserve and the Port Macquarie Koala Hospital.

Guests can relax by the solar-heated pool and enjoy a barbecue. There is a tour desk which books whale watching cruises, deep sea fishing charters and camel rides.

All self-contained villas feature a kitchen with a Nespresso machine. Each has a comfortable lounge area with a 50-inch plasma-screen TV and free satellite channels.

The Port Macquarie region is home to many wineries, several are within 10 km of Flynns on Surf.
