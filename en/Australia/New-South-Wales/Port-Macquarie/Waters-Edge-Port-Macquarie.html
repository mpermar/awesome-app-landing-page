Description

Waters Edge Port Macquarie is located along the picturesque Hastings River, between Settlement City and Port Central Shopping Centres.

Free parking is provided. Guests enjoy a restaurant and a swimming pool. You can relax around the outdoor pool, enjoy views of the Hastings River from Edge Cafe or dine at One The Edge Waterfront Restaurant and Bar, which serves local cuisine.

The refurbished conference and restaurant facilities can accommodate corporate functions for 250 people or board meetings of up to 8 people.

A variety of packages are available. Most rooms feature water views and all offer an en suite bathroom with a shower.

All rooms include a la carte room service and access to a guest laundry. Port Macquarie Waters Edge is adjacent to the Westport Bowling Club, and a 10-minute walk from the historical Courthouse.

It is a 15-minute drive from Billabong Koala and Wildlife Park. The Bago Vineyard is a 40-minute drive away.
