Description

Set on the stunning waterfront in the heart of the city, Rydges Hotel Port Macquarie boasts free WiFi, an outdoor swimming pool, a hot tub and a fitness centre.

Some rooms offer a private balcony or patio with river views. Rydges Port Macquarie is a 10-minute drive from Port Macquarie Airport.

Swimming and surfing beaches, vineyards, breweries and golf courses are all within a 9-minute drive.

Check out the extensive brochure display for activities such as camel rides, whale watching, water sports and wine tours, or indulge in a treatment at Port Day Spa located at the entrance to the hotel.

The air-conditioned rooms feature a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a desk, a refrigerator and tea/coffee making facilities.

There is an en suite bathroom with a shower and free amenities. Zebu Bar & Grill is open daily for breakfast, lunch and dinner, offering modern Australian coastal cuisine in a casual environment.
