Description

Located directly opposite Shelly Beach, this holiday resort offers air-conditioned villas with free WiFi and full kitchen.

Facilities include a swimming pool, spa pool, full-size tennis court and children’s playground.

Each apartment at Shelly Beach Resort Port Macquarie includes flat-screen satellite TV, DVD player, internal laundry and private patio.

All fully equipped kitchens feature a dishwasher. Guests have access to a barbecue area, tour desk, and a games room with pool table, table tennis, table soccer and Air Hockey Table.

Port Macquarie city centre is only 5 km from Shelly Beach Resort. Port Macquarie Golf Course is just a 6-minute drive away.

Sea Acres Rainforest Centre, located across the street, offers discounted tickets to guests.
