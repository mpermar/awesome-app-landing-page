Description

All 3 Sydney Darling Harbour Waterfront Premier Apartments "A", "B" and ''C'' are located adjacent to the ICC Sydney Convention Centre, and facing north east, overlook vibrant Darling Harbour and Cockle Bay.

They boast views over Sydney Harbour and the city skyline, which features a fireworks display at 21:00 Saturdays, all public holidays, Vivid Festival and Festival of Sydney nights.

The 3 large, air-conditioned, north east facing apartments all feature private kitchens, a large terrace for entertaining, 2 bathrooms with quality bathrobes, towels and toiletries and a washing machine and dryer.

The lounge room includes a 55-inch TV with cable channels, including all sports channels, Stan, Netflix and IPTV, which streams all overseas TV channels.

All 3 apartments have stair-free access into the building and into the 3 apartments as well as all bedrooms, bathrooms and showers.

Enjoy the on-site fitness centre, large swimming pool, sauna and hot tub. Nearby major attractions within a 5-minute walk away include The Star Casino, Sydney Fish Markets, Sydney Aquarium, Sydney Wildlife Park, Maritime Museum, Pitt St Mall shopping, the Sydney Tower, Powerhouse Museum and Madame Tussauds.

Within 100 meters of the apartments is a large multicultural supermarket and liquor store plus a variety of international restaurants, such as Thai, Chinese, French, Japanese, Italian, Australian, seafood, Lebanese, and including gluten free and vegan options.

The property is 1. 6 km from Circular Quay and 1. 7 km from the Overseas Passenger Terminal.

The properties personal airport shuttle bus is free for guests staying for 5 or more nights.

Guests staying less than 5 nights can pay AUD $150 all up which covers all guests in your group for the 2 way trip.

"Apartment A" is a large apartment and sleeps up to 6 adults plus 1 child. It has its own large private terrace for entertaining.

"Apartment B" is a large deluxe 1-Bedroom apartment which sleeps up to 2 adults plus 1 child and is suitable for couples, those travelling for business or solo travellers.

It also has its own large private terrace for entertaining. "Apartment C" is also a large apartment and sleeps up to 4 adults plus 1 child.

It also has its own large private terrace for entertaining. All 3 apartments provide, free of charge daily continental breakfasts supplies, including vegan options, filtered water, a bottle of wine, high-speed WiFi, Opal Travel Cards for all public transport which can be topped up at any bus or tram station or wharf, secure parking, Peter Alexander bathrobes, Sheridan linen, a baby cot and stroller, and 6 overseas universal power adapters.

Guests are offered a “Permanent Promotion 4-Hour Tour of Sydney, with commentary” in a limousine at a charge of AU$250 for groups of 4 and AU$300 for groups of up to 7 persons.

The tour stops at Sydney’s 8 most historic and current landmarks and you will be picked up from the apartment and visit places such as at La Perouse, Coogee Beach, Bondi Beach, Watsons Bay, drive over the Sydney Harbour Bridge and stop at Kirribilli, where guests can overlook the harbour to the Opera House and Sydney Harbour Bridge.

Other stops include Manly Beach and you will be dropped off at the Manly ferry wharf, to catch the ferry along Sydney Harbour back to Pyrmont Bay Wharf, next to the apartment.

Within the local area are the following services - a range of beauty shops, massages shops which provide Traditional Thai, Deep Tissue and Foot Reflexology.

Also Babysitting services and professionally qualified swimming, surfing and stand up paddle board lessons are also available.

Additional charges apply for these services. There is a 24/7 concierge to assist with your check-in and parking and any guest inquiries.

There are bus stops nearby which can take you to many destinations including Bondi Beach, Manly, the Opera House and Sydney Harbour Bridge.

For convenient transportation, the Hop-on-Hop-off double decker bus station, tram station and taxi rank are next to the apartments.
