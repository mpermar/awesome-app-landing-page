Description

Alstonville Country Cottages feature panoramic views down the valley to the ocean.

Facilities include an outdoor swimming pool and a barbecue area with a wood-fired pizza oven.

Alstonville Cottages are located on a rural property only a 2-minute drive from the historic village of Alstonville.

Ballina is 10 minutes by car, and Lismore is 21 kilometres away. The cottages at Alstonville feature wooden floors, high ceilings, and a balcony with views of the rainforest and the ocean.

They are equipped with a kitchen and ironing facilities. A DVD player and flat-screen TV are also provided for guest entertainment.

Guests can enjoy a walk through the 30-acre Heritage Rainforest, or unwind with a massage in their cottage.

The sun terrace is the perfect place to relax. Farm animals, including horses, donkeys, cows and chickens, are always happy to be fed by guests.
