Description

Located along the Murray River, Rivergum Holiday Retreat is 2. 5 km from the All Saints Estate, a120-year old castle set within a winery estate.

It offers an outdoor pool, a boat ramp and free parking on site. Air-conditioned guest rooms feature a full kitchen with a microwave, a fridge, and tea/coffee making facilities.

Each has a private deck and a dining area. The en suite bathroom comes with shower and a hairdryer.

The property has a tennis court and a 1. 2 km outdoor fitness trial along Murray.

Two guest laundry rooms are available. There is also a lounge area and a communal dining area.

Retreat Rivergum Holiday is 1. 6 km from the Corowa Chocolate Factory, and 2 km from the Corowa Federation Museum.

It is 6. 8 km from Corowa Golf Club, and 12. 5 km from the Rutherglen Wine Region.
