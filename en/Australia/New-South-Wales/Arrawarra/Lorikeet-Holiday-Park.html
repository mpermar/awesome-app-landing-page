Description

Located in a peaceful natural setting, Gateway Lifestyle Lorikeet offers direct access to 7 km of unspoilt beach, just 5 minutes’ walk away.

Each villa includes air conditioning and a fully equipped kitchen. All self-contained villas at Gateway Lifestyle Lorikeet include separate lounge and dining areas, cable TV and en suite bathrooms.

Some villas feature a private spa bath. Guests have access to an outdoor swimming pool with a hot tub, plus a barbecue area and a children's playground.

There is also a tennis court, guest laundry and convenience store located on site.

Gateway Lifestyle Lorikeet is 10 minutes’ drive from Woolgoolga, with many shops and restaurants.

Local activities include whale watching, deep sea fishing, white water rafting and scuba diving.
