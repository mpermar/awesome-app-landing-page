Description

Armidale Tourist Park is within a 5-minute drive of Armidale city centre and the beautiful New England National Park.

It offers an outdoor pool, a tennis court and a covered barbecue area. Free on-site parking is available.

All villas feature cooking facilities like a stove, a microwave, a toaster and kitchenware.

All include air conditioning and a flat-screen TV. Each also has a private bathroom with shower.

The property has a general store on site where you can purchase basic necessities.

There is also a recreation room and a children’s playground. Armidale Tourist Park is within a 6-minute drive of Armidale Shopping Centre and New England Regional Art Museum.
