Description

Seashells Beachfront Resort is located on Diamond Beach on the Mid North Coast of New South Wales.

Nestled 5. 4 km from Blackhead Rd, the property is surrounded by bushland and native wildlife on one side of the resort and the sand and waves of Diamond Beach on the other side.

The family-friendly resort features facilities including a swimming pool, children's playground, fitness room, tennis courts and more.

Taree is a 30-minute drive away. Each apartment at Seashells Resort includes a stove top, microwave, full-size refrigerator and kitchenware.

Tea and coffee are provided for your arrival. Some apartments have laundry facilities.

Guests have access to covered BBQ facilities, a sauna, games room and squash court.

There is a separate swimming pool for toddlers, and separate hot tubs for children and adults.
