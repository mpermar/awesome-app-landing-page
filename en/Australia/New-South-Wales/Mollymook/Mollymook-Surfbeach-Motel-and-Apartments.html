Description

Located across the road from Mollymook Beach, Mollymook Surfbeach Motel & Apartments offer a solar-heated outdoor pool, a hot tub and barbecue facilities.

The property offers free WiFi and free on site parking. Surfbeach Mollymook Motel & Apartments are 200 metres from Mollymook Golf Club and 5 minutes' drive from Ulladulla town centre.

The heated guest rooms and apartments are fitted with electric blankets, a refrigerator and a DVD player.

The en suite bathroom offers free toiletries and a hairdryer. The property can arrange massage services for guests with a prior request.
