Description

Blue Dolphin Holiday Resort offers a variety of comfortable accommodations, including waterfront villas and family cabins. Each unit has a fully-equipped kitchen and open-plan living and dining area. Some villas have a spa bathtub.

The resort features 2 pools and a children’s playground. You can go fishing on the riverfront.

By car, Yamba is 1.5 hours north of Coffs Harbour and 2 hours south of the Gold Coast. Brisbane is a 3 hour drive away.
