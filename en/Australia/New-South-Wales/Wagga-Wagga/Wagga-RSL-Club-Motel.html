Description

A 5-minute drive from central Wagga Wagga, Wagga RSL Club Motel offers an outdoor pool and a barbecue area.

Free parking and free WiFi access are provided. The air-conditioned guest rooms are equipped with a toaster, an electric kettle and a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

The en suite bathroom offers free toiletries and a hairdryer. Most rooms have a spa bath and a DVD player.

Guests enjoy free use of laundry facilities. Dry cleaning services can be requested.

Wagga RSL Club Motel is a 5-minute drive from Wagga Wagga Tourist Information Centre and Murrumbidgee River.

It is a 7-minute drive from Wagga Wagga Botanic Gardens and a 20-minute drive from Wagga Wagga City Airport.
