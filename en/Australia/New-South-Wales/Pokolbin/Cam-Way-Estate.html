Description

Guests staying at Cam Way Estate can relax on their private verandah overlooking the peaceful and serene landscapes of CamWays, 60 picturesque acres and Brokenback Mountain Range.

Cam Way Estate is situated in the very heart of the Central Polkolbin area of the Hunter Wine Country.

You can walk through to numerous cellar doors, restaurants, concert venues, the general store and many specialty shops as well as The Hunter Valley Gardens.

There are 4 golf courses, all within a 2-10 minute drive away. Cam Way Estate offers a range of well equipped accommodation types, including suites, 1-bedroom villas as well as 2, 3, or 4-bedroom self-contained cottages.

They all feature air-conditioning, a TV/DVD player and private verandahs, Most include a fully equipped kitchen.

You can enjoy the external fire pits in the cold winter months. Complimentary marshmallows are available from reception to toast over the fire.

Facilities that are featured at the property include a large saltwater pool with shade sail over the shallow end, a fully-sized synthetic grass tennis court with lights, a guest laundry, Weber BBQ and a conference centre.

Cam Way Estate has a liquor license and has a range of wine, beer and drinks available to purchase.

The tour desk can help with information about local vineyards, restaurants, attractions, tours and transfers.
