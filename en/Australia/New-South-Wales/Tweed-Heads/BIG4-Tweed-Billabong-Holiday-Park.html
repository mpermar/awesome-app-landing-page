Description

Offering an outdoor pool and free onsite parking, BIG4 Tweed Billabong Holiday Park is 10 minutes' drive from Coolangatta Beach and Kirra Beach.

All accommodation features a fully equipped kitchen. This holiday park is 4 km from Gold Coast Airport.

The air-conditioned villas offer a dining setting and a lounge area with a TV. Each has a private bathroom with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

At BIG4 Tweed Billabong Holiday Park you will find barbecue facilities, a shared kitchen and a mini-market.

Other facilities offered at the property include a shared lounge, a tour desk and luggage storage.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling.
