Description

Offering free WiFi, free on-site parking and a swimming pool, Shearing Shed Motor Inn is just 4 minutes’ drive from Taronga Western Plains Zoo.

All accommodation features a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. All air-conditioned rooms offer a DVD player and tea/coffee making facilities as well as kitchenware.

Each has an en suite bathroom with a shower. Some rooms feature a spa bath. Shearing Shed Motor Inn is 10 minutes' walk from the historic Old Dubbo Gaol.

It is 7 minutes’ drive from Dubbo Golf Course. Guests can enjoy an outdoor meal in the BBQ area, or dine at one of the several restaurants located within 2 minutes’ drive.
