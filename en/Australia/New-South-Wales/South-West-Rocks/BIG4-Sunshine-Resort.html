Description

Boasting a fun packed childrens water park, a tennis court and an outdoor pool, BIG4 Sunshine South West Rocks offers villa accommodation in South West Rocks.

The property offers free on-site parking. Trial Bay Beach is just 12 minutes' walk or 2 minutes' drive from BIG4 Sunshine South West Rocks.

South West Rocks Town Centre is and offers a variety of shops, restaurants and local markets.

The property backs on to Saltwater Lagoon, which guests can explore by hiring canoes.

BIG4 Sunshine guests can enjoy relaxing in the shared lounge area, or playing table tennis in the games room.

Surrounded by landscaped native gardens, you can prepare a meal using the BBQ facilities.

Each bungalow will provide you with a fully equipped kitchen including a microwave and a refrigerator.

All offer a seating area with a flat-screen TV with movie channels and a DVD player.
