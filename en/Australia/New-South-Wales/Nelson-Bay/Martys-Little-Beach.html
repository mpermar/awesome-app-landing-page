Quietly nestled between two of Port Stephensâ€™ most idyllic beaches, Marty's @ Little Beach features a heated swimming pool and poolside BBQ area.

All rooms feature Wi-Fi and free cable TV. Just a 2-minute walk from Shoal Bay Beach or Little Beach, Marty's @ Little Beach Apartments is a 3-minute drive from Port Stephens Marina, departure point for dolphin watching cruises.

Guests can choose between 1 or 2-bedroom self-contained apartments and motel-style rooms.

Air-conditioning is available in all apartments on a 2-hour cycle. Free access to self-service laundry room with washing machines and a DVD library are available, along with free car parking.
