Description

Resort Nelson has a steam room and a heated swimming pool. The proximity to the beach means that many water sports like snorkeling and windsurfing are within easy reach.

Guests can dine at the cafe or cook for themselves at the BBQ facilities provided.

The property is a 45-minute drive from Newcastle.
