Description

Located inside the Bankstown District Sports Club, Travelodge Bankstown is set in the heart of Bankstown.

It is 15 minutes' drive from Sydney Olympic Park. Sydney Motorsport Park is 30 minutes' drive away.

Guests receive 500MB of free WiFi per 24 hours. All rooms feature a TV with cable channels and an en suite bathroom.

Travelodge Bankstown offers well-appointed guest rooms and a business centre. The club offers several restaurants, bars and conference facilities.
