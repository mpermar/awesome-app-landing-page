Description

Each unit offers a fully equipped kitchenette, a flat-screen TV, a living room with a sofa, and a private bathroom. A microwave and toaster are also provided, as well as a electric tea pot.

The resort village has a grill.

A children's playground is also available at the property, while guests can also relax in the garden.

Coffs Harbour is 28 miles from Pelican Caravan Park, while Sawtell is 23 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Coffs Harbour Airport, 26 miles from the property.
