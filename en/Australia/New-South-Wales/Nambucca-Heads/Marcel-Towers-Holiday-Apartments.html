Description

In a beautiful waterfront location overlooking the Nambucca River, Marcel Towers Holiday Apartments offers air-conditioned apartments with a private balcony, fully equipped kitchen and free Wi-Fi.

Each apartment at Marcel Towers Nambucca Heads features a private bathroom and spacious lounge area with TV and DVD player.

The kitchen includes a full-size refrigerator, microwave and kitchenware. Guests have free use the property’s DVD and book library, row boats and kayaks.

The property can also provide children’s beach toys and equipment for snorkelling, fishing and cycling.

Laundry and barbecue facilities are available. Free on-site car parking is available.

There is a boat ramp located just a few metres across the road from Marcel Towers.
