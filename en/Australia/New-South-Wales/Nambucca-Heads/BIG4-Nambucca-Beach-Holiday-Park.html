Description

Located between Nambucca State Forest and Nambucca Beach, Big 4 Holiday Park offers direct beach access, an outdoor solar-heated swimming pool and a children's wading pool.

The property is 2 km from Nambucca Heads Town Centre. Accommodation options include self-contained bungalows, villas and chalets.

Some accommodation offer a fully equipped kitchen. All include a living area with a sofa.

Sawtell is 29 km from Big 4 Nambucca Beach Holiday Park. The nearest airport is Coffs Harbour Airport, 36 km from Big 4 Nambucca Beach Holiday Park.
