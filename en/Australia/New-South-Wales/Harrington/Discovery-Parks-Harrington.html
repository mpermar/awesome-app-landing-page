Description

Boasting 2 outdoor pools, a water park and an inflatable trampoline, Discovery Parks – Harrington offers something for everyone.

Guests can enjoy a game of mini golf, hire a go-kart or use the free WiFi. Discovery Parks – Harrington is a 2-minute drive from Harrington town centre and a 5-minute drive from Crowdy Head.

It is a 40-minute drive from Hallidays Point and a 50-minute drive from Port Macquarie.

The park offers a range of accommodation options, all including a dining area and free towels and linen.

Most rooms are air-conditioned, and feature a lounge area, a flat-screen TV and a balcony or patio.

You can share a meal using the BBQ facilities, or check out ‘The Shed’ offering a handball court, table tennis and a giant checkers board.
