Description

Andavine House B&B is 2 minutes' drive from the Pacific Highway and Gwyder Highway. The shops, pubs and services of Grafton city are 5 minutes' drive away.

The bed and Breakfast offers one queen-sized bedroom with balcony access, a flat-screen TV and a DVD player. Your private bathroom has a shower over a bathtub, free toiletries, large towels, bath robes and a hairdryer. Your private lounge room is air-conditioned and also contains a 4-person dining setting, flat-screen TV, DVD/CD player and a multimedia library.

You also have your own tea/coffee making facilities, a refrigerator, microwave, crockery and espresso coffee machine. Your private balcony contains a BBQ, and offers uninterrupted views of the mountains of the Clarence Valley and surrounding fields, home to kangaroos and bird life.

Reservations at nearby restaurant can be arranged at reception.
