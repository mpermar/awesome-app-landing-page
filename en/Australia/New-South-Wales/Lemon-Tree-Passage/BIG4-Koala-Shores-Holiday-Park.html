Description

BIG4 Koala Shores Holiday Park Port Stephens is located on the waterfront of Tilligerry Creek, close to Tanilba Town Centre and major supermarkets.

Free parking is included. For your entertainment, there are a range of on-site activities including canoeing and fishing.

There is also a tennis court, table tennis and a children’s playground available.

The property is a 4-minute drive from both Mallabula Sports Complex and Tanilba Bay Golf Course and a 5-minute drive from Native Corridor Koala Area.

All self-contained cottages, villas and suites offer a flat-screen TV, DVD player, air-conditioning and a fully equipped kitchen.
