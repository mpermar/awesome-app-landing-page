Description

Just 5 minutes’ drive from central Alice Springs, Alice on Todd Apartments offer self-catering accommodation with flat-screen TVs and free Wi-Fi internet.

Guests have access to the outdoor swimming pool. Alice on Todd Apartments are located just 1 minute’s walk from the Todd River.

Alice Springs Golf Course and Todd Mall are each a 5-minute drive away. The air-conditioned rooms come with a well-equipped kitchenette or kitchen and laundry facilities.

All rooms have a balcony or patio where guests can relax. The Alice on Todd barbecue area is the perfect place for a get-together with friends.
