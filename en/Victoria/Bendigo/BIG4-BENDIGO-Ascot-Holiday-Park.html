Description

Accommodations feature a seating area with a flat-screen TV and DVD player, as well as a fully equipped kitchen with a microwave and a dining area. Some units have a balcony and/or a patio with pool views.

The resort village offers a hot tub.

An outdoor swimming pool can be found at BIG4 BENDIGO Ascot Holiday Park, along with a grill.

Queen Elizabeth Oval is 2.9 miles from the accommodation, while Bendigo Marketplace is 3.2 miles away.
