Description

The Olsen rooms all feature kitchenettes and modern decor maximizing natural light and cosmopolitan views of the South Yarra and Toorak area. Each suite also features a print from painter John Olsen.

The Olsen features a large glass bottom swimming pool, day spa and a contemporary restaurant and bar.

It is right in the heart of Melbourne's best shopping district, cafes and restaurants.

South Yarra is a great choice for travelers interested in culture, food and restaurants.
