Description

This family-run hotel is 300 metres from Fanari Beach and features rooms with free Wi-Fi and a balcony overlooking the Thracian Sea or the village.

A buffet breakfast is offered. The air-conditioned rooms at Fanari Hotel have marble floors and are equipped with a satellite TV and fridge.

Some rooms have four-poster beds and a seating area. The restaurant serves Greek and European dishes and there is also room service.

The hotel bar serves drinks and coffee and an on-site nightclub plays music until late.

Younger guests can have fun at the playground in the garden. Staff at the 24-hour front desk can offer information on nearby sights such as Lake Vistonida 2 km away.

Fanari has many Blue Flag beaches, restaurants and shops. The scenic town of Xanthi is 30 km away.
