Description

Guest rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a electric tea pot, a shower, free toiletries and a desk. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom, while some accommodations at the hotel also provide guests with a seating area. All rooms have a closet.

Continental and buffet breakfast options are available each morning at King Maron Hotel & Spa. There's an on-site restaurant, which serves Greek cuisine.

At the accommodation guests are welcome to use a hot tub. You can play ping pong at King Maron Hotel & Spa.

Guests can relax in the in-house sauna or take advantage of the business center. Speaking French and Bulgarian at the 24-hour front desk, staff are willing to help at any time of the day.

Ancient Maronia is 2 miles from the hotel. Alexandroupolis Airport is 24 miles from the property.
