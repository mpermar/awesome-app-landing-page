Description

Nefeli Hotel overlooks the Thracian Sea and Samothrace and is situated close to the city centre and several beaches.

It offers spacious rooms with free internet access. The comfortable and simply decorated rooms at Nefeli Hotel are air-conditioned and offer views to the sea, pool or Nefeli's grounds.

Beautiful views of the pool can be enjoyed from Nefeli Hotel's lobby lounge. There is also a bar and a restaurant available to guests.

The restaurant overlooks the Aegean Sea. For leisure, Nefeli has an outdoor pool, tennis courts and fitness centre.
