Description

Featuring a seasonal outdoor pool amidst its blossomed garden, St. Thomas Bed and Breakfast is located in Paianía, 22 km from Athens.

Guests can relax at the furnished terrace and enjoy a drink from the on-site bar.

Free WiFi is provided throughout. Every individually decorated room at this bed and breakfast is air conditioned and is equipped with a TV.

Certain units include a seating area to relax in after a busy day. Enjoy a cup of tea while looking out at the pool or garden.

For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer. Piraeus is 36 km from St.

Thomas Bed and Breakfast, while Porto Rafti is 18 km away. Elefthérios Venizélos Airport is 13 km from the property.
