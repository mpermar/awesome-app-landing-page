Description

Featuring free WiFi and an outdoor pool, Ever Eden Beach Resort Hotel offers accommodation in Anavyssos, 33 km from Athens.

Set amid a pine-tree forest, the hotel has a children's playground and a private beach area, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

There is a private, blue-flagged beach. Opening to a balcony, each air-conditioned room of Eden Beach has a satellite TV and a minibar.

Some units feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day. Certain rooms have views of the sea or garden.

The rooms are equipped with a private bathroom. The hotel's beach restaurant and snack bar "Somewhere in Eden Resort" serves a menu, based on the Greek and Mediterranean cuisine and designed by a famous chef.

Season's and La Piscine restaurants complete the culinary proposals of the hotel.

There is a 24-hour front desk, gift shop, and shops at the property. Facilities include a business centre, suitable for conferences and business events.

This hotel has water sports facilities and car hire is available. You can play tennis at this hotel, and the area is popular for diving and canoeing.

Sun loungers and umbrellas, as well as cabins & showers are offered on the beach.

Piraeus is 33 km from EdenBeach Hotel Anavyssos. The nearest airport is Eleftherios Venizelos Airport, 23 km from Eden Beach Resort Hotel.
