Description

Free yourself of everyday worries and prepare to come and live in an inviting oriental fantasy.

Located right on the beach, Anatoli Hotel is a cosy hotel offering exceptional Greek hospitality in a unique environment.

The hotel is decorated in an exotic style. Playing with luminous colours, the hotel features amazing eastern décors, curtains, carpets and lights together with oriental cuisine and beautiful music to assist you on a journey to experience a true Arabic fairytale.

Extending the feeling of relaxation is the hotel’s location. Anatoli Hotel is situated close to a gorgeous beach of golden sand and clear waters.

You can enjoy these views, or you can sit lazily in our lush garden and feel the sea breeze.
