Description

Situated in Vouliagmeni, the renowned Athens Riviera, Vouliagmeni Suites is within easy reach from the beach, nightlife and Glyfada shopping area.

It enjoys panoramic views to the Saronic Guld and offers free Wi-Fi and digital, satellite TV.

The comfortable and contemporary accommodation consists of deluxe rooms and suites.

Each individual furnished room contain exclusive equipment such as a Bang and Olufsen TV and power showers.

In the winter, guests can dine in the brightly decorated Adam and Eve restaurant, while in the summer the restaurant’s veranda offers panoramic sea views.

There is also the pool bar which is a tranquil spot offering a terrace overlooking the sea.

Vouliagmeni Suites lies 20 km from the city centre of Athens with all the main attractions.
