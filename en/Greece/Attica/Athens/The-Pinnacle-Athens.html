Description

Offering city views from floor-to-ceiling windows, The Pinnacle Athens in Athens is situated at a high building and offers accommodation, a shared lounge and a terrace.

The accommodation is air conditioned and comes with a walk-in shower with hydromassage jets.

Fitted with velvet pillows and curtains, decorative fireplace and original artwork, all units offer a kitchen, a spacious seating area with a smart, flat-screen TV, dining area and a private bathroom with a hair dryer, free toiletries and shower.

Guests wishing to travel lightly can make use of towels and linens for an additional supplement.

Free, high-speed WiFi is provided. The Pinnacle Athens offers a roof garden with views of the Acropolis and other city sights.

Both a bicycle rental service and a car rental service are available at the accommodation.

Popular points of interest near The Pinnacle Athens include Ermou Street-Shopping Area, Syntagma Square and Monastiraki Square.

The nearest airport is Elefthérios Venizélos, 34 km from the aparthotel, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
