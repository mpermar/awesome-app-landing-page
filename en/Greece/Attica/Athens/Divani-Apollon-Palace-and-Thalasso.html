Description

Surrounded by the enchanting scenery of Athens Riviera, with magnificent sea views, Divani Apollon Palace & Thalasso features a state-of-the-art thalassotherapy centre.

In a majestic environment surrounded by sea, guests enjoy 3 pools, 4 restaurants and a private beach.

As a member of The Leading Hotels of the World, Divani Apollon Palace & Thalasso offers luxurious rooms with furnished sea-facing balconies.

The elegantly decorated rooms, are fitted with designer fabrics, oak furniture, paintings and all share stunning views to the sea and the pools.

At Mythos by Divani fine-dining restaurant, guests can indulge in a delicious gastronomy with treasures of the sea and culinary plates accompanied by fine wine and an intimate atmosphere just in front of the sea.

At Breakfast the extensive buffet includes a great variety of Greek delicacies as well as European favorites and of course fresh fruits and juices.

Casual restaurants include Anemos, with buffets and outdoor seating with views to the pool and sea, the bright and contemporary Atlantis Lounge and A&D bar, both offering light snacks, signature cocktails and live music.

Meltemi poolside snack bar offers a shaded seating area that overlooks the pools and is open during the summer period.

The Awarded Spa, by World Travel Awards 2014, invites guests to experience a completely different programme of rejuvenation and wellbeing with its programs.

Facilities include a 270-m² indoor pool, roman baths, 2 saunas, 2 Turkish baths and a variety of more than 100 facial and body therapies.

Active guests will enjoy the flood-lit tennis court and jogging route. Watersports at the property's private beach are also available.

Divani Apollon Palace & Thalasso is 18 km from downtown Athens. Glyfada, famous for shopping and night life, is only 5 minutes away by shuttle bus.
