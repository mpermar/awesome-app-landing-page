Description

Set in the Athenian Riviera, the 5-star Four Seasons Astir Palace boasts 8 restaurants and bars, a spa centre with 10 treatment rooms, a fitness centre and a private beach area.

Surrounded by pine trees, the hotel consists of 2 different buildings and also includes 5 tennis courts in its premises.

Elegantly decorated in soft tones, the air-conditioned rooms and suites at Four Seasons Astir Palace Hotel Athens are fitted with modern furnishings and floor-to-ceiling windows.

They all offer views over the pine trees or the sea, while some come with private pool.

Guests can start their day with an American buffet breakfast served in the dining area.

For lunch or dinner, guests can choose among Greek seafood, Italian cuisine or Latin American grill accompanied by fine wine and served at the on-site restaurants.

Some of the worlds finest cigars, rare cognacs and whiskeys can also be enjoyed at the cigar lounge.

The on-site spa centre includes an indoor lap pool, hammam, vitality pool and chill showers and also offers a variety of massages and other treatments.

Yoga classes are available at the fitness area, while water sport activities such as wind surfing, sea kayak and snorkeling can be enjoyed at the private beach area.

The centre of Athens lies within a 30-minute drive of Four Seasons Astir Palace Hotel Athens, while Glyfada Marina is 9 km away.

The nearest airport is Elefthrios Venizlos Airport, 24 km from the hotel. Free WiFi access and free, on-site parking are provided.
