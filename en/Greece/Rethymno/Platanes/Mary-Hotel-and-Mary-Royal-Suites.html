Description

Situated in a lush garden 100 metres from the sandy beach of Rethymno, Mary Hotel Apts & Mary Royal features rooms with a kitchenette and a furnished balcony.

It includes a swimming pool and offers free on-site parking. Mary air-conditioned rooms are very spacious and bright and offer views to the pool.

They have a well-equipped kitchenette with a dining area. Satellite TV, a hairdryer and a safety box are standard.

Next to the pool, guests can find a garden with sun loungers and umbrellas. Other Mary Hotel Apts & Mary Royal facilities include a sauna, a fitness room, a billiards table and a room with PCs and free Internet access.

The hotel’s bar features an open-air seating area by the pool. Greek nights with BBQ, Cretan music and dancing are organised often.

Free WiFi is available in public areas of the hotel. Shops, cafes and taverns are at 150 meters.

Within a few metres from Mary Hotel Apts & Mary Royal, there is a bus stop connecting to the centre of Rethymno located 4 km away.
