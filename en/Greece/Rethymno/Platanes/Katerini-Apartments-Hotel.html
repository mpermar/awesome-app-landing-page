Description

Featuring a pool and a snack bar amidst its lush garden, Katerini Hotel Apartments is located in Platanias of Rethymno, just 150 metres from the blue-flag beach.

It offers self-catering units with a balcony overlooking the garden or the pool. The air-conditioned apartments of Katerini are traditionally decorated with built-in sofas and soft colours.

Featuring a seating area and a kitchenette with dining area and cooking hob, each unit is equipped with a fridge, a TV and a safety deposit box.

The private bathroom comes with a shower. Guests can relax at the sun loungers by the pool or enjoy a refreshing drink and a light snack at the on-site snack bar.

Billiards are also available. Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas. Katerini Apartments Hotel lies within 150 metres from taverns and super markets.

Heraklion International Airport is at 83 km, while Chania Airport is 75 km away. Rethymno Town and Port are at 5 km.
