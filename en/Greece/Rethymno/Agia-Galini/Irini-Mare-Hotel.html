Description

Set on a gentle hill, with a panoramic view of Agia Galini bay, 4-star Irini Mare Hotel is only 50 metres from a pebble beach and a 10-minute walk from Agia Galini centre.

It features 2 outdoor pools and a children's pool, while a pool bar is situated amidst the Mediterranean garden.

Bright and airy, the rooms are air conditioned and have a bathroom with hairdryer.

A fridge is included and the TV comes with satellite channels. Most balconies and terraces offer a view to the sea.

A complimentary bottle of water upon arrival is offered. Irini Mare Hotel serves a rich breakfast buffet including fresh fruit and a cooking station where eggs and pancakes are prepared.

The air-conditioned restaurant serves a Greek dinner buffet with cooking stations for salads, pasta or pizza and local and vegetarian dishes using locally sourced olive oil.

Lunchtime snacks are available at the pool bar. On-site sports facilities include tennis and table tennis.

A children’s freshwater pool and a playground are featured in the garden. A library and a satellite TV corner are also available.

The multilingual staff are always at your disposal, and will be glad to help you with car rental or area information.
