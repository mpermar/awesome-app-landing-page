Description

Featuring a spacious pool with sun terrace, Ikaros Studios are built within an 8,000-square-metre olive grove in the Cretan village of Plakias.

The complex has a Mediterranean garden with sun beds and a play area for children.

The self-catered studios and apartments Ikaros are air-conditioned. Free in-room Wi-Fi is available, and rooms come with a TV and a well equipped kitchenette with fridge.

Nearby beaches include Plakias at 700 metres, Damnoni at 1. 2 km and Amoudi at 2 km.

A shaded parking area is available on site. Staff are always willing to offer you a raki and help you with information about the area.

Your two-way transfer to the airport and assistance with car rentals can also be arranged.
