Description

Grecotel Creta Palace enjoys a prime location, right by a fine sandy beach and 5 km from the centre of Rethymnon.

This exclusive 5-star resort boasts 10 restaurants and bars, 2 outdoor pools. one indoor pool and a private beach area.

The elegant rooms at the Grecotel Creta Palace are air conditioned and have wooded floors.

They come with a minibar, flat-screen TV comes with satellite channels and mini hi-fi.

Bathrobes and slippers are complimentary. Awarded for its fine cuisine, Grecotel Creta Palace offers no less than 10 food and beverage choices.

Guests can spend relaxed moments in the impressive pool area with outdoor pools, indoor pools and children’s pool with waterslides.

The new sea-front Elixir Spa proposes facial and body treatments from Indian therapists.

Features include an indoor pool with jet black mosaics, pool-view gym with state-of-the-art equipment and a hot tub sun patio.
