Description

Located right on the beachfront in the west side of Rethymno Town, Filoxenia Beach Hotel features an outdoor swimming pool and a kids pool with sea water.

It offers rooms and suites that open to a furnished balcony with magnificent Cretan Sea views.

Free Wi-Fi is available in all areas. All units are simply decorated and come with air conditioning and a satellite, flat-screen TV.

They also have a bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries. Free beach towels and sun loungers are available at the private beach area.

Guests can enjoy a homemade breakfast served daily until 11:00 at the on-site terrace offering views over the Cretan Sea and the Fortezza.

Light meals and drinks are also available at Filoxenia’s snack bar. Various taverns, supermarkets and bakeries can be found in close distance.

A gift corner, where guests can find the famous Greek handmade sandals "Sundali" is located in Filoxenia Beach Hotel.

There is free parking at an open area just 200 meters away from the hotel, direction to the city.

Nikos Kazantzakis International Airport, as well as Chania International Airport are approximately 80 km away.
