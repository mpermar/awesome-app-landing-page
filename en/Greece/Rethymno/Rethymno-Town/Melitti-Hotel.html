Description

This modern beach hotel offers a perfect blend of warm Cretan hospitality combined with advanced standards and facilities in a very pleasant setting.

The 22 recently refurbished studios and apartments have a private balcony or terrace with sea or pool view. Each unit features modern comforts including a kitchenette, safe and TV.

Meliti Hotel offers a pool especially for younger guests, equipped with free beach and pool toys. The pool area also offers a pool bar and free sun umbrellas and deck chairs.

Within easy reach of all historic, cultural and natural attractions of the island, one hour’s drive from Heraklion and Chania, the hotel is an ideal base to discover Crete.

The hotel is conveniently located next to bakeries, mini markets, bars and restaurants, while the bus stop is only 250 feet away.

Experiences

05/24/2017

No hesitation in recommending this hotel :)

"The hotel, room and facilities were spotlessly clean. The staff were so friendly and helpful and were wonderful with the children. Rooms are cleaned daily and towels and bed linen every other day. You can have a breakfast for 2 in the morning for just 10€ which has fresh bread, jam, honey, butter, 2 eggs, fruit, cake, ham and cheese toasties, juice and coffee and can be delivered to the room for no extra charge. The local shops, beach and restaurants at the doorstep were all great. Just a couple things for us as a family (2 adults a 3 year old and 6 month old): the balconies are wood decorative and being on the second floor we were really nervous with our 3 year old. There are no lifts so was difficult with a pushchair and climbing the 3 flights of stairs. The walk to town in the heat seemed longer than a mile away. We rented a car during our stay and parking is all on street. It is really horrendous finding a space and we often drive up and down the streets trying to park. If we go back we will do an airport transfer and rent a car for a couple days at around €25 a day. After all said I have no hesitation in recommending this hotel and we will definitely be returning when the kiddies are older :)"
