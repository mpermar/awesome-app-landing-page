Description

Alexander Hotel is situated in Gerakari, 35 km south of Rethymno. Set on the foot of Mount Kedros, it features an outdoor pool, tennis court and 50-person conference hall.

Free bikes are provided. Alexander Hotel rooms have balconies with garden or mountain views.

They all come with a kettle, fridge and hotplate. Spacious and bright, they also feature a satellite LCD TV, DVD player and safe.

A Continental breakfast prepared with fresh, local ingredients is served daily. The on-site restaurant serves traditional Cretan dishes.

In the hotel lounge guests can play table games, while in a specially arranged recreation room there are machine toys and electronic games units.

Free Wi-Fi access is available in all communal spaces. Hiking, climbing, bird watching and cooking classes are popular activities in the area.

Hotel staff are always willing to offer you information about Gerakari area, as well about certain activities you may take up or some routes you may wish to hike.
