Description

The 4-star Hotel Yalis is built in the countryside of Alonnisos, right beside crystal clear beaches, offering free Wi-Fi, free on-site parking and a swimming pool.

Hotel Yalis offers fully equipped rooms, featuring air conditioning and TV. Each offers a spacious seating area, private bathroom and kitchen.

Yalis Hotel is situated in the area of Botsis, overlooking a small bay, offering panoramic views of the sea.

It is only 5 km from the island's main town (Chora) and only 2 km away from the Patitiri harbour.
