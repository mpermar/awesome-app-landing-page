Description

Opening to a balcony with mountain or grounds views, all rooms are simply furnished with stone-paved floors. Each has a mini fridge, TV and private bathroom with hairdryer.

Guests can start their day with a buffet breakfast with homemade donuts. Refreshing drinks and cocktails are served at the poolside bar. Mini markets and taverns can be found within 200 feet.

Afroditi is located half a mile from the beaches of Milia and Kastani. The main town and port of Skopelos is 7 miles away. Free, private parking is available on site.
