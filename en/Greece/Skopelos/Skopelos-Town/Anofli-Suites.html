Description

Featuring a pool with a sun terrace and a snack bar amidst its blossomed garden, the family-run Anofli Suites is centrally located in the scenic Skopelos Town.

It offers rooms with free WiFi access and a balcony overlooking the pool and the garden.

The rooms of Anofli Suites have a seating area and fridge. Each unit is equipped with air conditioning, a fan and a TV.

The private bathroom comes with a shower. A buffet breakfast is served daily at the dining area, while guests can also enjoy coffees, drinks and light snacks at the poolside snack bar.

Restaurants and supermarkets can be found within a short walk from the property. Anofli Suites is situated 650 metres from Skopelos Port and within 5 km from Stafilos Village.

Panormos Village with its sandy beach is at 13 km. Free transfer from and to the port can be arranged and free private parking is possible on site.
