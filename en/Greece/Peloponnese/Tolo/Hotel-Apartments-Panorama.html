Description

Boasting an outdoor pool with sunbeds, Hotel Apartments Panorama is located in Tolo, just 250 metres from the sandy beach.

It offers self-catering accommodation with Argolic Gulf views from their balcony.

Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas. All air-conditioned units at Panorama feature a kitchenette with fridge, and a TV with satellite channels.

The private bathroom is fitted with a shower. Guests can enjoy Greek and international specialties at the on-site restaurant, while nights with live Greek music and dancing are also featured.

There is also a bar serving refreshing drinks on site. Room service is provided. A playground is available for younger guests.

Services such as car rentals and dry cleaning are also provided upon request. The picturesque town of Nafplio can be reached in 10 km and the Ancient Theatre of Epidavros lies within 25 km.
