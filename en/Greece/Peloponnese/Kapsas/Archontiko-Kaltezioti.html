Description

At the hotel, rooms include a desk and a flat-screen TV. The rooms have a electric tea pot and a private bathroom, while selected rooms also feature a terrace and some have mountain views. The rooms at Archontiko Kaltezioti have air conditioning and a closet.

The accommodation offers a continental or buffet breakfast. The restaurant at Archontiko Kaltezioti specializes in European and Greek cuisine.
