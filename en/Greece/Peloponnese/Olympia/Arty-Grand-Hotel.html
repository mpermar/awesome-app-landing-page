Description

The Arty Grand Hotel is located on top of a hill overlooking Olympia. During the summer, guests can enjoy an outdoor swimming pool, while on the weekends the hotel operates a spa centre with massage rooms.

The spacious rooms are colorful with modern furnishings. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, a telephone, and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Wi-Fi is free throughout. The Arty Grand serves a daily breakfast buffet. Lunch and dinner menus are also available at the in-house restaurant.

Guests can relax and enjoy a beverage from either the hotel's bar or the poolside bar.

Nearby local attractions include the Museum of the History of the Ancient Olympic Games, the archaeological ruins of Olympia, and the Archaeological Museum of Olympia.
