Description

Offering views of the surrounding area, Olvios Hotel is situated in Goura in the Peloponnese Region.

Lake Doxa is 9 km away. There is a restaurant and guests can have fun at the games room.

Free private parking is available on site. Each room at this hotel is air conditioned and has a TV with satellite channels.

Extras include free toiletries and a hair dryer. Olvios Hotel features free WiFi.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. Vytina is 33 km from Olvios Hotel, while Kato Trikala Korinthias is 14 km from the property.
