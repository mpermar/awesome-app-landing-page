Description

Rex is a family-run hotel located in the outskirts of the small town of Zacharo, at a distance of 1 km from the coastal line of western Peloponnese.

Decorated with well-kept plants, antiques and rattan furniture, it offers a sense of countryside living.

The hotel offers modern, spacious rooms with a view. All rooms are air conditioned and fitted with a TV.

They have balconies and private bathrooms with mosaic and terracotta tiles. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

The hotel offers free parking and a children's play-room as well as a children's loan-library.

Homemade breakfast with local products is served in the bright and colorful dining area or in the evergreen courtyard.

Olympia is at a distance of 23 km from Rex Hotel, while Vassai and Stemnitsa are 39 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Kalamata Airport, 57 km from Rex Hotel. Free private parking is available on site.
