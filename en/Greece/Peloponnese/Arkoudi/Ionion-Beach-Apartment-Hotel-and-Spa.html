Description

Featuring an outdoor pool and a restaurant with a sea-view terrace, Ionion Beach offers self-catering accommodation.

It is located in Glyfa within 2 km from Arkoudi. Apartments at Ionion Beach feature a TV, air conditioning and a seating area.

There is a full kitchenette with a refrigerator and kitchenware. Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and slippers.

At Ionion Beach you will find a garden, a bar and a snack bar. The property offers free parking.

The village of Vartholomio is 7 km away. The port of Kyllini connecting to the island of Zakynthos is 15 km away.
