Description

Kandia's Castle Hotel, Resort & Thalasso, is a 5-star hotel located in Kandia, Nafplio, right on a private beach of the Argolic gulf.

All rooms are luxurious, decorated with unique style and elegance, offering all modern amenities that guarantee a pleasant stay.

The hotels cuisine offers a combination of Mediterranean and Greek tastes and boasts unchallenged gourmet services.

Designed with a unique architectural style, which combines a castle appearance with the natural environment, Kandia's Castle offers modern and luxurious facilities and services, including a very big pool, a spa with sauna, 2 indoor heated pools and a fitness centre.

The location is ideal to explore many sights in the surrounding area such as Nafplio, Epidaurus and Mycenae.
