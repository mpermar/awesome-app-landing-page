Description

Papanikola Guesthouse is located on the slopes of the Mainalon mount. It offers charming, rustic rooms, traditional breakfast and a wooden porch with stunning views of the mountain range.

Rooms combine traditional architecture, a chic rustic charm with modern facilities.

Each is en suite with individual heating, air conditioning, TV, refrigerator, safe and mini hi-fi.

Rooms designed for people with mobility problems are available upon request. Hotel Papanikola serves homemade breakfast prepared with local produce and delicacies, including country eggs, homemade preserves and local honey.

From the hotels' wooden porch guests can enjoy the view to the Mainalon mountain range as well as to the Falanthos fields, and down to the Parnon and Taygetos mountains.

The guesthouses’ location in the village of Piana allows access to nearby attractions.

Worth visiting are the cave of God Pan and the old dome church Panagia Polykammeni.
