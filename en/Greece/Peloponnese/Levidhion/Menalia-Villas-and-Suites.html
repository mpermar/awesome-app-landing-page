Description

Built with respect to the local traditional architecture, Menalia Villas & Suites are 1 km from Levidi Town.

They offer free bikes and tasteful accommodation with a fireplace and views of Mount Mainalo.

Free Wi-Fi is available throughout. Fitted with wooden ceiling and stone details, all suites and villas at Menalia open to a furnished balcony.

Each features a seating area with flat-screen TV and a kitchen with stove and dining table.

Bathrobes and slippers are provided. Guests may use the free bikes and enjoy the property surroundings.

A sun terrace and a well-tended garden with trees and grassy areas are available.

More facilities include a breakfast room, cafe and snack bar. A mini market for the general supplies, a restaurant and a caf bar can be found 1 km away.

Mainalo Ski Centre is at a distance of 8 km. Elati Village is 22 km away and Vitina Village is 24 km away.
