Description

Set in well-tended gardens bordered by olive trees, Anthemion Suites and Villas lies within 800 metres from Nafplions Old Town.

Facilities include a heated swimming pool, bar, modern restaurant and private parking.

All rooms are fitted with a flat screen TV ,a fridge,a kettle,a,shower and desk,wardrobe and private bathroom.

Most open to balconies and some offer wide views to the surrounding countryside across Palamidi and the city of Nafplion.

Air conditioning comes standard. Guest can start their day with a buffet breakfast served daily at the on site restaurant,where they can also taste healthy,locally inspired dishes for lunch or dinner.

Anthemion Suites and Villas is convenient for day trips to the archaeological sites of Epidavros and Mycenae.

Karathona Beach is a 10-minute drive away, while Palamidi Castle is within walking distance.
