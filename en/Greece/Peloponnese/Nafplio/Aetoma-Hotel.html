Description

The rooms of Aetoma Hotel are decorated according to the building’s architectural style with works of art and handmade carpets. They are air-conditioned and feature flat screen TVs, refrigerators and free Wi-Fi. The private bathrooms have hydro-massage showers.

Breakfast is served until noon on linen tablecloths in the breakfast room. It includes homemade jams and traditional pastries.

The hotel is next to the historic church of St Spyridon where the first governor of the modern Greek state, Kapodistrias, was assassinated.
