Description

Fitted with floor-to-ceiling windows, artworks and elegant furnishings, the suites and villas of Mandola Rosa offer sea views. Each is equipped with air conditioning, safe and a minibar. The luxurious bathrooms come with bathtub, bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries. Some villas include private pools.

International buffet breakfast can be enjoyed daily at the terrace of the Breakfast Salon, while Gourmet Mediterranean dishes are served at the Cap Voyage Restaurant for dinner. The Asia Rosa serves flavours from the East in a sophisticated setting. Guests can also choose to dine in any of the 15 restaurants and bars of the Olympia Riviera Complex.

The 4,500 m² Elixir Thalassotherapy Centre, located within Olympia Riviera premises, includes an indoor pool, hot tubs, massage rooms, sauna and a fitness room. Younger guests can spend their time at the at the Grecoland Club including paddling and children’s pools, waterslides and playgrounds.

The 24-hour, multilingual staff can arrange car rental or airport transfer upon request. Concierge and room service, as well as currency exchange are also available. A spacious conference centre with a variety of modern equipped meeting rooms is featured at the premises of Olympia Riviera Complex.

Kyllini Town and Port, connecting to Zakynthos Island, are located 8.7 miles from Mandola Rosa, while Arkoudi Village is 1.2 miles away. Free on-site parking is provided and free WiFi access is available throughout.
