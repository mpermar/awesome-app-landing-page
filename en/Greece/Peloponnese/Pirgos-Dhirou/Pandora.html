Description

Offering a panoramic sea view, Pandora Hotel is centrally situated in Mani, a 4-km distance from Areopolis.

The 3-star hotel offers elegant, fully furnished apartments with panoramic sea views, and features a caf bar and a gym.

Surrounded by towers and medieval ruins, Pandoras homely apartments have private verandas.

Each has a fully equipped kitchen, and many offer a living area with fireplace. Breakfast with local, homemade products is served at the charming patio, surrounded by flower beds and cobbled pathways.

The caf bar is open throughout the day. Guests can also use the one-site gym facilities.

Pandora is 7 km from Diros Caves, and just 1. 5 km from the crystal clear waters of Diros Bay.

A 10-km drive will take you to Limeni Beach. Private parking is provided on site.
