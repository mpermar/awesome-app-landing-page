Description

Surrounded by the lush greenery of Mount Parnonas, the stone-built Pritanio offers traditional accommodation in Polydroso Village of Lakonia.

The property‚Äôs public areas include a restaurant and a bar and enjoy free Wi-Fi access.

Featuring wooden floors and beamed ceilings, all rooms of Pritanio are decorated with well-chosen furnishings and soft colours.

Each is equipped with iron, while most of them open out to a balcony enjoying views over the green surroundings.

Some units are split-level and some come with a fireplace. A rich breakfast is offered daily at the dining area or in the rooms upon request, while guests can enjoy local delicacies at the elegant on-site restaurant.

The hotel‚Äôs bar offers a warm setting for a night drink by the fireplace. Staff at the front desk can arrange bicycle rental and can provide information on excursions in the area.

A small spot where guests can buy local products is available in the reception area.

Pritanio is situated 42 km from Mystras and 35 km from the town of Sparti. The seaside Gytheio village is at 68 km from the property.
