Description

Cubaneros Holiday Inn is located in Laganas just 50 metres from the Beach and is a family business.

Featuring a beach bar directly located on beach, the hotel provides free sun-beds and umbrellas for the guests.

The rooms are air conditioned and WiFi is available throughout the property free of charge.

Each room has a flat screen TV. All rooms are on ground floor and they all have a terrace with sitting area.

You will find a small corner area in each room which is equipped with a refrigerator, kettle, plates, glasses, cups and cutlery for guests to prepare cold meals.

A basket of fruits and a bottle of water is offered upon arrival. All rooms are cleaned free of charge and towels and linens are changed every 2 days.

Guests can enjoy a drink at the on-site bar. The property includes water sports facilities and car, scooter or bike hire are available upon request.

The area is also popular for snorkeling and diving and Caretta Caretta Turtle spotting.
