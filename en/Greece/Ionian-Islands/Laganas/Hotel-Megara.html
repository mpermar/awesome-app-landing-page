Description

Backed by olive groves, Hotel Megara is just 250m from Laganas beach. Guests have access to the hotel’s saltwater swimming pool with poolside-bar.

Rooms at Hotel Megara are comfortably furnished and offer air conditioning, a safety box and a fridge.

Most of the rooms have balcony with sea view. Hotel Megara is ideally located 8 km from Zakynthos town, 1.

5 from Agios Sostis beach and near the lively centre of Laganas. Guests can park their cars at the on-site free car park.
