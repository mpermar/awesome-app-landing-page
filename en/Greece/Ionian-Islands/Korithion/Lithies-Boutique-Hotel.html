Description

Located in the area of Skinari, Lithies offers elegant self-catering rooms built within beautiful gardens.

It features a swimming pool with panoramic sea views and provides free Wi-Fi access.

Lithies Studios Apartments are spacious and stylishly decorated. They include a fully equipped kitchen with a dining area.

TV, air conditioning and hairdryer are standard. All studios and apartments have furnished balconies boasting views of the Ionian Sea and the neighboring Kefalonia.

In the swimming pool area guests can find sun beds, umbrellas, towels and showers, as well as a pool bar serving snacks, salads, cocktails and refreshing drinks.

There is also a separate pool for the children. Lithies Studios Apartments are 500 metres from the sea.

Agios Nikolaos port is 2 km away where boats depart for day trips to the famous blue caves and the shipwreck.

Hotel Lithies provides free private on-site parking, as well as free bikes to the guests.
