Description

Within 250 metres from the beach and 1 km from the popular town of Nydri, Poseidonio A has a swimming pool with poolside bar overlooking the Ionian Sea.

Its air-conditioned rooms enjoy sea or pool views. Poseidonio rooms are decorated in light colours and have a private balcony.

They are equipped with air conditioning and an LCD TV. All rooms also have a marble bathroom with shower and free toiletries.

Sun beds and umbrellas are available around the pool. Guests can also relax at the pool bar and enjoy refreshing drinks, fresh juices and cocktails.

A buffet breakfast is served daily. The main town of Lefkada is 16 km away from Poseidonio A.

Regular boats depart from Nydri Port and connect to the neighbouring island of Meganisi.
