Description

Offering an outdoor pool, children's playground and terrace, Almyros Beach is set in Acharavi in the Corfu Region, 36 km from Corfu Town.

Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. Certain units have a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

Some units feature views of the sea or mountain. Every room has a private bathroom.

Almyros Beach features free WiFi throughout the property. A flat-screen TV is featured.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. This hotel has water sports facilities and bike hire and car hire are available.

Glyfada is 42 km from Almyros Beach, while Sarandë is 17 km from the property. The nearest airport is Corfu International Airport, 42 km from Almyros Beach.
