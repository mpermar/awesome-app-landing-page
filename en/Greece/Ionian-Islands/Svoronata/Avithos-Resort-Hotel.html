Description

The fully furnished self-catering apartments at Avithos Resort combine traditional Kefalonian style with modern comforts. Each apartment or studio offers its own distinct personality, plenty of space and a well-equipped kitchen, creating an ideal home away from home.

Avithos Resort Hotel boasts beautiful gardens and a large freshwater swimming pool. Relax around the pool on one of the comfortable lounge chairs and enjoy a cocktail from the friendly pool bar.

The sandy beach of Avithos is just a short walk from the hotel and there are plenty of good local restaurants nearby when you want a break from cooking. Lassi Beach is just 5 miles away and the capital of Argostoli is 6 miles away, ideal for a day trip.
