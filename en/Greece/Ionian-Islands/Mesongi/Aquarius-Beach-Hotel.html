Description

Situated 10 metres from the sandy beach of Mesongi, Aquarius Beach Hotel provides an outdoor pool with sun loungers.

Free WiFi internet is available in all areas. All spacious units are air conditioned and offer a furnished private balcony.

The rooms and studios come equipped with a TV, safe, refrigerator and kettle, and feature private bathrooms with showers.

The studios include a dining area and a fully equipped kitchen. The centre of the village is only 50 metres away, and offers a wide choice of taverns, bars, snack bars and a supermarket and bakery.

A water sports centre is available at 200 metres. Aquarius Beach Hotel is 20 km from Corfu Town.

Frequent bus service to Corfu Town is available and the bus stop is 10 metres away from the property.

Car, scooter and bike rentals, as well as excursions can be arranged at the reception.
