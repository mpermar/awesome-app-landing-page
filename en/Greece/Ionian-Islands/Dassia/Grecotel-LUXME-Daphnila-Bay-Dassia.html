Description

Featuring 2 freshwater pools and a children's pool amidst its blossomed gardens, the Grecotel Daphnila Bay literally tumbles down the green hillside overlooking Dassia Bay in Corfu.

A variety of activities and facilities, such as children and teenagers’ clubs, fitness and spa facilities, are offered on site.

All well appointed guestrooms feature a balcony or terrace, and satellite TV. Rooms are overlooking the gardens and Ionian Sea, while bungalows are hidden amongst the olive trees.

The private, en-suite bathrooms come with shower or bathtub, hairdryer and free toiletries.

Greek and international flavours can be enjoyed at the Marco Polo restaurant for lunch or dinner, while homemade pizza and fresh pasta is served at the Giardino di Mama beachside restaurant.

Guests can also relax and enjoy a refreshing drink at the Waterfall poolside bar or at the elegantly decorated lobby area.

A selection of rejuvenating treatments are provided at the on-site spa centre featuring an indoor pool, a hot tub, sauna and steam rooms.

Guests who want to stay fit can also use the fitness facilities available on site.

The centre of Dassia lies within 2. 5 km from Grecotel Daphila Bay, while the scenic Corfu Town is 13 km away.

Corfu International Airport is at a distance of 14 km away. Hotel services include a 24-hour front desk and extensive concierge services.
