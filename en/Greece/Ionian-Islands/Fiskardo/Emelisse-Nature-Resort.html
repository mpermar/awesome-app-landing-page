Description

Offering an outdoor pool and surrounded by native cypress and cedar treest Emelisse Nature Resort is situated along the Ionian Sea shoreline, in Fiskardo of Kefalonia.

It offers free WiFi in the reception hall and pool area, and panoramic views of surrounding mountains and sea.

All air-conditioned rooms at Emelisse Nature Resort have a balcony and come complete with a private bathroom fitted with a hairdryer and Korres toiletries.

Each has a flat-screen, satellite TV and a minibar, while some rooms are fitted with a terrace.

The rooms will provide guests with a wardrobe and an espresso coffee machine. The outdoor pool, restaurant and lounge bar are set on a wide, sea-facing terrace.

The accommodation offers a fitness centre. You can play table tennis at Emelisse Nature Resort, and bike hire is available.

Homemade breakfast buffet and seafood or traditional Mediterranean dishes are served throughout the day for lunch and dinner.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can provide advice on what to do in the area. The nearest airport is Kefalonia Airport, 39 km from the hotel.
