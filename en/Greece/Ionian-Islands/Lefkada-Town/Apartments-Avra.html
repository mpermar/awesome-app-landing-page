Description

Located 3 km away from the main town of Lefkada, Apartments Avra is set amidst a large fruit-tree garden featuring a swimming pool.

It offers self-catering accommodation with free Wi-Fi access and private balconies.

The air-conditioned studios and apartments at Avra are bright and airy, enjoying views of the pool or the garden.

They come with a kitchenette equipped with mini fridge, electric kettle and microwave.

Each unit also has an en-suite bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer. Some studios overlook the sea.

The nearest restaurant can be found within 2 km from the property. Agios Ioannis Beach is 3 km away.
