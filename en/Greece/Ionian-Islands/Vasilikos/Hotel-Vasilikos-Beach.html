Description

The Hotel Vasilikos Beach is located in the bay of St Nicolas 150 meters away from two sandy beaches.

It is surrounded by a green garden and offers air-conditioned rooms. Room facilities include private balconies, refrigerators and safe deposit boxes.

They all have private bathrooms with shower. Vasilikos Beach features Wi-Fi throughout the premises, a swimming pool and a hot tub.

There is also a pool bar and a barbecue where guests can enjoy drinks and snacks.
