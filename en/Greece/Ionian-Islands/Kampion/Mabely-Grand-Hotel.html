Description

Covering a lush 60. 000-m² area in Kampion Village, the 5-star Mabely Grand Hotel features 16 pools, 1 children's pool and a restaurant.

The classically decorated property offers a variety of leisure and sports facilities, such as a bridge room.

Opening to a furnished balcony or a patio, the air-conditioned rooms of Mabely overlook the Ionian Sea or the garden.

Each features draped curtains, luxurious linen and parquet floors. A fridge and an LCD satellite TV are available.

The marble bathroom is stocked with toiletries and a hairdryer. Continental breakfast is served daily at the dining area.

Guests can enjoy local and traditional flavours at “Lydia” restaurant with views of the Ionian Sea.

Mabely Grand Hotel is located 29 km from Zakynthos Town and Port and 25 km from Zakynthos International Airport.

Free private parking is possible on site and free Wi-Fi is available in the hotel's lobby.
