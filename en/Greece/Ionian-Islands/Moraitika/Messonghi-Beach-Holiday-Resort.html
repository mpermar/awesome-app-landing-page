Description

Situated right on the beach in Moraitika Village, Messonghi Beach Holiday Resort features an aqua park consisting of 3 swimming pools.

Corfu Town and Corfu Airport are 23 km away. All accommodation types are built among gardens and lawns that run down to a pebbled beach.

Rooms are air-conditioned, have a satellite TV, and a private bathroom with a shower and hairdryer.

Hotel-owned beach umbrellas are spread along the beach free of charge and guests can rent sun beds at a extra charge.

Sunbeds at the swimming pools are free of charge. The complex consists of 3 outdoor swimming pools (2 adult pools and 1 mini aquapark), several bars, a beach bar, 2 restaurants, a restaurant on the beach serving snacks, a night club, several shops and boutiques, a conference centre and a play land.

Waterslides are also available upon charge. More facilities include a spa centre and a large choice of sport facilities.
