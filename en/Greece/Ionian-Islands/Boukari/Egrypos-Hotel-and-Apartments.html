Description

Egrypos Hotel & Apartments is located only 50 metres from the beach in the charming village of Petriti in Corfu.

It has a swimming pool with pool bar and a restaurant. You can find the sandy beach of Petriti 1-minute walk away.

The Hotel offers air-conditioned rooms and apartments equipped with free Wi-Fi, a fridge and private balcony with view to the sea.

Guests can relax on the sun terrace that features free sun loungers. Hotel Egrypos offers splendid gardens with a childrens playground area.

Next to the family run hotel, car and scooter hires are available. A wide variety of fish restaurants can be found nearby.
