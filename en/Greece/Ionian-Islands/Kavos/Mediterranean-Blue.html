Description

Offering an outdoor pool, a children's pool and a bar-restaurant, Mediterranean Blue offers accommodation with private balconies.

Asprokavos Beach offering water-sport facilities, is only 50 metres away. Studios, rooms and apartments at Mediterranean Blue come with a refrigerator.

Featuring a bath or shower, private bathrooms also come with free toiletries. Full English breakfast is served in the morning.

Mediterranean dishes, snacks and drinks can be enjoyed at the on-site restaurant, while many shops, cafes and bars can be found within 800 metres.

The main town and port of Corfu, as well as Ioannis Kapodistrias International Airport, are around 45 km away.

Free WiFi is available in the common areas and free private parking can be found nearby.
