Description

Rooms at Labranda Sandy Villas include a wardrobe and a TV. All rooms are fitted with a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower and free toiletries, while some rooms are equipped with a kitchenette.

A buffet breakfast is available daily at Labranda Sandy Villas. A gym and a sauna are available to guests, who can also enjoy a variety of courts and sports facilities.

Agios Georgios Village is just 650 feet away, while Labranda Sandy Villas is 24.2 miles from Corfu Town and 9.3 miles from Lefkimi. Corfu Airport is 23.6 miles away.
