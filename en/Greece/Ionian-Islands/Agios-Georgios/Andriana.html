Description

Situated amongst lush greenery, 100 metres from Agios Georgios Beach, Andriana offers air-conditioned units with a balcony overlooking the Ionian Sea, the mountains or the garden.

Guests are offered free bicycles. A kitchenette with cooking facilities, fridge and electric kettle is included in all units at the family-run Andriana.

Each comes with a seating area and TV. Guests can relax in the sun loungers in the garden with a book from the small library.

A children’s playground is provided for younger guests. Free Wi-Fi is provided in public areas.

Guests enjoy special rates when using jet skis, canoes and pedalos on the beach. Restaurants, supermarkets and cafés are within a 3-minute walk of the property.

The centre of Agios Georgios is 800 metres away. Corfu Town and the port are at 30 km.
