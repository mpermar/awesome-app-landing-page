Description

Featuring a seasonal outdoor swimming pool, Stars Hotel is set in Agios Georgios.

The property features a garden, as well as a terrace. The rooms are equipped with a terrace with views of the mountain.

At the hotel, rooms include a desk. All rooms have a private bathroom and a bath or shower, while each room also features a balcony.

All guest rooms have a wardrobe. Breakfast is available each morning, and includes continental and à la carte options.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the in-house restaurant, which specialises in steakhouse, Greek and Mediterranean cuisine.

Gluten-free options, vegetarian dishes and fresh fish are also available. A variety of popular activities are available in the area around Stars Hotel, including skiing, cycling and fishing.

The multilingual staff at the 24-hour front desk, staff are willing to help at any time of the day.

Corfu Town is 30 km from the accommodation, while Glyfada is 22 km from the property.
