Description

Within 250 metres from the sandy Argasi Beach, the 3-star Contessa Hotel has a large swimming pool, a poolside snack bar and a roof-garden restaurant with panoramic sea and city views.

Its air-conditioned rooms enjoy views of the pool or the mountains. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

Contessa rooms are bright and spacious, each with a private balcony. They all have a mini fridge, safety box and private bathroom with hairdryer.

The pool bar serves a variety of light meals, such as salads, club sandwiches and burgers.

A second bar offers ice creams, refreshing drinks and cocktails. The accommodation features a restaurant, set on the roof garden, overlooking the sea and city center.

Buffet breakfast can be enjoyed daily at the restaurant, which also prepares European and Greek traditional dishes.

BBQ theme nights and traditional Zakynthian nights are organized weekly by the pool.

The centre of Argasi is 100 metres away and a bus station and a taxi rank can be reached at 150 metres.
