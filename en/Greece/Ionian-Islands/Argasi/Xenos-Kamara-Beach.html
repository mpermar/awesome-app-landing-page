Description

Featuring a sea-facing sun terrace with swimming pool and pool bar, Xenos Kamara Beach offers self-catered accommodation right on Argassi beachfront.

A children's pool is also featured on site. Each with a sea, pool or garden view, the spacious apartments are fitted with Coco Mat mattresses and pillows and consist of two bedrooms and a kitchen and dining area.

A microwave oven, fridge, 2-ring cooker and toaster are included. A self-service launderette is provided.

Xenos Kamara serves a daily breakfast. Drinks and light snacks are available at the hotels all-day pool bar.

WiFi is free in all areas, and there is a games room with billiards and table tennis adjacent to the pool bar.

Guests can also relax with a drink at the air-conditioned lounge bar and watch TV.

A playroom and a children's playground are also provided. Xenos Kamara Beach Aparthotel is a 5-minute stroll from Argassis restaurants, shops, and bars.

Zakynthos Town and its port are 3 km away, while the island's airport is 7 km away.
