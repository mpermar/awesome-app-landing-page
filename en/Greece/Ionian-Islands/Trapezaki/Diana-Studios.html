Description

The newly built Diana Studios is a quiet small family self catering resort. Located in Trapezaki village, just 1.

5 km from the sandy beach. Offering a panoramic sea and mountain view, it features an outdoor pool.

Free WiFi is available throughout. All studios are air-conditioned, and have a fully equipped kitchen or kitchenette, satellite TV and balcony with views over the sea and surrounding countryside.

The Diana Studios provides the opportunity to have a continental breakfast, light meals and snacks in a romantic setting by the pool, enjoying the stunning combination of green nature and the deep blue sea.

At a distance of 200 metres, there are taverns and shops with local products like thyme honey.

The distance from Argostoli, the centre of Kefalonia, is only 12 km. Staff can arrange car rental service to visit the beaches and sights of Kefalonia.
