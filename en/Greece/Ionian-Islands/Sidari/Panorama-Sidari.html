Description

Panorama Sidari is located within a 10-minute walk from the beach of Sidari. It includes a free-form swimming pool, a restaurant and a poolside bar, while its rooms overlook the Ionian Sea.

Featuring a private balcony, the air-conditioned rooms at Panorama Sidari come with wooden furnishings.

Each is equipped with a satellite TV, mini fridge, electric kettle and safety box.

A hairdryer can be found in the en-suite bathroom. At the hotel’s restaurant, you can have lunch and dinner in buffet style including local and international flavours.

The poolside bar serves exotic cocktails and light meals until the evening. Guests can relax by the pool, while the more active ones can visit the hotel’s fitness room, the tennis court or the table-tennis table.

Within a short walk, you will find local shops, bars and restaurants. The town of Acharavi is 2 km away, while Corfu Airport is at 35 km.
