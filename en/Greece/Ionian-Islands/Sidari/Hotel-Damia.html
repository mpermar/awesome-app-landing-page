Description

Featuring a free-form, seasonal pool and restaurant, Hotel Damia is 1 km from a beach in the coastal village of Sidari.

It offers self-catered accommodation with free Wi-Fi and terrace or balcony overlooking the pool and the forest.

Canal d' Amour Beach is 800 metres away. A kitchenette with an electric kettle, fridge and coffee/tea facilities is included in all the air-conditioned studios.

The bathroom is stocked with free toiletries and a hairdryer. They offer either a garden, pool or mountain views.

A choice of a Continental or a Full English breakfast is offered every morning in the bright dining area.

Greek and International cuisine can be enjoyed in the covered terrace or by the pool for lunch or dinner.

The property's poolside snack bar serves a variety of refreshing drinks, exotic cocktails and light meals throughout the day.

Kids can enjoy their own paddling pool and playground. Free BBQ facilities are provided, while pool towels can be provided at extra charge.

Printing of electronic tickets and emails is available at the front desk free of charge.

A bus stop is located 100 metres from the property. Private parking can be found on site at no charge.
