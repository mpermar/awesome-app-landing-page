Description

Set among a large garden, Alessandro Apartments offer 20 apartments with modern facilities including a kitchenette and a balcony with garden and pool view.

The lounge area offers a TV with satellite channels and DVD. Barbeque facilities, a playground, as well as a quiet spot with sofas and tables, are available on the grounds.

The apartments are only one mile from the center of Sidari, offering bars and restaurants.
