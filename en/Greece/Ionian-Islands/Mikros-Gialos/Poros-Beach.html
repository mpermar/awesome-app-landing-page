Description

Just 50 metres from the white pebbled Mikros Gialos Beach in Lefkada, Poros Beach features a restaurant with Ionian Sea views and a pool with sun terrace.

It offers accommodation with free WiFi and a balcony overlooking the garden, the mountain or the sea.

The air-conditioned rooms of Poros have a fridge. Each simply decorated unit features a TV with satellite channels and a hairdryer.

A laptop and an iPad are also available upon request. Some types offer kitchenette.

Guests can taste Greek and Mediterranean dishes at the restaurant for lunch or dinner.

Coffees and drinks can also be enjoyed at the on-site snack bar throughout the day.

BBQ facilities are available in the garden for al fresco dining. Poros Beach is located 30 km from Lefkada Town and 13 km from the lively Nydri.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can arrange car rental to explore famous beaches, such as Porto Katsiki at 42 km and Vassiliki at 23 km.
