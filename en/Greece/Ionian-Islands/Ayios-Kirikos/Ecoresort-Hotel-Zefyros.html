Description

Surrounded by vineyards and olive groves in Agios Kirykos, Zefyros Hotel offers a large swimming pool, a restaurant, a pool bar and a beauty center.

All air-conditioned rooms at the Eco resort Zefyros are overlooking the gardens and pool and are equipped with free wifi, a satellite LCD TV and minibar.

Each includes a private bathroom with a hairdryer and amenities. Breakfast is served daily in the dining area with views of the garden.

The main restaurant offers rich dinner buffet with Mediterranean cuisine enriched with Greek dishes, based on local ingredients of high quality.

Vegetarian meals are offered upon request. The hotel guests are welcome to enjoy a variety of coffees, drinks, ice creams and delicious salads and snacks during the day at the Hotel's Lounge Bar, located in the poolside area.

Wellness options include massages, beauty treatments and a hammam. Other facilities include a gift shop, a football court and a children’s playground, a multipurpose hall for movies and games.

Staff can organize theme activity Programs for hiking, biking, horseback ridding, sailing cruises, visits to oil mills, cooking lessons & beekeeping seminars.

Zakynthos Town is 6 km away from the hotel and the nearest beach is less than a 10-minute drive.

Transfers to the airport and the beach can be arranged with an extra supplement. Free private parking is possible on site.
