Description

Just a 5-minute walk from the village square of Spartia and 1. 2 km from the beach, Residence Poseidon features a swimming pool with a pool bar and a tennis court.

Its self-catering units enjoy free Wi-Fi access. Poseidon studios and apartments are tastefully furnished opening out to private balconies with views to the Ionian Sea and Mount Aenos or to the swimming pool or the garden.

They include a kitchen or kitchenette, a flat-screen, satellite TV and DVD player.

Breakfast is served daily at the poolside bar, which also serves drinks and lights snacks throughout the day.

Guests can also have a BBQ at the facilities that can be found in the garden. Within 250 metres from the property, you can reach a mini market.

Argostoli, the capital and main port of Kefalonia, is 11 km away and the airport is at 6.
