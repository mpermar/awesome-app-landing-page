Description

Situated on a green hill at Tsilivi, only 600 metres from the local beach, Razis offers self-catering units just a 10-minute drive from Zakynthos town.

Razis Apartments have a pool and offer free private on-site parking. Hotel Razis air conditioned rooms feature wooden sloping ceilings and balconies with views of the pool or the garden.

In the kitchenette, guests can prepare a quick meal. All rooms also feature a TV and a hairdryer.

In the swimming pool area of Razis Apartments, there is a pool bar serving refreshing drinks, as well as hot and cold dishes.

Guests can use the sun beds and umbrellas free of charge, and there is also a smaller pool for the children.

Guests can dine at one of the taverns that can be found in Tsilivi. The village also features many bars and shops.
