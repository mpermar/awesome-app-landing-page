Description

Sentido Alexandra Beach Hotel is situated right on the sandy Tsilivi Beach in Zakynthos.

It features a freshwater pool and poolside bar, beach bar and a fitness centre. Free WiFi is provided.

All guest rooms are air-conditioned and equipped with telephone, refrigerator, hairdryer and en suite bathroom.

They all feature private balconies and satellite TV. A buffet breakfast is served at the property.

A seaside restaurant serving Mediterranean dishes and a bar are also available at Alexandra Beach Hotel.

A TV room and billiards are at guests' disposal. The hotel's beach bar is open from morning until late night and organizes parties with regional DJs during the summer.

Guests have direct access to Tsilivi Beach, where they can make use of sun lounges and try a ride with a canoe or a pedalo.

For those who want to explore the surroundings by themselves, the hotel offers the possibility to rent bicycles.

Sentido Alexandra Beach Hotel is a 15-minute drive away from Zakynthos Town where an abundance of restaurants, shops and museums is found.

The enarest clubs and tavernas are just 250 metres away. The property is less than 8 km away from Zakynthos Airport.
