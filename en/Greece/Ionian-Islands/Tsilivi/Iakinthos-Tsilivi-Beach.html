Description

Located by the famous Tsilivi Beach, Iakinthos features an outdoor swimming pool and a restaurant with sea-view terrace.

Free Wi-Fi is available at the hotel’s internet room. Air-conditioned rooms and suites at Iakinthos feature a mini fridge, TV and safe.

All units have private balconies with beautiful sea views. Water sports, umbrellas and sun loungers are available on the beach.

Your windsurfing equipment can be stored. Iakinthos' guests have access to the games and TV rooms.

A hearty breakfast is served each morning at the seafront terrace. The in-house restaurant serves Greek and international cuisine.

Refreshing drinks, ice creams and coffee are available at the pool bar. Iakinthos is 4 km from the Zakynthos Town and 10 km from the airport.

Staff are always at your disposal and services include taxi service and car rental.
