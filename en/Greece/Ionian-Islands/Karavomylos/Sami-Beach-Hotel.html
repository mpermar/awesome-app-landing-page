Description

Surrounded by green mountains, the seafront Sami Beach Hotel offers a swimming pool, a hot tub and poolside bar.

Sun beds and umbrellas are available for free. Standard rooms at the Sami Beach are air-conditioned and come with a bathroom with hairdryer and a private balcony offering views of the sea or the mountain.

A rich continental breakfast is served in the dining area between 07:30 and 10:30.

The spacious lounge area has a TV with satellite channels. Guests have free access to the library and the internet corner.
