Description

Surrounded by olive trees, near the village of Pentari, Archontiko Loukis offers a swimming pool with sun terrace and self-catering accommodation with a furnished balcony or patio.

Free Wi-Fi is provided throughout the property. Loukis studios and apartments are air conditioned, while most enjoy Ionian Sea views.

They include a well-equipped kitchen or kitchenette with dining area, and a living area with satellite TV and DVD/CD player.

Bathrobes, slippers and toiletries are offered. Guests can relax at the sun beds by the swimming pool or have a BBQ in the garden.

A smaller pool and a playground are available for the children. Within proximity, guests can reach the beach of Paramonas.

The main town of Corfu is at 18 km, while Corfu Airport is less than a 30-minute drive away.
