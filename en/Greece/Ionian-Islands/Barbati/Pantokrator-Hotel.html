Description

Set on a picturesque hillside overlooking the sea and 300 metres from the beach, this stylish hotel offers a fine blend of modern design with traditional Greek style on the island of Corfu.

All guests enjoy free Wi-Fi Access. Look out over the Ionian Sea from the Pantokrator's outdoor swimming pool, complete with its own snack bar serving refreshing drinks.

The pool is surrounded by sun loungers, allowing you to sunbathe in comfort. Each morning, head to the hotel restaurant for the delicious buffet breakfast.

A tasty selection is also served every evening, providing you with a convenient and great-value place to dine.

The balcony of each stylish guest room looks out over the sea and Corfu Town, 19 km away.

The Pantokrator's location in the charming village of Barbati makes it easy to explore the scenery of north-eastern Corfu.
