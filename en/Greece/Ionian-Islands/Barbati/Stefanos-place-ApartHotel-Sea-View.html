Description

With a panoramic view of the sea from each balcony, accommodation at family-run Stefanos Place is self-catered.

This waterfront property is 1 km from lively Ipsos of Northern Corfu and offers a sea-facing swimming pool with a hydromassage area.

Free WiFi is provided throughout. All apartments and studios come with a bathroom, TV and free WiFi Internet access.

A fridge and a kitchenette with hobs and cooking essentials are included. Stefanos Place is 10-minute walk from Barbati Beach and the scenic Prinias Beach.

For those who prefer to stay on site, free sun beds and umbrellas are provided by the pool.

The all-day restaurant is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner, prepared with fresh, local ingredients, with a focus on traditional Corfiot and Greek cuisine.

Drinks, cocktails, coffee and snacks can be enjoyed on the pool bar. Physiotherapy and massages can be arranged upon request and extra charges apply.

A bus stop is set right in front of the property. The hotel is 17 km from Corfu Town and the airport.

Water sports, Greek taverns as well as a variety of international restaurants are available within 2 km of the hotel.

Parking at the hotel is free. Transfer from/to the airport can be provided upon request and extra charge.
