Description

Located 900 metres from Gaios centre, Paxos Club Resort & SPA has a large pool with hot tub and a restaurant with a late 1800s dining room.

Its units open out to a furnished veranda overlooking the lush gardens. The air-conditioned units are tastefully furnished and enjoy free Wi-Fi.

They have a satellite LCD TV and a desk, while some also have a fully equipped kitchenette,.

Bathrobes, slippers and cosmetics can be found in the modern bathroom. Guests can have breakfast in the indoor dining area or by pool, while Greek and international cuisine is served for lunch and dinner.

Cocktails and local wine can be enjoyed at the shaded poolside bar. A fitness room, a mini gallery and a playground are available on site.

The resort’s staff offers free 2-way transfer to the port, as well as free daily transfer to the pebbled Gaios Beach and Kaki Lagkada Beach.

Gaios Port is 1 km away, while the beach of Monodendri can be reached at 3 km. Within walking distance, guests will also find taverns, shops and cafes.
