Description

Located among lush greenery in Bochali Hill and just 1. 2 km from Zakynthos Town Centre, Hotel Varres offers a swimming pool with a sun terrace and panoramic views.

Free WiFi is available in all areas. Opening to a balcony, all rooms and suites offer views of the pool or the surrounding area, and include air conditioning and a flat-screen, satellite TV.

They also have a safety box and refrigerator, while a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries comes standard.

Hotel Varres features an elegant dining area, where guests can enjoy buffet breakfast, as well as Greek and international dishes for lunch.

A poolside snack bar, restaurant and playground can be found in the complex. Zakynthos Airport is 4 km from Varres, while the town of Zakynthos is a 15-minute walk away.
