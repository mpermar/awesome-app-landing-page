Description

The guest rooms are air conditioned and have a private balcony or a terace. Each room includes a refrigerator, telephone, and has private bathroom.

Hotel Michaelia features a carefully designed rock garden and a spacious terrace. A lounge with TV corner is also available.

Guests of Michaelia can enjoy a meal at the hotel’s restaurant, which overlooks the sea, and have a drink at the bar.

Michaelia Hotel is only 500 feet from the town center, and 1,000 feet from the local beach. The town of Mytilene and Mytilene Airport are 37 miles from the hotel. Free parking is available nearby.
