Description

Inspired by the traditional Cretan Village architecture, with the Libyan Sea lying on its feet, the 5-star Ostria Resort & Spa is set in Lassithi, 4,5 km east of Ierapetra town.

The resort offers stunning ocean and sunset views, elegant accommodation, a bespoke range of dining options, exquisite Spa treatments and an unparalleled array of entertainment and recreation activities.

It features 6 restaurants, 4 bars, a luxurious Gala venue, a long sandy and pebbled beach with crystal clear waters and 7 swimming pools, one of which is solely for younger guests.

Other facilities include watersports, diving centre, tennis courts and beach volley.

Children may entertain themselves at the playground. Designed in soft lines with earthy tones and stylish décor, all rooms form relaxed spaces of unparalleled comfort and elegance.

Dining options range from traditional Cretan “Mezedes” to gourmet plates singed from awarded Chefs, promising an incredible journey of flavours through the bridges of cultural diversities.

Lassithi region offers numerous pristine beaches, freshwater springs, dense forests, historical and archaeological sites, beautiful villages, and traditional hamlets.

The small town of Ierapetra provides a vast choice of dining and entertainment options while the best of Crete’s treasure, Chrissi Island is right on your doorstep.
