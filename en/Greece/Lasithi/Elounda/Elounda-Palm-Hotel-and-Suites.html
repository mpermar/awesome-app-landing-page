Description

Located near the sea, the family-run Elounda Palm Hotel & Suites is located in the area of Elounda.

A restaurant and a lounge bar, as well as a large outdoor pool with poolside bar are featured.

Rooms open to balconies that face the Mediterranean gardens or the deep blue sea of the Elounda gulf.

Air conditioning and satellite TVs are standard, while rooms with sharing and private pools are available.

A rich and varied buffet breakfast is served daily. Lunch and dinner menus focus on Greek and Mediterranean dishes.

The hotel is only 1 km from the centre of Elounda, and 7 km from lively Agios Nikolaos.

Heraklion Airport is 68 km away. Free parking is available on site. Other amenities include a gift shop, free WiFi and air conditioning in public areas.
