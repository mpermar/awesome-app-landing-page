Description

Only 300 metres from Sitia centre, this 5-star hotel offers 3 pools, a tennis court and a spa, right on a private beach area of Sitia Bay.

Its rooms enjoy sea, pool or garden views and it offers a poolside snack bar for drinks and light meals.

The air-conditioned Sitia Beach rooms and suites have a balcony and a seating area.

Facilities include a smart TV and mini fridge. Bathrobes, slippers and toiletries are also provided.

Guests of Sitia Beach hotel can enjoy show cooking at the buffet-style restaurant.

There is also an indoor bar boasting panoramic sea views. The resort’s spa includes a sauna, a hammam and a hot tub.

The more active guests can visit the gym, while free towels are offered to those who want to relax on the beach.

Sitia Airport is less than 2 km away. Transportation links to Heraklion and Agios Nikolaos are available within 300 metres.
