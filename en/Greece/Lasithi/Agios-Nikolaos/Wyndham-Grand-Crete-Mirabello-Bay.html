Description

Wyndham Grand Crete Mirabello Bay enjoys a tranquil beachfront location in Agios Nikolaos.

This 5-star hotel features an outdoor pool, full-service spa and 9 restaurants. All guest rooms, bungalows and maisonettes at Wyndham Grand Crete Mirabello Bay are thoughtfully appointed with a private balcony, bright dcor, and modern amenities.

Some maisonettes offer access to a shared pool. Free WiFi is available throughout.

A buffet or American breakfast is available every morning at the property. Guests may enjoy Greek, Mediterranean and Italian cuisine at the on-site restaurants.

For the more energetic ones, the property features a well-equipped fitness centre,a tennis court, a volleyball court and a wide range of water sport activities.

Speaking German, Greek, English and French at the 24-hour front desk, staff are willing to help at any time of the day.

Meeting and banquet facilities are also available. Heraklion International Airport is 65 km from the property.
