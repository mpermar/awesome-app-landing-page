Description

Boasting a waterfront location, Candia Park Village features 4-star accommodation overlooking the Mirabello Bay.

It has 3 pools, 2 private beaches with sun beds and umbrellas, and a children’s club.

The air-conditioned rooms of Candia Park are spacious and decorated with a traditional touch.

They all include a well-equipped kitchenette with dining area, and have a satellite TV and hairdryer.

Their furnished balcony enjoys sea or garden views. Guests can relax by the 3 pools, set on 2 levels, including a children’s pool and a hot tub.

The more active guests can engage in a variety of sports, such as tennis, basketball, diving or windsurfing.

A range of rejuvenating massage treatments can be also enjoyed. Agapi restaurant serves local and international dishes by the pool, while the outdoor Fili tavern offers Cretan cuisine right by the sea.

Candia also includes 2 bars and a traditional Greek coffee house. The hotel is a 5-minute drive from the neighbouring towns of Agios Nikolaos and Elounda.
