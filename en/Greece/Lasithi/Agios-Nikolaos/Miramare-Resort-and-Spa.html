Description

All accommodations at the Miramare Resort have a stunning view of the town of Aghios Nikolaos and the Mirabello Bay. Decorated in traditional Cretan style, each unit is air conditioned with a large living space and satellite TVs.

A buffet breakfast is served daily in Miramares main restaurant. Lunch and dinner can be enjoyed on the large terrace with views of the bay. There is also a poolside bar and a lounge bar so guests can sip a refreshing drink poolside or indoors.

A variety of water sports are available on the public beach. You can find massage/treatment rooms and a gym on site. Other leisure facilities include an arcade/game room and billiards.

Agios Nikolaos cosmopolitan town center with plenty of shopping opportunities and nightlife is a 15-minute walk from the Miramare Resort. Free parking is available on site. Heraklion International Airport is 40 mi away.
