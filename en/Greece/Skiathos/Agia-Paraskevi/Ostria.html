Description

The Ostria studios are bright, air-conditioned and tastefully furnished. Prepare meals in your well-equipped kitchenette and relax with a drink after dinner on your spacious balcony.

Ostrias beautiful pool area is an ideal place to soak up the sun. The pool bar serves drinks and light snacks throughout the day.

The restaurant overlooks the pool area and the grounds. You can start your day there with delicious breakfast and enjoy homemade Greek dishes later on.

When you need a break from the heat, find a cool spot in the large grounds, enjoy a game of pool or watch a sports game on the widescreen TV.

Ostria is situated close to the road that goes between Skiathos and Koukounaries Beach. That means you can easily explore the island. The friendly staff can arrange car rental and tours to neighboring islands or mainland Greece.
