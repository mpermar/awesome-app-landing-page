Description

Combining pastels with bright hues as well as floral and striped decorative details, all accommodation types at the Mandraki Village include a satellite TV and air conditioning. Each has a fridge and hairdryer.

Breakfast is served at the dining area and includes a variety of aromatic Nespresso coffees. Elia’s Restaurant serves Mediterranean cuisine and selected wines. White Bar serves refreshing cocktails and offers views of the pool.

Mandraki Village Boutique Hotel is 8.5 miles from Skiathos Airport. Guests can get help at the 24-hour front desk and free private parking on site.
