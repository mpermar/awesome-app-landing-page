Description

Located in the area of Charaki within 150 metres from the beach, Alia Hotel & Suites consist of 3 buildings and offer self-catering accommodation with free Wi-Fi access.

They have a swimming pool, 2 hot tubs, a sauna, a fitness centre, 2 restaurants and a poolside bar.

Alia Hotel & Suites are designed in modern Caribbean rhythm. They are equipped with a 43 flat-screen, satellite TV and air conditioning.

Their furnished balcony enjoys views to the Aegean Sea, the pool and the palm trees.

Some suites come with a private swimming pool. Free WiFi is provided. The swimming pool area offers free sun beds and umbrellas.

The poolside bar features a shaded seating area. Breakfast, lunch and dinner can be enjoyed at the 2 restaurants.

The multilingual staff at the Alia Hotel & Suites are available 24/7. The Medieval town of Rhodes is 35 km away from the studios.
