Description

Situated just 350 metres from the beach of Kolymbia in Rhodes, Marathon Hotel offers a swimming pool and poolside bar.

It features air-conditioned rooms with a furnished balcony overlooking the garden, the pool or the surrounding streets.

A satellite TV and fridge are included in all rooms at the Marathon. Each comes with a private bathroom with bathtub.

Light snacks and coffee are served throughout the day in the pool bar area. Drinks are also provided in the main bar.

Guests can relax on the sun terrace that features sun loungers or play billiards in the games room.

A special shallow pool is provided for children as well as a playground. Free WiFi access is available in all areas.

There are many shops and traditional taverns surrounding the hotel. Staff at the 24-hour front desk can arrange car rental to explore Rhodes and Lindos less than 25 km away.
