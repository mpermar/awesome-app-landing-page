Description

Featuring a swimming pool with hot tub, children’s pool and a variety of restaurants and bars, leisure activities and evening entertainment, Lydia Maris Hotel is an ideal choice for family fun and relaxation.

Wi-Fi is free in all hotel areas. There is a range of accommodation available at the hotel, each with a private balcony or terrace.

All rooms are air conditioned and include a satellite TV, fridge with free water, hairdryer and safe.

American buffet breakfast is served each morning. Dining options are many and range from traditional Greek, Mediterranean and international buffet.

The pool restaurant serves a buffet lunch as well as hot and cold snacks throughout the day.

A separate garden restaurant with kids menu served in buffet style is available and weekly BBQ night with live music are organized.

A pizza restaurant and grill restaurant can also be found on site. The hotel’s animation team provides entertainment, including cabarets, shows, competitions and games.

There is a full range of activities for children, including their own club, playground and menu.

Babysitting is also available on request. The resort is close to Afandou Beach and less than 5 km from Agathi Beach and Tsampika Beach.

Rhodes Town is just 25 km away. If you feel like a round of golf, Afandou Golf Course is just 7 km away from Lydia Maris.
