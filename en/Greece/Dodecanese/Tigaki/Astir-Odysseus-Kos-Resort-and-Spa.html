Description

Located directly on the waterfront, the 5-star Astir Odysseus Kos Resort and Spa offers fine dining, 5 swimming pools, a spa with indoor pool and various sport facilities.

It is just 5 km from Tigaki village and the wonderful beach. Astir Odysseus rooms, maisonettes and suites are very spacious and each is equipped with LCD satellite TV and free WiFi access.

Amenities also include coffee & tea making facilities and mini safe. Some have a shared or a private pool.

The resort also offers a wide range of sports such as climbing wall, gym, beach volley, bike rentals, water polo, 5x5 mini soccer and tennis courts.

A kids club with various activities and two playgrounds are provided for children.

Achilleas Restaurant offers dining in buffet style. Barbecue buffet for lunch and la carte menu for dinner is served at Taverna Argo.

Moreover, international specialties are served for dinner at the sea front la carte restaurant Ithaka.

Gluten-free dishes are also available. Beverages, coffees and cocktails are offered at the pool bar Dawn and at the stylish Sirens Bar.

Astir Odysseus Kos Resort and Spa is 5 km from the main town of Kos and 20 km from the airport.

Within 13 km, you can also visit the mountain village of Zia and enjoy the panoramic views.
