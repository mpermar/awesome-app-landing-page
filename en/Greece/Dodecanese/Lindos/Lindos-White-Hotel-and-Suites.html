Description

Located 200 metres away from the beach, in Vlycha Bay, the 4-star Lindos White includes 2 swimming pools, 2 pool bars and a restaurant.

It offers minimally decorated rooms and has a tennis court. Lindos White rooms are decorated in light colours and have a balcony with garden views.

They are equipped with air conditioning, a satellite TV and mini fridge. The private bathroom offers a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Modernly decorated in green and white tones, the hotel’s restaurant serves Greek and international cuisine in buffet style.

The 2 pool bars serve light snacks, ice cream, coffees and alcoholic drinks until late at night.

Within 100 metres, guests will find a bus stop connecting to Lindos Town at 3. 5 km.

The old town of Rhodes is 48 km away, while Diagoras International Airport is at 50 km.
