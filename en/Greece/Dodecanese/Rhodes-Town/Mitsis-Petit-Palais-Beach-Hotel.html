Description

Mitsis Petit Palais Beach Hotel is located in the city of Rhodes, just 150 metres from the pebble beach.

The 4-star hotel features a freshwater pool, hot tub, buffet restaurant and all-day bar.

A total of 215 rooms are available, each equipped with a mini fridge, satellite TV with music channel, individual air conditioning and a balcony or terrace.

Pool towels can be provided upon deposit. A welcome drink is offered to guests for free.

Umbrellas and sun beds are available free of charge by the pools. A variety of water sports activities are available at an extra charge on the beach.

The main restaurant serves an American buffet breakfast, lunch buffet with show cooking and a rich buffet dinner with grill show cooking and pizza baked in a wood oven.

Light snacks and sandwiches are served between meal times at the bar. Walking distance from Rhodes’ sights, Mitsis Petit Palais Beach Hotel is only 150 metres from the Casino, 500 metres from Mandraki Port and 700 metres from Rhodes Old Town.
