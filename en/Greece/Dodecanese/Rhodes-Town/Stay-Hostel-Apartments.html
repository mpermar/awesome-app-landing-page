Description

Stay Hostel Apartments is quietly located in Rhodes Town and offers self-catering accommodation.

Free WiFi access is available. The property is 300 metres from Old Town Gates and 500 metres from Grand Master's Palace.

Modernly decorated in earthy tones, guests can choose among a variety of room types.

Extras include air conditioning, bed linen and towels. At Stay Hostel Apartments you will find a 24-hour front desk, a stylish bar and a terrace.

A games room, gym and home cinema are provided for your entertainment. Guests have also access to a 55-m2 shared kitchen and a lounge for breakfast, snacks, drinks and organised dinners.

If you feel like visiting the surroundings, check out Collachium and Clock Tower at 500 metres.

Rhodes International Airport is located 13 km from the accommodation. The property offers free parking.
