Description

Featuring a restaurant that expands around a medieval windmill, Bellevue Suites are on the beach of Rhodes City.

They offer elegant accommodation with hydromassage shower. Facilities include 2 pools and a snack bar.

Offering Aegean Sea views, all air-conditioned units at Bellevue have free WiFi. Each comes with a seating area with flat-screen TV.

Some accommodation types feature a kitchenette with Nespresso coffee machine. A bottle of wine and a fruit basket are offered.

Guests may start their day with a breakfast served at the on-site restaurant or enjoy dishes created by an awarded chef.

Refreshing cocktails and light meals are prepared at the snack bar. Several restaurants and shops are available within short walking distance.

The Medieval City of Rhodes is approximately 2 km away. Rhodes International Airport is at a distance of 14 km.
