Description

Rodos Niohori Elite Suites Boutique Hotel is set in a traditional house, a 5-minute walk from the Rhodes Casino.

It offers luxurious suites with 2 satellite LCD TVs, and a pebbled breakfast courtyard.

Each Niohori two-storey suite is fitted with Italian furnishings and a touch of Italian dcor.

They all have a private terrace with sun loungers and an umbrella. A dining table for 4, a seating room and 2 bathrooms are featured.

Rodos Niohori Elite Suites serves a homemade breakfast, including fresh fruit, at the communal courtyard.

Free WiFi access is offered throughout. Rhodes nearest sandy beach is 300 metres from the suites.

The lodging is easily reachable from Diagoras International Airport, 14 km away. The medieval town of Rhodes is 1 km away, while bars, clubs and restaurants are within 150 metres away.
