Description

Set on the outskirts of Faliraki and Kalithies, Venezia Resort Hotel features brand new Water park and swimming pools with pool bars, restaurants with local delicacies.

Guests have free access to the sun terrace, offering loungers and parasols. Complimentary transfer from and to the beach is provided 3 times per day.

Surrounded by pleasant gardens, all rooms at Venezia Resort are air-conditioned. Each opens out to a balcony or a patio and most of them offer views over the garden and the pool.

A fridge, LCD TV and a kettle are available. Some rooms also offer facilities for guests with mobility issues.

Children are provided with a separate pool area and water park with water slides, as well as a playground.

Cots are available free of charge. A rich buffet breakfast is served each morning.

A variety of drinks and snacks are available throughout the day at the pool bar. The on-site mini market sells newspapers and magazines.

The centre of Faliraki is just 1. 5 km from the Venezia Resort, and property offers a free 2-way shuttle to the beach 3 times per day.

The golf course, the beaches and the Faliraki water park are easily reached by the local bus, stopping just outside the hotel.

Guests wishing to further explore the island can benefit from the car/motorbike rentals provided at discounted rates at the reception.
