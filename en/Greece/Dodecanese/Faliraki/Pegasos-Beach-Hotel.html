Description

Located by Faliraki beach, Pegasos Beach Hotel features a private beach area and fresh-water swimming pool.

Free sun beds, umbrellas, pool and beach towels are offered. Free Wi-Fi internet is available in all areas.

The bright and airy rooms are air conditioned, each with a furnished balcony with area, mountain or sea views.

All units are spacious and include a seating area. Facilities include a satellite TV, radio and fridge.

The main restaurant serves Greek and international dishes in buffet style. A tavern-snack bar serving grills and beverages, main bar and pool bar are also featured on site.

Children’s menus and highchairs are available. Pegasos Beach guests can enjoy a game of tennis, basketball or beach volleyball, or enjoy the water sports facilities.

The animation team organizes live music nights and daily shows. A children’s pool with waterslides, playground and mini club are also featured.

Pegasos Beach Hotel is only 2 km from Faliraki centre, its restaurants, shops and nightlife.

It is 12 km from the airport and 13 km from Rhodes Town and port. There is free private parking on site.
