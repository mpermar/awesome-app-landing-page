Description

Set within a 120,000-m² area, La Marquise Luxury Resort Complex is just 9 km from the Medieval Town and the Rhodes main port.

It boasts elegant and spacious accommodation, fine dining, a wellness spa, 2 floodlit tennis courts and a floodlit basketball court.

La Marquise’s impressive pool complex consists of 6 fresh-water outdoor pools, and an indoor pool, while there are 25 private pools.

All guests enjoy free sun beds, umbrellas and pool towels. Each of the luxurious La Marquise rooms incorporates the latest modern technology, including 37-inch flat-screen TVs and electronic safes.

La Marquise hotel also allocates 10 rooms for clients with reduced mobility, close to all hotel services.

Wheelchair accessibility is also excellent all around the hotel and pool areas. Decorated with thematic paintings, waters and waterfalls, Da Vinci buffet restaurant serves Greek, Mediterranean and international specialities.

Special diet menus are available upon request. For those who prefer to spend the day in their room, La Marquise hotel offers an extensive menu of excellent dishes, snacks and drinks, via its 24-hour room service.
