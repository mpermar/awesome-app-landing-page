Description

The all-inclusive Mitsis Faliraki Beach Hotel & Spa is a 5-star hotel located just 10 metres from Faliraki Beach.

It offers outdoor swimming pools, spa facilities and an animation team organizing events and parties.

Guests have free access to the tennis court. Free WiFi is available in public areas.

The air-conditioned rooms are located in the two main buildings. Each has a minibar, satellite TV, and bathroom with hairdryer.

WiFi is available upon charge. Activities on site include billiards, aerobics classes, table tennis, beach soccer, and more.

Bicycles, horses and ponies are available for riding. A variety of water sports activities are available on the hotel beach.

A multilingual animation team provides an exciting entertainment program throughout the day and night.

Children will also enjoy the playground and indoor playroom. The main restaurant serves an American buffet breakfast and late continental buffet breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Other dining options include Greek, Italian, Asian, Mexican and Sushi. Drinks, cocktails, and light snacks are served at the 3 available bars.
