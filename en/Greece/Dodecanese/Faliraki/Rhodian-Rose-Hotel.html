Description

Surrounded by a beautiful rose garden, the complex is 400 metres from the beach and a 5-minute walk from Faliraki centre.

A large swimming pool, children’s pool and a hot tub are featured on site. Rooms at Rhodian Rose Hotel are spacious and tastefully decorated.

They feature a fridge, a satellite TV and a hairdryer. All units enjoy pool or garden views.

Guests may enjoy dinner and a buffet or a la carte breakfast upon charge, whilst watching the pool views.

Refreshing beverages, drinks and light snacks are served at the snack bar. A restaurant lies in the pool area.

Facilities include a sun terrace with sun loungers, available at the pool area. Faliraki Beach offers a variety of water sports, restaurants and sun beds for hire.

Safes are available through reception. Guests can watch satellite TV at the lobby or benefit from the free public WiFi access.
