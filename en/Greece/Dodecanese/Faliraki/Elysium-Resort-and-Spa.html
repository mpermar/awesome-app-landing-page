Description

Enjoying a beachfront location in Kallithea, the 5-star Elysium Resort & Spa boasts a lagoon-style pool, a hydromassage pool and an 800-m spa centre.

It offers fine dining, 3 bars and elegant accommodation. The resort's premises also include 2 escape rooms.

The stylish rooms of Elysium Resort & Spa are spacious, and each offers a balcony with sea views.

In-room facilities include a 26 LCD satellite TV, mini fridge and safe. Some units also offer a spa bath.

Rooms and suites located on the Elite floor have access at the Elite Club lounge offering complimentary light snacks and beverages.

The Serenity Spa offers a wide range of massages, facial and body treatments, and hydrotherapy.

Facilities include a fitness centre, hammam, sauna and vitality pool with 16 massage jets, as well as outdoor hot tub.

All are available upon extra charge. The property's Serenity Spa features the exclusive ESPA products and treatments.

A rich American breakfast buffet is served at the terrace of the main restaurant.

The a la carte poolside restaurant serves local and Mediterranean delicacies. The Emerald restaurant serves international cuisine.

Modern Rhodian cuisine is proposed at the rooftop gourmet restaurant Noble, awarded with a Toque d'Or 2018 and 2019 and whose chef has also been awarded the prestigious "Young Chef of the Year" for 2016.

Rhodes town is 8 km away from the resort. The popular seaside town of Faliraki, famous for its nightlife options is at 4 km.
