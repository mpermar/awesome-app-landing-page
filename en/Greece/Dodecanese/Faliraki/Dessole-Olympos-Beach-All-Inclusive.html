Description

All the guest rooms open out onto a simply furnished balcony. Each spacious unit has a satellite TV, minibar and a private bathroom.

Olympos Beach's main restaurant offers a buffet breakfast and dinner while the poolside restaurant serves lunch. Guests can sip on refreshing drinks in the lobby bar or at the beach bar.

Free lounge chairs and sun umbrellas surround the pool area. Kids can enjoy their own separate pool and playground. Sports and recreational activities include billiards, ping-pong, tennis courts and beach volley ball. Wi-Fi is available upon request for an additional charge.

Olympos Beach Hotel is just one mile from the center of the charming town of Faliraki. Rhodes Town is 8 miles. Free private parking can be found on site.
