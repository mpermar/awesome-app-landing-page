Description

Located right on Ialyssos Beach, the all-inclusive D’Andrea Mare Beach Hotel features free-form pools with waterslides and a lazy river.

The complex includes a main restaurant, a bar, a poolside bar and a snack bar, as well as wellness centre with steam bath.

Free Wi-Fi is available throughout. Opening to a balcony, all modernly decorated units offer air conditioning, satellite TV and fridge.

The private bathroom is fitted with a bathtub, free toiletries and hairdryer. Some accommodation types at D’Andrea Mare overlook the Aegean Sea.

Enjoying pool views, the restaurant prepares Greek and international dishes in buffet style.

Cold and hot snacks, including ice cream are offered at the poolside snack bar. In the evening, the main bar serves locally produced drinks and cocktails.

At the wellness centre, guests can find an indoor pool, sauna, hot tub and fully equipped gym.

A mini market, billiards and beach volley court is provided within the premises. For younger visitors, D’Andrea Mare offers a children’s pool, playground and animation team.

The 24-hour staff at D’Andrea Mare Beach Hotel can arrange car rental to explore the island.

The complex is situated 5. 5 km from Diagoras International Airport and 9. 5 km from Rhodes Town.
