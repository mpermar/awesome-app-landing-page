Description

Ialyssos combines modern and ancient Rhodes and has an enviable sun record with up to 300 days a year. It is an ideal accommodation for those seeking a relaxing vacation by the beach, as well a for those seeking to explore the world famous island of Rhodes.

Play a game of tennis or rejuvenate with a massage, followed by a dip in the pool. When ready to go out, use the car rental service for longer excursions. Return to tasty food at the restaurant and a favorite drink at the bar.
