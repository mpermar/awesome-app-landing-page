Description

Just 150 metres from Kefalos Beach, Villa Popi Studios offers a swimming pool and a poolside snack bar.

It features studios with free Wi-Fi and a balcony overlooking the mountains of Kos, or the Aegean Sea.

A kitchenette with cooking facilities, fridge, oven, electric kettle and toaster is included in all units at the Villa Popi.

Each has a private bathroom with hairdryer. Sun loungers and umbrellas are available on the sun terrace.

A childrens pool is provided for younger guests. For guests who wish to have breakfast there is a choice of continental or English upon request.

Drinks, pizzas, salads and other homemade flavours are served at the snack bar. Restaurants and shops are within a 4-minute walk.

Kos International Airport is at 15 km. Kos Town is 45 km away. Staff can arrange car rental and free private parking is possible on site.
