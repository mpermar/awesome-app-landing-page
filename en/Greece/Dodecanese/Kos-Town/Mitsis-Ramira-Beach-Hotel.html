Description

Mitsis Ramira Beach Hotel is located along its private stretch of sandy beach on Psalidi, only 3.

5 km from Kos Town. Free sun beds and umbrellas are provided on the beach and next to its 3 pools.

One of the pools features integrated hydromassage jets. The contemporary rooms are air conditioned, with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, mini fridge and balcony or terrace with outdoor furniture.

Guests enjoy free use of the well-stocked minibar. American buffet breakfast, lunch and dinner are served at the main restaurant.

Special theme nights include Greek, Asian and Mexican. A Chinese restaurant, Italian restaurant and 5 bars are also available.

With a water sports centre, the Ramira also features a mini soccer court, multipurpose court and tennis court.

Young guests will enjoy the children’s pool, playground or games room. The animation team organizes games, shows and competitions.

Wi-Fi is free throughout. Mitsis Ramira Beach Hotel is 27 km from Kos Airport and there is a bus stop in front of the hotel.
