Description

Eden Roc Resort Hotel is a waterfront resort on Kallithea Beach and surrounded by lush green gardens.

Its numerous facilities include many swimming pools, water slides, a tennis court and a private pebbled beach.

Eden Roc offers a variety of rooms, all with private balcony with views. Amenities include satellite TV, mini fridge and hairdryer.

Air conditioning is provided during the summer months. The Olympic-size pool is surrounded by deck chairs, while the children’s pool is located next to the waterfall.

There is a poolside bar next to each pool. Bungalows share their own pool. Guests can taste international cuisine at the sea-view a la carte restaurant, or Greek dishes at the beachfront Taverna Dikti.

Guests are required to follow a dress code applied to dinner. Drinks are served at the elegant champagne bar, and the various pool bars.

The hotel features a basketball court, beach volleyball, mini football and mini golf.

Eden Roc guests also enjoy a gym area with jogging path, pool with aqua sports and yoga garden.

The 4-star resort enjoys a prime location between Rhodes Town and Kallithea. It is 18 km from the airport and 4 km from the centre of Rhodes Town and the main port.
