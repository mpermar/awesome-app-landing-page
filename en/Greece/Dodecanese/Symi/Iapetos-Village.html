Description

Opening to a balcony or patio with pool and garden views, each air-conditioned unit features an LCD TV with satellite channels. The private bathroom is equipped with a hydro-massage shower cabin, bathrobes and slippers. Some accommodation types are kitchenette equipped, while others are divided in 2 levels.

Iapetos Village offers round trip shuttle service from Symi Port, at 650 feet. The organized beach of Nos is around one mile away. Free, private parking is available on site.
