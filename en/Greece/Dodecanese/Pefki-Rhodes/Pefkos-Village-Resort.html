Description

Situated 5 km south of Lindos on the sandy and sheltered Pefkos beach, the Pefkos Village Resort offers pool tables, and a 24-hour front desk.

Each unit at the Pefkos Village Resort is surrounded by beautiful greenery and offers balcony or terrace with views of the garden or the pool.

Kitchenette facilities are included. Air conditioning is provided free of charge, while a safety box is provided at extra charge.

General facilities include a pool with separate children’s area and a garden with playground.

Sun beds are complimentary. A hot buffet breakfast is served each morning at the People Snack Bar, and you can choose between full English and an enhanced Continental breakfast with fresh pastries, fresh orange juice, pancakes, and a variety of fruit and cereal.

The on-site traditional Greek restaurant serves local dishes, and drinks are served at the snack bar.
