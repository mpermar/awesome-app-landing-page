Description

Dionysos Hotel is set in a blooming tropical garden, amongst pine and olive trees, with bars and restaurants surrounding its water lake pool.

Loungers and umbrellas are complimentary. Ixia Beach is 200 metres from the hotel.

Accommodation at Hotel Dionysos is spacious and air-conditioned and includes en suite bathroom with bathtub and hairdryer.

Each room offers a balcony or terrace with view of the sea or the gardens and pool.

The enriched American breakfast buffet includes on-spot cooking stations. Mediterranean and Greek dishes are served at the main and poolside restaurant.

There is also a Souvlaki - Kebab corner, a Creperie and a pastry shop. Children are entertained at the children’s pool, the playground and the mini club.

Greek evenings with live music and shows are frequently hosted. Free WiFi is provided in all areas.

Rhodes Town is 3. 6 km from Dionysos Hotel. The nearest airport is Rhodes International Airport, 10 km from the property.
