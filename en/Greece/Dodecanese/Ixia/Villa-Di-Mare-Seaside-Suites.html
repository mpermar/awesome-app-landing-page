Description

The historic, 5-star Villa Di Mare Seaside Suites is right next to Ixia Beach and only 5 km from the Old Town of Rhodes.

It features a swimming pool with a pool bar and all-day music, and a gourmet restaurant.

Villa Di Mare Seaside Suites feature free WiFi and self catering facilities. Bathrooms include a spa bath.

Rooms have verandas or balconies with views to the sea or the mountains. Some units have private swimming pools.

Guests can relax at the caf with panoramic views of Ixia Bay. The restaurant serves creative Mediterranean cuisine dishes.

The pool bar hosts live DJ nights and serves finger food and signature cocktails.

Guests have access to an on-site mini gym, while they can also enjoy massage and beauty treatments on request.

Surfing lessons, horseback riding and water sports can be arranged on request. Staff at the 24-hour front desk can offer information on the famous Valley of the Butterflies 20 km away or other local interests.

Guests are within a 3-minute walk from shops and Diagoras Airport is at 12 km. Free private parking is available on site.
