Description

All the air-conditioned rooms at Resort Sheraton Rhodes feature a furnished balcony with views of the Aegean Sea or the mountains and gardens. Each unit includes a satellite LCD TV and a fridge. The marble bathrooms are stocked with bathrobes and luxury amenities.

A buffet breakfast and dinner are served in the relaxing setting of Castellania Restaurant. Mediterraneo Restaurant serves Greek cuisine close to the pool, and there is also an Italian-inspired restaurant overlooking the Aegean Sea. Guests can relax at the poolside bar and enjoy cocktails and refreshing soft drinks.

Sports facilities include racquetball, tennis and basketball courts. The childrenâ€™s club features organized activities with an entertainment team.

Rhodes Town, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, provides many entertainment options. Rhodes Diagoras International Airport is 9 miles away. Free private parking is available on site.
