Description

All rooms at the Michelangelo Resort & Spa are styled in smoothing, modern colors. Most include a seating area. Cable TV, mini-bar and air conditioning are standard.

The spa features an indoor heated pool with hot tub, sauna and a steam room. You can also indulge yourself with one of the great massage treatments on offer. Guests can enjoy the sunset from one of the hotel's 3 outdoor swimming pools.

A varied breakfast buffet is served at the elegant restaurant, overlooking the sea and the pool. A la carte dishes are available for lunch and dinner. There is also a pool bar, a lobby bar and a beach bar.

Agios Fokas with its shingle beach and small number of taverns is within easy walking distance, and Kos Town is 5 mi away reached easily by a 10-minute bus ride.
