Description

Just 300 metres from the beach of Theologos in Rhodes, Filoxenia Apartments & Studios features 2 swimming pools and a Greek restaurant.

It features self-catered accommodation with a balcony overlooking the gardens, the sea and the pools.

A kitchenette with cooking facilities and fridge are included in all accommodation types at the Filoxenia.

Each has a private bathroom with shower. Some units have a separate bedroom, and an extra bathroom.

Air conditioning and safety box are provided upon charge. A la carte breakfast can be enjoyed daily at the dining area.

Cocktails and coffee are served at the poolside bar. Lunch and dinner include family recipes made with local produce, such as spoon sweets and homemade liqueurs.

Free sun loungers are provided on the terrace. Leisure options include petanque, mini golf and table tennis facilities.

Younger guests can enjoy the playground and childrenâ€™s pool. Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas.

Staff can arrange car rental to explore the Butterfly Valley 6 km away. Rhodes Town is 18 km away.

Rhodes International Airport is within 5 km. Free private parking is possible on site and a bus stops right outside the property.
