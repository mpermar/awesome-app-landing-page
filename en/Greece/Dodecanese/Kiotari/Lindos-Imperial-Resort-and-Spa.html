Description

Situated on Kiotari Beach, Lindos Imperial offers rooms overlooking the Aegean Sea or pools and gardens.

Gourmet restaurants, tennis courts and a spa centre are featured. Free WiFi is offered throughout.

All air-conditioned units are spacious and elegantly decorated with light colours and marble bathrooms.

LCD satellite TV and fridge are standard. Lindos Imperial offers 3 outdoor pools and an indoor pool.

Two tennis courts, a mini football field, a boccia field and 2 beach-volley courts are featured.

An on-site playground is available. Wellness options at hotel Lindos Imperial include a fully equipped spa centre, a gym, sauna and hot tub.

A massage room and a beauty salon are also featured. Lindos Imperial’s 4 restaurants serve Greek, Italian and international cuisine.

Guests can relax at the lobby bar on the terrace, or at one of the outdoor bars. Staff at the 24-hour reception desk can help with car rentals, water sports equipment and offer travel information.

Child supervision services are also offered. Lindos town and archaeological site are at 8 km.
