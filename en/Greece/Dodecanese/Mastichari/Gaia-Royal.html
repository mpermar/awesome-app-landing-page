Description

Gaia Royal has everything you need for a fun, family break in Kos. You can relax on one of the many lounge chairs around the pools, while the children join in the fun and games with the hotel’s entertainment team. Enjoy a game of tennis on one of Gaia Royal’s large courts or have a quiet stroll to the nearby sandy beach. Relax and enjoy a glass of wine after a day of sunbathing.

Gaia Royal’s provides you with a comfortable base for exploring the rest of the charming island of Kos. Take advantage of the resort’s free private parking and rent a car, or use the nearby local bus, which has excellent connections to the center of Kos. The charming fishing village of Mastichari is also just 3 miles away.
