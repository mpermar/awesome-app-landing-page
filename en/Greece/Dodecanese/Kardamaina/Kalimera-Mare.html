Description

The hotel is within walking distance of a sandy beach. Kalimera Mare is 6 miles from Kos International Airport.

Standard room features include free Wi-Fi, a minibar and TV with satellite channels. Each has a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

Kalimera Mare’s restaurant serves breakfast, lunch and dinner. Local spirits and light food are available at the main bar.
