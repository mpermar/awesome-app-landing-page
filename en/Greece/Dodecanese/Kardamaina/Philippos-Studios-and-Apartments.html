Description

Philippos Apartments is just 30 metres from the beach and within a 4-minute walk from the centre of Kardamena.

It features air-conditioned accommodation with kitchenette and free Wi-Fi, and an outdoor pool and poolside snack bar next to the property.

The studios of Philippos open to a private balcony enjoying views over the Aegean Sea, mountain or the surrounding area.

Each unit features a safe and a private bathroom with shower and hairdryer. A welcome basket is offered to guests upon arrival.

The nearby beach creates a unique setting for walking or running next to the sea.

Philippos Studios & Apartments lies within 7 km from Kos International Airport and 26 km from Kos Town and Port.

Free public parking can be found nearby. Car and bike rental services can be provided.
