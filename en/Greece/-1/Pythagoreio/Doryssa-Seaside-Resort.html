Description

Doryssa Seaside Resort lies on a long sandy beach that is protected from the wind. A magnificent setting is the result of unique architecture. Environmentally friendly materials, energy saving policies and upholstery made of pure linen create a clean and refreshing ambiance for your stay. Special materials have been used in all the rooms in Doryssa Seaside Resort to make your stay as comfortable as possible.

To fully unwind, the spa is the ideal place to find relaxation and harmony for body and mind. Set in lush green gardens, choose from a variety of soothing treatments.
