Description

Glicorisa Beach Hotel has bright and air conditioned rooms, all with panoramic sea view. Each guest room also features a refrigerator and TV, and is cleaned on a daily basis.

Within 2 miles of Pythagorion town in Samos, this quiet, family-run hotel is 6 miles from the airport and 15 minutes from the main port town of Samos.

On the restaurant’s large veranda, breakfast and dinner are served as a buffet that covers all tastes. The beach bar serves all kinds of drinks, beverages, ice cream and snacks. In the hotel lounge, guests can also find a traditional Greek bar, with its local wood decoration.
