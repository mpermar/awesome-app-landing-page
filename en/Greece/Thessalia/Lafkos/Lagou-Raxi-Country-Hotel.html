Description

Set on an elevated position in the area of Lafkos, Lagou Raxi Country Hotel features an outdoor pool and a tennis court.

It offers tastefully decorated rooms with views of the sea or the surrounding mountains.

Rooms at Lagou Raxi will provide you with a bath or shower, private bathrooms also come with free toiletries.

Extras include a fan. Guests can enjoy lunch and dinner at the on-site Azure restaurant prepared with home-grown, organic produce.

There is also a wine bar offering a wide selection of wines from France, Italy and Greece.

Within 1 km, you can find cafes and shops. Milina Beach is 7 km away. At 45 km, you can reach the town of Volos.

Free WiFi is provided throughout and free parking can be found on the nearby streets.
