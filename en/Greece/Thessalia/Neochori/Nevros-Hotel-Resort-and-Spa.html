Description

Situated in Neochori village, Nevros Resort offers rooms with lake-view balconies, in the tranquil surroundings of Plastira Lake.

It features a fully equipped fitness room, as well as a seasonal pool. Nevros Hotel Resort & Spa consists of classically decorated rooms each with each own fireplace.

They all have a TV, a safety box and a private bathroom. Guests of Nevros Hotel Resort & Spa can pamper themselves at the Aloe Spa Center, which apart from the gym also includes a hot tub.

Nevros Hotel’s restaurant serves tasteful dishes prepared by an executive chef, where as Niala Tavern offers traditional specialties of the area.

The scenic area of Meteora is a 50-minute drive away. Free Wi-Fi is available in all public areas of Nevros Hotel and free parking is possible on site.
