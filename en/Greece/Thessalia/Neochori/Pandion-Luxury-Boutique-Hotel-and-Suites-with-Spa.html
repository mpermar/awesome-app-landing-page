Description

Surrounded by lush greenery, Pandion Luxury Boutique Hotel & Suites with Spa offers traditional accommodation with panoramic views to Lake Plastiras and its surrounding area.

Following the traditional architecture, the stone-built hotel is located in the village of Neochori and offers free Wi-Fi and 24-hour room service.

All rooms of the hotel are elegantly furnished with elements that combine the traditional atmosphere with the modern one and all come with a fireplace.

They are equipped with TV, electric kettle and mini bar and feature a balcony or a patio with lake views.

Bathrooms are stocked with bathrobe and shower slippers. Pandion Luxury Boutique Hotel & Suites with Spa comprises of a bar, a living room and a breakfast room.

The traditional breakfast, which includes homemade pies and sweets, can also be served in your room upon request.

The property is located above Lake Plastiras and at a distance of 30 km from the town of Karditsa.

The surrounding area is ideal for outdoors activities such as canoeing, mountain bike and trekking.
