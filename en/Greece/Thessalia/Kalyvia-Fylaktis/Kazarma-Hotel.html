Description

Just 400 metres from Lake Plastira, this 4-star hotel offers luxurious rooms with view and fireplace.

There is an underground parking on site. The Kazarma Hotel’s rooms have elegant furniture and décor.

Each features free Wi-Fi internet access, coffee corner and 32-inch LCD TV. The rooms’ balconies enjoy views over Lake Plastira or Agrafa mountain range.

The Kazarma restaurant serves traditional dishes, while the hotel’s café and bar lounge offers a spectacular view of the Lake.
