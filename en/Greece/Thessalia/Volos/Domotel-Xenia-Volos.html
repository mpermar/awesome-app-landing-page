Description

Xenia Volou Domotel is an elegant city and conventions resort in Volos. It offers fine dining and stylish rooms with free Wi-Fi and PAY TV.

Use of the Elxis Spa is free of charge. The wellness centre includes a hot tub, solarium and fully equipped gym, 2 saunas and 2 steam baths.

Guests can also swim in the indoor pool or have a massage treatment. The stylish rooms and suites of Xenia Volou Domotel offer balcony with city or sea views.

In-room features include satellite TV, air conditioning, minibar, safe, hairdryer, bathrobes and slippers.

Room service is available 24 hours a day. The a la carte restaurant, with a breathtaking view to the sea, an open kitchen and relaxed atmosphere, serves Mediterranean cuisine combined with local specialties.

Greek Breakfast is served daily, while at The Corner guests can enjoy all kinds of beverages and light snacks.

Guests will enjoy cocktails and wine with panoramic sea views at the on-site Yacht Club.

Xenia Volou Domotel offers 2 multifunctional meeting rooms complemented by audiovisual equipment, open-air parking, ISDN phone lines and Wi-Fi.

Situated at the centre of the city of Volos, close to the sea, the hotel serves as the perfect starting point for short trips and excursions to Sporades islands or to the picturesque villages of Pelio.
