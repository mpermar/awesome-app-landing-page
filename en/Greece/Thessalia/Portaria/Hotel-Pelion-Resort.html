Description

The 4-star hotel is situated at an altitude of 700 meters in the traditional settlement of Portaria, offering stunning views of Mount Pelion and Makrinitsa.

It offers an outdoor swimming pool. The rooms and suites, all with independent entrance through the gardens dispose luxury and space.

All units are fully equipped with minibar, CD player and satellite TV. Constructed with respect to the traditional local architecture and with accordance to its natural surroundings, Pelion Resort offers a complex of buildings surrounded by beautiful trees and vegetation.

There are also 3 private parking areas on site. The reception, bar and restaurant, as well as the comfortable seating areas with fireplace are housed in the 2 main buildings.

A spa with hot tub and sauna are also featured. Among the green gardens, the children’s playground awaits young guests who wish to be entertained.

At Hotel Pelion Resort, Greek breakfast buffet is served daily by the fireplace or in the garden.

It includes local yogurt, fresh fruits and fresh cheese bread as well as olive breads.
