Description

The year-round Boutique Hotel Kentrikon & Bungalows is located only 30 metres from Agios Ioannis beach.

It features rooms with partial or complete views of the Aegean Sea. Free Wi-Fi internet access is available throughout the hotel.

Each of the rooms has heating and air conditioning. They include a flat-screen satellite TV, minibar and toiletries.

Some also have a fireplace, hydro massage bath or shower, safety deposit box and balcony.

The hotel is divided in 4 buildings, distributed on a hillside and reachable by stairs.

The on-site bar offers traditional cuisine prepared with local products that can be enjoyed in the hotel’s atrium.

Room service is also accessible. A Swedish sauna, hot tub and a steam bath with aromatherapy is also available.

Centrally situated in the village of Agios Ioannis, Boutique Hotel Kentrikon & Bungalows is 50 km from Volos.

It is 500 metres from Plaka beach and 800 metres from Papa Nero beach. Private parking is possible on site only with Request!.
