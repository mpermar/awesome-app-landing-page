Description

Located in Hania, the year-round Anamar Pilio Resort offers rooms with satellite TV.

It has an outdoor pool, a traditional restaurant, and indoor parking. Agriolefkes Ski Centre is just 2 km from the hotel.

Each of the carefully decorated and furnished rooms has private balcony with delightful views.

All of them are further equipped with safe and heating. Many of the rooms have a seating area with fireplace.

Breakfast is served each morning at the breakfast hall. The hotel’s traditional restaurant serves freshly prepared meals with emphasis in local cuisine.

During the summer months, hotel guests are welcome to use the sun beds around the pool, and spend the day relaxing in the sun.
