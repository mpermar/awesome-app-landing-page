Description

Gouves Waterpark Holiday Resort is located in Gouves, only 300 metres from the sandy beach.

It features 3 sweet-water pools, a multipurpose court, a beauty centre and air-conditioned rooms with balcony.

Rooms are spacious with seating area and work desk. Each has a satellite TV, hairdryer and fridge.

A safe can be provided at an extra charge. Guests can visit the on-site water park featuring 4 water slides or play volleyball, tennis or football at the hotel’s court.

There is a children’s disco, and the animation team arranges many activities, such as painting and darts.

The main restaurant serves varied buffets for breakfast, lunch and dinner. The Gouves Park bar offers 2 terraces with panoramic view.

There is a poolside snack bar serving hot and cold dishes, a pizza restaurant, a self-service pool bar, and a sports café bar with billiards.

Children can enjoy delightful children menus. Gouves Park is located 12 km east of Heraklion Airport.

Hersonissos, with its numerous restaurants, shops, bars and night clubs, is only a short drive away.
