Description

The Sea Side Resort & Spa houses a Chinese Restaurant and a mini spa, and features 2 sweet-water pools and 2 pool bars.

Due to its elevated position, it offers panoramic sea views. Mononaftis Beach is at 80 metres.

The air-conditioned rooms have private furnished terraces or balconies with land or sea views.

Standard amenities include 20-inch satellite TVs and amenities for tea and coffee making.

Upgrades include rooms with sharing pools and panoramic sea views. With 3 restaurants, the Sea Side Resort covers a wide range of cuisines including Greek, Chinese and International.

The main restaurant’s highlights include show cooking and its large sea-view veranda.

Drinks are available from the lobby bar and pool bars. Sports and leisure facilities include a fitness room, exterior aerobic area, as well as tennis and table tennis facilities.

The spa features an indoor pool, sauna, hammam and hydrotherapy pools. Guests also benefit from free internet access in public hotel areas.

Located in Agia Pelagia in Crete, the Sea Side Resort & Spa is situated 22 km west of Heraklion Airport and 18 km west of Heraklion city centre.
