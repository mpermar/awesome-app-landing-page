Description

Overlooking the deep blue bay of Agia Pelagia, Peninsula Resort & Spa sits amidst lush plantations and is surrounded by 2 beaches.

Two large pools, 2 children’s pools and 2 tennis courts are featured. The Peninsula Resort & Spa is located on the North coast of Crete, just 25 km from Heraklio.

It is surrounded by 2 beaches that offer a wide range of water sports, as well as a certified diving centre.

The Peninsula Resort & Spa offers elegant guest rooms styled in a local Cretan décor.

In-room equipment includes air conditioning, satellite TVs and a fridge. The buffet restaurant serves Cretan, Greek and international dishes.

Breakfast, lunch and dinner can be enjoyed with a view of the sea and Dia Island in the distance.

Drinks can be enjoyed at the 4 bars available. Multi-use courts for football, basketball and volleyball, pool games, table tennis and billiards are available.

The animation program ends at 23:00 daily. A satellite TV area is available for gatherings and football games.

A spa centre with an indoor pool lies within the premises. Car parking is available and a Byzantine Chapel enhances the hotel's entrance with its frescoes.
