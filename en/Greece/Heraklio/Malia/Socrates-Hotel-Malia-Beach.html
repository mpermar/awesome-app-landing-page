Description

Socrates Hotel Malia Beach is located on the beach road connecting Stalida and Malia.

Guests enjoy a quiet location 2 minutes from the beach, 10 minutes' drive from Stalida and 10 minutes' from Malia.

Rooms are en suite with kitchenette facilities and private balconies overlooking the sea and the pool.

Other facilities include air conditioning, TV and safe. The complex offers a swimming pool and a separate pool for children.

A snack bar, a pool restaurant and free sun beds and umbrellas are featured on the sun terrace.

Sports fans can watch satellite TV at the snack bar. Sports and entertainment facilities at Socrates Hotel Malia Beach include table tennis, children’s playground and sauna.

Stalis is perfect place for lazy days on the beach with blue waters. There are plenty of water sports on offer.

There are number of historical places to visit such as the Palace of King Minos at Knossos and Heraklion, with its impressive Venetian architecture and imposing castle.
