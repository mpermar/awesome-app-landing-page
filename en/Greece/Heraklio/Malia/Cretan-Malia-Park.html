Description

Located in the midst of rich med nature, along a secluded sandy beach in Malia Village, the beachfront Cretan Malia Park features a long river pool, a main swimming pool, heated pool for the shoulder season and a children's pool.

The stylish hotel also includes 3 restaurants and offers a variety of activities from morning gym, aerobics, and table tennis, to volleyball and beach games.

Free WiFi access is provided throughout. Elegantly decorated with tiled floors and well-chosen furnishings, the rooms, bungalows and suites of Cretan Malia Park are equipped with air conditioning, safe and a satellite TV.

Each unit features a private marble bathroom with bath tub or shower, hairdryer and free toiletries based on Greek bio olive oil.

Scales, bathrobes, slippers and magnifying mirrors are available in all rooms. Guests can start their day with a Greek breakfast including various cheese types, homemade pies and honey.

An upgraded selection of wine and beverages is available. For lunch, guests can now choose between an extensive buffet in the Mediterranean Restaurant and the Pool BBQ.

For dinner, guests can choose between the Mediterranean Restaurant, Cretan Restaurant and the Italian Restaurant.

The hotel's entertainment team provides Greek cookery as well as evening shows, fun and games.

The younger guests can spend their time at the kid's club, where competitions and theater performances are organized, the playground or the kid's pool.

Labeled as a bike-friendly hotel, Cretan Malia Park also provides bike rental services.

Guests may cycle around the hotel and through nearby villages and explore at their own pace the wild beauty of Crete.

Other facilities include a bio-garden and herb garden. The beachfront is extensive and runs for about 5 km, between Stalida and Malia.

The centre of the lively Malia lies within 2. 5 km from Cretan Malia Park, while Heraklion International Airport is at a distance of 34 km.
