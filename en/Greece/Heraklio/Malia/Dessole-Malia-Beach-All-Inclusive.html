Description

Surrounded by a tended garden, you can spend the day by one of the pools or play mini-golf, while enjoying the warm sea breeze. The accommodations are spacious and rooms contain a balcony or terrace, with a stunning view of the grounds and Mediterranean Sea.

The Malia Beach Hotel offers a variety of services and activities for a wider audience. The resort also contains a restaurant, tavern, pool bar and beach bar. If you are looking for a diverse hotel resort and easy access to the center, The Malia Beach Hotel provides the ideal choice.
