Description

Situated at the sandy Malia Beach and just 2 km from the town centre, Alexander Beach Hotel & Village, offers a host of activities, in quiet surroundings.

The main building and bungalows are set on 50,000 m gardens, facing the beach. Rooms and suites are clean and simply decorated, featuring balconies with sea or garden views.

All rooms contain a refrigerator, separate bathroom with a bath, shower and bathroom amenities.

Extended in more than 1,000 m2, the on-site spa features an indoor pool, hot tub, sauna and hammam.

Guests can relax with face and body treatments, manicure, pedicure and peelings. Yoga and pilates courses can be provided upon request.

The property hosts 2 restaurants, Meltemi serving Greek, traditional specialties and Main Restaurant, which is more formal and boasts superb views of the bay.

Kriti Tavern is also available. For refreshing drinks, guests have a choice from the Apollo Pool Bar, Main Bar, Alexander Beach Club and Alexander Lounge.

Activities guests can enjoy without leaving the property include beach volley ball, tennis, and swimming in any of the 5 swimming pools.

Alternatively, the lively villages of Malia and Stalis are only few minutes away.
