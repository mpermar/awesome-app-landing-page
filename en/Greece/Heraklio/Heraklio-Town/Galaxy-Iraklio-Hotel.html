Description

All accommodations in Galaxy Iraklio offers work desks, satellite LCD TV and free Wi-Fi access. Air-conditioned rooms are equipped with pure Egyptian cotton linen and pure wellness or hypoallergenic rooms are available. Some units feature whirlpools. Each room and suite at Galaxy Iraklio is stocked with USB wall sockets.

Vetri Restaurant serves an American buffet breakfast prepared with fresh, local ingredients and Cretan cuisine. Per Se Lounge offers casual meals, wines and cocktails. The Pool Deck serves light meals and drinks. Every Monday between June and September, a live music event is hosted at Galaxy Iraklio Hotel.

24-hour room service is available with additional in-room dishes. Galaxy also includes a state-of-the-art conference center, with 9 fully equipped halls and a total capacity of 800 people.

Galaxy Hotel’s Wellness Club is open 24 hours a day and its use is free to all guests. It includes a state-of-the-art gym and an all-marble steam room.

Guests of Galaxy Iraklio Hotel are within a 10-minute walk from the city center and shopping and historic attractions. Knossos archaeological site is a 10-minute drive. Heraklion International Airport is at 3.1 mi. Staff at the 24-hour reception can arrange tickets and car rentals. Free public parking is possible on site.
