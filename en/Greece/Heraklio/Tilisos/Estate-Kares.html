Description

Situated 1 km outside the village of Tylissos, Estate Kares offers traditional self-catering rooms.

It features a pool and a well-equipped fitness room. Free WiFi is available throughout the hotel.

Kares units are warmly decorated and fully furnished. Each studio and apartment has its own kitchen and it is equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV and DVD player.

They all have private verandas. The studio also comes with a spa bath. The pool’s sun terrace is the ideal place to relax, while enjoying the views of the surrounding green hills.

For those wanting to stay fit, a sauna and a massage studio operates inside Estate Kares.

Local specialties are served in the Cretan tavern, but there is also a wooden stove, as well as BBQ facilities.

Estate Kares is 5 km away from the nearest beach. The city of Heraklion is within 12 km.
