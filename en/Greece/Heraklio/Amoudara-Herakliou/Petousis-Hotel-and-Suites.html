Description

Petousis Hotel & Suites features a restaurant, seasonal outdoor swimming pool, a fitness centre and bar in Amoudara Herakliou.

Each accommodation at the 4-star hotel has pool views, and guests can enjoy access to a shared lounge and to a garden.

The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and free WiFi.

All units are equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kettle, a shower, a hairdryer and a desk.

At the hotel every room is equipped with a wardrobe and a private bathroom. A continental breakfast is available daily at Petousis Hotel & Suites.

The accommodation offers a sun terrace. Heraklio Town is 8 km from Petousis Hotel & Suites, while Hersonissos is 34 km away.
