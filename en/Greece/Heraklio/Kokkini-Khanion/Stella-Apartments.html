Description

Just 20 metres from Kokkini Chani Beach, Stella Apartments offers self-catered rooms with a balcony overlooking the Cretan Sea.

The village centre with restaurants and shops is at 120 metres. The spacious units at Apartments Stella have a TV and radio.

Each has a well-equipped kitchen with a fridge and cooking hobs. All apartments include a private bathroom with shower.

Hotel staff can offer information on the archaeological site of Knossos at 15 km.
