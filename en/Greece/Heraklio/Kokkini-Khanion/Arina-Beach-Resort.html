Description

Located right on the beachfront, amidst beautiful gardens and only 1 km away from Kokkini Chani, Arina Beach Resort offers spacious rooms and waterfront bungalows overlooking the crystal clear waters of the Cretan sea.

Free WiFi is available throughout. The hotel offers an outdoor swimming pool and restaurants with international and Greek cuisine, a beach bar, a pool bar, a mini club and various entertainment and sports activities.

Designed to provide comfort, all rooms and bungalows offer stunning views over the Cretan sea.

Featuring a furnished terrace or balcony, all rooms come with individually controlled air-conditioning, a satelite TV and a DVD player.

A safe is also provided. At Arina Beach Hotel, guests can choose among two restaurants and three bars to enjoy their meal or refreshing drink in an always relaxed and friendly environment.

Ariadne Restaurant offers a selection of dishes for the whole family with themed buffets each evening and Minos Restaurant offers a unique Greek cuisine experience with amazing views over the Cretan sea and the pool.

Enjoy the glorious sunshine in the pool bar where guests may enjoy an abundance of local alcoholic and non alcoholic drinks.

The sandy beach with its shallow waters certainly makes a day at the sea ideal for children whereas guests may also enjoy various water sports activities.

Other available sports activities include tennis, table tennis, beach volley and a fitness room.

Arina Beach Resort is at the heart of the action as the city centre and the port of Heraklion is just 13 km away, while the airport is only 10 km away.
