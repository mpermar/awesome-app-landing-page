Description

Directly situated on the Blue Flag awarded beach of Anissaras village, Lyttos Beach is surrounded by a 150-acre garden of more than 500 palm trees and flowers.

It includes 10 swimming pools, an aqua park with waterslides, 27 tennis courts upon charge and 4 padel courts.

Its 349 spacious rooms have floor-to-ceiling windows facing the sea or garden. Some rooms boast a private swimming pool.

The fresh and bright rooms are equipped with air conditioning, safe, free WiFi, 42 satellite TV, mini fridge and marble bathroom with hair dryer.

Guests can taste local, traditional and international delicacies, served in rich buffets and live action cooking at the Main Restaurant.

A childrens menu is also offered. Taverna Kalypso serves buffets and a la carte menus, in a modern yet romantic seaside setting.

There are 4 bars available on site. Sports facilities also include a mini football pitch, a 3 PAR golf course and a basketball court.

Younger guests can spend their time at the children's pool, the on-site kids club or at the new aqua park also including a fast food restaurant.

Lyttos Beach also offers a daytime activity program and evening entertainment with shows in the amphitheatric stage.

SPA services are available upon charge. Guests enjoy free access to the gym and sauna.

There is also a bicycle rental shop (extra charge) for the more adventurous types.

Within a 20 km distance from the airport of Iraklion, Lyttos Beach is just 5 km from Hersonissos city center and Cretes unique 18-hole golf court.
