Description

Situated 100 metres from the scenic Koutouloufari village, Marni is a luxurious village-style complex overlooking the Hersonissos bay.

It has 3 swimming pools, a floodlit tennis court and a sauna. Marni Village consists of traditional stone-built apartments, studios and suites with furnished balcony or veranda.

They all include a kitchenette, a seating area and a satellite TV. Some units also include a private pool.

Air conditioning and a safe are available upon request with surcharge. The pools are surrounded by spacious stone-paved sun terraces offering panoramic views over the bay.

2 separate pools for the children are also available. A la carte breakfast is served daily at the main pool bars area.

There are 2 more bars serving Cretan and international dishes, local wine and refreshing cocktails.

Marni Village is 1 km away from the busy town of Hersonissos with its variety of bars, restaurants and cafes.

The traditional villages of Piskopiano and Old Hersonissos are located within walking distance.
