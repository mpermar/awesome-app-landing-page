Description

Set in Hersonissos, just 200 metres from the sandy beach, the family-run Vlychada Apartments features a pool, a relaxing sun terrace and a lush garden.

It offers self-catering accommodation with a balcony overlooking the pool, the garden or the Cretan Sea.

The studios and apartments of Vlychada are air conditioned, and traditionally decorated with built-in beds and tiled floors.

Each unit includes a kitchenette with fridge and a private bathroom. A safe is available at extra charge.

Guests will find restaurants and supermarkets within a short walk from Vlychada Apartments.

The marine park Star Beach Waterpark is at 100 metres, while Nikos Kazantzakis airport is 26 km away.
