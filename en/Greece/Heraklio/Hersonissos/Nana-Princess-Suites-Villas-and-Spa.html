Description

Nestled in a private beach in Hersonissos, Nana Princess Suites Villas & Spa boasts a state-of-the-art Health Club & Spa centre, 3 restaurants and 2 outdoor swimming pools.

Guests can choose amongst a sumptuous collection of 112 suites and villas, each one with unique design and modern appliances.

Almost all accommodations boast a private swimming pool and spacious outdoor areas where guests can enjoy mesmerising Cretan Sea views.

Some suites and villas come with a sauna, detoxifying steam areas or private gym area.

The luxurious bathroom in every suite and villa is stocked with a rain shower and separate bath.

Guests may taste a variety of fine dining options inspired by Cretan cuisine, Mediterranean flavours and international dishes in all 3 restaurants.

Signature cocktails and refreshing drinks can be enjoyed at the on-site bars. Nana Princess Suites Villas & Spa also boasts a cigar lounge and shisha bar.

The Royal Wellness Club and Royal Fitness Club, along with the calming indoor pool, invite guests to indulge themselves in a very discreet environment where holistic therapies can be enjoyed.

Beauty treatments and hairdressing services ensure that guests feel pampered and genuinely rejuvenated.

A wide collection of luxury goods and items can be purchased at the "Good Life" mini-mall, situated in the main building of Nana Princess.

It shares some sports facilities with its sister property including tennis court, beach volley, mini soccer and children's animation programs and shows.
