Description

Just 50 metres from the sandy beach and the port in Hersonissos, the Star Beach Village features a spacious pool complex with water slides, a children’s playground and free on-site parking facilities.

This exciting complex offers spacious accommodation with spectacular sea views. The resort consists of modernly decorated rooms with spacious terraces or balconies.

Family Rooms have a separate bathroom and WC. There are plenty of dining options at the Star Beach Village & Water Park including the main restaurant, a restaurant serving Cretan cuisine, lobby bar and pool snack bar.

WiFi is offered throughout, while an internet corner with desktop computers lies within the premises.

Star Beach Village is a 10-minute walk from Hersonissos’ main road, offering a variety of shops, bars and dining outlets.
