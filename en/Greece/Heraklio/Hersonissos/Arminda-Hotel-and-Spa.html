Description

Only 100 metres from the nearest beach in Hersonissos, Arminda Hotel & Spa is an all-inclusive 4-star hotel.

It features 4 large salt-water pools with 2 separate childrens sections. The spa centre offers a sauna, hamam and hot tub.

Featuring floor-to-ceiling windows, all air-conditioned units open to a balcony with pool, garden or Cretan Sea views.

They have a satellite TV, safe and a fridge. Each private bathroom is stocked with a hairdryer.

Guests at Arminda can enjoy meals, in buffet style, at the on-site restaurant. A variety of drinks are served at the pool-side snack bar or at the on-site Votsalo bar.

Sport facilities include tennis and mini-golf courts, as well as a fitness room. A playground and a kid's club is also provided for young guests.

Massage, face and body therapies are available at the spa centre. Arminda Hotel is 2 km from Hersonissos centre and 40 km from Heraklion Port.

Nikos Kazantzakis International Airport is at 38 km. Free parking is possible at a location nearby.
