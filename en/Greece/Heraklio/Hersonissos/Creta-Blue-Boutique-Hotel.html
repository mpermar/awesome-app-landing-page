Description

Situated in the picturesque Koutouloufari, this small boutique hotel offers luxurious accommodation with breathtaking views of the Aegean Sea, an infinity pool and a la carte dining.

From the balcony or terrace of Creta Blue’s elegantly designed units, guests can enjoy garden, pool or sea views.

Suites and rooms also feature air conditioning, LED TVs with media players and comfortable beds.

In the evening, the pool bar changes to a romantic open-air restaurant and guests are invited to enjoy homemade recipes using locally sourced ingredients in an intimate environment.

They can also enjoy the magnificent panoramic views of the area. The hotel’s infinity pool is the perfect place to enjoy the hot Mediterranean sun.

For further relaxation, guests can enjoy a massage in the privacy of their own room.

Free WiFi access is available in public areas. Hotel staff can arrange car rental and schedule excursions to important Cretan sights, such as the archaeological site of Knossos.

Creta Blue Suites is just 5 minutes’ walk from the centre of Koutouloufari and 1 km from the centre of Hersonissos.

It is a 30-minute drive from the airport and only a 10-minute drive from Greece’s most prominent 18-hole golf course.
