Description

The rooms of Annabelle Beach Resort are bright and airy and enjoy beautiful garden or pool views. They are equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV and refrigerator, and feature marbled bathrooms.

Annabelle guests can relax in one of the 2 outdoor pools or in the indoor pool. The beach in front of the hotel offers sun beds and umbrellas, as well as many water-sport options. Guests can also benefit from the spa facilities including a hot tub and a sauna.

Ambrosia restaurant serves buffet meals overlooking the sea, while Athena tavern offers Greek and International dishes by the pool. 3 bars, at the lobby, the pool and the beach serve fresh juices and cocktails.

Free private on-site parking is possible in Annabelle Village. Free WiFi is also available at the lobby and reception area. Nikos Kazantzakis International Airport is 14 mi away.

Anissaras is a great choice for travelers interested in the oceanside, beaches and relaxation.
