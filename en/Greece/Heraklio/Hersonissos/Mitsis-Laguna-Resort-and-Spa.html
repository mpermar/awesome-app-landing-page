Description

Mitsis Laguna Resort is located on Anissaras sandy beach, only 3 km from Hersonissos.

Sports and leisure facilities include a free-access tennis court, mini soccer field, watersports and a spa centre.

There are 3 outdoor and 1 indoor seawater swimming pool on site. The elegant rooms and bungalows are air-conditioned with LCD satellite TVs.

A minibar is available with free drinks. All rooms feature a balcony or terrace. Bathrooms come with toiletries, bathrobes, slippers, scales and hairdryer.

Mitsis Laguna Resort features 1 main restaurant, 5 theme restaurants offering cuisines that range from Mexican, Cretan and Italian, to Asian and Sushi, and 1 creperie.

There are 5 bars on site, including a champagne bar. Two of them are set by the pools, and 3 in the main building.

A full animation program, childrenâ€™s pool with water slides, outdoor playground and playroom are available for young guests.

Umbrellas and sun beds are provided on the beach and on the poolside. Free WiFi is available in specific areas in the lobby.

Mitsis Laguna Resort & Spa is 24 km from Heraklion Port and 22 from Heraklion Airport.

Free parking is available on site, while car, motorbike or bicycle hire is available through reception.
