Description

Albatros Spa & Resort Hotel rooms are tastefully decorated and feature a furnished balcony. They are equipped with air conditioning and a satellite LED TV. 24-hour room service is available.

Guests can indulge themselves at Euphoria spa offering massages and facial treatments, as well as manicure and pedicure. It includes a hot tub, steam bath, sauna and a gym.

The main restaurant serves Mediterranean and international cuisine in buffet style. Daphne restaurant prepares Cretan dishes made with fresh local products.

Prova wine bar is dedicated to local wine varieties. Information on Cretan wine tradition, varieties and wineries will be offered. Guests will be able to participate in wine tasting on Tuesdays and Greek cooking lessons on Thursdays. A seaside café bar is also featured.

Heraklion Airport is 16 mi away from Albatros. Within 1148 feet, there is a bus stop connecting to the major Cretan destinations.

Limenas Hersonissou is a great choice for travelers interested in nightlife, bars and the oceanside.
