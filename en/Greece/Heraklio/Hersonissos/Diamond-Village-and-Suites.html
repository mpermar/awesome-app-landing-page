Description

Surrounded by well-tended gardens, Diamond Village is a 10-minute walk from Hersonissos Beach and features a restaurant and swimming pool.

It offers self-catered accommodation with a balcony overlooking the Cretan Sea, the mountains or the lush greenery.

A kitchenette with cooking facilities, coffee machine and fridge is included in all the air-conditioned units at the Diamond Village.

Each has a seating area with a flat-screen satellite TV. Light meals and drinks are served at the poolside snack bar.

An indoor bar is also available. Guests can have lunch or dinner at the on-site restaurant.

Leisure facilities include billiards, table tennis and a library. Younger guests can enjoy the children’s pool or the playground.

Free Wi-Fi access is provided in public areas. Staff at the 24-hour front desk can arrange car rental to explore the lively Malia 6 km away.

A waterpark and golf course are within a 5-minute drive. Free private parking is available at the property.
