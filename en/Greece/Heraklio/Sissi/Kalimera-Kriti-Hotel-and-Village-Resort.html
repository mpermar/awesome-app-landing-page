Description

Located in the outskirts of the fishing village of Sissi, Kalimera Kriti Hotel & Village Resort boasts a private sandy beach, 3 pools and 6 tennis courts.

Most of its rooms enjoy views of the Cretan Sea. Suites and bungalows at Kalimera Kriti Hotel & Village Resort are elegantly furnished with respect to the Cretan style.

They come with a satellite TV, air conditioning and hairdryer. All units open out to private furnished balconies or patios.

The main restaurant overlooks the sea and serves Mediterranean and international cuisine.

Guests can enjoy drinks, cocktails and ice creams in one of the resort’s bars including a beach bar and a traditional Greek coffee house.

Sports facilities at Kalimera Kriti Hotel & Village Resort include a water-sports centre and a beach volleyball court.

Golf enthusiasts can receive privileges at Crete's Golf Club located a 20-minute drive away.

Kalimera Kriti Hotel & Village Resort is a 35-minute drive from both the Minoan Knossos Palace and Heraklion International Airport.
