Description

Tradition meets comfort and luxury in these traditional villas located in the settlement Kato Asites, hardly 20 kilometres from Heraklion City.

In a space of natural beauty we created a group of stone, self-contained and completely equipped villas, with combination of stone and timber.

Facilities at Asion Lithos include a swimming pool, traditional stone-made oven, barbecue and lush green gardens.

Asion LIthos combines all that the visitor wishes for: quietness, natural environment, wonderful view, luxury and tradition.

The European Mountaineering Path E4 is near the hotel, for those who plan to stretch their legs on the rugged terrain.

After an energetic walk, have a barbecue back at Asion Lithos and enjoy the unique wine of Asites.
