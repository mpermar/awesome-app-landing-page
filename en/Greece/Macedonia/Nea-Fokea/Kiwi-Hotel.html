Description

Just 30 metres away from the beach of Nea Fokea, Kiwi Hotel offers air-conditioned accommodation with a private sea-view balcony.

It has a swimming pool with sun terrace and a bar. Bright and spacious, Kiwi rooms are fitted with a satellite LCD TV, fireplace and a private bathroom with hairdryer.

There are also a refrigerator, crockery and cutlery to provide a light children's meal.

Guests can enjoy drinks and snacks at the outdoor shaded terrace of the hotel boasting unobstructed views over Kassandra Bay.

English or continental breakfast can be served upon request and at extra charge. The centre of Nea Fokea is 200 metres away from the complex and features traditional fish taverns and shops.

The seaside resort of Sani is at 9 km. Free parking can be found near the premises.
