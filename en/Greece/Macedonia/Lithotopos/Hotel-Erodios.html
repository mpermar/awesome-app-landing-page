Description

Enjoying panoramic views of Kerkini Lake in Lithotopos village and just 100 metres from its shore, Erodios hotel is surrounded by a green-covered area of 22,000 m2.

It offers spacious rooms with free Wi-Fi. There is a also caf√©, a restaurant and swimming pool with a pool bar.

All rooms open to a balcony overlooking Kerkini Lake and mount Belles. They include iron beds, a flat-screen TV, a mini fridge, private bathroom with a shower, individual heating and air-conditioning.

Guests at Erodios can start their day with a traditional breakfast. The restaurant serves local dishes along with a variety of wines.

Guests can also relax at the bar by the pool and enjoy refreshing drinks and coffee.

The 24-hour front desk can provide information about the area and the activities offered.

Erodios is located 20 km from Sidirokastro Town and 33 km from Serres Town. Free on-site parking is provided.
