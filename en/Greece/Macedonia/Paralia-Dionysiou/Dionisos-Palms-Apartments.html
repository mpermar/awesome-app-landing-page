Description

Just 80 metres from the beach of Dionisos, Dionisos Palms Apartments offer self-catering units with balcony.

Free Wi-Fi is available in all areas. The town of Nea Moudania is at a distance of 3 km.

All air-conditioned apartments of Dionisos Palms feature a separate bedroom. Each has a flat-screen, satellite TV and a kitchenette including a dining table and mini oven with cooking hobs.

Linen and towels are included. Guests may enjoy a game of billiards or table tennis at the property’s games room.

BBQ facilities are available. Several restaurants, café bars and shops can be found a 3-minute walk away.

Thessaloniki City is 48 km away and Macedonia International Airport is approximately 50 km away.
