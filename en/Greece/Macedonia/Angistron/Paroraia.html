Description

Surrounded by lush greenery, Paroraia is a boutique hotel featuring uniquely decorated rooms with free Wi-Fi and balcony overlooking the mountains.

Agistro spring baths are just 300 metres away. Featuring wooden ceilings and elegant furnishings in different styles such as English country, minimalist, modern and oriental, all rooms at the Paroraia have a flat-screen TV and minibar.

Some rooms offer a spa bath. The village centre with taverns and shops is at 800 metres while a bus stops at 300 metres.
