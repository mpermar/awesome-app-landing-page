Description

Royal Palace Resort & Spa is a luxury hotel situated in the heart of Platamonas in front of the marina.

Facilities include an outdoor pool and a Spa. The rooms and suites of the 4-star Royal Palace Resort & Spa are elegantly furnished.

All rooms have views of the Aegean Sea and Mount Olympus. All spacious rooms come with free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TV with DVD player.

The Armonia Spa Centre includes hammam, sauna, hydromassage and solarium. Access to the sauna and the hammam is free, while massages are also available upon request and extra charge.

The Royal Palace Resort & Spa has 3 restaurants offering gourmet meals. Anassa Restaurant is located right in front of the marina of Platamonas, offering intense Mediterranean flavors, fresh seafood and a delightful view.

Ambrosia Restaurant is located on the ground floor of the hotel, while the other restaurant is set on the roof garden and offers magnificent views.

Several beautiful beaches are located at a distance of only a few kilometres. Mount Olympus and its picturesque villages are very close by.
