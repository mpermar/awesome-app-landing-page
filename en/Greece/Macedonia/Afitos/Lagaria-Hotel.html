Description

Offering free WiFi and a shared outdoor pool, Lagaria Palace is located in Áfitos.

This property features a bar, a garden, and air-conditioned accommodation with a furnished balcony.

Overlooking the garden, the modern rooms at Lagaria Palace come with satellite flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

Free WiFi is provided throughout. At Lagaria Palace you will find a 24-hour front desk, a children's playground and a sun terrace.

The property offers free parking. Thessaloniki Airport is a 50-minute drive away.
