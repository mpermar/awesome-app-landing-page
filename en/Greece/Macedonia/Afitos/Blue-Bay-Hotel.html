Description

The rooms are tastefully decorated with a combination of traditional and modern design. Each includes air conditioning and heating, as well as a flat--panel TV and a mini fridge. Some rooms also include mini hi-fi systems.

On the edge of the cliff, the hotel’s restaurant serves buffet breakfast and dinner. Guests can taste dishes from the lunch menu, including Greek and Mediterranean options. A lighter fare is available at the snack bar. Private romantic dinners can also be arranged upon request.

Guests receive free access to the hotel’s fully-equipped fitness room. The Aqua Magic Spa facilities include a steam bath, hot tub and offers a variety of face and body treatments.

The Blue Bay Hotel is just over a quarter mile from Afitos, with its numerous taverns and nightlife spots. Free parking is available on site, and the property can also arrange for tours of the local area.
