Description

Family-run Stratos Hotel is centrally situated in picturesque Afitos, within a 15-minute walk of the beach.

It features an outdoor swimming pool, children’s pool and pool bar. Wi-Fi is free in public areas.

The bright rooms come with furnished balconies, most facing the spacious inner yard and the pool.

All rooms are air conditioned with a modern décor, and have a hairdryer, safe and TV.

Baby cots are available free of charge. A homemade breakfast in buffet style is served each morning, and includes freshly baked pies.

During the summer months, guests can also have breakfast or dinner on the charming terrace.

Hotel staff will be happy to assist you with area information, tour arrangements and car rental.

Safes are available to hire through reception. Your two-way airport transfer can also be arranged.

With the centre of Afitos only 150 metres away, Stratos Hotel is a convenient base for taverns and bars.

Free parking is available, 100 metres from the hotel, and is subject to availability.
