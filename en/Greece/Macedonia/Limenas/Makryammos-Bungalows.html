Description

The air-conditioned rooms feature marble floors and are decorated with light wood furnishings. They come with satellite TV, tea/coffee maker, fridge and bathroom with hairdryer and amenities.

Athletic options include facilities for tennis and basketball, as well as beach soccer and volleyball. A playground is near the shallow-water beach.

A lifeguard is always present on the beach, and there is a freshwater baby pool and a mini club. Sun beds and umbrellas, included in the rate, are reserved for you throughout your stay.

Breakfast and dinner are included at the main restaurant. The seaside tavern serves Mediterranean dishes, including seafood and fresh fish. Refreshing drinks and cocktails are available by the pool and on the beach.

Makryammos Bungalows is just one mile from the capital and port of Thassos. Free private parking is available on site.
