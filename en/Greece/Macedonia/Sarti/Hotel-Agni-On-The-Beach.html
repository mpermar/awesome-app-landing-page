Description

Just 20 metres from the nearest beach, Hotel Agni On The Beach is in Sarti Village.

It offers self-catering accommodation with partial views over the Aegean Sea and free WiFi.

Facilities include a snack bar and a children’s playground. All air-conditioned studios of Hotel Agni open to a furnished balcony.

Each has a TV or a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a kitchenette with cooking hobs and dining table.

A hairdryer is provided. Guests may enjoy light meals and refreshing beverages at the on-site snack bar.

A restaurant and a mini market can be found within a short walk. A shared lounge area and a children’s playground for the younger guests’ entertainment are available.

A sun terrace and a small garden with grassy areas and plants are offered. A bus stop providing connection to several parts of the area is at a distance of 50 metres.

Neos Marmaras Town is 50 km away and Thessaloniki City is 140 km away. Free public parking is possible at a nearby location.
