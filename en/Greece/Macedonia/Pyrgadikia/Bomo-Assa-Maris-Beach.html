Description

With a wonderful beachfront location in Chalkidiki, Bomo Assa Maris Beach offers beautifully landscaped grounds, a large pool area and a variety of activities for guests of all ages.

Bomo Assa Maris Beach provides a range of spacious and bright rooms and suites, with garden or sea view.

For lovers of active leisure, there are 2 tennis courts and a beach volley sand court.

There is also a children's pool, a playground and a wealth of organised activities and entertainment for everyone.

The main restaurant of Bomo Assa Maris Beach serves Greek and international cuisine and has a large terrace.

There is also a beach bar serving refreshing drinks. Theme nights and evening entertainment are organised a few times a week.

The resort is located between the Sithonia and Athos Peninsulas, 100 kilometres from Thessaloniki Airport.
