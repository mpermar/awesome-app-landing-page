Description

Just 120 metres from the Olympic Beach of Pieria, Ouzas Hotel offers simply decorated accommodation opening to a balcony.

It also features a restaurant and a bar. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout. All air-conditioned units at Ouzas Hotel are equipped with a flat-screen, satellite TV.

A kitchenette with fridge and a private bathroom with bath or shower are included in each.

Guests can relax in the garden or benefit from the BBQ facilities available on site.

The property can also assist with bicycle and car rentals. The legendary Mount Olympus is 5 km away.
