Description

Just 500 metres from the sandy Olympic Beach of Pieria, Atira Studios offers self-catering accommodation with a balcony overlooking Mount Olympus.

It includes a sun terrace, a 250-m² children's playground, BBQ facilities, and offers free WiFi throughout.

Bright and airy, the air-conditioned studios of Atira are simply decorated with wooden furnishings and come with a satellite TV.

Each unit has a kitchenette equipped with a fridge, cooking hobs and kitchenware.

Guests can make their own breakfast or other meal at the studios, while they can also enjoy their coffee at the lobby or the flowery yard with views over Mount Olympus.

A variety of taverns, cafés and mini markets can be found within a short walk of the property.

Atira Studios is situated 7 km from the city of Katerini and 65 km from Thessaloniki.

Nea Efessos is at 13 km, while the famous Litochoro Village is 27 km away from the property.
