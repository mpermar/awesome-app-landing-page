Description

The tasteful, air conditioned rooms offer a balcony that overlooks the sea, mount Olympus or the hotel’s extensive grounds. They all have a satellite TV, minibar and marbled bathroom with hairdryer.

Wooden deck chairs, lounge chairs and sun umbrellas are available around the fresh-water pool. Pool & Vitamin bar serves fresh juices and refreshments by the pool. There is free Wi-Fi access in all public areas.

A buffet breakfast is served at Myrtia Restaurant. Dinner buffets range from Italian, to BBQ, and show cooking. Magnolia Tavern serves traditional Greek dishes, and quality Greek wine from the Hotel’s well-stocked cellar.

Sports facilities include 2 tennis courts and ping-pong. Active guests will also find a gym with sauna and hot tub. Children enjoy their own pool and playground area.

Bustling Kallithea offers many entertainment opportunities. Possidi Holidays tour desk organizes excursions, and offers car rental and transportation service. Free parking is available.
