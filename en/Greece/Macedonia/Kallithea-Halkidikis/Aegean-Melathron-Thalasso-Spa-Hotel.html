Description

The 5-star Aegean Melathron is surrounded by a forest reaching down to a beach by Toroni Gulf.

Guests have free access to the gym and 9 swimming pools. The modern, luxurious rooms feature furnished balconies or patios with views to the well-kept gardens and pools or Aegean Sea.

Each air-conditioned room includes a satellite LCD TV and free Wi-Fi access. Hotel Aegean Melathron has 3 pool bars and a beach bar that offer ice creams, snacks and refreshments.

In the evening, the indoor bar features live music. Aegeas Restaurant serves a rich breakfast, as well as a dinner buffet, while the beachfront Mistral Restaurant offers local specialities.

Guests can relax in the indoor pool or around the seawater and freshwater outdoor pools with a shallow area for children.

The 200-metres long beach is equipped with deck chairs and umbrellas available upon charge.

Wellness options at the Melathron include massages and treatments with sea extracts.

The cosmopolitan resort of Kallithea is just 3 km away. Free private parking is possible on site.
