Description

Within 170 metres of the beach of Kallithea, Hotel Oceanis offers rooms and studios with air conditioning.

It has a swimming pool, a poolside bar and provides free Wi-Fi access. Bright and airy, the rooms and studios in Oceanis have a dining and a seating area, while most come with a kitchenette.

Facilities include a flat-screen TV, fridge and hairdryer. All units also have a furnished balcony enjoying views of the surrounding area.

Guests can enjoy a coffee, snack or evening drink at the seating area around the swimming pool under the shade of the trees.

The centre of Kallithea is 50 metres away and offers many bars, cafes and restaurant.

The hotel also serves as a convenient base for exploring the beaches of Kassandra Bay.

Afytos beach can be reached within a 5-minute drive away. Free on-site parking is available.
