Description

Decorated in bright colors, rooms at Korina features a fully stocked mini bar, satellite TV and in-room safe. The bathroom includes a hairdryer and a shower.

Traditional Greek breakfast is served in the morning.

Sports facilities include a floodlit tennis court and a basketball court. A billiards table and a table-tennis table are also available.

Korina Hotel is just 1,000 feet away from the famous Chrysi Ammoudia. Limenas, the main town and port of Thasos, is at 7.5 mi. Free private on-site parking is available.
