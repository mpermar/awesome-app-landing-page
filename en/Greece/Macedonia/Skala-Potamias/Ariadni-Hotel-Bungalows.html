Description

Set amidst a big garden, in the area of Skala Potamias, Ariadni offers rooms with balcony and views to the green-covered surroundings.

It includes a swimming pool with hot tub. Rooms at Hotel Bungalows Ariadni are tastefully furnished and air conditioned.

They come with a satellite TV and a mini fridge. The private bathroom is equipped with a hairdryer and toiletries.

The recently renovated Ariadni features a pool bar, as well as BBQ facilities in the garden which guests can use at a surcharge.

The nearest beach is 600 metres away from the complex. Chrysi Ammoudia Beach is at 800 metres.

Within 700 metres, the centre of Skala Potamias offers super markets, bakeries and restaurants.
