Description

Set within evergreen gardens, Flegra Palace is situated 150 metres from the sandy beach of Pefkohori in Chalkidiki.

Guests may enjoy 2 swimming pools and eat at the modernly decorated restaurants. The bright and modern guest rooms have a furnished balcony or terrace.

Each is equipped with central heating and air conditioning, along with an LCD satellite TV, DVD player, safe and fridge.

Offering pool views, Ambrosia restaurant serves Mediterranean dishes. Coffee and cocktails are prepared throughout the day at Soleil bar, next to the pool.

Flegra Palace’s leisure facilities include a beach volley court, table tennis and LCD satellite TV, while also available are a playground and hot tub.
