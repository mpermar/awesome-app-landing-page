Description

With an elevated position near Kriopigi village, the 4-star Hotel Kriopigi is within walking distance from the beach and offers views over the Toronean Gulf.

It features a pool, a hot tub, a fitness room and rooms with balcony. The air-conditioned rooms are equipped with satellite TV with music channels and free WiFi.

Each has a fridge and hairdryer. Guests can enjoy breakfast, lunch and dinner at the on-site restaurant.

Dolphins pool bar serves cocktails while there is also a traditional Greek coffee house.

Hotel Kriopigi is located 5 km from the lively town of Kallithea. The city of Thessaloniki and its airport is around 90 km away.
