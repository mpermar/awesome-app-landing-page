Description

Located along a private sandy, awarded with the Blue Flag, beach in Kriopigi, Kassandra Palace features a spa centre and an indoor pool.

Including a restaurant overlooking the Aegean Sea, the property offers free WiFi throughout, and rooms with garden, mountain or sea view.

Free beach towels are provided for the pool and the beach. There is also a childrens pool and a kids animation club.

The bright and airy guest rooms have private balcony. Each comes with a flat-screen satellite TV, air conditioning and mini fridge.

Bathrooms have a tub, hairdryer and amenities. The spa centre features a hot tub, sauna, hammam and indoor heated pool.

Sports facilities include a fitness centre and tennis court, while yoga and pilates classes are also held.

Ammos Tavern serves Greek and international dishes, as well as snacks and beverages.

Guests can enjoy refreshing cocktails at the hotels lobby or beach bar. Kassandra Palace is 1.

5 km from the centre of Kriopigi. Staff at the front desk can provide information on car/boat rental and for excursions on the surrounding area.
