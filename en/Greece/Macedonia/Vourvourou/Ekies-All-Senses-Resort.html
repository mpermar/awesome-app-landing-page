Description

Ekies All Senses has a beachfront location on the Gulf of Vourvourou in Sithonia, Chalkidiki.

It offers a beach bar, and restaurant and pool with romantic sunset and sea views.

Accommodation at Ekies All Senses Resort is stylish, bright and airy. All minimal rooms have distinctive décor and a private balcony.

Each is equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV and minibar. Wi-Fi is free of charge.

Guests enjoy free use of Mac for personal e mail check. Guests can relax on the private beach with white sand and crystal clear waters, or take a boat trip to a secluded beach.

The resort’s spa and wellness centre is at your disposal. Coffee can be enjoyed right on the beach.

Afternoon drinks and light snacks are served by the poolside. For the ultimate romantic setting, private dining can be arranged on the beach.

Thessaloniki’s Macedonia Airport is 110 km away from the resort. Free private parking is available.
