Description

Each of the elegant Sunny Hill guest rooms is appointed with light-colored furnishings and offers a seating area. They all come with satellite TV and air conditioning.

Guests can enjoy their coffee or drink at the Sunny Hill Studios café-bar, and enjoy American buffet breakfast in front of the pool or in the privacy of their guest room.

The scenic harbor of Sani is just 0.6 mi away, and offers plenty for guests to do during the evening. Free parking is available at the hotel.
