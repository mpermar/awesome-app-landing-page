Description

Bomo Tosca Beach enjoys a privileged location, combining unique beauty and grandeur of nature with the affordable luxury, only 6 km from the city of Kavala.

A restaurant, bar and on-site parking are featured. The hotel offers modern bungalow-style accommodation with private balcony, equipped with a fridge, air conditioning and TV.

Free WiFi is available in public areas. The property also features a kids club where several entertainment and sports activities are organised.

Visitors may visit historical and religious monuments in the region such as the ancient theatre of Philippi, the ancient thermal baths, which still operate today, the Lake Kerkini, the famous Delta of Nestos River, and the islands of Thasos and Samothrace.
