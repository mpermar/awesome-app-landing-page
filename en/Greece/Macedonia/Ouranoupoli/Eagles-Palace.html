Description

Set amidst lush gardens, Eagles Palace offers 5-star accommodation and a private sandy beach in Skala area.

It includes an outdoor pool, an awarded spa centre and a tennis court. The air-conditioned rooms of Eagles Palace are elegantly decorated in natural colours and feature luxurious marbled bathrooms.

They are fitted with a satellite LCD TV and offer sea or garden views. A certified Greek Breakfast including freshly baked local pies, cheese and fresh fruit is daily available.

Dining options include international gourmet cuisine at Kamares restaurant and fish specialties prepared on the spot at Almyra restaurant.

A café, a cocktail bar and a beach bar serve drinks and cocktails all day long. Eagles Spa offers a variety of body treatments from around the world.

Guests can also relax at the hot tub, the sauna or the indoor pool. Eagles Palace features a water-sports centre providing the necessary equipment for activities such as scuba diving, waterskiing and windsurfing.

The picturesque village of Ouranoupoli is within 4. 5 km from the property. Free private on-site parking is available.
