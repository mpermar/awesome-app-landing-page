Description

Alexandros Palace is located on a lush green slope in the area of Tripiti and boasts a private beach, a wellness centre and a tennis court.

It offers elegant accommodation and a variety of dining options. Rooms and suites at Alexandros are air conditioned and enjoy views of the pool, garden or Mount Athos from their balcony.

They are fitted with a satellite TV, tea/coffee maker and hairdryer. Guests can indulge with massage and beauty treatments at the hotels spa, which includes a hot tub, indoor pool and steam bath.

Alexandros Palace also features an outdoor swimming pool and a separate childrens pool.

Mediterranean and international cuisine is served at the restaurant of the hotel.

Guests can enjoy refreshing drinks and exotic cocktails at one of the 4 bars, including an outdoor bar with panoramic sea views.

The village of Ouranoupolis is a 5-minute drive from the complex. On-site parking is provided free of charge.
