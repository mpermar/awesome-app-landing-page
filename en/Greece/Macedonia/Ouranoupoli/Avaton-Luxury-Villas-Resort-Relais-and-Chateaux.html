Description

Overlooking Komitsa Bay in Athos Peninsula, the awarded Avaton Luxury Villas Resort - Relais & Chateaux offers seafront accommodation with Mediterranean gardens and free WiFi access in Néa Ródha.

A private beach area is available. Ouranoupoli is 5 km away. All 16 units are air conditioned and have a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Most units include a dining area and/or terrace, while some come with private pools.

There is also a kitchen, fitted with a dishwasher. An oven, a refrigerator and stovetop are also available, as well as a coffee machine and a kettle.

There is a private bathroom with bath robes in every unit. Towels are provided. Modern culinary creations of Mediterranean and international cuisine can be tasted at Avaton Restaurant and Champagne Bar.

Guests may enjoy their dish or signature cocktail while indulging in the endless blues of the Aegean Sea.

Concierge can organize massage treatments, private transfer with limousine or helicopter, and daily cruises upon request.

Breakfast, other meals and drinks can be enjoyed at the beach bar. The area provides plenty of opportunity for water sports.

Vourvourou is 25 km from Avaton Luxury Villas Resort - Relais & Chateaux. The nearest airport is Thessaloniki Airport, 86 km from the property.
