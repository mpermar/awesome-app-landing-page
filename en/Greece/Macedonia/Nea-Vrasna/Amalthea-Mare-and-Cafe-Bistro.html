Description

Offering a bar and a sun terrace, Amalthea Mare is located in Nea Vrasna Village.

It features self-catering accommodation with free WiFi and views over the Aegean Sean and the mountain.

The nearest beach is 10 metres away. All tastefully decorated and air-conditioned studios of Amalthea open to a balcony.

Each has a flat-screen, satellite TV and a kitchenette with hobs and mini fridge.

A hairdryer is included. Some units are split level. Guests may enjoy refreshing beverages and drinks at the property’s bar.

A restaurant can be found 10 metres away. A bus stop providing connection to several parts of the area is at a distance of 150 metres, while shops can be reached within proximity.

Amphipoli Museum is at a distance of 30 km. Thessaloniki International Airport is located 65 km from the property.
