Description

Boasting an outdoor pool, hot tub and free WiFi, the all-inclusive Glavas Inn Hotel is located in Trikorfo Gerakinis Village.

Rooms offer views of the garden or the Toroneos Gulf. The beach is 80 metres away.

Opening out onto a balcony, all the guestrooms at Glavas are tastefully decorated and come with a flat-screen TV.

Each includes a fridge and air conditioning. Free toiletries and a hairdryer stock the bathroom.

Refreshments and cocktails can be enjoyed at the bar or the pool bar. Guests can enjoy traditional Greek cuisine, for lunch and/or dinner, served by the on-site restaurant and refreshing cocktails and drinks at the bar.

A buffet breakfast is prepared daily. The all-inclusive option for lunch and dinner offers Mediterranean and traditional Greek dishes made with local and homegrown produce.

Complimentary sunbeds and umbrellas surround the pool area. There is also a playground for kids.

Parasols and umbrellas at the beach are provided upon charge. Bike hire can be provided upon charge and availability.

Free shuttle service to Gerakini Village is available every evening. Glavas Inn Hotel is 65 km from Thessaloniki international Airport.

Transfers to and from the airport are available upon request with extra charge. Private parking is free and situated on the property.
