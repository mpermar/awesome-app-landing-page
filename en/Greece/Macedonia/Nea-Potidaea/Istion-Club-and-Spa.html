Description

The newly built Istion Club & Spa in NeaPotidea in Halkidiki, provides guests with 5 star accommodation services.

The spacious spa centre features a hot tub, gym, and variety of available beauty treatments, while the hotel’s restaurant offers 3 cuisine choices, traditional Greek, Italian and international.

The tastefully decorated rooms offer luxury and comfort, and feature balconies with views of the garden or the sea.

Each room is equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV, minibar, and safe. Nea Potidea is a picturesque settlement located at the entrance of the Kassandra Peninsula in Chalkidiki, about 60 km from Thessaloniki.

Combining the natural beauty of the area, the azure waters and its comforts, the area offers numerous restaurants, cafes, taverns, super markets and public services.
