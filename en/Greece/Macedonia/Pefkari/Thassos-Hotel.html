Description

Located on the beach of Pefkari, only 900 metres from Potos village, Thassos Hotel features a pool, a beach bar and a restaurant amidst its lush garden.

Its air-conditioned accommodation comes with a private balcony overlooking the surrounding mountains.

The bright and airy rooms of Thassos are modernly decorated with bright colours. Each unit is equipped with satellite TV, fridge and a safety deposit box.

The private bathroom is stocked with a hairdryer. Guests can start their day with a continental breakfast served daily at the dining area.

Coffees, light snacks and drinks can also be enjoyed at the beach bar. The on-site restaurant serves Greek and Mediterranean dishes for lunch or dinner.

Sport facilities include a tennis court and a fitness centre, while guests can also relax at the sun loungers by the pool or by the sea.

Younger guests can enjoy themselves at the children’s pool or at the playground. Thassos Hotel is situated 26 km from Ormos Prinou and 50 km from Thassos Town and Port.

The famous Chryssi Ammoudia Beach is at 40 km. Free public parking can be found nearby.
