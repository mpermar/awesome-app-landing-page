Description

With the beach on its doorstep, his hotel is located on Nea Iraklitsa Beach. It features air-conditioned apartments and an outdoor swimming pool.

Studios and apartments at Kavala Beach feature kitchenettes and large bathrooms. All have balconies with a direct view of the beach, the village and the bay.

The city of Kavala is at a distance of approximately 10 km. Free wireless internet and parking are available on site.
