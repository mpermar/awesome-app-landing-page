Description

Featuring a private beach area and a spa centre, the modern, 5-star Cronwell Resort Sermilia Ultra All-Inclusive in Psakoudia Village of Halkidiki offers a swimming pool and a roof-top bar with panoramic Aegean Sea views.

Free WiFi is available throughout. Opening to a balcony, all rooms of Cronwell will provide you with a flat-screen TV and air conditioning.

Each comes with a minibar and a safety deposit box. They also have a bathroom with a bath or shower, free toiletries, hairdryer and slippers.

At Cronwell Resort Sermilia you will find a restaurant and bars. Breakfast and buffet dinner are served at the restaurant, while free tea and pastries are offered at the "Five o'clock tea" event of the Lobby Bar, daily at 17:00.

Other facilities offered at the property include a conference centre, a communal lounge and a kids' club.

A sauna and a fitness centre are available at the spa centre. Guests may find sun loungers and umbrellas on the beach.

Macedonia International Airport is approximately 75 km away. Free private parking is possible on site.
