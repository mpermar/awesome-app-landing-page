Description

Enjoying a prime beach-front location at Fourka Beach, this 5-star accommodation offers beautiful sunset views, spacious rooms and suites, as well as wellness facilities.

Free WiFi is available in all areas. Kallithea Town is approximately 15 km away. Olympion Sunset offers stylish rooms and suites, all with a satellite, flat-screen TV and air conditioning.

They all open to a balcony or patio with sea or pool views. Bathrobes and slippers come standard.

La Mediterranee Restaurant, located in the main building and overlooking the pool and the beach, serves breakfast and dinner.

A roof garden bar and a beach bar are available for refreshing drinks. The pool restaurant serves lighter meals and salads.

A piano bar is open in the evenings to provide you with some lovely melodies and drinks.

The hotel’s wellness centre provides various treatments including shiatsu massage, sauna and steam bath.

A gym and hot tub are also featured. The scenic Afytos is 20 km away. Free private parking is possible on site.
