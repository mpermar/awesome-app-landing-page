Description

Offering an outdoor pool, Greek Pride Hotel offers accommodation in Fourka. The nearest beach is located 300 metres of the property.

Free WiFi is offered throughout the hotel and free private parking is available on site.

All units are air conditioned and include a seating and/or dining area. There is also a kitchenette in some of the units, equipped with a refrigerator.

There is a private bathroom with a hair dryer in each unit. Towels are available.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant or a drink at the bar. The lively Fourka Centre is within walking distance of the property, offering a variety of taverns, restaurants and bars.

Bicycles are provided. Guests enjoy 50% discount at the nearby amusement park,as well as discounts at local clothes stores.

The owners can help with organising excursions to Agion Oros and other nearby destinations.

Fourka is just 7 km for Poseidi where great beaches can be found. The vibrant Kallithea Village with the numerous bars and night clubs is just 10 km away.

The nearest airport is Thessaloniki Airport, located within 88 km of Greek Pride.
