Description

Located by a private sandy cove, this 5-star spa resort is on Paradise Beach. It features air-conditioned rooms with furnished balconies, and offers 3 dining outlets, free Wi-Fi and a spa relaxation indoor pool.

The modern rooms of the Royal Paradise Beach Resort & Spa come with a laptop-size safe, a 26-inch flat-screen satellite/pay TV and a minibar.

Slippers and bathrobes are standard, while some rooms offer a private outdoor hot tub.

The property has a sauna room and a hot tub, as well as massage rooms. Also available within the premises is a children’s playground, table tennis facilities and a tennis court.

Offering a selection of buffet-style, a la carte and gourmet restaurants serve international, Mediterranean and Greek dishes.

Lighter fare is available at Royal Paradise Beach Resort’s lobby and pool bars. The hotel’s 24-hour front desk can assist with tourist information and arrange tickets.

Late check-out is possible, and room service is available 24 hours a day. On-site private parking is free.
