Description

Surrounded by a lush garden with roses and palm trees, the seafront hotel Alexandra Beach Spa Resort is within walking distance from the picturesque villages of Potos and Pefkari in Thasos Island.

The 4-star luxurious resort offers comfortable and hospitable guest rooms, fully equipped with a mini bar, a safety box, satellite TV and radio, air conditioning and hairdryer.

Free Wi-Fi is available in all units. Guests can enjoy the sports facilities including a gym, billiards, tennis courts, mini golf basketball and beach volley, and then relax at the on-site spa offering a range of facial and body treatments and massages.

Children are provided with a playground and a mini club. A Greek buffet breakfast is served at the main restaurant by the pool, offering views to the sea.

Fresh fish and Greek specialties are served at lunchtime at the seaside hotel tavern.

Drinks and exotic cocktails are served at the 3 bars, where a variety of events is hosted regularly.
