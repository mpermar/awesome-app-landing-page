Description

Located only 100 metres from the sandy beach of Potos in Thassos, this hotel features a Greek restaurant.

All units offer free Wi-Fi and a balcony overlooking the garden or the Aegean Sea.

Potos accommodation is equipped with satellite TV, refrigerator and air-conditioning.

Each includes a private bathroom with shower. Few have a kitchen with stove and dining area.

The restaurant offers traditional Greek cuisine, including fresh fish that is caught daily by the fishermen of Potos.

Refreshing drinks and locally produced tsipouro (traditional drink) and ouzo are also available.

Sun loungers and umbrellas can be found by the pool without additional cost, while a childrens playground and pool is provided for young guests.

The main port of Thassos, Limenas, is 35 km away. Guests can also find taverns, bars and shops within 100 metres.
