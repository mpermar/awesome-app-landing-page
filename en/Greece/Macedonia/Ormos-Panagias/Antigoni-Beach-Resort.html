Description

Located right on the beach in Trani Ammouda in Halkidiki, Antigoni Beach Resort features individually decorated rooms with a balcony overlooking Sigiticos Gulf or the pool and garden.

It offers a spa and a buffet breakfast. The spacious rooms are elegantly decorated with bright colours and have an LCD satellite TV and minibar.

Bathrooms include bathrobes and some have a shower with hydromassage. Some rooms also provide a hot tub.

Mediterranean dishes and fresh fish are served for lunch and dinner at the restaurant.

The pool bar and the beach bar serve coffee and refreshing drinks throughout the day.

The Greek Breakfast served at the property includes local products and homemade jams.

Guests of Antigoni Beach Resort can enjoy massages and body treatments at the spa.

A fitness centre is also featured. Table tennis facilities are included and there is a childrens pool for younger guests.

Horse riding courses and water sports are also available upon charge. The village of Agios Nikolaos with bars and restaurants is 3 km away.
