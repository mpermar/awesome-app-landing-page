Description

Featuring 6 outdoor, freshwater swimming pools and a garden, Hotel Aperio offers rooms with a balcony.

It is on the beach of Paralia Katerinis and 100 metres from cafeterias and restaurants.

The air-conditioned rooms feature a TV with satellite channels and fridge. The bathroom includes a shower.

Free Wi-Fi is offered throughout. Guests can relax at the garden or sun terrace. Alternatively, they can visit Mount Olympus, 35 km away and the archaeological site and Archaeological Museum of Dion, 25 km away.

Aperio Hotel is 9 km from the lively city of Katerini and 100 km from Thessaloniki International Airport.
