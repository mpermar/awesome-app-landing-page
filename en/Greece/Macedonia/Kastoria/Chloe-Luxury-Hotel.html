Description

Chloe is a modern and elegant hotel, situated 1. 5 km from the centre of Kastoria overlooking the lake and the magnificent Vitsi Mountain.

Free Wi-Fi is available in all the hotel areas. The spacious rooms and suites are decorated in earthly colours combining the classical style with all the modern comforts.

All Chloe Hotel rooms have balconies and feature a satellite TV, a minibar, a fridge and comfortable beds with feather duvets by COCO-MAT.

Chloe serves a rich American breakfast buffet, including homemade marmalades, sweets and pies.

Guests can enjoy drinks or coffee at the café-bar or the bright terrace. A tennis court is at 100 metres and can be used at extra charge.

Room service is available 24 hours and free parking is possible. Chloe Hotel also offers meeting facilities and can accommodate conferences and events.
