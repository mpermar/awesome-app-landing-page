Description

Offering an outdoor pool with sun terrace and a restaurant, Princess Golden Beach Hotel is just 80 metres from Chrysi Ammoudia Beach.

The hotel features air-conditioned accommodation with a terrace. Free WiFi is available in all areas.

Every room and suite at Princess Golden Beach is equipped with a flat-screen TV and a mini fridge.

Some rooms offer mountain and limited sea views. The property's restaurant serves Greek and international dishes made with local products, in buffet style.

Refreshing beverages and drinks are prepared at the poolside bar. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property.

Guests may exercise and keep fit at the on-site fitness centre or relax on the sun loungers of the sun terrace by the pool.

The hotel also offers bike hire and car hire. A supermarket and a bus stop can be found 100 metres away.

Potos is 45 km from Princess Golden Beach Hotel, while Limenas Port is 15 km from the property.

Panagia Village is 5 km away and Prinos is at a distance of 30 km. Free private parking is possible on site.
