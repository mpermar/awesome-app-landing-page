Description

Colors Urban Hotel boasts modernly designed rooms in the heart of Thessaloniki. Everything the city has to offer can be found in the surrounding area.

Free WiFi is provided. The uniquely themed rooms at Colors Urban Hotel are equipped with a satellite, flat-screen TV.

Some rooms boast Thermaikos Gulf views, while other overlook the vibrant street right in front of the hotel.

A minibar is also available in every room. Refreshing drinks and cocktails can be enjoyed at the bar.

Massage treatments can be provided at extra charge. Buffet breakfast is served daily from 07:30 until 10:30, while later guests may also enjoy a rich brunch buffet.

All lively neighborhoods and main business, cultural, entertainment and shopping areas are only minutes away.

The major bus stops are just a short stroll away. Thessaloniki International Airport is 17 km away.
