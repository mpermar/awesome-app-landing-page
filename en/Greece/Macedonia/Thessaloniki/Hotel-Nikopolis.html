Description

Guest room at the Nikopolis Hotel offer views of the pool or the gardens. Each is fitted with a marble bathroom with separate shower and tub. Amenities include LCD TVs with satellite channels, air conditioning, soundproof windows, safes and mini-bars.

Guest at Nikopolis Hotel can start their day with an American breakfast buffet, served each morning in Da Vinci restaurant. Greek breakfast options are also available. Since 2016 the property has gone through a massive renovation project, starting with the award-wining Dome Real cuisine, a new concept restaurant and the Aquarella pool bar and followed by the complete renovation of the Kasai Teppanyaki & Sushi restaurant, a Japanese Nikkei cuisine restaurant in 2017. The works were completed in November of 2017 with the refurbishment of Nereau Bar and the lobby area.

High-speed WiFi is provided throughout Hotel Nikopolis. For specific requirement conferences, internet with fiber optic up to 100 Mbps is available with additional charge.

The Aqua Club Spa features a state-of-the-art fitness center, as well as indoor heated pool, sauna, steam bath, hot tub and outdoor pool of 1,700 m². For tennis fans, the hotel offers 2 tennis courts.

Hotel Nikopolis provides guests with private parking options, either free parking at an open-air area on site, or in underground and guarded parking area at an extra charge.
