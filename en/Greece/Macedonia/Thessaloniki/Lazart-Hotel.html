Description

Located right across the arts centre Moni Lazariston, Lazart Hotel offers luxurious accommodation just 2 km from the centre of Thessalokini.

Guests can spend relaxing moments at the hotel's seasonal outdoor pool or at the on-site spa centre including a hammam and a hot tub.

Lazart Hotel rooms are tastefully decorated and have elegant furnishings and parquet floors.

They are equipped with air conditioning, flat-screen satellite TV, espresso coffee machine and a safe.

All guestrooms and suites enjoy twice-daily maid service and 24-hour room service.

Guests can start their day with a rich buffet breakfast served daily by the pool.

Greek and international flavours accompanied by a carefully handpicked wine list can be enjoyed for dinner at the elegant on-site restaurant.

The Baroque Lounge Bar also serves a variety of cocktails and a wide selection of spirits.

Extra facilities include 5 multipurpose rooms with a maximum capacity of 1000 participants, a fitness centre and a 24-hour front desk.

Massage treatments can be arranged upon request. Lazart Hotel's location allows direct access to the city centre, the White Tower and Ladadika.

Thessaloniki airport is 20 km away. Free WiFi is available in the entire hotel and free parking is possible on site.
