Description

The beachfront Hotel Naias in Chanioti features comfortable self-catered accommodation and 2 swimming pools nestled in lush gardens.

Facilities include a beach bar-restaurant and children’s playground. The accommodation at Naias Hotel is tastefully decorated and spacious.

All studios and apartments are air-conditioned and include a kitchenette. Some units enjoy views of the sea or the lush gardens.

A rich breakfast buffet is served daily at the Naias restaurant. During the day guests can enjoy light dishes and drinks at the beach bar-restaurant.

A variety of cocktails are available at the pool bar. Children are well-catered for with their own swimming pool, whilst adults can enjoy a leisurely swim in the larger pool.

Right in front of the hotel you will find all the water sports you need on the lovely beach.

Chanioti’s centre is only 200 metres from Naias, and it hosts plenty of restaurants and a vibrant nightlife.
