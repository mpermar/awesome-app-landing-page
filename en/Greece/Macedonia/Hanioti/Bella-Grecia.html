Description

Located between Chanioti and Polichrono village in Chalkidiki, just 150 metres from the beach, Bella Grecia features a pool area with a snack bar and children’s pool.

There are free sun beds and umbrellas by the pool, and Wi-Fi is free in the entire property.

Bella Grecia offers air-conditioned studios and rooms with TV, large beds and furnished balconies that face the garden or the sea.

A kitchenette is available in some units. The snack bar offers a wide range of drinks, snacks and light meals.

Guests can have breakfast, or an evening drink by the poolside sun terrace. Weekly barbeques are organized.

Children will enjoy the hotel playground. A TV lounge broadcasting satellite channels is also provided.

A 15-minute walk from Chanioti centre, Bella Grecia is an excellent base for shops, restaurants and bars.

Thessaloniki and Makedonia Airport are reached within an hour’s drive. Free private parking is available on site.
