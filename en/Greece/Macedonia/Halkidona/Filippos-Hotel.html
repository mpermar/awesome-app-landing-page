Description

Hotel Filippos is a 3-star recently renovated hotel, located in Halkidona just 5 minutes from the Palace of Alexander the Great, in Pella, and 30 minutes to the sea, the great museum of Vergina and the archaeological attraction of Pella.

Free WiFi is available throughout. Rooms are designed with respect to the traditional architecture.

Each comes with a satellite TV and fridge. Filippos Hotel has a spacious parking space.

There is an outdoor park with a children's playground, a restaurant and a bar. 24 hour room service is available.
