Description

Located right on the Blue Flag awarded beach of Paradisos, the family-run Meliton Inn offers elegantly decorated rooms with a balcony.

More facilities include a children's playground. The cosmopolitan centre of Neos Marmaras is 600 metres away.

Meliton features air-conditioned units with a satellite TV and DVD player. Some units include cooking facilities, while other enjoy views over Kassandra Bay.

Breakfast is daily served at the property's dining area, including traditional pies and homemade omelets.

Guests can enjoy an array of drinks, cocktails and snacks at the beach bar of Meliton Inn.

Free sun loungers and beach umbrellas are available. Meliton Inn’s staff can also arrange sports activities in nearby locations.

Visits to Mount Athos, located 1-hour drive away, are organised by hotel staff. Transfer from Thessaloniki International Airport can be arranged at an extra charge.
