Description

Towered by green hills and surrounded by well-kept gardens, the beachfront Blue Dream Palace is built at Tripiti.

Facilities include 2 outdoor pools and an indoor heated pool with attached hot tub.

A spa centre is available. Rooms at the Blue Dream Palace have modern furnishings.

They come equipped with a flat-screen satellite TV, as well as a minibar. The private bathrooms have a bathtub and a hairdryer.

Most rooms enjoy sea views. Guests may relax at the hotel’s pools, and free sun loungers and umbrellas, while pool towels are provided.

The Palace has a tennis court and features a gym, sauna and hammam. Other leisure activities at the Blue Dream include a playroom and a children’s playground.

Greek nights are organized by the hotel. Several massage treatments are available at the spa centre.

The Blue Dream features a main restaurant serving buffet meals, as well as a seaside tavern.

The hotel offers 2 bars, one of which is situated by the poolside. The fully air-conditioned Blue Dream has a 24-hour multilingual front desk.
