Description

Located in Limenaria, in the island of Thasos, Discovery Hotel offer spacious accommodation and free WiFi.

It is only 150 metres away from the beach and features an outdoor pool. Discovery Hotel offers air-conditioned studios, apartments and villas with wooden furniture and tiled floors.

Each one has a kitchenette, a private bathroom with a hairdryer and a balcony overlooking the surrounding area.

A satellite TV is available. Guests can prepare their own meals in the privacy of their room, or use the hotel’s barbecue facilities.

The hotel's bar offers refreshing drinks and light snacks. The hotel offers a free private parking on site.
