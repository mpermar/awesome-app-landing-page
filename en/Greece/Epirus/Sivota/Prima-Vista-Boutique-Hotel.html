Description

Certain rooms have sea view and a seating area to relax in after a busy day. A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms. A flat-screen TV with satellite channels is offered. Coco mat bedding and Olive amenities are featured. Free WiFi is provided throughout.

Fine Mediterranean cuisine is served at the restaurant. Guests may enjoy their drinks at the on-site bar. Breakfast is also served at the property, while guests will also find a gym.

Parga is 13.7 miles away, and Aktion International Airport is 54.1 miles away. Trasfer from/to the airport can be arranged upon charge.
