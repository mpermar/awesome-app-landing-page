Description

The traditionally built Princess Lanassa is in the 300-year-old country home at Kostitsi, North Tzoumerka.

It takes its name after the second wife of King Pyrhos of Epirus. WiFi is free in public hotel areas.

The property has comfortable rooms, suites and chalets, all named after the relatives and associates of King Pyrrhos, and each with its special character.

Each is equipped with air conditioning and heating, bathroom with hairdryer, mini fridge and TV.

Princess Lanassa Hotel also features a swimming pool, bar and mini gym. It also provides a sauna, hammam and a hot tub upon request and upon charge.

At the hotel restaurant guests can taste a variety of specialties, traditionally prepared with local products.

A buffet breakfast is served daily. Kostitsi, at an altitude of 900 m, is just a 25 minutes drive from Ioannina and its magnificent lake.

It commands a breathtaking vista of the Tzoumerka Mountains and the Arachthos River Gorge.

Guests can visit the scenic villages of Syrrako and Kalarrytes. Ioannina airport is found 30 km from the hotel.
