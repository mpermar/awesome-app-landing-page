Description

Hotel Loukas & Apartments is located by the sandy beach of Vrachos-Loutsa, between the cities of Preveza and Parga.

It has an on-site restaurants and offers air-conditioned accommodation with balcony and free WiFi access.

Room service and bicycle rental can be provided. Fitted with tiled floors, dark-wooden furnishings and earthy colours, all rooms, studios and apartments of the Hotel Loukas feature a 32'' flat-screen TV, a laptop-size safe and a fridge.

Each unit includes a private bathroom with shower and hairdryer, while most of them also have a well-equipped kitchenette.

Breakfast, coffee and drinks are served at the Loukas caf-snack bar, offering lovely views of the Ionian Sea.

Fresh fish, homemade local dishes, as well as Mediterranean cuisine can also be enjoyed at the hotel's restaurant for lunch or dinner.

On the beach there are sun beds and umbrellas, as well as a beach volleyball court and watersport facilities.

Fish taverns and restaurants surround the hotel, while Acherontas River can also be reached within 35 km.

Loukas Hotel is 30 km away from Preveza and 28 km from Parga. The distance from Igoumenitsa Port is 67 km.
