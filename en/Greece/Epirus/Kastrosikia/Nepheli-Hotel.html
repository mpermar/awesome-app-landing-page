Description

Located in the area of Kastrosikia, Nepheli Hotel features an outdoor pool with a pool bar and a hot tub surrounded by exotic gardens.

It offers rooms with free WiFi access and it is 80 metres from the sandy beach. Rooms and suites at Nepheli are tastefully decorated with iron beds and open out to private balconies with garden views.

They are equipped with a flat-screen TV, air conditioning and minibar. In the bathroom you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Guests can enjoy exotic cocktails and delicious local flavours at the bar featuring a stone-paved terrace shaded by the lush trees.

The town of Preveza is 18 km away, while at 7 km you can visit the castle of Riniasa.
