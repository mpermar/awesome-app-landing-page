Description

Set on a green-covered hill, 400 metres away from the beach of Kanali, Villa Pappas offers self-catering apartments with panoramic views over the Ionian Sea.

It also has a rich garden with swimming pool and poolside bar. Pappas apartments are tastefully furnished and open out to a private balcony.

Each with a kitchenette, they are also equipped with a TV and air conditioning. Surrounded by palm and citrus trees, the swimming pool area offers the ideal place to relax and enjoy a drink from the bar.

Free WiFi is available throughout. There are beaches nearby the property with free of charge sunbeds and umbrellas.

In the village of Kanali, guests will find taverns serving local specialities and seaside night clubs.

Parga Town and Lefkada are both within a 25-minute drive. WiFi access and private parking are provided free of charge.
