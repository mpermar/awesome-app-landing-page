Description

Faros Apartments is located 50 metres from the beach of Mitikas, and offers air-conditioned accommodation with free WiFi and furnished balcony with unobstructed views of the Ionian Sea and the sunset.

All apartments feature direct Ionian Sea views. A kitchen with cooking facilities, fridge, oven and dining area is included in all apartments at the Faros.

Each has a flat-screen TV, safety deposit box and a hairdryer. A playground is available in the garden for younger guests.

BBQ facilities are also offered. Guests can relax in the flowered garden that features seating areas, coffee tables and board games.

Mini markets, bakery, cafe and taverns with local food can be found within short walking distance.

Ancient Nikopolis is 2 km away and Preveza Town is at a distance of 6 km. Aktion National Airport lies within 8 km away.

Lefkada is accessible by car 25 km away and Parga Town is 45 km away. Free public parking is possible on site.
