Description

Surrounded by lush greenery, the 4-star Aar Hotel & Spa features an indoor heated pool, an outdoor pool and a restaurant.

It offers elegant units with autonomous heating. Ioannina City is accessible within a 14-minute drive.

Decorated with modern furnishings, all air-conditioned rooms and suites at the Aar Hotel have a flat-screen satellite TV and free WiFi.

Each comes with a mini fridge and a safety box. Greek and Mediterranean tastes can be enjoyed at the restaurant.

Free room service is also provided. A football field, a fitness centre, a business centre and banquet facilities are also featured on site.

Younger guests can enjoy the children’s pool. A shopping mall is just across the street.

The on-site spa centre features services including sauna, beauty treatments and steamed bath.

The ancient theatre of Dodoni is at distance of 6 km, while Vrelli Museum is within 200 metres.
