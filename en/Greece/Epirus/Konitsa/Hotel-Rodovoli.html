Description

Built according to the traditional architecture, Hotel Rodovoli in Konitsa Town offers modern rooms enjoying views of the surrounding area from their balcony or patio.

It features a hot tub and a kid’s club, while Wi-Fi is offered throughout. All rooms at Rodovoli include Coco-mat bedding products and come with air conditioning and a satellite, flat-screen TV.

Decorated in earthy tones, they also have a minibar, and a bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Some have a fireplace, and other are specially designed for disabled guests. A snack bar serving local specialties and drinks can be found onsite.

Molyvdoskepasto Monastery is approximately 15 km away. The area is ideal for various activities like hiking, horse riding and canoeing.
