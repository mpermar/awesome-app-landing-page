Description

Located in a garden within a 2-minute walk from the beach of Frangokastelo, Paradisos offers a tennis court and free bicycles.

It features self-catering accommodation with free WiFi and balcony overlooking the Libyan Sea and the mountains.

All accommodation types at Paradisos feature a kitchenette with a refrigerator. Each has a TV and air conditioning.

The private bathroom comes with a shower. BBQ facilities are available in the garden.

There is a library with books and magazines as well as a children’s playground for younger guests.

The property is 100 metres from Fortress of Frangokastelo. The well-known Orthi Ammos beach is a few metres away.

Chania Airport is located within 85 km. Free private parking is possible on site.
