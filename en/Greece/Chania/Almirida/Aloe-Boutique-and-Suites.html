Description

All guest rooms features air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fridge, a coffee machine, a bath or shower and a closet. The rooms come with a private bathroom and free toiletries, and some rooms will provide you with a terrace.

Continental and buffet breakfast options are available each morning at the hotel.

Speaking Greek and English, staff are always on hand to help at the 24-hour front desk.

Chania Town is 11 miles from Aloe Boutique & Suites, while Rethymno Town is 17 miles away. The nearest airport is Souda Airport, 7 miles from the property.
