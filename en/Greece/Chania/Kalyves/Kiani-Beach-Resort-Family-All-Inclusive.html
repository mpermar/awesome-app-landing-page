Description

All guest rooms have self-controlled air conditioning, satellite TV, kitchenette with fridge, hairdryer and balcony or patio.

The large swimming pool lined with sun-beds and umbrellas enjoys the service of the beach bar for snacks and beverages and is just steps from the beach of Kiani.

The shallow beach, the children’s swimming pool, the pirate ship-shaped playground or the open theater makes this resort a haven for families with young children.

A rich buffet breakfast is served daily at the hotel’s breakfast room just a short distance from the water. The buffet restaurant Symposium offers Cretan dishes for a romantic dinner at the open-air porch.

At the beach bar next to the sea and the pool, guests can relax to the sounds of soft music and enjoy the breathtaking views, with a cocktail, juice or refreshing beverage.

The staff at the 24-hour front desk can arrange car rental to explore villages such as Kalami, Armeni and the archeological site of Aptera. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout. It is 17 miles from Chania Airport.

Policies

Children and Extra Beds
All children are welcome.
One child under 2 years is charged EUR 5 per night in a crib.
One child from 2 to 12 years is charged 50 % of the room stay per night and person in an extra bed.
One additional older child or adult is charged 70 % of the room stay per night and person in an extra bed.
Free!One child from 2 to 12 years stays free of charge when using existing beds.
Any additional children from 2 to 12 year are charged 25 % of the room stay per night per person when using existing beds.
The maximum number of cribs in a room is 1.
The maximum number of extra beds in a room is 2.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
