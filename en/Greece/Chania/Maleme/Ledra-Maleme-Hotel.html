Description

Just 150 metres from the beach, the traditionally built Ledra Maleme Hotel offers a swimming pool, poolside bar and Cretan restaurant.

It features air-conditioned studios with free Wi-Fi and views of the sea or the garden.

Studios at Ledra Maleme Hotel feature a kitchenette with cooking facilities, fridge and a coffee machine.

Each unit has a flat-screen TV. Some of the studios have a balcony. Kids can play in the children's pool or have fun in the playground.

Table tennis facilities are also provided. Platanias is just a 5-minute drive away.
