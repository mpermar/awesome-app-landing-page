Description

Within 250 metres from Gerani Beach in Chania, Vagia Mare is set amidst well-tended gardens and features a pool, a poolside snack bar and a restaurant.

It offers free Wi-Fi and self-catering units with balcony or patio. Overlooking the pool, the garden or the Cretan Sea, the studios and apartments of Vagia have a kitchenette with dining area, fridge and cooking hobs.

Each unit is decorated in earthy colours and comes with air conditioning, a safe and a telephone.

Guests can relax at the sun loungers by the pool and enjoy a cold coffee or a refreshing drink from the snack bar.

Mediterranean flavours can also be enjoyed at the on-site restaurant for lunch or dinner.

The centre of Chania lies within 15 km from Vagia Mare, while Platanias is 2 km away.

Souda Port is at 20 km and Chania International Airport is at a distance of 27 km.
