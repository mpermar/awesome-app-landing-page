Description

Aris is a family-run 2-star hotel that rests on the edge of the old town of Paleochora, just a 5- minute walk from the centre and the beaches.

Promoting art, it offers rooms with free WiFi access and views over the Libyan Sea or the garden.

A small bar, open until midnight, is available on site, while free bicycles are offered.

Fitted with marble floors and bright colours, all rooms at Aris Hotel are equipped with air conditioning, a small fridge and an electric kettle.

The modern, private bathrooms come with shower. A breakfast buffet is served between 07:30 and 11:00 and can be enjoyed in the dining area, terrace and garden, or in your room.

Packed breakfast or small self service breakfast can be prepared. Guests can enjoy massage treatments at the on-site massage room.

A satellite TV and a book library are available in the lobby. Chania Town lies within 72 km from Aris Hotel, while Souda Port is at a distance of 77 km.
