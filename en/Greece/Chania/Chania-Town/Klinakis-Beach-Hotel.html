Description

Overlooking the sea and the picturesque town of Chania, Klinakis Hotel is situated on a low hill right by the seafront.

It offers free parking and free Wi-Fi in public areas. Klinakis Hotel features spacious, sunny and airy rooms, all complete with their own private bathroom.

A fridge is provided in each, and safes are available for hire through reception.

Several rooms enjoy beautiful views of the sea from their private balconies. In the morning, the hotel serves Greek breakfast in the dining room.

Guests can enjoy a cup of coffee or a drink in the bar, the hotel’s terrace or on their private balcony.

With a convenient location just 800 metres from Chania centre, Klinakis guests enjoy easy access to the many cafes, bars and some of the finest restaurants on the island of Crete.
