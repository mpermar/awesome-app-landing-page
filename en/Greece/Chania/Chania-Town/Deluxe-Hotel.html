Description

Deluxe Hotel is set in Chania Town, 200 metres from Koum Kapi beach. Municipal Market of Chania is 300 metres from the property.

The accommodation is air conditioned and is fitted with a flat-screen TV. Some units have a seating area and/or terrace.

A fridge is also featured, as well as a coffee machine and a kettle. Bed linen is offered.

Deluxe Hotel also includes a sun terrace. Cathedral of the Presentation of the Virgin Mary is 400 metres from Deluxe Hotel, while Archaeological Museum of Chania is 500 metres away.
