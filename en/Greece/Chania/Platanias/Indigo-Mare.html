Description

Lying on the golden sandy beach of Platanias, Indigo Mare offers a swimming pool with an elegant restaurant-pool bar, a mini market and self-catering studios and apartments with private balconies.

Guests also enjoy access to a private beach area. Indigo Mare studios and apartments are tastefully designed and include a kitchenette with refrigerator, a 28" LCD TV and balcony with view to the sea, the gardens or the external part of the hotel.

The bathroom is fitted with a bath or shower, while a safe box is also included in every room.

Beach towels and sunbeds are provided upon charge. The Indigo Mare restaurant serves buffet breakfast and served dinner of a set menu, whereas the pool bar serves poolside snacks, grilled meat and poultry dishes throughout the day.

Menu items include fresh made deli sandwiches, hamburgers, pasta and a range of beverages.
