Description

Just 600 metres from the beaches of Agioi Apostoloi, Zeus Village is located in Kato Daratso in Crete.

It features spacious accommodation and an outdoor seasonal swimming pool with a terrace, sun beds and umbrellas.

Its self-catered units are fitted with a terrace or balcony with pool, garden or sea views.

They offer air conditioning, TV, kitchenette with kitchenware, refrigerator, and seating and dining area.

Some apartments include a private swimming pool. A certified Greek buffet breakfast that includes local products is served daily in the breakfast area that overlooks the hotel gardens and pool.

The on-site à la carte restaurant offers lunch, and dinner in buffet style. The snack bar provides fresh juices, drinks, coffees and light meals.

Massages can be arranged with the hotel’s spa and wellness centre. The tour desk can inform you on local attractions and excursions such as, hiking and cycling.

There is a children’s pool for the younger guests. Zeus Village offers free WiFi in public areas.
