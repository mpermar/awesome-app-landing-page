Description

Offering a pool with sun terrace, Ikaros Apartments are located in Kalamaki, just 150 metres from the beach.

They feature fully equipped units with views of the garden and the Cretan Sea. Free WiFi is available in all areas.

Fitted with 1 or 2 separate bedrooms, all air-conditioned apartments of Ikaros open to a furnished balcony or terrace.

Each consists of a living room with a flat-screen TV and an open-plan kitchenette with cooking hobs and a dining table.

Free toiletries and a hairdryer are included. Guests may relax on the sun loungers of the sun terrace by the pool or enjoy the BBQ facilities.

A well-tended garden with grassy areas is offered and a children’s playground is featured for the younger guests’ entertainment.

A restaurant, a café bar and a supermarket for the general supplies can be found within short walking distance.

The scenic town of Chania with its Venetian old harbour is at a distance of 6 km.

Souda Port is 13 km away and Ioannis Daskalogiannis International Airport is 20 km away.
