Description

The rooms have a private entrance and include a TV and a DVD player. Each has a well-equipped kitchenette with a fridge. Some rooms at Blue Beach Villas Apartments feature a dining area and a microwave oven. Air conditioning comes standard, while safes are provided upon extra charge.

Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served at the Blue Beach restaurant. The bar serves drinks and coffee with panoramic views of the mountains and sea.

A children’s playground is available for younger guests. The hotel also features a basketball court.

Hotel staff can arrange car rental and trips to the archaeological site of Knossos or to Samaria Gorge. Lively Chania city is 9.3 mi away and Chania Airport is at 6.8 mi.
