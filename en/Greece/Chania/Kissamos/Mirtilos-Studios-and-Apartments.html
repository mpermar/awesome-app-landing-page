Description

In the centre of Kastelli and 150 metres from the beach of Telonio, Mirtilos offers studios and apartments with kitchenette facilities and free WiFi access.

There are free sun beds and umbrellas by the pool. Mirtilos Studios & Apartments have private balconies overlooking the gulf of Kissamos, or the hotel’s gardens.

Each room is equipped with air conditioning, TV and refrigerator and they all have private bathrooms with shower.

Safety box is also available upon charge. A buffet breakfast is served daily at the Hotel Mirtilos, while drinks and snacks are available throughout the day.

Guests of Mirtilos will find many cafes and taverns serving fresh fish and local wine surrounding the scenic port of Kissamos.

Free private parking is available close to the hotel. Balos is 15 km away, while Elafonisi is at 42 km.
