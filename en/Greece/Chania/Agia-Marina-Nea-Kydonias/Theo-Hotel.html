Description

Just 150 metres from the sandy beach of Agia Marina, Theo Hotel features an indoor and an outdoor pool, a fitness centre and a games room with billiards.

Leisure facilities include a poolside snack bar and a restaurant with views over the Cretan Sea.

Free WiFi is available in public areas. Opening to a balcony with views over the garden or the sea, the air-conditioned rooms of Theo are elegantly decorated with dark-wooden furnishings and earthy colours.

Each unit is equipped with a fridge, a safe and a TV with satellite channels. A hairdryer is also available.

An American breakfast is served daily at the dining area, while guests can also enjoy coffees and drinks by the pool throughout the day.

Greek and Mediterranean dishes are served at the on-site restaurant. The scenic Chania Town lies within 9 km from Theo Hotel, while Souda Port is at 16 km.

The long, sandy beach of Platanias is 1. 5 km away. Free Wi-Fi is available in the pool bar area, and free parking is possible on site.
