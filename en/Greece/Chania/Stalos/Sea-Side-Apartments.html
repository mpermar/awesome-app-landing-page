Description

Sea Side Apartments are located between the sandy beach of Kato Stalos and the coastal road.

It offers self-catering accommodation with free WiFi and private balcony. It includes a large swimming pool with separate children’s section and a poolside snack bar.

Studios and apartments at Sea Side are bright and air conditioned, enjoying sea or landmark views.

They come with a dining area and a kitchenette equipped with fridge, electric kettle and coffee maker.

They also feature a living room with satellite TV and a private bathroom with hairdryer.

Guests can enjoy the sun at the free sun loungers around the pool or have a refreshing drink, a cocktail or a light meal at the poolside bar.

At 50 metres, there is a bus stop connecting to the picturesque town of Chania located 7 km away.

Chania Airport is at 24 km, while Souda Port is at 15 km. Free parking is available on site.
