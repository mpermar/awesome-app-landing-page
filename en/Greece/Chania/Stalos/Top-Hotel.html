Description

Top Hotel is built upon the hill of Psathi at Stalos village, overlooking the Cretan Sea, the White Mountains and the landscape, 10-minutes drive from Chania Town and 10 km from the port.

It offers traditional Cretan-style and continental buffet breakfast made with products from the family farm.

Top hotel combines family environment with cleanliness and safety, offering well appointed rooms with air condition, TV, radio and music system, safety box and a kettle.

The traditional Cretan-style and Continental buffet breakfast consists of products from the family farm.

A frequent shuttle service from and to the city center with urban and long-distance bus as well as taxi are all available.

Top hotel provides guests with an abundance of facilities and comforts which include a 24h reception, free Wi-Fi access, complimentary transfer from the hotel down to Stalos village and back frequently, sitting rooms with satellite TV, playground, swimming pool with sun beds, parasols and pool snack bar, a la cart restaurant and free parking space.
