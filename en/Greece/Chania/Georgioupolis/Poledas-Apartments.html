Description

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool, garden and sun terrace, Poledas Apartments is located 200 metres from the beach of Kavros in Crete.

Free Wi-Fi is offered in public areas. Offering a balcony overlooking the pool or the Aegean Sea, all air-conditioned studios include a TV with satellite channels and a bathroom with bath and free toiletries.

The kitchen comes with an electric kettle, fridge and coffee machine. Apartments Poledas is located 200 metres from the beach of Kavros in Crete.

Guests can visit the resorts of Kavros and Georgioupolis, located 500 metres and 4 km from the property.

The property is 15 km from the city of Rethymno. Chania International Airport is 50 km away.
