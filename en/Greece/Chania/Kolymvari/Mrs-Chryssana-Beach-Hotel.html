Description

Featuring an outdoor pool with sunbeds and umbrellas, Mrs. Chryssana Beach Hotel enjoys a beachfront location in Kolymbari village.

It offers air-conditioned accommodation with balconies overlooking the Cretan Sea, the garden or the pool.

Tastefully furnished and bright, the rooms of Mrs. Chryssana feature a minimal design, combining white and blue tones.

They are equipped with satellite TV. Each has a safe box and a minibar. Interconnected rooms are also available.

Free WiFi is available throughout. Guests may relax at the sun loungers of the sun terrace by the pool.

Massage therapies and treatments are offered upon charge. Refreshing cocktails may be enjoyed by the pool at the property’s bar.

A selection of dishes for lunch and dinner is served at the à la carte restaurant.

The hotel is located next to a long pebble beach, where you can enjoy the peaceful surroundings.

Platanias Village with its restaurant and café bars is a 10-minute drive away. The scenic Chania Town is 22 km away and and Chania International Airport is 36 km away.
