Description

Just 30 metres from the beach in Loutra Edipsou, 9 Queens Spa Hotel features a wellness centre and an outdoor pool with its own natural, hot springs.

It offers modernly decorated accommodation overlooking the Evoikos Gulf, and free Wi-Fi.

Opening to a balcony, all air-conditioned rooms and suites include a flat-screen TV and minibar.

Bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries are provided. Fitted with dark-wood furnishings, suites also come with a fireplace and double spa bath.

An indoor pool with natural thermal water and hot tubs are available at extra charge in the wellness centre of 9 Queens.

A wide variety of massage treatments can be arranged on request. Guests at 9 Queens can start their day with an American breakfast.

Snacks and drinks are served at the on-site bar or at the seating area, by the fireplace.

Taverns and a mini market can be found within 100 metres. Within 20 km you can visit the village of Rovies with its pebbled beach and traditional, seaside taverns.
