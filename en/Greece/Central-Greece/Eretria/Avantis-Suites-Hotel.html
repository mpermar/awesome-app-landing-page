Description

Situated by the sea and only 700 metres from the centre of Eretria, Avantis Suites Hotel offers studios and suites with views of South Evoikos Gulf.

Free WiFi is offered in all areas. The air-conditioned units of Avantis have a kitchenette with cooking hobs and a fridge.

They also have a TV, while each bathroom includes a hairdryer, bathrobes and free toiletries.

Buffet breakfast is served each morning and includes traditional delicacies. The à la carte restaurant Neireides serves Mediterranean and local dishes, as well as a selection of wines, by the sea.

Water sports, sun beds and showers are available on the private beach in front of the hotel.

Avantis Suites Hotel features a hydrotherapy pool with deck chairs, as well as a children's pool.

Guests can take horse-riding lessons, or participate in various one-day excursions and cruises to the neighbouring islands.

Hiking, cycling and wine tours can be arranged upon charge. Avantis Suites Hotel is 21 km away from Chalkida, and approximately 50 km away from Athens.
