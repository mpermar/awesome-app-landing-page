Description

Situated on the outskirts of Arachova Village, with beautiful views of Delphi Valley and Parnassos Mountain, Domotel Anemolia Mountain Resort offers a heated pool, a fitness centre and a sauna.

WiFi is offered throughout. Guests can relax in stylish and spacious rooms and enjoy breathtaking views from their private balconies.

They can enjoy a great start of the day with a complimentary buffet breakfast. Domotel Anemolia Mountain Resort is the perfect base for all-year-round outdoor activities.

In the winter, guests can head to the nearby skiing resort and in the summer, the area is great for biking and hiking.

The hotel also offers free shuttle service to and from Arachova during the winter weekends.

The heated indoor pool is an ideal place to relax and enjoy a leisurely swim. It also offers picturesque views of Delphi Valley.

Those who wish to burn off some more energy can make use of Anemolias fully equipped fitness centre, before relaxing in the sauna.

At the end of a long day, guests can enjoy a warm drink in front of the open fireplace and some homemade food in Domotel Anemolia Mountain Resort's cozy restaurant.
