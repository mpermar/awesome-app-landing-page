Description

Chrissa Camping Rooms & Bungalows is spread amphitheatrically on a hill with unobstructed views to the Corinthian bay.

It offers accommodation with air conditioning, while facilities include a restaurant.

Delphi Village and Itea Town are approximately 7 km away. Free WiFi is available throughout.

Opening to a balcony, all rooms and bungalows of Delphi have Corinthian Bay, garden or mountain views.

They have air conditioning, mini fridge, free parking place just out of the room.

Chrissa Camping offers a restaurant with views to the sea where guests can taste traditional Greek food surrounded by olive trees.

Some of the ingredients come straight from the garden. Drinks and refreshing beverages are served at the bar.

Guests may relax at the sun loungers by the seasonal pool or visit the mini market for the general supplies.

A garden with olive trees and a children’s playground for the younger guests’ entertainment are available.

A football and a basketball court can be found 300 metres away. The scenic Arachova Village with several shops and taverns is 18 km away.

The picturesque village of Chrisso can be reached within a short drive. Free private parking is possible on site.
