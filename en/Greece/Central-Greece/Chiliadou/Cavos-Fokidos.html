Description

Boasting an outdoor pool and a well-tended garden, Cavos Fokidos is located just 150 metres away from Chiliadou Beach.

It offers self-catering accommodation opening out to a furnished balcony with Corinthian Gulf views.

Free Wi-Fi is available throughout. All air-conditioned units at Cavos are tastefully decorated with dark-wood furnishings and earthy tones.

Each comes with a kitchenette and dining table. A seating area with satellite, flat-screen TV is also available.

Guests can start their day with the continental or English breakfast served daily.

Later, they can enjoy a light meal or a refreshing drink from the on-site snack bar.

The picturesque town of Nafpaktos is 7 km away and Trizonia islet can be reached in 10 km.

Patras City lies within 25 km. Free private parking is available in the premises.
