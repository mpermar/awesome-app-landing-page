Description

Located in the magnificent south bay of Evia island, this hotel offers you the simplicity and peace of a typical fishing village.

Marmari Bay Hotel is built on a beautiful hill 350 metres from the port of the village.

The grand building dominates the endless blue horizon of Marmari Bay. The hotel offers a wealth of facilities, such as an on-site gift shop, pool bar and free transfers to the nearby beaches of Zastani, Kokkini and Fijias.

Business guests will appreciate use of the conference room, able to seat up to 100 delegates.

At the end of your day, you can taste Italian cuisine at the a la carte La Veranda Restaurant.

In the evening, you can enjoy a cocktail by the pool, while Greek evenings with Greek cuisine and live Greek music take place regularly.
