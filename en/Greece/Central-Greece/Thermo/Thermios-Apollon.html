Description

Situated in Thermo Town, Hotel Thermios Apollon features a lounge with fireplace and a traditional restaurant.

Its outdoor pool area offers a sun-lounger terrace, and free Wi-Fi is available throughout.

The rooms are simply furnished and equipped with heating and air conditioning. Each also offers a minibar and a TV.

All rooms have private balcony overlooking the pool or the surrounding countryside.

Breakfast is served daily at the hotel’s lounge area. Thermios Hotel’s traditional restaurant serves home-cooked Greek dishes.

The hotel allocates a spacious private parking area, which guests are free to use.
