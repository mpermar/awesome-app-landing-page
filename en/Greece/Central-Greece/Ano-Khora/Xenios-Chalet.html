Description

Located in Ano Chora of Oreini Naupaktia, Xenios Chalet is a complex of 13 log Finnish chalets, surrounded by a pine and chestnut forest.

It offers free WiFi and a terrace. Made entirely of wood, the heated chalets include a dining area and seating area.

A plasma TV is featured, along with a fridge and wardrobe. Complimentary toiletries, hairdryer and shower can be found in the bathroom.

The restaurant at Xenios prepares local cuisine whereas the bar offers various beverages and sweets.

A breakfast consisting of homemade products such as jams, breads and cakes, is served in the breakfast area that provides panoramic views of the ridges of Pindus.

A children’s playground is situated in the well-kept garden area. Guests can enjoy outdoor activities such as cycling, hiking and rafting at Evoinos River which is 20 km away.

Xenios Chalet is 53 km from the sandy Monastiraki beach and 50 km from the picturesque town of Nafpaktos.
