Description

4-star Thalatta Seaside Hotel is 50 metres from Agia Anna Beach in Evia and offers a spa and breakfast by the pool.

Rooms have a furnished balcony overlooking the Aegean Sea. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout.

This design hotel offers spacious and stylish rooms featuring minimalist furnishings and light colours.

Each air-conditioned room includes an LCD TV and minibar. A seating area with a sofa is included.

A breakfast buffet is served daily by the pool. Asado Restaurant offers Mediterranean food while the pool bar serves refreshing cocktails.

The fully equipped spa offers treatments such as aromatherapy. There is a well-equipped gym that is open throughout the day and a hair salon at Hotel Thalatta Seaside.

A children’s pool is available for younger guests. Agia Anna Beach has shops and traditional taverns with local food.
