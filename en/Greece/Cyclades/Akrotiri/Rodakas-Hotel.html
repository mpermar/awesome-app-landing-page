Description

Located 700 metres from the archaeological site of Akrotiri, Rodakas Hotel features an outdoor pool and a poolside restaurant.

It offers accommodation with free WiFi. Red Beach is around 1. 3 km away. Rooms and studios here will provide you with a flat-screen TV, air conditioning and a balcony.

There is also a refrigerator. Private bathrooms comes with a shower. Local specialities are prepared for lunch and dinner at the a la carte restaurant by the pool.

Drinks and cocktails can be enjoyed at the bar. At 30 metres you will find a bus stop.

If you feel like visiting the surroundings, check out the Folk Art Museum within 200 metres.

Thira Airport is located 7 km from the property. Free private, on-site parking is provided.
