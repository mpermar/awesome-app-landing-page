Description

This charming hotel offers sea and caldera views from its terrace. The family owners at Kokkinos Villas are available 24 hours a day to help with travel arrangements or provide personal recommendations.

On the beach, there is a scuba diving school for the more adventurous. For those who would like to relax, traditional Greek and local specialties are available at Remezzo tavern.

Akrotiri is a small traditional village located at the southern part of Santorini, known for its light-house, its old castle and the unique ancient ruins of 1500 B.C.
