Description

Boasting an outdoor pool and a poolside bar, Hotel Mathios is a family-run hotel located near the Red and White Beach, in the area of Akrotiri.

A restaurant serving Greek cuisine is featured on site. Free WiFi access is offered throughout.

Set among the green vineyards of the peaceful village of Akrotiri, the air-conditioned rooms of Hotel Mathios come with a satellite TV, minibar and a private bathroom with hairdryer.

Each unit opens to a balcony. The famous Red Beach is 2 km from the property, while the scenic Oia is 22 km away.

Ormos Athinios Port lies within 8. 5 km away and Santorini Airport is at a distance of 12 km.
