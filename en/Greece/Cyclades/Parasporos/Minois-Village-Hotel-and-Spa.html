Description

Decorated in light tones, all units are equipped with a 32” flat-screen, satellite TV, air conditioning and mini fridge. Free toiletries can be found in the bathroom.

Start your day with a Greek buffet breakfast including local cheese and sausages, homemade pies and jams, and a variety of fresh fruit. At the bar by the pool, you can enjoy cocktails, fresh juices and snacks. Avli Restaurant serves Greek and Mediterranean cuisine.

Parikia, the capital and port of Paros, is 1.9 mi away and Paros Airport is 3.7 mi away. A bus stop can be found within 1,150 feet.

Experiences

06/18/2017

Beautiful hotel with cracking views

"Minois is a beautiful hotel with an absolutely stunning view. The rooms were bright and clean, with very comfortable beds. The breakfast was plentiful with a good range of food which was replenished frequently. It was a bonus to be able to eat it whilst looking out at the wonderful view. The pool was big enough for our needs and due to the time of year we went, it wasn't overly busy although I can imagine at peak times it may be overrun with children. The staff were very friendly and eager to help, particularly Lorenzo who provides the shuttles bus service to the Port and transfer to the airport. The hotel is around an hour's walk from the main port in Parikia, although the hotel does offer a free shuttle service twice a day. We liked the quietness and remoteness of the hotel and didn't mind the walk in everyday, however, the isolation may not be for everyone and if we'd gone later in summer, I don't think I'd have fancied the walk in even hotter weather!"

07/12/2015

We were two families with two teenagers and two younger kids.

"We were two families with two teenagers and two younger kids. The Minois Village was outstanding. It was quiet and relaxed and offered great, friendly service in a stunning environment. The pool area was lovely and was great for lounging and for the kids (in the pool all the time). The breakfast buffet was fresh, and appealing and included anything that you could possibly desire for breakfast. The staff was friendly and helpful. The location was good - a little bit off the beaten track, but peaceful and quiet and so beautiful. I would recommend a rental car if you want to see other parts of the Island. We had a car and went all over the island. Overall, our stay was wonderful and we all can't wait to go back."
