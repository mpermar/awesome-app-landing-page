Description

Situated within a 5-minute drive of Naousa fishing village, Surfing Beach Village Paros is right on the beach and features an outdoor pool surrounded by a furnished sun terrace.

Guests will find an on-site restaurant, a snack bar and a mini market. Free WiFi access is provided in the public areas.

Surrounded by trees and flowerbeds, all rooms, bungalows and apartments at Surfing Beach Village are decorated according to traditional Cycladic style.

Each is equipped with air conditioning, fridge and TV. The complex also features a children's playground and a surfing and diving club.

Free transfer from/to the port or airport can be arranged on request. Paroikia Town and Port are 15 km away, while the sandy beach of Kolympithres is 8 km from Surfing Beach Village Paros.
