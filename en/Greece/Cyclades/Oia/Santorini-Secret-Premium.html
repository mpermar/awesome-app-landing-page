Description

Authentic Cycladic architecture fused with contemporary, boho-chic decor dominates the interiors of the suites. It features unobstructed views of the Caldera and Volcano from its private balconies'. Each suite includes a private bathroom with free toiletries, an outdoor hot tub and free WiFi. At the accommodation every room is fitted with a seating area and a king size bed dressed in designer linen and signature ‘Secret’ mattress.

A continental breakfast is served each morning at the property.

The nearest airport is Santorini (Thira) Airport, 6.8 miles from the hotel.
