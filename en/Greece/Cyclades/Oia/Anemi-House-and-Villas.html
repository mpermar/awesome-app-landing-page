Description

Perched on the caldera cliff side in Oia in Santorini, Anemi House offers panoramic views of the Aegean Sea.

Including a sun terrace, garden and balcony, it is 10 metres from the famous sunset spot of Saint Nikolas Castle.

The property combines the unique architecture of white-washed Santorinian cave houses with modern facilities.

It features free Wi-Fi and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. The fully equipped kitchen includes a microwave and coffee machine.

There is also a large living room with dining area and a bathroom with shower and free toiletries.

Anemi House is 50 metres from restaurants and cafeterias. Fira, with its Prehistoric Museum and the Archaeological Museum is 10 km away.
