Description

Overlooking the Aegean sea, Archipelagos Hotel is situated 10 km away from the town and the old port of Mykonos, by the beach of Kalo Livadi.

Facilities include a pool and a wellness studio. Archipelagos Hotel, a member of the Small Luxury Hotels of the World is located by the sea.

It is inspired by the colours and light of the Cycladic landscape. It is built amphitheatrically and with full respect for the traditional Mykonian architecture.

You can relax under an umbrella on the sandy beach, enjoy the crystal-clear water of the Aegean or try your skills in one of the water sports offered here.

An independent building houses the reception and lobby. You will also find the lounge with satellite TV and bar, restaurant, snack bar and large verandas with a breathtaking view of the Aegean.

Overlooking the area is a traditional Mykonian-style church, which adds to the beauty and individuality of this island.
