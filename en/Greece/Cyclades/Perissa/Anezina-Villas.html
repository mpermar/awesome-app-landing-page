Description

Along with wrought-iron beds and warm colors, the rooms, studios and apartments of Anezina have a fridge and a TV with satellite channels. Each unit includes a private bathroom with a hairdryer, while the studios and apartments also feature a well-equipped kitchenette.

Guests can start their day with a continental or a full English breakfast served daily at the dining area. Coffee, drinks and light meals can also be enjoyed at the poolside snack bar throughout the day.

Anezina Villas is a short walk from the center of Perissa and 7 miles from Ormos Athinios Port. Santorini National Airport and the capital, Fira, are 9 miles away. Car rental services are provided and free public parking is available nearby.
