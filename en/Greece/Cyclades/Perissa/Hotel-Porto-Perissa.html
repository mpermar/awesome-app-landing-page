Description

Located in Perissa, Hotel Porto Perissa is just 50 metres from the famous black-sand beach and features air-conditioned rooms.

It has a swimming pool and a children's swimming pool. The rooms of Porto Perissa are traditionally decorated.

They are equipped with a flat-screen TV, hairdryer and safety box and they all have balconies or terraces with pool views.

Some units are located at the semi-basement. Guests can start their day with a buffet breakfast served in the dining area.

Guests also have access to a lounge with TV, while a library is also at their disposal.

Free umbrellas and sunbeds are found by the pool. Free WiFi is provided. The village of Perissa offers many restaurants, bars and shops.

A bus stop and super market lie within a short walking distance. Fira, the islands capital, is within a 15-minute drive from Hotel Perissa.
