Description

Anemomiloi Andros offers pet-friendly accommodation in Andros. The hotel has an outdoor pool and year-round outdoor pool, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Free private parking is available on site. Rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV.

Some units feature a seating area where you can relax. You will find a coffee machine in the room.

Every room is equipped with a private bathroom fitted with a bath or shower. For your comfort, you will find bathrobes and free toiletries.

Anemomiloi Andros features free WiFi throughout the property. Beach towels are provided.

You will find a shared lounge at the property. Homemade breakfast prepared with local products and adjusted on guest's preferences is served daily in the pool area.

Bike hire is available at this hotel and the area is popular for hiking. The hotel also offers car hire.

Archaeological Museum of Andros is 500 metres from Anemomiloi Andros, while Museum of Contemporary Art of Andros is 500 metres from the property.
