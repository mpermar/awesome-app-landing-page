Description

Adonis Hotel & Apartments is in the vibrant town of Naoussa, 30 metres from Piperi Beach and 500 metres from the centre.

Most of its rooms enjoy views over the bay of Naoussa and the open sea beyond. Adonis offers carefully styled rooms and studios that reflect the Cycladic architecture.

Four-poster beds and sofas, as well as a kitchenette separated with an arched wall are featured.

All units have free Wi-Fi connection, air conditioning and a satellite TV. At Adonis Hotel & Apartments guests can enjoy continental breakfast at an extra cost.

Parikia port is 12 km away and the airport is approximately 20 km away. The bus stops right next to the hotel, and connects to the beaches of Santa Maria and Kolibithres.
