Description

Hotel Naoussa is facing the bay of Naoussa, just 10 metres from the beach and 5 metres from the bus stop.

Guests enjoy free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel and quick access to the bustling centre of Naoussa.

Hospitable and clean, the rooms at Hotel Naoussa boast incredible views to the bay of Naoussa.

Each is air-conditioned, and has a private bathroom with WC/shower, a coffee/tea maker and a refrigerator.

Guests can start their day with a homemade breakfast prepared with fresh, local ingredients and served each morning at the hotel’s breakfast lounge.

Also available is a bar featuring satellite TV. Private parking is provided and is free of charge.

The friendly reception offers tourist information and rental services. Excursions to nearby islands can also be organized upon request.
