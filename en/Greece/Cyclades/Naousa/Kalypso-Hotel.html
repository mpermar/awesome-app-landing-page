Description

Situated on Agii Anargiri Beach, just a short walk from the centre of Naoussa, Hotel Kalypso offers a picturesque courtyard and stunning views of the sea.

Surrounded by tamarisk trees, the family-run Kalypso Hotel offers a warm and friendly welcome to its guests.

Enjoy clean and spacious rooms, equipped with satellite TV , air condition and private balconies that overlook the beach.

Check your emails using the free wireless internet access. Relax in Kalypso’s charming courtyard and read a book, or enjoy a coffee.

Savour a tasty complimentary buffet breakfast, which can also be enjoyed on the beach.

Take a leisurely 10-minute walk to the centre of Naoussa and explore its picturesque streets.

You can also rent a bike or car from Kalypso and explore further, whilst making use of the free private parking.

Kalypso’s friendly and helpful staff are at hand to make sure you have everything you need for an enjoyable holiday.
