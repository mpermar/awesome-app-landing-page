Description

Syros Atlantis Hotel is a minute walk from the sandy beach of Vari and 8 km from Ermoupolis and the imposing Frankish old town of Ano Syros.

Respecting the Cycladic style, the family-run Syros Atlantis offers traditionally elegant accommodation and a colorful garden.

Surrounded by palm trees, the suites of Syros Atlantis offer air conditioning, a 32'' flat-screen TV and a fridge.

Each unit features a modern private bathroom with shower and free toiletries, and an indoor seating area.

They all open to a spacious furnished balcony with view of the sea or the surrounding area.

Homemade breakfast buffet including local specialties, can be enjoyed daily at the colourful garden, where Syros Atlantis Coffee House is also featured.

Room service is provided on a 24-hour basis and there is a 24-hour front desk at the property.

Tailor-made services include boat tours around the island and guided tours at Ermoupolis and Ano Syros.

Free WiFi access is provided in all rooms and public hotel areas. The seaside Galissas Village is 12 km away and Agathopes Beach is at a distance of 9 km.
