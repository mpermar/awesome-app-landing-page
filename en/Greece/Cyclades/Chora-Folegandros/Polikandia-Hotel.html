Description

Fitted with built-in or wrought-iron beds, all air-conditioned units include a TV, espresso coffee machine, fridge and kettle. They open to a furnished balcony with views to the pool and the blossomed garden or to Folegandros Town. Some accommodation types also come with a hydromassage shower.

Guests at Polikandia can start their day with a buffet breakfast, served at the shaded area by the pool or in the privacy of their room. An inside bar with a satellite TV is available.

Polikandia Hotel offers free transfer from Karavostasis Port, at 1.9 miles. A bus stop and public parking space can be found at 150 feet from the property.
