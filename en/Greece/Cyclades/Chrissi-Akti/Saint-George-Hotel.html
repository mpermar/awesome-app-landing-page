Description

Quietly located in New Golden Beach of Paros, the beachfront, 4-star Saint George Hotel has a beautiful pool with a poolside bar, overlooking the Aegean Sea.

Its air-conditioned rooms offer a private balcony and free Wi-Fi access. The famous Chryssi Akti Beach is 700 metres away.

The rooms of Saint George are decorated in natural colours and include traditional Cycladic features.

They are fitted with a satellite TV, laptop safe box and hairdryer. Most rooms enjoy views to the Aegean Sea.

Guests can enjoy traditional dishes or fresh fish and local wine at the poolside restaurant offering magnificent views to the neighbouring island of Makronisi.

Parikia port is a 20-minute drive from Saint George. The lively town of Naousa, famous for its nightlife, is within a 15-minute drive.
