Description

The Porto Fira Suites feature uniquely-shaped king size beds, wireless internet and satellite TV. Each unit has a furnished balcony or terrace overlooking Santorini’s world-famous sunsets.

An airport shuttle service is provided and the helpful staff is happy to arrange bike or car rental. Free Wi-Fi internet access is available throughout the property.

Porto Fira Suites’ location close to Fira provides the perfect base for walks around the old quarter of Fira or boat excursions to the volcano and the island of Nea Kameni.
