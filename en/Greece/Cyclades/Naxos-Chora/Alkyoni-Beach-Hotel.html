Description

The Alkyoni Beach Hotel is a beautiful resort boasting traditional Cycladic architecture and favourably located on St.

Georges Beach and only a 10-minute walk from Naxos Town. The rooms of Alkyoni Beach Hotel are bright and airy.

Most of them have sea views, while the rest look out on to the gardens or the pool.

They all come with air conditioning, fridge, satellite TV and private balcony or terrace.

Enjoy a refreshing dip in the pool and soak up the atmosphere around it. Take a relaxing walk in the lush, beautifully maintained grounds on the way to the beach.

Use the free Wi-Fi access to check your emails and surf the internet. Ask at the reception for advice when planning events and trips.

Hotel Alkyoni Beach also offers a car rental facility for excursions. Return for excellent, homemade Mediterranean food at the restaurant and relax with a drink at the bar.

Close by you will find many beachfront cafs and bars. A sports club offers the opportunity to try water sports, including windsurfing lessons.
