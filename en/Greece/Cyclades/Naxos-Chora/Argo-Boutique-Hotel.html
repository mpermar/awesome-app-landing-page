Description

Argo Hotel is a small family-run hotel situated in a quiet area of Naxos Town, only 100 metres from the beach.

A multilingual reception, free internet corner and swimming pool are available. Fitted with Cocomat mattresses, the elegantly decorated rooms and suites are equipped with air conditioning, fridge and satellite TV.

Each unit includes a modern bathroom with hairdryer, while some also come with a spa bath, an hydromassage shower cabin or an outdoor hot tub.

The centre of Naxos is 400 metres away. Free WiFi access is provided in public areas.
