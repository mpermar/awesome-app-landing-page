Description

All air-conditioned rooms of Myconian Imperial have modern décor and balconies with superb views of Elia Beach. They are equipped with an LCD satellite TV, DVD/CD player and safety box. Some feature outdoor hot tubs or private pools.

A rich American breakfast is offered at Meltemi. Fine Mediterranean dishes are served at the Celebrities a la carte restaurant. Guests also have the choice of 2 cocktail bars and a beach bar.

Mykonos Airport is around 6.2 miles away from the resort. The picturesque town of Mykonos with whitewashed, Cycladic-style houses, bars and shops is less than a 20-minute drive away.
