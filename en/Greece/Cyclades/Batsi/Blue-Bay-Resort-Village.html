Description

Just 70 metres from Delavogias Beach in Batsi, Blue Bay Hotel features a pool with a hot tub, a sea-view sun terrace and a poolside snack bar.

It offers a restaurant, free Wi-Fi in all areas and air-conditioned units with a balcony overlooking the Aegean Sea.

Tastefully decorated with dark-wooden furnishings and wrought-iron beds, the rooms and studios of Blue Bay have air conditioning, a fridge and an LCD TV with satellite and cable channels.

Each unit includes a private bathroom with hairdryer and free toiletries, while the studios also feature a well-equipped kitchenette.

A buffet breakfast is served daily at the dining area, while guests can also enjoy drinks and light meals at the poolside snack bar.

Greek and Mediterranean flavours are served at the on-site restaurant for lunch or dinner.

Blue Bay Hotel is situated 8 km from Gavrio Port and 1. 5 km from the centre of Batsi.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can arrange car rental to explore Andros Town at 32 km and Chryssi Ammos Beach at 6 km.
