Description

Located on the beachfront in Glyfa on the island of Paros, Glyfa Village offers luxury, self-catering apartments with views of the Aegean Sea.

The apartments also have a private terrace. Each accommodation is air-conditioned and features outdoor furniture.

There is a living area, bathroom with bathrobe and slippers and a kitchen equipped with a stovetop, refrigerator and kitchenware.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at Glyfa Village. Grocery deliveries can also be arranged upon request.

Guests have access to a private beach and free WiFi access as well as free private on-site parking is available.

The centre of Drios is only a 10-minute drive away and Paros Airport is located 7 km from the accommodation.
