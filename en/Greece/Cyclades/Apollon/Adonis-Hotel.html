Description

Situated in the seaside Apollon Village of Naxos, just a 2-minute walk from the beach, Adonis Hotel offers air-conditioned rooms with furnished balconies overlooking the Aegean Sea or gardens.

A garden with organic vegetables, which guests can visit and take part at the agricultural tasks, is located 50 metres away.

Cycladic-style Adonis’s comfortable rooms include satellite TV and fridge. Free Wi-Fi is available in all rooms.

Breakfast is served daily in the hotel’s restaurant, where guests can also enjoy freshly prepared lunch and dinner cooked with fresh, biological ingredients straight from the property's garden.

The 24-hour front desk can provide rental services, can arrange transfer or offer travel information on Naxos attractions and nearby beaches.

Hotel organizes Greek language courses and hiking to the ancient Mycenaean Tomb in Koronida.

Naxos Airport is at 40 km. The ancient Kouros statue of Dionysos is only a 10-minute walk.

An ancient castle, on Kalogeros mountain, is 1 hour's hiking away from the hotel.
