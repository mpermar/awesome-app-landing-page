Description

Dionysos Seaside Resort is located right at Mylopotas Beach. This 4-star property features elegantly decorated rooms, offering a view to the sea, pool or garden.

A tennis court, 2 swimming pools, a beach bar and the Almyra by the Sea Restaurant are enjoyed by guests.

All rooms and suites offer a balcony or terrace and come with air conditioning, free toiletries, a satellite TV and a mini fridge.

Free WiFi is also available throughout. A pool bar serving drinks and refreshing beverages is featured.

Enjoying a seafront location and a luxurious ambience, the à la carte, Mediterranean-cuisine Almyra by the Sea Restaurant prepares dishes with products from the property's organic garden.

Guests may swim in the hotel's pools, relax at the sun loungers or engage in fitness games with a gym instructor by the beach.

A private beach area and a fitness centre are offered. Shuttle service is provided upon charge.

More activities include trx training, pilates and aqua training. Offering a 24-hour front desk, the highly trained staff is readily available to assist guests, whether it be a car rental or tourist information or room service.
