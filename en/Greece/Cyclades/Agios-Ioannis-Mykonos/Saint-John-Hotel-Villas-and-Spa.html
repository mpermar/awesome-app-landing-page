Description

The elegant rooms offer sea or garden views. They come with many comforts, such as free Wi-Fi, cable and satellite TV, CD/DVD player, and tea and coffee maker.

A piano bar, 3 gourmet restaurants and 2 other bars offer guests different dining and drinking opportunities from casual to romantic.

The Aphrodite Spa & Beauty Center provides 5 treatment rooms. The state-of-the-art facilities include saunas, hammam and indoor heated pools.

Mykonos Airport is 2.5 miles away, and the new Mykonos Port is 4 miles away. The bustling Mykonos Town (Chora) is 2.5 miles away. Free private parking is available.
