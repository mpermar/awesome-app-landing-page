Description

Each of the elegant suites and rooms is air-conditioned and includes a sitting area, and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and DVD player. Guests are greeted with a welcome drink, fruit basket and freshly cut flowers. Korres toiletries can be found at the private bathroom.

Guests wake up to a rich breakfast buffet. La Veranda serves innovative Mediterranean cuisine in a romantic atmosphere. Signature cocktails and refreshing drinks are served at the pool bar.

Palladium guests receive free round-trip transportation to Mykonos Airport and the port, or Mykonos Town upon request. Free wired and wireless internet is available throughout the property. Other facilities include a fitness center with massage rooms.
