Description

The Aqua Marine Spa offers revitalizing and thalasso-therapy treatments, as well as honeymoon packages.

The luxuriously renovated Petasos Beach combines esthetically perfect premises with breathtaking nature. Its innovatively designed rooms offer stunning views of the Aegean Sea.

All accommodations are equipped with Wi-Fi internet access. Deluxe and superior rooms also feature a spa bath and steam cabin.

There are 2 restaurants within the premises of the Resort, with a great view of the Aegean sea.
