Description

Each with a cheerful décor, the elegant rooms open to terraces or balconies with a view of the lush gardens or the main pool. All the rooms and suites are luxuriously furnished and equipped with a flat-screen satellite TV and a well stocked minibar.

A rich and varied buffet breakfast is served daily until as late as noon. Guests may enjoy lunch, cocktails or dinner at the poolside restaurant.

Hotel guests can take advantage of the hotel's spa facilities, relax in the steamy sauna and soak in the hot tub. There is also a tennis court and a gym.

The hotel is 2.5 miles from Santorini International Airport, 6 miles from Athinios port and 6 miles from the capital, Fira. The ruins of Ancient Thira are on a hilltop which is a short distance above the hotel.
