Description

Just 30 metres from the black sandy Kamari Beach in Santorini, Blue Waves features a pool, a sun terrace and a poolside snack bar.

It offers free WiFi in public areas and accommodation overlooking the Aegean Sea or the mountain.

Opening to balconies, all suites, studios and apartments of Blue Waves have a kitchenette with dining area and mini fridge, while some include cooking hobs.

Each air-conditioned unit has a private bathroom stocked with a hairdryer. Guests can relax at the sun loungers by the pool and enjoy a drink or a light meal at the on-site snack bar or the terrace.

Bars, restaurants and mini market are within a short walk from the property. The lively Fira Town is located 10 km from Blue Waves, while the seaside Perissa is 13 km away.

Ormos Athinios Port is at 9 km. Car rental services can be provided and free parking is possible on site.
