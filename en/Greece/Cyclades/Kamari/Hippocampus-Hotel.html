Description

The accommodations at Hippocampus feature light-wood furnishings and wall paintings. All air-conditioned rooms have a balcony overlooking the pool or the gardens. They include a satellite TV, safe and refrigerator.

Guests can start their day with a buffet breakfast. Many shops, taverns and bars are found within easy reach from the property.

A local scuba diving school for diving enthusiasts is located close to Hippocampus Hotel. Free Wi-Fi is available in the public areas of the hotel and free parking is available on site.

Policies

Children and Extra Beds
All children are welcome.
Free!All children under 2 years stay free of charge when using existing beds.
One child under 2 years is charged EUR 5 per person per night in a crib.
One older child or adult is charged EUR 15 per person per night in an extra bed.
The maximum number of extra beds/cribs in a room is 1.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
