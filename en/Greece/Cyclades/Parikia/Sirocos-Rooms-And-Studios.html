Description

Siroco's Rooms And Studios are situated in a quiet area within walking distance of Parikia, the beach and nightlife spots.

It features an outdoor pool with sun-lounger terrace, as well as a flower-filled courtyard and a coffee shop with several breakfast and snack options.

This traditional property is typically designed and comprises of various room and studio types which are available all year round.

The area provides modern amenities and entertainment possibilities in the vicinity, as well as excellent transport links with the bus station nearby.
