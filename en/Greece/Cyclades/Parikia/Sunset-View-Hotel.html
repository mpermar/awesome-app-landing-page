Description

Situated less than a 10-minute walk from Paros Port, Sunset View features a pool and a lounge snack bar serving meals.

It offers accommodation with views over the Aegean Sea and the sunset, or the pool.

Guests are offered free 2-way port transfer. Fitted with anatomic mattresses, all air-conditioned units at the Sunset are minimally decorated in soft colours and include a fridge, a TV and hairdryer.

Each unit opens to a shared or private balcony or terrace. Free WiFi access is available throughout.

Breakfast is served by the pool with views of the whitewashed houses of Parikia. The sun terrace features free sun loungers and umbrellas.

A childrens pool is provided for younger guests. Airport shuttle can be provided upon charge.

Livadia Beach is 800 metres away. The famous Kolymbithres beach is within 10 km. Staff can arrange car rental and free private parking is possible on site.
