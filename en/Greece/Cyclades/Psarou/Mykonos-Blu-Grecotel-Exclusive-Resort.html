Description

Accommodations at Mykonos Blu consists of sophisticated bungalows and villas, with built-in bathtubs or phototherapy showers. Some units have private pools, indoor or outdoor hot bathtubs. B&O LCD TVs, DVD/CD players and free Wi-Fi are standard.

A state-of-the-art fitness room, sauna and beauty salon are available at the resort’s Elixir Fitness Gallery Spa. Guests can also find a jewelry shop and the largest conference center in Cyclades.

Aegean Poets serves a lunch and dinner with options from the menu, as well as seafood specialties. Poolside lunches and drinks are available at the L’Archipel brasserie. Delos Lounge Bar is ideal for an after-dinner drink.

Mykonos Airport is 2.5 miles from the resort. The town of Mykonos is a 5-minute drive away. The hotel can also arrange sunset champagne sailing or helicopter tours.
