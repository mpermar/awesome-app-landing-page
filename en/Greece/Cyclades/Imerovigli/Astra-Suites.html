Description

Featuring works of art, candles and ornaments throughout, the suites include a bedroom, kitchen and a lounge area with sofa. Several have their own spa tub or private pool.

The Astra Suites serves breakfast from a menu on your balcony or by the pool. Creative Greek and Mediterranean cuisine is served at the on-site Astra Suites gourmet restaurant. Private dinners can also be organized upon request.

The hotel's facilities feature a hot tub, steam room and massage area. Beach and pool towels are provided free of charge. There is wired and wireless internet connections, and you can borrow a laptop from the front desk.

The hotel front desk can arrange sailing tours, excursions and transportation. The staff will be happy to assist you with area info and suggestions on what to do on the island of Santorini.

Astra Suites is just over 1 miles from Fira, 5 miles from Santorini Airport and 6 miles from the port. Guests of the Astra can park their cars at the nearby municipal parking lot.
