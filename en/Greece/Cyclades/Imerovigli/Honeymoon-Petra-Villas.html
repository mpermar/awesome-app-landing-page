Description

This luxurious hotel on the edge of the town of Imerovigli provides guests with the opportunity to take trips on its private sailing yachts and speed boat. Guests can explore the island on a private shuttle bus.

All the air-conditioned units feature balconies with panoramic views of the volcanic Santorini Bay. Some rooms are literally carved out of the rock, creating a natural atmosphere. A satellite flat-screen TV and a CD-player are available.

The Petra hotel features a steam bath, an outdoor hot tub, a pool and a piano bar, as well as BBQ facilities and private lounge mattresses on the propertys rooftop offering breathtaking views. The rooftop pool is partly carved out of the volcanic rock.

The Petra Villas is close to the ancient fortress of Skaros and 6 miles from Santorini International Airport. The property can make arrangements for yacht rental. Free on-site parking is available.
