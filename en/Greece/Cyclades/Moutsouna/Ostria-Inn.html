Description

Quietly located right on the sandy Tigani Beach in Moutsouna, the Cycladic-style Ostria Hotel offers a swimming pool with poolside bar.

It features elegant rooms with a furnished balcony overlooking the Aegean Sea or the garden and countryside.

Free WiFi is available throughout. Featuring warm colours and wrought iron beds, all the air-conditioned accommodation at the Ostria includes a TV and fridge.

Some rooms have a kitchenette and a seating area. Breakfast including local honey and fresh fruit is served by the sea.

The seafront Ostria Restaurant serves local flavours, fresh fish and traditional recipes.

Drinks and coffee are provided at the pool bar. The sun terrace is surrounded by the gardens and offers sun loungers and umbrellas.

A childrens pool is available for younger guests, while sand and leisure games are also organized at the beach.

A sailing yacht is also available for daily cruises at the nearby islands. Staff at the tour desk can offer information on the nearby islands of Donousa and Koufonisia.

Psili Ammos, Panormos Beach and the traditional village of Apiranthos is less than a 20-minute drive away.
