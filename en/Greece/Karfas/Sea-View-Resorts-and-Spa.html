Description

The rooms at the Sea View Resorts & Spa have a balcony, a full kitchen and a sitting area with satellite TV.

Guests can enjoy rich local and international cuisine at the Sea View Resorts & Spa restaurant, which has a terrace overlooking the Aegean Sea.

Relaxation options at the Sea View Resorts & Spa include an outdoor pool with panoramic sea views, a children’s pool and a spa with a sauna, hot tub and treatment rooms. There is also a gym equipped with cardio equipment.

Chios capital and port are 4 miles from the Sea View Resorts. Free private parking is available.
