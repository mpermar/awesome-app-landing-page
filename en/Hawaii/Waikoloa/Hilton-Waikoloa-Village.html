Description

Guest rooms at the Waikoloa Village Hilton offers views of the Pacific Ocean and Waialua Bay. Flat-screen TVs and coffee makers are also included in each room.

Hilton Waikoloa Village features 3 swimming pools, including one with a 174 foot water slide. A saltwater lagoon and dolphin encounter are also offered. The hotel also includes on-site stores, several gourmet restaurants, canal boats, and a museum walkway with art from around the Pacific.

Guests can use the tram service to explore the Waikoloa Beach Resort. Free shuttle service is also available to the nearby Anaeho'omalu Bay, which offers many additional water activities.
