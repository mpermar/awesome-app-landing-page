Description

Featuring a private balcony, each room at Kauai Beach Resort comes equipped with a compact refrigerator and coffee maker. A cable TV and iPod docking station are also provided.

The Naupaka Terrace Restaurant serves a breakfast buffet and Pan-Asian steak and seafood specialties for dinner. The Naupaka Café has grab-and-go breakfast items. The Driftwood Bar and Grille features poolside lunches and drinks. Shutters Lounge has specialty drinks, appetizers and live music each evening. Room service is available.

A 24-hour front desk welcomes guests. A 24-hour fitness center is available. A tour and activities desk is available.

Lydgate State Beach Park is a 5-minute drive away. Puakea Golf Course is 5 miles from Kauai Beach Resort.
