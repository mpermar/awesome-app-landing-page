Description

A flat-screen TV with cable channels with pay options and Nintendo video game system is offered in each air-conditioned guest room. A re4frigerator and a coffee machine are provided. An private bathroom provides Noni Papaya toiletries and a hairdryer.

The beachfront Duke’s Canoe Club, offers breakfast and lunch buffets along with à la carte dinners. Hula Grill Waikiki features retro Hawaiian décor and serves breakfast, lunch and specialty cocktails.Room service is offered.

The rooftop Infinity Massage spa offers a spacious sun terrace with ocean views. Massage therapies, facials, body treatments and beauty services incorporating Hawaiian and European techniques and products are available.

Free child-friendly Hawaiian cultural activities are offered daily and may include fresh flower lei-making or ukulele lessons. An expansive lobby highlights Hawaiian artwork and koa wood furnishings. Married guests can participate in a free Hawaiian vow renewal ceremony on the beach.

Upscale shopping and dining at The Royal Hawaiian Shopping Center and Waikiki Beach Walk are within 5 minutes’ walk of Honolulu Outrigger Waikiki Beach Resort. Honolulu International Airport is 20 minutes’ drive away.

Waikiki is a great choice for travelers interested in beach walks, surfing and shopping.
