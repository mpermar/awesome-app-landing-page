Description

All suites offer a living room area, a kitchenette complete with a refrigerator, a microwave, and a coffee machine. Select suites offer views of the city or ocean, and a balcony.

Guests can enjoy the open-air Grand Lanai, which is located on the 4th floor. The lanai features a heated swimming pool, a hot tub and a children's pool. Relax on the large sundeck while drinking a cocktail from the Pakini Bar. Live Hawaiian music is played 4 times a week.

Guests are offered a complimentary, hot, full cook-to-order breakfast as well as free beverages and snacks poolside.

Guests can also take advantage of the 24-hour fitness center, the coin-operated laundry facilities, and the cash machines at the Embassy Suites by Hilton Waikiki Beach Walk.

Embassy Suites by Hilton Waikiki Beach Walk is centrally located on the 8-acre Waikiki Beach Walk. The lively area features an outdoor entertainment plaza, 40 retail shops and restaurants. Kids and adults will also love the variety of cultural activities offered at the Beach Walk. The suites are only a 3.5 mi from Diamond Head. Honolulu Zoo and Ala Moana Shopping center are 1.5 mi away.

Waikiki is a great choice for travelers interested in beach walks, surfing and shopping.
