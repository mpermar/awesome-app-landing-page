Description

Modern rooms feature a flat-screen TV and video games at Sheraton Waikiki. Rooms are simply styled and offer a private balcony, some with mountain or ocean views. In-room coffee and a work desk are also provided.

The Sheraton offers 4 on-site dining options, including Pacific Rim inspired RumFire, the Edge of Waikiki bar located just above the ocean, a breakfast and dinner buffet featuring Hawaii's locally grown products at Kai Market and Hapa's Pizza.

A tour desk and child care services are offered at this resort. Fitness and business facilities are also located on-site for guest convenience.

Honolulu Zoo and Kapiolani Park are 2 mi from the resort. Waikiki Shopping Center is just 2 minutes' walk away.

Waikiki is a great choice for travelers interested in beach walks, surfing and shopping.
