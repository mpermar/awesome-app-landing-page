Description

All the rooms at Castel Novel are individually decorated. They are all equipped with a safe, TV, air-conditioning and internet access. The castle is set in 39 acres of park and woodland.

Dinner is served in one of the three dining rooms or on the hotel terrace. Regional cuisine is served by the award winning chef and made from fresh local produce.

The Chateau is perfectly located for exploring the region. There are several golf clubs nearby and opportunities for canoeing, fishing, hiking and cycling.
