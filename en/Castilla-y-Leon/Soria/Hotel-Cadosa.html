Description

All rooms at Hotel Cadosa are equipped with free Wi-Fi, heating and a private bathroom. In addition, they all face abroad.

The hotel has a play area for children and large and beautiful gardens.

This hotel, which has a strategic location between Madrid and Pamplona, is 3 km from Soria and offers free parking.
