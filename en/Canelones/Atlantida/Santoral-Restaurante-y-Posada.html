Description

Guest rooms in the inn are equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Free WiFi is available to all guests, while selected rooms also feature a balcony. All guest rooms have a desk.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property.

Santoral Restaurante y Posada has a playground. Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Atlántida, like cycling.

Montevideo is 26 miles from Santoral Restaurante y Posada, while Piriápolis is 29 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Carrasco International Airport, 15 miles from the inn.
