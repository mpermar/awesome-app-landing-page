Description

Guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a electric tea pot. All rooms have a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hair dryer. At Clive Garden, the rooms come with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

The daily breakfast offers continental, Full English/Irish or American options.

The accommodation offers 5-star accommodations with a hot tub and playground. Free use of bicycles, bike rental and car rental are available at Clive Garden and the area is popular for cycling.

Colombo is 21 miles from the hotel, while Bentota is 16 miles away. Bandaranaike International Airport is 37 miles from the property.
