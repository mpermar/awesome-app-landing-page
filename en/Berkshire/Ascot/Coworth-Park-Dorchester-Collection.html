Description

Spacious and elegantly furnished, each room features spacious bathrooms with under-floor heating and copper baths. Guests can relax with high-end technology, including a Blu-Ray DVD player, an iPod/USB dock, and a Nespresso coffee machine. All rooms enjoy scenic views of the gardens.

With a roof of fragrant herbs, Coworth Park’s eco spa features stylish, contemporary architecture. It has a range of relaxing spa and beauty treatments, along with a swimming pool with underwater music and a gym.

Set among 240 acres of Berkshire parkland, Coworth Park is the only hotel in the UK with its own polo fields. The hotel’s equestrian center offers horseback riding lessons to guests, as well as extensive stabling facilities.

Restaurant Coworth Park serves gourmet fine cuisine and has an international wine list. Afternoon tea is served in The Drawing Room, and informal dining with idyllic views is available in The Barn restaurant.
