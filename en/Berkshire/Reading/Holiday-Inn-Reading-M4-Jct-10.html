Description

Newly opened, the Holiday Inn Reading M4, Jct 10 offers stylish, modern rooms each with a 32-inch flat-screen TV, luxury bedding and brand-name toiletries.

Esprit Fitness & Spa has a sauna, steam room, a large state-of-the-art gym, and a range of Dermalogica spa and massage treatments. Free private and secure underground parking is available, and Wi-Fi is accessible throughout the property.

The vibrant Caprice Restaurant has 2 AA Rosettes, serving freshly prepared modern British cuisine as well as an authentic Indian and Thai menu. Monty's Bar has a resident pianist, and serves an extensive range of quality bar meals and cocktails.

The hotel is next to Winnersh Triangle Train Station, providing easy access to London Paddington with a 20-minute ride. It is a 10-minute drive from Thames Valley and Suttons Business Parks, and within convenient reach of Legoland Windsor, Ascot Racecourse, the historic Windsor Castle and London Heathrow Airport.
