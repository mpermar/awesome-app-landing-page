Description

Guest rooms at the hotel come with a seating area and a flat-screen TV. TalaiBagh Palace has some units that include a terrace and garden view, and the rooms include a private bathroom and a desk. All rooms at the accommodation feature air conditioning and a closet.

A continental, buffet or à la carte breakfast is available each morning at the property.

The property has a hot tub.

TalaiBagh Palace can conveniently provide information at the reception to help guests to get around the area.
