Description

The Radisson Blu Resort and Spa, Alibaug is 2 miles from Alibaug Beach and the Local Bus Station. The Kolaba Fort is 2.5 miles away and the Murud Janjira Fort is 34 miles away. The Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport is just under 1 miles from the property.

With a balcony, the air-conditioned rooms include a minibar, an electric kettle and flat-screen satellite TV. A sofa and safe are also available. The private bathrooms include a bathtub.

To assist the guests, the property has a 24-hour front desk. It has a meeting/banquet space and a business center. Day trips are arranged at the tour desk. Guests who would like to explore the area on their own can rent a car.

The 3 dining options include Kokum and Spice, that serve Konkan cuisines. Aparanta is a multi-cuisine restaurant and Lounge Bar serves refreshing beverages. Room service is also available.
