Description

The accommodations at Costão do Santinho Resort offer picturesque views of the garden and feature cable TV and ambient music. Some rooms have barbecue and private parking.

There is a children's club with activities supervised by monitors. Visits are organized for guests of all ages to the natural reserve of Mount Morro das Aranhas. In addition, well-known Brazilian singers usually perform in the complex.

The spa offers varied treatments, from toxin elimination to thalassotherapy, as well as relaxing massages and hydromassage sessions. There is also a beauty salon.

Rancho do Pescador serves seafood dishes from the Azores and Santa Catarina in an informal setting. The Nossa Senhora Vitória offers a menu based on international contemporary dishes. You can also eat Italian, Japanese and barbecue dishes.

The Costão do Santinho has been awarded 7 times as the best beach resort in Brazil.

Praia do Santinho is a great choice for travelers interested in sandy beaches, tranquility and walking along the beach.
