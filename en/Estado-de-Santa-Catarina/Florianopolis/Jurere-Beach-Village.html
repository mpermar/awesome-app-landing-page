Description

All rooms have free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, cable TV, an equipped kitchen and a private balcony.

Jurerê Beach Village also houses a games room and a wet and dry sauna and provides massage services.

The establishment has a restaurant serving local and international dishes and a bar offering tropical cocktails and soft drinks.

Jurerê is a great choice for travelers interested in tranquility, walking along the beach and food.
