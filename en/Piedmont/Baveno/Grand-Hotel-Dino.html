Description

Rooms are air conditioned and have classic furniture, a mini-bar and satellite TV. They overlook the mountains or lake, and many include a private balcony.

There is a games room as well as 2 outdoor swimming pools and hot tubs with views across the lake. The wellness center has both a sauna and Turkish bath.

The Dino has a number of restaurants and bars, some of which overlook the lake. Surrounded by gardens, it is next to an interesting art gallery and the jetty for boat trips across the lake.

Stresa is 2.5 mi from the hotel, while Milan Malpensa Airport is a 50-minute drive away.
