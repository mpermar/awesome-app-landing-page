Description

Accommodations at Locanda Dell'arte have an individual design. Each room displays works of art by a different artist.

The dining room opens out onto a terrace, overlooking Valle Cerrina. The lounge features a fireplace and has large windows overlooking the greenery.

The natural park and UNESCO site of Santuario di Crea is just 4 miles away.
