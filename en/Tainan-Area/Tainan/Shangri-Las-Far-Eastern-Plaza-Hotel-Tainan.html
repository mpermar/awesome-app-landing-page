Description

Shangri-La's Far Eastern Plaza Hotel is a only a 10-minute drive from various touristic attractions including Chihkan Tower, Tainan Confucius Temple and the lively Tainan Flower Market. It is also close to shopping options such as Far Eastern Plaza Department Store.

Rooms are spacious and decorated with Asian-inspired decor. Stylish and comfortable it offers ample workspace, a pillow menu and free broadband internet access. Room service is also available 24-hours.

Unwind with a dip in the swimming pool or rejuvenate with a session in the sauna or indoor hot tub. For those looking to maintain their work-outs, head over to the well-equipped fitness center.

Shangri-La's Far Eastern Plaza Hotel offers 4 different food and beverage options. It ranges from a cozy atmosphere to socialize with a cocktail to an elegant setting serving authentic Chinese cuisine.

East District is a great choice for travelers interested in museums, food and culture.
