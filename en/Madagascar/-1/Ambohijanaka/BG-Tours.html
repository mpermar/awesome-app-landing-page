Description

At the bed and breakfast, the rooms include a desk. Rooms are complete with a shared bathroom equipped with a bath, while some accommodations at BG Tours also offer a seating area. All guest rooms feature a closet.

A continental breakfast is available daily at the property. There's an on-site restaurant, which specializes in French and European cuisine.

The accommodation has a playground. Guests at BG Tours will be able to enjoy activities in and around Ambohijanaka, like golfing.

The bed and breakfast provides a laundry service, as well as business facilities like fax and photocopying. Staff at the reception can provide information on what to do in the area.
