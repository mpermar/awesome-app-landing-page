Description

All guest rooms at the resort are equipped with a seating area. With a private bathroom, rooms at Samaria Lodge also feature free WiFi, while certain rooms include a sea view. The rooms include a desk.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at the property.

A number of activities can be enjoyed in the surrounding areas, including snorkeling, cycling and fishing.
