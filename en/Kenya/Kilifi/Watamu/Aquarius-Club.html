Description

Set in Watamu, 450 metres from Mapango restaurant, Aquarius Club features a private beach area.

The resort has air-conditioned rooms with a private bathroom and free WiFi. The property is situated in the Watamu Beach district, 1 km from ACK St.

Peter's church Watamu. At the club all rooms have a terrace. All guest rooms have a desk.

A continental breakfast is served each morning at the property. Aquarius Club offers a range of wellness facilities including a hot tub.

You can play table tennis at the accommodation, and car hire is available. Staff at Aquarius Club are always available to provide advice at the reception.

Popular points of interest near the resort include Marine Centre The Local Ocean Trust, Scuba Diving Centre/school/Aqua Ventures and Bio-Ken laboratory and snake farm.
