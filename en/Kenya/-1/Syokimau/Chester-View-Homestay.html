Description

At the bed and breakfast, the rooms are equipped with a closet. Free WiFi is available to all guests, while certain rooms include a DVD player. All rooms will provide guests with an oven.

A continental, à la carte or Full English/Irish breakfast is available every morning at the property.

Chester View Homestay has an outdoor pool. Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.

Staff at Chester View Homestay are always available to provide guidance at the reception.

Nairobi is 14 miles from the bed and breakfast.
