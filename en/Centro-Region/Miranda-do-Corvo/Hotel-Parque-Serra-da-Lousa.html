Description

At Hotel Parque Serra da Lousã, the rooms have a desk and a flat-screen TV. Rooms have a private bathroom. All guest rooms feature a closet.

A buffet breakfast is available daily at the property.

The services of the wellness area include an indoor pool and a hot tub. You can play pool at this 4-star hotel, and the area is popular for cycling and hiking.

With staff speaking Portuguese, English and Spanish, around-the-clock advice is available at the reception.

Coimbra is 10 miles from Hotel Serra da Lousã Park, while Figueira da Foz is 28 miles from the property.
