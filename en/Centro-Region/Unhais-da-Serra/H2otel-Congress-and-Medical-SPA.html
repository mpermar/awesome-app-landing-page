Description

Each room at the H2otel Congress & Medical SPA has panoramic views of the countryside. Each feature luxury bedding, contemporary design and have an LCD satellite TV. Some rooms have a balcony.

The restaurant Alquimia serves healthy cuisine and slow food. There is also a lounge bar with sofas that serves refreshing beverages and offers views of lavender, wild thyme and the glacial valley.

The Medical SPA staff can arrange various massages and recreational activities. The hotel has a fitness center, and indoor and outdoor pools which are connected and surrounded by waterfalls and rock formations.

H2otel Congress & Medical SPA is 12 miles from Covilhã, and is a 36-minute drive from Penhas da Saúde. Free public parking is available on site.
