Description

All units feature a dining area and a sitting area with a satellite flat-screen TV. Some units have a terrace and/or patio with mountains views. A dishwasher, a microwave and toaster are also available, as well as a coffee machine. There is also a kitchen in some of the units equipped with an oven, a refrigerator, and a stovetop. Each unit has a private bathroom with free toiletries. Towels and bed linen are provided.

Páteo do Duque also includes a year-round outdoor pool. The property also has convenience store (on site).

You can play darts at the property, and bike rental is available. Guests can enjoy various activities in the area, including biking and hiking. Fatima is 25 miles from Páteo do Duque, and Óbidos is 7 miles away. The nearest airport is Lisbon Humberto Delgado Airport, 47 miles from Páteo do Duque.
