Description

Tucked away on a property over 3 acres in size, Grande Hotel das Caldas da Felgueira is ideal for guests seeking pure relaxation. Read a newspaper in the hotel grounds before enjoying a swim in the large outdoor pool. In winter, you can rest in front of the fireplace.

All of the guest rooms and apartments are decorated with traditional Portuguese furnishings, adding to the Grande Hotels overall charming atmosphere. Indulge in traditional specialties during a theme party or treat yourself to a professional massage. The attentive staff will do its best to ensure you have a memorable stay.
