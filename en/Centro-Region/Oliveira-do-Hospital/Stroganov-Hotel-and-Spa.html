Description

At the hotel, rooms include a closet and a flat-screen TV. Rooms have a private bathroom with a bath.

A continental breakfast is served at the property.

At Stroganov Hotel & Spa guests are welcome to use a sauna and a Turkish bath. Bike rental and car rental are available at this hotel and the area is popular for cycling.

Speaking Spanish and German at the 24-hour front desk, staff are ready to help around the clock.

Coimbra is 30 miles from the accommodation, while Covilhã is 24 miles away.
