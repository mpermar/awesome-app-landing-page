Description

Victory Byblos Hotel & Spa is located in historical Byblos, just minutes from trendy boutiques and the Casino Du Liban.

The beach is 50 meters from the hotel. The hotel provides well-equipped rooms and suites, all with great views from private balconies.

The rooms have flat-screen TVs, free wireless internet, split AC, minibar, digital safe box and blackout curtains.

There is also a beauty center, offering body and facial treatments. Guests can enjoy a meal in the restaurant with its terrace and views of the Mediterranean Sea.

For drinks, there is a lounge bar. Victory Byblos Hotel & Spa is located 1. 5 km from Byblos old Souk & Castle and only 300 meters away from several restaurants and supermarkets.
