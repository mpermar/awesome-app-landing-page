Description

Villagio Hotel & Resort features a restaurant, free bikes, a seasonal outdoor swimming pool and bar in Al MurÅ«j.

The accommodation provides a ski pass sales point and ski storage space, as well as a garden and a terrace.

The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.

The units come with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, fridge, a kettle, a bidet, a hairdryer and a wardrobe.
