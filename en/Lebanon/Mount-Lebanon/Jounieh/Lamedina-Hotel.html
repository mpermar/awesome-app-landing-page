Description

Lamedina Hotel, Beach Club and Resort is at the heart of the Jounieh Bay featuring a private white-sand beach and a pier with sun loungers and rattan umbrellas.

It offers water sports and an outdoor pool. The resort has been renovated in January 2015, and is now at the service of all visitors that are looking to have a pleasurable and well taken care of visit to Lebanon.

Lamedina Hotel has air-conditioned rooms with a seating area and views of the Mediterranean Sea.

They feature a dressing table and a private bathroom with hairdryer. Guests can soak up the sun on the hotels pier or private beach.

Alternatively, they can take a cooling dive in the swimming pool. On-site water activities include windsurfing, canoeing, diving and snorkelling.

The hotels cosy restaurant with vaulted ceiling serves a host of local and international meals in a charming ambiance.

Guests can enjoy spirits and wine at the on-site bar. At a walking distance from the Telefrique du Liban from which you can take a cable car and reach the top of the mountain to visit our Lady of Lebanon in Harissa; Lamedina Hotel is just two streets away from the nightlife in Jounieh, Casino Du Liban and so close to the shopping area and market.
