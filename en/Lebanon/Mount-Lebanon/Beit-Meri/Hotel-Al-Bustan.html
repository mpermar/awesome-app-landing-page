Description

Hotel Al Bustan occupies a hilltop location, giving views across Beirut and the Mediterranean.

It has an outdoor pool, a children’s playground, 3 restaurants and free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel.

All guest bedrooms at Al Bustan Hotel are equipped with satellite TV, a radio and a hairdryer.

Every room also features a private bathroom, equipped with both a shower and a bathtub.

Hotel Al Bustan has several dining options, including an Italian trattoria and a stylish à la carte restaurant, Les Glycines.

There is a wine cellar, a Scottish bar, a piano lounge and a candle-lit summer terrace.

Guests can relax in the hotel’s gardens, enjoy a therapeutic massage or make use of the hotel’s fitness centre.

There is a children’s playground, and babysitting services are available. The hotel is host to an international music and arts Festival, which takes place every year during five weeks in February and March.

Al Bustan Hotel is 15 km from both the centre of Beirut and the city’s airport. Airport transfers are available at an added fee, as well as free on-site car parking.
