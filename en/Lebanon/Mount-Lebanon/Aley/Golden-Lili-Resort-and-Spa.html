Description

Just a 15-minute drive from Downtown Beirut, Golden Lili Resort & Spa is located in Aley.

Free Wi-Fi access is available in this resort. It offers an indoor pool, an outdoor pool and a fitness centre.

The rooms feature a modern décor and a balcony with pool view. It offers a small seating area with a flat-screen TV, a minibar and an electric kettle.

The bathroom is fitted with a shower, a bath and free toiletries. At Golden Lili's Resort & Spa you will find à-la-carte restaurant, buffet restaurant and a sports bar.

Other facilities offered at the property include a bowling centre, a games room and a Kids' Club.

Rafic Hariri International Airport is 15 minutes by car from Golden Lili’s Resort & Spa.

Guests can shop at Souk Aley which is within a short walking distance. Free private parking is possible on site.
