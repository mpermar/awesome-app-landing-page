Description

Hilton Beirut Downtown has a restaurant, seasonal outdoor swimming pool, a fitness centre and bar in Beirut.

Boasting family rooms, this property also provides guests with a sun terrace. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.

The rooms are fitted with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kettle, a bath or shower, a hairdryer and a desk.

At the hotel rooms have a wardrobe and a private bathroom. A continental breakfast is available each morning at Hilton Beirut Downtown.

At the accommodation guests are welcome to take advantage of a sauna. Bike hire and car hire are available at Hilton Beirut Downtown and the area is popular for skiing and cycling.

Guests can access the free WiFi or use the business centre. Popular points of interest near the hotel include Gemayzeh Street (Rue Gouraud), Place de l'Etoile - Nejmeh Square and Beirut International Exhibition & Leisure Center (BIEL).

The nearest airport is Beirut Rafic Hariri International Airport, 9 km from Hilton Beirut Downtown.
