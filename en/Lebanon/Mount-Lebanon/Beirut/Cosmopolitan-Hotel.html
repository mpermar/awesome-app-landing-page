Description

Located in the outskirts of Beirut, this 4-star hotel is only 10 km from the Rafic Hariri International Airport.

Leisure facilities include air-conditioned tennis courts and 3 swimming pools. Rooms at Cosmopolitan Hotel include wood furnishings and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

They come equipped with flat-screen TV and satellite channels. Free Wi-Fi is available in all the rooms.

In the morning, the hotel offers a continental breakfast with fresh fruit. The Garden Cafe provides indoor and outdoor seating and serves a variety of international cuisine.

Room service is also available. Guests can enjoy a game of basketball on the sport court, or relax on a sun lounger poolside.

The hotel also has a fitness centre with free weights that offers salsa dance classes.

Cosmopolitan Hotel is located less than 3 km from ABC Shopping Mall and the Beirut National Museum.
