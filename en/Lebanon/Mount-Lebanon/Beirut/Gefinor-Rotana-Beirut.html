Description

Gefinor Rotana, located in the famous Hamra area, has set the trend with its location “truly in the heart of the city”, being close to all city attractions.

It offers stylish rooms, a rooftop bar and swimming pool featuring panoramic Mediterranean Sea views.

Hamra Street is a 5-minute walk away. Each modern, air-conditioned room at Hotel Gefinor Rotana features large windows, a seating area and a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Soft bathrobes and slippers are provided and a coffee maker is available. For relaxation, guests can enjoy the sauna, steambath or a professional massage.

The fitness centre overlooks the pool. Buffet breakfast and à la carte meals are served at the open-kitchen restaurant, the Olive Garden.

The à la carte dinner menu features continental and Lebanese dishes. The Moodz Lounge & Bar is an ideal place to enjoy a coffee or an evening cocktail with friends.

Gefinor Rotana is also a short 5-minute walk from the vibrant Hamra street where you can find a wide choice of restaurants, pubs, bars and shops.

The down-town “Beirut Souks’, waterfront Corniche and Zaitounay Bay with its marina and restaurants, can all be accessed within a 10-minute stroll.

The hotel has 2 entrances that will facilitate your move to the vibrant area around the property.
