Description

Located in Al Hamra, 1866 Court & Suites Hotel offers modern accommodations with panoramic sea and city views.

It includes free WiFi access in all areas and a fitness centre All rooms here will provide you with a flat-screen TV, air conditioning and a minibar.

Each apartment includes a fully equipped kitchenette with a microwave and an electric kettle.

Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and bathrobes. At 1866 Court & Suites Hotel you will find a 24-hour front desk and a restaurant.

Other facilities offered at the property include luggage storage and shops (on site).

The hotel is 2. 5 km from Place de l'Etoile - Nejmeh Square and 2. 9 km from Monot Street (nightlife).
