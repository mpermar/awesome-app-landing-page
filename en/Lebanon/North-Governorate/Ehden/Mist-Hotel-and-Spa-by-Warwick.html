Description

Guest rooms has air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a electric tea pot, a shower, free toiletries and a closet. Some rooms also feature a kitchen with an oven. All guest rooms include a private bathroom.

Guests can dine in the in-house restaurant, which specializes in international cuisine.

The hotel has a terrace. A variety of popular activities are available in the area around Mist Hotel & Spa by Warwick, including skiing and cycling.

The nearest airport is Beirut Rafic Hariri International Airport, 43 miles from the property.
