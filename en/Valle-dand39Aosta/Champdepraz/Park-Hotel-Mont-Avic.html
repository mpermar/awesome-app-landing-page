Description

Hotel Parc Mont Avic is surrounded by nature, at 4,265 feet above sea level. The area is ideal for mountain bike and trekking excursions around the 14 Alpine lakes nearby.

Rooms at the Mont Avic are compact and welcoming, with hardwood floors and paneling. Free Wi-Fi is available in the lobby.
