Description

Villa Coco is a new Boutique Hotel in Santa Catalina. Featuring free WiFi in social areas, restaurant, bar and outdoor infinity pool.

Free private parking is available on site. The accommodation is 8 minutes walk from El Estero Beach.

The property has a garden with hammocks, and families with children are welcome. All rooms include air conditioning, safe, amenities, comfortable beds and private terraces are furnished.

Each room also has an outdoor shower inside the garden of the room. Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including horse riding and snorkelling.
