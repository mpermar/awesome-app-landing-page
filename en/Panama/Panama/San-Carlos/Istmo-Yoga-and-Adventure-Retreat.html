Description

Offering an outdoor restaurant, walking meditation labyrinth, a nature trail and hardwood floor yoga studio, Istmo Yoga and Adventure Retreat is situated in Playa Grande, San Carlos in the Panama Region.

Guests can enjoy views of the garden and a drink at the bar. All bungalows are air-conditioned, have a private bath with hot water, refrigerators, and a balcony with hammocks and panoramic views of the property.

There is free WiFi access in central areas of the property. There is a shared lounge at the property where guests can play board games, table tennis or watch a movie.

Yoga classes and massages are also available. El Valle is 30 km from Istmo Yoga and Adventure Retreat, while Playa Coronado is 14 km away and Playa Blanca 19 km away.
