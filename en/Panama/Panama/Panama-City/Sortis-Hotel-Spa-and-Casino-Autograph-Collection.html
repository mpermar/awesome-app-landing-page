Description

Located in central Panama City, Sortis Hotel Autograph Collection features an outdoor pool, a casino, a spa and a fitness centre.

The stylish rooms and apartments offer city views. Each room here will provide you with air conditioning, a seating area, a coffee maker and cable TV.

The modern bathrooms come with a hairdryer and free toiletries. Apartments also feature kitchen facilities.

At Sortis Hotel Autograph Collection you will find a restaurant, meeting facilities and a tour desk.

Plaza Obarrio Shopping Centre is just 400 metres away, while the seafront is 2. 5 km away.

You can reach the Metropolitan Natural Park and the Panama Canal within a 15-minute drive.
