Description

Dreams Playa Bonita Resort & Spa offers its guests 5 outdoor pools, spa facilities with massage services and a fitness centre.

Located in a private area in the closest beach to Panama City, only 17 km away. The fresh-decorated rooms and suites are air conditioned and offer ironing appliances, working desks and a coffee maker.

Cable TV, a mini bar and a hairdryer are also in-room amenities. The bathroom is private and may come with a shower or a bath.

The Lobby Bar and Pool Bar offer the guests a variety of drinks. Located 20 minutes’ drive from Panama’s downtown, Dreams Playa Bonita Resort & Spa is close to many touristic attractions, like Panama’s Old Town.
