Description

A 5-minute drive from Tocumen International Airport, this modern 4-star hotel offers free shuttle service to and from the hotel.

Guests can enjoy the spacious outdoor pool and tropical gardens. The stylish guest rooms at the Riande Aeropuerto Hotel Casino feature flat-screen TV and air conditioning.

The private bathroom includes bath amenities and a hair dryer. Room service is available.

Guests can enjoy local cuisine in the Cafe La Veranda. Recreational activities at the Riande Aeropuerto Hotel Casino include tennis, ping pong, padel, paintball and a hot tub.

A business centre and gift shop are also available. A childrens area is also available as well as a fitness centre, meeting rooms and events salons.

This dog-friendly accommodation is just a 5 minutes drive from Tocumen International Airport, while Panama City Casco Viejo is located 23 km away.
