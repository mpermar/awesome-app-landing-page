Description

Radisson Summit Hotel & Golf Panama is set in a tropical rainforest within the Camino de Cruces National Park, just 2 km from the Panama Canal.

It offers 2 outdoor pools, a nature trail, butterfly farm and frog farm, as well as an 18-hole golf course.

The stylish air-conditioned rooms at Radisson Summit Hotel & Golf feature a private balcony and flat-screen cable TV.

Each has an iPod docking station, Bathroom amenities are provided. The Radisson Summit Hotel has 3 restaurants, offering international cuisine and spectacular views.

A complementary Continental Breakfast is served daily. There is a lobby bar for snacks and cocktails.

You can hire a car from the 24-hour reception and tour desk. Free transport is provided to the Miraflores Locks and Albrook Shopping Centre with scheduled hours, both located nearby.

Panama city centre is a 25-minute drive away while the airport is a 30-minute drive.
