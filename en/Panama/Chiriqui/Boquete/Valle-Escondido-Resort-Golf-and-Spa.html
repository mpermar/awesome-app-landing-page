Description

This beautiful hacienda-style resort in Boquete features a golf course, a fitness centre and an on-site spa.

Valle Escondido Resort Golf and Spa also boasts a heated indoor swimming pool and free breakfast.

Each enormous room at this property features rock walls and vaulted ceilings. They also come with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a DVD player, as well as a safety-deposit box.

Free buffet-style or a la carte breakfast is served every morning, and there is an on-site restaurant that serves international cuisine for lunch and dinner.

The property also has a bar. Guests can make use of the property’s on-site sauna and hot tub, or visit Boquete City Centre, which offers shopping and sightseeing.

Playa Barqueta Beach is a 1-hour drive from Valle Escondido Resort Golf and Spa, while Enrique Malek International Airport is just 50 km away.
