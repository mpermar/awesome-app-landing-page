Description

Set on a coffee plantation, this boutique hotel offers a gourmet restaurant and luxurious rooms with spectacular views of the surrounding gardens, rainforest and mountains.

Baru Volcano National Park is 4 km away. Decorated in an elegant vintage style, rooms and suites at Finca Lerida offer a safe and a 42-inch LCD TV with satellite channels.

Free toiletries are included in the private bathrooms. Birdwatching and coffee tours are available at Hotel Finca Lerida.

The Finca’s restaurant serves fresh, seasonal cuisine and offers wonderful mountain views.

The café and gift shop sell fresh coffee grown on the plantation. Hotel Finca Lerida Coffee Plantation and Boutique Hotel is an ideal base for hiking and birdwatching.

The town of Boquete is just a 10-minute drive away. Free private parking is offered on site.
