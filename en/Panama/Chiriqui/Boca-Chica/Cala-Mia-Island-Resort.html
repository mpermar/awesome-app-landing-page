Description

Situated on the island of Boca Brava, this resort features a spa, an outdoor swimming pool and a private beach area.

It offers ecological accommodation with a sun-shaded patio and ocean views. Free WiFi is available throughout.

Cala Mia Boutique Island Resort rooms have large bedrooms, a bathroom with shower and a spacious seating area.

The patio has a thatched roof and hammocks. Cala Mia Restaurant serves a fusion of Italian and tropical fare with organic vegetables and dairy from the hotelâ€™s own farm.

Bird, whale or monkey-watching can be arrange as well as private boat rides to an uninhabited island, or kayaking.

A scenic water taxi from David Airport straight to the resort can be arranged for a fee.

The property offers boat transportation for at an additional cost to and from Boca Chica, though the transportation is free with a 3 night stay.

The resort has a 24-hour reception desk and offers a shuttle service from the airport at an additional cost.
