Description

Featuring a luxurious spa area with a sauna, steam room and gym, the 5-star Rasstal Spa Hotel is located in Naberezhnye Chelny city centre.

A rich buffet breakfast is also offered. Air-conditioned rooms are stylishly decorated in rich fabrics and warm colours.

Comforts include a flat-screen TV, a minibar and private bathroom with bathrobes and slippers.

A range of European dishes can be enjoyed in the restaurant with elegant furnishings.

A variety of drinks are offered at the Rasstal Spa Hotel's contemporary-style bar.

Guests can benefit from free access to the spa area. The Rasstal Spa Hotel is within a 10-minute walk of a variety of night clubs and restaurants.

It is 15 km to Naberezhnye Chelny Train Station, and Begishevo Airport is 35 km away.
