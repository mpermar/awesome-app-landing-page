Description

Located in Kazan city-centre, just one metro stop from Kazan Kremlin, this spa complex hotel features an aquazone with 6 swimming pools, a thermal spa zone with a variety of saunas and steam baths, and a fitness centre.

Free private parking is featured. Luciano Hotel offers air-conditioned rooms with a flat-screen cable TV, free WiFi, safe, minibar, complimentary drinking water and bathrobes, free toiletries and slippers in the bathroom.

Italian and European cuisine is offered at 3 Lucianos restaurants, where a buffet breakfast is also provided.

After dinner, guests can relax in 5 on-site bars or play billiards. Guests are granted unlimited access to the aquazone, spa zone and fitness centre.

Aquazone features a spa-pool, seawater pool, milk bath, water fitness pool, vitality pool, and paddling pool.

In the thermal spa zone guests can enjoy a herb steam bath, aroma steam bath, salt bath, laconium, hamam, Kneipp Path, ice-cold shower and shower of impressions.

A range of beauty treatments and massages are available in the hotels beauty salon and spa zone.

Koltso shopping centre and historic Baumana Street are both within a 3-minute walk of Luciano Hotel.

Tukaya Square Metro Station is within a 5-minute walk. Kazan International Aiport is 25 km from the hotel, while the central railway station is only 1,5 km away.
