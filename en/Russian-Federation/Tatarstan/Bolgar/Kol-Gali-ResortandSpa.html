Description

Situated in Bolgar, 9 km from Hazine National Art Gallery, Kol Gali Resort&Spa features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, free bikes and a bar.

With a garden, the property also has a terrace. The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and free WiFi throughout the property.

The rooms are fitted with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kettle, a bidet, a hairdryer and a desk.

At the hotel rooms are fitted with a wardrobe and a private bathroom. Kol Gali Resort&Spa offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

The accommodation offers 5-star accommodation with a sauna and children's playground.

You can play table tennis at Kol Gali Resort&Spa, and the area is popular for hiking and cycling.
