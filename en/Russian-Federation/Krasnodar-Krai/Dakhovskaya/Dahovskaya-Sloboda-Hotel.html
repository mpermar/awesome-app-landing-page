Description

Offering an outdoor pool and a restaurant, Dahovskaya Sloboda Hotel is located in Dakhovskaya, a 10-minute walk from Dakh River and Belaya River.

Free WiFi access is available. Each room here will provide you with a TV and satellite channels.

The private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer. At Dahovskaya Sloboda Hotel you will find meeting facilities, a shared lounge and a tour desk.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling, horse riding and fishing.
