Description

Rooms at the Hotel Natalie are equipped with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV. They all also come with a private bathroom with a shower.

The property offers a private covered terrace and is 10 minutes’ walk from supermarkets and a variety of restaurants.

Park Europa Entertainment Complex and Magistral Bowling Center are 2,950 feet away. Krasnodar International Airport is 30 minutes’ drive from the hotel.
