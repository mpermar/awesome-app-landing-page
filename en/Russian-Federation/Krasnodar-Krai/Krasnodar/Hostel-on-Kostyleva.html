Description

The rooms in the hostel are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. All rooms are equipped with a electric tea pot, while some rooms also feature a kitchen with an oven, a microwave and a fridge. All guest rooms include a seating area.

The area is popular for hiking, and bike rental is available at Hostel on Kostyleva.

Non-stop advice is available at the reception. Where staff speak French, Russian and English.

Krasnodar Expo is 5 miles from the accommodation, while Kuban Stadium is a 12-minute walk away. Krasnodar International Airport is 9 miles from the property.
