Description

All guest rooms at the hostel come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kitchenette, a dining area and a private bathroom. The rooms have a electric tea pot, while selected rooms include a patio and others also feature garden views. Guest rooms will provide guests with a dishwasher.

A continental breakfast is served at the property.

Kuban Stadium is 3.9 miles from Apartments Krasnodar Expo, while Vega Shopping Centre is 8 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Krasnodar International Airport, 12 miles from the property.
