Description

Located at the Black Sea coast in Chemitokvadzhe Village, Chemitka Hotel features a free WiFi and free parking.

The rooms offer a balcony with a sea view, working area, satellite TV and fridge.

Guests can dine in the property’s restaurant and use on-site barbecue facilities.

There is also a bar on site. Room service, grocery delivery and breakfasts in the room are provided upon request.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including snorkeling.

Chemitokvadzhe Train Station is 150 metres away, and Sochi International Airport is 81 km from Chemitka Hotel.
