Description

Located at the Sea of Azov coast in Yeysk, Parus Hotel features two outdoor pools and plunge pool for children.

A free parking is available. The rooms feature air conditioning, a fridge and TV.

Some rooms provide a terrace with a sea view. Guests can dine in the on-site restaurant, or eat out in the cafes and restaurants nearby.

There is also a bar on site. Yeysk Train Station is 3. 4 km away, and Rostov-on-Don International Airport is 210 km from Parus Hotel.
