Description

All guest rooms in the resort are equipped with a TV. Rooms come with air conditioning, and certain units at Ozdorovitelnyi Kompleks Orbita have a balcony. The rooms will provide guests with a private bathroom.

A buffet breakfast is available each morning at the accommodation. At Ozdorovitelnyi Kompleks Orbita you'll find a restaurant serving Italian cuisine.

Guests can use the indoor pool. Guests can take part in various activities in the surrounding areas, including cycling and hiking.

The reception can offer helpful tips for getting around the area.

Lazarevskoye is 30 miles from the resort, while Tuapse is 11 miles away. The nearest airport is Gelendzhik Airport, 50 miles from the property.
