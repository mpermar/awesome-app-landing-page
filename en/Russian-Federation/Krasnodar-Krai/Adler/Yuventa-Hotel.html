Description

Featuring an outdoor pool and a sauna, Yuventa Hotel is located 250 metres from the Black Sea shore in Adler.

Free WiFi is available on site. All rooms come with a TV and a private bathroom. Some units include a balcony and a seating area.

Guests can use on-site barbecue facilities and enjoy drinks in the bar. Other facilities include a terrace and 24-hour front desk.

Billiards can be enjoyed on site. Adler Train Station is 3 km away, and Sochi International Airport is 4.
