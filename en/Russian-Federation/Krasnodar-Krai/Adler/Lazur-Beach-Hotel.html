Description

Located on the Black Sea beach in Adler town, this hotel features free Wi-Fi and a direct beach access.

It offers air-conditioned rooms with a flat-screen TV. All the classically decorated rooms at Lazur Beach Hotel come with a fridge and floor-to-ceiling windows.

Some rooms overlook the sea, while others have a view of the Caucasus Mountains. European dishes are served in the Lazur’s restaurant which also offers a daily breakfast.

Refreshing cocktails can be enjoyed at the bar. Guests can sunbathe on the sun terrace or on the beach, 1 minute walk from the hotel.

Staff at the tour desk can help organise trips to Sochi. Yuzhnoe Vzmorye bus stop is 300 metres from Lazur Beach Hotel, providing access to Adler Train Station and the city districts.

Sochi Airport is 8 minutes’ drive away, and a transfer is available at a surcharge.
