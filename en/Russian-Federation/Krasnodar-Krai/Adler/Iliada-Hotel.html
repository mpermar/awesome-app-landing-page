Description

Set right on the beach of the Black Sea, this hotel is located in Adler. Free Wi-Fi and a 24-hour front desk are featured at Iliada Hotel.

The bright, air-conditioned rooms offer warm-coloured interiors and classic-style decor.

Each room comes with a TV, a refrigerator and a private bathroom. One of the hotel’s restaurants is decorated in Greek style and serves Mediterranean and European cuisine.

The other restaurant offers a terrace and features evening shows during the summer.

Trains to Krasnaya Polyana and the Olympic Park stop within walking distance from the hotel.

Kurortniy Gorodok Enterntainment Park is 1. 5 km from Iliada Hotel. Adler Central Train Station is 500 m away, and Adler-Sochi International Airport is just 5 km from the hotel.
