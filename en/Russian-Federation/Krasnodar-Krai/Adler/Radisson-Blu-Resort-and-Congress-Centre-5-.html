Description

Located on the Black Sea coast, Radisson Blu Resort & Congress Centre features an indoor pool, Pace Health Club and offers free WiFi throughout.

Every 30 minutes the property shuttle takes guests to the beach situated in front of the Fisht Stadium in Olympic Park.

The ride is only 2 minutes and the beach is also accessible on foot. The beach is equipped with 250 lounge chairs, umbrellas and sports ground.

The modern, air-conditioned rooms are decorated in beige and brown colours. Each comfortable room includes a flat-screen TV, a minibar, a private bathroom with bathrobes, slippers, a hairdryer and signature toiletries.

A balcony overlooks the Black Sea or the mountains. The Radisson Blu breakfast is an extensive buffet featuring American, Continental and European cuisines.

Grab & Run breakfast and a room service a la carte breakfast options are also available.

Grenadine Restaurant serves European cuisine and Mikado Restaurant offers Japanese dishes.

Mediterranean and Middle East specialities can be enjoyed at Anatolian Kebab and Grill.

Guests are also welcome to summertime pool bars and lobby bar & lounge. Guests can work out in the fitness centre, visit the outdoor heated pool or play lawn tennis.

A sauna, a Turkish steam bath, a hot tub and massage services are also available for guests’ relaxation.

A variety of entertainment for children is available such as a kids' playroom, water slides, sport playgrounds near the swimming pool and swings at the beach.

There are 9 conference rooms and 5 halls to hold large events and small meetings.

Sochi city centre is 27 km from Radisson Blu Resort & Congress Centre, and Krasnaya Polyana is 40 km away.
