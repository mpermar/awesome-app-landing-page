Description

Arli Hotel is located in Kurortniy Gorodok area in Adler, a 7-minute walk from the beach.

The hotel offers a swimming pool with sun loungers. All rooms are air-conditioned.

The rooms are decorated in warm-coloured tones and come with a TV and fridge. The balconies feature sea or mountain views.

Guests are welcome to visit a summer bar and a canteen. Free private parking is available on site.

The dophinarium and sea aquarium are located a 5-minute walk from the Arli Hotel.

Sochi International Airport is 13 km from the property, and Adler Train Station is 3 km away.
