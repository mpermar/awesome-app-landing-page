Description

Located less than a 20-minute walk of the Olympic Park in Sochi, Imeretinsky Hotel features a number of amenities to ensure that guests have a relaxing stay, including an indoor and outdoor pool, a spa and wellness centre, a sauna, massage services, and a Turkish steam bath.

There is also a health centre on site. The spacious and modern rooms here provide a minibar, a balcony, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a seating area with a sofa, and a private bathroom with a bath or shower.

Other property facilities include a fitness centre, an on-site restaurant and bar, a breakfast buffet, a terrace, meeting and banquet facilities, room service, and a shuttle service.

Animation for children and and adults is provided free of charge. Free WiFi is available throughout the hotel.

The Sochi Discovery World Aquarium is 11 km away, and St. Sarkis Cathedral is 2 km from the hotel.

Free parking is located within a 5-minute walk of the property. Imeretinskiy Hotel is 3 km from Adler Train Station, and 5 km from Adler-Sochi International Airport.
