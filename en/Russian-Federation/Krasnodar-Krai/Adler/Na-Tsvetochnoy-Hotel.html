Description

Offering a sun terrace, Na Tsvetochnoy Hotel is located in Adler, a 3-minute walk from the pebbly beach and a 10-minute walk from the central beach on the coast of the Black Sea.

Free private parking is available. Each air-conditioned room here will provide you with a flat-screen TV and satellite channels.

The private bathrooms come with a hairdryer and free toiletries. Na Tsvetochnoy Hotel has a restaurant and BBQ facilities.

It offers a common lounge area and a games room with darts. Krasnaya Polyana ski slopes are 12 km from the property.

Olimpiyskaya Derevnya Train Station is 2 km from the hotel. Sochi International Airport is 6.
