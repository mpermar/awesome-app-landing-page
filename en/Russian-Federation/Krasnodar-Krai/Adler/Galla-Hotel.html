Description

Galla Hotel is located in Adler, within a 5-minute walk of Olympic Square. Free private parking is provided.

The Black Sea is a 5-minute drive away, and free shuttle service is provided in summer.

The air-conditioned rooms feature a fridge and a TV with satellite channels. The bathroom comes with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Guests can order a meal in the on-site restaurant. Various cafes and supermarkets can be found within a 10-minute walk.

Free WiFi is available in all rooms. Adler Train Station is within a 10-minute drive of Galla Hotel and a shuttle to Sochi International Airport (7 km) is available at surcharge.
