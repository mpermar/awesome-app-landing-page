Description

This hotel in Adler offers rooms with a TV, free Wi-Fi and on-site canteen. Sochi International Airport is a 25-minute drive away.

Tetis Hotel include air conditioning, a refrigerator and carpeted floor. The private bathrooms include a shower with toiletries.

Breakfast awaits guests each morning in the cafe upon request. There is a children's playground on site.

Visitors can enjoy use of the sauna. Adler Train Station is a 10-minute drive from Tetis Hotel, while central Sochi is a 30-minute drive away.
