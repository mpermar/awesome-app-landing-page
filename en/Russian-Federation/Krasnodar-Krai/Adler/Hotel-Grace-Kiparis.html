Description

Located in Adler city centre, this hotel is 250 metres from the Black Sea shore. It features a 24-hour reception, billiards and air-conditioned rooms.

Decorated in classic style, rooms at Kiparis Hotel come with a fridge, TV and balcony.

Private bathrooms are fitted with a shower and free toiletries. Guests can enjoy European and Caucasian cuisine in the Kiparis Restaurant, or relax with a cocktail at the on-site bar.

A variety of cafés, shops and entertainment centres is 10 minutes’ walk from the premises.

Adler Train Station is 3 km from Kiparis Hotel. Sochi International Airport is a 10-minute drive away.
