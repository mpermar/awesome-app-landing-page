Description

Offering an outdoor pool and a restaurant, a garden and a sun terrace, Dubai Hotel is located 28 km from Sochi.

Sochi Discovery World Aquarium is 10 minutes’ drive away. Featuring classic-style décor, rooms at this property come with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, wardrobe, heating, air conditioning, seating area, balcony, terrace, outdoor dining area and a bathroom with a hairdryer.

At Dubai Hotel you will find a 24-hour front desk, meeting facilities, luggage storage and an ironing service.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including hiking.

The hotel is 1 km from Noviy Vek Moll shopping area and Adler-Sochi International Airport is 11km away.
