Description

Featuring free WiFi and a gym, Sport hotel Vitamin offers accommodation in Adler, within a 20-minute walk of Iceberg Winter Palace and Fisht Stadium.

Free private parking is available on site. All air-conditioned rooms have a flat-screen TV, kettle, fridge, kitchenware and orthopedic mattresses.

Some units feature a seating area, microwave, safety deposit box and iron facilities.

Every room includes a private bathroom equipped with a shower and free toiletries.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. You can play tennis at the hotel. The hotel also offers bike hire.

Sochi Discovery World Aquarium is 7 km from Sport hotel Vitamin, while Yuzhnye Kultury Park is 1 km away.
