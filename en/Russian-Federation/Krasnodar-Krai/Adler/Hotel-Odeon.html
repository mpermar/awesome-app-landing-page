Description

Situated in Adler, Odeon Hotel is just a 15-minute walk from Kurortny Gorodok and the coast of the Black Sea.

It offers an outdoor swimming pool and a garden with tropical trees. The comfortable air-conditioned rooms come with a flat-screen TV.

All are equipped with fridges and security boxes. Each has a private bathroom with a shower.

The pastime options include sauna and billiards. Hotel Odeon is located 10 minutes’ walk from night clubs, shops and a water park.
