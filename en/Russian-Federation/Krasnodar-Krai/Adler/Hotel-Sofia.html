Description

Sofia Hotel is located in Adler, 500 metres from the Olympic Park and Sochi Amusement Park.

The property is within a 15-minute walk of Sochi Autodrom. Free WiFi and free private parking under surveillance are available on site.

The property provides a free transfer to the sea during the summer season. The rooms will provide you with a flat-screen TV and air conditioning.

Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with free toiletries. The on-site cafe serves breakfast daily.

A variety of restaurants can be found within a 5-minute walk of the hotel. Bicycle rental and tour desk are available on site.

A shuttle service to Krasnaya Polyana ski slopes is available at surcharge. The bus stop providing commute to the centre of Sochi is within a 5-minute walk away and the property is 1.

5 km from the Black Sea coast. Adler Train Station and Sochi International Airport are within 8 km of Sofia Hotel.
