Description

Offering an outdoor pool with a sun terrace, Irena Mini Hotel is located in Adler, 300 metres from the Black Sea beach.

Free WiFi is available in all areas. Each room here will provide you with a TV, air conditioning and a balcony.

The private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer. You can enjoy mountain view and river view from the room.

At Irena Mini Hotel you will find a 24-hour front desk and barbecue facilities. Other facilities offered at the property include an ironing service, laundry facilities and a vending machine.

A shopping mall is a 2-minute walk from the hotel. Olympiyskaya Derevnya is located a 20-minute walk from the property.

The distance to Adler Train Station is 6 km from the property, and Sochi International Airport is 7.

5 km away. A shuttle bus service from Sochi-Adler Airport and Adler Train Station is provided at surcharge.
