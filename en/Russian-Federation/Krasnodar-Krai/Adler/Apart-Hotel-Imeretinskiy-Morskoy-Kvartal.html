Description

Offering free WiFi and a private beach area on the coast of the Black Sea, Apart Hotel Imeretinskiy - Morskoy Kvartal is set in Adler, 8 km from Sochi Discovery World Aquarium.

It offers an outdoor and indoor pools. All units are air conditioned and have a flat-screen TV.

They include a kitchenette fitted with a microwave and refrigerator. Each apartment is equipped with a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

Apart Hotel Imeretinskiy - Morskoy Kvartal also includes a spa center and fitness center.

There is also a health centre on site. Guests can enjoy a drink at the on-site bar and have a meal at the restaurant.

The property has bike hire is available. You can play tennis at the property, and the area is popular for cycling.

A private on-site parking is available at surcharge, and free parking is located within a 5-minute walk.
