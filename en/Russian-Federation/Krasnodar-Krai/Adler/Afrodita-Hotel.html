Description

Located directly on the beach, this hotel is just a 5-minute walk from Adler Water Park and Dolphinarium.

It features an outdoor pool, free Wi-Fi and a restaurant with bar. Afrodita Hotel’s air-conditioned rooms each feature a balcony with views of the Black Sea or Caucasus Mountains.

All rooms have a flat-screen TV and a minibar. Eastern cuisine and European dishes are served in the Afordita’s restaurant, and a varied breakfast menu is available every morning.

Guests can dine and enjoy drinks next to the pool. Adler Train Station in the city centre is 5 km from the hotel.

Sochi International Airport is a 15-minute drive away, and a shuttle service can be booked.
