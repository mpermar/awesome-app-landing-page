Description

Azaliya Hotel is set in Adler, within 10 km of Adler New Age Shopping Centre and 2.

2 km of Amfibius Water Park. With a bar, the 3-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom.

The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, room service and organising tours for guests.

At the hotel, rooms are fitted with a wardrobe. At Azaliya Hotel, all rooms have a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

The accommodation offers a continental or buffet breakfast. Azaliya Hotel offers a children's playground.

The area is popular for skiing, and car hire is available at the hotel. Adler Saint Sarkis Cathedral is 8 km from Azaliya Hotel, while Southern Cultures Park is 9 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Adler-Sochi International Airport, 7 km from the accommodation.
