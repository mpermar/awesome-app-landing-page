Description

Calipso Hotel is located in Estosadok, within a 15-minute walk of Gornaya Karusel Ski Lift, to which free transfer is provided.

Free WiFi is available. The rooms offer a flat-screen TV, a fridge and air conditioning.

The bathrooms provide a hairdryer. Most rooms come with a seating area. Buffet breakfasts and European cuisine are offered in the hotel’s restaurant.

Horse riding and cycling can be enjoyed in the surroundings. Other facilities include ticket service, luggage storage and 24-hour front desk.

The Mzymta River is 300 metres from the property. Esto-Sadok Train Station is 3 km away, and Sochi International Airport is 46 km from Calipso Hotel.
