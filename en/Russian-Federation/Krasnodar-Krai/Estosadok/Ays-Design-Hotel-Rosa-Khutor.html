Description

Set in Estosadok's Ski Resort Rosa Khutor, Ays Design Hotel boasts ski-to-door access and a ski pass sales point.

Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. Rooms provide a wardrobe, towels and bed linen.

Some rooms offer a private bathroom and others have shared bathroom facilities. A conference hall is available at the hotel.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. In summer and winter a free ticket to Olympia ski lift is available.

There is a conference room for up to 30 people in the hotel. This hotel has ski storage space.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including skiing, cycling and hiking.

Alpika-1 is 2. 7 km from Ays Design Hotel. The nearest airport is Adler-Sochi International Airport, 38 km from Ays Design Hotel.
