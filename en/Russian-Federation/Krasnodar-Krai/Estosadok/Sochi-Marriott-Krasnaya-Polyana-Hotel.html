Description

Located in Gorki Gorod ski resort in Krasnaya Polyana, within a 30-minute drive of Fisht Olympic Stadium, Sochi Marriott Krasnaya Polyana Hotel offers ski storage and equipment.

Gornaya Karusel ski lift is within a 5-minute walk of the hotel. Free WiFi is available throughout.

The elegant air-conditioned rooms feature luxury-style bedding, safety deposit box,fridge, work and seating areas with sofa and flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

The private bathrooms come with a bath or shower, hairdryer, bathrobe, slippers and free toiletries.

Guests are welcome to the on-site River Cafe where breakfasts are served daily. A-la-carte menu and kids meals are also offered throughout the day at L'Olivo, the Italian restaurant with a scenic outdoor terrace.

Lobby bar and pool bar welcomes guests for a drink. The property features a spa centre with a sauna, steam bath and indoor swimming pool.

The outdoor swimming pool is also at guests' disposal. Guests are invited to have a workout at the fitness centre.

A children's club with professional tutors is also available on site. Gorki Mall Water Park is within a 2-minute walk away.

Estosadok Train Station is 2 km from the hotel. Sochi Marriott Krasnaya Polyana Hotel is 45 km from Sochi-Adler International Airport, and 77 km from Sochi Train Station.
