Description

Offering free WiFi and a barbecue, Snegiri Village Chalet is situated in Estosadok.

Gornaya Karusel ski lift is 1. 3 km away. Free private parking is available on site.

Featuring a balcony, the accommodation is fitted with a dining and seating areas.

A flat-screen TV with cable channels is offered. All units have a kitchen fitted with a dishwasher, microwave, fridge and stove.

Towels and bed linen are featured. The property also offers grocery delivery. Russian sauna with a plunge tub and barbecue delivery are available at surcharge.

A number of activities are offered in the area, such as skiing, cycling and hiking.

Gazprom Ski Lift is 2. 3 km from Snegiri Village Chalet, while Alpika Service is 3 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Adler-Sochi International Airport, 36 km from the property.
