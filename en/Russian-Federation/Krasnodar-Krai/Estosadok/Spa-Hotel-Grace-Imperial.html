Description

Situated in Estosadok, 2. 4 km from Gorky Gorod, Spa Hotel Grace Imperial features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a bar and a shared lounge.

This 4-star hotel offers room service, a kids' club and free WiFi. The hotel has an indoor pool, spa centre, entertainment staff and a 24-hour front desk.

The rooms are fitted with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fridge, a kettle, a shower, a hairdryer and a desk.

At the hotel rooms come with a wardrobe and a private bathroom. Guests at Spa Hotel Grace Imperial can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

The accommodation offers a terrace. The area is popular for skiing, and bike hire is available at Spa Hotel Grace Imperial.

Gazprom Ski Lift is 3. 4 km from the hotel, while Roza Khutor Ski Resort is 5 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Adler-Sochi International, 44 km from Spa Hotel Grace Imperial, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
