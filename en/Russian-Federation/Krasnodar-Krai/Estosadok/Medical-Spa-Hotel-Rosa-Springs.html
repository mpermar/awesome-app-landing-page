Description

Situated in Rosa Khutor ski resort, Medical Spa Hotel Rosa Springs features a ski pass sales point and ski-to-door access.

The hotel has a spa centre with a swimming pool, sauna and fitness centre. Medical and spa treatment is also offered.

Free WiFi is featured throughout the property. Each room at this hotel is air conditioned and offers an electric kettle, tea and coffee facilities and flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Some units feature a seating area and sofa. Rooms have a private bathroom where you will find bathrobes, slippers, hairdryer and free toiletries.

A buffet breakfast is served daily. Guests are invited to dine at the on-site restaurants and have a snack at the fitobar.

You will find a 24-hour front desk, beauty parlour and gift shop at the property.

Rosa Khutor Train Station is 3. 5 km away. Adler-Sochi International Airport is 38 km from the property.
