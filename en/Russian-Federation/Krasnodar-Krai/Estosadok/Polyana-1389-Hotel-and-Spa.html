Description

Offering an outdoor and indoor pool, sauna, hammam and spa and wellness centre, Polyana 1389 Hotel & Spa is located in Estosadok.

Free Wi-Fi and free parking are available. The rooms feature a terrace with a view, safety deposit box, electric kettle and flat-screen TV.

The bathrooms provide a hairdryer and bathrobes. Guests can dine in the property’s a la carte restaurant.

There is also a bar on site. Other facilities include fitness centre and ski equipment hire.

Gazprom Ski Resort is a 10-minute walk of the property. Krasnaya Polyana Train Station is 1.

5 km away, and Sochi International Airport is 45 km from Polyana 1389 Hotel & Spa.
