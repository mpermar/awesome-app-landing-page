Description

At Guest House Rakushka, rooms come with a closet and a flat-screen TV. The accommodation has certain rooms that include a balcony, and all rooms have a electric tea pot.

Guests at Guest House Rakushka can enjoy a à la carte breakfast.

The guesthouse has a playground. You can play ping pong at Guest House Rakushka, and the area is popular for cycling.

The accommodation provides an ironing service, as well as business facilities like fax and photocopying.

Anapa is 15 miles from Guest House Rakushka. The nearest airport is Anapa Airport, 11 miles from the property.
