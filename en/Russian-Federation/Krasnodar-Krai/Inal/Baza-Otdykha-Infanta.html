Description

The rooms in the resort are equipped with a TV with cable channels. Rooms come with a electric tea pot, while some rooms will provide you with a kitchenette with a microwave. All guest rooms at Baza Otdykha Infanta have air conditioning and a closet.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at the accommodation.

Tuapse is 27 miles from the resort, while Yuzhniy is 30 miles away. Gelendzhik Airport is 35 miles from the property.
