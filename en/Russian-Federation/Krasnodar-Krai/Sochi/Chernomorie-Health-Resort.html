Description

All rooms in the health resort are equipped with a TV. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, and certain rooms here will provide you with a balcony. All guest rooms feature a closet.

Guests at Cher can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

The property has an indoor pool. You can play pool at the accommodation.

Chernomorie Health Resort also provides a business center and guests can use the on-site ATM machine at the health resort. Staff at reception can help around the clock with advice on the area.

Sochi Summer Theatre is 0.7 miles from Cher, while Sochi Circus is 0.8 miles from the property. Sochi International Airport is 14 miles away.
