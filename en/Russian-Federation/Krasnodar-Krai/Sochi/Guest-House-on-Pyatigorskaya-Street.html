Description

Guest House on Pyatigorskaya Street is located in Sochi, a 10-minute drive from the Black Sea coast.

Each studio here will provide you with a TV and a fully equipped kitchen. Private bathroom also comes with a shower.

You can enjoy mountain view and garden view from the rooms. At Guest House on Pyatigorskaya Street you will find a terrace.

Other facilities offered at the property include laundry facilities. Dendrary Park is a 10-minute drive from the guest house.

Sochi Central Train Station is 4 km away, and Sochi International Airport is 28 km from the property.
