Description

Each unit features a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge, a seating area with a sofa, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with hot tub and free toiletries. There's a fully equipped shared bathroom with bath and a hair dryer.

Guests at the villa can enjoy a continental or a buffet breakfast.

The wellness area at Villa OSTROVA Spa Hotel is comprised of a sauna and an indoor pool.

An outdoor swimming pool is available for guests to use at the accommodation.

Green Theatre is 1.2 miles from Villa OSTROVA Spa Hotel, while Sochi Winter Theatre is 2.6 miles away. The nearest airport is Sochi International Airport, 16 miles from the property.
