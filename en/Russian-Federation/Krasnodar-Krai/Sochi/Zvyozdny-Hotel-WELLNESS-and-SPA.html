Description

Located a 15-minute walk of the private Black Sea beach in Sochi, Zvyozdny Hotel & SPA features a seasonal outdoor heated pool, fitness centre, spa centre and indoor pool.

It consists of 2 buildings - the main building and the business building. A free shuttle service is provided to the Black Sea private beach from June until September.

The rooms are air-conditioned and offer a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a minibar.

The bathrooms feature a hairdryer. A daily buffet breakfast is served at the property.

Biblioteka gastrobar is available on site. Guests can also eat out in the cafes and restaurants nearby.

Guests are invited to play Russian billiards in the hotel. In summer, animation and discotheque for children, as well as dance classes and water polo tournaments are available.

The hotel is 600 metres from the Riviera Park, and the Olympic Park is 37 km from the property.

Sochi International Airport is 30 km from Zvyozdny Hotel & SPA. Shuttle service and excursions are available at surcharge.
