Description

Within walking distance of many of Sochi's attractions including winter theatre, Festivalny Concert Hall and Water Park, this elegant hotel offers comfortable accommodation, including buffet breakfast and a charming garden.

Nairi Hotel is conveniently situated in Sochi's city centre, not far from Kurortny Prospect.

Many of the spacious guest rooms have lovely sea views and all have air conditioning and a large work desk.

There is a free wireless internet hotspot in public areas. There is a 24-hour front desk at your convenience.
