Description

A large water park, private beach and sports complex with tennis courts are featured at this 4-star resort.

Guests are offered free medical services. The property specialises in cardiovascular, urological and skin diseases, along with nervous disorders and diseases of the endocrine and musculoskeletal systems.

This health resort is a 10-minute drive of Sochi town centre. Oktyabrskiy Resort’s health centre features a gym, 4 saunas and beauty salon.

Guests are welcome to use the hotel’s entertainment centre with cinema, or visit the mini zoo.

The air-conditioned rooms at Oktyabrsky each have a balcony with views of the Black Sea Coast or the Caucasus Mountains.

Each room is equipped with cable TV and a kettle. A range of Caucasian dishes are served in the poolside Café Oasis, and a buffet breakfast is provided every morning.

Exotic cocktails can be enjoyed at the bar. The hotel is a 30-minute drive from Adler Airport and 5 km from Sochi Train Station.
