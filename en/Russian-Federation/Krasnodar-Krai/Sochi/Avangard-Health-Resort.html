Description

Located at the Black Sea coast and featuring a private beach, Avangard Health Resort in Sochi offers an indoor pool, children’s playground and paid parking at a location nearby.

Free WiFi is available on site. The resort offers different treatment services. The rooms offer a flat-screen TV with cable channels and safety deposit box.

Certain rooms have a balcony with a sea or garden view. Guests can dine at the resort’s buffet restaurant or eat out nearby.

Children are offered a meal 6 times a day. There is also a sauna and beauty salon on site.

You can play a game of billiards at the property. Children can visit a plunge pool, games room, Lego- centre with X-box, sensorineural room and children's psychologist appointment.

The Olympic Park is 27 km from the property. Dendrary Park is a 2-minute walk away.

Sochi International Airport is 35 km away and Sochi Train Station is within 5 km of Avangard Health Resort.
