Description

Located on the territory of the Olympic University in Sochi, a short walk to the beach on the coast of the Black Sea, Brevis Apart-Hotel offers self-catering accommodation with free Wi-Fi.

The modern apartments feature a balcony. They have a living room with a sofa and flat-screen satellite TV.

The bathrooms come with a bath and free toiletries. Guests can cook at the fully equipped kitchens.

Cafes are a 5-minute walk from Brevis Apart-Hotel. The Concert Hall Festival is also nearby.

Free private parking is available. The nearest bus stop is 100 metres from the property.
