Description

Sea Galaxy Hotel Congress & Spa is located in the centre of Sochi, right by Frunze Park and just 100 metres from the Black Sea coast.

The central Kurortny Avenue is 300 metres away and Dendrarium Park is a 10-minute walk of the hotel.

The Summer Theatre and the Sochi State Circus are within a 3-minute walk away. The bright, air-conditioned rooms offer warm-coloured interiors and classic-style décor.

Each room is hypoallergenic and includes a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a private bathroom with a shower and free WiFi.

All rooms also have a balcony with a view of the sea and the mountains. Sea Galaxy Hotel Congress & Spa features an à la carte restaurant, a buffet, a 24/7 bar and a lobby bar.

The hotel restaurant has its own confectionary. Outdoor saltwater pool and private beach are available at surcharge at other properties.

The property offers 5 conference rooms with a capacity from 100 to 500 people. There are also 2 banquet halls seating up to 200 guests.

Sochi Train Station is within a 5-minute drive away and the Port of Sochi is 3 km from the hotel.
