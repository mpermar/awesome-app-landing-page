Description

Zolotoy Kolos Health Resort is located in the centre of Sochi, a 10-minute walk of the private beach on the Black Sea coast.

Sochi Dendrarium Park and some other city attractions are a walking distance from the resort.

Free WiFi is featured at the property. Property specialises on cardiovascular, nervous and musculoskeletal systems diseases, as well as gynecological, urological and digestive organs.

Guests are offered water mud treatment, functional and laboratory diagnostics, speleotherapy and massage services.

Guests can get a consultancy of such specialists as neurologist, cardiologist, urologist, gastroenterologist, coloproctologist and dentist.

Various spa treatment is also offered at surcharge. Rooms are decorated in classic style and provide an air conditioning.

Each room, suite and apartment comes with a TV, fridge and a private bathroom. Some rooms feature a balcony with a city view.

Guests can relax in the on-site sauna, play billiards or have a workout at the fitness centre free of charge.

There are also basketball, volleyball and football fields for sports lovers. A children’s playground is available for young guests.

Entertainment events and cinema are available every evening at Zolotoy Kolos health resort.

Sochi Central Train Station is within a 5-minute drive of the property, and Sochi International Airport is 25 km from Zolotoy Kolos Health Resort.
