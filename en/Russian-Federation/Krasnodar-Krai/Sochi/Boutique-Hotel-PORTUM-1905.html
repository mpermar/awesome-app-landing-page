Description

Located in Sochi, in the historical building dating back to the beginning of the 20th century, Boutique Hotel Portum 1905 features air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi.

The property has a bar, as well as a restaurant serving pizza cuisine. The property provides a 24-hour front desk.

At the hotel, every room has a desk. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with free toiletries, while some units at the property also provide guests with a balcony.

All units will provide guests with a fridge. A buffet breakfast is served each morning at the property.

The accommodation offers a terrace. Guests at Boutique Hotel Portum 1905 will be able to enjoy activities in and around Sochi, like hiking.

Green Theatre is a 3-minute walk from the hotel. Adler-Sochi International Airport is 24 km from the property.
