Description

Akter Health Resort is located in Sochi, on the Black Sea beachfront. The property is 8 km from Sochi Train Station and 29 km from Sochi-Adler International Airport.

Various treatment services are available after doctors' consultancy. The air-conditioned rooms feature a TV and a refrigerator.

They are all easily accessible by elevator. Guests are welcome to the on-site restaurant which serves buffet meals in summer and a-la-carte dishes in winter.

Diet menu is also available. Guests can visit an indoor swimming pool with sea water from October until May, and a private equipped beach.

The resort also offers a spacious sauna and massage service. Akter Health Resort also has a children’s playground and evening entertainment.
