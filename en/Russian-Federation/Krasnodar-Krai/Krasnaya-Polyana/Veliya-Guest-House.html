Description

Located in Krasnaya Polyana, a short 10-minute walk from the centre, Veliya Guest House is a rustically inspired mountain accommodation with a sauna, hammam, Russian steam bath and plunging pool available on site.

Guests are offered massage services. All of the rooms here come with a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom with a bath or shower, and free Wi-Fi access.

Guests can order a meal in the on-site café. Skiing equipment can be stored and dried in the guest house.

On the mountain river bank there is a terrace where barbecue facilities are offered.

Various ski resorts are 5 km from the property. Adler Train Station is 43 km away, and Adler-Sochi International Airport is 40 km from Veliya Guest House.
