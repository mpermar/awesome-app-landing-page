Description

Hotel Olivia Anapa is located in Anapa and features an outdoor heated pool with hot tub and children's section.

The hotel is within a 10-minute walk of the market and a 30-minute walk of the beach.

Free private parking is available on site. The air-conditioned rooms feature a TV and a fridge.

Guests can use private bathroom facilities. Buffet meals are served at the canteen.

A dietary and kids' menus are provided upon request. There is a sun terrace with sun chairs at the property.
