Description

Situated in Anapa, 550 metres from the Black Sea shore, Dublin & Dolphin Hotel features a terrace and 24-hour front desk.

The rooms offer air conditioning, a fridge and a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Guests can dine in the hotel's restaurant or use on-site barbecue facilities. Room service and breakfasts in the room are provided upon request.

AnapaTrain Station is 2 km away, and Anapa Airport is 10 km from Dublin & Dolphin Hotel.
