Description

Located 700 metres from the private beach in Anapa, Slavyanka Hotel features an outdoor pool, a garden and parking.

A free shuttle service to the hotel's private beach is available daily. The property offers accommodation in cottages and wooden houses as well as rooms in the main building.

The rooms offer air conditioning, a fridge and a flat-screen TV. A balcony or terrace is also featured in the rooms.

Guests can dine in the hotels restaurant, and there is also a bar on site. WiFi is free and available in public areas.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including tennis, billiards and table tennis.

Anapa Train Station is 6. 4 km away, and Anapa International Airport is 11. 2 km from Slavyanka Hotel.
