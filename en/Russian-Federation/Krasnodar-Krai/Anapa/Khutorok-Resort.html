Description

Khutorok Resort is located just 300 metres from the Black Sea sandy beach. It features an outdoor swimming pool.

Free private parking is provided. All bright rooms come with air conditioning, a TV and a safety deposit box.

Various cafes, bars and restaurants can be found within a 10-minute walk. Khutorok Resort offers children animation service.

Kavkaz bus stop is located in front of the property, and the trip to Anapa town centre takes 30 minutes.

Aquapark and Dolphinarium are within a 10-minute drive away. Anapa Central Station is 16 km away, ant the distance to Vityazevo Airport is 10 km.
