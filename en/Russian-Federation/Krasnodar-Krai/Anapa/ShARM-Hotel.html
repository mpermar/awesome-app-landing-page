Description

Situated in Anapa, 5 km from Nemo Anapa Dolphinarium, ShARM Hotel features accommodation with a bar, free private parking, a shared lounge and barbecue facilities.

Boasting family rooms, this property also provides guests with a children's playground.

The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, a concierge service and free WiFi throughout the property.

At the hotel, rooms include a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. A continental breakfast is available each morning at ShARM Hotel.

The accommodation offers a sun terrace. Gorgippiya Anapa Archeological Museum is 10 km from ShARM Hotel, while The Lighthouse - Anapa is 11 km from the property.
