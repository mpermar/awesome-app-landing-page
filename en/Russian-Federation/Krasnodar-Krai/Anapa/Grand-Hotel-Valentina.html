Description

Classic-style, air-conditioned rooms at the Grand Hotel Valentina include a flat-screen TV, minibar, and high-quality furnishings. The private bathrooms feature a hairdryer, bathrobe and slippers.

The elegant Freezia Restaurant has breakfast and a choice of European, Mediterranean and Eastern cuisine. Drinks can be enjoyed in the lobby bar.

Visitors enjoy use of the fitness center. In the spa center, guests can relax in the hot tub, massage room, or in the traditional Russian and Finnish saunas.

Anapa International Airport is a 20-minute drive from the Valentina. The Black Sea coastline and beautiful beach is a few minutes away by car.
