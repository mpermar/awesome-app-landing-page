Description

Offering an outdoor pool, Aleksandria Guest House is located in Su-Psekh, a 15-minute walk from the Black Sea coast.

Anapa is within a 10-minute drive. Free WiFi access is available. Each room here will provide you with a flat-screen TV, air conditioning and a balcony.

There is also a refrigerator. Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer.

You can enjoy sea view from the room. The on-site restaurant serves European cuisine.

Other facilities offered at the property include an ironing service. The property offers free parking.

Anapa Train Station is 12 km away, and Vityazevo Airport is 20 km from the guest house.
