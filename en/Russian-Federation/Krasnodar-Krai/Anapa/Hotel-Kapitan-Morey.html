Description

Kapitan Morey Hotel is located in Anapa, 10 minutes walk from the Black Sea sandy beach.

It features an outdoor swimming pool and a free private parking. All bright rooms come with air conditioning, a TV and a fridge.

Free toiletries are provided in a bathroom fitted with a shower cabin. The on-site canteen serves full-board meals upon request.

Guests can relax in the sauna, order massage services or to play billiards. Also Kapitan Morey Hotel offers the childrens playground.

Tik-Tak Water Park is just in front of the property, and the Dolphinarium is within a 15-minute walk.

The distance to Anapa city centre is 5 km. Anapa Train Station is 1. 5 km from the hotel, and Vityazevo Airport is 10 km away.
