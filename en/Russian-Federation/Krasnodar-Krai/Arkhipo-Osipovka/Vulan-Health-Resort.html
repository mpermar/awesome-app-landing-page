Description

All guest rooms at the health resort are fitted with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a shower. The rooms include a kettle, while selected rooms also feature a balcony and others also have sea views. The rooms at Sanatorium Vulan include air conditioning and a desk.

Meal for adults is served 4 times a day, and for children meal is served 5 times a day.

You can play table tennis and darts. Featuring an ATM, this property also provides guests with a children's playground.

Gelendzhik is 26 mi from the accommodations, while Divnomorskoye is 22 mi from the property. The nearest airport is Gelendzhik Airport, 30 mi from the property.
