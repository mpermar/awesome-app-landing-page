Description

Located in the centre of Armavir, this hotel offers free Wi-Fi, free parking and a sauna with rustic plunge pool.

Armavir Rostovskiy Train Station is 1 km away. Villa Club has air-conditioned rooms and suites with flat-screen TVs and DVD players.

Interiors feature elegant curtains and chandeliers, and some rooms are decorated in the style of a log cabin.

Your modern, tiled bathroom includes bathrobes, slippers and toiletries. Some rooms feature a spa tub.

Guests can enjoy breakfast in the Villas’ cosy café, or in their own room. European cuisine is served in the restaurant.

For your relaxation, table tennis facilities and a steam room are provided. Massages can also be booked.

Armavir Drama Theatre is a 10-minute walk away. Villa Club’s 24-hour reception staff can help book tickets for this and other local attractions.
