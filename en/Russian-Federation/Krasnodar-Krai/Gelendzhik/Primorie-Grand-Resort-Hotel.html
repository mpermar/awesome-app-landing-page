Description

This modern hotel is located in the centre of Gelendzhik, 80 metres from the Black Sea coast.

Primorie SPA Hotel offers a luxury spa with an indoor heated swimming pool with sea water.

A gourmet restaurants are offered on site. The comfortable rooms feature climate control system, a balcony, minibar and satellite TV.

The modern bathrooms include exclusive toiletries, bathrobes and slippers. Offering European design, Panorama Banquet Restaurant offers buffet-style meals and Ã  la carte menu.

It can fit up to 125 guests and has a panoramic park view. There is also a 3-storied Sea House Restaurant with a swimming pool and a pebble beach.

The spa at the Primorie Hotel includes a sauna, steam room, spa bath and massages, and there is a modern gym and tennis court.

The spa and wellness centre also offers a Finnish sauna, hammam, infrared sauna and various spa procedures.

The property features meeting facilities. Private parking is available at the Primorie SPA Hotel & Wellness.
