Description

Atlas Hotel is located in Gelendzhik, 10-minute walk from the embankment, and offers free billiards and a heated outdoor pool.

Free WiFi is available on site. The simply furnished rooms at Atlas Hotel feature homelike dcor and parquet floors.

All air-conditioned rooms include a TV, a fridge and a private bathroom with shower.

Breakfast and a selection of Russian and European dishes are offered in the caf, along with children menu.

Drinks can be enjoyed at the well-stocked bar or outside on the terrace. Atlas Hotel offers free secured parking and a 24-hour reception includes a tour desk.

A free shuttle is provided from and to Gelendzhik Airport and train station. A paid shuttle is available from and to any destination in Krasnodar Krai.

A Safari Park and the Begemot Water Park are within a 10-minute walk of the hotel.

Many shops and restaurants are to be found in Gelendzhik town centre, a 20-minute walk away.
