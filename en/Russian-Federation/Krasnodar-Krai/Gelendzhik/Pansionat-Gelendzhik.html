Description

Located 100 metres from the Black Sea beach in Gelendzhik, this pansionat features an outdoor pool, a 24-hour reception and a children’s playground.

Each air-conditioned room at Pansionat Gelendzhik features a large window with the sea or city view.

The rooms are equipped with a fridge and TV. A private bathroom comes with a bath or a shower.

European dishes, as well as fresh fruit are served in the Gelendzhik’s bright café.

Several restaurants and bars can also be found within a 5-minute walk of the pansionat.

Staraya Avtostantsiya bus stop is within a 5-minute walk of Pansionat Gelendzhik, offering a link to Gelendzhik Bus Station.

Begemot Water Park is a 5-minute drive away, and Gelendzhik Airport is 10 km away.
