Description

This hotel is set in Golubaya Bay of Gelendzhik, a 5-minute walk from the Black Sea.

A 24-hour front desk are featured at Dolce Vita Hotel. The bright, air-conditioned rooms offer warm-coloured interiors and classic-style dcor.

Each room comes with a TV, a refrigerator and a private bathroom. The on-site restaurant serves European cuisine, and a variety of drinks is offered at the bar.

A bus stop, providing access to Gelendzhik city centre (10 km) is 300 metres from Dolce Vita Hotel.
