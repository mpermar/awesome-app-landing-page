Description

Just 30 metres from the Black Sea beach in Gelendzhik, Brigantina Hotel offers free WiFi.

Brigantina Hotel’s rooms feature air conditioning and flat-screen TV. Guests can relax with a cup of tea on a balcony with mountain or sea views.

A private bathroom comes with a hairdryer and free toiletires. A daily breakfast is served at the hotel, and room service is available.

There are on-site bar and restaurant at the property where guests can have lunch and dinner.

The area is popular for snorkeling, diving and cycling. The hotel is 7 km from Gelendzhik Airport.
