Description

This hotel is located in the centre of Gelendzhik, a 10-minute walk from the Black Sea beach.

Free Wi-Fi and a 24-hour reception are featured at Vavilon Hotel. The bright, air-conditioned rooms offer warm-coloured interiors and classic-style dcor.

Each room includes a flat-screen TV, a refrigerator and a private bathroom. A variety of cafs and restaurants can be found within a 5-minute walk of the hotel.

The hotel provides meals at a surcharge. A transfer service in Krasnodar is provided for guests of Vavilon Hotel at a surcharge.
