Description

Located 5 minutes’ walk from the Black Sea beach in Gelendzhik town, this hotel features a sauna and a sun terrace with panoramic views of Gelendzhik Bay.

It offers rooms with a fridge. Each air-conditioned room at Afrika Hotel is decorated in brown and beige tones.

All rooms come with a flat-screen TV, and bathrooms are fitted with complimentary toiletries.

Guests of Hotel Afrika can relax in the sauna or on the summer terrace enjoying the views of the bay.

Dolphin Water Park is 10 minutes’ walk away. Gelendzhik Bus Station is 5 minutes’ drive from Afrika Hotel, while Gelendzhik Airport is 20 minutes’ drive away.
