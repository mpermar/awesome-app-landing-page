Description

Kremnievye Termy Spa Resort features a spa and wellness centre with outdoor hydromassage pools and saunas.

Free WiFi and free private parking are available on site. The rooms with a seating area are equipped with air conditioning, flat-screen TV and fridge.

The bathrooms offer hairdryers, bathrobes, towels and free toiletries. Zubrovka the on-site restaurant serves organic food and offers Russian, European, Caucasian, Pan-Asian cuisine.

Guests can also use a gazebo with barbecue facilities. Room service is provided upon request.

Tennis, mini-football, beach and outdoor volleyball, badminton, cycling and fishing can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings.

Guests can play board games (chess, backgammon, dominoes) and visit the zoo. Maykop Train Station is 35 km away, and Krasnodar is 170 km from Kremnievye Termy Spa Resort.
