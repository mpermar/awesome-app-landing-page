Description

Krylya Mini Hotel is set in Abrau-Dyurso and features on-site dining. Boasting a 24-hour front desk, this property also provides guests with a sun terrace.

The inn features a hot tub and ticket service. The units in the inn are fitted with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Featuring a private bathroom, rooms at Krylya Mini Hotel also have free WiFi, while certain rooms have a balcony.

All guest rooms feature a wardrobe. A continental breakfast is served daily at the property.

At the accommodation guests are welcome to take advantage of a sauna and a Turkish bath, as well as hookah which is offered at the terrace.

Anapa is 30 km from Krylya Mini Hotel, while Gelendzhik is 42 km from the property.
