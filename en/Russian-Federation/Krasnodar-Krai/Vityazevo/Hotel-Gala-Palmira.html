Description

Offering an outdoor swimming pool, Gala Palmira Hotel is located within 8 minutes walk to the beach on the coast of the Black Sea in Vityazevo.

Free Wi-Fi and parking are available. The elegant rooms are air conditioned and offer a flat-screen TV and fridge.

The private bathrooms come with a shower, hairdryer and free toiletries. Some rooms have a balcony.

Gala Palmira Hotel has a seasonal caf. Other dining options are a 5-minute walk away.

Laundry and ironing services are available. The nearest bus stop is a 2-minute walk away.
