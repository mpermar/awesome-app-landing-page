Description

Offering a private beach area, Park Hotel Dobrograd is situated in Velikovo. Free WiFi is provided throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Some units include a dining area and/or terrace. A minibar and kettle are also featured.

Some units also have a kitchen, fitted with a dishwasher. There is a private bathroom with bathrobes in each unit.

Towels and bed linen are offered. Park Hotel Dobrograd also includes a wellness centre, featuring sauna complex and massage services.

Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant, fitness centre and beauty salon. Various meeting and banquet facilities are also available.

Younger guests can appreciate a kid's club. You can play tennis at the property, and the area is popular for skiing.
