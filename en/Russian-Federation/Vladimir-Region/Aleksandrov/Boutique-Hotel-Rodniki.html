Description

This modern hotel is set in the wild forested area 80 km from Moscow and 22 km from Sergiyev Posad.

It consists of several high-tech cottages and includes a spa center with a sauna and massage services.

The spacious, air-conditioned suites and cottages of Boutique Hotel Rodniki offer elegant interiors with floor to ceiling windows and stylish décor.

Every spacious accommodation offers one or more bedrooms, a living room with a flat-screen TV and a real wood fireplace, a refrigerator, a coffee machine, a balcony and a private bathroom.

The on-site restaurant serves European cuisine. Guests can enjoy the quiet and remoteness of the cottages combined with the luxury atmosphere of a comfortable chalet.

Strunino Train Station is 8 km from Boutique Hotel Rodniki. Sheremetyevo International Airport is 95 km away.
