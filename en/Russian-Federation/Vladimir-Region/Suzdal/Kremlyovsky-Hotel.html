Description

In the heart of Suzdal, the 4-star Kremlyovsky Hotel features an on-site restaurant serving traditional Russian cuisine.

Free Wi-Fi is available in all public areas. All rooms at Kremlyovsky are air conditioned and equipped with satellite TV.

Each is individually designed and comes with a minibar and a safety deposit box. The private bathrooms come with free toiletries.

Each morning, guests can enjoy breakfast, which consists of fresh juice, pastries, a selection of traditional Russian choices and authentic Italian coffee.

The Russkaya Trapeza restaurant has three halls and offers roast duck and goose as well as a range of fine wines.

The restaurant features Russian cuisine, the menu translated into the Chinese language is available.

Hotel Kremlyovsky organises various hunting and fishing trips along with guided city tours.

The property has a garden with gazebos. This property is 500 metres from the Suzdal Kremlin, ancient convents and the Museum of Wooden Architecture.
