Description

Featuring free WiFi and saunas, BKC Country offers accommodation at the banks of the Kirzhach River, 5 km from Pokrov, The hotel has spacious premises, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Every unit includes a TV. Certain rooms feature a seating area for your convenience.

You will find a kettle in the room. The rooms are equipped with a private bathroom.

The property has its own farm providing ecological and fresh products. Every day guests receive an hour of extra services of facilities of their choice free of charge.

These include sports equipment, badminton, table or lawn tennis, billiards. gym or fitness centre, darts, bike hire, etc.
