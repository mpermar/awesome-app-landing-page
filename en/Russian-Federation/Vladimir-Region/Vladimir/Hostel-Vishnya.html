Description

Hostel Vishnya is located in Vladimir and offers a shared lounge, a games room and a tour desk.

Free WiFi access is available. The property features simply decorated rooms designed in bright colours.

Guests can use a shared bathroom. You can cook in the shared kitchen equipped with a microwave, stove, kettle and fridge.

The Golden Gate is 2 km from Hostel Vishnya and Vladimir Train Station is within a 5-minute walk.
