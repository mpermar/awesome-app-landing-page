Description

Offering a 24-hour front desk and bicycle rental, Vozduh MiniHotel is located the heart of Vladimir, a 5-minute walk from the Golden Gate.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. The rooms come with either shared or private bathrooms.

Guests can cook in the shared kitchen, and various cafés and restaurants are located within 50 metres of the property.
