Description

Samovar Hostel is located in Vladimir city centre, 500 metres from Zolotye Vorota and 200 metres from Patriarshiy Sad.

Free Wi-Fi access is available. Some rooms come with a wardrobe. Guests use shared bathrooms.

At Samovar Hostel you will find a garden and a terrace. Other facilities offered include a shared lounge, a ticket service and a tour desk.

The property offers free parking. The distance to Vladimir Train Station is 4 km from Samovar Hostel.
