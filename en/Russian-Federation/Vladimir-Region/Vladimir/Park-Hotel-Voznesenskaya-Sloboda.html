Description

Rooms and apartments at the Park Hotel Voznesenskaya Sloboda feature large windows and antique furnishings. They come equipped with a TV and minibar, and have balconies with panoramic views of the countryside.

Guests of the Voznesenskaya Sloboda can enjoy a breakfast buffet on the outdoor terrace. Restaurant Krucha serves Russian and international cuisine. There's also a bar and a steak house.

Park Hotel Voznesenskaya Sloboda is 7.5 miles from Bogolyubovo. The hotel has free on-site private parking.

Leninsky is a great choice for travelers interested in temples, museums and scenery.
