Description

Offering indoor pool, spa and wellness centre, Hotel Club Veles is located in Dvoriki village.

Free Wi-Fi access is available. All rooms come with a flat-screen TV, a minibar and a DVD Player.

Featuring a hairdryer, private bathrooms are also equipped with free toiletries and slippers.

At Hotel Club Veles you will find a la carte restaurant with European and Russian dishes.

Other facilities include entertainment staff, a nightclub and meeting facilities.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including horse riding, skiing and billiards.
