Description

Located at the banks of Vyshnevolotskoye Reservoir in Vyshny Volochyok, Zdeshevo Holiday Park offers a sauna and free parking.

The rooms feature a sofa, microwave, electric kettle and flat-screen TV. Guests can dine in the property’s restaurant, or use on-site barbecue facilities.

There is also a bar in the holiday park. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including windsurfing, wakeboarding, kiting, fishing and cycling.
