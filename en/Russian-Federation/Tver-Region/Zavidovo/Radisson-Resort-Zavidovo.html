Description

Surrounded by forests, this resort is located on the bank of Ivankovskoye Reservoir in Zavidovo.

A private beach, indoor pool, and gym are featured at Radisson Resort, Zavidovo. A spa centre comes with a sauna, hammam and a hot tub.

Free Wi-Fi is provided. There is also a kids' club on site. The elegant, air-conditioned rooms offer warm-coloured interiors and classic-style décor.

Every room comes with a flat-screen TV, a minibar, a balcony and a private bathroom Riverbank Restaurant serves buffet breakfast, lunch and dinner, while Lake Grill Restaurant offers elegant grilled dishes.

Waterfront Lobby Bar and Yacht Club Bar provide a variety of drinks and light snacks.

Coffee Dock café guarantees fresh-baked muffins and Promenade Take Away snack bar features a variety of packed dishes and ice cream that guests can take to the beach or to a sailing trip.

Within a 2-minute drive of the property there is the PGA golf course. The resort offers also a mini-golf course located on site.

Yachting, windsurfing, fishing, horse riding and other activities can be enjoyed in the surroundings.

M10 Highway is 6 km away, and Zavidovo Train Station is 12 km from the property. Moscow is 130 km away.
