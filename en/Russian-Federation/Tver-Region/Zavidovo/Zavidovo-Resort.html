Description

Resort Zavidovo Resort is located on the banks of the river reservoir Ivankovskoye.

It offers free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel, free private parking and a cinema hall.

There is a large children's playground area with animators. Rooms are equipped with cable TV.

The private bathroom provides free toiletries, slippers and bathrobes. The spa complex and the pool is a 15-minute drive away.

Local activities include windsurfing, horse riding and fishing with an an experienced guide.

M10 highway is 5 km away. Zavidovo Train Station is 7 km away, while Moscow is 110 km from the property.
