Description

Located on the Rybinskiy Reservoir in the quiet town of Vesyegonsk, this rustic hotel offers country-style rooms, a traditional restaurant with bar, and a sauna.

Free Wi-Fi is provided. Rooms at Alexandrovsky Prichal are fitted with wooden furniture and include a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Each has an en suite bathroom with hairdryer. Russian cuisine is served at the Alexandrovsky Prichal Hotel.

Eggs for continental-style breakfast are provided from the property's chicken and pheasant coops.

A range of beers and wines can be enjoyed at the bar or on the terrace, which has barbecue facilities.

Guests can go fishing or hire boats at the reservoir. Sights in the area include the Vesyegonsk History Museum and the Kirika Church, which are within a 10-minute drive of the hotel.
