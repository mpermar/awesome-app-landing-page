Description

Located at the banks of Lake Seliger, in a quiet area surrounded by nature, Ekaterininskaya Sloboda features an on-site restaurant serving Russian cuising, bicycle and boat rentals, and a steam room on the banks of the lake.

Rooms at the holiday home feature a TV, towels and linens, and a private bathroom with a shower.

Other facilities at the property include a bar, a childrens playground, a shared kitchen, a shared lounge area, and laundry facilities.

A number of activities can be enjoyed here, including cycling, fishing, hiking, and horse riding.
