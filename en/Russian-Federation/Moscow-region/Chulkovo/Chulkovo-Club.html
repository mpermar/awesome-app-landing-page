Description

Located in Ramenskoye in Moscow region, 32 km from Moscow, Chulkovo Club boasts a spa centre, fitness centre, indoor and outdoor swimming pools.

Guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Each room is equipped with a flat-screen TV and offers a private bathroom with a bath or a shower.

Extras include bathrobes and free toiletries. You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property.

Chulkovo Club offers a wine collection and karaoke. Younger guests will enjoy the children's playground.
