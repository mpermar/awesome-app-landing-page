Description

A wooden Russian-style holiday park located in Electrostal, Russkaya Usadba offers 4 houses with apple orchard.

A 5-bedroom house, Russkaya Usadba also includes a seating area with a fireplace and a satellite channel TV.

The bathrooms are fitted with a shower. There is a large dining area and a fully equipped open plan kitchen with kitchenware and a refrigerator.

At Russkaya Usadba guests can relax in a sauna. Skiing, cycling and hiking can be enjoyed in the surroundings.
