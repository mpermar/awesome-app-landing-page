Description

This hotel is set on the Pakhra River, 15 km south-east of Moscow in Grigorchikovo Village.

Free Wi-Fi and a private beach with boats are featured at Valesko Hotel. The bright, air-conditioned rooms offer warm-coloured interiors and modern décor.

Each room includes a flat-screen TV, a refrigerator, a balcony and a private bathroom.

The on-site restaurant serves European cuisine, and a variety of drinks is offered at the bar.

Barbecue facilities are also provided. A sauna, a solarium and massage services are available for guests’ relaxation at the hotel.

Fishing, table tennis and cycling are among the activities organised on site. A shuttle from Moscow or Domodedovo International Airport located 28 km away is available at Valesko Hotel upon request.
