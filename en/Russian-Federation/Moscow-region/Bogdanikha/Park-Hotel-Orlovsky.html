Description

Featuring a private sandy beach on Lyudovna lakeside, this neo-Russian-style hotel is located 13 km from MKAD Motorway in Moscow.

Each cottage at Orlovskiy Hotel comes with a country-style interior and wooden furnishings.

All cottages have a flat-screen TV and bathrooms include a hairdryer. Rooms of the main building feature air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, a minibar, a safety deposit box.

The bathroom is also private. Petrushka Restaurant, decorated as a traditional Russian peasant’s hut, serves both Russian and European cuisines.

Al fresco dining is available on the summer terrace, and cocktails are offered at the on-site bar.

Uniquely decorated karaoke bar welcomes guests on site. Guests of Orlovskiy can relax in the spa centre, featuring a sauna, a swimming pool, a hammam, salt chamber and a fitness area.

Beauty salon is also available. Barbecue facilities and sun terrace are available.

Water sports facilities are offered, and a small private zoo can also be found on site.

In winter guests can enjoy skiing, skating and tubing. Younger guests can enjoy a large children's room, a chocolate factory as well as game consoles.

Children's amination is also offered to guests of the Park-Hotel Orlovsky. Livadiya bus stop offering a link to Domodedovskaya Metro Station is 750 metres from Orlovskiy Hotel.
