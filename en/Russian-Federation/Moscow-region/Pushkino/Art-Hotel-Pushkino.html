Description

Surrounded by a garden full of live peacocks, this hotel is just 20 km from Moscow‚Äôs MKAD ring road.

Offering a swimming pool, spa treatments, and games room, it also organises tours within the Moscow region.

Behind a wooden-framed exterior, Art-Hotel Pushkino offers modern rooms, all featuring a TV, minibar and hairdryer.

Some include a separate bedroom and living room. Guests can relax with herbal tea in the spa area, sit in the sauna, or book beauty treatments, including massages and body wraps.

There is a playground and games room with billiards and table tennis. Breakfast and Eastern European cuisine is served in the Pushkino's antique-style restaurant.

Guests can enjoy drinks and karaoke at the bar. Nearby guests find nature hiking and ice skating facilities.

The M8 motorway is a 45-minute drive away, providing a direct connection to central Moscow.
