Description

Offering a restaurant, a terrace and a bar, Mini-hotel "Club Bon Ton" is located in Shustino, 65 km from Moscow.

Free WiFi access is available. The rooms and suites feature a desk and a garden view.

The private bathrooms have a bath or shower, bathrobes and free toiletries. At Mini-hotel "Club Bon Ton" you will find a garden, a shared lounge, and luggage and ski storage.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including hiking, skiing and cycling.

Sheremetyevo International Airport is 45 km away. The property offers free parking.
