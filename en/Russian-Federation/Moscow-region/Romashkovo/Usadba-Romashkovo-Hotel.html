Description

Hotel Romashkovo Farm is situated just 5 minutes’ drive from Crocus City Mall. It is built in the style of an ancient Russian farm.

The cosy rooms at Hotel Romashkovo Farm have wooden walls and country-style furnishings.

Each is equipped with a private bathroom and satellite TV. The in-house restaurant Pechka serves a mix of Russian, Italian and Japanese cuisines.
