Description

Surrounded by 420,000 m² of parkland and overlooking Kovershi Lake, this hotel outside Moscow offers spa and fitness facilities, and a private beach area with volleyball court and loungers.

Featuring antique, classical and oriental décor, all rooms at the Yakhonty Noginsk include a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and minibar.

Each has woodland or lakeside views. A buffet breakfast and European cuisine is served in the modern on-site restaurant.

Drinks are served at the karaoke bar and in the Laguna Summer Café with covered terrace.

The spa offers a sauna, indoor pool and massage service. Guests can go paintballing, bowling, and the Maximus nightclub opens in the evenings.

There is a small petting zoo and children’s club. Rental bicycles are available for exploring the surrounding countryside.

It is an ideal base for water sports, skiing and horse riding. The Moscow Ring motorway is 49 km away.
