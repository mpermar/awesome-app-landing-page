Description

Yakhontovy Les Apartments is situated in Yakhonty Resort, 5 km from Noginsk. Free WiFi is provided.

The pet-friendly accommodation is air conditioned and features a flat-screen TV. All units have a dining area and a balcony.

There is also a kitchen, fitted with a microwave. A refrigerator and stovetop are also available, as well as a kettle.

Each unit is fitted with a private bathroom with a bath. Towels are offered. You can play table tennis, billiards and mini-golf in the surroundings, and the area is popular for skiing.

You can engage in various activities, such as cycling and hiking. Moscow is 49 km from Yakhontovy Les Apartments.
