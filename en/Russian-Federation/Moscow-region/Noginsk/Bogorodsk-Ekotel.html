Description

Ekotel Bogorodsk Hotel is located in a picturesque park near Chernogolovsky Pond.

It features an indoor swimming pool and a wellness centre. Free Wi-Fi and private parking are provided.

The hotel’s restaurant offers European cuisine and is open 24 hours a day. All individually decorated rooms with classic-style interiors come with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and a fridge.

Ekotel Bogorodsk Hotel offers a sauna, a salt cave and a swimming pool with a hot tub.
