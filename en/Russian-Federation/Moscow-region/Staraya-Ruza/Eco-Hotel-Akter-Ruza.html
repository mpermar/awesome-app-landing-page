Description

Offering a sauna and Russian steam bath with massage, Eco Hotel Akter Ruza is located in natural surroundings on the bank of the Moscow River in Staraya Ruza.

Free WiFi access is available. The comfortable rooms have a flat-screen TV and fridge.

The private bathrooms come with a bath or shower. Some rooms are air conditioned.

This eco-friendly hotel offers a restaurant and a garden with BBQ faciltiies. Guests can enjoy hiking, skiing and fishing in the surroundings.
