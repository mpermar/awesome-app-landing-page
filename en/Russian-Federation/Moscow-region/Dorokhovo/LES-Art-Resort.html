Description

This countryside resort is set 70 km west of Moscow and it offers all inclusive system.

There are 3 spa centres on site, that feature an indoor pool, 2 outdoor heated pools, thermal zones and spa baths.

Restaurants, children's playgrounds, sports areas and a mini-zoo are also available to guests.

Free Wi-Fi is provided at LES Art Resort. The bright, air-conditioned rooms offer warm-coloured interiors and classic-style décor.

Each modern room includes a flat-screen TV, a refrigerator and a private bathroom with bathrobes and a hairdryer.

The on-site restaurant serves Russian and European cuisine, and a variety of drinks is offered at the bar.

Guests can play bowling, billiards and table tennis, or relax in the sauna or the Turkish steam bath.

A children’s playground with various entertainment programmes are available for young guests.

Dorokhovo Train Station is a 5-minute drive from LES Art Resort. Sheremetyevo International Airport is 100 km away.
