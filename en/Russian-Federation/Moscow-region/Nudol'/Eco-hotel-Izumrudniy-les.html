Description

Featuring free WiFi and a restaurant, Eco-hotel Izumrudniy les offers accommodation in Nudol'.

The hotel has a barbecue and spa centre, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Every room is equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Some units feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms. Extras include bathrobes and slippers.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. This hotel has ski storage space and ski equipment hire is available.

You can play billiards at this hotel, and bike hire is available. Klin is 35 km away, while Volokolamsk is 55 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Sheremetyevo International Airport, 59 km from Eco-hotel Izumrudniy les.
