Description

Featuring a large spa centre with aqua zone and a relaxation zone as well as a beauty salon, Velich Country Club is located 2.

5 km from Zvenigorod town centre. Free WiFi, a children's playground and, bike and ski equipment hire are also provided on site.

A shuttle service to Moscow city (40 km) is available upon request. Activities at Velich Club include billiards, bowling, tennis court and gym, and guests can take a dip into the indoor swimming pool that is located in the main building.

Rooms are located in four separate buildings. Every room is decorated in a modern style and comes with air conditioning, heated floors, a flat-screen TV, a minibar and a balcony.

Private bathrooms are fitted with slippers and bathrobes. Russian and European dishes as well as a buffet breakfast are served in Velich's two restaurants, and cocktails are offered at the bar.

The summer cafe offers a terrace on the roof. Room service is also available. A shuttle service to Sheremetyevo Airport (65 km) is available upon request.
