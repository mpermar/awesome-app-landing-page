Description

FDiamant is located in a quiet area near a forest in Denezhnikovo, a 20-minute drive from Domodedovo International Airport.

It offers comfortable rooms with free WiFi. Free bikes and parking are provided. The rooms feature a flat-screen cable TV.

The come with a fridge and seating area. The private bathrooms offer a bath or shower, hairdryer and free toiletries.

The restaurant at Diamant serves European cuisine and continental breakfast and on-site barbecue facilities are also available.

The hotel offers billiards and table tennis. Other hotel's facilities include a conference room, car rental and park area with gazebos.

The nearest bus stop is a 5-minute walk away. The property offers airport transfer service.
