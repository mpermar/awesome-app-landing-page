Description

Hotel Yacht Club Noviy Bereg is situated 7 km from Moscow on Pirogov Reservoir’s shores.

It offers free private parking and free WiFi in public areas and rooms. The classically furnished rooms at Noviy Bereg Hotel each have satellite TV and a work desk.

The private bathrooms come equipped with a hairdryer and free toiletries. Spa facilities at the Yach Club include indoor and outdoor swimming pools, a hot tub, a sauna, a hammam and a spa bath.

If offers water sports facilities. Guests are also welcome to use the karaoke equipment and pool tables.

Traditional Russian cuisine, European dishes and fine wines are served in the Restaurant Laguna.

Café Izba offers a variety of dishes, cooked on barbecue. Sheremetyevo Airport and the VDNKh Exhibition Centre can both be reached in less than 20 minutes by car.
