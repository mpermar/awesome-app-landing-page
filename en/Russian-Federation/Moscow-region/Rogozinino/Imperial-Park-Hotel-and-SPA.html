Description

The 5-star Imperial Park Hotel & SPA is situated in Moscow’s wooded Rogozinino suburb.

It offers a large sports hall and 3 restaurants. Trout fishing ponds are a 5 minutes’ walk.

Imperial Park Hotel’s classically decorated rooms feature Italian furnishings and pastel colors.

Each has a private balcony and a bathrobe and slippers. Room service is available throughout the day.

Restaurants Dvoryanskiy and Kupecheskiy serve classical Russian and European dishes.

Local beer, shish kebabs and other snacks are served at Beer Assembly. Café Confectionary serves a large selection of fresh-baked pastries.

Sports offered by Grand Arena Hall include bowling, tennis and shooting. Guests can ride horses or rental bicycles through the surrounding pine forests.

Imperial Park Hotel & Spa also has a 25-metre indoor pool and large spa offering massage treatments.

Moscow’s Ring Road can be reached with a 15 minutes’ drive. Vnukovo International Airport is less than 10 km from Imperial Park Hotel & SPA.
