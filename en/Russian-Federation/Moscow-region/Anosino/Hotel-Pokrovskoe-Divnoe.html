Description

Offering a children's playground, Hotel Pokrovskoe-Divnoe is situated in Anosino in the Moscow region, 25 km from the Moscow Ring Road.

Guests can enjoy the on-site Baskyul restaurant. Free WiFi is provided throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and is fitted with a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a minibar.

Certain units include a seating area where you can relax. You will find a kettle in the room.

Every room includes a private bathroom. Extras include free toiletries and a hair dryer.

There is a 24-hour front desk and canteen at the property. The property offers horse riding lessons.

Guests can visit a museum of military equipment. A petting farm is also available on site.

You can play table tennis and billiards at the hotel. Crocus Expo is 32 km from Hotel Pokrovskoe-Divnoe, while Zvenigorod is 14 km away.

The nearest airport is Sheremetyevo International Airport, 30 km from the property.
