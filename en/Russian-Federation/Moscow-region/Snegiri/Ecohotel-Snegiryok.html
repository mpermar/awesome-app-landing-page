Description

Offering a seasonal outdoor pool, barbecue and children's playground, Ecohotel Snegiryok is located in Snegiri in the Moscow region in Istra district.

Guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Free WiFi is offered throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

All rooms include a TV. Some rooms have a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. You can play tennis, table tennis and billiards at this hotel, and the area is popular for skiing.

The hotel also offers ski equipment hire. Khimki is 27 km from Ecohotel Snegiryok, while Zvenigorod is 20 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Sheremetyevo International Airport, 26 km from the property.
