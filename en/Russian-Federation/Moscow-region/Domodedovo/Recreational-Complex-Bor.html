Description

Offering a seasonal outdoor pool and barbecue, Recreational Complex Bor is set in Domodedovo.

Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant with live music. The property offers a variety of medical and spa treatments.

Certain units include a seating area for your convenience. A TV is featured. You will find a 24-hour front desk, a cash machine, hairdresser's and gift shop at the property.

This resort has a private beach area and ski equipment hire is available. Bike hire is available at this resort and its area is popular for skiing and horse riding.

Moscow is 44 km from Recreational Complex Bor, while Podolsk is 14 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Moscow Domodedovo Airport, 27 km from Recreational Complex Bor.
