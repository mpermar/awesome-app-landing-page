Description

Featuring free WiFi and a sun terrace, Inn Medovo House offers accommodation in Kotlyakovo, 15 km from Domodedovskaya Metro Station.

Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. Free private parking is available on site.

Each room at this inn is air conditioned and is fitted with a flat-screen TV. The rooms include a private bathroom.

Extras include slippers, free toiletries and a hairdryer. You will find free shuttle service at the property.

Podolsk is 19 km from Inn Medovo House, while Zheleznodorozhnyy is 32 km away. Moscow Domodedovo Airport is within a 12-minute drive of the property.
