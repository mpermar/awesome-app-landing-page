Description

Atlas Park-Hotel is situated at the picturesque district of Moscow region, 15 km from the Domodedovo International Airport.

The territory is about 50 hectares. Free private parking is available. Adults and children can easily find a favorite place for entertainment.

The property features a swimming pool, bowling and billiards. It also has a sauna, fitness centre and a kid's club.

The spa centre of Atlas Park Hotel offers massage, hairdresser's and beautician's services.

Modern and comfortable rooms are equipped with air-conditioning, TV, minibar, work desk and internet.

A great variety of bars and restaurants will not make you indifferent. Haute cuisine inspired by different countries will satisfy completely the tastes of delicate gourmets.

Combining of gorgeous nature and developed infrastructure is the perfect complement to well-run rest and European services of the Atlas Park-Hotel.
