Description

Fat Cat Hotel Domodedovo is located 10 km from the M5 motorway, providing a direct connection to Moscow city centre.

Guests can enjoy free Wi-Fi and free airport shuttle to Domodedovo Airport, 15 minutes' drive away.

Welcoming guests with a modern interior, all air-conditioned rooms at the Fat Cat Hotel Domodedovo come with a TV, telephone and a private bathroom with a heated floor and free toiletries.

The stylish restaurant offers a selection of Italian dishes. Drinks and snacks can be purchased from the vending machines.

A children’s playground with sandpit is located in the hotel garden. Guests can play table tennis on the sheltered outdoor terrace.

Domodedovo city centre is 20 minutes away by car and has metro services to Moscow.
