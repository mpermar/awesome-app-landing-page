Description

All guest rooms in the guesthouse are equipped with a electric tea pot. Sava Guest House provides certain accommodations with garden views, and rooms include a terrace.

Moscow is 19 miles from Sava Guest House, while Khimki is 30 miles from the property. Moscow Domodedovo Airport is 7 miles away.
