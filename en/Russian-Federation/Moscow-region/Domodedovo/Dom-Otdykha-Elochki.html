Description

Dom Otdykha Elochki, located 22 km from Moscow Ring Road, boasts a spa and fitness centre.

Free private parking is available on site. Each room at this hotel is air conditioned and is fitted with a TV.

Some units include a seating area where you can relax. Each room includes a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. Its welness centre is equipped with a sauna, a solarium and a hot tub.

You can enjoy various activities, like tennis, table tennis and darts at this hotel.

The hotel also offers bike hire. The nearest airport is Domodedovo International Airport, 17 km from Dom Otdykha Elochki.
