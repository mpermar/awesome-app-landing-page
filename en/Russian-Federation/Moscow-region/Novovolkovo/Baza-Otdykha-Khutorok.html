Description

Located in Novovolkovo in the Moscow region, Baza Otdykha Khutorok boasts a barbecue and terrace.

There is a bowling alley on site and guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant and bar.

All units have a seating area. There is also a kitchenette, equipped with a refrigerator.

There is a private bathroom with a bath or shower and free toiletries in each unit.

Towels are provided. Baza Otdykha Khutorok also includes a sauna. Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant, with grocery delivery available on request.

You can play table tennis at the property, and the area is popular for skiing. Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including snorkeling and cycling.

The nearest airport is Vnukovo International Airport, 68 km from Baza Otdykha Khutorok.
