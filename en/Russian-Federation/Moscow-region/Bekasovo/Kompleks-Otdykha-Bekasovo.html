Description

Spa-hotel "Kompleks Otdykha Bekasovo" is located in Podmoskovye forest area, Narofominsk region.

It features a spa and wellness centre with a sauna, swimming pool, hot tub and hammam.

The rooms come with satellite TV and a private bathroom. Some holiday homes provide a kitchen.

Guests can have full board meals in the resort restaurant or use on-site barbecue facilities.

Free WiFi is available in the public areas and free parking is provided. There is also a tennis court on site and guest can also enjoy bowling and billiards.

Zosimova Pustyn Train Station is 1 km away, and the Moscow ring road is 46 km Kompleks Otdykha Bekasovo.
