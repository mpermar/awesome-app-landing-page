Description

SkyPoint Hotel Sheremetyevo Airport offers a fitness centre with an indoor pool and a sauna.

A free shuttle service from Sheremetyevo International Airport and modern rooms with free Wi-Fi access are offered by this hotel.

Set on the outskirts of Moscow, it is surrounded by the Khimki Forest. Belorusskaya Metro Station can be reached by train in 35 minutes.

The hotel consists of two buildings. Each building features a room categories of different type and different facilities.

Modern light wooden furniture and rich fabrics are featured in each of the soundproofed rooms at SkyPoint Hotel.

Comforts include air-conditioning, a flat-screen TV, a working desk and a private bathroom with shower and free toiletries.

In the restaurant, guests can enjoy European cuisine or set menu in the evening. A buffet-style breakfast is also served every morning.

Business facilities are also available. Moscow’s famous sights such as the Kremlin and Red Square are 35 km from SkyPoint Hotel.

A free transfer to Crocus Expo Centre is provided upon request. Sheremetyevo International Airport is 800 metres from SkyPoint Hotel.

The Aeroexpress Train takes guests to Belorusskaya Metro Station within 35 minutes’.
