Description

The modern rooms are equipped with a private bathroom, satellite TV, 24-hour room service and free high-speed WiFi. Up to 2 children (16 years and under) stay free when sharing with parents.

Enjoy a hearty breakfast at the Cote Jardin Restaurant or try Fusion Grill & Bar for international dishes and a variety of drinks.

Guests can energize in the modern fitness center and use the swimming pool, sauna and hammam.

The Novotel Sheremetyevo Airport features 6 meeting rooms. The largest room caters to up to 350 guests. All public areas offer free Wi-Fi internet access. Crocus Expo is 12 mi away.
