Description

Located in Sergiyev Posad town, this hotel is a 5-minute walk from the Troitse-Sergiyev Monastery.

It features air-conditioned rooms and free Wi-Fi. Private parking is offered for free.

Decorated with elegant furniture, rooms at Imperial Village Hotel come with a TV, fridge and minibar.

Private bathrooms are fitted with a shower. Guests can enjoy Italian, Russian and European cuisine at the on-site restaurant, and relax at the bar.

Spa centre includes a sauna and massage service. The nearest bus stop, providing access around the town, is 300 metres from Imperial Village Hotel.
