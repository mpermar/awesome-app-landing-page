Description

Bowling, billiards and a sauna are featured at this hotel, set 10 km north of Moscow.

Free Wi-Fi and a 24-hour reception are offered at Novye Gorki Hotel. The bright hotel rooms offer warm-coloured interiors and classic-style décor.

Every room includes a TV and a private bathroom. The 24-hour Delight Restaurant serves Russian and European cuisine.

Vosmaya Liniya bus stop is a 7-minute walk from Novye Gorki Hotel. Sheremetyevo International Airport is 47 km away.
