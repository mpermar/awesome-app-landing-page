Description

Located in the Serpukhov Region, near the nature reserve, this hotel occupies a large area with an English-style park.

The Prioksko-Terrasny Biosphere Reserve is 12 km away. The elegant, classic-style rooms of the hotel include a flat-screen TV and a minibar.

Each air-conditioned room comes with a private bathroom and a balcony. Le Dome Restaurant serves Russian and European cuisine.

A bar is also available at the hotel, as well as an outdoor summer cafe. A variety of sports activities is on site, including an indoor swimming pool, volleyball ground and tennis court.

Adults can relax in the sauna, and a childrens playground is offered for young guests.

Avangard Train Station is a 15-minute walk from Vozdvizhenskoe Park Hotel. Moscow is 75 km from Moscow, and Domodedovo International Airport is 60 km away.
