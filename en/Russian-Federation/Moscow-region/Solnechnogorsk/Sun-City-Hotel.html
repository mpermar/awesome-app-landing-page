Description

Set in a shopping centre, Sun City Hotel is located in Solnechnogorsk town, 15 minutes' walk from Lake Senezh.

Guests are offered free Wi-Fi and rooms with air conditioning. Each room at Sun City includes modern décor and a flat-screen TV.

A shower is provided in the bathrooms. Guests can find several cafes and bars in the shopping centre or within a 5-minute walk of the property.

Podsolnechnaya Train Station and Solnechnogorsk Bus Station are 300 metres from Sun City Hotel providing a direct link to Moscow city.
