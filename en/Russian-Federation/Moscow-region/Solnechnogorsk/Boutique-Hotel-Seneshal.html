Description

Featuring private beach are on Lake Senezh, Boutique Hotel Seneshal is set in Solnechnogorsk in the Moscow region Region.

Free WiFi and free private parking are available on site. The rooms feature antique furnishings and are fitted with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

The rooms have a private bathroom equipped with a shower. For your comfort, you will find bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries.

Chalets provide a lake view. Guests can dine in the hotel's restaurant, enjoy a drink at the bar or use on-site barbecue facilities.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. Moscow is 55 km from Boutique Hotel Seneshal.
