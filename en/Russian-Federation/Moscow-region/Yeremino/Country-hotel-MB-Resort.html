Description

Guest rooms in the resort are equipped with a electric tea pot. Complete with a private bathroom, the rooms at Country hotel MB-Resort come with a flat-screen TV, and some rooms here will provide you with a seating area. All rooms feature a closet.

A continental breakfast is served at the property.

The accommodation has a playground. Ski equipment rental and bike rental are available at Country hotel MB-Resort and the area is popular for skiing and cycling.

Pushkino is 27 miles from the resort, while Balashikha is 30 miles away. Zhukovsky International Airport is 40 miles from the property.
