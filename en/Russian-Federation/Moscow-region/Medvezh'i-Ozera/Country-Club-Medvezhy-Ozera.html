Description

Featuring a private beach on Lake Medvezhy Ozera, this hotel offers spa facilities, and a restaurant with terrace.

Each spacious room has a balcony with lake or forest views. Wooden floors, a refrigerator and a TV are features of all rooms at the Country Club Medvezhy Ozera.

Every room has a modern bathroom with hairdryer. Free Wi-Fi is offered in the lobby.

European and Russian meals are served in the Country Clubs restaurant. Guests enjoy a continental breakfast on weekdays, and a breakfast buffet at weekends.

Relaxation is offered by the Finnish, Turkish and Russian saunas. There are also hot tubs, a heated outdoor pool and a range of spa treatments.

Active guests can take part in basketball, volleyball or billiards. Fishing and water sports are possible on the lake.

There is a bus stop just 300 metres away, and Schelkovo Train Station is 3 km away.
