Description

Featuring a Russian steam bath and a private forest with a pond, this hotel is located 10 minutes’ drive from Arzamas town.

It also offers summer sports facilities. Rooms at Yasnaya Polyana Hotel include classic-style interiors and come with a flat-screen TV.

Shared and private bathrooms have a shower. Yasnaya Polyana’s restaurant serves traditional Russian cuisine, as well as European specialities.

Fine wines are offered at the on-site bar with karaoke. Guests can relax in the steam bath or play table tennis.

The on-site activities include volleyball, badminton and paintball. The P158 motorway, offering access to Nizhniy Novgorod city, is 5 minutes’ drive from Yasnaya Polyana Hotel.
