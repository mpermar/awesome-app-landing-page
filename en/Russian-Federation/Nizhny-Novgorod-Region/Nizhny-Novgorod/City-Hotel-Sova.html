Description

City Hotel Sova is a 4-star business hotel located a 10-minute drive to the centre of Nizhniy Novgorod.

The property offers free WiFi, a business centre, a restaurant, a sauna and underground parking.

The Kremlin and the pedestrian street Bolshaya Pokrovskaya are just a few bus stops away.

The modern rooms are air conditioned and come with orthopedic mattresses, hypoallergenic pillows, a TV, a minibar and a safety deposit box.

The private bathrooms feature a bath or shower, hairdryer and free toiletries. A bathrobe and slippers are offered.

The Sova restaurant serves Russian and European cuisine, as well as buffet breakfast, business lunch and dinner.

The hotel offers a sauna and a tea station on each floor. Guests can use Fizkult fitness centre, located a 10-minute walk away.

The property has an ATM on site. A tour desk, shuttle and car rental services are also offered.

The nearest bus stop is a few metres away. Nizhniy Novgorod Train Station is 8 km away, while Nizhniy Novgorod International Airport is 23 km away.
