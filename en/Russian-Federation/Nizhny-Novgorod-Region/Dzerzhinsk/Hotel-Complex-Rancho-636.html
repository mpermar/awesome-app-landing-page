Description

Free Wi-Fi, a swimming pool and spa area with a sauna are features of this hotel in Dzerzhinsk.

It is located in a peaceful forested area set 10 minutes’ drive from Dzerzhinsk Train Station Hotel Complex Rancho 636 offers rooms with brightly decorated interiors.

Every room includes a flat-screen TV, minibar and private bathroom with complimentary toiletries.

A buffet breakfast is provided in the hotel restaurant, which serves European and Japanese cuisine.

The hotel also offers barbecue facilities. Lake Svyatoe is only a 10-minute walk from the hotel, and guests can enjoy cycling and hiking along its shores.

A bus stop is located 100 metres from the hotel and offers connections to Dzerzhinsk city centre, a 15-minute drive away.
