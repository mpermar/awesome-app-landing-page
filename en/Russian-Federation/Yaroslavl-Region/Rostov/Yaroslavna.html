Description

Yaroslavna is located on the banks of Lake Nero, 2 km from the town of Rostov Veliky.

It offers free WiFi, 2 restaurants, a free private beach, free barbecue facilities and a range of wellness treatments and water sports facilities.

All units at Yaroslavna are equipped with a TV, while the cottages and apartments come with a private bathroom and a kitchen as well.

The property also has a large garden. Guests can turn to one of the 2 restaurants for local or European dishes, or have a drink at the bar.

On request, special diet menus and packed lunches can be prepared. Yaroslavna features a wellness area with a sauna and a steam bath, for an additional charge.

Other available activities include fishing, mini golf, billiards and a children’s playground.

You can also rent a bike. Rostov Kremlin is 3 km from the Yaroslavna. Rostov Yaroslavsky Railway Station is a 10-minute drive.

Tunoshna Airport is 60 km from the hotel. A shuttle can be provided for a charge.
