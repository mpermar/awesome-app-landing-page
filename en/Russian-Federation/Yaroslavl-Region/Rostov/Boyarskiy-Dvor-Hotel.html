Description

Boyarskiy Dvor Hotel features a restaurant, bar, a shared lounge and garden in Rostov.

Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a children's playground.

The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and free WiFi throughout the property.

Guests at the hotel can enjoy a continental breakfast. Boyarskiy Dvor Hotel offers a terrace.

You can play table tennis at the accommodation, and the area is popular for skiing.
