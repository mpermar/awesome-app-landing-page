Description

Featuring spacious and bright cottages with free Wi-Fi, Park Hotel Bukhta Koprino is located on the Rybinskoye Reservoir coast, 40 km from Rybinsk city.

Guests are offered a terrace, barbecue facilities and bike rental. All the cottages at Park Hotel Bukhta Koprino include bright and modern interiors, as well as a flat-screen TV.

Bathrooms come with a shower or bath. You can enjoy Russian meals in Navigation Tavern Restaurant, and relax with a drink on the terrace.

The property also has Kalita Restaurant and Shturman Cafe on site. Relaxation at Park Hotel Bukhta Koprino is provided by the on-site saunas, large swimming pool with a shallow end, fitness centre and a massage service.

Younger guests benefit from the children's playground and kid's club Smile. Yaroslavl City with the Train Station is 120 km from Park Hotel Bukhta Koprino , and the M8 (140 km) highway provides a direct link to Moscow.
