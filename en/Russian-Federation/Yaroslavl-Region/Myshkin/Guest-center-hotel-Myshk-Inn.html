Description

Set in Myshkin, on the bank of the Volga River, Guest center-hotel Myshk Inn boasts a sauna.

There is a bowling alley on site and guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Free WiFi and parking space are provided.

All rooms include a TV. Certain rooms have views of the river or city. The rooms are fitted with a private bathroom.

The property offers a restaurant, and BBQ facilities are available. You can play table tennis and billiards at the hotel.
