Description

Located in Pereslavl-Zalessky, next to the picturesque Lake Pleshcheyevo, 3-star Victoria Plaza Hotel is only a 5-minute walk from the Pobedy Park.

The hotel offers free WiFi, conference room and a banquet hall. This hotel features modern interiors in brown and black tones.

Each room comes with a wardrobe and has a private bathroom with free toiletries. The restaurant serves European and Caucasian cuisine.

Guests can also have a drink at the lobby bar, or enjoy panoramic views of the Pleshcheyevo Lake in the rooftop café.

The Oscar Cinema and the Ice Palace are within a 10-minute walk. Khimik Stadium Bus Stop is in front of the Victoria Plaza Hotel.
