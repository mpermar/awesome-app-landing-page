Description

Located in the centre of Yaroslavl next to the Kotorosl River embankment and Millennium Music and Entertainment Centre, this hotel offers an indoor swimming pool, a fitness centre and a sauna.

Free Wi-Fi and a 24-hour front desk are featured at SK Royal Hotel. The elegant, air-conditioned rooms offer warm-coloured interiors and classic-style decor.

Certain rooms overlook the Kotorosl River. All rooms come with a flat-screen TV, a minibar at surcharge and a safe.

The private bathroom offers a bathrobe, a hairdryer and slippers. The hotel's SK Royal Restaurant serves European and Russian cuisine, while Aznavur banquet hall can accommodate up to 60 people.

A variety of drinks are served at the lobby bar. Room service is available. The spacious SPA & Fitness Centre offers a swimming pool with various types of saunas and a beauty parlour with cosmetic treatments.

Guests travelling for business will appreciate hotel's meeting facilities, featuring modern equipment.

The Spaso-Preobrazhensky Monastery is a 15-minute walk from the hotel, while the Volga River Embankment is a 20-minute walk away.

Yaroslavl Central Train Station is 3 km from SK Royal Hotel, and Tunoshna Airport is 26 km from the hotel.
