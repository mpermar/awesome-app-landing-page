Description

Ring Premier Hotel is situated in the cultural and business centre of Yaroslavl city, a 5-minute walk from the Planetarium and Circus.

Guests enjoy local and international cuisine. All comfortably furnished, air-conditioned rooms come with a telephone, minibar and safety deposit box.

The classical-style restaurant features an elegant, red interior. As well as a breakfast buffet, fine Russian, European and Eastern dishes are served here.

Guests can relax in a spa centre, including a swimming pool, Finnish sauna, salt sauna and hammam.

Yaroslavl Train Station is a 10-minute drive away. Yaroslavl Airport can be reached in 40 minutes by car.
