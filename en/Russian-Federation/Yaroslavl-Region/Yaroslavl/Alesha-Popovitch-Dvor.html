Description

Located in the historical centre of Yaroslavl, this hotel features free WiFi and a traditional-style restaurant.

It offers air-conditioned rooms with a minibar. Ploshchad Bogoyavlenia bus stop is 5 metres away.

Each room at Alesha Popovitch Dvor Hotel is elegantly decorated with warm colours and rich fabric bedding.

Bathrooms come with a hairdryer. Russian cuisine is served in the traditional restaurant, which features a design inspired by Russian folk tales.

Fresh draught beer is offered in the on-site beer restaurant. The restaurant hosts jazz concerts every Sunday.

You can relax in the sauna or Finnish bath area. Entrance to the Aleshino Podvorie Museum and Theatre is free for guests of the hotel.

Kotorosl River is 300 metres away, and the Volga River is 800 metres from the hotel.

The Yaroslavl Kremlin and the Monument to Yaroslav the Wise are 5 minutes’ walk from Alesha Popovitch Dvor Hotel, while Yaroslavl Train Station is 10 minutes’ bus ride away.
