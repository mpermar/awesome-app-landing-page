Description

Volzhsky Priboy is located in Borovikovo, 25 km from Kostroma town. It offers a garden, seasonal outdoor pool, Russian and Finnish sauna.

Cycling, horse riding, skiing and other sport activities can be organized on site.

Three types of accommodation are available for guests: main building, summer houses or Russian village cottages.

Guests can visit the on-site restaurant with Russian and European cuisine and snack bar.

There is also cafe-grill "Summer Kitchen" with open veranda which goes directly to the outdoor pool.

Guests can also enjoy winter and summer fishing at the river Poksha. Rental of various sports equipment is available.
