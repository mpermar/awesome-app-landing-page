Description

Featuring free WiFi and a hot tub, Grand Hotel & Spa Aristokrat Kostroma offers accommodation in Molodezhny, 17 km from the centre of Kostroma.

There is a games room on site and guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Guests can relax in spa-zone and one of the pools free of charge.

A gym is also available. Free private parking is available on site. The hotel offers full board meals.

Some rooms include a private bathroom with a spa bath and a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

A flat-screen TV is available in all units. The rooms at Grand Hotel & Spa Aristokrat Kostroma feature a picturesque view of Volga River.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. You can play table tennis and children's playground is available on site.

Krasnoye na Volge Jewellery Centre is 15 km from the property and Plyos is 33 km away.
