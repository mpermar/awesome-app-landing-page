Description

Located in the city centre of Kostroma, a short 10-minute walk from the city’s central square and a 3-minute walk from a public beach, Academy Hostel features a fully equipped shared kitchen, a shared lounge with video games, and free Wi-Fi access throughout the hostel.

The rooms here are equipped with towels and linens, heating, and a shared bathroom.

Other available facilities include a 24-hour front desk, daily maid services, laundry services, and a shuttle service.

A number of restaurants and cafés can be reached within a 5 to 10-minute walk, and Ipatievskiy Monastery is 4 km from the hostel.
