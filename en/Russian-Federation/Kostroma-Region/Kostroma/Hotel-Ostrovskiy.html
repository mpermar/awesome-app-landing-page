Description

Set in Kostroma, 1. 1 km from Epiphany Convent, Hotel Ostrovskiy offers accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking and a bar.

This 4-star hotel offers a concierge service and a tour desk. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and free WiFi throughout the property.

The units come with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a fridge, a kettle, a bath, a hairdryer and a desk.

At the hotel all rooms come with a wardrobe and a private bathroom. Guests at Hotel Ostrovskiy can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

The accommodation also provides a business centre and guests can check the newspapers or use the fax machine and photocopier at Hotel Ostrovskiy.

Popular points of interest near the hotel include Ostrovsky Drama Theatre, Museum of Romanov Family and Kostroma Market.
