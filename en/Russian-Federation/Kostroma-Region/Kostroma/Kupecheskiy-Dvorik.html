Description

Offering a children's playground and terrace, Kupecheskiy Dvorik is situated in Maloye Bezgachëvo.

Guests can enjoy the on-site bar. All rooms have a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Some rooms include a seating area to relax in after a busy day. The bathroom is either private or shared.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. Kostroma is 7 km from Kupecheskiy Dvorik, while Plyos is 46 km from the property.
