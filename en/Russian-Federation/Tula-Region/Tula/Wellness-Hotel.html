Description

Wellness Hotel has modern rooms with free WiFi. It features an indoor pool, a well-equipped fitness centre and a sauna.

It is a short drive away from Tula city centre. Free parking is available on site.

All rooms feature cable TV and a minibar. The on-site restaurant serves Russian and international cuisines for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Guests can try yoga and pilates or enjoy the gym at the Wellness Hotel’s fitness centre.
