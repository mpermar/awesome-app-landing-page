Description

Set in Yegnyshevka, Gostinichny Kompleks Priokskiy Dvorik offers a Russian sauna.

Free WiFi is provided in public areas and free private parking is available on site.

Some units have a seating area or terrace. There is also a kitchen in some of the units.

A private bathroom comes with a shower. Priokskiy Dvorik also includes a seasonal outdoor pool and children's playground.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant. You can play billiards at the property, and the area is popular for skiing.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including cycling and hiking.
