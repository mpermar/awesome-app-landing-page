Description

Each unit comes with with a terrace offering garden views, a cable TV, a dining area, a well-fitted kitchen and a private bathroom with free toiletries. A fridge and electric tea pot are also featured.

A continental or buffet breakfast is available every morning at the property. Guests can enjoy a meal at the in-house restaurant, which specializes in Russian cuisine.

The resort village has a grill.

Ski storage space can be found at the property, along with a playground.

The nearest airport is Kaluga Grabtsevo Airport, 32 miles from Park Hotel Premiera.
