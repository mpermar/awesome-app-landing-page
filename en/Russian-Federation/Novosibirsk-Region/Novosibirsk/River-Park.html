Description

Situated by the Ob River in Novosibirsk, this hotel offers modern rooms with flat-screen TVs and free Wi-Fi.

Facilities at River Park include a fitness studio, Turkish bath and sauna. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning and contemporary bathrooms.

Each comes with satellite TV, work desk and a minibar, and overlooks the Ob River.

The on-site River Park restaurant serves a fusion of Mediterranean, Siberian and international cuisine.

Guests can enjoy a cup of coffee at the 24-hour lobby bar, which features fine city views.

The River Park’s beauty salon offers hair-styling and nail services. Guests can also get a nice tan at the in-house solarium.

The hotel offers a free shuttle service to the Rechnoy Vokzal Underground Station.

The River Park Hotel is located 20 km from the Tolmachevo Airport and offers free private parking.
