Description

Providing a terrace, some units are air-conditioned and have a dining area and a seating area with a satellite flat-screen TV. Some units also have a kitchen equipped with an oven, a microwave, and a fridge.

A continental breakfast is served each morning at the property. At the resort village you'll find a restaurant serving European cuisine. Halal and vegan options can also be requested.

Park Hotel Shishka has a playground.

Skiing and hiking are possible within the area, and the accommodation offers ski-to-door access.

Novosibirsk State Circus is 5 miles from Park Hotel Shishka, while Novosibirsk Zoo is 6 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Novosibirsk Tolmachevo Airport, 15 miles from the resort village.
