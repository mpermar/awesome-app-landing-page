Description

Located in Veliky Novgorod, within a 5-minute walk of the Kremlin, the property offers a restaurant.

Free WiFi is provided at Intourist Apartments. The air-conditioned apartments are designed in classic style and feature a washing machine, ironing facilities and flat-screen TV with cable channels.

The bathroom comes with a hairdryer and free toiletries. Guests can cook in the kitchen equipped with a coffee machine, dishwasher, toaster, stove and fridge.

Veliky Novgorod Train Station is within a 5-minute drive of Intourist Apartments.
