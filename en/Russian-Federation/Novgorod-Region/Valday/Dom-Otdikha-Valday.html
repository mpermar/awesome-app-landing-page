Description

Valday Hotel is located on Uzhin Lake, 140 km from Novgorod. It features a sauna, an indoor pool and a spa centre with a steam bath.

Free wired internet is provided in all rooms and free Wi-Fi is available in public areas.

The air-conditioned rooms feature a flat-screen TV with cable channels. The private bathroom comes with free toiletries.

The rooms also offer a balcony and a fridge. Guests can enjoy buffet meals or order drinks and snacks in the caf.

Barbecue facilities are available on site. Two phytobars serve oxygen cocktails A fitness centre and a tennis court are available.

After a busy day, you can play billiards with friends or enjoy a relaxing massage.

The hotels tour desk staff will organise excursions to the surrounding sights. There is also a gift shop and a boat station on the pebble beach.

Valday Hotel offers a shuttle to Valday Train Station and Valday Bus Station, which are 15 km away.
