Description

Located in a quiet residential area of Khabarovsk, this hotel offers free WiFi and a 24-hour front desk.

An indoor swimming pool and a sauna complex are also featured at Lime Hotel. The modern, air-conditioned rooms include a flat-screen TV and a refrigerator.

Each room comes complete with a private bathroom. Lime Restaurant serves European and Chinese cuisine and offers light buffet breakfast in the morning.

A variety of grilled dishes are available for guests at Royal Cafe. Spa is featured at the hotel, as well as a range of massage treatments.

In the evening guests can relax playing billiards. Bolshaya Medveditsa Shopping Centre with a cinema, a business centre and cafes is a 5-minute drive away.

Khabarovsk Central Train Station is a 10-minute drive from Lime Hotel, and Khabarovsk Airport is a 10-minute drive away.
