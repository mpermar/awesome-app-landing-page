Description

A 5-minute walk from Revolution Square, this hotel is located in Ivanovo city centre, on the bank of Uvod River.

It offers a sauna and a fitness centre. Free WiFi and free parking are provided. Hotel Soyuz provides bright rooms and suites with a fridge, a safety deposit box and a flat-screen TV.

Bathrooms are fitted with a shower cabin, and a hairdryer is provided. All rooms feature original paintings by Vladimir Nemykin, a local artist.

Guests can enjoy Italian specialities and European dishes in the elegant on site restaurant.

Also guests can have a drink in the 24-hour bar, and a buffet breakfast is served every morning.

During the warm weather guests can rest on the outdoor terrace. Ivanovo Central Station is a 15-minute walk away, and the distance to TekstilProfi-Ivanovo Mall is 1.
