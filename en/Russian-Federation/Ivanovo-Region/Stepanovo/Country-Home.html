Description

All guest rooms come with a TV with satellite channels, a electric tea pot, a shower, free toiletries and a closet. The rooms include a private bathroom with a hair dryer, while selected rooms are equipped with a terrace and others also provide guests with river views. Guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

Guests at the hotel can enjoy a continental breakfast. The restaurant at Country Home serves European and Russian cuisine.

A variety of popular activities are available in the area around the property, including skiing and cycling.

Plyos is 29 miles from the accommodation.
