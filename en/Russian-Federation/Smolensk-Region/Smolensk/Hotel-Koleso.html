Description

Located only 10-minutes’ drive away from Smolensk city centre, Hotel Koleso offers rooms which all feature unique design.

Free Wi-Fi is provided throughout. Each room has an individual theme. They each come equipped with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV.

The private bathrooms contain free toiletries. The Koleso Hotel contains a restaurant which serves traditional European cuisine.

There are also barbecue facilities for outdoor dining. Every evening there is a tea party that guests can visit free of charge.

Guests can relax in the sauna, the spa bath and swimming pool, or with a game of billiards.

The Cathedral of the Assumption and the Lopatinsky Garden are both a 10-minute drive away.

Free private parking is available and the bus stop to the city centre is only 100 metres away.
