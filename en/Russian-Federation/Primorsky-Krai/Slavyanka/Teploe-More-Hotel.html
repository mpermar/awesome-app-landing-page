Description

Located on the Baklan Bay, in the village of Slavyanka, this hotel offers a private beach and 2 swimming pools with warm ocean water.

Free Wi-Fi is featured at Teploe More Hotel. The comfortable, air-conditioned rooms have light-coloured interiors and classic-style decor.

Each room includes a flat-screen TV, a refrigerator, a private bathroom and a balcony overlooking the sea.

A restaurant serving European and Russian cuisine is on site. Guests can playing billiards, work out in the gym or relax in the sauna or in hamam, and have a massage.

A children’s playground is also available. Vladivostok is a 1-hour ferry ride from Teploe More Hotel.
