Description

Located 500 metres from the centre of Ishim, just 2 km from Ishim Public Park, Hotel Darsiti features free Wi-Fi and a free car park.

Bogojavlenskiy Cathedral is a 10-minute drive away. The classic-style rooms feature a flat-screen TV and fridge.

The private bathroom comes with a shower, hairdryer and free toiletries. Breakfast can be ordered in the room upon request.

A number of cafes and restaurants is just a short walk away. Darsiti Hotel is 5-minute walk away from a bus stop and a 10-minute drive from Ishim Train Station.
