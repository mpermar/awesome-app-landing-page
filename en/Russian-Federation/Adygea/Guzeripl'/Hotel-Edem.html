Description

All rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Every room includes a electric tea pot, while certain rooms will provide you with a kitchen with a microwave and a fridge. All guest rooms have a closet.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at the property.

Hotel Edem has a playground. A selection of activities are offered in the area, such as skiing, cycling and hiking.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can give tips on the area.

Krasnaya Polyana is 23 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Sochi International Airport, 40 miles from Hotel Edem.
