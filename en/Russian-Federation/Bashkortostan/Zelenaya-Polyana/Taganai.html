Description

Surrounded by mountains and woodland and just a 6-minute walk from the scenic Lake Bannoe, Taganai features a seasonal outdoor heated pool and a Russian banya.

Free WiFi is offered and free secured parking is available. Taganais alpine-style apartments each feature an equipped kitchen area.

There is a garden with a barbecue area. A games room and a playground is available for smaller guests and there is a volleyball court for adults.

Metallurg-Magnitogorsk Ski Centre is 4. 1 km from the property and free shuttle service is provided.
