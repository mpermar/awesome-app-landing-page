Description

Set in the natural Bashkortostan region, Hotel Mayak features rooms and cottages just 2 km from Metallurg-Magnitogorsk ski resort.

It features a restaurant offering panoramic views of the surrounding mountains. Free WiFi is offered throughout the property.

All Mayak’s units are heated and come with a modern private bathroom and a flat-screen satellite TV mounted on the wall.

All rooms are carpeted and feature a seating area. The hotel’s restaurant serves local and international meals, including special diet menus on request.

In the evening guests can choose from a variety of drinks at Mayak’s bar or play billiards in the games room.

After a day exploring the surrounding nature, guests can enjoy the relaxing sauna.

Children can enjoy the hotel's indoor playing room. The scenic shores of pristine Yakty-Kul Lake are just 300 meters from Mayak Hotel.

The natural surroundings offer a range of recreational opportunities, such as skiing, horse riding, hiking and fishing.
