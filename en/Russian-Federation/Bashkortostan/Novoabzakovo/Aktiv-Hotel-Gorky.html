Description

Surrounded by pine forests, these studios and apartments are located in the resort area of Novoabzakovo.

Guests can benefit from a Finnish sauna. Gorky’s accommodation is decorated in eco style with lots of wood and natural materials.

Each unit includes a private entrance, a satellite TV and a kitchen area. Breakfast is served in the main restaurant, which offers a selection of Russian and European dishes for dinner.

There is also an Italian pizzeria. Guests at The Gorky can enjoy a sauna session or arrange an excursion at the 24-hour front desk.

The nearest ski lift is a 5-minute walk away, and ski equipment can be rented on site.

The property overlooks the landscape of the Southern Urals and is a 5-minute drive away from Novoabzakovo Train Station.
