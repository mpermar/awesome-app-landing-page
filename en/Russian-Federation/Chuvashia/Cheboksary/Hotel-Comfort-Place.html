Description

Located in a quiet area of Cheboksary, 7 km from the city centre and a 5-minute drive from Cheboksary Airport, Hotel Comfort-Place features an indoor pool, a sauna, a hot tub, massage services, an on-site canteen, and free Wi-Fi throughout the property.

Rooms here provide guests with a flat-screen TV, a desk, a wardrobe, and a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries.

Other property facilities include a breakfast buffet, a 24-hour front desk, a fitness centre, a billiards table, and a tennis table.

Cheboksary Train Station is 5 km away, and Cheboksary Airport is 2 km or one tram stop from the property.
