Description

Featuring a sauna, a spa and wellness centre, and an on-site restaurant serving Ukrainian, Hungarian, and European cuisine, Park-Hotel Vishnevaya Gora in Saratov offers guests free Wi-Fi.

Rooms here provide a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a refrigerator, a safety deposit box, air conditioning, and a private bathroom with a shower.

Other property facilities include a bar, a 24-hour front desk, a Turkish steam bath, banquet facilities and bowling.

The city centre of Saratov is 6 km away, and Saratov Train Station and Saratov Airport are 4 km from the property.
