Description

Located in Saratov, 20 km from Pokrov Church, Волга Star provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a seasonal outdoor swimming pool and a garden.

With a terrace, the 3-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom.

The hotel features family rooms. At the hotel, every room comes with a wardrobe. A continental breakfast is available daily at Волга Star.

Saratov Academic Theatre of Opera and Ballet is 22 km from the accommodation, while Saratov Art Museum Radishchev is 22 km away.
