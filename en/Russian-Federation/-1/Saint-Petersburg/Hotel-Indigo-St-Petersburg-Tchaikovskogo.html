Description

The air-conditioned rooms include a seating area with satellite TV, iPod docking station and mini-bar. Each private bathroom comes with a hairdryer, bathrobe and complimentary toiletries.

Guests can enjoy European cuisine at the restaurant, or have a drink at one of the hotel’s two bars. Free WiFi is available in all areas. The hotel features a swimming pool and spa center offering Thai spa treatments. Tour desk and ticket services are also provided.

Hotel Indigo St.Petersburg - Tchaikovskogo is located only a 10-minute walk from Chernyshevskaya Metro Station. Pulkovo Airport is 40 minutes’ drive from the hotel.

Tsentralny is a great choice for travelers interested in museums, culture and architecture.
