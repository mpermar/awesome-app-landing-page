Description

Featuring free WiFi, Park-Hotel Altay Green offers accommodation by the lake, 17 km from Belokurikha.

There is a private bathroom with bathrobes in each unit. Towels and bed linen are provided.

Rooms come with Smart TVs. Lunch and dinner can be provided upon request. Barbecue facilities, gazebos and a sauna with a pool are available for guests.

Tour desk services are offered. The property has a private beach area and bike hire is available.

You can play billiards at the property, and the area is popular for skiing. You can engage in various activities, such as horse riding, cycling and fishing.
