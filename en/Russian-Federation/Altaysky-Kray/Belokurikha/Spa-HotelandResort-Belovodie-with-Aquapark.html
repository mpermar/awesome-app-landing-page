Description

Situated in Belokurikha in the Altaysky Region, Belovodie Hotel&Resort boasts a water park and wellness centre.

The resort also offers business facilities and a restaurant. Each air-conditioned room offers cable TV, a minibar and phone.

The bathrooms come with a hairdryer, bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

Guests receive a daily visit to the hotel's solarium, gym and salt therapy room free of charge.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the Belovodie. The property offers guided tours.
