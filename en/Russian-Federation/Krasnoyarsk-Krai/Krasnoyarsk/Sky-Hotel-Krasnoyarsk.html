Description

This 3-star hotel offers modern spa facilities, traditional Russian food, and free Wi-Fi internet.

Krasnoyarsk city centre is 5 km away. The Krasnoyarsk Bridge and Central Stadium are within a 5-minute drive away by car.

Free private parking is available on site. The spacious rooms and suites at the Sky Hotel feature soundproofed windows, a flat-screen TV, and an electric kettle.

Some rooms feature a balcony. Free toiletries are provided in each bathroom. Coffee and tea are complimentary in rooms.

Guests are welcome to relax in the Sky’s sauna. Different massages and beauty treatments are offered here.

Russian specialities and varied breakfasts are served in the Hotel Sky’s café every day.

Guests can also enjoy breakfast in their room. The Sky’s 24-hour reception team can organise shuttle transfers.

Free private parking is available on request. Bobrovy Log is 6 km, while Stolby Nature Sanctuary is 10 km from the property.
