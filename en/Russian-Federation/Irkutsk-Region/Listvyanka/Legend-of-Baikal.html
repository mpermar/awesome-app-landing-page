Description

Featuring free Wi-Fi, this hotel is located on the Baikal lakeside at the source of the Angara River, 62 km from Irkutsk.

Air-conditioned rooms include a minibar. All the rooms at Legend of Baikal Hotel have unique décor with wooden panels and furnishings.

Bathrooms come with bathrobes and a hairdryer. The hotel’s restaurant with a panoramic lake view serves Russian and European cuisine, as well as a daily breakfast.

Meals can be enjoyed on the summer terrace. Guests of Legend of Baikal Hotel can relax in the sauna or play billiards.

Tour desk can organise day trips around the area. The famous Shaman Rock is opposite Legend of Baikal Hotel, and can be clearly seen from those rooms with the lake view or from the terrace.

A shuttle to Irkutsk Airport (64 km) and Irkutsk Train Station (70 km) is available on request.
