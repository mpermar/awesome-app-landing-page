Description

The quietly located Zvezda Hotel offers free Wi-Fi, a spa area, and elegant rooms with flat-screen TV.

It is 1 km from the Angara River and 5 km from Irkutsk Airport. Hotel Zvezdas classically furnished rooms feature air conditioning, tea and coffee facilities and a work desk.

Each has a private bathroom with hairdryer and toiletries. Restaurant Okhotnikov serves Russian cuisine, and Chinese dishes are available at Restaurant Leroglif.

A continental breakfast is provided every morning, and there is also a bar. The Zvezdas spa area features a spa bath and sauna, and massages can be booked.

Irkutsk Botanic Gardens and Irkutsk city centre are just 5 km from the hotel. Parking is free at the Hotel Zvezda Irkutsk.
