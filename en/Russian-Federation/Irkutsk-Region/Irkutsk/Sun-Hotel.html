Description

Situated 300 metres from SibExpo Centre in Irkutsk city centre, Sun Hotel offers heated and carpeted rooms with free Wi-Fi access.

Baikal Lake is less than 1 hour drive away. Each room has a private bathroom with bathtub, bathrobe and hairdryer.

Also available in the room are a coffee/tea maker, telephone and a minibar with refreshments.

Sun Hotel’s spacious lobby area offers a pleasant place to sit and chat or read a newspaper.

At the hotel’s bar guests can relax and have a drink while listening to popular tunes.

In the morning guests can enjoy a buffet breakfast in the hotel’s marble-floored restaurant.

In the evening the Sun Hotel offers a variety of international cuisine. Irkutsk International and Domestic Airport is less than a 5-minute drive away.
