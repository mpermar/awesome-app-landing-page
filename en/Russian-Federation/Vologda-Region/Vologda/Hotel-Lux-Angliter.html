Description

Located in Volodga, Hotel Lux Angliter offers a restaurant and free WiFi access. A 24-hour front desk and luggage storage are available.

Rooms and suites feature air conditioning, a minibar and a flat-screen TV. They also have a bathroom with a hairdryer, free toiletries and a multi-jet shower.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at Hotel Lux Angliter. Guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

This property offers ticket service and a shuttle service at an extra cost. Free public parking is available on site.
