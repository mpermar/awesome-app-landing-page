Description

All rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Complete with a private bathroom, guest rooms at Hotel Pokrovskiy are equipped with air conditioning, and certain rooms are contain a seating area. All guest rooms feature a desk.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental or a buffet breakfast.

At Hotel Pokrovskiy guests are welcome to use a sauna, a Turkish bath and an indoor swimming pool. Hiking is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the hotel.

Speaking English and Russian, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical information on the area at the reception.
