Description

Located in natural surroundings in Somino Village on the bank of a lake, Zagorodny Club Olenya Gora offers self-catering accommodation.

It features bike rental and ski equipment on site. The comfortable cottages and apartments have a TV and DVD player.

Guests can cook in the fully equipped kitchens with a stove, oven and microwave. The bathrooms come with a shower.

Zagorodny Club Olenya Gora offers a sauna and table tennis. Guests can enjoy hiking and skiing in the surroundings.

Various equipment for water activities can be hired on site. Velikiye Luki Airport is 111 km from the property.
