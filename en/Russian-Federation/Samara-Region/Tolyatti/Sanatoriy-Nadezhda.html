Description

At the resort, the rooms have a closet. Each room has a flat-screen TV, and certain rooms at Sanatoriy Nadezhda have a river view.

The daily breakfast offers continental, buffet or à la carte options. At the accommodation you'll find a restaurant serving European cuisine.

A variety of popular activities are available in the area around the property, including fishing and hiking.

Staff at the reception can assist guests at any time of the day.

Tolyatti Bus Station is 4.3 miles from Sanatoriy Nadezhda, while Tatishchev Monument is a 10-minute walk from the property. The nearest airport is Kurumoch International Airport, 34 miles from the property.
