Description

This 3-star hotel is located in a quiet forest area of Samara, within a 5-minute drive of Samara Arena Stadium.

Traditional cuisine is served in the restaurant, and during the summer guests can swim in the outdoor pool.

Free Wi-Fi is available in Dubrava Park-Hotel, and the bright rooms offer a flat-screen TV and a work desk.

Each room has tea and coffee making facilities and a minibar. The restaurant at Park-Hotel Dubrava offers a selection of Russian and European dishes, and drinks can be enjoyed at the bar.

Breakfast is provided and can be served in the rooms. Guests can relax in the sauna or steam room and massages can be booked.

A tennis court and a billiard table are also available. A bus stop is situated a 2-minute walk of the hotel with an access to the city centre, 20 km away.

M5 Motorway is within a 5-minute drive away. A shuttle service is available to Samara Airport, located within a 20-minute drive of the hotel.
