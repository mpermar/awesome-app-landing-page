Description

At the hostel, the rooms come with a desk. With a shared bathroom, rooms at Time Hostel Centr also have a city view.

A variety of popular activities are available in the area around the property, including skiing and cycling.

Staff at the reception can assist guests at any time of the day.

Samara State Academic Opera and Ballet Theatre is a 17-minute walk from the accommodation, while Metallurg Stadium is 8 miles away. Kurumoch International Airport is 22 miles from the property.
