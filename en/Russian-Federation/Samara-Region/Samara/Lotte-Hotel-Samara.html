Description

Lotte Hotel Samara is a 5-star hotel located in Samara city-centre. Samara Arena is only 13 km from the property.

The hotel features a fitness centre, massage services, a bar and on-site dining. Boasting elegant interior, guest rooms at the hotel offer luxurious accommodation.

The private bathroom is equipped with a bath. All rooms are fitted with a seating area and include a desk.

A buffet breakfast is served each morning at the Terrazza restaurant. Guests can access free WiFi or use the business centre.

The hotel also offers 12 conference rooms which fit up to 1400 people and can be transformed for different events from banquets to business meetings.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can provide assistance with advice about the area.

Samara Philharmonic Hall is 600 metres from Lotte Hotel Samara. Kurumoch International Airport is 35 km from the property.
