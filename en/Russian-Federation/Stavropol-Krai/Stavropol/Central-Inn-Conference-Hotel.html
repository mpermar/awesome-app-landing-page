Description

Central Inn Conference Hotel offers air-conditioned accommodation in Stavropol. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a 24-hour front desk and room service, along with free WiFi.

Guests can use the spa and wellness centre with a fitness centre, hammam, and sauna, as well as a bar.

At the hotel, each room comes with a wardrobe. At Central Inn Conference Hotel all rooms have a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

The accommodation offers a continental or buffet breakfast. Central Inn Conference Hotel offers a children's playground.

The nearest airport is Stavropol, 20 km from the hotel, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
