Description

Located in the heart of Stavropol, this hotel is near the Lenin Square and the Guardian Angel monument and just a 5-minute walk from TSUM department store.

It has 2 restaurants and 6 conference halls. Free Wi-Fi is features throughout the property.

Rooms at the hotel have a TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom. Some rooms have a separate seating area.

Eurootel Stavropol serves a large buffet breakfast consisting of hot and cold dishes.

Guests can also enjoy European cuisine at T-Bone Restaurant and Veranda Restaurant, which are located on the ground floor.

Eurootel Stavropol is also close to the Stavropol History Museum, the State Academic Theatre and the Dynamo Stadium.
