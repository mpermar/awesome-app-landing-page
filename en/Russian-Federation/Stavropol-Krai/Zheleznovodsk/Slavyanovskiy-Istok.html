Description

Located in the centre of Zheleznovodsk, next to the public park area, Slavyanovskiy Istok features a sun terrace, garden and tennis court.

It also offers a spa centre with a sauna and massage. The air-conditioned and soundproofed rooms feature extra-long beds, a flat-screen cable TV and fridge.

The private bathrooms offer a shower, hairdryer and free toiletries. The restaurant at Slavyanovskiy Istok serves European and Caucasian cuisine.

The property offers bike rental, free Wi-Fi and parking. It features a games room with table tennis and billiards and childrens entertainment.

Diagnostic procedures, tests, mineral water treatment and mud therapy are offered at the nearby resort.
