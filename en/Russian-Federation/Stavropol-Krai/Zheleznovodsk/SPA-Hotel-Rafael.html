Description

SPA Hotel Rafael offers accommodation with treatment in Zheleznovodsk. The hotel offers a medical care facility and has a children's playground and spa centre, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

A private parking is available on site at surcharge. All rooms come with a flat-screen TV.

You will find a kettle in the room. For your comfort, you will find bath robes and slippers.

SPA Hotel Rafael features free WiFi throughout the property. There is valet parking, swimming pool, hammam, sauna and fitness centre at the property.

You can play table tennis at the hotel. Kislovodsk is 34 km from SPA Hotel Rafael, while Pyatigorsk is 10 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Mineralnye Vody Airport, 15 km from the property. Mount Elbrus is 180 km away.
