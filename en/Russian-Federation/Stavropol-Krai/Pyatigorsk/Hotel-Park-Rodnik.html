Description

Hotel Park Rodnik provides air-conditioned rooms in Pyatigorsk. The property has a bar, as well as a restaurant serving Grill/bbq cuisine.

The accommodation offers a fitness centre, free WiFi throughout the property and shuttle service.

Guest rooms at the hotel are fitted with a seating area. The rooms have a private bathroom with free toiletries.

All guest rooms at Hotel Park Rodnik are equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

A buffet breakfast is served daily at the property. The accommodation offers a children's playground.

Hiking is among the activities that guests can enjoy near Hotel Park Rodnik. Staff speak English, Russian and Serbian at the 24-hour front desk.

Kislovodsk is 35 km from the hotel, while Essentuki is 20 km from the property. The nearest airport is Mineralnye Vody Airport, 20 km from Hotel Park Rodnik.
