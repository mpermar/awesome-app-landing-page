Description

Set in the scenic Kislovodsk Park, this 4-star hotel offers free Wi-Fi and a spa centre with indoor pool and gym.

It is a 10-minute walk from Kislovodsk Train Station. Plaza Resort specialises in gastrointestinal, gynecological diseases and metabolic disorders.

The Panorama dining hall offers rich buffets for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Guests can enjoy snacks and cocktails in the Plaza's lobby bar and Bar "Club 24".

Air-conditioned rooms with traditional wooden furniture are offered at Plaza Spa Resort.

All rooms feature satellite TV and hot drinks facilities, and the bathrooms include bathrobes and toiletries.

Beauty and balneological treatments and a range of massages can be booked at the Plaza’s Spa, which also features several saunas and steam baths.

Various medical examinations and health programmes are available on site. Kislovodsk city centre is a 15-minute walk from the Plaza Resort.
