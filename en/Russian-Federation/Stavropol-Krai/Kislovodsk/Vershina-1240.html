Description

Vershina 1240 offers accommodation in Kislovodsk near Maloye Sedlo mountain 1240 metres above the sea level.

The hotel has a terrace and sauna, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Every room is equipped with a flat-screen TV. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. You can play table tennis and billiards at this hotel, and the area is popular for hiking.

Pyatigorsk is 30 km from Vershina 1240, while Essentuki is 17 km away. Mineralnye Vody Airport is 43 km from the property.
