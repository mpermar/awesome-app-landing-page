Description

Featuring a sauna and an indoor pool, this hotel is situated in the centre of Yessentuki’s mineral spa area.

The Yessentuky 4 Mineral Spring is just a 2-minute walk away. Every room at Kras Hotel has unique features and comes with a TV, air conditioning and a refrigerator.

A hairdryer and slippers are provided in the en suite bathroom. A selection of European dishes is served in the Tango Café, with special diet menus available on request.

Guests can relax in KrasHotel’s Adam and Eva Spa, which has a solarium and hammam.

A small selection of books is offered in the hotel’s library. Yessentuki Train Station is a 5-minute drive from the hotel and Mineralnye Vody Airport is a 40-minute drive away.
