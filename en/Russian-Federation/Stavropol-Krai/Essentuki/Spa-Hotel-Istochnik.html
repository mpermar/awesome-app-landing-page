Description

Located in Essentuki, Spa Hotel Istochnik consists of 2 buildings and offers a variety of treatments, including movement therapy, physiotherapeutic, mineral water and mid treatments with balneo-mud.

It also includes carboxytherapy, ozone therapy, extracorporeal shockwave therapy and cryotherapy.

The resort provides diagnostic examinations and group exercises. Spa centre and mineral water spring is featured at the property.

Air-conditioned rooms have a flat-screen TV. All rooms have a private bathroom and a balconet.

For your comfort, you will find bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries. Dietary menus are available at the property's restaurant.

There is a lobby bar with the terrace at Spa Hotel Istochnik. Guests can benefit from the indoor swimming pool, sauna, hammam, and gym.

Massage is also offered. Children can enjoy onsite playground, game room and entertainment programmes.

There are table games at the health resort. Kurortny Park is within a 5-minute walk.

Mineralnye Vody Airport is 36 km from Spa Hotel Istochnik, while Train Station Essentuki is 300 metres from the property.
