Description

Offering a spa centre and a sauna, Kavkaz Hotel is located in Yessentuki, a 2-minute walk from Lechebny Park.

Free WiFi access is available. Each room here will provide you with a TV and air conditioning.

Complete with a refrigerator, the dining area also has an electric kettle. Private bathroom comes with free toiletries.

The on-site restaurant serves European cuisine, and a variety of drinks is offered at the bar.

At Kavkaz Hotel you will find a 24-hour front desk, a children's playground, an ironing service and laundry facilities.

The property offers free parking. Yessentuki Central Train Station is 1 km away. Mineralnye Vody International Airport is 44 km away.
