Description

Pansionat PLAZA Essentuki is located in the centre of Essentuki in Lechebny Park on Teatralnaya Square and Fontanov Square, within a 3-minute walk of the Ministry of North Caucasus.

Essentuki-4 and Essentuki-17 Mineral Water Wells are within a 5-minute walk. Each room will provide you with a TV, air conditioning, desk and a minibar.

There is also a refrigerator and a safety deposit box. The private bathroom comes with a hairdryer, slippers, free toiletries and bathrobes.

Free WiFi is provided in all areas. Guests can order a meal in Family Cafe which also offers entertainment programmes for children.

Khmel Beer Restaurant, Opera Karaoke Bar, Shatyor Summer Cafe are situated on site.

Oranzhereya Restaurant is decorated in Provence style and serves dishes of Mediterranean cuisine.

You can also enjoy a cup of coffee in Abazhur Cafe. The property features a spa and wellness centre offering balneological and mud treatments.

Various kinds of mineral baths and massages are provided. Sakura Beauty Salon provides cosmetic treatments.

Equipped meeting and banquet facilities seating 100 guests are provided. Essentuki Train Station is within a 7-minute walk of Pansionat PLAZA Essentuki and shuttle services are provided at surcharge.
