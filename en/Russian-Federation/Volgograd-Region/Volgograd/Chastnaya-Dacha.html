Description

With river views, Chastnaya Dacha is situated in Volgograd and has a restaurant, a 24-hour front desk, bar, garden, outdoor pool and children's playground.

Complimentary WiFi is offered throughout the property. A microwave and fridge are also available, as well as a kettle.

The holiday park offers a continental or à la carte breakfast. Chastnaya Dacha has both a barbecue and a terrace, along with a private beach area.

River Port is 45 km from the accommodation, while Volgograd Arena is 48 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Volgograd International Airport, 61 km from Chastnaya Dacha.
