Description

A 25-metre outdoor pool, Russian and Finnish saunas and outdoor tennis courts are offered at this hotel complex, 7 km from the centre of Rostov-on-Don.

Alexander offers free Wi-Fi and Baroque-style rooms. The air-conditioned rooms at the Alexander Rostov-on-Don are decorated in warm colours and feature classic interiors with wooden furniture.

A flat-screen TV, refrigerator and a private bathroom are included in all rooms. Breakfast and a selection of European and Caucasian dishes are served in the cosy restaurant with its traditional wooden furniture, billiard table and karaoke facilities.

An entertainment area with restaurants, bars and clubs is a 10-minute walk away. Private parking is free at the Alexander.
