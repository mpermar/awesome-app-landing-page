Description

Located in a quiet area in Myatlevo, Safari Park offers cottages with TV sets. Free private parking is available, as is free Wi-Fi in public areas.

The hotel has its own zoo. Safari Park’s log cabins are spacious and heated. Each has a private bathroom.

Most have kitchen facilities and a living room with a comfortable seating area. Some have a fireplace.

The Safari boasts 2 swimming pools, a mini golf course and a sauna. It arranges horse riding activities, fishing and hunting.

Guests can also relax during a game of billiards or enjoy a karaoke evening. The hotel’s restaurant serves local as well as European dishes.

In the mornings a breakfast buffet is available. There also is an on-site bar. The city of Medyn is within 20 km from the Safari Park.
