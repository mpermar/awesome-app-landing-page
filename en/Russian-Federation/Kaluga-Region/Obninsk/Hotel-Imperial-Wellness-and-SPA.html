Description

Hotel Imperial Wellness & SPA is located in Obninsk, right on Kievskoe Highway. It features a gym.

Free Wi-Fi and private parking are provided. Spa centre facilities are available at surcharge.

All classic-style rooms come with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, minibar and safety deposit box.

Slippers, bathrobes, free toiletries and a hairdryer are available in a bathroom.

The on-site restaurant serves various Russian and European dishes, and guests can have a drink in the bar.

Hotel Imperial Wellness & SPA offers a spa-centre, a gym and a beauty salon. It is possible to relax in a sauna, to sing karaoke or to play billiards.

Obninskoe Train Station is 1. 3 km from the property, and Kaluga is 76 km away. The distance to Moscow is 106 km.
