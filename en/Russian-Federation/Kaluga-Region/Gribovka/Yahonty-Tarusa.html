Description

Located in the picturesque area of Gribovka, Yakhonty Tarusa Holiday Park features an indoor pool, spa and wellness centre, sauna with a hot tub and fitness centre.

Free WiFi and free parking are offered on site. The rooms offer air conditioning, a seating area, hairdryer and flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Guests can dine in the hotel’s restaurant, or use hotel’s barbecue facilities. There is also a bar on site.

Room service, packed lunches and breakfasts in the room are provided upon request.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including horse riding, billiards, table tennis and bowling.
