Description

Situated on the edge of Tarusa, this hotel is just 70 metres from the sandy banks of the River Oka.

Serebryaniy Vek offers a restaurant with Russian cuisine, a spa area, massages and a gym.

Free WiFi is available. A flat-screen TV, refrigerator and kettle are included in every room.

Decorated in warm colours, Serebryaniy Vek offers spacious accommodation with classic-style furniture.

The hotel offers various sporting activities including fishing in the River Oka. Children will enjoy the activity playground and Serebryaniy Vek’s mini-zoo.

Tarusa’s historical centre is located 3 km away, where guests can explore the Tsvetaeva Museum and Tarusa Art Gallery.

The Tarusa bus stop is 3 km from the hotel, and offers connections to Serpukhov Train Station (30 km).
