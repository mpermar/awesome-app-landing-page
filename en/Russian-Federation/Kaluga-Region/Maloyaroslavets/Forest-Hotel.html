Description

The Forest Hotel is located within the quiet Russian woodland in the historical area of Maloyaroslavets.

There are meeting/banquet facilities, a 24-hour reception and Wi-Fi is free. The hotel offers rooms equipped with air-conditioning, minibar and a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

There is an a la carte restaurant, bar and various modern seating areas ideal for both relaxation and social meetings.

The contemporary hotel Forest is just a 10-minute walk from the local shops and cafes and only a 5-minute drive from the Luzha River.
