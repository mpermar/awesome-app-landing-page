Description

The vacation home has 2 bedrooms, a kitchen with an oven and a microwave, and a bathroom with a bath. A flat-screen TV is offered.

Guests at the vacation home can enjoy a continental breakfast.

For any tips on how to get around or what to do in the area, guests can ask at the reception.

Guests can also relax in the garden or on the shared lounge area.

Obninsk is 12 miles from House on the Tree. The nearest airport is Vnukovo International Airport, 42 miles from the accommodation.
