Description

This hotel is located in a green area of Kaluga, a 8-minute drive from the city centre.

An outdoor pool, free Wi-Fi and a sauna are featured at Yolki Hotel. The bright, air-conditioned rooms offer warm-coloured interiors and classic-style décor.

Each room offers a flat-screen TV, a refrigerator and a private bathroom with bathrobes, slippers and a hairdryer.

The on-site restaurant serves European cuisine. Guests of Yolki Hotel can work out in the gym and play billiards.

A children’s playground and a seasonal water park are available for young guests for free.

Tsiolkovsky State Museum of the History of Cosmonautics is 9 km away. Kaluga 1 and Kaluga 2 train stations are 8 km from Yolki Hotel.
