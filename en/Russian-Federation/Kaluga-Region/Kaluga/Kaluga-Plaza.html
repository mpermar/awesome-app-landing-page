Description

Featuring a large spa centre, elegant rooms with flat-screen TVs and a modern restaurant, this 4-star hotel is just a 5-minute drive from Kaluga city centre and a 20-minute walk from Victory Square.

Free Wi-Fi and free parking are available. Spacious rooms with a classical design are offered at Kaluga Plaza.

Facilities include a minibar, hairdryer and luxury toiletries. Bathrobes and slippers are provided.

A varied buffet breakfast and a range of international evening meals can be enjoyed in the Kaluga Plaza Hotel’s stylish restaurant.

Guests can relax in the spa centre, which features an indoor pool, sauna and steam bath.

Kaluga Plaza is 6 km from Kaluga Train Station, and a shuttle service can be arranged at the hotel.
