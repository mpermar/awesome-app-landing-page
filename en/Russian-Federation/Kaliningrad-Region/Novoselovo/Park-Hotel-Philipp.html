Description

An indoor swimming pool and a spa centre are featured at Park Hotel Philipp, located in Novoselovo village.

Guests are offered a children’s playground and rooms with free Wi-Fi. All rooms feature a minibar and a flat-screen TV.

Philipp Restaurant serves dishes of Haute cuisine and a large variety of cocktails, cognacs and wines.

Guests of Philipp Park Hotel can relax in a Russian banya on the bank of a picturesque lake.

A massage service is available on site. The hotel organizes business events, various celebrations and hosts marriage ceremonies.

Kaliningrad city is 40 km from Park Hotel Philipp, and a shuttle service is provided on request.
