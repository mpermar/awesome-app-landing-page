Located in Kaliningrad, Crystal House Suite Hotel & Spa features a fitness centre, a swimming pool and a terrace.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet or a-la carte breakfast. Guests can also grab a bite to eat in the in-house restaurant which serves modern Baltic cuisine.
