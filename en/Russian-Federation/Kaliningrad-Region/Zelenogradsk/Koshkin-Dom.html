Description

Featuring a small pool and free Wi-Fi, this guest house is situated 50 metres from the sandy Baltic Sea Coast.

There is a 24-hour reception, and a flat-screen TV is offered in each room. The bright rooms at Koshkin Dom offer a DVD player and a refrigerator.

Toiletries and a hairdryer are provided in the private bathroom. European cuisine is served in the Koshkin Dom restaurant, and barbecue facilities are available on the terrace.

A continental breakfast is provided. Guests can relax in the sauna or enjoy a game of billiards, and bicycle rental is available.

Kurshskaya Kosa UNESCO Heritage Site is 5km away, and Khrabrovo Airport is 11 km away.
