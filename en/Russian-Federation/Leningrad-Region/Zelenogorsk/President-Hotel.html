Description

Offering an outdoor and an indoor pool, President Hotel is located on the Gulf of Finland in Zelenogorsk.

Free Wi-Fi access is available. The property features a spa and wellness centre with a sauna, Turkish steam bath and salt cave.

The bright air-conditioned rooms feature a flat-screen TV and a minibar. The bathroom comes with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Guests can order a meal in the on-site restaurant, serving dishes of Russian and European cuisine, and enjoy their favourite drink at the bar.

A dietary menu is available upon request, and barbecue facilities are provided. In the summer, guests can dine in one of the outdoor gazebos.

You can play tennis, golf, football and basketball. Younger guests will enjoy the childrenâ€™s playground.

In the winter, there is also a skating rink. Zelenogorsk Train Station is within a 5-minute drive of President Hotel, and Pulkovo International Airport is 70 km away.
