Description

Located on the Gulf of Finland beach in Zelenogorsk town, this resort features free Wi-Fi, an outdoor swimming pool and a yacht club.

There is also a spa centre. All the air-conditioned rooms at Terijoki Resort Hotel are decorated in a modern style and include a flat-screen TV, as well as a minibar.

The Terijokis restaurants serve European cuisine, which can be enjoyed on the terrace.

Cocktails and drinks are offered at the on-site bar. Guests of Terijoki Resort can relax in the sauna or on the beach, and take a dip into the swimming pool with sea water.

A massage service, solarium and a steam bath are available in the spa centre. Primorskoye Motorway is 1 minutes drive from Terijoki Resort Hotel, providing a link to Saint Petersburg, and Zelenogorsk Train Station is 6 minutes drive away.
