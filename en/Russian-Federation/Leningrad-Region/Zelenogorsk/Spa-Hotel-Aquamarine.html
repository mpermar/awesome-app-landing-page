Description

Offering free Wi-Fi and a spa centre, this hotel is 500 metres from the Gulf of Finland in natural surroundings of Zelenogorsk.

It provides a bar with a panoramic view and rooms with a fridge. Guests of Aquamarine Spa-Hotel can work out in a fitness centre, or relax in the spa centre, which offers 3 saunas and beauty treatments.

A billiards room, 2 tennis courts and a volleyball court are also available. Russian and European cuisine is served in the Zhemtchuzhina Restaurant, while cocktails and drinks are offered at Mezonin Bar with panoramic views of the Gulf of Finland.

All the elegant rooms at Aquamarine Hotel feature individual dcor with carpeted floors.

Bathrooms come with slippers. Aquamarine Spa Hotel is 8 minutes drive from Zelenogorsk Train Station, which offers access to Saint Petersburg.
