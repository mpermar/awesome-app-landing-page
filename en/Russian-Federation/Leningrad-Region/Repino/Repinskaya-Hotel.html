Description

This hotel is set in the village of Repino, on the bank of the Gulf of Finland. Free WiFi and a children’s playground are featured at Repinskaya Hotel.

The bright rooms offer warm-coloured interiors and classic-style décor. Each room comes with a TV, a refrigerator, a balcony and a private bathroom.

The Morenado Restaurant serves Russian, European and Caucasian cuisine and offers panoramic views of the Gulf of Finland.

Children can enjoy the property's kids entertainment programme. Guests can play table tennis and billiards on site.

Sports equipment hire is available at this property. The Penaty Museum of Ilya Repin is a 10-minute walk from the hotel.

Repino Train Station is a 15-minute walk from Repinskaya Hotel. Pulkovo International Airport is 65 km from the hotel.
