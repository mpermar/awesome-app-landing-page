Description

Set in the picturesque area of Korobitsyno, Snezhny Resort offers a sauna, free private parking and 2 restaurants.

The rooms come with a kitchenette, free WiFi and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

The bathrooms offer a hairdryer and a washing machine. Snezhny Resort provides guests with barbecue facilities.

The accommodation also features a bicycle rental, ski school and a lake with a beach.
