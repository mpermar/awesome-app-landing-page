Description

At the guesthouse, rooms have a terrace with a lake view. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a bath, while selected rooms will provide you with a balcony. All guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

A continental or Full English/Irish breakfast is served at the property.

Eco-Usadba Belovo has a playground. Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Shapki, like skiing.

The nearest airport is Pulkovo Airport, 39 miles from the property.
