Description

Offering a restaurant and a spa complex, this resort is surrounded by pine woods in Roshchino village, 45 km from Saint Petersburg.

It features cottages with free Wi-Fi and Finnish sauna. Each of 40 cottages at Tsarstvo Korolevstvo Resort provides a TV, kitchenware, barbecue facilities, floor heating and terrace.

All units come with modern bathrooms. Offering a range of summer outdoor activities including football and volleyball, Tsarstvo Korolevstvo provides various winter activities too.

Guests can enjoy ice-skating, skiing, sleighing and snow-tubing. Tsarstvo Korolevstvo Resort is within an hour-long bus ride from Prospekt Prosveshcheniya Metro Station in Saint Petersburg.

Finland Train Station serves trains to Roshchino. The nearest airport is Pulkovo, 95 km from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
