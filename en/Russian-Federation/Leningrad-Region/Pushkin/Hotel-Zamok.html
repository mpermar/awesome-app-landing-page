Description

A 15-minute drive to Pulkovo Airport, Hotel Zamok is located in a quiet area of Pushkin, next to Aleksandrovskiy Sad.

The hotel offers a sauna and steam bath. Free private parking is available. The comfortable rooms are decorated in warm tones and come with a flat-screen TV and fridge.

The private bathrooms offer a shower, hairdryer and free toiletries. Zamok Hotel has a bar and garden with BBQ facilities.

It also offers billiards and free Wi-Fi in public areas. Aleksandrovskaya Train Station is 1 km away.
