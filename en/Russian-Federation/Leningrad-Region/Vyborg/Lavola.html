Description

Offering a restaurant, Lavola is located in Rappatily, 15 km from Vyborg city. Free Wi-Fi access is available in this holiday park.

The accommodation will provide you with a flat-screen TV and wardrobe. Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with towels.

At Lavola you will find a sauna and a garden. Other facilities offered at the property include water sport facilities (on site), meeting facilities and a children's playground.

There is also a pier for yachts and boats. The distance to Saint-Petersburg is 153 km from Lavola Holiday Park.
