Description

Featuring a professional 18-hole golf course, Gorki Golf Resort is a hotel set near Gorki.

Free WiFi, fitness centre and private parking are available at the hotel. Rooms in Gorki Golf Resort are fitted with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV, and some units at the accommodation have a terrace.

All rooms have a desk. Guests at Gorki Golf Resort can enjoy a continental breakfast.

The area is popular for skiing, and ski equipment hire is available at the hotel.

Guests can also have an access to the on-site Spa centre with a swimming pool, a hot tub, Finnish sauna and Russian steam bath for the extra cost.

Peterhof is 37 km from Gorki Golf Resort. The nearest airport is Pulkovo Airport, 50 km from the accommodation.
