Description

Located in Borisovo, Country Club Davinci Park features a rope course and a wake park.

Featuring a 24-hour front desk, this property also provides guests with a restaurant and bar.

At the resort, every room comes with a wardrobe. Each room is fitted with a private bathroom, while selected rooms come with a kitchen.

Guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge. Resort Davinci Park offers a sun terrace.

Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Borisovo, like skiing, cycling and hiking.
