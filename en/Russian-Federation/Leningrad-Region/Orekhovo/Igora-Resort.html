Description

Igora Resort is located in Orekhovo in the Leningrad Region, 26 km from Ladozhskoe Lake.

Free WiFi and private parking are available throughout the property. The resort features 3 swimming pools: indoor adult and children's pools at the spa centre, and a seasonal outdoor pool near the lake.

Rooms decorated in warm colours feature a flat-screen TV and air-conditioning. Certain units have a living room or a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms. You will find a 24-hour front desk.

Spa and welness centre where guests can have a massage is available. There is a restaurant and bar and guests can have fun at the bowling alley and games room.

This resort has a ski school and ski equipment hire is available. You can play tennis, table tennis and billiards at this resort, and the area is popular for skiing.

The resort also offers bike hire. The nearest airport is Pulkovo International Airport, 101 km from Igora Resort.
