Description

Featuring an outdoor pool, a beachfront, and an on-site restaurant, Country Club Pikhtovoye located 15 km from Vyborg offers guests free WiFi and a bicycle rental.

Rooms at Country Club Pikhtovoye offer air conditioning, a TV with satellite channels, and a private bathroom.

Other hotel facilities include BBQ facilities, billiards, a children’s playground, a bar, a sun terrace, table tennis, and a Turkish steam bath.

Vyborg city centre is 20 km from the property, and Vyborg Train Station is 24 km away.
