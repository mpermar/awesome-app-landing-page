Description

Grand Peterhof Spa Hotel is a 5-minute drive from Peterhof Palace. This luxurious hotel offers free Wi-Fi and a free spa with indoor and outdoor pool, 6 saunas and halotherapy treatment.

All rooms include satellite TV and a dressing room. Each spacious room at the Grand Peterhof features air conditioning, elegant décor, and a seating area.

A choice of saunas is available at Grand Peterhof Spa Hotel, including a Russian and Greek Sauna, as well as an infrared and salt sauna.

Guests can relax with a massage and keep fit in the gym. Breakfast buffet is served daily for the hotel guests.

Traktir Mikhailovsky serves European and German cuisine. The room service is available at any time.

There is also a conference hall on site. St. Petersburg is 15 km from Grand Peterhof Spa Hotel.
