Description

Located in the picturesque area of Solnechnoye, 100 metres from the Gulf of Finland, Staraya Melnitsa Holiday Park features spa and wellness centre, indoor and outdoor pools, a sauna and hammam.

The rooms offer a sofa, TV, DVD player, hairdryer, fridge and electric kettle. Some rooms come with a kitchen.

Guests are welcome to 3 on-site restaurants, serving European, Russian, Ukrainian and Georgian cuisine.

There is also a bar on site. Room service is available upon request. Free WiFi and free parking are available on site.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including billiards, table tennis and cycling.

Solnechnoye Train Station is 1. 5 km away, and Saint Petersburg is 25 km from Staraya Melnitsa Holiday Park.
