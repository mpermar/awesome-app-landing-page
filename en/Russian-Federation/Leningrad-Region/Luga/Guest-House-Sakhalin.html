Description

Offering a sauna and ski storage space, Guest House Sakhalin is located on Omchino lake side in Luga, the Leningrad Region.

Free private parking is available on site. Certain units feature views of the lake or river.

Guest House Sakhalin features free WiFi. A flat-screen TV is provided. You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including skiing and fishing.
