Description

Featuring free WiFi, a barbecue and a children's playground, Baza Otdykha Avrora offers accommodation in Sredniye Krupeli.

The resort has a sun terrace and hot tub, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Free private parking is available on site. The resort offers individual cottages and classic room.

Some units include a seating area for your convenience. You will find an electric kettle.

Every unit is equipped with a private bathroom. A flat-screen TV with satellite channels is available.

You will find room service at the property. This resort has a private beach area, water sports facilities and ski storage space and ski equipment hire is available.

You can play table tennis and billiards at the resort. The resort also offers bike hire.
