Description

Guest rooms in the hostel are equipped with a electric tea pot. All rooms come with a shared bathroom, while some rooms include a kitchen with an oven. All rooms will provide guests with a microwave.

The daily breakfast offers continental and Full English/Irish options.

Clever House Hostel has a playground. Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.

Sevastyanov's House is a 17-minute walk from Clever House Hostel, while Uralochka Sports Centre is one mile away. The nearest airport is Koltsovo International Airport, 9 miles from the hostel.
