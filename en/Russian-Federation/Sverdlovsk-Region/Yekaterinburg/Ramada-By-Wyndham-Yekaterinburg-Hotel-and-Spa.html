Description

A 10-minute drive from Koltsovo Airport, this hotel offers free WiFi and a free spa centre which offers 2 swimming pools, sauna and steam room.

This lakefront hotel is surrounded by forests. The Ramada Yekaterinburg Hotel & Spa offers rooms with modern décor and wonderful views of the surrounding countryside.

Each air-conditioned room has a seating area, a TV and tea/coffee facilities. The Ramada Ekaterinburg spa with free access for the hotel guests features an indoor swimming pool and a year-round outdoor heated pool, hot tub and gym.

Guests can also make an appointment in the hairdressing salon. Free SPA centre is offered to all guests at the hotel.

Le Vicomte restaurant serves European cuisine at lakeside tables, while the Lobby and Boogie's Bar serve afternoon tea and evening cocktails.

Guests can enjoy walks around the lake, in the forest or the hotel gardens. Ekaterinburg Exhibition Centre is a 5-minute drive away, and the hotel is a 20-minute drive from the city centre.
