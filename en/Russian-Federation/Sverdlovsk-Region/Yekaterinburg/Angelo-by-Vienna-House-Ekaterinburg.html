Description

Angelo by Vienna House Ekaterinburg is a modern hotel, located only 100 metres away from Koltsovo Airport.

The hotel is directly linked to the airport by covered heated access. Free Wi-Fi is provided throughout the property.

The hotel's 211 guest rooms are spacious and beautifully furnished. Each room combines bright colourings and furnishings with state-of-the-art technology, such as a flat screen TV and DVD player.

All rooms feature soundproof windows and blackout curtains. Continental breakfast is served from 03:00 every day, while buffet breakfast is available from 06:00.

The hotel restaurant Sunlight serves a wide range of cuisine incorporating both regional, national and international specialities.

The hotel's lobby Jazz Bar & Lounge is open 24-hours and serves a range of drinks, snacks and healthy food dishes.

The property also features a spacious fitness area, a conference centre. Guests can leave their cars for underground and outdoor parking.

There are direct express train links from the airport to Sverdlovsk (Ekaterinburg) Central Station and Pervomayskaya Station.

Angelo by Vienna House Ekaterinburg is located within 20-minute drive to the city centre and only 5-minute drive away from the international exhibition centre Ekaterinburg Expo.
