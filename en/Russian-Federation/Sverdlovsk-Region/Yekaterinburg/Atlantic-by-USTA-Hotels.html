Description

Located 1 km from Botanicheskaya Metro Station in Ekaterinburg, Atlantic by Usta Hotels offers elegant rooms.

It occupies the same building as Europe's largest covered waterpark and the Globus Shopping Centre.

The rooms of Atlantic by Usta Hotels are spacious and decorated with wooden furnishings.

All rooms have tea and coffee making facilities, a minibar, and a private bathroom with hairdryer.

Some rooms have a seating area. Atlantic by Usta Hotels is beside the large waterpark, which features several large waterslides.

It is a 5-minute walk from the Ekaterininskiy shopping centre, which offers a spa, bowling alley and gym.

The bar at the Atlantic by Usta Hotels serves a wide variety of drinks and refreshments.

The hotel is situated 8 km from Ekaterinburg’s city centre, where guests can visit the Romanov Death Site and the Church of Blood.
