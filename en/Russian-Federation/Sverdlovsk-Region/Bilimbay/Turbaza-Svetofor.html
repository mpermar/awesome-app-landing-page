Description

All guest rooms feature a bath, free toiletries, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and DVD-Player. Each room comes with a private bathroom and a shower, and certain rooms have a balcony. Guest rooms have a desk.

The daily breakfast offers continental and à la carte options.

The inn has a playground. The area is popular for skiing and cycling, and ski equipment rental is available at Turbaza Svetofor.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can give information on what to do in the area.

Ekaterinburg is 30 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Koltsovo International Airport, 39 miles from Turbaza Svetofor.
