Description

Offering free Wi-Fi, free private parking and ski storage, this hotel is located in the Sheregesh Ski Resort.

Traditional cuisine is served in the restaurant, and all rooms have a flat-screen TV.

The colourful rooms at Grünhof feature large windows with views of the surrounding mountains.

Each room offers a seating area, and toiletries are provided in the private bathroom.

Russian specialities are served in the restaurant, and drinks are offered at the bar.

Buffet breakfast is served at the property. The sauna at Hotel Grünhof features a plunge pool, and guests can enjoy a game of billiards in the games room.

The ski lift and Mount Gora Zelenaya are 200 metres from the hotel, and Tastago Train Station is 30 km away.
