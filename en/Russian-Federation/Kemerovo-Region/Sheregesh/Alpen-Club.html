Description

Located just 150 metres from the ski lifts, Alpen Club lies in the Gornaya Shoriya district of the Sheregesh Ski resort.

It offers free Wi-Fi in public areas, ski storage and a drying room. A modern kitchen, comfortable seating area and flat-screen TV are featured in Alpen Club’s stylish apartments.

Rooms are decorated in inviting, neutral tones with contemporary furniture and artwork.

In only a 5-minute walk from the apartments, guests can try traditional Russian and European cuisine served by the café and restaurant.

Tashtagol Train Station can be found within 20 minutes’ drive from Alpen Club and free private parking is available on site.
