Description

Gostinitsa Ogonek is offering accommodation in Voronezh. The property is set 2. 5 km from Patriot Park, 5 km from Solnechny Rai and 8 km from Tsentralnyi Profsoyuz Stadion.

The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and free WiFi throughout the property.

The units at the inn come with a seating area and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

All units will provide guests with a desk and a kettle. Gostinitsa Ogonek offers a à la carte or vegetarian breakfast.

The nearest airport is Voronezh International Airport, 24 km from the accommodation.
