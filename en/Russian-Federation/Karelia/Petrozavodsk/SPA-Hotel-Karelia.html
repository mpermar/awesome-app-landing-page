Description

Located in the very centre of Petrozavodsk, this modern hotel offers a well-equipped spa and rooms with panoramic views of Lake Onego and the surrounding parkland.

The bright rooms at the SPA Hotel Petrozavodsk feature wooden furniture and floors.

All rooms include a TV, work desk and a private bathroom, and Wi-Fi is available in the whole hotel.

The elegant Kareliya restaurant has large windows with views of Lake Onego. It serves a daily buffet breakfast and a selection of traditional dishes of the local cuisine.

Guests can relax in the sauna and book spa treatments at the Karelia Hotel. Laundry and dry cleaning services are available, and the hotel can arrange sightseeing tours.

SPA Hotel Karelia offers a 24-hour car park. Petrozavodsk Airport is a 15-minute drive away.

On weekdays, the hotel provides a free shuttle service to and from Petrozavodsk Train Station.
