Description

13 Chairs Boutique Hotel is located in Petrozavodsk city centre. It features unique décor and an indoor swimming pool with a sauna.

Free Wi-Fi and free private parking are provided. All classic-style rooms come with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, a safety deposit box and a fridge.

Free toiletries, slippers and a hairdryer are available in a bathroom. The on-site café offers a continental breakfast every morning.

The swimming pool with the sauna and the hot tub are available for extra charge. There is also the children’s playground for the youngest guests.

Onezhskoe Lake is 5 minutes’ walk away, and the lively Lenina Prospekt is 900 metres from the hotel.

Petrozavodsk Train Station is 1 km away, and the distance to the local airport is 28 km.
