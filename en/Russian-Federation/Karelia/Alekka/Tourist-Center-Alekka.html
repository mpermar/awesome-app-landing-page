Description

Featuring a sauna, Tourist Centre Alekka is located in a peaceful green area next to Lake Syamozero.

It offers bike rental and table tennis. Free parking is available. The comfortable rooms feature rustic décor.

Bathroom facilities are either shared or private. Some rooms have satellite TV. Tourist Centre Alekka has a bar and restaurant serving Russian cuisine.

It also offers BBQ facilities. Guests can enjoy fishing in the lake. The property can organize excursions in Karelia.
