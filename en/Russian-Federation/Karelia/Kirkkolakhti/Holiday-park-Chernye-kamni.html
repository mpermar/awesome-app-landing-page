Description

Each unit has a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge, a seating area with a sofa, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with shower and free toiletries. Some units have a dining area and/or a terrace.

A buffet breakfast is available daily at the property. At the resort village you'll find a restaurant serving European and Russian cuisine.

If you would like to discover the area, skiing is possible nearby and the property can arrange a bicycle rental service.
