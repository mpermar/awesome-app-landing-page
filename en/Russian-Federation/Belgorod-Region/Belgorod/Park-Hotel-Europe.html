Description

Offering a spa with indoor pool, free Wi-Fi and private parking, this 4-star hotel is located in an ecological area in a scenic forest.

Belgorod city centre is 6 km away. Spa facilities at the Park-Hotel Europe include a sauna, hot tub, and massage service.

Guests can also keep fit in the fitness centre, featuring a swimming pool, group trainings and various saunas or rent bicycles.

All of the soundproofed rooms and suites at the Park-Hotel Europe feature a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a balcony with forest views.

Guests can enjoy breakfast buffet and popular Mediterranean specialities in the Europe's Les&Lis restaurant.
