Description

Set 11 km from Belgorod State Regional History Museum in Belgorod, Riviera Wellness Resort features free WiFi access and free private parking.

The hotel has a terrace and spa centre, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and comes with a flat-screen TV. You will find a kettle in the room.

All rooms include a private bathroom. Extras include bathrobes and slippers. Guests can benefit from on-site beauty salon.

A number of activities are offered in the area, such as cycling and hiking. The hotel also offers free use of bicycles.

Belgorod State Fine Arts Museum is 11 km from Riviera Wellness Resort, while Smolensky Cathedral is 11 km away.
