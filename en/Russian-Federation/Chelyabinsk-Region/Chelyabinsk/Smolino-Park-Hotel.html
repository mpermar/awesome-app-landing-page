Description

Set in a quiet environment and overlooking the banks of Smolino Lake, Smolinopark Hotel has a sauna centre, small private beach and spacious accommodation with free Wi-Fi and satellite TV.

All rooms at Smolinopark are decorated with bright colours and have large windows facing the lake.

They each have a seating area and some rooms come with a balcony. The sauna centre consists of separate cottages that each have a sauna, Finnish and Turkish bath and a plunge pool.

On sunny days, guests can choose to relax at the beachfront area where sun chairs and umbrellas are provided.

A large breakfast buffet is served at restaurant Autograph. Dishes of European and Mediterranean cuisine are served at Tobacco Bar open 24/7 and XO Restaurant menu is offered.

Sunset restaurant is the place for a fresh cocktail or drink alongside the beach.
