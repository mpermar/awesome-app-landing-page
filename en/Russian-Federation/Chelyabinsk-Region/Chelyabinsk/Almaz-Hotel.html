Description

Quietly located beside a forest, a 5-minute drive from Chelyabinsk city centre, this 4-star hotel offers a gym, swimming pool and French cuisine.

Behind its modern glass exterior, Almaz Hotel has rooms and apartments with soft pastel colour schemes, overlooking the park or the courtyard.

In-room facilities include air conditioning, bathrobes and slippers. Guests can dine in the elegant La Rose D’Or restaurant.

The lobby bar offers free Wi-Fi, and serves a range of drinks and snacks. Almaz has a sauna and massage service.

Young guests will enjoy the playground. Free parking is provided at the Almaz Hotel.

Just 500 metres away, the large Lenin Avenue runs right across the city centre, and Gargarina Park is a 5-minute walk away.
