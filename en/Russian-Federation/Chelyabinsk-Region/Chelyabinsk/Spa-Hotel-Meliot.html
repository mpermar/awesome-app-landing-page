Description

Spa Hotel Meliot is located in Chelyabinsk, a 2-minute walk to the Ice Arena. Free WiFi access is available.

The comfortable rooms feature a flat-screen TV. You will find a SPA-club on the 2nd floor, that offers various types of saunas and spa baths, free for guests.

A gym and a beauty salon are also featured. King Gustav restaurant, coffee house and shared lounge area are available at Spa Hotel Meliot.

The hotel also offers laundry and ironing services. Meeting facilities can be also found on site.

Chelyabinsk Train Station is 11 km from the hotel, and Chelyabinsk Airport is 25 km away.
