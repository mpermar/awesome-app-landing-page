Description

Boasting eight hectares of forest and garden with BBQ facilities, Hotel Complex Utes offers rooms in Cherbakul, on the banks of the Lake Maly Terenkul and a 10-minute walk from the Lake Bolshoy Kisegach.

Surrounded by forest, it offers barbecue facilities. The property has free WiFi access in public areas.

With a balcony, each heated room is equipped with a TV with cable channels, electric kettle and refrigerator.

The private bathroom comes with a bath or shower and free toiletries. The restaurant serves Russian cuisine.

Guests can enjoy an array of activities on site, such as billiards, table tennis, swimming pool and sauna.

Guests can play a game of volleyball, mini-football, basketball on one of the sports grounds.

There is a conference hall and a children's playground. Cherbakul Train Station is a 4-minute drive from Hotel Complex Utes.
