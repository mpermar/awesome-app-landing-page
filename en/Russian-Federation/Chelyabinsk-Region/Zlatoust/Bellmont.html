Description

Located to Zlatoust’s city centre, Hotel Bellmont features a restaurant. It offers rooms with modern furnishings and free Wi-Fi access throughout.

Featuring a satellite TV, all rooms come with a minibar and an electric kettle. The private bathroom includes a shower and hairdryer.

A continental breakfast is served daily in the breakfast room. The restaurant serves European dishes at dinner.

Guest can enjoy themselves in the games room. Bellmont Hotel is a 5-minute drive from Zlatoust Trade Centre.

The Zlatoust ski slopes are a 15-minute drive from the property. Free private parking is available.
