Description

Anantara Dhigu Resort is surrounded by soft, white sand beach on Dhigufinolhu Island in the Maldives’ South Malé Atoll, a 35-minute speedboat journey from the island nation’s international airport.

The lagoon is shared with two sister islands Anantara Veli Resort and Naladhu Maldives, giving you access to a wider range of dining options, activities and facilities.

The villas and suites offer a choice of room types that are either poised over the water or nestled on unspoiled sands.

Choose between villas perfectly positioned to catch the sunrise or sunset, or those that have their own private plunge pool.

Among Maldives luxury accommodation, Anantara Dhigu is outstanding with its choice of stylishly designed villas and suites.

A number of these villas come with direct access to the powdery white beach and all only steps away from the tranquil lagoon.

The resort provides a harmonious combination of both the romantic, adventure and family friendly.

From the moment you arrive, peer into the crystal waters below the jetty at puffer fish, sting rays and baby reef sharks.

Head to the over water spa for a day of wellness and pampering. Hide away from the world with lazy days in a shaded hammock, drift slowly underwater through the reefs or thrill the family with adventures that include world-class scuba diving, surfing and deep sea fishing.

Take a short pontoon ride to the island of Gulifushi, picnic on the deserted beaches and experience some of the best snorkeling in the Maldives.

The cuisine at Anantara Dhigu Maldives Resort is an inspiring Maldives restaurant gastronomic experience.

Dining is relaxed and informal, with an array of seafood and meat grills at SEA. FIRE.

SALT. , fine Italian cuisine at Terazzo and varying themed buffet dinners, to lighter meals poolside for lunch.

Head over to the sister properties for Teppanyaki cuisine or authentic Thai dining overlooking the lagoon for an exquisite experience.
