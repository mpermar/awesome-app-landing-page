Description

Every Naladhu Private Island experience is one to treasure. Every space is yours to own.

Take residence in one of the classic houses inspired by colonial elegance or a majestic Two Bedroom Pool Residence overlooking the Indian Ocean.

A short 35-minute luxury speedboat transfer from Velana International Airport brings you to the secluded island where a personal butler will be awaiting your arrival and ready to tailor-create your holiday escape.

Blessed with beautiful white sand on one side, and the lullaby of rolling Indian Ocean waves on the other.

Naladhu Private Island Maldives is the realisation of the perfect holiday dream. Elegantly designed with an emphasis on recreation, world-class diving, fishing, surfing, indulgence and utter relaxation, this exquisite retreat features tennis courts, indoor-outdoor dining and a freshwater pool – all accessible to just 42 guests.

The island offers a unique holiday home-like feeling, underpinned by a utopia of seclusion that is created by private and tailored services.

Each house comes with a private pool and is hidden away in pristinely kept vegetation, entwined with sandy paths that seem to wind endlessly, along with your imagination and dreams.

Overlooking the lagoon, The Living Room is an elegant beachfront dining scene. The glass-walled wine cellar takes centre stage, stocked with some of the most esteemed (and a few of the most rare) vintages from both the Old and New Worlds.

Guests also have the option to take a short pontoon ride to sister properties Anantara Veli and Anantara Dhigu for endless dining possibilities including the award-winning SEA.

FIRE. SALT. restaurant or theatrical teppanyaki dining at Origami. Celebrate a special occasion with a private alfresco Intimate Dining Affair.

For a la carte Spa luxury coupled with wellness, Naladhu invites you to embark on a spa journey in the privacy of your villa or at the Anantara Spas on our nearby sister islands, with a choice of over water treatments at Anantara Dhigu and holistic therapies at Anantara Veli.
