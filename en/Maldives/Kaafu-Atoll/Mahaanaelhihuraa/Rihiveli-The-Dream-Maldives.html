Description

Rihiveli The Dream boasts sea-facing bungalows, each with its own private terrace overlooking the Indian Ocean.

The island is located in Kaafu Male South Atoll, 43 kilometers from the airport and is a 50-minute speed boat ride.

Charming bungalows with lagoon views, including terraces with sun loungers and hammocks.

The tastefully decorated rooms are equipped with fans. Laundry service included. The lagoon restaurant on stilts, offers a cuisine based on fresh produce.

Buffet meal with themed dinners on the beach. Enjoy tropical cocktails at the sunset bar.

Guests can enjoy a variety of water sports including sailing, water-skiing and night snorkelling during their stay at Rihiveli The Dream.

Other recreation facilities include volleyball, tennis and billiards. Rihiveli the Dream features a 5-star PADI scuba diving center offering all PADI courses with French speaking instructors.
