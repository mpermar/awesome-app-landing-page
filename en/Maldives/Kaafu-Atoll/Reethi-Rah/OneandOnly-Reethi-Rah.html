Description

The elegant villas at One & Only Reethi Rah are situated either over the lagoon or by the beach and have beautiful ocean views. Flat-screen cable/satellite TVs, iPod docks and DVD players can be enjoyed in all rooms.

Guests may enjoy massage treatments at One&Only Spa or rejuvenate with a game of tennis at the outdoor courts. The resort also has a beach club -the One Tribe, which caters to teenagers and KidsOnly, a spacious activity place for children facilitated by well-trained staff.

Built over the water, the Tapasake Restaurant serves modern Japanese cuisine, and the beachside Fanditha Restaurant has Middle Eastern food and champagne. Reethi serves Mediterranean cuisine. Other dining options include Rah Bar and 24-hour in-villa dining.

The One & Only Reethi Rah is a 1-hour and 15-minute luxury yacht ride or a 45-minute speedboat ride from Male International Airport.
