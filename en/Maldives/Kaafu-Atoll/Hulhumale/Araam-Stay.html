Description

Located in Hulhumale and with Ferry Terminal Park reachable within 700 metres, Araam Stay provides express check-in and check-out, non-smoking rooms, a restaurant, free WiFi throughout the property and a bar.

Featuring a shared lounge, this 4-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with a private bathroom.

The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.

At the hotel, each room includes a desk and a flat-screen TV. All units include a wardrobe.

Guests at Araam Stay can enjoy a continental breakfast. The accommodation offers a terrace.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike hire is available at Araam Stay. The nearest airport is Male International, 6 km from the hotel, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
