Description

Built on stilts, spacious air conditioned villas feature thatched roofs and a private wooden terrace with day beds. A flat-screen TV, CD player and minibar are included in all villas.

Werner Lau Diving Center offers professional courses for guests, while Filitheyo Spa provides relaxing body massage. Guests can also enjoy windsurfing, canoeing or volleyball by the beach.

The resorts main restaurant serves international buffet. For fresh juices and cocktails, The Sunset Restaurant & Bar is the place to go. Alternatively, the Filitheyo Bar offers a variety of drinks.

Filitheyo Island Resort is 73 miles south of Male International Airport. Seaplane transportation is provided.
