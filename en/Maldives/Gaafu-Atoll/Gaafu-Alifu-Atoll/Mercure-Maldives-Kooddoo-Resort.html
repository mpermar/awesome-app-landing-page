Description

Mercure Maldives Kooddoo Resort is located at a 55-minute domestic plane flight away from Male International Airport.

The resort boasts 43 Over-Water and 25 Beachfront villas with most villas offering breathtaking sunset views, and 20 villas with their own private pool.

The villa interiors use striking colours merged with stylish and contemporary design and free WiFi, USB charging ports, device docking stations and universal power sockets.

Alita Restaurant is open all day serving pan-Asian cuisine, with indoor and al fresco seating overlooking the swimming pool and lagoon.

The Pool Bar offers light snacks and favorites and Vistas is over-water lounge and bar.

A 24-hour in-villa menu is also available. Suvadiva Spa is a marine themed spa offering a range of treatments and therapies for guests including massages, scrubs, wraps and facials.

Suvadiva Divers Maldives is a PADI 5-star certified dive and water sports center offering easy access to Gaafu Alifu atoll.

Watersports such as snorkeling, sailing, kayaking are available to all guests free of charge.
