Description

Six Senses Laamu is a sustainable luxury resort surrounded by clear turquoise water and white sandy beaches.

Its villas come with a 37-inch flat-screen TV and Bose entertainment system. Rooms offer free WiFi.

Beautifully designed, air-conditioned villas feature an outdoor bathroom with rainshower where guests can shower under the stars.

Private day beds and sunloungers lets guests enjoy the Maldivian sun from the comfort of the villas.

Rooms offer electric kettle, slippers and dental kit. Newspaper can be acquired on request.

Six Senses Laamu is the only resort in the Laamu Atoll, in the south of Maldives.

It is located on Olhuveli Island which is a 35 minute inter-island domestic flight away from Male International Airport to Kadhdhoo, followed by a short speedboat ride Six Senses Spa features stylish private treatment rooms, Ayurvedic therapies and relaxing body massage.

The resort also provides a modern gym, yoga pavilion and an on-site diving centre.

LEAF serves modern Mediterranean dishes prepared with fresh organic ingredients, while premium wine and cheese can be enjoyed at Altitude Wine Cellar.

Breakfast options are available. Other dining options include international cuisine at Longitude, pizza at Sip Sip, ice cream at Ice and drinks at the Chill Bar.
