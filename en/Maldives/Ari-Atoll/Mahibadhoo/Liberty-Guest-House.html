Description

Guest rooms in the guesthouse are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Rooms contain a private bathroom with a shower, free toiletries and a hair dryer. All rooms include a desk.

The daily breakfast offers continental, à la carte or Asian options.

Liberty Guest House has a playground.

Speaking Hindi, English and Italian at the 24-hour front desk, staff are ready to help around the clock.

The nearest airport is Male Airport, 48 miles from the property.
