Description

This secluded island resort is 25 minutes from Male by air. It offers luxurious beachfront and over water accommodation with spectacular sea views.

It offers an on-site spa and yoga centre. The modern villas at Centara Grand Island Resort & Spa Maldives have large bathrooms with a rain shower, bathtub or a spa bath.

All have flat screen TVs with satellite channels and Chinese TV channels, electric kettle and high-speed Internet access.

Centara Grand Island has several restaurants offering Asian and Western meals which can be enjoyed al fresco.

Guests can enjoy the daily champagne breakfast at the Reef Restaurant. In addition, the resort offers swimming pool, water sports, kids club and a spa.

The property has staff who can assist guests in Chinese and arrange for daily excursion trips and activities such as diving and snorkeling.
