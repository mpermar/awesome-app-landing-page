Description

Plenty of water activities await guests. Snorkeling and fishing trips can be arranged, and canoes are available. The resort also offers open-air yoga sessions.

Other facilities include the Ginger Spa for Ayurvedic and traditional massages. A qualified diving center, well-equipped gym, beach volleyball court, and a library with books and DVDs are also available. The resort also has its own residential marine biologist.

Featuring spacious porches, the rooms are air-conditioned and equipped with a ceiling fan, a fridge, and a safe. The bathrooms have hot and cold showers.

Surrounded by coral reefs, Gangehi Island Resort is about 25 minutes from Male International Airport by sea plane. It is a 15-minute boat ride from Gangehi Kandu, a spectacular diving spot.

Veli Restaurant serves buffets with international cuisines, including Italian, Asian and vegetarian dishes. Offering panoramic views of the sea, the bar serves cocktails and wines.
