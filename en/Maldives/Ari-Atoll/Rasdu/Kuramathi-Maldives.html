Description

Set on a beautiful Maldivian island, Kuramathi Maldives offers luxurious private villas with a flat-screen TV and some offering a spa bath.

It features 12 dining options, kids club and a spa. Spacious air-conditioned villas feature spectacular views of the sea and provide easy access to the beach or gardens.

All villas have cable TV, DVD player and iPod docking station. All villas have minibar and in-room safe, while some villas offer an espresso machine.

Guests can relax at the resort’s 3 outdoor pools. A spa offers a range of massages, facial and body treatments.

Water sports and diving are available at water sports and dive centres. Kuramathi’s 3 main restaurants serve international buffets for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Guests can also enjoy fresh seafood at The Reef and traditional barbecues set on the beachfront at Island Barbecue.

7 bars offer refreshing drinks and cocktails. Kuramathi Maldives is a 20-minute seaplane ride from Malé International Airport.
