Description

Luxurious stays await in tropical-style villas located by the crystal-clear waters of a 4 star private island paradise.

Coco Palm Dhuni Kolhu features 4 dining options, fitness centre, spa facilities and a turtle sanctuary unique to the area.

Opening out to stunning views of the ocean or surrounding greenery, air-conditioned villas are fitted with fine wood and cane furnishings.

They feature private terraces with outdoor seating. Private bathroom comes with free toiletries including slippers and dental kit.

For leisure, head into the sparkling waters for a range of water sports including canoeing and diving.

Alternatively, indulge with soothing massages at Coco Spa. Union Pay is accepted here in the presence of the cardholder.

Dining highlights at Dhuni Kolhu Coco Palm include Cowrie, which features hearty international buffets in a large open-air pavilion.

Staff can arrange intimate in-villa dining experiences. Two bars, including one on the beach, are perfect for relaxing and watching the sun bid farewell to the day.

Coco Palm Dhuni Kolhu is 124 km or a scenic 30-minute seaplane flight away from Male International Airport.
