Description

Valamar Atrium Baška Residence is situated in Baška, on the island of Krk, just steps away from a long sandy beach.

Free WiFi is provided. The property enjoys a central location in Baška, with a variety of dining options and bars in the vicinity.

The hotel offers rooms and apartments equipped with air conditioning and a satellite TV.

Safe, hair dryer, a mini bar are standard facilities in all units. Apartments are fitted with a fully equipped kitchen.

Guests staying in rooms have a kitchenette at their disposal, upon a request. Complete your vacation experience with ritual of morning relaxation with freshly prepared fruit beverages in Atrium Café.

Together with a morning meal and dinner in the relaxing ambient of Atrium Café, a wide range of healthy nutriments and tastefully prepared meals are also served.

Guests can use the swimming pools and a wellness centre at the nearby Corinthia Baška Sunny Hotel by Valamar.
