Description

Offering a view of the sea, Apartments Sunčica is situated 150 metres from the beach in Mali Lošinj.

St Martin's bay, surrounded by a pine tree forest, can be visited nearby. The lush garden offers a barbecue and olive trees that provide shade and swings for children.

Free parking and free Wi-Fi are available. All apartments offer satellite TV, a kitchenette with a coffee machine and a private bathroom.

The furnished terrace or balcony overlooks the sea. Some apartments are air-conditioned.

There is a grocery shop available 200 metres away, while a fish restaurant can be reached in a few steps.

There is also a coffee bar 180 metres away. The centre of Mali Lošinj is 1 km away.

The museum in the centre offers the famous Apoksiomen statue, while a Wellness and Spa Centre can be reached in 2 km.

The Main Bus Station, as well as the Ferry and Catamaran Port can be found 1 km away, while Rijeka Airport is on Krk, 90 km away.
