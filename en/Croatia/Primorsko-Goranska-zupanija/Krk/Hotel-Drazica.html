Description

Situated right by the beach, 800 metres from the city centre of Krk, the Hotel Drazica features 2 outdoor pools with slides and various sports courts.

Live music entertainment and activity programs are offered, as well as free WiFi access.

All rooms are air-conditioned and come equipped with a satellite TV, a refrigerator and a private bathroom including a hairdryer.

Typical Croatian and international cuisine can be enjoyed in the restaurant on the ground floor of the Drazica Hotel or on the large terrace.

You can buy souvenirs and beach accessories, book excursions or exchange money on site.

There is also a fitness centre, a tennis court, a minigolf course and a massage parlour.
