Description

Set in Njivice, Camping Njivice offers free WiFi, and guests can enjoy a restaurant, a fitness centre and a bar.

All units come with a living room, a seating area with a sofa, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fully equipped kitchen with a dining table and a private bathroom with shower.

A fridge and stovetop are also featured, as well as a coffee machine. The holiday park offers a children's playground.

A kids' club is also available at Camping Njivice, while guests can also relax in the garden.

Baška is 36 km from the accommodation, while Krk is 18 km away. The nearest airport is Rijeka Airport, 12 km from Camping Njivice.
