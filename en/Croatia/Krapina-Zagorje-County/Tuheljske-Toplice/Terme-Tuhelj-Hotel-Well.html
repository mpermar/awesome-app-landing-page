Description

Terme Tuhelj Hotel Well spa hotel is located near a spring of thermal water, in the Hrvatsko Zagorje mountain region, 40 km from Zagreb.

It offers numerous indoor and outdoor pools, saunas and steam baths. All the rooms are distributed among 6 buildings, which are connected via closed corridors.

All rooms in the Terme Tuhelj are air-conditioned and feature a satellite TV and bathrobes.

WiFi is accessible in all rooms. You have free access to the indoor and outdoor swimming pools, to the fitness centre and to aqua aerobic and aqua stretching classes.

The large spa area includes 4 Finnish saunas, 3 steam saunas, a hot tub, a relaxation room with water beds and many more facilities.

The SpaeVita centre offers you a wide range of massages different facial and body treatments, a solarium and special healing mud treatments.

Activities offered at the Terme Tuhelj include guided hiking tours, morning gymnastics, wine tasting sessions in the Mihanovic Castle and various workshops.

Croatian and international cuisine is served in the restaurant. Healthy snacks and drinks can be enjoyed in the wellness bar.
