Description

Located on the D1 road in the village Oštarski Stanovi only 12 km north of Plitvice Lakes National Park, Tourist Centar Marko enjoys a picturesque location in well-preserved nature.

Sample rich diverse meals in a cosy and pleasant atmosphere at the restaurant, offering soups, side dishes, fish, meat on spike, pizza, lasagne and different desserts.

Venture out and explore the many possibilities for recreation, rest and entertainment.

8 km from Tourist Centar Marko you can visit the Barac's caves. The property offers an adrenaline park and an outdoor swimming pool.

Stretched along the canyon of the Korana River 8 km from the Marko, the Old Town trail is a great place for taking a walk.
