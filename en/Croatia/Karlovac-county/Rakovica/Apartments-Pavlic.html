Description

Located 7 km from the entrance to Plitvice Lakes National Park, Apartments Pavlić in Rakovica offers air-conditioned accommodation with free Wi-Fi and cable TV.

It features a ski storage room and an on-site playground. All units have a fireplace and a seating area with a sofa bed.

The kitchen or kitchenette is equipped with an oven, a fridge, and a tea/coffee maker.

Paintball and rock climbing facilities are within a 4 km radius, while guests can go horseback riding 5 km from the Pavlić.

Rafting facilities on Korana River can be reached in a 20-minute drive. Free private parking is provided on site.

The centre of Rakovica with a bus station is 300 metres away. Pavlić Apartments charges for airport shuttle service, which is available upon request.
