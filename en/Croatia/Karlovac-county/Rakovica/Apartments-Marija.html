Description

Located 8 km from the entrance to Plitvice Lakes National Park, Apartments Marija is set in a quiet area 1 km from Rakovica's centre.

It offers rooms and apartments with free Wi-Fi and a TV. All apartments feature a kitchenette with a dining area and a private bathroom, while some come with wood-panelled ceilings and a balcony.

In apartments, satellite TV and a DVD player are provided for guests' entertainment.

The lush and well-kept garden with a terrace and barbecue facilities invites guests to relax.

A children’s playground is also available. Free private parking is possible on site.

Barać Cave is 6 km from the Marija Apartments. Rastoke, known for its well-preserved mills and the picturesque little waterfalls can be reached within 15 km.
