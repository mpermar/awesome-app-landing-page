Description

Offering a restaurant and a tennis court, Hotel Lug is located 8 km from Bilje. Free WiFi access is available.

The property is 12 km from the Nature Park of Kopački Rit and 15 km from the centre of Osijek.

Each room is air-conditioned and provides a minibar and a flat-screen TV with both satellite and cable channels.

Featuring a hairdryer, the private bathrooms also include a bath or shower, as well as free toiletries.

Lug Hotel also offers meeting facilities, as well as a shared lounge. Cycling, hiking and hunting can be enjoyed in the nearby area.
