Description

Offering 2 outdoor pools and a sun terrace, Boutique Apartments Ad Astra is set in Bibinje, 200 metres from the Adriatic Sea.

Zadar is 7 km away. Free WiFi access is provided in all areas and free private parking is available on site.

All units are air conditioned and have a flat-screen TV, as well as a terrace or a balcony with sea views.

There is also a kitchen, fitted with a fridge and a stovetop, and a private bathroom with a bath or a shower.

Bed linen and towels are featured. Biograd na Moru is 19 km from Boutique Apartments Ad Astra.
