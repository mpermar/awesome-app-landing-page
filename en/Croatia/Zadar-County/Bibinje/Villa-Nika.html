Description

Located a 5-minute walk from the beach, Villa Nika offers 2 outdoor swimming pools, free WiFi and air conditioning.

Spacious terraces are available for all guests. The ancient city of Zadar is a 15-minute drive away.

Studios and apartments offer views of the garden, LCD TVs and satellite reception.

Each accommodation unit is themed according to a famous artist or painter – Renoir, Rubens, Leonardo, Dali, Michelangelo and Picasso.

The nearest supermarket is 50 metres from the Villa. D-Marine, that houses a diving centre, restaurants, bars and a gas station can be reached within 2 km.

Villa Nika offers an opportunity to organise boat trips to the surrounding islands.

Excursions to Plitvice Lakes National Park and to Krka National Park can also be arranged.

Zadar's most known sights include the Roman Forum, mediaeval St. Donatus Church and unique installations of Sea Organ and Greeting to the Sun.

Zadar Bus and Train Station can be found within 6 km and Zadar Airport is 6 km away.
