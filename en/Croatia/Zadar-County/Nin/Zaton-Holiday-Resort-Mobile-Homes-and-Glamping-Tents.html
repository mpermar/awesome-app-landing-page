Description

Zaton Holiday Resort Mobile Homes & Glamping Tents are located just 400 metres from sandy and pebbly beaches, between the towns of Nin and Zaton.

The complex is equipped with 7 restaurants, sports facilities, an outdoor pool and a children‚Äôs play area.

All mobile homes are air-conditioned and feature 2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms and a fully equipped kitchenette.

There is also a furnished patio at guests' disposal. Bathroom provides a shower. WiFi and free parking are provided.

The restaurants serve traditional specialties, pizzas and international cuisine. There is also a supermarket on site.

The tour desk offers excursions to the nearby national parks. Guests are entertained by the animation team that also organises workshops and shows for kids.

The town of Zadar can be reached in just 17 km, while Paklenica National Park can be reached in a 30-minute drive.
