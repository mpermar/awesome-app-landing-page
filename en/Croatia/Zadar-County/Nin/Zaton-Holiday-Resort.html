Description

Offering lots of activities for kids and adults, the Zaton Holiday Resort is located 3 km from ancient Nin and 15 km from Zadar.

It has its own beach and a pool complex. Spread over the large grounds with pine trees, all apartments have a fully equipped kitchen, a patio and a satellite TV.

Typical Croatian cuisine is served in the resort's restaurants with a shady terraces.

There is also a supermarket in the Zaton Resort. You can play tennis, mini golf, go diving, surfing or horse riding or take part in activity programs for all ages.

A large playground is there as well for the kids. Famous national parks like Plitvice Lakes, Kornati Islands, Krka Waterfalls, Paklenica and North Velebit are all situated around Zadar, about 2-hour drive away.
