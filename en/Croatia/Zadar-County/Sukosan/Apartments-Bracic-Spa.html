Description

Surrounded by lush greenery in the Zlatna Luca Bay, Apartments Bracic is only 500 metres from the sandy beach and 200 metres from Sukosan town centre.

It offers free Wi-Fi and a private outdoor heated pool with massage showers. All spacious and air-conditioned rooms and apartments provide en-suite bathrooms.

Most of them also have flat-screen satellite TVs and balconies with seating facilities and sea view.

A bakery and a supermarket can be found 20 metres from the property. Guests can benefit of the large garden with barbecue facilities.

Guests can use the outdoor pool free of charge, while sauna and massage are at an additional cost.

On request, trips to the Kornati Islands will be organised by Apartments Bracic. A secured parking lot is available free of charge.
