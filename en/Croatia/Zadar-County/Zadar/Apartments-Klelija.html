Description

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool, Apartments Klelija provides air-conditioned accommodation featuring a furnished terrace and free Wi-Fi.

The nearest beach is 400 metres away. All apartments comprise an equipped kitchen and dining area, sofa seating with a TV.

They have a bathroom with shower and a washing machine. A shared garden with seating area and barbecue facilities is at guests' disposal.

A restaurant, bar and a grocery store can all be find in a 500-metre radius. The nearby Borik holiday village offers a pebbly beach and various recreational activities, about 1 km away.

In the Old Town of Zadar, 2 km away, guests can visit the popular Greeting to the Sun and the Sea Organ, as well as the Roman Forum.

The Main Bus Station is 3. 5 km from the Apartments Klelija, and Zadar Airport is at the distance of 15 km.
