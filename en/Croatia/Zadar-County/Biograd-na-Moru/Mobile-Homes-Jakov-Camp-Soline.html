Description

The accommodations come with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with bath and free toiletries, while the kitchen features an oven, a microwave and a toaster. A terrace with garden views is offered in every unit.

The resort village has a grill.

Cycling and fishing can be enjoyed nearby, and a bicycle rental service is available.

Kornati Marina is 0.8 miles from Mobile Homes Jakov Camp Soline, while Biograd Ferry Terminal is a 17-minute walk from the property. The nearest airport is Zadar Airport, 12 miles from the accommodation.
