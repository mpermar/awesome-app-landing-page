Description

Pine Beach Pakostane - All Inclusive boasts a 1-km-long beach and it is set among an 180,000 m¬≤ big pine forest near the town of Pakostane and 30 km from Zadar.

The camp has a free internet corner, and free WiFi is available at the main bar and the beach bar.

The complex features 480 Polynesian-type straw cottages, equipped with ceramic tiles, beds and a wardrobe.

These natural straw cottages are without electricity and water. Every cottage is within 30 metres of toilet facilities, hot showers and water basins.

The seashore is not more than 200 metres from each cottage. Pine Beach Pakostane is a great way to experience direct contact with nature.

The all-inclusive offer features beach volleyball, five-a-side soccer, basketball, kayak and Italian bowls, archery and sailing.
