Description

Completely renovated in 2016, Senses Resort is located in a secluded and forested bay just 20 metres from the Adriatic Sea.

This resort is in the village of Vrboska, 5 km from Jelsa and 8. 2 km from Stari Grad.

All units are air-conditioned, feature a balcony and a seating area with a flat-screen satellite TV, a desk and a safe.

Private bathrooms come with a shower. At Senses Resort guests will find a swimming pool, a spa and wellness centre, fitness facilities and diving facilities.

A restaurant and a bar, as well as a 24-hour front desk can also be found on site.

Activities such as tennis, table tennis or mini golf are provided at the resort, while swimming, snorkeling and sunbathing are just some of the activities that can be enjoyed at the nearby beach.

Split Airport is located 78 km away and can be accessed by a ferry from Stari Grad to Split.
