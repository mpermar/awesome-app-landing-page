Description

Just steps away from the blue-flag Tučepi beach, the Bluesun Resort Afrodita in Tučepi is a charming complex of luxurious villas, set in a peaceful and intimate ambiance of a lovely Mediterranean garden.

It includes an outdoor pool, sun terrace with loungers and the lively Lido Pool Bar.

Afrodita offers you accommodation in rooms and apartments in authentic villas, situated around the central building and a swimming pool.

Boasting magnificent sea views, the complex is a 5-minute walk from the Slatina Sports Centre with football pitches, table tennis and terrains for tennis, basketball, beach volleyball and beach football.

The varied entertainment programme for children comprises an international Mini Club and the Mini Disco in the evenings during summer season.

Lido terrace features live music and different presentations by the animation staff.

Rich breakfast and dinner are served buffet style at the hotel restaurant, and there is also a possibility for lunch à la carte.

The nearby Kaštelet Restaurant has a large terrace and occasional live music during summer.

Guests can also visit the Hygge Bar and the Clemente's Pub, all conveniently located by the promenade and the Tučepi Beach.

There are also opportunities for parasailing, jet ski and water skiing on the beach.
