Description

Bluesun Hotel Alga is set in Tučepi at a picturesque pebble beach, offering modernly decorated rooms and healthy buffet meals at the hotel restaurant by the sea.

Free WiFi is provided in all areas. All rooms include air conditioning and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Private bathrooms come with free toiletries, a hairdryer and a walk-in shower. Some rooms also offer a balcony overlooking the sea.

The hotel has a tour desk and offers guided tours upon request. A varied children animation and entertainment programme is offered seasonally at the hotel.

Guests can refresh at the Hygge Bar with signature cocktails, while beer and burgers are served at the Clemente's Pub.

The typical Dalmatian specialities are served in the nearby à la carte restaurant Kaštelet.

Tennis, football and basketball courts are in the immediate vicinity of the hotel.
