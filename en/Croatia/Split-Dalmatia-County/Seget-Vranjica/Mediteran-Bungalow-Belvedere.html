Description

Offering accommodation with air conditioning set and surrounded by a Mediterranean vegetation , Bungalow Mobile Homes Belvedere is situated in Seget Vranjica, 1 km from the city centre and 5 km from the UNESCO-listed town of Trogir.

Hvar is 43 km from the property. All units include a seating and dining area. There is also a kitchen, equipped with refrigerator.

A stovetop and kettle are also provided. There is a private bathroom with a shower in every unit.

The on-site restaurant serves traditional Croatian meals and international cuisine.

Various sports can be enjoyed on-site, such as table tennis, football, basketball and volleyball.

Split is 20 km from Bungalow Mobile Homes Belvedere, while Šibenik is 35 km away.
