Description

This apartment with sea views features tiled floors, 3 bedrooms and a bathroom with a bidet and free toiletries. There's a bathroom with a hair dryer.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk speak Croatian, German and English.

Apartment Goleš has an outdoor pool. Snorkeling can be enjoyed nearby.

Split is 13 miles from Apartment Goleš, while Hvar is 27 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Split Airport, 6 miles from the property.
