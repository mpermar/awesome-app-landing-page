Description

The Villa Mediterana is set only 10 metres from the crystal-clear waters of the Adriatic Sea.

Only 4 km from the beautiful town of Trogir, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, the hotel offers rooms and apartments with view of the sea.

In front of the hotel there are several beaches. The hotel features an a la carte restaurant, as well as a spa and wellness centre with a sauna and a hot tub, and a relax zone.

Nearby a wide range of sports activities, as well as boat trips around the islands, are available.

You can also take trips to the Krka and Plitvice Lakes National Parks. The Dalmatian capital of Split is only 25 km away, and Split Airport can be reached in 9 km.

Yacht marina is only 3 km away. The property can organise shuttle service upon request.
