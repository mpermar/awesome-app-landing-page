Description

Offering outdoor swimming pools and a wellness centre with an indoor pool and a sauna, Waterman Milna Resort is set just steps away from the sea and 1.

8 km from Milna on the Brač Island. Free WiFi is featured and private parking is available on site.

Elegantly decorated and spacious rooms and suits are air-conditioned and equipped with a flat-screen TV.

A terrace or a balcony are featured in all rooms as well as a private bathroom with towels and a hairdryer.

Amenities include a mini fridge, a flat-screen satellite TV and a safety deposit box.

There is a children's playground and guests can enjoy buffet meals at the onsite restaurant which offers views of the Adriatic from its outdoor terrace.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property and the shuttle service can be arranged at the hotel at an additional cost.

Catamaran terminal to Split, Hvar and Dubrovnik is located in the centre of Milna, while the Ferry Port with lines to Split is set in Supetar, 21 km away.
