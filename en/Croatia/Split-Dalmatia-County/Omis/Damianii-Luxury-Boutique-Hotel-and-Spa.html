Description

Just 5 metres away from the Adriatic Sea and a private sandy beach area with canopies, the lavish boutique Damianii Luxury Boutique Hotel & Spa features a heated outdoor pool, a sun terrace surrounded by a garden with palm trees.

Secured parking garage is available at a surcharge. Free WiFi is available in all the stylishly decorated interiors of the Damianii.

Each individually furnished room comes with a terrace or a balcony, SMART cable TVs and a hot tub, while some units have a private sauna.

Guests have 24 hours access to fitness centre. The Damiiano restaurant offers a Dalmatian-Italian cuisine, prepared using fresh local ingredients.

It serves an à-la-carte breakfast, light meals during the day and high gastronomic delicacies for dinner.

Guests can also enjoy al fresco dining on the restaurant terrace that provides sea and pool views.

The Diamond Wellness & Spa center features a wide range of treatments as well as an indoor pool, steam room, sauna and Whirlpool jacuzzi.

Water sports rental facilities are available on the beach. Zipline, canoeing, rafting, photo safari, jeep safari, trekking, free climbing, as well as historic sightseeing and trips to islands can be arranged at the property.

The hotel is situated in the village of Duće within a 15-minute walk from the centre of the small town of Omiš.

A local bus stop with frequent lines to the centre of Omiš and to the UNESCO-protected historic center of Split can be found 50 metres away.

Upon previous request, the hotel can organise transfers to and from the Split ferry port, train and bus stations, as well as from Split International Airport.
