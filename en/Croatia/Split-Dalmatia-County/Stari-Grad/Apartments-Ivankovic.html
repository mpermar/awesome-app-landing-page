Description

Quietly located just 300 metres from the centre of Stari Grad, Apartments Ivanković offers free Wi-Fi, air conditioning and a TV.

It is within a 5-minute walk from a children-friendly beach with a bar and some sports facilities.

Surrounded by pine trees and other family homes, the property includes a garden with barbecue and a furnished terrace.

Several secluded coves can be found within a 10-minute walk from Ivanković Apartments.

Excursions to Pakleni Islands and Bol on Brač Island can easily be arranged with local boat owners on the seafront promenade in the centre.

Owners of Apartments Ivanković can help you organise jeep safaris and panoramic flights over the island.

Stari Grad Ferry Port with links to Split and Korčula is 1 km away. The vibrant town of Hvar is at a distance of 15 km.
