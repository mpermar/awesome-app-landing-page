Description

Located in a quiet street in Okrug Gornji, just 300 metres from the sea and a 10-minute drive from Trogir, Apartments Ars Vivendi is surrounded with greenery and offers air-conditioned accommodation with a balcony or a terrace set in a property with a garden.

Each apartment here has a TV and free WiFi access. The private bathroom comes with a bath or shower and a hairdryer.

There is also fully equipped kitchen with a microwave and a refrigerator as well as a dining area in all apartments.

A continental breakfast is served daily in the property's on-site restaurant. The restaurant specialises in local Croatian and Herzegovinian dishes.

Further offers from the proprietor include a boat rental, as well as organised trips to nearby historical sites, as well as to the pilgrimage destination of Meugorje.
