Description

Situated within a 5-minute walk from the beach, Apartments Delta offers air-conditioned apartments with balconies and free Wi-Fi.

Its outdoor swimming pool has a wave effect, a hot tub section and panoramic views.

Delta Apartments is located on the western side of the island Ciovo and its self-catered apartments have large windows, wooden floors and spacious living rooms with flat-screen satellite TV and DVD players.

The open-plan kitchens have dining tables and are fully equipped with dishwasher, ceramic cooker, oven and all necessary utensils.

Each apartment has 2 separate bathrooms and a washing machine. Delta Apartments offers organised fishing trips, a bicycle rental service and airport transfers.
