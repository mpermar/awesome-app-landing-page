Description

Featuring a swimming pool with sun terrace set right next to the sea, Hotel Brown Beach House offers pet-friendly accommodation in Trogir.

Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant or have a drink at the poolside bar. Free WiFi and free private parking are available.

The rooms are air-conditioned and fitted with a seating area with flat-screen satellite TV.

There is also a minibar and a safe in each. Certain rooms feature views of the sea or city.

Each room is fitted with a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower. Extras include bathrobes and slippers.

The property also features a gym and a spa centre. The use of the gym, sauna, indoor hot tub and relaxation area are free for all hotel guests.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. For an additional fee, guests can enjoy massage treatments.
