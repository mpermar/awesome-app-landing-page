Description

Set on the beachfront in Podaca and featuring a bar, MORENIA All Inclusive Resort has free WiFi throughout the property.

Boasting a 24-hour front desk, this property also welcomes guests with a restaurant, a water park and an outdoor pool.

The accommodation offers a fitness centre, karaoke and a kids' club. At the hotel, all rooms include a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

All guest rooms at MORENIA All Inclusive Resort feature air conditioning and a wardrobe.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet breakfast. MORENIA All Inclusive Resort offers a children's playground.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike hire and car hire are available at the hotel.

Makarska is 36 km from MORENIA All Inclusive Resort, while Brela is 49 km from the property.
