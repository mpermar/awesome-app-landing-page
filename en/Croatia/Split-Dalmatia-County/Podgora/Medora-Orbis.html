Description

Featuring a living room with a sofa, as well as a fully equipped kitchen, each unit has a private bathroom with shower. Some units are air-conditioned and include a terrace and/or a patio, as well as a seating area.

The restaurant at the resort village specializes in Mediterranean cuisine.

Medora Orbis has an outdoor pool.

A bicycle rental service is available at this property.

Međugorje is 30 miles from the accommodation, while Makarska is 5 miles away. The nearest airport is Split Airport, 44 miles from Medora Orbis.
