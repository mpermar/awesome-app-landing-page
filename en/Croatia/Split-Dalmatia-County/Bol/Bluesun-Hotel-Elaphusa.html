Description

Boasting the largest wellness and fitness centre on the Bra Island, a tennis centre with 26 clay courts and a bowling alley, Bluesun Hotel Elaphusa is located 50 metres from the sea and a short walk from the popular Zlatni Rat Beach and the centre of Bol.

Water world offers an indoor and outdoor pool, a paddling pool for kids and sunbathing area.

It also includes a tepidarium with heated water, ceramic beds and a cocktail bar.

Free children animation is organised. Elegantly decorated and spacious rooms comprise air conditioning, a king-size bed and a bathroom with a bathtub and hairdryer.

Amenities include a minibar, LCD TV with satellite channels, telephone and safety box.

For breakfast and dinner guests can choose from a large buffet, relaxing at a bar with a charming terrace.

An la carte restaurant Valium Tavern offers local and international specialties.

For night entertainment there is a Night Club Ella. Various saunas, hydromassage hot tubs and 12 beauty treatment rooms are provided at the Thalasso Wellness Centre.

Elaphusa Hotel houses 5 conference halls and meeting rooms fitted with first-class technological equipment.

Transfer service and various excursions around the region can be arranged at the hotel at an additional cost.
