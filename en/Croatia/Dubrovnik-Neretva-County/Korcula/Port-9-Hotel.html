Description

Completely renovated in 2017 and overlooking the bay, Port 9 Hotel features an outdoor pool with a sun terrace, wellness and fitness centre, as well as 3 restaurants.

Free WiFi access is provided in all areas. Korčula Old Town is at a distance of 2 km from the Hotel.

Air-conditioned rooms and suites feature a balcony and are located in 4 pavillion buildings.

Decorated in nautical style, the units include a flat-screen satellite TV, a minibar and a safe.

Private bathrooms come with a bath or shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer. Guests can enjoy the services offered at the resort area, including restaurants with local and international cuisine, as well as the pool and lobby bar.

The wellness area, featuring a sauna, spa bath and massages, as well as a fitness centre are available at surcharge.

Various activities such as tennis, cycling and water park are offered at the resort area as well as children’s playground and animation in the kids club.

The nearest airport is Dubrovnik Airport, 135 km away. Boats for Dubrovnik and the surrounding islands leave daily from the Ferry Port, within 550 metres from the Port 9 Hotel.
