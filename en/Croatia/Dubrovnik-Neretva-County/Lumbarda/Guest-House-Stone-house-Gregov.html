Description

The rooms at the guesthouse come with a seating area. The private bathroom is equipped with a bath and free toiletries. All rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at Guest House Stone house Gregov.

The accommodation has a grill. The area is popular for snorkeling and diving, and bike rental is available at Guest House Stone house Gregov.

Speaking English and German at the reception, staff are willing to help at any time of the day.

Beaches on Badija Island is 2.1 miles from the guesthouse.
