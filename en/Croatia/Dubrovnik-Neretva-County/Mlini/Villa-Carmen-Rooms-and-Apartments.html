Description

The family-run Villa Carmen Rooms & Apartments is set in the small tourist village of Mlini, in a quiet area of the Dubrovnik Riviera.

This house is surrounded by a Mediterranean garden and less than a 2-minute walk from sandy and pebbly beaches and the crystal-clear Adriatic Sea.

The rooms are furnished in a classic style and provided with modern amenities and a terrace.

They all offer a fan, a wardrobe and a private bathroom with shower. Villa Carmen Rooms & Apartments serves breakfast with home-made juices and jams on the terrace, which offers a spectacular view over the sea.

In the family-owned à la carte restaurant, situated only 50 metres from the Villa, local and traditional sea food, fish and meat specialities are provided.

A diving centre can be found nearby, while the surrounding area offers various possibilities for an active or relaxing holiday.

Mlini are a 20-minute drive from the centre of Dubrovnik and the Dubrovnik Airport.

In the vicinity of of Villa Carmen Rooms & Apartments guests can also find a port with regular boat lines to Dubrovnik and Cavtat.
