Description

All rooms at the Glavić are air-conditioned and offer flat-screen TVs and desks. Private bathroom provides bathtub or shower. A furnished terrace with a view of Dubrovnik Old Town is at guest disposal.

The Villa proves to be a good starting point for trips to Montenegro, 30 miles away. Dubrovnik Old Town with its many restaurants, boutiques, bars and cafés provides daily entertainment for all guests. The nearest grocery shop is 5 minutes away.

The facilities of the Grand Villa Argentina, available for all guests of the Glavić, include a private beach, an outdoor salt water swimming pool, an indoor swimming pool, a spa center and a gym, as well as a cafe, a restaurant and lounge bar and a spacious restaurant terrace.

Dubrovnik Airport can be reached in 11 miles and Villa Glavić provides the possibility to organize shuttle service for an additional charge.

Ploce is a great choice for travelers interested in food, beaches and scenery.
