Description

Just 300 metres from the Copacabana Beach on the picturesque Babin Kuk Peninsula, Adriatic Dream Apartments features air-conditioned accommodation with free Wi-Fi and a balcony or a terrace.

Local buses with direct links to the Old Town stop 200 metres away. A satellite TV and a private bathroom are provided in all accommodation units.

The property is surrounded by a furnished garden with typical Mediterranean greenery.

Free private parking is possible in front of house. A supermarket is next to the property, while restaurants and some other beaches can be reached in a few minutes' walk.

Daily trips can be organised on site. Transfer service from and to Dubrovnik Airport is available at a surcharge and upon request.
