Description

Enjoying a quiet location at a picturesque beach, the 5-star Sun Gardens Dubrovnik member of The Leading Hotels of the World, offers panoramic views of the Adriatic Sea and Elaphiti Islands.

Guests can relax at the one of the 3 outdoor pools. Free WiFi is available in all areas.

Modernly decorated hotel rooms and suites feature a private bathroom and a balcony.

The spacious Residences come with a fully equipped kitchenette, a dining area, seating area, and a balcony or terrace with sea or garden views.

The spa and sports facilities are spread over 6,000 m, and include a state-of-the-art fitness area, an outdoor tennis court, a football pitch and a rock-climbing wall.

A 3,500 m wellness and spa centre with an indoor pool, saunas, gym and a hot tub is as well included.

Various restaurants and bars of Sun Gardens Dubrovnik offer a wide selection of traditional Dalmatian specialities, Mediterranean cuisine with seafood dishes as well as International cuisine.

Sun Gardens Dubrovnik is set 11 km from the historic centre of Dubrovnik and the hotel provides the possibility to organise shuttle service with an extra charge.

The nearest airport is Dubrovnik Airport, 35 km away. Airport shuttles are available upon request and at a surcharge.
