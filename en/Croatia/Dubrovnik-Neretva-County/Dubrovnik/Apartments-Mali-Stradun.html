Description

Apartments Mali Stradun is situated in the Babin Kuk district in Dubrovnik, just a 3-minute walk from the nearest beach.

Dubrovnik Old Town - Pile Gate is 5 km away. Free WiFi is offered. The accommodation is air conditioned and features a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

All units feature a terrace and balcony. There is also a kitchen, equipped with a microwave.

A toaster and fridge are also provided, as well as a kettle and coffee machine. Each unit comprises 2 bathrooms, one on the lower and another on the upper level.

Towels and bathrobes are available. Restaurants, bars and shops, as well as a water park can be found a short walk away.

A number of activities are offered in the area, such as snorkelling and diving. The nearest airport is Dubrovnik Airport, 24 km from the property.
