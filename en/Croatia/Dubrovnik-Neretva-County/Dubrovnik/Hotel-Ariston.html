Description

Air conditioning and satellite TV are available in every room. All have tea and coffee making facilities and most have a large balcony with sea views.

Cocktails and snacks are served at the outdoor Beach & Lounge Bar, with wicker chairs and parasols. Breakfast can also be served here.

The buffet-style Neptun Terrace Restaurant includes a terrace with a view of the Adriatic Sea and the pool terrace. Mediterranean dishes are served at the à-la-carte Zoë restaurant.

The Ariston is located in the Babin Kuk part of the Lapad Peninsula, only 16 mi from Dubrovnik Airport. Dubrovnik's Old Town is 2.8 mi away.

The bus to Dubrovnik's center stops every 15 minutes just a few feet down the road. The bus ride takes only 20 minutes.

Babin Kuk is a great choice for travelers interested in beaches, the oceanside and sunshine.
