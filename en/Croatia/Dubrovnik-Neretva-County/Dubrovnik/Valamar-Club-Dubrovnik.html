Description

The family-friendly Valamar Club Dubrovnik Hotel on the lush Babin Kuk peninsula offers an easy access to the Old Town of Dubrovnik.

The whole property is air-conditioned and the rooms offer all modern amenities. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

The large swimming pool, the multitude of sports courts, aquatic sports nearby at the beach and organised entertainment programmes for your kids provide for a pleasant vacation.

The all inclusive light offer includes a rich buffet breakfast, lunch and dinner with drink during the meals.

Offer for children under 16 years includes soft drinks (from the soda machine) in the Maro Club throughout the day.

Because of its position on the Babin Kuk peninsula, the Valamar Club hotel is surrounded on 3 sides by the sea and beautiful pebbled and rocky beaches, including the famous Copacabana.

Valamar Club Dubrovnik Hotel offers the Maro Club for children, while somewhat older guests will enjoy the relaxing atmosphere of the lobby bar with a spacious sea-view terrace.
