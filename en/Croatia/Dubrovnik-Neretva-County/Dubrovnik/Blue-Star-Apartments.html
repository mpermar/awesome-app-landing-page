Description

Only 100 metres from the nearest beach, Blue Star Apartments is set 1. 5 km from Dubrovnik’s Old Town.

Featuring free Wi-Fi and free public parking, it offers air-conditioned studio apartments.

Overlooking the historic centre, Adriatic Sea and the island of Lokrum, two studios come with a balcony.

There’s also a fully equipped kitchen and private bathrooms in all units. A restaurant is 200 metres away, while a grocery shop is within 400 metres.

Tennis courts are at a distance of 1 km. Dubrovnik Cable Car with views of the surrounding islands is 1.

8 km away. The UNESCO-protected centre is full of sights. Some of them include the Town Walls, Stradun Promenade and Onofrio’s Fountain.

Many picturesque cafés, restaurants and shops can be found within its narrow streets.

A local bus stop is 50 metres away. Dubrovnik Bus Station and Ferry Port are at a distance of 4.
