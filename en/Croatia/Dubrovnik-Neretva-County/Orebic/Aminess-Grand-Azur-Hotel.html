Description

Boasting two newly renovated freshwater outdoor swimming pools and a sun terrace, Aminess Grand Azur Hotel features a beachfront location with panoramic sea views in Orebic and Korčula.

Free WiFi access is provided. All air-conditioned rooms come equipped with satellite TV, a safe and a minibar, while private bathrooms are fitted with a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

The restaurants serve Mediterranean cuisine using locally produced olive oil accompanied by a selection of local red wines.

The hotel also comprises a garden, a terrace, an aperitiv bar, massage room and meeting facilities.

All guests staying at this property can benefit from an all inclusive light offer that comprises buffet meals and various drinks during meals in the buffet restaurant (non-alcoholic, beer, wine).

Water sports, windsurfing opportunities, tennis, basketball and beach voleyball courts can all be found on site.

Entertainment and children's programs are provided. A strip of beaches with sun loungers and parasols is located nearby.

Bike rental is available. A seaside promenade connects Aminess Grand Azur Hotel to the centre of Orebić, while a ferry leading to the Island of Korčula is 1.

3 km away. Shuttle service from and to Dubrovnik Airport can be arranged at a surcharge and upon previous request.

This hotel has received a local certificate for eco friendly and sustainable business.
