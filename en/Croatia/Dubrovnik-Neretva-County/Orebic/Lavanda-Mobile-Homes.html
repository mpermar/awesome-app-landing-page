Description

The accommodations come with a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher and coffee machine, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with shower. There's also a microwave, fridge and a electric tea pot.

The resort village has a playground. Lavanda Mobile Homes provides a laundry service, as well as business facilities like fax and photocopying.

If you would like to discover the area, snorkeling, cycling and hiking are possible nearby and the property can arrange a bicycle rental service.

Međugorje is 28 miles from the accommodation, while Makarska is 23 miles from the property.
