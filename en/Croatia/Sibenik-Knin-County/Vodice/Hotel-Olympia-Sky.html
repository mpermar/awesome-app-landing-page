Description

Featuring a beachfront location in Vodice, 700 metres from Vodice Marina, Hotel Olympia Sky offers an outdoor pool and a spa and wellness centre.

The hotel provides views of the sea, restaurants and a bar. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

Each room at this hotel is air conditioned and comes with a flat-screen TV, a minibar and a safe.

Most rooms include a balcony. Private bathrooms are equipped with a bathtub or a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

For your comfort, you will also find bathrobes and slippers. There is an à la carte restaurant and a buffet restaurant on site.

Guests can relax in the hotel sauna or work out in the on-site fitness centre. The hotel offers bike hire and car hire.

You can play tennis and table tennis at the hotel. Split Airport is 80 km from the property.
