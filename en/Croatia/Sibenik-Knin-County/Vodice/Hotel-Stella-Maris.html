Description

Offering fully air-conditioned rooms only 120 metres away from the beach, the Hotel Stella Maris can be found a 10-minute walk from the centre of Vodice.

It offers free Wi-fi and free parking. The recently renovated rooms come with tiled floors, a modern bathroom and a satellite TV.

A buffet breakfast can be enjoyed every morning. Half board also includes a 3-course dinner with a choice of 2 menus.

Near the Stella Maris Hotel there are tennis and volleyball courts, a minigolf course and a football field.
