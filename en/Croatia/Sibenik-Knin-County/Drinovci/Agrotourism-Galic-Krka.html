Description

Offering an outdoor pool and a restaurant with local delicacies, Agrotourism Galic is located in Drinovci.

This property comes with free WiFi access and free private parking. Each apartment is equipped with a flat-screen TV and a dining area, as well as a kitchen or kitchenette.

Private bathrooms comprise a shower or a bathtub and free toiletries. Garden views are provided from all rooms.

On the premises of Agrotourism Galic, guests will also find a children's playground and barbecue facilities.

Activities like cycling and darts can also be enjoyed. Krka National Park can be reached in 5 km.

The historic town of Šibenik is 40 km away. Split Airport is located at a distance of 80 km.
