Description

Set at the entrance of Krka National Park, Country House Peace is located in Drinovci.

It offers 2 bedrooms and an apartment. It features free WiFi and a garden. The property is 2 km from the Island of Visovac and 4 km from Roski Slap.

The rooms and the apartment feature a private entrance, seating area and linen and towels.

Featuring a shower, the private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Homemade dishes are available upon request. This guest house is 62 km from Split Airport.
