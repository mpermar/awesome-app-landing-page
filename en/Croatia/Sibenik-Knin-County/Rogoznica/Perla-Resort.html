Description

Featuring an outdoor pool with sun loungers and parasols, Perla Resort is right on the private beach facing the picturesque Rogoznica bay.

The Resort houses a grocery store, and its luxurious air-conditioned apartments comprise a balcony with sea views and free WiFi.

This elegant modern-style apartments feature a living room with a flat-screen satellite TV, a fully equipped kitchen and a private bathroom with shower and free toiletries.

Laundry and ironing services are available at a surcharge. A selection of wines is offered at an on-site bar with spacious terrace.

The property also offers boat, jet ski and luxury boat rentals, as well as tours and excursions to nearby points of interest.

Guests can visit the historic town of Rogoznica, 2 km from the Perla Resort. Free private parking is provided on site.

Split Airport is at a distance of 35 km, and shuttle service is possible at a surcharge.
