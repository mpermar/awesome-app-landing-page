Description

Located right by River Krka and the yacht marine in Skradin, Villa Barbara offers air-conditioned apartments with balcony, LCD satellite TV and free WiFi.

The property includes a green garden with a terrace. Each apartment has a well equipped kitchen including dishwasher and dining area.

The private bathroom comes with shower and bathrobe. The restaurant next door offers local specialities on its open terrace, and a number of other restaurants and bars can be found all over the small town of Skradin.

Use of a whirlpool bath is also available at an additional surcharge. You can visit the nearby Krka Waterfalls National Park and the charming Visovac Island featuring a historic monastery.

The local bus stop is 300 metres away, while the nearest access to E65 Motorway is 5 km from Barbara Villa.
