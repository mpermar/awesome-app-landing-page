Description

Located 2 km from the centre of Šibenik and 900 metres from the beach, Apartments Marina offers self-catering accommodation with free Wi-Fi access.

The property is 4 km from Fortress of St. Nicholas. All units are air-conditioned and feature a balcony, a sofa and a flat-screen satellite TV.

Each one comes with a kitchen and a dining area, as well as a bathroom fitted with a shower and a washing machine.

A fast food restaurant and a pizzeria can be found within 300 metres. A grocery shop and a cafe bar are 350 metres away.

A public bus stop is just across the street and the Main Bus Station is 1. 5 km away.
