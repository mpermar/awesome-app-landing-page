Description

Surrounded by Mediterranean vegetation, pine woods and with modern facilities, Hotel Amadria Park Jakov is set in Å ibenik, only 50 metres from the beach.

Completely renovated in 2018. All rooms comprise air conditioning, a TV with satellite channels and a minibar.

Each accommodation unit has a private bathroom with a shower. There are diverse facilities on the beach and the hotel provides sports and entertainment possibilities.

Enjoy the comfortable interior, the rich restaurant menu with excellent wines and great dishes and the highly skilled and pleasant staff.

For breakfast guests can choose from the buffet arrangement. The hotel is 6 km south of the historical town of Sibenik, which is famous for its beautiful St.

James' Cathedral. The closest airport is Split Airport, 51 km from Hotel Amadria Park Jakov.
