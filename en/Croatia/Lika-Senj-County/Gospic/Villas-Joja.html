Description

Each unit features with a patio, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a well-fitted kitchen and a private bathroom with free toiletries. There's also a dishwasher, oven and a electric tea pot.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property.

The wellness area at the villa is comprised of a hot tub and a hammam.

If you would like to discover the area, skiing, snorkeling and hiking are possible nearby and the property can arrange a bicycle rental service.

Plitvička Jezera is 21 miles from Vilas Joja. Zadar Airport is 35 miles away.
