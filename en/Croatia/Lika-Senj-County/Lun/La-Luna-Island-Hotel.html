Description

Featuring a peaceful private beach with views of the surrounding islands, an outdoor pool, an on-site spa and wellness centre, as well as a restaurant, La Luna Hotel is set in the village of Jakišnica on Pag Island.

It comprises modern and spacious rooms, only 20 meters from the sea. The hotel rooms are at least 32 m2 in size and majority of them offers panoramic views of the Adriatic Sea and surrounding islands of Lošinj and Rab.

The private bathrooms are fitted with a bath or a shower and a hairdryer. Free toiletries are provided.

Spread over 1200 m2, the spa and wellness centre includes an indoor pool, relax rooms, Finnish, Turkish and bio-sauna, a steam bath and a sediment mud bath.

Its guests can also re-energize with a wide variety of massage and beauty treatments.

A rich buffet breakfast is served every morning and includes show cooking. Guests can also enjoy Mediterranean and International cuisine in the hotel's à-la-carte restaurant located just a few steps from the sea.

La Luna Hotel comprises a 250 m² business centre including 3 congress halls and an exhibition space.

Welcome drinks, coffee & tea breaks, cocktail parties and festive lunches and dinners featuring local delicacies can be arranged for congress participants at a surcharge.

La Luna Hotel is a 15-minute drive away from the town of Novalja and the famous Zrće beach known for its festivals and rich nightlife.

The hotel offers shuttle service to and from Novalja and Zrće Beach, as well as tickets and special packages for various parties.

Day trips to and speedboat cruises of Pag, Rab and other surrounding islands can be organized.

Private outdoor parking is free while garage parking is available at a surcharge.
