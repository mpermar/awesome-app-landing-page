Description

All the air-conditioned units have a kitchen with a dishwasher, an oven and dining area. A microwave is also available, as well as a coffee machine and a electric tea pot.

The apartment has a terrace.

Skiing and cycling can be enjoyed nearby, and a bicycle rental service is available.

Plitvička Jezera is 10 miles from Plitvice Diamond Apartment, while Bihać is 10 miles away. Zadar Airport is 48 miles from the property.
