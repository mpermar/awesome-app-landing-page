Description

Set in a quiet area, Apartments Zafran offers accommodation units with a balcony.

It is surrounded by a garden with a large terrace and BBQ facilities. The Plitvice Lakes National Park is 5 km from the apartment house.

Every unit is air-conditioned and comes with a fully equipped kitchenette, cable TV and a bathroom with a shower or a tub.

A safe deposit box can be used by all guests. Apartments Zafran provides shuttle services.

A breakfast can be requested and is served on the terrace during summer. A supermarket is just 20 metres away.

Zafran Apartments offers an on-site restaurant that serves a wide variety of local dishes for lunch and dinner.

The ski area in Mukinje can be reached within a 5-minute drive. The Korana River is 1 km from Apartments Zafran.
