Description

Renovated in May 2015, Park Plaza Arena Pula is surrounded by a fragrant pine forest just 50 metres from the Adriatic coast.

A health and wellness centre is complemented by 2 sea water swimming pools with sun deck offering sea views These modern-style rooms are fitted with design furniture.

Amenities also include air conditioning, flat-screen cable TV, minibar and a seating area.

Bathrooms come with a hairdryer and free toiletries. A short stroll leads to the Verudela Avenue, where guests can find Espressamente Illy and Champagne Bar, several dining options as well as some shops and designer boutiques.

The centre of Pula is 4 km away, preserving the well-known Roman Arena, ancient monument and today's venue for concerts and live performances.

Pula Airport is 10 km away. Park Plaza Arena Pula provides car and bike rental service.
