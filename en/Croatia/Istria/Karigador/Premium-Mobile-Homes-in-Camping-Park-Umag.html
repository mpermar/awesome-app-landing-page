Description

Located in the picturesque bay of Karigador just outside town, Camping Park Umag is a complex consisting of an outdoor pool, beach area and elegant mobile homes with flat-screen TVs.

Free WiFi is provided. There is a grocery shop about 300 metres from the mobile homes, while a restaurant and bar are on site.

You can have fun in the games room and shop for souvenirs, while children have a playground.

All mobile homes come with air conditioning and a fully equipped kitchen including basic kitchenware, 4 hot plates, sink and fridge.

An Istrian themed "Eco Village" is also part of the Camping Park Umag. Sports facilities include terrains and equipment for beach volleyball, football, cycling and windsurfing.
