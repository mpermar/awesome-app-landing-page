Description

Offering a patio and pool views, the vacation home includes 4 bedrooms, a living room, satellite flat-screen TV, an equipped kitchen, and a bathroom with a bath.

The vacation home has an outdoor pool. A grill is available on site and both cycling and fishing can be enjoyed within close proximity of Villa Cranzetti.

Morosini-Grimani Castle is 1.7 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Pula Airport, 12 miles from the property.
