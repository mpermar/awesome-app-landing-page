Description

Petalon Resort is located next to the sea and surrounded by a pine wood where you can enjoy the beautiful view of the green Mediterranean oasis.

Free WiFi is provided in public areas. The property enjoys a peaceful location and offers an abundance of sport and recreation.

Petalon is a member of the Maistra d. d hotel chain and we welcome our guests with thoroughly renovated rooms and suites.
