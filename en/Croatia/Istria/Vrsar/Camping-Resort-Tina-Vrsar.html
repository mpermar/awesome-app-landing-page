Description

Located on the Istrian west coast, a 10-minute walk from the old town of Vrsar, Camping Resort Tina Vrsar offers air-conditioned mobile homes with a terrace.

Blue Flag beach is just 150 metres away. An aqua park is 8 km away. All mobile homes comprise a flat-screen satellite TV and a kitchenette with a microwave, electric kettle and dining area.

Private bathrooms are equipped with a shower. Bedlinen and WiFi are provided free of charge.

Groceries, fruits and vegetables are provided at the local stalls or at the supermarket nearby.

Car rental can be arranged at the reception, and free private parking is available on site.

The coast of Vrsar is surrounded by 18 unpopulated islets providing a good destination for daily boat trips.

Guests of Camping Resort Tina Vrsar are also welcome to enjoy the local fishermen feasts, cycling and diving.

Istrian landscape is known for its picturesque little mediaeval towns like Motovun, Grožnjan, Hum and several others, which are within a 50 km radius.

Pula Airport is at a distance of 40 km. Bike rental and boat excursions can be organised on site, while beach volley courts and table tennis facilities can be found 500 metres from the property.
