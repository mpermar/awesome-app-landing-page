Description

Situated just 1 km from the town centre and directly by the sea and the beach, Hotel Sol Umag offers modern and elegant air-conditioned rooms with a magnificent sea view.

There is a wellness centre and several restaurants. The large outdoor pool has a children’s section, a rich daytime programme of activities and numerous evening events.

At the wellness centre, with indoor pool, 3 types of sauna, whirlpool and relaxation room, guests can book a number of beauty and massage treatments.

There is a buffet restaurant with show cooking, the à la carte Léon d’Or Restaurant and a pool bar serving a number of snacks and local specialities.

In addition to private parking, Hotel Sol Umag also offers possibility to reserve an exclusive parking place right in front of the hotel at a surcharge.

An integral part of the Sol Umag Hotel is also the Bike Point with professional support for road cyclists, mountain bikers, cycling tourists and amateurs.
