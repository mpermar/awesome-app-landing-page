Description

Featuring indoor and outdoor pools and a wellness area, Sol Umag Residence benefits from a beachfront location 1 km from the centre of Umag.

Guests can relax at an à la carte Léon d’Or Restaurant and a pool bar serving a variety of snacks and local specialities.

The rooms are air-conditioned and comprise a balcony, minibar, safe and a private bathroom.

Several restaurants are provided serving local and international dishes. All hotels facilities provided are located at the Hotel Sol Umag, which is 50 metres from the Sol Umag Residence.

The large outdoor pool has a children’s section, a rich daytime programme and numerous evening events.

The wellness area includes an indoor pool, 3 types of sauna, hot tub and a relaxation room, guests can also book a number of beauty and massage treatments.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including tennis and cycling.

An exchange office is also provided, as well as meeting and conference rooms. Stella Maris ATP Stadion is 600 metres from the Sol.

Pula Airport is 84 km away and shuttle service is possible at a surcharge. A secure parking is provided at an additional cost.
