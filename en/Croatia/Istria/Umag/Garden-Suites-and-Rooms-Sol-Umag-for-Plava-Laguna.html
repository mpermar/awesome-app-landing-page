Description

Featuring an indoor and outdoor pool, Garden Suites & Rooms Sol is set less than a 5 minute drive from Umag centre.

The property offers a seafront accommodation with free WiFi. A various wellness & spa treatments are available on-site as well as a fitness centre.

At the property, rooms and suites are fitted with a flat-screen satellite TV and air conditioning.

Private bathrooms come with a walk-in shower and free toiletries. Each unit features a balcony.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning at the property. There is an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of local and international dishes.

Garden Suites & Rooms Sol Umag offers a children's playground and kids animation.

There is also a bar with live music and evening entertainment programs. ACI Marina Umag is 700 metres away, while Umag Town Museum is less than a 10 minute drive from Garden Suites & Rooms Sol.
