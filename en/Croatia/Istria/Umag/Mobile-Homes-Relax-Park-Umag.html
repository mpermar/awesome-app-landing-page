Description

Mobile Homes Relax Park Umag is located under the shades of pine trees, just steps away from the beach.

The mobile homes provide a terrace with sun loungers and dining furniture, and are fitted with air conditioning.

Each unit has a kitchenette with dining area, and 2 bedrooms. There is a private bathroom with a shower, hairdryer and free toiletries.

Guests are welcome to bring their own towels and sheets. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling, hiking and windsurfing.

The property offers free parking. Umag and Novigrad are both within a 9-km radius.
