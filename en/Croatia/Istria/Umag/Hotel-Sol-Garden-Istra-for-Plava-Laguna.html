Description

The Sol Garden Istra is surrounded by pine and oak trees and only 250 metres from the sea in the Katoro tourist resort, near Umag.

All rooms have air conditioning and a balcony. The hotel offers an Aqua Park with numerous pools and the Sports Art centre where you can play basketball, handball and many other sports, as well as taking part in day and evening entertainment.

Guests can relax in the wellness centre, with sauna, indoor pool and relaxation room.

A number of beauty and massage treatments are also available. Istarska Taverna offers Mediterranean specialities à la carte and there are 3 restaurants with show cooking and several snack bars.

Free ice cream, refreshments and snacks are available for kids from 5 up to 12 years of age at a given time during the day in the period from 15th of May until 15th of September.

Sol Garden Istra offers possibility to reserve an exclusive parking place right in front of the hotel at a surcharge.
