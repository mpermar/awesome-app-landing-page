Description

Located in Komunela Apartment Village, 800 metres from the centre of Umag, Apartments Trbovic offers free Wi-Fi and free parking for all guests.

The lush garden offers a barbecue and a small children’s playground, while the nearest beach is 1 km away.

All apartments offer air-conditioning, a seating area, flat-screen TV and a fully equipped kitchenette with a dining area.

Private bathroom provides a shower. Laundry service is available upon request. There is a restaurant and bar 300 metres away, while a market can be reached in 150 metres.

Numerous wine routes can be found all around Umag. Football and basketball courts can be found 100 metres away, while a golf court is available 5 km from the property.

The Main Bus Station is 1 km away, while Pula Airport can be reached in 80 km. The town of Poreč is 35 km away, while colourful Rovinj can be reached in 65 km.
