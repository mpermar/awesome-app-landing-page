Description

Superior Mobile Homes in Camping Kastanija are set just 3 km from the centre of Novigrad and the Old Town.

The complex features an outdoor swimming pool, restaurant and bar, shop and numerous beaches and sunbathing areas.

All mobile homes are fitted with air-conditioning, a flat-screen TV and a fully equipped kitchen.

Private bathroom provides a shower, while terrace offers a seating area. A restaurant serving local specialties and international dishes can be found 200 metres away.

There is also a grocery store and a souvenir shop 200 metres away. Tennis courts are available 1 km away, while beach volley, mini golf and water sport facilities can be found 2 km away.

Guests can also visit the Lapidarij Museum, 3 km away. Main Bus Station is 2 km from the property, while a Ferry Port is 70 km away, in Pula.
