Description

Located in Aminess Maravea Camping Resort, 4 km from Novigrad, Relax Premium Village Holiday Homes features 2 outdoor swimming pools, restaurants and bars.

Free WiFi access and free private parking is provided on site. All air-conditioned holiday homes come with a patio and comprise 2 bedrooms, a living room with a sofa bed and a flat-screen satellite TV, as well as a fully equipped kitchen.

There are 2 bathrooms, fitted with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries. The camp also features a show cooking restaurant serving local delicacies, a grill and barbecue restaurant and a lounge bar with a rich offer of finger food and cocktails.

The newly renovated beach with a paved sunbathing area, sun loungers and showers, as well as some pebbly bays is located a short walk from the holiday homes.

In addition, guests can use the mixed-use football, basketball and handball playground and outdoor fitness facilities or stroll by the seaside promenade and enjoy the sunset.
