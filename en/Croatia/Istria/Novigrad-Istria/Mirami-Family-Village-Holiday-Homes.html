Description

Located steps away from the beach and 4 km from Novigrad, Miramì Family Village is tailor-made for kids, and features professional educators and a variety of watersport activities and educational programmes.

Free WiFi and free onsite parking is provided. Surrounded by greenery, each air-conditioned mobile home comes with 2 or 3 bedrooms, a sofa bed and 2 bathrooms with a shower.

A flat-screen TV and a kitchenette with a fridge and a micro are featured. All units feature a kitchenette fitted with a microwave and refrigerator.

Towels and linen are offered. The unique family village provides fun and stimulating experiences and workshops such as Biology Labirinth, Crazyology Funxperiments and Robotich Castle, as well as educational equipment and playful evening programs.

Children can enjoy mini-Olympics or take part at Mirami Mini Clubs. The camp also features a show cooking restaurant serving local delicacies, a grill and barbecue restaurant and a lounge bar with a rich offer of finger food and cocktails.

In addition, guests can use the mixed-use football, basketball and handball playground and outdoor fitness facilities or stroll by the seaside promenade and enjoy the sunset.

On the campgrounds guests will find a supermarket, a couple of restaurants and a beach café and lounge bar.

Miramì Family Village Holiday Home also comprises two outdoor swimming pools with sun decks and water slides.
