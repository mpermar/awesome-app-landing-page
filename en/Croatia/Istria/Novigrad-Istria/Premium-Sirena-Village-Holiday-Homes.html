Description

Set 300 metres from the Adriatic Sea and 800 metres from Novigrad, Premium Sirena Village Holiday Homes offers self-catering accommodation with a private parking space and a covered terrace.

Free WiFi is provided. Each air-conditioned mobile home comprises 2 bedrooms, a living room with a sofa bed and a flat-screen satellite TV, as well as 2 private bathrooms with a shower.

There is a fully equipped kitchen with a dining table and a set of dishes for 5 people.

Bed linen and towels are included. Camping Aminess Sirena has a concrete and a pebbly beach with lifeguards, as well as a tidal bathing area with seawater for adults and children that was awarded the Blue Flag for its cleanliness.

The Premium Sirena Village also provides two 24-metre pools with sun deck. Guests will find a gym, tennis courts and water sports facilities in the vicinity.

Bikes are also available for rent directly at Sirena Homes. Entertainment facilities and a couple of restaurants and bars are within easy reach.

Guests of Premium Sirena Village Holiday Homes have a 30% discount for use of the indoor swimming pool at the nearby Hotel Aminess Maestral and also benefit from a 10% discount to massage and beauty treatments at Hotel Aminess Maestral's wellness centre.

Guests can have breakfast and dinner service at the Aminess Laguna Hotel, available at a surcharge.

Delivery of baked goods is possible. The nearest airport is Pula Airport, 70 km from the property.
