Description

Featuring indoor and outdoor swimming pools, a restaurant and a lobby bar, Hotel Aminess Maestral is located just 80 metres from the Adriatic Sea and a 10 minutes walk from the town of Novigrad.

Free WiFi and free parking are provided. All air-conditioned rooms include a flat-screen satellite TV, a safe and a minibar, as well as a private bathroom with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

The outdoor swimming pool has a poolside bars, while the a la carte restaurant offers typical Istrian dishes.

The wellness centre is spread over more than 2. 500 m¬≤ and includes an indoor swimming pool with heatedse water, modern spa facilities, such as a gym area, a solarium, as well as massages and a beauty area.

Various sport and entertainment possibilities are available. This bike-friendly hotel comprises a bike room, a laundry service, as well as a tour desk where excursion tickets can be bought.

In front of the Aminess Maestral Hotel there are sun loungers and parasols. There is also a newly appointed beach with attractive tidal bathing area, new entertainment facilities and gourmet points.

This hotel has received a local certificate for eco friendly and sustainable business.
