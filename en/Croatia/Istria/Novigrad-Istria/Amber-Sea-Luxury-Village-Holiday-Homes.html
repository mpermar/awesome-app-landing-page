Description

Situated in Buraj, just 4. 5 km from Novigrad, Amber Sea Luxury Village Holiday Homes offers accommodation with free WiFi and a swimming pool.

Guests can use the seasonal outdoor swimming pool, barbecue facilities and a bar, located on the grounds of Aminess Maravea Camping Resort.

The air-conditioned 2-bedroom holiday homes come with a seating area, a satellite flat-screen TV and a kitchenette with a dishwasher, as well as a private bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer.

Free private parking is available in the vicinity of the holiday home. The Aminess Maravea Camping Resort also features a show cooking restaurant serving local delicacies, a grill and barbecue restaurant and a lounge bar with a rich offer of finger food and cocktails.

In addition, guests can use the mixed-use football, basketball and handball playground and outdoor fitness facilities or stroll by the seaside promenade and enjoy the sunset.

The accommodation offers a children's playground and a kids' club, located in the camp.

The area is popular for cycling and guests can park their bikes at the assigned bicycle rack near the holiday homes.

The nearest airport is Pula Airport, 70 km from Amber Sea Luxury Village Holiday Homes.
