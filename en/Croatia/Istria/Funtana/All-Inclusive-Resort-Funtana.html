Description

Located in the small fishermen's town Funtana close to the ancient Vrsar, Hotel Funtana provides excellent facilities and an extensive all-inclusive offer including an animation programme and superb eating venues.

Free WiFi is provided in the hotel lobby and pool area. Boasting a seafront location at a rocky beach with crystal-clear water, the property's accommodation units are arranged in 4 pavilions cascading towards the beach.

There are 2 swimming pools - one for children - with amazing views of the sea and the islets within the Vrsar Archipelago.

Hotel Funtana is well-protected from the winds, while being surrounded by woods and wine yards.

Benefit from the amazing all-inclusive offer which includes 3 meals at the main restaurant, a variety of snacks throughout the day as well as a wide selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages.

The rich sports offer provides great opportunities for everyone: You can rent a pedal boat, have fun at the aqua park, take part in hobby workshops or study at the Croatian language school.

Morning gymnastics and aerobic as well as dance lessons are offered as well. Have fun during the rich evening entertainment programme including live music.

The mini club for children from 4 to 11 years offers daily (except on Saturdays) entertainment for your little ones.

There is a specially arranged children's beach, and sun chairs on the beach are available at no extra charge.
