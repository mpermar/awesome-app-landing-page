Description

Located 6 km from Bale and a few steps from the beach, Mobile Homes Mon Perin features air-conditioned units.

The property offers 3 restaurants and a spacious garden filled with pine trees. Free private parking and free WiFi are provided.

All mobile homes come with an equipped kitchen and a dining area as well as a seating area and a flat-screen satellite TV.

Private bathrooms feature a shower. Guests can relax on a furnished terrace available in each unit.

A seasonal dinosaur-shaped swimming pool is at guests disposal as well as an aqua park.

Guests can enjoy free panoramic train rides through the camp and to the village of Bale.

Other services such as stand up paddle rental, diving school, windsurfing, outdoor theatre and sports playgrounds are available in the camp.

Fort Forno is 2. 5 km away while Rovinj is 19 km from the property. Pula Airport is 32 km from Mon Perin Mobile Homes.
