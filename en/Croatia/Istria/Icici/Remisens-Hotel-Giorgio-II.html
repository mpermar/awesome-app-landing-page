Description

Set right next to the sea and built in 2017, Remisens Hotel Giorgio II is set in Ičići surrounded by greenery.

The hotel features a seasonal outdoor pool and sun terrace. Guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the poolside bar.

Free WiFi access is available in public areas. The modernly-designed rooms feature air conditioning and a balcony.

A flat-screen TV with satellite channels is included in each. A safe and minibar are also available.

Each bathroom comprises a hairdryer and bath or walk-in shower. You will find a 24-hour front desk, dry cleaning services, gift shop and shops at the property.

Private on-site parking is available at a surcharge. Giorgio II Remisens Hotel is 2.

9 km from Opatija and 10 km from Rijeka. Rabac is 48 km away, while Crikvenica is 49 km from the property.
