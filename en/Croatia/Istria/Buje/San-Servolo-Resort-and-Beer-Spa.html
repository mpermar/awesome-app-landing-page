Description

Featuring a restaurant, a wellness centre and free WiFi throughout the property, San Servolo Resort & Beer Spa is set in the Istrian countryside near Buje.

This 4-star property offers a 24-hour front desk and free private parking. All rooms are air-conditioned and fitted with a flat-screen TV, a safe and a minibar.

Private bathrooms include a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer. San Servolo Resort & Beer Spa offers a continental buffet breakfast.

The wellness area can be used free of charge and consists of a fitness room, a swimming pool and a Turkish and Finnish sauna.

The use of the Beer Spa services is available upon request and at a surcharge. The property also provides bike rental and bike storage facilities.

The area is popular for cycling and hiking, while the nearest beach can be reached in 9 km.

Trieste is 26 km from the property, while Portorož is at a distance of 12 km. Pula Airport is 61 km away.
