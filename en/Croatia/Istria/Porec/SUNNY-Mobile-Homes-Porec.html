Description

Offering an outdoor pool and fitness centre, SUNNY Mobile Homes Poreč is set in Poreč in the Istria Region, 1 km from Aquacolors Water Park Poreč.

There is a water park on site and guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. Poreč Main Square is 3.

1 km from the property. Free WiFi is provided throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

The accommodation is air conditioned and has a satellite flat-screen TV. Some units have a seating area and/or terrace.

There is also a kitchenette, fitted with a microwave. A toaster, a fridge and stovetop are also available, as well as a kettle.

There is a private bathroom with a bath or shower in every unit. Mobile Homes Poreč also includes a seasonal outdoor pool.

The property also offers mini-market. You can play tennis and table tennis at the property, and the area is popular for golfing.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including cycling, fishing and hiking.

The Euphrasian Basilica is 3. 3 km from SUNNY Mobile Homes Poreč. The nearest airport is Pula Airport, 42 km from the property.
