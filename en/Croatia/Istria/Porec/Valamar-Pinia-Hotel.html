Description

The seafront Valamar Pinia Hotel offers a seawater outdoor pool. Poreč’s historical centre is a 10-minute walk away along the beach.

Free WiFi is available in all areas. Air conditioning and satellite TV are provided in all the rooms.

Each one comes with an en suite bathroom and some include a balcony. The all inclusive offer comprises breakfast, lunch and dinner.

During lunch and dinner the following drinks are included: local (red and white) wine, draught beer and soft drinks available for self service.

Show-cooking and themed dinners are offered at the restaurant. In the Lobby Bar, you can enjoy hot drinks, refreshing drinks and cocktails all day long.

In the afternoons during the high season, from 15 May until 15 September, you’ll find a great choice of sandwiches, toast, ice creams as well as several types of desserts.

The following activities and services are also available during the high season: Maro Kids Club, game lounge, recreational and entertainment programmes, free use of bikes and bike tours, Music&Fun evening entertainment, fitness centre, beach volleyball court, table tennis, as well as poolside loungers and parasols.

Other facilities and activities, available at a surcharge, include a sports centre, mini golf, tennis courts, water sports, sun loungers on the beach, Finnish and Turkish saunas, relax garden and hot tub, as well as the beauty centre's treatments.
