Description

Hotel Laguna Mediteran in Poreč offers peace and relaxation in the cedar and oak woods only 100 metres from the beach.

Its offers include an outdoor swimming pool, as well as a children's pool, with heated salt water, and free-to-use sun loungers.

Free WiFi is available in the entire property. This fully air-conditioned hotel offers comfortable rooms that include satellite TV and a private bathroom that is fitted with a shower and provides a hairdryer.

Some rooms feature a private balcony. A safety deposit box is available at a surcharge in all rooms.

All meals are served buffet-style and thematic dinners are held once per week during high season.

A variety of vegetarian dishes are provided and lunch packets can be ordered as well.

The hotel offers a restaurant with outdoor terrace, an aperitif and cocktail bar.

Free pool towels are provided. The hotel's beach is grassy, stone-paved and rocky, and offers loungers and parasols at an additional cost.

While staying at the beach, guests can choose from a number of bars and restaurants.

Various entertainment programmes are provided both for children and adults. Tennis, mini golf, beach volleyball and other sports, as well as water sports, can be enjoyed.

Evening entertainment with live music is provided on a regular basis, and guests can partake in various sports tournaments.

A safety deposit box is available at a surcharge in all the rooms. The town of Poreč is 2 km away.
