Description

Hotel Laguna Materada in Poreč is only 100 metres from the beach and surrounded by spacious parks and grassy sun-bathing areas.

Guests can relax at the swimming pool with a children’s section and sun loungers or at the beach awarded the Blue Flag.

Free WiFi is available in the entire property. All rooms are air-conditioned and comprise a balcony, satellite TV and a private bathroom.

The hotel restaurant serves local and international dishes. Guests can sample a selection of drink at a cocktail bar or a pool bar, while buffet breakfast is served daily.

Free pool towels are provided. During the season, you will be entertained with live music, professional show programmes and diverse animation.

A beauty salon and an exchange office are also provided, as well as meeting and conference rooms.

A variety of sport opportunities can be enjoyed on-site or in the vicinity, such as tennis, table tennis, minigolf, volleyball, mostly at an additional cost.

There is a mini club and teens club entertainment for children, as well as Lagunino happy meal for children up to 14 years old.

Poreč is located 3 km away. Local bus stops right in front of the hotel. Pula Airport is at a distance of 60 km.
