Description

Located within walking distance from 5 different beach types on the forested Lanterna Peninsula, Valamar Tamaris Resort features outdoor swimming pools and various restaurants, as well as a rich sports and entertainment offer.

Free WiFi access is provided in all areas. The air-conditioned accommodation is available in the central hotel building, as well as rooms, suites and apartments in nearby annex buildings.

All units include a flat-screen satellite TV, a safe and a minibar, as well as a furnished balcony or a terrace.

Private bathrooms are equipped with a bathub or a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Apartments also come with a furnished kitchen and a dining area. The resort restaurants provide a varied offer of international, Mediterranean and Istrian meals in a relaxed setting and guests can enjoy refreshments and snacks in one of the resort bars.

Valamar Tamaris Resort offers a rich entertainment and animation programme for both children and adults, while children can play in the designated outdoor Maro Play zone spread on more than 1900 sq metres.

The resort also comprises extensive sports grounds, including tennis courts, water sports facilities, mini golf and horseback riding.

After a day full of activities guests can relax in the hotel sauna, book a massage or work out in the hotel fitness centre.

Bikes are provided free of charge and upon availability. The nearest airport is Pula Airport, 52 km from the property.
