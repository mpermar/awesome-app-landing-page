Description

Overlooking the Adriatic Sea, Rezidencija Skiper Apartments is set on the beautiful Istrian coast near Savudrija.

It offers a large pool, access to the private beach and a sport centre. All apartments provide large terraces or balconies with seating facilities, as well as a bathroom and a kitchen with a living area.

Deckchairs and sunshades at the pool are available free of charge. Guests of Rezidencija Skiper Apartments can relax in the sun or get active at one of the tennis courts, the 18-hole Championship golf course or at the beach volleyball court.

The fitness centre is fully equipped; sports equipment can be rented on request. The Skiper’s à la carte restaurant serves Istrian and Mediterranean cuisine either in the air-conditioned dining rooms or on the terrace, boasting wonderful sea views.

Specialities of the house include seafood salad, grilled prosciutto with sheep cheese and home-made Pasta Tartufi with truffles.

Guests of the Rezidencija Skiper can choose a variety of snacks and drinks at bars.
