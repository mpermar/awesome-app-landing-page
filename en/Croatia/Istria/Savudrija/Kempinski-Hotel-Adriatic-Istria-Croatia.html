Description

Croatia’s first 5-star golf & spa resort is located in Savudrija, right on the Istrian coast.

It has a private beach, a 3,000 m² spa centre, indoor and outdoor pools. The golf course can be reached in just 300 metres.

Kempinski Hotel Adriatic Istria Croatia offers free WiFi. The spacious, elegantly furnished rooms have a balcony with views of either the park or the sea.

They feature air conditioning, a minibar, and a laptop safe. In addition to 3 bars, the Kempinski Adriatic features 2 restaurants.

The Dijana serves Mediterranean cuisine. The beachfront Kanova restaurant offers traditional Istrian specialities.

Guests can enjoy the privacy of their room or balcony for 24-hour in-room dining.

Guests can also play a round of golf on the 18-hole championship course at Kempinski Adriatic Istria.
