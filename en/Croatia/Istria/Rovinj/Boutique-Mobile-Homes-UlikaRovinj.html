Description

Fitted with a balcony, the units feature a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with free toiletries. Some units include a seating area and/or a patio.

Guests at the resort village can enjoy a continental breakfast. There's an on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of Mediterranean dishes and also offers vegetarian options.

Boutique Mobile Homes UlikaRovinj has an outdoor pool.

A bicycle rental service is available at the accommodation.

Rovinj Marina is 2.1 miles from Boutique Mobile Homes UlikaRovinj, while Carera Street in Rovinj is 2.2 miles away. Pula Airport is 17 miles from the property.
