Description

Offering views of Rovinj Old Town, the nearby islands and Adriatic Sea, Grand Park Hotel features a wellness and spa centre, outdoor swimming pool and a private marina next to the Mulini Beach.

Elegantly decorated rooms and suites come with a minibar, kettle and an espresso machine as well as with a seating area and a Smart TV.

The private bathrooms include a bathtub and a walk-in shower. All units offer a balcony or a terrace.

The hotel features various restaurants, bars and a pastry shop. The buffet breakfast is served daily in the main hotel restaurant.

The wellness area offers different types of saunas, steam rooms and indoor pools.

Guests can enjoy various activities, such as tennis, jogging and cycling. Rovinj Aquarium is 2.

5 km away while the Rovinj Heritage Museum is 3 km from Grand Park Hotel Rovinj. The nearest airport is Pula Airport, 40 km from the hotel.
