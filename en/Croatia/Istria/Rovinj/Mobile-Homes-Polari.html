Description

All mobile homes feature air conditioning, a furnished terrace, deck chairs and a flat-screen satellite TV. It offers a full kitchen with a dining area and 2 bathrooms.

Facilities include a large swimming pool and a children’s pool, restaurants, bars and a beach awarded with a Blue Flag. Other activities include tennis, beach volleyball, basketball, handball and ping-pong. Polari also offers a children’s playground, mini club and rich entertainment program.

The ancient city of Pula is 22 miles away. It is known for its amphitheater which is one of the six largest surviving Roman arenas in the world. Pula International Airport is 24 miles from the Polari Mobile Homes.
