Description

Stretching along a calm bay and a beach, 5 km from Rovinj, Mobile Homes Veštar offers an outdoor swimming pool and a range of sports and water activities.

Guests can enjoy on-site restaurants and a bar. Free WiFi and free private parking are provided.

Each mobile home features air conditioning, a furnished terrace with deck chairs and a flat-screen satellite TV.

It offers a fully equipped kitchen with a dining area and 2 bathrooms. Towels are provided.

Facilities consist of a large swimming pool and a children’s pool, restaurants, bars and a beach awarded with a Blue Flag.

Other activities includes a diving centar, waterpark, bikes hire, table tennis, beach volleyball and a trampoline.

A children’s playground, mini club and rich entertainment program are also provided.

The ancient city of Pula is 35 km away. There guests can visit the well-known Roman Arena and several ancient monuments.
