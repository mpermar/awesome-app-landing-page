Description

All of the air-conditioned units feature a private bathroom, flat-screen TV and fully-equipped kitchen. A microwave is also provided, as well as a coffee machine and a electric tea pot.

The resort village has a playground.

A terrace is available for guests at Plamar Mobile Homes Bi Village to use.

Brijuni National Park is 2.2 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Pula Airport, 6 miles from the property.
