Description

Situated next to the sea, just above the seaside promenade in Lovran's centre, Hotel Excelsior offers free Wi-Fi, an outdoor and indoor swimming pools with heated sea water, as well as a children's outdoor pool and a sauna.

The hotel's stone-paved beach is steps away. The hotel's Teens Club includes PlayStation games, air hockey, table tennis, sjoelen table shuffleboard, bocce, darts and a team game called City Quest.

The Mini Club features various games, activities and creative workshops for children aged 4-12.

Guests can also rent bikes, helmets and knee pads, as well as nordic walking poles.

Each room is air conditioned and comes with cable TV and a minibar. The bathroom includes a hairdryer and either a bathtub or a shower.

Room service is available throughout the day. The restaurant serves buffet breakfast, lunch and dinner, and it provides a Kids' Corner with entertainment staff during dinner.

Occasional themed or gala dinners are organised and are accompanied by live music.

There is also an on-site pizza restaurant with a terrace and a cocktail bar. The main attractions of Lovran include the remnants of the city walls, the gates to the harbour and the restored medieval tower which is now home to the Maritime Museum and the gallery of world renowned painter Charles Billich.

Renovated villas dating back to the Austro-Hungarian monarchy can be found nearby.

The town of Opatija is 5 km away and can be reached via a lively seaside promenade.
