Description

Decorated in a bright functional style, rooms at the Camino Real offer free Wi-Fi and cable TV. Each room includes a desk, a safe and a private bathroom with free toiletries.

The Casa Blanca restaurant serves international and Mediterranean cuisine. You can enjoy meals and drinks at the Las Palmeras poolside snack bar, which also offers Sunday barbecues.

Camino Real Managua is next to the Pan American Highway, a 10 minute drive from central Managua. Free private parking is available on site, and car rental can be arranged at the 24-hour front desk.
