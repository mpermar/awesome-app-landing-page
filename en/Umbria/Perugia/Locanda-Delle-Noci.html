Description

Guest rooms come with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, oven, a electric tea pot, a bidet and a closet. A seating area is available in all guest rooms, while selected rooms will provide you with a DVD player. The rooms will provide guests with a toaster.

A continental, American or Italian breakfast is served at the property.

The hotel has a playground. You can play ping pong and pool at Locanda Delle Noci.

Speaking English, Italian and Portuguese at the 24-hour front desk, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical advice on the area.

Piazza IV Novembre is 12 miles from the accommodation, while Corso Vannucci is 12 miles away. Sant'Egidio Airport is 16 miles from the property.
