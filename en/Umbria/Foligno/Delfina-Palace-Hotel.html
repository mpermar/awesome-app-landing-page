Description

The hotel is in Foligno, in close proximity of the Foligno Nord exit of the S.S. 75 highway and is closely linked to the most important Umbrian towns, such as Assisi, Perugia, Spello, Orvieto, and Montefalco.

During your stay, you can keep in contact with family, friends, and colleagues by taking advantage of the hotel’s free Wi-Fi access.

The swimming pool is open from June until mid-September.
