Description

Sillustani is a family-run guesthouse 3-blocks from the centre of Puno, 45-minutes drive from Manco Capac Airport.

It offers archeological tours, boat trips, and a restaurant serving local delicacies.

The property is located only 100 metres from Lima Main Street. The tour desk at Hotel Sillustani Inn can arrange boat trips to Uros Floating Island, nightly folkloric dances are performed, or excursions to Sillustani archaeological site, overlooking Umayo Lake.

Amantani Restaruant serves Novoandina food such as trout, quinoa and smoked cheeses, while the bar offers traditional Pisco Sour.

A breakfast with eggs, natural juices and yoghurt is served daily. Guests are welcomed in the spacious lobby featuring ornamental window panes and colourful hand-painted country scenes.

They may also enjoy free Wi-Fi and complimentary parking. Rooms at Sillustani Inn are spacious, with carpeted floors and laminated wood walls.

All have cable TV and en suite bathrooms with hairdryers. Hotel Sillustani Inn is 7-blocks from Puno Train Station.
