Description

All guest rooms at the hotel come with a seating area. Rooms include a private bathroom, while some rooms are equipped with a kitchen.

A buffet, à la carte or American breakfast is available each morning at the property.

Villa Mercedes Hotel Urubamba has a terrace. You can play ping pong and darts at the accommodation, and the area is popular for cycling.

Saint Peter Church is 0.7 miles from Villa Mercedes Hotel Urubamba, while Sir Torrechayoc Church is a 19-minute walk away. Alejandro Velasco Astete International Airport is 19 miles from the property.
