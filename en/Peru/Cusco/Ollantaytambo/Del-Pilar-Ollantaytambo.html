Description

Del Pilar Ollantaytambo offers accommodation in Ollantaytambo, 9 km away from the Train Station, and 30 km away from Machu Pichu.

The property offers a daily breakfast with tropical fruits. Del Pilar Ollantaytambo has 16 rooms and 2 family bungalows.

All of them equipped with WiFi, hot water, Cable TV and private bathroom fitted with showers.

The property also offers private parking, Guest can relax with drink by the fireplace or enjoy a meal at the restaurant with international and local cuisine.

Guest will also find a tour desk with a variety of options to offer including shuttles to Cusco Airport for a surcharge.
