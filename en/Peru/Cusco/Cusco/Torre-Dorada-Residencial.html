Description

This 4-star hotel situated in Residencial Huancaro offers free Wi-Fi and free parking in Cusco’s centre.

It is 5 blocks from the Central Bus Station and Plaza de Armas. Torre Dorada Residencial features individually decorated rooms with wooden flooring.

Room service is available throughout the day. A complimentary buffet breakfast is served daily and includes a selection of local dishes and drinks.

Upon request, the helpful staff can prepare packed meals for guests who wish to travel to Machu Picchu.

Facilities such as oxygen treatment, medical care, and free luggage storage are available.

The property offers guests free airport shuttle services with prior request. Torre Dorada Residencial's nearby attractions include the Inca Garcilaso de la Vega Stadium and the Santo Domingo Church.
