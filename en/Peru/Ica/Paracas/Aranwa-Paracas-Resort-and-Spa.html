Description

Featuring a fantastic outdoor pool and an oceanfront location Aranwa Paracas Resort & Spa offers accommodations in Ica, a 5-minute drive from El Chaco port and Cruz del Sur bus station.

Free WiFi access is available and guests are served a complimentary breakfast. Rooms here are all fitted with a private bathroom, a flat-screen cable TV, a minibar and air conditioning.

Some rooms offer great ocean or pool views and guests will also find the pool access deluxe room, from which one can step directly to the pool from the room balcony.

At Aranwa Paracas Resort & Spa guests will find an on-site restaurant, a 24-hour front desk and spacious gardens.

In addition, guests are welcome to use the fitness centre. Other facilities offered include luggage storage.

Dry cleaning and ironing service are available for a small fee. The hotel is located on Panamericana Sur Highway KM 245.
