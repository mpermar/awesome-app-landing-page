Description

All of the rooms at La Hacienda Bahia Paracas are air-conditioned and have full private bathrooms.

The El Coral Restaurant serves a variety of traditional Peruvian dishes and guests can choose to dine outside on the terrace.

The lagoon style outdoor swimming pool is furnished with umbrellas and lounge chairs. Guests can also choose from a number of relaxing spa or massage treatments at the hotel’s wellness center.
