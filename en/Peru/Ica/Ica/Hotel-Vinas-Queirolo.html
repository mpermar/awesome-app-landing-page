Description

Featuring an outdoor pool and a restaurant, Hotel Viñas Queirolo offers accommodations in Ica.

Free WiFi access is available. The spacious and light rooms feature air conditioning, a private bathroom with free toiletries, cable TV and a minibar.

A daily American breakfast is included. At Hotel Viñas Queirolo guests will find free bike rental, a tour desk, luggage storage and laundry facilities.

The property offers free parking and can arrange wine tours. Hotel Viñas Queirolo is located 15 km from Cruz del Sur bus station and 18 km from Ica's main square and Huacachina Lake.
