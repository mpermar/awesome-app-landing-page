Description

Situated 7 km from the centre of Ica, in an area surrounded by vineyards, Hacienda San Juan Resort offers accommodation in the famous Ruta del Pisco.

Guests are provided a complimentary breakfast daily. Free WiFi and private parking are available on site.

Rooms at Hacienda San Juan Resort feature a private bathroom, a minibar, a flat-screen TV and a desk.

Bed linen and towels are included. The suite is equipped with air conditioning. Hacienda San Juan Resort boasts of an outdoor swimming pool and a hot tub.

Children will be able to play in the gardens, the children's playground, the kid's club or enjoy activities with the entertainment staff.

The property also features a restaurant and a bar, where guests can savour local flavours and cocktails.

Room service is available. There is a 24-hour front desk on site, with a tour desk and a concierge service.

The front desk offers currency exchange and a luggage storage. Additionally, guests will find shops in the property.
