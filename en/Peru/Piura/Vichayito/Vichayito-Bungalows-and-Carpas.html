Description

Located right in front of Vichayito Beach, Vichayito Bungalows offers tents and bungalows with free Wi-Fi and TV sets.

It features a spa, a restaurant and a garden with a swimming pool. Free parking is provided.

Decorated with gabled beamed roofs, bungalows at are fitted with air conditioning.

The tents are fitted with TV sets. Some of them have terraces overlooking the sea.

An American breakfast is served daily. Sea Food specialties can be ordered at El Piquero Restaurant, whereas drinks and snacks from the bar can be enjoyed in the garden.

Guests can relax by the pool or benefit from beauty treatments at the spa. They can also engage in football, volleyball and table matches, or practice kayaking.

Vichayito Bungalows is 2 hours from Manglares de Tumbes and 1. 5 hours from Tumbes Airport by car.
