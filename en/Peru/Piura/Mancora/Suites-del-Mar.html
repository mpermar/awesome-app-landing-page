Description

Suites del Mar is located in Máncora, right in front of the beach and a 10-minute walk from the commercial area.

Free Wi-Fi access is available. Rooms at Suites del Mar will provide you with a private bathroom, a flat-screen TV, a telephone, and a minibar.

Most rooms include a terrace. Some rooms include a complimentary buffet breakfast daily.

The hotel has an outdoor pool, a lounge bar and a restaurant. Room service and 24-hour security services are provided at Suites del Mar.

Guest can also take kitesurfing lessons for a surcharge. The property is 102 km from Pedro Canga Rodríguez Airport in Tumbes and 185 km from Guillermo Concha Iberico Airport in Piura.
