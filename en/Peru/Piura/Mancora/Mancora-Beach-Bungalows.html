Description

Featuring an outdoor pool and a restaurant, Mancora Beach Bungalows offers accommodations in Máncora.

Rooms at Mancora Beach Bungalows are equipped with a fan, private bathroom, cable TV and an outdoor dining area.

All rooms also have a balcony and feature pool and sea views. Free breakfast is included.

At Mancora Beach Bungalows guests will find a private beach, a games room and a tour desk.

The property offers free parking. Mancora Beach Bungalows is a 1. 5-hour car ride from Capitán FAP Pedro Canga Rodríguez Airport.
