Description

Featuring an outdoor pool and a restaurant, Serra Nova offers accommodations in Cajamarca.

Free Wi-Fi access is available. Baños del Inca main square is 200 metres away. Rooms at Hostal Serra Nova are equipped with a desk, cable TV, a minibar and a private bathroom with hot water.

All rooms have a view. Breakfast buffet is included. At Hostal Serra Nova guests will find a 24-hour front desk, free luggage storage, 24-hour room service and a garden with tables and chairs.

The property offers free parking. Hostal Serra Nova sits 50 metres from the bus station and 7 km from El Quinde shopping centre.
