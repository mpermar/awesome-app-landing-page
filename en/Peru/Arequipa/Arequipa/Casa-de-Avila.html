Description

Located just 400 metres from the Arequipa's main square, Casa de Avila offers accommodation in Arequipa, the city built in white stone by Spanish conquerors.

Casa de Avila has a central garden with sunchairs and umbrellas, where guests can enjoy the city's mild climate.

A complimentary buffet breakfast is served daily in the garden, consisting of fruit salad, natural fruit juice, local bread, cheese and butter, Andean cereals, olives and yogurt.

During their stay at Casa Avila, guests are provided with free WiFi access in all areas and free coffee.

Rooms at Casa de Avila are distributed in two floors and feature a private bathroom with unlimited hot water, a flat-screen LED TV with cable channels, a wardrobe, a desk and garden views.

Some rooms feature a terrace with outdoor furniture. Bed linen, towels, a hair dryer and an ironing kit are included.

The library, the games room, the social area and the garden are available for guest use throughout their stay at Casa de Avila.

Casa de Avila features a restaurant where guests can savour local flavours and explore local cuisine with the available On Hands Cooking Classes.

The tour desk can help guests organise activities such as museum visits, outdoor adventures, cooking classes and more.

The property offers some activities of its own, such as Spanish classes. Guests staying for 2 or more nights are entitled to a complimentary city tour.

Casa de Avila is located 300 metres from Arequipa's historic centre. Santa Catalina Monastery is a 5-minute drive from the property, while the Museum of Andean Sanctuaries is an 8-minute walk away.
