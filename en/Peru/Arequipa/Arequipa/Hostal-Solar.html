Description

Set in a colonial style building with a garden and a terrace, Solar de Arequipa offers rooms with private balconies and free Wi-Fi.

It is located 200 metres from Santa Catalina Convent. Rooms at Hostal Solar de Arequipa are decorated in lively colours.

They feature balconies and large windows, making them very bright during the whole daylight.

A buffet breakfast is daily served. Guest can unwind in the garden or on the rooftop terrace, which features a shaded area and BBQ facilities.

Airport shuttles can be arranged by the 24-hour front desk. Rodriguez Ballon Airport is a 20-minute drive away.
