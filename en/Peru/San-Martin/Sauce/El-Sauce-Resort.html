Description

Situated on the peaceful Azul Lakeshore, El Sauce features rustic chalets surrounded by colourful tropical gardens.

It is activity packed, offering 2 pools and water sports. Guests at El Sauce Resort can horseback ride or hike to Sauce´s picturesque waterfalls.

They can also kayak, jet ski or water ski on Azul Lake, and unwind afterwards with a relaxing massage.

Rooms and chalets at El Sauce have rustic bamboo exteriors and traditional stone interiors, decorated with tropical art pieces.

All have cable TV, a fan, and a furnished patio with a hammock. An American breakfast with fresh fruits, natural juices and pastries is served daily.

For dining, the centre of Sauce is 5-minutes drive from the resort, and offers lively restaurants, bars and nightlife options.

El Sauce Resort is located 2 hours away from Tarapoto city. Free parking is available.
