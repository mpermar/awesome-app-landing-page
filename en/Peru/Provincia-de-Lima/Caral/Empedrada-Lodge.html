Description

Set within the green valley of Caral, Empedrada Lodge offers 22 rooms with panoramic views of Caral Valley.

The property also features a restaurant and bar with a large terrace and an outdoor swimming pool.

Private parking is provided on site. Rooms at Empedrada Lodge are bright and spacious and feature gabled roofs and tiled floors.

All rooms are fitted with a private bathroom and a terrace offering views of the valley.

Guests at Empedrada Lodge can enjoy themselves by the swimming pool and garden, or at the property's shared lounge.

Guests can request to take nature walks in the farm, especially through the avocado and tangerine plantations.

A 24-hour front desk is provided, with a tour desk and 24-hour security on site. Empedrada Lodge is 200 km from Jorge Chavez Airport in Lima, while Caral is only a 15-minute drive away.

Bandurria is a 50-minute drive away and Caleta Vidal Beach is a 40-minute drive away from Empedrada Lodge.
