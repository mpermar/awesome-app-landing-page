Description

Featuring an outdoor pool and a restaurant, Hotel Villa Kitzia Huacho offers accommodations in Lima.

Free WiFi access is available. Each accommodation here features a seating area, a fan, private bathroom and a flat-screen TV.

A daily breakfast is included. At Hotel Villa Kitzia Huacho guests will find a fitness centre and free bike rental.

Other facilities offered at the property include meeting facilities, a games room and a tour desk.
