Description

Offering an outdoor pool and an a la carte restaurant, Casa Verde Hotel Ecologico is located in San Vicente de Cañete, 5 minutes from Mala town by car.

Guests are served a complimentary Continental breakfast daily. Rooms here will provide you with a terrace and a seating area.

Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with towels and linen. At Casa Verde Hotel Ecologico you will find a 24-hour front desk, a large garden with flowers and fruit trees and a terrace.

Other facilities offered include a shared lounge and a children's playground. Casa Verde uses sun energy in stead of electricity.

The staff organises bonfires on Saturdays. The hotel is a 20-minute drive from Calango town and a 30-minute drive from Azpitia town.
