Description

Located at the financial heart of San Isidro, within walking distance from the main bus terminals, Daniel’s Apart Hotel offers its guests cosy rooms and spacious apartments including free WiFi and local calls.

Daniel's Apart Hotel has superior rooms with minibars and cable TV, as well as suites featuring a separate living, dining room, bedroom, and fully equipped kitchen.

Blue Café at Daniel’s serves a buffet breakfast every morning with delicious and various treats to choose from.
