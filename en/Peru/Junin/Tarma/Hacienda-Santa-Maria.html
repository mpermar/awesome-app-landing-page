Description

Located in Tarma, Hacienda Santa María is a colonial farm house surrounded by flowers and aromatic herb plantations.

Each room here boasts a private bathroom and a balcony with a magnificent countryside view.

The city's main square is 20 blocks away. Hacienda Santa María features a historical dining area and a restaurant which serves international and local dishes made with ingredients grown in the property.

A daily breakfast with eggs, fruits, jams, milk and coffee is included. The property features a bonfire or chimney room, a tranquil garden with hammocks and a vegetable garden.

Guests can enjoy a variety of activities onsite and in the surrounding area. At Hacienda Santa María guests will find a 24-hour front desk, meeting facilities and a games room.

The property offers free parking and free WiFi access throughout. The city's main square is a 5-minute car ride away.
