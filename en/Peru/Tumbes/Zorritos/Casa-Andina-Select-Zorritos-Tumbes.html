Description

Located near the beach in Zorritos, Casa Andina Select Zorritos Tumbes offers 75 rooms with free WiFi is available throughout the property.

This property features a large outdoor swimming pool. The rooms in Casa Andina Select Zorritos Tumbes feature a private bathroom, air-conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a minibar and a safety-deposit box.

Free private parking is possible on site subject to availability. Casa Andina Select Zorritos Tumbes has a restaurant, a café and a swimming pool bar, where guests can enjoy a meal or a drink.

Room service is provided. Additionally, this property features a sauna, a fitness centre and a games room.

Casa Andina Select Zorritos Tumbes has an outdoor bonfire for guests to enjoy during the cold nights.
