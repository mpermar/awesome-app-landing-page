Description

Featuring an outdoor pool and a restaurant, Punta Sal Suites & Bungalows Resort offers beach front accommodations in Tumbes.

Free WiFi access is available. Each room here features air conditioning, a private bathroom, satellite TV and a minibar.

Some rooms also have a seating area. Breakfast is included. At Punta Sal Suites & Bungalows Resort guests will find a tennis court and a fitness centre.

Other facilities offered at the property include a games room, a tour desk and luggage storage.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including mini golf.
