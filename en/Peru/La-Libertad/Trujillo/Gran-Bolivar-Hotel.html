Description

Tastefully decorated rooms with free Wi-Fi and gym facilities can be enjoyed just 4 blocks from Trujillo’s main square.

Pizarro Walk is 100 metres away and Carlos Martinez de Pinillos Airport is a 15-minute drive away.

Gran Bolivar Hotel has bright rooms styled in cream-coloured shades. They have air conditioning, flat-screen cable TV and work desks.

Private bathrooms include toiletries. A full American breakfast with breads, eggs and coffee is served daily at Gran Bolivar.
