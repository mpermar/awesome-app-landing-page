Description

Offering an a la carte restaurant, Hotel El Mirador is located in Pacasmayo. Guests can enjoy free WiFi access and a playground area at this hotel.

An optional breakfast is served daily for an extra fee. Rooms here will provide you with a flat-screen TV and satellite channels.

The private bathrooms have a shower. Bed linens, towels, and a fan are provided. Some rooms have a sea view.

At Hotel El Mirador you will find a 24-hour front desk, a garden and a terrace. There is also a shared lounge and luggage storage.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including darts, billiards and table tennis.

The property offers free parking. Hotel El Mirador is a 3 minute walk to Malecon Grau Beach.

The main square of Pacasmayo is a 5 minute walk away. The hotel is also a 5 minute walk to the Pacasmayo bus station.
