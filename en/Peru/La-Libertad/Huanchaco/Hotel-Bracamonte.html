Description

Featuring a swimming pool and a restaurant, Hotel Bracamonte offers rooms with free WiFi and private balconies 50 metres from Huanchaco Beach.

Trujillo’s historic centre is 13 km away. With private balconies offering pool and garden views, rooms at Bracamonte Hotel are very bright.

They feature spacious seating areas, a safety deposit box and cable TV. A complimentary breakfast is served daily.

Free private parking is provided. At the on-site restaurant, guests can order Peruvian and international specialties.

Guests can relax on the sun loungers by the pool as their children enjoy themselves in the playground.

A games room is also featured. Hotel Bracamonte is 10 km from Aventura Plaza Mall and 8 km from Chan Chan Archeological Centre.
