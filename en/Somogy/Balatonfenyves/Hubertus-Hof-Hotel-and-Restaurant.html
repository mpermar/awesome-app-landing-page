Description

All rooms at Hubertus-Hof Hotel & Restaurant feature a minibar, a TV, and a bathroom with hairdryer.

Hungarian and international gourmet cuisine, including game dishes, is served in the restaurant. Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas.

Spa facilities include a Finnish sauna, a steam bath, and massage and fitness rooms. Guests can play soccer and ping-pong on site.

For hunters, the Hubertus-Hof offers a private hunting preserve and a gun storage cabinet in every room. Secure private parking is available on site free of charge.
