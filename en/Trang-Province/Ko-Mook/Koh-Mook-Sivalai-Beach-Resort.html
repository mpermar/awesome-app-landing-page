Description

Each villa at Koh Mook Sivalai Beach Resort has air conditioning. All also include satellite TV and free bathroom amenities. Some villas offer outdoor living space.

Fresh seafood, Thai and European specialties are served at Koh Mook Sivalai’s restaurant. Cocktails and a selection of beer are also available.

Guests can relax on a lounge chair on the pool deck or enjoy sunset views from Hao-Laem Beach. Traditional Thai massages and a variety of water sports can be arranged by resort staff.

The train station and airport are a 70 minute drive away.
