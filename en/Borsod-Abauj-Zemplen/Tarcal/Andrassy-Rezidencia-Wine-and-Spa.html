Description

Andrássy Rezidencia’s spacious spa area features a sauna, a steam bath and a massage area. Various beauty and body treatments, as well as vinotherapy, are offered.

The Andrássy Rezidencia Wine & Spa’s restaurant serves international and local cuisine, as well as wine specialties. The generous bar is in the hotel’s atrium. Guests can relax with a drink in front of the open fireplace in the lobby.

Each lavish room at the Rezidencia Wine & Spa is air-conditioned and has a flat-screen cable TV, free access to wired internet, a sitting area and a minibar. All spacious bathrooms have bathrobes, slippers and a hairdryer.

Andrássy Rezidencia Wine & Spa has free private parking on site.
