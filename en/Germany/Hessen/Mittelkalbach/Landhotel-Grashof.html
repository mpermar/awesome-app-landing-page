Description

This family-run hotel is set in beautiful Rhön countryside, a 5-minute drive from the village of Kalbach.

Landhotel Grashof offers a spa area with 3 different saunas, a gym, beauty treatments and massages.

The spacious rooms at the 3-star Superior hotel are brightly decorated in a cottage style.

All rooms include free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen satellite TV, a balcony and a private bathroom.

Traditional German country cuisine and theme buffets are served in the hotel restaurant.

Guests are also welcome to relax at the rustic-style bar with wooden furnishings or in the beer garden.

Landhotel Grashof is an ideal base for hiking and cycling, and 20 km from the nearest ski region.

Electric bikes can be hired at reception. On-site parking is free, and the A66 motorway is 5 minutes away.
