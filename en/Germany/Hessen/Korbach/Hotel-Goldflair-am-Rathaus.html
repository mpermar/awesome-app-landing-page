Description

Set in the scenic old quarter of Korbach, directly opposite from the town hall, this historic hotel offers an original design and a traditional-style restaurant with beer garden.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. Each of the rooms at the family-run Hotel Goldflair am Rathaus offers cable TV and a spacious bathroom.

Some have a balcony. A variety of theme rooms are also available. A regional breakfast buffet as well as creative meals are served in the hotel restaurant.

Guests are welcome to try the Goldflair’s unique sparkling wine with real gold. Free parking spaces are available outside the Hotel Goldflair am Rathaus.
