Description

Offering great views of the Wasserkuppe Mountain, a modern spa with heated indoor pool, and varied leisure facilities, this hotel lies in Dipperz-Friesenhausen in the Hessische Rhön Nature Park.

Surrounded by a park with lake, the Hotel Rhön Residence has spacious rooms with satellite TV and a private balcony or terrace.

Guests are welcome to use the Residence’s wellness area with sauna and beauty salon.

2 bowling alleys and a minigolf course are available at the Rhön Residence. The Rhöner Markt Bistro restaurant serves traditional food from the Hesse region.

The Rhön Treff bar invites guests to relax with a drink. The historic city of Fulda is only 13 km from the Hotel Rhön Residence.
