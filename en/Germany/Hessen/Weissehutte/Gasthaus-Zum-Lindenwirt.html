Description

Offering a restaurant, adjoining beer garden and a quiet meadow, Gasthaus Zum Lindenwirt is located in Weißehütte.

Guests can rent bikes and e-bikes from the property to discover the surrounding countryside.

Featuring a shower, the private bathroom is each includes a hairdryer and free toiletries.

At Gasthaus Zum Lindenwirt you will find a communal sauna area, a garden and a terrace.

There is also a children's playground for kids to enjoy. The guest house is located directly on the Radweg R1 - a scenic cycling and hiking trail.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including fishing and canoeing.

Guests can also use the next-door Freibad Gieselwerder & Hallenbad Oedelsheim free of charge.

Bodenfelde Train Station is a 10-minute drive away. The property offers free parking and free bicycle storage.
