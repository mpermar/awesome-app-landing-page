Description

Set in Hofheim am Taunus, just a 30-minute drive from Frankfurt am Main, Vital Hotel Frankfurt boasts a spa centre, a hot tub, an indoor and an outdoor pool.

The property features an on-site bar. Every room is equipped with a flat-screen TV.

All rooms offer a private bathroom, bath robes and slippers. Vital Hotel Frankfurt features free WiFi throughout the property.

A TV with satellite channels is provided. The restaurant serves seasonal meals and a panorama bar and snack bar are featured.

A terrace, as well as a 24-hour front desk, is available at the property. Various activities in the surrounding areas, including golfing, hiking and cycling are popular among guests.

The hotel also offers bike hire. Wiesbaden is 25 km from Vital Hotel Frankfurt, while Mainz is 30 km away.

Frankfurt Airport is a 15-minute drive from the property. Private parking is available on site (for a fee).
