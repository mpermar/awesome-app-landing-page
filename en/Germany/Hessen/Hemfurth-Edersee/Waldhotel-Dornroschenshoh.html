Description

Close to Edersee Lake beach and Sailing School, this hotel is located between Edertal-Hemfurth and Bringhausen.

The hotel features a communal sauna and a terrace offering garden views. Each room has a balcony and a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

The private bathrooms come with a shower. Free WiFi is available throughout. A rich breakfast is served each day and guests can enjoy the delicious range of dishes served at the in-house restaurant.

There are plenty of activities to enjoy in the surrounding area, including water-sports, fishing and hiking.

Waldecker Bergbahn cable car is 6 km from the property. Free parking is available at Waldhotel Dornröschenshöh, and Paderborn-Lippstadt Airport is 68 km away.
