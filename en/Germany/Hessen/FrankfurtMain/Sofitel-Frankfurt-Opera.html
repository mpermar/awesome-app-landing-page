Description

The 5-Star Superior Sofitel Frankfurt Opera is in a prime location at the landmark Opera Square.

Goethestrasse, the famous shopping and entertainment area, is a short walk away. The spacious rooms feature designer furnishings and free Wi-Fi.

The rooms also offer a shower or bathtub, luxury brand toiletries, espresso machine and a kettle.

Guests can also enjoy air-conditioning, mini bar, a Bose sound system and private safe.

The luxurious suites feature exclusive designer furnishings and scenic views of Frankfurt city centre.

The suites also come with a Japanese toilet and a Skinjay™ shower system. In the stylish SoSpa guests are pampered in four treatment rooms and relaxation areas.

Guests are also able to enjoy the fitness and gym facilities, including the 4,000m² Fitness First Black Label fitness studio (for an additional charge).

An indoor swimming pool is also available. Sofitel Frankfurt Opera is home to the Bistronomique-concept Schönemann restaurant, which offers delicate and casual fare.

A private dining room is available and guests can also enjoy the outdoors and scenic views by dining on the terrace At Lili’s Bar, guests can enjoy the selection of cocktails or light meals from the restaurant.

An in-house patisserie also prepares snacks in authentic Parisian style. The Sofitel Frankfurt Opera is located within a short walking distance of the Goethe Haus, the German Architecture Museum and the lively Hauptwache area.
