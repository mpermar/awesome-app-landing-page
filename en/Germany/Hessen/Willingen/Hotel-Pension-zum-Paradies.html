Description

Located in the Stryck-Tal valley in Willingen, this traditional hotel offers country-style rooms with breakfast included.

It lies just 400 metres from Mühlenkopf, home of the Ski Jumping World Cup. The non-smoking rooms of the Hotel-Pension zum Paradies come with satellite TV and a seating area.

The cosy restaurant serves a rich breakfast buffet each morning, which is included in your room rate.

Willingen town centre is just 2 kilometres away. The surrounding Diemelsee nature park is ideal for hiking, cycling and skiing.
