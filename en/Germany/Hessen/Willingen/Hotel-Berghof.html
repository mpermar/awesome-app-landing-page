Description

The 3-star Hotel Berghof offers a wide range of meals in its Leggenskoster and Henriettenstube restaurants. A terrace is available when the weather's nice.

Guests have free use of the Berghof's large spa with indoor pool, jacuzzi, sauna and steam bath. Rental bikes are also available.

The hotel's playground, play room and petting zoo are popular with children. A children's menu and baby chairs are available in the restaurant.

Hotel Berghof offers a shuttle service to many hiking trails within the Diemelsee nature park. A ski school can be found just 1,000 feet away.

Usseln is a great choice for travelers interested in scenery, winter sports and nature walks.
