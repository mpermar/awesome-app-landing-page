Description

This hotel is located opposite the Kleiner Tiergarten park and just Turmstraße underground stop, which offers connections to the famous Kurfürstendamm shopping area and the Brandenburg Gate.

Hotel Tiergarten Berlin is a privately run hotel offering spacious, comfortable rooms with modern amenities.

Get your day off to a good start with the excellent, comprehensive breakfast buffet.

For dinner, a range of restaurants can be found in the hotel’s vicinity. Make the most of the excellent public transport connections.

Kurfürstendamm shopping area is 3 minutes away by underground. Unter den Linden, with the Brandenburger Tor, museum island and Friedrichstraße shopping boulevard, is a 15-minute bus ride away.
