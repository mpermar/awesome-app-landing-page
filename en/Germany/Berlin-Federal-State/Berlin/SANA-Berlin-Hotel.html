Description

Just 700 m from the KaDeWe department store on Berlin’s Kurfürstendamm, this stylish hotel offers a free spa with pool, soundproofed rooms, and dining in the Lobby Lounge, serving snacks and light meals throughout the day.

The 4-star-superior SANA Berlin Hotel has modern, elegant rooms and self-catering apartments.

They feature wooden floors, black-out curtains, and a laptop safe. Guests can work out in SANA’s gym, or relax in the Finnish sauna or steam room for free.

A range of massages can also be booked for an additional fee. The F8-Bar and the lobby bar offer snacks and drinks.

In warm weather, guests can enjoy drinks on the spacious terrace with sofas. Berlin Zoo and the Kaiser Wilhelm Memorial Church are a 10-minute walk from the Hotel SANA Berlin.

Augsburger Straße Underground Station is a 2-minute walk away. The hotel also offers bicycles and cars for hire.
