Description

This 3-star hotel is located in Berlin's southern district of Steglitz. Hotel Morgenland is a 5-minute walk from the Bundesarchiv Archives and a 10-minute drive from the Botanical Garden and Schlossstrasse shopping mile.

All rooms at Hotel Morgenland combine historic charm and modern design. WiFi is available in all room free of charge.

A rich breakfast buffet is served in the Morgenland each morning. Nearby attractions include Dahlem's Allied Museum and Brücke Museum.

The Hotel Morgenland also features a small chapel where guests can relax. A beauty salon and massages are also available on site.

Guests can reserve a parking space at the Morgenland's private car park throughout your stay.
