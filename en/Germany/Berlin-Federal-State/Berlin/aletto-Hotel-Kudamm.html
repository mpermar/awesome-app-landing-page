Description

A lounge bar and a roof terrace with panoramic views of Berlin are offered at this hotel, a 5-minute walk from the Ku’damm shopping street.

Zoologischer Garten Station is 200 metres away. aletto Hotel Kudamm provides modern rooms with air conditioning.

Each floor is decorated with photos of a particular Berlin attraction. A flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with heated floors are included in all rooms.

Free Wi-Fi is available in all areas of the hotel. A full breakfast buffet is served each morning.

Snacks and drinks can be enjoyed in the bar, in the courtyard garden or on the roof terrace.

Rental bicycles are available at the aletto Hotel Berlin. Guests are welcome to use the billiards and table tennis facilities.

There is also a children’s playground on site, as well as a football cage directly outside.

Reception at the aletto Hotel Kudamm is open 24/7, and private parking can be arranged.

Train, underground and bus connections are available from Zoologischer Garten Station.

The Messe Berlin exhibition grounds are a 10-minute underground or train journey away.
