Description

Each unit has a dishwasher, oven, a coffee machine, as well as a microwave and electric tea pot. Some units feature a seating area and/or a balcony.

A continental breakfast is available every morning at Badstraße Apartments. At Badstraße Apartments you'll find a restaurant serving European cuisine. Vegan and Gluten-free options can also be requested.

Guests can relax in the garden at the property.

Museum Island is 2.4 miles from Badstraße Apartments, while The Brandenburg Gate is 2.5 miles away. The nearest airport is Tegel Airport, 3.7 miles from Badstraße Apartments.

Mitte is a great choice for travelers interested in history, museums and architecture.
