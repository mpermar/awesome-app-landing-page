Description

This wellness hotel in Weiskirchen offers direct access to the large Vitalis spa with beauty farm, fine international food, and a scenic location in the Saar Hunsrück nature park.

The elegant Parkhotel Weiskirchen has classical-style rooms with satellite TV, a range of toiletries, and WiFi access.

The neighbouring Vitalis spa features swimming pools and a 1,500 m² sauna area. Guests can also relax in the Bel Etage beauty centre or in the Bistro Vitalis beach bar.

The Parkhotel Weiskirchen's Vier Jahreszeiten restaurant serves creative, gourmet meals.

Snacks and meals from the Hunsrück region are available in the Brasserie restaurant with terrace.

Leisure activities near the Parkhotel include cycling, hiking and Nordic walking.
