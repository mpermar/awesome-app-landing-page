Description

Peacefully located in Saarbrücken, the self-catering Ferienwohnung Salut is situated just 500 metres from the Saar River and 6 km from the city centre.

Free Wi-Fi is provided throughout the property. The warmly decorated apartment includes a living area with Sky TV, sofa bed and DVD player.

There is also a separate bedroom, 2 bathrooms and a fully equipped kitchen. Guests can park free of charge.
