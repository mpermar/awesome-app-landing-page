Description

The air-conditioned rooms at the Holiday Inn Express Bremen Airport include tea/coffee facilities, satellite TV and a private bathroom. Modern apartments with a private kitchenette are also available.

A extensive breakfast buffet is available each morning at the Holiday Inn. In the evenings, the Success Bar, equipped with free Nintendo Wii consoles, serves delicious cocktails and German beers.

Mike's Urban Pub offers a selection of various international dishes.

Guests are welcome to use the free internet terminals in the Holiday Inn’s 24-hour business corner. Or they can book a conference room for up to 30 people.

A tram stop can be found 650 feet away from the Holiday Inn, providing a link to the medieval Schnoor Quarter in 15 minutes.

Neustadt is a great choice for travelers interested in restaurants, Christmas markets and architecture.
