Description

Offering a sauna, a games room, and WiFi access, Jugendherberge Bremerhaven is located in Bremerhaven.

Klimahaus Bremerhaven is located 4. 6 km from the hostel. These classically styled rooms include carpeted floors and wooden furnishings.

Rooms are fitted with a sink and private toilet, with access to shared bathroom facilities.

Some rooms include a private bathroom. A hearty buffet breakfast is served every morning, and in fine weather guests are invited to use the BBQ facilities on the terrace.

A selection of restaurants can be found within a 10-minute walk. Jugendherberge Bremerhaven provides a variety of activities on site, including billiards, foosball, and table tennis.

Bicycle rentals can be arranged. Bremerhaven Train Station is 5 km from the hostel and the A27 motorway is a 3-minute drive.

Free private parking is available. Guests can enjoy free use of the public transport in Bremerhaven and free access to the indoor swimming pools.
