Description

Situated in quiet Malterdingen, Ferienwohnung Feist offers free WiFi access and a garden.

The apartment is fully furnished in a home-like décor. Riegel-Malterdingen Train Station is just 2 km from the property.

In addition, Ferienwohnung Feist features a flat-screen TV, a seating area with 2 sofa beds, as well as 2 showers and 2 toilets.

There is a fully-equipped kitchen with a dishwasher and a microwave. Other facilities include another TV with Blu-ray player and a selection of DVDs, as well as a child's high-chair.

Bed linen, towels, coffee, tea, mineral water, spices, vinegar and oil are provided for free.

The nearest supermarket is 300 metres from the apartment, and guests are welcome to use the on-site barbecue facilities.

A selection of restaurants can be found within a 5-minute walking distance of the accommodation.

Popular activities in the surrounding area include hiking and cycling. Guests can swim in the nearby lake, 1 km from the apartment.

Rebstock Malterdingen bus stop is directly outside the property, and free public parking can be found nearby.
