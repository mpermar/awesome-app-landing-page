Description

Boasting attractive leisure facilities, this family-run 3-star superior hotel in Wangen provides a cosy base for outings to Lake Constance, the Allgäu Alps, as well as Switzerland and Austria.

Free Wi-Fi is available in the entire hotel. The Hotel Landgasthof Mohren welcomes you to its peaceful, well-equipped rooms.

Take a dip in the hotel's indoor pool, relax in the modern sauna, and practise your drive on the 3 tennis courts.

Horse riding, golf, hiking, and skiing are only some of the activities you can enjoy in the Landgasthof Mohren’s idyllic surroundings.

After an active day outdoors, look forward to the country-style restaurant’s delicious Allgäu specialities.
