Description

Featuring a heated pool with sauna and a terrace with panoramic views of Mount Feldberg, this traditional hotel in St.

Märgen offers a quiet location in the Black Forest. A hearty breakfast buffet and cuisine from the Baden region are served in the Löwen’s restaurant.

Lactose- and gluten-free dishes are available on request. The classically furnished rooms at Hotel Löwen feature a seating area with satellite TV and desk.

All rooms have a private bathroom, and some offer views of the Glotter Valley. Guests can relax in the garden, which has a large sunbathing lawn and a children’s play area.

During the winter months Hotel Löwen offers direct access to the surrounding cross-country skiing trails, and the Thurnerspur ski run is 8km away.

The pretty town of Freiburg with its historic architecture is just a 30-minute drive away.
