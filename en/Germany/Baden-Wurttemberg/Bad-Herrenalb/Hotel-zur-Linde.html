Description

The family-run Zur Linde in Althof is located on a hill overlooking the Black Forest nature park.

It offers quiet rooms, free Wi-Fi and a large breakfast buffet each morning. The rooms at the Zur Linde have a private bathroom, modern furniture and a balcony.

Zur Linde provides a German-style breakfast buffet each morning. Guests can also try local and international food in the traditional hotel restaurant.

Guests can relax on the outdoor terrace and enjoy free use of the indoor swimming pool.

A tour desk and children's playground is also on site. The French border is just 40 km away from the Zur Linde.
