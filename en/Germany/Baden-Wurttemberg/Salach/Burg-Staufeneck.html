Description

Set within a historic castle, this 5-star-superior hotel offers luxurious accommodation and award-winning cuisine in Salach, a peaceful town in the Swabian Alb region, 50 km south-east of Stuttgart.

Guests can also enjoy a heated, panoramic outdoor pool and a modern fitness room.

Here at the Burg Staufeneck, you can expect elegantly furnished, spacious rooms with top-class amenities.

The hotel’s stylish wellness area features a sauna, several baths, massage showers, a grotto, and a state-of-the-art fitness room.

You can also choose among a wide range of massages and cosmetic treatments. Admire wonderful views of the Fils river valley as you enjoy a delicious breakfast buffet.

The Burg Staufeneck’s gourmet restaurant will spoil you with delicious cuisine and fine wines.
