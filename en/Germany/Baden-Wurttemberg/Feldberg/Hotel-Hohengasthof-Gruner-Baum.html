Description

Situated in the village of Neuglashütten in the Feldberg region, this hotel is situated in the picturesque countryside of the Black Forest nature park, perfect for walking and relaxing.

Free WiFi is provided. The 3-star superior Hotel Höhengasthof Grüner Baum offers quaint, cosy rooms decorated in a country-house style boasting wonderful views, as well as either a terrace or a balcony.

Guests can enjoy the Grüner Baum’s breakfast buffet in the Kachelofenstüble restaurant, which features a tiled stove.

Baden specialities are served in the evenings. The hotel features a children's playground.

There is a sauna, solarium and an infrared cabin, and Hotel Grüner Baum also offers a massage service.

This scenic region can be explored on foot or by bike. The free ski bus leaves from right outside the hotel taking you to 26 ski lifts.
