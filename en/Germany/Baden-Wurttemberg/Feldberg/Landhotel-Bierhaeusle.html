Description

Just 6 km from both Lake Titisee and Lake Schluchsee, this friendly and international hotel in Feldberg offers free WiFi, a garden with children’s playground and a restaurant serving homemade international and traditional specialities.

Country-style rooms and apartments with solid wooden furniture are offered at Landhotel Bierhauesle.

Each has a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom with a hairdryer, and some have a balcony.

A rich breakfast buffet is served at the Lemon Tree Room, while homemade regional dishes and South Pacific cuisine can also be enjoyed at the Schwarzwalder restaurant.

In warm weather, guests can enjoy German beers as well as local and international wines on the pretty garden terrace.

Altglashütten-Falkau Train Station is a 10-minute walk away, providing connections to other areas of the Black Forest.
