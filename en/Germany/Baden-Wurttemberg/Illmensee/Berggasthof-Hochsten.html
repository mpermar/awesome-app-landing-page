Description

Offering a spa, Berggasthof Höchsten is located in Illmensee. The hotel has a barbecue and ski storage space, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Free WiFi is featured and free private parking is available on site. All rooms feature a TV and boasts views of the Bodensee Lake and Alps.

Room service is available upon request. Guests can enjoy the restaurant with its terrace offering regional and seasonal dishes.

The property has a own shop with regional products and small souvenirs. This area is well known for hiking, cycling and swimming.

Bodensee Lake is in a 30-minute drive, Konstanz is 25 km from Berggasthof Höchsten, while Bregenz is 44 km away.

The nearest airport is Friedrichshafen Airport, 25 km from Berggasthof Höchsten. Friedrichshafen Fairgrounds is in a 30-minute drive.
