Description

This hotel is located in the Black Forest town of Ottenhöfen, opposite the Kurpark spa gardens and 200 metres from the Achertalbahn Train Station.

Rooms feature free Wi-Fi and large balconies. Hotel Pflug’s country-style rooms include flat-screen TVs and elegant wooden furnishings.

Some rooms have a balcony with views of the surrounding hills. Guests enjoy free phone calls within Germany.

Black Forest specialities are on offer in the Mark Twain Stube, and restaurants can be found within 10 minutes' walk of the property.

In the evenings, guests can relax in the cosy bar, in the lounge with fireplace, or in the sauna.

Achertalbahn Train Station offers a direct link to the Rheintalbahn (Rhine Valley Railway).

This connects you to the cities of Heidelberg, Karlsruhe, Baden-Baden, Freiburg and Basel.

The Europa-Park theme park in Rust and the city of Strasbourg are 45 minutes away by car.
