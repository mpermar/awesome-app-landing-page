Description

Boasting an idyllic and peaceful location in the heart of the Black Forest, this traditional hotel awaits you with fine cuisine, a spacious spa area with pool, and a beauty centre.

The Naturparkhotel Adler in Wolfach is a wonderful base for hikes and cycle trips through unspoiled scenery.

Sports fans can also make use of the hotel’s tennis courts and table tennis facilities.

A weekly sport program is also offered. Families with children will appreciate the hotel’s large garden with sunbathing area and the game reserve.
