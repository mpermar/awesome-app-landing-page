Description

The family-friendly Haus Insel Riechenau is set on the south bank of the island of Reichenau, directly on Lake Constance.

It offers free parking and free WiFi in all areas. Reichenau Train Station is 5. 8 km away.

The attractive rooms at Haus Insel Reichenau feature parquet floors and a modern bathroom.

Some rooms also have a terrace. No rooms have a TV to ensure a relaxing atmosphere for guests.

Meals made with local ingredients are served in the dining room at Haus Insel Reichenau, including vegetarian options.

Self-service hot and cold drinks are also available. Younger guests will enjoy the play area at Haus Insel Reichenau, with its panoramic lake views.

The hotel also offers a bowling alley (for an additional charge), outdoor chess, table tennis, a TV room with library, as well as an in-house chapel.

Nearby are many restaurants, leisure attractions, bicycle trails and wine-tasting locations.
