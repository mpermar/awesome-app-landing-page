Description

This cosy hotel enjoys a central but quiet location, just 300 metres from the Europa-Park theme park in Rust.

Hotel Im Ziegelweg offers free Wi-Fi and free parking. The modern rooms at Hotel Im Ziegelweg garni feature Mediterranean décor and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

The breakfast room is open 24/7, and guests can make use of an electric kettle to make tea, coffee or hot chocolate.

Drinks can be enjoyed in the lounge or on the garden terrace. The hotel is an ideal base for cycling or day trips to Freiburg.
