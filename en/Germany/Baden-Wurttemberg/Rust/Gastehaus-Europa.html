Description

This hotel enjoys a quiet location in Rust, just a 10-minute walk from the Europa-Park Theme Park.

It offers brightly decorated rooms, free parking and free Wi-Fi in public areas. Colourful rooms with satellite TV and a private terrace are offered at Gästehaus Europa.

Each has an en suite bathroom with shower. Coffee, tea and mineral water are provided in each room, and guests can also use the shared refrigerator at the guest house.

Breakfast options are available in a nearby bakery and on the way to the Europa-Park.

Gästehaus Europa is an ideal base for hiking and cycling tours in the Black Forest, just 13 km away.
