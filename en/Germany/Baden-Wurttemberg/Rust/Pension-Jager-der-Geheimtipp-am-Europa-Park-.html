Description

This family-friendly guest house offers brightly furnished rooms and apartments with free WiFi and free parking in the centre of Rust.

It is just 500 metres from the main entrance of the Europa-Park theme park. The rooms provided by the Gästehaus Jäger include satellite TV, air-conditioning and a seating area.

The spacious apartments feature a kitchenette and dining area. Most have a small conservatory.

Each morning, a rich breakfast buffet is served in the conservatory. Guests are welcome to relax here throughout the day and enjoy dinner at the pizza restaurant located across from the property.
