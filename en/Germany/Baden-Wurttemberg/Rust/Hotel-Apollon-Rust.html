Description

This family-friendly hotel in Rust offers free parking and free WiFi throughout. It lies in a quiet side street, 5 minutes from the A5 motorway and a 10-minute walk from Europa-Park.

A free shuttle service to Europapark theme park is provided. The non-smoking Hotel Apollon Rust provides bright, soundproofed rooms with a flat-screen cable TV and a safe.

All rooms include a refrigerator and adjustable air conditioning. A varied breakfast buffet is provided each morning.

Live music and entertainment can be enjoyed in the evenings in the Europapark Hotel Resorts, 2 minutes' walk away.

Open during the main season, the restaurant serves regional cuisine and a fine assortment of wines and beers as well as rare whiskies from the hotel's private collection.

In the restaurant is also a small games corner for children. Various shopping opportunities can be found within 5 minutes' walk.
