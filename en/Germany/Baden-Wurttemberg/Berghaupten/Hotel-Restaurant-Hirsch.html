Description

This modern, family-run hotel in the Black Forest village of Berghaupten offers free WiFi and gourmet food.

The Gengenbach train station is a 5-minute drive away. The Hotel-Restaurant Hirsch provides quiet rooms with a safe, private bathrooms and free internet.

A varied breakfast buffet is provided each morning. The Restaurant Hirsch with garden terrace offers fine regional cuisine and wines from the Ortenau region.

The Hirsch was given 2 recommendations in the 2019 edition of the Michelin restaurant guide.
