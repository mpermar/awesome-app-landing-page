Description

These family-friendly, 3-star rooms and apartments in Aulendorf offer an adventure playground, free breakfast, and free parking.

The Steeger See lake is a 10-minute drive away. The Hofgut Tiergarten holiday complex offers a petting zoo, large garden with sunbathing area, and a pond.

All rooms and apartments are spacious and well-equipped. The daily rate includes a big breakfast buffet, served in the Kaffeehaus bistro.

Free Wi-Fi is provided in the Tiergarten's public areas. A variety of board games, books, and toys are available in the Spielescheune house.

Hotel Hofgut Tiergarten is an ideal base for hiking and outdoor sports in the Upper Swabian countryside.
