Description

The rooms at the Hotel Traube am See include satellite TV, a modern bathroom and free broadband internet. A breakfast buffet is also included in the room rate.

The Hotel Traube am See's restaurant features local and international food. The hotel bar serves a variety of drinks, from cocktails to draft beers.

Guests are welcome to use the Traube am See's spa area, which includes an indoor pool, saunas and steam rooms.

Free parking spaces are available in the Traube am See's underground garage.
