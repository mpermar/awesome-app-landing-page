Description

This privately-run, 4-star hotel offers a modern spa with massage service, Wi-Fi internet, and a garden terrace with great views of Ulm.

It lies in the quiet Reutti district of Neu-Ulm. The family-run Meinl Hotel & Restaurant has brightly decorated rooms offering a flat-screen TV with free Sky channels, a seating area, and soundproofed windows.

A free bathrobe is provided and many rooms have been renovated since May 2015. The Meinl Hotel’s spacious spa area includes a Finnish sauna and a solarium, and beauty treatments and massages can be booked in advance.

Rental bicycles and a children’s playground are also available. The Meinl’s à la carte restaurant serves regional and international dishes, and beers and wines are available at the bar.

Guests are welcome to dine in the conservatory, or outside on the terrace in good weather.

Drinks are available at the well-stocked hotel bar. Ulm city centre is 8 km from the Meinl Hotel.

The Legoland Park theme park in Günzburg is 30 km away. Free parking is provided.
