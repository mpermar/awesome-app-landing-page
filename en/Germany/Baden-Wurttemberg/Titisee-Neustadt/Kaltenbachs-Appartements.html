Description

Located in the Black Forest town of Titisee, the family run Kaltenbach's Appartements offers comfortable accommodation with free WiFi.

It is 120 metres from the Badeparadies Schwarzwald water park and spa. All apartments are designed in modern style, and include a TV.

The bathroom has a hairdryer and the fully equipped kitchen contains a coffee machine.

The surrounding countryside of Baden-Württemberg is ideal for hiking, cycling and skiing, and there is ski storage available at the apartments.

It is 1. 5 km to the Hochschwarzwald Golf Club. Breakfast is offered each morning, and within a 5-minute walk of the apartments there are several restaurants and cafés.

Kaltenbach's Appartements is 400 metres from Titisee Train Station, and it is a 35-minute drive to motorways A5 and A81.
