Description

Set amid the idyllic Black Forest, this family-run hotel features a traditional design and is only a 5-minute walk from the Titisee lake and a 10 minute walk from the Badeparadie Schwarzwald.

Highlights at the Parkhotel Waldeck include the indoor pool with waterfall and modern hot tubs.

Catch up on sleep in the hotel's individually furnished rooms, featuring a Black Forest design and WiFi internet access for free.

In the mornings, treat yourself to a rich breakfast buffet. Delicious Black Forest specialities are served in the Parkhotel Waldeck's restaurant with terrace.
