Description

This 4-star hotel offers an indoor pool, 2 restaurants and south-facing rooms with views of the Black Forest.

It is located in Todtnauberg, 1,000 metres above sea level. The rooms and apartments at the Bergvital Hotel feature wooden furnishings, warm colours and a balcony or conservatory.

A hairdryer and a safety deposit box are provided. Regional food and fine wines are served in the Ennerbach and Sonnenwinkel restaurants.

Guests can enjoy drinks in the Zirbelbar or beside the open fire in the lobby. Additionally, guests receive a KONUS card, which allows discounted entry to a variety of nearby attractions.

The Bergvital’s spa is 800 m² in size. It features a pool, sauna area and relaxing conservatory.

Beauty treatments can also be booked. Hiking and cycling trails begin right outside the Bergvital Hotel.
