Description

This traditional, family-run country hotel is peacefully located in the Todtnauberg Mountains, a 30-minute drive from Freiburg and the Titisee and Schluchsee lakes.

It offers free Wi-Fi, a sauna, and a regional restaurant with panoramic Black Forest views.

Set at an altitude of 1,020 metres, Landhotel Herrihof offer rustic-style rooms with rich wooden furniture.

Each room has a TV and beds with orthopedic mattresses, and some have a balcony. A daily breakfast buffet and seasonal cuisine from the Baden region are served in the Landhotel Herrihof's historic restaurant, conservatory or summer terrace with lovely panoramic views.

Landhotel Herrihof is a 20-minute drive from the Feldberg Mountain. The surrounding area is ideal for hikers, mountain bikers, skiers (5 ski lifts), cross-country skiers and anyone just seeking relaxation.

Guests booking 2 nights or more in any of the units receive a free Hochschwarzwald Card, giving access to over 100 leisure attractions.

Both the French and the Swiss borders are a 50-minute drive away. Parking is free at the hotel.
