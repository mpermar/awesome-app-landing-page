Description

This 3-star Superior, country hotel in the Vimbuch neighbourhood of Bühl offers free Wi-Fi, traditional Baden food, and great motorway links.

The famous spa town of Baden-Baden is a 20-minute drive away. The Kohlers Hotel Engel has bright, quiet rooms with cable TV and free internet.

A wide range of local and international dishes are on offer in the Kohlers Hotel's spacious restaurant.

The A5 motorway is only 1 km away from the Engel. It provides fast links to Karlsruhe and the French city of Strasbourg.
