Description

Featuring a wellness area and a restaurant, Hotel Waldlust is set in Baiersbronn, 46 km from Strasbourg.

Free private parking is available on site. The wellness area is located in annex, whilst the restaurant is located in the main building.

Breakfast will be served in annex. The rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV. Certain rooms have a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

Each room includes a private bathroom. Bicycles and electric bike hire are available at this hotel and the area is popular for skiing.

Hotel Waldlust offers a wellness area including a swimming pool, steam room and sauna.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including cycling and fishing.

Baden-Baden is 27 km from Hotel Waldlust, while Baiersbronn is 2. 3 km from the property.
