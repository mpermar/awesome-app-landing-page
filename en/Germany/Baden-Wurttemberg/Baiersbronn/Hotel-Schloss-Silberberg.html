Description

This hotel is located high on the Silberberg mountain in Huzenbach, 7 km from Baiersbron.

Hotel Schloss Silberberg offers free WiFi and great views of the Murg Valley here in the Black Forest.

All rooms at Hotel Schloss Silberberg feature a flat-screen TV with free SKY satellite channels.

Room also include a private bathroom with bathtub or shower, and a balcony with views of the valley or forest countryside.

A balanced breakfast buffet is offered each morning, and half board is also available.

The Schloss Berg Stüble is a rustic-style restaurant offering regional cuisine, a sports bar with SKY channels, and a panoramic beer garden.

The hotel also has a games room for children. This includes a flat-screen TV where movies are shown, a Playstation, a Wii console, a dartboard, a professional table football table as well as various books and board games.
