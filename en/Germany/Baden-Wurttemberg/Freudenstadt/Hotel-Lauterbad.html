Description

Hotel Lauterbad features a restaurant, free bikes, an outdoor swimming pool and fitness centre in Freudenstadt.

Among the various facilities of this property are a bar, a shared lounge and a garden.

The hotel has an indoor pool, spa centre, evening entertainment and room service.

Guest rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and a shower.

The rooms include a desk. Hotel Lauterbad offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

The accommodation offers 4-star accommodation with a sauna and terrace. You can play table tennis at Hotel Lauterbad, and the area is popular for hiking and skiing.

There is a tour desk and car rentals are available, while the business centre has newspapers.

Zuflucht Ski Lift is 20 km from the hotel. The nearest airport is Baden Airpark, 76 km from Hotel Lauterbad, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
