Description

Hotel Mansio Bad Säckingen is located in scenic Bad Säckingen on 120. 000m² grounds.

It offers a restaurant, free Wi-Fi access all rooms either have a balcony or terrace.

The private bathrooms offer a shower and hairdryer. Extras include a desk and a safety deposit box.

The bistro on the property serves regional Baden cuisine as well as Italian specialities.

Guests may enjoy the bar, coffee and cake in the afternoons, as well as a regional buffet breakfast.

At Hotel Mansio Bad Säckingen you will find in a garden, a terrace and a bar. Other facilities offered include a games room, luggage storage and dry cleaning.

Activities that can be enjoyed on the picturesque grounds or in the surroundings include Nordic walking, cycling, golf and mini golf.

The hotel also offers free parking. The property is situated 1. 5 km from the Bad Säckigen town centre.
