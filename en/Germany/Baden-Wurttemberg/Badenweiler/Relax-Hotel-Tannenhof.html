Description

Rental bicycles (for a daily surcharge) and free use of the sauna and indoor swimming pool is offered by this family-run accommodation.

Located on the edge of the woods in the south-western Black Forest, Relax Hotel Tannenhof enjoys a quiet location in the spa town of Badenweiler.

The hotel features bright and spacious rooms with classic-style furnishings. Each room is complete with a TV, safe, free WiFi and an en suite bathroom.

A hearty buffet breakfast is provided each morning in the welcoming dining room. In the evenings, guests can enjoy Swabian cuisine and a great atmosphere in the hotel restaurant.

The Relax Hotel Tannenhof is a few minutes' walk from Badenweiler town centre with its spa facilities, shops and a unique park with exotic plants.

The hotel also has a modern converted indoor pool, an outdoor sauna, an infrared sauna and a gym.

Tanning beds, hydrojet massages, and massage chairs are available for a fee. Free on-site parking is available in front of the hotel.
