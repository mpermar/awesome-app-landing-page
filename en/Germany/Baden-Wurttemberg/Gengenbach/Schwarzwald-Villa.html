Description

This beautiful villa provides bed accommodation in Gengenbach, in the Black Forest.

Schwarzwald Villa offers free WiFi and a garden with a terrace and barbecue facilities.

The accommodation offers a 2-bedroom apartment as well as a range of deluxe and budget rooms.

All rooms include cable TV, a seating area and a private bathroom, while some include luxury interiors and a private balcony.

The scenic surroundings are ideal for outdoor activities such as hiking, cycling and hiking.

Schwarzwald Villa is next to the Mühlbach stream that flows into the Kinzig River.

Gegenbach Train Station is 250 metres away. The villa offers free parking and is a 45-minute drive from Baden Airpark Airport.

The swimming pool immediately behind the villa is open from May to September. Admission to the pool is free for guests who have the Konus card.
