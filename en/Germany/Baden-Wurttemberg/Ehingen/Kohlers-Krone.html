Description

Located in the Swabian Alps, this traditional, family-run hotel is about 1 km from the B465 road.

It offers high-speed internet and a restaurant, serving traditional specialities from the Baden-Württemberg region.

All bright, modern rooms and apartments at the Köhlers Krone feature a satellite TV, tea and coffee maker and work desk.

Some rooms offer a kitchen, dining area and DVD player. A buffet breakfast is served every morning at the Krone.

Guests can also enjoy meals on the outdoor terrace, overlooking a beautiful garden with a children’s playground.

The Köhlers is a good starting point for walking, hiking and cycling through the Swabian countryside.

Approximately a 10-minute drive away, the River Donau is ideal for boating and fishing.
