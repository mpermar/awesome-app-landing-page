Description

Set in a tree-lined garden, this modern hotel is just a 10-minute walk from Lake Constance.

It offers a restaurant, sun terrace and free public transport throughout Constance.

All standard rooms have wooden panelled walls at Seehörnle Hotel & Gasthaus. Many rooms are wheelchair accessible, and most rooms include a large bathroom.

Freshly caught fish and locally sourced meat and vegetables are served in Seehörnle’s stylish restaurant.

A hearty buffet breakfast is provided and can even be enjoyed on the terrace during warm weather.

A 12-minute drive takes you to Radolfzell am Bodensee, where visitors can discover the Gothic-style buildings in the historic Old Town.

Seehörnle is also ideally located for hiking trips in the surrounding countryside.

Free private parking is available on site. The A81 motorway is a 20-minute drive from the hotel.
