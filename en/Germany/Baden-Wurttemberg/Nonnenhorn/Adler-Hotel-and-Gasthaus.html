Description

This family-run hotel in Nonnenhorn offers spacious rooms with free Wi-Fi, traditional Bavarian food, and easy access to different sports facilities.

Lake Constance is only about 400 metres away. All of the modern rooms at the Hotel Gasthof Adler feature a bright design and satellite TV.

Varied breakfast buffets and local dishes are served at the Hotel Adler every day.

Leisure activities near the Gasthof Adler include tennis, mini golf, and sailing.

The island of Lindau is about 20 minutes away by car. Free private parking is available at the Hotel Gasthof Adler.
