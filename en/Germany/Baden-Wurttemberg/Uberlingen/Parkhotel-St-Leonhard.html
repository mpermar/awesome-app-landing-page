Description

Situated in a quiet location on the edge of Überlingen, this hotel offers luxurious spa facilities, a panoramic sauna, an indoor swimming pool and a beautiful view of Lake Constance.

The Parkhotel St. Leonhard provides first-class accommodation in spacious rooms with free broadband and Wi-Fi internet access.

Go for a swim in the pool and pamper yourself with a cosmetic treatment or massage in the hotel spa.

Go for a walk in the beautiful 70-hectare park surrounding the hotel. Relax and admire the panoramic view of Lake Constance all year round in the conservatory café with its beautiful outdoor terrace.

Help yourself to the delicious breakfast buffet each morning. Enjoy coffee and cake in the afternoon and bistro snacks in the evening.

Choose from a range of fish, game and poultry dishes in the stylish restaurant. Round off the day in the traditional rustic ambience of the Weinstube (wine bar).

Guests staying at the Parkhotel St. Leonhard can park for free on location. E-bikes can be rented for an extra fee.
