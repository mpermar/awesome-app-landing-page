Description

A 5-minute drive from the Europapark Rust theme park, this family-run hotel in Rheinhausen offers cosy accommodation with daily breakfast and free parking.

Regional cuisine and peaceful surroundings await you. The traditional 2-star-Superior Hotel-Restaurant Werneths Landgasthof Hirschen provides comfortable rooms with satellite TV and free Wi-Fi.

Family rooms are available for guests travelling with children. Enjoy seasonal dishes and specialities of the Baden region in the hotel's restaurant with conservatory.

During warm weather, take a seat in the Mediterranean-style beer garden with fountain and boccia lane.

Younger guests will enjoy playing in the hotel's playground. The hotel is situated just 5 minutes off the A5 motorway.

It is an ideal base for hikes, bike rides and boat trips through the Taubergießen conservation area, and is also a short drive from the Alsace region of France.
