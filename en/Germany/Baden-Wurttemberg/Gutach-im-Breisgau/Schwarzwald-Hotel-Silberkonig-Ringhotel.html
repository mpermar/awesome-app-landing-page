Description

This family-run 4-star hotel in the Black Forest offers accommodation with free parking, a sauna, and 2 restaurants with a terrace.

All of the rooms at the Schwarzwald-Hotel Silberkönig Ringhotel are fitted with a TV and minibar.

Most rooms also feature a balcony, and family rooms are available. A breakfast buffet is provided every morning.

The St. Georgs Stube and Schwedi Stube restaurants serve a range of specialities for lunch and dinner, as well as for brunch and barbecue events.

During warmer weather, meals and drinks can be served on the Silberkönig's terrace.

Massages and beauty treatments can be booked in the sauna area. Tennis courts and tennis rackets can be reserved and used free of charge.

The hotel comes with a playground for children and a bowling alley, as well as table football, billiards and darts facilities.

Bleibach town centre is a 12-minute walk from the Schwarzwald-Hotel. The A5 motorway and the city of Freiburg are a 15-minute drive away.
