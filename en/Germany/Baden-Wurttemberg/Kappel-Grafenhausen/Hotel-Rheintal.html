Description

This family-run hotel is located in Kappl-Grafenhausen, 800 metres from the A5 motorway, and the Europapark Theme Park in Rust can be reached after a 5-minute drive.

Parking and WiFi are offered free of charge in Hotel Rheintal. All rooms feature a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with free toiletries.

Certain units are air-conditioned and some come with a balcony. A buffet-style breakfast is served on a daily basis.

The restaurant offers regional, international and seasonal cuisine for lunch and dinner, and boasts a terrace that can be used in the summer months.

Freiburg im Breisgau is a 20-minute drive away, while the Swiss border can be reached within 50 minutes by car.
