Description

Hotel Restaurant Hohenblick is centrally yet quietly located next to the A8 motorway, between Stuttgart and Ulm.

It offers a regional restaurant and a terrace with scenic views of the Upper Fils Valley.

All of the AKZENT Hotel Restaurant Höhenblick's bright rooms feature a TV. Guests are welcome to use the hotel's sauna.

The restaurant serves Swabian specialties and international dishes, prepared with 80% regional ingredients.

Free WiFi is available in all rooms and throughout the property. The sun terrace offers excellent views of the Upper Fils Valley and the surrounding forests.

Hiking and cycling trails can be found directly outside the hotel. The property offers 2 charging stations for electric cars, available upon request.

Stuttgart and Ulm are just a 20-minute drive from the Höhenblick Hotel, and both feature museums, shops and theatres.
