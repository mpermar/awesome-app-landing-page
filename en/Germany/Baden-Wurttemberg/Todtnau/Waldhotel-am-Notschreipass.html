Description

This hotel in Todtnau is surrounded by beautiful forest countryside. It offers a spa area with panoramic indoor pool, saunas and massages.

The modern rooms at Waldhotel am Notschreipass all feature a flat-screen TV, carpeted floors and a private bathroom.

Guests can also have fun in the bowling alley, in the children's playground or have a game of billiards.

There is a ski lift in summer. The Waldhotel am Notschreipass is an ideal base for exploring the Vosges Mountains.

Freiburg, Basel, Colmar, Straßburg or Baden-Baden are easy day-trip destinations from here.
