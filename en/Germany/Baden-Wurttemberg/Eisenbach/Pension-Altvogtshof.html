Description

Offering an outdoor pool, sauna, and sun terrace, Pension Altvogtshof is scenically located in Eisenbach-Schollach, in the heart of the Black Forest.

Free WiFi access is available at this family-run guest house. These colourful rooms are decorated in a country style and offer a balcony with mountain views.

Each one features a TV, a table and a bathroom with hairdryer. At the Pension Altvogtshof, guests can enjoy a hearty breakfast buffet served every morning from 8:30 - 10:30.

The surrounding countryside is ideal for hiking, cycling and cross-country skiing in the winter.

A billiards table as well as snow shoes can be rented for a fee. In door entertainment including table soccer, table tennis and dart games are available for guests, free of charge.

Guests receive a complimentary KONUS card, which provides free transport on trains and buses in the Black Forest region.

The Hochschwarzwald-Card is not offered at the property. Neustadt (Schwarzw) Train Station and Lake Titi lie 10 km from the property and the A5 motorway is a 45-minute drive.
