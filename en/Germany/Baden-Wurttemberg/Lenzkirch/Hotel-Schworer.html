Description

This 3-star hotel is located in the town of Lenzkirch in the beautiful Black Forest.

It offers free WiFi internet access, and a restaurant serving local food. The Hotel Schwörer was built in 2009.

Each room includes a bathroom, and a large breakfast buffet is included in the room rate.

The Hotel Schwörer's restaurant offers Black Forest specialities and a variety of drinks.

During summer months, drinks and meals are served on the terrace. The hotel is next to the Bähnle cycling trail and the Schluchtensteig hiking trail, which leads to the Wutachschlucht gorge.

Guests staying for at least 2 nights receive a Hochschwarzwald Card (Black Forest Tourist Card).

This offers discounts on over 70 leisure activities, attractions, a ski pass for the Feldberg ski resort in the Black Forest area, and the admission to the Badeparadies spa.
