Description

This 4-star hotel features a large spa centre with 10 different saunas and an indoor pool.

It lies in Lauterbach, surrounded by the scenic Black Forest countryside. Elegant, country-style rooms are offered at Landhotel Basler Hof.

Each has a minibar, satellite TV and an en suite bathroom with bathrobes and hairdryer.

WiFi access is free. A daily buffet breakfast is served in Landhotel Basler Hof's breakfast restaurant, made with produce from the hotel’s own fruit and herb garden.

The hotel bistro offers small dishes and snacks from the Baden region. Spa facilities at the Landhotel Basler Hof include a hot tub and steam baths.

Massages and beauty treatments are available. Hiking and cycling trails can be found in the area, and the pretty city of Freiburg is a 1-hour drive away.
