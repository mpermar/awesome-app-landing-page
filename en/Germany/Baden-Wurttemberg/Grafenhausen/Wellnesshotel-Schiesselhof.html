Description

Surrounded by the beautiful scenery of the Black Forest, this hotel in Grafenhausen features a modern spa with an indoor pool.

The rooms are classically furnished and the traditional restaurant serves German specialities.

The Wellnesshotel Schiesselhof’s large spa area offers a sauna, spa tub and an indoor swimming pool.

You can even book a massage or a range of beauty treatments. The hotel is located on the family farmyard, where children and adults are welcome to feed and pet the animals.

Pony riding is available in the mornings, and tractor rides in the evenings. A rich breakfast buffet is available each morning in the Schiesselhof’s restaurant.

In the evenings, you can try hearty cuisine prepared with ingredients from local farms.

Rooms at the family-run Wellnesshotel Schiesselhof are bright and modern. All come equipped with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

Many pretty hiking trails can be found next to the hotel, and the lovely Schluchsee Lake is just a 15-minute drive away.
