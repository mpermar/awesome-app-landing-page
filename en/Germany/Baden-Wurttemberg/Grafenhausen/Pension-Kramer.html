Description

Offering free Wi-Fi access and country-style rooms, this family-run guest house is situated in the village of Grafenhausen.

It features an outdoor terrace, a spa area with a sauna and a fitness room. Rooms at Pension Kramer are traditionally furnished with wooden interiors.

All include a seating area, flat-screen TV with satellite channels, and a private bathroom.

A full breakfast buffet is provided each morning in the Kramer’s bright breakfast room, which features a view of the garden.

Pension Kramer is an ideal base for hiking, mountain bike or motorbike tours. Walking sticks, snow shoes and toboggans can be hired for free, and table tennis facilities are also available.

Pension Kramer is a 15-minute drive from Lake Schluchsee and a 50-minute drive from Freiburg.
