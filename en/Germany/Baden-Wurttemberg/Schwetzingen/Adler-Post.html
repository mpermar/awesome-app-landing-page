Description

Dating back to 1840, this 3-star-superior hotel is located in Schwetzingen town centre, 100 metres from the Schlossgarten park in Baden-Württemberg.

It is within a 15-minute drive of Heidelberg and Mannheim, and offers parking facilities.

The air-conditioned and non-smoking rooms are fitted with free WiFi, soundproofed windows and a bathroom with bathrobe, slippers, hairdryer, a cosmetic mirror and cosmetics.

A free bottle of water is provided in the room. Guests at the Adler - Post enjoy free use of the sauna.

Drinks can be enjoyed in the hotel lobby, that also features a fireplace during the winter months.

The Adler Post is 100 metres from Schwetzingen's Rococo-style theatre and Schwetzingen Castle.
