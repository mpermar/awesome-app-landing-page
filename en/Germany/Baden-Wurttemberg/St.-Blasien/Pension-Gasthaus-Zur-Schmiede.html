Description

Located 9km from the town of St. Blasien, Pension Gasthaus Zur Schmiede offers peaceful accommodation in the heart of the Black Forest.

All rooms at Pension Gasthaus Zur Schmiede are designed in classic style with a wooden interior.

They include cable TV, and have an en suite bathroom with a hairdryer and complimentary toiletries.

The beautiful St. Blasien Abbey, which dates back to the 9th century, is just a 10-minute drive from the hotel.

The surrounding countryside of Baden-Württemberg also offers the ideal chance for hiking.

A buffet breakfast is offered each morning, and the hotel restaurant serves a range of regional specialities.

Guests can also order packed lunches to be taken away. Pension Gasthaus Zur Schmiede is 15 km from Schluchsee Train Station, and it is a 50-minute drive to the Swiss city of Basel.
