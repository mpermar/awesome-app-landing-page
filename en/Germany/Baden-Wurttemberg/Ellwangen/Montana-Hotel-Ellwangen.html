Description

Located just 3 km from the centre of Ellwangen, this 3-star hotel offers free WiFi internet access, free parking and excellent access to the A7 motorway between Würzburg and Ulm.

The Montana-Hotel Ellwangen provides contemporary-style rooms with all modern amenities, including satellite TV with Sky Atlantic and Sky Sport News.

A rich breakfast buffet is available each morning (for a surcharge) to guests of the Montana Hotel.

Relax in the evenings with a meal or a drink in the bistro-bar-lounge which features a cosy fireplace and large-screen TV with Sky Sports.

The Montana is an ideal base for visiting the historical attractions in nearby Ellwangen, such as the castle, basilica and the abbey.

Other convenient services offered by the Montana-Hotel Ellwangen include a car wash, filling station and conference rooms.
