Description

Exclusive spa facilities, several restaurants and free WiFi are offered at this 5-star hotel.

It is set in scenic gardens in Baden-Baden, a 10-minute walk from the Kurhaus Spa Centre.

Brenners Park-Hotel & Spa - an Oetker Collection Hotel offers elegant rooms with antique furniture and balconies.

Each is equipped with satellite TV and a minibar, and the bathrooms feature Italian marble.

Facilities at Brenners Park-Hotel & Spa - an Oetker Collection Hotel include a Roman-style swimming pool, relaxation rooms and several saunas, as well as a fitness studio.

Massages and health treatments are available here. Dining options include the Wintergarten and the Rive Gauche restaurant.

The Kaminhalle lounge offers live piano music in the evenings. The Fritz & Felix restaurant offers original and authentic cuisine created by a leading Swiss chef.

The restaurant design is inspired by the Roaring Twenties (1920s) with natural elements from the Black Forest.

A wide selection of drinks is served, including the house's own vermouths and one of the largest collections of German gins and whiskies.
