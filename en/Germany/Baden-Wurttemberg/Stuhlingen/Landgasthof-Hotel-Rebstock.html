Description

Occupying a building which dates back to 1368, this traditional, family-run hotel in Stühlingen lies at the gateway to the southern Black Forest nature park, right at the Swiss border.

Ideally located for visiting Schaffhausen and various popular attractions, the renowned Landgasthof-Hotel Rebstock offers romantic, tastefully furnished guest rooms with free wireless internet access.

Now in their third generation, the family owners look forward to treating you to home-brewed schnapps and fine, regional cuisine in the rustic restaurant with beer garden.

A wide range of leisure activities are available nearby. The bus station Stühlingen Rathaus is next to the property.
