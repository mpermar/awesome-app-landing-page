Description

This comfortably furnished accommodation with a terrace and children's playground is 13 km from Ravensburg.

Adam & Eva Gasthof Paradies offers free WiFi, friendly staff and is an ideal base for motorbike tours in the Allgäu, Lake Constance and the northern Alps.

Newly renovated in 2017, all rooms have a large flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom with no-step shower, toiletries, a cupboard with mirror, a safe, a hairdryer and bathrobes.

Large and small towels and slippers are provided. A great breakfast is available at this accommodation, which has been family-run for over 100 years.

Guests can enjoy à la carte dishes made from regional products, as well as local draught beer and wines from Lake Constance.

The youngest guests can enjoy table football, a play area and skittle lanes. Situated in Upper Swabia and near the Upper Swabian Baroque Route, Adam & Eva Gasthof Paradies is near many forests, lakes and forests and trails for hiking, cross-country skiing, cycling, biking, canoeing, swimming and boating.

The nearest towns are Wangen, Ravensburg and Wolfegg. Parking spaces are included in the room rate, as well as motorcycle storage, a large ballroom and conference room.
