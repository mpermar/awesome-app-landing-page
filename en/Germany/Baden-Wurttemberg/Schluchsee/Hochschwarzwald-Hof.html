Description

Beautifully situated in the town centre of Schluchsee, this family-run 3-star hotel offers a traditional 16th-century lounge and an excellent panoramic view overlooking the largest lake in the Black Forest.

The Hochschwarzwald-Hof provides comfortable accommodation amid the scenic landscapes of the Southern Black Forest nature park.

Many of the rooms feature a balcony directly overlooking Lake Schluchsee. Treat yourself to traditional German cuisine and refreshing beer in the relaxed atmosphere of the Schluchsee-Stüble rustic lounge.

The bar now screens sporting events on satellite TV. You can also enjoy a coffee on the hotel terrace, or a drink in the beer garden.

The Hotel Hochschwarzwald-Hof is an ideal place to stay for guests of all ages who wish to go paddling, bathing, swimming, diving, surfing or sailing in Lake Schluchsee which is just a 5-minute walk away.
