Description

Just a 5-minute drive from Lake Schluchsee, this family-run hotel in the Black Forest village of Schönenbach offers non-smoking rooms with free WiFi and a traditional-style restaurant with beer garden.

The Bartlehof has spacious rooms with modern furniture, satellite TV, and a private bathroom with hairdryer.

Some rooms have a balcony. Rich breakfast buffets are available at the Bartlehof.

Guests can try Black Forest food and local wines in the Gaststube, Uhrenstube, and Weinstube dining areas.

Guests staying at the Bartlehof are welcome to bring their own horse or dog on request.

Guests staying 2 nights or more receive the Skipass Liftverbund Feldberg and Hochschwarzwald Card free of charge.

The Bartlehof is ideal for hiking, cycling, and skiing in the Schönenbach area. The cities of Freiburg, Basel, and Zurich can be reached from the Bartlehof in one hour by car.
