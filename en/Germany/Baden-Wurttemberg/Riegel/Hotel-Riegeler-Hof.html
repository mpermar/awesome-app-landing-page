Description

The charming town of Riegel offers an attractive range of sports and recreational activities throughout the year. Embark on stimulating hikes and bike tours, or enjoy the luxury of silence on the garden terrace. Discover the timeless appeal of the Black Forest and Swiss countryside, take your children to the Steinwasenpark wildlife reserve and admire the cultural heritage of Strasbourg.

After an active day, unwind in the cozy atmosphere of your room before enjoying a hearty meal in the friendly restaurant.
