Description

Conveniently situated just off the motorway exit 57B, this hotel is 4 km from the Europa-Park theme park Rust.

The Sun Parc Hotel offers free Wi-Fi and a free shuttle to and from the Europa-Park.

All rooms at the Sun Parc Ringsheim feature classic décor, a TV and a private bathroom with shower.

A large breakfast buffet is served each morning. A glass of Baden wine or a refreshing beer can be enjoyed at the hotel bar or on the garden terrace.

Offering a family-oriented and friendly service, the Sun Parc Hotel is an ideal base for visiting the nearby Black Forest or the Alsace region of France.
