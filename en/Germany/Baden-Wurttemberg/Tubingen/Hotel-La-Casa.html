Description

Located a 15-minute walk from Tübingen’s Old Town, the Hotel La Casa awaits you with elegant bedrooms, impressive spa facilities and a rooftop terrace overlooking Tübingen.

Enjoy your stay here, in the architecturally interesting Lorettoviertel area. Modern design meets Mediterranean flair in this peaceful district.

From here, Stuttgart’s exhibition grounds and airport can be reached in 25 minutes by car.
