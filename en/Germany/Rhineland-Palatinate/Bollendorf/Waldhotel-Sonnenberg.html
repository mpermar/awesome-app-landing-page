Description

This traditional family-run hotel is located in the spa town of Bollendorf. Waldhotel Sonnenberg offers free high-speed Wi-Fi and free use of spa with swimming pool and new panoramic sauna with outdoor terrace.

Waldhotel Sonnenberg features a 50,000 m² park with sunbathing lawn, barbecue area and horse paddock.

Guests have the possibility to book spa treatments at a surcharge. Breakfast is served in Restaurant Bellevue, which offers great views of the Eifel Mountains.

Regional and international specialities are also offered. The German-Luxembourg Nature Park is ideal for walking, hiking and cycling.

Guests are welcome to explore the nearby cliff formations and caves. Parking is free at Waldhotel Sonnenberg.

It is a 6-minute drive from the scenic Bollendorf Mountain and Weilerbach Castle.
