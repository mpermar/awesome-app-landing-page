Description

This hotel is located in Ramstein-Miesenbach, just 5 km from the Ramstein Airbase.

Hotel Restaurant Anna offers free WiFi, free parking and an à la carte restaurant.

Rooms at Hotel Restaurant Anna are air-conditioned and include a seating area, satellite TV, a microwave and refrigerator and hairdryer.

All rooms come with a private bathroom. Lunches include home-made burgers, soups and salads, while flame-grilled Argentinian steaks are served for dinner.

Guests can enjoy a selection of cocktails in the bar. Hotel Anna is a pet-friendly and child-friendly accommodation.

A café and 2 supermarkets can be found within a 10-minute walk. The 12th-century Nanstein Castle is 6 km away.

Hotel Restaurant Anna is 800 metres from Ramstein Train Station. The A6 and A62 motorways are a 7-minute drive away.
