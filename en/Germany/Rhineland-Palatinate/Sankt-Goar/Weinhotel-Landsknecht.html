Description

This family-friendly hotel on the Rhine offers great views and regional cuisine. Weinhotel Landsknecht is a 5-minute drive from Sankt Goar and the Lorelei rock, and 12 minutes from the A61 motorway.

All guests receive a free bottle of mineral water on arrival at the 3-star Weinhotel Landsknecht.

Most rooms are air-conditioned and offer direct views of the River Rhine. The non-smoking Landsknecht provides a breakfast buffet each morning.

Local ingredients and wines are used in the restaurant. The large riverside terrace is open during warmer months.

The Weinhotel Landsknecht provides rental bicycles for exploring the UNESCO-listed Middle Rhine region.
