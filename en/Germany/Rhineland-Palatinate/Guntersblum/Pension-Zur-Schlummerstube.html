Description

This guest house enjoys a central location in the village of Guntersblum. It offers parking and individually decorated rooms with free WiFi access.

Pension Zur Schlummerstube features bright rooms with homelike interiors. Each is complete with a flat-screen TV, work desk and a private bathroom.

Some rooms also have a balcony and garden views. Guests are welcome to use the barbecue facilities at Zur Schlummerstube, in the summer months.

A continental breakfast can also be ordered for an extra fee. On-site facilities include a shared lounge, ironing service and a vending machine.
