Description

Situated steps away from the River Moselle, in the Sehl district of Cochem, this family-run hotel offers a heated swimming pool, spa facilities and an excellent restaurant.

The Moselromantik Hotel Panorama offers comfortable rooms with modern amenities. Free Wi-Fi internet access is available throughout the hotel.

The older and newer parts of the hotel are connected by a beautiful garden. Hotel guests can relax on this terrace in summer.

A breakfast buffet is available daily each morning. Treat yourself to regional specialities and fish dishes in the Sehler Mark restaurant, and enjoy meals, snacks, coffee and cake throughout the day in the Apollo Lounge.

Go for a swim in the hotel pool, and unwind in the sauna, steam room, solarium or with an Ayurvedic massage.

The pool and sauna remain open until 22:00 for the convenience of guests. From the Moselromantik Hotel Panorama, guests can go on a hiking or cycling tour along the Moselle valley, in the Eifel mountains or in the Hunsrück hills.
