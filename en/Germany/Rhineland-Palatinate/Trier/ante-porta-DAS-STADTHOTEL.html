Description

Each of the brightly decorated rooms at the ante porta DAS STADTHOTEL features a desk, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with shower. Some rooms have a balcony.

A varied buffet breakfast is available each morning.

The historic Trier Cathedral is less than 0.6 mi away from the hotel. Trier Train Station is only a 10-minute walk away.
