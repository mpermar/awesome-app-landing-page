Description

Built in 1875, this family-run hotel near Breitscheid, in the heart of Wiedtal, offers country-style rooms and a traditional restaurant.

Landgasthof Zur Regen is surrounded by quiet countryside in the Rhine-Westerland Nature Park.

The bright rooms at Landgasthof Zur Erholung Paganetti include cable TV, wooden interiors and a private bathroom.

Some rooms offer a balcony and some have free Wi-Fi. Guests are welcome to enjoy a rich buffet breakfast from 06:00 each morning in Landgasthof Paganetti’s rustic-style restaurant or on the small terrace.

Regional German cuisine is also served for lunch and dinner. Gasthof Zur Erholung is an ideal base for hiking and cycling, and children can play in the small playground with swings and a sandpit.

There is a deer park just behind the hotel and the Wiedtalbad Swimming Baths are 5 km away.

Landgasthof Paganetti offers free parking and is 6 minutes from the A3 motorway. Transfers can be organised from the train stations in Neuwied, Linz or Bad Hönningen.
