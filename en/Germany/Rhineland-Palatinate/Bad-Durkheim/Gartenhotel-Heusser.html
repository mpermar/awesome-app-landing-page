Description

This 4-star hotel in Bad Dürkheim has a Japanese teahouse and oriental gardens. It offers free Wi-Fi internet, free use of the spa with swimming pool and free parking.

The family-run Gartenhotel Heusser is a 12-minute walk from the town centre. Bad Dürkheim Train Station is one kilometre away.

All rooms at the Heusser feature individually controlled air-conditioning, satellite TV as well as a balcony or terrace.

The spa area has a range of saunas and showers, a steam bath, swimming pool, Kneipp facilities and a selection of massage services.

A rich breakfast buffet is provided each morning. The Gartenblick restaurant with terrace offers international dishes and views of the gardens.
