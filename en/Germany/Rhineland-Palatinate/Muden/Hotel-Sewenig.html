Description

This charming, 3-star hotel lies directly on the banks of the River Moselle in Müden, 300 metres from the train station.

It features a riverside terrace and a mini-golf course. The Hotel Sewenig also offers guests use of the in-house sauna, solarium and infra-red cabin.

Look forward to comfortable, tastefully furnished rooms, some boasting a private balcony.

Free Wi-Fi is available in almost all rooms. After a peaceful night, wake up to the Sewenig's delicious breakfast buffet, before setting out to explore Müden and the beautiful vineyards of the area.

You can enjoy an excellent meal in the restaurant or on the terrace during the summer, together with a glass of fine local wine.

Be sure to try some of the hotel's house wines from its own vineyards. A 20-minute drive will bring you to the famous Burg Eltz castle, and the Nürburgring race track is 50 km away.
