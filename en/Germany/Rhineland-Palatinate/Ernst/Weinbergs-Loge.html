Description

Weinbergs Loge is ideally located in Ernst, and features free WiFi, an elevator and a garden.

This guest house offers a terrace. The accommodation provides a shared lounge and luggage storage space for guests.

At the guest house, all rooms are equipped with a desk and a flat-screen TV. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom, while certain rooms at Weinbergs Loge also feature a seating area.

All rooms feature a wardrobe and all rooms offer access for guests with limited mobility as there is an elevator.

A Buffet breakfast is available daily at the accommodation. Weinbergs Loge offers a children's playground.

A number of activities are offered in the area, such as golfing, cycling and canoeing.
