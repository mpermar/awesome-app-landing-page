Description

The Hotel zum Löwen is located on the banks of the Moselle River in the wine-producing town of Ediger-Eller.

It features bright rooms, a traditional restaurant and a private vineyard. All of the rooms at the Moselromantik Hotel zum Löwen have a modern bathroom, satellite TV and free Wi-Fi via hotspot.

The Löwen Restaurant features a terrace, and serves seasonal food and local specialities.

Guests can also try the Hotel zum Löwen’s own brand of wine here. Parking is free at the Moselromantik Hotel, and the historic city of Cochem is just a 20-minute drive away.
