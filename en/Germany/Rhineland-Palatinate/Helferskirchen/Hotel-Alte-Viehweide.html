Description

A 10-minute walk from the village of Helferskirchen and surrounded by the Westerwald Forest, this peaceful hotel features a large beer garden and a spa.

Free Wi-Fi is provided. Simple rooms with wooden furniture and floral fabrics are offered at the Hotel Alte Viehweide.

Cable TV and private bathrooms are included, and some rooms feature a balcony. A sauna, hot tub and solarium are available in the Alte Viehweide’s spa area.

Relaxing massages can be booked, while active guests can use the gym equipment. Guests can start the day with a German-style breakfast buffet.

Rhineland specialities are on offer in the cosy restaurant with traditional bar. After dinner, guests can relax in the on-site bowling alley.

Hiking and cycling paths begin outside the hotel, and the Westerwald Lake District is 5 km away.

The A3 motorway is 7 km away, allowing access to Koblenz and Limburg in 30 minutes.
