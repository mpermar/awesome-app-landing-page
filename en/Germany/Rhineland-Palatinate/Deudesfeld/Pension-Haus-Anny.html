Description

This family-run guest house is located in the quiet village of Deudesfeld-Desserath.

Haus Anny offers cosy rooms with a private balcony, a mini-golf course, and a spa with heated indoor pool.

The comfortably furnished rooms at Pension Haus Anny feature satellite TV, wooden interiors, and a private bathroom with shower.

A buffet breakfast is served daily in the spacious breakfast room. Half-board guests receive a rich 3-course meal.

Guests at Haus Anny can relax in the sauna, available for an additional fee. The surrounding Vulcan Eifel countryside and woodland is ideal for hiking and horse riding.

The Meerfelder Maar Crater Lake is 6 km away. Parking is free at Pension Anny. It is 15 minutes from the A60 motorway.
