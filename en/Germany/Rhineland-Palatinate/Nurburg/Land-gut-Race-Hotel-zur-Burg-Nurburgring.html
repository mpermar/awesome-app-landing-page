Description

This 3-star hotel in the health resort of Nürburg offers free Wi-Fi access. The Nürburgring racing venue and Nürburg Castle are both within 500 metres of Land-gut-Hotel zur Burg Hocheifel Nürburgring The Hotel zur Burg has individually decorated rooms and apartments with satellite TV.

In the Hotel zur Burg's breakfast room, a large breakfast buffet is provided each morning.

The hotel restaurant and beer garden serves hearty specialities from the Eifel region.

On request, guests can use the small wellness area with sauna and infra-red cabin.

The beer garden and sunbathing lawn are open during the summer. The Nordschleifeneinfahrt, the oldest part of the Formula One Racing track in Nürburg is also just 500 metres away.
