Description

Quietly located in the scenic Hunsrück Mountains and just 11 km from the Moselle Valley, this 3-star-superior hotel features free Wi-Fi and a country-style restaurant.

Hotel Waldesblick offers modern rooms and apartments. All have satellite TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

Some rooms have a balcony. Facilities at Hotel Waldesblick include a sauna and barbecue area.

Pool, table football or table tennis are available in the games room. The Waldesblick offers children a petting zoo and outdoor playground.

For rainy days, there is a play room and barn which has been converted into an indoor playground.

Motorbikes can be left for free in the secure storage facilities. It is also possible to arrange packed lunches.
