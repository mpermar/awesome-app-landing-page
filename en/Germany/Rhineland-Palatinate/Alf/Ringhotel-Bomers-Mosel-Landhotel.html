Description

This family-run, 3-star-superior hotel is located in the wine-growing village of Alf, a 5-minute walk from the Moselle River.

It offers country-style rooms and a restaurant. Each room at the Ringhotel Bömers Mosellandhotel features fine wooden furnishings, satellite TV, and free WiFi.

An elevator is available. There is also a free internet terminal in the hotel lobby.

The Mosellandhotel’s restaurant serves varied cuisine with fresh, regional products and local Moselle wines.

A breakfast buffet is available each morning. Guests can also relax in the bar or on the sheltered terrace in the garden.

Further leisure facilities at the Mosellandhotel include a large garden with a sunbathing area.

Guests may want to visit the Bad Bertrich’s Vulkaneifeltherme spa and wellness centre, which is just a 10-minute drive away.

Guests can use the hotel's rental bikes to discover the surroundings. Ringhotel Bömers Mosellandhotel is 7 km from Zell, 20 km from Cochem and 27 km from Bernkastel Kues.
