Description

This 3-star hotel offers cosy accommodation, its own wines and guided bus tours. It lies right on the banks of the River Moselle in Briedern, around 16 kilometres from Cochem.

The rooms of the Winzerhotel und Restaurant zum Moselstrand are comfortably furnished in a country house-style and feature satellite TV.

Many feature lovely views over the river. Look forward to a delicious, complimentary breakfast buffet each morning before setting off to explore the Moselle countryside on foot or by bike.

You can rent a bike directly from the hotel, or take advantage of the guided bus tour offered by the hotel on Tuesdays and Fridays.

The bus visits Trier and Luxembourg. The Winzerhotel und Restaurant zum Moselstrand produces its own award-winning wines, which you are welcome to sample in one of the wine tasting sessions held in the vaulted wine cellar.

During the summer, guests can relax outside on the terrace with a refreshing drink.

Those travelling by car can park on-site for free, and secure storage is provided for your own bikes.
