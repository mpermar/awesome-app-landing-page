Description

PAGA Hotel is quietly located in idyllic countryside, a 5-minute walk from the centre of Aidenbach.

The family-run hotel is 15 minutes from Vilshofen and 20 minutes from the Lower Bavarian spa towns of Bad Birnbach, Bad Grießbach and Bad Füssing.

Built in the style of a Spanish hacienda. PAGA Hotel features a magnificent rose garden and a sunbathing area with parasols.

The on-site tennis courts can be used for an extra charge. Each of the brightly furnished Bavarian-style rooms offers satellite TV, free WiFi and a private bathroom with shower.

All rooms are quietly situated away from any traffic noise, and some also include a balcony or a terrace.

A fresh breakfast buffet is available each morning at the hotel. There is a selection of restaurants and bars within a 10-minute walk.

The Irgnöd Bird Park is 15 km away from PAGA Hotel, and the Ortenburg Wildlife Park is 13 km away.

Other popular activities in the area include hiking and cycling. The A3 motorway can be reached within a 40-minute drive of the hotel, and there is free private parking available on site.
