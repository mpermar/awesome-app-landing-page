Description

This traditional, family-friendly hotel is set in the village of St Engelmar, right in the heart of the Bavarian Forest, and provides a great base for exploring the surrounding nature.

At Gasthof Reiner most of the comfortable rooms have their own balcony and are reachable via the hotel lift.

The restaurant offers an extensive menu that ranges from delicious simple snacks to sophisticated meals, all made with fresh, local produce.

A perfect starting point for biking and hiking excursions through the forest, the hotel recommends many day-trip destinations such as the nearby Böhmerwald (Bohemian Forest), Passau and Regensburg.
