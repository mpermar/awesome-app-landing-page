Description

Located in Warmensteinach, 600 metres from Anfängerlift Talstation Süd, WAGNERS Hotel im Fichtelgebirge provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a garden and a terrace.

Each accommodation at the 3-star hotel has garden views, and guests can enjoy access to a children's playground and to a sauna.

The accommodation features an indoor pool, free WiFi throughout the property and family rooms.

Guest rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and a shower.

The rooms include a desk. WAGNERS Hotel im Fichtelgebirge offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

You can play table tennis at the accommodation, and the area is popular for skiing and cycling.

WAGNERS Hotel in the Fichtelgebirge include Fleckllift, cable car Ochsenkopf Süd and Hempelsberglift.
