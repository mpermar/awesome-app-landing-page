Description

Hotel-Pension Dressel is located in Warmensteinach. The hotel features a garden and a terrace.

Each of the bright rooms at Hotel-Pension Dressel comes equipped with a TV, a balcony and a seating area.

They also include a private bathroom with a hairdryer. A fresh breakfast buffet is served each morning at the hotel, and the restaurant serves a range of international and regional, Bavarian cuisine.

A further selection of restaurants can be found within a 15-minute walking distance of the hotel.

Popular activities in the surrounding area include hiking and cycling. For day-trips, Nuremberg can be reached in 1 hour by car.

There is free private parking at Hotel-Pension Dressel, and it is a 20-minute drive to the A9 motorway.
