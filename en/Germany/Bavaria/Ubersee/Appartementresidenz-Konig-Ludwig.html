Description

Appartementresidenz König Ludwig is situated in Übersee, and is a 15-minute drive to Lake Chiemsee.

The apartments feature an outdoor pool and a tennis court. In addition, each of the apartments comes equipped with free WiFi, a TV and a bathroom with a hairdryer.

They each also include a kitchenette with a microwave. The nearest supermarket is located 600 metres from the accommodation.

A selection of restaurants can be found within a 10-minute driving distance of Appartementresidenz König Ludwig.

Other facilities available at the apartments include a sauna, a garden and a games room.

Popular activities in the surrounding area include skiing, cycling and hiking, and the property has ski storage facilities.

It is 4 km to the A8 motorway, and there is free private parking available at the accommodation.
