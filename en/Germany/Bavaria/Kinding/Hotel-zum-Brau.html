Description

This family-run, 3-star hotel in the village of Enkering is a great base for exploring the scenic Altmühltal valley.

It offers regional cuisine and rooms with free Wi-Fi. The non-smoking Hotel zum Bräu has spacious rooms with cable TV and a modern bathroom.

Rooms for disabled guests are available on request. Varied breakfast buffets are prepared at the Hotel zum Bräu in the mornings.

Local and seasonal food is served in the restaurant with terrace. The Hotel zum Bräu is officially listed as a bicycle-friendly hotel.

Attractions in the area include the Rumburg castle ruins. Guests are welcome to park their car in the hotel courtyard at no extra cost.
