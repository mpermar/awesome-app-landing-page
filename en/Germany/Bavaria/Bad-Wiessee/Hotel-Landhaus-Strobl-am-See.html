Description

Overlooking Lake Tegernsee, this 3-star superior hotel offers Bavarian-style rooms and apartments with free Wi-Fi.

It is a 10-minute walk from Bad Wiessee town centre. The Landhaus Strobl provides large breakfast buffets and afternoon cake with coffee.

In the evening, guests can enjoy a small menu and international wines. Free rental bicycles are provided at the Strobl.

Guests can also relax in the large garden, sauna or lounge areas. Nearby buses and boats allow you to enjoy a wide range of day trips throughout Upper Bavaria.

Hotel staff are dressed in traditional Bavarian clothing and are happy to provide tips for day trips and local activities.
