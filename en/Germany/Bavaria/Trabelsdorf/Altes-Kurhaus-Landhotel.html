Description

In the green valley of the river Aurach, where rare species of bird still nest, surrounded by the historic castle Trabelsdorfer Schloss and the romantic castle Burg Lisberg, the hotel “Altes Kurhaus” lies between forest and lake.

Built here many years ago because of the healthy climate and the idyllic setting, the hotel remains an oasis for nature lovers and provides a wealth of things to see and do.

For art lovers this is an ideal starting point from which to discover the Franken countryside, for workers it is a perfect meeting place, and for business travellers it is the perfect place in which to unwind.
