Description

This 4-star hotel offers free Wi-Fi and a spa in Munich's Parsdorf district, just off the A94 motorway.

The ICM exhibition centre is a 10-minute drive away. All rooms at the Best Western Plus Hotel Erb include a bright design and soundproofed windows.

Every room includes a flat-screen TV with Sky channels, and most have a Nespresso coffee machine.

On request and according to availability, Best Western Plus Hotel Erb can provide a shuttle service to Grub Train Station for free.

The property can alternatively arrange a taxi to Munich Airport or Munich Main Station for a fee.

The Almdorf Spa Lounge includes a Finnish sauna hut, a pine sauna, an herbal sauna, a brine salt stone sauna, an aroma steam bath and an infrared lounge.

A large breakfast buffet and Italian coffees are available for an extra fee. There is also a beer garden.
