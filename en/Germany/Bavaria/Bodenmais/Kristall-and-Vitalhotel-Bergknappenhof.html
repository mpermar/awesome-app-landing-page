Description

Offering a wide range of spa facilities, free Wi-Fi internet, and traditional food from the Bavarian Forest, this 4-star hotel is a 15-minute walk from Bodenmais Train Station.

The Kristall- & Vitalhotel Bergknappenhof has spacious rooms and suites with a balcony, modern décor, and an elegant bathroom.

Some include free high-speed internet. Guests staying at the Bergknappenhof can relax in the spacious wellness area with Jacuzzi, heated outdoor pool, and 4 saunas.

Massages, Ayurvedic treatments, and beauty options are available. Varied breakfast buffets are provided at the Vitalhotel Bergknappenhof.

The hotel also provides a selection of regional and international specialities, as well as an afternoon buffet with coffee, cake and salad.

The Czech border is a 20-minute drive from the Bergknappenhof. Free private parking is available.
