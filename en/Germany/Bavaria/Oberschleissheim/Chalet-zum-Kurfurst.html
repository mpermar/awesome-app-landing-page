Description

All rooms at the Chalét Zum Kurfürst include a private bathroom and a safe. Some rooms face the palace gardens and some also have a minibar.

The Chalét Zum Kurfürst is part of the Hotel am Schlosspark in Oberschleißheim. The chalets are 100 feet away from the main building, where you will find the reception, breakfast room, restaurant, bar and a bowling alley.

The Zum Kurfürst restaurant offers a wide range of meals for lunch and dinner.
