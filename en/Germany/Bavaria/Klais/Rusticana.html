Description

This family-run guest house is located in Klais at an altitude of 950 metres in the Northern Alps, and provides an idyllic rural environment between Garmisch-Partenkirchen and Mittenwald on the Bavarian/Tyrolean border.

The bedrooms furnished in a traditional Alpine style feature flat-screen TV, free WiFi and panoramic views.

Relax in the garden or on the panorama terrace of the Rusticana. The Café Rusticana serves homebaked cakes, offers a coffee-bar with snacks and every Friday night a barbecue is held.

There is also a drinks fridge available to guests. Your hosts at the Rusticana are certified mountain and cycling guides.

Rental bikes are available on site. Cross-country ski paths and ski runs begin directly in front of the hotel.

Right in front of the hotel is a stop for the free local bus service which takes guests to popular local hiking areas.
