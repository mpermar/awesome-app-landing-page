Description

Boasting modern spa facilities and quality cuisine, this 4-star-superior Wallgau hotel provides a comfortable base from which to explore the picturesque Karwendel Alpine region.

The hotel’s appealing accommodation and relaxing ambience will ease you straight into the holiday feeling.

The beautiful countryside at your doorstep invites you to pursue a wide range of sports and leisure activities.

Take in the mountain panorama from the garden terrace and pamper yourself in the hotel’s generous spa area.

At the end of a fun-filled day, treat yourself to the hotel’s mouth-watering traditional cuisine.
