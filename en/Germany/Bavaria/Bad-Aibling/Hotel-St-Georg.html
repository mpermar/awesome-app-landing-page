Description

This 3-star-superior hotel offers free parking, an indoor pool and a wide range of sports facilities.

It is a 5-minute drive from Bad Aibling’s town centre and train station. All rooms at the Hotel St.

Georg include a TV. A private bathroom with hairdryer is provided. The St. Georg offers traditional Bavarian food and a large beer garden.

A bar with fireplace is also available. The hotel’s spa includes saunas, a hot tub and a range of spa treatments.

St. Georg also has a gym, tennis court and rental bikes. Several ski resorts can be reached quickly by car from the Hotel St.
