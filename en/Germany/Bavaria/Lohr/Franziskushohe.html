Description

The Hotel Franziskushöhe in Lohr am Main is surrounded by the forests of the Bayerischer Spessart Nature Park.

It offers rooms with lovely views and a restaurant serving traditional cuisine. The peaceful rooms at the Franziskushöhe all have extra-long beds and a TV with DVD player.

The private bathrooms all have a hairdryer and shower products. The Franziskushöhe features table tennis and basketball outdoors.

Children will enjoy the games room and large outdoor playground, and adults can relax in the swimming complex, which offers a natural bathing pond with a sandy beach, water games and bathing lawn with sunbeds.

The restaurant, Franziskus, serves Franconian specialities. The terrace boasts views of Lohr am Main.

Parking at the hotel is free. The centre of Lohr am Main is less than 3 km from the Franziskushöhe.
