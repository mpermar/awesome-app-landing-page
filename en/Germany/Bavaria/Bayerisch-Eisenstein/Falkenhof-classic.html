Description

Offering stunning views of the Bavarian Forest and National Park, these self-catering apartments in Bayerisch Eisenstein are located directly on the Czech border.

They offer individually furnished apartments and spa facilities. Falkenhof classic's modern apartments include a well-equipped kitchenette and a large living and dining area with flat-screen TV.

Free WiFi is provided in all areas. There are several restaurants in the area. To relax, guests can use the Finnish sauna and steam for an extra charge.

Other facilities include a table tennis room and ski storage room, and there is a shuttle bus to the nearby ski slopes.
