Description

Free WiFi and 2 saunas are provided at this modern family-run guest house in Bayerisch Eisenstein.

Just 300 metres from the Czech border, it is an ideal base for hiking and mountain biking.

Pension Elan offers non-smoking rooms and apartments with boxspring beds, flat-screen TVs.

Each bathroom features bathrobes, toiletries and a hairdryer. Some rooms include balconies with wonderful countryside views.

A rich breakfast buffet is provided at the Elan, while a variety of restaurants are within a 10-minute walk.

Guests can enjoy a large sun-bathing lawn at the property. There are also covered grill spot with a seating area.

There is also a shared lounge with fireplace at the property. A large selection of local beers and schnaps are available for an additional charge.

Guests who stay at this property also receive a complimentary All Inclusive AktivCARDs, which allows free entry to over 130 local attractions.

Rental bicycles and electric bikes are available for exploring the surrounding forests.

A free garage to store bikes, motorcycles and ski equipment is available onsite. The Großer Arber Ski Resort is 4 km away.

Free shuttle service from and to Bayerisch Eisenstein Train Station is available upon request.

A bus stop is located 100 metres from the property. Bayerisch Eisenstein Train station is 1.
