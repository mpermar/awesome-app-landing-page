Description

Gasthof Pension Hirsch offers accommodation in Lechbruck. The accommodation features ski storage space, as well as a shared lounge and a terrace.

There is a children's playground and guests can make use of free WiFi and free private parking.

The rooms at the inn are fitted with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and a shower.

All units will provide guests with a desk and a kettle. Gasthof Pension Hirsch offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

The area is popular for hiking and skiing, and bike hire is available at the accommodation.

Füssen is 23 km from Gasthof Pension Hirsch, while Murnau am Staffelsee is 37 km from the property.
