Description

Offering a spa area and a restaurant, this countryside hotel in Hassberge Nature Park is a 10-minute drive from Königsberg in Bayern.

Its bright rooms feature flat-screen TV. The Rügheim’s traditional restaurant serves international and Bavarian cuisine, and a buffet breakfast is available each morning.

Guests can enjoy a drink in the hotel’s rustic wine tavern. Landhotel Rügheim’s classically furnished rooms have a work desk and telephone.

The private modern bathrooms feature a hairdryer and complimentary toiletries. Well-signposted hiking and cycling trails can be found directly outside the Rügheim Landhotel.
