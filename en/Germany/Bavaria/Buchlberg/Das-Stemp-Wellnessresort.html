Description

This 3-star hotel is situated in the heart of the Bavarian forest on the outskirts of Büchlberg.

It features an outdoor pool, saunas and a restaurant serving Bavarian specialties.

Free WiFi is featured throughout the property. Rooms at the Landpension Stemp offer country-style modern interiors.

Every room features a balcony or terrace, safe, flat-screen TV and a work desk. The bathroom comes with shower or bathtub, hairdryer, make-up mirror and free toiletries.

Additional amenities include spa towels, Nordic walking sticks and an umbrella. Breakfast buffet is served in the hotel restaurant, which features rustic interiors with wooden furnishings and high ceilings.

The restaurant serves a daily menu with three main courses. Guests can also enjoy a variety of drinks at the well-stocked hotel bar.

Landhotel Stemp features a wellness area with an indoor and outdoor pool, 5 different saunas, a relaxation room and a sunbathing lawn.

The A3 motorway is 30 km from Landpension Stemp. Free public parking is available nearby the hotel.
