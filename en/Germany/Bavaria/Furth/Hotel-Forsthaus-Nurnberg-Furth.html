Description

Peacefully located beside a forest in southern Fürth, this hotel offers spacious rooms with flat-screen TV, Franconian cuisine and plenty of free parking spaces.

Enjoy contemporary furnishings and modern facilities at the 4-star Hotel Forsthaus Nürnberg Fürth.

All rooms feature a large working desk, minibar and cable TV. The cosy Waldrestaurant offers traditional Bavarian, international and vegetarian cuisine between 18:00 and 21:30.

Try a cocktail at the hotel bar, open until 00:00, or enjoy a refreshing drink in the hotel's beer garden during the summer, open until 22:00.

Keep fit in the hotel's gym, relax in the sauna and steam room, or swim a few laps of the hotel pool.

Right outside the Hotel Forsthaus Nürnberg Fürth, you will find quiet paths and mountain biking routes which lead you through the forest.

The centre of Fürth is just a 10-minute drive away, while Nuremberg can be reached in 25 minutes.

Young children will enjoy a trip to the PlayMobil Funpark (theme park), which is a 15-minute drive away.
