Description

The Mercure Nürnberg West is located in the Poppenreuth district of Fürth, just 8 km from Nuremberg Airport.

It provides free Wi-Fi, spacious rooms, an international restaurant and an outdoor pool from June to August.

All rooms at the Fürther Hotel Mercure Nürnberg West have a modern bathroom, satellite TV.

The Mercure’s Chlêo Restaurant features a large terrace, and serves German food and local favourites.

The Chlêo Lounge offers a variety of drinks, from exotic cocktails to coffee specialities.

Younger guests are welcome to use the family-friendly Mercure’s outdoor playground.

The fitness centre can be used for free. The A73 motorway is 200 metres away, providing a link to central Nuremberg in 15 minutes.
