Description

Situated in Gößweinstein, Landgasthof Hotel Frankenstern has a restaurant, bar, garden, and free WiFi throughout the property.

Boasting room service, this property also provides guests with a terrace. Private parking can be arranged at an extra charge.

At the hotel, every room is fitted with a wardrobe. At Landgasthof Hotel Frankenstern the rooms include a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet breakfast. Guests at Landgasthof Hotel Frankenstern will be able to enjoy activities in and around Gößweinstein, like cycling and fishing.

Nürnberg is 49 km from the hotel, while Bamberg is 48 km from the property. The nearest airport is Nuremberg Airport, 48 km from Landgasthof Hotel Frankenstern.
