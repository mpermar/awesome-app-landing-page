Description

Located in the heart of the Bavarian countryside, the family-run Hotel Sonnengut boasts comfortable rooms and a large wellness area.

Free WiFi access is available throughout the 4-star resort. The contemporary rooms at Hotel Sonnengut all feature flat-screen satellite TV and have an en suite bathroom with a hairdryer and a bathrobe.

Some rooms also include a coffee machine. Facilities at the hotel include an indoor and outdoor pool, a fitness centre, and a sauna area with an aromatherapy steam room, tepidarium and a Finnish sauna.

Massages can be booked for an additional fee. A breakfast buffet is offered each morning, and there are 2 restaurants serving modern and regional cuisine.

Guests are also welcome to relax with a drink in the bar. Hotel Sonnengut is 2. 5 km from Bad Birnbach Train Station, and it is a 25-minute drive to the A3 motorway.
