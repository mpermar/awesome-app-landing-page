Description

This family-run hotel offers a spa, a heated outdoor pool and great mountain views.

It is located in the small Bavarian health resort of Anger, at the foot of Hochstaufen in striking Berchtesgaden countryside, 10 minutes from Salzburg and Bad Reichenhall.

Wellness & Landhotel Prinz is beautifully decorated with warm colors, bright fabrics, Florentine lamps, S√∂lk crystal marble and beautiful ironwork.

All rooms and apartments feature country-style wooden furniture and a flat-screen TV.

Each morning room guests can enjoy a buffet breakfast with Grander healing stone water and a juice corner, and apartment guests can prepare their own meals in the fully equipped kitchens.

The restaurant has 2 dining areas and a bar, and serves 4-course evening meals made from local organic ingredients.

Guests can relax in the Cabrio swimming pool featuring a terrace. The exclusive spa features a hot tub with Grander water and colour lighting, a Finnish sauna with light illumination, an aroma steam bath with starry sky, a rock salt tepidarium, a pinewood infrared cabin, 2 rain showers, a relaxation room with Feng Shui floating beds, a solarium and a cosy lounge with a Grander water fountain.

Massages, beauty and Ayurveda treatments can be booked daily until 21:00. Wellness & Landhotel Prinz is a great base for exploring the beautiful mountain countryside of the Berchtesgadener Land region, 3 minutes from the A8 motorway.
