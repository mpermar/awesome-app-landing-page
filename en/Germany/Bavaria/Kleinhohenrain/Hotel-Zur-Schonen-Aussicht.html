Description

Located near peaceful woodlands, this guest house is surrounded by idyllic lakes and offers remarkable views of the Southern Bavarian Alps.

Come stay in the delightfully appointed rooms in the heart of the untouched countryside.

Discover one of the well-marked hiking paths and renowned Kreuzweg trail. Take a plunge in the beautiful lakes, lie on the meadows and relish the fresh air.

After an action-packed day outdoors, unwind in the shady beer garden and enjoy a cool beer as you marvel at one of Bavaria's most stunning vistas.

The guesthouse is an ideal base for excursions. Among the attractive destinations, you might opt for Chiemgau and its beautiful lakes, Kufstein, Innsbruck or Salzburg.

In fine weather, you can admire a vast mountain panorama ranging from the Salzburg Alps to the Inn and Mangfalltal valleys all the way to the Zugspitze.
