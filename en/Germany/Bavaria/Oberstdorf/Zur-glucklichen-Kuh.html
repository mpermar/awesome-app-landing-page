Description

Zur glücklichen Kuh is set in Oberstdorf, 350 metres from the Audi Arena and 350 metres from Nebelhornbahn Valley Station.

The inn has a sun terrace and views of the mountain, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Each room is equipped with a shared bathroom. Guests will receive the Walser Card, which offers reduced entrance to 200 other attractions.

Nebelhornbahn Station Seealpe is 2 km from Zur Kuh, while Soellereckbahn is 3.
