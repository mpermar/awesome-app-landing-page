Description

This 4-star hotel in Oberstdorf features free Wi-Fi, an on-site swimming pond sourced from the surrounding mountains, and a free shuttle to the Nebelhornbahn Cable Car, 6 km away.

Alpe Dornach hotel offers homelike rooms, suites and apartments decorated in an Allgäu Alpine style.

Each one includes satellite TV, a minibar, and a balcony with panoramic views. A breakfast buffet can be booked at the accommodation.

The restaurant offers regional cuisine. Guests will find a swimming pool 6 km away.

Erdinger Arena Ski Jump Stadium is 7 km away. Free private parking is available on site at Alpe Dornach.
