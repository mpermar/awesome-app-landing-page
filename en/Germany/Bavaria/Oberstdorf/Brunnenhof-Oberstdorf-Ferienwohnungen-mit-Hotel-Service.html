Description

Located 922 metres high in the heart of the Allgäu Mountains, these family-run apartments offer a fully equipped kitchen, traditional Bavarian restaurant and modern spa area with swimming pool and sauna.

Apartments at the Ferienappartementhaus Brunnenhof are individually furnished in a country or Alpine style and feature a satellite TV.

A balcony or terrace offers scenic countryside views. A typical Allgäu breakfast buffet awaits guests each morning.

On Mondays and Thursdays, Bavarian specialities can be enjoyed in the rustic-style restaurant, with live music and dancing.

The Brunnenhof offers a fully equipped fitness centre and massage treatments. A children’s playground is available on site.

The Brunnenhof provides free tickets for the Kleinwelsertal Mountain Railway (May - November).

The area surrounding the Ferienappartementhaus is ideal for outdoor sports, walking and cycling.
