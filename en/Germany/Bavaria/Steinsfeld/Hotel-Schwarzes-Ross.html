Description

Surrounded by meadows and forests, this traditional hotel in Steinsfeld offers Franconian cuisine and Bavarian beers.

Rothenburg ob der Tauber is just a 5-minute drive away. Hotel Schwarzes Ross was built 200 years ago and has a wooden framed exterior.

Each room has a modern bathroom with shower. Free Wi-Fi is available in all areas of the Schwarzes Ross.
