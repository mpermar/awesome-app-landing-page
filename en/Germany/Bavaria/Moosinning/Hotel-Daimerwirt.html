Description

This family-run hotel offers well-equipped rooms with free Wi-Fi. Hotel Daimerwirt is located in Moosinning, 12 km from Munich Airport and 25 km from the Munich Exhibition Centre.

All rooms at Hotel Daimerwirt include flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Guests can enjoy traditional Bavarian hospitality and a daily breakfast buffet.

The Daimerwirt's restaurant features traditional vaulted ceilings. It serves a selection of light meals and hearty Bavarian specialities (every day except Wednesdays).

Parking is free at Hotel Daimerwirt. It is 4 km from the Therme Erding (thermal baths) and 6 km from the Heimatmuseum Erding (local history museum).
