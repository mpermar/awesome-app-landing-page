Description

Small guests will appeciate a children's playground and play area with building blocks and toy computers. Guests of all ages can enjoy the Fun Area with table football, table tennis, and a TV with games console.

Rooms at the Ibis Styles Regensburg include a flat-screen TV, desk and private bathroom with a shower. Guests enjoy free WiFi and complimentary landline calls within Germany.

A breakfast buffet is prepared each morning. Antipasti and fresh drinks are served in the Italian-style Volare bistro with its large terrace.

Private parking is available at Ibis Styles Regensburg for an extra charge. The hotel is 1,000 feet from the A93 motorway.
