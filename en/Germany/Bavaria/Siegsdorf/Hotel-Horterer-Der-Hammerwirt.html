Description

On the site of an 18th-century tavern, the Hotel Hörterer der Hammerwirt provides rooms in the main hotel building, the renovated blacksmith’s or the annex next to the lake.

Each room at the Hammerwirt features wooden furniture, cable TV and a bathroom with hairdryer. Most rooms include a terrace or balcony.

Large breakfast buffets, meals and Bavarian snacks are served in the Hörterer der Hammerwirt’s restaurant. In warm weather, guests can eat in the shady beer garden.

High-quality rental bicycles, mountain bikes, and electric bicycles can be rented at the Hammerwirt.

The Hotel Hörterer der Hammerwirt is 4 miles from the A8 highway. Lake Chiemsee is a 15-minute drive away and Salzburg can be reached in 30 minutes from the hotel.
