Description

Newly built in 2012, this modern hotel offers free Wi-Fi, spa facilities and 2 restaurants.

Panoramahotel Oberjochis located just 3 km from the Austrian border near various ski slopes.

Each modern room at Panoramahotel Oberjoch features a satellite TV with SKY sport channels and en suite bathroom with a bathrobe and free toiletries.

Highlights include a balcony with views of the Allgaü Mountains A daily breakfast buffet is offered to guests.

Guests can enjoy a variety of international and regional Bavarian cuisine at the hotel’s restaurants, including a 5-course dinner in the Panorama Restaurant.

Cocktails are served at the hotel bar and can be enjoyed on the terrace. The hotel’s extensive Alpin Spa has 5 saunas, a steam room, and massage services.

Guests can exercise in the gym or in the indoor and outdoor pools. Panoramahotel Oberjoch offers direct access to the Oberjoch ski slope, and during the summer the mountains are ideal for hiking.
