Description

Located in the the town of Bad Hindelang, the family-run Hotel Zum Senn offers comfortable rooms at the heart of the Bavarian ski district.

Free WiFi internet access is available in public areas of the hotel. All rooms at Hotel Zum Senn include a TV, and have a bathroom with complimentary toiletries.

They also offer a view of the mountains. The hotel is ideally situated for hiking, cycling and skiing, with ski storage space and bike rental available.

Guests can also relax in the hotel sauna which can be used free of charge. A hearty buffet breakfast is provided each morning, and the hotel restaurant serves a range of evening meals and cheese specialities.

Free private parking is available on site at the hotel, and guests can make use of a complimentary Bad-Hindelang-Plus card which gives free use of local transport and attractions.
