Description

Surrounded by forest and mountains, this family-run hotel in Lohberg lies just 3 km from the Czech border.

It offers a sauna, a sunny garden with terrace and a restaurant. The classically furnished rooms at Hotel Pension zur Linde include satellite TV, a desk and panoramic mountain views.

Many of the rooms also include a balcony. Hearty Bavarian cuisine is served in the traditional-style restaurant with wood-paneled walls.

Guests can also order a buffet breakfast. Hotel Pension zur Linde is situated 10 km from the Großer Arber Mountain and 5 km from the Kleine Arbersee Lake.

Hiking routes also start directly at the property. It is 6 km to Lam Train Station and free on-site parking is provided.
