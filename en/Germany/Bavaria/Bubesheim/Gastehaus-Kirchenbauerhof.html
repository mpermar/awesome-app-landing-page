Description

Located in Bubesheim, Gästehaus Kirchenbauerhof offers accommodation with a seating area and a kitchenette.

Legoland theme park is 6 km away. The apartments at the country house come with a toaster, a coffee machine, a fridge and kettle.

There is a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries in each unit, along with a hairdryer.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning at the property. Gästehaus Kirchenbauerhof offers a children's playground and a paddock with horses.

In summer, children can play in the paddling pool. The property also features a terrace.

The area is popular for cycling, and the Danube cycling path passes near the property.
