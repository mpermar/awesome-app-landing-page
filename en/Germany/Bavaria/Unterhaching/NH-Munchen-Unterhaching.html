Description

This 4-star hotel offers large suites with an american bar. It is located in the quiet town of Unterhaching, a direct S-Bahn ride from Munich's city centre and main train station.

The bright non-smoking rooms of the NH München Unterhaching include a separate lounge area, minibar and satellite TV.

Free Wi-Fi is available in the entire hotel. A wide range of drinks and snacks are available in the hotel's bistro, and the bar which is open 24 hours a day.

Situated just off the A8 motorway, the NH München Unterhaching is less than 20 minutes from the new exhibition grounds.
