Description

Surrounded by the Oberpfalz countryside, this guest house offers an outdoor hot tub and horse riding facilities.

It is located in Hirschbach, a 2-minute drive from Königstein. Free Wi-Fi and private terraces are available to all guests at the Landgasthof Zum Elsabauern.

All rooms include a refrigerator, while the apartments feature living rooms and fully equipped kitchens.

Guests can enjoy regional specialities in the cosy dining area or outside in the beer garden.

The family-run Zum Elsabauern provides a breakfast buffet each morning, and also offers barbecue facilities.

Children will enjoy the Landgasthof’s playground and trampoline, while adults can relax in the sauna or steam room.

The guest house organises guided cave tours, canoeing and archery. Amberg and the A6 motorway are 30 minutes away.
