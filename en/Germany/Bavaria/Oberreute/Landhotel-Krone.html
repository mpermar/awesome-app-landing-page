Description

Situated in Oberreute in the Bavaria Region, 30 km from Oberstdorf, Landhotel Krone features a children's playground and water sports facilities.

The hotel has a sauna and a ski school, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Free WiFi is provided throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Rooms include a flat-screen TV. Some units include a seating area where you can relax.

Certain units feature views of the mountain or garden. Each room has a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The spa area includes a heat cabin and an infrared cabin. This hotel has ski storage space and bike hire and car hire are available.

You can play tennis at the hotel. Lech am Arlberg is 42 km from Landhotel Krone, while Oberstaufen is 6 km from the property.
