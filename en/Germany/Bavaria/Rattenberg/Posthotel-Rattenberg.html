Description

The hotel’s strategic location between Straubing-Gäuboden and the Bavarian Forest and immediate proximity to the Sankt Englmar skiing area offers great activities throughout the year. The modern spa center has professional cosmetic treatments and health facilities.

The restaurant serves tasty cuisine.
