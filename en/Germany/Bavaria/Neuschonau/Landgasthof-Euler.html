Description

This family-run, country hotel is situated in the heart of Neuschönau, in the Bavarian Forest region.

The animal park National Park and Hans-Eisenmann House information center are located 1 km away.

Offering cosy en-suite rooms, the 3-star Landgasthof Euler provides an ideal holiday base in the Bavarian Forest low mountain range.

You can go hiking and cycling through the forest, ice skating or even swimming in local lakes.

Wi-Fi can be booked in all areas of the Landgasthof Euler. In the mornings, look forward to a rich breakfast buffet.

Treat yourself to traditional cuisine, including a range of Bavarian specialities, prepared from local produce.

Guests staying at the Landhof Euler can make use of the hotel terrace, and park for free at the hotel.
