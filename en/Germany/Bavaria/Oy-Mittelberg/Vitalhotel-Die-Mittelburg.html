Description

This 4-star hotel is located in the village of Mitterberg. It features a modern spa with indoor swimming pool, sauna area and a gym with a panoramic view.

The rooms at the Vitalhotel Die Mittelburg are elegantly furnished. All rooms include feature satellite TV, a work desk and a balcony offering a view of the surrounding Allgäu countryside.

A breakfast buffet is provided each morning at Die Mittelburg. Guests can enjoy Swabian specialities in the traditional lounge or on the south-facing terrace.

The Mittelburg is a 5-minute drive from the Grüntensee Lake, Rottachsee Lake or the A7 motorway.
