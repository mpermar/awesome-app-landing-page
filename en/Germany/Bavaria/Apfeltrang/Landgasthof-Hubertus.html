Description

Located in the village of Apfeltrang in the Bavarian countryside, this family-run, country-style hotel offers free internet, regional cuisine and an outdoor swimming pool.

The rooms in the Hotel Landgasthof Hubertus are furnished in a style typical of the Allgäu region.

All rooms feature a private bathroom, cable TV and high-speed internet connection.

A breakfast buffet is served every morning at the Landgasthof Apfeltrang. The traditional restaurant also specialises in a wide range of Bavarian food.

Parking is free at the Landhof Hubertus. Ebenhofen Train Station is just 4 km away.

The town of Kaufbeuren and peaceful Lake Bärensee are less than a 15-minute drive.

At the Landgasthof Hubertus guests can enjoy the hotel’s 2 skittle alleys, traditional beer garden and children’s playground.
