Description

This traditional Bavarian hotel offers country-style rooms, regional cuisine and a large beer garden.

It is by Buchenhain S-Bahn Station, a 20-minute train ride from Munich city centre.

The non-smoking rooms of the Hotel Waldgasthof Buchenhain include a TV, radio and private bathroom.

Wi-Fi internet is available in some rooms and in public areas. A rich breakfast buffet is served each morning.

The restaurant offers typical Bavarian dishes and seasonal specialities for lunch and dinner.

Guests are welcome to dine and relax in the Waldgasthof’s beer garden. There is also a small playground for children and a boutique gift shop.

The surrounding Forstenrieder Park and the Grünwald Forest are ideal for hiking and cycling, while Oktoberfest is 17 km away.
