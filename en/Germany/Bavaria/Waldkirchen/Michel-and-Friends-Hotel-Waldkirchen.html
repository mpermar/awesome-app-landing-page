Description

Enjoying a beautiful location overlooking the spa town of Waldkirchen, in the Bavarian Forest, this 3-star Superior hotel features 2 restaurants and spacious rooms with a terrace or balcony.

The rooms at Michel & Friends Hotel Waldkirchen are between 29 m¬≤ and 56 m¬≤ in size, and are beautifully decorated in a traditional Bavarian country-house style.

Relax in the fitness room or sauna area, or outside on the sunbathing terrace. Guests enjoy free access to the Badepark Karoli pool complex next door with its indoor and outdoor swimming pools and water slides.

A bathrobe can be provided for a surcharge. Begin your day's activities with an energetic buffet breakfast which is included in the room rate.

The Michel & Friends is a family-friendly hotel, and the family rooms accommodate up to 2 adults and 3 children.

Younger guests will also appreciate the outdoor children's playground and the large indoor playroom with toys, a reading area, table football, a pool table and video games.

Situated at an altitude of 635 metres on the Caroli mountain, the hotel is an ideal base for sporting activities all year round.
