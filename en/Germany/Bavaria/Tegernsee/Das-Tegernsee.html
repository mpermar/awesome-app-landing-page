Description

One of our top picks in Tegernsee.

This hotel stands within a grand castle estate overlooking Tegernsee Lake, and offers a wellness area, outdoor and indoor pools and a sauna. Guests enjoy free Wi-Fi and free parking.

Das Tegernsee offers rooms within the Art Nouveau-style Sengerschloß castle and in its neighboring buildings. All rooms include a modern bathroom with bathrobes and hairdryer.

The hotel's Senger restaurant serves regional and international dishes. In warm weather, guests can eat on the terrace overlooking the lake and the Alps. Das Tegernsee also features a beer garden and a Baroque hall available for events.

Guests can book professional massages and beauty treatments in Das Tegernsee’s spa. Rental bicycles and a large children’s playground are also available.

The Sengerschloß castle on Das Tegernsee’s grounds has hosted personalities including Count Metternich and the Queen of Spain.
