Description

Hotel Blankhof garni is located in Bad Endorf. Free Wi-Fi access is available. Each room here will provide you with a TV.

At Hotel Blankhof garni you will find free shuttle service from the train station, a garden and barbecue facilities.

Other facilities offered at the property include luggage storage, ski storage and a children's playground.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling, fishing and hiking.
