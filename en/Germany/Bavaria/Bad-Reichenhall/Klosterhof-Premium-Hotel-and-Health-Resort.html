Description

Klosterhof, Premium Hotel & Health Resort in Bayerisch Gmain is set at the foot of the Reichenhaller mountain range, just a 15-minute drive from Salzburg.

It features a spacious spa area with saunas and steam rooms, a swimming pool, fitness room and a unique floating pool The bright rooms are air-conditioned and come with a flat-screen TV, a safe and all rooms also come with a balcony.

Guests can enjoy the beautiful mountain view from the room. At the Klosterhof's own restaurant "Genussart", you can enjoy traditional Bavarian hospitality,The hotel enjoys a long-standing reputation for delicious Alpine cuisine.

Free live music events as well as art and culture lectures take place regularly at the hotel.

The building offers historical features including the monastery tavern with original masonry and vaulted ceilings.

Guests can also book professional massages, beauty treatments and yoga in the spa area.

Guests may joy the free activity programme with aqua gym, fitness classes and yoga.

The Klosterhof is a 15-minute drive away Salzburg. Nearby mountains include the Lattengebirge, the Unterberg and the Hochstaufen.
