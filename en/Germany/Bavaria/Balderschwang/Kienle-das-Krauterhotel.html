Description

Set in the picturesque Alps, Kienle - das Kräuterhotel in Balderschwang offers a wide range of facilities including a sauna, a steam bath and free ski storage.

The cross-country ski trails and ski slopes are 50 metres away. Each room at Kienle - das Kräuterhotel features a seating area, a private bathroom with a bath or shower.

Guests can enjoy the beautiful mountain view directly from the rooms. Free WiFi access is available in the restaurant and around the reception desk.

A rich breakfast and hearty regional dishes for lunch are served at the restaurant on site.

In the evenings, guests enjoy a 4-course meal with 3 different choices for the main course.

Half board is provided. Guests can enjoy the spa area on the 3rd and 4th floor, which consists of a sauna, steam bath and infrared cabin.

A wide selection of activities including cross-country skiing, cycling, hiking and Nordic Walking can be enjoyed in this area.

The ski lift is just 400 metres away from the property, This property offers free parking.
