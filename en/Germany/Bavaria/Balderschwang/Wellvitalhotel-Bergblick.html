Description

Offering a picturesque location in the mountains, just 1. 5 km from the Austrian border, this hotel offers a swimming pool and a wellness area.

Wellvitalhotel Bergblick includes a buffet breakfast. Renovated in November 2016, rooms at Wellvitalhotel are warmly decorated, featuring floor-to-ceiling windows and views of the mountains.

Each one includes a seating area and a private bathroom with bathrobes. The country-style restaurant serves regional cuisine, and drinks from the bar can be enjoyed on the sunny garden terrace.

After dinner, guests can also relax with a massage. Wellvitalhotel Bergblick offers ski storage, and a free shuttle bus to the ski slopes is also provided.

Cross-country skiing lessons are available on site. The hotel features a wild game reserve and forest on the property.
