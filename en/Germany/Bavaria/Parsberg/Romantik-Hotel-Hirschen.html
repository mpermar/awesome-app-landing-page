Description

The family-run 4-star hotel is situated in the centre of the Bavarian town of Parsberg.

The Romantik Hotel Hirschen encompasses the original main building, the garden house, suite house and a conference centre.

The traditional main building from 1850 houses the restaurant areas as well as some of the rooms.

It also contains the sauna and beauty area, which includes a Finnish sauna, steam room, coloured light chamber and cosy relaxation area.

The country-style breakfast buffet and regional dishes are served on the cosy Wirtsstuben restaurants.

Gourmet cuisine and fine wines are served on the hand-finished tables of the Hirschkönig restaurant.

The 600 m² garden spa is situated in the garden house and features a Finnish sauna, infra-red sauna, ice fountain, multi-sensory shower, indoor pool with counter-current system, wellness garden, sun terrace and many relaxation areas.

The rustic-style Hirschenblobb bistro with beer garden serves Bavarian tapas with in-house meat products and specially brewed Hirschenblobb beer.

In the suite house, situated in a quiet location between the original house and the garden house, you will find creatively designed suites with south-facing balconies or terraces providing a splendid view of the private garden.

The Culinarium has a large dining room and open kitchen which can be booked for exclusive use, providing a private atmosphere, for example for families.

In the separately located conference centre you will find 6 sophisticated, high-tech air-conditioned seminar rooms lit by daylight and with darkening options and garden access.
