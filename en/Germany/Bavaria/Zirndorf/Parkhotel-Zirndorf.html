Description

This family-friendly hotel offers free WiFi and free private parking. It is located beside a peaceful forest in Zirndorf, a direct train ride from Nuremberg.

The rooms at the Parkhotel Zirndorf include a private bathroom and TV. Family rooms are available for those travelling with children.

A breakfast buffet can be enjoyed here every morning. The hotel also has a bar. Zirndorf Kneippallee railway station is a 7-minute walk from the hotel.

Nuremberg can be reached by train in 30 minutes or by car in 20 minutes. The Playmobil FunPark is 10 minutes away by car.
