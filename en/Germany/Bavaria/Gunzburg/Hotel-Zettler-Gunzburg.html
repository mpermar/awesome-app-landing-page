Description

Just 2. 4 km from the A8 motorway and 15 km from the A7 motorway, this family-run 4-star hotel is centrally located in Günzburg.

Hotel Zettler offers well-equipped conference rooms for 6 - 60 people. Legoland theme park is a 10-minute drive from the property.

Private parking is available on site. Free WiFi is offered throughout the property.

The Hotel Zettler offers bright and modern rooms. A sauna, steam bath and infrared cabin are available.

The small fitness room has direct access to the hotel garden. A healthy breakfast is offered each morning at the hotel.

In the restaurant and on the terrace, guests can enjoy regional and seasonal cuisine in the evenings.

A children's menu is also available. Children benefit from a large playground in the hotel's garden and a toy corner.

Guest can visit the many lakes in the surrounding area. The forest outdoor swimming pool is 1.

6 km from the property. Günzburg train station is 1. 5 km away. Ulm is 30 km from Hotel Zettler Günzburg.

The nearest airport is Stuttgart Airport, 102 km from the property. 80 Parking spaces are available directly by the property.
