Description

This charming 4-star hotel enjoys an attractive setting within the historic Old Town of Günzburg, close to the River Danube and the Legoland theme park.

With its convenient access to the A7 and A8 motorways, the Vienna House Easy Günzburg is an ideal base for holidaymakers and families exploring the local area.

Vienna House Easy Günzburgl is a 10-minute drive of the Legoland theme park. Other convenient day-trip destinations are Augsburg Zoo or the Atlantis leisure baths in Ulm.

A rich breakfast buffet is served each morning. The hotel bar is open 24 hours a day.

Guests at the Vienna House Easy Günzburg can use the underground car park at the hotel.
