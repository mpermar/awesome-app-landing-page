Description

Ferienwohnung Ebersbach is located on the outskirts of Günzburg, offering free WiFi, a TV and a terrace with a BBQ.

There is also a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher and an oven. Featuring a shower, the private bathrooms also come with a hairdryer.

You can enjoy the garden view and children's play equipment. Local restaurants, supermarkets and bakeries are located within a few minutes drive.

Activities available nearby include hiking and bike riding. At Ferienwohnung Ebersbach you will find barbecue facilities and free parking.
