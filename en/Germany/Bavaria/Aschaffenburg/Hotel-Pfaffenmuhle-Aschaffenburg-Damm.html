Description

Located just off the A3 motorway in Aschaffenburg, this welcoming hotel offers soundproofed rooms with free Wi-Fi.

The city’s sights and the City-Galerie shopping centre are within walking distance.

Rooms at the Hotel Pfaffenmühle Aschaffenburg include a fridge, cable TV, and en-suite bathroom.

A rich and varied breakfast buffet is included in the room rate. Various shops, restaurants and pubs can be reached on foot from the Hotel Pfaffenmühle Aschaffenburg.

An ideal base for hikes and bike rides through the Bavarian Spessart Nature Park, the hotel offers secure bike shelters as well as free parking for cars and buses.
