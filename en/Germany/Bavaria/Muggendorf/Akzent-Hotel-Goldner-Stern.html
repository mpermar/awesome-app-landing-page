Description

In the popular holiday region of the Franconian Switzerland, within the triangle formed by Nuremberg, Bamberg and Bayreuth, you find the Hotel Goldner Stern.

Boasting an idyllic setting in the historic city centre of Muggendorf, surrounded by fascinating landscape, it offers a magnificent panoramic view.
