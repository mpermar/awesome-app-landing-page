Description

Landgasthof Zum Stern is situated in Oberthal, a 10-minute drive from the centre of Hammbelburg.

The family-run hotel offers free WiFi access, a garden and a skittles alley. Each of the rooms at the hotel comes equipped with a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

Some rooms feature a TV. A fresh breakfast buffet is available each morning at Landgasthof Zum Stern, and the on-site restaurant serves a range of regional and international specialities.

The hotel also makes its own wine and schnapps on the premises. Popular activities in the surrounding area include hiking, cycling and skiing.

Other facilities available at the hotel include a sauna and luggage storage facilities.
