Description

This traditional guest house is set in the heart of the Allgäu region, less than a 10-minute drive from the village of Pfronten.

It offers a regional restaurant, children’s playground and free WiFi access. Gasthof Fallmühle features bright rooms decorated with classic-style, solid wooden furnishings.

Each quiet room has a work desk and a private bathroom. Most have scenic views of the Allgäu Alps.

The rustic-style restaurant serves freshly prepared specialities from the region and locally brewed beer.

A healthy, fresh breakfast can be provided on request. Nearby attractions include Falkenstein castle ruins (10.

5 km) and the famous Neuschwanstein and Hohenschwangau castles (20 km). Winter sports enthusiasts will find the nearest ski lifts are 5 minutes’ drive away.
