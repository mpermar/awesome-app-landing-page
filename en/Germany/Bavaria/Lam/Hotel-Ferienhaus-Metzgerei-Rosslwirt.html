Description

This family-run hotel is situated right next to the cure facilities in the spa town of Lam, in the Bavarian Forest.

Come and stay in the Gasthof Rösslwirt for your sense of well-being. You will appreciate the tranquillity of the region, and the warmth and friendliness of the locals.

Be sure to try some Bavarian delicacies in the hotel restaurant. Meat dishes are the house speciality due to the hotel’s own in-house butcher’s.

Linger on a while on the hotel terrace, or relax in the hotel garden. Guests of the property can use the indoor and outdoor swimming pool Osserbad in Lam free of charge.

The hotel’s central position makes it ideal for a whole range of leisure pursuits in the beautiful surrounding countryside.
