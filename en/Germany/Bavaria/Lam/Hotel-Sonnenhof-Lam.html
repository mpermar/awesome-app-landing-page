Description

Beautifully located on a south-facing hillside in the Bavarian village of Lam, this magnificent 4-star hotel boasts excellent leisure facilities, including an indoor and outdoor pool, beauty farm and sauna area.

Hotel Sonnenhof Lam is set in dense forest in the Upper Bavarian Forest nature park, and provides a scenic and relaxing base throughout the year.

Most rooms have a balcony or terrace offering a view of the Lamer Winkel, an area of beautiful natural countryside.

Dine in style in the in-house Petrusstube gourmet restaurant, or in the garden restaurant.

Order a drink in the cocktail lounge, pool bar or on the summer terrace. From 07:00 to 22:00 each day, Sonnenhof guests enjoy use of the heated outdoor pool, indoor pool, loungers, Finnish sauna, biosauna, steam room, hot tub and outdoor tennis court.

The solarium, beauty treatments, massages, tennis hall, squash courts and bicycle rental are available at an additional charge.

The Hotel Sonnenhof Lam has its own on-site golf academy and 9-hole golf course. The hotel organises various entertainment programmes for guests of all ages.
