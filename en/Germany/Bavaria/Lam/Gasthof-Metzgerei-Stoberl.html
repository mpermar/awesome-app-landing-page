Description

This hotel in Lam, with its own butcher shop, has been run by the same family for over 300 years.

It offers a traditional restaurant serving meals made with homemade products. The rooms at the Gasthof Metzgerei Stöberl can all be accessed by lift and include a flat-screen TV.

Many rooms have a balcony and panoramic views of the Osser and Arber Mountains. Free Wi-Fi internet is available in all areas.

Other facilities at the Stöberl include a sauna and massages. Guests also receive discounted use of the facilities at the Osser-Erlebnisbad Spa.

The Gasthof Stöberl serves a large breakfast buffet each morning. In the evening, Bavarian specialities are prepared with meat from the Stöberl butcher shop.

Vegetarian meals are also available. Guests can enjoy free scenic bus rides through the Bavarian Forest.

The bus stop is just 100 metres away. The Großer Arber ski region is 15 km from the hotel.
