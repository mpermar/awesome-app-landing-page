Description

Offering an outdoor and an indoor pool, as well as free use of the spa and wellness centre, sauna, hammam, hot tub and fitness centre, Hotel Herzog Heinrich is located in Arrach.

Free WiFi access is available. Rooms at Hotel Herzog Heinrich are brightly decorated and spacious.

They each include free linen and towels, a satellite TV, and private bathroom with a shower or bath and a hairdryer.

Guests can enjoy traditional cuisine in the restaurant, as well as order breakfast to the room and packed lunches for day trips.

Room service and a buffet dinner are also available. Additional facilities include a children’s playground, bar, billiards, table tennis, massage services for an additional charge, a garden featuring a sun terrace, a shared lounge area, games room and library.

Hotel Herzog Heinrich is located directly next to Arrach Train Station. The Czech border is a 15-minute drive away and the A3 motorway can be reached in an hour by car.
