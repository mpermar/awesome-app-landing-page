Description

This family-run hotel offers free Wi-Fi, free parking and a terrace with mountain views.

It is located in Aschbach, in the foothills of the Bavarian Alps. The rooms at the Aschbacher Hof include natural wooden furniture and a private bathroom with hairdryer.

Some rooms feature a balcony with mountains views. International and Bavarian meals are served in the Aschbacher Hof’s dining areas.

Homemade cakes are available in the afternoons. The breakfast room, conservatory and summer terrace all offer views of the Alps.

Aschbacher Hof is a 5-minute drive from the Mangfall Valley golf club. Many hiking and cycling trails can be found nearby.
