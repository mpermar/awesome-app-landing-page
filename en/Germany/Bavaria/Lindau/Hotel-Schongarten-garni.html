Description

Just 2 km from Lake Constance, this 3-star superior hotel garni enjoys a scenic location in Lindau.

Hotel Schöngarten Garni is set in a charming building and features a large garden and rich buffet breakfasts.

The family-run Hotel Schöngarten Garni offers soundproofed rooms with contemporary furniture.

Each is complete with a cable TV and a modern-style bathroom. A German-style breakfast buffet is served each morning.

Guests can also dine in the bright and spacious conservatory. The garden features a comfortable seating area with sun chairs, as well as a small playground.

Active guests will find that Hotel Schöngarten Garni makes the ideal base for hiking and cycling excursions.

Parking is free and the Lindau-Aeschach train station is just 700 metres away. Guests may also rent bikes from the property.
