Description

This 3-star-superior hotel enjoys a scenic location on the Auerberg Mountain in Bavaria.

Guests at the Panoramagasthof enjoy free Wi-Fi and 360° panoramic views of the Allgäu Alps.

The family-run Panoramagasthof auf dem Auerberg offers brightly furnished rooms with elegant décor.

All rooms include a flat-screen TV, CD player and a private bathroom. A buffet breakfast is provided each morning.

Regional Bavarian cuisine is served in the bright conservatory restaurant or on the terrace.

The Panoramagasthof is an ideal base for hiking or cycling in the scenic Bavarian Alps.

Lake Forggensee is 15 km away. Parking is free at the Panoramagasthof Bernbeuren.

It is a 35-minute drive from the A7 motorway, and also from Neuschwanstein and Hohenschwangau Castles.
