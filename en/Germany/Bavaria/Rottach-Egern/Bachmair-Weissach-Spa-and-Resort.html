Description

Just a 3-minute drive from scenic Lake Tegernsee, this design hotel in the Bavarian town of Rottach-Egern offers elegant rooms, gourmet food and a spa with large swimming pool.

Decorated in neutral tones, all rooms and suites at Bachmair Weissach Spa & Resort feature elegant interiors and high-quality furnishings.

Rooms are complete with comfortable beds, free WiFi and a modern bathroom. Guests can enjoy rich breakfast buffets.

Traditional Bavarian food is served in the Stuben lounges. The 3000 mÂ² wellness area including a Japanese styled spa with hot and cold water basins, steam room and outdoor pool.

Furthermore this property offers a family spa including an indoor and outdoor pool, an organic sauna, children pool and relaxing room.

Guests are welcome to relax in the lounge with fireplace, or on the summer terrace, which overlooks the chapel in the hotel park.

A large program sport and activities as hiking, stand-up paddling and horse riding are offered.

There is also a Kids Club for children older than 2 and guests have exclusive access to the hotel owned Bachmair Weissach Beach at Lake Tegernsee.

Guests will also receive the TegernseeCard, which offers free public transport and reduced entrance to several attractions.
