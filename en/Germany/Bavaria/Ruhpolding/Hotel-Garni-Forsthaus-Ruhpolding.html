Description

Set in the picturesque Bavarian countryside, this traditional hotel enjoys a central location in Ruhpolding.

It offers spacious accommodation with free WiFi access. Hotel Forsthaus Ruhpolding features bright rooms with classic-style furnishings and charming views of the garden and Bavarian Alps.

Each is complete with a TV and a private bathroom. Some rooms have a balcony. A hearty buffet breakfast is included in most room prices at Hotel Forsthaus Ruhpolding.

Activities such as hiking, skiing, rock climbing and golfing can be enjoyed at any time of the year in Ruhpolding.

Guests receive the eXtra Urlaubspass card, which provides discounts and free entry to a variety of local attractions.
