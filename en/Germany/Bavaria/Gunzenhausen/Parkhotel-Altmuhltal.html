Description

The Parkhotel Altmühltal is located in the Franconia surrounded by lakes and being one of the most beautiful holiday destinations in South Germany.

Free Wi-Fi internet is available throughout. There is a restaurant, a bar, a swimming pool with sauna, cosmetic facilities and conference rooms.
