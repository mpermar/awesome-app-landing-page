Description

Quietly located in a large garden, this 3-star Superior hotel in Schönau am Königssee offers a heated indoor pool and Bavarian cuisine.

Berchtesgaden and Lake Königssee are a 10-minute drive away. The rooms at the family-run Stoll’s Hotel Alpina are furnished in a traditional Alpine style.

Most have a balcony with a view of the Berchtesgaden Alps. Guests can also relax in the sunbathing area with playground.

The restaurant at Stoll’s Hotel Alpina serves a range of regional and international dishes.

A half-board option with a 3-course menu and vegetarian options can be booked upon arrival.

Other facilities include the sun terrace and the lounge with fireplace. Stoll’s Hotel Alpina is a 20-minute drive from Salzburg.

Nearby attractions include the Hintersee, Königsee and Obersee lakes and Salt Mine Berchtesgaden, as well as the its National Park (5 km).

Mountain Obersalzberg and its Eagle's Nest are in a 10-minute drive, while mountain Jenner and its cable car and slopes are 4 km away.
