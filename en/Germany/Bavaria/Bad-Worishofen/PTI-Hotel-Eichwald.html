Description

Offering a sauna, a terrace, and a garden, PTI Hotel Eichwald is located in Bad Wörishofen.

The hotel is 1. 1 km from Bad Wörishofen Thermal Spa, and the rooms include a private balcony.

These classically decorated rooms are bright and spacious and include carpeted floors and wooden furnishings.

Each one features a desk, flat-screen TV, and a safety deposit box. A full breakfast buffet is provided every morning, and the restaurant serves classic German and regional specialities in the evenings.

A supermarket is located 1 km from the property. Popular activities in the area include hiking and cycling, and there is a mini golf course 3 km from PTI Hotel Eichwald.

The Sebastian Kneipp Museum is reachable in 12 minutes on foot. Bad Wörishofen Train Station is 850 metres from the hotel, and the A96 motorway is a 10-minute drive.
