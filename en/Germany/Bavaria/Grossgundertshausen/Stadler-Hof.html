Description

An outdoor swimming pool, free rental bicycles and spa area are offered by the family-run Stadler Hof.

Set on spacious grounds, it is centrally located in Bavaria’s hop-growing region.

Decorated in neutral tones, the spacious apartments at Stadler Hof feature country-style furnishings.

Each is equipped with a satellite TV and a balcony. Downstairs in the bright and welcoming dining room, a hearty buffet breakfast made with local produce is served daily.

Meals can also be prepared in the apartment’s well-equipped kitchen. The on-site games room, children’s playground and table tennis facilities will keep younger guests entertained throughout the day.

Guests wishing to relax can enjoy the sauna, steam room and massage facilities. Free parking is provided.

Rohrbach Train Station is 13 km away and transfers to Münich Airport (35 km) can be organised on request.
