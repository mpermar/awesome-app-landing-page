Description

This 3-star hotel offers free Wi-Fi. It is located in Bamberg's old town, just a few steps from Bamberg Cathedral.

All of the Hotel Weierich's non-smoking rooms feature cable TV and a private bathroom with hairdryer.

A large breakfast buffet is available each morning. Various local and international restaurants are located nearby.

Many popular attractions are within walking distance of the Hotel Weierich, such as the Altes Rathaus (Old Town Hall), the Dominikanerkloster (Dominican Monastery) and the River Regnitz.
