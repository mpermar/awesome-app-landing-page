Description

Just a 15-minute drive from Legoland Germany, these family-run apartments are located in the Swabian town of Burgau.

Set in a historic building, they offer a leafy garden and spacious accommodation.

Bed & Breakfast Burgau features bright, individually decorated rooms with homelike interiors.

Each non-smoking apartment is complete with a fully equipped kitchen and a satellite TV in the living area.

WiFi is available for an additional charge. Breakfast is served in Bed & Breakfast Burgaus rustic-style dining room.

Freshly baked bread rolls can also be brought directly to your apartment each morning.

After an eventful day, the garden is the perfect place to relax. Free private parking is provided and drivers will find the A8 motorway is just 4 km away.
