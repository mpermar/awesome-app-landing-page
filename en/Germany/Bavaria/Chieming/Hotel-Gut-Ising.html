Description

Located on a 170-hectacre estate in Chieming-Ising, the 4-star Hotel Gut Ising offers well-equipped rooms, 3 restaurants and a large spa area with indoor and outdoor pools.

The Chiemsee Lake is just 1 km away. Spread across 8 buildings, the Hotel Gut Ising’s rooms have satellite TV, a minibar with free soft drinks, and a modern bathroom.

The Goldener Pflug restaurant serves German food and international favourites. The Il Cavallo features a summer terrace overlooking Lake Chiemsee and serves Italian cuisine.

Alpine specialities can be enjoyed in the Usinga gourmet restaurant. Leisure facilities at the Hotel Gut Ising include a sauna, a gym and a private 9-hole golf course, as well as a riding school and polo club.
