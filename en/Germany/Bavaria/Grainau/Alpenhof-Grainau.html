Description

Scenic views of the Alps, a spa with large indoor pool, and traditional Bavarian cuisine are offered by this 4-star hotel in Grainau.

It has a beautiful garden terrace and free Wi-Fi. The Alpenhof Grainau has modern rooms decorated in a country style.

They feature a private balcony or terrace, a flat-screen TV, and a cosy seating area.

Bathrobes, slippers and a free bottle of mineral water are provided. The spacious spa area at the Alpenhof Grainau includes different saunas, a steam grotto, and a beauty salon.

Fitness equipment and massages are also available. Bavarian specialities and international dishes are served in the Grainau Alpenhof’s restaurant with traditional wood-panelling.

The pretty garden terrace offers great mountain views. Free rental bicycles are available at the Grainau Alpenhof.

Ski passes and tickets for the mountain railways can also be bought here. The ski resort of Garmisch-Partenkirchen is only 6 km away.
