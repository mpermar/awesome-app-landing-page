Description

The family-run hotel offers panoramic mountain views, an indoor swimming pool and a traditional restaurant.

It is located at an altitude of 900 metres in the Eastern Allgäu Alps, 1. 5 km from Nesselwang.

All of the Bavarian-style rooms at the 3-star Landgasthof Löwen feature high-quality wooden furniture, satellite TV and a private bathroom.

Free Wi-Fi is available in all public areas. The spa at the Löwen includes a sauna and relaxation area.

Massages and beauty treatments are available. The hotel garden has a view of the Alps.

Dating back to the 19th century, the Landgasthof Löwen's restaurant prepares a breakfast buffet and serves a selection of Bavarian, Swabian and modern dishes.

Guests can also try homemade cakes and coffee specialities in the hotel restaurant.

The Löwen is a short drive from Füssen, Kempten and the Austrian border. Parking is free, and the motorway is just 1 km away.
