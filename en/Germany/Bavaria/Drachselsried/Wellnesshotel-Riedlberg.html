Description

At the hotel, every room includes a desk and a flat-screen TV. Free WiFi is available to all guests, while selected rooms also offer a balcony. Guest rooms are equipped with a seating area.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at the property. The restaurant at Wellnesshotel Riedlberg serves Austrian and European cuisine.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool. Skiing is among the activities that guests can enjoy near Wellnesshotel Riedlberg.

Bodenmais is 2.5 miles from the hotel, while Sankt Englmar is 13 miles away.
