Description

Featuring golf courses, several restaurants and bars and a 1800m² spa with swimming pools, this Four-Star-Superior-Quellness & Golf Resort Hotel is located next to Bad Griesbach’s pretty Spa Gardens.

Das Ludwig features several restaurants serving hearty Bavarian dishes. Guests can enjoy drinks in the stylish bar or on the terrace, and a breakfast buffet is provided in the cosy parlour room.

Opening in April 2011, Ludwig offers country-style rooms with modern amenities. Each room features a flat-screen TV, balcony and minibar, and bathrobes are provided in the private bathrooms.

Ludwig Hotel’s large spa offers a range of beauty treatments, and has both an indoor and outdoor pool.

The resort also features a well-equipped gym. Bad Griesbach offers many cycling and hiking trails.
