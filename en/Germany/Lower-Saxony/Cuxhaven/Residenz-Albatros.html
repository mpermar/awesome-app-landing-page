Description

Located on the outskirts of Cuxhaven, Residenz Albatros is just 100 metres from the sandy North Sea shore and guests can enjoy free use of beach chairs as well as bicycles.

There is a wellness area in the building. This 2-bedroom apartment includes a living area with a sofa, iPad and a flat-screen satellite TV with a DVD and Blu-Ray player.

Bathrobes, slippers and a hairdryer are included in the bathroom. Guests are welcome to prepare meals in the well-equipped kitchenette, which includes a dining area, oven and coffee machine.

Meals can also be enjoyed outside on the furnished balcony. Guests are welcome to use the wellness facilities in the building, which include a pool, fitness centre, hammam and sauna.

The area is ideal for beach walks and cycling. Residenz Albatros is 5 km from the Alte Liebe harbour platform and an 8-minute drive from the Cuxhaven Train Station.
