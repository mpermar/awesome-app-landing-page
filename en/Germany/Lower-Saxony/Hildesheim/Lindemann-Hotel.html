Description

This hotel is located in a quiet road with no through-traffic in an industrial estate in the Drispenstedt district of Hildesheim, 2.

5 km from the city centre. It offers modern rooms, inclusive breakfast and good links with the A7 motorway.

The Lindemann Hotel's rooms include free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV and an en suite bathroom.

Guests are welcome to use the Lindemann Hotel's bright conservatory and the outdoor terrace.

A park is also located just steps away from the hotel. The nearby bus stop is a 7-minute walk away provides a fast link to the city centre.

The Messe Hannover trade fair is a 15-minute drive away from the Lindemann Hotel, while Hannover Airport is just a 40-minute drive away.
