Description

Set in Hodenhagen, directly in Serengeti Park, the Serengeti Park Resort provides a restaurant.

Complimentary WiFi is provided throughout the property. Fitted with a terrace, the units feature a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with shower.

A fridge and kettle are also featured. A buffet breakfast can be enjoyed in the breakfast area.

The lodge offers a children's playground. This property offers a terrace. Hannover Airport is 33 km from the property.
