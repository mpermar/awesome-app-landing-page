Description

Only 13 km from the Messe Hannover Exhibition Centre, this guest house in Bolzum offers traditional-style accommodation with satellite TV, free Wi-Fi in the lobby, and a small garden.

The family-run Landhaus Bolzum has spacious rooms and apartments with light wooden furniture and a work desk.

Non-smoking rooms are available here. Rich buffet breakfasts featuring homemade cake are prepared in the Bolzum’s bright conservatory.

In the evenings, guests can relax in the country-style bar. Rental bicycles are provided at the Landhaus on request.

Different hiking and cycling routes are located nearby. Sehnde town centre is only 2 km away from the Bolzum Landhaus.
