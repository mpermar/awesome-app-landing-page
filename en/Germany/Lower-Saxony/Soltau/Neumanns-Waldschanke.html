Description

Situated in Soltau, within 4. 1 km of Outlet Soltau and 7 km of Heide Park Soltau, Neumanns Waldschänke features accommodation with a restaurant and free WiFi throughout the property as well as free private parking for guests who drive.

All rooms boast a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom. The hotel has family rooms.

At the hotel, the rooms are fitted with a wardrobe. Guests at Neumanns Waldschänke can enjoy a buffet or a à la carte breakfast.

The accommodation offers a children's playground. Guests at Neumanns Waldschänke will be able to enjoy activities in and around Soltau, like cycling.

Soltau Spa is 2. 6 km from the hotel, while Heidewitzka is 1 km from the property.
