Description

Rooms at the Steigenberger Bad Pyrmont are at least 269 ft² in size. Some rooms have a balcony overlooking the grounds.

Restaurant Palmengarten serves international and Lower Saxon meals. Steigenberger's Kurparkterrasse offers snacks, cake and direct views of the spa. A breakfast cafe and piano bar are also available.

Certified by the German Wellness Assocation, the spa at the Steigenberger Bad Pyrmont's includes a pool, hot tub, a range of saunas and large grounds. The hotel offers rental bikes for exploring the surrounding Weserbergland Schaumburg-Hameln Nature Reserve.
