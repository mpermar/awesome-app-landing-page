Description

Free Wi-Fi and a breakfast buffet are offered at this hotel, located just 500 metres from Alfsee Lake.

The hotel offers water sports facilities on site as well as a car and bicycle rental service.

Rooms at Ringhotel Alfsee Piazza are brightly decorated and feature a flat-screen TV and sofa bed.

They also have a private bathroom with hairdryer. In addition to a daily breakfast, the restaurant at Ringhotel Alfsee Piazza serves traditional Lower Saxon dishes as well as other European cuisine.

Guests can also use the on-site barbecue facilities or enjoy a drink from the bar on the hotel’s terrace.

Guests can access the hotel’s tour desk for further information on surrounding attractions.

Hiking, fishing, and mini golf facilities are within a 10-minute walk from the hotel.

In addition guests can enjoy spa facilities in Alfen Saunaland, like sauna, cosmetics and massages.

Ringhotel Alfsee Piazza is 2 km away from the Rieste Train Station and 8. 5 km from the A1 motorway.
