Description

Offering free WiFi, this family-run hotel is set on the banks of the River Weser, just 4 km from Hannoversch Münden’s Old Town.

The surroundings of 4-star Flux-Biohotel Werratal boast hiking opportunities. Some of the rooms are fitted with a balcony and/or a TV.

A buffet breakfast is available every morning. The on-site restaurant serves organic meals, made with fresh seasonal produce from the region.

The Werra valley is a good base for cycling tours for both beginners and experienced cyclists.

The A7 motorway is 4 km from the hotel. Kassel city centre is only 30 minutes away.
