Description

This 4-star superior hotel is situated in the town of Weyhausen, less than 10-minutes drive from Wolfsburg and only a 6-minute drive from Volkswagen AG.

Braunschweig-Wolfsburg Airport is a 30-minute drive away. The 4-star Best Western Hotel Alte Mühle offers comfortable, individually furnished rooms, with modern amenities, including complimentary wireless internet access.

At the Alte Mühle’s rustic Kornkammer and Mühlenstube restaurants, expect delicious culinary treats, complemented by an exquisite wine from the list.

After dinner, relax with a drink in the cosy bar with open fireplace. Enjoy the surrounding countryside, or visit Wolfsburg – with its art museum, the Phaeno science adventure centre and the historical Wolfsburg castle, you’ll be sure to have plenty to do.
