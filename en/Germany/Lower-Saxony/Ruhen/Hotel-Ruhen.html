Description

Hotel Rühen offers bright rooms with free Wi-Fi, free parking and daily breakfast buffets.

It is located in the small town of Rühen, a 10-minute drive from Wolfsburg. The Rühen’s rooms include satellite TV and a work desk.

Spacious bathrooms with showers are provided. Drinks are served in the Hotel Rühen’s modern bar.

Guests can enjoy hiking and cycling in the nearby Drömling Nature Park. Wolfsburg’s Volkswagen Factory is just a 10-minute drive away.
