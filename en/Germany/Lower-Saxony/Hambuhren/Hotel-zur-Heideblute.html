Description

Surrounded by scenic green landscapes in the town of Hambühren, this 3-star, family-run hotel is located just 10 kilometres from historic Celle, and a convenient 35-minute drive from Hanover.

The Hotel zur Heideblüte features cosy rooms. Many are renovated with modern furniture.

Free WiFi internet is available at the property. The à la carte restaurant serves a selection of regional specialities.

In sunny weather, guests can relax in the traditional beer garden. Guests can also enjoy popular outdoor activities, such as hiking, jogging and cycling in the Hotel zur Heideblüte’s immediate vicinity.

The popular Serengeti Park attraction is a 30-minute drive away. Free parking is available on site.
