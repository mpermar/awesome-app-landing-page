Description

At the hotel, each room comes with a desk, a TV and a private bathroom.

Breakfast is available each morning, and includes buffet and American options.

Voss, das Schokoladenhotel offers a range of wellness facilities including a hot tub, a sauna and a hammam. Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Westerstede, like cycling.

Oldenburg is 14 miles from Voss, das Schokoladenhotel, while Bad Zwischenahn is 6 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Bremen Airport, 38 miles from the hotel.
