Description

The Hotel Braunschweiger Hof invites you to experience 4850 ft² of pure relaxation. Look forward to the heated pool, Roman steam bath and Finnish sauna, or enjoy the invigorating cosmetic treatments available.

Golf fans will enjoy the hotel’s special arrangements with the Bad Harzburg golf club.

The hotel has its own estate with ancient trees where you can relax after an active day outdoors.
