Description

This historic-style country hotel is located in the rural Wangerland region, 3 km from the North Sea Coast.

Zum Alten Krug provides traditional Frisian-style rooms and apartments with free Wi-Fi.

The country-style rooms and apartments at Zum Alten Krug are individually furnished.

All rooms include satellite TV and a private bathroom, and the apartments feature a fully-equipped kitchenette and living area.

A German-style breakfast is served with coffee or Frisian tea in the hotel's breakfast room or outside on the terrace.

A gluten-free breakfast may also be served at the request. Dogs are allowed in some of the rooms.

Guests can go hiking, cycling or Nordic walking in the surrounding Lower Saxon countryside, or explore the UNESCO Wattenmeer Nature Park along the North Sea coast.

The spa resort of Horumsiel is 5 km away, and the German Marine Museum is 20 km away in Wilhelmshaven.
