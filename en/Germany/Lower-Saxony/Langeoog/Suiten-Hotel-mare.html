Description

A spa area, outdoor swimming pool, 2 restaurants and cocktail bar are offered by this modern hotel.

Centrally located in Langeoog, it is just a 5-minute walk from the sandy shores of the North Sea.

Featuring rich carpets, contemporary furniture and large windows, rooms and suites at Suiten Hotel Mare are decorated in a classic-style.

All rooms come with a small kitchenette and some also include a balcony or terrace.

Freshly caught fish and North Sea specialities are served daily in the Restaurant BunteKuh.

The maritime-inspired Navigator’s Lounge Bar is the ideal place to enjoy fine wines and exotic cocktails.

Visitors can rent a bike for the day and explore the East Frisian Island. Massages and beauty treatments can also be enjoyed in Suiten Hotel Mare’s peaceful spa area, and guests can also relax by the outdoor pool and waterfall.

The wellness area is only for guests aged 16 and above. Free internet access is available in the hotel reception, as well as in all hotel rooms.
