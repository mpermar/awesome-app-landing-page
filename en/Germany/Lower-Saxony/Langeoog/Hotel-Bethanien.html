Description

This hotel is located on the East Frisian island of Langeoog, a 5-minute walk from the North Sea coast.

The hotel Bethanien features a garden and terrace with sun loungers and beach chairs.

The hotel Bethanien offers bright rooms with classic-style décor. All rooms include a TV and a private bathroom with shower, and Wi-Fi can also be booked.

Breakfast, lunch and dinner buffets are served in the restaurant. The hotel also has its own traditional East-Frisian tea room, and guests are also welcome to relax in the TV lounge or in the children’s playroom.

Rental bikes are available for exploring the island of Langeoog. Leisure baths are located just 200 metres from the hotel, and there is a golf course within 2 km.
