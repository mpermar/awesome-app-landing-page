Description

Facing the beachfront in Tossens, Hotel Am Friesenstrand features a shared lounge and a garden.

There is a terrace and guests can make use of free WiFi and free private parking.

The units at the hotel come with a seating area. At Hotel Am Friesenstrand all rooms come with a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet breakfast. You can play table tennis at Hotel Am Friesenstrand, and the area is popular for hiking and cycling.
