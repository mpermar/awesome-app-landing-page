Description

Situated just a 15-minute drive from Hanover, these holiday apartments and chalets enjoy a peaceful location beside the Arnumer See (lake), where guests can enjoy fishing.

The Appartementanlage am Arnumer See lies in the Arnum district of Hemmingen. It offers inviting accommodation with free WiFi, cooking facilities, a TV, and free parking.

Go hiking or cycling through the surrounding conservation area, or take the local bus to the centre of Hanover for a sightseeing trip.

The exhibition grounds are just 6 km away. Within walking distance you find a supermarket and various sport and leisure facilities, including indoor/outdoor swimming pools, golf and tennis.

At the end of a busy day, choose from a wide range of cuisine in the on site restaurant with beer garden.
