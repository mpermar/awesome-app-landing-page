Description

Situated in Neuharlingersiel, 1. 7 km from Ferry Terminal Neuharlingersiel, DJH Resort Neuharlingersiel features a restaurant and free WiFi throughout the property.

The resort has a sauna and water sports facilities, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Free private parking is available on site. The rooms include a flat-screen TV. Certain rooms include a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

You will find a coffee machine in the room. Each room comes with a private bathroom.

A rich breakfast, as well as lunch and evening meals are offered at the restaurant.

Guest can have coffee and cake after lunch. There is a shared lounge, babysitting service with baby food and gift shop at the property.

A range of activities are offered for children and adults at the property. The surrounding area is suitable for sports as windsurfing and cycling.
