Description

The 4-star Hotel Okelmann's is ideally located in the Nienburg district of Warpe, just 50 km south of Bremen.

It offers a restaurant and free WiFi. Each of the bright rooms at the accommodation is decorated in a modern style with natural materials and includes a small seating area, flat-screen TV and minibar.

Featuring a bath or shower, bathrooms have free toiletries. Homemade cakes and gifts are available in Café Heimatliebe.

Guests can enjoy traditional, regional cuisine in the cosy restaurant. At Landgasthaus Okelmann you will find a garden, barbecue facilities and a terrace.

Other facilities offered include a games room, a children's playground and shops (on site).

The surrounding area is ideal for jogging, Nordic walking and cycling. Guest house staff can provide tips on local routes.
