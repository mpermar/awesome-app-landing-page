Description

Ferienhof Westerwarf is set in Norden, 7. 5 km from Norddeich. It features a barbecue hut, a campfire area and a children's playground.

Children can enjoy the various animals at the farm, such as dogs, cats, horses, goats, guinea pigs and bunnies.

Most apartments were renovated in 2018. Each unit comes with a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher, a seating area, a flat-screen TV, a washing machine, and a private bathroom with shower.

An oven and microwave are also featured, as well as a coffee machine. The property offers an indoor playroom for children.

Guests can also rent electric and normal bikes on site. The dyke is 350 metres away.
