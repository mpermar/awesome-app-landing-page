Description

The family-run Hotel Luisenhöhe in Walsrode is located next to the world’s largest bird park, the Weltvogelpark Walsrode.

The hotel offers well-equipped rooms, a Mediterranean-style restaurant and a spa area.

Every room at the Hotel Luisenhöhe has satellite TV and a modern bathroom. Wi-Fi is free in all areas.

A varied breakfast buffet is prepared each morning at the hotel. In the evenings, international food and German specialities are served in the Restaurant Ums Mühlrad.
