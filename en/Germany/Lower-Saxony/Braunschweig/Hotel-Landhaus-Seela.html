Description

This family-run hotel in the Riddagshausen Nature Reserve offers rooms with flat-screen TVs and free parking.

The centre of Braunschweig and the A39 motorway are a 5-minute drive away. All rooms at the 4-star Hotel Landhaus Seela include minibars and some also have bathrobes.

Most rooms offer free WiFi, and some rooms feature a balcony. Regional and international food is served in the Klosterstube restaurant.

In the summer, guests can eat on Seela’s summer terrace overlooking Riddagshausen Abbey and Riddagshausen’s 3 large ponds.
