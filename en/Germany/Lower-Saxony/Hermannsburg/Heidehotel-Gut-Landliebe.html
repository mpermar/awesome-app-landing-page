Description

Boasting its own horse-riding facilities, including boxes for guest's horses, and an outdoor sauna complex, this quaint hotel in the town of Weesen, Hermannsburg, is an ideal holiday getaway.

Embedded in picturesque country side, Heidehotel Gut Landliebe offers peaceful, tastefully furnished accommodation in half-timbered, traditional buildings.

Free Wi-Fi internet can be accessed in the rooms and throughout the hotel. Wake up to the Gut Landliebe's delicious breakfast buffet.

In the evening, visit the restaurant to enjoy north German specialities, as well as tasty, innovative dishes.

During the warmer months, enjoy a hot drink in the terrace café or a refreshing beer in the beer garden.

Both have views over the lovely pond. The wonderful Südheide nature park is right on your doorstep.

With extensive woodland and open fields, it is a perfect area for horse riding, hiking and cycling.
