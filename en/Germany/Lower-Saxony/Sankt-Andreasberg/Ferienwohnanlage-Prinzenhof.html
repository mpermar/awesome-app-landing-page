Description

Surrounded by the Harz Nature Park, this quiet hotel offers an indoor pool, sauna and children’s playground.

Its modern apartments include a well-equipped kitchenette and free Wi-Fi. Completely renovated in 2010, the self-catering apartments at Ferienwohnanlage Prinzenhof each have a flat-screen TV.

They all have a balcony or terrace with garden furniture. Guests are welcome to relax in the common lounge, which has satellite TV, a DVD player and stereo.

Barbecue facilities are also available in the 1,500m² garden. Hiking trails, mountain bike routes and ski slopes can be found in the surrounding Harz Mountains.
