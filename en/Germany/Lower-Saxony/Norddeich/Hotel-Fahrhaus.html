Description

All rooms at Hotel Fährhaus come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a shower. Hotel Fährhaus provides certain units that feature a balcony, and the rooms come with a coffee machine. All guest rooms include a desk.

A buffet breakfast is served each morning at the property. There's an on-site restaurant, which serves German cuisine and also offers Vegetarian options.

Spa and wellness facilities including a sauna and a hammam are at guests' disposal during their stay at Hotel Fährhaus. Guests at Hotel Fährhaus will be able to enjoy activities in and around Norddeich, like cycling.

Ferry Terminal Norden is 1,500 feet from Hotel Fährhaus.
