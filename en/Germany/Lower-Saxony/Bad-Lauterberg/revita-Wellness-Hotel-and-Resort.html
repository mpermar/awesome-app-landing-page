Description

This quietly-located 5-star hotel features a large garden, a sun terrace, an indoor swimming pool, and a comprehensive spa area.

It is set in the forest in Bad Lauterberg, 29 km from the A38 motorway. Rooms at revita - Das Wellness Hotel & Resort offer free WiFi, a cable TV, a minibar, and a private bathroom.

Guests can also access free Wi-Fi in public areas. The hotel offers 7 different restaurants and bars, including a pool bar.

Room service is also available. From 2016, all stays include the indulgent 3/4-board option, consisting of breakfast, an afternoon treat and dinner.

revita - Wellness Hotel & Resort's spa area includes a sauna, hot tub, steam bath, and solarium.

Athletic guests can enjoy the hotel’s tennis courts or fitness centre, or go cycling or hiking in the surrounding area.
