Description

Set in the peaceful village of Trautenstein, the family-run Hotel Druidenstein offers comfortable rooms in the heart of Saxony-Anhalt.

Free Wi-Fi internet is available throughout the property. All rooms at Hotel Druidenstein are designed in classic style, and feature a seating area, cable TV, and have an en suite bathroom.

There is a hairdryer, complimentary toiletries, and some rooms also include a balcony.

There is an entertainment room, a café, and the surrounding countryside is ideal for hiking, cycling and skiing.

There are nearby trails for Nordic Walking, and ski storage is available on site.

A rich breakfast buffet is provided each morning at Hotel Druidenstein. Traditional Dutch and German specialities are served in the hotel's restaurant.
