Description

At See- und Waldresort Gröbern, every room comes with a closet and a flat-screen TV. Every room has a private bathroom with a hair dryer. Guest rooms have a seating area.

A continental or buffet breakfast is served at the property.

You can play ping pong at this 4-star resort, and the area is popular for cycling and hiking.

Leipzig is 24 miles from See- und Waldresort Gröbern, while Halle an der Saale is 25 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Leipzig/Halle Airport, 21 miles from the property.
