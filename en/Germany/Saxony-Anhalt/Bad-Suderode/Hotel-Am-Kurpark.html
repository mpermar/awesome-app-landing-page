Description

This 3-star-superior, family-friendly hotel and adjacent apartments lie just 100 metres from the centre of the spa town of Bad Suderode, in the Harz Mountains.

The Hotel am Kurpark offers bright and friendly accommodation to ensure that you have a relaxing break in these beautiful surroundings.

Choose between rooms, apartments or celebrate the most important day of your life by staying in the bridal suite.

Enjoy a range of delicious Harz specialities in the Hotel am Kurpark restaurant, and wash these down with local beers and fine wines.

During the day, homemade cakes and waffles, as well as homemade ice cream are on offer in the Spontan restaurant.

During warmer weather, guests are welcome to enjoy the terrace. The Hotel am Kurpark is ideally located for you to be able to explore local attractions on foot or via public transport which is available nearby.
