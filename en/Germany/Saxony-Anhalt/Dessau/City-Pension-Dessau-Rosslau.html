Description

This guest house is located in Dessau-Roßlau, less than 2 km from the town centre.

It offers modern rooms, free wired internet and WiFi, and good links with the A9 motorway.

Each room at the City-Pension Dessau-Roßlau has a private bathroom and cable TV channels.

Guests are welcome to use the Pension Dessau’s club room, which features an internet terminal.

Further facilities at the City-Pension include a washing machine and tumble drier.

The City-Pension Dessau is just 500 metres from the River Mulde and the Middle Elbe biosphere reserve.
