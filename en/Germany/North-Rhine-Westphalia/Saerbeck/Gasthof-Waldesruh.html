Description

This family-run guest house in Saerbeck offers traditional-style rooms, a garden, and traditional food from North Rhine-Westphalia.

Münster/Osnabrück Airport is only 3 km away. All of the rooms at the Gasthof Waldesruh have modern bathrooms.

Free Wi-Fi is available in the entire hotel. Children are welcome to have fun in the Waldesruh’s garden with a sandpit and trampoline.

Varied breakfasts are provided every day. Local and seasonal dishes are served in the Waldesruh’s country-style restaurant.
