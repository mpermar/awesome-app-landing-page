Description

Just a 25-minute drive from Weeze Airport, this family-run, country hotel in Nierswalde offers North Rhine-Westphalian cuisine, a spacious garden terrace, and easy access to scenic hiking and cycling routes.

Only 5 km from the Dutch border, the Nierswalder Landhaus has quiet rooms with satellite TV,a work desk and free WiFi Rich buffet breakfasts are prepared at the Nierswalder Landhaus.

The restaurant on-site serves regional dishes with a Mediterranean touch. Draught beers can be enjoyed in the Bier-Stube lounge.

During warmer months, the Nierswalder’s garden terrace is a great place to relax.

Homemade cakes, ice cream and freshly brewed coffee are served here. On request, the Nierswalder Landhaus Genuss-Hotel provides a shuttle to Weeze Aiport.
