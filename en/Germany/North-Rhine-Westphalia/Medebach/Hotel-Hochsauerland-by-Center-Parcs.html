Description

Located in Medebach, 6 km from Schlossberg Ski Lift, Hotel Hochsauerland by Center Parcs provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a bar.

Featuring room service, this property also provides guests with a water park. The property features an indoor pool, evening entertainment and a 24-hour front desk.

At the hotel, each room includes a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

Guest rooms at Hotel Hochsauerland by Center Parcs are equipped with a seating area.

The accommodation offers a continental or buffet breakfast. Hotel Hochsauerland by Center Parcs offers a children's playground.

The area is popular for skiing and cycling, and bike hire is available at the hotel.

The nearest airport is Kassel-Calden Airport, 68 km from Hotel Hochsauerland by Center Parcs.
