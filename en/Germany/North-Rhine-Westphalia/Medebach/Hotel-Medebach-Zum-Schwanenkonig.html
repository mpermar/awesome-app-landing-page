Description

Located just 15 km outside of popular Winterberg, Hotel Medebach offers classically furnished double rooms.

Guests are offered a sauna, a skittles alley on site, a balcony and free WiFi. Each traditional room at this family-run hotel includes a seating area, TV and desk.

The en suite bathroom comes with a shower and hairdryer. Guests are offered an optional breakfast buffet and are invited to relax at the hotel’s á la carte restaurant and bar in the evening.

A local highlight, the Castle Hill and high ropes course in Küstelberg are only a 8-minute drive away.

The surrounding countryside offers great routes for hikers and cyclists. Centre Parks Sauerland is also just 3 km away and boasts a large indoor pool.

The nearest train station is located in Korbach, 17 km away. Free private parking is available at the hotel.
