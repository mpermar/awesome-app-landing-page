Description

Set on a hillside in the scenic town of Monschau, this B&B offers home-like accommodation and an outdoor pool.

Guests can enjoy views over the city and the surrounding picturesque Eifel countryside.

Including free WiFi access, the rooms at Eleganza come with a seating area. Each includes a bathroom with a shower and/or bathtub and a hairdryer.

A rich breakfast is provided every day at Eleganza. Various dining options are available in town.

The Eifel region is ideal for activities such as hiking and cycling. The Belgian border is just 5 km away, and Aachen's Old Town is also just a 30-minute drive away.
