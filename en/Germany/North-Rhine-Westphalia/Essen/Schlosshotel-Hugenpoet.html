Description

This 5-star Superior hotel is a 17th century castle offering luxury accommodation, award-winning cuisine, and free parking.

It is located in Essen's south western district of Kettwig. The spacious rooms and suites of the Schlosshotel Hugenpoet feature both modern and antique furniture.

They all come with flat-screen TVs. Guests enjoy free entrance and transfer to the nearby fitness club and sauna.

Cosmetic treatments and a massage service are available in your room. The HUGENpöttchen restaurant is open daily.

Additionally, there is another restaurant, LAURUSHAUS open Thursday-Sunday. Tennis fans can enjoy a game of tennis on the hotel's outdoor court.

The nearby Ruhr river is ideal for cycling. Please note that Messe Düsseldorf is 19 km away while Messe Essen is 14 km from the accommodation.

A 20-minute drive will bring you to Essen city centre and Kettwig Stausee Train Station is 3 km away.
