Description

This traditional, timber-framed hotel in the Lennestadt countryside offers cottage-style rooms, free Wi-Fi, swimming pool and a traditional beer garden.

The Lenne River is a 2-minute walk away. All individually furnished rooms at the Landhotel Voss include a cable and satellite TV, seating area and bathrobe with slippers.

Balconies offer scenic countryside views. A rich breakfast buffet is served in the Landhotel's rooms or in the country-style restaurant, complete with fine, wooden furnishings and cheerful lighting.

Guests can enjoy regional specialities or relax with a book in the fireplace lounge with library.

Activities at the Voss include a sauna and massage treatments, games room, tennis court and children’s playground.

A safety deposit box, tour desk and room service are also available. The beautiful Sauerland countryside surrounding the Landhotel Voss Lennestadt is ideal for cycle tours and relaxing walks, offering over 120 km of sign-posted footpaths.
