Description

On the edge of the Xanten Hochwald Forest, these holiday homes are surrounded by a spacious cottage garden with a nature pond, playground and barbecue facilities.

The Rhine River is 6. 5 km away. All country-style apartments at the Ferienwohnung Haumannshof provide separate bedrooms, a living room and TV.

Covered with beautiful ivy on the outside, the inside features fine wooden beams and flooring.

Haumannshof offers a rustic-style dining area and fully equipped kitchen with fridge, kitchenware and tea/coffee facilities.

Upon request, the property can provide a breakfast basket upon arrival. Guests can relax on the terrace, play table tennis or sunbathe in the garden.

Rental bicycles are available for exploring the North-Rhine Westphalia countryside.

Xanten town centre is 7 km away. The Xanten Leisure Centre is 3. 5 km away, featuring steam baths, a high rope course, and water sport activities on the Xanten Nordsee and Südsee Lakes.
