Description

Quietly located in the town of Siebenstern, this family-run hotel sits right on the edge of the Gradberg Forest.

Haus Kanne features free Wi-Fi and an indoor swimming pool. The classic rooms feature satellite TV, a seating area and a wardrobe for storage.

The bathrooms are en-suite with a shower, and a hairdryer is provided. A buffet-style breakfast is served every morning in the dining area, and guests can relax in the bar in the evenings.

The restaurant serves German cuisine and a special dietary menu can be requested.

The Detmold open Air Museum is 35km from Haus Kanne. The town of Bad Driburg is 3km away and the Externsteine rock formations can be reached in 30 minutes by car.

Free parking is available and the A33 motorway is 29 km away. Bad Driburg Train Station is a 10-minute drive away with direct connections to Paderborn.
