Description

Offering international cuisine, modern rooms with free Wi-Fi, and free calls to German landline numbers, this 3-star Superior hotel is 900 metres from Oberhausen Train Station, in the Ruhr region.

The quietly located Hotel Residenz Oberhausen has simply decorated, spacious rooms with flat-screen TV, kitchenette, and free tea/coffee facilities.

Each room includes a cordless telephone, long desk, and large wardrobe. Guests can make free use of the billiards room, which features a TV and DVD player.

Varied breakfast buffets are available at the Hotel Residenz. German, Mediterranean, and Oriental dishes are served in the restaurant.
