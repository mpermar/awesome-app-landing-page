Description

Located beside the Jever Fun Skihalle Neuss indoor ski centre and the Kletterpark Neuss climbing range, this 4-star-superior hotel in Neuss offers modern wellness facilities, 2 restaurants, and free Wi-Fi.

Just a 20-minute drive from Düsseldorf city centre, the Hotel Fire & Ice has stylish rooms with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and a minibar.

The Fire & Ice’s unique spa area features a sauna overlooking the ski slopes. There is also a steam room and a fitness room.

Outdoor activities include fun football, available from April until October. Varied breakfast buffets, German cuisine and international dishes are served in the Fire & Ice’s restaurants.

Guests can relax beside the fireplace in the cosy lounge, while enjoying views of the ski slopes.
