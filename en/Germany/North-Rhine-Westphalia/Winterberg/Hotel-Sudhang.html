Description

Located in Niedersfeld on the northern edge of the Rothaargebirge nature reserve, this charming hotel offers cosy accommodation and easy access to many outdoor activities.

The rooms and apartments at the Hotel Südhang feature an en suite bathroom, comfy beds, and a balcony or a terrace.

Guests can also expect a complimentary breakfast each morning. Unwind in the Hotel Südhang's delightful fireplace room, or enjoy a freshing drink in the welcoming bar.

During winter months, the Hotel Südhang is an ideal base for winter sports fans. Nearby ski opportunities include Winterberg (8 km) and Willingen ski areas (21 km).
