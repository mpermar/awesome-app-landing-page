Description

Featuring a large spa area 'additional fees apply', Vakantiehotel Der Brabander is located just 500 metres from Winterberg’s centre, directly next to the popular ski resort.

Vakantiehotel Der Brabander features bright rooms with a flat-screen TV. Free telephone calls to Dutch landlines are offered by this Dutch-run hotel.

A hearty buffet breakfast and a variety of regional dishes are served in the hotel’s restaurant.

In the evenings, drinks are available at the bar. Der Brabander’s spa includes 4 saunas, a relaxation room, massage facilities and a peaceful garden, accessible for a fee.

Guests can work out in the modern gym or relax with a book in front of the cosy fireplace in the lounge.

The heated outdoor pool can be used in both summer and winter. From April to October, guests staying 2 days or longer receive a Sauerland SommerCard, offering free or reduced access to numerous tourist attractions.

Winterberg is great for winter sports, with 23 ski lifts located next to the hotel.
