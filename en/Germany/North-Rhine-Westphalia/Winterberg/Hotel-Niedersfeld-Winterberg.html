Description

This hotel is situated in Niedersfeld, 7 km from Winterberg town centre and a 5-minute drive from winter sports facilities.

Free WiFi is available in all areas of the hotel. Each of the rooms at Hotel Niedersfeld includes a seating area, TV and private bathroom.

The hotel also features a large garden. A fresh breakfast buffet is served every morning at the hotel, and is included in the room price.

The restaurant at Hotel Niedersfeld also serves a range of local and international dishes.

There is a spa and sauna at the hotel, which offers a range of beauty and wellness facilities, including massage and beauty treatments.

Other popular activities in the area include hiking, cycling, paintballing and go-karting.

It is a 25-minute drive to the A46 motorway and a 30-minute drive to Willingen. Free private parking is also available at Hotel Niedersfeld.
