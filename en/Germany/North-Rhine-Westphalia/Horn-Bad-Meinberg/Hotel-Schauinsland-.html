Description

This hotel is ideally located in the Teutoburg Forest, approximately 1 km from Bad Meinberg.

The country-style hotel offers free bicycle rentals and free Wi-Fi. Rooms at Hotel Schauinsland include a LCD TV, seating area, and private bathroom.

They also feature a balcony with garden views. Breakfast is served daily in the classic-style restaurant, which features German cuisine and seasonal dishes.

In the summer, guests can enjoy coffee and cake in the garden. Jogging and hiking are possible in the surrounding countryside.
