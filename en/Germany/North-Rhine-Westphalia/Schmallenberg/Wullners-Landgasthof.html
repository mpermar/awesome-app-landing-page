Description

Free Wi-Fi internet and free parking are offered at this family-run, country guest house.

It is in a half-timbered building in the centre of Oberhenneborn, surrounded by hilly countryside and hiking trails.

The quiet rooms at the Wüllner's Landgasthof have been decorated in a country-house style.

All rooms have handmade furniture made from wood from the region. The Wüllner’s restaurant serves a buffet for breakfast and regional dishes for dinner.

The tavern is open in the evenings, serving drinks and snacks. The terrace is open when the weather is warm.
