Description

Set in Schmallenberg in the North Rhine-Westphalia Region, 16 km from Rothaargebirge Nature Park, Landhaus Gnacke boasts two fully-furnished apartments.

Free WiFi is featured throughout the property. The property offers fresh bread delivery.

The apartments each feature a terrace, a dining area and a seating area with a flat-screen TV.

All apartments also include a kitchen equipped with a dishwasher and oven. A microwave is also provided, as well as a coffee machine and a kettle.

Every unit comes with a private bathroom with a hair dryer. Bed linen is offered.

Guests can also enjoy the fair weather on the sun terrace of the property. You can play mini-golf at the property, and the area is popular for skiing and golfing.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including horse riding and fishing.

Paderborn-Lippstadt Airport is 50 km from the property. Free parking spaces are available for guests.
