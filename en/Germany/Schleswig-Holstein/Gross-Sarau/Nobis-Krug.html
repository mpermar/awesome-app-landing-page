Description

Housed in a 300-year old thatched building, this family-run hotel features an on-site restaurant, and a terrace overlooking Ratzeburger See lake.

Canoe and bicycle rental are available at the Nobis Krug. The bright and simply furnished rooms and apartments at the Nobis Krug include a TV, and some have an en suite bathroom.

The hotel’s restaurant serves a menu including local Schleswig-Holstein specialities, freshly caught fish and German cuisine.

Breakfast is also available daily. Nobis Krug is just 250 metres from Ratzeburger See lake, where guests can try a variety of water sports.

The Baltic Sea coast is 25 km away. The hotel is situated on the Old Salt Route, and is 12.

5 km from the towns of Lubeck and Ratzeburg. Free private parking is offered on site at the Nobis Krug.
