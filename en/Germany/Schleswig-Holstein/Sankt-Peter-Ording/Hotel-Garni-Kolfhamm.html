Description

This hotel is situated directly at St. Peter-Ording's beautiful sandy beach and the X-H2O Water Sports Centre.

The Dünen Therme leisure baths are 2. 5 km away. The large rooms at the Kölfhamm Hotel feature a cosy living area, small desk and cable TV.

Bathroms are en-suite and come with a hairdryer and complimentary toiletries. The shops and restaurants in the district of Bad are just a 30-minute walk away.

Guests are also welcome to relax on the sunbathing lawn, and the famous Westerhever Lighthouse is a 1-hour cycle away.

Free parking spaces are available on site, and garages can also be booked for cars or surfing equipment.
