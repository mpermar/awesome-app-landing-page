Description

This family-friendly guest house in the North Sea resort of St. Peter-Ording is a 10-minute walk from the beach and town centre.

It offers rooms with internet and daily breakfast. The rooms of the Landhaus Ellerbrock are located in either the red or white building.

Each room includes cable TV and a seating area. Nearly all rooms have a balcony or terrace.

Guests are welcome to relax in the Ellerbrock’s garden with sunbathing area and beach chairs.

There is also a children’s play area. The Landhaus Ellerbrock is ideal for activities including cycling and beach volleyball.

Rental bicycles are available on request. The Westküstenpark Animal Park is just a 5-minute drive from the Ellerbrock.
