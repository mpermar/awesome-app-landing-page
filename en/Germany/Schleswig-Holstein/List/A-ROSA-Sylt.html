Description

Free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and DVD players can be found in all rooms at the A-ROSA Sylt. Bathrobes and slippers are provided.

The 3,500 m² large spa includes an outdoor pool, an indoor sea-water pool and various saunas. Guests can also work out in the gym, or book massages and beauty treatments. There is a stylish relaxation room looking out onto the North Sea across the dunes.

Mediterranean cuisine, seafood dishes and international food are served in A-ROSA Sylt's restaurants. There is also an on-site bar.

Rental bicycles are available for exploring the surrounding fields and sand dunes. List Ferry Port is 0.6 mi away and connects you to the Danish island of Rømø.
