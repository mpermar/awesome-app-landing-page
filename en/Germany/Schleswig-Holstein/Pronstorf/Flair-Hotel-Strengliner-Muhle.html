Description

This 3-star, country hotel between Bad Segeberg and Lübeck offers quiet rooms, spa facilities, and free Wi-Fi.

Regional fish dishes are available here. The family-run Flair Hotel Strengliner Mühle has spacious rooms with wooden furniture and modern bathrooms.

Each room includes a satellite TV and a delightful garden view. A varied breakfast buffet is prepared at the Strengliner Mühle at no extra cost.

The à-la-carte restaurant in the hotel conservatory offers a variety of seasonal dishes.

Guests are welcome to use the hotel wellness area and sauna throughout their stay.
