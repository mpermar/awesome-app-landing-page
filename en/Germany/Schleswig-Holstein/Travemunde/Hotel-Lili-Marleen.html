Description

Set in a historic 18th-century building, this boutique hotel offers maritime-themed rooms and cottages in the heart of Travemünde.

Features include free WiFi, seasonal cuisine and a garden with deckchairs. Featuring elegant interiors, the rooms at Hotel Lili Marleen include an en suite bathroom and satellite TV.

Some of the rooms come with a French balcony. Specialities from the north of Germany are served in the warmly decorated restaurant with wooden floors.

On sunny days, you can enjoy a drink from the bar on Hotel Lili Marleen’s terrace.

The Baltic Sea beach is 1 km from the property, while Travemünde Harbour is 300 metres away.
