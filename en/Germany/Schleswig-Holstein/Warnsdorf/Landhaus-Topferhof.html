Description

This 4-star, country hotel in the scenic village of Warnsdorf offers free wellness facilities, free Wi-Fi, and free rental bicycles.

The Timmendorfer Strand beach is just 3 km away. The Landhaus Töpferhof has bright rooms and apartments with cable TV and modern bathrooms.

Guests are welcome to relax in the Töpferhof’s sauna, steam room, and massage area.

The Landhaus Töpferhof also has a table tennis room. Pottery classes are available on request.

Big breakfast buffets are provided at the Landhaus every day. The hotel’s historic wine cellar offers fine international wines.

Guests staying at the Töpferhof get special golf discounts at the Maritim Golfpark Ostsee and Lübeck Travemünde Golf Club.
