Description

These apartments are located just 50 metres from the Baltic Sea Coast and a 10-minute walk from Timmendorfer Strand town centre.

The modern apartments at Appartment-Hotel Seeschlösschen each feature a large balcony or terrace with a view.

All have a kitchenette and a living room with flat-screen TV. Free Wi-Fi is provided.

A hearty breakfast buffet can be booked at the Seeschlösschen hotel, which is located across the road from these apartments.

Restaurants can be found just a 5-minute walk away. Guests are welcome to use the spa facilities in the Seeschlösschen hotel, which include a sauna, solarium and steam bath.

Guests can also relax in the garden, which has barbecue facilities. The beach is nearby on the opposite side of the street.

Timmendorfer Strand Train Station is 3 km away, and free parking is provided 300 metres from the property.
