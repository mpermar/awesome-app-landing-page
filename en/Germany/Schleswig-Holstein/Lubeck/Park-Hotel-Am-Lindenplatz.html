Description

The Park Hotel in Lübeck provides bright rooms and a daily breakfast buffet, within an Art Nouveau-style building.

Lübeck Central Station and the Holstentor Gate are less than 300 metres away. Each room at the Park Hotel Am Lindenplatz has a modern bathroom, satellite TV and Wi-Fi.

The staff at the Park Hotel’s reception can help make restaurant reservations and provide sightseeing information.

There is plentiful underground parking in the next to the hotel and a limited number of free spaces along the street.
