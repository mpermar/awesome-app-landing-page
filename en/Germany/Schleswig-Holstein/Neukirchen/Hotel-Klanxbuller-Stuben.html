Description

The Klanxbüller-Stuben offers country-style rooms, a continental breakfast and a restaurant serving international food.

It is located in Neukirchen, just a 10-minute drive from the Danish border. The rooms at the Hotel-Klanxbüller-Stuben have a TV and a private bathroom with shower.

The Klanxbüller’s restaurant offers local and international food, including pizza, pasta and Frisian dishes.

Lots of well-signposted hiking and cycling trails can be found next to the Klanxbüller.

Other nearby attractions include the island of Sylt and the Danish town of Tønder.
