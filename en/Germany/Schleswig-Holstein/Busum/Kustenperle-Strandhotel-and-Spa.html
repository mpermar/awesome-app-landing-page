Description

Featuring free WiFi, a restaurant and a sun terrace, Küstenperle Strandhotel & Spa offers accommodation in Büsum, 1.

4 km from Piraten Meer. The hotel has a spa centre and sauna, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Private parking is available on site. Every room has a flat-screen TV. All rooms have a seating area for your convenience.

Certain units include views of the sea or city. Every room includes a private bathroom.

Extras include bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries. There is a 24-hour front desk, gift shop, and shops at the property.

This hotel has water sports facilities and bike hire is available. The area is popular for windsurfing and cycling.
