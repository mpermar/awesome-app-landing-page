Description

Featuring a spacious spa area with an indoor pool and a modern fitness area, this elegant Sylt hotel is just 500 metres from Keitum town centre and a 15-minute walk from the Wattenmeer Nature Park.

Benen-Diken-Hof’s bright rooms are comfortably furnished with flat-screen TVs. The modern bathrooms have a hairdryer and toiletries.

Drinks from the minibar are included. Restaurant Kökken serves regional cuisine and a rich breakfast buffet.

Homemade cakes and can be enjoyed on the terrace or in Benen-Diken-Hof’s large garden.

In the evenings, guests can relax in the fireplace lounge with library. Massages and beauty treatments can be booked in one of the 5 treatment rooms at Benen-Diken-Hof.

Other features include saunas, a steam room and an infra-red cabin. Upon guests' arrival and departure the Benen-Diken-Hof offers a free shuttle to and from the airport and train station.
