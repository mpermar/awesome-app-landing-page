Description

At the family-run Weisser Hof hotel, you can look forward to tastefully furnished, cozy rooms with lovely views of the charming hotel grounds, and free wireless internet access.

The complimentary morning buffet offers a wide selection of breakfast fare, and in the evenings the restaurant serves delicious regional cuisine.

In the basement, you will find the Weisser Hof’s spa area, Oase des Lebens, where you can unwind in the sauna, Thelasso (seawater) bath, steam bath or with a relaxing massage.

More active guests can wish to work off that extra energy in the indoor pool or fitness room.

The terrace is the ideal place to enjoy an afternoon coffee and cake, and during the summer the hotel organizes barbecues here.
