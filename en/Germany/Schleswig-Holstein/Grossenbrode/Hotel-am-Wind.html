Description

This family-run hotel is directly on the beach promenade in the Baltic Sea resort of Großenbrode.

The garden features a sunbathing area and children’s playground. Parking is free.

All rooms at the Hotel am Wind include free Wi-Fi, satellite TV and a private bathroom.

Some rooms have a direct or side view of the Baltic Sea. The Hotel am Wind is near the yacht harbour, diving school, fishing school and surfing/sailing school.

The Fehmarn Sound Bridge is 3 km away. A rich buffet breakfast is provided each morning, and is enjoyed in the breakfast room which offers a view of the large hotel garden.

Großenbrode Train Station is a 20-minute walk from Hotel am Wind. Lübeck is a 50-minute drive away via the A1 motorway.
