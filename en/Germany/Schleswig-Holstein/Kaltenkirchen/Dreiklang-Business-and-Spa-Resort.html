Description

The country-style hotel is located in the heart of Schleswig-Holstein and close to the Hanseatic city of Hamburg.

Dreiklang Business & Spa Resort features free WiFi as well as a 7,200 m² spa area with 6 saunas and swimming pools.

The individually designed rooms feature a TV, a minibar and a seating area. The bathrooms are en-suite and come with a shower and a hairdryer.

Triad Business & Spa Resort's restaurant, pantry, serves German and international dishes.

Guests can enjoy a hearty breakfast, prepared every morning in the dining area. The scenic Holsteinische Schweiz, as well as the coasts of the Baltic and the North Sea can be reached in 45 minutes by car.

The spa centre which includes swimming pools, a hot tub and a solarium is open to the guests for a fee.

Free parking is available and the A7 motorway is a 2-minute drive away. Hamburg Airport is reached in 35 minutes by car, and Hamburg centre is a 45-minute drive away.
