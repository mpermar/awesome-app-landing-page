Description

Situated in the historic town of Lütjenburg, this hotel is just 7 km from the Baltic Sea Coast.

Hotel Ostseeblick offers a sauna, an indoor pool and bicycle rentals. A balcony and 2 arm chairs are included in individually furnished rooms at Hotel Ostseeblick.

Floor-to-ceiling windows and wooden furnishings feature throughout. Rich breakfast buffet are served on the terrace in the summer months.

The on-site Restaurant Bismarckturm offers excellent cuisine from the Holstein region.

The surrounding countryside provides the ideal base for hiking and cycling. Guests can also take a day trip to Lübeck (55 km) or Plön (20 km).
