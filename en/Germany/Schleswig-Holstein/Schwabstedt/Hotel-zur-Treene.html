Description

Offering a bowling alley, this family-run hotel is situated in the spa resort of Schwabstedt.

Regional cuisine is served in the restaurant, and rental bicycles are available. Wooden floors are featured in the bright rooms at Hotel zur Treene, and a hairdryer is provided in the private bathroom.

Each room comes with a TV, a radio, and a work desk, and some have a great view of the Treene Valley.

Season German specialities and fresh fish dishes are served in the elegant Zur Treene restaurant, which offers view of the River Treene.

Guests can explore the surrounding countryside through hiking and cycling trails, or guests can go fishing on the hotel’s small boat.

Husum Golf Club is a 20-minute drive from the hotel. Friedrichstadt Train Station is 10 km from the hotel, and free private parking is available.
