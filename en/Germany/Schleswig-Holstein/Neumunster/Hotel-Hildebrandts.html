Description

Set in a charming garden, this modern hotel is just a 5-minute walk from Neumünster town centre.

It offers free Wi-Fi access and free use of the Wellyou gym. Decorated in cheerful colours, the bright rooms at Hotel Hildebrandt’s feature contemporary furniture and colourful artwork.

Each room is complete with a flat-screen TV, complimentary hot drinks and delightful garden views.

A hearty buffet breakfast is served daily in the brightly decorated restaurant, and can even be enjoyed on the terrace during warm weather.

Cafés and restaurants serving European cuisine are less than a 2-minute walk away.

Animal lovers can take a trip to Neumünster Wildlife Park, which is 6 km from Hotel Hildebrandt’s.

Active guests will find the Wellyou gym is only 200 metres away. Guests also receive a 10 percent discount to the nearby McArthur Glen Designer Outlet Neumünster.

The A7 motorway is an 8-minute drive from the hotel and free private parking is available on site.

The property also provides a charging station for electric cars for a surcharge. Neumünster Train Station is situated 2 km from Hotel Hildebrandt’s.
