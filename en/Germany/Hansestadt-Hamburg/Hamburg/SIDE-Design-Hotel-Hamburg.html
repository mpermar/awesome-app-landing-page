Description

Completely renovated in 2018, rooms at the 5-star SIDE hotel feature an eco-friendly heating/cooling system. The suites offer a classic, modern design with wooden furnishings. A flat-screen satellite TV with sky channels and a bathroom are also included.

An international breakfast buffet is served daily. Quality cocktails and steak meals are served in the exclusive [m]eatery bar + restaurant which were renovated in the first quarter of 2018.

The SIDE Spa includes a gym, sauna and hot tub. Massages and beauty treatments can be booked. Guests can also relax in the spa's Sky Lounge on the 8th floor, which features a minimalist design.

SIDE is located in Hamburg’s Neustadt district, a 3-minute walk from the Hamburg State Opera and Gänsemarkt Underground Station. Private parking spaces and a public garage are available at the SIDE.

Hamburg City Center (New Town) is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, restaurants and city walks.
