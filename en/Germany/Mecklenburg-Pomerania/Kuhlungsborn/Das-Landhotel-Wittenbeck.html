Description

Landhotel Wittenbeck offers a spa with indoor pool, varied sports opportunities, and a conservatory restaurant specialising in fish.

It is set in a nature preserve near to the scenic Baltic Coast. Only 3 km from the Baltic Sea resort of Kühlungsborn and Heiligendamm.

Leisure activities at or near the Wittenbeck Landhotel include golf, bowling, and horse riding.

Rental bicycles are provided on request. Landhotel Wittenbeck has country-style rooms with cable TV and modern bathrooms including a hairdryer.

The elegant spa at the Landhotel features different saunas and a massage service.

Exclusive cosmetic treatments are on offer in the beauty salon. Free Wi-Fi is provided in all public areas, not to mention the skittle alley.

Rich buffet breakfasts and fine regional specialities can be enjoyed in the Wittenbeck’s spacious conservatory.

Guests can relax with a drink in the maritime-style Traditionsbar. The Ostsee Golf Ressort in Wittenbeck is 1 km away and Reiterhof 2 km.
