Description

Offering an indoor pool, a wellness centre, and free WiFi, Hotel Aquamarin is located in Kühlungsborn.

It is set just 100 metres from the sandy Baltic coast. All of the spacious rooms at Hotel Aquamarin feature a TV with cable channels, a seating area, and a bathroom with hairdryer and free toiletries.

Some rooms also include a balcony. Regional specialities and seasonal dishes are served at the hotel’s Bernstein Restaurant.

Guests can also enjoy a drink at the on-site Irish Pub, which also offers live music on Saturday evenings.

The spa area at Hotel Aquamarin includes saunas and a fitness centre. Massages and beauty treatments can also be booked for an extra charge.

Ostseebad Kühlungsborn West Train Station is 700 metres from the hotel, and Kühlungsborn Marina is 2.
