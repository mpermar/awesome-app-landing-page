Description

This 4-star hotel in Prerow offers a large garden, stylish spa, and scenic location in the Vorpommersche Boddenlandschaft reserve, beside the Darß Forest.

The beach is 1. 5 km away. The spacious rooms of the Hotel Bernstein Prerow have satellite TVs and balconies overlooking the garden, park, or forest.

All rooms feature a free bathrobe with slippers. The Hotel Bernstein’s Darßarium spa area features a heated garden pool and a modern gym.

Different massages and beauty treatments can be booked. Facilities for beach volleyball, tennis, and outdoor chess are available at the Bernstein.

Local and international meals are served in the Hotel Bernstein’s Garden Restaurant.
