Description

Just 300 metres from the Baltic coast, this family-run hotel enjoys a quiet location in the historic centre of Binz.

It offers spacious rooms with refrigerators and flat-screen TVs. Pension Sanddorn features modern seaside resort-style architecture.

Its bright rooms also feature safety deposit boxes, high-speed internet and private bathrooms with hairdryers.

A rich, fresh breakfast buffet is served daily in the Sanddorns large breakfast room.

In warm weather, breakfast can be enjoyed on the sun terrace. Binzs many shops, cafs and restaurants are just 150 metres from the Pension Sanddorn.

Tennis courts and rental bicycles are within 300 metres, and the hotel offers secure storage space for bicycles.

The Sanddorn is a great base for exploring the Rgen Nature Park. Granitzwald Forest and the Schmachter See Lake are great day-trip destinations.
