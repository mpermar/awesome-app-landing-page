Description

This accommodation offers apartments and holiday homes in the town of Stralsund. Some apartments offer sea views, and all are located within 300 metres of the sea and 3 km of the Old Town.

Boasting a fireplace, the apartments at Sonne am Sund offers a living room with a TV and CD player, as well as a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher.

Some of the apartments also come with a fireplace. Guests are free to use Sonne am Sund’s garden, which also has barbecue facilities.

There is a large variety of local cafés and restaurants in the Old Town. The UNESCO World Heritage Stralsund town centre is home to the popular Old Market Square (Alter Markt) and Gothic brick architecture.

The area is also ideal for sailing, fishing and hiking. Providing free private parking, the apartments are set a 30-minute drive from Greifswald and 3 km from the Rügen bridge.
