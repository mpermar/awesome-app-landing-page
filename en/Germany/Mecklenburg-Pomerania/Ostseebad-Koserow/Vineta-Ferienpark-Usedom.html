Description

Located in Koserow on the island of Usedom, Vineta Ferienpark Usedom offers an indoor pool, sauna and free WiFi access.

The sandy Baltic Sea beach is just a 10-minute walk from the property. Vineta Ferienpark Usedom features self-catering apartments with a balcony or terrace and a flat-screen TV.

The en suite bathroom includes a spa bath and shower. On-site facilities include a tennis court and beauty treatments.
