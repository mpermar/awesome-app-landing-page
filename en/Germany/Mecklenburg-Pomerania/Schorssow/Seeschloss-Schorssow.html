Description

This romantic hotel is located in a Baroque palace in Schorssow. Schloss Schorssow stands beside a lake in a large English garden and offers a spa with pool.

The spacious rooms at Schloss Schorssow include impressionist paintings, a minibar and a private bathroom.

Guests enjoy free WiFi in all rooms and public areas. Schloss Schorssow´s spa features a sauna, a steam room, massages and a pool.

Guests can also relax in the quiet library. Regional food is served in Schloss Schorssow’s gourmet restaurant and in the rustic wine cellar.

Local produce is used, including fresh fish and game. The hotel offers rental bikes for exploring Mecklenburg-Vorpommern’s countryside.
