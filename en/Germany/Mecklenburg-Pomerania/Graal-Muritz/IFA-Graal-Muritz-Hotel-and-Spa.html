Description

The 4-star IFA Graal-Müritz Hotel & Spa Tagungen has spacious rooms with minibars and satellite TV. Modern bathrooms with hairdryers are provided.

Regional and international cuisine is served in the Orangerie restaurant. Guests can enjoy drinks next to the fireplace in the Kaminbar. In the summer, the beach cafe offers ice cream and desserts.

Guests have free use of the IFA Hotel’s pool, gym and several saunas. Beauty treatments can also be booked. Guests can also simply relax on the large terrace.

The IFA Graal-Müritz offers rental bicycles for exploring the surrounding forests, the Rostock Heath and the Baltic coast. Guided tours of the Ribnitz Great Moor can be organized.
