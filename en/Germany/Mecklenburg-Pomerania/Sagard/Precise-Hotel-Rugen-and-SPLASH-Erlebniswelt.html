Description

This family resort hotel is situated in Neddesitz, near the Jasmund National Park on the island of Rügen.

Included in all rates are admission to the huge indoor playground, indoor pool, seasonal outdoor pool, river canal, kid's pool, steam showers, sauna, spa and fitness area.

The rooms at Precise Resort Rügen & SPLASH Erlebniswelt are located in 2 renovated building wings.

The Hofküche restaurant offers themed buffets and local specialities. Snacks and drinks are available at the Snack&Co Bistro.

The L’Osteria serves Italian cuisine, accompanied by Italian wines. Child care is available at Precise Hotel Rügen & SPLASH Erlebniswelt with its supervisors, playroom, and ball pool.

Lunch is possible here, as well as various activities in the afternoon. The Baltic Sea beach in Glowe is a short drive from the Precise Resort Rügen & SPLASH Erlebniswelt.
