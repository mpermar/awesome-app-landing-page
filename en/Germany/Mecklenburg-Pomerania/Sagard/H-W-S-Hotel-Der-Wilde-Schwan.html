Description

A skittle alley and sauna area are offered at this beautiful half-timbered hotel on the island of Rügen.

Der Wilde Schwan enjoys a scenic location, just 400 metres from the Jasmunder Bodden Lake.

H. W. S. Hotel Der Wilde Schwan consists of 3 half-timbered buildings (a main building and 2 apartment buildings) providing bright suites and apartments.

All accommodation in decorated with stylish Scandinavian-style interiors, wooden floors and a flat-screen TV.

Breakfast and a selection of Northern German specialities are served in the conservatory restaurant, which overlooks the Jasmunder Bodden Lake.

Guests are also welcome to relax in the lounge with its fireplace and small library, or in the pretty garden.

Younger guests will also enjoy the games room and children’s playground. On-site parking is free at the Wilder Schwan, and Sagard town centre is 3 km away.

There are many hiking and cycling routes here in the Jasmund National Park, and the famous white chalk cliffs along the Baltic Sea Coast are only 11 km away.
