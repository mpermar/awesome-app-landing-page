Description

Steigenberger Grandhotel and SPA is in a complex of grand buildings and villas connected by tunnels. Its air-conditioned rooms include large beds and flat-screen TVs. Guests can enjoy views of the park, beach promenade or Baltic Sea.

A breakfast buffet is available. German and international meals are served in the elegant restaurant or bistro with beach terrace. Guests can enjoy drinks in the Steigenberger’s wine bar or café-bar.

Heringsdorf’s famous pier is just under half a mile from the Grandhotel Steigenberger. Rental bicycles are available for exploring the Usedom Nature Reserve and the 7 miles Baltic Sea Promenade.

After a long day, guests can relax in the spa’s sauna and steam room.
