Description

This hotel is a historic farmhouse in Ketelshagen, 4 km from Putbus on the Baltic Sea island of Rügen.

Gutshaus Ketelshagen offers free Wi-Fi and a beautiful garden with a terrace and pond.

Rooms at the Gutshaus Ketelshagen are individually furnished and feature bright and homelike interiors with classic furniture.

All include a seating area with flat-screen TV, and apartments include kitchen facilities.

Breakfast can be booked each morning, and barbecue facilities are available in the garden.

Free rental bikes are offered at the guest house, which is an ideal base for hiking and cycling here in the Südost-Rügen (South-East Rügen) Nature Reserve.

A 9-hole golf course can be found just 3 km away in Karnitz. On-site parking is free for guests.
