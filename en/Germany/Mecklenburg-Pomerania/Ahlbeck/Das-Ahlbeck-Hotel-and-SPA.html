Description

This privately owned, design-oriented boutique hotel is located directly on the Baltic coast, in the spa town of Ahlbeck, only a few minutes drive from the Polish border.

Guests can enjoy relaxing on the sun-terrace as well as free WiFi. The 4-star Ahlbeck Hotel & SPA offers tastefully furnished spacious accommodation, which features high-quality, bamboo parquet flooring.

There is also a bathrobe, slippers and the first set of minibar items are provided.

Nearly all rooms have their own fireplace. Wake up to the Ahlbeck’s excellent breakfast with regional products and baked goods fresh from the Hotel's bakery, which is included in your room price.

In the evening, dine on extraordinary delicacies in the open-kitchen restaurant. During finer weather, sit on the terrace.

Guests can also enjoy the Ahlbeck-Ticket, which offers free public transport. Guests staying for 2 nights or longer are invited to a complimentary, delicious welcome dinner in the restaurant and a wellness cocktail on the day of their arrival.

Other benefits for guests include on-site sports and activities and a welcome drink.

Unwind in the Ahlbeck SPA’s indoor swimming pool, sauna and fitness room. Use of these facilities is free to guests of the hotel.

You could also treat yourself to a relaxing massage. There is a free phone service in the rooms.
