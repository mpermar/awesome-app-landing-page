Description

Featuring a sun terrace with views of the surrounding countryside, this wellness hotel provides a quiet country retreat.

Guests benefit from free parking, internet in public areas and use of the hotel’s sauna in former circus wagons.

Rooms at the Hotel Gutshaus Parin Bio- und Gesundheitshotel are decorated in neutral tones and furnished with natural wooden furniture.

Hotel Guthaus Parin Bio- und Gesundheitshotel offers organic, vegetarian cuisine in their restaurant, located in the building’s rustic-style arched cellar.

An array of cakes, breads and baked goods are available at the hotel's organic bakery.

The hotel is situated in the Mecklenburg-Vorpommern countryside, an area popular for cycling and walking.

The Baltic Sea coast and the seaside resort Boltenhagen are a 15-minute drive from the hotel.

Guests also enjoy free access to the larger sauna at the Gutshaus Stellshagen (6 km away).

The 13th century town of Grevesmühlen can be reached in 10 minutes by car. The A20 motorway is a 15-minute drive from the property and provides links to Lübeck and Hamburg.
