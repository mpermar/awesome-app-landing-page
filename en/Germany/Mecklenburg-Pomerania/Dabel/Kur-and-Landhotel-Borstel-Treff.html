Description

This hotel is located in Dabel. Kur-& Landhotel Borstel offers free WiFi, a spa with indoor and outdoor pool and a beautiful spa park with petting zoo and 2 small lakes.

Rooms at Kur-& Landhotel Borstel are complete with a balcony or terrace, satellite TV, a DVD player and a dining area.

Each room also has a private bathroom with hairdryer, toiletries and a bathtub or shower.

At Kur-& Landhotel Borstel - Treff you will find a restaurant, a tennis court and a fitness centre.

The hotel also offers meeting facilities, a ticket service and a children's playground.

A range of activities can be enjoyed in the surrounding area, including cycling, horse riding and fishing.
