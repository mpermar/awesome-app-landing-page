Description

This 5-star superior hotel lies in the Baltic Sea resort of Sellin, a 4-minute walk from the historic pier.

It offers many restaurants and a spa with pool. Many rooms have a balcony/terrace.

The rooms and suites at Romantik Roewers Privathotel are individually furnished in bright colours.

All rooms feature cable TV, a seating area, and a modern bathroom. Some include tea/coffee facilities and a fridge.

The Ambiance restaurant offers international cuisine. Traditional, regional meals are served in the Clou restaurant.

Guests can also enjoy light dishes in Romantik Roewers’ brasserie or relax with a drink in the elegant bar.

The spa at the Romantik Roewers Sellin offers saunas, massages and wellness treatments.

Guests can also enjoy the outdoor pool on the rooftop (adults only). There are many scenic walking paths on the island of Rügen.

The Romantik Roewers Privathotel also has a facility for charging electric cars for free.
