Description

Take a refreshing break in Sellin and breathe in the fresh sea breeze from the white sandy beach. Enjoy the intimate atmosphere of the small and friendly hotel.

For a special feeling of well-being, staff can arrange appointments for prophylactic physiotherapy treatments such as original Rügen chalk packs.
