Description

This hotel in Sellin offers a private beach, large spa area and fine dining. It enjoys a scenic location between Rügen's Selliner See lake and the Baltic Sea.

The quiet rooms of the Cliff Hotel Rügen are feature warm colours and classical furniture.

All rooms include a flat-screen TV, spacious bathroom and free WiFi. Most rooms have a balcony.

The Cliff Hotel's modern Rülax spa includes a 25-metre pool, meditation areas and fitness facilities.

Beauty and massage services are available. A full breakfast buffet and gourmet cuisine are offered in the Seeterrassen restaurant, which offers a view of the Selliner See lake.

Mediterranean and seafood dishes are served in the Casa Blanca restaurant. The 24-hour reception at the Cliff Rügen includes a tour desk and ticket service.
