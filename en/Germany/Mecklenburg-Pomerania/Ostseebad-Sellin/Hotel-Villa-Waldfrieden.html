Description

This family-run villa is 300 metres from Sellin Beach and Sellin's historic Seebrücke pier.

It offers a daily breakfast buffet, free Wi-Fi as well as a free internet terminal in the lobby.

All rooms at the Hotel Villa Waldfrieden include a flat-screen TV with radio stations, and apartments also have a fully equipped kitchen or kitchenette.

The elegant hotel building dates back to the 19th century. Northern German dishes and fresh fish specialities are served in the Waldfrieden's Zur Kajüte restaurant with Mediterranean lounge.

Guests can enjoy home-made cakes and coffee on the heated terrace, and a bar is also available.

Located on the stylish Wilhelmstraße with its beach-style villas, Hotel Villa Waldfrieden is an ideal base for discovering the island of Rügen.

It offers rental bikes and is just 1 km from the Selliner See lake. Guests can buy tickets for the Störtebeker Festival from the hotel reception.
