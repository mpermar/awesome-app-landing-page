Description

This 4-star hotel features a wellness area with a swimming pool, and a look-out tower with panoramic views of the Baltic Sea and the well known chalk cliffs.

It lies in Göhren, on the Baltic Sea island of Rügen. The Hotel Hanseatic Rügen und Villen is set on a cliff and offers spacious rooms with free Wi-Fi in the historic main building, as well as apartments in 2 nearby villas.

The Hanseatic's 1000 m² wellness area includes various saunas and a modern fitness room.

Cosmetic and health treatments are also available. A playground and play room are provided for children.

The hotel also has a library for guests of all ages. Lunch and dinner are served in the restaurant.

Half-board is available as a buffet or a set menu in the conservatory. Coffee and homemade cakes can be enjoyed on the seaview terrace from May to September.

The hotel bar offers a selection of cocktails and long drinks. Rügen's sandy beaches are just an 8-minute walk away.
