Description

Offering a sauna and fitness room, this hotel on the island of Rügen is located just 200 metres from the sandy beach.

Rooms and apartments include a balcony or terrace. Rügener Ferienhäuser am Hochufer offers rooms with classic-style décor, cable TV and seating area.

The apartments also feature a fully equipped kitchenette. WiFi is available in public areas and is free of charge for guests.

Upon request, a breakfast buffet is available and guests can also use the barbecue facilities in the garden.

Restaurants and cafés can be found within a 5-minute walk. Guests can enjoy a variety of on-site facilities, including table tennis and a library.

The centre of Göhren is 200 metres from Rügener Ferienhäuser am Hochufer, and the surrounding countryside offers opportunities for hiking and cycling.
