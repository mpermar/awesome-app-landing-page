Description

This 4-star hotel lies near sandy beaches in the Baltic Sea resort of Wustrow. It offers bright rooms, fully equipped apartments and a large spa with swimming pool.

All rooms and apartments at Dorint Strandresort & Spa Ostseebad Wustrow feature free WiFi, satellite TV, a balcony, seating area and private bathroom.

The apartments have a kitchenette. A buffet breakfast is provided in the Quarterdeck Restaurant until 12:00 each day.

The restaurant also serves regional Mecklenburg specialities and international dishes.

The Vital Spa at the Dorint Wustrow includes many saunas and steam baths. It offers massages, beauty treatments and courses in Tai Chi and Pilates.
