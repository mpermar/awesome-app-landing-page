Description

These apartments are located in Zweedorf near Rerik, on the Baltic Sea coast. Ostsee-Landhaus offers free WiFi and a garden with terrace, a barbecue house and a children's playground.

Ostsee-Landhaus is cosily decorated in a traditional country-house style. All apartments feature wooden furniture, satellite TV, a fully equipped kitchenette and a private bathroom with shower.

The surrounding area is ideal for outdoor activities such as cycling, horse riding and hiking.

Parking is free of charge for guests at Ostsee-Landhaus. It is 70 km from Lübeck Airport and 73 km from Rostock Laage Airport.
