Description

The Ferienwohnanlage Schwabe is located in Rerik, just 1 km from the Baltic Sea coast.

It offers well-equipped holiday homes and apartments with free use of the on-site indoor swimming pool and free parking spaces.

All of the holiday homes and apartments at the Schwabe have free WiFi, a comfortable living area and a modern kitchenetteas well as a private bathroom.

Many also have a balcony or a terrace. The Schwabe offers a bread roll service. Guests can order breakfast in their apartment or can enjoy a breakfast buffet.

There is also a communal barbeque area on site. The Ferienwohnanlage Schwabe features a pool, a sauna and a solarium.
