Description

Only 250 metres from the beach, this 3-star superior hotel in the Baltic Sea resort of Dierhagen offers a spa with indoor pool, fitness area, 3 saunas and a beauty farm.

Varied breakfast buffets are provided. The spacious rooms of the Ostseehotel Dierhagen feature light wooden furniture a flat-screen TV, a work desk, and a free bottle of water.

They all provide views from large windows and en suite bathrooms with hairdryer. Rental bathrobes are provided at the reception.

The Ostseehotel also has 6 sun terraces and a bicycle rental service. Guests can enjoy views of Saaler Bodden Bay from Dierhagen’s modern restaurant, serving local specialities and continental food.

In the evenings, they are welcome to relax with a drink in the café. The Dierhagen hotel is a good base for trips to the hanseatic city of Rostock and the island of Rügen.

Staff at the 24-hour reception can help plan tours and excursions, while free private parking is available on site.
