Description

This hotel is located in Ganzlin. Vila Vita Anneliese Pohl Seedorf offers free WiFi and free rental bikes.

All rooms at Vila Vita Anneliese Pohl Seedorf include a TV, a safe and a private bathroom with shower, hairdryer and bathrobes.

At Vila Vita Anneliese Pohl Seedorf you will find a communal sauna, a 24-hour front desk and a bar.

The hotel also has entertainment staff, meeting facilities and a tour desk. A range of activities can be enjoyed nearby, including cycling, fishing and hiking.
