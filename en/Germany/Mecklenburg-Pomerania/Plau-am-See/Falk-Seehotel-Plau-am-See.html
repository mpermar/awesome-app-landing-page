Description

The property is located a one minute walk from the beach. This 4-star hotel is directly on the Plauer See lake.

Wonderful lake views can be enjoyed from the hotel restaurant with terrace. Guests have use of a range of spa facilities, and free WiFi is provided in all areas.

The Seehotel Plau am See offers individually furnished rooms. All rooms have a TV, a work desk and a minibar.

The rooms are located either in the main building or in the Seeschlösschen annex, just 30 metres away.

The double rooms are located in the Seeschlösschen building, partly with a view of the lake and the double rooms with a balcony, the superior rooms and the deluxe double rooms are situated in the the hotel building.

Restaurant Seeblick features not only lake views, but also a menu of seasonal, regional cuisine.

This includes dishes with fish caught from the Plauer See. Use of the sauna and steam room is free for guests of the Seehotel.
