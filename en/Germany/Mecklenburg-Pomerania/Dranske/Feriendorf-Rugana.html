Description

These apartments are situated just 300 metres from a beach and 1 km from a sand beach on the Baltic Sea island of Rügen.

Feriendorf Rugana features a swimming pool, steam room and sports courts. A flat-screen TV, sofa and a dining area are offered in the apartment at Feriendorf Rugana.

Each unit is bright, modern and individually furnished. Each apartment comes with a fully equipped kitchen, and your fridge can be filled with groceries on request.

The restaurant also serves a variety of hot and cold regional dishes. Barbecue facilities are available for rent.

A breakfast buffet is available at the restaurant, which also serves a variety of local dishes.

Bikes can be rented to explore the pretty natural surroundings, and mini golf facilities are also offered.

A ferry point with connections to Hiddensee island is located 7 km away, and it is 12 km to Cape Arkona.
