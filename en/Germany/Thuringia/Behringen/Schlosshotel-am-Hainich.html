Description

This traditional hotel in Behringen is 7 km from the scenic Hainich National Park.

It features a breakfast buffet, and rooms with a minibar and cable TV. All rooms include solid wooden furniture, a work desk, and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

Some rooms in the hotel are wheelchair accessible. A hearty German breakfast buffet is offered in Behringen Schlosshotel's homelike breakfast room.

Beers and wines can be enjoyed at the well-stocked bar. Hainich National Park features many opportunities for hiking and cycling.

Schlosshotel Behringen is a 20-minute drive from Gotha. The hotel offers free private parking on site.
