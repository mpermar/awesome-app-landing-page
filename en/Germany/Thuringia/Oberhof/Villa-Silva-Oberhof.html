Description

Set in a historic, 19th-century villa, this modern hotel enjoys a scenic location in the popular winter sports resort.

It offers a spa area, bowling alley and spacious accommodation with free Wi-Fi access.

Renovated in December 2012, Villa Silva features stylish rooms decorated in forest-inspired green and brown tones.

Highlights include a flat-screen TV, minibar and a large bathroom. Each morning, breakfast is served in the bright and cheerful breakfast room of the neighboring Berghotel Oberdorf.

Guests can sample Thüringen specialities and international dishes at the Villa Silva's Restaurant Saltus, or relax with a drink in the hotel bar.

Oberhof’s ski lifts and ski centre are within 1. 5 km of Villa Silva. After a long day on the slopes, soothing massages, sauna and steam rooms are all available at the 4-star superior hotel.
