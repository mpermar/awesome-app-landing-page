Description

This hotel is set amid green, peaceful countryside in the town of Rodishain, making this an excellent base for going on long walks or exploring the surroundings by bike.

The Ferienhotel Wolfsmühle offers comfortable, brightly lit accommodation with all modern conveniences.

Free wireless internet can be accessed in the hotel’s public areas. Included in the room price is the Wolfsmühle’s hearty breakfast buffet.

In the evening, enjoy the wide range of dishes on the dinner menu, including seasonal specialities.

Children will find plenty to do here. There is a campsite near to the hotel with a large playground, as well as fields, the Wolfsbach stream and woods.
