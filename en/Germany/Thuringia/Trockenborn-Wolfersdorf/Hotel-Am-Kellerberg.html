Description

Situated in the quiet Trockenborn-Wolfersdorf countryside, this hotel offers modern rooms within a 16th-century building.

Traditional cuisine is served in the restaurant, and free Wi-Fi is available. The bright rooms at Hotel Am Kellerberg feature cable TV and a work desk.

A hairdryer is provided in the private bathroom. Regional Thuringian and international dishes are cooked in the stone bake oven, and served in the country-style restaurant which features traditional wooden beams.

During the summer months guests can dine on the terrace. The surrounding Thuringian countryside can be explored through the hiking and cycling trails in the area, and massages and beauty treatments can be booked.

The city of Jena is a 25-minute drive from Am Kellerberg, and Weimar is a 40-minute drive away.
