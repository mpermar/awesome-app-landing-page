Description

The Mühle Hotel und Erlebnisinsel GmbH is located in Eberstedt and features a terrace and restaurant.

The accommodation offers a children's playground, a shuttle service, while a bicycle rental service is also available.

Most units in the hotel are fitted with a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries.

Free WiFi is available to all guests, while selected rooms come with a balcony. The floating cabins feature bunk beds, heater and access to shared bathrooms.

A buffet breakfast is available each morning at the property. The area is popular for cycling and hiking.

Toskana Thermal Baths is 3 km from the property, while Weimar is a 30-minute drive away.
