Description

This hotel is quietly situated in Eisenach, only 300 metres from the city centre with its market square and city palace.

Wartburg Castle is just a 10-minute walk away. Hotel Garni Hennesburg offers classically furnished rooms with a TV and private bathroom.

A rich breakfast buffet is available each morning. This is an ideal base for exploring Eisenach's historical attractions.

It is 600 metres from the home of Johann Sebastian Bach and 900 metres from the villa of Reuther and Wagner.

Keen hikers will also appreciate the nearby hiking trails, such as the Rennsteig.
