Description

This 3-star Superior hotel in Floh-Seligenthal is a great base for sports activities in the Thuringian Forest.

It offers an outdoor pool, spacious rooms with balcony, and traditional regional food.

The family-run Hotel Thüringer Hof has brightly decorated rooms with wooden furniture and satellite TV.

Free Wi-Fi is available in all public areas. Varied breakfast buffets are on offer at the Thüringer Hof every day.

Thuringian dishes and fine wines are served in the country-style restaurant with terrace.

Guests are welcome to relax in the Thüringer Hof’s garden with sunbathing area in warm weather.

Leisure activities at or near the Thüringer Hof include hiking, cross-country skiing, and horse riding.
