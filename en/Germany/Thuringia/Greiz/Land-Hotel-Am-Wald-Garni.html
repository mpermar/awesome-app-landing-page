Description

The Land-Hotel Am Wald is a family-run hotel surrounded by woods and nature, located in the immediate vicinity of the peaceful Thuringian town of Greiz.

It offers refurbished modern apartments as well as a pool, garden terrace and barbecue facilities.

Guests can enjoy a rich breakfast featuring home-made jams and original Thuringian fresh sausage as well as a packed lunch.

A small evening menu and Greizer beer are also available upon request. Cyclists may use the lockable garage free of charge.
