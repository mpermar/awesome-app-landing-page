Description

This historical house with a new hotel is charmingly located between local forests and the town Tautenhain.

Situated in the triangle of the cities Jena, Gera, and Leipzig you have an excellent transport link to all sightseeings of Thuringia, Saxonia and Saxonia-Anhalt.

Wi-Fi is available in the hotel rooms and in all public areas, and is free of charge.
