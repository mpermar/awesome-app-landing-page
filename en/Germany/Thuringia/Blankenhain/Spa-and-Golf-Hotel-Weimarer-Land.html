Description

Set directly on the grounds of a golf course, this 4-star hotel in Blankenhain offers a large spa with pools and free yoga and aerobic classes.

A spacious restaurant with a large sun terrace also features. Spa & Golf Hotel Weimarer Land provides elegant, traditional rooms with satellite TV, a desk and a seating area.

Each one also includes free rental bikes and child care services. Regional specialities and Mediterranean dishes are served at the restaurant.

Cocktails can be enjoyed at the bar, and coffee and cakes are available in the afternoon.

A sauna, massages and beauty treatments are available in the spa. There is also a free programme of activities for children up to the age of 14.

The surrounding countryside is ideal for hiking, while the famous cultural heritage city of Weimar is just 15 km from the hotel.
