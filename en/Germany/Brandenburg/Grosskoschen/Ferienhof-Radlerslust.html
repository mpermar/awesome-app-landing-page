Description

Set in Großkoschen, in the heart of Lusatian Lake District, Ferienhof Radlerslust features a garden, barbecue, and children's playground.

Burg (Spreewald) is 38 km away. The accommodation features a flat-screen TV. Some units have a terrace and/or balcony.

There is also a kitchen, fitted with a dishwasher and microwave. A toaster and coffee machine are also offered.

Towels and bed linen are available. Ferienhof Radlerslust also includes a terrace.

A rich breakfast is provided every morning. The property also offers packed lunches.

Bike hire is available at the property and the area is popular for cycling. Lake Senftengberg is just 100 km away.

Bautzen is 43 km from Ferienhof Radlerslust, while Lübbenau is 42 km from the property.
