Description

These rooms and apartments enjoy a tranquil, rural setting in the Brandenburg village of Haida, in the Elbe-Elster region.

Ferienparadies Waldidylle provides the ideal base for a relaxing break or for exploring this unspoiled countryside of forests, meadows and lakes.

This Elbe-Elster region is popular with visitors who like hiking, cycling, horse riding, fishing and water sports.

In winter you can go skiing, ice skating, tobogganing, or simply relax at the hotel and enjoy a beer or a Glühwein (mulled wine).

Thanks to the spacious family apartments, Ferienparadies Waldidylle is also suitable for families with children.

  • This property has many entertainment options for kids and that is always a plus.
  • This property has large rooms available which could make a more pleasant stay.
