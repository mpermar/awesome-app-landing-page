Description

This hotel and our apartments are located in the middle of the beautiful Schorfheide, the largest continuous forest area of Europe, set amid wetlands and moorland, directly on picturesque Lake Grimnitz.

Popular with cyclists, the hotel is located on the Radfernweg Tour Brandenburg (Brandenburg long-distance cycle tour) which connects to the Radfernweg Berlin-Usedom (Berlin-Usedom long-distance cycle tour).

An on-site beach with jetty and sauna, invites you to sunbathe and relax. Lake Grimnitz offers ideal conditions for swimming, fishing, rowing, surfing and kite surfing.

Seehotel Schorfheide is ideal for a relaxing or active holiday. Accommodation is offered in a variety of rooms as well as in apartments with a terrace and lake view.
