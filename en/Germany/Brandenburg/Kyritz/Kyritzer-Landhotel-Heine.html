Description

This family-run hotel is a renovated farm just outside the town of Kyritz, in the Prignitz district of Brandenburg.

Landhotel Heine offers a daily breakfast buffet and spacious rooms with free Wi-Fi.

The rooms at Kyritzer Landhotel Heine are decorated in a country-house style. All rooms include a TV, separate seating area and a private bathroom.

The Kyritzer Landhotel is a good base for exploring Brandenburg’s lakes and rivers.

It is 35 minutes from Neuruppin and 80-minute drive from Berlin via the A24 motorway.
