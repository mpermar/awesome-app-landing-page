Description

One of our top picks in Bad Saarow.

Surrounded by countryside, Schlosspark Bad Saarow is located directly at the beautiful Scharmützelsee Lake. The self-catering apartments feature a hot tub and terrace, and some have a private sauna. Free WiFi is provided.

These wooden, Scandanavian-style holiday homes offer stylish interiors with large windows and a fireplace. Comforts include a sofa, floor heating, satellite TV and an private bathroom with a spa bath.

The kitchen comes fully equipped with a dishwasher, fridge, oven, microwave, kettle, coffee machine and dining area. A 3-minute drive takes you to the center of Bad Saarow-Pieskow, where a variety of restaurants can also be found.

You can rent a bike to explore the green surroundings, and young guests will enjoy the on-site children’s playground. Hiking and fishing are also possible nearby.

Bad Saarow Train Station is 2.5 mi away, and free on-site parking is provided. There is also a reception on site.
