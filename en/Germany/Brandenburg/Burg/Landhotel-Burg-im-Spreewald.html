Description

This 4-star hotel enjoys a peaceful location in Burg, surrounded by the scenic Spreewald Forest.

It offers free WiFi, a modern spa with heated outdoor pool, leisure activities and 4 restaurants.

The brightly decorated rooms, suites and apartments at Landhotel Burg all feature satellite TV and a modern bathroom with bathrobes.

The apartments were completed in 2012. Spa facilities at the Hotel Burg include 3 saunas and a steam room.

A range of cosmetic treatments and massages are offered, and the heated outdoor pool and sunbathing area are open all year round.

Italian specialities are served in the Il Fienile restaurant with conservatory, and the Aquario restaurant offers fish and seafood dishes.

Guests can also dine in the cosy fireplace lounge or on the heated terrace. The Landhotel Burg offers rental bicycles and is also an ideal base for paddle boat, rowing boat and horse carriage rides here in the Spreewald forest.

Children will appreciate the play room and the petting zoo with horses, alpakas, goats, emus, guinea pigs and donkeys.
