Description

This family-run hotel in Grünplan offers spa facilities, a lakeside beer garden and rental bicycles.

It lies between the Rheinsberg and Mecklenburg Lake Districts, a 15-minute drive from Rheinsberg Castle.

The rooms and apartments of the 4-star Hotel Heidekrug offer lake or park views. All rooms include a minibar and a seating area.

Hotel Heidekrug’s spa includes a sauna, and a gym. Massages can also be booked. Breakfast is available each morning.

Local game and fish are served in Heidekrug’s restaurant. The countryside surrounding the Hotel Heidekrug is ideal for cycling, hiking and horse riding.
