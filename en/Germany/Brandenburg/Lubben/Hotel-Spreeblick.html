Description

This family-run hotel in Lübben town centre offers country-style rooms with free Wi-Fi.

Hotel Spreeblick has a traditional restaurant, a spa area and a roof terrace overlooking the River Spree.

All rooms at the 3-star Hotel Spreeblick include a satellite TV, a seating area and private bathroom.

Most rooms have a balcony. The spa area at Hotel Spreeblick includes relaxation rooms, a light-therapy sauna, a cold water plunge pool, a Himalayan salt sauna and a outdoor Kneipp facility.

Hotel-Restaurant Spreeblick serves a large breakfast buffet and a selection of local Brandenburg dishes.

The traditional bar serves over 100 sorts of whiskey. The Spreeblick is 350 metres from Lübben Castle and 2 km from Lübben Train Station.

The Tropical Islands water park is a 20-minute drive away. Hotel Spreeblick offers free parking and good access to the A13 motorway, a 60-minute drive from both Berlin and Dresden.
