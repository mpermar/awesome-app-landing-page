Description

Each unit features a dishwasher, oven, a coffee machine, as well as a microwave and electric tea pot. Some units include a terrace and/or a balcony with lake views.

The property has a hot tub and a sauna.

A playground is available on site and both cycling and hiking can be enjoyed within close proximity of the property.

Burg is 27 miles from Marinapark Scharmützelsee, while Lübbenau is 24 miles away. The nearest airport is Schonefeld Airport, 24 miles from the property.
