Description

Offering a range of leisure activities and spa facilities, this American-style resort is located in the Brandenburg’s Döberitzer Heide Nature Park. It features a pool, free Wi-Fi, as well as rental bicycles.

The bright rooms at Havellandhalle Resort are equipped with wooden furniture and a flat-screen TV. Each room has large windows which open out onto the patio, and bathrobes are provided.

The restaurant offers a variety of regional and Italian specialties, and guests can enjoy a drink at the lounge bar or in the beer garden.

Havellandhalle provides the opportunity for many activities, such as tennis, volleyball, badminton and mini golf. Guests can use the fitness room, relax in the sauna or treat themselves to a massage.

Central Berlin is a 25-minute drive from the resort, and the Olympic Stadium can be reached within 12 minutes. Kurfürstendamm and Berlin's ICC Exhibition Center are a 15-minute drive away.
