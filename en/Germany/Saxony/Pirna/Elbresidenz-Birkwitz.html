Description

This bed and breakfast is situated on the banks of the Elbe, not far from Pillnitz Castle.

From every suite guests can enjoy a wonderful view of the Elbe valley, into Saxon Switzerland and the vineyards of Dresden city.

In the beautiful boarding house you will find furniture as in the suite of a luxury hotel but at low prices.

In your leisure time you may visit the sights of Dresden, 25 km away, and also make an excursion to the Saxon Switzerland nearby.
