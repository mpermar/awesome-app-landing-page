Description

This hotel offers its own on-site horse stables, horse riding facilities and bicycle rentals.

It is near hiking paths, a small ski slope and sledging hill and just 50 metres from the Badegärten Eibenstock with gym, saunas and spa treatments.

Rooms at Reit- und Sporthotel Eibenstock include a TV, telephone, free WiFi and a terrace.

Each room includes a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries. International cuisine and specialities from the Erzgebirge Mountains region are served in the hotel restaurant.

After an active day, guests can relax at the hotel bar or on the spacious terrace.

The hotel offers direct access to hiking paths, a small ski slope, and a sledging hill.

The hotel also offers bicycle rentals to guests. Central Eibenstock is 1. 3 km from the hotel and the S275 road is 20 metres from the hotel.
