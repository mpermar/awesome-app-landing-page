Description

This hotel in Eibenstock features a children's playground, sun terrace, restaurant and bowling alley.

Hotel Am Bühl offers free WiFi and free private on-site parking. All rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV and some rooms feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

Some rooms include views of the lake or garden. The rooms come with a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

There is a 24-hour front desk, a shared lounge and gift shop at the property. This hotel offers ski storage space and bike hire is available.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including skiing, cycling and hiking.
