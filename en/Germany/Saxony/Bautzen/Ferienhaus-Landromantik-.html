Description

Situated in Bautzen, Ferienhaus "Landromantik" offers accommodation with free WiFi.

The property is 1. 4 km from Bautzen Reservoir and free private parking is provided.

There is a seating area, a dining area and a kitchen complete with a microwave, a toaster and a fridge.

A flat-screen TV with satellite channels and Blu-ray player, as well as a CD player are available.

Other facilities at Ferienhaus "Landromantik" include a barbecue. A breakfast is offered upon request.

The area is popular for fishing. Bautzen Memorial is 6 km from Ferienhaus "Landromantik", while Dinosaur Park Kleinwelka is 7 km away.

The reservoir is 1 km away and Zittau Mountains are one hour drive away. The nearest airport is Dresden Airport, 51 km from the property.
