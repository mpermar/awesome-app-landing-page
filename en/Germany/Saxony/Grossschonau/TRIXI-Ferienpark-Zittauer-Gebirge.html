Description

A tropical-inspired water park with indoor and outdoor swimming pools, oriental spa area and spacious accommodation are offered by this holiday park.

It enjoys a scenic location in the Zittau mountain region. TRIXI-Ferienpark Zittauer Gebirge features bright and spacious holiday homes with a furnished terrace.

Each is complete with a comfortable living area, kitchenette and a modern bathroom.

Dining options include buffet meals at Gute Stube restaurant and light snacks from the Sprungturm beach bar.

Guests can also use the on-site barbecue facilities during warmer weather. There is also a cafe and guests can enjoy a drink in the Kämmerchen bar.

All guests enjoy free admission for one day to the leisure baths. Bikes can be rented at the holiday park, and younger guests will love the children’s playground and games room.

After a long day, visitors can unwind in the sauna or book soothing massages. The Czech Republic border is 3 km from TRIXI-Ferienpark Zittauer Gebirge.
