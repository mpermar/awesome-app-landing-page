Description

Offering a wide range of sports facilities, this hotel is located at the foot of the Fichtelberg Mountain in the winter holiday resort of Oberwiesenthal.

The spacious rooms at the k1 Sporthotel all include a TV and a private bathroom with shower.

Some rooms feature a view of the Fichtelberg Mountain, the highest mountain in the Ore Mountains.

The k1 Sporthotel has a sauna and a party room with table tennis table. Mountain bikes and skis can be hired.

A suspension monorail and ski slope are also available near the property, along with a bike path.

There is a free internet terminal in the lobby and free Wi-Fi in the café/bar. K1 also has a heated ski depot with direct access to cable car.

Tickets are available near the hotel for the ski lift and cable car. Guests can also receive professional ski instruction at the hotel.
