Description

Situated in Dommitzsch, in a building dating from 19th century, Schmidtalien Schlemmen und Schlummern provides a bar and guestrooms with free WiFi.

Boasting room service, this property also provides guests with a restaurant. Certain rooms at the property have a patio with a city view.

All rooms at the hotel come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with a hair dryer and a shower.

A continental breakfast is served daily at the property. Lutherstadt Wittenberg is 36 km from Schmidtalien Schlemmen und Schlummern, while Bad Schmiedeberg is 13 km from the property.
