Description

Ideally situated directly beside Lake Störmhal, Lagovida Das Ferienresort Am Störmthaler See is located in Großpösna.

It offers a private beach area, a restaurant, and free WiFi access. These modern suites and holiday homes are stylishly decorated with contemporary furnishings.

Each one features a seating area and a flat-screen TV, and some include a private balcony with lake views.

A hearty breakfast buffet is available every morning, and the restaurant serves classic German and regional specialities in the evenings.

Popular activities in the area include hiking, cycling, and water sports, and Lagovida Das Ferienresort Am Störmthaler See offers bicycle and water sports equipment rentals on site.

Leipzig is a 20-minute drive. Markkleeberg Train Station is 18 km from the property, and the A38 motorway is 10 minutes by car.
