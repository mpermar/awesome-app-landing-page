Description

Offering an ideal base for hiking and cycling tours in the scenic Erzgebirge Mountains, this rustic hotel in Neuhausen outside Seiffen features country-style rooms and a regional restaurant with beer garden.

A cross-country ski slope is located just 20 metres away. Hotel Dachsbaude & Kammbaude offers bright rooms with warm colours, each with a TV and radio.

The private bathrooms have a shower. The traditional Dachsbaude restaurant serves dishes and drinks from the Ore Mountains region.

The historic town of Seiffen with its famous Christmas traditions are just a 5-minute drive away.

Chemnitz, Dresden and Prague can all be reached in less than 90 minutes by car from the property.
