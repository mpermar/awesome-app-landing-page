Description

Situated close to Lichtenwalde Castle and its magnificent Baroque-style gardens, the Best Western Hotel am Schlosspark is a modern 4-star hotel providing a range of leisure and dining facilities.

All rooms at the Hotel am Schlosspark are non-smoking and offer free Wi-Fi internet, scenic countryside views, and a private bathroom.

Guests can relax in the gym, sauna or hire a bicycle to explore the surrounding natural countryside.

The Campus lounge serves a selection of buffets. The elegant Lichtblick restaurant offers international dishes.

Guests enjoy free on-site parking and easy access to the A4 motorway from the Best Western Hotel am Schlosspark.
