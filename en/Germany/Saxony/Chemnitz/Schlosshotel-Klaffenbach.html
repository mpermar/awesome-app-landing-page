Description

This 4-star hotel is set on the property of Chemnitz's historic Klaffenbach residence.

It offers a golf course, traditional-style restaurants, and big park with adventure playground.

The Schlosshotel Klaffenbach provides quiet rooms with an original design and modern bathrooms.

Free Wi-Fi is available. There is also a free internet terminal in the lobby. Dining options at the Schlosshotel Klaffenbach include the Gewölberestaurant with arched ceiling, Torwache restaurant, and Schlosscafé café with terrace.

Leisure facilities on the Schlosshotel property include a museum and art studios.
