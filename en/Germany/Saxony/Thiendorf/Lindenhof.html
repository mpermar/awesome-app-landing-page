Description

Set in idyllic hilly countryside in the peaceful village of Thiendorf, this hotel is a 20-minute drive from Dresden city centre via the A13 motorway.

It modern rooms include flat-screen TV and free Wi-Fi. Guests at the Lindenhof Hotel can enjoy a breakfast buffet every morning.

Hearty cuisine from the Saxony region is served in the restaurant, and guests can dine on the terrace in good weather.

Lindenhof offers brightly decorated rooms with traditional wooden furniture. Each room has a work desk and safety deposit box, and a hairdryer is provided in the en suite bathrooms.

The hotel is an ideal base for visiting the world-famous porcelain-manufacturing town of Meißen, the Schloss Moritzburg hunting lodge or for hiking and cycling in the Elbe Valley.

The A13 motorway is just 500 metres away, and free parking is provided. The hotel is 30 km from EuroSpeedway Lausitz and 60 km from the Spreewald Nature Reserve.
