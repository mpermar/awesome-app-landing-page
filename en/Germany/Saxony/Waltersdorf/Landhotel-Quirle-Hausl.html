Description

Set in Waltersdorf in the Zittau Mountains, the historic Landhotel Quirle-Häusl has a garden and a terrace.

This 3-star superior hotel is set in 3 listed heritage buildings. The main building is a traditional Upper Lusatian half-timbered house, built in 1838.

Landhotel Quirle-Häusl offers a shared lounge and free WiFi. At the hotel, every room is equipped with a wardrobe and a flat-screen TV.

The private bathroom is fitted with a bath or shower and free toiletries. Landhotel Quirle-Häusl offers a children's playground.

Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Waltersdorf, like skiing, hiking and cycling.

The accommodation provides airport transfers, while a bicycle rental service is also available.

Oybin is 10 km from the Quirle-Häsule, while Zittau is 13 km away. Dresden is 80 km from the property.
