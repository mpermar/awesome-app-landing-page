Description

Located directly on the shores of the Markkleeberger See (lake) to the south of Leipzig, this holiday resort offers modern and cosy holiday homes.

The Seepark Auenhain is a 2-minute walk from the beach, and offers other leisure activities on site, such as pool (billiards) and table football.

Belantis theme park is a 12-minute drive away. Pamper yourself in the hotel wellness facilities, consisting of an indoor swimming pool and 4 different theme saunas.

The Seeperle restaurant offers a range of German and Mediterranean specialities and also offers vegetarian options.

Relax in the afternoon with a coffee or ice-cream on the hotel terrace, which offers sea views.

Guests are welcome to use the beach volleyball court, and there is a canoe park just 300 metres away.

There is a children’s playground as well as a childcare service for children aged 3 upwards.

The resort is only a 25-minute drive from Leipzig city centre, and each holiday home has its own parking space.
