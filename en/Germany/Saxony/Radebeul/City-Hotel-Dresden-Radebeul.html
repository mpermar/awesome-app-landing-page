Description

This hotel has a quiet location in the district of Radebeul, 7 km north of Dresden.

Guests enjoy access to 2 restaurants and a garden terrace overlooking local vineyards.

All rooms at the City Hotel Dresden Radebeul feature air conditioning, satellite TV, Wi-Fi and a work desk.

Guests at the City Dresden Radebeul enjoy free use of the gym in the hotel next door.

The 25-metre pool, sauna, hot tub and massages are also available for an extra fee.

The Lößnitz restaurant serves regional and international dishes. The Nizza buffet serves light meals and drinks.

The A4 motorway is just 3 km from the City Hotel Dresden Radebeul. There are underground parking spaces at the hotel.
