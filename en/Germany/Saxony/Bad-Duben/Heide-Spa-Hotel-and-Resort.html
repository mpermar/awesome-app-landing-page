Description

Whether you are in search of relaxation or activity, this non-smoking hotel’s services and surroundings are ideal. Spacious indoor and outdoor swimming areas provide fun for the whole family, while a well-equipped fitness studio helps you keep in shape during your stay.

In addition to the hotel’s grounds, you find the Garten der Sinne (Garden of Senses), where various areas invite you to relax and explore in the open air. You also find an inviting library with a fireplace, a restaurant and a lobby bar on site.
