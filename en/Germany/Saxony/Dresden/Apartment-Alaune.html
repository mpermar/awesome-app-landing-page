Description

Alaune is situated in the historic city of Dresden, directly opposite Alaunpark. The stylish one-bedroom apartment features a terrace and garden.

Alaune is decorated in a modern style, and features a TV and dining area. It also offers a fully-equipped kitchen with dishwasher.

There is a large selection of restaurants and bars within a 10-minute walking distance of the apartment.

The nearest supermarket is 400 metres away. The trendy Kunsthof-Passage is less than a 5-minute walk from Alaune and includes a variety of cafes and shops.

It is 4 km to Dresden Old Town and 3 km to the picturesque Dresden Frauenkirche. Bischofsweg tram stop is 450 metres from Alaune apartment and provides connections to Dresden Main Train Station in 25-minutes.
