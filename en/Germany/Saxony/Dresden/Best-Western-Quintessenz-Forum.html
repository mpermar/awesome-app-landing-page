Description

Just 250 metres from the Elbe River, this Best Western in Dresden-Cotta offers well-equipped rooms and apartments and free Wi-Fi.

Dresden city centre is 3. 5 km away. The modern and spacious rooms and apartments at the family-friendly Best Western Quintessenz-Forum include satellite TV and a modern bathroom.

The Quintessenz-Forums Q-Linar Restaurant serves a daily breakfast buffet and international cuisine.

Exotic cocktails and German beers are available every night at the hotel bar. Younger guests are welcome to use the Best Westerns play room.

The Cossebauder Strae tram stop is 200 metres from the Best Western, providing a link to the city centre in 10 minutes.
