Description

This former farmhouse is now a family-run hotel featuring a sauna, a cosy breakfast room and a well-maintained garden area.

It is located in Reichenberg, a 20-minute drive from Dresden city centre. Free WiFi, a minibar and a work desk are included in the Hotel garni Sonnenhof’s rooms.

All have a private bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer. Some double rooms feature a balcony with a garden view.

An accessible room is available. A varied breakfast buffet is served every morning, and guests can eat outside on the terrace.

Various restaurants can be found nearby. Sonnenhof can organise rental bicycles for exploring the Friedewald and Moritzburger Teichlandschaft areas.

Hiking tours to the Radebeul vineyards and the Lößnitzgrund valley are other popular activities in the surroundings.

The A4 motorway is a 10-minute drive away, and free private parking is available on site.
