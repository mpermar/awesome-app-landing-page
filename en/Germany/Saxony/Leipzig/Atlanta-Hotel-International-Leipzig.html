Description

This 4-star hotel is located in southern Leipzig, 7 km from Leipzig Central Train Station.

It features a restaurant, soundproofed rooms, and spa facilities offering views of the Leipzig skyline.

Atlanta Hotel International Leipzig offers air-conditioned rooms with a flat-screen TV, work desk and minibar.

The private bathrooms feature Carrara marble. Wi-Fi is available in the whole hotel, and is free for up to 2 hours every day in the lobby.

International cuisine and Saxon specialities can be enjoyed in the Atlanta International’s Pavillon restaurant.

Varied buffet breakfasts are prepared in the Garden Bistro. Spa facilities at the Atlanta Leipzig include a modern sauna, hot tub, and small gym.

Guests can also choose to book massage or beauty treatments. Parking is available on-site at the Atlanta Hotel Leipzig.
