Description

This 3-star superior hotel is in the Schellerhau district of Altenberg, in the scenic Ore Mountains.

It offers sports and wellness options, 2 swimming pools, and a restaurant with beer garden.

The family-friendly Ahorn Waldhotel Altenberg has spacious and quiet rooms with bright décor and a flat-screen TV.

Allergy-free rooms are available on request. Nordic walking, archery, guided hiking tours and water aerobics are on offer at the Ahorn Waldhotel Altenberg.

Mountain bikes, skis, and sleds can also be rented. The hotel also puts on daily activities including laserbiathlon, minigolf and beach volleyball.

Wellness facilities at the Ahorn Waldhotel Altenberg include a sauna, gym, infra-red cabin and massage facilities.

They are available at an extra cost. The hotel also features a heated outdoor swimming pool, as well as an indoor pool.

Rich breakfast buffets are provided at the Ahorn Waldhotel Altenberg in the mornings.

The Ahorn Waldhotel Altenberg's restaurant serves varied, à la carte dishes and evening buffets.
