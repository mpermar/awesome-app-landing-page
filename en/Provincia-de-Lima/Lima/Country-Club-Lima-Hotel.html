Description

The elegant suites at Country Club Lima Hotel have antique beds, chandeliers, and lush cream carpeting. All feature king beds and air conditioning. Some have private balconies.

The Perroquet Restaurant serves Peruvian delicacies and a daily breakfast buffet. Los Vitrales Lobby Bar has a specialty afternoon tea with scones, and guests can enjoy a fine scotch at the English Bar.

Guests at Country Club Lima can enjoy the outdoor pool, surrounded by shaded palm trees, or use the well-equipped gym and beauty parlor. Lima´s premier Golf Club features 18 holes.

Country Club Lima Hotel has become a National Monument. It is only 7.5 miles from Jorge Chavez Airport, and has free parking.
