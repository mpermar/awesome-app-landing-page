Description

At the resort, rooms include a terrace with a river view. With a private bathroom, certain units at Tipparika Riverview Hotel also provide guests with a garden view. The rooms include a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

The accommodation offers a buffet or halal breakfast.

Tipparika Riverview Hotel has a playground. Guests at the resort will be able to enjoy activities in and around Hat Yai, like canoeing.

Hat Yai Clock Tower is 1.6 miles from Tipparika Riverview Hotel. Hat Yai Airport is 8 miles from the property.
