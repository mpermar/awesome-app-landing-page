Description

Featuring free WiFi, Hotel Prival is set in Grodno, a 10-minute drive from the city centre.

Guests can enjoy the lobby bar. Each room at this hotel is air conditioned and features a flat-screen TV.

Every room comes with a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower. Extras include bathrobes and slippers.

You can play table tennis at the hotel and there is a children's playground as well.
