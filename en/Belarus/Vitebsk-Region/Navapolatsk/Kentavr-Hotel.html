Description

Free Wi-Fi, a sauna and paintball facilities are provided at this hotel, located on the Western Dvina riverbank in Novopolotsk town.

It offers country-style rooms with a mosquito net. All the rooms at Kentavr Hotel are decorated with wood and come with a wardrobe.

Bathrooms are fitted with a hairdryer. The Kentavr’s modern restaurant serves national cuisine, and drinks are offered at the on-site bar.

Barbecue facilities are available. Guests of the hotel can play billiards or table tennis, and relax in the steam bath.

A massage service, a library and fishing trips can also be organised. Novopolotsk town centre is 5 minutes’ drive from Kentavr Hotel, and the P20 Motorway is 100 m away, offering a link to Vitebsk city.
