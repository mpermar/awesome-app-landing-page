Description

Featuring a private beach, Braslaw Lakes Hotel is located at the bank of Lake Navyaty in Braslaw.

Free WiFi is available. The rooms provide a flat-screen TV with cable channels, and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

Guests can dine in the hotel’s restaurant or use on-site barbecue facilities. Room service and breakfasts in the room are provided upon request.

Billiards, table tennis and darts can be enjoyed on site. Other hotel’s facilities include a tour desk, bicycle rental, 24-hour front desk and luggage storage.

Polatsk Train Station is 125 km away, and Minsk National Airport is 240 km from Braslaw Lakes Hotel.
