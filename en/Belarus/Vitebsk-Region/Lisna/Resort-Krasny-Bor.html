Description

Featuring a fitness centre and sauna, Resort Krasny Bor is situated in Lisna village, on the bank of Lake Belaye.

Free WiFi and free private parking are available on site. The rooms offer satellite TV and a safe.

The bathrooms come with a hairdryer, bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries. Some rooms include air conditioning, a fireplace and seating area.

Guests can dine in the on-site restaurant or use on-site barbecue facilities. Other facilities available in the resort are free bicycle rental, a 24-hour front desk and concierge service.

The distance to Polatsk Train Station is 80 km from Resort Krasny Bor. Minsk National Airport is 320 km away.
