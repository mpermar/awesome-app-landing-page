Description

A private sandy beach is offered at this hotel complex on the Lesnaya River, a 10-minute drive from Brest city centre.

Free WiFi is available in the restaurant and at the front desk of Rynkauka Tourist Complex.

The property has a sauna, cold plunge pool and fireplace. The air-conditioned rooms at the Rynkauka Complex Brest are simply furnished with warm colour schemes.

All rooms include satellite TV, a private bathroom and a view of the Lesnaya River.

A hot breakfast and a selection of Russian, Belorussian and European dishes are served in the spacious restaurant with its traditional wooden interiors.

Gazebos and barbecue facilities are also available. The surrounding area is ideal for hiking, cycling and fishing.

Guests are welcome to enjoy the outdoor playground, small zoo and the volleyball court.

Rynkauka Tourist Complex offers free parking and a 24-hour reception. A shuttle service is available to Brest Airport (15 minutes) and Brest Train Station (20 minutes).
