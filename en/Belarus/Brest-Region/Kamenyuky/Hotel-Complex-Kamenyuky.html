Description

Featuring free WiFi, Hotel Complex Kamenyuky offers accommodation in Belovezhskaya Pushcha National Park, 1 km from Kamenyuky.

An indoor pool, a sauna and a fitness centre are featured here. Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant and bar.

Barbecue facilities are also available at surcharge. Every room comes with a TV, a fridge and an electric kettle.

Certain units include a seating area where you can relax. The rooms come with a private bathroom fitted with a shower.

There is a 24-hour front desk and a small gift shop at the property. This hotel has ski storage space and ski equipment hire is available.

You can play tennis and table tennis at the hotel, as well as go fishing or cycling.
