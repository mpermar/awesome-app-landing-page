Description

Offering free WiFi throughout the property, Forum is situated in Ratomka, 9 km from Minsk.

The hotel is surrounded by forests and features a gym, an indoor pool, a sauna and a spa bath.

Private parking is available on site. There are four gazebos with barbecue facilities at the property.

All rooms offer a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a minibar. Regular rooms offer a balcony, while suite types feature a terrace with an outdoor dining area.

The private bathrooms are fitted with a shower or a bath. Guests can have a meal at the hotel's restaurant that features a terrace overlooking the pine wood.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. Shuttle services are offered. Various activities can be enjoyed in the surroundings, including skiing and cycling, and the hotel offers bike and ski hire.

You can play billiards and table tennis at Forum, and there is also a children's playground.

Kryzhouka Train Station is within a 10-minute walk of the property. Zaslavskoye Reservoir is a 15-minute walk.
