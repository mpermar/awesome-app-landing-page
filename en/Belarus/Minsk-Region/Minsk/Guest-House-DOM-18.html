Description

All rooms at the guesthouse come with a seating area and a flat-screen TV. Some rooms come with a kitchen with a dishwasher and an oven. Guest rooms include a desk.

Breakfast is available each morning, and includes continental and à la carte options.

Guest House DOM 18 has a grill.

The accommodation provides a laundry service, as well as business facilities like fax and photocopying. Staff at the 24-hour front desk can give tips on the area.

Belarusian Great Patriotic War Museum is 6 miles from Guest House DOM 18, while Belarusian State Circus is 6 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Minsk-2 Airport, 24 miles from the guesthouse.

Moskovsky District is a great choice for travelers interested in food, friendly locals and walking.
