Description

Located only 3. 5 km from the historical Minsk city centre, this apartment complex features a 24-hour reception, free WiFi and a free parking.

Apartment Complex Comfort units come with a flat-screen TV, hairdryer and balcony.

They feature an elegant dining area and a fully equipped kitchen. Various cafes, bars and restaurants can be found within a 5-minute drive of the property.

Also a buffet breakfast is served every morning at surcharge. Grushevka Metro Station is 750 metres from the Apartment Complex.

Minsk Train Station is 3 km away, and the distance to Minsk National Airport is 44 km, and a shuttle service can be arranged on request.
