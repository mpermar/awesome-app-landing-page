Description

Aqua-Minsk Hotel is located in Minsk, 150 metres from Lebyazhy Waterpark. The hotel offers its guests to enjoy a free access to aquazone in Water Park "Lebyazhy" and gym.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant too. Rooms include a TV with satellite channels.

Certain rooms have views of the lake or garden. All rooms have a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. Bike hire is available at this hotel and the area is popular for cycling.

National Exhibition Centre BelExpo is 5 km from Aqua-Minsk Hotel, while Holy Spirit Cathedral is 8 km away.
