Description

Situated in Sa Pa in the Lao Cai Region, 8 km from Fansipan Mountain, Sapa Highland Resort & Spa features an indoor pool, an on-site restaurant, a spa and fitness centre.

Guests can enjoy free WiFi access throughout the property, as well as free parking.

All rooms are fitted with air-conditioning, a flat-screen cable TV, and refrigerator.

Private bathroom comes with a shower and bidet. For your comfort, you will find bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries.

You will find a 24-hour front desk where staff can assist guests with currency exchange, luggage storage and tour arrangements.

Guests can enjoy daily buffet breakfast at Four Seasons Restaurant, as well as lunch and dinner with à la carte menu.

Room service and special diet menu are available on request. The hotel also offers bike hire and car hire.

Sa Pa Lake is 800 metres from Sapa Highland Resort & Spa, while Sa Pa Central Square is 300 metres from the property.
