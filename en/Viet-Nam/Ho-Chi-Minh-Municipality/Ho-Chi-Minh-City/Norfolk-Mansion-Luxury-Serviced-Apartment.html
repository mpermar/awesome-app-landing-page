Description

Located in Ho Chi Minh City, Norfolk Mansion - Luxury Serviced Apartments offers spacious apartments with dining room, fully equipped kitchen and flat-screen TV.

It features an outdoor pool, fitness centre and free Wi-Fi. Norfolk Mansion - Luxury Serviced Apartments is within walking distance of famous landmarks like Ben Thanh Market, Reunification Palace and Opera House.

Norfolk offers apartments with wooden flooring and private balconies. En suite bathrooms come with shower facilities and a bathtub.

Kitchens are complete with kitchenware, microwave and fridge. Guests can play a game of billiards, or relax at the sauna.

They can also take a dip in the spa pool. For a quiet read, a library is available.

The two restaurants at Norfolk Mansion - Luxury Serviced Apartments serve international, as well as Chinese dishes.

Alternatively, room service is provided. Continental Buffet Breakfast is served at this property.
