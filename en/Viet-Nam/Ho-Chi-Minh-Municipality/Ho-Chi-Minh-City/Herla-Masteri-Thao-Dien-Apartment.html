Description

Located in the heart of District 2 along the Hanoi Highway in Ho Chi Minh City, Brandnew 2BR Apartment features a balcony that opens up to views of the city and the Saigon river.

Free WiFi is provided in the apartment. The unit is equipped with a seating area, sofa and flat-screen TV.

There is a washing machine and a kitchen with a refrigerator. The private bathroom is fitted with a shower.

There is an outdoor pool on site where guests can enjoy a swim. Strategically located, the property is a minute's walk to Vincom Megamall Thao Dien.

Jade Emperor Pagoda is 8 km from Brandnew 2BR Apartment, while Opera House is 8 km away.

The nearest airport is Tan Son Nhat International Airport, 12 km from Brandnew 2BR Apartment.

Guests can enjoy meals at the restaurants surrounding the property. Family friendly activities and nightlife options are also available in the vicinity.
