Description

Hoasun Boutique Apartment - Vinhomes Central Park offers contemporary accommodation along the Saigon River and guests can enjoy fresh air from the large park nearby.

This air-conditioned apartment consists of a living room with sofa, cable flat-screen TV and a private balcony.

Kitchen is equipped with a microwave. Private bathroom comes with a walk-in shower, hairdryer and free toiletries.

The property is a 5-minute drive to Notre Dame Cathedral and Reunification Palace.

Ben Thanh Market can be reached within 10-minute driving distance. The nearest airport is Tan Son Nhat International Airport, a 30-minute drive away.
