Description

The apartment features 1 bedroom, a kitchen with an oven and a microwave, a washing machine and a bathroom with free toiletries. A flat-screen TV is featured.

Languages spoken at the reception include Chinese, English and Vietnamese.

Garden Gate offers a range of wellness facilities including a sauna and a fitness center. The accommodation has a garden and sun terrace, which can be enjoyed by guests if the weather permits.

Jade Emperor Pagoda is 2 miles from Garden Gate. The nearest airport is Tan Son Nhat International Airport, 1.9 miles from the apartment.

Phu Nhuan is a great choice for travelers interested in people watching, coffee and restaurants.
