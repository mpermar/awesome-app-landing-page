Description

On the banks of the scenic Saigon River, the 4-star Riverside Serviced Apartments come with free Wi-Fi, an outdoor pool and 2 tennis courts.

A free scheduled boat or bus transfer brings guests to and from Bach Dang Port. Riverside Serviced Apartments provides a free scheduled bus transfer to downtown Ho Chi Minh.

There is also a free bus service that goes to An Phu Supermarket and Metro Hypermarket every Wednesday and Saturday.

Spread over 13 buildings, the air-conditioned apartments all come with a living room and full kitchen facilities.

A cable TV, oven and stove are included. A private balcony enjoys views of the garden or river.

En suite bathrooms come with a bathtub and shower. The hotel is a 45-minute drive from Saigon Train Station and a 25-minute drive from Tan Son Nhat International Airport.

Parking is free. Guests can wash their clothes for free at the self-service laundry room.

There is also an on-site minimart, a business centre and indoor/outdoor children playgrounds.

Asian and Western dishes are served at Blu Bar & Grill, while coffee and cakes can be enjoyed at the bakery shop.
