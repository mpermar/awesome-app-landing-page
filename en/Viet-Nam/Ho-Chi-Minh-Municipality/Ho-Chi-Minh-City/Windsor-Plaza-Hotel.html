Description

The luxurious Windsor Plaza Hotel towers 25 stories above Cholon District (Chinatown) in central Ho Chi Minh City.

Just next to An Dong Shopping Centre, it features a rooftop swimming pool and the award-winning Ngan Dinh Cantonese Restaurant.

Modern and elegant, the air-conditioned rooms feature large windows with sweeping views of the city.

Each well-appointed room is fitted with a seating area and TV. Windsor Plaza Hotel is 3 km from the Reunification Palace and Ben Thanh Market.

Ho Chi Minh Museum is 4 km from the hotel. Guests can work out at the fitness centre, relax with a massage at the spa, or arrange a day trip at the tour desk.

The hotel also provides currency exchange services and a business centre. Café Central An Dong serves tasty Asian and international buffet.
