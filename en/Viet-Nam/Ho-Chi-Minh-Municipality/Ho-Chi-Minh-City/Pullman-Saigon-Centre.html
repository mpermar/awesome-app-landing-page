Description

Local attractions, including the Saigon River, Reunification Palace, Opera House and Notre Dame Cathedral, are just under a mile away. Tan Son Nhat International Airport is a 45-minute drive from Pullman Saigon Centre.

Offering city views, each air-conditioned room features a flat-screen cable TV, minibar and safe. The private bathrooms include a shower and free toiletries.

Guests can work out at the hotel’s fitness center or relax in the sauna and hot tub. Luggage storage services are available at the 24-hour front desk.

The rooftop restaurant serves breakfast and features a unique circle grill concept and live show kitchen. A selection of Vietnamese beer, tea and coffee, along with Eastern and Western dishes can be ordered at the lobby bar.

District 1 is a great choice for travelers interested in museums, nightlife and markets.
