Description

All units comprise a seating area, a dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen with various cooking facilities, including a dishwasher, an oven, a microwave and a toaster. There's a private bathroom with hot tub in all units, along with free toiletries and a hair dryer.

A continental breakfast is served daily at the property. Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant, which specializes in Belgian and Brazilian cuisine and also offers kosher and vegetarian options.

The apartment has an outdoor pool.

Both a bicycle rental service and a car rental service are available at An Dinh RiverGate 2, while cycling can be enjoyed nearby.

Ben Thanh Market is a 15-minute walk from the accommodation, while Nha Rong Wharf is 0.8 miles away. The nearest airport is Tan Son Nhat International Airport, 5 miles from An Dinh RiverGate 2.

District 4 is a great choice for travelers interested in local food, restaurants and culture.
