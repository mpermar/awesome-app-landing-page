Description

The apartment has 3 bedrooms, a living room, and a kitchen with a dining area and an oven. The air-conditioned apartment also has a seating area and a bathroom with a bidet.

Staff at the apartment are always available to provide guidance at the reception.

The property has a fitness center. Masteri Sunny Apartment features a garden and sun terrace, which can be enjoyed by guests if the weather permits.

Union Square is 3.2 miles from the accommodation, while General Post Office is 3.3 miles away. The nearest airport is Tan Son Nhat International Airport, 6 miles from the property.
