Description

Situated along the bank of Saigon River, Saigon Domaine Luxury Residences offers serviced apartments and boutique suites in Binh Quoi in Ho Chi Minh City.

It features an outdoor pool, on-site restaurant and fitness centre. Saigon Domaine Luxury Residences provides a free shuttle bus and free speed boat service to Bach Dang Harbour in the city centre of District 1, situated 15-minutes away.

The nearest airport is Tan Son Nhat International Airport, a 30-minute drive away.

All well-appointed apartments come air-conditioned and soundproofed, as well as feature large windows offering plenty of natural light.

A wardrobe, ironing facilities and personal safe are included. A TV and DVD Player are available for your entertainment.

Kitchen is equipped with a stovetop, dishwasher, minibar and an electric kettle. En suite bathroom has shower facility, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Guests can relax with a sauna session, or enjoy soothing massages at the spa. Going windsurfing or cycling is another good recreation chioce.

Besides room service, guests can head to Cormoran restaurant for other food and beverage options.

Light snacks and refreshing beverages are served at the pool and Jules Verne bars.
