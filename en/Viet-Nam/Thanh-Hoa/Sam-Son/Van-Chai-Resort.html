Description

Set in the tranquil district of Sam Son Beach, Van Chai Resort offers beautifully decorated rooms equipped with free Wi-Fi and 2 swimming pools.

Guests can lounge by the outdoor or indoor pool or treat themselves at the spa or enjoy a traditional massage.

A 24-hour front desk can assist with laundry/ironing services and room service. For convenience, airport shuttle services are available at a surcharge.

Air-conditioned rooms at Van Chai Resort are equipped with a fan, a desk, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a balcony with a garden view.

Private bathrooms are equipped with a bath and shower facilities. Guests can dine at 2 dining options, Ocean Restaurant – which serves seafood and Vietnamese cuisines while Ngu Ong Restaurant serves western cuisines.

Spread across 2 hectares of lush tropical gardens, the resort is 170 km away from Hanoi Capital.
