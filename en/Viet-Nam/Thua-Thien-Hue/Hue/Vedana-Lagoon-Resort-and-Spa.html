Description

The spacious villas and bungalows at Vedana feature modern décor with traditional accents. A flat-screen satellite TV, DVD home entertainment system and mini-bar are included. Private bathroom comes with shower facility and free toiletries. Room service is provided 24 hours.

Guests can enjoy relaxing massage treatments and beauty services at the spa. Tai Chi and yoga classes are available. The resort also opearates a tour desk and water sports facilities.

Fresh seafood and fantastic views are offered at The Horizon Restaurant. Cooking classes and private dinner can be arranged at The Lantern House upon request. A romantic private dining experience under the stars is available at The Twilight Experience.

It is 24 mi to Hue and 16 mi to Hue Airport from Vedana Lagoon Resort & Spa.

"It is located at a hidden lagoon lake which has no other hotels nearby. Very quiet place but frequent diesel train sound coming from the railway that connects the Vietnam from north to south reminds me of the boyhood when the railway system was not electrified in my country. The pool villa we stayed was visited by wildlife such as a baby bat and many frogs that children really liked.Even though it is isolated from the highway No.1, the resort is easily accessible with a shuttle which took us to the Hue Royal Palace before we checked in (with surcharge). The breakfast was excellent with many choices and a bowl of hot noodle in Hue style. They have free bicycle for guests' rental. We took a ride three-seated one that the children loved.
