Description

Tastefully decorated, all rooms have air conditioning, heating and a safety deposit box. Certain rooms feature a seating area for you to relax in after a busy day. A satellite TV and mini-bar are included for your convenience. Private bathroom comes with shower facilities, a hairdryer and free toiletries. Room service is available upon request.

Other facilities include an on-site bar, garden and business facilities. The resort also operates 24-hour reception where staff, who can speak in 5 different languages, can help you with a variety of services, including luggage storage, concierge service, tour arrangements and airport transfer.

Dong Ba Market is 2,150 feet from the hotel, while Dieu De National Pagoda is 0.6 mi away. The nearest airport is Phu Bai Airport, 8.7 mi away.

Boasting panoramic views of the Perfume River, Riverside Restaurant serves delectable dishes of Asian and Western dishes. Royal Restaurant offers royal meals of the Nguyen Dynasty, while Hoa Mai Restaurant has extensive variety of Vietnamese specialties. Korean cuisine can be enjoy at Dae Jang Geum Restaurant.
