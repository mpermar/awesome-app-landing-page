Description

Set in Ninh Binh, 18 km from Bai Dinh Temple, TRANG AN SECRET GARDEN offers accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a bar.

Among the facilities at this property are room service and a shared lounge, along with free WiFi throughout the property.

The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, a shared kitchen and currency exchange for guests.

At the resort, all rooms are fitted with a patio with a garden view. With a private bathroom fitted with a shower and free toiletries, rooms at TRANG AN SECRET GARDEN also have a lake view.

All guest rooms at the accommodation feature air conditioning and a wardrobe. Guests at TRANG AN SECRET GARDEN can enjoy a à la carte or an Asian breakfast.

The resort offers a children's playground. You can play billiards at TRANG AN SECRET GARDEN, and bike hire is available.

Phat Diem Cathedral is 33 km from the accommodation, while Ninh Binh Stadium is 5 km from the property.
