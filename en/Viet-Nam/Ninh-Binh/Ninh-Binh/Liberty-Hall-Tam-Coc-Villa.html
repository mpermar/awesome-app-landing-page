Description

Located in Ninh Binh, 26 km from Bai Dinh Temple, Liberty Hall Tam Coc Villa provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a seasonal outdoor swimming pool and a bar.

With free WiFi, this 3-star hotel has a shared lounge and a garden. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.

All units in the hotel are equipped with a kettle. At Liberty Hall Tam Coc Villa every room is fitted with a terrace, a private bathroom and a flat-screen TV.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental breakfast. Liberty Hall Tam Coc Villa offers 3-star accommodation with an indoor pool and children's playground.

Bike hire and car hire are available at the hotel and the area is popular for cycling and fishing.

Phat Diem Cathedral is 34 km from Liberty Hall Tam Coc Villa, while Thung Nham Bird Park Ecotourism is 8 km away.
