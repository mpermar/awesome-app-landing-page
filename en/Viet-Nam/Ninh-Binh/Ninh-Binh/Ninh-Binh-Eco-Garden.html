Description

All of the air-conditioned units feature a private bathroom, flat-screen TV, fully-equipped kitchen and terrace. An oven and microwave are also provided, as well as a electric tea pot.

The homestay offers a à la carte or Asian breakfast. Guests can enjoy a meal at the in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of Australian dishes and also offers vegetarian, dairy-free and kosher options.

The property has a fitness center.

In addition to an outdoor pool, Ninh Binh Eco Garden also provides a children's playground and shared lounge.

Phat Diem Cathedral is 19 miles from the accommodation, while Thung Nham Bird Park Ecotourism is 5 miles from the property.
