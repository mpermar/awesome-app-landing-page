Description

With Bai Dinh Temple reachable in 14 km, Trang An Bungalow features accommodation, a restaurant, free bikes, an outdoor swimming pool and a bar.

Both WiFi and private parking are accessible at the lodge free of charge. Guests at Trang An Bungalow can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

The accommodation offers a children's playground. After a day of cycling or fishing, guests can relax in the garden or in the shared lounge area.

Phat Diem Cathedral is 39 km from Trang An Bungalow, while Ninh Binh Stadium is 11 km from the property.
