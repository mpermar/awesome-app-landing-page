Description

Situated in Ninh Binh, Tam Coc Green Garden Homestay features free WiFi, and guests can enjoy a restaurant, a bar and a garden.

For added convenience, the property can provide towels and bed linen for an extra charge.

A à la carte breakfast is available each morning at the lodge. Tam Coc Green Garden Homestay offers a children's playground.

Both a bicycle rental service and a car rental service are available at the accommodation, while hiking and cycling can be enjoyed nearby.

Bai Dinh Temple is 27 km from Tam Coc Green Garden Homestay, while Phat Diem Cathedral is 35 km from the property.
