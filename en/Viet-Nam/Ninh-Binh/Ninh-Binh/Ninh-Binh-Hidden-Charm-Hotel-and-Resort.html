Description

At the hotel, the rooms come with a desk and a flat-screen TV. The private bathroom is equipped with a bath or shower. The rooms at Ninh Binh Hidden Charm feature air conditioning and a closet.

A buffet breakfast is served at the property.

The accommodation has a playground.

Around-the-clock assistance is available at the reception.
