Description

Golden Holiday Hotel & Spa offers affordable rooms with flat-screen TVs, a 10-minute drive An Bang Beach and Hoi Ans city centre.

Wi-Fi and parking are free of charge. Overlooking Hoi Ans skyline, modern rooms at Golden Holiday Hotel & Spa are fitted with a satellite TV, safe and an electric kettle.

A minibar is also provided. Private bathrooms have toiletries and a hairdryer. Guests can lounge by the outdoor pool.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can provide car hire and airport shuttle services.

A tour desk with ticket service helps with travel arrangements. Golden Holiday Hotel & Spa's restaurant offers a range of local specialities, European and Asian dishes.
