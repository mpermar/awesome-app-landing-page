Description

The villa offers a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with free toiletries, a hair dryer and shower. A fridge and electric tea pot are also provided.

The daily breakfast offers continental, buffet or Full English/Irish options. At Lavini Hoi An Boutique Villa you'll find a restaurant serving Vietnamese, Asian and pizza cuisine.

The accommodation has a playground.

A car rental service is available at this property.

Hoi An Historic Museum is an 11-minute walk from Lavini Hoi An Boutique Villa, while Assembly Hall of Chaozhou Chinese Congregation is an 11-minute walk from the property. The nearest airport is Da Nang International Airport, 14 miles from the villa.

Cam Chau is a great choice for travelers interested in beaches, relaxation and shopping.
