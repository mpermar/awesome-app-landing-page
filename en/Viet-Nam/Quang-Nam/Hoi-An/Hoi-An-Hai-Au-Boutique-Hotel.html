Description

Located on the edge of Hoi An's historical Old Town, Hai Au offers modern rooms with free WiFi.

The hotel features an outdoor pool, a restaurant, and a travel desk. It is a 10-minute walk to the centre of the Old Town from the hotel, while Danang International Airport is a 45-minute drive away.

Air-conditioned rooms come with a balcony and a small seating area. Each room has cable TV and a minibar.

The private bathroom is fitted with a bathtub and water heater. Some rooms also include a computer.

Computers with internet are available at the lobby for guests' use. Guests can use the in-house bicycle to explore the Old Town, which features shops and dining options.

The travel desk offers tour and ticket bookings, while the 24-hour reception provides currency exchange.

Buffet breakfast is served daily. The restaurant offers Asian and Western dishes, plus local specialities.
