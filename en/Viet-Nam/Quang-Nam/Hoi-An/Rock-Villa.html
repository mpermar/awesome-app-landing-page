Description

Set in the ancient town of Hoi An, Rock Villa features an outdoor pool and private restaurant.

The property also has a private beach area and free WiFi in its public areas. Located on the banks of the De Vong River and surrounded by green rice fields, the accommodation is only 1 km from Cua Dai Public Beach and 3.

5 km from Hoi An city. Rooms feature air conditioning, a seating area, wardrobe, safe and flat-screen cable TV.

Private bathroom comes with a shower, towels and hairdryer. A minibar and electric kettle are provided.

The friendly staff at Rock Villa's frontdesk can assist guests with laundry services and airport transfer or tour arrangements.

Activities like cooking class and excursions to My Son Temple, Marble Mountain and Cham Island for snorkeling can also be organised.

A hearty Vietnamese or Western breakfast can be enjoyed at the in-house restaurant.
