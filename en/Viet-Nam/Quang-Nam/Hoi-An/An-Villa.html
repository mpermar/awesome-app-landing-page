Description

Situated just a 10-minute ride by bicycle to Cua Dai beach, An Villa provides elegant accommodation in Hoi An.

Nestled among rice paddy fields and rural houses, the property welcomes guests with an outdoor swimming pool, on-site restaurant and cafe.

Tastefully furnished, each unit comes with different design and features a private balcony or garden and a kitchenette.

En suite bathroom in each unit is fitted with a bath or shower facility, hairdryer and free toiletries.

Bathrobes, slippers and towels are available. Selected units come with outdoor bathtub and/or Vietnamese-style barbecue facility.

Other facilities at An Villa include a children's play area, an outdoor lounge area and Vietnamese-style barbecue facility.

Guests can do yoga or meditation, or play board games at the property. The property operates 24-hour front desk where staff can assist you with queries or tour arrangements and bicycle/motorbike rentals upon request.

Mum's Kitchen is an all-day dining restaurant offering delectable Asian dishes for in-house guests.

Guests can enjoy authentic Vietnamese or European dishes for a hearty breakfast with coffee and tea.

Assembly Hall of the Hainan Chinese Congregation and Hoi An Historic Museum are within 3 km from An Villa, while Hoi An Ancient Town is 3.

3 km away. The nearest airport is Da Nang International Airport, 25 km from An Villa.
