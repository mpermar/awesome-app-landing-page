Description

Rice River Villa features a restaurant, outdoor swimming pool, a fitness centre and bar in Hoi An.

Each accommodation at the 3-star hotel has garden views, and guests can enjoy access to a garden and to a private beach area.

The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, a shared kitchen and currency exchange for guests.

All units in the hotel are equipped with air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a kitchen, a dining area and a private bathroom with a hairdryer, a bidet and a shower.

At Rice River Villa every room is fitted with a desk and a shared bathroom. The accommodation offers a la carte or Asian breakfast.

Rice River Villa offers a sun terrace. The area is popular for cycling, and bike hire and car hire are available at the hotel.

Hoi An Historic Museum is 2. 2 km from Rice River Villa, while Assembly Hall of Chaozhou Chinese Congregation is 2.

8 km away. The nearest airport is Da Nang International, 27 km from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
