Description

At the resort, every room includes a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. The rooms include air conditioning, and some accommodations at Hoi An Field Boutique Resort & Spa have a terrace. The rooms include a closet.

A buffet breakfast is available daily at the property. At the accommodation you'll find a restaurant serving Asian and local cuisine.

The area is popular for cycling, and free use of bicycles is available at Hoi An Field Boutique Resort & Spa.

Assembly Hall of Chaozhou Chinese Congregation is 1.2 miles from the resort, while Hoi An Historic Museum is 1.4 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Da Nang International Airport, 16 miles from Hoi An Field Boutique Resort & Spa.

Cam Thanh is a great choice for travelers interested in friendly locals, beaches and old-town exploration.
