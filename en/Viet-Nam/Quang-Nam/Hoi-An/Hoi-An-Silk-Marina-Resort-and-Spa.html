Description

Hoi An Marina Resort & Spa is within a convenient 7-minute walk from Hoi An Ancient Town, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

It features a spa and wellness centre, while free Wi-Fi access is available in the entire resort.

Fitted with parquet flooring, air-conditioned villas are equipped with a personal safe, a flat-screen cable TV and minibar.

A wardrobe and seating area are also included. En suite bathrooms come with a hairdryer, bathtub, shower facilities and free toiletries.

Operating a 24-hour front desk and security, Hoi An Marina Resort & Spa provides luggage storage, laundry and car rental services.

The tour desk can assist with bookings of tour excursions and travel arrangements.

Accompanied with beautiful views of the river, the in-house Marine Restaurant serves Vietnamese and international cuisines in a buffet style.

24-hour room service is available. The resort is approximately 4 km to An Bang Beach, while Da Nang International Airport can be reached within 30 km away.
