Description

Located 3. 5 km from Assembly Hall of Chaozhou Chinese Congregation in Hoi An, SILK SENSE HOI AN RIVER RESORT features free WiFi.

The resort has a year-round outdoor pool, barbecue and children's playground. All rooms are air-conditioned and come with a seating area, safety deposit box and wardrobe.

Rooms come with a private bathroom with a bath, shower and bidet, with bathrobes, towels and hairdryer provided.

Complimentary use of minibar is offered every day. You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property where staff can assist you with luggage storage.

Other facilities include a sauna, spa centre and fitness centre The resort also offers free use of bicycles.

Hoi An Historic Museum and Assembly Hall of the Hainan Chinese Congregation are 3.

6 km from the property. The nearest airport is Da Nang International Airport, 23 km from SILK SENSE HOI AN RIVER RESORT.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the on site restaurant and drinks at the on site bar,.
