Description

At the hotel, each room has a desk. Every room has a private bathroom with a shower, free toiletries and a hair dryer. At Vinci Villa Hoi An each room includes air conditioning and a flat-screen TV.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a à la carte or an Asian breakfast.

The reception at Vinci Villa Hoi An can provide tips on the area.

Japanese Covered Bridge is 2,100 feet from the hotel, while Assembly Hall of Chaozhou Chinese Congregation is a 10-minute walk from the property. The nearest airport is Da Nang International Airport, 14 miles from the property.

Son Phong is a great choice for travelers interested in walking, city walks and old-town exploration.
