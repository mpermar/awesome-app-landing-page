Description

It takes 5 minutes to walk to the famous Hoi An Ancient Town, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Phuoc Lam Pagoda is within a 10-minute drive, while a 45-minute drive takes you to Danang International Airport.

Featuring classic hardwood furnishings, all rooms are elegantly furnished in light beige and have air conditioning, as well as a ceiling fan. All rooms offer additional space, a personal safe and desk. A cable flat-screen TV, mini-bar and an electric kettle are included. Private bathroom comes with a bathtub or shower facility, hairdryer and free toiletries.

Guests can relax with a rejuvenating massage or enjoy a dip in the hot tub at the spa, as well as have a good work out in the fitness center. Operating 24-hour front desk, Hoi An Historic Hotel also has a tour desk where attentive staff can assist you with a variety of services, including baggage storage, currency exchange and tour arrangements.

Located in a rich garden setting, Indochine Corner offers traditional Vietnamese cuisine in an open air or indoor seating, while The Oaks restaurant is spread across the oak trees and serves an array of Asian and European cuisine. Western Bar has a selection of Italian dishes, including pastas, salads and pizzas from the wood-fired oven.

Son Phong is a great choice for travelers interested in walking, city walks and old-town exploration.
