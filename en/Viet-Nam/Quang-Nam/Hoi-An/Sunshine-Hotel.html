Description

Hotel Sunshine is one mile from An Bang Beach and a one-hour drive from Da Nang International Airport. UNESCO World Heritage Site, My Son Sanctuary, is 37 miles away.

Rooms offer grounds or pool views, with select rooms having a balcony. A satellite TV, minibar and electric teakettle are provided. Some rooms come equipped with a flat-screen TV.

Bicycles are available for free for guests wishing to explore the surroundings. The tour desk can make sightseeing arrangements or provide currency exchange services. Laundry and dry cleaning services are available.

A selection of Western and Asian dishes are served in the restaurant. Drinks are offered in the bar.

Cam Chau is a great choice for travelers interested in beaches, relaxation and shopping.
