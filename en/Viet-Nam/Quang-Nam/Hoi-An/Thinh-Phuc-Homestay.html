Description

A vegetarian or vegan breakfast is available each morning at the property. At the homestay you'll find a restaurant serving American cuisine. Vegan, gluten-free and dairy-free options can also be requested.

Thinh Phuc Homestay has a playground.

Cycling can be enjoyed nearby and a car rental service is available.

Assembly Hall of Chaozhou Chinese Congregation is a 16-minute walk from the accommodation, while Hoi An Historic Museum is 1.1 miles away. The nearest airport is Da Nang International Airport, 15 miles from the property.

Cam Chau is a great choice for travelers interested in beaches, relaxation and shopping.
