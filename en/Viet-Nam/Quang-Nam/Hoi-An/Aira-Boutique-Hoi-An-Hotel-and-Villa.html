Description

Located right by the white-sand An Bang Beach, Aira Boutique Hoi An Hotel & Spa offers luxurious rooms and suites with an on-site Vietnamese restaurant, a day spa and 2 outdoor swimming pools.

Nestled in lush tropical gardens, guests can enjoy complimentary WiFi access and make use of all in-house leisure facilities.

Spacious and stylish, each unit has contemporary furnishings and amenities. A cable flat-screen TV, a seating area, minibar and an electric kettle are included.

Attached bathroom is equipped with shower facility and free toiletries. For beach lovers, Aira Boutique Hoi An Hotel & Spa provides private sun beds, umbrellas and towels on the beach.

Water sports can also be enjoyed on the beach in front of the hotel. Other facilities and activities include a library, billiards and cooking class.

An array of restaurants, bars and shops can be found nearby, while a golf course is 4 km away.

The UNESCO-heritage Hoi An ancient town is less than 5 km from the hotel, while Da Nang International Airport is only a 20-minute drive away.
