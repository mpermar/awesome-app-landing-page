Description

Located within 3-minute walking distance from the nearest beach, Seasnails Homestay offers accommodation in Hoi An.

It provides free WiFi in public areas and also features a sun terrace. Certain units feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

Some rooms include views of the sea or garden. All rooms come with a private bathroom equipped with a shower.

For your comfort, you will find slippers, free toiletries and a hairdryer. A flat-screen TV is offered.

You will find a 24-hour front desk and hairdresser's at the property. This homestay has a private beach area and free use of bicycles is available.

Motorbike rental is available at this homestay and the area is popular for snorkelling.

Cua Dai Beach and An Bang Beach are within a 5-minute drive from Seasnails Homestay, while Tra Que Garden is an approximate 10-minute drive away.

A 50-minute drive can take you to Kim Bong Carpentry Village. The nearest airport is Da Nang International Airport, approximately 40-minute drive away.
