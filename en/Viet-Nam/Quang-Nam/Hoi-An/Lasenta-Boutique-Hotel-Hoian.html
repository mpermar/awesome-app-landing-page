Description

Lasenta Boutique Hotel Hoian offers accommodation set in Hoi An in the Quang Nam Region.

It features an outdoor pool and guests can enjoy meals at the in-house restaurant or have a drink at the bar.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. Guests can enjoy free use of bicycles, and free shuttle service to Hoi An Ancient Town and An Bang Beach with fixed schedule.

Lasenta Boutique Hotel Hoian is about 1 km from Hoi An Ancient Town and 3 km from An Bang beach.

Hoi An Historic Museum and Assembly Hall of the Hainan Chinese Congregation are 1.

1 km away. The nearest airport is Da Nang International Airport, 29 km from the property.

Earthy shades of green and brown harmonise with white colour are featured in most units of Lasenta Boutique Hotel Hoian.

They also feature air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and an electric kettle. Private bathroom comes with shower facility, free toiletries, bathrobes and slippers.

Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for tour arrangements, ticketing and concierge services.
