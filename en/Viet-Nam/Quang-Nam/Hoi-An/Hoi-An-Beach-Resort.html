Description

Nestled between Cua Dai Beach and De Vong River, Hoi An Beach Resort features 2 outdoor pools.

It provides free Wi-Fi and two-way shuttle services to Hoi An Ancient Town. Rooms at Resort Hoi An come with private balconies overlooking the garden, river or sea.

Each room is equipped with a TV, safety deposit box and tea/coffee making facilities.

Local cooking classes begin with a guided boat trip to Hoi An Market. Waterlily Spa offers Vietnamese massage therapies.

Other recreational activities include a game of billiards or a workout in the fitness centre.

At River Breeze Restaurant, guests can eat indoors or on the balcony overlooking the river.

Snacks and refreshments can be enjoyed at the Sunshine Bar and the beachfront Sands Bar.

Hoi An Beach Resort is a 45-minute drive from Danang International Airport and 4 km from Hoi An’s town centre.
