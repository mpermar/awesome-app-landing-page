Description

At the hotel, all rooms come with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. The rooms have a electric tea pot, while selected rooms will provide you with a balcony and others also feature pool views. All guest rooms at Hoian Central Hotel feature air conditioning and a closet.

A buffet breakfast is served at the property. At the accommodation you'll find a restaurant serving Asian and American cuisine.

Hoian Central Hotel has an outdoor pool.

Japanese Covered Bridge is 0.6 miles from the hotel, while Kim Bong Carpentry Village is 1.1 miles from the property. Da Nang International Airport is 14 miles away.

Son Phong is a great choice for travelers interested in walking, city walks and old-town exploration.
