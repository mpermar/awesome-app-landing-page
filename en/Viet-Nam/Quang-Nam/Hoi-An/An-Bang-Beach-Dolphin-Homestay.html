Description

Offering a pool and a private beach area, An Bang Beach Dolphin Homestay is situated in Hoi An in the Quang Nam Region, a 2-minute walk from An Bang Beach.

Every room is equipped with a flat-screen TV and fridge. En suite bathroom comes with hot/cold shower facilities.

For your convenience, slippers and free toiletries are provided. An Bang Beach Dolphin Homestay offers free WiFi and private parking.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. Other facilities include a shared kitchen.

Free use of bicycles is available at this hotel and the area is popular for cycling.

Assembly Hall of the Fujian Chinese Congregation and Assembly Hall of Chaozhou Chinese Congregation are 4 km from the property.
