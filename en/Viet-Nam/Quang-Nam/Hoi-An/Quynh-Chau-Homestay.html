Description

Quynh Chau Homestay is located in Hoi An, 200 metres from Japanese Covered Bridge.

Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. Every room at this homestay is air conditioned and comes with a flat-screen TV.

Some rooms feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day. All rooms have a private bathroom equipped with a bath.

For your comfort, you will find bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property.

The homestay also offers bike hire. Assembly Hall of the Cantonese Chinese Congregation is 200 metres from Quynh Chau Homestay, while Hoi An Ancient Town is 100 metres away.
