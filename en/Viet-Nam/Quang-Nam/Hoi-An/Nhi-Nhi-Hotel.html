Description

The air-conditioned rooms feature pool views and a satellite TV. Some rooms have a private balcony. All include an in-room safe and a refrigerator. The private bathroom features a bathtub and hot/cold shower.

Nhi Nhi Hotel is a quarter mile from Riverside Museum of History and Culture and 15 miles from Da Nang Airport.

Guests can plan a day trip at the tour desk. Airport shuttles, currency exchange and bike rentals are also provided. Laundry services are available upon request.

The Restaurant Nhi Nhi serves a range of Vietnamese specialties, European and Asian dishes for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Cam Pho is a great choice for travelers interested in culture, old-town exploration and atmosphere.
