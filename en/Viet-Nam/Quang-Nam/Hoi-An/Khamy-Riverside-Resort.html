Description

Every room comes with air conditioning, and some rooms also feature a balcony.

A buffet breakfast is available each morning at the resort.

Khamy Riverside Resort offers 3-star accommodations with a fitness center. A variety of popular activities are available in the area around the property, including cycling and fishing.

Assembly Hall of Chaozhou Chinese Congregation is a 10-minute walk from the accommodation, while Hoi An Historic Museum is a 15-minute walk away. Da Nang International Airport is 15 miles from the property.

Cam Chau is a great choice for travelers interested in beaches, relaxation and shopping.
