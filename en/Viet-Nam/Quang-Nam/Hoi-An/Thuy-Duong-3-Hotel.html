Description

Rooms at Thuy Duong 3 are equipped with air-conditioning, satellite TV and a minibar. They are spacious and feature private balconies. All rooms have private bathroom facilities.

The tropical grounds are perfect to relax and enjoy sunsets in Hoi An. Guests can enjoy massages and body treatments at the spa. There is also a sauna and an outdoor pool.

Facilities at Thuy Duong 3 Hotel Hoi An include laundry and currency exchange services. There is also a tour desk where guests can make travel arrangements. The business center provides free internet.

Local specialties can be enjoyed at the hotel’s restaurant or in the privacy of guest’s room. European dishes are also available.

Cam Pho is a great choice for travelers interested in culture, old-town exploration and atmosphere.
