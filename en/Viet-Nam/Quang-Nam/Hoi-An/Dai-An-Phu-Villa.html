Description

Located 200 metres from An Bang Beach, Dai An Phu Villa offers contemporary accommodation in Hoi An.

Featuring a garden, the property also boasts an outdoor swimming pool and terrace with furniture.

Guests can enjoy free WiFi access and free private parking on site. All well-lit rooms enjoy air conditioning, a cable flat-screen TV and minibar.

En suite bathroom comes with a rain shower and free toiletries. Views of the pool and/or garden can be seen from certain rooms.

Extras include bathrobes and slippers. The property offers free use of bicycle. Guests can play darts at the property, or approach staff at the 24-hour front desk to be assisted with luggage storage, currency exchange and airport transfer services.

Assembly Hall of Chaozhou Chinese Congregation is 4 km from Dai An Phu Villa, while Hoi An Historic Museum is 4 km from the property.

Da Nang International Airport is 21 km away. Guests can sample Vietnamese cuisine, as well as local fresh seafood at The Beach Restaurant.
