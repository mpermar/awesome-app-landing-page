Description

Strategically located in Hoi An, East West Villa offers elegant and comfortable accommodation with free WiFi access in its public areas.

It features an outdoor pool, restaurant and lush green gardens. Bicycles are available for rent at no charge.

The property is within 1. 8 km from Assembly Hall of Chaozhou Chinese Congregation and Assembly Hall of the Hainan Chinese Congregation.

Hoi An Historic Museum is about 1. 9 km away, while Da Nang International Airport is accessible with a 24 km drive.

Tastefully-furnished, air-conditioned rooms come with tiled flooring, a wardrobe, in-room safe, a flat-screen cable TV and seating area.

Dining table, minibar and electric kettle are also included. The en suite bathroom has a bathtub, shower facility, towels and hairdryer.

At East West Villa, friendly staff can assist guests with laundry/dry cleaning services and airport transfer arrangements.

A common lounge area, library and games room is also available for guests’ convenience.

The in-house restaurant serves a delectable spread of local dishes. A variety of drinks can be ordered at the bar, while room service option is provided.
