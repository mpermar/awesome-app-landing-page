Description

The rooms at the hotel come with a seating area. Rooms have a private bathroom and free toiletries, and certain units at Cam Thanh Village Villas have a terrace. All rooms will provide guests with a desk and a electric tea pot.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy an Asian breakfast.

Guests at Cam Thanh Village Villas will be able to enjoy activities in and around Hoi An, like cycling.

Speaking English and Vietnamese, staff are always on hand to help at the reception.

Hoi An Historic Museum is 2.3 miles from the hotel. The nearest airport is Da Nang International Airport, 16 miles from the property.

Cam Thanh is a great choice for travelers interested in friendly locals, beaches and old-town exploration.
