Description

Offering a refreshing outdoor pool and a spa centre, Venus Hotel and Spa is situated just 900 metres from Japanese Covered Bridge in Hoi An.

The hotel features a sun terrace where guests can relax, and a bar where guests can choose to indulge on their choice of drink.

Each room at this hotel is air conditioned and is equipped with a TV. A kettle is available for coffee/tea making.

Some units have a seating area. Rooms come with a private bathroom that have a bath or shower.

For your comfort, you will find bath robes and slippers. Venus Hotel and Spa offers free WiFi throughout the property.

There is a 24-hour front desk, gift shop, and shops at the property. Bike hire is available at this hotel and the area is popular for cycling.

The hotel also offers car hire. Assembly Hall of the Cantonese Chinese Congregation is 1 km from Venus Hotel and Spa, while Hoi An Ancient Town is 1.

2 km away. Da Nang International Airport is a 45-minute taxi ride from the property.
