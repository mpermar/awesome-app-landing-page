Description

All guest rooms come with a flat-screen TV with cable channels, fridge, a electric tea pot, a shower, free toiletries and a desk. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a hair dryer, while certain accommodations at the hotel also feature a balcony. The rooms at Riverside White House Villas have air conditioning and a closet.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at the property.

The accommodation has a sun terrace. The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental is available at Riverside White House Villas.

Staff at the reception can help guests with any questions that they might have.

Assembly Hall of the Cantonese Chinese Congregation is 0.7 miles from the hotel, while Japanese Covered Bridge is 0.7 miles away. The nearest airport is Da Nang International Airport, 15 miles from Riverside White House Villas.

Cam Chau is a great choice for travelers interested in beaches, relaxation and shopping.
