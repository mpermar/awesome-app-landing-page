Description

Modern rooms with free Wi-Fi await at Hoi An Garden Villas. Featuring a relaxing landscaped garden and an outdoor pool, the hotel is 2 km from Cua Dai Beach and Hoi An Ancient Town.

Da Nang International Airport is approximately a 45-minute drive away. All room types offer views of the pool and have a flat-screen satellite TV and private bathroom with shower.

Also included are a work desk, minibar and seating area with tea/coffee maker. At Hoi An Garden Villas, guests can approach the friendly staff for bicycle rental service, where guests can enjoy a 10-minute ride to the town centre.

Relax in the infinity pool or laze on a sun lounger. An à la carte breakfast is served at the in-house restaurant.
