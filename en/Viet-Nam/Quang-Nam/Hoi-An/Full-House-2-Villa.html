Description

The rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area. Featuring a private bathroom, certain units at Full House 2 Villa also offer a balcony. All rooms in the accommodation are equipped with a flat-screen TV and free toiletries.

Guests at Full House 2 Villa can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

The hotel has an outdoor pool.

Staff at reception can assist around the clock with advice on the area.

Assembly Hall of Chaozhou Chinese Congregation is 1.1 miles from Full House 2 Villa, while Thanh Ha Village is 1.7 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Da Nang International Airport, 14 miles from the property.

Tan An is a great choice for travelers interested in cycling, culture and ambiance.
