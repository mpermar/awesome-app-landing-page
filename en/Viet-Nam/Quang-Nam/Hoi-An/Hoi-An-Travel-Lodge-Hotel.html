Description

At the hotel, rooms contain a balcony with a city view. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with free toiletries, while certain units at Hoi An Travel Lodge Hotel also feature a seating area. All rooms at the accommodation feature air conditioning and a desk.

A buffet breakfast is served at the property. There's an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of Vietnamese dishes.

Hoi An Travel Lodge Hotel offers 3-star accommodations with an indoor pool.

Assembly Hall of Chaozhou Chinese Congregation is a 6-minute walk from the hotel, while Japanese Covered Bridge is 2,100 feet away. Da Nang International Airport is 14 miles from the property.

Son Phong is a great choice for travelers interested in walking, city walks and old-town exploration.

Experiences

03/01/2018

From an Hoi An vet - forget the newer hotels and stay here!

"I am a repeat visitor to Hoi An. Much of HA and Da Nang are undergoing an incredible explosion in tourism. There are so many newer hotels that have been constructed since I first visited 3 years back. Why should you stay here? 1) Location... newer hotels have gone up in areas further from points of interest. This hotel sits just on the border of the historic area. Easy to walk to while not being in the middle of the mobs and noise that goes with it. Close to many great eating and shopping options in all directions and 1 block from HA's tailor high street; 2) Facilities... very comfortable and spacious. Very clean. I stayed in 2 different rooms and was not disappointed. This is an older hotel in comparison to many of the newer boutique hotels that have gone up recently. However I can guarantee that however great they may be now will change in a year if not months. This place has held its quality over time. Small but good and consistent breakfast buffet. Pool on site. Great for kids. Bed in even their standard room was 3x6 meters; 3) Staff and serice... from bellhops to the buffet to housekeeping to administration, just incredibly friendly and attentive, the kind of attention that was sincere. Thank you all for a great stay."

Located at the edge of the Ancient Town

"10 min walk to Ancient Town. We opted to walk as we wanted to flexibility to move around freely without having to look after the complimentary bicycles. Within the Ancient Town, there's so much to see. Food was excellent. Ask for directions to Banh Mi Phuong for Baguette all types. Local & Foreigners alike queue for this. Tours can b arranged at Reception & I particularly enjoyed the Round Boat experience esp when the boat makes a 360 degrees spin. Fun for Adults & Children. I'd stay here again."

10/17/2016

Look forward to going back

"We stayed in a suite. The room was fantastic, very good decor, beautiful bathroom and the most comfortable bed I've ever slept in. The hotel is on a busy crossroads but there are double windows (not double glazing actually two sets of windows, European style) so even though we were right on the corner you couldn't hear anything on the room, we had really good sleep.The location is excellent, about 300m walk to the pedestrianised shopping area, lots of restaurants within easy reach so no need to worry about getting back at night. Very safe feeling. Also ATM on the next corner.The staff are lovely and nothing was too much trouble.This isn't a sunbathing/by the pool place even though it's easy to get to the beach if you want. It's a great spot for exploring Hoi An town and is perfect if you don't want to rely on bikes/taxis, you can walk everywhere, great with kids or when it's raining really hard.Breakfast is good, we stayed 6 days and there were very few repeats on the buffet selection. There's a decent selection of Vietnamese dishes and Western dishes. We'll be back in Da Nang and Hoi An in a few months so am happy that we found this place."
