Description

Located in the Cam Nam district in Hoi An, 600 metres from Hoi An Ancient Town, Hoi An Odyssey Hotel boasts a year-round outdoor pool and a sun terrace.

All in-house guests can enjoy daily complimentary shuttle bus to the property's private beach.

Guests can enjoy meals from the on-site restaurant or a drink at the bar. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

Japanese Covered Bridge and Assembly Hall of the Fujian Chinese Congregation are 700 metres from Hoi An Odyssey Hotel.

The nearest airport is Da Nang International Airport, 24 km from the hotel. All air-conditioned rooms are fitted with a flat-screen cable TV and a minibar.

Some units have a seating area to relax in after a busy day. Some units include views of the pool or river.

Rooms come with a private bathroom equipped with a shower. Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for currency exchange, ticketing and tour services.

There is also a gift shop on site. Guests are welcomed to rent bicycles and cars.
