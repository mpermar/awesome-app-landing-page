Description

This elegant resort is located within 2 miles to to Cua Dai Beach and a 45-minute drive to Da Nang International Airport. For convenience, airport shuttle services are available for a fee.

Tastefully furnished, each air-conditioned guest room is fitted with a flat-screen satellite TV, a sitting area and a personal safe. The attached bathrooms include a bathtub or shower facilities.

Guests can relax in the hot tub or enjoy a traditional massage. Phu Thinh Resort also includes a 24-hour front desk, car and bike rental and a business center.

Serving traditional Vietnamese and international cuisines, the Lily Restaurant also provides in-room dining services. Guests can also enjoy a drink at the pool bar.

Cam Chau is a great choice for travelers interested in beaches, relaxation and shopping.
