Description

At the hotel, every room includes a closet. Every room is equipped with a private bathroom with a bidet. Guest rooms have a TV with satellite channels.

A continental breakfast is available every morning at the property.

Green World Hoi An Villa has a playground. Guests can take part in various activities in the surrounding areas, including cycling and hiking.

Staff speak English and Russian at the reception.

Thanh Ha Village is 2.9 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Da Nang International Airport, 15 miles from Green World Hoi An Villa.

Cam Chau is a great choice for travelers interested in beaches, relaxation and shopping.
