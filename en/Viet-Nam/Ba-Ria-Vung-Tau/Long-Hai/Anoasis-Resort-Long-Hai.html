Description

Anoasis Resort Long Hai is a contemporary beach resort providing modern rooms, and villas in Long Hai.

Facilities include an outdoor pool and a beach-side restaurant. Gardens that surround rooms lead you from the resort to the beach.

Each room and villa features a seating area, flat-screen satellite TV, minibar, laptop-sized safe and free WiFi.

A private bathroom is fitted with a bathtub and shower. Guests can enjoy evening drinks at one or both of the two in-house bars.

You can also have 24 hour access to the front desk. Free private parking is available.

Guests can order a drink from the pool side bar and dine at the beach restaurant.

Anoasis Resort Long Hai is a 90-minute drive from Tan Son Nhat International Airport and Ho Chi Minh City.
