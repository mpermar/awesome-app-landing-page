Description

Situated 8 km away from the city centre, Carmelina Beach Resort welcomes guests with 2 outdoor swimming pools, a private beach area, business centre with meeting facilities and free Wi-Fi access.

Guests can also choose from 2 dining options. Enjoying sweeping sea views, each air-conditioned room at Carmelina Resort is fitted with a flat-screen cable TV, minibar and safety deposit box.

Free toiletries and a hairdryer are provided in the private bathrooms. The resort offers a myriad of services and activities that guests can engage in.

Relaxing spa treatments can be enjoyed and guests can work out at the on-site fitness centre or head outdoors for jet-skiing and tennis.

There is also a 3D cinema, karaoke lounge, paintball facilities and a water playground.

Set just a few steps from Ho Tram Beach, this well-appointed resort is located 8 km to Ba To Market.

Vung Tau Ferry Terminal is a 45-minute drive away and Vung Tau city centre is 55 km from the resort.

The resort is also 140 km from Ho Chi Minh City and 121 km from Tan Son Nhat International Airport.

Airport transportation can be arranged at a surcharge. Local Vietnamese and Western dishes can be enjoyed at Marina Restaurant.

For alternative dining options, Hill Site Bar will serve an array of beverages including hot snacks.
