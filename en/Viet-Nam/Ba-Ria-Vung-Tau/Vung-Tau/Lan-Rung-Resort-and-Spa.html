Description

All units at Lan Rung Resort & Spa have air conditioning, a desk and flat-screen TV with cable channels. An electric kettle and mini-bar are included. Private bathrooms come equipped with a shower, hairdryer and slippers.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property assisting a variety of services, including luggage storage, ticket service and tour arrangements. The resort features relaxing massage therapies, spa bath and sauna facilities. Guests can maintain daily workouts at the fitness center. The property offers Free WiFi access throughout the property and free parking is available at a site nearby.

Lan Rung Beach Restaurant serves delicious Western, Asian, Vietnamese and seafood dishes. Refreshing drinks can be enjoyed at the Pool Side Bar.

Lan Rung is 0.8 mi away from Back Beach, while Front Beach is 1.2 mi away. Christ of Vung Tau is 0.8 mi away from the property, while Hai Van pagoda is 0.6 mi away. The nearest airport is Tan Son Nhat International Airport, about 2-hour drive away.
