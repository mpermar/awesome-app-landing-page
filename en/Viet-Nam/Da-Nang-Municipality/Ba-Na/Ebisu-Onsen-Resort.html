Description

Ebisu Onsen Resort in Ba Na features accommodation with a garden and a terrace. With free WiFi, this 4-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk.

Guests can sample Grill/bbq and Asian dishes at the in-house restaurant. At the hotel, each room has a wardrobe.

Featuring a private bathroom with a bath and free toiletries, rooms at EBISU ONSEN RESORT also have a mountain view.

All guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge. The daily breakfast offers continental and buffet options.

At the accommodation guests are welcome to take advantage of a hot spring bath, a hot tub and a sauna.

Hoi An is 60 km from Ebisu Onsen Resort, while Da Nang is 30 km away. The nearest airport is Da Nang International Airport, 28 km from the property.
