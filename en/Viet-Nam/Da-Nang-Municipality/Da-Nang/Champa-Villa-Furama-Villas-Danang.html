Description

With direct access to a terrace, the villa consists of 3 bedrooms. The bathroom comes with a bath.

Breakfast is available each morning, and includes continental, buffet and Asian options.

Speaking English and Vietnamese at the 24-hour front desk, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical guidance on the area.

The property has a sauna, a fitness center and an indoor pool. Guests can relax in the garden at the property.

Love Lock Bridge Da Nang is 2.4 miles from the villa. Da Nang International Airport is 3.7 miles away.
