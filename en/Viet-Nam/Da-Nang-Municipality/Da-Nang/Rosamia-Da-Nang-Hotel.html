Description

Located in Da Nang, 3. 2 km from Love Lock Bridge Da Nang, Rosamia Da Nang Hotel provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a fitness centre and a bar.

With free WiFi, this 5-star hotel offers room service and a kids' club. The hotel offers an indoor pool, sauna, evening entertainment and a 24-hour front desk.

All units are equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a kettle, a shower, a hairdryer and a desk.

At the hotel every room has a wardrobe and a private bathroom. Guests at Rosamia Da Nang Hotel can enjoy a continental breakfast.

The accommodation offers a terrace. You can play darts at Rosamia Da Nang Hotel, and car hire is available.

There is a tour desk and bike rentals are available, while the business centre has newspapers and a fax machine and photocopier.

Cham Museum is 3. 8 km from the hotel, while Song Han Bridge is 4 km away. The nearest airport is Da Nang International, 6 km from Rosamia Da Nang Hotel, and the property offers a free airport shuttle service.
