Description

Welcoming guests with an outdoor infinity pool and a private beach area, Premier Village Danang Resort Managed by Accor is a luxurious accommodation nestled in the heart of Da Nang.

Guests enjoy free WiFi access and free airport shuttle services (subject to the availability).

Boasting a private pool and a spacious outdoor terrace, the villas are spacious and elegant.

It includes living and dining spaces with a kitchenette and oven. The marble bathrooms come with a hairdryer and plush bathrobes.

Premier Village Danang Resort also houses a business centre, an on-site mini-market and a library.

Other facilities include a 24-hour reception, dry-cleaning services and free bicycle rentals.

Guests can select from 3 different dining options. Lemongrass Restaurant will serve an array of international cuisines.

Featuring a rooftop extension, Nautical Beach Club offers a selection of wines and cocktails.

Just 6 km from Da Nang International Airport, the property is also 4. 4 km from the picturesque Marble Mountains and 21 km from the ancient town of Hoi An.
