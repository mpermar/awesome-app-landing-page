Description

Mercure Danang French Village Bana Hills features a 19th century European village and a cable car.

The hotel has free WiFi, a barbecue and spa centre, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

All rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Some rooms feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

You will find a kettle in the room. Each room comes with a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower.

Extras include bath robes and slippers. You will find a 24-hour front desk, business centre, gift shop and shops at the property.

The nearest airport is Da Nang International Airport, which is 22 km from the property.
