Description

A La Carte Da Nang Beach is located just 50 metres from Pham Van Dong Public Beach.

It features an outdoor pool, a fitness centre and a spa. Free WiFi access is provided.

Studios and apartment units feature living and dining areas, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, an electric kettle, and an en suite bathroom with toiletries and a hairdryer.

Some units open up to views of the sea. Both à la carte and buffet dining options are available at the on-site restaurant, while refreshing drinks are offered up at the bar.

Room service is provided. Guests can enjoy activities such as diving, snorkelling, cycling or fishing.

Other facilities at Alacarte include a games room, children’s playground, library and kids’ club.

A La Carte Da Nang Beach is 900 metres from My Khe Beach and 3 km from Han Market.

It is within 8 km of Marble Mountains and Non Nuoc Beach. Da Nang International Airport is 6 km away.
