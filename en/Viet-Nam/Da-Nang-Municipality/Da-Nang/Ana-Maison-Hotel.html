Description

Ana Maison Hotel is situated in Da Nang, 100 metres from My Khe Beach. Guests can enjoy the on-site bar.

Free WiFi is offered and free private parking is available on site. Rooms have a TV.

Certain rooms include a seating area where you can relax. Some units have views of the sea or city.

Each room comes with a private bathroom. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property.

Bike hire is available at this hotel and the area is popular for cycling. The hotel also offers car hire.

Cham Museum is 2. 5 km from Ana Maison Hotel , while Song Han Bridge is 2. 8 km away.

The nearest airport is Da Nang International Airport, 5 km from Ana Maison Hotel.
