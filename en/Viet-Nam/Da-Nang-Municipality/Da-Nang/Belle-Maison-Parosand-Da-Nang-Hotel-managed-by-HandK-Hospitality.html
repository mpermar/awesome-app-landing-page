Description

Belle Maison Parosand Da Nang Hotel -managed by H&K Hospitality offers accommodations in Da Nang.

It features an outdoor swimming pool and guests can enjoy meals at the in-house restaurant or have a drink at the bar.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. It is 1. 7 km to My Khe Beach. Love Lock Bridge Da Nang is 1.

8 km from Belle Maison Parosand Da Nang Hotel -managed by H&K Hospitality, while Song Han Bridge is 2 km away.

The nearest airport is Da Nang International Airport, 6 km from the property. Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and is equipped with a flat-screen TV.

All rooms come with a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find bathrobes and slippers.

Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for luggage storage. A fitness centre is available for guests to work out.
