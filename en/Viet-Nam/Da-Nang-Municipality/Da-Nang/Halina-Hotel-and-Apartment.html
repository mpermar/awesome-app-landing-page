Description

All rooms at the hotel come with a seating area and a flat-screen TV. Featuring a private bathroom, rooms at Halina Hotel and Apartment also offer free WiFi. Guest rooms have a desk.

A continental breakfast is available every morning at the accommodation. There's an on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of Asian dishes and also offers dairy-free options.

Halina Hotel and Apartment has a sun terrace.

Guests can use the business center to work or head for a sightseeing trip organized by the tour desk. The reception can provide advice on the area in order to help guests plan their day.

Asia Park Danang is 1.6 miles from the hotel. The nearest airport is Da Nang International Airport, 3.1 miles from the property.
