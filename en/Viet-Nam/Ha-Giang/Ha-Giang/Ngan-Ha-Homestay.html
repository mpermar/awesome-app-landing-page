Description

A balcony with mountain views is offered in each unit.

A Full English/Irish, American or Asian breakfast is available daily at the property. At the homestay you'll find a restaurant serving local and Korean cuisine.

Ngan Ha Homestay has a playground.

If you would like to discover the area, cycling is possible nearby and a bicycle rental service can be arranged.
