Description

The rooms has air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fridge, a electric tea pot, a bath, free toiletries and a desk. The rooms have a private bathroom with a shower, while some have lake views. All rooms have a closet.

An Asian breakfast is served every morning at the property.

Coi Nguon Museum is a 5-minute walk from the resort, while Su Muon Pagoda is 1.1 miles away. Phu Quoc International Airport is 2.5 miles from the property.

Duong Dong is a great choice for travelers interested in food, local food and beach walks.
