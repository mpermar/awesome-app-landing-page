Description

Camia Resort & Spa is located in Ong Lang Beach of Phu Quoc Island. With free WiFi, this 4-star resort offers a 24-hour front desk and a concierge service.

The accommodation offers room service, and organising tours for guests. At the resort, all rooms come with a desk.

Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a bath, while some rooms will provide you with a seating area.

At Camia Resort & Spa, each room comes with a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

A buffet breakfast is served each morning at the property. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the on-site restaurant, which serves Asian, local and Vietnamese cuisine.

The accommodation offers an outdoor pool. Vinpearl Land Phu Quoc is 15 km from Camia Resort & Spa.
