Description

Offering a sun terrace and a private beach area, Mango Beach Resort is located in Hàm Ninh in the Phu Quoc Island Region, 23 km from Duong Dong.

Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. Free WiFi is available throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Some units include a seating area where you can relax. The rooms are fitted with a private bathroom.

For your comfort, you will find slippers, free toiletries and a hairdryer. You will find valet parking at the property.

Kep is 36 km from Mango Beach Resort, while Kampot is 42 km from the property. Phu Quoc International Airport is 13 km away.
