Description

Featuring an outdoor pool facing the sea, Sea Sense Resort has suites and villas with a balcony offering direct sea views and free Wi-Fi access.

It has its own restaurant, a tour desk and ticket service. Located in Ong Lang, Sea Sense Resort is 7.

5 km to Duong Dong town. Duong Dong Lake is 11 km away while Phu Quoc Airport is 17.

2 km from the resort. All suites and villas come with air-conditioning, a flat-screen cable TV, electric kettle and minibar.

A seating area and private bathroom with hot-water shower and hairdryer are included.

Sea Sense Resort offers a 24-hour front desk, while daily housekeeping service is provided.

Other facilities include a tour desk. Airport transfers, laundry and ironing services can also be arranged at additional charges.
