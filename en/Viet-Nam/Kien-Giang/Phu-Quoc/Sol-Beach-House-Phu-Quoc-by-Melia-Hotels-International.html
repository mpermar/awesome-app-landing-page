Description

Boasting a private beach area in Duong To, Sol Beach House Phu Quoc by Melia Hotels International is nestled in a tropical garden and located on Truong Beach in the Phu Quoc Island Region.

Featuring a sun terrace, the property offers a year-round outdoor pool for guests to go for a dip or simply lounge under the sun on Bali beds.

Guests can enjoy serenity at the property by the pool or get a professional massage at the Body and Sol spa.

Free WiFi can be accessed in all areas and free public parking is available on site.

Each room is fitted with a flat-screen TV. Views of the sea, pool or garden are featured in certain rooms.

Each room includes a private bathroom with a walk-in shower and towels. For your comfort, you will find slippers and free toiletries.

Phu Quoc Pearl Farm is approximately 1. 5 km from the property. The nearest airport is Phu Quoc International Airport, 6.

4 km away. Complimentary airport shuttle is provided. Multilingual staff are available at the 24-hour front desk to assist with private check-in and check-out, luggage storage and other concierge services.

There is a business centre and gift shop on site. Overlooking the peaceful garden, The Kitchen restaurant serves an array of local and international dishes in buffet or a-la-carte dining style.
