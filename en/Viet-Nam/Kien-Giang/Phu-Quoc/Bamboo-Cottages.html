Description

Peace and quiet can be enjoyed at Bamboo Cottages, located on a private beach on Vung Bau Bay, Phu Quoc Island.

This beautiful resort features sea views from all its rooms, as well as free wired internet and computers in the common area.

Offering free kayaks and snorkeling equipment, guests can rent motorbikes to explore the island and visit Finger Nail Islet.

Enjoying cool sea breeze, all rooms and villas feature a private terrace with beach chairs.

The private open-air bathrooms are equipped with a shower and solar-heated water.

Bamboo Cottages is 19 km from Ganh Dau Bien Gioi, and 40 km from Phu Quoc Domestic and International Airport and Duong Dong Market.

From the domestic airport, it is a 50-minute flight to Ho Chi Minh or a 2-hour flight from Hanoi.

Airport transfers can be arranged at an extra charge. The on-site restaurant serves authentic home-made Vietnamese dishes prepared with fresh seafood and organic vegetables.

Western meals are also available upon request. Drinks can be enjoyed at Bamboo Bar.

Guests can arrange a tour to Turtle Island or rent a boat for those who like to go out to sea.

The resort also provides daily maid services, laundry services and luggage storage.
