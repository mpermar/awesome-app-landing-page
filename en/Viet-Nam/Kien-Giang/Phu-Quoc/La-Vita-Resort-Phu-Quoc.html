Description

Offering complimentary Wi-Fi access, La Vita Resort is just 300 metres from Dinh Cau night market.

It houses an outdoor pool and a restaurant which serves Vietnamese delights and the daily breakfast.

Located in central Duong Dong, the resort is 10 km from Phu Quoc International Airport and 10 km from the Ham Ninh fishing village.

Bai Sao beach is 25 km away. Rooms are air-conditioned, and feature cable TV, a wardrobe and a minibar.

The en suite bathroom comes with shower facilities, a hairdryer, slippers and toiletries.

Travel arrangements can be made at La Vita’s tour desk. The 24-hour reception staff can assist with laundry, transport rental and airport shuttle services.
