Description

At the resort, all rooms come with a terrace. Each room has a private bathroom with a shower and a hair dryer.

An Asian breakfast is available each morning at the property. There's an in-house restaurant, which serves Asian cuisine.

Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near Phu Quoc Valley Resort.

The reception can provide advice on the area in order to help guests plan their day.

Coi Nguon Museum is a 10-minute walk from the accommodation, while Su Muon Pagoda is 1.6 miles away. The nearest airport is Phu Quoc International Airport, 1.9 miles from Phu Quoc Valley Resort.

Duong Dong is a great choice for travelers interested in food, local food and beach walks.

Bester Ort um zu entspannen

"Zuerst sollte man wissen dass das Valley Resort kein Luxusresort wie zB das Mango Bay ist. Es liegt südlich von Duong Dong, dem Tourispot auf Phu Quoc. Am meisten empfiehlt es sich in einem der Deluxe Bungalow zu übernachten, da man dort ein ganzes Bungalow für sich allein hat.
