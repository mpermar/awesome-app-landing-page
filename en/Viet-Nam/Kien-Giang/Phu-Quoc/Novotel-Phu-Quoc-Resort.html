Description

Offering an outdoor pool and views of the sea, Novotel Phu Quoc Resort is situated in Dương Tô, 11 km from Duong Dong.

The hotel has a terrace and views of the lake, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Some rooms include a seating area to relax in after a busy day. A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms.

Novotel Phu Quoc Resort features free WiFi. A flat-screen TV with satellite channels is available.

You will find free shuttle service at the property with first come first served basis.

You can play tennis and table tennis at the hotel. The nearest airport is Phu Quoc International Airport, 7 km from the property.
