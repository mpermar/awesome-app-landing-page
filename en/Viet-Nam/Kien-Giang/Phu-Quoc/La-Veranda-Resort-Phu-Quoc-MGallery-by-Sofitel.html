Description

Set amidst a tropical garden on Phu Quoc Island, the 5-star La Veranda Resort Phu Quoc - MGallery Collection is built like a French seaside mansion.

Guests can enjoy free airport transfers and free WiFi access in all areas. A complimentary Sao Beach excursion is also on offer.

Spacious rooms feature French colonial architecture and décor comprising of dark woods, fine linen and mosaic tiles.

Each room has a private balcony or terrace overlooking the garden or beach. La Veranda Resort Phu Quoc - MGallery Collection provides free parking.

It is 4 km from Phu Quoc Airport, which is a 50-minute domestic flight from Ho Chi Minh City and a 2 hour, 10-minute domestic flight from Hanoi.

The property offers a day spa and operates 24-hour front desk. To relax, guests can go on fishing or snorkeling trips, or swim laps in the outdoor pool.

Bicycle rentals are also provided. Offering sweeping views of the sea, The Pepper Tree Restaurant serves international dishes, while oven baked pizzas can be enjoyed at Le Jardin restaurant.
