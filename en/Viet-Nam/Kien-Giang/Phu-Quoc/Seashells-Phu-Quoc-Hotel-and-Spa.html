Description

Situated in Phu Quoc Island, Seashells Phu Quoc Hotel & Spa offers contemporary rooms with free WiFi access in Duong Dong town.

Boasting an outdoor swimming pool, the hotel also has an on-site restaurant and bar.

Guests can enjoy free private parking on site. Featuring air conditioning, all spacious rooms have a sofa, wardrobe and personal safe.

A flat-screen TV, minibar and an electric kettle are provided. En suite bathroom is equipped with shower or bathtub, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Other facilities at Seashells Phu Quoc Hotel & Spa include a spa centre and 24-hour front desk.

Business or banquet facilities are available at a fee. Sung Hung Pagoda is 150 metres from Seashells Phu Quoc Hotel & Spa, while Cau Temple is 400 metres away.
