Description

Offering cosy accommodation in Cua Can, Fusion Resort Phu Quoc an outdoor swimming pool, on-site restaurant and a garden.

Guests are entitled to complimentary airport shuttle with fixed schedule, and free WiFi access in entire area.

Adorned in earth shades of cream and light brown, all air-conditioned rooms are finished with a private pool, terrace and glass-to-ceiling windows.

A seating area, personal safe and satellite flat-screen TV are included. You will find a minibar and an electric kettle for your convenience.

En suite bathroom comes with bath and shower facilities, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Fluently conversed in English and Vietnamese, friendly staff at the 24-hour front desk can assist you with express check-in or check-out, luggage storage and chargeable private transfer upon request.

Other facilities at Fusion Resort Phu Quoc include a spa centre offering sauna room and massage service, fitness centre and children's playground.

Vinpearl Safari Phu Quoc is a 10-minute drive from the resort, while Phu Quoc International Airport can be reached within a 55-minute drive.
