Description

Positioned along one of the longest beach in Phu Quoc, Famiana Resort & Spa sits on a 4 hectare land offering cosy accommodation on Truong Beach in Duong Dong town.

The resort boasts its own beautiful pristine beach with shady palm trees and white sand and stunning sunset views.

It also features an outdoor swimming pool, on-site restaurants, as well as a well-stocked bar and cafe.

Complimentary WiFi access is provided. Coi Nguon Museum is 1 km from Famiana Resort & Spa, while Su Muon Pagoda is 3.

2 km away. The resort is a 10-minute drive from Phu Quoc International Airport, which is a 50-minute domestic flight from Ho Chi Minh City.

Split into the beach zone and green villa zone, elegant rooms are surrounded by tropical gardens and fitted with a balcony.

The spacious rooms are equipped with a 42-inch flat-screen TV with cable channels, minibar and personal safe.

The en suite bathroom comes with either a shower or bathtub, hairdryer and free toiletries.

Garden Restaurant offers Asian and European dishes, while Famiana Restaurant has a vast array of Phu Quoc specialities, Vietnamese delights and international favourites.

Room service is also provided. Guests can enjoy beverages at the pool bar or by the beach.

Guests can head to the day spa for a rejuvenating treatment or have a good workout session at the gym.

Famiana Resort & Spa also has state-of-the-art sport facilities, including kayak, archery, tennis and mini-golf.

Professional staff at the 24-hour reception are willing to assist you with luggage storage, laundry and airport shuttle services.
