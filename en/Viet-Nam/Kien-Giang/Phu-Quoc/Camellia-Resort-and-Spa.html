Description

Nestled within private gardens, Camellia Resort & Spa offers accommodations in Ong Lang Beach area in Phu Quoc Island.

It boasts an outdoor pool and spa centre. Guests can enjoy meals at the in-house restaurant or have a drink at the bar.

Free WiFi is available in public areas. Each room at this resort is air conditioned and is equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Some rooms feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day. A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms.

Rooms come with a private bathroom equipped with a bath. Extras include bathrobes and a hairdryer.

Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for currency exchange, tour arrangements, concierge and ticketing services.

Bike hire and car hire are available at this resort and the area is popular for snorkelling.

Fish Sauce Factory is 5. 4 km from the resort, while Sung Hung Pagoda is 6. 3 km away.

The nearest airport is Phu Quoc International Airport, 20 km from Camellia Resort & Spa.
