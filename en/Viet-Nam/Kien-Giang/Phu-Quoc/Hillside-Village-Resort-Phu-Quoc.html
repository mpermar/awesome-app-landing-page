Description

Located in Duong Dong, Hillside Village Resort Phu Quoc is just a 5-minute drive from the famed Coco Bar on Vietnam's largest island, Phu Quoc.

It offers an outdoor pool and spa centre. With views of the sea, guests can enjoy a relaxing day on the terrace, as well as have a gastronomic meal at the on-site restaurant.

Featuring woody interiors with warm and neutral hues, each room comes equipped with a flat-screen TV and a clothes rack or wardrobe.

En suite bathroom is fitted with a shower facility. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

Staff at the resort can assist guests with services such as luggage storage, concierge services and other enquiries.

The property offers free shuttle service. Phu Quoc Night Market is within a 30-minute walk from Hillside Village Resort Phu Quoc.

The nearest airport is Phu Quoc International Airport, approximately 10 km from Hillside Village Resort Phu Quoc.
