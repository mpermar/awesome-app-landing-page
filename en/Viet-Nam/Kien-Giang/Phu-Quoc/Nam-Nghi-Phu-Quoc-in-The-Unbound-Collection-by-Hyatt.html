Description

Nestled on the shores of the exclusive Mong Tay peninsula, Nam Nghi Phu Quoc Island features lush forests, private white-sand beach area with turquoise water.

It boasts a year-round outdoor swimming pool, an on-site restaurant. Guests can enjoy free WiFi access in public areas.

Featuring balcony and/or terrace, each air-conditioned room at this resort is fitted with a seating area with sofa, cable flat-screen TV and wardrobe.

Minibar and tea/coffee maker are provided for your convenience. Certain units have a dining area with dining table.

En suite bathroom in each room comes with bath/shower facilities and a hairdryer.

Extras include bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries for your comfort. Guests can sweat it out in the sauna room, or relax in a hot tub in the spa centre.

The resort operates 24-hour front desk where staff, conversed in English and Vietnamese, can assist you with currency exchange and other concierge services.

Day trips, shuttle service, airport transfer can be arranged upon request. You can engage in various activities, such as golfing, snorkelling and diving.

The resort also offers bike hire. Duong Dong town and Tranh Waterfall are within 45-minute driving distance from Nam Nghi Phu Quoc Island.
