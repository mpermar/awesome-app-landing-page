Description

Boasting a private beach area in Ganh Dau, VinOasis Phu Quoc offers luxurious accommodation in Phu Quoc Island.

Guests can enjoy meals at the on-site restaurant, followed by a drink at the bar.

Complete with a private balcony that opens up to panoramic sea views, all units have air conditioning and a seating area.

En suite bathroom comes equipped with a bathtub, hairdryer and free toiletries. Free WiFi access is available in public areas.
