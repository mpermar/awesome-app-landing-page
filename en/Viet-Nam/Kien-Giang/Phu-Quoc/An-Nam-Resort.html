Description

Guest rooms in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV. With a private bathroom, certain units at An Nam Resort also provide guests with a garden view. All guest rooms have a desk.

Breakfast is available daily, and includes à la carte and Asian options.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool. The area is popular for fishing, and car rental is available at An Nam Resort.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can give tips on the area.

Fish Sauce Factory is 6 miles from the resort, while Coco Dive Center is 6 miles away. The nearest airport is Phu Quoc International Airport, 10 miles from the property.
