Description

Nestled in the heart of Hanoi's Old Quarter, Luminous Viet Hotel offers accommodation within 100 metres from the renowned Hoan Kiem Lake and a 5-minute walk from Thang Long Water Puppet Theatre.

It is just 250 metres to St. Joseph's Cathedral and about 20 km from Noi Bai International Airport.

The hotel is 2 km from Ha Noi Train Station and the prominent Ho Chi Minh Memorial.

Dong Xuan Market, which offers a variety of shopping options, is located a 10-minute walk away.

Furnished with parquet flooring, air-conditioned rooms are equipped with a flat-screen cable TV, a safety deposit box, minibar and tea/coffee making facilities.

En suite bathroom is equipped with a hairdryer and provides a bathrobe and free toiletries.

Free WiFi access is available. The hotel houses a restaurant which serves international and local delicacies.

Breakfast can be served in-room upon request. The 24-hour front desk staff can assist with laundry/ironing services, currency exchange and luggage storage.

Shuttle services and local tours can be arranged as well. Family lounges are available for guests to relax in.
