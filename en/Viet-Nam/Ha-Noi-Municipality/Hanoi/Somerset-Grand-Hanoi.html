Description

Standing tall in Hoan Kiem district of Hanoi capital, Somerset Grand Hanoi offers self-catering residences with access to its outdoor pool and fitness room.

It also houses a restaurant and bar. Guests can enjoy free WiFi access in all areas.

A 15-minute stroll from Sommerset Grand Hanoi can take you to many popular tourist attractions, including the iconic Hoan Kiem Lake, Hanoi's Old Quarter Area, St.

Joseph Cathedral and Hanoi Opera House. Noi Bai International Airport is a 45-minute drive away.

All spacious, well-appointed units are adorned in light beige and feature floor-to-ceiling windows offering sweeping views of the city.

A living room with sofa, fully-equipped kitchen with dining area and laundry facilities are featured.

All units also have a flat-screen TV, DVD player and home entertainment system. En suite bathroom has a shower, hairdryer and free toiletries.

Guests can enjoy a sauna session, relax with a dip in the hot tub or have a good tennis game.

The property also features a business centre, as well as provides child care and laundry services.

Hearty Vietnamese delights and international dishes can be enjoyed at Jaspa Restaurant, or in the comfort of rooms.
