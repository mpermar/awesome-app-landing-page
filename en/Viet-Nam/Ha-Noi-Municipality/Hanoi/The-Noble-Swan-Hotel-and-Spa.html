Description

The Noble Swan Hotel & Spa provides accommodation with air conditioning in Hanoi's Hoan Kiem District, attractively situated 200 metres from St.

Joseph Cathedral. The hotel has a restaurant, and a spa centre. Guests can enjoy free WiFi access in entire area and private parking.

All guestrooms are fitted with dark furnishings, a cable flat-screen TV and desk.

A minibar, and an electric kettle are included. En suite bathroom comes with a walk-in shower and free toiletries.

Bathrobes are available for your comfort. Guests can indulge in soothing massages or detox sessions at Swan Spa & Treatments.

The hotel operates a front desk and tour desk where staff can provide tips of the area, or assist you with your inquiries round-the-clock.

Hanoi Jade Restaurant & Patisserie features a range of Hanoi's local specialities and Vietnamese favourites to satisfy your taste buds.

Daily buffet is also served here. Hoan Kiem Lake is 500 metres from The Noble Swan Hotel & Spa, while Thang Long Water Puppet Theater is a 7-minute walk away.
