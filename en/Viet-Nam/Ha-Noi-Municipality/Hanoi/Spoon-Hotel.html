Description

A 7-minute walk from Hoan Kiem Lake, Spoon Hotel features a restaurant, a 24-hour front desk, and free WiFi.

It offers tour arrangements, ticket bookings, and visa services. Spoon Hotel is a 10-minute drive from One Pillar Pagoda, the Temple of Literature, and the Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum.

Fitted with dark wood flooring and furniture, the air-conditioned rooms feature a flat-screen TV, a safety deposit box, and a fridge.

Each room has a private bathroom with toiletries, slippers, and a hairdryer. The hotel provides car rentals, currency exchange, and laundry or dry cleaning services.

Luggage storage facilities are available. Breakfast is served at the lobby restaurant.
