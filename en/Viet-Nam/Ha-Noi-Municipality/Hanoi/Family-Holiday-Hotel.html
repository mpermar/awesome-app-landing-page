Description

Surrounded by dining and entertainment options, Family Holiday Hotel is conveniently situated in Hanoi’s Old Quarter Area.

It offers complimentary WiFi access and free public parking at a nearby location.

Featuring local furnishings, rooms are air-conditioned and feature a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a minibar and bottled water.

The en suite bathroom comes with a hairdryer and toiletries. Room service is provided.

Guests can rent a bicycle to explore the surrounding area, or make other travel arrangements at the tour desk.

Airport shuttle services are also available. Staff at the 24-hour front desk can assist with laundry, ironing and luggage storage services.

Other services include fax/photocopying, ticketing and currency exchange. Family Holiday Hotel is 250 metres from Hoan Kiem Lake and 350 metres from St.

Joseph Cathedral. The nearest airport is Noi Bai International Airport, 27 km from the hotel.
