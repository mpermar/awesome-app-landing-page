Description

Strategically located, Hanoi Guest House Royal offers elegant and stylish accommodation with free WiFi access in the guestrooms.

It operates a 24-hour front desk and features a restaurant. The property is just 400 metres from Old City Gate and 500 metres from Ngoc Son Temple.

The famous Dong Xuan Market is 600 metres away, while Noi Bai International Airport is accessible with a 21 km drive.

Featuring Victorian-inspired decor, air-conditioned rooms come with a smart TV. In-room safe, minibar and electric kettle are also included.

The en suite bathroom has hairdryer and a heated shower facility. At Hanoi Guest House Royal, the friendly staff can assist with bicycle/car rentals, luggage storage, laundry services and tour arrangements.
