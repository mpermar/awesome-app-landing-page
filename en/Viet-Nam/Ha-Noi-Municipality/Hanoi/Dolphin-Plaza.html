Description

All units come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV and a fully equipped kitchen with a dining area. Some units include a balcony and/or a patio with pool or garden views.

Guests at the condo hotel can enjoy a à la carte breakfast. Various dining options including international, European and Asian dishes are available at the in-house restaurant.

Dolphin Plaza has a playground.

Guests can also relax in the shared lounge area.

Vietnam Museum of Ethnology is 1.6 miles from the accommodation, while Vincom Plaza Bac Tu Liem is 1.6 miles away. The nearest airport is Noi Bai International Airport, 13 miles from Dolphin Plaza.

Cau Giay is a great choice for travelers interested in city trips, old-town exploration and traditional food.
