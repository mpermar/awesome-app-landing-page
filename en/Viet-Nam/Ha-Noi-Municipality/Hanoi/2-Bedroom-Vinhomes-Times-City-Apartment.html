Description

All units comprise a seating area with a sofa and a fully equipped kitchen with various cooking facilities, including an oven, a microwave and a toaster. Units include a terrace and feature a satellite flat-screen TV, as well as air conditioning.

A continental or Asian breakfast is served at the property. At the apartment you'll find a restaurant serving Japanese and Thai cuisine.

Vinhomes Times City Apartment has a playground.

Guests can also relax in the shared lounge area.
