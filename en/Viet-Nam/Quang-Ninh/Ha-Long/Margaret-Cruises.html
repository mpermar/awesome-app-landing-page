Description

Located at the main port of Ha Long Bay, Tuan Chau Island, Margaret Cruises offers a classic Vietnamese-style cruise accommodation departing and ending in the famous Ha Long Bay, just 7 km from Ha Long Night Market.

Margaret Cruises serves an array of local cuisines at the on-board restaurant daily, including buffet breakfast and lunch, as well as a Vietnamese set menu dinner.

Each room is equipped with a seating area, besides large, comfortable bedding. Each room comes with its own private bathroom with bath robes and slippers in every unit.

Towels are available. The 18-to-25 square-metre cabins come with connecting capabilities upon request.

Some rooms offer spa tubs. Margaret Cruises also includes various itinerary that allows guests to explore the famous islets and caves nearby.

Guests can enjoy activities including fishing and hiking. The nearest airport is Cat Bi International Airport, 30 km from the harbour.
