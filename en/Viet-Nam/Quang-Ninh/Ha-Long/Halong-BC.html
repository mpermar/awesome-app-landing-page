Description

Located 2 km from Bai Chay - Halong Bus Station, Halong BC offers accommodation in Ha Long.

Offering views of the sea, the property is 700 metres from Ha Long Night Market. Rooms have a flat-screen TV.

Enjoy a cup of tea while looking out at the sea or mountain. All rooms are fitted with a private bathroom with a shower and bidet, with slippers, free toiletries and a hairdryer provided.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. The hotel also offers bicycle, motorbike and car rentals.

Hon Gai - Ha Long is 6 km from Halong BC, while Cat Ba is 25 km away. Cat Bi International Airport is 34 km from the property.
