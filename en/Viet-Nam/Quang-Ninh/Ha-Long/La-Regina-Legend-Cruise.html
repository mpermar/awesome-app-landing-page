Description

La Regina Legend Cruise features a restaurant, fitness centre, a bar and shared lounge in Ha Long.

Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a terrace. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and free WiFi.

At the cruise, each room comes with a wardrobe. At La Regina Legend Cruise every room has air conditioning and a private bathroom.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental breakfast. La Regina Legend Cruise offers a hot tub.

Guests at the cruise will be able to enjoy activities in and around Ha Long, like fishing.

Tuan Chau Harbour is 3. 9 km from La Regina Legend Cruise, while Vincom Plaza Ha Long is 22 km away.

The nearest airport is Cat Bi International Airport, 71 km from the accommodation.
