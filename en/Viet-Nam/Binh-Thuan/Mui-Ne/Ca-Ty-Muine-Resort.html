Description

Nestled within private gardens, Ca Ty Muine Resort offers accommodation in Mui Ne.

The resort has an outdoor swimming pool and guests can enjoy a meal at the in-house restaurant or have a drink at the poolside bar.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. Windchimes Kitesurf School is 1. 9 km from Ca Ty Muine Resort, while Surfpoint Kiteboarding Center is 5 km from the property.

Air-conditioned rooms include a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Enjoy a cup of tea while looking out at the sea or pool.

Rooms include a private bathroom. Extras include bathrobes and slippers. Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for laundry services.

The property offers bicycle rentals for guests who wish to explore the surrounding area.

Alternatively, guests can relax at the shared terrace overlooking panoramic views of the swimming pool.
