Description

The rooms are spacious and air-conditioned. They feature private balconies offering beautiful views and an ocean breeze. They include a cable TV, a bathroom with a bathtub, and a minibar.

Guests can relax and enjoy the sun by the pool or on their own private balconies. Sun lounge chairs are available. Resort Romana features a spa which offers massages and body treatments, as well as a beauty salon.

The resort has 2 public pools and a well-equipped gym. Guests can use the tennis and volleyball courts. For a quiet afternoon indoors, Romana Resort provides free Wi-Fi in public areas of the property.

Outdoor dining is available at Panorama Restaurant, allowing guests to enjoy great sunset views with their meals. It serves fresh seafood and local specialties.
