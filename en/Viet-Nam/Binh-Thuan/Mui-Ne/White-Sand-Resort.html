Description

White Sand Resort features spacious rooms and bungalows with free WiFi, an outdoor pool and a restaurant.

Located in Mui Ne, the resort has a private beach area and garden for guests to relax in.

A 5-minute drive to the centre of Mui Ne, White Sand Resort is 2 km away from Sea Links golf course and 8 km from Phan Thiet city.

Each air-conditioned room and bungalow has a flat-screen cable TV, safe, minibar and seating area.

Private bathroom includes a shower, hairdryer and free toiletries. Facilities at the resort include a 24-hour front desk, ticket service and tour desk.
