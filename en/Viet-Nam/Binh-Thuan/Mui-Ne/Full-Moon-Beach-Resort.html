Description

This resort lies along the Mui Ne Beach and is less than 14 km from the centre of Phan Thiết.

It features an outdoor saltwater pool surrounded by lush tropical gardens, and has free Wi-Fi throughout.

The spacious, air-conditioned rooms at the Full Moon Beach Resort all include a seating area, satellite TV and tea/coffee making faciliteis.

Each includes a fresh fruit platter daily. Some rooms have a balcony or views of the South China Sea.

Full Moon restaurant serves Vietnamese and Western cuisine and Jibe’s restaurant is less than 50 metres away and is also part of the resort.

Guests can relax with a drink at the bar or make use of the resort’s barbecue facilities.

A range of activities are available at the hotel, including kite surfing, windsurfing, kayaking, and stand-up paddle boarding.

Massage and laundry facilities are also offered. Full Moon Beach Resort is just over 3 km from Sea Links City golf course.
