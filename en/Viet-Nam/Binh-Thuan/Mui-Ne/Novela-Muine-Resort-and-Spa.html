Description

Surrounded by coco-palm gardens, Novela Resort & Spa features rooms with wide balconies and beautiful views.

The resort offers a variety of services and facilities including Jeep rentals, free Wi-Fi and free parking.

All rooms at Novela include a satellite TV, mini bar and tea/coffee making facilities.

Suites also offer private balconies with views of Sand Hill or the sea. Connecting rooms can be arranged upon request.

Novela’s 2-floor restaurant serves Asian and European cuisines for breakfast, lunch and dinner.
