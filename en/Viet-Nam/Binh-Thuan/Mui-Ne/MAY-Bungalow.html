Description

All rooms at the hotel come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a shower. MAY Bungalow has some rooms with pool views, and each room has a balcony. The rooms will provide guests with a desk and a electric tea pot.

The accommodation has a terrace.

Windchimes Kitesurf School is 2.9 miles from MAY Bungalow, while C2 Sky Kitesurfing Vietnam is one mile from the property. The nearest airport is Lien Khuong Airport, 56 miles from the hotel.
