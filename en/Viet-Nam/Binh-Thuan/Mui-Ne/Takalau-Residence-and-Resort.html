Description

Takalau Residence & Resort is set just 200 metres from Sea Link Golf. It houses various spa treatments and sauna facilities.

Guests can also enjoy free Wi-Fi access available throughout the property. Situated 8 km away from Phan Thiet City, this modern property is also 17 km away from the popular Sand Dunes and 32 km from Ta Cu Mountain where guests can enjoy picturesque views.

Air-conditioned rooms are fitted with a seating area and a cable flat-screen TV. The en suite bathrooms come with a bathtub or a shower and free toiletries.

Larger villas feature a private pool and a kitchenette. Guests at Takalau Residence can go for a swim at the private beach area or utilize the barbeque facilities.

Other facilities also include a 24-hour reception and airport shuttle services. Ocean Song Restaurant will serve a daily spread of Western and Asian dishes with room service options available.
