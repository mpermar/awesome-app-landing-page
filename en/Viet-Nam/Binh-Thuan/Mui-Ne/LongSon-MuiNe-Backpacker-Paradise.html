Description

Situated between the Red and White Sand Dunes, LongSon MuiNe Backpacker Paradise, sits on a private beach area.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant, or have a drink at the 24-hour bar. Free WiFi and parking are available.

All dormitory and tents feature views of the garden or sea and individual lockers.

Guests have access to the shared bathroom and toilet. The property operates a 24-hour front desk where staff are available to assist with ticket service, tour arrangement, currency exchange and laundry service.

You will find a shop and surfboard at the property. Guests can enjoy happy hour from 19:00 to 21:00 daily, while special menus are available.

You can play table football and billiards at the resort. Mui Ne is 10 km from the property, while Phan Thiet City is 30 km away.

The nearest airport is Lien Khuong Airport, 87 km from the property. The on-site restaurant serves an array of Vietnamese and international dishes.
