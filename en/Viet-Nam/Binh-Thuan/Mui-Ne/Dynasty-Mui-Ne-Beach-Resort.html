Description

The air-conditioned guest rooms have Asian décor and come with a minibar, a hairdryer and a personal safe. The private bathrooms have a shower.

Dynasty Resort features a hot tub, a spa and massage services. Guests can sing karaoke or rent bicycles. Free Wi-Fi is available at the business center.

The resort’s on-site restaurant serves local and international specialties.
