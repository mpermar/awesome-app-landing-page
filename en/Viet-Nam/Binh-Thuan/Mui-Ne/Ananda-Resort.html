Description

The resort is 4 miles from Mui Ne Fishing Village Market and a 45-minute drive from White Sand Dunes of Mui Ne.

The bungalows are air-conditioned, and feature a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a minibar and a private bathroom with a shower and toiletries.

The restaurant serves local Vietnamese delights alongside international specialties. Refreshing drinks can be enjoyed by the pool in the evening.

Guests can request for a massage by the pool, or head to the 24-hour front desk for assistance with luggage storage, laundry and ticket services. Room service is provided.
