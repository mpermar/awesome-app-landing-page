Description

Surrounded by gardens, the beachfront Grace Boutique Resort is located in Mui Ne, 300 metres from Han Tien Mud Spa Centre and 400 metres from Surfpoint Kiteboarding Centre.

Housing a restaurant and an outdoor pool, it also offers complimentary WiFi access and parking.

Fitted with a large balcony to offer sea, pool or garden views, each room features air conditioning, satellite TV, a personal safe and a minibar.

The en suite bathroom offers shower facilities, toiletries, slippers and a hairdryer.

Guests can use the business centre, or head to the travel desk to make travel arrangements.

Staff at the front desk can assist with luggage storage, laundry, airport shuttle and ticketing services.

The resort is 1. 6 km from Mui Ne Bo Ke and 2. 4 km from Fairy Spring. The Red and White Sand Dunes are 6.
