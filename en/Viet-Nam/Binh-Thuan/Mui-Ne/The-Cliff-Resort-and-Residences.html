Description

Rooms include a flat-screen cable TV, safe and a mini-bar. Private bathrooms come with shower facilities and free toiletries.

The Cliff Resort & Residences is 3.1 mi from Po Sha Nu (Cham Tower) and 9.9 mi from Suoi Hong Spring. It takes 25-minutes to drive to the White Sand Dunes. Tan Son Nhat Airport in Ho Chi Minh City is approximately 118 mi away.

Pamper at Zest Spa, which has sauna rooms. Relax on a deck chair by the infinity pool or splash around in the kids pool. Other recreational facilities include a mini cinema, Karaoke and a souvenir shop.

On the Cliff Restaurant specializes in local seafood and also offers Western cuisine. 24-hour room service is available here.
