Description

Featuring large windows and hardwood floorings, spacious rooms at this resort offer views of the sea, pool or garden. Each airy room comes air-conditioned and is fitted with a personal safe, satellite TV and mini-bar. Attached marble bathroom is equipped with a bathtub, shower facility and a hairdryer. Free toiletries and slippers are available.

Cat Ba Sunrise Resort has a full-service spa, gym room and karaoke room. Guests can relax on the beachfront sun loungers, take a stroll along the cliff-side walkway, or play billiards.

The property is within walking distance from Cat Ba Town. Professional staff at the 24-hour reception can assist you with travel tour arrangements to Cat Ba National Park and Ha Long Bay, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Featuring an open kitchen, VOSO Restaurant serves an array of freshly-caught seafood, Vietnamese and international dishes which are complimented by a selected international wine list. The beachfront Coco Bar Lounge has many choices of beverages and cocktails.

Experiences

03/15/2018

A beautiful and relaxing way to enjoy the Ha Long Bay area.

"This resort is gorgeous. We went with our four year old. Playground, pool and beach were fantastic. They handled all our transportation which all went seamlessly. We also booked 2 tours with them: a full day private boat tour of Lan Ha and Ha Long Bay plus a half day Cat Ba National Park and Hospital Cave tour. Both were fantastic. In general, I was very impressed with how well everything went. We arrived early Wednesday afternoon and chilled out at resort. We were supposed to do our private boat ride Thursday but weather was overcast and chilly—they let me switch our tour days so we did a half day hike on Thursday instead of Friday. On Friday, the weather was great so we did a full day private boat cruise in Lan Ha Bay and Ha Long Bay. The natural scenery in Lan Ha Ba seemed identical to Ha Long Bay but with less garbage. Our tour included kayaking and swimming. We also had a delicious lunch on board. A half day tour without the kayaking is also available. I am sure they can tailor make tours too. Overall, this was a beautiful and relaxing way to enjoy the area. I am so happy we did this instead of an overnight cruise."
