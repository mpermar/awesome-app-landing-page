Description

Set in Cat Ba, within 1. 3 km of Dau Be Island and 20 km of Soi Sim Island, Golden Land Hotel offers accommodation with a shared lounge and free WiFi throughout the property as well as free private parking for guests who drive.

Boasting room service, this property also provides guests with a children's playground.

The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, a shared kitchen and currency exchange for guests.

At the hotel, all rooms have a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. All guest rooms at Golden Land Hotel feature air conditioning and a wardrobe.

An American breakfast is available every morning at the accommodation. Golden Land Hotel offers a terrace.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike hire and car hire are available at the hotel.

Popular points of interest near Golden Land Hotel include Cat Ba Ferry Station, Cannon Fort and Ben Beo Harbour.

The nearest airport is Cat Bi International, 54 km from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
