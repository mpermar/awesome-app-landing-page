Description

Situated along the private Bai Dai Beach, award-winning AVANI Quy Nhon Resort & Spa features rooms with Medieval Vietnamese décor and balconies with sea views.

An outdoor cliff-top restaurant is offered. AVANI Quy Nhon Resort's rooms feature open spaces and calming colours.

Rooms are generously equipped with flat-screen TVs, minibar and tea/coffee makers.

Marble/tiled floors and a sea-facing seating area provide guests with a tranquil experience.

Guests can enjoy kayaking and watersports on the private beach, or take a refreshing swim in the pool.

The spa offers wellness and massage services. AVANI Quy Nhon's tour desk can make sightseeing arrangements including boat trips to Hon Dat Island.

TRE Restaurant, situated atop a cliff, serves the best of Eastern and Western cuisine and seafood, while GIO Bar and Lounge offers coffee, snacks and tropical cocktails.

AVANI Quy Nhon Resort & Spa is located in the charming seaside town of Binh Dinh, 10 km from Quy Nhon City Centre.
