Description

At the resort, every room comes with a desk. Casa Marina Resort has some rooms with sea views, and every room has a balcony. At the accommodation all rooms include a closet and a flat-screen TV.

Casa Marina Resort offers a continental or buffet breakfast. There's an on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of Asian dishes.

The resort has an outdoor pool.

Languages spoken at the reception include English and Russian.

Phu Cat Airport is 22 miles away.
