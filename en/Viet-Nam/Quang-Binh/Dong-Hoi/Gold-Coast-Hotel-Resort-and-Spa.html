Description

The rooms in the resort are equipped with a electric tea pot. Gold Coast Hotel Resort & Spa has some accommodations with sea views, and every room includes a private bathroom. At the accommodation, every room has a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Breakfast is available daily, and includes buffet and American options.

Gold Coast Hotel Resort & Spa has a year-round outdoor pool. You can play pool at the resort, and bike rental and car rental are available.

Dong Hoi Airport is 5 miles away.
