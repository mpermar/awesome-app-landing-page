Description

Nguyen Shack - Phong Nha Eco Resort offers private bungalows in Phong Nha National Park.

The property boasts a garden and lake view. Guests at the homestay can enjoy an Asian breakfas at the on-site restaurant, which serves Asian cuisine.

The property is located next to the Phong Nha Bus Stop. The nearest airport is Dong Hoi Airport, 40 km from the property.
