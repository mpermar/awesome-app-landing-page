Description

Nestled with private gardens, Swiss-Belresort Tuyen Lam offers stylish accommodation in Da Lat.

It boasts recreational areas such as an indoor and outdoor pool with sun decks and lounge chairs, and a bar where guests can choose to kick back with their favourite drinks.

Guests may also enjoy meals at the in-house restaurant. Sacom Golf Club is located 240 metres away.

, while Dalat Centre is 17 km away. The property offers airport transfers and shuttle services at an additional charge.

It takes approximately 25 minutes to the city by car. Each sleekly-decorated room comes air-conditioned, with heater options.

All rooms are fitted with a flat-screen satellite TV and a minibar. En suite bathrooms come with a shower or bath.

Guests may approach the 24-hour front desk for currency exchange, tour and ticketing services.

There is a gift shop on site. Car hire is also available. The property is equipped with facilities such as a tennis court, fitness centre, spa and wellness centre, as well as a children's playground.
