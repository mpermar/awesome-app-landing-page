Description

Boasting French country-style architecture, SAM Tuyen Lam Resort overlooks Tuyen Lam Lake and offers a peaceful retreat away from the bustling Da Lat City.

Guests can enjoy free use of bicycles, and free WiFi access in public areas. The resort features an in-house restaurant, as well as a garden.

All rooms offers a flat-screen TV, personal safe and are furnished with parquet flooring.

Attached bathroom comes with shower facility and free toiletries. Most rooms feature view of the garden or lake.

The resort operates a 24-hour front desk where staff can assist you with a range of services, including airport transfer, car rental and laundry.

The Oriental-inspired restaurant serves an array of Vietnamese and European cuisine.

Outdoor BBQ party can be arranged. Room service is available. Truc Lam Temple is 1.
