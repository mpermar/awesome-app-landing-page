Description

Featuring an outdoor pool and spectacular sea views, Alana Nha Trang Beach Hotel is set in Nha Trang in the Khanh Hoa Region.

The non-smoking property is just a 2-minute walk to the beach. The hotel has a spa and hot tub, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and features a flat-screen TV. You will find a kettle in the room.

All rooms are fitted with a private bathroom with a bath or shower and bidet, with bath robes, slippers and free toiletries provided.

A TV with cable channels is offered. The friendly staff can assist with currency exchange, laundry/dry cleaning and room service.

Sauna and steam room are also available. This hotel has a private beach area and bike hire and car hire are available.

Sailing Club is 100 metres from Alana Nha Trang Beach Hotel, while Nha Trang Beach is 300 metres away.
