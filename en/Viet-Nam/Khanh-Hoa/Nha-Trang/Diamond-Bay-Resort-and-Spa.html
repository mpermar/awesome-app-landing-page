Description

The luxurious Diamond Bay Resort & Spa is located in the South of Nha Trang City on a private bay.

Set amidst tropical gardens, it features a swimming pool, private beach and free WiFi access.

The property offers free daily shuttle bus to Nha Trang city centre which is 10 km away.

Cam Ranh International Airport is 20 km away. Airport transfer services and free parking can also be arranged by the property.

The well-appointed rooms come with a balcony that offers views of the garden or the sea.

Each room is fitted with a minibar and TV with cable channels. Guests can indulge in a massage at Song Lo Spa, or work out at the swimming pool.

Sightseeing activities can be arranged at the on-site tour desk. Diamond Bay features 2 restaurants ranging from Vietnamese favourites to international specialities.

Vista Grill House serves local and international buffet for breakfast and dinner.
