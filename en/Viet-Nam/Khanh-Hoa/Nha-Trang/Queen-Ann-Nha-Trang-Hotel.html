Description

Queen Ann Nha Trang Hotel offers accommodations Nha Trang, 800 meters from Sailing Center Vietnam.

It features an outdoor swimming pool and guests can enjoy meals at the in-house restaurant or have drink at the bar.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. It is 900 metres to Nha Trang Night Market while Nha Trang Beach is 1 km away.

Long Son Pagoda and Nha Trang Bay Tourism Pier are 4 km from Queen Ann Nha Trang Hotel and Ponagar Tower is 4.

9 km away. The nearest airport is Cam Ranh International Airport, 24 km from the property.

Airport transfers are available at additional charges. A flat-screen TV with cable channels, as well as an iPod docking station are available.

You will find a kettle in the room. The rooms come with a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries.

Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for luggage storage. A free fitness centre is available for guests to work out.

Alternatively, guests can indulge in a massage treatment at the spa centre or get free access to the swimming pool.

Royal Restaurant offers a selection of local and international cuisine, while the Lobby Lounge serves up light refreshments.
