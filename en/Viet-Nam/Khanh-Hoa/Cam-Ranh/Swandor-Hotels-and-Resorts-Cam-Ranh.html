Description

Situated along the beautiful Bai Dai coast with crystal clear water, Swandor Cam Ranh Hotels & Resorts is a beachfront property providing elegant accommodation in Cam Ranh.

Offering an outdoor swimming pool, the resort has a spa centre, on site restaurant and bar.

Guests can enjoy free private parking on-site and free WiFi access in public areas.

Swandor Cam Ranh Hotels & Resorts is a 5-minute drive from Cam Ranh International Airport.

Nha Trang city is a 30-minute drive away. Furnished in neutral shades, all ample units feature sweeping views of the sea and air conditioning.

Complete with soundproofing, all rooms have a seating with sofa and coffee table, wardrobe, as well as personal safe.

A cable flat-screen TV, minibar and an electric kettle are available. En suite bathroom is equipped with bath and shower facilities.

For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hair dryer. Swandor Cam Ranh Hotels & Resorts operates a tour desk and 24-hour reception where staff, who speak Russian and English, can assist you with a variety of services.

Sitting on 900 m² land, the children's playground complex features Russian animators from 09:00 until 23:00 daily.

Other facilities include a gym, disco club and shops. The on-site restaurant is available for all-day dining, and the bar serves a range of local beverages and foreign alcoholic drinks.
