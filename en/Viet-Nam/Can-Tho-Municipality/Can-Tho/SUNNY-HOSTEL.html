Description

Situated in Can Tho and with Ninh Kieu Pier reachable within 1. 2 km, SUNNY HOSTEL features express check-in and check-out, non-smoking rooms, a bar, free WiFi and a shared lounge.

Boasting room service, this property also provides guests with a terrace. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, a shared kitchen and organising tours for guests.

The rooms at the hotel are fitted with a seating area. At SUNNY HOSTEL, rooms have air conditioning and a private bathroom.

Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Vincom Plaza Xuan Khanh, Can Tho Museum and Ninh Kieu Footbridge.
