Description

At the resort, each room comes with a balcony with a mountain view. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a shower, while some rooms also feature a patio. At Katerina Pool Villa Resort Phuket, the rooms include air conditioning and a flat-screen TV.

A continental or buffet breakfast is available every morning at the property. The on-site restaurant specializes in Thai cuisine.

Guests can use the fitness center.

Speaking English and Thai, staff at the 24-hour front desk can help you plan your stay.

Home Pro Village - Phuket is a 19-minute walk from the accommodation, while Phuket Zoo is 2.2 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Phuket Airport, 19 miles from Katerina Pool Villa Resort Phuket.
