Description

Stylish and spacious, the air-conditioned villas offer beautiful ocean views their outdoor decks and through their large glass walls. An espresso machine, flat-screen TV and iPod with music are included. The attached bathrooms include a bathtub and hairdryer.

In a secluded and quiet location, the Sri Panwa Phuket is 26 miles from Phuket International Airport. It is just under a quarter of a mile from the Phuket Aquarium and 3 miles from Khaokhad View Tower.

Cool Spa provides relaxing traditional Thai massage. For a refreshing workout, guests can swim at the resort's 2 main pools, exercise at the gym or take a boxing lesson. Island day trips and bike rentals can also be arranged at the tour desk.

Baba Pool Club features 3 dining options, including fusion Thai-Western dishes, Asian hot pot and seafood barbecue. Stylish partying can be enjoyed at the Baba88 club, with its international DJs and premium bar.
