Description

The rooms in the resort are equipped with a TV. On The Hill Karon Resort has some rooms with sea views, and every room includes a balcony. All rooms at the accommodation have air conditioning and a desk.

A continental breakfast is available every morning at On The Hill Karon Resort. Guests can dine in the in-house restaurant, which serves international and Thai cuisine.

The resort has an outdoor pool. Snorkeling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near On The Hill Karon Resort.

Staff at the reception can help guests with any questions that they might have.

The nearest airport is Phuket Airport, 17 miles from the accommodation.
