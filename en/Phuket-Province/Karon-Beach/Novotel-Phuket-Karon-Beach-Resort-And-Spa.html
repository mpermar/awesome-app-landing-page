Description

A short walk from the beach, Novotel Phuket Karon Beach Resort is 1,950 feet from Karon Night Market and a 10-minute drive from Wat Chaithararam Temple. The famous nightlife and entertainment area in Patong is a short drive across the hill, while Kata Beach is a 10-minute drive away. Phuket International Airport is a 45-minute drive away.

The air-conditioned rooms include a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a mini-bar stocked on request and tea/coffee making facilities. Each room includes bathrobes, slippers and a bathroom with free toiletries.

You can dine in the one of the 3 restaurants, grab a drink in one of the 2 bars, or work out in the fitness center. The property also offers a 24-hour front desk, a water slide at the pool and a kid's club.
