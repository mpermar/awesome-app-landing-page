Description

Centara Grand Beach Resort Phuket is a 45-minute drive from Phuket International Airport and 9.3 mi from Phuket Town. Shuttle services and free parking are available.

Some rooms enjoy stunning views of the Andaman Sea from spacious private balconies. Tastefully furnished with modern furnishings, large rooms have a flat-screen TV and luxurious bathroom with a bathtub.

Take in stunning views of the ocean while exercising at the fitness center. Other leisure options at Resort Centara Grand include tennis courts, a first-rate spa and a games room.

Centara Grand’s 7 dining options feature a range of international, Asian and Western dishes. Dining highlights include The Cove, which offers all-day dining, and Italian specialties at Mare.
