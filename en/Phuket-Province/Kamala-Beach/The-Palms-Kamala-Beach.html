Description

Modern suites at The Palms Kamala have a private terrace with either pool views or pool access. Each suite has 2 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms, a living room, kitchen and dining area. Amenities include flat-screen TVs and DVD players.

Guests can work out at the fully equipped fitness center overlooking the pool. Water activities include parasailing, wakeboarding and kayaking. The resort offers car or bike rentals and laundry services.

Fusions Restaurant offers an international menu. Cocktails are served at the Lounge Bar and Pool Bar.

The Palms, Kamala Beach is only a 10 minute drive from lively Patong, which offers a variety of entertainment, shopping and nightlife options.
