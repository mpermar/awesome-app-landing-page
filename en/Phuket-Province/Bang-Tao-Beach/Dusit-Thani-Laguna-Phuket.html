Description

Patong Beach is a 30 minute drive from Dusit Thani Laguna. A half hour drive brings guests to Phuket City.

Luxurious rooms in Dusit Laguna are decorated in classic wood furnishings and modern art pieces. Cable TV channels and tea/coffee-making facilities are provided.

Free Wi-Fi is offered in the entire hotel. Guests can also enjoy a game of golf at the course next to the hotel, or take up a Thai cooking class. Water sports are also available within the resort.

Dusit Thani Laguna Phuket offers 5 dining options where guests can sample international and Italian meals. Seafoods and grills are available at Casuarina Beach Restaurant and Pub, while Ruen Thai has traditional local delights. Guests can also enjoy live music with a cocktail at the Horizon Lounge.
