Description

The rooms come with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a electric tea pot, a shower, free toiletries and a desk. The rooms include air conditioning, and certain units at the resort have a balcony. All rooms include a closet.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning at the property.

Dinso Resort has a playground. You can play ping pong at the accommodation, and car rental is available.

Staff at Dinso Resort are always available to provide guidance at the reception.

Patong Boxing Stadium is 0.6 miles from the resort, while Bangla Road is 0.6 miles away. Phuket Airport is 15 miles from the property.
