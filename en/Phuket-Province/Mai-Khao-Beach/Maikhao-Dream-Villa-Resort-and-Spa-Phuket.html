Description

Featuring a design blending East and West, the Thai Royal-style villas are decorated with classic wood furnishings, Thai prints and fabrics. Each villa also features an outdoor pavilion, well-equipped kitchenette, home entertainment system as well as an private bathroom.

Maikhao Dream Villa Resort & Spa Phuket is within a 15-minute drive from a world-class golf course and Phuket International Airport. It is a 45-minute drive from Patong Beach.

Leisurely afternoons can be spent at Maikhao Dream Spa, which features an array of spa treatments, sauna and steam rooms. Staff can assist with travel and childcare needs. Wellness programs with an in-house Ayurvedic doctor is available at the property.

International buffet breakfasts and an extensive beverage menu are available at Dokbua restaurant while a variety of beverages are served at Wine Cellar and Pines Beach Club. A private chef or in-villa barbecues can also be arranged. Stunning sea views accompany cocktails at Dream Bar.
