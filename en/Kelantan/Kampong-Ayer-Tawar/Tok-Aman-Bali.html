Description

Modern and well-designed, the air-conditioned rooms come with a flat-screen TV and free drinking water. All rooms come with a private balcony and lake views. Private bathrooms have a bathtub.

Centrally located in Kota Bharu, Kelantan, Tok Aman Bali is approximately a 45-minute drive from Lapangan Terbang Sultan Ismail Petra. Parking is free.

Car rentals and day trips can be arranged at the tour desk. The hotel also provides bike rentals and laundry services.

International and local dishes are served at the hotel's restaurant.
