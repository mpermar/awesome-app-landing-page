Description

Harris Hotel Batam is a 5 minute walk from Mega Mall Batam Center and International Ferry Terminal Batam Center. Nagoya district is a 20 minute drive away.

Modern rooms include a flat-screen TV offering cable channels. A safe, minibar and tea/coffee-making facilities are provided. A hairdryer and slippers are also included.

Guests can head for a relaxing massage or buy a gift at the souvenir shop. Fax and photocopying services are available at the business center. Currency exchange services are also available.

Harris Café serves a selection of local and South East Asian cuisine. Italian delights can be found at Harrisimo restaurant. For convenience, room service is also available.
