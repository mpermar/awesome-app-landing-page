Description

Guest rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom, the rooms at Crocodile Bay Resort come with air conditioning, and some rooms are equipped with a terrace. All guest rooms have a desk.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental breakfast.

Guests at Crocodile Bay Resort will be able to enjoy activities in and around Puerto Jiménez, like hiking.

Golfito is 12 miles from the hotel. The nearest airport is Puerto Jimenez Airport, a few steps from Crocodile Bay Resort.
