Description

All rooms at Hotel San Bada offer ocean or park views. They feature hardwood floors, air conditioning and a ceiling fan. Each comes furnished with a 32-inch LCD TV, free Wi-Fi, a minibar and a safe.

There is a restaurant, bar and snack bar at the hotel. Guests can relax in the lounge chairs by the pool, on the terrace or on the grounds.

A wide range of outdoor and water activities are available in the surrounding area, including canoeing, diving, horseback riding or fishing. There is also a game room and the hotel rents bicycles.

Quepos Pier is about a 5 minute drive away. Quepos Managua Airport, with regular flights to San José, can be reached in about 10 minutes by car.
