Description

At the resort, every room includes a closet, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Arenas del Mar Beachfront has some accommodations that feature a balcony, and all rooms include a coffee machine.

A continental breakfast is available every morning at the property. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the in-house restaurant, which serves local cuisine and also offers Vegetarian options.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool. Guests at Arenas del Mar Beachfront & Rainforest Resort will be able to enjoy activities in and around Manuel Antonio, like cycling.

The nearest airport is Tobías Bolaños International Airport, 39 miles from the property.
