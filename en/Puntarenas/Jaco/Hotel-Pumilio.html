Description

Featuring modern décor, the rooms have LCD cable TVs, kitchens, coffee makers and private bathrooms with shower and free toiletries. The breakfast is included in the rate.

Jacó is only 2.5 miles from Hotel Pumilio, where guests can find food and entertainment options. "Gourmet Adventures" is a special menu available on site that includes the menus of different restaurants at the guest service.

Some of the popular activities available while staying at Hotel Pumilio are rafting, kayaking, surfing and visiting Tortuga Island and the Arenal Volcano.

Juan Santamaría International Airport is a 1-hour, 15-minute drive away. Jacó Beach and Herradura Beach are a short drive away.
