Description

The Holiday Inn's own Vapor Restaurant serves contemporary New Zealand cuisine, using the freshest produce, often locally sourced. The Executive Chef's menu includes local New Zealand lamb or seafood matched with a wine from the extensive wine list, and famous gourmet pizzas. Children under age 12 eat free, when dining with an adult.

The rooms feature contemporary design, private bathrooms with showers and a massage option shower head, soft or firm pillows and mattress toppers. All rooms offer individually controlled air conditioning, refrigerators, flat-screen TVs with Sky TV channels, in-room movies, work desks, and iPod docking stations. Tea/coffee making facilities are provided.

Holiday Inn Auckland Airport is only 5 minutes’ drive from Auckland Airport or by shuttle, and 25 minutes’ drive from central Auckland. Villa Maria Estate winery is a 20-minute walk away.
