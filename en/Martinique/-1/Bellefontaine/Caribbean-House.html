Description

Each unit features a oven, microwave, a coffee machine, as well as a fridge and electric tea pot. Some units feature a terrace and/or balcony.

The apartment has an outdoor pool.

A grill is available on site and cycling can be enjoyed within close proximity of Caribbean House.

Les Trois-Îlets is 11 miles from the accommodation, while Fort-de-France is 6 miles from the property.
