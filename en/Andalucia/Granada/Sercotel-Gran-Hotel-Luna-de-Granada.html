Description

The spacious rooms at the Sercotel Gran Hotel Luna de Granada feature air conditioning, a minibar, satellite TV with Plus Soccer channel and a private bathroom with a bathtub and shower. The suites also include a living area with a sofa bed.

The spa at the Sercotel Gran Hotel Luna de Granada has a hot tub, Turkish bath, sauna and paid massage service. There is also an indoor pool, paddle tennis court and free gym.

This hotel is 3 km from the Alhambra palaces. The University of Granada and the city's train station are less than 1 km away.

Parking comes at an extra cost. The hotel offers direct access to the A44 ring road in Granada.

The Sercotel Gran Hotel Luna de Granada is a 20-minute drive from Granada Airport.

Barrio de Ronda is a great choice for travelers interested in eating tapas, food and walking in the city.
