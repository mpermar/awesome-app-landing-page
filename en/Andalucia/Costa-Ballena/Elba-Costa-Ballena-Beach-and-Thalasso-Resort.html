Description

The rooms at Elba Costa Ballena Beach & Thalasso Resort are air conditioned and feature a private bathroom with a hairdryer. There is also free WiFi.

The Elba Thalassotherapy Center includes a thermal circuit and a sauna, as well as cabins in which seawater and seaweed treatments are carried out. These services carry a supplement.

The hotel has a poolside bar and a buffet restaurant with show cooking. There is also an elegant restaurant on the beach specializing in fresh seafood and a relaxation area.

The establishment has paddle tennis courts.

The hotel borders a 27-hole golf course designed by José María Olazábal. Cádiz is 30 km away.
