Description

It offers 278 tastefully decorated rooms, fully equipped. It has excellent views of the sea and the golf course. All include free Wi-Fi.

The hotel houses the Las Gaviotas restaurant, which opens in the summer months and serves international food. There is also a buffet restaurant, a lobby bar and a poolside bar.

In addition, this establishment has 2 swimming pools, one of them for children. There is also a fitness center with an indoor pool, hot tub and Turkish bath.

It has a mini club for children over 12 years and the Kike Club, which organizes activities for children between 3 and 12 years in summer.
