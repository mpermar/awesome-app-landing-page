Description

The spacious and modern rooms have a private terrace or balcony, a seating area, a flat-screen satellite TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries. Some overlook the sea.

The à la carte restaurant Cala Encendida offers Mediterranean cuisine and fresh fish from the area. The Ilunion Calas de Conil also offers a bar and a buffet restaurant serving traditional local dishes. There is free WiFi in all areas.

The establishment offers several services for children, such as a children's club and a park. It also has an entertainment program with aquafitness sessions and shows for adults at night.

Access to the indoor pool, spa, sauna, Turkish bath and Vichy shower comes at an additional cost. Massages and beauty treatments are also provided, and the fitness center is available for free. Sports facilities include paddle tennis, water polo and volleyball. In addition, discounts are offered for several golf courses in the area.

This hotel is adapted for guests with reduced mobility. On request, rooms adapted for people with visual or hearing disabilities are provided, as well as rooms adapted for wheelchairs.

The hotel is a 50-minute drive from Jerez Airport and 150 km from Seville.
