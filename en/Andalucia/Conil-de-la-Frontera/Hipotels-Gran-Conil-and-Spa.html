Description

The Hipotels Gran Conil & Spa offers 3 paddle tennis courts, sauna and Turkish bath for a fee. It also has a gym and indoor heated pool. In addition, massage service is provided.

All rooms are spacious and modern and feature a private balcony, flat-screen satellite TV, air conditioning, safe, minibar and private bathroom with hairdryer and toiletries.

Buffet breakfast and dinner are served in the Gran Conil's restaurant. The hotel also houses a cafeteria and a poolside bar.

The property offers parking, 24-hour reception, car rental and currency exchange.
