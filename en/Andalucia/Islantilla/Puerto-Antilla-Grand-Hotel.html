Description

Rooms at the Puerto Antilla hotel are decorated in a Mediterranean style and feature air conditioning, satellite TV, a safe, free Wi-Fi and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The Los Porches restaurant at Hotel Puerto Antilla serves buffet meals. There is also a poolside bar and 2 à la carte restaurants, one indoor and one outdoor, open in summer.

The hotel's spa, which offers massages and beauty treatments, includes an indoor pool, hot tub, sauna and Turkish baths. A varied entertainment program for adults and children is also provided.

The reception staff provides tourist information about the area. Faro International Airport is approximately 1 hour away by car.
