Description

Some accommodations at Family Life Ocean Islantilla have sea views. All rooms have a safe, minibar and satellite TV. Free WiFi is available throughout.

The establishment has 4 restaurants, including a buffet restaurant, the Green & Grill, La Bodeguita Wine & Food and La Taverna. There are also 2 bars, one of them next to the pool, varied entertainment program and family club.

Family Life Ocean Islantilla has beautiful gardens, a sun terrace with sun loungers around the pool, indoor pool and fitness center. Access to Islantilla beach is provided.

The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day and can organize excursions and car rental and bicycle rental services. An airport shuttle service is available upon request.
