Description

The rooms at Ilunion Islantilla are spacious, bright and modern. They feature air conditioning, a desk, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a safe, a minibar, a free bottle of water and a large furnished private terrace. Many enjoy breathtaking views of the sea. The private bathroom has a hairdryer and free toiletries.

The hotel has a spa with an indoor pool, fitness center, sauna and whirlpools, and offers massages and other treatments.

Ilunion Islantilla has a buffet restaurant and an Italian à la carte restaurant. There is also a cafeteria and a poolside bar.

The hotel offers live entertainment and music for adults and children, depending on the season. It also has tennis and paddle tennis courts and is next to the Islantilla golf course.

Parking comes at an extra cost.

This hotel is adapted for guests with reduced mobility. On request, rooms adapted for people with visual or hearing disabilities are provided, as well as rooms adapted for wheelchairs.
