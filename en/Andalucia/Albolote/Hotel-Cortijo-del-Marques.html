Description

Hotel Cortijo del Marqués is surrounded by wheat and sunflower fields and offers easy access to the A44 motorway and free parking. The Sierra Nevada ski resort is 60 km away.

Each room at the Marqués has rustic-style décor with wooden or tiled floors and wooden beams. All rooms include central heating, a fan, a seating area and a private bathroom with a bathrobe and a hairdryer.

Hotel Cortijo del Marqués has a terrace with beautiful views of the chapel and the countryside, several landscaped courtyards and free WiFi. It also offers a shuttle service to the airport upon request.
