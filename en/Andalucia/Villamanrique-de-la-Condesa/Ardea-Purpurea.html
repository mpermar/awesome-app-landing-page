Description

Rooms at the Ardea Purpurea feature a terrace overlooking the lake and the forest, a TV with a DVD and CD player, free Wi-Fi and air conditioning. All bungalows are equipped with open plan kitchen and living room. All have parking spaces next door.

The hotel restaurant serves Mediterranean cuisine. In summer you can eat on the terrace. The hotel organizes horseback riding and excursions in the countryside.

Ardea Purpurea is 800 meters from the town of Villamanrique de la Condesa and only 35 km from Seville. You can go horse riding 3.5 km away in the Doñana National Park.
