Description

Rooms at the Sol Marbella Estepona-Atalaya Park include a private balcony with views of the mountains, the gardens or the pool. For an additional fee, you can book rooms with sea views. They all have air conditioning, a minibar and a safe.

In the complex you can play football and volleyball, free of charge. There are also 6 tennis courts, a gym and a water sports center open in summer.

Sol Marbella Estepona-Atalaya Park offers free public WiFi. The complex has 2 restaurants and 3 bars and offers a family entertainment program with shows and live music. Also, children's activities are organized and there is a water park for children.

Sol Marbella Estepona-Atalaya Park is located between Estepona and Marbella, just 7 km from Puerto Banús. Private parking is available at an additional cost.
