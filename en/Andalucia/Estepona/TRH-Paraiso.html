Description

The hotel is between Marbella and Estepona, just 4 km from the beach of Atalaya. Guests receive discounts on the green fees of the nearby golf course, as well as other clubs in the area.

The rooms are simply and elegantly decorated with wooden furniture. They have a bathroom with a hairdryer and toiletries, as well as views of the mountains, the sea or the golf course.

The hotel's La Pergola restaurant serves a varied buffet breakfast. In addition, The Pyramid restaurant offers à la carte or buffet dishes for dinner.

The nearby AP-7 motorway offers connections with Marbella and Estepona in about 20 minutes. The city of Malaga is 1 hour away by car.
