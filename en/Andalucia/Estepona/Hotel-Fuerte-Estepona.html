Description

The rooms and suites at Fuerte Estepona are spacious, elegant and modern, and are equipped with air conditioning, balcony, satellite TV and tea and coffee making facilities.

Fuerte Estepona Hotel has a bar, an elegant restaurant, a sun terrace and beautiful gardens.

The Estepona's spa has a sauna and offers a wide range of massages and beauty treatments. In addition, there are Balinese beds and tennis, paddle and volleyball courts. The hotel's Forti club organizes activities for children. There is also an area for adults only with swimming pool.

The Fuerte Experiences hotel program offers gazpacho and sangria workshops. Also, guests can take excursions to the towns of Ronda, Gibraltar and Jerez or even to the city of Tangier, in Morocco.

The property offers discounts on the green fees of several surrounding golf courses and is a 15-minute drive from the Valderrama Golf Club. The nearby A-7 and AP-7 roads communicate directly with Sotogrande and Puerto Banús in 20 minutes and with Gibraltar, in 40 minutes. Private parking is available for a fee.

The airport shuttle service has an additional cost.
