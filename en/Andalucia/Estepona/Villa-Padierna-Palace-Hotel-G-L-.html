Description

The Villa Padierna Palace Hotel G.L. It has rooms and villas surrounded by beautiful gardens. All have views of the golf course, pool or gardens and, in some cases, the Mediterranean Sea.

The rooms and villas feature air conditioning, satellite TV, CD player and luxury Frette linens. They also include a private bathroom with a bath and shower, hairdryer, bathrobe and slippers.

All villas have a private garden and a kitchenette. Some have a private pool.

The Thermae spa features an indoor pool, gym, 2 saunas, 3 steam rooms and a Turkish bath. The Medical Wellness Institute offers a range of beauty and health treatments.

The hotel has 4 restaurants with outdoor terraces and magnificent views. There is also an English-style bar with a fireplace.

The hotel offers a free shuttle service to the summer beach club, which has a restaurant. In addition, sun loungers and beach towels are offered.
