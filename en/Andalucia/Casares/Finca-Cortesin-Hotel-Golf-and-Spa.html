Description

The hotel's 18-hole golf course was designed by Cabell Robinson and is where the Volvo World Match Play Championship was held in 2009, 2011 and 2012. The hotel also houses other facilities such as the Jack Nicklaus Academy, 2 tennis courts and 2 paddle tennis courts

The Cortesín spa has an indoor pool, gym, Turkish bath, sauna and a snow cave. Treatments include algae wraps and massages.

Finca Cortesín Hotel Golf & Spa offers air-conditioned suites with a private balcony, flat-screen satellite TV, free internet access and a bathroom with a hairdryer, bathrobe and slippers.

The hotel features the exclusive Kabuki Raw restaurant and 5 other dining options, such as the Don Giovanni Italian restaurant, the Beach Club, which prepares Mediterranean cuisine, El Jardín de Lutz, which prepares Spanish food, and the Blue bar, which offers tapas and cocktails. The golf club's restaurant overlooks the golf course and the Mediterranean Sea.

The property is a 35-minute drive from Marbella, a 20-minute drive from Sotogrande, a 40-minute drive from Gibraltar Airport and 60 minutes from Malaga Airport.
