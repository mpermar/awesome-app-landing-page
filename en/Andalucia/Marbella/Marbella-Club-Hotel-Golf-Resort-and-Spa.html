Description

The Marbella Club Hotel occupies the former private residence of Prince Alfonso von Hohenlohe, founder of the hotel. It has rooms with private terrace and views of the sea or the garden.

The establishment houses 5 restaurants with international and traditional Spanish cuisine. In Puerto Banús there is a wide variety of designer shops, bars and restaurants.

The hotel provides a shuttle service to the Marbella Club Golf Resort. The 18-hole golf course was designed by Dave Thomas and overlooks the Mediterranean Sea and Africa. The Thalasso spa offers sea views and various treatments, a sauna and a waterbed.

This spacious complex has a children's club that includes a dance studio, music room and enchanted forest, among other educational and leisure facilities.
