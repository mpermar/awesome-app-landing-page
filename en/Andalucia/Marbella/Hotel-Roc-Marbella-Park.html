Description

The spacious rooms at Hotel Roc Marbella Park have air conditioning, a private balcony, a TV and a private bathroom. A safe is available for a fee.

The buffet restaurant at Hotel Roc Marbella Park serves Mediterranean cuisine. There is also a bar next to the pool where you can have snacks, cocktails and drinks.

The hotel has a children's playground and a games room with table tennis. Free WiFi is available in all areas.

There are 3 golf courses within 2 km and the center of Marbella is 15 minutes away by car. Buses to Málaga and Marbella stop in front of the hotel.
