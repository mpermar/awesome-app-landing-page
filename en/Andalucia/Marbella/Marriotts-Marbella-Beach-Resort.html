Description

The complex has 2 indoor and 2 outdoor pools surrounded by sun terraces and gardens. In addition, the property provides towels and free access to the Marriott's gym.

The golf courses of Santa María and Santa Clara are less than 3 km from the hotel. The Funny Beach amusement park is just 3 km away.

Marriott's Marbella Beach Resort apartments feature a kitchen, separate living and dining areas, laundry facilities and a private bathroom with a spa bath.

The complex houses several bars and restaurants, such as the Beachside Grill and the Med Terrace, open seasonally. Among the facilities of the complex, it is also worth mentioning a supermarket.
