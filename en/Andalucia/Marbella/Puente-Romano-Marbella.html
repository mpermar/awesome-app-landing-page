Description

The Puente Romano Marbella has a beachfront bar, a poolside bar and a restaurant on the beach. It also houses a 2-star Michelin restaurant run by Spanish chef Dani García. There are also Italian and Japanese restaurants, a Spanish brasserie, an organic restaurant, Thai and Nikkei restaurants and gluten-free deli. There is also a chill-out area and disco.

The air-conditioned rooms have a private terrace with views of the sea or gardens, satellite TV, iPod docking station and free Wi-Fi. All include 1 green fee per person and stay at the Marbella Club golf club.

There are free sun beds on the beach and around the pool. In addition, the tennis club has a sauna and Turkish bath and offers free fitness activities. Yoga and Pilates classes are also provided, as well as a children's club.

The spa is located in the beach club and enjoys views of the Mediterranean Sea. It offers a wide variety of treatments in its 9 rooms with sea views, as well as hammam, wet area, steam bath and sauna.

The reception is open 24 hours. Car rental service and free parking are provided.
