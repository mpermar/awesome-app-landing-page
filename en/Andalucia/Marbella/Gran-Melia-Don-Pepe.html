Description

Rooms at the 5-star Don Pepe hotel offer views of the sea, the hotel gardens or the mountains. They also have interactive satellite TV, electronic safe and minibar. In addition, all rooms have free Wi-Fi.

The hotel has a heated indoor pool and a gym, which is free for hotel guests. There are also 2 tennis courts and 2 paddle tennis courts. The spa includes a Turkish bath, hot tub and sauna and offers a wide variety of massages and treatments.

The hotel has a grill restaurant specializing in meats, Mediterranean cuisine and barbecue dishes. Tahini Restaurant offers views of the ocean and serves Japanese cuisine.

The hotel has a bar next to the pool and a gourmet area, where oysters and caviar are served. There is also an area specialized in chocolate belda and macarons. In addition, the hotel has a private beach club, where guests can enjoy snacks, cocktails and smoothies.
