Description

Málaga Playa has a beautiful and bright décor and all studios and rooms are equipped with a TV and minibar. Some rooms have a balcony. There is free high-speed WiFi throughout the premises.

The complex has 4 bars, including a poolside bar and a lounge bar with a terrace and sea views. The hotel's buffet restaurant serves a selection of Spanish and international dishes.

The spa has an indoor pool and performs treatments and massages. The hotel also includes sports facilities, such as a gym, and is just a 15-minute drive from the nearest golf course, located in Caleta de Vélez.

During the summer months, the Iberostar Málaga Playa has many facilities for children, such as a children's club, disco for children, playground and splash pool.
