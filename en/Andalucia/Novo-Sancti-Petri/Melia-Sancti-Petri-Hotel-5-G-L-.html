Description

Rooms at Meliá Sancti Petri Hotel 5 * G.L are spacious, lightly decorated and equipped with free Wi-Fi, a minibar, a seating area and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

The hotel is located next to the Novo Sancti Petri golf course, on the Costa de la Luz, about 45 minutes by car from Jerez airport and the beautiful city of Cádiz.

Some of the superior rooms of the hotel enjoy personalized service The Level. This service includes, among other advantages, access to a private pool, free use of superior facilities, a la carte breakfast in the room, private breakfast in The Level lounge and open bar area.

In the Sancti Petri there is a beautiful marble patio with arches and fountains, as well as several restaurants in the courtyard and in the gardens, with buffet menus and à la carte options. The hotel provides free hammocks on the beach to all its guests.

The hotel spa has a Roman bath, footbath, water jets and a variety of massage services and beauty treatments.
