Description

The hotel gardens are on the Costa Tropical, are in Andalusian Arab style and have waterfalls, bridges and grottoes that communicate with the swimming pools.

The suites at Albayzin del Mar are large and bright, and have 2 or 3 bedrooms, air conditioning and heating. There is a large living room with satellite TV, a private terrace and a full kitchenette.

The terrace offers beautiful views of the beaches of the Costa Tropical and the surrounding mountains, located 1 km away.

The hotel has a buffet and à la carte restaurant, piano bar, poolside bar, games room, kids club, gym and pool tables.
