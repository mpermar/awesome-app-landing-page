Description

All rooms have air conditioning, flat screen TV, wardrobe, heating and seating area with sofa. The private bathroom is equipped with a bath, shower and hairdryer.

The hotel has a shared terrace, seating area, playground and an exclusive cafeteria for customers. There is also a golf shop and conference facilities. There are also banquet rooms, airport shuttle service and car rental service, for a fee.

The establishment has a paddle, tennis and minibasket court, all free.

There are shops and restaurants in Arcos de la Frontera, a 5-minute drive from Cortijo Fain. Jerez Airport is 40 km away.
