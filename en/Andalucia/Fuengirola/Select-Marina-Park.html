Description

The complex is surrounded by gardens and houses a fitness center. All apartments have a private terrace or balcony, satellite TV and CD player.

Select Marina Park's restaurants prepare a variety of international à la carte dishes and a daily buffet breakfast is served. The complex also has a cocktail lounge bar and 2 coffee shops, as well as entertainment program and children's club.

The property is just 300 meters from the beach and 6 km from the center of Fuengirola. Free parking is provided.
