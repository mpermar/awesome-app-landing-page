Description

The apartments at Sol Timor have a balcony, satellite TV and a kitchen with a hob, microwave, toaster and fridge. Some apartments offer sea views.

Around the pool there is a terrace with sun loungers and umbrellas. The Timor Sun also has tennis courts and a wide range of children's activities.

Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served in the buffet-style restaurant. The beach restaurant serves classic dishes such as paella, while snacks are served throughout the day at the pool bar. There is also a cafeteria open all day.

The Sol Timor has a supermarket and is surrounded by restaurants and leisure facilities. Near the hotel for a bus that connects with Malaga. Malaga airport is only 5 km away.
