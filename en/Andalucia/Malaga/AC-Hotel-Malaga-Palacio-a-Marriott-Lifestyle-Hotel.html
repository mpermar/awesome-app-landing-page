Description

The AC Hotel Málaga Palacio presents a simple and contemporary decoration in all facilities. The rooms are equipped with a minibar and satellite TV. Free WiFi is available throughout.

The hotel restaurant offers a buffet breakfast and a selection of Spanish dishes. The elegant dining room enjoys panoramic views of the sea and the park. On request, gluten-free menus are served.

Guests can enjoy a drink or snack at the AC Lounge or at the Attic Bar, located on the terrace on the 15th floor.

The famous Picasso Museum is just 700 meters from the hotel and the Alcazaba, a 5-minute walk away. Malaga train station is 2 km away.

Centro de Málaga is a great choice for travelers interested in museums, eating tapas and shopping.

Experiences

08/04/2016

Very good hotel, superb location but needs a few tweaks to justify star rating and price

"Superb location and very clean. Not sure how long it has been a Marriott but a few small additions would make this truly an international 4* hotel. First service at the terrace pool bar could be better it's quite slow and you have to wait some time to get the attention of a waiter! Also although fantastic views - possibly the very best of any hotel in Malaga there are not enough comfortable places to sit - too many high tables and chairs for the visiting drinkers not enough outdoor chairs or loungers for guests with older or younger children who want to use the pool late pm or early eve- hotel needs to decide if this is a place just for guests and cater accordingly or not. A huge benefit would also be some large umbrellas or canopies for shade - there is no shade and even at 4pm in the summer the heat and glare can be brutal. Other things - there is a kettle and mugs in my superior room but no tea, coffee etc and when I asked about this they said they did not normally provide this but could do so but nothing ever arrived! So what was the advantage of the kettle?? I would also say that the bathroom though lovely had a really bad smell at times and the only tissues were quite hard and in small packets. No cotton buds, nail file or other things you might expect in a vanity kit was provided. I travel a lot and normally stay in 4 and 5* hotels so expect these sorts of things. I really liked the hotel decor and room - really modern and interesting. A few minor thing - bathrooms need more hooks so people can hang towels and swimming costumes etc to dry - arm on make up mirror was far too short and if standing at the sink you had to really lean over to use mirror! Lighting at bedside needs to include a function where you can turn off each bedside light individually at the moment it's all on or off making it difficult if one person wants to read and one to sleep."
