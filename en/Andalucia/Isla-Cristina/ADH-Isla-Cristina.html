Description

The rooms are bright and spacious and feature a private balcony (some with sea views), a separate living room with a sofa and satellite TV, a safe, a minibar and a kitchenette with a fridge. There is free WiFi.

The ADH Hotel Isla Cristina's restaurant serves a daily buffet breakfast. There is also an à la carte restaurant, a poolside bar and a lounge bar.

The hotel has a games room with billiards. The reception is open 24 hours a day and has a car rental service. Islantilla golf complex is a 10-minute drive away. It is possible to practice sailing and windsurfing nearby.
