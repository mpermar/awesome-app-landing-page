Description

The rooms also have a ceiling fan, a safe and a minibar with a selection of free welcome drinks. They also include a desk, wardrobe and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Buffet breakfast is served in the restaurant. There is also a bar and a cafeteria. The entertainment program includes shows and live music 6 nights a week.

The tour desk offers car rental and currency exchange. In the area, it is possible to practice numerous activities, such as water sports, go horse riding and play tennis or golf.
