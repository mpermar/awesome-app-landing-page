Description

All villas have furnished porches overlooking the garden and barbecue facilities. The living room has a fireplace, sofa and flat-screen TV. The villas also include 2 bathrooms, 1 toilet and a large well-equipped kitchen with dishwasher and washing machine.

There are shops, bars and restaurants within walking distance of the property, as well as paddle and tennis courts. The Novo Sancti Petri golf course is 3 km away.
