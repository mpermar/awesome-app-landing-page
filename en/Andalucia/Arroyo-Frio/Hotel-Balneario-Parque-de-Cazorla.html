Description

Parque de Cazorla offers bright and spacious rooms with a hot tub, minibar and air conditioning.

For an additional fee, the spa area has a heated pool, hot tub and thermal showers. There is also a Turkish bath, sauna and relaxation area. Massages and treatments are also offered.

The restaurant at Parque de Cazorla serves buffet meals that include typical local dishes. There is also a cafeteria with terrace area and a TV lounge.

In the town of Arroyo Frío there are several bars and restaurants, as well as a variety of craft shops. This hotel also organizes guided walks through the mountains. Private covered parking is available for a fee.
