Description

The rooms are decorated in warm tones. The private bathroom has a hairdryer and toiletries.

The luxury spa at Cabogata Mar Garden Hotel is equipped with an indoor pool and hot tub. There is also a massage service and beauty treatments.

The Cabogata Mar Garden Hotel & Spa is 15 km from the center of Almeria. It is next to the El Toyo golf course and close to the Cabo de Gata National Park.

The hotel offers a buffet consisting of freshly prepared homemade specialties with fresh local products. There is also a poolside bar and another with a large terrace.
