Description

The spacious rooms at Barceló Cabo de Gata have a furnished terrace, satellite TV and a minibar. The rooms are decorated in natural tones and elegant wooden furniture.

Guests can enjoy drinks and snacks on the terrace overlooking the beach of Barceló Cabo de Gata. There is also a poolside bar and a buffet restaurant that serves freshly cooked Spanish food.

The Barceló's spa offers a gym, sauna, Turkish bath and hydrotherapy pool. Massages and treatments can also be requested.

The hotel is located next to the Alborán golf course, between the Cabo de Gata National Park and the city of Almería. Cañada Airport is only 5 km away.
