Description

The Terrazas Costa del Sol complex is built around a beautiful Andalusian-style main square, full of fountains and palm trees.

The complex has an infinity pool with magnificent views of the Mediterranean Sea and the impressive rock of Gibraltar. You can also sunbathe on a nearby beach, where there are sun loungers and a tapas bar. The establishment organizes several free activities for children.

The apartments are spacious, have exquisite contemporary decor with local Moorish touches and have air conditioning, full kitchen and a large furnished terrace. The safe is available for a fee.
