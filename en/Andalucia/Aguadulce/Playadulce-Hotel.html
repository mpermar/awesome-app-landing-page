Description

Playadulce facilities include a tennis court and a mini-golf, available for a fee. It also has a heated indoor pool. Within walking distance, you will find 3 golf courses, one of them is La Send. The hotel's Torito Beach Club bar is open in high season.

All rooms at Playadulce have a balcony or terrace and satellite TV. They are also equipped with air conditioning and heating.

Adults can access the Acquaplaya spa at Hotel Playasol, in Roquetas de Mar. It includes a spa circuit and a wide range of treatments.

Buffet breakfast is served in the Andalucía restaurant. The children's area has facilities for parents to use. A complete leisure program for adults and children is offered. This complex also has a bar and a pub.
