Description

The property features a rooftop terrace, a swimming pool with stunning views of the Giralda, free Wi-Fi, a tour desk and free afternoon snacks.

Casa 1800 Sevilla is decorated in neutral tones and has chandeliers and luxury furniture. The rooms have satellite TV, free mineral water, bathrobe and slippers.

Plaza España and the Maria Luisa Gardens are within walking distance of the property.

Historic center of Seville is a great choice for travelers interested in architecture, sensations and eating tapas.
