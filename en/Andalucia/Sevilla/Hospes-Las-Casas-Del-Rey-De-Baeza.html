Description

The rooms at Hospes Las Casas del Rey de Baeza are elegantly decorated in soft cream and brown tones. All include a TV and private bathroom.

The hotel restaurant, the Azahar, serves a wide variety of Andalusian dishes. You can also enjoy tapas in the patio.

Casas del Rey de Baeza is a 5-minute walk from Sierpes Street in Seville, famous for its shops. In addition, it is about a 12-minute walk from Real Alcazar and a 5-minute walk from the famous Metropol Parasol structure, located in the Plaza de la Encarnación.

Historic center of Seville is a great choice for travelers interested in architecture, sensations and eating tapas.
