Description

The rooms at this beautiful design hotel are spacious and feature air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, a minibar, satellite TV, a safe and a modern bathroom with a hairdryer.

The Silken Al Andalus's restaurant serves international cuisine and a varied buffet breakfast. The establishment also has a cafeteria that offers a daily menu, as well as a poolside bar and an elegant bar.

Al-Andalus Palace is located in the Seville neighborhood of Heliopolis, a 5-minute drive from the Plaza de España and next to a bus stop with regular service. It is also 1 km from Pineda Golf Club and 2 km from María Luisa Park.
