Description

Rooms at Hotel Palacio de Villapanés are spacious and combine original elements with wooden floors and modern-style furniture. They have satellite TV, telephone, air conditioning and desk. They also include a bathroom with bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries.

The hotel houses elegant lounges and a typical central courtyard with marble columns and tiles. The restaurant prepares traditional Andalusian dishes and has a garden terrace, where you can taste various types of tapas. Breakfast is served every morning for a fee.

Hotel Palacio de Villapanés is 350 meters from Casa de Pilatos and 900 meters from Seville Cathedral. The Real Alcazar is about 10 minutes away on foot. In addition, the hotel provides a shuttle service to Seville airport for a fee.

Historic center of Seville is a great choice for travelers interested in architecture, sensations and eating tapas.
