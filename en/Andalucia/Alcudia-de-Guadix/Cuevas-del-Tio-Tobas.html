Description

The caves, apartments and studios of Uncle Tobas are individually decorated and feature a fireplace, kitchen and bathroom. Some also offer beautiful views of the surrounding countryside.

Free internet is available in the restaurant and in the business center. The property includes a barbecue area and a small shop selling local handicrafts and products. Staff can provide information on activities that can be practiced in the area.

The caves are in Andalusia, at the foot of the Sierra Nevada. Granada is only 60 km away and the beaches of Cabo de Gata are only 1 hour away by car.
