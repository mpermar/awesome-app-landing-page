Description

The Miramar Spa & Beauty includes a water circuit with hot tub and indoor pool. There is a lounge with internet connection. Free WiFi is provided.

The establishment has an adventure park for children, which includes a green and sandy area with several games. Hotel Fuerte El Rompido is 400 meters from the charming town of El Rompido, known for its prawns. The hotel's restaurant, Dosfaros, specializes in seafood.

El Rompido Fort is next to the El Rompido golf course. The hotel offers a free boat transfer service to a pristine beach, 800 meters away. There is also a free indoor pool and an outdoor hot tub. The hotel has a club for children from 4 to 12 years old that offers numerous activities and an entertainment program.

The hotel offers a wide range of daily activities, a spa and cocktail workshops. Guests can also participate in olive oil tasting sessions or in flamenco classes. There is a shallow pool for children, plus 2 parks for them. There is also an adult pool. Private parking is available, for a fee and subject to availability.

The hotel is 94 km from Faro Airport, the closest, and 130 km from Seville Airport. El Rompido is located 85 km from the Doñana Natural Park.
