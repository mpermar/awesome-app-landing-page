Description

All rooms and apartments at the Alcazar have air conditioning and a balcony, some with sea views. The apartments have a kitchenette with a microwave and a refrigerator.

The hotel spa has Turkish baths, a sauna and an indoor pool. The hotel also offers a hairdressing salon, a beauty salon and a bowling alley.

The hotel's restaurant serves buffet cuisine that includes a wide variety of Mediterranean dishes. There is also a coffee bar.

The Best Hotel Alcazar is located on the coastal road N-340, which connects Malaga with Almeria. Granada and its airport are about 50 km away.
