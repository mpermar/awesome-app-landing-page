Description

The Barceló Jerez Montecastillo & Convention Center has a tennis court and fitness center. The Barceló Montecastillo also has a modern spa equipped with a hot tub, sauna, Turkish bath and hydrotherapy circuit. There is also an 18-hole golf course designed by Jack Nicklaus.

The rooms at Jerez Montecastillo are spacious and bright and have a private balcony and air conditioning.

The Barceló Jerez Montecastillo & Convention Center is well connected to the A-4 and A-382 motorways. In addition, the property is a 5-minute drive from the city of Jerez.
