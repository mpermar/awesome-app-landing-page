Description

Rooms at Drury Inn & Suites Cincinnati North all include a microwave and a refrigerator. A hairdryer and ironing facilities are provided.

Guests at the Cincinnati North Drury Inn & Suites can swim in the indoor-outdoor pool or relax in the hot tub. Free hot appetizers and cold beverages are served nightly.

A 24-hour business center is provided. Laundry facilities are also available.

Each morning guests can enjoy a free hot breakfast. Ruby Tuesdays, which serves traditional American food, Chinese Imperial, and a variety of other dining options are an 8-minute drive away.

Great American Ballpark and King's Island Amusement Park are 16 miles away.
