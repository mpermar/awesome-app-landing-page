Description

At the hotel, every room is equipped with a desk. The private bathroom is equipped with a bath. All guest rooms feature a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at Antika Hotel. The restaurant at the accommodation serves European and pizza cuisine.

At Antika Hotel guests are welcome to use an indoor swimming pool. You can play darts at the hotel.

Sochi Winter Theatre is 3 miles from Antika Hotel, while Sochi Summer Theatre is 3.2 miles away. The nearest airport is Sochi International Airport, 16 miles from the property.
