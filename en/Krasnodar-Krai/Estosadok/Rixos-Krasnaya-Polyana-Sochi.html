Description

Rooms and suites will provide you with a flat-screen TV and air conditioning. Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with a bath and a hairdryer. You can enjoy a mountain view from all the rooms. Most rooms include a balcony, and suites come with a living room.

At Rixos Krasnaya Polyana Sochi you will find a restaurant and a night club.

Rixos Royal Spa center features a swimming pool with hot tub, Turkish hammam, sauna, fitness center and a VIP area. Guests are offered classical or relaxation massages, cosmetology services and detox Ayurvedic programs. Other facilities at the hotel include a meeting room, luggage storage and children's club.

Estosadok Train Station is 1.9 mi from the hotel. Sochi International Airport is 23 mi away.
