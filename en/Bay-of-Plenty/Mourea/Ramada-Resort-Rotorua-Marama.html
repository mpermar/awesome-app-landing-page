Description

The rooms include a flat-screen TV. Certain accommodations feature a sitting area for your convenience. You will find a kettle in the room. Each room has a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer.

There is babysitting services at the property.

You can play tennis at this resort, and the area is popular for fishing. The resort also provides car rental. Mount Maunganui is 29 miles from Ramada Resort Rotorua Marama, and Tauranga is 26 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Rotorua Airport, 4.3 miles from Ramada Resort Rotorua Marama.
