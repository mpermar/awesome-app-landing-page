Description

The elegant rooms have flat-screen TV and a bathroom with exclusive toiletries. They are decorated with warm colors and earth tones.

The free form swimming pool features a slide. Recreational facilities include racquetball courts, an outdoor hot tub, and a modern spa area. Children of all ages can have fun at the Danyland Club, which has professional staff and lots of activities.

A varied and rich breakfast buffet is provided every morning. The Dan Eilat Hotel also includes restaurants and bars where international cuisine, drinks and gourmet menus are served.

The hotel is 2 miles from the center of Eilat. Eilat Airport is a 5-minute drive away.

Any additional older children or adults are charged ILS 810 per night for extra beds.
The maximum number of extra beds in a room is 1.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
