Description

The rooms in the hotel are equipped with a electric tea pot. Rooms contain a flat-screen TV, and some rooms come with a river view. All rooms at Terra Vita feature air conditioning and a closet.

A à la carte, halal or vegetarian breakfast is served at the property. At the accommodation you'll find a restaurant serving Mediterranean cuisine.

At Terra Vita guests are welcome to use a hot tub. Hiking is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the hotel.

Kazan State University is a 19-minute walk from Terra Vita. The nearest airport is Kazan International Airport, 15 miles from the property.
