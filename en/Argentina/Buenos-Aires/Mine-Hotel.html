Description

Boasting designer décor, rooms at Hotel Mine feature large windows and concrete floors. All of them have cable TV, minibars, and DVD players.

A buffet breakfast with complimentary hot dishes is served daily. Drinks and hot beverages from the coffee bar can be enjoyed on the grounds.

Guests can unwind or enjoy themselves reading a book on the lounge chairs by the pool. Evita Peron Museum is one mile away. Palermo's lively nightlife is literally around the corner.

The 24-hour front desk can assist guests with tips for exploring the city attractions and secure shuttles to Ezeiza airport, 31.4 miles away.

Mine Hotel is 4.4 miles from Jorge Newbery Domestic Airport, and 7.2 miles from the Bus Terminal. Malabia Metro Station is one mile away.

Palermo is a great choice for travelers interested in nightlife, gourmet food and restaurants.
