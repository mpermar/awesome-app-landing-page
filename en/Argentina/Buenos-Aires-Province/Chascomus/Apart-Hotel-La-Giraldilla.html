Description

Situated in Chascomús, Apart Hotel La Giraldilla provides accommodation with free WiFi, seating area and TV.

The accommodation consists of a living room, a well-equipped kitchen with a dining area, and a private bathroom with bidet.

A terrace with lake views is offered in all units. Guests at the aparthotel can enjoy a buffet or an American breakfast.

Apart Hotel La Giraldilla offers an outdoor pool. A garden and a terrace are provided at the property.
