Description

Hotel Santa Rosa offers a luxurious spa, fitness centre and free Wi-Fi in Olavarría.

It is located within the city's commercial centre. The rooms here are decorated in elegant colour themes and feature paintings on the walls.

They will provide you with a private bathroom with hairdryer, minibar and air conditioning.

There is also a desk, heating and a safety deposit box. Some have a TV and spa bath.

At Hotel Santa Rosa you will find a 24-hour front desk. There is an onsite wellness centre, restaurant and bar to enjoy.

Other facilities include a tour desk where local tours can be reserved, business centre and a laundry service.

Guests can also enjoy all facilities at a local nearby club. It includes a golf course, tennis courts, heated pool, semi-Olympic swimming pool, basketball court and rowing, among other activities.

The property lies 8 blocks from Olavarría bus station and 10 km from Olavarría Airport.
