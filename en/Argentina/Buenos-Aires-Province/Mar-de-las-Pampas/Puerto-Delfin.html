Description

The accommodations come with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with bath or shower and free toiletries, while the kitchen has an oven. A microwave and toaster are also featured, as well as a electric tea pot.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property.

Puerto Delfín has a playground.

A kids' club is also available at Puerto Delfín, while guests can also relax on the sun terrace.

Villa Gesell is 6 miles from Puerto Delfín, while Pinamar is 17 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Villa Gesell - Pinamar International Airport, 7 miles from the property.
