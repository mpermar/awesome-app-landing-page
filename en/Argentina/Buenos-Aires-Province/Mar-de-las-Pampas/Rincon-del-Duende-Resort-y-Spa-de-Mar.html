Description

Boasting a spa, 2 swimming pools, a fitness centre and a clay tennis court, Rincon del Duende Resort y Spa de Mar offers plush self-catering accommodation with free Wi-Fi in Mar de las Pampas.

It features a restaurant and private access to the beach. Decorated with tiled floors and gabled wooden roofs, apartments and bungalows at Rincon del Duende feature seating and dining areas.

Self-catering facilities include microwaves and refrigerators. Some of them have BBQ facilities, flat-screen TV and private hot tubs.

Breakfast is served daily. The restaurant offers a wide variety of international dishes.

Drinks from the bar can be enjoyed on the sun loungers by the pool. Guests can sunbathe by the pool or in the private beach zone.

They can also get fit in the fitness centre or engage in a tennis match. Free parking is provided.

Rincon del Duende Resort y Spa de Mar is 30 metres from the beach and 150 metres from de town centre.

Villa Gesell Bus Station is 5 km away. The heated pool is not available from Monday to Thursday during August and September.
