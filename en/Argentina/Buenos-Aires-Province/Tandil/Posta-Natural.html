Description

Featuring a garden, a terrace and an outdoor swimming pool, Posta Natural offers self-catering accommodation with free Wi-Fi and BBQ facilities in Tandil.

The city centre and commercial area are 6 km away. Providing a tranquil environment, the apartments in Posta Natural feature kitchens, flat-screen satellite TVs and air conditioning.

Posta Natural features a children’s playground, en event room and a game room with a TV.

Cribs and baby fedding chairs are available and 24-hour medical assistance is provided.

The 24-hour front desk can arrange room service and provide tourism information. Posta Natural is 2 km from El Centinela hill and 8 km from the bus terminal station.
