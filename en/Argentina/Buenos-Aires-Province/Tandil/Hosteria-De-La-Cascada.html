Description

This country-style hotel with cosy bungalows in a landscape framed by mountains and woodlands, features an indoor heated swimming pool and free WiFi.

It is situated at Tandil´s Cerro La Cascada. The bungalows at Hostería De La Cascada are designed out of stone combined with wood, providing a cosy atmosphere.

All accommodation is equipped with cable TV and a minibar. Guests can enjoy a stroll alongside the river that runs through the 50 acres of parkland.

Treks lead through the surrounding nature. Guests can enjoy a traditional breakfast.

Drinks can be ordered at the bar while enjoying the warm atmosphere of the fireplace in the communal living room.

They also host a restaurant that's open daily for lunch or dinner, and during Saturday night the restaurant offers exclusively the typical Argentinian Asado.

De La Cascada Hosteria is an 8-minute drive from downtown Tandil and a 2-minute drive from Del Fuerte Lake.
