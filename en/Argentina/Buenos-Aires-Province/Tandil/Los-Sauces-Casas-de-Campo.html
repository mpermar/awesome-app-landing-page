Description

The resort village offers a seating area with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with free toiletries, a hair dryer and bath or shower. Some units also have a kitchenette equipped with a microwave.

A continental breakfast is available every morning at the property.

Los Sauces Country Houses has a sun terrace.

The Cascade is 2.8 miles from the accommodation, while Don Quixote's Monument is 2.9 miles away.
