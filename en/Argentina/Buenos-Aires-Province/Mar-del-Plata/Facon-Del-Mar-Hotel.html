Description

Facón Del Mar Hotel offers accommodations in Mar del Plata, only 350 metres from the beach.

There is a restaurant and bar that serves a daily breakfast. Guests can have fun at the games room as well.

The rooms have a flat screen TV. All of them have a private bathroom with a bath or shower and a bidet.

Free toiletries and a TV are provided. Guests will find a 24-hour front desk at the property.

They can also play table tennis and billiards at the hotel. The hotel offers bike hire and car hire.

Mar Del Plata Central Casino is 500 metres from Facón Del Mar Hotel, while Bristol Beach is 600 metres from the property.

The Bus Station is 2500 metres away, whereas Astor Piazzolla International Airport is 7 km from Facón Del Mar Hotel.
