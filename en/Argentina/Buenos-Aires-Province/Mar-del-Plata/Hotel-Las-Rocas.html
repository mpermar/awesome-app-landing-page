Description

Boasting a swimming pool, a hot tub and a fitness centre, Hotel Las Rocas offers rooms with free Wi-Fi and air conditioning in Mar del Plata.

Breakfast is provided. Playa Grande is 500 metres away. Fully carpeted, rooms at Las Rocas feature cable TV and minibars.

some of them have private balconies overlooking the city. A buffet breakfast is served daily.

Snacks and drinks can be ordered at the bar. Guests can relax on the sun loungers by the pool, make use of the sauna and hot tub or get fit at the fitness centre.

Massage sessions can be requested. Airport shuttles can be arranged. Hotel Las Rocas is a 30-minute drive from Brigadier de la Colina Airport and 500 metres from the Bus Terminal.
