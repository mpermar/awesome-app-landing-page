Description

All units are equipped with a flat-screen TV and a fully equipped kitchen with a microwave. There's a fully equipped private bathroom with bath and free toiletries.

A buffet breakfast is available each morning at the property.

At the condo hotel guests are welcome to use a fitness room.

In addition to an outdoor pool, Puerto Hamlet also provides a children's playground and shared lounge.

Villa Gesell is 7 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Villa Gesell - Pinamar International Airport, 9 miles from Puerto Hamlet.
