Description

Fitted with a balcony, the units feature a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with bath and free toiletries. An oven and electric tea pot are also available.

A continental or vegetarian breakfast is available daily at the property.

The condo hotel offers a range of wellness facilities including a hot tub and a sauna.

A private beach area can be found at Carilo Soleil By HS, along with a shared lounge.

Villa Gesell is 7 miles from the accommodation, while Pinamar is 4.2 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Villa Gesell - Pinamar International Airport, 9 miles from the property.
