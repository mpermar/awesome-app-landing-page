Description

Situated in Costa del Este, Almarena Apart & Hotel Boutique offers an outdoor pool and sun terrace.

San Clemente del Tuy is 28 km away. Free private parking is available on site. All units are air conditioned and feature a cable flat-screen TV.

Some units have a seating area and/or terrace. There is also a kitchen in some of the units, equipped with an oven and microwave.

Almarena Apart & Hotel Boutique features free WiFi throughout the property. Towels and bed linen are featured.

Almarena Apart & Hotel Boutique also includes a hot tub, sauna, and hammam. Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant.

The property also offers packed lunches. Event facilities with views of the sea are available.
