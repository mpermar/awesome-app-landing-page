Description

Altos de la Reserva has barbecue facilities and a garden in Costa del Este. All rooms boast a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom.

Free WiFi is included throughout the property. All guest rooms at the hotel come with a seating area.

The rooms include a kettle, while selected rooms also boast a kitchen with an oven.

Guest rooms at Altos de la Reserva have air conditioning and a wardrobe. Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental breakfast.

Altos de la Reserva offers a heated outdoor pool. Cycling, fishing and hiking are among the activities that guests of the hotel can find nearby.

San Clemente del Tuyú is 29 km from Altos de la Reserva, while San Bernardo is 8 km from the property.
