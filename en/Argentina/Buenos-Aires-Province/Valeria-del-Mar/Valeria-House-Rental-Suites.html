Description

Featuring a swimming pool, a hot tub, a gym and a tennis court, Valeria House Rental Suites offers self-catering apartments with free Wi-Fi and plasma TVs.

Breakfast is provided. Valeria del Mar Beach is 200 metres away. Decorated with tiled floors and exposed-brick walls, apartments at Valeria House Rental feature heating and ceiling fans.

All of them are fitted with fully-equipped kitchens and separate dining areas, and some have patios with BBQ facilities.

Breakfast with coffee, croissants, cheese and butter is served daily. Guests can relax on the sun loungers by the pool, or get fit in the gym.

They can also enjoy themselves in the games room. Free parking is provided. Valeria House Rental Suites is 500 metres from the commercial centre and a 5-minute drive from Pinamar.
