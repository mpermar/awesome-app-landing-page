Description

Located 200 metres from Balneario Hemingway beach and from the commercial centre, Paihue Cabañas offers self-catering accommodation with free Wi-Fi in Valeria del Mar.

Breakfast is included and served daily. The property has an on-site garden and outdoor pool.

Luggage storage and cleaning services can be arranged. Guests at Paihue Cabañas are provided free bicycles to explore the area and free public parking on site.

Providing a tranquil environment, the apartments in Paihue Cabañas feature kitchens, dining areas and garden views.

Some apartments offer BBQ facilities. Paihue Cabañas is 5 km from Pinamar town and bus station.
