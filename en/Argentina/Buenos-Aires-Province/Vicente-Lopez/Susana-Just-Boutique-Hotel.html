Description

Featuring a garden with a swimming pool, Susana Just Boutique Hotel offers rooms and apartments with free Wi-Fi and private balconies in Vicente Lopez, Northern Buenos Aires.

The city centre is 20 km away and free parking is provided. Decorated with stylish furnishing and contemporary painting, rooms at Susana Just Boutique Hotel are fitted with heating and fans.

All of them afford garden views and have air conditioning. Breakfast is served daily.

Susana Just Boutique Hotel is 3. 5 km from Unicenter Shopping Mall and 2. 4 km from Dot Shopping Mall.

Ministro Pistarini International Airport is 56 km away, whereas Jorge Newbery Airport is 10 km from the property.
