Description

Comfortable rooms with free Wi-Fi are offered in downtown Mar de Ajó just in front of Cuerrías Beach.

A daily buffet breakfast is included and there is a 24-hour bar. Rooms Mar de Ajó will provide you with a flat-screen TV and cable channels.

Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with a hairdryer and towels. Extras include a safety deposit box and a fan.

Room service is available. At Hostería Mar de Ajó you will find a 24-hour front desk.

Other facilities offered include shared lounge, a games room and a tour desk. Santa Teresita Airport is 20 km away and the bus terminal is 1.
