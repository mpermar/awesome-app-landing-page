Description

Boasting a garden with a swimming pool, and a fitness centre, Hotel Villa de Merlo offers rooms in Villa de Merlo.

Breakfast is provided and there is a restaurant. The commercial area is 200 metres away.

Free Wi-Fi is available in common areas. With large windows offering valley and mountain views, rooms at Villa de Merlo feature fans and TVs.

Some of them have air conditioning. A buffet breakfast with coffee, tea, hot chocolate, milk, fruits and yogurt is served daily.

Regional and international dishes can be enjoyed at the restaurant and BBQ facilities are featured in the garden.

Massage sessions and beauty treatments can be requested at the spa. Guests can relax on the sun loungers by the pool or engage in paddle matches.

Free parking is provided. Hotel Villa de Merlo is 400 metres from Flamingo Casino and 2 km from the Bus Station.
