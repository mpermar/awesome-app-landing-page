Description

Comfortable rooms with garden views are offered in a quiet area 5 km off Potrero de los Funes.

Regional dishes are available at the restaurant and guests can make use of a library of movies and books.

La Posada Koslayna has rooms with satellite TV and private bathrooms with showers.

San Luis City is 12 km away. Guests staying at Koslayna can rely on the tour desk for getting around the area and there is 24-hour front desk assistance.

Merlo is 200 km away and Brigadier Mayor Cesar R. Ojeda is 17 km away. Private parking is free.
