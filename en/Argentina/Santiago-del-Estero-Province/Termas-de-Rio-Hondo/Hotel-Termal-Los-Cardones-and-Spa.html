Description

This hotel is located 300 metres from the centre of Termas de Río Hondo and features an outdoor swimming pool and an indoor pool with a hydromassage tub.

It also has a fitness centre and a garden. Free Wi-Fi is available in the entire hotel.

Hotel Los Cardones features rooms with garden or pool views. All rooms have a private bathroom, air conditioning, spa bath and LCD cable TV.

Los Cardones has a restaurant and a bar, and offers buffet breakfast. Guests can request laundry, ironing, and dry cleaning services at 24-hour front desk.

There is also a tour desk, where car rental is available, and a children's games room.

Guemes Park is 500 metres away, and the Airport of Santiago is 65 km away. Free parking is available in the hotel.
