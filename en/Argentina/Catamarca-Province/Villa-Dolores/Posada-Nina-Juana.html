Description

Offering a year-round outdoor pool and barbecue, Posada Niña Juana is located in Villa Dolores in the Catamarca Province Region, 9 km from San Fernando del Valle de Catamarca.

The inn has a sun terrace and views of the mountain, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Free WiFi is offered throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Each room comes with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. You will find a coffee machine in the room.

All rooms are fitted with a private bathroom. Superior rooms feature a spa bath or a hot tub.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. The inn also offers free use of bicycles.
