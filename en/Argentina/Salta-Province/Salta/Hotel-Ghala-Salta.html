Description

Located only 300 metres from 9 de Julio square, Hotel Ghala Salta features an indoor pool and a fitness centre.

Guests can enjoy an on site restaurant. Spacious and bright, rooms at Hotel Ghala feature carpeted floors and stylish furniture.

All of them have cable TV and air conditioning, and some boasts private spa baths.

A buffet breakfast is daily served. The restaurant prepares international dishes, and drinks from the bar can be enjoyed on the terrace.

Guest can unwind on the sun loungers or request a relaxing massage session. The property features a casino and a games room including billiards.

Guests can also make use of a golf course located 10 km away. Please note that the pool is open from 08:00 to 23:30.

The 24-hour front desk can help guest secure shuttles to Miguel de Güemes Airport, which is located 11 km away.
