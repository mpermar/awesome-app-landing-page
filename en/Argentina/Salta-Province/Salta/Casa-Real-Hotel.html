Description

Boasting an indoor swimming pool, Casa Real Hotel offers spacious rooms with a panoramic view of Salta and the surrounding mountains.

Free WiFi access is provided. A daily breakfasts is served. This hotel is located close to Salta's main tourist attractions, with easy access to the business district.

All of the guestrooms at Casa Real Hotel feature air conditioning, a dining area, a minibar and a flat-screen TV.

Bathroom facilities include a bath tub, free toiletries and a hairdryer. Guests can make use of a fully equipped business centre, fitness centre, and a spa club with an indoor heated swimming pool and hydromassage tub.

The 24-hour front desk can arrange massage sessions for an extra fee. Salta's city centre and 9 de Julio main square are just 600 metres from Casa Real.

The hotel is 100 metres from Paseo Balcarce street, full of lovely bars and restaurants.
