Description

Located in Cafayate, 300 m from main square, and cathedral, 200 m from bodega El Porvenir, 100 m from bus station.

Casa Cactus Cafayate provides rooms with air conditioning, cold and heat, WI FI, linen and towels provided, mais service daily and continental breakfast included.

Outside pool in season from November to April. The area is popular for cycling, and bike hire and car hire are available at the accommodation.
