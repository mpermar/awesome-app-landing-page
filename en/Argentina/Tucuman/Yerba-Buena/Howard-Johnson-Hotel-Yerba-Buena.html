Description

Featuring a semi covered swimming pool, Howard Johnson Hotel Yerba Buena is located in Yerba Buena.

Free WiFi access is available in all areas. The bright and comfortable rooms at Howard Johnson are equipped with air conditioning and cable TVs.

They also include minibars and private bathrooms. This elegant hotel offers its guests a large restaurant serving a wide variety of dishes.

Guests can enjoy fitness facilities. Massage treatments are available for an additional cost.

The property offers free parking. It is a 15 minute drive from downtown San Miguel de Tucumán.
