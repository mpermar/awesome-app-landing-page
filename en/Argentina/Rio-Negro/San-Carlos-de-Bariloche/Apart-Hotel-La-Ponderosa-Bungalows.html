Description

Located 200 metres from Bonita beach, La Ponderosa offers self-catering studios and apartments with lake views and BBQ facilities.

Catedral ski centre is 12 km away. The accommodation in La Ponderosa features fully equipped kitchens, heating and TVs.

Daily maid service is included. Free Wi-Fi is available and there is 24-hour front desk assistance.

San Carlos de Bariloche’s commercial area is 7 km away and Teniente Luis Candelaria international airport is 19 km away.
