Description

Hostería Güemes is set in San Carlos de Bariloche, 700 metres from Civic Centre. 11 km from Cerro Catedral Ski Resort, the property provides ski storage space.

Free WiFi is included. The units in the inn are fitted with a TV with cable channels.

With a private bathroom equipped with a bidet and free toiletries, rooms at Hostería Güemes also feature a garden view.

All rooms have a wardrobe. Guests at the accommodation can enjoy an Italian breakfast for a supplement.

The area is popular for skiing and cycling. Serena Bay is 11 km from Hostería Güemes.
