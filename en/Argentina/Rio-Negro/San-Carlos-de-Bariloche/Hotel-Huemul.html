Description

Featuring a regional restaurant, a bar, and a fitness centre, Hotel Huemul offers free Wi-Fi, buffet breakfast and direct beach access in San Carlos de Bariloche.

The city centre is 1 km away. Providing a tranquil environment, the rooms in Hotel Huemul feature private bathrooms.

Some overlook the lake and mountains, whereas the economic ones feature internal views.

Guests at Hotel Huemul are welcome to use the property’s computer during their stay.

Activities such as kayaking, boating,windsurfing, and fishing can be arranged for an extra fee.

The hotel has a sauna, a lounge and a heated pool with panoramic beach and lake views.

Lounge chairs and beach umbrellas are provided. Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas.

Hotel Huemul is 17 km from Catedral ski centre and 20 km from Luis Candelaria international airport.
