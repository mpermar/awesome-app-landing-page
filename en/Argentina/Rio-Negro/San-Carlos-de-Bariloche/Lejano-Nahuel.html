Description

Idyllically situated on the Nahuel Huapi Lakeshore, Lejano Nahuel is a wooden mountain lodge surrounded by colourful rose bushes.

It offers spacious long-stay bungalows with free Wi-Fi and complimentary parking.

The private bungalows at Lejano Nahuel feature red brickwork interiors and fully equipped kitchens.

All have separate dining areas and a cable TV, and some rooms offer panoramic frontal lake views.

The lobby bar, fully decked in honey-coloured wood, offers a continental breakfast with fruits, natural juices and cakes.

Due to its ideal location the hotel is surrounded by restaurants and bakeries. Lejano Nahuel is 3 km from both the centre of Bariloche, and the Cerro Cathedral Ski Station.

It is also 20 km from Bariloche Airport, and provides car rental and an airport shuttle service.
