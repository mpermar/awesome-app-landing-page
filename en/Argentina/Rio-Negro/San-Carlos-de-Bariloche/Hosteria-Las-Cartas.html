Description

Featuring a restaurant, Hosteria Las Cartas offers rooms with free Wi-Fi and lake views in Bariloche.

Catedral Ski Centre is 27 km from the property. Free parking is provided. Decorated with wooden walls, rooms at Hosteria Las Cartas are fitted with heating, fully-equipped kitchens and dining areas.

Guests can enjoy Patagonian dishes and craft beer at the restaurant located in front of Hosteria Las Cartas.

Transfers and excursions can be booked on site. Hosteria Las Cartas is 24 km from Nahuel Huapi lake and from the city centre.
