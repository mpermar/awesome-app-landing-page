Description

Located at the gateway of Chico Circuito de Bariloche, Boutique Hotel & Spa Bosque del Nahuel offers charming Alpine-style cottages with a hot tub.

Guests can enjoy garden views and private parking is free. Cerra Catedral Ski centre is 20 km away, whereas Bariloche City is 16 km from the property.

Cottages at Boutique Hotel & Spa Bosque del Nahuel are fitted with air conditioning, flat-screen satellite TV and full kitchen facilities.

They are styled with light wood furnishings, rustic stone details and natural fabrics.

Some of them feature spa baths and fireplaces. Spa facilities include a heated pool, a fitness centre and a dry sauna.

Guests can also enjoy a film at the small movie theatre, and request a massage treatment for an extra fee.
