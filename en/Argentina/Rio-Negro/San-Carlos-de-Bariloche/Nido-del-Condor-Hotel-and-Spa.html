Description

Nido del Cóndor Hotel & Spa invites you to enjoy the warmth of a cottage, the personal attention dispensed by accommodating staff and all the luxurious accommodation of a 5-star hotel.

Cerro Catedral Ski Centre is 11 km away. The Nido del Cóndor faces the Nahuel Huapi Lake and is just 300 metres from the Bonita Beach.

Bariloche city centre and the international ski centres are a short drive away. Transfers to and from the ski centre can be secured at the property.

Ski lessons can be taken and ski equipment can be hired. ski storage service is also provided.

Nido del Cóndor Hotel & Spa offers in- and outdoor pools for children and adults.

In addition, you will find a luxurious spa on site, where several body treatments and beautiful lake views await you.

Spa facilities include a sauna, Scottish showers and Finnish Baths. Massage sessions can be requested at a fee.

Under the surveillance of the hotel's team, your kids can also participate in activities involving physical exercises, video games and movies on the Playing Site.
