Description

Set on the shores of Lake Nahuel Huapi, this 3-star hotel in San Carlos de Bariloche is just a 15-minute drive from the airport and vast Cerro Catedral ski centre.

Boasting wonderful views of the lake, the Sol del Nahuel offers an attractive Spa and Health Club with sauna, hydromassage tub, and heated pool.

The Sol de Nahuel's spacious, tastefully furnished rooms are equipped with king-size beds, soundproofed windows, and individually adjustable heating.
