Description

The studios, duplexes and apartments range between 388 ft² and 700 ft² in size. In each of them, authentic rustic decorations are combined with a practical kitchenette, cable TV and other amenities.

Guests of Best Western Villa Sofía Apart Hotel also provide free parking lots and internet access in public areas.

In addition, they can enjoy a restaurant, bar and a spa with indoor pool, whirlpool, gym, sauna and other services.
