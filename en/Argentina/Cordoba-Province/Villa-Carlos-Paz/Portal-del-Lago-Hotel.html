Description

Featuring a garden, 4 swimming pools and a restaurant, Portal del Lago Hotel offers rooms with free Wi-Fi in Villa Carlos Paz.

Free covered parking is provided. The property is located right in front of San Roque Lake.

Decorated with exposed-brick walls, rooms at Portal del Lago are very bright. They feature heating, air conditioning and cable TV.

All of them have private bathrooms. Regional and international dishes can be ordered at the property’s restaurant.

Drinks and snacks from the bar can be enjoyed on the terrace, overlooking the lake.

American breakfast is included. Guests can relax on the sun loungers by the 3 outdoor pools, or dive in the heated one.

They can also make use of the sauna, or engage in tennis and paddle matches. San Martin Avenue is 400 metres away.

Portal del Lago Hotel is 2 km from 9 de Julio Avenue, the commercial area of Villa Carlos Paz.
