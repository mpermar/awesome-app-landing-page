Description

Featuring an outdoor pool, a fitness centre and a restaurant, Amerian Carlos Paz & Suites offers self-catering apartments with free Wi-Fi and plasma TVs in Villa Carlos Paz.

Free parking is provided. The commercial area is 50 metres away. Decorated with tiled floors and modern furnishing, apartments at Amerian Carlos Paz & Suites are fitted with air conditioning and heating.

All of them have fully-equipped kitchen and a dining area. International dishes can be ordered at Mirador Restaurant, which is located on the top floor and afford scenic city views.

Guests can make use of the shared barbecue facilities, subjected to availability.

Amerian Carlos Paz & Suites is 350 metres from the bus terminal and 45 km from Ambrosio Taravella International Airport.
