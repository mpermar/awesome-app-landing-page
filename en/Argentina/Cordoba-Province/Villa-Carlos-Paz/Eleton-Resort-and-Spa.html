Description

Located in Villa Carlos Paz in the Córdoba Province Region, 27 km from Cordoba, Hotel Eleton Resort boasts an outdoor pool and views of the mountain.

The hotel has a spa centre and hot tub, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

Free WiFi is offered throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Rooms include a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Some units have a seating area where you can relax.

All rooms are fitted with a private bathroom with a bidet and shower, with free toiletries and a hairdryer provided.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. Villa Carlos Paz center is 3.

7 km from Hotel Eleton Resort, while Villa Giardino is 39 km from the property. The nearest airport is Ambrosio L V Taravella Airport, 26 km from Hotel Eleton Resort.
