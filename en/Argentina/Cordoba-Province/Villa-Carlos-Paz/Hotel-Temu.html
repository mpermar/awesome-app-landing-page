Description

Located 600 metres from the city centre and 500 metres from the bus terminal, Hotel Temu features a heated indoor swimming pool, a bar and a restaurant in Villa Carlos Paz.

BBQ facilities are available for guest use. Providing a tranquil environment, the rooms in Temu feature private bathrooms, cable TVs and heating.

Air conditioned rooms are available for a surcharge. Guests at Temu Hotel are provided with daily breakfast.

Room service is available and luggage storage services can be arranged. A common TV room is featured.

Hotel Temu is 40 km from Ingeniero Tarabella Airport. Private parking is possible on site for an extra fee.
