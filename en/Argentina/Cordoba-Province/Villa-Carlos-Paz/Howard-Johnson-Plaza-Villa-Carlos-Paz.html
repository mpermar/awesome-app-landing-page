Description

Featuring a garden and free WiFi, Howard Johnson Plaza Villa Carlos Paz is located in Villa Carlos Paz, 1.

2 km from Uruguay Bridge. Built in 2016, the property is within just 1. 2 km of City Hall and 1.

8 km of Cuckoo Clock. Attractions in the area include Sports Centre, a 14-minute walk away, or Nuestra Señora del Carmen Church, set 1.

8 km from the property. At the hotel, the rooms are equipped with a desk. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom fitted with shower while some rooms at Howard Johnson Villa Carlos Paz also feature a seating area.

A buffet breakfast can be enjoyed at the property. At the accommodation you will find a restaurant serving Argentinian cuisine.

The property has an external heated covered pool. Round-the-clock assistance is available at the reception.
