Description

Featuring a spa, a heated pool, and a restaurant, Los Espinillos Hotel y Spa offers rooms with free Wi-Fi and air conditioning in Villa Carlos Paz.

The nearest river beach club is 500 metres away. Bright and spacious, rooms at Los Espinillos Hotel y Spa are fitted with heating and cable TV.

All of them have private bathrooms. Breakfast is served daily. International dishes can be ordered at the restaurant.

Guests can relax in the garden by the pool, or benefit from beauty treatments at the spa.

Spa facilities include a sauna, and Scottish and Finnish showers. Free parking is provided subject to availability.

Los Espinillos Hotel y Spa is 400 metres from the Pedestrian Area and 60 km from Cordoba International Airport.
