Description

Featuring a spa and a big garden with a pool and BBQ facilities, Hotel Pinares del Cerro offers rooms with free Wi-Fi and cable TV.

Breakfast is provided, and there is a restaurant. Down Town Villa Carlos Paz is 1.

5 km away. Offering garden views, air conditioned rooms and apartments at Pinares del Cerro are elegantly decorated with wooden furnishing and pastel-coloured bedspreads.

Some of them feature plasma TVs and balconies. A buffet breakfast including coffee, tea, yogurt, natural juices, bread varieties, ham, cheese and cornflakes is served daily.

There is a restaurant offering a wide variety of international dishes. Pinares Aqua Spa features a sauna and a massage room.

Guests can relax on the sun loungers by the pool, or engage in tennis table matches.

A children’s playground is also featured. Free parking is provided, and airport shuttles can be arranged.

Hotel Pinares del Cerro is 40 km from Pajas Blancas International Airport, and 1 km from San Roque Lake.
