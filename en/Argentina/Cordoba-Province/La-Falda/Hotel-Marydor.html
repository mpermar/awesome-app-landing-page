Description

Located on Eden Main Avenue, Hotel Marydor features a garden with a swimming pool.

It offers rooms with free Wi-Fi in La Falda. Breakfast and free parking are provided.

The commercial centre is 200 metres away. Rooms at Marydor have cable TV and ceiling fans.

All of them have private bathrooms. A continental breakfast is served daily. Bar service is provided during summer.

Guests can relax on the sun loungers by the pool while their kids play in the playground.

Hotel Marydor is 700 metres from the bus station and 80 km from Cordoba city and 60 km from Ingeniero Aeronáutico Ambrosio L.
