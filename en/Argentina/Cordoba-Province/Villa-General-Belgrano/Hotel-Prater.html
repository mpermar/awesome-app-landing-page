Description

Featuring free WiFi, Hotel Prater offers accommodation in Villa General Belgrano, 11 km from Santa Rosa de Calamuchita.

Free private parking is available on site. The property has a lush garden and a football course.

The rooms are equipped with a TV. Every room has a private bathroom. There is concierge service at the property.

Guests can enjoy massage therapies. A children's playground is also available. You can play table tennis at the hotel.

La Cumbrecita is 22 km from Hotel Prater, while Yacanto de Calamuchita is 37 km from the property, downtown is 500 metres away.
