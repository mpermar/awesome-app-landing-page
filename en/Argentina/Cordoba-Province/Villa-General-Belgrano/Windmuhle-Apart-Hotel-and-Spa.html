Description

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool and a garden, Windmuhle Apart Hotel offers self-catering accommodation with free Wi-Fi and Continental breakfast in Villa General Belgrano.

The city centre and bus terminal are 2 km away. Providing a tranquil environment, the rooms in Windmuhle Apart Hotel feature kitchenettes, air conditioning, and private bathrooms with Scottish showers.

Guests at Windmuhle Apart Hotel can enjoy the game room and paddle court. The tour desk can arrange hourse riding, trekking, and golf activities in the area.

Windmuhle Apart Hotel is 80 km from Cordoba city centre and 100 km from Ingeniero Ambrosio Taravella International Airport.
