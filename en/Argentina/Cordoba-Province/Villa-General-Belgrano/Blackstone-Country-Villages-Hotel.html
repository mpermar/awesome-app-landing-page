Description

Offering a year-round outdoor pool, barbecue and children's playground, Blackstone Country Villages Hotel is situated in Villa General Belgrano, 6 km from Brewer Park Villa General Belgrano.

Free WiFi is featured throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Each room at this hotel is air conditioned and has a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Some rooms feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day. A balcony or patio are featured in certain rooms.

Every room has a private bathroom with a bidet and shower, with free toiletries provided.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. You can play tennis at the hotel.

The nearest airport is Ambrosio L V Taravella Airport, 77 km from Blackstone Country Villages Hotel.
