Description

Fitted with a patio, the units feature a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with bidet and free toiletries. There's a seating and/or dining area in some units.

A continental breakfast is available every morning at the property.

The vacation home has an outdoor pool.

A grill is available on site and fishing can be enjoyed within close proximity of Complejo Tierra Mia.

Mina Clavero is 7 miles from the accommodation, while La Cumbrecita is 25 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Ingeniero Aeronáutico Ambrosio L.V. Taravella International Airport, 55 miles from the property.
