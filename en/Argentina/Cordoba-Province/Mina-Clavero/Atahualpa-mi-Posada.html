Description

Boasting a lush garden with a swimming pool and BBQ facilities, Atahualpa mi Posada offers rural accommodation in front of Los Sauces River.

Free WiFi access and continental breakfasts are provided at this inn. It is 600 metres from Mitre Avenue.

Featuring tiled floors and exposed brick walls, rooms at Posada Atahualpa have fans and heating.

Some rooms are located on the ground floor and others are on the 1st floor. Housekeeping and laundry services are provided.

A continental breakfast is served. Drinks, hot brews and snacks can be enjoyed at the bar.

A children's playground is available. Guests can relax on the sun loungers by the pool or read a book in the lounge, which features a fireplace.

There is a children’s playground as well. “Los Elefantes” river beach is 100 metres away.

Free parking is available, and shuttles to the Bus Terminal are provided. Atahualpa mi Posada is 100 km from Cordoba International Airport.
