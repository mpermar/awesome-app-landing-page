Description

Offering free WiFi, Balcón del Río, Hotel de Campo and Cabañas is set in Mina Clavero.

This 3-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk and a concierge service. The hotel features an outdoor swimming pool and room service.

At the hotel, rooms are fitted with a wardrobe. Rooms come with a private bathroom, and some rooms also offer a terrace.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning at the property. Balcón Río Campo Cabañas offers a barbecue.

Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Mina Clavero, like cycling.

La Cumbrecita is 29 km from Balcón del Río, Hotel de Campo and Cabañas, while Nono is 7 km away.

Ingeniero Aeronáutico Ambrosio L. V. Taravella International Airport is 89 km from the property.
