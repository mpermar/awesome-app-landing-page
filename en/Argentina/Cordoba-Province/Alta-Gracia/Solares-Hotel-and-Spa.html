Description

Featuring a seasonal outdoor swimming pool, a spa, a restaurant, and a fitness centre, Solares Hotel & Spa offers free Wi-Fi and buffet breakfast in Alta Gracia.

Providing a tranquil environment, the rooms in Solares Hotel & Spa feature private bathrooms and cable TVs.

Room service is available and laundry services can be arranged upon request. Guests at Solares Hotel & Spa can arrange bicycle rentals and tours at the hotel.

Free tickets to the Che museum and Manuel de Falla, located 300 metres away, are provided.

Drinks and snacks can be ordered at the on-site bar and Leyendas Sports Bar restaurant offers regional and international dishes.

Solares Hotel & Spa is 500 metres from the shopping centre and 200 metres from Alta Gracia golf club.

Ingeniero Ambrosio Tavarella International Airport is 50 km from the property. Free private parking is possible on site.
