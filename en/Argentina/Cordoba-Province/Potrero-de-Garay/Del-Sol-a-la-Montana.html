Description

Surrounded by a large parkland with native vegetation, Del Sol a la Montaña offers self-catering accommodation with free WiFi access.

The property has an outdoor swimming pool and barbecue facilities. A daily breakfast is included.

Apartments at Del Sol a la Montaña feature air conditioning, heating, kitchen facilities, a seating area and a complete bathroom, including a bidet and free toiletries.

All of them have lovely views of the garden and mountains. Bed linens are included.

A wide variety of activities can be enjoyed in this property, including meditation.

Other activities available in the area include massage treatments. Del Sol a la Montaña has 24-hour front desk assistance and a shared lounge are offered.

It is located in front of the lake, only 300 metres from the river. Parking is free of charge.
