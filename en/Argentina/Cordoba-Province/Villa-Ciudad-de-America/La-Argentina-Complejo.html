Description

Featuring a garden with an outdoor pool, La Argentina Complejo offers comfortable rooms in Villas ciudad de Amércia.

The property is 300 metres from Los Molinos Lake. Free WiFi access is available. Rooms at La Argentina Complejo include seating areas, dining tables and private bathrooms.

All of them are equipped with heating and ceiling fans, and offer views of the garden and surrounding mountains.

Guests can enjoy the property's outdoor barbecue facilities and make use of the common kitchen.

There is also a games room, a fitness centre and a lively shared lounge area. Guests staying here can enjoy a yoga and an art workshop.

Massage treatments, reiki sessions and dance classes are available for a surcharge.

At La Argentina Complejo the front desk is available for assistance 24-hours a day.
