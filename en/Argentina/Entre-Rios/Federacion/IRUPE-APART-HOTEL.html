Description

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool, a restaurant, and BBQ facilities, Irupe Apart Hotel offers free Wi-Fi and breakfast in Federación.

The Hot Springs are 100 metres away and the commercial area is 800 metres from the property.

Providing a tranquil environment, the rooms and bungalows in Irupe Apart Hotel features kitchenettes, cable TVs, and air conditioning.

Guests can relax in the garden or use the property’s bicycles to explore the area.

Free WiFi access is available in public areas. Irupe Apart Hotel is 600 metres from Federación Bus Terminal.
