Description

Boasting an outdoor swimming pool surrounded by garden and spa facilities, Club Valle Termal Resort has apartment-style accommodation in Federacion.

The hot springs are 500 metres away and a daily breakfast is offered. Club Valle has practical apartments with air conditioning, garden views and kitchen facilities.

There is an on-site restaurant. The commercial area and the bus station are 600 metres away.
