Description

Surrounded by a garden with a pool, BBQ facilities and a football pitch, Terrazas de Colón is only 1.

5 km from Colon hot spring. A daily continental breakfast is provided by courtesy of the property.

Boasting private decks overlooking the garden, rooms at Terrazas de Colón have spa baths and cable TVs.

All of them feature private bathrooms with spa baths. There is a restaurant offering homemade food, and drinks from the bar can be enjoyed in the garden.

Free WiFi access is available in the swimming pool area and the restaurant. Guests can unwind on the sun loungers by the pool, or engage in football and volleyball matches.

Leisure facilities also include children pool and playground. Massage sessions can be requested, and bikes can be rented to explore the city.

Free parking is provided. Terrazas de Colón is 1. 5 from the riverside and 800 meters from the Bus Station.
