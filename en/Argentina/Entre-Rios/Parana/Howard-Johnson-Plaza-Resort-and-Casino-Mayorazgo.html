Description

Featuring 2 swimming pool, a spa with massage rooms, saunas, a fitness centre and 2 hot tubs, Howard Johnson Plaza Resort & Casino Mayorazgo offers rooms with free Wi-Fi and plasma TVs in Paraná.

It features a restaurant. The Museum of Natural sciences is 500 metres away. Fully carpeted, rooms at Howard Johnson Plaza Resort are fitted with heating and air conditioning.

Some of them have private terraces and spa baths. An American breakfast is served daily.

Gourmet, regional and international dishes can be ordered at the property’s restaurant.

Drinks and snacks can be enjoyed at the bar. Guests can benefit from several beauty treatments at the spa, which features a fitness centre, a dry sauna, a wet sauna and 2 hot tubs.

They can also dive in the heated pool or in the outdoor pool. There is also a casino.

Howard Johnson Plaza Resort & Casino Mayorazgo is 1 km from 1 de Mayo square and from the bus terminal.

Justo José de Urquiza National Airport is 7 km away. Free parking is provided and it is subject to availability.
