Description

Tempting guests with an outdoor pool, Hotel Las Azaleas is located in Jardín América.

The refreshing Saltos del Tabay waterfall is found 1. 5 km away and Paraná River is 3 km from the hotel.

Rooms here are traditional and smart, with dark wood furniture and floors. Each provides you with a private bathroom, cable TV, air conditioning and a desk.

You can enjoy a garden view from all the rooms. At Hotel Las Azaleas you can depend on 24-hour front desk assistance and make the most of the garden.

Guests are located 4 km from the nearest commercial and financial area. Jardín América bus station is 5 km away.

Posadas Libertador General José de San Martín International Airport is 88 km from the property.
