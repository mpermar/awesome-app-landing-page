Description

Located just a 15-minute drive from Iguazu Falls, El Pueblito Iguazu offers an outdoor swimming pool and an on site restaurant.

Free WiFi access is available, and a daily breakfast is provided by courtesy of the property.

Located in Iguazu, the hotel is 1 km from Shopping Mall and 2 km from Iguazu Casino.

Featuring lovely views of the garden, each room at El Pueblito Iguazu is equipped with some kitchen facilities including a minibar, an electric kettle and a coffee machine.

All of them are fitted with comfortable seating areas, flat-screen TVs and safety deposit boxes.

Private bathrooms are completed with free toiletries, a bathrobe, a hairdryer and a bathtub.

Some units offer ironing facilities and balconies with outdoor furniture. Interconnected rooms are available as well.

Guests staying here can enjoy a lovely garden and a bar. There is a 24-hour front desk that provides tour information and assistance with ticket purchasing.

Tickets to visit thematic park Biocentro are included in the rate. Other facilities offered include a shared lounge, a solarium and a chapel.

A wide array of activities can be enjoyed in the surroundings, including hiking. Massage sessions and barbecue facilities can be requested for an additional cost.

Housekeeping services are provided. El Pueblito Iguazu offers free parking. The hotel is 2 km from downtown and 2.

3 km from Duty Free Shop Puerto Iguazu. Cataratas del Iguazu Airport is 15 km away.
