Description

Hotel Saint George boasts a lush garden with 2 pools and spa facilities. It is located 300 metres from Puerto Iguazu city centre, and 17 km from the Iguazu Waterfalls.

Rooms at Saint George are decorated in pastel colours. They feature cable TV and minibars.

Some of them have a garden view. A buffet breakfast is served daily. Doña María Restaurant features a terrace and serves regional and international dishes.

Frozen Margaritas can be enjoyed in the Quincho Bar, by the pool. Spa facilities include a hydromassage tub for 8 people, a dry sauna and Scottish showers.

There is also a massage room. The games room features table tennis and table football.

Hotel Saint George is located opposite a bus station where you can take a bus to the waterfalls, and 19 km from Puerto Iguazú International Airport (Argentina).
