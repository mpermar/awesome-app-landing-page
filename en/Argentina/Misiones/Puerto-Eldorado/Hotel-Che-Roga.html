Description

Offering an outdoor pool and a fitness centre, Hotel Che Roga features comfortable rooms in Puerto Eldorado, only 3 km from downtown.

Free WiFi is available, and a daily breakfast is served by courtesy of the property.

Each room here is air-conditioned and fitted with marble floors. They all include views of the garden, flat-screen TVs, minibar and private bathrooms with free toiletries.

Accommodations also feature heating and safety deposit boxes. Guests staying at Hotel Che Roga can relax in the communal sauna and unwind in the garden.

A 24-hour front desk, a shared lounge, a tour desk and a children's playground are also available.

Che Roga hotel offers free parking. It is 100 km from Cataratas del Iguazu and 120 km from Saltos del Moconá.
