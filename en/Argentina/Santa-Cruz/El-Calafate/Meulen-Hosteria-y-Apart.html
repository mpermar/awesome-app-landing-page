Description

Scenic views in a privileged natural scenario can be enjoyed at Meulen Hostería y Apartment, a small hotel with chic décor, located near an ecological reserve and the Argentino Lake.

Free transfers to the city centre are provided and tours to Perito Moreno Glacier can be booked.

Rooms feature panoramic views of the lake and forest and are fitted with chic, old style furnishings.

The glaciers can be reached within an hour and a half drive. Downtown Calafate is a 20 minute walk.

Guests can also take the free shuttle to the city centre offered by the hotel in the morning and in the afternoon.

The restaurant is a true highlight of Meulen Hostería y Apartment. After breakfast, guests can follow the chef's suggestions and experience the best of its regional dishes and desserts.

Meulen Hostería y Apartment offers airport transfers for an extra fee and currency exchange services for its guests.
