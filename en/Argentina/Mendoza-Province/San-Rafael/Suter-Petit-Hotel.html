Description

The ranch-style Suter Petit Hotel offers cosy accommodations in the centre of San Rafael.

Guests can explore the wine cellar, or make use of the outdoor pool and free Wi-Fi.

Rooms at the Suter Petit Hotel feature the stonework architecture found throughout the hotel.

All have air conditioning and private balconies, while larger suites have a separate lounge area.

Guests at the Suter Hotel can relax with a glass of regional wine in front of the fireplace, or explore the hotel's winery.

A concierge service is available for booking excursions. An American buffet breakfast is available daily, from 07:30 to 10:30.

For additional dining options, the hotel is within walking distance from the town centre.
