Description

Finca Los Almendros offers a wide array of activities in Tunuyán. This rural inn boasts an outdoor pool, and a spacious terrace including barbecue facilities.

Free WiFi access is available. Featuring lovely views of the mountains, accommodations at Finca Los Almendros are comfortably fitted with well-equipped kitchen facilities, extra long beds, and seating areas.

Each of them includes air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and heating. The inn offers a wide selection of accommodation types, including suites and 1 and 2 bedroom chalets.

Private bathrooms are completed with a hairdryer. Guests staying at this inn will find a lovely garden and a children´s playground.

The tour desk provides helpful information for getting around the area. A wide array of activities is available on site, including hiking, cycling and vegetable harvesting.

Shuttle services and daily housekeeping can be arranged for a surcharge. Free parking services are provided.

Finca Los Almendras is 800 metres from route 89, where the Caminos del Vino is located.

The Vines of Mendoza is 7 km from the property, whereas El Plumerillo Airport is 86 km away.
