Description

At the hotel, rooms come with a closet, a TV and a private bathroom. Selected rooms also feature a kitchen with a microwave.

Los Arrayanes National Park is 2.5 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is San Carlos De Bariloche Airport, 36 miles from the property.
