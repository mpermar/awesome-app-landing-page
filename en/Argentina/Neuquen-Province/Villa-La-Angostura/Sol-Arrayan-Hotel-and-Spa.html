Description

All guest bedrooms at the Sol Arrayan Hotel & Spa feature a TV, a minibar and a private bathroom. The rooms also offer views of Lake Nahuel Huapi and the mountains behind.

The hotel’s restaurant serves local and international dishes in a stylish setting with views of the lake. The restaurants of Villa La Angostura are just a 20-minute walk from the hotel.

Guests can enjoy the swimming pool facilities or relax with a soothing spa treatment. Hotel staff can organize local horseback riding excursions, and there is excellent skiing in the nearby Cerro Bayo.

The Hotel & Spa Sol Arrayan is just a 1-hour drive from Bariloche Airport. Free on-site parking is available.
