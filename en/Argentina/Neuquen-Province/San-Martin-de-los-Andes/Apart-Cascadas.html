Description

Offering year-round outdoor and indoor swimming pools and barbecue facilities, Apart Cascadas is located in San Martín de los Andes in the Neuquén Province Region, 900 metres from National Park Lanin.

Lacar Lake is 600 metres away. Free WiFi is offered throughout the property. The accommodation has a flat-screen TV and CD player.

There is a seating and/or dining area in all units. There is also a kitchen, equipped with an oven and microwave.

A toaster, a fridge and stovetop are also featured, as well as a coffee machine and a kettle.

There is a private bathroom with a bidet, shower and a spa bath in every unit. Towels and bed linen are provided.

Apart Cascadas also includes a spa centre, hot tub, and sauna. Free private parking is available on site.
