Description

The rooms in the resort are equipped with a TV. All rooms come with a private bathroom, while some have a pool view. All guest rooms will provide guests with bed linen and towels.

A buffet breakfast is served daily at the property.

Porto South Beach (Porto Vacation C has a terrace.

Ras Sedr is 17 miles from the accommodation.
