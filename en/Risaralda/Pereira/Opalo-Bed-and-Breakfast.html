Description

All guest rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area. Featuring a private bathroom with a shower, rooms at Opalo Bed & Breakfast also feature free WiFi, while some rooms also feature a mountain view. Guest rooms include a closet.

The accommodation offers a continental or à la carte breakfast.

The wellness area at Opalo Bed & Breakfast is comprised of a hot tub and a sauna.

Sanctuary of Our Lady of Fatima is 2.9 miles from the hotel, while Consota Recreational Park is 4 miles away. The nearest airport is Matecaña International Airport, 1.9 miles from Opalo Bed & Breakfast.
