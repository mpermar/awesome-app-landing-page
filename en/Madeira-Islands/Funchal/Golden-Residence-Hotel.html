Description

All rooms of the Golden Residence are equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and a teakettle. Some rooms have side views of the North Atlantic Ocean.

The GoldenSpa offers various health and beauty treatments, including hot stone massages. It also features a heated indoor pool, a gym, and a meditation room.

Golden Flavours Restaurant serves regional and international cuisine made with fresh produce from the hotel’s vegetable garden. The Golden View Bar serves a variety of drinks and refreshments, including cocktails.

Central Funchal is only 2 miles away. The promenade, located next to the hotel, features views of one of the highest sea cliffs in Europe, the Cabo Girão.

Sao Martinho is a great choice for travelers interested in relaxation, nature and nature walks.
