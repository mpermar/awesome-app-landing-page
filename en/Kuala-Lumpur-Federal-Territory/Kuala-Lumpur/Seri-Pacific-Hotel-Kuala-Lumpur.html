Description

Featuring a modern décor, the luxurious rooms at t Seri Pacific Hotel are fitted with hardwood floors. They all feature a flat-screen TV with movie channels and a fully stocked minibar. Select rooms offer views of the Putra World Trade Centre, the Genting Highlands or the Petronas Twin Towers.

On the lobby level, Zende Restaurant serves all-day buffets of Asian delights and international favorites. With views of the landscaped garden, Kofuku Japanese Restaurant opens for lunch and dinner. Guests can sip drinks at Cheraza Lounge, accompanied by live music entertainment.

The Seri Pacific Hotel Kuala Lumpur is a 10-minute drive from Kuala Lumpur’s central business district and within 2 miles of Pavilion Kuala Lumpur and Chinatown. The hotel is 47 miles from Kuala Lumpur International Airport.

The Seri Pacific Hotel Kuala Lumpur has a business center and a gift shop which sells souvenirs.
