Description

With elegant décor and free Wi-Fi throughout, each of the guest rooms include air conditioning, a satellite TV, DVD player and minibar. The modern bathroom is stocked with bathrobes, slippers, a scale and hairdryer.

Guests can enjoy traditional Greek and Mediterranean cuisine at the on-site restaurants. Choose from cocktails, refreshing drinks and coffees prepared by the various bars throughout the Pomegranate Spa Hotel. Room service is also available.

Sporting activities include a fitness center, billiards, tennis court and beach volleyball. Children can have fun in their own pool and playground or participate in the kids club.

Pomegranate Spa Hotel provides private parking. The center of town is just over half a mile away, and Nea Moudania is 3 miles away. It is 34 miles from Thessaloniki International Airport. Shuttle services to and from the airport are available upon request and for a fee.
