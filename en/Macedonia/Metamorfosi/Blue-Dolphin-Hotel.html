Description

Rooms at Blue Dolphin are tastefully decorated and have a balcony with gardens views. They come equipped with a satellite TV, air conditioning and mini fridge. Toiletries and a hairdryer can be found in the bathroom.

The hotel’s spa includes a hot tub, a sauna and a hammam. Massage treatments can also be arranged. The more active guests can engage in water sports on the beach or take scuba diving lessons.

Guests can start their day with a rich buffet breakfast. Traditional delicacies and fresh seafood are served at the buffet-style restaurant. A poolside bar and a beach bar serve drinks and exotic cocktails.

The seaside town of Nikiti is around a 5 minute drive from the complex. Polygyros, the capital of Chalkidiki, is at 19 miles. Free on-site parking is available.
