Description

Ikos Oceania rooms are elegantly decorated in soft colors. They have a DVD and CD player, satellite TV and coffee and tea-making facilities. They all include furnished balconies or terraces.

Ikos Oceania Spa features 7 private treatment rooms, and includes an indoor heated pool with hot tub, enclosed by floor-to-ceiling windows offering incomparable views of the Aegean Sea. The fitness studio comes with state-of-the-art cardio-fitness facilities.

The all-inclusive Ikos Oceania Club offers a vast choice of dining options; guests can choose from a fantastic variety of buffet and menu options. Greek and international dishes are served, including Italian and Asian cuisine. Many of the restaurants and bars overlook the sea.

Nea Moudania is a 10-minute walk, and the hotel also runs a free daily shuttle service to the center of Nea Moudania. Thessaloniki International Airport is a 30-minute drive away.
