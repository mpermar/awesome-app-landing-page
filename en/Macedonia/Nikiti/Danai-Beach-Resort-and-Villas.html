Description

The luxurious rooms feature free Wi-Fi, fresh flowers and marble bathrooms with Bulgari amenities. Each includes a sitting area with a satellite TV and a DVD player. All rooms have a furnished balcony while some feature private pools.

The open-air Squirrel restaurant is ideal for romantic meals by the sea while the Andromeda prepares creative cuisine. The beachfront Seahorse Grill serves fish and Greek specialties. On the beach, guests can relax in the sofas of the Seaside Bar that offers cocktails.

The spa center features relaxing massages next to the swimming pool as well as beauty treatments. A floodlit tennis court and water sports facilities are also available.

Nikiti is within a 30-minute drive from the ancient site of Toroni and lively Neos Marmaras. Free private parking is provided at the Danai Beach Resort .
