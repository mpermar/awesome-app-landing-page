Description

The air-conditioned rooms are bright and airy, and come with a mini fridge and a TV. Other amenities include a hairdryer and a safe. Most of the rooms enjoy sea views.

Guests can start their day with a fresh cup of coffee and breakfast served at the hotel’s café restaurant. Later in the day the bar serves drinks on the beachfront.

The Grand Beach is within a short distance of water sports and shopping outlets. It is a 20-minute drive from the port of Prinos and a half-hour drive from Limenas. Free private parking is provided.

Experiences

08/28/2017

Family friendly, safe , convenient.

"It is only steps away from the sea. You get the view and the convenience of not having to walk far to the beach. There is a small dirt road between the property and the beach, so not much traffic. It feels very safe and very relaxed. The room was adequate size. It has a small refrigerator too. The balcony is big and comfortable. There were no mosquitoes at the time. Exellent for families with young children, or people who prefer queter locations. For the kids there are some air castles and other games. The restaurant has tables under a roof, but also right above the beach, under some trees. The place looks unassuming, but sitting under the trees feels both cooler and romantic. Highly recommend it."
