Description

The spacious and sunny rooms open to a balcony or patio with sea or mountain views. Each is modern with a sitting area with a luxurious armchair. Facilities include air conditioning and a 32-inch LCD satellite TV. Villas with a private pool and a fireplace are also available.

Greek and Mediterranean cuisine are served at the main restaurant Elia and the Gourmet Restaurant, which features a magnificent sea view. Drinks and refreshing cocktails are featured at the Café Bar.

Aphrodite Private Spa features 2 internal heated pools with hydro-massage jets, sauna and Relax Beds facilities. A gym and 2 private massage rooms and many body treatments are also available.

Litohoro Olympus Resort Villas & Spa is 2 miles from Litohoro and only just over 1 miles from Mount Olympus National Park. The city of Katerini is 7.5 miles away. Free private parking is available on site.
