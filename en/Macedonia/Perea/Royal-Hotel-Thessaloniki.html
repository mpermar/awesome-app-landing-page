Description

Hotel Royal's modern rooms feature comfortable furniture, handmade carpets and unique works of art. The spacious bathrooms have bathtubs, hairdryers and bathroom amenities.

Fine dishes, prepared by the hotel chef, are served at the restaurant with view of the pools and the Thermaikos Gulf. The hotel also offers full-menu, in-room dining.

The 4-star hotel Royal is on the national road Thessaloniki-Perea, allowing easy access to Perea beach. It is 6 miles from Thessaloniki Concert Hall and less than 9 miles from the Museum of Byzantine Culture. Mediterranean Cosmos shopping center is at 6 miles.

In addition the hotel offers a 24-hour multilingual front desk staff, dry cleaning, car rental and limo services.
