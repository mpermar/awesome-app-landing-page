Description

All guest rooms comes with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a electric tea pot, a shower, free toiletries and a desk. Rooms have a private bathroom, while some feature a city view. Guest rooms feature a closet.

A continental, buffet or halal breakfast is served at the property. There's an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of Italian, Asian and Middle eastern dishes and also offers Halal options.

At the hotel guests are welcome to use a sauna.

Guests can use the business center or rent a car to explore the area.

Yanbu Fish Market is 1.6 miles from Novotel Yanbu Albahr. The nearest airport is Prince Abdul Mohsin Bin Abdulaziz Airport, 5 miles from the property.
