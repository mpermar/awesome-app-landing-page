Description

The rooms at Hotel San Francisco Rural feature heating, a flat-screen TV, a wardrobe, a desk and a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries.

Guests can enjoy a shared lounge and library. The hotel also offers a bar and vending machines for drinks and snacks.

Hotel San Francisco offers different activities, such as horse riding, canoeing and fishing. The area is popular for hiking.

The Sella River is a few meters from the hotel. In addition, there are a variety of restaurants, shops and bars within a 15-minute walk of the property. Los Picos de Europa National Park is 50 km away. The hotel has free private parking.
