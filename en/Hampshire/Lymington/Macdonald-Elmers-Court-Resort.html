Description

All rooms include a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Some units include a sitting area to relax in after a busy day. There is a coffee machine and a kettle in the room. Every room has a private bathroom fitted with a bathtub and bathtub or shower. A TV with cable channels is provided.

You will find dry cleaning services at the property.

You can play tennis at this resort, and the area is popular for horseback riding. Bournemouth is 16 miles from Macdonald Elmers Court Resort, and Southampton is 11 miles away. The nearest airport is Bournemouth Airport, 14 miles from Macdonald Elmers Court Resort.
