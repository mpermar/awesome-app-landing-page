Description

Modern air-conditioned rooms are equipped with a private bathroom with a rainshower and a bathtub. A TV with satellite channels and a mini-bar are included.

Borobudur Hotel Jakarta features 8 tennis courts, a business center and a children’s playground. Guests can enjoy massage services or have beauty treatments at Sari Indah Beauty Salon.

The hotel’s 5 restaurants serve a selection of Italian, Japanese, Chinese, Indonesian and international cuisines. Drinks are available at Pendopo Bar and Churchill Wine & Cigar Bar.

Hotel Borobudur Jakarta is about 7.5 mi from Halim Perdanakusuma Airport. Soekarno Hatta International Airport is a 30-minute drive away.
