Description

The Kirkley Hotel features guestrooms with upgraded linens and body care products. After a busy day, guests can relax and watch the flat-screen TV or use the free high-speed wireless internet access.

Visitors at the Kirkley can go for a swim in the indoor pool or workout in the fitness center. After working in the business center, guests can take a tour of this historic city with help from the hotel's concierge services.

The on-site restaurant at The Hotel Kirkley serves a variety of American dishes for breakfast and dinner. After dinner, guests can relax with a drink in the lounge. After a restful night's sleep, guests can wake with free coffee and tea in the lobby.
