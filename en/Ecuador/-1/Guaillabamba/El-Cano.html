Description

At the hotel, each room comes with a patio. The private bathroom is equipped with a bath or shower. The rooms feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property.

At EL CANO guests are welcome to use a hot tub and a sauna. You can play ping pong at the accommodation.

Quito is 16 miles from El Cano, while Mindo is 29 miles away.
