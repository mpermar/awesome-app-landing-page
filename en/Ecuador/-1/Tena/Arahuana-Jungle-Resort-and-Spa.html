Description

All guest rooms at the resort are equipped with a seating area and a flat-screen TV. Rooms include a private bathroom and free toiletries, and some rooms will provide you with a terrace. All rooms have a closet.

Guests at Arahuana Jungle Resort & Spa can enjoy a continental breakfast.

The accommodation has a playground. The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental is available at Arahuana Jungle Resort & Spa.

Languages spoken at the reception include Spanish, German and English.
