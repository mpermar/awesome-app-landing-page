Description

Offering an outdoor pool and a la carte restaurant, Hotel Club del Sol is located in Atacames, right on the beach.

It boasts free daily breakfast, plus free on-site parking and WiFi. The rooms here are air-conditioned and feature a flat-screen TV, a minibar and a private bathroom.

At Hotel Club del Sol you will find a garden, a bar and a snack bar, and there is also a games room and children’s playground.

Whale watching is possible from July to September. The popular Tonsupa beach is 5 km from the property, while the city of Esmeraldas is 30 km away.
