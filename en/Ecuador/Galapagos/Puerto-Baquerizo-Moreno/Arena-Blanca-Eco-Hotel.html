Description

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool, a restaurant, and a hot tub, Arena Blanca Eco Hotel offers free Wi-Fi in San Cristóbal.

The shopping area, the beach, and the airport are 1 km away. The rooms in Arena Blanca Eco Hotel feature cable TVs, bathrooms with showers, air conditioning, and balconies.

Room service is provided. Guests can order regional and international dishes at the restaurant or relax in the game room.

Laundry services can be arranged upon request. Free Wi-Fi access is available in public areas.
