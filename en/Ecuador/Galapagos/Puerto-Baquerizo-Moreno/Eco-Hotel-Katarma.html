Description

Katarma is a beautifully decorated and environmentally oriented hotel located in San Cristobal that offers an indoor swimming pool and adjacent restaurant and bar, a tour desk and free WiFi access.

Each one of the 14 spacious bedrooms is decorated with recycled ceramic tiles and offers a minimalistic design.

They feature a private bathroom with a hot-water shower and free toiletries, a wardrobe and 110v connections.

You will also find black-out curtains in every room. Katarma is inspired entirely by art; it mixes different architectural, design, painting and sculptural influences to create a space where peace and life take the upmost importance.

Being in Galapagos, Katarma can assist you with outings to Leon Dormido rock where you can swim with sea turtles and sharks, Playa Ochoa beach filled with sea lions or Isla Lobos, amongst many other options.

Galapagos naval Museum can be reached after a 5-minute drive, while San Cristobal Airport is even closer located just 600 metres away.
