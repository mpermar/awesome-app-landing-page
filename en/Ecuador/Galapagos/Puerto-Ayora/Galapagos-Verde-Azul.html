Description

Located within Puerto Ayora, Galapagos Verde Azul offers accommodation with sea views, Charles Darwin Research is 400 meters away where guests can see iguanas and turtles.

Guests can also enjoy free WiFi and an American breakfast. Free fresh water is available.

The rooms in Galapagos Verde Azul feature private bathrooms with hot water, air conditioning, and cable TVs.

The rooms feature views of the sea and mountains. Guests can relax in the pool after day trips.

There are restaurants in the area. Day trips and excursions can be booked in the surrounding travel agencies.

b Los Alemanes Beach is 2,5 km from Galapagos Verde Azul while Tortuga Bay Beach is 15 minutes' drive or boat ride away.
