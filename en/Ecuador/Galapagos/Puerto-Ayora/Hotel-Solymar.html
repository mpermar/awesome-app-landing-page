Description

Hotel Solymar is a Mediterranean-style hotel located on Pelican Bay, in the heart of Puerto Ayora just minutes away from the Charles Darwin station.

It has a bar on site, plus an outdoor pool. Offering stunning ocean views and a private furnished balcony, the rooms boast blackout curtains, a private bathroom with shower, as well as free toiletries.

The TV features local channels, plus a minibar is included. Featuring climate control, a desk, and a wake-up call service are among the amenities offered.

These rooms are smoke-free facilities. Guests can enjoy the tranquility overlooking Pelican Bay and Galapagos flora and fauna at the hotel, yet be within walking distance to boutiques, outdoor cafés, art galleries, restaurants and nightlife.

You can see marine iguanas at Playa Tortuga beach, a short 5-minute drive away. The ocean side restaurant offers a sushi menu or guests could try the chef’s blending of Pacific Fresh seafood and local flavors.
