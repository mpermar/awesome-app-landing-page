Description

Hosteria Llanovientos is located in Baños and features free WiFi access, an on-site restaurant and bar, and a games room with table tennis.

The property offers free parking. Each room here will provide you with bed linen and a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries.

You can enjoy city view from the room and access the shared lounge and TV area. At Hosteria Llanovientos you will find a garden, a bar and a shared kitchen.

Other facilities offered at the property include meeting facilities and a shared lounge.

The inn offers a tour of the waterfalls and hot springs located 5 km away. Baños’ town centre is a 3-minute walk away, while Cotopaxi International Airport is a 1-hour drive away.
