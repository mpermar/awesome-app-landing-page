Description

Guest rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a bidet. Hotel Medina Del Lago provides some rooms with lake views, and rooms are equipped with a patio. All rooms feature a desk.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental or an American breakfast.

Hotel Medina Del Lago offers a sauna. The area is popular for cycling and hiking, and bike rental is available at the hotel.

Ibarra is 11 miles from Hotel Medina Del Lago. Mariscal Sucre Airport is 25 miles away.
