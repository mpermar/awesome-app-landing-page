Description

Offering free WiFi in all areas, Hotel El Indio Inn is located 5 km from San Pablo Lagoon.

Free parking is available. A spa with a hot tub is available for an extra fee. Each room here will provide you with a TV and cable channels.

Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with free toiletries. Extras include a tumble dryer and cleaning products.

At Hotel El Indio Inn you will find a 24-hour front desk. You will also find a bar and a shared lounge, along with a games room and a tour desk.

This property is 20 km from Cucocha Lagoon and 87 km from Mariscal Sucre International Airport.
