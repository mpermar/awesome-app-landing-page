Description

The rooms in Hosteria Totoral are equipped with a flat-screen TV. The accommodation has certain rooms that include a terrace and mountain view, and every room is equipped with a private bathroom and a desk. All guest rooms have a closet.

Guests at Hosteria Totoral can enjoy a continental or a buffet breakfast. At the resort you'll find a restaurant serving seafood and international cuisine.

Hosteria Totoral has a grill. Guests can take part in various activities in the surrounding areas, including horse riding, cycling and fishing.

For guests' convenience, the accommodation has a business center.

Otavalo is 15 miles from Hosteria Totoral. The nearest airport is Mariscal Sucre Airport, 39 miles from the resort.
