Description

The classical suites at La Mirage Garden Hotel & Spa are decorated with antique furniture and crystal chandeliers. All have spacious seating areas with fresh cut flowers, free Wi-Fi, and cozy fireplaces.

Guests at La Mirage can book a guided horseback ride to Cuicocha Crater and San Pablo Lake. They can enjoy a game of tennis, and relax afterwards with a volcanic mud treatment and massage.

The restaurant features a stained glass roof and serves gourmet specialties such as fresh quail and seared duck breast. A breakfast with buttered croissants, natural juices and scrambled eggs is available.

Surrounded by fragrant flower gardens and a picturesque water lily pond, La Mirage Hotel is a 2-hour drive from Quito. Free parking is provided.
