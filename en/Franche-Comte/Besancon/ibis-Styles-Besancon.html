Description

The air-conditioned rooms are spacious and contemporary in style. They include an LCD TV and free Wi-Fi access.

A buffet breakfast is provided every morning at this hotel. Guests can enjoy a drink at the bar or have dinner at the hotel's restaurant. A playground and meeting rooms are also available on site.

Providing free private parking, the property has a 24-hour front desk. The hotel is a 10-minute drive from the Parc des Expositions Micropolis and just 3.4 miles from Citadel of Besançonet, which is one of the UNESCO World Heritage Sites.
