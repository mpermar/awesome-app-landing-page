Description

Le Kinkéliba lodge has a restaurant, outdoor swimming pool, a bar and shared lounge in Mar Lodj.

Among the various facilities are a garden and a terrace. The accommodation offers evening entertainment and a 24-hour front desk.

All guest rooms at the hotel come with a seating area. At Le Kinkéliba Lodge all rooms are fitted with a desk and a private bathroom.

A continental breakfast is available every morning at the accommodation. Le Kinkéliba lodge offers a children's playground.

Guests at the hotel will be able to enjoy activities in and around Mar Lodj, like hiking and canoeing.

The nearest airport is Blaise Diagne International, 98 km from Le Kinkéliba Lodge, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
