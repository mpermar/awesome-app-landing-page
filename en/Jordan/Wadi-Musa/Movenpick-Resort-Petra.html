Description

The spacious rooms feature white linen and bright windows. Each has a work desk and is equipped with air conditioning and satellite TV.

The Al Saraya all-day-dining restaurant and Mediterranean Al Iwan restaurant offer a large selection of dishes. The Al Maqa'ad Bar features carved wooden screens, while the Al Ghadeer roof Garden boasts views over Petra's hills. Guest can also enjoy desserts in The Al Baraka Tea Room or get a takeaway from the Caravan Stop Shop.

Guests can relax in the outdoor swimming pool or enjoy a cocktail in the hotel’s stately lobby. There is also fitness centre and game room.

The centre of Wadi Musa nearby and offers many restaurants and shops. Aqaba King Hussein International Airport is a one hours’ drive.
