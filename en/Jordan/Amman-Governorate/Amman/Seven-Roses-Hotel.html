Description

Featuring free WiFi and a terrace, Seven Roses Hotel is a luxurious 4-star hotel in the center of Amman’s business district and it is only 5 minutes’ walk from Cozmo Shop Mall.

It features 3 restaurants. Each room at this hotel is air conditioned and is equipped with a flat-screen TV.

Some units have a seating area to relax in after a busy day. You will find a kettle in the room.

All rooms have a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find bath robes and slippers.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. The hotel also offers car hire.

US Embassy is 2. 5 km from Seven Roses Hotel, while Al Hussein National Park is 4.

2 km from the property. The property 15 minutes drive from Queen Alia international airport , 20-minute drive from Marka International Airport and the Dead Sea is 45-minutes away by car.
