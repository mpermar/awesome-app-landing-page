Description

The soundproof rooms and suites at Petra Moon come with satellite channels, a DVD player and extra-long beds. Each has air conditioning and a private bathroom.

Guests can enjoy a buffet breakfast in the restaurant. Local and international meals are available for lunch and dinner. There is also a grocery store and a vending machine with drinks.

Car rentals can be arranged by the staff at Petra Moon. Horseback riding and hiking trails are available to all guests.

The hotel is only 300 feet from Jett bus station. Aqaba Airport is 100 miles away. A shuttle to Aqaba, Amman, and Aqaba Airport can be arranged by Petra Moon's staff.
