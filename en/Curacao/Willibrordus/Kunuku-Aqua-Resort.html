Description

Spacious apartments have a living room with a sofa bed. There are 2 bathrooms with a shower. The well-equipped kitchen includes a fridge, microwave and coffee maker.

The complex has a bar and a restaurant. There is also a small shop. You will find restaurants, shops and a diving school 6 miles from the resort.

Car rental is available and you can request an airport shuttle service for an additional charge. Children’s entertainment and babysitting services are available in high season.
