Description

Each unit has with a terrace offering garden views, a cableFlat-screen TV, a well-fitted kitchen and a private bathroom with shower. A toaster, a fridge and stovetop are also provided, as well as a coffee machine and a electric tea pot.

The property has a hot tub.

If you would like to discover the area, snorkeling is possible nearby and a car rental service can be arranged.

Curacao Sea Aquarium is 3.5 miles from the apartment, while Jan Thiel Beach is 5 miles from the property.
