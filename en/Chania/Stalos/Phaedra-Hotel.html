Description

The complex consists of 22 apartments, bungalows and studios and a beautiful, chemical free, pool with children’s section. All accommodations offer fully catered kitchen with fridge, TV and private bathroom with hair-dryer.

Guests can relax on the complimentary sun beds at the swimming pool or at the private beach area, while gazing at the beautiful view of the Cretan Sea. Free beach towels are offered. Should they wish to explore the area, the hotel is situated only metres from the bus stop.

The nearby villages of Agia Marina and Platania offer a wide variety of shops, rentals, supermarkets as well as traditional taverns and beach-bars.
