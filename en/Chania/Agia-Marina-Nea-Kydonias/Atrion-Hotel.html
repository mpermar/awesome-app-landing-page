Description

Rooms have a balconies with either mountain, garden or pool views. They also have air conditioning, satellite TV, a safety deposit box and fridge. The private bathroom is stocked with toiletries and a hairdryer.

Start the day with a buffet breakfast. The on-site restaurant serves Cretan and Mediterranean cuisine for lunch and dinner. Children’s menus are available. After a dip in the pool, enjoy a drink at Atrion’s pool bar.

Staff is available 24 hours a day at the front desk, and can provide information regarding local attractions and excursions around Crete. A short walk leads to traditional tavernas (or restaurants), mini markets and shops. Within 100 yards, you can also find many clubs and beach bars.

Chania Airport is 14 miles away. Transfers to and from the airport can be arranged upon request for an additional cost. A regular bus service runs close to Atrion Hotel, connecting Agia Marina to nearby coastal towns and Chania is just 5 miles away. Atrion Hotel also provides complimentary private parking.
