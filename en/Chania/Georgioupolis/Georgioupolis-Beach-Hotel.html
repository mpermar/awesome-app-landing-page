Description

Rooms at the Georgioupolis Beach Hotel have a satellite TV, safe and mini fridge while some units also have a kitchenette. A private bathroom with hairdryer is standard.

Guests can relax at the pool bar that serves cocktails and snacks until late in the evening. The hotel restaurant serves traditional Cretan specialties using local products.

The center of Georgioupolis with shops, restaurants and bars is 350 feet away. Chania with the Venetian Port is at 24 mi and Rethymno with the scenic Old Town and the Fortezza is at 16 mi. Free private parking is available at the hotel Georgioupolis Beach.
