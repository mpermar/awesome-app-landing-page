Description

Relax in the well-furnished accommodations of your choice and enjoy the beautiful natural surroundings. Enjoy a great start to the day with the extensive complimentary breakfast buffet, before choosing from a wide variety of recreational activities.

Choose from 4 inviting outdoor pools and one heated indoor pool. Enjoy a relaxing walk along the long, private beach. Make time for yourself in the sanctuary of Pilot Beach Resort’s professional spa. Join in with one of the fitness classes on the resort, including yoga, Pilates and aqua gymnastics. Get creative at the art workshop.

In the evening, Pilot Beach Resort offers a variety of dining options. Children can enjoy their own menu under the supervision of the energetic entertainment team, while you savor traditional Greek and Cretan delicacies at the seaside restaurant. Afterwards, enjoy a drink at the cocktail bar or enjoy dance music at the lively Ermis Bar.
