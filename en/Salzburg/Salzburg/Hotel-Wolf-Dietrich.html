Description

It is just a short walk from Mirabell Palace and Mozart’s Residence. You can leave your car in the garage as all of the city’s sights are within walking distance. For example the cathedral or the fortress, Mozart’s birthplace and the new conference hall.

The hotel’s staff at the front desk is happy to assist you in reserving concert or theater tickets. The internet terminal in the foyer is free of charge for hotel guests as are use of DVDs. You can also relax in the tastefully decorated sitting room.

The spa, with its warm colors and simple design, is ideal for relaxation. This compact recreation area comes complete with swimming pool, Finnish sauna, infrared cubicle and a pretty 'silence' room with couches for relaxation after the sauna. Massages are available.

Altstadt is a great choice for travelers interested in culture, history and atmosphere.
