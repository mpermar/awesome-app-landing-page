Description

Guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. With a private bathroom, certain accommodations at Genusshotel Fichtenhof also feature a balcony. All guest rooms include a desk.

A continental breakfast is served at the property.

At the accommodation guests are welcome to use a Turkish bath. You can play ping pong at Genusshotel Fichtenhof.

Hirschkogel is 2.4 miles from the hotel, while Panoramabahn Grossarltal I is 2.7 miles away. W. A. Mozart Airport is 37 miles from the property.
