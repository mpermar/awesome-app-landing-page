Policies

Children and Extra Beds
All children are welcome.
Up to two children under 2 years are charged EUR 8 per night in a crib.
Up to two children under 4 years are charged 15 % of the room stay per night for extra beds.
Up to two children from 5 to 9 year are charged 30 % of the room stay per night for extra beds.
Up to two children from 10 to 17 year are charged 50 % of the room stay per night per person for extra beds.
Up to two older children or adults are charged 80 % of the room stay per night per person for extra beds.
The maximum number of extra beds/cribs in a room is 2.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
