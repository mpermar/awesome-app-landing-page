Description

The spacious rooms at The Castlecourt Hotel, Spa & Leisure all feature TVs and free Wi-Fi. Some rooms have 4-poster beds and some have spa bathtubs.

With an outdoor rock pool hot tub, the luxurious Spa Sula offers a range of massages, body wraps and beauty treatments. There is also a sauna, steam room and a fully equipped gym.

The Orchard Restaurant offers a bright and stylish space, and the Courtyard Bistro provides light lunches and pastries throughout the day.The Westport and Mulranny golf clubs are both within a 5-minute walk of the Castlecourt. Westport Train Station is a 10-minute walk from the hotel.
