Description

The elegantly designed rooms feature flat-screen TVs and luxurious bathrooms with designer toiletries. The hotel was once a vacation retreat for John Lennon.

The Mulranny Health and Beauty Rooms offer an extensive range of treatments, and guests receive free use of the Health Club, which features an outdoor hot tub, sauna, gym and steam room.

The Nephin Restaurant serves traditional homemade dishes, while the Waterfront Bar & Bistro features an open fire and summer porch. Mulranny Park Hotel also has a guest lounge and a 24-hour front desk.

The Blue Flag Mulranny Beach is a 5-minute walk away, overlooking Croagh Patrick and Clare Island. Achill Island is within a 15-minute drive and there is plenty of private free parking available at the hotel.
