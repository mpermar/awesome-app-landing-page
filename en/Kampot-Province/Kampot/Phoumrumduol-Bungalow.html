Description

Guest rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels, fridge, a electric tea pot, a shower and a closet. The rooms are equipped with a private bathroom with free toiletries.

The daily breakfast offers continental, à la carte or American options.

The hotel has a grill.

Kampot Provincial Museum is 1.6 miles from Phoumrumduol Bungalow, while Teuk Chhou Rapids is 5 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Sihanoukville International Airport, 37 miles from the property.
