Description

Each room here includes air conditioning and a minibar. There is also an electric kettle. Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also have a bathtub and a hairdryer.

At Taj Palace New Delhi there is a fitness center. Other facilities available at the property include meeting facilities, a ticket service and a tour desk. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the area, including golf and mini golf. The property provides free parking.

The hotel is 2.2 miles from Rashtrapati Bhavan, 4 miles from Jantar Mantar and 4 miles from India Gate. Delhi International Airport is a 15-minute drive away.

Dining options include Blue Ginger, a contemporary Vietnamese restaurant and Masala Art, a show kitchen. Overlooking the lush garden, Kafe Fontana is a 24-hour coffee shop. The Blue Bar serves a selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages. Orient Express serves a selection of European delights.

Chanakyapuri is a great choice for travelers interested in architecture, local food and food.
