Description

Hotel Imperial is a 10-minute drive from New Delhi Train Station. Nizamuddin Train Station is 5 miles from the property. The Indira Gandhi International Airport is a 25-minute drive from the hotel. The historic Chandni Chowk is a 10-minute drive away as well. India Gate, Presidential Palace, Jantar Mantar are a few of the sight seeing options which are less than 5 miles from the hotel.

The modern and spacious rooms have high ceilings, a flat-screen cable TV, a minibar and tea and coffee-making facilities. Bathrobes, toiletries and a hairdryer are also provided.

This hotel features concierge assistance for tour and theater information, as well as mobile phone rentals. Babysitting services and free overnight shoe shine services are provided. Yoga classes and a book shop are available.
