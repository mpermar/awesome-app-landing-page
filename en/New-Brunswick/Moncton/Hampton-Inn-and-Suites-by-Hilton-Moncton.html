Description

Hampton Inn and Suites by Hilton Moncton rooms include a 32-inch flat-screen TV. The rooms are equipped with a coffee maker and work area.

The Hampton Inn Moncton provides guests with a daily breakfast.

The Hampton Inn and Suites is 12 miles from the Greater Moncton International Airport. The hotel is 5 miles from Crystal Palace, an indoor amusement park.
