Description

A microwave, fridge and coffee maker are provided in the contemporary rooms at Holiday Inn Express Hotel & Suites Saint John Harbour Side. There is also free Wi-Fi and a desk.

Guests can enjoy a daily breakfast buffet featuring cheddar omelets, sausage and cinnamon rolls. CopperHead Pub, which adjoins Saint John Holiday Inn, serves lunch and dinner.

The New Brunswick Museum, Interaction Theatre Company and Canada Games Aquatic Centre are within a 16-minute walk of the hotel.
