Description

Featuring a fantastic outdoor pool, a sauna and a fitness centre, Toborochi Suites offers accommodations in the tropical city of Santa Cruz de la Sierra.

Free WiFi access and a complimentary breakfast are included. The elegantly decorated suites are all fitted with a seating area, a private bathroom with free toiletries, a dining area, a safety deposit box and a minibar At the hotel guests will find a 24-hour front desk, a large garden and terrace, and services such as luggage storage or room service.

Toborochi Suites enjoys a privileged location, only 2. 5 km from the Main Square of Santa Cruz, Plaza 24 de Septiembre.

The city has two airports, El Trompillo which is 8 km away and Viru Viru International Airport, which is the main airport and is 13 km away, shuttle service can be arranged for an extra fee.
