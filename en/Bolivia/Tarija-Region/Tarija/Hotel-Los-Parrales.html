Description

Featuring a garden, an outdoor swimming pool, a restaurant, and a bar, Hotel Los Parrales offers spacious rooms with panoramic views of Guadalquivir river.

Free Wi-Fi and breakfast are provided. The rooms in Hotel Los Parrales feature private bathrooms and balconies with river and garden views.

Buffet breakfast is served daily and includes juices, regional fruits and hams, cheese, and homemade pastries.

Guests can order national and international dishes in La Viña restaurant, and enjoy drinks and cocktails in La Falca bar.

Hotel Los Parrales has a sauna (dry and steam), a hydromassage tub, and a fitness centre.

There is also a children’s playground and a business centre. Massage sessions and car rentals can be arranged.

The commercial area, Luis de Fuentes main square, the Paleontology museum, and Casa Dorada (a cultural castle-like building) are 3,5 km away.

Capitán Oriel Lea Plaza Airport is 7 km from Los Parrales. Free private parking is possible on site.
