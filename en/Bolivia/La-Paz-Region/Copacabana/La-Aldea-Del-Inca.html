Description

Only 300 metres from Titicaca Lake, La Aldea Del Inca offers a picturesque location, free private parking and Incan-inspired architecture.

A Wi-Fi zone is available here. In the garden, guests can relax in a hammock. Heating machines are available upon request and for an extra fee.

Guests can enjoy a full american breakfast. La Aldea Del Inca has a tour desk which can offer information about the local sights.
