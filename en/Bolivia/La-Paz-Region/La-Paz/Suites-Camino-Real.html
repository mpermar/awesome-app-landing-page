Description

Located in the district of Calacoto, Suites Camino Real offers accommodations in La Paz.

Free WiFi access is available throughout the hotel. Each morning the hotel serves a breakfast buffet, including freshly baked Bolivian pastries.

All suites come with a private bathroom and a seating area. A minibar and a coffee machine are also standard amenities.

Guests can visit the renovated indoor pool and whirlpool from Tuesday to Sunday. Gym and spa facilities are available offering relaxing massages.

Chairs and loungers are offered around the Felling's Spa. Guests can work out in the gym and enjoy a relaxing massage afterwards.

Alternatively, chairs and loungers offer relaxation possibilities around the Felling's SPA.

For lunch and dinner the La Tranquera Restaurant has a variety of local and international cuisine on offer.

Barbecued meat is the house speciality. The hotel has a great location right on the Ballivián Avenue.

The commercial district of San Miguel is a 10-minute drive away. Irpavi Teleferico Station is a 5-minute walk away.
