Description

The 5-star Hotel Regina Resort offers uniquely decorated suites with free Wi-Fi 15-minutes drive from Cochabamba City.

Facilities include 7 swimming pools, 2 restaurants and a tennis and football court.

The floodlit rooms at Hotel Regina Resort & Convenciones feature a stylish blend of classical and contemporary décor.

All have LCD and cable TV and queen-size beds. Some have air conditioning. Guests at the Regina Hotel can make us of the well-equipped gym, sauna and hydromassage tub.

They can also book an aromatherapy massage at the spa or rent a bike at the tour desk and explore the region.

Dining options include international cuisine or traditional barbecued meats, while the bar serves a variety of exotic cocktails.

An American breakfast buffet with fruits and pastries is served daily. The Regina Resort Hotel is 16 km from Wilsterman Airport.
