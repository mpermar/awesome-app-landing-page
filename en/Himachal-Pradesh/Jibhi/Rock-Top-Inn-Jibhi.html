Description

Guest rooms in the inn are equipped with a electric tea pot. Featuring a private bathroom, certain rooms at Rock Top Inn Jibhi also offer a terrace. All rooms at the accommodation feature air conditioning and a desk.

A continental, à la carte or Full English/Irish breakfast is available every morning at the property.

The property has a hot spring bath. Ski equipment rental and bike rental are available at this inn and the area is popular for skiing.

Speaking English and German at the 24-hour front desk, staff are always at hand to help.

The nearest airport is Kullu–Manali Airport, 23 miles from Rock Top Inn Jibhi.
