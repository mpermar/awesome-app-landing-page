Description

Grand Hi Lai Hotel is a 10-minute walk from Love River and MRT Central Park Station, while Kaohsiung Railway Station is a 10-minute drive away. It takes 20 minutes to drive to Taiwan High Speed Rail - Zuoying Station and Kaohsiung International Airport from the property.

Featuring air conditioning and a wardrobe, each guest room is fitted with a flat-screen TV, an electric kettle and a refrigerator. Some rooms enjoy beautiful city or sea views. The private bathroom has shower facilities, bathtub, free toiletries and hairdryer.

The fitness center offers professional trainer to meet guests' need; the spa center brings everyone a place to unwind. The meeting room and audio-visual center come with state-of-the-art meeting equipment. For guests' convenience, the 24-hour front provides free luggage storage and touring information.

Here at Grand Hi Lai Hotel, the restaurants and bars serve world-class delicacies and sensational dining experiences. Just 1.2 mi away, guests can also find local specialties in Liuhe Tourist Night Market.

Qianjin District is a great choice for travelers interested in seafood, food and friendly locals.
