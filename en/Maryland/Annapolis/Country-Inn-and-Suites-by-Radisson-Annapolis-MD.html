Description

A flat-screen cable TV, microwave, and refrigerator are featured in every guest room at the Country Inn and Suites. They are decorated in bright colors and have a coffee machine. Select suites have a sofa bed.

Guests can use the on-site fitness center and the laundry facilities at the Annapolis Country Inn. A 24-hour front desk is provided for convenience.

Several restaurants, including Sakura Japanese Steak House and Ledo Pizza, are within 1 miless of the hotel.

The Annapolis Westfield Mall is 1 mile away. The Maryland State House is 4 miles from the hotel.
