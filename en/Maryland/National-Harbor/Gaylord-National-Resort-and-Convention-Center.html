Description

The Gaylord National offers many on-site dining options like the Old Hickory Steakhouse Restaurant and the Pienza Restaurant. The hotel also has an indoor pool with a poolside bar.

All of the rooms at Gaylord National Resort & Convention Center include a cable TV and a refrigerator. Select rooms offer a view of the atrium.

The hotel has an on-site convention center and guests are steps from The Awakening sculpture by the Potomac River.

Gaylord National Resort & Convention Center provides transportation to the MGM and Tanger on the Harbor Circulator Bus as part of the resort fee.
