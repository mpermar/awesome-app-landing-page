Description

Guests staying at the Comfort Suites Ocean City can enjoy in-room features like a cable TV and a refrigerator. The rooms also include a sitting area.

Along with the swimming pool, the Ocean City Comfort Suites also includes a game room and a fitness center. The on-site business center is available.

The Jolly Roger Amusement Park and the Roland E. Powell Convention Center are within a 10-minute drive of the hotel.

West Ocean City is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, seafood and food.
