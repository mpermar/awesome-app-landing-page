Description

All guest bedrooms at Courtyard Ocean City Oceanfront come with an LCD satellite TV and an iPod docking station. Every room also features a microwave, a mini-refrigerator and hot drink facilities.

Courtyard’s restaurant, The Captain’s Table, is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner, and is one of Ocean City’s most popular dining destinations. There are also several excellent seafood restaurants within a few minutes’ walk of the hotel.

Guests can play a round of golf at the challenging Glen Riddle Golf Club 6 miles away. The Ronald E. Powell Convention Center is 1.5 miles from the hotel.
