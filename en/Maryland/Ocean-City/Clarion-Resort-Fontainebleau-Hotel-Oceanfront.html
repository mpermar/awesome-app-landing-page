Description

Ocean City features a beach experience with added bonuses like miniature golf courses, amusement parks, water attractions and an antique carousel. Many challenging golf courses are in the surrounding area.

During your visit, enjoy a nice meal and a refreshing beverage at the on-site Breakers Pub. For more full-service dining, the Horizons restaurant is also on the premises and serves breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Guests at this hotel can enjoy amenities and features like free wireless high-speed Internet access, free newspaper, health club with sauna and hot tub, indoor heated pool and poolside bar.

Business travelers will appreciate the on-site business center. Featuring 40,000-square-feet of meeting and banquet space, this is the place to host any special event or business function.

All guest rooms are warmly decorated and feature microwaves, refrigerators, voice mail, ironing facilities, safes, hairdryers and cable TV.

The hotel has room service, a gift shop and a Sunday brunch for added guest enjoyment. Golf enthusiasts will want to take advantage of the hotel's golf packages; please contact the hotel for more details.

This Ocean City, MD hotel is minutes from Assateague Island National Seashore, Ocean City Factory Outlets mall, the Ocean City Boardwalk, Ocean City Beach, Jolly Roger Amusement Park and the Tanger Outlet Center mall in Rehoboth Beach.

The Ocean City Municipal Airport is only 9 miles away. Ocean City is a place to indulge in delicious, fresh seafood from local restaurants, several of which are located close to the hotel.

North Ocean City is a great choice for travelers interested in seafood, restaurants and food.
