Description

The Turf Valley Resort is conveniently placed just off Interstate 70. This location makes popular area sites, including Baltimore's scenic harbor as well as Washington, D.C. within driving distance.

Guests at Turf Valley can enjoy relaxing in indoor and outdoor on-site pools, working out with modern fitness facilities or browsing the internet with free wireless access. The hotel also features on-site tennis courts.

The Turf Valley Resort offers gourmet on-site dining options, including classic regional dishes and an extensive wine list at Alexandra's Restaurant. The Fairway Lounge, also on-site, offers a variety of beverages and snacks along with live weekend entertainment.
