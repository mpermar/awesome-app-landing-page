Description

Beach Haven B&B is a family-run guesthouse, offering good value accommodation and free Wi-Fi.

The idyllic beach and the town centre are within walking distance. All of the comfortable rooms at Beach Haven House have a TV and en suite facilities.

Some rooms have beautiful harbour views. Relax in the cosy guest lounge and there is an inviting dining room.

An excellent breakfast is available. Waterford City is close by. The owners are friendly and welcoming.
