Description

Set within 240 acres of stunnig parklands, Citywest offers an impressive 764 bedrooms, health and leisure club, an 18-hole golf course, and a selection of on-site bars and restaurants. Free parking and WiFi are also available.

Each of the spacious and modern rooms has an private bathroom. Rooms also have a TV, tea and coffee facilities and a work desk. Some rooms have wonderful views, and a wake-up service is available.

Citywest Health and Leisure Club features a large gym, swimming pool, with a hot tub, sauna and steam room. Guests can also make use of the Haven Hair & Beauty.

The Woodlock restaurant is designed with three sections, each with a subtly different look, feel and ambiance ensuring the perfect table for you. The award-winning chef explores rare culinary techniques that reflects in the menu and your dining experience. The Pavilion Lounge offers light snacks and hot drinks, and the Swift bar serves a range of cocktails and rare Irish whiskeys.

The 4-star Citywest Hotel and Resort is on the outskirts of Dublin and has easy access to the M50 motorway. The Guinness Storehouse is 20 minutes’ drive away, and the Baldonnell Business Park can be reached in 10 minutes by car.
