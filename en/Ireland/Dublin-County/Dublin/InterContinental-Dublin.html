Description

Sumptuous decor, marble bathrooms, and beautiful landscaped gardens are offered at this luxurious, 5-star hotel.

The InterContinental Dublin (formerly the Four Seasons Hotel) is located in Ballsbridge, one of Dublin's most affluent areas, and boasts huge rooms, and a fantastic spa with an 14-metre swimming pool.

Elegantly furnished and spacious, each room has beautiful views of the hotel’s landscaped courtyard and the city.

Guests can relax in the luxury bathrooms with a glass-walled shower and extra deep bathtub.

Rooms also include a satellite TV/DVD player and cosy bathrobes. The InterContinental Dublin offers 5 treatment rooms, a relaxation area, and a range of treatments including massages and skincare.

Guests can relax in the sauna, steam room, or heated whirlpool. The state-of-the art gym features elegant surroundings and iPods for use by guests.

The restaurant serves international cuisine made from the finest Irish ingredients, while the Lobby Lounge overlooks the garden and provides morning coffee, pastries, and afternoon tea.

Herbert Park is just 5 minutes’ walk from the hotel, and Dublin’s centre can be reached in 10 minutes by car.

Lively Temple Bar is 15 minutes’ drive away, and the Aviva Stadium is a 15-minute walk.
