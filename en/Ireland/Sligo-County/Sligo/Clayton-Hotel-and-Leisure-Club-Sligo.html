Description

With a swimming pool, gym, and Essence Spa, the modern Clayton Sligo is 5 minutes’ drive from Sligo’s centre.

Guests can enjoy Egyptian cotton sheets, free Wi-Fi, and a relaxing spa bath. Featuring private bathrooms with fluffy towels and free toiletries, each modern room includes a TV.

Guests can make use of tea/coffee facilities and an in-room safe. Sanvitae Health and Fitness Club boasts a range of fitness classes and a modern gym with flat-screen TVs and luxurious changing areas.

There is a large sauna, a steam room, and an additional children’s pool. Guests can dine in Sinergie Restaurant, with its classical decor and modern, European cuisine.

The stylish Kudos bar provides international bottled beers, flavoured vodka, and Asian dishes.

Clayton Hotel Sligo is overlooked by Benbulben Mountain and is close to the Atlantic coast.
