Description

Within 200 acres of beautiful countryside, this luxurious hotel and spa features an 18-hole championship golf course and a 50 feet swimming pool.

Elegant decor and a private bathroom with complimentary toiletries are provided in each room at Mount Wolseley. Guests can relax with free internet access, free tea and coffee, and a TV in the room.

The award-winning leisure center at Mount Wolseley Hotel Spa & Gold Resort offers a state-of-the-art gym featuring machines with integrated TVs. Guests can relax in the hot tub, sauna, or steam room. The Wolseley Spa offers a range of exotic and luxurious treatments.

Frederick's Restaurant serves creative cuisine and an Irish breakfast buffet among large windows and an outdoor terrace. The Aaron Lounge provides coffees, cocktails, and light lunches.

Guest's can enjoy a game of golf at the 18 hole championship golf course or enjoy afternoon tea in the adult only library. Tullow’s center can be reached in 10 minutes on foot, and guests can enjoy horse-riding in nearby Rathvilly.
