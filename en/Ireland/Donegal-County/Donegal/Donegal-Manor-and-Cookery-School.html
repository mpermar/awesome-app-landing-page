Description

Situated within 20 minutes' walk of the town centre of Donegal, the 4-star boutique Donegal Manor & Cookery School offers baking classes.

The award-winning and family-run property is ideal for exploring the Wild Atlantic Way.

Surrounded by green landscapes and with its own tree-lined entrance, Dongegal Manor also features a cosy lounge with a fireplace.

Offering views of the Donegal countryside, individual rooms at Donegal Manor feature free WiFi, flat-screen TVs and fridges.

Each has a seating area with tea and coffee-making facilities. Buttermilk pancakes, handmade preserves, marmalades, breads, and cakes, and Bailey's porridge are some of the specialities guests can enjoy for breakfast.

A hearty, full Irish breakfast is also available. Free parking is available, and nearby beaches can be reached in 10 minutes by car, while local golf clubs are a 20-minute drive away.

Slieve League Mountain are 40 minutes' drive from the manor. The manor was awarded Guesthouse of the Year 2008 and Hosts of the Year 2008 by Emerald Awards, USA.
