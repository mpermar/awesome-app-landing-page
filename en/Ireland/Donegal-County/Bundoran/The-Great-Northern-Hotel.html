Description

The Great Northern Hotel is on a historic and challenging 18-hole championship golf course. The on-site leisure club has a modern gym, indoor pool, sauna, steam room and hot tub. Wi-Fi is available in all public areas.

All rooms have a TV, telephone and private bathroom. Many rooms also feature views of the coast or golf course.

Guests can dine with views of the golf course in Tir Connaill Restaurant. The relaxed Bistro Bar and Atlantic Bar are both open throughout the day and serve varied menus.

The surrounding parkland and coastline are perfect for fishing, hiking, and surfing. The beach is less than 950 feet away and the center of Bundoran is a 10-minute walk from the hotel.
