Description

An Chúirt Hotel is overlooking the Clady River with views of the Errigal Mountain Peaks and lakes, this 4-star Country House Hotel is also home to the Serenity Spa.

The elegant rooms at An Chúirt, Gweedore Court Hotel have attractive décor. They include a TV, tea and coffee making facilities and a bathroom.

Free high speed Wi Fi is available. The Duck Restaurant offers a wide variety of dishes including locally caught seafood.

The PJD Bar offers a variety of fine wines, champagne, local craft beer, single malts and cocktails.

Situated with the hotel grounds, there is the Serenity Hair & Beauty Spa offering a wide range of Elemis Treatments.

The Errigal Health Club has a full-size swimming pool, a hydrotherapy pool, a steam room, sauna, Jacuzzi®, gym.

The Glenveagh National Park is located 13 km from the hotel and the area is popular for horse riding, walking and sightseeing.
