Description

Radisson Blu Hotel Letterkenny is designed in a contemporary Irish style, with the comfort of both the business traveler and tourist in mind. The hotel is within walking distance of 4 shopping centers, the theater, cinema, all the culture and nightlife.

For your relaxation, after a long day of meetings or sightseeing, the hotel offers a gym with a 50 feet lap pool, relaxing steam room and sauna and a fully-equipped gym.
