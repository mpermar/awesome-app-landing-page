Description

Featuring a leisure centre and a kids club during school holidays, Springhill Court is just 2 km from the centre of Kilkenny.

Guests can enjoy free parking, free internet access, spacious rooms, and entertainment twice a week.

There is also a children's playground. Rooms at Springhill Court Hotel & Leisure Club Kilkenny all feature a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Free tea/coffee facilities are provided in all rooms along with a TV. A 19-metre swimming pool, a jacuzzi, and a steam room and sauna are available at the modern leisure centre.

With a family-friendly changing room, it also includes a gym and fitness classes.

Oscar's Bistro serves a breakfast buffet and locally sourced, traditional Irish dishes in elegant surroundings.

Special dietary needs are also catered for. The traditional Paddock Bar provides a range of drinks, bar food, and a carvery lunch.

Kilkenny’s medieval centre can be reached in less than 5minutes by car. Nowlan Park, home to traditional Irish sports, is a 5-minute drive away.
