Description

Overlooking the ocean and offering a seasonal outdoor swimming pool, Derrynane Hotel & Holiday Homes offers accommodation in Caherdaniel, 3.

6 km from Derrynane House. Among the various facilities of this property are a tennis court, a fitness centre and a garden.

Staigue Stone Fort is 7 km away. At the hotel, the rooms include a desk and a flat-screen TV.

Each room is equipped with a private bathroom equipped with a bath and free toiletries, while selected rooms here will provide you with a kitchen.

All units feature a wardrobe. A continental or buffet breakfast can be enjoyed at the property.

Derrynane Hotel & Holiday Homes offers 3-star accommodation with a sauna. A business centre and vending machines with drinks and snacks are available on site at the accommodation.

Speaking English and Irish at the reception, staff are ready to help around the clock.

Killarney is 35 km away from the property. The nearest airport is Kerry Airport, 60 km from the property.
