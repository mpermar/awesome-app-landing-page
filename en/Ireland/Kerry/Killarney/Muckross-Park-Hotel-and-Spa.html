Description

The Spa is available to all residents in the hotel. The award-winning spa includes The Vitality Pool and Thermal Area, which guests can access for a fee. A range of treatments are available for a fee. The Spa recently won a World Luxury Award as Best International Spa 2011. The 50 feet Vitality Pool features hot tub jets and thermal currents, and is only available to guests over 18.

Deluxe, individually designed rooms have plush furnishings, custom furniture and luxurious fabrics. These spacious rooms feature a king bed, 24-hour room service, heated towel rack and soft towels, a drinks cooler, and a large private bathroom with bathtub and walk-in shower.

The Jarvey's Rest Traditional Irish Pub has beamed ceilings, open fires and regular live Irish music. Cocktails are served in the chic Monks lounge with its deep leather couches and wood paneled walls.

Killarney town is just 5-minute away, and Lough Leane is a 10-minute walk away. The Atlantic coast can be reached in 30 minutes by car.
