Description

The Gleneagle River Apartments offer self-catering luxury in Killarney. Designed to a 4-star standard, each spacious self-catering apartment has 2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms and a family room. Each comfortably sleeps 4 to 5 people.

While visiting Killarney, residents of the self-catering River Apartments can enjoy exclusive use of all of Gleneagle's extensive facilities.
