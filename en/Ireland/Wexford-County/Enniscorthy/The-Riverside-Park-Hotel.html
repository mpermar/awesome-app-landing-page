Description

The Riverside Park Hotel and Leisure Club in Enniscorthy town centre has luxurious rooms, a gym and a pool.

There are 2 restaurants and the bar offers live music and entertainment. The property contains 100 rooms equipped with hypnosis beds, TVs, ironing facilities and tea/coffee making facilities, free WiFi and 24-hour room service.

Guests can upgrade to suites and deluxe rooms, or a balcony room overlooking the River Slaney.

Family rooms are also available. The hotel boasts 2 restaurants; The Moorings, offering classic cuisine or The Alamo Steakhouse offering a wide variety of dishes.

The Prom Bar also serves food and has charming views of the River Slaney and features live music and entertainment.

The Riverside Leisure Club is fully-equipped. There is a childrenâ€™s splash pool in addition to the main pool, as well as a sauna, steam room and whirlpool.

The hotel is family friendly with a Kids Club running throughout the summer, Easter and Halloween school holidays.

A children's playground is also located adjacent to the hotel and there are several activities available nearby the hotel such as fishing, water sports, quad biking and equestrian activities to name a few.

Enniscorthy Castle and the National 1798 Visitors Centre, an interactive museum, are both 380 yards from Riverside Park.

The hotel is 600 yards from Enniscorthy Train Station and a 15-minute drive from Wexford Town.
