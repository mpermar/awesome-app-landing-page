Description

One of our top picks in Gorey.Located just a 10-minutes walk from the town of Gorey, the family-run Amber Springs Hotel offers a bar, restaurant, leisure center and spa. With free parking, it is just a 10-minute drive to miles of sandy beaches.

The spacious and contemporary rooms offer a TV and tea and coffee making facilities. Each room benefits from an en suite bath or shower room with a hairdryer.

Award-winning cuisine is available from BBG's and Kelby’s Bistro. Dishes are prepared using organic and locally sourced produce where possible.

The Amber Springs Hotel offers numerous outdoor activities for children, including mini-golf, a playground, and go-karting. The indoor Playzone has a bouncy castle, ball pool, and games.

Guests can relax in the Club Amber Springs Leisure Center. This features an 50 feet swimming pool with an attached pool for children, a sauna, hot tub, and a fitness studio. Adults can pre-book treatments in the Cocoon Spa.

The Ballymoney Golf Course is just 3.7 mi away.
