Description

All guest rooms come with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a electric tea pot, a shower, free toiletries and a closet.

Guests at Corcoran's Lodge can enjoy a Full English/Irish breakfast.

The accommodation has a sun terrace.

Wexford is 16 miles from Corcoran's Lodge, while Carlow is 26 miles away. The nearest airport is Waterford Airport, 35 miles from the bed and breakfast.
