Description

The Brandon House hotel and Eco Spa is situated on the N25 in New Ross, between Wexford and Waterford.

It offers a pool, gym, an award-winning restaurant and free parking. The rooms at the Brandon feature satellite TVs, tea/coffee making facilities, hairdryers and irons.

The Gallery Restaurant serves modern and traditional cuisine. The Library Bar has views over the River Barrow and a wide selection of drinks.

Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas of the hotel. The Solas Croí Spa has a wide range of treatments and massages available for a surcharge.

There is also a steam room, sauna, sanarium, Jacuzzi® and outdoor plunge pool, which guests can also enjoy for a surcharge.
