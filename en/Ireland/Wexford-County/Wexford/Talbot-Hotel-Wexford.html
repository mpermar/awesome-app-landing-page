Description

Fresh, local produce is served in the Oyster Lane Restaurant. Guests can enjoy a casual lunch or evening drink in the hotel's Ballast Bank Bar and Grill.

Wexford Harbour, and its ferry connections to South Wales, is less than a 20 minute drive from the Talbot Hotel Wexford. Central Wexford with its Train Station is a 10 minute walk away.

All rooms have air-conditioning and include tea and coffee-making facilities. Some rooms feature views out over the Slaney River.
