Description

Emmet House is a small boutique B&B located in Emmet Square, Birr, County Offaly.

It offers rooms with flat-screen TVs, free Wi-Fi and home-cooked food. Birr Leisure Centre is a 5-minute walk away.

The rooms at Emmet House feature seating areas and work desks. They also include hairdryers, ironing facilities and tea/coffee-makers.

Some rooms have a 4-poster bed. Traditional Irish breakfasts are served in the dining room, which has polished wooden floors, a chandelier and views out across the square.

Emmet Restaurant is situated opposite the guest house. Birr Castle is just a 5-minute walk away, and is famous for the Leviathan of Parsonstown telescope.

There are plenty of countryside walking routes in the area and the shores of Lough Derg are a 10-minute drive away.

The house is situated near historic Clonmacnoise, Shannon Harbour, and Shannon Bridge.

Roscrea Monastery, Kinnitty Castle, and Birr Equestrian Centre are also located nearby.
