Description

Situated in the coastal town of Bantry, Aran Lodge offers free WiFi access and free parking.

Just 4 km from Bantry Bay Golf Club, Glengarriff Nature Reserve is also a short 13-minute journey by car.

Each room includes a TV and an electric kettle. Featuring a shower, the private bathroom also comes with a bath and a hairdryer.

Guests can enjoy a sea view and garden view from the room. Extras include bed linen and ironing facilities.

At Aran Lodge there is a garden and a terrace. Other facilities offered at the property include a shared lounge, luggage storage and an ironing service.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including golfing, cycling and fishing.

From the property, Bantry Aerodrome is 13 minutes' drive away and Bantry pier‎ is 6.
