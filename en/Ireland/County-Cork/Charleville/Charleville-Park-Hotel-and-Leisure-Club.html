Description

The Charleville Park Hotel is 40 minutes from Cork and 30 minutes from Limerick.

The recreation center has a 80-foot pool, a sauna, a steam room, hot bathtubs and an aerobics studio which guests can use free of charge. Modern Irish cuisine is served in the stylish, relaxed Greenfinch Restaurant. A selection of drinks and food is also provided in the bar.

The hotel has 5 dedicated conference and meeting rooms and can accommodate up to 550 attendees with the latest in audio/visual technology.
