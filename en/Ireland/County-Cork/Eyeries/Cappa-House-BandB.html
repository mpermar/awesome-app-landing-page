Description

At the bed and breakfast, rooms have a closet. All rooms include a electric tea pot, while some have a patio. The rooms have a private bathroom with a shower.

A continental breakfast is served at the property.

Guests at Cappa House B&B will be able to enjoy activities in and around Eyeries, like cycling and fishing.

Kenmare is 21 miles from the accommodation, while Bantry is 22 miles away. Kerry Airport is 39 miles from the property.
