Description

In the heart of Clonakilty, this luxury hotel has a leisure centre with an indoor pool, sauna and steam room.

With a modern restaurant and bar, the beautiful coastline is just 10 minutes away by car.

The private bathrooms all include a hairdryer. Serving a varied menu with traditional Irish dishes, Twist Bar & Bistro uses fresh local produce.
