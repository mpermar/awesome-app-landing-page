Description

Lahinch Golf & Leisure offers a sauna, spa bathtub, and a steam room.

Each room at Lahinch hotel has satellite TV, tea/coffee-making facilities and a spacious bathroom.

Lahinch Golf & Leisure features the cozy Aberdeen Bar, with a fireplace. Dunes Restaurant serves home-cooked meals and full Irish breakfasts. The Emperor Restaurant serves Chinese and Asian cuisine.

The famous Cliffs of Moher are just a 10 minute drive away, offering views of the Aran Islands.
