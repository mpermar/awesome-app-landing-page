Description

The Clanard Court Hotel is situated in Athy, Kildare, just a 10-minute walk from Athy Train Station.

It offers spacious rooms with free internet access, a brasserie and free parking.

Rooms at the Clanard Hotel have bathrooms with baths, power showers and hairdryers.

They also include TVs, safes and luxurious duvets and bed linen. Bailey’s Bistro serves modern cuisine and has an extensive wine list.

Bailey’s Bar offers traditional Irish food and regular live entertainment. K Leisure is one mile away in the centre of Athy and guests of Clanard have free use of its facilities.

These include a pool, sauna and gym. Athy farmers Market and the Athy Heritage Centre Museum are both a 15-minute walk from Clanard.
