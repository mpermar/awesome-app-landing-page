Description

Each smartly furnished bedroom features calm and luxurious décor, free internet access, and plush bedding to relax in at the end of the day.

The fully equipped Clann Fitness & Leisure Club provides a wide range of equipment and facilities, and the newly refurbished Jule Spa offers an indulgent range of treatments.

The N2 road on which the hotel is located is the main route to the North of Ireland, and provides access to locations such as Slane, Trim, Navan and Ballymagarvey village.
