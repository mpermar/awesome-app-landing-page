Description

At the guesthouse, the rooms have a closet. Rooms come with a electric tea pot, while selected rooms also offer a kitchen with a dishwasher and an oven. The rooms will provide guests with a microwave.

At Rock Farm Slane - Limehouse guests are welcome to use a hot tub. A variety of activities are offered in the area, such as cycling, fishing and hiking.

Jumping Church of Kildemock is 8 miles from the accommodation, while Slane Castle is 1.1 miles from the property. Dublin Airport is 23 miles away.
