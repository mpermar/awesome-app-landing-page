Description

Ideally located on the outskirts of Cavan, the Cavan Crystal Hotel is less than 5 minutes' drive from the town centre.

It features an 18-metre swimming pool, an award-winning health and fitness club, a bar, and a fine dining restaurant.

The spacious rooms feature elegant, wooden decor. Each includes free WiFi and a complimentary morning newspaper.

There is a private bathroom in each room, along with a hairdryer, iron and iron board, tea and coffee making facilities.

The Zest Health and Fitness Club includes a modern and fully equipped gym, along with a sauna and a steam room.

Guests can enjoy relaxing beauty treatments in the Utopia Health and Beauty Clinic.

Opus One Restaurant provides award-winning dining, while the Atrium Bar offers afternoon tea and scones, weekend live entertainment, and a bar menu.

Guests can also relax in the elegant Atrium Lobby. Cavan town and County Cavan Golf Club are both just 5 minutes’ drive away, and Headfort’s championship golf course is a 30-minute drive away.

Guests can enjoy horse riding at Cavan Equestrian Centre, less than 10 minutes away by car.
