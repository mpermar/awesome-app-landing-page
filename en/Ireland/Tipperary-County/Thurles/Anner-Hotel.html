Description

Located on the outskirts of Thurles town, Anner Hotel and its leisure centre is a pleasant base with friendly staff, fine food and plenty of comforts.

Originally a country house, the hotel has expanded to include modern amenities whilst still holding on to its old world charm and character.

Anner Hotel is set in a landscape garden and has bedrooms fitted with modern conveniences and soft colours.

The hotel is equipped with conference facilities for up to 350 people, and a car park.

The leisure and fitness centre serves as a pleasant place to relax, with a 20-metre pool, a kids' pool, and a sauna, steam room and hot tub.

The restaurant is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner and serves tasty cuisine to residents and locals.

Ed's Bar & Bistro offers a selection of beverages, snacks, daily carvery, and an evening à la carte menu.

Seasons Restaurant serves a range of traditional Irish and European dishes in a more formal setting.

The M8 motorway can be reached in a 6-minute drive. Dublin is 152 km away, and Cork is 115 km away.
