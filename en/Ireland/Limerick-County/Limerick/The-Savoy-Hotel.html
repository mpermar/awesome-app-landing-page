Description

The air-conditioned rooms at the Savoy Limerick all have satellite TVs, bathrobes and slippers. They also include lap-top safes. Executive rooms and suites have access to the 7th-floor Executive Lounge.

The Savoy Aqua and Fitness Club provides a modern gym, spa tub, and plunge pool. Beauty treatments and Thai massages are available at the spa by appointment.

The Liszt Lounge has a light lunch menu and the Savoy Bar serves signature cocktails. Fine dining is available at the Market Square, and the Hamptons Restaurant is a New York-style grill house.

The Hunt Museum and Roxboro Shopping Center are both within a 5-minute walk of the hotel.
