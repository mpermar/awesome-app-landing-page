Description

The colonial-style rooms have tile floors and have air conditioning and a fan. There is cable TV, a desk and private bathroom with a shower and toiletries.

The hotel has a restaurant, a bar and poolside bar. It is possible to have breakfast in the room.

Guests can rent a car from the tour desk and an airport shuttle service is available for an additional charge.
