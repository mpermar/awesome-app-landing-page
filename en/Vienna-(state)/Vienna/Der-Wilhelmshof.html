Description

Der Wilhelmshof is in a quiet side street, a 5-minute walk from the famous Riesenrad Ferris wheel and the Praterstern public transportation hub. Underground lines U1 and U2 stop there.

The UN institutions, the Messe Wien fairgrounds and St. Stephen's Cathedral are within a 7-minute underground ride of the hotel. Garage parking is available on site.

02. Leopoldstadt is a great choice for travelers interested in city walks, old-town exploration and monuments.
