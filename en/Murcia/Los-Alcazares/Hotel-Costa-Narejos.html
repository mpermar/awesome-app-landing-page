Description

The Costa Narejos includes a spa with Turkish bath, sauna and hot tubs. It offers massages and beauty treatments and has a gym.

The lounge area of Costa Narejos has comfortable armchairs and a large TV, which often broadcasts sports programs. The pool is surrounded by a solarium with sun loungers.

Hotel Costa Narejos has a buffet restaurant that serves various hot and cold Spanish and international dishes. Light meals are served in the hotel cafeteria.

San Javier Airport is 3 km from the hotel. Las Velas Shopping Center is 400 meters away and houses a cinema, shops, bars and restaurants. The La Serena golf course is less than 5 km away.
