Description

Each of the rooms at Hotel Restaurant Tychon AG includes free Wi-Fi, cable TV and a bathroom with a bathtub or shower. Most rooms feature garden views. There is also a room with facilities for disabled guests. The hotel serves a buffet-style breakfast each morning.

Eupen is a 10-minute drive from the hotel. The historic city of Liege is 30 minutes away by car. Hotel Tychon AG has packed lunches for guests who would like to explore the local area.
