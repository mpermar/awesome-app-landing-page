Description

All bungalows at Patachocas Beach are equipped with air conditioning, minibar and balcony overlooking the tropical garden. The rooms have a private bathroom with shower, hairdryer and free toiletries.

Patachocas Beach offers eco-tours to the neighboring islands and free transportation to the Villa district, where there are several bars, restaurants and shops. The property provides bicycles and rental kayaks. In the area you can go diving. In addition, there is a tennis court and games room.

The restaurant specializes in Mediterranean cuisine, although it also serves regional dishes. The establishment also includes several shops and provides a free shuttle service.

Patachocas Beach is 2.1 km from Second Beach and Saudade Island. Luis Eduardo Magalhães Airport is 82 km away.
