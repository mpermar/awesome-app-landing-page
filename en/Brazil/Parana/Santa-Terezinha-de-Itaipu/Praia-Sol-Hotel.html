Description

The hotel has 52 rooms equipped with air conditioning, minibar, TV and Wi-Fi connection.

Praia Sol serves a buffet breakfast with pastries, bread and fruit in a luxurious dining area.

The hotel also has swimming pools for adults and children, garden and restaurant.

Foz do Iguacu / Cataratas International Airport is a 35-minute drive from the hotel.
