Description

The San Martin Resort & Spa has a large swimming pool surrounded by gardens, an ecological path through the surrounding native forests and various sports facilities, such as volleyball, soccer and tennis courts.

Breakfast is served from 06:30 to 10:30. The Tarobá restaurant prepares specialties of international cuisine until late at night. The Lapacho Video bar offers a cozy atmosphere and music from 11:00 to 00:00.

Foz do Iguaçu International Airport is 1.5 km away, while Parque das Aves is less than a 4-minute walk away.
