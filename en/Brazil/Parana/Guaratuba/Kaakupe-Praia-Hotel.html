Description

Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and is equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Views of the sea, pool or garden are featured in certain rooms. Each room includes a private bathroom. A electronic safe, refrigerator and telephone are offered.

A sauna and a games room are available for guests, as well as a 24-hour front desk for assistance.

Ilha do Mel is 46 km from Kaakupe Beach Hotel, while Matinhos is 11 km away. Lauro Carneiro de Loyola Airport is 42 km from the property. Hotel is 115 km from Afonso Pena International Airport. On-site parking is free.
