Description

Castelo Pink Boutique Hotel features a restaurant, outdoor swimming pool, a bar and garden in Uruau.

Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a terrace. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and free WiFi throughout the property.

At the hotel each room comes with air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kitchen, a dining area and a private bathroom with a hairdryer, a shower and free toiletries.

Guest rooms include a desk. Guests at Castelo Pink Boutique Hotel can enjoy a continental breakfast.

Beberibe is 21 km from the accommodation, while Cascavel is 39 km away. The nearest airport is Pinto Martins Airport, 100 km from Castelo Pink Boutique Hotel.
