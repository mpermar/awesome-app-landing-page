Description

All the cottages and suites of the Eco Resort Vento Leste offer a quiet atmosphere and feature a TV, mini fridge and large bathroom.

The Eco Resort Vento Leste features a 20-meter outdoor pool surrounded by tropical gardens and a games room with 2 pool tables and a professional ping pong table.

The IL Faro Blanco restaurant at Vento Leste serves Chinese, Brazilian and Italian dishes, while the bar offers a wide variety of drinks.
