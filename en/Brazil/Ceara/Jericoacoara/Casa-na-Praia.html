Description

The rooms at Casa na Praia are elegant and have a typical decoration. All rooms also include a large private balcony with sea views, air conditioning and mosquito nets.

The property has a solarium and lovely tropical gardens with direct access to the beach. It also offers beach service with sun loungers and umbrellas.

A buffet breakfast with fresh tropical fruit is served daily. In addition, the hotel offers room service and the restaurant serves a wide variety of local and international dishes.

Once a week, the establishment organizes a private show with music, capoeira and afro dance at night. After the show dinner is served on the beach in front of a large bonfire. Casa na Praia is located in front of a panoramic natural environment and enjoys an ideal location just a few steps from the city center.
