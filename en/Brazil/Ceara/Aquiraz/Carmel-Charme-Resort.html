Description

The spacious rooms of the complex have air conditioning, 42-inch LCD TV with cable channels, telephone and minibar. The bungalows have a private pool.

The restaurant serves a tropical buffet breakfast every morning, with fresh fruit and local products, as well as typical specialties from northeastern Brazil. The complex also houses a bar that offers a selection of exotic drinks.

Guests of the Carmel Charme Resort can relax with a massage in the spa or play pool in the games room. The complex also features a hot tub, a sauna and a modern gym.

Carmel Charme is 40 km from Fortaleza and its coastline. The complex has free parking.
