Description

Offering outdoor pools and a fitness centre, Carmel Cumbuco Resort offers beachfront accommodations located in Cumbuco, 9 km from Barragem da Lagamar.

Free WiFi access is available. Daily breakfast is served free of charge. Boasting a balcony with sea and pool views, all rooms are fitted with a 42-inch LCD TV, cable channels, air conditioning, a minibar, a safe and a telephone.

Private bathroom comes with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries. Guests can relax in the spa and communal sauna, or enjoy refreshing drinks at the swim-up bar.

Carmel Cumbuco Resort also offers a 24-hour reception, massage services, hot tubs and a restaurant.

The nearest airport is Pinto Martins Airport, 25 km from Carmel Cumbuco Resort. Free private parking is available.
