Description

All suites have HD LED TV, hot tub, air conditioning and balcony. They include luxury items, such as L'Occitane toiletries, Nespresso coffee maker and bathrobe and Egyptian cotton bedding.

The Zorah Beach Hotel has a restaurant that prepares Asian dishes with local ingredients. Various international chefs spend 2 to 3 months in the restaurant and create a special menu for each visit. The bar has a wine list and a selection of international beers.

Fortaleza is 140 km away and Paracuru Beach is 75 km away. Pinto Martins International Airport is 144 km away.
