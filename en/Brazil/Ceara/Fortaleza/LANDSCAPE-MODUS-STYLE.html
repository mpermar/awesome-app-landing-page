Description

Located in Fortaleza, 2. 5 km from Mucuripe Fish Market and 11 km from North Shopping, LANDSCAPE - MODUS STYLE provides accommodation with free WiFi, a garden with an outdoor swimming pool, sea views, and access to an indoor swimming pool and a fitness room.

Each unit comes with a sofa, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a well-fitted kitchen with a dining area, and a private bathroom with free toiletries.

A microwave, a fridge and oven are also provided, as well as a coffee machine. The apartment offers a children's playground.

Guests at LANDSCAPE - MODUS STYLE can enjoy billiards on site, or go hiking or snorkelling in the surroundings.

Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Meireles Beach, Ceara Image and Sound Museum and Abolition Palace.

The nearest airport is Pinto Martins, 11 km from LANDSCAPE - MODUS STYLE, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
