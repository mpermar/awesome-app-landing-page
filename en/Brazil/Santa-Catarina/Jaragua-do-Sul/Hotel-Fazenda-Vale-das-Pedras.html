Description

Rooms at Hotel Vale Das Pedras have air conditioning, TV and minibar. The private balcony includes a hammock.

In the immediate vicinity you can do many leisure activities, such as fishing and horse riding. You can also do paintball or ride a quad. The property has a mini-golf area and a children's play area.

The restaurant serves a rich breakfast, lunch and buffet dinner. A variety of national and international dishes with excellent cocktails and wines is served.

Vale das Pedras is 59 km from Joinville and 84 km from Blumenau. The town of Navegatnes is 111 km away and Curtitiba is 193 km away. There is free parking.
