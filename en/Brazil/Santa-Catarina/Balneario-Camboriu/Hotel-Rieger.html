Description

The hotel has 2 outdoor swimming pools surrounded by a wooden terrace with sun loungers. Inside there is also a wellness area with heated pool and well-equipped gym. There is a bar next to the outdoor pool and another bar next to the indoor pool.

The Coral restaurant serves international cuisine and rich buffets with local specialties. The cafeteria offers Brazilian dishes with culinary touches from the Azores, Germany and the East.

The Rieger's air-conditioned units feature a full kitchen with a microwave, oven and minibar. Some include a private hot tub.

The Cristo Luz monument is less than 1 km from the hotel.
