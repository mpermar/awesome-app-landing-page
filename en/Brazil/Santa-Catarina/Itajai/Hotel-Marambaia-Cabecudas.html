Description

Rooms at Hotel Marambaia Cabeçudas are bright and elegant and feature a minibar, cable TV and a balcony with sea views. The bathroom is private and includes toiletries.

The hotel facilities include a games room with table football and ping pong table and a lounge with TV.

The hotel is located 4.5 km from the center of Itajaí and 10 km from the Minister Víctor Konder International Airport.
