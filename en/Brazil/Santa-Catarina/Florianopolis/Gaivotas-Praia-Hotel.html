Description

The Gaivotas Praia Hotel is located 100 metres from Ingleses Beach and around 450 metres from Barra Shopping Mall.

It features an outdoor swimming pool and free Wi-Fi. Rooms have a practical design and a light décor with tiled floors.

They are air conditioned and equipped with cable TV. Some rooms offer a separate dining and seating area and include a private balcony.

A buffet breakfast is served in the eating room. For leisure Hotel Gaivotas Praia offers a games room.

From the hotel it is a 7 km drive to Bom Jesus Cachoeira Waterfall and another 5 km to Rio Vermelho Park.
