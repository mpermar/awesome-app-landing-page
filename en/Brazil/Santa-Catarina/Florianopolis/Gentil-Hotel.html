Description

Gentil Hotel offers a swimming pool, and air-conditioned rooms only 50 metres from Praia dos Ingleses Beach in Florianópolis.

There is free WiFi in public areas and private parking. The rooms at Gentil Hotel are simply decorated with tiled floors, a ceiling fan and ample wardrobe.

In addition to air conditioning, they have a minibar, TV and private bathroom. A buffet breakfast is served each morning in the breakfast room.

It includes a variety of fresh fruits, breads and cold meats, as well as a selection of hot and cold drinks.

Gentil Hotel is within a few steps of dining and shopping options. Oceania events venue is 3 km away.

The property is 10 km from famous Jurere Internacional Beach and 5 km from Costão do Santinho.
