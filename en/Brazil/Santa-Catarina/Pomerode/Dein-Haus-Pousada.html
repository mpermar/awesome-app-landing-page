Description

Offering an outdoor swimming pool and spa bath, Posada Dein Haus offers rooms in the centre of Pomerode, and nearby from the Zoo and Vila Encantada.

The property also has a bar and free WiFi. Featuring air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and a minibar, rooms have a private bathroom.

A breakfast buffet is served daily in the common area, and includes hot beverages, sweet pastries and cakes, and savoury snacks.

Navigating Minister Victor Konder International Airport is 80 km away, while Immigrant House Museum is 6 km from the property.
