Description

Hotel Fazenda Pommernland has a restaurant, free bikes, an outdoor swimming pool and bar in Pomerode.

Among the facilities at this property are a 24-hour front desk and a shared kitchen, along with free WiFi throughout the property.

The accommodation features room service, a shared lounge and luggage storage for guests.

At the hotel the rooms are fitted with a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

Guests at Hotel Fazenda Pommernland can enjoy a continental or a buffet breakfast.

The accommodation offers a children's playground. You can play billiards and table tennis at Hotel Fazenda Pommernland, and the area is popular for hiking.

Blumenau is 37 km from the hotel, while Indaial is 37 km from the property. The nearest airport is Ministro Victor Konder International Airport, 71 km from Hotel Fazenda Pommernland.
