Description

Offering an outdoor pool and a seasonal outdoor pool, Casa Branca Pousada Boutique is set in Pomerode in the Santa Catarina Region, 21 km from Blumenau.

Free private parking is available on site. Every room is equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a minibar and air conditioning.

The rooms include a private bathroom fitted with a shower. Casa Branca Pousada Boutique features free WiFi throughout the property.

There is a shared lounge at the property. The daily breakfast buffet offers a variety of cakes and hot beverages.

Free use of bicycles is available at this inn and the area is popular for hiking.

Brusque is 45 km from Casa Branca Inn Boutique, while Indaial is 17 km away. The nearest airport is Navegantes - Minister Victor Konder International Airport, 53 km from Casa Branca Boutique Inn.
