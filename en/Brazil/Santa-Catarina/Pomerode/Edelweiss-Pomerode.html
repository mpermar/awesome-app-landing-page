Description

Providing free WiFi throughout the property, a sun terrace with a swimming pool, shared lounge and garden, Edelweiss Pomerode is located in Pomerode.

Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a children's playground.

The accommodation provides room service, a kids' club and organising tours for guests.

At the hotel, rooms have a wardrobe. At Edelweiss Pomerode each room comes with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

A buffet breakfast is available daily at the accommodation. The area is popular for hiking and cycling, and bike hire is available at Edelweiss Pomerode.

Blumenau is 19 km from the hotel, while Indaial is 22 km away. The nearest airport is Ministro Victor Konder International, 64 km from Edelweiss Pomerode, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
