Description

Hotel Bergblick is located in Pomerode, an area settled by Germans in the 19th century and features many unique, half-timbered houses with lovely gardens.

The town centre is just 2 km away and free Wi-Fi is available. The Bergblick Hotel offers large rooms with air conditioning, a TV and a minibar.

They are elegant with floor-to-ceiling windows, wood furniture and floral prints.

Some rooms offer extra luxury and feature a hot tub. An international buffet breakfast is provided daily.

Guests can also venture to the Pomerode centre and enjoy mouth-watering, typical German dishes in the many cafes and restaurants that are located there.

The Pomerano Zoo is one of the largest in Santa Catarina and is a popular tourist area that displays a variety of exotic animals.
