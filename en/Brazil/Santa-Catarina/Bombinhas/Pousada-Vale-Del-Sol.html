Description

The apartments at Pousada Vale Del Sol have a balcony, barbecue, air conditioning, living room with fan and TV. They also have heating, safe and telephone. The kitchen is equipped with a fridge, cooking utensils, stove and oven. The bathroom has a shower.

The beautiful Mariscal beach is 1.2 km away. The center of the town of Bombinhas is 500 meters from Vale del Sol. Itapema Bus Terminal is 20 km from the property. Victor Konder International Airport, in the city of Navegantes, is 70 km from the accommodation.
