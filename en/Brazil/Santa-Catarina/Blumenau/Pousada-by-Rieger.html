Description

Located in Blumenau, 5 km from Blumenau Bus Station, Pousada by Rieger provides accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a bar and a garden.

The property is set 700 metres from Vila Germanica, 2. 4 km from Theather Carlos Gomes and 2.

7 km from Sao Paulo Apostolo Cathedral. The air-conditioned rooms provide a garden view and come with a wardrobe and free WiFi.

At the inn every room is fitted with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

Guests at Pousada by Rieger can enjoy a continental breakfast. The area is popular for hiking and cycling, and bike hire is available at the accommodation.

Castelinho da Havan is 2. 9 km from Pousada by Rieger, while Water Museum is 3. 1 km away.

The nearest airport is Ministro Victor Konder International, 59 km from the inn, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
