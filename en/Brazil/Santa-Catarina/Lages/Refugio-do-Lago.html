Description

This chalet features mountain views and features a fireplace, cable TV, minibar and private bathroom.

The Pousada Refugio do Lago also has a children's playground. It houses a restaurant that serves menus for special diets and packed lunches upon request.

You can play table tennis and billiards at the property. It is in an ideal area to play golf. Other activities such as horse riding, fishing and hiking can also be practiced nearby.
