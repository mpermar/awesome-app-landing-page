Description

The chalets have wooden accessories and are surrounded by virgin jungle. They feature a balcony, air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, room service, bathroom with toiletries and living and dining areas with TV, stereo and minibar.

Pousada Caminho do Rei is 1.5 km from Praia do Rosa, 5 km from the bus station, 18 km from Garopaba and 90 km from Hercilio Luz de Florianopolis International Airport. The property offers free parking and transfer services, tourist information and car rental.
