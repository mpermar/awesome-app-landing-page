Description

The rooms at the Solar Mirador Exclusive Resort and SPA are warm and cheerful. They have air conditioning, minibar, balcony with hammock, LCD cable TV, DVD player, private bathroom and seating area.

Buffet breakfast is served in a luxurious dining area. The Solar Mirador Exclusive Resort and SPA offers a selection of seasonal fruits and breads. The restaurant and bar has panoramic views and serves drinks and cooked dishes.

The complex organizes yoga and meditation sessions and massages, upon request. It also provides beach services, such as sun loungers, umbrellas and towels. It has a reception with airport shuttle service, car rental and dry cleaning and lounge with fireplace and pool table.

It is a 3-minute walk from the center of Praia do Rosa and 25 km from Garopaba bus station. It has free private parking.
