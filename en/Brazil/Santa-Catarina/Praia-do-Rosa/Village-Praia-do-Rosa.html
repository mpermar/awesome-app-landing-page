Description

Rosa Village houses feature a patio with panoramic sea views, a fully equipped kitchen with a stove, minibar and dining area, TV, safe, private bathroom and another additional bathroom.

The Village is 70 km from Hercílio Luz International Airport, 16 km from Garopaba Bus Station, just 1 km from Praia do Rosa center, 5 km from Ibiraquera Beach and 15 km from Imbituba center .

The hotel also has a restaurant and serves a buffet breakfast with tropical products.
