Description

The apartments are decorated in light colors and include a flat-screen cable TV, air conditioning, a minibar and a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries.

In addition, the Pratas Thermas Resort & Convention features a spa and wellness center, a restaurant and a tennis court. There is also a shared lounge, games room and ironing facilities.

The outdoor pool and hot tub are surrounded by nature and enjoy a lot of natural light.

In the surroundings you can go hiking and there is a water park. The property provides free parking.

Hercílio Luz International Airport is 606 km away.
