Description

The complex serves a buffet breakfast every day. There is also a restaurant and a pizzeria. The complex facilities include a hot tub, a games room and several tennis courts.

The rooms at Refúgio do Estaleiro are spacious and bright. All rooms have air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and a balcony with sea views. They also have spacious closets and toiletries from Natura Ekos.

The center of Bombinhas and the Porto Belo bus terminal are less than 15 km from the hotel. The town of Balneário Camboriú is located about 40 minutes away. Navegantes Airport is 60 km away.
