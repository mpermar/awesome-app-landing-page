Description

Hotel Rural Sítio Do Sol has a restaurant, outdoor swimming pool, a bar and shared lounge in Brusque.

Among the various facilities of this property are a garden and barbecue facilities.

There is a children's playground and guests can make use of free WiFi and free private parking.

At the hotel, all rooms have a balcony with a lake view. A continental breakfast is available every morning at Hotel Rural Sítio Do Sol.

Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Brusque, like fishing.

Speaking German, English, Spanish and Italian at the reception, staff are ready to help around the clock.

Itajaí is 49 km from Hotel Rural Sítio Do Sol, while Blumenau is 30 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Ministro Victor Konder International, 53 km from the hotel, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
