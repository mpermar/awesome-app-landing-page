Description

The rooms are beautiful, spacious and bright; They have views of the garden or the sea. They are equipped with air conditioning, cable TV, minibar and fan. Some have a balcony and hot tub.

The hotel serves a delicious buffet breakfast, which includes juices and tropical fruit. Brazilian and international dishes are offered for lunch and dinner.

Guests of Pousada Recanto das Flores can go on a boat trip to Anhato Island, 15 minutes away. They can also play football in the same establishment. Inside there is a modern home cinema.
