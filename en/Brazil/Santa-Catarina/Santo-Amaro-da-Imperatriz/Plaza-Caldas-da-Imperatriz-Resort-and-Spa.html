Description

The hotel is in the middle of the Atlantic forest, which allows you to enjoy contact with nature and participate in many ecological activities, accompanied by professionals.

The Caldas da Imperatriz Square is located near different beaches and houses several hot tubs, as well as indoor and outdoor pools.

Guests have the opportunity to go hiking in the surroundings, go to the gym or practice beach volleyball, soccer or tennis.

The 2 restaurants serve grilled dishes, salads and the best international cuisine. There is also a bar in the lobby and another by the pool. In addition, room service is available 24 hours a day. The convention center is large and has a wide variety of catering options for conferences.
