Description

Surrounded by nature and located near the São Francisco River, Xingó Parque Hotel offers a large outdoor pool and free WiFi.

Featuring views of São Francisco River, all the rooms are equipped with air conditioning, a minibar, a TV, a telephone,a safety deposit box and a private bathroom with hot shower.

Guests benefit from a buffet breakfast served daily at the on-site restaurant Maria Bonita.

Other facilities at Xingó Parque Hotel include a fitness centre, an on-site bar, room service and a 24-hour front desk.

Set near the hydroelectricity Xingó and the famous canyons of the Velho Chico, the property is within a distance of 77 km from Paulo Afonso Airport.
