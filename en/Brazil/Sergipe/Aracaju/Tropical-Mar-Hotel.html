Description

Tropical Mar Hotel has a restaurant, outdoor swimming pool, a fitness centre and bar in Aracaju.

Featuring a shared lounge, the 3-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with a private bathroom.

The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk and room service for guests. At the hotel, all rooms include a patio with a garden view.

At Tropical Mar Hotel the rooms are equipped with a desk and a flat-screen TV. Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

Tropical Mar Hotel offers a children's playground. Guests can enjoy playing billiards, or take advantage of the business centre.

Aruana Beach is 5 km from the hotel, while Atalaia Beach is 8 km from the property.
