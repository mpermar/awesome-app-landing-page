Description

The cozy suites and wooden chalets at Hotel Pedra Bonita are decorated with rustic-style furniture and feature air conditioning and cable TV. The villas also have a large hot tub.

Guests of the Pedra Bonita can go horse riding, play polo and visit the small zoo. There is also a tennis court, a fitness center and a sauna.

In the restaurant, with a rustic and contemporary design, international dishes of meat and seafood are prepared. A buffet breakfast with homemade cakes, cookies and jams is served every morning.

Hotel Pedra Bonita is 60 km from Galeao Airport, a 10-minute drive from the Itaipava neighborhood, known for its cuisine, and a 20-minute drive from the center of Petrópolis.

One older child or adult can be accommodated paying 50% of the room price per person per night in an extra bed.
Maximum number of extra beds in the room: 2.
Extra beds are available upon request and must be confirmed by the accommodation.
Supplements are not automatically calculated in the total amount and must be paid separately during the stay.
