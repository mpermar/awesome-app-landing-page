Description

All rooms have flat-screen TVs. Some have a seating area. The bathrooms are private and include a bath or shower, bathrobes and free toiletries. The superior rooms have a hot tub.

There is a 24-hour reception, hairdresser and gift shop.

You can play tennis and table tennis at this resort, and the area is ideal for fishing. Teresópolis is 20 km from Bomtempo Resort, while Itaipava is 6 km away. The nearest airport is Rio de Janeiro / Galeão International Airport, 45 km from Bomtempo Resort.
