Description

Its elegant rooms have a modern private bathroom, a safe, flat-screen cable TV, air conditioning and a minibar.

Tom Jobim Airport is 98 km away. The marina of Mont Blanc is 72 km from Angra dos Reis and 85 km from the island of Ilha Grande. Praia do Abraão Beach is 9 km away.

This hotel also houses a restaurant that serves a daily buffet breakfast and offers a menu for lunch and dinner with regional specialties. You can enjoy exotic drinks by the pool.
