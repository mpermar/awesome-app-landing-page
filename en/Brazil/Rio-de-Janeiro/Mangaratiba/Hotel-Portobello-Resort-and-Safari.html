Description

The accommodations have a minibar, bathroom, TV with Sky channels, air conditioning and sea views. All have free Wi-Fi and some offer direct access to the beach. The suites are larger and include a seating area and sofa or sofa bed. Some have a balcony.

Hotel Portobello Resort & Safari is 4.3 km from the Rio-Santos motorway and 47 km from Angra dos Reis beach. Rio de Janeiro's Galeão International Airport is 100 km away and Ilha Grande Bay is 30 minutes away by boat. The property has a helipad, a port and free private parking.

Portobello guests can go water skiing, canoeing or kite surfing. They can also ride a horse or bicycle. Children can have fun with the entertainment team staff.

The resort's main restaurant, the Escuna, serves a daily buffet breakfast, lunch and dinner. Guests can dine at the Pergola restaurant, located next to the pool, or enjoy pizzas and crepes at the pizzeria.
