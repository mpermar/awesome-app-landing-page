Description

Rooms at Casa do Telhado Verde Pousada feature air conditioning, a TV, minibar and a private bathroom with shower.

Pousada Casa do Telhado Verde offers barbecue facilities, a cafeteria, a shared lounge and a games room.

This hostel is located 3.9 km from Dunes Park, 4.4 km from Alair Correia Municipal Stadium and 5.2 km from Forte Beach.
