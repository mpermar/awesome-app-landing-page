Description

The hotel rooms have a desk, flat-screen TV and air conditioning. Some accommodations at the Hotel Boutique Recanto da Passagem have a balcony. The rooms include a private bathroom with free toiletries.

The Hotel Boutique Recanto da Passagem serves a daily buffet breakfast.

Guests have access to the sauna.

The reception staff is available 24 hours a day and speaks English and Portuguese.

The property is 1.5 km from the Surf Museum and the municipal theater.
