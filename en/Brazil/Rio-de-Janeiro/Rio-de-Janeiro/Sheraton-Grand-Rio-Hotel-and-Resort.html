Description

The complex houses a well-equipped gym and a newly opened spa, the Shine. The emblematic statue of Christ the Redeemer is 10 km away.

The Sheraton Grand Rio is home to the Casarão restaurant, a casual and elegant restaurant located in the pool area that serves buffet lunches and excellent cuts of meat for dinner. The Bene restaurant offers Italian cuisine. There is also a bar, the Dry Martini, where guests can enjoy a modern and innovative atmosphere.

Casa da Cachaça, located in the pool area, offers sophisticated snacks, caipirinhas and classic cocktails. The L'Etoile restaurant, located on the 26th floor, serves French cuisine and offers panoramic sea views.

The rooms at the Sheraton Grand Rio are spacious and have been refurbished with great detail. All rooms are modernly decorated and have contemporary furniture. The rooms are air-conditioned and have a private balcony with magnificent views of the beach.

Leblon Beach is 1 km away and Tijuca National Park is 3.4 km away. Santos Dumont Airport is located 16 km from the Sheraton Grand Rio. The hotel offers airport shuttle services.

Leblon is a great choice for travelers interested in food, sandy beaches and nature.
