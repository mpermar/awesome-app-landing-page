Description

The rooms at the Windsor Barra Hotel are magnificent and feature comfortable seating areas, air conditioning and satellite TV. Some also offer a hot tub and stunning ocean views.

The Windsor's restaurant serves a buffet of international dishes that includes fresh oysters and prawns. On Saturdays a traditional lunch of black beans is offered and on Sundays a brunch with select champagne is organized. In addition, there are several international and regional restaurants within a 2-minute walk.

The hotel is located 4 km from Barra Mall and the Parque da Barra National Park, which has a picturesque nature trail. The famous Ipanema beach is 15 km away and Copacabana beach is 20 km away.

The Olympic Park is 14.1 km from Windsor Barra Hotel and Pontal Beach is 16.6 km away. Santos Dumont Airport is 27.5 km away and Galeao International Airport is 37.2 km away.

Barra da Tijuca is a great choice for travelers interested in sunsets, sandy beaches and food.
