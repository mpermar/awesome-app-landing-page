Description

The rooms of this 5-star hotel are elegant and spacious. They have a balcony, air conditioning, a flat-screen satellite TV, a minibar and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and a bath or shower. They also have wooden floor and leather furniture and high quality wood.

The Fasano Hotel in Rio de Janeiro has the distinguished Fasano restaurant, famous for its contemporary Italian cuisine. In addition, the establishment is located within a 5-minute walk of restaurants serving local and international cuisine.

Guests can relax in the sauna, have a drink at the bar or work out in the fitness center, which is very well equipped. The establishment also offers massages, a terrace, concierge service, a solarium and a disco.

Fasano Rio de Janeiro is 1 km from Copacabana Fort and 4 km from Lagoa Stadium. Santos Dumont Airport is 11 km away, while Galeão International Airport is 27.8 km away.

Ipanema is a great choice for travelers interested in the beach, sandy beaches and walking along the beach.
