Description

The rooms and suites are elegantly decorated in cream tones and feature a Nespresso coffee machine and a private bathroom with free L'Occitane toiletries and a hairdryer. Some room types include free WiFi. Room service 24 hours.

The Hilton Barra Rio de Janeiro has a 24-hour business center, 2 large dance halls and 5 meeting rooms where events and conferences can be held.

This 5-star hotel is located 2 km from Barra Olympic Park, 5 km from RioCentro Convention Center, 11 km from Tijuca National Forest Park and 300 meters from Metropolitan Mall. Galeão International Airport and Santos Dumont Airport are 30 km away.

Barra da Tijuca is a great choice for travelers interested in sunsets, sandy beaches and food.
