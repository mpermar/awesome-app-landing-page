Description

The property has an amusement park, the Magic Park, accessible for a fee. In the park there are facilities for skiing and bowling. In addition, it has a small castle with activities for all ages. The property offers other services and attractions that can be booked for a fee and are available based on availability.

Rooms at Le Canton have air conditioning, a flat-screen cable TV, a minibar and a private bathroom. Free private parking is available.

The property is 88 km from Tom Jobim Airport, 10 km from Teresópolis center and its bus station, and 12 km from Serra dos Orgãos National Park. The Teresópolis Fair is 7 km away.

The establishment has a bar and restaurant, and serves a buffet breakfast with a variety of regional products. On the premises there is a games room and tennis court.
