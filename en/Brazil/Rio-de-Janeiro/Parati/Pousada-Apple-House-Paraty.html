Description

Some units have a seating area and some overlook the pool or the river. The private bathroom has a shower. Pousada Apple House Paraty offers free 30 MB Wi-Fi access throughout and a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

The reception is open 24 hours a day and there is a gym, gourmet area, hot tub and a floating bar.

Pousada Apple House Paraty is 3.7 km from Paraty Bus Station and 4.4 km from Paraty Historic Center.
