Description

All rooms have a practical design and tiled floors. They have air conditioning, ceiling fan, minibar and TV.

You can sunbathe on the pool terrace. Águas de Paratii offers canoes for private use. Massages are also offered. In the hotel there are board games and billiards.

This property is 900 meters from the historic center and 1.5 km from Pontal Beach. Private parking available.
