Description

The accommodation includes a dining area, kitchen, private bathroom with free toiletries and hairdryer, flat-screen cable TV and DVD player. In addition, in the Casa Verde e Mar there is a hot tub, sauna and outdoor pool open all year.

Frade Beach is 11 km from Casa Verde e Mar and Ribeira Beach is 5 km away. Private parking is free.
