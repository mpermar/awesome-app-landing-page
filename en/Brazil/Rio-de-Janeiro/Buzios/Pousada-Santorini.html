Description

The rooms have luxurious tropical décor, offer panoramic sea views and are equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV and a minibar. They also include a cleaning service. Some rooms have a bathtub and hydromassage, balcony and LCD TV with DVD player.

Pousada Santorini is located 1 km from the center of Armação dos Búzios and 1.6 km from its bus station. Azeda beach is about 8 minutes walk. There is free parking and a 24-hour reception where excursions, buggy rides and a car rental service are organized.

João Fernandes is a great choice for travelers interested in relaxation, tranquility and landscapes.
