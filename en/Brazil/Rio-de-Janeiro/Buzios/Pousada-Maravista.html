Description

Maravista's rooms are decorated in bright colors with paintings by local artists. They have plenty of natural light, air conditioning, cable TV and minibar. Some have sea views, hot tub and balcony or patio.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning in a room with panoramic views of the beach. Breakfast includes tropical fruit, natural juice and regional pastries. The à la carte restaurant serves Brazilian dishes and the bar prepares exotic drinks.

The hotel has a games room equipped with pool and ping pong tables. There is also a sauna. Beach service is provided with chairs, umbrellas, food and drink.

Pousada Maravista is 5 km from Rua das Pedras, in the center of Búzios, where you will find many bars, shops and restaurants.

Geribá is a great choice for travelers interested in sandy beaches, walking along the beach and food.
