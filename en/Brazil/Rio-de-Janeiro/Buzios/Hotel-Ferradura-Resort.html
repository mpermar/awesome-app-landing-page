Description

The suites at Hotel Ferradura Resort are elegant and modern and feature an LCD TV, air conditioning, a minibar and a private balcony with panoramic views.

Hotel Ferradura Resort is 70 meters from Ferradura Beach and 4 km from Porto da Barra, where there are many restaurants.

Ferradura is a great choice for travelers interested in nature, relaxation and tranquility.
