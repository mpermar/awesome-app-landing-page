Description

The rooms include a flat-screen satellite TV and a private bathroom with a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer. Some have a seating area. There are also with views of the garden or the sea.

The hotel has an outdoor swimming pool with sun loungers, a bar and sea views and a restaurant serving delicious seafood dishes.

It has a tennis court and gym and is in an ideal area for diving. Organize diving courses and boat trips for a fee. It has a 24-hour reception and car rental service.

It is well located a few meters from the beach, restaurants and shops. The famous Rua das Pedras street is also a short walk away.Hotel El Cazar is 300 meters from Armação Beach and 100 meters from Brigitte Bardot Statue.

Humaitá is a great choice for travelers interested in food, restaurants and relaxation.
