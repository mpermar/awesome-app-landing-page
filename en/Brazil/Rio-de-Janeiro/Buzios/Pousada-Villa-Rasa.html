Description

This boutique hotel is located on the seafront of Praia Rasa, in a quiet area of Búzios, and is well connected to the airport and the Búzios golf club.

The accommodations are distributed on both sides of the street and overlook the garden.

Villa Rasa Marina has spacious rooms and suites, an outdoor pool, an oceanfront bar and a restaurant that offers dishes inspired by Mediterranean cuisine.

The property features sun loungers by the pool and on the beach, fitness center and spa with massages and other treatments.

Villa Rasa is 3.7 km from Turtle Beach and 4.6 km from Amores Beach.

Praia Rasa is a great choice for travelers interested in nature, walking along the beach and landscapes.
