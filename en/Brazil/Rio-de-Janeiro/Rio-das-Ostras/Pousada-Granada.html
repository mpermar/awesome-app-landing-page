Description

The 4-star rooms at Pousada Granada are equipped with a minibar, telephone, air conditioning, cable TV and a ceiling fan.

Facilities include a barbecue area and a games room with table tennis and pool tables. There is also a reading area and an internet cafe.

This property is a 5-minute walk from Lagoa do Iriri and a 20-minute drive from the bus station.
