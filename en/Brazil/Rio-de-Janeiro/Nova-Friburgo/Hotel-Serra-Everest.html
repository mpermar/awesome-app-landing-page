Description

The rooms are classically decorated and feature cable TV, minibar, telephone and private bathroom. Some rooms are bigger.

Hotel Serra Everest, which admits pets, is 300 meters from the main square and 6 km from the Nova Friborg bus station. The city of Rio de Janeiro is 140 km away.
