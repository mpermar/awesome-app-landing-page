Description

The chalets at the Warabi Hotel have quality wooden interiors with an elegant decoration with cream tones. All include a cozy fireplace and some have a hot tub, pool table, private pool and a wine cellar.

During your stay at the Warabi Hotel you can visit the picturesque waterfall of Santa Clara, located less than 2 km from the hotel. The property also offers free Wi-Fi, a library with DVD and massage service.

The Warabi Hotel houses a quality Japanese restaurant, with different types of sake and wine. A Japanese-style breakfast is served daily and a rich breakfast buffet with seasonal fruits is also prepared. The bar area offers snacks and cocktails.

Warabi Hotel is a 3-hour drive from Santos Dumont Airport and offers free parking.
