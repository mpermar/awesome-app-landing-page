Description

Guests of the Vassouras Eco Resort can go hiking on the property's grounds and visit a couple of waterfalls located 2.5 km from the starting point. You can play tennis or billiards and practice extreme sports. There is a children's club and a nightclub.

Rooms at the Vassouras Eco Resort have views of the lake or garden, air conditioning, 32-inch LCD cable TV, safe and minibar.

In the morning a varied buffet breakfast is served, including fresh fruit, cakes and cakes. Brazilian and international dishes are prepared for lunch and dinner.

The spa has a hot tub and offers massages. There are also steam and dry saunas and a Turkish bath.

This 5-star resort is a 2-hour drive from Rio de Janeiro, the state capital. There is free private parking and Wi-Fi, also free, in public areas.
