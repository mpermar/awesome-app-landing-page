Description

With Caldas Novas Yacht Club reachable in 1. 5 km, Riviera Park Apartamentos e Flats offers accommodation, a restaurant, an outdoor swimming pool, a bar and a garden.

Both WiFi and private parking are accessible at the apartment free of charge. All units come with a fully equipped kitchen including a microwave, tiled floors, and a living room with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

For added convenience, the property can provide towels and bed linen for an extra charge.

A buffet breakfast is available every morning at Riviera Park Apartamentos e Flats.

At the accommodation guests are welcome to take advantage of a hot spring bath and a sauna.

Guests can spend time in the water park or enjoy the indoor pool at Riviera Park Apartamentos e Flats.

Acqua Park Di Roma is 2. 8 km from the apartment, while Liberty Square is 700 metres from the property.

The nearest airport is Caldas Novas Airport, 4. 4 km from Riviera Park Apartamentos e Flats.
