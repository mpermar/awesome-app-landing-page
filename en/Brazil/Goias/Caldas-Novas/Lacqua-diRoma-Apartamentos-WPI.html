Description

With Acqua Park Di Roma reachable in 1. 3 km, Lacqua diRoma - Apartamentos WPI provides accommodation, a restaurant, an outdoor swimming pool, a fitness centre and a bar.

The units come with tiled floors and feature a fully equipped kitchenette with a microwave, a dining area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, and a private bathroom with shower and a hairdryer.

A buffet breakfast is available daily at the apartment. Spa and wellness facilities including a hot spring bath, a hammam and a sauna are at guests' disposal during their stay at Lacqua diRoma - Apartamentos WPI.

A water park and a kids' club are available at the accommodation, while guests can also relax in the garden.

Caldas Novas Yacht Club is 5 km from Lacqua diRoma - Apartamentos WPI, while Liberty Square is 2.

9 km away. The nearest airport is Caldas Novas Airport, 3. 3 km from the apartment.
