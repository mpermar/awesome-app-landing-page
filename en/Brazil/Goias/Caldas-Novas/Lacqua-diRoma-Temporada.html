Description

Set in Caldas Novas, 5 km from Caldas Novas Yacht Club, Lacqua diRoma Temporada offers accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre.

Among the various facilities of this property are a bar, a shared lounge and a garden.

The hotel provides an indoor pool, hot spring bath and a 24-hour front desk. The units at the hotel come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and a shower.

All units at Lacqua diRoma Temporada feature air conditioning and a wardrobe. The accommodation offers a range of wellness facilities including a sauna and a hot tub.

You can play billiards and table tennis at Lacqua diRoma Temporada. Acqua Park Di Roma is 5 km from the hotel, while Liberty Square is 4 km away.
