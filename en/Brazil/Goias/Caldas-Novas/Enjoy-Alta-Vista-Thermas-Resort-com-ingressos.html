Description

Enjoy Alta Vista Thermas Resort in Caldas Novas provides accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre.

The hotel has an indoor pool and a shared lounge and free WiFi. Guest rooms in the hotel are fitted with a flat-screen TV.

The rooms are fitted with a private bathroom. Guests at Enjoy Alta Vista Thermas Resort can enjoy a buffet breakfast.
