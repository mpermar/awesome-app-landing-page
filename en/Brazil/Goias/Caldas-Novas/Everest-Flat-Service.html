Description

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool, a bar, and a shared lounge, Everest Flat Service provides accommodation in Caldas Novas with free WiFi and city views.

The air-conditioned accommodation is 1. 4 km from Caldas Novas Yacht Club. Composed of 1 bedroom and 1 bathroom with a shower, this apartment is equipped with a satellite flat-screen TV.

Guests wishing to travel light can make use of towels and bed linen for an extra charge.

A buffet breakfast is available daily at the apartment. Staff at Everest Flat Service are always available to provide guidance at the reception.

The accommodation offers a children's playground. Guests can spend time in the water park or enjoy the indoor pool at Everest Flat Service.

Acqua Park Di Roma is 3 km from the apartment, while Liberty Square is 500 metres away.
