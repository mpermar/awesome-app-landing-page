Description

Featuring free WiFi and a children's playground, Ibis Catanduva offers pet-friendly accommodation in Catanduva.

Guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Free private parking is available on site. All air-conditioned rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

At Ibis, guests will also find a 24-hour front desk. Olímpia is 47 km from Ibis Catanduva, while Termas do Ibirá is 23 km away.
