Description

Providing free WiFi and a fitness centre, Royal Star Thermas Resort is situated in Olímpia, a 9-minute walk from Thermas of Laranjais Water Park.

Featuring a 24-hour front desk, this property also provides guests with a restaurant.

All rooms in the resort are fitted with a flat-screen TV. The private bathroom is equipped with a hair dryer.

The rooms come with a balcony. Some rooms include a spa bath. A buffet breakfast is served every morning at the property.

The property has a sauna and a number of outdoor pools. A 24-hour front-desk is also available.

The nearest airport is Prof. Eribelto Manoel Reino State Airport, 59 km from Royal Star Thermas Resort.
