Description

Situated in Olímpia, the recently inaugurated Enjoy Olimpia Park Resort provides an outdoor swimming pool and a kid's swimming pool.

Equipped with the latest technology and solar heating system, the resort welcomes guests with a restaurant and a children's playground.

The resort features a hot spring bath and valet parking. The spacious suites in the resort are equipped with a smart flat-screen TV with cable channels, air conditioning, WiFi and a minibar.

Guests also benefit from a dining area and an equipped kitchenette, which includes a microwave.

The private bathroom is fitted with a shower and free toiletries. For guest's security, the entrance in the units is enabled via a mobile phone app.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning at the property. Guests can work out at the equipped fitness centre and benefit from private covered parking and two cinema lounges.

A games room is also available for guests entertainment. Thermas of Laranjais Water Park is just 50 metres from the accommodation.

The nearest airport is Prof. Eribelto Manoel Reino State Airport, 52 km from the property.
