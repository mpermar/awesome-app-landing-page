Description

Offering an outdoor pool and spa centre, Hotel Vicino al Mare is located beachfront to Enseada beach in Guarujá.

Free WiFi is offered throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

All the air-conditioned rooms at Hotel Vicino al Mare include a cable TV, a safety deposit box, a telephone and a minibar.

The private bathroom includes a shower. Some units also boast a balcony with sea views.

The suites include a spa bath. The hotel has barbecue facilities and children's playground, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

A daily buffet breakfast is served at the hotel,which includes a wide selection of fresh fruits,cakes,breads,cold cuts,eggs and hot and cold beverages.

You can play billiards at the hotel. Guaruja Bus Station is 4. 4 km from Hotel Vicino al Mare, while Spike of the Beach Harbor is 7 km away.
