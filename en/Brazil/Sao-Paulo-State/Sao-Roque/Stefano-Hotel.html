Description

Stefano Hotel is located in São Roque. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a shared kitchen and a shared lounge, along with free WiFi.

The property provides an outdoor swimming pool, a garden and rooms with garden views.

At the hotel, rooms are equipped with a wardrobe. At Stefano Hotel every room is equipped with a desk, a TV and a private bathroom.

A buffet breakfast is available daily at the accommodation. Stefano Hotel offers a children's playground.

You can play billiards at the hotel. Sorocaba is 44 km from Stefano Hotel, while Barueri is 30 km away.

The nearest airport is Sao Paulo/Congonhas Airport, 56 km from the accommodation.
