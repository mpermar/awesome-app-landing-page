Description

Situated in Bertioga, 26 km from Restingas of Bertioga Estadual Park, Pousada da Morada features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a bar and a garden.

All rooms boast a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom. The hotel has an outdoor swimming pool, entertainment staff and a 24-hour front desk.

At the hotel, rooms come with a balcony. The rooms at Pousada da Morada have air conditioning and a wardrobe.

The accommodation offers a continental or buffet breakfast. Pousada da Morada offers a sun terrace.

You can play billiards and table tennis at the hotel. SESC-Bertioga is 30 km from Pousada da Morada, while Ultraleve Club is 29 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Guarulhos International Airport, 114 km from the accommodation.
