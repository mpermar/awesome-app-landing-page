Description

Pousada Aldeia Manacás features an outdoor swimming pool, a bar, a shared lounge, and a garden in São Bento do Sapucaí.

The accommodation offers an indoor pool, free WiFi throughout the property and family rooms.

The units at the guest house come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and a shower.

All guest rooms feature a wardrobe. Guests at Pousada Aldeia Manacás can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

The accommodation offers a sauna. Guests at Pousada Aldeia Manacás will be able to enjoy activities in and around São Bento do Sapucaí, like hiking.

Monte Verde is 50 km from the guest house, while Campos do Jordao is 46 km away. The nearest airport is Sao Jose Dos Campos Airport, 111 km from Pousada Aldeia Manacás.
