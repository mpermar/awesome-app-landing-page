Description

Plaza Dutra Hotel has a restaurant, fitness centre, a bar and garden in Caçapava.

This 3-star hotel offers room service and a concierge service. The hotel features pool views, an outdoor pool, a 24-hour front desk, and free WiFi is available throughout the property.

At the hotel, rooms include a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. The rooms at Plaza Dutra Hotel have air conditioning and a wardrobe.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at the accommodation. Plaza Dutra Hotel offers a sun terrace.

You can play billiards at the hotel, and car hire is available. São José dos Campos is 23 km from Plaza Dutra Hotel, while Taubaté is 21 km from the property.

The nearest airport is São José dos Campos, 24 km from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
