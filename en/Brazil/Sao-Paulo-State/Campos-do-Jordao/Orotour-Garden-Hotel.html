Description

On a forest reservation high on Campos do Jordão's mountain, the European-style Orotour features an indoor heated pool and a tennis court.

WiFi and parking are free. Orotour Garden Hotel´s 4-star rooms are fitted with framed artwork and a touch of colour.

They provide TV, heating, telephone and minibar. Guests enjoy a rich buffet breakfast with fresh fruits and homemade cakes at Capriccio Restaurant, as well as fondues and regional dishes.

La Cave Bar offers a good selection of wine. For relaxation, guests at Orotour Garden can have a massage or a sauna session.

For the more active, there is a modern gym and sports courts. Children can have fun at the playground.

Orotour Garden Hotel is situated just 4 km from Capivari district, the tourist area where visitors find bars, restaurants and souvenir shops selling regional arts and crafts.
