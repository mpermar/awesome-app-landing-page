Description

Águas de Lindóia’s Plazza Hotel is located 450 metres from Adhemar de Barros Square and offers indoor and outdoor pools, a sauna and full board.

Wi-Fi in public areas and private parking are free. All rooms are decorated with light colours and come with a TV, minibar and private bathroom with a hot shower.

The deluxe rooms have air conditioning and a flat-screen TV. The restaurant serves regional dishes for breakfast, lunch and dinner, as well as a selection of drinks.

Other Plazza Hotel facilities include a games room, TV room, and room service. An outdoor play area is available 3 km away from the property, a shuttle service is available for guests every morning.

Plazza Hotel is located on central Águas de Lindóia, 1 km from Balneario Municipal public thermal water park.
