Description

Located in Aguas de Lindoia, within 30 meters of Adhemar de Barros Square and 1.1 km from Ancient Redeemer Christ, Hotel Monte Real provides water sports facilities.

Each accommodation at the 4-star hotel has mountain views, and guests can enjoy access to an outdoor swimming pool and to a hot spring bath.

The hotel has an indoor pool, sauna and a 24-hour front desk. All guest rooms in the hotel are fitted with a flat-screen TV.

Each room is equipped with a private bathroom with a shower. The rooms at Hotel Monte Real include a wardrobe.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental or a buffet breakfast. The wellness area at Hotel Monte Real is comprised of a fitness centre and you can play tennis in one of the four courts available at the hotel.

A business centre is at guests' disposal at Hotel Monte Real. King Christ Church is 400 metres from the accommodation, while Good Cheese Center is 1 km away.
