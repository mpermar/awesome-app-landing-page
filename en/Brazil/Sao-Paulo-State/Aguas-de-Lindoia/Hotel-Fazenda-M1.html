Description

Featuring an outdoor pool, M1 Farm Hotel offers accommodation in Aguas de Lindóia, 1.

9 km from Ancient Redentor Christ. The hotel has a children's playground and sauna, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Each room comes with a flat-screen TV. A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms.

Each room has a private bathroom equipped with a shower. You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property.

You can play tennis, table tennis and billiards at this hotel. Adhemar de Barros Square is 2.

8 km from Hotel Fazenda M1, while Good Cheese Center is 2 km away. Viracopos International Airport is 82 km from the property.
