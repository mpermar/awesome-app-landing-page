Description

Only 1 km from Serra Negra bus station, Rovi Plaza Hotel boasts indoor and outdoor swimming pools, a children playground and fitness centre, as well as massage treatments and a games room.

All rooms at Rovi Plaza are decorated with tiled floors and light colours, and offer free WiFi access.

There is a balcony, TV with cable channels, minibar and a private bathroom with a hot shower.

The daily breakfast buffet offers a variety of fresh fruits, breads and cold meats.

Guests can also enjoy regional specialties, as well as a selection of drinks in the hotel’s restaurant and bar.

Also available is a tennis court, meeting facilities and laundry services. The hotel also offers facilities for disabled guests.

It is only 500 metres from Serra Negra centre. Disneylândia dos Robôs is 1. 5 km from Rovi Plaza Hotel, while Guarulhos International Airport is 150 km away.
