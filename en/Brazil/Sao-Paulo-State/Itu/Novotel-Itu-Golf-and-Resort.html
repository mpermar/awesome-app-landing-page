Description

Novotel Itu Golf & Resort offers an outdoor swimming pool, a fitness centre and a convention centre for up to 1.

200 people. Free WiFi is available throughout for guests' convenience. Rooms at Novotel Itu Golf & Resort are modern, colourful and air-conditioned.

They offer a minibar, a work desk and a telephone. Some offer a seating area and a balcony.

Room service is available to all guests. A buffet breakfast is served every morning at the property, offering a variety of home made delicacies such as cakes, breads, jams and juices.

For lunch and dining options, guests can choose from Raro Restaurant, serving international cuisine, where guests can eat while appreciating views to the golf court.

Novotel Itu Golf & Resort provides a 24-hour front desk, a spa and wellness centre, a sauna and a kids' club.

Guests can benefit from the golf course, the tennis court and the 20. 000 square metres of green area.

Sorocaba is 33 km from Novotel Itu Golf & Resort, while Jundiaí is 53 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Viracopos International Airport, 52 km from the accommodation.
