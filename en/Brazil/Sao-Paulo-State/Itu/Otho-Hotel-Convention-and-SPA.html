Description

Otho Hotel Convention & SPA is located in Itu, 50 km from Campinas. Within the property, you will find the Spa Sport, offering all spa services and leisure structure of the hotel.

Guests can also enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar. You will find a 24-hour front desk and gift shop at the property.

You can play tennis and table tennis at this hotel, and the area is popular for hiking.

Sorocaba is 29 km from Otho Hotel Convention & SPA, while Jundiaí is 49 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Viracopos International Airport, 36 km from Otho Hotel Convention & SPA.
