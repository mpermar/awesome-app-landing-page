Description

Located in Taubate in the Sao Paulo State Region, 8 km from Sitio do Pica-Pau Amarelo, Ibis Styles Taubate boasts an outdoor pool.

Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. Free private parking is available on site.

Every room is equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels, air conditioning, telephone, hairdryer, desk and a minibar.

All rooms include a private bathroom equipped with a shower. Extras include free toiletries.

Ibis Styles Taubate features free WiFi throughout the property. There is a 24-hour front desk, a shared lounge and gift shop at the property.
