Description

Located in Bragança Paulista, Hotel Mil Flores features a bar, garden, terrace, and free WiFi.

Featuring a shared lounge, this property also welcomes guests with a restaurant, a water park and a year-round outdoor pool.

The resort provides an indoor pool, fitness centre and karaoke. At the resort every room comes with air conditioning, a desk, a balcony with a garden view, a private bathroom, a flat-screen TV, bed linen and towels.

All units include a wardrobe. A buffet breakfast is available daily at Hotel Mil Flores.

The accommodation offers a children's playground. You can play billiards, table tennis and mini-golf at Hotel Mil Flores, and the area is popular for hiking.

Atibaia is 34 km from the resort, while Extrema is 44 km away. The nearest airport is Viracopos International Airport, 74 km from Hotel Mil Flores.
