Description

The rooms are simply decorated and feature a clothes rack, flat-screen TV, minibar, balcony, hammock and mountain views.

The spa facilities include a hot spring bath and sauna. Pousada Penhasco has a restaurant, a bar and a business center where meetings can be organized. The bar serves exotic drinks.

This property is 2.5 km from the center of Chapada dos Guimarães and 80 km from Cuiabá International Airport. Covered parking is free.
