Description

All rooms in the complex have a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a balcony and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a shower. Some rooms at the Malai Manso Resort Hotel Chapada have mountain views.

Buffet breakfast is served in the breakfast area.

The accommodation has a sauna. Activities near the Malai Manso Resort Iate Golf Convention & Spa include hiking.

The reception staff provides information about the area.

Marechal Rondon International Airport, the nearest airport, is 88 km away.
