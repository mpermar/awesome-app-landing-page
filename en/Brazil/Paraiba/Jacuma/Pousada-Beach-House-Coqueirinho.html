Description

Some rooms have a balcony or terrace, and Pousada Beach House Coqueirinho offers free Wi-Fi throughout the property. A flat-screen TV is offered.

There is a 24-hour reception on the premises.

Pousada Beach House Coqueirinho is 47 km from Itamaracá, 40 km from Cabedelo and 27 km from the nearest Presidente Castro Pinto International Airport.
