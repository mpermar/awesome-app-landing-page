Description

Set in João Pessoa, 200 metres from Tambau beach, Solar Tambau Residencial has an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre.

A sauna is available for guests. Facilities include a children's playground and free WiFi.

Air-conditioned rooms offer a balcony with a view, as well as a flat-screen TV, a sofa and a safety deposit box.

Selected rooms also feature a kitchen with a microwave. The hotel offers a terrace and a wellness centre.

Sun loungers, beach chairs and sun umbrellas are offered for guests' benefit. Handicraft Market is 1 km from Solar Tambau Residencial.

The nearest airport is Presidente Castro Pinto International Airport, 14 km from the accommodation.
