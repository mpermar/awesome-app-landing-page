Description

Featuring a bar and a garden, Hotel Fazenda Triunfo is set in Areia. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a 24-hour front desk and room service, along with free WiFi.

The hotel features an outdoor swimming pool, evening entertainment and a concierge service.

Guests at the hotel can enjoy a continental breakfast. Hotel Fazenda Triunfo offers a children's playground.

You can play billiards and table tennis at the accommodation. The nearest airport is President Joao Suassuna Airport, 57 km from Hotel Fazenda Triunfo.
