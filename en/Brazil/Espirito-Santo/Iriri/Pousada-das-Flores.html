Description

The rooms have simple décor and enjoy views of the sea and the mountains. All have TV, air conditioning, balcony and minibar.

A buffet breakfast with a variety of fresh fruit, breads and cold meats and a selection of hot and cold drinks is served every morning in the breakfast room.

The Aguico Salles Eurico Airport is 77 km away.
