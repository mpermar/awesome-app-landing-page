Description

The accommodation at the Fazenda Flamboyant Hotel is equipped with air conditioning, dark wood furniture and its walls have a touch of color. They also have free Wi-Fi, TV, telephone and minibar.

The buffet breakfast includes tropical fruit, various types of bread and freshly baked cakes. Lunch and dinner menus include regional cuisine, with typical seafood dishes such as stews and empanadas.

Guests can play tennis or soccer, go hiking or use the gym. Farm activities for children are offered and there is an entertainment team. To relax, the hotel offers a games room and a sauna.

The center of Guarapari is 20 km from the hotel and the city bus station is 40 km away. The Acquamania water park is just 50 meters away.
