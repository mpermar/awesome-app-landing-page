Description

Monte Verde Golf & Resort Hotel has a restaurant, a bar, a shared lounge and a garden in Vargem Alta.

All rooms boast a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom. There is an outdoor pool and guests can make use of free WiFi and free private parking.

At the hotel, the rooms have a balcony. All rooms at Hotel Fazenda Monte Verde Golf & Resort have air conditioning and a wardrobe.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet or a à la carte breakfast. Hotel Fazenda Monte Verde Golf & Resort offers a children's playground.

You can play billiards, mini-golf and tennis at the hotel, and the area is popular for hiking and fishing.

Pedra Azul is 10 km from Hotel Fazenda Monte Verde Golf & Resort. The nearest airport is Victoria Airport, 108 km from the accommodation.
