Description

The Ecoland offers rooms with balcony, air conditioning, cable TV, minibar and bathroom. All are elegantly decorated with a sofa, table, chairs and desk.

A breakfast with fresh fruit, bread and sausages is served every morning. In the hotel restaurant you can enjoy regional specialties and various drinks.

You can go horse riding, fishing and cycling. The establishment has a football and volleyball court, games room and 2 TV rooms.

It is 7 km from the city bus terminal and 22 km from Laranjeiras Park. The center of Gramado and Canela are 35 km away.
