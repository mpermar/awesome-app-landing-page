Description

Vestena Hotel Canela has a restaurant, bar, a shared lounge and garden in Canela.

Among the facilities at this property are a shared kitchen and room service, along with free WiFi throughout the property.

The hotel features an indoor pool, fitness centre, evening entertainment and a 24-hour front desk.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at the hotel. Stone Church is 2.

1 km from Vestena Hotel Canela, while Mundo a Vapor is 2. 1 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Hugo Cantergiani Regional Airport, 73 km from the accommodation.
