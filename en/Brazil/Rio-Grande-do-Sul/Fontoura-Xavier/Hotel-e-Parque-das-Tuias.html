Description

Tuias Hotel and Park features a restaurant, fitness center, a bar and garden in Fontoura Xavier.

Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a children's playground.

The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, a kids' club and organising tours for guests.

At the hotel, each room comes with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

Guest rooms at Hotel e Parque das Tuias include air conditioning and a wardrobe. A à la carte breakfast is available daily at the accommodation.

Hotel e Parque das Tuias offers a sun terrace. You can play tennis at the hotel, and the area is popular for hiking.
