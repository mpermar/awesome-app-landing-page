Description

Located in Nova Petrópolis, 3 km from Flower Square, Hotel Jardins da Colina provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre.

Among the various facilities of this property are a bar, a shared lounge and a garden.

The property features an indoor pool, free WiFi and a 24-hour front desk. Guest rooms are equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fridge, a kettle, a shower, a hairdryer and a wardrobe.

At the hotel, each room has a private bathroom with free toiletries. Guests at Hotel Jardins da Colina can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

The accommodation offers a terrace. You can play billiards and table tennis at Hotel Jardins da Colina.

Imigrant Valley Park is 4. 3 km from the hotel, while Imigrant Mall is 3. 4 km away.

The nearest airport is Hugo Cantergiani Regional Airport, 32 km from Hotel Jardins da Colina.
