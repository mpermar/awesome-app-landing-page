Description

Some rooms have a seating area, and all are equipped with a flat-screen TV and DVD player. There are rooms with views of the mountains, the pool or the garden. The rooms also have a private bathroom with bathrobes and slippers.

The establishment has a 24-hour reception and hairdresser.

This hotel has a tennis court and the area is perfect for hiking. There is car rental service. The Kurotel Medical Center is 1.4 km from the Palace of Festivals and St. Peter's Church. The nearest airport is Salgado Filho Airport, located 115 km away.
