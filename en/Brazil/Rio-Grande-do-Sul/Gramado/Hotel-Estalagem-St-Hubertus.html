Description

The suites at Estalagem St. Hubertus are cozy and feature classic wooden floors, elaborate white fabrics, air conditioning and LCD TV. All offer views of the green hills.

St. Hubertus is 3 km from Sierra Park and features a gym, heated pool with waterfall, massage service and a library with bar and fireplace service.

A breakfast with fresh fruits, juice, bread and pastries is served every morning on the terrace, which overlooks the lake. Gramado houses several gastronomic restaurants with Thai, Italian and French cuisine.

St. Hubertus Estalagem is 2 km from the center of Gramado and 109 km from Salgado Filho Airport.
