Description

All rooms at Pousada Betânia have a fan, TV and minibar. They feature simple-style décor with dark wood furniture. They also have a private bathroom.

The hotel serves a daily buffet breakfast in the breakfast room, which includes fruit, bread, cake, sausage and a selection of hot and cold drinks.

The property is 500 meters from Mini Mundo Park and 800 meters from Black Lake.
