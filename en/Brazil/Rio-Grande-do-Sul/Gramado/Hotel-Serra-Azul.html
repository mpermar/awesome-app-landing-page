Description

The accommodations are classically decorated and feature air conditioning, cable TV, a minibar and a bathroom. The suites are more spacious and have a seating area with a sofa.

A buffet breakfast is served daily, including a variety of fruits, breads and cakes. The restaurant serves regional and international dishes. The bar features cozy wood décor.

Hotel Serra Azul is 8 km from the Canela flight path and 120 km from Salgado Filho International Airport in Porto Alegre. There is public parking.
