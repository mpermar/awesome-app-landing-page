Description

Wyndham Gramado Termas Resort & Spa features a restaurant, fitness centre, a bar and shared lounge in Gramado.

Among the facilities at this property are a kids' club and a concierge service, along with free WiFi throughout the property.

The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.

At the hotel, every room includes a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

The units at Wyndham Gramado Termas Resort & Spa feature air conditioning and a wardrobe.

The accommodation offers a continental or buffet breakfast. Wyndham Gramado Termas Resort & Spa offers 5-star accommodation with a sauna and hot tub.

You can play mini-golf at the hotel. Black Lake Gramado is 6 km from Wyndham Gramado Termas Resort & Spa, while Mini Mundo is 7 km away.

The nearest airport is Hugo Cantergiani Regional Airport, 59 km from the accommodation.
