Description

Rooms at the Park Hotel Termas Machadinho have a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bidet. Some have a balcony.

A buffet breakfast is served daily.

Guests can make use of the hot spring bath and fitness center, and cycling and fishing can be enjoyed in the area.

The reception staff is available 24 hours a day and speaks Spanish and Portuguese.

Joaçaba is 48 km away.
