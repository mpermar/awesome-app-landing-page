Description

To ensure the comfort of its guests, the establishment offers bicycles for free. The surroundings offer a magnificent environment for hiking. Among the facilities of the establishment, it is worth mentioning a soccer field, a mini-golf and several tennis and volleyball courts. There is also a massage service. The property serves a buffet breakfast. There is also a bar and a restaurant, where guests can taste a wide variety of dishes. The property also houses a children's playground.

Rooms at Fazenda Fiori are decorated in bright colors and feature air conditioning, minibar, safe and TV. They also have a closet and a balcony overlooking the garden. In addition, Wi-Fi is available throughout the property, for a fee.

The center of Paripuera is located just 2 km away and the center of Macei, 30 km away. Zumbi dos Palmares Airport is 32 km away. In addition, there is free parking.
