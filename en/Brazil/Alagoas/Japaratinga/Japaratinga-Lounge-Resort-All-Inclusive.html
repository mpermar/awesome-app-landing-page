Description

Japaratinga Lounge Resort - All Inclusive features air-conditioned accommodation in Japaratinga.

Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, room service and a kids' club, along with free WiFi.

The resort has an outdoor swimming pool, fitness centre, evening entertainment and a 24-hour front desk.

At the resort, all rooms are equipped with a balcony. At Japaratinga Lounge Resort rooms are equipped with a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

Maragogi is 10 km from Japaratinga Lounge Resort - All Inclusive, while São Miguel dos Milagres is 27 km from the property.
