Description

The rooms are decorated in beach style and include a balcony or patio, air conditioning, TV, minibar and private bathroom. Some are more spacious and have a safe.

The restaurant serves a daily buffet breakfast and various regional specialties. Guests will find a barbecue area next to the garden.

Pousada Paraíso dos Coqueirais is located just 500 meters from the center of Japaratinga, next to a lake and in front of a bus stop. The city of Maragogi is 12 km from the property, which provides free private parking. The nearest airport is Recife International Airport, located 132 km away.
