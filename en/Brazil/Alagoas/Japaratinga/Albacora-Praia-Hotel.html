Description

The rooms have a balcony, air conditioning, satellite TV, minibar and private bathroom. All offer elegant tropical-style décor and feature room service. Some include picturesque sea views and LCD TV. Childcare is also available upon request.

Albacora Praia Hotel is located just 300 meters from the center of Japaratinga and 10 km from Maragogi Beach. Zumbi dos Palmares International Airport is 135 km away, in Maceió. There is free private parking.

The restaurant serves a selection of Brazilian and Portuguese specialties, as well as a daily breakfast buffet. You can also enjoy refreshing drinks at the hotel's elegant pool.
