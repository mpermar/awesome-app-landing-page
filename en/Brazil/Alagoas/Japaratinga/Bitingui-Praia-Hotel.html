Description

The rooms offer views of the sea or the garden, are decorated in bright colors, are spacious and bright and feature rustic décor. They have a seating area, patio with hammock, air conditioning, TV and minibar.

The daily buffet breakfast includes a variety of fresh fruit, bread and sausages. The hotel restaurant offers regional specialties and a selection of drinks.

The Bitigui Praia Hotel has a barbecue area, a games room and a sports court. The 24-hour reception staff speaks several languages and will provide information about the area and airport shuttle service. Maceió is 120 km from the hotel.
