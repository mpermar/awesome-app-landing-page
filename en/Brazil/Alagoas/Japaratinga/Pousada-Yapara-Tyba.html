Description

The rooms are decorated with splashes of color and feature cable TV and a balcony overlooking the pool. The private bathroom includes a walk-in shower. The property provides room service.

Pousada Yapara-Tyba, surrounded by tall palm trees and a large garden, offers rustic-style beach accommodations. Guests can relax on the sun loungers around the outdoor pool or check with the tour desk to visit places of interest in the area.

The center of Japaratinga is 3 km away, and the Nossa Senhora das Candeias church is 1 km away. There is a manatee sanctuary at a distance of 5 km. The Zumbi dos Palmares International Airport, located in Maceió, is 115 km away.
