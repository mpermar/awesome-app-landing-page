Description

Hotel Aconchego do Velho Chico features a restaurant, outdoor swimming pool, a bar and shared lounge in Piranhas.

With a garden, the 4-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi. There is free private parking and the property provides paid airport shuttle service.

At the hotel, every room has a wardrobe and a TV. A continental breakfast is available daily at Hotel Aconchego do Velho Chico.

The accommodation offers a terrace. The area is popular for hiking and cycling, and car hire is available at Hotel Aconchego do Velho Chico.
