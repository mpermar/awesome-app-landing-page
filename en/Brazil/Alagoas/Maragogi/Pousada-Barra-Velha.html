Description

The Barra Velha villas have a fully equipped kitchenette, air-conditioned bedrooms, a minibar, a TV and a DVD player.

The hotel's leisure area has a lush garden with palm trees and a grassy sports field. There are also natural pools and boat trips or canoeing can be organized.

Massages, beach services and exotic cocktails are offered on the beach.

Pousada Barra Velha is 13 km from the center of Maragogi and 500 meters from the bridge over the Persinunga River.
