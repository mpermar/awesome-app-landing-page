Description

The chalets feature views of the pool and the sea, air conditioning, flat-screen TV, cable channels, patio, full kitchen with fridge and kitchenware and private bathroom with shower.

Prive Costa Dourada has a communal sauna, garden, barbecue area and games room. Various activities, such as diving, can be practiced in the establishment and its surroundings. The accommodation also has free private parking.

The property is 5.1 km from Wales' natural pools and 90 km from Zumbi dos Palmares International Airport in Maceió.
