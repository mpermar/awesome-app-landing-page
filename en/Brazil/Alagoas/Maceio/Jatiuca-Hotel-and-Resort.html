Description

Its rooms have an architecture that integrates nature with modernity and includes air conditioning and TV. Some also offer a balcony overlooking the pool or garden.

The Jatiúca Hotel has been remodeled and includes tennis courts, games room, massage service, sand court to play soccer and volleyball and zip-line areas and rock and tree climbing. There is also a team of animators that organizes activities for the whole family. Children also benefit from the services of recreational staff.

There are several restaurants, overlooking the ocean or the 62,000 m² of tropical gardens with a natural pond. Drinks and snacks are served on the pool terrace and on the private beach.

Jatiúca Hotel is a 25-minute drive from the airport and provides free parking.
