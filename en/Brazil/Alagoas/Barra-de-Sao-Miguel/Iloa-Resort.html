Description

The rooms at the Iloa Resort are spacious and elegantly decorated. They feature a flat-screen cable TV, a seating area with a sofa and a table, a private bathroom and a balcony overlooking the garden. Some also include an equipped kitchen.

Mabu Iloa Resort is 45 km from Zumbi dos Palmares International Airport, 25 km from Maceió Bus Station and 20 km from the center of Maceió. It is also 5 km from Praia do Frances and 3 km from Barra de São Miguel beach.

The property has a bar and a restaurant. A buffet breakfast with tropical products is served every morning.
