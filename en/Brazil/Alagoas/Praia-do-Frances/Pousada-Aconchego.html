Description

Rooms at Pousada Aconchego Hotel are bright and feature colorful bedding. Each private bathroom offers a shower. The rooms also include a TV and a minibar.

Pousada Aconchego offers an outdoor pool and barbecue facilities. Guests can relax in the TV and games room. The children's play area houses a variety of toys.

The center of Marechal Deodoro is 5 km from Pousada Aconchego. You can reach the shops and restaurants of Maceió in 15 minutes by car.
