Description

Rooms at Hotel Sete Ilhas feature designer furniture and a 26-inch flat-screen TV with cable channels. They also have a lounge, kitchenette and dining area.

Guests can prepare their own meal or enjoy a selection of regional dishes, served in the hotel restaurant. There are also many bars and cafes nearby.

The Sete Ilhas hotel is 23 km from the center of Florianopolis. Free private parking is provided.

Jurerê is a great choice for travelers interested in tranquility, walking along the beach and food.
