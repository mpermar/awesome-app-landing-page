Description

Some of the rooms at Il Campanário Villagio Resort include a kitchen equipped with a stove. Some of the suites' balconies offer scenic views of the Atlantic Ocean. The rooms are equipped with elegant and modern accessories.

Il Campanário Villagio Resort offers its guests the option to relax in the spa sauna and enjoy a massage. The complex offers other facilities such as a gym.

Local and international dishes are served in the restaurant of the Il Campanário Villagio Resort. The hotel bar offers drinks and snacks.

San Jose da Ponta Grossa Fort is 3.5 km from Il Campanario Villagio Resort.

Jurerê is a great choice for travelers interested in tranquility, walking along the beach and food.
