Description

The rooms in this complex have air conditioning and a flat-screen cable TV. Some of them have a seating area. Coffee maker is also provided. In addition, a hairdryer and free toiletries are provided.

This accommodation has a 24-hour reception and hairdresser.

The complex has a ping pong table. Studio em Resort 5 estrelas is 13 km from Floripa Mall and 17 km from Florianópolis Iguatemi Mall. The nearest airport is Florianópolis-Hercílio Luz, 25 km away.

Jurerê is a great choice for travelers interested in tranquility, walking along the beach and food.
