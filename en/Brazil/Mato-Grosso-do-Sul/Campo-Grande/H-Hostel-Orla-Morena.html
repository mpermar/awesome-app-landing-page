Description

Set in Campo Grande, 6 km from University Stadium Pedro Pedrossian, H Hostel Orla Morena offers accommodation with free bikes, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a shared lounge.

9 km from State Park and 2. 4 km from Jose Octavio Guizzo Cultural Center, the property features a garden and barbecue facilities.

The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, a shared kitchen and free WiFi throughout the property.

At the hostel, all rooms come with a shared bathroom with a shower. H Hostel Orla Morena offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

The accommodation offers a terrace. Santo Antonio Church is 2. 8 km from H Hostel Orla Morena, while Obelisk is 3 km away.

The nearest airport is Campo Grande International Airport, 10 km from the hostel.
