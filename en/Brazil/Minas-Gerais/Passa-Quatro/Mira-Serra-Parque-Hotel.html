Description

Offering a spa centre and hot tub, Mira Serra Parque Hotel is set in Passa Quatro, 99 km from Penedo.

The hotel has an outdoor pool and sauna, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Free private parking is available on site. Each room has a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, blu-ray player and DVD player.

Rooms have a private bathroom. Mira Serra Parque Hotel features free WiFi throughout the property.

There is a 24-hour front desk and a shared lounge. You can play tennis, table tennis and billiards at this hotel, and bike hire is available.

Sao Lourenco is 52 km from Mira Serra Parque Hotel, while Visconde De Maua is 123 km away.
