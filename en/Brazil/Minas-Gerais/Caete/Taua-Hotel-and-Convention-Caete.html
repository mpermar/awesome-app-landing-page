Description

The rooms of the Tauá Hotel are decorated with neutral colors and framed pictures. All include air conditioning, balcony, minibar, TV and Wi-Fi connection.

The Tauá has a bowling alley, soccer field, cinema and games room. There is also a children's club that organizes circus activities.

The complex has 3 restaurants and 2 bars, where regional specialties, good meat, exotic cocktails and a rich buffet breakfast with 80 dishes are served.

The Tauá Hotel's spa has a sauna and offers massages. There is also a modern gym.

Tauá Hotel & Convention Caeté is 7 km from the center of Caeté and 45 km from Belo Horizonte. The complex has free parking.
