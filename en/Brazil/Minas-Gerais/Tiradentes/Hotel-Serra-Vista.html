Description

Featuring a sauna, playground and a pool with a waterslide, Serra Vista is 350 metres from Tiradentes’ Maria Fumaça train and right across Libertas.

Surrounded gardens, it offers free Wi-Fi and buffet breakfast at the restaurant. Offering hill views, rooms at Hotel Serra Vista offer air conditioning, a TV, a telephone and a minibar.

The private bathroom includes a shower. This pet-friendly hotel is 20 km from São João del Rey Airport, and Tiradentes bus station is reachable within a 12-minute walk.
