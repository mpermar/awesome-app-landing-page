Description

This charming colonial mansion is filled with unique hand-sculpted antiques, which guests can purchase and take home.

Its complete leisure facilities include one outdoor pool, one indoor heated pool, a sauna and a modern gym.

Guests at Pousada Pequena Tiradentes can visit Santo Antonio church or Padre Toledo Museum, both 3 km away.

They can also have a massage or use the indoor pool featuring a waterfall and a hot tub.

The classical rooms at Pequena Tiradentes have elegant cream décor with fine Egyptian cotton sheets and antique furniture.

All have air conditioning, an LCD TV and minibar, while some offer a cosy fireplace and a hot tub.

A breakfast buffet with croissants, eggs and jams is served. Guests can enjoy delicious regional cuisine and classic cocktails and wines at the restaurant, decorated with tasteful country furniture.

Pequena Tiradentes Pousada is 1. 4 km from Largo das Forras. It offers free WiFi and complimentary parking.
