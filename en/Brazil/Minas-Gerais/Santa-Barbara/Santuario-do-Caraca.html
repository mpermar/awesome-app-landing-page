Description

Situated in Santa Bárbara, 700 metres from Caraca Sanctuary, Santuário do Caraça features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a bar and a shared lounge.

The hotel has a garden and provides a children's playground. At the hotel, the rooms are fitted with a desk.

All rooms have a wardrobe. Guests at Santuário do Caraça can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

You can play billiards at the accommodation, and the area is popular for hiking. The nearest airport is Belo Horizonte/Pampulha – Carlos Drummond de Andrade Airport, 119 km from Santuário do Caraça.
