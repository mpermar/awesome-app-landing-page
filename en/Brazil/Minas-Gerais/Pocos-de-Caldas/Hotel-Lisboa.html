Description

The rooms are bright and brightly decorated with light wood floors. They are equipped with a minibar, TV, ceiling fan and private bathroom. Some include a flat-screen TV with DVD player.

The Lisboa hotel has a games room with a pool table and table football, as well as a children's area with a variety of games.

The hotel is 1 km from the Thermas Antonio Carlos spa, 5 km from the Poços de Caldas bus station and a 7-minute walk from Pedro Sanches Square.
