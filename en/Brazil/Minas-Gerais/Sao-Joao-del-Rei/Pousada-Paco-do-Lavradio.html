Description

Rooms at Paço do Lavradio have beautiful local-style décor and wooden floors. All are equipped with air conditioning, TV, minibar and a private bathroom with hot shower.

The hotel has a reading area with a large fireplace and several seating areas in the garden and orchard. In addition, it has a volleyball court and board games.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning in the breakfast room, which includes a variety of local specialties cooked in a wood-fired oven, as well as a selection of hot and cold drinks.

Paço do Lavradio is located 3 km from the historic center of São João del Rei and the Octavio de Almeida Neves Airport, 2 km from the bus terminal and about 20 minutes by car from the city of Tiradentes.
