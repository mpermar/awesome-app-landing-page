Description

The rooms are spacious and bright, offer views of the garden and have a fan, cable TV, telephone, minibar and private bathroom with shower.

Parque das Águas is 5 km away. Hotel Fazenda Vista Alegre is just 1 km from São Lourenço Airport and 7 km from São Lourenço Bus Station.
