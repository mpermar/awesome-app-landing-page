Description

Offering an outdoor and an indoor pool, Hotel Guanabara is located in São Lourenço.

The hotel also boasts a restaurant and on-site parking. Free WiFi access is available.

Each guestroom features a TV, air conditioning and a minibar. Private bathroom also comes with a walk-in shower and bath towels.

Extras include bed linens. Facilities also include a tennis court and a fitness centre.

Other facilities offered at the property include meeting facilities, a shared lounge and a games room.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including a water park.

Hotel Guanabara is 2.4 km from Sao Lourenco Bus Station, 300 meters from Dom Pedro II Avenue and 550 meters from Águas Park.
