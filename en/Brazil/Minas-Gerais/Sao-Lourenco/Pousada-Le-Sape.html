Description

Surrounded by nature, Pousada Le Sapê has a large pool with water slides,a thermal swimming pool, sauna and games room.

Located 2. 5 km from São Lourenço centre, it has a gym, a playground and bikes can be provided.

Each room features a flat-screen TV with cable channels, air conditioning and a private bathroom with a hot shower and hairdryer.

There is also a minibar, desk and free Wi-Fi. We have heating in all rooms. The daily breakfast buffet offers a variety of fresh fruits, breads and cold meats.

Guests can also enjoy regional specialties, as well as a selection of drinks in the guesthouse’s restaurant.
