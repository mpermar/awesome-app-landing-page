Description

Located in trendy São Paulo Street, in the Lourdes neighborhood, Hotel Fasano Belo Horizonte is the first Fasano Group venture in the city.

The Hotel features a spa with wellness pool, sauna and a fitness center. Complimentary WiFi is offered throughout.

The Fasano tradition and excellence in more than 100 years of gastronomy is one of the greatest differentials of the hotel, which houses the Gero restaurant.

The hotel also features the Baretto, the number 1 bar by the Wallpaper Magazine. Guests can enjoy a meeting point equipped for events, business meetings, celebrations, and a great gastronomic experience.

Guests can also benefit from the rich buffet breakfast served daily. Hotel Fasano Belo Horizonte features an architectural design signed by the renowned Bernardes Arquitectura.

Belo Horizonte's Central Market is 1.3 km from the property. Tancredo Neves International Airport is 43 km from Hotel Fasano Belo Horizonte.
