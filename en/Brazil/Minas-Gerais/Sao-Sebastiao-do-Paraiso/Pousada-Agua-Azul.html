Description

Rooms at Pousada Água Azul are equipped with a flat-screen TV and some have views of the mountains, the pool or the garden. All rooms have a private bathroom and there is free Wi-Fi in all areas.

The establishment has a shared lounge.

Various activities, such as horse riding and hiking, can be practiced in the area. Passos is 27 km from Pousada Água Azul, while Termopolis is 13 km away.
