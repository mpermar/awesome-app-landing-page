Description

Guests can fish in the property's lake, go horseback riding and go hiking (both guided), milk cows in the morning and feed the chickens.

Varginha Airport is 140 km away, and the property is 10 km from Cruzilia city center and its bus terminal. The Cachoeira dos Garcias waterfall and the city center of Aiuroca are 30 km away.

The rooms are simple and cozy and equipped with wardrobe, wooden floor and private bathroom.

The buffet breakfast includes numerous fresh products, and guests can enjoy regional cuisine and drinks in the restaurant.
