Description

Located in Ouro Preto and with Sao Francisco de Paula Church reachable within 1 km, É Hostel provides a tour desk, non-smoking rooms, a shared lounge, free WiFi throughout the property and a garden.

Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a barbecue. The accommodation features karaoke and a shared kitchen.

At the hostel, rooms include a patio. At É Hostel, each room is fitted with a seating area.

An American breakfast is available daily at the accommodation. É Hostel offers a terrace.

Popular points of interest near the hostel include St. Francis of Assisi Church, Oratorio Museum and Nossa Senhora do Carmo Church.
