Description

The 4-star rooms offer views of Caxambu, Parque das Águas or the hotel's leisure area. They all have classic décor and are equipped with free Wi-Fi, TV, telephone and minibar. Some also include an LCD TV and a seating area.

Hotel Glória's restaurant has a colonial style and serves a daily buffet breakfast. Here, guests can taste typical regional dishes and international specialties.

The wellness center features a Scottish shower, sauna, gym and hot tub. There is also a playground, movie theater and children's playroom.

The hotel is located in the center of Caxambu and its staff organizes excursions in the area and locomotive rides. Parking is free and the hotel is only 500 meters from the city bus station.
