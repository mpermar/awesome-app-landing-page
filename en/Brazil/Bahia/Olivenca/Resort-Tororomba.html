Description

The rooms at the Tororomba Resort feature a functional design and are equipped with air conditioning, private balcony and satellite TV.

The Tororomba Resort puts at your disposal many facilities and services, such as water slides, a natural lake with canoes and pedal skates and a children's area with entertainment.

If you wish, you can rent a bicycle, use the sports track or go hiking in the ecological jungle behind the complex.

It houses 2 buffet restaurants serving regional and international dishes. There's also a barbecue zone.

The property is 21 km from the center of Ilhéus and 5 km from Olivenza. There is free parking.
