Description

Villa Justen duplex chalets feature a patio with hammock, fully equipped kitchenette, small table and chairs. They also include a fan, TV and private bathroom with shower. There is also free Wi-Fi connection.

The property provides luggage storage.

Villa Justen is in one of the many hills of Lençóis, about 25 minutes walk from the nearest waterfall. Lençóis bus station is 500 meters away, while Coronel Horácio de Matos Airport is 20 km away.
