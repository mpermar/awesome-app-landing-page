Description

The rooms have a private patio with hammock, fans, mosquito nets and minibar. They include a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries. In addition, the chalets have thatched roof.

Pousada Capitu offers luggage storage, free parking, a 24-hour reception and a garden. A massage service is also available for a fee.

The accommodation is located between the river and Imabassai beach and has access to both. The property is 10 km from Praia do Forte and 67 km from the nearest Luis Eduardo Magalhães Airport.
