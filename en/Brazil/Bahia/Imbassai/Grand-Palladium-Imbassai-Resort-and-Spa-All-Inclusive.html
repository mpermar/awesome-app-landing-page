Description

The suites at the Grand Palladium Imbassaí Resort & Spa have a king-size bed, a minibar, a flat-screen satellite TV and an iPod docking station. They also have air conditioning, a seating area and a bathroom with a spa bath.

The Grand Palladium Imbassaí is a 5-star all-inclusive hotel with 5 swimming pools and a basketball court. Various water activities can be practiced in the area, such as volleyball, polo, kayaking and windsurfing.

Likewise, activities for babies, children and adolescents are carried out in the hotel's mini clubs. The gym also organizes spinning and aerobics classes and the spa offers massages and facial and body treatments.

Guests can enjoy evening shows and 5 restaurants. Of these, 3 serve Indian, Japanese and Brazilian à la carte dishes. There are also 2 buffet restaurants.

In addition, the complex houses 10 bars, where it is possible to have Brazilian and imported soft drinks. Rates include food and drinks, both alcoholic and non-alcoholic.

The Grand Palladium is located in the natural reserve of Imbassaí, on the coast of the Green Line of Bahia. Salvador is 95 km away.
