Description

The rooms include air conditioning, LCD TV and minibar. Some have a balcony overlooking the pool and a hairdryer.

The Nauticomar All Inclusive Hotel & Beach Club also has swimming pools for adults and children, spa with 2 hot tubs, sauna, massage service, kids club, fitness center, games room and sport classes.

The hotel is 6 km from the city center and the Paradiso Club, which provides exclusive beach services to guests. Porto Seguro Airport is 9 km away.

Praia de Taperapuã is a great choice for travelers interested in family beaches, sandy beaches and the beach.
