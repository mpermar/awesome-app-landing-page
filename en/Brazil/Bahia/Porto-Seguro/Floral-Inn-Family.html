Description

This 4-star hotel is surrounded by lush Atlantic vegetation and brightly decorated in bright colors. All rooms have air conditioning, minibar and TV.

The restaurant serves a varied buffet breakfast, which includes homemade cakes, regional specialties, fresh fruit and juices. You can also taste a wide variety of light meals and drinks.

Hotel Florida Inn Ocean is located 6 km from the center of Porto Seguro, which houses several charming bars and shops. The bus station is 5 km from the hotel and Porto Seguro Airport is 6 km away. In addition, there is free parking.

Curuipé Beach is a great choice for travelers interested in local food, sandy beaches and family beaches.
