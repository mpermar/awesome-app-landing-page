Description

The property is pet-friendly and provides accommodation in modern houses and apartments with air conditioning. All have a fully equipped kitchen, living area and private bathroom. Some also have a patio overlooking the garden.

The establishment has a restaurant and a bar.

The Toko Village is 800 meters from bars and restaurants and a supermarket, 12 km from the center of Porto Seguro and 15 km from the city airport.

Praia do Muta is a great choice for travelers interested in nature, sandy beaches and relaxation.
