Description

The 1, 2 and 3 bedroom apartments are located in 2 villas and are decorated with furniture made by local artisans. All have air conditioning, satellite TV, kitchenette and terrace with hammock.

You will enjoy the ocean breeze while sunbathing by the pool. Residence Pé na Areia organizes leisure activities, such as boat trips, horse riding, canoeing, diving and kitesurfing.

There are several places of interest in the area, such as the Costa do Descobrimento and Caraiva Museum, a small fishing town that can only be reached by canoe. Bike rental service is available and golf is possible in the area. Residence Pé na Areia is 3.6 km from Pitinga Beach and 5 km from Lagoa Azul and the nearest Porto Seguro Airport.
