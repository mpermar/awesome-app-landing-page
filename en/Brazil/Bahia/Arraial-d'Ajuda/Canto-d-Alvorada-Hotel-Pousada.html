Description

Rooms and chalets at Pousada D'Alvorada feature a minibar, cable TV and a balcony with a hammock.

Guests can enjoy a cocktail at the beach bar or eat at the Stella del Mare à la carte restaurant, which also offers service in the pool area. Canto D’Alvorada Hotel serves a daily buffet breakfast and offers an outdoor swimming pool, solarium, laundry service and free Wi-Fi throughout, and there is free parking and car rental.

The property offers a bicycle rental service and canoeing is possible in the surroundings. Guests can access the historic center of Porto Seguro by ferry. Canto d'Alvorada Hotel Pousada is 2.4 km from Mucuge Beach and 5 km from Pitinga Beach and Porto Seguro Airport.
