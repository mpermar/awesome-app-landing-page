Description

The property has an outdoor pool with a waterfall and barbecue facilities.

The rooms have air conditioning, balcony, flat screen TV, fan, minibar, safe and private bathroom with shower. Some accommodations also include cable channels and a hairdryer.

To relax, guests can use the shared lounge, equipped with a flat-screen TV and a DVD collection. The establishment also features volleyball and badminton courts, a restaurant and a beach bar serving drinks and snacks.

Right in front of the hotel there is a bus stop that provides connections to the center of Arraial d'Ajuda, which is only 4 km away in 10 minutes. Porto Seguro Airport is only 3.4 km from the hotel.
