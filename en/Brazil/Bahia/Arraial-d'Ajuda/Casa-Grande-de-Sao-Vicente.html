Description

The exclusive and luxurious suites of the São Vicente Hotel have neoclassical furniture, air conditioning and quality wooden floors. All have a terrace with views of the sea, the pool or the garden.

A buffet breakfast is served daily, including a variety of juices and tropical fruits, as well as freshly baked bread and cakes. Breakfast can also be taken to the rooms.

Hotel Casa Grande de São Vicente is less than 2 km from the center of Arraial d'Ajuda and 6 km from Porto Seguro. It has private parking.
