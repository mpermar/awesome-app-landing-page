Description

All rooms have air conditioning, a flat-screen cable TV and a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries.

Sauipe Pousadas - All Inclusive has free access to the resort's villa, where you can enjoy live music and some shows. The Sauipe children's club organizes entertainment programs for children run by qualified staff.

Free access to some areas of the complex is offered, including Sauípe Sports, the gym and the wellness center.

This complex is 76 km from Luis Eduardo Magalhães Airport and free public parking is available.
