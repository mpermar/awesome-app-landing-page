Description

The rooms are bright and have a modern and colorful decoration. They feature air conditioning, a balcony, a flat-screen cable TV, a minibar and a private bathroom with a bath or shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

The Sauipe Resorts - All Inclusive offers several free shows, such as live music, dance shows and art exhibitions. The Sauipe children's club is a space specially enabled for the fun of the little ones, with the help of qualified staff.

Guests can enjoy free access to some areas of the complex, such as the Sauípe Sports Sports Center, the gym and the Japanese-style hot tub in the wellness center.

The complex is 76 km from Luis Eduardo Magalhães Airport. There is free public parking.
