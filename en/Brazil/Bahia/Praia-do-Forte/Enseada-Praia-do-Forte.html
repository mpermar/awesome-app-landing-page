Description

The apartments at Enseada Praia do Forte have air conditioning, LCD TV with satellite channels, DVD player and radio. They have a living and dining area and a kitchen equipped with a fridge, stove and oven. Cleaning service is offered, for a fee.

The Projeto Tamar turtle protection center is 20 meters away and the Mata de São João bus terminal is 1 km from Enseada Praia do Forte. The famous Imbassaí beach is located 10 km from the property.
