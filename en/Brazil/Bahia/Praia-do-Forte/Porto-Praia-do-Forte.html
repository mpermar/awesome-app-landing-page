Description

The apartments are duplex and feature air conditioning, a living area with a sofa and a flat-screen TV, as well as a fully equipped kitchen on the ground floor. One of the bedrooms is on the top floor.

This independent establishment is 10 km from Imbassaí and a short walk from the center of the Tamar Project for turtle conservation. Luis Eduardo Magalhães de Salvador International Airport is 50 km away.
