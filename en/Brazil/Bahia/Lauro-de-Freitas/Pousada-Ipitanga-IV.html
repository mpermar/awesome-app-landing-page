Description

The rooms of the Ipitanga IV are cheerful. All rooms include air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a minibar and a private bathroom. Wi-Fi connection is free.

Luiz Eduardo Magalhães International Airport is 9 km away. The property is 25 km from Salvador Central Bus Station and 4 km from the city center of Lauro de Freitas. Buraquinho Beach is 3 km away. Private parking is free.
