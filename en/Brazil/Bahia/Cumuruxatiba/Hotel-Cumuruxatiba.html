Description

The rooms feature air conditioning, balcony, private bathroom with free toiletries, safe, cable TV, minibar and spectacular views of the sea and the garden.

Hotel Cumuruxatiba has a restaurant, a fitness center, free bicycles, a games room, a children's club and a children's playground. Various activities, such as canoeing, can be practiced both in the establishment and in the surroundings.

There is free parking.
