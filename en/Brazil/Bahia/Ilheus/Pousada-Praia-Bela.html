Description

The rooms at Praia Bela feature functional décor and are equipped with tiled floors, air conditioning, cable TV and a private balcony overlooking the garden.

The inn serves a tropical breakfast in the dining room and features a bar, which offers snacks and drinks throughout the day.

Pousada Praia Bela is 3 km from Ilheus Airport and Praia dos Milionários. Free parking is provided.
