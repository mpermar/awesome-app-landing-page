Description

The accommodation at Jardim Atlântico Beach Resort occupies an area of 15,000 m² and overlooks the Atlantic Ocean and the garden. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, cable TV and minibar.

The Jardim Atlântico has a fitness center, billiards and a tennis court, and guests also have children's entertainment programs.

A buffet breakfast with fruit, juices, cereals and various types of bread and cakes is served every morning. Regional and international dishes are offered for dinner, and room service is also available.

Hotel Jardim Atlântico Beach is just 2 km from Jorge Amado Airport, and guests have an airport shuttle service upon request. There is free parking.
