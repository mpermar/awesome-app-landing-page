Description

The accommodation features a kitchen with a microwave, oven and toaster and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. In addition, the Flat Club Meridional Carneiros has a thermal bath.

Flat Club Meridional Carneiros is 700 meters from Sao Benedito Church and 7 km from Tamandaré Fort. The closest Gilberto Freyre International Airport in Recife-Guararapes is 66 km away.
