Description

Pontal dos Carneiros Beach is 85 km from Guararapes International Airport, 54 km from Porto de Galinhas Beach, 70 km from Cape Santo Agostinho and 47 km from the famous Maragogi Beach.

The bungalows at Pontal dos Carneiros are decorated in a beach style and feature a table, a seating area, a patio with garden views, cable TV and a private bathroom with a hot shower.

Laundry and room services are provided. A delicious breakfast is served every morning. The restaurant serves regional specialties.
