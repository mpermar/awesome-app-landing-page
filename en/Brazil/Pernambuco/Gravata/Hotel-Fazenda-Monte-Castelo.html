Description

The rooms at the 4-star Monte Castelo Hotel have a balcony overlooking the beautiful plains. They are spacious and bright, and have LCD satellite TV, air conditioning, minibar and telephone.

The restaurant has a panoramic terrace and serves lunch and breakfast buffet with regional and international specialties. Dinner includes à la carte dishes and fondues, as well as live music on Saturdays. In addition, you can taste a special selection of wines from the hotel's cellar.

During the day, you can practice activities such as football, hiking and cycling. There is also a park and farm activities for the little ones. The hotel offers a 24-hour reception service.

The hotel is located 8 km from Gravatá and less than 10 km from Bezerros. Recife is 92 km away. Free parking is available on site.
