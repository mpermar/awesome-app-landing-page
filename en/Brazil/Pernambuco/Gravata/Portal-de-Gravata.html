Description

The rooms of the Portal de Gravatá have air conditioning, TV and stereo. Some also have a balcony with a hammock.

The Portal's restaurant serves regional dishes, such as feijoada, pernambucano and sweet and savory fondues, accompanied by top quality local wines. Dairy products are also offered from the farm.

The Portal de Gravatá offers mountaineering and rappelling excursions. You can also go hiking or fishing nearby. In addition, the property is an hour's drive from Guararapes Airport and a 40-minute drive from Recife.
