Description

Samoa Beach Resort is located in Porto De Galinhas, only a 5 minutes from the city centre and offers free WiFi, concierge services and a tour desk.

This 4-star hotel offers an outdoor swimming pool. The hotel has a garden and features a children's playground.

At the hotel, all rooms are fitted with a desk and a flat-screen TV. Each room comes with a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hair dryer.

All units at Samoa Beach Resort have air conditioning and a wardrobe. The accommodation offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

Guests can make use of the fitness centre. You can play billiards at Samoa Beach Resort.

Speaking English and Spanish, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical information on the area at the reception.

Cupe Beach is 5 km from the hotel, while Merepe Beach is 10 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Recife / Guararapes-Gilberto Freyre International Airport, 50 km from Samoa Beach Resort.
