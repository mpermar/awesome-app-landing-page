Description

The Beach Class Resort Flat Muro Alto features a balcony with sea views, air conditioning, cable TV, a seating area and a dining table. It includes a kitchenette with a microwave and a minibar, and a private bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer.

The Flat Beach Class Resort Muro Alto has a tennis court, fitness center and games room. In addition, there is free parking.

Recife / Guararapes International Airport - Gilberto Freyre is 39 km away.
