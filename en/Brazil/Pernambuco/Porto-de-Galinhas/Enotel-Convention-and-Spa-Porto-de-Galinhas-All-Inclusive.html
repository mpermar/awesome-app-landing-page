Description

Diving classes are offered in the hotel pools. There are also activities such as beach volleyball, beach soccer and tennis.

The elegant rooms, decorated in gleaming white, have blackout curtains and windows. All have air conditioning, TV and minibar.

The hotel offers a spa with relaxing jet pools, a beauty salon and massage rooms. Guests can use the gym.

The Enotel Resort has its own disco, the Boite Enotel. There is also another disco that offers live music and shows. There are several activities and games for children.

The hotel's 2 restaurants serve Portuguese and Northeast Brazilian specialties. All restaurants and bars include unlimited consumption of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages.

This luxury resort is 60 km from Guararapes International Airport.
