Description

Rooms at Hotel Solar Porto de Galinhas include air conditioning, a minibar and cable TV. The deluxe rooms are more spacious and have a private hot tub and direct sea views.

The hotel has a kids club and entertainment staff. Adults can enjoy the gazebos and sun loungers. In addition, for a fee, they have access to a private space and can have a romantic dinner on the beach.

Merepê Beach is 1.4 km from Hotel Solar Porto de Galinhas, while Maracaipe Beach is 5 km away.
