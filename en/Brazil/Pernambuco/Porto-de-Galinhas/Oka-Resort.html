Description

The accommodation has a kitchen with a dishwasher, microwave, toaster and coffee maker, as well as a flat-screen TV. The Oka Resort has a sauna and an outdoor pool.

You can play tennis, table tennis and billiards at the property, and snorkeling and diving in the area. Apartamento Oka Resort is 1.1 km from Cupe Beach and 5 km from Merepe Beach. The nearest airport is Recife / Guararapes-Gilberto Freyre International Airport, located 36 km from the Oka Resort.

Playa de Muro Alto is a great choice for travelers interested in family trips, boat trips and reef diving.
