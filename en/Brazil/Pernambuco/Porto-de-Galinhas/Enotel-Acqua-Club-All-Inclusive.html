Description

Its rooms have modern décor and include air conditioning, a balcony and cable TV. Some offer sea views, while all have a well-equipped private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries. Free Wi-Fi is provided throughout the premises.

The entertainment staff of the Enotel Acqua Club organizes children's activities. You can entertain yourself in the games room, as well as savor exotic drinks and cocktails at the Coconut Bar.

In the surroundings you can visit the beaches of Maracaipe and Merepe, located 5.8 km and 2 km, respectively. The resort provides a shuttle service to Gilberto Freyre International Airport, 39 km away, for a fee.
