Description

Featuring a restaurant, an outdoor swimming pool, a bar and a shared lounge, Marulhos resort Muro Alto is set in the Muro Alto Beach district of Porto De Galinhas, only 13 km from Maracaipe's Beach.

This beachfront property offers access to a pool table, ping-pong and free private parking.

Boasting pool views, the apartment is fitted with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, air conditioning, a seating area, a wardrobe and 1 bathroom.

There is a dining area and a kitchen complete with a microwave, a fridge and an oven.

Speaking English, Spanish and Portuguese, staff are willing to help at any time of the day at the reception.

The apartment offers a sauna. A water park and a children's playground are available at Marulhos resort Muro Alto, while guests can also relax in the garden.

Cupe Beach is 2. 9 km from the accommodation, while Merepe Beach is 8 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Recife / Guararapes-Gilberto Freyre International, 48 km from Marulhos resort Muro Alto, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
