Description

All accommodation units feature air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, an oven and a private bathroom with a shower. Some also include a terrace and / or balcony with sea views.

The property serves a daily continental breakfast.

The apartment consists of an outdoor pool.

There is also a garden on the premises.

Maracaipe Beach is 10 km from Muro Alto Beach Class, while Cupe Beach is 2.1 km away. The nearest Recife / Guararapes-Gilberto Freyre International Airport is 35 km away.

Playa de Muro Alto is a great choice for travelers interested in family trips, boat trips and reef diving.
