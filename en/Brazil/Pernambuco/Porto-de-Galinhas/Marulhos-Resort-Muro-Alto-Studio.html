Description

The accommodation has a kitchenette with a microwave. This apartment has towels and bed linen. Marulhos Resort Muro Alto Studio includes a sauna and an outdoor pool. The apartment offers free WiFi.

You can play tennis, table tennis and billiards at the property or snorkel in the surroundings. Merepe Beach is 7 km from Marulhos Resort Muro Alto Studio, while the Hippocampus project is 8 km away. Guests can practice various activities, such as diving and hiking. Gilberto Freyre International Airport in Recife-Guararapes is 35 km from the property.

Playa de Muro Alto is a great choice for travelers interested in family trips, boat trips and reef diving.
