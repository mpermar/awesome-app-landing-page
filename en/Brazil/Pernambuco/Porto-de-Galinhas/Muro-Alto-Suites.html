Description

Muro Alto Flats offers fully equipped accommodation with a balcony. The apartments include a kitchen, a seating area with sofas and cable TV, and a private bathroom with a hot shower.

The establishment has a restaurant. The reception is available 24 hours a day and there is a children's entertainment area.

Muro Alto Flats is 5 km from Porto de Galinhas Beach and 50 km from Guararapes International Airport in Recife. This property organizes guided tours, fishing trips and scuba diving. It also offers bicycle rental and jet ski rental.

Playa de Muro Alto is a great choice for travelers interested in family trips, boat trips and reef diving.

Fantastic beach for families

"This was absolutely the best beach I can possibly imagine for a family with small children. The ocean could not be safer with the long reef protecting the shore from waves and strong tide. During low tide I could play with my 1 and 3 year olds comfortably using a float and even during high tide we felt safe being in the water. Even compared to neighbouring properties this part of the beach was worth the entire trip. The distance to the reef is probably a couple hundred meters which you could walk across to explore the reef. There is also a possibility for kits surfing and paddling. The hotel itself is also comfortable, and very convenient since it is directly on the beach."
