Description

Beach Life Porto de Galinhas has a restaurant, outdoor swimming pool, a bar and garden in Porto De Galinhas.

Boasting family rooms, this property also provides guests with a barbecue. The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, a shared kitchen and free WiFi throughout the property.

The inn offers a buffet or American breakfast. Beach Life Porto de Galinhas offers a sun terrace.

The area is popular for fishing, and bike hire and car hire are available at the accommodation.

Maracaipe's Beach is 3. 2 km from Beach Life Porto de Galinhas, while Hippocampus Project is 400 metres from the property.

The nearest airport is Recife / Guararapes-Gilberto Freyre International Airport, 52 km from the inn.
