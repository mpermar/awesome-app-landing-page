Description

Situated in Búzios, 5 km from Armação Beach, Pousada Melendez provides accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool.

200 metres from Geriba Lagoon, the inn features barbecue facilities and a terrace.

Turtle Beach is 3. 7 km from the inn. All units at the inn are equipped with a seating area.

With a private bathroom equipped with a hair dryer, rooms at Pousada Melendez also have free WiFi, while selected rooms here will provide you with a balcony.

The property has an indoor pool. Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.

Tucuns Beach is 3. 7 km from Pousada Melendez, while Bus Central Station is 4 km from the property.
