Description

Situated in Búzios, 2. 6 km from Armação Beach, Eco Vila Beija Flor features accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a garden and a terrace.

Among the facilities at this property are a 24-hour front desk and room service, along with free WiFi throughout the property.

The guest house has family rooms. At the guest house, the rooms include a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

All rooms will provide guests with a fridge. Guests at Eco Vila Beija Flor can enjoy a continental or a buffet breakfast.

Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Búzios, like snorkelling.

Brava Beach is 2. 4 km from Eco Vila Beija Flor, while Azeda Beach is 2. 8 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Cabo Frio International Airport, 33 km from the guest house.
