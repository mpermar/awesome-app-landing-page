Description

Hotel Bucsky is a hotel and convention centre set in 159,000 square metres of tropical gardens in Nova Friburgo.

It has tennis courts and a fitness centre. All rooms at Hotel Bucsky include cable TV, a mini bar and a hairdryer.

Wireless internet is available throughout the hotel and there is also an internet room with PCs for guests to use.

Hotel Bucsky has extensive sporting facilities including soccer fields, mini-golf, and a playground.

An adult-only swimming pool and a children's swimming pool are featured. Hotel Bucsky’s restaurant serves Brazilian and international cuisine during the week and themed meals on Saturdays.

The hotel’s bar offers a happy hour with drinks promotions and a tasting of local products.
