Description

Rooms with a balcony, an outdoor pool and free Wi-Fi are offered at Mara Palace. The hotel is situated in Vassouras, 119 km from Rio de Janeiro.

All air-conditioned accommodation feature cable TVs and minibars. Garden views are offered from the balconies of the rooms.

A game of billiards is played in the games room and children are entertained at the playground.

The hotel also features a communal reading and TV room. Guests can enjoy a typical Brazilian buffet breakfast with fresh fruit, juices, homemade breads and cakes.

The à la carte restaurant serves regional specialties and international dishes during the evening.

Free parking is possible at Hotel Mara Palace, when arriving by car. Casa de Cultura is just one block away and the hotel is a short drive to the centre of Vassouras.
