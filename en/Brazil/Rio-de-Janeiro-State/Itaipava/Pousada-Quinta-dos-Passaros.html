Description

Pousada Quinta dos Pássaros features tasteful colonial-style architecture, as well as a pool, gym and tennis court, 5 km from Itaipava town centre.

The rooms at Quinta dos Pássaros are decorated with wooden floors and antique furniture, and have air conditioning and heating and a TV.

Some also offer a private spa bath. Guests can also relax in the communal lounge areas or use the games room.

The guest house is 2 km from Itaipava’s popular arts and crafts fair and 7 km from Itaipava Bus Station.

Petrópolis is 15 km from the property and Galeão International Airport is 85 km away.
