Description

Situated in Engenheiro Paulo de Frontin, Hotel Sta Barbara Farm has an outdoor swimming pool and a tennis court.

With free WiFi, this 3-star hotel has a fitness centre and a garden. There is a restaurant.

The rooms in the hotel are fitted with a flat-screen TV. Each room includes a private bathroom with a shower, while certain rooms include mountain views.

A continental or buffet breakfast is available every morning at the property. The wellness area at Hotel Fazenda Sta Barbara is comprised of a hot tub and a sauna.

Guests can enjoy a range of activities in the surroundings, including cycling, fishing and hiking.

Petrópolis is 46 km from the accommodation. Rio de Janeiro/Galeao International Airport is 100 km away.
