Description

Quinta dos Doges features a restaurant, free bikes, an outdoor swimming pool and fitness centre in Valença.

Among the various facilities of this property are a bar, a shared lounge and a garden.

The inn offers an indoor pool, hot spring bath and a shared kitchen. At the inn all rooms are fitted with a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

Guests at Quinta dos Doges can enjoy a buffet breakfast. Guests staying at the accommodation have access to the on-site spa and wellness centre which includes a hammam, a hot tub and a sauna.

You can play billiards and table tennis at Quinta dos Doges. Conservatória is 22 km from the inn, while Vassouras is 33 km away.
