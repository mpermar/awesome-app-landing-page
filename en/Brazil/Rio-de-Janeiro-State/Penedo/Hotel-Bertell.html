Description

Located in Penedo, 1. 5 km from Pequena Finlandia, Hotel Bertell provides accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a fitness centre and a shared lounge.

With a garden, the 3-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom.

The property offers garden views, a children's playground and a 24-hour front desk.

All units in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Guest rooms include a wardrobe.

A buffet breakfast is available daily at Hotel Bertell. Spa and wellness facilities including a sauna and a hot tub are at guests' disposal during their stay at the accommodation.

You can play billiards at Hotel Bertell. Finnish Museum is 2. 3 km from the hotel, while Cachoeira de Deus is 6 km away.
