Description

Boasting a bar and a shared lounge, Hotel Halloween Inn Penedo is set in Penedo, less than 1 km from Pequena Finlandia and a 20-minute walk from Finnish Museum.

Among the facilities at this property are a shared kitchen and room service, along with free WiFi throughout the property.

The hotel offers an outdoor swimming pool, fitness centre, karaoke and a 24-hour front desk.

The hotel offers a continental or buffet breakfast. Hotel Halloween Inn Penedo offers a children's playground.

Cachoeira de Deus is 5 km from the accommodation, while Antonio Correa Municipal Stadium is 12 km from the property.
