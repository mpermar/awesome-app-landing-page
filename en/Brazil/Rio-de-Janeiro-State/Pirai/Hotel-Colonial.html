Description

Hotel Colonial has a fitness centre, bar, a shared lounge and garden in Piraí. Boasting a 24-hour front desk, this property also welcomes guests with a restaurant, a water park and an outdoor pool.

The property features a hammam, evening entertainment and room service. At the hotel, the rooms are fitted with a wardrobe.

At Hotel Colonial all rooms have a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

A buffet breakfast is available every morning at the accommodation. Hotel Colonial offers a children's playground.

You can play billiards and table tennis at the hotel, and the area is popular for hiking.

Volta Redonda is 46 km from Hotel Colonial, while Conservatória is 39 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Rio de Janeiro/Galeao International Airport, 112 km from the accommodation.
