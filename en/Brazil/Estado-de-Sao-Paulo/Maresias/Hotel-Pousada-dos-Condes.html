Description

The rooms feature a flat-screen satellite TV and a private bathroom with a shower.

The hotel offers a 24-hour reception, a shared lounge and several shops. Free umbrellas and sun loungers are offered in the pool area and on the beach.

Facilities include a ping pong table. Hotel Pousada dos Condes is 1.4 km from Morocco and 1.9 km from International Surf Plaza.
