Description

Rooms at Pousada Porto Mare are bright and feature rustic Brazilian-style furniture, air conditioning, free Wi-Fi and a private balcony with large French windows that let in plenty of natural light.

A buffet breakfast with tropical fruits, pastries and hot products is served every morning. The restaurant serves regional specialties and the pool bar offers tropical cocktails.

The hotel enjoys a central location, a few steps from several nightclubs, bars and restaurants. Free parking is available nearby.
