Description

All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, cable TV, minibar and bathroom, and some have a balcony.

Buffet breakfast is served every day in a restaurant that offers views of the pool and beach. In the bar, refreshing drinks are served. In addition, the establishment houses a sports court, a children's playroom and a large green area.

The Beach Hotel Maresias is a 10-minute walk from the world-famous Sirena nightclub. The São Sebastião bus station is 27 km from the hotel, while the city of São Paulo is 175 km away. Private parking is provided.
