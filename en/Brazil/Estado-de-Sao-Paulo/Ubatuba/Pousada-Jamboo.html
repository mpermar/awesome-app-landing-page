Description

The rooms have a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries. They also include bed linen and towels.

The property has a 24-hour reception and bicycle rental service.

The area is perfect for cycling. Pousada Jamboo is 2.4 km from Grande Beach and 2.7 km from Vermelha Beach.

Praia do Itagua is a great choice for travelers interested in restaurants, luxury restaurants and aquariums.
