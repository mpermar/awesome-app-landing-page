Description

The rooms are classically decorated with a rustic touch and feature room service, TV, minibar and private bathroom with shower. Some are larger and have a balcony with views of the sea or mountains.

A rich breakfast buffet is served in the bright dining area, which includes a selection of tropical fruits and cakes. The restaurant of the Hotel Porto Di Mare offers homemade Italian pasta.

The reception is open 24 hours a day and can arrange massage and laundry services. The establishment also has a bar, a games room with billiards and a changing room for babies.

The hotel is 10 km from the bus station, 5 km from the Ubatuba Aquarium and the Tamar project and a 3-minute drive from the city center of Ubatuba. At 50 meters there is a pier from where tour boats depart.

Enseada Beach is a great choice for travelers interested in family beaches, relaxation and sandy beaches.
