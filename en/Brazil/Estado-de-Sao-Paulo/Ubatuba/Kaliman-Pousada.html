Description

Guests of the Kaliman Pousada can enjoy the outdoor pool or a tropical cocktail on the beach, and outdoor sports such as paddle surfing and surfing in Maranduba or on the Lagoinha beach, near the property, can be enjoyed.

Kaliman's pleasant rooms feature white interiors and lots of natural light. They also have air conditioning and cable TV. Some have a balcony with hammocks.

Kaliman Pousada's restaurant serves regional meat, fish and pasta dishes. The bar offers snacks and cocktails. A breakfast with seasonal fruits, fresh juices and freshly baked cakes is served.

Praia do Sape is a great choice for travelers interested in family beaches, walking along the beach and sandy beaches.
