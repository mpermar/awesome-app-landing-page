Description

The rooms feature cable TV, a minibar, a private bathroom, a balcony with garden views and classic décor. All have wooden floor.

Colonial Plaza Hotel is located in Pindamonhangaba, 800 meters from the access to the Rodovia Presidente Dutra and 145 km from Guarulhos International Airport in São Paulo. It has free private parking.

The restaurant serves lunch, dinner and a buffet breakfast that includes a variety of breads, cakes and fruits. The bar offers refreshing drinks.
