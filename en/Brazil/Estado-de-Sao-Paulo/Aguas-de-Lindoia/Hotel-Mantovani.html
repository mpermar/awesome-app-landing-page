Description

The rooms have a TV, minibar, fan and private bathroom. In addition, there is room service.

Hotel Mantovani also includes a children's playground, shared lounge and laundry service.

Hotel Mantovani is 1 km from Águas de Lindóia bus station and 3 km from Morro do Cristo. Viracopos-Campinas International Airport is 120 km away. Parking is free.
