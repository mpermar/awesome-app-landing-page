Description

This property accepts pets and offers free Wi-Fi.

The rooms have air conditioning, TV, minibar and private bathroom with hairdryer. In addition, room and laundry services are provided.

Spa facilities include a sauna, a heated pool and an oriental garden; All of this can be used for free.

There is also a full board meal plan. In the morning a buffet breakfast is served, based on a variety of fresh fruits, breads, tapioca, tortillas and sausages, as well as a selection of drinks.

Oscar Inn Eco Resort is 4 km from the bus station and the center of Águas de Lindóia and 100 km from Campinas Viracopos Airport. There is free private parking.
