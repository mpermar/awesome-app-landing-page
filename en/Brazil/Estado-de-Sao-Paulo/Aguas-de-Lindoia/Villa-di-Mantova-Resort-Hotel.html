Description

The rooms are decorated in light colors and feature air conditioning, TV, minibar and dressing area. Some also have a fireplace.

A buffet breakfast with a variety of fresh fruits, breads and cold meats is served daily. The resort's 2 restaurants offer regional specialties, as well as a large selection of drinks.

Villa di Mantova Resort Hotel is located 2 km from the thermal water park of the Municipal Balneário de Águas de Lindóia, the town center and Adhemar de Barros Square and 1.5 km from the bus terminal. Moreover, parking is free.
