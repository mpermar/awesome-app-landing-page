Description

The Mavsa's spacious rooms have air conditioning, cable TV, bathrobes, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

The reception is open 24 hours. In addition, there is a shared lounge, a gift shop and several shops on site.

In the complex you can practice tennis, table tennis and billiards. The area is ideal for fishing and Viracopos International Airport is 88 km away.
