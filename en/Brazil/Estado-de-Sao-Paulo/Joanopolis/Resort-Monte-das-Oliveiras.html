Description

Most of the villas enjoy views of the garden or the complex and some have a fireplace. All have a TV, minibar and private bathroom with hot shower.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning, including a variety of fresh fruit, breads and cold meats. The hotel restaurant offers regional and international specialties and a selection of drinks.

Monte das Oliveiras Resort is located in Joanópolis, 10 km from the city center and 12 km from the bus terminal. The complex also houses sports courts, games room and fitness center. There is free parking.
