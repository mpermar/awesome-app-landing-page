Description

The modern rooms are decorated in red tones and have a balcony with views, flat-screen cable TV, minibar, safe and air conditioning. The private bathroom is equipped with a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer. Some suites have a bathtub and kitchenette with coffee maker and microwave.

The hotel serves a daily buffet breakfast that includes a variety of fresh fruit, hot and cold drinks, cakes, cold cuts and American and vegetarian options. The OUIBar + KTCHN offers snacks, cocktails and drinks. The ROD Grainne's restaurant serves a selection of exquisite dishes.

There is an outdoor pool with sun loungers on site. The Radisson Red Campinas has event facilities equipped with the latest audiovisual equipment. It also has a ping pong table, pool table and board games.

The Unicamp - Campinas State University falls at 11.5 km and Viracopos Airport is 21 km away. Sao Paulo / Guarulhos International Airport is 118 km from the hotel.
