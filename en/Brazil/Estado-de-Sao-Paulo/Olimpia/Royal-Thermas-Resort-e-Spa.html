Description

All rooms in the complex have a desk, TV and private bathroom. The rooms at the Royal Thermas Resort Spa also include air conditioning and a closet.

A daily buffet breakfast is served.

The establishment also has a ping pong table.

The property is 52 km from the nearest Prof. Eribelto Manoel Reino Airport.
