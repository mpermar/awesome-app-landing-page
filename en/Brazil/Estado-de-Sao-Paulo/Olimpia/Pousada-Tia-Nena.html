Description

The rooms have a TV, air conditioning, minibar, private bathroom with shower, garden views and bed linen.

Tia Nena offers a garden, barbecue facilities, a terrace, laundry and ironing services and free private parking.

The Thermas dos Laranjais water park is 1 km from the property.
