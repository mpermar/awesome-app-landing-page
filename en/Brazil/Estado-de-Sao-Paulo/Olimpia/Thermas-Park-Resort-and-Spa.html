Description

The rooms at the Thermas Park Resort have air conditioning, a balcony or terrace, a flat-screen TV, a minibar, a sofa and a safe. The private bathroom includes bathrobes and free toiletries.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning. The on-site restaurant serves regional cuisine and has children's menus.

This complex is 1 hour drive from Sao Jose Do Rio Preto airport and about 50 km from Barretos.
