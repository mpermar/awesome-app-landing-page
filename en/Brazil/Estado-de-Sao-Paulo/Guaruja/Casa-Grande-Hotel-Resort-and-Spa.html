Description

All rooms at Casa Grande have air conditioning and cable TV. Free WiFi is provided.

The Casa Grande has 5 restaurants serving a wide variety of international dishes, such as Brazilians and Thais. In addition, the complex has a tennis court and mini golf. There is also entertainment staff for children.

Casa Grande Guarujá Hotel Resort is 7 km from Santos and 8 km from Pernambuco Beach.
