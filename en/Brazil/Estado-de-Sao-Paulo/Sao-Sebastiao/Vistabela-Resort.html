Description

The suites and apartments at Vistabela Resort feature cable TV, a small dining area and a patio with a hammock and views of the garden. The apartments include a full kitchen, while the suites have a kitchenette with microwave and minibar.

Vistabela is 6 km from the center of São Sebastião and 8 km from Cambury Beach. Boiçucanga and Maresias beaches are less than 20 km away and Guarulhos de São Paulo International Airport is 208 km away.
