Description

The units have a flat-screen TV, microwave, toaster, coffee maker, kitchenware and a private bathroom with a spa bath and free toiletries. Some have a kitchen, a seating area and a fridge.

Bangalô No Condomínio Yacamim also includes a fitness center. Guests can play tennis at the property and the area is ideal for diving.

El Bangalô Condominio Yacamim is 14 km from the historic center and 2.2 km from playa Praia Grande.
