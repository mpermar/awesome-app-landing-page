Description

The units feature a TV with cable channels, air conditioning and a private bathroom with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Hotel Estância Atibainha - Resort & Convention has a spa and wellness center, restaurant and tennis court. There is also a shared lounge, meeting facilities and entertainment staff.

Various activities, such as horse riding, fishing and hiking, can be practiced in the surrounding area. Free parking is also available.This farmhouse is 30 km from Guarulhos International Airport.
