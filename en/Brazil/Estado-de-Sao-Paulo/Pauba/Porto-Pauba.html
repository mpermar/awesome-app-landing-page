Description

All accommodation includes an equipped kitchen and cable TV, and some of them offer a seating and / or dining area. There is a kitchen with a fridge and stove, and towels and bed linen are provided.

The Porto Paúba Condominium has saunas and an outdoor pool available year-round. There is a bar and restaurant on the premises.

You can play table tennis and billiards at the property, and diving and scuba diving are common activities in the area. Condomínio Porto Paúba is 21 km from Ilhabela and 50 km from Caraguatatuba.
