Description

The rooms have LCD cable TV, minibar, dining table for 2 people and views of the garden or mountains. They also include air conditioning, a king-size bed, tiled floors and free wired internet. Room service is available 24 hours a day.

Tabatinga Hotel is 10 km from the center of the town of Caraguatatuba, a 20-minute drive from Lazaro Beach and about 40 minutes from the center of Ilhabela and Ubatuba. The property offers free private parking (1 vehicle per reservation).

Manduca Restaurant serves a daily buffet breakfast and a variety of regional and international dishes. Guests can also enjoy the wide selection of wines from the complex's cellar.

The Tabatinga Hotel has a cinema, a playground and a sauna. In addition, there is a 24-hour reception and room service available.
