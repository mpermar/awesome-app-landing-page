Description

All rooms at Pousada Sol e Mar have a balcony or patio and have white bedding and white walls. They include wooden furniture, TV, minibar and private bathroom. The 1 bedroom apartment also has a kitchen.

A buffet breakfast is offered every morning, which includes a selection of fresh fruit, bread and sausages. The on-site restaurant serves regional specialties and various drinks.

Pousada Sol e Mar provides sun loungers and parasols. Monjolo Shopping Center, located in Juquehy, is 100 meters away, while Boiçucanga and the local bus station are 10 minutes away by car.
