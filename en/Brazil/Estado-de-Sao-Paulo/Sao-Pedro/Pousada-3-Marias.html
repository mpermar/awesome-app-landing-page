Description

The rooms have a balcony and mountain views, TV, minibar and fan. All offer rustic-style décor and are equipped with a private bathroom.

Pousada 3 Marias is 5 km from São Pedro bus station and 12 km from Saltão Waterfall. Campinas Viracopos International Airport is 100 km from this pet-friendly hostel, and Sao Paulo Guarulhos International Airport is 195 km away. Free private parking is available.
