Description

The rooms are elegantly decorated and have heating, LCD TV, DVD player, minibar, parquet floors and wooden furniture in light tones. Some have a hot tub and kitchenette.

Campos dos Holandeses serves a buffet breakfast on its terrace, with panoramic views of the garden. It is 6 km from the popular Baden Baden restaurant, which serves various dishes for lunch and dinner.

The property has a living room with a fireplace and a games room with billiards. Children can do activities in the toy and computer rooms, where there are also video games.
