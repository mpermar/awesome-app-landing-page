Description

All rooms at L.A.H. Hostellerie have views of the valley, heating, LCD TV and balcony. Some have a hot tub, cellar, separate seating area and coffee maker.

Room service includes gourmet lunches and dinners until 10 pm and guests can enjoy a selection of drinks at the bar. Massage service and a convenient 24-hour reception are offered.

The property is a 20-minute drive from Floresta Encantada Park and a 15-minute drive from Itapvea Peak. Campos do Jordão bus station is 10 minutes away by car.
