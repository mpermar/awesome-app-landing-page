Description

The rooms at Santa Clara Eco Resort feature air conditioning, pleasant views of the garden, LCD TV with cable channels, telephone, safe, sofa and minibar. The private bathroom is equipped with a shower.

Santa Clara Eco Resort is 2 km from Dourado Bus Terminal and 35 km from the natural attractions of the city of Brotas.
