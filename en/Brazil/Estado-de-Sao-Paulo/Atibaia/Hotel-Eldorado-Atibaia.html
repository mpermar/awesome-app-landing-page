Description

The rooms are simply decorated and equipped with a TV, desk, private bathroom and air conditioning. Room service is also offered.

In addition, there are several restaurants, outdoor and covered. A buffet breakfast is served daily and drinks are available for a fee.

Hotel Village Eldorado is 6 km from the center of Atibaia and 75 km from São Paulo Guarulhos International Airport. It also has free private parking.
