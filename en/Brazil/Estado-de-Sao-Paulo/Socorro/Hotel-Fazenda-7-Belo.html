Description

Its practical rooms include a minibar and a private bathroom. Some are larger and have air conditioning, fireplace, LCD TV and patio with barbecue facilities.

Pousada Resort 7 Belo is 3 km from the Fashion Mall and the famous Feira das Malhas Market. Gruta do Anjo is 6 km from this 4-star resort and Viracopos International Airport is 110 km away. Free private parking is provided.

The spa offers a hot tub, a gym and relaxing massage sessions. You can play football, volleyball and tennis on the sports courts, while the children have fun in the playground. It is possible to participate in fishing excursions.

A daily buffet breakfast is served. The restaurant offers regional and international cuisine, while in the pool area you can enjoy refreshing drinks.
