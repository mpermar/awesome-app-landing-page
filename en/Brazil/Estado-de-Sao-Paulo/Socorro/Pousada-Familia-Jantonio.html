Description

The rooms feature tiled floors, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fan and a private bathroom.

Guests of the Pousada Família Jantonio have full access to an adjoining sauna, garden and terrace. There is also a shared lounge, a pub, a small soccer field, a fishing pond exclusively for guests and free parking.

Viracopos Airport is 80 km away.
