Description

The modern apartments include a 40-inch flat-screen TV, WiFi, air conditioning, living room, private bathroom, fully equipped kitchen and terrace with ocean views.

The Pipa Centro Residence offers an airport shuttle service, car rental, buggy and fishing trips, horseback riding, canoe rentals and surf and kitesurf lessons. Free private parking is available upon request. Breakfast and housekeeping carry a supplement.

Pipa Centro Residence is a short walk from bars, restaurants and shops.
