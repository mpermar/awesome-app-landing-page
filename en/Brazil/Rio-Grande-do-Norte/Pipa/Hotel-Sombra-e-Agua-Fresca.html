Description

All rooms and suites at Sombra are equipped with air conditioning and a minibar. The rooms overlook the sea or the forest, and some include a hot tub or a private pool, depending on availability.

From the hotel pool you can enjoy panoramic views of the beaches of Chapadão and Amor. There is a poolside bar that prepares cocktails and a tropical garden. The property also has a fitness and wellness center and a sauna.

The restaurant, with panoramic views of the area, serves a selection of international and regional cuisine in an intimate atmosphere, decorated with numerous works of art and wicker furniture.

Hotel Sombra e Água Fresca is located 500 meters from the city center.
