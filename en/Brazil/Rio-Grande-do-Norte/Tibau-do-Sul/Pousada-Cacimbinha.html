Description

The rooms at Cacimbinha feature a flat-screen TV, air conditioning, a minibar, a balcony with a hammock and a private bathroom with a shower and towels. They also enjoy garden views.

The inn is 5 km from Pipa Beach, 6 km from the Chapadão area and 800 meters from Tibau do Sul town center. São Gonçalo do Amarante Airport is 94 km away.
