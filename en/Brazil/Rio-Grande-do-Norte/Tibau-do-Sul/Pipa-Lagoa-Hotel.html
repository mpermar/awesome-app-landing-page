Description

All accommodation has a large balcony with panoramic views of the Guararas lagoon.

Pipa Lagoa Hotel also features an outdoor pool, spa with sauna and massage room.

Guests can enjoy contemporary la carte recipes and an extensive wine list at the award-winning Lagoa Lounge Resto Bar. In addition, the pool bar serves drinks.

Pipa Lagoa Hotel is located in the largest ecological sanctuary of Rio Grande do Norte and is located a few km from the city and the beaches of Pipa.
