Description

All rooms have a classic and elegant atmosphere and have sea views, air conditioning, flat-screen TVs and minibars, as well as free tea, milk and soluble coffee. The suites have a balcony, bathrobe, slippers and L'Occitane toiletries.

At the Best Western Premier Majestic, guests can enjoy the French restaurant La Brasserie de La Mer, run by renowned chef Erick Jacquin. Some of his famous dishes are foie gras and petit gateau. The hotel also serves a daily buffet breakfast with a varied selection of fruits and breads.

The hotel also offers valet parking, a business center, baby kitchen, games room and reading room. There is free Wi-Fi and parking. 2 free bottles of water are provided upon arrival.

The Best Western Premier Majestic Ponta Negra is a short walk from several bars and restaurants. The property is 500 meters from Praia Shopping Mall and approximately 1 minute walk from a craft market. São Gonçalo do Amarante International Airport is 32 km away.
