Description

The rooms at this establishment are equipped with air conditioning and flat-screen cable TV. They have a private bathroom with a bath or shower and bidet. They feature free toiletries and a hairdryer. Some units have a seating area.

This property provides room service. Daily breakfast is served in the rooms and includes local dishes.

The establishment provides car rental services. Oxente Travel Apart Hotel is 14 km from Forte dos Reis Magos and 25 km from Genipabu Beach. São Gonçalo do Amarante International Airport is 26 km away.
