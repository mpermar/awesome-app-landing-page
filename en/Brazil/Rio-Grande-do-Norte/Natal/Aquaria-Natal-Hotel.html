Description

The pleasant suites of the Aquaria Hotel Natal have 1 or 2 bedrooms, as well as air conditioning and a private balcony with full or partial sea views. Some rooms also have a private hot tub.

In the morning a breakfast buffet with fresh tropical fruit, juices and regional products is served. There are several bars and restaurants just 100 meters from the hotel.

Morro do Careca is 1 km away and Artesanato Shopping Center is less than 1.5 km away. Natal Aquaria Hotel is 50 km from São Gonçalo do Amarante International Airport. Free parking is provided.
