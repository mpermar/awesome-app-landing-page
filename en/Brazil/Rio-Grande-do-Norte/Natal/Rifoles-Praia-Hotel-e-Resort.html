Description

The rooms feature air conditioning, a balcony, 32 "LCD cable TV, minibar and bathroom with hairdryer and vanity mirror. Some categories offer sea views and all have free Wi-Fi.

The restaurant is open 24 hours and serves regional and international dishes. Breakfast includes bread, cakes, cakes and pastries from the hotel's exclusive bakery.

There is a games room and a gym, among other leisure offers. In addition, massages and a team of animators are provided throughout the day.
