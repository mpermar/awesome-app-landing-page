Description

The Yatch Village has fully equipped rooms with kitchen, LED TV, air conditioning and seating area. They are simply decorated with wooden furniture, windows and tiled floors. Some include a balcony and offer views of the Morro do Careca dune.

The Morro do Careca dune is located at the end of the Ponta Negra beach. On Erivan Franca Avenue, 70 meters away, there are some of the best restaurants, bars and commercial galleries in the region.

The nearest bus station is only 25 meters away. A supermarket and a pharmacy are just a few steps away, and São Gonçalo do Amarante International Airport is 40 km away.
