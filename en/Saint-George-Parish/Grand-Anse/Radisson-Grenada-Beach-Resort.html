Description

The air-conditioned rooms at this resort feature a flat-screen cable TV, a work desk, a coffee maker and a minibar. The bathroom is private and has a shower and free toiletries. Rooms also have garden and sea views.

Grenada Grand Beach features the Tradewinds, where guests can enjoy a buffet and the menus for breakfast and dinner. A cappuccino in the Bamboo Bar & Caffe can also be enjoyed, or guests can go for a snack or lunch at the Oasis Grill and get drinks at the Oasis Bar and Sunset Beach Bar.

Activities like water sports and scuba diving are an excellent option for guests. They can also use 2 surfaced tennis courts.

The resort is a 10-minute drive from Parc a Boeuf Beach and Magazine Beach. Point Salines International Airport is 3.5 miles from the property.
