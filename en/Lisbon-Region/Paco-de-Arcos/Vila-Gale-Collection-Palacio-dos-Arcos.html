Description

Rooms feature earthy tones and décor with inspiration from Portuguese poetry. All the new wing rooms have river and garden views. The private bathroom has free toiletries and a bathrobe.

A breakfast from the menu is served daily, and guests can enjoy meals at the on-site restaurant. There is also a bar available with drinks.

The palace's old wine cellar will be converted into a modern conference room, fully equipped for events. For relaxation, guests can head to Satsanga Spa offering a variety of massage treatments, Turkish bath, hot tub, and sauna.

Lisbon International Airport is 12 miles away, and Paço de Arcos Train Station is 800 feet away. Paço de Arcos Beach is just under a quarter of a mile away.
