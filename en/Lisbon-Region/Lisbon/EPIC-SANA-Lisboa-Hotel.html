Description

Featuring contemporary-style décor, each room includes a flat-screen TV and a minibar. Some rooms offer views of Lisbon. Free Wi-Fi access is available.

A breakfast buffet is served every day in the elegant breakfast room or in guests’ rooms. After a meal at the gourmet restaurant, guests can enjoy a drink in the Upscale bar.

EPIC SANA Lisboa Hotel provides meeting rooms for guests’ business needs. The 24-hour front desk can arrange babysitting services or help organizing laundry and ironing services. Guests who want to relax can head to the indoor and outdoor pools and enjoy a pampering massage at the spa.

Lisbon International Airport is 4 miles away, and Amoreiras shopping center is around 950 feet away. EPIC SANA Lisboa Hotel is around 20 minutes away from the lively Bairro Alto district with public transportation.

Santo Antonio is a great choice for travelers interested in monuments, friendly locals and architecture.
