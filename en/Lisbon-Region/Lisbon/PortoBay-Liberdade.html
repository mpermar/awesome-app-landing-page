Description

Designed by the architect Frederico Valsassina, Porto Bay Liberdade preserved the original early 20th century façade creating an environment that evokes details from the past and the contemporary. Each room includes a sitting area, a desk, a minibar, a flat-screen cable TV and a private bathroom with free toiletries.

At Porto Bay Liberdade guests can enjoy a daily breakfast, for an extra fee. An à la carte restaurant is available where guests can taste Mediterranean and international menus. Guests can relax at the bar in the hotel.

With a 24-hour front desk and facilities for disabled guests, Porto Bay Liberdade includes room service, business center, laundry service and private parking on site.

Porto Bay Liberdade is a few minutes’ walk from Avenida da Liberdade and just under 1 miles from main commercial areas like Rua Augusta and Chiado. The Marquês de Pombal Metro Station is a quarter mile away and the El Corte Inglés Shopping Center is a 20-minute walk. Lisbon International Airport is 4 miles away.

Santo Antonio is a great choice for travelers interested in monuments, friendly locals and architecture.
