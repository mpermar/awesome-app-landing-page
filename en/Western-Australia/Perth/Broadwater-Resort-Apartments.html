Description

This premium resort specializes in long-stay business trips, small business seminars and regional guests. It is ideally located for those wishing to mix business with pleasure in city style, without the hassles of city traffic.

Relax around the pool or in the garden. When ready to go out, ask first at the tour desk for advice. Rent a car for longer excursions. Return for fun in the garden using the barbecue facilities.

Como is a great choice for travelers interested in relaxation, food and beaches.
