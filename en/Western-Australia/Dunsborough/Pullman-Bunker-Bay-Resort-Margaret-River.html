Description

The spacious villas of Pullman Bunker Bay Resort Margaret River are air-conditioned and have direct beach access. All include cable TV, a full kitchen and a private bathroom.

The award-winning Vie Spa provides a wide range of special treatments, massages and therapies.

Other Side of the Moon Restaurant serves fresh Australian cuisine with a Mediterranean touch. Private dining among olive trees with views of the Indian Ocean can also be arranged. Guests can enjoy a selection of regional wines and relax at the poolside bar.

Pullman Bunker Bay Resort Margaret River is a 3-hour drive south of Perth, and less than 28 miles from Busselton Regional Airport. Free on-site parking is available.
