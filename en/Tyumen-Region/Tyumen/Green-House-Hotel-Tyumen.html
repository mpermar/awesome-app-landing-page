Description

Each room at Green House Hotel is decorated in an individual antique style with rich fabrics and furnishings. There is a mini-bar, and bathrooms are fitted with a bidet and slippers.

The Green House’s elegant restaurant serves Russian and European cuisine, as well as Japanese specialties and barbecue dishes.

Relaxation facilities at the hotel include a Thai massage service and a terrace to have a rest on. Guests can go for a walk in the Berezovskaya Roshcha Park, 2 minutes’ walk away.

Tyumen Airport is 20 minutes’ drive from Green House Hotel, and a shuttle service is available on request.
