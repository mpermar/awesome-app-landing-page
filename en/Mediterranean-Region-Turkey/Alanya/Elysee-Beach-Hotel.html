Description

All rooms of Elysee Beach Hotel are air conditioned and come with a balcony and satellite TV. Some rooms have a view of Alanya Castle and the city walls.

Hotel Elysee Beach provides guests with a fitness center and sauna. Guests can enjoy a game of pool in the hotel’s game room.

The hotel’s restaurant with views of the grounds and the Mediterranean Sea offers a buffet of local and international dishes. Elysee Beach Hotel’s snack bar serves snacks of Mediterranean and Turkish cuisine as well as refreshing drinks.

Free parking is available at the hotel.
