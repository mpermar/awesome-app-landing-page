Description

The air-conditioned rooms at Alva Donna Hotel are spacious and bright. Each includes a satellite flat-screen TV, free Wi-Fi and a minibar. All rooms have balconies and some feature a spa tub.

Alva’s guests can choose from 7 restaurants serving Asian, Italian, Mexican and Turkish cuisine. The restaurants serve meals outdoors or indoors, depending on the weather. There is a nightclub for entertainment.

Recreational facilities include a tennis court, fitness center and mini golf. Visitors can relax in the steam bath or get a massage.

The luxurious Alva Hotel is 30 miles from the center of Antalya. A car rental service and free private parking are also provided.
