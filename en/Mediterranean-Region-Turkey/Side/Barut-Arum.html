Description

The air conditioned rooms of Barut Arum Resort & Spa feature a modern interior, decorated with hardwood floors and furniture. All rooms have a sitting area, an electric teakettle, and a private bathroom.

The main restaurant serves an international buffet for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Guests can enjoy authentic Turkish and Mediterranean dishes from varied dining menus at Barut Arum’s restaurants. The bakery offers homemade cake and fresh coffee.

Barut Arum Resort’s spa offers a wide variety of health and beauty treatments, and features an indoor pool, a Turkish bath and a sauna.

The entertainment team offers daily activities and shows with live music and dance. Children can have fun at the mini club, or at the children’s pool which has water slides.

Barut Arum Resort & Spa is 2 miles from Side’s Theater and the Temples of Apollo and Athena. The Manavgat Waterfalls are 6 miles away.
