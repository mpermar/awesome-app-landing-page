Description

Rooms at Delphin Palace are spacious and offer dark wood furniture and Turkish décor accents. Each room has a private outdoor balcony, some feature side sea views. All rooms include satellite TV, a work desk and bathroom amenities.

Delphino Restaurant serves international cuisine including Ottoman, Italian, Japanese and fresh seafood dishes. Cocktails are available at Lobby Bar 24/7. Evening entertainment includes live music, a nightclub and comedy shows

During the day guests can enjoy the water slides or participate in on-site sports tournaments. The health club includes a full-service spa and well-equipped fitness center. A movie theater, children’s club and seafront lounge chairs are also available.

Antalya Airport is a 15 minute drive from Delphin Palace Hotel.
