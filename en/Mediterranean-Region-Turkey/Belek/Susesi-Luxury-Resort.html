Description

La Calisse Spa features 2 heated pools with glass ceilings, 2 marble hammams and state-of-the-art beauty treatments. Stone therapy, ayurveda and foam massages are performed by Susesi’s highly trained staff.

All spacious accommodations at Susesi Luxury Resort come equipped with flat-screen TVs. Some suites have a private terrace pool, a wind-protected balcony and a hydro-massage tub.

A variety of fine restaurants enables guests of Susesi Luxury Resort to choose for indoor or outdoor dining. Italian specialties can be tasted at the sit-down restaurant.

A bowling hall, morning yoga and Turkish courses are some of the sports and animation activities available day and night. There is also a golf course and tennis courts.
