Description

All guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. The rooms have air conditioning, and some units at Cesars Temple De Luxe have a terrace. All rooms feature a wardrobe.

A buffet breakfast is available each morning at the accommodation.

The wellness area at Cesars Temple De Luxe is comprised of a fitness centre and a hammam. You can play tennis, table tennis and billiards at the hotel, and car hire is available.

Garden of Tolerance is 5.7 miles from Cesars Temple De Luxe. Antalya Airport is 23.6 miles away.
