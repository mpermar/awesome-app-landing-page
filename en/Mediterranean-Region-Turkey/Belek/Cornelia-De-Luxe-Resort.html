Description

The air-conditioned rooms at Cornelia De Luxe Resort feature a modern interior. All rooms have a sitting area, satellite TV, and a minibar. Some rooms have panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea.

Cornelia De Luxe offers 24-hour food and beverage service, with a main buffet restaurant and 5 other restaurants offering dishes from Italian and Mediterranean, Turkish, French and Far East cuisines. There are also 8 different bars on site.

Guests can enjoy a large selection of sports and activities at Resort Cornelia De Luxe, including tennis, aerobics, billiards and bowling. Children can have fun at the mini club, which features a kids nightclub.

Cornelia De Luxe Resort’s entertainment staff organizes live shows with folk dancing, music and acrobatics during the evening.

Antalya Airport is 22 miles from Cornelia De Luxe Resort, and Antalya’s city center is 28 miles away.
