Description

The bungalows of the Mussulo Resort By Mantra are decorated with light wood and orange details. All have air conditioning, cable TV and kitchenette. The balconies offer panoramic views of the complex.

The Mussulo By Mantra outdoor pool has walkways, shade areas with palm trees and a central fountain. Guests can also enjoy a tennis court, gym and sauna.

The Mussulo Resort By Mantra offers all-inclusive accommodation, with a varied buffet breakfast, lunch and dinner buffet. The bar serves drinks and cocktails.

The hotel is 91 km from Guararapes Airport, and offers free parking and free Wi-Fi.
