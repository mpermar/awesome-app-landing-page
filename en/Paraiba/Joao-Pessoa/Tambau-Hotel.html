Description

The rooms have magnificent views of the sea or the garden. They also have air conditioning, radio and cable TV. The complex offers WiFi and 24-hour reception service.

Guests of the Tambaú Hotel will have access to the large wellness center with a Turkish bath, sauna and massage facilities. Guests can play beach volleyball and tennis. Guests can enjoy the playground and the games room.

The hotel is housed in an eclectic building and houses a restaurant serving international and local cuisine, a bar, a travel agency and a hammock area. The complex's business facilities include 12 meeting rooms and an auditorium with a capacity for 1,700 people.

Tambaú Hotel is 9 km from the center of João Pessoa and 23 km from Presidente Castro Pinto International Airport.

Tambau is a great choice for travelers interested in food, sightseeing and local food.
