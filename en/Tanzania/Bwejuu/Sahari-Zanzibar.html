Description

The rooms are decorated in a typical Colonial style and feature a small patio with sea views. They include a four-poster bed with mosquito nets, a fan and a fridge. The private bathrooms have a shower with hot and cold water.

A breakfast buffet is served every morning. Sahari Zanzibar features a restaurant, which serves international, European and Swahili cuisine. The hotel’s bar serves cocktails and refreshments.

Guests can relax under the palm trees by the pool. Sahari Zanzibar also provides chairs and canopy beds for guests on the beach.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surrounding area for a fee, including diving, snorkeling and fishing. The Paje Kite Beach is 2 miles from the hotel and Zanzibar International Airport is a 45-minute drive away.
