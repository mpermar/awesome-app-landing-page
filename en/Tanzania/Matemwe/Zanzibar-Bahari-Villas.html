Description

Each room here includes a terrace. Featuring a shower, private bathroom also has a bathtub or shower and free toiletries. Extras include a sofa, a desk and an outdoor sitting area.

At Zanzibar Bahari Villas, there is a 24-hour front desk and a bar. Other facilities at the property include water sports facilities, a shared lounge and a tour desk. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the area, including cycling, horseback riding and fishing. The property provides free parking.

Kisauni Airport is 25 miles away.
