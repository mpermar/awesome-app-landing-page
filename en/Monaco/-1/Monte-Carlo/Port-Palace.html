Description

All of the luxury air-conditioned guest rooms at Port Palace include satellite TV and a minibar. Each has a private dressing room and private bathroom complete with a whirlpool bathtub, bathrobe and slippers. Free Wi-Fi is provided throughout the property.

Restaurant La Marée serves fresh fish and seafood, and guests can dine on the terrace overlooking the marina. Guests can shop in Monaco’s large range of luxury stores nearby and the famous Monte Carlo casino is just a 6-minute walk away.

Access to Monaco Train Station is a 5-minute walk from the property. Private parking is available on site and Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel.
