Description

A 5-minute walk from the People's Liberation Monument and Linjiangmen metro station, Glenview ITC Plaza Chongqing houses an indoor swimming pool, a fitness centre and 2 dining options.

Free Wi-Fi is provided. This property is a 15-minute walk from Chongqing Hongyadong and a 20-minute drive from Chongqing North Railway Station.

Chongqing Jiangbei International Airport is a 30-minute drive away. Tastefully decorated, elegant guestrooms feature modern interiors and clear floor-to-ceiling windows.

Each unit is equipped with a comfortable living area, flat-screen satellite TV and an en suite bathroom with a shower and bathtub.

Guests can enjoy beautiful views of the garden, relax in the sauna or make sightseeing and ticketing arrangements at the tour desk.

The 900-square-metre pillarless grand ball room and 4 other meeting rooms suit the needs for conferences and events of different scales.

Impression Chinese Restaurant serves Sichuan and Cantonese dishes. International dishes and daily buffet spreads are available at the 6th Sense Restaurant.

Guests can also spend a relaxing afternoon enjoying tea and refreshing beverages at the Pearl Roth Lobby Lounge.
