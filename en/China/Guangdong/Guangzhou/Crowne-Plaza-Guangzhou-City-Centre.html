Description

A relaxing stay awaits at Crowne Plaza Guangzhou City Centre, whose spacious rooms offer stunning skyline views.

Featuring free Wi-Fi, it has a spa, fitness centre and 4 dining options. Just 200 metres from Guangzhou Metro Station, Crowne Plaza Guangzhou City Centre is a 20-minute drive from Tianhe Express Train Station.

Guangzhou Baiyun International Airport is about 52 minutes' drive away. Offering the comfort of carpeted floors and sofas, large rooms are decorated with classic wood furnishings and artwork.

They come with flat-screen TVs, iPod docks and dressing areas. Guests can explore Guangzhou easily with city shuttles and tour assistance.

Over 2000 square metres of meeting space including a 985-square-metre pillar-less ballroom with 9-metre-high ceiling and 8 function rooms with state-of-the-art audio-visual equipment.

Dining highlights include Cantonese organic dishes at Baiyuelou restaurant and handmade noodles at Hao Chi restaurant.
