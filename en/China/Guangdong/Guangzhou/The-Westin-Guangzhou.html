Description

The Westin Guangzhou is just a 7-minute drive from Guangzhou East Train Station. It is a 45-minute drive to Guangzhou Baiyun Airport and a 5-minute walk to the airport express bus in Citic Plaza from the hotel.

Westin's contemporary and elegant rooms feature wood-paneling and floor-to-ceiling windows offering city views. Rooms are equipped with a 32-inch flat-screen TV, Bose Wave radio and private bathroom with separate rain shower and soaking tub. Free toiletries are provided.

The Westin Guangzhou features a 24-hour gym, sauna and pools. The Heavenly Spa by Westin™ offers hydrotherapy and traditional Asian treatments. The well-equipped business center is suitable for conferences and events.

Chinese specialties can be enjoyed at Hong Mian while Italian dishes are served at Prego. Seasonal Tastes offers an all-day buffet and à-la-carte dining featuring international favorites and plentiful continental options. A variety of beverages are offered at lobby lounge.

Tian He is a great choice for travelers interested in gourmet food, food and local food.
