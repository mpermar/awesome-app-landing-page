Description

Good View Hotel Tangxia offers accommodation in Dongguan, a 30-minute drive from Shenzhen city.

It is located beside Dazhongling Reservoir. Free WiFi is available in all areas. The hotel is a 15-minute drive from Tangxia Coach Station.

Zhangmutou Railway Station is located a 25-minute drive away. Each room at this hotel is air conditioned and is fitted with a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Guests may find an electric kettle and a mini bar. The rooms are fitted with a private bathroom with a bath and shower, with slippers and free toiletries provided.

Guests may enjoy views from the room. Good View Hotel Tangxia has a 24-hour front desk offering tour and ticketing services.

Well equipped business facilities are provided. Parking is available for free. Guests may enjoy the on-site restaurants.
