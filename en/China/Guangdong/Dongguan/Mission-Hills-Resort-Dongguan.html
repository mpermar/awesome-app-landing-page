Description

Surrounded by the Great Barrier National Forest Park and Mission Hills Golf Club, Mission Hills Resort Dongguan offers luxury with five golf courses, a fitness centre with panoramic views, and a spa and wellness centre.

A hiking trail overlooking the golf courses awaits the nature lovers. Free parking on site and free Wi-Fi in rooms are both provided.

Resort Mission Hills Dongguan is a 40-minute drive from city centre or Shenzhen Bao’an International Airport.

It takes 1. 5 hours by car to the Central of Hong Kong. Air-conditioned guest rooms feature either views of the mountain or views of the lake.

Amenities include a work desk, cable television, a DVD player and a minibar. The property offers a heated indoor pool and an outdoor pool.

There is also a trampoline paradise, a mini zoo and botanical garden, and a picnic barbecue area.

The resort boasts ample space for meetings and events including a grand ballroom and 38 multi-function rooms.

World Kitchen serves fine cuisine of different flavours across the globe. Traditional Cantonese meals can be enjoyed at the Tao Yuan Chun Xiao Restaurant while lite bites can be savoured at the Melange of piano bar style.
