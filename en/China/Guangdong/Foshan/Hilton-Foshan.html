Description

Only 6. 2 km from Foshan Ancestral Temple, Hilton Foshan features sumptuous comfort with luxurious guestrooms, convenient modern facilities and a variety of recreational activities.

Blending in by splashing around in the 2 swimming pools, relaxing at the spa centre or dining at the 5 on-site restaurants and bars seems like a nice getaway from daily work.

Free WiFi is available in public areas. Hilton Foshan is a 20-minute drive from Guangzhou South Railway Station, while Bai Yun International Airport is an 1-hour drive away.

Pazhou International Exhibition Centre can be reached in 1 hour car journey from the property and hotel provides daily shuttle bus service between hotel and Conton Fair Complex during Canton Fair period.

Guests can reach Foshan Century Lotus Sports Center in 5 minutes' drive and Foshan Lingnan Pearl Gymnasium in 15 minutes' drive from the property.

Offering lovely views, each room comes with a 42-inch flat-screen satellite TV, a minibar, an in-room safe, ironing facilities and a sofa.

The en suite bathroom has a bathtub and a separate shower. Some units have Executive Lounge access.

Guests who are on vacation can laze in soothing massage and pampering sauna. Ticketing services and car rentals are available for those who would like to explore around by themselves.

Currency exchange, concierge service and daily maid service are all provided. The all-day restaurant OPEN serves all meals, with an innovative array of seafood buffet and a-la-carte delights, while Yuxi Chinese Restaurant provides authentic Chinese dishes and features traditional Shunde Cuisine.

Yuu Japanese restaurant offers a fine selection of Japanese food. Refreshing beverages and afternoon tea set can be enjoyed at Lu Bar and Aqua Lobby Lounge.
