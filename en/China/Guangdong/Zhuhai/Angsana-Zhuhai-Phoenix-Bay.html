Description

Located at the foot of Phoenix mountain, set along a the coast, ANGSANA ZHUHAI PHOENIX BAY features accommodation in island style.

The 202 luxurious and spacious rooms and suites are fitted with a private balcony or garden with great sea views.

ANGSANA ZHUHAI PHOENIX BAY is 6 km from Shijingshan Park, while Zhuhai Jinwan Airport is 41 km away.

Free public parking is possible on site. Featuring air conditioning, all rooms and suites come with a wardrobe, sofa, flat-screen TV, minibar and refrigerator.

The private bathroom has bath and shower facilities, free toiletries and hairdryer.

During the stay, guests can enjoy aromatherapy massage at the spa and wellness centre, or workout at the fitness centre.

The front desk is available 24 hours. The on-site buffet restaurant and bar serve elegant and rich dining options.
