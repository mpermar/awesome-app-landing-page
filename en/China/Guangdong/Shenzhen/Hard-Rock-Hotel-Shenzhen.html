Description

With Songyuanxia Park as its backdrop, Hard Rock Hotel Shenzhen offers upscale accommodation with a rooftop pool and a fitness centre in the Mission Hills area of Shenzhen.

In combining modern Chinese architecture and world-class service, this hotel aims to deliver a lavish experience in true Hard Rock fashion.

Free WiFi is available in all public areas of the property. Free private parking is also available on site.

Shenzhen Stadium is a 30-minute drive from Hard Rock Hotel Shenzhen. Shenzhen Convention & Exhibition Centre is about a 40-minute drive from this property.

Shenzhen Bao'an International Airport can be reachd within 50-minutes by car. The 258 extravagant guestrooms and suites are here to take your vacation or business to the next level.

Fitted with extra long beds, each room also provides a private bathroom equipped with a hot tub and a walk-in shower.

Chic contemporary decor and thrilling amenities are on offer at this hotel. Extras include bathrobes, towels, slippers and free toiletries.

Tennis, golf and bowling, whichever you prefer, your need for sports can be satisfied here.

Guests can kick back at the hotel bar with wine or champagne, or relax on a sun lounger by the rooftop pool.

A steam room can be found at this property. The hotel also offers car hire services and currency exchange service.
