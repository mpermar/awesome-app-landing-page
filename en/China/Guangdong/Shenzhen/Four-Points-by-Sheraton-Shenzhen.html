Description

Four Points by Sheraton Shenzhen is a 10-minute walk from Lok Ma Chau Port, Huanggang and Futian Border Crossing, and a 10-minute drive from Shenzhen Exhibition Centre.

The stylish property offers an outdoor pool, a spa and a fitness centre along with 4 dining options and a lobby bar.

Parking is possible on site. Modern guest rooms and suites are fully air-conditioned and come with a private bathroom.

Amenities include a working desk, electric kettle and a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

The private bathroom has shower facilities, free toiletries and a hairdryer. Guests can enjoy a variety of massage and beauty treatment at the on-site spa.

Laundry and dry cleaning services are also available on request. Meeting facilities and fax/photocopying service are available.

International dishes can be enjoyed in Taste Signature Restaurant, while casual dining and quick bites can be found in The Lounge.

China Spice offers exquisite Cantonese cuisine and flavours from all across the provinces.

The Grill & Bar provides guests with a wide collection of steaks, wines and signature cocktails.

Four Points by Sheraton Shenzhen is a 10-minute drive from the central business district and a 20-minute drive from Window of the World.

The nearest airport is Shenzhen Bao'an International Airport, 30 minutes' car journey from the property.
