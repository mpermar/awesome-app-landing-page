Description

Kingkey Palace Hotel Shenzhen is a 10-minute drive from Sha Tau Kok Control Point. It is 0.6 mi from OCT East and 12 mi from Luohu Port and Luohu Railway Station. Bao’an International Airport is 47 mi away.

Each soundproofed guestroom is equipped with a flat-screen cable TV, mini-bar and balcony. The luxurious bedding and fabric, cozy seating area and clear windows offering ample natural lights create an elegant atmosphere. Bathrobes and slippers are stocked in the attached bathrooms.

For an indulgent experience, it is a good choice to stay fit with a workout in the fitness center, hold a blistering barbecue party, or rent a car to explore the surroundings. Local souvenirs can purchased at the gift shop. Staff can assist with babysitting, currency exchange and concierge services.

Tian Tao Court serves a wide range of seafood, while international flavors can be found at Palace Café. Tounoki Japanese Restaurant offers a fine selection of Japanese dishes.

Yantian is a great choice for travelers interested in beaches, seafood and sightseeing.
