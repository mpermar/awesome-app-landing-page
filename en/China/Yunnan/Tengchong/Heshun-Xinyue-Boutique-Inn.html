Description

Situated in Heshun Old Town, Heshun Xinyue Boutique Inn provides accommodation with a garden and free WiFi.

Each unit has garden views, and guests can enjoy access to a terrace and a shared lounge.

Heshun Library is just a 2-minute walk from the property. Laifengshan National Forest Park can be reached in 12 minutes' car ride.

The nearest airport is Tengchong Tuofeng Airport, about 30 minutes' car journey from the accommodation.

At the hotel the rooms are equipped with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV. The private bathroom is equipped with a bath.

Bathrobes, free toiletries and a hairdryer can be found for your convenience. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property.

Assistance with tickets, tour arrangement and currency exchange can be provided at the reception.

Guests may relax at the on-site garden, or have fun cycling in the surrounding area.
