Description

All rooms features air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a electric tea pot, a shower, free toiletries and a desk. Each room comes with a private bathroom and a hair dryer, and certain rooms are equipped with a terrace. The rooms include a seating area.

A continental or buffet breakfast is available each morning at the property.

The hotel has a grill. Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near Hotel Wonderland.

The reception can provide advice on the area in order to help guests plan their day.

Nine-Tripod Dragon Pond is half a mile from the accommodation, while Lijiang Hua Ma Street is 2.6 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Lijiang Sanyi Airport, 17 miles from Hotel Wonderland.
