Description

The Sheraton Jinan Hotel boasts indoor and outdoor pools, a 24-hour fitness centre and luxurious rooms with free wired internet.

A 30-minute drive from Jinan Airport and Railway Station, it offers 4 dining options.

Local attractions such as Black Tiger Spring is 7 k m from Sheraton Jinan Hotel while Thousand Buddha Hill is 8 km away.

Each modern room comes equipped with a flat-screen satellite TV, safe and minibar.

An electric kettle and bottled water are also provided. Rooms offer views of either the Olympic Sports Centre or the surrounding mountains.

Recreation facilities for relaxation include a steam bath and spa services. Sheraton Jinan has a 24-hour front desk and provides laundry services and free private parking.

Feast features an international buffet while traditional Chinese dishes are served at YUE.

Miyabi overlooks Jinan and offer Japanese cuisine. Cakes, pastries, coffee and cocktails are available at ConneXions lobby lounge and café.
