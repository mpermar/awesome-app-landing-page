Description

Within walking distance of the Olympic Sailing Center in Shinan District and a 15-minute walk from Yan'erdao Road Subway Station (line 2), the InterContinental Qingdao offers its guests breathtaking sea or city views.

It is 2 km from the downtown May Fourth Square. Free WiFi and free parking are both available.

The InterContinental Qingdao is about 8 minutes' walk from Qingdao Haixin Square and a 12-minute walk from Qingdao Jiashike Shopping Centre.

It is 10 km from Qingdao Railway Station while Qingdao Liuting International Airport is located 35 km away.

Qingdao InterContinental has fully air-conditioned guest rooms featuring a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Rooms are equipped with a tea/coffee maker and a minibar. Guests can enjoy a workout at the fitness centre or unwind at the spa & wellness centre.

An indoor swimming pool is also available. Services provided by the hotel include laundry, car rental and a tour desk.

This luxurious hotel also provides the most convenient access to popular yachting and sailing activities that Qingdao is famous for.

The Lounge offers a range of snacks throughout the day. The property is also surrounded by various dining, shopping and entertainment venues.
