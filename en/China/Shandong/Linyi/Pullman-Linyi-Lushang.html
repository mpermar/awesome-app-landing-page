Description

Offering sweeping views of Yihe River, Pullman Linyi Lushang boasts an indoor pool, karaoke facilities and luxurious rooms with free wired internet.

Linyi’s city centre is a 5-minute drive away. On-site parking is free. Pullman Linyi is a 5-minute drive from The Former Residence of Wang Xizhi, a tourist spot.

Linyi Airport is 10 km away. It takes 10 minutes by car from the hotel to Linyi International Conference and Exhibition Centre.

Decorated in pleasant pastel colours, all rooms are fitted with extra long beds, ironing facilities and an iPod docking station.

A minibar and a flat-screen TV are also included. En suite bathrooms have both a bathtub and shower facilities.

Guests can enjoy a soothing massage treatment, check emails at the business centre, or visit the nightclub.

Car rentals, meeting facilities and a chargeable airport shuttle service are also available.

Café One serves international buffet spreads and à la carte dishes all day, while Gold Garden has delicious Chinese cuisine on its menu.
