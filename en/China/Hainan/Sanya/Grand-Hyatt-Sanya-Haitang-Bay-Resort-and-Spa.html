Description

Grand Hyatt Sanya Haitang Bay Resort and Spa is located in Haitang Bay, right beside the beach.

Three themed swimming pools, the Huan Spa and a fully equipped fitness centre offer guests complete options for renewal and well-being.

Spacious guestrooms and suites are equipped with modern-style decoration and air conditioning.

Each unit comes with a bathtub, free toiletries and a hairdryer. The 8 villas provides private and relax stay experiences.

The exclusive Camp Hyatt provides a paradise for children too - an imaginative playground with indoor and outdoor adventures, programmed activities and junior culinary classes.

The hotel is about 30 minutes' drive from Yalong Bay Train Station, 45 minutes from Sanya Train Station and Sanya Phoenix International Airport.

The Haitang Bay duty free shopping centre is only around 5 minutes' car journey away.

Five restaurants and bars combine Hainan traditional culture with contemporary design and extraordinary international cuisine.
