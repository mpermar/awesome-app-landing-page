Description

Featuring a private beach and a private pool, Lihe Hotel is located in Sanya, about 10 minutes' drive from Sanya Phoenix airport.

It offers business venue, a swimming pool, kids' club, fitness centre and SPA services.

Free WiFi is covered in all areas and guests can enjoy free parking. Nanshan Temple is 26 km from Lihe Hotel, while Tianya Haijiao Tourist Area is 9 km away.

It is a 6-minute drive from Coconut Dream Corridor and a 32-minute drive from Sanya Bay.

Sanya Phoenix International Airport is 6 km from the property. Featuring beautiful sea view and a private balcony, each room at this hotel is air conditioned and is equipped with a TV.

A sofa, a mini bar and a desk are provided. The private bathroom has a bath or shower, slippers and free toiletries.

There is a 24-hour front desk providing free concierge, luggage storage as well as currency exchange service.

Free use of bicycles and bike hire are available at this hotel. At leisure time, guest can play in the water park, stroll in the garden or exercise in the gym.

For dining, the hotel's restaurants serve buffet and a la carte menu featuring delicious seafood barbecue on the beach with live band performance.
