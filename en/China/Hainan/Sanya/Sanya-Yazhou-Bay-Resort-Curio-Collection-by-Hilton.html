Description

Located in Yazhou Bay in western Sanya, Sanya Yazhou Bay Resort, Curio Collection by Hilton is just about a 10-minute drive from Nanshan Temple, Sanya Dongtian Park and Yazhou Old Town.

The property features a private beach area with beautiful sunset view. Sanya Yazhou Bay Resort, Curio Collection by Hilton is a 15-minute drive from Tianya Haijiao Tourist Area.

The nearest airport is Sanya Phoenix Airport, 25 minutes by car from the property.

Sanya city centre is 35 km from the resort. Tropical garden or sea views can be enjoyed from the balcony in all 266 units.

All rooms and suites come with carpeted floor, a seating area with sofa, a flat-screen satellite TV and an electric kettle.

Private bathroom is equipped with a bathtub and shower facilities. Guests can have fun playing mahjong and cards, splash in the 2,000-square-metre outdoor pool or exercise at the well-equipped 24-hour fitness centre.

A free kids club can be found on site as well. There are 5 on-site restaurants and bars.
