Description

Located in Yalong Bay, Grand Metropark Resort Sanya offers luxurious air-conditioned accommodation in Sanya.

This tropical resort presents an outdoor pool, a gym and free Wi-Fi access. Surrounded by landscaped gardens of Southeast Asian style, spacious rooms feature 37-inch flat-screen TVs and private balconies.

Comfortable sofas and a large bathroom with a bathtub and hairdryer are provided.

Guests may enjoy exercise at the fitness centre. Added conveniences include DVD rentals, laundry service and free parking.

Lake View Café serves Western and South-East Asian dishes, while fresh seafood barbecue buffets are available at Sunset Garden.

Other dining options include Oriental Noodles and a British Pub. Grand Metropark is just 5-minute walk to Yalong Bay Golf Course.

It is a 5-minute drive from Yalong Bay Central Square and Bai Hua Vally Food and Beverage Centre.

Yalong Bay Railway Station is a 15-minute drive away. It takes 30 minutes from the resort to Sanya Phoenix International Airport or City downtown.
