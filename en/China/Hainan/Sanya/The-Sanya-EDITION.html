Description

Set within 50 acres of lush tropical greenery, The Sanya EDITION features a striking 20,000 square metre artificial saltwater pond with various water sports offered.

The hotel has a year-round outdoor pool and spa centre, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

The Sanya EDITION is a 10-minute walk from Duty Free Mall and a 15-minute drive from Wuzhizhou Island Recreation Park.

Tianya Haijiao Tourist Area is 41 km from The Sanya EDITION, while Sanya University is 20 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Sanya Phoenix International Airport, 35 km from the property.

All of the 506 guestrooms in the horseshoe-shaped hotel offer spectacular ocean views.

In addition, 11 hotel villas are nestled into a terraced hillside with ocean views, private pools, and pavilion spaces.

The Sanya EDITION features a 24-hour front desk and boutique shops at the property.

An art gallery, lifestyle retail shop, Playland for children and the sanctuary at the Spa provide a diverse selection for every guest.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including golfing and cycling.

From signature seafood fine dining Xian Hai, modern European restaurant Egret, to casual beachside Balinese grill Barbacoa, or trendy rooftop lounge Sky Bar, 4 restaurants and 2 bars on site celebrate culinary innovation and creative design.
