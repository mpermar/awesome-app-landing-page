Description

This hotel is located in Haitang Bay Sanya, a place full of palms, white beaches and abundant sunshine.

The beach is a 6-minute walk away. It features a private beach, a well-designed lagoon swimming pool, a tennis court, a fitness center, Spa, a children's park, a 2500-square-metre camping area, a water park and a zoo.

Sheraton Sanya Haitang Bay Resort is only 2 minutes' drive from Duty Free Shopping Malls.

It takes 40 minutes to drive to Sanya Phoenix International Airport or downtown Sanya.

Yalong Bay Railway Station is around 25 minutes' drive away. The spacious, air-conditioned rooms at the Sheraton Sanya Haitang Bay Resort each have a modern bathroom with bathrobes, slippers, and toiletries.

The resort has 4 restaurants serving a range of local and international cuisine, as well as 1 bars offering a wide selection of cocktails and wines.

Those who want to have meals in their room can order room service 24 hours a day.

Here at Sheraton Sanya Haitang Bay Resort, guests will find convenient stores, tropic fruit stores and a children's game room.
