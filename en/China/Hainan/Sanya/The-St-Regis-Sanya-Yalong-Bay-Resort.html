Description

The St. Regis Sanya Yalong Bay Resort offers relaxing beach-side living with a private beach, luxurious yachts, exclusive St.

Regis butler services and a 1200-square-metre infinity pool. Designed to look like intertwining dragons, its spacious rooms come with a balcony.

Sanya Yalong Bay Resort is located on Hainan Island, about 10 minutes' drive from Yalong Bay Tropical Paradise Forest Park.

It is a 1-hour drive from The End of the Earth and a 40-minute drive from Sanya Phoenix International Airport.

Sanya Duty Free Shop and Sanya Railway Station are both 30 minutes' drive away. Elegant rooms come with modern interiors and floor-to-ceiling windows that provide plenty of natural light.

Each tastefully decorated room is equipped with a 46-inch flat-screen TV, iPod dock and private bathroom with a bathtub.

Recreational choices include playing a game of tennis and enjoying a massage. Snorkelling and fishing activities are also available.

The property features a private home cinema. Driftwood Seafood Restaurant serves a fine selection of fresh seafood, while Chinese specialities are offered at Ming Xuan Chinese Restaurant.
