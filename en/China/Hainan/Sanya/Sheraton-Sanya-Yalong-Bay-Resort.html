Description

Featuring a beachfront location, the luxurious Sheraton Sanya Yalong Bay Resort is 1 km from Yalong Bay Central Square and just opposite the Yalong Bay Golf Club.

It features 5 swimming pools, water sport activities including snorkelling, canoeing, surfing or motor-boating as well as a spa centre.

Free Wi-Fi is provided in public areas. Sheraton Sanya Yalong Bay Resort is a 19-minute drive from Yalong Bay Railway Station and a 45-minute drive from Sanya Phoenix International Airport.

It is a 15-minute walk from Flower Valley Commercial Street and a 5-minute drive from Yalong Bay Outlets.

It is 25 km from Sanya City Centre and 38 km from the world renowned Tian Ya Hai Jiao (The End of the Earth).

Spacious rooms feature stylish decor and modern amenities. Each is equipped with tea/coffee making facilities and flat-screen TV with cable channels.

The attached bathroom comes with rain shower or a separate bathtub. Guests can pamper themselves with an extensive choice of massage therapies at the Shine Spa.

Other recreational facilities include a well-equipped fitness centre, a hair salon and a full tennis court.

The meeting facilities are also available for business needs. Guests can choose from a variety of dinning options at the resort.

Overlooking Yalong Bay, the Lotus Cafe serves a selection of international dishes.

Asian delicacies can be enjoyed at the Spice Garden. Baiyun Restaurant offers a fine selection of Cantonese, Hainanese and seafood specialities.
