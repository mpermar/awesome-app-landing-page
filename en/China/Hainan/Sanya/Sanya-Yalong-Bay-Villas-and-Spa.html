Description

Exclusive beach retreats await at Yalong Bay Villas and Spa with a private beachfront and private pools.

It boasts several water sports and a large lagoon pool stretching through tropical landscapes.

Located within the Yalong Bay National Tourism Resort Area, Yalong Bay Villas is about 5 minutes' car ride from Yalong Bay Tropical Forest Park and 20 minutes' drive away from Sanya city.

A 30-minute car ride takes you to Sanya Phoenix International Airport. Free parking is available.

Set in beautifully landscaped grounds, Balinese-style units open out to tropical or pool views from private balconies.

Free wired internet, bathrobes and a tea/coffee maker are provided. Each villa has a private inner courtyard and a swimming pool.

The villas all come equipped with a living room, dining room, kitchen, babysitting room, garden, lotus pond, SPA pavilion and parking space.

The property boasts spa treatments and children's club. Added conveniences include a business centre and concierge services.

Traditional Hainanese dishes are served at Leisure Island and Orchid Restaurant. Southeast Asian cuisine is featured in Baba Nyonya Restaurant while light refreshments are offered at Lobby Bar.
