Description

Located in the heart of Yalong Bay National Resort district where is close to Yalongwan Central Square, Flowers Valley Shopping Centre and local food courts, Palace Resort & Spa Yalong Bay Sanya is only about a 10-minute walk from Yalong Bay beach.

The property is around a 5-minute drive from Yalong Bay Tropical Forest Park. Yalong Bay Railway Station is about a 20-minute drive away.

It takes approximately 40 to 50 minutes to drive to Sanya Railway Station and Phoenix International Airport.

Each spacious guest room is fitted with a 55-inch curved TV set with international channels, branded mattress, minibar, in-room safe and ironing facilities.

High-speed internet access is provided. The private bathroom comes with shower facilities and a bathtub.

Guests can enjoy swimming pool view at the balcony. Sea views are available from corridor for some units.

Colorful swimming pool covers the area of 3000 and with the Heated Kids Pool, Pirate Ship and Water Park.

Indoor Kids Club covers the area of 650 for kids to start a cool holiday trip, which consists of 6 theme areas including Naughty Fun Castle, Warriors Training Camp, Drift Bumper Cars, Sketch Aquarium, Passion Climbing Area and Parent-Child Interaction Area.

Here at Palace Resort & Spa Yalong Bay Sanya, guests can enjoy Chinese and western buffet breakfast with more than 4,000 fish of nearly 30 kinds in the first ocean theme restaurant in Yalong Bay and expect special dining experience served by the chef.

Qiongzhong Xiaochu also offers authentic local cuisines. Guests are welcome to savour at the lobby lounge or on-site bar.
