Description

Located in Sanya, Wingate by Wyndham Sanya Luhuitou has an outdoor pool, 2 children's pools and water sports facilities.

Facing the Sanya Harbour and Sanya River, it is set beside the Luhuitou Park and surrounded by palm trees and lush mountains.

There are restaurant and bar at this hotel. Free WiFi is available in all areas. The hotel is a 16-minute drive from Dadonghai Scenic Area and a 20-minute drive from Phoenix Island.

It is a 40-minute drive from Sanya Phoenix International Airport. Rooms and suites here are all soundproofing and air-conditioned.

Each room has a satellite TV. Private bathroom comes with a walk-in rain shower. Some units also offer views of the sea or mountain.

Wingate by Wyndham Sanya Luhuitou has an outdoor children's playground as well as an indoor children's club.

Guests can also work out at the fitness centre. Well-equipped meeting and banquet rooms are also available.
