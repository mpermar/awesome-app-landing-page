Description

Boasting a private beach area, 4 outdoor swimming pools and a rooftop indoor swimming pool, Crowne Plaza Sanya Haitang Bay Resort offers an uber luxurious stay in Sanya.

Sanya Duty Free Store is a 10-minute walk from Crowne Plaza Sanya Haitang Bay Resort.

The nearest airport is Sanya Phoenix International Airport, a 45-minute drive away.

Resembling a gigantic cruise ship sailing into Haitang Bay, the architectural design of Crowne Plaza Sanya Haitang Bay Resort is visually stunning.

Featuring 3D ventilation, this property offers upscale guestrooms. The spa centre provides tranquil treatments while you also have the option to keep fit at the gym with sea views on the top floor.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can provide tips on the area. Children will be well accommodated with outdoor children's playground as well as the children's club.

Cai Feng Lou provides you with special Chinese and Western dishes. You can also enjoy a fine rooftop barbecue buffet dinner at the 270-degree Restaurant and Bar with live band performance.
