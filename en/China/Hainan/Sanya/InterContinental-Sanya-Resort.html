Description

InterContinental Sanya Resort is a 5-minute drive from Luhuitou Park or Xiaodonghai Tourist Area, while Dadonghai Beach can be reached by a 10-minute drive. It is 30 minutes from Sanya Phenix International Airport and 25 minutes from Sanya Train Station by car. It is a 10-minute drive from Dadonghai.

All rooms at the Sanya InterContinental feature a 42-inch flat-screen TV, an iPod docking station and high-speed internet access. A tea/coffee maker and mini-bar are also provided. The hotel offers 24-hour room service.

Guests can enjoy massage treatments at InterContinental Sanya’s spa or have a workout at the fitness center. A business center, convenience store and florist are available at the resort.

A range of Asian dishes can be found at Asian Village while the café serves freshly brewed cappuccino. The Beer House has an in-house live band and offers European & Asian dishes. A 5-minute drive takes you to the shops and restaurants of Dadong Hai.
