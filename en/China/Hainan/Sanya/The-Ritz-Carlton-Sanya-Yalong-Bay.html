Description

The Ritz-Carlton in Sanya is located within Yalong Bay National Resort District. The hotel offers 4 outdoor pools and 2 tennis courts.

The property boasts 8 dining options, pampering spa services and yoga lessons are also offered.

Sanya Phoenix International Airport is about a 50-minute drive away. Yalong Bay Tropic Paradise Forest Park is a 10-minute drive from the Ritz-Carlton Sanya.

Yalong Bay Central Square Totem Pole is an 8-minute drive from this property. Rooms at this hotel are luxurious and modern, fitted with elegant marble flooring.

The air-conditioned rooms come with private balconies overlooking the ocean, mangrove or garden.

Flat-screen TVs with Russian channels, DVD players and a minibar are included in all rooms.

The en-suite bathrooms are equipped with a bathtub, branded toiletries and a separate shower.

Ironing facilities and a safe can be found in each room. A seating area along with a work space are also provided.

This property boasts one of the largest spas in the region, The Ritz Carlton Spa.

Relaxing massages can be enjoyed in private treatment rooms and steam baths. There is also a fitness centre, beach volleyball courts and an ocean-front wedding chapel.

The property also provides sunscreens, mineral water and beach towels. The hotel's 4 restaurants offer food choices ranging from modern Cantonese cuisine, southern Italian dishes to fresh barbecued seafood.
