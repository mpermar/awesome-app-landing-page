Description

Located in Sanya Yalong Bay, right next to the beautiful ocean and the beach, Aegean Boutique Suites Resort Sanya offers fully furnished family suites with a fully equipped kitchen, a washing machine, a balcony and a spa bath.

Offering a daily shuttle bus to downtown Sanya local trade market, the hotel features a landscaped outdoor swimming pool and fitness and water sports facilities.

Aegean Boutique Suites Resort Sanya is a 3-minute drive from Yalong Bay Central Square or Baihua Valley and a 5-minute drive from Yalong Bay Golf Course or Mid-Peninsula Yacht Harbor.

It takes 5 minutes by car to Yalong Bay Forest Park. Sanya Phoenix International Airport is about 40 minutes' drive away.

Fitted with a spa bath and a balcony, suites at Aegean Boutique Suites Resort Sanya feature spacious layouts and classic furnishings in calming neutral shades.

A fully equipped kitchen with a washing machine is provided. Lazy afternoons can be spent relaxing with a soothing massage or with an active workout at the gym.

A variety of activities like hiking, cycling and diving are available. Themed evening buffets and a blend of Asian and international flavours are on offer at Fusion Restaurant.
