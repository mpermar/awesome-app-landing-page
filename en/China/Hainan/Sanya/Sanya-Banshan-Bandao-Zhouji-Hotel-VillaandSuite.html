Description

Featuring free WiFi and a spa centre, Sanya Intercontinental Hotel Villa&Suite offers accommodation in Sanya, 34 km from Nanshan Temple.

The hotel has an outdoor pool and hot tub, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

Each room is fitted with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and an electric kettle.

Certain units include a seating area. All rooms are fitted with a private bathroom fitted with a bath.

Extras include bathrobes and slippers. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property.

This hotel has a private beach area and bike hire and car hire are available. Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including golfing, snorkelling and cycling.

Luhuitou Park is 1. 7 km from Sanya Intercontinental Hotel Villa, while Tianya Haijiao Tourist Area is 19 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Sanya Phoenix International Airport, 15 km from Sanya Intercontinental Hotel Villa&Suite.
