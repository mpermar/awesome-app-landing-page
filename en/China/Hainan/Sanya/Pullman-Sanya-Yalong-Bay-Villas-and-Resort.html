Description

Exclusive getaways await at Pullman Sanya Yalong Bay, with luxurious villas in Southeast Asian styles, each with its own pool.

It boasts a spa, fitness centre and a direct access to Yalong Bay Golf Club. Pullman Sanya Yalong Bay Resort & Spa is set within the famous Yalong Bay National Resort.

It is 30-minute drive to Duty Free Shop or Sanya Phoenix International. Featuring a tasteful blend of Southeast Asian architectural styles, spacious units boast easy pool access and a bathroom with a view into the room.

Villas come with a living room, a hot tub, private swimming pool and garden. Leisure options include 9-hole and 18-hole golf courses, relaxing spa massages and a range of water sports at the beach.

Staff can provide concierge and tour planning services. Overlooking the swimming pool, which is built like a lagoon, Rive Gauche serves hearty international buffets with a selection of seafood and meats.
