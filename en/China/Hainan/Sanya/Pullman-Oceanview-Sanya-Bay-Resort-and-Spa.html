Description

Located in Sanya Bay, Pullman Oceanview Sanya Bay Resort & Spa is only a 2-minute walk from the beach and the Coconut Dream Corridor.

Decorated in French styles and surrounded by lush tropical plants, it features a private beachfront, day spa and several landscaped pools.

Pullman Oceanview Sanya Bay Resort & Spa is a convenient 15-minute drive from the city centre and Sanya Phoenix International Airport.

Free private parking is available. Opening out to stunning bay views from private balconies, modern rooms at this luxurious property feature full-length glass doors.

Kitchen, living room and dining areas can all be found in the spacious apartments with ocean view and 8 villas.

Leisure options include tennis courts, a golf court and water sports facilities. Guests can laze in a soothing massage, splash around in the pools or work out at the 24-hour fitness centre.

Hearty international meals and all-day dining are available in the stylish Sands Western Restaurant while The One Chinese Restaurant offers a daily Dim Sum menu.

There are 5 bars and lounges. Red Sun Bar on the rooftop serves afternoon drinks and offers amazing ocean views of sanya bay coast.
