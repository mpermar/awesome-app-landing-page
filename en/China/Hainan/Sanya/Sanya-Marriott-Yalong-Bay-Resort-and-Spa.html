Description

Featuring over 450 metres of beach, Sanya Marriot Resort & Spa provides a relaxing setting with the ocean, mountains and landscaped gardens as backdrop.

The direct access beach is complimented by a large 10 hectare tropical garden perfect for peaceful walks in the warm sea breeze.

Colourful fabrics and wooden furnishings give each air-conditioned room a tropical feeling.

Each comes with a 42-inch flat-screen TV, minibar, a private balcony with commanding views of Yalong Bay.

Sanya Marriott Resort & Spa is around 500 metres from Yalong Bay Golf Club and around 30-minute drive from Sanya Phoenix International Airport.

Yalong Bay high-speed train station is approximately a 10-minute drive away. Free on-site parking is available.

Multiple swimming pools, a dedicated Kids Club, and signature Quan Spa ensure the complete resort experience while an Ocean Front Wedding Chapel, outdoor self-contained meeting center surrounded by water features combined with a range of conference rooms offer innovative venues for meetings and celebrations of all kinds.

When it comes to meals, 3 on-site restaurants offer Japanese, Chinese and international favourites.

Exotic cocktails can be enjoyed at View Lounge, Sea Breeze Pool Bar & Grill and Deep Blue Beach Bar.
