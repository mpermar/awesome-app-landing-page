Description

Featuring southeast Asian tropical style, Sanya Haiyun Resort Hotel offers accommodations with panoramic sea views and a 350-metre wide private beach area.

It is only 6 minutes drive from Sanya Phoenix Airport, 8 minutes drive from the city and 15 minutes drive from the nearest railway station.

The hotel has a forest-themed children's restaurant, a marine theme children's playground and water slides for children.

It also boast tennis courts, a 2000-square-metre outdoor pool and pools of various temperatures.

Offering sea views from its private balcony and large windows allowing for lots of natural light, all air-conditioned units come with a minibar, a flat-screen cable TV, a personal safe and ironing facilities.

The attached bathroom has a bathtub. Butler service is provided upon request. Blending in by relaxing at the spa centre, working out at the fitness centre or making use of the business centre seems nice.

Convenient guest services include currency exchange, luggage storage and concierge service.

Laundry, dry cleaning and ironing facilities are all available. There are 4 on-site dining options with international flavours.

Bali-style Western Restaurant serves cuisines from around the world, while Yunhexuan Restaurant offers a selection of local Chinese cuisine and a mix of Southeast Asian flavours.

Seafood buffet hotpots can be enjoyed at the Fisherman's Wharf Restaurant with live music performances.
