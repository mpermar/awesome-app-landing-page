Description

Located about a 5-minute drive from Haitang Bay International Shopping Complex, Shangri-Las Sanya Resort & Spa features accommodation with a private beach area and 6 on-site dining options.

It also offers three outdoor swimming pools including two heated ones. Free Wi-Fi is provided throughout the whole resort.

Shangri-Las Sanya Resort & Spa is about a 30-minute drive from Yalong Bay Railway Station.

Sanya downtown area and Phoenix International Airport are both approximately 50 minutes' drive away.

Offering lovely views from the balcony, all rooms come with a minibar, fridge and electric kettle.

A satellite TV is equipped in each room. They also offer a safe, a sofa and ironing facilities.

Free toiletries bathrobes, slippers and a hairdryer are all provided in the en suite bathrooms.

The Rock is an indoor playground customised for children and teenagers. Chi, the spa, features Shangri-La signature treatment and massages.

There are 4 restaurants and 3 bars serving international cuisines and lovely drinks.
