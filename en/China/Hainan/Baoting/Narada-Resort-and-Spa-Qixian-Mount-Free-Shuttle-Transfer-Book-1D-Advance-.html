Description

Narada Resort & Spa Qixian Mount is located at Qixianling National Forest Park. The resort is decorated in Li and Miao style and is built in a tropical rainforest with on-site hot spring.

It is a 5-minute drive from the golf club and a 10-minute drive from Qixian Mount.

Qixian Mount Narada Resort is a 30-minute car ride from Yanuoda Rainforest Tourism Area.

It is a 15-minute drive from Baoting Bus Terminal and a 50-minute drive from Lingshui High-Speed Railway Station.

The nearest Airport is Sanya Pheonix Airport, approximately a 2-hour drive away. Shuttle service from the Lingshui Station or Sanya Airport to the resort is available upon reservation.

The air-conditioned rooms at Narada Qixian Resort Spa feature private balconies overlooking the natural green landscape and hot spring bath.

A flat-screen cable/satellite TV and tea/coffee making facilities can be found in all rooms.

Guests can enjoy pampering massage therapies at the spa or lounge by the natural hot springs or laze in the hot spring bath.

The property owns 3 outdoor swimming pools. Children will have fun at the kid's club and the adults can have a T'ai chi lesson.
