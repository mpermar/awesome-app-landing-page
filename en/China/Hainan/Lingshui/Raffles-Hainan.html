Description

Featuring a private beach area, Raffles Hainan is a 5-minute drive from Qingshuiwan Golf Club and a 15-minute drive from Chiling Scenic Area. Wuzhizhou Island is a 20-minute drive away and downtown Sanya is a 45-minute drive away.

The accommodations include a flat-screen TV, air conditioning and a balcony. Complete with a refrigerator, the dining area also has an electric kettle and a coffee machine. Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with a bathtub and a hairdryer. Some rooms offer sea views. Extras include a minibar, an iPod dock and a sitting area.

At Raffles Hainan there is a playground, pool tables, ping-pong facilities and tennis court. Free shuttle services to airport and tourist attractions are provided.

Cantonese cuisine and seafood can be enjoyed at Bai Wei Chinese Restaurant, and Mediterranean meals are available at Sapori. Other dining options include Villa Club, Clearwater Bay Loung, Long Bar, Desa Asian restaurant and Sing Ken Ken bar.
