Description

Positioned on Haidian Island which is the centre of Haikou, Hualuxe Haikou Seaview is an up-scale setting offering comfortable and elegant accommodation as well as lovely dining options and authentic Chinese tea and tea wares.

Additionally, free WiFi and free parking space are provided. The property offers both city views and sea views.

You may splash around in the pool, or maintain regular workouts in the gym. Hualuxe Haikou Seaview is 10 minutes’ drive to Haikou’s central business district.

You may reach Haikou Railway Station in 20 minutes' drive and reach Meilan International Airport in 40 minutes' taxi ride.

Blending traditional Chinese elements with modern fixtures as well as nature elements, all rooms include a flat-screen cable TV, an iPod dock and a relaxing seating area.

The bathroom has slippers, bathrobes and free toiletries, along with a hairdryer.

Through the windows of certain rooms, you may take in views of Qiongzhou Strait and Haikou City.

The hotel includes the largest pillar-less ballroom in Haikou and 2 beachfront lawns for events and activities.

Guests can have a relaxing stroll in the tropical garden, or rent a car to explore the city.

The 24-hour front desk can also help with luggage storage and currency exchange. There are 5 restaurants here, ideal for leisure dining with families or friends.
