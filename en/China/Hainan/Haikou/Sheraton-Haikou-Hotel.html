Description

Sheraton Haikou Hotel is located on the west coast of Haikou City, close to Haikou Wuyuanhe National Wetland Park.

The elegantly styled hotel gardens are connected to the beach. The hotel has a coastal infinity pool and 8 hot spring pools with water from 700 metres underground.

The hotel has 4 restaurants and lounges. Free parking is provided. The hotel is a 10-minute drive from Haikous downtown business district and a 5-minute drive from Hainan International Convention and Exhibition Centre.

It is also adjacent to 2 international standard golf courses, and close to Wuyuanhe Cultural and Sports Center and Changying Global 100 Wonderland.

Each room has an oversized private balcony with views of the elegant tropical gardens or views of the sea.

Each room features an ergonomically designed bed, a spacious work area, free high-speed broadband Internet access and a personal safe.

The bathroom has a rain shower and an extra large bath with hot spring water from 700 metres underground.

The hotel has a 24-hour gym, billiards, basketball court, private mahjong room, indoor and outdoor playgrounds, and a water playground for children.

The hotel's garden features a variety of flowers and trees, a fish pond and waterfall waterscape.

The environment has attracted many cute little squirrels to settle in here. It also offers over 1,300 square metres of meeting and banquet space including sea view lawn, pillarless banquet halls, function rooms and a VIP room.

The 2 restaurants offer exquisite traditional Cantonese cuisine, Chaozhou cuisine and local delicacies as well as global cuisines.
