Description

Set near Mission Hills and Feng Xiaogang Movie Town, Renaissance Haikou Hotel offers rooms with air condition and free WiFi.

It features an all-day restaurant serving international gourmet made of fresh local ingredients.

Other dining options include a traditional Hong Kong bakery and a bar. The property is 9 km from Haikou South Bus Terminal and 14 km from Meilan International Airport.

Rooms here are all fitted with a flat-screen TV, in-room minibar and electric kettle.

The private bathroom comes with a walk-in shower and a bathtub. Free toiletries, bathrobes and slippers are also provided.

Certain rooms also have a balcony and feature city views. At Renaissance Haikou Hotel guests can take a dip into the outdoor pool or work out at the well-equipped fitness centre.

There is also a Spa centre where a variety of facial and body treatments can be enjoyed.
