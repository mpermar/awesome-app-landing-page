Description

All rooms at the hotel are equipped with a shower, free toiletries, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and Blu-ray player. Rooms have a electric tea pot, while certain rooms come with a balcony and others also offer river views. At Yue Jv Tong Li every room comes with a seating area.

An Asian breakfast is available each morning at the accommodation.

Staff at the reception can provide guidance on what to do in the area.

Tiger Hill is 13 miles from Yue Jv Tong Li, while The Lingering Garden is 13 miles away. The nearest airport is Sunan Shuofang International Airport, 30 miles from the property.

Wu Jiang District is a great choice for travelers interested in local food, walking and riverside walks.
