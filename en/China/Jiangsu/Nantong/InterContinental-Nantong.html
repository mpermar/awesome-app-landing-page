Description

Located in Binjiang Park, just steps away from the Yangtze River, InterContinental Nantong is 2.

6 km away from the city’s business district. The Wolf Mountain, where the famous Nantong Buddhist Temple is located, is only 1.

9 km away. InterContinental Nantong is just a 25-minute drive from Nantong Xingdong International Airport.

It takes only 30 minutes to drive to Yangtze River Bridge, which connects the city with Shanghai or Suzhou.

The trendy guest rooms and suites offer river or park views. All rooms are equipped with flat-screen TV, minibar and coffee/tea makers.

The spacious bathrooms are facilitated with a shower area, an over-sized bathtub and branded bathroom amenities.

InterContinental Nantong provides free parking, gym, indoor pool and 24-hour front desk service.

Guests are very welcome to enjoy the landscape of hotel garden and the fish pond while taking a walk or jogging in the yard.

Within walking distance of the property, famous local delicacies can be found. The property has 4 restaurants and 1 lobby lounge.

The all-day-dining restaurant, West Commune, provides Asian and western selections.

The East Commune is the place to enjoy Cantonese cuisine and traditional Nantong flavours.

Cai Feng Lou delights diners with delicacies of Huaiyang cuisine and personalizes services.

Hitori Japanese Restaurant is a vibrant Japanese eatery catering to urban tastes.
