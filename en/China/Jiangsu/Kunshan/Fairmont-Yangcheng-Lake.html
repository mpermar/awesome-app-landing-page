Description

Situated by Yangcheng lakeside, Fairmont Yangcheng Lake offers you comfort with luxurious guestrooms, modern and well-appointed facilities, convenient guest services and 2 on-site restaurants with international flavours.

Free WiFi and free wired internet are available throughout the entire property. Fairmont Yangcheng Lake is a 15-minute drive from Suzhou Industrial Park and 25-minute drive from Zhouzhuang Water Town and Kunshan South Railway Station.

Shanghai Hongqiao Airport is about an hour's drive away. Free parking is available.

Offering views of the park or the lake, each room comes with an iPod docking station, flat-screen satellite TV with DVD player, minibar, fridge, in-room safe, wardrobe and en suite bathroom with shower facilities.

Some units have a coffee machine or a bathtub. Fairmont Yangcheng Lake features a variety of event spaces, suitable for conferences and banquets of different sizes.

Guests may pick vegetables at the on-site farm, or enjoy themselves with entertainment activities in the park.

Blending in by swimming in the indoor and outdoor pools, lazing in soothing massage or going fishing for a day seems like a nice getaway.

The Western restaurant Essence serves a wide selection of buffet, while Yi Feng Court serves authentic local food and Cantonese cuisine.
