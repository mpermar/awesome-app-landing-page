Description

Located in the East Lake Scenic Area of Wuhan, Wanda Reign Wuhan features a beautiful scenery view and comfortable surroundings with nearby shopping streets including Wanda Plaza and Han Show.

Rising up on the banks of Donghu River, Wanda Reign Wuhan offers luxurious accommodation with free Wi-Fi in all areas.

Featuring free wired internet, the upscale European-style rooms offer either city views or lake views.

Wanda Reign Wuhan is a 3-minute drive from Tingtao Scenic Area, a 5-minute drive from Hubei Provincial Party Committee office, Hubei Provincial Government, Wuhan University and Hubei Museum.

Tower of Yellow Cranes, Hubu Street, Happy Valley are within a 15-minute car journey away.

Hongshan Square is 3. 6 km away. Wuhan Tianhe International Airport is a 30-minute drive away.

Rooms here will provide you with a flat-screen TV, air conditioning and a minibar.

There is also an electric kettle. Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with a bathtub and a hairdryer.

Some rooms offer lake views. Extras include a seating area. The hotel offers sauna facilities.

Other facilities offered include ticketing service and luggage storage. The property offers free parking.

Pillar-less grand ballroom and spacious function rooms are equipped with simultaneous interpretation systems, video conference systems and digital video conference systems.

The unparalleled Club Reign provides the distinguished members exclusive services and a gathering place to relax.

Wanda Reign Wuhan features 3 restaurants with different flavour. International buffet and a la carte are offered at Café Reign while the original flavors of Huaiyang cuisine combined with the essence of Cantonese and traditional local dishes are served in the River Drunk Huaiyang Restaurant.

Exquisite and contemporary cuisine incorporated with both Japanese and French styles are provided in HE Japanese Restaurant.
