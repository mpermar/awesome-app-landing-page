Description

Shangri-La Hotel Xi'an is situated in the Gaoxin High-tech Development Zone. It is about a 55-minute drive from Xi’an Xianyang International Airport.

The nearest Airport Express Coach Station is a 6-minute drive away. It offers stylish accommodation with 4 dining options, an indoor pool and rooms with free wired internet and WiFi.

To ensure a high quality of fresh air, air purifiers are fitted in the lobby, all restaurants and every guest room.

Shangri-la Hotel Xi'an is 1 hours' car journey to Terracotta Warriors Museum. Xi'an City Wall (Yongning Gate) is 20 minutes' drive away.

It takes 10 minutes walking to Keji Road Subway Station (Line 3). Along Line 3, guests can transfer to Line 2 at Xiaozhai Station and connect to the city centre or North Railway Station.

Transfer from Line 3 to Line 1 at Tonghuamen Station, guests can connect to Xian Textile City or Xian West Bus Terminal.

Spacious guest rooms offer modern furnishings and are fully air conditioned. Each is equipped with a work desk, tea/coffee making facilities and a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

A spacious marble bathroom comes with a separate shower and bathtub. Guests can work out at the 24-hour gym or relax with a massage.

The hotel features spa and a 24-hour fully equipped health club with heated pool.

Besides, staff at the tour desk can assist with making travel arrangements. Meeting facilities and the business centre are available.

Yi Cafe serves a selection of western dishes. Chinese food can be found at the Tian Xiang Ge Restaurant, while Siam Garden offers a variety of Thai delicacies.
