Description

Located in Pingyao Ancient City and set in a Chinese quaint siheyuan house, Jinxuan Hotel Pingyao offers accommodation which is full of Chinese elegance.

The stone and wood carvings create a unique taste of local life style. The nearest airport is Taiyuan Wusu International Airport, 73 km away from the property.

Each refined room features air conditioning, wardrobe and cable TV. A seating area is also available in all rooms.

The private bathroom comes with shower facilities, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

For guests' convenience, free WiFi is featured throughout the property. Here at Jinxuan Hotel Pingyao, recreation facilities including KTV, Mahjong room and tea house.

Staff are glad to assist guests with luggage storage and provide touring information at the 24-hour front desk.

A shared kitchen is available for guests to prepare their own meal. The in-house restaurant serves local cuisines.
