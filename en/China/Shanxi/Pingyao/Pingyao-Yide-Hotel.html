Description

Housed in a traditional courtyard property with a 300-year history, Pingyao Yide offers Chinese-style rooms with hand-made fine wood fittings and artwork.

It is located in Ancient City of Pingyao, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It provides free WiFi.

Yide Hotel Pingyao is a 5-minute walk from the West Street and Rishengchang Former SIte.

Tastefully furnished with bright prints, the air-conditioned rooms come with a flat-screen cable TV and side table.

Private bathrooms offer heated showers and a hairdryer. Guests can enjoy a relaxing massage or explore the neighbourhood on a bike.

For guests convenience, the hotel provides tour-booking, car hire, ticketing services and laundry services.

The on-site restaurant opens from 06:50 to 22:30 daily, serving Chinese and western style meals.
