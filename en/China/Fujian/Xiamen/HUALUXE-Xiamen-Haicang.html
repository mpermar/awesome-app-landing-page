Description

Ideally located on the coastal landscape belt of Haicang Bay, HUALUXE Xiamen Haicang features rooms with unparalleled view of Xiamen Harbor.

Free WiFi is provided throughout the entire property. HUALUXE Xiamen Haicang is a 5-minute drive from Gulangyu Island and Xiamen Island Terminal (Layuyu Ferry Terminal) and a 10-minute drive from Xiamen Island.

Xiamen Gaoqi International Airport is a 25-minute drive away, while a 30-minute drive takes you to Xiamen North Railway Station.

Spacious and elegant, the carpeted flooring guest rooms here feature a heated pool, a dressing room, a well-stocked minibar and a cable TV.

Guests can relax on the sofa and enjoy the city view in the room. A hot tub, pool towels and a personal safe can also be found in the room.

Each en suite bathroom comes with bathrobes, a hairdryer, slippers and free toiletries.

Barbeque facilities are available for guests to have fun with family and friends.

Guests can maintain their work out at the fitness centre. Yoga classes are also available.

Guests can arrange their travel plans and purchase tickets to attractions via the tour desk.

A business centre with well-equipped meeting facilities is also accessible for business needs.

A buffet style breakfast is served at the property. Caifenglou and Xianyan Restaurant offer an a la carte menu of authentic Chinese cuisines.
