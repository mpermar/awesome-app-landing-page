Description

Overlooking the Pacific Ocean and surrounded by vast greenery, Seaview Resort Xiamen is a 10-minute walk from the beach.

With an alluring tropical garden designed by international architect, this hotel offers free internet access, 3 swimming pools and one of them is an infinity pool.

Xiamen International Conference & Exhibition Centre is a 5-minute drive away. Xiamen Seaview Resort is a 20-minute drive from Gulangyu Ferry Pier, Zhongshan Pedestrian Street and Xiamen University.

Xiamen Railway Station is a 25-minute drive away while Xiamen Gaoqi International Airport is 30 minutes' drive away.

Xiamen North Railway Station is a 40-minute car journey away. Blending with luxury, nostalgic charm of the old times, the rooms at Seaview Resort come with modern interiors and have plenty of natural light.

All are fitted with a flat-screen TV and a seating area. Toiletries and a hairdryer are provided.

Through the balcony of some rooms, you may take in refreshing views of the garden.

Guests can work out at the fitness centre, or enjoy a game of tennis. A wide range of leisure facilities is available for guests' choice, including a luxury cinema, a table tennis room, a spa centre and a children's club.

The hotel provides car rental services and free parking. Ticket bookings can also be made at the tour desk.

To host various upscale meetings or activities, the hotel would be a nice option offering advanced facilities and spacious space.

Fragrance Tea Palace serves a variety of Chinese dishes while French food is available at Jazz Bar & Restaurant.

Other dining choices include organic dishes at Villa Restaurant and wine at Skyview Lounge.
