Description

Boasting a private hot spring bath, elegant rooms feature classic furnishings and soothing neutral shades. They come with free broadband internet, comfortable sitting area and spacious bathroom.

Resort Riyuegu Hotsprings is 20 miles from Xiamen Gaoqi International Airport and Xiamen Train Station. It offers hourly shuttles from Lundu Ferry Terminal and provides free parking.

Guests can relax in the hot spring pool or enjoy traditional Asian massages. Other recreation options include an archery range, tennis courts and karaoke lounge.

Local specialties can be sampled at Jing Yue Xuan while the 24-hour Sunflower Café serves international favorites. Room service is available.
