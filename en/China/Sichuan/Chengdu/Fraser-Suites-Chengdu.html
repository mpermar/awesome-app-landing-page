Description

Located about a 6-minute drive from Tian Fu Square and the bustling Chunxi Road, Fraser Suites Chengdu is around a 30-minute drive from Shuangliu International Airport.

The luxurious hotel offers a 2-storey health club with an indoor pool. Step out, you may walk to the airport shuttle bus spot and Jinjiang Hotel Underground Station (Line 1) in around 5 minutes.

Chengdu Fraser is centrally situated close to various commercial and shopping complexes in Chengdu.

You may reach Chengdu Railway Station, Wuhou Memorial Temple, Jinli Ancient Street and Kuanzhai Ancient Street in around 20 minutes' drive.

Conceived by world-renowned architect, the stylish rooms of Fraser are well-fitted with a flat-screen TV and an in-room safe.

Each room comes with a kitchen and en suite bathroom. In addition, free internet is available in all rooms.

Featuring shower facilities, the private bathroom comes with free toiletries and other lifestyle amenities.

Guests can work out at the fully equipped gym or relax in the massage room. The hotel also offers steam and sauna facilities.
