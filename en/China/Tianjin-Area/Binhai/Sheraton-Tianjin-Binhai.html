Description

Centrally located in the Tanjian Economic-Technological Development Area, Sheraton Tianjin Binhai features luxurious non-smoking rooms with a 37-inch flat-screen TV.

Facilities include an indoor pool and 5 dining choices. Modern rooms feature elegant interiors and floor-to-ceiling windows.

All well-appointed rooms come complete with a minibar, sofa seating area and an attached bathroom with a bathtub.

Guests can indulge in relaxing body treatments at the spa, or exercise at the gym.

For convenience, the business centre provides ticketing and currency exchange services.

Car rental services are available at the tour desk. Available for all-day dining, Feast restaurant serves international dishes.

Other dining alternatives are Italian specialities at Bene Italian Kitchen and Chinese food at YUE restaurant.

Sheraton Binhai Tianjin is 3 km from Binhai International Convention and Exhibition Centre and 38 km from Tianjin Airport.
