Description

Guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a coffee machine. Every room includes a electric tea pot, while some have a balcony. At Zhi Shang Luxury Hotel, all rooms have a TV with satellite channels.

A buffet or à la carte breakfast is served at the property. The on-site restaurant serves Chinese cuisine.

Bike rental and car rental are available at this hotel and the area is popular for cycling.

Speaking English, Korean and Chinese, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical advice on the area at the reception.

Zixia Taoist Temple is a 10-minute walk from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Zhangjiajie Hehua Airport, 15 miles from Zhi Shang Luxury Hotel.

Wu Lingyuan is a great choice for travelers interested in family-friendly trips, cleanliness and traditional food.
