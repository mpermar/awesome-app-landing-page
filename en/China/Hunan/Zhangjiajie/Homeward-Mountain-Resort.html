Description

Featuring free WiFi and an outdoor pool, Homeward Mountain Hotel is a pet-friendly accommodation with mountain view.

The hotel provides free pick-up and drop-off service between the property and Yangjiajie Ticket Office in Zhangjiajie National Forest Park.

Guests staying for 2 nights or more can enjoy free airport pick-up once during 06:00-22:00.

Wulingyuan is 2 km from Homeward Mountain Hotel. Yuanjiajie Scenic Area is 3. 7 km away.

The nearest airport is Zhangjiajie Hehua Airport, 42 km from Homeward Mountain Hotel.

Rooms have a TV. Certain rooms include a seating area where you can relax. Some units feature views of the pool or river.

All rooms come with a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries.

You will find a 24-hour front desk and a barbecue area at the property. There is a mini library, a tea house and a bar.
