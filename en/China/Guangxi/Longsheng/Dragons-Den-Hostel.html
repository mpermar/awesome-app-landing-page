Description

Surrounded by large expanses of rice terraces, peaceful retreats await in the quiet environs of Dragon’s Den Hostel.

Featuring wooden architecture and a games room, it provides affordable and cosy accommodation with free WiFi.

The charming property is located in the scenic Tiantouzhai Village, around a 40-minute hike from Dazhai Village.

There is a shuttle bus service between Dazhai Village and Guilin Railway Station, which departs daily.

Simply furnished, the naturally cooled rooms are equipped with a writing desk and a seating area.

Guests get to choose between staying in lively shared rooms, or in quiet private rooms.

All guest rooms have a bathroom with a hairdryer, shower facilities and slippers.

Guests can play billiards, or organise sightseeing trips at the tour desk. The staff can also help guests arrange hiking trips.

Luggage storage and laundry services are provided. The hostel has a Chinese restaurant and a western restaurant.
