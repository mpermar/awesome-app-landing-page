Description

Nestled in the idyllic Chaoyang Village, Wada Hostel in Yangshuo is a secluded retreat where you can escape from the city buzz.

Here, free WiFi is provided throughout the all property. Local typical limber bamboo tree and Yulong River view will make your an unforgettable experience.

Yangshuo West Street is 4. 4 km from Wada Hostel in Yangshuo, while Darongshu Scenic Area is 5 km away.

Both Yangshuo Railway Station and Guilin Liangjiang International Airport can be reached in 1 hour 10 minutes' drive.

Decorated with pastel shades, the units offer soothing, natural feel and are equipped with air conditioning and a kettle.

The private bathroom is fully stocked with a hairdryer and slippers. Diverse leisure venues are set here, including a shared lounge, a library and a games room.

You may also chill out in the lush garden. Bike rental service and airport transfers can be organised as well.

The hotel provides laundry and daily maid service free of charge. You can expect hearty fare in the on-site restaurant, from where you can taste delicious and fresh dishes.

Alternatively, there is a snack bar where you can enjoy some light bites. Moreover the staff in Yangshuo Wada Hostel (Yulong River) speak both Chinese and professional English, they can provide the customized service for your trip demand.
