Description

Nestled on peak of Dongling Mountain, Dongling Resort overlooks the beautiful Yangshuo County and the Li River.

It features accommodation with free Wi-Fi, several dining options and oriental-style rooms.

Free parking is available on site. Well integrated with the natural landscapes, the elegant rooms of Yangshuo Resort have high ceilings and wooden floors.

They either come with a terrace overlooking Li River, or offer garden views full of greenies and flowers.

Each room features latex pillows, a flat-screen satellite TV and a private bathroom with shower facilities.

Reading a book, drinking a cup of tea or having a sun bath on the sun terrace seems pretty charming.

Staff will be happy to help with organising day trips nearby. There is also an indoor babies playground.

The resort is about 3 km from Impression Liu Sanjie and 5 km from Yangshuo West Street and Yangshuo bus station.

Fuli Town is around 7 km away, while Yangshuo High-speed Railway Station is approximately 29 km from the property.

The in-house restaurant of Dongling Resort serves seasonal fruits and Chinese specialties.
