Description

Yangshuo Resort is 3 miles from Yangshuo West Street, 3.5 miles from Impression Sanjie Liu and a 10-minute drive from The Big Banyan Tree. It is also 43 miles from Guilin Train Station, 45 miles from Guilin Coach Station and 59 miles from Guilin Liangjiang International Airport.

All rooms have free Wi-Fi, a sitting area with a sofa, a minibar, an iPod docking station, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a DVD/CD player. The private bathroom has shower facilities.

Guests can work out at the fitness center, store luggage at the 24-hour front desk and make trip plans at the tour desk with ticket services provided. For those who want to explore the city by themselves, car rental can be arranged by the property. After a long ride, taking a walk on the grounds or relaxing on the terrace is a nice choice. Laundry, dry cleaning and ironing are all available for an additional fee.

Riverside Restaurant serves Western food, and Yulong Chinese Restaurant has local dishes and Cantonese food. Guests can enjoy western refreshments and tea at Ancient Ferry Restaurant. Dragonfly Bar provides refreshing beverages.
