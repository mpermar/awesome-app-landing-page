Description

Set in the historical ancient village Jima, a 15-minute drive from Yangshuo West Street, Yangshuo Ancient Garden Boutique Hotel boasts an outdoor pool, a spa centre and fairy natural sceneries around.

The resort has a barbecue and views of the mountain and the stunning Yulong River, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

Free private parking is available on site. Yangshuo South Bus Station is a 12-minute drive from Yangshuo Ancient Garden Boutique Hotel, while Impression Liu Sanjie is within a 20-minute drive from the property.

Guilin Liangjiang International Airport is about 90 minutes' drive away. Yangshuo Ancient Garden Boutique Hotel offers rooms featuring a Qing-dynasty style, and each room has a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Some units feature a seating area where you can relax. Certain units have views of the pool or garden.

The rooms are equipped with a private bathroom. Extras include bathrobes and slippers.

Yangshuo Ancient Garden Boutique Hotel features free WiFi throughout the property.

You will find a 24-hour front desk, gift shop, and shops at the property. Medicare, traditional workshops as well as Tai Chi meditation are uniquely featured.

A swimming pool is on site, along with meeting rooms and restaurants. You can also enjoy drinks at the bar.

You can play darts at this resort, and the area is popular for cycling. The resort also offers bike hire.
