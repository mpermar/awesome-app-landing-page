Description

Surrounded amidst impressive Karst mountains, Yangshuo Mountain Retreat is located at the edge of Yulong River.

Free WiFi access is available. Free parking space is offered. Guests can rent bikes for free at the property.

Furnished with timber framing and bamboo furnitures, each unit has air conditioning, a safety deposit box and a minibar.

Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and bathrobes. You can enjoy mountain view from the room.

Sum up the day with a nice walk in the garden or explore the city with travel advices from the tour desk.

Mountain Retreat offers a complimentary shuttle to town three times daily. Guilin Liangjiang International Airport is 69 km away.
