Description

Only a 10-minute bike ride from Big Banyan Tree or Nine-Dragon Cave, Yangshuo Moon Nest Travelling With Resort features mountains and lush greenery around the property.

It offers traditional Chinese-style accommodation, free bicycles, an outdoor pool, a garden and an on-site restaurant.

Free WiFi is available in all areas. Yangshuo Moon Nest Travelling With Resort is 1.

5 km from Shi Li Hua Lang (Ten Li Gallery) and 1. 6 km from Moon Mountain. It is a 15-minute drive from Yangshuo South Bus Station.

Guilin Airport is about 70 minutes' drive away. Each room here will provide you with a cable TV and a work desk.

There is also an electric kettle. Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with free toiletries and slippers.

You can enjoy mountain views from the room. You can make trip plans at the tour desk, and then rent a car to explore around by yourselves.

Fishing, cycling activities are available. Both local Chinese style dishes and Western cuisines are served at the property.
