Description

Located in Yangshuo, The Bamboo Leaf Yangshuo offers a seasonal outdoor swimming pool.

Popular points of interest near The Bamboo Leaf Yangshuo include Yangshuo West Street, Impression Liu Sanjie and Li River.

Yangshuo Bus Terminal is a 10-minute drive from The Bamboo Leaf Yangshuo. Guilin Liangjiang International Airport is a 90-minute drive away.

The air-conditioned accommodation has a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a kitchenette, a dining area and a private bathroom with a bath.

All rooms offer a balcony. All units will provide guests with a fridge. The hotel offers a children's playground.

You can play billiards at this hotel and bike hire is available. Among the various facilities are a garden, a terrace, as well as a shared lounge.

Guests can enjoy a selection of local and international cuisine in the on-site restaurant.
