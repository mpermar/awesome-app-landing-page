Description

Located in Xiazhangzhai Village, Old Manor House Yangshuo is surrounded by karst mountains and fields.

The houses were originally built about 400 years ago in late Ming and early Wing dynasties and have been carefully restored.

Old Manor House Yangshuo is located only about 500 metres from Yulong River and a 10-minute car ride from Yangshuo West Street.

Guilin Liangjiang International Airport is about 75 minutes' drive away. Featuring traditional décor, each room offers air conditioning, free WiFi and a seating area.

Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Extras include a sofa and bed linen. Car rentals and ticketing services are provided at the tour desk.
