Description

Rooms come with air conditioning, and some units at the hotel have a balcony.

A continental breakfast is served daily at the property.

Mountain Stream Hostel Yangshuo has a year-round outdoor pool. The area is popular for cycling and hiking, in addition, free use of bicycles and bike rental are available at this hotel.

Speaking English and Chinese, staff are always on hand to help at the 24-hour front desk.

Yangshuo Park is a 19-minute walk from the accommodation, while Tuteng Gudao is 2.8 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Guilin Liangjiang International Airport, 40 miles from the property.
