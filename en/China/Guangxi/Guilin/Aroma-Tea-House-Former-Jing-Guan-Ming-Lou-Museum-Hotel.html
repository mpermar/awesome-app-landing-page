Description

Right opposite Two Rivers and Four Lakes, Guilin Rong Lake Aroma Tea House, formerly Jing Guan Ming Lou Museum Hotel, is located in the city centre.

It features collections of ancient Chinese antiques, modern art galleries, a Chinese-style courtyard and accommodation with free wired internet access.

Guilin Rong Lake Aroma Tea House is a 2-minute walk from Xi Cheng Pedestrian Street, Guilin Night Market or Ancient South Gate.

It is a 12-minute walk from Elephant Trunk Hill or Sun & Moon Twin Pagodas and a 15-minute walk from Guilin Railway Station and Bus Station.

Liangjiang International Airport is 26 km away. Air-conditioned guestrooms at Guilin Rong Lake Aroma Tea House feature views of the lake.

Each room is fitted with a work desk, an electric kettle and a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Selected rooms offer views of the Rong Lake. This traditional style hotel offers a collection of antiques for guests' viewing pleasure and lessons of cooking, making tea, learning Chinese and painting.

In addition, laundry and dry cleaning services are offered. Luggage storage can be found at the 24-hour front desk.

Featuring floor-to-ceiling windows and 180-degree views of Rong Lake Scenic Area, the on-site restaurant on the 5th floor serves a mix of Chinese and Western food.
