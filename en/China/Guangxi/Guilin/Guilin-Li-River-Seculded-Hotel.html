Description

Guests at the hotel can enjoy a buffet or a à la carte breakfast. The restaurant at Guilin Li River Seculded Hotel specializes in Cantonese and Chinese cuisine.

The accommodation offers a range of wellness facilities including a sauna and a fitness center.

Non-stop assistance is available at the reception, where staff speak English and Thai.

Elephant Trunk Hill is a 7-minute walk from Guilin Li River Seculded Hotel, while Seven Star Park is 1.1 miles away. The nearest airport is Guilin Liangjiang International Airport, 16 miles from the property.

Xiufeng is a great choice for travelers interested in food, excursions and boating.
