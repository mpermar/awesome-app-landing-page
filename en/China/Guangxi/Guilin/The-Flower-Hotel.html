Description

The Flower Hotel offers comfortable accommodation in Guilin. It features free WiFi in all areas.

The hotel is located a 10-minute walk from Ludi Stone Scenic Area. Every room enjoys mountain views.

The inn is a 10-minute drive from Guilin Coach Station. It takes 20 minutes to drive to Guilin North Railway Station and Guilin Railway Station.

Guilin Liangjiang International Airport is located a 35-minute drive away. The well decorated guestrooms offered by the property are all equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and an electric kettle.

The room comes with french window. Certain rooms come with a balcony or terrace. The individual private bathroom is fitted with shower.

Guests can find a hairdryer, slippers, free toiletries, coffee and mineral water.

The Flower Hotel in Guilin has a 24-hour front desk offering tour and ticketing services.

Luggage storage and airport shuttle service are also possible. Parking is available for free.
