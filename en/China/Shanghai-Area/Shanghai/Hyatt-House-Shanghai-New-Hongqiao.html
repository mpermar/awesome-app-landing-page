Description

Strategically situated about a 20-minute drive from the Hongqiao Transportation Hub and Hongqiao International Airport, Hyatt House Shanghai New Hongqiao boasts an indoor pool and a 24-hour fitness centre.

National Exhibition and Convention Centre (NECC) is around 15 minutes' drive from the property.

Free high-speed WiFi and remote printing are available throughout the hotel. Hyatt House Shanghai New Hongqiao is less than a 10-minute drive to Hopson New Plaza and Bailian Outlets Plaza.

Zhujiajiao, Shanghai Happy Valley and Sheshan National Forecast Park are within 20 minutes' drive from the property.

All units at this hotel are air conditioned, and comes with a flat-screen TV and a 1.

2-metre sofabed. Each guest room comes with a hairdryer and an electric kettle. Bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries are provided in the private bathroom attached.

The property features 5 functional meeting places suitable for conferences and events.

There are billiards, board games, vending machines and massage armchairs for guests' convenience.

The in-house restaurant Gallery Kitchen serves international cuisine, while Coffee to Cocktails Bar offers a variety of coffee, tea, soft drinks and cocktails.
