Description

Occupying floors 79 to 93 of Shanghai World Financial Centre, a skyscraper with a trapezoid aperture at the peak, Park Hyatt Shanghai is surrounded by Super Brand Mall and Oriental Pearl TV Tower.

The luxury hotel boasts a spa and wellness centre, gourmet cuisine across 8 dining options and modern Chinese-style rooms with sweeping views of the city skyline and Huangpu River.

Free WiFi is available. Situated in the heart of the Lujiazui financial district, Park Hyatt Shanghai is 500 metres from Lujiazui Subway Station (Line 2), a 5-minute drive from Shanghai New International EXPO Centre and 10-minute drive from People's Square.

Shanghai Railway Station and Hongqiao Airport are a 30-minute drive away, while Pudong International Airport is a 45-minute car journey away.

Elegantly decorated in relaxing shades of white and dark brown, each spacious room comes with a flat-screen TV with satellite and cable channels, a walk-in dressing room, a coffee machine and a daybed for in-room pampering and wellness treatment.

The private bathroom features an over-sized rain shower, a deep-soaking bathtub and heated floors.

24-hour in-room dining is provided. Guests are welcomed to indulge in a calming spa treatment at Water’s Edge, try out the state-of-the-art equipments at the Health Club, take a dip in the indoor pool, or exercise with the master at the Tai Chi courtyard.

Staff at the tour desk can assist with sightseeing arrangements and car rental service.

Also, hotel is an ideal venue for meetings and weddings. A wide range of local and international dishes can be found at Park Hyatt, such as 100 Century Avenue Restaurant featuring Western, Japanese and Chinese cuisines, and Dining Room, popular for its European specialties.

Tailor-made suppers are served at Chef’s Table, while casual all-day dining and breakfast items are available at Pantry.

Alternatively, guests can spend a relaxing evening enjoying beverages and music at Living Room, Music Room, Shanghai Lounge or Bar Level 87.
