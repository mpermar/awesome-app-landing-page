Description

Rising up on the scenic banks of Taihu Lake, Sheraton Huzhou Hot Spring Resort features ring-shaped architecture and leisure options including a games room and a fitness centre.

Huzhou Hot Spring Resort is a 6-minute drive from Yuwan Park, a 7-minute drive from Taihu Paradise, and a 10-minute drive from Qianshan Park.

It takes 15 minutes by car from the resort to Huzhou’s city centre. Huzhou Hot Spring features a lavishly designed lobby and a spa.

Guests can also laze in the solarium, go bowling, or enjoy a soothing massage treatment.

There is also a gift shop and a beauty shop on site. Beautifully decorated in pastel colours, the luxurious rooms are fitted with an iPod docking station, ironing facilities.

The ample bathrooms include both a bathtub and shower facilities. All rooms offer a private balcony and a cosy seating area.

For meals, guests can enjoy fusion international dishes at Feast, or delicious Cantonese cuisine at Yue.

Other dining options include the Lobby Lounge and Starlight offering beverages and snacks.
