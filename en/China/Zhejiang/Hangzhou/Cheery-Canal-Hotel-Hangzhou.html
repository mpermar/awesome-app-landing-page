Description

Located in the Gongshu district in Hangzhou, 8 km from West Lake, Cheery Canal Hotel Hangzhou features free WiFi access and free private parking.

The hotel has a spa centre and fitness centre, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

The renovated property is protected as one of the historical architectures of the National Silk Warehouse.

Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and features a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

You will find a kettle in the room. Extras include slippers, free toiletries and a hair dryer.

There is a 24-hour front desk, on-site shop, pet-sitting, a cash machine and hairdresser's at the property.

Meeting rooms and function areas are offered for guests' convenience while a number of recreational facilities are also offered as SPA, cards room and cigar bar.

The hotel also offers bike hire and car hire. Wushan Square is 9 km from Cheery Canal Hotel Hangzhou, while Xixi Wetland is 9 km away.

The nearest airport is Hangzhou Xiaoshan International Airport, 29 km from the property.
