Description

Sheraton Grand Hangzhou Wetland Park Resort is located next to Xixi National Wetland Park and 5 km from the West Lake and Lingyin Temple.

The resort combines traditional Chinese architecture alongside sleek and tasteful furnishing in soothing natural colours, offering landscape views of the signature gardens of Hangzhou.

Each room also features a spacious balcony providing panoramic views of Xixi Wetland.

Air-conditioned guest rooms feature spacious interiors with modern furnishings. Each is equipped with an iPod dock, a personal safe and a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

The property offers an indoor pool, a fitness centre and spa facilities. Guests can work out at the aerobics room or enjoy a spa treatment on site.

There is abudant event space on site. Free parking on site and free WiFi throughout the property are provided.

Feast Restaurant features all-day dining options, and serves a variety of Asian and international dishes.

Hangzhou and Cantonese delights can be found at Yue Restaurant. Satine provides a refined French dining experience to the guests.

Sheraton Grand Hangzhou Wetland Park Resort is 5 km from Hangzhou City Train Station, 18 km from Hangzhou East Station and 40 km from Hangzhou Xiaoshan International Airport.
