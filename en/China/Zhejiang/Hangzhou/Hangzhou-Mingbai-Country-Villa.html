Description

At the resort, every room has a desk. Rooms come complete with a private bathroom equipped with a hot tub and free toiletries, while certain rooms at Hangzhou Mingbai Country Villa also feature a seating area. The rooms have a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy an Asian breakfast.

Hangzhou Mingbai Country Villa has a playground.

Languages spoken at the reception include English and Chinese.

Lingyin Temple is 2.8 miles from the resort, while Xixi Wetland is 6 miles away. The nearest airport is Hangzhou Xiaoshan International Airport, 17 miles from Hangzhou Mingbai Country Villa.

Xihu is a great choice for travelers interested in tranquillity, nature walks and temples.
