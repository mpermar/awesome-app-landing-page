Description

Featuring free WiFi throughout the property, free on-site parking and a terrace, West Lake State Guest House is surrounded by lush greenery and is elegant in style.

Guests can enjoy sophisticated a la carte dish or varied buffet meal at the on-site restaurant.

This property was the official designated hotel where the Chinese State leaders met and hosted state dinners with foreign heads of states during G20 summit in 2016.

Chairman Mao once stayed in this property for a grand total of 27 times, giving this place specific historical significance.

Conveniently located in Xihu District, the well-known West Lake is only a 10-minute walking from West Lake State Guest House while Wushan Square can be reached in 20 minutes by car.

Hangzhou Xiaoshan International Airport can be reached in 40 minutes by car. Hangzhou Railway Station can be reached in 26 minutes by car.

All the famous touristic attractions of Hangzhou are within 30 minutes' drive of the hotel.

Every elegant and cosy air-conditioned room at this property is equipped with a flat-screen TV with both cable and satellite channels.

Rooms come with great views. Guests may find kettle and safety deposit box in the room.

The private bathroom in all units is fitted with a hot tub and shower. For your comfort, you will find bathrobes, slippers, free toiletries and hairdryer.

There is a 24-hour front desk offers a variety of services including car rental, ticketing, concierge as well as luggage storage.

During the break time, strolling in the garden or resting in the sun terrace are both great leisure choices.

Hiking is a must-do activity in the local area. There are sophisticated meeting facilities, as well as tea-houses with distinctive local flavour.
