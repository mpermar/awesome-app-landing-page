Description

The air-conditioned guest rooms has spacious interiors with modern amenities. Each has a minibar, a personal safe and a flat-screen TV with cable channels. The private marble bathroom includes a bathtub and a separate rain shower.

Sheraton Ningbo Hotel is a 10-minute drive from Ningbo Train Station and a 30-minute drive from Ningbo Lise Airport.

Guests can pamper themselves with an extensive choice of massage services at the Zenses Spa. Other recreational facilities include a reflexology parlor, sauna rooms and a full tennis court.

Mezzo's on 2 Restaurant features Italian delicacies prepared by the award-winning Chef Nelson. Japanese cuisine can be found at the Akitaya Restaurant. Guests can relax with drinks and live Jazz music at Aqua Lounge.

Yinzhou District is a great choice for travelers interested in tranquillity, friendly locals and culture.
