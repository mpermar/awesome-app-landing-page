Description

Happy Dragon City Culture Hotel (Tian'AnMen Lama Temple) is ideally located in the heart of Beijing, a 3-minute walk from Zhangzizhong Road Subway Station (Line 5).

Housed in a historic Beijing hutong, it offers free Mandarin lessons. Guests can get a special offer to Mutianyu Great Wall once you check in.

Happy Dragon City Culture Hotel (Tian'AnMen Lama Temple) is a 17-minute drive from Wangfujing Pedestrian Street.

It takes 10 minutes by subway to many tourist attractions such as Lama Temple, National Museum and Tian’anmen Square.

South Luogu Alley, Bell and Drum Towers are a 20-minute walk away. Both Beijing West Railway Station and Beijing South Railway Station can be reached in 30 minutes' drive.

Beijing Capital International Airport is 50-minute away by taxi. There is a 24-hour front desk.

The hotel’s tour desk can assist guests with making travel arrangements. Happy Dragon City Culture Hotel (Tian'AnMen Lama Temple) offers a selection of day-tour routes.

Guests can also rent a car or a bicycle to explore the city. A business centre, free Wi-Fi and free luggage storage are also provided.

The air-conditioned rooms are fitted with an electric kettle, flat-screen TV, a seating area and wooden furnishing.

For guests' comfort, towels, free toiletries and a hairdryer are provided. Some rooms are designed in traditional Chinese style.

The on-site restaurant serves Western and Chinese dishes. A wide range of drinks are also available at the bar.
