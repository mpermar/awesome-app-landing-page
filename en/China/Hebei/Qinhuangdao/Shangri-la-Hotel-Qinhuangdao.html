Description

Qinhuangdao Shangri-la Hotel is situated in Haigang district and only steps from the beach.

Guest can take a dip in the heated indoor pool, or have a relaxing massage at the spa centre.

This luxurious hotel provides WiFi and parking. Qinhuangdao Shangri-la Hotel is about 10-minute walk to Xin’ao Underwater World and Pigeon Nest Park is about 7.

8 km away. Qinhuangdao Railway Station is about 9 km away and Beidaihe Railway Station is 14.

2 km away. Overlooking the city or Bohai Bay, air-conditioned rooms come with a balcony, flat-screen TV, mini bar, refrigerator, in-room safe box and electronic kettle.

Slippers and toiletries can be found in the private bathroom. Qinhuangdao Shangri-la Hotel features a fitness centre and meeting facilities.

Currency exchange, car rental and ticketing service are possible at the front desk.

There is also a gift shop on site. For dining options, the hotel’s Lobby Lounge, Chinese Restaurant Qinxiangge and Café Qin cater for different tastes.

Guests can enjoy an international buffet spread at Café Qin or various Chinese cuisines at Qin Xiang Ge.

Premier tea and coffee as well as homemade pastries are served at the Lobby Lounge.
