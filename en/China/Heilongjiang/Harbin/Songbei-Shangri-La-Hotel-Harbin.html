Description

Set in the Song Bei district in Harbin, on the edge of the Songhua River north bank, Songbei Shangri-La Hotel, Harbin boasts an infinity pool, CHI spa centre and sauna facilities.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. Ice and Snow Big World is only about 5 minutes' drive away.

Harbin West Railway Station is 40 minutes' car journey away. The nearest airport is Harbin Taiping International Airport, 30 minutes' drive from Songbei Shangri-La Hotel, Harbin.

The rooms offer rich locally accented ineriors which reflect the area‘’s surrounding nature.

A flat-screen, free toiletries and a kettle are provided for your comfort. CHI, The Spa at Shangri-La presents traditional therapies.

The infinity swimming pool boasts a 270°degree view of the Songhua River. Work-out options include various fitness equipment and yoga courses.

There is ample space for events and conferences. Café Song is the lively hub of dining at the Songbei Shangri-La, Harbin – a modern all-day venue with buffets.

Peking roast duck and Clay Pot dishes are served at Shang Kitchen. De Drunk’n Pig Bar serves beverages with live band performances.
