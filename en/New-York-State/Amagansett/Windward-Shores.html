Description

A full kitchen and dining area is provided in every accommodations at Windward Shores Ocean Resort. The spacious units include a living area with a flat-screen cable TV. Units also offer a private patio.

Seasonal cabana service is available at this resort.

Resort Windward Shores is conveniently located along Montauk Point State Parkway. It is a 15-minute drive from East Hampton Airport, and a 13-minute drive from Maidstone Golf Club.
