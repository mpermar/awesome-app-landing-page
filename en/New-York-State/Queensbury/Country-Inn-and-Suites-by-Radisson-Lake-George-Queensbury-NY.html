Description

Featuring cable TV, all rooms at the Country Inn and Suites Queensbury hotel include a fridge, microwave and coffee facilities. Decorated in warm tones, all rooms are bright and comfortably furnished.

The Queensbury Country Inn and Suites offers a 24-hour front desk and a business center with fax and photocopying services. Complimentary newspapers are available.

Laundry facilities and dry cleaning services are available on site. A children’s playground and picnic area with BBQ facilities is on site.

Pyramids Aviation Mall is just a few feet from the hotel.
