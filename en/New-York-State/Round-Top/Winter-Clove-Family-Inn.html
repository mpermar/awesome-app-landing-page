Description

Guest rooms at the Winter Clove Family Inn include a private bathroom and free Wi-Fi. They are also air-conditioned.

Meals are served in the dining room and include a variety of American cuisine.

Tennis courts and basketball courts are provided for guests. The Round Top Winter Clove Family Inn has an antique bowling alley and children’s playground. Relax by the waterfall or visit the game room.

Hunter Mountain skiing is 20 miles from the resort. Windham Mountain is 19 miles away.
