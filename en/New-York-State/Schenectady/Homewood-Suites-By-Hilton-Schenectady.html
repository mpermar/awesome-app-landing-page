Description

At the hotel all rooms are equipped with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kitchen, a dining area and a private bathroom with free toiletries, a shower and a hair dryer. Rooms have a coffee machine, while selected rooms contain a terrace and others also feature river views. Guest rooms have a desk.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at Homewood Suites By Hilton Schenectady.

The nearest airport is Albany International Airport, 9 miles from the property.
