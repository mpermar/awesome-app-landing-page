Description

Guests staying at the Colonel Williams Resort and Suites can enjoy a picnic area complete with BBQ facilities. There is also a game room with ping-pong. Free Wi-Fi is supplied in public areas.

A flat-screen cable TV is included in every room at the Colonel Williams Resort and Suites. Guests also have access to a patio. Select suites have a kitchenette.

Lake George and Lake George Village are within 2 miles of the property. The Lake George Outlets and Six Flags Great Escape amusement park are a 5-minute drive away.
