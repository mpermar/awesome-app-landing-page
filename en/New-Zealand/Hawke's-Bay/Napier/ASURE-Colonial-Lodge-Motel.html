Description

Located in the Napier suburb of Taradale, 4. 5 star ASURE Colonial Lodge Motel offers apartments with free WiFi and a fully equipped kitchen.

Guests enjoy a hot tub and a heated swimming pool with a water slide. The property is located midway between the cities of Napier and Hastings.

The property offers spacious air-conditioned studio,1, 2 and 3 bedroom apartments.

All feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a DVD player. They offer ample relaxing outdoor dinning and BBQ areas.

ASURE Colonial Lodge features a games room with free internet, a PlayStation3 gaming console, a billiards table and table tennis.

Free parking is available on site for cars, boats and trailers. The motel is nearby two of New Zealand's oldest wineries, Mission Estate & Church Road, and is within a 5-minute walk to cafes, restaurants and shops.
