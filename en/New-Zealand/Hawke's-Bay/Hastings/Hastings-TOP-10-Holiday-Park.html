Description

Offering 2 hot tubs and a heated pool, this holiday park is set on 10 acres of gardens in the centre of Hastings.

Guests enjoy an outdoor dining area with a fireplace and barbecue facilities. Hastings TOP 10 Holiday Park is 2 minutes walk from the Splash Planet Theme Park and 12 minutes drive from Black Barn Vineyards.

Waimarama Beach is a 19-minute drive away. On site facilities include a childrens playground, a shared kitchen and a guest laundry.

All accommodation has a refrigerator, toaster and tea/coffee making facilities. Most accommodation has an LCD TV and a DVD player.

Hastings Holiday Park is within 10 minutes walk of local cafes, restaurants and shops.
