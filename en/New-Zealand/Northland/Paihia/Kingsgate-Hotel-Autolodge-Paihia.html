Description

Kingsgate Hotel Paihia offers a memorable waterfront location, just 100 metres from Paihia wharf.

The hotel offers views across the Bays brilliant blue sea and out to Russell. Situated within 5 minutes' walk of local shops and dining options, the hotel offers an ideal base to explore the scenic and historic Bay of Islands.

Accommodation includes guest rooms and one bedroom suites. The suites offer a kitchenette and a private living area.

There is satellite TV and air conditioning in all rooms. Facilities include a restaurant and bar, a swimming pool, hot tub, fitness centre and games room.

Guest enjoy unlimited WiFi during their stay. McKenzie's Restaurant is located on the first floor, and has fabulous views over the water.
