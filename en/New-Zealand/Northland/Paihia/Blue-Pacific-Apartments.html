Description

Offering luxury apartments with balconies and free high-speed Wi-Fi, Blue Pacific Apartments is situated on the waterfront of Te Ti Bay in Paihia.

It offers direct beach access and apartments with beautiful water views. All of the accommodation at Blue Pacific Apartments has a flat-screen TV and DVD player.

They also have a kitchen with a microwave, refrigerator and tea and coffee making facilities.

Some offer a private hot tub. Blue Pacific Apartments offer complimentary daily newspapers.

It also features a tour desk and free private on-site parking. Paihia Wharf is a 15-minute walk, and Waitangi Golf Club is a 25-minute walk away.

The Bay of Islands Airport is 25 minutes away by car. Activities nearby include paddle boarding, kayaking, golf, swimming, culture experiences, mountain biking,cycle trails, many water activities, hiking tracks and horse riding.
