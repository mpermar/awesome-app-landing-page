Description

Located in Kerikeri, this property is only 200 metres from the town centre. Guests have access to free WiFi and free private parking.

All rooms at Stay Kerikeri boast a low-rise retro architecture style. The rooms are large, fully self-contained and are serviced daily by housekeeping.

Facilities include flat-screen satellite TV, hairdryers, iron and ironing board. Set in a large tropical garden, the apartments feature access to a seasonal pool and barbecue area.

Free laundry facilities are available. Continental or cooked breakfasts are available and guests can enjoy in-room dining.
