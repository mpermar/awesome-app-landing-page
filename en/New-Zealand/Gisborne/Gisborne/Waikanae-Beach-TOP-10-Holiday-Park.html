Description

Waikanae Beach TOP 10 Holiday Park is located in the heart of Gisborne and features a refurbished outdoor pool.

It offers a range of accommodation options with free on-site parking. Guests enjoy 500MB of free WiFi every 24-hours.

Waikanae Beach Holiday Park is just a few minutes walk from several restaurants, cafes and shops.

Wine tastings can also be enjoyed nearby. The property is 45 minutes' drive from Tolaga Bay.

This beachfront holiday park offers self-contained apartments, bungalows and rooms with shared bathroom facilities.

All accommodation offers an outdoor seating area and tea/coffee making facilities.

Some accommodation has a fully equipped kitchen. Guests enjoy a garden with BBQ facilities and a shared kitchen.

Other facilities offered at the property include a shared lounge and a games room.
