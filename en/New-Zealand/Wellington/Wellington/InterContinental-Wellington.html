Description

The elegant guest rooms are spacious and well-furnished. All rooms feature a TV with cable channels and a large bathroom with modern amenities and bathrobes. Some rooms include access to the club lounge.

InterContinental’s Chameleon Restaurant offers contemporary cuisine and the Arizona restaurant offers a Tex-Mex menu. Guests can enjoy a snack and a cocktail at The Lobby Lounge.

The Emerge Health Club offers an indoor heated pool, a spa, sauna and massage treatments.

InterContinental Wellington provides the perfect base for exploring the region's coast line and wineries. The Te Papa Museum of New Zealand is a 5 minute walk away.
