Description

58 On Cron is located the rainforest, 5 minutes from Franz Josef town centre. The glacier is a 10-minute drive away.

The motel offers self-catering accommodation with free on-site parking. Guests enjoy satellite TV in the rooms and a voucher for free Wi-Fi access during their stay.

Guests have a choice of studio units or spacious 1 and 2-bedroom apartments. All units provide kitchen facilities and a dining area.

Some feature a spa bath. There is a spacious garden area with picnic furniture and BBQ facilities.
