Description

Voyager Apartments Taupo is located 50 metres from a safe swimming beach and it is a 10-minute stroll along the lakefront to Taupo town centre.

Guests enjoy free Wi-Fi, a heated outdoor swimming pool and a hot tub. Each of the fully self-contained studios and apartments at Voyager Apartments Taupo includes king-size beds, heating, air conditioning, satellite TV and a DVD player.

Some include a full kitchen and a spa bath. Voyager Taupo offers free on-site car parking and BBQ facilities.

Continental breakfast is available and includes options such as fresh fruit, yoghurt, cereals and toast.

Situated just a 2-minute walk from the shores of Lake Taupo, it is 10 minutes’ drive to Huka Falls and the Huka Jet.

Taupo Bungy and Taupo Golf Club are both less than 10 minutes’ drive from Voyager Apartments Taupo.
