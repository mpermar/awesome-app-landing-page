Description

Just 300 metres from the lakefront of Waipahihi Bay, 52 Onrifle Motel offers an outdoor swimming pool and a hot tub.

The self-contained apartments feature a fully equipped kitchen and a flat-screen TV.

Guests enjoy a children's playground and a games room. 52 Onrifle Motel is 3 minutes' drive from central Taupo.

It is 10 minutes' drive from Huka Falls. Orakei Korako - The Hidden Valley is 30 minutes' drive away.

Each apartment has a dining setting, a lounge area and a private bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer.

Guests can share a meal in the outdoor BBQ area. Free parking is provided on-site.
