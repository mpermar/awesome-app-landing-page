Description

Miranda Holiday Park offers a choice of dormitory and private accommodation on the scenic Seabird Coast.

Guests can relax in the hot mineral pool after a workout on the tennis court. All private rooms at Miranda Holiday Park Waitakaruru feature kitchen or kitchenette facilities, living area with TV, and a private bathroom.

Some rooms feature a spa bath. Facilities include a barbecue area, childrenâ€™s playground and shared bathroom facilities.

Guests also have access to a shared kitchen and a shared lounge room with TV. Miranda Holiday Park is located next door to Miranda Hot Springs, the largest thermal pool in the Southern Hemisphere.
