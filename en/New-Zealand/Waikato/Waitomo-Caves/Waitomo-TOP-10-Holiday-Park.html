Description

Just 600 metres from the famous Waitomo Glow Worm Caves, this award-winning holiday park features a large swimming pool, outdoor hot tub, BBQ area and a children’s playground with trampoline.

Waitomo TOP 10 Holiday Park is surrounded by the shops, cafes and restaurants in Waitomo Caves Village.

Waitomo Golf Club is only a 5-minute drive away. Each chalet includes a flat-screen TV, private terrace, private bathroom and heating.

Some have a fully equipped kitchen with a microwave, stove, refrigerator, toaster and coffee/tea making facilities.

The deluxe cabins have a private patio, a fridge and breakfast making facilities in the room.

Guests have access to communal bathroom and kitchen facilities. Facilities at Waitomo Top 10 Holiday Park include a fully equipped modern communal kitchen, a guest laundry (coin operated), 100 MB free WiFi per guest, and a shared TV room with library.
