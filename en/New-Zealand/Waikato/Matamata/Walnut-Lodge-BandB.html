Description

The newly built ​Walnut Lodge B&B offers accommodation in Matamata. Every room at this bed and breakfast is air conditioned and features a flat-screen TV.

Guests can enjoy the rural setting with views to the Kaimai ranges, as well as rolling countryside with beautiful sunsets.

Each individual studio has its own bathroom, free toiletries, kitchenette with fridge, microwave and tea/coffee facilities, separate spacious living room and dining, contemporary in design.

Complimentary continental breakfast provisions are included with a selection of cereals, yoghurt, condiments, fresh bread, juice, teas and freshly ground coffee.

Gluten Free breakfast available is available upon prior request. Hamilton is 58 km from Walnut Lodge B&B, while Mount Maunganui is 59.
