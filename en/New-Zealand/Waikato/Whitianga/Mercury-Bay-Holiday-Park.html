Description

Located next to Whitianga Estuary, this property features an outdoor pool and a BBQ area.

Guests enjoy free on-site parking. Just 15 minutes’ walk from The Lost Spring, Mercury Bay Holiday Park is 400 metres from Whitianga's town centre.

Whitianga Airport is a 5-minute drive, and Hot Water Beach is a 40-minute drive away.

All rooms are heated and offer a kitchenette and dining area. Some rooms have a TV.

Mercury Bay Holiday Park Whitianga also offers a children's playground and an outdoor dining area.
