Description

Distinction Hamilton Hotel & Conference Centre offers spacious 4-star accommodation, just 10 minutes' drive from central Hamilton.

Facilities include an indoor swimming pool, fitness centre, spa pools, a bar, and a restaurant.

Guests receive free WiFi. The hotel has 147 spacious and comfortable guest rooms.

Each room has been refurbished and is decorated and furnished in a neutral style with many modern in-room amenities including satellite TV and internet access.

Guests can enjoy breakfast and dinner at Distinction Hamilton Hotel. The on-site restaurant, Garnett’s Restaurant is a great place to enjoy a meal with friends.

Distinction Hamilton Hotel & Conference Centre is within a 5-minute walk to Te Rapa Racecourse.

Waikato Stadium, Claudelands Event Centre and Hamilton City Centre are all within a 10 minutes' drive away.
