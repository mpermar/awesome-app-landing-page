Description

Each of these stylish self-contained holiday apartments includes a fireplace and under-floor heating heating.

Guests can relax in the hot tub or seasonal outdoor heated pool. Guests enjoy access to free WiFi.

Wanaka Luxury Apartments are situated only 600 metres from the shores of Lake Wanaka.

The ski fields at Cardrona Alpine Resort and Treble Cone Ski Area are just 35 minutes’ drive away.

All apartments have a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, DVD player. Each has either a kitchen or kitchenette.

Guests have access to an outdoor wood-fire pizza oven and BBQ. Apartments with spa bath are available.

Some apartments have a wall-mounted Miele espresso coffee machine. There is an outdoor fireplace by the hot tub and a sun terrace around the pool.
