Description

Situated just 7 minutes’ drive from Queenstown’s lively centre, the 3. 5-star Swiss-Belresort Coronet Peak features a restaurant for breakfast and dinner, 2 bars and a bowling alley.

Guests enjoy free WiFi throughout the hotel. Ideally located for those looking to escape the town and get in touch with nature, Swiss-Belresort provides a free shuttle service to and from Queenstown.

Free parking and ski storage are also provided. All rooms include a private bathroom, satellite TV and a refrigerator.

Guests can enjoy use of billiard tables, air hockey and Foosball. Swiss-Belresort Coronet Peak is located 1 km from the Shotover River Jet area, which offers a range of outdoor adventure activities.

Guests can enjoy the peace of the mountains with nearby hiking and biking trails.
