Description

Take in stunning views of the beach, that is within a 2-minute walk from Pakiri Beach Holiday Park.

Located in Pakiri, in the Auckland region, this property offers a children's playground and free private parking.

The park offers a range of accommodation including self catering apartments with sea views and a flat-screen TV.

Basic rooms are also provided where guests have access to shared kitchen and bathroom facilities.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including horse riding and fishing.

Canoes are available for hire. Matakana is 18 km from Pakiri Beach Holiday Park. The property is situated along the renowned Te Araroa walkway.
