Description

Offering a lovely outdoor swimming pool and a sauna, Runciman Berries and Emus B&B overlooks a peaceful green valley, just outside of Auckland city.

Guests enjoy free WiFi. Some rooms offers a private spa bath. All rooms are heated and include satellite TV, a private bathroom, an outdoor dining area and electric blankets.

Self-contained apartments are also available and include a fireplace, kitchenette and sofa bed.

Studios with a kitchenette are also on offer. Both the studio and apartments boast views over the berry farm.

Friendly emus graze the paddocks and you can admire their chicks which hatch between August and October.

In December and January, you can pick your own berries including boysenberries and raspberries.

Runciman Berries and Emus is a 15-minute drive from Pukekohe Train Station and Papakura Train Station.

Auckland International Airport is a 25-minute drive away, and Hunua Rainforest & Waterfall is a 30-minute drive.

Delicious breakfast is available only for B&B Room with Spa Bath and includes muesli, cereals, cheeses, cold cuts, bacon, yoghurt, tea and coffee.

Free range eggs are also served and have been freshly laid by chickens on the property.
