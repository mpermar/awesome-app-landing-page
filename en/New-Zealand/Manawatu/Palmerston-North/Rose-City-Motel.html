Description

Rose City Motel is a 10-minute walk from Ongley Park, and a 15-minute drive from Palmerston North Airport.

It offers a fitness centre, free unlimited WiFi and rooms with a fully equipped kitchen.

The heated guest rooms are fitted with a work desk, ironing equipment and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

The attached kitchen has an oven, a microwave and tea/coffee making facilities. Laundry and dry cleaning services are offered.

The property has barbecue facilities with a seating area. Motel Rose City is 3 km from Palmerston North Train Station, and a 1-minute drive from the Victoria Esplanade.
