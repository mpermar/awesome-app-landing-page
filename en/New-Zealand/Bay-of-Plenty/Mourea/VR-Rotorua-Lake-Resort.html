Description

Set on 6 acres on the shores of the beautiful Lake Rotoiti, VR Rotorua Lake Resort is located is just 15 minutes’ drive from central Rotorua.

It features an outdoor pool, 2 hot tubs, a sauna and tennis courts. Guests enjoy free unlimited WiFi.

All rooms feature a private balcony or patio offering either stunning views over Okawa Bay or the surrounding gardens.

Each includes satellite TV, tea/coffee making facilities and a minibar. Some offer a relaxing spa bath.

Exercise in the small fitness centre or explore the surrounding areas using a rental bicycle.

The hotel has a private boat service that can be used for fishing charters and trips to the Manapouri Hot Pools.

Free transfers to Rotorua City Centre are also available. The Grill Restaurant is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner and serves Modern New Zealand cuisine.

There is a lounging area with a fireplace and the bar offers a large variety of local New Zealand wines.

VR Rotorua Lake Resort is 7 minutes' drive from Rotorua International Airport, Hells Gate Massage & Hot Pools and Kaituna River Wild Water Rafting & Scenic Walks.
