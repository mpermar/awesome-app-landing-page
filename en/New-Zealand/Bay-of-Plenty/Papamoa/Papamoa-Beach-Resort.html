Description

Situated on Papamoa Beach, this beachfront resort offers accommodation with a patio boasting lovely views.

Guests enjoy a small tennis court, covered barbecue area with picnic tables, children playground with a jumping pillow.

Papamoa Beach Resort is 15 minutes’ drive from Mount Maunganui and Tauranga Airport.

Waimarino Adventure Park and Spring Loaded Fun Park are both within 30 km. All accommodation features a fully equipped kitchen, a sofa and a flat-screen TV.

Each include a private bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer. Electric blankets and tea/coffee making facilities are also provided.

Guests have access to communal bathroom facilities. There are bicycles available for hire on site.

The tour desk can book local activities such as helicopter rides, dolphin tours and fishing charters.
