Description

Aura Accommodation is centrally located, 3 minutes' walk from the lake front, 4 minutes' walk from the famous Eat Streat and 9 minutes' walk from both Government Gardens and Rotorua Museum.

Guests staying at Aura Accommodation can enjoy summer, bathing in the large outdoor geothermal heated swimming pool or fire up the BBQ on a beautiful Rotorua evening.

The property is within walking distance of all the central city attractions and local markets on offer.

The fully equipped units have stove tops, microwaves and free unlimited WiFi. Also featured in the units is a Smart TV with Netflix.
