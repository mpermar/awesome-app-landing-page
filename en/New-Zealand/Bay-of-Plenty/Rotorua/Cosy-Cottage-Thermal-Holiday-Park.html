Description

Cosy Cottage Thermal Holiday Park is located in the heart of Rotorua in the Bay of Plenty Region.

Guests enjoy free access to 3 pure Geo-thermal mineral spa pools, a seasonal outdoor swimming pool, barbecue facilities and a children's playground.

300MB of free WiFi is offered per person per stay. All accommodation types have a kitchen/kitchenette fitted with a microwave, toaster, stove top, kettle and refrigerator.

Through the back door, there is a Geo-thermal land reserve and hot water sand lake beach where you can make your own foot spa pool.
