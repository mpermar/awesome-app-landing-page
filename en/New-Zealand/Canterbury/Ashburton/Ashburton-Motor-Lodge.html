Description

Ashburton Motor Lodge is a 5-minute drive from Ashburton’s town centre. It offers a children's playroom, cooked and continental breakfast delivered to your room and free use of laundry facilities.

The property is located opposite Ashburton Racing and Trotting Club and is also a 5-minute drive from the Aviation Museum and the Trotts Nursery & Garden.

It is within a 10-minute walk from at least 3 dining options. All accommodation features a kitchenette with a stove top, microwave and a refrigerator.

The heated guest rooms feature electric blankets, air-conditioning, free WiFi and a flat-screen TV with over 50 satellite channels.

The en suite bathroom offers free toiletries and a hairdryer. Ashburton Motor lodge also boasts a conference room, which can accommodate up to 25 guests.

Catering is available. Ashburton Motor Lodge is an hour’s drive from central Christchurch and Christchurch International Airport.
