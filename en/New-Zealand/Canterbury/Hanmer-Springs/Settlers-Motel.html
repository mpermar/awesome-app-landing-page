Description

Settlers is a 5-star boutique motel offering comfort and privacy, in the village of Hanmer Springs.

The motel is centrally located, just a short walk from the village’s popular thermal pools, shops, and restaurants.

Settlers Motel is well-decorated and features quality fittings and furnishings. All of the studios and apartments include air conditioning, private en suite bathrooms, and flat-screen TVs.

There is a complimentary DVD library of over 400 titles for guests to choose from.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. The Settlers Motel is a short walk from the thermal pools of Hanmer Springs.

Complimentary towels are available from the motel. Guests can enjoy many leisure activities in the natural surroundings, including hiking, kayaking, skiing and mountain biking.
