Description

Tussock Peak Motor Lodge offers modern studio's and units, with free WiFi and Netflix, just 2 minutes’ walk from the famous Hanmer Springs Thermal Reserve, the village of Hanmer, walking trails in the forest and mountain bike trails.

Each room includes heating, electric blankets, fans and a 40-inch LCD TV with satellite channels, Netflix access and a DVD player.

Most studios have a spa bath. The studios located upstairs include a balcony and the studios located downstairs offer a private courtyard.

Facilities include mountain bike storage, complimentary DVD hire and complimentary towels for use at the thermal pools.

Continental breakfast and other packages can be arranged. Hanmer Springs Golf Course is a 3-minute drive from Tussock Peak Motor Lodge.
