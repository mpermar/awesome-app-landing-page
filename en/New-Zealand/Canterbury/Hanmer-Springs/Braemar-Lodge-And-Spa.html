Description

All suites include an open-plan sleeping and living area with LCD TV, gas fire and super-king beds. Guests can pamper themselves in the marble bathroom with double spa bathtub and double shower. All suites have a private balcony, many with a hot tub.

Braemar Lodge has a fantastic restaurant with panoramic views of the Hanmer Basin and surrounding mountains. Guests can enjoy the dining menu and a selection of New Zealand wines. Dining on the outdoor terrace is also an option in the summer months.

The lodge’s own Spa Braemar offers a range of massages and body treatments.

Select Braemar Lodge & Spa is approximately a 10-minute drive from Hanmer Springs and 90 minutes from Christchurch.

Disappointing stay

"We booked several weeks in advance, however, were told only 2 nights before that the kitchen was due to have a deep clean on the day we arrived and so there would be no restaurant available, nor any room service. The hotel is around a 15 min drive from town, so we were obviously disappointed with this, as we wanted to have a few drinks with dinner and not want to worry about having to drive. So, we brought in our own food and ate in our room. As this trip was booked for our 40th birthday, it was a special treat away from our 4 children -- something of a rarity. We particularly chose Braemar for it's luxury and facilities, and I was looking forward to a facial or massage. However, when we booked in we were told that the beautician was unavailable that day, so that wasn't an option. Again - as Hanmer Village is a 15 min drive way, it was not an easy option to just "pop into town" for a massage. So, after our dinner (consisting of a cooked chicken and salad from the local supo! ) we settled into watch a movie - only to discover that Sky was not working, and so we had to settle for local, normal TV >> we couldn't even watch a movie!Although we were offered a room upgrade to compensate, as well as the opportunity to pay $10 for a breakfast upgrade from continental to cooked (which we accepted), on balance, we were disappointed with our experience.I spoke to the person at the desk upon our departure (I think his name was Jack?) and told him of our overall disappointment with our stay. He agreed that the combination of the fact the kitchen was closed, the beautician was unavailable and that Sky was not working made for a much compromised stay - quite different from what expected, and what they pride themselves on offering."
