Description

Christchurch Kiwi Holiday Park & Motels is located 5 km from the city centre. Free parking is provided.

Featuring a patio, specific units are air conditioned and feature a dining area and a seating area with a flat-screen TV.

There is also a kitchen in some of the units equipped with a microwave and a toaster.

The holiday park offers a swimming pool, children's playground, BBQ, camp kitchen, bathrooms and laundry facilities.

Guests receive 1 hour of free WiFi per 24 hours. Christchurch Art Gallery is 5 km from Christchurch Kiwi Holiday Park, while Christchurch Arts Centre is 5 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Christchurch International Airport, 14 km from the property.
