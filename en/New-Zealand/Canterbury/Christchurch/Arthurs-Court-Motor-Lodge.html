Description

Arthur’s Court Motor Lodge is situated in the centre of Christchurch, just 1 km from the Victoria Street shopping area.

All studios and apartments feature air conditioning and satellite TV. Guests receive unlimited free WiFi.

There are a number of restaurants, takeaway shops and supermarket within 2 minutes' walking distance.

Local attractions within 15 minutes' walking distance of Arthur’s Court Lodge include the Christchurch Casino, Christchurch Art Gallery and Hagley Park.

It is 10 km away from Christchurch International Airport. There is a children's play area and a garden with a barbecue area.

Other on site facilities include free parking, a shared laundry and a tour desk. All accommodation feature a private bathroom with free toiletries, bathrobes and a hairdryer.
