Description

Just 10 minutes walk from Christchurch Casino, 5 minutes' walk from Hagley Park, easy stroll to Victoria Street shops, restaurants and bars and 5 minutes' drive from central Christchurch.

Merivale is also just a 5 minute stroll where you can enjoy shopping, cafes, trendy restaurants and bars.

The Christchurch International Airport is a 15-minute drive away. Pavilions Hotel features an outdoor pool, a spa pool and a well-equipped fitness centre.

Guests also enjoy our restaurant and a bar. The spacious accommodation each offer free parking and a flat-screen TV.

The restaurant is open 7 days a week and serves a full la carte menu as well as lighter snacks.

The room service menu is available 24 hours a day. Check in/out is also available 24 hours per day.

Our room types vary and will have a solution for every need. Including our newly renovated rooms with new kitchens, bathrooms, reverse cycle air-conditioning/heat pumps to our cottages with full ovens and full services of a home away from home.

There are room types to suit every need including the business traveler, families, couples and just that quick overnight stop over.

Guests can make use of the free in-house DVD library. The hotel also features a 24-hour front desk, luggage storage facilities and 2 meeting rooms.

Unlimited free Wi-Fi is also available. We are family owned and operated for over 25 years where our hotel is our passion.

Our recent rating of 4 Star Qualmark Gold means that we are amongst the top 15% of hotels in New Zealand with operation and service procedures.

The courtyard area provides you with a great walk down memory lane and New Zealand in days gone by.

Our staff will provide you with service to exceed your expectations and your stay will be sure to memorable.
