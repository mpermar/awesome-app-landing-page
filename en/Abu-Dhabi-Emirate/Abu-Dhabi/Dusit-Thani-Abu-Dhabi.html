Description

All the rooms at Dusit Thani have warm and elegant décor. Each one has large floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the city, a flat-screen TV, an iPod docking station and a spacious working area. The bathroom has a shower and a bathtub.

A fine selection of restaurants includes an international all-day-dining venue, a contemporary steakhouse and an authentic Thai restaurant that welcome guests with personalized services and friendly atmosphere.

Guests can enjoy a workout at the fully equipped gym or take a dip in the outdoor pool. They can relax in the hot tub or indulge in a signature massage treatment at Namm Spa. Some facilities are available at an added fee.

Abu Dhabi Exhibition Centre is 15 minutes by car from Dusit Thani. Abu Dhabi International Airport is a 25-minute drive away. An airport shuttle is available upon request.
