Description

Furnished with African décor, the air-conditioned villas all include a minibar and a terrace. The private bathrooms include a bathtub, a shower and free toiletries. Some villas have a personal plunge pool.

Five dining outlets are available in the surrounding area of Anantara Sir Bani Yas Island Al Sahel Villas. There is also a gym and a private beach area that is available at a nearby resort.
