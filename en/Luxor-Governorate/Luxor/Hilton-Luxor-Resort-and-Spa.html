Experiences

12/03/2017

Superb hotel, great location - good for couples, families or solo travelers

"Amazing hotel, the breakfast buffet was the best we enjoyed during our 2+ week stay in Egypt. This is the perfect accommodation for tourists in luxor. They have a set price list for all taxi services to surrounding archeological sites, museums and downtown for both the east bank & West Bank. This eliminates one of the most frustrating aspects of traveling - especially in Egypt (dealing and negotiating a fair price with taxi drivers as Uber is only available in Cairo, Alexandria & Giza ) the prices are very fair to both the tourist and the driver (example / 5 hour tour to valley of kings and other West Bank sites 250 egp about $14usd). The hotel had a lovely bar - fantastic pool with a splendid view of the Nile and meticulously clean rooms. The pool side menu was great and portions were generous. The location is superb, a very short taxi to the temples and museums. Our only regret is that we did not stay longer."

01/01/2015

The staff was amazing, very friendly and unbelievably...

"The staff was amazing, very friendly and unbelievably helpful, literally nothing was too much trouble. The location is quiet and secluded, which we appreciated very much. A free shuttle bus runs on the hour at 11.00, 12.00, 13.00 and in the evening 20.00, 21.00 and 22.00 into Luxor downtown and back. There is internet in the room but do not forget to bring a connecting cable, as it is not WIFI. There is free WIFI but only in the public areas. The hotel also has its own boats on site but we didn't check them out. There are 2 swimming pools: a big one mainly with kids and parents and a separate small infinity pool with a cosy seating area which the children seemed to avoid.After having looked at the other high end hotels we definitely would go back here and can highly recommend it. The Winter Palace Hotel is more central but the view onto the nile is spoiled by a big noisy road. The gardens are fantastic though. The Hilton is much more modern and has a fantastic location straight on the Nile - depends what you are looking for."
