Description

Preserving original design of heritage and thematic architecture and interior, the rooms at this property are warmly lit and are equipped with a satellite TV and mini-bar. Private bathrooms come with shower facilities.

This family-friendly property even features its own reptile area and bird park. pony rides are also available. Other recreational facilities include the flying fox, tennis and badminton courts, as well as a fish spa. Karaoke is available.

Royal Safari Garden's Arumba Restaurant serves Indonesian dishes, while Sky Garden Restaurant serves local and Western food. Guests can enjoy coffee and snack at Mug&Co.

Royal Safari Garden Resort and Convention is 48 mi from Jakarta and 56 mi from Bandung. Free private parking is available.
