Description

The hotel is in the Nirwana Bogor Residence complex, which also houses The Jungle Water Park and Orchard Walk entertainment area. Jakarta is a 45 minute drive away.

Rooms at Aston Bogor Hotel have private balconies with mountain views. Each comes with a cable TV, safe and coffee/tea-making facilities. Some rooms have a separate living/dining area.

Guests can relax in the landscaped grounds, or visit the business center for any professional needs. Car rental and concierge services provide travel assistance. Aston Resort Bogor offers express check-in and laundry services.

International dishes are served at Batutulis Coffee Shop. The 24-hour facility offers both indoor and outdoor seating. Snacks and refreshments are available at Katulampa Lounge Bar and the Pastry Corner.

Bogor Selatan is a great choice for travelers interested in cold weather, nature and family-friendly trips.
