Description

In the heart of the picturesque market town of Peebles, The Tontine is a family-run property which provides home-baked sweets and shortbread and elegant rooms.

The Georgian Adam Room restaurant enjoys hill views and serves a weekly changing menu.

With warm, classic décor, rooms at The Tontine all offer an en suite TV and a hairdryer.

Guests can relax with free tea and coffee and a TV in the room. Some rooms boast lovely views of the River Tweed.

The Adam Room Restaurant features beautiful views, a Georgian minstrel’s gallery, chandelier, and fireplace.

It offers an à la carte menu, while the modern bistro provides relaxed, informal dining.

Classic cocktails are served in the hotel bar. For active guests, the famous Glentress Mountain Biking Centre is just 3 miles away, and excellent walking and cycling trails can be enjoyed from outside the hotel.

Peebles Golf Club is just 10 minutes away on foot. Edinburgh is a 30-minute drive away.
