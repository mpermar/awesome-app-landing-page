Description

Greene King Inns, Milton Keynes (formerly The Broughton Hotel), with the 'Hungry Horse' pub-restaurant, is a friendly lodge-style hotel offering free WiFi, free parking and a good location near the M1, 5 km from Milton Keynes.

It is 1 minute from the M1 motorway (junction 14). The town of Milton Keynes is 5 km away, with its indoor shopping centre, theatre, gallery, multi-screen cinema and indoor ski slope.

There is a Sports Bar with Sky Sports package and a pool table. A lounge is also available for a more relaxed atmosphere.

This hotel has wheelchair access. There are miles of cycle paths to explore the countryside, including Willen Lake and Campbell Park, whilst within just 10 km you will find Guilivers Land, Bletchley Park, Milton Keynes National Bowl and Woburn Safari Park and Abbey.

Silverstone Circuit, Towcester Racecourse and Luton Airport are just a short drive away.
