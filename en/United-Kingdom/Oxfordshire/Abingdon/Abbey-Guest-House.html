Description

At the guesthouse, each room includes a desk and a flat-screen TV. A private bathroom is at guests' disposal in guest rooms, while selected rooms here will provide you with a DVD player. All rooms will provide guests with a microwave.

A continental breakfast is served each morning at the property.

Oxford is 5 miles from Abbey Guest House. London Heathrow Airport is 38 miles from the property.

All children under 12 years are charged GBP 30 per night for extra beds.
Any additional older children or adults are charged GBP 30 per night for extra beds.
The maximum number of extra beds in a room is 1.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
