Description

Built in 1692, Buscot Manor is a tranquil estate set in the heart of rural Oxfordshire.

Surrounded by the beautiful countryside, guests can enjoy activities such as horse riding, golf, and cycling.

Oxford is only 20 miles away. Stunning views, sumptuous furniture and fabrics characterise the luxurious rooms, which also retain original features and oak floors.

Rooms also have a fully stocked tea tray with refreshments, free Wi-Fi, and TV with DVD player.

Breakfast is served in the elegant dining room and includes a full range of choices with locally sourced ingredients.

Restaurants, pubs are within 1 mile and guests can visit the Buscot Manor Tea Rooms in the village for a light snack.

With free parking and within less than 5 minutes’ walk from the River Thames, guests can enjoy watersports and fishing.
