Description

All units include a fully equipped kitchen, allowing guests to prepare their own meals. There's a seating and/or dining area in some units.

The resort village offers a sauna.

Liverpool is 15 miles from Talacre Beach, while Chester is 21 miles away. The nearest airport is Liverpool John Lennon Airport, 19 miles from the accommodation.
