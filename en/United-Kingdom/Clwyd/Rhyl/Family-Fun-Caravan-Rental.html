Description

Located in Rhyl, Family Fun Caravan Rental offers accommodation with a patio or a terrace, free WiFi and flat-screen TV, as well as a bar and a garden.

All units comprise a seating area with a sofa, a dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen with various cooking facilities, including a microwave, a fridge, an oven and a toaster.

A children's playground is available on site and hiking can be enjoyed within close proximity of the holiday park.

Llandudno is 29 km from Family Fun Caravan Rental, while Betws-y-coed is 48 km away.
