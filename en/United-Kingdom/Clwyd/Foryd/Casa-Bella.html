Description

The resort village offers a seating area with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a hair dryer and shower. There's also a kitchen equipped with an oven and microwave.

Guests can grab a bite to eat in the in-house restaurant, which specializes in American and British cuisine.

Casa Bella has a terrace.

If you would like to discover the area, cycling is possible nearby.

Liverpool is 24 miles from the accommodation, while Chester is 28 miles away. Liverpool John Lennon Airport is 28 miles from the property.
