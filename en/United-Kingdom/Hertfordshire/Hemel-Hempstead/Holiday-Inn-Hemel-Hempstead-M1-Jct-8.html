Description

Holiday Inn Hemel Hempstead M1, Jct. 8 offers an excellent location just off the M1 (Junction 8) and is easily accessed from the M25.

This hotel offers high-speed internet throughout. A flat-screen HD TV and pillow menu is provided in each air-conditioned room at the hotel.

Guests can enjoy a fully equipped leisure club including a swimming pool and fitness centre.

The Traders restaurant serves traditional British dishes and hosts regular themed menus.

Enjoy Pizzas and steaks in the hotel bar and Starbucks Coffee is served in the lounge.

Kids under 13 eat for free. The Holiday Inn Hemel Hempstead M1, Jct. 8 is just 8 miles from Luton Airport, 21 miles from Heathrow Airport and 15 miles from Wembley Stadium.

There are also good local rail links to London Euston. The Warner Brothers Studio attraction can be reached in a 10-minute drive.
