Description

Just 3 miles from the Sandringham Estate, The Ffolkes features 24 en suite rooms and free Wi-Fi throughout.

There is ample free secure parking. All of the rooms have Egyptian cotton bed linen, seating, and TVs with Sky channels.

The property has an on-site restaurant, including a heated outdoor dining area. It has a children's playground.

The Ffolkes has an indoor games room, including pool, table football and board games.

This friendly 300-year-old inn is 6 miles from Kings Lynn and 5 miles from the coast.
