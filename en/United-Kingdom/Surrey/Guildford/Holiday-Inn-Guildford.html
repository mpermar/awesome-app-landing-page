Description

With a modern leisure centre and swimming pool, The Holiday Inn Guildford offers spacious bedrooms with air conditioning.

Just 3 miles from Guildford Castle, it features an international restaurant. The bright bedrooms at Guildford Holiday Inn feature sofas and en suite bathrooms.

Rooms also offer tea and coffee facilities and a large work desk. Free Wi-Fi is available in all rooms.

The Spirit Health and Leisure Centre has a spa bath, sauna, steam room and a fully equipped gym.

Treatments are available in the beauty rooms, including aromatherapy massage. The restaurant serves a varied evening menu and hot breakfasts, and there is also a courtyard terrace.

The Lounge Bar offers a range of snacks and beverages, and features plasma-screen TVs.

Holiday Inn Guildford Hotel offers free on-site parking. Central London can be reached in just 30 minutes, and Merrist Wood Golf Club is a 10-minute drive.
