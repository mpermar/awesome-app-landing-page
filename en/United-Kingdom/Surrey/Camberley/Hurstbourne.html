Description

At the bed and breakfast, each room is equipped with a desk. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a shower, while some accommodations at Hurstbourne also provide guests with a seating area. Guest rooms have a closet.

A continental breakfast is available every morning at the accommodation.

Hurstbourne has a playground.

The nearest airport is London Heathrow Airport, 16 miles from the property.
