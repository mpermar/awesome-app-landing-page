Description

Set in the heart of a 400-acre estate, this 19th century mansion hotel includes 2 championship golf courses, tennis and squash courts, a state-of-the-art gym and a luxury health spa with pools.

Elegant rooms at Foxhills enjoy views of the golf courses, courtyard or pool and include an armchair, Freeview TV with Sky Sports and a minibar.

Tea and coffee making facilities and The White Company toiletries are included. Hot Foxhills offers dining in 3 unique restaurants.

The Manor Restaurant serves traditional and seasonal dishes, while the Summerhouse offers light bites.

A Brasserie-style features at Nineteen Restaurant. The property has 4 indoor and outdoor swimming pools and you can enjoy various treatments and therapies in the spa.

There is also a youth club and a beauty salon. The Foxhills is less than 3 miles from the M25 and 35 minutes’ drive from Gatwick Airport.
