Description

The Hurtwood Hotel is a boutique hotel set in an elegant 1920’s building is located in Peaslake.

The property is situated within the Surrey Hills, 28 miles from London, 18 miles from Gatwick Airport and a short drive from both Guildford and Dorking.

The Hurtwood Hotel has 13 individually furnished bedrooms. The hotel offers bed-and-breakfast accommodation and there is a separately operated pub and restaurant within the building.

The pub features a bar with an open fire and serves a selection of wines and food options.
