Description

All rooms in Bonners Lodge are equipped with a coffee machine. A seating area is at guests' disposal in all guest rooms, while certain rooms also offer a DVD player. At the accommodation, every room has a private bathroom with free toiletries.

The daily breakfast offers continental, Full English/Irish or vegetarian options.

Newcastle upon Tyne is 16 miles from Bonners Lodge, while Durham is 13 miles from the property. Newcastle Airport is 18 miles away.

We were spoilt and we loved it!

"We are a family with young children and a five month old Irish Setter. Not only was Michelle and Ken's welcome amazing but so was our stay. Michelle went beyond the call of duty - posting forgotten things, texting us about how to get there and much more, washing the dog's bed, not batting an eyelid at a night time accident in bed, washing pyjamas and letting us run free in their house. Outside there is no traffic so children and dogs were free and above all safe to play with all the dogs, groom the pony and pet the enormous Irish mare. Our breakfasts were simply the best too. The standard maintained is on a par with an expensive hotel but the warm reception we received beat everything. Bonners Lodge is isolated which was fantastic too. The countryside is stunning. Loads of walks, both children and dog safe! Michelle and Ken's guide to the area (found in the bedroom) is very comprehensive. We thoroughly enjoyed staying at Bonners Lodge and would recommend it to everyone."
