Description

Strathview offers pet-friendly accommodation in Strathpeffer, by the North Coast 500 driving route.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Rooms have wonderful views of the surrounding hills and include a flat-screen TV and an en-suite shower room.

Some units feature a seating area where you can relax. Enjoy a cup of tea while looking out at the mountains or garden.

Extras include free toiletries and a hair dryer. You will find luggage storage space at the property.

A Continental or Full English breakfast is available. Inverness is 30 km from Strathview, while Nairn is 52 km away.
