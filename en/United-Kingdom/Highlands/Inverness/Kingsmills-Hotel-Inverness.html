Description

Guests can enjoy full use of the recreation club, with a sauna, steam room, spa tub and gym. There is also a hair salon.

Many of the spacious rooms at Kingsmills Hotel have garden views. Features include satellite TV and tea and coffee-making facilities.

Inglis Restaurant serves excellent cuisine, with a menu of fresh, local produce. Guests at Hotel Kingsmills can also dine on the grounds conservatory brasserie, which features an informal menu.
