Description

Each unit is fully fitted with a washing machine, a flat-screen TV, a sofa and closet. A dishwasher and electric tea pot are also featured.

The resort village has a grill.

If you would like to discover the area, cycling is possible nearby and the property can arrange a bicycle rental service.

Inverness is 22 miles from Glen Affric Holiday Park, while Fort Augustus is 14 miles away. The nearest airport is Inverness Airport, 30 miles from the property.
