Description

In an area of outstanding natural beauty, overlooking Arisaig Bay and out towards the Isles of Eigg and Muck, Arisaig Hotel is an old coaching inn built in around 1720.

It offers en-suite rooms, most of which boast sea views. All feature a TV and tea and coffee making facilities.

It is a family-friendly hotel and has a children's playroom with a wide variety of toys.

The Crofter’s Bar has a pool table and hosts live music at weekends. The comfortable lounge bar stocks a good range of malt whiskies.

Lunches, dinners, teas and coffees are served daily. Breakfast is served in the dining room and there is a comfortable drawing room with an open fire where you can relax.

During the warmer months you can sit outside in the sun and admire the views from the deck.

Fort William is a 50-minute drive from Arisaig Hotel. A variety of picturesque lochs can be reached in around 10 minutes' drive.
