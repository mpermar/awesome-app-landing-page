Description

Situated 100 metres from Loch Linnhe and providing a garden, Highland Croft B&B features accommodation in Onich.

Fitted with a terrace, the units feature a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with shower.

The bed and breakfast offers a continental or Full English/Irish breakfast, with eggs from the property's free range hens.
