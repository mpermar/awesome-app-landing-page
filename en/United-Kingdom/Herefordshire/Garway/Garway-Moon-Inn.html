Description

Featuring free WiFi throughout the bed and breakfast, Garway Moon Inn is a family-run 18th century free house in Garway, 13.

7 miles from Hereford, 7. 6 miles from Monmouth and 10 miles from Ross-on-Wye. The bed and breakfast has a children's playground and overlooks the beautiful Garway Common.

Guests can also enjoy a meal at the restaurant. The M50 motorway is a 10-minute drive away.

Rooms include a flat-screen TV with DVD player and a complimentary drinks tray. The rooms are also equipped with a private bathroom equipped with a bath.

Extras include free toiletries and a hair dryer. The on-site restaurant serves a British pub and grill menu inspired by local growers and farmers.

There is an award-winning dinner menu. You can engage in various activities, such as circular walking trails, Three Castles Walk, Symonds Yat, fishing and quad trekking.

Gloucester is 37 km from Garway Moon Inn, while Newport is 38 km from the property.
