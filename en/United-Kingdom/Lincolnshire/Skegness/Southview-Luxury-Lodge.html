Description

Each of the units consists of a living room, a well-equipped kitchen, and a private bathroom with bath and shower. A dishwasher and electric tea pot are also offered.

The resort village has a playground.

A bicycle rental service is available at Southview Luxury Lodge.

Skegness Beach is 2.1 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Humberside Airport, 40 miles from the property.
