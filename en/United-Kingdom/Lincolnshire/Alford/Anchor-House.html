Description

Anchor House features rooms in Alford. Providing an evening menu, the property also boasts a garden.

The bed and breakfast has newspapers and luggage storage service that guests can use.

All units at the bed and breakfast are fitted with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a bath or shower.

Anchor House offers some units with garden views, and all rooms have a kettle. The units feature a desk.

The daily breakfast offers vegetarian and Full English/Irish options. A number of activities are offered in the area, such as cycling and hiking.

Lincoln is 48 km from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Humberside Airport, 50 km from the property.
