Description

The Loudor is a 4-star family run guest house in North Hykeham, 3 miles from Lincoln city centre.

It offers rooms with free Wi-Fi, homemade biscuits and free parking. The rooms at The Loudor are decorated in light shades and feature work desks, TVs and tea/coffee-making facilities.

They include bathrooms with a shower and hairdryers and ironing equipment are available on request.

The open-plan dining room serves cooked English and continental breakfasts each morning.

There is a comfortable lounge with sofas for guests to relax in. There is also a large garden with a seating area.

Directly opposite the property is The Forum shopping centre. Lincoln has many fantastic sights for guests to enjoy including the Quayside, Lincoln Cathedral and Lincoln Castle which holds one the four original copies of the Magna Carta.
