Description

DoubleTree by Hilton Forest Pines Spa & Golf Resort is set amongst the Yorkshire and Humberside countryside with a 27-hole Championship golf course, close to Junction 4 of the M180.

Surrounded by 190 acres of woodland, it features a health club and a restaurant. The spacious rooms have an en suite-bathroom, a flat-screen TV with Sky channels and free WiFi.

The health club includes a spa with an 18-metre swimming pool, steam room, sauna and whirlpool.

The 6 treatment rooms offer massage, facials and manicures. There is also a relaxation room.

The hotel has 400 free parking spaces. The hotel offers easy access to motorway networks and is within easy reach of Humberside Airport.
