Description

Serving food daily, the family-run Prince of Wales Inn is on the edge of the Brecon Beacons National Park.

With free WiFi in public areas and free onsite parking, it is just 1 mile from the Bryn Bach Country Park and Lake.

Each room includes a TV and tea and coffee making facilities. The rooms also benefit from an en-suite bathroom.

Fridges, electric heaters, and ironing supplies are available upon request. Guests at the Prince of Wales Inn can enjoy traditional bar meals in front of the log fire in the lounge bar.

Dishes include Welsh steak. Cardiff is just a 40-minute drive from the traditional Prince of Wales Inn and there are numerous activities offered in the surrounding area, including golfing.

The property is ideally situated for touring local historic sites and cities. Along with beaches, Swansea and Newport are both within a 30-minute drive.
