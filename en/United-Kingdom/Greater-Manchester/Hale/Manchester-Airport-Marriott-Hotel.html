Description

The hotel has stylish and spacious rooms, some with access to an Executive Lounge. The property also includes 2 restaurants and a bar which serves a full range of Starbucks coffee. Free Wi-Fi is available in these areas.

Located off of the flight path, the Manchester Airport Marriott Hotel is near the villages of Hale, Wilmslow, and Alderley Edge.
