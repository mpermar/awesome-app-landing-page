Description

Situated 15 minutes’ walk from the bustling shopping town of Coleraine, The Lodge Hotel features an on-site hair and beauty salon, and is just a 20-minute drive from the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Giant’s Causeway.

Free Wi-Fi and parking are available. Each bedroom includes a flat-screen TV and an en suite bathroom.

A wide range of classic and contemporary dishes are served in Elliot’s Bistro in a lively wine bar setting.

A buffet lunch is also available, as well as weekend entertainment. Romanoff's Restaurant offers fine dining, or a more relaxed experience can be found in the Vine Conservatory Monday to Thursday.

The seaside resorts of Portrush and Portstewart, and Royal Portrush Golf Club are only a 10-minute drive away.
