Description

In the heart of Crossmaglen, with views of Slieve Gullion Mountain, the Cross Square has a bar, a restaurant and free parking, as well as live entertainment at the weekend.

The bedrooms all have their own bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer. Other facilities include tea/coffee, flat-screen TVs and ample storage space.

The hotel’s restaurant serves fine food; guests can also enjoy a drink at the bar.

A continental breakfast, as well as a full Irish breakfast, is available every morning until 11:30.
