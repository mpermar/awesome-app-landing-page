Description

Situated just 3 miles from Bradford city centre The Highfield Hotel is a family-run hotel with free on-site parking and free Wi-Fi in all public areas.

All rooms include an en-suite bathroom with complimentary toiletries, a hairdryer, a flat-screen TV with built-in radio, and a seating area.

Majority of rooms have a DVD player. They also benefit from tea and coffee making facilities.

The Highfield also boasts a bar, restaurant and leisure club. Leisure club access available free of charge from check in at 14:00 until check out at 11:00, featuring a gym, indoor salt-water swimming pool, jacuzzi, sauna and steam room.

Solarium sunbeds are available for an extra charge. The bar serves a selection of ales, fine wines and spirits which guests can enjoy.

It also hosts regular quiz nights. The world-inspired on-site BBQ restaurant offers a food and drinks menu, which is also served in the public bar.

This property offers a free light breakfast with a great variety. A cooked breakfast is only available on Saturdays and Sunday mornings from 08:30 for an additional charge when booked the evening before with reception.

A Sunday lunch is also available. It is a 10 minute walk to the closest supermarket.

Saltaire World Heritage Site is 5 km from The Highfield Hotel. From the hotel, the National Media Museum is a 12-minute drive away.

Bradford Cathedral is 8 minutes’ drive away and Kirkgate and Broadway Shopping Centre is a 9-minute journey in the car.

Knaresborough is 31. 7 km from the property, while Lightwater Valley theme park is 52.
