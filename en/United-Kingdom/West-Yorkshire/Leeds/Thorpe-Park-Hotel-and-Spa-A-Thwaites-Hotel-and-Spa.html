Description

All rooms have free WiFi, tea/coffee making facilities, complimentary fresh milk and mineral water in your fridge, and each has a modern bathroom with a bath and walk-in shower; plus a hairdryer and fluffy bathrobes.

The AA Rosette awarded restaurant at Thorpe Park serves contemporary British cuisine, using locally sourced produce. Guests can enjoy drinks on the sun terrace or beside the open fire in the cozy bar.

The first-class spa features a 40 feet indoor swimming pool and fully equipped gym. Guests can relax in the sauna, steam room and spa bath, or try indulgent treatments in the beauty salon.

Just a 15-minute drive from Leeds Rail Station, Thorpe Park Hotel is 2 miles from Temple Newsam Golf Club. The Royal Armories Museum is a 15-minute drive away. On-site parking is available, and is free on weekends.
