Description

Located 1. 5 miles from Brentwood town centre, this contemporary hotel boasts well-equipped rooms, convenient amenities and modern business facilities; within easy reach of the Bluewater and Lakeside shopping complexes.

Free WiFi is available throughout the entire hotel and guests can also relax in the on-site Starbucks lounge.

Guests can enjoy air-conditioned, broadband enabled room and relax in the indoor swimming pool, whirlpool and sauna.

On-site gym facilities and a treatment room are also available. The Trader's restaurant is on-site to cater for all tastes, offering dedicated 24-hour room service.

The 9 multi-functional conference rooms can be reserved to provide a tailored solution to any group occasion.
