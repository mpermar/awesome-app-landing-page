Description

In 10 acres of gardens and wooded grounds, this family-run hotel offers a gym, tennis courts and a heated outdoor swimming pool.

Brentwood rail station is within easy reach. Each en suite room at De Rougemont Manor has satellite TV.

The hotel offers free Wi-Fi and free parking. Heseltines Restaurant provides quality cuisine, and has a fresh seasonal menu and a lounge area.

Guests can also enjoy a drink in the on-site bar. This country house hotel is only 3 minutes from Junction 29 of the M25 motorway.
