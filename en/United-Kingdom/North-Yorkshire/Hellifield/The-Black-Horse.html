Description

With an on-site bar and restaurant, The Black Horse is located in the village of Hellifield, in the outskirts of Yorkshire Dales National Park.

It offers bed and breakfast accommodation, free WiFi and free private parking. All the rooms at The Black Horse benefit from en suite bathroom facilities.

Each room also includes a flat-screen TV and tea/coffee making facilities. Guests can enjoy a hearty full English breakfast in the morning.

A vegetarian option is also available. This family-run guest house is within 10 miles from Skipton and Settle.
