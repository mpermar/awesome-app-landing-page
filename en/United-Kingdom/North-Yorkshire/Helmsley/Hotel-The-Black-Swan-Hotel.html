Description

One of our top picks in Helmsley.In the picturesque North Yorkshire market town of Helmsley, The Black Swan is a stylish hotel with boutique bedrooms. The historic inn has an award-winning restaurant and tearoom.

The beautiful, 500-year old building is furnished with antiques and classic furniture. Guests can enjoy indulgent afternoon teas, featuring handmade artisan pastries and teas from around the world.

The elegant bedrooms have fresh, traditional decor and tea and coffee facilities. All rooms feature a private bathroom and a cozy seating area, and each has a TV, a telephone and free Wi-Fi.

The acclaimed 3 AA rosette restaurant offers a menu including homemade puddings, quality steaks and fine wines using seasonal produce.

On the edge of the North Yorkshire Moors, The Black Swan Hotel is within easy reach of Rievaulx Abbey and Castle Howard. Free parking is available on site.
