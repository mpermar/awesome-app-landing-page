Description

Located on the outskirts of Giggleswick, 1. 9 km from the town of Settle, The Craven Arms is a family-friendly property offering free WiFi and free parking.

Each room has an en-suite bathroom with complimentary toiletries, TV and tea/coffee making facilities.

All guests will receive a full English breakfast with their stay and have the option to dine within The Craven Arm's traditional British restaurant or enjoy a drink from a selection of real ales from local breweries at the bar.

The Craven Arms is the perfect base for exploring the Yorkshire Dales and for guests taking the Three Peaks Challenge.
