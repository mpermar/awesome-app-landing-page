Description

In the popular North Yorkshire town of Scarborough, just a pleasant 20-minute walk from the town’s South Bay beach, this long-established hotel welcomes families and children of all ages.

The hotel offers bed and breakfast and self-catering accommodation throughout the year.

All of the rooms are en suite and have TVs and tea/coffee making facilities. The hotel is completely non-smoking throughout.

Private parking is available for guests’ use free of charge during their stay and the facilities include a heated indoor swimming pool, a full-sized snooker table, a games room (with table tennis, table football and a pool table), a licensed bar and a lift to all floors.

Green Gables offers spacious, comfortable, affordable accommodation and a friendly welcoming atmosphere that makes it ideally suited for families.

Children of all ages are welcome. Please note this is a family-friendly hotel, for this reason it does not accept any stag, hen or same sex parties.
