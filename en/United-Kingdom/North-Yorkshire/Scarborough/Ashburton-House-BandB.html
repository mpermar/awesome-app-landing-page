Description

Set in Scarborough's beautiful valley, the Ashburton House is a family-run B&B that is just 10 minutes’ walk along the tree-lined Valley Road from the South Bay and town centre.

The B&B has its own off-street car park. All of the charming and comfortable bedrooms have their own en-suite bathroom and are well equipped with TVs, DVD players, tea/coffee making facilities, hairdryers and radio alarm clocks.

Every year at least one of the 8 bedrooms is fully refurbished. Throughout the public areas, the B&B aims to capture the charm and elegance of the late Victorian period, during which the property was built.

The B&B offers an extensive breakfast menu and as well as serving a full English breakfast, also offers grilled kippers, smoked salmon and many other lighter options.
