11/22/2017

Great weekend with friends

"We booked this as a group of 12 people staying in two lodges for a long weekend. The overall look and feel of the property was very clean, and homely.As we were a young group, a couple days before our stay i had a very long phone call with the park manager, as he was telling me his concerns on the fact we were a group of 20 - 30 year olds and he was obviously worried about us damaging the lodges. This was not really necessary as we then felt as if we were being watched and untrusted. Of course no damage was made, but the half hour conversation of him questioning how a group of younger adults got through the booking system made us a bit wary.The hot tubs were a bonus and really made the weekend great ! They could of been hotter, but we spent a lot of time in them which would of maybe put the temperature down. The ducks were a funny add on to the weekend. Waking up to 30 ducks at your door is pretty entertaining.Through the weekend we took the short drive to Ripon, which is good for shops and restaurants. With there being no shop on site, we took the drive to stock up on things. We also had a walk to the near by village of West Tanfield, which has some lovely walks and pubs.The only downside which was an issue after we left was the £200 deposit being taken from our bank account. The woman upon check in assured us the card was just on their system incase any damage was made, and that if any money needed to come out, they would call us first. Unfortunately the £200 was taken without us being told, but we sorted it through the bank the next day.Overall it was a great weekend. The lodges are really nice, with them being good for both families and groups of friends. There are a lot of things close by, and when it gets warmer Light water Valley is a short drive away."
