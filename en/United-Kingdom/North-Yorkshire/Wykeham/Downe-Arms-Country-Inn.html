Description

On the edge of the beautiful Yorkshire Moors, but only 10 km from the coast, this 17th-century former farmhouse has 10 unique en suite rooms, many with attractive original features.

The Downe Arms Country Inn has a great location, on the borders of the Yorkshire Moors.

Perfectly placed for exploring the region’s stunning natural beauty, the hotel is great for walkers, cyclists, shooters and anglers or simply for a peaceful, relaxing break.

Free parking is available for guests’ use. Part of the Dawnay Estates, founded in 1656, the hotel offers a traditional, historical setting.

All of the rooms are individually decorated with their own unique charm. Some of the rooms have beautiful Georgian windows, while others have low original wooden beams and unusual arched windows.

All of the rooms offer traditional country elegance alongside contemporary comforts and modern facilities, allowing you to enjoy the best of both worlds.

Free wireless internet access is available throughout the hotel. A full English breakfast is included in the price.

The cosy bar offers a range of cask ales, lagers, wines and spirits. The charming restaurant has large Georgian windows offering lovely views of the village of Wykeham and beyond.

The restaurant offers just the right mix of international favourites and traditional Yorkshire cuisine, all made with the best seasonal produce available locally.
