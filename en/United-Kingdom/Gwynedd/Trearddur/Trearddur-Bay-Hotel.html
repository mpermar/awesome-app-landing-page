Description

Just a few metres from an award-winning Blue Flag beach, the Trearddur Bay Hotel boasts an on-site restaurant and bar and bright, elegant rooms with free WiFi.

Some rooms have fabulous sea views and balconies, whilst all include a TV and private bathroom.

There is also a separate six-bedroom house with its own sitting room and kitchen.

Overlooking the sea, the hotel restaurant offers the finest local produce from Anglesey and North Wales.

For a less formal occasion, guests can enjoy bar meals at The Inn at the Bay, and there are facilities for meetings and events in the Penrhos Suite.
