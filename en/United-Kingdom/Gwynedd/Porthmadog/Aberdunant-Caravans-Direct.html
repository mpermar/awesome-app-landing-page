Description

Fitted with a patio, the units feature a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with shower. An oven, a microwave and toaster are also offered, as well as a electric tea pot.

There's an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of British dishes and also offers Vegetarian options.

The resort village has a terrace.

Guests can also relax in the garden.

Llandudno is 28 miles from Aberdunant Caravans Direct, while Betws-y-coed is 16 miles away.
