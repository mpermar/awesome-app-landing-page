Description

Offering a seasonal outdoor pool and views of the garden, Macdonald Plas Talgarth Resort is situated in Pennal.

Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. Free private parking is available on site.

Lodges are fitted with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Some lodges include a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

You will find a cafetiere in the Lodge and each Lodge includes a private bathroom with a bathtub and bathtub or shower, with a hairdryer provided.

You can play tennis at this resort, and bike hire is available. Aberystwyth is 22 km from Macdonald Plas Talgarth Resort, while Bala is 43 km away.
