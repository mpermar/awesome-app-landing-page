Description

Situated within the picturesque Northumbrian countryside, Rye Hill Farm sits within 30 acres of grounds and offers free off-road parking.

A games room is available, with a skittle alley, table tennis and a pool table. Free Wi-Fi is provided in each room at Rye Hill, along with en-suite bathrooms and a flat-screen TV.

A full English breakfast is served in the dining room each morning. The dining area also features a cosy open-log fire and patio doors which look onto the farm.

Food is locally sourced where possible and many items are homemade. The edge of Northumberland National Park can be reached in a 25-minute drive, and offers a wealth of outdoor activities and historic landmarks such as the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Hadrian’s Wall.
