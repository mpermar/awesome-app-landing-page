Description

With an award-winning restaurant and free Wi-Fi, Riverdale Hall Hotel is set in large grounds, which lead onto the banks of the North Tyne River.

It also boasts an indoor heated swimming-pool and a sauna, along with a bar which serves meals.

The bedrooms are spacious with en suite facilities. Each room offers a flat-screen TV and tea and coffee making facilities.

Some have balconies or patios overlooking the grounds. The award-winning restaurant uses fine local produce and the bar serves bar meals; both have open log fires.

In the mornings, full English breakfast is served. At Riverdale Hall, guests can enjoy an indoor heated swimming pool and a sauna.

They can also fish salmon and trout on one of the hotel's private beats on the North Tyne.

Guests can also enjoy discounted rates at the Bellingham Golf Course, a short walk away.
