Description

Dating from the 1580’s, Hawthorn Farm is set in a Grade II listed house, 10 minutes’ walk from the centre of Buxton.

It offers country-house style rooms and home-cooked food. Rooms at Hawthorn Farm are located in the main building or the converted barn and farm buildings.

They feature flat-screen TVs with digital radios, feature beds and bathrooms with free toiletries.

All rooms have free Wi-Fi access. The traditional dining room has original exposed wooden beams and serves cooked and continental breakfasts including yoghurts, muesli, cereals and jams.

Countryside walking routes surround the house and Buxton Train Station is a 10-minute walk away.

Buxton And High Peak Golf Course is less than a mile away and free parking is available at the property.
