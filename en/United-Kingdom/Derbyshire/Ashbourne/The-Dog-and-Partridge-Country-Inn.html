Description

The Dog and Partridge is a 17th-century inn just a 10-minute drive from Alton Towers, situated on the edge of the idyllic Peak District with countryside views.

Free WiFi is offered within the main building. All rooms at The Dog and Partridge are mainly located in the grounds of the inn and feature en suite bathrooms, a TV with Freeview, a DVD player, tea and coffee-making facilities and hairdryers.

The hotel provides extensive lawns and gardens, and offers indoor and outdoor play areas for children.

Free parking is also provided. Guests can enjoy local specialties and traditional dining with à la carte, a traditional pub menu and also set and table d’hote menus in the lounge bar.

Full English breakfasts are also offered to guests. The nearby Peak District provides walkers with numerous scenic routes, and the town of Ashbourne and Ashbourne Golf Club is just 5 minutes’ drive away.
