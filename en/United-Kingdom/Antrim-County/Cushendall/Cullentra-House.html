Description

This award-winning, family-run bed and breakfast offers panoramic views of the Antrim Glens, generous breakfasts, and elegant bedrooms.

Just 30 minutes from the Causeway Coast, there is free parking and free Wi-Fi. The spacious rooms at Cullentra are traditionally decorated with stylish linens and wooden furniture.

All rooms offer tea and coffee facilities, and some have modern en suite bathrooms.

The Cullentra serves a range of delicious breakfast options in the quaint dining room, which offers views towards Lurig Mountain.

Cooked and vegetarian choices are available, and homemade bread regularly features.

Overlooking The Sea of Moyle, Ballycastle is just 25 minutes’ drive away. There are a number of popular walking routes nearby, including Glenariffe Glen and Waterfalls.

Larne Ferry Terminal can be reached in 40 minutes, offering ferries to the Scottish Coast.
