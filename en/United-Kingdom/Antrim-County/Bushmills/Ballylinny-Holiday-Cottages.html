Description

Within 10 minutes’ walk of the Giant’s Causeway World Heritage Site, the 3-star Ballylinny Holiday Cottages offer beautiful country views.

A flat-screen TV is featured in the wooden-floored lounge. There is also a tennis court and a children’s playground.

The Old Bushmills Distillery, the oldest licenced distillery in the world, is just a 5-minute drive away.

Salmon fishing is popular on the River Bush, which can also be reached after 5 minutes’ drive.

The 16th-century Dunluce Castle is a 10-minute car journey from Ballylinnny. A dishwasher, washing machine and a microwave are included in the modern, fully-fitted kitchen.

A fireplace, dining table and sofas also feature in the open-plan kitchen, lounge and dining area.

Bedrooms have traditional wooden furnishings, with a hairdryer and free WiFi available.
