Description

Located on the beautiful Gloucestershire and Herefordshire border, The Farmers Boy is a 17th-century country inn.

Family owned, this award-winning inn offers charming accommodation. Just a short drive from Wales and within easy reach of the Royal Forest of Dean, The Farmers Boy Inn is just 20 minutes from Cheltenham Race course.

There are a number of good golf courses in the area. This Inns' restaurant and bar area still retains many attractive period features, including the original oak beams and open log fires.

There is also a lounge. The friendly staff can advise guests on local attractions and help arrange a number of activities including fishing and horse riding.

Rooms are located in a modern, purpose-built annexe. Each room has a TV and a shower room.
