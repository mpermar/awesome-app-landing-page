Description

The Edward Hotel is a just 10-minute walk from Gloucester Cathedral and Gloucester Train Station.

Set in 3 historically listed town houses, it offers en suite rooms with free Wi-Fi, traditional breakfasts and free parking.

Rooms at the Edward Hotel feature a flat-screen TV with FreeSat, a writing desk, and a hairdryer.

Each also comes with tea and coffee making facilities. In reception guests can use the computer, which has Skype and a webcam, free of charge.

The corridors are lined with photographs of Gloucester’s historic buildings, and the friendly staff can arrange transport and sightseeing recommendations.

Gloucester Rugby Club is less than one mile from the Edward. Gloucester city centre has a good range of shops, bars and restaurants and is a 10-minute walk away.

With a seating area, the hotel also has a self-serviced kitchenette so guests can prepare their own meals.

The Edward Hotel has a safe lockup for cycles. Good off-road cycling and three golf clubs and are within a 5-mile radius of the hotel.

Gloucester Airport and junctions 11 and 11a of the M5 motorway are within a 10-minute drive.
