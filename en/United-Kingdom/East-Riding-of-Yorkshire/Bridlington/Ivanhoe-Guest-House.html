Description

This family-run guest house is located in the Hilderthorpe Conservation Area of Bridlington, only 200 metres from South Beach.

It offers en suite rooms with free Wi-Fi, traditional cooked breakfasts and some off-road parking.

Rooms at Ivanhoe Guest House are bright and comfortable and include a TV and tea and coffee making facilities.

A full English breakfast or other cooked options are served, along with cereals, juices, toast and preserves.

You can also relax in the homely Ivanhoe lounge, which has a TV. Bridlington Harbour and the Spa Royal Hall and Theatre are within 5 minutes’ walk, while the town centre and Bridlington Train and Bus Stations are less than half a mile away.

Bridlington and the surrounding area has 5 impressive golf courses, and there are various cycling and walking routes nearby.
