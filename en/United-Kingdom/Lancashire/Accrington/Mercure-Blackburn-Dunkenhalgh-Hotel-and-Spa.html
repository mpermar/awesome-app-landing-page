Description

The rooms have private bathrooms with baths and showers, and they also include satellite TVs and radios. There are tea and coffee-making facilities, and Wi-Fi is available. Free private parking is also available on-site.

The Spa Naturel & Leisure center has a swimming pool, a sauna and a spa. There is also a fully equipped gym, a hot tub and massage treatments.

The Cameo Restaurant and Cameo Bar have international cuisine and light dishes. A few Lancashire specialties are also on the menu, and a fine wine list is available.

Close to highway M65, Blackburn is a 10-minute drive from the property. Manchester and its fantastic shopping and nightlife are 20 miles from the Mercure.
