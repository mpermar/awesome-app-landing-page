Description

The stylish, meal preparation capable accommodations are in either the historic main house or in modern courtyard buildings. All apartments and studios have a satellite TV and a private bathroom.

The leisure center at Thurnham Hall has a large indoor swimming pool and a state-of-the-art fitness suite. Guests can relax in the sauna and spa bathtub, or enjoy treatments in the beauty salon.

Thurnham Hall Restaurant opens at 12 noon and offers both fine and casual dining, and serves an international menu and wine list. All apartments have a kitchen/kitchenette with full facilities.
