Description

Located in the Lancashire village of Tewitfield, Carnforth, these luxury holiday apartments and townhouses have free private parking.

A country pub features on site and views of the marina can be enjoyed from most of the apartments.

Features include flat-screen TVs with Freeview, a DVD player. There is also a fully-fitted kitchen with dishwasher and washing machine.

Tewitfield Marina Aparthotel is on the Lancashire and Cumbria border, less than 30 minutes' drive from the Lake District and Yorkshire Dales.

Homemade food is available from the farm shop, which is located opposite the complex.
