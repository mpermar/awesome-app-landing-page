Description

At the hotel, rooms have a desk and a flat-screen TV. The private bathroom is equipped with a bath. Guest rooms have a closet.

The Morecambe Hotel offers a continental or Full English/Irish breakfast.

Fishing is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.

Blackpool is 19 miles from The Morecambe Hotel, while Windermere is 21 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Liverpool John Lennon Airport, 51 miles from the hotel.
