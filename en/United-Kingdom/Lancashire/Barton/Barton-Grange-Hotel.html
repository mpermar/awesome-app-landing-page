Description

With a convenient location, superb dining and great facilities, this 4-star Preston hotel is stylish and contemporary, whilst simultaneously retaining all the traditional charm of the original manor house.

Just 2 miles to junction 32 of the M6 motorway and with ample free parking, this family-run hotel offers beautifully decorated accommodation with superb en suite bathrooms.

Recent refurbishments have created a stylish, contemporary look for the hotel, while carefully preserving all the traditional charm of the original manor house.

The hotel’s superb facilities include 'The Walled Garden Bistro' and exclusive leisure facilities with a swimming pool, sauna and gym.

A couple of miles up the road the property has its own garden centre and narrow boat marina.

The property is 5 miles from the centre of Preston, and 20 minutes' drive from Blackpool and its attractions.
