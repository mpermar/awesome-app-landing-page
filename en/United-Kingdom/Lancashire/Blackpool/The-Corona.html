Description

Just 10 minutes' walk from Blackpool Pleasure Beach in a semi-residential area, this 4-star Art Deco-style building offers modern rooms with en suite bathrooms, free Wi-Fi, and free parking is available in the local area.

The seafront can be reached in just a 2-minute walk. On Blackpool's quiet South Shore, The Corona has a Mediterranean theme, with tiled floors, palm trees and marbled pillars.

It is a 5-minute drive from Blackpool International Airport and the nearest tram stop is a 2-minute walk away.

All rooms feature an en suite shower room, wall-mounted TVs, a hospitality tray and a hairdryer.

Freshly cooked breakfasts are served daily, and in the evenings there is casual dining, with a children's menu also available at busy periods.

Guests can also choose breakfast to go or enjoy a continental selection. There is a spacious bar leading to the terrace, and a relaxing conservatory to the front of the building where guests can enjoy a game of pool.
