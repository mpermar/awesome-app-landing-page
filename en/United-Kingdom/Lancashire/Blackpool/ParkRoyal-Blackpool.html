Description

All guest rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area. Free WiFi is available to all guests, while some rooms also offer a DVD player. The rooms have a desk.

A continental breakfast is available every morning at ParkRoyal Blackpool.

Coral Island is 1.2 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Liverpool John Lennon Airport, 33 miles from ParkRoyal Blackpool.
