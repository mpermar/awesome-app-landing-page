Description

Ideally located in Blackpool’s Promenade, the Royal Seabank Hotel offers en suite accommodation, evening entertainment, a restaurant and a bar.

Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas. Each of the rooms at the Royal Seabank Hotel feature a flat-screen TV with cable channels and tea/coffee making facilities.

All rooms offer an en suite bathroom, with a shower or a bath. The property also offers a 24-hour front desk, a night club and a games room.

Nearby popular attractions include Blackpool Illuminations and Madame Tussauds museum, both only 7 minutes’ walk away.

Blackpool Pleasure Beach is just over 1 mile away. Manchester Square Station is just in front.
