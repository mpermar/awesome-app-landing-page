Description

The New Guilderoy Hotel has a great location for families and couples. Each clean bedroom has a private bathroom, tea/coffee, daily fresh towels and a TV with DVD player.

The local tram and bus stop is nearby and can take you into Cleveleys or Fleetwood Market. The North Shore Golf Course is about 0.7 miles away.

The restaurant has an Oriental-style décor and offers evening meals, a bar menu and good breakfasts.

Uncle Tom’s Cabin is a short walk away and has well-priced drinks and live entertainment on some nights.
