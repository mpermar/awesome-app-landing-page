Description

Opposite Blackpool Beach, Sandford Promenade is just 10 minutes’ walk from Blackpool Pleasure Beach.

With free parking and cooked breakfasts, the hotel has a games room, sun lounge and bar.

WiFi is available throughout the hotel. The individual rooms at Sandford each have a private bathroom and flat-screen TV with Freeview channels.

Some rooms have wonderful sea views, and all feature tea/coffee facilities and a radio.

The lounge bar serves light meals, snacks and beverages, and there is also a dining room.

Full English breakfasts are served daily, and the games room features a pool table and slot machines.

With a central location, just metres from the sandy beach, Sandford Promenade Hotel is a 20-minute walk from Blackpool Tower.
