Description

Situated in Blackpool, the family-run Danescourt Lodge offers free Wi-Fi throughout.

It is just a minute's walk from Ocean Boulevard, home to Blackpool Pleasure Beach, the South Pier, and amusement arcades.

The rooms feature a TV and tea and coffee making facilities. Each of the rooms benefits from an en suite shower room.

In the mornings, the Danescourt Lodge serves a breakfast menu. Evening meals are also available, in the spacious dining room.

Guests can also relax with a drink in the lounge bar, which offers a TV and a pool table.

It is often host to live entertainment. The family-friendly Danescourt Lodge is located along a bus route.

It has links to central Blackpool, which is just 2 miles away. The nearby promenade also offers frequent tram connections to the Golden Mile, the Three Piers, and the famous Blackpool Tower.

Local coach operators offer a wide range of daily excursions to many of the area's popular attractions.
