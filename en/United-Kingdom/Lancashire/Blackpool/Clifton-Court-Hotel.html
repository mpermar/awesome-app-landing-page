Description

Located in South Beach, next to Pleasure Beach, Sandcastle Water Park, trams, the pier and all attractions.

The hotel has some parking, and free parking is also available on the road opposite.

Clifton Court Hotel has Pirates Rum Pub for cold beers, and the Banthai Lounge with free hot drinks.

It has won a Visit England breakfast award for its food, and a full English breakfast is provided each morning.

With a 'Metro Hotel' rating from Visit Britain, Clifton Court Hotel has en suite rooms with Freeview TV, DVD player, an iPod dock and bottled water.

Pleasure Beach Rail Station is next the hotel, and Blackpool Airport is 2 minutes away.
