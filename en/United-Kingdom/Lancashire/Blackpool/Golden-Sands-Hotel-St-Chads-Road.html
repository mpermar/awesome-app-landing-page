Description

Golden Sands Hotel on St Chads Road offers bed and breakfast just 1 minute walk from Blackpool’s promenade, between the South and Central Piers.

Each guest room is uniquely styled with an en-suite shower room, a flat-screen TV, towels, a hairdryer, free tea and coffee facilities, together with toiletries and daily housekeeping.

The Deluxe Loft Room And Family Suites also have a table top fridge. Guests can enjoy a full English/American breakfast together with toast, jams, cereal, fruit and yoghurt, served each morning with vegan and vegetarian options.

This hotel is a 15 minute’ walk from Blackpool Pleasure Beach. Blackpool Tower is around 20 minutes away.
