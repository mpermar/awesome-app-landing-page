Description

Located in Blackpool, Canberra Hotel offers free WiFi in public areas, free parking, and a bar.

The hotel is just 3 minutes' walk from the seafront and Blackpool Pleasure Beach can be reached after a 5-minute stroll.

Each room includes a flat-screen TV and tea and coffee making facilities. The en-suite shower rooms have complimentary toiletries.

In the mornings, the Canberra Hotel serves both continental and full cooked breakfasts.

There are numerous shops, restaurants, and bars within a 10-minute walk. With a shared lounge, Canberra Hotel is 5 minutes' walk from the popular Sandcastle Water Park.
