Description

The units have a fully equipped kitchen with dining area, an oven, electric tea pot, and a microwave.

Spa and wellness facilities including a hot tub, a sauna and a fitness center are at guests' disposal during their stay at the resort village.

A a private beach area is available on site and fishing can be enjoyed within close proximity of Freshwater Beach Holiday Park.

Weymouth is 14 miles from the accommodation, while Taunton is 27 miles away. The nearest airport is Exeter International Airport, 30 miles from Freshwater Beach Holiday Park.
