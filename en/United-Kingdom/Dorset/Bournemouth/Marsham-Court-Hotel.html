Description

With a stunning location on East Cliff, overlooking Bournemouth’s Pier, bay and beaches, the family-run award-winning Marsham Court Hotel offers free Wi-Fi.

Boasting a south-facing swimming pool, heated May to September, there is also a restaurant.

Stylish bedrooms are individually decorated have modern bathrooms, many featuring a large walk in shower.

A flat-screen TV, laptop safe, ironing facilities and tea and coffee are available in every room.

Guests can enjoy the on-site bar, which serves a selection of drinks, as well as afternoon tea and homemade dishes.

The property features award-winning gardens, with sun terraces overlooking the bay and offering sea views.

Overlooking Bournemouth’s sandy beaches, Marsham Court Hotel is just metres from the seafront.

Bournemouth centre and shopping streets, can be reached in 5 minutes’ walk. Bournemouth Pavillion Theatre is a 5-minute walk from the property, while Bournemouth International Centre is a 9-minute walk away.
