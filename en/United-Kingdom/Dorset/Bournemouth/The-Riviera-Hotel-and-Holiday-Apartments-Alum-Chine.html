Description

The Riviera Hotel has a peaceful location, looking out over the trees of Alum Chine to the bay.

With free Wi-Fi and free parking, leisure facilities include a hot tub and indoor pool.

The property also features a small studio gym and offers spa treatments. Every bedroom is well-equipped, featuring tea/coffee facilities, a flat-screen TV, work area and private bathroom.

Many also have a balcony with sea views. Pets are allowed on request and charges will apply.

The Riviera Hotel & Holiday Apartments Alum Chine is just a few hundred metres from the clean, sandy beach.

Bournemouth pier is a mile away along the seafront. Boasting sea views, the award winning Cunarder Conservatory Restaurant provides a contemporary take on a traditional British menu, which is adjusted accordingly with the seasons.

The hotels offers a games area with pool table, table tennis, Nintendo Wii, table football, board games and childrens' toys.
