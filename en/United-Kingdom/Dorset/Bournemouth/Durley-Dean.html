Description

This Victorian townhouse offers bright, modern rooms, located just 5 minutes’ walk from Bournemouth’s award-winning beach.

With a restaurant and lounge bar, there is also free high-speed WiFi. The Durley Dean’s rooms boast contemporary design and each features a flat-screen TV, tea and coffee facilities, and a work area.

All rooms also have a private bathroom with a hairdryer. The hotel is well-placed, 0.

3 miles from the bars and shops of the town centre. 10 minutes’ walk brings guests to the Bournemouth International Centre and Bournemouth Pier.

Residents have free use of the swimming pool, gym, sauna, and steam room that make up the hotel Leisure Complex.

There is also a golf club within 5 minutes’ drive. The menu of British cuisine is made using local produce, and served in the stylish No.

28 restaurant. The Durley’s bar offers Dorset Cream teas, beverages and snacks during the day.
