Description

The restaurant has a AA Rosette and features fixed menus prepared with local produce, including seafood. Alfresco dining is possible on the terrace in summer.

The comfortable bedrooms have traditional decor with a modern twist and fine bedding, toiletries and furniture. The staff is warm, friendly and ready to help.

Guests can enjoy free access to the Merton Hotel's Aquadome Leisure Club with its indoor pool, sauna, gym and café.
