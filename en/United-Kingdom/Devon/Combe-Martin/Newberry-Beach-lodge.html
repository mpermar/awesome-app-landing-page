Description

Newberry Beach Lodge offers bed and breakfast accommodation in the Newberry Valley, in the North Devon Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty.

The Lodge offers views of Combe Martin Bay. Every room is fitted with a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

You will find a kettle in the room. Each room comes with a private bathroom. A hairdryer and free toiletries are also provided.

Newberry Beach lodge features free WiFi. Popular local activities include walking, the South West Coast Path passes by the front gate of the property.

Newberry Beach Lodge is 800 metres from the edge of Exmoor National Park. Barnstaple is 19.
