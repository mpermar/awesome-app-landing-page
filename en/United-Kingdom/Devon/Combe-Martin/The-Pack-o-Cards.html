Description

Located in Combe Martin, The Pack o' Cards features a restaurant, bar, garden, and free WiFi throughout the property.

Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a children's playground.

Private parking can be arranged at an extra charge. All units at the hotel are equipped with a seating area and a flat-screen TV.

The rooms will provide guests with a wardrobe and a kettle. A à la carte breakfast is available daily at The Pack o' Cards.

The accommodation offers a sun terrace. Guests at The Pack o' Cards will be able to enjoy activities in and around Combe Martin, like hiking and cycling.

Barnstaple is 21 km from the hotel, while Ilfracombe is 9 km away. The nearest airport is Exeter International Airport, 95 km from The Pack o' Cards.
