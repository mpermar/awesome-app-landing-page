Description

Immersed within 19 acres of child-friendly woodland on the beautiful Devon coast, this family-run hotel boasts magnificent views over the golden sandy beaches and Dawlish Warren Nature Reserve.

A former Victorian gentleman’s residence, Langstone Cliff now provides comfortable hotel accommodation suitable for long and short breaks for both business and leisure travellers.

With so much to see and do in the area, the hotel is also a popular destination for families.

The 64 en-suite bedrooms are located on just 2 floors with 10 rooms located on the ground floor.

The remainder are on the first floor which can be reached by a lift or 4 staircases.

For moments of relaxation, you can stroll through the gardens or enjoy views of the sea from one of the communal rooms.

3 exceptionally comfortable lounges offer you a quiet corner for a chat or even a snooze.

The hotel has both indoor and outdoor swimming pools for guests to enjoy. The indoor pool is open all year round and the outdoor pool is seasonally opened.

There are some limited spa facilities and massages available. The hotel offers holistic and beauty therapy and also a hairdressing salon on site.
