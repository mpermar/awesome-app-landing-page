Description

Dawlish Warren Holidays consists of four 2 bedroom chalets - Beaches, Sunny View, The Cove and Driftwood.

They are located on the 4-star Welcome Holiday Park in Dawlish Warren. Guests are invited to use the Holiday Park's indoor swimming pools, bar, restaurant and evening entertainment, which are available from Easter until the end of October each year, for a surcharge.

Offering free WiFi, each chalet consists of an open-plan living, kitchen and dining area.

The living room has a flat-screen TV and DVD player. The kitchen contains a full-size oven and hob, microwave, toaster, kettle and fridge with ice box and is fully equipped for up to four guests.

There is one double bedroom with a flat-screen TV and DVD player, and one twin bedroom.

Each chalet has an outside seating area. The bungalow offers free parking. The chalets are a 500 metre flat walk from the European Blue Flag awarded beach and 300 metre flat walk to Dawlish Warren railway station.

There are shops, supermarkets, pubs and restaurants within a short walk of the chalets.

The nearest airports are Exeter Airport, which is 14 miles away, Bristol Airport, which is 75 miles away, or Newquay, 92 miles away.
