Description

On the edge of Dartmoor National Park, Moorland Garden Hotel is situated between Plymouth and Tavistock.

The elegant hotel features a traditional restaurant and 9 acres of beautiful grounds.

The individually decorated rooms at Moorland Garden Hotel have Wi-Fi access and a TV with satellite channels.

All rooms feature a cosy seating area, tea/coffee facilities and a work desk. With wonderful views across Dartmoor, the bright and airy restaurant serves British cuisine.

The Dartmoor Bar offers a range of light meals, snacks and indulgent afternoon teas.

Situated in Yelverton, the Moorland Garden Hotel is surrounded by the scenic countryside of the Tamar Valley.

Free on-site parking is available, and Plymouth Harbour is a 20-minute drive away.
