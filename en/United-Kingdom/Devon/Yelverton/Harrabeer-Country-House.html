Description

This delightful little country house offers relaxed surroundings with many of the services of a hotel.

Harrabeer is rated Visit England 4-star Guest Accommodation with Silver Award, AA 4 Gold Star Guest Accommodatoin and is a past finalist for the AA Landlady of Year award.

This licensed country house has an attractive dining room, a sitting room, and a refurbished bar with a wood-burning stove.

Located on a quiet residential lane in Yelverton, Harrabeer is conveniently placed to enjoy the outdoor activities in and around Dartmoor.

The property is only 5 miles from Tavistock (Market Town of the Year) and 9 miles from Plymouth for shopping, culture and nightlife.
