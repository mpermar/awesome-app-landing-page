Description

St. Weonards Hotel offers bed and breakfast accommodation in Paignton on the south Devon coast.

Just 4 minutes’ walk from Paignton Train Station and the Eastern Esplanade, St. Weonards Hotel provides free parking and free Wi-Fi.

Each room has en suite, private or shared bathroom facilities with a shower and complimentary toiletries.

There is a flat-screen TV with Freeview and tea and coffee facilities and mineral water in all rooms.

Cooked breakfast is served in the elegant dining room each morning. The guest house is also within a short walk of Paignton’s many cafés and restaurants.

Paignton lies around halfway between Brixham and Torquay, both 10-12 minutes’ drive away.
