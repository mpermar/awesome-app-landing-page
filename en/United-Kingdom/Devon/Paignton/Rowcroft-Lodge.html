Description

The 3-star Rowcroft Lodge has a peaceful location overlooking Goodrington Park. A 3-minute walk from the beach, views from the balcony look onto the sea and Brixham in the distance.

Each en suite room is individually decorated in neutral colours, and large family rooms are available.

All rooms have tea/coffee-making facilities and TV with Freeview channels. Dogs are also welcome in selected pet-friendly rooms.

Rowcroft Lodge offers fresh, home-cooked breakfasts. The guest house has its own bar area with a wide selection of drinks.

The many attractions of Torbay are close at hand, with Paignton Zoo within a mile of the hotel.

The centre of Paignton is less than a 10-minute walk away with its harbour and pleasure pier.
