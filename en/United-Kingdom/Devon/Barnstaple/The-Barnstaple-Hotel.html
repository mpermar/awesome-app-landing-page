Description

With indoor and outdoor pools, a fully equipped gym, and free Wi-Fi, this hotel is just a 15 minute stroll to the town centre.

Offering free parking, it is only a 10 minute drive to the glorious beaches of North Devon.

Each elegant room enjoys a flat-screen TV and tea/coffee facilities for guests to relax with.

Most rooms have views over the outdoor pool and garden, and all feature a modern en suite bathroom.

Free access to the hotel's leisure centre is available to all guests, which also provides an indulgent relaxation suite with a range of luxurious therapies and treatments.

The Barnstaple Hotel has a stylish brasserie restaurant and a health and leisure club including indoor and outdoor heated swimming pools.
