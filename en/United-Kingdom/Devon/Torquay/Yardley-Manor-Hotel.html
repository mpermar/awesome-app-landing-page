Description

With a quiet location away from traffic noise, Yardley Manor has free Wi-Fi and guaranteed free parking on site.

It boasts an outdoor swimming pool, and is only 10 minutes walk from Torquays popular harbour.

Rooms have simple, elegant dcor and traditional wooden furnishings. Each has its own en suite shower, a work desk, TV, and tea/coffee making facilities.

There is a spacious dining room with a rich red carpet and exposed brick walls. Breakfast is provided each morning, and you can enjoy a range of drinks in the bar, where it is free to play pool in the evenings.

Guests can enjoy karaoke and darts at Yardley Manor, and attractions like Princess Theatre and the towns seafront restaurants can be reached in 15 minutes walk or less.
