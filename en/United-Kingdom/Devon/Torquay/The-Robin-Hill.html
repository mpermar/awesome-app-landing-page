Description

Perched at the top of a hill, The Robin Hill is a charming bed and breakfast in the heart of the seaside town of Torquay.

With views overlooking Torwood Gardens, it has free Wi-Fi, free on-site parking and is a 10 minute walk from restaurants, the harbour, and Fleet Walk Shopping Centre.

Originally built in 1896, the rooms each include a TV with Freeview channels, tea and coffee making facilities and an en suite bathroom with complimentary toiletries.

In the morning guests can relax in the spacious and inviting breakfast room, where a hearty full English breakfast, a selection of omelettes, assorted eggs or a continental breakfast is served.

Vegetarian and special dietary requirements can also be catered for. A separate lounge and conservatory are available for guest use during the day.

Guests at Robin Hill can also enjoy the licensed bar in the evenings. It has a variety of beers, wines and spirits on offer.

The historic Torre Abbey and Torquay Rail Station are both less than 2 miles away.

Babbacombe Model Village is a short 6-minute drive away and Kents Cavern Prehistoric Caves are also a 3-minute journey in the car.
