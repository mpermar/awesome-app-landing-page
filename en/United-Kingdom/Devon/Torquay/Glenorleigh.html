Description

Featuring free WiFi and a seasonal outdoor pool, Glenorleigh is a family-run accommodation in Torquay, 480 metres from Torre train station, and a 15-minute walk from Torquay Bay.

Each bedroom offers a flat-screen TV, tea and coffee making facilities, and an en suite shower with complimentary toiletries.

Some of the bedrooms offer a bathtub. A cooked English breakfast including vegetarian options is available each morning.

Guests are able to enjoy the on-site bar and the sun terrace overlooking a Mediterranean-style garden with feature palms and a heated swimming pool.

There is a shared lounge and a games room at Glenorleigh. Torre Abbey is 600 metres from Glenorleigh, while Mount Stuart Hospital is 600 metres from the property.

Torquay Harbour is 1. 3 miles from Glenorleigh. The nearest airport is Exeter International Airport, 31 km from the property.
