Experiences

11/28/2017

Cottage was immaculate and in a wonderful setting with lots...

"Cottage was immaculate and in a wonderful setting with lots of country walks all around - yet just a few minutes' drive from the heart of Cardiff city.Hosts were extremely helpful, kind and considerate yet discreet despite living close by on the Estate. The decor and furnishings were unexpectedly glitzy and highly modern for a country property - but so comfortable and lots of fun! Plenty of board games ; lovely toiletries; excellent AV provision. The kitchen was splendidly designed with luxurious white and electrical goods including a nespresso machine as well as a cafetière. However, anyone who cooks meals from scratch would welcome the provion of basics such as a colander and seive; salad spinner and serving implements; assorted wooden spoons; measuring jugs; potato peeler; whisk; an assortment of food prep and serving platters and bowls; a few plastic storage containers . Most importantly - a couple of much bigger pans with lids for pasta, soups etc. The outside facilities were amazing and convenient to the cottages, for those with younger children in particular. The entire set up would make a wonderful venue for a multiple family holiday where all the occupants of the various cottages could enjoy their privacy but also the sociability of the shared games room, barbeque, playground, pool and hot tub. Our party of 4 adults thoroughly enjoyed our brief stay and would certainly plan to return!"
