Description

Situated on a tree-lined boulevard in Southports centre, The Bold Hotel features a bar with an extensive menu.

Each room at The Bold Hotel has large windows, and includes a TV, hospitality tray and free Wi-Fi.

Private bathrooms include complimentary toiletries and hairdryers. Full English breakfasts are served in the elegant dining room, and guests can enjoy traditional British cuisine in the open-plan dining area, with a number of specialties, including fresh fish.

Discounted rates for advanced bookings are available. There is also a range of fine wines and a relaxing bar.

The outside dining area is filled with umbrellas, tables, and chairs for warm days, and has views along bustling Lord Street.

Southport Rail Station is just 5 minutes walk away. All of Southports shops and attractions can be reached in 10 minutes on foot.
