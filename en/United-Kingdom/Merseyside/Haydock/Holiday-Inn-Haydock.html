Description

The hotel is superbly located, just off junction 23 of the M6 motorway, next to Haydock Park Racecourse, and serves as a gateway to the entire North West region.

The Holiday Inn Haydock Hotel is in close proximity to Manchester and Liverpool, via the A580 that links the two cities.

Also within a 15-mile radius of the hotel are the towns of St Helens, Warrington, Wigan, Leigh and Bolton.

Whether you are exploring the area as a tourist or visiting the region on business, the hotel’s excellent location and superb access to the area’s major road network make it a convenient base.

WiFi is available throughout the hotel. All rooms have interactive flat-screen TVs.
