Description

On the edge of Basingstoke, The Hampshire Court Hotel - QHotels is a modern 4-star hotel with an award-winning restaurant and brasserie bar, as well as leisure facilities, including 2 indoor pools.

It offers easy access to transport networks, including the M3, and is within an hour's drive of London and Heathrow Airport.

The Hampshire Court Hotel offers en-suite rooms, all with a bath and a separate walk-in shower.

Each room has a TV with selected Sky channels and comes with free WiFi. The leisure facilities include 5 indoor tennis courts, and there is also a spa with a steam room and sauna, as well as a gym.

The award-winning Hampshire Restaurant offers a traditional menu with a modern twist, featuring classic dishes made with the best of British sourced produce.

Relaxed and informal, the Brasserie Bar is open all day, offering speciality Costa coffees, light meals and snacks.

Alfresco dining is also available in the warmer months in the stunning Courtyard Garden.

There are over 200 free parking spaces. Nearby attractions include Jane Austen's House Museum, Legoland and Winchester Cathedral.
