Description

The 4-star Silver AA-rated Balmer Lawn is situated in the heart of the New Forest and has a bar, a 2 AA Rosette-awarded restaurant, and a fitness centre.

There is space for free private parking on site. The area offers ageless beauty and tranquillity with unspoilt paths and walks around the New Forest, where ponies and wild deer roam.

Many activities can be enjoyed, including walking, cycling, and mountain biking. Balmer Lawn offers its own extensive leisure facilities, including a swimming pool, gym, and courts for playing squash.

The rooms are elegant and modern, with a TV with Freeview channels and facilities for making tea and coffee.

Each also has an private bathroom with a hairdryer. Beresfords Restaurant serves fine food, made with local ingredients.

Guests can enjoy modern English and traditional dishes, and a large wine list. In the summer months, al fresco dining is available.
