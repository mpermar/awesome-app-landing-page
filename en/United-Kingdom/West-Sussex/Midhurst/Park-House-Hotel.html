Description

In an Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty, this charming Victorian house is surrounded by 10 acres of grounds in the village of Bepton in West Sussex.

It has free Wi-Fi, free parking, and beautiful views of the South Downs National Park.

Park House Hotel provides a farmhouse buffet and full English breakfast each morning, and in the drawing room you can enjoy traditional afternoon tea with freshly baked biscuits and homemade cakes.

Sunday lunches are also served, with food made from fresh, locally produced seasonal ingredients.

Leisure facilities include a 15-metre indoor swimming pool, Jacuzzi®, steam room, sauna, gym and fitness studio.

The award-winning spa offers a range of beauty treatments and therapies. Bedrooms at Park House are elegant and individually designed, are equipped with satellite TV, tea and coffee-making facilities, hairdryers and complimentary slippers.

The charming market town of Midhurst is a a 5-minute drive and the historic city of Chichester is just 20 minutes away.
