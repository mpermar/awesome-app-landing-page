Description

The rooms at St Brelade’s Bay Hotel feature excellent sea or garden views, and some rooms also include balconies. The rooms also provide LCD TVs with satellite channels, a fresh fruit bowl upon arrival, and heated towel racks.

The garden features an astro-turf tennis court, and guests can also enjoy a heated swimming pool, heated children’s pool, a fitness center and a children’s play area.

Dining can be enjoyed at The Bay Restaurant, serving a wide choice of fresh cuisine made from local produce. On the grounds, The Petit Port Cafe features views of the swimming pool and St Brelade’s Bay.
