Description

Great care has been taken to preserve the unique character of this charming house, while every modern facility has been discretely installed for the comfort of today's discerning traveler, with easy access to the nearby Glasgow Prestwick Airport.

The hotel offers a fully equipped health club with swimming pool, sauna and steam room, gym and in-house beauty therapist.

All the rooms with private bathrooms are beautifully furnished and decorated to a standard one would expect from a 4-star hotel. The creation of additional bedrooms results in a great balance between old and new.

The new Martins Bar and Grill restaurant was voted Best Informal Dining Restaurant in Scotland at the Scottish Hotel of the Year Awards in 2006.
