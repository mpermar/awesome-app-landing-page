Description

Bright and stylish, this modern hotel is just minutes from junction 9 of the M6 and Bescot Stadium Railway Station, offering direct trains to Birmingham city centre with links to the NEC and airport.

It offers free Wi-Fi throughout. The Park Inn by Radisson Birmingham Walsall has a convenient location, with excellent transport links for travel all around Birmingham, Walsall, Wednesbury and the West Midlands.

Trains run regularly to Birmingham New Street Railway Station, with a journey time of just 20 minutes.

The fresh, light and modern rooms are stylish and well equipped, with modern en suite facilities with walk-in power showers.

All rooms have plasma flat-screen TVs with Freeview channels. The well-lit desk areas have extra telephone ports and good lighting.

The hotel has a stylish, modern meeting room for up to 30 delegates, as well as a brand new fitness suite with state-of-the-art cardiovascular and fitness equipment.
