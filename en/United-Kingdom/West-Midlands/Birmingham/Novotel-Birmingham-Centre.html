Description

The 4-star bedrooms at Novotel Birmingham Centre each have a luxury bathroom with a hairdryer. Rooms also feature a minibar and a flat-screen TV and internet access.

Elements Restaurant serves international cuisine in contemporary surroundings. The sleek bar has a range of beverages and light snacks, and 24-hour room service is also available.

Novotel Birmingham City Centre has a 24-hour front desk and secure on-site parking. Guests can work out in the gym or relax in the sauna in the fitness center.

On Broad Street, Novotel Birmingham City Centre is a 5-minute walk from Symphony Hall. The National Indoor Arena is a 10-minute walk from the property, and the ICC is 950 feet away.
