Description

The Belfry Hotel & Resort boasts 319 bedrooms and suites, 2 restaurants and 2 bars, Bel Air Nightclub, 22 conference and event suites, a state-of-the-art leisure club and a luxury spa, PGA golf academy, onsite golf shop and 3 world-class golf courses, The Brabazon, PGA National and The Derby, which are all set in 550 acres of north Warwickshire countryside.

The Leisure club features a fully-equipped gym, and a pool, sauna and solarium where hotel guests can relax free of charge.

At a surcharge, the unique Fire & Ice experience includes a tranquil journey through 12 bio thermal rooms and the spa offers a range of luxurious treatments and therapies to include within a delightful spa day with a partner or friends.

Featuring a TV, radio and complementary tea and coffee, each room also has a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

Some rooms have stunning views of the courses, whilst others overlook the hotel grounds.

Guests have a wide selection of dining options, all using fresh ingredients. The Brabazon Bar has a range of specialist coffees, served alongside sandwiches and afternoon tea and Bel Air nightclub which operates on Thursday, Friday and Saturday nights.
