Description

In a tranquil area of South Wirral, this grand Victorian hotel features an elegant restaurant and luxury rooms with spa baths.

Brook Meadow Hotel is situated between Chester and Liverpool, just 5 minutes' drive from Junction 5 of the M53, providing easy access to all major motorways.

The bright and airy bedrooms have free Wi-Fi access. All rooms feature a private modern bathroom and tea/coffee facilities, and many overlook the leafy gardens.

The popular restaurant serves traditional British cuisine using locally sourced produce.

Guests can relax in the conservatory lounge bar, which offers light meals, snacks and hearty breakfasts.

The Brook Meadow Hotel is set in mature gardens and woodland, in the rural surroundings of Childer Thornton.
