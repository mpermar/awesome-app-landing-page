Description

In the centre of rural village of Pickmere, this charming grade II listed Georgian farmhouse offers light, airy rooms with flat-screen TVs.

The house has a small garden, and serves fresh coffee with locally sourced (wherever possible) home-cooked breakfasts.

With free tea, coffee and hot chocolate, each room also has a bathroom with a hairdryer.

Rooms are simply but brightly decorated, and are supplied with ironing facilities on request.

Pickmere Country House’s varied breakfast menu includes classic cooked items as well as lighter continental options.

The property is fully licensed and serves evening meals from Monday - Thursday (booking recommended) 18:00 to 19:30 and weekends by prior arrangement.

The guest lounge has a flat-screen TV, a fireplace, a small library and a piano. Guests can relax on the terrace in the small garden.

Pickmere Country House is placed in the Cheshire countryside, 8 minutes’ drive from both Knutsford and Northwich.
