Description

Elegantly furnished with colorful cushions and original artwork, the spacious rooms at Iffin have flat-screen TVs and modern bathrooms. All rooms have views of the grounds, and tea/coffee facilities are provided.

A variety of traditional cooked breakfasts are served daily in the dining room, featuring fresh Kentish produce. Evening meals can also be arranged, with vegetarian and special diet options available.

Iffin Farmhouse has been awarded a Green Tourism Silver Award for its environmental friendliness.

Just a 10 minute drive from Canterbury Cathedral, Iffin Farmhouse is a 5-minute drive from Canterbury East Train station. Dover International Ferry Port can be reached in a 30 minute drive.
