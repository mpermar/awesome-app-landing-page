Description

Vale Farm Cottages are set on a farm 6. 4 km from the Brecon Beacons National Park.

The pet-friendly cottages feature either a sun room or a balcony offering mountain views.

Each cottage has a well equipped kitchen, a barbecue and a patio. There is also a dining area and a sitting room with a flat-screen TV.

A dog walking service is available on request for an additional fee. There is a shared (between the 6 cottages) heated indoor swimming pool and hot tub with views of the Brecon Beacons.

There is a play area at the farm, with areas to explore. Popular local activities include hiking, cycling, mountain biking and golf.

There are also wildlife watching opportunities nearby. Brecon Cathedral is 9 km from Vale Farm Cottages.

Pen y Fan is 19 km away. The nearest airport is Cardiff Airport, 92 km from the property.
