Description

Set in private parklands in a Georgian mansion house, Penventon Park Hotel is just 4 km from the A30, well situated for exploring all that Cornwall has to offer.

Free public on-site parking and high-speed WiFi is provided. Guests can take advantage of complimentary leisure club access for all guests including a heated indoor pool, Jacuzzi® and sauna with a range of spa treatments available when booking in advance.

Kick start your evening in the Copper Bar which features over 160 gins before dining in the restaurant.

Each room includes a TV and tea and coffee facilities with a private bathroom and a selection of toiletries, hairdryer and iron with free WiFi throughout.

Some Superior rooms are pet-friendly and have access to an outdoor patio and the hotel's gardens.

Redruth Rail Station and the town centre are a 10-minute walk away. The small coastal town of Portreath is 10 minutes’ drive away and St Ives’ beaches and art galleries are less than 30 minutes away by car.

Visit the Great Gardens of Cornwall which are all in driving distance to the hotel.
