Description

Situated in a sheltered valley, the family-run Trengilly Wartha Inn offers free Wi-Fi in public areas and free private parking.

With a traditional pub, it is just 15 minutes’ drive from both Helston and Falmouth.

The rooms include a TV with a DVD player and tea and coffee making facilities with biscuits.

Each of the rooms benefits from an en suite bathroom. DVDs are available to hire from the breakfast room.

Dishes prepared using locally sourced produce are served in the relaxed bar at the award-winning Trengilly Wartha Inn.

Guests can also enjoy more private dining in the bistro or conservatory area. Serving real ales, the bar is often host to live musical events and game nights.

The Trengilly Wartha Inn is ideal for exploring the Cornish countryside, which is surrounded by stretches of coastline.

There are numerous routes, where guests can enjoy scenic walks or bike rides. The Trebah Garden is just 10 minutes’ drive from the property and the Flambards Theme Park is 5 miles away.
