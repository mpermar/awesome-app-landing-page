Description

A 5-minute drive from Fowey, this elegant Victorian manor house has beautiful views.

Set in 24 acres of flowing countryside, the property also features an à la carte restaurant, free Wi-Fi in public areas, and free parking on site.

Trenython Manor has a children’s playground, a fitness centre, and an on-site spa offering personalised body treatments.

There is also a heated indoor swimming pool, a spa bath and a sauna for you to enjoy.

Alternatively guests can enjoy croquet, try pétanque, take a woodland walk, build dens or test archery skills.

Rooms at Trenython Manor Hotel & Spa are traditionally furnished, and each room’s bathroom has free toiletries and a hairdryer.

All rooms also come with a flat-screen TV, a DVD player, and facilities for making tea and coffee.

Self-catering lodges are also available. Lunch is served in the Trenython Bar and Lounge, with the exception of Sundays where a Sunday Roast is served in the TM's Restaurant.

In the morning, a choice of Continental Buffet as well as a full cooked breakfast is provided.

Guests can enjoy local attractions such as the Eden Project, established in 2001 and only 5 minutes' drive away.

The historical Tintagel Castle, where children can explore the nearby Merlin's Cave, is 30 miles away.
