Description

Perched high on Newquay's Pentire Headland, Pentire Hotel sits between Fistral Beach and the Gannel Estuary.

The hotel has wonderful views, a modern leisure centre and free Wi-Fi. The bright and airy bedrooms at Pentire each have a private modern bathroom with toiletries and a hairdryer.

All rooms feature tea/coffee facilities and a TV, and some have sea views. Pentire Leisure Centre features table tennis facilities, a pool table and an indoor heated swimming pool.

Guests can relax in the steam room, or in the leafy gardens and spacious sun terrace.

The traditional restaurant serves a varied menu, featuring quality local produce and fresh seafood.

Cooked breakfasts are available daily, and the lounge bar offers fresh coffee and speciality beers.

Just a 20-minute walk from Newquay centre, The Pentire Hotel is a mile from Newquay Golf Course.

Free on-site parking is available, and Newquay Railway Station is a 5-minute drive away.
