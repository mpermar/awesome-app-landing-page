Description

Newquay Deluxe Holiday Homes is situated in Newquay View Resort, within just a few minutes' drive of the centre of Newquay.

The resort has a seasonal outdoor heated pool, an indoor pool, a sauna and a steam room, a children's playground and offers evening entertainment.

A coffee shop and Bistro, a fish and chip shop, and a bar can be found on site. Further facilities include a fitness studio, bike hire, and a surf school all within the park.

A launderette, ATM and a grocery store are available for guest's convenience. Each holiday home features a fully-equipped kitchen, a seating area with a flat-screen TV with DVD player, and a decked area.

Free WiFi is available at many locations around the resort. Fistral Beach is 4. 4 km from Newquay Deluxe Holiday Homes, while Newquay Zoo is 2.

8 km from the property. The nearest airport is Newquay Cornwall Airport, 4 km from Newquay Deluxe Holiday Homes.
