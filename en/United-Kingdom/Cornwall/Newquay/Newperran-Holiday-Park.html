Description

With garden views, Newperran Holiday Park is situated in Newquay and has a restaurant and bar.

Each unit features a terrace, a fully equipped kitchen with a microwave, a seating area with a sofa, a TV and a private bathroom with shower.

There is also a fridge, oven and a kettle. At the camping guests are welcome to take advantage of an indoor swimming pool.

A children's playground is also available for guests at Newperran Holiday Park. Tolcarne Beach is 10 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Newquay Cornwall Airport, 19 km from Newperran Holiday Park.
