Description

Located in Newquay in the Cornwall Region, 4. 4 km from Fistral Beach, Newquay Bay Resort features an outdoor swimming pool with terrace and children's playground.

Newquay Zoo is 2. 8 km from the property. WiFi is available in the Venue Bar and Cafe.

Porth beach is a 15 minute walk from the property. The accommodation features a seating area.

Some units have a dining area and/or terrace. A microwave and kettle are also provided.

Some units also have a kitchen, fitted with an oven. Newquay Bay Resort also includes a sauna and sun terrace.

Guests can enjoy a drink at the on-site bar. The property also offers mini-market.

The property offers surfing lessons and hire of surf equipment, as well as bike hire.

You can play table tennis at the property, and the area is popular for golfing. Newquay Hospital is 3.

1 km from Newquay Bay Resort, while Newquay Blue Reef Aquarium is 3. 4 km away. The nearest airport is Newquay Cornwall Airport, 3 km from the property.
