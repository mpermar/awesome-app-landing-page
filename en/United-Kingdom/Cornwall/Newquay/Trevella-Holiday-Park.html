Description

Situated in Crantock, Trevella Holiday Park offers award-winning accommodation with a range of static caravans and safari tents.

The holiday park provides guests with a variety of accommodations, some with a balcony, all offering a seating area, a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchenette with a microwave, kettle and toaster, and a private bathroom with shower.

Some of the accommodation is pet-friendly upon request. Trevella Holiday Park offers a seasonal outdoor pool.

The accommodation provides a children's playground. St Ives is 34 km from Trevella Holiday Park, while Newquay is 2.
