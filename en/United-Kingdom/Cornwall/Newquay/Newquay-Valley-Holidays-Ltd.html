Description

Located in Newquay, 4. 4 km from Fistral Beach, Newquay Valley Holidays features a seasonal outdoor pool and views of the garden.

Newquay Zoo is 2. 8 km away. The property is situated on Newquay Views Resort and offers free private parking on site and free WiFi.

All units feature a seating area with a satellite flat-screen TV, CD player, and DVD player.

There is also a kitchen, fitted with an oven. A microwave, a toaster and refrigerator are also offered, as well as a tea/coffee making facilities.

Bed linen is provided. There is a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries in every unit.

Towels are available at a surcharge. Newquay Valley Holidays also includes a children's playground.

Bike hire is available at the property and the area is popular for golfing. Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including windsurfing and cycling.

Newquay Hospital is 3 km from Newquay View, while Newquay Blue Reef Aquarium is 3.

3 km from the property. The nearest airport is Newquay Airport, 4 km from Newquay Valley Holidays.
