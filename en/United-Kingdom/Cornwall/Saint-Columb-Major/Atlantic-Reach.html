Description

Boasting a sun terrace, an indoor swimming pool and a 25-metre outdoor swimming pool, as well as gym and fitness studios, Atlantic Reach is located in Saint Columb Major, on the outskirts of Newquay.

With free WiFi in the main leisure complex and on site pub, this 4-star resort offers an ATM and evening entertainment during the school holidays.

The on-site Manor Arms is open seasonally from March to September. At the resort, the rooms have a patio area.

Atlantic Reach offers some properties that include pool views and each property comes with it's own bathroom, kitchen and living area.

At Atlantic Reach the soft play centre, swimming pool, gym and children's playground can be used.

There is also a seasonal evening entertainment programme. St Ives is 43 km from the resort, while Newquay is 8 km away.
