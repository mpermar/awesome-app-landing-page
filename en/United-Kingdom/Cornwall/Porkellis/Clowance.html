Experiences

09/04/2016

Overall, a very good holiday!

"We stayed in one of the coach apartments. The rooms were clean and all the kitchen utensils we needed were available and of good quality.The grounds were amazing and there were lots to do! We rowed in the lake,fed the ducks, played tennis, football, basket ball etc. The kids clubs were very good and both my 7 year old and 4 year old enjoyed them. The survival club was the highlight of all the clubs- it was very educative for the kids and they were outdoors in the woods for 3 hours making a den, learning about plants, lighting a fire and cooking etc.Overall, a very good holiday. Would love to visit again but stay in one of the lodges instead!"
