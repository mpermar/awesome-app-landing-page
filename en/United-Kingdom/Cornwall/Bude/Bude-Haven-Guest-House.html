Description

Featuring barbecue facilities and garden, as well as a restaurant, Bude Haven Guest House is located in Bude.

The property features a shared lounge, as well as a bar. Both free WiFi and private parking are available at the guest house.

All rooms feature an en suite bathroom with either a bath/shower, a Smart TV with Freeview channels, tea/coffee making facilities and complimentary biscuits.

The room also features white fluffy bath sheets and hand towels plus hand and shower washes.

Double glazed windows, carpets and central heating. Breakfast is freshly prepared and ingredients are locally sourced.

Vegetarian and continental options are available. A hot tub is located in the garden and is for the sole use of guests.

Reservations can be made. Padstow is 43 km from Bude Haven Guest House, while Port Isaac is 34 km away.
