Description

Set in 12 acres of mature cliff-top gardens, this luxury seaside hotel offers wonderful views across Mount's Bay and the Atlantic Ocean. The hotel features a restaurant, leisure center and a cinema room.

All of the stylish bedrooms feature fluffy towels and quality feather bedding. Many rooms have sea views, and each has a private bathroom and a flat-screen TV.

Polurrian on the Lizard has a games room and a leisure club with a swimming pool, gym and luxury spa.

The Polurrian Restaurant serves fresh local produce and seasonal menus, and there are 2 cozy coffee lounges with stunning views.

Polurrian on the Lizard is located next to the Cornish Coastal Path, and has direct access to a private sandy beach. There is free on-site parking, and Mullion Harbor is just a few minutes’ walk away.
