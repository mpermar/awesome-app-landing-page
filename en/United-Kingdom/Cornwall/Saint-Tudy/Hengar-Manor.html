Description

At the resort village you'll find a restaurant serving British cuisine. Vegetarian, Gluten-free and Dairy-free options can also be requested.

Hengar Manor has a playground.

Guests can enjoy the indoor pool at the property.

Plymouth is 28 miles from the accommodation, while Newquay is 19 miles away. The nearest airport is Newquay Airport, 15 miles from the property.
