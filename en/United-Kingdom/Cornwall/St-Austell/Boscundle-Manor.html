Description

Set in 3 acres of grounds, Boscundle Manor boasts a heated indoor swimming pool, an award-winning restaurant, and a spa.

Just 1 mile from The Eden Project, the property also offers free parking and free WiFi.

Boscundle Restaurant serves a seasonal menu and homemade specialities using local produce.

The candlelit restaurant features real silver cutlery, elegant glassware and fresh flowers.

The Boscundle Manor Hotel has terraced gardens, woodlands and a croquet lawn. The Zento Spa has 2 swimming pools and a hot tub.

Guets can also enjoy a conservatory and lounge with a DVD collection and library.

Just 5 minutes from St Austell, Boscundle is a 10-minute walk from Pinetum Park. The Lost Gardens of Heligan can be reached in 20 minutes' drive, and Charlestown is just 1.
