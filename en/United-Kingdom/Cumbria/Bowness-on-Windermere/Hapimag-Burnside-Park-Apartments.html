Description

Hapimag Burnside Park Apartments offers self-catering accommodation in a resort, and is located in Bowness on Windermere, 500 metres from Lake Windermere.

Free private parking and free WiFi are provided. A seating area with a flat-screen TV, a safe and a DVD player are provided in each apartment.

You will find a coffee machine in the fully equipped kitchen. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer.

There is a children's play area and barbecue facilities at Hapimag Burnside Park Apartments.

Guests have access to an indoor pool, sauna, steam bath and fitness room in the hotel next to the accommodation.

Massage and beauty treatments are available here for a surcharge. The area is popular for hiking, fishing and golfing.

The resort also offers bike hire and car hire. Keswick is 31 km from Hapimag Burnside Park Apartments, while Ambleside is 8 km away.
