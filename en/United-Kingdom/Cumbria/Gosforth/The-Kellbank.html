Description

Offering a restaurant, The Kellbank is located in Gosforth. Free WiFi access is available throughout the property.

Featuring a bath or shower, the private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer. Extras include free phone calls, a desk and ironing facilities.

At The Kellbank you will find a bar that serves food throughout the day. Other facilities offered at the property include meeting facilities.
