Description

Set in Aberfoyle, Macdonald Forest Hills Resort has a fitness centre. All rooms feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom.

The resort has a terrace and a hot tub. At the resort, every room comes with a balcony.

Selected rooms include a kitchen with a dishwasher, an oven and a microwave. At Macdonald Forest Hills Resort all rooms come with a seating area.

Guests can make use of the sauna. A variety of popular activities are available in the area, including cycling and hiking.

Loch Katrine is 9 km from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Glasgow Airport, 35 km from the property.
