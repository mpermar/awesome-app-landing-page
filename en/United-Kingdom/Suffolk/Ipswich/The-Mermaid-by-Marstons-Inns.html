Description

Set in Ipswich, The Mermaid by Marston's Inns features a garden and bar. The property is 2 km from IP-City Centre - Conference Venue and 700 metres away from Portman Road.

Ipswich Institute is 1. 3 km away and University Campus Suffolk is 2. 1 km from the hotel.

The units are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kettle, a bath, free toiletries and a desk.

Rooms include a private bathroom. All rooms at the hotel come with a seating area.

A continental, Full English/Irish or American breakfast can be enjoyed at the property.

At The Mermaid by Marston's Inns you will find a restaurant serving British cuisine.

The accommodation offers a children's playground. The Ipswich Hospital is 4. 3 km from The Mermaid by Marston's Inns.
