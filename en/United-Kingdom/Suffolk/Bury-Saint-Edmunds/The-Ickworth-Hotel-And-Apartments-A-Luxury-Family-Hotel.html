Description

The Ickworth Hotel is a few miles from Bury St Edmunds and the A14, which leads to Newmarket and Cambridge. On-site activities include cycling, swimming, walks, and tennis.

The East Wing of the grand building forms the luxury hotel, and there are apartments and bedrooms in the Lodge, a 10-minute walk away, or a 3-minute drive from the main hotel. The lodge is just a short bike ride away and bicycles can be rented on site free of charge.

You can dine in the relaxed conservatory or opt for a more formal adults-only dinner in Fredericks Restaurant.
