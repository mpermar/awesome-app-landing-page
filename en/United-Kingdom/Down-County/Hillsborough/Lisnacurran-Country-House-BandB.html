Description

Lisnacurran B&B is located between Belfast and Dublin, just off the A1 dual carriageway, close to a variety of shops, restaurants and pubs.

Furnished with antiques and rich fabrics, the property is also completed with the modern technology and comforts for the 21st century traveller.

All rooms feature modern comforts and facilities, including Wi-Fi, TV and tea/coffee with crisp white bed linen and designer toiletries.

Superior rooms also boost Smart TV, fridges and microwaves in the rooms. There is also a 3 bedroom apartment that sleeps 8.

There are two guest lounge areas with TV and music. A selection of DVDs, books, board games and magazines are also available.

Set in mature gardens, guests can enjoy the out door hot tub and sauna found in the garden Pavilion, while little guests can play in the Wendy house, tree house, trampoline, jungle gym or soil pit.

Breakfast include a continental buffet with hot choices such as French toast, bacon, maple syrup, traditional Ulster fry and homemade pancakes with bacon and maple Syrup.
