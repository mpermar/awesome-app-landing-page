Description

With a peaceful, tranquil location in the Castlereagh Hills, surrounded by the beautiful countryside of County Down, this 4-star hotel boasts luxury accommodation and great relaxation and leisure facilities.

Just 15 minutes by road from the very heart of Belfast city centre, La Mon Hotel & Country Club is a great choice for a restful, relaxing and restorative break.

The hotel boasts 121 luxurious and well-appointed rooms, as well as award-winning cuisine and an impressive health club.

The superb leisure facilities include 2 fully equipped fitness suites and a 15-metre indoor swimming pool.

You can take advantage of the health club’s tennis court or relaxin the sauna, the spa bath or the steam room.

Alternatively, you could indulge yourself with a little pampering from the hotel’s own highly trained beauticians and hair stylists.

Free Wi-Fi wireless internet access is available throughout the hotel and free parking is available for guests.
