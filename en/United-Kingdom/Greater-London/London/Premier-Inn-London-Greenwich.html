Description

The colorful rooms each provide a flat-screen TV and Wi-Fi access. All the rooms have a large work area and tea/coffee-making facilities, and the bathrooms include a hairdryer and a shower.

An all-you-can-eat breakfast buffet is available daily, including cooked and continental options. The restaurant serves hearty evening meals, and the bar provides fresh coffees and light snacks.

Greenwich is a great choice for travelers interested in live music, riverside walks and galleries.
