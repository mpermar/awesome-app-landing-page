Description

A selection of London's best restaurants and bars, cafes, theaters and nightlife are within walking distance of the hotel, as well as the historic area of Soho Square which features a park and garden.

Each air-conditioned room at The Soho includes a flat-screen TV, fully stocked minibar and an iPod docking station. A private bathroom is also provided. Guests can also use a beauty treatment room, with full body massage, facials and manicures.

Modern European cuisine is served in the on-site restaurant, which also offers a breakfast menu and afternoon tea. A relaxing drink can be enjoyed in the award-winning bar.

Westminster Borough is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, parks and history.
