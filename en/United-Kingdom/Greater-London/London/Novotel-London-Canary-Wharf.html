Description

Featuring a roof terrace with 360 views across the London skyline, Novotel London Canary Wharf also benefits from an on-site gym, pool and contemporary restaurant and bar.

Free WiFi is available throughout the hotel. Each air conditioned guest room features a flat-screen TV, en-suite facilities and a work desk.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property, as well as room service which offers a variety of hot dishes and cold snacks.

The Bōkan Restaurant, Bar and Roof Terrace serves artisanal food and drink made using a mixture of local, ancient and adopted British ingredients.

The menu is inspired by European cultural cuisines. There are panoramic views of London and the Thames, high-speed WiFi and triple-story rooftop dining.

Greenwich Park is 3. 4 km from Novotel London Canary Wharf, while Tower Bridge is 3.

6 km away. The nearest airport is London City Airport, 5 km from Novotel London Canary Wharf.
