Description

Each uniquely designed guest room has floor-to-ceiling windows providing spectacular views of the city, and maintains the theme of Oriental elegance found throughout the hotel. Featuring the body-contouring-technology "Shangri-La Bed," rooms include a TV, iPod dock, coffee machine, complimentary Wi-Fi and a set of binoculars.

The private marble-clad bathrooms have underfloor heating, separate bathtub and a glass-enclosed shower. They include plush bathrobes, Acqua di Parma toiletries and mirrors with integrated TV screens. All rooms feature climate control to complement the building’s integrated sun shielding.

The hotel features 3 dining and drinking venues, including TING, the signature restaurant and lounge on level 35, and LÁNG is a high-quality artisan deli and café. GŎNG, set on level 52, provides the setting for London's highest champagne and cocktail bar.

Shangri-La Hotel at The Shard is on St Thomas Street, less than a 1-minute walk from London Bridge Train and Underground Station.

The Tower of London and Tower Bridge can be reached in 20 minutes by foot. You can enjoy a walk along the Thames River' South Bank to The London Eye, Big Ben and Westminster, approximately 1.5 miles away.

Southwark is a great choice for travelers interested in city walks, monuments and culture.
