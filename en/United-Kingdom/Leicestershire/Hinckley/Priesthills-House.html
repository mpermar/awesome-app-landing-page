Description

Priesthills House in Hinckley boasts a garden and a shared lounge. Located within an Edwardian House, it is a short walk away from the local bars, restaurants and shops.

The property offers free private parking and free WiFi. All rooms come with a smart TV, hairdryer, and a beverage tray.

Some rooms also have a private bathroom. A full traditional English breakfast is freshly cooked and served in the dining room overlooking the garden.

Cereals, yoghurts, fresh fruit and a lighter breakfast choice is also available, along with seasonal items including home made pancakes or smoked salmon.

Special diets, vegetarian and early breakfasts can be provided by prior request. Birmingham is 36 km from Priesthills House, while Nottingham is 49 km from the property.
