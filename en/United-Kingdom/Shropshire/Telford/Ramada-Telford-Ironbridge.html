Description

Ramada Telford Ironbridge is set in landscaped grounds and features a stylish restaurant and free parking.

The hotel is located in Telford centre, only a 2-minute drive from the M54 and just 15 minutes’ walk from Telford Station.

Bedrooms have an en-suite bathroom, a TV, WiFi, tea/coffee facilities, an iron and ironing board, as well as a safe.

There is a gym, indoor swimming pool accessed by steps, and a game room. Ramada Restaurant has a varied menu with the emphasis on "Home Cooked Food”.

Full English breakfast is served daily, and guests can enjoy fresh coffees and light snacks in the lounge bar.

The Hotel is a 5-minute walk from Telford Shopping Centre and Southwater Entertainment Quarter with IMAX Cinema, ice rink and ten pin bowling.

Nearby is the wonderful Telford Town Park which offers acres of woodland walks and free fun in the many play areas, alongside high ropes, zip wire and mini golf activities.

Ironbridge Gorge with its interactive museums is just 15-minutes’ drive away. Discounted tickets for Shropshire tourist attractions are available from the Hotel Reception.
