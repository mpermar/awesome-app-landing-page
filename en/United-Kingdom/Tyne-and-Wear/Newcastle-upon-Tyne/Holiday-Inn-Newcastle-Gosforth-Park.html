Description

Set in 18 acres of grounds, Holiday Inn Newcastle Gosforth Park is next to the A19/A1 (North).

Only 6 miles from Newcastle centre, it is within easy reach of the beautiful Northumbrian coastline.

Free WiFi is available throughout. At the hotel, the rooms have a desk, a flat-screen TV, tea/coffee making facilities and a hairdryer.

The popular Metro Centre and Eldon Square are close by, offering some of the best shopping facilities in Europe.

Inn Fitness & Spa boasts an indoor swimming pool, sauna, steam room and a spa bath.

There are 3 treatment rooms offering a range of beauty treatments. Business guests are within 15 minutes drive of Nelson Industrial Estate, Apex Business Village, Quorum Business Park and Cobalt Business Park.
