Description

Each room at L'Horizon Beach Hotel & Spa is modern and spacious. Guests can enjoy free mineral water, free Wi-Fi, and toiletries by Gilchrist & Soames. Some rooms also offer sea views.

There are 2 award-winning restaurants: The Grill features a seafood menu and The Crystal Room, which serves traditional dishes. Guests can also have a snack at the brasserie, dine outdoors on the beachfront terrace, or relax with a drink in the lounge.

The hotel features two spa treatment rooms and a hot tub with a view of St Brelade’s Bay. There is also a sauna and a steam room, along with a small gym.

The ferry from St. Malo only takes 45 minutes to reach Jersey. The hotel provides free parking on site.
