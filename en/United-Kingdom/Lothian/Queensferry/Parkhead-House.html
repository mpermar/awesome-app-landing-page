Description

Parkhead House is located in Queensferry, and set in a former gamekeeper cottage, on the edge of the idyllic Deer Park in the heart of Hopetoun Estate.

Free WiFi access is available. The rooms at Parkhead House feature an en suite bathroom, a Freeview TV and tea/coffee making facilities.

All rooms offer wonderful views over wooded parkland northwards to the river Forth and Fife.

Bathrobes and free toiletries are also included. A hearty Scottish breakfast is served every morning, and prepared using the best local organic produce.

Other facilities offered include grocery deliveries, a shared lounge and luggage storage.

The property offers free parking. The bed and breakfast is 400 m from Hopetoun House.
