Description

Located in Moravske Toplice, a 2-minute drive from the Livada Golf Course, Apartments Brunarica offers self-catering accommodation units with free WiFi, air conditioning and access to a garden with barbecue facilities.

Private parking spaces are available on site free of charge. The apartments at the Brunarica all come with a terrace, a well-equipped kitchen and cable TV.

In addition, they feature a living area with a sofa and a bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer.

Several cycling tracks start directly from the premises and horse riding is also possible nearby.
