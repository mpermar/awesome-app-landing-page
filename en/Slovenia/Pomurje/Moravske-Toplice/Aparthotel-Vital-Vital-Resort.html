Description

Located 400 metres from Terme 3000, Aparthotel Vital - Vital Resort offers air-conditioned rooms and apartments with flat-screen cable TVs and free WiFi.

All units come with a private bathroom with shower. The apartments also have a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling and hiking.

Guests can use a shared lounge with a terrace. Guests staying at Apartments & Rooms Vital - Vital Resort can buy tickets for the Terme 3000 spa at a discount.

All the meals are served in the hotel nearby the property. There is a Mercator market 200 metres away and a golf course approximately 300 metres from Apartments & Rooms Vital - Vital Resort.

Horseback riding is possible on farms at Rakican and Tisina, both about 9 km away.
