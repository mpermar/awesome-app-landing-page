Description

Peacefully located in the village of Polana, this modern country hotel offers free Wi-Fi and a spa centre with pool.

Some rooms have a balcony or terrace. Your air-conditioned room at Hotel Strk also includes a minibar and a TV.

Guests enjoy a private bathroom with a shower and slippers. Slovenian and international meals are served for lunch and dinner in the rustic cellar restaurant.

Breakfast is provided, and packed lunches can be prepared for you on request. For relaxation, Strk offers a hot tub, sauna and massages.

Active guests will enjoy the gym, or can use the hotel’s rental bicycles. Strk is surrounded by countryside and farmhouses.
