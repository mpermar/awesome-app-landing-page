Description

Surrounded by the green forests and mountains of the Upper Savinja Valley, the 4-star Apartments & Wellness Skok is located 1 km from the centre of Mozirje.

Free Wi-Fi and free private parking are provided. Skok offers modern and spacious air-conditioned apartments fitted with designer furniture.

They are decorated with splashes of vibrant colours and feature a fully equipped kitchen or kitchenette and a balcony or terrace.

Shopping and dining options, as well as the bus station in the centre of Mozirje, can easily be reached within a 2-minute drive.
