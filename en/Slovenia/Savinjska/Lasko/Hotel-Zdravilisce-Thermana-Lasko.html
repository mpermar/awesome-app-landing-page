Description

Situated in a peaceful park, this spa hotel is close to Savinja River, and a 15-minute walk to the center of Lasko.

It provides a spa centre and free LAN access. The air-conditioned rooms are fitted with cable TV and a minibar.

The bathroom includes a shower, hairdryer and a robe. The restaurant serves healthy dishes with international influences.

In the summertime you can enjoy a refreshing drink on the sun terrace next to the outside thermal pool.

The extensive wellness facilities include swimming pools, fitness classes and beauty treatments.
