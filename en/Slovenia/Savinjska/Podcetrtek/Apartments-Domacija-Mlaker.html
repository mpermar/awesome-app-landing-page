Description

Set in quiet countryside surroundings in Podčetrtek and only 1. 5 km away from the Terme Olimia Spa Centre, Apartments Domačija Mlaker provides accommodation with free WiFi access.

A terrace and free-to-use barbecue equipment is at the guests' disposal. Apartments here come with a seating area and a TV.

Each provides a fully equipped kitchen or kitchenette with a dining area and a fridge.

The private bathrooms are fitted with a shower. A mountain view and a garden view can be enjoyed from all rooms.

Domačija Mlaker Apartments comprises a garden. A variety of activities can be enjoyed in the surrounding countryside, such as hiking or cycling.

The Podčetrtek Golf Course is 4. 5 km away from the property. Bike rental is available.

A local train station is within a 900-metre distance from the apartments. Free private parking is provided on site.
