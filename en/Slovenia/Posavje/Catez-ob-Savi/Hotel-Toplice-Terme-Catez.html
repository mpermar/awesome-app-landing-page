Description

Located within the Čatež Thermal Spa complex and connected to the Winter and Summer Thermal Riviera via a covered enclosed pathway, Hotel Toplice offers a restaurant, a bar and a sports centre.

Free WiFi access is provided in all areas. All rooms come with satellite TV, a safe and a sofa.

Private bathrooms feature a bath or a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries. Most rooms in the hotel are air-conditioned and also feature a balcony.

The Čatež Thermal Riviera covers more than 10. 000 sq metres of water surfaces including indoor and outdoor swimming pools that can be used all year long (Summer and Winter Riviera).

The Thermal Spa sauna park comprises 8 distinct sauna zones that can be accessed at an additional surcharge.

Guests can book massages and beauty treatments, as well as enjoy an additional sauna offer at the on-site spa and wellness centre.

Activities such as tennis, billiards, bowling and mini golf can be played on the premises.

The Terme Čatež Spa complex also comprises a shopping arcade, a cake shop, a casino and a varied gastronomic offer in different casual and formal restaurants.

The nearest airport is Zagreb Airport, 45 km away. Ljubljana is at a distance of 107 km from Hotel Toplice.
