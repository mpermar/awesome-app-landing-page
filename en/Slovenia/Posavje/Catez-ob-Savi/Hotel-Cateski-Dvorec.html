Description

Set in Dvorce, only 6 km away from the Croatian border and 150 metres from the A2 motorway, Hotel Cateski dvorec offers elegantly furnished rooms with free wired internet and a spacious, covered terrace.

This 4-star hotel features spacious air-conditioned accommodations with a balcony, flat-screen satellite TV and a seating area.

Bathrobes and slippers are also provided. The Cateski dvorec offers a restaurant with a tastefully decorated dining room.

Regional cuisine is served there. A wine cellar is also available on site. A sauna and a beautiful garden with a children’s playground can be used.

Bicycles are also available for rent at the Cateski dvorec. Guests can explore the historic town centre of Brezice with its 16th century castle and Brezice Water Tower within a short walk.

Catez Spa can be reached within a 10-minute drive. Free private parking is offered on site.

The hotel is 150 metres away from the exit of the A2 motorway from Ljubljana to Zagreb and a few minutes' drive from the historic town of Brezice.
