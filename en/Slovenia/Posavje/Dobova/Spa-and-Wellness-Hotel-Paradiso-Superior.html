Description

Conveniently located near the A2 motorway, Spa & Wellness Hotel Paradiso is situated at the edge of the Posavje region.

It offers free Wi-Fi, a breakfast buffet and free parking. These spacious and air-conditioned rooms have a balcony with views of the surrounding park.

Each room has a king-size bed, bathroom and cable TV. Some also feature a private sauna or a spa bath.

There is also a small spa section offering 2 swimming pools, a fitness centre and 8 saunas.

The swimming pools are free of charge, while the saunas can be used at an additional charge.

The hotel's surroundings are suitable for hiking and fishing. An 18-hole golf course can be reached within a few kilometres.

The Medieval Otocec Castle, a 30-minute car ride away, dates back to 1252 and is situated in the picturesque lush region of Dolenjska, famous for its rivers, valleys, forests and vineyards.

The Dobova train station is right opposite the Hotel Paradiso. The Croatian capital Zagreb can be reached within 25 km.
