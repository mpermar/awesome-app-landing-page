Description

Tourist Farm Hudičevec is set on the south slopes of Mount Nanos, within 1 km from Razdrto Motorway Exit and 2 km from the village centre.

Featuring free Wi-Fi and accommodation with a flat-screen satellite TV, it offers a restaurant with local cuisine, a sauna and various sports facilities.

All rooms and apartments comprise hardwood floors, wooden furniture and a private bathroom with a shower or a bathtub.

In addition to this, apartments have a kitchen with a dining area. A gym, boccie court and an outdoor volleyball court are located within the property.

Guests can also play table tennis or relax on the playground or in the company of farm animals.

A grocery shop and a café can be found 3 km away. Postojna, a town with popular Postojna Cave, is 10 km away.

The Italian town of Trieste is at a distance of 35 km. Cerkno Ski Centre is 50 km away.

Ljubljana, the capital of Slovenia, can be reached within 60 km. A local bus stop is at a distance of 1 km, while Postojna Train Station is 10 km away.

Ljubljana and Trieste International Airports are both within an 80-km radius from the Hudičevec Tourist Farm.
