Description

The luxuriously decorated Hotel Kras in Postojna, close to the world-famous Postojna Cave, offers well-equipped, elegant accommodation with magnificent views of the Nanos Plateau and the town.

All air-conditioned rooms and apartments have panoramic windows that offer views of the city and the surrounding mountains.

There are many convenient services offered by the Hotel Kras. The garage is under video surveillance.

Sip your coffee in the coffee room and enjoy your rich breakfast with homemade bread and cakes from the hotel's own bakery.
