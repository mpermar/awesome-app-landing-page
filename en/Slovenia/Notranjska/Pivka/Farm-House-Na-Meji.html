Description

Enjoying a peaceful position on the edge of a forest, Farm House Na Meji offers an elegant dining room, while accommodation units benefit from free Wi-Fi and TV.

Guests can use the shared fridge or sample homemade brandy for free. Front desk can arrange bike rental and organise bear watching in Javornik Forest, 1 km away.

Located in the small village of Trnje, Na Meji Farm House is 2 km from Pivka, where you can find restaurants, grocery shops, a military museum and a bus stop with links to Ljubljana.

The famous Postojna Cave is at a distance of 12 km from Na Meji. The impressive Škocjan Caves and Predjama Castle are within 20 km, while Lipica Stud Farm is 25 km away.
