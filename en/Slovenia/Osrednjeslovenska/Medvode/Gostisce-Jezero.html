Description

Set in the peaceful surroundings of Lake Zbiljsko, just 12 km away from the centre of Ljubljana, Guesthouse Jezero offers beautiful lake views, a restaurant and comfortable rooms.

Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the property. The rooms at Jezero offer hardwood floors, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a work desk.

Many of them offer beautiful views across the lake. Jezero’s restaurant set directly by the lake.

It specialises in seafood dishes and traditional Slovenian cuisine. Numerous recreational possibilities including fishing are available around the Lake and can be reached within few steps.

Cycling and hiking trails lead through the forests and sleepy villages in the surroundings.

Smlednik Golf Course is 4. 5 km away. Ljubljana Airport is 12 km away. Free on-site parking is provided.
