Description

Surrounded by unspoiled nature, Hotel Rakitna is set next to Rakitna lake. The property offers a sauna and a fitness.

An on-site restaurant serves Slovenian and Mediterranean dishes. Wi-Fi and an LCD cable TV are provided in all units.

Some rooms feature a private balcony overlooking the Rakitna Lake. The apartments come with a fully equipped kitchenette with a dining area.

A sandy volleyball court is available. Hiking and cycling trails can be found near the Rakitna.

Picnic excursions can be organised upon request. Ljubljana can be reached within 25 km.
