Description

Hotel Krona Domzale is located in the city Domzale, 16 km from the centre of Ljubljana.

It offers air-conditioned rooms with a balcony, free access to Wi-Fi and a flat-screen satellite TV.

A bar and a restaurant with a terrace are on site. Slovenian dishes are served there.

Guests can either enjoy breakfast in the restaurant or in the privacy of their own room.

Hotel Krona provides laundry facilities and a ski storage room. Free private parking is also offered at the hotel.

Tennis courts and a football pitch can be reached within 500 metres. Golf Arboretum Domzale golf course is 5.
