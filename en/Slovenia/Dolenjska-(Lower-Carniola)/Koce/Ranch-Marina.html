Description

The rooms in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Certain rooms will provide you with a kitchen with a dishwasher. At Ranch Marina all rooms come with a private bathroom with a shower.

A continental or buffet breakfast is available each morning at the property.

A range of activities are offered in the area, such as cycling and hiking.

Opatija is 30 miles from the accommodation, while Dolenjske Toplice is 17 miles from the property. Brnik Airport is 47 miles away.
