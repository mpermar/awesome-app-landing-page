Description

At the hotel, the rooms come with a desk. Every room includes a closet and a flat-screen TV, and some rooms at Hotel Tisa Pohorje have a balcony. At the accommodation, rooms come with a private bathroom with a hair dryer.

The daily breakfast offers continental, buffet or vegetarian options.

Hotel Tisa Pohorje has a playground. Guests can enjoy a selection of activities in the surrounding areas, including skiing, cycling and hiking.

The nearest airport is Brnik Airport, 56 miles from the hotel.
