Description

Surrounded by a large green surface with wooden benches, the bright Panorama Rooms come with free WiFi, LCD satellite TV and a terrace.

The garden features naturally grown tomatoes, salad, cucumber and gourde, which are often served for breakfast.

Guests can make use of the shared barbecue and a common kitchen with dishwasher, microwave and coffee machine.

Free private parking is provided. A number of archaeological sites from the Roman peiriod can be found in the area.

Part of the nearby woods is a bird reserve. A hiking route starts from the property and leads to the hill peak.

You can rent bikes on site and reach the long Drava - Mura cycling route just 500 metres from the Panorama.
