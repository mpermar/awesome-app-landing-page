Description

Amidst the vineyards in the small village of Gorišnica, Hotel Botra is located 10 km from the Croatian border.

Featuring a restaurant and a spa area, it offers rooms with free Wi-Fi, free parking and a breakfast buffet.

All rooms of the Botra provide cable TV and a bathroom with hairdryer. Some have a balcony.

The on-site restaurant serves slow food meals as well as traditional Slovenian cuisine.

It features a summer terrace and a children's corner. Hotel Botra's fitness and spa area spreads over 650 m² and comprises saunas, a solarium and a wide range of training devices.

Tennis courts can be reached within 300 metres. The nearest grocery store is 1 km away.

Ptuj, the oldest city in Slovenia, is only 10 km away from Hotel Botra. The town of Maribor is at a distance of 38 km.
