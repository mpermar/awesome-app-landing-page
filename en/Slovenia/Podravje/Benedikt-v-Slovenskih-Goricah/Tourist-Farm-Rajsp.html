Description

Situated in a quiet area, near the district of Benedikt, Tourist Farm Rajšp offers a spacious garden with a barbecue and fresh vegetables, a private sauna, and bicycle rental.

There is a children’s playground on site, while the apartment terraces overlook the garden.

All apartments feature a seating area with a sofa, a fully equipped kitchen and a private bathroom with a shower.

Free parking is provided, while a shared seating area offers a place where guests can socialise and enjoy their meals.

A bar and restaurant can be found in the centre, 2. 5 km away. There is also a night club 15 km from the property.

The town of Lenart, 7 km away, features a shopping street. Local buses stop 2. 5 km away, while the intercity bus station can be found in Maribor, 30 km away.
