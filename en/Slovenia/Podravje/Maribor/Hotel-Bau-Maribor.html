Description

The family-run Hotel Bau is situated on the south-western area of Maribor boasting wonderful views over Pohorje.

It offers comfortable rooms with light furniture and free WiFi access. The hotel is only a 5-minute drive with car from the Pohorje ski slopes and the city centre.

In front of the property there is a bus stop and nearby there are also a drug store, a cinema centre and the Tabor sports hall.

For a good start of the day, a rich buffet breakfast is served in the hotel's restaurant.

Guests can unwind in the wellness area at additional charges. Wellness procedures include Finnish, Turkish and infra red saunas, a massage pool, Kneipp baths and heated relax chairs.
