Description

Just 4. 5 km from the centre of Maribor, the family-run Motel Lesnik offers a spa area and a miniature golf course.

Free WiFi and wired internet is available in all rooms. The spa area is available against an extra charge and includes a sauna, a steam bath, and a hot tub.

A 20% discount on spa treatments is available to guests. Rooms at Motel pri Lesniku provide a TV, a safe, and a bathroom.

The restaurant includes a winter garden and a terrace. It serves Slovenian and international cuisine, as well as a daily breakfast buffet.

A children’s playground and free private parking are available on site. The Maribor East motorway exit is just 1 km away, and the Pohorje ski area is a 10-minute drive away.
