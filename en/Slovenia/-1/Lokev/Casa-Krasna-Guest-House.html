Description

All rooms at the guesthouse come with a seating area. Featuring a private bathroom with a bath or shower and free toiletries, rooms at Casa Krasna Guest House also provide guests with free WiFi. The rooms include a desk.

A continental breakfast is served at the property. There's an in-house restaurant, which serves European cuisine.

Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Lokev, like cycling.

Trieste is 8 miles from Casa Krasna Guest House.
