Description

Featuring a spa and wellness centre with swimming pools and saunas and offering magnificent views of the surrounding mountains and forests, Špik Alpine Wellness Hotel is located in Gozd Martuljek, 5 km from Kranjska Gora.

Free WiFi access is provided in all areas. All modernly furnished rooms are air-conditioned and feature a balcony, a flat-screen cable TV, as well as a minibar and a safe.

Private bathrooms come with a bathtub or a shower and a hairdryer. Guests staying at Špik Alpine Wellness Hotel can relax in the Water World that comprises 3 indoor pools for free or enjoy the World of Saunas with various saunas, a whirlpool and a relaxation area at a surcharge.

Various massages and beauty treatments can be enjoyed at a surcharge. Špik Alpine Wellness Hotel also features the Alpine themed rooms with a unique Alpine wellness concept that can be booked upon request in advance.

The hotel restaurant offers modern interpretations of traditional Slovene cuisine with locally sourced ingredients, while the Aperitif bar provides a selection of desserts and hot beverages.

Activities that can be enjoyed in the vicinity include skiing, nature walks, hiking or cycling.

Kranjska Gora Ski Centre is 5. 6 km away, while the world famous Planica Nordic Centre is 12 km from the hotel.

Ljubljana can be reached in 80 km, while Ljubljana Airport is at a distance of 64 km.
