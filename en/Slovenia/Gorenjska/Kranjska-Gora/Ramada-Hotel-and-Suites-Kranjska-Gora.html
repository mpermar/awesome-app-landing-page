Description

Ramada Hotel & Suites Kranjska Gora is approximately 100 metres from the ski slopes right in the centre of Kranjska Gora next to the main promenade.

The comfortably furnished rooms offer mountain views and free internet access. The Napoli pizzeria, serves wood-fired pizzas.

At the London tearoom you can taste fresh, homemade sweets. Ramada Hotel & Suites Kranjska Gora provides transfer from the Klagenfurt and Ljubljana (Joze Pucnik) Airports against extra charge.
