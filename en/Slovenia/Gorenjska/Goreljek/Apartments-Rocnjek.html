Description

Enjoying a quiet countryside location between lakes Bohinj and Bled, Apartments Ročnjek features traditionally designed interiors and a relaxation area with wellness facilities.

Free wired internet is available in all accommodation units, which have wooden furnishings and private bathrooms with shower.

Parking is free of charge. The on-site restaurant offers traditional homemade specialties and serves breakfast upon request.

Finnish and infrared saunas, as well as a Turkish bath and massage treatments, can be arranged at an extra charge.

Guests can also relax on the common terrace and use the barbecue. The nearest grocery store is a 15-minute drive away.

Pokljuka Ski Centre is at a distance of 10 km from the Ročnjek, and Vogel Ski Area is 20 km away.
