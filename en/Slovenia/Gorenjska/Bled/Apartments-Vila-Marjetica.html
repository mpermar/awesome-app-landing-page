Description

Apartments Vila Marjetica lies in the foothills of the Julian Alps, 5 minutes’ walk from the shores of the Lake Bled.

Apartments are furnished with a blend of traditional and modern décor. Apartments are fitted with a balcony or terrace with views of the garden.

All come with a fully equipped and modern kitchen, dining area and a naturally lit seating area with cable TV.

Private bathrooms are decorated in different colours and come with a shower. Free of charge, guests have access to private parking and Wi-Fi available throughout Apartments Vila Marjetica.

Guests can unwind on the terrace or out in the garden. The nearest grocery store and bus station is 100 metres away.

The famous Bled Castle is located on Lake Bled and the other attraction, Triglav National Park, can be reached by car in 10 minutes.
