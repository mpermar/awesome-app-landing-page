Description

Garden Village Bled is entirely ecologically designed and offers a restaurant with a river stream flowing through it, a beach bar under trees and pools with a natural massage waterfall.

Its unique accommodation consist of tree houses, tents with a wooden hot tub and deluxe apartments.

It is located 300 metres from Bled Lake and provides free Wi-Fi throughout the resort.

In a special pool with refreshing spring water you can enjoy kneipp therapy and reflexive foot massages.

Finnish sauna is set between the trees over the stream. You can socialize at a common lounge room with open fireplace.

A variety of sports activities can be enjoyed in the area, such as kayaking and rafting on nearby rivers or hiking on the National Park Triglav, 6 km away.
