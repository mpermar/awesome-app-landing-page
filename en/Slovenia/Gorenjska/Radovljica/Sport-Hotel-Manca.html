Description

Located in the heart of the region Gorenjska in the small and quiet town Radovljica 6 km from Bled, the family-run Sport Hotel Manca offers comfortable accommodation and features a seasonal outdoor pool.

Radovljica is a climatic alpine resort on the sunny side of the Alps with especially healthy dry air, which is recommended by doctors.

Sports and activities available here and in the surrounding area include skating, skiing, horse riding, parachuting, rafting and golf.

Guests can also use a wellness facilities such as sauna and water massage available at a surcharge.
