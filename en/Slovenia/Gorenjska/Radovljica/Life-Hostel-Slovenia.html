Description

Life Hostel Slovenia is situated in Radovljica, 41 km from Ljubljana. There is a restaurant on site and guests can enjoy the on-site bar.

Some rooms feature views of the mountain or a city park. A flat-screen TV and air conditioning are available in all rooms.

Each room offers a bathroom. You will find a hairdresser's at the property, bike and car hire.

You can play tennis at this hostel, and the area is popular for golfing. Numerous activities can be enjoyed in the vicinity like canyoning, rafting, paragliding and hiking.

Villach is 39 km from Life Hostel Slovenia, while Bled is 5 km from the property.

The closest airport is Ljubljana Joze Pucnik Airport, 25 km from Life Hostel Slovenia.
