Description

The Hotel Center Bohinjsko Jezero, nestled in peaceful surroundings, yet in the very tourist centre of Ribčev Laz, only 100 metres from the lake, is a perfect base to venture out and go mountain-biking and hiking.

Hotel Center offers newly equipped, bright and spacious rooms with balconies, and the friendly and hospitable staff makes sure that all your wishes are catered for.

A pleasant ambience and small number of rooms will make your stay enjoyable and relaxing.

In the Hotel Center restaurant you can taste specialities and dishes à la carte, varying daily menus, special beverages and pizza baked in the bread oven.

Different social occasions like celebrations, meetings with friends and business lunches can be organised in the restaurant, and to entertain the little ones, a playground near the terrace is provided.
