Description

The units have a fully equipped kitchen with dining area, a microwave and a fridge. A stovetop and coffee machine are also offered.

Apartment Pri Boštjanovcu has an outdoor pool.

The property has a garden and sun terrace, which guests can enjoy if the weather permits.

Ljubljana is 19 miles from Apartment Pri Boštjanovcu, while Bled is 18 miles away. The nearest airport is Brnik Airport, 18 miles from the property.
