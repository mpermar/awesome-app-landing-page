Description

All of the Rodos Palladium’s spacious guestrooms offer modern accommodations and panoramic sea views.

A well-appointed spa and wellness center offers massages and skin care. For guests who want a good work out, there is an fitness center with an expert staff and many outdoor sport facilities. The property's spa features Thalgo® products and treatments.

Guests can enjoy a cocktail by the impressive island-inspired outdoor pool or head inside for a private hot tub session. The Rodos Palladium staff offers cooking classes and aqua aerobic.

Classic Mediterranean cuisine with a contemporary style is served at the hotel’s 3 restaurants. An American-style breakfast buffet is served daily, while the hotel also offers a dedicated section with local traditional products.

The hotel offers a supervised kids’ club with daily activities.

The Rodos Palladium is just 9.3 mi from the Rhodes International Airport.
