Description

Rooms are bright and spacious, with air conditioning and private terraces or balconies that open to garden and pool or sea views. Standard room amenities include a mini fridge and satellite TV.

American buffet breakfast, and buffet-style lunch and dinner featuring 2 show cooking stations, are served at the main restaurant. Six theme restaurants serve a la carte dishes for lunch and dinner (Greek, Italian, Mexican, Asian, Burgers, Souvlaki), upon prior reservation. The hotel offers drinks, snacks and meals throughout the day at no extra charge.

A water sports center and a tennis and basketball court are featured on site. The various activities offered at the Mitsis Rhodes Village Beach Hotel include canoes, windsurfing, beach football, beach volleyball, archery and boccia. The amphitheater bar is open daily for the evening's animation program.

Mitsis Rhodos Village Beach Hotel & Spa is 37 mi from Rhodes Town. Rhodes International Airport is 34 mi away. Bus connections to Lindos and Rhodes Town are available 4 times daily, and stop in front of the hotel.
