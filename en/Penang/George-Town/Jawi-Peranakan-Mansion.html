Description

Boasting Mughal inspired architecture, each room is fitted with a king bed and a cozy seating area. It is decorated with ethnic upholstery and wooden window arches. The private bathroom includes a shower and free toiletries. Selected units have a 4-poster bed and a top bath.

Buffet breakfast is served at the property and you can also grab a meal at the restaurants nearby.

Staff at the reception are happy to assist you with laundry service and airport pick-up at a surcharge. You can also ask about things to do and store luggage.

Jawi Peranakan Mansion is 0.9 mi from Little India and 1,950 feet from China Town. Penang International Airport is 12 mi away.
