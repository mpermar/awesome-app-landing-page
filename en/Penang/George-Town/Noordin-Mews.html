Description

Noordin Mews is a 5-minute walk from Komtar and 1st Avenue. Heritage Area is a 10-minute walk from the hotel while Gurney Drive is a 15-minute drive away.

Rooms and houses come with both air conditioning and a fan. A flat-screen cable TV, ironing essentials and free toiletries are provided. Select rooms have an attached living area.

Guests can head to the library for a quiet read, or opt for a relaxing massage to unwind. Ticket services and car rental are available. Luggage storage is provided at the 24-hour front desk.

This hotel has a bar. Local Penang food is available a 5-minute walk from the hotel.
