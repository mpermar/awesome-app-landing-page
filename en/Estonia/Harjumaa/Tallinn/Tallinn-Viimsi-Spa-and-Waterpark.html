Description

Located on the scenic Viimsi Peninsula, a 15-minute drive from Tallinn city centre, Tallinn Viimsi Spa offers a Water Park, wide range of health and beauty treatments, as well as sports activities.

Free access to saunas, gym, pool complex and children's centre is provided. The Tallinn Viimsi Spa is a highly professional health care centre offering a selection of health and wellness services, as well as relaxing beauty treatments.

There is a regular programme of events and activities like morning gymnastics, stick walking and balance exercises.

Tallinn Viimsi Spa offers free wireless internet access in public areas, as well as free parking.
