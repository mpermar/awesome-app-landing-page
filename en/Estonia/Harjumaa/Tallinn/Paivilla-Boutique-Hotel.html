Description

All rooms in the bed and breakfast are equipped with a TV.

An American breakfast is available every morning at the property.

Paivilla Boutique Hotel has a playground.

The reception can provide advice on the area in order to help guests plan their day.

Toompea Castle is 2.4 miles from the accommodation, while Maiden Tower is 2.4 miles away. The nearest airport is Lennart Meri Tallinn Airport, 2.5 miles from the property.

Kristiine is a great choice for travelers interested in culture, atmosphere and city walks.
