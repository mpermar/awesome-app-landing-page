Description

Located in Tallinn’s Old Town, which belongs to the UNESCO World Heritage List, Kalev Hotel offers free Wi-Fi throughout the property.

All rooms at the Kalev Spa Hotel & Waterpark feature unlimited access to water park and gym and rich buffet breakfast.

The Health & Beauty centre features manicure and pedicure services, a broad selection of treatments for face and body, also nurse consultations and services.

Kalev Spa has one of the largest indoor water parks in Estonia with unique 8 lane 50-metre pool, slides, children pools, relaxing massage-pool and saunas.

Sauna-Oasis service provides access to 5 different saunas, Jacuzzies and relaxing area as a child-free zone.

Fitness Centre has a wide selection of group workouts and a spacious and well-lit gym.

The new and spacious restaurant Allee invites our visitors with a delicious menu and terrace with a panoramic view of the Kanuti park.

The lobby-bar offers refreshing drinks and snacks for fast meetups. A modern meeting room for 50 persons is suitable for business meetings and group events.

The port and the train station of Tallinn are a 10-minute walk away. Bus station and airport about 15-30 minutes of public transport or taxi.
