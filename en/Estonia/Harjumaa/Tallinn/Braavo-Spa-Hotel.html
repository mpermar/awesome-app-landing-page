Description

Located in Tallinn’s Old Town, Braavo Spa Hotel offers rooms with cable TV and free WiFi.

It offers its guests free daily access to a sports club and spa centre from 6:00 until 22:00.

The Braavo’s rooms are painted in bright pastel colours. Each features a work desk, seating area and a private bathroom.

For guest’s convenience, a fan is included in every room. Continental breakfast is provided in the dining area with a kitchen.

Braavo Spa Hotel's sports club includes a very ample gym and the spa centre features 9 different pools, 2 of which are outdoor, a wide choice of saunas and a relaxation area.

There also is a massage parlour and squash court on-site. A large private parking is available.

Most of the most popular landmarks of Tallinn’s Old Town are within walking distance.

Raekoja Plats and the Town Hall are within 500 metres. Balti Jaam Railway Station is 850 metres away.
