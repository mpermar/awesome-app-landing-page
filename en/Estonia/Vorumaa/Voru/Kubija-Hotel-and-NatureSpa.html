Description

Situated among the tranquil pine forests and lakes of Southern Estonia, the cosy and comfortable Hotel Kubija offers relaxing holidays and natural health treatments.

Guests can enjoy a wide range of classic and natural health and beauty treatments and wellness procedures, with a choice of 5 saunas and four spa baths available in the spa.

The restaurant serves traditional Estonian delicacies as well as international cuisine.

In summer you can dine on the restaurant’s open-air terrace and enjoy the homemade bread and exquisite chocolate truffles, which are the chef’s speciality.

Each room is comfortably equipped with many amenities, including free internet access and beautiful views of the forest or the lake.

In the forest near the hotel you will find both a walking track and an illuminated route for skiing or cycling.

Guests can play mini golf and then relax in the sauna. There is a special play room for children and a playground.

Staff will help to organise excursions and hikes through the beautiful natural landscape around Hotel Kubija.
