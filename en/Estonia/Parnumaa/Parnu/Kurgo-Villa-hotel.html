Description

Kurgo Villa Hotel & Restaurant, situated 400 metres from the beach in Pärnu, offers rooms and a suite with free Wi-Fi.

It features a sauna and an on-site café-restaurant. Each spacious and bright room comes with cable TV and a refrigerator.

The private bathrooms have heated flooring. Some rooms feature a balcony. The hotel's Piparmünt Cafe-Restaurant serves breakfast buffet.

After midday, the restaurant serves à la carte dishes and drinks till evening. Guests can make use of the bicycle rental service.

The hotel has free on-site parking and offers airport shuttle service upon request to Tallinn or Riga airports.

Museum of New Art is 10 minutes’ walk from the hotel and Ranna Park is less than a 15-minute walk away.
