Description

Rannahotell enjoys a great location on a sandy beach, surrounded by parks, only a 15-minute walk away from the centre of Pärnu.

The hotel is a true gem of functional style, and is considered to be one of the most beautiful hotel buildings in Estonia.

Relax in the spacious, modern restaurant, with daylight flooding in through the floor-to-ceiling windows, while enjoying the delicious local and international dishes of the 1930s cuisine.

A breakfast buffet is offered daily at the restaurant and at the bar you can drink a glass of wine and admire the superb views of the sea.

Rannahotell's beauty salon offers a variety of beauty treatments, such as massage, hair care, manicure and pedicure.

Pärnu’ business and entertainment areas are both just a few minutes’ walk away from Rannahotell.

The hotel is 11 km away from Pärnu Bay Golf Links, 12 km from Lottemaa and 13 km from White Beach Golf and the Auto24ring racing track.
