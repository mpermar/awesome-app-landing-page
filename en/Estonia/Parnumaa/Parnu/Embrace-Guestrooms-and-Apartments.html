Description

Located 1 km from the Pärnu Bay beach and the city centre, Embrace Guestrooms & Apartments offer accommodation with free Wi-Fi and satellite TV.

The rooms are decorated in classic style with parquet floors. Each comes with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

Apartments also feature fully-equipped kitchens. Guests at Embrace Guestrooms & Apartments are welcome to use the on-site library and barbecue facilities.

Access to a sauna is also possible at surcharge. Breakfast is served for guesthouse clients, but can also be provided for the apartments.
