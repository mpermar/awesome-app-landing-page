Description

Kate Sea Apartment offers accommodation in Pärnu, an 8-miunte walk from the sea. Free WiFi is featured throughout the property.

The kitchen has an oven and there is a private bathroom. A flat-screen TV with cable channels is available.

Pärnu City Center is 2. 9 km from Kate Sea Apartment, while Endla Theatre is 3. 1 km from the property.

A 12-minute walk will take you Kaubamajakas Shopping Centre and Tervise Paradiis Water Park is within a 20-minute walk, via a fitness trail.
