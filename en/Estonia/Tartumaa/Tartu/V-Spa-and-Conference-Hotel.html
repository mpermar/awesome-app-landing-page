Description

V Spa & Conference Hotel offers accommodation in Tartu. The hotel has a spa centre and hot spring bath, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

Free WiFi is featured. Rooms come with a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Some rooms feature a seating area for your convenience.

You will find a coffee machine in the room. All rooms are fitted with a private bathroom.

For your comfort, you will find bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property, as well as a SPA centre where guests can relax.

V Spa & Conference Hotel is located in the centre of city and there are various restaurants, bars and shops in the vicinity.
