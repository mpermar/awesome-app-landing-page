Description

Located 400 metres from the Baltic Sea, in the village Haeska, this hotel is located in a simple 2-storey building from the beginning of the 18th century.

It offers rooms with free Wi-Fi. The rooms of the Heaska manor are simply furnished, done in bright pastel colours.

Each has a private bathroom with a shower and is heated. In the morning breakfast is served in the breakfast room.

Lunch can be also ordered. Free private parking is also available. An electric stove may be provided upon request and kitchenware is available for guests use at any time.

The hotel also has a banquet hall and a dining room. Haeska Manor is within the Matsalu National Park.

The Gulf of Matsalu is located 1 km away and Haapsalu in Western Estonia is about 21 km away.
