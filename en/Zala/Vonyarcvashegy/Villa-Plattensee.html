Description

Plattensee has garden surroundings with a large terrace area including lounge chairs and sun umbrellas. The unique bar at the hotel is raised above the pool on stilts and has views of the resort.

There are many recreational activities around the hotel, and the lake, including fishing, hiking and horseback riding. Guests can also rent bicycles right at Villa Plattensee.

All of the air conditioned rooms include satellite TV, a refrigerator and a work desk.

Keszthely is a 10-minute drive from the hotel and guests enjoy a 10% discount at a partner optical store.

Villa Plattensee is only a 15 minute walk from Vonyarcvashegy Station.
