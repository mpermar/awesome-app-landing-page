Description

All rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Country Spa Retreat has certain units that include pool views, and each room comes with a private bathroom with a shower. All guest rooms have a seating area.

The accommodation offers a continental or buffet breakfast. There's an in-house restaurant, which specializes in European cuisine and also offers dairy-free, vegetarian and vegan options.

You can play ping pong at Country Spa Retreat.

Bucharest is 17 miles from the hotel, while Otopeni is 9 miles away. The nearest airport is Bucharest Henri Coandă International Airport, 7 miles from the property.
