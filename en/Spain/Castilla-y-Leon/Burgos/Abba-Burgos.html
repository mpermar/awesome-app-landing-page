Description

One of the bestsellers in Burgos!

Abba Burgos is located 300 meters from Burgos Cathedral, and offers a heated indoor pool, sauna, gym and paddle tennis court, all free of charge. There is also free WiFi in all areas.

Abba Burgos is located on the road to Santiago, next to the Castillo park and in front of the Atapuerca convention center. Burgos Castle is a 5-minute walk away. The hotel offers splendid views of the city.

The rooms are very bright and feature air conditioning, satellite TV, safe, minibar, desk, trouser press and private bathroom with hairdryer and toiletries.

The Abba restaurant serves seasonal dishes made with fresh produce, and includes a garden terrace overlooking the city. There is also a café bar and room service is provided.

The reception is open 24 hours a day and laundry, ironing and dry cleaning services are offered. Parking comes at an extra cost.
