Description

The fantastic facilities of the Abbey of the Templars include an indoor pool, an outdoor pool with hammocks and a bar. There are also tennis courts, where you can enjoy the fresh country air.

The Templar also has a sauna, Turkish baths, gym, massage service and a large restaurant.
