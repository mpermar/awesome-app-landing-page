Description

All rooms overlook the garden and the mountains and have a TV, a terrace and a private bathroom with a bath or shower and free toiletries. They also include an outdoor seating area and bed linen.

The Sabinares del Arlanza Bioclimatic Rural Hotel has a garden, terrace, meeting facilities, common room and games room. In the hotel and in the surroundings you can enjoy a wide variety of activities, such as hiking. Free parking is available at the hotel.
