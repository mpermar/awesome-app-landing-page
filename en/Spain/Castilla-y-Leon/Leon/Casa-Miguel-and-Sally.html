Description

The rooms have a TV and access to a shared kitchen with dishwasher and oven. They also include a private or shared bathroom, towels and bed linen. At Casa Miguel & Sally there is a barbecue area.

The property is 3 km from the A-321 motorway, which connects Barcelona and Galicia, and 10 km from the A-6, which connects Madrid and Asturias.

Mansilla de las Mulas is 7 km away and Valencia de Don Juan is 21 km away. Casa Miguel & Sally is 16 km from Valdevimbre, 64 km from Astorga and 55 km from La Bañeza.
