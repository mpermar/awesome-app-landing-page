Description

The house has 3 bedrooms, 1 bathroom with a hairdryer, a full kitchen with a dishwasher and a microwave, and a living area with a flat-screen TV and a sofa.

You can practice many activities, such as hiking, cycling and horse riding, on site or in the surrounding area.

La Gala offers free parking and is 58 km from Valladolid Airport and a 90-minute drive from Madrid.
