Description

The Parador de La Granja is a good starting point to explore the wonderful baroque-style palaces and gardens of La Granja de San Ildefonso. This establishment, located in the mountains of Guadarrama, 80 km north of Madrid, has been home to Spanish royalty since the 18th century.

The hotel has modern facilities that complement its historical surroundings. These include a rooftop pool, an indoor spa pool and modern-style sun loungers. The interiors, carefully restored, retain the original charm of the building and give it an elegant and modern touch.

The elegant restaurant, located in the main lobby, serves a modern version of typical Castilian dishes.
