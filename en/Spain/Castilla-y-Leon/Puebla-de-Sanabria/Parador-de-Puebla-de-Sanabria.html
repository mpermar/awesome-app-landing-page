Description

The rooms are cozy, comfortable and quiet and feature air conditioning, flat-screen TV, minibar and bathroom with bath or shower, hairdryer and free toiletries.

The pleasant interiors of the Parador de Puebla de Sanabria are carefully decorated to create a warm and cozy atmosphere with fine touches of luxury. The living room, with its attractive fireplace, is the perfect place to chat, read or enjoy the views of the fantastic historic city of Puebla de Sanabria.

In the restaurant, typical local dishes and regional wines are served in a relaxed atmosphere.

In addition, guests can visit the surroundings with an excursion through the countryside and discover beautiful places, as well as visit the Romanesque tower of the town of Mombuey and the monastery of San Martín de Castañeda, from the 16th century.
