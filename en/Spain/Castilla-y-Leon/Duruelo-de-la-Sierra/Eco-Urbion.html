Description

The rooms have a private terrace, wooden floors, a fireplace and a private bathroom. They include towels and bed linen.

The Eco Urbion has a large garden and its guests can participate in the tasks of the field and enjoy their products. There is a public pool less than 10 minutes away on foot.

Duruelo de la Sierra houses several bars, restaurants and supermarkets. The property is 8 km from Picos de Urbión, 15 km from Laguna Negra and 50 minutes' drive from Soria.
