Description

The rooms at the Novotel Valladolid feature air conditioning, an electric kettle and free tea / coffee making facilities. 2 children up to 15 years old can stay for free in each room, if they share it with their parents. The parents' breakfast also includes that of the children.

The Novotel Valladolid has an outdoor swimming pool, open from June 15 to September 15. There is also a children's play area and a garden. Free access to a nearby gym is provided. In addition, there is a 22 kw charging point for electric vehicles.

The cafeteria restaurant meals can be taken on the terrace. Buffet and continental breakfasts are prepared. The hotel's wine list includes a wide selection of wines from Rueda, Toro, Ribera de Duero and Cigales.

The property is 250 meters from the Valladolid Science Museum and a 5-minute drive from the Miguel Delibes Auditorium.
