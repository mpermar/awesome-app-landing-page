Description

The property has a pool with views of the surrounding landscape and sun loungers. Guests can also enjoy a massage or play pool.

The hotel also has a restaurant with large windows overlooking the gardens

Many activities can be practiced near the establishment. The game reserves of Arcos de Jalón and Utrilla are only 5 minutes away. The Morón de Almazán golf course is 30 minutes away by car. Guests can also fish in the Jalón River, which flows in front of the hotel.
