Description

Las Fontaninas is a traditional stone and wood building with modern facilities. It has colorful rooms with private bathroom with hydromassage shower, bathtub or hot tub.

The hotel has a bar and restaurant that serves traditional Bierzo cuisine and innovative dishes.

The Alto Bierzo mountains are ideal for hiking and paragliding. The hotel organizes these types of activities, in addition to horse riding.

The area surrounding Las Fontaninas is known for its healing spring, whose river runs alongside the hotel. Las Fontaninas hotel is a 10-minute drive from the A-6 motorway, which connects La Coruña with Segovia.
