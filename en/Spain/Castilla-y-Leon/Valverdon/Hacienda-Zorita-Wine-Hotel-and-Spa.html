Description

Hacienda Zorita features wooden beams, sturdy stone walls and cozy lounge areas with comfortable sofas and a fireplace. Various treatments can be received in the spa area.

The Hacienda has a gourmet restaurant, which serves dishes prepared mainly with products from its organic farm. The hotel offers guided tours of its wine production facilities.

The establishment organizes various activities during the summer season, such as open-air cinema, dinner in the vineyards, picnics and snacks.

Hacienda Zorita offers free parking and is just a 15-minute drive from the beautiful Salamanca.
