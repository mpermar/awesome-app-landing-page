Description

The rooms are modernly decorated and feature a flat-screen satellite TV, air conditioning, a minibar and a private bathroom with a hairdryer, free toiletries and towels.

The hotel is next to the Valladolid motorway, 400 meters from a restaurant and 300 meters from the bus stop.

The Resotel is located 5 minutes by car from Salamanca and 45 minutes by car from the natural reserve of the Riberas de Castronuño-Vega del Duero.

Conditions for children and extra beds
Children of all ages can stay.
Free! 1 child under 2 year (s) can stay for free using existing beds.
One child from 0 to 3 years is charged EUR 12 per person per night in a baby cot.
One child from 4 to 14 years is charged EUR 12 per person per night in an extra bed.
One adult can stay for EUR 25 per person per night in an extra bed.
Maximum number of extra beds / cots in the room: 1.
Extra beds and / or cots are available upon request and must be confirmed by the accommodation.
Supplements are not automatically calculated in the total amount and must be paid separately during the stay.
