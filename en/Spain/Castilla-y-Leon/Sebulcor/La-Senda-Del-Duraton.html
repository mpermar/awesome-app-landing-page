Description

The apartments at La Senda feature rustic décor and have a living room with a sofa, fireplace and flat-screen TV. In the kitchen there is a washing machine, hob and kitchen utensils and the bathroom has a bath or shower.

All apartments in the complex share a lounge area with a library, billiards and board games, as well as a barbecue area. Some typical dishes of the area are prepared on request.

There are good paths for excursions on foot or by bicycle in the nearby countryside. Lagunas de Cantalejo is less than a 30-minute drive from the apartment complex. The nearest airport is Madrid-Barajas, which is 126 km away.
