Description

The rooms at the Parador de Lorca are bright and have dim lighting and elegant décor. All rooms have panoramic views and include free Wi-Fi, a minibar, a safe, plasma TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom.

A buffet breakfast is served daily. The hotel restaurant prepares typical cuisine of the region, made with local products and accompanied by selected wines. The property also has a bar and offers room service.

Lorca Castle was listed as a Site of Cultural Interest in 1931 and includes 10th-century Islamic buildings, a synagogue and an old wall. In the establishment you can buy tickets for the archaeological site.

The Parador has free parking and is 35 minutes 'drive from Águilas and its beaches and 50 minutes' drive from Murcia.
