Description

The apartments have air conditioning, a living room with a sofa bed and a flat-screen satellite TV, a private bathroom and a well-equipped kitchenette.

The complex has a bar, a restaurant and several barbecue areas. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout.

The hotel has a games room with billiards and ping pong. The tour desk can organize diving excursions and other activities.
