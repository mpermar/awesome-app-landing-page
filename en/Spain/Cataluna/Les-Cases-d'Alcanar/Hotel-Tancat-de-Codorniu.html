Description

The farm, completely walled, has more than 2,500 orange trees in full production. In the tranquility of this garden, 200 meters from the beach, you can enjoy ample spaces for cycling, bathing in its pools, playing miniature golf on the farm itself and savoring the excellent cuisine of the area.

Enjoy this luxury accommodation and the spectacular surroundings of the Tancat de Codorniz estate.
