Description

All rooms have heating, wardrobe, private bathroom with shower and bed linen.

Access to a common area with microwave, coffee maker and refrigerator is provided. You can also use a barbecue area.

A wide variety of activities can be practiced in the accommodation as well as in its surroundings, such as cycling, horse riding and fishing.

The property offers free parking. Girona-Costa Brava Airport is 68 km away.
