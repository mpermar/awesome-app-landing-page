Description

All rooms include a private bathroom with a bath or shower. They also have pool towels, bath towels, toiletries and an ecological hair dryer. Hotel Mas Pelegri is a non-smoking hotel.

The hotel has a shared lounge and a common dining room. Serve a healthy breakfast without sugar. It also has private parking, bicycles and luggage storage, all free services.

Outdoor activities such as cycling, horse riding and mountain biking can be practiced nearby.

Hotel Mas Pelegri, located between Besalú and Bañolas, is 40 km from the beaches of the Costa Brava. The nearest airport is Girona-Costa Brava Airport, located 30 minutes drive from the accommodation.
