Description

One of the pools is on the roof and enjoys magnificent views of the Mediterranean. There is another large outdoor pool, a heated indoor pool and a children's pool.

The rooms are spacious and have air conditioning and a balcony overlooking the sea, the garden or the pool. All have private bathrooms and minibars.

The Tropic Park's buffet restaurant serves a selection of traditional and international cuisine and offers show cooking.

There is a free Wi-Fi zone. Children can have fun in the park and the kids club. There is also a miniature golf.

Guests can book parking at the property for a fee. The hotel has a 24-hour reception.
