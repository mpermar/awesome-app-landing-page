Description

The rooms have air conditioning, exposed stone walls and period details. The bed structure is oak and is handmade in Sweden. In addition, all rooms have free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and slippers.

The hotel is close to the Baix Empordà fields and the surrounding area is ideal for cycling and hiking. The property also offers bicycle rental.

Hotel Mas Carreras 1846 is about 30 minutes drive from the beaches of the Costa Brava and the town of Figueres, where you can visit the Salvador Dalí Museum.
