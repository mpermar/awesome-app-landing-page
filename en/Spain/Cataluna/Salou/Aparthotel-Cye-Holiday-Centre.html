Description

The Cye has a simple decoration and has tiled floors. All apartments have a TV and a small dining area.

The Holiday Center's restaurant offers Spanish cuisine. Facing the sea, 10 minutes walk from the apartments, you will find many bars and restaurants.

The PortAventura theme park is less than 4 km from Cye and Tarragona is a 20-minute drive away.
