Description

The rooms are spacious and feature heating, tiled floors, light wood furniture and a private bathroom. You can also rent safes.

The Sol D'Or's buffet restaurant serves Spanish and international cuisine. There is a bar and several vending machines for drinks and snacks. Packed lunches are also prepared upon request.

The hotel has a children's playground and offers day and night entertainment programs for children and adults.

The reception is open 24 hours a day and offers currency exchange and car rental services. On-site parking is available for a fee. The lively center of Salou is 2 km away. Near the hotel you will find bus stops.
