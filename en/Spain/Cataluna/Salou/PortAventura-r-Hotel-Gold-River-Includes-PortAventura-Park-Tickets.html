Description

The Gold River Hotel presents a traditional and authentic theme inspired by the American gold rush and guarantees an experience in the purest style of the Far West. In addition, the hotel offers direct access to the Far West area of the theme park.

Guests can enjoy access to the spa and wellness center at Hotel PortAventura and Hotel Caribe, for a fee. Some rooms include access to the spa. Spa facilities include a sauna, a hot tub, steam baths, a gym and a heated indoor pool.

The beds have period furniture and have patchwork bedspreads in bright colors. They also have satellite TV. The rooms are distributed among different thematic areas of the hotel, such as a Victorian mansion, a town hall and a typical Far West street.

The Gold River’s Grand Hall restaurant serves a varied buffet and the New Texas and Opera House restaurants offer à la carte specialties. There are also 2 bars and a cafeteria.

The PortAventura theme park is located on the outskirts of Salou and is very well connected with the AP7 motorway. The city of Barcelona is located north of the complex, 1 hour by train. Camp de Tarragona train station, where the AVE has service, offers bus connections to Reus and Barcelona airports.
