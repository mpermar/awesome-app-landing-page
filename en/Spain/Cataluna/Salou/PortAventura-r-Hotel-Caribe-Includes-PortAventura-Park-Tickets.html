Description

The Caribe Hotel offers a wide variety of children's activities. Access to the spa and wellness center at Hotel PortAventura® & Hotel Caribe comes at an additional cost. Access to the spa is free for some rooms. Services include sauna, hot tub, steam baths, gym and indoor heated pool. There is also a fitness center, a running track and a small soccer field. All rooms include free access to the PortAventura theme park during the total stay, including both check-in and check-out dates.

The hotel has 2 restaurants serving a wide variety of Mediterranean and international dishes. It also has 1 cafeteria and 2 bars.

The property is located 1 hour by car from Barcelona, 500 meters from Salou Beach and 15 minutes by car from Tarragona.

Hotel Caribe is a 10-minute drive from Reus Airport and a 20-minute drive from the AVE train station.
