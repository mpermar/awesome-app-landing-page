Description

This roulette-style package implies that guests will receive by email the confirmation of the hotel assigned the day before their arrival and will enjoy the best prices offered by the PortAventura® Resort.

The fascinating themed hotels include the tropical paradise and the American Far West. The rooms are elegant and feature air conditioning, TV, safe and private bathroom. Each hotel offers a different buffet and à la carte restaurants.

This rate provides free and unlimited access to the PortAventura® theme park and a 30% discount for the Costa Caribe water park (please consult the park opening calendar). For an additional fee, guests can also access the wellness services of Hotel PortAventura and Hotel Caribe, which include a sauna, hot tub, steam baths, indoor pool and gym.

The PortAventura® Resort has a train station with direct service to Barcelona and Valencia. The A7 motorway is 2 km from the complex, while the AVE station is a 20-minute drive away.

The property is located 10 minutes by car from Reus airport and 1 hour by car, train or bus from Barcelona airport.
