Description

The rooms of the family-run establishment Casa Rural Mas Garganta feature charming rustic décor. All include heating, a seating area, a private bathroom and an orchard, where guests can collect, water and plant their own vegetables.

Mas Garganta offers homemade dinners and breakfasts based on fresh produce from the area. Many of the fruits and vegetables used are grown in the establishment itself. Meals are served on a covered terrace with spectacular views of Vall de Bas. Free coffee and pastries are offered, as well as vegetarian and gluten-free menus.

A free childcare service is offered during dinner time. This service carries a supplement the rest of the time.

The house has a sandpit for children, chickens and other animals. Staff can inform guests about hiking trails. The house is just 2 km from the historic city of Olot and a 45-minute drive from Girona airport.
