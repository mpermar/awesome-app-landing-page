Description

Chalet del Golf has a lounge with armchairs and an open fire. There is also a Bridge room, for card games, and a children’s play room. Ski storage is available. Guests can enjoy a sauna or relax in the small wellness centre.

The restaurant offers panoramic views of the countryside. The main bar serves cocktails, which can also be enjoyed on the terrace. The snack bar offers informal light meals.

The town of Puigcerdà is just 3 km away. The Cadí Tunnel is about 20 minutes’ drive away.
