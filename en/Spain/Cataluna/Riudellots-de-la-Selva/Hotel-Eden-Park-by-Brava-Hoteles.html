Description

The air-conditioned rooms are bright and feature a flat-screen TV. They also include a minibar with 1 free bottle of water per stay. The bathroom comes with free toiletries, bath, and hairdryer. The toilet is separated.

Hotel Eden Park by Brava Hoteles is set in pleasant gardens, and has a bar with an outdoor terrace. Regional cuisine is served in the à la carte restaurant. Meeting rooms are also available.

The hotel is around 30 km from Lloret de Mar. The Dalí Museum in Figueres is a 40-minute drive away, and Girona’s historic city centre is just 11 km away.
