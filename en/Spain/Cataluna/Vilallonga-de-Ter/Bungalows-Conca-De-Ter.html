Description

All Conca de Ter bungalows are equipped with heating, kitchenette with fridge and living area with sofa and TV.

The campsite houses sports facilities, such as volleyball and basketball courts. It also has a games room with board games and ping pong tables. The staff organizes a varied entertainment program for children.

A combination of French and Mallorcan food is served in the Conca de Ter's restaurant. There is also a bar where you can have a drink.

The Vallter 2000 ski resort is a 30-minute drive from the campsite. The Camprodón golf club is a 15-minute drive away.
