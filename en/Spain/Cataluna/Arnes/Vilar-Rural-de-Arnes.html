Description

The family establishment Vilar Rural de Arnes offers many activities for children and for the whole family.

The units feature a TV and a private bathroom with a bath or shower and free toiletries.

In addition, the Vilar Rural de Arnes has a hot tub, terrace and bar.

Bicycle rental service is also provided. The area is ideal for hiking. Vilar Rural de Arnes is 46 km from L'Ametlla de Mar, 25 km from Tortosa and 80 km from the nearest airport, Reus.
