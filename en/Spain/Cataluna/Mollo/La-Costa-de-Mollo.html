Description

The Coast, distributed in 2 levels, has 2 rooms on the ground floor and the rest on the first floor. All have a private bathroom with free toiletries.

There is a common living room with a fireplace and TV on the ground floor and another living room with access to the gallery on the first floor. There is also a piano there.

There is a large rustic-style table in the dining room, where you can taste traditional dishes made with seasonal local products.

You can access La Costa by a small winding rural road. It is about 10 minutes drive from the border with France.
