Description

All rooms at Hostal Nou de Crespià are bright and have beautiful rustic décor, as well as exposed stone walls. They also have free Wi-Fi, heating and a private bathroom.

Guests of Hostal Nou de Crespià can enjoy a buffet breakfast and a selection of Catalan dishes for dinner. It also houses a charming seating area, equipped with a fireplace and library.

The surrounding area of the establishment is ideal for cycling, horse riding and excursions. Girona, Olot and the volcanic area of La Garrotxa are 25 km away. Figueres and Besalú are only 15 km away. The beaches of L'Escala and Roses are 35 minutes away by car.

Great place to read a book on a porch and just letting time pass by while listening to the birds

"We stayed for just one night and we were lucky enough to be basically alone. We spent most of the time reading a book on a porch and just letting time pass by listening to the birds. We had a good dinner served outdoors as well. The room had a great view and the bed was large, new, and remarkably comfortable. There was a ceiling fan on the room and we needed to turn it on (this was early June). The bathroom was fine but it was poorly lighted and the shower definitely lacked a minimum reasonable pressure, which is something that could be irritating if staying for a longer period. Rooms don’t have tv sets which is something we liked. However, although we notified it to the owners, they were not able to resolve the problem for us to get any usable WiFi reception through our stay. 3G signal was very weak too in the area. So if this is an issue for you I guess that you should be aware of. As a matter of fact though, when you choose to stay in a place on a beautiful location and environment like this, being forced to disconnect from the outside world might be an asset these days! The house is decorated with taste. If traveling with kids they have everything that could make the place a dream come true for them because the playground is amazing, and basically infinite for them, including a soccer field and a small pool. So overall, our experience was great and we plan to come back!"

Maximum number of extra beds in the room: 2.
Extra beds are available upon request and must be confirmed by the accommodation.
Supplements are not automatically calculated in the total amount and must be paid separately during the stay.
