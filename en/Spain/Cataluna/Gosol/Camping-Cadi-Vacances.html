Description

The wooden bungalows of the Cadi Vacances have a porch with magnificent views of the mountains. They include a living room with sofa and TV and a kitchen equipped with microwave, refrigerator, coffee maker and electric hob.

The complex also has a bar, shop, games room, paddle tennis court and multi-purpose sports courts. You can also rent bicycles at the reception.

The Sierra del Cadí and Mount Pedraforca, which are nearby, are ideal for hiking, cycling and skiing. The nearest slopes are in Alp, 50 minutes by car.
