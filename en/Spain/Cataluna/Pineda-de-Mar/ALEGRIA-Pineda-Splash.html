Description

This accommodation is 5 minutes walk from the beach. Hotel ALEGRIA Pineda Splash is located 300 meters from Pineda de Mar Beach and features a large outdoor pool and sun terrace with garden areas. All rooms include air conditioning, balcony and satellite TV.

The hotel has a park and games room with table tennis and darts. In addition, the entertainment staff organizes activities and shows on a regular basis.

The rooms at ALEGRIA Pineda Splash are bright and feature functional décor with tiled floors. All include a safe and a private bathroom with free toiletries.

Breakfast is served daily, while the bar offers drinks and snacks. The property is also less than 5 minutes' walk from the bars and restaurants of downtown Pineda.

The hotel is located on the Maresme coast, about 50 km from Barcelona and Girona and 400 meters from the Pineda train station, which offers regular services to Barcelona.
