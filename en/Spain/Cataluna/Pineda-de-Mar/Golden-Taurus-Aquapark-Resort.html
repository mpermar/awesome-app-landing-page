Description

Rooms at the Golden Taurus are decorated in a cheerful style and have air conditioning and satellite TV.

The hotel restaurant serves a hot and cold buffet of Spanish and international cuisine. The hotel bar offers drinks and light meals throughout the day.

The tour desk staff will help you rent a bicycle and provide information about Pineda de Mar and the surrounding area. Barcelona is just over 1 hour by train.
