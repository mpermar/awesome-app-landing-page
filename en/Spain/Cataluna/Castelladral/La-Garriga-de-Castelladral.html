Description

The rooms at La Garriga de Castelladral are decorated with natural materials in neutral tones. They include a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a large bathroom with bathrobe and slippers.

La Garriga has an outdoor pool, located in a natural environment. The property grounds include a beautiful lake and stables.

The old barn next to the house has been transformed into a restaurant. Meals are prepared with vegetables from the garden. Breakfast includes homemade cakes, baked by the family's grandmother.

La Garriga is 17 km from Manresa and 69 km from Barcelona. Barcelona airport is 1 hour away by car.
