Description

The modern rooms have a flat-screen TV, a minibar and a bathroom with a hairdryer. Many offer views of the pool.

The NM Co's SA Cova restaurant serves creative Mediterranean cuisine. The bar has its own terrace and a garden area.

The NM Suites Hotel is located on the Costa Brava, in the Baix Empordà region. The city of Girona is less than 30 km away. You can organize excursions at the tour desk.
