Description

Apartments at Hapimag Mas Nou have a kitchenette with kettle and fridge, TV and balcony or terrace. In addition, there is a supermarket on site.

The Brasa, the Mas Nou restaurant, prepares modern versions of the typical dishes of the area. There is also a poolside bar and a sports bar in the lounge.

This complex offers a space to store golf equipment for free and, in addition, can organize green fees and a shuttle service to the golf course. There is also a water park and nightlife activities are offered. Parking is available for a fee.
