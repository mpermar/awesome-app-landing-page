Description

Rooms at Peralada feature modern décor in bright colors. All rooms have plasma TVs and free Wi-Fi.

The Peralada wine therapy spa has a wide variety of treatments and massages.

The Olivera restaurant has a terrace and serves Mediterranean cuisine. In addition, this hotel offers its guests a free entrance to the Castillo casino.

Peralada is located in the town of Peralada, a 15-minute drive from Figueres and less than 40 km from Girona and its airport.
