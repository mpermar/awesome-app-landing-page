Description

The studios and apartments have air conditioning, a balcony and a living room with a sofa bed, a safe and a flat-screen TV. They also include a private bathroom with a hairdryer and shower. They also have a kitchenette equipped with a fridge, microwave, hob, oven and washing machine.

The property offers luggage storage, tourist information and excursion organization. The reception also organizes an airport shuttle service for a fee. The property is a 10-minute walk from Calella train station and a 2-minute walk from the airport bus stop. The accommodation is also located 42 km from Girona Airport and 1 hour's drive from Barcelona Airport.
