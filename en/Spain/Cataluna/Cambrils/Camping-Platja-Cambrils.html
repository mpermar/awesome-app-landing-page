Description

All bungalows have heating and there are pitches for shops. The bungalows also have a kitchenette, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom.

Camping Platja Cambrils houses the Don Camilo restaurant, supermarkets, 2 swimming pools, playgrounds and sports centers. There is also a bicycle rental service, conference room, entertainment during the weekends and every day during the high season and free Wi-Fi in public areas.

Camping Don Camilo is just 3 km from Salou and 5 km from PortAventura and FerrariLand theme parks.
