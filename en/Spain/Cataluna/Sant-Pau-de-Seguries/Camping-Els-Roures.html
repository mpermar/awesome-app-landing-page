Description

Càmping El Roures is located in a beautiful area of centuries-old oak forests, at the foot of the Pyrenees. During your stay in this unpolluted natural paradise you can participate in hiking trips and other outdoor activities. The establishment is a perfect place to stay with family or a group of friends and enjoy independence in a relaxed atmosphere.

In the Valley of Camprodon valley you can practice outdoor activities, such as hiking, and visit the Roman ruins in the area.

There is also a spa center with a hot tub and Turkish baths, open on weekends and holidays.
