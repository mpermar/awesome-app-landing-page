Description

Cabanes Dosrius has houses built on the trunks of large pines. There is also a cabin at ground level, adapted for people with reduced mobility and running water and electricity.

The other houses in the trees have no electricity or water and offer access to shared bathrooms. All accommodation includes a terrace overlooking the nature reserve.

The establishment has a reception, lounge and a cafeteria.

The surrounding countryside is perfect for hiking, horse riding and cycling. The municipality of Dosrius is 650 meters above sea level and close to places of interest from the medieval, Iberian and Neolithic times.

The beaches of Mataró and El Maresme are 30 minutes by car from the property and the city of Granollers, 15 minutes by car.
