Description

The lounge and bar area of the Maritim Hotel overlook the bay of Roses. A bar service is available on the pool terrace and the restaurant serves a wide variety of local and international dishes.

Maritim Hotel is located on the Costa Brava, a 10-minute walk from the center of Roses, about 10 minutes by car from the Aiguamolls de l'Empordà Natural Park and 15 km from Figueres. There is free public parking next to the hotel.
