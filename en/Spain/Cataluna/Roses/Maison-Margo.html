Description

The villa has a kitchen with a dishwasher, oven and microwave. It also has a private bathroom, flat-screen cable TV and laptop. The MAISON MARGO includes a hot tub, solarium and barbecue facilities.

Guests can play tennis, miniature golf and squash nearby. Bicycle rental service is also provided. Golfing and horse riding can be practiced nearby.

Maison Margo is 2.1 km from the Citadel of Roses and 1.1 km from Aquabrava Water Park. Girona-Costa Brava Airport is 54 km away.
