Description

The bright rooms of the S'Agaró Hotel Spa & Wellness are decorated in pastel colors. They have a private balcony, classic furniture, air conditioning and a modern bathroom with a hairdryer.

The restaurant serves cutting-edge Mediterranean cuisine. The hotel also has a bar with a terrace next to the pool that offers snacks and various dishes, such as seafood, tapas and typical dishes such as paella and fideuá.

All guests have free admission to the indoor pool and gym. For a fee you can enjoy the spa area, with a water circuit, sauna, steam baths and massage service.

The hotel is located between Sant Feliu de Guíxols and Platja d'Aro, 40 km from Girona.
