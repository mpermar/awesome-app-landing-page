Description

The rooms at Aqua Hotel Montagut Suites feature contemporary décor and feature a private balcony and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The hotel's buffet restaurant offers show cooking. In the bar you can have snacks and drinks.

Aqua Hotel Montagut Suites has a 24-hour reception, which provides car rental and currency exchange.

The beautiful coastal town of Tossa de Mar is about 40 minutes away by car. Barcelona is less than 1 hour away by car.
