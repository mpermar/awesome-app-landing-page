Description

All rooms have satellite TV, safe and air conditioning.

In summer, the hotel organizes live music sessions on the large terrace. There is also a nightlife club.

The restaurant serves buffet breakfast, lunch and dinner. It offers a combination of Mediterranean and international dishes. There is also a bar and an ice cream shop.

Santa Susanna is a very quiet destination located in the Maresme, on the north coast of Barcelona, and enjoys a very wide sandy beach. The Santa Susanna train station is very close and offers direct connections with Barcelona.
