Description

The hotel has a wellness and fitness center, the Aquamarina Wellness & Spa, which guests can use for a fee. The center offers a heated saltwater pool, a gym and beauty treatments.

Aqua's rooms are simple and spacious and feature air conditioning, satellite TV and a well-equipped bathroom.

Aquamarina Santa Susanna's restaurant serves a buffet breakfast and offers a selection of Mediterranean dishes for lunch and dinner. The hotel also has 3 bars.

The property is located just a 5-minute walk from the center of the town of Santa Susana, which offers several cafés, bars and restaurants. The hotel is also less than 6 km from Piñeda del Mar and the fishing village of Calella.
