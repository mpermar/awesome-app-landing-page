Description

All rooms have a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer. Some of the rooms have a seating area and others have views of the sea or the pool.

The establishment has a common room.

Various activities such as windsurfing, sailing, golf, horse riding and hiking can be done nearby.

The property is just 10 minutes from Ocata train station, a 45-minute drive from the Costa Brava and 29 km from Barcelona-El Prat Airport. Girona airport is 50 minutes away by car.
