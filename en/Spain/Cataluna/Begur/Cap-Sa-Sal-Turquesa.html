Description

This modern and elegant apartment has a private terrace with dining table, solarium and sea views. The living room has a flat-screen TV with free Netflix. There is free high speed WiFi.

The kitchen has a hob, oven, microwave, dishwasher, kettle and Nespresso coffee machine. The apartment has a clothes dryer and washing machine.

Guests can directly access the beach and the pool in the elevator that will take them through an underground tunnel.

The property is a 10-minute drive from the Sa Riera sandy beach and a 20-minute drive from the Pals beach. In the immediate vicinity you can do several water sports, such as diving, kayaking and sailing.

The property is a 10-minute drive from Begur and a 1-hour drive from Girona and its airport.
