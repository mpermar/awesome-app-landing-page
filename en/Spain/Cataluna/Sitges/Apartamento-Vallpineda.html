Description

The kitchen has a dishwasher, oven and microwave. The bathroom is private. There is a flat screen TV. The Vallpineda Apartment includes a barbecue. Free Wi-Fi is also provided.

The property has a tennis court and mini-golf and golfing can be enjoyed in the area. Sitges Convention Bureau is 1.1 km from Apartment Vallpineda and Sitges-Aiguadolç Marina is 2.8 km away. In the surroundings you can practice various activities, such as horse riding, windsurfing and diving. Barcelona El Prat Airport is 24 km away.
