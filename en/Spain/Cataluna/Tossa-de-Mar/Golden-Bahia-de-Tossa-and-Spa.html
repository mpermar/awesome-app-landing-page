Description

All spacious rooms at Bahia de Tossa have a private balcony, air conditioning, TV and tea and coffee making facilities.

The Golden Bahia de Tossa & Spa has a buffet restaurant serving traditional and international dishes and a bar where you can have sandwiches, salads and ice cream.

The spa comes at an extra cost and has a hot tub, an indoor pool, a sauna, a massage service and beauty treatments.

The hotel is located 10 minutes by car from Lloret de Mar, 25 minutes from the beach of Platja d'Aro and about 45 minutes by car from the airport and the city of Girona.
