Description

The rooms include a desk, flat-screen TV, private bathroom and air conditioning. Some have a balcony.

A daily buffet breakfast is served. The hotel restaurant organizes theme nights.

Tossa de Mar Castle is a 6-minute walk from URH Tossa de Mar. Girona-Costa Brava Airport is 24 km away.
