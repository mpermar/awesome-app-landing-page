Description

The Catalonia Diagonal Centro offers cozy accommodation in modern style located a short distance from some of the most popular tourist places in Barcelona.

The hotel is just 300 meters from Passeig de Gràcia Avenue, which houses many modernist buildings, such as La Pedrera, one of Gaudí's famous works. In addition, nearby you will find Plaza Catalunya and the lively area of Las Ramblas.

All rooms are elegantly decorated and consist of modern-style wooden furniture and a private marble bathroom. They also include satellite TV and free Wi-Fi.

The hotel is also a short walk from the modern Gràcia neighborhood, which has bohemian shops, tree-lined plazas and numerous bars and restaurants. In addition, nearby there are several metro and bus lines that provide quick connections with other points in the city.

Eixample is a great choice for travelers interested in architecture, food and shopping.
