Description

The hotel has been elegantly renovated and occupies a building of the early twentieth century representative of Catalan architectural Novecentismo.

The rooms at the Grand Hotel Barcelona have a modern design and are equipped with air conditioning, a 32 "Loewe TV, a free minibar with daily refreshment of refreshments and snacks, and a bathroom with a large rain shower and luxury toiletries.

The Grand Central houses the City restaurant, which prepares innovative Catalan cuisine. There is a modern rooftop bar that serves cocktails and enjoys views of Barcelona.

The hotel also has a fitness center, massage service and a 24-hour reception with a tour desk. It also offers car and bicycle rental services. The establishment provides City WiFi devices, which allows you to enjoy internet connection outside the hotel.

The Grand Central Hotel is just a 3-minute walk from the Born district, where you will find several shops and restaurants.

Ciutat Vella is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, food and culture.
