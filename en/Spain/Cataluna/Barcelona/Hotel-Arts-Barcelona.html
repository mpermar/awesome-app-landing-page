Description

The Arts is located next to the Olympic port of Barcelona and is surrounded by shops and lively bars. Barceloneta Beach is 250 meters away and Ciutadella Park is 300 meters away.

The rooms at the Arts Hotel are elegant and spacious and include a flat-screen TV, sound system and a bathroom with a separate hot tub and hydromassage shower.

The hotel's Enoteca restaurant, awarded with 2 Michelin stars, serves quality Mediterranean cuisine, while the creative restaurant Arola is managed by the famous chef Sergi Arola. The establishment offers several bars and restaurants, such as the poolside bar, which serves light meals in summer.

The Arts Hotel has a hairdresser and several luxury fashion stores. The hotel also has a team of concierges who speak several languages and a fully equipped business center.
