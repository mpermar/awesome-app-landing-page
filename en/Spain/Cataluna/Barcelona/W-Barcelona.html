Description

The rooms of the W Hotel have a spectacular avant-garde design and views of the Mediterranean Sea or the city. All rooms have a chaise lounge, flat-screen TV, iPod dock and rain shower.

The W is home to 2 elegant gourmet restaurants: the BRAVO24, led by Michelin-starred chef Carlos Abellán, and the WAVE. The rooftop bar, called ECLIPSE, is located on the 26th floor and offers cocktails and panoramic views of the city and the sea. The W Lounge also prepares cocktails. The SALT Beach Club & Restaurant restaurant prepares an innovative hamburger menu accompanied by cocktails specially designed to enjoy a unique dining experience by the beach.

There are 2 swimming pools, SUN Deck and WET Deck, surrounded by sun loungers. In addition, the hotel has direct access to the beach. Bliss Spa facilities include a hot tub, sauna and steam room, and a new relaxation area, GETAWAY, available since 2018. You can enjoy a wide selection of massages and beauty treatments.

The W is a 20-minute walk from the Barceloneta metro station. Several buses stop in front of the hotel.

Ciutat Vella is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, food and culture.
