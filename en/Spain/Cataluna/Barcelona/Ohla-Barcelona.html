Description

Rooms at Ohla Barcelona feature contemporary décor and feature large windows, flat-screen TVs, free Wi-Fi, iPod docks and modern bathrooms with bathrobes and slippers. Some also enjoy views of the Gothic Quarter.

Romain Fornell is the current chef director of all the culinary spaces of the Ohla Barcelona hotel: the Caelis restaurant, the Vistro49 and La Plassohla. This last restaurant specializes in Catalan cuisine and offers tapas and small plates. Cocktails can be enjoyed at the famous Ohla Boutique bar.

The rooftop pool enjoys fantastic views of the city and has a quiet terrace. The hotel also has a gym and wellness area with sauna.

Ohla Barcelona is 300 meters from Plaza Catalunya, 200 meters from Urquinaona Metro Station and a 10-minute walk from Passeig de Gràcia and Gaudí's modernist buildings.

Ciutat Vella is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, food and culture.
