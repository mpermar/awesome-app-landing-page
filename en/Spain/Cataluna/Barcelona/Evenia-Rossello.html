Description

The modern restaurant L'Illa del Rosselló serves Mediterranean cuisine. You can also enjoy the typical tapas on the Placeta Joan Brossa terrace, which is surrounded by small gardens.

The Evenia Rossello tour desk can provide useful information about the city and help you with car rental. Luggage storage is also provided.

The Diagonal metro station is 400 meters from the hotel and offers direct access to the city center of Barcelona. Barcelona airport is 12 km away.

Eixample is a great choice for travelers interested in architecture, food and shopping.
