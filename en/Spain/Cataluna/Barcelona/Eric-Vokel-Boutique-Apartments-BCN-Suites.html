Description

The apartments at Eric Vökel BCN Suites feature a flat-screen TV in the living room. All are soundproofed and have heating and air conditioning. Each apartment has modern amenities such as dishwasher and washing machine. The apartments on the ground floor include a private garden.

At the reception is the Eric's Corner area, where free products are offered that can be very useful during the stay, such as dishwasher tablets, coffee, tea, sugar, oil and napkins.

Eric Vökel BCN is in the famous Gracia neighborhood, within walking distance of the Passeig de Gracia. The area consists of numerous bars, shops, cafes and traditional squares.

Eric Vökel BCN has a tour desk and ticket service. There are also lockers available to guests. In addition, express check-in and check-out and free luggage storage are offered.
