Description

The hotel is located next to the Espanya metro station. In the square there are numerous bus stops that provide connections with other points of the city, with the Fira Barcelona fairgrounds and with Barcelona airport.

All rooms are spacious and include air conditioning, satellite TV, safe and private marble bathroom with hairdryer. There is also free WiFi.

The Catalonia Barcelona Plaza serves a daily buffet breakfast. In addition, Filigrana restaurant prepares Catalan cuisine based on local products, while Gourmet Corner offers late meals. The hotel also houses a Japanese restaurant, the Kurai.

The Catalonia Barcelona Plaza also has a business center and meeting rooms equipped with the latest technology.

Sants-Montjuïc is a great choice for travelers interested in monuments, culture and architecture.
