Description

The rooms at OD Barcelona feature a desk, private bathroom, free Wi-Fi and a closet.

A buffet and à la carte breakfast is offered every morning.

Free access to the Urquinaona Holmes Place Gym is located 800 meters away. In addition, OD Barcelona offers a terrace.

The hotel is 300 meters from Casa Batlló and 13 km from Barcelona-El Prat Airport.

Eixample is a great choice for travelers interested in architecture, food and shopping.
