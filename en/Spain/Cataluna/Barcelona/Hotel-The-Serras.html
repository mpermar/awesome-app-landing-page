Description

The hotel occupies a historic building in which the famous painter Pablo Picasso began his career in 1896. The hotel retains the original facade of the building and provides luxury services, such as airport pick-up, limousine transfer, concierge or room service. Valet parking

The rooms at The Serras are soundproofed and elegantly decorated. They have air conditioning, a private balcony overlooking the Vell port or the Mercè street of the Gothic quarter of Barcelona, high definition Smart TV, wireless sound system and bathroom with bathrobes and slippers.

The hotel has magnificent views of the sea and a rooftop terrace with an elegant lounge with chill out music serving breakfast and designer cocktails. The mezzanine of The Serras Hotel includes a cozy library with a bar and a restaurant with Catalan specialties and Mediterranean tapas that opens for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

This boutique hotel is a 10-minute walk from the Picasso Museum and Estació de França train station, 650 meters from Plaça Reial Square, 500 meters from Santa Maria del Mar Cathedral and a 15-minute drive from El Prat Airport from Barcelona.

Ciutat Vella is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, food and culture.
