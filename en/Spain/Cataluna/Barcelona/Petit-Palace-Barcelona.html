Description

All rooms at Petit Palace Barcelona feature modern décor and feature a flat-screen TV and hydromassage shower.

The hotel has a 24-hour reception and an internet area with free connection.

Petit Palace Barcelona is a 7-minute walk from the Gothic quarter, where the Palau de la Música Catalana is located. The Urquinaona and Paseo de Gracia metro stations are less than 5 minutes away on foot. La Pedrera de Gaudí is 750 meters away and Ciutadella Park is a 15-minute walk away.

Eixample is a great choice for travelers interested in architecture, food and shopping.
