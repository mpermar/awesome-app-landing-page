Description

All the bungalows at Serra de Prades have a living room with sofa and TV. The kitchen has a gas hob, microwave and coffee maker. Each bungalow has a free parking space.

Camping Serra de Prades has a soccer field, a basketball court and a playground. Sports and activities are organized daily for all ages. There are 2 clubs for children.

In the Els Ceps restaurant you can taste Catalan and international dishes. The establishment also has a small supermarket and a souvenir shop.

The campsite is located between the natural parks of Poblet and Montsant, 12 km from the monastery of Poblet. You will find several wineries less than 15 minutes away by car. Tarragona is 1 hour away by car.
