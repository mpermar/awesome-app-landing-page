Description

Hotel Món Sant Benet occupies the medieval monastery of Sant Benet de Bages, which has been equipped with modern facilities. La Fábrica, an old Catalan factory, is part of the building. This property also has 4,000 m² of quiet gardens, as well as a library, business center and massage service.

All rooms include a private terrace with views of the monastery and the natural park of Sant Llorenç del Munt i l'Obac. They have a private bathroom with a bath and shower, air conditioning, heating, flat screen TV, minibar and safe.

The Hotel Món Sant Benet has 3 restaurants, among which L'Ó, awarded with 1 Michelin star, serving creative and healthy versions of traditional recipes.

The property offers free private parking and is just 65 km from Barcelona. The Abadal winery is 20 minutes away by car and Barcelona - El Prat airport, about 50 minutes away by car.
