Description

The elegant Aquarium Hotel features brightly colored rooms and includes air conditioning, a private balcony and satellite TV. In addition, there is a safe and small fridge available for rent.

The Aquarium has a buffet restaurant with show cooking and a bar. From June to September, a daily entertainment program is offered with different activities and live shows.

There are several shops and bars in the streets surrounding the hotel and the lively town center is 800 meters away. The Lloret bus station, with regular service to Girona airport, is a 10-minute walk away.
