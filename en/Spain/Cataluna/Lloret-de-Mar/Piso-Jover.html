Description

The accommodation features a kitchen, living and dining areas, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bathtub.

The property provides a bicycle rental service and car rental. In the area you can ride a horse. Piso Jover is 400 meters from Lloret Beach and 1.2 km from Fenals Beach. Various activities can be practiced in the area, such as snorkeling, windsurfing and diving. Girona-Costa Brava Airport is 23 km away.
