Description

All rooms include furnished balconies overlooking the pool, garden or street. They also have satellite TV and a modern bathroom with a hairdryer.

Hotel Anabel is located on the Costa Brava, about 6 km from the coastal town of Blanes. Girona Airport is 25 km away.

In the hotel game room there are table football, video games and board games. The Anabel hotel also has a ping pong table and a TV room.
