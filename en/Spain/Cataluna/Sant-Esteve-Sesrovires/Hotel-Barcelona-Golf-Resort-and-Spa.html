Description

The rooms of the Hotel Barcelona Golf Resort & Spa are spacious and bright and have a modern and attractive decoration. All rooms include heating, desk, TV, minibar and bathroom.

The Xarel·lo restaurant serves traditional Catalan cuisine. In addition, there is a beautiful terrace overlooking the gardens.

For an additional fee, guests can access the spa, equipped with a hydromassage pool, sensation showers, exercise pool, foot bath, Finnish sauna, Scottish shower and Turkish bath. There is also a gym and a wide range of treatments, such as massages, chocotherapy, Vichy showers, wine therapy and cavaterapia. The entrance carries an additional cost.

The complex is 10 minutes drive from Martorell and 30 minutes drive from the beaches of Castelldefels.
