Description

The house features wood and stone finishes and has colorful bedrooms, a living room with a flat-screen TV and DVD player, and a kitchenette with a dishwasher and oven. The bathroom includes free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Mas La Riera has a garden with a furnished terrace and barbecue facilities. In addition, there is a tennis court, bicycles and a playground.

The surrounding area is ideal for outdoor activities, such as hiking. Ripoll is a 10-minute drive away and offers a variety of traditional Catalan cuisine restaurants. Vic is 30 minutes away by car and Olot is 35 km away.
