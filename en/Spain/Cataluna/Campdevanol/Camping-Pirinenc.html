Description

The bungalows and mobile homes feature a furnished porch, mountain views, heating, a living area with a sofa or a sofa bed, a private bathroom and a kitchenette with a fridge, microwave and coffee maker.

Pirinenc has a restaurant bar and barbecue facilities. Also, prepare packed lunches.

In summer a children's entertainment program is organized. There is also a children's playground and a games room with table tennis and table football tables, while the La Molina ski resort is 20 km away.
