Description

Bonavista Bonmont offers Mediterranean-style apartments with furnished terraces or balconies. The kitchenette is equipped with a fridge, microwave and dishwasher. The use of the safe comes at an additional cost. Bed linen and end-of-stay cleaning are included.

The hotel houses the El Olivo bar-restaurant, which has a terrace and serves dinner every day. You can buy local products and basic products in the store of the establishment.

Bonmont's Pierre & Vacances Village Bonavista has a 24-hour front desk and free parking. Platja Cristal Beach is 7 km away.
