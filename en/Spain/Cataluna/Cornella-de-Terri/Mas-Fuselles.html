Description

The rooms have a rustic style and feature a TV and a private bathroom with a bathtub, as well as access to a living room, a library and a games room for shared use, as well as a children's playground and a soccer field.

Mas Fuselles is a 10-minute drive from Banyoles, where there is a wide variety of traditional bars and restaurants serving Catalan cuisine. The surrounding natural area is perfect for outdoor activities, such as hiking.

This farm stay is 8 km from Banyoles Lake and the national park, 30 minutes 'drive from the Costa Brava, 20 minutes' drive from the center of Girona and 30 km from the airport.

Maximum number of extra beds / cots in the room: 3.
Extra beds and / or cots are available upon request and must be confirmed by the accommodation.
