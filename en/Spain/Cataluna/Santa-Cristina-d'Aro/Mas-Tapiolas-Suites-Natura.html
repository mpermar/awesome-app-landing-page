Description

All suites have air conditioning, private garden and state-of-the-art items, such as flat-screen TVs and Bluetooth speakers. It also offers adjustable temperature and lighting systems. The modern design bathrooms include a walk-in shower, slippers and bathrobes.

Mas Tapiolas Suites Natura offers a spa with a salt cabin, an ice cave, an indoor pool, thermal sun loungers and a foot bathtub. A range of treatments is also provided. There is a paddle court, fitness center and an 18-hole pitch and putt course.

Mas Tapiolas Suites Natura offers a daily breakfast. The property has an à la carte restaurant, which serves dishes made with local products.

There is a 24-hour reception and free private parking. Guests will find several beaches 10 km away and the Costa Brava golf course 3 km away. Girona is 30 minutes away by car.
