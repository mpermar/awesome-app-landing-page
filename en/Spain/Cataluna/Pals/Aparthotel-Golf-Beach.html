Description

All accommodation at Golf Beach is air-conditioned and simply decorated with tiled floors. The studios and apartments have a kitchenette and a living area with a flat-screen TV.

All the villas of the Village have a separate kitchen and a lounge, which opens onto a terrace overlooking the pool.

The reception staff, which is open 24 hours a day, offers a car rental service. Guests of Aparthotel & Village Golf Beach can enjoy discounts at one of the 3 neighboring golf courses.

Golf Beach is located in the area of Sa Punta de Pals and is approximately 1 km from a picturesque cove. Girona and its airport are 1 hour away by car.
