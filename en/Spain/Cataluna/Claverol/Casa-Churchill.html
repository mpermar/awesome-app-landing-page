Description

Rooms at Casa Churchill are beautiful and feature free Wi-Fi, flat-screen satellite TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer. Most rooms offer views of the Talarn reservoir.

The Churchill House has a lounge, which opens onto a terrace, with board games, sofas and fireplace. The property serves typical homemade Catalan food made with seasonal products, upon request.

Casa Churchill is 50 km from Ski-Pallars Ski Resort and 55 km from Aigüestortes National Park. The mountains of the Serra de Boumort and the Sant Antoni reservoir, where bathing is possible, are 4 km away.
