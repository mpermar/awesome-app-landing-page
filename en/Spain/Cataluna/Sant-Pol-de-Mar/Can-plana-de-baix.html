Description

Some units have a seating area and / or terrace. There are also a kitchen equipped with a dishwasher and oven. The bathroom is private and has a hair dryer. Bed linen is also provided.

Can Flat from below houses barbecue.

The property has a tennis court, and the area is popular for golf facilities and for horse riding and windsurfing. Can Flat de Baix is 23 km from Lloret de Mar and 45 km from Girona. Girona-Costa Brava Airport is 35 km from Can Flat de Baix.
