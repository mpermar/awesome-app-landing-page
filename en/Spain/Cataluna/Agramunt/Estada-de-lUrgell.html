Description

The units feature a flat-screen TV, a dining area and a kitchenette equipped with a microwave, toaster, fridge, stove and coffee maker. Some have a seating area and / or terrace.

Beauty treatments and a massage service are available for a fee.

The property also offers free bicycles. The area is ideal for horse riding and cycling. Estada de l Urgell is 49 km from Montblanc. Reus Airport is 74 km away.
