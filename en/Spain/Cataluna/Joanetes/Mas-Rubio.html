Description

Mas Rubió serves dishes made with local products in its dining room. It also has a shared lounge, terrace and barbecue area.

The rooms are decorated in a rustic style and overlook the gardens.

All include a private bathroom.

Mas Rubió is close to the natural park of the Garrocha volcanic zone and is ideal for athletes. In the surroundings there are hiking and cycling routes, and the hotel has a bicycle storage area.

The property offers free parking and is a 20-minute drive from Olot and a 30-minute drive from the beautiful town of Besalú.
