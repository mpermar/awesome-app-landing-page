Description

The Masia Casa Roja is tastefully decorated in a rustic style, with stone walls and wooden beamed ceilings. The apartments have a living area with a flat-screen TV and a dining table. The kitchen is equipped with a microwave, fridge and coffee maker. There is free Wi-Fi connection.

La Masia, located in the town of Casa Roja, is ideally located for hiking, cycling and horse riding. The Cornudella River is 200 meters away.

The beaches of Sitges and Calafell are about 35 minutes away by car. Tarragona and Barcelona are 65 km from La Masia.
