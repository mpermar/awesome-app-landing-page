Description

The hotel's large spa has a gym and hydrothermal circuit with hydromassage, several swimming pools and sauna. Reiki massages and beauty treatments are also provided.

The rooms have free Wi-Fi, satellite TV and a closed music circuit. They also include a minibar and a safe.

The restaurant at Montanyà Hotel & Lodge serves seasonal cuisine prepared with local seasonal produce. In addition, the hotel has a bar.

The hotel is 4 km from the Montanyà golf course and is close to the circuit of Catalonia, in Montmeló.
