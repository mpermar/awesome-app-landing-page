Description

The complex is very well located, 1 hour drive from Barcelona, ​​Andorra and France and about 20 ski resorts and the natural park of Cadí-Moixeró, a perfect environment to practice numerous outdoor activities throughout the year . The establishment has a 12,000 m² wellness center with thermal pools, relaxation areas, gym and treatment rooms, where you can relax, rest and forget about the routine.

Other facilities are offered, such as a games room, a playground, a large indoor park with slides and colored balls, a mini club and an entertainment team. In summer sports schools are organized.
