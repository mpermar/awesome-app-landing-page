Description

Hotel Ca l´Andreu offers elegant, spacious and bright rooms, many with balconies. All rooms have a flat-screen TV, free Wi-Fi, central heating and a private bathroom with a hairdryer. The suites also have a lounge.

The hotel offers breakfast service and the gourmet restaurant serves traditional Catalan cuisine and varied wines. The establishment has a peaceful lounge with fireplace, library and board games.

Ca l'Andreu is ideally located for cycling and hiking. It offers various outdoor activities, guided tours and picnics to take during excursions.

The hotel offers free parking. It is a 40-minute drive from the Masella ski resort and just over 30 km from the city of Berga.
