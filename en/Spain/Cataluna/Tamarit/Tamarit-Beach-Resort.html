Description

The air-conditioned units have a seating area and a kitchenette with a microwave, fridge and coffee maker. They also include towels and bed linen.

The property offers massage services, beauty treatments and a hairdresser. You can also organize water activities, such as canoeing and rowing, as well as activities for children.

Tamarit Park Resort Camping is 17 km from Reus Airport, 7 km from Tarragona and 1 km from Altafulla.
