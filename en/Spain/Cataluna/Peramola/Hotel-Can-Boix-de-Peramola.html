Description

Some rooms include a private bathroom with a spa bath and others have a bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries. Hotel Can Boix de Peramola also offers free Wi-Fi throughout and a flat-screen TV.

The property has a concierge service.

You can play tennis and table tennis at the hotel, while hiking is possible in the surroundings. Hotel Can Boix de Peramola is 40 km from Sort and 38 km from Cardona.
