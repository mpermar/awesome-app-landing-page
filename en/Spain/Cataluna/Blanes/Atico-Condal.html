Description

The accommodation of the ATTIC CONDAL has a seating area, kitchen, towels and bed linen. In addition, in the ATTIC CONDAL there is a playground.

Guests can play miniature golf at the property and the surrounding area is ideal for fishing and diving. The nearest airport is Girona-Costa Brava, located 26 km from the CONDAL PENTHOUSE.
