Description

This traditional rural mansion features stone walls and wooden beams. It is surrounded by gardens and has a terrace with tables and chairs. It also has a lounge area with a fireplace.

A buffet breakfast is served each morning. Guests can use the kitchen to prepare their own dishes at any time of the day.

Guests of Mas Ramades can visit the fantastic beaches of the Costa Brava, such as L'Escala, or the cultural centers of Figueras and Girona.
