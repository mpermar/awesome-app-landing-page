Description

The Gran Palas Hotel has an impressive entrance with columns and a large central staircase.

Rooms at the Gran Palas Hotel are equipped with air conditioning, private balcony, flat-screen TV, minibar and private bathroom with hairdryer.

The Gran Palas spa has a sauna, Turkish bath, hot tub, indoor pool and gym. There is also a massage service and several beauty treatments.

The hotel has 3 seasonal restaurants and an outdoor brasserie where you can taste traditional and international dishes, including Japanese specialties.

The reception is open 24 hours a day and offers car rental services. On-site parking is available for a fee.
