Description

Most rooms have a balcony with mountain or garden views. All have air conditioning and heating, desk, closet and flat-screen satellite TV. The private bathroom has a hairdryer.

The establishment has a library with a rustic fireplace. Luggage storage and laundry services are provided. In addition, Can Barrull is a 10-minute drive from Empordà Golf Club and 20 minutes from Pals Golf Club.
