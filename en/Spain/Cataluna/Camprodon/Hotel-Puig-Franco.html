Description

The rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV, hairdryer and free toiletries. Some include a seating area and there are views of the mountains, the pool or the garden.

The property provides sports equipment such as bicycles and paddle rackets for a fee. In addition, guests have free access to the gym and volleyball equipment.

In the area you can ski and play golf, and the hotel has ski storage. Girona-Costa Brava Airport is 55 km away.
