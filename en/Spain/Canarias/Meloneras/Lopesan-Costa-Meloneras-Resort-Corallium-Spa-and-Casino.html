Description

The rooms at Lopesan Costa Meloneras Resort, Corallium Spa & Casino are spacious and elegant. They have marble floors and air conditioning. All rooms also have satellite TV, a minibar and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and bathrobes.

The elegant Ambassador restaurant at Costa Meloneras Resort offers nouvelle cuisine. The complex also houses buffet restaurants, cafés and a cocktail bar.

The Lopesan spa offers a hydrotherapy circuit and salt grotto. It also offers various wellness treatments. The complex has an artificial beach and terraces with hammocks.

Lopesan Costa Meloneras Resort is located next to the Maspalomas lighthouse, a 10-minute walk from the dunes of Maspalomas. The hotel offers a free shuttle service to the Meloneras golf course, just 1.5 km away.
