Description

The cozy apartments have a private terrace or balcony. They have a well-equipped kitchen and a lounge with satellite TV.

The Lanzaplaya Apartments offer a buffet restaurant that often holds themed dinners. The poolside bar offers light meals.

The Lanzaplaya has a playground, table tennis and billiards. The lounge bar has a TV with international channels.

Located south of Lanzarote, the town of Puerto del Carmen has many bars, restaurants and shops. Lanzarote Airport is 10 km away.
