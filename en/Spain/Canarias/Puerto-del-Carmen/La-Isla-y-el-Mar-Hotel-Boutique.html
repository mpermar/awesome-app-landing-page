Description

The establishment has a chill out area with a Balinese bed and sun loungers overlooking the sea. There is also a garden, terrace, gym, wellness and beauty center, outdoor pool (heated in winter) and a small adjoining pool with a waterfall and hydromassage areas.

The property houses the à la carte restaurant Kentia Gourmet Club, which combines international flavors with Canarian cuisine. At La Isla and Mar Hotel Boutique you will also find a buffet restaurant.

This green hotel uses the latest technologies and renewable energy to reduce CO2 emissions.

The suites have a private furnished terrace, a seating area with a flat-screen TV, a Nespresso coffee machine and a minibar. The bathroom includes a rain shower, hairdryer, bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries.

The shops, bars and restaurants in the harbor area are a 10-minute walk away. At 50 meters there is a supermarket.

La Isla y Mar Hotel Boutique is a 20-minute drive from Timanfaya National Park and a 15-minute drive from Arrecife. Lanzarote airport is 10 minutes away by car.
