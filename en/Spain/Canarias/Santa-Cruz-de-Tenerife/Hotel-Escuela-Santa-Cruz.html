Description

This hotel features free Wi-Fi, an outdoor pool open seasonally and a solarium with a panoramic terrace overlooking the city.

It houses a first class gastrobar that serves varied cuisine. The restaurant has 2 terraces, 1 of them with ocean views.

The rooms have plenty of natural light, air conditioning, satellite TV, a minibar and a bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries.
