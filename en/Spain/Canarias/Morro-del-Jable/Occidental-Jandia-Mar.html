Description

The rooms have air conditioning and a private balcony. Some overlook the sea.

The Occidental Jandía Mar's spa includes an indoor pool, hot tub and sauna. In addition, there is a gym and massage service.

Various activities and water sports, such as windsurfing, can be practiced near the hotel. Next to the hotel there is a diving school. On the other hand, the hotel has a club and children's pool.

Occidental Jandía Mar's restaurant serves buffet meals with a large selection of regional and international dishes. Guests can also enjoy à la carte dishes at La Toscana restaurant for a fee.
