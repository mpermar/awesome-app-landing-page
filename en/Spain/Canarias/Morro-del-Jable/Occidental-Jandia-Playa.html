Description

The establishment has 2 different buffet-style restaurants, where you can enjoy Canarian and international cuisine. It also houses 4 bars, among which the poolside bar and piano bar stand out.

All rooms at Occidental Jandía Playa are equipped with a private balcony or terrace, air conditioning, satellite TV, a safe and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The property is 5 minutes' drive from Morro del Jable and approximately 1 hour's drive from Puerto del Rosario and Fuerteventura Airport.
