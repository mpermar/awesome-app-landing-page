Description

The rooms at Iberostar Playa Gaviotas are spacious and bright and feature satellite TV, air conditioning and a minibar.

The Iberostar Playa Gaviotas has 2 restaurants, the Atlantic and the Adventure, which serve a varied cuisine, including traditional Canarian dishes. Guests can also enjoy a drink in one of the 4 bars of the establishment.

The hotel's Spa Sensations is equipped with Turkish baths and hot tub; In addition, it provides various types of treatments. The hotel offers an evening entertainment program and bicycle rental service.

Iberostar Playa Gaviotas is a 10-minute drive from the town of Morro del Jable. Fuerteventura airport is less than 90 minutes drive from the hotel.
