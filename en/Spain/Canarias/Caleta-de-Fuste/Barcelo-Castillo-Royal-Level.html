Description

Guests can use the facilities of the Barceló Castillo Beach Resort, located next door, which has 3 pool areas and a spa.

The spa comes at an extra cost and includes saltwater pools, hot tubs, sauna, sensation showers, massages and gym.

The suites have a flat-screen satellite TV, a seating area, a kitchenette and a private bathroom.

The complex houses several restaurants serving local and international cuisine. It also offers several bars, including one by the pool, a sports bar and a theater with night shows.

The complex also has multiple sports facilities, such as a gym and a miniature golf course. There are 2 golf courses nearby and water sports can be practiced.
