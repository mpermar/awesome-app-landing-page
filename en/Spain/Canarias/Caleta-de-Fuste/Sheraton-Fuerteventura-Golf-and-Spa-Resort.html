Description

The rooms at the Sheraton Fuerteventura Golf & Spa Resort are spacious and beautifully decorated. They include satellite TV, a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free Wi-Fi.

The hotel has 4 restaurants, including an international buffet restaurant and an Asian restaurant. There are also 3 bars, one of them next to the pool on a wooden terrace.

The Thalasso Hespérides spa has a steam bath, ice igloo and hammam. The hotel houses a tennis court and offers discounts on the green fees of the adjacent golf course.

Fuerteventura Airport is a 10-minute drive from the Sheraton Fuerteventura.
