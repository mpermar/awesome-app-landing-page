Description

The 1-bedroom Castillo Playa bungalows are elegantly decorated with tiled floors and pine wood furniture. They also have a safe, lounge with TV, kitchenette and private bathroom. In addition, there is free Wi-Fi at the reception.

Castillo Playa has a children's play area and a children's pool. Among the facilities of the establishment, it is also worth noting a sauna and a gym. If you need it, you can request laundry services.
