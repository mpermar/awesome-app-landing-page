Description

The hotel rooms have a balcony with views of the mountains, the pool or the sea. They also have air conditioning.

The GF GRAN COSTA ADEJE has 3 swimming pools, surrounded by a solarium and tropical gardens, and a rooftop pool for adults, offering panoramic views of the Costa Adeje and Fañabe beach. There are also 2 exclusive areas for naturists.

The hotel spa has a sauna, hot tub and Turkish bath. The hotel also has a gym, and nearby you can play tennis and miniature golf.

The hotel has a buffet restaurant, 2 à la carte restaurants and 3 bars. In addition, it offers a full entertainment program with games and live shows.

Tenerife South Airport is only 20 km away.
