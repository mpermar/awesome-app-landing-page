Description

Rooms at this 5-star hotel feature a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kitchenette, a dining area and a private bathroom with free toiletries, a bathtub and a hairdryer. All rooms are equipped with a coffee maker, while some have a terrace.

It houses the Starfish à la carte restaurant, which serves a wide variety of international and local food. In addition, the Olivia-Mediterranean Market buffet restaurant offers a wide variety of Mediterranean dishes. A continental breakfast is served in the breakfast area.

The reception staff speaks German and English and provides guests with practical information.

This hotel is 2.8 km from La Pinta Beach and a 20-minute walk from Plaza del Duque Shopping Center. The closest airport in Tenerife Sur is 18 km away.
