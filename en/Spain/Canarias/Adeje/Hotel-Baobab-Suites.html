Description

The suites are bright, modernly decorated in white tones and equipped with large windows, a living-dining area, a flat-screen TV and a kitchenette with an oven, microwave, dishwasher and washing machine. There is free Wi-Fi throughout, and guests receive a welcome package that includes water and a bottle of wine.

The hotel offers 2 breakfast options, buffet or a selection of products served in the room.

Hotel Baobab Suites is a 15-minute walk from the restaurants, bars and shops of downtown Costa Adeje.

The hotel's Activate sports and leisure center organizes sports and activities such as spinning, pilates and beach volleyball, and features paddle and petanque courts.

This complex offers free private parking and is about a 45-minute drive from Teide National Park and only a 20-minute drive from Tenerife South Airport.
