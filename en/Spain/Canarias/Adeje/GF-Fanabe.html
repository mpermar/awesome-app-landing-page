Description

The GF Fañabe has a basketball and mini golf course. The wellness center has a massage service, sauna and hot tub. There are 2 more pools on the roof terrace. There is also a separate area for naturists.

The hotel has a children's club and an entertainment team that organizes day and night activities. There is also a children's pool.

GF Fañabe's restaurant offers a varied buffet with show cooking and regular themed dinners. The casual pool bar serves light meals and cocktails, while live music is available in the lobby bar. Free WiFi is also provided.
