Description

The accommodations, dating from the 18th century, are individually decorated and feature a kitchen with a fridge and coffee maker, a private bathroom and a living room with a sofa bed, satellite TV and a CD and DVD player.

Casa El Morro serves a free buffet breakfast and has barbecue facilities. The shops, bars and restaurants of Yaiza are 1.5 km away.

The complex has a sauna and offers massages, as well as car and bicycle rental. In addition, yoga classes are taught twice a week for a fee.

The beaches of Quemada, El Barquito and El Golfo are 15 minutes away by car. Lanzarote Airport is 16 km away. Free public parking is available nearby.
