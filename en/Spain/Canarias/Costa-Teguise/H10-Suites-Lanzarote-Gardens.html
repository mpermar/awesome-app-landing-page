Description

All suites are equipped with a kitchenette and living room, while the bungalows have a fully equipped kitchen and living room. In addition, both suites and bungalows include a private terrace or balcony.

The hotel has a tennis court. The Daisy club of H10 organizes activities for children between 6 months and 17 years.

The H10's restaurant serves buffet breakfast, lunch and dinner, as well as themed buffets 3 times a week. There is also a lounge bar with music and a poolside bar.
