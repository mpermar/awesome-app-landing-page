Description

The rooms at Be Live Experience Playa La Arena are spacious, brightly decorated and feature air conditioning, satellite TV and a private bathroom.

The establishment has a tennis court, miniature golf and table tennis.

The Be Live Experience Playa La Arena is located in the town of Puerto de Santiago. Teide National Park and Tenerife South Airport are 45 minutes away by car.
