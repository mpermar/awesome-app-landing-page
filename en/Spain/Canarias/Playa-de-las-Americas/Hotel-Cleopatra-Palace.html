Description

Hotel Cleopatra Palace has a fitness center, a spa offering a wide range of health and beauty treatments and a thermal pool area.

Hotel Cleopatra's rooms include a marble bathroom and open onto a balcony or terrace. They also have free Wi-Fi, satellite TV and a minibar.

The Cleopatra Palace Hotel offers a variety of restaurants and cafés, such as the Marco Antonio bar offering natural juices and salads and the sports bar.

In addition, the hotel is part of the Mare Nostrum Resort complex, which includes sports facilities and a children's play area. The Safari Mall, with numerous shops and restaurants, is a 3-minute walk away.

The hotel is 1 km from Las Américas golf course and less than 15 km from Tenerife South Airport.
