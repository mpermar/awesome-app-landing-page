Description

Villa Cortes GL is decorated in the style of Mexican luxury mansions. The rooms include Mexican-style furniture, satellite TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The Cortes has a beach club with restaurant and offers a variety of gourmet options, as well as Mediterranean, Mexican and Bavarian cuisine. It also has 4 bars, one of them beach. The establishment offers a buffet breakfast and half-board includes à la carte dishes.

The hotel's outdoor pool includes a waterfall and children's area. The hotel also has a tennis court and solarium with Balinese sun loungers.

Villa Cortés is just a 5-minute drive from Las Américas Golf Course and a 15-minute drive from Tenerife South Airport.
