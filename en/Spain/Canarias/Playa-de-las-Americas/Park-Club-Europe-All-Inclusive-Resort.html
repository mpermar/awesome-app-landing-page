Description

Rooms at the Europe Park Club are decorated in warm colors and all have a balcony, ceiling fan, satellite TV, seating area and private bathroom.

The restaurant's restaurants serve buffet dinners every night. Guests can also enjoy grilled meals on the terrace of the Gavina restaurant, open seasonally.

The Park Club has a gym and offers a program of activities such as archery and volleyball.

The Europe Park is located in the popular town of Playa de las Américas. The beach is a 7-minute walk away and the center of Los Cristianos is just 2 km away.
