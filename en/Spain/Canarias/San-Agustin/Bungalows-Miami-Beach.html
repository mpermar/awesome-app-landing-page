Description

The Miami Beach Bungalows has an unbeatable location. From the terrace you can smell the sea literally and the sandy beach is a short walk away. The establishment is in Gran Canaria, next to the sea.

In the kitchen of Miami Beach you can prepare food to enjoy it with others, in the purest Spanish style, as among friends or family.

Depending on the occupancy, the Miami Beach Bungalows offers 1 or 2 bedrooms, as well as a living room with an open-plan kitchen. Free Wi-Fi is also available.

Miami Beach Bungalows is just 400 meters from the San Agustin Casino and there is a shopping center nearby.
