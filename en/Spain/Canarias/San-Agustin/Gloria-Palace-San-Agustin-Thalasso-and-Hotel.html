Description

All rooms at the Gloria Palace Hotel San Agustin have a simple design and are equipped with air conditioning. Most of them enjoy sea views.

The Gloria Palace has several outdoor swimming pools for adults and children, gardens and solariums equipped with sun loungers. The use of paddle or tennis courts, the gym and the rooftop pool has an additional cost.

The establishment also houses a bicycle shop where you can buy equipment. It also offers a bicycle loan service.

The property provides a free shuttle bus service to Maspalomas Beach and Playa del Inglés. Gloria Palace Hotel Gran Canaria is 5 km from Maspalomas Golf Course, where guests enjoy special discounts.
