Description

The apartments are simply decorated and feature a seating area with sofas and a flat-screen TV. The kitchenette is equipped with a ceramic hob, coffee maker and toaster.

Todoque Bungalows is a 10-minute walk from the center of Playa del Inglés, which houses shops, bars, restaurants and nightclubs.

The property is 25 minutes 'drive from Gran Canaria Airport and 40 minutes' drive from Las Palmas.
