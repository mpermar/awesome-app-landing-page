Description

The accommodation features a garden, terrace and seating area. The kitchenette is fully equipped with a fridge and utensils. The private bathrooms have a shower and a hairdryer. In addition, the accommodation consists of barbecue, common room and laundry. The room overlooks the sea and the garden.

In the surroundings it is possible to practice various activities, such as cycling, fishing and horse riding. The Cumbre Vieja Natural Park is 8.1 km away. The house is 28 km from La Palma Airport and offers free parking.
