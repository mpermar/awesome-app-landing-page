Description

All apartments at Vista Serena have a private terrace. They also include a bedroom, a living room with sofa bed and a bathroom. The kitchen is equipped with a hob, fridge and microwave. In the living room there is a satellite TV. The complex offers a DVD rental service.

Vista Serena has a restaurant, the Celebration, which serves contemporary cuisine. There is also a pool bar where you can buy a drink and drink it on the terrace.

Maspalomas Golf Club is 1.2 km from Vista Serena and the sand dunes are 2 km away. The Aqualand water park is also 2 km away.
