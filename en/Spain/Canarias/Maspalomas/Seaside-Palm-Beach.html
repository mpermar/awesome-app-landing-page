Description

The interiors of the hotel, designed by Alberto Pinto, are colorful and retro in style. The rooms are air-conditioned, modern and decorated based on 4 possible color combinations. All include satellite TV and a minibar.

Guests can practice yoga, tennis and water gymnastics for free. In addition, the spa has saunas, a saltwater hot tub and a heated saltwater pool.

In winter, guests can eat in the gourmet restaurant or in the Italian Trattoria of Palm Beach, or enjoy a buffet on the terrace. The complex also has a poolside bar and cocktail lounge.
