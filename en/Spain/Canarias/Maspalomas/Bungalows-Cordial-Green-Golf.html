Description

All accommodation at the Cordial Green Bungalows has modern décor with bright colors and a private terrace. They also include a well-equipped kitchenette and a lounge area with a sofa bed and TV.

The Cordial Green complex has a poolside bar and a buffet restaurant with a terrace. It also has a supermarket.

In the center of Maspalomas, located within walking distance, there are many shops, restaurants and bars. Las Palmas Airport is a 20-minute drive from the bungalows.
