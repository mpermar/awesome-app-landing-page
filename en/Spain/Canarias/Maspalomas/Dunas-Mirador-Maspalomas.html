Description

Rooms at Hotel Mirador Maspalomas have tiled floors and light-colored furniture. They are also equipped with a ceiling fan, flat-screen TV with international channels and a balcony or terrace. There is free WiFi throughout the premises.

The Mirador Maspalomas restaurant serves a varied selection of Spanish and international dishes. There is also a poolside bar and a nightclub.

Children can enjoy a separate pool and a club with an entertainment program.

The establishment has a pool table. The wellness center offers beauty treatments and massages.

The hotel is located 3 km from Playa del Inglés and Playa Meloneras, and offers a free shuttle bus service 4 times a day. There are also various facilities for water sports and 2 golf courses, 2 km away.
