Description

The Costa Calero has elegant rooms with air conditioning, decorated in bright colors. All are equipped with satellite TV and a balcony with sea views. The private bathrooms include a hairdryer.

Buffet breakfast and dinner are served in the restaurant. The menu includes varied Canarian cuisine. There are also 3 bars, including a pool bar and another with live entertainment. Only 300 meters from the hotel you will find the port of Puerto Calero and a promenade with bars and restaurants.

The hotel houses the Squalo Diving diving center. The Lanzarote golf club is about 15 minutes away by car. Costa Calero is 12.5 km from Lanzarote Airport and about 25 minutes by car from the Arrecife ferry port, which offers regular services to the island of Gran Canaria.
