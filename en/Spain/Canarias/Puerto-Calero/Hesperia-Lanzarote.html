Description

The Hesperia Lanzarote has several free sports facilities such as tennis courts, paddle tennis, volleyball and squash. The spa comes with a supplement and features a sauna, hot tubs, steam bath, massage service and other treatments.

The hotel serves a buffet breakfast. It also has a children's mini club, games room with ping pong, and diving center.
