Description

The elegant and modern suites of the Atlantis Fuerteventura Resort Hotel Suite feature air conditioning, tiled floors and smart furniture. There is satellite TV, fridge, USB port and private bathroom with hairdryer.

The Suite Hotel Atlantis Fuerteventura Resort has 4 restaurants, of which one is buffet, 2 are themed restaurants and 1 restaurant is exclusively for guests staying in the adults-only Premium club. There are also 6 bars, including a cocktail bar and a cafeteria next to the pool. The adults-only Premium club has a solarium, relaxation area with Balinese beds and sun loungers, and rooms and services only for people over 16 years.

At the Atlantis Fuerteventura there is an amphitheater and entertainment service throughout the day. Activities for children and adolescents are also organized. The establishment offers a supermarket, an exclusive beach club and free shuttle service to the beaches. Fuerteventura airport is about 40 minutes away by car.
