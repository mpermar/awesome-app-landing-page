Description

The units are equipped with a flat-screen TV and a kitchen with a microwave, toaster, fridge, coffee maker and kettle. Towels are provided. There is also free private parking.

In addition, the Corralejo Lodge has an outdoor pool open all year.

This property has a tennis court and billiards and offers bicycle rental. The closest airport to Corralejo Lodge is Fuerteventura, 30 km away.
