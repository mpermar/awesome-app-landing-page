Description

The Atlantis Bahía hotel accommodations are spacious and bright. They are uniquely decorated and have air conditioning, TV, pillow menu and minibar. Some rooms and suites have a seating area or private balcony overlooking the Atlantic Ocean. The rooms include 1 entry per person per day to the hotel spa, the Vital Bay. Free WiFi is provided throughout the premises.

The resort's restaurants offer international cuisine, both à la carte and buffet style, which can be enjoyed on terraces overlooking the sea, between the gardens or by the pool. Among them, is the award-winning restaurant La Cúpula, by Carles Gaig. In addition, there is a bar in the lobby.

The Bahía Vital spa has an area of 3,000 m² and has a hot tub and sauna. It also offers a wide range of treatments, such as massages, wraps and hydrotherapy. The establishment's gym has state-of-the-art equipment and offers fitness classes and personal trainer.

The complex enjoys stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean and the neighboring islands of Lobos and Lanzarote. The dunes of Corralejo are located a few meters from the complex. The hotel beach includes sun loungers and umbrellas. Coco Bahía Chill Out has Balinese beds and personalized services.
