Description

After a day exploring the island, you can return to the Puerto de las Nieves Hotel to enjoy the spa circuit. In the spa, you can enjoy massages that will soothe your aches, under water jets, and relax in the bubbling Jacuzzi, as well as activate your circulation with contrast pools.

Relax with your friends in the cozy lounge, where you can also surf the net for free with the wireless internet connection. Then, go to the restaurant-bar to enjoy a pleasant evening with delicious local cuisine.

Puerto de las Nieves is located in a beautiful coastal town, which offers typical Canarian architecture. The white houses with blue roofs provide a climate of tranquility in the local community. Puerto de las Nieves is located on the ferry route to Tenerife, in case you want to enjoy an excursion to the neighboring island.
