Description

The Ritz-Carlton, Abama is composed of Moorish-inspired buildings with rooms and villas. The complex is located on a cliff, surrounded by lush tropical gardens, with a funicular that reaches the exclusive beach.

The rooms and villas are decorated in an ethnic style and most have views of the gardens and the Atlantic Ocean. All include a luxury marble bathroom and interactive TV with internet connection.

The Ritz-Carlton, Abama has 10 restaurants. The MB, with 2 Michelin stars, serves dishes created by chef Martín Berasategui. The Kabuki, with 1 Michelin star, prepares innovative Japanese cuisine.

There is a large spa with 10 treatment rooms, water circuit and gym. The hotel also has a Ritz Kids for the little ones.
