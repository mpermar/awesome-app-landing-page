Description

The house has a 50-inch flat-screen smart TV, kitchenette with hob, oven and washing machine and 2 bathrooms.

The terrace is equipped with sun loungers, dining table and barbecue area. On the roof of the establishment there is also a terrace overlooking the Atlantic Ocean and the islands of Fuerteventura and Lobos. Private parking for 2 cars is provided.

The accommodation is 300 meters from Pechiguera Lighthouse, about 20 minutes 'drive from the virgin Papagayo beaches, 25 km from Timanfaya National Park and 35 minutes' drive from Lanzarote Airport.
