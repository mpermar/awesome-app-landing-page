Description

The rooms of the complex consist of air conditioning, LED TV with satellite channels, safe, minibar, tea / coffee making facilities, fan and private bathroom with large shower, hairdryer and cosmetic mirror.

The establishment has 4 restaurants serving Ã  la carte and buffet-style dishes. There is also a poolside bar.

In addition, the complex has several swimming pools with waterfalls, children's area and sun terrace with sun loungers. Various activities, such as football, tennis and volleyball, can be practiced at the establishment. There is also a gym that offers yoga, Pilates and Zumba classes. The accommodation also has a spa equipped with different services and treatments.

Elba Lanzarote Royal Village Resort is a 5-minute drive from Playa Blanca, 20 km from Timanfaya National Park and 28 km from Lanzarote Airport.
