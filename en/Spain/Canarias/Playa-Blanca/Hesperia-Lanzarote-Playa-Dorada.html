Description

The rooms have air conditioning, private balcony, satellite TV and private bathroom. The hotel also offers deluxe rooms with exclusive services, such as a reserved area by the pool.

The hotel has 3 restaurants, including a buffet restaurant and a lounge bar with live music. It also has a wellness center equipped with an indoor pool, gym and beauty center.

The Hesperia Lanzarote Playa Dorada has direct access to the promenade of Playa Blanca, full of shops, bars and restaurants.

This hotel is an 8-minute walk from the Rubicon Marina and a 12-minute walk from the center of Playa Blanca.
