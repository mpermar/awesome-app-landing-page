Description

The H10 Lanzarote Princess features an elegant and bright decoration. It also offers air-conditioned rooms with satellite TV and a desk.

The hotel's main restaurant has a buffet and show cooking. The restaurant and bar La Choza is located in the hotel gardens. There are another 2 bars in the complex.

The Princess 24-hour front desk staff provides information about Playa Blanca and the surrounding area. There is free WiFi in the hotel lobby and in some rooms.

The Lanzarote Princess has tennis and volleyball courts, minigolf and giant chess board.
