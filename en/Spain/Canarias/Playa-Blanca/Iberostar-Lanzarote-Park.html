Description

The rooms at the Iberostar Lanzarote Park feature a TV and a bathroom with free toiletries. Star Prestige rooms include beach towels, bathrobe, slippers, Nespresso coffee machine, kettle and free safe.

This family-run hotel offers 4 outdoor swimming pools for adults, 3 children's pools, free gym, solarium with sun loungers and, for a fee, entrance to a spa with sauna, hammam and hot tub.

The property is 20 km from Timanfaya National Park and a 30-minute drive from Lanzarote Airport.
