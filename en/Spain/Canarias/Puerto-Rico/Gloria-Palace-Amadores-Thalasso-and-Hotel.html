Description

The Gloria Palace, one of the largest thalassotherapy centers in Europe, has a seawater pool, massage services and various treatments, sauna, Turkish bath and physiotherapy pool.

The rooms have air conditioning, a private balcony with sea views, and satellite TV. You can rent the minibar and safe and there is free Wi-Fi in the rooms and throughout the hotel.

You can receive tennis training on the lighted tennis court, and there is also a gym, ping pong tables and petanque, archery and dart game facilities.

The establishment includes children's areas, such as swimming pool, playground and mini club, and night shows for adults and children are held.

The Amadores buffet restaurant offers live cooking and theme buffets at night and there are also several bars, such as a piano bar and a pool bar.

Gloria Palace offers free parking, a 24-hour reception and car rental.
