Description

Paradise Park's rooms have air conditioning, a private terrace or balcony, satellite TV and a minibar. There are several types of rooms at the establishment, from classic double rooms to family rooms and junior suites.

Paradise Park Fun Lifestyle Hotel has a free shuttle service to Las Vistas Beach and downtown Los Cristianos. The golf courses of Costa Adeje and Los Palos are a short drive from the hotel

The hotel houses the Sparadise center, which has a sauna, a small fitness center and a Turkish bath. It also offers aerobics classes and different treatments.

The hotel's Tenerife restaurant prepares buffet meals and organizes theme nights.
