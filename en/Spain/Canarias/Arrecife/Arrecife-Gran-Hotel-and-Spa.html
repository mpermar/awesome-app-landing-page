Description

The hotel rooms are spacious and modern and feature wooden floors, large windows, TV and air conditioning. Some rooms have views of the city or the beach.

The hotel spa offers a sauna, a Turkish bath and a hot tub for a fee. In addition, massages and other treatments can be requested.

The Gran Hotel's à la carte restaurant is elegant and offers Mediterranean and international cuisine. It is located on the 17th floor and offers spectacular views of the sea. The hotel also houses several bars, one of them located on the rooftop terrace.

The hotel is the tallest building on the island and enjoys an ideal location in the center of Arrecife. Lanzarote airport is less than 10 minutes away by car.
