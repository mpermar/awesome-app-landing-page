Description

The property also has a beach club with a chill out terrace on Taurito Beach, 800 meters away. During the day there is a free shuttle service to the beach. There is also a panoramic pool for adults and a children's pool.

All rooms have air conditioning, satellite TV with international channels, terrace or balcony overlooking the sea, and refrigerator. The private bathroom includes a bath, shower and hairdryer.

The hotel offers nightly shows and has a disco. Guests also have a 24-hour concierge and concierge service. There is a bar and a small supermarket.

WiFi is available in the lobby and in the internet area for a fee.

Mogan Princess and Beach Club is a 30-minute drive from Gran Canaria Airport.
