Description

It has spacious rooms with elegant décor, air conditioning and views of the Teide volcano or the Atlantic Ocean. The rooms include satellite TV and a private bathroom.

International cuisine is served in the buffet restaurant. In the 2 à la carte restaurants you can taste Mediterranean and Italian dishes. Drinks are served at the poolside bar.

The Bahia Principe San Felipe's spa is equipped with an indoor pool, gym, sauna and hot tub. It also provides relaxing massages and beauty treatments.

The hotel is in the center of Puerto de la Cruz, 25 minutes walk from Playa Jardín. Free parking is available on site. Tenerife North Airport is a 20-minute drive away.
