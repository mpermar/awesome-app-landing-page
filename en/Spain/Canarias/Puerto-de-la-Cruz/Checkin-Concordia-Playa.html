Description

During your stay, you can choose to spend the day at one of the amazing local beaches or relax at the outdoor pool of Concordia Playa. You can also take a dip in the salt waters of Lake Martiánez, one of the works of the famous Canarian artist Cesar Manrique.

To start the day, you can enjoy a delicious breakfast in the stylish and modern restaurant of Concordia Playa, where you can also taste buffets for lunch and dinner, before going out to enjoy the lively nightlife of the city.
