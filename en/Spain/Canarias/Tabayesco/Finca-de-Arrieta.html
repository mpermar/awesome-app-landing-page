Description

In the complex, all rooms are equipped with a terrace overlooking the pool and a private bathroom with shower and free toiletries. Some rooms also have a kitchen with an oven.

Finca de Arrieta offers barbecue and table tennis. The area is ideal for snorkeling and hiking.

Finca de Arrieta is located 30 km from Puerto del Carmen, 46 km from Playa Blanca and 24 km from the nearest Lanzarote Airport.
