Description

Los Cinco Enebros is located between the Sierra de Guadarrama and the Sierra de Gredos, 15 km from the Monastery of El Escorial. It has a seasonal outdoor pool and spa with sauna and massage service.

Rooms at this boutique hotel are individually decorated and enjoy mountain views. All rooms have a flat-screen TV, free Wi-Fi, a minibar and a modern bathroom with bathrobes, slippers and toiletries.

Cinco Enebros houses the Lavanda restaurant, which offers Mediterranean cuisine. Guests can also have a drink at the bar or on the terrace.

Hotel Los Cinco Enebros is located on the outskirts of Robledo de Chavela, 1 hour drive from the center of Madrid. This property offers free private parking. The reception can organize a shuttle service to Madrid Barajas airport.
