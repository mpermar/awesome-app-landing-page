Description

The rooms of the Hotel Rural Torreblanca have air conditioning and wooden floors. All have a flat-screen TV and garden views.

Torreblanca's restaurant serves traditional regional dishes, cooked with seasonal produce. It also offers tapas and drinks on the terrace.

The hotel, which has good access to the A6 and AP6 motorways, is only 15 minutes from the El Escorial palace. The Upper Manzanares Basin Park is also 15 minutes away by car.
