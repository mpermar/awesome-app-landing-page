Description

Hotel Villa Real is located in the Art Triangle, just 150 meters from the Thyssen-Bornemisza Museum. The Puerta del Sol, the Plaza de Cibeles and the Retiro Park are also nearby.

Hotel Villa Real is a classic among luxury hotels in Madrid and is decorated with wood and marble. The art collection includes Roman mosaics and Greek pottery, some from the 3rd century B.C.

The hotel rooms are soundproofed and have a laptop safe and minibar. Some also include a sofa or balcony.

The East 47 restaurant serves creative Mediterranean cuisine, tapas and cocktails.

Guests have free access to the Hotel Urban's swimming pool, open in summer. In addition, pets are allowed.

Centro de Madrid is a great choice for travelers interested in eating tapas, architecture and parks.
