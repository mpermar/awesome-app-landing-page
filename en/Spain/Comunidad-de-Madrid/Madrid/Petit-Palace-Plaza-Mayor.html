Description

The rooms at Petit Palace Plaza Mayor are modern and have a flat-screen TV. An iPad is provided upon request.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning in the breakfast room. In addition, the hotel has free bicycles.

The Petit Palace Plaza Mayor is in the center, 200 meters from the Opera Metro Station, which allows you to reach the Prado Museum and Retiro Park in 5 minutes. In the immediate vicinity there are multiple shops and restaurants.

In front of the hotel is the San Miguel market, where a wide variety of typical Spanish products is offered. Petit Palace Plaza Mayor is 900 meters from Sabatini Gardens and just 5 minutes' walk from Santa María la Real de la Almudena Cathedral.

Centro de Madrid is a great choice for travelers interested in eating tapas, architecture and parks.
