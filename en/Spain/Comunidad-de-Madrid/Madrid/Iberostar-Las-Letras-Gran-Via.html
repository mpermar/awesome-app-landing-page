Description

The Iberostar Las Letras Gran Via is a thematic hotel inspired by literature and houses a well-stocked library. The rooms have high ceilings, large windows and a seating area. They also present a unique decoration with quotes from famous writers and poets.

The hotel restaurant serves haute cuisine in a casual atmosphere. There is also an elegant and quiet lounge bar where you can have breakfast, snacks and tapas throughout the day. In the evening, the lounge bar offers several types of drinks and innovative cocktails.

Iberostar Las Letras Gran Via is on Gran Vía, a 10-minute walk from the Thyssen-Bornemisza Museum and the Prado Museum. Puerta del Sol is 500 meters away, while Gran Vía Metro Station is only 150 meters away.

Centro de Madrid is a great choice for travelers interested in eating tapas, architecture and parks.
