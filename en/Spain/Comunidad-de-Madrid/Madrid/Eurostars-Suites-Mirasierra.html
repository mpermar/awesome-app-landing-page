Description

The Eurostars Suites Mirasierra has a spa and is located in Madrid, 9 km from the city center and 12 km from Madrid-Barajas Airport. It also offers free Wi-Fi and free parking.

The suites at this modern design hotel are spacious, receive plenty of natural light and feature air conditioning, an interactive LCD TV, a seating area with a sofa, a dressing room and a bathroom equipped with a hairdryer, bathrobe and slippers.

The hotel also has a gourmet restaurant, cafeteria and cocktail bar. Room service, tour desk and car rental service are provided. Herrera Oria Metro Station is 600 meters away.
