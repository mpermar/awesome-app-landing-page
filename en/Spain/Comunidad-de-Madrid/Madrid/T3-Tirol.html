Description

Hotel T3 Tirol is the first of the T3 Hotels in Spain, and is located right in front of the El Corte Inglés department store on Princess Street and a short walk from the Plaza de España, the Royal Palace and the University Campus.

Centro de Madrid is a great choice for travelers interested in eating tapas, architecture and parks.
