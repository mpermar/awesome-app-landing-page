Description

The comfortable Meliá Castilla is within walking distance of the Paseo de la Castellana. Featuring classic décor, it offers a seasonal outdoor pool, fitness center and Caroli wellness center.

The L'Albufera restaurant serves a delicious selection of rice and paellas. A buffet breakfast is prepared with a variety of fresh products. There are also other restaurants and bars in the establishment and its surroundings.

The rooms are spacious and feature a pillow menu, free Wi-Fi, private bathroom and air conditioning. This Madrid establishment has a wide variety of family rooms.

Some areas of the hotel are dedicated to the exclusive rooms and services of The Level, which includes private check-in and check-out area, private buffet breakfast, open bar and snacks, 24-hour personalized service and daily cocktails in a private area, as well as a concierge service and organization of activities in the city.

There is also a children's club, open on weekends and holidays, childcare for a fee, children's menu and high chairs.

Cuzco metro station is 200 meters away. The nearby highways allow easy access to the IFEMA, the Municipal Conference Center and the Adolfo Suárez Madrid-Barajas airport. The Santiago Bernabéu stadium of Real Madrid is a 10-minute walk away.

Tetouan is a great choice for travelers interested in monuments, culture and tourist visits.
