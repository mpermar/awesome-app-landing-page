Description

The rooms at Hospes Puerta de Alcalá are elegant and equipped with a flat-screen TV, bathrobe and special facilities.

The Bodyna's spa area has 4 treatment rooms, soundproofing relaxation room, hammam, gym and small relaxation pool. All guests can access the spa.

The Malvar restaurant offers meals, tapas and a large terrace overlooking the Puerta de Alcalá. Guests can also enjoy a drink or relax at the Patio Chill Out. Private parking is available, available for a fee.

Hospes Puerta de Alcalá is an elegant 5-star hotel located in a historic building dating from 1883. The Prado, the Reina Sofía Museum and the Cibeles Fountain are just a short walk away.

Salamanca is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, culture and food.
