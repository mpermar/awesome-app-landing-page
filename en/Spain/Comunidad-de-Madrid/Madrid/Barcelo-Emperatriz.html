Description

All rooms have a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with a professional hairdryer and quality toiletries. GHD straighteners are also offered.

The Barceló Emperatriz has iPads for use in the hotel and offers portable WiFi devices for use in the city.

The hotel restaurant serves Spanish and international cuisine, as well as healthy haute cuisine recipes.

The attentive staff can recommend restaurants, shops and events to guests.

Barceló Emperatriz is 500 meters from Gregorio Marañón Metro Station, 300 meters from the United States Embassy and 10 minutes' drive from Santiago Bernabéu Stadium, the Prado Museum and the Thyssen Museum.

There is private parking 150 meters away. In addition, a valet parking service is available for a fee.

Salamanca is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, culture and food.
