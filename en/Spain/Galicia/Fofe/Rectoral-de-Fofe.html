Description

The rooms in this 18th-century stone building are spacious and have a private bathroom. They also enjoy fantastic views of the valley that surrounds them.

Located near the Tea River and the Sierra del Suído, the Rectoral de Fofe is the ideal place for hiking, horse riding, fishing and bird watching.

At the hotel reception you can rent bicycles. The nearest golf course is only 20 km away.

In the Rectoral restaurant you can taste the delicious local food and have good wines. The hotel has a beautiful terrace where you can drink while enjoying the free Wi-Fi connection.

You can also receive massages and perform reiki therapy sessions. The hotel also has a playground and playground for children, and a quiet library.
