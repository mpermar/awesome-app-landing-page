Description

The accommodation at Playa Mar has a bright and attractive decoration. All apartments have a private bathroom, a spacious living area with an LCD TV and a fully equipped kitchen with a microwave.

This establishment houses both a gym with a varied equipment and a relaxation area with hot tub, sauna and dynamic shower as well as a games room and a children's water park with slides.

Guests can relax on the terrace of Playa Mar and use the barbecue area in the pine forest opposite. The property is 1.5 km from the selection of restaurants and bars in the center of Portonovo and only 2.5 km from the popular seaside town of Sanxenxo.
