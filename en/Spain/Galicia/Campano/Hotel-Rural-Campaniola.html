Description

Hotel Rural Campaniola is 5 km from Pontevedra and offers views of the countryside around Campañó. It has a hot tub and a seasonal outdoor pool in beautiful gardens. The elegant rooms have free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs.

The rooms at Hotel Rural Campaniola feature modern décor and include air conditioning, heating and a private bathroom. Some also have a private terrace.

Campaniola's restaurant serves Galician dishes made with fresh local produce. There is also an elegant bar with a terrace and views of the countryside. The gardens are large and have a children's area.

The surroundings are perfect for hiking, diving and cycling. The Meis golf course is 9 km away.

Campaniola is a 40-minute drive from Santiago de Compostela and about 20 minutes by car from the beaches of Sanxenxo, Chancelas and Lourido. In addition, it is well connected with the AP9 motorway.
