Description

Vilamoure Hotel de Naturaleza, located in Vilamoure, Galicia, 21 km from Orense, has a children's play area and sauna. It also has a restaurant and free private parking.

Some units have mountain or garden views. Some rooms also have a seating area. All rooms have a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bath or shower, a bidet, bathrobes, slippers, a hairdryer and free toiletries. In addition, the Vilamoure Hotel de Naturaleza offers free Wi-Fi throughout.

Guests can practice various activities in the surroundings, such as golf, horse riding and hiking. Vilamoure Hotel de Naturaleza is 40 km from Allariz and 67 km from Mondariz. Vigo Airport is 84 km away.
