Description

The rooms at Casa Grande are spacious and have traditional country-style décor. All also include exposed stone walls, wooden furniture, heating and a flat-screen TV.

The Cristosende's restaurant serves a wide variety of traditional Galician cuisine. In addition, the House has a café-bar with satellite TV, a shared lounge and a traditional-style interior patio.

Casa Grande de Cristosende is less than 1 km from the Sil Canyon, where guests can enjoy outdoor activities such as cycling, hiking and horse riding. The property is located on the route of the monasteries of the Ribeira Sacra and a 5-minute drive from A Teixeira.
