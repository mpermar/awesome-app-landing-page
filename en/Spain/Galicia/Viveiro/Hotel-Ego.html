Description

The rooms at Ego Hotel feature large windows, sea or mountain views, plasma TVs and private bathrooms with hairdryers.

The Ego is home to the Nito restaurant, specializing in fresh seafood, and has windows overlooking the sea.

The hotel has 3 paddle tennis courts and a gym. There is also a playground for children.

Viveiro is a walled medieval town that preserves some of the stone doors of the time. The hotel is 4 km from the historic center of Viveiro.
