Description

The accommodation has a large garden with trees and native vegetation of Galicia, a traditional Galician cereal warehouse and chapel, all surrounded by a forest belonging to the Pazo. It has rustic rooms with beautiful views of the landscape. There is also a library with a fireplace and a pool.

A personalized breakfast is served.

It is located between the beach and the mountain, so you can go scuba diving and hiking.
