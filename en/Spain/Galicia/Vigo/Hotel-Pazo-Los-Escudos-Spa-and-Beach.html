Description

It is in our selection for Vigo.This Galician country house converted into a hotel offers gardens with sea views and direct access to Carril beach. There is free WiFi. Vigo Airport is only 20 minutes away by car.

The luxurious rooms at Hotel Pazo Los Escudos are spacious and have a marble bathroom with a hot tub and free toiletries. Many of the rooms enjoy stunning views of the sea and gardens.

Pazo Los Escudos has a seasonal outdoor pool. The Spa Water Club includes a hot tub, sauna, heated indoor pool, hammam and gym.

The hotel's restaurant, Alcabre, offers a Galician menu. The Pazo’s cafeteria has an outdoor terrace overlooking the estuary and the Cies Islands. In addition, there is a bar with a wide selection of wines.

Guests can take an excursion to the Cies Islands nature reserve from the port of Vigo, take a wine tour or play golf nearby. Samil beach is next to Carril beach.
