Description

The rooms at the Talaso Hotel Louxo La Toja feature magnificent views and include air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, a seating area, satellite TV, a minibar and a safe. They also include a private bathroom with a hairdryer and toiletries.

For an additional fee, guests can access the hotel spa, which is equipped with Turkish baths, sauna, water beds and heated swimming pools. Various beauty and health treatments are also offered.

Ensenada restaurant serves Galician seafood dishes, while the cafeteria serves drinks and snacks.

The reception is open 24 hours. Nearby free parking. The property is 38 km from Pontevedra.
