Description

Casa O Muiño is located in the coastal town of Poio, 3 km from Pontevedra. Privated and free apparcment to your dispposition. The environment is ideal for visiting the Rías Baixas.

All spacious apartments include an oven and a washing machine. They also offer an open plan lounge / kitchen with a fireplace. They also have a dining table, a bathroom and a TV.

Casa O Muiño offers a library and a terrace. There are also gardens with barbecue and garden furniture.

Santiago de Compostela is only 65 km away. The surroundings of O Muiño are ideal for hiking and horse riding. The Cancela River passes near the establishment. Other attractions in the area include the Areas Beach, La Lanzada Beach and the Cíes Islands National Park.
