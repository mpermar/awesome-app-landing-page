Description

This complex is 600 meters from Silgar Beach, in Sanxenxo. It offers elegant rooms with free Wi-Fi and views of the Pontevedra estuary. It also has a large spa and an outdoor pool.

The spa has 3 heated swimming pools (1 for adults only), hot tubs, saunas and a Turkish bath. It also provides massages and beauty treatments, including manicures and pedicures.

Augusta's rooms have flat-screen satellite TV, DVD player, a large terrace, living room and a minibar with free bottles of water. The building has parking and a playground.

The hotel houses the Via Augusta restaurant, which prepares Galician dishes, such as fish specialties, stews and paella. Via Nuvo prepares international dishes based on seafood and vegetables.

Augusta Spa Resort is 250 meters from the center of Sanxenxo and 600 meters from the congress center.
