Description

All bright and modern rooms at Hotel Santiago feature an LCD satellite TV, a minibar and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The establishment also has a heated pool, gardens, reception with free internet area and free covered parking. Garage parking is available upon request.

You can also use the hotel's spa for free, which includes hot tubs, bubble beds and water jets.

Hotel Santiago & Spa's restaurant serves typical Galician dishes, made with fresh produce. There is also a cafeteria.

This hotel is located on the N-540 road, near the Angel Carro stadium and the Pazo fair and exhibition center. The Lugo bus station is 10 minutes away and there are 2 bus lines that stop in front of the hotel.
