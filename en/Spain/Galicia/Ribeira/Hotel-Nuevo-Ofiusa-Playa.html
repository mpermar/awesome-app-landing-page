Description

Situated on Galicias Arousa Estuary, Hotel Ofiusa has a quiet setting, overlooking Playa de Ro Beach.

This hotel has an outdoor swimming pool, restaurant and terrace. The Hotel Ofiusas bedrooms are bright and have simple dcor.

All rooms have a TV, free WiFi, a safe and some have a balcony. There is also a private bathroom with a hairdryer The Hotel Ofiusas restaurant offers a range of traditional, Galician cuisine.

Guests can enjoy a drink in the hotel bar and make use of its terrace with views of the beach.

Other facilities at the property include a reading lounge with panoramic views. Guests can go hiking or on river cruises in the area.

Hotel Ofiusa is 5 km from Ribeira, whilst Pontevedra, Vigo and Santiago de Compostela can all be reached within an hour, by car.
