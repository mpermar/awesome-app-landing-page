Description

The rustic-style bungalows have heating, a porch overlooking the surrounding countryside and an open-plan living area with kitchenette with hob and fridge. The private bathroom has a shower.

Acauremento Caurel's restaurant has a wood-burning oven and offers traditional dishes made with local products. There is also a cafeteria, a playground and free Wi-Fi in public areas.

Several signposted hiking trails can be found in the surrounding countryside of Caurel Camp. The property offers free private parking and is a 70-minute drive from Lugo and 1 hour from Ponferrada.
