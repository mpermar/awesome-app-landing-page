Description

The hotel has a seasonal outdoor pool, surrounded by a sun terrace with sun loungers. Guests also have access to a spa with an indoor pool, hot tub and sauna. Beauty treatments and massage services are offered. There are also paddle and tennis courts.

The Galatea has a restaurant with traditional cuisine and a café that offers light snacks.

The rooms at Hotel Spa Galatea feature air conditioning, a minibar and a private bathroom. There are also rooms adapted for people with reduced mobility.

Bicycle and car rental services can be organized at the tour desk, as well as surfing, sailing and diving courses. Information about the surrounding area is also provided. The property is less than 3 km from the coastal town of Sanxenxo.
