Description

This traditional Galician complex is surrounded by gardens and is located in the center of Sanxenxo. The beaches of Paxariñas, Montalvo and La Lanzada are in the immediate vicinity.

Villa Cabicastro has a heated indoor pool with hydromassage jets and offers a massage service. It also has a restaurant, a large solarium and a bar.

All apartments have heating, flat screen TV and free safe. The kitchen is equipped with a fridge, coffee maker, toaster and microwave.

Children benefit from discounts at the resort's restaurant. The property also houses a children's playground. Free parking is provided within the complex and covered parking for a fee.
