Description

The rooms have a rustic style, are decorated in warm tones and have exposed stone walls. All rooms have heating, satellite TV, a sofa and a private bathroom with free toiletries and bathrobes.

Car and bicycle rental, visits to a local winery and information about the area are provided. There is also a gym and spa approximately 1 km from the Casa do Sear. Massages are available upon request.

Sanxenxo, where there are several restaurants and shops, is a 5-minute drive away. Pontevedra is 20 minutes away by car.
