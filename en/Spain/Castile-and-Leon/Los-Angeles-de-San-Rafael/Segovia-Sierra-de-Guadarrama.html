Description

Located in the Sierra de Guadarrama, Hotel Segovia Sierra de Guadarrama features a private lake and water sports can be arranged for guests.

It also offers access to seasonal swimming pools and 2 golf courses. Hotel Segovia Sierra de Guadarrama is situated 20 km from Segovia, in the Residential Complex of Los Angeles de San Rafael.

Madrid is approximately 45 minutes away by car. The property offers classic-style rooms equipped with air conditioning, cable TV, and a minibar.

They include a private bathroom complete with amenities and a hairdryer. Some feature a private balcony with views of the mountains.

Guests are able to choose from 2 restaurants. The Premier Restaurant serves traditional cuisine, specialising in rice dishes.

The Temple Tavern Snack Bar provides the ideal location to relax with a drink. Room service is also available.

The hotel is situated close to places of cultural interest, such as the El Escorial Monastery, the Royal Glass Factory, the Riofrío Palace and Avila’s medieval walls.
