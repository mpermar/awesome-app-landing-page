Description

This accommodation is 5 minutes walk from the beach. The Gran Castillo Tagoro Family & Fun Playa Blanca offers luxury accommodation with views of the Atlantic Ocean in Playa Blanca. It occupies a Moorish-style building, provides free Wi-Fi throughout and has 11 swimming pools, including a children's pool with slides.

The establishment has a large children's club called Castleland, with different activities for children, childcare and a children's bedroom.

The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV and furnished terrace or balcony overlooking the sea or the surrounding area. Some include a hydromassage shower.

The establishment houses several restaurants, including an international buffet, an adult-only restaurant and restaurants serving Italian and Oriental cuisine. There are also 4 bars in the complex, one of them for sports and another for adults only.

The VitaNova spa features an indoor whirlpool and provides a wide range of treatments. There is also a gym, hairdresser and 5D movie theater. The complex offers different packages of spa treatments for families and couples.

This hotel is a 10-minute walk from the center of Las Coloradas Marina and 20 km from Lanzarote Airport.
