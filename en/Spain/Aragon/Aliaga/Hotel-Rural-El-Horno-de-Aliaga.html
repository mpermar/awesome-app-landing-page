Description

The rooms and suites are bright and feature a flat-screen TV, minibar and safe. The rooms have a private bathroom with a hydromassage shower and the suites include a hydromassage bath. Free toiletries and a hairdryer are also provided. All suites also have a balcony or terrace overlooking the town or the mountains.

The charming El Horno restaurant, with stone walls and a wood-burning stove, serves breakfast, lunch and dinner, including traditional regional dishes made with local olive oil. The beautiful restaurant has stone walls and a wood stove. There is also a cozy living room with a wood-burning stove, TV and board games.

Local products are sold in the house. El Horno de Aliaga is an hour's drive from the Valdelinares ski resort and 70 km from the city of Teruel.
