Description

All 2-bedroom cabins feature air conditioning, TV with DVD player and sofas, plus a bathroom with shower or bathtub. The kitchenette is equipped with a microwave, stove and coffee maker.

The complex has a children's playground and outdoor picnic tables. It has free Wi-Fi in public areas.

Teruel is 35 minutes by car and Valencia, 1 hour and a half.
