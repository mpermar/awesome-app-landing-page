Description

The Aneto hotel has ski storage and fantastic views of the mountains. In addition, the room key can be used as a ski pass in Cerler.

Hotel Aneto's rooms are individually decorated and feature parquet floors, DVD players and elegant, modern décor.

Hotel Aneto's restaurant serves traditional mountain dishes and offers a daily menu. There is also a café bar with panoramic mountain views.

The nature reserve of Posets-Maladeta is just 5 minutes drive from the Aneto hotel, while Huesca and Lleida are about 2.5 hours away by car.

The Aneto hotel offers treatments, massages and discounted admission to a nearby spa.
