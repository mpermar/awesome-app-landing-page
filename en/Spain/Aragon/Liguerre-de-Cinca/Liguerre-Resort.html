Description

The resort houses 2 hotels and apartments. All accommodation has a TV, private bathroom and views of the countryside. The apartments have a small kitchenette. Guests can also eat in the restaurant on weekends.

The Ligüerre Resort has a soccer field. Staff can arrange canoe rentals and rafting and caving excursions. In summer an entertainment program is offered.

Ligüerre Resort is 17 km from Ainsa and a 20-minute drive from the Sierra and Cañones de Guara Nature Reserve. The national park of Ordesa and Monte Perdido is about 30 km away.
