Description

Hotel Castellote's rooms are simply decorated and feature a balcony, air conditioning, free Wi-Fi and a flat-screen TV.

The Castellote has a bar and a restaurant serving Aragonese cuisine.

In the surroundings there is a great variety of hiking trails. The Aliaga Geological Park is 1 hour away by car, while the famous dinosaur remains and the archeological sites of Teruel are 2 hours away by car.

The restaurant is closed on Sunday nights.
