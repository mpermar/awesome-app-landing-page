Description

All rooms at Hotel Viñas de Lárrede have a flat-screen TV. Some units have a seating area and some rooms also have views of the mountains or the pool.

The establishment also has a 24-hour reception, a shared lounge, gift shop and concierge service, as well as a bar and restaurant.

This hotel provides ski equipment rental service and the area is ideal for skiing and golfing. Jaca is 19 km from Hotel Viñas de Lárrede.

Conditions for children and extra beds
Children of all ages can stay.
All children under 12 years are charged EUR 50 per night for extra beds.
All further older children or adults are charged EUR 72 per night for extra beds.
Maximum number of extra beds in the room: 2.
Extra beds are available upon request and must be confirmed by the accommodation.
Supplements are not automatically calculated in the total amount and must be paid separately during the stay.
