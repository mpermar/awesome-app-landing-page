Description

The rooms are comfortable and have central heating, air conditioning, safe and flat screen TV with satellite channels. The private bathroom has a bath or shower, hairdryer and free toiletries.

The wellness center of the Hotel Golf & Spa Real Badaguás Jaca has a heated indoor pool that can be used for a fee. In addition, it offers a variety of beauty and relaxation treatments.

A ski pass can be purchased at the hotel reception for any of the nearby ski resorts, in order to directly access the ski lifts.

The hotel's Los Tilos restaurant serves traditional Aragonese cuisine and local wines. The Marboré bar has a terrace overlooking the golf course and the Pyrenees.

Jaca is a 10-minute drive from Hotel Golf & Spa Real Badaguás Jaca.
