Description

This old monastery is located in the quiet town of Boltaña, near the French border. The hotel has a careful decoration, combining a modern design with original details, such as exquisite stone exteriors and arches.

Enjoy a quiet stay in your quiet and isolated room, with large and comfortable beds and air conditioning.

The huge seasonal outdoor pool, surrounded by gardens, is perfect for a relaxing day, and there is also a fitness center.

In the luxury spa (for a fee) you can enjoy a massage or a Turkish steam bath.

Taste the high quality cuisine in the impressive dining area, with a beautiful vaulted ceiling.

The Monastery of Boltaña Spa, with a good location to visit the beautiful countryside of Huesca, with 2 large natural parks nearby, offers free parking and bicycles for rent.
