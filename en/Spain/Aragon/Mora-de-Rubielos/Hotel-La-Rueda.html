Description

Hotel La Rueda has an inflatable castle and a ball pool for children. There are also ping pong tables, basketball court, tennis court and mountain bike rental service.

The hotel restaurant La Rueda offers roast lamb, among other specialties of traditional Aragonese cuisine. In the bar, snacks and drinks are served.

All rooms at Rueda Hotel, decorated with light wood, have heating and tiled floors. They also include a TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The La Rueda hotel is easily accessible from the A-23 motorway, which connects Valencia with Zaragoza.
