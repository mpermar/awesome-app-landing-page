Description

The hotel has 4 meeting rooms, 54 double rooms, 16 duplexes and 6 junior suites, as well as a spa available for a fee, a gym and indoor and outdoor pool.

The hotel is located in the new residential area of Jaca and offers meeting facilities as well as services to spend a quiet stay.

Experiences

06/13/2017

A quiet hotel with great food , comfort and friendly staff

"The hotel was in a quiet residential area with lovely views from our windows. We really liked the large window which made the room bright and airy. The room was comfortable, as were the beds.It was a 10-15 minute easy walk into the centre of Jaca. There is a large supermarket just around the block, so any bits that we wanted were easy to get - and an added bonus is that they don't close in the middle of the day.We could have had on site underground parking, but there was plenty of space around, so we just parked in the street. The food at the hotel was truly excellent. Breakfast was superb with a lot of choice, and we ate in the restaurant 3 evenings out of four because the food was so good and the price (16-17 euros) was excellent.Staff were very friendly and helpful.The two massages I had were great - thanks Laura. Since the weather was hot and it was a weekend, there were quite a number of noisy children in the two pools, so we avoided the spa area.There are lots of interesting places within an easy drive; Pamplona, Ainsa and the Ordesa national park which was really beautiful.We very much enjoyed our stay there (4 nights) and will go back."
