Description

This accommodation is a 2-minute walk from the beach. The Sol Milanos Pingüinos is located on the beach of Son Bou, which is 2.5 km long. It is well equipped with a large lagoon-style pool and rooms with balconies.

All rooms at Milanos feature striking décor and include air conditioning, satellite TV, a minibar and views of the sea or the garden.

The 2 buffet restaurants at the Pingüinos establishment serve a selection of international food, including Spanish, Italian and Asian. In addition, there is a self-service bar with a terrace next to the pool.

Kathmandu adventure clubs offer entertainment for children from 8 months to 17 years. The 24-hour reception staff can rent cars and provide information about Son Bou, Sant Jaume and its surroundings.
