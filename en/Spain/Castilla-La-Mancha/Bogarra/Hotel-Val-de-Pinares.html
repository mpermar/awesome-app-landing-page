Description

The rooms have a balcony and overlook the spectacular surrounding landscape. They include a private bathroom and TV.

The hotel has an impressive Andalusian interior patio with plants and a central fountain. The lounge of the Val de Pinares Hotel has leather sofas and a large fireplace.

The restaurant serves regional dishes and upon request prepares menus for special diets. The hotel also houses a café bar.

Hotel Val de Pinares is surrounded by pine forests, 7 km from Bogarra, on the road that goes to La Dehesa del Val. It is 1 hour drive from Albacete.
