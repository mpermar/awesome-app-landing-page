Description

The Isis Hostal occupies an area of 6,000 m² and features striking décor. In addition, it has a playground, minizoological and mini golf. Isis also has a sun terrace with sun loungers.

All rooms at the Isis are soundproofed and feature satellite TV, a spacious bathroom with a hairdryer and attractive wooden floors.

The restaurant prepares homemade dishes of Manchego cuisine and snacks. In summer, barbecues are organized at the establishment.

The Isis is located just off the main road CN 220 and a 15-minute drive from the Serranía Baja.
