Description

Aire Turismo Rural has a garden, barbecue area and terrace. Guests can enjoy a variety of activities in and around the property, such as hiking and fishing.

The bungalows have a flat-screen TV, private bathroom with shower, towels and bed linen. All rooms offer views of the mountains.

A 5-minute walk away, you can find several restaurants and supermarkets.
