Description

Hotel Hierbaluisa in Alarcón has 3-star accommodation with a shared lounge, a garden and a terrace.

With a bar, the 3-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom.

The hotel features family rooms. At the hotel the rooms include a wardrobe and a flat-screen TV.

Guests at Hotel Hierbaluisa can enjoy a continental or a à la carte breakfast. Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Alarcón, like hiking.
