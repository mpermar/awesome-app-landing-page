Description

Situated in Consuegra, 38 km from Alcazar de San Juan, "El Patio de los Jazmines" Casa Rural features a seasonal outdoor swimming pool and free WiFi.

Private parking is available on site. All units feature air conditioning and a flat-screen TV.

There is a fully equipped private bathroom with shower and free toiletries. Guests at the country house can enjoy a continental or a buffet breakfast.

El Patio de los Jazmines offers a sun terrace. Guests can also relax in the garden or in the shared lounge area.
