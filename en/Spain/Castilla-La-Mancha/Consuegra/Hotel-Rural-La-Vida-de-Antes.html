Description

The magnificent inner courtyard is flooded with natural light. It will call your attention for its decorative tiles and wooden doors, all typical of traditional local architecture. Throughout the hotel you will find small corners with comfortable seats to rest, read a book or simply admire the pleasant surroundings.

On the hotel terrace you can taste a selection of ice cream, drinks, coffees and pastries. The same terrace houses a permanent exhibition of photographs, paintings and sculptures.
