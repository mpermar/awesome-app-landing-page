Description

Rooms and suites at Hotel Boutique Pinar are located in bungalows and feature a fireplace, flat-screen TV, air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, minibar, kettle and coffee maker.

Breakfast is available in the dining room of the Pinar hotel. There are shops, cafes and restaurants in Cuenca. The hotel spa is available for a fee and features a sauna, hot tub, Turkish bath, hydromassage shower and gym.

The hotel is located about 2 km from the town of Pernala and less than 15 minutes by car from the famous Hanging Houses of Cuenca. The cities of Madrid and Albacete are less than 2 hours away by car.
