Description

The rooms at Cortijo Puente Faco are bright and have rustic décor. They have heating, a seating area and free Wi-Fi.

The Faco Bridge has a terrace, a barbecue area and a lounge with a library and fireplace.

The staff is responsible for organizing different activities, such as horse riding, 4x4 rides, hunting, cycling and hiking. You can also fish in the nearby river.

Elche de la Sierra is 35 minutes away by car. The Faco Bridge is 114 km from Albacete.
