Description

The rooms at Finca El Encinar de Haldudo have tiled floors and are decorated in a rustic way. The living area has a fireplace, sofa bed and TV. The kitchen includes a microwave.

The Encinar de Haldudo offers a restaurant with beautiful wooden beams and dark wooden furniture. It serves innovative and traditional cuisine and has an extensive wine list.

Located in the province of Ciudad Real, Finca El Encinar de Haldudo is a 15-minute walk from the town of Pedro Muñoz.
