Description

This country house features rustic décor and features a fireplace, air conditioning, a seating area with a flat-screen TV, a dining area and a kitchen with a dishwasher, oven and microwave.

The house also has 2 double bedrooms and 2 bedrooms with 2 single beds each. The bathrooms have a shower and a hairdryer.

Casa Rural Santa María del Guadiana also has barbecue facilities. There is a free public paddle tennis court nearby.

Various activities, such as fishing, hiking and canoeing, can be practiced on the premises or in the surroundings. The property has free parking.
