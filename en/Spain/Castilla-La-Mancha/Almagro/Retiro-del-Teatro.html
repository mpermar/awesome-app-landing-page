Description

Set in Almagro, Retiro del Teatro offers accommodation with free WiFi and flat-screen TV, as well as a terrace.

Units are fitted with a well-equipped kitchen with a dining area, and a private bathroom with a free toiletries and shower.

A microwave and coffee machine are also available, as well as air conditioning. The Retiro del Teatro, offers a complimentary breakfast, the first day only.

Bicycles are available for guests. Ciudad Real is 22 km from the apartment, while Valdepeas is 32 km from the property.
