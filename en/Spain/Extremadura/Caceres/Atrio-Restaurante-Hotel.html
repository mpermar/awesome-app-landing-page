Description

Its elegant rooms are air-conditioned and soundproof and air-conditioned. All have a minibar, safe and flat-screen TV. They also have a private bathroom with a hairdryer, toiletries, bathrobe and slippers. The suites also have a living room.

Drinks and snacks are served in the Atrio café bar. The hotel also has room service and has a garden terrace.

The reception is open 24 hours a day and there is a tour desk that offers car rental. The property offers an airport shuttle service for a fee. The Norba golf club is 15 minutes away by car.
