Description

Each apartment has air conditioning, a living room with Ibiza-style beds and a flat-screen TV. There is central heating, furnished balcony and private bathroom with bathtub, hairdryer and toiletries. The kitchen is equipped with a fridge, microwave, stove, toaster and coffee maker.

A continental breakfast is served in the Del Apartamentos Rurales El Canchal de la Gallina cafeteria. There is also takeaway service.

The property offers free parking and you can rent a bicycle at the tour desk. The A66 motorway is 2 minutes away and Salamanca is 1 hour away by car.
