Description

All apartments have a furnished porch and a living room with a TV and a sofa or a sofa bed, and a kitchen equipped with a fridge, microwave and coffee maker. The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries. Some apartments have a fireplace, air conditioning or hydromassage shower.

The complex has a bar and a cafeteria, where breakfast is served. There is room service, barbecue facilities and laundry and massage services. The hotel also has a tour desk and is well connected to the A66 motorway.

Hervás is famous for its charming Jewish Quarter, located 2 km from the establishment. The La Covatilla ski resort is a 45-minute drive away and a beautiful chestnut forest is a short distance away.

Número máximo de camas supletorias en la habitación: 2.
Las camas supletorias y/o cunas están disponibles bajo petición y deben ser confirmadas por el alojamiento.
Los suplementos no se calculan automáticamente en el importe total y deben pagarse por separado durante la estancia.
