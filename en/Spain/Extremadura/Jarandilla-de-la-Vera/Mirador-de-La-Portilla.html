Description

Mirador de la Portilla is located just 800 meters from the town of Jarandilla de la Vera and is surrounded by oaks and chestnut trees. The Jaranda River is nearby and offers natural pools.

The rooms are spacious, decorated in light tones and feature heating and flat-screen TVs. There is a bathroom with toiletries and a hairdryer.

The Mirador's restaurant, San Valentín, specializes in grilled meats and other regional dishes. In summer, you can eat on the garden terrace. There is also a tapas bar and an elegant lounge.

Monfragüe National Park is located 1 hour's drive from Mirador de la Portilla and the city of Plasencia, 45 km away.
