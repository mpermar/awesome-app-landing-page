Description

The hotel and the spa form a complex surrounded by 60,000 m² of gardens and vegetation. The Raposo also offers a swimming pool (open in summer), a reading and games room, a chapel and function rooms to host celebrations. Book a room and you can enjoy a natural environment and therapeutic treatments.
