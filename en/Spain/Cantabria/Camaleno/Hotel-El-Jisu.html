Description

This attractive house is in the town of Camaleño. It offers stunning views of the surroundings. The rooms have heating, bathroom, desk and TV.

El Jisu's restaurant serves comical homemade Cantabrian. There is also a large terrace and a comfortable reading room.

Private parking available at El Jisu. The coast is a short drive away, while the Natural Park of the Picos de Europa is only a few minutes away.
