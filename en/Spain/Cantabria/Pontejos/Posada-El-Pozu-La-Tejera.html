Description

Situated in Pontejos, Posada El Pozu La Tejera features accommodation with private balconies.

The inn has a terrace and sea views, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

All rooms at the inn are fitted with a seating area. The shared bathroom is fitted with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

At Posada El Pozu La Tejera the rooms include a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, and a patio with a city view.

The reception at the accommodation can provide tips on the area. Santander is 12 km from Posada El Pozu La Tejera, while Santillana del Mar is 34 km from the property.
