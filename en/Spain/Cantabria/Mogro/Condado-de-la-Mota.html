Description

Offering a garden and a terrace Condado de la Mota is housed in a 17th-century building.

Located in Pas Bay area in Mogro, it is 2 km from Cantabria’s Pueblo del Sol Beach.

All rooms include a TV. The bathroom comes with a shower. Free Wi-Fi is offered in public areas.

Condado de la Mota is a 10-minute drive from Santander. Abra del Pas Golf Course is 1 km away.
