Description

All bungalows have heating, 2 bedrooms and 1 living room with a sofa bed and flat-screen satellite TV. There is also 1 private bathroom and 1 kitchenette and dining area with fridge, microwave and coffee maker.

The Costa San Juan complex has its own restaurant and bar, where guests can enjoy a meal or a drink.

An airport shuttle service is available for a fee. There is easy access to the A67 motorway and you can reach Cabárceno or Santillana del Mar in 20 minutes by car.
