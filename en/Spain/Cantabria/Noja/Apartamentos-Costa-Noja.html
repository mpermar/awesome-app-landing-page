Description

The Costa Noja apartments have a living room with a sofa bed and satellite TV, as well as a bathroom with a hairdryer. The kitchen is equipped with a hob, microwave, coffee maker and washing machine.

The reception staff can provide tourist information about Cantabria. Santander airport and ferry terminal are about 40 minutes away by car and Bilbao is approximately 1 hour away by car.
