Description

The hotel is a converted 18th-century noble house, decorated in a traditional and elegant style. Its facilities have wooden beams and stone walls.

All rooms at Trasvilla have heating, a flat-screen TV, wooden floors and views of the countryside. The private bathrooms include a hairdryer, bathrobe and slippers.

The Palace has a café-bar. There is also a lounge where you can relax in front of the fireplace.

The hotel is surrounded by nature. You may see foxes or badgers. In the area there are also vultures and hawks.
