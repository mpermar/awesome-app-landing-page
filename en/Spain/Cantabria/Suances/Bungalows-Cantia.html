Description

All accommodations have a seating area. Some have a dining area and / or terrace. The kitchen includes a microwave, refrigerator, stove and coffee maker. The bathroom is private and has a bath or shower. Towels and bed linen are also provided.

The Cantia Bungalows are located 5 km from Santillana del Mar and 41 km from Isla. The nearest airport is Santander Airport, located 19 km from the Cantia Bungalows.
