Description

Some types of accommodation have a seating area. Some rooms also have views of the mountains, the lake or the pool. All rooms have a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer. In addition, La Fuentona de Santillana offers free WiFi throughout.

The reception is open 24 hours.

The nearest airport is Santander Airport, 23 km from La Fuentona de Santillana.

Conditions for children and extra beds
Children of all ages can stay.
One child under 12 years is charged EUR 25 per night when using existing beds.
All children under 12 years are charged EUR 25 per night for extra beds.
All further older children or adults are charged EUR 36 per night for extra beds.
Maximum number of extra beds in the room: 2.
Extra beds are available upon request and must be confirmed by the accommodation.
Supplements are not automatically calculated in the total amount and must be paid separately during the stay.
