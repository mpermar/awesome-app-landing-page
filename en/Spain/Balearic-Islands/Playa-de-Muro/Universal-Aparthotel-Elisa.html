Description

Universal Aparthotel Elisa is near the S’Albufera National Park and 400 metres from Alcudia's beaches.

All units have a private balcony and there is an outdoor pool, sun terraces and gardens.

All have free WiFi and all a well-equipped kitchen area. Studios and apartments are air-conditioned and spacious.

They have a comfortable living area with a flat-screen TV. The property has a bar and an elegant restaurant, with views of a nearby lake.

The surrounding area is ideal for walking or cycling. Guests can rent bicycles from the hotel.
