Description

Facing the beachfront, TUI FAMILY LIFE Alcudia Pins offers 3-star accommodation in Playa de Muro and features a restaurant, bar and garden.

Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a children's playground.

The hotel offers an outdoor swimming pool, fitness centre, evening entertainment and a 24-hour front desk.

The hotel offers a continental or buffet breakfast. TUI FAMILY LIFE Alcudia Pins offers 3-star accommodation with a hot tub and sun terrace.

You can play table tennis at the accommodation, and bike hire and car hire are available.

Alcudia is 10 km from TUI FAMILY LIFE Alcudia Pins, while Cala Millor is 39 km away.
