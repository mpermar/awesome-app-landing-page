Description

Zafiro Palmanova is located 200 metres from the beach in Palma Nova in Mallorca. It offers a spa and 3 outdoor pools with loungers, Balinese sun beds and a waterslide.

All apartments at the Zafiro Palmanova Hotel are air-conditioned and have a private balcony.

The kitchenettes are equipped with fridge, microwave and kettle. There is also a TV in each apartment.

Between May and October the property organises a varied entertainment programme, including sports and a mini-disco.

There is a children’s pool with a waterslide and a fountain. The Zafiro Palmanova offers buffet and à la carte restaurants, as well as a pool-side snack bar.

There is also an on-site supermarket. Palma Nova is 20 km from Palma, Mallorca’s capital.
