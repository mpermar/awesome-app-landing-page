Description

All apartments at RuralSuite Hotel Apartamentos include a kitchen equipped with a dishwasher, induction cooker and coffee maker. They also have a bathroom with hydromassage shower with chromotherapy and steam treatment.

The hotel cafeteria serves breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks. There is a terrace surrounded by pine trees.

The RuralSuite Hotel Apartamentos organizes various activities, such as quads and bicycles, and includes a children's play area.

The Bardenas Biosphere Reserve and the Moncayo Nature Reserve are 20 km away. The RuralSuite is just 3 minutes drive from the AP-68 motorway, which connects Bilbao and Zaragoza.
