Description

The rooms have double beds and bunk beds, and have mountain views. All have private bathrooms with showers. The bathrooms can be both inside and outside the room. The outdoor bathrooms are adapted for people with reduced mobility.

The establishment has a restaurant with a terrace and offers dishes made with local products. Guests can learn to prepare pacharan, a traditional Basque liquor, or make corn cakes. The restaurant offers à la carte dishes and daily menus.

The surroundings are perfect for hiking and excursions. In addition, the Matxitxulo organizes a wide variety of activities and guided excursions on foot through the mountains and along the riverbeds. The natural reserve of Señorío de Bertiz is 25 minutes away by car.

The property is 15 km from the A-15 motorway and a 45-minute drive from the town of San Sebastián and its beautiful beaches.
