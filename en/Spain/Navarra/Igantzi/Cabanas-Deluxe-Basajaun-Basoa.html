Description

Each apartment, with a private terrace and mountain views, has a living room with a sofa, dining table and TV. The kitchen is equipped with a microwave and cooking utensils. Some units offer a dishwasher. The bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries.

A wide variety of outdoor activities can be done in the park, such as rappelling, mountain biking and bungee jumping.

The property is 50 minutes' drive from San Sebastián and approximately 1 hour's drive from Pamplona.
