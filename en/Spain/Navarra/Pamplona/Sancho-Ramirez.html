Description

This accommodation offers great views of Yamaguchi Park. The rooms, decorated in warm tones, are very bright and have wooden floors and satellite TV, as well as desks and minibars.

Guests can access for free a gym located 50 meters from the hotel. Car rental and tourist information about the area are available at the 24-hour reception.

The Sancho Ramirez hotel restaurant, La Barrica del Sancho, serves traditional Basque and Navarre cuisine, and tapas and stews can be enjoyed in the cafeteria. There is also a lounge with plasma TV and board games.

There are a lot of shops, restaurants and bars within a 5-minute walk of the Sancho Ramirez hotel, and the Pamplona planetarium is right in front.
