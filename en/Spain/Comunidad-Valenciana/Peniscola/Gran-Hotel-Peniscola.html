Description

Rooms at the Gran Hotel Peñiscola are air-conditioned and have a balcony. The property has self-service washers and dryers.

The spa area includes a heated pool, sauna, steam room and hot tubs, available for a fee. Various wellness and beauty treatments are also provided for a fee.

The Gran Hotel Peñiscola has a buffet restaurant with show cooking. The restaurant at the Gran Hotel Peñiscola also serves Mediterranean cuisine. There is also a cafeteria and live shows are organized at night.

The historic center of Peñíscola is located 3 km away. Gran Hotel Peñiscola is a 45-minute drive from the Delta del Ebro Natural Park and 11 km from the Serra d'Irta Natural Park. Castellón Airport is 36 km away.
