Description

The rooms feature air conditioning, a balcony, a minibar, a payment safe, a flat-screen TV and a bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

The hotel restaurant serves buffet-style meals and prepares Mediterranean dishes. There is also a terrace overlooking the city.

RH Don Carlos is a 15-minute walk from Peñíscola Castle.
