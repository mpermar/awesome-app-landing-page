Description

The accommodation is equipped with a kitchen, dining area, flat-screen TV and free private parking.

The establishment has a tennis court, and the area is perfect for playing golf and practicing activities such as windsurfing and diving. Apartamento Marjal is 41 km from Altea and 16 km from Alicante. The nearest airport is Alicante, 16 km from the establishment.
