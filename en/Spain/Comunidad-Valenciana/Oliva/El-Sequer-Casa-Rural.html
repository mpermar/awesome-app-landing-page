Description

All rooms at this hotel have air conditioning and a flat-screen TV. Some rooms have a seating area and others a terrace or balcony. The rooms include a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

The hotel has a shared lounge and bicycle rental service.

Sequer Casa Rural is 41 km from Benidorm, 8 km from Gandia and 71 km from Valencia Airport, the nearest.
