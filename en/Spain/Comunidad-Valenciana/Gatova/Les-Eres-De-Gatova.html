Description

The units feature a flat-screen TV and a kitchen equipped with an oven, microwave, fridge and coffee maker. They also have a private bathroom with a bidet, shower and hairdryer. Bedding is provided.

Les Eres De Gatova also has a seasonal outdoor pool.

Several activities can be practiced in the area, such as cycling and hiking. Les Eres De Gatova is 48 km from Castellón de la Plana. The nearest airport is Valencia Airport, 31 km from Les Eres De Gatova.
