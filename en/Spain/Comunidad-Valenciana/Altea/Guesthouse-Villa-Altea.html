Description

Rooms and studios at Guesthouse Villa Altea are colorfully decorated and feature free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, a minibar and a private bathroom with free toiletries.

The studios also include a flat-screen TV and a kitchenette equipped with a Smeg microwave, washing machine, hob, toaster and kettle.

In addition, Villa Altea has a dining area where breakfast is served, and a spacious lounge with leather sofas, TV and reading area.

Villa Altea is surrounded by fruit trees and a beautiful countryside. The area is ideal to enjoy activities such as hiking and cycling.

Villa Altea Guesthouse is 20 km from the towns of Calpe, Polop and La Nucia. Denia, Benidorm, Finestrat and the nearest large supermarket are 20 minutes away by car. Alicante is 50 km away.
