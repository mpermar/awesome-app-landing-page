Description

The rooms at SH Villa Gadea feature a flat-screen satellite TV, hot tub, pillow menu, heating and air conditioning. Most have a bathroom with a separate shower.

Guests can enjoy the counter-current pools, the Vichy showers and the Turkish bath at the Salux SH Villa Gadea Thalasso Spa. Villa Gadea Altea also provides a wide variety of massages, wraps and treatments. You can check the spa schedule at the reception.

The Kitsume restaurant at SH Villa Gadea serves Japanese dishes and the Miramar restaurant serves local specialties. There is also a buffet restaurant, a lobby bar with live music and a summer terrace with barbecue facilities and sea views. Half board packages include breakfast and dinner.
