Description

This accommodation is 5 minutes walk from the beach. This modern and functional 3-star hotel enjoys exclusive views of the Mediterranean Sea and is located in the Marina d'Or holiday complex, facing the sea.

The Marina d'Or holiday complex is the largest in Europe and houses several hotels, apartments and state-of-the-art facilities and is surrounded by lush nature, such as lakes, waterfalls, fountains, exotic vegetation and 10.1 hectares of beautiful landscaped gardens .

The establishment's seawater spa is a unique opportunity to immerse yourself in a world of authentic relaxation and tranquility. There is also a medical center specializing in modern beauty and wellness techniques. The complex houses a children's theme park and a sports area with tennis courts, soccer fields, basketball, squash and bowling, among others.

The Marina D'Or spa provides various treatments. An evening entertainment program and a selection of bars and cafes are also offered.
