Description

The rooms are spacious, modernly decorated and air conditioned.

The establishment has a spa, beauty center, sports area with tennis, basketball and squash courts, 5 playgrounds and an entertainment program for children during the summer.

The hotel restaurant serves buffet meals with free drinks. In addition, a show with music is organized every night and there are frequent events and parties on the street.

The Grand Duke is well connected with Benicàssim, which hosts the famous music festival. It is also close to the AP7 motorway and a 55-minute drive from Valencia.
