Description

The rooms are decorated in warm colors and feature air conditioning, views of the mountains and gardens, a flat-screen TV, free Wi-Fi, a fridge and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Caseta Nova also has a seating area with a fireplace, a billiard room and a restaurant with elegant dining rooms, specializing in rice. In Ibi you will also find several restaurants and shops.

The Caseta Nova Hotel has free private parking and is well connected to the A7 motorway, which connects with Alicante in 35 minutes.
