Description

All accommodations include a flat-screen TV and some have a seating area and / or terrace. There is also a dining room and a kitchen equipped with a microwave. Toaster, fridge and coffee maker are also offered. Each accommodation has a private bathroom with shower and hairdryer. Towels and bed linen are provided.

Camping El Torres has a playground, a solarium, a restaurant. Facilities include a small supermarket.
