Description

The rooms at the Bayren hotel are spacious, modernly decorated and have a private terrace. Almost all have front or side views of the sea. Accommodations also include a flat-screen TV, minibar and safe.

Mare Nostrum Restaurant has a terrace overlooking the sea and offers a buffet of Valencian and international dishes. There is also a relaxed bar opposite the beach, where drinks are served.

The spa area has an indoor pool, flotation tanks, Scottish shower, steam bath, sauna and treatment room. In addition, there is a second pool, a hot tub and outdoor water beds overlooking the sea.

The RH Bayren Hotel & Spa offers a daily entertainment program. There is also a children's club available in summer.
