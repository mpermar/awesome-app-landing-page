Description

The hotel is a 3-minute drive from the beaches of Valencia. It is just 2 km from the city center, which houses the impressive City of Arts and Sciences.

The Mozaira restaurant serves typical cuisine from eastern Valencia. Guests can collect fruits and vegetables and ask to be cooked in the restaurant. In addition, the hotel has its own wine cellar. Room service is also provided.

There are laundry, ironing and dry cleaning services. Bicycles and cars can be rented at the tour desk. The hotel has a solarium with a garden and a library.
