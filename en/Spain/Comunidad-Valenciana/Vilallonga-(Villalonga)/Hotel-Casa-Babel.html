Description

The rooms at Hotel Casa Babel are modern and bright and feature rustic elements such as exposed stone walls and ceiling beams. All rooms have a balcony with views of the surrounding countryside, free Wi-Fi, air conditioning and a TV.

The beautiful La Huerta restaurant overlooks an interior courtyard and is decorated with stained glass windows from the 18th century. The establishment also has a library and terrace with views of the mountains.

Hotel Casa Babel offers a bicycle rental service and there are several cycling and hiking routes in the Safor. The Marjal de Pego-Oliva Natural Park is about 20 km away.

The Babel's spa offers a hot tub and massage service. The property also houses a lounge where guests can enjoy tea or coffee.

The city of Gandía, which has a train station, is a 20-minute drive away. Valencia is about 80 km away.
