Description

All rooms have TV. Some rooms have a seating area. Some rooms have mountain or pool views. They also have a private bathroom.

The reception is open 24 hours.

The hotel has table tennis and pool tables. Complejo Turistico la Pinada is 38 km from Cheste, 42 km from Castellón de la Plana and 24 km from Valencia Airport.
