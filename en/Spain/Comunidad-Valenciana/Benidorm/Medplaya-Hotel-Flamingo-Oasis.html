Description

Benidorm is a modern city composed of skyscrapers and large avenues, located in the province of Alicante forming part of the Community of Valencia and on the shore of the Mediterranean Sea. This privileged situation gives it a special microclimate, which offers visitors very pleasant temperatures throughout the year.

Located in the new part of this city is the Hotel Flamingo Oasis, an all-inclusive 4-star hotel that has 398 comfortable rooms and many facilities to make your vacation as fun and entertaining as possible.
