Description

The Regente Hotel has a beautiful and simple decoration. All rooms have air conditioning and TV.

The hotel restaurant offers a buffet-style cuisine and varied Mediterranean dishes. Guests of the Medplaya can also eat on the terrace.

This hotel is less than 4 km from the nearest golf club and just a 5-minute walk from the center of Benidorm, where there are numerous restaurants, bars and cafés.
