Description

Las Palmeras complex is located between several mountain nature reserves, just 20 minutes from the beautiful beaches of the Costa Blanca. This quiet Mediterranean property has an outdoor pool. The complex also has an outdoor whirlpool, where you can relax under the shade of the abundant palm trees.

The resort's restaurant serves a delicious selection of traditional Mediterranean cuisine in an elegant atmosphere. To accompany the meal, you can choose from the extensive menu of good wines offered by the complex's winery.
