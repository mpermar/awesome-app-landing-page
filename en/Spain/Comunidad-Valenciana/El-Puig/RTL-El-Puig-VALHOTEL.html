Description

The rooms have air conditioning, heating, tiled floors, wooden furniture, TV, desk, closet and private bathroom.

In the El Puig complex there is a cafeteria, a 24-hour reception, a playground and a games room and a laundry service and on-site parking are provided for a small daily fee.
