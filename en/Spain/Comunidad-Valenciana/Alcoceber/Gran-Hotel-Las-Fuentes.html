Description

All rooms at Gran Hotel Las Fuentes have air conditioning, a balcony, 2 double beds, a flat-screen TV and a safe for a fee.

Las Fuentes offers large gardens, 4 swimming pools and 30 free minutes per day for the spa area, which includes a sauna, Turkish baths and gym.

There is a buffet restaurant and a café with terrace and sea views. In addition, the hotel has a children's club with activities throughout the year.

The Gran Hotel Las Fuentes is close to shops, restaurants and the natural park of the Sierra de Irta, and about 15 km from Peñíscola, the nearest city.
