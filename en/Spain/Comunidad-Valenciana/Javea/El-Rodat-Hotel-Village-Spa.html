Description

Its beautifully decorated rooms have a private terrace, air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV and a bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

The Rodat’s Wellness Spa, available for a fee, has an indoor pool with chromotherapy, contrast showers, a Finnish sauna and a variety of treatments, including Ayurvedic massages.

The Mimbre restaurant offers typical Valencian dishes with an innovative touch. It also serves a wide assortment of rice dishes and houses a cellar.

It is just 5 minutes drive from the Arenal beach and 10 minutes from the center of Javea.
