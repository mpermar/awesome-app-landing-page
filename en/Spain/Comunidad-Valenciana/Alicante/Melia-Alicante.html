Description

The rooms at Meliá Alicante are spacious and have air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a minibar and a safe, as well as a private and modern bathroom with toiletries and a hairdryer.

Terra, the hotel's restaurant, specializes in rice and fresh fish. There is also a cafeteria next to the pool and an elegant lounge bar.

For an additional fee, Meliá Alicante offers direct access to the wellness center, located in the hotel building. Guests enjoy discounts on spa facilities.

The hotel also offers The Level personalized service in some of the hotel rooms. This exclusive service for adults includes a range of superior services and facilities, such as private reception, express check-in and check-out, access to a private pool with magnificent sea views, access to the open bar with free drinks and snacks, etc.

The hotel is located opposite the Casino of Alicante, 50 meters from the yacht club and the marina and 800 meters from the Santa Bárbara Castle and the Archaeological Museum.
