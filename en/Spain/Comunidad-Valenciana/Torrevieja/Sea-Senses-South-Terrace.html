Description

The apartment has 2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, a living room, a flat-screen TV and a kitchen with a dishwasher, oven, coffee maker and kettle. Other facilities at Sea Senses include a shared garden with an outdoor pool, fitness center, hot tub and Turkish bath. The apartment offers free private parking.

The establishment has tennis and basketball courts, table tennis and soccer and miniature golf courses. Nearby you can do activities such as fishing and hiking.

Murcia-San Javier Airport, the nearest airport, is 21 km away.
