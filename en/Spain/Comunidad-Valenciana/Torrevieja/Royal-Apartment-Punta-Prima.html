Description

The apartments include air conditioning, a terrace, a living room with a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen and a washing machine. Free Wi-Fi is provided in public areas.

The Royal Apartment Punta Prima has a fitness center overlooking the gardens and the sea, as well as 2 saunas, one Turkish and one Finnish. In addition, it has 2 Balinese beds on the terrace.

The Royal Apartment Punta Prima has a paddle tennis court, a soccer field, a basketball court and a children's playground.

The nearest airport is Murcia-San Javier, located 21 km away.
