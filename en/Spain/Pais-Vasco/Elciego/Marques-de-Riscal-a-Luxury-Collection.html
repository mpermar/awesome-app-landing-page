Description

Most of the exclusive rooms of the Hotel Marqués de Riscal have distinctive zigzag windows overlooking the valley. The black marble bathroom is fully equipped and includes a hairdryer.

The Vinothérapie Caudalie Marqués de Riscal spa offers several grape-based treatments. It also has an indoor pool, hammam, Finnish sauna, fitness center and massage service.

The Marqués de Riscal restaurant proposes dishes from the cuisine of northern Spain, while the 1860 Tradition restaurant prepares typical Spanish dishes with a modern twist and local products. Breakfast is served as a tasting menu.

At the top of the hotel, there is a library with a rooftop seating area and wine bar. In addition, you can enjoy the panoramic views from any of the 8 terraces.

The hotel organizes wine tours to the Marqués de Riscal vineyards and the Vivanco Museum, the largest wine museum in Europe. The Hotel Marqués de Riscal is easily accessed from the AP-68 motorway that connects Bilbao with Zaragoza.
