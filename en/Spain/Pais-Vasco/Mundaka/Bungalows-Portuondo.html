Description

All Portuondo bungalows include a furnished terrace with barbecue, TV and private bathroom. They also have heating and a kitchen with microwave and utensils. WiFi is available in public areas, available for a fee.

Bungalows Portuondo has 2 restaurants, one serving fresh fish and another that prepares menus and prepares meals. In addition, there is a bar overlooking the estuary.

The Mundaka area is ideal for surfing, fishing and hiking. Bungalows Portuondo provides free parking.
