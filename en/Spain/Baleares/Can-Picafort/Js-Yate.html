Description

Js Yate enjoys a quiet location in an area full of shops, a few steps from the town. In addition, you can visit the natural park of S'Albufera.

The hotel is surrounded by gardens with sun loungers and has a children's playground.

The rooms feature air conditioning, heating, satellite TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer. A fridge and safe are available for a fee.

Js Yate's restaurant offers magnificent views of the garden and offers a buffet and show cooking. You can also have snacks on the outdoor terrace.

This is a seasonal hotel, open from May to October.
