Description

The rooms at Bahía Príncipe Coral Playa are bright and feature air conditioning, a minibar and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. In addition, a safe is available for a fee. Many rooms have a private terrace and enjoy sea views. WiFi is provided for a fee.

The Bahia buffet restaurant serves international food. There is also a poolside bar.

Coral Playa is located a 5-minute walk from the center of Magalluf, 14 km from Palma and 25 km from the airport.
