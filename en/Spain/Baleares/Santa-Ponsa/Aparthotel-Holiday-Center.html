Description

Each of the small apartment blocks of the Holiday Center Aparthotel has a Mediterranean style. They are located around a central garden area with a pool where children can play. WiFi is available for a fee.

The Holiday Center Aparthotel has a poolside bar and a buffet restaurant serving breakfast, lunch and dinner. The complex offers several options, from independent accommodation to all-inclusive rates.

It is less than a 10-minute drive from the Santa Ponsa Golf Club and 400 meters from the center of Santa Ponsa. The center of Palma is only 20 minutes away by car. Palma Airport is 30 km away.
