Description

The hotel has free Wi-Fi and direct access to the beach, where there are free sun beds.

All suites are bright and have air conditioning, satellite TV and terrace. Most enjoy views of the sea. They also have a minibar, central heating and a desk.

There is a café, bar, TV room and restaurant with air conditioning. Guests staying at the hotel for at least 7 days on a half-board or full-board basis will enjoy dinner at the Nuka restaurant.

There is a pool with a terrace, sun loungers, umbrellas and a bar.

Fergus Style Cala Blanca Suites is located about 5 km from the Western Water Park and Aqualand water parks. The center of Palma de Mallorca is 16 km away.
