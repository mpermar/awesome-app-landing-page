Description

Jutlandia Family Resort apartments feature Mediterranean-style dÃ©cor and include tiled floors. They also have a private bathroom and a kitchenette with a microwave and coffee maker. There is free Wi-Fi in the lobby.

The Jutlandia Family Resort has a varied entertainment program for adults and children. It also has a playground, pool table and ping pong.

The complex's cafeteria serves international dishes. There is also a pool bar, another bar with an outdoor terrace and vending machines for snacks and drinks.

The apartments are just 650 meters from the Santa Ponsa Golf Club, and a 20-minute drive from the center and Palma Airport.
