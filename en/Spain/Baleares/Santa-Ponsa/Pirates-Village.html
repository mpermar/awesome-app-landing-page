Description

The Pirates Village has several swimming pools with slides, games room and cinema. There is a photography studio and a TV lounge. There is also a restaurant and a poolside bar. Free WiFi is available throughout.

All apartments have a balcony overlooking the pool. They have a spacious living room with a sofa bed, a well-equipped kitchenette and a bathroom. The safe is available for a fee.

The staff organizes thematic activities that include live music and magic shows. The establishment also has a children's club.

Pirates Village is 600 meters from Santa Ponsa Golf Course. Palma Mallorca Airport is 20 minutes away by car.
