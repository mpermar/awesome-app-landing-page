Description

The apartments at Playas Ca's Saboners have air conditioning, a balcony, a spacious living-dining room with a sofa bed and a fully equipped kitchenette. The bathroom includes a bath, shower and hairdryer.

In the establishment there is a well-stocked supermarket and in the center of Magaluf you will find a wide variety of bars, restaurants and shops.

Playas Ca's Saboners also has a children's playground and a ping pong table. It also offers 24-hour reception and car rental service.

The Palma de Mallorca ferry terminal is a 15-minute drive away.
