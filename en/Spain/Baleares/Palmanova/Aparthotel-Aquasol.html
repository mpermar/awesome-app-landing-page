Description

All Aquasol apartments include a terrace with views of the pool or gardens, are decorated in light colors and feature air conditioning and parquet floors.

The apartments have a well-equipped kitchen and spacious living areas with 2 sofa beds and flat-screen TVs. Safe is also provided free of charge.

The Aquasol's restaurant serves hot and cold dishes buffets. Guests can enjoy à la carte cuisine on the terrace by the pool, where there is also a bar. Free WiFi is provided in the bar and in the pool area.

Aparthotel Aquasol is just 17 km from Son Sant Joan de Mallorca Airport. The beaches and golf club of Santa Ponsa are only 15 minutes away by car.
