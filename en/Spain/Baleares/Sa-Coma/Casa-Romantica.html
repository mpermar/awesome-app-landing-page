Description

The rooms in the complex have air conditioning, flat-screen satellite TV and a private bathroom. Some rooms have a seating area, while others have a terrace or balcony. Casa Romantica also offers free Wi-Fi throughout.

This complex has free bicycles and offers a car rental service. The area is ideal for golf.

The complex has a tennis court and dart board. Casa Romantica is 3.6 km from Cala Millor and 17 km from Cala Ratjada. Palma de Mallorca Airport is the closest and is 55 km away.
