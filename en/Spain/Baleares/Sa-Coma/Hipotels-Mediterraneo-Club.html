Description

All apartments at Hipotels Mediterraneo Club feature tiled floors, air conditioning, a furnished balcony with views of the sea or the pool, a spacious living-dining area and a kitchen with a hob, microwave and fridge.

Live shows and concerts are offered at the Blau Club. There are also several mini clubs for children of different ages.

The property has 2 buffet restaurants and a café with a terrace located next to the pool. Once a week a theme dinner is organized.

The Hipotels Mediterraneo Club also features a sauna, squash and tennis courts, a fitness center, a bicycle and car rental service and a currency exchange service.

Palma de Mallorca Airport is more than 1 hour away by car.
