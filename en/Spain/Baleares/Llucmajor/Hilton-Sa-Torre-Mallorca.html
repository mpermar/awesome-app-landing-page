Description

The rooms at the Hilton Sa Torre Mallorca feature elegant and modern décor. They have air conditioning, an electric kettle and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The Arxiduc restaurant prepares contemporary Mediterranean cuisine and has a large wine cellar. Sa Clastra Bar serves light meals and has an outdoor terrace.

Guests of the Hotel Sa Torre can enjoy a modern fitness center and tennis courts.

Sa Torre is just a 10-minute drive from the beautiful beaches of southern Mallorca. Palma Airport is 22 km away.
