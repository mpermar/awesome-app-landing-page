Description

Free WiFi is available throughout. For a fee, premium high-speed WiFi is provided.

The rooms at the Iberostar Pinos Park Hotel, spacious and decorated in a Mediterranean style, are equipped with a private bathroom and a furnished terrace or balcony.

The property has a poolside bar, gardens and solarium. In summer, night shows are organized on a large terrace. There is also a children's play area, where the little ones can play in the company of their parents.

Iberostar Pinos Park is 3 km from Capdepera and 4 km from Cala Rajada. The coves of Cala Agulla are a 13-minute drive away and Son Sant Joan Airport is 79 km away.
