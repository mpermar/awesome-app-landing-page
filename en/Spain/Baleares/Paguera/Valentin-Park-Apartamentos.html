Description

At Valentin Park Apartments you will find a swimming pool and a snack bar.

The complex is only 20 meters from Hotel Valentín Park, where both children and adults can enjoy a program of leisure activities.

There is also a sauna, a gym and a games room. There is also a large terrace.

All apartments at Valentín Park have a balcony. They have a master bedroom, kitchenette and a sofa bed in the living room.
