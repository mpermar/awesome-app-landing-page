Description

The hotel, located in a magnificent area of Ibiza, provides personalized services.

Likewise, the hotel is simply and happily decorated and the rooms have air conditioning, satellite TV and a fridge.

The OD Talamanca buffet restaurant offers a buffet breakfast and overlooks the bay of Talamanca. There is also a bar in the hotel that serves lunch.

The hotel is located 25 meters from a bus stop that connects directly with the nearby city of Ibiza, 15 minutes by car from several discos, such as Pachá or Space, and 2 km from the city port, where ferries depart that link with the other Balearic Islands and with the Peninsula.

This seasonal hotel is open from May to October.
