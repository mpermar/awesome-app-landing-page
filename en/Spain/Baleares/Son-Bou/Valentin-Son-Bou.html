Description

Rooms at Valentin Son Bou Hotel & Apartments feature tiled floors and soft-colored décor. All rooms have satellite TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The hotel's buffet restaurant serves international food and offers show cooking. There are also 3 bars, including a bar / cafe next to the pool.

The Valentin Son Bou Hotel & Apartments has tennis courts and a fitness center. There is also a games room, and the hotel offers a varied entertainment program for adults and children.

Menorca Airport is a 20-minute drive from the hotel. You can reach La Cova d'en Xoroi, a well-known bar in a cave, in 25 minutes.
