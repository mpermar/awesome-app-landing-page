Description

Hotel Caribe Es Canar is located just 250 meters from the famous hippy market in Es Canar. There are boats that offer frequent links between Es Canar and Santa Eulalia, the city of Ibiza and Formentera.

The rooms at the Caribe have air conditioning, private bathroom, satellite TV with international channels and a terrace. Wi-Fi in public areas is free, while in rooms there is an additional cost.

The restaurant at the Caribe Hotel Es Canar serves a variety of hot and cold buffet dishes with drinks included. The establishment also offers an entertainment program with activities and shows.

The hotel is a 30-minute drive from Ibiza Airport and 20 km from the city and its famous nightclubs, such as Pacha. Free parking is provided.
