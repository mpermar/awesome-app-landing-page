Description

All rooms have a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a microwave, a kettle, a bath or shower, a hairdryer and a closet. Some include a kitchenette with a fridge and stove.

A continental breakfast is served daily.

The establishment also has a hot tub. This hotel has a ping pong table, bicycle and car rental service, and a 24-hour reception

Alcudia is 36 km from Marins Playa. The nearest airport is Palma de Mallorca, located 57 km from the accommodation.

Extra beds and / or cots are available upon request and must be confirmed by the accommodation.
Supplements are not automatically calculated in the total amount and must be paid separately during the stay.
