Description

The rooms include a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kettle, a microwave and a wardrobe. All rooms have a private bathroom with a bath or shower and a hairdryer. Some rooms have a seating area.

In the morning, Marins Cala Nau serves a full continental or English / Irish breakfast.

This 4-star hotel has a children's playground and facilities for table tennis. Guests can also use the bicycle loan service.

Marins Cala Nau is 37 km from the town of Alcudia and 15 km from Cala Ratjada. Palma de Mallorca Airport is 56 km away.
