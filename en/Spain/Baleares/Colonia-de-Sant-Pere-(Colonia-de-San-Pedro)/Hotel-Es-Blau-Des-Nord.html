Description

It has a large private garden with several seating areas and a pool with sea views.

In this hotel you can choose between rooms and holiday homes. All accommodation units are equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV and a private terrace, as well as Wi-Fi access available for a fee. The houses are modern and have a bedroom and a living room.

Mediterranean and local dishes are proposed in the hotel restaurant, and a bar where you can enjoy snacks and drinks throughout the day. There is also a small supermarket. Guests can visit the shops, bars and restaurants of the center of Colònia de Sant Pere, located 2 km away.

The hotel also has a TV room, a games room with a pool table and a garden with magnificent views of the sunset.

Hotel Es Blau is a 30-minute drive from Alcúdia and about 50 minutes by car from Palma de Mallorca and its airport.
