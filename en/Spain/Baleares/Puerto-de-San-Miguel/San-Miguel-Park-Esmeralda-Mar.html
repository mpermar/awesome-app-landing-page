Description

The San Miguel Park / Esmeralda Mar has studios, 1 and 2 bedroom apartments, and superior apartments with sea views. All have air conditioning, a balcony or terrace, a kitchenette and satellite TV with DVD player.

The San Miguel Park offers a hot tub, tennis court, a buffet restaurant and free Wi-Fi in the bar, restaurant and lounge. Guests receive 1 free umbrella when they rent sun loungers on the beach.

The complex organizes daily activities and there is an entertainment program for children, as well as a playground.

The local artisan market is 4 km from San Miguel Park, while the Can Marsa caves are 1 km away.
