Description

Hotel Cap Rocat is located in a quiet area of Palma Bay, near Cala Blava. The property provides direct access to the beach and features an outdoor pool and sun terrace.

The hotel has a free spa and a hamman, available for a fee. There are also tennis courts and a fitness center with personal training service and outdoor yoga and pilates classes are organized.

The hotel rooms include a private balcony, air conditioning, minibar, bathrobe, slippers and CD and DVD player.

Cap Rocat's restaurant serves Mediterranean cuisine. The hotel also has spacious common rooms and patios and there is free Wi-Fi in all areas.

For security reasons, due to the unique architecture of the fortress, children under 15 years old cannot be accommodated in Cap Rocat.
