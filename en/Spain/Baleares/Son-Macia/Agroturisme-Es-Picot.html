Description

Es Picot is located in a quiet environment and occupies an estate of 12.5 hectares, in an area of special natural interest. Guests can enjoy the gardens, sunbathe and breathe the fresh country air.

The property offers an outdoor pool during the summer months and is perfect for cooling off. You can also walk to the beach and bathe in the Mediterranean Sea.

Es Picot has spacious country-style rooms, equipped with a bathroom and free Wi-Fi.
