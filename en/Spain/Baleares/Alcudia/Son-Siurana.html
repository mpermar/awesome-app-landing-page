Description

The Son Siurana is immersed in a beautiful spot planted with almond and fig trees. The building, built in 1784 and carefully preserved, integrates perfectly into the landscape.

The interiors of the hotel are decorated with luxurious antique-style furniture. Its modern facilities include a large outdoor pool with stunning views of the open fields and the hills.
