Description

Each apartment has a living room with satellite TV, a kitchen or kitchenette with an oven and microwave, and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

A wide variety of activities, such as diving and table tennis, can be practiced on site. Bicycles and cars are rented.

The establishment has a small market and houses the Miramare restaurant, which is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner and enjoys beautiful views of San Antoni Bay and the magnificent sunset. There are bars, restaurants and nightclubs 500 meters away.

The beaches of Port des Torrent and Des Pinet are 900 meters from the Marina Palace Prestige. San Antonio is 5 km away, while the city of Ibiza is a 20-minute drive away. Ibiza Airport is 19 km away.
