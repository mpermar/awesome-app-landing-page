Description

All of Venice's apartments feature air conditioning, satellite TV, a private terrace and a kitchenette with a microwave, fridge with freezer and coffee facilities. There are several shops, bars and restaurants within 10 minutes walk.

The establishment offers a car rental service so that guests can visit Alcudia and its fantastic beaches, such as Alcanada and Formentor. If you wish, the reception staff can offer you interesting information.

The apartments are a 20-minute walk from the port of Alcudia, where there are lots of bars, shops and restaurants. In addition, the regular bus service to Alcudia stops at the entrance of the complex.
