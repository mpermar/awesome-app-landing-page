Description

The apartments and villas of the Aucanada Club also have air conditioning, heating, a living room with a terrace, a bedroom with a small balcony and a shower bathroom.

In addition, the hotel's Aucanada spa provides relaxation and beauty treatments and a spa circuit.

WiFi is available for a fee in public areas, including the garden and bar.

The Aucanada Club is located in a residential area of Alcanada, in Alcudia Bay, 700 meters from the beach and within walking distance of the Alcanada golf course.
