Description

The hotel is surrounded by shops and nightlife, and has a large outdoor pool and a children's pool. There are free sun loungers and umbrellas. The wooden terrace has a hot tub, a sauna and a pressure shower.

The buffet restaurant at JS Sol de Alcudia prepares regional and international cuisine, features show cooking and organizes a weekly themed dinner. There is also a terrace bar next to the pool, which offers live music every night.

The reception is open 24 hours a day and there is a tour desk with car rental service. Free parking is available nearby and the Aucanada Golf Club is 3 km away.

This seasonal hotel is open from May to October.
