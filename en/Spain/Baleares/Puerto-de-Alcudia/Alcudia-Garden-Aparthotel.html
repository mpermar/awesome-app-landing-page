Description

The family-friendly Alcudia Garden Aparthotel offers spacious accommodation with air conditioning and a balcony. In addition, there is free Wi-Fi connection throughout.

Guests can relax in the sauna or hot tub and swim in the sea or in the hotel's swimming pools.

Alcudia Garden has a fitness center and organizes free activities, including sports such as archery, table tennis and aerobics.

In the evening performances and shows are offered, as well as live music during the summer. There is also a poolside bar and a pizzeria.

The establishment has a children's playground, children's pool and an entertainment program by the hotel staff. In addition, there is a children's club for children from 4 to 12 years.

The Alcudia Garden Aparthotel is close to Albufera, the best natural park in the Balearic Islands.

The hotel provides a bicycle rental service, ideal for exploring the surrounding landscape or moving to the cosmopolitan port of Alcudia, which offers numerous activities.
