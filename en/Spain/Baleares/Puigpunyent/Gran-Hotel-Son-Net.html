Description

Some parts of the Gran Hotel Son Net date from 1672. The renovation of the building has preserved the original charm of the building, integrating modern and elegant facilities.

The 30 m outdoor pool is surrounded by cabins for relaxation. The exquisite Oleum restaurant adds another touch of luxury.

The hotel's surroundings are also idyllic, as it is surrounded by citrus trees and lush gardens, overlooking the town of Puigpunyent and the mountains.
