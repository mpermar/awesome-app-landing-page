Description

S'Era Vella is a traditional Mallorcan building set in a large garden surrounded by hills and has several outdoor dining areas. A full breakfast is served daily. Dinner, based on Mediterranean dishes, is prepared twice a week.

The establishment has rooms and apartments. All offer views of the gardens and mountains, free Wi-Fi, towels, bed linen and a private bathroom with a bath or shower and a hairdryer.

The establishment's staff will help you organize your stay in Mallorca. A car and bicycle rental service is offered. In addition, there are excellent cycling and hiking trails nearby.

The nearest cafes, restaurants and supermarkets are 5 km away.
