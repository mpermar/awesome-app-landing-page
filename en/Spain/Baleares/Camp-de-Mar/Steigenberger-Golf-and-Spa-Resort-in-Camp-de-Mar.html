Description

Its Moorish-inspired buildings are surrounded by the paths of the Andratx golf club, which allow direct access to one of the most prestigious courses on the island.

Rooms at the Steigenberger Golf & Spa Resort in Camp de Mar overlook the pool, the sea or the golf course and feature a private bathroom with bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries. There is free WiFi in all units.

The spa center has an indoor pool and a free gym. Massages and beauty treatments are performed for a fee.

You can have a drink by the pool at any time of the day. The resort's restaurant serves Mallorcan specialties.
