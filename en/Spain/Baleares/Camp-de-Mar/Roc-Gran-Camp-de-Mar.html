Description

The hotel has a gym, tennis courts and sports courts to play football, basketball or volleyball. In addition, bike and canoe trips are organized at certain times of the year.

The rooms at Roc Gran Camp de Mar feature air conditioning, satellite TV, a refrigerator and a private bathroom. They also have a private terrace or balcony.

The hotel's buffet restaurant serves Mediterranean and international cuisine. There is also a lounge bar and a poolside bar.

The hotel is right next to the Andratx golf course and 25 minutes by car from Palma de Mallorca.
