Description

This resort has many sports facilities, such as 2 tennis courts, table tennis, bowling and gym. It also has a sauna and a hot tub, available for a fee.

It has a playground and organizes water gymnastics, aerobics, soccer games and activities for children. The disco offers various shows at night.

The restaurant is located on the pool terrace and enjoys stunning views of the sea. It serves Mediterranean cuisine, a wide selection of wines and fixed and children's menus.

Ibiza Airport is 31 km away.
