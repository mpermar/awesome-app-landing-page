Description

The rooms and apartments at Punta Reina Resort are simply decorated in bright colors and feature air conditioning, satellite TV and a kitchenette.

The hotel's buffet restaurant offers a wide variety of international dishes, while La Finca restaurant serves traditional Spanish tapas. There is also a pizzeria and a terrace bar.

The Blau Punta's spa includes Turkish baths, a hot tub and an indoor pool. This property also houses a beauty salon, tennis and volleyball courts, a mini-golf course and a gym.

This hotel is just over an hour's drive from Palma and its airport and 14 km from the Mondragó Natural Park.
