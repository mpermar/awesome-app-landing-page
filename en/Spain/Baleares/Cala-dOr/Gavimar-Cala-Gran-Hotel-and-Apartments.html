Description

The apartments, studios and rooms at Cala Gran are bright and have air conditioning, a safe and a private bathroom. Most have a balcony. Free Wi-Fi is available in all rooms.

The establishment also has a children's playground, 3 bars, a tennis court and volleyball court. In addition, animation programs are organized every day.

This aparthotel is located a few minutes walk from the center and other beaches. It is 58 km from Palma de Mallorca Airport
