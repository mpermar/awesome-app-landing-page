Description

The apartments are spacious, simply decorated and feature a kitchenette and a living-dining area with a sofa bed and satellite TV. In addition, there is WiFi in the rooms for a fee.

The pools are surrounded by a solarium with sun loungers. Inturotel Esmeralda Park also features a tennis court, sauna and hot tub and offers direct access to the beach.

Esmeralda Park is a 10-minute walk from the shops, bars and restaurants of the center of Cala d'Or. Guests can enjoy activities such as hiking in the natural park of Cala Mondragó, located about 4 km away.

The hotel has a buffet restaurant with spectacular sea views. There is also a pizzeria and the beach bar serves drinks and snacks.
