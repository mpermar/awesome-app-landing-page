Description

The elegant rooms are equipped with a seating area with a flat-screen TV and a minibar, while the private bathroom includes a bathrobe and free toiletries. There is free WiFi.

Hotel Cort is a 5-minute walk from shops, bars and restaurants and 200 meters from Palma Cathedral and the Plaza Mayor.

The Intermodal-Palma train station is a 15-minute walk away, while Palma de Mallorca Airport is a 15-minute drive away. The property can arrange an airport shuttle service, upon request and for a fee.
