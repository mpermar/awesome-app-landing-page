Description

The rooms of the Valparaiso Palace & Spa are spacious and modern and include a private balcony with spectacular views of the city, the bay or the mountains. They also have air conditioning, satellite TV, minibar and bathrobes.

The Valparaiso Spa has a huge heated pool, sauna, hot tub, steam room, gym, ice cave and juice bar. All spa facilities are at an additional cost.

In addition, the Valparaiso Palace & Spa is located between gardens over the bay of Palma and the city. It has tennis and paddle courts, 2 large swimming pools and a poolside bar that opens all day in summer.
