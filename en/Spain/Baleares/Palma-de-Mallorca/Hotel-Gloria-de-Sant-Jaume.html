Description

Hotel Gloria de Sant Jaume is a restored 19th-century building and features traditional elements such as exposed wooden beams, hydraulic tiles and marble bathrooms.

The rooms include free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, heating, Smart Satellite TV, Nespresso coffee machine, tea set, bathrobe, slippers and bathroom with hairdryer, vanity mirror and luxury toiletries.

Hotel Gloria de Sant Jaume's restaurant and bar serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner. There is also a meeting and event room.

The wellness area of the property houses an indoor pool. Treatments and a massage service are available for a fee.

The property is a 12-minute walk from the Palma Yacht Club, 500 meters from the Cathedral and 8 km from Palma de Mallorca Airport.
