Description

The buildings of Sa Rota originally date from the 18th century, and have been carefully restored using local materials. Modern facilities include an outdoor pool, ideal for cooling off from the warm Mediterranean sun.

You can contemplate the views of the surroundings from your own apartment. All have antique furniture, and retain their original facilities, with style and charm.
