Description

All apartments are air-conditioned and include a living room with a sofa and flat-screen satellite TV. The private bathroom has a shower and free toiletries, while the rustic kitchen is equipped with a fridge, microwave and coffee maker.

The upper apartment has 2 large terraces and has ceiling fans in the bedrooms. It is away from the main buildings, next to the pool and playground.

The property is located 5 km from Es Trenc-Salobrar de Campos Natural Park and offers free parking. Palma Airport is 30 minutes away by car.
