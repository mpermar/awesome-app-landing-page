Description

This palatial building offers comfort and elegance. The rooms are equipped with heating and air conditioning, and have a balcony to enjoy the sun.

Bavaria is ideally located for exploring the east coast of Mallorca, and offers airport shuttle and tourist information services.

Guests can go hiking, fishing, windsurfing, snorkeling and diving, or just relax on the fantastic beaches.

They can also rent a bicycle to discover the area or play golf or tennis near the hotel.

The Baviera has an outdoor pool and massage service, and has a restaurant serving delicious meals.
