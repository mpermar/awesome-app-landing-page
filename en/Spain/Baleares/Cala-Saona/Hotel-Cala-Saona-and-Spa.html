Description

Rooms at Hotel Cala Saona are bright and air-conditioned, with French doors with access to a balcony, TV, minibar, private bathroom with shower and free beach towels.

The hotel also has a restaurant and serves a buffet breakfast in the morning. The hotel also has a tour desk and free Wi-Fi.

The hotel is about a 15-minute drive from the La Savina ferry terminal, which links to Ibiza and Formentera.
