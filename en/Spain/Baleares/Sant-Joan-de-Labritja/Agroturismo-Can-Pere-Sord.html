Description

All rooms have air conditioning, fireplace, flat-screen satellite TV, minibar and private bathroom with shower. Some have a private terrace and hot tub.

Agroturismo Can Pere Sord serves snacks. The establishment also houses a bar, vegetable garden and fruit trees.

The quiet beaches of the north coast of Ibiza are 10 minutes away by car. Agroturismo Can Pere Sord offers car and boat rental service. Ibiza Airport is 30 minutes away by car and the city of Ibiza is 20 km away
