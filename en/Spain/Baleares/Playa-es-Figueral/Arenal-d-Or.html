Description

The attractive and secluded pool area is located in a subtropical garden and is equipped with sun loungers. The hotel also has a hot tub and outdoor fitness area.

The Arenal d'Or apartments have air conditioning, free Wi-Fi and a spacious living room with a sofa bed and flat-screen TV.

There are 2 private terraces with table, chairs and sun loungers. One of them offers views of the sea, and the other, the countryside. The kitchen includes an oven, hob and toaster.

This family-run establishment is 200 meters from the beautiful beach of Aguas Blancas, a 15-minute drive from Santa Eulalia and a 40-minute drive from San Antonio and the capital of Ibiza.
