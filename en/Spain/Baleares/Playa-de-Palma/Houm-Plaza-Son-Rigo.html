Description

The apartments at Houm Plaza Son Rigo are bright and feature air conditioning, satellite TV and a kitchenette with a microwave, fridge and kettle.

The restaurant offers a varied buffet breakfast, as well as dinners that include daily vegetarian dishes. There is also a poolside bar.

The aparthotel has a 24-hour reception, a tour desk, car rental service and free Wi-Fi throughout. The Houm Plaza Son Rigo offers a peaceful environment, ideal for family vacations.

Houm Plaza Son Rigo is just 5 km from Palma de Mallorca Airport and about 12 km from Palma's city center.

This seasonal hotel only opens from March to November.
