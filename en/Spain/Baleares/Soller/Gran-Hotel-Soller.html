Description

The Gran Hotel Soller is in the center of Sóller, 3 km from the Port de Sóller silver and 50 meters from a tram stop, which crosses the orange grove.

The hotel has a beautiful 19th-century facade and various leisure facilities, such as a spa center, with its heated indoor pool, hot tub, steam room and massage service and a gym. The Son Termens golf course is 15 minutes away by car.

At the end of the day, you can enjoy a delicious Mediterranean meal at the C'an Blau restaurant, a magnificent, typically Mallorcan restaurant, which opens onto a terrace for outdoor dining. Intimate dinners can be accompanied by an excellent wine from the hotel's cellar.
