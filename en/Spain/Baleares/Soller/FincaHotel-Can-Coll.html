Description

The rooms at Can Coll are air-conditioned, elegantly decorated in a Mallorcan style and overlook the garden and the mountains. All rooms have a flat-screen satellite TV, a sofa and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The establishment offers a wide range of Mallorcan wines and dishes such as pasta and local cheese. There are many outdoor seating areas in Can Coll, where you can have a drink. There is free Wi-Fi in public areas.

You can go hiking in the surrounding hills. The beach is about 3 km from Port de Sóller. Palma de Mallorca and its airport are about 30 minutes away by car.
