Description

The rooms are surrounded by gardens and have a traditional Menorcan decoration, with whitewashed walls and exposed beams on the ceiling. The property has a pleasant seating area with flat-screen satellite TV.

The Torralbenc's restaurant serves typical cuisine of the island, made with products from local organic farms. In addition, the establishment has a furnished solarium with unbeatable views of the Mediterranean Sea. There is free parking.

In addition, Cala en Porter is just 10 minutes away by car. There you will find a beautiful beach, as well as La Cova d'en Xoroi, a discobar of cocktails, located on a cliff with stunning views.
