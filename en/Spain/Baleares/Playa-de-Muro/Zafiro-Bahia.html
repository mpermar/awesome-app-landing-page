Description

In addition, the Bahia apartments are modern and have a lounge, bedroom, satellite TV, safe and kitchen with microwave.

The Bahia complex has tennis courts, a fitness center and indoor and outdoor pools. The chillout pool has water jets and is surrounded by Balinese beds. There are also saunas, hot tubs and children's play area.

The Sapphire Bay has 2 restaurants, one buffet and one à la carte menu, and both offer entertainment. There is also a poolside bar.

The Sapphire Bahia is located in the bay of Alcudia, in the middle of the Albufera natural park. Guests can enjoy free parking nearby.
