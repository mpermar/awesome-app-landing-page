Description

This 4-star hotel has a buffet restaurant, cafeteria and day and night entertainment for all guests. The area is ideal for shopping, dining out or having a drink. Guests will find a variety of restaurants, bars and shops.

The junior suites and suites are fully equipped and feature air conditioning, balcony, satellite TV and bathroom with shower.

The nearby nature reserve of L'Albufera offers a quiet environment of lush natural beauty to explore and enjoy.

In August, the hotel requires a minimum stay of 5 nights.
