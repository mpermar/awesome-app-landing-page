Description

Both the apartments and the studios of the resort have air conditioning, a balcony, a seating area with a TV and a kitchen. The bathroom is private and includes a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Among the leisure facilities of the BQ Alcudia Sun Village, it is worth highlighting a game room with darts and pool tables, a tennis court and a mini-golf. There are also facilities for the little ones, such as a children's pool with slides, a children's club and a children's playground.

The resort has a poolside bar. One of the restaurants serves buffet meals. Packed lunches are available upon request. The reception staff, available 24 hours a day, offers tourist information, car rental service, currency exchange service and massage service.

Palma de Mallorca Airport is 45 minutes away by car.
