Description

All Viva Blue apartments have air conditioning, satellite TV, private balcony and safe. Some units have views of the S'Albufera Natural Park. The open-plan kitchen is equipped with a fridge, microwave and coffee maker.

There is also a poolside bar, a small supermarket and a buffet restaurant serving children's menus.

The hotel is well equipped to receive athletes. The Viva Blue spa includes a sauna, fully equipped gym and massage service upon request. The hotel has special facilities for cyclists, such as a bicycle storage area, cycling shop, tools and a bicycle cleaning area.

A wide variety of outdoor activities can be practiced in the area, such as cycling and hiking. In addition, the establishment has a games room, playground and children's club where activities are organized with an entertainment team.

This seasonal hotel only opens from March to November.
