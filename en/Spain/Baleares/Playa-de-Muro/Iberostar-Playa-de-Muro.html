Description

Playa de Muro has an elegant decoration in all areas. The rooms include a TV with satellite channels and a minibar.

The hotel has 4 restaurants, including an à la carte restaurant, a cafeteria overlooking the sea and a food truck by the pool.

The hotel spa has an indoor pool, hot tub, heated sun loungers, rain shower and 2 saunas. It also has a gym

The Iberostar Playa de Muro offers unique services for cyclists and sports enthusiasts. There is a repair shop, a garage and a fully equipped gym. In addition, a bicycle rental service is available and there are special menus, a sports shop and specialized equipment.

S’Estany Gran Lake is only a 15-minute walk away.
