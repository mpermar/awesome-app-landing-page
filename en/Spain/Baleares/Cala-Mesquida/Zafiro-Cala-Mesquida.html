Description

The apartments and studios are bright and have air conditioning and a balcony. The living room offers flat-screen satellite TV. There is also a safe and a private bathroom with a hairdryer. The kitchen is well equipped with a fridge, microwave and coffee maker.

Zafiro Cala Mesquida, which was previously called Viva Cala Mesquida Club, offers free Wi-Fi in public areas. There is a miniature golf course and a games room with billiards. It also offers a varied entertainment program for children and adults.

Zafiro Cala Mesquida's restaurant serves buffet-style food and the pool bar offers drinks. There is also a bar that serves snacks at lunchtime.

The 24-hour reception offers a currency exchange service. Car and bicycle rental are also available and Palma Airport is 80 km away.
