Description

The apartment is equipped with 1 bedroom, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a fully equipped kitchenette that provides guests with an oven and a microwave. The accommodation also has a bathroom with a bath.

A buffet or Full English/Irish breakfast is available daily at the property.

The 24-hour front desk can offer helpful tips for getting around the area.

The apartment offers an outdoor pool. A children's playground is also available at the property, while guests can also relax in the garden.

Puerto de la Cruz is 43 km from Studio Los Cristianos, while Adeje is 9 km away. Tenerife Sur Airport is 13 km from the property.
