Description

Eurostar Mijas offers 1, 2 and 3 bedroom apartments with modern and elegant décor, a private terrace, free Wi-Fi, a well-equipped kitchen, a comfortable living area and 1 or 2 bathrooms. Kitchen utensils can be rented at the property.

This complex has a spa with sauna and sensation shower, a large garden area, a cafeteria and a terrace with sun loungers.

Eurostars Mijas is less than a 15-minute drive from Fuengirola and the beach.
