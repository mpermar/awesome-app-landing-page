This is a charming place, with large rooms - don't...

"This is a charming place, with large rooms - don't expect an actual villa - helpful staff, and a surprisingly good restaurant that attracts local families. The breakfast was good - again, don't expect a German breakfast feast, but it's substantial enough to feed you until that very late hour when Spaniards eat.Dinner was very nice at 13 Euros. And do not miss the drive into the Sierra da Cazorla, where we saw an Ibex, Griffon Vultures, and a Lammergeier."

Las villas súper calentitas, si te agobia la calefacción pues la apagas un rato y listo, que también he leído opiniones que si el gasto, que si poco ecológico... pues apagas el botón y ya está.
