Description

The Medplaya Bali Hotel consists of well-equipped rooms with heating, air conditioning, satellite TV and a hire safe.

The buffet restaurant at Medplaya Hotel Bali serves a selection of Mediterranean cuisine. The pool bar is open during the summer months.

The hotel provides free Wi-Fi throughout. It also provides private parking.

Medplaya Bali Hotel is a 10-minute walk from the marina and Puerto Marina shopping center. Malaga and Malaga airport are a 30-minute drive from the hotel.
