Description

The rooms at Vincci Selección Aleysa are modern in style and feature air conditioning, 42-inch LED satellite TV and CD and DVD players. They are also equipped with a laptop safe, small fridge and electric kettle. The bathrooms are elegant and include a bathrobe, slippers and toiletries.

On request, free yoga and Pilates classes are held every morning in the hotel garden, which overlooks the sea. Vincci Selección Aleysa provides free cars, upon request.

The Vincci Selección Aleysa Hotel Boutique & Spa's restaurant serves international cuisine. The hotel breakfast includes products suitable for coeliacs. There is also a cafeteria, a terrace and a lounge bar.

The hotel is 1.5 km from the marina, where there is a shopping center, bars and discos. Torrequebrada golf course is less than 10 minutes away by car.
