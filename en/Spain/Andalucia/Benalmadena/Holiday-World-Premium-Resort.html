Description

The rooms at Holiday World Premium Resort are decorated in an oriental style and are equipped with air conditioning, WiFi, satellite TV and a kitchen with a hob and microwave.

For an additional fee, guests can access the Holiday World Premium Resort's spa, with Turkish baths, an indoor dynamic pool and a separate children's area. The establishment also has a fitness center, tennis courts, mini-golf course and cinema.

The hotel has several restaurants, such as the Uro Wok oriental restaurant and the Maeva buffet restaurant. There is also a piano bar and a karaoke bar.

Holiday World Premium Resort is just a 5-minute drive from Torrequebrada Golf Course. The hotel can be easily accessed from the A-7 motorway, which runs along the Spanish east coast.
