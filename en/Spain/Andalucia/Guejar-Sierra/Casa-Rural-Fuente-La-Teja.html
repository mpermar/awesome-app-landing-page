Description

The rooms at La Teja Rural Hotel have a TV, private bathroom and central heating. Many also have a balcony.

The Fuente la Teja is located in the Sierra Nevada National Park. The hotel's tour desk can provide more details about the area. The mountains are ideal for horse riding, hiking or skiing. The Sierra Nevada ski resort is only 30 minutes away by car.

The Fuente la Teja's restaurant serves typical regional food. Room service and packed lunches are available upon request.

The hotel also has a comfortable lounge. The business center offers free Wi-Fi. La Teja's reception is open 24 hours a day. The hotel has free private parking.

Just 20 km away is the wonderful Granada, the city of the fascinating Alhambra and the charming neighborhood of Albaicín. The beaches of the Costa Tropical are about 80 km away.
