Description

The Saray Hotel has an elegant Andalusian design, with typical arches, fountains and mosaics. The rooms are elegant and feature a laptop safe, minibar and private bathroom with hairdryer. All rooms are non smoking.

The Saray has an à la carte restaurant serving national and international dishes, a poolside bar and a well-known bar in the area, the Qubba.

The hotel is next to the Granada Congress Center, just 150 meters from the airport bus stop and less than 2 km from the Alhambra Palace and the Albaicín district.

Zaidín is a great choice for travelers interested in eating tapas, the good atmosphere and monuments.

Car parking cost extra (17 euro) but underground and handy. Some might complain about the tight squeeze but I got my SUV parked up fine. We arrived late on the day of check-in and they let us keep the car parked after check out so we could explore the city center for a few hours. Excellent customer service!! We didn't eat at the hotel so I can't comment on the food but I would stay again if in Granada."
