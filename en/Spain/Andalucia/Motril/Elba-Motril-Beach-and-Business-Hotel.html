Description

All rooms at Elba Motril have tiled floors and air conditioning. They are equipped with satellite TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The establishment has 3 meeting rooms with air conditioning, sound system, audiovisual equipment and simultaneous translation equipment. It also offers catering services, such as coffee breaks and special menus for groups. There is also the possibility of setting up a tent with a capacity for 450 people, for celebrations and outdoor meetings.

The hotel's Executive restaurant serves a variety of regional and international dishes. In the summer months there is also a piano bar where you can have some tapas and drinks, as well as enjoy live music. Gluten-free dishes are also available upon request.

Elba Motril Beach & Business Hotel is 1 km from Los Moriscos Golf Course. The Nerja Caves are 35 km away, and the wonderful city of Granada is 55 km away.
