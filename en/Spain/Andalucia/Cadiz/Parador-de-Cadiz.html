Description

The bright rooms of this hotel have air conditioning, a terrace overlooking the sea or the city and modern and cozy furniture. All rooms have satellite TV, a minibar and a private bathroom.

The Parador Hotel Atlántico offers quality regional cuisine in its à la carte restaurant, as well as traditional tapas in the bar. The hotel also has large rooms for events and celebrations.

This hotel is just a 5-minute walk from Caleta Beach and is an excellent starting point to explore the charming fishing villages nearby. San Sebastián Castle is a 15-minute walk away.

The Parador de Cádiz has a 24-hour reception and a tour desk. It is 5 minutes drive from the ferry port. Late check-out is available upon request and subject to availability.

Amazing Location, Sensational Sunsets, Fantastic Pools

"The location is fantastic. Our bedroom overlooked the Pool area and the sea. The room and the whole Hotel is very modern but in a good way. The Pool Area is the star of the show. It has 4 pools in total, 1 for swimming, 1 SPA pool and 2 shallow pools for children or floating sunbeds. There is also a SPA where you can get treatments and massages etc. and a Pool Bar. The parking was easy and secure, although it isn't cheap so you'd expect it to be good. The sunsets from the balcony were amazing and the view from the Breakfast Area was excellent too overlooking the ocean. It's only a 10 minute walk into town or if you go via the sea there are lovely walks in either direction. The Breakfast was good, although I've had better at other Paradores. The freshly made eggs were very nice."
