Description

All rooms at Playa Victoria have air conditioning, TV, minibar, safe and private bathroom with hairdryer and toiletries.

The Victoria hotel has a bar and an elegant restaurant serving Mediterranean cuisine. There is also room service.

Guests can access the beach directly from the hotel. In addition, the reception is open 24 hours a day and there is a tour desk with car rental service. Laundry, ironing and dry cleaning services are also provided.

Jerez Airport is the closest and is 43 km away.
