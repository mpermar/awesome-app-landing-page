Description

The rooms are elegantly decorated and enjoy views of the beach, the garden or the city. All of them consist of air conditioning, flat screen TV with satellite channels, multimedia library, Bluetooth sound system and USB and HDMI connections. They also include a private bathroom with bathtub, mirror with thermometer and clock and Hermès toiletries.

The Prince of Asturias restaurant serves international cuisine with a Mediterranean touch and houses an informal bar. It also has a rooftop chill out terrace, which offers drinks and enjoys stunning sea views.

Breakfast is served by the pool and consists of an extensive buffet that includes a varied selection of fruit, sausages, cheeses and pastries. In addition, it has a live cooking section where food is prepared on request.

There is also a children's pool and a seasonal mini club. There are also several meeting facilities and conference rooms.

Gran Hotel Miramar is 700 meters from Alcazaba, 10 minutes' walk from the city center and 9 km from Malaga Airport.

Centro de Málaga is a great choice for travelers interested in museums, eating tapas and shopping.
