Description

The rooms have a modern design and include welcome products, a safe, a flat-screen TV and a modern bathroom.

The Vincci Selección Posada del Patio offers the exclusive Essence experience, with rooms located on a separate floor. These rooms include daily water bottle, national and international daily newspapers, private terrace and snacks and drinks, among other amenities.

Important archaeological remains have been found under the Vincci Selección Posada del Patio, as a part of the city's Arab wall, which can be visited from the hotel.

The hotel houses Le diner restaurant & champagnerie, which serves modern Mediterranean dishes. The hotel breakfast includes food suitable for coeliacs. There is also an elegant bar.

The Posada del Patio hotel is just a 10-minute walk from the AVE high-speed train station and 500 meters from the Cathedral and the Picasso Museum.

Centro de Málaga is a great choice for travelers interested in museums, eating tapas and shopping.
