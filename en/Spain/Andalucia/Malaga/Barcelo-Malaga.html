Description

This uniquely designed hotel features a stainless steel slide, which connects the 1st floor with the B-Lounge cocktail bar. It also houses an award-winning gourmet restaurant, El Andén.

The rooms at Barceló Málaga have modern and colorful décor, air conditioning, pillow menu, flat-screen satellite TV and an elegant bathroom with hydromassage shower.

Malaga's contemporary art center is just 600 meters away, while Malaga Cathedral and the Picasso Museum are within a 20-minute walk of the hotel. The train station offers direct connections to Malaga airport.

Centro de Málaga is a great choice for travelers interested in museums, eating tapas and shopping.
