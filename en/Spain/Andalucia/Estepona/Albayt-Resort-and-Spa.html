Description

The Albayt has spacious and bright apartments with air conditioning and views of the mountains or the garden. All are equipped with a private kitchen with an oven, washing machine, fridge with freezer, toaster and kettle.

The complex has a seasonal restaurant and a poolside bar that opens in the summer months. Breakfast is served throughout the year.

Albayt has its own golf school. There are several golf courses within 2 km of the Albayt. The hotel spa, which has an extra cost, provides various treatments and includes a steam room and sauna. In the Albayt fitness center you can perform a variety of activities.

Albayt is a 30-minute drive from Puerto Banús and Marbella.
