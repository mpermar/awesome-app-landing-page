Description

All apartments at Cortijo del Mar Resort have air conditioning and an elegant and contemporary décor. They also have a large terrace or garden.

The kitchens in the apartments have an oven and microwave. They also have a washing machine, dishwasher and coffee maker. There are 2 bathrooms with hydromassage bath or shower.

Cortijo del Mar is a 20-minute drive from the center of Marbella and offers easy access to the AP7 motorway. Malaga Airport is a 50-minute drive from the property, which offers an airport shuttle service for a fee and upon request.
