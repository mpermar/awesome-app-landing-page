Description

The rooms at the Gran Hotel Elba Estepona & Thalaso Spa are air-conditioned and elegantly decorated. All rooms have satellite TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The Elba complex has 3 themed restaurants and offers all-inclusive service. In the summer months, activities are organized in the children's club.

Elba Estepona's spa has a sea water circuit, hot tub, sauna and igloo, for a fee. A wide variety of relaxing, therapeutic or beauty treatments are offered.

The hotel is a 20-minute drive from Marbella and is well connected to the AP7 motorway.
