Description

This modern and stylish apartment features 2 double bedrooms and 2 bathrooms with a spa bath and a hairdryer. The living room includes a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, an iPod docking station and a sofa. The kitchen is equipped with an oven, dishwasher, microwave and electric kettle.

The Mar Azul ApartHotel also offers several shared use facilities, such as sauna, hot tub, fitness center, garden, games room and library.

Malaga is 90 km away and Malaga airport is 50 minutes away by car. There is free private parking.
