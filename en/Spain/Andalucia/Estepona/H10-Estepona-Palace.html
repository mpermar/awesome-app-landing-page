Description

The H10 Hotel Estepona Palace features an Andalusian-style architecture combined with an avant-garde design. The bright rooms are elegantly decorated and feature tiled floors, a minibar and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The Despacio Spa at Estepona Palace features a sauna, Turkish bath and fitness center. The indoor spa pool has water jets and hot tub.

The Alborán restaurant serves buffet meals and offers show cooking. The property has a poolside restaurant and a mini club. In summer, beach loungers and a chillout terrace with sea views are also offered.

The town center is 2.5 km away.
