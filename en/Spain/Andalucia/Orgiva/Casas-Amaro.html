Description

The houses have free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, heating, private garden and parking. The living area has a sofa, TV and dining table. The kitchenette is equipped with a washing machine, oven and microwave.

There is a private bathroom with a bath or shower. Towels and bed linen are also provided.

The Sierra Nevada National Park is just 12 km from the property. The Mediterranean coast is a 50-minute drive south.

Granada is 55 minutes away and Federico García Lorca Airport is 78 km away.
