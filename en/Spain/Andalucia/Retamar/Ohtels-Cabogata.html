Description

Rooms at CaboGata are simply decorated and have air conditioning and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

The CaboGata's buffet restaurant offers live Mediterranean cuisine. The pool bar is open during the summer months.

The Minos spa has an indoor pool and hot tub, available for a fee. There are also treatment booths and a gym.

Ohtels Cabogata offers views of the Mediterranean Sea and is next to the Alborán Golf Course. In addition, it is well connected with the picturesque beaches of the Cabo de Gata park. Almeria airport is just 4 km from the complex.
