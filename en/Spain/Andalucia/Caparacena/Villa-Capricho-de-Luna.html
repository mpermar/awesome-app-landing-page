Description

The house consists of 4 bedrooms, living room with fireplace, flat screen TV, 2 bathrooms with shower and an external bathroom in the pool area (available in summer).

In addition, the house has 2 terraces (one of them with barbecue) and free private parking.

In the surroundings you can practice water activities. Villa Capricho de Luna is 31 km from the famous Alhambra and 26.6 km from the nearest Federico García Lorca Airport in Granada-Jaén.
