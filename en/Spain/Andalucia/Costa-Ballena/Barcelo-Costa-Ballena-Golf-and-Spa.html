Description

The rooms at the Barceló Costa Ballena Golf & Spa are spacious and bright and have a furnished terrace overlooking the pool, the golf course and the sea.

At the Barceló Costa Ballena Golf & Spa there is a hot tub, fitness center, beauty salon and massage service.

During the summer, a free shuttle service to the beach is provided and entertainment activities for children and adults are organized, as well as live music shows.

Barceló Costa Ballena is located in the Bay of Cádiz, surrounded by protected natural dunes, a 10-minute drive from the beaches of Rota and 36 km from Jerez Airport.
