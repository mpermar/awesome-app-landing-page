Description

The ALEGRÍA Costa Ballena's restaurant serves Mediterranean dishes and the bar prepares light meals and drinks. There is room service.

The hotel has gardens, a paddle tennis court and an entertainment program during the summer. In addition, it offers a shuttle service to the beach.
