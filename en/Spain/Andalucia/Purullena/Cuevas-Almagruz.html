Description

This country-style accommodation is located in Purullena, near Guadix. Its apartments occupy renovated troglodyte caves with extraordinary results.

The area is well connected by road. Granada is about half an hour away by car, and the coast and the ski resort of Sierra Nevada are 1 hour away by car.

The Almagruz Caves has an outdoor pool and children's area.

Guests receive a free guided tour of the interpretation center of Almagruz and the megalithic park of Gorafe and the Toril aqueduct. The Almagruz Caves organizes other excursions, such as tapas tastings and wine tastings.
