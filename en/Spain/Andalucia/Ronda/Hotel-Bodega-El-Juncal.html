Description

The Juncal is located in a beautiful stone building located in a rural estate. The rooms have air conditioning, TV and parquet floor. It also includes a free guided tour of the vineyards and the winery.

Juncal's restaurant serves Spanish cuisine. In addition, guests can enjoy a drink on the terrace or in the gardens, where barbecues are organized during the summer months. There is free Wi-Fi in public areas.

The surrounding mountains are ideal for hiking and cycling. The beautiful historic center of Ronda and the famous gorge are only 5 minutes away by car. The nearest airport is Malaga, located 85 minutes away by car.
