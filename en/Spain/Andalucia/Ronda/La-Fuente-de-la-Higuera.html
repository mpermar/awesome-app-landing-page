Description

You can also enjoy a dip in the beautiful pool in the hotel gardens, or simply lie down for sunbathing in the summer months. In winter, you can relax by reading a book in the hotel lobby, which has a fireplace.

La Higuera is in a renovated oil mill, so it creates a traditional and familiar atmosphere, which will help you have a relaxing holiday.

La Fuente de la Higuera also has an excellent restaurant, where you can try local cuisine without leaving the hotel.
