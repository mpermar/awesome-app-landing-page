Description

This accommodation is 5 minutes walk from the beach. This Iberostar is located next to Isla Canela Beach, 1 km long, and offers views of the Atlantic Ocean. The complex is surrounded by gardens and has an outdoor pool and spa.

There is free WiFi throughout the property.

The modern rooms at Isla Canela have air conditioning, a balcony and a contemporary style. All rooms have a minibar, flat-screen satellite TV and a full bathroom with a hairdryer.

In addition, the Iberostar Chef restaurant offers buffet dishes and show cooking. There is also a poolside bar and a gastrobar with an evening entertainment program.

The Sensations spa center features an indoor pool and hot tubs. It offers massage and aromatherapy treatments. In addition, the establishment has a gym and a spinning room.

Various activities can be practiced nearby, such as horse riding, golfing or fishing in the nearby Guadiana River. The beaches of Isla Cristina are 35 minutes away by car.
