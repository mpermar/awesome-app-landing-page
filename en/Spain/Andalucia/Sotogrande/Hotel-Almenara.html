Description

The rooms at the Almenara are spacious, decorated in natural tones and have air conditioning, stunning views of the golf course and gardens and minibar. Free WiFi is provided throughout the premises.

The Almenara Golf Resort's Spa Elysium features a hot tub, gym and sauna. It also houses the Sotogrande golf academy and a riding center.

There are several restaurants of Andalusian and international cuisine. The property has outdoor terraces and the Pentagrama's bar.

Almenara Golf Hotel is just 5 km from the Costa del Sol and 25 km from Gibraltar Airport. Discounts are offered for nearby golf courses.
