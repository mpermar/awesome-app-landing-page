Description

This accommodation is a 6-minute walk from the beach. Barceló Punta Umbría Beach Resort is a complex located 200 meters from the beach of Punta Umbría. It offers 2 large outdoor pools, spa and several restaurants.

Punta Umbria Resort features free Wi-Fi access throughout and features elegant décor. It offers cozy and bright rooms with air conditioning, satellite TV and minibar. Most have views of the pool area or the natural landscape of Los Enebrales.

The complex has 4 buffet restaurants serving a wide variety of Mediterranean and international dishes. There are 6 bars distributed in the lobby and pool areas. On request, gluten-free dishes are prepared.

The spa and wellness center has a thermal circuit that includes an indoor pool with water jets, thermal sun loungers and contrast shower, available for a fee. A large fitness center and massage and hydrotherapy treatments are also available for a fee.

For a fee, private parking is provided. The Barceló Punta Umbria has many sports facilities, such as paddle tennis courts, sports courts and archery area. You will find several cycling routes around the complex.
