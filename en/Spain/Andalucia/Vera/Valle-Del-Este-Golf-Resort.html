Description

The golf course of the Valle Del Este Golf Resort has 18 holes and is surrounded by a 9 km panoramic path. Also, on Vera beach there is a beach club with swimming pool and sailing school.

The Valle del Este offers guests free access to the gym and a spa with rain showers, sauna, Turkish shower and hot tub, all available for a fee.

The buffet restaurant serves international cuisine and overlooks the golf course. There is also a grill restaurant by the pool and a restaurant in the hotel's beach club.

The AquaVera water park is a 15-minute drive from the Golf Resort. The hotel is next to the A-7 motorway, which runs along the Spanish Mediterranean coast.
