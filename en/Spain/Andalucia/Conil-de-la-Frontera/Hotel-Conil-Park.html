Description

Hotel Conil Park features simple and striking décor and includes air-conditioned accommodation with flat-screen satellite TV. Its rooms have a balcony, while the apartments have a kitchenette, seating area and terrace.

The hotel restaurant serves a buffet breakfast and a selection of Mediterranean dishes for lunch and dinner. There is also a poolside bar overlooking the sea and a children's club open during July and August.

The gym at the Conil Park complex has a sauna.

Hotel Conil Park is next to La Atalaya Park, just a 5-minute walk from the center of Conil de la Frontera. The picturesque coastal towns of Sancti Petri and La Barrosa are 30 minutes away by car.
