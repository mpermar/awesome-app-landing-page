Description

All rooms at Playacálida are bright and equipped with air conditioning and satellite TV. There is also a minibar and a fully equipped bathroom.

The Playacálida Spa Hotel Luxury has 4 more pools, including a river-style pool. The spa has Turkish baths, sauna and indoor pool.

Mediterranean cuisine is served in the hotel's buffet restaurant. There is also a bar and a daily entertainment program.

The center of Almuñécar is approximately 3.5 km from Playacálida. Malaga and its airport are less than 1 hour away by car.
