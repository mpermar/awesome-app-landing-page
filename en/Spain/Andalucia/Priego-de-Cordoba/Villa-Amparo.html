Description

The accommodation has a seating area and a kitchen with a dishwasher. It also includes a flat-screen TV and DVD player. The bathroom is shared and has a bidet and shower.

The establishment has a ping pong table and is in an ideal area for hiking. Villa Amparo is 20 km from Cabra, 19 km from Rute and 48 km from Federico García Lorca Granada-Jaén Airport.
