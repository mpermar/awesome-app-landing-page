Description

Featuring an outdoor heated pool, Hotel & Spa La Residencia Puerto is set in Tarifa, 180 metres from Los Lances beach and Playa Chica beach.

This 4-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk and free WiFi. All rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV and desk.

Each room comes with a private bathroom with free toiletries. A buffet breakfast is available daily at Hotel & Spa La Residencia Puerto.

There is also a bar and a restaurant serving light meals. La Residencia Puerto is 11 km from Punta Paloma.
