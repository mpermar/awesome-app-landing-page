Description

The rooms include air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, coffee maker, kettle and a private bathroom. Some of them have a seating area.

This property has a concierge service.

The surrounding area is ideal for horse riding and cycling. In addition, guests can use the bicycle loan service. Balcones de Piedad is 24 km from the town of Guadix and 16 km from Baza. The nearest airport is Federico García Lorca Granada-Jaén Airport, 79 km away.
