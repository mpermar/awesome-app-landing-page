Description

The rooms at Los Monteros overlook the gardens, the sea or Marbella, are spacious and equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV and a private bathroom with bathrobe, hairdryer and free toiletries. The 5 * GL state-of-the-art spa center offers massage services from 11:00 to 19:00 for a fee.

A buffet breakfast with champagne is served every morning at the Flamingo restaurant, located next to the pool. There is also an à la carte restaurant, El Corzo, in addition to live shows and a children's club in the summer months.

Hotel Los Monteros is less than 200 meters from Santa Clara Golf Club, 7 minutes 'drive from the center of Marbella, 45 km from Malaga Airport and 55 minutes' drive from Gibraltar.

Free private parking, an airport shuttle service is available for a fee and a free shuttle service to La Cabane Beach Club.
