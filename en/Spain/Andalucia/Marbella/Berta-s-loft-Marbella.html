Description

The apartment has 1 double bed, 1 sofa bed, bathroom with bathtub, shower, hairdryer and free toiletries, bed linen and towels.

The apartment also includes a flat-screen TV and a kitchenette with a small stove, microwave, coffee maker and utensils.

The apartment complex also has a garden area and offers free private parking. Bertas´s loft is 600 meters from Nikki Beach, 8 minutes 'drive from Marbella Golf and Country Club, 18 km from Puerto Banús and 40 minutes' drive from Malaga Airport.
