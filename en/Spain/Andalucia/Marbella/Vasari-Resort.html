Description

The pool bar serves breakfast and lunch and is open from April to September.

Vasari apartments are spacious and feature a living room with satellite TV and a modern kitchen with a hob, microwave and washing machine.

Vasari Resort has a children's play area and a free Wi-Fi zone. The complex has 3 paddle courts and a hairdressing and beauty salon.

The center of Marbella is just 7 km away and Las Brisas and Los Naranjos golf courses are 5 km away.
