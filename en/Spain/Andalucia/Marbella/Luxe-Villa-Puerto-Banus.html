Description

The kitchen is equipped with a dishwasher, oven and microwave. The apartment also has a flat-screen TV and 3 bathrooms.

Facilities include a tennis court. In the immediate vicinity there are 10 golf courses. Luxe Villa Puerto Banus is 44 km from Torremolinos and 33 km from Ronda. During the stay you can practice various activities, such as horse riding and cycling. Malaga Airport is 47 km away.

Close enough to be in the bustle, far enough out for peace

"Just returned from a lovely stay at Luxe Villa Puerto Banus with the missus and here are my thoughts…So the villa itself. A wonderful large villa furnished to a very high standard with everything you could wish for – decent ipod dock/stereo, satellite tv, washing machine, dishwasher, large fridge freezer, hairdryers, great little terrace with table and chairs, perfect for breakfast and lunch outside. Whilst my stay was just me and my wife you could comfortably sleep a couple of families with all of the additional beds/sofabeds and numerous bathrooms located throughout. Going back to the beds, they are soooo comfy, that and the great blackout blinds meant I slept like a log that had taken a plethora of sleeping tablets, stuffed its ears with cotton wool and put out a “do not disturb” sign.The complex itself is a great place to chill and hang out. Just outside the villa, is a well-kept garden area with one of the complex’s three pools and some sun loungers that you just help yourself to – no more than 10 steps from door to flop. Just what we needed!Locationwise the villa is a short walk, 15 mins or so (or cheap cab) to the bustling centre of Puerto Banus, with the marina and its close by beaches with an even shorter walk to the famous/infamous La Sala and its sister restaurant The Oak. Tip - the food at The Oak is great, try the steak and lobster, fantastic! I found the villa’s location perfect for us, close enough that we could get everywhere we wanted to be easily but just outside the bustle so we got the peace we were also seeking. Just outside the complex is a great little café bar, an ice cream shop, a shop that sells all you could imagine you’d need for a holiday (and some stuff you couldn’t imagine you’d need), and as you’re self-catering a decent supermarket - although just round the corner are a few good restaurants if you really don’t want to wander far for dinner!"
