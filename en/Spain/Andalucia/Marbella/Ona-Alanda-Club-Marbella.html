Description

The apartments are spacious and bright and have air conditioning, a private balcony, a well-equipped kitchenette and a living area with satellite TV. Daily cleaning service is provided.

The Arlanda Club apartments are a short walk from the bars and shops of the Elvira neighborhood. The Marbella golf club is located just 1 km away and Marbella, about 12 km away. There is free parking.

The Alanda Club has several squash courts, a games room with a pool table and a children's club during the high season.

The hotel restaurant serves buffet breakfast, Spanish cuisine specialties and a variety of Mediterranean and international dishes. During the summer months, the hotel offers live music on the terrace.
