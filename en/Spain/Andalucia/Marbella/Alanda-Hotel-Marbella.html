Description

This hotel overlooks the Mediterranean Sea and the Sierra Blanca mountain range. It has modern and elegant rooms, decorated with neutral tones and wooden floors. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

The hotel houses the Aguas del Sol spa, with a full hydrothermal circuit that includes a hot tub, sauna and relaxation area. The property also offers massage services and a fitness center, available for a fee.

The pool bar offers light meals and snacks. No pork products or alcoholic drinks are served in any of the hotel's restaurants and bars.

Santa Ana Beach is 500 meters away. Marbella and Puerto Banús are less than 3 km from the property. Malaga Airport is 56 km from Alanda Hotel Marbella.
