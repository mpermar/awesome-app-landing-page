Description

This luxurious 5-star hotel offers 4 outdoor swimming pools with a bar, sun loungers and free Balinese beds, a 50 m² private spa, small fitness center, restaurants and gardens. All rooms have free Wi-Fi, a seating area and a furnished balcony.

The Estrella de Mar provides access to a beach club for a fee. It has a spa, places to eat and wellness activities in its 4,000 square meters. The spa and wellness area is equipped with 7 massage rooms, hot tub, sauna, Turkish bath and fitness center with panoramic sea views. Various fitness activities are organized, such as aquapilates and fityoga. It remains open all year and access comes at an additional cost.

All rooms at Vincci Estrella del Mar are bright and spacious and include a balcony or terrace with views of the sea, swimming pools or gardens. They also have air conditioning, heating, 42 "flat screen TV with satellite channels, minibar and free tea / coffee making facilities.

In the Estrella del Mar beach club there is an à la carte restaurant specializing in Mediterranean cuisine. You will also find sun loungers and Balinese beds by the pool and on the beach, a lounge bar, gardens and an adult-only infinity pool with sea views (access comes at an extra cost). Restaurants prepare special dishes for people with food allergies or intolerances.

Vincci Estrella del Mar Hotel is 400 meters from Elviria Beach, 8 km from Marbella and 32 km from Malaga. The Greenlife Marbella golf club is nearby. There is free outdoor parking.
