Description

This air-conditioned apartment has a terrace, a living room with a flat-screen satellite TV and a kitchenette with a microwave and refrigerator. The sleeping area has 1 double bed or 2 single beds. There is also a bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer and towels and bed linen are provided.

The Marbella Elviria Apartment features a garden, terrace, water and fitness sports facilities, a shared lounge and a vending machine. In the surroundings you can practice various activities, such as fishing, diving and windsurfing. In addition, there is free parking.

The Santa María and Santa Clara golf clubs are 2 km from the property. Malaga airport is 33 km away.
