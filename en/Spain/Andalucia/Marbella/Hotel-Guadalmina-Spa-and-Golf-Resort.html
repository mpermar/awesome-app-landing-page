Description

The rooms at Guadalmina Spa & Golf are spacious and have air conditioning and a terrace overlooking the sea, gardens or golf course. They also have a minibar, safe, free Wi-Fi and flat-screen satellite TV.

The hotel offers free access to the fitness room, solarium and outdoor swimming pools. It also provides special discounts for access to the spa, which includes a Turkish bath, hydrotherapy pool and Finnish sauna. In addition, guests can enjoy a wide variety of massages and beauty treatments.

The Taray restaurant overlooks the sea and serves buffet breakfast and dinner. The Cocoa restaurant specializes in Mediterranean cuisine and the Acqua Terrace Bar has cocktails and snacks. There is also a beach club open in summer with sun loungers and large beds for vips with cava, fruit, cold towels and access to the pool available for a fee. Also, in summer a children's club is organized.

Around the hotel there is an 18-hole golf course and a 9-hole golf course. The hotel also has a putting green.

Hotel Guadalmina Spa & Golf Resort is located just 6 km from Puerto Banús and a 10-minute drive from the historic center of Marbella. It offers secure parking for a fee. Malaga airport is a 40-minute drive away.

San Pedro de Alcántara is a great choice for travelers interested in boardwalks, the coast and sunbathing.
