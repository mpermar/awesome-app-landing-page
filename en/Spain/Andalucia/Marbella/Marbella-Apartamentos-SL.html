Description

The accommodation features a terrace, flat-screen satellite TV, CD player, DVD player, dining area, balcony and kitchen with dishwasher, oven, microwave, toaster, fridge, stove and coffee maker. All accommodations have 2 private bathrooms with bath, shower and bidet. Some apartments have a walk-in shower. Towels, pool towels and bed linen are provided.

The property has a bar and a restaurant.

The area is perfect for golfing and windsurfing. In the surroundings you can perform various activities, such as snorkeling, diving and horse riding. Marbella Apartamentos SL is 9 km from Marbella Bus Station and 60 km from Malaga Airport.
