Description

The caves have been renovated and renovated with pleasure. All of them retain their original splendor and include modern and luxurious amenities. The caves have a traditional style, original pavement, vaulted ceilings and rustic furniture. They also have a separate kitchen and free Wi-Fi.

The hotel has excellent facilities such as reception, outdoor swimming pool, children's playroom and fully equipped meeting room. In addition, there is a forest area with a gazebo and a playground.

Experiences

06/25/2018

Wonderful family stay near Guadix - Estancia en familia cerca de Guadix

"We stayed in a 2BR/2BA cave (los emigrantes) which was very comfortable for our family of 5 (3 children ages 6, 5 and 3). The cave was well-equipped with everything we needed to make lunch both days we were there. The room was perfectly clean, towels and basic toiletries were provided. The personnel we interacted with were all very friendly, the breakfast was a great value, and the pool was wonderful. There is a sectioned off area for children which was a good depth for my children (all > 1m tall). It took about 10 minutes to Guadix from the caves. We didn't have a long enough stay to use all the services, there is also a playground and a small inside play area with toys, coloring books etc. We had a great stay and would absolutely recommend this location for a family stay."
