Description

Offering a restaurant and seasonal outdoor swimming pool, El Cercado is set in Andalusia’s beautiful Alpujarras Mountains.

Located in Bérchules, the hotel features free Wi-Fi and a spa and wellness centre available for a surcharge Each heated rustic-style apartment has a fireplace, a kitchenette equipped with a microwave, and a bathroom with a shower.

Traditional dishes from the Alpujarra región are served in the on-site restaurant.

El Cercado’s spa offers various beauty treatments, massages, a hot tub, sauna, and Turkish bath, among others.

The Costa Tropical is 40 km from the property, and Granada, with its world-famous Alhambra Palace, is 100 km away.
