Description

The beautiful ecological villas of La Ermita are located in an old church and retain some original details, such as stone walls, beamed ceilings and courtyards. There is a living room with a sofa bed, a flat-screen TV and an iPod dock. The kitchen is equipped with a microwave, refrigerator, oven and toaster.

The nearby towns of Las Hortichuelas and Las Negras are 2 km from the complex.

The villas, located in the Rodalquilar Valley, are in an ideal area for hiking, cycling, horse riding and fishing. Playazo Beach is only 1.5 km away.

Carboneras is a 50-minute drive from La Ermita. Almeria is 40 km away.
