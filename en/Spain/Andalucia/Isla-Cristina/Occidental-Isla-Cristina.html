Description

Occidental Isla Cristina is 20 km from the Portuguese border and a 45-minute drive from Huelva. Isla Cristina is a charming fishing town surrounded by the protected area of the Marshes of Isla Cristina.

Occidental Isla Cristina has a buffet restaurant and a poolside bar. It also offers entertainment activities at night in the lobby bar, as well as children's activities, including camping in the hotel garden. There is free WiFi throughout the property.

The rooms are bright and decorated in soft tones. They have a ceiling fan, satellite TV and a minibar. Some enjoy views of the sea.
