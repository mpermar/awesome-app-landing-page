Description

Each room has been decorated inspired by different floral species in the area and whose names have also been baptized. The facilities it has, such as the spa, a large garden and an exceptional restaurant, are aimed at providing a relaxing and relaxing stay with maximum charm.

The Torcal de Antequera is a unique enclave for outdoor activities such as hiking, horseback riding and bird watching.
