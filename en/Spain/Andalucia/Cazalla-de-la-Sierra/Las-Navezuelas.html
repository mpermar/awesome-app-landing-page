Description

The studios, suites, rooms and apartments of Las Navezuelas have a traditional Spanish decoration. They have heating and a private bathroom and the apartments also have a kitchen and a seating area.

Guests can enjoy Andalusian dishes at Navezuela's restaurant. In Cazalla de la Sierra there are cafes, bars and shops.

Huéznal and Los Melonares lakes are 45 minutes away by car. Seville is 75 km away.
