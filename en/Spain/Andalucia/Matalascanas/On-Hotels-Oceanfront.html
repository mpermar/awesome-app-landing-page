Description

Rooms at the On Hotels Oceanfront feature a balcony or terrace, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a safe and a minibar. The private bathroom has a bath or shower, hairdryer and free toiletries.

The hotel is made for adults and has a Japanese à la carte restaurant, a modern bar and nightlife activities. The reception is open 24 hours a day and provides shuttle and car rental services for a fee. There is also free luggage storage.

Several hotels and restaurants are 10 minutes 'drive from On Hotels Oceanfront, 50 minutes' drive from Huelva and 101 km from Seville Airport.
