Description

The units are equipped with a seating and dining area, toaster, fridge and coffee maker. Some have a terrace and / or patio. There are also accommodations with a kitchen equipped with a hob. Cottage Cuernavaca Ranch offers free Wi-Fi and provides towels and bed linen.

In addition, this establishment has a covered outdoor living room with a fireplace and a games room within the common areas. There is also a delivery service for food products.

In this establishment you can play minigolf and the surroundings are ideal for activities such as horse riding, fishing and hiking. Cottage Cuernavaca Ranch is 28 km from Grazalema. The closest airport to the Cuernavaca Ranch Cottage is Jerez and is 37 km away.
