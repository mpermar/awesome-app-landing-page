Description

The outdoor pool overlooks the sea and is equipped with umbrellas and sun loungers for free. The spa includes a hot tub, sauna, Turkish bath and fitness center. There is miniature golf and a bicycle rental service is provided. There is also a thermal pool available for a fee.

Puertobahia's rooms are spacious, receive plenty of natural light and are equipped with air conditioning. They also have a 32-inch flat-screen TV and a private terrace. Guests can access the fitness center for free. Some rooms have stunning sea views.

The Puertobahia has a restaurant serving local dishes and extensive gardens where concerts and live shows are offered during the summer.

This hotel is a 5-minute drive from the center of El Puerto de Santa María and about 15 km from Cádiz. Jerez Airport is 30 km away. The train station, located 600 meters away, offers connections with Madrid, Seville and Cádiz. There is free parking at the hotel (subject to availability).
