Description

The rooms are soundproofed and feature flat-screen TVs, minibars, and safes. The private bathroom includes a shower. There is a bathrobe, slippers and toiletries. Some rooms have a French balcony.

This elegant hotel is housed in a 19th-century building and houses both a gourmet restaurant and a dining area with a fireplace. Use of the sauna and massage service comes at an additional cost.

Fuengirola Train Station is just 500 meters from the property, and Malaga Airport is a 20-minute drive away. The area is ideal for golf. The nearest golf club, Chaparral, is 6 km away.
