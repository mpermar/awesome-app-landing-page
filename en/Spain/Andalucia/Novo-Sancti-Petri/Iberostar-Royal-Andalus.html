Description

The Andalus's rooms are simply and brightly decorated and feature air conditioning, satellite TV and a minibar for a fee. There is free WiFi connection.

This hotel has 3 restaurants. Al Andalus serves a daily buffet, Manolete is only open during high season and offers à la carte dishes and Sea Soul prepares lunches during the summer months.

The hotel spa comes at an additional cost and includes a sauna, Turkish bath, hydrotherapy pool and hot tub. There are also sensation showers and relaxation area. An adult-only heated pool is also available for a fee.

The property's golf course is just 250 meters away and there is a tennis court 500 meters away.
