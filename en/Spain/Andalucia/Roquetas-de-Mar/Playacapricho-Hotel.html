Description

The rooms at Playacapricho have a balcony or terrace, air conditioning and heating. They include a safe and WiFi for a fee.

The pool is equipped with slides and hot tub for guests over 15 years. There is also a solarium with free sun loungers and an indoor pool. The Olympo beach club is open in high season.

The buffet restaurant at Playacapricho serves local and international dishes and features show cooking.

In Roquetas de Mar you will find several good quality beaches and water sports facilities. Tennis and horse riding can be practiced nearby. The Playaserena golf course is 400 meters away.

A child up to 14 years old can stay for free if they share a single room with 1 adult or a double room with 2 adults.
