Description

The Playasol has comfortable and cozy rooms with balcony or terrace. Most have sea views. They also include satellite TV and air conditioning or heating, depending on the time of year.

The hotel has a large terrace with free sun loungers, which can be accessed by those over 15 years old. Many treatments are performed at the Acquaplaya wellness and beauty center. The hotel also houses a seasonal gym.

The hotel has a buffet restaurant that serves breakfast, lunch and dinner. Gluten-free dishes are prepared upon request. There is also a lounge bar and a poolside bar, the El Pirata bar, which opens in summer. You will also find a children's mini club for children over 4 years and entertainment activities for adults.

Roquetas de Mar is on the coast of Almeria, in a natural setting. The Playaserena golf course is 800 meters away.
