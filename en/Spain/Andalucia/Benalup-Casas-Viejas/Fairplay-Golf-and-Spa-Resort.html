Description

The Fairplay complex is designed as a small typical Andalusian white town, with narrow streets and squares. The rooms and suites are modernly decorated and include a flat-screen TV, free Wi-Fi and a minibar. Most have views of the golf course, courtyard or field.

Fairplay guests can enjoy 3 à la carte restaurants. La Atalaya restaurant serves Mediterranean food, while Mar de Campo offers modern cuisine made with local products. There is also a buffet restaurant called Los Acebuches, a poolside brasserie and a Club House bar with an outdoor dining area and views of the golf course. The complex also has 4 bars.

The luxury spa and wellness center features an Aquamedic pool, hammam, hot tub and sauna. In addition, hydrotherapy, massage and facial treatments are offered for a fee. The use of the gym is free for guests.

Fairplay Golf & Spa Resort is located in the province of Cádiz, 25 minutes by car from the beautiful beaches of the Costa de la Luz and 1 hour by car from Gibraltar. The historic cities of Jerez de la Frontera and Cádiz are about 45 minutes away by car.
