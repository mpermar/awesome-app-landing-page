Description

Exe Gran Hotel Solucar's rooms include air conditioning, satellite TV, minibar, safe, desk and private bathroom with hairdryer. In addition, some are duplex and have a lounge.

The Don Curro restaurant serves traditional Andalusian cuisine. In addition, guests can enjoy a buffet breakfast in the patio surrounding the pool, as well as light snacks in the hotel cafeteria.

The property has a 24-hour reception and a tour desk with car and bicycle rental services. The Isla Mágica theme park is only 20 minutes away by car.
