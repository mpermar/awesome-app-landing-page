Description

The rooms at the Sol Don Pablo hotel are spacious and have air conditioning and a balcony. Most have partial sea views. There is also a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

The Sol Don hotel complex offers access to other swimming pools and the spa / wellness center for a fee. It also has a gym, tennis court and entertainment program.

The Don Pablo hotel has a buffet restaurant that is open all year and a bar with a terrace next to the pool.

The Sol Don Pablo hotel is a 15-minute drive from Malaga airport and a 15-minute walk from the center of Torremolinos. The Parador Málaga golf club and the Aqualand water park are 4 km away.
