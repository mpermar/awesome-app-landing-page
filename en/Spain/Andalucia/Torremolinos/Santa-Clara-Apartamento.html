Description

The accommodation features a terrace, air conditioning, a dining area and a seating area with a flat-screen TV. It also includes a kitchen equipped with an oven, microwave, toaster, fridge and coffee maker. The bathroom is private and has a bath or shower. The apartment has free WiFi.

Santa Clara Apartamento has a spa center and offers massages.

The property also offers car and bicycle rental and is in a perfect area to play golf. In the surroundings you can also enjoy other activities, such as horse riding, snorkeling and windsurfing. Santa Clara Apartamento is 1.2 km from Crocodile Park and 6 km from Malaga Airport.
