Description

Accommodations include a flat-screen satellite TV, a CD player, a kitchen with a dishwasher, an oven, a microwave, a toaster, a coffee maker and a kettle, a private bathroom with a bathtub and a hairdryer, towels and bed linen. Some have air conditioning and a seating and / or dining area.

Playamar has a thermal bath and an outdoor pool and offers grocery delivery and packed lunches.

It has a tennis court, ping pong and minigolf and is in an ideal area for golf, horse riding, snorkeling, windsurfing and diving. Playamar is 1.5 km from Aqualand Torremolinos and 500 meters from Casa de los Navajas. The nearest airport is Malaga, located 5 km from Playamar.
