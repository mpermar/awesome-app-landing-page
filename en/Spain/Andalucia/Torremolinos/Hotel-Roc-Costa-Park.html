Description

All rooms at the Hotel Roc Costa Park are spacious and equipped with satellite TV and air conditioning. They also include a private bathroom.

The hotel has a games room with a pool table, mini-golf course and children's play area. In addition, it hosts live shows and has an animation team.

The property offers a free shuttle service to the city center of Torremolinos and the beach from April to October.

Hotel Roc Costa Park's restaurant serves local dishes. There are also poolside bars.

The free shuttle bus service, with limited places, links to the beach and the center of Torremolinos, 2.1 km away. Malaga airport is 6 km away.
