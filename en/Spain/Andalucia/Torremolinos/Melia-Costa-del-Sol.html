Description

The rooms are modern, spacious and bright. They include a terrace with views of the Mediterranean, air conditioning, safe, flat-screen satellite TV and a well-equipped bathroom.

Meliá Costa del Sol serves a buffet and continental breakfast. For dinner, prepare Mediterranean cuisine specialties and à la carte dishes. The Windward bar offers live music.

The Thalasso spa, available for a fee, includes a dynamic pool, sauna and steam room.

In addition, Carihuela Beach, the center of Torremolinos and Puerto Marina are 1 km from the hotel, while Benalmádena Casino is 5 km away and Malaga Airport is 8 km away.
