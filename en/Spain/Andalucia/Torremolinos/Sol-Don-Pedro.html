Description

Sol Don Pedro's rooms are spacious and have air conditioning, private bathroom and satellite TV. The double rooms include a balcony, and those on the ground floor are equipped with a terrace. A safe and a minibar are also available for a fee.

Don Pedro is located in the Sol Dones complex, which is made up of 3 hotels. It houses a buffet restaurant, and guests also have access to the bars and restaurants of nearby hotels. Other swimming pools, the fitness center, a spa, the tennis court and the leisure areas, including a seasonal children's club, can also be used.

Sol Don Pedro is a 15-minute walk from the center of Torremolinos, which has easy access to the beach and water sports facilities. There are many golf courses in the area, such as the Parador Málaga Golf, located less than 4 km away.

The hotel complex is 30 minutes drive from the city and Malaga airport and about 25 minutes drive from the town of Mijas.
