Description

The private heated pool has a removable child safety fence. This spacious and bright house includes a solarium, garden, barbecue, 5 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, 2 additional toilets, a well-equipped kitchen, a flat-screen TV lounge area, a library, air conditioning and free Wi-Fi.

Villa Celia is 2.9 km from Crocodile Park and 3 km from Aqualand Torremolinos and Malaga Airport. In the area you can practice various activities, such as golf, windsurfing and cycling.
