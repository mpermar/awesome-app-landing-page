Description

All rooms at NH Córdoba Guadalquivir feature modern décor and are equipped with air conditioning, heating and satellite TV. The bathroom is private and includes Modern Nature toiletries.

The hotel has a restaurant and bar, Al Punto, where guests can enjoy a selection of drinks and regional dishes.

The center of Córdoba, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is located on the other side of the Roman bridge, just 7 minutes away on foot.
