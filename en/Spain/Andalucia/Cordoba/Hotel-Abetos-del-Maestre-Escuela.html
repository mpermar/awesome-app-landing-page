Description

The rooms have free Wi-Fi, a safe, a flat-screen TV with international channels and a private bathroom with a bath or shower and free toiletries.

The Master School enjoys a quiet location and is surrounded by gardens. Outside there are 2 soccer fields 7 and 2 swimming pools that open from May to October.

The hotel also has a traditional Andalusian interior patio and a terrace with views of the mountains where you can eat or have a drink. Breakfast is served in the hotel cafeteria.

The reception is open 24 hours a day and provides tourist information service. Free shuttle service to the city center is available every day (subject to availability).

The property includes free parking, a bicycle storage area and a bicycle repair service.
