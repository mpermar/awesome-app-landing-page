Description

Hotel Jerez is traditionally decorated with wooden furniture and offers air-conditioned rooms with satellite TV and a bathroom with free toiletries.

In addition, El Cartujano restaurant serves traditional Mediterranean dishes and, on weekends, barbecues are prepared on the terrace. There is also a coffee bar by the pool.

The Jerez's spa has an indoor pool, sauna and oxygen tank. Aromatherapy treatments, wraps and massages are provided.

This hotel is 1 km from the center of Jerez de la Frontera and a 15-minute walk from the Andalusian School of Equestrian Art and Hontoria Park. The bullring of Jerez and the historic center are a 20-minute walk away. The reception provides tourist information.
