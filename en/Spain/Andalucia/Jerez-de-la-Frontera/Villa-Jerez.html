Description

Villa Jerez is right next to the Plaza del Caballo, within walking distance of most important monuments of this sunny Andalusian city. The hotel has a steam bath, gym, swimming pool, massage service, restaurant and cafeteria.

Around the hotel there are numerous samples of the city's rich cultural heritage. Jerez is known mostly for 3 things: horses, flamenco and wine. It is recommended to visit one of the local wineries.
