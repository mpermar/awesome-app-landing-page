Description

Barceló Montecastillo also has tennis courts, a fitness center, spa with hydrotherapy circuit, sauna and Turkish bath. It also houses an 18-hole golf course designed by Jack Nicklaus. The property provides 1 small bed and bowls of pet food.

El Lagar restaurant prepares specialties of international and Spanish cuisine, while Ricardo bar serves snacks. There is also a children's playground and a separate children's pool.

The Barceló Montecastillo Resort Hotel is well connected with the A-4 and A-382 motorways. The establishment is also a short drive away from the Formula 1 Jerez circuit, a 15-minute drive from Jerez and a 1-hour drive from Seville.
