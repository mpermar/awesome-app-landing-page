Description

Molino La Nava offers quiet accommodation and has extensive olive groves located a short distance from the Sierra Morena mountain range. The property is 45 km from the historic city of Córdoba, where you can visit the famous Moorish-style mosque. The accommodation is also within walking distance of the municipality of Montoro, which houses whitewashed buildings and narrow streets.

This country house has a spacious living area with a fireplace. There is also an outdoor terrace that enjoys views of the sunset and surrounding olive groves.
