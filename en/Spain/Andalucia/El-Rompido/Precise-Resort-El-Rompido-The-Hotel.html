Description

The Precise El Rompido hotel, set among beautiful gardens, has bright and beautiful rooms with balconies, most with sea views. All include satellite TV and a minibar.

El Rompido offers a buffet breakfast. It also has a lounge bar and a restaurant serving local cuisine.

The Rompido's spa has an indoor pool, Turkish baths and sauna. It also offers massages and facials.

The Precise Resort offers free parking and is an hour's drive from Faro and Seville Airport. Huelva is less than 16 km away.
