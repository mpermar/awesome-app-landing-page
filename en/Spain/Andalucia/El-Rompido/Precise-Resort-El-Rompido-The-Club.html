Description

The Precise El Rompido is surrounded by fabulous gardens and presents a simple and elegant decoration. All apartments have a kitchenette and a spacious living area. There is free Wi-Fi connection throughout.

Rompido's restaurant serves a daily buffet breakfast. The bar / lounge and restaurant service includes 5 excellent regional and seasonal cuisine.

Guests can access some of the facilities at the El Rompido complex, including tennis and paddle tennis courts. Spa facilities, as well as the fitness center, are subject to a surcharge. There is also an entertainment program for children and adults. Free access to the beach is offered.

The famous Aquapolis water park is 4 km away, while Huelva is a 30-minute drive from the complex. Boat trips to the beach and train transfer service to the port are offered.
