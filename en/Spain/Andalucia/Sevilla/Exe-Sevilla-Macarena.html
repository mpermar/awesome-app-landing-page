Description

This hotel is located around a typical Sevillian-style courtyard that houses a restaurant and cafeteria. The hotel also has a sports bar equipped with a TV with international sports channels and serves a daily international breakfast buffet made with varied top quality products. In addition, it provides sushi delivery service in the rooms.

The hotel has a 24-hour reception and a tour desk, where information about the surroundings is offered and where tickets are reserved for guided excursions, live shows or golf games. There is also a free shuttle service to the Seville fair.

Hotel Sevilla Macarena is 500 meters from the Guadalquivir River. The Isla Mágica theme park is on the other side of the river and the Cathedral of Seville is a 20-minute walk away. There is a taxi rank and several bus stops in front of the establishment. Seville airport is 17 minutes away by car.

Macarena is a great choice for travelers interested in walking through the city, ancient places and visiting the historic center.
