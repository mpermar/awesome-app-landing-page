Description

The rooms feature period décor and feature air conditioning, a smart TV, a free laptop safe, a seating area and a minibar. The private bathroom includes bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries. Some rooms have a balcony or city views.

The Ateneo Sevilla offers a 24-hour reception, luggage storage and a tour desk. Guests receive a welcome drink upon arrival.

The cathedral of Seville and the Giralda are about 10 minutes away. Real Alcazar is a 15-minute walk away and Santa Justa Train Station is a 20-minute walk away.

Historic center of Seville is a great choice for travelers interested in architecture, sensations and eating tapas.
