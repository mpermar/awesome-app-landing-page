Description

The bungalows have walls decorated in bright colors and tiled floors. They feature a seating area with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. All have air conditioning and heating.

In the nearby town of Los Baladejos there are some restaurants.

Tikal is a 30-minute drive from Vejer de la Frontera, a typical white village in Andalusia. The beaches of Conil de la Frontera, Caños de Meca and El Palmar are about 40 minutes away by car.
