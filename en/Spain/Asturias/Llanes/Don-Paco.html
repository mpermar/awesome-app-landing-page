Description

Don Paco is located next to the walls of the historic center and the promenade of Llanes, in the Asturian region of Green Spain. The baroque style of the building, carefully preserved, contrasts with its modern facilities.

The accommodation houses a restaurant in the impressive chapel, which still retains the original stone and the high and vaulted ceilings, and has a cozy lounge with a fireplace.

There is a golf course nearby and the area is ideal for hiking and cycling.

The establishment has a garden terrace. Discounts are provided for a golf course in the area.

The hotel is 50 meters from an area with free parking.
