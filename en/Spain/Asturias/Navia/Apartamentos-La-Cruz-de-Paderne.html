Description

La Cruz presents a beautiful country-style decoration. The apartments are spacious and air-conditioned. All have a kitchenette and a living area with a flat-screen TV and DVD player.

Guests can relax in the Paderne's lounge, which features DVD movies, games and books. The establishment is located in beautiful gardens, where there is also a terrace.

La Cruz de Paderne has a quiet and rural setting and local activities include fishing and surfing. The staff of the apartments will provide information about the surroundings, beaches and hiking trails. The hiking trails of Las Cascadas de Oneta and Las Cuevas de Andina are located nearby.
