Description

Casa Rural Ofelia presents a typical architecture with stone walls, exposed beams and a tiled roof. It also has a library, shared kitchen and restaurant service available upon request.

All rooms at the Ofelia house have a rustic décor and have pillows and mattresses made of viscoelastic foam. They also include a flat-screen TV, a wardrobe and a bathroom with a hairdryer. Guests can make free use of the TV and DVD player in the lounge.

The establishment is surrounded by a large garden with an orchard. In addition, there are 2 hiking trails near the Casa Rural Ofelia. The establishment's staff organizes excursions in the surroundings.

The property is a 10-minute drive from the fishing town of Cudillero. Oviedo is 60 km away.
