Description

Hotel Rural Playa de Aguilar offers beautiful rooms with a terrace. They are equipped with a flat-screen TV, a selection of pillows, a minibar, a safe and a bathroom with a spa bath.

Breakfast is served in the dining room. The hotel has a beautiful terrace overlooking the gardens. The restaurant serves local dishes, such as seafood or Asturian meat, for lunch and dinner.

The hotel enjoys a picturesque location, surrounded by forests and overlooking the sea. It is located on the Camino de Santiago.

This hotel is 3 km from the A8 motorway, which connects with Gijón in 35 minutes. Asturias Airport is 12 km from the hotel.
