Description

The rooms at the Hotel del Alto Sella are decorated in a country style. All have private bathrooms and some views of the Sella River.

The hotel library has an 18th-century altarpiece, while the lounge has a fireplace. The hotel also houses a chapel.

The traditional restaurant serves Asturian homemade dishes, including stews, game meat and local trout. There is also a bar.

The hotel is located in the western part of Asturias, just 6 km from Cangas de Onís and 22 km from Covadonga. Very close to the hotel you will find trails. The hotel staff will be happy to help you book other adventure sports.
