Description

This house features a flat-screen TV, a Blu-ray player, a DVD player, a seating area, a kitchen and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries. In addition, La Quintana Sinariega has a barbecue area.

Quintana Sinariega is 7 km from Cangas de Onís and 31 km from Llanes. During the stay you can practice various activities, such as canoeing and hiking. Asturias Airport is the closest and is 74 km from La Quintana Sinariega.
