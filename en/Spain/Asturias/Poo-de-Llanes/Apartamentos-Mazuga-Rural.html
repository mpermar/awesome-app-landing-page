Description

The apartments have bright colors and patterned fabrics. All of them feature free Wi-Fi, satellite TV, DVD player and bathroom with hydromassage shower. They also include a kitchen equipped with an oven, microwave, dishwasher and washing machine. Upon request, a laptop can be provided.

The Mazuga Rural apartments are just 2 km from the town of Llanes, where there are several bars, restaurants and shops.

The Picos de Europa National Park and the Covadonga Lakes are 1 hour away by car. The town of Poo is well connected by the A8 motorway and is 1 hour drive from Santander, Gijón and Oviedo.
