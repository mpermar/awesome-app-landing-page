Description

Hotel Casa Albets has a restaurant, bar, a shared lounge and garden in Lladurs. Among the facilities at this property are a 24-hour front desk and room service, along with free WiFi throughout the property.

Private parking can be arranged at an extra charge. At the hotel, the rooms include a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

All rooms will provide guests with a fridge. Guests at Hotel Casa Albets can enjoy a à la carte or a halal breakfast.

The accommodation offers a terrace. The area is popular for hiking and skiing, and bike hire is available at Hotel Casa Albets.
