Description

Situated next to the ski slopes of Baqueira Beret, Tuc Blanc offers free mountain activities with expert guides.

It is set in the spectacular Aran Valley, just 14 km from Vielha. The Tuc Blanc's convenient location, directly in front of the ski slopes, makes it an ideal base for winter sports.

The hotel also features an indoor pool and a hot tub. The spa offers beauty treatments and physiotherapy.

Besides guests can enjoy of the sauna and Turkish bath. There is breakfast buffet with a wide variety of products including orange juice.
