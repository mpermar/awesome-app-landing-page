Description

Located in Vilafames, LA COVETA - Aptos. Vilapart offers accommodation with a balcony.

Each unit comes with a patio, a fully equipped kitchenette with a microwave, a seating area with a sofa, a flat-screen TV, a washing machine, and a private bathroom with bidet.

For added convenience, the property can provide towels and bed linen for an extra charge.

The apartment offers a children's playground. There is a shared lounge at this property and guests can go hiking and cycling nearby.

Castellón de la Plana is 29 km from LA COVETA - Aptos. Vilapart, while Benicàssim is 38 km away.
