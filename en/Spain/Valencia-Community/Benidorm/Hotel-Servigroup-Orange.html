Description

Servigroup Orange has an ideal setting 5 minutes’ walk from Benidorm’s Levante Beach and 250 metres from the resort centre.

It features an outdoor swimming pool and free WiFi. Each room at the Servigroup has simple, bright décor, and most rooms have a balcony.

All accommodation has air conditioning during the summer months, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fridge and safe.

The Orange’s buffet restaurant serves a selection of Mediterranean dishes and features show cooking.

Guests can enjoy a drink in The Sanctuary bar, or by the pool. The hotel offers an evening entertainment programme with live music and dancing at the bar during the summer months.

Benidorm’s marina and Poniente Beach are just 15 minutes’ walk from the Servigroup Orange.

The nearest golf club is 3 km away. The entrance to the hotel, reception area and the restaurant have access adapted for disabled guests.
