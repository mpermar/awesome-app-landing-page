Description

Situated on Caleta de Fuste Beach, this hotel has a large outdoor pool, great gym and luxurious spa area.

The 462 rooms feature a private balcony or terrace with views of the sea, garden or pool.

Enjoy a varied entertainment programme organised by the Fuerteventura Thalasso Spa team.

The hotel also has tennis and paddle courts, as well as mini golf. You can practise a range of water sports nearby.

Barceló Fuerteventura Thalasso Spa offers a buffet breakfast, and there is also a snack bar by the pool.

In the evening, savour a delicious cocktail from the bar. The hotel is 7 km from Fuerteventura Airport, and Puerto del Rosario is 12 km away.
