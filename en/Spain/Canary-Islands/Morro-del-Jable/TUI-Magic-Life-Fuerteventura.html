Description

Located in Morro del Jable, less than 1 km from Esquinzo Beach, Magic Life Fuerteventura provides accommodation with a tennis court.

With a garden, the property also has a terrace. The 98,000 m² complex has 5 outdoor pools, wellness and fitness centre, and several restaurants and bars.

At the hotel, the rooms have a desk and a flat-screen TV. The private bathroom is equipped with a hair dryer.

All guest rooms at Magic Life Fuerteventura include air conditioning and a wardrobe.

A buffet breakfast is served each morning at the property. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the on-site restaurant, which specialises in International cuisine.

The accommodation offers a children's playground. The area is popular for cycling, and car hire is available at Magic Life Fuerteventura.

Cofete Beach is 8 km from the hotel. The nearest airport is Fuerteventura Airport, 61 km from the property.
