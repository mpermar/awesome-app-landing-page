Description

The SBH Maxorata Resort, on the south-western coast of tropical Fuerteventura, has all of the facilities to be a real ocean paradise.

Stroll down the white beach of Jandia, just a short walk away, or admire the natural beauty of Saladar de Jandia, a nearby protected nature reserve.

Explore the neighbouring harbour town of Morro Jable, where you can enjoy a refreshing drink overlooking the port.

Savour the iconic aroma of aloe vera, and learn about the plant at the information centre.

Delight in the tranquillity of your setting, and gaze out your window at the beautiful views.

Relax on your balcony in the glorious sea breeze. Play in one of the 3 outdoor swimming pools with your kids, or lounge on the sun terrace.

The SBH Maxorata Resort offers something for everyone, with excellent sports facilities, such as bicycle rental, fitness room and games room, and entertainment shows 6 times a week.
