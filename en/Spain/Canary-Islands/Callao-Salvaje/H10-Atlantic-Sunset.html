Description

H10 Atlantic Sunset is located in Callao Salvaje and features a bar. Featuring a 24-hour front desk, this property also has a restaurant and an outdoor pool.

The accommodation provides room service, a concierge service and currency exchange for guests.

All units in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. At H10 Atlantic Sunset, every room is fitted with air conditioning and a private bathroom.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental breakfast. A Spa is available for a surcharge.

H10 Atlantic Sunset offers a children's playground. The property features a buffet restaurant where meals for all-inclusive rates are served and several themed restaurants, available for a surcharge.

Playa de las Americas is 15 km from the hotel, while Los Cristianos is 14 km away.

The nearest airport is Tenerife Sur, 28 km from H10 Atlantic Sunset, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
