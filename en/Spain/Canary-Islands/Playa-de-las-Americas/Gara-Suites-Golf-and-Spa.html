Description

Located in Playa de las Americas, 1 km from Siam Park, Gara Suites Golf & Spa offers amenities including an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre.

Offering a garden, the property is located within 1. 3 km of Piramide de Arona convention centre.

The tour desk is available to assist guests in planning their days out. Featuring a terrace, all guest rooms in the hotel are fitted with a flat-screen TV.

Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower. A buffet breakfast is available every morning at the property.

There is an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of Mediterranean dishes. Gara Suites Golf & Spa offers a children's playground.

Speaking German and English, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical information on the area at the 24-hour front desk.

Safari Shopping Centre is a 16-minute walk from the accommodation, while Chayofa is 2.

6 km from the property. The nearest airport is Tenerife Sur Airport, 14 km from the property.
