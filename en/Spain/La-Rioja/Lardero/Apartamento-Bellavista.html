Description

The Bellavista Apartment includes towels, bed linen, a kitchen with a dishwasher, a microwave and an oven, as well as a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer. In addition, the Bellavista Apartment has a seasonal outdoor pool.

Apartamento Bellavista is 49 km from Vitoria-Gasteiz, 36 km from Haro and 76 km from the nearest Pamplona Airport.
