Description

All guest rooms at the resort are equipped with a seating area. Rooms are equipped with a coffee machine, while certain rooms also offer a balcony and others also have mountain views. Guest rooms have a private bathroom.

A continental breakfast is served at the property. At Luxe Safari lodge Verde you'll find a restaurant serving European cuisine. Gluten-free and Vegetarian options can also be requested.

The accommodation has a playground. You can play ping pong and darts at Luxe Safari lodge Verde, and the area is popular for cycling.

Braga is 25 miles from the resort, while Guimarães is 18 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Francisco Sá Carneiro Airport, 40 miles from the property.
