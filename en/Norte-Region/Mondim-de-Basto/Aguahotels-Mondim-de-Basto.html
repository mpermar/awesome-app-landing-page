Description

The state-of-the-art spa of Água Hotels Mondim de Basto features professional physiotherapists and masseurs. Next to a Turkish bath, hot tub and indoor pool, the hotel’s spa also features a fitness room.

Regional products and traditional recipes are served at Mondim de Basto’s restaurant. Guests can also taste several local wines from its stone-carved cellar.

The guest rooms of Mondim de Basto Água Hotels are simply yet uniquely decorated, and panoramic views can be enjoyed from the extra-large windows and furnished balconies. The rooms include flat-screen TVs with satellite channels and free internet.

Various adventure sports, including paragliding and mountain biking, can be found nearby the hotel. Hiking in Alvão Natural Park is also possible.
