Description

Guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a coffee machine. Each room comes with air conditioning, and selected rooms are equipped with a terrace. All rooms include a desk.

A continental breakfast is served daily at the property. At Hotel Torre de Gomariz Wine & Spa you'll find a restaurant serving Portuguese cuisine. A vegetarian option can also be requested.

At the accommodation guests are welcome to use a sauna and a Turkish bath. Guests at Hotel Torre de Gomariz Wine & Spa will be able to enjoy activities in and around Cervães, like cycling.

Staff speak English, Spanish and Portuguese at the 24-hour front desk.

Porto is 31 miles from the hotel, while Braga is 6 miles away. The nearest airport is Francisco Sá Carneiro Airport, 25 miles from the property.
