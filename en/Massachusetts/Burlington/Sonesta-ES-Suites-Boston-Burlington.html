Description

Sonesta ES Suites Boston Burlington features rooms with a sofa bed, free Wi-Fi, and cable TV. They are furnishing with light colors and carpeted floors. Each suite has a private bathroom.

Guests can visit the on-site fitness center and outdoor pool. Basketball courts and laundry facilities are also provided. Newspapers are available at the 24-hour front desk.

Downtown Boston is 17 miles from the Boston Burlington Sonesta ES Suites. Downtown Cambridge is 10 miles away.
