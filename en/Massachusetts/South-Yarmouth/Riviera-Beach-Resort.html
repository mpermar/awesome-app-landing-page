Description

While staying at the Riviera Beach Resort, guests can enjoy lounging by the spacious outdoor or indoor swimming pools or relaxing on the beach with free-use beach chair and sun umbrella. The resort also offers on-site dining for breakfast and lunch as well as an on-site lounge.

The Cape Cod Riviera Beach Resort is also close to the Blue Rock Golf Course. Guests can also enjoy all the amenities and activities at the resort's nearby sister property, the Red Jacket.
