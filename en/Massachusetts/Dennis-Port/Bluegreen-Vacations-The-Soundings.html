Description

Rooms at The Soundings Seaside Resort are furnished with work desks. They have kitchenettes with microwaves and refrigerators. All Seaside Resort rooms have balconies, many of them with views of the ocean. Cable TV and free Wi-Fi are also available in each room.

Soundings Seaside Resort has an indoor and an outdoor pool, as well as a hot tub and sauna.

Soundings is 30 miles from Cape Cod National Seashore. Sea Street Beach and Glendon Road Beach, considered to be Cape Cod’s best spots for swimming and windsurfing, are less than 5 minutes away. Many golf courses, such as Bayberry Hill Golf Course, are within 5 miles of the resort.
