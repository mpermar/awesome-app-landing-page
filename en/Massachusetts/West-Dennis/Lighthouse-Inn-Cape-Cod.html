Description

Select rooms at Lighthouse Inn Cape Cod offer ocean views or a wood burning fireplace. All rooms include a private bathroom and air conditioning.

The Waterfront at Lighthouse Inn Restaurant serves breakfast daily. The Pool Snack Bar serves a light fare along with frozen drinks and ice cream.

Guests can swim in the seasonal outdoor heated pool or play a game of tennis on the tennis court. Miniature golf, volleyball, and a game room are also available.
