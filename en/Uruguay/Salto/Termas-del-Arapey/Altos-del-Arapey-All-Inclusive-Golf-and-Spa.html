Description

Tempting guests with an outdoor pool with a swim-up bar, an indoor pool and an 18-hole golf course in front of the hotel, Altos del Arapey All Inclusive Golf & Spa is located in Termas del Arapey.

All beverages are included here. Salto bus station is found 90 km away. The rooms are modern, spacious and comfortable, with floor-to-ceiling balcony windows.

Each provides you with a private bathroom with hairdryer and bathrobe, flat-screen cable TV, a minibar, heating and air conditioning.

There is also a safety deposit box. There is a total of 8 thermal baths, 6 outdoor and 2 indoor.

Guests can unwind in the Wii games room and make use of the 3 large conference rooms.

At Altos del Arapey All Inclusive, Golf & Spa you can relax in the spa and keep in shape at the fitness centre.

The onsite restaurant serves international cuisine. Children can have fun in the play room and at the kids' club.

Guests at Altos del Arapey All Inclusive, Golf & Spa are situated 100 km from Nueva Hespérides International Airport.
