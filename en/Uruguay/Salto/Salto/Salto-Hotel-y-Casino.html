Description

Featuring a pool, a fitness centre, a casino and a restaurant, Salto Hotel y Casino offers rooms with free Wi-Fi and plasma TVs in Salto.

Free parking is provided. Artigas Main Square is 15 metres away. Decorated in soft hues, rooms at Salto Hotel are fitted with heating and air conditioning.

All of them have private bathrooms, and some afford park views. Breakfast is served daily.

International and regional dishes can be enjoyed at La Botica Restaurant. There is also a bar serving snacks and drinks.

Guests can try their luck in the casino or relax by the pool. They can also get fit in the gym or visit San Juan Bautista Cathedral, which is located only 10 metres away.
