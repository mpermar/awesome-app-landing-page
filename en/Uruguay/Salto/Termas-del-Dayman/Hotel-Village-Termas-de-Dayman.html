Description

Hotel Village Termas de Dayman offers accommodations with cable TVs and free WiFi access.

The property is 150 metres from Acuamanía Aquatic Park, 250 metres from the thermal swimming pools and 8 km from Salto.

Rooms at Village Hotel feature a contemporary design with tiled floors. They are equipped with a heating system and a ceiling fan.

A continental buffet breakfast is included. The accommodation offers a barbecue facility.

Private parking is available. The hotel is located 100 metres from the bus station.
