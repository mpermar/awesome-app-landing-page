Description

Boasting a spa and 2 swimming pools, Punta del Este Resort & Spa offers elegant accommodation just 400 metres from the beach in La Barra area in Punta del Este.

Guests can enjoy a tennis court and beach bar services in the summer. WiFi access is free.

The property is a 10-minute drive from La Peninsula. Punta del Este Resort & Spa boasts luxurious interiors with white leather chairs and cream-coloured carpeted floors.

Air-conditioned rooms have flat-screen TVs, minibars and white marbled bathrooms with bathrobes.

Some of them include baths. A full buffet breakfast with croissants and fresh juices is served daily.

The bar features full-length windows. Guests can enjoy Mediterranean cuisine or light lunches at the 2 on-site restaurants.

There is 24-hour room service. Welcome drinks are offered. Laguna del Sauce Airport is 33 km from Punta del Este Resort & Spa.
