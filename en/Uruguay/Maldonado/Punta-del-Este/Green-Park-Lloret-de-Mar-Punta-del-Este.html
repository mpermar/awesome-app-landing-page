Description

Located 200 metres from Solanas beach, Green Park - Lloret de Mar Punta del Este offers self-catering accommodation with free WiFi in Punta del Este.

A restaurant, bar and fitness centre are featured on site. The hotel also offers a private beach area, a tennis court, a seasonal indoor pool and a hot tub.

Horse back riding and hiking excursions can be arranged. Providing a tranquil environment, the apartments in Green Park - Lloret de Mar Punta del Este feature kitchenettes and air conditioning.

Cleaning service is provided daily. Guests can relax at the on-site sauna and Turkish bath or request sun chairs and umbrellas to use at the beach.

24-hour security services are provided. The swimming pool, fitness, games room and playground, restaurant and sport facilities will remain closed during May and June.

Green Park - Lloret de Mar Punta del Este features a mini on-site market and a beauty shop.

Car and bicycle rentals can be arranged at the 24-hour front desk and tourism information is offered to explore the area.

The property is 2 km from Laguna del Sauce international airport and 5 km from Punta del Este’s commercial area and bus station.
