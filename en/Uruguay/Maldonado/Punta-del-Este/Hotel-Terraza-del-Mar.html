Description

Featuring a big garden with a swimming pool overlooking Punta Ballena, Hotel Terraza del Mar offers stylish rooms with free Wi-Fi and cable TV.

Breakfast is provided and there is a restaurant. Portezuelo Beach is 70 metres away.

With stylish decor, rooms at Terraza del Mar have large windows overlooking the garden.

All of them have air conditioning and ceiling fans. Some of them have flat-screen TVs.

A buffet breakfast is served daily. Las Vieiras Restaurant offers a wide variety of international dishes.

Guests can enjoy beach services, including towels, parasols and deck chairs for an additional cost.

They can also relax in the garden or on the sun loungers by the pool. Table games are also available.

Free parking is provided, and airport shuttles can be arranged. Hotel Terraza del Mar is 9 km from Punta del Este centre, 4 km from Laguna del Sauce Airport and 500 metres from the bus stop to Montevideo.
