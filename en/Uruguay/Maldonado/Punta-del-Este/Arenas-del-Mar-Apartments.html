Description

Featuring a swimming pool and a sauna, Arenas del Mar Apartments offers self-catering apartments in Punta del Este.

The Brava Beach is 100 metres away. Free parking and WiFi are provided, as well as beach service.

Apartments at Arenas del Mar are very bright. They are fitted with air conditioning, heating and cable TV.

Self-catering facilities include fully-equipped kitchens and dining areas. There is free WIFI access Guests can relax by the pool or enjoy themselves in the games room.

there is a 24 hour reception. There is a launderette with tokens for an extra fee, a game room, from December to March there is open air heated pool during high season, the property is fitted with barbecue facilities which can be used for an extra fee.

Guests can also energize for the day in the gym or sauna. It is wheelchair-accessible Arenas del Mar Apartments is 600 metres from Gorlero Avenue and from the Bus Terminal.
