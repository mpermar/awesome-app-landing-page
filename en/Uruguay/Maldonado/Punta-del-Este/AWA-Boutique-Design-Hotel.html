Description

AWA Boutique Hotel is situated 900 metres from Playa Brava and from Playa Mansa. It features an outdoor pool, a spa and wellness centre and free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel.

All the guest rooms are equipped with design furniture and provide views from the large, double-glazed windows.

Each room at AWA Hotel features individual hot and cold air conditioning, a minibar and a telephone.

The AWA restaurant, Life Bistro is located beside the pool, and serves a wide range of cuisines from around the world.

There is also a pool-side bar and terrace where guests can relax with a drink. The on-site spa offers a full range of health and wellness treatments.

There is also a well-stocked library, a state-of-the-art cinema room and a business center.

The AWA design hotel is just 3 minutes' walk away from Punta Shopping and 1 km from Cantegril Golf Club.
