Description

Right by the white sands of Punta del Este´s Brava Beach, Il Belvedere offers 2 exterior pools, restaurant and gym.

Its self-catering bungalows provide free Wi-Fi and garden or sea views. Bungalows display fine wooden and brick finishing, combining rustic and modern styles.

They feature a full kitchenette, seating area and balcony. Also included are cable TV and DVD player, air conditioning and heating.

Daily, guests enjoy a varied breakfast with fresh fruits, breads and eggs. The restaurant and the bar both serve Uruguayan specialities and typical drinks.

The 5-star hotel features a playground and a children´s club with entertainment staff, as well as direct access to the beach and bicycles.

Indoors, guests can watch DVDs in the lounge or explore reading options in the library.

Il Belvedere is located a 5-minute drive from La Barra Beach and 15 minutes by car from downtown Punta del Este.
