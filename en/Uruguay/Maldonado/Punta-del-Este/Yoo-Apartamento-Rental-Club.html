Description

Featuring a garden with a swimming pool, a spa, and a fitness centre, Yoo Apartamento - Rental Club offers self-catering rooms with free Wi-Fi and plasma TVs in Punta del Este.

Free parking is provided. Both Mansa and Brava Beaches are 1 km away. With stylish decor, rooms at Yoo Apartamento - Rental Club are styled with tiled floors covered with fine rugs.

All of them feature spacious seating area with comfortable sofas, and private balconies.

Self-catering facilities include microwaves and refrigerators. Guests can relax on the deck by the pool, or get fit in the property’s gym.

Spa facilities include a sauna and a massage room. A games room is also featured.

The property's onsite restaurant, Margaret, is open until midnight. Yoo is 150 metres from Punta del Este Shopping Mall and a 25-minute drive from Laguna del Sauce Airport.
