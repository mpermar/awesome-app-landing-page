Description

This air-conditioned apartment features 2 bedrooms, a cable flat-screen TV, a dining area, and a kitchen with an oven and a microwave.

A à la carte breakfast is served at the property.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can help guests with any questions that they might have.

At the apartment guests are welcome to use a sauna. An outdoor swimming pool and a playground are offered at the property.

Shopping Mall is 1.8 miles from Arleo's House, while Punta del Este Harbour is 2.6 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Capitán de Corbeta Carlos A. Curbelo International Airport, 9 miles from the property.
