Description

Featuring 2 swimming pools with hot tubs, this beach front hotel offers splendid panoramic sea views.

The property is equipped with 2 professional tennis courts and a children's playground.

It is 100 metres from the beach. The rooms in Hotel Las Olas Resort feature private bathrooms and balconies, some of them offer sea views.

Room service is provided, and some units include free WiFi access. Hotel Las Olas Resort offers a cable TV in common areas as well as 24-hour front desk assistance.

The tour desk can arrange car and bicycle rentals. The tennis courts can be booked for an additional cost.

Guests can also request a daily breakfast for an extra fee. The beach is only 100 metres from the hotel.

The commercial area of La Barra is 2 km away, Leonel Viera Bridge is a 5-minute drive away, and Aeropuerto Laguna del Sauce Punta del Este Airport is 32 km away.
