Description

Located on a lake side, Hotel del Lago Golf & Art Resort offers free golfing and an on site restaurant.

Guests staying here can enjoy a spa centre and and 10 floodlit tennis courts. This eco-friendly property is 2 km from Laguna del Sauce Airport, 3.

3 km from Beach Club Mansa and 3. 5 km from Solanas Beach. Fully carpeted, rooms at Hotel del Lago Golf and Art Resort feature contemporary art paintings by local artists.

All of them have private balconies overlooking the garden and the lake, and plasma TVs.

A breakfast is served daily. The restaurant offers a wide variety of dishes and scenic lake views.

Drinks from the bar can be enjoyed in the garden. The property offers car hire services at preferential rates.

Different beauty treatments and massages are available at the spa. Guest can make use of the fitness centre, relax on the sun loungers by the pool or rent bikes to explore the area.

Horse riding activities are available. Regular shuttles to and from the beach are also provided.

This property is 5. 8 km from Punta del Este Airport, 8. 8 km from Casa Pueblo and 20 km from Gorlero Avenue.

Punta del Este harbor is 21. 5 km away, and "La Barra" is 29. 5 km from the property.
