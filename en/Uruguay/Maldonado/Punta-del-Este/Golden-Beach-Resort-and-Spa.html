Description

Featuring 2 swimming pools, a fitness centre and spa facilities, Golden Beach Resort & Spa offers air conditioned rooms.

Only 50 metres from El Emir beach. The yacht harbour is 700 metres away. Rooms at Resort & Spa Golden Beach have tiled floors and large wooden wardrobes.

They feature cable TV and air conditioning. Parking is available for a supplement.

A buffet breakfast is served daily. Snacks and simple dishes can be ordered at the bar, which features a grill.

BBQ facilities are available. Spa facilities include a sauna, a hot tub, and a massage room.

In summer, guests can unwind on the sun lounger by the pool, and they can make use of the heated pool all year long.

Billiards and table tennis are available, and there is a children playground. The 24-hour front desk can secure shuttles to the airport, which is 20 km away.

Golden Beach Resort & Spa is 50 metres from Gorlero Avenue and 100 metres from the designer shops on Calle 20.
