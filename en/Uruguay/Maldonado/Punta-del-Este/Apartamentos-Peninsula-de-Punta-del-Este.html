Description

Offering a sauna and fitness center, Apartamentos Peninsula de Punta del Este is set in the Peninsula district in Punta del Este.

The Fingers beach is 300 metres away. Free WiFi is featured and free private parking is available on site.

The accommodation is air conditioned and has a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Some units feature a dining area and/or balcony. An oven, a microwave and toaster are also provided, as well as a coffee machine and a kettle.

Some units also have a kitchen, equipped with a fridge. There is a private bathroom with a hairdryer in each unit.

Bed linen is available. Apartamentos Peninsula de Punta del Este also includes an outdoor pool.

Artisans Craft Fair is 100 meters from Apartamentos Peninsula in Punta del Este, while Punta del Este Harbor is 800 meters from the property.

The nearest airport is Capitn Carlos A. Curbelo International Airport, 18 km from the property.
