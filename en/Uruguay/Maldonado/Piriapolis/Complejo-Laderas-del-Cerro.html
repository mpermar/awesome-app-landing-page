Description

Featuring an indoor and an outdoor pool, a football pitch, Complejo Laderas del Cerro offers cottages only 800 metres from Piriapolis Beach.

A bar and BBQ facilities are featured. Decorated with gabled roof and exposed brick walls, the accommodation at Complejo Laderas del Cerro features a fan, heating, and private bathrooms.

A continental breakfast is served daily. Snacks and drinks can be ordered at the bar, and BBQ facilities are featured in the garden.

Guests can sunbathe on the sun loungers by the pool. They can also engage in football matches.

Free parking is provided. Complejo Laderas del Cerro is 800 metres from the city centre and 400 metres from the bus station.
