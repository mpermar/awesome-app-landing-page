Description

Featuring an outdoor pool, Marinas del Riachuelo offers rooms with free Wi-Fi and air conditioning in Colonia del Sacramento.

Free parking is provided. The historic centre is 10 km away. Decorated with tiled floors and contemporary painting, rooms at Marinas del Riachuelo are fitted with satellite and minibars.

All of them have private bathrooms. Marinas del Riachuelo is 5 km from El Calabres Beach and 10 km from the bus terminal.
