Description

Boasting a large parkland and an outdoor swimming pool, Mon Petit Hotel is located in Rosario.

Free WiFi access is available in this hotel. Guests can enjoy the property's spa facilities.

With a classic décor and comfortable beds, each room at Mon Petit Hotel is equipped with a satellite TV and a private bathroom.

Some rooms include air conditioning, and all of them afford lovely garden views. Other services available at Mon Petit Hotel include 24-hour front desk assistance, barbecue facilities, meeting facilities and a tour desk.

Massage treatments can be requested for a surcharge. A full breakfast is included and provided daily in the hotel's dining area.

The property also offers free parking on site. Mon Petit Hotel is 50 km from Colonia del Sacramento.
