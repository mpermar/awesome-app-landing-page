Description

Boasting an outdoor swimming pool, 2 indoor heated swimming pools, a fitness center and and spa facilities, Colonia West All Inclusive Hotel offers rooms with all inclusive or standard rates, only 7 km away from Conchillas.

Free WiFi access and free parking are possible at the property. Colonia West Hotel offers rooms and suites with chic décor and fitted with central VRV air-conditioning, a 32" LED TV, a minibar and a private bathroom.

Some rooms also include a seating area and a private patio. Guests can enjoy an American buffet breakfast daily, including fruits, cheese and jams, among other, and savour a varied selection of dishes at Arroyo Restaurant.

They can also relax with an after-dinner drink at Evans Pub, which serves a unique selection of drinks and wines.

The tour desk can offer tips for getting around the area and Colonia del Sacramento Harbour is 55 km away.
