Description

With an outdoor swimming pool surrounded by gardens, the inn features chic design with a fireplace and free Wi-Fi.

Rooms have iPod docking stations. Massage sessions can be booked. Posada El Capullo, features an integrated computer area with flat-screen monitors and bar chairs in the living room, decorated with moccha hues, white furnitures and details of contemporary art.

Guests can rent a bicycle to visit Colonia’s historic district. The tour desk can provide tips.

A buffet breakfast with juices and regional jams is served daily. Guests have access to BBQ facilities.

The rooms are styled in off-white fabrics and earth tones, and feature air conditioning and flat-screen cable TV.

Some rooms include kitchenettes and feature views of the patio or garden. There is a currency exchange service.
