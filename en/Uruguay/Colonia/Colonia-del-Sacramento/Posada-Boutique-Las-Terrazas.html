Description

An outdoor swimming pool and rooms with iPod docking stations can be enjoyed just 100 metres from Colonias historical circuit.

Posada Boutique Las Terrazas offers free Wi-Fi and netbooks for guest use in a solar-powered environment.

Posada Las Terrazas has air-conditioned rooms with flat-screen cable TV and private bathrooms with showers.

Some rooms feature private balconies with river views. Posada Boutique Las Terrazas has a cosy living room area styled with rustic exposed stones and a fireplace.

A full buffet breakfast with juices and pastries is served daily. Colonias harbour and bus terminal are 400 metres from the hotel.
