Description

Located in front of the beach in La Paloma, this hotel features an indoor pool, a garden, and rooms with either pool or sea views.

Some rooms offer direct access to the beach. Free Wi-Fi is available. The rooms in Hotel Palma de Mallorca feature private bathrooms.

Guests at Hotel Palma de Mallorca are provided parasols for the beach. The hotel has a game room with a table tennis set.

Continental breakfast is served daily and public parking is possible on site for an extra fee.

Hotel Palma de Mallorca is 3 km from the bus terminal and 114 km from El Jagüel Airport, in Maldonado city centre.
