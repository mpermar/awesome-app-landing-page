Description

Featuring a large heated swimming pool, a hydromassage tub and spa services. Paradise Complejo Turístico is located in front of Mansa Beach and offers spectacular views of the sunset.

Free WiFi access is available in the entire property. Reiki massages and beaty treatments are available for an extra fee.

At Paradise Complejo Turístico guests can work out at the gym with views of the pool or enjoy a beach day with the hotel’s complimentary beach umbrellas and chairs.

Paradise offers fully equipped apartments and bungalows with air conditioning and cable TVs.

Apartments have private balconies with sea views and bungalows feature outdoor seating areas with lovely views as well.

All of them have modern kitchenettes with microwaves and refrigerators, and bungalows also include private BBQ facilities.

Guests can also enjoy a delicious complimentary breakfast with regional jams in the privacy of their rooms.

This property is only 4 blocks from Atlantida City’s main entertainment venues, shops, restaurants and pubs.

The tour desk can provide complimentary bikes and arrange airport shuttles. Private parking is free.
