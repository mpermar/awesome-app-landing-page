Description

The units at Chuiba Bay lodge feature a spacious en suite bathroom, al-fresco patios, air conditioning, ceiling fans, satellite TV, a safe, personal bar, linen, bathrooms and guest amenities.

Breakfast is served in the morning and the restaurant is also open for lunch and dinner. There is inside and outside sitting, a large deck veranda with day bed and pool double loungers.

Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around the area, such as snorkeling, canoeing or scuba diving. Guests can also experience the culture on a visit the local village.

Airport transfers to Pemba Airport, 4.7 miles away, can be arranged for a surcharge.
