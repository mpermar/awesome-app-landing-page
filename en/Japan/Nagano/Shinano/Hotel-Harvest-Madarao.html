Description

All rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, fridge, a electric tea pot, a bath, free toiletries and a closet. Featuring a private bathroom with a bidet and a hair dryer, rooms at the resort also feature a garden view. At Hotel Harvest Madarao all rooms have a seating area.

A buffet breakfast is available daily at the property.

A variety of popular activities are available in the area around the property, including skiing and cycling.

Staff at reception can help around the clock with information on the area.

The nearest airport is Matsumoto Airport, 50 miles from the property.
