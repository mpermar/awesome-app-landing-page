Description

An official hotel of Tokyo Disney Resort, Sheraton Tokyo Bay offers indoor and outdoor pools, specialty Disney shops, a kids’ play area and 4 dining options.

A 2-minute walk from Bayside Station on the Disney Resort Line, it offers spacious rooms featuring a Sheraton Sweet Sleeper bed with satin sheets.

Each room at Sheraton Grande Tokyo Bay Hotel has a seating area and an LCD TV with satellite and cable channels.

From the balcony, most rooms have views of the Disney Resort or Tokyo Bay. Slippers and bathrobes are provided, and room service is on call 24/7.

A coffee machine is provided. A fitness room and a public bath with sauna are on site, and massages can be requested.

The lobby has free-use internet PCs with a printer as well as free W-Fi. On-site luggage storage is available on the day of arrival prior to check-in.

Designed like a luxury cruise liner, Café Toastina serves aromatic coffee and pastries.

The hotel also offers Japanese cuisine at Asuka, grilled teppanyaki specialities at Maihama and an international menu at Grand Cafe.

A free shuttle runs to nearby Bayside Monorail Station, from which JR Maihama Train Station can be reached via a 10-minute monorail ride and walk.

From Maihama Station, Tokyo Station is a 20-minute train ride away. The hotel is directly accessible from Narita and Haneda Airports via the Airport Limousine bus.
