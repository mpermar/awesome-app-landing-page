Description

Situated at Kawaguchi-ko Lake, Weekend Shuffle treats its guests to a terrace, a spacious lounge and complimentary tea, while free WiFi is provided in all areas.

A Mount Fuji view can be enjoyed from some rooms, and every room comes with a private bathroom.

The Weekend Shuffle is centrally located to reach the areas attractions, like Kawaguchiko Music Forest Museum, Kawaguchiko Art Museum and the Kawaguchiko Sarumawashi Theatre, all within a 5-minute walk.

Kawaguchiko Train Station is a 10-minute bus ride, and the popular amusement park Fuji-Q Highland is just a 20-minute drive.

Guests at Shuffle Weekend can relax in a hot public bath, or enjoy some quiet time with a book and Mount Fuji views in the cosy lounge.

Free parking is available. Rooms are air conditioned and feature a wooden floor. They come with a TV, while toiletries, slippers and a hairdryer are provided.

A simple breakfast with bread, cereal, fruits, yogurt and beverages can be arranged at an additional charge.
