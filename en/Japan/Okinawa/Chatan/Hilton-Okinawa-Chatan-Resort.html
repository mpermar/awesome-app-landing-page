Description

Centrally located in the heart of Chatan, just a 5-minute walk away from Mihama American Village, Hilton Okinawa Chatan Resort offers a spa, a 24-hour front desk, and indoor and outdoor pools with a waterslide for children.

Guest rooms feature a panoramic view of the ocean. WiFi is available in all rooms.

Air-conditioned rooms are fitted with an electric kettle, refrigerator and flat-screen TV with satellite and cable channels.

The en suite bathroom comes with free toiletries, a bath and a hairdryer. At Chatan Resort Hilton Okinawa, guests can order room service, use the currency exchange service or work out at the 24-hour fitness centre.

A concierge service is available for guests who wish to explore the area. Other facilities offered include a luggage storage service and safety deposit boxes.

Guests can enjoy activities including diving, fishing and snorkelling at an additional cost.

Guests have several dining options on site, including Italian and Japanese restaurants and a bar.

Sunset Beach is a 7-minute walk away, while the Mihama 7Plex movie theatre is a 6-minute walk away.
