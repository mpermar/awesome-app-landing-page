Description

Boasting private beaches and golf courses, Kanucha Bay Hotel & Villas provides luxurious resort accommodation surrounded by untouched beauty of Okinawa’s nature.

Beauty massage spas and outdoor pools are also on site. Free WiFi and free Parking is available.

Featuring wooden flooring and a large balcony, spacious rooms are filled with bright sunshine and sweet tropical fragrance.

The rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV and comfortable sofas. A minibar and an electric kettle with tea bags are also offered.

Some rooms come with a spa bath on the balcony, looking out to the ocean. While staying at Hotel & Villas Kanucha Bay, guests can enjoy a range of activities like diving, fishing and golfing.

After a day of activities, they can relax at one of the 4 massage salons. Shops for diving and golfing gear, as well as gift shops, are available.

The property is an 80-minute drive from Naha Airport. Popular Churaumi Aquarium is a 50-minute drive away.

There are 8 restaurants at the hotel, offering a variety of meals like Japanese, Okinawan and barbecue grills.

Paradis provides fresh Italian dishes, while Chinese delicacies are served at Ryukyu restaurant.
