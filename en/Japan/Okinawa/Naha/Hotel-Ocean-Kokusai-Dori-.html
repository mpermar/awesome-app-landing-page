Description

Located right on Kokusai Street within a 2-minute walk from Makishi Monorail Station, Hotel Ocean offers both Western style rooms with beds and Japanese style tatami (woven-straw flooring) rooms.

Naha International airport is only a 12-minute ride away from Makishi Monorail Station.

For travellers who are interested in shopping and experiencing local culture, the Makishi Public Market is only a 7-minute walk away, and Shuri Castle is a 20-minute drive away.

Free WiFi is available throughout the entire property. Tea and coffee is also freely available for guests at the hotel lobby 24 hours a day.

There is a childrenâ€™s play space on the 1st floor of the property and up to 5 baby diapers are provided for free each stay.

All rooms are air-conditioned and feature a flat-screen TV and a desk. The en suite bathrooms come with a private shower and bath.

Free toiletries are provided, and guests can chose a pillow from a selection of 5 pillows.

Hotel Ocean offers a rich Okinawan style breakfast buffet of over 100 different dishes at their Sky Lounge Restaurant.
