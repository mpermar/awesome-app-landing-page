Description

At the resort, every room is equipped with a desk. Each room includes a flat-screen TV, and certain rooms include a sea view. All guest rooms have a closet.

The daily breakfast offers American and Asian options.

Villabu-resort has an outdoor pool. A range of activities are offered in the area, such as snorkeling, cycling and fishing.

Kurima Bridge is 7 miles from the accommodation, while Cape Nishi Hennazaki is 8 miles away.
