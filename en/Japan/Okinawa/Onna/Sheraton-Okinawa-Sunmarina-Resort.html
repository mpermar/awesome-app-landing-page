Description

Located right on the beach, Sheraton Okinawa Sunmarina Resort features snorkelling, a restaurant and lounge.

All stylish rooms include a satellite flat-screen TV and balcony. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

All rooms at Sheraton Sunmarina Resort are decorated in lively colours and modern wood furnishings.

Each room features air conditioning, a balcony, flat-screen TV, sofa, refrigerator and tea and coffee maker.

The private bathroom has a bathtub, shower and free toiletries including toothbrushes, shampoo and conditioner are provided.

Windsurfing, canoeing and other water activities are available by the beach. Massage therapies and beauty treatments are featured at Churaku Spa.

The hotel has a fitness centre, tennis courts and a golf course is just 1 km away.

Billiards and table tennis facilities are also available. Modern Japanese/Okinawa dishes can be enjoyed at Dining Room Senses, which has an open kitchen where chefs prepare omelettes, grilled fish and sushi.

Barbecue meals and sunset views can be enjoyed at The Grill. Sheraton Okinawa Resort is an hour drive from Naha Airport, and direct airport limousine buses are available to/from the hotel at an extra fee.
