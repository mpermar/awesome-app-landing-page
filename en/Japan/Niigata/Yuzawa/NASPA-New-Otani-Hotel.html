Description

Right next to NASPA Ski Garden, New Otani Hotel boasts hot-spring baths, an indoor pool and 7 food and beverage options.

Spacious Japanese and Western rooms are offered, all with a private bathroom. Guests can enjoy skiing during the winter season on site.

The area is popular for skiing and cycling. Guests at NASPA New Otani Hotel stay in air-conditioned rooms with a tea maker and fridge.

The en suite bathroom includes a bathtub, slippers and amenities. The hotel is 2 km from JR Echigo Yuzawa Train Station.

Parking is free and bicycles can be rented. Guests can relax at the sauna or hot tub, or unwind with a massage.

Both tennis courts and table tennis are on offer, as is a fitness room. Ski equipment rental and ski storage are available.

There is a shop at the property. Fontana serves Continental cuisine, and Japanese dishes are on offer at Senbazuru.

Bar Festa offers a lively time with drinks and karaoke, while Bar Capri provides calm sophistication.
