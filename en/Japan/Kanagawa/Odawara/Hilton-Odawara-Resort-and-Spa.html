Description

The Hilton Odawara boasts outdoor and indoor swimming pools, 6 tennis courts and many spa and recreation facilities.

Rooms feature panoramic ocean views while karaoke rooms and a children’s playground are on-site.

Free shuttles run to/from Nebukawa and Odawara Station. Guests at Odawara Hilton can take an aerobics class at the fitness centre, play a game of golf at the 18-hole course, or relax with a massage treatment.

The hotel features 10 hot spring baths, a bowling centre and a jogging track in lush nature.

Concierge services are available and free WiFi is available at the lobby. The lobby lounge serves beverages and light meals daily, and a dessert buffet on the weekends, while Brasserie Flora serves breakfast and a themed lunch buffet, as well as a kaiseki (traditional multi-course) and Western course dinners and a wine buffet.

Modern and cosy rooms come with sea views through floor-to-ceiling windows, comfortable beds and a seating area.

Some have a balcony, and all rooms are fitted with an LCD TV, a fridge and a safe.

Bathrobes and toiletries are provided. Resort & Spa Hilton Odawara offers a complimentary shuttle to/from Nebukawa Station in 5 minutes, and on weekdays only to/from Odawara Station in 20 minutes.

Odawara Station offers a 20-minute train to Hakone and a 35-minute shinkansen bullet train to Tokyo Station.

This hotel has been awarded the 2015 TripAdvisor Certificate of Excellence Award.
