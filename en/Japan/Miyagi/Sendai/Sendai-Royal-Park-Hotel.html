Description

Surrounded by lush gardens, Sendai Royal Park Hotel is a resort hotel that offers elegant accommodation with European dcor and free WiFi.

Guests can enjoy rejuvenating beauty treatments at the hotels beauty salon. Free pick-up shuttles are available from Sendai and Izumi-Chuo stations.

The hotel is located a 15-minute drive from Izumi Chuo Station, and a 30-minute drive from JR Sendai Station.

The Sendai Izumi Premium Outlet shopping mall is just a 1-minute walk, and Izumi Park Town Golf Club can be reached within a 5-minute drive.

Sendai Airport is a 40-minute drive. Guests can enjoy shopping at Izumi Park Town Tapio, located a 3-minute walk away.

Each room at Sendai Royal Park Hotel features warm colours and large windows, and comes with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fridge and a minibar.

An electric kettle and tea bags are also provided. Some rooms feature a balcony and views of the garden.

Activities at Sendai Royal Park Hotel include yoga in the garden and cycling. Guests can borrow bicycles to explore the local area, or relax with massages in the guest room.

The 24-hour front desk can handle luggage storage and currency exchange. Guests can have breakfast on the garden terrace or enjoy a picnic breakfast in the garden.

Teppanyaki restaurant Nanatsumori offers Sendai wagyu steak, while Chinese restaurant Keikaen offers Cantonese dishes with fresh ingredients.
