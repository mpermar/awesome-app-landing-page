Description

Located in the famous Shirahama hot-spring area, Shirahama Key Terrace Hotel Seamore features comfortable accommodations with an ocean view.

Guests can relax in the sauna or hot-spring baths, and also request for massage services.

Free WiFi is available in the lobby areas, and the Nanki Shirahama Airport is a 10-minute drive away.

The air-conditioned rooms feature a desk, a flat-screen TV, and a refrigerator. Japanese-style rooms have tatami (woven-straw) flooring, while Western-style rooms feature a seating area with a sofa.

The en suite bathroom comes with slippers and a hairdryer. Guests can enjoy playing at the game room or purchasing local products at the gift shop.

An outdoor swimming pool is open during the summer season, and a lounge with karaoke is available as well.

The 24-hour front desk provides photocopying and currency exchange services. Refreshments are available at the Ocean View Cafe Lounge, and Japanese dishes are served at an on-site restaurant.

The Summit has beautiful views of the beach. Breakfast is served at one of the many restaurants.

Shirahama Key Terrace Hotel Seamore is a 15-minute walk from the Shirahama Beach, and a 10-minute walk from the Senjojiki Seashore.
