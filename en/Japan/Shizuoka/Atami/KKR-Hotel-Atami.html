Description

All guest rooms in the resort are equipped with a electric tea pot. All rooms include air conditioning, a closet and a flat-screen TV, and some rooms at KKR Atami have a terrace. Guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

At KKR Hotel Atami guests are welcome to use a hot spring bath.

Izusan Shrine is an 18-minute walk from the resort. Tokyo International Airport is 50 miles away.
