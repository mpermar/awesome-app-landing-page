Description

The rooms are equipped with a microwave, fridge, a electric tea pot, a bidet, free toiletries and a desk. Every room comes with air conditioning, and some rooms at the resort have a terrace.

Guests at The SPA Resort Bettei Rakuyu can enjoy a continental or an American breakfast. Various dining options including European and French cuisine are available at the in-house restaurant.

Guests can use the sauna.

Shizuoka Airport is 46 miles away.
