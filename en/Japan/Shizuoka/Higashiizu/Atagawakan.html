Description

Boasting hot spring baths that overlook a beautiful beach, Atagawakan provides comfortable guest rooms fitted with tatami (woven-straw) flooring.

Carefully prepared meals with seasonal ingredients are served in the comfort of guests’ own room.

Free Wi-Fi is provided in the public area. The spacious rooms feature large windows offering plenty of sunlight and views of the ocean.

A TV, a telephone and an electric kettle are included. Guests sleep on traditional futon bedding on the tatami floor.

Atagawakan Ryokan offers both indoor and outdoor hot spring baths with splendid views.

One of 4 small baths can be booked for a private use at an extra charge. Guests can relax with a massage treatment.

The property is a 7-minute walk from Izu-Atagawa Train Station, and a free pick-up service is available if requested at time of booking.

Atagawa-Onsen Beach is right in front of the ryokan, and Atagawa Tropical Alligator Garden is an 8-minute walk away.

Japanese multi-course dinner is prepared using fresh seafood, and served in the guest room with a Nakai (personal attendant) service.
