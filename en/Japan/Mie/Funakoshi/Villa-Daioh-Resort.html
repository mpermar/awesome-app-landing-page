Description

All rooms in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Featuring a private bathroom with a bath and free toiletries, rooms at Villa Daioh Resort also provide guests with a sea view. Guest rooms will provide guests with a desk and a electric tea pot.

An Asian breakfast is available daily at the accommodation.

Villa Daioh Resort has a terrace. You can play ping pong at the resort.

Ise is 18 miles from Villa Daioh Resort, while Toba is 14 miles away. The nearest airport is Chubu Centrair International Airport, 40 miles from the property.
