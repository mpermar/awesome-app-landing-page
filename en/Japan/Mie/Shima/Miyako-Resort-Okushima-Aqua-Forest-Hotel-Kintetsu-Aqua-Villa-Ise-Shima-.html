Description

Miyako Resort Okushima Aqua Forest features a natural hot spring, indoor and outdoor pools.

Free WiFi, parking and return shuttle bus services to Kashikojima Subway Station are available.

Rooms at the Miyako Resort Okushima Aqua Forest are air conditioned and come with a private bathroom.

A TV, refrigerator and safety deposit box is included. Guests can enjoy Karaoke facilities and the services of a beauty salon.

The hotel has a souvenir shop. Meals and beverages are served at the hotel’s restaurant.

Miyako Resort Okushima Aqua Forest is a 25-minute drive from Kintetsu Kashikojima and is within walking distance to the beach.

Daiousaki Lighthouse is a 10-minute drive away. Please note there are no restaurants or supermarkets around the property.
