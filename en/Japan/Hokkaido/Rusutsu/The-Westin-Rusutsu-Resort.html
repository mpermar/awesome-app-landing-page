Description

At the hotel, the rooms come with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Rooms are complete with a shared bathroom equipped with a hot tub, while some units at The Westin Rusutsu Resort also offer a seating area. Guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

A buffet breakfast is served each morning at the property. There's an on-site restaurant, which specializes in international and Japanese cuisine.

The accommodation offers an indoor pool. The area is popular for skiing and cycling, in addition, ski equipment rental and bike rental are available at this hotel.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can give tips on the area.

Rusutsu Resort is 0.4 miles from The Westin Rusutsu Resort. New Chitose Airport is 40 miles away.
