Description

Shiretoko Daiichi Hotel features indoor and outdoor natural hot spring baths, a hot tub and 6 dining options.

It offers contemporary Japanese-style rooms, a free shuttle service and free Wi-Fi.

Rooms at the Hotel Daiichi Shiretoko have tatami (woven mat) flooring and either beds or traditional futons.

They come with an LCD TV, a fridge and a safe. Guests can relax in the sauna or unwind with a massage.

For entertainment, there is karaoke and a game hall. The hotel has coin-operated laundry facilities.

Buffet restaurant Marusukoi with open kitchen serves more than 80 different Japanese and Western dishes made with local ingredients.

The hotel also offers a noodle restaurant, a bar and a coffee shop. Shiretoko Daiichi Hotel offers a free shuttle to/from Utoro Onsen Bus Terminal.
