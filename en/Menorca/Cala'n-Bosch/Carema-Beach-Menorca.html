Description

This accommodation is 1 minute walk from the beach. Carema Beach Menorca is located on the beachfront in Cala'n Bosch and offers rooms and apartments with a shared outdoor pool and sun terrace.

The rooms feature air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and a seating area. The apartments have a kitchen with a hob, electric kettle, microwave, fridge and coffee maker. All accommodations have a bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer, as well as a balcony or terrace.

The property offers a 24-hour reception, nightlife activities and a restaurant serving breakfast and dinner buffets.

Ciutadella de Menorca is 16 km away and Menorca Airport is 50 minutes away by car.

Good location, friendly staff, disappointing facilities and room standards

"The positives: the location is very good in comparison to other local options as it opens onto one of the few sandy beach areas for sunbathing. There's also a supermarket right across the street for all your basic needs and it's very close to the harbour with nightlife, restaurants and a few arcades and childrens rides. The staff is also very friendly and nice. The hotel itself is well designed and the pool and bar area are nicely set up too. You could hang out here all day going back and forth to the beach and the neighboring bar/restaurant. There's also a good amount of nice sunlounge chairs around the pool and at the beach. The breakfast buffet is pretty good although we felt it was a bit overpriced for what you get. The price would be more reasonable if it were available as a breakfast "brunch" and the hours extended to include the early afternoon as well. The beds in the rooms were quite comfortable with lots of extra cosy pillows. The shower had lovely strong water pressure although the design is a bit flawed as it spills all over the bathroom floor."
