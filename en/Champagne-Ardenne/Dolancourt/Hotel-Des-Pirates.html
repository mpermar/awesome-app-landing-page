Description

The Hôtel des Pirates boasts a lounge bar and the Pirate’s Tavern serves a varied buffet.

The historic town of Troyes is 25 miles from the hotel and with its Museum of Modern Art and impressive cathedral, it is well worth a visit.

Free Wi-Fi internet access is available throughout the hotel.

Free public parking is available on site.
