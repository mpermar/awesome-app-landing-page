Description

Homewood Suites by Hilton at the Waterfront provides a free hot buffet breakfast every morning. Monday through Thursday, guests can also enjoy a free hot meal and beverages in the evening.

Guests at the Waterfront Homewood Suites can go for a swim in the indoor pool or take advantage of the fitness center. After playing a game of basketball on the hotel's sports court, guests can practice on the 5-hole putting green.
