Description

Noclegi u Banysia is located in a quiet and green area of Krajno Pierwsze, 1. 6 km from the Świętokrzyski National Park.

At a surcharge, guests can use a sauna and a salt cave. Free WiFi access and private parking are available.

The accommodation will provide you with a TV and soundproof doors. Featuring a bath or a shower, private bathrooms also come with towels.

You can enjoy a mountain view from all the rooms. There is a shared kitchen with a gas cooker, a microwave, a fridge, a high chair and kitchenware.

Noclegi u Banysia offers homemade, regional breakfast and features barbecue facilities and a terrace.

Other facilities offered at the property include a children's playground. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling, hiking and mini golf.
