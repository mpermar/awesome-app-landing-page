Description

Offering ski-to-door access and a ski pass sales point, Willa pod Czantorią is set in Ustroń, 200 metres from Czantoria Ski Centre.

The property features a seasonal heated swimming pool. A flat-screen TV with satellite channels and DVD player, as well as a CD player are featured.

Some units feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day. A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms.

Rooms have a private bathroom fitted with a shower. A TV with cable channels is offered.

A children's playground is available. Guests have free access to a games room with foosball, a cross trainer and a bike.

A playstation game console is offered at a surcharge. There is a common kitchen with a dining area, a designated bonfire area, as well as a gazebo with a barbecue.

Breakfast buffet is available for extra charge at restaurant Adriano Spa, 100 meters from the property.

A number of activities is offered in the area, such as skiing and cycling. Kolej linowa na Czantorię is 200 metres from Willa pod Czantorią, while Mała Palenica Ski Lift is 1.

1 km from the property. The nearest airport is John Paul II Airport, 81 km from Willa pod Czantorią.
