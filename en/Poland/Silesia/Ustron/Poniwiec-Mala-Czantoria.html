Description

Featuring free WiFi and a spa centre, Poniwiec Mała Czantoria offers accommodation in a peaceful area on the outskirts of Ustroń, in the foothills of Mala Czantoria.

Mała Czantoria Ski Lift is a short walk away. The resort has a hot tub and a ski school, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Rooms come with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Some feature views of the mountains or garden.

There is a 24-hour front desk and a terrace offering panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.

Guests can relax during a massage or in various beauty treatments for your face, body, hands and feet.

Daily breakfast includes freshly baked bread and fresh fruit juices. This resort has a discounted ski pass sales point and ski equipment hire is available nearby.

You can engage in various activities, such as skiing, cycling and hiking. Poniwiec Ski Lift is a short walk away from Poniwiec Mała Czantoria, while Czantoria Ski Centre is 1.

5 km away. The nearest airport is John Paul II Airport, 136 km from the property.
