Description

This modern, 3-star Hotel Diament Ustroń is picturesquely located in the heart of Ustroń and offers beautiful panorama views of Beskid Slaski mountains.

It features free WiFi. Each room comes with a desk and a TV with satellite channels.

A spacious bathroom comes with a shower, towels and free toiletries. Guests can access the wellness zone with a swimming pool with cascades and starry sky ceiling decor, underwater massage and counter-current.

There also is a small pool with warmer water, as well as a hot tub, sauna, air-conditioned fitness room.

There is a Spa with treatment rooms. Playgrounds, a skate park and climbing walls are available to guests for free.

At a surcharge, children can also enjoy their time at the kid's corner with professional staff, as well as a multimedia corner.

Bike, sledge and Nordic walking poles, as well as billiards tables are all available at an extra charge.

The restaurant at Hotel Diament Ustroń serves regional and international cuisine and offers a special menu for children.

Guests can enjoy a relaxing drink in a bar with a summer terrace offering beautiful views of the mountains.
