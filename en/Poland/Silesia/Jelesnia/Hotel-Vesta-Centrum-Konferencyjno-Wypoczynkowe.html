Description

Located in Jeleśnia in the Żywiec Beskids, Centrum Konferencyjno Wypoczynkowe Vesta offers rooms with a TV set and a free WiFi.

The property boasts a spa and wellness centre. The rooms of the Centrum Vesta are elegant and stylish, and each is done in pastel colours.

Each one has a private bathroom with a shower. While staying at the Vesta you may work out in the fitness centre or relax in the hot tub.

The hotel also boasts a sauna, tanning beds and a steam bath. The hotel has 2 restaurants.

One serves Italian cuisine, and the other, Złota Karczma, specialises in regional dishes.

Guests are offered a number of discounts for hotel restaurants, as well as massage parlour and beauty salon.

The Vesta is in a calm area. The nearest city is Żywiec, located 8 km away. The Polish- Slovakian border is in Korbielów, 11 km from the hotel.
