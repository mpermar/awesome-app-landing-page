Description

Rezydencja Luxury Hotel is a 4-star boutique hotel located a 15-minute drive from the centre of Katowice, in Piekary Śląskie.

It offers uniquely decorated interiors with free Wi-Fi and a minibar. Guests can relax in a 6-person hot tub and a sauna.

Each room at Rezydencja is air-conditioned, features a private modern bathroom with a glass shower cabin and a hairdryer.

Interiors are carefully designed, with heavy wooden furniture, elegant chandeliers and classic, patterned wallpaper.

The property features a restaurant serving Old-Polish and European cuisine. A breakfast buffet and a room service are also available.

Rezydencja Luxury Hotel offers free private parking and a 24-hour front desk service.

The city centre is 2 km away. The centre of Bytom is 2. 8 km away. Katowice-Pyrzowice Airport is within a 15-minute car ride.

A1 highway exit is only 500 metres away. Zabrze is a 15-minute drive away from Rezydencja Luxury Hotel, while Gliwice is a 25-minute drive away.
