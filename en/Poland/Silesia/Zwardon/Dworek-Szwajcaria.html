Description

At the bed and breakfast, the rooms come with a closet. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with free toiletries, while some accommodations at Dworek Szwajcaria also provide guests with a seating area. All guest rooms have a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

Dworek Szwajcaria has a playground. Skiing and cycling are among the activities that guests of the bed and breakfast can find nearby.

Wisła is 11 miles from Dworek Szwajcaria, while Szczyrk is 15 miles from the property. John Paul II International Kraków–Balice Airport is 54 miles away.
