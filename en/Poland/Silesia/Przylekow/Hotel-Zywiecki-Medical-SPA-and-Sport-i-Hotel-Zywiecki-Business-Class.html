Description

Located in Przyłęków, 11 km from the centre of Żywiec, Hotel Żywiecki Medical Spa & Sport features a modern spa centre and its own ski slope.

Guests have free access to a swimming pool with spring water and saunas. The hotel houses a restaurant.

Some rooms feature a seating area for your convenience. Every air-conditioned room comes with a private bathroom equipped with a shower.

For your comfort, you will find bath robes, free toiletries and a hair dryer. A TV with satellite channels is provided.

There is a 24-hour front desk and a hot tub, available at a surcharge. Quad and bicycle hire is provided.

The hotel offers physiotherapy and beauty treatments. Free private parking is provided.

This hotel has a ski school and a ski pass sales point and ski equipment hire is available.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including skiing and cycling.

Szczyrk is 19 km from Hotel Żywiecki Medical Spa & Sport, while John Paul II Airport is 96 km from the property.
