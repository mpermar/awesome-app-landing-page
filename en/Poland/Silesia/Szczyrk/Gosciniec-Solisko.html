Description

Gościniec Solisko is located in the Beskid Śląski Landscape Park, 5 km from the centre of Szczyrk.

It is next to the Solisko Ski Lift. Free Wi-Fi access is available. Rooms here will provide you with a seating area, as well as satellite and cable channels.

There is also a dining table. Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with towels.

You can enjoy a mountain view and a garden view from all the rooms. Extras include linen.

At Gościniec Solisko you will find barbecue facilities and a terrace. Other facilities offered include a shared lounge.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including skiing, cycling and hiking.

The property offers free parking. The property is 14 km from Wisła. The Biały Krzyż Ski Lift is 2.
