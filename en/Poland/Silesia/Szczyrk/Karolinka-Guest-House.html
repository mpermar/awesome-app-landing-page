Description

Located in Szczyrk, the resort town in the Beskids, Karolinka Guest House offers accommodation with free private parking and a terrace with views of the mountains.

Numerous ski lifts are available nearby, including Kaimówka, 700 metres away. The rooms are decorated in a modern style and each features a private bathroom with a shower.

Free Wi-Fi is available. The studios at Karolinka Guest House come with a well-equipped kitchenette and all guests have use of a common kitchen where breakfast is served.

The guesthouse houses a sauna, available at a surcharge. Ski storage is offered and there also is a common room with a fireplace and great views of the mountains where guests can relax.

Czyrna-Solisko, the skiing complex, is 1. 6 km away and the cable car going to Skrzyczne is 900 metres from Karolinka.
