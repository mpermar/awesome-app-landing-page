Description

Willa jak u Babci is located in a quiet area of Wisła, a 15-minute walk from the centre and 1,4 km from the Wisła-Uzdrowisko Train Station and Museum of Skiing.

Free WiFi access is available. There is a fully equipped kitchenette with a microwave and a refrigerator in common area.

Bathrooms come with a shower. You can enjoy mountain view and river view. Some of the rooms have a fan.

At Willa jak u Babci you will find a garden with barbecue facilities and a shared kitchen.

Other facilities like ski storage are offered. Activities in the surrounding area include cycling and hiking.

The property offers free parking. If you feel like visiting the surroundings, check out Stozek Ski Lift 7 km away and 7,2 km from Adam Małysz Ski Jumping Hill.
