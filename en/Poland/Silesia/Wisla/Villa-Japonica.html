Description

Housed in a historical residence and featuring a museum of Japanese Arts and Crafts with over 1000 items, Villa Japonica offers elegant rooms with free WiFi and a private bathroom with a shower.

Villa Japonica features a mixture of Japanese art pieces and style with interiors and furniture from the 1930s in Poland.

Most rooms feature furniture from the interwar period. Meals, including a buffet breakfast, are served in a traditional Japanese bar that also functions as a gallery.

There is a mountain stream passing by the building, which is situated in a landscape park and a Nature 2000 area.

The property includes a volleyball court and a bonfire and barbecue area. Villa Japonica features a spa that includes a Japanese spa bath, as well as a traditional sauna.

Guests can also admire an extensive collection of Japanese art and craft. An array of massages is offered on site.
