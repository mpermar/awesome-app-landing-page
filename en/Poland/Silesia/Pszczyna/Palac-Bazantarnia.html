Description

Housed in a historical building which used to serve as a residence to a Pszczyna prince, Pałac Bażantarnia is a boutique property located in Pszczyna in the Silesia Region.

Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. Free private parking is available on site.

KL Auschwitz-Birkenau is 27 km from the property, while Kapias Demonstration Gardens is 8 km away.

Certain rooms have views of the mountains or garden. Rooms include a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower.

For your comfort, you will find slippers and free toiletries. Pałac Bażantarnia features free WiFi throughout the property.

A flat-screen TV is provided. You will find a children's playground and terrace at the property.

Room service can also be requested. Free bicycles are offered (subject to availability).

Free piano concertos take places at Pałac Bażantaria every Friday. The area is popular for skiing and golfing.

Katowice is 32 km from Pałac Bażantarnia, while Wisła is 38 km away. The nearest airport is Pyrzowice Airport, 55 km from the property.
