Description

Featuring free WiFi and a spa centre, Neptuno Resort & Spa offers pet-friendly accommodation in Dźwirzyno, 330 metres from the beach and 12 km from the centre of Kołobrzeg.

Guests can enjoy the on-site bar in this 2-hectare complex, as well as private parking.

Accessed separately form the outdoor corridor, each room is equipped with a flat-screen TV.

Some units include a seating area for your convenience. Enjoy a cup of complimentary tea or from your terrace or balcony.

All rooms come with a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. Guests can also enjoy several treatments in the spa which is located in a separate building or take part in yoga or nordic walking classes, all at surcharge.

The hotel restaurant offers wellness lunch, buffet breakfast and dinner. You can play table tennis, table footbal and billiards at the resort and also rent a bike here.

Koszalin is 49 km from Neptuno Resort & Spa, while Mielno is 43 km from the property.
