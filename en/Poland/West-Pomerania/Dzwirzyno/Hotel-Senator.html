Description

Hotel Senator is situated just 300 metres away from the beach on the Baltic Coast and it is surrounded by a forest.

It offers free WiFi, a selection of wellness services, which include a hot tub and an indoor swimming pool.

Senator has a beach with deck chairs. The Senator’s rooms are done in warm colours and feature wooden furniture, which includes a work desk.

Each has satellite TV and a minibar. A kettle can be provided on request. Guests can use the Senator’s various recreational facilities, which include 6 bowling lanes and a billiard room.

A sauna, steam bath and solarium, are also on-site. The 24-hour front desk rents out bicycles, so guests can explore the area’s bike routes.

A screening room is also on site. Meals and an everyday breakfast buffet are served in the hotel’s restaurant Arena which specialises in international cuisine.

A lobby bar and a club where guests can relax with a drink is also available. Senator allows guests to relax away from fast-paced city life.

The small town of Dźwirzyno is 1. 4 km away. The city of Kołobrzeg is 10 km away.
