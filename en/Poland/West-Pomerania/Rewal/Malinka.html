Description

Malinka is located 400 mestres from the centre of Rewal and 600 metres from the beach.

There is a garden with a children’s playground and a free private parking. The Internet is available in the reception area.

Decorated in pastel colours, all bright classic-style rooms here will provide you with a TV and a table, as well as a fridge.

Each comes with a bathroom fitted with a shower cabin. At Malinka there is a bar with a dining room where guests can enjoy buffet breakfast and a shared kitchenette is provided as well.

The property is a 7-minute walk from the local bus stop. The nearest grocery can be found within 500 metres.
