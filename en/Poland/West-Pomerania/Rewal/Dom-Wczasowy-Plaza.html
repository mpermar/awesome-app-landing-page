Description

Located only 30 metres from the sandy beach and a short walk away from city amenities, Dom Wczasowy Plaża features warm rooms with a TV with satellite channels.

Free Wi-Fi is also offered. A buffet breakfast is served in the dining room. There is a sun terrace and beach equipment can be rented.

Children are welcome to enjoy the game room and the playground in the garden. The property offers high chairs.

Dom Wczasowy Plaża is situated 660 metres from the Rewal Train Station and 1. 3 km from the Trzęsacz castle ruins.
