Description

Featuring a balcony, all units have a dining area and a seating area with a flat-screen TV. There is also a kitchenette, equipped with a microwave. A fridge and stovetop are also available, as well as a kettle. Free private parking is also available at this apartment. Bed linen is offered.

Międzyzdroje is 25 mi from Villa Baltica Rewal. Szczecin Goleniów Airport is 34 mi away.
