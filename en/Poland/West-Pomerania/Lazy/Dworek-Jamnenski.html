Description

Offering a barbecue and terrace, Dworek Jamneński is located in Łazy in the West Pomerania Region, 200 metres from Lake Jamno and 500 metres from the beach on the Baltic Sea.

The resort has a sun terrace and views of the city, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

Free WiFi is offered throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Each room includes a flat-screen TV and a fridge. All rooms have a private bathroom.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. Koszalin is 12 km from Dworek Jamneński, while Mielno is 11 km away.
