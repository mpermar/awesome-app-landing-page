Description

Situated in Łazy, OWR Universal has a bar, shared lounge, garden, and free WiFi. Boasting family rooms, this property also provides guests with a children's playground.

Private parking can be arranged at an extra charge. The resort offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

OWR Universal offers a barbecue. Koszalin is 20 km from the accommodation, while Mielno is 11 km from the property.
