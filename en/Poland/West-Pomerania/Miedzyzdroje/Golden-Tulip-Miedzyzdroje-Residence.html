Description

Golden Tulip Międzyzdroje Residence is located in a quiet part of the seaside resort and surrounded by forests of the Wolin National Park.

A sandy beach is 150 metres away. The spacious corner suites and studios offered by Golden Tulip are decorated in a modern style and feature fresh green and white colours.

Each comes with a seating area with a large sofa, a large, flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a balcony.

Bathroom is fitted with a hairdryer and free toiletries. Further facilities include a CD player and a telephone and free Wi-Fi.

There is also a fully equipped kitchenette with a microwave and a hob in every apartment.

The breakfast is served every morning and there is a restaurant specialising in international cuisine and a bar.

Guests can use a sauna, an indoor swimming pool for children and a steam bath for free.

Various treatments and massages can also be booked at a surcharge. A private parking is available at surcharge.

There are also equipped conference rooms which can be rented. Międzyzdroje Train Station is 2.
