Description

The apartment has 3 bedrooms and 1 bathroom with a bidet, a shower and a hair dryer. A flat-screen TV and Blu-ray player are featured.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at the property.

The apartment has a grill. If you would like to discover the area, hiking is possible nearby and a bicycle rental service can be arranged.

Kołobrzeg is 30 miles from Apartamenty Platinum. Solidarity Szczecin-Goleniów Airport is 32 miles from the property.
