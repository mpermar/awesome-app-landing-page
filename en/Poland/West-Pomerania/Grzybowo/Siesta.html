Description

All rooms in Siesta are equipped with a electric tea pot.

A continental, buffet or vegetarian breakfast is available daily at the property.

The accommodation has a playground. Cycling and hiking are among the activities that guests of Siesta can find nearby.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can assist with guidance about the area.

Kołobrzeg is 3.4 miles from the resort, while Koszalin is 28 miles away. Solidarity Szczecin-Goleniów Airport is 47 miles from the property.
