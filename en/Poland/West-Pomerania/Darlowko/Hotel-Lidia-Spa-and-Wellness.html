Description

At Lidia Spa & Wellness guests can enjoy free access to swimming pools, hot tub, steam bath and fitness centre.

All rooms feature free internet, LCD TV and a seating area. Each room at the Hotel Lidia is fitted with a minibar and a safety deposit box.

A private bathroom comes with a shower and a hairdryer. Some have air-conditioning.

Every morning buffet breakfast is served in the hotel restaurant, Łososiowa Restaurant, specialising in Polish and international dishes.

Guests can also try a selection of lighter choices at the hotel’s Zielona Restaurant At the Lidia Spa & Wellness guests can book a variety of facial and body treatments.

In the evening, they can visit a night club with a bowling alley and billiards. Hotel Lidia Spa & Wellness is situated 150 metres from the Darłówko Port and the sandy beach.
