Description

Trawa Hotel Sport is a 3-star property where guests can enjoy free access to the indoor swimming pool.

There is also free Wi-Fi access available in all areas. Rooms and junior suites here will provide you with a TV and a telephone.

Each features a bathroom fitted with a shower cabin. Some units boast a seating area, a balcony and a minibar.

At Trawa Hotel Sport you will find a restaurant and a bar. It is possible to relax in the sauna, at a surcharge, or explore the area on the rented bicycle.

Tennis court, billiards and darts are also available at an extra cost. The property offers free private parking.

The hotel is 3. 7 km from the centre of Koszalin. A 20-minute drive will take you to a sandy Baltic Sea beach.
