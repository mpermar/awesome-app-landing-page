Description

Offering an outdoor pool, barbecue and children's playground, Holiday Park & Resort Ustronie Morskie is situated in Ustronie Morskie in the West Pomerania Region.

only 500 metres from the Baltic Sea. It is 14 km from Kołobrzeg. Koszalin is 30 km away.

Free WiFi is featured throughout the property and private parking is available on site.

All units include a satellite flat-screen TV. Some units have a terrace and/or balcony.

All units feature a kitchenette equipped with a fridge and stovetop. Every unit has a private bathroom with a shower.

Bed linen is offered. Holiday Park & Resort Ustronie Morskie also includes a sun terrace and an on-site bar.

The property also offers packed lunches and mini-market. It is also possible to rent out barbecue facilities.

Sun loungers and sun umbrellas can be made available. There is also a playing field, a shared games room and the youngest guests can benefit from entertainment provided by qualified staff.

Bike hire is available at the property and the area is popular for cycling. Mielno is 19 km from Holiday Park & Resort Ustronie Morskie, while Dźwirzyno is 24 km from the property.
