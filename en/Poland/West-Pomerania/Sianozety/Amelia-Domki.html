Description

Guest rooms in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Amelia Domki provides some accommodations that have a terrace, and rooms are equipped with a electric tea pot. The rooms are equipped with a seating area.

The accommodation has a grill. You can play darts at Amelia Domki, and bike rental is available.

Kołobrzeg is 6 miles from the resort. The nearest airport is Solidarity Szczecin-Goleniów Airport, 54 miles from Amelia Domki.
