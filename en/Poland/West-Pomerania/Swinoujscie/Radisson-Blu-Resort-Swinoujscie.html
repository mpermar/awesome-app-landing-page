Description

The 5-star Radisson Blu Resort Swinoujscie is set in Świnoujście and 20 metres from Baltic Park Molo Aquapark where every guest enjoys free 1-hour daily access and 50% discount for each subsequent hour to such attractions as swimming pools, saunas and hot tubs.

Promenade is 130 metres from the hotel. Featuring free WiFi, all air-conditioned rooms come with a private bathroom.

Each is equipped with a TV and a desk. A buffet breakfast is served every morning at the property.

The on-site restaurant serves international cuisine. The area is popular for cycling and hiking, and bike hire is available at Radisson Blu Resort Swinoujscie.

Languages spoken at the reception include German and English. Zdrojowy Park is a 10-minute walk from the accommodation.

It is 1. 7 km from the Stawa Młyny beacon. The nearest airport is Szczecin Goleniów Airport, 70 km from Radisson Blu Resort Swinoujscie.
