Description

Offering direct entrance to the beach and a seasonal outdoor pool, Dom Przy Plaży SPA is located in Sarbinowo, next to a Promenade.

Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant serving European and Polish cuisine. Children's playground is available and the property has direct beach access.

Garden with sun terrace featuring panoramic sea view is available. Some apartments has sea view and all boast private terrace or balcony.

An on site spa includes sauna and hot tub and massages and beauty treatments are offered.

Bicycles are rented for free. Beach gear is free for Guests. In every room you will find hairdryer, towels, free toiletries and mineral water.

All units have flat-screen, kettle, fridge and microwave. Restaurant serves breakfast and dinner.

Every room comes with a flat-screen TV. A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms.

Each room has a private bathroom equipped with a shower. Both free WiFi and free private parking are available at this property.
