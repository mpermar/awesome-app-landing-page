Description

In peaceful, naturally beautiful surroundings, Król Plaza Spa offers high-quality accommodation and superb spa facilities available free of charge and including a pool, a dry sauna an infrared sauna, a steam bath, a salt cave as well as a spa bath.

In one of the most naturally beautiful and unspoilt parts of Jarosławiec, the property has a high-quality restaurant, a nightclub and a modern spa offering a comprehensive range of relaxing and therapeutic treatments.

Whatever the weather, you can relax and enjoy your stay, with a health or beauty treatment from fully qualified and experienced staff.

Some rooms here feature a sea view. Król Plaza Spa also boasts conference rooms and a delightful summer garden.

Guests can rent bicycles and room safes. There is a baby bathtub, nappy changer, electric nanny and water heater for babies.
