Description

Situated in Jarosławiec, West Pomerania region, Hotel NAT Jarosławiec is set 200 meters from Jaroslawiec Aquapark.

This 3-star hotel offers free WiFi. The property features a children's playground.

Rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV. All rooms come with a kettle, while some have a balcony.

At Hotel NAT Jarosławiec all rooms include a private bathroom with free toiletries.

Guests can try a wide selection of massage and body treatments. There is also a small gym and a basketball and volleyball court for the guests’ benefit.

Guests can rent a bicycle or play billiards and table tennis. Outdoor barbecue facilities are available.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning at the property. The on-site café at the Za Wydmą offers drinks and snacks.
