Description

Sandra Spa is located in Pogorzelica, 400 metres from a sandy Baltic Sea beach. Guests enjoy free access to the fitness centre and the swimming pool complex, which includes thermal, indoor and outdoor pools, slides and an all-year water playground for children.

Numerous other leisure facilities are also available. The rooms, classically decorated in fair colours, will provide you with a flat-screen TV and a mini-bar.

There also is a fridge and a balcony. Featuring a shower, the private bathrooms also come with a hairdryer and towels.

Extras include a desk. At Sandra Spa you will find saunas, steam baths and a solarium.

The spa and wellness centre also features a salt cave, a tepidarium and sensory showers.

Guests can also enjoy bowling, billiards, tennis and mini golf. There also is a roofed ice skating rink available in the winter season and a volleyball court.

Guests are welcome to dine in the on-site restaurant, which serves Polish cuisine.

There also is a pump room and a café and a pub. Sandra Spa is 500 metres from the Lake Liwia Łuża.
