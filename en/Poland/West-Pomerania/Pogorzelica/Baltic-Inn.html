Description

Baltic Inn is located in Pogorzelica, 150 m away from the nearest beach, in a pine forest.

The resort has 2 pools and free Wi-fi. The resort’s rooms are furnished with wooden furniture, and have their own private bathrooms with a shower.

Satellite TV is also present in each room. The guests of the Baltic Inn have free access to 2 exterior swimming pools.

Tennis courts and a fitness room are also at their disposal. In the summer barbecue dinners with music are held.

The resort also caters to the needs of its youngest guests. There is a smaller swimming pool dedicated for children, as well as a playground and a play room.

The resort has its own café and dining room, which serves a buffet breakfast in the morning.
