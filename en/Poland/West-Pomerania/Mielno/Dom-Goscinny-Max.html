Description

Dom Gościnny Max offers accommodation in Mielno, just 300 metres from a sandy beach by the Baltic Sea and 215 metres from Lake Jamno.

Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. Free WiFi is available and free private parking is available on site.

Each room is fitted with a flat-screen TV and a terrace. The rooms are fitted with a private bathroom and a fridge.

The property features a children's playground, billiards, a barbecue area and a seasonal outdoor pool.
