Description

Located by the Jamno lake and just 300 metres from a sandy beach by the Baltic Sea, Herbals&SPA-Tree&beach houses is situated in Mielno.

Guests can enjoy free use of bicycles, tandems, pedal boats, motorized dinghies, kayaks, bonfires, grills, board games.

The use of the sauna on the water is included in the price. Guests can also book a jet-ski ride, a speedboat, and a water or sailboat cruise.

They can take advantage of sailing training for children and adults. Massages are performed in the guest house with a view of the lake.

It is possible to decorate the bed with rose petals, order a basket of fruit, a cake with candles, a bottle of champagne and a bouquet of red roses.

Breakfasts and dinners are brought directly to the guest's house. Guests can choose from vegan, vegetarian, lactose-free or gluten-free options for breakfast.

There is also a multipurpose sports court, a tennis court, a small rope park, a trampoline, a playground for children, a designated bonfire area and a herb garden.

Bungalows offer a bathroom with a shower, a kitchenette and a fireplace. Some of them offer a sauna and a jacuzzi.
