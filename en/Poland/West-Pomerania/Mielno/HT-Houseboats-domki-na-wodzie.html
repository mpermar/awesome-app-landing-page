Description

HT Houseboats is located on Lake Jamno in Mielno, 300 metres from the sea. Guests can rent a pedalo, bicycles and tandem bikes.

They can also book a ride on a floating sauna, sailing boat, sea scooter and speedboat, as well as enjoy free use of kayaks, tennis court and a seasonal pool.

A sailing course is available to adults and children. HT houseboats offers all-year-round wooden houses located on the lake piers, with direct access to the water.

The accommodation will provide you with a flat-screen TV, a panoramic window and a lakeside patio.

Each houseboat includes a kitchenette and a bathroom with a shower, while Superior houseboats boast a sauna and a fireplace.

Extras include a barbecue. Some houses come with a motor boat to use free of charge.

Breakfasts, including vegan, vegetarian, gluten free and lactose free options and dinners are delivered to a guest's houseboat.

Guests can also order a massage, a bottle of champagne, a birthday cake or a red rose bouquet for an additional charge.

Motor boats, rafts with an engine and pedalos are available outside winter season.

There is a multipurpose sports field, a small rope park, a trampoline, a children's playground, a designated bonfire area and a herbal garden.
