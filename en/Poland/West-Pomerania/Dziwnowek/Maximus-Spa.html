Description

Only 100 metres from a sandy beach and 500 metres from the Wrzosowska Bay, Maximus Spa offers accommodation with impressive spa and wellness facilities.

A playroom for children is also available. The rooms at Maximus Spa are carpeted and elegant.

Each comes with a flat-screen TV as well as a balcony. A bathroom that comes with every room has a shower.

Rooms are located in the main building and in a separate one which has no lift. There is a spacious dining room where the meals can be enjoyed.

The bar serves a wide variety of drinks. Maximus Spa has an indoor swimming pool with waterfalls, a fitness room, as well as a sauna and a hot tub.

Various beauty treatments can be arranged. Dziwnów seaside resort is 3. 2 km away and Kamień Pomorski Train Station is 9 km from Maximus Spa and garage and parking are also available.
