Description

The Marine Hotel is located in the seaside resort town of Kołobrzeg, right by the beach front.

This designer building offers luxurious accommodation with air conditioning and free WiFi, as well as various leisure facilities.

This hotel's apartments feature designer furniture and offer a scenic view of the Baltic Sea.

A flat-screen satellite TV and private bathroom with a shower is in every apartment.

There are 2 restaurants at the Marine. The PerMare Restaurant and Lobster Restaurant specialise in international, Mediterranean and Asian dishes.

Marini Cafe is located on the top floor and offers picturesque sunset views. The Marine features multiple spa and wellness facilities.

Apart from relaxing in the sauna or hammam, guests may get one of the many massages or treatments the hotel offers.
