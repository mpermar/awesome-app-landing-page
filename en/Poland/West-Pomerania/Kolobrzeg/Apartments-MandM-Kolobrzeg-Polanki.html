Description

Apartments M&M offers are located in Kołobrzeg in a green area by Seaside Park, 350 metres from the beach on the Baltic Sea, 1.

6 km from the New Old Town and 1. 8 km from Town Hall. The apartments feature a living room, a kitchenette with a dishwasher and an oven, as well as a balcony or a terrace.

Free WiFi is available. There is a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Bathroom comes with a bath or shower, a washing machine, a hairdryer, towels and free toiletries.

The property offers free access to a seasonal heated outdoor swimming pool. There are also Finnish and infrared saunas, a gym and free private parking.

The apartment is located in a gated area with a children's playground and a kid's club.

The area is perfect for hiking and cycling, and guests have access to four bicycles available free of charge.

Catering meals, including gluten-free, lactose-free and children's menu are offered.

Guests are welcome to order catering including 5 meals a day and several options are available, including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free and lactose-free.

Meals need to be pre-ordered when booking. It is 3,3 km to the Kołobrzeg Lighthouse and 3,6 km from Kołobrzeg Pier.
