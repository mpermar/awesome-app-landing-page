Description

Health and Relax Center "Verano" is situated just 100 meters from the beach in Kołobrzeg.

It provides guests with a large recreational area and comfortable rooms with a balcony.

Free WiFi is available in the whole property. The rooms at the Verano come with satellite TV and a telephone.

All have an electric kettle and a refrigerator. Centrum Zdrowia i Relaksu "Verano" has an indoor swimming pool available at extra charge, as well as a jacuzzi.

Guests are also offered a tennis court, bicycle rental and a wide range of treatments.

The Verano is located 2 km from central Kołobrzeg. The Kołobrzeg Railway Station is about 500 metres from the property.
