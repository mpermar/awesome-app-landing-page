Description

The apartment is composed of 1 bedroom, a living room with dining area, a fully equipped kitchenette and 1 bathroom. A flat-screen TV and DVD player are provided.

Guests can relax at the in-house wellness area, where there's a sauna and a hammam. A grill is available on site and cycling can be enjoyed within close proximity of the apartment.

Town Hall is 1.7 miles from Luxury Apartment Baltic Polanki. Solidarity Szczecin-Goleniów Airport is 48 miles from the property.
