Description

The 4-star Shuum Boutique Wellness Hotel is located just 70 metres from a wide sandy beach and stands next to a beautiful, tranquil seaside park.

It offers air-conditioned rooms and free Wi-Fi. In the summer guests can enjoy a private beach with sun loungers, at a surcharge.

Each room at Shuum Boutique Wellnes Hotel includes a balcony and a bathroom. An LCD TV with satellite channels is also there, as well as a minibar, an electric kettle and a safety deposit box.

The restaurant offers a wide range of healthy and light homemade dishes, including vegan options, live cooking and a special menu for kids.

Meals are prepared from fresh, local and seasonal produce. In summer, guests can enjoy meals outside in the hotel garden.

The cafe serves a selection of coffees, homemade cakes and desserts, as well as alcoholic beverages and soft drinks.

Children can enjoy a separate swimming pool in the aqua park, 2 playgrounds in the garden and a kids club.
