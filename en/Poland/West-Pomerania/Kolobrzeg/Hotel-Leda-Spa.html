Description

Located 380 metres from the Baltic Sea, Leda Spa is a unique, boutique hotel offering air-conditioned spacious rooms with free WiFi and an LCD TV with satellite channels.

Guests can relax in luxurious spa facilities. All modern rooms at the Leda are fitted with a minibar.

Guests can benefit from a comfortable seating area and a safety deposit box. Bathrobes are provided.

The property features 2 restaurants. Every morning buffet breakfast is served in the buffest restaurant while the a-la-carte La Maison Restaurant serves Mediterranean and French specialities.

Guests can visit the Lobby Bar with a wide selection of delicious desserts and confectionery.

At the Hotel Leda Spa, guests can relax in a 2-storey wellness centre with an indoor swimming pool, a salt grotto and a 3 saunas as well as a resting area.

Elegant hot tub and a solarium are also available. In the summer Hotel Leda Spa provides a private beach featuring beach equipment, changing rooms, lifeguards, and a beach volleyball court.

At the Leda Spa, front desk staff is available 24 hours a day and can arrange shuttle services and luggage storage.
