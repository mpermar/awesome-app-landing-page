Description

Located 600 metres from the Baltic Sea, Ośrodek Wypoczynkowy Wiga has green surroundings and offers rooms with balconies and free Wi-Fi.

Kołobrzeg city centre is 2 km away. Each bright room comes with a large bathroom and satellite TV.

A wireless kettle and a small refrigerator are also available. Home-style Polish meals are served on request at the hotel's seasonal dining room.

Wiga also has a Tea Room where on-request breakfasts are served offseason. With the extensive Aleksander Fredro Park just 30 metres from Wiga, the setting is perfect for walks.

Children will enjoy the on-site playground. A 3-minute walk takes you to the Kołobrzeg Amphitheatre, where concerts and events are organised.

Kołobrzeg Train Station is 1. 7 km away and free hotel parking is also available.
