Description

Baltic Natur Park - Holiday Resort has a restaurant, seasonal outdoor swimming pool, a fitness centre and bar in Niechorze.

Among the various facilities are a garden and a terrace. The accommodation offers evening entertainment and a shared lounge.

All guest rooms at the resort come with air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kitchen, a dining area and a private bathroom with a hairdryer, a shower and free toiletries.

All units will provide guests with a wardrobe and a kettle. Baltic Natur Park - Holiday Resort offers a children's playground.

The area is popular for hiking and cycling, and bike hire is available at the accommodation.

Kołobrzeg is 47 km from Baltic Natur Park - Holiday Resort, while Dźwirzyno is 35 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Solidarity Szczecin-Goleniów Airport, 81 km from the resort.
