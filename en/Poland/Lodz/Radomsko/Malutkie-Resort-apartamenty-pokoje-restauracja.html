Description

Malutkie Resort occupies 20 hectares of woodland by Radomsko. It offers accommodation with free Wi-Fi and a very broad selection of leisure and sports facilities.

The rooms are classically appointed and done in warm colours. Each comes with a TV and a bathroom with a shower or a bathtub.

Some feature air-conditioning. At Malutkie Resort guest will fill find a sports hall, tennis courts and volleyball field.

There is a quad rental, a karting track, a rope park and a mini-zoo. On the premises there also are fishing ponds.

Other activities available include paintball and horse riding. Breakfast buffet is provided in the morning.

The property features a restaurant, serving Polish and regional dishes, and a 24/7 cocktail-bar.

There is also a gazebo with barbecue facilities. Malutkie is 15 km from Góra Kamieńsk Ski Centre.

There is a swiming pool 7 km away. The centre of Radomsk, with its train station, is 4 km away.
