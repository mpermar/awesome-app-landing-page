Description

Located just 300 metres from the centre of Aleksandrów Łódzki, the Pelikan offers a sauna and free Wi-Fi.

Its rooms come with an LCD TV with satellite channels. Every morning a buffet breakfast is served at the on-site restaurant, which specialises in Polish and international dishes.

Guests can enjoy a drink at the bar with a fireplace. All rooms at the Pelikan come with a working space and a modern bathroom with a hairdryer and a shower.

Some offer a seating area. Guests can enjoy billiards, as well as one of a kind virtual shooting range.

Front desk staff can arrange laundry and shuttle services. Free on-site parking is provided.

The Pelikan is located just 11 km from the centre of Łódź. The famous Manufaktura Shopping Centre is 10 km away.
