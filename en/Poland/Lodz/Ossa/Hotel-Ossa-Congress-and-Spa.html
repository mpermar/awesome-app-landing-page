Description

Set in the middle of the historic Masovian region, in the town of Ossa the classy 4-star Hotel Ossa Congress & Spa aims to bring business and leisure together.

Located on 23 hectares of beautiful greenery, the hotel has its own pond with a sandy beach and pedalos that guests can use in the summer season.

Various sports pitches and a tennis court are also offered. Located only an hour-long drive from Warsaw city centre, Hotel Ossa Congress & Spa offers high-quality accommodation.

Spend the morning attending meetings or making use of the free wireless internet, available in all public areas.

The spa centre is the perfect place to relax in style throughout the day. Pay a visit to the sauna, a hot tub or enjoy a swim in the large indoor pool with a slide.

Various beauty and body treatments are offered. Guests can also rent a bicycle or Nordic walking sticks.

Afterwards, you can rest in your elegantly furnished room or indulge in delicious cuisine at the restaurant or dance in the night Club 84.
