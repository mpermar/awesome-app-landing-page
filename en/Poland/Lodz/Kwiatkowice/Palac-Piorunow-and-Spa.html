Description

Pałac Piorunów & Spa is housed in a historic manor house, just 20 km from Łódź. Guests have free access to a swimming pool, a sauna and a steam bath.

All rooms at the Pałac Piorunów are fitted with a private bathroom with a bathrobe and a shower.

Each has a TV and a telephone. Some have a seating area. A buffet breakfast is served every morning in a hotel restaurant which specialises in traditional Polish dishes.

Guests can relax in a hotel bar and a try a wide selection of wine. At hotel’s spa & wellness centre, guests can benefit from massages and other body and facial treatments.

Bicycles and billiards are available free of charge. Pałac Piorunów & Spa offers free parking on site.
