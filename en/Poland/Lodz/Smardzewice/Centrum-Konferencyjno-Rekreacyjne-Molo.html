Description

Located only 50 metres from the coast of Lake Sulejowskie, Centrum Konferencyjno Rekreacyjne Molo is surrounded by the Sulejów Landscape Park.

It offers rooms with satellite TV and a free internet connection. The rooms of the Centrum Molo feature an elegant interior design with a seating area and a balcony that offers a picturesque view of the area.

All private bathrooms have a shower. The Molo provides a bicycle rental service and arranges canoeing activities.

It also has its own nightclub and water sports facilities. Guests can also enjoy a game of Foosball.

The hotel’s restaurant specialises in traditional and contemporary Polish dishes, as well as European cuisine.
