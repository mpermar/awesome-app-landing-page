Description

Ośrodek Wypoczynkowy Forest is a lake resort on the shores of Zemborzycki Reservoir.

It is located in a quiet, forested area on the outskirts of Lublin, 9 km from the city centre.

There are bicycle tourist trails, watersports equipment rentals and rope courses near the resort.

All the apartments are basically furnished. They have with tiled floors and a food preparation area.

Some feature a microwave and an electric kettle. There is a flat-screen TV in every apartment.

There is free, private parking in the resort. The Lublin Południowy Bus Station is located 7 km from Ośrodek Wypoczynkowy Forest.
