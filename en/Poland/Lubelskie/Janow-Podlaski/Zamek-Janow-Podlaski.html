Description

Zamek Janów Podlaski offers pet-friendly accommodation with free WiFi and private parking in Janów Podlaski.

The hotel features a children's playground, football field, tennis court and Biological Renewal and Rehabilitation Center.

The property also features U Biskupów restaurant, Lobby Bar and Irena Eris Spa centre.

All rooms have a flat-screen TV. Certain rooms include a seating area for your convenience.

For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hair dryer. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property.

Guests can relax in Biological Renewal and Rehabilitation Centre. Bike hire is available at this hotel and the area is popular for cycling.

Biała Podlaska is 19 km from Zamek Janów Podlaski, while Brest is 35 km from the property.
