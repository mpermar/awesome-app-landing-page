Description

Surrounded by a 6 hectare park, Dwór Sanna is a 4-star hotel in a historic manor house, located in Wierzchowiska Drugie, away from busy cities.

Its rooms feature flat-screen satellite TVs, seating areas and free Wi-Fi. The Sanna’s air-conditioned rooms feature a stylish interior design that combines traditional elements with an elegant modern décor.

Guests can fish at the fish pond or go hiking through the Dwór’s vast park with Sanna River's springs.

Guests can also rent bikes and quads on-site. There also is a sauna for visitors' use.

In the mornings guests can get breakfast at the hotel. The restaurant serves Polish and international meals.

Guests can also enjoy wines made in a nearby vineyard. Dwór Sanna is situated in the Roztocze Hill Range.
