Description

Featuring free WiFi, the Łubinowe Wzgórze Eko Resort is located in a peaceful area on the outskirts of Kazimierz Landscape Park, 2.

2 km from the Spa Nałęczów. It offers bright and elegant rooms with an LCD TV. All rooms at the Wzgórze are individually decorated with pastel tones and stylish, wooden furniture.

Each comes with an electric kettle, a desk or a table, as well as a hairdryer. The property houses a restaurant with fusion elements, which serves gluten-free and vegan meals.

There are also 3 conference room. Free private parking is provided. Łubinowe Wzgórze is situated 2.

4 km from the centre of Nałęczów. The nearest railway station, Nałęczów, is 5. 6 km away.
