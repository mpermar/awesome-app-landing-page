Description

Hotel Dwór Choiny is located in Kazimierzówka, 10 km from Lublin. It features stylish rooms with free internet, a flat-screen TV and a seating area.

Free WiFi access and free parking are provided. All rooms at Hotel Dwór Choiny are bright and decorated with warm colours.

Each comes with a private bathroom with a shower. Guests can relax on the terrace or in front of the fireplace.

The on-site restaurant offers Polish and European dishes. Breakfast is served there every morning.
