Description

Located on the shore of Białe Lake surrounded by woods and featuring a private beach with a pier, Ośrodek Sanvit features rooms and private bungalows with free Wi-Fi.

Every room in Sanvit features a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a bathroom with a shower.

Bungalows feature a terrace and rooms, a balcony or a terrace exit. Guests can rent bicycles and water equipment – sailing boats, kayaks and waterbikes, at extra charge.

There are swimming lessons available for children. There is a playground featuring a trampoline, a games room with video games and kids club.

You can also play beach volleyball or table tennis. Sanvit features a grill bar with a spacious patio offering great lake views.

There is also a restaurant specializing in Polish and regional dishes – offering buffet breakfast.

A choice of drinks is available in a lobby bar. It is 2. 2 km to Glinki Lake. River Bug is 8 km away.
