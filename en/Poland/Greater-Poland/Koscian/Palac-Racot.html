Description

Offering a la carte restaurant and horse stable, Pałac Racot is a historic palace located in Kościan.

Free Wi-Fi access is available. Rooms here will provide you with a TV and satellite channels.

Featuring a bath or a shower as well as free toiletries, private bathrooms also come with towels.

You can enjoy a garden view from all the rooms. Extras include ironing facilities and a fan.

At Pałac Racot you will find a 24-hour front desk, a garden and a terrace. Other facilities offered include a shared lounge, a games room and luggage storage.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling, billiards, horse and carriage rides, as well as tours of the stable and Carriage Museum.
