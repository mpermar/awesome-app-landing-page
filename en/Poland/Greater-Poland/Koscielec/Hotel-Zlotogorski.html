Description

Featuring free WiFi, Hotel Złotogórski offers pet-friendly accommodation in Kościelec, 2 km from the A2 motorway exit and 7 km from Koło.

Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. Free private parking is available on site.

The rooms include a private bathroom fitted with a bath or shower, as well as a flat-screen TV.

There is a business centre at the property. Billiards table is available free of charge.

Uniejów is 24 km from Hotel Złotogórski, while Konin is 25 km away. The nearest airport is Lodz Wladyslaw Reymont Airport, 72 km from the property.
