Description

Situated in the picturesque surroundings of Przemęt Landscape Park on the Dominickie Lake, Sułkowski Conference Resort offers air-conditioned rooms.

The town of Leszno is about 20 km away. The rooms offered by Sułkowski are stylishly decorated in a modern style.

There is a flat-screen TV, a wardrobe and a bathroom with a hairdryer in each. Free Wi-Fi is available.

The restaurant specialises in Polish and international cuisine and in the summer season guests can dine on the terraces with a beautiful view of the lake.

At Sułkowski Conference Resort the guests can enjoy a new indoor pool with hydro massage lounge seats.

Right next to the pool there is a wellness area, steam room, sauna and a fitness room.
