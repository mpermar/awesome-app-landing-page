Description

Hotel Podjadek is situated in a historic palace and park complex, Górzno, on the route between Ostrów Wielkopolski and Poznań.

It features rooms with free wireless internet and satellite TV. All rooms at Podjadek come with a private bathroom with a shower and necessary amenities.

Each room is fitted with a work area with a desk. A varied breakfast is served every morning in a hotel restaurant which specialises in Polish dishes.

Front desk staff at Hotel Podjadek is available 24 hours a day. Free parking is provided on site.
