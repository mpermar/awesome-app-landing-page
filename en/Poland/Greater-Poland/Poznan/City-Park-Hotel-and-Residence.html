Description

The City Park Hotel & Residence is located 1 km from the Poznań International Fair and 220 metres away from Hala Arena.

The property features two buildings - one with direct access to the wellness area, the restaurant and the bar, and the other with direct access to the Ułan Brewery and Cappuccina cafe.

Free Wifi is available throughout. All luxurious City Park suites come with a 40-inch LCD TV and a work desk.

Each room has a comfortable seating area and an elegant bathroom with a bath. All are air-conditioned and soundproofed.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the Cucina restaurant, awarded by the Gault&Millau guide, or try one of 700 types of whisky at the Whisky Bar 88.

The hotel features a swimming pool, a steam and dry sauna, a salt cave and a massage parlour.

City Park Hotel & Residence is situated 3 km from the Old Town Square and about 2 kilometres from Stary Browar Shopping and Art Centre.
