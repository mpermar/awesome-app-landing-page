Description

Novotel Poznań Malta is situated by the Lake Malta and features an outdoor swimming pool.

The hotel offers rooms with free Wi-Fi, satellite TV and tea/coffee making facilities.

The Novotel Poznań Malta features spacious and modern rooms, equipped with a minibar, a work desk and a telephone.

Each room comes with a private bathroom with a hairdryer and a bath. Novotel Malta has a modern fitness centre, to which all guests have free access.

In the morning guests can enjoy varied buffet breakfast. The Novotel’s restaurant serves international and French dishes and features a summer terrace.
