Description

Hotel Kama Park is a 3-star facility located in a picturesque area of the Sieraków Landscape Park, 700 metres from Jaroszewskie and Lutomskie Lakes.

It features sports and wellness facilities, free internet and rooms with an electric kettle.

A refrigerator, a safe and a TV can be found in each of the en suite rooms. They are all classically decorated and furnished.

Guests can enjoy Nordic walking, exercise in the on-site gym or simply relax in the sauna.

Table football, billiards and darts facilities are available. Breakfast and other meals are served in the on-site restaurant, which offers seasonal Polish dishes.

Drinks can be enjoyed at the bar or on the terrace. Hotel Kama Park is located in a peaceful area, surrounded by pine and birch woods.
