Description

Pałac Tłokinia is a historic palace surrounded by a classical park, located in Tłokinia Kościelna, 7 km from Kalisz.

It lies in a quiet, green area. Wi-Fi is available in the entire hotel. Every guest is greeted with a welcome drink.

All the rooms and apartments of Pałac Tłokinia and Pałacowa Oficyna are traditionally decorated, in a baroque, palace style.

They all have a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a bathroom with a shower.

Guests can enjoy a hotel restaurant, that serves Polish and international dishes.

Buffet breakfast is also served there. There is also a private parking. Pałac Tłokinia is 9 km from the Kalisz Train Station.
