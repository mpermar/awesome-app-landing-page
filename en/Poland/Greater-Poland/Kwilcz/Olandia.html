Description

Located in the middle of a large park, Olandia hotel is a renovated manor house complex.

It offers unique and comfort accommodations and spa treatments. All rooms in Olandia have an en suite bathroom with a shower.

They are modernly designed and have all necessary amenities. Olandia’s restaurant specializes in local and traditional polish cuisine.

Guests can choose from a wide selection of wine. Buffet breakfast is served every morning.

There are also BBQ facilities available. Olandia hotel features a garden with a recreation area.

Spa offers a selection of body and facial treatments. Hotel has a large garden and a terrace where guests can relax and enjoy an afternoon drink.
