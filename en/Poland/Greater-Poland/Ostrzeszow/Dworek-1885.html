Description

Dworek 1885 is located in Ostrzeszów, in the valley of Trzebnicko-Ostrzeszowskie Hills.

It offers elegant rooms with free Wi-Fi, a minibar and air-conditioning. All spacious rooms in Dworek come with a private bathroom with either bath or shower.

There is satellite TV in every room. Breakfast is served daily in Dworek Restaurant where guests can enjoy their meals on a terrace or in front of a fireplace.
