Description

Located on the shore of Bedany Lake Galindia Refugia is a themed property focusing on the pagan Galind tribe.

Free Wi-Fi access is available in this resort. The property features a restaurant and canoe and bicycle rental.

The accommodation here feature wooden elements and will provide you with a terrace and a TV with cable channels.

Featuring a bath or shower, private bathrooms also come with towels. You can enjoy a lake view and a garden view from all the rooms.

At Galindia Refugia you will find a garden with a playground for children, as well as a sculpture park.
