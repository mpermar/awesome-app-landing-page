Description

Siedlisko Morena is situated next to Łaśmiady Lake, in the scenic Masuria region.

It features country-style rooms with free WiFi, an indoor swimming pool and a Polish restaurant.

Rooms at Siedlisko Morena have wooden ceilings and come with satellite TV and a private bathroom with a bath and a shower.

Guests can relax in the hotel’s indoor and outdoor swimming pool, fitness room, dry sauna and fireplace room free of charge or play on volleyball courts.

The hotel features a playroom for children, as well. There is also free access to a private beach, located 100 metres from the property.

Siedlisko Morena has a restaurant serving traditional Polish food and international buffet meals for breakfast, lunch and dinner.
