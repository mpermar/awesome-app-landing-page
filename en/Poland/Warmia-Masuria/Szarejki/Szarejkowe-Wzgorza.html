Description

Located in a 27 hectare green area, Szarejkowe Wzgórza offers air-conditioned wooden bungalows with bathrooms, as well as a number of outdoor wooden steam baths.

Every house offered by Szarejkowe Wzgórza has two floors and features a living room with a kitchenette and a bathroom with a shower downstairs, and two bedrooms and a toilet upstairs.

There is also a spacious terrace. Szarejkowe Wzgórza has a 24-hour reception with a bathroom.

There is a camping area and a sauna, as well as bicycle and quad rental. Meals can be delivered from the local restaurant.
