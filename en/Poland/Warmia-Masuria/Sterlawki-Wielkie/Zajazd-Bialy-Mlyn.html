Description

Part of Zajazd Biały Młyn is housed in a renovated 19th-century, 3-storey mill. It is located 13 km from Giżycko and features comfortable rooms with free Wi-Fi and a TV.

Free private parking and an outdoor swimming pool are available. Breakfast is served every morning at the motel’s restaurant, which specialises in Polish and Lithuanian dishes made of fresh on-site produce.

Guests can enjoy a drink at the bar. All rooms at the Biały Młyn are bright and come with wooden furniture.

Each has a modern bathroom with a shower. Guests can enjoy barbecuing in the garden and children will enjoy the outdoor swimming pool and a playground provided on site.

Zajazd Biały Młyn is located 2 km from the nearest lakes. Famous Ryn Castle is 9 km away.
