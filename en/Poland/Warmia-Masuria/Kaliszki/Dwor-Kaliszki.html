Description

This classically-decorated house features 30 beautifully presented guest rooms and suites. Rooms are stylish in design and feature antique furnishings alongside more modern touches.

The Dwór Kaliszki is surrounded by a historic parkland, home to two 300-year old oak trees that tower over the gardens. Guests at the property can enjoy leisurely walks, play outdoor games or explore by bike. For the more adventurous, horseback riding and 4-wheel drive adventures can be arranged.

The hotel's beautiful restaurant is open daily for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Guests can enjoy many traditional Polish dishes alongside European cuisine.
