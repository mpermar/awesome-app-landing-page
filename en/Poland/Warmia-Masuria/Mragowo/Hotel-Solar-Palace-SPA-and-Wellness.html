Description

This attractive hotel, located in the town of Mrągowo, boasts beautiful views of Lake Czos and welcomes guests seeking pure relaxation.

An indoor pool with water attractions, a hot rub and a gym are at their disposal free of charge.

Set in a romantic garden, Hotel Solar Palace SPA & Wellness includes 60 individually furnished guest rooms.

Most of the rooms feature a balcony or a terrace. The property also offers a bar serving refreshing drinks.

Aqua aerobics classes can be arranged upon request in the property's swimming pool.

Other wellness facilities include a dry and infrared sauna, as well as a relaxation room.

A cryotherapy room is also available upon request. A variety of face and body treatments is also provided upon prior request.

The garden area features a creek and pond, making it the ideal spot for a short stroll or memorable barbecue.

After attending a meeting or special event at the hotel, you can either play billiards with new-found friends or watch TV in your room.
