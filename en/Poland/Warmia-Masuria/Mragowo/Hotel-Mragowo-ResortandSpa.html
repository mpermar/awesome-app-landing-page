Description

The 4-star Hotel Mrągowo Resort&Spa is located directly on the Czos Lake. It offers rooms and bungalows, all of which are equipped with a TV, a telephone, a minibar, a safety deposit box and free WiFi.

The building’s architecture harmonises with the natural surroundings and the terrace offers a beautiful view of the area.

Guests have access to the Spa & Wellness zone where a pool with water massages, a hot tub, and a swimming pool with a slide are available.

Various treatments can be booked. Further facilities include a sauna, a gym, a fitness room and a year round outdoor hot tub.

The property offers a beach and a pier where guests can hire water sports equipment.

Hotel Mrągowo Resort&Spa has various sports facilities, such as 5 tennis courts, mini golf, volleyball and mini football pitch, bike hire, bowling alley, billiards and table tennis.

The youngest guests will have lots of fun in the colourful play room. Furthermore, there is a children's zip-line, foosball table, outdoor playgrounds and a paddling pool.

A children’s menu is also offered at the restaurant. The stylishly decorated restaurant with large windows and a terrace with a beautiful view of the lake specializes with Polish and regional cuisine but the menu offers international dishes too.

A relaxing drink or coffee can be enjoyed in the lobby bar or bowling alley bar. Hotel Mrągowo Resort&Spa provides fully equipped conference rooms and can organize tailor-made team building events.
