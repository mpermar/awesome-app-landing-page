Description

Offering a restaurant, Folwark Żuławski is located in a quiet area in the outskirts of Elblag, 6 km from the historic centre.

Surrounded by floral fields, the elegantly-decorated hotel features roof windows, studios and en suite rooms.

Free Wi-Fi access and free bicycles are available. Rooms here come with private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries.

You can enjoy garden view from the accommodations. A full English breakfast is served daily.

Guests can also prepare a tea or coffee at any time of the day, use barbecue facilities or sunbathe on a terrace.

Other facilities offered at the property include a tour desk, luggage storage and a children's playground.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling and hiking.

The hotel is 3. 6 km from Elbląg Canal, 6. 1 km from Lake Drużno and 5. 3 km from Elbląg Railway Station.
