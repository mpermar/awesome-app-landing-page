Description

Hotel Gołębiewski Mikołajki is situated among beautiful surroundings of Masuria, offering elegant, spacious rooms with free internet connection.

The hotel includes the Tropikana Aqua Park, a spa centre and horse riding facilities.

Each room at the Gołębiewski features a bathroom with shower and hairdryer, as well as free toiletries.

There is a balcony and satellite TV, as well as free, bottled mineral water. Some rooms offer wonderful views of the nearby lakes.

The aqua park boasts both indoor and outdoor water facilities. There is also a wide choice of spa and wellness treatments, saunas and spa baths.

The property has its own marina with water sport equipment. The hotel includes the Stajnia horse riding club, as well as golf lessons.

Guests can also play bowling, enjoy game machines. There is also a year-round sleigh run, a cable car, a ski run and an inflatable slide and carousel.

There are 2 restaurants on site – the Zielona serves Polish and European dishes à la carte and the Czerwona offers buffet meals, as well as cuisine from a different country every day.

Hotel Gołębiewski Mikołajki has free public parking, as well as paid, monitored one.
