Description

The 3-star Mikołajki Resort Hotel & Spa Jora Wielka is located by the Lake Tałty, 8 km from the centre of Mikołajki.

Guests have free access to the indoor pool, saunas and beach area. Free WiFi access is available.

Every room comes with a TV. There is also a refrigerator and a private bathroom in each unit.

Extras include a desk, a safety deposit box and bed linen. The on-site restaurant serves breakfast and a wide selection of Polish dishes.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling, sailing, fishing and hiking.

The on-site spa offers treatment and it is possible to rent out a private hot tub at a surcharge.

In the summer season a number of attractions and animation activities for children is organized.
