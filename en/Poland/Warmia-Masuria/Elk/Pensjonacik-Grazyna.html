Description

Pensjonacik Grażyna is located on the shore of Ełckie Lake, in the centre of the town of Ełk.

It offers modernly furnished rooms and apartments with free Wi-Fi. Every room in Pensjonacik Grażyna features a TV with cable channels.

There is also a bathroom with a shower. Grażyna features free beauty treatment for women.

There is a playground for children, with a slide and a sandbox. Breakfast is served in the common dining room.

It is 300 metres to the horseriding stable, a volleyball field and tennis courts.

A Teutonic Order Castle is 1. 2 km away and a station of Ełk Narrow Gauge Railway is 1.
