Description

Omega Hotel is surrounded by forest at the scenic Ukiel Lake, within a 5-minute drive of the Olsztyn Old Town.

It features spacious rooms with satellite TV, work desks and free Wi-Fi. The hotel has a restaurant serving delicious European cuisine.

There is also a bar and a summer garden with a playground for children. A full breakfast buffet is provided each morning at the Omega.

There are also barbecue facilities. The reception at the Omega Hotel is open 24/7.
