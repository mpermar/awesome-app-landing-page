Description

Set in Olsztyn and only 5 km from Olsztyn Stadium, Calamo Park offers accommodation with pool views, free WiFi and free private parking.

With garden views, this accommodation provides a balcony. The apartment features 1 bedroom, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, an equipped kitchen with a dishwasher and a microwave, a washing machine, and 1 bathroom with a shower.

The apartment offers a children's playground. A bicycle rental service is available at Calamo Park, while cycling can be enjoyed nearby.

Olsztyn Planetarium is 5 km from the accommodation, while Olsztyn Bus Station is 6 km away.

The nearest airport is Olsztyn-Mazury Regional, 59 km from Calamo Park, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
