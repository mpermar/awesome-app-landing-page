Description

Surrounded by a forest and located on the lake, Hotel Venus offers accommodation with free internet.

Gdańsk city centre is 20 km away. The rooms at Hotel Venus are decorated in warm colours and come with a bathroom and flat-screen TV.

Guests can also stay at wooden cottages equipped with a refrigerator and kitchenware.

The elegant restaurant offers regional food and there are also a café and a wooden hut available with animal pelts and a fireplace where private parties can be organised.

Guests can also barbecue and a bonfire can be arranged. Guests at Venus have free access to a swimming pool while billiards, darts and bowling are offered at surcharge.

There is a playground for children and a petting zoo open between spring and autumn.
