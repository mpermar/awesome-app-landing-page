Description

Set within the beautifully restored Rekows Estate Palace, originally built in 1871, this modern hotel provides extensive facilities to ensure a comfortable and relaxing stay in Rekowo Górne.

The Wieniawa enjoys a beautiful setting within a 2-hectare private park, complete with a small pond.

It also features a fine restaurant and cellar bar and 3 comprehensive meeting rooms.

For relaxation, guests can enjoy the fitness room and sauna, as well as a golf simulator.

Wieniawa is located near the centre of Gdynia (about 20 minutes), only 5 km from E6 Route home to a busy port on the Baltic Sea.
