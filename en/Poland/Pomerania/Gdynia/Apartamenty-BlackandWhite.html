Description

Located only 450 metres from the Baltic Seafront, the Apartamenty Black&White offers luxurious and fully equipped apartments with free Wi-Fi, free private parking and LCD TVs.

All Black&White apartments have high quality furnishings and come with wooden floors.

Each has a fully equipped kitchenette with a dining area. Most have a seating area, where guests can relax and enjoy cable TV.

Baby equipment, including a high chair, a baby bath and a foldout bed, can be provided upon request.

Apartamenty Black&White is located in Gdynia city centre, 800 metres from Gdynia Główna Railway Station.
