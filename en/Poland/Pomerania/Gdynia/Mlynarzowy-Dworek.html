Description

Młynarzowy Dworek is located in the historic house built in the year 1868 on the grounds of a XIVth century mill.

It is 1. 5 km from the beach with a pier and 1 km from Science and Technology park in Gdynia.

It offers apartments with free Wi-Fi and satellite TV. Each apartment offers homely interiors and a flat-screen TV.

There is a seating area and wooden furniture. The bathroom includes a shower. Guests are welcome to cook their own meals in the kitchenette with an induction cooker and an electric kettle.

There is a possibility to purchase freshly caught fish next door, by the historic marina.

Guests can relax in the garden with barbecue facilities and a children’s playground.

Młynarzowy Dworek is situated 200 metres from a forest and 1. 8 km from the golf course.

Bistro Serwus restaurant is within 750 metres and the menu is available at the reception.

The Gdynia Orłowo City Rail Station with easy access to all parts of Tricity is 1 km away, and the nearest bus stop is 30 metres from Młynarzowy Dworek.

There is a 15% discount for a taxi corporation services and guests can receive more details by contacting the property directly.
