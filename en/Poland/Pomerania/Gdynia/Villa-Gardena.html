Description

Villa Gardena offers rooms and apartments in Gdynia, only 650 metres from the beach on the Baltic Sea.

Each has free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a kitchenette with kitchenware.

Gardena consists of two buildings, one offering modern apartments, and the the other, elegant rooms.

Every unit features a bathroom with a shower and free toiletries, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and an electric kettle.

The apartment kitchenettes have an electric kettle, refrigerator, dishwasher and hob.

There is a dining area in each apartment. The Gardena has a large garden with garden furniture and a brick barbecue.

The apartments are situated on the ground floor and each has its own entrance. Breakfast can be provided in a 3-star hotel next door.

The apartments are located in a calm villa district. Klif Shopping Centre is within 200 metres, and Gdynia Orłowo Railway Station is within 300 metres.
