Description

Hotel Różany Gaj is situated in the prestigious part of Gdynia, Kamienna Góra, by the boulevard going along the sea.

It offers accommodation with a sauna, an indoor swimming pool and a hot tub. The hotel is eco-friendly and uses solar panels.

It offers elegant rooms decorated in light, warm colours and there is a flat-screen TV and a bathroom with a hairdryer in each of them.

Most have a balcony or terrace exit. Free Wi-Fi is available. The restaurant at Różany Gaj serves Polish food made with local produce.

Guests can also relax with a drink at the bar Horyzont or admire the views of the sea from the terrace on the top floor.

Gdynia’s city center is about 2 km away and Gdynia Główna Train Station is 2.5 km from Hotel Różany Gaj.
