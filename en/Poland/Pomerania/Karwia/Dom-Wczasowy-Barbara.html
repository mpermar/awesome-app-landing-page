Description

Dom Wczasowy Barbara is located in Karwia. Free WiFi access is available. The accommodation will provide you with a TV and a balcony.

There is also an electric kettle. Private bathrooms come with a shower. Extras include an outdoor seating area and bed linen.

At Dom Wczasowy Barbara you will find a garden. Other facilities offered at the property include a shared lounge and a children's playground.

Breakfast and dinner are served in the dining room. This resort is 53 km from Gdansk Lech Walesa Airport.
