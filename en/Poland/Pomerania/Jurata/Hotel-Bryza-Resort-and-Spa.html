Description

Hotel Bryza Resort & Spa is located just over the sea in Jurata. It features a spa and wellness centre, elegant restaurants and bars, and rooms with a balcony and free Wi-Fi.

Guests can use the complex of indoor swimming pools and later on relax in a sauna, a steam bath, dry sauna and herbal sauna.

A wide selection of relaxation, beaty and rejuvenation facial and body treatments is available, as well as a variety of massages, including Bali and Thai massage.

There is also a well-equipped fitness centre, 2 tennis courts and a seasonal outdoor pool.

All air-conditioned rooms at the Hotel Bryza Resort & Spa are bright and come with a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a desk, a minibar and a free bottle of mineral water.

Each room has a seating area and a bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer. Guests can admire the garden view or forest view and apartments overlook the sea or the main promenade.

A varied buffet breakfast is served every morning at the elegant Biała Restaurant, which is also open for lunch and dinner.

Guests can enjoy a cake, coffee, tea or a cocktail at the Monte Carlo café. In the summer, the Markiza Restaurant and the beacch bar, Coco-Beach, are also available.

All the restaurants specialise in Polish and European dishes made with local and ecological ingredients.

Hotel Bryza Resort & Spa is located 200 metres from Jurata’s main street. Free on-site guarded parking is provided, as well as an electric and hybrid vehicle charging station.
