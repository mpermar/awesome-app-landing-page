Description

Balaton Rowy in Rowy has accommodation with barbecue facilities, a garden and a terrace.

This guest house offers free WiFi. The accommodation provides a shared kitchen and a shared lounge for guests.

At the guest house, all rooms have a wardrobe. A seating area is at guests' disposal in the units.

The rooms have a private bathroom. The accommodation offers a children's playground.

The area is popular for cycling. You will find shops at the property. Breakfast is served in the dining room from April to September.

You can engage in various activities, such as cycling and fishing. The guest house also offers free use of bicycles and bike hire.
