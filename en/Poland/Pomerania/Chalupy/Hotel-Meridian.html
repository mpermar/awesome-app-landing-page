Description

Hotel Meridian is located at the Hel Peninsula, just 100 metres from the beach. It features rooms with free Wi-Fi, an LCD satellite TV and a refrigerator.

Guests can use the indoor pool, sauna and hammam free of charge. All air-conditioned rooms at the Meridian are fitted with a private bathroom with a shower as well as a hairdryer.

Most have balconies or terraces with a view of the Puck Bay. Each has a refrigerator, a safety deposit box and a work desk.

There is a mosquito net installed in every window. Breakfast is served in a hotel restaurant which specialises in Polish and European food, especially fish dishes.

Guests can enjoy their meals on a large terrace or in a garden. Front desk staff is available 24 hours a day and can help with bicycle or windsurfing equipment storage.

Guests can benefit from a modern spa centre and enjoy a massage at a surcharge. There is also a sauna, a hot tub, as well as a wide range of cosmetic treatments.

Billiards is available for free. Lobby features a computer available for guest free of charge.

Hotel Meridian is located just 20 metres of the Bay of Puck. Władysławowo is only 8 km from the hotel.

Right by the hotel you will find the a picturesque cycling route to Hel , which runs directly on the seafront for almost entire length.
