Description

Pensjonat Luan is located in the centre of Władysławowo, 800 metres from the beach on the Baltic Sea.

It offers modern rooms with free Wi-Fi. Every room in Luan features a spacious terrace, a TV, a fridge and an electric kettle.

There is also a bathroom with a shower. Pensjonat Luan features a playground for children.

There is an elevator. Luan also has an underground parking lot available at a surcharge.

Buffet breakfast is served in the restaurant. It is 500 metres from Pensjonat Luan to Władysławowo Train Station.
