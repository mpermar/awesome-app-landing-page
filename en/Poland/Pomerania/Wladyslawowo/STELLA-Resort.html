Description

Set 400 metres from a beach in Władysławowo-Chłapowo, STELLA Resort & Spa provides accommodation with private balconies.

Featuring room service, this property also provides guests with free WiFi. All units at the hotel are equipped with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with a hair dryer and a shower.

The rooms will provide guests with a desk and a kettle. A buffet breakfast is available daily at STELLA Resort & Spa.

Sopot is 42 km from the accommodation, while Gdynia is 34 km away. The nearest airport is Gdańsk Lech Wałęsa Airport, 48 km from STELLA Resort & Spa.
