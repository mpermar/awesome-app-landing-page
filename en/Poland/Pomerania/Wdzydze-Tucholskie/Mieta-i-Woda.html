Description

Guest rooms feature a shower, free toiletries, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and DVD-Player. With a private bathroom, rooms at the resort also feature a garden view. All guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

A buffet breakfast is available each morning at Mieta i Woda.

The accommodation has a playground. You can play ping pong, pool, and darts at Mieta i Woda.

Guests can use the business center or rent a bike to explore the area.

Chojnice is 24 miles from the resort. The nearest airport is Gdańsk Lech Wałęsa Airport, 35 miles from the property.
