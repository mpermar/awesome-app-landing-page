Description

Featuring free WiFi and a spa centre, Hotel Sopot offers pet-friendly accommodation in Sopot, 700 metres from Sopot Aquapark where guests enjoy 20% discount.

The hotel has a children's playground and views of the sea, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Private parking is available on site. Each room at this hotel is air conditioned and comes with a flat-screen TV.

You will find a kettle in the room. Each room includes a private bathroom. Extras include free toiletries and a hairdryer.

The helpful staff at the 24-hour reception can arrange shuttle, for guests' convenience.

Sopot Pier is 1. 2 km away. The nearest airport is Gdansk Lech Walesa Airport, 10 km from Hotel Sopot.
