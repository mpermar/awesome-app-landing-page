Description

Just a 3-minute walk away from Sopot’s promenade, Hotel Haffner features a luxurious spa and wellness centre with a swimming pool, a sauna and a gym.

Rooms come with satellite TV and free internet access. Hotel Haffner’s spacious and air-conditioned rooms feature a private bathroom with either a shower or a large bath.

A bathrobe awaits in each one. Rooms include a fully stocked minibar and a work desk.

The hotel restaurant offers Mediterranean cuisine and traditional Polish specialities.

For American dishes, guests can visit the hotel bar. There is also a hotel terrace where guest can relax and enjoy the view.

Front desk staff is available 24 hours a day to help or answer any questions. The hotel also features facilities like a Baby Hotel and a play room for its youngest guests.

Hotel Haffner is located within a 14-minute walk of the famous Sopot Pier. The city’s railway station is only one kilometre away.
