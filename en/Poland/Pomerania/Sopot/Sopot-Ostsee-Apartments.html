Description

Offering free WiFi and a garden, Sopot Ostsee Apartments is set in Sopot. Crooked House is 400 metres away.

Free private parking is available on site. The accommodation has a seating area. There is also a kitchen, equipped with a fridge.

There is a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer in each unit. Towels and bed linen are featured.

Sopot Ostsee Apartments also includes a hot tub. Bike hire and car hire are available at the property and the area is popular for golfing and windsurfing.

Sopot Pier is 800 metres from Sopot Ostsee Apartments, while Leny Stadium is 1. 1 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Gdansk Lech Walesa Airport, 9 km from Sopot Ostsee Apartments.
