Description

Situated directly by the beach and Sopot Pier, Sheraton Sopot offers quiet rooms with views of the sea or the park.

The hotel has a spa with an indoor swimming pool. Rooms at Sheraton Sopot come with air conditioning and a flat-screen satellite TV.

All include a private bathroom with a bath and a shower. The hotel has 2 restaurants, 2 wine bars and 2 regular bars.

The Wave serves Polish and international dishes, as well as seafood. The Vinoteque Sopot located in the cellar of the historic Rotunda offers a wide selection of wines, a meeting spot and a Cigar Lounge.

It is possible to order food as well. The hotel is situated only 200 metres from Bohaterów Monte Cassino Street (Monciak), Sopot’s main street, which is full of shops, bars and cafes.

Opera Leśna lies 2 km from Sheraton Sopot, Ergo Arena is 2. 9 km away and PGE Arena, 10.

9 km. Old Town of Gdańsk lies 12 km from Sheraton, and Skwer Kościuszki, a central street of Gdynia, is 9 km away.
