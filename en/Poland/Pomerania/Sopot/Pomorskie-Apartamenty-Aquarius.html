Description

Pomorskie Apartamenty Aquarius offers self-catering apartments located in the centre of Sopot, in the Aquarius Complex, 800 metres from a beach on the Baltic Sea.

Free Wi-Fi access is available. The property is 800 metres from Forest Opera and 1.

5 km from Aquapark Sopot. The modern, one-bedroom apartments will provide you with a flat-screen TV and a terrace.

There is a full kitchenette with a dishwasher and a microwave. Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with a hairdryer.

The property offers free parking space. There is a basketball and tennis court in the vicinity.

Fitness zone is available at extra charge. It is 500 metres to Monte Cassino Street, a town promenade.
