Description

Offering garden views, all units come with a kettle, a satellite flat-screen TV, ironing facilities and a living room. There is a fully equipped private bathroom with shower and a hair dryer.

A buffet breakfast can be enjoyed at the property.

The vacation park offers a children's playground.

If you would like to discover the area, hiking is possible in the surroundings and a bicycle rental service can be arranged.

Leśny Stadium is 1.5 mi from PARK45, while Forest Opera is 2 mi from the property. The nearest airport is Gdańsk Lech Wałęsa Airport, 5.6 mi from the property.
