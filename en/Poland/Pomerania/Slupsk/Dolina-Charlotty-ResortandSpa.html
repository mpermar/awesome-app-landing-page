Description

With a magnificent lakeside location, surrounded by beautiful countryside, stylish interiors and great leisure facilities, Dolina Charlotty Resort & Spa offers a fairytale-like atmosphere for a relaxing stay.

The rooms and apartments of Dolina Charlotty Resort and Spa are comfortably equipped and elegantly decorated with handmade furniture, brick walls and soft fabrics.

One of the things that make Dolina Charlotty stand out is its wonderful spa and wellness facilities.

The views from the swimming pool room are great, and the frescoed ceilings and walls create an enchanted atmosphere.

Dry and steam saunas and beauty and massage parlours are also at your disposal. During your stay, you can visit the horse stables and enjoy horse riding.

From the restaurant, which is built on the lake, you can fully appreciate the beauty of the surroundings.
