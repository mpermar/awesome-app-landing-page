Description

Papaj Resort is surrounded by the beautiful Coastal Landscape Park and offers elegant or maritime-themed rooms with free Wi-Fi.

The beach is located only 150 metres from Papaj Resort. Each room includes a private bathroom with shower, as well as a flat-screen TV, a small fridge and an electric kettle.

Beach screens and deckchairs are also provided. The property features a playground for children and runs bicycle rentals.

There is a Papaj restaurant serving seafood and Polish cuisine. A wide choice of drinks can be enjoyed in the bar.

There is also the Przypiecek smokehouse, offering fish, smoked with traditional methods, using alder wood.

Papaj Resort has free private parking and is situated by the Światowida Promenade.
