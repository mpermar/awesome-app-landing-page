Description

Offering houses with a barbecue, children's playground and sun terrace, Domki Letniskowe Błękit is situated in Jastrzębia Góra in the Pomerania Region, 46 km from Sopot.

Gdynia is 37 km away. Free WiFi is available throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

The houses feature two bedroom and a living room with a kitchenette, a seating area with a flat-screen TV and CD player.

All units feature a dining area and a terrace The kitchenette comes equipped with a microwave.

A fridge and kettle are also offered. Every unit features a private bathroom with a shower.

Bed linen is provided. You can play darts at the property, as well as table football, badminton, volleyball and basketball.

The area is popular for hiking. The whole property is enclosed and the stairs are baby-proofed.

Władysławowo is 7 km from Domki Letniskowe Błękit, while Łeba is 49 km from the property.
