Description

Just 100 metres from the beach, Hotel La Siesta Jastrzębia Góra features a free access to a sauna, steam bath and fitness centre.

All rooms are fitted with free WiFi and a refrigerator. Each room at Hotel La Siesta is fitted with cable TV and a private bathroom.

Most have a balcony. A varied buffet breakfast is served every morning in a hotel restaurant specialising in Polish and European dishes.

Vegetarian and children menu are available. Guests can enjoy a game of billiards or table tennis.

Front desk staff is available 24 hours a day and can arrange shuttle service. A modern children’s playground is provided on site.

Hotel La Siesta is located 300 metres from the centre of Jastrzębia Góra. The Rozewie Lighthouse is only 3 km away.
