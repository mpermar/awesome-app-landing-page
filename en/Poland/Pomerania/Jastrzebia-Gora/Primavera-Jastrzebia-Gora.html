Description

With just 100 metres from the sea and the sandy beach, Primavera Jastrzębia Góra offers free access to a swimming pool, and a fitness centre.

All bright rooms at the Primavera have free Wi-Fi, a balcony and a safety deposit box.

Each comes with a private bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer. Beach equipment is provided in season.

A varied buffet breakfast is served every morning in the hotel restaurant, which specialises in international dishes.

In the evening guests can enjoy a drink at the nightclub. Primavera Jastrzębia Góra offers a spa & wellness centre with massage treatments and saunas.

Bicycle rental is available. Guests can also enjoy playing billiards or table tennis.

Primavera Jastrzębia Góra is located 1. 5 km from the center of Jastrzębia Góra and 2 km from the Rozewie Lighthouse.
