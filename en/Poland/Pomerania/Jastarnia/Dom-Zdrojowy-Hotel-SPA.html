Description

Dom Zdrojowy Hotel SPA is located on the Hel Peninsula, between the Baltic Sea and a picturesque bay.

It offers a spa centre and air-conditioned rooms with free internet. It features a recreational centre with a swimming pool, saunas, a hot tub, a steam bath and a gym.

All these facilities are available free of charge. The spacious rooms at Dom Zdrojowy Hotel SPA come with a balcony.

All are equipped with cable TV and a safe. An electric kettle and a minibar are also provided.

All come with complimentary bottled water. The hotels's air-conditioned, 2-level restaurant serves regional and Mediterranean specialities.

The hotel café has a terrace overlooking the Bay of Puck. There is a billiards table.

The hotel is located just 50 metres from the beach and 20 km from the seaside resort of Władysławowo.
