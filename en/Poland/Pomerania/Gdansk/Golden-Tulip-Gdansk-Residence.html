Description

Golden Tulip Gdańsk Residence is located in a quiet area of Gdańsk, near the sandy beach on the Baltic Sea, and features spacious rooms and apartments with free WiFi access, as well as a free indoor pool, a sauna and a steam bath.

The property consists of 10 separate buildings. Rooms in Golden Tulip Gdańsk Residence feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

There is also an iron with an ironing board. There is also a kitchenette in every room and a bathroom with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Golden Tulip Gdańsk Residence features a 24-hour reception, a green garden and a sun terrace.

There is a spa and wellness centre with a choice of treatments and facilities available, as well as bicycle rental.

Buffet breakfast is served in the property restaurant. There is also a bar. The property also features a modular conference room for up to 200 people.

It is 150 metres to a green Ronald Reagan Park, 2,6 km to the nearest fast city train (SKM) Train Station, and 2,6 km to Gdańsk Oliwa Park.

It is 3,5 km to the Sopot city centre, with a famous pier, and 9 km to the historic Old Town of Gdańsk.
