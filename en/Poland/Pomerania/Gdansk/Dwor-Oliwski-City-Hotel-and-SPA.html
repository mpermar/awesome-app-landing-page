Description

Housed in a 17th-century manor house the five-star Dwór Oliwski City Hotel & SPA is located in one of the most picturesque parts of Tricity - Dolina Radości.

It provides luxurious accommodation with free access to the hotel spa. It is located in green and peaceful surroundings, yet only 5 km from Sopot’s centre.

Guests are welcomed with a sweet snack. The rooms at Dwór Oliwski City Hotel & SPA come with individually controlled air conditioning, a minibar and a safe, as well as a satellite 32-inch flat-screen TV.

All have a large bathroom with a hairdryer. The spa includes a 15-metre long indoor swimming pool, a hot tub, gym and saunas, as well as artificial beach area.

Front desk staff can arrange massage and facial treatments. Conference facilities can be used at surcharge.

During the 2012 football championship Dwór Oliwski City Hotel & SPA had the German national team as their guests.

Guests of the Oliwski can also enjoy international and traditional Polish dishes at the restaurant, where a varied breakfast buffet is served and special lunches on Sunday.

There is also a wine cellar with a wide choice of wines on offer and a tea house where guests can relax.

Dwór Oliwski City Hotel & SPA is located at the borders of Tricity Landscape Park, just 10 km from the Gdańsk Old Town and only 11 km from Gdańsk Airport.
