Description

Hotel Bartan Gdansk Seaside is conveniently located amongst tranquil natural surroundings on the picturesque Sobieszewo Island, a 15-minute walk from the beach.

Each room offers free internet and a private bathroom. All rooms at the Bartan have bright, classic interiors with carpeted floors.

There is a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, as well as a work space with a desk and a telephone.

The hotel's restaurant, Bursztynowa, specialises in Polish and international cuisine.

In summer, it additionally offers seats located on the adjoining terrace, with a view of the garden.

There is also a pub, Bohnsack. The hotel provides a range of facilities including a small health centre and a winter garden.

There is a 24-hour reception service, as well as an airport shuttle service available.

Shuttle to the Old Town and back can be provided at a surcharge. The Bartan Gdansk Seaside is only a 15-minute drive from the city centre.
