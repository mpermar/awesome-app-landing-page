Description

Dom Aktora is located in a quiet area of the Old Town of Gdansk. It offers well-equipped apartments with kitchens, seating areas.

cable TV and free internet access In the morning you can have tasty breakfast, which will give you energy to explore Gdansk.

Dom Aktora is situated just 300 metres from St. Mary’s Basilica and 500 metres from the famous Neptune Fountain.

All of museums, galleries and famous sites of Gdansk’s Old Town are within walking distance.

Dom Aktora’s friendly staff will arrange bicycle and car rental, as well as ticket reservations for you.

The property regularly hosts exhibitions of works of art by local artists from Gdansk.
