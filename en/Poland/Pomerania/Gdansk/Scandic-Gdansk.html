Description

Scandic Gdansk is located close to the Old Town of Gdansk, just 800 metres from the famous Crane over Motlawa River.

It offers soundproofed rooms with bathrooms, TVs and free WiFi. The gym is open 24 hours.

Property also features a sauna. In front of the hotel there is an airport bus stop, from which you will get to the airport in just 20 minutes.

The Central Railway Station is situated opposite Scandic Hotel. Ergo Arena is 8 km away.

Senso Restaurant & Bar is a great place to meet with friends and taste snacks, European dishes and a wide selection of wines, teas and coffees.

Here buffet breakfast is also served. You can enjoy alcoholic and soft drinks, as well as desserts and light meals in Scandic’s lobby bar.
