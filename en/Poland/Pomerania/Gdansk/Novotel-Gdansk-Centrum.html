Description

The modern Novotel Gdańsk Centrum is a hotel located in the centre of the seaport city of Gdansk, just 200 metres from the Old Town.

It offers spacious, air-conditioned rooms and free WiFi throughout. Each of the Novotel’s modern rooms has cable TV, a minibar and a work desk.

A private bathroom is also common to all rooms. The staff is at your disposal 24/7.

The reception is happy to help guests organise their stay in Gdańsk and provide information on the many activities the city offers.

Novotel Gdańsk Centrum has a fitness centre. Guests can also rent out a bike or relax in the spacious garden or on the terrace.

The hotel features a bar and an open-air restaurant, the NOVO SQUARE Lounge Bar, where you can taste international dishes.

Breakfasts are served in the morning. The Main Train Station in Gdansk is 1. 5 km from the hotel and parking is also provided.
