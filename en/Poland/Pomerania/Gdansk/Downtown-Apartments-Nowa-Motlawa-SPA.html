Description

Downtown Apartments Nowa Motława SPA offers accommodation in in centre of Gdańsk, 500 metres from Green Gate and 500 metres from Long Market.

The unit is 500 metres from Marina Gdańsk. Free private garage parking is available on site.

Other facilities at Downtown Apartments Nowa Motława SPA include a spa centre. TV with flat-screen is included.

Kitchen with a fridge and a dishwasher is included. Bathroom is featured. Extras include free toiletries and towels.

Guests can use swimming pool, gym, sauna, spa and jacuzzi which are all located in this same building.

Long Bridge is 500 metres from Downtown Apartments Nowa Motława SPA, while Artus Court is 600 metres away.
