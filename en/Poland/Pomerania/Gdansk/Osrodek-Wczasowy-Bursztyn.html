Description

Located in Gdańsk’s Sobieszewo district, Ośrodek Wczasowy Bursztyn offers accommodation with free WiFi and an LCD TV located in 3 separate building.

The main building houses a reception, a restaurant, 3 conference rooms and a Spa.

Free private parking is available on site. The bright rooms of the Bursztyn feature a classic interior décor and all includes wooden furniture in warm colours.

All come with a desk, seating area and a bathroom. Bursztyn features saunas and a hot tub.

Guests are welcome to enjoy billiards, snooker, table tennis and a football table.

Bikes and Nordic walking equipment are also available. There is a children's playground on site.

The Sosnowa restaurant and cafe offers traditional Polish cuisine prepared with seasonal ingredients.

Guests can enjoy a view of the pine forest. The Baltic Sea is 10 minutes walking from the property.
