Description

Located in Rytro, Hotel Perła Południa offers rooms with an LCD TV with satellite channels.

Free private parking is offered and free Wi-Fi is available in the entire property.

It offers spa and wellness facilities. The rooms of the Perła Południa are done in warm colours and feature a classic interior décor.

Each one includes a balcony that offers a view of the surrounding mountains. Guests of the Perła Południa may relax in the sauna, the salt cave or work out in the fitness centre.

They may also enjoy a game of tennis or use the sports grounds, which include a football pitch.

The spa offers massages a hot tub and cosmetic treatments. The hotel’s restaurant, Pod Jasionową Polaną serves Polish and European cuisine.

The Nad Potokiem serves regional meals, and there also is a lobby bar. The nearest ski lift is 200 metres from the Perła, and there is a total of 3 ski pistes within 1.
