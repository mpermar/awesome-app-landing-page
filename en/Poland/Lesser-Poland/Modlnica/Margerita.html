Description

Margerita is located in the surroundings of the Polish Jura Chain, just a 10-minute drive from Kraków city centre.

It features rooms with free Wi-Fi, TV and a private bathroom. All rooms at the Margerita are fitted with either bath or shower.

Each has a radio and a telephone. Breakfast is served every morning from 7:00 to 10:00 in Margarita, a hotel restaurant, which specialises in Polish and European dishes.

Room service is available. Front desk staff is available 24 hours a day. There is a grocery store in the hotel.

Laundry and ironing services are available. Margerita is located 9 km from the Balice International Airport.
