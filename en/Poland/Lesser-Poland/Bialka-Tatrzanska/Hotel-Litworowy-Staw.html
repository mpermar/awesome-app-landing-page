Description

Set in Białka Tatrzanska, 1. 6 km from Bania Thermal Baths, Hotel Litworowy Staw offers accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a bar and a shared lounge.

Featuring a garden, the 3-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom.

The accommodation provides entertainment staff and room service. At the hotel, all rooms are fitted with a wardrobe.

At Hotel Litworowy Staw all rooms come with a desk and a flat-screen TV. Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

Hotel Litworowy Staw offers a terrace. Guests at the hotel will be able to enjoy activities in and around Białka Tatrzanska, like skiing and cycling.

Popular points of interest near Hotel Litworowy Staw include Zebra Ski Lift, Kotelnica III Ski Lift and Pasieka Express Ski Lift.

The nearest airport is John Paul II International Kraków–Balice, 105 km from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
