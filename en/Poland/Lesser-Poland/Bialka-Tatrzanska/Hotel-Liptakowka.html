Description

3-star Hotel Liptakówka is located in Białka Tatrzańska, in the picturesque Tatra Mountains and it offers accommodation with free Wi-Fi, two types of saunas and a hot tub.

Biały Dunajec Train Station is 7. 5 km away. The non-smoking rooms at Hotel Liptakówka are elegantly decorated, spacious and carpeted.

Each comes with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a desk, and a bathroom with a hairdryer.

Most rooms come with balconies. Buffet breakfast is available every morning and the restaurant at Liptakówka specialises in traditional Polish and international cuisine.

Barbecue facilities are provided outside. Children will enjoy spending time on the playground and billiards table is also available.

The hotel offers laundry and ironing services. At extra charge, massage treatments are available.

The nearest ski lift, Bania, is 800 metres away and Bukowina Tatrzańska is 4 km away from Hotel Liptakówka.
