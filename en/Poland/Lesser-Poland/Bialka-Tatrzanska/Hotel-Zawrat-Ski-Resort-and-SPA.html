Description

Hotel Zawrat Ski Resort & SPA is a 3-star property located just next to the Kaniówka Ski Slope.

It features modern rooms and studios with free internet and an LCD TV. Free parking is provided on site.

All rooms are fitted with satellite TV and a private bathroom. A breakfast is served every morning in a restaurant specialising in regional dishes.

A'la carte dishes are available in the winter season. Guests can relax in front of the fireplace or at the drink-bar.

Outdoor BBQ facilities are available. Front desk staff at Hotel Zawrat Ski Resort & SPA can arrange ski equipment hire.

Guests have free access to a games room with billiards and table tennis. There also is a spa and wellness zone with dry and steam saunas, a swimming pool and a hot tub, as well as massage parlours.

Zawrat is located 3 km from the Białka Tatrzańska centre. Kotelnica Ski Slope is just 3.
