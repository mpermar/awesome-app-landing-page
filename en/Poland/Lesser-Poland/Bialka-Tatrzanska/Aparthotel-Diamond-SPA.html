Description

Aparthotel Diamond SPA offers accommodation in Białka Tatrzanska. Kotelnica Bialczanska Ski Resort is 900 metres from the property.

Free private parking is available on site and guests can enjoy free WiFi in the reception area.

All units have a seating area and free cable Internet. Some units feature a dining area and/or balcony.

There is also a kitchenette, equipped with a microwave, fridge and stovetop. There is a private bathroom with a bath or shower in each unit.

Bed linen is featured. Bania Thermal Baths is 1 km from Aparthotel Diamond SPA. The nearest airport is Krakow - Balice Airport, 78 km from the property.
