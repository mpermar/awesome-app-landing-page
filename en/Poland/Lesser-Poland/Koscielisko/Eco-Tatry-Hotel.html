Description

Situated in Kościelisko, only 300 metres from the Tatra National Park and Chotarz Ski Lift, Eco Tatry Hotel offers rooms with free WiFi, a TV set and a minibar.

Free private parking is available on site. The rooms of Eco Tatry Hotel feature bright interiors painted in a warm pastel colour.

Each one is heated, has a seating area, and includes a private bathroom with a hairdryer and a shower.

The hotel offers ski storage facilities, and has a restaurant that serves Polish and regional dishes.

In addition, guests have access to the garden. For an additional charge, guests can relax in the Spa area.

Guests have free access to billiards and foosball. Biały Potok ski lift is within 1 km, and the centre of Zakopane is a 8 minute car ride away.
