Description

Osada Kościelisko offers apartments done in the regional, Tatra Mountain style, which come with free private parking and impressive mountain views.

Zakopane’s town centre is 6 km away. The apartments are decorated in wood. There is a fireplace, a balcony, as well as a seating area and a flat-screen TV.

The elegant bathroom is fitted with a shower and free Wi-Fi is available in the whole apartment.

A fully equipped kitchenette offers a stove, a refrigerator and a dishwasher, as well as all the necessary kitchenware.

Szymoszkowa ski lift is 3.5 km away and Chochołowska Valley is 3 km from Osada Kościelisko.
