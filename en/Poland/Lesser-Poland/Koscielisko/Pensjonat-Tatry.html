Description

Located in the centre of Kościelisko, a village in the Tatry Mountain Range, Pensjonat Tatry offers rooms with TV sets and private bathrooms.

Free Wi-Fi is available in the entire hotel. Each room at Tatry is warmly decorated and features wooden furniture, as well as carpeted floors.

There also is a room prepared for disabled guests. Pensjonat Tatry’s guests can enjoy a small spa and a table tennis.

Billiards and darts are also available at a surcharge. There is a playground for children.

There are 4 shops and 1 restaurants around the hotel. Guests can enjoy a breakfast buffet.

They also have access to a communal kitchenette. Hot water, free tea and coffee from the coffee machine at surcharge are available 24 hours a day.

The guesthouse is located within easy reach of many hiking trails and ski lifts. The more popular hiking destinations include Kościelisko Valley and Gubałówka.
