Description

Willa Baciarka is located in Kościelisko, 4. 5 km from the center of Zakopane and 12 km from Chocholowskie Termy.

It features spacious rooms with flat-screen TV, free Wi-Fi and tea/coffee making facilities.

All units at Baciarka have a refrigerator and a private bathroom with a shower. Most rooms have balconies with a panoramic view of the Tatra Mountains.

Buffet breakfast is served daily. Guest can use a common kitchenette with a microwave at the property.

Room service and packed lunches are available. Willa Baciarka offers free indoor, heated swimming pool.

Sauna and a spa bath are available at a surcharge. Front desk staff is available 24/7 and can arrange extra activities, such as ski school or evenings with regional culture.

Guests can relax in a games room with Playstation or playing table tennis. Willa Baciarka is only 2 kilometres from Szymoszkowa ski lift.
