Description

At Baza Grapa Pokoje Gościnne, all rooms include a patio. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom, while some units at Baza Grapa Pokoje Gościnne also offer a balcony. All rooms will provide guests with an oven.

Continental and buffet breakfast options are available every morning at Baza Grapa Pokoje Gościnne.

Baza Grapa Pokoje Gościnne has a playground. A variety of activities can be enjoyed in the surrounding areas, including skiing, cycling and hiking.

Zakopane is 22 miles from Baza Grapa Pokoje Gościnne, while Białka Tatrzanska is 26 miles from the property. John Paul II International Kraków–Balice Airport is 40 miles away.
