Description

Offering a barbecue and a private beach area, Osada Turystyczna Agata is set in Szczawnica in the Lesser Poland Region.

The resort has a children's playground and views of the mountains, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Free private parking is available on site. Rooms have a flat-screen TV and kettle.

Certain units include a seating area for your convenience. Each room includes a private bathroom fitted with shower.

Osada Turystyczna Agata features free WiFi throughout the property. There is a shared kitchen at the property.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including skiing and horse riding.
