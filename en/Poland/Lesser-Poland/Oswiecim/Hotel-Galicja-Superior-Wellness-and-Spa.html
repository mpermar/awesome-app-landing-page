Description

Hotel Galicja Superior Wellness & Spa is located in Oświęcim and surrounded by greenery.

It offers rooms with an LCD TV and free access to hot tub and saunas. Auschwitz-Birkenau Memorial and Museum is about 4 km away.

The rooms at the Galicja Superior come with a laptop safe and a work desk. All feature a minibar and an electric kettle.

Some rooms have a balcony. Wi-Fi is free at the hotel. Guests can relax in the Garden Wellness and Spa.

It includes dry, steam, and herbal saunas. It offers many face and body treatments as well as beauty services and massages.

Hotel Galicja Superior Wellness & Spa houses the Piwniczka u Szwejka pub, serving Italian and Czech dishes.

The Stara Poczta restaurant offers Polish specialities, and Trattoria Viva – Italian dishes.

Hotel Galicja Superior Wellness & Spa is located 38 km from Krakow Airport and 49 km from the Katowice Airport.

Guests are offered free parking places on site. It is situated only a 5-minute drive from Museum Auschwitz-Birkenau and within a walking distance to the center of the city.
