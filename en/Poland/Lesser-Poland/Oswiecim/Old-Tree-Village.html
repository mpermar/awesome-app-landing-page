Description

All units come with a living room, a seating area with a sofa bed, a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchenette and a private bathroom. A dishwasher and electric tea pot are also featured.

Guests at the resort village can enjoy a continental or a buffet breakfast. There's an on-site restaurant, which serves European and Polish cuisine and also offers vegan and vegetarian options.

Old Tree Village has a playground.

The accommodation has a sun terrace.

Auschwitz-Birkenau is 2.4 miles from Old Tree Village. The nearest airport is John Paul II International Kraków–Balice Airport, 25 miles from the property.
