Description

Located within a recreational and sport complex in Tarnów, Hotel Kantoria offers rooms with free Wi-Fi and a flat-screen LCD TV.

Private parking is available. The Kantoria’s rooms feature an elegant interior design in bright, warm colours.

All have a work desk and a private bathroom with amenities. The Kantoria restaurant serves traditional Polish meals.

In the mornings breakfast is available. The complex on which the hotel is located has a municipal sauna, solarium as well as a tennis court and football field.

Bem’s Mausoleum is nearby. Tarnów’s centre is within 1 km, and the railway station is 1.
