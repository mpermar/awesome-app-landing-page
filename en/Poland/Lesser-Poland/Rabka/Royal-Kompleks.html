Description

Royal Kompleks offers accommodation in bright rooms with free Wi-Fi and private bathroom facilities.

Guests can enjoy a tennis court and there is a children’s playground. Each classically appointed room at the Royal includes an LCD TV and a work desk.

There are also night stands and a wardrobe. The property has a restaurant serving Polish cuisine.

There is a bar, where a drink or two can be enjoyed. Royal Kompleks includes free private parking and is located 1.
