Description

Set in Zubrzyca Górna, 39 km from Zakopane, Pensjonat Za Borem boasts a barbecue and views of the mountains.

Free WiFi is featured throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Rooms come with a flat-screen TV. Certain units include a seating area for your convenience.

You will find a kettle in the room. Every room is equipped with a private bathroom fitted with a shower.

You will find a shared lounge at the property. There is a rope park and a seasonal outdoor swimming pool in the area.

You can engage in various activities, such as cycling and hiking. Białka Tatrzanska is 41 km from Pensjonat Za Borem, while Bukowina Tatrzańska is 43 km away.
