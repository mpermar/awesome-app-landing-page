Description

Hotel Zalesie is located a 15-minute walk from Bochnia Salt Mine and the town’s Market Square.

Free Wi-Fi access is available all throughout the property. Classic-style and warmly decorated rooms will provide you with TV, a seating area and a balcony.

There is also an electric kettle. Featuring a shower, private bathrooms come with complimentary towels.

At Hotel Zalesie you will find a garden and Zalesie Restaurant where buffet breakfast is served.

Other facilities offered include luggage storage and a children's playground. The property offers free private parking.

The property is 2. 5 km from Bochnia Railway Station. The nearest grocery is just 20 metres away.
