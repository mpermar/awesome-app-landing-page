Description

Willa na Olczańskim Wierchu offers rooms in the green, quiet area of Bukowina Tatrzańska, located 3 km from the upper part of Kotelnica Ski Lift.

Most rooms have mountain views, and all of them have free Wi-Fi. All the rooms in the Willa have a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

There also is an electric kettle and a bathroom with a shower. Willa na Olczańskim Wierchu features a games room that has a large flat-screen TV, a billiards table, shufflepuck and table football.

Breakfast is served in the dining room. At extra charge guests can use a sauna. Bukowina has a number of ski lifts, with RusińSki ski complex 2.

5 km away. The centre of Bukowina Tatrzańska, where thermal baths can be found, is 2 km from Willa na Olczańskim Wierchu.
