Description

Willa Grywałd is located in Grywałd in a quiet area with a mountain view of the Tatras, Trzy Korony and Gorce.

Free Wi-Fi access is available in all areas. With walls of light wood logs, all bright rooms here offer a small table with chairs.

Every private bathroom is fitted with a shower. You can enjoy a mountain view from all the rooms and some feature a balcony.

Bed linen is provided free of charge and towels are available at an extra cost. At Willa Grywałd you can enjoy breakfast buffet.

There is also an electric kettle and a refrigerator for guests' use. Other facilities offered include a garden, basketball court, a shared lounge with a dining area, a flat-screen TV and a fireplace, as well as a free ski storage.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including skiing, cycling and hiking.

The property offers free private parking. Willa Grywałd is 13 km from Lake Czorsztyn where you can go on a cruise.

Czorsztyn Castle is a 23-minute drive away and Niedzica Castle can be reached in 35 minutes by car.
