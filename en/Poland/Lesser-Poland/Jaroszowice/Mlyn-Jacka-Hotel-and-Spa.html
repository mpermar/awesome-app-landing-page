Description

Located in the Little Beskids mountain range, in a renovated mill, Młyn Jacka Hotel & Spa offers 4-star accommodation with free Wi-Fi and monitored parking.

Wadowice Train Station is 3. 2 km away. The air-conditioned rooms at Młyn Jacka are decorated in an elegant style and feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a desk.

The bathroom comes equipped with a hairdryer and free toiletries. A bathrobe and slippers are also offered.

A buffet breakfast is available at the restaurant, but it can also be served in the room.

Restauracja Spichlerz Smaków specialises in gourmet European cuisine. A hotel spa features a number of facilities and services, including saunas, a spa bath, a swimming pool and massage cabinets.

Młyn Jacka Hotel & Spa is located 3. 7 km from the Pope John Paul II’s family house in the centre of Wadowice.
