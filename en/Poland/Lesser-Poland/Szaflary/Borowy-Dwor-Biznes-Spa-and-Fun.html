Description

Borowy Dwór- Biznes, Spa & Fun is set in Szaflary in the Lesser Poland Region. It features Spa zone for adults, with three saunas and a spa bath, a games room and a modern bowling alley on site.

The rooms have a TV and a balcony. Every room includes a private bathroom with a shower, hairdryer and free toiletries.

Free 24-hour tea and coffee, free WiFi in all areas and free private parking with video surveillance are provided.

The reception is open 24 hours a day. The nearest airport is Krakow - Balice Airport, 76 km from Borowy Dwór- Biznes, Spa & Fun.
