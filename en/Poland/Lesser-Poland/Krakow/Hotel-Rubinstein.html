Description

Rubinstein Residence is housed in a 15th-century building, located in the heart of the Kazimierz District in Kraków, just off the Old Synagogue.

Its rooms and apartments offer air conditioning. All rooms and apartments at the Rubinstein come with a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a minibar and a safe.

Many feature impressive wooden ceilings and beams from the 17th and 18th century.

A varied buffet breakfast can be enjoyed every morning. It is served at the Rubinstein Restaurant, situated on a glazed patio and specialised in international dishes.

After a long day, guest can relax in the Rubinstein’s sauna. Front desk staff is available 24 hours a day and offers tourist information on what to see and do in Kraków.

Rubinstein Residence is located within just a 10-minute walk of the beautiful Wawel Royal Castle.
