Description

Hotel Kolna is situated 3 km from the Benedictine Abbey in Kraków’s Tyniec, by the Vistula River and 7 km from the Balice Airport.

It offers free Wi-Fi in the entire building and rooms with air conditioning. The rooms at the Kolna have a private bathroom with a shower cabin and a hairdryer.

All feature a TV. The Hotel Kolna’s restaurant serves traditional specialities, including vegetarian options.

Guests can also visit the onsite café. During their stay at the Kolna hotel, guests can use the hotel swimming pool, sauna, gym and solarium.

Table tennis and billiards are also available, as well as kayaks and pedalos. Guests receive free 60-minute entrance to the pool or a ticket to the gym.

Kraków's city centre is a 20-minute drive away and A4 motorway exit is just 1 km away.
