Description

Vienna House Andel's Cracow is a contemporary 4-star hotel, located just 9 minutes’ walk from the Main Market Square in Kraków.

Its designer rooms come with air conditioning and satellite TV. A varied buffet breakfast is served every morning at the Mavericks Restaurant, which serves creative, California-style dishes with Asian, Mexican and European influences.

In the evening, guests can relax over a drink in the bar Smok. The spacious rooms at Vienna House Andel's Cracow come with a flat-screen TV and a laptop safe box.

Some are located on the top floors and feature panoramic views of the beautiful Old Town.

Front desk staff is available 24 hours a day and can arrange airport shuttle service and tips on what to do and see in Kraków.

Guests can relax in the sauna or work out in the fitness centre. Vienna House Andel's Cracow is situated in the very heart of the city, just off the Main Railway Station and Galeria Krakowska, Kraków’s main shopping centre.
