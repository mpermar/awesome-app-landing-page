Description

Located in a quiet mountain village of Czerwienne, Hotel Bachledówka offers comfortable air-conditioned accommodation with free Wi-Fi.

This 3-star hotel provides a discount for ski passes at the Czerwienne Budz Ski Station 2 km away.

The rooms are elegantly decorated and bright. Each comes with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries.

Guests of Hotel Bachledówka can relax in one of the saunas or in the club room. For children, a playroom was prepared.

The hotel can also organise snowmobile rides. The hotel restaurant, decorated to incorporate traditional local architectural elements, specialises in regional and Italian cuisine.

The hotel is 18 km from the popular winter resort town of Zakopane, and 15 km from the Witów Ski Centre.
