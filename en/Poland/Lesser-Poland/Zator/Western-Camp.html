Description

At the resort, all rooms have a closet. All rooms come with air conditioning, and selected rooms also feature a balcony.

The daily breakfast offers continental and buffet options.

Western Camp has a playground. Fishing is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.

Energylandia Amusement Park is 0.9 miles from Western Camp. The nearest airport is John Paul II International Kraków–Balice Airport, 17 miles from the property.
