Description

The family-run Villa Słonecznego Wzgórza is situated in Zakopane, only 100 meters from Bachledzki Wierch Mountain.

It offers regionally-styled rooms, free Wi-Fi and an electric kettle. Some rooms at the Villa come with wooden floors and a seating area with a sofa.

Each has satellite TV, a fridge, a kettle and a private bathroom with a shower. Some rooms feature a balcony or terrace exit.

Apartments come with floor heating. Barbecue facilities are also available and can be used in the on-site garden.

Villa Słonecznego Wzgórza is located within a 5-minute drive from famous Krupówki Street.

The popular Aquapark is a 20-minute walk away. Free parking is provided for the guests.
