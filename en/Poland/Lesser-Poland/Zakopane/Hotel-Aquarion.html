Description

Featuring free WiFi and a bar, Hotel Aquarion offers accommodation in Zakopane, a few steps from Zakopane Aqua Park and 1.

7 km from Pardalowka Ski Lift. Offering on-site dining, the property is located within 1.

8 km of Wielka Krokiew Ski Jump. The accommodation offers entertainment staff and a 24-hour front desk.

At the hotel, every room has a desk and a flat-screen TV. All rooms have a private bathroom with a shower and some rooms also offer a balcony.

All units have a wardrobe. A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property.

The property has wellness area with sauna and Turkish bath. Guests have direct access to Zakopane Aquapark.

There are a hair dryer, bathrobes and slippers available. Additional room facilities include tea and coffee making facilities and ironing board with an iron.

The hotel has a small fitness centre and a spa and wellness centre. For children there is a playroom with animations, the hotel is pet-friendly.

The property offers outdoor parking and space in the underground garage. Guests can take part in various activities in the surroundings, including skiing and hiking.

Gubalowka Mountain is 2. 5 km from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Krakow - Balice Airport, 87 km from Hotel Aquarion.
