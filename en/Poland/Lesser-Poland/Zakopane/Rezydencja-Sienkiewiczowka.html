Description

Rezydencja Sienkiewiczówka is situated in a peaceful part of central Zakopane, 5 minutes’ walk from the Krupówki Street.

Its rooms come with free Wi-Fi. The rooms at Rezydencja Sienkiewiczówka feature satellite TV.

All have a modern bathroom with a shower cabin, a hairdryer, towels and a set of toiletries.

Some rooms have own balcony. Guests of Rezydencja Sienkiewiczówka have free access to a small fitness room.

Sauna is available at extra charge. The property also provides them with a playground and a playroom for children.

A refrigerator, a microwave and a baby set including a baby cot, a baby bath, a baby changing unit, a bottle warmer and a humidifier, can be rented at surcharge at the reception.

An electric kettle can be rented free of charge. It is also possible to rent bikes for adults and kids.

Business facilities such as conference rooms, multimedia projector with a screen, a flipchart and audio equipment are also available.

The Sienkiewiczówka is housed in a 19th-century building, which used to be frequently visited by a famous Polish writer, Henryk Sienkiewicz.

The property is located about 1. 5 km from the Zakopane Railway Station. Guests are just 500 metres from the local ski jumps.
