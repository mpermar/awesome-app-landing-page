Description

Rezydencja Sąsiadów Zakopanego is situated in Zakopane, near Szymoszkowa and Butorowy ski lifts.

It offers rooms finished with wood and appointed in mountain style, with free Wi-Fi and en-suite bathrooms.

The villa is located in a quiet area of Zakopane, which offers perfect conditions for relaxation and beautiful views of Giewont Mountain.

The centre of Zakopane is about 25 minutes’ walk away. You are always welcome to ask the friendly owner of Rezydencja Sąsiadów Zakopanego for advice on what to do and see in Zakopane.

After a long day in the mountains, you can relax in a free sauna. There is also a dining room with a fireplace.
