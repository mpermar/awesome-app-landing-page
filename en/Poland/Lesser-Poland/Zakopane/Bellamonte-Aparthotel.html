Description

Bellamonte Aparthotel offers spacious apartments with kitchenettes, seating areas, satellite TV and free internet.

The complex is located in 3 buildings, just 300 metres from Krupwki, in the very centre of Zakopane.

The excellent location of the hotel will make exploring the city and the surrounding mountains easy.

You will be just 1. 5 kilometres from the Zakopane Railway Station and 300 metres from Gubawka Mountain.

After a long day, you can relax in Bellamonte Aparthotels sauna, gym and hot tub, to which you have free access.

Various kinds of massage are also on offer. There is an attested kid's corner and an outdoor playground with a sandbox and a slide.

The friendly staff of the Bellamonte is at your disposal 24/7. The reception offers ironing facilities and hairdryers for rent.

You can also benefit from free parking on site. During winter holiday, there is a free ski bus going from the property to the Polana Szymoszkowa Ski Station, which is located over 1 km away.
