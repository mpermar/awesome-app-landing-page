Description

Situated in Zakopane, in a building dating from 2017, 700 metres from Zakopane Aqua Park and 750 metres from Krupówki, Kaplowa Chata offers guestrooms with free WiFi.

This property is located a short distance from attractions such as Wielka Krokiew Ski Jump and Pardalowka Ski Lift.

The property is non-smoking throughout and is set 2. 4 km from Kasprowy Wierch Ski Lift.

All guest rooms in the guest house are fitted with a flat-screen TV. The rooms include a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries, while selected rooms also boast a terrace.

Guest rooms feature a wardrobe. Guests at Kaplowa Chata will be able to enjoy activities in and around Zakopane, like skiing.

Szymoszkowa Ski Lift is 2. 8 km from the accommodation. Poprad Airport is 29 km from Kaplowa Chata and Krakow - Balice Airport is 88 km away.
