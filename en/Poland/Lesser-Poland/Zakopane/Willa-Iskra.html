Description

Willa Iskra is located in the holiday resort of Zakopane, 800 meters from the popular Krupówki Street and it offers accommodation with free Wi-Fi and parking.

The non-smoking rooms at Willa Iskra are decorated in warm colours and come with a bathroom and a TV.

Each room is also equipped with an iron, a refrigerator and an electric kettle. Breakfast buffet is served in the dining room every morning.

The nearest restaurant and a grocery shop are 200 meters away. Willa Iskra is situated in a garden where guests can relax and use tables and chairs provided and in the common room billiard is available.

Ski lifts Nosal and Szymoszkowa are 2 km away. The impressive ski jump where sports events take place is a 10-minute walk away from the guest house.
