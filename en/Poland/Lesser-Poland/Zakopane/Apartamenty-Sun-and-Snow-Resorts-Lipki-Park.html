Description

All rooms in the resort are equipped with a electric tea pot. Sun & Snow Resorts Lipki Park has some units that feature mountain views, and all rooms have a private bathroom with a shower. All guest rooms include a seating area.

A continental or buffet breakfast is available each morning at the property.

Skiing is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.

Tatra National Park is 1.9 miles from Apartamenty Sun & Snow Resorts Lipki Park. The nearest airport is John Paul II International Kraków–Balice Airport, 55 miles from the property.
