Description

This hotel is located on the Czarny Potok stream and is 5 minutes’ walk from the main street, Krupówki.

It features a spa & wellness centre with a swimming pool. Czarny Potok has a homely atmosphere and displays elements of the local architectural style.

The building was extended and thoroughly modernised in 2000. The hotel is surrounded by a garden with a fireplace where you can have a cup of coffee or a drink.

Guests can benefit from a sauna and a hot tub as well as a selection of facial and body treatments.
