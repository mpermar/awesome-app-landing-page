Description

Hotel św. Norberta is housed in the former monastery complex dating from the 12th century and located in Nowe Brzesko at the Vistula River meander.

It offers spacious rooms with free Wi-Fi and satellite TV. All air-conditioned rooms at the św.

Norberta are bright and classically furnished. Each comes with a private bathroom with a shower.

Front desk staff is available 24 hours a day and can arrange shuttle transfer, store guests’ valuables in the safe or laundry services.

Guests can relax in the garden. There is also a historic chapel. A buffet breakfast is served in the hotel’s air-conditioned restaurant, which offers Polish and European dishes.

Barbecue facilities are available. Hotel św. Norberta is located 2 km from Nowe Brzesko Railway Station.
