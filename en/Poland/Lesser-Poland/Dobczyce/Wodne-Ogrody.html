Description

At the farm stay, every room comes with a balcony. Wodne Ogrody has some rooms with river views, and rooms include a coffee machine. All guest rooms feature a seating area.

The accommodation offers a buffet or Full English/Irish breakfast.

Cycling and hiking are among the activities that guests of Wodne Ogrody can find nearby.

Krakow is 13 miles from the farm stay. The nearest airport is John Paul II International Kraków–Balice Airport, 17 miles from the property.
