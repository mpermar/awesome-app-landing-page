Description

Willa Pod Kozińcem is located 1 km from Czarna Góra Ski-Lift and 5 km from Bialka Tatrzanska Therms.

The property offers rooms with free WiFi and a discount to the Therms. There is also a bathroom with a shower.

Some rooms come with a kitchenette and a balcony. Willa Pod Kozińcem features a common kitchenette with a fridge.

There is a playground for children, featuring a trampoline, a playground and a barbecue area in the garden.
