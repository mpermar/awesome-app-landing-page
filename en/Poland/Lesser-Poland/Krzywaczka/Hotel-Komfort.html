Description

Located within just a 20-minute drive of Kraków, Hotel Komfort features modern spacious rooms with free Wi-Fi, LCD TV with satellite channels.

Free on-site parking is provided. All rooms at the hotel come with a private bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer.

A seating area and a work desk are provided. Every morning guests can enjoy a varied buffet breakfast served at the hotel restaurant which specialises in international dishes.

Room service is provided. Front desk staff is available 24 hours a day and can arrange shuttle services.

Guests can also relax at the hotel terrace. Hotel Komfort is located 30 km from the Balice International Airport.
