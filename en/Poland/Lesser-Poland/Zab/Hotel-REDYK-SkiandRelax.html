Description

Hotel REDYK Ski&Relax is located in Ząb, north of Zakopane and offers stunning views of the Tatra Mountains.

Guests can use spa facilities which include a sauna and a hot tub. Its rooms have free Wi-Fi and a private bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer.

All rooms at the Hotel REDYK Ski&Relax are spacious, bright and are decorated with wooden furniture.

Almost every room comes with a balcony and a working space. Ironing facilities are available at the reception.

A buffet breakfast can be enjoyed every morning at the restaurant. In the evening, guests can relax with a drink at the bar.

Guests can relax at the on-site wellness centre with a hot tub, sauna and massage treatments.

Front desk staff is available 24 hours a day and can help with organising sightseeing trips.

There is a children's playground and bicycle rental on site. Guests can also organise a bonfire.
