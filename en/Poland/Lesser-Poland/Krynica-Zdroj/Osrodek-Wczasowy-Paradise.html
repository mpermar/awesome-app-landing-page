Description

Featuring a hot tub and a sauna, the Resort Paradise is located within a 15-minute walk of the Krynica-Zdrój centre.

It offers bright rooms with free Wi-Fi and satellite TV. Free parking is provided.

All rooms at the Paradise are classically decorated with wooden furniture and carpeted floors.

Each comes with a modern bathroom with a shower. Breakfast is served every morning in the elegant restaurant, which offers traditional Polish and international dishes.

You can try a wide selection of drinks at the on-site bar. The Paradise Resort is located 1 km from the Henryk Ski Lift and 4 km from the Jaworzyna Ski Lift.
