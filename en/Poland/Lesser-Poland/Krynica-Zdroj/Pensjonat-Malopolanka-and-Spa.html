Description

With its elegant interiors, artworks and antiques, Pensjonat Małopolanka takes you back to a bygone era in the heart of Krynica-Zdrój, one of Poland's most famous health resorts.

Located on the river bank, overlooking the historical promenade, Małopolanka has been welcoming guests since 1924.

Its recently renovated facilities include a cafe bar and a restaurant where breakfast, lunch and dinner are served.

The spa offers sauna, massage and steam bath. The property regularly organises jazz and blues live concerts.

Pension Małopolanka has a range of rooms, studios and apartments to suit your personal needs.

All accommodation types benefit from modern, private bathrooms, free Wi-Fi, television and radio.

Apart from being the biggest spa town in Poland, Krynica-Zdrój has also become a popular ski and winter sport resort.
