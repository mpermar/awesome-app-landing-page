Description

The rooms at 4 Pory Roku come with satellite TV and a work desk. Most have a balcony with mountain views.

The on-site spa offers a range of massages, as well as body and face treatments. Guests can also relax in the sauna.

4 Pory Roku & Spa houses a bar and a restaurant, serving healthy dishes, prepared of natural ingredients.

The hotel is located on the slope of the Jakubik hill, about one mile from the center of Krynica Zdrój.
