Description

Located in the mountain resort of Krynica Zdrój, Hotel Spa Dr Irena Eris offers spacious rooms with satellite TV, a private balcony and free Wi-Fi.

The Eris Spa is situated in the Popradzki Landscape Park, 2 km away from Krynica Zdrój’s busy centre.

The nearest Jaworzyna Krynicka Ski Lift is only 175 metres away. The rooms of the Hotel Spa Dr Irena Eris Krynica Zdrój feature an elegant interior design.

Each has bright walls and wooden furniture. All have a safe, minibar and electric kettle.

The hotel has a sauna, steam bath and hot tub. It also organises a ski school and has ski storage facilities.

Guests can also get a work out in the fitness centre. It has many spa facilities, including a pool.

Hotel Spa Dr Irena Eris Krynica Zdrój offers a variety of face and body treatments, as well as several types of massage, including classic and oriental ones.

Guests are kindly requested to book them in advance, prior to arrival. The hotel’s restaurant, Szósty Zmysł, serves gourmet meals and boasts a selection of wines.

The distance from Hotel Spa Dr Irena Eris Krynica Zdrój to Słotwiny Ski Station is 2.

4 km on foot or 9 km by car. Henryk Ski Lift is within a 15-minute drive, while a 22-minute drive will take you to Master-SKI Ski Centre.
