Description

Kinga Hotel Wellness is situated in picturesque surroundings, 1 km from the Czorsztyn Lake and on the border of the Pieniny and Gorce Mountains.

It offers free access to spa facilties, such as an indoor swimming pool, a sauna and a hot tub.

The rooms at Kinga are spacious and decorated in light colours. In each of them there is a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a bathroom with a heated floor and a hairdryer.

Free Wi-Fi is available. The restaurant serves Polish cuisine. Tea/coffee making facilities and mineral water are available in the rooms for free.

Barbecuing area is provided in the garden. Children will enjoy spending time at the playground provided at Kinga Hotel Wellness.

A range of body treatments is offered. The Czorsztyn Castle ruins are just 700 metres away.
