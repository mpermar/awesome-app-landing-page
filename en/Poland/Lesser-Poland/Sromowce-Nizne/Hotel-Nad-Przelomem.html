Description

Located at the bank of the Dunajec River, the 3-star Hotel Nad Przełomem features views of the Trzy Korony Mountains and the river.

There is a pool and a wellness centre on site. All rooms at the Nad Przełomem are bright and decorated with warm colours and wooden furniture.

Each comes with a seating area, safe and modern bathroom with a hairdryer and a shower.

They also include free WiFi, and feature a minibar and air conditioning. Breakfast is served every morning in the hotel restaurant, which specialises in regional and Polish dishes.

Guests can enjoy a drink at the bar. The on-site garden comes with barbecue facilities and a small gazebo.

Guests can enjoy horse riding and cycling. In winter, there is an ice rink and skate rental is provided.

Hotel Nad Przełomem is located next to the Polish-Slovak border, 5 km from the Polana Sosny Ski Complex.
