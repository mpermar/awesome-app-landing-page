Description

The 3-star Hotel Wojciech offers accommodation with a private pier in the picturesque surroundings of the Białe Augustowskie Lake.

Use of an indoor swimming pool, a sauna and a gym is free of charge. The rooms at Wojciech are decorated in warm colours.

There is a TV with satellite channels and a minibar in each of them. The bathroom comes with bathrobes, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

The spacious restaurant specialises in Polish and international cuisine. Guests can enjoy a drink at the bar or barbecue outside in the garden.

Spa facilities are available and guests can book various treatments at a surcharge.

Sports equipment, such as Nordic walking sticks and bicycles, as well as kayaks and rowing boats, can be hired on site.

Augustów’s town centre is 10 km away and Augustów Train Station is 4. 6 km from Hotel Wojciech.
