Description

Borowinowy Zdrój Hotel Wellness Spa & Conference is located among picturesque surroundings of Knyszyńska Forest and offers classically decorated rooms with free Wi-Fi.

The hotel has a spa centre. Each room at the Borowinowy includes satellite TV, a private bathroom with bath or shower, as well as a phone.

The hotel’s spa offers a wide choice of professional wellness treatments, a sauna and a hot tub.

There is a Russian Banya, as well as massage services available. There is a restaurant offering regional cuisine, as well as a breakfast buffet.

The Karczma Regionalna decorated with folk elements and pelts, serves traditional dishes of Polasie, Lithuania and Belarus.

There is also a cosy stone fireplace. A drink or two can be enjoyed in the bar. Borowinowy Zdrój Hotel Wellness Spa & Conference has free private parking and a 24-hour front desk service.

The Supraśl River promenade is 500 metres away. The Museum of Icons is within 700 metres.
