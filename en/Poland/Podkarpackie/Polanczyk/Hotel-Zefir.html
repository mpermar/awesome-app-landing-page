Description

Hotel Zefir is situated by Lake Solińskie and offers warmly decorated rooms with free Wi-Fi, as well as spa facilities.

Guests can enjoy unique in the area fish pedicure and relax in a hot tub. Each room at Zefir includes a TV, as well as private bathroom facilities.

Some feature a balcony and a view of the mountains or the lake. The hotel offers a sauna, a Russian banya and an outdoor tub.

Guests can also rent a bicycle and Nordic walking equipment to explore the area. Billiards and karaoke can also be enjoyed.

The Zefir features a restaurant serving traditional and regional cuisine, as well as fish specialities including homemade smoked trout.

During summer, there is a pizza parlour and barbecue spot. The hotel has free private parking and is located 1 km from the centre of Polańczyk.
