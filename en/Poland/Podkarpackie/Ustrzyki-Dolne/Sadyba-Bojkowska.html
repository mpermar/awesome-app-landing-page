Description

Offering free WiFi and a barbecue, Sadyba Bojkowska is quietly situated in Ustrzyki Dolne, 6.

5 km from the centre and 9. 7 km from Laworta Ski Lift. Free private parking is available on site.

The lodges feature a kitchenette with fridge, a ceramic stove and a microwave. The private bathroom comes with a shower and toilet.

The Towels and bed linen are provided. Sadyba Bojkowska also includes a terrace. The property also offers grocery delivery.

The property has a cooperation with the local ski school and the ski rental and bike hire is available.

The area is popular for skiing and cycling. The nearest airport is Rzeszow-Jasionka Airport, 89 km from the property.
