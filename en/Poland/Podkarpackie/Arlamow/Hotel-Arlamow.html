Description

Located on top of a hill 4-star Hotel Arłamów constitutes a recreation, conference and a sport centre.

It offers a golf course and a ski complex, an outdoor thermal pool, a helicopter and a small plane landing pad as well as a three storey spa and wellness centre.

4-star Hotel Complex Arłamów features air-conditioned, modern and luxury rooms, some adapted for disabled people.

It consists of two parts, a modern one - Hotel Arłamów **** and a historic uncategorised residence connected by the underground passage.

Every room in Arłamów has free Wi-Fi access, a safe, a flat-screen TV and an electric kettle.

There is also a bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer. The hotel features a sports swimming pool, a recreational pool and a children’s pool.

The spa centre offers beauty treatment, massages, a choice of saunas and steam baths, an ice room and an outdoor and indoor spa baths.

Children will enjoy a day care and a games room, with qualified staff. There is also a fishing pond with fishing equipment, a ski, bicycle and Nordic walking equipment rental, indoor and outdoor tennis courts as well as a squash court and a sports hall featuring a volleyball, basketball and futsal fields with audience seats.

Hotel Arłamów also has a football field, a fitness room with a gym, a climbing wall and a shooting range.

There are three restaurants offering international and Polish, as well as regional dishes, featuring game meat.

There are two taverns, one located right by the skiing slopes, a café and a bar, as well as a night club with a bowling alley and billiards tables.

It is 18 km from Hotel Arłamów to the oldest brick Orthodox church in Poland. Town of Ustrzyki Dolne is 29 km away.
