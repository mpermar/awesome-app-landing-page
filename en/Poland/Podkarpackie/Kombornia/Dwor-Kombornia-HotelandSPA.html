Description

Dwór Kombornia Hotel&SPA is a 4-star boutique hotel located on the 10-hectare area, 11 km from Krosno.

It offers elegant, traditionally appointed and spacious rooms with air conditioning, minibar and satellite TV.

Each individually designed room at Kombornia features a private bathroom with free toiletries, a hairdryer and a bathrobe.

During their stay, guests can relax in the hotel’s English-style park and on the terrace.

They also have free access to an indoor swimming pool and the hot tub. Other facilities including 2 Finnish saunas, 2 steam baths and spa treatments with oriental massages are available at surcharge.

Dwór Kombornia features free WiFi access throughout the property, as well as parking on site.

Guests are offered room service and 24/7 front desk. There are 2 restaurants serving Polish and international cuisine, a bar and a shop offering toiletries and swimming accessories at the hotel.
