Description

Dwa Bratanki is an intimate inn in a quiet part of Rzeszów, which offers comfortable accommodation, as well as free Wi-Fi.

All the rooms in Dwa Bratanki are modernly furnished, and feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, as well as a bathroom with either a bath or a shower.

It also has a balcony and noise-protected windows. Some of rooms are air-conditioned.

On a sunny day, you can have a drink in the garden or on the terrace while the children are having fun in the playground.

The air-conditioned restaurant serves traditional Polish and Hungarian cuisine, as well as freshly baked pizzas.

There are also 2 conference and function rooms with multimedia equipment, which can accommodate up to 120 people.
