Description

Four Seasons hotel is a 3-star hotel in Rudna Mała, 3 km from Głogów Małopolski with simple, yet elegant rooms with free Wi-Fi.

It is conveniently located near the Rzeszów Jasionka Airport. The restaurant offers Polish and international cuisine, with dished prepared from local, seasonal ingredients.

There also is a complimentary buffet breakfast served every morning. A wide range of drinks and hot beverages can be enjoyed in a bar.

The green garden features a terrace with a seating area as well as and children’s playground.

There also is a free, private parking. The area around Cztery Pory Roku offers many opportunities for horse riding, winter sleigh rides or cycling.

The hotel is situated 1. 3 km from the Bór wildlife reserve and only 10 km from the city of Rzeszów.
