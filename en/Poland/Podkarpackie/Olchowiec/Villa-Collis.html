Description

Guest rooms in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Every room includes air conditioning, and some units at Villa Collis have a balcony. All guest rooms will provide guests with a private bathroom.

A buffet breakfast is available every morning at the property.

The accommodation offers a range of wellness facilities including a hot tub and a sauna. The area is popular for cycling and fishing.

Arłamów is 20 miles from Villa Collis, while Polańczyk is 5 miles from the property.
