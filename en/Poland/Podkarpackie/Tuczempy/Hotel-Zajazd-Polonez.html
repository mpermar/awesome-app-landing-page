Description

Located on the international E40 route and about 6 km from the centre of Jarosław, Hotel Zajazd Polonez offers accommodation with free Wi-Fi and private parking.

The Ukrainian border is about 25 km away. Featuring air-conditioning, a TV and a telephone, every room in Polonez is spacious and carpeted.

A private bathroom comes with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries. Guests can enjoy their meals at the hotel’s restaurant which specialises in traditional Polish and international cuisine.

In the summer season guests can dine or relax with a drink outside on the terraces provided.
