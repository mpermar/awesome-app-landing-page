Description

The Hotel Kormoran Resort & SPA is surrounded by greenery and features a spacious garden with barbecue facilities.

Guests can relax in spa and wellness centre with a swimming pool and a sauna. All rooms, apartments and bungalows come with a refrigerator, satellite TV and free wired internet.

Each has a private bathroom with a shower. Some offer air conditioning. Guests of the Hotel Kormoran Resort & SPA can enjoy fishing, canoeing and different sports activities.

Massage and other body treatments are available, as well as billiards and darts. A buffet breakfast is served every morning in the hotel restaurant, Tawerna.

A selection of drinks can be enjoyed at the bar. The Hotel Kormoran Resort & SPA is located in Sulęcin, 40 km from both Gorzów Wielkopolski and Świecko border crossing.
