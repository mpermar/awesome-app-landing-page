Description

Providing its guests with free access to the indoor pool, Hotel Gracja is located in a quiet area of Gorzów Wielkopolski.

Free Wi-Fi is available in all areas. All bright rooms and apartments are classically furnished and offer a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Featuring a shower cabin, private bathrooms also come with towels. Apartments boast a kitchenette with a refrigerator and kitchenware.

Complementary bottled water is provided. A minibar is available at surcharge. At Hotel Gracja you will find a 24-hour front desk and a restaurant specialising in Polish and European cuisine, where breakfast is served.

At a surcharge, guests can use the on-site fitness centre, sauna and hot tub. It is 1 km from the property to the centre and Gorzów Wielkopolski Railway Station is 1.
