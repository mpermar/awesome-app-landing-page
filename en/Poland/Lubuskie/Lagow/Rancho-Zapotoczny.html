Description

Located in Łagów, Rancho Zapotoczny offers self-catering apartments with access to a garden, terrace and BBQ facilities.

Łagów Lake is only a 5-minute drive away and Międzyrzecz is a 35-minute drive away.

Majaland Kownaty is 24 km from the property. All apartments feature a view of the garden, a flat-screen TV, a wardrobe and a bathroom with a shower.

There is also a dining area with a refrigerator and electric kettle. Guests can grab a quick bite to eat from the on-site snack bar and packed lunches are also possible upon request.

Additional features include bicycle rental, massage treatments and an airport shuttle.

Activities in the surrounding area include cycling, horse riding and fishing. The property offers free parking.
