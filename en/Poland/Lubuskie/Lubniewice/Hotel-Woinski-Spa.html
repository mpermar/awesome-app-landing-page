Description

Located by the Lake Lubiąż, Hotel Woiński Spa offers a private beach with a jetty, where water sports facilities can be rented.

Its guests enjoy free access to a wide variety of spa facilities, such as an indoor pool, a hot tub, three types of sauna, a salt cave and a gym.

Gorzów Wielkopolski is about 35 km away. The air-conditioned rooms at the hotel are stylishly decorated in lively colours.

There is a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a minibar and a seating area in each of them, as well as a private, well-equipped bathroom.

Tea and coffee making facilities are available. Complementary bottled water is provided.

The elegant restaurant specialises in European cuisine and a breakfast buffet is available every morning.

Hotel Woiński Spa boasts impressive spa facilities which include a swimming pool, a hot tub, three kinds of sauna, a salt grotto, as well as a selection of beauty and body treatments.
