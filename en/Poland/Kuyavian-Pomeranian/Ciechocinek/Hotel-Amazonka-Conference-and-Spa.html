Description

Hotel Amazonka Conference and Spa is located just next to the Zdrojowy Park, 1. 5 km from central Ciechocinek.

It features spa facilities, Wi-Fi access and air-conditioned rooms with satellite TV.

The rooms offered have a bathroom with floor heating, a shower cabin and a hairdryer.

All offer a safe and a minibar. The Amazonka Restaurant serves international cuisine.

In the morning, guests can enjoy a varied breakfast. Hotel Amazonka Conference and Spa hotel provides guests with 24/7 front desk service and free parking places on site.

It is 1. 6 km to Brine Graduation Towers in Ciechocinek and 30 metres to the historical Salt Works.
