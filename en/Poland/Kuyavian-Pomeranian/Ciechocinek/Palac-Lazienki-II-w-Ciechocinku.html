Description

Pałac Łazienki II w Ciechocinek is situated in central Ciechocinek, 1.5 km from graduation towers.

This elegant hotel is housed in a former palace and a modern guesthouse building and offers rooms with a TV and free WiFi.

The Łazienki II Palace in Ciechocinek features a rehabilitation center, offering a wide range of professional medical treatments.

A sauna is available free of charge. The rooms at Pałac Łazienki II w Ciechocinku have a private bathroom.

Luxurious 2-level suites are also available. The on-site restaurant serves Polish and European cuisine.

Pałac Łazienki II w Ciechocinek is situated 750 meters from the Ciechocinek Railway Station.
