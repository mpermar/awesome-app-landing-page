Description

The Głęboczek is a luxury spa hotel located in the Brodnica Lake District. Its guests can relax in the Vinery Spa or an indoor or outdoor swimming pool.

Głęboczek Vine Resort & Spa is the only spa hotel in Poland to offer treatments based on wine therapy.

It also features an innovative Alphasphere. The air-conditioned rooms at Głęboczek Vine Resort come with cable TV, a safe and a work desk.

All offer free Wi-Fi and a bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries. The Głęboczek has a wine cellar with a wide choice of wines from all over the world.

The restaurant specialises in food based on fresh local products and combines Polish and light Mediterranean cuisine.
