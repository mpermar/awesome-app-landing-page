Description

4-star Copernicus Toruń Hotel is located on the shore of Wisła River, by picturesque Planty Park, and 500 metres from the Old Town, within a 600-metre distance from the leaning tower of Toruń and 700 metres from the Town Square.

Original and modern building features an indoor and outdoor pool, a spa and wellness centre, as well as rooms with a great view of the Old Town, panorama of Vistula or a park.

All rooms in Copernicus Toruń are air-conditioned and have high-class furniture and bathrooms featuring marble elements.

They boast a flat-screen TV, a safe, a minibar and smartphone with unlimited data plan and international calls.

Some feature a balcony. Guests of the hotel can enjoy a lobby bar, a bowling alley and a nightclub, as well as laundry and ironing service.

Buffet breakfast is served in the restaurant, which also features original dishes created by a hotel chef.

Copernicus also boasts a hairdressing salon. The hotel is surrounded by a number of pubs, restaurants and important sights.

Toruń Miasto Train Station is located 1,6 km away, and Toruń Bus Station – 1,5 km away.
