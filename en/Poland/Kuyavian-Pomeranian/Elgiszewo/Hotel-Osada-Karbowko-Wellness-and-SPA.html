Description

Located in the valley of the Drwęca River in Elgiszewo, 30 km from Toruń, Hotel Osada Karbówko Wellness & SPA offers free Wi-Fi and satellite TV.

Free, monitored parking is available. The complex offers different types of accommodation.

All rooms and apartments have an electric kettle, refrigerator and a bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer.

Guests of Hotel Osada Karbówko can relax in the spa & wellness centre, with a hydro-massage swimming pool, saunas, a spa bath and a fitness centre.

There is a salt cave and an outdoor pool as well. Guests are also welcome to enjoy a game of bowling or billiards.

The hotel also rents bicycles and can arrange for horse riding activities or a kayak cruise.

Children will enjoy numerous playgrounds and a mini ZOO. The resort’s restaurant serves game and traditional Polish cuisine prepared with local produce.

On the hotel's grounds there are 4 sites by the banks of Drwęca River serving barbecue dishes.

Hotel Osada Karbówko Wellness & SPA is located in a calm and quiet area, away from the rush of cities.
