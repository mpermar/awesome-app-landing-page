Description

Hotel Pałac Romantyczny in Turzno near Łysomice is a 4-star hotel housed in a 19th century palace surrounded by an English-style park and offers rooms with free internet connection.

The hotel is located only a 5-minute drive away from "Turzno" A1 exit and offers private parking.

Rooms at Pałac Romantyczny include minibars and LCD TVs, as well as coffee making facilities.

Each one is air-conditioned. The hotel features relaxing spa facilities with a Finnish sauna, beer baths, massages and a spa bath.

For all those keen on sports, there is an on-site tennis court as well as a playground for children.

Guests can rent a bike or Nordic walking equipment. A volleyball court is also there.

Free buffet breakfast is served every day in a restaurant Marconi, offering Polish and international cuisine.

Coffee can be tasted in the Lobby Bar and there is a fireplace room to relax in. The Old Town of Toruń is 14 km away and the hotel offers free monitored parking.
