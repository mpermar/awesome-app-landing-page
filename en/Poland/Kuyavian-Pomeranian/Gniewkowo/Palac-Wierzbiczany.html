Description

Pałac Wierzbiczany has a restaurant, bar, a shared lounge and garden in Gniewkowo.

Boasting room service, this property also provides guests with a children's playground.

The air-conditioned rooms provide a garden view and come with a desk and free WiFi.

All rooms in the hotel are fitted with a kettle. At Pałac Wierzbiczany rooms are equipped with a wardrobe and a private bathroom.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet breakfast. Pałac Wierzbiczany offers a barbecue.

Guests at the hotel will be able to enjoy activities in and around Gniewkowo, like hiking.

Toruń is 29 km from Pałac Wierzbiczany, while Bydgoszcz is 49 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Bydgoszcz Ignacy Jan Paderewski, 53 km from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
