Description

Pałac Lucja is a 3-star hotel located in Zakrzew, in a park with a pond. It offers air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi and 32” LCD satellite TV.

Free private parking is available. Each room features an elegant interior design with oak furniture and beds with high quality mattresses.

All rooms include a safe and a minibar, as well as a bathroom with a shower. The restaurant is air-conditioned and has a bar that offers a wide selection of drinks.

It serves Polish, Silesian and German dishes. In the morning a breakfast buffet is served here free of charge.

Banquet and conference facilities are available for groups. The nearest railway station is in Gogolin, 4 km away.
