Description

Złota Podkowa is located in quiet Olszowa near Strzelce Opolskie and offers a restaurant and free WiFi.

There is a business centre and meeting facilities. Each room here will provide you with a flat-screen TV and satellite channels.

The private bathrooms come with a shower. Extras include bed linen and towels. At Pensjonat Złota Podkowa you will find barbecue facilities and a children's playground.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including hiking.

The property is next to the Sacral Miniature Park Olszowa and 11 km from St. Anne Mountain with its Sanctuary.
