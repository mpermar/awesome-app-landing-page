Description

Located a 5-minute walk from Opole's Market Square, Szara Willa hotel offers modern, air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi.

The hotel restaurant serves original European specialities and a varied breakfast.

The rooms at Szara Willa come with satellite TV and a radio. They have a bathroom with a shower cabin and a hairdryer.

In each there is complementary bottled water and sweets. Deluxe rooms feature tea and coffee making facilities.

At Szara Willa’s colonial-style restaurant, guests can enjoy excellent European dishes and a breakfast buffet.

The property offers a wide variety of equipment for children, such as a bath, a high chair, potties, food warmers, kitchenware and cribs, which are offered for free.

During their stay at Szara Willa, guests can use the hotel gym for free. Children are offered a play room, also available for free.

The hotel houses a sauna and a bowling alley with a bar. An underground parking is available free of charge.
