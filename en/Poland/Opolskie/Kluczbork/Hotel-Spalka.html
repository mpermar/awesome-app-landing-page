Description

4-star Hotel Spałka is located in the quiet area of Kluczbork, in a 19-th century tenement house, offering its guests rooms with free Wi-Fi.

Every modern room in Hotel Spałka has a flat-screen TV and a spacious bathroom. The hotel features a 24-hour reception, a safe and a spacious terrace, at extra charge guests have access to a choice of spa treatments and massages.

Buffet breakfast is served in the hotel restaurant. There is also a pub. It is 5 km from the hotel to Stobrawa Landscape Park.
