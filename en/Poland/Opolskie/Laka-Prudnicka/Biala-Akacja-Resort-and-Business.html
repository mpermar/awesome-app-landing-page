Description

Biała Akacja Resort & Business is located in the scenic surroundings of the Opawskie Mountains and offers accommodation with free W-Fi.

The Czech border is just 9 km away. The rooms at Biała Akacja are classically decorated in warm colours and each comes with a flat-screen TV, an electric kettle and a private bathroom with a shower.

Guests are offered free tea/coffee making facilities and mineral water. There is a restaurant in the building which specialises in Polish and international cuisine.

Roofed barbecuing facilities, as well as tables and benches are provided in the garden.
