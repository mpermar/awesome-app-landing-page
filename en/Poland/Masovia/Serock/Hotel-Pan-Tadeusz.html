Description

The 3-star Hotel Pan Tadeusz is located at the Zegrze Reservoir in Serock. It offers free access to a swimming pool, sauna and jacuzzi, elegant rooms with free internet and water sports equipment.

All rooms at the Pan Tadeusz are bight and classically furnished. Each comes with satellite TV and a modern bathroom with a hairdryer.

Some feature a seating area. Breakfast is served at the hotel’s air-conditioned restaurant, the Poezja, which specialises in Polish and international dishes.

Guests can enjoy a drink at the Lobby Bar or at the nightclub. Guests can go canoeing, cycling or simply relax in the garden.

A wide variety of massages and body treatments is also available. The hotel offers a direct access to reservoir pier.

Hotel Pan Tadeusz is located 1 km from the beach at the Narew River. The railway station in Nieporęt is 8 km away.
