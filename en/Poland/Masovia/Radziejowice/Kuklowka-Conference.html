Description

Offering a restaurant, Kuklówka Conference is located in Radziejowice, in a quiet area by the forest and a stream.

Free WiFi access is available in this resort. Rooms here come with a private bathroom with a bath or shower.

Extras include a desk and bed linen. They are private, and located in and number of buildings on 27 hectare grounds.

At Kuklówka Conference you will find a communal sauna and a terrace. Other facilities offered at the property include a shared lounge, a games room and a children's playground.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling and hiking.

Upon request any other outdoor activity can be arranged. The property offers free parking.
