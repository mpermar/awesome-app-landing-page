Description

Pałac Żelechów Spa & Wellness is situated in 3 buildings with all the modern facilities.

This historical palace offers a 24-hour reception, free Wi-Fi and private parking.

Guests can enjoy a selection of craft beers from the hotel’s cellars. The rooms are air conditioned and feature elegant palace-style interiors.

Each comes with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bath or a shower. Guests are welcomed to take free advantage of the selection of services provided by the Pałac Żelechów spa cenre.

There are numerous types of saunas and steam baths, as well as one of two indoor poolsand a walk through a rainforest path.

The on-site restaurant serves Polish cuisine and also features a kid’s menu. In the summer season it offers barbecue dishes.
