Description

The rooms at the Sielanka resort offer an LCD TV with satellite channels. All feature a modern bathroom with a hairdryer.

Guests of Sielanka Nad Pilicą have free access to some spa facilities, including a jacuzzi, a Finnish sauna and a steam bath.

During their stay at the Sielanka, guests can play beach volleyball, fish in the on site pond and enjoy horseback riding. Nordic walking poles rental is free. Children are offered a sandpit, a slide, swings and a trampoline.

The on site Impresja Restaurant serves French, Italian and Polish dishes. Guests can also relax in the Fireplace Room and the Equiclub drink bar with a pool table.

Sielanka Nad Pilicą is 30 miles south of Warsaw, just off the Puszcza Stromiecka forest.
