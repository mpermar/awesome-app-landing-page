Description

Karo offers accommodation 7 km from the centre of Radom. The hotel has a terrace and sun terrace, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Free WiFi is featured throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

The rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV. The rooms include a private bathroom fitted with a shower.

For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hair dryer. You will find a shared lounge at the property.
