Description

The 3-star guest house Krzysin is located in charming Podkowa Leśna, a small town near Warsaw.

It offers accommodation with free Wi-Fi and private parking. The rooms at Krzysin are bright and spacious and there is a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a shower in each of them.

The restaurant offers breakfast and other meals can be requested too. A shared kitchen is available and guests can also barbecue in the garden.

There is a common lounge area at the guest house and a massage can also be arranged at an additional price.

The Warsaw Commuter Railway (WKD) station is 1 km away and Galeria Podkowa Shopping Centre is 2.
