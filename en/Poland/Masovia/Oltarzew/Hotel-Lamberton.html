Description

Hotel Lamberton is located in Ołtarzew, near the A2 motorway, connecting Warsaw and Poznań.

It offers free parking and modern, elegant rooms with a 32’’ LCD TV and free internet.

The luxury rooms at the Lamberton have a large private bathroom with a shower cabin and a hairdryer.

All offer satellite TV and a dressing table. Each room offers a complimentary bottle of water.

The hotel restaurant features modern design with Art Nouveau motifs. It serves traditional Polish specialities, international dishes and a wide range of wines.

Hotel Lamberton is situated 19 km from central Warsaw and 20 km from Warsaw Chopin Airport.
