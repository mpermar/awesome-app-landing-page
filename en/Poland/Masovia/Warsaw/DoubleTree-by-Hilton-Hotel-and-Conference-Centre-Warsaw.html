Description

Surrounded by a picturesque historic park with a pond and jogging trails, 4-star DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel & Conference Centre Warsaw is located in a quiet area of Warsaw, 11 km from the centre of the city.

Complimentary high speed WiFi access is available, as well as complimentary outdoor parking.

There also is a parking area for buses with a roofed driveway. Modern, spacious rooms come with a flat-screen TV.

Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with a hairdryer, towels and free toiletries.

The hotel rooms feature Sweet Dreams™ beds. Guests booking Executive Rooms can enjoy a complimentary breakfast, refreshments and an evening cocktail with an open bar in the Executive Lounge.

At DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel & Conference Centre Warsaw you will find a fitness and Spa centre, as well as a conference centre, luggage storage, bars and a night club.

Other facilities offered at the property include concierge services, luggage storage and meeting facilities.

There is also a bar. It is 19 km to Warsaw Chopin Airport. Wilanów Palace and Park are 13 km from the hotel.

The distance to both Warszawa Centralna and Warszawa Wschodnia Train Stations is 12.
