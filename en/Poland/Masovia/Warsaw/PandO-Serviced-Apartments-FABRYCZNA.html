Description

Boasting an attractive location in the popular Powiśle district, Apartamenty Powiśle offers accommodation in a modern, monitored building.

The apartments are stylishly decorated and come with a seating area with a flat-screen TV and a bathroom with a hairdryer.

Some feature a balcony. Free Wi-Fi is available. The trendy kitchen done in white includes all the equipment needed to prepare a meal: a stove, a refrigerator, a microwave and a dishwasher.

A dining area is also provided. The Royal Park Łazienki is about 1. 5 km away and so is the Centre for Contemporary Art.
