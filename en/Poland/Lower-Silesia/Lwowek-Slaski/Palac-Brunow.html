Description

The Brunów Palace is a beautiful mansion consisting of 3 historic buildings, surrounded by lovely gardens that extend into a park.

There are a variety of rooms that are uniquely decorated in classical style. Each room contains well contained bathrooms, satellite TV, and internet connection. The property is fully wheelchair accessible and secure parking is available.

Guests can find many ways to relax at Brunów. Take long garden/park strolls or explore the area on bike. There is a sauna, jacuzzi and for the more active guest, a tennis court. For meals and beverage, the Brunów Palace offers a restaurant, cafe and bar.
