Description

Interferie Sport Hotel is located 300 metres by the Kwisa River, offering rooms with free Wi-Fi and satellite TV.

Guests have free access to the aqua park including a sauna, a hot tub, a swimming pool with two slides, a saltwater pool, and a paddling pool for children.

Each room at the Interferie includes private bathroom facilities with shower. There is a flat-screen TV, a radio and a phone.

Some rooms offer access to view terraces surrounding the hotel. Some also come with a fridge, bottled water and a hairdryer.

The hotel offers a sport hall with a basketball and a volleyball court. There is a gym, billiards facilities, saunas and a salt cave to relax in.

There is a wide selection of leisure and wellness facilities, such as a winter ice skating rink and several types of swimming pools.

Guest can also benefit from a beauty parlour and wellness treatments. The property has a restaurant, serving Polish cuisine à la carte, as well as a café offering desserts and hot beverages.

Interferie Sport Hotel has a 24-hour front desk service and is situated 2 km from Świeradów Zdrój.
