Description

Situated just 100 metres from the cable car, Aparthotel Flinski Sport & Spa offers accommodation in modern apartments.

Guests can use a swimming pool, a sauna and a hot tub located in the building. Electronic locking system is featured throughout the property.

The apartments are originally decorated in lively colours and patterned wallpapers.

Each comes with a balcony, a seating area with a flat-screen TV and a bathroom fitted with a shower and a hairdryer.

Each apartment has a well-equipped kitchen or a kitchenette. There also is a restaurant at Aparthotel Flinski Sport & Spa which specialises in Italian and eco cuisine.

The fitness room can be used free of charge and guests can also book spa treatments at an additional fee.

Children will enjoy a playground and a play room with professional staff. Świeradów Zdrój’s town centre is just 400 metres away and so is the bowling alley.
