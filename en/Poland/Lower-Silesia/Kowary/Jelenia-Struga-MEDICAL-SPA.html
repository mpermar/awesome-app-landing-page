Description

Located in the small town of Kowary, amid the beautiful Karkonosze Mountain Range, Jelenia Struga MEDICAL SPA hotel provides guests with first-class wellness treatments.

Experience pure relaxation for body and mind at Jelenia Struga MEDICAL SPA. Featuring the only Radon Chamber in Poland, the hotel specializes in anti-oxidant therapies and other certified beauty therapies.

Visit the hamam and enjoy an exotic oriental bath. The restaurant serves meals prepared with organic products, complementing the health-oriented approach of Jelenia Struga MEDICAL SPA.
