Description

The rooms at Sofitel Hotel come with tea/coffee makers, cable TV and work desks. Some also include a Bose sound system with iPod docking stations and Nespresso machines.

The Sofitel is within short walk of the major attractions of Wroclaw, including Ostrow Tumski. The Galeria Dominikańska Shopping Center is just half a mile away.

The Sofitel Wroclaw Old Town has 2 restaurants serving international delicacies. In the morning you can enjoy a varied breakfast buffet. The Louis D'Or Bar has a wide choice of drinks.

Sofitel Wroclaw Old Town has a spa center with a fitness center with TechnoGym equipment, hot tub, sauna, steam bath and massage parlor.

Stare Miasto is a great choice for travelers interested in history, nightlife and restaurants.
