Description

Invite is located in the centre of Wrocław, 800 metres from the Wrocław Główny Train and Bus Stations, and 2 km from the Old Town.

It offers accommodation in modern rooms and apartments with free Wi-Fi. Accommodation at Invite features a 32-inch flat-screen TV.

Complete with a refrigerator, work desk, electric kettle and a bar table. Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Extras include a large wardrobe and a night stand. Some of the rooms have a seating area.

At Invite you will find a 24-hour front desk. There is also an on-site restaurant and a bar.

If you feel like visiting the surroundings, check out Ostrów Tumski, the oldest part of Wrocław, 2.

5 km away. The Centennial Hall and the Zoo are 4 km away. The nearest public transport stops are only 100 metres from the property.
