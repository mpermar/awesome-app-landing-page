Description

The air-conditioned rooms come with a coffee machine, a mini-bar and a bathroom complete with a shower. A bottle of water is also provided, as well as ironing facilities. Some units feature a bathtub and a terrace with a city view.

AC Hotel by Marriott Wrocław features an on-site Fuego restaurant. It is located in the AC Lounge which also offers an open bar, a breakfast room, a library and an entrance to a spacious hotel patio decorated with plants.

Guests can relax in the modern Wellness area which includes a swimming pool, a dry sauna and a steam sauna. There is also a well-equipped fitness center and a massage parlor.

Staff at the reception is available 24 hours a day and free WiFi is also provided for guests’ convenience.

It is 0.9 mi to Wrocław Main Train Station and the distance to Wrocław-Starachowice Airport is 6.2 mi.

Stare Miasto is a great choice for travelers interested in history, nightlife and restaurants.
