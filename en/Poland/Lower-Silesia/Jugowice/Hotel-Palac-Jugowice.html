Description

Pałac Jugowice is located among the Owl Mountains and offers stylish, air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi and LCD TVs.

There is a restaurant and a 24-hour front desk service. Each air-conditioned room at the palace offers unique interiors with visible wooden beams and modern furniture.

The bathroom done in beige and black features a glass shower cabin. The hotel offers a sauna and spa complex with 7 wooden houses, 4 saunas, a wellness bar and a relaxation area with an available variety of health treatments.

Guests are welcome to dine in the restaurant offering breakfasts, as well as international fusion cuisine à la carte.

There also are barbecue facilities. The palace is situated 5 km from the nearest ski lift, Rzeczka.
