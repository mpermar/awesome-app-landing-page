Description

Hotel Zadrna is located in Chełmsko Śląskie in Lower Silesia on the South of Poland.

It offers rooms with private bathroom facilities. Zadrna's heated rooms feature satellite TVs and free Wi-Fi access.

Guests can also have a game of billiards or table tennis. The restaurant specialises in Polish, regional, and international dishes.

It also has a pizzeria. There is a bar on-site. Situated 2. 4 km from the Czech boarder, the Zadrna is surrounded by many forested hills.

The Karkonoski National Park is 15 km away. The nearest major city, Wałbrzych, is within 26 km.
