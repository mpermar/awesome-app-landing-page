Description

Hotel Dębowy Biowellness & SPA is situated in a quiet and green area of Bielawa, in the foothills of the the Sowie Mountains, the property consists of 2 buildings (Pałac Dębowy and Rezydencja Dębowa) surrounded by a historic 10 hectare park.

It offers private parking, free Wi-Fi, and a variety of leisure facilities. The rooms are decorated in warm colours.

Each comes with a TV, as well as a private bathroom with a shower. Open-air barbecue parties, honey tasting, healthy lifestyle workshops and other activities can be organized in the property.

Guests have also access to a unique bio wellness zone, bio pond and a sense SPA featuring apitherapy.

Hotel Dębowy Biowellness & SPA features Restauracja Ogrodowa offering meals made of natural products from local suppliers as well as local crops.

The hotel provides services such as airport shuttle, tour desk, car rental, bicycle rental and child minding.

The property is located 3. 5 km from the centre of Bielawa and 8 km from the city of Dzierżoniów.
