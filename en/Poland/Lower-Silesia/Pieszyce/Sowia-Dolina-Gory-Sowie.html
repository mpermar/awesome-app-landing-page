Description

Sowia Dolina- Góry Sowie is located in Pieszyce in the Lower Silesia Region, 9 km from Dzierżoniów and 30 km from Polanica-Zdrój.

Guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Free high speed WiFi is featured throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Every room includes a flat-screen TV. Certain rooms feature a seating area where you can relax.

A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms. The rooms include a private bathroom.

There is a shared lounge at the property. A selection of activities are offered in the area, such as skiing and horse riding.

The bed and breakfast also offers ski equipment hire, bike hire, and car hire. Kudowa-Zdrój is 33 km from Sowia Dolina- Góry Sowie, while Lądek-Zdrój is 44 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Wroclaw – Copernicus Airport, 54 km from Sowia Dolina- Góry Sowie.
