Description

Featuring a sauna and a hot tub, the Pensjonat L&B is situated at the foot of the Śnieżka Mountain in Karpacz.

It offers rooms with a balcony, a seating area and an electric kettle. Free Wi-Fi is provided.

All rooms at the L&B are bright and decorated with warm colours. Each comes with a mountain view, a refrigerator and an LCD TV with satellite channels.

Some offer a bathtub. You can enjoy a game of billiards, table football or darts.

Front desk staff can assist with ski storage service. Hairdryer and microwave use are available at the reception.

Children will enjoy a well-equipped outdoor playground. A buffet breakfast is served every morning in the dining room.

The on-site bar offers a selection of drinks, which you can enjoy in the fireplace room.

Pensjonat L&B is located 500 metres from Liczyrzepa Ski Lift and 400 metres from Pod Wangiem.
