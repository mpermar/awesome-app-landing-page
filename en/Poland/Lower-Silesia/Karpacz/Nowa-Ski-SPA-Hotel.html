Description

Nowa – Ski Spa Hotel is located in Karpacz and features panoramic views of the Karkonosze Mountains and its Śnieżka Peak.

It offers a spa centre with a massage parlour and rooms with free WiFi internet. All rooms at the Ski Spa Hotel are bright and decorated with warm, pastel colours.

Each comes with a safe, a work desk and a modern bathroom with a hairdryer. Guests can relax in the whirlpool bath or try a selection of available body treatments.

There is an on-site fitness centre while bicycles and Nordic walking equipment are available free of charge.

Sauna, hot tub and solarium are available free of charge upon request. Breakfast is served in the hotel restaurant, which offers regional and European dishes.

A wide range of drinks is available from the bar and can be enjoyed on the terrace.

Guests can also play billiards at no extra charge. Nowa – Ski Spa Hotel is situated in a quiet location at the foot of the Księża Mountain and is surrounded by forests.

The nearest bus stop is 500 metres away. The property offers its own transport to ski lifts.
