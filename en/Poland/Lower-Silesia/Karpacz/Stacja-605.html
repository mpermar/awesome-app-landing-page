Description

Located in Karpacz, 2. 1 km from Kolorowa Lift, Stacja 605 provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking and a bar.

Among the facilities at this property are a 24-hour front desk and room service, along with free WiFi throughout the property.

The accommodation features a kids' club, a tour desk and luggage storage for guests.

At the hotel, all rooms include a wardrobe. At Stacja 605 every room is fitted with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

A buffet breakfast is available daily at the accommodation. Stacja 605 offers a range of wellness facilities including a hot tub and a sauna.

You can play billiards at the hotel, and the area is popular for hiking. Western City is 2.
