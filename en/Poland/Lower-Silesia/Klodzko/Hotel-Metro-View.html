Description

This 3-star, elegant Hotel Metro View is conveniently situated in the centre of Kłodzko Valley, 2 km from Kłodzko.

It features spacious rooms with free WiFi, satellite TV and a refrigerator. There is a bowling alley with two full lanes.

All rooms at the Metro View are fitted with a seating area and a safety deposit box.

Each comes with a private bathroom. Hotel’s air-conditioned restaurant specialises in Polish and international dishes and serves a varied buffet breakfast every morning.

Guests can relax in the drink bar. There is also sauna, a salt cave, table tennis, darts and billiards.

Front desk staff is available 24 hours a day. Guests can enjoy workout in the gym.
