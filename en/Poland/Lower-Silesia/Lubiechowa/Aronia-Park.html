Description

Located in the quiet Lubiechowa village, surrounded by hills and mountains, Aronia Park offers rooms with linens made of hypoallergenic materials.

Every room in Aronia Park is decorated in warm colours and features a private bathroom with a shower.

Beds are connectible. A spacious patio the green garden with a pond allow for a relaxing time.

There is a barbecue and a bonfire area, as well as a bicycle rental available at extra charge.

There is also a common microwave and a fridge. Guest can also use the washing machine and iron if needed.

Free Wi-Fi is available in the public area. Guests can order homecooked breakfast and dinner with homemade honey and meat products.

Hotel staff can arrange for horsecart and sleigh rides, massage and beauty treatments and pottery classes, as well as horseriding lessons.

It is 8 km to Łysa Góra Mountain with a ski lift. Jelenia Góra city is 17 km away.
