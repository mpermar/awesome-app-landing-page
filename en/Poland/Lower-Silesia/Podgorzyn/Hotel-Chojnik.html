Description

Tucked away in beautiful countryside, surrounded by forests and mountains, this charming hotel is a haven of tranquillity and relaxation.

Each pet-friendly guest room is comfortably furnished, providing peace and quiet after a long and active day spent outdoors.

At Hotel Chojnik guests can play a game of tennis or visit the fitness centre. Afterwards, they can enjoy a delicious meal in the hotel restaurant or simply relax in the sauna.

Thanks to its 2 ski slopes, the hotel also provides ski-to-door access. Hotel Chojnik provides affordable accommodation in a scenic setting next to a national park.

Guests can soak up the sun while strolling through the garden or enjoying a barbecue with friends.

For guests looking for a special event venue, the hotel features banquet facilities as well as numerous other amenities.
