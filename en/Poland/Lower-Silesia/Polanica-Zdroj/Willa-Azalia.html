Description

Located on the slope of Mary’s Hill, in a quiet, forested area of Polanica, 800 metres from the centre of the town, Willa Azalia features bright rooms with a kitchenette and free Wi-Fi.

The rooms in Azalia are bright and spacious, and feature a TV with satellite channels, a bathroom with a shower and a seating area.

Azalia features a 24-hour reception and a green garden. It is 800 metres to a spa park with a mineral water pump room.

It is 450 meters to Bystrzyca Dusznica stream and 400 meters to Polanica Zdrój Bus Station.
