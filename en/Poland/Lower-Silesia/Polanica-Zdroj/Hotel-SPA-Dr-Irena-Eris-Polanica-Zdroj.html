Description

Hotel SPA Dr Irena Eris Polanica Zdrój is located in Polanica-Zdrój near Zdrojowy Park.

It features Centrum SPA with a pool complex and saunas, as well as Instytut Kosmetyczny Dr Irena Eris offering various beauty and body treatments.

The surrounding countryside is ideal for skiing, cycling, and hiking. Providing free WiFi access, each spacious room features air conditioning and a balcony.

Each one comes equipped with a telephone, a minibar, a hairdryer, an LCD TV and ironing facilities.

At Hotel SPA Dr Irena Eris Polanica Zdrój guests will find a squash court, as well as a cardio studio and fitness room.

Other facilities offered at the property include meeting facilities and luggage storage.

The property offers free outdoor parking. The hotel’s Art Déco restaurant serves a variety of dishes and offers a wide selection of wines.

Guest can spend their time bowling or playing billiards. For kids, there is a children’s playground and a kids’ club.
