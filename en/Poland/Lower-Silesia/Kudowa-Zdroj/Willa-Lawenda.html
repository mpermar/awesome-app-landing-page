Description

Willa Lawenda is situated in a historic spa town of Kudowa Zdrój. It offers accommodation with free Wi-Fi and a private bathroom.

Zdrojowy Park with mineral water pump room is 800 meters away. The rooms at Willa Lawenda are bright and spacious and come with wooden furnishings.

Each features a flat-screen TV, a refrigerator, an electric kettle and a hairdryer.

Some feature a balcony. Buffet breakfast is served at the guest house’s dining room.

Guests of Lawenda can relax in the sauna or the common room. Those wanting to spend their time actively can hire a bicycle, a sledge or Nordic walking sticks.

Children will enjoy a play room or a playground in a beautiful garden. Kudowa Zdrój Train Station is 1.

2 km away, a bus stop and taxi rank are 200 meters away and a monitored parking is also available on site.
