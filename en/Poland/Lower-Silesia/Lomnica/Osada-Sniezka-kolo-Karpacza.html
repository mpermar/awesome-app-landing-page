Description

Osada Śnieżka is located in the picturesque area of Łomnica by the foothill of the Karkonosze mountains.

The property houses an indoor swimming pool complex, a number of saunas, 2 restaurants, as well as a barbecue hut.

There is a 24-hour front desk. Guests can choose from a variety of rooms and apartments, all featuring free WiFi, bathrooms with free toiletries and hairdryers, flat-screen TVs and electric kettles.

Osada Śnieżka boasts a bar & restaurant, Tabakiera, as well as a lunch restaurant, Miód Malina.

There is also a Family Fun Centre, which features billiards, table tennis, darts, air hockey, a playstation game console, a ball pit and a kids' corner.

In the winter, guests will benefit from a ski rental. The property also offers Nordic walking equipment, bike rental, sports equipment rental, a sports field, as well as an outdoor swimming pool complex with 2 pools for adults and 2 pools for kids.

Osada Śnieżka also has a modern spa centre with rehabilitation treatments. Activities that can be enjoyed in the surroundings, include skiing, cycling and hiking.

The property offers free parking. Wroclaw – Copernicus Airport is located 82 km from the accommodation.
