Description

Akademia Kuraszków has a restaurant, bar, a shared lounge and garden in Kuraszków.

Boasting room service, this property also provides guests with a terrace. The air-conditioned rooms provide a garden view and come with a desk and free WiFi.

All rooms at the resort are fitted with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and a shower.

Akademia Kuraszków offers a continental or buffet breakfast. The accommodation offers a sauna.

The area is popular for hiking and cycling, and bike hire is available at Akademia Kuraszków.

Wrocław is 37 km from the resort. The nearest airport is Wroclaw – Copernicus Airport, 46 km from Akademia Kuraszków.
