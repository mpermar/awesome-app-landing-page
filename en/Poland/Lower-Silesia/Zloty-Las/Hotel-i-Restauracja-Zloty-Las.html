Description

Hotel i Restauracja Złoty Las is located in a quiet and calm area in the Sudetes mountains.

It offers rooms with free Wi-Fi and a TV set. Free private parking is available. The rooms of the Złoty Las feature a classic interior design with wooden furniture in tones-down colours.

All have a work desk and a private bathroom with a shower, and some include a flat-screen TV.

The Złoty Las restaurant serves Mediterranean and Polish cuisine. In the mornings breakfast is offered, and there is a bar on site as well.

In addition, there is a designated barbecue area. The Złoty Las is in a calm area surrounded by a forest, away from the rush of cities.

Świdnica and Wałbrzych are within 9 km, and Jugowice Railway Station is 5. 5 km away.
