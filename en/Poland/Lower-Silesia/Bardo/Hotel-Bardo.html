Description

This 3-star Hotel Bardo is situated in a quiet area in Bardo, in the vicinity of hiking and cycling trails.

The property features an array of facilities, such as a spa & wellness centre, an indoor pool, an outdoor gym and a pumptrack.

Each room at Bardo is air conditioned and features free WiFi and a flat-screen TV.

The private bathrooms come with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries. Apart from the wellness facilities, Bardo's guests can benefit from a conference room, equipped with modern multimedia equipment, and entertainment staff.

The hotel also offers a restaurant and a bar. A 24-hour front desk and free private parking are provided.
