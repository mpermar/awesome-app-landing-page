Description

Offering an indoor pool, ski rental and horseback riding, Granada Pensjonat & SPA is located in the quiet area of Duszniki Zdrój on the border of Góry Stołowe National Park, 3 km from the spa park with a mineral water pump room.

It’s classically furnished rooms feature free Wi-Fi access. Bright rooms come with a TV and a balcony, as well as a private bathroom with a shower.

Guests of Granada Pensjonat & SPA have free access to the spa & wellness area including the pool, a Finnish sauna, a steam bath and a hot tub.

There is a garden with barbecue facilities and a children's playground with a trampoline and swings.

Guests can enjoy a billiards room and a common lounge with a ping-pong and foosball tables and a choice of board games.

There is a bicycle and Nordic walking stick rental. Breakfast is served in a restaurant.

There is also a bar. The premises are fenced and monitored. There is a car park and a barbecue hut.

It is 6 km to Błędne Skały nature reserve, Museum of Papermaking in Duszniki Zdrój is 3.
