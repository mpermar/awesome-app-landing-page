Description

Hotel Impresja is located in the heart of Duszniki Zdrój, in the centre of the Spa Park, a 3-minute walk from the mineral water pump room.

All the modernly furnished rooms in Hotel Impresja have a seating area with a sofa, a flat-screen TV, a fridge, a bathroom with a shower and a set of cosmetics.

The rooms feature mineral water and a set of tea bags. Buffet breakfast and dinner menu is served in the spacious dining room that features a wide, varied choice of meals, including vegetarian, gluten-free, as well as traditional choices.

Free Wi-Fi is available. The hotel also offers free private parking. Hotel Impresja also features a choice of massage treatments.

At the property guests will also find a cafe and a playroom for children. There is also a modern conference hall for up to 200 people, equipped with a large automatic display, a projector, a flipchart, Wi-Fi and a professional audio system.

Duszniki Zdrój Train Station is located 1. 3 km away from the hotel, and the Museum of Papermaking is 1.

7 km away. Zieleniec Ski Arena is 7 km away and there is a free hourly ski bus available from the property.
