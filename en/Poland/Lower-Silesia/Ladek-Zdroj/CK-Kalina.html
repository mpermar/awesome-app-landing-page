Description

CK Kalina is a 4-star guesthouse located in the health spa area of Lądek-Zdrój, just 200 metres from Tysiąclecia Park.

The property offers a restaurant and a bar with billiards and a wide selection of beers.

Free WiFi access is available. Each room here features a flat-screen TV with both satellite and cable channels.

The private bathrooms come with a shower and a hairdryer. Extras include a desk, bed linen and towels.

CK Kalina provides banquet facilities and a 24-hour front desk. Guests can also enjoy a communal sauna.

There is luggage storage and ski storage, as well as free private parking. Photography exhibitions by local artists are held in the lobby.

CK Kalina is 20 km from Złoty Stok Gold Mine and 17 km from the 33 national road.
