Description

Offering a la carte restaurant and buffet restaurant, Dwa Dęby is located in Lubawka, at an ecological and picturesque farm.

Rooms feature rustic décor. Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with towels.

Some rooms have a mountain view and a garden view. Extras include linen. At Dwa Dęby you will find a garden, barbecue facilities and a terrace.

Other facilities offered at the property include meeting facilities, a tour desk and ski storage.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including skiing, cycling and hiking.

The property offers free parking. This homestay is about 2. 5 km from the Bukówka Lake.
