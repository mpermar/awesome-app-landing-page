Description

Willa na Cichej is situated in the center of Szklarska Poręba, a popular resort in the Karkonosze Mountains.

It offers accommodation with free bicycle rentals and private parking. The rooms are done in light, warm colours and each comes with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

A private bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer is available. There is a buffet restaurant in Willa na Cichej where breakfast is served.

Guests can use a shared kitchenette which includes a microwave, an electric kettle and a refrigerator.

The terrace is perfect for relaxation and children can enjoy spending time in the play room or at the playground.

The Alpine Coaster is 300 metres away and the Dino Park is 1 km away from Willa na Cichej.
