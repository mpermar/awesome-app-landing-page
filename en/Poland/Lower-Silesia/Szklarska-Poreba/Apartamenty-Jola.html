Description

Only 100 metres from the centre of Szklarska Poręba, Apartamenty Jola offers accommodation in apartments with free Wi-Fi and an LCD TV with cable channels.

Free parking is available. The apartments feature pastel colours and wooden furniture.

The balcony offers picturesque views of the Karkonosze Mountains. You can store your skiing equipment in the designated ski storage area.

The apartments have a kitchen with kitchenware, a stove and an oven. A dining area is also there.

For quest’s convenience an electric kettle and a dishwasher are included. Szrenica ski lift is within 700 metres, and the local shuttle bus station is a 10 minute walk away.
