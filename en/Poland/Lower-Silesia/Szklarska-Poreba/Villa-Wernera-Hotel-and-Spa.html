Description

Situated in a picturesque area and surrounded by a forest, Villa Wernera Hotel & Spa offers accommodation with free Wi-Fi and private parking.

Guests enjoy free access to the sauna, steam bath and the hot tub. The non-smoking rooms at Villa Wernera are classically decorated in warm colours.

Each features a TV with satellite channels, a desk and a minibar. In addition, a private bathroom is provided.

Guests of Wernera Hotel & Spa are offered buffet breakfast every morning and there is a bar and a restaurant specialising in homely, traditional cuisine.

There is an all year barbecuing area where a bonfire can also be organised. Guests can enjoy table tennis or darts on site.

There is a special room where children can play. Villa Wernera Hotel & Spa can arrange sightseeing tours.

The skiing complex SkiArena Szrenica is 3 km away and the Bus Station is also 3 km away from the property.
