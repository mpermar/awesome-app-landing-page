Description

Cristal Resort Szklarska Poręba is located in a quiet area, and offers modern apartments with free Wi-Fi and a flat-screen TV - most offering a view of Szrenica Mountain.

There is an outdoor hot tub, an indoor pool, as well as a spa and wellness centre.

Each apartment consists of a living room with a seating area, combined with a well-equipped kitchenette.

There are essential cooking utensils, a kettle and a dishwasher. The Cristal includes spa facilities with a sauna and a steam bath.

The on-site restaurant serves dishes à la carte and has a cosy fireplace. A paid private parking is possible on site.

Cristal Resort is situated 1. 5 km from the center of Szklarska Poręba and 2. 7 km from the Szklarska Poręba Średnia Train Station.
