Description

Situated 800 meters from Szklarska Poreba Bus Station in Szklarska Poręba, Hotel EUROPA - Górnicza Strzecha features a restaurant, bar and free WiFi throughout the property.

Free private parking is available on site. Each unit features a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Certain rooms include a seating area to relax in after a busy day. A flat-screen TV is featured.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. A number of activities are offered in the area, such as skiing and cycling.
