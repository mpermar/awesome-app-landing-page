Description

Offering an indoor pool and a restaurant, Malina Apartments offers accommodation in a quiet part of Szklarska Poręba, 300 metres from Szrenica Ski Lift.

Free WiFi access is available. Malina Apartments feature a TV and a balcony. There is a full kitchenette with a microwave and a refrigerator.

The pool area includes a paddling pool for children, a finish and steam sauna. Other facilities like ski storage and children playground are also offered.

It is 2 km to Szklarska Poręba Train Station and 1 km to Szklarska Poreba Bus Station.
