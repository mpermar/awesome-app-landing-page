Description

Set in Szklarska Poręba, Aparthotel Czarny Kamień Resort & Spa boasts an indoor swimming pool, jacuzzi, sauna and gym.

Dinopark is 2. 1 km from the property. Free WiFi is provided throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

The accommodation has a flat-screen TV. There is also a kitchenette, equipped with a fridge and stovetop.

There is a private bathroom with bathrobes and a hairdryer in every unit. Towels and bed linen are available.

The stay in Czarny Kamień Resort & Spa also includes free entrance to SPA & Wellness area.

Guests can enjoy buffet breakfast and dinner at the hotel's restaurant. The property has ski storage space and bike hire is available.

Guests can also find seasonal outdoor pool with ozonised water, children's playground, children's playroom and game room.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including skiing, cycling.
