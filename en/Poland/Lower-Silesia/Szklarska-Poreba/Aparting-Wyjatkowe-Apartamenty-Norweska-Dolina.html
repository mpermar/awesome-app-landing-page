Description

Fitted with a terrace, the units feature a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a hair dryer. A dishwasher, a microwave and toaster are also offered, as well as a electric tea pot.

Aparting Wyjątkowe Apartamenty - Norweska Dolina offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

Aparting Wyjątkowe Apartamenty - Norweska Dolina offers a range of wellness facilities including a hot tub, a sauna and a hammam. For guests' convenience, Aparting Wyjątkowe Apartamenty - Norweska Dolina has a business center with printing, copying and fax services.

A playground is available on site and both skiing and cycling can be enjoyed within close proximity of the property.

Popular points of interest near Aparting Wyjątkowe Apartamenty - Norweska Dolina include Death Turn, Szklarska Poreba Bus Station and Ziemi Juna Museum.
