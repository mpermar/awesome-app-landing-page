Description

Spacious and elegant, all villas include air conditioning and a flat-screen TV. Guests can lounge at the private deck, in the comfortable living room, or even by the private pool in some villas. Semi-outdoor bathrooms have a bathtub and rain shower.

Saree Samui is a 20 minute drive from shopping and dining at popular Chaweng Beach. It is a 10 minute drive from Samui International Airport.

Saree Rarom Spa provides relaxing herbal steam baths, Thai massage and morning yoga sessions. The staff at the tour desk can help arrange day trips to Angthong Marine National Park, the Crocodile Farm and The Samui Aquarium.

Surya Chandra Restaurant serves local and international dishes, with the choice of al fresco beachfront dining. Drinks can be enjoyed by the Beach Bar.
