Description

The suites and villas at Kirikayan are equipped with a flat-screen TV, DVD player and free Wi-Fi. They also come with a separate living area, dining area and kitchenette. Room service is available.

A fitness room is available for guests looking to maintain their regular work-outs. For activities and day trips, the helpful staff at Kirikayan Luxury Pool Villas can make arrangements. Free private parking is provided.

Sankala Restaurant serves Thai and international food with fresh seafood. Coffee and cocktails are served at the Lobby bar.

Kirikayan Luxury Pool Villas & Spa provides a free shuttle service to Chaweng beach and the hotel's Beach Club located at Kirikayan Boutique Resort. A free shuttle service to Maenam Walking Street is also provided every Thursday.
