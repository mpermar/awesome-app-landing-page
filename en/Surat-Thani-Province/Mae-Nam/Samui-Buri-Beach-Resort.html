Description

The resort features villas with private pools, as well as rooms built around the main pool. All rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV, a DVD player and a safe. The bathrooms have a shower and a double vanity sink.

The Samui Buri Beach Resort is a 25-minute drive from Samui Airport and a 20-minute drive from Nathon Pier. The center of Chaweng Beach is a 25-minute drive away.

Guests can enjoy a relaxing massage at the spa, or exercise in the fitness center. A kids club features activities for younger guests, and adults can take Thai cooking classes. The resort also has a tour desk and a business center.

International dishes are served at the open-air Seetawaree Restaurant, where meals are prepared with fresh ingredients from the resort gardens. Drinks are available at 3 bars.
