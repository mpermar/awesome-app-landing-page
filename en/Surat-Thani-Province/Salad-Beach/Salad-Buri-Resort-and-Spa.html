Description

Just over half a mile from Haad Yao Beach, Salad Buri Resort & Spa is 17 miles from Samui International Airport. A shuttle service from Koh Samui Airport and Koh Phangan’s Thongsala Pier are available for a fee.

Fitted with hardwood floors, the Thai-style accommodations have air conditioning and a flat-screen TV with a DVD player. Some rooms include a private pool.

Fresh seafood, Thai and International dishes are served at Paradise Restaurant, which offers views of Haad Salad Beach. Cocktails are available at the Pool Bar.

Guests can make travel arrangements at the resort’s tour desk, or enjoy water sports like diving and snorkeling on the beach.
