Description

The resort will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms with a shower, free toiletries, a TV with satellite channels and DVD-Player. The private bathroom is equipped with a hair dryer. At Samui Le Reve every room has a seating area.

Continental and à la carte breakfast options are available daily at the accommodation.

Guests at Samui Le Reve will be able to enjoy activities in and around Laem Set Beach, like snorkeling.

Samui Butterfly Garden is 1.9 miles from the resort. The nearest airport is Samui International Airport, 10 miles from Samui Le Reve.
