Description

Located from the crowds of Samui, ShaSa Resort & Residences, Koh Samui offers shuttles to the Chaweng and Lamai beaches. It is a 45 minute drive from Samui International Airport.

Bright and airy, well-designed suites feature modern interiors with fully furnished kitchens. They come with a flat-screen cable TV, laundry facilities and spacious bathrooms with a large bathtub.

Guests can take relaxing dips in the hydro-therapy pool or get a Thai massage at the spa. The resort also houses fitness facilities, a library and game room.

Beyond The Sea Siamese Brasserie offers a spread of fine Thai and international dishes. It also features an extensive wine list and gourmet coffees.
