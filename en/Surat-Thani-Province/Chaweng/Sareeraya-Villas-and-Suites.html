Description

The Sareeraya Villas & Suites is a short walk from the main stretch of shops, restaurants and bars on Chaweng Beach. It is about 3 miles from Fisherman’s Village.

Decorated in a modern Oriental style, the suites and villas at Sareeraya feature flat-screen TVs and DVD players. Some rooms have an outdoor hot tub while some have a private plunge pool.

Guests can enjoy relaxing massage services at Raya Spa or by the pool. The hotel has a fitness center, a library, and a transit lounge.

Buffet breakfast is served at Chef’s Table restaurant, which also offers local, international and fresh seafood dishes. Cocktails and refreshing drinks are available at the hotel’s 2 bars.
