Description

Centara Grand Beach Resort Samui is a 20-minute drive from Samui International Airport and Samui ferry pier. The Namuang Waterfall located 6 miles away provides good trekking routes.

Well-lit rooms feature air conditioning and private bathrooms with a hairdryer. A minibar and a tea/coffee maker are included, along with a spacious sitting area. Room service is provided.

Guests of Centara Grand Beach Resort can exercise in the fitness center or play a game of tennis. Other facilities include a sauna and a tour desk. A dive center on Chaweng Beach offers various water sports and equipment.

Dining options at Centara include Japanese, Thai and Western specialties. Other options include Zico’s, which serves Brazilian grilled food accompanied with live music and entertainment and Piccolo, which features pastries and snacks. Centara houses 3 bars.
