Description

The resort’s spacious rooms feature handmade furniture, hardwood floors and stone sinks. Each room has a living area with a daybed. Modern amenities include a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and free Wi-Fi access.

Hansar Samui’s H-Bistro serves local and Western dishes and seafood. To sip drinks with views of the sea, guests can head to the resort’s Pool and Beach Bar or the Chill Lounge.

Guests of Hansar Samui can work out at the fitness center or enjoy body treatments at the spa. A tour desk can assist with travel and sightseeing arrangements.

Hansar Samui Resort & Spa is a 10-minute drive from Koh Samui Airport.
