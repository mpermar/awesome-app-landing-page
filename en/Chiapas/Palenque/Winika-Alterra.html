Description

At Winíka Alterra, rooms come with a terrace. Winíka Alterra provides some accommodations that include mountain views, and all rooms include a private bathroom and a closet.

A à la carte breakfast is served every morning at the property.

A variety of popular activities are available in the area around the property, including cycling and hiking.

Staff are available to help at the 24-hour front desk.

02/07/2018

In my mind, you couldn’t improve upon this place. Easily the best hotel (but not the most expensive)

"I loved everything about it but specifically:-the family owners, sweet smiling staff who were attentive but didn’t over hover. When we got our bill for 3 days including meals and drinks, there was not an extra charge on it. The family makes a commitment to the environment with solar panels, composting, and sustainable building. Bravo!-the casitas,pool, and grounds. Simple, elegant and not fussy. The pool has a natural plant (separate) filtration system so the water is clean and clear but no chlorine smell. Lovely hibiscus, bougainvillea and other planting’s make for a peaceful setting. - the location. Enough out of town to feel like you were part of the jungle but close enough for easy access by taxi or car. First time in during this visit to Mexico I didn’t hear Road noise. Wonderful quiet interrupted only by howler monkeys, parrots and occasional squeal of delight by children in the pool. -the Food! Where do I start. The best fruit plate in Mexico, really. Beef, chicken and lamb from the on grounds farm. Fresh salad also sourced from the farm. My husband had the pork ribs 3 nights in a row. Cause he couldn’t get over how good they were. I LOVED this place. You can really tell when every detail has been given a lot of thought."
