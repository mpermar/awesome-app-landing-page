Description

The units at the Choromar include a balcony or terrace, a full kitchen and a living area. In the apartments, the living area is separate from the bedroom.

Guests can relax in the sauna and hot tub after working out at the well-equipped gym. There is also a spacious terrace where guests can sunbathe while enjoying a refreshing drink.

The rustic restaurant serves authentic regional dishes prepared with seasonal ingredients for lunch and dinner. In the evenings, the Choromar features live music as well as a Karaoke Bar.

The Choromar Resort also has 2 swimming pools for children and a game room with a snooker table. Other facilities include an airport shuttle and currency exchange.

Local bus stops are available right outside the Choromar.
