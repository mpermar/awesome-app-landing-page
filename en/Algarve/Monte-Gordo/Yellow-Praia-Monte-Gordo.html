Description

All suites at Yellow Monte Gordo Beach Hotel come with satellite TV, a minibar and safe. Every room has a private bathroom which includes a hairdryer.

A buffet breakfast is served daily. The spacious restaurant has indoor and outdoor dining areas, and specializes in Algarve cuisine. The well-stocked bar provides a range of drinks all day.

Featuring a hot tub and sauna, the property also has a 24-hour front desk and an entertainment program all year round. For golfers there are several courses nearby, including Castro Marim, 6 miles away.

Casino Monte Gordo is 1,000 feet away and Faro International Airport is a 40 minute drive away. Underground parking and bike rental are available.
