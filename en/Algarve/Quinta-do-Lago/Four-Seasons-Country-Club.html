Description

The rooms at the resort come with air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kitchenette, a dining area and a private bathroom with a bath or shower. Four Seasons Country Club has some rooms that feature a balcony, and the rooms include a coffee machine. Guest rooms have a closet.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental breakfast.

Four Seasons Country Club offers 4-star accommodations with a hammam and indoor pool. You can play ping pong at the resort.

Guests can rent a car or take advantage of the business center.

Albufeira is 13 miles from Four Seasons Country Club, while Faro is 5 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Faro Airport, 3.1 miles from the accommodation.
