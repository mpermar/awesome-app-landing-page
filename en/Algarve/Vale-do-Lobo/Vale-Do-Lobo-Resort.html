Description

Vale do Lobo shopping center includes a beauty center, a newsstand, a bank and several boutiques, including Gant and Henry Cotton’s. A grocery store and a fine wine shop are available on site.

Dining options at Vale do Lobo Resort range from French to Italian to seafood specialties. Many restaurants are located on the beach, and there is a crêperie providing light lunches and snacks. There is also a Chinese restaurant and a café located near the tennis courts.

The famous Royal and Ocean Golf Courses are within easy walking distance of the resort. Spinning and yoga classes, as well as several water and beach sports are available on site, along with a state-of-the-art gym, including a hammam.

Different accommodation options are available at Vale do Lobo. Some villas include a private pool or a hot tub. The resort provides free use of the seasonal Rotunda swimming pool, which is open from June to September.
