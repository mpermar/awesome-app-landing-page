Description

Country Club villas have a living room with a TV and DVD player. The modern kitchens include large cupboards for storage, a microwave and an electric teakettle.

Guests can enjoy international cuisine at Vistas Restaurant, which features vaulted ceilings and views of the ocean and the 18th hole.

Monte Rei Golf & Country Club is a 12-minute drive from sandy beaches and is 11 miles from Tavira. Free private parking is available on site.
