Description

Relax at the resort's Satsanga Spa and spa center, which includes a gym, a sauna, a steam bath and a massage room (for a fee).

There is an indoor swimming pool as well as a huge outdoor swimming pool in a 12,917 ft² garden. Gourmet cuisine is served in the restaurants, and a Kids' Club and 3 tennis courts complete the picture.

All air-conditioned rooms feature modern audiovisual equipment and are beautifully designed.

The beaches around Lagos are some of the most beautiful of the Algarve. This former fishing village has a long history and is now a lively holiday resort with a beautiful new marina. The area provides great opportunities for water sports, including windsurfing, scuba diving, water skiing and fishing.

The hotel includes a convention center, with a choice of several conference rooms for meetings, presentations or banquets.
