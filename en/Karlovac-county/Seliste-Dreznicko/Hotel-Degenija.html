Description

The rooms at Degenija Hotel feature a modern interior with dark wooden furniture and parquet floors. All rooms have a work desk, a minibar, and a private bathroom with a bathtub.

Degenija’s restaurant offers traditional Croatian dishes as well as international cuisine. The restaurant’s specialty is freshly caught trout.

For those who want to explore the hotel and the beauty of its natural surroundings, Hotel Degenija offers rental bicycles. Children can have fun in the playground. Free private parking is available on site.
