Description

Luxurious rooms at the resort feature spacious layouts. Fitted with hardwood floors, they offer views of the grounds or sea. Modern comforts include a TV with cable channels and a minibar.

Massages and body treatments are offered at the Spa Village. Guests can enjoy a game of tennis or have a quiet read in Tanjong Jara Resort’s library.

Traditional Malaysian cuisine is served at the Di Atas Sungei Restaurant. The Nelayan Restaurant specializes in seafood and a variety of local and Western meals. Teratai Terrace serves cocktails by the poolside, accompanied with the sweet strains of live gamelan music.

Located on Malaysia’s East Coast, Tanjong Jara Resort is a 15-minute drive from Desa Golf Club, Dungun. The Kuala Terengganu Airport is a one hour drive away.
