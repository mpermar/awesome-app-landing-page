Description

Parque Cristóbal Gran Canaria is a complete holiday complex located in the center of Playa del Inglés, a short distance from the incredible beaches of southern Gran Canaria.

It offers spacious bungalows, sun throughout the year, tropical gardens and 2 giant outdoor swimming pools for adults with air conditioning in the winter months.

Children can have fun in the supervised mini club or in the 2 children's pools. Daytime and evening entertainment programs are also organized for the little ones.

The complex has an outdoor hot tub, a tennis court and its own gym.

The apartments have a private terrace and a well-equipped kitchen.
