Description

Featuring hardwood floors and well-crafted furnishings, the open-concept villas feature views of surrounding greenery or the lagoon. Each well-equipped villa has amenities like a flat-screen TV, iPod docking and free Wi-Fi.

Resort Constance Halaveli is a 35 minute seaplane ride and a 75 minute ride from Male. Airport transportation is provided.

To relax, guests can indulge in a massage at the spa, or go on fishing and diving trips. Regular workouts can be maintained at the well-equipped fitness center.

JAHAZ Restaurant serves international dishes and is available for breakfast and dinner. Other dining options include fresh seafood at MEERU and Asian food at JING.
