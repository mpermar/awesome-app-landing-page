Description

Set within pretty gardens, the spacious rooms at Hotel Museo Casa Santo Domingo feature elegant décor, a fireplace and scenic views. Plasma cable TV, a safe, minibar and private bathroom all feature.

The gourmet El Tenedor restaurant serves breakfast and traditional cuisine and guests can enjoy snacks and refreshments on the terrace from the El Cabido bar.

Casa Santo Domingo offers guided tours of its attractions, which include a pharmacy, colonial art and archeological museum. There is a pottery and wax workshop, a chapel, and Antigua´s largest jade factory and museum.

Car rental is available and Hotel Museo Casa Santo Domingo offers free private parking and daily airport shuttles for a fee. It is 28 miles from Guatemala City.
