Description

Zapote Tree Inn is located 16 minutes’ drive from Santa Elena, Guatemala. This property offers an on-site restaurant, car rental services, a games room and a library.

The functional and simple accommodations feature a desk and mosquito nets. The private, full bathroom includes free toiletries.

Zapo0te Tree Inn also has a garden, 24-hour front desk service and a shared lounge.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling.

The property offers free parking. Mundo Maya International Airport is 15 minutes’ drive from the property, as well as Metroplaza Mundo Maya Shopping Mall.
