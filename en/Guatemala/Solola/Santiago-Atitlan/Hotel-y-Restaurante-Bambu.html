Description

Located on Lake Atitlan, Hotel y Restaurante Bambu is located in Santiago Atitlán.

This secluded, tropical-style property also offers guests free kayak rental. The rooms at this property feature a wardrobe and private bathrooms, while the bungalow is equipped with a private terrace with lake and river views.

Guests at Hotel y Restaurante Bambu enjoy local and international cuisine, and other dining options can be found within 10 km.

There is also a fully stocked bar at the property. Atitlan Volcano is only 10 km from the property, and the hotel’s tour desk can help arrange outings on the lake.
