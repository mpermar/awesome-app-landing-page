Description

Each guest room at The Common Man Inn & Spa features a flat-screen cable TV and free Wi-Fi access. They are warmly decorated with rich colors and wood furnishings.

Guests can relax at The Common Man Spa and enjoy massage services, facials, salon services, and yoga classes. A fitness center, indoor pool, and hot tub are also provided.

Foster’s Boiler Room Restaurant serves American classics, like hearty New England chowder, while the Rise and Shine cafe offers breakfast options.

Highland Links Golf Club is 3 miles from the Plymouth Common Man Inn. Waterville Valley skiing is 19 miles away.
