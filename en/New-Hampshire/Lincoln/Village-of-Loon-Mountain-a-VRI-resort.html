Description

Guests staying at the Village of Loon Mountain can enjoy activities like swimming in the outdoor pools, relaxing in the hot tubs or playing games in the game room. There is also a basketball court, tennis court and fitness center.

Featuring a balcony with views of the mountains, the rooms at the Loon Mountain Village also have a sitting area with a sofa bed and a fireplace. The kitchen includes an oven, refrigerator and microwave.

Hiking trails are near the property and kayaking on the East Branch Pemigewasset River is 3 miles away.

"The unit was extremely clean and everything was in great repair. The kids were thrilled with their room and my husband and I slept very well on that comfortable king-sized bed. Meanwhile, my boys had a blast playing basketball in the pool and at the court and all of us enjoyed the hot tubs and the sauna. We also had fun in the game room with a competitive game of billiards and in the arcade. My kids couldn't believe that I could play (and actually get into) a video game; little did they know that I take my Galaga very seriously. There was so much to do onsite, we decided not to visit the tacky touristy places, but stayed at the resort and enjoyed the mountain views and just time spent together as a family. The kitchen had all the tools I needed to whip up some meals with food bought at the 24-hour market only a few minutes from the resort. We felt comfortable and completely at home.In addition, the staff was wonderful; polite and friendly.
