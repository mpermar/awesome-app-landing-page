Description

All rooms in the hotel are equipped with a TV with satellite channels. Featuring a private bathroom, rooms at Hacienda Santa Cristina also provide guests with a garden view. The rooms feature a desk.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental breakfast.

Hacienda Santa Cristina has a playground. You can play tennis at the hotel.

The nearest airport is La Florida Airport, 45 miles from Hacienda Santa Cristina.
