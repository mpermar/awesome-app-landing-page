Description

At the hotel, every room is equipped with a closet and a flat-screen TV. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with free toiletries, while some rooms have a seating area.

A buffet breakfast is served each morning at the property. Guests can dine in the in-house restaurant, which specializes in Argentinian and steakhouse cuisine.

Primera Hacienda Hotel Boutique has a terrace. You can play minigolf at this 4-star hotel, and bike rental is available.

Speaking French, English and Spanish, staff are ready to help around the clock at the reception.

President Gabriel González Videla Regional History Museum is a 7-minute walk from the accommodation. The nearest airport is La Florida Airport, 3.1 miles from the property.
