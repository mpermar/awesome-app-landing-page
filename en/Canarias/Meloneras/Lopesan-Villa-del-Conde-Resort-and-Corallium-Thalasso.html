Description

Lopesan is 800 meters from Maspalomas Nature Reserve. This huge complex and spa has garden areas and spacious rooms.

All rooms are elegantly decorated in bright colors. They have air conditioning, balcony or terrace, satellite TV and minibar.

There are 5 restaurants, including a buffet restaurant, one with Mediterranean cuisine and the other Italian. The Alpendre Salt Steakhouse serves a large salad buffet, while the Patio Café offers cakes and crepes. The complex has 6 bars, one of which offers live music.

The spa occupies 1,200 m² and is on the seafront. It has Turkish baths, African sauna, flotation pool and hydrothermal circuit. It offers a wide variety of treatments, for a fee.
