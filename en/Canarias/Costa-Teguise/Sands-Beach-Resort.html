Description

The Sands Beach Resort is located in Costa Teguise. These apartments surround 6 places that have their own pool and offer direct access to the beach.

The resort offers a private lagoon pool and a 25-meter pool with 8 streets. On the other side of the bridge, at the Paradise Island Complex, there is a heated pool.

The hotel restaurant offers international meals. The pool bar, Mai Tai, serves snacks and barbecues. Free WiFi connection is provided.

The Sands Beach Resort has a modern health and fitness center and a Get Active corner, with detailed information on cycling and racing routes. The resort organizes the international marathon of Lanzarote every year.

The apartments include a balcony or terrace with sun loungers, dining table and kitchen with kettle and toaster.

There is also a full day and night entertainment program, kids club, tennis court, games room and playground. Massages and beauty treatments are available for a fee.
