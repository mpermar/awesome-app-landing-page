Description

The suites are air-conditioned and have a balcony overlooking the Atlantic Ocean or the mountains. There is a living area with a sofa bed, a safe and a flat-screen satellite TV. They have a fridge, toaster and coffee maker. Microwaves are provided upon request.

The HD Beach Resort has 2 restaurants. It also has a bar and cafeteria and offers a packed lunch service upon request.

The complex has free Wi-Fi and water sports facilities. There is a nightclub and entertainment staff. You will find free parking nearby and you can rent a car or a bicycle at the tour desk. An airport shuttle service is offered for a fee.
