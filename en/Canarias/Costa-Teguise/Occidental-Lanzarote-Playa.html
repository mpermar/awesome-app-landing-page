Description

Occidental Lanzarote Playa has an outdoor pool. There is a golf course 4 km away.

The rooms are modern and feature air conditioning, a flat-screen satellite TV, a small refrigerator and a private bathroom with a hairdryer. Some also have a private terrace with sea views.

The accommodation is a short drive from the island's capital, Arrecife, and just 12 km from Lanzarote Airport.
