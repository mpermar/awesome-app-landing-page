Description

The bright and bright apartments of Cay Beach have 1 or 2 bedrooms. You can prepare your own meal in the well-equipped kitchen, and then eat with your friends.

You can sunbathe in the sun of Lanzarote all year, and then cool off in one of the 2 swimming pools. Children will have a great time and make friends in the park and the children's pool.

Enjoy entertainment with the great program at Cay Beach, with plenty of activities for the whole family.

You can enjoy the outdoors and stay active with a game of tennis, and then relax with a soda at the bar. Enjoy a light snack at the bar.

If you wish, you can enjoy an afternoon reading a good book or napping in the shade of the palm trees of Cay Beach.
