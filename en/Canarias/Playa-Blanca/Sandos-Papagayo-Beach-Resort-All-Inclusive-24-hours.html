Description

The rooms at Sandos Papagayo Beach Resort - All Inclusive have satellite TV and air conditioning. The safe and the minibar can be rented.

At Sandos Papagayo Beach Resort - All Inclusive there are 5 bars and a quiet terrace overlooking Fuerteventura and Isla de Lobos. The hotel also houses an international buffet, a pizzeria, an oriental restaurant / Tex-Mex and a poolside bar.

The on-site spa includes hot tubs, Finnish saunas and Turkish baths. There is also a gym, mini golf course and sports court.

The hotel staff organizes sports, recreational and night entertainment activities. Children have a playground, children's pools and children's club.

The hotel is 600 meters from Papagayo Beach, 4 km from the fishing town of Playa Blanca and 20 km from Timanfaya National Park.
