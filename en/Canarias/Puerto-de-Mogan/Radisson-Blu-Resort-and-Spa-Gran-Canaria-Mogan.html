Description

All rooms and suites at Radisson Blu Resort & Spa, Gran Canaria Mogan are spacious and have air conditioning, large terrace or balcony and modern décor. They have a seating area with satellite TV, free Wi-Fi and a bathroom with bathrobe and slippers.

The spa is equipped with an indoor pool with hydromassage, hot tub, sauna and Turkish bath. Massages and beauty treatments are offered.

The Larder, the main restaurant, offers themed buffet dinners with a previous show. The Filini à la carte restaurant offers Italian cuisine. Drinks and snacks are served in the lounge and pool bar, Il Taglio.

At the Radisson Blu Resort & Spa, Mogan, Gran Canaria there is also a climbing tower, tennis court, volleyball court and running track.

In addition, you can practice various activities in the surroundings, such as snorkeling, diving and cycling. Radisson Blu Resort & Spa, Mogan, Gran Canaria is 18 km from Maspalomas and 46 km from Las Palmas de Gran Canaria. Gran Canaria Airport, the nearest airport, is 38 km from Radisson Blu Resort & Spa, Mogan, Gran Canaria.
