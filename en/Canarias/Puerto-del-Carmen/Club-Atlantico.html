Description

The apartments are oriented to the southeast and have a balcony or terrace overlooking the pool, living room with sofa bed, bathroom, kitchenette with hob, toaster, microwave and kettle and satellite TV with a selection of international channels.

Club Atlántico has a club next to the pool and sun loungers. On Fridays a barbecue is organized for all guests and some nights live shows are offered. The reception staff speaks several languages, organizes excursions and offers car and bicycle rental.

Puerto del Carmen is famous for its restaurants, bars and shops. The property is a 10-minute drive from Lanzarote Airport.
