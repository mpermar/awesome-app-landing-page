Description

The rooms at Seaside Los Jameos Playa are spacious and decorated with soft tones and light wood furniture. All rooms have air conditioning, a balcony and satellite TV. Minibars and safes can be rented.

The main restaurant has a nice covered terrace and serves varied buffet dinners, with theme nights and barbecue. The Belingo Bar offers an entertaining entertainment program with live music and dancing.

Los Jameos offers daily activities for adults and children, such as miniature golf and beach volleyball, and there are also games and children's activities. There are several bars, restaurants and shops within 10 minutes walk.

The complex is a 10-minute walk from Matagorda and 2 km from the center of Puerto del Carmen. Lanzarote Arrecife Airport is 5 km away and there is a golf course less than 3 km away.
