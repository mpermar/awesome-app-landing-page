Description

Rooms at Sol Lanzarote - All Inclusive are bright and functional, equipped with a 39-inch TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer. Some include a microwave, refrigerator and kettle.

The Sol Lanzarote - All Inclusive has an international buffet restaurant and a tapas and sushi bar. Drinks are served in the pool bar and lobby bars. The establishment organizes an entertainment program for adults and children over 5 years old. There is also a children's club and an adventure playground.

The all-inclusive rate includes breakfast, lunch, dinner and snacks.

The hotel is 3 km from Puerto del Carmen and 5 km from Lanzarote airport. Public parking is free. The Sun's reception is open 24 hours a day and provides car rental and bicycle rental.
