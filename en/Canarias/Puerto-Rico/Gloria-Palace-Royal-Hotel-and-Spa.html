Description

The popular town of Puerto Rico is a 15-minute walk away and houses many shops, bars and restaurants. Gran Canaria Airport is a 40-minute drive from the hotel.

The rooms are modern and have air conditioning and a balcony with sea views. They are also equipped with satellite TV, bathrobe, slippers and free Wi-Fi, available throughout the establishment.

The Gloria Palace Royal's spa features an indoor pool, hot tub and gym. There is also a ping pong table at the property.

06/04/2018

Fantastic beach holiday

"This is an amazing place if you wish to rest, eat well and chill out. We had all inclusive which was fantastic and we did not get fed up with it, which is rare. Selection of food and drink is very good, there is something to eat all day. The hotel is comfortable, there is a lift to the beach. The gym is well equipped, we enjoyed working out every day. The pool seems small but it is huge and is mostly empty although we were there at half term. The spa is good, we had a child (13) and he could only attend twice a day, but it was more than enough. I think the hotel is best with older children, although there were some families with babies. The pool I thought was better suited for older children but this is my personal opinion. There is a baby pool too. The beach is around 5 minutes walk, beautiful and best in the mornings, it is not crowded at all, it gets busier around 2 pm. If you want a quiet holiday, chilling out at the pool and beach and good food this is the place. However if you are looking for action and a lot to do or want to do shopping and walk on the beach at night then maybe another resort would suit you better. It is quiet at night. We went to two excursions by taxi and enjoyed it, but mostly we stayed in. There is animation with some activities, but it is not as noisy and disturbing as in other hotels. You can also ask for a late checkout if you flight is at night, which was very comfortable for us. The staff is amazing, always smiling, very happy and assertive."
