Description

The hotel provides a free shuttle service to the popular town of Playa de las Américas, 4 km away. In addition, the Costa Adeje golf course is less than 2 km away. The hotel also offers golf packages and special green fees for some courses.

The luxurious Despacio spa center features a heated indoor pool, gym and Turkish bath, and offers beauty and health treatments. In addition, a complete sports program and other activities are provided.

The H10 Costa Adeje Palace has numerous places to eat, both buffet style and à la carte. The hotel also has a poolside bar, a barbecue and a piano bar.
