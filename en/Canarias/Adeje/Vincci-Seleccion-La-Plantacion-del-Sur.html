Description

The rooms at the Vincci Selección La Plantación del Sur are elegantly decorated and have a balcony and sea views. All rooms have interactive flat-screen TVs, plus separate bathtubs and showers.

The spa at Vincci Selección La Plantation of the South, the Nammu, includes thermal circuits. Massages and beauty treatments are also offered.

Duque Beach is just 500 meters away. The hotel has a fitness center. There is also a car and bicycle rental service.

The Vincci La Plantation of the South has 2 restaurants. One of the restaurants serves creative Canarian dishes made with locally sourced products and the other specializes in grilled meats. The hotel breakfast includes alternatives for coeliacs. There is also a bar available to guests.

Vincci Selección La Plantation of the South is a 20-minute drive from Reina Sofía Airport.
