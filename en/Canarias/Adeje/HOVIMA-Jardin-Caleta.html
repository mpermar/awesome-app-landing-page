Description

The apartments at HOVIMA Jardín Caleta are bright, have a furnished balcony overlooking the pool or the sea and are equipped with a lounge with a sofa bed, satellite TV and a hire safe, as well as a kitchenette equipped with a hob , kettle and toaster.

The hotel has a buffet restaurant with barbecue and a sun terrace bar by the pool. The sports bar offers evening entertainment and has a pool table and darts.

The Caleta Garden has a park and a children's club and a ping pong table. One of the pools is heated during winter. The property also offers car and bicycle rental. The Costa Adeje golf complex is 1 km away.

HOVIMA Jardín Caleta offers an airport shuttle service for a fee. Tenerife South Airport is 25 km away.
