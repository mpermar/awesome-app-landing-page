Description

The establishment has a gym, tennis courts, spa, playground and karaoke.

The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, private balcony, TV and private bathroom with hairdryer.

Playa de las Américas is a 20-minute drive away and hosts a variety of shops and places to eat. Tenerife South Airport is a 20-minute drive away.
