Description

The rooms at the Grand Tacande are spacious, elegantly decorated in bright colors and enjoy views of the surrounding mountains or the hotel gardens. Some of them enjoy sea views. Gold Level rooms offer access to the private San Sebastian Exclusive Plaza area, which includes an infinity pool and 8 hot tubs.

The Zurrón buffet restaurant serves international and themed cuisine, while the Bocana grill specializes in Mediterranean dishes. Both restaurants have a terrace overlooking the sea. In addition, the Gran Tacande has a cocktail bar.

The hotel spa has hydrotherapy, massage service and various beauty treatments for a fee. There is also a free gym and a hairdressing salon.

The hotel is located in the immediate vicinity of the Playa de Fañabé urbanization, which has several shops and bars. The Gran Tacande is also a 15-minute drive from the Costa Adeje golf complex.
