Description

The Eurostars suites are modern and have a well-equipped kitchen.

The hotel has a buffet restaurant, gym, tennis court, 2 squash courts and a children's playground.

Eurostars Las Salinas is less than 4 km from Fuerteventura Golf Club and a 20-minute drive from Puerto del Rosario. Fuerteventura airport is about 10 minutes by car.
