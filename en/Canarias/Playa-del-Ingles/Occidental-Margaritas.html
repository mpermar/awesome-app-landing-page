Description

Occidental Margaritas offers rooms and apartments. All units have tiled floors and simple décor. They are equipped with air conditioning and satellite TV. The apartments are also equipped with a hob, coffee maker and microwave.

The hotel has a buffet restaurant serving international cuisine. There are also 4 bars, of which one is also a disco and 2 are located next to the pool. Guests can enjoy a day and night entertainment program.

The establishment has outdoor training facilities, sauna, hairdresser and a wellness center where health and beauty treatments are carried out.

Occidental Margaritas offers a free shuttle bus service to the beaches of Maspalomas and Playa del Inglés. The reception is open 24 hours a day. There is a car and bicycle rental service.

The property is a 20-minute walk from Maspalomas Golf Course and about 25 minutes on foot from the beach.
