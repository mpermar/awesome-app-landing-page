Description

Seaside Sandy Beach offers spacious rooms with air conditioning, satellite TV and furnished balconies. Upon request, hair dryers and bathrobes are provided.

The establishment has an outdoor gym that has wonderful sea views. There is also hammam, sauna and massage service. If you wish, you can rent the hot tub on the private terrace.

Sandy Beach has an air-conditioned buffet restaurant that serves meals on the beautiful garden terrace. The hotel also has a cafeteria next to the pool and a bakery. In the afternoon coffee and cakes are served.

The hotel is in the center of Playa del Inglés, a 10-minute walk from the popular Yumbo shopping center. The Maspalomas golf course is 1.5 km away.
