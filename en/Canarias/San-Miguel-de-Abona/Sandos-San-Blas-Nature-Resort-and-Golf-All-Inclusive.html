Description

The all-inclusive San Blas has spacious rooms with contemporary design. All rooms have a balcony and hot tub.

Facilities include a terrace and a small spa with Turkish bath, available for a fee. The hotel also offers a nightly entertainment program and has a disco.

Meals can be enjoyed at the Cueva Atxona or La Proa buffet restaurants. The complex also has 4 bars, including one next to the pool.

Sandos San Blas Nature Resort & Golf - All Inclusive has beautiful gardens and is well connected to a 800,000 m² nature reserve. There are also tennis and volleyball courts. In addition, the hotel provides a free shuttle service to Playa de las Américas, which leaves the establishment twice a day.
