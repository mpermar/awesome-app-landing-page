Description

It has elegant apartments with colonial-style décor with a balcony, terrace or garden. The living room has a ceiling fan, sofa bed, cable TV and CD and DVD player. The bathroom includes toiletries and a hairdryer. The kitchenette is equipped with a microwave and coffee maker. You can rent safes.

The hotel has a games room with ping pong table. There is an area with Wi-Fi connection and massage services are provided for a fee.

The reception is open 24 hours a day and offers car rental and currency exchange. The hotel parking has an additional cost. Tenerife South Airport is 10 minutes away by car.
