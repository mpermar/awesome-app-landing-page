Description

Rooms at Hotel Mediterranean Palace feature traditional white and blue Canarian décor and feature air conditioning, a private balcony, satellite TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The Mare Nostrum Resort's buffet restaurant offers international cuisine, while the Vitamina Bar serves fresh juices and light snacks. The Safari Mall is nearby and houses shops and restaurants.

The establishment has a hairdressing salon, boutique shop, large children's area and entertainment program for children.

Las Américas golf course and Siam water park are less than 10 minutes' drive from the Mediterranean Palace. Tenerife South Airport is about 15 minutes away by car.
