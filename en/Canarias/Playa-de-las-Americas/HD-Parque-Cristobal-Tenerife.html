Description

Enjoy a fun and quiet family vacation on this sunny Canary Island. Parque Cristóbal Tenerife has tropical gardens that surround two large outdoor swimming pools for adults, both heated in winter.

Children can have fun in the mini club (guarded) or in the two children's pools. You can also keep fit by playing tennis on the tennis court or exercising in the gym.

All apartments open onto a private terrace and have a full kitchen.
