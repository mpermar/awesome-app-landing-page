Description

The apartments at the Regency Country Club Apartments Suites are spacious and feature an Asian-style design with marble and stone finishes. They have air conditioning, fully equipped kitchenette, bathroom and four-poster bed in the bedrooms.

The hotel restaurant serves breakfast, lunch and dinner. Various types of drinks and snacks can be taken at the bar next to the pool.

The Regency Country Club Tenerife has a gym and massage service available upon request.

The complex is about 4 km from Los Cristianos beach and Playa de las Américas.
