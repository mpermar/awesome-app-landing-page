Description

The rooms are warmly decorated and feature a fireplace with a seating area, a minibar, cable TV and a private bathroom.

Guests can relax in the spa or in the TV room, which has the latest technology. The Pousada Spa Mirante da Colyna also offers pool tables, located next to the restaurant bar.

The restaurant serves contemporary cuisine, fondue and soups. On the other hand, the buffet breakfast includes a wide variety of freshly made breads, sausages and juices.

This hotel is located 500 meters from an ice skating rink and Monte Verde Avenue, the main one in the area.
