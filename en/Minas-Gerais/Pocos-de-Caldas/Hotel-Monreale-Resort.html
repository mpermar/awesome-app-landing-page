Description

The accommodation features satellite TV, air conditioning, mountain and pool views and a minibar. The private bathroom includes a shower, hairdryer and free toiletries.

The Hotel Monreale Resort has a spa and wellness center, restaurant, tennis court, entertainment staff, disco and meeting facilities. In the immediate vicinity or on the property you can do a variety of activities, such as hiking and water sports in a park. There is free parking.

The property is 6.6 km from Cristo Redentor de Poços de Caldas and 4.1 km from Water World theme park.
