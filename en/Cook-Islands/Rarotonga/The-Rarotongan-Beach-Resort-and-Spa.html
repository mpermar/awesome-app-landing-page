Description

The Rarotongan Beach Resort has a range of accommodations, from rooms in tropical gardens to beachfront suites. All have a balcony or courtyard, air conditioning, DVD player and refrigerator.

Guests can stay in the honeymoon bungalow, which has its own private swimming pool. The SpaPolynesia offers aromatherapy massages, body wraps, relaxing facials, manicures and pedicures.

Te Vaka Restaurant is on the waters edge and features themed nights and cultural shows. Captain Andy's Beach Bar serves drinks and casual meals by the pool. Free kids club activities are available.

The Aroa Lagoon Marine Reserve is located right in front of the resort, and offers some of the best snorkeling in the Cook Islands. Rarotonga Airport is less than a 10-minute drive away.

Rutaki is a great choice for travelers interested in sunsets, beaches and beach walks.
