Description

The rooms at the resort come with air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a kitchen, a dining area and a private bathroom with free toiletries, a bath or shower and a hair dryer. Rooms include a coffee machine, while some have a balcony. All rooms include a closet.

Port Ferdinand offers a continental or Full English/Irish breakfast.

The accommodation has a playground. Guests at Port Ferdinand Marina and Luxury Residences will be able to enjoy activities in and around Saint Peter, like snorkeling.

Staff at the reception can provide information on what to do in the area.
