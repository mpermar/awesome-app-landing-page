Description

Elegantly furnished, each spacious room includes a flat-screen TV, free Wi-Fi, and a refrigerator. The private bathroom features free deluxe toiletries and a hairdryer. Some rooms offer views of the golf course or corner balconies.

Guests can play games of tennis on the outdoor tennis courts, or relax in the hotel’s hot tub, steam room, or sauna. The swimming pool also features massage and whirlpool seats. Ciúin Spa offers relaxing Elemis treatments, with a range of massages, spa rituals, and therapies.

Setanta Restaurant serves modern European dishes in an atmosphere of Italian marble floors and leather furnishings. Conall Cearnach Restaurant serves fine Irish cuisine, and Summit Restaurant overlooks the golf course with panoramic views, providing a range of light meals.

The hotel’s fantastic 18-hole course incorporates the hotel’s beautiful lakes, and beginners can use the club’s PGA academy, with golf lessons from PGA professional coaches.

There is also a game room for guests, including air hockey, a pool table, arcade games and foosball. There is a soft play area for kids, as well as a kids club during peak season times.
