Description

The units come with tiled floors and feature a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with bath and free toiletries. There's a seating and/or dining area in some units.

A continental, buffet or à la carte breakfast is available daily at the property.

The lodge has an outdoor pool.

Manna Resorts provides a playground.

Harare Botanical Gardens is 8 miles from the accommodation, while Avondale Shopping Centre is 8 miles away. The nearest airport is Robert Gabriel Mugabe International Airport, 13 miles from the property.
