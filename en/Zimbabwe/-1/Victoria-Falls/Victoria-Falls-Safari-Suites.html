Description

Guest rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area and a flat-screen TV. Every room is equipped with a private bathroom, and certain accommodations at Victoria Falls Safari Suites have a terrace. All guest rooms will provide guests with a desk and a coffee machine.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental breakfast. The restaurant at Victoria Falls Safari Suites specializes in African and international cuisine.

The hotel has an outdoor pool.

The reception can provide advice on the area in order to help guests plan their day.

David Livingstone Statue is 1.7 miles from Victoria Falls Safari Suites, while The Stanley and Livingstone Private Game Reserve is 5 miles away.
