Description

The bungalows at this beautiful resort are totally air-conditioned and come equipped with a private balcony with excellent sea views. The spacious suites have a sitting area with a sofa.

The property's restaurant serves international-style cuisine, and guests also enjoy a poolside “swim-up” bar.

Activities like snorkeling, diving, fishing and biking can be arranged on-site, with Hopkins Beach just 350 feet away. The property’s Butterflies Spa also offers a variety of relaxing treatments.

Transportation to Belize City and the town of Dangriga can be arranged for an extra fee. The Philip SW Goldson International Airport is 112 miles away.
