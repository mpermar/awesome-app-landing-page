Description

The air-conditioned cottages at this tropical resort come with excellent beach views, satellite TV, a private outdoor sitting area and a refrigerator.

The property’s on-site restaurant features 2 professional chefs and serves international cuisine and seafood. It's open for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

The property can arrange a variety of outdoor activities for guests, including water skiing, kayaking and fishing. They also provide tours of Belize and its ancient archaeological sites.

Guests also enjoy an on-site spa and daily included transportation services to the mainland. Philip SW Goldson International Airport is about 10 minutes away by boat and the mainland is about 20 minute away by boat.
