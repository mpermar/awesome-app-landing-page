Description

In charming cabins, each villa and suite at the Midas Resort has bright décor and includes a terrace. All accommodations have air conditioning, cable TV and a ceiling fan.

Guests can enjoy an English breakfast in the Midas’ dining room. There is also a bar, where free Wi-Fi is available.

The Midas Resort has an ideal setting for fishing and horseback riding. The resort arranges trips to nearby caves and Mayan ruins, as well as bird watching excursions.
