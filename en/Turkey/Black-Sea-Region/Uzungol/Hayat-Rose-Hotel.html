Description

Located in Uzungol, 300 metres from Uzungol Plateau & Lake, Hayat Rose Hotel provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a shared lounge and a garden.

Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a children's playground.

The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and free WiFi throughout the property.

At the hotel each room is equipped with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

Guests at Hayat Rose Hotel can enjoy a buffet or an Asian breakfast. The nearest airport is Trabzon Airport, 90 km from the accommodation.
