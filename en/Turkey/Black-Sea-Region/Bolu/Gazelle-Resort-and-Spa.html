Description

Located in an oak tree forest in Karacasu area, Gazelle Resort & Spa offers spacious rooms with satellite LCD TV and free Wi-Fi.

The hotel features a sauna, gym, hot tub and 2 swimming pools. The rooms at Gazelle Resort & Spa are modern in style and have lots of daylight.

They include air conditioning, a minibar and a stylish bathroom. Extensive menus of Turkish and Italian cuisine are available in Gazelle’s restaurants, which offer either à la carte choices or open buffets.

The bar and snack bar serve refreshing drinks. A wine menu is also available. Guests at the Gazelle Resort & Spa can benefit from free parking and a 24-hour reception.

The indoor and outdoor pools offer some refreshment after a day of hiking or cycling.

The surrounding forest has walking tracks. Children are kept entertained at the children's pool or children's club.

Gazelle Resort & Spa is an 18-minute drive from Bolu. Istanbul is a 2. 5-hour, Ankara is a 1.
