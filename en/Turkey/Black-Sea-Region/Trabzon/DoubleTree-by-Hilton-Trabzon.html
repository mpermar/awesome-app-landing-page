Description

Situated on the seafront, along the Trabzon - Giresun Road, DoubleTree by Hilton Trabzon is within easy reach of the city center, Trabzon's industrial areas and many other popular attractions.

Free WiFi is available at this hotel. Comfortable and spacious rooms at this hotel in Trabzon, offer a flat-screen TV, safety box, well-appointed bathroom with free toiletries, slippers and a hairdryer.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. Enjoy a dip in the swimming pool or work out with the modern equipment in the fitness center.

The hotel also features a terrace. Relax with a drink, before enjoying international and local cuisine in the Garden Grille & Bar, which is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Stock up on snacks and essentials 24/7 in the Pavilion Pantry®. Trabzon city centre is 10 km from DoubleTree by Hilton Trabzon, while Macka is 38 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Trabzon Airport, 15 km from Hilton DoubleTree by Hilton Trabzon.
