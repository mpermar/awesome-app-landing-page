Description

Located near Bodrum's marina, Su Hotel offers modern rooms along with a large Mediterranean garden and an outdoor swimming pool.

The nearest bus stop is a 5-minute walk away. All rooms at Su Hotel - Bodrum have a brightly coloured décor with traditional decorations.

Most rooms have balconies with views of the courtyard, which has fig trees, orange trees and pomegranates.

Su Hotel's 24-hour reception can help arrange trips to Bodrum's main attractions.

The hotel also offers bicycle and car rental. Free WiFi is available in its public areas.

Airport transfer service is also available at an extra charge. Su Hotel Restaurant serves light snacks and Aegean dishes, including a variety of fresh seafood.

The bar offers a variety of refreshing drinks and cocktails. Su Hotel is 200 metres away from the centre of Bodrum, and a 30-minute drive from Camel Beach.
