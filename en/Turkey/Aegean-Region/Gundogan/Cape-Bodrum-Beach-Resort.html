Description

Offering an outdoor pool and spa centre, Cape Bodrum Beach Resort is situated in Gundogan in the Aegean Region.

The hotel has a children's playground and a private beach area, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

Overlooking to views of sea, each room at this hotel is air conditioned and is fitted with a flat-screen TV.

Some rooms include a seating area for your convenience. A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms.

Rooms have a private bathroom with a bath and bath or shower, with bath robes, slippers and free toiletries provided.

There is a 24-hour front desk, a shared lounge and gift shop at the property. You can start the day with a rich breakfast.

You can also enjoy lunch and dinner at the on-site restaurant. This hotel has water sports facilities and bike hire is available.

You can play table tennis at this hotel, and car hire is available. Bodrum is 23 km from Cape Bodrum Beach Resort , while Didim is 110 km away.
