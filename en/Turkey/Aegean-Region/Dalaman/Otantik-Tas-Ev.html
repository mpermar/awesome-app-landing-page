Description

Featuring free WiFi and a restaurant, Otantik Tas Ev offers pet-friendly accommodation in Sarigerme, only 12 km from Dalaman Airport.

The hotel has a sun terrace and views of the garden, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms. Each room comes with a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower.

Breakfast comes with homemade jams, eggs, homemade bread, bagel and cheese rolls.

There is a shared lounge at the property. The hotel also offers car hire and a taxi services.

There is a live music performance at the restaurant on weekends. In summer, the property offers massage services to be booked in advance.

Free private parking and bike rental are available on site. Fethiye is 50 km from Otantik Tas Ev, while Oludeniz is 66 km away.
