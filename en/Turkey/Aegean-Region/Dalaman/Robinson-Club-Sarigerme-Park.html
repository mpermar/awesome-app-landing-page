Description

The modern rooms at the Robinson Club Sarigerme Park include a flat-screen TV and minibar. Most have sea views and a balcony. Wi-Fi is available in the entire hotel and is free of charge.

Activities available at Robinson’s Sarigerme Park include tennis, volleyball, watersports and fitness classes. Guests can relax in the large park or visit the WellFit health club’s spa facilities.

Traditional Turkish cuisine and international dishes are served in the Das Edel restaurant. The all-inclusive option includes buffets, snacks and most drinks.

The Robinson provides evening entertainment such as theater shows and singles evenings. There is also a Nite Club and lobby bar.
