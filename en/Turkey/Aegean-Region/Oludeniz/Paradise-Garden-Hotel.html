Description

Featuring tiled floors, Gardens’ spacious rooms offer a traditional décor and wood furnishing. Rooms come with private bathrooms with a bathtub or shower and hairdryer. TVs and direct-dial telephones are provided.

Outside dining is possible at Paradise Garden’s restaurant. A buffet-style breakfast is served including traditional Turkish fare, eggs, and homemade jelly. Menu options are available for evening meals. Light snacks are offered at the snack bar.

A laundry service is offered by the 24-hour front desk. A car rental or shuttle service can also be arranged.

Paradise Garden Hotel is located 7.5 miles from Fethiye center and 34 miles to Dalaman airport.
