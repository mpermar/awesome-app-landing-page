Description

Located a 15-minute walk from Oludeniz Beach, Manaspark Deluxe Hotel - Ultra All Inclusive offers 24-hours all-inclusive service, a sun terrace and 2 outdoor pools.

The property has a private beach area and free shuttle service is available. Modernly furnished, the air-conditioned rooms of Manaspark Deluxe Hotel - Ultra All Inclusive include a flat-screen TV, electric kettle, minibar and a private bathroom.

All rooms offer sea views. At this all-inlcusive hotel, breakfast, lunch and dinner are served in open buffet style with unlimited local drinks at the main restaurant.

There is also a terrace bar, pool and a garden bar for resfrehing drinks. Manaspark Deluxe Hotel - Ultra All Inclusive is just a 5-minute drive from Hisaronu and a 10-minute drive from Kayakoy.
