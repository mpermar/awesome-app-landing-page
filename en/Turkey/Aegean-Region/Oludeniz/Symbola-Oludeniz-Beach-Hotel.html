Description

Situated among the pine and olive trees with its unique stone-built architecture, this hotel is just 300 metres from the beautiful Oludeniz Beach.

It offers an outdoor pool and a terrace with views over the sea. Symbola Oludeniz Beach Hotel has air-conditioned rooms with a balcony.

Featuring tiled floors, each room includes a satellite TV, minibar and a private bathroom with a bath tub.

All rooms offer sea or garden views. The day starts with an open buffet breakfast at Symbola Oludeniz Beach Hotel.

There is also an à la carte restaurant at the hotel. During the day, you can enjoy refreshing drinks and snacks from the bar.

Kayakoy Village is 9 km from the hotel. Fethiye city centre is 12 km away. Dalaman Airport is within 65 km.
