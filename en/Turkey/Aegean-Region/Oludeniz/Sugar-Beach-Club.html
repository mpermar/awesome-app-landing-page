Description

Located on the shores of the picturesque and famous Blue Lagoon in Oludeniz, this resort offers a private beach and wooden bungalows with sea views.

Free Wi-Fi is available. Cosy bungalows at Sugar Beach Club come with air conditioning and a seating area.

Private bathroom includes a shower and free toiletries. Sugar Beach Club’s funky restaurant serves dishes from the Mediterranean cuisine.

The bar is known for its tasty cocktails and aromatic coffees. A Turkish breakfast is served every morning.

Guests can relax on the free sun lounger, shadowed by parasols. Rental bicycles, canoes and pedal boats are provided.

Paragliding from the surrounding Babadag Mountain is a popular activity in the area.

Fethiye, a bustling traditional Turkish harbour and market town, is a 15-minute drive away.

Dalaman Airport is 70 km from Sugar Beach Club. A shuttle service can be arranged upon request with an additional fee.
