Description

Featuring a large private beach area in Bodrum’s Torba Bay, The Bodrum by Paramount Hotels & Resorts offers an outdoor and an indoor pool, luxurious accommodation and free WiFi.

A spa centre with extensive massage facilities, a gym, sauna and a Turkish bath are also available.

Elegantly decorated, the air-conditioned units at The Bodrum by Paramount Hotels & Resorts have a balcony with sea views and a seating area.

They are equipped with a satellite TV, DVD player and a minibar. A safety deposit box is provided in each room.

Certain rooms also come with an iPad. Guests can sample a range of Turkish, Italian, Asian and international cuisine dishes at the hotel’s many dining options including the The Stage, B Lounge, Shimeji, Vito's and the Malibu Deck.

You can also enjoy drinks at the Tableau, 5555 or Malibu Deck pool bar. For younger guests, the hotel has a mini kids club as well Bodrum town centre is 10 km from The Bodrum by Paramount Hotels & Resorts.
