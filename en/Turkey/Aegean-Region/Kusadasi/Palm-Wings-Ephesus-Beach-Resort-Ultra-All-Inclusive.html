Description

Palm Wings Ephesus Beach Resort is a grand hotel set among lush tropical gardens along the Aegean Sea, Pamucak Bay.

Directly opposite beachfront, it offers contemporary rooms along with eleven swimming pools with loungers, tennis court and Turkish steam bath.

500 metres long, sandy, private beach of the hotel provides loungers and umbrellas.

Property also offers a spa and wellness centre. The stylish rooms are tastefully furnished and modern.

Parquet floored rooms feature a kettle, minibar and a satellite TV. The private balconies enjoy views of the Aegean Sea or gardens.

Some rooms have a balcony and bathroom with built-in sound system. With 6 restaurants and 7 bars, there is no shortage of places to eat and drink.

Besides the main restaurant, you can savour bites from Turkish, Mexican and Italian cuisine at the à la carte restaurants while there is also a chance to enjoy a variety of fishes.

Child menus are also available. There is an on-site patisserie for you to taste freshly-baked pastries.

You can have a sip of your drink by the pool at the pool bar. You will find a sauna and a hammam at Palm Wings Ephesus Beach Resort.

An array of activities can be enjoyed include squash, basketball, table tennis, beach volleyball, jet ski, canoeing and diving.

There is also a meeting/conference room at the property. You can have fun time at the evening entertainments with various shows.

There are facilities for children such as children's club, animation shows, playground and children's pools.

Ephesus Ancient City is 9. 5 km away while Kusadasi city centre is located within 11.

8 km of Palm Wings Ephesus Beach Resort. The Izmir Adnan Menderes Airport is 67 km from the hotel.
