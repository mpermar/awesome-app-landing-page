Description

Located in Kusadasi, CLC Kusadasi Golf & Spa Resort features 11 outdoor pools, an aquapark, a spa & wellness centre, a gym, an indoor pool and is within close vicinity an 18-hole golf course.

Air-conditioned apartments are equipped with a safe box, washing machine, sofa and a cable TV with a DVD player.

There is a kitchen with a refrigerator, dishwasher and an oven. Private bathrooms include a hairdryer.

All apartments feature a balcony. There is a restaurant at the property, serving international dishes.

A variety of beverages, refreshments and snacks are available at the bar and snack bar.

Numerous activities including golf, mini golf and tennis are available in the property or in the surrounding area.

Guests who want to keep up with their exercise schedule can take advantage of the fitness center.

Sauna and hammam is available for guests who want to relax. Free parasols and sunloungers await the guests by the pool.
