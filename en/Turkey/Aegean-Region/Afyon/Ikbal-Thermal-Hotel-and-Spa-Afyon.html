Description

Located on the Afyon-Izmir highway, the newly renovated Ikbal Thermal Hotel & Spa Afyon offers bright rooms with LCD TV and free WiFi.

The property includes an outdoor pool, Turkish bath and spa centre with thermal treatments.

The elegant rooms of Ikbal Thermal Hotel feature heating and carpeted floors. They all include a seating area and a minibar.

The restaurant serves breakfast, lunch and dinner as an open buffet. You can enjoy regional delicacies as well as international dishes.

For relaxation, you can benefit from the hot tub, sauna or the massage rooms of the hotel.

An indoor pool, solarium, fitness centre and a squash court are also available on site.

There is also a football field on site. Afyon city centre is 9 km from the hotel while Afyon Sport Complex is 3 km from hotel and Zafer Airport is 49 km away.
