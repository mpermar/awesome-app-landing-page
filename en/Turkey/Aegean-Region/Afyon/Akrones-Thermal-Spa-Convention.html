Description

Situated in Afyon, Akrones Thermal Spa Convention boasts a spa centre which includes thermal pools, sauna, steam rooms and a snow fountain.

Free private parking and free WiFi throughout the property is available at the hotel.

Each room at Akrones Thermal Spa Convention offers a minibar, bathrobe and coffee/tea set-up.

Some rooms offer hot tub and sauna with natural thermal water. Guests can start the day with a rich open-buffet breakfast and freshly-squeezed fruit juices.

In the evening, guests can taste local and international cuisines. À la carte restaurant offers a traditional steakhouse experience.

At a surcharge guests can enjoy the spa centre that includes a variety of massages, beauty treatments, solarium and private family thermal baths.

You will find a 24-hour front desk, hairdresser's, and shops at the property. Afyonkarahiser City centre is 8.
