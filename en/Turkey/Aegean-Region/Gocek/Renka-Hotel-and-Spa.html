Description

Set in a stone building decorated with handmade stained-glass in Gocek, Renka Hotel & Spa features a spa&wellness centre, Turkish and steam baths, massage rooms and skincare centre.

Free WiFi and a 24-hour front desk are available in the property. Rooms at Renka Hotel & Spa are equipped with a 42" satellite LCD TV, minibar, safe box and an electric kettle with coffee&tea setup.

Equipped with a shower, private bathrooms include a hairdryer and eco-friendly toiletries.

Hard or soft pillow options are available to the guests. Guests can start the day with a Traditional Turkish breakfast or enjoy a selection of dishes by the fireplace in à la carte restaurant.

There is also a snack bar in the property. Renka Hotel & Spa offers a semi-olympic swimming pool.

Free bikes are available to guests in the property. Staff can assist the guests about organising tours or exchanging currency.

Renka Hotel & Spa is 500 metres from Gocek Shopping Street and 1 km from Gocek Bus Terminal.
