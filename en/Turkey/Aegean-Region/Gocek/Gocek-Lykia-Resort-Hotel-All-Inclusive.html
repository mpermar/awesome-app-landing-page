Description

This resort is located in a peaceful area with a beautiful garden and an outdoor pool shaped in the symbol of ancient Lycia.

Further facilities include a spa centre with a Turkish bath, sauna and a fitness centre.

The rooms of Göcek Lykia Resort are tastefully decorated with modern furniture in soft colours.

They are all equipped with air conditioning and TV. Free Wi-Fi is also provided in the rooms.

The restaurant serves delicious dishes of Mediterranean, local and international cuisine.

Guests can enjoy their meals at the poolside. The hotel bar offers refreshing drinks and special fruit cocktails.

The Aegean Sea and the yacht marina is only a 12-minute walk from Göcek Lykia Resort.

Dalaman Airport is 23 km away. The resort also provides shuttle service with extra charge.
