Description

Only 600 metres from Cesme Marina and a 10-minute walk from Cesme Castle, this hotel features an outdoor pool, 24-hour front-desk and free Wi-Fi access in all rooms.

A children’s playground is available. Decorated in a modern style, rooms at Ege Tulip Cesme offer floor-to-ceiling windows, air conditioning and a TV with satellite channels.

A safety deposit box, mini refrigerator and a bathroom with hairdryer are provided.

The hotel’s à la carte restaurant serves lunch and dinner. Breakfast is offered in buffet style every morning.

The bar with refreshing drinks is open until 23:00. Guests can benefit from laundry and dry cleaning.

Izmir Adnan Menderes Airport is 93 km from Ege Tulip Cesme. Izmir city center is 85 km away.
