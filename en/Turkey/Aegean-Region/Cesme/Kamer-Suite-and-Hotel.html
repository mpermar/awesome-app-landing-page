Description

Situated a 2-minute drive from the beaches of the Aya Yorgi Bay, Kamer Suite & Hotel features modern rooms with a kitchen and a healthy breakfast buffet with fresh farm products.

There is a free shuttle to Sera Beach, Kafe-Pi- Aya Yorgi Beach and Palmiye Beach.

Kamer Suite & Hotel’s spacious guest rooms are comfortably equipped with free WiFi, a satellite TV, kitchenette and air conditioning.

Each of the private bathrooms comes with a shower and has a hairdryer. Guests can start the day with the freshly prepared breakfast at the restaurant and enjoy a range of fresh fruits, crispy bread and hot coffee.

Restaurant offers a variety of Aegean and Greek cuisines. The poolside terrace with its sun loungers and parasols offers a relaxing setting to spend the afternoon.

The fitness room features a variety of up-to-date equipment and there is an entertainments hall for hours of fun.

Kamer Suite & Hotel is situated a 10-minute walk from the Aegean Sea and its beaches.
