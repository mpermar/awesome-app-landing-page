Description

Located within walking distance to Temple of Artemis, St. John's Basilica, Ayasoluk Citadel and Isabey Mosque, Ayasoluk Hotel is located centrally in Selcuk.

It offers an outdoor pool with a sun terrace to soak up the sun and a large garden to relax.

Free WiFi is available throughout the premises. With an authentic décor and a handmade Turkish carpet, the rooms at Hotel Ayasoluk features air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and a minibar.

A safety deposit box is standard in each unit. Some rooms come with a bay window.

If you like the handmade carpet in your room, you can purchase it. Every morning, breakfast is offered and guests can have it served to their room upon request.

The on-site restaurant offers meals with a mixture of comfort food and traditional Turkish food.

There is also a bar, where you can enjoy a refreshing drink. The staff, available 24 hours a day, offers concierge services and provides laundry and dry cleaning services.

Airport shuttle services are also arranged at an additional fee. The ancient town of Ephesus is 3 km from the property.
