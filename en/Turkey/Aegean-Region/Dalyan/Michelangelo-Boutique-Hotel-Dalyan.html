Description

Located at a central position in Dalyan, Michelangelo Boutique Hotel Dalyan offers an à la carte restaurant serving international cuisine, lobby bar and a pool bar.

Hotel also offers a seasonal outdoor pool with 3D images. Fitness, hammam, massage and therapy rooms, steam room, sauna and spa centre are also offered.

Free WiFi is available. Each room at this property will provide you a 43" smart LED TV, laptop-size safety box, minibar, tea&coffee setup, desk, vanity mirror, blackout curtains and air conditioning.

Bathroom comes with a hairdryer. The breakfast is served as an open buffet in Michelangelo Boutique Hotel Dalyan.

Michelangelo Boutique Hotel Dalyan offers dry cleaning, ironing and laundry service with an extra fee.

Dalyan Camping Grounds is 1. 3 km from the property. Dalaman Airport is 28 km away.
