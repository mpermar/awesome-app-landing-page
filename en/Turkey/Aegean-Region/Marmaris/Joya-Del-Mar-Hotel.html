Description

Offering a private yacht marina and a private beach, Joya Del Mar Hotel is located in Marmaris.

Free WiFi and a 24-hour front desk are available in the property. Each room features a flat-screen satellite TV, air conditioning and a minibar.

Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and bathrobes. You can enjoy garden view from the room.

Breakfast is served everyday at Joya Del Mar Hotel. A variety of dishes and special diet menus are served at hotel's restaurant.

Drinks and beverages are available at the bar. There is a sauna and a hammam at Joya Del Mar Hotel.

Activities including cycling are available on site or in the surrounding area. Hotel is 5 km from Marmaris city centre.
