Description

Located right near the seafront, this hotel has a private beach area. It offers a swimming pool, free Wi-Fi and air-conditioned rooms with a satellite TV.

Each room at Mares Hotel features a seating area with a sofa bed. They all have a balcony with either sea or garden view.

A laptop safe box is also standard. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served in buffet style at Yunus Restaurant.

You can sample fresh seafood at the restaurant Meyhane. Various cocktails are served at Sultan Bar and the pool bar.

The spa centre includes a Turkish bath, sauna and massage services. You can play a game of table tennis, darts or billiards at Hotel Mares.

A childrens playground is also available. Marmaris town centre is 3 km from Mares Hotel.
