Description

This family-run hotel is situated on the Marmaris seafront, overlooking Uzunyali Beach.

The hotel features an outdoor swimming pool, a private beach area and free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel.

All guest bedrooms at Hotel Yunus are equipped with a private balcony, giving panoramic views of Marmaris Bay.

In addition every room has satellite TV, air conditioning and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

Guests can benefit from the safety deposit box and minibar at an additional fee. Continental breakfast is served each morning in Yunus Hotel’s spacious dining area.

For lunch and dinner, the restaurant offers local and international specialties à la carte, and there is a beach-front bar.

A special discount of 10% is offered at the a-la-carte restaurant. Yunus Hotel has separate swimming pools for adults and children, and guests can also use the sun loungers on the beach.

The hotel is less than 1 km from Atlantis Water Park, and 5 minutes’ walk from Marmaris Castle.
