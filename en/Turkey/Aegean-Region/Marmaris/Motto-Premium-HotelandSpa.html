Description

Only 50 metres to the Uzunyali Beach, Motto Premium Hotel offers an outdoor pool, a Turkish bath and free WiFi.

It has air-conditioned accommodation with a satellite TV and balcony. All the rooms at Motto Premium Hotel come with a private bathroom.

A safety deposit box is also standard. The hotel also offers some spacious suites.

This all-inclusive hotel offers all meals buffet style. The à la carte restaurants serve world and local cuisines.

You can enjoy alcoholic drinks and beverages at the bar. The pool area is ideal for sunbathing and relaxation.

The front desk is available 24/7. The Greek Island Rhodes is only a 45-minute ferry ride from the Marmaris city centre.

Dalaman Airport is less than 100 km away. Free private parking is available on site.
