Description

Located in Ortakent district, Zest Exclusive Hotel features an outdoor pool and an à la carte restaurant and a private beach are with free parasols and sunlongers.

This beachfront hotel also offers nightly entertainment. Air-conditioned rooms in Zest Exclusive Hotel are fitted with a 32" satellite plasma TV, laptop safe, minibar, electric kettle and a safe box.

There is a furnished outdoor dining area. Featuring a shower, private bathrooms come with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Rooms also has a balcony. Some the of the rooms also feature views of the sea. The day starts with a rich Aegean-style breakfast at Zest Exclusive Hotel.

Rokka Restaurant also serves a fine selection of Turkish and international dishes and delicious fish from Aegean Sea.

Hotel has a children's pool. Free WiFi and a 24-hour front desk is also available in the property.

Milas-Bodrum Airport is 48. 4 km away. Guests can take advantage of airport shuttle service at an extra cost.
