Description

Located at the seaside, in the ancient Lycian lands, Club Tuana - All Inclusive offers a private beach area.

It has an on-site hammam, sauna and spacious swimming pools surrounded by palm trees and wild flowers.

Situated in a unique area where the Mediterranean and the Aegean seas meet, Club Tuana features the endemic floral elements and orange gardens, frankincense trees and green areas by the seashore.

The bright rooms of Club Tuana are all air-conditioned and include a minibar and LCD TV.

Private balconies with a seating area are also offered in the rooms. The hotel's restaurant is located next to the pool area and serves Turkish cuisine.

Guests can enjoy their meals with pool and botanical garden views. The bar offers variety of drinks for 24 hour a day.

The spa of Club Tuana offers massages for relaxation. Guests can benefit from the fitness centre or enjoy the hot tub of the hotel.

Mini Club offers a variety of activities for children and teenagers. Hotel is 17 km from Fethiye city centre.
