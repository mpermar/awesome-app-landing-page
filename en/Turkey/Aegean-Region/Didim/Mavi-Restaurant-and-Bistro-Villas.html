Description

All guest rooms in the guesthouse are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Rooms have a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries. All rooms at Mavi Restaurant & Bistro Villas are equipped with a seating area.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental or a à la carte breakfast. At Mavi Restaurant & Bistro Villas you'll find a restaurant serving British and pizza cuisine. A halal option can also be requested.

The guesthouse has a playground. You can play pool at Mavi Restaurant & Bistro Villas.

Miletos is 11 miles from the accommodation, while The Temple of Serapis is 11 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Milas Airport, 25 miles from the property.
