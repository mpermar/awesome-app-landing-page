Description

Located just 200 metres from Ataturk Museum, this 5-star luxury hotel offers panoramic views of Bursa with a backdrop of the famous Uludag Mountain.

Facilities include an outdoor pool and a spa centre offering many treatments and therapies.

The stylish rooms of BB Çelik Palace Bursa are decorated with elegant dark wooden furniture and carpeted floors.

All rooms have free WiFi and a flat-screen TV. Room service is available 24/7. The Çelik Café serves international cuisine with a terrace overlooking the city.

The Celik Café offers light bites, and hot and cold beverages in a cosy ambience.

Guests can get a variety of health and beauty treatments at the spa, which also features a sauna, Turkish bath and a fitness centre.

A large pool is filled with natural thermal spring water. Himalayan miracle salt room provides treatments for upper and lower respiratory conditions, ear infection and skin conditions.

Bursa Ataturk Stadium is 1. 3 km from BB Çelik Palace Bursa, and the Bursa Grand Mosque is 2.

5 km away. Kultur Park is right opposite the hotel, the famous historical village of Cumalikizik is just a 20-minute drive away.
