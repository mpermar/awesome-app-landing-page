Description

Located within walking distance to Sapanca Lake, Villa Kirkpinar is located centrally in Sapanca's Kirkpinar District, surrounded by a peaceful nature.

It offers an outdoor pool and free bikes. With large private gardens, the self-catering villas feature free Wi-Fi.

Accommodation provides a flat-screen satellite TV, DVD player, washing machine, fireplace, air conditioning and a patio.

There is a full kitchen with a dishwasher and a microwave. Featuring a bath or shower, private bathrooms also come with a hairdryer and towels.

You can enjoy a mountain view. At Villa Kirkpinar you will find a 24-hour front desk, a garden and barbecue facilities.

Other facilities like a games room and a children's playground are offered. Activities in the surrounding area include cycling.
