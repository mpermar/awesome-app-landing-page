Description

Located in the historical Sultanahmet district, this new hotel is just 250 metres from Grand Bazaar and 500 metres from Blue Mosque.

Innova Sultanahmet Istanbul offers luxurious accommodation, a spa centre and free WiFi.

All equipped with extra long beds, the comfortable rooms of Innova are decorated elegantly with modern furniture.

They all feature air conditioning and a LED TV. Guests can enjoy a rich breakfast buffet in the mornings.

Meals are served at the à la carte Revan Restaurant and Mey Lounge offers drinks.

There is also a panoramic roof terrace with beautiful views over the sea of Marmara.

The spa centre of Innova Sultanahmet Istanbul offers a traditional Turkish bath, sauna, fitness room and massages.

There is also a cinema room on site. The hotel is 800 metres from Topkapi Palace and 600 metres from Hagia Sophia.

Cemberlitas Tram Stop is just 100 metres from the hotel, offering easy access to many other sites of Istanbul city.
