Description

Located in the heart of the city, this luxurious Istanbul hotel is within walking distance from Taksim Square, and main convention and exhibition centres.

It offers an outdoor pool, a spa and fitness centre, and modern meeting rooms. Grand Hyatt Istanbul has luxury rooms decorated in light colours.

All rooms include a seating area, LCD TV and a work area. The marble bathrooms come with a bath tub and a glass-enclosed rain shower.

Free wiFi is available in the rooms. ‘34’ offers wide culinary variation and features both regional Turkish and Mediterranean cuisine.

Mezzanine Lounge & Bar is the perfect place to relax in the cozy and live atmosphere.

The Library Bar offers a relaxing atmosphere reflecting the style of English Gentlemen’s Clubs.

On sunny days, there is nothing more refreshing than a barbecue and cocktails by the pool at the Gazebo.

Gaia Spa&Fitness Centre offers modern spa treatments and authentic hammam rituals.

There is also a floodlit tennis court on site. Taksim Metro Station is only 400 metres from Grand Hyatt.

The historical Sultanahmet area is 5 km away including Topkapi Palace, Hagia Sophia and Blue Mosque.

Ataturk Airport is within 20 km. Private parking is available on site for an additional fee.
