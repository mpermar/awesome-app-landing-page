Description

Built in harmony with neo classical and baroque architecture, the air-conditioned accommodations features a flat-screen TV with satellite and pay-per-view channels, a stocked mini-bar and an electric kettle. Each unit also includes a seating area, working desk and a safety deposit box.

Daily breakfast is served buffet style. For other meals, authentic examples from Turkish and international cuisines are served at the hotel restaurant. At Coffee Company, you can relax with a sumptuous cake while sipping your coffee and at One Bar you can enjoy a refreshing drink and the music.

Elite World Business Hotel offers complimentary round airport shuttle services to/from Ataturk Airport. Guests can also benefit from the concierge desk and business center on site. CNR Expo Center is 3.1 mi from the property.
