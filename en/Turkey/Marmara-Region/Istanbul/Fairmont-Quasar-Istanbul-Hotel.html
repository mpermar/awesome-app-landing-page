Description

A TV with satellite channels, as well as an iPad are offered. All rooms have a seating area, dining table and working desk for your convenience. All rooms have one touch button motorized curtains. Some rooms have a bath tub or rain shower, and some rooms have a heated Turkish bath in the bathroom.

Stations Restaurant offers relaxed dining at any time of the day, serving seasonal and fresh options prepared by chefs at live cooking stations. Daily breakfast is served as a rich open buffet featuring classic breakfast items, charcuterie varieties, egg stations, both traditional and international bakery selections among many others. Demlique Restaurant serves a modern interpretation of traditional Turkish tea and coffee culture accompanied with local pastries and sweets. Marble Bar is situated in the Fairmont’s vibrant lobby for wine and spirits.

Meditation, yoga, and PaviGym classes are available at the Willow Stream Spa. 24-hour front desk and concierge services are also available for your convenience. Fairmont Quasar Istanbul offers luxury airport transfer and meet&greet service at the airport upon request. This pet-friendly hotel also has a designated helipad area and offers helicopter transfer upon request.

Istanbul Convention Center is 2 miles from Fairmont Quasar Istanbul while Cevahir Shopping Center, Zorlu, Astoria and Profilo Shopping Centers are in a walking distance. The nearest airport is Istanbul Ataturk Airport, 12 miles from the property.
