Description

Conveniently located in Yesilyurt, just 230 metres from the beach, luxurious 5-star Renaissance Polat Istanbul Hotel offers panoramic view of the Marmara Sea.

Featuring uniquely large swimming pools, the hotel includes a 2600 m sports area with renovated gym rooms, massage facilities and a Turkish bath.

Spacious and air-conditioned rooms come with a comfortable bed, safety box and an LCD TV.

Guests can enjoy the views of the sea, garden or the city. Complimentary tea & coffee setup, pillow menu, hairdryer and ironing facilities are also fitted in rooms.

You can relax at Noblesse Bar while sipping your drink accompanied by pleasing views of the sea.

Live music performance can be enjoyed at Noblesse Bar during the night and the hotel also offers live piano performance at the lobby during the day at certain times.

Renovated Marmara Fish Restaurant serves selective sea food dishes on the terrace throughout the year.

Featuring idyllic view of the Marmara Sea and the Princes' Islands, Daphe Restaurant & Grill's delicious menu includes Mediterranean and Turkish dishes.

Aromatic tea, coffee, cakes, chocolates, and homemade cookies can be tasted at Polat Pattisserie.

The hotel also offers a club lounge with snacks and drinks served throughout the day.

Guests can discover the wooden Art Nouveau style houses from the era of tha late 19th and early 20th century in the surrounding area.

Yesilkoy Marina is located 1. 5 km from the property where you can take a relaxing walk, ride a bike, hike, swim and enjoy a nice picnic.

CNR EXPO and IDTM are located within a 6 km distance from Renaissance Polat Istanbul Hotel, while Istanbul city centre is a 20-minute drive from the property.
