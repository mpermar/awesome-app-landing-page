Description

Sheraton Grand Istanbul Atasehir is located in Ataşehir, which is an emerging residential and business centre on the Asian side of Istanbul.

The hotel is only 1 km from Istanbul Finance Centre development and Ulker Sports Arena is within walking distance.

Several freeways and bridges are nearby. Palladium Mall is just 3 minutes' walk from the property.

Tastefully decorated rooms are dispersed throughout the 27 floors, which allow views of city and Bosphorus and Princes' Islands.

A range of modern amenities include high speed internet, a flat-screen TV and a minibar.

Make time for a good workout in the Sheraton Fitness centre which is equipped with the latest technology, or swim in the convertible indoor pool.

Spa centre offers relaxation with massages and other treatments. You can enjoy gourmet dishes in all-day dining venue.

Cafe Patisserie offers snacks and drinks throughout the day. Sheraton Grand Istanbul Atasehir conference centre comprises a 1084 m² grand ballroom, which can be divided into two rooms, as well as 10 meeting rooms, most of which can be illuminated by natural daylight.

All are equipped with state-of-the-art audiovisual equipment for meetings. There are many shopping areas nearby.

Optimum Outlet Centre is 3. 2 km from Sheraton Grand Istanbul Atasehir while Emaar Square is 5.

9 km away. Akasya Mall with many luxurious brands is just 15 minutes' drive and Viaport Outlet Centre is 33 minutes' drive from the property.

Dolmabahce Palace is 10 km from Sheraton Grand Istanbul Atasehir, while Topkapi Palace is 10 km away.

The nearest airport is Sabiha Gokcen Airport, 28 km from the property. Istanbul Airport is 63 km away.
