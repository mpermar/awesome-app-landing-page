Description

Situated in Istanbul's historic district of Sultanahmet, only a 2-minute walk from Blue Mosque and Hagia Sophia, this Ottoman-style hotel combines traditional dcor with modern facilities, including free wireless internet access.

Arena Hotel - Special Class is an excellent example of a 19th-Century Ottoman stone house.

It has been carefully restored in keeping with Istanbul's historic architecture and features antique furnishings.

Each of the spacious rooms at Arena Hotel - Special Class is decorated in rich fabrics and a creative blend of warm colours.

You are warmly welcomed to Arena Hotel - Special Class with complimentary coffee and cookies upon your arrival.

You can also benefit from a buffet breakfast each morning, included in your room rate.

The cooking classes take place in hotels kitchen. The guests can cook and then enjoy the amazing Turkish food, in Arena Hotels Delight.

The marbling classes are available on Friday and Saturday, you may also ask for appointments for other days.

Skin care treatment is offered and complimentary foot massage is also provided. Live music is available every Thursday.

The location of Arena Hotel - Special Class is ideal for exploring Istanbul's history.

The friendly and helpful staff is happy to offer you recommendations for restaurants, shops or day trips.
