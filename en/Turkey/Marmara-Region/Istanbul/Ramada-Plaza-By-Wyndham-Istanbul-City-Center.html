Description

Less than 2 km to Taksim Square, within walking distance to Istanbul Military Museum and Istanbul Convention&Exhibition Center, this elegant hotel offers a rooftop swimming pool and renovated spa facilities.

It also features a Turkish Bath. There is an entrance to Osmanbey Metro Station right across the property.

You will also find complimentary charging units for your electric cars. Guest bedrooms at the environment-friendly Ramada Plaza By Wyndham Istanbul City Center include flat-screen satellite TVs and free WiFi.

All rooms have an bathroom with a tub or shower, and a hairdryer. Some rooms come with a kitchenette or a seating area.

There is also a hot tub in some rooms. Turkish and international dishes are served in Pancaldi Restaurant.

Breakfast is served as an open buffet. Relax in the marble steam room or sauna, or indulge yourself with a massage treatment.

There is also an on-site gym, helping you to stay in shape during your stay. Cevahir Shopping Centre is only 2 km from the hotel.

Memorial Sisli Hospital is 2. 7 km away, while Florance Nightingale Hospital is 3 km from the property.

Liv Hospital is 6 km away and Kolan International Hospital is 2 km from the hotel.
