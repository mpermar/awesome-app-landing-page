Description

Located in Karakoy district, Hotel Morione has a marble and wooden building renovated in 2014.

Only a few metres from the seaside, the hotel offers spa facilities including a sauna and a hammam.

You can also enjoy a massage upon request at a surcharge. Tastefully furnished with modern furniture, the rooms of Morione Hotel include a flat-screen TV, air conditioning, electric kettle and a minibar.

Free WiFi is available in the rooms. You can enjoy your breakfast in the indoor or outdoor dining area of the restaurant.

Adap Restaurant offers fine dining and a wide range of wine including Balkan wine.

There are also numerous restaurants and cafés nearby serving international and Turkish cuisine.

Karakoy Ferry Port is only 150 metres away, offering easy access to the Asian side of the city.

Taksim Square is 2 km away and can be accessed by the Tunnel only a 10-minute walk away.

Galata Tower is 500 metres away. Ataturk Airport is 18 km from the property. Istanbul Airport is 52 km away.
