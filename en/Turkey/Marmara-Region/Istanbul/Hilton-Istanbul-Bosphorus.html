Description

Centrally located, Hilton Istanbul Bosphorus offers stunning views over Bosphorus Strait and it is just a 10-minute walk from Taksim Square.

Featuring both an outdoor and an indoor pool, beautiful green gardens, a traditional Turkish bath and 6 restaurant&bar options, this iconic hotel provides resort accommodation in the heart of the city.

Taksim metro station is within a 5-minute walking distance while Osmanbey Metro Station is located 13 minutes' walk away.

Hilton Istanbul Bosphorus has bright rooms with balconies overlooking the gardens or Bosphorus.

Featuring traditional dcor with Turkish accents, each room has large windows, a separate sitting area, and a marble bathroom.

Executive Lounge serves complimentary continental breakfast, drinks and snacks throughout the day and private check-in and check-out.

Cantonese and Szechuan dishes are served in the Dragon Restaurant, open-air restaurants with panoramic nature views.

Regional delicacies such as seafood and grilled meat are served in Bosphorus Terrace Restaurant.

Veranda Bar&Terrace features one of the best views of the Bosphorus with its brand-new decor overlooking the lush gardens of the hotel.

Guests can also visit the Dubb Indian Bosphorus Restaurant with its Indian cuisine.

Children's menus and high chairs are also provided at the hotel. Offering Turkish tavern concept, Lalezar Meyhanesi serves variety of selective dishes and mezes from Turkish cuisine.

Live music also can be enjoyed on Fridays. The extensive Health Club of Hilton Istanbul Bosphorus is open for 24 hours, and offers tennis and racquetball facilities, and aerobics classes.

The Turkish bath has 2 saunas, 2 steam rooms, and massage treatments. Hilton Istanbul Bosphorus also offers a variety of entertainment options and facilities for younger guests, including a garden with playgrounds for toddlers and kids, and different items including bottle warmers and baby carriages.

Istanbul Congress Centre, Lutfi Kirdar Convention&Exhibition Centre and Harbiye Cemil Topuzlu Open-Air Theatre are right next to Hilton Istanbul Bosphorus.

The hotel is only a 10-minute walk from the fashionable shopping district of Nisantasi with luxury brand stores, designer shops, exclusive restaurants and cafs, and lively bars.

There are also numerous hospitals nearby: Acibadem Taksim Hospital is only 650 metres, Clinicana Hair Transplant and Esthetic Surgeries is 750 metres and American Hospital is 1.

8 km, away. Istanbul Airport and is 45 km from the property while Sabiha Gokcen Airport is 43.
