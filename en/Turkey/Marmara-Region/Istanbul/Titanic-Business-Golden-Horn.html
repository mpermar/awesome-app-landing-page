Description

Titanic Business Golden Horn is located in Bayrampasa district. It offers luxurious rooms with free WiFi.

This hotel includes a year-round indoor pool, seasonal outdoor pool, gym and a spa centre with a Turkish bath.

Free private parking is also available on site. Modernly decorated, the rooms of Titanic Business Golden Horn are spacious and equipped with air conditioning.

All rooms include an LCD TV with satellite channels. Some rooms also offer sea views.

Refilling everyday, a 0. 5 litres of bottled water and tea and coffee setup are also featured in the rooms.

Start your day at Alesta Restaurant and choose from a broad choice of hot and cold dishes at the breakfast buffet while enjoying a freshly brewed coffee or tea.

Located on the 17th floor, Hasır Turkish Cuisine offers authentic Anatolian specialties, accompanied by panoramic views of the city’s skyline.

Titanic Coffee is open all day for drinks, light meals and afternoon tea. The glass-roofed wellness centre includes an indoor pool and a sauna.

You can also relax with massages. Forum Istanbul and Axis Shopping Centre are within 2 km with many eateries and shops.

The historical Sultanahmet area is just a 15-minute drive from Titanic. Ataturk Airport is 12 km away while Istanbul Airport is 53 km from the property.
