Description

Only 1 km to Taksim Square, Hotel La Piano is situated 350 metres from the shopping centre Nisantasi.

It offers free Wi-Fi, a spa centre and air-conditioned rooms with an LCD TV and private bathroom.

The rooms have a modern decor and are equipped with air conditioning, a minibar, widescreen satellite TV and large bathrooms.

Some units rooms also feature a small kitchenette and a sitting area. Daily breakfast is richly served buffet style at the lobby restaurant.

There also is a stylish bar corner in the lobby area, where you can get large variety of drinks, cocktails, hot and cold beverages including soft drinks.

The spa centre at La Piano Hotel includes a sauna, Turkish bath and massage facilities.

You can also benefit from tour desk and car rental services. Lüutfi Kirdar Convention Centre is a 5-minute walk from the property and American Hospital is 850 metres away.

Taksim Metro Stop is a 20-minute walk away, offering easy access to the historical Sultanahmet area.
