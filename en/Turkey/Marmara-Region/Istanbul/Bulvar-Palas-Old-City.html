Description

With a very central location and a wealth of facilities, Hotel Bulvar Palas is just a 3-minute walk from Aksaray Tram Stop and 3 tram stops from the Grand Bazaar.

The hotel offers a fitness centre and spa facilities. Accommodation at Hotel Bulvar Palas comes in the form of spacious, classically furnished and decorated rooms.

They are all equipped with individually controlled air conditioning, a minibar, tea and coffee making facilities and a satellite LCD TV.

Buffet breakfast includes 85 hot and cold items every morning. The restaurant serves Turkish specialities, and is open for lunch and dinner.

After a day spent taking care of business or exploring the city, you can relax at the hotel's spa and wellness centre, including a sauna, hammam, hot tub and massage facilities.

There is also a small gym on-site. Hotel Bulvar Palas is only 2 tram stops away by tram from the Blue Mosque, Hagia Sophia and Topkapi Palace.

Many of Istanbul's business, shopping and touristic attractions are easily accessible on foot.
