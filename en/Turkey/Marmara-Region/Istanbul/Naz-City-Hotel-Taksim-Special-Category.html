Description

Situated in Taksim, this stylish hotel features a restaurant, bar and free WiFi throughout the property.

The hotel has a spa centre and an indoor pool. Guests can enjoy selective dishes at the hotel's restaurant.

The hotel offers free shuttle service to Taksim, Osmanbey, Acibadem Hospital, Memorial Hospital, Lutfi Kirdar International Congress and Exhibition Center and Istanbul Congress Centre upon guest's request throughout the day.

Spacious and elegantly-designed rooms at the property include a flat-screen TV and air conditioning, and offer comfortable accommodation.

Some units come with a seating area to relax after a busy day. Private bathrooms are equipped with slippers, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Manhattan Restaurant's award-winner chefs serve dishes with fresh ingredients. Pasha Spa Centre features a Turkish bath, sauna and a steam room.

An indoor pool and a fitness centre fitted with modern equipment are also available at the hotel.

This centrally located hotel is 1. 3 km from famous shopping district Nisantasi and 750 metres from Taksim Square.

Sultanahmet is 6 km from the property. The nearest airport is Ataturk Airport, 20 km away.
