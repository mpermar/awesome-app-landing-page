Description

Offering a spa&wellness centre, fitness centre, indoor and outdoor pools, Ağaoğlu My Mountain is located in Uludag.

Featuring a ski-to-door access, the property offers ski storage to its guests. Free WiFi access is available.

Each room provides heating, telephone, TV and a minibar. You can enjoy mountain view from the room.

The day starts with a breakfast every morning at Ağaoğlu My Mountain. Guests can enjoy a hot drink at the Bistro Café.

There is also a restaurant at the property which serves a variety of dishes. Guests can play tennis at the tennis court or rent ski equipment to enjoy skiing.

Children can enjoy Every Friday and Saturday night, there is a live music performance at the property.

Ağaoğlu My Mountain is only a few steps to the cable car. Bursa city centre is 44 km from the property.

Uludag National Park is 6. 2 km from the property. Bursa Yenisehir Airport is 82.
