Description

Providing guests with every conceivable facility needed for a memorable holiday, this hotel is located on Uludağ, a mountain in Turkey's Bursa Province.

It offers ski-to-door access. Ski school Karinna, private ski and snowboard tracks as well as snowmobiles are available in the vicinity.

Après-ski parties and barbecues are held on the terrace. Guests can benefit from the indoor pool and the spa centre with a hammam, sauna and a gym.

Children, in the meantime, can make the most of the games room or the children’s club.

Live music can be enjoyed at Kaptan Köşkü Bar six days a week. Alternatively, you can chat with fellow guests in front of the large fireplace.
