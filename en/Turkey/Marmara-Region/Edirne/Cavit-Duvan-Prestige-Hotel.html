Description

Located in central Edirne, Cavit Duvan Prestige Hotel is 1. 6 km from Selimiye Mosque.

Property features a terrace and free private parking. Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and features a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Rooms also include a minibar and a kettle. Certain rooms feature a seating area. Private bathrooms come with a shower, free toiletries, slippers and a hairdryer.

Restaurant serves traditional Cretan food and various dishes. At the weekends, guests can enjoy brunches.

Breakfast in room is also offered. Special diet menus can be prepared upon request.

There is an on-site coffee shop in the hotel. Guests can enjoy the evening entertainment and read a book from the library.

Property also offers 24 hours open front desk and a gift shop. Property is 5 km from historical Kırkpınar area.
