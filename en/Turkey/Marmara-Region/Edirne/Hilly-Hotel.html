Description

Located only 1. 3 km from the famous Selimiye Mosque, which is included on UNESCO's World Heritage list, Hilly Hotel has a restaurant with panoramic views and 3 meeting rooms.

The hotel offers air-conditioned accommodation with free WiFi and a satellite LED TV.

All rooms and suites at Hotel Hilly come with modern decoration and they feature a safe box, minibar and a coffe/tea machine.

Some suites and rooms offer a sitting group and a spa bath. A rich buffet breakfast is served in the mornings.

The hotel also has an à la carte restaurant that serves international and local dishes such as yaprak ciger (liver cooked in Edirne style).

The front desk is available 24/7. Room service can also be provided. Only 2. 5 km to Edirne Palace, where the wrestling championship takes place, Hilly Hotel is 1.

5 km to Rustem Pasa Kervansarayi (Caravanserai). Greek-Turkish border gate is 6 km, Bulgarian-Turkish border gate is 14 km from the property.
