Description

Located on the largest island of the Princes Island, Buyukada, this charming historic mansion offers rooms with free WiFi and satellite TV.

Each unit has air conditioning. All Anastasia Meziki Hotel rooms feature hardwood floors and antique furnishings.

Private bathroom is offered in all units. There are shared balconies on each floor with a panoramic views of Marmara Sea.

Hotel Anastasia Mezikis restaurant serves regional and international cuisine. The hotel has barbecue facilities, and boasts a lush garden with a seating area.

Anastasia guests can rent a bike and explore the island. Staff at the tour desk can provide information on local attractions.

Other facilities include a laundry service and a currency exchange service. There are no cars allowed on the island, so guests can visit sights such as the Ayia Yorgi Church and Monastery undisturbed.
