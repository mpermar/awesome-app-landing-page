Description

Located 3 km from city centre, 5 km from IDO Ferry Port. Lova Hotel SPA offers modern rooms with free WiFi.

The hotel includes a spa centre for women and a mixed spa centre. Facilities include massage rooms, indoor pools, steam rooms, sauna and a Turkish bath.

An outdoor pool and a gym are also available. Nearest beach is located 500 metres away while guests can find shopping centres 700 metres from the property.

Featuring carpeted floors, the rooms of Lova Hotel include a LED TV, air conditioning, minibar and an electric kettle.

Each room has a balcony with views of the pool or the surrounding nature. Daily breakfast is served as an open buffet at the hotel.
