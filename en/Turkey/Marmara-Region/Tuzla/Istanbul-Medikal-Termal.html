Description

Located at the Asian Side of Istanbul, close to industrial area, this property features a thermal hot tub and a pool with hot spring water.

Guests will also find a spa and fitness centre at İstanbul Medikal Termal. Physiotherapy and hydrotherapy departments are also available.

Each room at this hotel is air conditioned and is equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Each room is equipped with a private bathroom. Extras include bathrobes and slippers.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. İstanbul is 33 km from İstanbul Medikal Termal, while Adalar is 14 km from the property.
