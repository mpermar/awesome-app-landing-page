Description

Only 350 metres to the beachfront, Venus Hotel has indoor and outdoor pools and spa facilities.

It offers air-conditioned rooms with a satellite TV and balcony. All the rooms at Hotel Venus come with simple furnishings.

They feature a private bathroom with hairdryer. A minibar is also standard. Daily breakfast is served in buffet style.

You can find many examples of traditional and international cuisine at the buffet restaurant.

The bars serve alcoholic drinks and beverages. The spa facilities include a Turkish bath, sauna and fitness room; massage services can also be provided.

The front desk is available 24/7. There is also a children’s playground. 5. 5 km to the Manavgat Waterfall, Venus Hotel is 8 km to Side town centre, where Apollo Temple is located.

Antalya Airport is a 1. 5-hour drive away. Free private parking is available on site.
