Description

Featuring the Kid's World where children can enjoy extensive and unique activities during your stay on a 3350 m designated area protected by a UV resistant roof, Otium Eco Club comes with a mini club equipped with security cameras, movie screening sessions and variety of board games.

You will find pools for children and babies. Ecological activities such as flower and tree planting and animal feeding are offered to support children's connection with the nature.

Along with a special kids restaurant and a playground, creativity activities are available such as origami, cooking classes, handcrafting workshops, English and Russian language classes and puzzle games to keep your kids inspired during your stay at Otium Eco Club.

Babysitting service, baby monitoring radio and strollers can be provided upon your request at a surcharge for your convenience.

Baby cots, baby bathtub and high feeding chairs are offered free of charge. Other baby facilities include bottle warmer and blenders to facilitate your meal preparations.

The 5-star all-inclusive Otium Eco Club Side is located 450 metres from a blue-flag beach and boasts an aqua park with 3 water slides and a wellness centre with a Turkish bath.

All rooms have a balcony. The property organises shuttle services between beach and the hotel once in every 30 minutes.

Otium Eco Club Side is situated 7 km from Side and 80 km from Antalya. Antalya Aiport is 70 km away and can be easily accessed by the hotels private airport transfer for an additional fee.
