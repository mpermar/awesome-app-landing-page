Description

Offering extensive facilities specifically-tailored for kids, cat-friendly VONRESORT Golden Beach & Aqua comes with a mini club equipped with security cameras, mini language classes and variety of games.

You will find pools for children and babies. Eco-friendly activities are available such as animal feeding.

Dancing sessions will be held for younger guests. Along with a special kids restaurant, mini disco and a playground, creativity activities are available such as origami, handcrafting workshops and puzzle games to keep your kids inspired during your stay at VONRESORT Golden Beach & Aqua.

Babysitting service, baby bathtub and strollers can be provided upon your request at a surcharge for your convenience.

Baby cots and high feeding chairs are offered free of charge. Other baby facilities include bottle warmer and blenders to facilitate your meal preparations.

Only 450 metres to the beachfront, this resort has a private beach area, outdoor pool with an aquapark and spa facilities.

A free shuttle to the beach is offered. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served in buffet style.

Guests can enjoy their meals at the on-site buffet or taste international cuisines at la carte restaurants at a surcharge.

Coffee and cakes, gozleme (Turkish pancake) and midnight soup are also available.

Side town centre is 9 km to the resort. Belek is 20 km and Antalya Airport is 55 km away.
