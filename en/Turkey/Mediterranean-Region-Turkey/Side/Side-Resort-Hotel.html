Description

The rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Featuring a private bathroom with free toiletries, rooms at Side Resort Hotel also provide guests with free WiFi, while selected rooms here will provide you with a balcony. All guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

A continental breakfast is served at the property.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool. You can play ping pong at Side Resort Hotel, and bike rental is available.

Side Antique City is 0.6 miles from the hotel, while Side Museum is 0.6 miles away. Antalya Airport is 34 miles from the property.
