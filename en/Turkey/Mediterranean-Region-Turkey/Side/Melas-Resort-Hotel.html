Description

All guest rooms comes with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a electric tea pot, a shower, free toiletries and a closet. The hotel has certain rooms with sea views, and each room is equipped with a balcony. All rooms have a seating area.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property. There's an in-house restaurant, which specializes in Turkish cuisine and also offers halal options.

Melas Resort Hotel offers 5-star accommodations with a hammam. You can play ping pong and pool at the accommodation.

Side Museum is 1.4 miles from Melas Resort Hotel. The nearest airport is Antalya Airport, 33 miles from the property.
