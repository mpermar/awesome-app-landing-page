Description

Bungalows comprise a double bedroom with traditional woodcarvings and artwork. They all open out on to a private deck overlooking the surrounding valley and include a sitting area and private bathrooms.

Regional specialties from the Antalya Province are served at the restaurant of the hotel. Dishes are homemade and prepared using fresh vegetables from the on-site vegetable garden. Free coffee and teas are available.

Guests can borrow books and guide books from the library or rent bikes and explore the green surroundings. The ruins of the ancient city of Olympos are a 15-minute walk from the Kibala Hotel.
