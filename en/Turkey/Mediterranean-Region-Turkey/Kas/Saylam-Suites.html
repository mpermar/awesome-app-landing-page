Description

Nestled over the hill overlooking the bay and the village, suites at Saylam feature sea views and fine furnishings.

Free Wi-Fi is accessible in public areas. All the suites include air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and a seating area.

Floor-to-ceiling windows open to a small balcony with 2 chairs and a table. Private bathroom offers a shower and a hairdryer.

The kitchen in each suite comes with a stove, electric kettle, utensils and a minibar.

A buffet breakfast is offered every morning on site serving local natural fruits, vegetables, eggs and other delicious products.

A bar is also available with refreshing drinks. There is a free shuttle service to surrounding beaches and Kas centre between the hours of 09:00 to 18:00.

Local beaches are about 600 meters away. Local cafes and restaurants are within a walking distance and offer tasty dishes, especially seafood specialities.

Dalaman Airport is 149 km from Saylam Suites. A shuttle service can be arranged on request at an additional fee.
