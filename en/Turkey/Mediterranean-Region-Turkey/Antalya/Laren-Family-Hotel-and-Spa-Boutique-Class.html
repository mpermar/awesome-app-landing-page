Description

Offering a garden and a seasonal outdoor pool, the pet-friendly Laren Family Hotel & Spa is situated in Muratpasa in Antalya.

Free WiFi is accessible throughout the property. A flat-screen TV with satellite channels and DVD player, as well as a CD player are featured.

Certain units feature a seating area where you can relax. Some units have views of the garden or city, whereas all has a private bathroom with a shower.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. Laundry, dry cleaning and ironing services are also available upon request at a surcharge.
