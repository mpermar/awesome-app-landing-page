Description

Situated in Alanya, Bayram Apart Hotel features a restaurant, 600 metres from Alanya Bus Station and 700 metres from Kleopatra Beach.

The units come with tiled floors and feature a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge, a TV with two Norwegian channels, free WiFi and a private bathroom with shower.

Some units include a seating area and/or a terrace. Guests at the aparthotel can enjoy a continental breakfast.

Bayram Apart Hotel offers an outdoor pool. A car rental service is available at this property and cycling can be enjoyed nearby.

Alanya Aquapark is 800 metres from the accommodation, while Alanya Archaeological Museum is an 11-minute walk away.
