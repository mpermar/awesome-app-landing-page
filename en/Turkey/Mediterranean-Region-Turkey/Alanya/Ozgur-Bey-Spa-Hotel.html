Description

Located in Alanya, Oba district, Ozgur Bey Spa Hotel is just 100 meters away from the famous 'Orange Beach' offering a seasonal private beach area.

The rooms are designed modernly and decorated with warm colors for your comfort. All rooms are provided with air-conditioning, flat-screen TV, telephone, desk, wardrobe, electronic safe and a minibar with a surcharge.

Each room offers a spacious private bathroom with telephone, free towels and toiletries.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. Ozgur Bey Spa Hotel offers 24-hours front desk at the property, a seasonal outdoor pool, café/restaurant where you can enjoy a good cup of coffee and dishes from international cuisines.

The property also has a Turkish spa center where you can relax and treat yourself with a full package of hamam, sauna, steam room and a variety of different massage sessions and beauty treatments.

Car and bike rentals are offered at the property. Public transportation is available within walking distance.

The property is surrounded by many restaurants and boutique shops for everyone’s needs and taste.

The city center is 15 minutes by public transportation while Alanya Castle is a 15-minute drive away.

The cable car can be accessed by public transportation within 15 minutes. Alanyum Shopping Centre is a 20-minute walk away.
