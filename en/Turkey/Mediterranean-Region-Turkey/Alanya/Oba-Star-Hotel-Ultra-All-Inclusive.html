Description

Less than 100 metres from a private sandy beach, this 4-star hotel is located 2 km from the centre of Alanya.

It has an outdoor pool with sun-lounger terrace and offers air-conditioned rooms.

Rooms at the Oba Star Hotel have balconies with panoramic views and are decorated in neutral colours.

They have modern wood furnishings and come equipped with satellite TV and a minibar.

The on-site restaurant serves international cuisine and Turkish buffet dinners. At the Oba Star, beach bar guests can enjoy a refreshing beverage.

Alanya Castle is 6 km from the Oba Star Hotel. Staff at the 24-hour front desk assist with car hire and free public parking is possible on site.
