Description

Featuring a private beach, the ultra-all-inclusive Azura Deluxe Resort & Spa is 1 km from the town centre of Avsallar.

It offers an outdoor pool with a water park and an extensive spa centre. WiFi is free in all areas.

The modern guestrooms at Azura Deluxe Resort & Spa come with air conditioning, a balcony and a safety deposit box.

A minibar and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels are available in each unit.

Most rooms offer sea views. Guests can sample various dishes and drinks from the hotel’s main buffet restaurant, 3 a-la-carte restaurants and snack bar.

Alcoholic or non-alcoholic drinks are offered free of charge at 5 bars on site. A-la-carte restaurants include seafood, Ottoman and Italian restaurants.

Guests can enjoy the indoor pool. The spa centre includes a sauna, fitness centre, massage rooms, and a hammam.

An array of activities can be enjoyed n site including billiards, table tennis, darts, cycling, diving and water sports.

Concierge services are provided. On-site parking is possible. The property is 98 km from Antalya Airport and 69 km from Gazipasa Airport.
