Description

Situated in Tekirova, this 5-star hotel offers modern rooms with a seating area and a private balcony.

Facilities include an outdoor pool a private beach area, a baby beach, 3 tennis courts and a spa.

The air-conditioned rooms at Rixos Premium Tekirova are decorated with wooden furniture and floor-to-ceiling windows.

All rooms have a minibar and a satellite TV. Some rooms have panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea.

Rixos Premium Tekirova’s entertainment team organises various games and sports activities during the day, such as water polo.

During the evening, guests can enjoy live shows with music and dance. A mini club is available for younger guests.

The children will love the Rixy Kids club, which features a cinema, a playground, a mini-disco and a children’s restaurant.

Turquoise Restaurant offers a wide choice of delicious meals in open buffet concept.

There are also other restaurants in the property offering different cuisines such as Turkish cuisine or Italian cuisine.

Guests can enjoy a variety of international cuisine at the main buffet restaurant.

American and international cuisines, as well as seafood are also offered at an additional fee.

Rixos Premium Tekirova is a 10-minute walk from Tekirova’s shopping centre, and Kemer’s touristic centre is a 15-minute drive away.
