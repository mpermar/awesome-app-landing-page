Description

Located right on the seafront, Sailor’s Beach Club features 2 outdoor pools, 1 indoor heated pool and a private beach area with free sun loungers and towels.

Free Wi-Fi and free private parking are available. Air-conditioned rooms include a satellite TV, minibar and a balcony with 2 chairs.

Bathroom comes with a shower, hairdryer and free toiletries. Rooms have garden or sea views.

All 3 meals are served in buffet style at Savarona Restaurant with an outdoor seating option.

Hotel’s à la carte restaurant offers Italian specialities in the evening. There are 2 bars for refreshments and a snack bar for quick bites in the afternoon.

Guests can work out in the fitness centre and then relax in the Turkish bath, sauna and spa baths.

Herbal bath, pedicure and massage facilities are offered. There are 2 water slides, children’s pool and a playground on site.

Antalya Airport is 65 km from Sailor’s Beach Club. Shuttle service can be arranged upon request with an additional charge.
