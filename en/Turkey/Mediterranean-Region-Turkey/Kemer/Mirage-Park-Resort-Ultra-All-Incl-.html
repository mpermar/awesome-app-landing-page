Description

Situated in Göynük, this 5-star hotel is surrounded by Taurus Mountains and Mediterranean Sea with pine trees and orange groves all around on a 110.

000 m² area. Mirage Park offers 4 swimming pools, spa centre with Turkish Bath, steam room and a sauna.

The bright rooms of Mirage Park have floor-to-ceiling windows, safety box and a satellite LED TV.

You will also find air conditioning, minibar which is replenished on a daily basis, balcony or terrace and a private bathroom with a shower.

Turkish and world cuisine is served at the à la carte Turkuaz restaurant and can be enjoyed indoors or outdoors.

Bosphorus à la carte restaurant serves sea food. Hotel has 9 bars and guests can taste delicious cocktails from the Mia Casa bar.

The spa centre offers variety of massages for relaxation. Ranging from table tennis, mini golf and beach volley to basketball, sportive guest can find a wide range of activities to enjoy.

Kids club is also available for younger guests. Kemer city centre is only 6 km from the hotel.
