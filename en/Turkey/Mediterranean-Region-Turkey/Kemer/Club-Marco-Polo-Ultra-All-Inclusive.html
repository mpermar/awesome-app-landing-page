Description

Renovated in 2018 and featuring a private sandy and pebble beach, Club Marco Polo is located on the Mediterranean coast on a 126.

700 m² area. The property offers 5 outdoor pools, an aqua park, spa centre and 4 tennis courts.

There is also a kids club for younger guests. Marco Polo has modernly decorated spacious rooms with split air conditioning system.

They are air-conditioned and equipped with an LCD TV. All rooms have a balcony with sea, garden or mountain views.

There are 4 restaurants on site, offering a variety of choices. Turkuaz restaurant serves all three courses as open-buffet while the other 3 restaurants are à la carte.

You can enjoy sea food at Marin Restaurant while Gusto restaurant serves a wide range of dishes from Italian cuisine.

Pasha Garden Grill offers traditional grilled Turkish dishes. There are also 3 bars on site.

For relaxation, the spa centre includes a Turkish bath, sauna and massage rooms. Fitness centre and 1 squash courts are also available on site.
