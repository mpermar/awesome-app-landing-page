Description

Air-conditioned accommodations at Melas Lara have a sitting area, flat-screen satellite TV, and a minibar. The private bathroom includes a shower, hairdryer, slippers and free toiletries. Each features a balcony offering garden views, and some also have sea views.

You can work out at the gym, or get relax in the sauna. Recreational facilities include tennis, billiards, ping-pong, darts, racquetball, mini golf and water sports. You can also get massage treatments, and the little ones can have fun at the kids playground.

Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served buffet style. You can enjoy your meal on the terrace restaurant overlooking the stunning view of the Mediterranean Sea.

Antalya Airport is 9 miles from Hotel Melas Lara, and airport shuttles can be arranged upon request, at an extra fee.
