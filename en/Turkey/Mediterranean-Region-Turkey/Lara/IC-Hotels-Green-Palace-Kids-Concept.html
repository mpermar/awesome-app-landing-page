Description

Offering extensive and one-of-a-kind facilities specifically tailored for kids, IC Hotels Green Palace comes with a mini club equipped with a 200 m spray painting activity area, a kids activity pool set on a 380 m area, 2 cinema room, video gaming room and a pizza cooking area.

Along with the kids club, the property offers a teenage club as well. The teenage club includes a video game area, DJ desk and a seating area.

Mini club has a soft play area, toy blocks and face painting facilities to keep your kids inspired during your stay at IC Hotels Green Palace.

A 136 m sandpit, photography activities, kids ferris wheel and a chair swing ride are also featured IC Hotels Green Palace's exclusive mini club.

A 120 m baby pool and a baby care room are also available for your convenience. The hotel's aquapark comes with 7 different slides for adult guests.

These slides includes two racing slides; super bowl and bomerango, while other slide types are twisters, black hole, multislides and a body slide.

Green Palace has 6 la carte restaurants, which includes Japanese, Turkish and seafood specialities.

Two pastry outlets offer treats throughout the day. Guests can choose among 3 poolside bars, a vitamin and a disco bar.
