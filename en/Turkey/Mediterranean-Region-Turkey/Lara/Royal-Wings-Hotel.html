Description

The air conditioned rooms at Royal Wings Hotel feature a modern interior decorated with wooden furniture. All rooms have a private balcony, some with scenic views of the sea.

Dining options at Royal Wings include traditional Turkish and international buffets, fresh seafood and Italian cuisine. Guests can savor fresh-baked goods at the bakery.

The entertainment staff organizes various activities during the day, including pool games. During the evening, guests can enjoy live shows. Royal Wings also has a spa, tennis courts, and a bowling alley.

Children can have fun at the mini club, or at the carnival, which features a merry-go-round and a Ferris wheel.

Royal Wings Hotel is 9 miles from Antalya International Airport and 11 miles to downtown Antalya. Free private parking is available on site.
