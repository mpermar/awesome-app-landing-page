Description

Located in Lara area, 500 metres from the seaside, the all-inclusive Aska Lara Resort & Spa Hotel has 2 territories divided by road.

It offers facilities including an aqua park, pools, restaurants, a fitness centre and Turkish bath.

The property offers a private area on the beach. Free WiFi is accessible in public areas and a 24-hour front desk are also available.

Rooms at Aska Lara Resort & Spa provide a flat-screen IP TV, air conditioning, electric kettle and a minibar.

Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Some rooms in the property have a balcony and some also feature land or pool view.

The hotel offers a courtesy room after check-out with a private bathroom for the day of your departure.

You can enjoy daily breakfast lunch and dinner in open buffet style. There are also 2 à la carte restaurants for winter season and 4 à la carte restaurants for summer season, which serve a variety of dishes from traditional Turkish, Far East, and Italian cuisines.

The property also has bars, where you can have a variety of refreshments and beverages.

Special facilities for younger guests are also available. A playground and a gaming room are offered.

Kids can enjoy the movies or enjoy the fountains in the water park area. Other facilities in Aska Lara Resort & Spa includes basketball fields, tennis courts, table tennis, a night club and water sports.

Services including massage and beauty treatments are also available at surcharge.

Sauna, Turkish bath, indoor and outdoor heated pool is available free of charge (heating is available only in winter season).

Laundry service is also offered for an extra fee. Antalya Airport is 12 km from the property while Antalya city centre is 15 km away.
