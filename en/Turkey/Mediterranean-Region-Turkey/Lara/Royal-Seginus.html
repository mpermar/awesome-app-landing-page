Description

Offering a seasonal outdoor and indoor pool, Royal Seginus is located in Lara. Guests can enjoy the Mediterranean breeze at the hotel's garden and terrace.

The property has a kid's club and a playground on site. Featuring a balcony, all rooms are fitted with a TV with satellite channels.

You will find a dressing room, safety box, electric kettle and a private bathroom in each room.

The hotel has an à la carte restaurant where you can taste variety of dishes from international and local cuisines.

Meals are served at the hotel's restaurant in buffet style as well. A bar and a snack bar are also available.

Guests can relax at the spa and wellness centre with a massage. A hammam, sauna and hot tub are also available.

The front desk is available around the clock. Concierge service also offered for your convenience.

Kaleici (the old town area) is 19 km away with many bars, cafés and local shops. Antalya Airport is 14 km away and airport shuttle can be arranged at a surcharge.
