Description

Featuring extra-large pools surrounded by extensive landscaped gardens, this 5-star hotel offers private access to the sandy Lara Beach.

Its stylish restaurants and bars provide ever-changing dining and entertainment opportunities.

The spacious rooms of Delphin BE Grand Resort re furnished with wall-mounted flat-screen TV, DVD and VCR players.

Some rooms also offer a balcony with views of the Mediterranean Sea. The spa facilities at Delphin BE Grand Resort include a marble hammam, a salon offering massages and beauty treatment, and a gym with professional trainers.

6 illuminated tennis courts, a beach volleyball field and aquagym classes are available.

The dining offer at Delphin BE Grand Resort ranges from Italian to Chinese cuisine, and barbecue specialities.

Several lounge bars provide night entertainment, and the beach bar serves light snack on the shore.

Delphin BE Grand Resort is just 8 km from Antalya Airport and 5 km from the city of Lara.
