Description

Situated on Lara Beach, this spa resort boasts an elegant landscaped pool area with palm trees and waterslides.

It offers air-conditioned guestrooms with balconies and panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea.

Rooms at the Concorde De Luxe Resort are spacious and have modern furnishings. They come equipped with satellite TV, free Wi-Fi, and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The De Luxe Resort boasts 17 restaurants and bars. Da Vinci offers Italian dishes with an à la carte menu.

The Concorde has a steakhouse, Havana Bar and a beach bar with sun loungers and sea views.

Guests can relax on the private beach or play a game of tennis on the flood-lit court.

The resort’s spa offers massages and body treatments. There is also an indoor pool and a games room with a bowling alley and billiards.

The Concorde De Luxe Resort is located 10 km from Antalya Airport and offers 24-hour front desk service and free public parking is possible on site.
