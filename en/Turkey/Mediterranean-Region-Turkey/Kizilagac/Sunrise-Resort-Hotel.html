Description

Featuring a private sandy beach, Sunrise Resort Hotel has a seafront location. Surrounded by a beautiful green garden, the resort offers large outdoor pools, water slides, and spa facilities including a sauna and a Turkish bath.

Modernly decorated, the rooms of Sunrise Resort include air conditioning, satellite TV and a minibar.

Some rooms also offer sea views. The main restaurant serves breakfast, lunch and dinner in open buffet style.

The à la carte restaurants offer selected dishes of Mexican, Indian, Russian, Japanese and Mediterranean cuisine, and seafood.

There are also 7 bars on site. During the day, the entertainment team organises activities, and nightly shows are also available.

There are 17 tennis courts and 4 football fields at the resort. Side town centre is within 20 km from the resort.
