Description

Offering extensive and unique facilities specifically tailored for kids, Ela Quality Resort Belek comes with a mini club equipped with security cameras, movie screening sessions and variety of board games.

You will find pools for children and babies. Depending on the seasonal harvest periods, children can join farming activities such as picking olives from the trees and planting flowers in the garden to enhance their horizon by connecting with the nature.

Ela Quality Resort Belek has open Q City, a new training and entertainment center where children can explore different professions.

Education through entertainment gives an opportunity to spend quality time while having fun and learning useful skills.

Q City offers several learning zones: Fashion House, Hospital, Dentist House and Fire Station.

In the Fashion House children choose their looks, run through a stage show with a professional choreographer, arrange photo sessions and conduct fashion shows.

In the Hospital and the Dentist House children try themselves as doctors: take care of patients, learn to provide first aid in case of sunstroke, bruises and cuts, study how the X-ray is arranged, find out how dangerous dental caries is and how to properly brush the teeth.

In the Fire Station children participate in a fire elimination and learn to behave in emergency situations.

Each zone has its own scenario and qualified trainers make sure each child is involved, learns something new and determines his or her profession preferences.

Babysitting service and strollers can be provided upon your request at a surcharge for your convenience.

Baby cots, baby bathtub and high feeding chairs are offered free of charge. Other baby facilities include bottle warmer and blenders to facilitate your meal preparations.

Designed like an Ottoman palace, Ela Quality Resort Hotel has a seafront location and a 270-metre private beach with a spacious pier.

Situated in an area where 15 golf courses are located, the hotel offers outdoor pools, water slides and spa facilities with a sauna and a Turkish bath.

The property also has 110,000 m² green area inviting you to enjoy the Mediterranean atmosphere.

Belek town centre is just 1. 2 km from Ela Quality Resort Hotel, while Antalya city centre is 42 km away.
