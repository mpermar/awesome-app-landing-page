Description

Located at the seafront, Limak Atlantis Deluxe Hotel offers a sandy beach, 5 swimming pools decorated with palm trees and a spa centre with Turkish bath and sauna facilities.

All rooms are air-conditioned and include furnished balconies. Modernly decorated rooms of Limak Atlantis feature a minibar and a TV.

All rooms have sea, garden or pool views. A complimentary bottle of wine and fruit basket is also offered in each.

Dynasty Restaurant serves dishes from rich Turkish cuisine. Sushi and Mexican food are also available in the à la carte restaurants of the hotel.

Guests can enjoy the live music with a glass of wine at the Lycia Bar. The swimming pools are constructed in double-level and they include hot tubs for relaxation.

Guests can also benefit from the fully equipped fitness centre. Nightly entertainments are also available.

The Manavgat Waterfall is 35 km from the hotel and the ancient city Aspendos is within 30 km.
