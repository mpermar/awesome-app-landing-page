Description

Offering extensive and one-of-a-kind facilities specifically tailored for kids, Asteria Club Belek comes with Asterix Kids Club equipped with security cameras, movie screening sessions and variety of board games.

You will find pools for children and babies. Along with a special kids restaurant and a playground, creativity activities are available such as origami, cooking classes, handcrafting workshops and puzzle games to keep your kids inspired during your stay at Asteria Club Belek.

Babysitting service and strollers can be provided upon your request at a surcharge for your convenience.

Baby cots, baby bathtub and high feeding chairs are offered free of charge. Other baby facilities include bottle warmer and blenders to facilitate your meal preparations.

Right near the beachfront, Asteria Club Belek has its own beach area, spa facilities and outdoor pools with water slides.

It offers air-conditioned rooms with a satellite TV and balcony. Only 40 km to Antalya city centre, Club Asteria is 31 km to Antalya Airport.
