Description

Featuring a private beach on the Mediterranean Sea in Belek, the 5-star Bellis Hotel is set in a large estate surrounded by pine trees with 5 outdoor pools, an aqua park and an indoor pool.

After a workout at the fitness centre, guests can relax at the Turkish bath or sauna.

Elegantly decorated accommodation at Hotel Bellis is equipped with free WiFi and air conditioning.

With parquet floors, each unit includes a balcony, a seating area and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Snack and bar services are provided in almost every part of the facility. 2 snack restaurants, 5 bars and 1 patisserie offer their services throughout the day.

You can also enjoy your meals at the hotel's 5 different à la carte restaurants at a surcharge.

You can enjoy yourself at the Zoopark or horse riding centre with horses and ponies.

Massage and spa facilities are provided at a surcharge. Various activities such as yoga, pilates, interactive games, volleyball, tennis, football are also available.

Guests can benefit from the concierge desk on site and airport shuttle services are provided at a surcharge.
