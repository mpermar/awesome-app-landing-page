Description

Featuring a private sandy beach and air-conditioned rooms, this resort has a seafront location in Belek.

The property includes 3 outdoor pools, 4 restaurants, and a spa centre with a Turkish bath and a sauna.

Zeynep Hotel offers elegant rooms decorated in warm colours. Each room includes an LCD TV and a minibar.

All rooms offer garden or sea views. The main restaurant serves meals in buffet style.

You can enjoy Chinese, Italian and Mediterranean cuisine at the à la carte restaurants.

The bars offer a variety of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, and delicious cocktails.

The resort includes a fitness centre and a tennis court for the sports-minded guests.

You can also benefit from the golf course on site. Belek town centre is 6 km from Zeynep Hotel.
