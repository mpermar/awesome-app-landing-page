Description

This 5-star hotel is located right at the beach, directly facing the Mediterranean Sea.

Surrounded by palm trees, Limak Arcadia offers a private beach, 4 tennis courts and 5 swimming pools, one with Pamukkale decorations.

The cosy rooms of Limak are decorated in warm colours and they all include a minibar, TV and a private balcony.

Some rooms also offer views of the Mediterranean Sea. Pinea Restaurant serves Mediterrenean cuisine combined with Turkish cuisine.

The hotel also has 4 `a la carte restaurants serving different world cuisines. The Odeon Bar offers drinks accompanied with live music and guests can also enjoy drinks at the seaside beach lounge.

Specially constructed, the outdoor swimming pool is decorated in the shape Pamukkale Travertines and the other pools feature water slides.

The hotel spa includes a Turkish bath and a sauna. Football field and a fitness centre are also available for sports-minded guests.

The Manavgat Waterfall is 35 km from the hotel and the ancient city Aspendos is within 30 km.
