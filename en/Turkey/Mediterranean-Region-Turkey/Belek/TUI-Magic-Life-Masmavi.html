Description

Offering a spa centre and a private beach area, TUI Magic Life Masmavi is located in Belek in the Mediterranean Region, 1.

2 km from Garden of Tolerance. The resort has an outdoor pool and water sports facilities, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Free private parking is available on site. All rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Certain rooms include a seating area where you can relax. You will find a coffee machine in the room.

Extras include bathrobes and slippers. TUI Magic Life Masmavi features free WiFi throughout the property.

You can enjoy a meal at the hotel's à la carte restaurant or sip your drinks at the bar.

A snack bar is also available. A playground and a kid's club is also provided with activities and special facilities for children staying at TUI Magic Life Masmavi.

The hotel's entertainment team organises live performances and evening shows during your stay.

Guests can relax in the sauna, hammam or enjoy a massage at the spa centre. For guests who wish to exercise can benefit from the fitness centre as well.

An indoor pool is also fitted. You will find a 24-hour front desk, a cash machine, hairdresser's and gift shop at the property.

You can play tennis, table tennis and darts at this resort, and the area is popular for windsurfing.

The resort also offers car hire. The nearest airport is Antalya Airport, 28 km from TUI Magic Life Masmavi.
