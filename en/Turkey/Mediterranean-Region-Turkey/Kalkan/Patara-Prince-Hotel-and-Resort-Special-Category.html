Description

Overlooking the bay of Kalkan, Special Category Patara Prince Hotel & Resort offers rooms with air conditioning and a Phillips plasma TV with 90 satellite channels.

Facilities include 3 outdoor seawater pools, 1 indoor pool, and a spa. The property has platforms and terraces at the seaside as a private beach.

With a unique design, all rooms of Patara Prince are equipped with a minibar and a private bathroom.

Most rooms feature views of the surrounding Taurus Mountains and the Turquoise Coast.

Patara Prince Hotel & Resort’s spa offers a variety of health and beauty treatments, such as Swedish and Thai massages.

Other facilities include a sauna, a hairdresser, 2 tennis courts, billiards and diving.

With live piano performances in the high season, Patara Terrace Bar is ideal for relaxing.

Guests can have open buffet breakfast in the garden at the Jasmine Patisserie. Patara Prince’s seaside à la carte Agora Restaurant serves Mediterranean cuisine with Italian dishes, pizzas and beef.

Breakfast in room option is available. Snack and barbecue services are also offered throughout the day.

It is only a 15-minute drive to several historical and Lycian archeological sites, such as Xanthos, Patara, Letoon and Sydima.
