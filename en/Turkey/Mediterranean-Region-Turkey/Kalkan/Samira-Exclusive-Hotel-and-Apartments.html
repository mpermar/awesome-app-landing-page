Description

Located in Kalkan, just 700 metres away from the beach, Samira Exclusive Hotel & Apartments offer an outdoor pool, a childrens' pool, a sun terrace and a garden.

A 24-hour front desk and free Wi-Fi access is available. Renovated in 2014, air-conditioned rooms in Samira Exclusive Hotel & Apartments feature a flat-screen cable TV, a safe box and a minibar.

Featuring a bath or shower, private bathrooms also come with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Rooms also offer a balcony where you can enjoy pool, sea and a garden views. Snack bar at Samira Exclusive Hotel & Apartments offer delicious samples of world cuisine.

There are also numerous restaurants and bars in walking distance from the property.

The bed and breakfast is 300 m from Kalkan Local Bazaar and 400 m from Kalkan Yacht Marina.
