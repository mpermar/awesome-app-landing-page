Description

At a wonderful location overlooking the Mediterranean Sea, the Elixir Hotel offers romantic accommodation in a building with natural stone exterior, wooden balconies and hand-crafted furniture inside.

Apart from its perfect location, the Elixir offers good facilities including a jewellery and gift shop, the largest natural Turkish bath in town, outdoor swimming pool, a gym, and free wireless internet.

All the cosy and warmly decorated rooms have beautiful wooden floors, private bathrooms, and balconies.

The hotel's restaurant offers a menu, as well as snacks and drinks. The inclusive breakfast is served as a buffet.

You can also enjoy barbecue on Thursday nights. Billiards is available in hotel's sports themed bar.

Dalaman Airport is 126 km away from the hotel and Antalya Airport is 241 km away.
