Description

The rooms at Rixos Sungate have satellite LED TV and a mini-bar. Air conditioning and safety box comes as standard. Certain units feature a terrace and spa bath. Bathrooms come with a bathtub and a hairdryer.

The extensive leisure facilities at Sungate include a gym, tennis courts and a 4-lane bowling alley. Guests can also enjoy a range of relaxing treatments at the spa including facials, massages and hydrotherapy. For younger guests, the spacious mini club, Rixy Club is available.

The all-inclusive Rixos has 2 buffet restaurants and 7 à la carte restaurants serving cuisines including Mexican, Italian and Chinese. Sungate also has a range of bars, a disco and evening entertainments.

Various theme parties, pool parties, DJ parties, world famous shows, concerts are organized during the summer season. Guests can also listen to live music several days a week.

Göynūk is a 5-minute drive from the resort. The center of Antalya, which features sights including the Archaeological Museum and the Hadrianus Gate, is 35 minutes away by car. Antalya Airport is 31 mi away.
