Description

Offering extensive and unique facilities specifically-tailored for kids,Akka Antedon Hotel comes with a mini club, movie screening sessions and variety of games.

You will find pools for children as well. The hotel's entertainment crew organises children events with themes such as Peter Pan, pirates and sleepover parties.

Children can join ecological activities such as tree planting and gardening. Along with a special kids restaurant and a playground, creativity activities are available such as origami, cooking classes, handcrafting workshops and puzzle games to keep your kids inspired during your stay at Akka Antedon Hotel.

Babysitting service can be provided upon your request at a surcharge for your convenience.

Baby cots, strollers, baby bathtub, baby monitoring radios and high feeding chairs are offered free of charge.

Other baby facilities include bottle warmer and blenders to facilitate your meal preparations.

With a seafront location a short drive south of Antalya, Akka Antedon Hotel is a luxury resort with a lagoon-style outdoor pool, a tennis court and a lavish spa and fitness centre.
