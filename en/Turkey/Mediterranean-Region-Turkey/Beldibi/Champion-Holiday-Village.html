Description

Located right on the shores of the Mediterrenean Sea, this resort has an outdoor pool, spa facilities and a private beach area.

It offers air-conditioned rooms with a satellite TV. All rooms at Champion Holiday Village come with a balcony.

A private bathroom with hairdryer is standard. Some family rooms include interconnecting rooms.

You can find bread and soup buffet, pita-pizza oven, hot meals as well as cold salad bar, fruit and dessert buffet at the main restaurant.

The à la carte restaurants serve seafood and Turkish dishes. Spa facilities include a Turkish bath, sauna and fitness room; massage services can also be provided.

You can play a game of tennis, table tennis and billiards on site. There is also a mini club for children at Holiday Village Champion.

Only 30 km to the Antalya city centre, Champion Holiday Village is 15 km to Kemer town centre.
