Description

At the hotel, all rooms include a patio with a garden view. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom, while certain rooms at Seraphim Cave Hotel also feature a balcony. All rooms at the accommodation come with a seating area.

A buffet or à la carte breakfast is served at the property. Guests can enjoy a meal at the in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of Turkish dishes.

Seraphim Cave Hotel offers a hammam. A variety of popular activities are available in the area around the hotel, including skiing and cycling.

The reception can provide advice on the area in order to help guests plan their day.

Kapadokya is 7 miles from Seraphim Cave Hotel, while Ürgüp is 3.6 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Nevsehir Airport, 24 miles from the accommodation.
