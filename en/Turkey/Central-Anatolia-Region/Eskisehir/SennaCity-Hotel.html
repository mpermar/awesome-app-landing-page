Description

Senna City Hotel is located in the city centre, along Porsuk River. The hotel offers 24-hour front desk service.

Tastefully decorated with modern furniture, the rooms of the eco-friendly Senna City Hotel include a flat-screen TV, air conditioning, electric kettle and a minibar.

All rooms have soundproofing and free WiFi. Some rooms also offer river views. Daily breakfast is served as an open buffet.

You can enjoy your breakfast at the on-site Senna Restaurant which features river view, and have good times on the terrace.

The tram stop is only a 2-minute walk from the hotel. The train terminal is a 10-minute walk away.
