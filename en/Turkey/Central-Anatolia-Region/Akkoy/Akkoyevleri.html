Description

At Akköyevleri, each room comes with a closet. Akköyevleri has some accommodations with mountain views, and rooms have a patio. All rooms come with a seating area.

Breakfast is available every morning, and includes continental, buffet and à la carte options. Guests can dine in the on-site restaurant, which serves American and Asian cuisine and also offers kosher options.

Guests at Akköyevleri will be able to enjoy activities in and around Akköy, like skiing.

Kapadokya is 13 miles from Akköyevleri. The nearest airport is Erkilet International Airport, 27 miles from the property.
