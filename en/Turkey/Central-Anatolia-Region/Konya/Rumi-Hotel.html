Description

Just 100 metres from the Mevlana Museum and Tomb in Konya, Rumi Hotel offers rooms with free Wi-Fi and satellite TV.

Facilities include a gym, a sauna, and a Turkish bath. The air-conditioned rooms of Hotel Rumi are decorated with wooden furniture.

All rooms have a minibar, a hairdryer, and a private bathroom. Some rooms have a spa bath.

Guests can play a relaxing game of billiards in Rumi Hotel’s games room. The hotel also offers a 24-hour reception and laundry facilities.

Rumi Hotel’s indoor restaurant serves local and international cuisine. It is also possible to dine outside on the terrace, which features panoramic views of the city.

Alaaddin Park is 1 km from Rumi Hotel, and the Archaeological Museum is 1 km away.

The historical sites, bazaars and shopping malls of the city are within walking distance.
