Description

This hotel in central Konya offers modernly appointed rooms with free Wi-Fi and 82 cm flat-screen TVs.

The in-house restaurant serves local and international cuisine, vegetarian and special diet menus.

The Pasapark’s rooms are fitted with contemporary furniture, and feature modern art and décor.

The bathrooms include hydromassage showers and ceramic design sinks. The Pasapark Hotel’s staff can arrange car rentals.

A convenient free private parking is available on site. As an alternative, Konya bus station is about 10 km away.

The Hotel Pasapark is located 400 metres from the Mevlana Museum. Guests can make use of the hotel's ticket service or request a packed lunch before heading out to visit the city.
