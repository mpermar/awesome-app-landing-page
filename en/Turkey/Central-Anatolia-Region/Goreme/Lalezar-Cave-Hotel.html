Description

Along with magnificent views overlooking Goreme, Lalezar Cave Hotel provides spacious cave rooms with modern amenities and a personalised service.

There are just 10 cave rooms at Lalezar Cave Hotel. Each one is carefully crafted from volcanic stone, allowing a cool nights' sleep in summer and cosy warmth in winter.

The well-presented rooms are appointed with wooden floors and satellite TV. Guests can make use of free wireless internet access in the public areas and rooms of the hotel.

Elevated above the town of Goreme, Lalezar Cave Hotel allows for magnificent views over the valleys and rock formations of Goreme National Park.

The friendly staff is happy to arrange day trips for you. After exploring Cappadocia, enjoy a glass of local wine on Lalezar's terrace as the sun sets over the valley.
