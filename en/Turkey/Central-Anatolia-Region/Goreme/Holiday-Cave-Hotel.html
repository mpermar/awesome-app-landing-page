Description

Holiday Cave Hotel offers a unique experience, providing accommodation in a converted traditional Cappadocian cave.

It offers an outdoor pool with hot tub, free WiFi and free private parking. The guestrooms of the hotel combine an archaic setting with modern amenities.

They all offer comfortable beds and bathrooms with hairdryer. Some feature an en-suite spa bath.

Guests of the Holiday Cave Hotel can enjoy the local flavors mixed with a selection of international dishes, served at the restaurant.

The property has horse riding facilities; hiking and cycling, are popular activities in the surroundings.

The hotel is located in Goreme, a getaway to explore the World Heritage geological formations and the famous rock churches.
