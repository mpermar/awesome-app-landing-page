Description

Built from Cappadocia’s natural lava stone, this unique hotel has an outdoor pool surrounded by picturesque hills.

The traditional Anatolian-style suites at Hotel Karlıkevi feature hot tub and spacious seating areas.

Admire the spectacular view over the valley and Uchisar village from your very own balcony.

Alternatively, you can sit out in the garden and soak up the Turkish sun. All suites at the Karlıkevi Hotel are individually decorated, each in a different colour, following the principles of feng shui.

Guests receive a customised bottle of wine, produced from the hotel’s own vineyard’s grapes.

Guests can decide to spend some time in the Yoga and Meditation Room or get creative in the Arts and Crafts Centre.

A hiking trip can lead to historical sites, such as the Üç Hisar Castle, and unique geological formations.
