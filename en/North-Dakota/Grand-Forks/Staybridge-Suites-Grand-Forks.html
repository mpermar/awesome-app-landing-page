Description

Free W-Fi is available in every modern suite at Staybridge Suites Grand Forks. A 32-inch flat-screen TV and separate sitting area are also included.

A daily hot breakfast is served at Grand Forks Staybridge Suites. Free appetizers and cocktails are available on select evenings and guests can enjoy the fitness center and laundromat facilities provided on site.

The University of North Dakota campus is a 15-minute walk from this hotel. Kings Walk Golf Course is 5 miles away.
