Description

Guest rooms at the Homewood Suites Harrisburg-West Hershey Area are furnished with all the comforts of home, including 2-burner stoves, microwaves and sofa beds. Wireless internet access is also available.

Additional amenities at the Harrisburg-West Hershey Area Homewood Suites include a free daily hot breakfast buffet and a free evening reception. The hotel also offers an outdoor swimming pool as well as a billiard room and a 24-hour convenience store.

The Civil War Museum, the Hershey Zoo and the battlefields of Gettysburg are all situated within driving distance of the West Hershey Area Homewood Suites. The offices of IBM, Delta Dental and many more are also easily accessible.
