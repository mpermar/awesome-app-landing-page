Description

The Nemacolin Woodlands Resort features elegantly decorated rooms with cable TV, coffee-making facilities, and an private bathroom. Select rooms have a spa bath or 24-hour butler services.

Enjoy a day of relaxation at the Woodlands Spa. Guests can visit the fitness center, tennis courts, or go zip-lining at the on-site Adventure Center.

Five-star fine dining at Lautrec hosts American cuisine with French flair. The Tavern Restaurant serves pub-style fare, while other options include full bar service or outdoor dining.

Ohiopyle State Park is 4.7 mi away. White water rafting tours from the Ohiopyle Trading Post are 7.5 mi away. Nemacolin Woodlands Resort is 12 mi from Uniontown and 33 mi from the Morgantown Municipal Airport.
