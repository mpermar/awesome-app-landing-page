Description

Each simply furnished room includes cable TV and air conditioning. The private bathrooms include a hairdryer. Ironing facilities are also provided.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the area, including tennis, basketball, mini-golf and swimming. The on-site petting zoo features rabbits, goats and more. Free Wi-Fi access is available.

Guests at Family Inn Bird-in-Hand can dine at the Bird-in-Hand Family Restaurant and Smorgasbord. The restaurant is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Amish Country Homestead and Amish Experience Theater are both just 2 miles from the property. Strasburg Rail Road is 4.5 miles away.
