Description

A flat-screen TV is included in each guest room. The private bathrooms have a hairdryer and free toiletries. The suites have a sitting area with a sofa.

Guests at the Seisholtzville Bear Creek Mountain Resort have access to the spa and health club. The resort also has a tennis court, ski storage and ski equipment rental. Meeting facilities are available.

The Grille serves contemporary cuisine and offers mountain views. Guests can also enjoy casual dining at Trail’s End Café.

Dorney Park and Wildwater Kingdom is a 20-minute drive from the resort. Da Vinci Science Center is 12 miles away.
