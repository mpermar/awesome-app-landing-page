Description

All units come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with a hair dryer and bath. There's also a dining area and a kitchen with a dishwasher.

A continental, à la carte or halal breakfast is available each morning at the property.

Couleurs K'fé has a grill.

A terrace is available on site and both snorkeling and windsurfing can be enjoyed within close proximity of Couleurs K'fé.

Saint-Gilles-les Bains is 25 miles from Couleurs K'fé, while Saint-Denis is 31 miles from the property.
