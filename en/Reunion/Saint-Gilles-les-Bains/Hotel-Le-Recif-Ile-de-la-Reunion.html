Description

All of the rooms and suites have balconies overlooking the grounds or swimming pool. They all come with air conditioning and a satellite TV. The private bathroom has a bathtub and hairdryer.

Le Récif’s Le Maloya Restaurant offers buffet-style dining throughout the day, with themed buffets every evening. The Comptoir Sud Pool Bar provides drinks, snack and a continental breakfast.

Guests at the Récif Hotel can rent a car or all terrain bikes for exploring Réunion island. On-site facilities include a gym, tennis and beach volley courts, ping-pong and traditional pétanque.

The hotel is one mile from St. Gilles-Les-Bains and Roland Garros Airport is a 45-minute drive away. Free Wi-Fi is available at the business center and parking is also without charges.
