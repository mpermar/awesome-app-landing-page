Description

From Male International Airport, guests have to take a 55 minute domestic flight to Kaadedhdhoo Airport, followed by a 20 minute speedboat ride to Robinson Club Maldives. Airport shuttle service are available 24 hours, and can be arranged for an extra fee.

Robinson Club’s bungalows and villas each feature a free standing massage bathtub. Interconnecting villas and bungalows are available. Internet access and flat-screen cable TV are provided.

The resort has a fitness program featuring free activities such as aqua gym and aerobics. A spa and outdoor swimming pool provides guests with relaxing respite. Laundry services are available.

Robinson Club Maldives’ restaurants offer stunning views of the island. They feature an extensive all-day dining and drinks menu.
