Description

A kitchenette and a living area with a sofa bed are furnished in each suite at Tuckaway Shores Resort. The traditionally styled suites feature island décor; they include a cable TV with DVD player.

Resort Tuckaway Shores is a 25 minute drive from Sebastian Inlet State Park and Cocoa Beach Pier. Melbourne Airport is about 6 miles away.
