Description

Rooms feature a flat-screen cable TV with free movie channels and a DVD player. A refrigerator and coffee-making facilities are also provided in each room.

Jimmy B’s Beach Bar offers panoramic views of the Gulf of Mexico. Guests can grab a bite to eat and a refreshing tropical-inspired beverage. After dining, guests can enjoy live music while taking in stunning views of the beach. Also offered at the Beachcomber is Player's Sports Bar and Grill, which offers a full liquor bar, satellite TV, and dining areas. Pool table and trivia nights are also available.

Downtown Tampa is within a 40-minute drive of the Beachcomber Beach Resort. Gulfport Historical Museum is within 5 mi of the hotel.
