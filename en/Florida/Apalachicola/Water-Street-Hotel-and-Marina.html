Description

A full kitchen and laundry facilities come standard in each suite at Water Street Hotel and Marina. Guests can also lounge in outdoor furniture on a private patio.

Free yoga classes are available to guests at Water Street Hotel and Marina. A business center and fitness center are also on site.

The hotel is a 10-minute walk from historic downtown Apalachicola. Locally owned and operated shops and restaurants are also within walking distance of the hotel.
