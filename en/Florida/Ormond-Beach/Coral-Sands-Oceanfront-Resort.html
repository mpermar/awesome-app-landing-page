Description

A microwave and a small refrigerator are in every room at the Coral Sands Oceanfront Resort. The air-conditioned rooms are decorated with tropical tones and wood accents.

After a day at the beach, guests at the Oceanfront Coral Sands Resort can enjoy a meal at the barbecue facilities. A laundromat and a 24-hour front desk are available for added convenience.

The Daytona International Speedway is 10 miles from the resort. The Ponce de Leon Inlet Lighthouse & Museum is less than 18 miles from the property. Shopping and dining on Daytona's Main Street are just a 14 minute-drive from the resort.
