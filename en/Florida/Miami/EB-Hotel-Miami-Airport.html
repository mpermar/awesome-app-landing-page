Description

A flat-screen cable TV, free Wi-Fi, and modern glass desk are featured in all rooms at EB Hotel Miami. A walk-in shower with dual shower heads is standard in each private bathroom.

The 24-hour fitness center has weights, treadmills, and elliptical machines. This Miami EB Hotel also features concierge services. A shuttle service is available upon request. Guests can mingle at the full-service lobby bar.

University of Miami is 7 miles from EB Hotel. Guests are also a 15-minute drive from shopping at Bayside Marketplace.
