Description

A small refrigerator, microwave, and 42-inch flat-screen cable TV are featured in every suite at SpringHill Suites Miami Airport South. A sitting area is also included.

Miami Airport SpringHill Suites guests will have access to free property-wide Wi-Fi, a business center, and a fitness center. A 24-hour on-site convenience store is available as well.

Downtown Miami and the American Airlines Arena, home to the Miami Heat professional basketball team, are a 15-minute drive from the hotel. Dolphin Mall is 5 miles away.
