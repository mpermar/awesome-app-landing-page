Description

Crystal Shores Resort offers luxurious two- and three-bedroom beachfront villas, all equipped with full kitchens, separate living areas and spacious bedrooms. All villas feature flat-screen satellite TV with DVD player.

Guests can dine in the Stilts Bar and Grill, which serves lunch and dinner in an attractive waterfront setting. There are also several other dining options within a few minutes’ walk of the hotel.

The Crystal Shores Marriott features contemporary health and fitness facilities, including a gym and swimming pools. Tigertail Beach is less than 1 miles away from the hotel, and it is just 30 miles to Big Cypress National Preserve.

Marco Island Airport is less than 5 miles from the hotel, and Everglades Airport is less than 40 minutes’ drive. Free on-site parking is available.
