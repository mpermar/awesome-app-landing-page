Description

All air-conditioned rooms include a work desk with ergonomic chair. Guests can enjoy pay-per-view movies, video games, and bottled water. Select rooms feature a sofa bed.

The Vinoy Renaissance St. Petersburg Resort Hotel has 5 dining options including Paul's Landing and The Veranda Café. The Marchand’s Bar offers an extensive variety of drinks.

At the Health Club guests can take part in daily fitness classes. After work out, the hot tub and sauna offer a chance for body relaxation. Guest can also borrow a book from the on-site library.

The Vinoy Renaissance offers guests access to an 18-hole golf course within 3 miles from the hotel.

The Vinoy Renaissance St. Petersburg Resort is just 2 miles from Tropicana Field and 1 miles from the St. Petersburg Aquarium. St. Pete Beach is 11 miles away.

07/28/2016

Incredible location, intoxicating ocean smell and incredible staff!

"The Vinoy is a beautiful property situated on the Bay in downtown St. Petersburg. There are tons of restaurant options in walking distance as well as most of the major museums. The staff at the Vinoy is exceptional. From the excellent bellhop, Dave, to the outstanding concierge, Cassie, no detail was ever overlooked. You do need to know the hotel also serves at a Country Club so the attire is a bit dresser than the average resort. Tennis is a big draw at the Vinoy and they have several tennis centered activities going on throughout the week. The fitness center is busy during the week around 5 so better to go earlier or later. We enjoyed the Alfresco Restaurant while we were there. The waitstaff was kind and the food was quite good. The pool shows dive in movies for Friday nights. That's the only kid activity we could find at the Vinoy. I would like to have seen an in room sitting service available. There are several bar areas to visit and although children are welcome, it would be easier to enjoy a glass of wine while they were in the room with a certified staff person. We did take home a diffuser filled with the signature scent that wafts through the halls of the Vinoy. It is an amazing scent that brings us back to the wonderful time we spent there. Last thing, the bay front rooms with a balcony are worth the extra money. We will stay at the Vinoy again when we visit St. Petersburg."
