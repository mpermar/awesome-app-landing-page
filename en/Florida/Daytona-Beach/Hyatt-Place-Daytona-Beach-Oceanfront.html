Description

A private balcony, 42-inch flat-screen cable TV, and small refrigerator is provided in all rooms. A corner sofa and coffee-making facilities are also provided by this oceanfront Daytona Beach Hyatt Place.

Complimentary daily breakfast is included. Free property-wide Wi-Fi and a business center are available. When not on the beach, guests can burn a few calories in the fitness center.

An outdoor tiki bar serves wine, beer, and spirits. Sandwiches, salads, and pastries can be ordered from 2 snack bars 24 hours a day. Fresh gourmet pastries and coffee are served at the Bakery Café.

Daytona International Speedway is 10 miles from the hotel. Guests can also explore local history 7 miles away at Ponce de Leon Inlet Lighthouse and Museum.
