Description

A fully stocked wet bar is included in all rooms at Ponte Vedra Inn and Club. Free Wi-Fi access, a flat-screen TV, and an iPod docking station are also provided.

On-site dining options range from the formal and elegant Seafoam Room to the casual Surf Deck Grille, serving beachside food and drinks.

Guests swim in one of the 4 heated pools at Ponte Vedra Club or cycle around the property. There are 15 tennis courts on site, as well as 10 shops and boutiques.

The Jacksonville Zoo and Gardens is 28 miles from the resort. Jacksonville International Airport is 32 miles away.
