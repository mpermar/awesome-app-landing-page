Description

A minibar and coffeemaker are standard in every classically-designed room at The Lodge and Club. With elegant style and modern amenities, each room includes a flat-screen cable TV, iPod docking station and DVD player. All rooms have a view of the ocean.

The Innlet Dining Room at the Lodge specializes in seafood dishes. Guests can enjoy afternoon tea with a variety of snacks or enjoy an evening cocktail at the Innlet Lounge.

Free access to the jacuzzi and modern gym is provided to all guests of the Lodge and Club. A spa offering massage services is also available.

Jacksonville Beach and the Beaches History Museum are both a 10-minute drive away.
