Description

Each room includes a flat-screen cable TV, iPad, and balcony. A small refrigerator and sitting area are also provided in each air-conditioned room. Featuring a bathtub or shower, the private bathroom comes with a hairdryer.

Massages are offered at the Florida's Hotel Pearl spa for a fee. Bicycles can be rented to explore the surroundings.

Camp Creek Golf Club is a 5-minute drive from the hotel. Deer Lake State Park is 4 miles away. Pier Park and Panama City Beach are less than a 15-minute drive from the property.
