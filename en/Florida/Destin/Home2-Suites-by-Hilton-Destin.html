Description

At the hotel, every room comes with a desk. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom, while certain rooms at Home2 Suites by Hilton Destin also provide guests with a seating area.

The daily breakfast offers continental and buffet options.

The accommodation offers a business center equipped with printing, copying and fax services. When guests need guidance on where to visit, the reception will be happy to provide advice.

Emerald Coast Centre is 1.4 miles from Home2 Suites by Hilton Destin. Northwest Florida Regional/Eglin Air Force Base Airport is 8 miles from the property.
