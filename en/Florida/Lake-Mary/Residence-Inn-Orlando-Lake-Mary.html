Description

Suites offer 37-inch flat-screen TVs with HBO, sofa beds and sitting and dining areas. They provide large work desks and 2 phones. The rooms also include hairdryers and ironing facilities.

Orlando Lake Mary Residence Inn serves a daily hot breakfast and offers a Social Hour Monday through Thursday. The hotel's Market sells snacks, souvenirs, and alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages. Grocery shopping service is available and guests can enjoy the poolside barbecue grill and fire pit.

The hotel features a exercise room and outdoor sports court. Guests can also take advantage of on-site coin laundry facilities.

Residence Inn is 3 miles from Seminole Towne Center and 5 miles from Lake Monroe. It is 16 miles from Blue Spring State Park and Morse Museum of American Art.
