Description

All accommodations have a microwave, small refrigerator, and tea and coffee-making facilities. All standard and accessible rooms at the Aqua Hotel in Fort Lauderdale have modern decor with aqua accent colors.

After a day at the beach, Aqua Hotel guests can relax on the terrace. Bicycle rental and iPad rentals are also available for added convenience.

The restaurants, bars and night clubs of Fort Lauderdale city center are a 10-minute drive from the property. Bonnet House Museum and Gardens and the shops of Galleria Fort Lauderdale are less than 2 miles away.
