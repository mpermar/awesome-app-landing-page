Description

Cable TV, microwave, and small refrigerator are provided in all rooms and suites at the Fort Lauderdale Victoria Park Hotel. Some suites have additional counter space with a sink.

A hot tub is on site at Victoria Park Hotel as well as a computer in the lobby for guest use. A guest laundry, free parking, and a Tesla as well as universal electric vehicle charging stations are available.

The hotel is 1.5 miles from Las Olas Boulevard shopping venues and just under 1 mile from Bonnet House Museum and Gardens. The Fort Lauderdale Hollywood International Airport is 7 miles away.

Wonderful homey hotel with superb staff

"We spent 2 x 3 nights in June/July at this small and familiar hotel. Nice international breakfast at a reasonable price. Ok sized pool, with a bit too warm water, in lovely green surroundings, with the luxury of complementary pool towels. We managed to get (free) parking close by every time, but suspect that parking can be a problem in high season. Would definitely stay here again. Cool that a circus used to have winter location here!"
