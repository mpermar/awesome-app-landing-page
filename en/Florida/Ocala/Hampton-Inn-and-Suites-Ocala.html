Description

All guest rooms have a microwave, small refrigerator, and flat-screen cable TV. A desk with an ergonomic chair is provided by Ocala Hampton Inn and Suites as well.

Access to a business center and free Wi-Fi are available to all Hampton Inn and Suites Ocala guests throughout their stay. A laundromat and snack bar are also available.

Downtown Ocala is 6 miles away. Guests can explore nearby trails on horseback 7 miles from the hotel at Hidden Lark Farm.
