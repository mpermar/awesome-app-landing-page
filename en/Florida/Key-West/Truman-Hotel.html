Description

All rooms at the Truman Hotel come equipped with a refrigerator. Each room is styled with hardwood floors, and bathrooms have tile showers and marble vanities. Some rooms have a private balcony or patio.

This hotel has a daily continental breakfast, and poolside sitting is available on the deck. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel.

The Truman is a 6-minute walk from the Ernest Hemingway Home and Museum, and Jimmy Buffett’s Margaritaville Café is half a mile away. Duval Street, where guests can find restaurants, bars, and shopping, is a 7-minute walk from the hotel.
