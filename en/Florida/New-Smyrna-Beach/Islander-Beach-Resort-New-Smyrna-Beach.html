Description

All units at this Smyrna Beach resort have a full kitchen and a flat-screen TV with cable. Free Wi-Fi is also featured and studios with ocean views are available.

A fitness center and a games room are on site at the Islander Beach Resort. A children’s playground and launderette is also available to guests and their families throughout their stay.

Daytona Beach International Airport is 23 mi from this hotel. New Smyrna Beach city center is 2.7 mi away. Flagler Avenue Boardwalk is 1 mi from the Islander Beach Resort - New Smyrna Beach.
