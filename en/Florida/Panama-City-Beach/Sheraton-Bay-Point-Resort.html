Description

A flat-screen cable TV, iPod docking station, refrigerator, and coffee-making facilities are provided in the rooms at Sheraton Bay Point Resort. Select rooms offer a balcony or patio with a golf or resort view.

Two championship golf courses, which includes a Nicklaus Design course, can be enjoyed by guests throughout their stay. A fitness center is also offered at Sheraton Bay Point Resort.

Bar 72 Pub & Eatery is an on site restaurant that serves a selection of soups, salads, and sandwiches for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

The Bay Point Lady Shuttle boat to Shell Island, fishing, and water sport rentals is available at the resort. Shipwreck Island Waterpark is 20 minutes’ drive away.

06/05/2018

Cost effective Resort Stay

"The overall price versus value really made the difference here. We will definitely be going back! Check-in was literally done in 5 minutes. I even signed up for the free Starwood Preferred program offered through Marriott. They gave me a temporary card on the spot.The overall resort amenities they provided were really top notch. They were very friendly and helpful right from the start. One of the grounds keepers even offered to show us how to get to the building. So, we followed him by driving behind his golf cart. That was a pleasant surprise. Breakfast as stated was not included. However, the selection was large but a little pricey. We opted for IHop 10 minutes away. The daily resort fee was resonable in comparison to many others and included parking. The 24-hr gym was definitely on point. You can set-up a dolphin tour for a decent price along the resort pier. However, we found a cheaper deal on Groupon through IslandTimeSailing. Keep in mind you will NOT be swimming with dolphins, but rather watching them from the boat. At the end of the long pier, you have access to the small beach area. It's an alternative to driving to the main part of the beach or only if your'e looking to suntan. Keep in mind that the water in that area is not as clear and has more seaweed. The pool area was nice, but kinda small. It was kinda hard to share if multiple families were out in the pool. Only 5ft deep, so no true swimming. The room was spacious and the bed was very comfortable. Every furniture piece was functional and added to the modern room style. The room included a 45" flat screen tv. The bathroom was on the small size. It can be tight if two people are trying to get ready at the same time. The shower was very spacious with a great waterflow. A definitely plus if you like long showers. The resort's location is about 10-15 minutes from everything. Overall, I would definitely recommend this resort! You will not be let down. 😁"
