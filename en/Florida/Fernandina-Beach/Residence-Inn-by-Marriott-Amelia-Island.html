Description

Every guest suite at the Amelia Island Residence Inn is furnished with a spacious living area and a work desk. Suites also offer free Wi-Fi and a 42-inch flat-screen cable TV with movies.

Residence Inn Amelia Island provides a daily breakfast buffet and a nightly manager’s reception. The gym and full-service business center are available for free. Guests can also relax in the outdoor jacuzzi or by the firepit.

Downtown Fernandina Beach and Amelia Island Plantation mall are a 10-minute drive from the Residence Inn on Amelia Island. Nearby activities include river cruises and carriage rides.
