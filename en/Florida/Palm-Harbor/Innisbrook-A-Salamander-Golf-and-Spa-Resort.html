Description

A flat-screen cable TV and kitchenette are provided in all suites. Select suites have a fully equipped kitchen, balcony, and sofa bed at Innisbrook A Salamander Golf and Spa Resort. Hypo-allergenic bedding available upon request.

A fitness facility and a full-service spa with massage service and sauna are on site at this Palm Harbor Innisbrook Salamander Golf and Spa Resort. A 24-hour reception and a business center are offered as well.

There are 5 on-site restaurants including Packard's Steakhouse and Market Salamander Grille. Osprey Bar and Turnberry Pub are also offer extensive wine and beer menus.

Innisbrook Golf and Spa Resort is 2 mi from the Gulf of Mexico. Guests will also be 10 minutes' drive from Honeymoon Island and 23 mi from Tampa International Airport.
