Description

An iPod docking station is provided in the contemporary rooms of this Atlantic Beach resort. Free Wi-Fi is also available and a small refrigerator is provided in each room.

Families will enjoy a Kid’s Club on-site and adults can take care of business at the hotel’s meeting facilities. A fitness center is also available so guests can keep up with their workouts.

The Azurea Restaurant is on site at the One Ocean Resort and Spa. Guests will enjoy a variety of European and American cuisine and a lounge for drinks is also available.

Adventure Landing is a 40 minute drive from this resort. Downtown Jacksonville and EverBank Field are a 25 minute drive away.
