Description

A flat-screen TV comes standard in each of this resort's suites. Select suites at the Westin Cape Coral Resort at Marina Village also feature full kitchens and private balconies.

A fitness center and 2 seasonal outdoor pools are available for guest use. The property is adjacent to a full-service spa and salon as well as retail stores at Marina Village shopping center.

The Nauti Mermaid Dockside Bar and Grill at the Westin Cape Coral has a casual menu and live music on weekends. Fine dining is available at the on-site Marker 92 Waterfront Bar & Bistro.
