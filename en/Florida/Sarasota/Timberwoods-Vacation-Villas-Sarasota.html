Description

A full kitchen is featured in every villa of this Sarasota Resort. Guests will enjoy free Wi-Fi in all units and a flat-screen TV with cable and a DVD player.

A mini golf putting green is on site at the Sarasota Timberwoods Vacation Villas and guests can also enjoy an exciting game of shuffleboard. Barbecue facilities are available on site.

This property is less than a 10 minute drive from the shores of Siesta Key Beach. The Ringling Museum of Arts is 13 miles away and Sarasota city center is a 15 minute car ride from the resort.
