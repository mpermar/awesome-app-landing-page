Description

Each one or two-bedroom villa at Royal Flamingo Villas has a living room with cable TV and a DVD player. Decorated with light colors, all spacious villas include a dining area and a private patio with view of the Ocean.

Free access to the outdoor pool and barbecue facilities is provided to all guests of the Royal Flamingo. With beautifully landscaped gardens, the resort has boat docking spots and beach tiki huts.

Hillsboro Pines Golf Course and the Boca Raton Museum of Art are both 5 miles from the resort.
