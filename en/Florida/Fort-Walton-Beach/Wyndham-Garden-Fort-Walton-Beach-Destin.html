Description

A microwave and refrigerator come standard in each room at Wyndham Garden, and select rooms have a kitchenette. A flat-screen cable TV and a DVD player are provided as well. Each private bathroom is complete with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

A fitness center and business center are accessible to guests at the Fort Walton Beach Destin Wyndham Garden hotel. Bicycles are available for guest use, and fishing and snorkeling are possible in the nearby surroundings. For added guest convenience, a 24-hour front desk, daily maid service, and luggage storage are offered.

Gulfarium Marine Adventure Park is 3 minutes’ drive from this hotel. Guests will be just over 1 miles from Emerald Coast Convention Center, and 1.7 miles from Indian Temple Mound Museum.
