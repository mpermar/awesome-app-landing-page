Description

All rooms feature cable TV, microwave, and small refrigerator. Select rooms at Flamingo Waterpark Resort area character-themed suites and may include a spa tub or a balcony with water park view.

Free transfer service is provided to area theme parks. A heated lazy river, water slides, and shaded pool seating at the water park are available for a fee at this Florida Flamingo Waterpark Resort.

The cafe offers continental breakfast, American fare a la carte, and a full bar. A snack bar and grill, coffee shop, and lounge are on site.

The Orlando Premium Outlets luxury shopping center are 19 miles away. Aquatica, SeaWorld’s water park, and Discovery Cove dolphin experience are 17 miles away.
