Description

A flat-screen cable TV is included in all Hyatt Place rooms as well as a small refrigerator and coffee machine. The private bathroom features a hairdryer and bathrobes.

A fitness center is on site, and fishing, diving, and snorkeling can be enjoyed in the surroundings. Water sports hires, a marina, and boat docking are available at the Marathon Florida Keys resort for a charge.

The resort is near Fishermen's Hospital, which is just over half a mile away. The Turtle Hospital is a short drive from the property. Key Colony Beach Golf is 6.8 miles away.
