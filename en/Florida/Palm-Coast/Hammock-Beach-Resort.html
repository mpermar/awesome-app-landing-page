Description

Guest can practice on the putting green, float down the lazy river, or take advantage of the state-of-the-art tennis complex. Spend the afternoon in the spa, work out in the fitness center, or simply relax on the private beach and enjoy the warm Florida sunshine.

Suites include cable TVs and a fully equipped kitchen or kitchenette. Dining areas are also featured in Hammock Beach Resort guest suites, as are private bathrooms.

After a restful night's sleep in the Hammock Resort's spacious one to 4 bedroom suites, dine at one of 4 on-site restaurants or explore scenic trekking and running paths. On-site children's program are also available.

Washington Oaks Gardens State Park is 8 minutes' drive from the resort while Daytona Beach International Airport is 40 minutes' drive.
