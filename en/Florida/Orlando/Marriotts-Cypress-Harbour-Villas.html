Description

All 2-bedroom apartments feature a separate living room furnished with a sofa bed and dining table. Also featured is a balcony or porch overlooking the lake. The utility room contains a washing machine and tumble dryer.

There is a lakeside outdoor pool, a hot tub, and sauna available for use at the Marriot's Cypress Harbor. Cypress Spa is located on site. Younger guests can enjoy themselves at the games room.

Cypress Harbor offers Edy’s Icecream and Starbucks on site. Marriott's Cypress Harbor is just 6 miles from Walt Disney World and the Universal Orlando. Orlando International Airport is only 13 miles away.
