Description

A full kitchen and cable TV are featured in all fully furnished villas at Westgate Blue Tree. A spa tub, washing machine, and tumble dryer are provided in select villas.

A tennis, volleyball, and basketball court are available for active guests who want to stay in shape. A game room and miniature golf course are available as well at this Florida Westgate Blue Tree.

Japanese, Lebanese, and Italian cuisine are 5 miles away in Restaurant Row. Aquatica, SeaWorld's water park, is a 10-minute drive away. A limited complimentary transportation service to theme parks is available to guests.

Lake Buena Vista is a great choice for travelers interested in Disneyland, family-friendly trips and theme parks.
