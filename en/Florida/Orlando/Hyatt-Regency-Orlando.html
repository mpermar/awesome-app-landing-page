Description

Rooms feature conveniences such as complimentary WiFi, a small refrigerator, and a single cup coffee maker. Entertainment options include a 65-inch flat-screen TV, a built-in bathroom mirror TV, and a dockable iPod alarm clock radio. Business guests will enjoy the convenience of a workstation with a desk and chair.

Hyatt Regency Orlando is within walking distance of the Orange County Convention Center, which is connected to the hotel via two pedestrian walkway bridges.
