Description

Marriott Vacation Club Lakeshore Reserve provides each room and villa with cable TV and a DVD player. They are furnished with a dining area and in-room safes.

A biking trail and a sauna are available at the hotel. Guests can use the gym and movies are available for rent at the Lakeshore Reserve Marriott Vacation Club.

Blossom’s Pool Bar and Grill serves sandwiches, appetizers and drinks for lunch. The Marketplace also offers sandwiches and Starbucks coffee.

A children’s maze and activities are available at Marriott Vacation Club. For added convenience barbecue grills and laundry facilities are on site.

SeaWorld Adventure Park is 5 miles from the hotel. Marriott Lakeshore Reserve is 12 miles from Orlando International Airport and 19 miles from Disney.
