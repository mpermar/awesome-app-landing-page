Description

A water park with 2 water slides, a 200 feet slide and a 50 feet slide is on site. A kids' activity center and an arcade with console and touch-screen games are also available at Marriott Orlando World Center. An on-site business center is provided.

Cable TV, coffee machine, and minibar are standard in all rooms. Some rooms have a pool view and a sofa bed. Desks are available, as is WiFi.

This resort has 10 restaurants including Siro Italian Urban Kitchen, Mikado Japanese Steakhouse, and a fast-food court. A poolside bar and grill and a Starbucks® Coffee shop are on site as well.

Universal Studios Orlando and SeaWorld are less than a 15-minute drive from this resort. Orlando Premium Outlets is 3 miles away.

Lake Buena Vista is a great choice for travelers interested in Disneyland, family-friendly trips and theme parks.
