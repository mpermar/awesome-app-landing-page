Description

Courtyard Orlando Lake Buena Vista in Marriott Village features a swimming pool and fitness center. The hotel also offers a food court with ice cream, pizza, deli items, and coffee.

The Bistro, an on-site restaurant, serves breakfast and dinner. Guest rooms at the Lake Buena Vista Courtyard are equipped with cable TV and work desks. Coffee machines and refrigerators are also included.

SeaWorld is 4.5 mi from the property.Universal Orlando Resort is 7.8 mi away. The Orange County Convention Center is 6.1 mi from the Courtyard by Marriott Orlando Lake Buena Vista in the Marriott Village.

Lake Buena Vista is a great choice for travelers interested in Disneyland, family-friendly trips and theme parks.
