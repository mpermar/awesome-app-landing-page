Description

Free WiFi, a cable TV, and microwave are featured in each room at the Fairfield Inn Suites Orlando At SeaWorld. Rooms also include access to the Shamu Sea World pool.

Guests can enjoy a large continental breakfast in the mornings. For lunch and dinner, there is the Splash Zone Bar & Grill, which offers a casual dining experience.

An outdoor pool with a children's play area is offered. The Fairfield Inn Suites also has a gym which includes treadmills and elliptical machines.

The Aquatica Water Park is 1.1 mi away. Orlando International Airport is a 12.3 mi from the Fairfield Inn Suites by Marriott Orlando At SeaWorld .
