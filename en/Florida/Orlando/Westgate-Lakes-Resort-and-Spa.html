Description

A kitchenette, cable TV with DVD player, and WiFi are included in each spacious villa and studio at Westgate Lakes Resort and Spa. Select villas include a fully equipped kitchen, washer, and dryer.

Tennis courts, a miniature golf course, and a game room are all part of this Orlando resort. A 24-hour reception is also available for added convenience. Electric car charge stations are on site.

Guests can also enjoy a meal at on-site restaurants Drafts Sports Bar & Grill, Sid's Bistro, or Cordavano Joe's Pizza. Guests can enjoy the convenience of the on-site Marketplace & Deli.

The Orange County Convention Center is 10 minutes' drive from the Orlando Westgate Lakes Resort. Orlando International Airport is 13 miles from the property.
