Description

Some rooms feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day.This Homewood Suites features free WiFi throughout the property. A flat-screen TV with cable channels is available.

Start the morning at the Universal Studios Homewood Suites with a free breakfast buffet before purchasing theme park tickets from the front desk. Free parking is provided on site. Guests can also take advantage of the hotel's free shuttle service to many of these popular locations.

At the end of the day, enjoy the free evening manager's reception from Monday through Thursday. The limited menu includes beer and wine as well as small plate food options, which change nightly.
