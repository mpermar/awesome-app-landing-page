Description

A refrigerator is available in each room at Sheraton Lake Buena Vista Resort. A flat-screen cable TV is also included.

An outdoor hot tub is featured at the Sheraton Resort Lake Buena Vista. Guests can pamper themselves in the Top of the Palms Spa or relax in a poolside cabana. A 24-hour fitness center, a games room and a convenience store are also on site. Car hires and parking are available on site.

A variety of dining options are also available at the Sheraton Resort. Guests can enjoy casual poolside dining at the 27 Palms Grill and Zest Restaurant features a la carte and buffet options with American cuisine.

Universal Studios Orlando is 8 mi from this resort. Guests will be 4.4 mi from SeaWorld Orlando.

Lake Buena Vista is a great choice for travelers interested in Disneyland, family-friendly trips and theme parks.
