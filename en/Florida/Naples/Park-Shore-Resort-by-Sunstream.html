Description

Rooms include a TV and DVD player. Every room is equipped with a private bathroom with a shower. Extras include complimentary toiletries and a hairdryer.

A heated outdoor pool and hot tub are available for guests to enjoy. BBQ facilities are also offered. A tennis court, racquetball court, and fitness center are available onsite. Complimentary private parking is also available .

Tin City is 4.3 mi from Park Shore Sunstream, while Naples Museum of Art is 1 mi from the property. Southwest Florida International Airport is 23 mi away.
