Description

Two flat-screen TVs are included in each suite at Homewood Suites by Hilton Chicago-Downtown. The kitchenette has a 2-burner stove and full-size refrigerator. Grocery shopping services are offered.

The gym features a flat-screen TV and views of the city. A gift shop is open to guests, and a free dinner buffet with drinks on select weekdays is served.

Millennium Park, Navy Pier, and the Art Institute are within 1.2 mi of the Chicago Downtown Homewood Suites. Access to the CTA Red Line is down the street.

River North is a great choice for travelers interested in restaurants, shopping and food.
