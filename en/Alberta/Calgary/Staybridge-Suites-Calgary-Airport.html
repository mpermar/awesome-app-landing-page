Description

A flat-screen cable TV, sitting area, refrigerator and private bathroom are included in rooms at Staybridge Suites – Calgary Airport. Dark wood furnishings, light-colored linens and neutral accents are featured.

A fitness center and a hot tub are available at Staybridge Suites. Free shuttle service to and from the airport is provided. A free guest laundromat is open 24 hours a day. Guests can enjoy social gatherings several nights per week with complimentary appetizers.

The Calgary Zoo is 3.5 miles and Calgary city center is 5.5 miles drive from Staybridge Suites- Calgary Airport. Scotiabank Saddledome is 14 minutes away.
