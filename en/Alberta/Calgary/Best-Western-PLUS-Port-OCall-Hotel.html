Description

A flat-screen TV and a desk are available in rooms at Best Western PLUS Port O'Call Hotel. A refrigerator, a microwave and coffee-making facilities are provided. Each air-conditioned room features a balcony.

Showcasing mountain views, the Sky Harbour Restaurant has all day dining options. Jetz Bar and Lounge features a light menu and large screen TVs to enjoy sports.

A fitness center, hot tub and a sauna are available for guest use. A business center is available, as well as a free airport shuttle.

Calgary Zoo Botanical Garden & Prehistoric Park is 7 miles away. Aero Space Museum is a 5-minute walk from Port O'Call Best Western PLUS.
