Description

A flat-screen cable TV is provided in all air-conditioned rooms at the Hampton Inn & Suites Edmonton/West. A coffee maker and a work desk are also included. Free toiletries are provided for added convenience.

A 24-hour front desk greets guests at the Edmonton/West Hampton Inn & Suites. Laundry services and an airport shuttle can be arranged. A business center is on site and free newspapers are provided.

The Royal Mayfair Golf Club is an 11-minute drive away. Royal Alberta Museum is 4 miles from the Hampton Inn & Suites Edmonton/West.
