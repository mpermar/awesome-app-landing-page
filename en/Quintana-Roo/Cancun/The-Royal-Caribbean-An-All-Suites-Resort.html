Description

Each air-conditioned room includes free WiFi, flat-screen cable TV, a mini-bar and coffee maker, while suites also have a full kitchen. All include mahogany furniture and original artwork.

The Royal Caribbean - An All Suites Resort offers free kayaks and sailing on the lagoon. There is an on-site tennis school and a watersports center offering PADI scuba diving courses.

The Trade Winds restaurant serves international cuisine and has a takeaway counter. There is also a café, a lobby bar, a gym and various on-site shops. An express spa offering massages, facials or manicure is also available.

The Royal Caribbean - An All Suites Resort is located in the Cancun Hotel Zone, 7.5 mi from Cancun Airport. A free shuttle connects the resort with sister resorts.
