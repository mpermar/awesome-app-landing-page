Description

The Beachscape Kin Ha Villas and Suites provide guests free Wi-Fi in public areas, a tour desk and a 24-hour front desk. Guests can work out in the gym or use the laundry facilities.

Suites include cable TV and a private balcony. A full private bathroom, cable TV, balcony and full kitchen are also some of the features provided at these accommodations.

Kin Ha Villas and Suites features 2 on-site restaurants that include a buffet breakfast and lunch, and an à la carte menu is available for dinner. Guests can also dine at La Palapa, a beach bar and grill. There is a wide range of restaurants and bars within a short walk.

The hotel has direct access to the beach, which is popular for snorkeling and swimming. The property also provides convenience stores and spa services on site.

Beachscape Kin Ha Villas is 12 miles from the Cancun Airport.
