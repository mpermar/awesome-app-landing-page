Description

All the rooms and suites feature a stylish Caribbean décor. Each one has an air conditioning system, satellite TV, iPod docking station, DVD player, daily restocked minibar and wireless internet access. The balcony or porch of each room offers spectacular views of either the property's gardens or the Caribbean Sea. Some units are furnished with a large hot tub, or swim-up facilities.

The Gourmet Experience include restaurants and bars mixing sit-down menus, exotic drinks, and fine international cuisine. The restaurants include Mexican, Italian, Asian and international-style dishes. The bars serve tropical drinks or a Tequila Lounge.

Azul Beach is also furnished with meeting rooms, a kid's club, scuba diving trips and lessons, car rental services, and shuttle transportation to Cancun and Playa del Carmen.
