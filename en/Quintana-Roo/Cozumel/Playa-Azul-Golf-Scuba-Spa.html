Description

Playa Azul Golf Scuba Spa Hotel rooms include a private balcony with views of the Caribbean Sea. The rooms also have satellite TV, access to free Wi-Fi and a private bathroom.

After a day of touring the island or golf at the Cozumel Country Club with passes from the hotel, guests at the Playa Azul can relax in the full-service Cozumel Hotel Spa.

The Playa Azul Scuba Hotel provides a breakfast buffet at the Palma Azul Restaurant and the La Palapa Snack Bar at the Beach serves a light menu and drinks.

The hotel is located 3.5 miles from La Plaza Cozumel. Playa Del Carmen is 10 miles from the Playa Azul Hotel.
