Description

The elegant and fully furnished suites all include satellite TV, a DVD player, stocked minibar with beer, water and soft drinks, a liquor cabinet with a bottle of whiskey, rum, vodka and tequila, bathrobes and slippers, plus a full bathroom with a hot tub for 2.

The Gourmet Inclusive Experience brings the guests a variety of gourmet options to choose from. La Vista Restaurant has breakfast, lunch and dinner services with an extensive menu. Every day, guests are provided with a new menu. There are also swim-up and lounge bars where guests can sip a cocktail or relax with a drink. Room service is available 24 hours a day.

Romantic dinners by the beach can be arranged for an additional fee. Daily morning and evening activities are organized by the friendly staff at Hidden Beach Resort. Activities such as diving and taking diving lessons in the resort's pool can be arranged. A library that includes a DVD selection is on the guests disposition. The fitness center includes a sauna and steam bath.

Playa del Carmen can be reached in a 25-minute drive.
