Description

Each of the rooms at Crowne Plaza Birmingham City have air conditioning, a flat-screen satellite TV with pay movies and a private bathroom. There is 24-hour room service.

The stylish b1 Restaurant and Bar offers guests a place to enjoy a drink with friends or a delicious meal. Modern cuisine is served in the chic restaurant with a seasonal à la carte or a table d’hôte menu available.

There are 11 meeting rooms at the property, with the largest room holding up to 300 delegates. Free WiFi is available in all meeting rooms.

The famous Birmingham Bull Ring shopping center can be reached with a 10-minute walk. Cultural attractions such as Birmingham Cathedral and the Museum and Art Gallery are both within 0.5 miles of the hotel.
