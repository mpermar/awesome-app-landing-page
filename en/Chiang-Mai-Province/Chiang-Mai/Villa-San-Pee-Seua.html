Description

Well-designed traditional Thai rooms feature free Wi-Fi and scenic river views. Each comes equipped with a flat-screen TV and personal safe. Private bathrooms have a bathtub and hairdryer.

From Villa San Pee Seua, a short boat ride takes guests to Nawarat Bridge, which is a 5-minute walk to the famous Night Bazaar. It is just over half a mile from a grocery store and a 20-minute drive from Chiangmai International Airport.
