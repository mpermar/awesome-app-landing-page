Description

DusitD2 Chiang Mai is a 15-minute drive from Chiang Mai International Airport. The Sunday Walking Street is about half a mile from the hotel.

The designer hotel blends modern interiors with a touch of traditional Thai décor. Each room is equipped with a flat-screen TV, DVD player and minibar. They have large sitting areas and some rooms have a walk-in closet.

Guests can enjoy a relaxing massage in the award-winning Devarana Spa’s luxurious treatment suites, or admire city and mountain views while exercising at the well-equipped DFit Health Club on the top floor. The hotel also provides car rental and concierge services.

Named Thailand’s best restaurant in 2009 by a local magazine, Moxie Restaurant offers a regularly updated international menu. Mix Bar boasts of a lighted wine wall behind the bar. There is 24-hour room service.

Chang Khlan is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, night markets and street markets.
