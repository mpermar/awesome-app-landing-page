Description

At the hotel, every room is equipped with a desk and a flat-screen TV. Rooms contain a private bathroom and a shower, and some rooms will provide you with a balcony. All rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

A continental, Asian or vegetarian breakfast is served at the property.

IWualai Hotel has an outdoor pool.

Languages spoken at the reception include English and Thai.

Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Wat Phra Singh, City Art & Cultural Center and Three Kings Monument. Chiang Mai Airport is 1.2 miles away.

Hai Ya is a great choice for travelers interested in street food, markets and night markets.

08/06/2017

Newish hotel. Clean, modern, very close to airport and extremely friendly staff!

"Travelling family of 6 with 4 kids (19, 17, 13 and 10).I don't know what it is about Chiang Mai but we had 2 accommodation cancel on us at very short notice and had to find replacements quickly! So we stumbled across iWualai and were delighted. Having just done a 3 day, 2 night trek we were tired and dirty and needed somewhere inexpensive to have a hot shower and put our heads down for a night before flying into Phuket the next day. Actually, this hotel met our expectations and more. I would describe it for Uk readers S a bit like a Premier Inn in style. Modern, clean, largish rooms (comfortably sleeping 3 each) with a lovely hot shower! There was a pool that we didn't use but looked nice. Fresh coffee available at all times. Free bottled water (as much as you want). Very friendly staff. Location wise, is 10 minutes by red taxi to the airport (30 baht per person - about 75p). Also only 10-15 minutes walk to the night bizarre and Walking Sunday market where there are loads of places to eat and drink as well as temples galore to see!A short one night stay, but we were very comfortable. Great value for money and I would recommend for families."
