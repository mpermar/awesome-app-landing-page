Description

At the hotel, all rooms are equipped with a terrace with a pool view. Each room has a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hair dryer. All rooms at Viangngam Lanna Villa come with a seating area.

The daily breakfast offers continental and buffet options.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental is available at this 4-star hotel.

Speaking English and Thai at the reception, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical information on the area.

Kad Suan Kaew Shopping Center is 1.3 miles from the accommodation. Chiang Mai Airport is 3.1 miles away.

Chang Phueak is a great choice for travelers interested in nature, friendly locals and relaxation.
