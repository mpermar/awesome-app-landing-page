Description

The rooms has air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a fridge, a electric tea pot, a shower, free toiletries and a desk. Every room is equipped with a private bathroom, and some accommodations at the hotel have a balcony. Guest rooms feature a closet.

My Chiangmai Boutique Lodge offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

Tha Pae Gate is half a mile from the accommodation, while Wat Gate is half a mile from the property. The nearest airport is Chiang Mai Airport, 2.5 miles from My Chiangmai Boutique Lodge.

Chang Moi is a great choice for travelers interested in street food, sightseeing and local food.
