Description

Yaang Come is just under 1 miles from the Sunday Walking Street and Chiang Mai Train Station. Chiang Mai Airport is 2.5 miles from the resort.

Rooms at Village Yaang Come feature dark wood furnishings with Thai touches. All rooms have plenty of natural light, and come with a DVD player. Bathrobes and slippers are in the elegant bathrooms.

Guests can enjoy a relaxing body massage at Lanna Come Spa, or unwind in the spa tub. The resort provides car rental services and free parking. Sightseeing arrangements can be made at the tour desk.

Yaang Come Restaurant serves a breakfast buffet spread of local and international dishes. Outdoor dining options on the grounds are also available.

Chang Khlan is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, night markets and street markets.
