Description

All guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Rooms are equipped with a private bathroom with a shower. Guest rooms at ISTY Hotel feature air conditioning and a desk.

A continental breakfast is served each morning at the property.

The accommodation has a terrace.

Guests can also take advantage of the business center and a fax machine and photocopier. Languages spoken at the reception include English and Thai.

Kad Suan Kaew Shopping Center is 0.6 miles from ISTY Hotel, while Wat Chiang Man is 0.6 miles away. The nearest airport is Chiang Mai Airport, 2.5 miles from the property.

Chang Phueak is a great choice for travelers interested in nature, friendly locals and relaxation.
