Description

The air-conditioned rooms include DVD players, a personal safe and minibar. The private bathrooms have a hairdryer, bathrobe and slippers.

The hotel features laundry services, a tour desk and massage services at Pool Spa. Computers and internet access are available at the hotel's library.

The courtyard restaurant serves a daily breakfast and specializes in Asian, Chinese, European and American cuisines.

Rachamankha a member of Secret Retreats is a 10-minute drive from Chiang Mai International Airport.
