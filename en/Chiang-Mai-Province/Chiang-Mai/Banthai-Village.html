Description

The stylish rooms at Village Banthai are decorated with a mixture of modern and traditional Thai furnishings. All rooms have a private balcony, a DVD player and sitting area. Some overlook the tropical grounds and swimming pool.

The Pool Bar and Bistro provides exotic cocktails and meals. Room service is also available.

The Banthai’s central location in the heart of old Chiang Mai has stores, restaurants and entertainment spots just moments away. Warorat Market and the Chiang Mai Night Bazaar are less than a 10 minute walk from the hotel, and the Chiang Mai Train station is a short 15-minute drive away.

Chang Khlan is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, night markets and street markets.
