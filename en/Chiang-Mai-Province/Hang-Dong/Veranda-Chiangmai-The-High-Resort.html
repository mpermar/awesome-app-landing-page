Description

Veranda High Resort Chiang Mai - MGallery Collection offers rooms set on the valley slope, enjoying breathtaking views of natural mountain streams, and rice and tea terraces. One side represents North Thailand’s cultural heritage while the other side is furnished in a contemporary style.

In addition to warm, personalized service, Veranda Chiangmai also provides rooms with open-plan bathrooms and spacious, private balconies complete with lounge chairs.
