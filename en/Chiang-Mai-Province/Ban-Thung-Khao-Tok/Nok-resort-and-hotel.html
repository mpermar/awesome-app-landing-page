Description

At the resort, every room has a balcony. The rooms include a private bathroom with a shower, while some feature pool views. All guest rooms in Nok resort & hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV and free toiletries.

A continental breakfast is served each morning at the property. There's an in-house restaurant, which specializes in Asian cuisine.

Chiang Mai is 11 miles from the accommodation, while Mae Rim is 10 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Chiang Mai Airport, 12 miles from the property.
