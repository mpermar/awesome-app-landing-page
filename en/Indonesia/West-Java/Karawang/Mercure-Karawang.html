Description

Mercure Karawang offers accommodation in Karawang. The hotel has a year-round outdoor pool and spa centre, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Each room at this hotel is air conditioned and features a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Certain units feature a seating area for your convenience. You will find a kettle in the room.

Rooms are equipped with a private bathroom fitted with a shower. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hair dryer.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. Cibubur is 45 km from Mercure Karawang, while Purwakarta is 30 km away.
