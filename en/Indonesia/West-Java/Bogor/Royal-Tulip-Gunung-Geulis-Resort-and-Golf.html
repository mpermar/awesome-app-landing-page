Description

Offering an outdoor pool, barbecue and sun terrace, Royal Tulip Gunung Geulis Resort and Golf is located in Bogor in the West Java Region, 48 km from Jakarta.

The hotel has a spa centre and fitness centre. The on-site restaurant offers buffet and a la carte menu.

Every room comes with a flat-screen TV. You will find a kettle in the room. The rooms include a private bathroom.

For your comfort, you will find slippers and free toiletries. Royal Tulip Gunung Geulis Resort and Golf features free WiFi.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property that can help with airport transfer arrangement.

The area is popular for golfing and cycling. Puncak is 16 km away, while Cisarua is 10 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Halim Perdanakusuma Airport, 40 km from Royal Tulip Gunung Geulis Resort and Golf.
