Description

Situated in Puncak and about a 5-minute drive from Taman Safari Indoneisa Zoo and Theme Park, Pesona Alam Resort & Spa offers a great mountain getaway featuring an outdoor swimming pool, on-site restaurant, and free WiFi access throughout the property.

It takes a 10-mintue drive from the property to Cisarua Traditional Market, and about a 20-minute drive to the iconic Puncak Pass.

Getting to Soekarno-Hatta International Airport will take around 3 hours by car via the toll road.

Each room at Pesona Alam Resort & Spa is air-conditioned and comes with seating area, balcony, flat-screen cable TV, personal safe, a desk, tea/coffee making facilities and a minibar.

There is also an en suite bathroom fitted with hot and cold shower facilities complete with slippers and fresh towels.

Guests staying at this property will also find a 24-hour front desk where the professional staff can help guests to arrange laundry service and access to meeting/banquet facility at additional charges.

Other facilities such as, kid's club, bicycles, table tennis, luggage storage, and daily housekeeping are available for free.

As for dining option, the on-site The Banyan All Dining Cafe serves a wide range of hearty Indonesian and International dishes.

Alternativele guests can explore the surrounding area where various restaurant can be found just a 5-minute drive away.
