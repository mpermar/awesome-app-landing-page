Description

Guest rooms in the resort are equipped with a electric tea pot. Featuring a private bathroom, rooms at Griya Inkoppabri also provide guests with free WiFi. All guest rooms include a desk.

An Asian breakfast is available daily at the property. At the accommodation you'll find a restaurant serving Indonesian cuisine. A halal option can also be requested.

Fishing is among the activities that guests can enjoy near Griya Inkoppabri.

Guests can enjoy playing pool or sing karaoke, or take advantage of the business center. Staff at the 24-hour front desk can lend a hand with information about the area.

Little Venice Kota Bunga is 8 miles from the resort, while Ciherang Waterfall is 3.1 miles away. The nearest airport is Halim Perdanakusuma Airport, 28 miles from Griya Inkoppabri.
