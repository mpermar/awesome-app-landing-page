Description

HARRIS Hotel & Conventions Ciumbuleuit - Bandung is situated right next to Parahyangan University.

The hotel offers free WiFi access and complimentary parking privilege. The hotel offers comfortable rooms decorated with HARRIS Hotel's signature orange colours.

The air-conditioned rooms are fitted with a flat-screen cable TV, safety deposit box and a seating area.

A minibar and an electric kettle are provided. The en suite bathroom comes with a shower, bathrobes and a hairdryer.

Guests enjoy mountain view. Operating a 24-hour front desk, the friendly staff at HARRIS Hotel & Conventions Ciumbuleuit - Bandung can assist guests with luggage storage and laundry services at additional charges.

Airport transfer can be arranged at an extra charge. There is also a business centre and meeting rooms for guests' convenience.

Relaxing massage can be enjoyed at the spa and wellness centre and guests can also enjoy working out at the gym.

Harris Café serves a selection of Indonesian and international dishes. Alternatively, guests may find plenty of eateries around the well-known Cihampelas and Dago area, located a 15-minute drive away.

The hotel is a 15-minute drive from Bandung Train Station and a 30-minute drive from Husein Sastranegara International Airport.
