Description

Strategically located in the heart of Bandung, Crowne Plaza Bandung offers 4 dining options and an outdoor pool.

Free WiFi access is available throughout the property. Modern and elegantly decorated, each room features air conditioning, iPod docking station and a flat-screen cable TV.

All rooms come with a sofa, a personal safe and a minibar. En suite bathroom is equipped with a shower, a bathtub and a hairdryer.

Guests enjoy city and mountain views. Mosaic All Day Dining Restaurant serves a fine selection of Indonesian and international cuisines, while coffee and desserts can be sampled at Connexions Lobby's Lounge.

More dining options can be found a 5-minute walk away, at Braga Citywalk. The hotel has a fitness centre, a business centre and meeting facilities.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can assist guests with valet parking, luggage storage and laundry.

Other facilities include a kids' club and a spa where guests can enjoy relaxing treatmments.

Crowne Plaza Bandung is a 5-minute walk from Braga area and a 10-minute walk from Bandung Cathedral.

Bandung Train Station is reachable by a 15-minute drive, while Husein Sastranegara International Airport is a 25-minute drive away.
