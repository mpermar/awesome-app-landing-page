Description

Located on the famous shopping street in Bandung, Grand Tjokro Hotel offers modern accommodation with free WiFi access in the entire area of the property, rooftop swimming pool and an on-site restaurant and bar.

It takes a 10-minute drive from the hotel to Cihampelas Walk Shopping Mall, and around a 15-minute drive to the famous fashionable Rumah Mode factory outlet.

Bandung Indah Plaza Mall is reachable within a 20-minute drive, whilst getting Husein Sastranegara Bandung International Airport will take around 30 minutes by car.

Accessible via lifts, rooms at Grand Tjokro Hotel boast a modern and clean design that are air-conditioned and fitted with flat-screen cable TV, a seating area, a wardrobe, and a minibar.

Each room also comes with an en suite bathroom featuring hot and cold shower facility with hairdryer and slippers.

A 24-hour front desk with concierge service, and luggage storage are offered at this hotel.

Other facilities like valet parking, airport transfers, shuttle service, laundry, dry cleaning, meeting/banquet facility, and business centre are available at extra charges.

The on-site Tjokro Restaurant offers a dining option which serves Indonesian, Chinese, and Western food.

Guests can also explore the surrounding area where Glosis Restaurant can be found just a 15-minute drive away.
