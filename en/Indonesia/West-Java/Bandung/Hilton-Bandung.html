Description

Modern and stylish, the spacious air-conditioned rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV and a minibar. En suite see-through marble bathrooms feature double sinks, as well as a rain shower.

Hilton Bandung is approximately 2.5 miles from Husein Sastranegara Airport. It is just 2,150 feet from Bandung Train Station, and shopping options at Pasar Baru Bandung and Braga City Walk.

Beautiful views of Tangkuban Perahu Mountain are presented from the rooftop pool, which is lined with sun loungers. The hotel also provides a gym, Connectivity Station and concierge services.

Freshly made pizzas and Western specialties are served at Fresco Restaurant, while Purnawarman Restaurant offers Asian and Indonesian cuisine. Drinks and snacks are served at Magma Lounge.
