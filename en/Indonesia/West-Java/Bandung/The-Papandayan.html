Description

The Papandayan offers 5-star luxury in Bandung’s city centre with an outdoor pool, fitness facilities and a day spa.

It features personalised attention with 24-hour butler service, free WiFi and 4 dining options.

Featuring elegant interiors in shades of cream and silver, the spacious rooms offer a flat-screen cable TV and ample desk space.

They are provided with a tea/coffee maker and minibar. The Papandayan is a 15-minute drive from Husein Sastranegara International Airport and a 25-minute drive from the popular Cihampelas Shopping Street.

It is a 25-minute drive from Bandung’s tea plantations. For leisure, a relaxing beauty treatment awaits at the salon.

Other conveniences include a business centre, a 24-hour front desk and daily newspapers.

Hearty international meals are served at Cantigi Fine Dining while Pago Restaurant offers Asian and Japanese specialities.
