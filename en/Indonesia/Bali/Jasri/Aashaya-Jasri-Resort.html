Description

Situated in Eastern Bali along Jalan Jasri Beach, Aashaya Jasri Resort features a garden which ends at Jasri Beach.

Rustic bungalow and villas with a private bathroom await guests. Fishermen launch their boats at Jasri Beach, which is where guests can swim or dive in the Indian Ocean.

A 15-minute drive away is Pasir Putih Beach while Amed Diving Spot is a 30-minute drive from Aashaya Jasri Resort.

It takes approximately 2 hours to drive to Ngurah Rai International Airport. Surrounded by the garden, each bungalow comes with either air-conditioning or a fan and features a terrace.

A private bathroom includes a shower and toilet. Recreation facilities include an outdoor pool and table tennis.

Airport shuttle and laundry services are available at an extra charge. Guests have access to free Wi-Fi in the property’s common area.
