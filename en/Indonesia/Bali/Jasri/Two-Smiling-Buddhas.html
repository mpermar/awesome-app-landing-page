Description

At the resort, every room is equipped with a patio with a mountain view. Featuring a private bathroom with a shower and a hair dryer, certain units at Two Smiling Buddhas also offer a pool view. All rooms have a desk.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at the property. Guests can dine in the on-site restaurant, which serves Asian cuisine and also offers gluten-free and vegetarian options.

Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.

Ngurah Rai International Airport is 37 miles away.
