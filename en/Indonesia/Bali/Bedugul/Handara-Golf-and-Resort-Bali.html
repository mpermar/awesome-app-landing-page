Description

Handara Golf & Resort Bali features a par-72 golf course set against mountain scenery and Lake Buyan.

Situated 1,142 metres above sea level, it offers free WiFi access in all areas. The resort is a 2-hour drive from the resort area of Nusa Dua and a 90-minute drive from Kuta Beach.

Ngurah Rai International Airport is a 2-hour drive away. Airport transfers and shuttle services can be arranged for an extra charge.

The golf course can be viewed from rooms at Handara Golf & Resort Bali. With average temperatures ranging from 14 to 20 degrees Celsius, each room has an adjustable heater.

Room amenities include a cable TV, seating area and minibar. Handara Golf & Resort Bali offers souvenir golf items at the Proshop.

It also provides golf lessons and equipment rental. Guests can have a relaxing massage or enjoy an evening of karaoke with friends.

Meeting rooms and tennis court are also available. Kids can play at the outdoor playground or the tree house.

Meals at the restaurant are accompanied by panoramic views of the Buyan Lake. Soyokaze Restaurant specialises in fusion Japanese cuisine, while Breeze Terrace Lounge served international and Indonesian dishes.

Handara Golf & Resort Bali is a 10-minute drive from Bedugul Botanical Garden as well as Pura Ulun Danu Bratan and a 20-minute drive from Gitgit Waterfall.
