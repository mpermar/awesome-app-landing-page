Description

The accommodations come with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with shower and free toiletries, while the kitchen features an oven. A microwave and electric tea pot are also offered.

Breakfast is available daily, and includes continental and à la carte options. There's an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of Asian, American and Indonesian dishes and also offers dairy-free and gluten-free options.

The resort village has a terrace.

Cycling is possible within the area and Umadhatu Resorts offers a private beach area.

Secret Garden Village is 13 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 19 miles from Umadhatu Resorts.
