Description

WakaGangga offers a luxurious seaside retreat with a bar, restaurant and a spa on site.

Guests can enjoy a refreshing dip in the outdoor pool and access free Wi-Fi in all areas.

A private terrace is attached to each spacious villa. Fitted with wooden flooring, all villas are tastefully appointed with an electric kettle, iPod dock and a flat-screen TV with DVD player.

Select units feature a private pool, while all en suite bathrooms provide a bathtub, shower and a set of free toiletries.

The property is a 30-minute drive from Tanah Lot Temple and a 45-minute drive from Alas Kedaton Monkey Forest.

Ngurah Rai International Airport is 90 minutes’ drive away. Staff are available to assist with airport transfers, laundry requests and area shuttles.

Day trips can also be arranged on site. Guests can enjoy a variety of international favourites in the restaurant.
