Description

Hotel Puri Taman Sari is a 1-hour 30-minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport. Attractions like Taman Ayun Temple and Mengwi Royal Palace are less than 15 minutes by car away.

The spacious guest rooms at Sari Puri Taman come with a cable/satellite TV and air-conditioning. A tea/coffee maker and sitting area are included.

Guests can enjoy traditional dance performances or walk to a nearby village to help the villagers plant rice. Other activities include cooking lessons and river rafting.

Delicious local and Western dishes are served at Puri Taman Sari’s restaurant. Outdoor dining by the grounds is available.
