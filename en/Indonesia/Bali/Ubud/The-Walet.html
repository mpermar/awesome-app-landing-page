Description

This villa with mountain views has 2 bedrooms and a bathroom with a hot tub. The villa also includes a fully equipped kitchen with an oven, a microwave and a toaster, and a bathroom with a bath.

At the villa guests are welcome to use an indoor swimming pool. Both a bicycle rental service and a car rental service are available at The Walet.

Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Ubud Monkey Forest, Ubud Market and Ubud Palace. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 17 miles from the property.
