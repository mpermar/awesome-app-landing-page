Description

Bambu Indah is a 15-minute drive from central Ubud and a one hour drive from Ngurah Rai Airport. Mount Batukaru, which is popular for hiking and cycling, is a 1.5-hour drive away.

Each bungalow artistically blends bamboo interiors with antique Javanese teak wood fitting. They include both air conditioning and a fan. Guests can enjoy views of the gardens, pool or rice fields from the outdoor terrace. The private bathrooms include free toiletries and a rain shower.

The hotel provides yoga lessons upon request. Car rental and sightseeing arrangements can be made at the tour desk. An airport pick up service can be requested.

Guests enjoy free afternoon tea daily. Organic Balinese and traditional Indonesian home-cooking are served daily in Bambu Indah's Dapoer Restaurant. Room service is also available.
