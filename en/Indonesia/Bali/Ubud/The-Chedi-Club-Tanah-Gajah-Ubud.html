Description

The Chedi Club provides a relaxing getaway surrounded by rice fields. Located 3 km from central Ubud, it offers a 28-metre outdoor pool and free scheduled yoga classes.

Morning rice-paddy trekking, accompanied by personal butlers, are also available.

Spacious rooms comes with a private terrace and semi-outdoor bathroom. Amenities include a 32-inch flat-screen TV and Bose home theatre system.

Some rooms feature a private pool with outdoor dining area. The Chedi Club At Tanah Gajah, Ubud is a 1-hour drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport.

Among the activities offered at the property, guests can join cooking classes or experience a unique hot air balloon ride.

Other facilities include an organic farm and a gift shop. The hotel provides car rental, business services, and a 24-hour concierge.

Free WiFi is provided throughout The Chedi Club. Overlooking Gunung Agung Volcano, The Restaurant and Club Lounge serves Indonesian and Western dishes prepared with fresh ingredients from the resort’s gardens.

Cigars and drinks are available at The Bird Lounge, adjacent to Tanah Gajah Aviary.
