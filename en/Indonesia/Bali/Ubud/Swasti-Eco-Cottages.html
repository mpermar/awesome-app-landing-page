Description

Set in a lush tropical garden with an outdoor pool, Swasti Eco Cottages is an accommodation with a spa centre and a sauna in Ubud.

Boasting a rustic atmosphere for optimum tranquility, this property offers spacious bedrooms and relaxing common areas close to nature.

Free WiFi is provided throughout the property. Designed with the Balinese way of living in mind, rooms at this property are fitted with mosquito nets for your comfort.

Guests enjoy private bathrooms where fresh towels are provided. There is an on-site restaurant at the property.

You can approach the friendly staff at the front desk for health and wellness activities.

Ubud Market is 2. 3 km from Swasti Eco Cottages, while Ubud Palace is 2. 3 km away.

Ubud Monkey Forest is 1. 1 km from the property. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 26 km from Swasti Eco Cottages.
