Description

At the hotel, the rooms come with a desk and a flat-screen TV. Featuring a private bathroom, rooms at Swahita Ubud also provide guests with free WiFi. At the accommodation every room is equipped with a seating area.

The daily breakfast offers continental, à la carte or American options. There's an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of Indonesian and local dishes and also offers vegetarian and gluten-free options.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental is available at Swahita Ubud.

Ubud Monkey Forest is 1.2 miles from the hotel, while Elephant Cave is 1.8 miles away. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 18 miles from the property.
