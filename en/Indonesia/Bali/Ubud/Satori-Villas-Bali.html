Description

Offering a year-round outdoor pool, Satori Villas is a collection of boutique villas with private pools located in Ubud.

Surrounded by lush rice fields, this property is set in a quiet area, 600 metres from Ubud Market and Ubud Palace.

Free WiFi is available in all areas. All units include a seating area with a satellite flat-screen TV and iPod docking station.

Some units have a terrace and/or balcony with garden or pool views. There is also a dining area and a well-equipped kitchen.

Satori Villas also includes a hot tub. Towels and bed linen are provided. The property also offers grocery delivery and packed lunches.

There is an in-house chef that can help plan a menu for in-villa dining. There is also full-time staff dedicated to make your stay pleasant.

Bike hire and car hire are available at the property and the area is popular for cycling.

Ubud Monkey Forest is 1. 8 km from Satori Villas, while Elephant Cave is 3. 4 km from the property.

The property offers a free scooter shuttle service. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 26 km from Satori Villas.
