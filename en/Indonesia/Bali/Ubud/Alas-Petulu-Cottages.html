Description

Located a 10-minute walk from Ubud City Centre, Alas Petulu Cottages features select villas with kitchens and private pools.

The hotel provides free Wi-Fi access in public areas. Alas Petulu is a 15-minute walk from Monkey Forest.

It is approximately 45 km from Ngurah Rai International Airport. Alas Petulu’s accommodation offers a safety deposit box and a private bathroom with toiletries.

Some villas offer duplex-style living and separate seating areas, which is ideal for gatherings.

The hotel provides laundry and car rental services. A tour desk makes sightseeing arrangements for guests.
