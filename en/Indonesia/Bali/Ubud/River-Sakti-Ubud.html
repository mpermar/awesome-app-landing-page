Description

All rooms at the resort are equipped with a seating area and a flat-screen TV. Rooms include air conditioning, and certain rooms at River Sakti Ubud have a balcony. At the accommodation, each room has a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hair dryer.

An American breakfast is served daily at the property. Guests can dine in the in-house restaurant, which serves sushi cuisine and also offers gluten-free and halal options.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental is available at River Sakti Ubud.

Staff at reception can assist around the clock with guidance on the area.

Ubud Market is 1.7 miles from the resort, while Ubud Palace is 1.7 miles from the property. Ngurah Rai International Airport is 19 miles away.
