Description

All units come with a terrace with garden views, a kitchen with a toaster, and a private bathroom. Some units have a seating area and/or a patio.

A continental breakfast is served each morning at the property.

Ubud Palace is 1.1 miles from the resort village, while Ubud Monkey Forest is 1.1 miles away. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 17 miles from the property.
