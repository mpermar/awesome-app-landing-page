Description

Ketut's Place Villas Ubud features a restaurant, outdoor swimming pool, a bar and shared lounge in Ubud.

2. 3 km from Ubud Market and 2. 3 km from Ubud Palace, the property features a garden and a terrace.

The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and free WiFi throughout the property.

The units come with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kettle, a hot tub, a hairdryer and a desk.

Featuring a private bathroom with a bidet and free toiletries, rooms at the hotel also have a garden view.

All rooms at Ketut's Place Villas Ubud are fitted with a seating area. Continental and la carte breakfast options are available every morning at the accommodation.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike hire and car hire are available at Ketut's Place Villas Ubud.

Saraswati Temple is 2. 6 km from the hotel, while The Blanco Renaissance Museum is 3.

4 km away. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 39 km from Ketut's Place Villas Ubud.
