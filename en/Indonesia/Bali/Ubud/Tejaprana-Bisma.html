Description

Set in Ubud, 1. 6 km from Ubud Market, Tejaprana Bisma offers accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a bar and a garden.

Featuring room service, this property also provides guests with a sun terrace. The resort features pool views, an outdoor pool, a 24-hour front desk, and free WiFi is available.

The units come with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a fridge, a kettle, a shower, a hairdryer and a desk.

At the resort every room has a wardrobe and a private bathroom. Tejaprana Bisma offers a continental or à la carte breakfast.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike hire and car hire are available at the accommodation.

Popular points of interest near Tejaprana Bisma include Ubud Palace, Saraswati Temple and Ary's Warung.

The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International, 38 km from the resort, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
