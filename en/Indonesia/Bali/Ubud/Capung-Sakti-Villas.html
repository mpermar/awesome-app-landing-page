Description

Located approximately 2. 5 km from Ubud Palace, Capung Sakti Villas boast 180 panoramic views of rivers and rice paddy fields.

It features an outdoor infinity pool and beautiful Balinese dcor. On-site yoga and massage treatments are available upon request.

Free high-speed WiFi can be accessed from the entire property. Cooled by ceiling fan and air conditioning, the rooms at Capung Sakti Villas are decorated in Balinese style.

Guests can enjoy the views of the pool and the garden from the room. Each room is fitted with a flat-screen cable TV, iPod docking station, a safety deposit box and minibar.

Mosquito nets and tea/coffee making facilities are also available in the air-conditioned rooms.

The en suite bathroom comes with shower facilities, hairdryer and free toiletries.

Capung Sakti Villas is approximately 2,6 km from Ubud Market and 4 km from the famous Monkey Forest.

The property's staff can also arrange for tours at additional charge. Guests can request airport transfers and car rentals with additional charges.

Laundry services and other activities such as diving, canoeing, hiking and cycling can also be arranged at extra cost.

A fine selection of Asian dishes can be sampled at the Fair Warung Bal Restaurant which is 2.

5 km from the villa. In case of emergency, the Foundation Medical Centre is located 3 km from Capung Sakti.

The nearest airport is Denpasar International Airport, 30 km away from the property.
