Description

Fully air-conditioned, the spacious guest rooms at Beji Ubud come with a minibar, satellite TV and a tea/coffee maker. Private bathrooms feature hot showers and bathtubs.

Guests can join peaceful yoga and meditation sessions, or relax with a massage at the resort's spa. The resort can also help arrange cooking, cultural dance and batik painting lessons. A free scheduled shuttle service takes guests to Central Ubud 3 times a day.

Beji Restaurant serves a selection of Indonesian and international cuisines. A daily buffet breakfast is also available every morning.

Beji Ubud Resort is approximately 28 miles from Ngurah Rai International Airport. Airport transportation can be arranged at additional charges.
