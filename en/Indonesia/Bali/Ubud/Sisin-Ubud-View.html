Description

A terrace with pool views is offered in all units.

Guests at the resort village can enjoy a continental breakfast.

Sisin Ubud View has an outdoor pool.

If you would like to discover the area, cycling is possible nearby and a bicycle rental service can be arranged.

Ubud Monkey Forest is 2.7 miles from the accommodation, while Ubud Market is 3.4 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 16 miles from Sisin Ubud View.
