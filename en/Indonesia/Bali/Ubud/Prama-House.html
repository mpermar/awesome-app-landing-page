Description

Prama House is a villa located in Ubud in the Penestanan district, 1. 8 km from Ubud Market.

You can relax by the outdoor pool in the garden in fair weather. Free WiFi is featured throughout the property.

Free shuttle service is available for guests who want to visit central Ubud. The kitchen comes with a dishwasher, a fridge and a stovetop and there is a private bathroom.

Towels and bed linen are offered at Prama House. Other facilities at Prama House include a year-round outdoor pool.

Bike hire and car hire are available at the property and the area is popular for horse riding and snorkelling.

Ubud Palace is 1. 8 km from Prama House, while Ubud Monkey Forest is 1. 9 km away.

You can engage in various activities, such as diving and cycling. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 28 km from the property.
