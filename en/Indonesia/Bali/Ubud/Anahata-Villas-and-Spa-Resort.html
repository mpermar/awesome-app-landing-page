Description

Offering free shuttle service to and from Ubud Market, Anahata Villas and Spa Resort features spacious villas with private pools and gardens.

A spa and pool with jacuzzi are provided. Anahata Villas is a 10-minute drive from Ubud Town.

Ngurah Rai International Airport is a 60-minute drive away. Anahatas villas offer 3 floors including a living and dining room.

A master suite is located on the highest floor. Villas come well-equipped with CD and DVD players.

A minibar and tea/coffee maker is also provided in the kitchenette. Anahata Villa and Spa Resort offers free Wi-Fi throughout.

Hiking, tracking and cycling are some of the recreational activities provided by the resort.

The resort also provides free daily afternoon tea, free 15-minute welcome massage, free Yoga session on select days and free daily tracking to Anahata River.

Guests can enjoy views of the river valley while having a local, Asian or Western meal at the resorts restaurant.

BBQ facilities are available for an alternative meal option. A wide variety of drinks can be enjoyed at Anahatas bar.
