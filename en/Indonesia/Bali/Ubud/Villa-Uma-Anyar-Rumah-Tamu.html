Description

All guest rooms come with a toaster, fridge, a electric tea pot, a bath, free toiletries and a desk. Free WiFi is accessible to all guests, while selected rooms here will provide you with a patio. At the resort, all rooms include air conditioning and a flat-screen TV.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property.

Guests at Villa Uma Anyar - Rumah Tamu will be able to enjoy activities in and around Ubud, like cycling.

Ubud Monkey Forest is 3.6 miles from the accommodation, while Ubud Market is 4.4 miles away. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 14 miles from Villa Uma Anyar - Rumah Tamu.
