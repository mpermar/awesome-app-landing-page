Description

Housing a bar and a restaurant, The Lokha Ubud Resort, Villas & SPA offers a tranquil riverside retreat a 10-minute drive from Elephant Safari Park.

The resort features an outdoor pool, spa and a gym on site. Free Wi-Fi is accessible in public areas.

The property also offers free shuttle service to Central Ubud area, free daily afternoon tea, free use of bicycles and activities like trekking, village walk, fruit carving, and many more.

The Lokha Ubud Resort, Villas & SPA is a 15-minute drive from central Ubud and a 60-minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport.

Offering views of Ubud's tropical terrain, each spacious unit tastefully blends contemporary designs with traditional Balinese touches.

They are fitted with elegant teak furnishings and local artistry. Amenities include a safe, refrigerated minibar and a flat-screen cable TV with DVD player, while the a lavish bathtub, overhead rain shower and a complete set of toiletries are included in each en suite bathroom.

Leisurely afternoons can be spent enjoying a quiet read in the library or lounging by the pool, while kids can play in the kids' club.

Guests can check emails in the business centre and make use of the meeting/banquet facilities.

Airport transfers and vehicle rentals can be arranged for guests' travel needs. Offering room service, Bukit Cinta Restaurant serves a spread of international delights.
