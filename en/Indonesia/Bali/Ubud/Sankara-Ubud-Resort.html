Description

Sankara Ubud Resort is a 15-minute drive from central Ubud, 20-minute drive from Monkey Forest and a 90-minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport.

Blending modern decor with traditional touches, each air-conditioned unit has a seating area, flat-screen satellite TV and a personal safe. An electric kettle and a mini-bar are also among the amenities provided. Private bathrooms offer a bathtub, double sink and free bath amenities.

Airport transfers and car rental can be arranged on site. Guests may also request for laundry service.

Offering room service, The Kumbuh Restaurant serves a spread of Indonesian and Western delights.
