Description

Luxurious air conditioned rooms boast a scenic terrace, private entrance and a seating area. Fitted with flat-screen satellite TV, CD/DVD player and a laptop safe. It also includes a mosquito net, wardrobe and sofa. The room is fitted with a mini-bar and electric kettle. The spacious marble bathroom comes with a shower, bathtub and hairdryer. Free WiFi access is available throughout the property.

Guests can enjoy dining on fusion dishes at KE-PI-TU Restaurant which features beautiful Balinese royal setting. Alcoholic beverages can be enjoyed at on-site bar. The resort also offers room service from 07:00 to 22:00.

Spa treatments and relaxing massage are available at Serayu Spa. There is also an on-site beauty salon.

Operating a 24-hour front desk, the friendly staff at The Kayon Resort can assist guests with laundry, dry cleaning and ironing services at additional charges. Airport shuttle, car hire and bicycle rental are available at an extra charge. The hotel’s tour desk and ticket service helps guests with trip planning. Other facilities offered on-site include concierge service. Guests can relax in the garden or read a book in the library. Business center is also available.

The Kayon Resort is a 10-minute drive to Ubud Palace, a 15-minute drive to Ubud Monkey Forest and a 90-minute drive to Ngurah Rai International Airport.

Experiences

10/21/2016

Absolute Paradise!

"From the moment we arrived staff greeted us with flowers and completely took over, all our bags were just whisked away! Check in was a breeze, lovely welcome drink in the restaurant with staff introducing themselves. We were in total luxury and paradise! The Resort is set on the side of a hill so many stairs up and down but it was great! I seemed to get lost only a couple of times! The river runs past the resort at the bottom, so when having a massage or anything from the Spa, you can hear the peacefulness of the running water. Grounds were immaculate. Service was impeccable. Room was big and lovely and romantic! Kayon Resort is a definite must if you want a quiet, relaxing get away - i would suggest if you have children - look elsewhere. This is a romantic, quiet, couples retreat. I cannot wait to come back!"
