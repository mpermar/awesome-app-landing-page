Description

Inspired by Balinese royal palace architecture, Dwaraka The Royal Villas offers accommodation in Ubud, 1.

7 km from Ubud Market. The resort features an infinity pool set in the lush greenery.

Free WiFi can be accessed throughout the premises. Free parking is available and free scheduled shuttle to central Ubud is provided.

Elegant rooms at Dwaraka The Royal Villas are adorned with traditional carvings and furnished with a bed and mosquito nets.

Each room has air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and a seating area. Certain units come with a terrace or balcony overlooking the pool or the mountain.

For extra comfort, the en suite bathroom is stocked with a hairdryer, robes, slippers and free toiletries.

Guests can enjoy dining at the on-site restaurant, coffee shop or in the privacy of their rooms.

Pampering massages and beauty treatments can be had at the spa centre. The resort provides free bicyles to explore the surrounding areas.

The 24-hour front desk can assist with airport transfer and car hire with additional charges.

Ubud Palace is 1. 7 km from Dwaraka The Royal Villas, while Ubud Monkey Forest is 2.

9 km from the property. The nearest airport is Bali Denpasar International Airport, 30 km from Dwaraka The Royal Villas.
