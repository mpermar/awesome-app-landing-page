Description

Located in the peaceful area of Ubud, Lodtunduh Sari is set amidst the green stretches of rice fields.

Featuring an outdoor pool and air-conditioned accommodation, the budget-friendly villa offers shuttle services to the city centre of Ubud.

Free Wi-Fi is available. Lodtunduh Sari is a 5-minute drive from Ubud Art Market and a 25-minute walk from Ubud Monkey Forest.

Denpasar City Centre is a 45-minute drive away, while it takes 1 hour to drive to Ngurah Rai International Airport from the property.

Airport transfers are available at an extra charge. All rooms provide a 4-poster bed with a mosquito net.

Select units come with shared kitchen and bathroom, while the villas offer a private bathroom and a private terrace overlooking the mountain.

Shower facilities are provided in the attached open-air bathroom. Laundry and car rental services are available at an additional charge.
