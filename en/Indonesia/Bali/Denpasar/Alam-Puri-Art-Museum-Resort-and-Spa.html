Description

Alam Puri Art Museum Resort & Spa is a 20-minute drive from the center of Ubud and Ngurah Rai International Airport is a 30-minute drive away.

Alam Puri Art Museum Resort & Spa’s grand villas feature wide open spaces and marble floors.Some include a private plunge pool and a kitchen. The balconies offer views of the Ayung River or the surrounding rice paddies. All villas have a DVD player and cable TV channels to provide for a relaxing night in. A tea/coffee maker and minibar are also included.

Free bike rental is available for those who want to explore the area, and the spa provides a refreshing and relaxing recreational option. Guests can also have a picnic or BBQ at the resort or visit the library for a quiet read. Puma Museum features a range of Indonesian artwork.

The poolside Terrace Restaurant serves Western and local cuisine all day. A free European or traditional Indonesian breakfast is served at the restaurant.
