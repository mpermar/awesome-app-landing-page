Description

Located in the central Denpasar area, The Banyumas Villa offers modern rooms situated near the business and entertainment area.

The hotel features a large outdoor pool and provides free internet access and on-site parking.

Free drop off service within the radius of 5 km from the villa is available during working hours and with prior reservations.

A 25-minute drive from Ngurah Rai Airport, The Banyumas Villa is 30 minutes' drive from Seminyak and Kuta.

It is a 10-minute drive from Sanur Beach and is a 5-minute drive from Matahari Mall and Niti Mandala Renon Museum.

Simply furnished, The Banyumas Villa's air-conditioned units has a kitchen, dining and living area.

They are also equipped with a satellite TV and private bathroom with shower. Day trips and tours can be arranged at the hotel's tour desk.

Guests can also relax with a massage and spa services, which is available upon request.
