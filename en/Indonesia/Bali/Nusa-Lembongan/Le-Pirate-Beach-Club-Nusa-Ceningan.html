Description

Fitted with cheerful interiors, the brightly painted rooms are fan-cooled and fitted with a desk. They each have a private terrace with a sitting area and a hammock. The private bathrooms have shower facilities.

A 5-minute drive from Secret Point surf break, Le Pirate is a 10-minute drive from Nusa Lembongan and is approximately 12 miles from East Bali Coast.

Guests can rent a motorbike to explore the island, or go on a snorkeling trips arranged by staff. Water sports facilities are available on site.

The on-site restaurant serves Indonesian dishes, local specialties and barbecued delights.
