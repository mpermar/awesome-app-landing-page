Description

Set in traditional thatch-roofed buildings, the rooms are equipped with air conditioning, a safety deposit box and mosquito nets. Each room has a private bathroom with a shower.

A la carte breakfast is served daily at Water Blow Huts.

Popular activities in the area include fishing, snorkeling and diving. Available 24 hours, the front desk staff can arrange shuttle and airport transfer with additional fees.

Sandy Bay Beach Club is 1,950 feet from Water Blow Huts, while Mushroom Bay is 0.6 mi from the property. Ngurah Rai International Airport is 19 mi away.
