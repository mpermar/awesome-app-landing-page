Description

Rooms at d'Nusa Beach Club are fitted with a flat screen TV, air conditioning and private bathroom. Some rooms have a seating area with a sofa.

Babysitting services and water sport activities can be arranged at the property. Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including snorkeling and canoeing.

Mangrove Point is 1 mi from d'Nusa Beach Club , while Mushroom Bay is 1.7 mi from the property. The nearest airport is Bali Denpasar International Airport, 20 mi from the property.
