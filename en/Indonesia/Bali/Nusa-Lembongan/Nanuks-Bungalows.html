Description

Only 200 metres from scenic Mushroom and Secret bays in Nusa Lembongan, Nanuks Bungalows offers thatched-roof accommodation with private balconies.

It provides an outdoor pool and activities like fishing, canoeing and snorkelling.

Inspired by Balinese traditional barn, the hut-like rooms feature wood ceiling and flooring.

They are fully air-conditioned and come with hot shower facilities. They all have a seating area on their balconies.

Guests can enjoy massages or make use of the bicycle rental facilities and explore the area.

Other activities include diving. The restaurant at Nanuks Bungalows serves Balinese specialities and select international favourites.

Simple a la carte breakfast is served daily. Devil's Tear is 1. 7 km from the property, while Yellow Bridge is 2.

7 km away. Reaching the hotel takes around 1 hour by fast boat from Sanur Beach, which is a 30-minute drive from Ngurah Rai Airport.
