Description

Fitted with a terrace, the units offer air conditioning and feature a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with free toiletries. Some units have a seating area and/or a balcony.

A continental or à la carte breakfast is served at the property. The restaurant at the resort village serves Asian cuisine.

A bicycle rental service is available at Damara Lembongan.

Sandy Bay Beach Club is 0.4 miles from the accommodation, while Devil's Tear is 0.6 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 19 miles from Damara Lembongan.
