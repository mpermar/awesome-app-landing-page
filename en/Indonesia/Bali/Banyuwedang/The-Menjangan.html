Description

Elegantly decorated, the rooms at Menjangan are equipped with a flat-screen TV, DVD player, minibar and tea/coffee-making facilities. A personal safe and toiletries are also included.

Bird watching and forest trekking trips can be arranged at The Menjangan’s tour desk. For added convenience, the hotel also provides laundry services and a 24-hour front desk.

Bali Tower Restaurant serves authentic Balinese and Mediterranean cuisines. Vegetarian dishes are also available. The beachside Pantai Restaurant specializes in fresh seafood barbecue. Drinks can be enjoyed at the hotel’s lounge.

The Menjangan is a 3.5-hour drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport.
