Description

Located in the heart of Sanur Village, Prime Plaza Suites Sanur - Bali offers fully furnished apartment-style suites that overlook the pool or garden.

The resort features a luxurious spa, a kids club and free WiFi. The hotel also offers free scheduled shuttle service to Sanur area.

Built around lush tropical gardens, suites at Prime Plaza Suites Sanur - Bali come with modern décor and have plenty of natural light.

All suites are fitted with a well-equipped kitchen, a dining area and a separate living area with a flat-screen TV and a DVD player.

At Camp Splash Kids' Club, children can play on the 30-metre water slide. Guests can also use the facilities at the fitness centre or take a dip in the outdoor pool.

Available for all-day dining, Terrace Restaurant & Bar serves local and international dishes.

Live entertainment are available every Saturday night. Prime Plaza Suites Sanur - Bali provides a free shuttle service to Sanur's shopping area, a 5-minute drive away.

Free shuttle is also provided to Sanur Beach, a 10-minute drive away. The accommodation is 15 km from Ngurah Rai International Airport.
