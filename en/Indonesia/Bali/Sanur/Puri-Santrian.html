Description

Nestled along the golden shores of Sanur Beach in Bali, only a 15-minute drive from Denpasar Town, Puri Santrian boasts 2 spectacular outdoor pools, free Wi-Fi and a spa.

The spacious rooms at Puri Santrian are elegantly decorated and come with a satellite TV, a tea/coffee maker and a minibar.

Large balconies feature lovely views of the tropical gardens and allow the warm Balinese breeze in.

Luxurious Balinese massages and beauty treatments await at the hotel’s spa, offered half price to hotel's guests.

Guests can also enjoy yoga classes to complement the treatments. For a quiet afternoon indoors, there is a library on site.

Other recreational activities such as cooking classes, snorkelling and parasailing are available.

The tour desk helps guests arrange for excursions, and provides bicycle rental facilities for guests’ convenience.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet breakfast. The Beach Club serves fresh seafood and international dishes, with weekly traditional performances.

The open-air Mezzanine Restaurant serves Western, Thai and Japanese cuisines. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 11 km from the accommodation.
