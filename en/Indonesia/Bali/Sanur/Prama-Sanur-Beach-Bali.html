Description

Sitting directly on the sandy shores of Sanur Beach, Prama Sanur Beach Bali boasts 2 outdoor pools, tennis courts and 24-hour room service.

Surrounded by 6 hectares of tropical gardens, it also has pampering spa services and a fitness centre.

Free WiFi is provided in all areas of the hotel. The spacious air-conditioned rooms at Prama Sanur Beach Bali all come with parquet flooring and modern conveniences, such as a satellite TV and a personal safe.

A private balcony, a minibar and a private bathroom are included. Prama Sanur Beach Bali is a 15-minute drive from Denpasar and a 25-minute drive from Ngurah Rai Airport.

Free parking is available. Water sports activities are available at the beach. The hotel also has beach volleyball courts and a Kid's Club.

The Tirta Poolside Restaurant serves international favourites and grilled seafood.

Other dining options include Basilico Italian Pavilion & Bar, Lada Restaurant, and Bamboo Beach Bar.
