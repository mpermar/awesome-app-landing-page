Description

Set along the coastline of Sanur, Parigata Resorts and Spa offers boutique style accommodation with an outdoor pool and sun loungers.

Located within a 5-minute walk to Cafe Batu Jimbar, this property also boasts a private beach club located about 10-minute walk away.

Free scheduled shuttle service is provided to go to the beach and around Sanur. Rooms at the resort have teak and marble furnishings as well as a balcony that overlooks a waterfall and swimming pool.

Modern room amenities include air conditioning, minibar and TV. The en suite bathroom comes with a shower or a bath.

An array of relaxing massages and spa treatments can be enjoyed at Lantana Spa. There's also a gift shop on site.

Guests can enjoy buffet breakfast, international fare and Indonesian delicacies at the 24-hour Flamboyant Restaurant by the pool side or have cocktails while live music plays at the Lotus Bar.

Ubud is 23 km from Parigata Resort and Spa, while Kuta is 13. 5 km away. Ngurah Rai International Airport is 16.
