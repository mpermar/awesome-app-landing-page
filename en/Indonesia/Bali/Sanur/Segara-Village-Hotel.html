Description

Modelled after a traditional Balinese village, Segara Village Hotel in Sanur offers villas set within tropical gardens.

This beachfront hotel offers 3 outdoor pools and free Wi-Fi in all areas. Sunrise views can be enjoyed along with buffet breakfast at The Beach Restaurant.

This oceanfront outlet also serves Asian and international dishes in indoor or outdoor settings.

Byrdhouse Bar lets guests sip cocktails while relaxing in a whirlpool overlooking the sea.

Segara Village Hotel is a 30-minute drive from Ubud Town. Ngurah Rai International Airport is a 40-minute drive away.

Rooms at Hotel Segara Village feature Indonesian furnishings. Private balconies provide garden or pool views.

Each room is fitted with a TV, minibar and tea/coffee maker. Guests can join a local market tour and cooking class.

Weekly cultural evenings feature Balinese dances and music. Relaxation options include a massage at the spa or some quiet reading in the library.

With a reservation a day in advance, guests can enjoy free Yoga session every Tuesday and Thursday from 07:00 to 08:00.
