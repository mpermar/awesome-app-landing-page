Description

Enjoying beachfront location, The Lovina features a collection of Balinese-style villas with a private pool and ocean views.

Guests enjoy free use of snorkelling gear, canoe and stand-up paddle board. Free daily Yoga session and weekly Balinese dancing class are also available.

Free WiFi access and free shuttle service to the surrounding area in Lovina are provided.

The well-designed villas are spacious and feature an open concept design. Full kitchen facilities are available in select units, while a flat-screen TV and DVD player are included in all units.

Eco-friendly bottles are provided in each room and guests can refill unlimited drinking water free of charge.

En suite bathrooms have either a bathtub or shower. Guests can arrange area shuttles or day trips at the tour desk.

Pampering massages can be requested at the spa centre. The resort also provides a gym and laundry services.

Dining options at The Lovina include the Heliconia Restaurant and Pizzeria, and Beach Bar by the pool and beach.

Barbecue parties can be arranged upon request. The property is 5 km from Krisna Water Park and 11.
