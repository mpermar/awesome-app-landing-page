Description

Furnished in a Balinese style, rooms at Nugraha Lovina have a private porch or balcony with a sitting area. Each room has satellite TV, a fridge, and tea/coffee making facilities. The private bathrooms provide hot and cold water.

In addition to massage treatments, Araminth Spa offers facials, manicures and pedicures. Activities at Lovina Beach include diving and snorkeling. The staff at the resort’s 24-hour front desk can arrange car rental and laundry services.

Local and Western dishes are served at the restaurant, which features ocean views. Guests may also arrange to have their meals right on the beach. Drinks are available at the bar.

Nugraha Lovina Seaview Resort & Spa is a 2-hour drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport.
