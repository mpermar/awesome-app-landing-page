Description

Located on Lovina Beach in northern Bali, Adirama Beach Hotel offers rooms with views of the ocean, garden or swimming pool.

It features a spa and a beachfront restaurant. Free Wi-Fi is accessible in all areas.

Adirama Beach Hotel features rooms with Balinese furnishings. Each room has a patio or balcony, air conditioning and an en suite bathroom.

Guests can enjoy massages, facial and body treatments at the Adiramas Spa & Health Centre.

The hotel provides traditional dance lessons and can arrange daytrips, such as boat trips to see playful dolphins, snorkeling to Tulamben shipwreck or Menjangan Island and canoeing.

Indonesian, Western, Italian and Mexican cuisines are offered at the hotels seaside restaurant.

Drinks and internet access are available at the poolside bar. Steps away from snorkeling spots, Adirama Beach Hotel is approximately3-hour drive away from Ngurah Rai International airport.
