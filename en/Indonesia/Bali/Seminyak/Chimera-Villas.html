Description

With wooden furnishings, the spacious villas have a flat-screen TV and DVD player in the living area. Guests may relax by the lounge chairs by the private pool, or enjoy an in-room massage.

A open-plan kitchen is available in all villas, fully equipped with an oven stove top, washing machine and dish washing machine. A daily American breakfast is served to the rooms. Chimera can also arrange for private chefs to cook in-villa.

Chimera Villas is a 15-minute walk from La Lucciola Beach. It takes about 20 minutes by car to get to Ngurah Rai International Airport, as well as Kuta Beach and Legian. Free parking is available.

The resort provides bike rentals and laundry services. Basic business facilities like fax and photocopying are also available.
