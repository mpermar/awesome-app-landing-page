Description

Annora Bali Villas is a 4-minute walk from Seminyak Beach, north of Kuta. It offers exclusive accommodation in luxurious villas within a lush garden and features massage facilities.

Guests can join daily morning cycling and a yoga class is available from Monday to Saturday.

Guests can benefit from the resort's location and visit Petitenget Beach and Petitenget Temple, both within minutes from the Annora.

Seminyak Square is a 10-minute drive away, while Ngurah Rai Internatinal Airport is 6 km from the property.

The villas in Annora Bali Villas combine traditional architecture with modern amenities, including free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen satellite TV and DVD player.

They have a private balcony, living room and a separate bedroom decorated with ethnic furnishings.

The bathrooms are equipped with a bathtub and an outdoor rain shower. Guests can enjoy a selection of fresh local specialities and international classic dishes, served at the restaurant of the hotel.

Alternatively, Gado Gado Restaurant is steps away from the property, offering a selection of Indonesian delicacies.
