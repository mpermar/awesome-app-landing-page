Description

Situated a 7-minute walk from Seminyak Beach and right beside Potato Head, Montigo Resorts Seminyak boasts a 24-hour front desk and concierge service.

Free WiFi access is available throughout the resort. Offering ample of natural light, the air-conditioned rooms here are fitted with a personal safe, a sofa and a flat-screen cable TV.

En suite bathroom comes with a shower, a spa bath and hairdryer. At Montigo Resorts Seminyak, guests can order from the rooms service.

Other facilities offered include luggage storage. It takes a 10-minute walk to reach Seminyak Square, while Denpasar Bali International Airport can be reached by a 45-minute drive.
