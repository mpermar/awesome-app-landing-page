Description

Located just 30 metres from Seminyak Beach and the famed Ku De Ta Restaurant and Beach Club, Luna2 Seminyak, Bali presents an outdoor pool, restaurant and a rooftop bar.

It features spacious open plan studios with a private balcony. WiFi access is free in all areas.

Luna2 Seminyak, Bali is conveniently situated a 5-minute drive from the area’s shopping and fine dining options.

Ngurah Rai International Airport is a 25-minute drive away. Fully air-conditioned, each studio boasts funky interiors with colourful décor.

It comes with 2 flat-screen TVs, a kitchenette and a dining area. Guests can lounge in the seating area and on the private terrace or balcony overlooking the pool.

En suite bathroom is fitted with a rain shower, separate bathtub and free bath amenities.

For guests’ recreational enjoyment, Luna2 Seminyak, Bali provides a mini movie theatre on site.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can assist with luggage storage, laundry requests and airport pick-up arrangements.

A spread of Western delicacies are served at Orbit Restaurant, which also offers room service.

Pop! Bar is a retro-style lounge bar with a dance floor, while Space Bar is located on the rooftop offering scenic ocean views.
