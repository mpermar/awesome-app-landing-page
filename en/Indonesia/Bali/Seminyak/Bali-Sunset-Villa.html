Description

Welcoming guests with an outdoor pool and a 24-hour front desk, Bali Sunset Villa is located in the lively Seminyak area, just a 10-minute drive from Double Six Beach.

Guests enjoy twice-a-day free drop off service to Kuta Beach, Legian, Seminyak areas, and Double Six Beach.

Free WiFi access is available throughout. Decorated in neutral and soothing colours, the air-conditioned rooms here are fitted with a flat-screen TV, an electric kettle and a minibar.

There is also a seating area, a desk and a wardrobe. En suite bathroom comes with a shower, hairdryer and slippers.

The friendly staff at Bali Sunset Villa can help with laundry, dry cleaning and ironing services.

Day trips can be arranged at the tour desk, while relaxing massage can be enjoyed in the guests rooms.

Getting around is easy with vehicle rentals and shuttle service. Bali Sunset Café serves a selection of Indonesian, Chinese and western dishes.

Alternatively, guests can order from the restaurant for in-room dining. The holiday park is a 15-minute drive to Seminyak Square Shopping Cente, while Bali Denpasar Airport is a 30-minute drive away.
