Description

The elegant rooms at The Haven Bali Seminyak have balconies and plenty of natural light. Each room has a minibar and tea/coffee maker. Select bathrooms have a bathtub.

Guests can enjoy a range of beauty and body treatments at ATMA Spa, or work out at the fitness center. For convenience, the hotel also provides laundry services and free parking.

Sabeen Restaurant serves a variety of organic dishes, and Italian food is available at Lemon Cello.

The Haven Bali Seminyak is half a mile from Legian Beach and 2 miles from Kuta Square. Ngurah Rai International Airport is a 15-minute drive from the hotel. Free shuttle services to Seminyak Beach are provided.
