Description

Operating a 24-hour front desk, Maison at C Boutique Hotel & Spa Seminyak is a 4-star hotel located is Seminyak featuring French colonial designs combined with Balinese accents.

The hotel is a 8-minute walk from the nearest beach, and a 10-minute drive from Kuta as well as Legian area.

Free WiFi access is available throughout. Guests enjoy free shuttle service to Seminyak Square and Petitenget Beach 3 times a day.

The hotel offers air-conditioned rooms with a flat-screen cable TV, a personal safe and a seating area with sofa.

Certain units boasts a private pool, while others have a semi-outdoor shower. Each includes en suite bathroom fitted with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Maison at C Boutique Hotel & Spa Seminyak houses an outdoor pool and meeting/banqueting facilities.

The staff can help arrange for airport transfer, while the concierge can assist with luggage storage.

There is also a business centre and a garden. Relaxing treatments can be enjoyed at the spa and wellness centre.

A selection of Italian and Indonesian dishes can be sampled at La Table, while guests can enjoy dining on international cuisine at Metis Restaurant, located a 5-minute drive away.

Guests can reach the boutiques and restaurants in Seminyak within just a 12-minute walk.
