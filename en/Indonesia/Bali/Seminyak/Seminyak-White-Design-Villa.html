Description

Offering direct access to a patio, the villa consists of 4 bedrooms and a fully equipped kitchen. A flat-screen TV is available.

Breakfast is available every morning, and includes continental, buffet and à la carte options.

Staff speak English, Indonesian and Chinese at the reception.

The villa has a terrace. Cycling can be enjoyed nearby.

Oberoi Street is a 14-minute walk from Seminyak White Design Villa, while La Lucciola Restaurant is 1.4 miles away. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International, 3.7 miles from the accommodation, and the property offers a free airport shuttle service.
