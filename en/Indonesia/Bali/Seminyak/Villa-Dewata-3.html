Description

This 5-bedroom villa includes 4 bathrooms equipped with a hot tub and free toiletries. The kitchen comes with a dishwasher, a microwave and a toaster, as well as a coffee machine and an electric tea pot.

Breakfast is available daily, and includes continental, à la carte and American options.

Speaking English and Indonesian, staff are willing to help at any time of the day at the reception.

The villa has an outdoor pool. A grill is available for guests at Villa Dewata 3 to use.

Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Petitenget Temple, Potato Head Beach Club and Oberoi Street. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 3.7 miles from Villa Dewata 3.
