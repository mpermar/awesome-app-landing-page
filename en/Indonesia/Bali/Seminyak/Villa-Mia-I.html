Description

The rooms come with a flat-screen TV with cable channels, oven, a coffee machine, a bath and a desk. Each room is equipped with air conditioning, and some rooms have a terrace. All guest rooms feature a closet.

Guests at the guesthouse can enjoy a continental breakfast.

Popular points of interest near Villa Mia I include Oberoi Street, Bintang Supermarket Seminyak and Made's Warung Seminyak. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 3.1 miles from the accommodation.
