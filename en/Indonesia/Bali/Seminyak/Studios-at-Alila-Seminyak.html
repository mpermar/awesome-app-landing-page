Description

Featuring modern living with plenty of natural light and greenery, Studios at Alila Seminyak offers an outdoor pool and city views.

Guests can enjoy a walk in the elevated park or lounge by the outdoor pool. There is an on-site restaurant, a bar, and a spa centre.

This hotel provides free WiFi and free private parking. Every room at Studios at Alila Seminyak has air conditioning and a flat-screen TV.

You can enjoy a seating area in certain rooms. Amenities provided include an electric kettle, bathrobes, slippers, and free toiletries.

This hotel provides free shuttle service. There are hairdresser's and gift shop on site.

Free use of bicycles is available at this hotel and the area is popular for cycling.

The hotel also offers car hire. Petitenget Temple is 200 metres from Studios at Alila Seminyak, while Ku De Ta is 800 metres away.
