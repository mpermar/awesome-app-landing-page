Description

The rooms at Ananda Resort Seminyak are simply furnished with a cable TV, safe, closet, desk and sitting area. The room includes a kitchen with a fridge and dining area, as well as a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries.

Ananda Resort Seminyak is a 10-minute drive from Kuta Beach and it takes 30 minutes to drive to Ngurah Rai International Airport from the property.

Day tours and vehicle rentals can also be arranged through the hotel for a fee. The hotel also provides airport transportation and laundry services when arrangements are made in advance.

Ananda Restaurant serves Indonesian, western and international dishes at the hotel. Other dining options are available just a short walk away, in the center of Seminyak.
