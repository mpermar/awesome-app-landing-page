Description

Suka Sari Cottages is located in the Pemuteran area, a 5-minute drive from Pulaki Temple, and a 30-minute drive from Gilimanuk Harbour.

It offers free on-site parking. Guest rooms come with a patio and have a seating area.

The en suite bathroom comes with hot/cold water showers. Recreational choices include diving or snorkeling trip to Menjangan Island.

Sightseeing and transportation can be arranged upon request. The on-site restaurant serves a selection of traditional Balinese dishes.
