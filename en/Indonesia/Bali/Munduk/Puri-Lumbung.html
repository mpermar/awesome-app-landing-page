Description

Each cottage at Puri Lumbung comes with an attached balcony made of solid wood and presents stunning views of the surrounding. The attached bathroom is equipped with hot shower facilities.

Guests can indulge in a range of traditional Balinese massage at Puri Lumbung Cottages. The hotel also conducts basic Hatha Yoga and meditation classes at Victoria Hall and Yoga Hall.

Featuring the spectacular view of Mount Batukaru, Warung Kopi Bali Restaurant serves a variety of local, Western and Vegetarian dishes.
