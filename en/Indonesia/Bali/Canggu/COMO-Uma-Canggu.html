Description

Set in Canggu, COMO Uma Canggu features offers panoramic beach view. The property is close to several noted attractions.

It's only a few steps from Echo Beach and around 500 metres from Batu Bolong Beach.

Guests can chill out in the outdoor swimming pool. At the resort, all rooms have flat-screen TVs and private bathrooms.

Every room comes with air conditioning and a wardrobe, and selected rooms also offer a terrace.

Breakfast is available daily at the property. At COMO Uma Canggu you will find a restaurant serving International dishes.

Gluten-free and vegetarian options can also be requested. At the accommodation guests are welcome to take advantage of the wellness centre.

Staff at the reception can provide guidance on what to do in the area. Berawa Beach is 1.
