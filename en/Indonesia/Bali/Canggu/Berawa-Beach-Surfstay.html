Description

The rooms in the guesthouse are equipped with a coffee machine. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom, while some accommodations at Berawa Beach Surfstay also feature a seating area. All rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

You can play pool at the accommodation.

La Laguna Bali is 0.8 miles from Berawa Beach Surfstay. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 6 miles from the property.
