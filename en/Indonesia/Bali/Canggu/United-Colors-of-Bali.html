Description

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool with a sun terrace, United Colors of Bali offers villas with different colour themes.

It is surrounded by tropical greenery and provides free Wi-Fi access. Nestled within the rice fields, the eco-resort is located only a 10-minute drive to Brawa Beach and Batubolong.

It is also just a 15-minute drive to Seminyak area, while Ngurah Rai International Airport is a 40-minute drive away.

An airport pick-up is available at a charge. Fitted with a terrace and views of the garden, air-conditioned villas provide a personal safe, a wardrobe and a seating area.

Each villa has a dining area and a kitchenette which is equipped with a fridge, electric kettle and kitchenware.

En suite bathrooms come with hot/cold shower facilities and free toiletries. Guests can request for massage services or rent a motorbike to explore the area.

United Colors of Bali can also assist with laundry, car rental and babysitting services.

A tour desk and free private parking is available for guests convenience. A restaurant serving International dishes is situated within a 10-minute drive from the property.

Alternatively, the in-house chef can prepare lunch and dinner at an additional fee.
