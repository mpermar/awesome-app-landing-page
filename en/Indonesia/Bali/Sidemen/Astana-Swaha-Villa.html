Description

Situated in the tranquil Sidemen Village, Astana Swaha Villa presents an amazing getaway featuring an outdoor swimming pool overlooking fantastic views of mountain and lush valley.

Guests staying at this villa can also enjoy free WiFi access and free parking for guests who drive.

Each unit at this villa comes with a seating area, flat-screen TV, DVD player, personal safety box, air conditioning, ceiling fan, and minibar.

The en suite bathroom has hot and cold shower, hairdryer, and free toiletries. Some units have dining area, balcony, and a kitchen fitted with an oven and microwave, Bed linens are provided in each room.

Other facilities available at Astana Swaha Villa include a terrace, an on-site restaurant service Indonesian and Balinese dishes, and a 24-hour front desk where the friendly staff can help guests to arrange motorbike and car hire, airport transfers, shuttle service, laundry and ironing requests, and babysitting service at additional charges.

There is also a tour desk where guests can organise day tours and activities like meditation, relaxing massages, cycling and hiking.

Luggage storage and safety deposit box are available for free. It takes about a 30-minute drive from the villa to the iconic Grand Besakih Temple, and around a 45-minute drive to Padang Bai Harbour.

The famed Kuta and Seminyak areas are about 41 km away and Ubud is 20 km away. Getting to Bali Denpasar International Airport will take around 2 hours by car.
