Description

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool, Darmada Eco Resort is located in Sidemen. This lovely and homey accommodation also offers an on-site restaurant and free on-site parking for guests who drive.

It takes a 5-minute drive from the property to Telaga Waja rafting spot, and a 30-minute drive to the famed Besakih Grand Temple.

Getting to Bali Denpasar International Airport will take around 2 hours by car. The accommodation will provide you with a balcony and a terrace.

Private bathroom also comes with free toiletries. You can enjoy mountain view and river view from the room.

Extras include bed linen and a fan. At Darmada Eco Resort you will find a garden and a terrace.

Other facilities offered at the property include a ticket service, a tour desk and luggage storage.

As for dining option, the on-site Warung Melita serves Balinese, Indonesan and Western dishes.
