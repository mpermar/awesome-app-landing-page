Description

Offering a year-round outdoor pool, barbecue and terrace, Coco Resort Penida is situated in Nusa Penida.

Kuta is 33 km from the property. Featuring traditional architecture, the accommodation is fitted with a seating area with a TV and CD player.

Some units include a terrace and/or patio with mountains views. There is a private bathroom with a bidet and shower in each unit, along with slippers, free toiletries and a hair dryer.

Bed linen is available. Coco Resort Penida also includes a sun terrace. Guests can enjoy a drink at the on-site bar, while special diet menus and packed lunches are available on request.

Coco Resto serves a fine selection of Indonesian and international dishes. The property has water sports facilities and bike hire and car hire are available.

A number of activities are offered in the area, such as snorkeling and diving. The resort is a 30-minute boat ride from Sanur Harbour and a 3-minute drive from Crystal Bay Beach.

Ubud is 33 km from Coco Resort Penida. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai Airport, 34 km from Coco Resort Penida.
