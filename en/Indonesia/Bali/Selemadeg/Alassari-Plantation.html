Description

Boasting the richness of Balinese culture, Alassari Plantation offers luxury accommodation in Selemadeg, 39 km from Kuta.

Offering free WiFi and a restaurant, the property sits high in the rain forests of the sacred Mount Batu Karu in central Bali.

The resort has an outdoor pool and spa centre, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Free private parking is available on site. The exclusive private villa is decorated with carved teak wood carved imported from Java.

Each room includes a TV and DVD player. Some units feature a seating area where you can relax.

You will find a coffee machine in the room. All rooms come with a private bathroom with a bidet and shower, with bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries provided.

Ubud is 24 km from Alassari Plantation, while Seminyak is 35 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 41 km from Alassari Plantation.
