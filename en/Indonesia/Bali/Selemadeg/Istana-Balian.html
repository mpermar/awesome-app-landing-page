Description

Offering a year-round outdoor pool, Istana Balian is situated 250 metres from Balian Beach.

The restaurant offers lunch and dinner menu. Free high-speed WiFi is available in all areas.

Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and comes with a flat-screen cable TV as well as a DVD player.

All rooms feature a terrace, some offers sea view. You will find a minibar and tea/coffee making facilities in the room.

Each room includes a private bathroom. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property.

Guests can also visit nearby restaurants and cafes. The hotel also offers airport transfer.

Kuta is 50 km from Istana Balian, while Ubud is 33 km away. Ngurah Rai International Airport is 51 km from the property.
