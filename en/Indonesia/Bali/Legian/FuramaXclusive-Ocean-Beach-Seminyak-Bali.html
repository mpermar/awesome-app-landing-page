Description

Natural light coming in from the clear floor-to-ceiling glass windows bounces off the white décor of each apartment. Modern touches, such as flat-screen TVs, DVD players and air conditioning are included.

Guests may enjoy a relaxing massage at O-CE-N Spa, or go horse riding by the beach. FuramaXclusive Ocean Beach Seminyak Bali also provides convenient guest services like a 24-hour concierge and free parking.

With views of the white beach, THE DECK Café offers a selection of Mediterranean, European and Asian dishes. For an alternative, The Barrel Pub & Grill also provides a beachfront dining area.

FuramaXclusive Ocean Beach Seminyak Bali is 3.7 mi, or a 10-minute drive, from Ngurah Rai International Airport. Local attractions like Ku De Ta Restaurant and The Seminyak Square Shopping Mall are within 1.2 mi away.
