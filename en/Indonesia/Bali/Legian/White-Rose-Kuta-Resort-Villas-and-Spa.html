Description

Located 650 metres from Beachwalk Shopping Centre, White Rose Kuta Resort, Villas & Spa is surrounded by the colourful nightlife of Kuta-Legian Street.

It offers affordable rooms and features an outdoor pool with a poolside bar. The property also provides free parking.

Situated amidst 1. 6 hectares of tropical landscapes, White Rose’s air-conditioned rooms offer scenic views and classic wood furnishings.

They are well-appointed with a safe, a hairdryer and a fridge. For leisure, enjoy soothing massages at the spa.

The staff can provide tour and vehicle rental services. A variety of grilled seafood dishes and local specialities can be sampled at the 24-hour Lotus Restaurant.

Bunut Café & Bar serves wood-fired pizzas and steaks. White Rose Kuta Resort, Villas & Spa is 1.

6 km from Hardys Supermarket and 3. 3 km from Lippo Mall Kuta. Potato Head Beach Club is 10 km from the property, while Ngurah Rai Airport is 6.
