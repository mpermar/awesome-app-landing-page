Description

Club Bali Family Suites @ Legian Beach is located next to Legian Beach in Kuta. Offering free parking, the hotel features an outdoor pool, a spa and live cultural shows.

Free Wi-Fi is also provided throughout the hotel. Air-conditioned suites feature spacious interiors with modern furnishings.

Each is fitted with a kitchen and a private balcony. All rooms come with tea/coffee making facilities and a TV with satellite channels.

Club Bali Family Suites @ Legian Beach is 10 minutes' drive from the vibrant streets of Legian and is 20 minutes' drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport.

Guests can enjoy massages and beauty services at the spa. Other recreational facilities include a fitness centre and a tennis court.
