Description

Bali Jayakarta Hotel is a 25-minute drive from Denpasar and less than 3.4 mi from Ngurah Rai International Airport. Nearby attractions include Tanah Lot Temple and Kuta Handicraft Market.

Fully air-conditioned, rooms come with either a balcony or garden terrace, as well as hot water showers. Each is equipped with a cable/satellite TV and a mini-bar. Free WiFi is available.

Guests can recharge with a workout at the hotel's fitness center or play a round of tennis at the courts available. The hotel also features a spa with pampering body massage treatments.

Jayakarta Hotel Bali provides 5 food and beverage options serving a fine selection of cuisines that include local and international favorites and delicious Italian fare.

The reception can provide advice on the area in order to help guests plan their day. Popular points of interest near the hotel include Cocoon Beach Club, Sunset Star and Double Six Beach.
