Description

Situated in Kuta, 1. 2 km from Discovery Shopping Mall, Beach Hut Hostel features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a bar and a shared lounge.

With a garden, the 3-star hostel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi. The hostel provides an outdoor swimming pool, evening entertainment and a 24-hour front desk.

A Full English/Irish breakfast is available each morning at the hostel. Beach Hut Hostel offers a sun terrace.

Guests can enjoy playing billiards, or take advantage of the business centre. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Waterbom Bali, Kuta Center and Kuta Square.

The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International, 2 km from Beach Hut Hostel, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
