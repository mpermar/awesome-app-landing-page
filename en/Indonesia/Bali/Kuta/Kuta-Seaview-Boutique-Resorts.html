Description

Kuta Seaview Boutique Resorts is a 5-minute walk from Kuta Art Market and Kuta Shopping Center. Ngurah Rai International Airport is a 15-minute drive from the hotel.

Rooms, suites and cottages feature a relaxing, peaceful atmosphere and come equipped with a 42-inch flat screen cable TV. A minibar, a safe and tea and coffee-making facilities are provided. The private bathrooms feature both a bathtub and a shower.

Guests can choose from a variety of relaxing massage treatments in the spa or visit the library for a quiet read. An internet corner and tour desk are also available.

Beachfront Rosso Vivo Dine and Lounge serves Italian and international cuisine. A selection of fine wines are available.
