Description

Sheraton Bali Kuta Resort offers a 5-star accommodation in Bali's Kuta area. Located next to Beachwalk Shopping Mall and overlooking Kuta Beach, it features an outdoor pool and spacious rooms with free WiFi.

Each air-conditioned room is equipped with an LCD TV, a minibar and a safety deposit box.

A rain shower and bathtub are a standard across all en suite bathrooms. Most rooms offer ocean views.

Sheraton Bali Kuta Resort offers laundry and concierge services. A transfer from the airport can be arranged at an additional fee.

For relaxation, guests can head down to the spa to enjoy a massage or treatment. The property also offers kid's friendly facilities and amenities.

Feast Restaurant serves a variety of International dishes, while a menu of Italian specialities can be enjoyed at Bene Italian Kitchen Light.

Refreshments are offered at The Lounge. Room service is available. Located 5 km away from Sheraton Kuta Resort is Ngurah Rai Airport, Kuta Souvenir and Art Market and Discovery Shopping Mall.
