Description

Located only a 10-minute walk from the iconic Kuta Beach, Four Points by Sheraton Bali, Kuta offers accommodation in the heart of Kuta.

This hotel also offers and outdoor swimming pool, free WiFi access and several on-site dining options.

Every air-conditioned room at this hotel comes with a flat-screen satellite TV, seating area, an electric kettle, a personal safe, and a minibar.

There is also an en suite bathroom featuring shower facilities and free toiletries.

Bathrobes, slippers, and fresh towels are also provided. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property.

Guests can also find a concierge service, dedicated kid's club, fitness centre, luggage storage and room service.

Airport transfers, shuttle service, babysitting service, laundry and meeting rooms are arrangeable at additional charges.

An on-site fitness centre is accessible for free. Beachwalk Shopping Centre is located about 10-minute walk away, and Hard Rock Cafe is about 900 metres from Four Points by Sheraton Bali, Kuta.

The iconic Waterbom Bali is only about 2 km away while getting to Bali Denpasar International Airport will take around 25 minutes by car.

There are several on-site restaurants at this hotel. Best Brew offers a range of grilled menu, while The Eatery serves a great selection on international cuisine.

Vertigo presents a selection of refreshing beverages and snacks at their rooftop bar.
