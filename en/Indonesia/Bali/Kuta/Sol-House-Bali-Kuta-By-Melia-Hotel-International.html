Description

Sol House Bali Kuta is located across from Kuta Square. A 10-minute walk to Kuta Beach, the hotel offers a restaurant, swimming pool and rooms with views of the tropical garden.

Guests enjoy free WiFi in the lobby and free parking. The air-conditioned rooms at Sol House Bali Kuta feature modern colourful décor and private balconies overlooking the gardens or the pools.

Amenities include a flat-screen TV and minibar. Guests can enjoy live music performances at the dining outlets.

The hotel also has a gift shop and on-site tour desk. Food Factory serves a fine selection of local and international specialities.

Light snacks and refreshing cocktails are available at Playbar and Resting Palm. Sol House Bali Kuta is a 2-minute walk to Matahari Square and traditional market.

It is a 5-minute walk to Waterbom Park and Centro Discovery Mall. Ngurah Rai International Airport is 3 km from the hotel.
