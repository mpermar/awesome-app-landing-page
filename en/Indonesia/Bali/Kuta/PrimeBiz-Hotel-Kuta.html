Description

Located strategically in Kuta, Primebiz Hotel Kuta features a 24-hour front desk and a rooftop outdoor pool.

Guests can work out at the gym and enjoy relaxing massages. Free Wi-Fi is provided in all areas of the hotel.

Minimalist-style bedrooms are fitted with a flat-screen satellite TV, safety deposit box and tea/coffee-making facilities.

It also has a fridge and electric kettle for guests’ conveniences. The private bathroom comes with a shower and free toiletries.

Primebiz Hotel Kuta is a 5-minute drive to Discovery Shopping Mall and Ngurah Rai International Airport.

Kuta Beach is a 10-minute drive away. Local souvenir shops are within walking distance from the hotel.

Free scheduled shuttle service within Kuta area is available. The hotel provides a 24-hour room service and laundry service upon request.

Guests can also arrange airport transfers through the hotel. On-site restaurants include Flavours Restaurant serving international and Indonesian food.
