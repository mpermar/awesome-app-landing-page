Description

Just 100 metres from Amed Beach, this beachfront property houses an outdoor pool and offers free Wi-Fi access.

It features villas with a private patio, offering garden and ocean views. Onlyou Villas is a 30-minute drive from Tirta Gangga Water Palace and Lempuyang Temple.

It is an hour’s drive from Puri Besakih Temple and a 3-hour drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport.

The air-conditioned villas feature tiled flooring, teak wood furniture and natural stones.

They are equipped with satellite TV and a DVD player, along with a minibar. The private patio comes with a seating area.

Onlyou’s restaurant serves local Indonesian delights, along with international cuisine.

Alternatively, barbecue facilities are available and in-room dining is possible. Guests can head to the 24-hour reception for assistance with tour arrangement, airport shuttle or luggage storage services.
