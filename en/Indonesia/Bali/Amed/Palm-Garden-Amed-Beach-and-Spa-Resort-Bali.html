Description

Every unit features a private bathroom and bath, air conditioning, a TV and a fridge. Some units include a seating area and/or a balcony.

Breakfast is available daily, and includes continental and Full English/Irish options. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the in-house restaurant, which serves seafood and German cuisine and also offers vegan and gluten-free options.

Both a bicycle rental service and a car rental service are available at the resort village.

Ubud is 31 miles from Palm Garden Amed Beach & Spa. Ngurah Rai International Airport is 45 miles from the property.
