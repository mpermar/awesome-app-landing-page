Description

Rooms have a private bathroom with a shower, while some feature sea views.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the in-house restaurant, which serves Indonesian cuisine.

Ubud is 29 miles from the resort, while Gili Trawangan is 26 miles away. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 43 miles from the property.
