Description

Guest rooms at the resort come with a seating area. Free WiFi is accessible to all guests, while certain rooms will provide you with a balcony. The rooms have a desk.

A continental breakfast is available every morning at the property. Guests can dine in the on-site restaurant, which specializes in Mediterranean and Indonesian cuisine and also offers vegan, gluten-free and vegetarian options.

Guests at Balila Beach Resort will be able to enjoy activities in and around Amed, like cycling.

Ubud is 28 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 43 miles from the property.
