Description

Set in Keramas, 24 km from Kuta, Vision Villa Resort boasts a tranquil accommodation with a restaurant, bar and free WiFi throughout the property.

The resort has outdoor facilities including water gardens, swimming pool, wooden sundeck terrace, pavilions and daybeds.

Free private parking is available on site. Designed with Balinese style, each suite comes with a four-poster king-size bed, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, an en suite bathroom and an outdoor shower.

Guests can enjoy garden view from a private terrace. Free toiletries, bathrobes and slippers are featured in each room.

Guests can enjoy healthy dishes and signature drinks at Genius Cafe, or enjoy relaxing time at the spa and the library, surrounded by lush gardens.

The resort offers airport transfer, concierge service, bike and car hire at additional costs.

Vision Villa Resort is a 3 minutes' drive to Bali Safari & Marine Park, and 5 minutes' drive to Pantai Lebih.

Ubud is only 12 km away and Seminyak is 23 km from the property. Ngurah Rai International airport is 26 km away.
