Description

Mara River Safari Lodge is located within the Bali Safari & Marine Park near Mount Agung.

The resort offers free internet access, an outdoor pool, a spa and a restaurant. Free WiFi is accessible in all areas.

Accommodations features timber furnishings, earthy colors, and tribal artifacts. All lodges feature an open air terrace, giving guests the opportunity to observe different animals at close range.

The air-conditioned lodges are equipped with flat-screen satellite TV, a personal safe and a minibar.

A tea and coffee maker is also included. En suite bathrooms provide a hairdryer and free toiletries.

The resort has a business centre and offers in-room massage services. Tsavo Lion Restaurant serves international cuisines and offers views of the lions through full-length glass windows.

Mara River Safari Lodge is approximately 27 km from Ngurah Rai International Airport, 20-minute drive from Sanur area and a 45-minute drive from Ubud.
