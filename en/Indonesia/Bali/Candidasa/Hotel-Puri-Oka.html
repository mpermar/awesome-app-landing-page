Description

Puri Oka Hotel is a 15 minute walk from Candidasa Temple while Tenganan Village, the oldest village in Bali, is a 15 minute drive away. Tirta Gangga (Water Palace) is a 30 minute drive from the hotel.

Rooms and bungalows come with either a fan or air conditioning. A terrace with a sitting area is provided. Each room and bungalow also has a private entrance.

Car rental services and sightseeing arrangements can be done at the tour desk. Hotel staff can provide airport shuttle and laundry services as well.

Puri Oka Bar and Restaurant serves a selection of Indonesian and international food. This restaurant is located by the beach.
