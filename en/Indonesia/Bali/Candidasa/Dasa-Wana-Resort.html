Description

All rooms in the resort are equipped with a electric tea pot. All rooms are equipped with a private bathroom, while some have a garden view. The rooms include a closet.

A continental, American or Asian breakfast is available every morning at the property.

Dasa Wana Resort has a terrace.

Kuta is 31 miles from the accommodation, while Ubud is 20 miles away. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 32 miles from Dasa Wana Resort.
