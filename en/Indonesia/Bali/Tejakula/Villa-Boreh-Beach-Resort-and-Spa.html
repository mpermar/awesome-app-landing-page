Description

Located in the tranquil village of Sembiran in Bali's North East Coast, Villa Boreh Resort offers rustic accommodation with 2 outdoor swimming pools overlooking the garden or sea.

Tejakula Beach is only a few minute's walk away. Villa Boreh Beach Resort's rooms are air-conditioned and feature a desk and en suite semi-outdoor bathroom with shower facilities.

Decorated in a Balinese style, villas come with a fan and seating area. In select villas, air conditioning and a terrace offering garden views are offered.

Guests can indulge in a variety of treatments ranging from massage and body scrubs at Villa Boreh's spa, which offers its own line of spa products.

They are hand-made from locally grown ingredients. The resort also offers yoga lessons, cooking classes and tour arrangement.

The on-site restaurant offers sea or pool views and serves Indonesian and Western dishes.

Villa Boreh Beach Resort and Spa is a 1 hour drive from Lovina Beach. A 2-hour drive leads to Ubud Cultural City and Ngurah Rai Airport is a 3-hour drive away.
