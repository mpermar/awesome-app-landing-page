Description

Located along Nusa Dua Beach Bali Relaxing Resort and Spa houses an outdoor swimming pool and 2 dining options.

This beachfront property offers free drop-off to Tanjung Benoa and Nusa Dua areas.

Free Wi-Fi is accessible in all areas. Bali Relaxing Resort and Spa is within a 15-minute drive from Bali Collection Nusa Dua and a 30-minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport.

The hotel can arrange for airport pick-up services at a charge. Elegant rooms boast modern interiors and big windows, allowing much natural light in.

A flat-screen TV, minibar and tea/coffee-making facilities are included in all units.

Shower facilities and free toiletries are provided in the attached bathroom. Surrounded by coconut trees, the luxurious hotel offers far-reaching views of the ocean.

Guests can take a stroll in the landscaped gardens or pamper themselves at the spa & wellness centre.

Luggage storage and a safety deposit box are available at the 24-hour front desk.

Indonesian and international specialties are served at the on-site restaurant and coffee shop.

Guests can have cocktails and non-alcoholic drinks at the hotel’s lounge and bar.
