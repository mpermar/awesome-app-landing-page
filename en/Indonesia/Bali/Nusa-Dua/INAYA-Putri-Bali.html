Description

Situated along the beachfront Nusa Dua, a 15-minute walk from Bali Collection, INAYA Putri Bali offers modern and elegant accommodation with free in-room WiFi access.

It features an outdoor pool. The well appointed rooms here are air-conditioned and features a flat-screen satellite TV with English and Mandarin channels, a laptop safe, wardrobe and a seating area.

There is also an electric kettle, a desk and a terrace. One-time non-alcoholic minibar is provided.

The en suite bathroom comes with a bathtub, hairdryer, slippers and free toiletries including dental kit.

Some rooms offer a convenient pool access while others overlook the lush garden view.

Fresh towels and bed linens are provided in each room. The on-site restaurant is open for all day dining and serves a selection of Indonesian and Western cuisine.

INAYA Putri Bali operates a 24-hour front desk that provides dry cleaning and laundry services.

The concierge can assist with luggage storage, while day trips can be arranged at the tour desk.

Meeting and banquet facilities are available. Airport transfer along with vehicle rentals can also be arranged with additional charges.

Guests can have relaxing treatments at the spa and wellness centre. The resort is a 20-minute drive from Pandawa Bach and a 25-minute drive from Bali Denpasar International Airport.
