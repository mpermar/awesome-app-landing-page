Description

Novotel Bali Nusa Dua features a spa and 4 dining options. It boasts spacious rooms, as well as suites with living, dining and kitchen areas.

This property is located in the heart of 350-hectare, secured and exclusive Indonesia Tourism Development Complex (ITDC) in Nusa Dua.

Novotel Bali Nusa Dua provides a free shuttle service to Nusa Dua Beach for sunrise.

The hotel is a 30-minute drive from Kuta Town and a 15-minute drive from the cliffs of Jimbaran Bay.

Rooms at Nusa Dua Novotel feature private balconies with pool or garden views. Each room has a flat-screen TV, safety deposit box, and ironing facilities.

Relaxation options include a 1500-sqm lagoon pool and a fitness centre, as well as massage services.

Car rentals and travel arrangements are provided for those who wish to explore the area.

Guests can also enjoy a day of golfing at Nusa Dua Golf Course - Bali National Golf Club which is only a 5-minute drive away.

Guests can enjoy open-air dining at The Square, or a wide variety of delicious light meals and refreshing cocktails at 2.

28 Beach Club which can be reached by a short walk of free shuttle service. Gourmet Bar offers a casual and friendly space where guests can enjoy popular drinks and food.
