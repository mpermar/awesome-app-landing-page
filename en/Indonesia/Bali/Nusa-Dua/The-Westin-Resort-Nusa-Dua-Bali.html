Description

The Westin Resort Nusa Dua Bali is located right on the white sandy beach of Nusa Dua and a 5-minute drive from Bali Collection Shopping Gallery.

It offers 5-star luxury with 7 dining options, a spa and 5 outdoor pools. Free parking on site and free WiFi in all areas.

Air-conditioned guest rooms are fitted with a personal safe, tea/coffee making facilities and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

The en suite bathroom comes with free toiletries and a bathtub. Recreational facilities include a fitness centre and 2 full size tennis courts.

Massage services can be enjoyed at the spa centre. The staff at the tour desk can assist with making travel arrangements.

Guests can enjoy dining on barbecued seafood dishes by the beach at Ikan Restaurant & Bar, while a selection of Italian cuisine can be sampled at Prego.

Hamabe Restaurant serves authentic Japanese dishes and a variety of sumptuous Chinese, Indian, Mediterranean to Western is served at Seasonal Tastes.

Guests can also enjoy tasty tapas at Velada Tapas Bar & Kitchen. A selection of refreshing beverages, light snacks and desserts can be enjoyed at Kites Lounge & Bar.

Guests can also sample wholesome buffet breakfast featuring nutritious SuperFoodsRX selection at an additional charge.

Resort Nusa Dua Bali Westin is a 15-minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport.
