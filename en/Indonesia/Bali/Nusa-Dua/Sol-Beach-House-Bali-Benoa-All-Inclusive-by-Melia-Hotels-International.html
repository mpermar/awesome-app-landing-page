Description

Boasting seaside accommodation along Tanjung Benoa Beach, Sol Beach House Bali Benoa All Inclusive by Melia Hotels International is nestled within tropical gardens.

It offers a spectacular freeform pool, a luxurious spa menu and memorable dining possibilities on the private beach.

Formerly known as Melia Benoa All Inclusive Resort, Sol Beach House Bali Benoa All Inclusive by Melia Hotels International is easily accessible from Nusa Dua Shopping Centre.

It is a 5-minute drive from the Bali International Convention Centre and a 15-minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport.

Featuring Balinese-style d√©cor, guestrooms come with private balconies. They are spacious and feature flat-screen TVs with international channels and elegant bathrooms with rain shower facilities.

For extra comfort, slippers and dental kit are provided. Exclusive dining experiences await guests at the on-site restaurants.

Enjoy beautiful views of the garden with Spanish specialties at The Kitchen, sample Italian dishes at The Amarta Restaurant or make the most of the tropical climate at Menega, the open-air restaurant.

Recreation facilities available include a fitness centre, games room and karaoke room.

The well-appointed business centre provides wired internet access for a fee, or guests can enjoy free Wi-Fi in all the rooms and throughout the hotel.
