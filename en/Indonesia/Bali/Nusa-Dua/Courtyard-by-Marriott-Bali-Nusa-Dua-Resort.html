Description

Featuring a large landscaped lagoon pool bordered by sun loungers, the 5-star Courtyard by Mariott Bali Nusa Dua Resort houses fitness facilities, a beach club and 5 dining venues.

Free WiFi is accessible in all areas. Guests can stroll for 15 minutes or take the 3-minute complimentary shuttle ride to reach the white sandy Nusa Dua Beach.

A flat-screen cable TV and marble bathrooms with rain showers or bathtubs are standard in all air-conditioned rooms.

Tea/coffee-making facilities are included. Courtyard by Marriott Bali Nusa Dua Resort is a 3-minute drive from Bali Collection Shopping Complex and 20-minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport.

Leisurely golfing sessions can be enjoyed at the course within 500 metres of the resort.

The hotel provides concierge services, spa, and has a business centre. Other facilities include a yoga pavilion, a kids' pool and a kids' club.

All-day dining with international dishes is available in the stylish Momo Café. The lobby lounge and pool bar are ideal for light snacks and after-meal drinks.
