Description

Offering beachfront accommodation, Holiday Inn Resort Bali Benoa features 4 outdoor pools including a kid's pool with slides and water bucket.

It has a fitness centre and a spa centre. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

The air-conditioned rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV and a safety deposit box.

Certain rooms have a seating area to relax in after a busy day. Enjoy a cup of tea while looking out at the sea or pool.

Stunning sunrise can be viewed from the balcony. The rooms have a private bathroom with a bidet and shower, with bath robes and slippers provided.

Extras include free toiletries and a hair dryer. The resort offers 24-hour front desk, concierge services, tour desk and currency exchange.

Guest can enjoy evening entertainment at the bar or the complimentary daily recreational activities around the resort.

The area is popular for cycling and water sport activities. Holiday Inn Resort Bali Benoa is a 5-minute drive from the popular Bali Collection Shopping Complexes and Bali International Convention Centre.

The nearest airport is Bali Denpasar International Airport, 8 km from the property.
