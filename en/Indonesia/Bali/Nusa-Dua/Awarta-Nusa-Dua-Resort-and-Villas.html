Description

Awarta Nusa Dua Luxury Villas & Spa offers a tropical retreat in the Nusa Dua area.

Offering free WiFi in all areas, the property houses an on site spa & wellness centre where guests can relax with pampering treatments.

It takes about a 10-minute stroll from Awarta Nusa Dua Luxury Villas & Spa and the white sand of Nusa Dua Beach.

Getting to Bali Denpasar International Airport will take 15 minutes by car. The villas at Awarta Nusa Dua Luxury Villas & Spa features a private pool, a dining area and a separate living area.

Each air-conditioned bedroom also comes with a fan, wardrobe, iPod docking system, a flat-screen satellite TV, and an en suite bathroom featuring shower, hairdryer, and free toiletries.

Complimentary minibar, 24-hour butler service and safe are also available in each villa.

A 24-hour front desk with concierge service, room service, free access to fitness centre, sauna, and luggage storage services are offered at this resort.

Other facilities and services like meeting/banquet facility, business centre, laundry and dry cleaning, and airport shuttle are available for guests at additional costs.

Guests can also enjoy selections of menus offered from three of the resort's restaurants and bar.

The Long Table Restaurant serves Western and Asian dishes, whilst Ru Yi Chinese Fine Dining Restaurant serves exotic Chinese cuisines and seafood.
