Description

Located on the Southeastern coast of Bali, The Sakala Resort Bali boasts an extensive outdoor pool, spa and two dining options with 24-hour room service.

Suites in this resort blend modern interiors with traditional Balinese touches. Free WiFi is accessible throughout the property.

The Sakala Resort Bali is a 10-minute drive from Bali Collection shopping centre, 30-minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport and a 40-minute drive from trendy Seminyak.

Fitted with air conditioning and wooden flooring, spacious suites each feature a kitchenette, dining area and a living area with cushions.

Amenities include 42-inch flat-screen cable TVs and bath amenities. Free tropical fruits are served upon arrival.

Sakala Beach Club is located on the beachfront, offering international cuisines, while Asian delights are served at the restaurant by the main pool.

The Lobby Bar specialises in Bali-inspired cocktails and light snacks. Alternatively, guests may order from the 24-hour room service.
