Description

Located just a few steps from Jimbaran Bay Beach, The Open House Jimbaran Bali offers cozy accommodation with organic and eco-friendly design featuring an outdoor pool, garden and gazebo.

This hotel is only 1-minute walk away from Jimbaran Bay Beach, 5-minute walk to Jimbaran Traditional Market and 25-minute drive to the famous Uluwatu Temple where you can see traditional Balinese dance performance during sunset on top of a cliff.

Ngurah Rai International Airport is about 15-minute drive away from the property.

Rooms at The Open House Jimbaran Bali are well decorated with a mix of modern and vintage style fitted with a king size bed, flat screen TV, an iPod dock.

Also, all rooms feature either a terrace or a balcony overlooking the swimming pool.

At this hotel, you will also find a 24-hour front desk, tour desk and shared lounge.

Guests can also spend some quiet reading time in the relaxing area and enjoy homemade dishes in a little coffee shop on-site.

The Open House Jimbaran Bali also offers a restaurant which serves international food and cocktails.
