Description

Set amidst tropical gardens, Belmond Jimbaran Puri features spacious Balinese-style cottages and villas with walled terraces and indoor/outdoor bathrooms.

It has an outdoor pool, 3 dining options and a beach spa. Located along Jimbaran Bay, Belmond Jimbaran Puri is a 15-minute drive from Kuta and Nusa Dua areas and a 10-minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport.

Fitted with thatched roofs, each stand-alone accommodation in Belmond-Jimbaran Puri Bali features a large marble or stone bathtub and double sinks.

Modern comforts include a flat-screen TV and a DVD player. Some offer private pools.

Open for lunch and dinner, Nelayan Restaurant serves seafood as well as French Mediterranean dishes, with beachside dining.

Tunjung Café features an American buffet breakfast. In the evening, it offers Indonesian specialities.

Light snacks and beverages are available at Puri Bar. Belmond Jimbaran Puri provides transport rentals and room service.
