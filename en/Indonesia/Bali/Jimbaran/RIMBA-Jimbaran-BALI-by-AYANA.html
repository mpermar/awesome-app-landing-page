Description

RIMBA Jimbaran BALI by AYANA is a 5-star accommodation with 12 outdoor pools, gym and 19 dining options.

Operating a 24-hour front desk, the resort houses well furnished rooms with scenic views of the tropical landscapes.

Free WiFi is accessible in all areas, including in the guest rooms. Fitted with a private balcony, each air-conditioned room in RIMBA Jimbaran BALI by AYANA is equipped with an iPod dock, minibar and a flat-screen TV.

A DVD player is available upon request. The en suite bathroom comes with a bathtub and free bath and dental amenities.

On some selected rooms, there's a direct lagoon pool access and interconnecting doors.

The resort is a 20-minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport and a 15-minute drive from Jimbaran area where an array of fresh seafood restaurants can be found.

Guests of RIMBA Jimbaran BALI by AYANA enjoy priority access to the famous Rock Bar and the spa & wellness centre at AYANA Resort and Spa, BALI.

The resort also provides free shuttle service to Bali Collection Shopping Centre as well as AYANA Resort and Spa, BALI.

A children's playground, business centre and a tour desk are among the facilities.

Union Pay is accepted here. Among the many dining options, Ah-Yat Abalone specialises in authentic Chinese cuisine, while Toge Restaurant serves a spread of international favourites.

In-room dining is possible through room service. As an alternative, guests can enjoy breakfast at the sister property, AYANA Resort's Padi Restaurant.

Staff at RIMBA Jimbaran BALI by AYANA speaks English, Mandarin, Japanese, Korean, Russian and Indonesian.
