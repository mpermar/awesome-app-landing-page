Description

InterContinental Bali Resort is situated along Jimbaran Bay, just 10 minutes’ drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport.

It features 6 pools and a 24-hour fitness centre. Free WiFi is accessible in all areas.

The resort offers rooms with Balinese artwork, a private terrace and a spacious marble bathroom with amenities and bathtub.

Rooms are fitted with satellite TV, a safe and tea/coffee maker. Guests can enjoy a game of tennis or attend yoga classes and the weekly culinary events.

The spa offers a sauna, massage services and a beauty salon. The InterContinental Bali Resort also features a well-stocked DVD, book and music library.

Breakfast buffet and pizzas are offered at Jimbaran Gardens Restaurant, which features open-air seating and live entertainment.

The hotel is a 5-minute walk from Jimbaran Seafood Café. The Uluwatu Temple is 16 km from the resort, while New Kuta Golf Course is 10.
