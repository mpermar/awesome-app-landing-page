Description

Featuring sea views, the villa is composed of 4 bedrooms and 4 bathrooms with free toiletries. There's a seating area, a dining area and a kitchen equipped with an oven.

Breakfast is available every morning, and includes continental and buffet options.

The villa has an outdoor pool. Guests can also relax on the sun terrace.

Jimbaran Corner is 2.2 miles from Villa Lebah, while Balangan Beach is 2 miles away. Ngurah Rai International Airport is 4.3 miles from the property.
