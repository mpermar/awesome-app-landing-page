Description

Located a 10-minute drive from the famed Rock Bar, The Sintesa Jimbaran Bali offers accommodation featuring semi-indoor and outdoor swimming pools, children's playground.

There are free WiFi access and free parking for guests who drive. The on-site restaurant offers a wide range of international dishes.

Guests can also enjoy free scheduled shuttle service to Jimbaran Bay and Jimbaran's iconic seafood restaurant row, as well as to Samasta Lifestyle Village located 1.

7 km away. It takes a bout a 15-minute drive from the property to the iconic Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park, and getting to Bali Denpasar International Airport will take around 20 minutes by car.

Every room at The Sintesa Jimbaran Bali is air-conditioned and comes with flat-screen satellite TV, minibar, wardrobe, and an en suite bathroom featuring bath or shower facilities.

Some rooms offer a separate dining area. The professional staff at the 24-hour front desk are available to help guests to arrange airport shuttle and laundry requests at additional charges.

Guests can get a free access to fitness centre and there is also an on-site shop.
