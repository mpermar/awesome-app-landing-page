Description

Situated 8 km from Central Jimbaran, AYANA Resort and Spa, BALI offers luxurious rooms overlooking the private beaches of Jimbaran Bay.

It is set on 90 hectares of cliff-top tropical garden, perched 35 metres above Jimbaran Bay.

Home to the famous Rock Bar, the resort provides 12 swimming pools and 19 dining options.

Multilingual staff are always ready to assist guests during stay. Decorated with elegant wooden furnishings, rooms at AYANA Resort and Spa, BALI come with 42-inch flat-screen TVs and stylish marble bathrooms with bathtubs, slippers, a dental kit and free toiletries.

Tea/coffee making facilities and a chargeable minibar are also available. Guests may enjoy Thalasso massage therapies at the spa, which uses fresh seawater that is rich in minerals.

They can also play a game of golf at the 18-hole putting course. The resort also provides an on-site tennis court, fitness centre and a tour desk.

Union Pay is accepted here. Surrounded by lotus ponds, the open-air Padi Restaurant serves a daily buffet breakfast and international favourites.

As an alternative, guests can also enjoy breakfast at RIMBA Jimbaran BALI by AYANA's Toge Restaurant.

The resort provides free shuttle service to Bali Collection Shopping Centre in Nusa Dua.
