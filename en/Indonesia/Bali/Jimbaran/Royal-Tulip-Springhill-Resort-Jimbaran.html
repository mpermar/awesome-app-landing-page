Description

Located 1. 1 km from The Rock Bar in Jimbaran, Royal Tulip Springhill Resort Jimbaran welcomes guests with a year-round outdoor pool, kids and game rooms, a fitness centre, a spa centre and a Yoga room.

Free WiFi is provided throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

A flat-screen TV with cable channels and DVD player are available by request. Certain units feature a seating area where you can relax.

You will find a kettle in the room. All rooms are fitted with a private bathroom.

Extras include bathrobes and slippers. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property and the staff is able to converse in English, Mandarin, Arabic and Indonesian.

Meeting and banquet facilities are also available. The property features Basil & Thyme, a restaurant that offers international authentic cuisines of Asian, Grill and Mediterranean.

Bicycle hire and car hire are available at this resort and the area is popular for golfing and cycling.

The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 6 km from Royal Tulip Springhill Resort Jimbaran.
