Description

Temple Hill Residence Villa offers a selection of spacious villas with large private pools.

A 7-minute walk from Jimbaran Bay, it has in-villa spa services, 24-hour front desk and free Wi-Fi.

The open-plan villas have a living room with flat-screen TV, DVD player and DVD library.

All connect to a private sundeck by the pool, complete with sun loungers and umbrellas.

All villas have modern amenities provided, including a fully equipped kitchen. Each villa has household staff to do the daily chores while you relax by the pool or head off on a day of exploring the beautiful island of Bali.

The tropical escape of Temple Hill Residence Villa is a 15-minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport.

It is a 20-minute drive from Waterbom Park and Uluwatu Temple. Free parking is available.

Guests can arrange car rentals or day trips at the tour desk. Free return shuttle service to Ayana Resort is provided.
