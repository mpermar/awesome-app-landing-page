Description

Featuring free WiFi, a restaurant and an outdoor pool, Suites by Watermark Hotel and Spa offers accommodation in Jimbaran, 5 km from The Rock Bar.

The hotel provide butler service, has a spa centre and fitness centre. Guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Free private parking is available on site. Featuring contemporary designs, the air-conditioned suites are fitted with a flat-screen cable TV, a personal safe, a wardrobe and an electric kettle.

Each unit has a seating area and an en suite bathroom. For convenience, guests are provided with slippers, bathrobes and free toiletries.

Towels and bed linens are also available. Suites by Watermark Hotel and Spa operates a 24-hour front desk where the staff can arrange services such as laundry and dry cleaning.

Other on-site facilities include a fitness centre, a spa centre and a kids club. Guests at the property can find 2 dining options.

W Senses Restaurant serves international dishes, while Nishiazabu Imadoki presents selections of Japanese cuisine.

Room service is also available upon request. Jimbaran Corner is 2. 7 km from Suites by Watermark Hotel and Spa, while Balangan Beach is 7 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 2 km from Suites by Watermark Hotel and Spa.

The property provides complimentary shuttle to Beach Club, Kuta area and night shuttle to Seminyak.
