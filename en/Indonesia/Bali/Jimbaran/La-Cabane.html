Description

A 5-minute stroll from the white sand Balangan Beach, La Cabane boasts an outdoor pool and wooden bungalows amidst tropical greenery.

Each unit features sliding doors that open onto a private terrace facing the garden.

WiFi access is free throughout the property. La Cabane is a 5-minute walk from Biu-Biu Beach.

The property is a 20-minute drive from Uluwatu Temple and a 30-minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport.

Offering spacious layouts, the bungalows are fitted with mosquito nets, personal safe and a seating area.

Shower facilities, towels and free bath amenities are available in the private bathrooms.

Airport transfers can be arranged at a fee, while on-site parking spaces are provided free of charge.

For meals, guests can have it at the on-site restaurant. Breakfast is delivered to guests rooms every morning.
