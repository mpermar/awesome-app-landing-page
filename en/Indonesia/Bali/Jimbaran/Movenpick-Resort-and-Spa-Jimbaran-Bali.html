Description

Boasting 4 different outdoor pools, Mövenpick Resort & Spa Jimbaran Bali is set in Jimbaran in the Bali Region, 100 metres from Jimbaran Beach.

The family resort has a spa centre, a fitness centre, 5 dining outlets and 7 venues for meetings and events.

Guests enjoy free daily chocolate hours and free minibar upon arrival. Air-conditioned rooms at Mövenpick Resort & Spa Jimbaran Bali feature a flat-screen TV with 74 satellite channels, as well as an iPod docking station.

All rooms include a private bathroom equipped with a bath, a hairdryer and free toilteries.

For extra comfort, guests are provided with bathrobes and slippers. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property and the staff can assist with babysitting services, valet parking, bike hire and car rental.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including golfing, surfing and snorkelling.

Mövenpick Resort & Spa Jimbaran Bali is 7. 8 km from Balangan Beach and 8 km from the nearest airport, Ngurah Rai International Airport.
