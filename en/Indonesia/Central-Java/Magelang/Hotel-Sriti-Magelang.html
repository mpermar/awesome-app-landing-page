Description

Centrally located, Hotel Sriti Magelang is within walking distance from downtown Magelang.

Offering an outdoor pool, it has an Indonesian restaurant and free Wi-Fi in public areas.

The hotel is a 30-minute drive from Borobudur Temple and a 45-minute drive from Ketep Pass.

Adisucipto International Airport is an hour's drive away. The air-conditioned guestrooms are equipped with a cable TV and tea/coffee making facilities.

En suite bathrooms come with cold and hot-water showers. Room service is available.

Guests of Sriti Magelang can rent bicycles or cars to explore the area. Traditional biking trips and tours to Borobudur Village can be arranged.
