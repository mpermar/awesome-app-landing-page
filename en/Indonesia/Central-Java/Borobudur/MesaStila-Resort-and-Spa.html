Description

Located in the centre of a coffee plantation, MesaStila Resort and Spa offers villa-style accommodation, an outdoor infinity pool and a luxurious spa among its facilities.

Free WiFi access is available in public areas. Surrounded by lush, natural surroundings, MesaStila Resort and Spa is accessible via Semarang, Yogjakarta and Solo Airports.

Villas feature original antiques and art collections. Each air-conditioned villa consists of a spacious living area, outdoor verandas with views of coffee plantations or mountains, satellite TV and DVD player.

Recreation facilities include an outdoor swimming pool overlooking green valleys, tennis courts and a restored Dutch mansion that is now a Club House.

Daily scheduled activities such as coffee plantation tour, yoga, Javanese dance class and martial arts class, can be enjoyed in the property.

Breakfast is included in the room rates. Guests can also enjoy daily afternoon tea.
