Description

Fitted with a terrace, the units feature a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with free toiletries. A balcony with mountain views is offered in every unit.

The resort village offers a buffet or Asian breakfast. At The Wujil Resort & Conventions you'll find a restaurant serving Indonesian cuisine. A halal option can also be requested.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool.

The Wujil Resort & Conventions provides a playground.

The nearest airport is Ahmad Yani International Airport, 14 miles from the resort village.
