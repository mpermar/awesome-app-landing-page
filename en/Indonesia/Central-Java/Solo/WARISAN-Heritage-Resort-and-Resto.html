Description

Fitted with a terrace, the units feature a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with free toiletries. Some units are air-conditioned and include a seating and/or dining area.

Breakfast is available daily, and includes continental, buffet and à la carte options.

The resort village has an outdoor pool.

Both a bicycle rental service and a car rental service are available at this property.

The Park Solo is 2.2 miles from WARISAN Heritage Resort & Resto, while Radya Pustaka Museum is 3.2 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Adisumarmo Airport, 6 miles from the property.
