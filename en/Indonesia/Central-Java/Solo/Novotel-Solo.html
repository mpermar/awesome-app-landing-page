Description

Located in Solo, Novotel features an outdoor landscaped swimming pool and a variety of fitness facilities.

This 4-star hotel offers 2 dining options, free WiFi and free parking. Large rooms come with modern interiors and windows that offer plenty of natural light.

All rooms are equipped with a TV, personal safe and private bathroom. Guests can enjoy a relaxing body massage.

Car rental and sightseeing arrangements can be made at the tour desk. Available for all-day dining, Andrawina Restaurant serves a variety of international dishes.

Refreshing beverages are offered at Sarawati Bar. Novotel Solo is a 10-minute drive from Surakarta Palace and Klewer Market.
