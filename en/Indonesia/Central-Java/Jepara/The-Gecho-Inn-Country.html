Description

At the resort, rooms contain a desk and a flat-screen TV. With a private bathroom equipped with a shower and free toiletries, rooms at The Gecho Inn Country also provide guests with free WiFi. At the accommodation all rooms are equipped with a seating area.

A continental or Full English/Irish breakfast is available every morning at the property.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental is available at The Gecho Inn Country.

The nearest airport is Ahmad Yani International Airport, 35 miles from the property.
