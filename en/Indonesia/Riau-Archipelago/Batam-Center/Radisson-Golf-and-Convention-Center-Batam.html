Description

Featuring free WiFi, a restaurant and a year-round outdoor pool, Radisson Golf & Convention Centre Batam offers accommodation in Batam Center.

Guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Some units feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

You will find a kettle in the room. All rooms are equipped with a shared bathroom fitted with a shower.

For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer. A flat-screen TV with satellite channels is provided.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. With prior booking, complimentary drop-off service to Mega Mall are offered on the weekend based on schedule and availability.

Car hire is available at this hotel and the area is popular for golfing. Batam Centre Ferry Terminal is 4.
