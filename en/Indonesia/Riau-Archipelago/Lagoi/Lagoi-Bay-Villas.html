Description

Welcoming guests with luxury suites and villas with private pools, Lagoi Bay Villas is located in Lagoi in the Bintan Region.

Free parking is available on site and free WiFi can be accessed throughout. All units at this property are well-appointed with modern amenities and furnishings.

The suites and villas all have a private pool and garden. They all have air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a minibar, a fridge, and a personal safe.

The spacious bathrooms are fitted with a shower and a bathtub, complete with a hairdryer, bathrobes, slippers, and free amenities.

Select units have a dining area. Lagoi Bay Villas has daily housekeeping service.

The property offers a 24-hour front desk, luggage storage and valet parking. Guests can find a hairdresser, a gift shop and a mini market on site.

Free shuttle to surrounding area is available and the staff can arrange bike rental, car hire and airport transfer for a fee.

Languages spoken at the reception include English and Indonesian. Desaru is 47 km from Lagoi Bay Villas, while Tanjung Pinang is 29 km from the property.
