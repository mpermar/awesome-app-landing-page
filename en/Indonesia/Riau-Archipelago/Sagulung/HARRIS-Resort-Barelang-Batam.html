Description

Welcoming guests with sweeping sea views, HARRIS Resort Barelang Batam in Airnanti provides a private beach area, outdoor swimming pools and a fitness centre.

At this 4-star resort, guests can enjoy a drink at the bar or a meal in the restaurant.

Free WiFi and free shuttle service are provided. The brightly lit units in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

The rooms are fitted with a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries. Towels and linen are provided.

Some units are fitted with a terrace overlooking the pool or the sea. Every morning guests can enjoy buffet or Asian breakfast at HARRIS Resort Barelang Batam.

Guests can enjoy pampering treatments at the spa centre, while the little ones can enjoy playing at the children's playground.

With staff speaking English and Indonesian, around the clock information is available at the reception.

Singapore is 40 km from Harris Resort Batam Barelang, while Tanjung Pinang is 46 km from the property.
