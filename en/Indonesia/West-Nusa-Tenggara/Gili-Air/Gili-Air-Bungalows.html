01/13/2018

Loved this place - ticks all the boxes

"We are a couple with kids 17, 19 and 22. Their room had a hot shower and we chose not to so I was a bit concerned about the cold shower, but it was fine as it was so hot outside. The outdoor shower is private and really suits the warm climate. Loved the location because it was so close to the beach and main drag but far enough away to be quiet and peaceful. We hired bikes from Gili Air Bungalows to get around the island which is a great way to see everything. The gardens and pool are beautifully maintained and the views from every room, whether garden or pool are great. Breakfast is from the Chill Out Bar and you can have it there or delivered to your room. We loved the Chill out bar and went there for many meals. The staff are really helpful. Loved the water cooler which provides chilled water and also serves as a hot water dispenser for tea and coffee. It was good not having to worry about buying bottled water. Snorkelling off the beach from the Chill Out Bar was easy and we saw turtles. You can also reserve beach chairs under umbrellas in front of the Chill Out Bar as part of the link with Gili Air Bungalows (for free). You can also snorkel directly out from the road that leads to the bungalows (just to left slightly as you face the sea). Loved the laid back atmosphere. It rained when we first arrived but having the little sitting area outside the room was a great place to chill without getting wet. Beds really comfy."
