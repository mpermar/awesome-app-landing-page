Description

Set in traditional thatch-roofed buildings, the units offer air conditioning, a personal safe and a flat-screen TV. Each unit has a terrace with sea view and a private bathroom with shower and free toiletries.

A continental breakfast is available every morning at the property.

If you would like to discover the area, cycling is possible in the surroundings and a bicycle rental service can be arranged.

Gili Trawangan is 3.7 mi from the vacation park, while Tanjung is 5 mi from the property.
