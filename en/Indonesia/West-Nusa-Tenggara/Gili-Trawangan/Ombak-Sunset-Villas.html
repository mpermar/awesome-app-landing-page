Description

Spacious and fitted with plush pillows, all air-conditioned rooms at the property comes with a flat-screen TV and a coffee/tea maker with complimentary bottles of water. Guests can enjoy a terrace or a balcony with a seating area overlooking the pool or garden. The large private bathroom provides a shower, fresh towels, a hairdryer, and free toiletries.

Guests at Ombak Sunset Villas can enjoy a buffet breakfast. Meals are also available through room service.

Speaking English and Indonesian, staff are available to help at the 24-hour front desk. They can assist you with laundry and ironing service at additional cost.

The area is popular for cycling and snorkeling. Bike hire is available at Ombak Sunset Villas. Sunset Point is only 200 feet away, while other popular attractions such as art market, turtle conservation, and harbor are also within walking distance.
