Description

Located in North Lombok, Jeeva Klui Resort is set in a spacious tropical garden on Klui Beach, overlooking the Indian Ocean and the island of Bali.

The resort has traditional Sasak design and offers a beachfront infinity pool, two spa centres and an adults only pool and gym.

Guests enjoy free Wi-Fi and parking. The air-conditioned suites at Klui Jeeva Resort come with traditional Indonesian dcor and have plenty of natural light.

A rainshower and toiletries are in the private bathroom. Guests can relax on the terrace or enjoy sunbathing on the sun loungers.

The resort offers an on-site boutique and car rental services. Day trips can be arranged.

Laundry services are available upon request. Asian and Western dishes are served at The Warung, while complimentary daily afternoon tea time snacks and fruits are offered.

Resort Jeeva Klui is 4 km from Sengigi Beach and 55 km from Lombok International Airport.
