Description

The rooms at the resort are equipped with a seating area. All rooms come with a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries. All rooms at Lina Cottage Senggigi have air conditioning and a desk.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool.

Staff at the reception can provide information on what to do in the area.
