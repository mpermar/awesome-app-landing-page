Description

Perched 150 metres above sea level, Villa Umbrella offers an accommodation with a spectacular view of Senggigi Bay.

Senggigi Beach is 2. 8 km away. Away from busy areas of Lombok, this property boasts an outdoor pool and room service.

Free WiFi is accessible in all public areas. Free private parking is available on site.

Guests staying at this boutique-style property will enjoy a private balcony overlooking the pool and the sea.

All rooms are air-conditioned and the kitchen is fitted with modern appliances. Guests will also enjoy the en suite bathrooms fitted with a shower.

You will find free toiletries and towels are provided. You can approach Villa Umbrella's friendly staff for help in arranging day trips or shuttle service around the area.

Airport transfer, boat trips, bike rental, and laundry service are available at additional charges.

The property is 5-minute drive away from Senggigi centre and a 20-minute drive to the harbour, where guest can take a 10-minute boat ride to Gili Islands.
