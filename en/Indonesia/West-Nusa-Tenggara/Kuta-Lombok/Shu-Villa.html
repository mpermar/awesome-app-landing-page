Description

Surrounded by a tropical garden, the spacious Shu Villa offers chargeable on-site laundry services.

Free WiFi access is available in public areas. The air conditioned houses are fitted with a flat-screen cable TV, CD and DVD player.

Also fitted with sofa, carpeted floor and separate living area. Fully equipped kitchen has a refrigerator, stove and kitchenware.

Barbecue facilities are available upon request. Complete with an electric kettle and dining table.

Private bathroom comes with a shower and free toiletries. The friendly staff at Shu Villa can help arrange for car hire, airport transfer and bike rental at a surcharge.

Guests can have a relaxing massage for an additional charge. Shu Villa is 400 metres from Kuta Lombok Traditional Market.

Surfers can catch a wave in Mawi Beach located a 25-minute drive away while the unspoilt Tanjung Aan Beach is a 20-minute drive away.
