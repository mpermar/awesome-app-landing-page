Description

At the resort, each room has a balcony with a sea view. Each room is equipped with a private bathroom with a shower, free toiletries and a hair dryer. All guest rooms at Murex Bangka Dive Resort include air conditioning and a desk.

The accommodation offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

Manado is 27 miles from Murex Bangka Dive Resort, while Bunaken is 27 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Sam Ratulangi Airport, 20 miles from the resort.
