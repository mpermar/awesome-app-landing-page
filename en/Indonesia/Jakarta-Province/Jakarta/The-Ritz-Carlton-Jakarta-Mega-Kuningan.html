Description

The Ritz-Carlton Jakarta, Mega Kuningan offers a luxury accommodation in Jakarta.

Featuring a pampering spa, a modern gym and a 30-metre outdoor pool, it also has 2 restaurants.

Free WiFi throughout and free parking are available. All air-conditioned rooms are appointed with floor-to-ceiling windows and elegant dark wood furniture.

Each has a flat-screen satellite TV, a seating area and luxury amenities. The en suite marble bathroom provides a bathub, a shower and free toiletries.

Asia Restaurant boasts a large international dishes spread, while Lobo Restaurant serves prime grilled steak and Italian specialties.

Relaxing body massage and foot reflexology can be enjoyed at the spa. The hotel also provides a business centre and a jogging track.

Other facilities include live music at Avanti Lounge, a cigar haven in Macanudo Lounge and luxurious karaoke rooms at Jade.

Airport transfers, scheduled shuttle service within Mega Kuningan area and limousines service with a private chaffeur are available at extra charges.

The Ritz-Carlton Jakarta, Mega Kuningan is a 45-minute drive from Soekarno-Hatta International Airport.
