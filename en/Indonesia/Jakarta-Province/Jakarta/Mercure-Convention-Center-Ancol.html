Description

Conveniently located in Jakarta’s Dreamland Ancol Park, Mercure Convention Center Ancol offers accommodation with free WiFi.

Featuring an outdoor pool, it has 2 dining options and free parking. Mercure Convention Center Ancol is situated between the Seaworld Oceanarium and Fantasy World.

It is a 20-minute drive from the Soekarno Hatta International Airport. Simply furnished and decorated in warm colours, rooms at Mercure Ancol are all air-conditioned.

In-room comforts include satellite TV, minibar and an attached bathroom with a bathtub, a shower, and a hairdryer.

For leisure, get an active workout at the tennis courts or jogging track. Alternatively, guests can explore the hotel’s surroundings with the bike rental and tour desk available.

The accommodation offers a children's playground. You can play table tennis and billiards at Mercure Convention Center Ancol, and the area is popular for cycling.

Boasting an open-air terrace with ocean views, Sunda Kelapa Coffee Shop serves Asian and international dishes.

Live entertainment accompany after-meal drinks at the 24-hour Nelayan Bar. Staff speak English and Indonesian at the 24-hour front desk.

Mangga Dua Square is 1. 9 km from the hotel, while Jakarta International Expo - JIExpo is 3.

2 km away. The nearest airport is Halim Perdanakusuma Airport, 17 km from Mercure Convention Center Ancol.
