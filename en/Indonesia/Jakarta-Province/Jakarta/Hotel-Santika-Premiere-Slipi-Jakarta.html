Description

Santika Premiere offers air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi in West Jakarta’s business district.

Featuring fitness facilities and an outdoor pool, it is a 10-minute drive from Grand Indonesia Shopping Town.

Hotel Santika Premiere Jakarta is a 10-minute drive from the shopping options at Wholesale Center Tanah Abang.

Offering free parking, it is a 20-minute drive from Soekarno Hatta International Airport.

Fitted with dark wood furnishings, all rooms at Santika Premiere have a large flat-screen TV and a safety deposit box.

Offering spacious layouts, they have a sofa bed, a safe and tea/coffee making amenities.

After an active game at the tennis courts, guests can relax with the sauna facilities.

For convenience, staff can provide business, tour and car hire services. Specialising in Indonesian cuisine, The Harmony restaurant also offers a spread of Western and Asian dishes.

Yan Palace Restaurant serves a selection of Chinese delicacies. Meals can also be enjoyed in rooms.
