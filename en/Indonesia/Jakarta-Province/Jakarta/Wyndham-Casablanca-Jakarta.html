Description

The Wyndham Casablanca Jakarta offers 5-star luxury in Jakarta’s Golden Triangle, with a gym, spa and landscaped pool.

Featuring rooms with flat-screen TVs, it has free parking and an award-winning restaurant.

Kota Kasablanka Mall is within a 5-minute walk away. The hotel is 9 km from Gelora Bung Karno's Main Gate.

Featuring views of the city or outdoor pool, spacious air-conditioned rooms have classic wood furnishings and comfortable sofas.

They are fitted with cable TV and a safe. Private bathrooms have hot/cold showers and hairdryers.

For leisure, The Wyndham Casablanca Jakarta offers a well-equipped fitness centre and relaxing massages at the spa.

Travelling is made easy with the car hire and ticket services provided. It also has a business and beauty shop.

Guests can enjoy dining at Riva, which features international food made from fresh ingredients.

Located in the business district, The Wyndham Casablanca Jakarta is easily accessible from the Plaza Semanggi and Ambassador shopping malls.

Pasar Festival Kuningan Sport and Entertainment is an 8-minute walk away and Kuningan City is a 10-minute walk from the property.
