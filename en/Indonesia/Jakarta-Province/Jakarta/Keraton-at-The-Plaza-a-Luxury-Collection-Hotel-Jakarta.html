Description

The hotel has direct access to Plaza Indonesia and is a 3-minute walk to Grand Indonesia shopping malls. Keraton at The Plaza is a 10-minute drive to Monas (National Museum). Soekarno-Hatta International Airport is 19 miles away.

The non-smoking and soundproof rooms have city views. Modern amenities include a flat-screen cable TV, an iPod dock and bathroom with a separate bathtub and rain shower. A bathrobe, slippers, a hairdryer, and free bottled mineral water are provided.

Enjoy a work-out at the hotel’s fitness center or use the hotel's business center. Guests can expect 24-hour concierge service. Airport shuttle, laundry, and dry cleaning services are available for an extra fee. The hotel also provides complimentary early check-in and late check-out.

Dine on Indonesian cuisine at Bengawan Restaurant and have a drink afterwards at the Keraton Lounge.
