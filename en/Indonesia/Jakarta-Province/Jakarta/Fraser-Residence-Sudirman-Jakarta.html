Description

Located 3 km from Plaza Indonesia shopping centre, Fraser Residence Sudirman Jakarta features an outdoor pool and a fitness centre.

A kids' pool and playground are also available. The luxury apartments have floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the city.

The modern and stylishly designed apartments have spacious living space, a separate dining area and a well-equipped kitchen.

Each apartment is appointed with a flat-screen satellite TV, an iPod docking station and a washing machine.

The private bathroom comes with bath/shower facilities and free toiletries. Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant, serving a variety of local and western dishes.

Alternative dining options are plenty around the property and within 5 minutes' walk.

Friendly staffs at the property can assist you with laundry, dry cleaning and ironing services.

Fraser Residence Sudirman Jakarta is 5 minutes 'walk from Setiabudi Astra MRT and 15 minutes' drive away from Grand Indonesia and Plaza Senayan.

The nearest airport is Soekarno-Hatta International Airport, a 60 minutes' drive from the property.
