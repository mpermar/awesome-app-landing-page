Description

A 10-minute drive from Araya Golf Course, HARRIS Hotel & Conventions Malang boasts 3 outdoor pools, a fitness centre and a restaurant.

Free WiFi access is available throughout the building. Rooms are fully air-conditioned and they come furnished with a coffee/tea maker, a flat-screen satellite TV and a sofa.

A hairdryer, slippers and a set of toiletries are included in the attached bathroom.

HARRIS Hotel & Conventions Malang is a 15-minute drive from Malang Town Square and a 20-minute drive from Abdul Rachman Saleh Airport.

Batu Town is 30 minutes' drive away. Airport shuttle service is offered for a fee.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can assist with safety deposit box usage and laundry requests, while day trips and car rental can be arranged at the hotel’s tour desk.

Guests can indulge in pampering treatments at the spa. Indonesian and international menu are on offer at HARRIS Café.
