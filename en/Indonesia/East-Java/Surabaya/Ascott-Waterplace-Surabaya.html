Description

Welcoming guests with an outdoor swimming pool, Ascott Waterplace Surabaya is located right in front of Pakuwon Trade Center which houses convention halls, department stores and various dining outlets.

This apartment also features a gym that guests can access for free. The apartment is also a 10-minute drive from Ciputra World Surabaya.

The fully furnished apartments are all air conditioned and has a living room with sofa, a flat-screen TV and a DVD player.

The bedroom is fitted with a personal safe, a wardrobe and a desk. The fully equipped kitchen comes with a refrigerator, electric kettle and microwave.

The en suite bathroom has a bathtub, hot/cold shower and hairdryer. Guests enjoy city view.

The Dining Room serves a selection of Continental dishes. The local mini-market can be found within a 5-minute walk.

The staff at Ascott Waterplace Surabaya will be happy to assist with laundry, dry cleaning and ironing services.

Airport transfer can be arranged at an additional charge. Other facilities offered include in-room dining service, barbecue facilities and daily housekeeping.

Guests who drive enjoy free parking privileges. The apartment is a 5-minute drive from Ciputra Golf Club & Hotel, a 20-minute drive from Plaza Tunjungan and a 30-minute drive from Surabaya Zoo.
