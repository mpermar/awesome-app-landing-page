Description

A 10-minute drive from Adi Sucipto Airport, Royal Ambarrukmo Yogyakarta offers modern and spacious rooms with free WiFi in the heart of Yogyakarta.

Boasting an outdoor pool and spa, the hotel shares the same grounds as Ambarrukmo Museum, a famous shopping area.

Rooms at the the Royal Ambarrukmo come with wood furnishings and a private balcony.

A flat-screen TV with cable channels, minibar and safety deposit box are included.

Laundry and dry cleaning services are available. Other facilities at the property include a fitness centre, a sauna and a children's playground.

The staff at the 24-hour front desk can assist with valet parking and luggage storage.

The Royal Restaurant offers a taste of traditional Indonesian and international cuisines, while cocktails can be enjoyed at Punika Deli or The Lobby Lounge.

The Royal Ambarrukmo Yogyakarta is a 20-minute drive from Malioboro and the Sultan Palace Residence.
