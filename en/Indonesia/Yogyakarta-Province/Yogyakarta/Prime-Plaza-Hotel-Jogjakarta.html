Description

A 15-minute drive from Adisucipto Airport, Prime Plaza Hotel Jogjakarta is located in the city’s university district.

It features a fitness centre, swimming pool and free Wi-Fi access in rooms and public areas.

A kids pool and a baby pool are also available. Combining traditional Javanese decor with modern minimalist design, rooms at Prime Plaza Hotel Jogjakarta come with large windows and hardwood furnishing.

Each room is equipped with a cable TV and safety deposit box. Free toiletries are provided in the en suite bathrooms.

Recreation facilities include a tennis court. Enjoying a massage is also a good relaxation choice.

The hotel provides bicycle and car rental services. Laundry and dry cleaning services are available upon request.

Srikaton Restaurant serves traditional Indonesian cuisine, while Colombo Pool Terrace offers a barbecue dinner by the pool.

For light snacks and drinks, guests can visit the Lobby Lounge. Prime Plaza Hotel Jogjakarta is a 15-minute drive from Tugu Railway Station and around 1 hour drive from Borobudur Temple.
