Description

Located 7 km from Villavicencio, Hotel Boutique Duranta offers a spa and wellness centre and an outdoor pool with a swim-up bar and sun loungers.

Free Wi-Fi is available throughout. Rooms at the Hotel Boutique Duranta feature stylish contemporary décor, air conditioning and flat-screen TVs with cable channels.

The private bathrooms include a shower. A variety of dining options can be found in Villavicencio city centre.

Unicentro and La Sabana shopping centres are both within 10 km. Guests can also relax in the shared lounge and games room.

Hotel Boutique Duranta offers free on-site parking. Marsella Park is 2 km away, while Merecure Eco-Park and La Vanguardia Airport are within a 20-minute drive.
