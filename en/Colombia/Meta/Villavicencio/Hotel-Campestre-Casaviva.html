Description

Offering an outdoor pool and a restaurant, Hotel Campestre Casaviva is located in Villavicencio.

Just a 15-minute drive from the city centre, it features an on-site river where guests can swim.

Each room includes free WiFi, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with free toiletries.

Most overlook the property’s gardens. At Hotel Campestre Casaviva you will find a 24-hour front desk and barbecue facilities.

Other facilities offered at the property include a shared lounge, a tour desk and a children's playground.

Other activities like on-site including hiking and volleyball are possible. Vanguaria Airport can be reached in 20 minutes by car.
