Description

Located 10 minutes’ drive from Villavicencio, Hotel Rosalinda Campestre del Llano features an outdoor pool and an on-site restaurant.

The property has free WiFi access available. The property features air-conditioned rooms that include a coffee maker, free toiletries, an outdoor seating area and views of the garden and pool area.

The full bathrooms may feature either a bath or a shower. Hotel Rosalinda Campestre del Llano offers its guests bicycles on loan, a games room and friendly staff that can help with important information about interesting sightseeing and touristic sports in the area.

Free parking is featured on site. Popular activities in the vicinity include hiking and cycling.
