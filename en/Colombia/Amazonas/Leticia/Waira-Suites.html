Description

Featuring two outdoor swimming pools and a garden, this boutique hotel offers a restaurant.

Acuarios Shopping Centre is 5 blocks away. The rooms at the Waira Suites feature LCD cable TV, a minibar, and a private bathroom.

Room service is provided. Guests can enjoy activities in the area such as fishing, canoeing, hiking, and cycling.

The hotel has a 24-hour front desk service and laundry services are available upon request.

Waira Suites is 2 blocks from Orellana Park, and Alfredo Vazquez Cobo Airport is a 10-minute drive away.
