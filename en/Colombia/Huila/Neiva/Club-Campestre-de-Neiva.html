Description

All guest rooms features air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fridge, a coffee machine, a bath and a desk. The hotel provides some accommodations with garden views, and each room has a balcony. At Club Campestre de Neiva, every room comes with a seating area.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy an American breakfast. At Club Campestre de Neiva you'll find a restaurant serving American, Argentinian and caribbean cuisine. Vegetarian and vegan options can also be requested.

The hotel offers a sauna. A number of activities can be enjoyed on site or nearby, including cycling and bowling alley.

Guests have access to the on-site business center where they can check the newspapers.

The nearest airport is Benito Salas Airport, 8 miles from Club Campestre de Neiva.
