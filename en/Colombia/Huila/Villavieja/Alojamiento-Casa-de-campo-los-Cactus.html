Description

Accommodation Country house Los Cactus features a restaurant, bar, a shared lounge and garden in Villavieja.

Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a children's playground.

Private parking can be arranged at an extra charge. Each room is fitted with a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries.

Guests at the hotel can enjoy a la carte breakfast. Accommodation Country house Los Cactus offers a barbecue.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike hire is available at the accommodation.

Neiva is 48 km from Alojamiento Casa de campo los Cactus. The nearest airport is Benito Salas Airport, 47 km from the hotel.
