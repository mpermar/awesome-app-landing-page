Description

Hostal La Casona is located in the heart of San Gil. Free WiFi access is available.

Gallineral Park is just 550 metres away from the hostel. Each room features a fan and access to 5 shared bathrooms.

There are also rooms with a private bathroom. A cable TV is included in most rooms.

A 24-hour front desk, tour desk, and shared kitchen and lounge are available at La Casona Hostal.

A garden is offered for guests to enjoy. The Fonce River is 2 minutes' walk from the property, and Principal Park is 2 minutes' walk away from Hostal Casona.
