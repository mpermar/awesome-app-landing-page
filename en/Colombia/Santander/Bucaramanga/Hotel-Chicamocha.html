Description

Hotel Chicamocha offers contemporary rooms with free WiFi in downtown Bucaramanga, 30-minutes drive from Palonegro Airport.

Facilities include a gym, massage service and discounted use of Ruitoque Golf Club.

The bright rooms at Hotel Chicamocha are decorated with lime green bedding and white tiled floors.

All are equipped with queen-size beds, an LCD TV and a seating area. Some have a dining area.

The buffet-style restaurant at Hotel Chicamocha serves Italian specialties and the bar offers exotic cocktails.

A daily breakfast with fresh fruits, natural juices and pastries is available. Guests at Hotel Chicamocha can contact the 24-hour reception desk to arrange an airport shuttle service.
