Description

Featuring a garden with an outdoor pool, a spa and a restaurant, Hotel Campestre El Santuario offers rooms with free Wi-Fi and fans in Barichara.

The town centre is 5 km away. Free parking is provided. Decorated with tiled floors and contemporary paintings, rooms at Hotel Campestre El Santuario are fitted with TV sets.

Some of them have plasma TVs and afford pool views. International dishes can be ordered at the restaurant, whereas drinks and snacks from the bar can be enjoyed in the garden.

Guests can benefit from beauty treatments at the spa, which features Turkish and steam baths.

They can also unwind by the pool or visit the shrine. San Gil is 15 km away. Hotel Campestre El Santuario is 13 km from Guane and 56 km from Chicamocha National Park.
