Description

Set in Pereira, 3. 5 km from Parque Ukumari, El Lugar Boutique Hotel offers accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a bar and a shared lounge.

5 km from Consota Recreational Park and 10 km from Expofuturo Convention Center, the property provides a garden and a terrace.

The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.

The units at the hotel feature air conditioning, a TV with satellite channels, DVD Player, and a private bathroom with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

The rooms have a desk. El Lugar Boutique Hotel offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

You can play darts at the accommodation. A business centre and vending machines with drinks and snacks are available on site at El Lugar Boutique Hotel.

Pereira's Olympic Village is 10 km from the hotel, while Hernan Ramirez Villegas Stadium is 11 km away.

The nearest airport is Matecaña International, 17 km from El Lugar Boutique Hotel, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
