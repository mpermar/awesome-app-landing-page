Description

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool, a garden and a restaurant, Hotel Pueblito Cafetero offers free Wi-Fi and American breakfast.

Pereira Bus Station is 300 metres away. The rooms in Hotel Pueblito Cafetero feature private bathrooms and a balcony.

Room service is provided. The restaurant in Pueblito Cafetero serves regional and international dishes.

Guests can order snacks at the bar or relax in the Turkish bath. There is also a football court.

Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas. Cartago City is a 15-minute drive away and Parque del Café is a 1-hour drive away.

Matecaña zoo and airport are 10 km away. Free private parking is possible on site.
