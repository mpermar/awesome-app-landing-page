Description

Featuring a restaurant and a garden with a swimming pool and a football pitch, San Antonio del Cerro offers rooms and bungalows with free Wi-Fi and air conditioning in Pereira.

The commercial area is 10 km away. Bright and spacious, room at San Antonio del Cerro are fitted with minibars and satellite TVs.

All of them have private bathrooms. Guests can unwind on the sun loungers by the pool, or engage in football and volleyball matches.

There is a games room, and a children’s playground is featured. Horseback riding activities can be booked.
