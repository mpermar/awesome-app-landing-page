Description

Set 8 km from Pereira, Hotel Valle De Las Camelias welcomes guests with a restaurant and bar.

BioPark Ukumari is just a 15-minutes walk away. Every room at this guest house is air conditioned and is fitted with a flat-screen TV.

All rooms are fitted with a private bathroom. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property.

Hotel Valle De Las Camelias offers an outdoor soccer field, a basketball court, a swimming pool and a meeting room.

Car hire is available. Hernan Ramirez Villegas Stadium is 8 km from hostal Valle De Las Camelias, while Parque Panaca is 48 km away.

The nearest airport is Matecaa International Airport, 10 km from hostal valle de las camelias.
