Description

Offering an outdoor swimming pool and a restaurant, Ecohotel La Casona is located in Pereira.

Free WiFi access is available, along with private parking. Each room here will provide you with a minibar, a fan and a view of the property's garden.

At Ecohotel La Casona you will find a communal sauna and a hot tub. Other facilities offered at the property include a shared lounge, a games room and a tour desk.

The hotel is 3. 9 km from Pereira's Botanical Garden, 4 km from Technological University of Pereira and 5.

2 km from Pereira's Art Museum. Matecaña International Airport can be reached after a 25-minute drive.
