Description

Las Americas Casa de Playa is a tropical beachfront complex located in northern Cartagena.

The hotel has 2 swimming pools, a mini-golf course, a spa and an entertainment center for kids.

Guests can enjoy the two on site bars: La Isla and El Chicho. Rooms at the Hotel Las Americas Casa de Playa are very spacious with private balconies.

They are all equipped with free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen satellite TVs, air conditioning and fans.

The Hotel has 2 restaurants, Mesón de Don Cristobal, that serves local and international food while Fogon del Navegante serves grilled meats and sea food with fresh salads.

A 24-hour desk and free parking are available on site. Hotel Las Americas Casa de Playa is located less than 5 km from Cartagena’s Old Town and a 20-minute drive from the bustling Boca Grande District.
