Description

Las Americas Torre del Mar is a luxurious beach complex boasting a beachfront location in north Cartagena upon La Boquilla Beach.

It features 5 swimming pools, spa services, fitness centre and mini- golf facilities.

Coffee and clothing stores are available at the property. Free WiFi is provided throughout the property.

Guests can enjoy the two on site bars: Sport and Infinito. Rooms at Las Americas Torre del Mar have air conditioning, mini bar, flat-screen TV and balconies with lake views, sea views or city views.

Guests can enjoy a buffet breakfast with tropical fruits, natural juices in the private beach area, or a poolside cocktail with a view of Cartagena.
