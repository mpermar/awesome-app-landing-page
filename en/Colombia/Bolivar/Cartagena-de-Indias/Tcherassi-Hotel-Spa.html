Description

Set in refurbished colonial mansion only a 5-minute walk from Santo Domingo and San Diego squares, Tcherassi Hotel + Spa features a a restaurant on the ground floor and a terrace swimming pool.

It offers rooms with free WiFi and garden views. Certain rooms at Tcherassi Hotel + Spa boast balconies overlooking the inner vertical garden.

All of them have plasma TVs and spacious wardrobes. Some rooms feature a working desk and a high-end coffee maker.

Breakfast including fruits, bread, eggs, coffee, milk and tea is served daily upon request.

Café Tcherassi offers gourmet street food. A welcome drink is offered to newcomers.

Guests can benefit from spa facilities and wellness treatments, including massage sessions.

They can also relax by the pool or on the terrace where the pool is and a bar is set.

The terrace restaurant, Terraza Tcherassi, offers Mediterranean and oriental dishes.

The 24-hour front desk can secure airport shuttles. Tcherassi Hotel + Spa is a 10-minute drive from Rafael Nuñez Airport and a 10-minute walk from Caribe Plaza Shopping Mall.
