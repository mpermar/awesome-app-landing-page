Description

At Charleston Santa Teresa guests can book snorkeling and scuba diving trips. Tours of the bay can also be arranged and San Pedro Claver Church is a 5-minute walk.

Chic rooms come with air conditioning, minibars, cable TV and full bathrooms with bathtubs, slippers, free toiletries and bathrobes.

Charleston’s on-site restaurant serves international cuisine and a variety of pizzas.

Shuttles to Rafael Nuñez Airport, 4.6 miles away, can be arranged for a fee.

Centro is a great choice for travelers interested in architecture, food and history.
