Description

Wachakyta Ecohostel features a restaurant, bar, a garden and private beach area in Calabazo.

Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a terrace. The accommodation provides nightclub and free WiFi.

Guests at the inn can enjoy a à la carte breakfast. You can play table tennis at Wachakyta Ecohostel, and the area is popular for hiking.

Santa Marta is 26 km from the accommodation, while Taganga is 29 km away. The nearest airport is Simón Bolívar International Airport, 37 km from Wachakyta Ecohostel.
