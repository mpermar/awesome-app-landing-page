Description

Located 100 metres from the sea, this beach resort offers onsite marine activities, a fitness centre, and an outdoor swimming pool.

Some rooms have panoramic sea views and free Wi-Fi access. All rooms in Irotama XXI feature a kitchenette, private bathroom, and satellite TV.

Newspapers are delivered daily to each room. Guests Irotama XXI can arrange golf lessons at the practice court or engage in a tennis match.

Activities for children are provided, as well as massage sessions. Simon Bolivar Airport in Santa Marta is 2 km away.
