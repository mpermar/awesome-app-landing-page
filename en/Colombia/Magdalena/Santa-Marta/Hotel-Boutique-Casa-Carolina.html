Description

Offering an outdoor swimming pool, rooftop hot tubs and a spa, Hotel Boutique Casa Carolina is located next to San Francisco Monastery in Santa Marta's historic center, and 200 metres from Santa Marta beach and Simón Bolívar Square.

The air-conditioned rooms, suites and apartments offer flat-screen satellite TVs, free Wi-Fi and bluetooth sound systems.

They also include a minibar and fridge, a safety deposit box, a desk and a private bathrooms with a hairdryer, bathrobe, hot shower and free toiletries.

All rooms have a lounge area with a sofa bed and some have a balcony or terrace. Guests can find supermarkets and convenience stores within 200 metres.

The nearest restaurants are just 300 metres away, and the property's on-site restaurant, Eli's is open all day.

Other facilities offered at Casa Carolina include luggage storage, laundry service and concierge service.

Transportation and tours are also offeredd, as well as private tours to Parque Tayrona inthe property's own speedboat.

Santa Marta International Airport is a 20-minute drive away. Secure public parking is available next to the hotel.
