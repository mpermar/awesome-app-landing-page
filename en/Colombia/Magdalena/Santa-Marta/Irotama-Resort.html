Description

Located 100 metres from the beach, Irotama Resort offers on-site marine activities, 3 outdoor swimming pools and a spa and fitness centre.

WiFi is available. Activities for children are organised. There are complimentary airport-pick ups.

Bungalows at Irotama Resort feature a private bathroom, satellite TV, and minibar.

Some room types offer sea views. Room service is provided. Guests at Irotama can arrange golf practices and tennis matches.

Dining is available at one of the resort's 9 restaurants and guests can enjoy a drink on one of the 3 bars.

Additionally, massage sessions and car rentals can also be arranged upon request.

Simon Bolivar Airport is 2 km away. Free private parking is possible on site. Every guests receives a free souvenir upon arrival.
