Description

This hotel offers an outdoor swimming pool, a spa and free Wi-Fi. It is located at Sochagota Lake and just 3 km from the centre of Paipa.

Rooms at Estelar Paipa Hotel & Spa Y Centro De Convenciones feature large windows, providing views of the lake and surrounding forests and hills.

They are equipped with a TV, minibar and bathrobes. Wellness facilities include a hydromassage tub and a sauna.

A variety of massage treatments are also available. Guests can work out in the gym.

Hotel Estelar Paipa invites guests for breakfast and dinner in Terra restaurant. During the day, drinks and refreshments can be ordered at Sol restaurant and, on weekends, at Agua restaurant.

It is situated just 3 km from the airport which serves charter flights and 6 km from the bus terminal.
