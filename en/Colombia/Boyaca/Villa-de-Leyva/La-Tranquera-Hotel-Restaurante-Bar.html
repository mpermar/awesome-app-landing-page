Description

Offering a restaurant, Hotel La Tranquera is located in Villa de Leyva, 1 km from the main square.

It features free daily American breakfast, plus on-site horse shows and live music.

Each room here features a cable TV, a wardrobe and a private bathroom with a shower and toilet.

Free WiFi access is available throughout the property. At Hotel La Tranquera you will find a 24-hour front desk, a garden and a terrace.

Other facilities offered at the property include a shared lounge, a games room and a tour desk that can arrange a range of affordable packages.

El Dorado International Airport in the capital city of Bogota is a 3-hour drive away.
