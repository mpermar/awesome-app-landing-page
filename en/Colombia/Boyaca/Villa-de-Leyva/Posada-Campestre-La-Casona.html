Description

Located 2 km from the main square and 1 km from the bus station, Posada La Casona offers free Wi-Fi and breakfast in Villa de Leyva.

A garden, a games room, a chimney room and a minigolf course are featured on site.

Providing a tranquil environment, the rooms in Posada La Casona feature private bathrooms and minibars.

Guests at Posada La Casona are provided free private on-site parking. Posada La Casona is 3 km from the Paleontological museum, from the National university and from the Dinosaur park.
