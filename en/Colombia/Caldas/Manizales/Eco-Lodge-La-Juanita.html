Description

Offering Spanish style galleries with hammocks and views of the hills, La Juanita has rooms with free Wi-Fi.

Downtown Manizales is a 10-minute drive. Private parking is free. Guests staying at Eco Hotel La Juanita can enjoy a full breakfast with Colombian coffee, corn flatbreads, eggs and chocolate.

There is an on-site restaurant and a common kitchen. La Juanita’s comfortable rooms are equipped with cable TV and private bathrooms with showers.

Suites include bathtubs and garden views. Los Nevados Natural Park is a 90-minute drive the area’s water park is a 35-minute drive.
