Description

Set in Rozo, 27 km from Cali, Hotel Campestre Tardes Caleñas boasts an outdoor pool.

There is a water park on site and guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Free private parking is available on site.

Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and is equipped with a flat-screen TV.

Some units include a seating area for your convenience. Views of the lake, pool or garden are featured in certain rooms.

Each room is fitted with a private bathroom equipped with a shower. Extras include free toiletries.

Hotel Campestre Tardes Caleñas features free WiFi in the communal areas. There is a business centre at the property.

You can play table tennis and billiards at the hotel. Palmira is 15 km from Hotel Campestre Tardes Caleñas, while La Cumbre is 20 km away.

The nearest airport is Alfonso Bonilla Aragón International Airport, 10 km from the property.
