Description

San Laurent is located in Cali, 6 km from Cañaveralejo's Bullring. The hotel has an outdoor pool, year-round outdoor pool and children's playground, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Free WiFi is featured throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Rooms come with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Certain units have a seating area for your convenience.

Some rooms feature views of the garden or city. Every room is fitted with a private bathroom fitted with a bath.

For your comfort, you will find slippers and free toiletries. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property.

Airport shuttlesare available. Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including golfing, cycling and hiking.

Pan-American Park is 8 km from San Laurent, while Saint's Peter Cathedral is 10 km away.

The nearest airport is Alfonso Bonilla Aragón International Airport, 26 km from San Laurent.
