Description

An outdoor swimming pool and spa facilities can be enjoyed in Cali. There is an on-site cinema room and a charming chess garden.

Wi-Fi is free and San Fernando Mio bus station is a 5-minute walk. Hotel San Fernando Real has chic rooms equipped with air conditioning, minibars and flat-screen TVs.

Some rooms feature spa baths and very large sun terraces. An American breakfast can be enjoyed daily for a fee and there is an on-site restaurant and nightclub which hosts regular karaoke nights.

Cañaveralejo bullfight arena is 4 km from Fernando Real and the town’s football and baseball courts are 3 minutes walking.

The hotel offers a handy currency exchange service and shuttles to Alfonso Bonilla Airport, 25 km away, can be arranged.
