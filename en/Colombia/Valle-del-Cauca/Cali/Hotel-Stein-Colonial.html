Description

Located 500 metres from Centenario shopping centre, Hotel Stein Colonial features an outdoor swimming pool, a children’s playground, a restaurant, and a gym.

Free Wi-Fi and American breakfast are provided. The rooms in Hotel Stein Colonial have private bathrooms and satellite TV.

Room service is provided. Hotel Stein Colonial is a colonial building with a solarium and a garden.

The 24-hour front desk can arrange laundry services. Cali city centre is 1 km away and the Zoo is 10 km away.

Alfonso Bonilla Aragon airport is a 1-hour drive away. Free private parking is possible on site.
