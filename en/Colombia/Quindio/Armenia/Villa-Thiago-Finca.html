Description

Situated in Armenia, 22 km from Quimbaya, Villa Thiago Finca features an outdoor swimming pool and free WiFi.

All units feature air conditioning and a flat-screen TV. There is a fully equipped private bathroom with shower and free toiletries.

Guests at the lodge can enjoy an American breakfast. Villa Thiago Finca offers a hot tub.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy hiking nearby, or make the most of the garden.

The nearest airport is El Edén International, 14 km from Villa Thiago Finca, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
