Description

Casa Hotel Linares features a seasonal outdoor swimming pool, fitness centre, a shared lounge and garden in Armenia.

Among the facilities at this property are a shared kitchen and room service, along with free WiFi throughout the property.

The hotel has family rooms. Guest rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area.

At Casa Hotel Linares each room has a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

The accommodation offers a Full English/Irish or American breakfast. Casa Hotel Linares offers a children's playground.

You can play billiards at the hotel, and the area is popular for hiking. Free private parking and a business centre are available, as well as a 24-hour front desk.

Pereira is 42 km from Casa Hotel Linares, while Quimbaya is 26 km away. The nearest airport is El Edén International, 19 km from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
