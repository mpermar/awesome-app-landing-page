Description

All units have a kitchen, a private bathroom and a patio with views of pool. A microwave and fridge are also featured, as well as a coffee machine.

A continental or à la carte breakfast is available each morning at the property. There's an on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of Grill/bbq, Cajun/creole and local dishes.

The country house has an outdoor pool.

Finca Hotel La Cuyabrita has a grill and a playground.

Santa Rosa de Cabal is 27 miles from the accommodation, while Pereira is 22 miles away. The nearest airport is El Edén International Airport, 4.3 miles from Finca Hotel La Cuyabrita.
