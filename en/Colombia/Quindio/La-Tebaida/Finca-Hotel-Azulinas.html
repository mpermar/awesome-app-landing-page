Description

Featuring a garden with a swimming pool and BBQ facilities, Finca Hotel Azulinas offers rooms with plasma TV and garden views in Armenia.

The historic centre is 15 km away. Decorated in lively colours, rooms at Hotel Azulinas feature large windows overlooking the garden.

All of them have seating areas and private bathrooms. Breakfast is served daily. Guests can enjoy barbecue sin garden, which features a shaded dining area.

Sun loungers are featured by the pool. There is also a game room. Finca Hotel Azulinas is 12 km from El Eden Airport and 5 km from the main road.
