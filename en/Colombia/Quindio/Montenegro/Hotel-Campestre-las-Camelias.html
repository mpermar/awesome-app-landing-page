Description

Hotel Campestre las Camelias is located in Montenegro. A swimming pool, restaurant and free WiFi access are available.

The property is 2 km from the Coffee Park, while Los Arrieros Park is 10 km from the hotel.

Each room here will provide you with a cable TV, air conditioning and a minibar. Private bathroom also comes with free toiletries.

At Hotel Campestre las Camelias you will find a tennis court and a fitness centre.

Other facilities offered at the property include a ticket service, a tour desk and luggage storage.

The property offers free parking. Hotel Campestre las Camelias is 16 km from Panaca.
