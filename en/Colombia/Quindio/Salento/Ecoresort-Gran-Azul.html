Description

Set in Salento, 60 km from Santa Rosa de Cabal, Ecoresort Gran Azul boasts views of the mountain and a a children's playground.

A terrace boasting mountain views is featured in all rooms. Every room is equipped with a private bathroom.

There is a spacious lounge, a dining room and a fireplace at the property. You can engage in various activities, such as horse riding, bird-watching and mountain biking.

The hotel also offers bike hire. Pereira is 40 km from Gran Azul Ecoresort. The nearest airport is Matecaña International Airport, 42 km from Gran Azul.
