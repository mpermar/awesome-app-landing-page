Description

Featuring a garden with a swimming pool, a restaurant, and a games room, El Caney Finca-Hotel offers rooms with fans and TV sets in a coffee farmhouse in Quimbaya.

Free parking is provided. The town hall is a 5-minute drive away. Decorated with parquet floors and practical furnishing, rooms at El Caney Finca-Hotel are very bright.

All of them have large windows, a TV and a private bathroom with hot water. Breakfast with regional ingredients is served daily.

Regional specialties dishes can be ordered at the property’s restaurant. Guests can unwind in the garden or by the pool.

They can also enjoy themselves playing table tennis and billiards in the games room.

Panaca Farm Theme Park is 4 km away, whereas the National Coffe Park is 15 minutes away by car.
