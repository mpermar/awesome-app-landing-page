Description

All rooms in Hotel De La Opera feature private bathrooms. Some rooms have a balcony with views of La Candelaria, and some units feature stunning downtown views.

The breakfast buffet offers cereals, cheese, ham, fresh fruit, juice and typical Colombian specialties. De La Opera Hotel features 2 restaurants on site and both restaurants serve Mediterranean specialties as well as local and international dishes.

The spa offers a variety of health and beauty treatments, such as chocotherapy and aromatherapy. It also features a sauna, hot tub and a Turkish steam bath. Please note the spa is closed for maintenance on Mondays.

Housed in a historic building with bright and spacious public areas, guests can enjoy a panoramic view of the rooftops and bell towers in El Mirador and La Scala. There is live music on Fridays and Saturdays.

Bolivar Square and the National Cathedral are only 2 blocks away. Several classic churches from the 16th century, Botero Museum and the Gold Museum are a short walk away.

Candelaria - Centro Historico is a great choice for travelers interested in museums, old-town exploration and history.
