Description

Offering a spa and wellness centre and à la carte restaurant, Hotel La Esperanza is located in Polinesia, just a 45-minute drive from North Bogotá.

Free WiFi access is available in this chalet. The accommodation will provide you with a tv.

Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with towels and linen. Some rooms have a mountain view.

Other facilities offered at the property include a games room, a children's playground and an ironing service.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including billiards.

This chalet is 72 km from El Dorado International Airport. The property offers free parking.
