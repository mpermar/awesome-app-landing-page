Description

At the guesthouse, every room comes with a closet and a flat-screen TV. Each room includes a private bathroom and free toiletries, and selected rooms come with a balcony.

Guests at El Secreto Campestre can enjoy a continental breakfast.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool. You can play ping pong at El Secreto Campestre.

Bogotá is 24 miles from the guesthouse. The nearest airport is El Dorado International Airport, 25 miles from El Secreto Campestre.
