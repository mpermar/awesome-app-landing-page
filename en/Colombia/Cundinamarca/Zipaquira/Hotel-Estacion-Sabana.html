Description

Located 10 minutes’ walk from Sal de Zipaquira cathedral, Hotel Estación Sabana offers free WiFi and breakfast in Zipaquirá.

El Dorado airport is a 1-hour drive and Gutavita lagoon is a 90-minute drive away.

Providing a tranquil environment, the rooms in Estación Sabana feature private bathrooms, TVs and minibars.

Room service is available and laundry services can be arranged upon request. Free parking is available.

Hotel Estación Sabana is a 2,5-hour drive from Villa de Leyva. A 12- hour parking is included in the rate.
