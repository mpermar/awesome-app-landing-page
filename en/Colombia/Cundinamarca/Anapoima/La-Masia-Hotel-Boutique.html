Description

Boasting an outdoor swimming pool surrounded by palm trees and sun loungers, La Masia has bright rooms in Anapoima, a 5-minute drive from the downtown area.

Piscilago Water Park is a 45-minute drive. La Masia Hotel Boutique has comfortable rooms with terraces and garden views.

They are equipped with private bathrooms with showers and all of them have fans. Guests can enjoy regional and Spanish cuisine at the restaurant.

La Masia features a spacious outdoor dining area. Room service is available. Girardot is 47 km away and private parking is free.
