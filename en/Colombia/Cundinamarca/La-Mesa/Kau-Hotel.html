Description

Kau Hotel has a restaurant, outdoor swimming pool, a bar and shared lounge in La Mesa.

Boasting a kids' club, this property also provides guests with a children's playground.

The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, a shuttle service, room service and free WiFi throughout the property.

At the hotel, all rooms are equipped with a terrace. At Kau Hotel rooms are fitted with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

A buffet breakfast is available each morning at the accommodation. Kau Hotel offers a hot tub.

You can play table tennis at the hotel, and bike hire is available. Anapoima is 17 km from Kau Hotel.

The nearest airport is El Dorado International Airport, 65 km from the accommodation.
