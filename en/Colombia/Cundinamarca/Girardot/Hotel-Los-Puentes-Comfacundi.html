Description

Offering a large outdoor swimming pool with slides and a spa and wellness centre, Hotel los Puentes Comfacundi is located in Girardot, 500 metres from Magdalena River.

The rooms offer colourful décor, flat-screen cable TV, air conditioning and a minibar.

Private bathrooms also come with a shower. At Hotel los Puentes Comfacundi you will find a restaurant and a fitness centre.

Other facilities offered include a games room, a laundry service and a garden. Santiago Vila Airport is 3 km away and the Financial District is 500 metres away.
