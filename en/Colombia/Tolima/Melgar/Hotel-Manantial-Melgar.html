Description

Centrally located in Melgar, 4 blocks from the Main Square, Hotel Manantial Melgar offers modern rooms plus 2 adult pools, bar and spa.

Free WiFi and parking are provided. Manatial Melgar features spacious suites all equipped with air conditioning, cable TV and minibar.

Guests can also make use of room service and a 24-hour front desk. In its bright and airy building, guests can enjoy an American breakfast.

A variety of refreshments is available at bar. For relaxation, Manantial Melgar features a spa offering massages and body treatments, plus a sauna unit.

Also provided are a TV room and a game area where guests can play billiards and table tennis.

Sumapaz River and Cafam Sports Centre are only a 5-minute drive from Hotel Manantial Melgar.
