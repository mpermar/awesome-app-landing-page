Description

Featuring a garden with a swimming pool, and a restaurant, Hotel Melgar Villa Valeria offers rooms with free Wi-Fi and cable TV in Melgar.

Free parking is provided. Cafam melgar is 900 metres away. Decorated with contemporary paintings, rooms at Hotel Melgar Villa Valeria are fitted with air conditioning and fans.

All of them have private bathrooms. Breakfast is served daily. International dishes can be ordered at the property’s restaurant.

Guests can enjoy themselves playing table tennis in the games room, or unwind by the pool.

Hotel Melgar Villa Valeria is 10 10 from Piscilago and 30 km from Girardot. Melgar’s Main Square is 300 metres away.
