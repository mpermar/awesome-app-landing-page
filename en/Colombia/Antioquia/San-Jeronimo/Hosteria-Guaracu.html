Description

Featuring a garden, an outdoor swimming pool, and a restaurant, Hosteria Guaracú offers free Wi-Fi.

San Jerónimo main square is a 5-minute drive. Providing a tranquil environment, the rooms in Guaracú feature private bathrooms, air conditioning and flat-screen cable TVs.

Guests at Hosteria Guaracú are provided room service and laundry services can be arranged upon request.

Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas. The on-site restaurant serves creole food and international dishes.

Hosteria Guaracú is 35 km from Medellin. Free public parking is possible on site.
