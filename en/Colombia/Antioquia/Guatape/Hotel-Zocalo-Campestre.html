Description

Located in Guatapé, 79 km from Medellín, Hotel Zocalo Campestre boasts a restaurant and free WiFi.

Guests can enjoy the on-site bar. The property offers rooms in the main house and bungalows.

Every unit comes with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Views of the mountain, lake or garden are featured in certain units.

All the units come with a private bathroom fitted with a bath or shower. There is a shared lounge at the property.

You can play billiards at the japanese-style business hotel. The japanese-style business hotel also offers bike hire.

Barbosa is 29 km from Hotel Zocalo Campestre, while Itagüí is 48 km away. José María Córdova International Airport is 50 km from the property.
