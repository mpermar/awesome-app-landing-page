Description

Situated in Santa Fe de Antioquia, 3. 5 km from Kanaloa Water Park, Hotel Caseron del Parque features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre.

With a bar, the property also has a shared lounge, as well as a terrace. The accommodation provides a hot tub, free WiFi and family rooms.

All rooms in the hotel are fitted with a TV. At Hotel Caseron del Parque, the rooms are fitted with air conditioning and a private bathroom.
