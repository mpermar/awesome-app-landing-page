Description

Featuring a tropical garden with a swimming pool, this colonial-style hotel offers spacious and air-conditioned rooms in the historical center of Sanda Fe de Antioquia.

The hotel has a spa and a massage room. With free Wi-Fi, all uniquely decorated rooms at Mariscal Robledo come with air conditioning, a flat-screen cable TV and private bathroom.

The rooms are either modern in style or traditional, with beamed ceilings and wooden panels.

Local and international dishes are served in the restaurant. There is a terrace with views of the village and surrounding mountains.

Guests can order drinks at the bar at an extra charge. Billiards and ping pong are available for sporty guests.

It is also possible to visit the TV lounge next to the reception to catch up on the latest news.

With free on-site parking, guests have easy access to Medellin City, a 70-minute drive away, Medellin's North Bus Terminal is within a 1.
