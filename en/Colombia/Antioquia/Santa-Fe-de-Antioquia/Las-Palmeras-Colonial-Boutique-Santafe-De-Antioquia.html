Description

Situated in Santa Fe de Antioquia, 3. 4 km from Kanaloa Water Park, Las Palmeras Colonial Boutique - Santafe De Antioquia features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, free bikes and an outdoor swimming pool.

Among the facilities at this property are a 24-hour front desk and room service, along with free WiFi throughout the property.

A children's playground, an in-house bar and shared lounge are available. All rooms at the hotel are fitted with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and a shower.

Guest rooms at Las Palmeras Colonial Boutique - Santafe De Antioquia have air conditioning and a wardrobe.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy an American breakfast. At Las Palmeras Colonial Boutique - Santafe De Antioquia guests are welcome to take advantage of a hot tub and a sauna.

You can play billiards and table tennis at the hotel, and the area is popular for cycling.

The nearest airport is Olaya Herrera Airport, 61 km from Las Palmeras Colonial Boutique - Santafe De Antioquia.
