Description

Hotel Las Ardillas’s guests can choose to relax in the outdoor swimming pool, spa, Turkish bath and garden, in Santa Fe de Antioquia.

There is free Wi-Fi. Puente de Occidente bridge is 7 km from the hotel. The rooms have tropical-coloured cushions and bedspreads.

Some are in thatched wooden huts and have mosquito nets covering the beds. Each has a private bathroom, cable TV and air conditioning.

Guests can keep fit using the hotel’s gym and soccer, volleyball and basketball courts.

Hotel Las Ardillas features a bar and an onsite restaurant serving regional food.

The hotel also offers 24-hour front desk assistance and onsite tour desk services.

Bicycle rental is available. Both the Juan Del Corral Museum and the Religious Art Museum are a 5-minute drive away.

Enrique Olaya Herrera Airport is reached by car in 1 hour. Free private parking is possible on site at Hotel Las Ardillas, without reservation.
