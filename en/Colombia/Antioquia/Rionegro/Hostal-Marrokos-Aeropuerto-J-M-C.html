Description

Set amidst nature Hostal Marrokos, offers accommodation in Via a Guarne, 5 minutes away from José María Córdova International Airport.

Free private parking is available on site and an airport shuttle is available for a surcharg.

Rooms feature independent entrances, bed linen and towels in their private bathrooms with hot shower.

Units include views of the mountains or gardens. The property offers breakfast included and WiFi Access and satellite TV are available.

Medellín is 45 km from Hostal Marrokos, while Guatapé is 31 km from the property.
