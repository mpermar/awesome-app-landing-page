Description

The contemporary suites at Hotel Las Lomas have tasteful brown and beige décor with soft carpets. All include air conditioning and a minibar, while some have comfortable sitting areas. Free Wi-Fi is available.

Guests can enjoy a game of soccer, basketball or volleyball. They can then relax in the hot tub in the furnished terrace, or walk through the gardens and relax by the impressive stone water fountain.

Las Lomas has 3 restaurants serving traditional Colombian cuisine, international fine-dining and seafood specialties. The stylish Teka Bar serves classic cocktails, and a breakfast buffet is available.

Hotel Las Lomas is a 30-minute drive from downtown Medellin, and a 15-minute drive from the tourist Rio Negro Town. Free parking is available.
