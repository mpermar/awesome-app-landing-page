Description

At the hotel, the rooms include a desk. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a shower, while selected rooms include a seating area. At Hotel Aguatur, the rooms have a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

Hotel Aguatur has a playground.

Medellín is 30 miles from the hotel, while Guatapé is 12 miles from the property. The nearest airport is José María Córdova International Airport, 18 miles from Hotel Aguatur.
