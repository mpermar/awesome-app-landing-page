Description

Featuring a restaurant and a rooftop hydro massage tub with panoramic views of Medellín, Hotel Alcaraván is located just a 5-minutes’ drive from Medellín in Itagüí.

Wired internet access is available at this property. Hotel Alcaraván features Rustic-style rooms with satellite LCD TV.

Rooms also include private bathrooms with shower. Tropical cocktails can be ordered at the bar and room service is available.

Guests can visit cultural sights in the city such as Metropolitana Cathedral which is 10 km away or enjoy a stroll in Jardin Botanico gardens located just 12 km away.

At the Hotel, guests can play billiards in the games room. It is situated a 5-minute drive from the commercial centre while El Poblado neighbourhood is a 10-minutes’ drive away.

Free private parking is available at Alcaraván. Located 5-minutes’ drive to Olaya Herrera Airport and a 10-minute drive to Ayurá Metro Station.
