Description

Featuring a bar, shared lounge, garden and free WiFi, Nativo Hostel is located in Medellín, 1.

5 km from 70 Avenue and 5 km from Pueblito Paisa. All rooms feature a kitchenette and a shared bathroom.

The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, a shared kitchen and currency exchange for guests.

A à la carte breakfast is available daily at the guest house. NATIVO HOSTEL offers a barbecue.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike hire is available at the accommodation.

El Poblado Park is 7 km from Nativo Hostel, while Lleras Park is 8 km away. The nearest airport is Olaya Herrera, 7 km from the guest house, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
