Description

Intercontinental is situated in the area of El Poblado and overlooking the city of Medellin.

It offers luxurious accommodation, free WiFi, a spa and a semi-Olympic sized heated pool.

Rooms at Hotel Intercontinental are spacious and feature LCD cable TV and complimentary high speed internet access.

Some rooms have a fully-equipped kitchen and a living area. Room service is available 24/7.

Dining options include an extensive buffet breakfast at La Brasserie. Grill dishes and stone-oven pizzas are served at Fogon de Piedra.

Exclusive dining, with creative dishes and fine wines can be done at Cava Del Inter Wine Bar.

Wellness facilities offered are a hot tub solarium and a dry and steam sauna. Work out can be done in the gym and several massage treatments are available.

The tour desk can arrange different trips and activities in the area. The hotel is located 30 km from Jose Maria Cordova Airport and just a short drive from the Antioquia Museum.
