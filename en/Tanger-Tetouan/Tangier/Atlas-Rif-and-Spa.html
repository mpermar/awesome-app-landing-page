Description

Each of the Hotel Rif & Spa’s guest rooms has a private bathroom, air-conditioning. Satellite TV is also available in hotel rooms.

The hotel Rif’s restaurant serves traditional Moroccan and international specialties. Guests can enjoy a cocktail in the lounge bar and free Wi-Fi is available in the public areas.

Guests of the Hotel Rif & Spa can access Central Tangier in a few minutes by foot. The Forbes Museum and the Museum of Moroccan Arts are also nearby.
