Description

Offering outdoor pool, Leaders Plaza Apartments features self-catering accommodation.

Free Wi-Fi access is available. The property is 22. 1 km from Marina Mall and 29.

4 km from Kuwait Towers. Apartments at Leaders Plaza Apartments feature a TV, air conditioning and a balcony.

There is a full kitchen with a microwave and a refrigerator. Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with free toiletries and towels.
