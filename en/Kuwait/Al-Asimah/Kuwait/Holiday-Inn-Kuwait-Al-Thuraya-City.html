Description

Holiday Inn Kuwait Al Thuraya City is a 5-minute drive from Kuwait International Airport, and as such a great location for business and leisure travelers alike.

Grand Avenue Mall (largest mall in Kuwait) and 360 Mall are just a 5 minute drive from the property.

The property offers a complimentary airport transfer. Free WiFi is available in all areas of the hotel.

All 296 room and suites at the hotel, offers comfort and privacy as well as luxurious amenities.

Non-smoking rooms and rooms with facilities for disabled guests are available. With a wide variety of 8 different dining choices, the restaurants at the Holiday Inn serve delicious international and local cuisines.

Holiday Inn Kuwait Al Thuraya City boasts a leisure area that includes Edge Fitness Centre with a separate kids' pool and a hot tub, a ladies' gym, and a range of aerobics and fitness classes.

There is a 650 m² Aqua-tonic swimming pool with 163 underwater jets. The spa features 17 treatment rooms and 4 relaxation rooms.

The hotel has free valet parking and spaces for up to 500 cars. It is just 7-minute drive to the Avenues which is the biggest shopping mall in Kuwait and 5 minute drive to 360 Mall which is the luxurious Shopping centre.
