Description

All the Marina’s rooms are bright and spacious. They have a separate balcony or direct beach access and free Wi-Fi. Bathrooms feature separate walk-in shower, a bathtub and signature amenities.

The luxurious Atlantis Restaurant is shaped as a boat and overlooks the Kuwaiti Towers, and the Six Palms is in an atrium with a large glass ceiling which offers plenty of natural light. The Six Palms has various dining outlets which offer pancakes, health foods and international buffets.

The Coral Reef Health Club offers a range of relaxation treatments, including Swedish massages, hot stone massages and aromatherapy massages. There's also a well-equipped gym featuring cardio equipment and free weights.

The Marina hotel is in the heart of the vibrant shopping district in Salmiya nearby the Marina Mall and Marina Crescent. It is a 20-minute drive from Kuwait International Airport.

Hawally is a great choice for travelers interested in beach walks, clothes shopping and museums.
