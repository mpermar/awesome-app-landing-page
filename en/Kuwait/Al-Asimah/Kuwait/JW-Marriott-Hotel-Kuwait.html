Description

Located in the heart of Kuwait City, the JW Marriott Hotel is connected to the luxury Salhia Shopping Mall and is within walking distance of many of the city’s top commercial and business centres.

Guests can enjoy 5-star luxury at the JW Marriott Hotel. Guest rooms and suites are spacious, beautifully decorated and feature a range of modern amenities alongside luxury bathrooms.

There is a choice of 5 food restaurants within the hotel where guests can enjoy a wide range of international cuisine.

The hotel’s Terrace Grill is renowned for it steaks and seafood dishes or guests can enjoy lavish buffets and à la carte dining at the hotel’s Crossroads.

There are also numerous dining options available within the adjoining Salhia Shopping Mall.

The JW Marriott Hotel Kuwait has its very own Elite Health Club. Guests can make the most of the beautiful indoor pool and the hot tub or work out in the hotel’s gym.

Long-staying guests can take advantage of special hotel offers which include discounted rates for stays of 21 consecutive nights or more, plus additional benefits and discounts on food, beverage and laundry.

The JW Marriott Hotel is located a couple of minutes away from the city’s main landmark, the Kuwait Towers.
