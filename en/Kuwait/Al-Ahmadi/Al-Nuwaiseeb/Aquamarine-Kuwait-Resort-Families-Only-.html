Description

Set on a private beach, Aquamarine Kuwait Resort offers villas and chalets with beautiful beach view.

The resort has a children's playground and two swimming big pools with dedicated children’s pool areas, and guests can enjoy a drink at the snack bar.

All chalets have a living room with seating area and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels where you can relax.

You will find a kettle in the room. The rooms come with a private bathroom fitted with a bath.

For your comfort, you will find bath robes, slippers and free toiletries. You will find a super market and 24-hour front desk at the property.

Aquamarine Kuwait Resort has water sports facilities and bike hire is available. Kuwait International Airport is 88 km away.
