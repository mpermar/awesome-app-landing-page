Description

Offering rooms and apartments with free WiFi and free parking, Hotel Herc is located in the heart of Sarajevos Old Town, a 2-minute walk to Baarija.

Hotel apartments are located in the same building but are accessed through a private entrance.

A welcome drink will be offered to all guests. Herc Hotel has elegantly designed rooms with parquet flooring and LCD TVs.

A mini bar and fridge are included. The modern bathrooms include hydro-massage showers, slippers and a hair dryer.

All apartments feature a private terrace that overlooks the Old Town. Buffet breakfast is served every morning in the hotel restaurant.

There is a separate smoking room available within the bar. A variety of dining options can be found in the hotel's vicinity.

Hotel Hercs offers free secured parking with video surveillance and a night guard.

Sarajevo Airport is about 12 km away. Staff at the tour desk can arrange excursions and money exchange.
