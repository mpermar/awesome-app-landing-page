Description

Provided with wood furniture, rooms at Mediterraneo Hotel are all air conditioned and come with a balcony and satellite TV. You have free Wi-Fi access in the hotel’s lobby.

Italian and international cuisine, along with Friuli specialties and wines, are served at the 2 on-site restaurants. Breakfast is a rich buffet of both sweet and savory food.

The hotel is a 20-minute drive from San Michele Al Tagliamento and from Latisana Train Station.

Pineta is a great choice for travelers interested in tranquillity, relaxation and family-friendly trips.

Children and Extra Beds
All children are welcome.

Free!One child from 0 to 4 years stays free of charge when using existing beds.
One additional child from 0 to 4 years is charged 50 % of the room stay per night and person in an extra bed.
One additional child from 0 to 4 years is charged 50 % of the room stay per night and person when using existing beds.
Up to two children from 5 to 11 year are charged 70 % of the room stay per night per person when using existing beds.
Up to two children from 5 to 11 year are charged 70 % of the room stay per night per person for extra beds.
The maximum number of extra beds in a room is 2.
Any type of extra bed is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
