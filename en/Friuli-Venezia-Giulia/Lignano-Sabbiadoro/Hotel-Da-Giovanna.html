Description

Da Giovanna has a restaurant specializing in fish dishes and a bar in the hotel garden serving cocktails and ice cream. Breakfast is served on the terrace in fine weather.

Guests can enjoy the private beach which includes lounge chairs and umbrellas.

Da Giovanna Hotel is a one-hour drive from Udine.

Sabbiadoro is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, food and restaurants.
