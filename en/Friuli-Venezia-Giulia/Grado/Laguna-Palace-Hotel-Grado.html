Description

The stylish rooms of the Laguna Palace Hotel are spacious and come with parquet floors. Each room features a comfortable bathroom, a walk-in closet, and satellite TV.

The fully equipped spa center at the Laguna Palace offers a relaxing experience. It includes a large, heated pool, a Turkish bath, and a full range of massage and beauty treatments.

The terrace is ideal for sunbathing and enjoying breakfast. You can also choose from a tempting menu of local food at the restaurant, which is open for both lunch and dinner.
