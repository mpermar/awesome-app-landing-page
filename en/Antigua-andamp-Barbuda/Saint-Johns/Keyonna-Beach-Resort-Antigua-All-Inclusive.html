Description

The air-conditioned rooms and cottages at Keyonna Beach are spacious and feature beach décor. Each has 4-poster beds and private porches or patios, as well as a private bathroom with a shower. This hotel caters to couples only and does not allow children.

Featuring a wooden terrace with lounge chairs and Balinese beds, the Beach Restaurant serves fresh seafood and daily specialties. The bar features a selection of international wines, Caribbean spirits and rums.

Keyonna Beach All Inclusive is located a 20-minute drive from the St. Johns’ capital, and a 30-minute drive from V.C Bird International Airport. The lively Jolly Harbour Marina is 5 minutes away by car.
