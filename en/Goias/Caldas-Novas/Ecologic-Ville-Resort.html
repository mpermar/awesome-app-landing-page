Description

Ecologic Ville Resort & Spa facilities include dry and steam saunas, solarium, water park, games room, children's play area, piano bar and entertainment staff. There is also free WiFi.

The rooms of the complex are bright and elegantly decorated in pastel colors, air conditioning, flat-screen TV, minibar and private bathroom with hot shower. Some include a hot tub.

The resort's restaurants offer several menus, from weight loss meals and children's dishes, to sophisticated international dishes and vegetarian food. In the bar you can enjoy a selection of cocktails and wines.

Offering free private parking, Ecologic Ville Resort & Spa is 1 km from Caldas Novas Bus Station and 7 km from Caldas Novas Airport.
