Description

Rooms at the Dubai Grand Millennium have modern furnishings. Each room provides a bathrobe and slippers, a DVD player and flat-screen TV.

Grand Millennium features a range of restaurants and bars. The Asian restaurant on the 18th-floor has live cooking stations, and views of the Gulf and Jumeirah coastline. The pool-side bar offers light snacks and drinks.

Hotel Grand Millennium Dubai is located just 10 minutes’ drive from the Mall of the Emirates and Dubai Marina. Dubai Media City and Internet City are also just just over 1 miles from the hotel and Dubai International Airport is a 25-minute drive away.
