Description

Four Points by Sheraton Sheikh Zayed Road offers spacious, air-conditioned accommodations. It features floor-to-ceiling windows, a 32-inch interactive LCD TV and a sitting area. A minibar and a work desk are also included.

The rooftop swimming pool is on the 43rd floor and has a pool bar and wide terrace boasting panoramic views of Dubai’s skyline and the Jumeirah coastline. Guests can work out at the fitness center or book a massage treatment.

Guests have a choice of five different restaurants serving a variety of Italian, International and Moroccan cuisine. A pub is also available on the Mezzanine floor. Free valet parking is also offered.

Dubai Mall and Burj Khalifa are a 5-minute drive from Four Points by Sheraton. Dubai International Airport is less than 9 miles away.

Trade Center Area is a great choice for travelers interested in clothes shopping, luxury brand shopping and food.
