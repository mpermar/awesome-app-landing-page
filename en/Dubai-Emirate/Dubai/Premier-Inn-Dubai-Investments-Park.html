Description

Premier Inn’s spacious rooms include a flat-screen TV and a work desk. A hairdryer is provided in the private bathroom. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the entire hotel.

A rich breakfast buffet, including cooked-to-order eggs, is available in the morning. Bedouinn’s Bistro serves an international menu in the evening. Light snacks and drinks are provided in a casual setting at the snack bar.

A regular shuttle service operates between the Premier Inn and Ibn Battuta Mall and the Dubai Metro.

Premier Inn Dubai Investments Park is located 5 minutes from Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Road, in the Green Community. It is the close to Al Maktoum International Airport (Dubai World Central). Free parking is available on site.
