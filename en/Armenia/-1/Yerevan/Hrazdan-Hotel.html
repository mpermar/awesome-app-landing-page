Description

Armenian and international cuisine is served in the Hrazdan’s restaurant, and guests can dine outside next to the pool. The cozy lobby bar serves a range of drinks.

The spacious and well-equipped rooms with flat-screen TVs and air conditioning can be found at Hrazdan Hotel. They each have a modern bathroom with toiletries and hairdryer, and some offer views of the Ararat.

The Museum of Armenian History and the National Art Gallery are a 10-minute walk away. Zvartnots Airport is a 20-minute drive away.
