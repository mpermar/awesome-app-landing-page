Description

The guesthouse will provide guests with an oven, a microwave, a coffee machine, a bidet, free toiletries, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and DVD-Player. Every room comes with a electric tea pot, while some rooms also feature a kitchen with a toaster, a fridge and a stovetop. All rooms come with a seating area.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning at the property.

Komitas Avenue has a terrace.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can give tips on the area.

Yerevan State University is 1.7 miles from the accommodation, while History Museum of Armenia is 2 miles away.
