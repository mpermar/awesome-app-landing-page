Description

Just a 10-minute drive from Greystones and within easy reach of the Sally Gap, Roundwood, Glendalough and Britta Bay, Druids Glen Resort is also just 25 miles from Dublin.

Awarded 5-stars by the Irish Automobile Association and Failte Ireland, Druids Glen features 145 guest rooms, many with views of either the Wicklow Mountains or the golf course.
