Description

The rooms at the Akgün Istanbul Hotel are carpeted and have wood furnishings. They come equipped with an LCD TV and a minibar. Some rooms have a separate living area.

Guests can enjoy a massage, or relax in the steam room. The hotel has a gift shop and a tour desk that assists with car rental and provides information on local attractions.

There is a restaurant with a terrace that serves international cuisine and regional Turkish dishes. There is also a pool bar that has refreshing beverages.

Akgün Istanbul Hotel is located 6 miles from Ataturk International Airport. It has 24-hour front desk services and free on-site private parking.

Fatih is a great choice for travelers interested in monuments, architecture and history.
