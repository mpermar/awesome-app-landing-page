Description

The rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. All rooms include a private bathroom with free toiletries. Guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

Buffet and à la carte breakfast options are available each morning at Asia Pacific Hotel Beitou.

At the accommodation guests are welcome to use a sauna and an indoor swimming pool.

Guests can use the business center or relax in the bar. Around the clock assistance is available at the reception.

Taipei Songshan Airport is 6 miles away, and the property offers a free shuttle service.

Beitou District is a great choice for travelers interested in hot springs, relaxation and gourmet food.
