Description

With far-reaching views towards Eyjafjallajökull, this guest house offers horse riding tours and homemade food.

Selfoss is only 40 km away, and the iconic Gullfoss waterfall is only 52 km away.

Guesthouse Steinsholt provides free Wi-Fi in public areas and advice on arranging local activities.

The property has its own hot tub and an outdoor terrace for guests to relax. Each bright room has a modern décor and comes with either a private bathroom or shared bathroom facilities.

All rooms do come with their own wash basin. A breakfast buffet is served at Steinsholt featuring homemade bread and produce from the owners’ farm.

Dinners can be served on request, with lamb being a speciality. The famous site of Geysir is 46 km from the property, while Thingvellir National Park is 72 km away.
