Description

Featuring an outdoor pool, hot tub and sauna on site, this hotel offers accomodation in Hveragerdi, 45 km from Reykjavik.

There is free private parking and free WiFi. Hveragerdi Geothermal Park is 600 metres away.

Hotel Örk’s guest rooms include a refrigerator, work desk and satellite TV. Some also have a seating area.

The spacious bathrooms contain both a bath and shower. Traditional Icelandic cuisine is served at Hver Restaurant.

Other hotel facilities include a bar and games room with billiards and table tennis.

Guests can also enjoy free access to the neighbouring 9-hole golf course. Hiking trips to the Hengill volcanic area, as well as horseback riding excursions can be organised by Hotel Örk staff.

Raufarholshellir Lava Tunnel is 15 km away from Hotel Örk, while Selfoss is 13 km away.
