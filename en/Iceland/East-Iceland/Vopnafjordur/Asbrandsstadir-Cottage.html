Description

Set in the quiet countryside of Eastern Iceland, these basic cottages are 7 minutes’ drive from the village of Vopnafjördur.

It provides a kitchenette and free toiletries. The open-plan, compact Ásbrandsstadir Cottage includes a seating area and simple furnishings.

The spacious area around the cottages provides a campsite that can host group gatherings.

Hiking and nature sightseeing can be enjoyed in the area surrounding Cottage Ásbrandsstadir.

Trout fishing in the Hofsá River is another common activity. The staff offers guided tours around Vopnafjörður and the surrounding area.

Guests can also make use of bird watching binoculars that can be found at a lookout spot.

Selárdalslaug geothermal pool and the Bustarfell Museum are both within 17 km, while the closest grocery store is found in Vopnafjördur.
