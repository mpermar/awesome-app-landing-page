Description

In Midfjordur, midway between Reykjavik and Akureyri, is Hótel Laugarbakki. Stay close to one of Iceland’s best salmon rivers and the natural hot springs, which heat up the entire community.

All rooms have private bathrooms, a flat-screen TV and a desk. Free WiFi is available in all rooms.
