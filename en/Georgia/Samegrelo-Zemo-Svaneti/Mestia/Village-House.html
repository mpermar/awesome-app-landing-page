Description

All rooms in the guesthouse are equipped with a flat-screen TV. All guest rooms will provide guests with a microwave.

The daily breakfast offers continental and à la carte options.

Village House has a playground. Skiing is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.

Languages spoken at the reception include English and Georgian, and guests are invited to request advice on the area when needed.
