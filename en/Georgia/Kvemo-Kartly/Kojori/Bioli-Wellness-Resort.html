Description

Located 15 km from the centre of Tbilisi, Bioli Welness Resort is situated in the recreation area of Kojori at an altitude of 1200 meters.

Bioli Wellness Resort features free WiFi throughout the property. Guests will find an on-site restaurant and a terrace with views of the mountain.

Personalised wellness program will be developed in accordance with the guest's needs and diagnostics.

These programs are set for duration of 2, 7 or 14 days. The nearest airport is Tbilisi International Airport, 20 km from the property.
