Description

Just a few minutes from the heart of Kakheti region, Lopota Lake Resort & Spa is located on the private lakeside.

Featuring a deluxe spa, the property is 26 km from Telavi town. It offers 4 swimming pools and air-conditioned rooms with a fridge.

Offering an extensive Great Caucasus Mountain Range or Lopota Lake view, each room at Lopota is decorated in a classic style and includes a balcony.

Bathrooms are fitted with bathrobes and slippers. Buffet breakfast, lunch and dinner cooked from local organic products is served in All Day Dining Restaurant and Kakhetian Corner featuring Georgian traditional live-cooking.

There is also a wine tasting terrace with a view of the lake. Lopota Lake Resort & Spa offers 4 swimming pool and a relaxing area.

The Adventure Center offers a wide range of activities, including horse riding, quad bike tours, Mini-golf and fishing.

Bike rental is also available on-site. The 11th-century Alaverdi Monastery is 20 minutes’ drive from Lopota, and a shuttle service to Tbilisi, which is 120 km away, can be organised on request.
