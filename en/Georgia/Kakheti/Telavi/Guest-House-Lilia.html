Description

Featuring free Wi-Fi throughout, Lilia is located in the centre of Telavi, at the foot of the Caucasus Mountains.

Free public parking is available on site. With a simple décor, rooms all come with wooden floors and a work desk.

Each shares a bathroom. Guests can cook in the shared kitchen or find cafes within a 5-minute walk of Lilia.
