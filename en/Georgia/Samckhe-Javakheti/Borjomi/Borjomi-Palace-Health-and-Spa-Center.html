Description

Borjomi Palace Hotel & Spa is located in Borjomi town centre, 800 metres from the Mineral Water Park.

It features a spa centre and a gym. Free Wi-Fi and free private parking are provided.

All bright rooms come with a flat-screen TV, a kettle and a minibar. Modern bathrooms are fitted with a spa bath, and free toiletries are available.

Georgian Wine House Restaurant offers various Georgian and European dishes. Also guests can have a drink at the bar and sing karaoke.

The on-site spa centre offers various medical and cosmetic treatments, including massage services.

You can work out in a gym, relax in a sauna and play billiards. Borjomi Train Station is 10 minutes’ walk away, and the distance to Tbilisi International Airport is 170 km.
