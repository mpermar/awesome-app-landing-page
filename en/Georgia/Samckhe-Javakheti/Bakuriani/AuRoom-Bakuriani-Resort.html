Description

Situated in Bakuriani, 2. 2 km from Bakuriani Ski Resort, AuRoom Bakuriani Resort features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a bar and a shared lounge.

The accommodation offers ski storage space, as well as a garden and a terrace. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and free WiFi throughout the property.

All rooms at the hotel are fitted with a seating area. At AuRoom Bakuriani Resort the rooms include a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet breakfast. AuRoom Bakuriani Resort offers 4-star accommodation with an indoor pool and children's playground.

Bike hire and car hire are available at the hotel and the area is popular for skiing and cycling.
