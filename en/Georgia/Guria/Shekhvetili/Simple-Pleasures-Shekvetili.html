Description

Offering a children's playground and sun terrace, Simple Pleasures Shekvetili is situated in Shekhvetili, 38 km from Batumi.

Located a few steps from the beach, the property is surrounded with pine and eucalyptus trees creating a cozy and tranquil atmosphere.

Each room includes a TV with satellite channels. Some rooms feature a seating area for your convenience.

A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms. Every room includes a private bathroom.

For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer. Simple Pleasures Shekvetili features free WiFi.

There is a 24-hour front desk, a shared lounge, a tour desk and gift shop at the property.

An array of activities are offered in the area, such as horse riding and snorkelling.

Tsitsinatela Park is 6. 8 km away and black Sea Arena is 3. 2 km from Simple Pleasures.
