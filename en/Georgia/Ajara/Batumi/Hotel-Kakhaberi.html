Description

Kakhaberi Street Hotel is located in Batumi, 1 km from Batumi Airport. Free WiFi and free private parking are provided.

The air-conditioned rooms feature a balcony and a flat-screen TV. The bathroom comes with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

At Kakhaberi Street Hotel you will find a garden and a terrace. The property also offers an ironing service and laundry facilities.

The Black Sea is 2 km from the property and Batumi Train Station is within a 15-minute drive.
