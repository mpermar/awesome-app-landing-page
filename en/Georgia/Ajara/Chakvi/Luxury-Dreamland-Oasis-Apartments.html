Description

Each unit comes with air-conditioned, a seating area with satellite flat-screen TV and a fully equipped kitchen. A microwave and fridge are also featured, as well as a electric tea pot.

The restaurant at the apartment serves Italian and European cuisine.

At Luxury Dreamland Oasis Apartments guests are welcome to use a fitness room and an indoor swimming pool.

Guests can enjoy the outdoor swimming pool and garden at the accommodation.

Batumi is 7 miles from Luxury Dreamland Oasis Apartments. The nearest airport is Batumi (Chorokh) Airport, 10 miles from the apartment.
