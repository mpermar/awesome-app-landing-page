Description

Just a 2-minute walk from the private beach, this hotel features a Water Park, 2 swimming pools and a restaurant serving traditional cuisine.

A furnished balcony with view is featured in all rooms, and free WiFi is available.

Property is 15-minute walk away of Batumi Botanical Garden. Housed in 4 separate buildings, the spacious rooms at the Hotel Oasis are individually decorated, featuring air conditioning and satellite TV.

A seating area is offered in each room. Free toiletries, slippers and bathrobes are provided in the private bathroom.

Georgian and European specialities are served in the restaurant, and guests can enjoy their meals and drinks on the beachside terrace.

Barbecue facilities are also available. The Oasis Hotel features a tennis court, beach volleyball, entertainment centre for children and a gym.

The spa area located in a Water Park provides the opportunity to relax with a sauna and hot tub, and massages can be booked.

Makhinjauri Train Station is situated 10 km from the hotel, and Batumi Airport is 20 km away.
