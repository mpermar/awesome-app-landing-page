Description

Located in Kvariati, 13 km from Batumi, Hotel Zura features air-conditioned rooms and free private parking.

The hotel has an outdoor pool and a sun terrace. Each room comes with a flat-screen TV.

A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Free WiFi is available. Guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

Barbecue is also available. There is free shuttle service at the property. Batumi International Airport is 8 km away.
