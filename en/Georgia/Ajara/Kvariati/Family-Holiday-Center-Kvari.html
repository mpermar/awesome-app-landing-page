Description

Family Holiday Center Kvari is located in Kvariati village, 400 metres above sea level, 20-minutes drive from Batumi and 10-minutes from the boarder of Turkey.

Property offers free transportation to the beach, which is 2 km away. Free parking, sun terrace, swimming pool, sauna, playground and nursery with animator are provided.

Massage, fitness room, library, games and toys for children are also available. WiFi is accessible in all areas and is free of charge.

Each room comes with a balcony, flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Mountain or sea view is also featured in the rooms.

You will find free toiletries and hairdryer without extra charge. On-site à la carte restaurant offers various European, Georgian and vegetarian dishes.

Property also offers an on-site bar for the guests, where they can taste homemade wine and other drinks.

Hiking and diving are among the activities that guests can enjoy in the area. Batumi International Airport is 11.
