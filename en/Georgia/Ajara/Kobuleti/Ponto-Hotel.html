Description

Offering an outdoor pool with with an artificial waterfall and an outdoor bar, Ponto Hotel is located by the Kobuleti Nature Reserve, within a 1-minute walk of the Black Sea beach.

Free WiFi access and free private parking are available on site. The 24/7 security service is provided throughout the hotel territory.

All rooms feature a flat-screen TV with cable channels, air conditioning and electric kettle.

Seating area is available for your comfort. The bathroom comes with free toiletries.

Guests can eat in the property's own restaurant that serves national, European and Eastern cuisine.

Buffet breakfasts are offered on site. Other facilities offered at the property include a shared lounge.

Kobuleti Train and Bus Stations are 6 km away. Batumi Airport is within a 1 hour drive, and a shuttle is available upon request.
