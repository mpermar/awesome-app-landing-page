Description

Located at the seafront, Castello Mare Hotel&Wellness Resort is consisted of three buildings and has a private beach area and an infinity pool.

The hotel offers spa facilities, indoor pool tennis court and a fitness centre. Each unit in all three buildings boasts sea view.

Modernly furnished, the rooms of Castello Mare Hotel&Wellness Resort include flat-screen TV, air conditioning and a private bathroom.

For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer. Some rooms also offer a private balcony or a terrace.

The restaurant serves meals with à la carte and buffet options. There is also a bar on site.

Batumi is 19 km from Castello Mare Hotel&Wellness Resort, while Hopa is 55 km away.

The nearest airport is Batumi International Airport, 25 km from Castello Mare Hotel&Wellness Resort.
