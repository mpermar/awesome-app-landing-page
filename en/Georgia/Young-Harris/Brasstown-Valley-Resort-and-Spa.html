Description

Every contemporary room at Georgia resort features free Wi-Fi and coffee-making facilities. The rooms also provide a safe and bathrooms have a hairdryer and free toiletries for added convenience.

Guests can go horseback riding at the Brasstown Valley Resort or use the on-site tennis courts. The property also has an indoor and an outdoor pool as well as a fitness center.

The Brasstown Dining Room offers mountain views and is open for all meals. Buffet-style dining is available for breakfast and there are options from the menu for lunch and dinner. Brassies Grill is on site and serves burgers, salads, and sandwiches.

This resort is within a 2-hour drive of both downtown Atlanta and Chattanooga. The Lake Chatuge Recreation Area is 5 miles away.
