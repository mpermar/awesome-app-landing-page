Description

Located in Tbilisi City, less than 1 km from Freedom Square, Betlemi25 provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, free bikes and a bar.

1. 8 km from Tbilisi Opera and Ballet Theatre and 1. 9 km from Rustaveli Theatre, the property features a shared lounge and a garden.

The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, a shared kitchen and free WiFi.

Guest rooms in the hostel are equipped with a kettle. The units will provide guests with a fridge.

Betlemi25 offers a continental or buffet breakfast. The accommodation offers a terrace.

Tbilisi Zoo is 6 km from Betlemi25, while Saint George's Armenian Cathedral is 300 metres away.
