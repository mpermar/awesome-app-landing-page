Description

Featuring a bar, shared lounge, garden and free WiFi, Siberia Hotel is located in Tbilisi City, 3 km from Freedom Square and 3.

2 km from Tbilisi Opera and Ballet Theatre. Boasting room service, this property also provides guests with a children's playground.

The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, a shared kitchen and luggage storage for guests.

All units are equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kettle, a shower, a hairdryer and a desk.

Each room includes a private bathroom with a bath and free toiletries. All rooms at the hotel are fitted with a seating area.

Popular points of interest near Siberia Hotel include Sameba Cathedral, Presidential Palace and Metekhi Church.

The nearest airport is Tbilisi International, 15 km from the hotel, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
