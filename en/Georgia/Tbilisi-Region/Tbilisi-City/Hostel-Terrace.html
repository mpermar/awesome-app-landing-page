Description

This central hostel is just a 3-minute walk from Rustavelli Metro Station in Tbilisi, close to many bars and clubs.

It offers panoramic city views, a 24-hour reception and free Wi-Fi. The simply furnished rooms and dormitories at Hostel Terrace each feature a balcony.

The shared bathrooms are in the hallway. Shops and restaurants can be found within walking distance of Hostel Terrace.
