Description

Free WiFi and free parking are offered at Alliance Hotel, located in a green residential area of Tbilisi.

It is a 10-minute drive from the Old City. Spacious, modern rooms with rich wooden flooring and elegant bathrooms are offered at Alliance.

All are equipped with cable TV, air conditioning, a minibar and work desk. Toiletries are provided.

A continental breakfast and local cuisine are served at the on-site restaurant. Guests can enjoy Georgian wine on site bar and wine shop.

Guests can relax in the billiards room or on the sun terrace, which offers views of Tbilisi’s rooftops and features barbecue facilities.

24-hour front desk is available. Tbilisi Airport is 22 km away, and a shuttle service can be booked at the hotel.
