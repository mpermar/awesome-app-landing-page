Description

Namaste Hostel is located in Tbilisi Old Town, within a 5-minute walk from the Narikala Fortress.

It features a sun terrace, free ironing facilities, and free Wi-Fi is provided. Namaste offers bright dormitory and private rooms with nice city views.

Guests can use a shared bathroom; and free toiletries and a hairdryer are available.

Various cafes, bars and restaurants can be found within a 5-minute walk from the hostel.

Also guests can have a snack in a fully equipped shared kitchen. The Metekhi District is 750 metres from the property, and the lively Rustaveli Avenue is 1.

2 km away. Freedom Square Metro Station is 850 metres from Namaste Hostel, and the distance to Tbilisi International Airport is 16 km.
