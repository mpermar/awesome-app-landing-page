Description

Hotel King Tom is located 2. 5 km from the historical center of Tbilisi. Featuring a 24-hour front desk, this property also provides guests with a restaurant.

Freedom Square is 3. 5 km from the property. Hotel boasts panoramic views of the forest and the city.

WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge. All units come with air conditioning, minibar and flat-screen TV with satellite and cable channels.

You will find private bathroom with free toiletries for your comfort. Some rooms include a balcony and others also offer panoramic views of the city.
