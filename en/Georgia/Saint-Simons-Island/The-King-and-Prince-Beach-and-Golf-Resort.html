Description

A 37-inch flat-screen cable TV is provided in all rooms at The King and Prince Beach and Golf Resort. A small refrigerator and coffee-making facilities are included. Some rooms offer an ocean or resort view.

Access to 5 swimming pools, including a children’s pool, is provided to all guests at The King and Prince Beach Resort. Free Wi-Fi, massage services, and tennis courts are available as well.

Guests can dine in a relaxed oceanfront atmosphere at ECHO Oceanfront Restaurant. It serves coastal cuisine and handcrafted cocktails.

Pier Village, 1 miles away, features recreational activities, shopping, and dining. Saint Simons Island Lighthouse Museum and Neptune Park are a 5-minute drive from the resort.
