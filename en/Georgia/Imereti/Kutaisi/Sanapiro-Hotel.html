Description

Featuring free WiFi and a sun terrace, Sanapiro Hotel offers pet-friendly accommodation in central Kutaisi.

Free private parking is available on site. Some units have direct river view or garden view.

Laundry service is offered with an extra fee. Guests can enjoy their meals at the dining area.

Coffee and tea making facilities are available to guests. You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property.

Airport shuttle can be arranged upon request. The nearest airport is Kutaisi International Airport, 21 km from Sanapiro Hotel.
