Description

At the hotel, every room is equipped with a desk and a flat-screen TV. Guest rooms feature a closet.

The daily breakfast offers buffet and American options. The restaurant at Le Stelsia Resort specializes in French cuisine.

The accommodation has a playground. The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental is available at Le Stelsia Resort.

Agen is 17 miles from the hotel, while Moissac is 25 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Agen - La Garenne Aerodrome Airport, 19 miles from the property.
