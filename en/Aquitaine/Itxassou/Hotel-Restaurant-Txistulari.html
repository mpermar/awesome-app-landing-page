Description

All the heated room at Hotel Restaurant Txistulari feature hardwood floors and a flat screen TV. They also include a bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer.

Guests are able to enjoy a continental breakfast every morning at Hotel Restaurant Txistulari. The hotel’s restaurant serves regional dishes made from local products in the dining room or on the terrace. There is also a bar on the property.

This hotel is 9 miles from the Spanish border and 14 miles from Bayonne. Free private parking is available on site.
