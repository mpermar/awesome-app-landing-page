Description

The rooms are decorated and modernly furnished and feature air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a spa bath and a hairdryer.

The Parador Estaleiro Hotel also features a tennis court, fitness center, restaurant and conference rooms.

The hotel is 100 meters from Estaleirinho Restaurant, 5.2 km from Laranjeiras Beach and a 17-minute drive from the center of Balneário Camboriú. Navegantes International Airport is 42 km away.
