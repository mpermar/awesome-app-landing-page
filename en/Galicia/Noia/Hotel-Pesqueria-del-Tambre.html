Description

Offering free Wi-Fi and free private parking, Hotel Pesqueria del Tambre is located in Noia, 38 km from Santiago de Compostela. The accommodation has a restaurant.

Some rooms have a terrace or balcony. There is a flat screen TV.

The establishment has a shared lounge.

Various activities can be practiced around the hotel, such as fishing, canoeing and horse riding. The hotel also provides bicycle rental.

Pontevedra is 69 km from Hotel Pesqueria del Tambre and the nearest airport is Santiago de Compostela Airport, 52 km away.
