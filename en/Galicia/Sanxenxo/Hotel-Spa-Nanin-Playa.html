Description

The rooms and apartments at Nanin Spa Playa are simply decorated and air conditioned. Some accommodations have views of the Atlantic Ocean. All units are equipped with satellite TV and a fridge and the apartments also include living and kitchen areas.

The Nanin's restaurant serves traditional Galician cuisine, overlooking the pool and gardens. There is free Wi-Fi in the bar and on the terrace.

All guests enjoy access to the spa.

Nanin Playa is 2 km from the center of Sanxenxo and the marina. The beautiful white-sand beaches of the region are easily accessible and the nearest golf club is only a 5-minute drive from the hotel.
