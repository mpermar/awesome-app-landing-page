Description

All rooms at the Carlos I Silgar hotel are elegant and comfortable, with air conditioning, marble floors and soft carpets. They have a TV and the private bathroom includes a hairdryer, bathrobe and slippers.

The hotel restaurant serves a wide selection of Galician and international dishes. It also has a bar where you can have a snack or a drink.

Hotel Carlos I Silgar is located in gardens that house an outdoor swimming pool. The spa has a heated indoor pool and offers a wide range of relaxation treatments.

The Ría de Pontevedra is very popular for water sports and the hotel is just 150 meters from the Sanxenxo Marina. You can also get discounts on the green fees of the Meis golf course, located 20 minutes away by car.
