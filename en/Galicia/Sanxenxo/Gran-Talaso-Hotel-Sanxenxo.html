Description

The hotel also has free Wi-Fi, hairdressing service, childcare, a well-known restaurant and a café with a balcony and spectacular views where snacks are served.

The elegant rooms at Sanxenxo are bright and spacious and have air conditioning. They also have sea views, satellite TV and a minibar.

The restaurant serves a selection of traditional Galician cuisine and specializes in a la carte seafood dishes. It also has views of the bay.

The spacious spa has a Finnish sauna, swan necks, flotation pool, seawater pool, thalassotherapy massages, Vichy treatments, body wraps and sensation showers. The Gran Talaso Hotel Sanxenxo has a gym and spa facilities.

The Grand Thalasso is located between 2 beaches, just 10 minutes walk from the center of Sanxenxo and the marina. Pontevedra is 30 minutes away by car and Santiago de Compostela is 50 minutes away by car.
