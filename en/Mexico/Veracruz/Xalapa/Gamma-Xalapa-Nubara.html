Description

Located in central Xalapa, Gamma Xalapa Nubara has a heated outdoor pool, a sauna and gym.

The spacious rooms offer flat-screen cable TV, a CD player and views of the city or mountains.

Each air-conditioned room has elegant décor and carpeted floors. Rooms include a work desk and sofa, while bathrooms come with a hairdryer and toiletries.

Overlooking the pool and gardens, the hotel’s Huapango restaurant serves fresh regional cuisine.

Room service is available 24 hours a day. The lobby bar has a charming central fountain.

Juarez Park and Xalapa Cathedral are just a 15-minute walk from the Gamma Xalapa Nubara.

Free private parking is offered at the hotel, and staff are on hand to provide local area information.
