Description

Situated in a select area of Boca del Río, this luxurious hotel offers relaxing spa services and enjoyable on-site facilities, such as 2 gourmet restaurants, just minutes from the convention center.

Rooms are air-conditioned and have a safety deposit box. All rooms also have private bathrooms.

Grand Fiesta Americana Veracruz is beach-front and offers excellent views. Go swimming in the indoor or outdoor pools, then play a game of tennis.

After a busy day, relax with a specialty music therapy or aromatherapy treatment in the spa.

Enjoy seafood, Italian and Argentinian dishes at Arantzia Restaurant and Rosato Restaurant and have a drink at the snack bar and lobby bar.

For casual dining in a vibrant atmosphere, stop by the hotel's cafe, where a daily continental breakfast is served.
