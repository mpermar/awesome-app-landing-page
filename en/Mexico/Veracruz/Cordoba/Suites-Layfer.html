Description

All guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a coffee machine. Every room has a private bathroom with free toiletries, while certain rooms are contain a patio. At Suites Layfer each room has air conditioning and a flat-screen TV.

A continental, Full English/Irish or American breakfast is available every morning at the property. There's an on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of international dishes and also offers vegetarian options.

Guests can use the hot tub. You can play pool at the accommodation.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can give tips on the area.

Orizaba is 11 miles from Suites Layfer, while Nogales is 16 miles away. The nearest airport is General Heriberto Jara Airport, 52 miles from the property.
