Description

The rooms feature basic décor, a ceiling fan, air conditioning, cable TV and DVD player. The bathroom is private and has a shower and free toiletries. The suites have a kitchenette.

The restaurant at Hotel San Sebastian features a breakfast buffet and American cuisine. There is a pool snack bar.

This hotel is 2 miles from Galerias Shopping Center and 1 miles from Expoforum Convention Centre. Hermosillo International Airport is a 20-minute drive away. Free private parking is available.
