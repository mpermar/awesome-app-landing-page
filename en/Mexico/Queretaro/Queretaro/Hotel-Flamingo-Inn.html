Description

Featuring a spacious garden and outdoor swimming pool, this hotel is situated in Santiago de Querétaro’s financial district.

The city's historic centre is only 5 minutes’ drive away. Free Wi-fi is available throughout hotel.

The Flamingo Inn’s spacious, air-conditioned rooms are equipped with cable TV and a work desk.

They also include a safety deposit and ironing facilities. Private bathrooms include a hairdryer.

La Parrilla Leonesa restaurant is designed in a rustic style and serves both Mexican and international dishes, including grilled meats and enchiladas queratanas.

Flamingo Inn hotel features an on-site currency exchange desk, a fitness centre and a business centre.

It is within 5 minutes’ drive of several museums in the historic city centre, including the City Museum.
