Description

Boasting a full-service spa, on-site dining options and many modern amenities, this hotel is situated in an exclusive area of Juriquilla, Mexico, only a short distance from many popular sites.

In the area surrounding the Misión Querétaro hotel, guests can discover Mexico Valley University, and the UNAM Neurobiology Center.

There are also a number of local shops and restaurants situated nearby. While staying at Querétaro Misión, guests can enjoy relaxing in the hotel's on-site gardens, swimming in the large pool, or playing on the tennis courts.

The hotel also features free wireless internet access and in-room coffeemakers. On-site at Querétaro, guests can dine at Aranjuez Restaurant, serving a variety of international cuisine or enjoy fine dining at Mavericks Specialty Restaurant.
