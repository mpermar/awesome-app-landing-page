Description

Each suite at Doña Urraca Hotel & Spa features a minibar. They are furnished with a seating area and coffee facilities.

Guests can relax in the outdoor pool at the Doña Urraca Hotel. Tour information is available at the concierge desk and the hotel offers laundry facilities.

Tequisquiapan vineyards is nearby the hotel. The Valleys, Semidesierto and Sierra Gorda, the 3 tourist regions, are located around Hotel Doña Urraca.
