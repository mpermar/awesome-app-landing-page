Description

Guest rooms features air conditioning, a dishwasher, a microwave, a coffee machine, a bath or shower, free toiletries and a desk. The rooms include a flat-screen TV, and some units at the hotel have a garden view. The rooms have a private bathroom, a hair dryer and bed linen.

Candlewood Queretaro has an outdoor pool. You can play pool at the accommodation.

Candlewood Suites - Queretaro Juriquilla offers a business center for guest use.

Uptown Center Queretaro is 1.4 miles from the hotel, while Centre is 8 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Quétaro Intercontinental Airport, 17 miles from the property.
