Description

Situated in Tequisquiapan, 9 km from Los Rosales Vineyard, Gran Hotel Firenze features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a bar.

Featuring a shared lounge, the 5-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom.

The property offers an indoor pool, a garden and rooms with pool views. All rooms in the hotel are fitted with a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Guests at Gran Hotel Firenze can enjoy a à la carte or an American breakfast. The accommodation offers 5-star accommodation with a hot tub and sun terrace.

You can play billiards at Gran Hotel Firenze. The reception at the hotel can provide tips on the area.

The nearest airport is Querétaro International, 37 km from Gran Hotel Firenze, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
