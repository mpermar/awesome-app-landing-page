Description

This hotel is located in Culiacan, Mexico, just over 10 minutes by car from Aeropuerto Internacional de Culiacan.

It features an on-site restaurant, bar, and fitness center. El Tabachin, the on-site restaurant, serves a large buffet breakfast every morning.

Del Tomatero Lobby Bar is a good place to get a drink and enjoy live music. Stylish, contemporary rooms at Hotel Executivo have cable TV and free WiFi.

They are furnished with a work desk and include a coffee maker. Hotel Wyndham Executivo Culiacan has a front desk that is staffed 24 hours a day.

There are also 3 business centres and a tour desk in the lobby area. Free parking is available on site.

Los Jardines de Splash Club, a family recreation center, is 14 km from Hotel Wyndham Executivo Culiacan.
