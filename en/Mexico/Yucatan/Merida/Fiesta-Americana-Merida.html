Description

Fiesta Americana Merida features on-site facilities for a restful escape. Spend the day at the outdoor pool or work out in the well-equipped gym. Relax with a massage in the spa, while the kids have fun at the Fiesta Kids Club.

The elegant lobby is decorated with Yucatecan stone quarry, a fountain in the center and a big stained glass window.

Enjoy a variety of dishes from Merida Fiesta's 2 on-site restaurants: the Café Montejo and the Los Almendros, and the La Hach bar as well. For private, in-room dining, 24-hour room service is available.

This hotel is set between the 2 most important avenues: Colón and Paseo Montejo.
