Description

This hotel is located in northern Mérida, next to the Plaza Altabrisa Shopping Centre.

It offers a spa and air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi, satellite TV and a private bathroom with free toiletries.

Rooms at Mesón de la Luna Hotel & Spa also feature a desk, a safety-deposit box, a minibar and an ironing kit.

The spa at the Mesón de la Luna Hotel & Spa features a hot tub and massage services upon request.

The property's restaurant specializes in traditional Mexican food and serves a breakfast buffet.

There is also a bar where guests can relax and enjoy a drink or play pool. You can drive to central Mérida in under 20 minutes.

Here you can find the Monumento a la Bandera and the Casa de Montejo. Manuel Crescencio Rejón Airport is a 25-minute drive away.

Puerto Progreso is 30 km from the property, while Chichén-Itzá Archaeological Site is 100 km away.
