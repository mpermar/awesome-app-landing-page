Description

Villas Flamingos has bungalow-style accommodations next to the ocean. Each room has a balcony with hammocks and a private bathroom.

The hotel has scuba diving, kayaking and snorkeling activities for an extra charge. There are regular ferries from the island to the mainland town of Chiquilá, which is around a 90-minute drive from Cancun International Airport.
