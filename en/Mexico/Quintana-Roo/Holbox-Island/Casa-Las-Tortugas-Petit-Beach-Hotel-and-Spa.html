Description

The rooms at La Casa de las Tortugas Petit Beach Hotel & Spa feature bamboo frames and ethnic furniture. Each one has a balcony.

The Casa’s restaurant serves a range of international food. There is also a bar by the pool.

Holbox Island is a popular destination for kiteboarding and scuba diving. Cancún Airport is around 43 miles away, and a shuttle service is available upon request.
