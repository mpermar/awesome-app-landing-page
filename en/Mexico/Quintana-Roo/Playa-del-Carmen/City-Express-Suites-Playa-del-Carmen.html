Description

Located in Playa del Carmen, 1. 5 km from ADO International Bus Station, City Express Suites Playa del Carmen features an outdoor pool and fitness centre.

Free WiFi is featured throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Each room is fitted with a flat-screen TV. You will find a coffee machine in the room.

The rooms include a private bathroom equipped with a shower. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property.

Transportation Hotel - Beach Club (Sr. Frogs y Kool Beach) schedule: 9:00 am, 11:00 am y 1 :00pm, limited seats, with previous reservation.
