Description

Brightly decorated, Fabiola’s studios and apartments feature cable TV and a private bathroom. The kitchenettes include a microwave, stove, and coffee maker. The apartments also have a balcony overlooking the street.

The complex is in the center of Playa del Carmen, just 2 blocks from lively Fifth Avenue. You can find a wide variety of shops, bars and restaurants within a 5-minute walk.

Free private parking is available at the Fabiola. Cancún Airport is a 45-minute drive away, and the staff at the front desk can arrange airport transportation for an extra fee.
