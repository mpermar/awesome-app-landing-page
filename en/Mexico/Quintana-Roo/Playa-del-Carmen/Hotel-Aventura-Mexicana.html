Description

Located in downtown Playa del Carmen, this hotel is 1. 5 km from the Cozumel ferry and just 2 blocks from the beach.

It features 2 outdoor pools, a waterfall and 3 sun decks. The rooms at Hotel Aventura Mexicana include air conditioning, cable TV, a mini-fridge and a safety deposit box.

Free WiFi is available in all rooms. This Playa del Carmen hotel features the Mayan Bistro restaurant and bar, Continental breakfast, and the Xtabey spa.

Onsite yoga classes available with several different classes each day. Shuttle services from Cancun Airport may be arranged.

The local shops and restaurants of 5th Avenue are just 1 block away from Hotel Aventura Mexicana.
