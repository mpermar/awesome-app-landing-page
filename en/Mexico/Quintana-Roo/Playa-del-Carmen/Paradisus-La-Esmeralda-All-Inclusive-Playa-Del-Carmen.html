Description

Guests can relax at the resort's Yhi spa and enjoy a variety of body and facial treatments, Thalassotherapy or a massage.

Paradisus La Esmeralda Playa Del Carmen’s spacious, air-conditioned rooms feature marble floors and an elegant décor with neutral color tones.

All rooms have a modern bathroom including a sensory tub and a rain shower. Some include a hot tub and special children’s bathroom amenities.

La Esmeralda hotel offers a range of international cuisine, including Mexican, contemporary Asian, Latin fusion and Mediterranean. There is also a chill-out club including a bar serving premium brand drinks and a restaurant on the beach.

The hotel is about 45 minutes from Cancún International Aiport and just 2 miles from the center of Playa del Carmen. Xcaret eco archeological park is a 15 minute drive away.
