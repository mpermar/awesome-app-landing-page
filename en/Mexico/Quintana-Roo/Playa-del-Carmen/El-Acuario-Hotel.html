Description

This downtown Playa del Carmen hotel is within a 10-minute walk of the Caribbean Sea Beaches.

El Acuario Hotel offers an outdoor pool, a barbecue area and a palapa seating area.

The air-conditioned rooms provide cable TV, free WiFi, a safety-deposit box and ceiling fan.

They also have kitchenette with sink, microwave oven, electric grill, refrigerators, kitchenware and dishes.

Additional hotel services include babysitting, massages, concierge service, bicycle and car rentals.

El Acuario is one mile from Mahekal Beach Resort. The hotel is 22 miles from Aquanauts Dive Adventures.
