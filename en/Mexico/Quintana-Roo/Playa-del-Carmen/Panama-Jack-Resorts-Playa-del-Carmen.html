Description

Nestled on a secluded private beach, only minutes from the village Playa del Carmen, this all-inclusive resort features spacious accommodations and a spa offering various services.

Al rooms arel air-conditioned, and some feature semi-private terraces or balconies, mini bar, WiFi, flat-screen TV, DVD player, safety deposit box and coffee maker.

The bathroom have marble fixtures. Panama Jack Playa del Carmen places guests a short distance from many popular sites.
