Description

Offering an outdoor pool, plus a spa and wellness centre, Eco Boutique Hotel Amarte is located 15 minutes’ drive from Playa del Carmen.

Free Wi-Fi access is available, and yoga lessons are provided on site. The hotel’s accommodations will provide you with air conditioning and a terrace.

The private bathrooms come with a bath or shower, and free toiletries. You can enjoy the garden views from all the rooms.

Extras include a desk, outdoor furniture and linen. At Eco Boutique Hotel Amarte you will find an à-la-carte restaurant.

Other facilities offered at the property meeting facilities and a ticket service.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including horse riding, a water park and windsurfing.

The property offers free parking. Playa del Carmen offers nightlife entertainment and a variety of bars and restaurants.

Xcaret Eco-Park is 25 minutes’ drive away. Maroma Bay is located within a 25-minute walk from the property.
