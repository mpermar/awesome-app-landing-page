Description

Located just a 5-minute drive from Akumal Bay, CampAkumal offers an outdoor pool and a tropical garden with BBQ facilities.

Each stylish air-conditioned apartment has a private balcony. CampAkumal apartments are decorated in light tones and feature free Wi-Fi.

Some apartments have a living-dining area, a fully equipped kitchen and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Akumal Bay is a popular snorkelling and diving spot and is famous for its turtles.

Nearby Akumal town has a range of cafés, bars and restaurants. The beautiful Tulum Mayan Ruins are just a 15-minute drive away, while Cancún is a 90-minute drive away.
