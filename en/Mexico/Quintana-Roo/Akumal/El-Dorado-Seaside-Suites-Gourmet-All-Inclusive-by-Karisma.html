Description

Each well furnished suite has a hot tub for 2, satellite TV, DVD player, a stocked minibar with beer, soft drinks and water, coffee maker and a furnished balcony or terrace. The floors can be made of marble or Mexican tiles.

The Gourmet Inclusive Experience consists of selection of 8 different restaurants and 9 bars where guests will be able to enjoy Latin fusion cuisine, authentic Mexican cuisine, contemporary Italian, Asian, a pub-style venue, world-style dishes and a beach BBQ. The bars can be located outdoors, by the pool area or in the lobby.

Guests will be received with a welcome glass of sparkling wine, and can enjoy of the daily and nightly activities program prepared by the resort's staff, kayaking ans snorkeling, bathrobes and beach towel services, diving lessons in one of the pools, and a sauna and steam room in the spa.

Naay Spa features massages, a temazcal (sweat lodge), facials and special body treatments. A tour desk, jewelry store and money exchange services can also be found within.
