Description

Offering an outdoor lagoon pool, B&B Villa Morena EcoLiving is a eco-friendly hotel powered by solar panels.

located in Akumal, only 3 km from the beachfront. A mini refrigerator and a dining table are provided in each room.

Free WiFi access is available. A private bathroom offers a shower with provided towels.

Views of the garden and pool are available from each room at B&B Villa Morena EcoLiving.

Breakfast is served in the guest rooms each morning. A restaurant and bar feature on the sun terrace at the resort.

BBQ facilities are also available. Xcacel-Xcacelito is 11 km and Calica Cruise Terminal is 25 km from the property.
