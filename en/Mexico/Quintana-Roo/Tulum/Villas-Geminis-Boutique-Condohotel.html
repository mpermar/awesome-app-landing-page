Description

Set in the heart of Tulum, Villas Geminis Boutique Condo Hotel offers an outdoor pool, tropical gardens and air-conditioned apartments with terraces.

Guests will enjoy free access to several private beach clubs, located within a 5-minute drive.

Tastefully decorated, the luxury Villas Geminis Boutique Condo Hotel apartments have views of the swimming pool and gardens.

Each apartment includes free WiFi, cable TV and a modern kitchen with an espresso machine, hob and fridge-freezer.

Free bottled water, tea and sugar are provided. The toiletries are made with organic honey.

Daily housekeeping is included. Shops, restaurants and bars can be found within a 5-minute walk of the complex, while the stunning Tulum Archaeological Site and the beaches of the Mayan Riviera are just 3 km away.

Free bicycles are available to use on request. The staff can help guests book tours to the Mayan ruins of Cob and Chichen-Itz, or to go swimming with the dolphins at Xcaret and Xel Ha.

A complimentary continental breakfast with fresh squeezed juice, breads, jam, yogurt, granola and fresh fruits is offered.

Free private parking is available on site, and airport transfers can be arranged on request.

You can also book in-room massages and private yoga classes. The owners visit daily and provide local area information, discounts at La Vita e Bella Restaurant and Beach Club and local taxi service to the Mayan ruins and nearby cenotes.
