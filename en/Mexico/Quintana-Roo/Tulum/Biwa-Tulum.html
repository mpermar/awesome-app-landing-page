Description

At the hotel, all rooms include a balcony with a city view. Complete with a private bathroom equipped with free toiletries, the rooms at Biwa Tulum have a flat-screen TV and air conditioning, and selected rooms also feature a patio. All rooms will provide guests with a desk and a coffee machine.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet breakfast. There's an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of local dishes.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can give tips on the area.

Parque Nacional Tulum is 2.4 miles from Biwa Tulum. Cozumel Airport is 40 miles away.

Tulum City Centre is a great choice for travelers interested in archaeology, Mayan ruins and snorkeling.
