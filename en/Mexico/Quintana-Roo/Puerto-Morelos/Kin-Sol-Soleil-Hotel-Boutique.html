Description

Set beachfront in Maroma Bay and offering an outdoor pool, gym and spa, Kin Sol Soleil is 18 minutes’ drive from central Playa del Carmen.

Massages are provided for a fee. The bright rooms here will provide you with a flat-screen TV, air conditioning and a minibar.

Featuring a bath, the private bathrooms also come with a hairdryer. You can enjoy the sea views form all its accommodations.

A DVD player and cable channels are also included, while some units have a private plunge pool or a kitchenette.

Pavo Real by the Sea, the on-site restaurant, also features an ocean-side bar and serves breakfast, lunch and dinners.

Daily breakfast is free of charge. Playa del Carmen offers a wide variety of nightlife entertainment, bars and restaurants.

The ferry dock to Cozumel is located 20 minutes’ drive away. Diving and snorkelling are popular activities to practice.
