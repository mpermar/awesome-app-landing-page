Description

The spacious, air-conditioned rooms at the Puerto Aventuras Resort & Spa come with a balcony. Each has subtle, Mexican-style décor with tiled floors. All feature a flat-screen TV and minibar.

A range of cuisine including Italian, Mexican and grilled seafood, is on offer in the Aventuras’ restaurants. There is also an international buffet and a beach bar.

Guests can relax on the charming terrace, which features thatched palapa shelters. The on-site spa has a range of hot tubs, plunge pools and steam baths. Treatments on offer include reflexology, aromatherapy and wraps.

The resort is a 20-minute drive from the Mayan ruins at Tulum. The popular resort of Playa del Carmen is less than 12.4 miles away.Family friendly hotel, please see fine print for additional information.
