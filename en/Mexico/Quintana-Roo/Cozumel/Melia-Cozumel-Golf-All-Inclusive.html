Description

This All Inclusive Resort is located in Cozumel, Mexico. It offers full spa services, child care, water sports, car rental services, three restaurants and five bars.

Free WiFi is available in the property's lobby. The Melia Cozumel Golf All Inclusive offers many activities and attractions for guests.

Snorkeling, windsurfing and kayaking can be arranged at the front desk. Tours to San Gervasio Ruins and Chankanaab Park are also available through the tour desk.

Daytime activities include cooking and dance lessons and beach volleyball. Melia Cozumel offers several unique dining experiences.

Fine dining can be found at Cafe Paraiso Restaurant with international la carte menus.

La Isla is the resort's main restaurant, with buffets for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

La Iguana Snack serves pizza, burgers, nachos, hot dogs, French fries, and other light foods from late morning until afternoon.

There are also 2 seasonal restaurants, La Veranda and La Iguana. Guests can also enjoy a pool-side bar and lobby bar with international drinks and ambient music.

Each room at Cozumel Melia offers air conditioning, 43 inch satellite TV and marble bathrooms.
