Description

Located in Cancún in the Quintana Roo Region, 4. 1 km from Cancun Bus Station, Caribbean apartment located in an resort "All Ritmo Cancun" features an outdoor pool and year-round outdoor pool.

There is a water park on site and guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant and bar.

Cancun Government Palace is 4. 3 km from the property. Free WiFi is provided throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

The accommodation has a seating area. There is also a kitchen, equipped with a fridge and stovetop.

There is a private bathroom with a bath or shower in every unit. Towels are offered.

Caribbean apartment also includes a fitness centre. Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant.

You can play tennis and table tennis at the property, and the area is popular for snorkelling.

Beto Avila Stadium is 6 km from Caribbean apartment, while Coco Bongo is 9 km away.

The nearest airport is Cancún International Airport, 19 km from Caribbean apartment.
