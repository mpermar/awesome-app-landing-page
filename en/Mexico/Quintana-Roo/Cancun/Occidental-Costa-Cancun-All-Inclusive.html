Description

Ideally placed along the shores of the Caribbean, this all-inclusive family-friendly resort offers easy access to area attractions, and provides spacious accommodations, numerous on-site facilities and friendly service.

The Occidental Costa Cancun strives to make every stay enjoyable with amenities such as on-site boutiques, state-of-the-art fitness facilities and a gift shop.

The hotel also features a large outdoor pool as well as a children's club and daily activities.

Guests at Occidental Costa Cancun can enjoy a variety of dining options. From international buffets and classic Mexican dishes, to traditional Spanish meals and quick snacks, there is something for every palate.

Area attractions, including the ancient city of Chichen Itza and the Mayan ruins are easily accessible with the Occidental Costa Cancun on-site car rental services and travel agency.
