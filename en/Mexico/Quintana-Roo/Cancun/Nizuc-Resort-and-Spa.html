Description

Offering panoramic views of the Caribbean Sea, Nizuc Resort & Spa is located on the secluded Punta Nizuc and features an outdoor pool, private dock and spa.

Free Wi-Fi and free private parking are available to guests. The air-conditioned rooms offer Nespresso coffee makers and flat-screen TVs.

The spacious bathrooms feature a bath tubs with rainfall showers. The 6 on-site restaurants serve modern Mexican dishes, Peruvian ceviches as well as Asian and Mediterranean food.

Tours and activities can be arranged by the concierge. Nizuc Resort & Spa has event and meeting facilities.

Guests can enjoy a variety of massages or treatments in the luxurious on-site spa.

The Nizuc Thermal Experience is a feast to awaken the senses through a guided circuit of hydrotherapy.

Laguna Nichupté can be reached 6. 9 km. Cancun International Airport is a 10 minutes’ drive from Nizuc Resort & Spa.
