Description

Guest rooms at the GR Caribe Deluxe All Inclusive Resort include an iPod docking station and satellite TV. Each is furnished with a minibar, coffee facilities and a DVD player.

Many dining options are available at the resort. They include French pastries in Plaza Socorrito with live music and story telling. Italian, Asian and Mexican cuisine is available and Café Solaris offers a breakfast buffet.

All-Inclusive GR Caribe Deluxe features a well equipped gym and miniature golf. Guests can use the business center for added convenience.

Downtown Cancun is 20 minutes from the resort. GR Caribe Deluxe All Inclusive is 15 minutes from the main shopping malls.
