Description

Offering free Wi-Fi in all areas, a terrace with a fountain and Colonial style décor, the Hotel Trebol is located 2 minutes’ walk from the Oaxaca Zocalo Square and 500 metres from the Handcraft Market.

The rooms with classic décor offer ceiling fan, cable TV, telephone and a private bathroom with shower and free toiletries.

Guests at Hotel Trebol will find a variety of Mexican restaurants at Benito Juárez Market located in front of the property as well as locally made products.

Los Portales restaurant offering regional and international cuisine is only 100 metres away.

A business centre and safety box at the front desk is also available. This hotel is 10 minutes’ drive from the Guelaguetza Festival Auditorium and 10 km from the Atzompa Archaeological Site.
