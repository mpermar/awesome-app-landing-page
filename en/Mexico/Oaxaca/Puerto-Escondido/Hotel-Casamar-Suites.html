Description

Hotel Casamar Suites is just 30 metres from Playa Zicatela Beach in Puerto Escondido and features free Wi-Fi and a shared swimming pool.

The property also has BBQ facilities, yoga and fitness lessons. The traditionally furnished studios and apartments at this property come with a seating and dining area, as well as a fully equipped kitchen and a private patio.

One furnished room comes with shared kitchen and bath. Air conditioning is available for a surcharge.

There are several supermarkets and markets within 2 km of Casamar Suites for guests who want to cook at home, and the area offers restaurants serving national and international cuisine.

The property has onsite massage services and acupuncture treatments can be arranged for an extra fee.

Puerto Escondido City Centre, where guests will find shopping, is 2 km away, while the city’s international airport is a 15-minute drive away.
