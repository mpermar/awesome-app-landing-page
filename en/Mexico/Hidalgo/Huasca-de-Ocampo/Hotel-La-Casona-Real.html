Description

The rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area. The rooms have a private bathroom with a shower, while some feature garden views. All guest rooms come with a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Breakfast is available every morning, and includes à la carte and American options.

Hotel La Casona Real has a playground. Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.

There's a tour desk and car rentals are available, while the business center has an ATM machine. Staff at the 24-hour front desk can give information on what to do in the area.

San Miguel Regla is 0.4 miles from Hotel La Casona Real.
