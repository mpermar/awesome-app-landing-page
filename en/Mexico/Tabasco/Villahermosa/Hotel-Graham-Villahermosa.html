Description

Featuring free WiFi and outdoor pool, Hotel Graham Villahermosa is set beside Villahermosa’s Laguna de Ilusiones Lake, across the street from the Tabasco Juárez Autonomous University.

It has a bar-restaurant. Each air-conditioned room at the Graham Villahermosa has tiled floors.

All accommodation has cable TV and a private bathroom. The Wellington bar-restaurant serves a buffet breakfast, as well as a selection of Mexican and international cuisine for dinner.

Guests can enjoy a drink on the poolside terrace, which overlooks the lake. Hotel Graham Villahermosa is located 3 minutes away, by foot, of the Tabasco Juárez Autonomous University, location for national and international events.

The centre of Villahermosa is less than 2 km from the Hotel Graham, while Parque Mueso de La Venta Park is a 5-minute drive away.
