Description

Set in San Luis Potosí’s Lomas District 4th Section and steps from Plaza San Luis Shopping Centre, Hotel Ankara offers free Wi-Fi access trhoughout.

The hotel features an international restaurant and the Kizilay Bar. Each spacious, air-conditioned room at Hotel Ankara has a flat-screen TV and a coffee maker.

Guests receive access to the neighbouring La Loma Sports Club and its golf course with a special cost.

Facilities include an outdoor pool, business centre and a gym. The centre of San Luis Potosí is a 10-minute drive from the Ankara.
