Description

The air-conditioned rooms at the Novit feature carpeted floors and smart, modern décor. Each one has free WiFi and a seating area with cable flat-screen TV, sofas and great views of the city.

The hotel’s restaurant serves avant-garde Mediterranean food. There is also an outdoor lounge bar which serves cocktails and offers live music.

The World Trade Center is just 3 minutes’ walk away and guests can find an array of restaurants, entertainment and shops within walking distance. You can drive to Mexico City Airport in 20 minutes and the Historic Center is just 4.3 mi away.
