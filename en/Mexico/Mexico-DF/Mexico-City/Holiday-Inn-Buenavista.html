Description

This modern hotel offers free Wi-Fi, free private parking and spacious, air-conditioned rooms with cable TV.

It is situated 400 metres from Revolución Metro Station and 600 metres from Buenavista Train Station.

Holiday Inn Buenavista's rooms have elegant décor, with carpeted floors and wooden furniture.

The granite bathrooms come with a hairdryer and amenities kit. The restaurant at Holiday Inn Buenavista offers Mexican and international cuisine.

The on-site bar serves fine wines and has a terrace. Located in the Santa Maria La Ribera district, the hotel is a 5-minute walk from Reforma Avenue and within a 10-minute walk of the Monument to the Revolution.

The Alameda Central Park and Bellas Artes Palace are 5 minutes away by Metro; while the US Embassy is 3 km away.
