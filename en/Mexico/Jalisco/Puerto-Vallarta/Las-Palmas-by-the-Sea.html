Description

Located in Puerto Vallarta’s hotel district, this hotel offers fantastic sea views from its outdoor hot tub and free WiFi access in all rooms and public areas.

It offers 2 outdoor pools and activities including scuba diving, yoga and beach volleyball.

The air-conditioned rooms at Las Palmas by the Sea feature cable TV. The private bathrooms include a hairdryer.

Las Palmas by the Sea has a seafood restaurant by the ocean, as well as a romantic restaurant with live music.

There is also a poolside bar overlooking the beach. The hotel has a children’s miniclub offering a range of activities.

They can also arrange trips such as fishing, catamaran trips or birdwatching. There are many restaurants, shops and bars in the streets around the Las Palmas.
