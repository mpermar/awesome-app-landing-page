Description

Guest rooms at the Crown Paradise come with air conditioning and a private balcony. Cable TV and coffee makers are also included.

Crown Paradise Club Puerto Vallarta features 5 bars, nightclub, a full gym and 2 tennis courts. A sports court and playground area are available. Evening entertainment is also provided.

Guests can use the family game area or the activities pool at the Puerto Vallarta Crown Paradise. A beachfront snack bar is also available.

This property is 2 miles from the World Trade Centre and 2.5 miles from Puerto Vallarta main square.
