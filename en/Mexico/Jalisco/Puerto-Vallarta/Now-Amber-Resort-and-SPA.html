Description

Now Amber Spa includes hot tubs, a sauna and steam cabin, a Swiss shower and an acupressure shower. There is also a relaxation lounge and guests can enjoy a range of beauty and massage treatments.

Now Amber Resort’s air-conditioned rooms feature an elegant décor and offer garden or ocean views. In-room amenities include free Wi-Fi, cable and satellite TV, and free bathroom toiletries.

Offering 5 restaurants including 1 buffet, 4 à la carte, 1 barefoot grill and 1 café with no reservations required. All restaurant openings rotate throughout the week.

In addition, as part of our Sip, Savour & See dining program the adult guests will be delighted with our three more venues for dinners only, located at our sister Resort Secrets Vallarta Bay.

This complex also features free-roaming local and international calls through the Now Resorts & SPA mobile app using the on-site Wi-Fi connection.

Family Friendly hotel, please see fine print for additional information.

Puerto Vallarta International Airport is about 15 minutes’ drive away.
