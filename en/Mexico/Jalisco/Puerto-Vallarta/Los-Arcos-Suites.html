Description

Located steps from the public beach of Puerto Vallarta and 5 minutes' drive from the shops and restaurants of downtown, this hotel features a swimming pool, 2 restaurants, and suites with balconies.

The accommodations at Los Arcos Suites are furnished with a kitchenette and sofa bed.

An electronic safety deposit box and cable TV are also provided. A hot tub and bar are part of the facilities of this Puerto Vallarta hotel.

Concierge services and room service are available. Butler services are available between 10:00 and 20:00.

A 24-hour currency exchange is located on the property. The Vallarta Marina Golf Club and Puerto Vallarta International Airport are 10 minutes from Los Arcos Suites.
