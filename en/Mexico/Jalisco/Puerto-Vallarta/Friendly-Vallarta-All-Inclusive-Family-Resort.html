Description

This all-inclusive Mexico hotel offers private beach access and views of Banderas Bay.

The hotel offers a full-service spa with salon and variety of dining options. The rooms include a balcony.

Friendly Vallarta Beach Resort & Spa rooms include a seating area and work desk. The rooms are equipped with a private bathroom, which includes a shower.

Guests at the Friendly Vallarta Beach Resort & Spa can relax in one of two hot tubs or swim in the outdoor pool.

The hotel also offers a game room and tennis court. The Friendly Vallarta is within a 5-minute drive of Puerto Vallarta city centre and the Malecon, which features shopping and dining.
