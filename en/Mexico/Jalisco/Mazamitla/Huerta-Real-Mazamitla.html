Description

Situated in the charming town of Mazamitla, Huerta Real Mazamitla offers views of Colima Volcano and has an orchard, tennis courts and a gym.

All rooms include free Wi-Fi and a fireplace. Decorated with original local artwork, Huerta Real’s rustic rooms and cottages include a TV and a private bathroom.

Cottages also have a seating area. You can enjoy a homemade breakfast in the dining room, or outside on the terrace.

There are also several restaurants within a 10-minute walk. Huerta Real Mazamitla is situated in the Sierra Tigre Mountains, an hour’s drive from the Colima Volcano National Park.

Guadalajara is a 2-hour drive away. Free parking is available at the Huerta Real.

Staff at the 24-hour reception are happy to provide information about the region.
