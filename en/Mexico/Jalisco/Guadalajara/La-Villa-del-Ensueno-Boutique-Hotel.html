Description

Rooms at the Hotel Boutique La Villa del Ensueno are spacious and uniquely decorated. They have cable TV and private bathrooms. Some rooms feature hot tubs and private balconies.

La Villa del Ensueno features traditional, Mexican décor and free Wi-Fi. The concierge desk can help arrange tours and ticket reservations. Business services and free on-site parking are also available.

La Villa del Ensueno Boutique Hotel is 4 blocks from the center of Tlaquepaque. Guests can visit the Museo Pantalon Panduro Art Museum and the Metropolitan Cathedral. Tonala is a 20-minute drive from the hotel and has open air markets.

Tlaquepaque is a great choice for travelers interested in history, traditional food and friendly locals.
