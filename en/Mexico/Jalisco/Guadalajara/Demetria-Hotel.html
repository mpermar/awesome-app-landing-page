Description

Every suite at the Demetria features elegant hardwood furniture. They are all air-conditioned and include an iPod dock, a minibar and a buffet breakfast.

Free parking is provided at the Demetria Hotel. It is located a 15-minute walk from Guadalajara University. The city's cathedral is less than 2 miles away.

The hotel has a bar and an à la carte restaurant serving a daily breakfast buffet. You can also find various restaurants and cafes in the surrounding streets and along Chapultepec Avenue.
