Description

Beach front property Hotel y Suites Corita features a private beach area with access to the beach, sun terrace and outdoor swimming pool.

Free Wi-Fi and and free private parking are available to guests. The air-conditioned rooms offer TV with cable channels.

The private bathrooms feature showers and free toiletries. Some of the rooms have a fully equipped kitchen.

Guests can find restaurants in the immediate vicinity of Hotel y Suites Corita. Hotel y Suites Corita is 20 meters from Plaza los Amores.

Flamingos Golf Club can be reached within 10 minutes by car. The fishing village of Cruz de Huanacaxtle is at a distance of 6 km.
