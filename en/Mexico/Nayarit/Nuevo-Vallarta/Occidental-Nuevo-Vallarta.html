Description

Located a 1-minute walk from Nuevo Vallarta Beach and Marina, Occidental Nuevo Vallarta offers outdoor pools for adults and children, tennis courts and a gym.

The stylish air-conditioned rooms include flat-screen satellite TV and a coffee maker.

Unlimited food and both local and international drinks are available from Occidental Nuevo Vallarta's 4 restaurants, which serve Mexican, Mediterranean and international cuisine, and from the snack bar, beach bar and lounge bar.

You can also order your meals to the privacy of your room 24 hours a day. The Occidental Nuevo Vallarta offers daytime and evening activities, including waterpolo, windsurfing and kayaking.

Children from 4 to 12 years old can enjoy games in the supervised kids’ club. The hotel is located just a few meters from Nuevo Vallarta’s main shopping centre.

Puerto Vallarta International Airport is a 25-minute drive away, while central Puerto Vallarta is a 35-minute drive away.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk are happy to organize excursions or arrange car hire.
