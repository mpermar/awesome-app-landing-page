Description

Located within the Riviera Nayarit Complex, Marival Resort & Suites is surrounded by pretty grounds. There is a golf course half a mile away.

Each air-conditioned room and suite at the Marival has bright décor. All accommodations comes with satellite TV and a minibar. Suites also have a sitting area and some come with a microwave.

Mexican, Italian and steak options are offered in the Marival’s 7 restaurants. There is also an international buffet and a themed restaurant, as well as cocktail bar.

The spa features a sauna and a hot tub. The beauty salon offers treatments including massage, facials and manicures.

Guests can use the resort’s flood-lit tennis courts and there are also basketball and volleyball facilities. Water sports are available on the beach, while staff at the 24-hour front desk can arrange horseback riding and bike rental.
