Description

The accommodations at this Nuevo Vallarta property include cable TV and air conditioning. The floors are tiled marble and a dining table is provided.

A gym, game room, and children’s playground are part of the facilities at Samba Vallarta. Meals and snacks are available to guests throughout their stay. Activities for adults and children are planned daily and dance nights are scheduled periodically.

Dowtown Puerto Vallarta is 25 minutes from Samba Vallarta All Inclusive. Ocean Friendly Whale Watching Tours is 10 minutes from the hotel.
