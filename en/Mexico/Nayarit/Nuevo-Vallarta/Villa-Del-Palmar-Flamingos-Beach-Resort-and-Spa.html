Description

Guest rooms at the Flamingos Beach Resort are furnished with a kitchenette and a dining area. A flat-screen TV and an in-room safe are included for added convenience. The large marble bathrooms are stocked with deluxe toiletries and bathrobes.

Dining at the Villa Del Palmar Flamingos includes fine dining at the Mexican El Patron Restaurant and Italian cuisine at Trattoria Restaurant and Pizza Bar. Palmita Market specializes in breakfast pastries, sandwiches and snacks.

Guests can relax in the beachside hot tub and lagoon-style pool. On-site activities at the Beach Resort and Spa Palmar Flamingos include water volleyball, theme shows, golf and bartending classes.
