Description

The five-star Fiesta Americana Aguascalientes is located next to the Feria de San Marcos fair site.

Decorated in colonial style, it has an outdoor swimming pool set in large gardens.

The spacious, air-conditioned rooms in Fiesta Americana Aguascalientes have plasma satellite TV and free Wi-Fi.

All rooms have tea and coffee-making facilities, as well as a private balcony and a minibar.

La Fronda Restaurant offers regional Mexican dishes and seafood, as well as weekly themed nights.

The Antioquia Bar offers international drinks and music, and is open until midnight.

24-hour room service is also available. By the pool there is a sun terrace with loungers.

The hotel also has a fitness centre. Aguascalientes Bull Ring is just 300 metres from the hotel.
