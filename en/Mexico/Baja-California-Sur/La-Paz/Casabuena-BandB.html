Description

At the bed and breakfast, each room has a closet. The rooms include a private bathroom, and some units at Casabuena B&B have a balcony. All rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

A continental or buffet breakfast is available each morning at the property.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool.

Speaking English and Spanish at the reception, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical information on the area.

Manuel Marquez de Leon International Airport is 6 miles from the property.
