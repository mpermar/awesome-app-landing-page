Description

Lagoon-style swimming pools, a spa and gym are offered at this luxury all-inclusive resort, situated on San José del Cabo Beach.

The stylish suites have a balcony overlooking the gardens, pools or sea. Decorated in warm colours, suites at Hyatt Ziva Los Cabos have a spacious seating area.

An iPod dock, flat-screen satellite TV and coffee maker are provided. Bathrooms include bathrobes and slippers.

Hyatt Ziva Los Cabos offers Mexican and international cuisine, including Japanese and Italian restaurants.

The property also offers an Adult only French restaurant. There are swim-up pool bars and an American sports bar, as well as an elegant cigar lounge.

Located in San José’s Golden Zone, the resort is just 2 km from the city centre. Los Cabos Airport is a 20-minute drive away and Cabo San Lucas is 25 minutes away.
