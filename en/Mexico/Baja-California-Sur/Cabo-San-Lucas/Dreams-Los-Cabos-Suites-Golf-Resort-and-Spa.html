Description

This all-suite Dream Los Cabos Suites Golf Resort & Spa features suites with a private terrace overlooking the ocean. Each suite includes a stocked minibar and iPod docking station. The marble bathrooms boast separate bathtubs and showers with slippers and bathrobes provided.

On-site dining includes steaks, seafood, Pan Asian and more. Guests at Dream Los Cabos Suites Resort can also choose from a la carte items and buffets. Room service is available as well.

Dream Los Cabos Suites Resort & Spa offers tennis clinics, cooking classes and cocktail lessons. A seasonal turtle release event allows guests to participate in helping the Caray Green Sea Turtles.
