Description

Set in Los Cabos, Baja California Sur, this resort features a full-service spa and golf course.

Six outdoor pools and 4 outdoor Jacuzzis are available on-site. Every room at the Grand Fiesta Americana Los Cabos All Inclusive Golf & Spa includes a coffee maker and balcony.

Select rooms provide access to the club lounge with free snacks and drinks. During your stay, you will enjoy the Grand All Inclusive concept where you and your family will enjoy a culinary tour where you can savor in any of our different 9 different restaurants, including the best of the Mexican, Asian, Argentinian, Italian and International cuisine and the possibility to taste gastronomic alternatives even 24 hours a day.

The Arch at Lands End is a short drive from the Grand Fiesta Americana Los Cabos All Inclusive Golf & Spa.

Located only 10 minutes from downtown Cabo San Lucas, its world famous marina and a luxurious shopping mall and 30 minutes from the International Airport of San Jose del Cabo.
