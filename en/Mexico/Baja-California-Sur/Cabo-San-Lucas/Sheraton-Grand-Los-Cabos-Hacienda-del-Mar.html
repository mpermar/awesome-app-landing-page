Description

With an exclusive private beachfront location in Cabos San Lucas, and surrounded by lush gardens, this hotel offers first-class accommodations, exceptional services and all the amenities needed for an unforgettable stay.

The Sheraton Grand Los Cabos, Hacienda del Mar boasts a number of modern conveniences and facilities that the entire family can enjoy, including children's programs, a full-service spa, and a knowledgeable concierge desk to help arrange the day's activities and adventures.

The area surrounding the Sheraton Grand Los Cabos, Hacienda del Mar is filled with an endless variety of recreational activities.

Enjoy numerous water sports in one of its 5 swimming pools. Guests can also go fishing, or horseback riding.

Also available are many restaurants, and a vibrant nightlife with 6 different bars at the guests' disposal.
