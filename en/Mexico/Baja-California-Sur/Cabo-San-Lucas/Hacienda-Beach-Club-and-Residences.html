Description

All air-conditioned villas feature an elegant décor with natural stone floors, granite countertops and ceramic tiles. All bedrooms include a flat-screen TV and private bathrooms. The master bedroom includes a spa bathtub and walk-in shower.

Each villa has a furnished balcony with sea views and comes with a dishwasher, washing machine, dryer and wine storage cabinet. The spacious living room comes with a recessed sound system and satellite TV.

Guests can enjoy local and international meals and drinks at Hacienda Cocina y Cantina restaurant. There is also a poolside bar.

Hacienda Beach offers a concierge service, 24-hour security and a tour desk. A shuttle service is also available for an additional fee.
