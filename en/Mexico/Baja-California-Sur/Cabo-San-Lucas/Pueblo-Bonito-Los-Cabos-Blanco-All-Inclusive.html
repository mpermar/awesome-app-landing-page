Description

The suites at Pueblo Bonito Los Cabos include kitchenettes and panoramic views of Land’s End. Each spacious suite provides a private safe and the kitchenettes contain a stove.

Cilantro’s restaurant, located at Blanco Pueblo Bonito Los Cabos, serves fresh seafood and international cuisine. Las Palomas offers Mexican cuisine.

Pueblo Bonito Los Cabos Blanco is 6 blocks from downtown Cabo San Lucas. Guests have access to the facilities located at the partner resorts with free shuttle services available. Golfing is nearby.
