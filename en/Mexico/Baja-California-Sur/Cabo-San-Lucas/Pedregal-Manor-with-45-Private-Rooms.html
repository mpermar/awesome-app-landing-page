Description

Just 900 metres from Cabo San Lucas Main Dock, Cabo Pedregal Hotel offers its guests an outdoor pool, 24-hour surveillance services and is less than 10 minutes’ walk from the area’s main attractions.

The rooms are fully appointed and have balconies where guests will be able to enjoy stunning views of the surroundings.

The full bathroom includes free toiletries and some of the available amenities include a cosy seating area.

Cabo Pedregal Hotel also includes ticket services, a tour desk and luggage storage.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling.

The property offers free parking. Several renowned restaurants are located about 10 minutes’ walk from Cabo Pedregal Hotel.
