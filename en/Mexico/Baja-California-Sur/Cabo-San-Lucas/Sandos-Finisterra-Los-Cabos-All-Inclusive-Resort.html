Description

The rooms at Sandos Finisterra offer views of the bay or ocean. Each room features cable TV, a mini-bar, a seating area and a spacious private bathroom with a hairdryer and toiletries.

The resort has 3 specialty restaurants and a buffet restaurant with panoramic city views. There are also 6 bars and a café.

The resort has a sauna, a hydrotherapy area and a gym with views of the city. Massages and beauty treatments are available for an extra cost.

The property offers 2 tennis courts, a kids' club and a range of activities. You can organize activities such as golf, scuba diving and sport-fishing trips, as well as boat rides in the day and night.

San Lucas del Cabo is a great place to go whale watching, and the golf course is just 10 minutes’ drive away. You can drive to Los Cabos Airport in around 45 minutes.
