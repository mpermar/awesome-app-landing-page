Description

At the resort, rooms have a closet, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Every room comes with a coffee machine, while some have a terrace. All rooms will provide guests with a dishwasher.

The on-site restaurant specializes in international cuisine.

Speaking English and Spanish, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical information on the area at the reception.

El Arco is 7 miles from Grand Solmar at Rancho San Lucas Resort, Golf & Spa. The nearest airport is Los Cabos Airport, 24 miles from the property.
