Description

Hotel Quinta Paraiso offers a free Wi-Fi zone, attractive gardens and a large outdoor pool with a slide.

Free parking is available on site, and Mexico City is an hour’s drive away. Each bright, functional room at Hotel Quinta Paraíso has traditional décor.

Rooms include a TV, a ceiling fan and mosquito nets, and all rooms have a private bathroom.

The Quinta Paraiso is situated in the town of Cocoyoc, just a 5-minute drive from Oaxtepec Water Park.
