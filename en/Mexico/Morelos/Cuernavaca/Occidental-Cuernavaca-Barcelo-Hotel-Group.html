Description

Set near downtown Cuernavaca, the capital of Morelos, surrounded by cultural and historic attractions, this hotel offers relaxing facilities and comfortable guestrooms with modern amenities.

The guestrooms at Occidental Cuernavaca offer amenities such as Cable TV and wireless internet access.

Guests will also appreciate the well-stocked minibar and spacious balcony. Occidental Cuernavaca features a heated outdoor swimming pool as well as a relaxing solarium.

After working out in the fitness center, guests can enjoy a meal in the on-site restaurant or a drink in the lounge.
