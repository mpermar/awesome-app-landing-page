Description

At the hotel, all rooms are equipped with a closet and a TV.

The daily breakfast offers continental, buffet or à la carte options.

Hotel Jacarandas has a year-round outdoor pool. You can play tennis at the accommodation.

The reception can offer helpful tips for getting around the area.

Robert Brady Museum is 1.4 miles from Hotel Jacarandas. The nearest airport is Benito Juarez International Airport, 37 miles from the property.
