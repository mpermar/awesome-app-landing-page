Description

Offering an outdoor pool, a 24-hour restaurant, free Wi-Fi and a garden with a children’s playground, Hotel San Carlos is located between Yautepec and Oaxtepec.

Cuernavaca is a 30-minute drive away. The rooms here will provide you with a cable TV, a wardrobe, a fan and a private bathroom with a shower.

Some rooms also have a spa bath, while bungalows include kitchen facilities. Hotel San Carlos offers a shared lounge and a games room with a pool table and ping pong table.

Football field, 24-hour front desk vending machine and grocery deliveries. The property offers free parking.

Tepoztlán and the Tepozteco National Park are within 15 km, while Mexico City is within 80 km.
