Description

This beachfront hotel features 4 outdoor swimming pools and a fitness centre. Most of the hotel’s rooms offer ocean views.

There is a golf course 30 minutes' drive away. All rooms at HS HOTSSON Smart Acapulco feature an elegant décor with contemporary furnishings.

Each room includes a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a telephone and ironing facilities.

The private bathroom with shower includes free toiletries and a hairdryer. Guests can enjoy Mexican or international food at one of the hotel’s restaurants.

The poolside bar serves snacks and drinks, and live music is played in the Lobby Bar every Friday and Saturday night.

There is a tour desk, travel agents’ and currency exchange desk at the hotel. Baby-sitting services and grocery shopping services are also available.

HS HOTSSON Smart Acapulco is a 10-minute drive from Acapulco’s zócalo main square.
