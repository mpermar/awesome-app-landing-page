Description

Offering an outdoor pool and pool bar A&V Hotel Boutique is set in Acapulco in the Guerrero Region, 19 km from Acapulco Convention Center.

The hotel has a terrace and views of the sea, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

Free WiFi is offered throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Certain rooms have a seating area for your convenience. A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms.

All rooms include a private bathroom. You will find a 24-hour front desk. This hotel has a private beach area and bike hire is available.

A number of activities are offered in the area, such as skiing and cycling. Acapulco Navy Historic Museum is 19 km from A&V Hotel Boutique, while Chapel of Peace is 20 km from the property.

The nearest airport is General Juan N Alvarez International Airport, 33 km from A&V Hotel Boutique.
