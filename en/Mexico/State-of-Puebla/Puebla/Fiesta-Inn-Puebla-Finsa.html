Description

Fiesta Inn Puebla is surrounded by golf courses and hiking opportunities. Guests can have fun at the Valle Fantástico Amusement Park or explore the African Safari Zoo. The Cholula archeological site is also nearby.

Guests will enjoy a daily breakfast buffet with regional dishes at Puebla Fiesta Inn. Cafe La Fiesta, located on-site, also offers lunch and dinner menus. After a busy day, visitors can relax with a drink at the casual Lobby Bar.
