Description

Featuring a pool with crystal front, this stylish hotel includes a rooftop hot tub with panoramic views.

Hotel & Spa Quinta Las Alondras is located on the outskirts of Guanajuato. The rooms at Quinta Las Alondras feature a mix of Mediterranean and Mexican décor.

Each comes with a flat-screen TV and a balcony, as well as air-conditioning and free Wi-Fi.

Room service is available. This luxury hotel features a unique winery located on subterranean caves, while the restaurant serves Italian specialities and international fine wines.

The colonial centre of Guanajuato is 10 minutes’ drive from Quinta Las Alondras. The hotel can be reached through the Federal 110D motorway.
