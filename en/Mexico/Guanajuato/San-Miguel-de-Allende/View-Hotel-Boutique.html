Description

Offering an outdoor swimming pool and a hot tub, View Hotel Boutique is located 9 km from San Miguel de Allende Historic Centre.

Free Wi-Fi access is available in all areas. The rooms and suites offer a flat-screen TV, cable channels and a terrace.

Featuring a hairdryer, the private bathrooms also come with free toiletries and towels.

You can enjoy a lake view and a mountain view from all the rooms. At View Hotel Boutique you will find a 24-hour front desk, free parking and a garden.

Other facilities offered include a tour desk, luggage storage and an ironing service.

The property offers an a la cárte menu. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling and hiking.

This property is 8. 8 km from Juárez Park and 10 km from San Miguel de Allende Historic Museum.
