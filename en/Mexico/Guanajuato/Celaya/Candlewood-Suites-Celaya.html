Description

All rooms has air conditioning, a dishwasher, a microwave, a coffee machine, a bath or shower, free toiletries and a desk. Some rooms come with a kitchen with a fridge. All guest rooms feature a closet.

The hotel also provides a business center and guests can use the on-site ATM machine at Candlewood Suites - Celaya.

San Miguel de Allende is 27 miles from the accommodation, while Querétaro is 28 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Quétaro Intercontinental Airport, 41 miles from the property.
