Description

The hotel spa offers a variety of massages and beauty treatments, including a hot stone massage and a hydrating Maya Mud body wrap.

Rooms at Villa Montaña Hotel & Spa are decorated in an elegant rural style. They feature tile floors and Mexican and European art and antiques. All rooms feature a fireplace and come with a TV. The private bathrooms include a bathrobe and hairdryer.

Villa Montaña’s Sunday breakfast includes a variety of Michoacan delicacies. The terrace bar-restaurant offers panoramic views of Morelia. It is open for lunch and evening cocktails.

The hotel is about a 10-minute drive from the historic center of Morelia. The orchid museum, planetarium and Convention Centre are 1.5 miles away. The International Airport is 19 miles away.
