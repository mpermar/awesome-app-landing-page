Description

Located on the western coast of Mexico, this oceanfront all-inclusive resort in Manzanillo features 6 outdoor pools with a waterfall and 4 restaurants.

All suites include a balcony and panoramic views. Surrounded by lush tropical gardens, Barcelo Karmina - All Inclusive has Mayan-styled architecture resembling authentic temples.

A variety of leisure facilities are available, including a spa with massage services.

For recreation, Karmina Palace has tennis courts, beach volley ball, as well as a fitness center.

Water sports are also offered. Guests can enjoy a variety of cuisine at Barcelo Karmina, such as traditional Mexican dishes at Agave; Italian served at Capri and sushi specialties at Kyoto.

The hotel also features 5 bars and a nightclub with live music. Each suite is bright and airy with marble floors and a seating area.

A dining area and a stocked minibar are also provided. Two satellite TVs are included in every suite.
