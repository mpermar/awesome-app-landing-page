Description

Satellite TV and coffee-making facilities are included in the guest rooms at The Buccaneer. Each has a private balcony.

Open-air dining is available at The Terrace, serving classic cuisine. The Martel Lounge features cocktails and The Mermaid is a beachside restaurant with American favorites. Breakfast is included in the rate.
