Description

Guest rooms at the hotel come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a bidet. Every room is equipped with air conditioning, and some rooms have a terrace. All rooms feature a desk.

A continental or à la carte breakfast is available daily at the property. The restaurant at Hotel Ashrey specializes in Indian cuisine.

The accommodation has a playground. Guests at Hotel Ashrey will be able to enjoy activities in and around Dehradun, like cycling.

There's a kids' club for the children and guests can use of the business center.

Indian Military Academy is 4.1 miles from the hotel. Dehradun Airport is 12 miles away.
