Description

Located just 500 metres from banks of the mighty Ganges river and the famous Lakshman Jhula (bridge), Dewa Retreat offers a 24-hour front desk, free Wi-Fi and features an outdoor swimming pool on property.

The hotel is located 22 km away from Jolly Grant Airport. The Rishikesh Railway Station is 5 km away.

The hotel offers car rental facilities and a tour desk for those looking to explore the surrounding areas.

Guests can also choose to work out with fitness centre or take a leisurely stroll in the garden.

A meeting and banquet centre is also available. Each air-conditioned room features a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, spacious wardrobe, desk and seating space.

There is also an electric kettle with tea and coffee provisions. The attached bathroom has shower facilities.

You can enjoy views of the Himalayas from the rooms. Sattvik, the in-house restaurant, serves Indian, Chinese and Continental cuisines while Pure Soul Cafe and Organic Kitchen offers healthy organic wellness menu.
