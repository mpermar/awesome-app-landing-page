Description

Offering an indoor swimming pool, a spa and wellness centre, L'Occitane and a fitness centre, JW Marriott Mussoorie Walnut Grove Resort & Spa is located in Mussoorie.

Guests can enjoy a game of bowling and billiards. The property has a 12,000 sq. feet kid’s club/entertainment area.

Each elegant air-conditioned room here will provide you with a satellite TV, a seating area and a balcony.

Featuring a shower and a bath tub in a private bathroom which comes with a bath robe and a hairdryer.

At JW Marriott Mussoorie Walnut Grove Resort & Spa you will find a sauna and massage parlour.

Other facilities offered at the property include entertainment staff, a games room and a ticket service.

The property offers free parking. Guests can experience activities like a hilltop-outdoor adventure activity area, putting green for golf lovers, amphitheater for showcasing cultural activities and hosting karaoke nights, JW farm lunches, picnics, trekking trails, a green house and an in-house souvenir shop.

The all-day dining restaurant, JW Cafe is multi-cuisine, Teppan serves Asian while Wisteria Deck serves Italian.

Trout House Grill and Bar serves grilled delights while Perch is a tea lounge. Refreshing beverages are served at the poolside bar.

The hotel is 6 km from Kempty Fall, 9 km from Gun Hill Vintage Point and 30 km from Dhanaulti.

The Library Bus Station is 8 km and the Dheradun Railway Station is 39 km. The Jolly Grant Airport is 62 km away while the Delhi International Airport is 292 km.
