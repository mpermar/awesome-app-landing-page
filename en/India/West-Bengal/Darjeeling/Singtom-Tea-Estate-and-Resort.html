Description

Built in 1862 and spread over 1600 acres of private estate, Singtom Tea Estate & Resort is located in the scenic locales of Darjeeling.

The famous Darjeeling Mall is 4 km away. The accommodation will provide you with a seating area.

Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with free toiletries. You can enjoy garden view from the room.

Extras include a fireplace, a sofa and a desk. At Singtom, you will find a garden and a kitchen.

Other facilities offered at the property include a shared lounge, a games room and a ticket service.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including hiking and badminton.

The property offers free parking. The in-house restaurant serves vegetarian Indian, Chinese and continental delicacies.

Room service can be requested for in-room dining comforts. This resort is 12 km from the Tiger Hill.

It is 5 km from Darjeeling Bus Station, 9 km from Darjeeling Railway Station and it is 72 km from Bagdogra Airport.
