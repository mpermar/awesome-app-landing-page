Description

Situated in Banjara Hills, Park Hyatt Hyderabad boasts 4 dining options, a spa and a fitness centre.

Its luxurious rooms feature a Nespresso machine and a walk-in closet. Guests can also expect 24-hour room, concierge and laundry services.

Spacious contemporary rooms feature non-allergenic bedding, an iPod dock and a laptop safe.

They offer a flat-screen TV with cable channels, ironing facilities and a minibar.

The large bathroom comes with a bathtub, oversized rainshower and a hairdryer. The all-day restaurant, The Dining Room, offers European cuisine and authentic Indian delights from a show kitchen.

Tre-Forni Restaurant and Bar serves northern Italian specialities, accompanied with refreshing drinks from Italian aperitifs, to cocktails and wines.

Oriental Bar & Kitchen offers East Asian dishes and a selection of whiskeys and cognacs, while The Living Room serves light refreshments, tea, wines and champagnes.

Park Hyatt is 6 km from Nampally Railway Station and 8 km from Hi-Tech City and Hyderabad Golf Course.

It is about 9 km from Hyderabad Convention Centre and 40 minutes from Hyderabad International Airport.

Guests can avail a chauffeur-driven BMW 7 series limousine service at an additional cost with free WiFi, bottled water, shoe mitts, newspaper, cold towel and choice of music.
