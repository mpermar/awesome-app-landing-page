Description

The rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, fridge, a electric tea pot, a shower, free toiletries and a closet. The resort has some units with pool views, and the rooms come with a balcony. At The Ocean Pearl Resort And Spa rooms are equipped with a seating area.

Breakfast is available each morning, and includes continental and buffet options.

Guests can use the hot tub. You can play ping pong at the accommodation, and car rental is available.

The Ocean Pearl Resort And Spa also provides a business center and guests can check the newspapers at the resort. Speaking English and Hindi at the reception, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical information on the area.
