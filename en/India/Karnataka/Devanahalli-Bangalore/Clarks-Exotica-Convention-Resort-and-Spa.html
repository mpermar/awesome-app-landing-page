Description

Spread over 70 acres of greenery, Clarks Exotica has an outdoor swimming pool, pampering spa treatments, a tennis court and a well-equipped gym.

This 5-star property in Bangalore also has air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi and a kitchenette.

The well-designed and spacious guestrooms all come with a personal safe and minibar.

Some rooms have a flat-screen TV. En suite bathrooms are equipped with either a bathtub or shower.

Clarks Exotica is a 15 minutes' drive from Bengaluru International Airport and a 30 minutes' drive from Bangalore City Centre.

On-site parking is free. The resort has three dining options, Ambrosia, Illusion and Buvette which are framed by spectacular views of the pool.

The resort also provides a business centre and car rentals. Day trips can be arranged at the tour desk.
