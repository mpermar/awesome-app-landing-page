Description

Rooms at Silent Shores come with modern décor and hardwood floors. Each room has a flat-screen TV and tea/coffee maker. Shower facilities and a hairdryer are provided.

Guests can work out at the fitness center or enjoy a relaxing body massage at the spa. The resort also has a racquetball court, game room and provides water sports facilities.

Ambrosia restaurant serves a variety of international dishes, while refreshing beverages are available at Amphora bar.

Silent Shores Resort offers free parking, and is 7 miles from Mysore Zoo. Mysore Airport is 12 miles from the resort.
