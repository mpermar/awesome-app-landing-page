Description

Featuring easy access to VR Bengaluru mall, The Waverly Hotel & Residences offers accommodation in Bangalore.

The hotel has Bounce, an in-house spa and a fitness centre and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Free private parking is available on site. Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and is fitted with a 42 inch LED TV with cable channels.

Certain rooms have a seating area for your convenience. Each room is fitted with a private bathroom.

For your comfort, you will find bath robes, slippers and free toiletries. There is a 24-hour front desk, a cash machine and hairdresser's at the property.

Located in the IT corridor in Whitefield, it is located 40 km from Kempegowda International Airport and 10 km from the Central Business District.

The Waverly offers facilities such as the SkyDeck, an outdoor jogging track, an open-air temperature-controlled pool, a yoga deck and a fitness centre.

The Hotel provides dining options like Alt – a rooftop lounge & bar while The Whitefield Arms is a a microbrewery & pub, Toscano a fine dine Italian restaurant.
