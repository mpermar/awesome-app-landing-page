Description

Located in Bangalore, 1. 7 km from Commercial Street, Radisson Bengaluru City Center provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre.

3. 4 km from Chinnaswamy Stadium and 3. 5 km from Brigade Road, the property offers a bar and a shared lounge.

The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.

Guest rooms are equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kettle, a shower, a hairdryer and a desk.

At the hotel each room comes with a wardrobe and a private bathroom. Guests at Radisson Bengaluru City Center can enjoy a continental breakfast.

The accommodation offers a terrace. Visvesvaraya Industrial and Technological Museum is 4.

4 km from Radisson Bengaluru City Center, while Cubbon Park is 5 km away. The nearest airport is Kempegowda International, 37 km from the hotel, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
