Description

Evolve Back Coorg is 68.4 miles from Mysore Airport and 99.4 miles from Ooty’s hill station. Bangalore International Airport is 170.9 miles away.

Cottages and villas feature a flat-screen TV, a fridge and an electric kettle. Private bathrooms come with hot water facilities. Select villas offer a private plunge pool and a sit out.

Dining options include the Granary multi-cuisine restaurant, or Pepper Corn – Barbeques & Grills Restaurant and Plantain Leaf, which offers pure vegetarian delights. Hunter’s Lodge features refreshing drinks.
