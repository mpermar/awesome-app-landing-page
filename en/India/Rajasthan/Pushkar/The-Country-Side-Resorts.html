Description

Located in the holy city of Pushkar, Country Side Resorts offers rooms equipped with a private bathroom.

Just a 10 minutes walk from Pushkar Lake, it has a restaurant and features camel safaris, which come with a guide.

The air-conditioned rooms include a seating area, dining table and en suite bathroom with shower area, hot/cold running water and toilet.

A study table and newspapers are available on request. Guests can hire a taxi service in the form of a horse ride or practice yoga.
