Description

Featuring intricately carved Haveli-style architecture, Raas Jodhpur, a boutique hotel houses a spa, an outdoor pool, and 2 dining options, including a restaurant overlooking the magnificent Mehrangarh Fort.

It also offers 24-hour room service and complimentary WiFi access. The property is 500 metres from Clock Tower and 2 km from Mehrangarh Fort and Jodhpur Railway Station.

Jodhpur Airport is 7 km away. Fitted with a balcony, the contemporary air-conditioned rooms feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a wardrobe and a seating area.

The stylish bathroom comes with shower facilities. Darikhana offers meals, accompanied with splendid views of the Mehrangarh Fort from its terrace.

Offering international delights, Baradari Restaurant serves the daily breakfast and lunch, with Thai and Mediterranean speciality dishes.

Guests can head to Raas’ 24-hour reception for assistance with use of meeting facilities, or laundry and car rental services.

Travel arrangements can be made at the tour desk. A pampering spa can be enjoyed at RAAS spa.
