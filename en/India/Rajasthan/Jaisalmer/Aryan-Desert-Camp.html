Description

All guest rooms at the resort come with a seating area. Each room is equipped with a private bathroom. All rooms at Aryan Desert Camp feature air conditioning and a closet.

A vegetarian breakfast is served daily at the property. At the accommodation you'll find a restaurant serving Indian cuisine.

Aryan Desert Camp has a playground.

For guests' convenience, the resort has a business center with printing, copying and fax services. Staff at the 24-hour front desk can give tips on the area.

Bara Baag is 3.9 miles from Aryan Desert Camp, while Kuldhara Village is 10 miles away.
