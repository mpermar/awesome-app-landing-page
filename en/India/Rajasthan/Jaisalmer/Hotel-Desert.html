Description

Featuring free WiFi, a restaurant and a terrace, Hotel Desert offers accommodation in Jaisalmer, a minutes walk from Jaisalmer Fort.

Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. A TV with cable channels, as well as a computer are offered.

Certain rooms have a seating area where you can relax. You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property.

The hotel also offers car hire. Patwon Ki Haveli is 400 metres from Hotel Desert, while Lake Gadisar is 1.
