Description

Situated in Jaipur, JW Marriott Jaipur Resort & Spa is 8 km from Seesh Mahal and offers facilities like fitness centre, swimming pool, 24-hour front desk and tour desk.

Dry-cleaning, laundry and ironing services are available. The property provides on-site parking and free WiFi access in the public areas.

All air-conditioned rooms boast a 49 inch LED TV, tea and coffee making facilities and 4 fixture bathrooms.

The room comes with minibar, iPod docking station and safety deposit box. 24 hour in-room dining is available.

Spa by JW offers a variety of experiences, from express treatments to full-service indulgences.

The treatments are tailored to your unique needs, and focus on delivering four distinctive benefits — Calm, Indulge, Invigorate and Renew.

Sukh Mahal is an all-day dining restaurant serving international and local cuisine with an interactive live kitchen concept to suit every palate.

Architecturally inspired from the Sheesh Mahal at Amer Fort in Jaipur, Mohan Mahal is lit up only with candles and its mirror décor lends an atmosphere of refined elegance.

Jharokha is a perfect combination of a lounge and bar offering array of coffees and cocktails Overlooking the Aravalli Hills, Hawa Mahal, the rooftop restaurant offers a distinctive ‘Under the Stars’ dining experience.

It is an ideal place to enjoy the weather and scrumptious grills along with your family and friends.

Jaigarh Fort is 9 km from JW Marriott Jaipur Resort & Spa, while Jalmahal is 12 km from the property.
