Description

Featuring a private bathroom, rooms at the guesthouse also offer a garden view.

Jaipur Sundeck hostel offers a continental or Full English/Irish breakfast.

The accommodation has a playground. The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental and car rental are available at Jaipur Sundeck hostel.

Staff are available to help at the 24-hour front desk.
