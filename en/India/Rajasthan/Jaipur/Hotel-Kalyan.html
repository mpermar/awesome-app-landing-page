Description

Featuring a beautiful rooftop restaurant that offers live screenings of matches in the comfort of a gentle misting system and 360 degree panoramic view of the city, Hotel Kalyan is centrally located in the Pink City of Jaipur.

It is just 1 km from Jaipur Railway Station and the Central Bus Stand. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

Fitted with tiled flooring, rooms are equipped with a desk, cable TV and tea/coffee making facilities.

Private bathrooms offer a hairdryer, hot shower facilities and free toiletries. Hotel Kalyan is 4.

1 km from Jantar Mantar Observatory and 4. 4 km Hawa Mahal. It is 12. 6 km from Jaipur Airport and Amber Fort.

Day trips and car rentals can be arranged at the tour desk. The 24-hour front desk provides laundry services and fax facilities.

A 24-hour back-up power generator is available. Both local and international dishes can be enjoyed at the rooftop restaurant and the main dining hall.
