Description

Four Points By Sheraton Jaipur, City Square is located in Jaipur’s city centre, 5.

3 km from Jaipur Airport. It features an outdoor swimming pool, restaurant and rooms with a flat-screen TV.

Modern rooms at Four Points By Sheraton feature floor-to-ceiling windows and light wood furnishings.

All rooms are well-equipped with a safety deposit box and minibar. Hot shower facilities are also provided.

Guests can work out at the fitness centre or enjoy a relaxing massage at the spa.

For convenience, the hotel staff can assist with business and tour services. A well-equipped business centre is available.

The Eatery offers a buffet spread of local and Western dishes. Light snacks and refreshing cocktails are available at Best Brews.
