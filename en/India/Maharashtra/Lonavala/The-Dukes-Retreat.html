Description

Dukes Retreat is 3 miles from Lonavala Train station. The nearest airport is in Pune, about 37 miles from the hotel.

Fitted with a fan, rooms at Dukes Retreat feature floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the mountains and beyond. Each is equipped with a flat-screen cable/satellite TV and minibar. A comfortable sofa sitting area is also included.

Guests can work out in the fitness center or play a game of tennis on the on-site courts. The hotel also provides a helpful tour desk and business center.

Panaroma Café serves a variety of international dishes and local cuisine. Cocktails and liquor can be enjoyed in High Point Bar.

Khandala is a great choice for travelers interested in family-friendly trips, adventures and mountains.
