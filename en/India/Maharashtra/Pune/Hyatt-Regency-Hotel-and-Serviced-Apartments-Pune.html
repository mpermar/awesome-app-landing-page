Description

The 5-star Hyatt Regency Pune offers a luxurious experience with spacious rooms featuring flat-screen TVs and free Wi-Fi.

Located on Nagar Road in the Weikfield IT Park, it hosts the Arogya Spa & Fitness Centre and an indoor heated pool.

Lohegaon Airport is just 2. 5 km away. Phoenix Market City Mall is 500 metres away while nearby attractions include the Aga Khan Palace, which is less than 1.

3 km from the hotel. EON Special Economic Zone, Koregaon Park and Pune Golf Course are within 4.

5 km of the property. Magarpatta IT City is 8. 5 km while Hinjewadi IT Park is 20 km from Hyatt Regency Pune.

Hyatt Regency Pune’s rooms are equipped with an iPod dock, minibar and laptop safe.

Satellite channels can be viewed on a 55-inch smart TV. A coffee maker and 24-hour room service are available.

Arogya Spa offers Ayurvedic treatments, steam and sauna rooms. Other recreation facilities include a yoga room and shopping arcade.

Zeta is the casual venue that offers a clock-less dining experience. The hotel’s bar and lounge features malts, beers, wines and cocktails.
