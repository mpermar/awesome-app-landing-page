Description

Offering an outdoor swimming pool, a fitness centre and 3 dining options, Crowne Plaza Pune City Centre is situated near Koregaon Park 7 km from Lohegaon Airport and 37 metres from Jahangir Hospital.

Poonawalla Business bay is 3 km away. The modern and refreshing rooms at this Crowne Plaza City Centre hotel have a flat-screen TV, iPod dock and rain shower.

The spacious and trendy rooms at Crowne Plaza Pune City Centre feature a glass-walled bath area with premium toiletries and ergonomic workspace.

They come with a tea/coffee maker, hairdryer, iron and iron table and over-sized windows.

Ruby Hall Clinic is 20 metres away. The hotel is less than 1 km from MSRTC Bus Depot Pune Station and Pune Railway Station.

Babasaheb Ambedkar Museum and Aga Khan Palace are both 4 km away. Mosaic is a multi-cuisine restaurant.

For a romantic dinner out or a business meeting over dinner, one can head to the rooftop Evviva Sky Lounge that boasts of a panoramic view of the city.

The hotel also offers 24 hours room service, daily newspapers and laundry service.
