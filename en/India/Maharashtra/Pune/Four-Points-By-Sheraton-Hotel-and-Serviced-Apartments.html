Description

Four Points by Sheraton Pune is 650 feet from Phenix Market City.

Modern rooms come fitted with flat-screen cable TVs and ironing facilities. A mini-bar and tea and coffee making facilities are provided. En suite, marble bathrooms have toiletries provided.

Car rental and currency exchange services are available. For business services, guests can head to the business center. The hotel also provides laundry and dry cleaning services.

Asian cuisine can be enjoyed at Panash Restaurant while The Eatery specializes in international dishes throughout the day. A variety of drinks are served at The Best Brew.
