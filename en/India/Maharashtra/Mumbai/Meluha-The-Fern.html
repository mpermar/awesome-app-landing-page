Description

The Fern is 3.1 mi from Andheri Railway Station.

Pleasant rooms at Meluha Fern are fitted with flat-screen TVs and laptop safe boxes. Mini-bars and tea/coffee makers are provided. Private bathrooms offer toiletries and hairdryers.

Guests can visit the business center for their communication needs. Car rental services are available for those seeking to take a drive. Laundry and dry cleaning services are also provided in the hotel.

A variety of international cuisine can be enjoyed at Tiara Restaurant. Drinks are served in the bar.

Powai is a great choice for travelers interested in nightlife, street food and city trips.
