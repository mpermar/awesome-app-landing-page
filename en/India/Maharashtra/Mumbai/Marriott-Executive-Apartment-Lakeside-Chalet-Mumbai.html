Description

Marriott Executive Apartments offers pet friendly accommodation with splendid lake or hill views.

It features an outdoor pool and a kids pool. Laundromat use is free. Each spacious apartment features living and dining areas and a bathroom with shower cubicles, hairdryer and bidet.

They are fitted with cable TV and a DVD player. The kitchen boasts a convection oven, microwave and a dishwasher.

Marriott Executive Apartments Mumbai is within 6 km of Kanjurmarg Train Station and 7.

6 km from Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport. Guests can go for a run along the jogging track, exercise in the gym or relax at the spa.

Yoga sessions can be arranged. Other facilities include a kids’ play area and beauty salon.

Crimson offers all-day dining of international dishes, while Emperor’s Court serves a range of Asian dishes.

Italian cuisine can be found at Fratelli Fresh and Indian food is available at Nawab Saheb.
