Description

Rest and relax in the spacious air-conditioned rooms, all equipped with soundproof walls, hardwood floors and a flat-screen TV. A well-stocked minibar and a personal safe are also included. The private bathrooms include a bathtub and soft bathrobes.

The Vivanta by Taj-Surajkund, NCR is 11 miles from the center of New Delhi. It is a 45-minute drive from Indira Gandhi International and Domestic Airports.

Spread over 15,500 square feet, The Spa offers luxurious spa treatments. Guests can exercise at the fitness center, or check email at the business center. Daily newspapers and laundry services are also available.

Oasis Restaurant features 3 interactive kitchens, which serve delicious Italian, Chinese and Indian cuisines. The Sunday weekend brunch includes free flow beverages and desserts. The well-stocked wine collection has over 500 selected wines. Central Courtyard provides an outdoor dining experience.
