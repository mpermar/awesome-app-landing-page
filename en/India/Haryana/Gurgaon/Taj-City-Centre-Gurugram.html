Description

Located 100 metres away from Huda City Centre Metro Station, Taj City Centre Gurugram offers luxurious accommodation, a swimming pool and 5 dining options.

It is within walking distance from the central business district and recreational facilities like the Kingdom of Dreams and DLF Golf Course.

Air-conditioned rooms feature light wood furnishings, as well as blue and cream accents.

They feature flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a desk and safety deposit box.

The en suite bathroom offers a shower and free toiletries. Select rooms offer a seating area.

24-hour room service is available. Guests can maintain their exercise regimen at the fitness centre or relax with a swim.

Other facilities at Taj City Centre Gurugram include a business centre and concierge.

Laundry and currency exchange services are also available. Delicious all-day dining is available at Latitude, which serves European, Mediterranean and Asian dishes with Haryana specialities.

Thai food is served at Thai Pavilion and Indian, Western and Pan Asian grilled food can be enjoyed outdoors at Alfresco, which is linked to the bar, Tease.

Coffee, tea and freshly baked cakes are offered at Deli 4. Taj City Centre Gurugram is 1 km from Gurgaon Bus Station and 10 km from Gurgaon Railway Station.
