Description

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool and complimentary WiFi, Hyatt Regency Gurgaon is located on Expressway NH8, which connects Delhi, Gurgaon and Jaipur.

With easy access from Southern periphery road and Dwarka express highway, Manesar and Bhiwadi are 9 km and 32 km away respectively.

Bilaspur is 1-hour drive away. The property is at a 15-miunte drive from Cyber City.

The Rockland Hospital is 5. 4 km while the Karma Lakelands Golf is 4. 5 km away. This luxurious property is situated just 1 km from Aapno Ghar Amusement and Waterpark and Indira Gandhi International Airport is 40-minute drive away.

The Medanta is 14. 2 km. Elegantly furnished rooms are fitted with a 42-inch LED satellite TV, a plush seating area and a writing table.

The en suite bathroom comes with a rain shower cubicle, bathtub and free bath amenities.

Free WiFi access is available. Guests at Hyatt Regency can enjoy refreshing workouts at the 24-hour gym and utilize the business centre.

Other facilities include a spa 24-hour front desk, car rental services and airport shuttle services at a surcharge.

Dining options include a mult-cuisine restaurant Kitchen District. An eclectic collection of spirits, wines and other beverages are available at The Long Bar while snacks and pastries are available at The Lounge.
