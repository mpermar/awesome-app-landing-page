Description

All rooms in the resort are equipped with a TV. Vatika Resort provides some units that feature mountain views, and every room comes with a private bathroom with a shower.

The daily breakfast offers continental, Asian or vegetarian options. There's an in-house restaurant, which serves Indian and Asian cuisine.

The accommodation has a sun terrace.

Vatika Resort provides an ironing service, as well as business facilities like fax and photocopying. Around-the-clock assistance is available at the reception, where staff speak English and Hindi.

Circular Road is 5 miles from the resort, while The Ridge, Shimla is 5 miles from the property. Chandigarh Airport is 32 miles away.
