Description

Set in Dharampur Kasauli, 60 km from Shimla, Jungle Lodge Resort features free WiFi access and free private parking.

The resort has a barbecue and views of the mountain, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

The rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Some rooms include a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms. All rooms include a private bathroom.

For your comfort, you will find slippers, free toiletries and a hairdryer. You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property.

You can play darts at the resort. The resort also offers car hire. The nearest airport is Chandigarh Airport, 35 km from Jungle Lodge Resort.
