Description

Set in Dharamshala, Prakriti Aalay has a garden, a terrace and a shared lounge. Featuring a restaurant, the property is located within 6 km from HPCA Stadium.

The property offers a 24-hour front desk and free WiFi is available. At the resort, rooms are fitted with a wardrobe.

With a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower and free toiletries, rooms at Prakriti Aalay also feature a mountain view.

A buffet, Asian or vegetarian breakfast can be enjoyed at the property. The accommodation offers a children's playground.

You can play darts at this 4-star resort, and bike hire and car hire are available.
