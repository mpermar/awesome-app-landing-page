Description

At the resort, each room is equipped with a balcony. Camp Lungta provides certain units with mountain views, and all rooms have a private bathroom. The rooms are equipped with a seating area.

A continental or Asian breakfast is available every morning at the property. At the accommodation you'll find a restaurant serving Indian and Chinese cuisine.

Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near Camp Lungta.

HPCA Stadium is 3.4 miles from the resort. The nearest airport is Kangra Airport, 7 miles from Camp Lungta.

Experiences

06/29/2018

If you are in Dharamshala, this is a must do.

"If you are in Dharamshala, this is a must do.It has an amazing location and the Dholadhar range is right in your face. The continuous flow of the river is soothing to the ears. The mountains are so imposing and beautiful that you are awestruck by the shear mass and magnanimity. You can sit and gaze at the mountains all day or buckle up for an early morning trek which is equally exciting. The views change every 100 feet and you cross waterfalls and can go all the way up to the slate mines.Kids will love this adventure. The accommodation is adequate from a camp perspective and the food is also good. The staff is quite helpful. Please don’t follow google maps as it takes you to a completely opposite direction. Please stop at Aghanjar Mahadev Temple and wait for directions or pick up. Your car will be parked away from property and you will be taken in a bolero in a crazy bumpy ride much enjoyed by kids."
