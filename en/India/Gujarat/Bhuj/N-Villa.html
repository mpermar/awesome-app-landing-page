Description

A terrace with mountain views is offered in every unit.

Buffet and Asian breakfast options are available every morning at the homestay. At N Villa you'll find a restaurant serving Grill/bbq cuisine. A vegetarian option can also be requested.

At the accommodation guests are welcome to use a hot tub and a fitness room. A business center and a bicycle rental service are also offered by N Villa.

An outdoor swimming pool is available on site and cycling can be enjoyed within close proximity of the property.
