Description

Novotel Ahmedabad – a 5 star contemporary business hotel located in the central business district of Ahmedabad on S G Highway with easy access to Gandhinagar, Sanand, Changodar industrial area and 15 minute drive to the city centre.

In addition to a full-service spa, Novotel Ahmedabad features an outdoor pool and a 24/7 fitness centre- ‘In Balance’.

The hotel is a 30 minute drive away from Sardar Vallabhai Patel International Airport.

The Sanand industrial belt is located 17 km away from Novotel Ahmedabad. Gandhi Nagar & Mahatma Mandir are 27.

5 km away. Each contemporary guest room and suite features floor-to-ceiling windows and offers a flat-screen satellite TV, an electric kettle, electronic safe, minibar, sofa & a work desk.

The attached bathrooms are equipped with a shower, hairdryer and free toiletries.

The Square – award winning all day dining restaurant serves Indian, Pan Asian, Japanese, Italian & European fare.

The hotel offers Sunday brunch at The Square from 12 noon to 4 PM. Novotel Ahmedabad boasts the longest breakfast from 4.

30 AM - 12 noon. Room service can be requested for in-room dining comforts and is available 24 hours.
