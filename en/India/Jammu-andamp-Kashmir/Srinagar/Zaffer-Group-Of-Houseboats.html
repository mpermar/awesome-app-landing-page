Description

All rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area and a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a bath and free toiletries, while certain units at Zaffer Group Of Houseboats also provide guests with a balcony. Guest rooms will provide guests with a dishwasher.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental or a buffet breakfast. Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of Asian dishes and also offers kosher and vegetarian options.

Zaffer Group Of Houseboats has a playground. The area is popular for skiing, and ski equipment rental is available at the hotel.

The reception at Zaffer Group Of Houseboats can provide tips on the area.

Nehru Park is 2.2 miles from the accommodation, while Lal Chowk Ghantaghar is 3.5 miles from the property. Sheikh Ul Alam International Airport is 10 miles away.
