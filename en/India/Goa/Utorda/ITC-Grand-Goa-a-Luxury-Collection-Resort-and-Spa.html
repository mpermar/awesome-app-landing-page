Description

ITC Grand Goa Resort & Spa is set within 45 acres of landscaped gardens with glimmering waterways and lagoons along the pristine Arossim Beach which is just a 15 minutes drive from Goa International Airport.

It offers an outdoor swimming pool overlooking the Arabian Sea, 6 dining options and a spa.

The well appointed rooms and suites look out in to the tropical gardens, the lagoons and water bodies.

All rooms and suites feature personal outdoor spaces in the form of a balcony or patio with some rooms also featuring outdoor rain showers and secret gardens.

At ITC Grand Goa Resort & Spa you will find a 24-hour front desk and a concierge desk.

Other facilities offered at the property include a children's play zone area where kids can enjoy.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including water sports.

The property offers a state-of-the-art fitness center open 24 hours a day. Kaya Kalp - The Royal Spa is nestled amidst lush beachfront gardens, featuring exquisitely designed treatment suites and an expansive space for Yoga and relaxation.

Pavilion at the Village Square brings to you the finest Indian and International fare round the clock.

It also features an outdoor open kitchen, allowing guests to interact with in-house culinary masters.

Kebabs & Kurries offers heritage cuisine while Ottimo Cucina Italiana is inspired by the concept of a homely Italian kitchen.

Guests can also try the Signature Goan restaurant or The Royal Afghan Sea side Barbeque & Grill offering charcoal-grilled flavours of the North-West Frontier under the stars offering views of the sea.

Arossim beach is 2. 6 km away. The Cabo Da Rama Fort is 25 km away while Baga Beach and Fort Aguada are 45 km away.
