Description

Offering a spa centre and sauna and 24-hour concierge service, W Goa is set in Vagator.

Guests can enjoy the outdoor swimming pool or grab a drink at the WooBar. Some units include a seating area where you can relax while some rooms have views of the sea or garden.

Certain rooms offer a sun deck and plunge pool. Rooms feature attached private bathrooms offering free toiletries.

Extras include slippers, bathrobe and a hairdryer. You will find a 24-hour front desk and shops at the property.

The property offers an indoor play area, fitness centre, yoga classes and a garden.

The W Goa offers a variety of restaurants including The Kitchen Table serving local cuisine and Spice Traders offering Pan-Asian cuisine specially curated by the Director of cuisine.

Guests can refuel at the Rock Pool consisting of a naturally carved facade ideally located to enjoy the breath-taking views of the sunset.

The resort also offers car hire, bicycle rental and complimentary high speed internet throughout the property.
