Description

Encompassed within a rustic surrounding and lush gardens, Jasmin Hotel is located a 25-minute drive from Dabolim Airport.

The property offers free Wi-Fi and an outdoor pool. Situated only 2 km to Colva Beach, the hotel is also within a 5 km distance to Margao City and 8 km from Margao railway station.

Margao bus stand is 5 km away. Air-conditioned rooms at Jasmin Hotel are equipped with a ceiling fan, a TV, a mini fridge and individual tea/coffee-makers.

Attached bathrooms include hot/cold shower facilities. Each room also features a direct view of the hotels lush gardens.

A 24-hour front desk can assist guests with laundry and ironing services, car rental as well as Airport shuttle services at a surcharge.

Breakfast can be served in the room upon request. Jasmin Hotels Xo Cafe serves local and international cuisine.
