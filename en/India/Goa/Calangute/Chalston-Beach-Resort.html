Description

The elegant air-conditioned accommodations include cable TV and a sitting area. There is also a refrigerator. Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also provide towels.

At Chalston Beach Resort there is a 24-hour front desk, a garden and a bar. Other facilities available at the property include a tour desk and a laundry area. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the area, including billiards and ping-pong. The property provides free parking.

If you feel like exploring the area, check out Baga Beach, a 2-minute walk away. Mapusa Bus Station is 6 miles away and Thivim Train Station is 19 miles away. This resort is 28 miles from Goa International Airport.

The property houses a restaurant that serves Indian, Chinese, Italian and local specialties. Private dining is available with room service.
