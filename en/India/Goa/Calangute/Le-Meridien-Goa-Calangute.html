Description

Offering an outdoor pool and fitness centre, Le Méridien Goa, Calangute is located in Calangute, 800 metres from Calangute beach.

The hotel has a terrace and views of the city, and 5 onsite dining options including Jazz & Grills Goa’s first whiskey bar.

The rooms are fitted with a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property and a Family Kids Club. You can play billiards at the hotel.

Infantaria is 1. 6 km from Le Méridien Goa, Calangute, while Tito's Club is 2. 9 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Goa International Airport, 42 km from Le Méridien Goa, Calangute.
