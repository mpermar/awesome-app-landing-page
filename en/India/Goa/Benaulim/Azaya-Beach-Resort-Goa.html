Description

Located on Benaulim beach and overlooking the Arabian Ocean, Azaya Beach Resort Goa is set amidst 10 acres of land with unparalleled views of the Goa Shoreline.

The property features a swimming pool, fitness centre and free WiFi. Goa International Airport is 26 km away.

Azaya draws inspirations from the Maldivian sense of place with uninterrupted sea views and powder-soft sands right up to your doorsteps.

Azaya also celebrates its Portuguese past with a beautiful Portuguese Garden and other design influences.

Rooms come with a private bathroom and a bath, while certain rooms come with a patio.

All units are fitted with a TV with satellite channels. Located at the heart of Azaya, the Prana Spa is an oasis of tranquility, beauty and relaxation.

The interior decor is refined with a harmonious combination of wood, stone and natural fiber furniture.

It comprises five treatment rooms including a Couples Massage Suite. The Spa also has an open-air relaxation area and a quaint little boutique showcasing all the House’s products.

Cerulean Poolside Lounge features a refreshing beverage menu that includes healthy tropical drinks as well as frosty, flavoured frozen margaritas and cocktails.

Enjoy the most spectacular views of the hotel from the Library Lounge. It offers an innovative menu from tea, coffee beverages to exclusive alcoholic beverage along with a selection of canapés.

The restaurant, La Concha features live kitchens revolving around the concept of eating out of the pan, off the grill and out of the wok.

Lynx – Bar & Lounge is a New York style bar and lounge, overlooking the Arabian Sea, that serves innovative cocktails, also featuring a world class resident DJ.
