Description

Overlooking the scenic Mandovi River, DoubleTree by Hilton Goa-Panaji is located just a few minutes away from Panaji and Miramar beach.

The hotel features an outdoor infinity pool, 24-hour access to fitness center, kids club, along with a full-service Spa.

Adults and children can enjoy a variety of daily activities along with complimentary beach shuttle service.

Guestrooms and suites offer panoramic views of the Mandovi river or the lush greens surrounding the hotel.

Each room has a spacious private balcony. The executive rooms and suites offer exclusive access to the executive lounge with complimentary refreshments during the stay.

The presidential suite offers a private terrace, a spa bath and lounge benefits. The all-day dining restaurant, Comida, serves buffet breakfast as well as local and international specialties through the day.

Dine at Feliz for authentic flavours from Mangalore, Goa and other coastal regions.

Spend an evening sipping your favourite cocktail at the bar or pick something from the eclectic menu at Rio Salao, located at the lobby level which serves hot and cold beverages, healthy drinks and fresh ‘grab and go’ options.

Languages spoken at the reception include English and Hindi. The hotel is 28 km to the Dabolim International Airport.
