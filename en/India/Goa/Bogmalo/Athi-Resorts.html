Description

Offering a restaurant, Athi Resorts is located in Bogmallo. Wi-Fi access is available in this resort.

The accommodation will provide you with a TV, a seating area and cable channels. Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with free toiletries.

At Athi Resorts you will find a 24-hour front desk, a garden and a terrace. Other facilities offered at the property include a tour desk, luggage storage and dry cleaning.

The property offers free parking. Room service options are available throughout the day.

The property is located 7 km from Vasco Railway Station and Vasco Bus Stand. Bogmalo Beach is just 1 km away.
