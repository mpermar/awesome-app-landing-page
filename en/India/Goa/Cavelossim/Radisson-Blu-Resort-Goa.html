Description

Located on Cavelossim Beach in Goa, Radisson Blu Resort features Goan-Portuguese-inspired architecture with bright exterior colours.

Its offers rooms with free Wi-Fi and a free-form outdoor pool, spa and fitness centre.

Offering sea, lawn or pool views, the air-conditioned rooms feature a flat-screen satellite TV, electronic safe and work desk.

Private bathroom includes a temperature controlled rain shower, hairdryer and slippers.

Radisson Blu Resort is 4 km from the Carmona local market and 17 km from Madgaon City.

Madgaon Railway Station is a 30-minute drive away. It takes 45-minutes by car to reach Dabolim International Airport.

Relax at Sohum Spa, which has a sauna, steam room and hot tub. Express laundry service and free private parking are available.

Traditional Goan-Portuguese cuisine is served at Lucio. The Upper Deck coffee shop offers all day Western and Indian meals while meals are accompanied by live music in the evenings.

Other dining options include Greek dishes at Greco restaurant and the trendy lounge bar, Sagres.
