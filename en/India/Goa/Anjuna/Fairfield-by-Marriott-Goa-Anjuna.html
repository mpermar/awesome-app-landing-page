Description

Fairfield by Marriott Goa Anjuna is located in Anjuna in the Goa Region. The property offers complimentary shuttle service to Anjuna beaches.

Anjuna beach is 3 km away while Calangute is 5 km from the property. Guests can enjoy complimentary port wine upon arrival.

All rooms include 43-inch televisions and private balconies. Some rooms have a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

Fairfield by Marriott Goa Anjuna features free WiFi throughout the property. It features a garden.

Express check-in and check-out are offered. The property has an ice cream cart on-site.

The hotel features an on-site multi cuisine restaurant-‘Kava’ with indoor and outdoor seating and guests can also sit at the attached bar in the restaurant to grab a refreshing drink.

It also features an outdoor swimming pool with hot tub section, outdoor kids’ pool, kids’ club, pool side food and beverage service.

It includes a 24 hrs fitness center, 24 hrs reception, luggage storage, laundry facilities, in-room dining service.

Saturday Night Market is 1 km away while Anjuna Flea Market on Wednesdays is 3 km away.

St. Michael's Church is 1. 7 km from the property. Panaji is 20 km, Tito’s 3. 5 km, Britto’s 3 km, Antares 4.

2 km and Thalassa 6 km from the property. The nearest airport is Goa International Airport, 48 km from the property.
