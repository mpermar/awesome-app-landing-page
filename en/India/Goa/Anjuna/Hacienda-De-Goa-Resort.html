Description

This elegant property is located within 1.6 mi from Vagator Beach and Chapora Fort. It is 2.3 mi from the popular Anjuna Beach. Thivim Railway Station is 11 mi away while Dabolim Airport is 27 mi from the hotel.

Tastefully furnished, air-conditioned rooms are fitted with a TV and ample seating area. The private bathroom comes with either a shower. Ironing facilities are available upon request.

Hacienda De Goa’s 24-hour front desk will be happy to assist with car rental services and laundry/dry-cleaning services. Guests can also make travel arrangements at the tour desk.

Guests can dine at Romeos Restaurant which serves a selection of International dishes, as well as Indian cuisine. Room service options are available.
