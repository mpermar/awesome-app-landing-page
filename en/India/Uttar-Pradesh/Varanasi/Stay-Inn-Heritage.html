Description

Boasting a shared lounge, BBQ facilities and views of city, Stay Inn Heritage is situated in Varanasi, less than 1 km from Kedar Ghat.

Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a 24-hour front desk and a shared kitchen, along with free WiFi throughout the property.

The accommodation features room service, a kids' club and currency exchange for guests.

All rooms at the hotel are fitted with a seating area. At Stay Inn Heritage the rooms include a desk and a private bathroom.

The accommodation offers a continental or buffet breakfast. Stay Inn Heritage offers a children's playground.

Bike hire and car hire are available at the hotel and the area is popular for cycling and fishing.

For business travellers' convenience, meeting and banquet facilities, a business centre, newspapers and a fax machine and photocopier are available.

Popular points of interest near Stay Inn Heritage include Harishchandra Ghat, Dasaswamedh Ghat and Manikarnika Ghat.

The nearest airport is Lal Bahadur Shastri International, 31 km from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
