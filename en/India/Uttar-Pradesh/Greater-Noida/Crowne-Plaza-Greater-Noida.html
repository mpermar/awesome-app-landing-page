Description

Rooms here will provide you with a flat-screen TV, air conditioning and a mini-bar. There is also an electric kettle. Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with a bathtub and a hairdryer. Extras include a seating area and satellite channels.

At Crowne Plaza Greater Noida you will find a fitness center, business center and a meeting space. Other facilities offered include luggage storage, dry cleaning and a laundry. Dining options include Mosiac that serves breakfast, lunch and dinner. Chaobella serves Indian, Chinese, Continental and Italian cuisines.
