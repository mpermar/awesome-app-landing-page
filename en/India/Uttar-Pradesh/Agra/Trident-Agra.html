Description

Located 1. 5 km from Taj Mahal, Trident Agra features an outdoor pool and 24-hour front desk.

A business centre, restaurant and bar are available. Some rooms offer garden or pool views.

Free WiFi is available in the rooms of the property. Air-conditioned rooms are fitted with a minibar, tea/coffee-making facilities and a personal safe.

En suite bathrooms include a hairdryer. Each room has a seating area. Currency exchange and car rental services are available for convenience.

There is a beauty salon for guests wishing to pamper themselves. Laundry and ironing services are offered.

European, South East Asian and Indian cuisine is served in the restaurant, which also offers room service.

Drinks are served in the bar. Kid meals are available on an additional cost. Trident Agra is 7 km from Agra Fort and a 20-minute drive from Agra Cantonment Station.
