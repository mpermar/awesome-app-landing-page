Description

Offering an outdoor swimming pool, a massage parlour and a spa and wellness centre, Hotel Clarks Shiraz is located in Agra.

It is 1. 5 km from the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Taj Mahal and Agra Fort. Complimentary WiFi is available for one hour per day.

Rooms here will provide you with a flat-screen TV, air conditioning and a minibar.

Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with free toiletries. Extras include a seating area and cable channels.

At Hotel Clarks Shiraz you will find a 24-hour front desk, BBQ and a fitness centre.

Other facilities offered include meeting facilities, a tour desk and luggage storage.

The property is 35 km from Fatehpur Sikri. It is 4 km from Agra Cantonment Railway Station and 5.

5 km from Agra Airport. The ISBT Bus Station is 8 km. Dining options include Mughal Room that serves Indian and Chinese while refreshing beverages can be enjoyed at the Mughal Bar.
