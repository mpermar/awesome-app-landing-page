Description

Situated on the site of an Old Dutch Fort, Taj Fisherman’s Cove Resort & Spa offers 5-star luxury by Chennai’s coast.

Taj Fisherman’s Cove Resort & Spa is just 2. 6 km from the heritage village of Dakshina Chitra and offers free parking.

The heritage town of Mahabalipuram is a 20 minute drive away. Siruseri IT park is 12 km from the property.

It is 16 km from Tiger Cave and 32 km from Chennai Airport. Set in tropical landscapes, air-conditioned units feature minimalist interiors and flat-screen TVs.

They come with a hairdryer and bathrobes. Guests can enjoy relaxing massages after active workouts at the gym or tennis courts.

Offering concierge service, staff can also attend to business and travel needs. Overlooking the Bay of Bengal, Upper Deck offers intimate outdoor dining with Mediterranean dishes.
