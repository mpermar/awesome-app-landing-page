Description

The 5-star Park Plaza offers luxurious accommodation in Salem. Just 2 km from Salem Railway Station, this property boasts 2 dining options, an outdoor swimming pool and free Wi-Fi.

Overlooking the city, the elegant rooms feature modern interiors, floor-to-ceiling windows and warm lighting.

They are equipped with a flat-screen TV, a work desk and a private bathroom with shower facilities.

Guests can swim, exercise at the gym, or simply take a stroll in the garden. Car rental and ticketing services are provided for those who wish to explore the area.

Tangerine Restaurant serves a spread of local, Chinese and Continental dishes. Barbecued and grilled specialities are offered at Coral restaurant.

Park Plaza Salem is 4 km from Salem Bus Terminal and Kandaswara Mall. It is 13 km from Salem Airport and 18 km from 1008 SivLinga Temple.
