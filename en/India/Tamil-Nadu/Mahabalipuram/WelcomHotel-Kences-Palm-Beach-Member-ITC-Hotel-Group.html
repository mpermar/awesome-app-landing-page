Description

Featuring an outdoor pool, free Wifi and private parking on-site, WelcomHotel Kences Palm Beach is situated in Mahabalipuram in the Tamil Nadu Region, 44 km from Chennai.

The property boasts of a spa and fitness centre. Guests can enjoy the on-site bar and restaurant.

Every room at this resort is air conditioned and comes with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

All units have a balcony, seating area, dining area, outdoor furniture and a walk-in closet for your convenience.

The rooms are also equipped with a mini bar and an electric kettle. Enjoy a cup of tea while looking out at the sea or lake.

The rooms include a private bathroom equipped with a bath and bidet. Extras include bathrobes, slippers, hairdryer and free toiletries.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. Room service and barbecue facilities are available.

This resort has a private beach area and car hire is available. Chennai International Airport is 58 km from the property.
