Description

At the resort, all rooms have a patio. Hanging Huts Resorts has certain rooms with mountain views, and all rooms include a electric tea pot. All rooms have a private bathroom, free toiletries and bed linen.

The daily breakfast offers continental, buffet or Asian options. The restaurant at the accommodation specializes in American cuisine.

Speaking English and Hindi at the 24-hour front desk, staff are always at hand to help.

Coimbatore is 28 miles from Hanging Huts Resorts. Coimbatore International Airport is 27 miles from the property.

Experiences

08/21/2016

Away from the crowd in the lap of nature

"Tastefully done interiors and every item is well thought off. The place is neat, clean and well maintained. There is a nice sit out with a view of valley, a great place to relax with a book in hand. The only sound that you hear is that of birds, insects and frogs. The resort is self contained. DTH HD TV is available if you are so inclined, though why would anyone want to watch TV if you have such a beautiful scenery to watch? The service is good and they will try to accommodate your requirements to the maximum extent possible. Food is freshly cooked in the kitchen and cook will gladly customise it based on your taste. We asked for food with low spice for our daughter and he made it. Tea/Coffee/Snacks/Meals are just a call away. Newspaper is provided along with morning tea. Place is off the main road to Kotagiri, you have to take a turn on the main road and drive for some distance. The road is well maintained, only the last 50 feet stretch is over rough ground. The best way to reach this place is to store the GPS coordinates on your phone and save the offline route map on Google map since network connectivity can be patchy. You can go out for a walk if you are a nature lover.We would definitely visit this place again. Recommended for families"
