Description

Novotel Chennai OMR - An AccorHotels Brand is a 5-star hotel in Chennai spreading across 3 acres of greenery and featuring premium accommodation in the heart of IT hub, Sholinganallur on Rajiv Gandhi IT expressway.

The hotel features modern air-conditioned and spacious rooms, 24-hrs fitness center, 2 restaurants & bars and free 8-mpbs WiFi.

The hotel is in close proximity to Standard Chartered, Cognizant, Accenture, HCL, PayPal, ELCOT and TECCI IT Park and is a 7-minute drive from ECR beaches and Marina Mall.

Apollo Proton Hospital, Global hospital, and CURI are in close proximity. At the hotel each room has air-conditioning, a desk, a TV and a private bathroom and some units provides city view or pool view and all rooms have a kettle, a tea coffee maker, bottled water.

The rooms will provide guests with a mini bar, bathroom amenities and a shower. Suites are spacious and feature a bath.

'The Square’ - All day dining restaurant serves an excellent breakfast buffet & authentic North Indian, South India, Veg friendly food as well as American, European, Asian food with live counters.

NICO located at Lobby level serves international cocktails, short eats and comfort food.

Meeting facilities include 12 indoor and outdoor venues, concierge, travel desk, business center facilities.

A sports zone of 20 outdoor and indoor sports activities including cricket, gaming console, rent free bi-cycles, kids play area, concierge services and round trip airport shuttle are available for guests.

Popular points of interest around the property include Indian Institute of Technology, Madras and Anna University and ISKON temple.

Chennai International Airport is 18 km away. Hotel is also close to leisure destinations like Phoenix Market city, beaches and provides easy access to Mahabalipuram, Kancheepuram and Pondicherry.
