Description

A short 10-minute drive from major software and IT park of Chennai, the hotel offers well appointed rooms with breathtaking view of the IT corridor.

The hotel features well-furnished rooms and suites. Free high speed WiFi access is available in hotel including rooms.

Rooms will have a flat-screen TV, air conditioning and an iPod dock. Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with a hairdryer and free premium Boutique toiletries.

A lot of global delicacies can be enjoyed at the on-site multi-cuisine restaurant Cafe G.

The lounge offers a fine selection of tea, a wide array of beverages, scrumptious snacks, and desserts.

For wellness and recreation, guests can workout at the fitness area. Other facilities offered here include pick-up/drop facility, laundry, concierge, babysitting, foreign currency exchange, doctor on call, storage space and gift shop.

For conducting events and function, the hotel offers spaces that can accommodate up to 280 people.

Strategically located on the Rajiv Gandhi IT Expressway, Holiday Inn Chennai OMR IT Expressway is 16 km from Chennai International Airport.

Major IT Parks like Software Technology Park of India, TIDEL Park, Ramanujan IT City, Ascendas IT Park and TVH Agnitio Park are all within a 10-minute drive from the hotel.
