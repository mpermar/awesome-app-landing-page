Description

Offering a restaurant, Lake Restaurant that serves Indian and Chinese cuisine, Palmgrove Lake Resort is located in Alleppey.

Free Wi-Fi access is available in this resort. The air-conditioned accommodation will provide you with a cable TV and a seating area.

Private bathrooms also come with a shower. Some rooms have a lake view. At Palmgrove Lake Resort you will find a garden.

Other facilities offered at the property include a tour desk and a laundry. The property offers free parking.

Room service is provided. The property is 4. 5 km from Alleppey Town, 6. 5 km form Alleppey Beach and 45 km from Kumarakom.

The KSRTC Bus Station is 4. 3 km. The Alappuzha Railway Station is 7. 5 km while the Cochin International Airport is 83 km.
