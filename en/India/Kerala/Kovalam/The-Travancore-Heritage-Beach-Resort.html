Description

Located right on the Chowara beach, Travancore Heritage is a sea side resort features accommodations in a traditional architecture with smooth interior lighting and spacious bathrooms.

Guests can relax with traditional Indian Ayurveda treatments and massages or enjoy swimming at the 2 outdoor pools.

Trivandum International Airport is a 40-minute drive away. The air-conditioned rooms feature wooden floors, a satellite TV, a minibar and a coffee maker.

The spacious bathroom includes a garden bath and showers. The property features a small library and a common area with a satellite TV, card games, and a computer with internet connection.

Guests can purchase cosmetics and beachwear at the small shop, and request for car rentals and information at the tour desk.

A fitness centre and laundry services are also available. Open House overlooks the pool, serving seafood and local ethnic cuisines.

The Ocean Grill is situated by the beach and offers seafood dishes as well. Heritage Travancore is a 45-minute drive from Trivandum Railway Station, and a 40-minute drive from Shangumugham Beach.
