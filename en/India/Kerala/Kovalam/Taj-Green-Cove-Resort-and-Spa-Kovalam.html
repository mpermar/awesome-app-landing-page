Description

The luxurious Taj Green Cove Resort and Spa Kovalam is located along Kovalam Beach offering a backdrop of natural surroundings.

It features a traditional Indian spa providing rejuvenation therapies and a private plunge pool and dining nook.

An inifninity pool and fitness centre are also available. Free WiFi is available.

Taj Green Cove Resort and Spa Kovalam is situated 12 km from Thiruvananthapuram Airport.

Trivandrum Railway Station is 14 km away. Rooms and suites have a balcony and large French windows overlooking the resort’s Balinese-style landscaped garden or Arabian Sea.

A flat-screen TV and tea and coffee-making facilities are provided. Some suites offer a private infinity pool.

Guests can enjoy a leisurely boat ride in the resort’s lagoon or head for a game of beach volleyball or cricket.

Cocktail-making classes are held for guests. The resort also has a golf putting green and 9-hole pitch.

The kids activity room has carom and other board games, books, toys along with a full-time care-taker and baby-sitting services on request.

The Seaside Specialty Restaurant “Bait” is uniquely located with the tranquil backwaters to one side and the roaring Arabian Sea with sandy dining as its facade.

The world-renowned “Bait” is also frequented for its exhaustive variety of vegetarian platters and wood-fired marinades give plenty of options for every palate.

An all day-Diner “Jasmine Bay” offers poolside dining with Asian and Continental menus.

A daily wellness routine can be curated on request. Curries serves typical Kerala & other Indian specialties while Neera Bar serves signature cocktails and zesty bar-snacks.
