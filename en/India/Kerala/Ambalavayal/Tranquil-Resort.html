Description

All rooms in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV. All rooms are equipped with a private bathroom with a shower, while some rooms include a terrace and others also provide guests with mountain views. Guest rooms will provide guests with a desk and a electric tea pot.

Breakfast is available each morning, and includes continental and buffet options. There's an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of Indian dishes and also offers halal options.

The area is popular for hiking, and car rental is available at Tranquil Resort.

Heritage Museum is 1.9 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Calicut International Airport, 39 miles from the property.
