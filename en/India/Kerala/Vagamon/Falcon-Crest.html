Description

Offering a restaurant that serves Indian, Chinese and continental deligths, Falcon Crest is nestled in the greenery of Vagamon.

Free WiFi access is available in the public areas of this resort. The accommodation will provide you with a balcony and a seating area.

Complete with a refrigerator, the dining area also has a dining table. Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with free toiletries.

You can enjoy mountain view from the room. At Falcon Crest you will find a garden.

Other facilities offered at the property include a games room and luggage storage.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including fishing and hiking.

The property offers free parking. The Trekking Point is 2 km and the Pine Forest is 10 km.

The Pala Bus Station is 35 km, the Kottayam Railway Station is 70 km and the Cochin International Airport is 100 km.
