Description

At the resort, every room has a closet and a flat-screen TV. Rooms are equipped with a private bathroom and free toiletries, and some rooms here will provide you with a balcony. All rooms come with a seating area.

A continental breakfast is served at the property.

The area is popular for fishing and canoeing.

The nearest airport is Calicut International Airport, 35 miles from Bamboo Creek Resort.

This resort, we had the treehouse, is not comparable with...

"This resort, we had the treehouse, is not comparable with other resorts/hotel, because the have a different philosophy. First of all, we had there the best indian/kerala food and everything was from own plantation, even their coffee! And we were in a lot indian restaurants in different citys and places, nowhere we had such good food and every dinner, breakfast was different and we had the chance to learn more about the food. The stuff was very helpful, kind and we learned a lot about their philosophy, their resort, their plantation and the food. It was expensive compared with other resorts, but it was worth it. I guess the people will understand when they are there. Altough we had the treehouse, it was not the only highlight during our stay. Nevertheless the treehouse was very comfortable with a beatiful view from the balcony, a little dream from the childhood is becoming true:-).The resort is highly recommended and we hope to stay again their."
