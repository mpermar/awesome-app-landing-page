Description

The units have a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher and a stovetop. A patio with mountain views is offered in each unit.

An Asian breakfast is served at the property. Guests can enjoy a meal at the in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of Asian dishes.

The resort village has a playground.

Both a bicycle rental service and a car rental service are available at Marsim Holiday Resort, while cycling can be enjoyed nearby.

Lakkidi Viewpoint is 3.6 miles from the accommodation, while Chembra Peak is 5 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Calicut International Airport, 30 miles from the property.
