Description

All guest rooms in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Some rooms are equipped with a kitchenette with a dishwasher.

A continental breakfast is available every morning at Jungle Edge Resort. There's an on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of Indian and Chinese dishes and also offers vegan options.

The accommodation has a terrace. Guests at Jungle Edge Resort will be able to enjoy activities in and around Tindummal, like cycling.

Wayanad is 13 miles from the resort. The nearest airport is Calicut International Airport, 45 miles from the property.
