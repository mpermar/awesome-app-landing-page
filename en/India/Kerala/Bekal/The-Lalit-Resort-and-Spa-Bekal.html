Description

Boasting its very own helipad and helicopter service, The Lalit Resort & Spa Bekal provides Ayurvedic treatments, yoga facilities and luxurious rooms with hot tubs.

Spread over 26 acres of gardens, it also provides an outdoor pool and free Wi-Fi.

Decorated in rich wood furnishings, the well-designed rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV, iPod dock and DVD player.

All include a yoga mat, minibar and free tea/coffee making facilities. Views of the lagoon and gardens are presented from the private balcony.

Surrounded by the beautiful backwaters of Kerala, The Lalit Resort & Spa Bekal is less than 2 km from downtown Bekal and 24 km from Neelashwar.

It is 45 km from Ullal Beach and Someshwar Temple. Mangalore International Airport is 51 km away.

Aside from the signature Rejuve Spa, the resort also features a state-of-the-art fitness centre, a library lounge and bird observatory spots.

Other activities include local village tours, fishing competitions and kite flying.

Nombili Restaurant & Bar serves the finest of the traditional Kerala cuisines with culinary concepts from all over the world.
