Description

The rooms in the resort are equipped with a TV with satellite channels. Every room is equipped with a electric tea pot, while some have a balcony. At 36 Palms Boutique Retreat each room has a private bathroom with a shower.

A continental or buffet breakfast is available daily at the property.

A variety of popular activities are available in the area around the accommodation, including cycling and fishing.
