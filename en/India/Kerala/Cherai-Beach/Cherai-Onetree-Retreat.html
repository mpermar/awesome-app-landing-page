Description

Certain rooms feature a sitting area where you can relax. Certain rooms include views of the sea or garden. The rooms have a private bathroom equipped with a bathtub. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Bike rental and car rental are available at this resort and the area is popular for windsurfing.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the area, including biking and fishing. The nearest airport is Kochi International Airport, 14 miles from Onetree Beach Retreat.
