Description

All guest rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area. With a private bathroom equipped with a shower and free toiletries, rooms at Novotel Kochi Infopark also provide guests with free WiFi. At the accommodation every room is equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

A continental breakfast is served daily at the property.

Novotel Kochi Infopark has an outdoor pool.

Languages spoken at the reception include Hindi and English.

Wonderla Kochi is 2.1 miles from the hotel, while Oberon Mall is 3.5 miles from the property. Cochin International Airport is 10 miles away.
