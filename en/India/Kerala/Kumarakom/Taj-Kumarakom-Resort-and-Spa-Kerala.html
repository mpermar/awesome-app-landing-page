Description

Surrounding the scenic lagoon or pool, the well-designed rooms include air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and a minibar. Some rooms have a separate lounge area. Private bathrooms have a bathtub.

Mediterranean and North Indian cuisines are served at Baker's Gourmet House, and seafood dishes and snacks are available at Open Coffee House. Premium wine can be enjoyed at Easy Bar.

Free bike rentals are available. Guests can arrange day trips at the tour desk, or check their email at the business center.

Vivanta by Taj Kumarakom is a 30-minute drive from Kottayam Train Station and a one-hour drive from Cochin City. Cochin Airport is 62 miles away.
