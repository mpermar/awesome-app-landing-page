Description

The rooms at the resort are equipped with a seating area. Each room has a private bathroom with a shower and some rooms also feature a balcony. All rooms come with a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Guests at Joys Resort Poovar can enjoy a continental or an Asian breakfast. At the accommodation you'll find a restaurant serving Asian cuisine.

Joys Resort Poovar has an outdoor pool. Guests at the resort will be able to enjoy activities in and around Trivandrum, like fishing.

Speaking English and Hindi at the 24-hour front desk, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical guidance on the area.

Napier Museum is 16 miles from Joys Resort Poovar, while Poovar Backwaters is 1.2 miles away. The nearest airport is Thiruvananthapuram International Airport, 17 miles from the property.
