Description

Featuring free WiFi, Parakkat Nature Resort offers accommodation in Munnar. The resort has a barbecue and views of the mountain, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Free private parking is available on site. Each room comes with a flat-screen satellite TV.

You will find a kettle in the room. Rooms include a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find slippers, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. The resort also offers bike hire and car hire.
