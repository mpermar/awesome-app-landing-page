Description

All rooms are beautifully decorated with wood furnishings and fine beddings. They are cozy and have private balconies and various entertainment options like flat-screen cable TVs and DVD players.

Enjoy excellent meals at The Orchid, an international cuisine restaurant or have a quick snack outdoors at Jungel Café. The pool bar offers cocktails and barbecue on the pool deck, while Tongba Bar features a selection of cigars and champagnes.

The Mayfair Hotel’s award-winning spa provides customized treatments. It also has a well-equipped fitness center and other recreational facilities like a billiard room and a library.

Bagdogra Airport is 71 mi away.
