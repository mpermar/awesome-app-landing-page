Experiences

10/30/2017

What an excellent stay in Bansko!

"We visited Bansko a four-member family in October. We arrived early in the hotel and checked in at 9am. Thus we had enough time for a little rest after the trip. The hotel is located just at the foot of the mountain and the view from the room is amazing. It offers free private parking for the car and there is also an elevator for your heavy luggage. The use of all facilities is free, such as the spa center with the indoor heated pool, the sauna, the steam bath and the Jacuzzi. There were game rooms with tables for playing cards, board games and billiards as well as a children's play area. Generally the hotel and its surroundings are very attentive and the staff is willing and smiling. Most of them speak very good English but also Greek and other languages. The same applies to the staff at the spa center, which operates from 9am to 9pm. Our room was on the third floor and was very comfortable and warm, with a beautiful mountain view balcony. It had a super-fast Wi-Fi connection, a fully equipped kitchen, water kettle, fridge and all the necessary stuff for a meal or tea - coffee. In the living area there is a dining table for 4 persons, a sofa that converts to a comfortable double bed, 2 armchairs and a flat TV - dvd player set with cable channels. The bathroom was spotless with a glass shower screen, soap, shower gel, shampoo, extra towels and bathrobes for everyone. Finally the separate bedroom had a large comfortable double bed with clean sheets and warm bedding, a second flat TV with cable channels, large double wardrobe with hangers, electric iron and a hairdryer. All in all it was a very pleasant and comfortable stay and we really felt like home. I recommend it for couples but also for families with 2 children."
