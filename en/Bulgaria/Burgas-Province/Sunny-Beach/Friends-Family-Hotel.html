Description

Every room is equipped with a cable TV and a balcony. The Friends Family Hotel provides its own outdoor pool with lounge chairs.

Friends Hotel runs its own pub and bar with open terrace, offering a variety of Bulgarian and international salads, dishes, pizza and steaks.

The pub features a wide-screen flat TV set showing a variety of cable channels, sports and SKY Sport satellite channels. There is an outdoor bar where guests can enjoy cocktails and play pool.
