Description

For those with more material pursuits in mind, the shopping mall boasting 99 shops is definitely worth a visit. If you need some rest and relaxation the spa is the place to be, featuring a heated indoor pool, massage services, a hot tub, a sauna, a steam bath, a hammam, relaxing foot baths, an infrared sauna and a gym. After days full of summer pleasures you can spend restful nights in exquisitely furnished rooms.

A wide range of restaurants and bars are available at the complex. Overlooking the pool, the lobby bar serves a variety of international drinks from beer to fine wine and champagne, from premium liquors and spirits to delicious cocktails.

You can park your car securely in the 2-story underground parking garage.
