Description

Each unit is fitted with air conditioning, private bathroom and a kitchenette including a microwave. A terrace with garden views is offered in every unit.

Breakfast is available daily, and includes continental and à la carte options. Guests can dine in the on-site restaurant, which specializes in Italian cuisine.

The condo hotel has a playground.

Guests can also relax in the shared lounge area.

Old Nesebar is 2.1 miles from Apart Complex Poseidon. The nearest airport is Burgas Airport, 11 miles from the property.
