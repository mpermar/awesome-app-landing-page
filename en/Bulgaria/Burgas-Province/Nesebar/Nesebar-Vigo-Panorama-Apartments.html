Description

All units come with a seating area, a sofa, a flat-screen TV and a fully equipped kitchenette. There's a private bathroom with shower in all units, along with free toiletries and a hair dryer.

A buffet breakfast is available each morning at the apartment. The restaurant at Nesebar Vigo Panorama Apartments serves European cuisine.

The accommodation has a playground.

If you would like to discover the area, snorkeling and hiking are possible nearby and the property can arrange a bicycle rental service.

Aqua Paradise is 1.9 miles from Nesebar Vigo Panorama Apartments. The nearest airport is Burgas Airport, 12 miles from the apartment.
