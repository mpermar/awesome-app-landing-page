Description

Embrace the attractive position with a great view of the Black Sea, just 350 feet from the beach or explore the neighboring forests, the Veleka River and the Strandzha Nature Reserve.

The bright cream façade gives way to inviting public and private spaces. After a long day, retreat to your comfortable apartment and benefit from the range of amenities it has to offer.

Sample traditional, regional cuisine as well as European dishes in the elegant restaurant. The buffet breakfast is bountiful and delicious, prepared according to your wishes.

Take advantage of the fitness facilities, soak up the sun at the outdoor swimming pool, play tennis or unwind in your own green corner at Asti Arthotel.
