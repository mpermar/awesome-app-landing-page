Description

A TV with cable channels, a minibar and a refrigerator are featured in each room. The private bathroom comes with a shower or a bathtub and a hairdryer.

The Hotel Velina's restaurant with an outdoor terrace features traditional Bulgarian cuisine and the lobby bar is an inviting place to relax. The hotel also features a fully equipped conference room.

Right outside the Velina hotel you can find many hiking paths leading trough the lush green surroundings.
