Description

Velingrad is renowned for the healing effect of its mineral water and recognized as the spa capital of Bulgaria. The hotel features 4 indoor and outdoor swimming pools with mineral water.

In this SPA hotel you will discover a sense of traditional Bulgarian hospitality blending with a modern approach to service and standards, promising a genuinely warm and comfortable welcome. Prepare your body and soul to sink into the pleasure of the spa procedures.

Additionally, Grand hotel Velingrad provides meeting rooms and is designed to meet the ultimate requirements in conference tourism in Bulgaria. The expert staff will arrange and plan your seminar, conference or event.
