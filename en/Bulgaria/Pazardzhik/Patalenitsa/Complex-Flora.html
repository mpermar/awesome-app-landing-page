Description

All rooms in the resort are equipped with a TV with cable channels. The rooms are equipped with a private bathroom and free toiletries, and certain rooms are contain a balcony. All guest rooms at Complex Flora feature air conditioning and a closet.

A buffet breakfast is available every morning at the accommodation.

You can play pool at Complex Flora, and the area is popular for hiking.

Velingrad is 12 miles from the resort, while Plovdiv is 28 miles away.
