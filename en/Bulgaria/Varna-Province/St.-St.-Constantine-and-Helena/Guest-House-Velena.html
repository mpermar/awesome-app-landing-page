Description

Guest rooms in the guesthouse are equipped with a TV with cable channels. Each room includes a private bathroom, while some feature a sea view. All rooms have a closet.

A Full English/Irish breakfast is served every morning at the property. There's an on-site restaurant, which specializes in European cuisine.

Guest House Velena has a terrace.

University Botanical Garden is 0.7 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Varna Airport, 8 miles from Guest House Velena.
