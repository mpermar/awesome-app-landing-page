Description

Rooms at the Ice Angels Hotel are comfortable and spacious, with carpeted floors, seating area and a work desk. They are equipped with TV and telephone.

Ice Angels is located in the Borovets mountain resort, the biggest in Bulgaria and at an altitude of 4.4 feet and the hotel features an extensive choice of facilities, such as ski school and children games room, a restaurant and a night club.

The hotel’s relax area which offers aromatherapy treatments and an indoor swimming pool, is also worth mentioning.

Ice Angels Hotel is only 45 mi away from Sofia and 78 mi from Plovdiv.
