Description

Boasting large floor-to-ceiling windows, Hotel Sense’s accommodations features a 42' LCD TV with web browser, an iPod docking station as well as individually controlled air conditioning and light. Free Molton Brown’s cosmetics can be found in the modern bathrooms. Most of the units are overlooking the nearby Alexander Nevski Cathedral.

Work out in the fitness center, equipped with extensive Technogym facilities, or pamper yourself to a massage after an active day. Aqua gym with a professional trainer is available free of charge and upon request and availability during weekdays.

Concierge services are offered at the reception, and a business corner is also available. Highlighted by its open fireplace, the lobby bar invites you to linger. At the lobby guests can find a Modern Art Gallery.

Various trendy bars, cafés and restaurants in the vicinity tempt you to venture out and feel the contemporary pulse of the city. All major attractions and institutions of Sofia are within short walking distance.

Free high-speed WiFi is provided on the premises. Metro Station Sofia University is 1,300 feet away, offering convenient connections throughout the city. Business Park Sofia is 6.8 mi from the hotel.

Centrum is a great choice for travelers interested in churches, budget-friendly traveling and monuments.
