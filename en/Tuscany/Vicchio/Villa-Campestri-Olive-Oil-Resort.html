Description

This fine hotel was carefully restored using authentic materials and maintaining the building’s original appearance.

Relais Villa Campestri is just 22 miles from Florence and its fascinating atmosphere echoes the history of the surrounding area, which was the home of Giotto, Beato Angelico and the Medici family.
