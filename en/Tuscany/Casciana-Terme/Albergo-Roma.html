Description

Rooms have a classic design with tiled floors and decorated rugs. They are all air conditioned and come with a minibar, TV and panoramic views out over the town.

Guests at this family-run hotel will enjoy relaxing in the bar and TV lounge, where there is also free Wi-Fi access.Roma Albergo's restaurant is Michelin guide recommended and serves Tuscan cuisine for lunch and dinner. Breakfast is buffet style.

Guests have good bus services to Pisa and Livorno, both around 45 minute drive away. Lucca can be reached in just over an hour by car.
