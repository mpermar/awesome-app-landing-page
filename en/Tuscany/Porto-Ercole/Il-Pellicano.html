Description

Il Pellicano includes a main building and a cluster of cottages scattered around the hotel's gardens. The heated swimming pool is filled with seawater, and there's also a tennis court with personal trainer.

The Tuscan rooms are air-conditioned and feature a flat-screen TV with DVD player. Guests will also have free Wi-Fi access, plus a minibar and satellite and pay-per-view channels.

Breakfast is buffet style, and guests have a choice of 2 restaurants. The Il Pellicano gourmet restaurant serves gourmet dishes and has 1 Michelin star, and Pelligrill Restaurant is a Tuscan restaurant with sea views.

Pelliclub Health & Beauty spa center includes a fitness room, boutique and a range of massages and beauty treatments. Argentario Golf Resort & Spa is 2 miles away.
