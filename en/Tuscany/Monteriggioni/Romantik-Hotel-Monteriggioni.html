Description

Each guest room has a unique design. They all come with air conditioning and satellite TV. Breakfast is included and can be enjoyed out in the garden in warm weather.

The Monteriggioni Romantik Hotel also features a large lounge and a bar. There is also a private car park.

There is a characteristic restaurant right in front of the hotel where you can sample Tuscan cuisine.

Siena is a 20-minute drive away.
