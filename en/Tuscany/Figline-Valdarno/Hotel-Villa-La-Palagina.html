Description

Accommodations here come equipped with satellite TV and air conditioning. The apartments include a kitchenette and dining area.

Your day at La Palagina Villa begins with a generous breakfast buffet. The restaurant serves Tuscan dishes and a wide selection of wines.

Greve in Chianti is 8 miles away, while the A1 highway is reachable by car in around 20 minutes. Parking at the property is free.
