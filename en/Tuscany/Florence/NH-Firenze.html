Description

Air conditioning and satellite flat-screen TV with pay-per-view channels come as standard in the rooms. Some have a balcony.

Breakfast at the NH includes cappuccino or herbal tea, together with Tuscan cheese and freshly baked croissants. The restaurant serves both local and classic Italian cuisine.

Fortezza da Basso convention center is a 15-minute walk from the hotel. Florence's historic center and cathedral, the Duomo, are a 10-minute bus ride away.
