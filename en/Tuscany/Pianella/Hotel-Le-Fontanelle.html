Description

Hotel Le Fontanelle's pretty main building dates back to the 13th century, part of an old country estate. All rooms have beamed ceilings and terracotta floors. They overlook the surrounding vineyards, woods, and valley. Modern facilities such as LCD satellite TV and internet access are also provided.

Enjoy a refreshing swim and stunning views in Le Fontanelle's outdoor pool or borrow one of the hotel's bikes free of charge, and go exploring the countryside.

Indoors you can work out in the gym, go for a swim in the indoor pool with hydro-massage area or really pamper yourself and go to the spa area. Relax in the sauna or the Turkish bath, or request one of a range of beauty treatments or massages.

Visiting Tuscany is easy from Le Fontanelle. A free scheduled shuttle service is available into Siena town center and a number of excursions can be organized.

Le Fontanelle's La Colonna restaurant has a panoramic terrace and serves traditional Italian cuisine with a creative twist.
