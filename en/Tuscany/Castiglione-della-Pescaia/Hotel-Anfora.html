Description

The rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. All guest rooms have a closet.

Guests at Hotel Anfora can enjoy an Italian breakfast. At the accommodation you'll find a restaurant serving Italian and local cuisine.

Grosseto is 11 miles from Hotel Anfora, while San Vincenzo is 29 miles away. The nearest airport is Grosseto (Baccarini) Airport, 9 miles from the hotel.
