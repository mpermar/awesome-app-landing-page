Description

The air conditioned accommodations at this rustic hotel include a converted mill and a former granary. On site, you will find a well-equipped gym, a tennis court, and a large outdoor pool.

The Fattoria Belvedere's restaurant serves regional cuisine made using local ingredients. The dining room has panoramic views of the countryside, and meals are served outside in the summer months.

Casino di Terra Train Station is 1,650 feet away, with direct services to Volterra and Pisa. The beaches of the Tyrrhenian Coast are a 30 minute drive away.
