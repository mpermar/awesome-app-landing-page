Description

Villa Scacciapensieri's main building was built in the late 18th century and has rooms on the 2 upper floors. Other rooms are in 2 annex buildings.

On the ground level of the main villa, there is the 24-hour front desk desk, a bar with lounge, a library, a private chapel, and a media room with internet access.

The Altri Tempi Restaurant serves typical Tuscan cuisine, including homemade pasta.

The center of Siena is just 1.5 miles away. Public buses to the center of the city stop in front of the Scacciapensieri, and run every 15 minutes during weekdays.
