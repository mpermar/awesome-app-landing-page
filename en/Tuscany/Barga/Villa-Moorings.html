Description

This boutique hotel is family-run just outside Barga's medieval center. It has a terrace on the first floor with tables and chairs. A buffet breakfast is served each morning, while the restaurant opens on request only and uses local, 0 mi, ingredients.

Rooms have warm color schemes and attractive wrought iron beds. Some have wood-beamed ceilings. They all come with TV, free Wi-Fi access and a mini-bar. Each bathroom has a hairdryer and toiletries.

Free parking is provided at Moorings Villa. It is around 45 minutes' drive from the historic town of Lucca and the A11 motorway.
