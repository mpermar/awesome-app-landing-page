Description

At the hotel, each room is equipped with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. All rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

Breakfast is available every morning, and includes buffet and Italian options.

The nearest airport is Galileo Galilei Airport, 28 miles from the property.
