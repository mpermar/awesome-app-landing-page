Description

All spacious rooms at the Gaige House feature private entrances. An iPod docking station and a 32-inch flat-screen TV are included in the rooms. Select rooms have a private patio with creek views.

Every evening, Gaige House Glen Ellen has a free evening wine and cheese tasting. Fresh baked cookies are available. The hotel has a daily full buffet breakfast as well.

An outdoor heated pool and hot tub are on site.
