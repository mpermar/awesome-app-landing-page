Description

Every suite at Embassy Suites Hotel La Quinta offers a separate living room with a dining area, microwave and refrigerator. An iPod docking station and 2 TVs are included.

Embassy Suites La Quinta Hotel and Spa features a well-equipped gym, full-service business center and a library. A games room and bicycle rentals are also available.

Spa Hibiscus at Hotel La Quinta Embassy Suites has 8 treatment rooms offering a variety of services including massages, facials, body and beauty treatments.

Café LQ is located in the atrium. Guests are provided a continental breakfast, evening manager’s reception and room service.

The Living Desert Southern California Zoo and Botanical Gardens are a 15-minute drive from this hotel.
