Description

Each air-conditioned room at this resort offers free WiFi and a flat-screen TV. Certain units include a seating area for your convenience. A balcony or patio are featured in certain rooms. Rooms are equipped with a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries.

Accommodations consist of Private Cottages, Bungalows, and Estate Rooms located on the Calamigos Ranch. Onsite dining available at the Malibu Cafe, the Pizza/Bar lounge, Leo's Lobby Bar, and the Pool Studio. The property offers three distinct pools and a large heated Spa Pool.

The property suits guests who are looking for accommodations for business or leisure. It offers a free shuttle service and bike hire. There are a variety of beach activities available through the property as well. Table tennis and billiards are also available.

The property's guest services team can aid guests in organizing beach days, horseback riding in the Santa Monica Mountains, private wine tasting, or Helicopter tours along the coast.

In addition, resort guests have exclusive access to the Calamigos Beach Club, a private 5.5 acre beach estate located just 12 minutes away by the complimentary shuttle. The Calamigos Beach Club also offers full dining and beach bar service with views of the Malibu Coastline from every window.

Santa Monica is 19 mi from Calamigos Guest Ranch and Beach Club, while Malibu is 6.2 mi away. The nearest airport is Los Angeles International Airport, 26 mi from Calamigos Guest Ranch and Beach Club.
