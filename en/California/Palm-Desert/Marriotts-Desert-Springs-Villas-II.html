Description

Each of the brightly colored rooms offered at this Palm Desert Marriott includes a private balcony and a kitchenette with a microwave, refrigerator, and plenty of cabinet space. Guests can watch cable TV on the sofa bed or use the free Wi-Fi.

A gym and sports court for basketball and tennis are on the grounds of this hotel. Guests can pick up snacks and coffee at the on-site convenience store and teenagers can play video games and do crafts in the teen den area.

Knott's Soak City Water Park is a 20-minute drive from Marriott's Desert Springs Villas II. Several golf courses including Indian Ridge Country Club are within 4 miles of the hotel.
