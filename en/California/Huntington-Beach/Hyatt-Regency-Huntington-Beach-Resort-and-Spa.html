Description

A 42-inch flat-screen satellite TV with pay-per-view channels is included in each air-conditioned room at Hyatt Regency Huntington Beach Resort and Spa. Each guest room includes a patio or a balcony.

Guests can enjoy creative fare and boutique wines at the Californian. Pete’s Sunset Grille features family-style favorites including fish tacos and burgers. Poolside dining is available at Shubee's Bar and Café and Mankota's Grill.

Guests can work out in the on-site fitness center or relax at the spa with a massage or facial. A game room, a playground and a children’s pool with waterslides on site. Bikes are available for rent.

Meadowlark Golf Course is 8 miles from this resort. Disneyland is 23 miles away. A seasonal shuttle to the theme park is available. Los Angeles International Airport is a 44-minute drive away.
