Description

Every air-conditioned guest room is equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a refrigerator, a coffee machine, free toiletries and a desk. Some rooms also feature a balcony.

The hotel has two locations where guests can dine. Goat Tree offers casual dining all day offering fresh local fare including pastries and baked goods, a full breakfast menu, and cold-pressed juices and coffee. Blackbird offers Mediterranean-influenced cuisine with the Pacific Ocean and Funk Zone as a backdrop.

The hotel spa, Majorelle, offers a steam room, a salon, and an indoor and outdoor lounge.

Kunin Wines is 350 feet from Hotel Californian, while Santa Barbara Wine Collective is 650 feet from the property. The nearest airport is Santa Barbara Airport, 8.7 mi from the hotel.

West Beach is a great choice for travelers interested in beach walks, beaches and relaxation.
