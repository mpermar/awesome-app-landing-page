Description

A cable TV with HBO movie channels and a DVD player are included in all rooms at Old Town Inn. Tea and coffee-making facilities are provided. Select rooms with full kitchens are available.

A 24-hour front desk greets guests at the Old Town Inn. Guests have access to the microwave in the lobby. A game room is available on site.

This hotel is less than 3 miles from San Diego International Airport. Presidio Hills Golf Course is less than 1 miles away. SeaWorld San Diego is 3 miles away. Free guest parking is available.

Midway-Pacific Highway is a great choice for travelers interested in family-friendly trips, zoos and beaches.
