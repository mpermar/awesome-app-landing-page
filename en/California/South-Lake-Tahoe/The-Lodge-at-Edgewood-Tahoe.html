Description

At the resort, rooms contain a terrace. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a bath and free toiletries, while certain accommodations at The Lodge at Edgewood Tahoe also offer a balcony. The rooms at the accommodation come with a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

At The Lodge at Edgewood Tahoe you'll find a restaurant serving American cuisine.

The resort offers 5-star accommodations with a hot tub. Guests at The Lodge at Edgewood Tahoe will be able to enjoy activities in and around South Lake Tahoe, like skiing.

With staff speaking English and Spanish, guidance is available at the reception.

Heavenly Ski Resort is 2.4 miles from the accommodation, while Washoe Meadows State Park is 7 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Reno-Tahoe International Airport, 39 miles from The Lodge at Edgewood Tahoe.
