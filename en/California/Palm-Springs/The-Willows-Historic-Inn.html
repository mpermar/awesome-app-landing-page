Description

Views of the San Jacinto Mountains or outdoor pool are included in all rooms at The Willows Historic Inn. The traditionally decorated rooms feature a sofa and a large private bath stocked with bath amenities and robes.

Breakfast at The Willows Historic Inn is served in a formal dining room with views of a waterfall. Specialty dishes include baked eggs with black truffle sauce and Parmesan, banana-stuffed French toast and homemade granola. Guests are also treated to an evening wine reception.
