Description

Each spacious room at Parker Palm Springs includes a flat-screen cable TV. The air-conditioned rooms feature unique décor and include an iron and an ironing board and a sitting area. Select rooms offer garden views.

Guests of Palm Springs Parker can enjoy a game croquet or play tennis on one of the resort’s clay tennis courts. After a spa treatment, guests can go for a swim in the resort’s saline pool. A family-friendly pool and a 24-hour “adults-only” pool are also available.

Serving a variety of bistro food in romantic atmosphere and open late night, Mister Parker’s bistro is on-site at Parker Palm Springs. Guests can also stop by Norma’s for diner-style comfort food and an all day breakfast menu.

Palm Springs town center is just 2 miles from this resort. Palm Springs Aerial Tram is a 10 minute drive from Parker Palm Springs.
