Description

Guest rooms at The Langham Huntington, Pasadena boast comfortable linens and luxurious bath products with an Italian marble bathroom. Guests will also enjoy flat-screen TVs and the well-stocked mini-bar.

Guests can chose several dining options at The Langham Huntington, Pasadena. These include The Royce | Wood-Fired Steakhouse, which features a selection of seasonal aged USDA Prime Cuts, Australian Wagyu and Japanese Kobe-Style Beef, prepared on a wood-fired grill and an interactive Sunday Market Brunch. For more casual dining, The Terrace serves California bistro-style fare and a Sunday a la carte brunch. Serving signature afternoon tea and hot chocolate, The Lobby Lounge provides picturesque views of the Horseshoe Gardens and San Marino, while The Tap Room serves handmade cocktails, artisanal beers on tap and innovative bar bites.

Guests can visit Chuan Spa at The Langham Huntington, Pasadena. The spa features 12 treatment rooms including a couple’s suite and VIP suite, locker facilities with showers, sauna, herbal steam and whirlpool, a fitness center and full service salon. The luxurious spa offers signature treatments inspired by Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM), cutting-edge skin care technology and boutique product lines. The Fitness Center offers TechnoGym equipment and scheduled classes including yoga, Tai Chi and both Mat/Reformer Pilates.

Rose Bowl Stadium is 3 miles away from the property. Los Angeles International Airport is 28 miles away from The Langham Huntington, Pasadena.
