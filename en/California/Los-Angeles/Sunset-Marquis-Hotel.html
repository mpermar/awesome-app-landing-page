Description

Villas at the property include unique amenities like private elevators, kitchens,dining rooms, televisions, and living rooms. Suites include similar amenities with toiletries and Bluetooth enabled mobile speakers.

There is an on-site bar available at the hotel. There is also a fitness center and spa available on-site. Guests can enjoy the gardens and 24-hour front desk. The property also is home to successful recording studios and an art gallery.

The Sunset Strip is less than 0.6 mi from the property, while the Farmer's Market Los Angeles is 1.7 mi away. Dolby Theater and the Hollywood Walk of Fame are 2.3 mi away. Los Angeles International Airport is 12 mi from the property.

The Sunset Marquis Hotel was a unique, magical experience...

"The Sunset Marquis Hotel was a unique, magical experience that stood out above the other hotels we have visited. It is tastefully decorated with artwork of iconic musicians and celebrities throughout, with water features, soft seating outdoor areas, and candlelit outdoor areas surrounded by fairy lights create atmosphere. Every detail was attended to during our stay. Hotel staff were friendly, like Jordan the bellman, who gave us a wonderful tour, local tips, and was great with the kids. The house keeping staff attended to everything, even arranging the kids bunnies on each bed, keeping everything peefect, and putting slippers by the beds. There were rubber ducks sitting on the bath to make it feel homely. Our children were delighted to receive individual surprise gift packs that were delivered to the door after arrival, including a "passport" to be stamped and activities to do to receive a treat at the concierge. The pool lounge chairs were soft and well maintained, the beds, bed covers, and pillows much softer and more comfortable than anywhere else. The bathroom had a gorgeous, deep standalone bath. There were fluffy bath robes to use, and high quality bathroom items - from mouthwash to a sewing kit - all the extra items, not just the basics, and in good quantities. The minibar was stocked with a nice variety of drinks, and a range of healthy and yummy snacks tastefully presented. Dinner at the hotel restaurant was absolutely divine (e.g. plump, mouthwatering sea scallops, cocktails) with great kids meals. The hotel is a short walk to the famous Sunset Boulevard, and a range of eating places. I highly recommend this hotel for a real holiday."
