Description

Beach House Half Moon Bay features a separate bedroom and living area in each spacious suite. Guests can enjoy in-suite microwaves and refrigerators. Patios or balconies are also included.

A continental breakfast is served daily at the Half Moon Bay Beach House. A free wine reception is available on Friday and Saturday evenings.

San Francisco is 25 miles from the Beach House in Half Moon Bay. The Half Moon Bay State Beach is a 5-minute drive from the hotel.
