Description

Featuring mountain views, the modern rooms at Agua Caliente Casino Resort Spa are bright and comfortably furnished. All rooms include luxury linens.

Conveniently located at the Rancho Mirage Agua Caliente Casino Resort Spa, the Sunstone Spa features body treatments and massages as well as hair style, design and color services. Couples therapy rooms with private outdoor decks are available.

The Palm Springs International Airport is a 20-minute drive from the resort. The Palm Springs Aerial Tram is a 20-minute drive away.
