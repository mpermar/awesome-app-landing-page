Description

The Doubletree Guest Suites Anaheim Resort/Convention Center is also situated only a few minutes drive from Orange County Airport as well as Knott's Berry Farm amusement park. Hollywood, Newport Beach and Universal Studios are also within driving distance, and are ideal for a short day trip.

Guests at the Anaheim Resort/Convention Center Doubletree can enjoy on-site dining at 3 different restaurants, Agio Ristorante, Grappa Lounge and Cafe Biscoti. The hotel also features a rooftop swimming pool, offering beautiful views of the surrounding area.
