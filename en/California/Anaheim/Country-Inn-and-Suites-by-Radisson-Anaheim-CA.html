Description

At the hotel, every room comes with a desk. Complete with a private bathroom equipped with free toiletries, guest rooms at Country Inn & Suites Anaheim have a flat-screen TV and air conditioning, and certain rooms include a seating area. The rooms will provide guests with a microwave.

A buffet breakfast is served each morning at the property.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool.

Anaheim Convention Center is a 16-minute walk from Country Inn & Suites by Radisson, Anaheim, CA. John Wayne Airport is 9 miles away.
