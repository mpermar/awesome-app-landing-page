Description

Holiday Inn Anaheim Resort Area has an outdoor pool with a whirlpool tub. Guests can sip on a drink from the seasonal poolside bar or work out in the 24-hour fitness center. For on-site dining, guests can enjoy American dishes in the casual American Brasserie restaurant.

The guest rooms at the Anaheim Resort Area Holiday Inn include several amenities for a comfortable stay. Guests can use the free, in-room wireless high-speed internet access or order a meal from the hotel's room service.
