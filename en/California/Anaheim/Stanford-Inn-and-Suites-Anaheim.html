Description

A flat-screen cable TV and a spacious work desk are provided in each air-conditioned guest room at Stanford Inn & Suites Anaheim. All rooms include coffee-making facilities, an in-room safe and a hairdryer. Free long distance calls are provided.

After a day of activity, guests can relax in the hot tub. A 24-hour front desk greets guests at the Anaheim Stanford Inn & Suites. Free parking for vehicles of all sizes is provided to all guests.

Anaheim Convention Center is just a 10-minute walk from this hotel.

The maximum number of extra beds/cribs in a room is 1.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
