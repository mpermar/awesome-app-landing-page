Description

Guests at the Best Western Plus Stovall's Inn will find numerous popular points of interest, including the Anaheim Convention Center only moments from the hotel. The Disneyland shuttle service also makes a stop right in front of the hotel, which allows easy access to all of the park's attractions.

The Best Western Plus Stovall's Inn strives to make every stay enjoyable and pleasant with amenities including a free breakfast, in-room microwaves and 2 on-site swimming pools. Guests can also easily purchase tickets to area theme parks and tours at the hotel's front desk.
