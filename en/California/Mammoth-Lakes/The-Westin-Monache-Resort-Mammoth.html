Description

Boasting a fireplace and a sofa bed, all guest accommodations at Westin Monache Resort are comfortably furnished. Free WiFi and a flat-screen TV are included.

A heated outdoor pool and 2 hot tubs are available at Westin Monache Resort Mammoth Lakes. A 24-hour fitness centre is located on-site.

Located on-site at Westin Monache, Whitebark Restaurant, Bar and Lounge offers contemporary ambiance, views of Mammoth and a gourmet dinner menu. Dishes include Pappardelle pasta, grilled stuffed Portobello and WesterRoss Scottish salmon. Decadent desserts are available. The lounge offers evening drinks and a menu with soups, salads and sandwiches.

Yosemite National Park is 1 hours’ drive from this resort. Yosemite/Mammoth Airport is 8 miles away.
