Description

A flat-screen HD cable TV with pay options is available in each room and suite at the Pechanga Resort and Casino. Floor-to-ceiling windows feature mountain or valley views. Premium bath amenities, a make-up mirror and hairdryer are included in the private bathroom. A fully-stocked minibar is provided.

Fine dining choices include upscale steaks at The Great Oak Steakhouse, Umi Sushi & Oyster Bar, a coffee-wine bar, a food court and Italian fare at Paisano’s. More casual dining options include an all-you-can-eat buffet, a Chinese/Vietnamese cafe, a casual diner, a sports bar and grill, a golf-course restaurant and an authentic Pan-Asian bistro.

A seasonal pool, hot tub and terrace with private cabanas is available for guest relaxation. A spa offers a full range of massage therapies and body treatments. A fully-equipped health club is located at the Pechanga Resort. Free valet parking is included.

Rancho California Road, home of over 20 Temecula Wineries are within a 10-minute drive of Pechanga Resort and Casino. Old Town Temecula is a 10-minute drive away.
