Description

Wine & Roses Hotel Restaurant Spa Lodi features a balcony or patio in every guest room. A refrigerator and coffeemaker are also provided. Guests will also enjoy free in-room Wi-Fi.

The restaurant at the Wine & Roses Lodi offers a historic dining room and outdoor dining on the porch, which is surrounded by gardens. The lounge offers a fireplace and comfortable seating.
