Description

A flat-screen TV is featured in each room at Embassy Suites Mandalay Beach. All 2-room suites include a living room with sofa bed, 2 bathrooms and a dining area with a microwave and refrigerator.

Free access to the outdoor pool, hot tub and business center is provided to all guests of the Mandalay Beach Embassy Suites. A state-of-the-art gym and on-site tennis court are available for recreation.

A cooked-to-order breakfast is served each morning and Coastal Grill Restaurant is open for lunch and dinner. There is a full bar menu in the evening.

Seabee Golf Course and Oxnard Airport are within 8 minutes' drive.
