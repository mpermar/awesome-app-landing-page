Description

Featuring a pillow-top mattress, each guest room at Madonna Inn has premium bedding, custom furnishings and a flat-screen satellite TV with pay options. Plush bathrobes, free spa toiletries and a free bottle of water are also provided in the private bathroom.

The famous Bakery and Pastry Shop features a variety of delectable treats daily at San Luis Obispo Madonna Inn. Copper Caf & Coffee Bar serves fresh coffee daily. Styled with lavish pink accents, Alex Madonnas Gold Rush Steak House serves fresh steaks and seafood in an elegant atmosphere. Located nearby, guests can enjoy cocktails at Silver Bar Cocktail & Lounge.

A fitness center, tennis and basketball courts, horseback riding, hiking and biking are available on site. The Spa at the Madonna Inn offers massage therapy and beauty treatments.

Historic California Mission is just 2 miles away. Hearst Castle is a 1-hour drive from Madonna Inn.
