Description

All units at Waldstrand-Hotel Großschönau come with a seating area and a flat-screen TV. Waldstrand-Hotel Großschönau offers some rooms that include a balcony, and the rooms have a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries. Guest rooms feature a desk.

A continental or buffet breakfast is available each morning at the property. Guests can dine in the on-site restaurant, which serves European cuisine.

Spa and wellness facilities including a hot tub, a sauna and an indoor pool are at guests' disposal during their stay at Waldstrand-Hotel Großschönau. The area is popular for skiing, and ski equipment hire and bike hire are available at Waldstrand-Hotel Großschönau.

The nearest airport is Dresden Airport, 43 mi from the property.
