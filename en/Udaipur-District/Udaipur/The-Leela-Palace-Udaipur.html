Description

Decorated with warm shades of golds and reds, rooms feature local artwork and fine Indian carpets. They come with a flat-screen TV and iPod dock. Marble bathrooms have a bathtub and premium toiletries.

Surrounded by lounge chairs, the large outdoor pool has an attached hot tub. Guests can also enjoy quiet moments at the peaceful Library Bar or take a day trip organized by the tour desk.

Soft breezes and views of a starlit sky accompany fine Indian specialties at the Sheesh Mahal. For an international dining experience, guests can head to the Dining Room at The Leela Palace.

In “the city of lakes,” Leela Palace Udaipur is within 2.5 miles of the center of the city and City Palace Museum. It is a 45 minute drive from Maharana Pratap Airport.

Experiences

07/18/2016

One of the best luxury hotels !!

"We visited last weekend for a 3 night stay with my wife and kids - 10 years and 1.5 years. We were offered a free airport pick up with a very friendly and caring driver giving us a history of Udaipur along with way.. Hotel is located right at Lake Pichhola in vicinity of Taj Lake Palace. We have to take the hotel boat from their own jetty to reach the hotel across Lake Pichhola. The ride is super pleasant and the boats were very nice. The welcome was unprecedented with a folk singer welcoming us with a folk song followed by the traditional teeka. A family photograph was taken which was a surprise memento neatly framed at the time of check out. Upon entering the hotel, we were straight escorted to our room where a refreshing drink awaited us. We were offered a free upgrade to a balcony room (Thank my stars for not booking a balcony room which was being initially considered). Our check in procedure was completed in OUR ROOM - My first experience of so many years of extensive world-wide travel. The hotel did not charge us for my 10 year old except for a very nominal charge for a few meals. My 18 month old is a fussy eater and the hotel was kind enough to accommodate every request for a customised meal each time for her, which turned out perfect as well. Every hotel employee right from the guard at the jetty to the front office people, the ones at the restaurant made us feel very special. The hotel has a beautiful open deck by the lake with fountains and water pools. Although the open area is not very big (compared to the massive Leela Goa - our stay last year), this was never really missed. My wife and I had a 60 min treatment at the Spa. I'm an avid Spa lover and can easily say the best one I ever had was here. There is plenty to write about the different pleasant experiences we had, but I'll leave it at that."
