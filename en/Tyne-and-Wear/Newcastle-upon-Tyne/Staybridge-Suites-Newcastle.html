Description

Staybridge Suites Newcastle is an all-suites hotel offering both studio and one-bedroom suites. All of the suites are stylishly furnished and each one has a living room area, a work space and a flat-screen TV with cable and movie channels. Every suite has contemporary comforts and facilities.

The suites are just a few hundred feet from the shops, bars and restaurants of the Quayside area and some have views of the river and the Millennium Bridge. The Baltic Centre, the Tyne Bridge and the eye-catching Sage Gateshead are also within easy walking distance.

A generous buffet breakfast is included in the price and on-site parking is available for an additional fee. Guests can use the free laundry room, as well as the fitness center.
