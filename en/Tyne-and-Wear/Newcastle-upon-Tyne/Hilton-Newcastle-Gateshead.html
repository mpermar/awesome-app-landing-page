Description

The Hilton Newcastle Gateshead is 1,300 feet from the Sage Gateshead and less than a mile from Newcastle Central Train Station.

The bar/lounge and the Windows on the Tyne restaurant have views of the Tyne Bridge and the river. The restaurant serves modern international cuisine.

Rooms have private bathrooms, work desks, minibars and TVs with video games and pay-per-view channels. The Hilton Newcastle also has a business center and an Executive Lounge.

The LivingWell Health Club has a modern gym, a 50 feet pool and a spa, sauna, steam room, solarium and beauty room.
