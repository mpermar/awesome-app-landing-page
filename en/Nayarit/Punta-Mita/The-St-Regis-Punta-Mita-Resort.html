Description

At the St. Regis Punta Mita Resort, guest rooms features an iPod docking station and a minibar. The spacious rooms and suites are furnished with a refrigerator and flat-screen satellite TVs with pay-per-view movies.

On 22 tropical acres, the Punta Mita St. Regis Resort includes 3 outdoor pools and a children’s club. Tennis courts and 2 golf courses are also available.

A variety of dining options are also provided at the St. Regis Punta Mita. Fresh seafood is available at the hotel’s signature restaurant, Carolina. Guests can also enjoy classic Mexican cuisine at Las Marietas or 24-hour room service.

Whale watching tours, exotic wildlife at Marietas Island and scuba diving in Banderas Bay are a short walk from Punta Mita Resort.
