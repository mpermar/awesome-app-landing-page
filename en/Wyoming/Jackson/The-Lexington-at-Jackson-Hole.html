Description

The Lexington at Jackson Hole features suites with a kitchen with coffee-making facilities or a fireplace. The suites include a living room and 2 TVs.

Jackson Hole’s The Lexington offers a daily breakfast buffet and free Wi-Fi. The hotel provides an indoor pool and hot tub. Laundry facilities are available at the hotel as well. A transfer service to Jackson Ski Resort if provided during winter months.

The entrance to Grand Teton National Park is 4.7 mi from The Lexington.
