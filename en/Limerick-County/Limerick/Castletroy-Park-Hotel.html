Description

Next to the National Technological Park, the Castletroy Park Hotel is close to Shannon International Airport and the main highways. There is a bus stop located outside the hotel with regular services to the center of the city and Shannon Airport.

The Aqua and Leisure Club features a relaxing sauna, and guests have free access to all of the recreational facilities. The Beauty Salon also offers a relaxing massage or full body scrub.

Traditional Irish breakfasts are cooked to order each morning in the award-winning McLaughlin’s Restaurant. Guests can enjoy tea and scones in the conservatory, or a range of beers and homemade Irish cuisine in The Merry Pedlar Bar.
