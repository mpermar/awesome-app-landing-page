Description

Each spacious suite features separate living and sleeping areas and a dining table. Free Wi-Fi, a 32-inch flat screen TV and DVD player are standard amenities in all suites at Oconomowoc Staybridge Suites.

Guests can relax in the indoor pool or work out in the 24-hour gym. A business center is on site as well as a courtyard with barbecue facilities.

Staybridge Suites Milwaukee West-Oconomowoc offers a hot breakfast buffet every morning. Snacks are available at the 24-hour BridgeMart.

This hotel is situated within a 10-minute drive of numerous lakes including Lac La Belle and Oconomowoc Lake. Johnson Creek Premium Outlets is a 20-minute drive from this hotel and the Milwaukee Zoo is 30 minutes away.
