Description

At the hotel, each room is equipped with a closet, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Hotel Klaudia provides certain rooms that have a balcony, and every room comes with a electric tea pot.

A buffet breakfast is available each morning at the property.

At the accommodation guests are welcome to use a sauna. You can play ping pong at Hotel Klaudia, and the area is popular for skiing.

Krakow is 28 miles from the hotel, while Zakopane is 29 miles from the property. The nearest airport is John Paul II International KrakwBalice Airport, 27 miles from the property.
