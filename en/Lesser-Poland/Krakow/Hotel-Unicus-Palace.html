Description

All rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Rooms contain a private bathroom, while some feature a city view. The rooms feature a desk.

A buffet breakfast is available every morning at the property.

Hotel Unicus Palace has a terrace. The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental and car rental are available at the accommodation.

With staff speaking English and Polish, information is available at the reception.

Cloth Hall is 900 feet from Hotel Unicus Palace, while Adam Mickiewicz Monument is 900 feet away. The nearest airport is John Paul II International Kraków–Balice Airport, 6 miles from the hotel.

Old Town is a great choice for travelers interested in food, restaurants and history.
