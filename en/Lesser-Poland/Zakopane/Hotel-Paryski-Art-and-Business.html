Description

Guest rooms come with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, fridge, a electric tea pot, a shower, free toiletries and a desk. The hotel has some accommodations with mountain views, and each room is equipped with a balcony. All guest rooms include a closet.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property.

The property has a hot tub and a sauna. Guests can take part in various activities in the surrounding areas, including skiing and hiking.

Speaking English and Polish at the reception, staff are always at hand to help.

Zakopane Aqua Park is 0.9 miles from Hotel Paryski Art & Business, while Harenda Ski Station is 1.5 miles away. John Paul II International Kraków–Balice Airport is 54 miles from the property.
