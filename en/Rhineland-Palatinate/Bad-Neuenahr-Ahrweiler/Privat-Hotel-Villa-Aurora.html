Description

The rooms at the Privat-Hotel Villa Aurora have a modern bathroom, cable TV channels and Wi-Fi. All floors are easily accessible by elevator.

A German-style breakfast buffet is served each morning at the Villa Aurora. In the evenings, local and seasonal dishes are available in the hotel restaurant.

Free parking spaces are available next to the Aurora. Guests can also reserve spaces in the secure underground parking garage.

The Kurgarten (spa gardens) are just 650 feet away.
