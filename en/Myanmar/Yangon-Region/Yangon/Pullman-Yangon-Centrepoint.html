Description

All rooms at the hotel come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a bath. Pullman Yangon Centrepoint provides some rooms with pool views, and every room includes a electric tea pot. Guest rooms at the accommodation include air conditioning and a desk.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at Pullman Yangon Centrepoint. At the hotel you'll find a restaurant serving sushi, Italian and international cuisine.

Guests have access to the on-site business center where they can use the on-site ATM machine or check the newspapers.

Shwedagon Pagoda is 1.9 miles from Pullman Yangon Centrepoint. Yangon International Airport is 9 miles away.

Downtown Yangon is a great choice for travelers interested in street food, street markets and city walks.
