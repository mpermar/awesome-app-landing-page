Description

A 10-minute walk from Inya Lake, Hotel Parami is located in Yangon. This hotel boasts a swimming pool and a rooftop bar with panoramic city views.

Free WiFi access is available. It is a 5-minute drive from Kabar Aye Pagoda and 8.

6 km from Sule Pagoda. Yangon International Airport is a 30-minute drive away. Air-conditioned rooms provide a flat-screen cable TV, a seating area and a safety deposit box.

An electric kettle and a minibar are also included. Featuring a bath, the en suite bathroom comes with a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

At Hotel Parami you will find a 24-hour front desk. Café Café restaurant serves local cuisine and evening entertainment, while cocktails can be enjoyed at rooftop Piano Bar & Grill.

Arrangements can be made for pampering massages or car hire. Drivers enjoy free private parking.
