Description

This hotel is located 150 meters from Santandria Beach, on the west coast of Menorca. It offers a large outdoor pool, air-conditioned rooms with a private balcony and free Wi-Fi throughout.

Prinsotel La Caleta's rooms are elegantly decorated in soft tones and feature satellite TV and a private bathroom.

The buffet restaurant serves international cuisine and offers live cooking and theme nights. You can have breakfast contemplating the sea, and next to the pool there is a cafeteria.

Hotel La Caleta has a varied entertainment program for adults and children and a 24-hour reception with bicycle rental service. On Santandria beach you can practice water sports.

The hotel is 5 minutes drive from Ciutadella and about 45 minutes drive from Mahón airport.
