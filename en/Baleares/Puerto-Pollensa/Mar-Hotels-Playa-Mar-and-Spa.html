Description

The apartments are equipped with a balcony, a bedroom with 2 single beds, a living room with a sofa bed and a TV, and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The complex has an air-conditioned restaurant, bar, cafeteria, several lounges, TV and video room and free Wi-Fi in public areas. The Playa Mar & Spa also offers a 24-hour reception and a car or bicycle rental service. An airport shuttle service is also available for a fee.
