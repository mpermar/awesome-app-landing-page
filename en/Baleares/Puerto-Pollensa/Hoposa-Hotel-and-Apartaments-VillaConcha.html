Description

All apartments at Hoposa Villaconcha include a balcony, kitchenette with microwave and fridge and satellite TV.

The Hoposa Villaconcha has a cafeteria with terrace and views of the pool, an inflatable castle, pool table, kids club and water activities for the little ones.

The surrounding area is pleasant and quiet and guests can dive and go horse riding nearby.

Sports facilities at the establishment include a heated outdoor pool, fully equipped gym, spa and bicycle facilities, such as free bicycle storage, workshop, tools and washing equipment. There are also areas to relax, exercise or enjoy massages and a TV room with sports channels. The gym is free to use, but other facilities carry a surcharge.
