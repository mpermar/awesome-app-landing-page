Description

All apartments in the complex have a private balcony overlooking the pool, gardens or the sea. They also include free Wi-Fi, a Nespresso machine and air conditioning.

The complex has incredible sea views and has a large terrace, cycling center, miniature golf facilities and games room. The fitness center is for adults only and includes a sauna, thermal circuits and massage service.

This complex offers activities, shows and a full entertainment program. There is also a supermarket, a bar and a traditional restaurant serving buffet breakfast.

Free public parking is available nearby. The Club del Sol is located in the north of Mallorca, a short drive from the S'Albufera natural park.
