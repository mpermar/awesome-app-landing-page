Description

All rooms have air conditioning, satellite TV, dining table, private bathroom with hairdryer and free toiletries and free Wi-Fi. Some rooms have a terrace and offer views of the historic center of Palma.

The hotel is modernly decorated and features a restaurant, bar, 24-hour reception, concierge service and a shared terrace and library.

Boutique Hotel Posada Terra Santa is located 5 minutes' drive from Cala Mayor Beach and 5 km from Son Vida Golf Resort. Son Sant Joan Airport is 7 km away.
