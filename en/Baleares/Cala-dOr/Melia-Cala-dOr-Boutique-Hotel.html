Description

Cala d'Or Melia is located in one of the most important tourist areas of Mallorca, a few minutes from the center of Cala d'Or, Cala Gran and Cala Ferrera. In the surroundings you can enjoy the beautiful nature of this Mediterranean island, as well as its quality facilities and services.

The Meliá Cala d'Or offers luxurious rooms. In addition, it has free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel, internet area, outdoor pool with hot tub and children's pool surrounded by gardens. It also houses several restaurants and bars, including the Es Llaüt restaurant and the Sa Pastera By Night (open from June 15 to September 15), the Sa Pastera bar / cafeteria and the Wistaria piano bar.

This elegant hotel also has a central solarium and gardens. The rooms have an elegant Mediterranean design and are equipped with air conditioning and free Wi-Fi. Junior suites also include kitchen and lounge.

Es Llaüt Restaurant serves breakfast and dinner with local and international cuisine. For its part, the Sa Pastera bar, located next to the pool, offers drinks throughout the day and snacks at noon. During the summer season, it also opens at night and offers dinner in a quiet and ideal environment.

The hotel spa has a heated pool, hot tub, sauna, steam room, bithermal shower and gym. A massage and beauty treatment service is also provided. The rate includes free access to the spa and gym.
