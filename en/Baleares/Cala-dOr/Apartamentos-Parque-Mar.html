Description

The Parque Mar apartments have a heated pool, a saltwater pool and a children's pool in its facilities. The sunbeds are free. The establishment also has a tennis and volleyball court, table tennis and billiards.

The Parque Mar apartments are located in very large gardens and the establishment staff prepares daily entertainment activities for children and adults, such as a children's club and a nightclub. Guests can also practice taichi, go to painting classes or go Nordic walking.

The restaurant serves a varied buffet and themed dinners with live music. There is also a bar and a cafeteria next to the pool.

The Vall d'Or golf course is a 15-minute drive away, the Cala Mondragó nature reserve is 14 km away and the Palma de Mallorca airport is 1 hour away by car.
