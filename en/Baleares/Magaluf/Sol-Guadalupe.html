Description

The bright rooms feature air conditioning, satellite TV, small fridge, safe and private bathroom with hairdryer. They also have a large terrace overlooking the pool, mountains or the sea.

The Sol Guadalupe restaurant offers buffet-style meals and show cooking in the dining room, with air conditioning in summer. There is an Italian corner and a poolside bar.

The hotel organizes a day and night entertainment program with fitness, taichi and yoga sessions. There is also a playground and a mini club for the little ones. Free WiFi is provided throughout the premises.

The property has a 24-hour reception, tour desk and car rental service. Parking is available for a fee. The hotel is 2 km from a water park and a 5-minute drive from the Poniente golf course.

The reservation includes a free Sol Katmandu Fun Pass, which allows you to enjoy discounts at the Kathmandu amusement park and the Wave House water park.
