Description

The hotel overlooks the sea and the mountains and has a hot tub and 2 tennis courts. There is a tennis coach available, sauna and wellness center. In addition, free boat excursions are organized in summer.

All hotel rooms are unique and decorated in traditional Mallorcan style, with period furniture. The WiFi connection is free in all facilities. Flowers and fresh fruit are delivered to the room every day.

The Belmond La Residencia houses a permanent art exhibition and a sculpture garden. Classical music concerts are also offered and there is a Steinway piano. Fun walks are organized through the olive groves on the donkeys of the establishment. Staff can arrange a private airport shuttle service.

The hotel's à la carte restaurant, El Olivo, has been awarded. The Miró café has a casual atmosphere and offers tapas and local cuisine. There is also a poolside bar and restaurant, as well as free private parking.
