Description

The complex offers everything you need for a fun family holiday fun. It has 2 large swimming pools with a children's pool, mini club and children's playground, entertainment programs, a free spa and wellness center, a conference room with capacity for 40 people and a sports center.

Grupotel Gran Vista & Spa is 12 km from Alcudia and 50 km from Palma de Mallorca. Palma de Mallorca Airport is 50 minutes away by car.
