Experiences

05/25/2016

Utterly blown away by this place.

"Utterly blown away by this place. We literally could not believe what we got for our money! The hotel has about 16 rooms, a few of which are suites, like the one we stayed in. My partner and I run a luxury Motor Yacht so we know what proper service, and attention to detail is. This place had that in spades and the staff made us feel right at home. Big, VERY comfy four poster bed, that had that 'home' laundered smell. Huge bathroom with a bath and separate shower wet room with a large shower head. We also had a terrace outside with two sun loungers to enjoy the evening before dinner. Dinner was fantastic. Great menu options, delicious food and the waiter paired our wine with our food perfectly. My partner had a massage in the room the following morning, €35 for 30 minutes and she loved it. The surroundings were breathtaking and it showed us a side of Mallorca we never knew existed. We would highly recommend this place to everybody - it might not be the best spot for young children though as it has a relaxed, quiet vibe to it."
