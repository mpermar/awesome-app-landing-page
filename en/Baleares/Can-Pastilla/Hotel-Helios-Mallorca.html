Description

Hotel Helios Mallorca has an indoor pool, miniature golf and table tennis. In addition, leisure activities are organized every day.

This establishment is the ideal option for cycling enthusiasts, as it has a large garage for bicycles and an area to clean them.

The buffet restaurant includes live cooking and serves a selection of regional and international dishes. During the summer months, drinks and snacks can be enjoyed on the outdoor terrace.

The Helios Hotel Mallorca is next to the Palma Aquarium. Just in front of the hotel there are buses that connect to the city center of Palma and the airport of Palma de Mallorca.
