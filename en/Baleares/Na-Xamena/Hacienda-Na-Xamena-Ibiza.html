Description

The spa at the Hotel Hacienda Na Xamena has indoor and outdoor areas, including the Thalasso Suspended Thalassotherapy circuit, which offers fabulous views. There is a massage area located in a bamboo hut, facing the sunset in the west.

The Na Xamena has 2 restaurants, including a poolside bar that serves snacks and drinks for casual meals throughout the day and a very elegant Mediterranean-inspired space with terraces on several levels overlooking the sea.

Rooms at Hotel Hacienda Na Xamena, bright and air-conditioned, feature a flat-screen TV, minibar, private bathroom and private hot tub. Many have a small pool overlooking the Mediterranean Sea.

The reception is open 24 hours a day and staff can provide information about what Ibiza offers and help organize transportation, car rental and restaurant or nightclub reservations.

Hotel Hacienda Na Xamena is a 30-minute drive from the city of Ibiza and 35 minutes from Sant Antoni de Portmany. Es Canar, with its famous hippy market, is 30 minutes away by car.
