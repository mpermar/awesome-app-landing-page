Description

The Bon Sol has a Moorish tower with magnificent views of the bay of southern Mallorca. The hotel offers lush gardens, terraces, dream pools, spa facilities, tennis courts and a conservatory for winter. You can rest on the small sandy beach washed by the Mediterranean Sea.

The interior decoration features elements of Arab influence and includes antique-style furniture and precious crafts, which create a medieval castle atmosphere. This unique environment makes the hotel a cozy, informal and familiar place.
