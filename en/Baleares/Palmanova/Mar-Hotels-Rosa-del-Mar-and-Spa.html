Description

Rosa del Mar's studios and apartments are functional and feature a private balcony, satellite TV and kitchenette. They include air conditioning and heating, depending on the season.

The restaurant at Aparthotel Rosa del Mar serves buffet meals and offers show cooking. There is also a bar on site. Evening shows are organized and there is a daytime entertainment program for both children and adults.

The aparthotel is located in the center of Palma Nova, in an area full of bars, restaurants and shops.

Several water sports can be practiced on the nearby San Matías beach. The golf courses of Bendinat and Santa Ponsa are 6 km away. Palma Airport is 20 km away.
