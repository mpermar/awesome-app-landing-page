Description

All rooms at Castell Son Claret have a design and individual size. All rooms and suites are elegant and include air conditioning, Egyptian cotton linens and flat-screen TVs. The luxurious bathroom is equipped with a large shower, bathrobes, slippers and free designer toiletries. There is free wired internet and Wi-Fi in all rooms.

The suites are elegantly designed and have a private terrace or garden and a separate living area with dining table and sofa.

The Claret Bellesa spa offers a variety of treatments, such as outdoor massages in the hotel's beautiful gardens.

The Castell bar, located in the old chapel, offers exclusive cocktails and a wide selection of whiskeys. The hotel has 2 restaurants, Olivera and Zaranda, winner of Michelin stars. Castell Son Claret also has meeting rooms inside a restored mill, located on the same grounds.

Puerto Portals is 14 km from Castell Son Claret and the center of Palma de Mallorca, 25 km away.
