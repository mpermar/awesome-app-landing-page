Description

This accommodation is 1 minute walk from the beach. Iberostar Albufera Park is located 100 meters from the Albufera de Mallorca Natural Park and offers direct access to Muro Beach. It also has a lagoon pool, an excellent spa and sports facilities. There is also free high-speed WiFi throughout.

Albufera's rooms are spacious and air conditioned. All are decorated in bright colors and have a private terrace or balcony and satellite TV. The family rooms have a kitchenette and a seating area.

The hotel offers gym classes and has a fitness center, paddle tennis court and tennis courts. It also provides a bicycle loan service. In addition, the Sensations spa is equipped with a sauna, indoor pool and hot tub, and provides beauty treatments and massages. You can check in through the hotel's website.

Iberostar Albufera Park has 3 buffet-style restaurants serving local and international cuisine prepared by its own chefs. There is also live entertainment during the summer season, which includes performances and dance shows.
