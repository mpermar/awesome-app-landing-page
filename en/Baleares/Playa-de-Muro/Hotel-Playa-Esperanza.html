Description

All rooms at the Esperanza hotel are bright and equipped with air conditioning. They also have a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom.

The buffet restaurant serves a wide variety of Mediterranean cuisine and has a terrace overlooking Alcudia Bay. There is also a poolside bar and a lobby bar with free Wi-Fi.

The spa features an indoor pool, hot tub and sauna, and offers treatments such as massages, wraps, and reflexology.

Playa Esperanza is just 100 meters from the S'Albufera Nature Reserve, an ideal place for hiking and cycling. The nearest golf club is in Alcudia, a 15-minute drive away.
