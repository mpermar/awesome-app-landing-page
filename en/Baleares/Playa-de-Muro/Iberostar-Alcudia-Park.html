Description

All rooms have satellite TV. They also have a private bathroom with shower.

There is a terrace with sun loungers and large Mediterranean gardens. The complex offers activities and entertainment during the day and night, and there is a children's mini club. Guests of Star Prestige rooms have access to the exclusive Star Prestige exclusive adult area, which includes a relaxation area with drinks and snacks, a solarium with Balinese beds and a pool or hot tub.

The Alcudia Park Complex has a café bar and a buffet restaurant with show cooking.

The hotel organizes a wide variety of activities, such as bicycle tours organized by the Max Hürzeler Bicycle Holidays team.

The Iberostar Alcudia Park is located next to the natural park of S'Albufera de Mallorca. Bike rental service is offered. Public parking is available in the surrounding streets.
