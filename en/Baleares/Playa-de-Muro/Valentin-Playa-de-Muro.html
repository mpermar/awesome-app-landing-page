Description

Valentin Playa de Muro is located in the S'Albufera Nature Reserve in Mallorca, 500 meters from the central Can Picafort. Outside the complex there is a bus stop. Free parking is provided.

All bungalows are bright and house a huge terrace with sun loungers. They also have air conditioning, a seating area with satellite TV and a kitchenette with a microwave and hob.

The Valentin Playa de Muro offers repair and loan service for bicycles. The establishment also has a gym and sports court to play football, basketball and tennis.

The Valentin Playa de Muro has a buffet restaurant with a terrace next to the pool. The restaurant serves Mediterranean and international cuisine.
