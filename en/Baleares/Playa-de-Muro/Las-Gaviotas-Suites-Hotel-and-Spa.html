Description

Las Gaviotas has elegant suites and rooms with private balconies, free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs. Bathrobes are also included.

The hotel is close to the beautiful natural park of S'Albufera, in the bay of Alcúdia. The historic center of Port d'Alcúdia is located about 5 km away.

Las Gaviotas Suites Hotel & Spa has a free spa, gym and hot tub. Massages are provided for a fee. There is a quieter pool area that is for adults only and a poolside bar, serving international dishes.

The hotel reception has a tour desk, currency exchange and car rental.
