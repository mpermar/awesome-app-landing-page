Description

There are also sports courts, tennis court, bar, cafeteria, TV room, games room, children's club, children's playground, air-conditioned restaurant, and free Wi-Fi in the bar.

The hotel has 397 accommodations distributed in 5 buildings with elevator, and there are accommodations of 2 types: apartments and rooms.

All rooms have air conditioning, mini fridge, safe, balcony, private bathroom, satellite TV, and telephone and WiFi for a fee.

The resort's buffet restaurant is international cuisine, and there are snacks, drinks and ice cream at the bar. The complex offers a variety of activities for all ages.
