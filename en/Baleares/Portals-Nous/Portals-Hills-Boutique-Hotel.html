Description

The elegant Portals Hills Boutique Hotel is a 15-minute walk from the Portals Marina. It has gardens with an outdoor pool, restaurant, poolside bar and disco.

The accommodations are spacious and modern and elegantly decorated. They have spectacular sea views, a private balcony, air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, a seating area, an iPod docking station, a flat-screen TV, a minibar and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and bathrobes. Some suites include Fendi furniture, rooftop terrace and private elevator.

The Portals Hills Boutique Hotel offers a concierge service and features a modern fitness center and a wellness lounge.

It is 3 km from Real Bendinat Golf Course and 23 km from Palma de Mallorca Airport.
