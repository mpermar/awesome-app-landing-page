Description

It is in our selection for Punta Prima. Occidental Menorca is located in the southeast of Menorca, 300 meters from the beach of Punta Prima. It has 5 outdoor swimming pools, water park, spa and air-conditioned rooms with private balconies.

Rooms at Occidental Menorca feature modern décor and are equipped with tiled floors, free Wi-Fi and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

This hotel has a buffet restaurant that serves international food and offers show cooking. There is also a bar by the pool and another in the sports area.

Occidental Menorca has tennis courts.

Menorca Airport, located on the outskirts of Mahón, is only a 20-minute drive away.
