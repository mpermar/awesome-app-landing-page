Description

The apartments feature a balcony or terrace, air conditioning, a kitchenette with a microwave and a refrigerator, and a lounge area with 2 sofa beds and a flat-screen satellite TV. High chairs are provided, upon request and for a fee.

The Sea Club complex has a buffet restaurant serving international cuisine, 3 poolside bars offering drinks and snacks and 2 supermarkets. There is also free WiFi in the lobby area.

The reception can organize car rental and bicycle rental services, as well as excursions to local attractions such as the S'Albufera National Park, located about 5 km away. The hotel offers playgrounds and an entertainment program. In addition, there is free public parking directly opposite.
