Description

All rooms are air-conditioned and feature 1 double bed or 2 single beds. They have a desk, an electric kettle and a private bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer. Some rooms have a terrace.

This boutique-style establishment also features a garden with lemon, fig and almond trees and a barbecue area.

The beaches of Alcudia and Pollença are a 20-minute drive from the hotel.
