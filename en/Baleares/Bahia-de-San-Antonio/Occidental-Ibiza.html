Description

The rooms include air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, a minibar and a private bathroom with a hairdryer. There is also free WiFi.

The buffet restaurant at Occidental Ibiza has a terrace. There is also a lounge bar and a pool bar, open during the summer.

The hotel is just 6 km from San Antonio and 18 km from the city of Ibiza and its airport.
