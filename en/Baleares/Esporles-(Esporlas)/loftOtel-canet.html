Description

The lofts are air conditioned and occupy remodeled barns with modern décor. They also have an open-plan living area, a fully equipped kitchenette and, for a fee, Wi-Fi. Some also have a terrace, fireplace or hot tub.

The loftOtel Canet has a barbecue area, a small water park, table tennis, miniature golf and a restaurant serving local dishes.

In addition, the loftOtel Canet is 4 km from Esporles and a 20-minute drive from Mallorca International Airport.

Lovely place to stay with kids

"LoftOtel Canet has a really nice vibe - it's like a little village with all the accommodation in separate houses set round a wiggly courtyard! The pool is great, with a large wading pool at one end and a large normal pool at the other, and there are some fabulous double bed-type recliners with curtains for shade. The restaurant is really excellent - everything we tried during our 3-day stay was fresh and delicious. The staff are very happy to help, there's a tennis court which you can borrow rackets and balls for and the play fountains were amazing. My only very small niggle was that our bedroom only had double beds (which were very hard) rather than twin beds so we had to share a bed with our children and didn't sleep very well. Would happily go again."

One child under 14 years is charged EUR 30 per person per night in an extra bed.
One older child or adult is charged EUR 50 per person per night in an extra bed.
Maximum number of cots in the room: 2.
Extra beds and / or cots are available upon request and must be confirmed by the accommodation.
Supplements are not automatically calculated in the total amount and must be paid separately during the stay.
