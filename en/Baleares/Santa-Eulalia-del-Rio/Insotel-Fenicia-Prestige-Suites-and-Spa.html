Description

The spacious rooms have a balcony, a flat-screen TV and a bathroom with a separate bathtub, hydromassage shower, bathrobe and slippers.

The spa, which can be used for a fee, is equipped with indoor and outdoor pools, herbal steam room and ice fountain. Foot baths and various massages are also offered.

The establishment houses 4 restaurants that prepare a selection of Mediterranean, international and Asian dishes. In addition, the Insotel Fenicia Prestige Suites & Spa has several bars serving snacks, cocktails and ice cream.

In the Insotel Fenicia Prestige Suites & Spa you can perform various activities, as well as rent yachts. Also, less than 6 km from the hotel are several golf courses.

09/23/2016

Worth a second visit...

"This is a well appointed modern hotel which we have stayed in before. Our junior suite was spacious and very comfortable with balcony view over the kids pool. The mini bar although well-stocked was expensive - a bottle of water is five euros. There are a large selection of TV channels. The large bed is on the firm side and there is a separate sofa bed if you have a child. It was kept very clean and fresh towels daily. The bathroom also had a separate multi-jet shower which if I were being picky, was a bit small. Good quality hairdryer, slippers and dressing gown were also welcome. There is plenty wardrobe/hanging space too. The buffet breakfast is 'free' and every conceivable requirement is met. If you want to drink prosecco, eat a selection of ice-creams or freshly made crepes, eggs, bread, fill your boots and go back for more. The pool is beautiful and clean. Fresh beach towels are free from the Life Guard. There are plenty of sunbeds around the poolside but beware the ones on the grass as the mattresses are soaked daily by the sprinklers. The poolside bar is lovely, the staff are extremely pleasant but it is expensive. We have eaten in the main restaurant when we stayed previously but avoided it this time as there as so many lovely restaurants on the 'munchie mile' or along in the marina - try Skuma.The main reception is cool and spacious with a cocktail bar and two verandas. There is table service should you choose to sit outside but be patient. They occasionally have some evening entertainment but otherwise there is little atmosphere. We made use of the library - I read a couple of books that had been left by other guest and we accessed the internet/printer to print out club tickets. We did not use the spa which looks beautiful but is expensive.Santa Eulalia is beautiful and this hotel reflects its surroundings - away from the hubbub of San Antonio and Ibiza Town."
