Description

One of the bestsellers in Playa de Palma! Iberostar Cristina is located among pine trees, just 100 meters from the beach of Playa de Palma. It was renovated in 2015 and features a relaxing spa area and an outdoor pool.

Free WiFi is available throughout, plus premium high-speed WiFi is available for a fee.

The rooms include a private terrace or balcony, private bathroom and air conditioning.

The hotel restaurant prepares buffet meals and there is also a seasonal cafeteria in the pool area that offers snacks all day.

The Iberostar Cristina's spa, Spa Sensations, has a fitness center and a seasonal indoor pool. In addition, there is a complete wellness service with massages by highly qualified professionals. It also consists of a tennis court, a soccer field and 3 paddle courts.

This hotel offers unique services for cyclists and sports enthusiasts, such as a repair shop and a garage. A bicycle loan service is provided and there are special menus, a sports shop and specialized equipment. Iberostar Cristina also organizes excursions for adventure lovers.

The reception, which has a car rental and bicycle rental service, provides useful information about Playa de Palma and its attractions. The hotel is located 5 km from Palma de Mallorca and about 14 km from the port of Bahía de Palma.
