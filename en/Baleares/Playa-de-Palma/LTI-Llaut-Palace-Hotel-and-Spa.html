Description

Llaut Palace Hotel is located on the beach in Palma and features an outdoor pool and sun terrace. The hotel also has a spa center, hot tub, bar and private parking.

All hotel rooms include air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, slippers and a hairdryer. Llaut Palace Hotel offers free Wi-Fi throughout.

The establishment houses 3 restaurants; an Asian fusion restaurant, a bistro restaurant and a buffet restaurant.

The reception is open 24 hours. There is a bicycle storage area available for a fee.

The hotel also provides car rental service. Llaut Palace Hotel is 2.8 km from Palma Aquarium, 200 meters from Palma Beach and 4 km from Palma de Mallorca Airport, the nearest.
