Description

The accommodation features a flat-screen TV and kitchenette. All rooms have a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries. The rooms at Agroturisme Can Bessol are air conditioned and have a desk.

A continental breakfast is served.

Playa de Palma is 44 km away and Cala Millor is 27 km away. The property is 45 km from Palma de Mallorca Airport.
