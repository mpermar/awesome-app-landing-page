Description

This accommodation is a 2-minute walk from the beach. This complex is located on large landscaped grounds and enjoys stunning views of Cala Mesquida beach. It houses outdoor pools surrounded by sun terraces with sun beds and umbrellas.

The Viva Cala Mesquida Resort aparthotel offers accommodation with a flat-screen TV and free Wi-Fi. The apartments also have a balcony and air conditioning.

Spa facilities at Viva Cala Mesquida include a sauna and a Turkish bath. There is also a heated pool surrounded by sun loungers and massage service is provided.

The Viva has several themed restaurants, such as Mexican or Italian food, as well as a poolside bar, which serves cocktails, snacks and ice cream.

This seasonal hotel is open from April to October.
