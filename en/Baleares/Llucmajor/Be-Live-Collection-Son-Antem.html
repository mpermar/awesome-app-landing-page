Description

Guests have free access to the 3,000 m² gym and spa. A wide variety of beauty treatments and massages, indoor pool, sauna, steam room and hot tub are provided. Pool towels are also provided.

Rooms at the Be Live Collection Son Antem feature elegant décor, air conditioning and a balcony or terrace overlooking the golf course or the outdoor pool. They also include a Nespresso coffee machine with 4 free capsules.

The restaurant El Olivar of the Be Live Collection Son Antem proposes Spanish food and opens for breakfast, lunch and dinner. There is also a grill restaurant that opens from June 15 to September 15. The Llevant, the hotel lobby bar, offers free live music shows every night. In addition, the Es Trenc bar, located next to the pool, serves drinks and light snacks. Guests with full board and all-inclusive rates can enjoy Sunday brunch.

This hotel has an entertainment program for adults and has a children's club. It also houses a professional golf shop, a social club and a golf school. Free shuttle service to the beach is provided, in high season. The 3 18-hole golf courses at the Be Live Collection Son Antem complex are the Son Antem (where there are 2 courses) and the Maioris Golf, located just 17 km away.

The ferry port of Palma de Mallorca is about 25 minutes away by car and offers frequent connections with Barcelona, Ibiza and Valencia. Palma de Mallorca Airport is 18 km from the Be Live Collection Son Antem.
