Description

Rooms at The Leela Kovalam Beach combine wood décor with modern amenities like a flat-screen TV and tea/coffee making essentials. Each room provides views of the grounds or the sea.

The outdoor pool and the fitness center both overlook the sea. The Leela also has a game room and tennis court. Travel services include tour and ticketing arrangements and car rental.

On the beach, The Tides Restaurant serves Asian specialties and grilled seafood dishes. The Terrace is a 24 hour buffet restaurant offering indoor, outdoor, and poolside seating. Cocktails can be enjoyed at rooftop The Sky Bar while watching the sunset.

The Leela Kovalam Beach is 9 miles from Trivandrum City and the famous Padmanabhaswamy Temple. Trivandrum International Airport is a 40 minute drive away.
