Description

Rooms have spacious interiors with high ceilings, fine beds and furnishings. They are luxurious and well-furnished with a flat-screen satellite TV, ironing facilities and a minibar. Some rooms have private balconies.

The Ayurvedic Center provides specialized treatments like rejuvenation therapies. Guests can also indulge in massages and beauty treatments at the spa. Recreational facilities include a game room with darts and a playstation.

Fennel Restaurant offers an international spread and an outdoor dining experience, while Club 11 Hundred provides a casual lounge bar setting and a nightclub. Strokes is the sunken pool bar, serving refreshing beverages while guests relax by the pool.

Ramada Resort is only a 1-hour drive from Cochin International Airport. It is conveniently located near tourist attractions like the Santa Cruz Cathedral, Jain Temple and Mattancherry Palace, 6 miles from the hotel.

Ernakulam is a great choice for travelers interested in boating, nature and shopping.
