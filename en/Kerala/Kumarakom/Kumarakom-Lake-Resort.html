Description

Surrounded by large gardens and swimming pools, the rooms feature wooden ceilings and Asian-style furnishings. Each air conditioned room has cable TV, a safe, and tea/coffee making facilities.

Water sports at the resort include water skiing and banana boat or speedboat rides. The staff can arrange sunset cruises, village walks, and visits to Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary. The resort has a 24-hour front desk and a business center.

Local, Chinese, and continental dishes are served at Ettukettu restaurant. On the banks of quiet backwaters, Vembanad Seafood Bar offers fresh seafood and sunset views. Guests can enjoy a cup of tea in a garden setting at Thattukada Tea Shop.

Kumarakom Lake Resort is 45 miles from Cochin International Airport.
