Description

Set in Olbia, 9 km from Isola Bianca Harbour, Il Salice b&b offers accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a garden and barbecue facilities.

All rooms feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom.

The air-conditioned rooms provide a garden view and come with a wardrobe and free WiFi.

The units will provide guests with a fridge. Guests at the guest house can enjoy a continental or a buffet breakfast.

Il Salice b&b offers a terrace. The area is popular for cycling, and bike hire and car hire are available at the accommodation.

Speaking English and Italian at the reception, staff are willing to help at any time of the day.

The nearest airport is Olbia Costa Smeralda, 14 km from Il Salice b&b, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
