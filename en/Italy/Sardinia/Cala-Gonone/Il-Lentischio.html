Description

Set 2 km from Cala Cartoe beach in Cala Gonone, Il Lentischio offers a shared garden with BBQ and functional apartments with a balcony.

WiFi is free throughout. Apartments all have far-reaching sea views and views of the garden.

Each has a kitchenette, washing machine and a fully fitted bathroom with shower. At the Il Lentischio a continental breakfast is served each morning in the common lounge.

Parasols and deckchairs are provided free on request. Free on-site parking is also provided.
