Description

Spread over 164 ft² of land, Arenas Resort Tirreno is located in Cala Liberotto on the Gulf of Orosei. It offers a wide range of free sports facilities, plus a large swimming pool.

Overlooking the gardens, each air-conditioned unit includes a private bathroom with hairdryer.

Use of 2 deckchairs and an umbrella is free. There are also tennis courts, mini golf and mountain bikes, together with a Children's Club.

Arenas Resort Tirreno has 2 restaurants. Set by the beach, the Corallo serves Italian and Sardinian specialties and is open to the public. The Liberotto is private and serves an American breakfast buffet and buffet dinner, with a different theme every evening.
