Description

Boasting an outdoor pool with hydromassage, Borgo Degli Ulivi is 10 minutes' walk from both the centre of Arbatax and its harbour.

It offers a sun terrace, air-conditioned accommodation and free Wi-Fi. With a private terrace, elegant apartments have a TV, tiled or marble floors and a fully equipped kitchenette with a dining area.

The private bathroom includes a hairdryer. Beach towels are provided. On request, you can enjoy a sweet and savoury buffet-style breakfast of cold cuts, pastries, fruit juices and cheese.

It is served in the bar or on the furnished patio beside the pool. A bus stop 200 metres from the property takes you to Arbatax Harbour and the town centre.

Free private garage for motorbikes is available on site. On request, a private garage for cars is also possible.
