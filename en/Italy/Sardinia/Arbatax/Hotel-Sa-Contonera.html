Description

Enjoy all the facilities of Hotel Sa Contonera. At this modern and elegant hotel you can relax in the luxurious pool. Savor a free and rich breakfast buffet served on the beautiful hotel terrace.

Each room in Hotel Sa Contonera features an individual color scheme that ranges from pink to blue, green to yellow. Classical furnishings are highlighted by their sophisticated shades of ivory and white.

Found in the city of Arbatax, Hotel Sa Contonera is close to the most popular beaches of the area. The city name refers to the nearby Saracen watchtower where you can enjoy splendid views of the area.

The helpful hotel staff will assist you with your travel and tourism needs. Ferries from Civitavecchia and Genoa arrive at the city’s harbor, which also provides an excellent base for exploring the eastern coastline.
