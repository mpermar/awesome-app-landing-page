Description

Located less than 600 metres from the coast with its white sandy beach, Hotel Baia Cea offers non-smoking only accommodation and a large, outdoor pool, perfect for hot summer days.

Each room is decorated in a typical local style with wooden furniture and comes with a private patio overlooking the pool, garden or mountains.

Themed evenings are organised around the pool and give you the chance to try some Sardinian culinary specialties at the on site restaurant.

You can enjoy special rates at the pizzeria restaurant down at the beach. The helpful team of staff can give you lots of information about fun, local activities, including water skiing, Jeep excursions, as well as courses at the diving and sailing school.
