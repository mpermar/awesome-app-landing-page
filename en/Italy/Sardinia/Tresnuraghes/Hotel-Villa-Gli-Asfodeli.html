Description

Villa Gli Asfodeli is a fully restored Art-Nouveau style villa, in a large, well-tended garden with its own swimming pool. The rooms are spacious and decorated with antique furniture, and providing modern comforts including free internet access and satellite TV.

Now a bike hotel, the Villa Gli Asfodeli provides bike rental and can organize bike tours with different levels of difficulty. Guests are welcome to bring their own bicycles too.

Across from a 17th-century church in the heart of the traditional Sardinian village of Tresnuraghes, Villa Gli Asfodeli is close to mini-markets, bakeries and cafes. At Porto Alabe, 3 miles away, you can enjoy a variety of water sports at unspoiled beaches and you are also just half an hour from an 18-hole golf course.
