Description

Set right on the seafront at Castelsardo on the north coast of Sardinia, Hotel Meli offers a bar and panoramic sea-view terrace.

All air-conditioned rooms come with satellite TV and a private bathroom. Rooms at the Meli have simple wooden furnishings and cool tiled floors.

Each bathroom is equipped with a hairdryer and toiletry set. Some rooms offer sea views.

Breakfast is Italian style, with cakes, biscuits and cappuccino coffee. The restaurant specializes in fresh local fish and homemade desserts.

Sant'Antonio Abate Church in the town's historic centre is a 15-minute walk away.

Ferries to Spain, France and Corsica leave from Porto Torres, a 50-minute drive away.
