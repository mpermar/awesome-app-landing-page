Description

Featuring a garden and terrace, Villa Piana offers accommodation 3 km from the centre of Castelsardo, and just 300 metres from the beach.

It offers accommodation with free WiFi. The air-conditioned rooms come with soundproofing, an LCD TV and mini fridge.

The bathroom comes with a shower. A sweet and savoury breakfast is served daily, and can be enjoyed on the terrace in fine weather.

A selection of activities are offered in the area, such as snorkelling and windsurfing.

The bed and breakfast also offers bike hire and car hire. A bus stop located 30 metres from the property offers links to Sassari, 26 km away, and Alghero, 60 km away.
