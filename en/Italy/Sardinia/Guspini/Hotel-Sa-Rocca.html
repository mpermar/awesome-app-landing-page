Description

Nestled amid the rolling hills of the Sardinian countryside, the hotel boasts panoramic views over the picturesque surroundings.

Guests can enjoy a seasonal outdoor swimming pool. This new hotel has been built to reflect the traditional architectural style of the region, whilst offering all modern comforts.

All rooms are air conditioned and feature a balcony, minibar and TV. In the evenings you can sample typical local meals in the restaurant and pizzeria, before enjoying an evening show.
