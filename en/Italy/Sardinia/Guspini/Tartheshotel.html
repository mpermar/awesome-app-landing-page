Description

Tartheshotel has a luxurious design, spacious rooms and a wellness centre. With its location in the contrasting and tranquil mining town of Guspini, this property features extensive gardens and a summer pool.

Tartheshotel is surrounded by archaeological sites and natural landmarks. Enjoy the relaxing atmosphere and typical Sardinian architecture.

At the hotel you can enjoy a variety of therapeutic and relaxing massages including reflexology.

Tartheshotel offers small meeting rooms, a comfortable lobby, a reading room and an elegant lounge bar, open 24-hours-a-day.

Free wired internet point and free parking are also provided. You will stay in large, beautifully designed rooms at Tartheshotel.

Junior suites and family rooms offer independent entrances directly from the gardens.

Enjoy en suite bathrooms, satellite TV, and air conditioning. During summer, you can enjoy the free breakfast on the outdoor terrace and candlelight dinners on the restaurant patio.

Traditional Sardinian foods and wines can be sampled at the hotel’s rustic wine cellar.
