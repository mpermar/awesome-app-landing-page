Description

On the northern tip of Sardinia, 200 metres from the beach Rena Bianca, Grindi Suite Relais de Chambre offers elegant rooms with free Wi-Fi.

Piazza San Vittorio is 50 metres away. A continental breakfast is also available, and with a location in the historical centre of Santa Teresa di Gallura, several restaurants and bars are within a 10-minute walk.

Featuring hardwood floors, rooms come with high ceilings, large windows with drapes, and luxurious bathrooms.

Rooms are also equipped with a flat-screen TV and air conditioning. For your convenience, free toiletries are offered.

In summer, guests can enjoy discounts at the nearby partner spa, La Perla del Porto, which includes a sauna, Turkish bath, and relaxation area.
