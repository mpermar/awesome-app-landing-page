Description

Offering a free private beach area, tennis courts and 2 restaurants, Santo Stefano Resort is on the Santo Stefano Island.

It offers free WiFi in public areas and air-conditioned rooms. Each room at the Santo Stefano Resort will provide you with a flat-screen TV, a desk and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

Some rooms feature sea views. The main restaurant features large windows with far-reaching views of the Maddalena Archipelago.

The other restaurant is by the beach area. On request, guests can have relaxing moments in the wellness centre with sauna.

A diving centre, boutique shop and kids' club are also available. The nearby ferry pier links to Palau and La Maddalena island.

Free shuttle service to Santo Stefano island is included on the day of arrival, and at check-out the shuttle to Palau is also free.
