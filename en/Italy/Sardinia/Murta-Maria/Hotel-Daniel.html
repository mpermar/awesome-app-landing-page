Description

Featuring a free shuttle service to its private beach area in Porto Istana, 2. 5 km away, Hotel Daniel offers rooms with a patio or terrace in Murta Maria.

Parking is free at the property. All rooms come with air conditioning and a minibar.

The en-suite bathroom has free toiletries and hairdryer. Breakfast is offered on the ground floor, and it features traditional products of Sardinia.

Guests can rent parasols and deck chairs on the beach. Many tournaments are organised at the 5-a-side football pitch available on site.

Bike rental is also available. The surroundings are filled with great restaurants, pizzerias, cafés and bars.

The Marina Maria beach is 1. 5 km away and is recommended for windsurfing and sailing.
