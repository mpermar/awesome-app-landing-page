Description

Offering a peaceful location 3 km from Carbonia, the Tanit features an outdoor pool and traditional restaurant.

All air-conditioned rooms come with an LCD satellite TV and free Wi-Fi. Plage Mesu in the Gonnesa Marina, Portoscuso and Sant'antioco Island are a 15-minute drive away.

Tanit Hotel Ristorante is just 1 km from the Monte Sirai archaeological site, set on a hill behind the hotel.

Boats to the picturesque San Pietro Island leave from nearby Portoscuso. Rooms have simple furnishings and a private bathroom with hairdryer and toiletry set.

All rooms overlook the garden. Breakfast is continental style with croissants and hot drinks.

The restaurant serves traditional local specialities of meat and fish at lunch and dinner, accompanied by Carignano, Vermentino, and Cannonau wines.

Light refreshments are available at the bar throughout the day. The Port of Cagliari is 60 km away, and it is a 1-hour drive to Elmas Airport.
