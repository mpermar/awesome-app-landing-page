Description

Guests can enjoy views of S. Pietro Island from Lido Degli Spagnoli. The hotel features an on-site restaurant overlooking the sea.

Take advantage of the TV room, the bar, the conference center, and free private parking.

There is a natural path at Lido Degli Spagnoli that leads to the nearby free beach.

Rooms at Lido Degli Spagnoli are spacious, simply furnished and have private bathrooms. In-room amenities include air conditioning, minibar and a safe.
