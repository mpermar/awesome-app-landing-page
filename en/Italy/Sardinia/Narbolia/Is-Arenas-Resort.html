Description

Set within a large pine forest, Is Arenas Resort is a 5-star resort, blending harmoniously with the natural surrounding environment of Narbolia, Sardinia.

It features a free private beach, restaurant and free spa centre. The resort is set 400 metres from the sea and all buildings are only 1-floor high.

The entire establishment has been carefully planned to protect the natural landscape and beauty of this oasis, found just 25 km from Oristano.

Hotel Is Arenas provides creative and fresh dishes that combine the best of both local and typical Mediterranean cooking.

Rich buffet breakfasts and healthy dinners in the fresh open-air terrace will help make your stay at Hotel Is Arenas a memorable one.

Set behind sand dunes separating the long beach, the hotel is near main motorways and provides free private parking if you are travelling by car.

The hotel provides luxurious rooms with panoramic views of the forest, the hotel’s spacious green courtyards or the surrounding hills.
