Description

Set in its own organic orange garden within the Parco degli Agrumi di Muravera park, Domu Incantada is located a 5-minute drive from the sea.

WiFi and parking are both free at the property. Hotel Domu Incantada's rooms are air conditioned and have their own balconies with tables and chairs.

You will have pretty views and a TV. Served on the patio in summer months, breakfast here is a sweet and savoury buffet including homemade jams, cakes, and organic fruit.

Fresh orange juice is available during the citrus fruit harvest period. The traditional restaurant serves fresh pasta, seafood, and meat dishes.

Sardinian deserts are also available. Costa Rei can be reached by car in 25 minutes.
