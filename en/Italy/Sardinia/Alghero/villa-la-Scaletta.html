Description

The villa has 3 bedrooms, a kitchen with a dishwasher, and a bathroom. A flat-screen TV with cable channels is offered.

Speaking Spanish, French, Italian and English at the 24-hour front desk, staff are willing to help at any time of the day.

Guests can use the hot tub. A grill can be found at the property, along with a garden.

Maria Pia Beach is 4.5 miles from the villa. The nearest airport is Fertilia Airport, 7 miles from villa la Scaletta.
