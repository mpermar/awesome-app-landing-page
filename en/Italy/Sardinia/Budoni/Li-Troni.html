Description

Only 4 km from Budoni on the north-eastern coast of Sardinia, Li Troni offers functionally furnished apartments with a terrace.

The on-site restaurant specialises in regional cuisine. Apartments at Li Troni feature a satellite TV, air conditioning and a kitchen.

The bathroom is complete with a shower. During summer, guests enjoy free use of the outdoor pool.

A sweet Italian buffet breakfast can be prepared on request, while drinks are sold at the bar.

Free Wi-Fi can be enjoyed in public areas. The sandy beaches in Budoni can be reached via a public bus stopping 50 metres from the property.
