Description

Featuring a variety of sports facilities including 6 tennis courts, VOI Tanka Resort is in Villasimius.

It includes a private beach and 5 restaurants. With different locations around the resort, accommodation here ranges from rooms to suites to bungalows.

All air-conditioned, they also feature a fridge, satellite TV and a balcony or patio.

Children will have entertainment, a mini club and their own restaurant. VOI Tanka Resort includes football pitches, bowling greens and volleyball courts.

You will also find a gym. Rates are on a half-board basis and include meals at the international and Sardinian buffet restaurant.

There is also a pizzeria and a poolside restaurant, plus a total of 6 bars. The centre of Villasimius is 1 km from VOI Tanka Resort, while Cagliari Elmas Airport is can be reached in approximately a 1-hour drive.
