Description

Le Zagare is surrounded by gardens and fragrant lemon and orange orchards. It is divided into small villas and has a main building which features the restaurant and bar. All of the guest rooms feature either a balcony or patio.

The hotel provides a shuttle service which takes you to the center of Villasimius and the white sandy beach, Campus, where there are lounge chairs, sun umbrellas, showers and a beach bar.
