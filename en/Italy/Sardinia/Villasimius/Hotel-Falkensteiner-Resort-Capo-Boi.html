Description

One of our top picks in Villasimius.Nestled in Mediterranean grounds among granite headlands and white-sand beaches, the Falkensteiner Resort Capo Boi is located by the sea, with views of Capo Carbonara Bay.

The Falkensteiner Resort's beach is equipped with parasols and deck chairs. The resort offers sailing, water sports, and 5-a-side football. Tennis courts are also available.

If you prefer a relaxing evening, you can enjoy a refreshing drink in the open air, with accompanying music. The Acquapura Spa offers massages, beauty treatments and an outdoor swimming pool with hot tub.

Villasimius is 4.3 mi from the property. Capo Carbonara is a 15-minute drive away.
