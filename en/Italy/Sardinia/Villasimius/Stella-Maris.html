Description

On the south-eastern coast of Sardinia, this hotel overlooks the fabulous Bay of Campulongu, the strip of shoreline facing west on the coast of Villasimius.

Surrounded by mastic, pine and juniper trees, Stella Maris was established over 50 years ago by the Jesuits, who saw this location as the ideal place for peace and meditation.

Enjoy the close proximity of the beach where you can swim in the blue waters and relax on the sand.

At the hotel, 2 elegant sea facing dining rooms serve traditional cuisine accompanied by a local wine list.

Each room includes access to the private beach equipped with sun loungers, deck chairs, and beach towels.
