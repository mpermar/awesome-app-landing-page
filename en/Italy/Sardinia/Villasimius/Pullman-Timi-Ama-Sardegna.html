Description

Surrounded by Mediterranean vegetation, Pullman Timi Ama Sardegna offers spacious rooms with a balcony facing the garden or the bay.

It features a free outdoor swimming pool, a wellness centre, and a private white-sand beach in Villasimius.

Rooms come with a classic-style décor. Each offers a satellite TV, free Wi-Fi, and air conditioning.

There are 4 restaurants and 4 bars, including the one by the pool and seaside. The Pullman Timi Ama is in the heart of the Capo Carbonara Protected Marine Area.

Cagliari Airport is 55 km away. Guests can reach Timi Ama beach with a 10-minute walk or with the hotel’s free shuttle.
