Description

White sandy beaches lie just 200 metres from Residence Fenicia, which provides a heated outdoor pool, free WiFi throughout, and a tennis court set in large colourful gardens.

Villasimius is 1 km away. The air-conditioned accommodation comes with satellite TV and tiled floors.

Apartments and studios offer a kitchenette with microwave and fridge. Each apartment includes a terrace, while the studios have a balcony.

Cereals, pastries and freshly prepared coffee are included in the Fenicia's breakfast, which is served on a terrace overlooking the garden.

Restaurants and shops are located in nearby Villasimius. The residence boasts its own private beach which is free for all guests.

During summer a parasol and 2 sun loungers are included. The residence is 16 km from Solanas, and a 1-hour drive from Cagliari.
