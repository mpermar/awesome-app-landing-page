Description

Just 300 metres from its private beach in Stintino, Hotel Ancora offers an outdoor pool, free bike rental, and entertainment services.

It features rooms with a balcony, a garden, and a restaurant. Overlooking the garden or sea, air-conditioned rooms at the Ancora are decorated in a traditional Sardinian style and include satellite TV plus a minibar.

The private bathroom has free toiletries. During summer, the hotel offers a wide range of activities such as water aerobics, canoeing, beach volley, and windsurfing.

A tennis court is also available. Delicious Italian cuisine is served for lunch and dinner at the restaurant with terrace.

Barbecues are organised throughout the summer and children's menus are available.

La Pelosa Beach is just 700 metres away, while Porto Torres is reachable by car in 40 minutes.
