Description

Set in the mountains overlooking the Emerald Coast, Locanda Sant'Andrea Hotel & Relais is located in the heart of San Pantaleo.

The rooms offer views of the mountains or of the free sun terrace with swimming pool.

Breakfast is a buffet with homemade cakes. This family-run property is a 10-minute drive from Renabianca Beach and 20 minutes from Olbia Airport.

Parking is free. All rooms come with a flat-screen TV with Sky and pay-per-view channels, and feature local materials and original paintings.

Each room is also equipped with minibar, air conditioning, and private bathroom with shower.
