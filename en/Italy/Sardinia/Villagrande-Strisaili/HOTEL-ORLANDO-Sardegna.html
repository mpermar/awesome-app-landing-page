Description

Hotel Orlando is set at the foot of Gennargentu National Park, in one of Sardinia's most unspoilt areas.

It is surrounded by oak woods and features a large outdoor pool and 1 traditional restaurant and 1 à la carte restaurant open for special events.

Rooms at the Orlando come with air conditioning, a TV and a minibar. Some include a balcony or a hydromassage bath.

Local organic ingredients are used at the restaurant to prepare Sardinian specialities.

A buffet breakfast with typical Sardinian produce is included. You can book a massage or a session at the on-site spa which features a Turkish bath and sauna.

Located in Villagrande Strisaili, Hotel Orlando is a 30-minute drive from Arbatax on the coast.

Here you can catch a ferry to Italy's mainland. Lido di Orrì beach is 35 minutes away by car.
