Description

Just 650 feet from the beach, Hotel Punta Est features an excellent location for a relaxing vacation on Sardinia’s beautiful northwestern coast.

When not on the beach, you will find ample and pleasant spaces within the hotel grounds where you can relax. Try lounging around the pool and sun deck with well-stocked bar or have a tranquil stroll through the gardens.

During the day, you can find a large amusement park nearby. Before venturing out to discover Baia Sardinia’s nightlife, stop in the hotel’s characteristic restaurant to enjoy local cuisine and Sardinian specialties.
