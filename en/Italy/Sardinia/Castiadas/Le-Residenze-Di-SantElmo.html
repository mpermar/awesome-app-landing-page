Description

Each house is coated in granite or Sardinian stone. The apartments have a balcony or patio, a kitchenette, air conditioning and a satellite TV.

The Sant'Elmo features a bar/pizzeria, a grocery store, a cocktail bar, and a tobacconist's. Sporting facilities include tennis and 5-a-side football courts, and a mini golf.
