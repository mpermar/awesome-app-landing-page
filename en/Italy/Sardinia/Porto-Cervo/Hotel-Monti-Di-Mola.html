Description

Hotel Monti di Mola's bright, spacious rooms include free wired internet and a large bathroom. All rooms are air-conditioned and soundproof, and have an LCD TV with satellite channels.

Some rooms also include a furnished patio or balcony. Some have an independent entrance and direct access to the swimming pool.

Buffet breakfast includes bread, croissants, yogurt, fruit, and hot drinks. During the summer, the bar is open 24 hours a day.

Famous clubs are only a third of a mile away. Facilities for sailing, diving, tennis and horseback riding are available on the coast.
