Description

Just 50 meters away from the marina, Luci di la Muntagna offers panoramic views across Porto Cervo Bay.

These views can be admired from the swimming pool, Kabuga restaurant and terraces.

Hotel Luci di la Muntagna's design is typical of Sardinia's Emerald Coast. You will find a total of 2 bars, one poolside offering light lunches, and one with a terrace overlooking the bay.

The hotel offers modern rooms with views of the marina or surrounding mountains. Each room is air conditioned and features an LCD TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom.

The breakfast buffet includes homemade bread, croissants, cold cuts and cheese. Kabuga is an à la carte restaurant serving Mediterranean cuisine.

Meals can be enjoyed out on the terrace with views of the yacht-club. This 4-star hotel is just a few steps from Porto Cervo's Piazzetta, the town's main square.

It is surrounded by exclusive shops and restaurants. The beaches of Porto Paglia, Pevero and Cala Granu are less than 10 minutes' drive away.
