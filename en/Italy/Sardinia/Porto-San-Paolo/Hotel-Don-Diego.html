Description

Surrounded by 60,000 m² of green valley, Hotel Don Diego is a 4-star hotel set directly on the coast of Sardinia, just 12 km south of Olbia Airport.

At Hotel Don Diego you will enjoy a fully equipped private beach and splendid views of the flower gardens, Costa Dorata Bay and Tavolara Island.

Providing a variety of room types, Hotel Don Diego is the perfect getaway for all types of travellers.

Here you can also find free public parking, free Wi-Fi in the hotel’s business centre, a fitness centre and a swimming pool with panoramic views.

Located in a tranquil spot in Porto San Paolo, Hotel Don Diego offers international and regional cuisine in a traditional setting.

Here you can savour your buffet breakfast and lunch on the panoramic veranda overlooking Tavolara and Molara islands.
