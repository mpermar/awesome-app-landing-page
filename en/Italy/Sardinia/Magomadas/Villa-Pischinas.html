Description

Featuring panoramic views of the Bosa Coast, Villa Pischinas is set among vineyards in Magomadas.

It offers free WiFi and air-conditioned rooms with an LCD TV and private bathroom.

Breakfast at the Pischinas includes cappuccino and fresh fruit juice, together with pastries and Sardinian cheese.

The beaches of Bosa Marina, Turas and Porto Alabe are all just a 5-minute drive from the Pischinas Villa.

On request, the property can provide a shuttle service to Alghero Fertilla Airport, 58 km away.
