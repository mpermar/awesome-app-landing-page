Description

All guest rooms in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV. All rooms at Wellness Country Spa Inghirios include air conditioning and a desk.

An Italian breakfast is served daily at the property.

Alghero is 7 miles from the accommodation, while Sassari is 15 miles from the property. Fertilia Airport is 1.9 miles away.
