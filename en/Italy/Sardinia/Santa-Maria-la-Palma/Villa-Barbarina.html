Description

This stunning property is a traditional farmhouse, which still produces a lot of its own food today. Encircling the villa, there are 12 acres of green meadows. This beautiful setting makes the villa an ideal venue for weddings, special events, and conventions.

Each guest room features a number of modern comforts such as an LCD TV and minibar. The rooms are elegantly decorated with natural colors and handmade lamps. From your private balcony, you can admire the view of the hills and promontories.

Dining is always a treat at Villa Barbarina where you can sample traditional Sardinian cuisine, which includes typical roasted meats and exquisite cake.

Resort Villa Barbarina is in the heart of the region of Nurra; between Porto Torres, Alghero, and Sassari. Nurra is renowned for its beautiful scenery, unspoiled nature, and interesting trekking paths. Alghero Airport is less than 2 miles away, and the hotel provides a transportation service upon request and for an additional fee.
