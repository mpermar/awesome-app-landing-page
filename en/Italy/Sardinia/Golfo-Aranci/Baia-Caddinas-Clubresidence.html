Description

Baia Caddinas Clubresidence is located in extensive Sardinian parkland, directly on the sea.

It offers self-catering accommodation with a balcony or patio, 2 restaurants, 2 outdoor pools and panoramic sea views.

Apartments and studios at the Caddinas Clubresidence, part of the Hotel Resort & Spa Baia Caddinas, include fully equipped kitchenettes, bed linen and towels, and satellite TV.

The sun loungers and parasols in the Bougainvillea garden, around the pool, or in limited areas of the private beach are free to use.

The fully equipped private beach is next to the hotel. The AquaVitalis wellness centre provides beauty treatments, a Turkish bath, and a biosauna.

Special wellness treatments include Ayurveda massages, Thalassotherapy and use of skin products by Maria Galland Paris.

The Clubresidence is set inside a larger holiday village where a children's miniclub, a restaurant and the wellness centre are located.
