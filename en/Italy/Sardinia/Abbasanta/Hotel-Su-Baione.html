Description

A country house hotel in the heart of historic Sardinia, Su Baione offers air-conditioned rooms set among cork oaks.

It has an outdoor pool and a fitness corner. These country-style rooms are spacious and come with wrought-iron beds and a private bathroom with hairdryer.

Some rooms have a patio or balcony. As a guest of Hotel Su Baione, you can enjoy activities including horse riding, canoeing, mountain biking, and hiking in the surrounding area of Abbasanta.

These activities as well as cultural excursions can be organised on request. The famous archaeological site of Nuraghe Losa is only a short walk away.
