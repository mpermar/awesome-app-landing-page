Description

Hotel Cardedu is surrounded by the Mediterranean vegetation in the hilly and peaceful Ogliastra area, 2 km from the beach.

It offers air-conditioned rooms with flat-screen satellite TV. Rooms are decorated with light furniture.

They include a minibar and a private bathroom with hairdryer and toiletries. The restaurant serves traditional Sardinian cuisine and local fish.

It overlooks the lush garden. The Cardedu Hotel is 30 km from the Gennargentu National Park.

The beaches of San Perda Pera and Su Sirboni are less than 20 minutes’ drive away.
