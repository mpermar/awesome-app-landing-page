Description

Sa Fiorida is just 450 metres from the white sands of La Ciaccia beach, 2 km outside Valledoria.

This peaceful, family-run residence offers well-equipped apartments with a furnished balcony or patio, plus barbecue facilities and free parking.

All air conditioned, apartments come with wood furniture and tiled floors. They all feature a comfortable seating area, washing machine and a kitchenette, plus a satellite TV.

Some overlook the sea. This property is 100 metres from the nearest restaurants and supermarkets.

Popular local activities include surfing and canoeing and the property's staff organise excursions to nearby towns and the Coghinas River Nature Park.

Sa Fiorida Case Vacanze is on the Gulf of Asinara, close to 17 km of sandy beaches.
