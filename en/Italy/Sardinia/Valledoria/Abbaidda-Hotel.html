Description

The Abbaidda Hotel is a pleasant, new hotel in the province of Sassari. You can enjoy beautiful sea views, a fitness centre, five-a-side football pitch and a garden with a relaxing swimming pool with whirlpool.

Abbaidda Hotel offers spacious rooms and suites with air conditioning and Wi-Fi access.

Some rooms feature sea views. A self-service laundry is also available. An extensive continental buffet is served for breakfast.

From June to September, the restaurant serves local specialities and seafood dishes along with an extensive vegetarian buffet.

Massages can be purchased at reception. The Abbaidda is near the medieval town of Castelsardo, and a few kilometres from the thermal baths in Casteldoria.

Private parking is provided at the hotel. During summer months, a free scheduled shuttle service is offered to/from the beach and town.
