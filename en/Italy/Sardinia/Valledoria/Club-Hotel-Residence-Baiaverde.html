Description

Surrounded by pine trees and junipers, Club Hotel Residence Baiaverde is located 120 metres from a golden sandy beach.

It offers 2 pools, and suites and apartments with private entrances. The apartments at the Baiaverde include a kitchenette, private bathroom and a patio or balcony.

The suites feature air conditioning, a safety deposit box, minibar and an LCD TV, and they also include 1 parasol plus 2 sun loungers at the beach.

Breakfast is an extensive a sweet and savoury buffet including scrambled eggs, sausages, and croissants, served in the property restaurant which specialises in international cuisine.

A pizzeria/grill is also available on site. The Baiaverde Club Hotel Residence has a children’s playground.

Entertainment is provided for both children and adults, and the beach includes umbrellas and sun loungers.

One of the pools is for children. Ferries to Genoa and Corsica leave from Porto Torres, 41 km from the Baiaverde Club Hotel.
