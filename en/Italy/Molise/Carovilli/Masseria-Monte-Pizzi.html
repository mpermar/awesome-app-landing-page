Description

Offering a barbecue, children's playground and sun terrace, Masseria Monte Pizzi is set in Carovilli.

Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. The rooms have a TV. Some units feature views of the mountain or garden.

Each room is equipped with a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Both free WiFi and free private parking are available at this inn. There is a shared lounge at the property.

You can engage in various activities, such as cycling and hiking. Roccaraso is 45 km from Masseria Monte Pizzi, while Pescasseroli is 70 km away.
