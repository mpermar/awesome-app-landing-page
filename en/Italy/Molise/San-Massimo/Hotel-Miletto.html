Description

Offering an indoor pool and a traditional restaurant, Hotel Miletto is just 100 metres from the Campitello Matese ski slopes.

It features a garden, a sauna, and rooms with mountain views. These simply furnished rooms come with satellite flat-screen TV, a safe and parquet floors.

Each has a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries. WiFi is free throughout the property.

A sweet, Italian-style breakfast is served daily at Miletto Hotel. The restaurant specialises in typical regional cuisine.
