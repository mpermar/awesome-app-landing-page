Description

Set in the mountain village od Civitanova del Sannio, Locanda degli Illustri offers a restaurant and spacious, classically furnished rooms with a balcony.

You benefit from free WiFi in all public areas. Each room includes a TV and a private bathroom with free toiletries.

One room has a spa bath and offers extra space. At the Locanda degli Illustri you you can relax in the shared lounge and epxlore the surrounding area on 1 of th property's free bikes.

Popular activities in the surroundings include fishing and hiking. The inn is a 30-minute drive from Isernia, and Campobasso is 40 km away.
