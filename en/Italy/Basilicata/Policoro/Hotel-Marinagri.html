Description

Surrounded by nature and offering indoor and outdoor pools, Marinagri Luxury Resort is a 5-star hotel in Policoro.

It features a complete wellness centre and a private beach 600 metres away. All rooms at Marinagri have design furniture and air conditioning.

In-room luxury amenities include free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs with satellite channels, some rooms also have a balcony with sea or pool views.

Marinagri’s restaurant serves both Italian and international cuisine, along with traditional dishes from South Italy.

Meals are served in the modern dining room with works of art. Guests can enjoy the hotel’s wellness centre, which includes spa facilities and a Turkish bath.

Sport lovers will have tennis courts and water sport facilities available on site.

Beach towels are free. Offering free parking, the resort is a 10-minute drive from the E90 state road and Policoro centre.

The city of Matera and its famous Sassi can be reached by car in around one hour.
