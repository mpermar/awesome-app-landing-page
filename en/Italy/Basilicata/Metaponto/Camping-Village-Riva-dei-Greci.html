Description

Featuring a private beach just 500 metres away, Camping Village Riva dei Greci offers accommodation in Metaponto Nature Reserve.

The property has a terrace, BBQ facilities and a sports pitch for tennis, basketball and five-a-side football.

Free WiFi is available in public areas. All units come with air conditioning and a patio.

The private bathroom includes a shower and towels. In the summer months guests will find a bar, a mini market and a restaurant that serves typical dishes from Basilicata.

A kids' club and evening entertainment are also available in summer. Camping Village Riva dei Greci is a 40-minute drive from Taranto, while Matera is 50 km away.
