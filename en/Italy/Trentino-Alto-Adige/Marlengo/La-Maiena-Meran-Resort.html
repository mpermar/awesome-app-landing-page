Description

This golf and wellness hotel is located 30 km north of Bolzano. Surrounded by the mountains of the Trentino, La Maiena Resort provides its guests with numerous facilities to relax.

The rooms feature wooden fittings. Each room comes with a panoramic balcony, a bathroom with shower, and satellite TV.

La Maiena Resort features 2 swimming pools, a fitness centre, and a sauna. The hotel also offers discounts for golf related activities.

In the mornings, guests start the day with an English breakfast buffet. During the Summer, the property organises animation activities.
