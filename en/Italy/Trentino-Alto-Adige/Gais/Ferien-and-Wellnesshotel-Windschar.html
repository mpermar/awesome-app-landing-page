Description

Located at the opening of the Aurina Valley, 5 km from Brunico, this family-run hotel is set on a large estate offering separate guest houses, a wellness centre, and an outdoor pool.

Ferien und Wellnesshotel Windschar is in an excellent position to discover the nearby ski resort of Plan de Corones.

The free ski bus leaves from right outside. Rooms are located in the main building or in one of the annexes, and facilities include en suite bathrooms, satellite TV channels and a panoramic balcony.

Hypoallergenic bed linen is available on request. Discover typical flavours of South-Tyrol cooking in the on-site restaurant and a variety of foods during the themed buffets.

A buffet breakfast is served daily. Windschar's wellness centre is connected to the main building by an underground passage.

It offers a hot tub, massages and beauty treatments. The Windschar organises many activities and events, including guided hikes, night tobogganing, and sleigh hires in winter, and barbecue parties in summer.
