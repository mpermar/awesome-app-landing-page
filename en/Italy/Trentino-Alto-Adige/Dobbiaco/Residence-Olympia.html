Description

Set along a cyciling path 3 km from Dobbiaco, family-run Apparthotel Olympia Hotel is part of a camping site featuring a seasonal pool and spa.

It offers contemporary apartments with a mountain-view balcony, free Wi-Fi and parking.

Apartments at Olympia Hotel residence are functionally furnished. They include a fully equipped kitchenette, dining area with sofa bed and TV, and bathroom with bathtub.

On request and at extra costs, you can dine in the à la carte restaurant offering a sweet and savoury buffet breakfast and regional cuisine for lunch and dinner.

Guests can cook outside while their children play on the playground. You can take a relaxing dip in the camping’s pool, which is equipped with sun loungers and parasols.

Alternatively, you can book a session at the spa including a Finnish sauna, steam bath and hot tub together with a solarium.

Hiking tours are bookable at reception. The on-site laundry and bike rental service are available at additional costs.

A public ski bus stopping in front of the property will drop you on the Sextner Dolomiten slopes, located 14.
