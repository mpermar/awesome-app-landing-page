Description

Offering a 200 m² wellness centre with 3 saunas, Hotel Dosses has a quiet setting at an altitude of 1180 metres.

It is just 3 km outside Tires and has excellent bus links to the Carezza ski area.

The 4-star Dosses Hotel offers rooms with furnished balconies and stunning views of the surrounding mountains and valley.

They also feature free WiFi and an LCD TV with satellite channels. Homemade jams and cakes are served alongside cheeses and cold cuts at the breakfast buffet.

Open at lunch and dinner, the restaurant serves a choice of Mediterranean or South Tyrol dishes.

In summer, you can enjoy breakfast out in the 300 m² garden, where BBQ evenings are held weekly.

You also have a library with a cosy fireplace. Free parking is provided and buses to Bolzano stop right outside.

There are also plenty of hiking and cycle paths, with hiking tours organised once a week.
