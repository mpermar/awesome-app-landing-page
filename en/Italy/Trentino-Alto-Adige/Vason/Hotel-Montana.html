Description

Hotel Montana's indoor pool offers views of the Brenta Dolomites. Located in Vason at an altitude of 1650 metres, this modern hotel is a 2-minute walk from the ski lifts.

Garage parking is free, and you can drive to Trento in 40 minutes by car. At this 4-star hotel you have access to the wellness centre, which includes a range of saunas, and a Turkish bath.

The relaxation area has panoramic views. Guests will receive a free Trentino Guest Card, which gives free access to public museums, castles and nature parks.

The welcoming Montana Hotel has its own winter ice rink and a sports field, together with a kids club in a spacious area with balcony.

There are 2 hydromassage areas in the pool. Rooms and apartments are available, and both come with flat-screen satellite TV and wood-beamed ceilings.

Most rooms offer a balcony. A ski deposit is provided and there is Wi-Fi throughout.

The restaurant serves tasty cuisine, with gluten-free options. Breakfast is served daily.

The hotel is located along the SP85 road, just 50 metres from the bus stop to/from Trento Train Station, which is 20 km away.

The suggested address for GPS navigators is Localit Vason, Trento. Molveno can be reached in 1 hour.
