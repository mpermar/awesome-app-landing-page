Description

Chalet Caminetto offers enchanting views of the Dolomites, and is just 100 metres from the ski slopes.

Rooms are furnished in a typical alpine style. Caminetto Chalet offers an indoor swimming pool.

There is also a wellness centre with sauna and Turkish bath. The Caminetto is approximately 20 km from Trento.
