Description

The ultra-modern Le Blanc Hotel is just 250 metres from the Monte Bondone ski lifts.

Its 400 m² spa includes an indoor swimming pool and Turkish bath. WiFi is free throughout the hotel.

All rooms at Le Blanc Hotel & Spa are spacious and feature clean white linens and light wood furniture.

Each room has a flat-screen TV with Italian digital channels, and some include a balcony.

Guests can enjoy a mix of local dishes and international cuisine at Le Blanc's restaurant.

Beauty treatments can be booked at the spa. Located in Vason, Le Blanc is a 25-minute drive from the centre of Trento.

The surrounding area is perfect for cycling and trekking in the summer. In winter, a ski school and kid's camp are situated 60 metres from the hotel.
