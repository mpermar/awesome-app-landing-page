Description

Set in a green park, this 4-star hotel has a quiet location just outside the centre of San Paolo.

It offers wellness facilities and panoramic mountain views from its garden and rooms.

Out in the park you will find a terrace, sun loungers, deckchairs and a lake suitable for swimming.

Indoors you will find a steam bath, Finnish sauna and swimming pool with direct access to the park.

There is also a volleyball court and a children's playground. All rooms at the Weingarten Hotel are spacious and offer satellite TV and a private balcony.

Some have rustic-style furnishings, while others feature more modern Tyrolean décor.

A buffet breakfast is served each morning, while the restaurant offers Trentino specialities at lunch and dinner.

Friendly staff will assist you with booking guided tours of surrounding attractions including the local vineyards.

Hotel Weingarten is 10 minutes' drive from Bolzano and 20 minutes' drive from Merano.
