Description

With panoramic mountain and valley views, Hotel Girlanerhof is 3 km outside the centre of Appiano sulla Strada del Vino.

It offers a swimming pool with glass roof. Rooms are bright and spacious featuring a traditional design with wood floors.

Each one has a balcony overlooking the mountains. You will find a flat-screen TV, minibar and a private bathroom with hairdryer and toiletries.

Breakfast is buffet style at the Girlanerhof Hotel and drinks are available from the bar.

The free wellness centre includes a sauna and Turkish bath. There is also a children's playground, a library and table tennis.

Free parking is provided and the hotel is 20 minutes' drive from Bolzano centre. Buses for Bolzano and Appiano stop just 300 metres away.

The nearest train station is 3 km away in Ponte D'Adige and a shuttle is available on request.
