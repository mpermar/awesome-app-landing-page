Description

Surrounded by fruit orchards in the village of San Michele Appiano, Angerburg Blumenhotel has a swimming pool with hydromassage and a garden sauna.

It features a sun terrace and free WiFi throughout. The generous buffet breakfast includes cold cuts, together with homemade cakes and jams.

Rooms at the Blumenhotel Angerburg feature satellite TV, a seating area, and a private bathroom.

Some rooms have a balcony overlooking the villages of Appiano or Caldaro. The Angerburg offers facilities for cyclists and mountain bikers.

Bolzano, the provincial capital, is 12 km from the hotel. Merano is a 30-minute drive away.
