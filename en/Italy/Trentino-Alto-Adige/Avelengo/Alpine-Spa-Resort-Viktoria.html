Description

A traditional mountain hotel located a 10-minute drive from Merano, Alpine Spa Resort Viktoria offers an infinity pool and a free wellness centre with 8 saunas and Turkish baths, an indoor pool and 2 hot tubs.

Free bikes and a children's playground are available. Rooms are elegant and refined and each is fitted with contemporary wooden furniture.

Facilities include an LCD satellite TV and a wellness basket including bathrobes, slippers and towels.

Most rooms overlook the natural surroundings. When the weather is good, guests can have breakfast on the terrace, and enjoy both savoury and sweet food.

At the restaurant, traditional local cuisine is served. The 4-star superior hotel is 4 km from the access to the Meran 2000 ski slopes.
