Description

Set in the Avelengo-Merano hiking area, Apartment Hotel Christine is 12 km from Merano and a 15-minute drive from the Gardens of Trauttmansdorff Castle.

It features free Wi-Fi throughout. Each air-conditioned apartment has a satellite flat-screen TV, a balcony or terrace, and a dishwasher.

Some have a washing machine, mountain views and two bathrooms. In the morning, you can enjoy breakfast with local organic products, such as jam, cheese and bacon.

Free private parking and a relax room are provided. Guests have access to a sun terrace, a sauna, and a garden furnished with sun loungers.

The centre of Avelengo is a 5-minute walk from the property, while the Merano 2000 ski area can be reached in 10 minutes by car and a shuttle service is available nearby.
