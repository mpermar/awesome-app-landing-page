Description

Featuring a free sauna and spa with mountain views in Avelengo, family-run Hotel Chalet Mirabell offers luxury accommodation and a gourmet restaurant.

It features a garden-side pool and free Wi-Fi plus bike rental. Both modern and Alpine-style rooms and suites at the Mirabell include a balcony or terrace, a seating area, and a minibar.

Some have a fireplace and spa bath. The en-suite bathroom has bathrobes plus slippers.

Breakfast is a buffet and includes cakes, fresh fruit and milk, accompanied by cold cuts, cheese and eggs with bacon.

The on-site restaurant serves à la carte dining. Guests can enjoy a drink on the mountain-view terrace or relax in the exclusive spa with a pool, Finnish sauna and a free-standing hot tub.

Massages are bookable on request while the garden is always equipped with sun loungers and parasols.

During winter you can board the free ski bus to the Merano 2000 slopes, set 4 km away.
