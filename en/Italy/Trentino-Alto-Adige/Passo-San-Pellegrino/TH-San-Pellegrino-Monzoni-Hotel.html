Description

Set in the Val di Fassa valley, 12 km from the centre of Moena, Hotel Monzoni features a free wellness centre and a traditional restaurant.

All carpeted rooms include an LCD satellite TV. At the spa, you can relax in the Turkish bath, sauna and hot tub.

There is a curved indoor pool and a fitness centre. Massages and other beauty treatments are available at extra cost.

With warm wooden furnishings and a minibar, all rooms at the Monzoni include a fully equipped private bathroom.

Some rooms feature a balcony with views of the surrounding Dolomites. Breakfast is a varied continetal buffet.

The restaurant serves a mix of Italian and international cuisine. A traditional Ladin dinner is held once a week, with local specialities.

In the winter, the hotel enjoys ski-to-door access from the Tre Valli Slopes. The Costabella Chairlift is just 70 metres away, and use of the heated ski deposit is free.
