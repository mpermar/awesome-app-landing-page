Description

Set in San Martino Di Castrozza, Natur Garni Alpino is just 200 metres from the Colverde ski slope.

This family-run hotel offers a free gym, games room. Rooms at the Natur Garni Alpino have a rustic design.

They all offer views of the surrounding mountains and many have a balcony. Each room includes a TV and private bathroom.

The buffet breakfast includes sweet and savoury items such as homemade jams, eggs, cold meats and cheese.

The hotel's cocktail bar is open until midnight, while its cosy library has a small selection of books and a fireplace.

A free public ski bus stops just 10 metres away with departures every 15 minutes.

Excursions are available every season, organised by the local tourist board or the hotel itself.
