Description

Hotel Cima Rosetta is in San Martino di Castrozza, in the Trentino region. It features a wellness and beauty centre and has a good location for both summer and winter activities.

Rooms at this 3-star superior hotel feature work desk, satellite TV and private bathroom.

Most of them have a balcony. The hotel’s restaurant serves local cuisine and national specialities accompanied by local and Italian wines.

The area is ideal for trekking, cycling and Nordic walking in summer, and for ski and downhill ski in winter.
