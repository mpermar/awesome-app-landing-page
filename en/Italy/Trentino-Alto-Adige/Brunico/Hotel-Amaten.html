Description

The 3-star Hotel Amaten is surrounded by the Dolomites and features a nice furnished garden overlooking the landscape and Brunico.

Set 9 km from Brunico, it features a well-equipped wellness area. Your room at the Amaten is fitted with traditional light-wood furnishings and either carpeted or wooden floors.

Each one comes with an LCD satellite TV and a private bathroom. As a guest you will have free access to the wellness centre, featuring a Finnish sauna, Turkish bath and hay bath.

2 hot tubs are available on request. The relaxation room is equipped with newspapers, magazines and sun loungers.

Lunch and dinner can be enjoyed at the restaurant, where regional cuisine is served.

Breakfast is buffet style and includes cakes, eggs, sausages and cheese. Meals are served on the terrace during summer.
