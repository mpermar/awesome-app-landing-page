Description

All rooms at Hotel Petrus feature a balcony with mountain views. Each room comes with soft carpeted floors, a private bathroom with shower, and an LCD TV with satellite channels.

The restaurant serves specialties of South Tyrol and international dishes. Breakfast is a varied buffet. Drinks and snacks are available in the terrace area out on the grounds, equipped with tables, chairs, and lounge chairs.

The hotel is half a mile from the Pustertal Golf Club, and a 5-minute drive from Brunico Train Station. The nearest highway exit is in Bressanone, 19 miles away.

Riscone is a great choice for travelers interested in wellness, excursions and nature walks.
