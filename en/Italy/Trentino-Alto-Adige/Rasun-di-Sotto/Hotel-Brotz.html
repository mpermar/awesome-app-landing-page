Description

Hotel Brötz is an Alpine-stile building with mountain views and rooms with a balcony.

It is a 7-minute drive from the Kronplatz ski resort, connected with a free shuttle.

The Brötz Hotel's wellness centre includes a Finnish sauna, Turkish and Kneipp baths, and an indoor swimming pool with waterfall.

Relaxation and fitness rooms are also available. All rooms at the Brötz offer Wi-Fi access, a satellite TV, and a private bathroom with shower, hairdryer and bathrobe.

During winter toboggans, snow shoes and hiking sticks can be borrowed free of charge.

The restaurant serves Mediterranean cuisine. During summer drinks and snacks are available in the outdoor terrace.

Buses run from Rasun/Rasen to Olang/Valdaora Train Station. Private transfers can be arranged on request.
