Description

Set in Santa Cristina in Val Gardena, the Diamant is a 4-star superior hotel, 1. 5 km from Sella Ronda ski slopes.

It offers a free wellness centre with indoor pool. Hotel Diamant has a traditional Alpine design.

It offers stunning views of the Dolomites and valley, plus range of facilities including a tennis court, children's playroom and games room.

Rooms are elegantly furnished and come with a balcony overlooking the woods or mountains.

Each has a flat-screen TV, minibar and a private bathroom with hairdryer. Breakfast is buffet style while the restaurant serves local and Mediterranean dishes accompanied by a buffet of appetisers, side orders and desserts.

A South Tyrol evening is held weekly with a 7-course gourmet dinner. Guests of the Diamant have a free heated ski deposit and free use of the gym, sauna and outdoor hot tub in the spa.
