Description

Set in its garden offering panoramic views of the surrounding mountains, Genusshotel Pfeiss has elegant rooms and apartments.

It features a free fitness centre, a free swimming pool with hot tub, and a shared terrace.

Apartments are spacious and provided with a dining area and a fully equipped kitchenette, a bathroom, a separate bedroom, and some also have a private balcony or patio.

Rooms are modern and have a satellite TV and en suite bathroom. At the Pfeiss Genusshotel you can relax in the garden, where there is the swimming pool and the hot tub.

There also is a fitness area, as well as darts and table tennis you can use for free.

Breakfast is served daily on the terrace, and it consists of sweet and savoury products.

It includes cheese, ham, cereal, and yoghurt, while eggs and bacon are available on request.

Merano ski slopes are a 15-minute drive from the property, while Bolzano is 24 km away.
