Description

The Tiefenbrunn Gardensuites & Breakfast is a 4-star superior hotel offering an outdoor pool and a wellness centre equipped with indoor pool, Finnish sauna and Turkish bath.

Set in central Lana, it is a 15-minute drive from Merano. Rooms are fitted with wooden furniture and carpeted floors.

Facilities include bathrobes, an LCD satellite TV and a furnished balcony overlooking the Dolomites.

Breakfast is buffet style and includes cold cuts, cheese, as well as homemade juices and cakes.

Open for lunch and dinner, the restaurant serves South Tyrolean and Italian cuisine on its terrace.

The pretty garden at Tiefenbrunn Gardensuites & Breakfast features a pool surrounded by sun loungers, ideal for relaxing.

Guests can also rent bikes for free at the reception and ride around the surroundings.

Offering free parking, the hotel is a 20-minute drive from Bolzano. Buses stop 300 metres away and link with Merano.
