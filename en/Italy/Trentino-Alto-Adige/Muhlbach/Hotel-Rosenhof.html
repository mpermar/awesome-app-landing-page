Description

Hotel Rosenhof is set in the centre of Rio Di Pusteria, just 200 metres from the ski lifts to the Gitschberg and Jochtal ski areas.

Its 500 mÂ² garden includes a pool, playground and BBQ during summer. Rooms are comfortable and cosy with a sofa bed and satellite TV.

Each one features a private bathroom and most have a balcony. Some offer views of the surrounding mountains.

The breakfast buffet includes sweet and savoury items such as cold meats, eggs and homemade cakes.

The restaurant is exclusively for hotel guests and serves a mix of Trentino and international dishes.

The Rosenhof Hotel features a sauna and provides a free shuttle to destinations within a 10 km radius.

An airport transfer is also available. The nearest public bus stop is 200 metres away.

Free parking is provided and Wi-Fi is free in public areas. During summer, guests have a free pass for local chair lifts.
