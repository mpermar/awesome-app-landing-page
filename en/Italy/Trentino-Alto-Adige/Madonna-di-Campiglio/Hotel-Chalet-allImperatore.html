Description

Hotel Chalet all'Imperatore is just a 5-minute walk from Madonna di Campiglio town centre and the ski lifts.

If offers a free wellness area, breakfast and a traditional restaurant. With free Wi-FI, rooms at the Chalet all’Imperatore have a TV, minibar and wooden floors.

The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries. Most rooms have a balcony overlooking the mountains.

Guests can relax on the sun terrace or in the wellness area, including a hot tub, gym, sauna and Turkish bath.

Parking at the property is free. Both Bolzano and Trento can be reached by car in about 1.
