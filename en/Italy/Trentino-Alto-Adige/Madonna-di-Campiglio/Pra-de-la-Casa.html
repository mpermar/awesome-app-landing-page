Description

Completely surrounded by meadows and pine trees of the Adamello Brenta Natural Park, Pra de la Casa is located 9 km from Pinzolo and a 10-minute drive from Madonna di Campiglio.

It is characterised by the use of stone and wood, with full views over the Dolomites.

The Alpine-style rooms feature wood interiors and offer mountain views. The private bathroom includes a shower and free toiletries.

Breakfast is offered every morning. The on-site restaurant is available only to guests and serves Tyrolean specialties.

Guests can enjoy special rates on group tours in the surrounding areas. Pra de la Casa is set inside the Adamello Brenta Natural park, while Madonna di Campiglio is 6 km away.
