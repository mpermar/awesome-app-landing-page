Description

With a swimming pool, family-run Hotel Ariston is located in the city centre, just 40 metres from the Belvedere ski slopes in Madonna di Campiglio.

It offers free WiFi, free summer parking, a wellness centre and traditional mountain-style rooms.

Before heading for the Madonna di Campiglio ski slopes, enjoy a generous and energetic breakfast buffet.

Dinner is served à la carte and features both Trentino and international dishes. The wine cellar boasts over 100 labels.

The Ariston has spacious rooms with satellite TV and pine-wood furniture in an Alpine style.

Some rooms come with a balcony. Before enjoying your night’s sleep, relax in the wellness room equipped with sauna, Turkish bath and whirlpool.

You will also find an indoor pool. At Ariston Hotel you can leave your skiing gear in the large guarded storage room.
