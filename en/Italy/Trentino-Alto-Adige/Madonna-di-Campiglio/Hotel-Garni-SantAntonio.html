Description

Located in Sant’Antonio di Mavignola and 6 km from Pinzolo and Madonna di Campiglio, Hotel Garnì Sant'Antonio features a free ski bus shuttle and free ski storage.

Wi-Fi and bikes are also free. The rooms with wooden floors come with a TV, bathroom with free toiletries and balcony.

A minibar is available upon request. Breakfast is served buffet style and consists of sweet and savoury food.

It includes homemade pastries. Guests can use the garden's BBQ facilities free of charge.

Garnì Sant'Antonio Hotel also features a bar, outdoor children’s playground and fitness centre.

Trento is 60 km away, while Bolzano is a 1. 5-hour drive away. Parking on site is free.
