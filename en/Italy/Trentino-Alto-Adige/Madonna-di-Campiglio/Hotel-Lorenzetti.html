Description

Featuring a free spa, Hotel Lorenzetti is 200 metres from the Colarin cable car, and offers a sun terrace with sun loungers.

With free Wi-Fi and free bikes, the hotel has rooms with a balcony. Each room comes with views of the Brenta Dolomites, a minibar and a satellite TV.

The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries. A breakfast consisting of homemade cakes and hot drinks is served daily, while eggs are on request.

A restaurant specialising in local cuisine, and a bar with a furnished terrace are available on site.

The free spa has a sauna, an indoor pool and a hot tub, together with a Turkish bath, while massages are on request.

Guests can enjoy cycling excursions or relax in the garden. Madonna di Campiglio centre and the Spinale ski lifts are 1 km from Lorenzetti Hotel.

Free access to a ski shuttle is provided, while a public bus stopping 100 metres away has links to Trento.
