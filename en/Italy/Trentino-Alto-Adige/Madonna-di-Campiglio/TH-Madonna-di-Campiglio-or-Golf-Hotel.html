Description

On the slopes of Campo Carlo Magno, Golf Hotel Campiglio is a 5-minute walk away from the Grosté ski lifts.

It features sports and wellness options, available on request at an extra cost. Cross-country skiing and trekking start from right in front of the Golf Hotel, and ski storage is available.

There is a 9-hole golf course on site, together with bike rental. Rooms are spacious and comfortable.

Many overlook the Brenta mountain range and each has an LCD TV. Golf Hotel Campiglio provides free shuttles into Madonna di Campiglio centre every 30 minutes.

Entertainment is organised at the piano bar, and children have a kids' club and a playroom.
