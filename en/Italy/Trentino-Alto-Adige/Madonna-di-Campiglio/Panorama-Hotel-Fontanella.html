Description

Rooms at Panorama Hotel are comfortable and spacious, with LCD TV and private bathroom with bathtub or shower. Some rooms have a balcony.

The restaurant is opened for dinner, from 7:30 p.m. to 9 p.m. Meals are served in the dining room with views of the mountains.

Panorama Hotel Fontanella is near many hiking and cycling paths. During winter there is a free shuttle bus to the ski slopes.

The Bonapace family and the friendly staff can help arrange transportation and tours.

Policies

Children and Extra Beds
All children are welcome.
Free!One child under 2 years stays free of charge when using existing beds.
One child under 2 years is charged EUR 15 per person per night in a crib.
Up to two children from 2 to 7 year are charged 50 % of the room stay per night per person for extra beds.
Up to two children from 8 to 11 year are charged 70 % of the room stay per night per person for extra beds.
Up to two children from 12 to 17 year are charged 80 % of the room stay per night per person for extra beds.
Up to two older children or adults are charged 90 % of the room stay per night per person for extra beds.
The maximum number of extra beds in a room is 2.
The maximum number of cribs in a room is 1.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
