Description

The Berglandhotel Untertheimerhof is a 4-star hotel located 5 km from Chiusa. It offers a Tyrolean restaurant, a spa, 2 pools, and Alpine-style rooms with satellite LCD TVs.

Rooms at the Untertheimerhof are decorated with natural-wood furniture. Each comes with a private bathroom, and some rooms have balconies with Dolomite views.

A daily buffet breakfast is provided, including homemade pastries and jam. The restaurant serves a 5-course dinner and guests can enjoy a free welcome drink on arrival.

A indoor pool, outdoor pool, infrared cabin, sauna, jacuzzi and Turkish bath await guests at the wellness centre.

Free bike hire is available, and in summer the hotel provides a free hiking excursion with barbecue included.

The Gröden and Plose ski slopes are a 30-minute drive from the hotel. Bressanone is 18 km away.
