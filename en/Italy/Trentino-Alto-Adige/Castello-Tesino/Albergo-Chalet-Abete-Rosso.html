Description

Albergo Chalet Abete Rosso is located in Castello Tesino, 60 km from Trento. Guests can enjoy the on-site bar.

Free WiFi is featured and free private parking is available on site. At the family-run Abete Rosso Albergo Chalet, the rooms are all provided with a private balcony and TV.

A buffet-style breakfast is provided daily, and the restaurant serves typical regional cuisine.

You will find a shared lounge at the property. Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including skiing, golfing and cycling.

Bassano del Grappa can be reached in 1-hour drive. Treviso Airport is 64 km from Hotel Chalet Abete Rosso.
