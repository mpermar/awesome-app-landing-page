Description

Offering heated ski storage and a free private ski bus, Chalet Val Di Rabbi is a converted farmhouse that is typical of 18th-century Rabbi.

It features a 37°C hot tub and Alpine-style rooms with free Wi-Fi. This eco-friendly property utilises energy-saving strategies and has been renovated using local products such as pine and stone.

Offering views of the Alps, the rooms come with memory pillows, satellite LCD TV and a professional hairdryer.

Homemade cakes, bacon and cheese are included in the breakfast buffet. The restaurant specialises in Tyrolean cuisine, fresh local ingredients and wines and spirits.

Meals are served on the panoramic terrace in fine weather. You will find a small shop selling local produce and packed lunches are available on request.

The 400 m² garden includes a children’s playground, picnic area and fenced area for dogs.

Discounts are available at castles and museums in the surroundings and the free shuttle takes you to the Daolasa ski lifts and Malè Train Station.
