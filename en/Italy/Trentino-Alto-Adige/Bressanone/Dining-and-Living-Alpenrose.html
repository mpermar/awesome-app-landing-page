Description

Located a 10-minute drive from the centre of Bressanone and 4 km from Bressanone Train Station, Dining & Living Alpenrose offers accommodation with free WiFi and free private parking.

Featuring a balcony and mountain view, each room comes with a TV and a private bathroom.

There is a a restaurant and a bar on site. Guests can also relax in the hotel's wellness area.

The area is popular for hiking, cycling and skiing. Bolzano is 40 km from Dining & Living Alpenrose.
