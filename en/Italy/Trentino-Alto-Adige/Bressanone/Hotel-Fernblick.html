Description

Hotel Fernblick is situated on a hillside location in San Leonardo, 10 km from Bressanone and a 10-minute drive from the Plose ski area.

It features an outdoor pool and panoramic views of Isarco Valley. The rooms in Alpine style feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a balcony with mountain views, and a private bathroom.

A hearty sweet and savoury breakfast buffet is served in the morning. The Fernblick’s restaurant serves traditional South Tyrolean cuisine.

The Fernblick features a sauna, a sun terrace, and a ski storage room. Guided tours for motorcyclists and a parking garage with a tool kit for motorcycles are also available.

A public bus with links to Bressanone and a free ski bus stop next to the Fernblick.
