Description

Situated in Bressanone in the Trentino Alto Adige Region, 7. 5 km from Cathedral of Bressanone, Apartment Lodge Gasserhof boasts a year-round outdoor pool and views of the mountain.

The hotel has water sports facilities and ski storage space, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Free WiFi is offered throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Some rooms include a seating area where you can relax. A flat-screen TV with satellite channels is featured.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including skiing and horse riding.

Pharmacy Museum is a 15-minute drive from Apartment Lodge Gasserhof, while Plose Kabinenbahn / Cabinovia Plose is 1 km from the property.
