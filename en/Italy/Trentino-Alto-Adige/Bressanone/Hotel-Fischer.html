Description

Overlooking the Dolomites and set 3 km from the Plose ski area, family-run Hotel Fischer offers a free infinity pool and a free BrixenCard.

Featuring a spa, it has rooms with a satellite flat-screen TV. A buffet breakfast consisting of sweet and savoury items is served daily, and includes bread, homemade jams and juices as well as cheese and cold cuts.

A restaurant specialising in traditional dishes, and a bar are available on site.

Each room has wooden furniture, carpeted floors and a private bathroom with a shower or a bath tub.

Some comes with a balcony with views of Bressanone. The wellness centre includes a sauna, Turkish bath and hot tub.

Guests can relax in the garden or in the sun terrace, while the BrixenCard gives free access to public transport, museums and outdoor activities.

The centre of Bressanone is 10 minutes’ drive from Fischer Hotel, while a ski bus stops 700 metres away.
