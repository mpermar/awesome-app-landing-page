Description

Set in Trentino, Solea Boutique & Spa Hotel is located in Fai della Paganella in Adamello Brenta Natural Park.

The hotel features a 1500 mt² wellness centre, and a traditional restaurant. WiFi is free throughout.

The wooden-floored bedrooms have a balcony, a flat-screen TV, a minibar, and a stylish courtesy set.

Some rooms feature traditional Alpine-style furniture, while others have a more design feel.

Solea Boutique & Spa Hotel prides of its wellness centre, which includes a sauna area, a Turkish bath, a frigidarium, and indoor and outdoor pools.

This spa of excellence spans over 1500 mt² offering panoramic views of the Paganella mountains and the Brenta Dolomites, and SPA Vital treatments provided by qualified staff of professionals.

The restaurant offers typical dishes of local and national cuisine, a rich breakfast buffet.

Whether in summer or winter, you can choose for you and your family activities with Alpine guides, instructors of Nordic walking and mountain bike.

Solea Boutique & Spa Hotel is equipped with free covered parking. In winter, it offers access to a heated ski and boot warmer.

Every 30 minutes a free shuttle links the hotel to the ski lifts. This hotel is located just 800 meters from the ski slopes and lifts of Mount Paganella.

The property is only 25 km from the highways Trento North and San Michele all'Adige.
