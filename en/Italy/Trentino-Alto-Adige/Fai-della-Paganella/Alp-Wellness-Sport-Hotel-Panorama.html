Description

Alp Wellness Sport Hotel Panorama offers extensive grounds, numerous leisure facilities and panoramic views of Mount Paganella.

It has 5 tennis courts and both indoor and outdoor pools. Rooms have a traditional Alpine style with wood-beamed ceilings.

They feature a TV with satellite channels, free Wi-Fi access and a private balcony.

Hotel Panorama provides a free shuttle to the Paganella Andalò ski area, 500 metres away.

Guests also have a free ski storage area and discounts at a local ski school. Your day at this Wellness Sport Hotel begins with a large buffet breakfast including homemade cakes and fresh croissants.

Lunch is buffet style while dinner is à la carte. In warmer weather, guests can relax on the bar's terrace, on the sun terrace, and out in the large garden.

They also have free access to the spa, volleyball pitch, gym and a relaxation area with piano and reading corner.
