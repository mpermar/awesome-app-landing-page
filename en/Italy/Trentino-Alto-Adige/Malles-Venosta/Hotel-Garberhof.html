Description

One of our top picks in Malles Venosta.The Garberhof offers a wellness center with spa, luxurious accommodations, and delicious cuisine. The resort overlooks the Vinschgau Valley, and the free shuttle to the Watles slopes stops nearby. WiFi is free throughout.

The Garberhof’s wellness center features a huge range of beauty treatments and massages, as well as different types of saunas, showers and hot tubs. There is also a heated indoor pool.

There is a fitness center on site and bikes can be rented from reception. Children have their own playground.

Rooms at the Garberhof are beautifully furnished and spacious. Some suites come with their own spa bath and a large panoramic terrace.

The restaurant serves a mix of South Tyrol and Mediterranean Italian specialties, for lunch and dinner. The bar prepares excellent tea.
