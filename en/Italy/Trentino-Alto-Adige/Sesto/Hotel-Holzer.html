Description

Set in a building dating back to 1905, the family-run Hotel Holzer is situated at the foot of Sesto Dolomites, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

With free WiFi, it has Tyrolean-style and individually designed rooms with an LCD TV and light wood furniture.

Guests can be picked up at San Candido Train Station at no extra cost. A daily breakfast includes different qualities of tea and coffee, homemade jams and sweet options.

Gluten and lactose free menus are also available. Guests can have relaxing moments in the small spa equipped with a sauna, Turkish bath, two hot tubs and a solarium.

Every week the Hotel Holzer organises hiking tours in summer and ski trips in winter.

A bus stop with connections to San Candido and Brunico is 100 meters away. The Tre Cime Natural Park is a 5-minute drive away.
