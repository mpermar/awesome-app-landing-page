Description

Alpenwellnesshotel St. Veit is set in Sexten, 100 metres from Monte Elmo's ski slopes.

The Helm-Rotwand ski lifts can be reached directly from the hotel's garden. It offers a spa, an indoor pool and free Wi-Fi.

The restaurant is part of the Slow Food movement, and the breakfast is buffet style.

All en suite, the rooms feature a balcony, satellite TV, and a minibar. Facilities of the luxurious wellness centre include a sauna and a Turkish bath.

Shuttle transfers and hiking equipment are also available. Professional staff lead guided tours and Nordic walking expeditions.

A library with guidebooks and maps is also available. The Austrian border is less than a 20-minute drive away and Parking is free.
