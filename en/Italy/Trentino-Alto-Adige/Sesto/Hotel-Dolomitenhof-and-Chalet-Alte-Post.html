Description

Set deep in the Tre Cime National Park, Hotel Dolomitenhof & Chalet Alte Postoffers a wellness centre and 2 swimming pools.

The hotel is 10 km from San Candido Train Station. The property is comprised of 2 buildings, the Dolomitenhof and the Alte Post, which are connected by an underground tunnel.

Each provides flat-screen satellite TV, and a private bathroom with hairdryer. Some rooms face south.

The rooms located in the Dolomitenhof are furnished in a 70s vintage style, while the units in the Chalet are decorated in the traditional alpine style.

Breakfast at the Dolomitenhof can be eaten on the terrace, and includes freshly baked croissants and local cold meats and cheeses.

In the evening, the restaurant offers regional dishes and an extensive salad bar.

At the spa, guests can book massages and beauty treatments. Facilities include a sauna, steam bath and an indoor pool.

The hotel provides free parking, and is a short drive from Brunico, Sesto, and Lienz in Austria.
