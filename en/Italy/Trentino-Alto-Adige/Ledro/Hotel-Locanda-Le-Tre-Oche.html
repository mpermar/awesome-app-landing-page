Description

Hotel Locanda Le Tre Oche offers rustic-style rooms, a generous buffet breakfast, and a Tyrolean restaurant, all in the heart of Molina di Ledro.

The southern shores of Lake Ledro are a 10-minute walk away. Featuring mountain views and a TV, rooms come with chequered fabrics, and carpeted floors.

The private bathroom includes a hairdryer. You can have dinner in the restaurant, and choose traditional recipes from the à la carte menu.

Cold cuts, jams and muesli feature for breakfast. Eggs are prepared on request. Trekking paths and a tennis court are found 300 metres from Locanda Le Tre Oche.

Guests enjoy discounts on massages and beauty treatments at a wellness centre 400 metres away.
