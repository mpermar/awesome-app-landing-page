Description

Featuring a free wellness centre, the Ecolabel Hotel Madonnina Resort is nestled in the Val di Fassa valley, 500 metres from the centre of Soraga.

All rooms come with an LCD satellite TV and views of the surrounding Dolomites. An indoor pool, sauna and Turkish bath are provided at the wellness centre, which features ambient lighting and relaxing loungers.

Massage and solarium sessions can be booked at extra cost. With carpeted or parquet floors, all rooms at Hotel Madonnina Resort And Wellness have a minifridge and private bathroom.

Some rooms include a comfortable seating area. Breakfast is a buffet, with cakes, cold meats and cheeses.

Open every evening, the restaurant serves classic Italian dishes and local specialities.

In the winter, ski storage and boot warmers are provided, and the Tre Valli Slopes are 3 km away.
