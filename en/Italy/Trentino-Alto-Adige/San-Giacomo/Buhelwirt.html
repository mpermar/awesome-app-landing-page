Description

Set in the hiking area of the Valle Aurina, Wanderhotel Bühelwirt offers free Wi-Fi in public areas and ski storage.

Guests can enjoy the sauna and table tennis. With a panoramic balcony, rooms all come with carpeted floors and a satellite LCD TV.

Each has a private bathroom equipped with a hairdryer. The restaurant serves local specialities.

A sweet and savoury breakfast is offered daily. Wanderhotel Bühelwirt overlooks the Rieserferner-Ahrn Nature Reserve, where hiking, climbing and skiing are possible.
