Description

Featuring a garden and a restaurant, Gasthof Pizzeria Kreuzwirt is situated in San Giacomo.

The property has a terrace, as well as a shared lounge. Facilities include a children's playground and free WiFi is accessible.

At the hotel, all rooms are fitted with a desk and a flat-screen TV. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom fitted with a bidet and free toiletries, while certain rooms are fitted with a seating area.

The rooms feature a wardrobe. A continental or buffet breakfast is available each morning at the property.

Skiing and cycling are among the activities that guests of Gasthof Pizzeria Kreuzwirt can find nearby.

Sand in Taufers is 14 km from the accommodation, while Bruneck is 30 km from the property.
