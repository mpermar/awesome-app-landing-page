Description

Hotel Lener is set in a historic building in the centre of Campo di Trens/Freienfeld, 5 km from the Rosskopf slopes.

It offers a typical restaurant and wellness facilities. Accommodation at the Lener includes self-catering apartments and mountain-style rooms, some with balcony.

Standard facilities include free Wi-Fi and an LCD TV with satellite channels. The restaurant specialises in cuisine of South Tyrol, made with the freshest, high-quality ingredients.

Pizza is made in an original wood-burning oven. Lener Hotel features a furnished sun terrace, and a wellness centre with an indoor heated pool, Finnish sauna and Turkish bath.

The hotel offers a free, heated ski deposit. The free ski bus to the ski slopes runs each hour and stops just outside the property.

From 1 May until 4 November the All-inclusive Guest Card is included in the rate.
