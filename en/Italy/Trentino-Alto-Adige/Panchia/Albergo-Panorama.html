Description

Albergo Panorama is a quiet, Alpine-style hotel, offering panoramic views of the mountains surrounding the village of Panchià.

It has a wellness centre, a 3,000 m² garden, and free parking is provided. Rooms at Panorama Albergo hotel are all en suite and feature a balcony with views.

An LCD TV with satellite channels and a direct dial phone are also available. A rich breakfast buffet is served daily and includes items such as cold cuts, cheese, bread, and homemade cakes.

The restaurant serves regional and Italian cuisine. The garden is equipped with sun loungers, parasols, and a playground.

The wellness centre features a Finnish sauna with a steam bath, and an outdoor hot tub.

Bicycles are available for free. The town centre is 500 metres away. Cavalese is 7 km from the property, and Trento can be reached in 1 hour by car.

The A22 Autostrada del Brennero motorway is a 30-minute drive from Panorama. Guests enjoy a free FiemmE-Motion Guest Card, including some leisure activities, public transport and access to several museums and nature parks.

Please contact the property for more details, as included services vary according to seasonality.
