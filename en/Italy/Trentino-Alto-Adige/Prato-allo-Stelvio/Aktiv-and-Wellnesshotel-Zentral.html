Description

Aktiv & Wellnesshotel Zentral is located in the centre of Prato allo Stelvio, offering panoramic mountain views.

Its large wellness centre includes an indoor pool, sauna and Turkish bath. Free parking is provided, as well as a Venosta Valley Card.

Each room at the Zentral is spacious and has a unique design. All rooms feature free Wi-Fi, satellite TV and a minibar.

This Aktiv & Wellnesshotel features 2 games rooms and a children's playground. Meals are served in 2 traditionally decorated dining halls.

A children's menu is also available. The Venosta Valley Card includes free access to public transport and discounts at museums and facilities.

During winter, a free ski bus connects guests to ski slopes around the Val Venosta.

Popular summer activities include mountain biking and trekking in Stilfserjoch National Park.
