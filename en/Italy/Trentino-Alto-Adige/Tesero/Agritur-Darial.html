Description

The family-run Agritur Darial in Tesero offers panoramic mountain views in the heart of South Tyrol.

Its extensive grounds include a children's playground, BBQ and plenty of farm animals.

The apartments are decorated with spruce-wooden furniture and come with a fully equipped kitchen.

The private bathroom comes with a large shower. A buffet breakfast including cakes, homemade jams and biscuits, cold cuts and scrambled eggs is served in the morning.

Guests can relax in the lounge where afternoon snacks can be served. There is a small wellness area with sauna, hot tub and Turkish bath.

A more active choice is the nearby Val di Fiemme ski area, easily reached by free ski bus.

Free parking is provided and the property is easily reached from the A22 motorway.

Bolzano is a 40-minute drive away. Guests enjoy a free FiemmE-Motion Guest Card, including some leisure activities, public transport and access to several museums and nature parks.

Please contact the property for more details, as included services vary according to seasonality.
