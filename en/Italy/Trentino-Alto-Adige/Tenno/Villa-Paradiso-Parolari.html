Description

Villa Paradiso Parolari offers self-catering accommodation set in a renovated, 19th-century farmhouse.

Situated in Tenno, it is 40 km from Trento. All units include a seating area and a kitchenette.

There is a private bathroom with a shower and bidet in each unit. The property also offers grocery delivery.

Madonna di Campiglio is 50 km from Villa Paradiso Parolari, while Riva del Garda is 14 km away.
