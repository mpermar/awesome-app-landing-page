Description

Surrounded by nature in the heart of South Tyrol, at Hotel Bergschlössl you can relax in the 2 swimming pools or in the sauna or Jacuzzi on the panoramic terrace.

Set near the picturesque town of Luson, this charming hotel is ideal for a peaceful break.

Take a swim in the indoor or outdoor pool, and choose from a variety of relaxing massages.

Each room at Bergschlössl is modern and comfortable and complete with your own, private balcony.

Start your day with a rich buffet breakfast including local and organic ingredients.

In the evening, enjoy regional cuisine in the elegant restaurant. This is followed by drinks in the traditional bar, accompanied by music from the hotel’s orchestra.
