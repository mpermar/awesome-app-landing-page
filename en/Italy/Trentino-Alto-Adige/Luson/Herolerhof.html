Description

Set in Luson, Herolerhof has direct access to 3 km of cross-country skiing. It has a large garden with children's playground, plus a free sauna and Turkish bath, and a restaurant exclusive to guests.

Herolerhof gives you the choice of rooms and apartments, all with a traditional Alpine design.

All have mountain views, which vary according to their direction, south, east or west.

They feature satellite TV, carpeted or wood floors and a private bathroom. Breakfast is buffet style, while the restaurant offers a fixed menu of South Tyrol specialities at dinner.

Guests also have free parking and free ski storage. The small ski lift of Balbein is 6 km away and takes you to the children-friendly Luson ski slopes.

The Plose and Gitschberg ski slopes are 20 km away. There is a toboggan run next to the hotel, and staff arrange guided tours on snow shoes.
