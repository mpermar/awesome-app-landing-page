Description

Surrounded by the Dolomites, Hotel Schneeberg Family Resort & SPA offers a 19685 ft² spa and wellness area. It features a heated open air pool, 3 restaurants and an indoor children playground with a swimming pool.

With a balcony with mountain views, all rooms and suites are decorated with Alpine-style furnishings and carpeted floors. Each comes with free Wi-Fi access, a satellite LCD TV and a private bathroom equipped with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

The 3 restaurants, one of which is reserved for guests and has a terrace, serve regional specialties, as well as international cuisine and pizza. A sweet and savory breakfast is served daily.

Guests can boat and ice skate on the natural lake. Free hiking tours, free babysitting for kids of 3 years and older, billiards, bowling, mini golf and table tennis are all available service.

Racines ski area is 9.3 mi away and ski passes can be directly purchased at Hotel Schneeberg. A bus stop to Vipiteno is right in front of the property.
