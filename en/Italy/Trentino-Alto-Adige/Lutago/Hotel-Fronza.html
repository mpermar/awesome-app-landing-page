Description

Boasting a hot tub and saunfa, Hotel Ahrntaler Alpenhof offers accommodation with mountain views in Lutago, in the Trentino Alto Adige Region.

Guests can enjoy a drink at the bar. Free WiFi and free private parking are available on site.

All rooms have a flat-screen TV. Some units have a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

All rooms come with a private bathroom. This hotel has ski storage space and free use of bicycles is available.

An array of activities are offered in the area, such as skiing and hiking. The hotel also offers ski equipment hire.
