Description

At the hotel, every room has a desk. Every room comes with a flat-screen TV, and some accommodations at Hotel Alpenblick have a terrace. All rooms have a closet.

Continental and buffet breakfast options are available every morning at the accommodation. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of Grill/bbq, international and Italian dishes.

The property has a sauna. Skiing is among the activities that guests can enjoy near Hotel Alpenblick.

Alm-Express is 1.7 miles from the hotel, while Seenock is 1.9 miles from the property.
