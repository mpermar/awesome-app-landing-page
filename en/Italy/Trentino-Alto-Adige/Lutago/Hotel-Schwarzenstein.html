Description

The rooms and suites at the Schwarzenstein are decorated in a classic or Alpine style. Each includes a balcony and an LCD TV.

An extensive buffet breakfast is provided daily. Guests can enjoy eggs, bacon and sausage, along with pastries and fresh fruit.

Free access to the sauna, pools and gym is included. There is also a Turkish bath and massage treatments available. Outdoor enthusiasts will appreciate the free ski storage, ski bus and weekly mountain excursions.

A shuttle is available to/from Brunico Train Station, 12 miles away. The A22 Autostrada del Brennero highway is a 50-minute drive from the hotel.

Policies

Children and Extra Beds
All children are welcome.
All children under 2 years are charged 20 % of the room stay per night per person in a crib.
All children from 2 to 12 year are charged 65 % of the room stay per night per person for extra beds.
All older children or adults are charged 80 % of the room stay per night per person for extra beds.
The maximum number of extra beds in a room is 2.
The maximum number of cribs in a room is 1.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
