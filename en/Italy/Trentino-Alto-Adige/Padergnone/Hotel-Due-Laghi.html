Description

Set between Lake Toblino and Lake Santa Massenza, Hotel Due Laghi is 1 km from Padergnone town centre.

It offers an outdoor pool and a furnished garden. Air-conditioned rooms at the Due Laghi have TV, classic-style furnishings and a private bathroom.

WiFi in all areas is free. Guests can enjoy a buffet breakfast every morning. It includes cakes, cold cuts and cheeses, together with hot drinks.

A bus stop with links to Riva del Garda and Trento is 50 metres away. Parking at the property is free.
