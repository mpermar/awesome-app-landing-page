Description

Surrounded by the Dolomites in Riva di Tures, family-run Hotel Berger has a restaurant, wellness area and its own cattle farm.

The traditionally furnished rooms feature a balcony, sofa and satellite flat-screen TV.

Rooms at the Berger all have carpeted floors and a private bathroom including a hairdryer, bathrobe and free toiletries.

Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas. Homemade cakes, biscuits and bread feature in the buffet breakfast along with cheeses, eggs and their local veal.

The restaurant serves South-Tyrolean and Italian specialities, while a snack bar is also available.

Guests can work out at the gym, or relax in the wellness area , which includes an indoor pool, hot tub and sauna.

The property is well placed for hiking and biking activities in the Rieserferner-Ahrn Nature Park.
