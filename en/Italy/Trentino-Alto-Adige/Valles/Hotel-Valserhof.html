Description

Hotel Valserhof is set in the heart of the Val Pusteria, surrounded by the Dolomites.

The views here are simply unforgettable. The elegant Hotel Valserhof has a relaxing wellness centre with spa, accesible for free.

Treatments and massages are also available (extra costs). Rooms are spacious and all have a private balcony.
