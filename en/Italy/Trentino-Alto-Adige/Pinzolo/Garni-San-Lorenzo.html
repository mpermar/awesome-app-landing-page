Description

Centrally located in Pinzolo, family-run Garni San Lorenzo offers contemporary-style rooms with a flat-screen TV and a breakfast with homemade cakes and eggs.

The Doss del Sabion ski slopes are a 10-minute walk away. The en suite rooms face the main square from their balcony.

They have simple décor, and are decorated with red and white colour schemes. Sweet and savoury items is served for breakfast, and gluten-free food can be catered on request.

San Lorenzo Garni’s guests can dine at a special rate at nearby restaurants. In winter, a ski bus stopping 50 metres from the hotel will link you to the Doss del Sabion ski slopes, part of the Madonna di Campiglio-Marilleva ski circuit.

Madonna di Campiglio, with its lively promenade and boutique shops, is less than 15 minutes away by car.
