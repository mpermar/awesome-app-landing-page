Description

Featuring an outdoor pool and a fully equipped Wellness area, Naturhotel Sonnenhof offers rooms with free WiFi, a 10-minute drive from Chiusa.

A Continental breakfast is served daily. The en suite rooms feature parquet floors and views of the surrounding mountains.

All come with a safe and a satellite flat-screen TV and a balcony. Naturhotel Sonnenhof has an à la carte Restaurant where guests can enjoy local and Italian cuisine.

Alpe di Siusi, Val Gardena and Plose ski lifts are within 25 km of the property and a free shuttle service is available daily.
