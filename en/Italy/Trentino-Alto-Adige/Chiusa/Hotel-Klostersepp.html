Description

Set in Chiusa, Gasthof Klostersepp is a 10-minute walk from the historic centre and 500 metres from the public outdoor pool.

It features a free parking garage and restaurant open at lunch and dinner. The rooms offer mountain views and feature a flat-screen TV and private bathroom.

Most have a balcony overlooking the Monastery of Sabiona. Cakes, fresh fruit and cold meats and served as part of the buffet breakfast.

The bar is open all day and the restaurant offers Italian and international dishes and local specialities.

Gasthof Klostersepp features a garden with a small swimming pool and a terrace that is connected to the restaurant and bar.

Indoors, there is a bowling alley and games room. You are 1 km from Chiusa Train Station and 20 km from the Val Gardena ski area.
