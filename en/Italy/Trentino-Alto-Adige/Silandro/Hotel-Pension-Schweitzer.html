Description

Hotel Pension Schweitzer is in Silandro, 400 meters from the Stelvio National Park.

It features a garden, a furnished patio and terrace. Some rooms offer mountain views.

A free Wi-Fi hotspot is available throughout. All the renovated rooms have an LCD TV, large windows and elegant furnishings.

They are fitted with a private bathroom with shower and free toiletries. Some offer either views of the town or Alps.

At the Schweitzer guests can enjoy a sweet and savoury buffet breakfast each morning, it includes cereal and cold cuts.

A snack bar is also available. You can reach the popular Belpiano ski slope in less than a 50-minute drive from the property.

The hotel offers a shuttle service to/from Bolzano and Verona Airports at an extra cost, while it is free to/from Silarno Train Station.
