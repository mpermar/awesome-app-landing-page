Description

Hotel Norge is located in a quiet location, at an altitude of 1400 metres and offers ski-to-door access and panoramic views of the Dolomiti del Brenta mountains.

It features a free indoor pool and wellness area. The city of Trento can be reached in 20 minutes by car.

The modern rooms at Hotel Norge come with an LCD satellite TV, a hair dryer, and a minibar.

Most rooms feature a balcony with mountain views. Guests can enjoy lunch and dinner at the on-site Ristorante Norge, serving typical cuisine of the region which can also be enjoyed on the terrace in fine weather.

Free WiFi is available in public areas. A spa centre with a sauna, a steam bath, and a relaxation area with chromotherapy and sensory showers invites to relax.

The pool is heated and includes a hydro-massage corner. A range of beauty treatments and massages can be booked upon request.
