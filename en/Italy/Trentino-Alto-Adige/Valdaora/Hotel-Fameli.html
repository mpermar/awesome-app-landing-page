Description

Featuring a wellness centre and two indoor pools, Hotel Famelì offers accommodation with free WiFi in Valdaora di Mezzo.

Plan de Corones cable car in the Dolomiti Superski area is 1 km away. Each unit comes with a satellite TV and balcony.

Some rooms feature a spa bath. A varied breakfast buffet is served daily, including cappuccino, pastries, local cheese and yogurt.

Guests can enjoy local specialties and wines at the on-site restaurant. The area is famous for mountain hiking, climbing, and Nordic walking.
