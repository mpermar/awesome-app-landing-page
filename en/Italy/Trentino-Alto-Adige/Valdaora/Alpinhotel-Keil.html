Description

Located in the picturesque village of Olang, near the Kronplatz ski resort, this family-run hotel provides a friendly atmosphere and excellent food.

Rooms at Alpinhotel Keil come with a TV and private bathroom. Most have a balcony with mountain views and some are located in an annex building.

Your day at the Alpinhotel begins with a delicious buffet breakfast. You will find a children's playground and table tennis in the garden, while indoors there is a sauna and hot tub.

Keil provides a ski pass service and a heated ski storage area. Staff can plan excursions around the Alps and Dolomites as well as providing useful maps and tourist information.

Packed lunches are available upon request. At Alpinhotel Keil you can indulge in a 5-course meal in the evening.

The restaurant specialises in South Tyrolean cuisine using fresh, regional ingredients and herbs from the garden.
