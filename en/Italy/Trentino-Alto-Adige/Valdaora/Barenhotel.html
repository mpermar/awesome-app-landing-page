Description

Situated in Valdaora, directly at the ski slopes. Bärenhotel has a number of amenities including an all year sky pool, a sauna, fitness centre and a private helicopter port.

Rooms at the hotel all come with private bathroom and a balcony. Free WiFi is also available throughout the property.

Guests can enjoy a continental breakfast every morning. A restaurant is also available on site.
