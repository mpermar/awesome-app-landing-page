Description

Set in the Plan de Corones ski area, surrounded by the Dolomites, Apparthotel Winklwiese offers rooms and studios with a balcony or terrace.

The centre of Ober Olang is 5 minutes' walk away. Alpine-style accommodation at the family-run Winklwiese includes satellite TV, WiFi access, and a private bathroom.

The studios also feature a kitchenette. Guests can enjoy a sweet and savoury buffet breakfast, served in the spacious dining hall, including cold cuts, cheeses, bread and yoghurt.

During the winter, a ski shuttle to the Valdaora ski slopes stops 20 metres away.

Staff can arrange guided excursions to nearby nature parks, including Fanes-Sennes-Prags, Rieserferner Ahrn, and the Puez-Geisler and Sextner.
