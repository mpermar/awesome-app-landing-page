Description

Set in Ponte Arche, Comano Cattoni Holiday offers a wellness area with indoor swimming pool, Turkish bath and sauna.

It is 600 metres from Terme di Comano thermal area. With a private bathroom, all rooms have a classic design with carpeted or wood floors and some with wood-beamed ceilings.

Some feature a TV with pay-per-view channels. Breakfast at Comano Cattoni Holiday is buffet style, while the restaurant offers a creative menu of Trentino specialities and international favourites.

Bread and cakes are homemade, and children's and special diet meals are also available.

This 4-star hotel hosts BBQs and dancing and live music in its bar. During the day, children can have fun at the mini club.

At the wellness centre beauty treatments and massages can be booked. Free parking is provided and transfers to the Bolbeno ski slope, 10 km away, are also available.
