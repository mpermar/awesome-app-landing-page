Description

Offering a free outdoor pool and garden with children's playground, RTA Hotel Le Vallene is located 4 km from the centre of Terlago and 12 km from Trento.

It offers free Wi-Fi, an indoor games area and a garage for bikes and motorbikes.

Each Alpine-style room offers mountain views, a flat-screen TV and carpeted or parquet floors.

The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries. A breakfast consisting of cheese, cold cuts and eggs, together with pastries and hot drinks, is served daily.

The on-site restaurant specialises in regional cuisine. A wide range of activities are available nearby, including cycling and climbing.

Lamar Lakes are 2 km from RTA Hotel Le Vallene, while Molveno Lake is 36 km away.
