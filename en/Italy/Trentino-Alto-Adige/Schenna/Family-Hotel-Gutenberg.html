Description

Offering a wellness centre and a garden, Hotel Gutenberg has rooms with a balcony, mountain views and free WiFi.

Located 2 km from the centre of Schenna, it comes with ski storage and a sun terrace.

Each room here will provide you with a satellite flat-screen TV and carpeted floors.

Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and bathrobes. A restaurant serving local specialities is available on site, as well as a bar.

The wellness centre includes a sauna, fitness centre, an indoor pool and a Turkish bath.

The Ifinger cable car is reachable in less than 10 minutes' drive. Merano is 6 km from Hotel Gutenberg.
