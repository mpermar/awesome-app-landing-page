Description

Set at an altitude of 800 metres in Scena, Hotel Fürstenhof offers Alpine-style rooms, 7 km from Merano.

It features a furnished garden with a pool, a restaurant, and a spa. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

Suites and rooms at the family-run Fürstenhof have a balcony with mountain or valley views, an LCD satellite TV, and a refrigerator.

The private bathroom offers soft bathrobes and slippers, free toiletries, and a hairdryer.

Served on the terrace in summer, a sweet and savoury breakfast buffet is provided daily, with cold cuts, bacon and eggs, and fresh fruit.

The restaurant, open for lunch and dinner, serves Tyrolean and international dishes.

After a busy day enjoying nature, guests can relax at the wellness centre, including 3 saunas, steam bath, an indoor pool and fitness centre.

Guests can also use the hotel’s free bikes, or head to the free tennis court, located 500 metres away.

The Trauttmansdorff Castle Gardens are 6 km from the property, whilst the Gruppo di Tessa Nature Park is 20 minutes’ drive away.
