Description

Hotel Starkenberg is a modern 4-star property at an altitude of 600 metres, offering views of the surrounding mountains, Merano and Schenna Castle.

It features a summer pool and 350 m¬≤ of wellness facilities. Only 5 minutes' walk from Schenna centre, the Starkenberg Hotel is 150 metres from the bus stop for Merano.

Hiking tours are organised once a week. The rooms have balconies overlooking the mountains, a 32" LCD TV with satellite channels, and either carpeted or parquet floors.

Homemade cakes, cold cuts, cheeses and much more are available at the buffet breakfast, which can be enjoyed out on the terrace with views.

The restaurant is open to the public at lunch and dinner and serves top-quality South Tyrol dishes.

Indoors you have a Finnish sauna, steam bath and pool with hydromassage area. Outdoors, the pool and hot tub are open from May until September.
