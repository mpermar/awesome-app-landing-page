Description

Located 2 km from the Merano 2000 ski area, Residence Sonne has an outdoor pool with panoramic mountain views.

It offers elegant apartments with a balcony and satellite TV. Apartments at the Sonne Residence are fitted with fine fabrics and parquet floors.

Each comes with mountain views, a fully fitted kitchen and a bathroom. A daily continental breakfast is available on request.

There is a supermarket 2 km from the property. Merano Station is 6 km from the Sonne Apartments.
