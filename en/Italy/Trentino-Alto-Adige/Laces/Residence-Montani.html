Description

Surrounded by fruit orchards in Laces and close to a panoramic cycling path, Residence Montani offers self catering apartments with a garden-view balcony or terrace.

On site you will find a gym and indoor pool. Each apartment at Montani Residence features parquet floors and alpine-style furnishings, as well as an LCD TV.

They also include a dining area with kitchenette, dishwasher and a sofa bed. The private bathroom comes with a hairdryer and a bathtub or a shower.

Pasta dinner costs 6,50 per person from 5 years. is held once a week while in the summer there is a 2-hour babysitting service free of charge.

Children entertainment includes a games room and a playground in the garden. Guests enjoy free use of the pool, sauna and gym, which is open throughout the day.

The kidney-shaped pool offers direct access from the garden, equipped with sun loungers.

Wired internet connection is at extra charge. You can go trekking around the Stelvio National Park or visit Bolzano, set a 50-minute drive away.
