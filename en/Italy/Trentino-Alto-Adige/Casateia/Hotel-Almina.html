Description

Set on the edge of a wood and surrounded by the Dolomites, Hotel Almina offers a free wellness centre, 2 free indoor pools and children's playground.

Featuring free bikes and free WiFi, it has rooms with a mountain-view balcony. A buffet breakfast including eggs, bacon and fresh fruit is served daily.

A restaurant specialising in South Tyrolean and Italian dishes, and a bar, are available on site.

Rooms have traditional Alpine style with carpeted floors, and a satellite TV. Some rooms have parquet floors.

The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and shower. The wellness centre has 3 saunas, Turkish bath and hot tub.

You can go cycling and hiking in the surroundings. A free bus with links to the Racines ski slopes, 8 km from Almina Hotel, is provided in winter.

A bus stopping outside the property has links to Vipiteno, while the centre of Casateia is 5 minutes' drive away.
