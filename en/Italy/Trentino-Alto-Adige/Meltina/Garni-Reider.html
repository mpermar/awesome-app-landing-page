Description

Located in the village of Schlaneid (Salonetto) at an altitude of 1130 metres, Garni Reider is 5 minutes’ drive from Meltina.

It offers free Wi-Fi and a snack bar offering hot and cold dishes and local wines.

Overlooking the mountains or Lake Caldaro, the rustic-style rooms at the Reider come with wooden furnishings and carpeted floors.

Some have a balcony. A buffet breakfast is served in the lounge area or on the terrace.

It consists of sweet and savoury food such as homemade cakes and cold cuts, together with cereal and juices.

Eggs are available on request. Guests can relax in the furnished garden with a children’s playground or in the living room with TV.

A washing machine and microwave are also available. A bus stop with links to the Merano 2000 ski resort and Terlano is 20 metres away.

Parking nearby is free and you are around 30 minutes' drive from Merano and Bolzano.
