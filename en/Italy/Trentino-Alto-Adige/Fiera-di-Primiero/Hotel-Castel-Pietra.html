Description

Set in a quiet location, this 4-star hotel is only a 5-minute walk from Transacqua di Primiero.

It features a spa and wellness centre, a traditional restaurant, free Wi-Fi and free parking in the garage.

Hotel Castel Pietra provides modern rooms and apartments with wooden furniture. They come with heating, a private bathroom, and satellite TV.

Every morning, this hotel serves a rich breakfast buffet. The restaurant is mentioned in the Gambero Rosso restaurant guide and features a terrace.

It offers a good selection of local and international dishes. The surrounding greenery provides an ideal area for outdoor activities like hiking and cycling.

The hotel offers free bikes. During the winter, Hotel Castel Pietra offers free ski storage with boots warmer, and ski classes at the hotel.
