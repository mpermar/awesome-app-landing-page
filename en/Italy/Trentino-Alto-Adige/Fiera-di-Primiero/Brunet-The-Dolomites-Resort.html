Description

Surrounded by the mountains of Trentino, Brunet - The Dolomites Resort offers a wellness centre, free outdoor parking, and rooms with free WiFi.

The restaurant serves local cuisine. The hotel is located on the main road of Tonadico Di Primiero, 12 km from the ski slopes of San Martino di Castrozza.

The Belluno Dolomites National Park natural park is 40-minute drive from the property.

All rooms at Brunet - The Dolomites Resort feature soft carpeted floors, an LCD TV, and a minibar.

Some rooms enjoy full mountain views. The wellness and fitness centre is complete with indoor swimming pool, sauna, and Turkish bath.

Massages are available on request. The hotel offers free bicycles during summer and a free ski deposit with boots warmer.
