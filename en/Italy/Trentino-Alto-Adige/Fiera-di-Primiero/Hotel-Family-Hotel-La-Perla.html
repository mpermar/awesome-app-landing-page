Description

Set in the picturesque Valle di Primiero Valley, this family-run hotel offers a restaurant, bar and free mountain bike hire. All rooms come with flat-screen TV and a balcony.

Rooms at Hotel La Perla have a classic style and wooden furnishings. Some overlook the Paneveggio Natural Park, others feature a small kitchenette and oak-beamed ceilings. Wi-Fi is free in public areas.

Breakfast at Family Hotel La Perla is a buffet of freshly baked pastries, cold meats and cheeses. The restaurant serves Mediterranean cuisine and local specialties.

In the winter, the hotel offers ski storage and equipment hire. Ski passes can be bought at reception, and a free ski bus takes guests to the San Martino Slopes, a 15-minute drive away.

The Dolomiti Bellunesi National Park is 8.7 mi away, and it is a 90-minute drive to Trento.
