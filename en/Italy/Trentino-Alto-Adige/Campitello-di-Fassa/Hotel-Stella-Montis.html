Description

Located in Campitello di Fassa, Hotel Stella Montis has a spa with sauna, gym and hot tub, plus free ski storage.

It offers Alpine-style rooms with a balcony, mountain views and a hydromassage shower.

Tyrolean specialities are served at the hotel's restaurant. There is also a bar, and the daily breakfast is served buffet style.

Free outdoor parking is provided, and guests can also choose to park in the garage, available at extra charge.

A ski bus is available to the Col Rodella ski slopes. Canazei is a 5-minute drive away.
