Description

Located in the Renon Plateau, Hotel Lichtenstern offers free access to its Turkish Bath, Finnish and infrared saunas, and hot tub. This family-run hotel has a free outdoor pool, free Wi-Fi and a sun terrace with views on the Dolomites.

Guests are given a Ritten Card upon arrival, granting free public transport and free entry to Alto Adige’s museums and public swimming pools. Hiking tours are organized twice a week.

Rooms include a satellite flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with free toiletries, a balcony or a terrace, and wooden ceilings. Some rooms offer views of the mountains.

The buffet breakfast consists of sweet and savory products including homemade cakes, cold cuts, cheese and eggs. Breakfast can be served either indoors or on the terrace.

South Tyrolean and Mediterranean specialties, prepared with fresh local products, can be enjoyed at the restaurant which comes with a terrace. A library with German and Italian books is available on site.

The electric train Ferrovia del Renon stopping few feet from the property takes you to the Renon cable car, which has links to Bolzano. On request, a shuttle service can take you to Bolzano Train Station. The Rittner Horn ski slopes are 7.5 mi away.
