Description

Set in a park with outdoor pool, the 3-star superior Park Hotel Il Vigneto is 800 metres from the centre of Arco, and 3 km from Riva del Garda.

Rooms offer a balcony and free Wi-Fi. The property features a free wellness centre, a gym, and free parking.

Rooms at Il Vigneto feature air conditioning, an LCD TV, and a minibar. Guests enjoy every day a varied buffet breakfast including local specialities such as smoked ham, cheese, homemade cakes and gluten-free food.

The snack bar serves sandwiches and first courses. The modern wellness centre includes a sauna,and Turkish bath, while the gym has running machines, exercise bikes and weights.

Surrounded by a vineyard, the hotel is well placed for hiking and biking activities.
