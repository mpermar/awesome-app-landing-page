Description

Set in Arco, 4 km from Riva del Garda and the lakeshore, Hotel Garni Al Frantoio offers free bike rental.

It also has a large garden, a free wellness area, and free private parking. With its yellow façade, this 3-star hotel stands out from the natural surroundings.

Guests can relax out in the equipped garden with sea-water pool, or enjoy the sauna, hot tub and Turkish bath in the wellness area.

Rooms at Garni Al Frantoio come with free WiFi, air conditioning and wooden floors.

They also feature a flat-screen TV, a safe, and a minibar. Breakfast is buffet style at this eco-friendly hotel, and consists of locally produced items.

Gluten-free food is also available. You will have great public transport links with frequent buses stopping in front of the hotel for Arco centre or Riva del Garda.
