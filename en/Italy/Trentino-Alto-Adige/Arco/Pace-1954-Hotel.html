Description

Located in the historical centre of Arco, Pace 1954 Hotel offers free WiFi throughout and a terrace where you can enjoy drinks from the bar, open daily until midnight.

The hotel also features a free bicycle deposit with workshop. The modern rooms come with air conditioning, a satellite TV and private bathroom.

An extensive buffet breakfast is served every morning. Gluten-free options are also available.

At reception you can organize tours and excursions in the area, and request a card providing discounts in selected shops.

You can also rent city bikes and mountain bikes. The Pace Restaurant and Pizzeria can be found at ground floor.

The Pace 1954 Hotel has an underground garage, available at extra cost, while there is a free outdoor parking 50 metres away.
