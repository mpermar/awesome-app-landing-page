Description

Offering a quiet location in the mountains, Hotel Almhof is 6 km outside San Candido.

It features a free wellness area with a variety of saunas and a terrace overlooking the Dolomites.

Rooms here have classic dcor with parquet floors. Each one includes a flat-screen TV and en suite bathroom.

Croissants, cheese, ham and a selection of jams are served buffet style each morning.

The restaurant is open at dinner and serves a mix of Italian and South Tyrol dishes.

Almhof Hotel has a children's play room, ski storage and business centre with free WiFi.

The wellness area includes a hot tub and 3 types of saunas. Ideal for skiing, the property is 3.

5 km from the Helmgebiet ski slope. It is also great for hikes around the local nature reserves during summer.
