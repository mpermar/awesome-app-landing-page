Description

Featuring a free spa and wellness centre and a large garden with sun loungers, Hotel Strasser is located 3 km from San Candido centre.

It offers free Wi-Fi and Alpine-style accommodation. Rooms at the Strasser come with a satellite TV, carpeted floors and a desk.

The private bathroom includes a hairdryer. Homemade cakes feature for the daily sweet and savoury breakfast.

You can enjoy it in the dining room. The restaurant specialises in regional cuisine.

You can have relaxing moments in the free Turkish bath and sauna. Free on-site parking is provided.

Elmo ski slopes are 50 metres from the property. A bus stopping 300 metres will take you into San Candido centre.
