Description

This 4-star hotel offers a family-run atmosphere, bright and spacious accommodation, an on-site restaurant, and a peaceful location in a traffic-free zone in the centre of Innichen.

Free WiFi is available throughout. Rooms are individually furnished and come with historic, modern, or country style design.

They all include a minibar, a TV, and a private bathroom with a vanity mirror and hairdryer.

Run by the Ladinser family, the Orso Grigio uses eco-friendly cleaning products, and renewable energies for air conditioning and heating the hotel.

To respect the environment, the hotel will replace bath linen only on request. Boutique & Gourmet Hotel Orso Grigio offers a spa area including a Finnish sauna, a bio-sauna and a steam bath.

Guests can savour regional meals, local specialities and a wide variety of organic foods at Hotel Orso Grigio's restaurant.
