Description

Hotel Tolderhof is located a 10-minute drive from Innichen, 3 km from the closest lift of the Sextner Dolomiten ski area.

It features free WiFi and a spa centre with a sauna and Turkish bath. The rooms are furnished in Alpine style and come with a wooden beamed ceiling.

They offer a balcony with mountain views, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, and a private bathroom.

A sweet and savoury breakfast buffet with homemade cakes is served in the morning.

The Tolderhof’s à la carte restaurant serves Tyrolean and international cuisine. Hiking and mountain biking trails start right on the doorstep.
