Description

Surrounded by the Dolomites and offering an equipped terrace, Hotel Onach is located in Onies.

Featuring Alpine-style rooms with a balcony, it has free bike rental and a free ski storage room with boot warmer.

A buffet breakfast consisting of juice, homemade cakes, eggs, and cold cuts is served every morning.

A restaurant specialised in South Tyrolean dishes is available on site, as well as a bar.

Each room comes with wooden furniture, a satellite flat-screen TV and carpeted floors.

The private bathroom includes a bath tub or a shower. Massages can be booked at Onach Hotel.

Guests can read a book from the library or relax in the games room. The Plan de Corones ski area is reachable in 15 minutes by car, while Brunico is 10 km away.
