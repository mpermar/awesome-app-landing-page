Description

Residence Klementhof is located within 17 km of the Plose and Jochtal-Gitschberg ski areas, and offers a garden with a seasonal outdoor pool.

Free bikes and free Wi-Fi are available. The self-catering apartments in Alpine style come with a balcony with mountain views, wooden floors, and a living area with a fully equipped kitchenette.

Linen and towels are included. Guests of the Klementhof have access to a furnished garden with barbecue facilities, a ski storage room, and a games room.

The property also provides a small zoo with farm animals for children. A restaurant, a grocery shop, and a bar are 150 metres from Klementhof Residence.

A ski bus and a public bus with links to Bressanone, 7 km away, stop a 2-minute walk from the property.
