Description

Offering a sun terrace and a garden, Hotel Restaurant Putzerhof is located a 3-minute drive from Sciaves.

It features free Wi-Fi and free private parking, and includes rooms with a balcony.

Each room has a satellite flat-screen TV, wooden furnishings and carpeted floors.

The private bathroom comes with a shower. Sausages, cheese and fruit are served daily in the breakfast buffet, as well as toast and hot and cold drinks.

A restaurant serving South Tyrolean and Italian specialities is available on site, as well as a bar.

Putzerhof Hotel Restaurant is well placed for hiking excursions. A ski bus stops opposite the property.

The Gitschberg Jochtal ski area is 11 km away, while the Plose ski slopes are 16 km from the hotel.
