Description

Hotel Christine offers modern accommodation with free Wi-Fi, a large garden, and a swimming pool with hydromassage jets.

It is located in the small town of Gargazzone, a 15-minute drive from Bolzano. All rooms and apartments at Christine come with refined light-wood furniture.

Facilities include an LCD satellite TV and a balcony or terrace. Apartments have a living/dining area with kitchenette.

Homemade jams and freshly made cakes can be enjoyed at breakfast. BBQ evenings are organised once a week.

Free services here include indoor and outdoor parking, bike rental, and table tennis.

The property is 10 km from Merano and 13 km from a cable car linking with the Meran 2000 ski slopes.
