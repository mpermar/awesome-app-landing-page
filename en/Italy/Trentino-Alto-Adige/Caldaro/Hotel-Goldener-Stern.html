Description

Located on the Strada del Vino wine road in Caldaro, Hotel Goldener Stern is located in a renovated 18th century building.

It features a large outdoor pool and a panoramic-view sauna. All modern rooms are uniquely furnished.

Rooms at the Goldener Stern are spacious and come with air conditioning, a minibar and an LCD TV with satellite channels.

Some offer a balcony, and free Wi-Fi is available in the lobby. A daily buffet breakfast is provided, including cappuccino and herbal tea, together with local cheese and mountain yogurt.

There are many restaurants in the area. The wellness centre has a sauna, hot tub and a Turkish bath.

Massage treatments are also available. Lake Caldaro is 2 km from this family-run hotel.
