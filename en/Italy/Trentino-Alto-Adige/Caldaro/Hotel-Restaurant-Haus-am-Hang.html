Description

All rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, microwave, a electric tea pot, a bath, free toiletries and a desk. Free WiFi is accessible to all guests, while some rooms include a terrace. At the hotel, each room is equipped with a seating area.

A buffet breakfast is available daily at Hotel Restaurant Haus am Hang.

The accommodation offers a range of wellness facilities including a fitness center and a hammam. You can play tennis at Hotel Restaurant Haus am Hang, and bike rental is available.

Merano is 20 miles from the hotel, while Bolzano is 9 miles away.
