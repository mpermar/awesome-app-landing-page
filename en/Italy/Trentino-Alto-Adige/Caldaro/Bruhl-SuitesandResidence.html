Description

The units come with hardwood floors and feature a fully equipped kitchenette with a fridge, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with bidet and a hair dryer. There's a seating and/or dining area in some units.

A buffet breakfast is served each morning at the property.

Brühl Suites&Residence has a seasonal outdoor pool.

A bicycle rental service is available at Brühl Suites&Residence, while cycling and hiking can be enjoyed nearby.

Merano is 18 miles from Brühl Suites&Residence, while Bolzano is 8 miles from the property.
