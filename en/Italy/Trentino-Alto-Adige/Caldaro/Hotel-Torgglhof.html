Description

All rooms at the hotel come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a bath. Hotel Torgglhof provides certain rooms with mountain views, and each room is equipped with a balcony. Guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

The daily breakfast offers continental, buffet or Italian options.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool. Guests at Hotel Torgglhof will be able to enjoy activities in and around Caldaro, like cycling.

Merano is 18 miles from the hotel.
