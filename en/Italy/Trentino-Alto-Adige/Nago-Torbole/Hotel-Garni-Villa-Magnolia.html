Description

Located in the village of Torbole on Lake Garda, Hotel Garnì Villa Magnolia is 100 metres from the beach and features a furnished garden and an outdoor swimming pool.

Free Wi-Fi and free parking are provided. Rooms at the Garnì Villa Magnolia are decorated in classic style and have wooden furniture.

They have an LCD TV with satellite channels, and some of them have a balcony, which overlooks the lake or the pool.

Breakfast is served daily and offers a variety of sweet and savoury food, as well as hot drinks.

In the summer, you can enjoy it outside, under the patio. The swimming pool and the terrace, which is located on the second floor, are surrounded by sun loungers, parasols, tables and chairs.

A hot tub is also available. A sail, surf or bike storage is available. The Hotel Garnì Villa Magnolia is 13 km from the A22 Autostrada del Brennero motorway.

A bus stop, located 50 metres from the hotel, connects with Verona and Trento. Verona Airport is 80 km away.
