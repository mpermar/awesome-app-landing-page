Description

Surrounded by the Dolomites, Melodia Del Bosco is in the Alta Badia region, 600 metres from the Santa Croce ski slopes.

It features a restaurant, a large wellness centre, and free Wi-Fi in the lobby. Hotel Melodia Del Bosco is a fully renovated hotel that combines design and natural materials.

Its elegant rooms are decorated with light-wood furniture, and each one comes with a balcony and a flat-screen TV.

The wellness centre is complete with free sauna, Turkish bath and gym. The solarium and hot tub are on request.

Melodia Hotel’s restaurant serves traditional dishes of Italian and South Tyrol cuisines, along with international specialities.

Drinks are served on the terrace with sun loungers. Located in Badia, the hotel provides a free shuttle to the ski area on request.
