Description

Featuring an indoor pool and a spa and wellness centre, Sporthotel Teresa offers rooms and suites in Pedraces, at the foot of the Dolomites.

Guests enjoy an on-site restaurant and bar. Free WiFi is available in public areas All rooms feature a mini bar and flat-screen TV.

Some rooms come with a balcony. Pool towels are provided. An breakfast buffet is available daily.

The restaurant serves regional cuisine at dinner, and you can relax in the traditional lounge with an open fireplace.

During summer, a fitness area and indoor tennis court are also available. The Teresa Sporthotel also offers a garden with BBQ facilities and a terrace with views of Val Badia.

A free shuttle service to/from the Santa Croce and Piz La Ila cable car is available.
