Description

Set at the foot of Mount Sassongher, La Pineta offers a terrace, a garden and apartments with a balcony. With free Wi-Fi and free ski storage, it is 1,000 feet from the center of Colfosco.

Each apartment features views of the Sella Group mountain range, a TV and wooden furniture. The bathroom includes a shower and the kitchen comes with a dishwasher.

La Pineta is well placed for hiking excursions. The Colfosco cable car, which has links to the Dolomiti Superski and Sellaronda ski areas, is a 5-minute walk.
