Description

Set in Corvara, Hotel Col Alto is at the foot of the mountain of the same name, in the heart of the Dolomites.

It provides free access to the large wellness centre. Decorated in Alpine style, including light wood and bright colours, accommodation at the Col Alto features a balcony.

Each room offers a satellite TV and a private bathroom. The Col Alto Hotel’s indoor pool features a hydromassage area and the hotel’s wellness centre includes Finnish sauna, Turkish bath, aromatherapy, and a massage and beauty centre.

The hotel is surrounded by nature and is 400 metres from the ski slopes. Hotel Col Alto is composed of 2 buildings linked through an underground passage.

During certain times of the year, the hotel may only be bookable for weekly stays, with arrival on Saturday or Sunday.
