Description

With panoramic views of Val Venosta valley, Garni Almrausch offers free mountain bike hire and free Wi-Fi.

All rustic-style rooms come with a mountain-view balcony. Rooms at the family-run Almrausch have carpeted floors and wooden furnishings.

Each includes an LCD satellite TV and a private bathroom with hairdryer. The balcony is furnished with a table and chairs.

Freshly prepared eggs and a variety of breads feature at breakfast, together with cold cuts, cheeses and fruit.

You will find a restaurant just 100 metres away. A 5-minute walk from the centre of Burgusio, the property is 4 km from Malles Venosta.

The Watles Ski Slopes are 5 km away and in the winter, they can be reached by a free public ski bus.
