Description

Surrounded by nature, Falken is in Pfalzen, in the province of Bolzano. It offers panoramic views across the valley and a small wellness centre with Finnish sauna, infrared cabin and Turkish bath.

Rooms at Hotel Falken are air conditioned and come with a private bathroom and satellite TV.

Some have a balcony with views of the valley. Services at the hotel include bike rental and free parking.

You can enjoy a drink from the bar out in the garden overlooking the valley. The Falken Hotel is a 5-minute walk from the city centre.
