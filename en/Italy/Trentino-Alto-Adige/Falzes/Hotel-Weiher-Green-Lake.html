Description

Featuring two pools and a wellness centre, Hotel Weiher Green Lake is located in Lago di Issengo, 15 minute's drive from the Kronplatz ski slopes.

Rooms offer a balcony, and the restaurant serves specialities of South Tyrol. The wellness centre includes a sauna and Turkish bath.

An indoor and outdoor pool, as well as a fitness centre are also available to guests.

The restaurant serves classic Italian cuisine, local dishes including lake fish specialities, and a vegetables buffet.

The wine cellar is stocked with Italian and South Tyrolean wines. The garden of the Weiher Green Lake is equipped with free sun beds and parasols.

Parking is also free, and the management provides a free pick up service from Ehrenburg and Bruneck train stations.
