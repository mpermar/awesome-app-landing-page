Description

Set between Selva and Santa Cristina and offering panoramic views of the Sassolungo mountain group, Hotel Jägerheim has Alpine-style rooms with a balcony.

It also features free Wi-Fi, bike rental and a spa. A continental breakfast is served daily at Jägerheim Hotel.

Dinner is served buffet style or you can choose from 2 regional menu options. Special menus and packed lunches are arranged on request.

Overlooking the Sassolungo, Fermeda or Pic mountains, rooms have traditional décor.

They all have private bathrooms with bathrobes and slippers. They also include an LCD satellite TV.

Guests can unwind in the garden equipped with a wooden cottage, terrace and playground.

In the evenings it is possible to relax for free in the Finnish sauna or the hot tub.

Massages and beauty treatments are available at extra costs. The property is right on the ski slopes.

The Val Gardena Ronda Express train station is 50 metres away and provides links to the Sassolungo World Cup Slope.
