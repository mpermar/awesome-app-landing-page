Description

Few steps from central Lagundo and surrounded by fruit orchards, Plonerhof offers a summer pool and a petting zoo with bunnies and kittens.

Rooms and apartments have a balcony facing the Texelgruppe mountain range. Plonerhof’s guests can cook on the shared BBQ in the garden, which is equipped with tables and a playground.

History and hiking books can be borrowed for free. Accommodation includes a flat-screen satellite TV, a private bathroom and wooden furnishings.

Apartments have a kitchenette with dishwasher, while some rooms also face the garden or pool.

A breakfast of homemade organic jam, cold cuts and honey from the farm is available.

The Senales Valley slopes of the Merano 2000 ski area are about 3 km away. Lagundo Train Station is 400 metres from the Plonerhof.
