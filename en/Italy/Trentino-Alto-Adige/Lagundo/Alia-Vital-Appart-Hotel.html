Description

Featuring a free wellness centre and 2 pools, Alia Vital Appart-Hotel is a brand-new establishment offering room and independent apartment accommodation, with all the services of a modern 4-star hotel, in a pleasant South-Tyrolean setting near Merano, 3 km away.

At the Alia Hotel you will enjoy staying in spacious apartments (suitable for 2 to 6 persons), furnished with a balcony and a bathroom complete with slippers and bathrobes.

The free spa includes a hot tub, a Finnish sauna and a Turkish bath, together with a gym, a beauty centre and Kneipp path.

The Alia is situated in Algund and offers a rich variety of entertaining facilities and services.

It lies close to beautiful sight-seeing destinations reached by long bicycle paths going through the Val Venosta.
