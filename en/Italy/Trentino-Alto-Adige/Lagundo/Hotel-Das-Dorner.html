Description

Offering 3 pools and a large garden, family-run Hotel Das Doner in Lagundo, features a traditional restaurant, modern rooms, and free bikes.

A free AlgundCard is included. Rooms at the Hotel Das Dorner include free WiFi, satellite LCD TV, a balcony and a minibar.

The private bathroom comes with soft bathrobes plus slippers. Breakfast here includes yoghurt, cakes, and cold cuts.

The restaurant with terrace offers Tyrolean and Mediterranean cuisine, and the hotel has a wine cellar.

A cake buffet is provided every afternoon. The spa features an indoor pool with hydromassage corner, a gym, and 3 saunas.

Guests can unwind in the relax area equipped with water beds and a fireplace. Massages can also be reserved.

The AlgundCard offers free access to public transport in South Tyrol and to 80 museums.

Weekly hiking tours are organised by the hotel staff. The Merano 2000 cable car is 7.
