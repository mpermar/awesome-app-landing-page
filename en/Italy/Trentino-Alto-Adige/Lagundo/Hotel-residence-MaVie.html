Description

Situated in Lagundo, 600 metres from Algund-Vellau - Lagundo-Velloi Chairlift, Hotel residence MaVie features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a seasonal outdoor swimming pool and a bar.

The accommodation provides ski storage space, as well as a garden and barbecue facilities.

The accommodation offers room service, a shared lounge and currency exchange for guests.

At the hotel, the rooms include a wardrobe and a TV. Guests at Hotel residence MaVie can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

The accommodation offers an indoor pool. The area is popular for hiking and skiing, and bike hire is available at Hotel residence MaVie.
