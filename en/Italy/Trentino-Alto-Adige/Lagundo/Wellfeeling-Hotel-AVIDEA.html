Description

Located 3 km from Lagundo, Wellfeeling Hotel AVIDEA features panoramic views of Merano and the Texelgruppe Mountains.

It offers a spa with outdoor pool, and Alpine rooms with balcony and satellite TV.

Featuring free WiFi access, the rooms at the Hotel AVIDEA are spacious and fitted with natural wood furniture and cosy carpets.

A buffet breakfast is served on the terrace in the summer, and includes fresh fruit and local yogurt, together with cappuccino and herbal teas.

The nearest restaurant is 50 metres from the hotel. The hotel's pool and hot tub are surrounded by Alpine gardens, and guests can relax on the sun loungers in fine weather.

The wellness centre offers a sauna, Turkish bath, and Kneipp-path treatments. The bus stop in front of the hotel has services to Lagundo Station and the village centre.

The Claudia Agusta Cycle Path is one kilometre from the Wellfeeling Hotel AVIDEA.
