Description

All rooms in the farm stay are equipped with a flat-screen TV. The rooms come with a private bathroom. All guest rooms include a closet.

Continental and buffet breakfast options are available daily at Bio Agritur Le Millemele.

The accommodation offers a hammam. Guests at Bio Agritur Le Millemele will be able to enjoy activities in and around Spormaggiore, like skiing.

Bolzano is 24 miles from the farm stay, while Trento is 11 miles from the property.
