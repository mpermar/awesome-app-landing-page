Description

The Adler Family and Wellness ApartHotel is in Moena, 3 km from the Tre Valli ski lifts.

It has a wellness centre, free Wi-Fi, and modern alpine-style suites with balconies.

The suites at the Adler Hotel are decorated with natural wood panelling and floors.

Each comes with a sofa bed, satellite LCD TV and a bathroom with luxurious bathrobes.

A buffet breakfast is served daily, including coffee and herbal tea, with local cheese and mountain yogurt.

The AquaVitalis spa offers 2 pools, a Finnish sauna and Kneipp therapy. Sports lovers will appreciate the gym and ski storage.

There is also a miniclub open all day. The Paneveggio Pale di San Martino Natural Park is 19 km from the hotel.
