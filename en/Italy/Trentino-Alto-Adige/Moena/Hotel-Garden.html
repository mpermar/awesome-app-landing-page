Description

Hotel Garden is located 30 miles from Bolzano and just one mile from the Lusia Comprensiorio Tre Valli ski slopes. It offers an indoor pool, wellness center and free parking.

The hotel is well located in the Dolomites for outdoor activities all year round including hiking and skiing. The bus stop to the ski area is just 100 feet away.

Garden Hotel’s rooms are decorated in a traditional alpine style, some have a balcony. All are equipped with satellite TV and private bathrooms.

The glass-roofed indoor swimming pool offers views of the mountains, while the spa center has relaxing therapies such as massage, Turkish bath and sauna.

In Hotel Garden’s restaurant you will find typical regional dishes and special catering for vegetarians and those with specific dietary requirements. Guests can relax in the lounge area which has a wood-burning stove.
