Description

Nestled in the picturesque San Cassiano, the Störes features a restaurant, climbing wall, and a wellness area.

All spacious rooms come with a balcony, and free hot tub and sauna use. Wi-Fi is free.

All rooms at Hotel Störes - Mountain Nature Hotel provide flat-screen satellite TV and large windows.

Each has a private bathroom with soft bathrobes and amenities. Some rooms offer a minibar and parquet floors.

A varied breakfast buffet is available daily, and includes a selection of local cheeses and cold meats.

The restaurant serves both local and Mediterranean dishes. In the winter, the hotel provides ski storage and equipment hire.

The Piz Sorega slopes are just 120 metres away, and form part of the Dolomiti SuperSki zone.

The nearest train station is at Brunico, 35 km to the north. The hotel can arrange transport to both towns on request.
