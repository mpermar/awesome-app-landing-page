Description

Offering indoor and outdoor pools and a whole variety of leisure facilities, Family Hotel Posta is in the center of Santa Cristina Di Val Gardena. It is 150 feet from the Monte Pana ski elevator.

Out in the 9843 ft² garden you will find a children's playground, sun loungers, a bowling green and much more. You can treat yourself to 1 of the 3 saunas, a hot tub or relaxing massage.

Rooms come with free Wi-Fi and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. They offer views of the village or mountains.

As the name suggests, Posta Family Hotel is especially great for families and features 5 games rooms and lots of facilities and entertainment for children. A partner ski school comes to pick up the children for lessons directly from the hotel.

Homemade cakes, fruit salad and muesli are part of the buffet breakfast each morning. Drinks are available from the bar and the restaurant offers 4 and 5-course meals. You have a choice of regional, Mediterranean and international dishes.

Don't forget to ask staff about the many trips they organize, which include hiking tours and mountain climbing.
