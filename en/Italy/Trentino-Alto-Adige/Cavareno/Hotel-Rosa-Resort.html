Description

Hotel Rosa Resort has an indoor pool and wellness facilities, along with mountain-style rooms with light-wood furniture.

It is in Cavareno, offering panoramic views of the Brenta Group mountain range. Rosa Resort’s rooms come with wood or carpeted floors and a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

All rooms feature a balcony overlooking the surrounding mountain landscape. The resort’s restaurant has both set and à la carte menus of Italian cuisine and Trentino specialities.

Guests can rent bikes for free or just relax in the wellness area, complete with a sauna, spa centre and Turkish bath.

Offering free parking, Hotel Rosa Resort is less than a 5-minute drive from the SS42 state road, which links to Bolzano in 40 minutes.
