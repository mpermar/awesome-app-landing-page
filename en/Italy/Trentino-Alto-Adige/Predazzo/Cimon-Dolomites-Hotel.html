Description

Surrounded by the Dolomites, the family-run Cimon Dolomites Hotel offers rooms with a mountain-view balcony.

Located in the Val di Fiemme valley, it features a wellness area, free bike rental and free Wi-Fi in public areas.

Newly renovated in 2012, this 3-star hotel has spacious rooms with wooden furniture and an LCD TV with satellite channels.

A hairdryer and toiletries are provided in each private bathroom. International and Trentino cuisines are part of the restaurant’s specialities.

A varied breakfast is served each morning and includes homemade cakes and cereal, as well as some savoury foods.

The wellness centre comes with a Turkish bath, infrared and sanarium saunas and a relaxation area.

For relaxing outdoors, the property’s equipped garden is ideal for taking in the sun.

Situated in Predazzo, Cimon Hotel is a 15-minute drive from the towns of Moena and Cavalese.

During winter, a free public ski bus stops 50 metres away and takes you to the Latemar ski slopes, 2 km away.
