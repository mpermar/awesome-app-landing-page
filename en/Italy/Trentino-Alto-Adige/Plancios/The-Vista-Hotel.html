Description

A 30-minute drive from Brixen, the Vista offers panoramic views of the Dolomites.

It features an indoor pool and luxury wellness centre, 24 km from Val de Funes valley.

Rooms at The Vista Hotel are light and spacious, with wood panels and carpeted floors.

Each room includes a TV and private bathroom. Most rooms come with a balcony. An extensive breakfast buffet is available daily, including local cheeses and cold meats.

The restaurant serves Tyrolean dishes and national specialities. At the spa, guests have free use of all facilities including the sauna, hot tub and Turkish baths.

Massages and beauty treatments can be booked for an additional charge. In the winter, the Vista provides free heated ski storage and boot warmers.
