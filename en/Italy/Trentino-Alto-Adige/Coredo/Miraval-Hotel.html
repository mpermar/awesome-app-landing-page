Description

Admire the view across the valley and the Dolomites from your private balcony at Miraval Hotel.

Relax in the wellness centre, which includes a gym, swimming pool, and sauna. This cosy hotel features 39 guest rooms, each with satellite TV and a balcony.

The hotel has a large garden where you can sit back and enjoy the peace and quiet.

A self-catering studio is also available; giving you complete independence. If you are looking to really pamper yourself then the wellness center is ideal.

You will find a hot tub, Turkish bath, and a swimming pool. Your day at the Miraval Hotel begins with a generous breakfast buffet.

The traditional restaurant serves a daily set menu of regional and international dishes.
