Description

With a panoramic sunny location 3 km from Moso in Passiria, this family-run hotel offers a wellness centre, restaurant and sun terrace.

All rooms and apartments come with mountain-view balcony. Rooms at the Hotel Kronhof have modern wooden furnishings and an LCD satellite TV.

Apartments include a kitchenette with a fridge and stove. Each balcony is furnished with a table and chairs.

Breakfast at the Kronhof is a buffet with cold meats, cheeses and freshly prepared eggs.

The restaurant serves dishes from South Tyrol as well as classic Italian dishes. At the spa, you can relax in the indoor pool, hot tub and Finnish sauna.

The hotel organizes weekly hiking trips, and electric bikes can be rented on site.

The hotel also has charging stations for electric cars. Set at an altitude of 1315 metres, the hotel is 30 km Merano and a 1-hour drive from Bolzano.
