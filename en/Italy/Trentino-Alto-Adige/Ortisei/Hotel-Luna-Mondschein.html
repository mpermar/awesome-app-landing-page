Description

You will be near mountain trails at Hotel Luna Mondschein. The hotel can organize excursions with Catores mountain guides during summer, and sled tours during winter.

Public buses stop directly in front of the Hotel Mondschein. A private shuttle service to and from the ski tracks is available from 8:30am to 5:30pm.

Relax in the elegant, comfortable lounge area decorated in a classical style. The large garden and the terrace are equipped with deck chairs and garden furniture.

Spacious rooms come with classical furnishings and offer a warm environment. Enjoy a welcome drink on arrival every Saturday and Sunday. The breakfast buffet includes a rich variety of foods and is included in the rate.

The on-site restaurant is only open to hotel guests and serves regional and Mediterranean cuisine, local specialties and fine wines. Themed dinners are held once a week. Gluten-free menus are available on request.

Starting at 4pm you can help yourself to the hotel's afternoon buffet, complete with freshly baked cakes and cookies.

Take advantage of the spa facilities.You will find an indoor pool with water jets and hydromassage features, a Finnish sauna, a Turkish bath and a Thermarium.

Take a break in the relaxation area where you can enjoy herbal infusions and fresh fruit every day after 6pm. Treat yourself to the hotel's beauty and wellness center. Bathrobe and slippers are provided.

A private fitness room is available every day from 9am to 9pm. You also have have free use of the tennis facilities in the nearby St. Ulrich Tennis Centre.

During certain times of the year, the hotel may only be bookable for at least 7 nights, with arrival on Saturday or Sunday.
