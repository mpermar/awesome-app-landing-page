Description

With panoramic views of Val Gardena, Hotel Albion Mountain Spa Resort is a 10-minute drive from both Kastelruth and St.

Ulrich. The free wellness facilities include an indoor pool and a panoramic heated outdoor pool.

Parking is free. All rooms come with free WiFi and a private balcony. They also feature a satellite TV and a minibar.

At Hotel Albion all accommodation is decorated in a traditional mountain style, with wooden or carpeted floors and elegant furniture.

The spa is complete with a panoramic sauna, Kneipp and Turkish baths. Outdoor and indoor hot tubs are also available.

Massages and beauty treatments can be booked. Children can enjoy an extra-large play room with computer games and slide, as well as an outdoor playground.

The restaurant serves specialities of South Tyrol. Breakfast is a varied buffet. A free public ski bus stops directly outside the hotel.
