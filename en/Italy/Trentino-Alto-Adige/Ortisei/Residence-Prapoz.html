Description

Offering a peaceful location on the slopes outside Ortisei, these self-catering apartments come with a dishwasher, microwave and free parking.

The town centre is a 10-minute walk away. Apartments at Residence Prapoz have wooden furnishings and carpeted or parquet floors.

Each includes an LCD TV and a bathroom with hairdryer. All have views of the garden and mountains.

Guests have free entry to Ortisei public swimming pool, 800 metres away. The Prapoz provides an on-site sauna and a sun terrace with loungers, tables and chairs.

In the winter, the residence offers ski storage. The Seceda and Alpe di Siusi Ski Slopes are just 800 metres away.
