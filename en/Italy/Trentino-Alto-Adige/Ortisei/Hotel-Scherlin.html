Description

Offering a panoramic valley location 5 km from the centre of Ortisei, Hotel Scherlin features a wellness centre with indoor and outdoor pools.

All Alpine-style rooms come with an LCD satellite TV and a balcony. At the spa, you can relax in the hot tub, sauna and Turkish bath.

The Scherlin's fitness centre is open 24 hours a day. Rooms and suites have carpeted floors and a fully equipped private bathroom.

Each balcony is furnished with a small table and 2 chairs. A rich breakfast buffet is provided daily, with eggs, fresh fruit and freshly baked croissants.

The restaurant serves specialities from South Tyrol as well as classic Italian cuisine.

Buses to the centre of Ortisei stop in front of the property. The hotel is 12 km from Selva di Val Gardena and a 40-minute drive from Bolzano.
