Description

Set in the wine town of Termeno, Hotel Traminerhof is surrounded by vineyards and features a 600 m² garden with swimming pool, children's playground and sun loungers.

It also has a free wellness centre. Rooms have a modern design with parquet floors and LCD TVs with satellite channels.

Each room features a balcony overlooking the garden or vineyards. Breakfast is a sweet and savoury buffet including homemade cakes, eggs and fresh fruit.

The stylish restaurant is open in summer, and serves international dishes and South Tyrol specialities at lunch and dinner.

Drinks are available at the new Bar62 bar, also featuring an al-fresco area. The Traminerhof Hotel has a free indoor pool, sauna and steam bath.

It provides free parking, free wired internet in rooms and free Wi-Fi in public areas.

The hotel is well connected by the Autostrada del Brennero motorway and is 30 minutes' drive from Petersberg Golf Club.
