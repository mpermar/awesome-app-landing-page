Description

At Hotel Mühle Mayer, rooms come with a balcony. With a private bathroom equipped with free toiletries, rooms at Hotel Mühle Mayer also have free WiFi, while certain rooms will provide you with a patio. All rooms include a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

A continental or buffet breakfast is served at the property.

Spa and wellness facilities including a sauna and a hammam are at guests' disposal during their stay at Hotel Mühle Mayer. Cycling and hiking are among the activities that guests of Hotel Mühle Mayer can find nearby.

Merano is 23 miles from Hotel Mühle Mayer.
