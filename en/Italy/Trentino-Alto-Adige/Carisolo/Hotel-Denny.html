Description

Offering a spa and wellness centre, and a free gym, Hotel Denny has a restaurant serving Trentino and Italian cuisine.

It is in the mountain town of Carisolo, a 10-minute drive from Madonna di Campiglio.

Wi-Fi is free throughout. Rooms are fitted with wooden furniture and parquet floors.

Each one has a balcony and an LCD satellite TV. Some rooms overlook the surrounding mountains.

The hotel features a ground-floor terrace equipped with tables and chairs, ideal for relaxing.

In the morning, a varied breakfast buffet is available and includes fresh croissant, homemade cakes and savoury products.

Guests can rent bikes for free at reception, and during both winter and summer guided excursions can be organised.

Discounts apply at a wellness centre and a golf club located 2 km away. Denny Hotel offers a free shuttle to the Pinzolo ski area, 500 metres away.
