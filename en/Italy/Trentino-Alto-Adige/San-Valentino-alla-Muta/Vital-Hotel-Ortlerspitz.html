Description

Set between Muta and Resia lake and just outside San Valentino alla Muta, 3-star Vital Hotel Ortlerspitz is a family-run property with restaurant, spa and ultra-modern pool.

It features modern traditional rooms and free car park. Rooms at the Vital Ortlerspitz come with South Tyrol décor, carpeted floors and a private bathroom.

Reached by a lift, they include an LCD satellite TV. Most of them have a balcony facing Lake Muta or the mountains.

Wi-Fi connection is provided at additional costs. During summer the buffet breakfast can be enjoyed on the terrace and includes eggs dishes and sweet and savoury produce.

Lunch and dinner are served in the restaurant, which specialises in national and traditional recipes.

Guests can take a dip in the pool or relax in the spa boasting a steam bath and infrared and Finnish saunas.

The 2 relaxation rooms have a direct access from the garden equipped with sauna and hot tub.

Massages are at extra costs. Nintendo Wii and videogames are available in the games room.

At reception bikes and ski equipment can be rented. The property is 100 metres Malga di San Valentino ski slopes.
