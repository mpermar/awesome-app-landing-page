Description

With a sunny location in Velturno, the 4-star Taubers overlooks Isarco Valley. Its Castanea wellness centre includes indoor and outdoor swimming pools.

The restaurant serves local cuisine. Taubers Unterwirt Aktiv & Vitalhotel offers bright and large accommodation, tastefully furnished with chestnut wood and natural stone.

All rooms and suites feature a private balcony. The wellness centre is entirely made from wood from the valley, and features a sauna and a hot tub.

Beauty treatments using chestnuts, herbs, and other ingredients from the area, are available.

The restaurant hosts a number of culinary weeks based on different themes. The highest quality food is made using local ingredients.

Breakfast is a generous buffet. The Taubers Vitalhotel has a complimentary bike and excursion equipment rental service.

It is also part of the Alto Adige excursion network and 4 guided tours are included in the rate of your stay.
