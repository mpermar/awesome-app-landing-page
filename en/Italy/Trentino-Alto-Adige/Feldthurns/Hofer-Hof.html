Description

Surrounded by vineyards, Hofer Hof offers Tyrolean-style apartments with balcony and mountain views in the countryside around Velturno.

The garden features a BBQ, playground, and sun loungers. With light wood furnishings, apartments at the Hofer include a fully equipped kitchen, a living/dining area with sofa, and satellite TV.

The private bathroom has a hairdryer and shower. On request, breakfast is available and can be served on the terrace.

It consists of homemade ingredients, cheese, fruit juices, ham, yoghurt, muesli, warm and cold drinks.

Wine tasting is also arranged in the property's on-site cellar. Bressanone is 7 km from the property, while the Plose ski slopes are reachable in 20 minutes by car.

Guests receive a Brixencard on arrival, with discounts on public transport and the Acqua Arena.
