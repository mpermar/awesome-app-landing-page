Description

Just 1 km from the centre of Terento, family-run Hotel Dolomitenblick offers an indoor pool, Finnish sauna and steam bath, all free of charge.

A free shuttle to Brunico Train Station is provided. The spacious en suite rooms have traditional or modern décor, with dark or light wood furniture and a mountain-view balcony or terrace.

Each includes an LCD satellite TV, sofa and parquet floors. You can have dinner at the restaurant, which specialises in Mediterranean and Tyrolean cuisine.

A buffet breakfast of egg dishes, cold cuts but also homeamade cakes and fruit salad is served daily.

In summer, BBQ evenings are organised once a week in the garden, which is equipped with sun loungers and parasols.

The Plan de Corones ski area is 18 km away, and is reached by a public bus stopping opposite the property.
