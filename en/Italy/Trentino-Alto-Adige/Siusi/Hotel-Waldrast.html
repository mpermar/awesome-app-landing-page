Description

Set in Siusi, at an altitude of 1000 metres, Hotel Waldrast offers Alpine-style rooms, just 300 metres from the Alpe di Siusi cable car.

It features a furnished garden, an outdoor pool, and a spa. Lobby Wi-Fi is free. Each with a balcony, rooms at the family-run Waldrast Hotel come with carpeted or parquet floors, and an LCD satellite TV.

Some feature wood-beamed ceilings, whilst the private bathroom has free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Breakfast is a sweet and savoury buffet, including cold cuts, cheeses, and homemeade cakes.

The hotel restaurant with a terrace serves both Tyrolean and classic Italian cuisine, and special menus can be requested.

After a day on the slopes, guests can relax in the hot tub and sauna, or enjoy one of the hiking tours arranged.

Discounts are provided at the Castelrotto Golf Course, 8 minutes’ drive away. A bus stop with links to Bolzano and Bressanone stops 200 metres from the hotel.
