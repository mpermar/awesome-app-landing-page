Description

Guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. All rooms include a private bathroom and a bidet, and some rooms also offer a terrace. All rooms have a desk.

Guests at Vitalpina Wanderhotel Europa can enjoy a continental or a buffet breakfast.

The accommodation offers 4-star accommodations with a hot tub, sauna and hammam. Skiing, cycling and hiking are among the activities that guests of Vitalpina Wanderhotel Europa can find nearby.
