Description

Set on the edge of Schlern-Rosengarten Nature Park, Hotel Vigilerhof is at an altitude of 900 metres and offers panoramic mountain views.

It offers a free wellness centre and summer swimming pool with sun loungers. All rooms have an elegant Alpine design and views of the mountains or countryside.

Each room has an LCD TV and free toiletries, bathrobes and slippers. Breakfast is served outdoors in warm weather.

It features sweet and savoury food such as cold meats, cheese, bacon and cakes. The Vigilerhof Hotel has a restaurant serving Italian cuisine and specialities from South Tyrol.

This family-run hotel has lots of relaxing wellness facilities including a Turkish bath, saunas, hot tub and aromatherapy showers.

You can relax in the reading lounge with a fireplace and collection of German and Italian books.

Guest have discounts at Castelrotto Alpe Di Siusi Golf Club 1 km away. Siusi is 2 km away, while the Alpe di Siusi ski slopes are 1.

5 km away. The public ski bus is free for those with a ski pass and other buses for Bolzano, Merano and Bressanone stop 30 metres away.
