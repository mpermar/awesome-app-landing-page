Description

All rooms have a private bathroom and a patio or a balcony. Some rooms include extra space.

At the restaurant, you can enjoy a mix of delicious food from Italy and South Tyrol. Breakfast is served buffet-style.

The Magdalener Hof Hotel is easily reached from the A22 Highway. It is a short drive to the center and Bolzano Train Station.

One older child or adult is charged EUR 35 per person per night in an extra bed.
The maximum number of extra beds/cribs in a room is 1.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
