Description

Set in a quiet area 500 metres from the centre of Pietramurata, Camping Daino offers self-catering accommodation in mobile homes.

It also includes a swimming pool and a garden. Mobile homes at Camping Daino all come with a fully equipped kitchenette and furnished seating area.

They feature a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with shower, hairdryer and toiletries.

Restaurants and pizzerias are a stroll away from the Daino. Guests can relax in the swimming pool or in the large garden with a children’s playground.

Free BBQ facilities are available on site. Situated 1. 5 km from Becco dell’Aquila parachuting centre, the camping is a 20-minute drive to Lake Garda.

A bus leading to Arco, Riva del Garda and Trento stops right in front of the property.
