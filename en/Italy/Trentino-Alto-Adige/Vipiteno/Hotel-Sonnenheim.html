Description

Hotel Sonnenheim is situated on a hillside location, a 10-minute walk from the centre of Vipiteno and a 5-minute drive from the Monte Cavallo ski lift.

It offers free Wi-Fi in public areas. The simple rooms in Alpine style offer mountain views, satellite TV, and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

Some rooms also have a balcony. A sweet and savoury breakfast buffet is available in the morning.

The Sonnenheim’s restaurant includes a snack bar and serves Tyrolean cuisine. Guests have access to a ski storage room and a furnished garden with a playground for children.

A small spa area with a sauna, an infrared cabin and a relaxation room is also available.

Vipiteno train station is 600 metres from Sonnenheim Hotel, and Bressanone is 30 km away.
