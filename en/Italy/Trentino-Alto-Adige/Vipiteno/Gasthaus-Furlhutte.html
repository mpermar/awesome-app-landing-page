Description

Situated on a mountain close to Vipiteno, Gasthaus Furlhütte provides ski-to-door access in the middle of Monte Cavallo Ski Area.

The property has a bar, as well as a restaurant serving Austrian cuisine. Guestrooms provide free WiFi.

Every room features a bathroom with shower and toilet and guests can enjoy mountain views.

Access to Gasthaus Furlhütte is through cable car only. Free luggage service upon request is provided by the property owner.

Guests at Gasthaus Furlhütte can enjoy a buffet breakfast. The accommodation offers a children's playground.

A selection of activities can be enjoyed in the surroundings, including skiing, cycling and hiking.
