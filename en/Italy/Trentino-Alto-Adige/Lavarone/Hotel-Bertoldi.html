Description

Albergo Bertoldi is 1 km from the Lavarone ski slopes, connected by free public ski bus.

It features a free 70 m² wellness centre, free bike rental and a restaurant serving South Tyrol specialities.

Rooms have a classic design with wooden furniture and views of Lavarone or the surrounding mountains.

Each one includes free Wi-Fi and an LCD TV. Breakfast at the Bertoldi Hotel is buffet style.

The restaurant is open at lunch and dinner and serves Italian and international dishes, as well as local recipes.

The wellness centre, open afternoons, features a sauna, Turkish bath and hot tub.

You will also find a free ski storage room and free car park on site. The hotel is near a children's entertainment park, ski rental shop and ski school.

Upon arrival, you will receive the Trentino Guest Card, which provides discounts to castles, museums and attractions in the area.
