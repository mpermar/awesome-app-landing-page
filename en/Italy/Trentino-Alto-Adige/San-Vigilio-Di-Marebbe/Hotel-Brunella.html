Description

Hotel Brunella is located in the mountain town of Marebbe, only 500 metres from the Miara ski slopes.

It offers free parking, free Wi-Fi in public areas, and a restaurant serving Italian cuisine.

Overlooking the mountains, rooms at the Brunella are fitted with wooden furniture and have either carpeted or wooden floors.

Facilities here include an LCD TV and a private bathroom. All rooms come with a balcony.

A ski bus stops right opposite the hotel and links with the ski slopes. Brunico is a 20-minute drive away.
