Description

Garni Diamant is an Alpine-style hotel in San Vigilio di Marebbe, just 500 metres from the Plan de Corones ski lifts and 2 km from the Fanes Natural Park.

It offers mountain-view accommodation, a spa, and childrenâ€™s playground. Lobby Wi-Fi is free.

The wooden-furnished rooms and apartments at the Diamant come with a balcony, a satellite LCD TV, and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

The spacious apartments also feature a dining/living area with a kitchenette. A sweet and savoury buffet breakfast is provided daily, whilst the area offers several restaurants serving both Tyrolean and classic Italian dishes.

BBQ facilities are also available at the hotel. After a day on the slopes, guests can relax in the furnished garden or at the wellness centre, which offers a hot tub, a sauna, and a steambath.
