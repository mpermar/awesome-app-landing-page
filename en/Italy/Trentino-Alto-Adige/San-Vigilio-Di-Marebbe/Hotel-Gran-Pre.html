Description

Only 500 metres from the centre of San Vigilio di Marebbe, Hotel Gran Pre offers a restaurant, a sauna and indoor pool, both for free.

The traditional rooms overlook the surrounding mountains. WiFi is free throughout.

Breakfast consists of a sweet and savoury buffet, and Tyrolean specialities from the menu can be sampled in the restaurant.

BBQ evenings can be organised in summer, weather permitting. The spacious rooms have a satellite flat-screen TV, and a bathroom with hairdryer.

Some have extra space, and are fitted with light wood floors. Guests can relax on the shared terrace after a session in the hot tub, bookable at reception.

The Gran Pre lies 600 metres from the Transbrenta ski lift. A free ski bus stopping 50 metres away, takes you to the Plan de Corones ski area.
