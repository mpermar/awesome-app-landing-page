Description

Once the summer residence of the Austrian Royal Family, the 4-star Grand Hotel Imperial is set in the centre of Levico Terme, surrounded by a park of over 150000 m².

It features a free wellness centre and elegant rooms. The on-site Principessa Sissi spa and wellness centre is complete with indoor swimming pool, sauna, Turkish bath and fitness centre.

The spa provides both beauty and health treatments. The outdoor swimming pool is available during summer.

All rooms offer an LCD TV and a mini bar. Free WiFi is available in the lobby. A varied breakfast buffet is served daily.

The Grand Hotel Imperial offers free garage parking, 100 metres away. The property is 1 km from Levico Terme Train Station and 1.
