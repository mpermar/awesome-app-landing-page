Description

Offering an organic restaurant and rooms with satellite TV, Bio Hotel Elite is set in Levico Terme, in the Valsugana Valley.

It features a sun terrace. Spacious rooms at the Elite include a private bathroom with free toiletries plus a hairdryer.

Some rooms have a balcony and wood-beamed ceilings. The family-run Art d'Elite restaurant serves Trentino specialities, made with local, organic ingredients.

Guests can relax in the large, furnished garden, while children can enjoy the playground.
