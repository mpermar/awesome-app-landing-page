Description

Newly renovated in 2018, a 5-minute drive from the centre of Sterzing, Naturhotel Haller offers an indoor pool, an outdoor pool, and a restaurant.

A wellness centre is also available. The rooms feature an Alpine-style décor, partially wood panelled walls and a flat-screen satellite TV.

They also include a fully equipped private bathroom. Most rooms boast a balcony. All rooms on all floors can be reached via lift.

Breakfast is a buffet with sweet and savoury produce such as homemade preserves. Creative South Tyrol cuisine, along with gourmet desserts, hors d’oeuvre and vegetables can be sampled at the Naturhotel Haller's restaurant.

During summer, you can swim in the pool or just relax in the free octagonal hot tub, sauna and steam bath.

Wi-Fi is available in the whole property. Free heated ski deposit can be found on site while a public ski bus will take you to the Racines slopes, about 8 km away.
