Description

The Berghotel is located in Ratschings, 300 metres from the closest lift of the Ratschings-Jaufen ski area.

It features an indoor pool, free bicycles, and a wellness area. The Alpine-style rooms come with wooden furnishings and include a balcony, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The Berghotel’s restaurant serves a hearty sweet and savoury buffet breakfast. Half board comprises a 6-course dinner menu, featuring traditional South Tyrolean cuisine.

The restaurant also offers a well-stocked wine cellar. Two saunas, a pool, hot tub, and Turkish bath invite to relax.

Massages can be booked on request, and guests also have access to a fitness room, a sun terrace, and a games room.

Sterzing can be reached in 20 minutes by car, and Gossensaß is 20 km away. The Vipiteno Exit of the A22 Autostrada del Brennero motorway is 10 km from the Berghotel.
