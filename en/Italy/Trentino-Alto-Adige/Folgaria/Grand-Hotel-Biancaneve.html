Description

Offering an indoor pool and a spa and wellness centre, Grand Hotel Biancaneve is located in Costa, 300 metres from the ski lifts.

It features free WiFi throughout, and heated ski storage. Each room comes with a flat-screen TV, a balcony and a minibar.

Towels and a hairdryer are included in the private bathroom. At Grand Hotel Biancaneve you will find a restaurant.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including skiing, golfing and cycling, and tennis courts are also available nearby.

Folgaria centre is 1. 5 km from the property. Rovereto Train Station is a 30-minute drive away, while you can reach Verona Airport in about 1 hour by car.
