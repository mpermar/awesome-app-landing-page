Description

The Village features a spa center with indoor swimming pool, sauna and Turkish bath. Massages can be booked.

Rooms at the Muu are spacious and all come with private balcony, LCD TV with satellite channels, and free Wi-Fi. Breakfast is a varied buffet of local specialties.

The restaurant is open for both lunch and dinner.
