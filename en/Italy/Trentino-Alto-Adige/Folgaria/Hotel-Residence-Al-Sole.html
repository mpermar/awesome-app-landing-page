Description

Hotel Al Sole offers elegant rooms with wood furniture and a balcony. Surrounded by the Alps, it is 5 minutes' drive from Folgaria centre.

Rooms at the family-run Hotel Al Sole have either carpeted or wood floors and come with a TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and toiletries.

Some rooms also feature characteristic wood ceilings. Guests can relax in the hotel’s stylish common spaces.

Every morning, a varied breakfast buffet is served in the dining room. Staff can book ski and Nordic walking lessons, along with trips around Folgaria.

Offering free parking, Al Sole is 15 km from the A22 motorway and a 30-minute drive from Trento.

Folgaria is easily reached by a free ski bus. A free shuttle to the ski slopes is provided.
