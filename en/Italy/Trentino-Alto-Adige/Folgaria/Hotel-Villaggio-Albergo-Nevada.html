Description

One of our best sellers in Folgaria! Villaggio Albergo Nevada is in the Frazione Costa area of Folgaria, in the Trentino region. Rooms come with warm parquet floor, private bathroom with bath or shower, and satellite TV. Parking is free.

Albergo Nevada is near the start of many walk paths, and just 1,300 feet from the ski elevators. The multilingual staff can provide info on local attractions.

A free wellness center with gym and heated indoor pool is available in high season, when babysitting and entertainment activities are also provided.
