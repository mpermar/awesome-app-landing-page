Description

With an à la carte restaurant, sun terrace and free WiFi throughout, Hotel Hubertusstube offers Alpine-style rooms with wooden furnishings.

The property is located a 5-minute drive from the centre of Laion. Rooms at the Hubertusstube Hotel feature a balcony or terrace, satellite flat-screen TV and parquet floors.

The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries. Some have garden and mountain views.

A buffet including cold cuts, cheeses and jams is provided daily for breakfast. The restaurant specialises in South Tyrolean cuisine.

Facilities include free on-site parking, a ski deposit with a heated boot storage, and sun loungers in the garden.

The property is home to chickens and rabbits. The hotel is 10 km from the Ortisei ski area reachable by the Ski Express bus, which stops in front of the property.
