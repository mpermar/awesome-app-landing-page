Description

Olympic Hotel is in the picturesque village of Fiemme Castle, part of the Val di Fiemme ski area.

It offers afternoon tea, a free spa with hot tub, and a shared terrace. The Olimpionico is family run.

Rooms come with free WiFi, satellite TV and some offer views of the Lagorai Mountains.

Your day at the Olimpionico begins with a large buffet breakfast, including a selection of organic food.

The restaurant serves South Tyrol specialties. Lots of cosy common areas including a tavern, bar, TV lounge, and meeting room are also available.

The spa offers a sauna, Turkish bath, and a range of massages and beauty treatments.

Guests enjoy a free Fiemme Card, including some leisure activities, public transport and access to museums and nature parks.

Please contact the property for more details, as included services vary according to seasonality.
