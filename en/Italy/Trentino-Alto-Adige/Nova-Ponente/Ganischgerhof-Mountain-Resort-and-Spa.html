Description

Ganischgerhof Mountain Resort & Spa is set in the town of Deutschnofen in the heart of the Dolomites. Buses stop right outside and provide access to Bozen in 20 minutes and Latemar ski center.

All rooms offer satellite TV, bathrobe and slippers. Free Wi-Fi is available in common areas and parking is also free.

The large spa area includes a sauna, Turkish bath, 6 beauty treatment rooms, and a modern fitness center with a fantastic view of the green landscape.

The restaurant serves regional specialties including homemade pastas and desserts. Barbecues take place on the grounds during summer.

Guests here have discounts at Petersberg Golf Club, a 10-minute drive away.
