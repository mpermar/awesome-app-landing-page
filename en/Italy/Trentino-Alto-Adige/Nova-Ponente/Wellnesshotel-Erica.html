Description

The rooms at the Erica Wellnesshotel are large and decorated with natural-wood furniture. Each room includes satellite TV, and comfortable bathrobes and slippers are also provided. Some rooms offer a balcony with mountain views.

A continental buffet breakfast is served every morning and the Erica Hotel’s restaurant specializes in Tyrolean and Mediterranean cooking. Guests can enjoy drinks in the bar or on the sun terrace.

The spa center offers saunas, massages and a gym. Beauty treatments are also available.

The Ochsenweide cable car and the Obereggen ski slopes are 6 miles from the Erica Wellness Hotel and can be reached by bus.
