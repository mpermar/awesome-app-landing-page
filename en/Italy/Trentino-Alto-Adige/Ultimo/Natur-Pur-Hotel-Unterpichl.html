Description

Located between the town of Santa Valpurga and Lake Zòccolo, Natur Pur Hotel Unterpichl offers a wellness centre and organic restaurant.

You have ski bus links to the Schwemmalm slopes 5 km away. Rooms offer furnished balconies overlooking the mountains or the street.

Each one has a TV with satellite channels. The breakfast buffet includes homemade organic bread, fresh fruit, eggs and much more.

On Sunday, you can enjoy a glass of Prosecco with your breakfast. Homemade organic pasta is available for lunch and dinner.

All meals are light and healthy. Unterpichl Natur Pur Hotel offers free wellness facilities such as a Finnish sauna, steam bath and Kneipp hydrotherapy.

There is also a terrace where you can enjoy an aperitif and a garden with ping pong, slide and garden chess.

This family-run hotel organises 3 hiking trips a week, plus ski lessons during winter.

Buses to Merano stop right outside. Guests will receive a free UltentalCard, which gives free access to public museums and swimming pools.
