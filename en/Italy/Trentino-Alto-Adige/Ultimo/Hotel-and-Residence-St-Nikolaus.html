Description

Hotel & Residence St. Nikolaus is in the picturesque village of San Nicolò in Val d'Ultimo.

It features a 1000 m² garden with children's playground and a restaurant that uses natural ingredients.

Rooms here are elegant and spacious with wooden furniture and wood floors. They all come with a TV, free WiFi and a private bathroom with toiletries.

A breakfast buffet is available daily, including fresh bread from the organic bakery, juices, jams, teas and home-made cakes.

Other various locally-sourced items are also available. You will find indoor and outdoor play areas, plus a variety of board games and facilities for children.

Guests can also relax in the wellness area, with a panoramic sauna and relaxation area.

St. Nikolaus hotel is 30 km from Merano. It is a 2-minute drive by free ski bus from the Schwemmalm ski area and snow shoes are free to rent.

During summer, you have over 700 km of trekking paths within Stelvio National Park.
