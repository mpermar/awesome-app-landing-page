Description

All rooms come equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, and parquet or carpeted floors. Some rooms are complete with a balcony.

The new Millelire restaurant overlooks the swimming pool and is open in summer. The chef prepares international dishes and specialties from Trentino. Breakfast is a sweet and savory buffet, with hot and cold dishes.

The interiors of the Luise combine art, design and technology with a vintage atmosphere. Free bicycle rentals are available at the front desk and the garden has a playground.
