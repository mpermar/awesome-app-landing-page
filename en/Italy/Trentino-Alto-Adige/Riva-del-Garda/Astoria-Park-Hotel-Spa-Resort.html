Description

Surrounded by a 15,000 m² park at 1 km from Lake Garda, Astoria Hotel features both outdoor and indoor swimming pools.

Rooms offer free Wi-Fi and satellite TV. Located in different buildings around the garden, rooms have contemporary-style décor and wooden floors.

The bathroom provides hairdryer, bathrobe and slippers. The wellness centre includes the indoor pool, a sauna and steam bath, at an extra cost.

A range of massages and beauty treatments are available on request. Astoria Park Hotel features a mix of modern design and minimalist décor.

Its circular hall has large glass windows overlooking the garden. Here, drinks from the bar can be enjoyed on the comfortable sofas.

The property is set on Via Trento, a road leading to Riva del Garda’s city centre, 500 metres away.
