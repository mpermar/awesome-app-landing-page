Description

Immersed in the greenery, Hotel Campagnola features a spa and offers a garden with swimming pool.

It is 2. 5 km from Lake Garda and from Riva del Garda city centre. Parking is free.

Rooms are air conditioned and come with free Wi-Fi and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Breakfast is buffet style. The restaurant of Campagnola Hotel serves Italian cuisine and specialities from Trentino.

This eco-friendly hotel is committed to protecting the environment and offers recycling facilities.

The spa is complete with hot tub, Turkish bath, sauna and relaxation area. A 5000-m² children's playground is also available on site.
