Description

Rooms at Al Maso are well-kept and come with satellite TV, and air conditioning.

You can enjoy a sauna, a Turkish bath or a Kneipp treatment at the wellness center. Chromotherapy showers are also available.

Al Maso is less than 5 minutes' drive from the Varone Waterfalls.
