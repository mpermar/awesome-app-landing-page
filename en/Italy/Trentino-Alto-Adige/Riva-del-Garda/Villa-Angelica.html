Description

Surrounded by a large garden with a shared outdoor pool, Villa Angelica offers apartments in a historic 14th century villa in Riva, a 4-minute drive from the shores of Lake Garda.

It features free Wi-Fi. All apartments boasts different design and offer an equipped kitchen and a living room with a sofa and a satellite flat-screen TV.

Guests have access to barbecue facilities. Restaurants, bars, and grocery shops are within 900 metres of the Angelica.
