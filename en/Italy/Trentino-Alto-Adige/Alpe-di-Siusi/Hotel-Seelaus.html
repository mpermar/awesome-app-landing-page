Description

Set on the Seiser Alm ski slopes and hiking trails, Hotel Seelaus offers elegant, Alpine-style rooms with a balcony in Alpe di Siusi.

It features a free gym, a restaurant, and a spa with indoor pool. Spacious rooms at the family-run Seelaus come with a flat-screen satellite TV, and some have wooden floors.

The private bathroom offers a bath or shower. Breakfast here is a sweet and savoury buffet with homemade cakes, cold cuts, and cheeses.

The restaurant serves Tyrolean dishes and classic Italian cuisine. Gluten-free products are also available.

After a day on the slopes, guests can relax in the sauna, steam bath, and hot tub.

Children can enjoy the hotel’s playground in the furnished garden, which features a trampoline.

Kastelruth is 15 km from the hotel, and Seis is a 17-minute drive away. Bolzano, the regional capital, is 34 km from the property.
