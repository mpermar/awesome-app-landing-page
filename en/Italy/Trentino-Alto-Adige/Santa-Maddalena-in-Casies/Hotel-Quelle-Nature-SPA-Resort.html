Description

Nestled in Valle di Casies valley, this hotel features a 3000-m² luxury wellness centre, 7 pools and an outdoor hot tub overlooking the Dolomites.

All spacious rooms come with LCD satellite TV and a balcony. Parking and Wi-Fi are free.

A 5000-m² garden with a small lake surrounds the Hotel Quelle Nature SPA Resort, which includes 10 different saunas and Kneipp course.

Bike hire is free at reception, and ski storage is provided in the winter. All rooms feature wood-panelled walls and a minibar.

Soft bathrobes and slippers are provided in each private bathroom. Some rooms overlook the garden, others offer mountain views.

A generous breakfast buffet is served daily, and includes omelettes, homemade wholegrain bread and local organic cheeses.

The restaurant serves Mediterranean cuisine and South Tyrolean specialities. Buses to Monguelfo Train Station stop in front of the property.
