Description

Located 400 metres from Monguelfo Station, Hotel Weisses Lamm features a spa, bar and a Tyrolean restaurant.

It offers free lobby WiFi, free ski storage, and country-style rooms with satellite LCD TV.

Rooms at the family-run Weisses Lamm come with exposed-beam ceilings and natural-wood furniture.

Each offers a private bathroom with hairdryer, and some have balconies with Dolomite views.

A daily buffet breakfast is provided. The restaurant serves both local and international cuisine with local products.

After a day skiing or hiking, guests can relax with a sauna, hot tub or Turkish bath.

A ski train has services to/from the Plan de Corones Ski Slopes, 8 km away. Bolzano, the provincial capital, is a 70-minute drive away.
