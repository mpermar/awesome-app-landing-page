Description

Set in Perca, Alpin Hotel Sonnblick is only 200 metres from the Kronplatz ski lift.

It offers a free spa and wellness centre, a saltwater relaxation pool, a furnished garden, and a free ski shuttle service.

With a flat-screen TV and free WiFi, air conditioned rooms at the Sonnblick feature wooden furnishings.

The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and shower. A buffet breakfast consists of sweet and savoury food such as pastries, cold cuts and cheeses.

The restaurant serves local specialities. A wellness centre including a hot tub, an infrared cabin, various saunas, and a Turkish bath invites to relax.

A traditional wooden dining room and a lounge area are also available. Parking at the property is free.

Brunico can be reached by car in 5 minutes, while Bolzano is a 1-hour drive away.
