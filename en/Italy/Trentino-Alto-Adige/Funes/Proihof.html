Description

Featuring a farm and free BBQ facilities, Proihof offers rooms with a mountain-view balcony.

Located a 5-minute drive from the centre of Funes, it produces its own jam and eggs and has free Wi-Fi access.

Alpine-style rooms come with a flat-screen TV, wooden furnishings and carpeted floors.

The private bathroom includes a bath tub or a shower. A breakfast consisting of homemade jam and eggs, cold cuts and yoghurt is served daily.

Guests can relax in the sun terrace or visit the farm animals. Proihof is also well placed for hiking excursions.

Situated 1200 metres above sea level, the property is 10 minutes’ walk from the cross-country ski slopes.
