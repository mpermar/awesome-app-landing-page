Description

Offering a hot tub and ski storage space, Albergo Aurora is situated in Vignola in the Trentino Alto Adige Region.

Guests can taste meals at the on-site restaurant. Free private parking is available on site.

The rooms come with a TV. Albergo Aurora features free WiFi throughout the property.

There is a shared lounge at the property. The spa centre and the breakfast can be enjoyed in a separate building, located 20 metres from the property.

The area is popular for skiing and hiking. Levico Terme is 10. 7 km from the property, while Trento is 24 km from Albergo Aurora.
