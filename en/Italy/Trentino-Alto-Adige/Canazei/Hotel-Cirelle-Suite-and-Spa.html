Description

Offering an indoor pool and a spa, Hotel Cirelle Suite & Spa is a traditional Tyrolean property in Alba di Canazei, just 300 metres from the Ciampac Buffaure ski slopes.

It offers free bikes, a relaxing sun terrace, and Alpine-style accommodation with a satellite LCD TV.

Featuring free WiFi, rooms at the Hotel Cirelle Suite & Spa are fitted with natural-wood furniture and panelling.

Each comes with a private bathroom, while other units also have a kitchenette. A buffet breakfast is provided, including ham, cheese, eggs and fresh fruit.

Guests will also find homemade pastries and cakes, baked daily in the property’s kitchen.

After a day skiing or hiking, you can book access to the wellness centre on site.

Relaxation facilities include Finnish sauna, Turkish bath and hot tub. The centre of Canazei is 2 km from the property.
