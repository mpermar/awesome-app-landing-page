Description

This hotel in Stelvio National Park is set at the foot of the Ortles-Cevedale Group.

It offers a free shuttle to the ski slopes of Pejo, free Wi-Fi, and free bikes. Rooms at Hotel Centrale are furnished in natural wood with typical Alpine style.

They all include a private bathroom with shower, and satellite TV. Some of them come with a balcony.

The Centrale offers a breakfast buffet. This morning treat is served daily in the breakfast room.

The restaurant, which offers local food, features a salad bar. The hotel provides discounts on a thermal bath property in Pejo located 2 km away.
