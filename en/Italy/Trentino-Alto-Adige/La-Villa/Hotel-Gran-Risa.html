Description

Right next to the Piz La Ila ski lift connecting to the Dolomiti Superski and Sellaronda ski areas, Hotel Gran Risa offers Alpine-style accommodation in La Villa.

It features a wellness centre and traditional restaurant. Rooms at Gran Risa are fitted with light-wood furnishings and carpeted floors, in a traditional mountain style.

Facilities include a balcony overlooking the mountains, as well as an LCD TV with satellite channels.

Guests can spend relaxing moment on the furnished terrace or at the free spa, where 3 saunas and indoor pool are available.

Meat specialities and traditional polenta are served at the property's restaurant, specialising in both local and italian cuisine.

The property is in the heart of the mountains, 5 minutes' drive from Badia. Brunico is a 30-minute drive away.
