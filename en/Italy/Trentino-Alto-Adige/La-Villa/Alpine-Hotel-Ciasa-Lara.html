Description

At the hotel, every room includes a desk and a flat-screen TV. Guest rooms include a closet.

A continental or Full English/Irish breakfast is available daily at the property.

Spa and wellness facilities including a hot spring bath and a sauna are at guests' disposal during their stay at Alpine Hotel Ciasa Lara. Skiing is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.

Colz is a 6-minute walk from Alpine Hotel Ciasa Lara, while Piz La Ila 6,800 feet is a 7-minute walk from the property.
