Description

Located right on the Doninz ski slopes, Hotel Dolasilla features a fitness area and a 600-m² spa with free hot tub, indoor pool, sauna and Turkish bath.

It offers free WiFi and free outdoor parking. Boasting a balcony and mountain views, the guest rooms have an Alpine-style décor, with wooden furnishings and parquet floors.

Each includes a private bathroom, safe and flat-screen TV. The Dolasilla Hotel has its own garden with sun terrace, a restaurant and snack bar, plus free ski storage.

Mountain bikes can be rented at reception, and guests can park in the underground garage at extra charge.

A buffet breakfast is served daily and includes sweet and savoury items. The hotel is in La Villa, part of the Alta Badia Ski Resort.

Brunico can be reached by a bus stopping 300 metres away, while the Alta Badia Golf Club is a 10-minute drive away.
