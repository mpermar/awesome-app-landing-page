Description

Family run Wiesenhof Gardenresort offers a wellness centre and large rooms in the rich valley of Leonhard in Passeier.

It features free WiFi and indoor and outdoor swimming pools. Featuring typical Alpine wood furnishings and decorations, accommodation includes satellite TV and en suite bathrooms.

Some rooms provide a furnished balcony. The Stube, a typical South-Tyrol tavern, offers local specialities and regional cuisine.

Wiesenhof Gardenresort provides a hearty buffet breakfast every morning, which can be enjoyed on the terrace in fine weather.

The spacious gardens include a playground for children and sun loungers and deck chairs.

A Finnish sauna and a Turkish bath invite to relax. Massages and beauty treatments can be booked on request.

Activities in the surrounding area include golf and tennis. Hiking and mountain biking trails start right on the doorstep.

Free private parking is available at Wiesenhof Gardenresort. Guided walking tours can be booked directly at the hotel.
