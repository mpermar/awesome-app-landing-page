Description

Featuring a wellness area with a sauna and hot tub, Unterer Obereggerhof is a 10-minute drive from San Leonardo in Passiria.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant or a drink at the bar. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

All rooms have a flat-screen TV. Each room comes with a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer.

You can engage in various activities, such as skiing and hiking. Bolzano is 52 km from Unterer Obereggerhof, while Merano is 28 km away.
