Description

Featuring a wellness and thermal center, Hotel Terme Antico Bagno is located in Pozza di Fassa.

It includes a restaurant with healthy options. All rooms have a balcony overlooking the mountains or the woods.

Rooms at Terme Antico Bagno are classically decorated, most of them include soft carpeted floors.

Each has a private bathroom complete with bath or shower, toiletries, and hairdryer.

The restaurant serves 3 kind of menus in combination with the wellness treatments, named vital, creative, or diet.

A typical dinner with local specialities is organised weekly. Breakfast is a sweet and savoury buffet, including homemade pastries.

At the spa and thermal centre guests can relax in the aromatherapy, the steam shower, or take advantage of the physiotherapy gym.

In addition, it offers a wide variety of treatments and massages. The hotel is 1.

5 km from the Vigo di Fassa and Buffaure slopes. Free parking and a free ski deposit with boots warmer are also provided.
