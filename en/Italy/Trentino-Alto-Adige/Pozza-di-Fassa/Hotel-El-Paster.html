Description

Nestled in the Val di Fassa Valley in Pera di Fassa, family-run Hotel El Paster offers a restaurant, free bikes and a 220-m² wellness centre.

Facing beautiful Mounts Catinaccio and Monzoni, it includes rooms with a panoramic balcony.

Rooms at the El Paster have all-wood furniture, white textiles and a flat-screen satellite TV.

They feature a fully equipped private bathroom and a sofa bed, while some also overlook the well-kept garden.

A generous buffet breakfast is served in the restaurant and includes cereals, fresh fruit and juices.

The restaurant, which is also open for external guests, offers Trentino and international specialities.

After a day trekking or skiing, you can relax at spa, which includes a Turkish bath, Finnish sauna, emotional showers and heated pool.

A local ski bus stops opposite the property and will take you to the Catinaccio ski slopes.

Pozza di Fassa is a 5-minute drive away. At reception, bike lovers can book bike tours.
