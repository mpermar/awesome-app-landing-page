Description

Just 50 metres from the Alloch Ski Slopes, this family-run hotel offers a traditional restaurant and a wellness centre.

All Alpine-style rooms come with a private balcony. Sport Hotel Majarè provides a free heated ski deposit.

The nearby slopes are also open for night skiing. Free public buses stop nearby and connect you to the Sellaronda Slopes, a 5-minute ride away.

Rooms at Hotel Majarè have carpeted floors and wooden furnishings. Each includes an LCD satellite TV and a private bathroom with hairdryer.

Homemade cakes feature at breakfast, together with freshly squeezed juices, cheeses and eggs.

The restaurant serves Ladin dishes as well as a selection of pizzas. The hotel is a 5-minute walk from the centre of Pozza di Fassa and a 45-minute drive from Bolzano.
