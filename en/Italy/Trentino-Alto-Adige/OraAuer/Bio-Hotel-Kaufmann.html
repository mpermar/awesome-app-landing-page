Description

Surrounded by nature, Kaufmann is in Ora centre. It offers an organic restaurant and extensive grounds with a swimming pool, children's playground, BBQ and plenty of sun loungers.

Bio-Hotel & Residence Kaufmann is great for every season. In winter you have a free ski deposit and are 20 km from the Obereggen ski slopes.

Rooms are elegant and bright, with wood or carpeted floors and large windows or a balcony.

All feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom. Hairdryers are available for free on request.

Breakfast is buffet style, while the restaurant serves organic food at dinner. It is also open for lunch on weekends.

You can enjoy a drink at the bar or out on the terrace. Free outdoor parking is provided and Wi-Fi access is also free in public areas.

The property is 4 km from the A22 motorway exit and 1 km from Ora Train Station for trains to Bolzano and Trento.
