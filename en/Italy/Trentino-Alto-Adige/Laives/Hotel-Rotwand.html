Description

Enjoying typical Alpine surroundings in a peaceful location on Steinmannwald Mountain, Hotel Rotwand is just 10 minutes' drive from Bolzano.

This family-run hotel features a swimming pool and a restaurant. Parking and WiFi are free.

Featuring a balcony, all the rooms at the Rotwand are elegantly decorated with typical South Tyrolean wooden interiors and colourful details.

The spacious accommodation offers a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom fitted with free toiletries.

The restaurant serves specialities from the region, including local game, fish and asparagus.

It includes fine Italian meals and traditional South Tyrolean specialities. A variety of regional and national fine wines complement the cuisine.

The Bolzano Exhibition Centre is 5 km away, while The Obereggen and Alpe di Siusi ski slopes are reachable by car in around 45 minutes.
