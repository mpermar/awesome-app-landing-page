Description

Family-run Hotel Edelweiss is set at an altitude of 1380 metres with panoramic mountain views.

It offers Alpine-style rooms with free Wi-Fi and also features a spa. The Gitschberg/Jochtal ski slope is just 300 metres away.

Rooms at the Edelweiss Hotel are elegantly furnished and all feature a balcony and seating area.

They come with an LCD TV, a minibar, and a private bathroom with shower or bath tub.

Breakfast is a varied buffet of sweet and savoury products, including homemade cakes and freshly prepared egg dishes.

A free cake buffet is offered to guests each afternoon. The on-site restaurant, fitted with wood-panelled walls, is open for lunch and dinner.

Tyrolean dishes and classic Italian cuisine are offered. Guests can relax at the wellness centre, which features saunas, steam baths and a hot tub.

Massages can also be booked. Maranza centre is a 5-minute walk from the hotel, and the Rio di Pusteria cable car is just 250 metres away.
