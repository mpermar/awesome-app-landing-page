Description

Set in Maranza, Pension Sonnenhof is 100 metres from the Brunner ski lift and 50 metres from the free ski bus stop.

It offers four saunas and rooms featuring balconies with sun loungers. Rooms have a traditional mountain design with wooden furniture and carpeted floors.

They all include free Wi-Fi and an LCD TV with satellite channels. A varied buffet of sweet and savoury food is served at breakfast each morning.

You will have a selection of fresh fruit, homemade bread and fruit salad. The restaurant is open at dinner and coffee is served out on the terrace in the afternoon.

The Sonnenhof Pension is a 5-minute walk from the Alpin Pool where guests have discounts on the swimming pool and massages.

Free parking and ski storage are provided and you can return from the slopes on your skis.
