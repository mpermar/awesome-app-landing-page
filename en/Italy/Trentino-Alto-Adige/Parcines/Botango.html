Description

BOTANGO is a 3-star hotel located 6 km from Merano. Offering a free wellness centre and swimming pool, it features free WiFi throughout, a garden, and a traditional restaurant.

A buffet-style breakfast is provided daily. The rooms are bright and fitted with wooden furnishings.

Each comes with a sofa, a work desk, and an en suite bathroom. All rooms include satellite-TV with radio, telephone, safe, hairdryer, bathrobe and bathing shoe, coffee machine & water boiler.

At the hotel you can relax in the free wellness centre. It includes sauna, Turkish bath, and an indoor swimming pool.

Massages are also available on request. Breakfast consists of sweet and savoury products.

The restaurant serves typical Tyrolean cuisine, and there is also a pizzeria on site.

A bus stop offering links to the centre of Parcines is just a 5-minute walk from BOTANGO.
