Description

The Miramonti, is located in the small village of Daiano, 2 km from Cavalese, and enjoys beautiful views of the Dolomites.

It offers free parking, and rustic apartments with balcony. All accommodation at Residence Hotel Miramonti features a fully equipped kitchen with microwave, a satellite TV, and a private bathroom with hairdryer.

Residence Miramonti features a sauna, and a heated ski deposit. The property is near the public ski bus to the Alpe Cermis slopes, 2 km away.

A newspaper stand, a bakery, a café, together with a hairdresser, a bank with ATM, and a restaurant with pizzeria are available nearby.
