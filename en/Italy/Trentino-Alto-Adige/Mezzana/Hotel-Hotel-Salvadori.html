Description

One of our top picks in Mezzana.Hotel Salvadori is set just 650 feet from the center of Mezzana-Marilleva in the heart of Valley Di Sole. It features a heated salt-water pool, and a free shuttle bus to Folgarida ski elevators.

Your room at the Salvadori Hotel features Satellite TV and WiFi connection. Some rooms have a balcony with views over the Stelvio National Park.

This family-run hotel guarantees a warm welcome and a personalized service. A luxury wellness center on site features a swimming pool, hot tub, and sauna. Massages and beauty treatments are also available.

After a day skiing or trekking you can enjoy characteristic cuisine at the restaurant. An à la carte service is accompanied by a vegetable buffet.

Hotel Salvadori is 2,650 feet from Marilleva 900 cable car.
