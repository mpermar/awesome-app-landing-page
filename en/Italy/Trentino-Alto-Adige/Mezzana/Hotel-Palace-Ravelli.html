Description

Hotel Palace Ravelli is in Mezzana, 1. 5 km from the Marilleva 900 ski lifts. It offers a large indoor swimming pool, a spa, and rooms with free unlimited Wi-Fi and satellite TV.

In the Val di Sole, the Palace Ravelli Hotel is between the Stelvio and Adamello Brenta natural parks.

Some of the hotel's rooms have views across the mountains. The Palace Ravelli's spa and wellness centre offers a small gym and a relax zone with loungers.

Massages and beauty treatments are also available. Ravelli Palace's restaurant serves a range of local and international cuisine, accompanied by regional wines.
