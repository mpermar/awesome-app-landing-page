Description

With a panoramic location in Val di Non, Hotel Casez features a comfortable rooftop terrace overlooking the mountains.

Its free wellness centre includes an indoor jacuzzi and gym. Rooms at the Casez are all themed and individually designed.

They come with wood or carpeted floors and have a minibar and flat-screen TV. Most have a balcony.

Breakfast is buffet style and the restaurant serves Trentino specialities in a cosy atmosphere with wood-beamed ceilings and wooden furniture.

Bikes are free to rent at Casez Hotel and the surroundings are ideal for cycling and trekking.

Guests will also find a sauna, hot tub and Turkish bath in the wellness centre, all completely free.

Guests can enjoy discounts on the entrance fee at the partner 18-hole golf court 7 km away.

Located just outside the town of Sanzeno, the hotel is 2 km from Santa Giustina Lake and 40 km from the city of Bolzano.
