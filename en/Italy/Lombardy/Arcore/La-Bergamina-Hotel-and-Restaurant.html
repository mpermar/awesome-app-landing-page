Description

Featuring a garden, La Bergamina Hotel & Restaurant offers accommodation with free WiFi in the Brianza countryside.

The property is a 5-minute drive from Monza. The air-conditioned rooms include a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries.

Some rooms are located in an annex building. A continental breakfast is served daily in the common room, including various local produce, homemade bread and also gluten-free options.

There is a bar and a restaurant serving traditional and modern Italian cuisine. Arcore Train Station is 3 km from Hotel & Restaurant La Bergamina, while Milan and Bergamo are a 20-minute drive away.
