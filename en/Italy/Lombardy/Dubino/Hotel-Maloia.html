Description

At 5 km from Lake Como, Hotel Maloia offers rooms come with flat-screen TV and free Wi-Fi.

It features a mountain-view garden, and a restaurant serving traditional cuisine from Lombardy.

Rooms include a private bathroom with hairdryer and toiletries. They have wooden floors, classic furnishings and walls with pastel colours.

Some also offer a balcony and garden views. An extensive wine list is available at the restaurant, which serves dishes typical of the Valtellina area.

It can also provide gluten-free meals and special menus upon request. During nice weather, breakfast can be enjoyed in the furnished garden.

The Maloia Hotel is in Dubino, 37 km from Sondrio. Staff can recommend trekking and cycling itineraries.
