Description

Offering a rooftop terrace with lake views, free Wi-Fi throughout, and an outdoor swimming pool, Castello Oldofredi is a renovated building on the island of Monte Isola surrounded by Lake Iseo.

Each room has a unique theme and features air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom. Apartments also have a living/dining area with kitchenette, some can be in the annex or in the main building.

The restaurant specializes in classic Italian cuisine.

Ferries to/from Sulzano leave a few steps from the property.
