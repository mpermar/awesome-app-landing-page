Description

Featuring an indoor pool, Saronno's Starhotels Grand Milan is a design hotel 500 metres from Saronno Station, providing links to Milan.

With free WiFi, the property has a fitness centre and a spa with a sauna, a Turkish bath and a Kneipp.

This 4-star hotel is 2 km from the A9 motorway exit and 20 minutes' drive from Fiera Milano.

A shuttle service is available during major trade fairs. Rooms and suites have a contemporary design and modern bathrooms.

All air-conditioned, they feature a minibar. Breakfast is buffet style and the Hostaria by Eataly restaurant offers a selection of local dishes, while the Hostaria Cafè by Eataly is the perfect venue to relax and enjoy a drink.

The Grand Milan also includes impressive business facilities with a total of 14 meeting rooms.

On the 9th floor, guests will also find the swimming pool, the fitness area and the wellness centre.
