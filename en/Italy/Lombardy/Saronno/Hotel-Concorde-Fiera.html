Description

Hotel Concorde Fiera is located in Gerenzano, 3 km from Saronno and 1 km from Gerenzano-Turate train station, with connections to Milan.

It offers air conditioned rooms with minibar and satellite TV. Rooms feature classic wooden furniture, elegant parquet floors, and a 28'' flat-screen TV with premium channels.

The bathroom comes with free toiletries and a hairdryer, some also offers a chromotherapy shower.

Breakfast is a varied buffet and includes the property's homemade and gluten free products upon request.

Guests can enjoy a dinner at a partner restaurant, located 500 metres from the hotel.

Staff at the Concorde are multilingual and are available 24 hours a day. A shuttle service to Rho Pero exhibition centre, the airport and nearby cities can be organised at extra cost, while a private parking is available at a surcharge.

The Rho Fiera Milano Exhibition Centre is 9 km from the Concorde Fiera. The Saronno A9 highway exit is 2 km away.
