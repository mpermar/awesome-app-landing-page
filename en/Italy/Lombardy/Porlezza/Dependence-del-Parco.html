Description

Featuring a garden with a pool and views of Lake Lugano, Dependence del Parco is located on the lake shore and has a private beach.

Lugano in Switzerland is 12 km away. Rooms here are elegant and come with a balcony and a living area that features a sofa and satellite flat-screen TV.

Wi-Fi access is offered for free in public areas. Guests can enjoy an array of wellness facilities, including a fitness centre, Turkish bath and hot tub.

An indoor pool is also available. Italian and local dishes are served at the restaurant.

Porlezza is 1 km from the property, while Menaggio and Lake Como are only 12 km away.
