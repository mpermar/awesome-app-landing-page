Description

Set in the centre of Lecco, Villa del Cigno has a peaceful location even thought it is just 800 metres from the main square.

It has a garden with children's playground and offers free parking and free WiFi throughout the property.

Rooms come with 32-inch flat-screen TVs, Memory foam mattresses and minibars. Villa del Cigno is a 20-minute walk from the lakeside promenade along Lake Como.
