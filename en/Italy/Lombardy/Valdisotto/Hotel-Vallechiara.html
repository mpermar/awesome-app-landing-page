Description

Set on the Bormio ski slopes, Hotel Vallechiara offers a sauna, a terrace, and Alpine-style rooms.

Overlooking the town, 4 km away, it includes a football pitch and tennis plus basketball courts.

Each room comes with a TV and overlooks the town or ski slopes. Some have a balcony or terrace.

Your day at Vallechiara starts with a large and varied buffet breakfast. You can enjoy traditional homemade dishes at the restaurant.

Part of the Alta Valtellina ski area, Hotel Vallechiara is around 50 metres from the nearest ski lift to the Bormio ski area.

The hotel also offers a ski and snowboard school, and winter sports equipment is available to rent.
