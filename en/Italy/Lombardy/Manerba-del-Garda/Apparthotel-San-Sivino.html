Description

Apartments come with a fully fitted kitchenette and a balcony or terrace. Rooms are air conditioned and are complete with a satellite LCD TV.

The park of the San Sivino Apparthotel features oleanders and palm trees, and ends on a gently sloping beach on Lake Garda. There are also 4 swimming pools, for adults and for children, a free minigolf and free ping pong tables.

The Apparthotel's restaurant serves classic Italian cuisine, drinks and snacks. In fine weather, meals are served on a panoramic terrace.
