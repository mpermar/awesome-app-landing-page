Description

Overlooking Manerba del Garda Bay, Residence Bellavista features an outdoor swimming pool with views of Lake Garda in the distance.

Its self-catering apartments come with free internet. With a balcony or terrace, all the spacious apartments offer lake views.

Each has a fully equipped kitchen, plus a 32" LCD TV with satellite channels and USB/HDMI socket.

You will also enjoy panoramic lake and mountain views from Bellavista's gardens. Here you will find the outdoor pool, sun loungers and poolside bar which is open to the public.

Supermarkets in Manerba town centre are a 5-minute walk from the Bellavista. Buses stop nearby and link to local train stations.
