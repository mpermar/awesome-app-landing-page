Description

Miralago Residence is just 100 metres from the lakeshore and 5 minutes’ drive from Manerba del Garda.

You can relax by the pool and in the peaceful gardens. Friendly and welcoming, this property is a pleasant walk from Manerba town centre and La Rocca, with its beautiful view of the lake.

Wi-Fi is available in public areas at a small extra cost and free parking is provided.

Residence Miralago Rooms & Apartments features a children's playground and table tennis in the garden, as well as a terrace where drinks are served.

A wide variety of sports can be enjoyed nearby, from tennis to golf and water sports.

Served indoors or outdoors in the garden or patio, breakfast is buffet style. It includes fine cold cuts, cheese, fruit juices and bread, plus hot drinks, croissants and more.
