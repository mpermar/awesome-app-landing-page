Description

Boasting a wellness centre and views over Lake Como, Mandarin Oriental Lago di Como offers elegant guestrooms and suites, with 2 stand-alone villas, in Blevio.

It features a fine restaurant, free indoor and outdoor pools, free parking and vast gardens.

The wellness centre at Mandarin Oriental Lago di Como includes 2 massage rooms, 2 private Spa Suites, a fitness centre with state-of-the-art equipment, a beauty studio, a 16-metre heated pool with hot tub, and a sun deck.

With free WiFi and air conditioning, rooms at this Mandarin Oriental include a minibar, safe and satellite TV.

Some offer a balcony or terrace overlooking Lake Como. Open for lunch and dinner, the restaurant serves regional specialities from Lombardy and Italian classics, as well as fresh homemade pasta.

Free private parking is available on site, and a shuttle service to/from Como can be arranged on request.
