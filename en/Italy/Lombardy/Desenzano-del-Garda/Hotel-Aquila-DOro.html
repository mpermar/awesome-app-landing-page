Description

In an elegant setting with superb views, the Hotel Aquila D’Oro provides rooms with free internet access, air conditioning and an LCD TV. Some feature a balcony overlooking the lake.

Hotel Aquila D'Oro Hotel has a restaurant with a panoramic view of the lake, perfect for romantic meals and special events. Its convention center also offers lake views.

The hotel is 2.5 miles from the highway exits for Sirmione and is ideal for exploring Lake Garda. The hotel is within a 16 miles radius of Verona Montichiari and Verona Villafranca airports.
