Description

Set on the shores of Lake Garda, Camping Village San Francesco features an outdoor pool, tennis court, table tennis and children’s playground.

Bikes and water sport facilities can be rented on site. Guests can enjoy the bar by the outdoor pool.

The restaurant serves local and international cuisines, as well as pizza. The air-conditioned mobile homes come with a patio or a garden fitted with tables and chairs.

Each has an equipped kitchenette, a private bathroom and BBQ facilities are available, as well.

Evening entertainment is provided at Camping Village San Francesco. Desenzano del Garda’s centre is 5 km away.
