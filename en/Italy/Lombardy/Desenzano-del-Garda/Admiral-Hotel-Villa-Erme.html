Description

Overlooking Lake Garda and a few steps from its shores, the Admiral Hotel features a panoramic rooftop terrace with hot tub.

Set 2 km from central Desenzano del Garda, it offers free Wi-Fi and parking. With a balcony and air conditioning, rooms at the Admiral Hotel Villa Erme all include a satellite TV, safe and minibar, plus wooden floors.

A semi-covered swimming pool, small fitness corner and sauna are available on site, as well as a 2-hole golf putting garden.

Bikes are available at no extra charge. Attentive staff serves a varied buffet breakfast, Italian specialities are also prepared at the pool-view restaurant.

After a day exploring the lakeside, guests can sip a drink from the bar on the terrace.

Sirmione, set on a promontory overlooking the lake, is 7 km from the hotel. The Gardaland theme park is a 25-minute drive away.
