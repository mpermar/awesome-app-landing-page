Description

Desenzano Camping Village is set on the shores of Lake Garda, at 1. 5 km from Desenzano del Garda city centre.

It boasts panoramic views of the surrounding protected nature area. The residence is immersed in a 50.

000 m² park, which hosts a variety of sports and leisure activities. It includes a restaurant and a bar on the lake beach, and a boat storage area at Marina del Vo’.

Guests can enjoy the property’s private beach, a seasonal outdoor pool, tennis and volleyball courts, and a garden with a playground.

WiFi is free at reception. Desenzano Camping Village offers simply furnished studios and apartments.
