Description

Featuring an outdoor pool and a garden, Hotel Silvana Garnì is located in Limone sul Garda, 100 metres from the lake shore.

Guests can benefit from a patio overlooking the lake and free WiFi available in public areas.

All rooms at the Silvana Garnì come with a garden-view balcony. Some have lake views and a satellite TV.

The private bathroom is fitted with a showers and include towels. The surrounding area is popular for hiking, windsurfing and tennis.

Limone sul Garda centre is less than a 10-minute walk from Hotel Silvana Garnì. Riva del Garda is a 14-minute drive away.
