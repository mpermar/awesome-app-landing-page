Description

Hotel Leonardo Da Vinci offers you all-inclusive accommodation right by Lake Garda.

This large park has a private beach and sports, wellness and entertainment facilities provided.

You can walk up the lake shore to Limone Sul Garda in 20 minutes or get the Da Vinci Hotel's free shuttle.

On the complex, you will find indoor and outdoor pools and a wellness centre with gym, sauna and hot tub, all of which you can use for free.

There are water sports on the beach. Free snacks and drinks are provided throughout the day and there is a kid's club.

The garden has play areas. All modern-style rooms come with a small fridge and a satellite TV.
