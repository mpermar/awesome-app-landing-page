Description

You can walk to Lake Garda in 5 minutes from Hotel Coste.

The friendly, family-run hotel has a large terrace with bar and restaurant. Home-grown food and local wines are served.

The yard has a children's area, a bowling green and ping-pong.

Hotel Coste's rooms overlook the grounds or the lake and have satellite TV.
