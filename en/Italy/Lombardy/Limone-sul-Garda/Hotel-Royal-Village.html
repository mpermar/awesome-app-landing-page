Description

Just a 10-minute walk from Lake Garda, the 4-star Hotel Royal Village is in Limone sul Garda.

It offers an indoor pool and 2 outdoor pools with panoramic lake views. At the Royal Village’s restaurant, guests can enjoy international cuisine and Italian specialities.

Meals can be served in the elegant dining room or outdoors, with views of the lake.

Offering a balcony, terrace or garden, rooms at Royal Village Hotel have either carpeted or tiled floors.

They are air conditioned and offer satellite TV. Some have lake views. During summer, the property also offers a miniclub, evening entertainment programmes and piano bar.

The hotel is a 10-minute drive from Riva Del Garda. The pier where boats leave for Malcesine is 1 km away.
