Description

Located in Limone sul Garda and 10 minutes’ walk from Lake Garda, Hotel Villa Belvedere is a family-run villa offering a swimming pool and a small fitness room.

Featuring air conditioning, the classic-style rooms and suites at the Villa Belvedere Hotel come with a TV, safe and tiled or wooden floors.

Some have a patio or balcony, and overlook the lake. You can relax with a drink in the bar, lounge in the TV room or play a game of table tennis.
