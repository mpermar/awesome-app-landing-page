Description

Set in a park 4 km from Rovagnate and Olgiate Molgora Train Station, Oasi di Galbusera Bianca offers a garden, and free bike rental.

A sweet Italian-style breakfast is available daily and includes fruit juices, cappuccinos, and pastries.

The rooms are furnished with themed wooden furniture and a private bathroom with hairdryer and free toiletries.

Some have wood-beamed ceilings. Lake Como is a 30-minute drive from the property, while Milan is 35 km away.

The area is ideal for hiking and biking tours. Free WiFi is available in public areas.
