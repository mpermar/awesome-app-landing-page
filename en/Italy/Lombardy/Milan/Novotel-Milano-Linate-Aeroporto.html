Description

Novotel Milano Linate Aeroporto offers a free scheduled shuttle service to/from Linate Airport, a 10-minute drive away.

It also features an outdoor swimming pool, elegant bar, and a fitness centre open 24h.

All air-conditioned rooms come with coffee and tea facilities, and an LCD TV with satellite channels.

Guests will enjoy a sweet and savoury breakfast every morning. The on-site restaurant serves Italian and international cuisine.

The hotel is just off the Tangenziale Est ring road and A1 Motorway exit. The tram that stops 300 metres away will take you to Milan's Cathedral in approximately 25 minutes.
