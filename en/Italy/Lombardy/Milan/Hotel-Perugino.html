Description

Hotel Perugino provides modern rooms with free WiFi and air conditioning. Set in a tranquil area of Milan, it is next Largo Marinai d'Italia Park and has great bus links around the city.

Each room has an LCD TV with international and Sky TV channels, free high-speed WiFi, a minibar and a private bathroom with hairdryer.

Some rooms have a balcony. At the Perugino Hotel you will find a spacious lounge, a 24-hour free tea corner, and a small garden where you can relax.

On weekends, fine cocktails and drinks can be prepared for you at the hotel's bar.

Milan centre is a 15-minute walk or 5-minute bus or tram ride away. Milan Porta Vittoria Train Station, 1 km away, offers direct links to Rho Fiera Exhibition Centre.
