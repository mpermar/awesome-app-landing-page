Description

Set on the shores of Lake Garda in Salò, Antiche Rive Holidays Apartments has a peaceful garden and an on-site delicatessen.

It offers elegant apartments with exposed-beam ceilings. The apartments at the Antiche Rive Holidays are fitted with a mix of traditional and modern furnishings.

All come with a kitchen and 2 satellite LCD TVs, while some feature lake views and raised sleeping areas.

Both wired and wireless internet are available. Guests can also rent bikes for exploring the lake shore.
