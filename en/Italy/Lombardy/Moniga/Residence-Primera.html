Description

Set in Moniga, Residence Primera offers a large garden with a pool, tennis court and BBQ.

Apartments all come with a fully equipped kitchenette and a balcony or patio, some overlooking Lake Garda.

The Primera Residence includes a mini market, restaurant and bar. It provides entertainment in April and from the end of May until mid September.

The swimming pool has a hydromassage area and is open from April until October. There is also a children's playground, table tennis and darts.

In the apartments there is a comfortable living room with satellite TV and a bathroom with shower.

The residence provides free parking and is 10 minutes' drive from Gardagolf Country Club.

You have discounts for a number of local attractions including Aqua Paradise, Natura Viva and Movieland.
