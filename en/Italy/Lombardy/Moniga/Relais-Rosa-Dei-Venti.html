Description

Relais Rosa Dei Venti is located on a wide green area, and surrounded by big olive trees.

It is just 200 metres from the shores of Lake Garda, and each apartment has a lake-view patio or balcony.

The residence is made up of numerous self-catering apartments, located in different buildings.

Common facilities include an outdoor swimming pool, a garden with children's playground.

Parking is free. Every apartment has a comfortable living room, and a kitchen. All accommodation comes with air conditioning, a satellite TV with DVD player, and a private bathroom with bath or shower.

The new tourist harbour in Moniga and the beach, are about a 2-minute walk away. The town's historic centre is a 5-minute walk from the Rosa Dei Venti.
