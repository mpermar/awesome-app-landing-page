Description

Boasting modern apartments with lake view terraces and an outdoor pool, La Giolosa Gardapartments is located in Moniga.

Free WiFi is available throughout. Air-conditioned apartments come with dining area and kitchen with a dishwasher.

Private bathrooms feature a hairdryer and free toiletries. The laundry room is equipped with 2 washing machines, 2 dryers, iron and ironing board for free use.

La Giolosa Gardapartments is set 100 metres from Moniga Harbour and a 10-minute drive from Arzaga Golf Club.
