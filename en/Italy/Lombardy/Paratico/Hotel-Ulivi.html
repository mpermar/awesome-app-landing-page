Description

The hotel features an outdoor swimming pool, surrounded by a sun terrace and grounds. You can sunbathe and enjoy the views of Lake Iseo.

A buffet breakfast is served daily, and you can eat out on the sunny terrace during warm weather. Daily newspapers are provided in the breakfast room.
