Description

The Monastero Hotel offers extremely spacious, terraced rooms, all overlooking the hotel's private park and the outdoor swimming pool.

A host of spa and beauty treatments, including various massages, will provide a truly regenerating stay. The spa center is situated in a second building connected to the hotel.

The surroundings of Monastero Hotel offer a series of entertaining facilities, like golf and tennis courses, riding schools, and sailing clubs. Alternatively you could simply enjoy a walk in the beautiful, natural landscape surrounding the premises.

Complete your pleasant stay at the Monastero Hotel savoring the tasty dishes of the regional and national tradition. The restaurant is open between 12 p.m. and 2 p.m. and between 7 p.m. and 10 p.m.
