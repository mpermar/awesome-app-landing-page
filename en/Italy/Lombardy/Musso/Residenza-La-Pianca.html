Description

Residenza La Pianca offers modern apartments, each with a terrace with a beautiful view of Lake Como.

It is just a 10-minute walk from the centre of Musso. You will find a swimming pool and a sun terrace, both with panoramic views.

Each apartment has a furnished terrace and bath towels and bed linen are included.

Residenza La Pianca dates back to the 18th century and has been completely renovated.

It features a garden and a free car park. The residence is 500 metres from the lake, where you can enjoy surfing, fishing, and sailing.
