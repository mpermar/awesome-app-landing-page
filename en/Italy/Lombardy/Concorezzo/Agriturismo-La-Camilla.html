Description

Set in 7 hectares of private parkland, Agriturismo La Camilla is a working farm with a traditional restaurant specialising in Lombardy dishes and horse riding school.

Concorezzo centre is 1. 3 km away. With light contemporary furnishings, all rooms at La Camilla are spacious and air conditioned.

They include free WiFi, minibar and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Each has a modern private bathroom complete with hairdryer and toiletry set.

Breakfast is a continental buffet, with cold meats, cheeses and croissants. The cakes and marmalades are all homemade.

The restaurant serves local specialities prepared from the estate's own produce, including fruit, vegetables and meat.

Staying here means you are just 2. 5 km from the A51 motorway exit and a 30-minute drive from Milan.
