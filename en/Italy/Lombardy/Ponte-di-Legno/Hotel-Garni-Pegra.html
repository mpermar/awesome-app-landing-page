Description

Located directly on the ski slopes of Ponte di Legno and 10 minutes' walk from the centre, Hotel Garni Pegrà is near mountain paths during summer and ski pass office, ski school and rental facilities in winter.

The Garni Pegrà hotel is at an altitude of 1300 metres and is located in Val Camonica.

It is within easy reach of the SS42 state road. This family-run hotel offers comfortable rooms with an LCD TV with satellite channels, and mountain views.

Some rooms have a balcony. Guests can walk to Ponte di Legno centre from Garni Pegrà.

In the neighbourhood, there are sports facilities, a swimming pool, restaurants, and a children's playground.
