Description

L'Albereta Relais & Chateaux offers free WiFi and free spa access. The resort comes with a fitness centre and a tennis court, and is 600 metres from Erbusco’s centre.

Air-conditioned rooms and suites are elegant and luxurious, and come with satellite TV with pay-per-view channels, and bathrobes and slippers.

Some have a balcony and some have a spa bath. The 2000m² wellness area includes a sauna, Turkish Bath, hot tub, a pool featuring counter-current swimming, and fitness facilities.

L’Albereta is complete with 3 restaurants specialising in Italian cuisine, as well as a bar and terrace.

With free bike rental, the resort is 3. 5 km from Franciacorta Golf Club. Lake Iseo is a 15-minute drive from the resort and Borgonato-Adro Train Station is 5.
