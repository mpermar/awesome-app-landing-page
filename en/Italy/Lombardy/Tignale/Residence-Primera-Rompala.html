Description

Residence Primera/Rompala offers self-catering apartments with bathrooms, balconies and satellite TV.

Set in peaceful gardens with a pool, it is located in Tignale, overlooking Lake Garda.

The Residence Primera is surrounded by the Alto Garda Bresciano Natural Park. Riva del Garda, with its many water sports and boating centres, is 33 km away.
