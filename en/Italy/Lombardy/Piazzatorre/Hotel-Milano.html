Description

Featuring panoramic views of the Orobie Alps, the family-run Hotel Milano offers rooms in Piazzatorre, 1.

5 km from Piazzatorre Ski Area. With free WiFi, rooms at the Milano Hotel have a private bathroom, TV and safe.

A continental buffet breakfast is served daily. The Milano's restaurant serves both regional and classic Italian cuisine, featuring regional ingredients and wines from the extensive wine cellar.

Massages are available on request and ski passes can be purchased on site with a discount.

The Hotel Milano is 50 km from Bergamo Orio al Serio Airport. An airport shuttle is available on request.
