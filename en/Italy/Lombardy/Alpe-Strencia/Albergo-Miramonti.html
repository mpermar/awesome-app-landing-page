Description

Set in a historic building, Albergo Miramonti offers free Wi-Fi throughout. It is in the alpine village of Corteno Golgi and a 30-minute drive from the Swiss border and from Tirano.

Free private parking is available on site. All rooms feature a balcony with mountain or valley views, a flat-screen TV and a bathroom with hairdryer.

Some rooms have wood-beamed ceilings. The restaurant serves pizza. The nearest train station is in Edolo, 8 km away.
