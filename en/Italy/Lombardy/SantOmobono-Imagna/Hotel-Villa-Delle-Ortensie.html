Description

At an altitude of 500 metres, 19th-century Villa Delle Ortensie has over 130,000 m² of private parkland.

It is in the heart of the Imagna Valley, 25 km from Bergamo. Hotel Villa Delle Ortensie is ideal for relaxing break with plenty of landscaped gardens and a wellness centre with beauty farm.

Here you will find an indoor swimming pool, sauna and Turkish bath. There is also a jacuzzi and gym, and a tennis court outdoors.

All air-conditioned, rooms here have a classic design and include a TV, minibar and private bathroom.

Guests at the villa have a free internet point and free parking. Breakfast is buffet style and the restaurant serves Italian cuisine with lots of organic produce and vegetarian meals.
