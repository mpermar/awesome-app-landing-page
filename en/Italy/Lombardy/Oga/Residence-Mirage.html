Description

Set in Oga, 6 km from central Bormio, Residence Mirage offers free Wi-Fi throughout, a spacious garden with playground area, and a free laundry room.

A ski and bike storage space is available. The apartments feature a living/dining area with sofa and fully equipped kitchenette, a TV and a bathroom with hairdryer.

The Mirage has a wellness centre with sauna, hot tub and steam bath. You can ask for discounted rates to enjoy local cuisine at the bar and restaurant/pizzeria.

You will find a supermarket in the village of Oga, a 15-minute walk from the property.

Free parking is provided, a garage is also available. Livigno is 36 km from Residence Mirage, while San Colombano ski area is 1.
