Description

The Tata-o Spa & Resort is located in the Bergamasco Hills, 14 km from Bergamo. It features a wellness centre, an indoor pool, and air-conditioned rooms with satellite LCD TVs.

Rooms at the Tata-o Resort are decorated with wood panelling and light-coloured furnishings.

Each comes with a bathroom with soft bathrobes. You have free Wi-Fi access every day.

A buffet breakfast is provided daily. Guests can enjoy drinks at the bar and try the on-site restaurant.

The spa features a Finnish sauna, indoor pool, chromotherapy and aromatherapy showers.

Lecco, on the shores of Lake Como, is 21 km from the resort. Bergamo Orio al Serio Airport is a 20-minute drive away.
