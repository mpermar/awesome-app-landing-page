Description

Cardano Malpensa is a new, modern hotel located 10 minutes' drive from Milan Malpensa Airport.

It offers free Wi-Fi and a free shuttle service on request, between 05:00 and 00:00.

Hotel Cardano boasts a cosy and friendly atmosphere. Rooms are comfortable and spacious, with family rooms also available.

The outdoor swimming pool and garden are ideal to relax. The Rho fiera exhibition centres can both be reached in 30 minutes by car, and the centre of Milan is 39 km from the Cardano Hotel.
