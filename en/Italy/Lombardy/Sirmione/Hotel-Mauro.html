Description

Located just 200 metres from Lake Garda, Hotel Mauro is a family-run hotel with a quiet location on the peninsula of Sirmione.

It is close to the Virgilio Spa Centre, and offers free WiFi and a garden. All air-conditioned, rooms have a flat-screen TV with Sky channels.

Some have a balcony. Hotel Mauro is family-run hotel. There is a TV lounge with internet point as well as a bar.

Public tennis courts and children's playgrounds are located right outside the hotel.
