Description

Relax on the rooftop terrace and swim in the panoramic swimming pool at Hotel Arena, which overlooks Lake Garda.

You are just a 5-minute walk from the Virgilio thermal baths. Your room is complete with air conditioning and a satellite LCD TV.

Some also feature a private balcony. Relax in the garden or on the terrace with swimming pool.

With a fantastic location, just 100 metres from the lake, the Arena Hotel is an excellent base for exploring the surrounding area.
