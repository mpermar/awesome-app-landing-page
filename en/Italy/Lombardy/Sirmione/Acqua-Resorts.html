Description

Acqua Resorts offers design apartments with balcony or garden, 300 metres from Lake Garda's shores.

The building offers free parking, and a small but well-equipped spa. Each apartment of the Resorts Acqua is finely furnished, and comes with air conditioning and heated parquet floors.

Apartments also include a fully equipped kitchen with dining area, and flat-screen TV.

Bed linen and towels are included. The spa area includes an indoor pool with hydromassage area, plus an infrared sauna, Technogym equipment and outdoor pool with solarium.

Guests of the Acqua can access the beach and harbour of the nearby Yachting Club Mistral.
