Description

The Continental Hotel is in the historic center of Sirmione. It features a small garden with a swimming pool and hydromassage area, and a new wellness center with sauna, steam bath, and relaxation area.

The restaurant serves Italian and international cuisine. In warm weather, you can enjoy your meal out on the patio.
