Description

Albergo Meublè Olivi features a restaurant, free bikes, a bar and garden in Toscolano Maderno.

All rooms feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom.

The air-conditioned rooms provide a lake view and come with a desk and free WiFi.

At the hotel, all rooms come with a wardrobe. Albergo Meublè Olivi offers a buffet or Italian breakfast.

The accommodation offers a children's playground. Staff at Albergo Meublè Olivi are available to provide information at the 24-hour front desk.

Sirmione is 39 km from the hotel, while Riva del Garda is 35 km away. The nearest airport is Verona Airport, 65 km from Albergo Meublè Olivi.
