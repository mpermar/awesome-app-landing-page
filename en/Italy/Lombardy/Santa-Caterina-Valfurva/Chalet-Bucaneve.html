Description

Surrounded by the mountains in Santa Caterina Valfurva, Chalet Bucaneve boasts traditionally decorated apartments and studios with wooden furniture.

Free WiFi is provided. Accommodation at Bucaneve Chalet features a kitchenette, TV and balcony overlooking the garden and the mountains.

Apartments have a separate dining area. Bed linen, towels and kitchen linen are included.

Guests of Bucaneve can take advantage of a number of discounts at nearby locations.

These include the pizzeria restaurants, the thermal centers Bagni Vecchi, Bagni Nuovi and Bormio Terme, and a ski equipment shop.

The ski lift “Cevedale 2” is only 150 metres away, and parking at the property is free in the summer.

Central Valfurva is 10 km away, while Bormio can be reached in 15 minutes by car.
