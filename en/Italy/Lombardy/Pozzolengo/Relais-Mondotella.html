Description

Located on a quiet hillside in Pozzolengo, 10 km from the shores of Lake Garda, Relais Mondotella offers accommodation with free WiFi and a garden with an outdoor swimming pool.

All units feature air conditioning and a flat-screen TV, mostly with satellite channels.

Rooms include a minibar, free toiletries and a hairdryer. The daily buffet breakfast offers sweet and savoury options.

Drinks from the bar and meals from the on-site restaurant can be enjoyed out on the furnished terrace overlooking the countryside.

The country house offers outdoor and indoor play areas for children. A range of activities are available nearby, including cycling and golfing.

Desenzano del Garda is a 20-minute drive from Relais Mondotella, while Sirmione is 13 km away.
