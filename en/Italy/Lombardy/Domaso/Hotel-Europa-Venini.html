Description

Offering an outdoor pool and a private beach area, Hotel Europa Venini is located in Domaso on Lake Como.

Free WiFi access is available in all rooms. With air conditioning, each room comes with a satellite TV, fridge and a balcony.

Free toiletries are included in the private bathroom. At Hotel Europa Venini you will find a fitness centre, meeting facilities, and a games room.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including windsurfing.
