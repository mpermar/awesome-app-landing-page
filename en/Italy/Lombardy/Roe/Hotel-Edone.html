Description

Located in Roe, Hotel Edoné is 4 km from Lake Garda. It offers free Wi-Fi, a Lombard restaurant, and traditionally furnished rooms with satellite LCD TV.

Air conditioning and a minibar come as standard in the Edoné's rooms. Each is fitted with wooden furnishings and parquet flooring.

The restaurant serves pizza from a traditional wood-fired oven, along with local cuisine.

It is open for lunch and dinner, and gluten-free options are available. Brescia is 30 km from this family-run hotel.

The Gardaland theme park is a 45-minute drive away, and parking is free at the Roe Edoné.
