Description

Set in the green hills around Lake Garda, Agriturismo Lupo Bianco is 5 minutes' drive from the town of Monzambano.

It breeds its own farm animals and produces white and red wines. Rooms are air conditioned and offer views of the garden.

They include free Wi-Fi, a TV and fridge. A sweet Italian breakfast is served buffet style each morning.

Savoury items such as cold meats and cheese are available on request. Breakfast can be enjoyed in the garden during summer.

Surrounded by woods, fruit trees and olive groves, the farmhouse is 5 km from the medieval town of Borghetto, famous for its windmills.

Peschiera del Garda is 8 km away and Gardaland theme park is 20 minutes away by car.

The property sells tickets at discounted price for Gardaland, Parco Giardino Sigurt and Parco Natura Viva amusement parks.
