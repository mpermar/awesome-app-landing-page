Description

Set in the Castellaro Lagusello Nature Reserve and Mincio Park, this family-run farmhouse is about 4 km from Cavriana.

It offers a green setting with gardens and rustic rooms with views of the natural surroundings.

Rooms at Agriturismo Dondino feature a private entrance. Each one has simple furnishings combined with air conditioning and satellite TV.

Views are of the gardens. You can enjoy free bikes to explore the surroundings, and taste the local wine stored in the family's wine cellar.

Breakfast at Dondino Agriturismo is savoury and sweet. You can also sample the farm's own products such as homemade jams, bread, and cakes.
