Description

A 10-minute drive from Lake Como’s shores, Residence La Collina offers a lake-view summer pool and apartments with a patio, in the village of Vercana.

Free BBQ facilities are found in the garden. The classic-style apartments all face the property garden and Lake Como, and include a flat-screen TV, washing machine and kitchenette with dishwasher.

Guests can challenge themselves in a game of table tennis, available on site. Horse stables, a tennis pitch and golf court are 10 km from the La Collina Residence.

Colico Train Station is 15 km from this property, which is 1 hour’s drive from Como.
