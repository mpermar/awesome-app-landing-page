Description

Apartments have a contemporary design and all feature free Wi-Fi. All have a flat-screen TV with DVD player and a fully equipped kitchenette with fridge and microwave.

The property provides towels for the swimming pool. A bar and restaurant are just 1,700 feet away.

Il Giardino Di Lory is close to the Alps and the Swiss border. Menaggio is a 20 minute drive away, for boat trips across the lake to Bellagio and Varenna.
