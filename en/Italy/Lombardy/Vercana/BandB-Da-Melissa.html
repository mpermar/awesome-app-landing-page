Description

Guest rooms in the bed and breakfast are equipped with a electric tea pot.

A continental, buffet or American breakfast is served at the property.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental is available at B&B Da Melissa.

Lugano is 21 miles from the accommodation, while Como is 27 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Orio Al Serio Airport, 39 miles from B&B Da Melissa.

Children and Extra Beds
All children are welcome.
All children under 2 years are charged EUR 5 per night in a crib.
Any additional older children or adults are charged EUR 10 per night for extra beds.
The maximum number of extra beds in a room is 1.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
