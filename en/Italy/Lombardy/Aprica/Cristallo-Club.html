Description

Cristallo Club is set in a quiet area, a 5-minute walk from the centre of Aprica.

Each guest room features a panoramic balcony. Free Wi-Fi is also available throughout.

At the Cristallo Club Hotel you will find an entertainment hall and a mini club for children.

You can enjoy a mix of local and international dishes at the restaurant, which hosts a typical dinner each week.
