Description

All renovated rooms at La Mirabella are air-conditioned, and come equipped with a flat-screen TV and a minibar. Some have views of the lake, and some overlook the garden.

The villa features a reading room, plus a dining hall and porch with lake view, where a sweet Italian breakfast is served daily. The restaurant is open for dinner, while snacks are available during the day.

There is a small mooring post in front of the villa for private boats and water taxis to tour the lake. Ferries and other boats depart from the dock 500 feet away.

Guests have discounts at the golf clubs in Menaggio and Cadenabbia. Villa Carlotta Museum is also just 500 feet away, while buses to Como, Menaggio and St.Moritz stop nearby.
