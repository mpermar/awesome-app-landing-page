Description

Only 200 metres from the shore of Lake Maggiore, Albergo Paradiso provides free parking and free Wi-Fi right in the centre of Maccagno.

Featuring an outdoor pool, the property provides a free bike rental service. Rooms here come with either a private or shared bathroom, as well as a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Some rooms have a terrace and overlook the lake. Every morning you can enjoy a varied continental breakfast including sweet and savoury items.

Common spaces at the Paradiso hotel include equipped garden and terrace, as well as BBQ facilities.

Local liqueurs can be enjoyed at the bar. The hotel is 150 metres from Maccagno Train Station.
