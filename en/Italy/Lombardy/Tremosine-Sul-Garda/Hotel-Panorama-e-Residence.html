Description

Set 600 metres above Lake Garda, Hotel Panorama e Residence is in Tremosine. It offers panoramic lake views, a restaurant with pizzeria, and simply decorated rooms and apartments with satellite TV.

The outdoor pool is set in tree-lined gardens with sun loungers. Guests can relax with a drink from the bar while gazing at the lake below.

All accommodation at the Panorama comes with a private bathroom, and some rooms and apartments feature views of the lake.

The apartments also have a dining area and kitchenette. Bed linen and towels are included and changed weekly.

A daily continental breakfast is provided. The restaurant serves Lombard cuisine, prepared by the family chef.

Pizza is available at dinner. Limone sul Garda is 13 km from the hotel. Rovereto Train Station is an 60-minute drive away.
