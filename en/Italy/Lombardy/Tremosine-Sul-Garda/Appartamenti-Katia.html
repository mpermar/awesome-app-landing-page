Description

Appartamenti Katia is located in Tresimone, a 15-minute drive from the shores of Lake Garda.

Free WiFi and free parking are available. It offers apartments with kitchenette, satellite TV and bathroom.

The Katia Apartments has a garden with children’s games and a barbecue area. Guests can also hire bikes here.

An outdoor pool is available 150 metres from the main building. The Katia is 12 km from Limone sul Garda, along the SS45bis National Road.
