Description

Hotel Pineta Campi offers a beautiful hilltop position with views of Lake Garda, just a 10-minute drive from the lake shores.

It features wellness facilities, and one indoor and outdoor pool. Your morning starts with a varied breakfast buffet.

This is served on the large lake-view terrace on sunny days. Rooms at this 4-star hotel have an elegant atmosphere and feature a balcony with either lake or mountain views.

Standard facilities include an LCD TV with satellite and cable channels. Relaxing moments await you at the wellness centre, complete with a sauna, hot tub and Turkish bath.

You can also keep active and play tennis on 1 of the 12 tennis courts, or work out at the gym.

Group lessons of tennis can also be organised. Pineta Campi Hotel is less than a 10-minute drive from the lakeside town of Limone Sul Garda.
