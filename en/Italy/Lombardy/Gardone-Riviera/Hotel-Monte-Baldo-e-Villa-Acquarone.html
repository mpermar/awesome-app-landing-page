Description

Walk to the center of the city and the tourist harbor, just 1,000 feet from Hotel Monte Baldo. The staff can organize tours of the lake.

The reception and some rooms are found in Hotel Monte Baldo. Other rooms and suites are set in Villa Acquarone, the annex, which is a historic villa featuring late 19th-century furnishings and decoration.

All accommodations at Hotel Monte Baldo have private bathrooms. Modern comforts include air conditioning, a safe and satellite TV.

You can take advantage of boat docks at Hotel Monte Baldo. Relax on the hotel's private beach with a small dock and a diving board.

The sun deck surrounding the outdoor swimming pool is equipped with dressing rooms, tables, lounge chairs, deck chairs and umbrellas.

On the grounds, you will find an adult swimming pool and a children's pool. Take advantage of the free Wi-Fi and the free private parking lot.

The restaurant provides a panoramic terrace along the lakeshore where you can enjoy your meals with amazing views. Here you can savor typical meals, local specialties and fine wines.
