Description

The Castelbarco is set in a historic villa, surrounded by a large private park populated by deer.

The hotel is within the Adda Nord Regional Park, just 1. 5 km from the Trezzo sull’Adda exit of the A4 Motorway.

Breakfast is a generous buffet. Rooms are elegant and large, and some feature exposed wooden beams on the ceilings.

Each room is equipped with air conditioning, LCD TV, and free WiFi. Some rooms have a spa bath.

There is a sauna at the property. Hotel Castelbarco offers free parking, and is 18 km from Orio al Serio Airport.

Car and bicycle rentals and tours of the Crespi d’Adda area can be arranged at the 24-hour reception.
