Description

Barchi Resort is in the heart of San Felice del Benaco, a medieval village with Roman origin.

A 15-minute walk from the shores of Lake Garda, it features air-conditioned apartments and reserved underground parking, upon request.

The 2 farmhouses of Resort Barchi have been completely refurbished, and now offer apartments with fully equipped kitchenettes.

Each apartment features floor heating, and has its own table on a communal terrace.

Bed linen and towels are included. Porto San Felice beach is a pleasant 20-minute walk away, or less than 5 minutes away by car.
