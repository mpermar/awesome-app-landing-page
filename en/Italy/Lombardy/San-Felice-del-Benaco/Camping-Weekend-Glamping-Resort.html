Description

Surrounded by olive groves, Camping Weekend Glamping Resort overlooks the Bay of Sal on Lake Garda.

It comes with 2 hot tubs and 3 swimming pools with water slides. Free parking is provided.

At Camping Weekend Glamping Resort you have a choice of bungalows and mobile homes.

Each one has a patio and a fully equipped kitchenette. Entertainment is provided during high season.

Bikes are available to rent and there is a games room and table tennis on site. Drinks and wine tastings are available at the bar.

You can enjoy local specialities at the restaurant/pizzeria or shop at the mini market and bakery.

The campsite is 2 km from San Felice Del Benaco centre. The lakeshore is just 10 minutes away on foot.
