Description

Offering free access to its 2 outdoor pools, Campeggio Europa Silvella is located on the shores of Lake Garda.

It features air-conditioned accommodation, a tennis court and 5-a-side football field.

Mobile homes and apartments come with separate bedrooms, a living room and a kitchenette with dishwasher.

Each unit includes a private bathroom with shower. Campeggio Europa provides BBQ facilities and is complete with an la carte restaurant and poolside bar serving drinks and ice creams.

During summer, entertainment staff are available to organise activities. A fitness centre, soccer field, and a beach volleyball field are available.

Bikes and cars can be rented on site. The property is 2. 5 km from the centre of San Felice Del Benaco and a 15-minute drive from Sal.
