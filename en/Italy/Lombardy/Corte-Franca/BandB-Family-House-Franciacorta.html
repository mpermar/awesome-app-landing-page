Description

Guest rooms in the bed and breakfast are equipped with a electric tea pot. Rooms contain a private bathroom with a bidet and a hair dryer. All guest rooms at B&B Family House Franciacorta feature air conditioning and a desk.

A continental breakfast is served daily at the property.

Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Corte Franca, like cycling.

Bergamo is 16 miles from B&B Family House Franciacorta, while Brescia is 13 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Orio Al Serio Airport, 14 miles from the property.
