Description

Alp Wellness Mota is located in the centre of Livigno, a pedestrian-only, duty-free area.

The spa includes a heated indoor pool, a salt-water hot tub, and Möta Kneipp facilities.

Wi-Fi and parking are free. All spacious rooms at the Alp Wellness have a classic alpine style and a balcony.

They are well equipped and feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, and tea/coffee making facilities.

Bathrobes and slippers are provided. The wellness centre offers a range of relaxation and beauty facilities, including the Sun & Tan solarium, a choice of saunas, and a luxurious sensory shower.

Throughout the hotel, the latest Klimahaus energy-saving techniques are used. A ski storage room and ski pass purchase service are both found on site.

You can ski directly onto the slopes from the hotel's entrance, and the Tagliede cable car is just a 5 minutes' drive away.
