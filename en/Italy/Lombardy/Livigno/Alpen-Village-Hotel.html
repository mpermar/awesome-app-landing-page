Description

Offering panoramic views of Livigno, Alpen Village Hotel is next to Teola Pianoni Bassi cable car, linking to Mottolino ski slopes.

It offers a restaurant, a spa with indoor pool and sauna, and free WiFi. Free buses to Livigno centre run every 15 minutes and stop 50 metres from the hotel.

Ski storage is provided in the winter, and there is an on-site ski school. Rooms have a satellite TV, simple furnishings and a private bathroom with hairdryer and free toiletries.

Some rooms feature a balcony. Guests can rent bikes from the reception or take part in guided walks organized by the hotel.

There is a children's playground and a fully equipped fitness centre. Two internet stations are available on site.

Larix Outdoor Adventure Park is a 5-minute walk from Hotel Alpen Village. The UNESCO-protected Bernina Railway is 22 km away.
