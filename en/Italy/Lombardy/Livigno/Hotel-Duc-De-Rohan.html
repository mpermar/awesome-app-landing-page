Description

Featuring panoramic terrace and a restaurant Hotel Duc De Rohan is located 250 metres from Mottolino ski lift and 900 metres from Livigno centre.

Rooms are spacious and have a characteristic Alpine style with wood floors and free WiFi.

Each room features a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with a shower and toiletries.

Some have a balcony with a view of the mountains. Guests can relax at the hotel‚Äôs bar and on the terrace, which offers views of the Alps.

There is a heated ski deposit and the TV lounge features a play area for children and a widescreen TV for cinema evenings or games on the Wii.

The restaurant of this family-run property serves a rich breakfast including homemade cakes, omelettes and crepes as well as hot drinks.

For lunch and dinner pizza, as well as meat and fish dishes are available. Providing free parking, the Duc De Rohan is 2 km from the Carosello 3000 ski area and 45 minutes' drive from Terme di Bormio thermal spa.
