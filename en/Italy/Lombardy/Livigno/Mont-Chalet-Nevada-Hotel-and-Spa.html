Description

Located just 50 metres from Livigno cross country skiing tracks and 1 km from the Carosello 3000 cable car, Mont Chalet Nevada - Hotel & Spa offers a restaurant, snack bar and private parking.

WiFi is free throughout and gluten free meals are available. Featuring carpeted floors and simple dcor, rooms at the Mont Chalet Nevada - Hotel & Spa include a safe, flat-screen satellite TV and work desk.

Each bathroom comes with a shower, hairdryer and free toiletries. In summer, the area is ideal for cycling tours and for Nordic walking.
