Description

Breakfast at this hotel is an extensive buffet of homemade food,served outside in the courtyard in the summer months. On request, a dinner of specialties from the Lombardy region is available.

Petronilla Hotel features a sauna, a hot tub, a small meeting room and a gym. The staff is available 24 hours a day and can recommend the best places to visit during your stay.

Hotel Petronilla is just a few steps from Bergamo's main shopping district and public buses stop 300 feet away. Bergamo Train Station is a 15-minute walk away.
