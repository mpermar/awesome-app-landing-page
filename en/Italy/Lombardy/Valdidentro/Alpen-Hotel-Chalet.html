Description

Set in Valdidentro and surrounded by nature, Alpen Hotel Chalet offers modern themed accommodation with traditional features including wood floors and natural slate bathrooms.

Each accommodation at Alpen Hotel has a unique theme. All rooms come with free Wi-Fi, a minibar and a flat-screen TV.

The suites and apartments have fully equipped kitchenettes. Breakfast and dinner are served in the dining hall with floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the old farmyard.

Meals are prepared using organic healthy ingredients. Set in the heart of Valtellina, the Chalet is 10 minutes' drive from Bormio and Stelvio National Park.
