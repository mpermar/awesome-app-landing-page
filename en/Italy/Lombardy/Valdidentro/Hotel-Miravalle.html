Description

Surrounded by mountains in central Valdidentro, Hotel Miravalle is 2 km from the San Colombano ski runs.

It offers free access to the spa including a hot tub and sauna. Wi-Fi is free throughout.

All rooms feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries.

They have modern light wood Alpine-style furniture. Most rooms have a balcony. A buffet-style breakfast including homemade cakes and cold cuts is served daily.

The restaurant prepares both a local and classic Italian dishes. Special menus can be arranged on request.

Guests at the family-run Miravalle hotel can enjoy a match of table soccer and ping pong or relax in the wellness centre.

They can also go on hiking and biking tours from the nearby park. A public ski bus stop is a 2-minute walk from the hotel, while the Stelvio National Park and Lake Cancano are both 3 km away.

Bormio, famous for its thermal baths, is 10 km away. Guests enjoy discounted rates at Bormio thermal spas.
