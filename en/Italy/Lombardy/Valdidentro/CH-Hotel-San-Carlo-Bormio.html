Description

Hotel San Carlo is set in San Carlo, Valdidentro, a quiet area located 8 km from the Stelvio National Park.

The property is a 20-minute drive from Bormio and 23 km from Livigno. Featuring panoramic views of the surrounding natural landscape, rooms at the San Carlo offer simple décor, a satellite TV and free WiFi.

Most rooms come with a balcony. Guests can enjoy the on-site pizzeria restaurant offering both traditional and modern cuisine.

Other common areas include a lounge where you can read or watch TV, an on-site bar and a playroom for children.

This family-owned hotel is ideally located for a holiday in any season. In winter, you can enjoy some of the best ski slopes in Alta Valtellina.

During the warmer months, you can spend time trekking and cycling in the natural park.
