Description

Featuring free WiFi, free private parking and a games room, Hotel Woodhouse offers rooms a few steps from Cinisello Balsamo town centre.

Bikes can be rented on site. The Cinisello Balsamo Nord exit on the A52 motorway is 1.

7 km away. All rooms are equipped with a desk and offer city views. The bathroom is either shared or private.

Soap, slippers, a hairdryer and electric kettle are also available upon request. Woodhouse Hotel is 3 km from Parco Nord park.

Milan is a 15-minute drive away, while Linate Airport can be reached by car in about 20 minutes.
