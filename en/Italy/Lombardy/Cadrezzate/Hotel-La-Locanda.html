Description

Set in the centre of Cadrezzate, overlooking Lake Monate, Hotel La Locanda is 1 km from the Euratom JRC Research Centre.

It offers air-conditioned rooms with free internet access, modern decor and a satellite TV with Sky channels.

Hotel La Locanda is 20 km from Milan Malpensa Airport. It is 3 km from the town of Ispra on the shore of Lake Maggiore and 5 minutes' drive from Golf dei Laghi Golf Club.

Travedona-Monate Train Station, with connections to Milan, is 2 km from the property.

Rho Fiera is a 45-minute drive away. Guests can enjoy swimming in peaceful Lake Monate, which is free from motorboats.

Your room comes with a safe, work desk and a private bathroom with hairdryer. A continental breakfast is served in the breakfast room.
