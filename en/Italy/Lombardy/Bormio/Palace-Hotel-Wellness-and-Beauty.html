Description

Palace Hotel Wellness & Beauty is in Bormio, just 500 metres from Bormio 2000 cable car.

Its rooms are provided with satellite TVs including Sky Channels and minibars. The Palace Hotel has a wellness and beauty centre, including a large heated indoor pool, where you can treat yourself to massages, saunas, hot tubs, revitalising showers and much more.

Tennis courts are also available and the fitness centre is newly built. Great for après-ski, at the Hotel Wellness & Beauty you can sit by the open fire in the bar and enjoy anything from cocktails to hot chocolate.

The hotel’s restaurant offers a choice of traditional local specialities and international dishes and boasts an impressive selection of wines.

To add to its great services, the hotel also offers private shuttle rides to the slopes and as a guest at the Palace you will be entitled to special discounts at Bormio’s famous spa resort.
