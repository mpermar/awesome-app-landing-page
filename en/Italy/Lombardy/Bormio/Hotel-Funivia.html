Description

Hotel Funivia is 200 meters from the cable lift , just outside the limits of Stelvio Natural Park and a 5-minute walk from the city centre.

At the Funivia guests will enjoy a wellness centre, a restaurant and spacious rooms with mountain views.

Featuring Alpine-style décor, all rooms come with a satellite TV and a private bathroom.

Some have a balcony. A buffet breakfast is available daily, including both sweet and savoury items.

Funivia Hotel also has its own restaurant overlooking the slopes, serving traditional specialities of the region accompanied by wines of Valtellina.

The wellness area includes fitness facilities, an indoor pool, sauna and hammam. You can also play tennis at the property.
