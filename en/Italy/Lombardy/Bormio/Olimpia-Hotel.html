Description

This typical mountain hotel is set 50 metres away from the slopes of the Ski World Cup.

The Olimpia features a fully equipped wellness centre, free Wi-Fi and free parking.

Hotel Olimpia offers modern rooms with parquet floors, a bathroom with bath or shower, and satellite TV.

Some rooms come with a balcony. A self-catering apartment with 2 bedrooms is also available.

The property features a wellness centre, available at a surcharge and upon reservation, including facilities such as a sauna, Turkish bath, hydromassage baths, emotional shower and Kneipp path.

The hotel also features a bar and a typical wine cellar. Guests can enjoy discounted rates at the Keller Steakhouse partner restaurant, located next to the hotel, and at the Heaven 3000, located in a nearby ski area.

The Olimpia is only a 5-minute walk from the centre of Bormio. The property, which is at the edge of Stelvio National Park, is a 50-minute drive from Livigno.
