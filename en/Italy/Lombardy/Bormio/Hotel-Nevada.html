Description

Next to the cable car for Bormio 2000 ski area, Hotel Nevada features a wellness centre and free WiFi, just 5 minutes’ walk from Bormio town centre.

The Nevada’s restaurant serves regional and typical Italian dishes. Guests can relax in the lounge area while looking onto the extensive gardens.

Here there is also a terrace where you can enjoy your evening meal or a barbecue in the summer months.

The Stelvio National Park is ideal for sports and nature lovers- offering seasonal hiking, mountain biking, and skiing.

Some rooms at Hotel Nevada have a balcony with mountain views. All are equipped with TV, minibar and private bathrooms, some with a spa bath.
