Description

Agriturismo Rini is committed to preserving the tradition and lifestyle of farming.

It offers Alpine-style accommodation and organic food, just 900 metres from the Bormio 2000 ski lifts.

A free shuttle is available to the ski lift upon request. Rooms and apartments at the Rini come with balconies overlooking Mount Vallecetta or Mount Reit.

Breakfast includes milk and yoghurt freshly made on site. Accommodation at the Rini Agriturismo features with wood floors and a TV with satellite channels.

The apartments have a fully equipped kitchenette with dishwasher. The Rini serves homemade dishes for dinner.
