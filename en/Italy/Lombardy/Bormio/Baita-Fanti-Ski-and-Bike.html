Description

Located just outside Bormio's city centre, Baita Fanti Ski & Bike offers accommodation with a garden and a bar, and free WiFi.

The property is within a 10-minute drive of Terme di Bormio - Bagni Vecchi and Terme di Bormio - Bagni Nuovi, while Bormio Spa - Bormio Terme Spa is a 15-minute walk away.

At the hotel, each room includes a desk and a flat-screen TV. The private bathroom is fitted with a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

An Italian breakfast is available daily at Baita Fanti Ski & Bike. Skiing is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.
