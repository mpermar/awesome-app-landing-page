Description

This 4-star hotel is located in a quiet area, a 5-minute drive from Vigevano centre.

It provides an airport shuttle, free parking and a beauty and wellness centre. Hotel Parco and Residence’s rooms are spacious and soundproofed.

They all come with satellite TV, minibar, air conditioning and free wired internet connection.

Free Wi-Fi connection is provided in all public areas. Parco and Residence Hotel’s wellness facilities include a fitness area and the BB Club, which offers a Turkish bath, a Finnish sauna, a hot tub, massages and beauty treatments.

Set in the ancient farmhouse, Trattoria Podazzera restaurant serves traditional cuisine for lunch and dinner.

Outdoor dining is possible on Podazzera’s old porch. The hotel is set at the entrance of Vigevano, in the Ticino Park area, near the SS494 access road.

Main sites like the Sforza Castle with Bramante’s Tower, Piazza Ducale and the Vigevano Cathedral are around a 5-minute drive away.
