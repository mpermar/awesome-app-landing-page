Description

With a quiet and green location in Ponte Di Legno, Chalet Presanella has a garden, free bike hire and free Wi-Fi.

It offers Alpine-style apartments with views of the Adamello and Stelvio Mountains.

Alpine-style apartments at the Presanella come with a kitchen, bathroom and satellite TV.

The area surrounding Presanella is ideal for hiking and cycling tours. In addition, you will find free ski storage, and ski passes can be purchased at discounted rates at reception.

Free on-site parking is provided as well as a free garage for bikes. The Temù-Ponte di Legno-Tonale Ski lifts are 700 metres from the property.
