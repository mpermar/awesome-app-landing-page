Description

At the Lenno, you can spend relaxing moments on the lake-view terrace or by the the swimming pool. There is also a sauna.

Lenno Hotel is right in front of the wharf and is provided with an indoor garage, available at convenient rates.

You can visit Como by boat and return to the hotel in the same day, or take the funicular train up to Brunate and admire the lake from above. Multilingual staff can provide tourist information.

At Hotel Lenno, you can taste typical Italian dishes and prestigious wines, enjoying an enchanting view of the lake.

Some rooms offer a lake view, and other rooms are spacious attics.
