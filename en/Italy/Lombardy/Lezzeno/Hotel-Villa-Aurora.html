Description

The hotel includes an early 20th-century property and a lake-view villa built in 2009. It combines tradition and modernity with elegant furnishings and all the latest facilities, including free Wi-Fi.

The Aurora Hotel has 2 bars and an attractive breakfast room. You have free access to the beach with lounge chairs and sun umbrellas. Canoes, kayaks and bikes are available free of charge.

Dining at the restaurant on the terrace, you can admire the view of Comacina Island while enjoying fresh-fish specialties.
