Description

Set in the hill town of Fonteno, Esprit D'Hotel Panoramico offers a terrace with sun loungers overlooking Lake Iseo.

It features a hot tub, gym and a children's playground. Rooms at Esprit have tiled floors, a TV with satellite channels, and free Wi-Fi.

Many have a balcony and a lake view. D'Hotel Panoramico has a peaceful location and is surrounded by a park.

It is just one kilometre from Fonteno Valley where you can visit many picturesque villages and caves.
