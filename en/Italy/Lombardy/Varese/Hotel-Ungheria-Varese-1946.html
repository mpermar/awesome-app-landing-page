Description

The Hotel Ungheria is an environmental family-run hotel offering bike rental and free parking, just 10 minutes' drive from Varese city centre.

Rooms have satellite TV, WiFi and air conditioning. The Ungheria has received the European Eco-label certification and promotes environmentally sustainable tourism.

Every morning the breakfast of fresh fruit salad and pastries is served in the breakfast room.

The Ungheria Hotel is just 800 metres from the A8 motorway with connections to Milan where the Expo 2015 takes place.

Varese railway station is at 1 km distance, while Malpensa Airport is 25 km away.

On arrival, guests will receive a free welcome card with discounts on museum entry, shopping centres and sports facilities.
