Description

The Swallows By Francesco Di Assisi is in the heart of the Umbria Valley, 15-minute drive from Assisi and Spello.

It offers rustic apartments with free Wi-Fi, LCD TV, and balcony or patio. Bike hire is also free and Perugia is 23 km away.

In Agriturismo Le Rondini Di Francesco Di Assisi' gardens you will find a swimming pool with a hydromassage area, plus a sun terrace.

The gardens also feature a children's playground and free parking. Agriturismo Le Rondini has a reception and lounge area, where you will find an exhibition of local products.

You will receive a welcome kit featuring basic items such as milk and tea on arrival.

Breakfast is available on request and includes genuine ingredients such as fresh bread, fruit and croissants.
