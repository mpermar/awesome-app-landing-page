Description

The property is just 8.1 mi from Assisi, the home town of Saint Francis, and features the sober elegance of a Franciscan convent.

An ideal fusion of ancient and modern styles, rustic and refined details, the rooms provide views on the surrounding nature, and are elegantly fitted with antique furniture.

Enjoy dining on the large terrace, where you can appreciate a view that inspired nonetheless but Dante Alighieri.
