Description

Terra Umbra Hotel is set in the green Umbria countryside between Terni and Narni, both 15 minutes' drive away.

There is a large pool with a hydromasage area in the gardens. You can enjoy the quiet at Terra Umbra or play sports instead.

There is a beach volleyball court here, a football pitch and a gym with sauna. Terra Umbra's rooms are air conditioned and have a satellite TV and minibar.

There is also a large-screen TV room and free Wi-Fi throughout. Plenty of free parking is available at Hotel Terra Umbra.

Drinks are served at the bar and there is a restaurant next door offering traditional Umbrian dishes.
