Description

Featuring a garden and 2 seasonal outdoor pools, Il Rosciolo has a restaurant serving local cuisine.

The property is a 5-minute drive from the centre of Piccione, and it is surrounded by olive and fruit trees.

Featuring a private bathroom with a shower and bidet, the rooms have views of the garden.

They are equipped with free Wi-Fi, heating and wardrobe. Guests at Il Rosciolo can enjoy an Italian breakfast buffet with coffee, tea and milk.

It is possible to taste a variety of local and homemade products at the restaurant.
