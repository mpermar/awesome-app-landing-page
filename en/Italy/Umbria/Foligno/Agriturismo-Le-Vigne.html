Description

Surrounded by olive groves, 7 km from Foligno, Agriturismo Le Vigne is a working farm in the Umbria hills.

It offers a traditional restaurant, a pool and country-style rooms with a satellite flat-screen TV.

Rooms at Le Vigne have exposed-beam ceilings and terracotta floors. Each comes with free Wi-Fi, together with a hairdryer in the private bathroom.

The daily buffet breakfast includes eggs, ham, and homemade cakes. The restaurant is open for both lunch and dinner and specialises in local cuisine.

Guests can also buy Extra Virgin olive oil produced on the farm. Assisi is 23 km from the property.

A free shuttle service from Foligno Train Station is provided. Perugia San Egidio Airport is a 30-minute drive away.
