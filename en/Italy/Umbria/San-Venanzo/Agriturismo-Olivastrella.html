Description

Guest rooms in the farm stay are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Featuring a private bathroom with a bidet and free toiletries, some rooms at Agriturismo Olivastrella also offer a pool view. All rooms at the accommodation include air conditioning and a closet.

A buffet breakfast is served each morning at the property.

You can play ping pong at Agriturismo Olivastrella.

Assisi is 23 miles from the farm stay. The nearest airport is Sant'Egidio Airport, 21 miles from the property.
