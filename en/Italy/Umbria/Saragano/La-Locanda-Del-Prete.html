Description

La Locanda Del Prete is a resort surrounded by the Umbrian countryside, in the medieval village of Saragano Gualdo Cattaneo, It features an outdoor pool overlooking the landscape.

Each apartment at Locanda Del Prete has a cosy atmosphere created by light colours and wood furniture.

All apartments feature a kitchen with dining area and a living room with a flat-screen TV.

The property’s restaurant serves Italian cuisine on its terrace with panoramic views.

La Locanda Del Prete is a 15-minute drive from the E45 state road. Perugia can be reached by car in 30 minutes.
