Description

Each room in this 18th-century villa is unique and some feature original frescoes on the ceilings. Your room is equipped with either a fan or air conditioning.

Views from Ca' De' Principi - Residenza D'epoca include the surrounding forests and medieval architecture.

You can enjoy a wide selection of local wines and homemade pasta dishes in the hotel's restaurant.

Free parking is available in the neighborhood. Lake Trasimeno is just a 30-minute drive away. A number of interesting villages in the Umbria region are also easily reached by car.
