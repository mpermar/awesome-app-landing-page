Description

Featuring an infinity pool, Domus Umbra is set in the Umbrian hills. Guest can enjoy the panorama from the garden, which also has a BBQ.

With free Wi-Fi access, studios all come with a flat-screen TV and a kitchenette equipped with kitchenware, a stove and refrigerator.

The private bathroom has free toiletries. This farm house produces its own olive oil.

An Italian breakfast, with hot drinks, and home-made jam and cakes, is served daily.
