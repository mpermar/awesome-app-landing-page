Description

Surrounded by a 30. 000 mq. garden, Il Casale di Monica boasts a public swimming pool, open from 1 June to 31 August, with hydromassage corner, a picnic area with BBQ, and sports facilities.

It is 5 km from Assisi centre. Overlooking Mount Subasio and the quiet countryside, this newly built property offers self-catering apartments.

A playground, suitable for children, table football and flipper are also available.

A private patio or terrace is included in each apartment. With rustic décor and tiled floors, each unit comes with 1 or 2 bedrooms, a living room with kitchenette, and 2 bathrooms.

The Agriturismo Il Casale di Monica is 2. 5 km from Rivotorto and 6 km from the Saint Francis of Assisi Basilica.
