Description

Set in the Umbrian countryside, Farmhouse Casale Delle Lucrezie is 3 km outside Todi.

Dating back to the 16th century, this stone farmhouse now offers an outdoor pool and modern wellness centre.

Casale Delle Lucrezie offers rustic-style accommodation with wood-beamed ceilings and tiled floors.

All rooms and apartments are air conditioned and come with a TV, fridge and a view of Todi or the surrounding countryside.

Apartments also have a kitchenette. A sweet Italian breakfast is served each morning and includes a selection of homemade cakes.

A continental breakfast is available upon request. The wellness centre includes a sauna, hot tub, Jacuzzi, steam bath and a relax area.

There is also a gym and a games room with table football and a BBQ in the garden.

Spa services are available on request. A car is recommended to all guests and free parking is provided.
