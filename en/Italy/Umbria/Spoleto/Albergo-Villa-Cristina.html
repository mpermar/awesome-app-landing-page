Description

Offering panoramic views across the Umbrian countryside, Villa Cristina is a 15-minute walk from Spoleto's historic centre.

It offers free parking, and a terrace where you can enjoy breakfast. Rooms at Albergo Villa Cristina come with free Wi-Fi, a TV, and a private bathroom.

Some rooms have private entrances, while others have countryside views. Villa Cristina dates back to the 18th-century and is now a family-run hotel.

At breakfast you can enjoy honey produced on site, and you can also visit the hives.

The cathedral, Duomo di Spoleto, is 2 km away. A shuttle service is available to and from Spoleto Railway Station on request.
