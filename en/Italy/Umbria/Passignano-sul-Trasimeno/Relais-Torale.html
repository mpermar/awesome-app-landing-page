Description

At the resort, rooms come with a closet and a flat-screen TV. Certain rooms come with a kitchenette with a dishwasher. All guest rooms will provide guests with an oven.

You can play ping pong at Relais Torale, and the area is popular for cycling.

Assisi is 27 miles from the accommodation, while Perugia is 16 miles from the property. Sant'Egidio Airport is 21 miles away.

Would come back!

"The farmhouse has fantastic view of the lake, surrounded by beautiful nature and olive farm. The pool with panoramic view of the lake is great for families. The grounds are well-maintained. The apartment has a fully-kitted kitchen, and the washing machine is particularly handy for families. The apartment is spacious. The set-up of the apartment is comfortable with good beds and sofa beds. Not luxury, but very comfortable. Roberto is very friendly and timely in answering all the questions. The level of guest occupancy was also well-managed, it wasn't over-crowded at all. The location is accessible to many major sights and historic towns in Umbria and Tuscany by car (but you definitely need a car to stay here). Two local supermarkets are within 10 minutes drive away."
