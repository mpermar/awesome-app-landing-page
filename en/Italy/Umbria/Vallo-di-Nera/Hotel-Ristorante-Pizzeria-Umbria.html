Description

Located in the Valnerina valley, Hotel Ristorante Pizzeria Umbria is a 15-minute drive from Spoleto.

It has free Wi-Fi, a restaurant, and air-conditioned rooms with an LCD TV. With whitewashed walls and ivory-coloured tiled floors, rooms at the Umbria Hotel Ristorante are decorated in a simple Mediterranean style.

Each comes with a private bathroom and some have a balcony. The restaurant serves both classic Italian and international dishes.

A buffet-style breakfast is served daily and it includes sweet pastries, locally produced cheese and cold cuts, and omelettes, together with cappuccino, coffee or fruit juice.

Norcia, in the Monti Sibillini National Park, is 26 km from the hotel. Assisi is a 50-minute drive away and Cascia is 30 km from the property.
