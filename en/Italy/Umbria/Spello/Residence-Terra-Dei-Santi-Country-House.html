Description

Residence Terra Dei Santi Country House enjoys a peaceful location, 1. 5 km from the centre of Spello, and features an outdoor swimming pool and barbecue facilities.

The property offers large air-conditioned apartments, and free bike hire. All accommodation at Residence Terra Dei Santi Country House has traditional terracotta floors.

Apartments feature a 26" flat-screen TV, a kitchen and furnished private patio. WiFi is free at reception and in the apartments.

A picturesque cycle path linking Spoleto and Assisi passes next to the residence.

Assisi is 15 km to the north, and it is a 30-minute drive to Perugia. The Spello SS75 National Road exit is only 850 metres away.
