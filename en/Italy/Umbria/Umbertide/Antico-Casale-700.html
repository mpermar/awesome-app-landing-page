Description

Each unit is comprised of a kitchen with a dining area, a coffee machine and a fridge, a living room, and a private bathroom with free toiletries. A stovetop and electric tea pot are also available.

Guests at the vacation home can enjoy an Italian breakfast.

Antico Casale 700 has a grill.

A bicycle rental service is available at the accommodation.

Assisi is 22 miles from Antico Casale 700. Sant'Egidio Airport is 17 miles from the property.
