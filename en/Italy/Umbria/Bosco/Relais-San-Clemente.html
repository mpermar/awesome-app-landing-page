Description

Originally a church, this impressive property is just 20 minutes from Perugia by train. You will find horseback riding stables and an 18-hole golf course, both a 5-minute drive away.

Relais San Clemente features a game room with billiards and ping-pong, plus all of the outdoor facilities. Wi-Fi access is available throughout the entire building.

During the summer the poolside bar provides drinks throughout the day. In the evening you can relax in the piano bar after dining on fine Umbrian cuisine at the restaurant.
