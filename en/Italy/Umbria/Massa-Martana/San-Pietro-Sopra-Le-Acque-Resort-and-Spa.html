Description

Set in Massa Martana, around 20 km from Todi, San Pietro Sopra Le Acque Resort & Spa is immersed in the Umbrian countryside.

It offers panoramic views, and a wellness and fitness centre. Residenza San Pietro Sopra Le Acque boasts air-conditioned rooms featuring satellite TV with Sky channels, minibar and free internet.

All rooms, former monks' cells, offer views of the internal cloisters, the swimming pool, or the valley.

The outdoor swimming pool at the propeprty is set in the garden and offers sun loungers and umbrellas.

The modern wellness centre has been completely renovated and uses the latest innovative therapies.

It includes a gym, Turkish bath, heated swimming pool, sauna and much more. A variety of massages and beauty treatments are available.

Residenza San Pietro’s Antica Fonte restaurant serves Umbrian cuisine based on fresh typical ingredients.

Offering free parking, the residence is located halfway between Perugia and Terni, both reachable in approximately 30 minutes by car.
