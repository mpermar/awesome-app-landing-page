Description

Set in a renovated villa, a 10-minute walk from the centre of Cascia, Hotel Villa Stella offers classic rooms.

It also features a restaurant with local cuisine and a fully equipped garden. With a TV and desk, the rooms come with a safe, mini fridge and wooden or tiled floors.

The bathroom is complete with a hairdryer and free toiletries. Some rooms have a town view.

Wi-Fi throughout is free. In summer you can swim for free in the salt-water outdoor pool.

Breakfast at the Villa Stella consists of sweet and savoury items. It may also include homemade products.

The restaurant serves traditional homemade dishes. The city of Spoleto is 40 km away and Norcia is 18 from this property.
