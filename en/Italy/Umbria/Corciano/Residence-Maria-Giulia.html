Description

Residence Maria Giulia is a restored Umbrian farmhouse set in the hills 11 km from Perugia.

It offers a pool with jacuzzi area, and spacious studios and apartments with private entrances.

The accommodation at the Maria Giulia is decorated in a country style with exposed-stone walls and terracotta flooring.

Each comes with a kitchenette, private bathroom and air conditioning, and free Wi-Fi is available at reception.

The property has a children’s playground and ping pong table. A barbecue area is available for relaxed outdoor dining.

The motorway junction Perugia-A1 motorway is 500 meters from the Maria Giulia Residence.
