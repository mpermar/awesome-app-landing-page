Description

Free Wi-Fi, satellite TV and air conditioning are standard in all rooms at Le Rocce.

The hotel is 10 minutes’ drive from Lake Trasimeno and 17 km from Perugia. The rooms feature a private bathroom with hairdryer and toiletries.

They have classic-style décor and cool tiled floors. Rooms with balcony are available upon request.

Country Hotel Le Rocce offers an outdoor swimming pool with furnished sun terrace.

Other on-site facilities include a tennis court, bowling green and archery. The garden also provides barbecue facilities.

A continental breakfast with both sweet and savoury ingredients is served every morning.

During summer time, the restaurant serves dinner on its terrace. Drinks can be enjoyed at the bar.
