Description

Fortebraccio is a renovated country home outside the medieval city walls of Montone, near Perugia.

It offers panoramic views of the countryside, an outdoor pool with hot tub, and free parking.

Each of Fortebraccio's bright rooms features en suite facilities and satellite TV.

Free Wi-Fi is available in the entire building. The Fortebraccio's Gonfalone restaurant serves typical Umbrian cuisine in an elegant dining room or out on the covered terrace overlooking the Umbrian countryside and hills.

An airport shuttle service is available at the Fortebraccio. The E45 motorway is just a few km away and can easily connect you to Lake Trasimeno and the cities of Assisi, Perugia and Arezzo.
