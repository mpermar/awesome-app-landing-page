Description

Set in Monti Sibillini National Park, FonteAntica Agriturismo is 10 km outside Norcia and ideal for visiting the Valnerina.

Built in the 1700s, this family-run property offers rooms with antique furniture.

FonteAntica is a charming farmhouse featuring a garden with children's playground and relaxation area.

You will also find a games room, library and games such as bowls and ping pong. There also is a wellness area featuring a sauna overlooking the valley and a Kneipp path.

The spacious rooms all have a private bathroom with hairdryer. They offer original 18th-century furniture and tiled floors.

A sweet and savoury breakfast is served each morning. The restaurant is set in the old barn and serves produce both homemade and from the local area.

Set in Campi, the property is surrounded by nature. You can arrange trekking and rafting excursions, nature trips and river sports on site.
