Description

Hotel Vega Perugia is situated in 2 hectares of grounds in the beautiful Umbrian countryside between Assisi and Perugia.

Cool off in the outdoor pool in this peaceful location. Wi-Fi is free throughout.

The swimming pool at Hotel Vega comes fully equipped with showers and sun loungers and there is also a poolside bar.

Enjoy a walk around the hotel’s shady gardens or rent one of the bikes available on site and head off to explore the surrounding countryside.

Tennis courts and horse-riding stables are located nearby. Tourism is easy from here.

Hotel Vega is close to Sant’Egidio Airport and a railway station, and good road links mean that Perugia town centre is only 10 minutes’ by car.
