Description

Offering a terrace, Le Torri Apartment is set in Perugia, 500 metres from Perugia Train Station.

Free private parking and high speed internet connection are available on site. Apartments are complete with a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen/kitchenette, air conditioning and a private bathroom.

San Severo Church is 1. 5 km from Le Torri Apartment, while Corso Vannucci is 1. 1 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Perugia San Francesco d'Assisi Airport, 10 km from Le Torri Apartment.
