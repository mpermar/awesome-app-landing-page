Description

Guest rooms in the farm stay are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Rooms come with a private bathroom and a bidet, and certain units at Agriturismo Beata Vanna have a balcony. The rooms at the accommodation feature air conditioning and a desk.

Continental and buffet breakfast options are available each morning at Agriturismo Beata Vanna.

The area is popular for cycling and hiking, and car rental is available at the farm stay.

Speaking Italian and English at the 24-hour front desk, staff are always on hand to help.

Assisi is 31 miles from Agriturismo Beata Vanna, while Perugia is 23 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Sant'Egidio Airport, 27 miles from the accommodation.
