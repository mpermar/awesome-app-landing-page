Description

At the resort, rooms come with a closet, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

Guests at Borgo dei Sapori can enjoy a continental or a buffet breakfast.

Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.

For guests' convenience, Borgo dei Sapori has a business center with printing, copying and fax services.

Perugia is 27 miles from the resort. The nearest airport is Sant'Egidio Airport, 31 miles from Borgo dei Sapori.
