Description

Hotel Catignano is located a 5-minute drive from the centre of Gubbio and has views of the surrounding hills.

It offers free parking and a garden with swimming pool. WiFi is free throughout. Catignano's rooms are comfortable and come with satellite TV, mini fridge and and a private bathroom.

The restaurant offers a rich buffet breakfast and serves local cuisine at lunch and dinner.

The hotel is a 20-minute drive from Gubbio Train Station and 45 km from Perugia Airport.
