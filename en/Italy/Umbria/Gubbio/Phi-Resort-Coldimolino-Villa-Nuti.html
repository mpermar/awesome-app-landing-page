Description

Coldimolino offers rooms and apartments 10 minutes' drive from Gubbio's medieval centre.

There is a panoramic pool and you can play tennis or football here. Bike rental available.

Coldimolino is made up of a country house with refurbished apartments and Villa Nuti, an 18th-century building which houses comfortable suites, some with a hydromassage bath or shower.

All have pretty views of the Umbria countryside. There is a chapel on site and children will find a play area in the gardens.

Internet access is available. Coldimolino Country Houses' restaurant serves traditional dishes with seasonal ingredients.
