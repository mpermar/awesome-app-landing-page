Description

Lo Spedalicchio overlooks the main square of Ospedalicchio, a pleasant Umbrian village, near Assisi and Perugia.

The hotel is set in a restored medieval fortress. Enjoy a period feeling in a comfortable establishment, surrounded by the peaceful Umbrian landscape.

Lo Spedalicchio provides warm, welcoming accommodation. The hotel's elegant restaurant features local specialities including homemade pasta and biscuits.

You can taste typical regional produce such as truffles, porcini (mushrooms), Castelluccio lentils and Norcia ham.

The hotel offers rooms with modern facilities, including free cabled Internet and a TV.

Some rooms are located in the annex opposite the hotel. Enjoy free Wi-Fi in the lobby, a free internet point, and a free private and large car park.
