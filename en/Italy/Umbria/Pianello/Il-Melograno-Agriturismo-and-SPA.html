Description

Surrounded by olive trees and fields, Il Melograno Agriturismo & SPA is located in Pianello, 12 km from Perugia International Airport and a 20-minute drive from Perugia.

It features a seasonal outdoor pool and free Wi-Fi. The colourful, air conditioned rooms come with a wooden beamed ceiling and offer a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

A continental breakfast buffet is served in the common room. Il Melograno’s restaurant includes a bar and serves traditional cuisine from the region.

Guests of Il Melograno Agriturismo & SPA have access to a garden with a playground for children.
