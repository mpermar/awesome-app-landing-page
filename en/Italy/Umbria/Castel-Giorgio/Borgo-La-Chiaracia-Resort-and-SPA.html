Description

Borgo La Chiaracia Resort & SPA has a restaurant, outdoor swimming pool, a bar and shared lounge in Castel Giorgio.

With a garden, the 5-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom.

The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.

At the hotel, the rooms are fitted with a desk and a flat-screen TV. All rooms have a wardrobe.

Borgo La Chiaracia Resort & SPA offers a continental or buffet breakfast. The accommodation features a spa and wellness centre which includes an indoor pool, a hot tub and a sauna.

Orvieto is 19 km from Borgo La Chiaracia Resort & SPA, while Viterbo is 43 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Perugia San Francesco d'Assisi, 96 km from the hotel, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
