Description

Set next to a centuries-old pinewood in the Tasso Nature Reserve, this hotel is in Camigliatello Silano’s centre.

It offers classic rooms with a balcony, most overlooking Monte Curcio. Located at an altitude of 1,300 metres above sea level, Hotel Tasso is 700 metres from the Camigliatello ski slopes and from the bus stop to/from Cosenza.

Lago Cecita is a 10-minute drive away. The restaurant serves Italian cuisine and local dishes from Calabria.

Various mushroom specialities are served in October, in occasion of the Mushroom Festival.

Gluten-free meals are also available. The Tasso Hotel offers free Wi-Fi throughout and free private parking.

Guests can relax in the common TV room or on the sun terrace, furnished with tables and chairs.
