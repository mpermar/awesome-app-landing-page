Description

Immersed in parkland, Hotel Parco dei Principi is 150 metres from the blue flag beach and 3.

5 km from Roccella Jonica town centre. In the summer months, the Parco dei Principi offers a free shuttle service to the hotel's private, fully-equipped beach.

Guests can also relax in the hotel's extensive gardens. As well as meeting and banquet facilities, Parco dei Principi has a restaurant serving delicious international and local cuisine.

Well-located for discovering all of Calabria’s most important cultural sites, Parco dei Principi also provides airport transfers.

For extra luxury, a private helicopter service is available for up to 6 people to the airports of Lamezia Terme, Reggio Calabria , Crotone and Catania.
