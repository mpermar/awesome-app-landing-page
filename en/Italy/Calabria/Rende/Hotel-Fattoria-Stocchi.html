Description

This 1950s farmhouse is just 250 metres from the Cosenza Nord exit of the A3 motorway.

Fattoria Stocchi features a 500 m² garden with a children's playground. Set in Rende, Hotel Fattoria Stocchi is 2 km from the University of Calabria Campus.

Cosenza centre is 10 minutes away by car. Accommodation is air conditioned and comes with a TV, private bathroom and wood or tiled floors.

Apartments also feature a kitchenette. Breakfast is buffet style. Guests have a TV lounge with library, plus a free car park.
