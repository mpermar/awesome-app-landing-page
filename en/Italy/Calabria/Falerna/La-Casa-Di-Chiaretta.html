Description

Situated in Falerna, 45 km from Paola, La Casa Di Chiaretta features a bar and free WiFi.

Guests at the bed and breakfast can enjoy a buffet or an Italian breakfast. La Casa Di Chiaretta offers a barbecue.

After a day of skiing, diving or cycling, guests can relax in the garden or in the shared lounge area.

Cosenza is 49 km from the accommodation, while Lamezia Terme is 27 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Lamezia Terme International, 20 km from La Casa Di Chiaretta, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
