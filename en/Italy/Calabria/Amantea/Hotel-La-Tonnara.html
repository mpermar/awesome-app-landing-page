Description

This comprehensive hotel includes a beach with umbrellas and deckchairs, tennis courts, and a large sun terrace. Bikes and small boats are available completely free of charge.

Each guest room comes with free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and an LCD TV with Sky and pay-per-view. Most rooms have a private terrace with a view of the sea.

At La Tonnara you will find a free parking lot, a game room, meeting room, nightclub, and a newsstand's.

La Tonnara features an excellent restaurant offering fish specialties and a mix of local and Italian cuisine. A full catering service is available for meetings.

The helpful staff is always available. At the front desk you can arrange a transportation to the airport or the station in Lamezia Terme.
