Description

Set on a quiet stretch of the Calabrian coast, Trevi Village offers a variety of sports, from swimming to volleyball and tennis.

Amantea town centre is a 10-minute drive away. Sandy and pebble beaches are a 2-minute walk from the Trevi.

There are sports tournaments and a children's mini club. All air-conditioned guest rooms feature satellite TV and a minibar.

Each room has a private bathroom complete with hairdryer. A simple breakfast is available daily in the dining room.

There is a dedicated restaurant for the half and full-board options. Guests can relax in the hot tub or work out at the gym.

Massages and beauty treatments can be booked for an additional charge. Lamezia Terme Airport is 33 km from the property, and it is a 1-hour drive to Cosenza.
