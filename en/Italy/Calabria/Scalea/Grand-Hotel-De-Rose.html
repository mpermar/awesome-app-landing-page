Description

Featuring a free-form outdoor pool, the Grand Hotel De Rose is located in Scalea, next to its private beach.

Wi-Fi and parking are free, while some rooms have Tyrrhenian Sea views. The pool at Hotel De Rose includes a hydrobike and is set on a terrace overlooking the coast.

Fitness lovers can also enjoy a workout in the gym, followed by a relaxing massage treatment.

A continental breakfast is provided daily. Rooms are air conditioned and come with a minibar and a hairdryer in the private bathroom.

Scalea-Santa Domenica Talao Station is 3 km from the hotel. The A3 Autostrada Salerno-Reggio Calabria motorway is a 45-minute drive away.
