Description

Featuring a free outdoor pool and a relaxing garden, Hotel Ristorante Villa Brazzano is 2 km from Scalea centre.

It offers an à-la-carte restaurant and accommodation with a balcony. Classic-style rooms and apartments at the Ristorante Villa Brazzano come with a TV and cool tiled or marble floors.

Each apartment provides an equipped kitchenette with a coffee machine. The daily Italian breakfast of hot drinks and croissants is served buffet style.

On request, savoury options can be prepared. The restaurant specialises in traditional regional cuisine.

In addition, guests can enjoy discounts with rafting, go-kart racing and horse-riding schools.

A free shuttle to/from Scalea Train Station is provided. Valle del Fiume Lao Nature Reserve is a 20-minute drive from the property.
