Description

Popilia Country Resort is a 4-star property set 7 km from the seafront and a 10-minute drive from Pizzo Calabro.

With its hillside position, the property has a seasonal outdoor pool, free WiFi and a bar.

The Popilia offers air-conditioned rooms and cottages with satellite LED TV and minibar.

Some cottages have a kitchenette, and some rooms include a hydromassage shower. Guests can book a session at the wellness centre, or play a game at the 9-hole minigolf course, tennis court or 5-a-side football pitch.

There are hiking paths you can explore in the surroundings. A continental buffet breakfast also featuring homemade pastries and fresh fruit is provided daily.

The restaurant specialises in regional cuisine. Pizzo Calabro Train Station is 10 km from the resort, and Lamezia Terme Airport is a 30-minute drive away.
