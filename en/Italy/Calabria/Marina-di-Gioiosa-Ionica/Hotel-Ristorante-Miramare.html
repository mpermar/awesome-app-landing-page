Description

Set on Marina di Gioiosa Ionica’s seafront, Hotel Ristorante Miramare offers a private beach and panoramic views over the Ionian Sea.

Parking and internet access are free. Miramare’s rooms are air conditioned and come with satellite TV and a minibar.

They feature a balcony or terrace overlooking the sea. The restaurant-pizzeria at the Miramare Hotel serves Mediterranean cuisine, and Italian and international specialities, using fresh products.

The hotel’s bar features an ice cream parlour and also provides outdoor seating. Staff at reception are available 24 hours a day and can recommend sights in the surrounding area.

An entertainment programme is also offered. Hotel Miramare is a 2-minute drive from the SS106 state road.
