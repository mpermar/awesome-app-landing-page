Description

Located a 5-minute walk from the beach, Hotel Villaggio La Mantinera offers rooms in Praia a Mare.

Guests can enjoy the seasonal swimming pool. Free WiFi is available in common areas.

Rooms come with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV. Some also offer a balcony or a patio.

The bathrooms feature a shower and free toiletries. A buffet breakfast is served each morning at the property.

The on site restaurant serves Italian cuisine. Diamante is 25 km from the Hotel Villaggio La Mantinera, while Maratea is a 25-minute drive away.
