Description

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool and a garden with children's playground, New Hotel Blu Eden offers rooms in Praia a Mare, 2.

5 km from the beachfront. Free Wi-Fi access is available throughout the property.

With a balcony with sea views, each air-conditioned room is equipped with a flat-screen TV and minibar.

The private bathroom comes with a shower, bidet and free toiletries. A continental breakfast buffet is served daily in the breakfast room, including freshly brewed coffee or cappuccino, sweet pastries, cold cuts and cheeses.

The restaurant serves modernly presented regional specialities. Maratea is a 27-minute drive from New Blu Eden Hotel, while Scalea is 9 km away.
