Description

Situated in Sibari, 4. 3 km from Sibartide Archaeological Ruins, SIBARI RESORT features accommodation with a seasonal outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a bar and a shared lounge.

Among the various facilities are a garden and a terrace. The accommodation offers evening entertainment and room service.

The hotel offers a continental or buffet breakfast. You can play table tennis and darts at SIBARI RESORT, and bike hire and car hire are available.
