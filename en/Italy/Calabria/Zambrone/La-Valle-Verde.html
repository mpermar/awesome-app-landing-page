Description

Only 800 metres from Marina di Zambrone's white sandy beaches, La Valle Verde is on the SS522 national road, 10 minutes' drive from Tropea.

Its restaurant serves fresh fish and Calabrian specialities. Rooms are simply furnished and air conditioned.

Each one has an LCD TV and a fridge, and some have a balcony overlooking the Mediterranean Sea.

Wi-Fi is free. Homemade cakes are served at breakfast along with croissants, fruit, marmalade and juice.

Meals can be enjoyed out in the garden during summer. La Valle Verde has a free internet point and its own bar.

The nearest bus stop is 30 metres away for buses to Zambrone town centre and train station.
