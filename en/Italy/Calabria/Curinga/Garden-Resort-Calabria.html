Description

Situated in Curinga and surrounded by Mediterranean greenery, Garden Resort Calabria is 300 metres from its free long sandy beach.

Among the various facilities of this property are a tennis court, a football pitch and a volleyball court.

Rooms at the resort are fitted with a flat-screen TV, air conditioning and a small fridge.

Each room has a private bathroom with hairdryer. A buffet-style continental breakfast is available at the Garden Resort Calabria.

The restaurant specialises in international, italian and local cuisine. In summer you can enjoy 2 outdoor pools.

Free WiFi is provided in common areas. Staff speak German, English and Italian at the front desk.

Tropea is a 45-minute drive from the resort, while Pizzo Calabro famous for its tartufo ice cream is 9 km from the property.
