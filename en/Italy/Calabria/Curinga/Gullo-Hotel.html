Description

Gullo Hotel is a 15-minute drive from the A3 motorway and Lamezia Terme Airport. Located 3 km from the sea, it offers an outdoor swimming pool, a garden, and rooms with balconies.

Each air-conditioned room features free Wi-Fi, a Simmons 5-star mattress and a flat-screen TV.

Some rooms overlook the garden, some the swimming pool, and others have a sea view.

Parking is free at the Gullo Hotel. The hotel restaurant overlooks the swimming pool and serves Mediterranean cuisine and dishes from the Calabria region.

Set in Acconia Di Curinga, the hotel is close to the Lamezia Terme industrial area.
