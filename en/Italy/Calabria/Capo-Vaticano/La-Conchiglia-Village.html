Description

Offering a private beach and a summer swimming pool, La Conchiglia Village is in the popular seaside resort of Capo Vaticano.

It has a football field, volleyball court and games room. Overlooking the garden, the rooms feature a private entrance, fridge and en suite bathroom with hairdryer.

A continental breakfast is served each morning and all drinks and meals can be enjoyed on the poolside terrace.

La Conchiglia has a beach shuttle available on request as the walk back to the hotel is quite steep.

Ricadi Train Station is less than 1 km away and a free shuttle takes you to the train station and the centre of Capo Vaticano.
