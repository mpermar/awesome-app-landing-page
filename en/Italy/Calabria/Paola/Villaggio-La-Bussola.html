Description

Featuring a private beach area and a garden, Villaggio La Bussola offers self-catering bungalows on the beachfront of Paola.

Free Wi-Fi access is available in the shared areas. With air conditioning, each bungalow is equipped with a TV and a kitchenette with refrigerator and coffee machine.

The private bathroom comes with a shower. The restaurant serves specialities from Calabria and Italian classics.

Guests can enjoy a beach ombrella and two sun loungers. Paola Train Station is a 14-minute walk from Villaggio La Bussola, while the lively area of Lungomare San Francesco di Paola starts 400 metres away.
