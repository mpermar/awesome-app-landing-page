Description

Parco Hotel Granaro offers simple accommodation and good home cooking, all just 2 km from Lake Passante and Sila National Park.

Surrounded by greenery, it has a peaceful setting. Parking is free at the Granaro Park Hotel.

You will also find a TV lounge, bar, and a large restaurant where you can sample local specialities.

All rooms have satellite TV and a private bathroom. Some feature a balcony or terrace.

Set just outside the village of Sorbo San Basile, the hotel is just 20 km from the ski slopes and the national park.
