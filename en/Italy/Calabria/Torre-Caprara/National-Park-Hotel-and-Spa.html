Description

Located in front of the ski slopes of Sila National Park, 80 metres from the Brigantessa ski lift, National Park Hotel offers a restaurant and ski equipment hire.

It features rooms with wooden furnishings and balcony. All mountain-style rooms come with a TV, tiled floors and a private bathroom with shower and free toiletries.

Wi-Fi is available upon request. Some rooms offer views of the ski slopes. An Italian buffet breakfast is served daily in the common room.

The restaurant serves local dishes at dinner. After a day on the mountain, guests can relax in the bar.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including ice-skating, skiing and trekking.

Ski lessons can be organized at Hotel National Park upon request. The hotel is 900 metres from Lake Ampollino.
