Description

Offering a restaurant and bar, Hotel Palaghiaccio is located in Cotronei, just a 5-minute walk from Lake Ampollino.

The hotel also features a shared lounge area and karaoke facilities. The heated rooms are all equipped with a desk and safety deposit box.

Some rooms also come with a balcony and mountain views. Each private bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Guest can go hiking or skiing in the surrounding area, and ski storage is provided at the hotel.

Free Wi-Fi access is available in public areas. Palaghiaccio is situated 40 km from the A3 main highway, and 90 km from Lamezia Terme International Airport.
