Description

Just 2 km from the beach in Giallonardo, Borgo Giallonardo is a 5-minute drive from the Scala Dei Turchi.

Offering free WiFi, air-conditioned accommodation and a garden, this property produces and sells its own olive oil and wine.

The classic-style rooms and apartments feature satellite flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with hairdryer and toiletries.

Apartments also include a kitchen, seating area and terrace. Breakfast is served in the breakfast room or in the shared garden under the shade of a gazebo.

The property features a swimming pool with hydromassage and solarium. The centre of Realmonte is a 5-minute drive away.
