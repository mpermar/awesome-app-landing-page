Description

Located a 6-minute walk from Fontane Bianche Beach, Valle di Mare Resort is just a 15-minute drive from both Siracusa and Noto.

The property offers a peaceful atmosphere, an outdoor swimming pool, and extensive gardens.

All rooms come with air conditioning and a satellite TV, and there is free WiFi in the lobby.

The rooms are on the ground floor, and each offers a patio. In summer months, free Sky channels are available.

Breakfast includes hot and cold drinks, bread, cakes, and cereal, and it is served near the reception until 11:00.

During summer the restaurant is open for lunch and dinner. The Valle di Mare is located just 3 km from the Cassibile exit of the A18 Autostrada Messina-Catania motorway.

The nature reserve of Vendicari and the protected coastal area of Plemmirio are just a short drive away.
