Description

Relax by peaceful Brucoli bay. Hotel Capo Campolato offers rooms and apartments set in beautiful gardens just 50m from the sea and with panoramic views of the sea and Mount Etna.

The Capo Campolato’s extensive grounds are home to 2 outdoor pools with sun terrace, beach volleyball courts, a bowling green, mini-golf and a children’s playground.

It also features a spa and wellness centre and a variety of massages and beauty treatments.

The independent apartments and rooms located here are spacious and well-equipped.

All have free WiFi internet access and the apartments also have hydromassage showers.

Also in the Capo Campolato complex is a sea-view bar and restaurant with terrace.

Live music and theme nights are sometimes organised at the bar and the restaurant offers regional and Italian cuisine prepared with fresh ingredients.
