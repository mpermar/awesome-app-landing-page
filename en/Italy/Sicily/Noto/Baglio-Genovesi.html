Description

At the bed and breakfast, each room is equipped with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. The rooms have a electric tea pot, while some have a balcony. Guest rooms include a closet.

A continental or buffet breakfast is available each morning at the property.

The area is popular for snorkeling, and bike rental and car rental are available at Baglio Genovesi.

Vendicari Natural Reserve is 6 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Comiso Airport, 25 miles from Baglio Genovesi.
