Description

At the bed and breakfast, the rooms are equipped with a closet. Certain rooms are contain a kitchen with an oven.

Breakfast is available each morning, and includes buffet and Italian options.

Guests can take part in various activities in the surrounding areas, including cycling and hiking.

Church of the Annunciation of the Catalans is 5 miles from Villa Pollicino, while Duomo Messina is 5 miles away. The nearest airport is Reggio di Calabria Tito Minniti Airport, 15 miles from the accommodation.
