Description

All rooms in the bed and breakfast are equipped with a coffee machine. Every room includes a electric tea pot, while selected rooms are equipped with a kitchen with an oven. The rooms include a seating area.

A buffet, Italian or gluten-free breakfast is served at the property.

The property has a hot tub.

Sant Elia Church is 6 miles from Villa Grande, while University of Messina is 6 miles away. The nearest airport is Reggio di Calabria Tito Minniti Airport, 13 miles from the accommodation.
