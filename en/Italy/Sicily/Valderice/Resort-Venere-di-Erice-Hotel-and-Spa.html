Description

Featuring free WiFi and an outdoor pool, Venere di Erice offers accommodation in Valderice.

It features a shared terrace with panoramic views of the Gulf of Bonagia and San Vito Lo Capo.

There is a business centre at the property, as well as a sea-view restaurant serving typical Sicilian specialities.

The rooms have a modern dcor, air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

Trapani is 9 km from Venere di Erice, while San Vito lo Capo is 19 km away. Trapani Airport is 17 km from the property.
