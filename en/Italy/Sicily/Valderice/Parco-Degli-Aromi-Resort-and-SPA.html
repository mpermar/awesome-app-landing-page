Description

Located a 10-minute drive from the historic town of Erice, Parco Degli Aromi is surrounded by olive groves.

It offers a swimming pool with panoramic views across sea and mountains. Rooms are air conditioned and come with satellite TV and a furnished balcony overlooking the Mediterranean Sea.

Parking is free, as is Wi-Fi internet in public areas. Parco Degli Aromi Resort & SPA offers plenty of wellness facilities, including a sauna, Turkish bath, and hydrotherapy showers.

The restaurant serves Sicilian dishes and wines, and your meal can be enjoyed out on the terrace overlooking Mount Cofano.
