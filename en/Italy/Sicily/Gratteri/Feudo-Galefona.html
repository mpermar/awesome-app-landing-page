Description

Set inside Madonie Regional Natural Park, 8 km from Cefalù, Feudo Galefona features air-conditioned rooms and free private parking.

The farm stay has a barbecue, children's playground and terrace, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

It offers free spa with hot tub and sauna. Rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV.

Some units feature a seating area for your convenience. Enjoy a cup of tea while looking out at the mountain or garden.

All rooms come with a private bathroom equipped with a bidet and shower. Feudo Galefona features free WiFi throughout the property.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including horse riding, cycling and hiking.

The farm stay also offers free use of bicycles. Cerda is 33 km from Feudo Galefona, while Castelbuono is 16 km from the property.
