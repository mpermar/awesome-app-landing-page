Description

The Dioscuri Bay Palace overlooks the sea and the harbour in San Leone, with views including Agrigento's Valley of the Temples which is a 10 minutes' drive away.

All rooms offer a balcony, WiFi is free, and the outdoor swimming pool is right by the sea.

The 4-star Dioscuri Bay Palace features spacious, air-conditioned accommodation. Each room is soundproofed, and some offer views of the sea or the garden.

The restaurant serves Mediterranean cuisine made with the finest, seasonal ingredients.

Breakfast is buffet style. The hotel is set on San Leone's lively seaside promenade.

Agrigento's historic centre is reachable in 15 minutes by car, and the Scala dei Turchi beach is 18 km away.
