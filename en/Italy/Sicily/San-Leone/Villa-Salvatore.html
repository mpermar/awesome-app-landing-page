Description

Each unit comes with a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge, a seating area with a sofa, a flat-screen TV, a washing machine, and a private bathroom with bidet and free toiletries. A balcony with garden views is offered in every unit.

Breakfast is available every morning, and includes buffet, Italian and vegetarian options. There's an on-site restaurant, which specializes in local and Italian cuisine and also offers dairy-free, vegetarian and gluten-free options.

The apartment has an outdoor pool.

Villa Salvatore has a garden and sun terrace, which can be enjoyed by guests if the weather permits.

Agrigento is 5 miles from the accommodation.
