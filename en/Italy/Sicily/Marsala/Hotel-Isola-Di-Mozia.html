Description

Located just 6 km from Trapani's Vincenzo Florio Airport, Hotel Isola Di Mozia is close to the Stagnone Nature Reserve and 500 metres from Marsala's Salt Museum.

WiFi and outdoor parking are free. Each air-conditioned room at the Isola Di Mozia features a TV, a fridge, and a private bathroom.

Some rooms also have a private balcony and some others feature sea views. A sweet and savoury buffet breakfast is provided daily.

Guests can also enjoy discounts at a restaurant/pizzeria next to the hotel. On site there is a small shared garden with a small an outdoor pool.

The team of staff of Isola Di Mozia Hotel can arrange tours. Themed evenings and live music events are organised at the hotel.
