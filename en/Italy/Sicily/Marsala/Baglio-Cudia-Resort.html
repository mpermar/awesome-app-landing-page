Description

Set close to the unspoilt Stagnone Lagoon where you can enjoy water sports, and only 6 km from the centre of Marsala, Baglio Cudia Resort is a traditional Sicilian country residence.

Beautifully restored, Baglio Cudia Resort features classic 19th-century décor and furnishings.

All rooms are air conditioned and come with minibars. There is a lounge and a bar on site and the restaurant serves typical Sicilian dishes accompanied by renowned local wines.

Baglio Cudia makes a charming and relaxing base for exploring this interesting part of Sicily.

Visit the nearby salt plains or take a boat across the lagoon to the ancient island of Mozia, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
