Description

Hotel Baglio Oneto dei Principi di San Lorenzo is set in a restored former feudal dwelling from the 18th-Century and is located in the countryside of Marsala, 10 km from the centre.

The property offers a summer swimming pool, restaurant and free WiFi throughout. Rooms feature stylish furnishings and memory-foam mattresses.

All accommodation comes with a smart TV, air conditioning, and a private bathroom with bathtub.

All rooms have a balcony or small terrace, and some also offer views of the sea and Egadi Islands.

Restaurant La Calandra serves local specialties for lunch and dinner, and the wine cellar offers a fine selection of local wines and its own wine produced on site.

During summer light lunches are served at the pool bar. A free electric car charging station and an outdoor fitness pathway are available on site.

A personal trainer is available on request. Baglio Oneto is a 5-minute drive from the centre of Marsala, and offers free parking and free bike rental.
