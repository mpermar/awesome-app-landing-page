Description

Offering a spa and a restaurant, Just be & b is situated in Piraino in the Sicily Region.

The bed and breakfast has a sun terrace and hot tub. Free WiFi is featured and free private parking is available on site.

Each room at this bed and breakfast is air conditioned and comes with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Certain units have a seating area for your convenience. Every room has a private bathroom.

Extras include free toiletries and a hair dryer. There is a shared lounge at the property.

The restaurant serves local and organic products. You can engage in various activities, such as snorkeling, diving and cycling.

Lipari is 35 km from Just be & b, while Vulcano is 29 km from the property. Tito Minniti Airport is 69 km away.
