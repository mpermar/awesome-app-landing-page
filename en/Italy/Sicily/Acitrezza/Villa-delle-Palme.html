Description

Surrounded by pine and citrus trees, Villa delle Palme overlooks a protected marine area of the Ionian Sea in Aci Castello, 10 km outside Catania.

It features a garden with swimming pool and hot tub. Each apartment has its own patio or balcony, complete with a BBQ and a view of the garden or sea.

They all feature a comfortable dining area and kitchenette, a washing machine and air conditioning.

Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas and free parking is provided. Villa delle Palme also rents dinghies and sailing boats.

You will also find ping pong and table football in the garden, plus a rooftop sun terrace that can be used in summer.

The nearest bus stop is just 50 metres away for buses to Catania. On request, staff organise excursions to Mount Etna.

A pick-up service is included. Mount Etna and Taormina are both around 40 minutes away by car.
