Description

Featuring a sun terrace with hot tub and a sauna, Sicilia's Art Hotel & Spa is 1 km from Aci Trezza, where you can find restaurants and the beach.

Wi-Fi and private parking are free at the property, where an art exhibition is also featured.

With a private equipped garden or terrace, accommodation here is modern and air conditioned.

Each unit is complete with a 32” flat-screen TV, and a bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Apartments and studios include a kitchenette. Breakfast is available daily, with traditional Sicilian delicacies.

Situated in eastern Sicily, Sicilia's Art Hotel & Spa is a 10-minute drive from Acireale, and 32 km from Mount Etna.
