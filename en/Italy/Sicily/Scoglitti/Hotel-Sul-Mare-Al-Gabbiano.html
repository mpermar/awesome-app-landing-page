Description

Offering free bikes, a private beach area served by a panoramic lift, furnished terrace and restaurant, Hotel Sul Mare Al Gabbiano is located just 10 metres from Scoglitti’s sandy beaches.

It offers sea-view rooms and free Wi-Fi. Comiso Airport is 16 km away. Air-conditioned rooms at the 4-star Sul Mare Al Gabbiano will provide you with a 43" Smart flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a minibar.

Featuring a shower, private bathrooms come with a bathrobe. Some rooms have a balcony.

A sweet and savoury breakfast, served buffet style is available daily. The air-conditioned restaurant specialises in Mediterranean cuisine.

There is also a 24-hour bar. Paddle boat and canoe can be rented free of charge on site.

A bus stop, 30 metres away, will bring you to Vittoria, 10 km from the property. Kamerina Archaeological Site is 3 km away.
