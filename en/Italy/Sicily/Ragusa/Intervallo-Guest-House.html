Description

Intervallo Guest House is situated in the historical centre of Ragusa. Every room at this guest house is air conditioned and is fitted with a flat-screen TV.

Every room has a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Intervallo Guest House features free WiFi throughout the property. You will find a shared kitchen, a terrace with panoramic views and BBQ facilities at the property.

Scicli is 27 km from Intervallo Guest House, while Modica is 19 km away. The nearest airport is Comiso Airport, 29 km from Intervallo Guest House.
