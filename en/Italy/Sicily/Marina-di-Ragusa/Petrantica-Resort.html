Description

Located in Marina di Ragusa, Petrantica Resort has an outdoor swimming pool, BBQ facilities and free WiFi.

With a garden, the property also features a shared lounge. The property features a children's playground.

All rooms features air conditioning, a toaster, a fridge, a coffee machine, a bidet, free toiletries and a desk.

Every room is fitted with a private bathroom and kettle, while certain rooms include a terrace and others also provide guests with sea views.

The rooms feature a wardrobe. The resort offers a sun terrace. Guests at Petrantica Resort will be able to enjoy activities in and around Marina di Ragusa, like hiking.

Ragusa is 19 km from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Comiso Airport, 23 km from the property.
