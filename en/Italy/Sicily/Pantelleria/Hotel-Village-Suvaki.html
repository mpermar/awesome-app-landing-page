Description

Hotel Village Suvaki was the first 4-star complex on the island of Pantelleria. Enjoy the beautiful views of the coast from your own private balcony.

Set in a fantastic location, this elegant hotel has something for everyone. Relax in the pool and have a hydromassage.

Water sports fans can enjoy diving lessons and excursions to the nearby beach. Each guest room is complete with a balcony or veranda, and most rooms have a sea view.

At the Suvaki Village you can relax in the lounge with a drink or wait for the sunset at the pool bar.

Enjoy regional and Italian cuisine in the restaurant, including meat and fish specialities, pizza, and Sicilian pastries.
