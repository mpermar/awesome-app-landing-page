Description

Offering a terrace, specific units are air-conditioned and have a dining area and a seating area with a flat-screen TV. There's a fully equipped private bathroom with bidet and free toiletries.

The apartment offers a continental or Italian breakfast. Guests can enjoy a meal at the in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of seafood, Italian and Grill/bbq dishes.

TGI Dammusi Preziosi has a grill.

If you would like to discover the area, hiking is possible nearby and the accommodation can arrange a car rental service.
