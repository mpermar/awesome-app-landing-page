Description

This charming farmhouse is set on the north coast of Sicily, just half an hour from Palermo.

Enjoy dishes made with fresh ingredients from the farm for breakfast and dinner. Wi-Fi is free throughout.

Set in an 18th-century noble residence, the Agriturismo Villa Cefalà is surrounded by beautiful olive and citrus groves.

The comfortable guest rooms feature wrought-iron beds, wooden furniture and ceramic tiles.

Relax in the private gardens or in the pool. Villa Cefalà also features bike rental for those of you who wish to explore the surrounding area or go down to the beach.

Why not try a cooking class or fishing trip, all of which are organised by the professional team of friendly staff.

Start your day with a generous breakfast including freshly squeezed orange juice, selection of bread, cookies and homemade cakes accompanied by cold cuts, cheese and yoghurt.

At dinner, try some typical Italian and international cuisine with homemade extra virgin olive oil and wine.
