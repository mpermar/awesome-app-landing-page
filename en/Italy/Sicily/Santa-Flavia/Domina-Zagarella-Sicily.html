Description

Surrounded by a Mediterranean garden, Domina Zagarella Sicily features outdoor swimming pools set on a sea-view terrace.

Its vintage-style rooms offer LCD satellite TV and free Wi-Fi. Most rooms have a balcony.

Rooms come with retro décor and bright colours. All air conditioned, they include a minibar and a private bathroom with hairdryer.

Some are located in detached houses with private entrance. Friendly staff at the 24-hour reception can provide tourist information and organise diving courses.

The on-site gym is available for free. The Domina Zagarella Sicily includes 2 restaurants serving Sicilian and international cuisine.

Drinks can be enjoyed at the elegant wine bar or on the panoramic terrace with stylish furnishings.

Right on a rocky shore of the northern coast of Sicily, the hotel overlooks the Tyrrhenian Sea.
