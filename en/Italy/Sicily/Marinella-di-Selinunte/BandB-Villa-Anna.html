Description

Located in Marinella di Selinunte, just 1. 5 km from the Mediterranean Sea, B&B Villa Anna is a bed and breakfast with a free outdoor pool and a furnished garden.

It offers free Wi-Fi, BBQ facilities and modern accommodation. Rooms at Villa Anna B&B come with a wardrobe and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

A typical Italian breakfast of hot drinks and croissants is served daily. In addition, this property has a children’s playground and free luggage storage.

Free on-site parking is provided. Trapani and Palermo Airports are both 90 km from the property.
