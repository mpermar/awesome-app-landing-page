Description

Surrounded by its 10000 m² garden with hammock among the olive trees, Agrisicilia (Relax Mare e Natura) is located in Marinella di Selinunte, 2.

9 km from Selinunte Archaeological Park. Free private parking is available on site.

All units have a flat-screen TV with cable channels, fridge and minibar. Some of the units boast a kitchenette and panoramic view of the Templi of Selinunte.

Agrisicilia (Relax Mare e Natura) features free WiFi. Towels are featured. The property also offers a restaurant.

The nearest airport is Trapani Airport, 46 km from Agrisicilia (Relax Mare e Natura).
