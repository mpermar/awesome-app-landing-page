Description

Set on the beach just outside Porto Empedocle, Case Vacanze Ancora offers a pool with sun loungers.

Apartments are air conditioned and feature a balcony or terrace, some with sea views.

Each apartment has a classic design with wood furniture and tiled floors. They all have a lounge with TV and a fully equipped kitchenette.

Breakfast at Ancora Case Vacanze is buffet style, while the restaurant serves Italian meals at dinner.

There is also a bar with outdoor seating area and a garden with children's playground.

Free parking is provided and Agrigento and the Valley of the Temples are around 15 minutes away by car.

The unique rock formation of Scala dei Turchi is just 2 km away, as is Porto Empedocle harbour for boats to Lampedusa.
