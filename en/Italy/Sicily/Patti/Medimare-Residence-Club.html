Description

Only 600 metres from the sandy beach and Thyrrenian Sea, the Medimare is 5 minutes' drive from Patti in Sicily.

It boasts a swimming pool with sun terrace, a restaurant, and free Wi-Fi in public areas.

Featuring either a small garden or balcony, all apartments are air conditioned. Each includes a bedroom, a separate living room with kitchenette and dining area, and a bathroom.

The property offers a free shuttle to/from Patti-San Piero Patti Train Station, and staff is happy to organize tours to nearby attractions.

A playground and table tennis are also available on site. The Medimare Residence Club is in the countryside 60 km from Messina, right in front of the Aeolian Islands.
