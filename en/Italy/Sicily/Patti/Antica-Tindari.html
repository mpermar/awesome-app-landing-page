Description

Antica Tindari is a farm stay surrounded by a vineyard and situated in the hills of Tindari.

The ruins of the Greek Theatre and the Cathedral are nearby. you can taste the property's wine in the on-site restaurant, which also features locally grown, organic ingredients.

The staff at the Antica Tindari give tours around the farm. The beaches of Marina di Patti are 12 km away.
