Description

Ottomood B&B is a 10-minute walk from Tremestieri Etneo. It offers air-conditioned rooms with a hydromassage shower, a free outdoor pool, and a garden with olive trees and a sun terrace.

With a satellite flat-screen TV and free WiFi, modern-style rooms at the Ottomod feature tiled floors.

The private bathroom is complete with a hairdryer, bathrobe and free toiletries. A continental buffet breakfast is available daily and includes gluten-free products.

Catania is 10 km away, while Taormina can be reached by car in 40 minutes. Parking at the property is free.

You are advised to bring your own vehicle as the property is not serviced by public transport.
