Description

Park Hotel Silemi offers a free equipped outdoor pool and private beach, plus a restaurant with terrace overlooking the Taormina Gulf.

It is 10 minutes’ drive from ancient Taormina and just outside Letojanni. Parking and WiFi are free.

With a Mediterranean décor and balcony, all rooms at the Silemi come with air conditioning, a minibar and a satellite TV.

Some feature a sea-view balcony. The Park Hotel Silemi is a quick 500-metre stroll from Letojanni’s lively restaurant and bar area.

Buses leave regularly from just outside the hotel for Taormina city centre, which is just 5km away.

Park Hotel Silemi offers professional service and a peaceful, relaxing location with stunning views of Taormina Bay.
