Description

Hotel Achibea is located on the slope of Mount Passo del Lupo, part of the Zingaro Nature Reserve in Sicily.

It features an outdoor pool and a traditional restaurant. WiFi is free throughout.

San Vito Lo Capo is located 8. 5 km away, Overlooking the pool or the mountains, all rooms at the Achibea Hotel are air conditioned.

Each includes a TV, safe and desk. Some rooms also have a balcony. Traditional Sicilian specialities are served at the restaurant.

The breakfast buffet includes sweet and savoury food. Surrounded by a 4500-m² garden, the hotel also has a terrace with a bar and table tennis.

Only 1200 metres from the nearest beach, the hotel is in Castelluzzo. The surrounding area offers a stunning tract of coast on the Tyrrhenian Sea, including spots such as the Monte Cofano Nature Reserve located 13 km away.
