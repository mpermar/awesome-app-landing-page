Description

Just 50 metres from the beach, Krimar Hotel is a Mediterranean-style property with gardens, terraces, and traditional restaurant.

All rooms offer free Wi-Fi and a flat-screen TV. The bar is open all day and night.

The Krimar features classically furnished rooms with tiled floors and light-wood furniture.

All the suites come with a furnished patio, and some rooms are complete with a balcony.

The restaurant specialises in Sicilian cuisine and is open daily for lunch and dinner.

Breakfast is a continental buffet including eggs, cheese and ham. The centre of San Vito lo Capo is 600 metres away.
