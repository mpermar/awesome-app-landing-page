Description

Located in San Vito lo Capo, a 5-minute walk from the city centre, Artemide Hotel offers free WiFi and air-conditioned rooms.

A sweet buffet breakfast is provided daily, served under the shaded patio in fine weather.

A flat-screen TV is offered. The modern rooms at the Artemide Hotel have tiled floors and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

Some units come with a balcony or a furnished patio. A buffet breakfast is provided daily including sweet and savoury items.

It can be served on the patio or on the terrace in fine weather. Trapani is 35 km from Artemide Hotel, while Zingaro Nature Reserve is 12 km away.
