Description

Overlooking Taormina's coastline, the Baia Taormina Hotels & Spa offers 2 outdoor swimming pools, a free private beach, and a free scheduled shuttle to Taormina.

All rooms come with a sea-view balcony or terrace. WiFi is free throughout. The private beach is equipped with sun beds, parasols, and beach towels, for free use.

One of the swimming pools has sea water. The rooms feature typical Sicilian furnishings.

They provide a TV with satellite channels, air conditioning, and a minibar. The Baia Taormina includes 2 panoramic restaurants serving both Sicilian and international food.
