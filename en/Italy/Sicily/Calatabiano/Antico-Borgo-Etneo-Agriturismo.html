Description

With full views of Mount Etna, Antico Borgo is a restored country house surrounded by lemon and orange trees.

It offers free parking and free WiFi throughout. The rooms of the Agriturismo Antico Borgo are set in a farmhouse.

The views include the garden, Taormina, and the sea in the distance. Rooms feature air conditioning or a fan, and a tea/coffee maker.

The Agriturismo is located between the Fiumefreddo and Giardini Naxos motorway exits.

Calatabiano Railway Station is 300 metres away. You can drive to the nearest beach in 5 minutes.

For dinner, you can taste homemade Sicilian cuisine at the restaurant, set in a traditional wine cellar.

On weekdays, guests can enjoy discounted rates at a partner restaurant set 5 minutes' drive from the property.
