Description

At the bed and breakfast, every room is equipped with a desk and a flat-screen TV. Each room comes with a private bathroom with a bidet, free toiletries and a hair dryer. The rooms at Casa Azul feature air conditioning and a closet.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property.

Palermo is 25 miles from the accommodation, while Trapani is 22 miles from the property. Falcone-Borsellino Airport is 16 miles away.
