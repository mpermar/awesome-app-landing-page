Description

The Galea Farm House is set in 13 hectares of organic lemon groves on the East Coast of Sicily.

It offers rooms with panoramic views of Mount Etna or the sea and free parking. The rooms at the Galea are all air conditioned with patios and include private bathroom and satellite TV.

Wi-Fi internet access is also provided. The Galea Farm House has a restaurant and breakfast can also be served in guests’ rooms.

There is a swimming pool and a large garden with a terrace. A children’s playground and table tennis are also available.

The Galea Farm House is 2 km from the beach and 1. 5 km from Riposto Marina. Catania airport is a 40-minute drive away.

Rooms are located around the grounds. The furthest distance from the restaurant and swimming pool is 1 km.
