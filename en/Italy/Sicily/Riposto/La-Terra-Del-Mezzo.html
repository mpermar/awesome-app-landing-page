Description

Set in its own lemon grove, family-run La Terra Del Mezzo is at the foot of Mount Etna.

It features an outdoor swimming pool and restaurant, and is 800 metres from the sea and 2 km south of Riposto.

Rooms have an elegant rustic design with handmade Sicilian terracotta floors and many with wood-beamed ceilings.

They are all air conditioned and come with an LCD satellite TV, minibar and a private bathroom with toiletries.

They all overlook the sea or Mount Etna. Breakfast at La Terra Del Mezzo features cold cuts, homemade cakes, fruit and fresh juices produced on site.

The restaurant serves traditional Sicilian dishes at dinner. Guests have free parking and free Wi-Fi in public areas.

Discounts are available for local beach clubs. Catania Fontanarossa Airport is a 30-minute drive away.
