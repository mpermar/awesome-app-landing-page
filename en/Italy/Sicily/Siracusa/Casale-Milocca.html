Description

Located 750 metres from the beach, next to the Plemmirio Peninsula, Casale Milocca features free WiFi access, a restaurant and a swimming pool with hydromassage jets, a sun terrace and a pool bar.

It offers rooms with a satellite flat-screen TV. Set in a traditional Sicilian farm house dating back to the 19th century, all rooms come with a private bathroom.

Some have a terrace or balcony, The property has a children's playground, a children's swimming pool and a fitness trail.

It offers 24-hour front desk and a shop where guests can buy food specialties and locally handcrafted items.

The on-site restaurant offers Sicilian dishes made from locally sourced ingredients.

Free on-site parking is provided and free bikes are at guests' disposal to reach the beach.

Ortigia, the historical centre of Siracusa, is 10 km from the property. Noto is a 40-minute drive away.
