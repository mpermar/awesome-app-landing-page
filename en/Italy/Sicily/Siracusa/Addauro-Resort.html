Description

Featuring an outdoor pool, garden, and a restaurant, Addauro Resort offers air-conditioned rooms with modern décor, 2.

7 km from the beach in Arenella. WiFi and parking are both free at the property. All rooms have a satellite flat-screen TV and a minibar.

The private bathroom includes free toiletries and a hairdryer. Some rooms have pool views.

Syracuse is 20 minutes' drive from the Addauro, while the Plemmirio Marine Reserve is 9 km away.
