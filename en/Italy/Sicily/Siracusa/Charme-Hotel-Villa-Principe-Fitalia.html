Description

Charme Hotel Villa Principe Fitalia offers friendly service, a natural setting in a wildlife reserve, and a calm, peaceful location outside the centre of Syracuse.

You will have a wide selection of free facilities at Charme Hotel Villa Principe Fitalia, formerly a late 19th-century historic residence, and now a renovated 4-star hotel.

Work out in the fitness room, relax in the hydromassage bath and take advantage of the in-house beauty parlour.

Take a dip in the large, outdoor swimming pool Explore the area on free mountain bikes, and enjoy the fully equipped private beach club.

There are free shuttle services to the sea and to Ortigia. Charme Hotel Villa Principe Fitalia offers spacious, elegant en suite rooms with air conditioning, minibars and satellite TV.

Savour genuine cooking and rich meals at Principe di Fitalia. A free breakfast buffet is served every morning.

You will be near Arenella beach and the pavilions of the Fiera del Sud, the site of important annual international events.
