Description

Residence Villaggio Petruso features a sun terrace and an outdoor pool with a panoramic view of the Mediterranean Sea.

Set in Balestrate, it is 10 km from Castellammare del Golfo. The residence has free Wi-Fi in all public areas and guests have free access to the outdoor swimming pool.

Petruso Village also offers darts, table tennis and snooker. All rooms and apartments are modern and air conditioned.

They include a TV, fridge and private bathroom. The apartments also have a kitchenette.

This village is located 30 minutes' drive from Palermo Falcone Borsellino International Airport.
