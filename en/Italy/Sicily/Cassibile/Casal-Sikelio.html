Description

Casal Sikelio is located in Cassibile, 500 metres from Fontane Bianche Train Station and 1.

5 km from Fontane Bianche beach. It offers free WiFi, self-catering accommodation and a pool with hydromassage corner.

All the apartments at Casal Sikelio feature a satellite flat-screen TV, air conditioning and a fully equipped kitchenette.

Featuring a bath or shower, the private bathroom also comes with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

At Casal Sikelio you will find a garden with Bocce bowls and a children's play area.

The property offers free parking. Avola and Lido di Noto are both a 20-minute drive from the property.
