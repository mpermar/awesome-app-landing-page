Description

With a beautiful, tranquil setting in Corleone, this charming, family-run hotel offers bright, airy and comfortable accommodation at great rates with free Wi-Fi in the rooms.

Each of the spacious, elegantly furnished, air-conditioned rooms has a private balcony with great views.

The comfortable, well-equipped accommodation and serene, peaceful setting make the hotel ideal for a relaxing break.

The restaurant offers superb Mediterranean food with traditional Corleone specialities and typical Sicilian dishes.

With just 7 rooms, this small, charming hotel offers a warm, friendly welcome and a personal, yet professional, service.
