Description

Guest rooms in the resort are equipped with a electric tea pot. All rooms include a private bathroom and free toiletries, and some rooms also feature a balcony. At Resort Fontes Episcopi, every room has a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

A continental breakfast is available every morning at the accommodation. There's an on-site restaurant, which specializes in Mediterranean cuisine and also offers Gluten-free, Vegetarian and Vegan options.

Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near Resort Fontes Episcopi.

Agrigento is 6 miles from the resort.
