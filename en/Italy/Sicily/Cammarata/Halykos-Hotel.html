Description

Halykos Hotel offers rooms with free Wi-Fi access in Cammarata centre. The property has a shared terrace overlooking the garden and has free private parking.

The air-conditioned rooms feature a flat-screen TV, minibar and safety deposit box.

The private bathroom comes with a bath or shower, free bathrobe and hairdryer. A continental breakfast with homemade pastries is offered daily.

Halykos Hotel is 20 minutes’ drive from Monte Cammarata mount peak. Agrigento is 50 km away.
