Description

All rooms in the farm stay are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. All rooms come with a private bathroom, while some rooms also feature a terrace. The rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

A continental breakfast is served each morning at the property.

Agriturismo Alle Riserve Cavagrande has a grill. Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.

Syracuse is 11 miles from Agriturismo Alle Riserve Cavagrande. The nearest airport is Comiso Airport, 30 miles from the farm stay.
