Description

Featuring free WiFi, B&B Le Torrette offers accommodation in Avola, a 9-minute walk from the local beaches.

Free private parking is available on site. Some units have a seating area for your convenience.

A balcony or patio are featured in certain rooms. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries, a shower and a hairdryer in the bathroom.

A sweet and savoury breakfast is provided daily. Bike hire is available at B&B Le Torrette.

Ragusa is 37 km from the property, while Noto is 7 km away. CataniaFontanarossa Airport is reachable by car in less than 1 hour.
