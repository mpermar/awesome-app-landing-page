Description

Featuring a free hot tub, sun terrace and garden, Villa Pia Cornelia is peacefully located in Trapani’s countryside.

It offers free Wi-Fi in public areas and air-conditioned accommodation with a garden-view patio.

Rustic-style rooms at Villa Pia Cornelia come with a flat-screen TV, exposed-beam ceilings and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

They also include a fridge and table. The daily Italian breakfast of hot drinks and pastries is served buffet style.

There is also a bar. In the summer, guests can relax in the free outdoor pool. Free on-site parking is provided.

Trapani and its beaches are less than a 15-minute drive away. Marsala is 25 km from the property.
