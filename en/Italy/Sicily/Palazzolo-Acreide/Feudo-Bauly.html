Description

Rooms come with air conditioning. They all feature terracotta floors and beamed ceilings.

Parking is free at Feudo Bauly.

Horseback riding and hiking are both possible in the Bauly Forest, right next to the property. Feudo Bauly is a noble residence that dates back to the 14th century.
