Description

At the farm stay, rooms have a desk and a flat-screen TV. Rooms include a private bathroom with a bidet, free toiletries and a hair dryer. All rooms feature a closet.

A continental breakfast is served each morning at the property. Guests can dine in the on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of German, Italian and local dishes.

Agriturismo Villa Nicetta has a playground. Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Acquedolci, like cycling.

Cefalù is 30 miles from Agriturismo Villa Nicetta. The nearest airport is Fontanarossa Airport, 48 miles from the farm stay.
