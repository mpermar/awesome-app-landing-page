Description

La Suite Di Segesta offers accommodation with air conditioning and free WiFi in the Sicilian countryside, 5 km from Calatafimi.

The property features a garden, a swimming pool and a sun terrace. There is also a children's playground.

Rooms at the Segesta all come with a flat-screen TV and tiled floors. The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Both sweet and savoury products feature in the breakfast served daily. The property is 1.
