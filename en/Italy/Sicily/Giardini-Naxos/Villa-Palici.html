Description

Offering a barbecue and views of the garden, Villa Palici is set 5 minutes' drive from the centre of Giardini Naxos.

The accommodation is air conditioned and comes with a cable flat-screen TV. Some units include a seating area and/or terrace.

There is also a kitchen, fitted with a fridge. A stovetop is also featured, as well as a coffee machine and a kettle.

There is a private bathroom with a bath or shower in each unit. Bed linen is available.

Villa Palici also includes a terrace. You can play tennis at the property, and bike hire is available.

The area is popular for horse riding and cycling. Taormina is 5 km from Villa Palici, while Messina is 49 km from the property.
