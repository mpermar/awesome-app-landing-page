Description

Offering an outdoor pool, a gourmet restaurant and a spa, the 4-star Masseria Della Volpe is located in Casale Modica.

Set in the Sicilian countryside, this property also features a tennis court, a fitness centre and a pool-side bar.

All air-conditioned accommodation comes with a minibar, a private bathroom, and a flat-screen TV with satellite and pay-per-view channels.

Apartments also include a fully equipped kitchenette and a washing machine. WiFi is free throughout.

The spa is complete with a hammam and a hot tub. Guests will also find a children's playground.

Grilled dishes can also be sampled at the restaurant. Masseria Della Volpe is 15 km from Noto.
