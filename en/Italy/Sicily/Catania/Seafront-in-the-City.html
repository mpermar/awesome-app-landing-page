Description

Seafront in the City is located in Catania, a 5-minute walk from Corso Italia shopping street.

Catania Cathedral is 2. 9 km from the property, which offers a shared garden and a playground.

Seafront in the City apartments have air conditioning, wifi, Flat screen TV with Netflix, bathrooms with hairdryer, fully equipped kitchen, dining and seating areas.

Some apartments may have a private terrace or balcony. All Seafront in the City apartments have access to condominium gardens.

Teatro Romano Catania is 3. 1 km from Seafront in the City. The nearest airport is Catania Fontanarossa Airport, 7 km from the property.
