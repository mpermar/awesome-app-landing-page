Description

Inside Oasi del Simeto Nature Reserve, Sicily Country House & Beach is set in 7 acres of private farmland full of homegrown produce.

It is a 5-minute walk from the nearest beach. Sicily Country House & Beach has a swimming pool and children's playground.

The private beach is just a short walk through the shaded woods. Rooms are air conditioned and feature a TV, bathroom and outdoor patio.

Free WiFi is available throughout. A sweet Italian and savoury breakfast is served each morning and includes homemade products.

The restaurant specialises in homemade dishes from Sicily. Free parking is provided and Catania is 20 minutes away by car.
