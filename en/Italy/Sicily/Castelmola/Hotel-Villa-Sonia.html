Description

Each room is complete with free Wi-Fi and a private balcony or patio. Rooms also include air conditioning, a satellite TV, and a bathroom with slippers and bathrobe.

Villa Sonia is complete with a furnished terrace, small gym, and sauna. A library, lounge, and bar are also available.

The restaurant specializes in traditional, Sicilian cuisine. The cellar is stocked with an extensive collection of fine wines.

The friendly staff always offer a personalized service. Tourist and travel info for visiting Sicily are available at the tour desk.

Experiences

05/06/2018

A spacious and quiet hotel with a breathtaking view on the coast and the Etna

"We loved the location of the hotel, with an amazing view on Taormina's coast and the Etna, close to beautiful Taormina (we walked down to the city center in just 30 min with the kids and climbed up in the same time, nice stroll) but up enough on the hill to be quiet and far from the city's agitation. The hotel is spacious and beautiful. The staff is very nice and helpful. The breakfast is fantastic, served on a huge patio with a terrific view on the volcano."

07/24/2017

Wonderful hilltop retreat!

"Everything you need for breakfast.Rooms spacious and hotel is very clean.Pool area is really nice; clean good sun beds and umbrellas, cushions.Nice pool bar and poolside eating area. Hardly any children in the hotel, so poolside nice and quiet.Fantastic views of Mount Etna.Castelmola itself is a very pretty place with a few restaurants and bars. People all very friendly everywhere. Although there's a bus to and from Taormina and the beach, we had a small car which was very handy if we came back late. Spent half our evenings in Castelmola and half in Taormina. Taormina is a bit more expensive."
