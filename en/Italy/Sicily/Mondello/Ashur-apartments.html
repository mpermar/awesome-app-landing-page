Description

This air-conditioned vacation home has 1 bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen, a dining area and a cable flat-screen TV. The vacation home also provides a seating area, washing machine and a bathroom with a bidet.

Speaking English, Spanish and French, staff are willing to help at the 24-hour front desk.

The vacation home has a playground. Hiking can be enjoyed nearby and a bicycle rental service is available.

Falcone-Borsellino Airport is 12 miles from the property.
