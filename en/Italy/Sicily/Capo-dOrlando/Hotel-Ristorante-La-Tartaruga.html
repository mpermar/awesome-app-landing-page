Description

Overlooking the sea, La Tartaruga is located in Capo D’Orlando and it features a bar, outdoor pool and free Wi-Fi.

It also has a private beach area with parasol and deck chairs for the guests. Air-conditioned rooms come with a TV and safe.

The private bathroom includes free toiletries and hairdryer. Guests can enjoy local products at the restaurants.

Breakfast is served daily including hot drinks, croissants and jams. La Tartaruga is 6 km from Castello Bastione and a 35-minute drive from Barcellona Pozzo di Gotto.
