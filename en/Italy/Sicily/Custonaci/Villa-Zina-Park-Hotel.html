Description

The Villa Zina Park is in the Zingaro Nature Reserve and has large gardens. Beaches at San Vito Lo Capo are 10.5 miles away, and Trapani Airport is only 19 miles away. Free parking is available on site.

Rooms at Villa Zina are bright and air-conditioned. All have a balcony, TV and free Wi-Fi. A free internet point is also available in the lobby.

Zina Park's terrace restaurant serves Italian and Sicilian food and has an extensive wine list. There are 2 bars, one of which is by the pool.

Guests can enjoy a range of health and beauty treatments on site, as well as a sauna, Turkish bath, hot tub, and color therapy shower.
