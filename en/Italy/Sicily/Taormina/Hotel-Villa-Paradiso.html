Description

Villa Paradiso enjoys views of the Bay of Naxos and Mount Etna. This renovated, historical property is in the centre of Taormina, and offers unique rooms with a private balcony or terrace, overlooking the sea, garden, or town.

The cable car to the beach is 10 minutes' walk away. Rooms at Villa Paradiso feature antique furniture and original works of art.

Each room has a minibar and satellite TV. Romantic candlelit dinners are served at Restaurant Settimo Cielo.

Set on the 6th floor, it boasts a breathtaking view. At Villa Paradiso the Ginger Bar is open 24 hours a day.

You will find a lounge with a well-stocked library and a veranda overlooking the gardens.

The shared lounge also features an original Mirò painting. In summer, Hotel Villa Paradiso provides a free shuttle to the Paradise Beach Club in Letojanni.

Here there is an outdoor pool and a free parasol and sun lounger. Guests can also enjoy year-round use of the hotel's tennis courts.
