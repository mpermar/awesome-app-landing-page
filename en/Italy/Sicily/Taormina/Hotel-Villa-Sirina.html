Description

Villa Sirina offers a sun terrace with sea-view swimming pool, free parking, and free Wi-Fi.

It is located between Taormina centre and the Giardini Naxos Bay. Each room is classically decorated and equipped with terracotta floors and a private patio or balcony.

All rooms are air conditioned and come with satellite TV and radio. At Hotel Villa Sirina you will find a TV lounge, and a library with antique volumes of English classics.

An internet terminal is also available in the lobby. You can enjoy the buffet breakfast in the dining room or out on the terrace.

During summer, a selection of light Sicilian meals are served out by the pool. A scheduled shuttle bus to the beach is available on request.
