Description

VOI Arenella Resort is nestled in a spacious park on the southern coast of Sicily, alongside long sandy beaches, close to Syracuse and the protected marine area of Plemmirio.

VOI Arenella Resort offers a wide range of sports and leisure facilities. Try the resort’s new wellness centre with a number of relaxing and therapeutic spa facilities including massages and beauty treatments.

You can choose from numerous sports activities including archery in the summer months.

There is also a fully equipped scuba-diving centre on site with lessons available at an additional cost.

Enjoy the entertainment programme at Resort Arenella. Here you will find professional staff, including French and German speakers, and an extensive line-up of daytime and evening entertainment.

There are a number of children's clubs each for a speciifc age range and with a wide range of dedicated activities and services.

At VOI Arenella Resort guests will find a restaurant offering delicious breakfast and dinner buffets of local and Mediterranean cuisine, which can be enjoyed on open-air terraces.

Gluten-free options are also available. The restaurant features an open-cooking area where you can watch your meal being prepared as well as a large grill and barbecue area.
