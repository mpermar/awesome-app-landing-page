Description

With 3 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms with a bath and a bidet, this villa features a satellite flat-screen TV and Blu-ray player. A dishwasher and an oven can be found in the kitchen.

The villa has an outdoor pool. Villa Calipso provides a grill and a playground.

Catania is 24 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Fontanarossa Airport, 27 miles from the property.
