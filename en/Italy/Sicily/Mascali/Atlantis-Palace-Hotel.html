Description

Rooms at the Atlantis Palace each have a balcony and air conditioning. You will have free use of lounge chairs and an umbrella on the private beach.

The beach side bar has a cozy lounge area overlooking the Ionian Sea. Guests can also enjoy drinks and snacks by the pool.

Hotel Atlantis Palace is half a mile from the bus stop for Giardini Naxos and Taormina. Mount Etna Regional Park, is a 30-minute drive away.
