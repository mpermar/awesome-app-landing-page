Description

All rooms at Hotel Villa Enrica feature a patio with chairs or deckchairs. They are air conditioned and include a mini-bar, satellite TV and a bathroom. Some have a hydromassage shower or bathtub.

A buffet breakfast is served on a sea-view terrace. In the summer, cold meals, salads and fresh fruit are served for lunch at a partner restaurant nearby which can be reached by a free shuttle.

Villa Enrica features an infinity pool with hydromassage and a sun terrace. It offers free Wi-Fi in the ground-floor public areas, and free parking. Towels, bikes, cars and motorboats can be rented on-site.

The hotel offers a free shuttle service to/from Lipari harbor on guests’ arrival and departure. It provides booking and ticketing services for trips in the surrounding islands.
