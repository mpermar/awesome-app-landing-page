Description

Rooms at the Mea are air-conditioned, and decorated with bright colors and tiled floors. Each comes with a mini-bar and a private bathroom with a hairdryer. All include free high-speed WiFi access.

The restaurant serves local cuisine and specializes in fish dishes. A daily Italian buffet breakfast is provided.

The beaches of Canneto are 2.5 mi from the property. Ferries depart from the port of Lipari, a 5-minute walk away, to all Eolian islands, Milazzo, Messina and Reggio Calabria.

Upon request, the hotel arranges free transfers to and from the port of Sottomonastero upon arrival and departure.
