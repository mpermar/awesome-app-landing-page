Description

Les Sables Noires is located on the island of Vulcano, 90 metres from a black-sand beach.

It offers a garden with outdoor swimming pool and views of the splendid Ponente Bay.

WiFi is free throughout. The elegant, spacious rooms of Les Sables Noirs offer air conditioning, satellite TV and telephone and minibar.

At the bar near the reception guests can enjoy breakfast and light snacks. Drinks are available on the rooftop bar.

The spa includes a sauna, Kneipp path and a hammam. Massages are available on request.
