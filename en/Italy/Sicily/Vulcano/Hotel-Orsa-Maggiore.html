Description

Located in a small pine wood, the hotel is 350 metres from Isola di Vulcano's main bay and 1 km from the thermal spa.

The scheduled hotel shuttle takes guests to and from the port, 1. 5 km away. Free transfers are provided if booked in advance.

All of the simply furnished rooms have cool tiled floors and come with a flat-screen satellite TV, air conditioning, and a small balcony or patio.

Each includes a private bathroom complete with soft bathrobes. Breakfast at Orsa Maggiore is Italian style and features local organic products and freshly squeezed Sicilian orange juice.

The restaurant serves specialities from the Aeolian Islands including freshly caught fish.

Surrounded by lush gardens, Hotel Orsa Maggiore is just 400 metres from the famous Sabbie Nere black sands and 1 km from the town centre.
