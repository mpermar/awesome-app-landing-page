Description

Borgo San Francesco is made up of separate villas and has large, quiet gardens. There is a sun terrace, a bowling green and an archery area as well as a playground.

San Francesco Residence's apartments have well-equipped kitchens, private entrances and furnished patios. Satellite TV is provided.

Staff here are multilingual. A reading area and a free internet point are provided.

The Borgo's restaurant has lovely views and serves fresh, local food.
