Description

Set in a 19th-century house about 1 km from the historic centre of Piazza Armerina, Villa Clementine offers rooms with views of the city, garden and swimming pool.

Air-conditioned rooms vary in style and come with an LCD TV and free toiletries. Wi-Fi throughout is free of charge.

Buffet breakfast is composed of sweet and savoury food and can be served in the breakfast room or on the patio.

Clementine Villa features a garden, terrace and outdoor swimming pool. Private parking on site is free of charge.
