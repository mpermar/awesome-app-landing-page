Description

Set between Sicily's hills and beaches, 'A Nuciara Park offers an outdoor swimming pool and a modern wellness centre.

Rooms are bright and colourful with air-conditioning, a TV and a private bathroom.

Some rooms have views of the Ionian Sea. During winter, breakfast is served at your table while in summer it is buffet style.

Guests can relax at the hotel's wellness centre, strictly for adults only and including an indoor pool, sauna and Turkish bath.

'A Nuciara Park Hotel & Wellnes Center has a peaceful location just outside Furci Siculo, 15 km north of Taormina.

It is easily reached from the A18 motorway, offering quick and easy access along the coast.
