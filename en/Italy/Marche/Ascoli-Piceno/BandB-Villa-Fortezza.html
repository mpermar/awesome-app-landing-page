Description

Set in Ascoli Piceno, B&B Villa Fortezza is an elegant property with its own garden and children's playground.

Rooms are spacious and each one has a different design. Parking is free. Ranging from classic to modern, rooms here have a TV, wood floors and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas and in some rooms. The Villa Fortezza serves homemade cakes, jams and biscuits at breakfast.

Guests can relax out in the garden where there are plenty of parasols and deckchairs.

The villa is near Ascoli's Medieval Fortress and a 10-minute walk from the Castellano River and the main square of Piazza del Popolo.

You are 30 km from the beaches of San Benedetto del Tronto and the ski slopes of Monte Riselli.
