Description

Located in Porto San Giorgio, Hotel Nettuno offers 3-star accommodation with a terrace.

This 3-star hotel offers a shared lounge and a concierge service. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and free WiFi throughout the property.

A continental breakfast is available daily at the hotel. Hotel Nettuno offers a children's playground.

San Benedetto del Tronto is 30 km from the accommodation, while Civitanova Marche is 24 km away.
