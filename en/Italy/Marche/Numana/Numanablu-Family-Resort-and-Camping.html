Description

Featuring a seasonal outdoor pool and a restaurant, Numanablu Family Resort & Camping is located in Numana, a 5-minute drive from the Conero Regional Park.

A private beach area equipped with sun loungers and parasols is 400 metres away. All air-conditioned units come with a kitchenette, a satellite TV and a private bathroom with shower.

An outdoor dining area is available to guests. Various activities can be enjoyed on site, such as tennis, canoeing and cycling.

A children's playground and entertainment for adults and children are at guests' disposal.

Conero Riviera is a 10-minute drive from Numanablu Family Resort & Camping, while Shrine of the Holy House in Loreto is 9 km away.
