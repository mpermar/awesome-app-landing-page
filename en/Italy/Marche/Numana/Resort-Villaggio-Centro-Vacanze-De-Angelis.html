Description

One of our top picks in Numana.Just 800 feet from its free private beach, Centro Vacanze De Angelis features a large outdoor pool and tennis court.

Accommodations is air conditioned and includes a living area with kitchenette and flat-screen TV. Each has a bathroom with shower plus an equipped garden or veranda.

You can sample characteristic local specialties at the restaurant, or enjoy drinks, cocktails and snacks at the 2 bars. Wi-Fi is free in public areas.

You will also find a hot tub, nightclub and sun terrace on site.

Villaggio Centro Vacanze De Angelis is a 5-minute drive from the center of Numana and 5 mi from Loreto Train Station. Ancona is 16 mi away.
