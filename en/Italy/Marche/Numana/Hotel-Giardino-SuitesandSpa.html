Description

Offering a free wellness centre and air conditioned rooms with free WiFi, Hotel Giardino is on the hills next to the Conero Natural Park, just outside Numana.

In summer the free shuttle takes to the beach, 600 metres away. The hotel is surrounded by a beautiful garden, featuring an outdoor swimming pool with hydromassage area.

The wellness centre includes a Finnish sauna, a Turkish bath, a Kneipp path, and an indoor swimming pool.

Rooms come with cool tiled floors, white furniture, and a private bathroom with shower.

The suites are set in the annex building 30 metres away, and feature parquet floors and a balcony or terrace with views of Mount Conero.

A sweet breakfast is served daily. During spring and summer the restaurant is open for lunch and dinner.
