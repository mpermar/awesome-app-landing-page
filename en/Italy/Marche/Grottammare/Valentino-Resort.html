Description

Featuring a rooftop hot tub with panoramic views of the Adriatic Sea, the Valentino Resort is a design hotel with a pool and a wellness centre.

Its minimalist suites have glass bathrooms. With satellite LCD TV and a kitchenette, the suites at the Valentino offer modern comforts.

Each is air conditioned and includes a minibar and balcony. All rooms include free access to the beach.

Guests at Hotel Valentino can start the day with a cappuccino or herbal tea, together with fresh fruit and pastries.

Breakfast can also be served in guests’ rooms. The Valentino Hotel’s spa includes a Turkish bath and massage services.

A private beach 20 metres from the hotel offers discounts to the hotel’s residents.

San Benedetto del Tronto Station is one kilometre from the Valentino Resort. The A14 Autostrada Adriatica motorway is a 10-minute drive away.
