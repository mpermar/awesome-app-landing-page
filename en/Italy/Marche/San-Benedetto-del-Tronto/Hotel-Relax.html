Description

Just around the corner from the seafront and the beach, Hotel Relax is a 3-star property offering free parking in San Benedetto del Tronto.

The garden features a summer outdoor pool surrounded by sun loungers. Rooms at the Relax Hotel are modern and bright, and each one is air conditioned and has an LCD TV.

Free Wi-Fi access is provided in the hotels public areas. The restaurant on site serves meat and seafood specialities, as well as classic regional cuisine.

Breakfast is buffet style and includes a varied selection of sweet products. San Benedetto del Tronto Train Station is 1.
