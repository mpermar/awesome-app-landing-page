Description

The Imperial is a modern 4-star hotel set just 150 metres from the seaside promenade in San Benedetto del Tronto.

There is an outdoor pool, free Wi-Fi throughout, and bikes for rent. All rooms have a balcony.

Rooms at the Imperial have a a flat-screen Sky TV, a minibar, and air conditioning.

The private bathrooms are complete with shower and hairdryer. The family-run Imperial Hotel features a poolside area where the buffet breakfast is served.

The on-site restaurant is open during springtime and summer and serves traditional local cuisine.

If you book the half-board option you will have 2 deck chairs and a parasol on the private beach.
