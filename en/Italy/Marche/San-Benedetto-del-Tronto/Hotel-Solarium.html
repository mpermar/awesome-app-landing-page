Description

Set on the seafront in San Benedetto Del Tronto, Hotel Solarium features a private beach in summer and a traditional restaurant.

It offers classically furnished rooms with free WiFi and a balcony. Rooms at the Solarium all come with a satellite LCD TV, while some have Adriatic Sea views.

A sweet and savoury buffet breakfast is provided daily. The restaurant specialises in local cuisine and fish dishes.

Guests can also enjoy soft drinks served on the panoramic terrace. Free bikes are available for exploring the Le Marche coastline.

Ascoli Piceno is 39 km from the hotel, and Abruzzo Airport in Pescara is a 1-hour drive away.

A beach service is available on request from May to September, and includes access to the private beach, 1 parasol, and 2 deckchairs.
