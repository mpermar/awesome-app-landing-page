Description

Locanda Montelippo is halfway between Pesaro and Urbino, each about 20 minutes' drive away.

It offers rooms with countryside views, and features a rustic restaurant serving Le Marche cuisine.

Rooms are air conditioned and provide an LCD TV with satellite channels. Their private bathroom offers a shower and toiletries.

Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the property. The Montelippo’s restaurant features stone walls, exposed wood beams and a rural fireplace.

It is open for lunch and dinner, and prepares meals with locally produced food. This period farmhouse was restored in an eco-friendly way according to the principles of bio-architecture.
