Description

At the farm stay, rooms contain a terrace with a garden view. The private bathroom is equipped with free toiletries. All rooms have a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at the property.

Ancona is 29 miles from Agriturismo delle Rose, while San Benedetto del Tronto is 21 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Falconara Airport, 32 miles from the property.
