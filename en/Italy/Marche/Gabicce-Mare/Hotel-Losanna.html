Description

Just 90 metres from the beach, Hotel Losanna is located in Gabicce Mare. It offers an outdoor pool and free WiFi access in all public areas.

Each room here comes with air conditioning, a balcony and a minibar. A flat-screen TV with satellite channels is also included.

A sweet and savoury breakfast is offered every morning. The on-site restaurant serves local cuisine.

Losanna Hotel is 1. 5 km from Cattolica San Giovanni Train Station. Riccione is a 20-minute drive away.
