Description

Just a 2-minute walk from the beach in Gabicce Mare, Hotel Atlas features free Wi-Fi access in public areas.

There is an on-site bakery, and guests can enjoy their meals at the on-site restaurant.

With a balcony, rooms come with a TV and a work desk. Each has a private bathroom equipped with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

The restaurant serves local specialities, as well as Italian cuisine. A sweet and savoury breakfast is offered daily.

A bus, with links to Cattolica-San Giovanni-Gabicce Train Station, stops 200 metres from Atlas Hotel.
