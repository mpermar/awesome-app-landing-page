Description

Situated on Cupra Marittima beachfront, family-run Hotel baia marina has rooms with air conditioning and sea views.

Featuring free WiFi and a shared lounge, the property has a private beach, with parasol and sun beds provided.

Each room here will provide you with a flat-screen TV and a minibar. The bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries.

A restaurant and a bar are available on site. The centre of Cupra Marittima is a 2-minute drive from Hotel baia marina.
