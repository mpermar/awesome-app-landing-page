Description

Set on Senigallia's seafront, Hotel Sirena offers discounts on sun loungers and parasols at the partner beach in front.

Its restaurant serves Mediterranean specialities. Rooms at the Sirena Hotel are air conditioned and feature a TV, safe and private bathroom.

Many have a balcony overlooking the Adriatic Sea. Breakfast is buffet style, while the restaurant offers a good choice of meat and fish dishes at lunch and dinner.

Your meals are accompanied by a large buffet of side dishes. On the ground floor you will find the TV and games room, the lounge and a bar that is open 24-hours a day.

Free Wi-Fi is also available in public areas. You have excellent bus links to Senigallia Train Station and a free tourist bus around the town during the summer.

There are many restaurants and bars in the area and a sports centre with pool and sports fields just 500 metres away.
