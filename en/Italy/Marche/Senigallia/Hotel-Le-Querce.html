Description

Offering rooms with a balcony and free Wi-Fi, family-run Hotel Le Querce is set in a quiet area of Senigallia, 350 metres from fine sandy beaches and far from the railway.

It features a restaurant, a garden, and free private parking. Traditional local and Italian dishes are served at the restaurant, along with gluten-free options on request.

A sweet and savoury breakfast buffet is provided daily. Air-conditioned rooms at Le Querce come with a 32-inch LED satellite TV and a minibar.

Each has a fully equipped private bathroom. Some rooms overlook the Adriatic Sea.

The hotel offers a free shuttle service to Senigallia Train Station, 1. 4 km away, and Ancona Airport is reachable by car in 20 minutes.
