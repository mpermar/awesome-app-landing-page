Description

Parco del Lago is set in the heart of historic Montefeltro between the regions of Marche, Romagna and Tuscany.

The hotel is set in Villagrande di Montecopiolo, in a land of Renaissance tradition, at a stunning altitude of 1000 metres above sea level.

Adjacent to the hotel you will find a picturesque natural lake, a corner of paradise on Earth, where St.

Francis of Assisi once came to pray. Hotel Parco del Lago features a small grass soccer field, a paved basketball/volleyball court and a tennis court.

You can also enjoy a running track that goes around the lake. The property's 450 m² wellness centre includes an indoor pool with warm water, Turkish bath, aromatherapy and a Finnish sauna.

Massage rooms are also available. The kitchen in Parco del Lago offers the best of regional and national cuisine, with a choice of 3 menus for lunch and dinner including a vegetable buffet in the evening.
