Description

Country House Ca' Virginia offers a terrace with views of the Montefeltro countryside, 15 km from the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Urbino.

It features a spa and a salt-water outdoor swimming pool. WiFi is free throughout.

Parking is free at Ca' Virginia. The car park is not under surveillance. The farmhouse dates back to the 15th century and has been completely renovated.

The restaurant serves local specialities. It is an eco-friendly property powered by solar panels.

Nearby you will find the River Foglia and Pesaro's coastline is a 30-minute drive away.
