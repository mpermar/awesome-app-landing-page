Description

Featuring a children’s playground and outdoor swimming pool, Agriturismo Tre Querce is 2 km from Penna San Giovanni.

It offers country-style accommodation on a farm specialising in organic produce. A sweet Italian breakfast is included daily.

The on-site restaurant serves Marchigiani cuisine and uses homegrown products in each of its dishes.

With free WiFi throughout, the rooms include a TV, garden view, and private bathroom with free toiletries.

They have simple furnishings with tiled floors and wood-beamed ceilings. There is plenty of outdoor seating spread around the property, and guests might enjoy taking long walks through the vineyards and orchards.

Church of San Francesco in Penna San Giovanni is 2. 5 km away, while Fermo is a 45-minute drive from the farm.
