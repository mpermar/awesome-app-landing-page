Description

Set on a hill overlooking the surrounding Marche countryside, the Cadabò is a stone country house offering free Wi-Fi and a large garden with swimming pool.

It is located a 40-minute drive from Ancona. Rooms are decorated in rustic style and feature an LCD TV and air conditioning.

They all come with a fridge and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and toiletries.

Some feature wood-beamed ceilings. At the restaurant, you can enjoy local specialities and traditional Italian cuisine.

Here you can try the property’s own wine, oil and honey. Guest house Cadabò also features a furnished terrace with views of the surroundings, ideal for relaxing.

The property is 7 km from the SS76 state road. The coastal cities of Senigallia and Falconara Marittima can be reached by car in 30 minutes.
