Description

Most of the rooms at Hotel Continental Fano have a private balcony. Guests will find grounds and a bar on site.

Breakfast is buffet style with sweet and savory foods, plus homemade and organic products. The Continental Hotel's restaurant serves dishes from the Marche region with a choice of 3 daily specials.

Guests have discounts at a local beach. There is a free shuttle service to Fano Train Station, less than 5 minute drive away.
