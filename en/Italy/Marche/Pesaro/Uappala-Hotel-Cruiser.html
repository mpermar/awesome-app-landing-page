Description

Located right on the promenade of Pesaro and only a few steps from the historical centre, Mercure Pesaro offers panoramic views of the sea and free WiFi throughout.

With air conditioning and a satellite TV, rooms at the Mercure are en suite. Each includes a safe and minibar.

Most rooms have sea views and a balcony. Enjoy your breakfast on the sea-view terrace with a rich choice of sweet and savoury products such as cakes, croissants, and cold cuts.

A special attention is paid to vegetable dishes, and room service is available on request.

The Docks Café is an ideal place for a brunch or aperitif, while the Tormentina Sky Lounge offers you dinner with sea views.

You can relax on the partner’s beach or sunbathe at the hotels’ swimming pools. A fitness centre is also available.

The Mercure Pesaro's congress centre is equipped with high technology and provides a wide range of services.

Pesaro Train Station is 2 km away, while Miramare Airport is 35 km from the hotel.
