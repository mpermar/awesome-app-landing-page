Description

Chalet du Lys Hotel & SPA is 2 km from Gressoney La Trinité, and just 150 metres from the Saint Anna cable-car at the bottom of Monte Rosa.

It offers free Wi-Fi and a spa. The Chalet is in the village of Tschaval at the end of the regional road 44.

A sport shop, a small grocery shop and a cash-machine are just across the road. Rooms have panoramic mountain views of the Saint Anna Slopes, and some feature a fully equipped kitchenette.

The style is rustic, with wooden furniture and exposed beams on the ceilings. The spa is complete with hot tub, sauna, and Turkish bath.

There is also a library with games and toys for children, and a free ski deposit.

The restaurant specialises in local dishes, and the Irish pub on site serves the best draught beers until late.
