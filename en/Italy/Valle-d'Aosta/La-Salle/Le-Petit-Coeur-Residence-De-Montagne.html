Description

Offering spacious Alpine-style apartments with 3 balconies and mountain views, Le Petit Coeur Residence De Montagne is in Derby-Villaret, 4 km from La Salle.

It has a bar, garden and private parking. Set on 2 floors, guest apartments come with wooden furniture and floors.

Each includes bathroom, living room with kitchenette, and small lounge. You can book a typical Valdosta breakfast on site.

This traditional chalet is about 20 minutes' drive from both Aosta and Courmayeur, both also reachable by bus.
