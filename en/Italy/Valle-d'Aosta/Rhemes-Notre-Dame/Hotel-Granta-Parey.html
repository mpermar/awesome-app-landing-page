Description

Set right opposite the ski lifts and cross-country skiing slopes, Hotel Granta Parey is only 10 minutes’ walk from Rhêmes-Notre-Dame town centre.

It offers a free fitness centre and a wellness area, available with an extra cost.

Featuring free Wi-Fi and mountain views, the rooms at the Granta Parey come with a satellite flat-screen TV, fridge and wooden floors.

A buffet breakfast consists of sweet and savoury food, including handmade cakes. The restaurant provides local traditional dishes.

You can unwind in the wellness area, which consists of a hot tub, Turkish bath and sauna.

For children, there is an indoor games area. A bus stop next to the hotel provides links to Aosta.
