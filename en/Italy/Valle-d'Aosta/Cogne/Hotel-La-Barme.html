Description

La Barme offers rooms with free Wi-Fi, in the Gran Paradiso National Park. The hotel enjoys panoramic views of the mountains, and offers seasonal ski and mountain bike hire.

La Barme Hotel’s rooms are decorated in a traditional Alpine style, and come with private bathrooms, television and DVD player.

The restaurant of Hotel La Barme serves a variety of dishes typical of the Aosta Valley, complimented by a selection of regional wines.

A breakfast buffet is provided daily. Outdoor activities you can enjoy all year round include skiing, mountain biking and hiking.

You can also relax in the hotel’s spa with sauna and jacuzzi, or book a massage. Hotel La Barme is located in a small village, 2 km from Cogne, around 40 minutes’ drive from Aosta.
