Description

Hotel Sant'Orso enjoys a panoramic location in Cogne city center, overlooking the Gran Paradiso mountain group and the green fields surrounding it. The hotel features a 6562 ft² garden.

The brand new 400-m² spa center is complete with saunas, Turkish bath and a free indoor pool with hydromassage. Massages are available upon request.

Rooms at Hotel Sant'Orso have a traditional Alpine design with wood furniture and carpeted or wood floors. They come with a mini-bar and a TV with satellite channels.

Guests have free Wi-Fi access throughout. Daily newspapers are available at breakfast, which is buffet style.

The hotel is connected by a elevator to a free covered garage. At the Sant'Orso you will find a gym, cinema and ski storage area. It offers guests discounts at a partner hotel's small spa.

Set in the Aosta Valley, the hotel has good bus links to Aosta center, 16 mi away.
