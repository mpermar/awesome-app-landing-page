Description

One of our top picks in Cogne.Offering panoramic views of the Sant'Orso valley, La Madonnina Del Gran Paradiso Wellness Hotel is 650 feet from Montzeuc ski elevators. It offers free Wi-Fi, a spa and traditionally styled rooms with private bathroom and an LCD TV.

Rooms are fitted with natural-wood furnishings and paneling. Each comes with a balcony with Alpine views and free toiletries.

The wellness center includes a Finnish sauna, eucalyptus Turkish bath and ice waterfall. Guests can also enjoy a visit to the hairdresser, massage treatments or simply sipping a herbal tea in the relaxation area with Cogne Valley views.

The restaurant at La Madonnina serves local dishes and includes vegetarian options. After a day’s walking or skiing, guests can enjoy one of the hotel’s massage services.

This family-run hotel is 13 mi from the A5 Autostrada Torino-Aosta motorway.
