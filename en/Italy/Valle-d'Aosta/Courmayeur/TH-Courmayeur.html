Description

Located in Courmayeur, 700 metres from Skyway Monte Bianco, TH Courmayeur provides accommodation with a restaurant, private parking, a fitness centre and a bar.

This 4-star hotel offers ski storage space, a kids' club and free WiFi. The accommodation features evening entertainment and a 24-hour front desk.

At the hotel, rooms have a wardrobe. At TH Courmayeur every room has a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental breakfast. TH Courmayeur offers a sun terrace.

The area is popular for skiing, and bike hire is available at the hotel. Mont Blanc is 8 km from TH Courmayeur, while Courmayeur Cable car is 3.
