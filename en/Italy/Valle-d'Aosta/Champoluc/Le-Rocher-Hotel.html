Description

Set in the centre Champoluc and 450 metres from the ski lifts, Le Rocher Hotel offers a traditional, Alpine restaurant and a cosy bar.

Le Rocher Hotel has its own health centre with great amenities including aromatic shower and Kneipp treatment.

All guest rooms in the Rocher have satellite TV, a minibar, and some have balconies with mountain views.

During winter the hotel may only be available for long stays of 2 or more nights.
