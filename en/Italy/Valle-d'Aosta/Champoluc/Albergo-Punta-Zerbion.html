Description

Set at an altitude of 1450 metres, Albergo Punta Zerbion is 3 km from Ayas ski slopes.

This traditional mountain hotel offers a heated indoor pool and sauna. Its restaurant serves homemade pasta.

Rooms have a classic design with Alpine furnishings. Each one includes free WiFi, Sky TV and an en suite bathroom with hairdryer and toiletries.

Some rooms overlook the garden. Breakfast at Punta Zerbion is a sweet and savoury buffet including homemade cakes and biscuits.

The à la carte restaurant is open at lunch and dinner and serves dishes from the Aosta Valley.

Meals can be enjoyed out on the terrace in summer. Set on the Monte Rosa mountain chain, this non-smoking hotel has a ski bus to the Ayas and Champoluc ski slopes.

This service is free for guests staying 7 nights or more. Ski storage and garage parking are also provided.
