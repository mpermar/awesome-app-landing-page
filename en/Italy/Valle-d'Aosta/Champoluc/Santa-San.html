Description

Santa San is a typical mountain cottage in Antagnod (1710 metres ASL). Located close to ski lifts to the Monterosa ski area, it offers beautiful mountain views.

The Santa San is a family-run hotel, an ideal starting point for nature walks during summer, and a skiing holiday in the winter.

Cross-country skiing in Brusson is just 10 km away. Rooms come with satellite TV, a safe and private bathroom.

WiFi internet is available in public areas. Guests can leave their car at the hotel's free car park.

There is also a large communal terrace, a bar and a TV. Guests have use of a partner hotel's fitness centre.
