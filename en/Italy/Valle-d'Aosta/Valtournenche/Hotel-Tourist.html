Description

Centrally located in Valtournenche town, Hotel Tourist is only 1 km from the Valtournenche ski slopes.

It offers rooms with mountain views and free wired internet. The Tourist’s accommodation has wooden furnishings and comes with a TV, safe and en suite bathroom.

A ski deposit with boot warmer is provided for each room. Some rooms also have a balcony.

A sweet and savoury buffet breakfast is served at Tourist Hotel, while the restaurant features local specialities and Italian dishes.

Guests can enjoy drinks and snacks in the hotel’s tavern with fireplace. You will also find here a TV/reading room, while a public pool with free access and a tennis court are about 100 metres from the property.

A bus stop with links to Cervinia and Chatillon is next to the hotel. On-site parking is free.
