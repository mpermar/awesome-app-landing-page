Description

Casa Chamonin is a 10-minute drive from Pila ski slopes and just 700 metres from the cable car connecting Petite Cerise to Pila.

It offers Alpine-style accommodation with free ski storage and parking. Offering mountain views, apartments come with a flat-screen TV and wooden furnishings.

Each has an equipped kitchenette, and some also boast wood-beamed ceilings. The surrounding area is ideal for many sports, such as hiking, skiing and cycling.
