Description

Featuring a free wellness centre with sauna and hot tub, Maison Cly is set in the centre of Chamois, the only traffic-free town in Italy.

It is only reachable with the Buisson cable car. The Maison Cly is a charming property in wood and stone.

Massages are available upon request and you can find a lounge area with fireplace, a games room and a communal terrace.

All the rooms come with a flat-screen TV and free WiFi. The private bathroom is complete with towels and a hairdryer.

Some rooms offer mountain views. The property's restaurant specialises in local delicacies using only the best regional ingredients.

Meals are accompanied by a fine selection of local wines. The surrounding area where the Italian, French and Swiss Alps meet, is one of Italy’s best winter sport destinations.
