Description

The Dalai Lama Village features independent bungalows and chalets at 1500 metres above sea level.

It offers a 2-storey building with restaurant, gym, and a spa with indoor swimming pool.

The centre of Chatillon is 15 km away. The chalets and bungalows at the Dalai Lama are suitable for 2 to 4 guests.

Some of them include a kitchenette. On the ground floor of the main building you will find the bar, serving local specialities, a lounge, and the sun terrace.

The restaurant is located in the basement. Free Wi-Fi is available at reception, and a garden is complete with children's playground.
