Description

Offering elegant apartments and an independent chalet, all with panoramic views across Monte Rosa and Colle Ranzola mountains, Villa Fridau & spa is set in a mountainous area 1.

5 km outside Gressoney-Saint-Jean centre. With a traditional Alpine style, all units offer a TV, free WiFi and a fully equipped kitchenette with a microwave.

All are equipped with bed linen and towels. Some apartments feature wood-beamed ceilings, wooden floors and colourful furniture.

The villa dates back to the 1800s and features a large sun terrace, a communal lounge with a bar corner, and a library.

The property includes a modern wellness centre with sauna, Turkish bath, and 2 hot tubs.

Guests can also enjoy hay treatments, 2 waterbeds with chromotherapy and a relax area with complimentary infusions.

The Weissmatten ski slopes are around 1 km away and are easily reached via the cross-country ski tracks in front of the hotel.

The Monterosa Ski area is about 10 km away and can be reached via a local shuttle service.
