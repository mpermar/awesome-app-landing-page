Description

Hotel Aplis offers Alpine-style accommodation in Ovaro, a 10-minute drive from the Monte Zoncolan ski area and 19 km from Tolmezzo.

It features a large park with jogging trails, 2 small ponds, and a museum. Rooms and apartments are soundproofed and come with wooden floors and furnishings.

They include a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer. Free WiFi is available throughout.

Guests of the Aplis have access to a garden with barbecue facilities and a playground for children.

Hiking and mountain biking trails to Monte Zoncolan start 1 km from the Aplis. Villa Santina is 11 km away.
