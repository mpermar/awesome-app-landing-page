Description

Featuring a spa and wellness centre, fitness centre and Turkish bath, Hotel Bladen offers rooms in Sappada, a 27-minute drive from Auronzo di Cadore.

Ski storage space and sauna are also available. Each heated room is equipped with a flat-screen TV, free WiFi and a safety deposit box.

The private bathroom comes with a bath or shower, bidet and hairdryer. A continental breakfast buffet is offered daily, including hot beverages, home-made sweet pastries, eggs, cold cuts and cheese.

The à la carte restaurant serves local cuisine. The nearest ski slope is 400 metres from Bladen Hotel, while Nevelandia Theme Park is a 15-minute walk from the property.
