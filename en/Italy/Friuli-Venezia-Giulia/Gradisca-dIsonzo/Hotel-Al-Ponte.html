Description

Just a 10-minute drive from Trieste Airport, Hotel Al Ponte offers a restaurant, small spa and accommodation with free Wi-Fi.

Located in Gradisca d’Isonzo, it is surrounded by green countryside. Spacious rooms at Al Ponte are elegantly furnished with rugs, warm colours and works of art hanging on the walls.

They also feature air conditioning and a satellite flat-screen TV. In the morning, guests can enjoy a varied breakfast buffet that includes both sweet and savoury food.

During summer, breakfast can be served on a small outdoor terrace. Guests can relax in the sauna, Turkish bath and solarium, or have a massage upon request.

Cycling tours can be organised at reception. Al Ponte Hotel provides free parking, and is a 10-minute drive from the Villesse exit of the A4 motorway.
