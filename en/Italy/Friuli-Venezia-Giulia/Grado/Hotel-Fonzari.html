Description

Relax and enjoy beautiful sea views from Hotel Fonzari's 7th-floor rooftop swimming pool.

A 2-minute walk from the beach, the hotel enjoys a great position in Grado's historic centre.

Piazza Biagio Marin and Grado's Roman ruins are just a few steps away from Hotel Fonzari, as are shops, restaurants, bars and ice-cream parlours.

Go for a stroll along the seafront or take out one of the hotel's bikes for the day.

Enjoy special rates at the beach and Grado Golf Club. Hotel Fonzari's top-floor terrace has a hot tub as well as a pool, and plenty of space for sun bathing and enjoying drinks.

The restaurant is also located in this panoramic setting. You can pamper yourself with facials, massages and beauty treatments at the beauty centre, or enjoy the free fitness centre, while the kids have fun in their very own playroom.

On request the hotel can provide classic suites with children's facilities including high chairs, baby baths and more.

Hotel Fonzari's suites are spacious, with their own living room. Some have lovely sea views and all come with a furnished terrace and satellite TV.

A free internet point and Wi-Fi access are available downstairs at reception, where you can also book trips and tours.
