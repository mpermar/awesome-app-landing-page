Description

Set in in a pedestrian area in Grado's historical centre, the family-run Residence Aprilia offers modern air-conditioned accommodation with a balcony.

The beach on Grado Lagoon is just 100 metres away. Wi-Fi is free throughout. Featuring a private entrance and kitchenette, all apartments at the Aprilia Residence include a comfortable living area with LCD satellite TV.

Some overlook the lagoon. The Aprilia Residence offers bike rental. It is just 50 metres from the nearest bus stop and is 20 km from Friuli Venezia Giulia Airport.
