Description

Tenuta Primero offers a private beach and outdoor swimming pool. This 4-star camping is in Grado, facing the Valle Cavanata Natural Reserve and Golf Club Grado.

The Tenuta offers large independent bungalows with patio, air conditioning, and fully equipped kitchenette.

Each bungalow has a reserved parking place. During summer entertainment for children and adults is provided.

Guests can book one of the many sports facilities, including football pitch and tennis court.

The property is 15 km from Trieste Airport, and 20 km from Monfalcone Train Station.
