Description

Best Western Plus Park Hotel is a short walk from Pordenone Railway Station, and a few minutes from Pordenone Fiere exhibition centre.

Free Wi-Fi and bike hire are available. The eco-friendly hotel is air-conditioned and offers room service and daily newspapers.

All rooms come with an LCD TV with Sky channels, minibar and private bathroom with hairdryer.

A breakfast buffet is served daily and includes fresh coffee and a wide choice of organic, local produce as well as homemade cakes and bread.
