Description

International Beach Hotel overlooks its own private beach in Lignano Sabbiadoro. It features an outdoor swimming pool and provides air-conditioned rooms with satellite TV and balcony.

Rooms offer views of the sea or of a Mediterranean pinewood. They have classic décor and carpeted floors.

Minibar and bathroom with hairdryer are also included. Upon arrival, guests receive a beach bag with a bottle of mineral water, fruit juice and biscuits.

The Beach Hotel also offers free bicycles, and features a 24-hour reception. The on-site restaurant serves international cuisine daily and local dishes at least twice a week.

A varied breakfast is served buffet-style until 11:00 and includes homemade pastries.

The bus for Latisana stops right in front of the hotel. Golf Club Lignano is 5 km away, while the A4 Motorway is 30 minutes’ drive away.
