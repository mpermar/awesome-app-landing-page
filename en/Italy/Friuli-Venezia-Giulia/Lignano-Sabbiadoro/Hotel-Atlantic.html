Description

An ideal place to unwind, try the heated outdoor swimming pools and Jacuzzi. Relax in your spacious room with sea view balcony and free Wi-Fi connection.

Sample some local cuisine at the Atlantic Hotel restaurant specializing in fresh fish dishes. It guarantees a constantly changing, creative menu. The hotel organizes a cocktail evening every week by the pool accompanied by live music.

Hotel Atlantic has its own private beach area including free lounge chairs and sun umbrellas. Its unbeatable location right on the beach is ideal for a fun-filled vacation in Lignano Sabbiadoro.

Sabbiadoro is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, food and restaurants.
