Description

Offering free parking and free bike rental, Hotel Desiree is in central Lignano Sabbiadoro.

It is located just 50 metres from the pedestrian area and the beaches. All rooms at Desiree are air conditioned and feature an LCD TV and a private bathroom with toiletries and a hairdryer.

Included in the rate, guests enjoy 2 sun loungers and 1 parasol for each room. Breakfast is served buffet style and includes homemade cakes, fresh fruit and eggs.

The hotel’s restaurant specialises in Italian cuisine and vegetarian options. Gluten-free food is also available.

Guests have a TV and reading room, while children can enjoy the property’s games room.

Staff also offers many services for babies and children, such as baby food and highchairs.

Desiree Hotel is 200 metres from a bus stop providing links with many surrounding areas.
