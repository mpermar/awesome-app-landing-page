Description

Just 140 metres from its own private beach, the Meridianus is a 4-star hotel in Lignano Sabbiadoro.

It offers a heated swimming pool with indoor and outdoor sections and its own restaurant.

Rooms at Hotel Meridianus are air conditioned and feature satellite TV. Some have a terrace overlooking the swimming pool or pine trees.

A sweet and savoury buffet is served each morning and can be enjoyed outdoors in warm weather.

At lunch and dinner you have a fixed menu accompanied by a buffet of side dishes.
