Description

Next to Lignano Sabbiadoro's pine wood, Hotel Villa Luisa is 500 metres from its private beach with sun loungers, parasols and changing huts.

It also has swimming pools for adults and children. Rooms at Villa Luisa are air conditioned and feature a fridge and a flat-screen TV.

All rooms overlook the pine wood, and some have a private balcony. A buffet breakfast is served each morning and can be enjoyed out by the pool in warm weather.

The restaurant serves Italian cuisine including homemade cakes, bread and pasta. It hosts a dinner of Friuli specialities once a week.

Entertainment is provided on the beach in June, July and August. Children also have a play area.
