Description

Hotel Florida is in the heart of Lignano Sabbiadoro. Rooms have a balcony and free Wi-Fi.

The restaurant serves traditional Italian dishes. This family-run Florida Hotel features a host of common areas where you can sunbathe and unwind.

There is an outdoor play area, and a wellness area with sauna and hot tub. The heated swimming pool has a view of the lagoon.

A parasol and sun loungers are provided to use at the beach, from the beginning of May until the end of September.

The Florida Hotel has a spacious terrace with sun loungers, which boasts a fantastic sea view.

Guests can enjoy a lounge bar with panoramic views on the 7th floor and a bar on the 1st floor.
