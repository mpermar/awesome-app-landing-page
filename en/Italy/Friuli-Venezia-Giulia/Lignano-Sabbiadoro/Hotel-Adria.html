Description

Hotel Adria is a 2-minute walk from its private beach in Lignano Sabbiadoro, where you will find free sun loungers and parasols.

Bike rental is free and Wi-Fi is available in the lobby. Rooms are air conditioned and feature satellite TV and an en suite bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Some rooms include a balcony. A generous buffet breakfast is prepared at the Adria Hotel each morning.

The restaurant is open at lunch and dinner and has a daily menu of regional specialities.
