Description

Boasting a swimming pool, a bar and a shared lounge and views of the city, ADRIATICA aparthotel is set in Lignano Sabbiadoro and offers accommodation with free WiFi.

Fitted with a balcony, the units offer air conditioning and feature a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with bidet and a hairdryer.

A fridge is also offered, as well as a kettle and a coffee machine. A continental breakfast is available every morning at the aparthotel.

ADRIATICA aparthotel offers a sun terrace. A bicycle rental service is available at the accommodation.

Porto Lignano is 2. 1 km from ADRIATICA aparthotel. The nearest airport is Trieste Airport, 70 km from the aparthotel.
