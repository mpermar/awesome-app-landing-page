Description

Hotel Al Prater is in the seaside resort of Sabbiadoro, just 150 metres from the sandy beach.

It offers rooms with a balcony, free WiFi is also available. Air-conditioned rooms at the Al Prater feature an LED TV with SKY channels, wooden floors, and a mini bar.

Al Prater has its own restaurant, serving fish and meat dishes with a salad buffet.

A variety of other bars, restaurants and cafés are also nearby. Free bicycles are available so you can explore the local area.

Staff can advise on the outdoor activities nearby including tennis, mini golf and beach volleyball.
