Description

This sport hotel is only 300 metres from Lignano Sabbiadoro’s lively town centre and 600 metres from the sandy beach.

It features an outdoor swimming pool, 2 tennis courts, and a traditional restaurant.

All rooms at Hotel Al Cigno come with a balcony. They include a bathroom with shower, satellite TV, and a refrigerator.

The restaurant provides a generous breakfast buffet, served until 11:00. For lunch and dinner, it offers traditional food from the region.

The pool is equipped with sun loungers, chairs, and parasols. Al Cigno also provides free parasols and sun loungers for the beach as well as a free shuttle service to it.

More active guests will enjoy the 2 tennis and badminton courts, a bowling green, ping pong table, plus a children's play area and paddling pool.

You can also take advantage of the free bike rental service. Hotel Al Cigno is located 100 km east of Venice Airport, and 1-hour drive from Udine.
