Description

Appart-Hotel Holiday offers independent apartment accommodation in an excellent position, only a short distance from the centre and the beach of Lignano Sabbiadoro.

Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas. All apartments are elegantly finished and equipped with modern commodities, including a satellite TV.

They are comprised of an equipped kitchenette, and living room with double sofa bed.

Some feature a large terrace. The resort features all types of shops and facilities, restaurants, pizzerias and entertainment venues, including gardens with playgrounds.

Bicycles are available free of charge. Reception is open during the day. A private garage is available, 200 metres away from the property.

A daily cleaning service (including kitchen cleaning and dish washing) is available at extra costs.

During summer the hotel may only be bookable for weekly stays, from Saturday to Saturday.
