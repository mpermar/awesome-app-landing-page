Description

Located in the centre of Tarcento, Albergo Al Tarcentino offers modern-style accommodation with free Wi-Fi.

The property is 3 km from Tarcento Train Station which takes you to Udine. Rooms here come with a flat-screen TV and a wardrobe.

The private bathroom includes a shower, hairdryer and free toiletries. A sweet Italian breakfast with croissants, jam and hot drinks is served daily, while savoury options like eggs, cold cuts and cheese are available on request.

There is also a restaurant serving Italian cuisine and pizza. Albergo Al Tarcentino is a 15-minute drive from the Villanova Caves, while the Sappada ski slopes are 40 km away.
