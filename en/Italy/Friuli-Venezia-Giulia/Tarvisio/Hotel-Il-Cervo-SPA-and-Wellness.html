Description

Hotel Il Cervo is set in Tarvis Valley, close to the ski slopes and 500 metres from the centre of Tarvisio.

It also offers a wellness area with pool, available at an extra cost. The onsite restaurant serves regional cuisine and is open for lunch and dinner.

Massages and other beauty treatments can also be booked and enjoyed on site. Located at the foot of Mount Priesnig, Il Cervo is close to tennis courts and a golf course, located 1 km away.
