Description

This hotel in the centre of Malborghetto is only 5 km from the ski runs of Valbruna and Campo Rosso.

It features room with mountain views, free Wi-Fi, and a restaurant. Hotel La Baita comes with comfortable rooms offering design beds in wood, a bathroom with shower, and TV.

La Baita offers a breakfast buffet that includes fresh fruit. During the Winter, the property very often offers homemade cakes.

The restaurant offers Italian and international cuisine. Gluten free menus are available on request.

Upon request, this 3-star hotel offers a shuttle service to Tarvisio Boscorverde Train Station located 10 km away.
