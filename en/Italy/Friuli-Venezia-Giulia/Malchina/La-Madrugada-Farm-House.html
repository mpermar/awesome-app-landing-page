Description

Surrounded by vineyards, La Madrugada is a farm, Spa and large garden. It offers free WiFi in public areas, and a daily buffet breakfast.

Organic produce is grown and sold on site. Offering garden views, rooms at La Madrugada Agriturismo are en suite.

Each has a safe, flat-screen TV and minibar. Some rooms are suitable for disabled guests, while other rooms include a balcony.

The Spa at La Madrugada is available at extra charge. It includes a Turkish bath, sauna and hot tub.

The property can organize tasting of wine, food and oil produced on site. The property is 450 metres from the village of Malchina.

A 15-minute drive away, you can visit the Duino Castle, in the centre of Duino. Trieste is a 30-minute drive from the farm.
