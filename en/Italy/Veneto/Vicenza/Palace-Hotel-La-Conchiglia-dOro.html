Description

Offering a sun terrace and free parking, Palace Hotel La Conchiglia d'Oro is located in Vicenza and has rooms with free WiFi and air conditioning.

Most units have a balcony or a terrace. Each room here will provide you with a satellite flat-screen TV, a minibar and carpeted floors.

Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and a bidet. A restaurant serving regional specialities and a bar are available on site.

The Basilica Palladiana is a 5-minute drive from La Conchiglia d'Oro. Vicenza Train Station is 1 km away.
