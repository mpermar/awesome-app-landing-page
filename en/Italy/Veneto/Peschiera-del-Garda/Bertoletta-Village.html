Description

This 20th-century farmhouse now offers air-conditioned apartments. Bertoletta Village has a peaceful location in Peschiera del Garda and features 2 outdoor swimming pools and a hot tub.

Bertoletta is located in the Frassino Park area of Peschiera, on the banks of the small Frassino Lake.

Peschiera's historic centre and the beaches of Lake Garda are just 1. 5 km away. Apartments come with satellite TV, a fully equipped kitchen and a private balcony.

Surrounded by olive groves, the large grounds include a volleyball pitch and children's playground.

Gardaland theme park is just 5 km away, while Verona centre, Verona Arena and Fiera di Verona exhibition centre are all 25 minutes away by car.
