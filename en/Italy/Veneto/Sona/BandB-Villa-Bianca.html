Description

B&B Villa Bianca is an elegant, independent building with garden and children's playground, located 10 km from Gardaland amusement park and a 10-minute drive from Villafranca Airport.

Rooms come with free WiFi. Parking is free. Rooms at Villa Bianca come with comfortable memory foam mattresses, and a private bathroom with shower.

A generous buffet is served daily in the breakfast room. The B&B is in the centre of Sona, 15 km from Verona and 23 km from Sirmione.

The Aquardens Thermal Park and the Villa dei Cedri thermal baths are a 10-minute drive away.
