Description

Featuring a bar and free Wi-Fi throughout, Hotel Al Bosco offers rooms with classic décor in Sona.

The garden comes with tables, chairs and children’s playground. Each air-conditioned room has a TV, desk and garden views.

The private bathroom is complete with a hairdryer, shower and free toiletries. Guests can enjoy local cuisine from Veneto at the on-site restaurant.

A sweet breakfast is offered daily and includes hot drinks, croissants and pastries.
