Description

At the hotel, all rooms have a balcony. The rooms include a private bathroom with free toiletries, while some feature city views. At Hotel Danieli all rooms are equipped with air conditioning and a TV.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental breakfast.

Hotel Danieli has a playground.

Speaking English and German, staff are always on hand to help at the 24-hour front desk.

Bus Station is 1.4 miles from the hotel, while Palaturismo is 0.8 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Marco Polo Airport, 14 miles from the property.
