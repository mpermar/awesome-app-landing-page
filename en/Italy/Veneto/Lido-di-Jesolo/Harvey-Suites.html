Description

Harvey Suites is set in Jesolo's pine wood, just a few steps from its private beach.

It features a sun terrace where you can enjoy breakfast each morning. Harvey Suites has 2 swimming pools, 1 for adults and 1 for children, plus a whirlpool.

Sun loungers and umbrellas are available for use around the pool or on the beach.

This 4-star Hotel in Jesolo also offers a snack bar and a concierge service where you can get lots of useful tourist information.
