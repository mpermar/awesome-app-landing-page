Description

The rooms at the Adriatic Palace Hotel are airy and bright. Each room has a balcony with sea views, bathroom with shower, and satellite TV. The rooms are provided with free access to Sky Vision Gold.

The Palace Hotel's terrace, in front of the swimming pool, is the ideal spot to enjoy a drink or dine in style. A buffet breakfast is served at the White Restaurant which is open daily for lunch and dinner.

The Adriatic Palace is only a 1-hour drive from Venice. Guests can take advantage of the free bikes provided by the hotel to visit the town of Jesolo.

Beautiful surroundings, right on the beach, we also used the free to hire bikes from the hotel, cycled along the front and stopped to have ice cream, the children loved this. We walked to the main bus depot purchased tickets for a bus and boat to take to Venice for the day, took about an hour this way but worth it. All in all a fantastic break was had by all and would highly recommend this hotel."
