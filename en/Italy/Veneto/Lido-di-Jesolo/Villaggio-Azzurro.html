Description

The units come with tiled floors and feature a fully equipped kitchenette with a dishwasher, a dining area, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with bidet. A balcony with garden views is offered in every unit.

The resort village has a seasonal outdoor pool.

Guests can relax in the garden at the property.

Aqualandia Water Park is 5 miles from Villaggio Azzurro, while Bus Station is 2.9 miles away. The nearest airport is Marco Polo Airport, 17 miles from the accommodation.
