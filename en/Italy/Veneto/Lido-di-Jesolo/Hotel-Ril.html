Description

Overlooking its own private beach in Lido di Jesolo, Hotel Ril also features a large swimming pool, a restaurant and ultra-modern rooms.

Rooms are elegantly decorated with light-coloured bed linen, wood floors and LCD TVs.

They all feature air conditioning, satellite TV channels and a balcony with partial or full sea views.

Breakfast at the Ril Hotel is buffet style, while the restaurant serves Veneto specialities out on the terrace overlooking the beach.

Guests have a snack bar and a 24-hour reception. Free Wi-Fi is available and free parking is provided.

The hotel is surrounded by some of the most popular shops and bars in Lido di Jesolo.
