Description

Aqualandia water park is a 10 minute walk from this family-run hotel. Buses to the harbor at Punta Sabbioni stop 0.3 miles away.

All air-conditioned guest rooms provide a private bathroom complete with bathrobes, toiletries and a hairdryer. Each balcony is furnished with table and chairs.

Breakfast at the Trevi is buffet style, and includes cold meats, cheeses and sweet pastries. The restaurant serves traditional Italian dishes and local wines.

Jesolo town center is 5 miles away, and the A4 Highway is a 40 minute drive away. Parking is free.
