Description

Domino offers self-catering accommodation in the heart of Lido di Jesolo, only 250 meters from the beach.

It features an outdoor swimming pool, and free WiFi is available by the pool and in the rooms.

Hotel Domino offers a spacious hall with a bar. Guests can enjoy sun loungers and parasols by the pool and in the private beach area, and the garden is equipped with a children's area.

A partner fitness centre is also available. Each spacious suite has a fully equipped kitchen, air conditioning and a satellite flat-screen TV with Sky channels.

A balcony is also available. The restaurant offers a children's menu, Italian and pizza dishes.

There is one parking space for each studio. Guests can enjoy regional cuisine at the property's restaurant, Da Conte.
