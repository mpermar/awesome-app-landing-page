Description

Located in Lido di Jesolo, LA POSTA Sweet Home offers pet-friendly accommodation with free WiFi and pool views, 2.

4 km from Aqualandia. Free private parking is available on site. The air-conditioned apartments have a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher and an oven.

Some units feature a terrace and/or a balcony with garden views. Guests will find breakfast items in the apartment.

LA POSTA Sweet Home offers a shared seasonal outdoor pool. Guests can also enjoy free use of parasols and sun loungers at the nearby beach.

A bicycle rental service is available at this property. Congress and Convention Bureau is 90 metres from the accommodation.
