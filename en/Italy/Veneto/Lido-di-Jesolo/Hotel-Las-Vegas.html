Description

Hotel Las Vegas is right on the sea front at Lido di Jesolo and provides a private beach with snack bar.

The hotel offers beautiful views from its panoramic terrace, 2 swimming pools, and a gourmet restaurant.

The rooms are air-conditioned, and come with simple furnishings and light-wood furniture.

Some rooms offer partial or full views of the Adriatic Sea. Adults and children have their own separate pools at Las Vegas Hotel, and the beach is just a few steps away.

Sun loungers and parasols are provided free of charge, starting from the third row.

A children's play area is available, and in the hotel you will also find a reading room, and a bar.

Free bicycles are provided at reception and free WiFi is available at the internet point.

Breakfast is served until 11:00. You can enjoy lunch on the sea-view terrace, while dinner is served in the Las Vegas restaurant indoors.

The surrounding area is full of restaurants, clubs and bars, making evening entertainment easy.
