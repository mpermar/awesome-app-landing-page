Description

Offering free bikes, a private beach and outdoor pool, Hotel Manila is right in front of Lido di Jesolos beach.

It features air-conditioned rooms with a balcony, and front or side views of the Adriatic Sea.

Guests at the Manila enjoy free use of 1 parasol, 1 sun lounger and 1 deckchair on the beach.

They can relax in the hotel's garden with playground, or have a drink at the bar with outdoor patio.

Homemade cakes are served daily for breakfast, with an extensive choice of sweet and savoury items.

The Manila's restaurant prepares regional and international cuisine, plus children menus.

The hotel is about 2 km from Lido di Jesolo centre and a 5-minute drive from the Jesolo Golf Club.

Guests can park at no extra charge, either on site or in a partner parking nearby.
