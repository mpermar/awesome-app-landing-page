Description

All rooms are air-conditioned and feature a flat-screen TV, a minibar and a balcony. Most rooms include free sun umbrellas and lounge chairs to use on the beach. Some rooms have sea views.

Breakfast is served buffet style and the restaurant and bar both have large terraces overlooking the Adriatic Sea. The restaurant is open during the summer.

Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas at the Adlon Hotel. All rooms have Wi-Fi access, which is free in some rooms.

Guests have discounts at mini golf courses and tennis courts nearby.
