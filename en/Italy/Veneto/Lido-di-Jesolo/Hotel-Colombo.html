Description

Hotel Colombo is set 200 metres from its beach in Lido di Jesolo. It offers a rooftop swimming pool, and air-conditioned rooms with private balcony and satellite TV.

The Colombo offers free secured parking. The private beach is equipped with free deck chairs and parasols.

Colombo Hotel is located 300 metres from Via Bafile seaside promenade, right in front of Jesolo's Tourism Palace.

Buses to/from San Donà di Piave, Mestre and Venice are available 500 metres away.
