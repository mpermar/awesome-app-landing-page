Description

Set in a 5000-m park in the countryside, Agriturismo Ai Carpini is in Marcon, 7 km from Venice Marco Polo Airport.

It offers free parking and free bike rental. All accommodation is air conditioned and comes with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with hairdryer.

They offer views of the quiet surroundings. The apartment also features a kitchenette.

Homemade cakes and marmalades are included in Ai Carpini's continental breakfast.

WiFi is available and free of charge in the lounge and in the furnished patio. The Agriturismo is 10 minutes' drive from Golf Club Villa Condulmer, while Venice is 20 minutes away by car.

Venice can also be reached by train from the station of Mogliano Veneto, which is 4 km away.
