Description

At Colonìa Resort, all rooms are equipped with a desk. All rooms include a private bathroom equipped with a bidet and free toiletries, while certain rooms here will provide you with a kitchen. All guest rooms have a closet.

Continental and buffet breakfast options are available every morning at Colonìa Resort.

Colonìa Resort has a sun terrace. Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near Colonìa Resort.

Speaking Italian, German and English at the reception, staff are always on hand to help.

Mestre is 31 miles from Colonìa Resort, while Treviso is 19 miles away. The nearest airport is Canova Airport, 20 miles from the property.
