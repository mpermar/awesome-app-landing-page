Description

Set along Via Roma main street in Forno di Zoldo, Locanda Tana de 'l Ors offers an à la carte regional restaurant, and both spacious rooms and apartments.

A shared garden is also available. Accommodation at the Tana de 'l Ors come with modern or mountain-style décor, with parquet floors, wood-beamed ceilings and a flat-screen TV.

Apartments include a dining area with kitchenette. You can sample local meat dishes and delicious desserts in the restaurant, which is open for lunch and dinner.

Free Wi-Fi is provided in public areas, which include a snack bar. This small property lies 8 km from the Mount Civetta slopes, part of the Dolomiti Superski circuit.
