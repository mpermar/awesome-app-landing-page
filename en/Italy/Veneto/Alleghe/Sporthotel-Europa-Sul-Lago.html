Description

Set on the banks of Lake Alleghe, this hotel is only 300 metres from the ski run of Civetta.

It features rooms with a balcony, a restaurant, and free parking. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

Sporthotel Europa Sul Lago offers rooms with heating and carpeted floors. They include a private bathroom, satellite TV, and a minibar.

Guests start the day with a typical Italian breakfast. The restaurant offers international food and a good selection of wines.

Hotel’s facilities include a hot tub, a Turkish bath, and a large terrace equipped with chairs and tables facing the lake.

The staff helps guests with trekking and cycling itineraries. Guests can also enjoy fishing and sailing activities in the lake.
