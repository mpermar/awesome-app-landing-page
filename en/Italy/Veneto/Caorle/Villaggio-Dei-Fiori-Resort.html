Description

Residence Villaggio Dei Fiori offers accommodation in Caorle, a 10-minute walk from the city center.

All air conditioned, apartments feature either a balcony or patio. You will have a fully equipped kitchenette, washing machine and private bathroom.

Villaggio Dei Fiori has 2 swimming pools, open from May until September, and a sun terrace.

Guests can enjoy discounts at a partner water park, 300 metres from the property.

Italian breakfast is available at a surcharge at a caf nearby. Free parking is provided, and the nearest bus stop is just 100 metres away.
