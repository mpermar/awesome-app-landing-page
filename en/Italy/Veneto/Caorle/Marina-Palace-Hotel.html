Description

Located on the seafront and offering free private parking, Marina Palace Hotel features a private beach area, a swimming pool, and a gourmet restaurant.

All rooms have a private balcony. Each room features a cooling fan or air conditioning, and a private bathroom complete with a shower and toiletries.

Some rooms are located in an annex building. At the Marina Palace Hotel breakfast is a varied buffet, offering both sweet and savoury products.

The restaurant specialises in regional and Mediterranean cuisine. The garden is surrounded by pine trees, and features the swimming pool.

You can have a dip, or just sit and enjoy the sun as there are plenty of deckchairs and parasols.

The centre of Caorle is just 500 metres from the hotel. Jesolo and its popular beaches and clubs is a 40-minute drive away.
