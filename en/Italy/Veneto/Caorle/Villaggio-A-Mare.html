Description

Only a 2-minute walk from its free private beach, Villaggio A Mare is a 5-minute drive from Caorle town centre.

It boasts a free outdoor pool with hydromassage corner, a restaurant and 4 shops.

Offering air conditioning and free wired internet, apartments and villas come with a fully equipped kitchen, a dining/seating area with a satellite flat-screen TV, and a patio, terrace or balcony overlooking the sea or pool.

Villaggio A Mare’s guests can enjoy free international calls to Italy and Europe.

In the surroundings, sports activities such as windsurfing, snorkelling or golf are available.

During summer, guests also benefit from 1 free parasol and 2 sun loungers on the nearby private beach.

San Donà di Piave Train Station is 25 km away, while Venice Airport is 50 km from the property.
