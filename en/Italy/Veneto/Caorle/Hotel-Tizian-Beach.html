Description

Featuring free and direct access to the Spiaggia di Ponente beach in Caorle, Hotel Tizian Beach offers free Wi-Fi and classic-style rooms with a balcony.

A sweet and savoury breakfast buffet is served each morning. Free transfers to/from Caorle bus terminus are arranged on request.

A parasol and 2 sun loungers on the Hotel Tizian’s beach are also available. Rooms all have a LED satellite TV, wrought-iron beds and a bathroom with hairdryer, while some face the Mediterranean Sea.

Air conditioning and an empty fridge are available upon request. Breakfast includes homemade jams and cakes.

The restaurant and bar on the seafront is open daily and serves traditional dishes and seafood specialities.

Vegetarian and vegan options are available upon request. The hotel’s staff can help you renting car, and free bikes are available on site.

Caorle’s Cathedral is a 20-minute walk away, and Venice is reachable in 1 hour by car.
