Description

Offering free sun loungers and parasols on its private beach, Hotel Universal also features an outdoor swimming pool.

Rooms are air conditioned and feature an LCD TV with satellite channels. Breakfast is a sweet and savoury buffet.

The restaurant is open each day and serves international dishes with meat and fish options.
