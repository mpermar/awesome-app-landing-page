Description

Hotel Nederland is located in Caorle, 100 metres from the seaside. It offers free bike rental, and free sun loungers and umbrellas at its private beach.

Nederland’s rooms are air conditioned and feature a minibar and satellite TV. The hotel’s restaurant serves classic Italian cuisine, including grilled fish and meat, together with vegetarian options.

The Nederland Hotel is 24 km from the A4 Autostrada Venezia-Trieste motorway. The hotel provides free parking.
