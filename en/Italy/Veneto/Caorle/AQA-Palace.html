Description

Offering a free shuttle service to the private beach, AQA Palace is in a quiet area only a 10-minute walk from the sea and the historic centre of Caorle.

With free WiFi throughout, it offers a garden, a seasonal outdoor pool and an all-year indoor pool.

Fully air conditioned, rooms at AQA Palace feature smart TV. The private bathroom includes a shower and free toiletries.

A continental buffet breakfast is served every morning and guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant.

The wellness centre comes with an indoor heated pool, hot tub, sauna and Turkish bath.

The hotel is by a canal and close to Caorle's harbour. Buses stopping nearby offer direct links to Portogruaro Train Station and free bikes are available to guests.
