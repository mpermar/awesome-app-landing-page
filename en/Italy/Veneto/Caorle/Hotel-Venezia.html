Description

The family-run Hotel Venezia is a 2-minute walk from the seafront and its private beach in Caorle centre.

It offers parking, bike rental, and Wi-Fi, all free. Some rooms and apartments offer a balcony.

Accommodation is air-conditioned and comes with a TV. Apartments have a separate dining area with fully equipped kitchenette.

Rates include 1 sun umbrella and 2 sun loungers at the beach. Served in a dedicated room at ground floor, breakfast is sweet and savoury and includes eggs and cold cuts.

Organic products are also available on request. There is a bar with terrace and a traditional restaurant, where guests enjoy discounts.

Venezia Hotel is around a 40-minute drive from Jesolo and Lido Di Jesolo. Venice is 60 km away.
