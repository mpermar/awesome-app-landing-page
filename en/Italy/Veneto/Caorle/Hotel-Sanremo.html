Description

Hotel Sanremo is located on the beachfront in Caorle, and has a private beach area and a restaurant.

Free Wi-Fi is also available. Including a private balcony, each air-conditioned room at the Sanremo features blue furniture and curtains, as well as flowery decorations.

All of them have an LCD TV with satellite channels and a safe. Their en-suite bathrooms come with free toiletries.

Breakfast consists of a wide variety of sweet and savoury items, such as eggs and homemade cakes, and is served on the first floor with front sea view.

The restaurant is open in the summer and offers local and international cuisine. You can find a wide range of activities on offer in the private beach area, such as beach volleyball, beach soccer and a small gym.

The hotel also has a furnished terrace. The hotel is a 5-minute walk from central Caorle.

The A4 Autostrada Serenissima motorway is a 30-minute drive from the hotel. A free shuttle to the local bus station is provided.
