Description

Set in Meolo, this family-run hotel offers 3 swimming pools, a golf course and 3 tennis courts.

All air-conditioned rooms come with satellite TV. Venice is just 38 km away. Wi-Fi is free.

Ancora Sport Hotel offers a large range of sports facilities, including beach volleyball and table tennis.

Guests can book tennis, golf and swimming classes. Guests can relax with a drink or snack at the Ancora's poolside bar.

The traditional restaurant serves classic Italian cuisine. The hotel is 4 km from the A4 Motorway, and a 25-minute drive from the beaches at Lido di Jesolo.
