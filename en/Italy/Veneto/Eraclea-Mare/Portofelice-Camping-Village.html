Description

Featuring a water park and football fields, Portofelice Camping Village is located in Eraclea Mare, 5 minutes’ walk from the beach.

All units are air conditioned and feature a kitchenette and living area with sofa bed.

They open onto a patio, where guests can dine. The Portofelice is 12 km from Caorle.
