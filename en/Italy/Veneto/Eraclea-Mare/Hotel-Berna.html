Description

Offering free parking and a garden, Hotel Berna is in central Eraclea Mare, 400 metres from its own private beach.

Free bike rental and free Wi-Fi in public areas are provided. Rooms and apartments have tiled floors and wooden furniture and each features air conditioning and satellite TV.

Apartments have a fully equipped kitchenette. During summer, rates include 1 parasol and 2 sun loungers at the beach.

The ground-floor restaurant specialises in set menus of international cuisine and is open for both lunch and dinner.

A rich buffet breakfast served on the hotel terrace. Berna Hotel is a 20-minute drive from the famous seaside resort of Lido di Jesolo.
