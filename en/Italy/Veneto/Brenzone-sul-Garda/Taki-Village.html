Description

Offering an outdoor pool and private beach area, Residence Taki has self-catering accommodation with air conditioning and a lake-view balcony.

Located 200 metres from Lake Garda, it comes with free WiFi in public areas. Apartments at Residence Taki feature a TV and a kitchenette with a refrigerator and kitchenware, while some have a terrace.

Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer. A breakfast buffet consisting of sweet and savoury options is served daily.

A restaurant specialised in local and international cuisine is available on site.

Guests can relax in the garden, the shared lounge or in the hot tub. Private parking is free.

A bus stopping 100 metres from Residence Taki has links to Verona. The property is located 3 km from the centre of Castelleto di Brenzone.
