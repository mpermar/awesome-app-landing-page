Description

With an exclusive location, on its own island, the San Clemente Palace Kempinski Venice features 2 bars, 3 gourmet restaurants, and elegantly decorated rooms.

It has a swimming pool and a terrace overlooking the lagoon. The rooms are spacious and fitted with elegant furnishings.

Each comes with an LCD flat-screen TV and an en suite bathroom made of Italian marble.

Some rooms have Terrazzo floors, some overlook the lagoon, and some have a seating area.

At the San Clemente Palace you can relax and unwind by the pool, set in the property's own park and equipped with gazebos and plenty of tables and chairs.
