Ca‚Äô Maddalena is a family-run bed and breakfast immersed in the countryside around Villafranca di Verona.

It offers stylish, ethnic rooms in a renovated 18th-century country house with a rustic feel.

It boasts a scenic location on the renowned wine route, a special itinerary winding through the vineyards of Bardolino and Custoza, the most famous local wines.
