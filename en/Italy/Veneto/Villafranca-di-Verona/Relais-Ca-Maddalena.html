Description

Ca’ Maddalena is a family-run bed and breakfast immersed in the countryside around Villafranca di Verona.

It offers stylish, ethnic rooms in a renovated 18th-century country house with a rustic feel.

It boasts a scenic location on the renowned wine route, a special itinerary winding through the vineyards of Bardolino and Custoza, the most famous local wines.

A breakfast buffet of sweet and savoury items is served each morning and includes homemade cakes and marmalades as well as home-grown fruit.

The property is 1 km from Custoza, 15 km from the historic centre of Verona, and 20 km from Lake Garda and Sirmione Spa.

A shuttle is available on request to the airport and Fiera di Verona exhibition centre.
