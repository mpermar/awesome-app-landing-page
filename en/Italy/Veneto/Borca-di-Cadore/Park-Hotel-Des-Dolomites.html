Description

Just a 10-minute drive from Cortina d'Ampezzo, Park Des Dolomites is a classified historic residence immersed in a private 9-hectare park.

Rooms are spacious, with beautiful mountain views. The hotel provides free WiFi throughout, and rooms come with parquet floors.

The bathrooms have spacious showers. Breakfast is served daily at your table in the breakfast room.

You can park your car for free here. The hotel is just outside San Vito di Cadore, 13 km from Cortina d'Ampezzo.
