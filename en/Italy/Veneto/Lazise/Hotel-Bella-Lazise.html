Description

Boasting an outdoor and indoor pool, as well as a wellness centre, Hotel Bella Lazise offers rooms in the peaceful countryside, 1.

5 km from Lazise and from Lake Garda. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

All air-conditioned, rooms at this 4-star hotel include a TV, functional furniture and a private bathroom.

Some rooms have either a private balcony or terrace. There are a snack bar and shared lounge at the property and luggage storage is available.

A sandy beach on Lake Garda's shores is a 3-minute drive from the Hotel Bella Lazise, while Verona is 25 km away.
