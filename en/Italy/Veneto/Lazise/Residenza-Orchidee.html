Description

Featuring an outdoor pool and free Wi-Fi, Residenza Orchidee offers a peaceful location, a 15-minute walk from the shores of Lake Garda.

All air-conditioned apartments include a garden or balcony. Lazise is 4 km away. Apartments at the Orchidee have modern furnishings and a comfortable lounge with an LCD satellite TV.

Each includes a kitchenette with oven and fridge. Some apartments feature a washing machine and lake views.

With free parking, the apartments are 2. 5 km from the Gardaland Theme Park and a 15-minute drive from the Natura Viva Safari Reserve.
