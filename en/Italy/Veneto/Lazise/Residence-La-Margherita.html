Description

Offering a tennis court and summer swimming pool, Residence La Margherita has a quiet location in Lazise, 1 km from the shores of Lake Garda.

The garden includes a BBQ, playground and hot tub. Offering a balcony, the apartments are air conditioned and feature free Wi-Fi and a kitchenette with coffee machine and microwave.

Bed linen and bathroom towels are included. La Margherita Residence provides free parking and has a shared washing machine.

There are sun loungers and parasols around the pool, which is open from April until mid October.

This family-run hotel is 200 metres from the nearest bus stop and 9 km from Peschiera del Garda Train Station.
