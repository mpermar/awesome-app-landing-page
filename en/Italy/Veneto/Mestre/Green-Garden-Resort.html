Description

Offering a shuttle service to Venice, 12 km away, Green Garden Resort is surrounded by greenery in Mestre.

It features a tennis court, outdoor swimming pool and fitness area. Rooms come with free internet access, air conditioning, and LCD TVs.

They are all soundproofed and have extra-large beds and showers. All rooms are bright with large windows.

Access to the swimming pools and fitness area is free at scheduled times. The restaurant at Green Garden Resort is set in the large park and features an outdoor eating area.

An Italian breakfast is served between 07:00 and 10:00. Shuttles to Mestre, Marco Polo Airport, and Venice are available on reservation and at an additional cost.
