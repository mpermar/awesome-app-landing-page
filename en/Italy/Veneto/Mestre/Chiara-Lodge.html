Description

Set in a quiet, green area of town and 400 metres from Venezia Mestre Train Station, Chiara - Lodge is located just a 10-minute train ride from the centre of Venice.

Guests enjoy free WiFi, air-conditioned rooms and a free welcome drink upon arrival.

Other facilities at the Chiara - Lodge include a shared garden and luggage storage.

The nearest motorway exit is Mestre Via Miranese. Venice Airport is 12. 5 km away.
