Description

The elegant 4-star Montresor Hotel Tower is a 15-minute drive from Verona and Lake Garda.

It offers modern accommodation and a generous breakfast buffet with savoury and sweet food.

Rooms are very spacious and come with refined wood floors, large beds and private marble bathrooms.

Each one features a satellite LCD TV and air conditioning and some rooms overlook the surrounding countryside or have free Wi-Fi.

Montresor’s restaurant specialises in Italian cuisine and Veneto dishes. Meals are accompanied by the finest Italian or international wines.

The hotel is located in Bussolengo. Gardaland amusement park can be reached by car in 20 minutes.

A free shuttle bus to the fair district during the Marmomacc and Vinitaly expositions is provided.
