Description

Hotel Europeo is set just 150 metres from its own private beach in the seaside town of Lido di Sottomarina in Chioggia.

All rooms offer air conditioning, free Wi-Fi and satellite TV. The Europeo Hotel's restaurant uses only the finest ingredients to prepare classic Italian dishes.

Guests can have breakfast, lunch and dinner directly at the hotel. In summer months, rooms also include 2 sun loungers and 1 sun umbrella.

Set on the Gulf of Venice, the hotel is ideal for day trips to Venice, with ferries leaving from Sottomarina.
