Description

Airone’s rooms all come with air conditioning, satellite TV and a minibar, and some have views of the Adriatic Sea. Their bathrooms feature a hairdryer and toiletries.

The restaurant at Airone Hotel serves Italian and international cuisine, offering both a menu and buffet. Guests can also enjoy a drink on the hotel’s terrace, furnished with tables and chairs.

The hotel is 150 feet from many bus stops that provide services to historic Chioggia, Venice and Padua. The pier for private and public boats to Venice is 300 feet away.
