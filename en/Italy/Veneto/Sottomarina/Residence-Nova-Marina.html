Description

Residence Nova Marina is immersed in a 2000 m² garden. At 300 metres from the beach in Sottomarina, it features 3 swimming pools and free private parking.

The apartments are functional and simply decorated with classic-style furniture. All air-conditioned, they come with an equipped kitchenette, a balcony, and a bathroom with shower.

The residence provides tennis courts, a children's playground, and free Wi-Fi in the lobby.

In summer, kids' entertainment is also available. Residence Nova Marina is 2. 5 km from Sottomarina city centre and 4 km from Chioggia.
