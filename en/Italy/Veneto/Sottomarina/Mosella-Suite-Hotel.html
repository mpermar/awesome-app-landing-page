Description

Mosella Suite Hotel features a restaurant, free bikes, a seasonal outdoor swimming pool and fitness centre in Sottomarina.

Among the various facilities of this property are a bar, a shared lounge and a private beach area.

The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and free WiFi throughout the property.

At the hotel, every room is equipped with a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

An Italian breakfast is available daily at Mosella Suite Hotel. The accommodation offers a terrace.

Padova is 50 km from Mosella Suite Hotel. The nearest airport is Venice Marco Polo Airport, 62 km from the hotel.
