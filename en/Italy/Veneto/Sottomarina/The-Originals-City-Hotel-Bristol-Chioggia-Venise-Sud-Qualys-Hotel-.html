Description

Offering free parking and a nice garden with a salt-water-electrolysis outdoor pool, The Originals City, Hôtel Bristol Chioggia, Venise Sud is 150 metres from beach in Sottomarina di Chioggia.

Some rooms overlook the Adriatic Sea. All rooms at the Bristol feature free WiFi, air conditioning, and elegant furniture.

Some rooms also have a furnished terrace. During summer, the exclusive restaurant serves local cuisine.

Activities and entertainment are also available, some on the private beach. The hotel is a 15-minute walk from the historic centre of Chioggia, known as the little Venice.
