Description

Boasting a park with 2 swimming pools, a snack bar and a BBQ area, Residence Villa Rosa is 1.

5 km from Garda centre and from the lake shores. It offers free on-site parking and free parking by the city centre.

The Villa Rosa Residence offers apartments with a balcony or terrace. Each has a safe, cooling fan and flat-screen TV with DVD player, as well as a complete kitchenette and a bathroom with shower.

On-site facilities at the Residence include a library with international books, an ice-cream parlour with poolside terrace, an open-air fitness path and a communal laundry, operated by coins.

Guests enjoy free access to a nearby fitness centre too, and a free shuttle service to the nearest supermarket.

The property is a 20-minute drive from Caneva World Aqua Park, while Gardaland is 25 minutes away.
