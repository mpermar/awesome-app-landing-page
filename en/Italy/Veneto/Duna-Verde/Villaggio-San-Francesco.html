Description

Set in 33 hectares of private lands, Villaggio San Francesco is just 500 metres from its free private beach.

This large property offers 4 free swimming pools, 3 restaurants, a tennis court and fitness centre.

Accommodation here comes with a kitchenette and a private bathroom with shower. Each has a veranda or patio.

Towels and linen are provided. Villaggio San Francesco is complete with a mini market and nightclub.

You can enjoy a variety of water sports such as windsurfing, snorkelling and canoeing.

The property has a private beach where dogs are allowed and also an area for agility sports.

Evening entertainment and kid's clubs are included in the rate. This village is a 10-minute drive from the centre of Caorle.
