Description

At the guesthouse, every room comes with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Certain rooms are equipped with a kitchen with a dishwasher and an oven. All rooms will provide guests with a microwave.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at Villa Oriani.

The accommodation has a playground. You can play pool at Villa Oriani, and bike rental is available.

Canova Airport is 2.5 miles away.
