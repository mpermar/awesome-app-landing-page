Description

Hotel Casa A Colori is a renovated 16th-century convent a 10-minute walk from Dolo city centre.

It offers free private parking and a restaurant specialising in Veneto cuisine. All rooms have a private bathroom, and rooms for the disabled are available on request.

They have views of the gardens surrounding the hotel. At Casa A Colori bikes are available for rent, to explore the surrounding Venetian villas and the Brenta Riviera.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. The Dolo/Mirano Railway Station is 3 km away.
