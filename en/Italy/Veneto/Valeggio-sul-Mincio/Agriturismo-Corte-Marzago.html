Description

Set in a 16th-century building surrounded by vineyards, family-run Corte Marzago offers rustic-style rooms with a fireplace.

Located a 10-minute drive from Lake Garda, it features a furnished garden with BBQ facilities.

En suite rooms at the Margazo are all air conditioned and come with a sofa, TV and electric kettle.

There is a shared communal washing machine which can be used free of charge. The property features a wine cellar where guests can taste and buy the propertys own organic wines.

There is also a reading area with books and a computer. Pastries, yoghurts and cold cuts are provided daily as part of the buffet breakfast, while eggs and bacon are available on request.

In fine weather, breakfast is served outside in the courtyard. The property provides free private parking and is 4 km from Valeggio sul Mincio.
