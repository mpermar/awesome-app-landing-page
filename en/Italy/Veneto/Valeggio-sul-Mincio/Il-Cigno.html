Description

Set in the Veneto countryside, Il Cigno dates back to 1900. It now offers rustic-style apartments in a 100 m² garden featuring a swimming pool with hot tub and barbecue area.

Your apartment has free Wi-Fi and overlooks the garden and the swimming pool. All air conditioned, they come with a fully equipped kitchenette and a flat-screen TV with international satellite channels.

Bikes are available to rent from reception and you will find a number of cycle paths in the area.

With a quiet location 2. 5 km outside the village of Valeggio sul Mincio, Il Cigno is surrounded by olive groves and greenery.

Just 500 metres away you will find a bus stop for buses to Peschiera del Garda and Mantua, 14 and 24 km away respectively.
