Description

Altomincio Family Park has something for everyone with its own water park, swimming pools, and countless more sports and leisure activities; all in the breathtaking setting of Lake Garda.

Each mobile home is comfortable and fully equipped with all modern comforts. You can relax out on your veranda and in the living room with a modern kitchenette.

At Altomincio everything has been thought of for your convenience. Pick up fresh groceries at the supermarket and international newspapers, books, and souvenirs at the shop.

Surf the internet whenever and wherever you want via the comprehensive Wi-Fi connection.

Get all of the assistance you require at the tourist info point. When you are not splashing around in the water park why not try a game of volleyball, table tennis, or pool? In the summer there are bikes for hire; a great way to explore the surrounding area.

You can enjoy a water aerobics course or sunbathe around the pool while the children have fun at the kids' club.

There are a variety of sports tournaments and activities for children from 0 to 16 years.

The Altomincio Family Park serves everything from a traditional Italian breakfast to a quick snack or a reasonably priced family meal.
