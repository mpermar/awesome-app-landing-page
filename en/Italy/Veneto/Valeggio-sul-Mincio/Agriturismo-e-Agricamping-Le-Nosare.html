Description

A 10-minute drive from Lake Gardas southern shores, Agriturismo e Agricamping Le Nosare offers elegantly furnished rooms, free Wi-Fi and free bike rentals.

This property with its own farm animals sells wine, honey, and jam. The air-conditioned rooms at the Nosare Agriturismo come with parquet floors, precious textiles and a flat-screen TV.

Each also has a sofa and a fully fitted bathroom with shower. Breakfast is a sweet and savoury buffet of homemade cakes, yogurt, cereals, and coffee.

In fine weather, it can be enjoyed on the garden-view veranda. Free guided tours to the farm are arranged by the propertys staff.

You can enjoy discounts on the entrance fee at Gardaland theme park and Sigurt Park.

The Nosare is surrounded by the Morenici hills and lies 5 km from Valeggio sul Mincio.
