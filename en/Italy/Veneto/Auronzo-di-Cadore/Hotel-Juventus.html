Description

Set on the shores of Lake Auronzo, Hotel Juventus boasts rooms with views of the Dolomites and the lake.

It is 1 km from Auronzo di Cadore centre and features a bar and traditional restaurant.

Set on 4 floors, this family-run hotel offers rooms with a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with free toiletries and hairdryer.

Wi-Fi is free in public areas. Each morning you will be served a buffet breakfast of sweet and savoury foods, while the restaurant specialises in local cuisine including meat and fresh pasta dishes.

The Juventus Hotel is ideally placed for hiking and cycling excursions. You can go skiing just 3 km from the property or if you want to relax, you can take advantage of the equipped garden.

Shops and restaurants can be found approximately 200 metres from the hotel which provides free private parking.

Cortina d’Ampezzo is a 40-minute drive away and Calalzo-Pieve Cadore-Cortina Train Station is at 15 km.
