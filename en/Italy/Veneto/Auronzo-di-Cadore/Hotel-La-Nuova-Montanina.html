Description

Auronzo's La Nuova Montanina is a traditional mountain-style hotel just 100 metres from a bus stop, providing links to Cortina in summer, and to the Monte Agodo ski slopes in winter.

On-site parking is free and there is free WiFi throughout. The rooms of La Nuova Montanina offer a mountain-view balcony, a private bathroom with shower and hairdryer, and a desk.

Breakfast is a generous sweet and savoury buffet. The restaurant specialises in local dishes.
