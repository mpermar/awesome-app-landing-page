Description

The 4-star Princess Aparthotel offers free bike rental, an outdoor pool and indoor and outdoor parking areas.

It is 50 metres from its own private beach in Bibione, near the pedestrian area and shops.

Free WiFi is available. Each room and apartment have a balcony with chairs, tables and a sun lounger overlooking the Adriatic Sea.

Fitted with modern wooden furniture, each is bright and air conditioned, apartments have a living area, LCD TV, and a fully equipped kitchenette.

Each unit includes 2 deckchairs, and 1 parasol at the private beach. Breakfast is served buffet style and includes croissants and pastries, along with savoury food including cold cuts, cheese and eggs.

Aparthotel Princess features a TV and reading lounge, newspapers and a children's play area.

Staff can recommend hiking and cycling itineraries in the surrounding area. Bibione Terme spa is 200 metres away, while the pedestrian area with cycle paths is just 40 metres away.
