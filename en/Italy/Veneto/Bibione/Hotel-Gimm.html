Description

Only 5 minutes' walk from Bibione Terme spa, Hotel Gimm is a family-run property with its own private beach in Bibione.

It features an outdoor pool and rooms with balconies. Every room comes with a TV, free Wi-Fi and a balcony with tables and chairs.

The private bathrooms include a shower, bidet and hairdryer. A varied buffet breakfast is served each morning.

The Gimm Hotel's restaurant serves a choice of meat and fish dishes, accompanied by a buffet of vegetables and salad.

There are also vegetarian and children's menus. A gala dinner with music is hosted weekly and every Sunday there is a special aperitif.

You will find a parasols and deck chairs at the private beach where you have free access.

Staff can organise a range of activities including volleyball, tennis lessons and water sports.

Free bikes are available and the area features 30 km of cycle lanes. Free parking is provided and the nearest train station is in Latisana, 20 km away.
