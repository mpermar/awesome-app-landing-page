Description

Offering a heated outdoor pool, the family-run Hotel Mayer is only 100 metres from its own private beach, in central Bibione.

It features a wellness centre, and free services include Wi-Fi access, parking and bike rental.

Rooms are bright and simply furnished. Each one is air conditioned and comes with an LCD satellite TV, fridge and private bathroom.

Rates include 1 deck chair, 1 sun lounger and 1 sun umbrella at the beach. The restaurant is open every day for lunch and dinner.

Italian cuisine and local dishes can be enjoyed here. The wellness centre includes a sauna, Turkish bath and emotional showers.

Lignano Pineta and Lignano Sabbiadoro are around a 20-minute drive away from the Mayer.
