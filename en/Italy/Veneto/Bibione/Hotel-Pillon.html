Description

Hotel Pillon is located in the main street of Bibione just 200 metres from its free private beach and it has a gourmet restaurant and rooms with a private balcony.

Private parking and Wi-Fi are free. All rooms have air conditioning and an en suite bathroom with shower and free toiletries.

Each is equipped with a satellite TV and a work desk. The restaurant serves regional recipes, as well as Mediterranean cuisine.

The central location gives access to shops, restaurants and bars. Jesolo, with its discos and beaches, is a 1-hour drive from the Pillon Hotel, while Lignano Sabbiadoro is just 11 km away.
