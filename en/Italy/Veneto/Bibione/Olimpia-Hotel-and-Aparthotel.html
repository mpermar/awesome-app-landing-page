Description

Offering a swimming pool and a rooftop sun terrace, Bibione's Olimpia Hotel & Aparthotel is 50 metres from the beach and 15 minutes' walk from the Bibione Terme spa.

It features air-conditioned accommodation, and free parking is provided subject to availability.

Boasting a balcony, rooms at the Olimpia come with a TV, safe, and private bathroom.

The apartments also have a fully equipped kitchenette and 1 or 2 balconies or terraces.

The restaurant serves a buffet brunch between 07:30 and midday. For dinner you can enjoy an international buffet served at the table.

There is a bar on site, plus a TV and reading lounge. During summer rates include a parasol, sun lounger, and deck chair on the beach.
