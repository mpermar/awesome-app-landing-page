Description

One of our top picks in Bibione.Featuring private beaches, 3 pools and a wellness center, Bibione Palace Spa Hotel is an elegant seafront hotel in Bibione. With a contemporary design, it offers modern rooms with an LCD TV and balcony.

Bright and spacious, rooms at Bibione Palace Spa Hotel feature design furniture, satellite TV, air conditioning and free Wi-Fi.

Breakfast is buffet style and includes freshly baked cakes, hot food, fruit juices and smoothies. The hotel has 2 restaurants, 1 buffet-style and the other à la carte.

Staff organize kids clubs, games, and live entertainment. The wellness center, for adults only, features a heated pool, hot tub, sauna and Turkish bath. There is also a fully equipped gym.

Free bicycles are available for exploring the Veneto coastline. Venice is a 1-hour drive from the hotel, along the A4 Autostrada Venezia-Trieste motorway.
