Description

Just 400 metres from its private beach in Bibione, Grand Hotel Esplanada offers a pool with hydromassage area, a garden with gazebo, and a tennis court.

Wi-Fi is free, and all rooms have a balcony. Air-conditioned rooms at the Esplanada include an LCD satellite TV and a minibar.

The private bathroom has a hairdryer. Each room includes a parasol, sun lounger, and deckchair at the beach.

Breakfast is served in the veranda daily, and a bar is also available. At the restaurant with grill, you can enjoy a buffet lunch or an à la carte dinner.

Free soft drinks are provided with meals. A Junior Club is arranged for children, who can also have fun in their dedicated pool area.

Guests can sunbathe on the pool deck with sun loungers and deck chairs, and free towels are provided.
