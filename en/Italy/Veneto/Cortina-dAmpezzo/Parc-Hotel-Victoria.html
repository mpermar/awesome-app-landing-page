Description

Just 100 metres from the Faloria cable car, the family-run Parc Hotel Victoria has free ski storage.

It offers a pool table, games room, and a traditional bar with 36 types of international beers.

The library has an excellent range of novels and mountain guide books. Bikes are available from reception free of charge.

Children have their own play area and menu in the traditional restaurant. At lunch and dinner you can enjoy a mix of regional, Italian, and international cuisine.

The Victoria Parc Hotel hosts a happy hour at the bar and afternoon tea in the lounge every day.

A public ski bus stops 50 metres from the hotel. The Olympic Stadium is a 2-minute walk away.
