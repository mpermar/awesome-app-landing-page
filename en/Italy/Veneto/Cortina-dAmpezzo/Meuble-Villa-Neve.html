Description

Meuble Villa Neve is a simple, family-run hotel set in the heart of Cortina. It offers free outdoor parking, free Wi-Fi and a garage parking for motorbikes.

Buses stop right outside and take you to the Faloria and Tofana ski areas. The guest rooms at the Meuble Villa Neve are all en suite and equipped with parquet floors.

Some feature a balcony with views over the surrounding Tofane and Cristallo mountains.

Breakfast is a varied buffet, including sweet and savoury food. There are many restaurants nearby, open for lunch and dinner.

This charming villa is just 50 metres from the pedestrian area in the centre of Cortina.
