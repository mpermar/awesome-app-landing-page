Description

Surrounded by the Valpolicella hills, eco-friendly Hotel Valpolicella International offers a generous buffet breakfast and classic-style accommodation with free Wi-Fi.

It is located in San Pietro In Cariano, a 20-minute drive from Lake Garda and Verona.

All rooms at the Valpolicella International have a bright atmosphere and feature air conditioning or heating, a minibar, and flat-screen TV.

The private bathrooms are each complete with a hairdryer and complimentary toiletries.

Guests can enjoy pizza cooked in a wood-burning oven, traditional dishes of the region, and local wines at the restaurant next door.

Guests can also take advantage of a shared garden, reading room, and TV lounge. Discounts are available at thermal baths located 10 minutes' drive from the Valpolicella International Hotel.

The hotel is a 25-minute drive from Verona Villafranca Airport and around 10 km from the A22 motorway.
