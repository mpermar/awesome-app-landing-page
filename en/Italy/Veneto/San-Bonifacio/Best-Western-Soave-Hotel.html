Description

Upon arrival, you will be treated with a free aperitif at the property's bar.

All rooms of the Soave Hotel feature a king bed, a 20" LCD TV with Sky satellite channels, and an MP3 and DVD player. Playstation games are available to rent from the front desk.

During the summer, breakfast at the Best Western Soave Hotel is served on the grounds with a swimming pool. The buffet is complete with a wide range of fresh fruit and vegetables, cake, eggs, sausage. There is even a new, organic selection.

The elegant La Cantinetta restaurant serves Italian and regional dishes, as well as local wines.
