Description

Set in the centre of San Bonifacio, Hotel Stadio & Spa is composed of 2 buildings and offers a restaurant and rooms with free pay-per-view channels.

It features a terrace, and is just 200 metres from the central station. Air-conditioned rooms at the Stadio include a minibar and a flat-screen TV with Sky channels.

The private bathroom has free toiletries and a hairdryer. Rooms are in the main building or in the annex.

The Ristorante Stadio restaurant serves both classic Italian specialities and regional recipes accompanied by a selection of wines, including the local Soave wine.

The wellness centre is available at an extra cost and includes Finnish sauna, a Turkish bath and a hot tub.

There is also a relaxation area with herbal teas. Hotel Stadio is 1. 5 km from the Soave-San Bonifacio A4 Motorway exit, while Verona centre is reachable by car in 25 minutes.
