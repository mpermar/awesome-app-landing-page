Description

Best Western Titian Inn Hotel Treviso provides a unique environment in the Treviso’s countryside.

Situated 1 km from Treviso Sud Exit of the A27 Motorway, it offers classic air-conditioned rooms, a wellness centre and restaurant.

Each room offers air conditioning, a minibar, and free Wi-Fi. Family rooms are also available.

The Titian Inn Hotel's excellent restaurant, Il Concerto, offers exquisite cuisine, specialising in both local and international fish and meat dishes.

Breakfast is buffet-style. The wellness centre is available for relaxing treatments at extra costs.

It is complete with gym, hot tub, Turkish bath, and sauna. Free underground parking is provided.

The property is 6 km from the centre of Treviso and a 10-minute drive from its airport.

Available on request, transfers to Treviso and Venice Marco Polo airports are organised.
