Description

Set on its private beach in Cavallino Treporti, Vela Blu Camping Village features a water park with 2 swimming pools and a pizzeria restaurant.

The mobile homes offer a flat-screen TV and air conditioning. All have a private bathroom with a shower.

Vela Blu provides entertainment and a children's mini club. It also organises beach games, treasure hunts and a mini Olympics.

A buffet breakfast is available at the restaurant and there is a self-service laundry room on site.

Free parking is provided and the property is 10 minutes' drive from the port at Punta Sabbioni for boats to Venice.
