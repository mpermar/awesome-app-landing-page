Description

All units at Belvedere Village have air conditioning, and a terrace or balcony with table and chairs. There is a restaurant and a grocery store on site.

There is a bus stop 1,650 feet from the Belvedere Village, and Castelnuovo Del Garda is a mile away. Gardaland amusement park is a 10 minute drive away.
