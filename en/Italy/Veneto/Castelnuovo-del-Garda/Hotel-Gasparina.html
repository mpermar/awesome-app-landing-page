Description

Offering a private beach area, Hotel Gasparina is located in Castelnuovo del Garda.

It features air-conditioned rooms with a TV, a summer pool and a restaurant. Free parking is available on site.

Rooms at the Gasparina have a modern design, parquet floors and a private bathroom with toiletries and a hairdryer.

Some have views of Garda Lake. Homemade croissants and cakes, but also eggs and ham are served at the buffet breakfast.

During summer, guests can enjoy swimming in the free outdoor pool. A shared garden and a terrace with sun loungers are also available.

The Gardaland theme park is a 15-minute walk from the property, while Peschiera del Garda is 4 km away.
