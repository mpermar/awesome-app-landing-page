Description

Meridiana has a peaceful location in the countryside, a 5-minute drive from Castelnuovo del Garda centre.

It offers free parking and a large garden with children's playground and terrace.

Free WiFi available throughout. Rooms are air conditioned and feature a TV and a private bathroom.

Meridiana's restaurant specialises in dishes from the Veneto region, and offers both a daily 3-course fixed menu and an à la carte menu.

In warm weather, meals are served out on the terrace with panoramic views across the countryside.

A sweet breakfast is served daily, while savoury options are available at an extra cost.

Lake Garda is 7 km away from the Meridiana, and you can reach Gardaland in 7 minutes by car.

The thermal spa of Villa dei Cedri is just 3 km away, while buses to Lazise and Verona stop right outside.

On site guests can buy tickets for Gardaland, Parco Natura Viva and other amusement parks at discounted rates.
