Description

Set on its private beach on the shores of Lake Garda, Campeggio Gasparina offers free parking and modern mobile homes with air conditioning and kitchenette.

You will be less than 1 km from Gardaland theme park. The camping features an outdoor swimming pool and a free-form pool.

A children's playground, and a free ping pong table are also available. The Gasparina's restaurant is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Drinks and snacks are served at the bar, open during the day. The property is 1. 5 km from Peschiera del Garda Train Station.
