Description

Gardaland Hotel features landscaped gardens with a large lagoon pool, and free parking. It is 1.2 mi from Lake Garda, and offers a free shuttle to Italy's most famous theme park every 30 minutes.

Set in 4 fairy-tale themed pavilions, all air-conditioned rooms include a seating area and light-colored décor. Some rooms are themed, and some have a view of the gardens.

The Blue Lagoon water area is 9843 ft² and is open from late May until 15 September. It includes an outdoor poool with a large hydromassage area, a waterfall and a children's pool. Free loungers are provided, while towels are available at a small charge.

Children can enjoy entertainment shows, the games room, and many playgrounds. Tickets for Gardaland and Sea Life amusement park can be bought on site at discounted prices.

Gardaland Hotel Resort has 2 restaurants, 3 bars and a pizzeria. Breakfast is a varied buffet.
