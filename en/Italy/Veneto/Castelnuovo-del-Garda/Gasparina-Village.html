Description

Just 350 metres from Lake Garda, Gasparina Village is set in Castelnuovo del Garda and is surrounded by a garden with gazebo.

It features an outdoor swimming pool and a restaurant with rooftop terrace. The Gasparina self-catering apartments offer garden and pool views.

They feature satellite TV and a living/dining room with fully equipped kitchenette and sofa bed.

A restaurant and pizzeria serving both local and international dishes is open daily.

There is a snack bar open every day from 8:00 until 23:00, plus a minimarket is also provided.

Guests can enjoy several sports activities such as table tennis, beach volley, and soccer.

Entertainment staff speaking both English and German is available. Gardaland Fun Park is 1 km away.
