Description

Set on a 18-hole golf course, Albarella Golf Hotel offers ultra-modern rooms feature touch-screen air conditioning and a flat-screen TV with Sky channels.

Located on the peaceful island of Albarella, the Golf Hotel is surrounded by greenery.

It provides a private beach area. Beach services are available on site at an additional charge.

Each room is equipped with a satellite flat-screen TV, free WiFi access and a minibar.

Some have a spa bath. The restaurant serves specialties from the Veneto region and has panoramic views over the golf course.

A buffet breakfast is served in the bright breakfast room. Hotel Albarella Golf has a 24-hour reception where staff can book tours of the area, including boat excursions across the lagoon.
