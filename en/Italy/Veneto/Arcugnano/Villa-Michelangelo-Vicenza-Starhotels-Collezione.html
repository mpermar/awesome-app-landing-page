Description

Admire the wonderful view of the Berici Hills and enjoy the peace and quiet at Villa Michelangelo Vicenza – Starhotels Collezione, an elegant, 18th-century, stately home.

Located 6 km from Vicenza, it is surrounded by a 12-acre park with century-old trees and olive groves.

Featuring the original Palladian-style interiors, with high ceilings, antique furniture and Venetian mosaic floors, the hotel offers elegant rooms and suites, all air conditioned and equipped with a flat-screen TV, a minibar and an electric kettle.

The marble bathrooms come with bathrobes, slippers and complimentary toiletries. Relax at the semi-covered swimming pool and enjoy a drink from the poolside bar.

The restaurant is open for both lunch and dinner. It features a terrace, which overlooks the beautiful garden full of lemon trees.

It is an excellent place to sample some regional and Italian cuisine, accompanied by an exclusive selection of fine, local wines.

Business travelers will take advantage of the elegant meeting rooms, hosting up to 300 people.

Villa Michelangelo Vicenza – Starhotels Collezione is close to the city’s exhibition centre, Fiera di Vicenza, and is well connected by the A4 motorway to the rest of the region.
