Description

Each garden-view room includes air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with Sky channels, and wooden or carpeted floors. The private bathrooms each have a bathtub or shower.

At Terme Mioni Pezzato guests can enjoy fine, regional cuisine at the restaurant. Breakfast is served buffet style.

The spa town of Montegrotto Terme is just under a mile away, and the A13 Highway is reachable in 15 minutes. Vicenza is a 45-minute drive from the property.
