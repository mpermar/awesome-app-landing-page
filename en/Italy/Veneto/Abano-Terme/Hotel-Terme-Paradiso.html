Description

Boasting a wellness centre, an indoor and outdoor pools, Hotel Terme Paradiso offers accommodation in Abano Terme, a 20-minute drive from Padua.

All air conditioned, rooms at the hotel feature a balcony, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels ad a fridge.

The private bathroom is fitted with a bidet. Free toiletries and a hairdryer are provided.

Open for lunch and dinner, the on-site restaurant specialises in Italian cuisine.

Guests can also enjoy a drink at the bar. A continental buffet breakfast is available daily.

Hotel Terme Paradiso is 55 km from Venice. Venice Marco Polo Airport is a 40-minute drive from the property.
