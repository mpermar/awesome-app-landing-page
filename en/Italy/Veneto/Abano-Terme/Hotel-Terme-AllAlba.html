Description

At the Terme All'Alba Hotel, guests can relax in the spa center, featuring both an internal heated swimming pool, and an external one in the garden. Guests can book a massage, or even work out in the gym center.

The rooms are large and elegantly decorated in a classic style. Each is provided with air conditioning, a satellite TV, and free wired internet.

Breakfast is a buffet with both sweet and savory items. The restaurant serves Mediterranean and international cuisine.

The city of Padua is just 7.5 miles away. Venice Marco Polo Airport is a 45-minute drive away.
