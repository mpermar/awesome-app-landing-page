Description

Located in Abano Terme city centre, this historic Hotel Terme Patria features a full-service spa, 2 thermal pools, and rooms with free WiFi.

Guests enjoy free aquagym classes and use of the fitness centre. A balcony, satellite TV, and private bathroom with a bidet are featured in each room.

They also include a hairdryer and linen. Gardens surround Hotel Terme Patria and the spa includes beauty, massage and hair treatments, Finnish sauna, Kneipp path and hydromassage shower, all available at extra charge.

The property also includes a games room and free bike rental. The restaurant offers traditional Venetian cuisine for breakfast, and dinner with international influences.

Live piano entertainment is performed nightly. The hotel is 11 km from both Sant'Antonio Basilica and Golf Club Frassanelle.
