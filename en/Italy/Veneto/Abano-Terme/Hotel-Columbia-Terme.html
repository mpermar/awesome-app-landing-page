Description

Boasting a large heated pool with indoor and outdoor areas, Hotel Columbia Terme is in the spa town of Abano Terme.

It features a spa with sauna, Turkish bath and hot spring bath. Wi-Fi is free in public areas.

Guests at the Columbia Terme can enjoy a meal at the restaurant, rent a bike, or relax in the garden with sun terrace and playground.

The hotel's private parking is free, as well as the fitness area on site. Air conditioned and en suite, guest rooms feature a satellite TV, orthopaedic mattresses and allergy-free carpeted floors.

A sweet and savoury buffet breakfast is offered every morning. The hotel lies about 1 km from Abano Terme's city centre, at the foot of the Euganean Hills.
