Description

Offering a terrace, some units are air-conditioned and include a dining area and a seating area with a satellite TV. There's also a kitchen in some of the units equipped with a dishwasher, an oven, and a microwave.

Breakfast is available daily, and includes buffet and Italian options.

The condo hotel has a grill.

There's a garden at this property and guests can go cycling nearby.

Verona is 16 miles from Relais Villa Olivi, while Sirmione is 9 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Villafranca Airport, 15 miles from the property.
