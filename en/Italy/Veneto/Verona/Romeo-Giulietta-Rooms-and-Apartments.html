Description

Set in a 16th century building and 350 metres from Giusti Gardens and Roman Theatre and 850 metres from Juliet’s House in Piazza Dei Signori square, Romeo Giulietta offers a furnished patio, free Wi-Fi and air-conditioned rooms.

This eco-friendly accomodation has solar panels to provide energy and hot water. Guests will be offered both a free Welcome and Fun Card, providing discounts to museums, restaurants and fun parks.

Air-conditioned accommodation at the Giulietta Romeo have a flat-screen TV and a private entrance.

They feature traditional furnishings, a sofa, and tiled floors. The apartment comes with a fully-equipped kitchen with a living and dining area.

A sweet Italian-style breakfast is served in a bar 50 metres from the property, which includes a cappuccino, pastries and juices.

Guests can enjoy discounts at numerous restaurants in the area, serving both local and international specialities.

From the property you can visit the famous Verona Arena in less than a 15-minute walk.
