Description

Best Western Plus Hotel Villa Tacchi is a Palladian villa of the 17th-century, set in parkland 7 km from Vicenza.

It offers an outdoor swimming pool and a private lake surrounded by tall trees. Hotel Villa Tacchi's rooms are spacious and come with typically Venetian ceilings, with exposed beams.

Free Wi-Fi is available in the elegant lobby. Ideally located in the centre of the Veneto region, Villa Tacchi Hotel offers free parking.

Venice and Verona are both less than an hour away by car. This Best Western's restaurant prepares typical regional dishes for lunch and dinner.
