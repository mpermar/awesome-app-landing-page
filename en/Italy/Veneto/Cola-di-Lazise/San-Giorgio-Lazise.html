Description

At the bed and breakfast, every room is equipped with a closet and a flat-screen TV. All rooms come with a private bathroom, and certain rooms also offer a terrace. Guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

The daily breakfast offers buffet and Italian options.

Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near San Giorgio Lazise.

Verona is 13 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Villafranca Airport, 9 miles from San Giorgio Lazise.
