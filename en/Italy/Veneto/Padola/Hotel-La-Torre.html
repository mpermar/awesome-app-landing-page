Description

Set at the foot of the Dolomites, this modern hotel features a sun terrace, a restaurant and a free wellness centre.

All ultra modern rooms come with a flat-screen satellite TV. Wi-Fi is free in public areas.

Hotel La Torre is just 100 metres from the Val Comelico Ski Lifts, part of the Dolomiti Superski zone.

The hotel provides a heated ski deposit, and a cross-country skiing stadium is right next door.

All ultra modern rooms have parquet floors and a fully equipped private bathroom.

Many offer lovely mountain views, and some have a balcony. At the spa, you can relax in the Finnish sauna and Turkish bath, or sunbathe on the rooftop terrace.

A Technogym fitness area is also available. Traditional regional dishes made with local ingredients are served at the restaurant.

Later, you can relax in the cosy lounge area and enjoy a cocktail from the bar. The hotel is 30-minutes’ drive from Calalzo di Cadore Train Station, and buses for the station leave from outside the hotel entrance.
