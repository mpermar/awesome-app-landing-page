Description

Set in the hills around Lake Garda, this 4-star property is 5 minutes' drive from Torri del Benaco centre.

It offers panoramic lake views and extensive grounds with excellent sports facilities.

Le Torri Del Garda includes 2 outdoor pools, one for adults and one for children, as well as a wellness centre with 2 indoor pools, a sauna and steam bath, and massages can be booked.

You will also have a children's playground, and a volleyball and football pitch. Indoors there is a gym and a games room with snooker and table tennis.

Rooms and suites have warm colour schemes and tiled or wood floors. All very spacious, they also come with air conditioning in the sleeping area, satellite TV and a private balcony or patio.

Suites also include a kitchenette. Le Torri Del Garda serves a large buffet each morning and offers tourist information and bike rental at reception.

Free parking is provided. Located in Albisano, the property is a short drive or a 35-minute walk from the lake.
