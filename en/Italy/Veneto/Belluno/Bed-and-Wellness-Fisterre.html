Description

Offering a tennis court, spa and wellness centre, Bed & Wellness Fisterre is located in Belluno, a 15-minute walk from the train station.

Rooms boast free WiFi, a private sauna and Turkish bath. All the rooms here will provide you with a satellite flat-screen TV and an electric kettle.

Featuring a bath or shower, the private bathrooms also come with free toiletries, bathrobes and a hairdryer.

A shared hot tube and a heated outdoor pool are also available on site. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including skiing, cycling and fishing.

Ponte Nelle Alpi is a 10-minute drive from the property. The Dolomiti Bellunesi National Park is 11 km away.
