Description

Resort La Mola is an 18th-century country house with elegant, rustic rooms. Set on the hills of Custoza in the Verona countryside, it is just 10 km from Lake Garda.

Parking is free. The property is 8 km from Verona Airport, and offers a free shuttle during the day.

Sommacampagna is 6 km away and you can reach the centre of Verona in 30 minutes by car.

With terracotta floors and exposed beams on the ceilings, each room is equipped with air conditioning, minibar, and private bathroom.

Breakfast is buffet style. La Mola is set in its own private park, equipped with outdoor swimming pool and including a small river.

Tables, chairs and parasols are available free of charge. The restaurant is set in a separate building at the centre of the park and offers delicious samples of Veronese cuisine.

Many nature trails start nearby. You can follow them on foot, on horseback or by bike.
