Description

Set a 5-minute drive from Caldiero and 15 km from Verona, Hotel Ristorante Brusco offers a restaurant and classic-style accommodation with air conditioning.

Free Wi-Fi is available throughout. Rooms at the Ristorante Brusco Hotel feature a TV, desk and private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

An international buffet breakfast is served daily. The restaurant specialises in typical Veneto cuisine.

The property provides free private parking on site. Caldiero Train Station, with links to Vicenza, is 350 metres from the property, while Lake Garda is a 30-minute drive away.
