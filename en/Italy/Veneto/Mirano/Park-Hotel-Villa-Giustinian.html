Description

Former summer residence of a Venetian patriarch, Villa Giustinian dates back to the 18th century.

The hotel is surrounded by an 18,000-m² park with free swimming pool. The bus to Venice stops outside the main gate.

Park Hotel Villa Giustinian also includes 2 modern buildings. All rooms are elegantly furnished with antiques, and offer air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, and a satellite TV.

Breakfast is a varied buffet. The hotel is located in Mirano, along the Brenta Riviera, and just 4 km from Golf Club Ca’ della Nave.
