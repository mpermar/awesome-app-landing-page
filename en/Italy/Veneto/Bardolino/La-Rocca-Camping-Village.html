Description

Offering a peaceful location on the shores of Lake Garda, Camping La Rocca is 2. 5 km north of Bardolino.

All modern mobile homes and apartments come with air conditioning and a terrace or patio.

With a summer outdoor pool and sun terrace, La Rocca is set in large grounds with a playground and fitness centre.

Bikes can be hired on site and staff organise cycling tours. Accommodation includes a barbecue and a kitchen with fridge and stove.

Mobile homes have a garden-view patio, while apartments boast a dishwasher, microwave and a lake-view terrace.

The complex includes a supermarket and bazaar. The restaurant serves classic Italian dishes and is also open for breakfast.
