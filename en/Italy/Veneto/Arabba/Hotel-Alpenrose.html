Description

Hotel Alpenrose is located a 10-minute walk from the centre of Arabba and only 100 metres from the Burz ski lifts.

Overlooking the Dolomites, it offers rooms with free WiFi and apartments located in an annex, 100 metres from the hotel.

Featuring a mountain view, the accommodation comes with wooden furnishings, an LCD TV and bathroom with free toiletries.

All apartments have a balcony and free covered parking. Breakfast at Alpenrose Hotel consists of sweet and savoury food.

Guests can try typical Ladin dishes in the hotel’s restaurant. A sauna, hot tub and Turkish bath are found in the wellness centre.

Bikes can be rented for free. The closest bus stop providing connections to Arabba is 400 metres away and a free shuttle service from the centre of Arabba can be organized on request.
