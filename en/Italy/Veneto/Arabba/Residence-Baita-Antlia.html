Description

Residence Baita Antlia offers traditional mountain-style apartments in the charming little village of Arabba, close to ski slopes and mountain trails.

You will find a wellness centre on site. All the apartments enjoy beautiful mountain views, some come with hydromassage bath or shower.

Commodities, bed linen, and towels are free. Residence Baita Antlia's wellness centre features sauna, Turkish bath and hot tub.

There are also a solarium and a gym. Baita Antlia’s Stube bar is a welcoming wooden tavern.

There is a free internet point at reception, and children’s toys are provided. The property is near the Arabba - Porta Vescovo cable car.
