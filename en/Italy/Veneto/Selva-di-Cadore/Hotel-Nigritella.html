Description

Set in front of the 2 UNESCO World Heritage Sites of Mount Civetta and Massiccio del Pelmo, Hotel Nigritella offers a free bus to the nearest ski lifts and a games room with billards.

Rooms at the Nigritella have a traditional design and come with satellite TV, a safe and a private bathroom with hairdryer.

Most have a balcony. Breakfast at the Nigritella Hotel is buffet style. The restaurant offers various types of menus at dinner.

Snacks and drinks are provided for children during the entertainment programme in the afternoons.

This hotel has a small wellness area where you can use the indoor pool with whirlpool for free.
