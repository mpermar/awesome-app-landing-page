Description

Situated in a tranquil location on Venice Lido, Oasis Lagoon & Residence offers air-conditioned accommodation with free Wi-Fi.

It is an 8-minute walk from Santa Maria Elisabetta Vaporetto water bus stop, which links to San Marco Square in 10 minutes.

This family-run property offers a choice of suites and apartments. All feature a satellite flat-screen TV, a seating area with sofa and a private bathroom.

Some have a balcony with lagoon views. Guests can enjoy special rates at a nearby beach club, and there are plenty of bars and restaurants in the immediate area.

Set just 3 minutes’ walk from a sandy beach, the Oasis Lagoon is well placed for both sightseeing and relaxation.
