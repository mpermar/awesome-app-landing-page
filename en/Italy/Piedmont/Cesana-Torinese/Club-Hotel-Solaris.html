Description

Located in Cesana Torinese, 300 metres from Rafuyel, Club Hotel Solaris provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a bar and a shared lounge.

The accommodation offers ski-to-door access and a ski pass sales point, as well as a garden and a terrace.

The accommodation features evening entertainment and a kids' club. At the hotel, each room comes with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

Guest rooms include a wardrobe. Club Hotel Solaris offers a buffet or Italian breakfast.

The accommodation offers a children's playground. The area is popular for skiing, and ski equipment hire and car hire are available at Club Hotel Solaris.

Popular points of interest near the hotel include Vialattea, Cesana - Ski Lodge and Cesana San Sicario.

The nearest airport is Turin, 99 km from Club Hotel Solaris, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
