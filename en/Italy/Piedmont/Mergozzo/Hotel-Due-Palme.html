Description

Hotel Due Palme overlooks peaceful Lake Mergozzo and is just 2 km from Lake Maggiore.

Offering panoramic lake and mountain views, it also features a private beach area.

Due Palme is close to the Ossola Valley. It has excellent bus links to Lake Maggiore and Stresa.

All rooms come with satellite TV and free Wi-Fi. Some have lake views. In summer, Hotel Due Palme offers a lake-view terrace where you can dine on foods typical of the region.
