Description

At the bed and breakfast, the rooms are equipped with a closet. Rooms are equipped with a coffee machine, while some have a terrace. At Il Bumbunin, all rooms are equipped with a seating area.

A continental or Italian breakfast is served at the property.

The accommodation has a playground. Guests at Il Bumbunin will be able to enjoy activities in and around Belveglio, like cycling.

Alba is 17 miles from the bed and breakfast, while Acqui Terme is 14 miles from the property. Cristoforo Colombo Airport is 39 miles away.
