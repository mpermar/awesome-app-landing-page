Description

With its lush garden and outdoor swimming pool, 4-star La Ruota provides nicely furnished accommodation with free Wi-Fi access.

It is a 15-minute drive from both Cuneo and Mondovì. Your room at Hotel La Ruota has an elegant feel and features satellite TV, air conditioning and a balcony.

Some rooms also come with either a seating or dining area. A varied breakfast including sweet and savoury options is served daily in the breakfast room.

Fresh seafood and meat, as well as Piedmont cuisine can be enjoyed at the property’s restaurant.

Relaxing moments can be spent in the sauna or at the gym, and there is also a terrace on the ground floor, equipped with tables, chairs and sun umbrellas.

The property is a 20-minute drive from Borgo San Dalmazzo and 18 km from the A33 motorway.
