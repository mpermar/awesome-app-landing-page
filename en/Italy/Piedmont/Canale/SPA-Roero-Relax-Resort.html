Description

At the resort, the rooms come with a desk. Every room comes with air conditioning, and certain rooms come with a balcony. The rooms include a closet.

A buffet breakfast is served daily at the property.

Guests at SPA Roero Relax Resort will be able to enjoy activities in and around Canale, like cycling.

Staff at the accommodation are available to give advice at the 24-hour front desk.

Turin is 24 miles from SPA Roero Relax Resort. The nearest airport is Levaldigi Airport, 24 miles from the property.
