Description

In Costigliole d'Asti in the Piedmont countryside, this hotel is set in the Monferrato hills.

It offers free parking and air-conditioned rooms with a balcony. Your room at Hotel Langhe & Monferrato comes with free WiFi, a minibar, and a private bathroom with hairdryer.

Some rooms are complete with a spa bath. Upon arrival you will be welcomed with a bottle of wine in the room.

Breakfast is buffet style, and drinks are served at the bar. Langhe & Monferrato Hotel is located just off the A33 motorway and is 20 minutes' drive from the towns of Alba and Asti, famous for its wine.
