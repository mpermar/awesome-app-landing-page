Description

The villa is composed of a fully equipped kitchen, and 1 bathroom. A flat-screen TV with satellite channels is offered.

The villa offers a hot tub. A grill and a playground are available for guests at Villa Cometa to use.

Stresa is 27 miles from the accommodation, while Varese is 28 miles away. Malpensa Airport is 14 miles from the property.
