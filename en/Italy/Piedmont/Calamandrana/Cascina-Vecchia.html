Description

At the guesthouse, each room has a desk. Every room is equipped with air conditioning, and some units at Cascina Vecchia have a terrace. Guest rooms include a closet.

An Italian breakfast is served daily at the property. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of Italian dishes.

The accommodation has a playground. Guests at Cascina Vecchia will be able to enjoy activities in and around Calamandrana, like cycling.

Alba is 15 miles from the guesthouse, while Acqui Terme is 9 miles away. The nearest airport is Cristoforo Colombo Airport, 35 miles from Cascina Vecchia.
