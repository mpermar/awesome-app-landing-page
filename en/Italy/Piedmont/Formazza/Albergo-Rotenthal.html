Description

The rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels. With a private bathroom, rooms at Albergo Rotenthal also feature a mountain view. Guest rooms have a desk.

An Italian breakfast is served at the property.

The accommodation has a playground. Guests at Albergo Rotenthal will be able to enjoy activities in and around Formazza, like skiing.

Toce Falls is 2.2 miles from the hotel. The nearest airport is Malpensa Airport, 53 miles from Albergo Rotenthal.
