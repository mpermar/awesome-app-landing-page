Description

Set in the countryside of Novi Ligure, 4 km from the central train station, Cascina degli Ulivi produces organic wine, vegetables and grain.

It features rustic-style rooms and a traditional organic restaurant. Rooms at Agriturismo Cascina degli Ulivi have wooden furnishings and a private bathroom with shower.

Some rooms are on 2 levels and come with wood-beamed ceilings. The restaurant, open daily, serves regional specialities made with bread, meat, cheese and vegetables produced on site.

The property is a 15-minute drive from the A7 Motorway, and 3 km from the historical centre of Novi Ligure, where buses link to Milan and Genoa.
