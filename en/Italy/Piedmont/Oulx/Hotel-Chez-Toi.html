Description

Chez Toi Hotel is centrally located in the historic village of Oulx, in the High Susa Valley, part of the Via Lattea Ski Resort.

The free shuttle to/from the slopes stops nearby. Offering free Wi-Fi, rooms at the Chez Toi include a satellite TV, safe and minibar.

Each has a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer. There is a ski trainer right on site, and guests of Hotel Chez Toi enjoy discounted rates at nearby ski schools and ski rental stores.

Guests also enjoy discounted rated at a partner restaurant, 5 km away. The area surrounding the Chez Toi includes shopping centres, restaurants and pubs; the railway station is also nearby.

During summer you can enjoy touring the area on horseback, on mountain bikes, quad motor bikes or hiking and trekking.
