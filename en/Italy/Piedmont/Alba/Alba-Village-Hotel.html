Description

Set in a quiet and green area, Alba Village Hotel is 2. 5 km from the centre of Alba.

It offers air-conditioned accommodation with satellite TV, and features a swimming pool.

Rooms, apartments and mobile homes come with a private bathroom with hairdryer. They all have cool tiled floors, and some feature exposed wood beams.

Free Wi-Fi is available at the hotel's reception. A wellness area with a sauna are available at a surcharge.

Alba Village is just 50 metres from a sports centre with football pitch, and tennis and volleyball courts.
