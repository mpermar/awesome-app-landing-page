Description

Featuring a seasonal outdoor pool and a garden with BBQ facilities, Camping Village Lago Maggiore offers modern accommodation in Dormelletto.

Located on the shores of Lake Maggiore, the property is a few steps from the beach and 5-minute drive from Arona.

The apartments come with a TV, a dining area and a fully equipped kitchen. The private bathroom includes a shower.

Guests can have breakfast at the property’s bar. The on-site restaurant is also available and serves local, Italian and International cuisine.

Free WiFi is available in outdoor areas. Camping Village Lago Maggiore is a 30-minute drive from Milan Malpensa Airport.
