Description

Set in Castel Ratti, Il Fiorile's rooms overlook the garden. Each one is furnished in a typical Piedmont style, with retro furnishings and modern facilities including free Wi-Fi and LCD TV.

Il Fiorile is a family-run hotel set in a converted farmhouse. The restaurant serves local cuisine, and a savoury breakfast is available on request.

The hotel offers free parking and is a short drive from Borghetto Borbera in the Borbera Valley.
