Description

Set in an 18th-century castle in Castell’Alfero, La Foresteria del Castello offers a free spa and rustic-style rooms with free WiFi access.

It is surrounded by a vast garden and offers panoramic views of the Piedmont hills.

The air-conditioned rooms are in classic style and provide a flat-screen TV and minibar.

The private bathroom includes a shower, hairdryer and free toiletries. Relaxing massages and physiotherapy sessions can be booked on request.

Guests of the Foresteria have free access to a spa featuring Himalayan Salt treatments.

A salt cave and tub, spa showers, and a sauna with pink salt crystals are available.

La Foresteria del Castello is 10 km from Asti center. The Sacred Mount of Crea UNESCO Heritage Site UNESCO Heritage Site is a 25-minute drive away.
