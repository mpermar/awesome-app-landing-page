Description

Relais Palazzo Paleologi Sec. XIV offers elegant, spacious accommodation with self-catering facilities, and a fine restaurant.

It is in the ancient village of Lu Monferrato, 20 kilometres north of Alessandria.

This historic property is an ideal base for exploring the hills of the Langhe in the heart of Piedmont.

In your room or apartment you will find air conditioning, free internet access, and cooking facilities with a fridge.

Breakfast is available between 08:00 and 10:00. Parking is free at Palazzo Paleologi.

Bikes are available to rent if requested in advance. Staff can organise wine and food tours around the area, and inform you about discounts at nearby health centres, golf clubs, and horse-riding stables.
