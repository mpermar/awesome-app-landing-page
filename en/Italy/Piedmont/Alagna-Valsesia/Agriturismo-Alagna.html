Description

Just 600 metres from the Alagna Freeride Paradise ski area, the Alagna offers country-style rooms with wooden floors and free Wi-Fi.

It features a furnished garden and terrace, where breakfast is served in summer. Wooden furnished rooms at Agriturismo Alagna include satellite flat-screen TV, a sofa, and a fan.

The private bathroom has free toiletries plus a hairdryer. Some rooms have a dining area and refrigerator.

Breakfast here includes homemade cakes, fresh milk from the farm, and cheeses. Cold cuts and eggs can also be requested.

Activities nearby include fishing, horse riding, and canoeing. The surrounding area also offers hiking and cycling paths.

A free shuttle service takes guests to the nearest ski lifts. Set in the Valsesia Alpine Valley, the property is 500 metres from a bus stop linking to Novara and Vercelli.

Alagna is connected to Gressoney with a cable car passing through the Salati Pass.
