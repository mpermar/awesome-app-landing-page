Description

The Fontaine Bleue is a small hotel overlooking Lake Orta, and located 3 km from the centre of town.

It offers a restaurant and free entry to the lake. All rooms offer lake views and free WiFi.

The traditional restaurant features an indoor dining room and a terrace overlooking the water.

Breakfast is buffet style. Rooms at Hotel Fontaine Bleue are soundproofed and en suite, and come with a flat-screen TV and minibar.

Some rooms include a balcony. A free internet terminal is available at reception.

Boats to picturesque Giulio Island leave from Bagnera, 4 km away. It is a 25-minute drive to the A26 Motorway, and on-site indoor parking is free.
