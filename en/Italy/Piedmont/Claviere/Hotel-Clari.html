Description

Hotel Clari is set in Claviere, a charming village of chalets in the Via Lattea winter sports area.

It offers a wellness centre and gym. Parking is free and there is a free shuttle to the ski slopes.

Rooms feature elegant parquet floors, exposed wooden beams on the ceilings, and a private bathroom.

Each room comes with a TV with satellite channels. At the Clari Hotel you will find a typical restaurant and a pub serving drinks, snacks, and pizza.

Breakfast is a varied buffet. The wellness centre includes a sauna, hot tub, and Turkish bath.

There is a volley ball pitch and a large garden. As a guest at the Clari you can enjoy special rates at nearby ski and snowboard schools.

During summer the hotel organises horse riding, Nordic walking, and mountain biking with a guide of the National Academy of Mountain Bike.
