Description

At the bed and breakfast, each room is equipped with a closet and a flat-screen TV. All rooms include a private bathroom with free toiletries, while some rooms will provide you with a terrace and others also feature pool views. Guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

A continental breakfast is served daily at the property.

Villa Gremi' has a playground. The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental is available at the accommodation.

Alba is 5 miles from Villa Gremi', while Cuneo is 27 miles away. Levaldigi Airport is 18 miles from the property.
