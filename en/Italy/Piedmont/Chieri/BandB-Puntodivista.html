Description

At the bed and breakfast, each room comes with a balcony with a mountain view. Rooms include a private bathroom with a bidet and free toiletries. At B&B Puntodivista every room comes with a closet and a TV.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at the accommodation.

B&B Puntodivista has a playground. Cycling and hiking are among the activities that guests of the bed and breakfast can find nearby.

Turin is 7 miles from B&B Puntodivista, while Alba is 25 miles away. The nearest airport is Turin Airport, 14 miles from the property.
