Description

Boasting a peaceful location and lake views, Casa Fantini - Lake Time is a boutique hotel on the shores of Lake Orta, in Pella.

Combining contemporary design and natural materials, rooms and suites at Casa Fantini come with wooden floors and elegant textiles.

All units are air conditioned and feature a balcony or a terrace overlooking San Giulio Island.

The spacious bathroom includes a shower with Turkish bath. You can enjoy the surrounding nature while relaxing in the garden.

In the warmer months, an outdoor pool is at guests' disposal. Free WiFi is featured throughout.

Orta San Giulio, on the other side of the lake, can be reached via taxi boat. Milan Malpensa Airport is 45 km away, while Milan city centre can be reached by car in about 60 minutes.
