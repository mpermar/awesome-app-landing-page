Description

Offering a swimming pool, sun terrace and restaurant, Stevano Albergo e Ristorante features simple rooms in Cantalupo Ligure.

Set on the quiet hills of Piedmont, it is a 5-minute walk from the mediaeval Borgo Adorno Castle.

All rooms at the Stevano are en suite and come with a TV, desk and shower or bath.

Each offers views of either the swimming pool, the valley or the town. Floors are carpeted or wooden.

In summer, guests can ask the property's bar to be served drinks at the sun loungers around the seasonal pool.

A continental breakfast is prepared daily, with a chance of requesting the full english option.

Offering local and Italian cuisine, the restaurant is open every day during summer and from Wednesday to Sunday during winter.

A private parking is provided for free. A 20-minute drive from the A7 motorway to Genoa and Milan, the property is 1 hour from the Aquarium of Genoa.
