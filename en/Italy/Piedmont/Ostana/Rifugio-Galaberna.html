Description

Offering a restaurant and a tennis court, Rifugio Galaberna is located in Ostana.

It offers rooms with rustic décor and free WiFi throughout. Each room here comes with a balcony, wardrobe and a patio.

The private bathroom includes towels. A typical Italian breakfast is offered every morning.

It includes hot drinks, pastries and croissants. Rifugio Galaberna is a 15-minute drive from Paesana.
