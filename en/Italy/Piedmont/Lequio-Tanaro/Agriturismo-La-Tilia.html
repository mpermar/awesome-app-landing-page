Description

Located in the countryside of Lequio Tanaro, Agriturismo La Tilia is a farmhouse growing hazelnuts.

It offers a garden-view restaurant with local homemade specialities. A buffet breakfast consists of sweet food, including homemade jams and cakes.

On request, savoury food is also provided. La Tilia’s rustic-style rooms come with a satellite flat-screen TV and private bathroom.

All rooms feature free Wi-Fi and overlook the garden. Some have a balcony. The city of Alba, Italy’s truffle capital, is 30 km from the farm.
