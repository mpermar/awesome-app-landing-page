Description

Offering a tennis court, hot tub and bike rentals free of charge, Albergo Miramonti has a quiet location in the village of Frabosa Soprana. This hotel also features classic-style rooms, free Wi-Fi and 2 restaurants.

Albergo Miramonti is just 150 feet from the Mondole ski slopes and ski elevator. Special rates for guests are provided at a ski school opposite to the Miramonti.

Facing the surrounding valley and pinewood or the hotel’s garden, the rooms with attached bathroom have classic décor and a flat-screen satellite TV. Most include a balcony.

One of the 2 restaurants proposes local and national dishes from the à la carte menu. A full American breakfast is served daily.

This property is 1,000 feet from a bus stop linking you to Mondovi and its train station, 9.3 mi away. From there, you can catch scheduled trains to Turin and Savona.
