Description

Featuring ski-to-door access and a garden, Albergo Martinet has rooms with a satellite flat-screen TV and mountain views.

Located in Vernante, it comes with free WiFi and free parking. Each room here will provide you with a private bathroom including a hairdryer, slippers and a hairdryer.

Some have a balcony. A restaurant offering local specialities and a bar are available on site.

The property is well placed for hiking and cycling excursions. You can also relax in the shared lounge or in the sun terrace.

Cuneo is reachable in 25 minutes by car. The Maritime Alps Nature Park is 3 km from Albergo Martinet.
