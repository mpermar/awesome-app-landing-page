Description

Featuring an on-site restaurant, Villa Val Lemme is surrounded by vineyards and authentic Italian villages in the beautiful Piedmont, an hour's drive from Milan.

Free WiFi is available. Certain units feature a seating area for your convenience.

Rooms have a private bathroom. Guests can enjoy the large pool at Villa Val Lemme or the scenic park.

You can play billiards at this hotel, and free use of bicycles is available. Free private parking is available on site.

Villa Val Lemme is 10 minutes' drive from the Golf Club Villa Carolina and 17 km from the Capanne di Marcarolo Nature Park.
