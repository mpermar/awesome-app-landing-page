Description

Albergo Miniere offers accommodation in Traversella, a 12-minute drive from Brosso and 25 km from Ivrea.

It features a garden, a sun terrace, and free WiFi throughout. Rooms all include a balcony with mountain views, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and complimentary toiletries.

Albergo Miniere serves a sweet and savoury breakfast. Hiking and mountain biking trails start right on the doorstep.

Activities in the surrounding area also include climbing, kayak excursions, horse riding and cross-country skiing.
