Description

Located 1 km from the centre of Stresa, B&B Villa Lucciola offers a garden with BBQ facilities and free Wi-Fi throughout.

Apartments feature a lake-view balcony. Breakfast ingredients such as milk, yoghurt, coffee and bread are provided in each apartment.

Restaurants and cafés can be found within 400 metres of the apartments. Apartments at the Villa Lucciola all come with a kitchenette and flat-screen TV.

The private bathroom includes a hairdryer. The property is 800 metres from Lake Maggiore and the cable car which links to the Mottarone ski slopes.
