Description

Set on the shores of Lake Maggiore, Villa e Palazzo Aminta offers a luxurious wellness centre, a private beach, and an outdoor heated swimming pool overlooking the Borromean Islands.

Parking is free, and there is also a private pier. Accommodation includes free Wi-Fi, a marble bathroom, original frescoes, and Murano glass lamps.

Most rooms have a balcony with views of the lake or the park. Junior suites and suites are also available.

All rooms and suites features free minibar, goose-feather duvets and a pillow menu.

Part of the Leading Hotels of the World, the Aminta has a bar open all day. Drinks and snacks are served out on the sun terrace or by the pool.

The Le Isole restaurant serves gourmet cuisine, and Italian dishes can be enjoyed at the I Mori restaurant.

The Grotta di Palazzo Aminta wellness area features a Turkish bath, a hot tub, and a variety of massages and beauty treatments.

The property also offers a gym and free bikes. Villa e Palazzo Aminta Hotel is a 10-minute walk from Stresa.
