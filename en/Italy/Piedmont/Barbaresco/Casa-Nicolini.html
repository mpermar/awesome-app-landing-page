Description

Surrounded by the green Piedmont countryside, Casa Nicolini features a large garden with outdoor swimming pool and hot tub, a sauna and a gym.

Alba is a 10-minute drive away. Offering free Wi-Fi, rooms have an LCD TV with satellite channels, plus a minibar.

They are fitted with classic-style furniture and feature characteristic wall décor.

Some rooms overlook the garden or the Langhe hills. Breakfast at hotel Casa Nicolini is buffet style and includes fresh fruit, along with savoury and sweet products.

The on-site restaurant opens for lunch and dinner and serves Italian cuisine and Piedmont specialities.

Just 3 km from the small town of Barbaresco, this 3-star hotel is a 30-minute drive from Asti and around 1 hour by car from Turin.
