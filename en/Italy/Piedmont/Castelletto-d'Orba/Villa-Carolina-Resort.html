Description

Set in the Monferrato area, Villa Carolina Resort offers accommodation in Castelletto d'Orba.

The St. George restaurant, an American bar and a terrace are available on site, as well as a swimming pool.

Rooms have air conditioning, free WiFi and views of the surrounding golf club. Some also boast a terrace.

During summer months, guests have free access to the Lavagello water park, 2 km away.

The Serravalle Designer Outlet shopping centre is a 30-minute drive from Villa Carolina Resort.
