Description

Chiostro Hotel is a renovated monastery set in Intra, Verbania's old town, on the shores of Lake Maggiore.

The hotel has peaceful gardens and its own gym. Wi-Fi is free. The Chiostro has lounges with frescoes, antiques and a real fire.

There is also a bar and cellar and the hotel restaurant serves traditional Italian dishes.

Il Chiostro's rooms have satellite TV and air conditioning. Breakfast is buffet style.
