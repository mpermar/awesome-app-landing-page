Description

Offering an above ground pool and a garden with BBQ, the eco-friendly Wellness Suite Na' Canuna' has houses with air conditioning, free WiFi and a private sauna.

Located in Roddi, it includes free parking and a sun terrace. The accommodation will provide you with a smart LED TV, a washing machine and a DVD player.

There is a kitchen with a dishwasher, while the 2 bathrooms come with a hairdryer.

Wellness Suite Na' Canuna' provides bike rental and massages on request. The property is well placed for hiking excursions.

Alba Train Station is 5 km from the houses. The centre of Alba is a 10-minute drive away.
