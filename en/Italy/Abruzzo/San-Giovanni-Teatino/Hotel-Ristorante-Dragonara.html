Description

Just off the A14 motorway, Dragonara is 4 km from San Giovanni Teatino centre. It offers a large garden with a year-round swimming pool and a free daytime shuttle to Abruzzo Airport.

Rooms have light colour schemes and wood floors. All air-conditioned, they also come with free Wi-Fi, a minibar and pay-per-view TV channels.

Each bathroom features a bathrobe, hairdryer and toiletries. Breakfast is buffet style at Hotel Ristorante Dragonara.

The restaurant serves Italian cuisine and Abruzzo specialities. Free parking is provided at Hotel Dragonara, which is 10 minutes' drive from Chieti.
