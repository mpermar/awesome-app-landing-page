Description

Hotel Trieste is located in Roccaraso, 3 km from the Monte Pratello ski area and 5 km from Seggiovia Crete Rosse.

Guests can enjoy the on-site bar, and ski storage is free. Rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV.

Rooms are equipped with a private bathroom fitted with a bath or shower and bidet.

Extras include free toiletries and a hair dryer. The property offers a free scheduled shuttle to/from the Pizzalto and Aremogna ski areas, 8 km away.
