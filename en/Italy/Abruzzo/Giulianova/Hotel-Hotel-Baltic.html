Description

With an outdoor pool, restaurant, and children’s playground, Hotel Baltic offers accommodations in Giulianova. Bikes are free to rent on site and a sandy beach is 350 feet away.

Featuring a balcony, the rooms include air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and private bathroom with free toiletries. WiFi is free in all areas.

A sweet and savory breakfast is served each morning. Guests can also enjoy a sun terrace, games room, and massage services.

Gran Sasso e Monti della Laga National Park is a 55-minute drive away, while Pescara about 43 mi from the hotel.
