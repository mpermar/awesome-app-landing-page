Description

Located next to Giulianova‘s Golden Beach, this residence provides the comfort of a modern structure.

It features an outdoor swimming pool, a private beach area, and free Wi-Fi. Kiara Residence offers air-conditioned apartments with a balcony.

They include a bathroom with shower, a fully equipped kitchen, and satellite TV. Guests are provided with an entrance card to access the building in a secure way.

The Kiara’s swimming pool is located on a sun terrace with sun loungers and parasol.

This residence also features a large playground for kids. At the beach area, guests have an umbrella with a chair and sun lounger available for free.

The property comes with a stylish restaurant offering typical food from Abruzzo, and a nice selection of pizzas.

It includes a bar, and a coffee and an ice cream shop. Kiara Residence is only a 5-minute drive from Giulianova Train Station.
