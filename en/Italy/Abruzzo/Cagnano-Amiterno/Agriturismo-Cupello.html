Description

Agriturismo Cupello is a working farm in Cagnano Amiterno, 20 km from L'Aquila and 30-minute drive from Lake Campotosto and the Gran Sasso and Monti della Laga national park.

The classic rooms come with free Wi-Fi, mountain views, and a TV. The private bathroom includes a hairdryer.

A sweet Italian breakfast is served at the Cupello, which also offers a traditional restaurant.

The farm’s products, such as meat and cheese, can be enjoyed on site. Guests of Cupello Agriturismo have access to a garden, a tennis court, and free bicycles.

Horse riding is available on request. Cycling trails start right on the doorstep.
