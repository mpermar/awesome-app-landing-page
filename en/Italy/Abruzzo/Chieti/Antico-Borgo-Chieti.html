Description

Located in a historic building with vaulted ceilings, the Antico Borgo is in the centre of Chieti.

It offers free Wi-Fi and air-conditioned rooms mixing modern and traditional styling.

Rooms at the Antico Borgo Chieti come with a private bathroom, LCD TV and minibar.

Each has parquet flooring, and some feature exposed-beam ceilings. A daily buffet breakfast is provided, including cappuccino and herbal tea, together with local cheese and freshly made pastries.

Chieti Train Station is 4 km from the property, and Abruzzo International Airport is a 20-minute drive away.
