Description

Surrounded by the vineyards of Abruzzo, 3 km from Canosa Sannita, Magnificat Hotel&Resort offers views of the sea and Majella mountains.

It offers a restaurant, garden and bar. WiFi is free. Spacious rooms are air conditioned and feature a satellite flat-screen TV, parquet floors and a minibar.

Some rooms include a terrace. The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries.

A sweet, Italian-style breakfast is served daily and includes pastries, croissants as well as hot and cold drinks.

The restaurant offers fresh and local dishes as well as Abruzzo wines. A shuttle can be booked to the sandy beaches of Francavilla al Mare, a 15-minute drive from the hotel.

A shuttle to the Majella Mountains is also available. Ortona is 15 km away, while Pescara can be reached by car in 30 minutes.
