Description

Set on a hill in Silvi, Casa Ferretti di Ferretti Village overlooks the Adriatic Sea.

Just 1 km from the nearest sandy beaches, it offers a free outdoor swimming pool and air-conditioned rooms.

Rooms at this family-run hotel come with satellite TV, a minibar and a private bathroom with hairdryer.

Guests have discounts at the à la carte restaurant that serves local specialities from the Abruzzo region and a selection of fine wines.

In summer, you can dine outdoors at the pool-side pizzeria. A special Christmas lunch and New Year's Eve dinner are also served here.

Casa Ferretti di Ferretti Village provides free parking and is a short drive from the beach in Silvi Marina.
