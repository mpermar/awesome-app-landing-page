Description

All rooms in the bed and breakfast are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Each room comes with a private bathroom and a bidet, and certain rooms come with a balcony. Guest rooms at B&B Rosa Dalfonsina feature air conditioning and a desk.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy an Italian breakfast.

B&B Rosa Dalfonsina has a playground. A range of activities are offered in the area, such as windsurfing and diving.

The bed and breakfast provides a dry cleaning service, as well as business facilities like fax and photocopying.

Pescara is 27 miles from B&B Rosa Dalfonsina. The nearest airport is Abruzzo Airport, 27 miles from the property.
