Description

Offering an outdoor pool and a private beach area, Salinello Camping Village is located in Tortoreto.

With a hot tub and tennis court, this large property also features a traditional restaurant and mini golf.

The classic-style bungalows include a furnished patio, a kitchenette and satellite flat-screen TV.

Each has a bathroom with a hairdryer. Salinello Camping Village is complete with a mini-market, a 5-a-side football pitch, and a bar.

Guests can also enjoy activities organised by the on-site entertainment staff, and a kids' club.

Tortoreto Lido Train Station is 3 km from this holiday park. Abruzzo Airport is a 50-minute drive away.
