Description

On the hills outside Tortoreto, Incantea offers self-catering accommodation, and a panoramic sun terrace with hot tub and swimming pool.

There is a free shuttle to the seaside, 3 km away. At Resort Incantea studios and apartments feature a kitchen or kitchenette and windows overlooking the Adriatic Coast or the hills.

The rate includes free parking and free internet. Incantea Resort is a short walk from the bus stop to Ascoli Piceno.
