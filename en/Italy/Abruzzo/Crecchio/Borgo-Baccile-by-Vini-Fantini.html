Description

All rooms at the hotel come with a seating area and a flat-screen TV. The rooms come with a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hair dryer. Guest rooms at Borgo Baccile by Vini Fantini feature air conditioning and a closet.

The daily breakfast offers continental, buffet or à la carte options.

Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.

Pescara is 13 miles from Borgo Baccile by Vini Fantini, while Chieti is 9 miles away. The nearest airport is Abruzzo Airport, 12 miles from the property.
