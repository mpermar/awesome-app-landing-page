Description

Set in a tranquil location in the Gran Sasso National Park, Hotel Nido dell'Aquila offers mountain-style rooms with free Wi-Fi.

It features an outdoor swimming pool and restaurant. Uniquely furnished rooms at the Aquila have either mountain or swimming pool views, and feature a flat-screen TV.

The private bathroom includes free toiletries and a hairdryer. Homemade cakes and croissants are available at the buffet breakfast, and savoury items are also available.

Guests can enjoy a typical Abruzzo dinner at the Nido’s stylish à la carte restaurant.

The cable car to Campo Imperatore ski area is 350 metres away. The property is a 20-minute drive from L’Aquila, while the mountain-top fortress of Rocca di Calascio is 40 km away.
