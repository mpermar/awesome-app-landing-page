Description

At the hotel, each room comes with a closet. Every room includes a private bathroom equipped with a shower and free toiletries, while selected rooms here will provide you with a kitchen. At Hotel San Berardo, all rooms include air conditioning and a flat-screen TV.

A continental breakfast is available every morning at the property.

The accommodation has a terrace. Guests at Hotel San Berardo will be able to enjoy activities in and around Pescina, like skiing.

Roccaraso is 25 miles from the hotel, while Pescasseroli is 16 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Abruzzo Airport, 39 miles from Hotel San Berardo.
