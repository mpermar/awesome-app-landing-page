Description

Immersed in the Abruzzo National Park, Red Beech is 1 km from Pescasseroli city center.

Its rooms offer panoramic mountain views. Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas.

Overlooking the Monti Marsicani, rooms provide a TV and a private bathroom with a shower and toiletries.

A hairdryer is available on request. The restaurant serves homemade cuisine from the Abruzzo region, and also prepares gluten-free meals.

A varied buffet breakfast is served every morning. Faggio Rosso hotel is a 1-hour drive from Avezzano.
