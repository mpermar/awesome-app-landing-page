Description

Set in the National Park of Abruzzo, Albergo Il Picchio offers a free shuttle to/from the Pescasseroli ski resort, 1 km away.

The hotel also features a furnished garden and a traditional restaurant. With mountain views and cotto floors, rooms at Il Picchio come with TV, mini fridge and private bathroom with a hairdryer and a bath or a shower.

Some rooms also have a balcony and a terrace. Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas.

You can enjoy a continental buffet breakfast every morning, including pastries, cold cuts and eggs, together with hot drinks and juices.

The restaurant serves regional specialities. In July and August, free guided tours are organised.

Upon availability, the property also provides an entertainment programme, disco and miniclub.

On-site parking is free. In summer and on request, a partner outdoor pool is available 1 km away.
