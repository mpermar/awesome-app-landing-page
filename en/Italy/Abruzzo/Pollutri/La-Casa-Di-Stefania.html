Description

The apartment comes with 3 bedrooms, a flat-screen TV and a fully equipped kitchen that provides guests with a dishwasher, an oven, and a washing machine. There's a bathroom with a bidet and shower, and free toiletries and a hair dryer are provided.

A continental breakfast is served at the property.

The apartment has a grill. Guests can also relax in the shared lounge area.

Pescara is 30 miles from La Casa Di Stefania, while Chieti is 26 miles away. The nearest airport is Abruzzo Airport, 29 miles from the accommodation.
