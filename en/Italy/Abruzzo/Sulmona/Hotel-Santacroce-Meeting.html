Description

Hotel Santacroce Meeting is a 5-minute drive from the center of Sulmona, just outside Maiella National Park.

Its garden features a swimming pool with hot tub and a sunbathing area. All rooms are air conditioned and come with satellite TV, a minibar and free Wi-Fi.

Breakfast is a varied buffet. The restaurant serves local dishes and, during summer, evening entertainment and barbecues take place around the pool.

This 4-star hotel boasts a panoramic view of the mountains, and includes a gym and children's playground.

A free shuttle service is available to Sulmona Station and centre on prior request.

Located on the SS17 state road, the Santacroce Hotel is ideal for exploring the national parks of Abruzzo.
