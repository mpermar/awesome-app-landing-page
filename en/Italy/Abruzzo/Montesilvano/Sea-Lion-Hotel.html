Description

The Sea Lion offers elegant, modern accommodation on Montesilvano’s picturesque seafront, just 5 km from Pescara.

The hotel features a private beach with swimming pool, and free access to the wellness centre.

Wi-Fi is free. Simple and elegant, the rooms offer air conditioning, a TV with Sky channels, and a private balcony.

Sea Lion Hotel's wellness centre includes a sauna, hot tub and a Turkish bath. A fitness area with a treadmill and other equipment is also available for free.

Continental buffet breakfast includes sweet and savoury food and homemade pastries, and is served in the hotel's restaurant with sea views.

The cuisine includes Abruzzo specialities, and the bar serves international cocktails.
