Description

Set on its own private beach in Montesilvano, Club Esse Mediterraneo offers a large swimming pool and plenty of sun loungers and parasols.

Most of its rooms feature a balcony with Adriatic Sea views. Breakfast consists of sweet and savoury products, together with hot drinks.

The buffet-style restaurant serves regional and Mediterranean cuisine. Rooms at the Esse Mediterraneo are air conditioned, spacious and have a contemporary design.

They all feature a TV, fridge and private bathroom. At the Esse Club you will also find a tennis court, organised entertainment and beach volleyball.
