Description

Surrounded by the Tuscan countryside, Il Paradiso is a rustic farmhouse, located 1 km from Asciano and a 10-minute drive from the spas of Rapolano.

It features a large garden with swimming pool, air-conditioned accommodation, and a traditional restaurant.

Fitted with antique wooden furniture and rustic details, the studios and apartments come with a kitchenette and LCD TV.

Each feature a furnished private or shared terrace. All guest rooms are en suite, and some face the garden and pool.

Breakfast buffet at Agriturismo Il Paradiso includes sweet and savoury items, and it can be served in the garden during summer.

The restaurant has traditional Tuscan dishes and uses olive oil and Chianti wine produced on site.

Guests here can enjoy a barbecue in the open air and can also use the free WiFi access available in all areas.

The nearest train station is in Asciano, 2 km away, and the property provides a free pick-up service.
