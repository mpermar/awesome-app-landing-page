Description

Set 1 km from the centre of Montepulciano, Agriturismo Nobile offers an outdoor pool, garden with BBQ and a restaurant.

This working farm also features classic-style rooms. Olive oil, jams and wine are produced on site.

Homemade cakes, biscuits, cheese and eggs are served every morning for breakfast.

Tuscan specialities can be tasted at the restaurant. Rooms at the Nobile have a satellite flat-screen TV, view of the garden and a cooling fan.

Each includes a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer. A free gym is also available.

A free shuttle to the centre of Montepulciano is available. Chiusi Train Station is 20 km from this family-run property.
