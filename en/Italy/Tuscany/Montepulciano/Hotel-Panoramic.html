Description

Overlooking the Tuscan countryside from an altitude of 600 metres, Hotel Panoramic has a large garden and an outdoor pool with hydromassage area.

A charger for electrical cars is also available on site. All the spacious, air-conditioned rooms have parquet floors and free Wi-Fi.

Each includes a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped private bathroom. Some rooms feature a balcony.

Breakfast at the Panoramic is a varied buffet with freshly baked cakes, cheese and cold cuts.

A choice of restaurants, cafés and wine bars are available in nearby Sant'Albino and Montepulciano.

The hotel is a 5-minute drive from Montepulciano. Well placed to visit the area's thermal baths, the Panoramic is 7 km from Chianciano Terme and a 30-minute drive from Bagni di San Filippo.

Parking is free. This eco-friendly hotel runs entirely on renewable energy sources.
