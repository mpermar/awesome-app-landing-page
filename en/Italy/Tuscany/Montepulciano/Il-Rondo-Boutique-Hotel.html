Description

Set just 10 minutes’ walk from Montepulciano’s historic centre, Il Rondò offers personalised service, a bar, and free garage parking.

Rooms offer free WiFi and some overlook the lovely gardens. Beautifully decorated and containing antique furniture, interiors at Il Rondò maintain an 18th-century Tuscan charm.

Each room offers air conditioning and satellite TV. A buffet breakfast with local products is included daily and can be served in the garden.

Guests staying 4 nights or longer receive a complimentary bottle of red Montepulciano wine.
