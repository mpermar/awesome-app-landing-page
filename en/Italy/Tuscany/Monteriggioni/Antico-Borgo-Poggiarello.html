Description

Enjoy comfortable accommodation in the heart of Tuscany's green countryside. Antico Borgo Poggiarello is set in a former 17th century village.

The establishment also comprises an ancient tower of the 9th century. Sit at its base and enjoy a breathtaking landscape.

Each of the Poggiarello's 14 apartments is charmingly furnished in original Tuscan style, and provided with modern commodities, including satellite TV and internet connection.

The Antico Borgo also offers a swimming pool, surrounded by green olive trees and close to the woods. The establishment's most relaxing facility is probably the ancient-Roman thermal bath, a cave containing water with temperatures of 95-100°F.

The Palio Di Siena restaurant offers the best foods of the traditional Tuscan cuisine and, of course, the best wines of the Chianti Siena area.
