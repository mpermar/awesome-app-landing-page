Description

Immersed in the Tuscan countryside between Siena and Florence, this 4-star hotel offers high-quality service on 20 acres of rich parkland, just outside of Monteriggioni.

You can enjoy a peaceful and relaxing setting at Borgo San Luigi. This 17th-century palace is just 2 km from the motorways and set on an unpaved road.

Rest in the shade of the thick gardens, enjoy a swim in the Olympic-sized pool, or savour breakfast outdoors poolside during the summer.

You can participate in a variety of sports on this rich, resort-like, country estate.

There is a tennis court and a football field. Go quad biking or horseback riding and explore the surrounding rural area.

Borgo San Luigi has friendly, professional staff who can organise tours, excursions or team-building events, an ideal way to relax between meetings.

Enjoy high-quality rooms with period furniture and Tuscan-style furnishings. Featuring vineyard or mountain views, rooms include rustic fire places, historic frescoes and Tuscan four-poster beds.

Nearly all rooms offer a garden or a balcony. Savour typical Tuscan cooking at La Limonaia, the chic on-site restaurant at Borgo San Luigi.

La Limonaia is suitable for a variety of events, including large receptions, ceremonies, banquets and business dinners.

Borgo San Luigi is the perfect location to enjoy a unique holiday, where you can participate in a cooking school or English-language school.
