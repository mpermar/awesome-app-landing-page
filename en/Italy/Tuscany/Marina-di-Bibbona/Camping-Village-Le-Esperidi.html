Description

Offering a restaurant, on-site shops and a wellness centre, Camping Village Le Esperidi is set in a pinewood with direct access to Marina di Bibbona sandy beaches.

Free WiFi access is available. The poverty is surrounded by pine woods and offers a direct beach access.

Facilities include sports activities and evening entertainment. There is a supermarket and a hairdresser.

Each air-conditioned mobile homes come with a furnished patio, a fully equipped kitchenette and a bathroom with a shower.

For lunch and dinner, a Pizza restaurant and a restaurant serving traditional Tuscan cuisine are available.

On-site parking is free. San Vincenzo is a 20-minute drive from Le Esperidi, while Rosignano Solvay is 30 km away.
