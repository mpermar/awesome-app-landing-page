Description

Featuring a year-round outdoor pool and an on-site restaurant, iConic Resort and Spa offers accommodation with free WiFi in Arezzo, a 15-minute drive from Piazza Grande.

Free private parking is available on site. The rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV and air conditioning.

You will find a coffee machine and a kettle in the room. The private bathrooms come with a shower and a bathrobe.

For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer. Guests can enjoy the on-site spa and a fitness centre.

iConic Resort and Spa is 3 km from Arezzo Train Station. Cortona is a 30-minute drive from the property, while Montepulciano is 50 km away.
