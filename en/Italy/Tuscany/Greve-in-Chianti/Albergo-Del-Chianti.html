Description

All rooms are air conditioned, and come with free Wi-Fi and a plasma-screen TV. Each room has its own private bathroom with a shower and hairdryer.

Breakfast is buffet style and includes scrambled eggs, cold meats, cheese and cereal. All cake are homemade.

Albergo Del Chianti's central location means you can easily reach Greve's most popular restaurants and shopping. The town is ideally placed halfway between Florence and Siena.

The Bussotti family is available 24 hours a day. They can provide tips on local activities along with useful tourist and travel info.
