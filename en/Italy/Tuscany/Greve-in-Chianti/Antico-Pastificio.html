Description

Located in the village of Greve in Chianti, Antico Pastificio is just behind the main square with its restaurants and shops.

It offers rooms and apartments and a large garden with swimming pool. Antico Pastificio has its own vineyard where you will find the BBQ.

It also features a children's playground, five-a-side football pitch and a sun terrace overlooking the Tuscan hills.
