Description

The rooms in the resort are equipped with a coffee machine. The rooms are equipped with a private bathroom with a bidet, free toiletries and a hair dryer. All rooms at La Veronica Resort have air conditioning and a closet.

An Italian breakfast is served each morning at the property.

Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Greve in Chianti, like hiking.

Piazza Santa Croce is 2.7 miles from La Veronica Resort. The nearest airport is Peretola Airport, 14 miles from the resort.
