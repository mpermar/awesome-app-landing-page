Description

Agriturismo Antico Borgo Poggitazzi is surrounded by the Tuscan countryside in Terranuova Bracciolini, a 40-minute drive from Arezzo.

It offers a panoramic outdoor pool in a rural location, surrounded by olive trees.

Rooms and apartments at Borgo Poggitazzi are all air conditioned and come with satellite TV and private bathroom.

Other services here include bike rental and free parking. Only 150 metres from the Agriturismo, a Medieval villa hosts events and celebrations.
