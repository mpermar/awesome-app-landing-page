Description

Located in Rio Marina on the island of Elba, TH Ortano Mare Village has a private beach and outdoor pool.

It offers free lobby Wi-Fi and classically furnished accommodation with a private bathroom.

Rooms at the TH Ortano Mare come with marble floors and a TV. The sweet and savoury breakfast is served buffet style.

There is also a bar and restaurant. Guests enjoy free soft drinks and snacks at fixed times during the day.

TH Ortano Mare Village is set on the beach, 300 metres away. Here you will find the restaurant, the Mini Club, and the entertainment team.

Portoferraio is 18 km from the property. Elba Airport is a 40-minute taxi ride away.
