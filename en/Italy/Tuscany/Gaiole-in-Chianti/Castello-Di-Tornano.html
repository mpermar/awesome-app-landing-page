Description

Castello Di Tornano is located on a hillside in the Chianti Classico wine region.

It offers free WiFi, free parking, and rooms and apartments with satellite TV and bathroom.

Rooms are air conditioned and include a minibar. The apartments offer a fully fitted kitchen.

The restaurant at the Castello Di Tornano serves Tuscan specialities and classic Italian cooking.

The swimming pool is set in gardens and surrounded by trees. There is a tennis court, and guests can also enjoy massage treatments under previous request.
