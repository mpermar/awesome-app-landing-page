Description

Apartments are bright and spacious with comfortable living and dining areas. All air conditioned, they also feature satellite TV, a bathroom with hairdryer and a kitchen or kitchenette with coffee maker.

Casa in Maremma has something for everyone, with 2 floodlit tennis courts, a five-a-side soccer field and 2 basketball courts. There is also a playground and a kids' pool.

A buffet breakfast is available and the restaurant serves a combination of Tuscan recipes and international cuisine.

Free parking is provided and the property is a 5 minute drive from Scarlino for trains to Grosseto, Pisa and Florence.
