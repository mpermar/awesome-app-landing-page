Description

Nestled on a hill overlooking the Mediterranean Sea, Relais Vedetta Boutique Hotel provides luxurious accommodation with a mix of antique and design elements.

It is a 5-minute drive from the sea. Rooms have iPod docking stations, LCD Sky TV and pillow menus.

You will be surrounded by beautiful countryside of Tuscany's Maremma area, including woods and olive groves.

Relais Vedetta Boutique Hotel features a panoramic swimming pool with sea views. Rooms at the Relais are quiet, well-equipped and beautifully designed.

They have free Wi-Fi and stone bathrooms and offer great views. Breakfast consists of homemade produce and is served on the porch until late morning.

Biscuits and fruit are always available in the lounge. The property is a 10-minute drive from Follonica.

You can park your car on site. Castiglione della Pescaia is 30 minutes' drive away.
