Description

Located just 100 metres from Pisa Centrale Train Station, B&B Tuscany Gallery offers air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi.

Rooms at Tuscany Gallery B&B have a modern design with bright colour schemes. They feature a flat-screen TV, a selection of local wines and a kettle.

A continental breakfast is served each morning at Tuscany Gallery. There is also a communal fridge and kettle for guests' use.

The owner can offer useful tourist information and organise sightseeing tours around Pisa.

The property also has a book-exchange library. Pisa Airport is just 5 minutes' drive away, while the centre and the Leaning tower of Pisa are a 20-minute walk or short bus ride away.
