Description

Surrounded by the Tuscan countryside, 8 km from Lucca, Relais Del Lago is a 17th-century stone country house.

Its large garden features a pool with a whirlpool. Each room at Del Lago Relais features antique furniture, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a minibar with free bottles of water.

Bathrooms come with a hairdryer and a courtesy set. Restaurant Serendepico is an à-la-carte restaurant, open for dinner, and serving Italian cuisine along with national and local wines.

The whole property also has a lake for fishing and a small wood. Guests can rent bikes and visit the surrounding areas.
