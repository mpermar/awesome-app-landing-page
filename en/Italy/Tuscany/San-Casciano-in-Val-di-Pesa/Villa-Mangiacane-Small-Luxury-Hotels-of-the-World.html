Description

With a panoramic location in the Chianti wine area, Villa Mangiacane dates back to the 16th century.

It offers views over the Tuscan countryside, vineyards and Florence. Set in 2 villas, rooms at Mangiacane Villa are spacious and decorated with high-quality Tuscan furniture.

They offer air conditioning, free internet access and views of the surrounding vineyards and olive groves.

Homemade wines and olive oil are available to taste on site. The restaurant is open at breakfast, lunch and dinner and serves Italian cuisine with an emphasis on Tuscan specialities.

Cookery classes are also available. Located in San Casciano in Val di Pesa, the villa is 20 km from the historic centre of Florence.
