Description

The resort's main building is a beautiful historic villa surrounded by a green park and the Tuscan countryside. A tour desk includes information about the area.

Norcenni Girasole has different types of well-equipped accommodations to suit your needs. It includes functional rooms, apartments, bungalows and mobile homes.

Florence is a 30-minute drive away, and Siena is 34 miles from the property.
