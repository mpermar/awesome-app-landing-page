Description

Totally refurbished, the villa is provided with a characteristic Italian garden and 2 swimming pools, one of them set by ancient walls. You can also enjoy a modern spa, available upon request and at additional cost.

Full of artistic heritage, Villa Casagrande offers 60 rooms equipped with all modern comforts, yet preserving the building's 14th century charm.

Hotel Villa Casagrande also offers 2 restaurants where you can enjoy traditional Tuscan cuisine. Wine and wine tasting tours are available on site, while oil is produced on site.
