Description

Bacco is a 3-star superior hotel in Lido di Camaiore, just a few steps away from the beach.

It features a restaurant and a swimming pool, and its rooms come with a balcony. Wi-Fi is free.

All rooms at Bacco are equipped with air conditioning, minibar, and satellite TV.

Their en suite bathrooms come with a hairdryer. Other amenities include a safe and a direct dial phone.

Bacco's beautiful garden is ideal to relax. The restaurant with terrace serves delicious Tuscan cuisine and selected wines.

The solarium located on the 5th floor offers a perfect spot to enjoy the view. The property also offers a fitness centre with aquagym lessons, whilst massages can be arranged upon request.
