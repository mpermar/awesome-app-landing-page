Description

Offering free parking, La Palma is a 15-minute stroll from the seaside in Lido Di Camaiore, on the Tuscan coast of Versilia.

The hotel has an outdoor pool with a bar, and rooms come with a balcony. Rooms at Hotel La Palma are simply decorated, and feature tiled floors and a flat-screen TV.

Breakfast is a varied buffet and Wi-Fi is free. Guests can enjoy discounts at a restaurant next door.

There is a public beach nearby, or you can use one of the partner private beaches.

Viareggio Train Station is 3 km from La Palma Hotel. The A12 Autostrada Azzurra is a 5-minute drive away.
