Description

Just 100 metres from the beaches, Hotel Eur is in central Lido di Camaiore, a 5-minute drive from Viareggio.

It offers free parking, a large outdoor pool and complete wellness facilities. Rooms are air conditioned, soundproofed and decorated with pastel colours.

Each one comes with satellite TV and a balcony, some overlooking the sea, others the mountains.

The Eur’s 120 m² wellness area includes an equipped fitness centre, along with a hot tub.

Guests can enjoy a drink on the furnished top-floor terrace or in the large garden with gazebos.

At the restaurant, you can treat yourself to fish specialities and international cuisine, all accompanied by famous Tuscan wines.

Eur Hotel is 50 metres from a bus stop providing links with Viareggio Train Station, Forte Dei Marmi and Camaiore.
