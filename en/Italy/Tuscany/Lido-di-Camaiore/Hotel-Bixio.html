Description

Hotel Bixio is set on Lido di Camaiore's seafront and has its own gardens with free parking and swimming pool.

Rooms are air-conditioned and offer free Wi-Fi and satellite TV. The Bixio provides a free gym with sauna.

The property can organise mountain bike tours through a local association, and also has a bike workshop.

Bixio Hotel's rooftop terrace and bar enjoys great sea views. This family-run hotel also has a restaurant and pizzeria.
