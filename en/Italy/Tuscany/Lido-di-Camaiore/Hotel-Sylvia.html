Description

Just a 3-minute walk from the beach, Hotel Sylvia offers panoramic sea and mountain views from its rooftop terrace.

It provides air-conditioned rooms and free parking in Lido di Camaiore centre. Sylvia’s rooms are all simply furnished and come with a TV and a window or balcony.

Their private bathrooms are complete with a hairdryer and toiletries. The hotel has a well-stocked cellar of top-quality regional wines.

The restaurant is open at lunch and dinner and you can enjoy meals out on the veranda, which overlooks the garden.

Guests can relax in the garden with playground and they also have discounts at Bagno Venezia beach.

The family-run Sylvia Hotel is a 10-minute drive from the A12 motorway and from Viareggio.
