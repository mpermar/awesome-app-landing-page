Description

Just 200 metres from the beach, Hotel Club I Pini is an early 20th-century building with large green gardens.

It offers free Wi-Fi in the lobby, and classic rooms. The centre of Lido di Camaiore is a 10-minute walk away.

Each room offers air conditioning, and a private bathroom with shower. Many have a terrace overlooking the gardens.

The property's elegant and spacious interiors feature frescoes, sculptures and original paintings.

Drinks and snacks are available in the lounge or bar. Residenza D'Epoca Hotel has its own small restaurant, specialising in traditional Tuscan cuisine and delicious local seafood.

More than 150 different Tuscan wines are available. The area offers private and public beaches, shops, public transport and entertainment.
