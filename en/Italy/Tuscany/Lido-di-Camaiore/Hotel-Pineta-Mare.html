Description

The 3-star Hotel Pineta Mare is in Lido di Camaiore's centre, 70 metres from its own private beach and 3 km from Viareggio.

Parking, bike rental and Wi-Fi access are all free of charge. Each room offers a bright atmosphere and is air conditioned.

Rates include free use of a parasol, a chair and a deckchair at the beach, where free Wi-Fi is also available.

The Pineta Mare Hotel has 2 restaurants, one on site serving set menus or buffets, and another one on the beach, with à la carte menus.

They both specialise in local recipes and fresh seafood. The hotel is 8 km from the famous seaside resort of Forte dei Marmi.
