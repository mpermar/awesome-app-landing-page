Description

Enjoy a pleasant stay on the Tuscan Tyrrhenic coastline, which includes the municipalities of Viareggio, Camaiore, Pietrasanta and Forte dei Marmi.

In 2000 the Senatore – Carleo family restored this beautiful Liberty villa, in order to provide modern services and excellent accommodations.

Hotel Europa is surrounded by the magnificent Alpi Apuane, a mountain range that offers countless beauties.

Hotel Europa offers 30 rooms with refined furniture and fittings. Rooms are equipped with all services and comforts of a 4-star hotel.

Enjoy a homey reception and a modern dining room, have a drink at the bar, or relax in the TV room. Wi-Fi internet connection is available for free in these areas.

Extra relaxation is available at the hotel's swimming pool with Jacuzzi and at the sauna.

The indoor parking area features video surveillance.
