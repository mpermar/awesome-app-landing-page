Description

Just 150 metres from its own private beach, Hotel Mariani is in Lido Di Camaiore’s centre.

It offers free Wi-Fi, free bike rental and a free wellness corner with a fitness centre and hot tub.

The air-conditioned rooms at Mariani Hotel all have an LCD TV with satellite channels, plus a private bathroom.

They come with either parquet or carpeted floors. The property features a small outdoor pool for children.

At the on-site restaurant you can enjoy traditional Tuscan cuisine and Italian classics.

Buses stop 20 metres from the hotel and link with Lido Di Camaiore Train Station.
