Description

With its countryside location close to the border between Tuscany and Liguria, La Castellana offers an outdoor pool, free Wi-Fi and modern-style rooms with air conditioning.

A sweet buffet breakfast is served every morning. Rooms at the hotel La Catsellana have a satellite TV, parquet floors and a minibar.

Each features a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer. Some overlook the sea, while others include a balcony.

You can relax at the on-site bar or enjoy traditional regional specialities at the à la carte restaurant, which is open for lunch and for dinner.

This property is 3 km from Fosdinovo and a 10-minute drive from the Alpi Apuane Regional Park.
