Description

Set in the Tuscan countryside, a 5-minute drive from the Mediteranean Sea, Antico Podere San Francesco offers air-conditioned apartments with free reserved parking spaces.

In the garden at San Francesco you will find a large swimming pool, a children's playground, and 2 barbecues to use at your leisure.

The apartments all feature original features such as beamed ceilings as well as wrought-iron beds.

Guests can also enjoy a 24h-honesty bar at the property. The Antico Podere is just 2 km from Vada Station, where you can catch the train to Florence, Pisa, and Livorno.
