Description

Set in Montecatini Terme, Grand Hotel & La Pace Spa is an example of fine 19th-century Tuscan architecture.

Surrounded by greenery, it offers indoor and outdoor pools and a modern spa. Wi-Fi is free throughout.

This luxurious 5-star hotel has featured in numerous films and inspired many artists.

Its Art Deco style is evident in each of its comfortable rooms, overlooking the garden, internal courtyard or town.

Rooms have a classic and elegant design. All air-conditioned, they also feature a minibar and TV with satellite and pay-per-view channels.

Each one has a private bathroom with a hairdryer and toiletries. Grand Hotel & La Pace Spa serves a buffet breakfast each morning, while during summer lunch is served poolside.

Michelangelo restaurant offers Mediterranean cuisine which can be enjoyed on the patio in summer.

The spa features a heated swimming pool, steam bath, hydrotherapy showers and massage chairs.
