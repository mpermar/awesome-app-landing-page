Description

The Croce di Malta is located on Viale IV Novembre, an elegant avenue bordering the thermal baths in Montecatini Terme.

Facilities include an outdoor pool and a spa with a heated indoor pool with chromotherapy.

Restaurant Le Anfore is open every day. Grand Hotel Croce di Malta features design rooms and suites with free WiFi and LCD satellite TV, Some rooms have a balcony with views of the park, swimming pool, or the main street.

Available at a surcharge, the Thai Silk spa of this 4-star superior hotel is complete with sauna, Turkish bath, sensory showers, single and twin massage rooms and a solarium.

Beauty treatments can be booked. The restaurant offers local cuisine and international dishes for lunch and dinner.
