Description

Located in the centre of Montecatini Terme, 500 metres from Montecatini Centrale Railway Station, Hotel Reale offers an outdoor swimming pool with hot tub and free bike rental.

Rooms are air conditioned and feature an LCD TV with satellite channels. The private bathroom has free toiletries and a hairdryer.

A laundry room is available for all guests. At the Reale Hotel, you can relax in the bar and in the garden.

The restaurant serves Tuscan specialities, with gluten-free and vegetarian options available on request.

The hotel is a 2-minute walk from Montecatini thermal spas and only 5 minutes' drive from the A11 motorway exit.
