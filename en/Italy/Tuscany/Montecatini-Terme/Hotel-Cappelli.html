Description

Just 450 meters from Terme di Montecatini spa center, Hotel Cappelli is in Montecatini Terme center, 800 meters from the train station.

It offers an outdoor pool and free Wi-Fi. All with elegant décor and light colours, rooms at Cappelli Hotel are air conditioned and feature a TV with cable and satellite channels, plus a private bathroom complete with a hairdryer and toiletries.

The hotel’s restaurant serves Italian cuisine, along with Tuscan specialities and wines.

Guests have discounts for various treatments at Terme di Montecatini. They can also relax in the property’s garden with sun loungers.

The Cappelli is a 5-minute drive from the Montecatini Terme exit of the A11 Motorway.
