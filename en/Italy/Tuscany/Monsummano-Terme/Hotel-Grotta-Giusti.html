Description

One of our top picks in Monsummano Terme.Grotta Giusti Resort Golf & Spa Tuscany is a 19th century villa, set in a tranquil private park 1.2 mi outside Monsummano Terme. All elegant rooms include free access to the wellness center.

Grotta Giusti Resort Golf & Spa features an outdoor thermal pool measuring 2461 ft², with a waterfall, hydromassage jets, and a natural cave with steam bath. There is also an outdoor Bioaquam thermal circuit.

All air-conditioned rooms are equipped with flat-screen TV and a mini-bar. Each room has a marble bathroom with soft bathrobes. Some bathrooms have thermal water and overlook the park.

The "La Veranda" restaurant serves classic Italian cuisine, regional specialties and a menu of light dishes. Breakfast is buffet style.

The A11 Motorway is 2.8 mi away, and it is a 1-hour drive to Florence. Parking is free. Guests at Grotta Giusti have a discount on the green fee at Montecatini Golf, 1.9 mi away.
