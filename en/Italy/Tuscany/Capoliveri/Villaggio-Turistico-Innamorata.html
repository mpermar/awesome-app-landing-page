Description

Set on the seafront at the Innamorata Bay in Elba, Villaggio Turistico Innamorata offers different kind of accommodations semi detached from each other, most of the apartments and rooms offer beautiful sea views.

Facilities include an outdoor pool, tennis court, 2 restaurants and 2 bars, all surrounded by a Mediterranean park.

The Villaggio's self-catering apartments are set in 3 buildings spaced around the park: Villa Adalgisa, Villa Moresca and Dependance Gisella.

All accommodation is air-conditioned and comes with satellite TV. The Lo Scial√† restaurant/pizzeria boasts a terrace right on the beach and is open for lunch and dinner.

During the summer, the Conte Domingo restaurant offers romantic al fresco evening meals.

Boats to mainland Italy leave from Portoferraio and Rio Marina, both 20 km away. Indoor parking is free.
