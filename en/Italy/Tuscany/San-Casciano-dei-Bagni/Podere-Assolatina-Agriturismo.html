Description

Set in the countryside, a 10-minute drive from San Casciano dei Bagni, Podere Assolatina Agriturismo offers free WiFi, an outdoor pool and spacious rooms with a flat-screen TV.

Organic jams, vegetables and oil are produced on site. Rooms overlook the Orcia Valley and feature tiled floors, a minibar and private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Some have a kitchenette and a seating area. Breakfast is available daily and includes sweet and savoury items.

Drinks are available at the bar and local cuisine is served at the on-site restaurant.

In summer guests can relax by the pool and in the shared garden, which includes BBQ facilities.

Chiusi Train Station is a 40-minute drive from the Podere Assolatina. Lake Bolsena is 40 km away.
