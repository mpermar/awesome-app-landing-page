Description

Surrounded by a private garden, in Pistoia’s countryside, Podere Delle Fanciulle has an outdoor swimming pool and a furnished sun terrace.

The self-catering apartments have modern furniture. Wi-Fi is free in the entire property.

The apartments are on the ground floor, and are fitted with terracotta floors, modern furnishings, and a fully equipped kitchen with microwave or electric oven.

Some are on 2 levels, and have 2 bathrooms. They boast an LCD TV, and a patio with garden views.

The first floor features a bedroom with a wrought-iron double bed and bathroom with shower.

Some have wooden-beamed ceilings. The Fanciulle provides you with several facilities, as a tennis court, a BBQ, and table tennis.

A wood-burning oven is also available. The bus stop to Pistoia Train Station is 600 metres from Podere delle Fanciulle.
