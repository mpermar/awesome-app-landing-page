Description

At the farm stay, rooms are equipped with a closet. Agriturismo La Valle dei Castagni has certain units with pool views, and the rooms come with a coffee machine. At the accommodation every room comes with a seating area.

You can play ping pong at Agriturismo La Valle dei Castagni.

Speaking English and French at the reception, staff are willing to help at any time of the day.

Florence is 29 miles from the farm stay, while Pisa is 24 miles from the property. Galileo Galilei Airport is 25 miles away.
