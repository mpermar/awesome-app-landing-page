Description

At the bottom of the valley, close to the Ombrone River that borders the property, there is a polo field; farther away olive trees, vineyards and grain fields. The woods with winding paths make it an ideal place to ride, walk or just relax.

Originally the building was a 13th century castle part of a medieval village and nowadays it is an exclusive 148-acre resort.

Enjoy the unique combination of a historic location combined with the art, culture and exceptional landscapes of Tuscany.
