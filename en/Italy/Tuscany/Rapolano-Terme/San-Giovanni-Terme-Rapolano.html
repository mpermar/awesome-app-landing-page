Description

San Giovanni Terme Rapolano has free bikes, outdoor swimming pool, a tennis court and fitness centre in Rapolano Terme.

With a garden, the property also features a terrace, as well as a bar. Local cuisine is served at the restaurant.

At the resort, the rooms come with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

All rooms have a wardrobe. A buffet breakfast is served every morning at the property.

Guests can make use of the indoor pool. Speaking German and English, staff are willing to help at any time of the day at the reception.

Siena is 21 km from San Giovanni Terme Rapolano, while Arezzo is 31 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Florence, 66 km from the accommodation, and the property offers a free shuttle service.
