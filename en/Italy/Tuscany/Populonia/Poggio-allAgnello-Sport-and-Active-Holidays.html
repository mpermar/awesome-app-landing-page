Description

With a total of 3 swimming pools, this charming Tuscan farmhouse has been completely refurbished to offer modern comfortable apartments.

It is set in Populonia, a 5-minute drive from the sea. Parking is free. Poggio All'agnello Sport & Active Holidays features both studios and one-bedroom apartments.

Each one is air conditioned and features a fully equipped kitchen with microwave and dishwasher.

There is a range of sports facilities, including tennis courts and five-a-side football pitches.

The property has 3 swimming pools, 1 for children, 1 Olympic-sized, and 1 with water jets.

During summer, children also have a mini club. Boats to Elba Island leave from Piombino harbour 15 minutes' drive away.
