Description

Located just 300 metres from the beach and surrounded by a vast park, California Park offers spacious rooms with elegant furnishings in Versilia, a 10-minute walk from Forte dei Marmi city centre.

Rooms at the California feature a balcony overlooking the gardens or the swimming pool area.

Each room is equipped with air conditioning, free WiFi and Sky TV. The restaurant serves traditional Tuscan cuisine.

Breakfast is a varied buffet. The hotel also boasts a pool and hot tub, as well as a fitness area with Technogym equipment.

Lucca is a 30-minute drive from the California Park, while Viareggio is 20 km away.
