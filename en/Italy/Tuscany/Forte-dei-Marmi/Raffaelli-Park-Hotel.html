Description

Just 200 metres from the beach in Forte dei Marmi, Raffaelli Park Hotel is surrounded by a garden with trees, and offers free WiFi, free parking, and air-conditioned rooms with LCD TV.

The hotel's Taverna Toscana restaurant serves fine Tuscan cuisine. Breakfast is a varied buffet, and can be also served in your room.

Rooms at the Raffaelli come with soft parquet floors, classic-style wooden furniture, and a tea/coffee maker.
