Description

Located 700 metres from the centre of Forte dei Marmi, Hotel Tarabella offers an outdoor pool, restaurant and accommodation with a balcony.

Wi-Fi is free throughout, and there is also a children's playground. Rooms at the Tarabella Hotel all come with satellite flat-screen TV, a safe and air conditioning.

The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries. Sweet and savoury items feature in the buffet-style breakfast served daily.

The restaurant, open for lunch and dinner, specialises in Mediterranean cuisine. Guests can enjoy discounted rates on beach facilities, golf and cycling activities nearby.

The property provides free private parking on site, and is a 5-minute walk from the beach.
