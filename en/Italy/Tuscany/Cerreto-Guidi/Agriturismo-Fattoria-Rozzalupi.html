Description

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool, this farming estate from the Renaissance period is a 30-minute drive from Montecatini Terme and 8 km from Vinci.

It offers free bike hire, plus accommodation with Tuscan rural-style décor and original brick floors.

Rooms at Fattoria Rozzalupi have wood-beamed ceilings, while apartments feature antique furniture.

They all offer countryside views, satellite TV and private bathroom. Wi-Fi is free at reception.

Traditional Tuscan cuisine is served on-site once a week. The property produces and sells wine, oil and honey.

The large garden hosts a fishing lake and a horse stable. Guests can relax on the outdoor terrace, with panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.

The Rozzalupi farmhouse is 11 km from Empoli and 40 km from Florence. A supermarket, shops and restaurants are less than 1 km away.
