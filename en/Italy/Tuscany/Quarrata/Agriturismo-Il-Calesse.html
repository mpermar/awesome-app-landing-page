Description

Located in the small countryside village of Montorio, Agriturismo Il Calesse is a farmhouse using photovoltaic energy and producing organic vegetables.

An à la carte restaurant, free Wi-Fi and rooms with antiques are offered here. Rooms are set in a Tuscan-style building with brick walls.

Each has terracotta floors and a modern bathroom with toiletries and shower. All rooms overlook the Il Calesse’s garden.

Tuscan and national dishes, and special-dietary options on request, can be sampled in the restaurant using home-grown produce.

This agriturismo has a peaceful location just 3 km from Quarrata, and lies 10 km from an exclusive golf club.
