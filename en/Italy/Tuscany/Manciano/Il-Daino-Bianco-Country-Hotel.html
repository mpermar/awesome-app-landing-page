Description

With its countryside location 4 km from Manciano, Il Daino Bianco features an outdoor pool and offers rustic-style rooms with a patio and views of the mountains.

This property is surrounded by a park inhabited with deer, goats and rams. Homemade cakes, jams and hot drinks are served every morning for breakfast.

Savoury items are available upon request. Rooms at Il Daino Bianco are uniquely furnished and come with a private entrance and a minibar.

Each has an en suite bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer. Orbetello is a 40-minute drive from this property, while Saturnia is 9 km away.
