Description

Relais Santa Chiara is set just 400 metres from the medieval walls of San Gimignano, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

It offers elegant rooms, a garden, and an outdoor pool with a hot tub. Air-conditioned rooms at the Relais come with satellite TV and a minibar, whilst the private bathroom offers free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Some rooms have views of the surrounding vineyards. A rich breakfast buffet is provided daily, while during warmer months, light lunches are served on the terrace overlooking the pool.

The staff can help reserve a table in one of the many restaurants in the nearby historic centre.

The hotel overlooks the countryside near San Gimignano, with its 14 medieval towers still standing.
