Description

The rooms in the farm stay are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Rooms come with a private bathroom with free toiletries. Guest rooms at La Tenuta di Castelvecchio have air conditioning and a desk.

A continental breakfast is served at the property.

The accommodation has a terrace. Cycling is among the activities that guests can enjoy near La Tenuta di Castelvecchio.

Peretola Airport is 27 miles from the property.
