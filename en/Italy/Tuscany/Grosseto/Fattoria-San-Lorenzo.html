Description

Fattoria San Lorenzo is a 450-hectare estate set outside Grosseto, 12 km from the beach.

It offers traditional Tuscan apartments, an outdoor pool and 2 restaurants. The San Lorenzo's apartments feature terracotta floors and exposed wooden beams in the ceiling.

They are located in different buildings around the estate. All are air conditioned and have a balcony and a separate seating area.

Kitchenettes are fully equipped with dishwasher, microwave, and fridge. On request, a basket of continental breakfast ingredients is provided in your apartment.

One of the estate's restaurants specialises in local cuisine. The other offers Bavarian beer and German dishes.

Sun loungers and parasols are provided around the pool. The grounds also include barbecue facilities and gardens.

There is an on-site bicycle and car rental service. Fattoria San Lorenzo is 3 km from Grosseto Train Station.

The estate is well placed for visits to the Uccellina National Park, a 30-minute drive away.
