Description

At the farm stay, rooms come with a balcony. Rooms have a private bathroom with free toiletries, while some rooms are equipped with a patio. At Azienda Agricola Il Pozzo all rooms are equipped with a closet and a flat-screen TV.

Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Capolona, like cycling.

Arezzo is 9 miles from Agriturismo Il Pozzo, while Rapolano Terme is 25 miles from the property. Peretola Airport is 37 miles away.
