Description

Located in the hills of Tuscany, this family-run estate offers a restaurant, horse riding stables and a summer outdoor pool.

Its elegant rooms are located in part of a restored castle, 14 km from Florence. Rooms at Tenuta dei Cavalieri have wrought iron beds, wood-beamed ceilings and terracotta floors.

Each includes an LCD satellite TV. Wi-Fi is free throughout. Traditional local cuisine is served at the restaurant, which opens on request.

Vegetarian and gluten-free options are available. A breakfast buffet is served daily, from 08:00 until 10:00.

Home to sheep, donkeys and rabbits, the Tenuta runs special farm tours for both adults and children.

The estate is 2 km from Molino del Piano and a 40-minute drive from Florence-Peretola Airport.
