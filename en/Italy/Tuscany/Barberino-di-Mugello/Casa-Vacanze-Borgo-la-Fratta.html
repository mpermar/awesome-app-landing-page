Description

Only 100 metres from Lake Bilancino, Casa Vacanze Borgo La Fratta features a garden with BBQ and outdoor pool.

Offering free Wi-Fi in public areas, it is in Barberino di Mugello, 40 km from Florence.

Featuring a furnished patio, the apartments have a kitchenette, a seating area and a dining area.

Guests at Borgo La Fratta can enjoy a sweet breakfast on request. There is a children’s playground.
