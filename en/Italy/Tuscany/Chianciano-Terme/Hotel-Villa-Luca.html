Description

Offering free WiFi, and free bike rental, Hotel Villa Luca is located Chianciano Terme.

The property has a panoramic rooftop pool with views of the Tuscan countryside, Montepulciano and Lake Trasimeno.

A sweet and savoury buffet breakfast is provided. A snack bar is also available. The on-site restaurant serves Tuscan cuisine.

All air conditioned, rooms at the Villa Luca come with a TV and minibar. Some rooms include a balcony, spa bath or hydromassage shower.

The private bathroom has a hairdryer and free toiletries. Montepulciano is 15 minutes’ drive from the property.

Pienza historic centre, reachable by car in 25 minutes, is a UNESCO heritage site.
