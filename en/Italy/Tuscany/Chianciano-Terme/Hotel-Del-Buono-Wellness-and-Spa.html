Description

Offering comfortable rooms and a long list of wellness facilities, Hotel Del Buono Wellness & Spa is in the spa town of Chianciano Terme and features a rooftop swimming pool.

The gym and WiFi are free. Here you will find a Kneipp path, an indoor pool and a Turkish bath available at extra costs.

Wellness treatments are personalised and advised by a team of qualified staff. Rooms here come with a TV, safe and private bathroom with hairdryer.

Pool towels and slippers are included. The restaurant offers special dietary meals.

Hotel Del Buono Wellness & Spa has been welcoming guests for over 60 years. It overlooks the 3 lakes of Chiusi, Montepulciano and Trasimeno.

It is just a few steps from the thermal spa of Chianciano Terme and around an hour's drive from Siena.
