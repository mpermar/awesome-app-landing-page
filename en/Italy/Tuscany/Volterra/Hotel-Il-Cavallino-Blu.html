Description

Set in an 18th-century country manor, Hotel Il Cavallino Blu is a family-run hotel a 5-minute drive from Volterra.

It offers an outdoor swimming pool, a pool bar and a terrace with panoramic Tuscan countryside views.

Rooms at the Cavallino Blu Hotel come with a balcony and views of the surrounding parkland.

Each one includes a fully equipped bathroom with hairdryer and toiletry set. WiFi is free in the entire building.

A breakfast buffet is available daily, while lunch can be served at the pool bar.

A computer is available in the communal rooms or on the panoramic terrace. The Hotel Il Cavallino Blu lies 30 km from San Gimignano, and Siena is a 1-hour drive away.
