Description

Tenuta Casteani Wine Resort is set in the Maremma region, surrounded by olive groves and vineyards, 4.

8 km from Ribolla. The property boasts a seasonal outdoor and a kids' pool. Guests can enjoy an on-site restaurant.

With panoramic views, rooms at the Tenuta Casteani have a private bathroom. Some units include a seating area.

A buffet breakfast is served daily and features home made cakes, jam and cold cuts.

The restaurant serves traditional Tuscan cuisine and wine tasting is available on site.

The resort also offers bike hire. Grosseto is 28 km from Tenuta Casteani Wine Resort, while Follonica is a 20-minute drive away.

Volterra is 47 km from the property, while the nearest airport is Marina di Campo Airport, is 67 km away.
