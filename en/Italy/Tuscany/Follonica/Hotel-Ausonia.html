Description

Hotel Ausonia is set on the Tuscan coast among pine woods, facing Elba Island. It features a private beach and air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, a private bathroom and a minibar.

Ausonia's restaurant serves Tuscan cuisine with vegetarian and other dietary options.

Drinks can be enjoyed in the garden or on the terrace. The Ausonia Hotel is located in Follonica, 30 km from Piombino where ferries depart for Elba.
