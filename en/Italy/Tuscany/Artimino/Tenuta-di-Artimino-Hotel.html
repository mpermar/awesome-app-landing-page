Description

At the hotel, rooms contain a closet. The rooms include a private bathroom and a hair dryer, and selected rooms have a patio.

A continental breakfast is served each morning at the property.

Tenuta di Artimino Hotel offers 4-star accommodations with a hot tub and terrace. Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Artimino, like cycling.

Speaking French, German and Italian at the 24-hour front desk, staff are ready to help around the clock.

Florence is 11 miles from Tenuta di Artimino Hotel, while Lucca is 27 miles away. Peretola Airport is 8 miles from the property.
