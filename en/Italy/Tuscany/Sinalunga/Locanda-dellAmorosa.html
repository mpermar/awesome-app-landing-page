Description

Boasting an outdoor swimming pool and an à la carte restaurant, Locanda dell'Amorosa is a restored 14th-century castle, located 3 km from Sinalunga, in the Val di Chiana Valley.

It offers elegant rooms with free Wi-Fi. Offering air conditioning, all rooms come with a satellite TV with DVD player.

The private bathroom includes a bath tub or shower and hairdryer. An American buffet breakfast is served daily in the breakfast room.

Guests can enjoy the sun in the garden, or have a snack in the bar. The restaurant serves local cuisine at dinner.

The Locanda dell'Amorosa hotel is a 15-minute drive from the picturesque Tuscan city of Montepulciano.
