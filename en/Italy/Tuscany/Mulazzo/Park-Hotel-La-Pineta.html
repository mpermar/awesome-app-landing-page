Description

Surrounded by green parkland, La Pineta features a traditional restaurant, an outdoor pool with hot tub and peaceful countryside views.

All bright rooms come with satellite TV, located 5 km from Mulazzo's historic centre.

Rooms at Park Hotel La Pineta have colourful furnishings and cool tiled floors. Each has a private bathroom equipped with hairdryer and toiletry set.

Breakfast is buffet style, with sweet pastries, cereals and fruit. The Da Giovanni restaurant serves specialities from Lunigiana and a good selection of wine.

Sports facilities include a tennis court. The poolside terrace is furnished with sun loungers, and there is a playground for children.

The Tosco Emiliano Appennine Mountains are 50 km from the hotel, and it is a 30-minute drive to La Spezia and the coastal towns of the Cinque Terre area.
