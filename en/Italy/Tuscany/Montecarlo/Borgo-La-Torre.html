Description

Rooms are decorated in a modern country style with wood-beamed ceilings and flat-screen TVs. Many have views of the surrounding vineyards and olive groves.

Restaurant Enoteca La Torre serves traditional Tuscan cuisine and you can enjoy wine tasting from the hotel’s on-site wine cellar.

La Torre is located in the small Tuscan town of Montecarlo, just a 30 minute drive from the historic city of Lucca. The A11 Florence-Mare highway is a 10 minute drive away.
