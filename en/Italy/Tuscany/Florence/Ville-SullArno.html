Description

Ville Sull'Arno offers accommodation in Florence, right on the banks of the river Arno.

It features a restaurant and a free wellness centre with an indoor pool, sauna, and Turkish bath.

Free WiFi access is available. Rooms here will provide you with a minibar, satellite channels and cable channels.

All include an electric kettle and a private bathroom with slippers. Some have river views.

The restaurant serves traditional Tuscan cuisine with a modern twist. It has rustic décor and some tables come with panoramic views.

At Ville Sull'Arno you will find free bikes. Other facilities offered include a free shuttle service to/from Florence, ticket service and luggage storage.

The hotel is 2. 3 km from Piazza della Signoria, 2. 9 km from Santa Maria Novella and 2.
