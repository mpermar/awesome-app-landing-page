Description

Just 700 metres from the main railway station, Lungarno Vespucci 50 features old-world charm, friendly staff and a roof garden overlooking the Arno River in Florence.

The Vespucci 50 is located in the historical centre, far from the crowded tourist areas.

You will be facing the river, near Le Cascine park, the American Consulate and the Teatro Comunale.

Take advantage of the private indoor car park. You will find a 24-hour front desk, room service and express check-in and check-out.

Guests can enjoy an aperitif served daily at the bar with frescoed ceilings from 18:00 until 20:00.

Stay in spacious refurbished rooms with modern bathrooms. Enjoy the high ceilings, some with historic frescoes.
