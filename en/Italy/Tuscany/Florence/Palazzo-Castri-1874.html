Description

Featuring a wellness centre with an outdoor pool and a beautifully landscaped garden, design hotel Palazzo Castri 1874 has been recently refurbished and is just 400 metres from Santa Maria Novella Train Station.

Every day, you enjoy a sweet Italian breakfast, which includes fresh fruit and Italian croissants.

The restaurant's dining room overlooks the garden and in warmer weather you can enjoy your breakfast outside.

This property dates back to the 19th century. All air-conditioned rooms come with a satellite TV, a desk and private bathroom with free toiletries, most overlook the garden, a total of 1000 m² in size.

The wellness centre includes a sauna, fitness centre and and indoor pool which is connected to the outdoor pool.

Beauty treatments and massages can be booked. Galleria dell'Accademia Gallery is 650 metres from Palazzo Castri, while Santa Maria del Fiore Cathedral is a 10-minute walk away.
