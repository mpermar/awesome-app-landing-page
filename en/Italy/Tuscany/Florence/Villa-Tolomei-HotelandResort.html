Description

Elegant and spacious, the rooms at this restored Renaissance villa overlook the beautiful gardens. Each is air-conditioned and features frescoed ceilings, classic furnishings and a sitting area.

The restaurant features a combination of traditional Tuscan dishes and fine cuisine, accompanied by a choice of local wines. Each morning, guests are served a buffet-style continental breakfast.

Villa Tolomei has a gym and panoramic terrace, and is a 15-minute drive from the Uffizi Gallery and the Ponte Vecchio bridge. Florence's Santa Maria Novella Train Station is 3 miles away.
