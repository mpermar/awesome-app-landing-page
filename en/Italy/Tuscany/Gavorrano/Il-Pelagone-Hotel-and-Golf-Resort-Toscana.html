Description

Immersed in a lush Mediterranean park with artificial lakes, Il Pelagone Hotel & Golf Resort Toscana is open throughout the year.

In the heart of the Tuscan countryside, it offers fully equipped apartments with air conditioning and a balcony or garden.

A small, yet comprehensive Tuscan-style wellenss centre offers Ayurveda and beauty treatments.

With sauna, steam bath, and state-of-the art equipment, it is always up-to-date with the latest developments in well-being technology.

The resort also boasts 2 superior restaurants (one of which was voted the best among Golf resort restaurants in Italy), 2 swimming pools, and 2 tennis courts.

A private beach with sun loungers and bar service is also available, set 18 km from the property.

Set right next to the apartments, a wonderful 18-hole golf course is surrounded by palms, olive trees and cypresses.

Numerous hiking and mountain biking trails are located in the surroundings. The Maremma Natural reserve is 50 km from the property, and Follonica's beaches can be reached by car in just 20 minutes.
