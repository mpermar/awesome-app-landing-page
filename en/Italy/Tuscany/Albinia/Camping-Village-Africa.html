Description

Located half way between Albinia and Fonteblanda, Camping Village Africa is set in a century-old pine forest, right on the beach.

It offers self-catering bungalows and direct access to the public beach. Village Africa is complete with a food market, a bar, and a restaurant and pizzeria.

A tennis court and soccer field are also on site. Entertainment during summer includes aqua gym, beach games, and discos.

Bungalows are air conditioned and feature a seating/dining area with kitchenette, and a private bathroom.

Each comes with an equipped patio. The camp area also has shared laundry facilities for guests.

Wi-Fi is available. Albinia Train Station is 4. 5 km away, with links to Rome and Pisa.

Within a 20-minute drive from the property, you will reach Talamone, Orbetello and Mount Argentario.
