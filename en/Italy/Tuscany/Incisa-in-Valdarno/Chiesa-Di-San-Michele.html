Description

Surrounded by olive groves and woods, Chiesa Di San Michele is set in a 12th-century building.

It features free Wi-Fi throughout and a garden with a children’s playground. All units feature a private bathroom and a private or shared kitchen.

The rustic-style apartments come with a panoramic terrace, a seating area and a TV.

Church of San Michele is a part of a farm producing olive oil. Engraved in Val d’Arno’s center is a 10-minute drive from Chiesa di San Michele.
