Description

Offering a seasonal outdoor pool and a children's playground, TOSCANA CHARME RESORT is set in Tirrenia, in the Tuscany Region, a 2-minute walk from the sandy beach.

Guests can enjoy a private beach area. An on-site restaurant and bar are featured.

Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and features a flat-screen TV and a minibar.

Every room comes with a private bathroom fitted with a bidet and a shower. Extras include free toiletries and a hairdryer.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. Free WiFi is available in public areas.

Free on-site private parking is at guests' disposal. TOSCANA CHARME RESORT is 12 km from Livorno and 17 km from Pisa.
