Description

Set on Aliano Hill in a 7000-m park filled with ancient cypress trees, Villa La Cappella dates back to the 12th century.

It features 1 outdoor heated pool with views of the Chianti countryside. The Villa offers free parking, and is located along the wine road connecting Montespertoli and the medieval towns of Certaldo and San Gimignano.

Units offer rustic furnishings, free wired internet, and a private bathroom with either a bath or a shower.

Units on the first and 2nd floor enjoy views of the Tuscan hills including Aliano Castle.

A buffet-style breakfast is provided daily from 08:30 until 11:00. The panoramic sun terrace is equipped with sunbeds and parasols, and features a large outdoor swimming pool and a shallow pool for children.

The park is complete with playground and ping-pong table. Florence is 21 km from Hotel di Charme Villa La Cappella.

San Gimignano is a 30-minute drive away. A bus stop with links to Florence, Pisa and Lucca is located just in front of the property.
