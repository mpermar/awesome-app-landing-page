Description

Each of Anselmo's apartments features a satellite TV, a fully equipped kitchenette and a garden or a terrace.

The Podere dell'Anselmo's restaurant serves typical Tuscan cuisine along with the farm's own wine produced on site. Dining outdoors under the gazebo is possible during the warmer months.

The Anselmo farm is just a 20-minute drive from Florence. Shuttle service to and from the airports of Pisa, Bologna and Florence can be arranged.
