Description

Agriturismo Le Baccane is surrounded by the Tuscan countryside and features en suite rooms and self-catering units.

Set in its garden featuring vineyards and a swimming pool, it has a traditional restaurant and free Wi-Fi throughout.

Units and rooms have a rustic style, a TV, a cooling fan, and a bathroom with shower.

Studios and apartments have dining/living area and kitchen, while apartments also feature a cosy fireplace and 2 separate bedrooms.

At the Baccane Agriturismo you can relax in the furnished garden, where you will find the swimming pool, darts, and a playground for children.

The property produces its own olive oil and wine. Breakfast consists of sweet and savoury products, while the restaurant specialises in typical Tuscan recipes and Mediterranean cuisine.

Gluten-free and vegetarian options are available on request. Montecatine Terme spa town is 12 km from the property.
