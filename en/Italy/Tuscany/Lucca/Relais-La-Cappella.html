Description

Relais La Cappella is set in an old monastery in the countryside of Lucca. It features a little chapel and free WiFi.

With views of the pool and garden, classic-style rooms come with air conditioning, parquet floors and a private bathroom.

A rich sweet and savoury breakfast is served daily at Relais La Cappella's traditional Tuscan dining room.

Guests can use 2 relaxing lounges equipped with chairs, sofas and TVs, and a library.

Lucca centre is 7 km from the property, while the Ligurian Sea is a 40-minute drive away.
