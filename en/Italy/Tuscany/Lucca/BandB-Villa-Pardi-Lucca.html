Description

This charming 18th-century villa is set in the Tuscan countryside, 6 km outside Lucca.

Villa Pardi Lucca offers a large garden with swimming pool and rooms with many original features.

Free parking is available on site. Throughout Villa Pardi you can admire original frescoes, chandeliers and wood-beamed ceilings.

Each room has free Wi-Fi access, a TV and private bathroom. Breakfast is buffet style and is served in the cosy breakfast room.

Located in Nozzano Castello, Villa Pardi is easily reached from the A11 motorway and is 2 km from Nozzano Train Station for connections to Lucca and Viareggio.
