Description

Situated in Lucca in the Tuscany Region, 5 km from Guinigi Tower, Tenuta San Giovanni Lucca boasts a seasonal outdoor pool and barbecue.

Villa Reale is 11 km away. Free private parking is available on site. The accommodation is fitted with a dining area and seating area with a flat-screen TV.

There is also a kitchen, fitted with a fridge and stovetop. Every unit has a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Bed linen is offered. Lucca Cathedral is 4. 3 km from Tenuta San Giovanni Lucca, while Basilica of San Frediano is 5 km from the property.
