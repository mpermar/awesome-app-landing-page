Description

Located in Poppi, Il Contado Country House & Spa offers a garden. Florence is 41 km from the property.

Free WiFi is featured throughout the property. The accommodation is equipped with a flat-screen TV.

Some units are air conditioned and include a seating and/or dining area. Some units also have a kitchen, fitted with a dishwasher and oven.

There is a private bathroom with a bidet and free toiletries in every unit. Free private parking is available at the aparthotel.

Il Contado Country House & Spa also includes a hot tub, sauna, and hammam. Bike hire and car hire are available at the property and the area is popular for golfing.

The area is popular for cycling and hiking. Arezzo is 30 km from Il Contado Country House & Spa, while Bagno di Romagna is 20 km away.
