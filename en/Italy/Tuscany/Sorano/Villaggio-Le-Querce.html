Description

Villaggio Le Querce consists of 25 luxury apartments in an idyllic setting. The property is located in a beautiful 2-hectare park in Sorano, an ancient medieval town in the province of Grosseto.

The estate consists of a series of terraced houses, each with independent entrances activated by electronic keys.

Each well-appointed, air-conditioned apartment has a private bathroom, a bedroom and a fully-equipped kitchen area.

Downstairs, there is a private garden and upstairs there are balconies with great panoramic views of the estate and its beautiful surroundings.

The superb, tranquil parkland setting, surrounded by oak trees, makes Villaggio Le Querce ideal for a peaceful, relaxing holiday.

The high-standard luxury apartments offer freedom, independence and convenience. The good facilities include a large outdoor swimming pool, a children’s activity playground, a tavern with a view of the park and free private car parking for 80 cars.
