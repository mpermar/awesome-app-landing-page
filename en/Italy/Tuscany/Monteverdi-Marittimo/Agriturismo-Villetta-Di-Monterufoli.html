Description

The Villetta di Monterufoli is a nice agriturismo set in a former railway station dating back to 1850.

Wine and oil are produced on site. Tastings are arranged. Agriturismo Villetta Di Monterufoli has a garden with children's playground, table tennis and table ball.

The highlight of the garden is the outdoor swimming pool. All rooms offer air conditioning and views of the garden or countryside of nearby Cecina.

The Villetta serves delicious Tuscan cuisine. Outdoor dining is possible in spring and summer.
