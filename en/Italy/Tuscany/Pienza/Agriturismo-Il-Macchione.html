Description

Set in 50 hectares of private land, Il Macchione is an 18th-century stone farmhouse.

Just 1. 5 km outside Pienza, it features a swimming pool that overlooks Val d'Orcia Natural Park.

Apartments have a rustic style with wood-beamed ceilings and antique furniture. They all feature a comfortable living and dining area with a fully equipped kitchenette.

You will also have air conditioning, satellite TV channels and free Wi-Fi. Outdoors, you will find the 70-m terrace featuring a swimming pool with hydromassage area and plenty of sun loungers, chairs and tables.

The grounds also include a children's playground, gazebos and lots of vineyards, olive trees and colourful flowers.

Agriturismo Il Macchione serves a buffet breakfast each morning and a BBQ is available in the garden.
