Description

A former theatre, Hotel Residence San Gregorio is 50 metres from Pienza’s historical centre and near the Val d'Orcia Natural Park.

The property offers free WiFi, covered parking and spacious accommodation. The San Gregorio features a large private courtyard and a garden.

Traditional Tuscan food is served at the La Piazzetta restaurant. A buffet breakfast including homemade cakes, yogurt, cold cuts and cheese is served daily.

Accommodation is decorated with wood furnishings. Enjoy a 32-inch satellite LED TV, air conditioning, and a minibar in every room and suite.
