Description

Located 10 km from the beaches of Castiglione della Pescaia, the Casa Badiola Golf House has a spa, 2 pools and tennis courts.

It offers free Wi-Fi and elegant accommodation with satellite TV. Suites and studios at Casa Badiola come with air conditioning, together with bathrobes and slippers in the private bathroom.

The daily sweet breakfast is served buffet style. There is also a bar and restaurant.

The wellness centre includes a sauna, hot tub and Turkish bath. Massages are also available.

Free on-site parking is provided. Grosseto is 14 km from the property. Piombino is a 1-hour drive away.
