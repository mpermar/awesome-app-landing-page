Description

Roccamare Resort - Casa di Ponente offers an à-la-carte restaurant and a swimming pool with sun terrace.

Guests enjoy free use of 1 parasol and 2 sun loungers at the beach, as well as free access to the outdoor pool.

Rooms at the 4-star Roccamare will provide you with air conditioning, a desk and a private bathroom complete with free toiletries.

An extensive buffet breakfast with sweet and savoury food is served daily in the dining room.

The restaurant specialises in national and international cuisine. In summer, the hotel offers mini club, entertainment activities and sport tournaments, as well as live music.

Grosseto Airport is 24 km away. A free private parking is available on site, outdoors yet shaded and fenced.
