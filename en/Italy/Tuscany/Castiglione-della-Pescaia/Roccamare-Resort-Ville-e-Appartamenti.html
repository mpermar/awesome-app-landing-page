Description

Offering a free swimming pool in the garden, Roccamare Resort - Villas and Apartments is located in Castiglione della Pescaia.

It features apartments and villas with a kitchen and air conditioning, a gourmet restaurant, free WiFi and sports facilities.

The restaurant serves Mediterranean and international cuisine. Accommodation features a rustic décor with warm colours, and garden views.

Each comes with satellite flat-screen TV, bathroom and outdoor dining area. At the Roccamare Resort - Ville e Appartamenti you can enjoy a game of tennis or table tennis.

All rooms include access to the property's private beach, and the use of 1 parasol and 2 sun loungers.

Free parking is available. Grosseto is 29 km from the property. Piombino is a 50-minute drive away.
