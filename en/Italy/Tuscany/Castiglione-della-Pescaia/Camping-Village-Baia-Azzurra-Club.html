Description

Offering a private beach, swimming pools and lots of sports facilities, Camping Village Baia Azzurra Club is on the coast of the Maremma area of Tuscany, 200 metres from Le Rocchette Beach.

Camping Village Baia Azzurra Club offers bungalows and apartments, all air conditioned and with a private entrance.

You will find a fully equipped kitchenette, satellite TV and a balcony or patio. It is easy to find your way around the Camping Village as it is divided into streets named after flowers.

The main square area is brand new and features 2 swimming pools, one with hydromassage corner, and a large games room.

There is also an amphitheatre for evening entertainment and a pizzeria restaurant overlooking the pool.

The Baia Azzurra Club features a children's pool and play area, plus a five-a-side football pitch.

It is 6 km from the town of Castiglione della Pescaia and 20 minutes' drive from Punta Ala Golf Club.
