Description

Located 200 meters from the beaches of Castiglione della Pescaia, Hotel Rossella features free bikes and free Wi-Fi.

Guests can look forward to a Tuscan restaurant and rooms with a satellite LCD TV.

Rooms at the Rossella are classically furnished and all air conditioned. Each comes with a private bathroom with hairdryer, while some rooms have a balcony.

A buffet breakfast is provided daily. The restaurant serves grilled meat and pasta, together with seafood specialities.

Grosetto Station is 24 km from the property and is serviced by a bus stop 150 metres away.
