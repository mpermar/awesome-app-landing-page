Description

Offering 9 bedrooms, this villa features 9 bathrooms with a hot tub, a bath and a hair dryer. There's a dining area and a kitchen complete with a dishwasher, an oven and a microwave.

The daily breakfast offers continental, buffet or American options.

The villa has a grill. Guests can also relax in the garden.

Siena is 22 miles from Villa Il Poggiolino, while Arezzo is 16 miles away. The nearest airport is Sant'Egidio Airport, 39 miles from the property.
