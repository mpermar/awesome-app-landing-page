Description

Set in the Tuscan countryside a 10-minute drive from central Cavriglia, Camping Village Orlando in Chianti offers a spacious garden with swimming pool, hot tub and sports facilities.

The restaurant/pizzeria serves regional specialities, drinks and snack are available at the bar, and you can buy your own groceries at the mini market.

The mobile homes feature a furnished patio with BBQ, a TV, and a fully equipped kitchenette.

They are air conditioned with a living/dining area, and a bathroom with shower. Entertainment staff is provided and biking and hiking tours can be enjoyed in the areas nearby.
