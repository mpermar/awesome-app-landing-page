Description

Offering a garden with free outdoor pool and BBQ facilities, Prato A Baldo is set in a 19th-century farmhouse, surrounded by the Tuscan countryside.

This property produces organic extra-virgin olive oil, fruit and vegetables. Spacious rooms here feature a private patio and a rustic décor with wood-panelled ceilings.

Each comes with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer. Wi-Fi is free throughout.

A continental buffet-style breakfast is served daily in a dedicated room or out in the garden.

You can sample typical regional cuisine at the on-site restaurant. A bus stopping 300 metres from Prato A Baldo links to the centre of Massa Marittima and Follonica.

You can reach Piombino harbour in 40 minutes by car, where ferries connect with Elba Island.
