Description

Situated between Versilia and the Cinque Terre, Marina di Massa's 4-star Hotel Excelsior offers views of the Tyrrhenian Sea and Apuan Alps.

The property features a garden with swimming pool and a roof garden. All rooms come with free wired internet.

Excelsior is near some of Tuscany’s natural beauties, such as the Apuan Alps, the Carrara marble quarries and the Tuscan-Emilian Appennine National Park.

Guest enjoy discounts at a nearby partner beach. The rooms are spacious, air-conditioned, and equipped with a satellite TV and a minibar.

Some rooms have a private balcony. Hotel Excelsior has meeting rooms, TV lounge, bar and even a children’s playroom, which is available during the summer months.
