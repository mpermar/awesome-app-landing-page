Description

Just 20 metres from the beach, Hotel Villa Elsa is in Ronchi Poveromo, on the Tuscan Riviera.

Its large garden features a swimming pool. A restaurant is also available. Rooms are all air conditioned and include satellite TV, free Wi-Fi and a minibar.

Each one has a private bathroom and views of the surroundings. Some also have sea views.

You will find plenty of parasols and sun loungers around the pool. Bikes are available to rent from reception.

Breakfast is buffet style and, during the summer months, lunch can be enjoyed outdoors.

Villa Elsa provides free parking and is 10 minutes' drive from Forte dei Marmi. Viareggio is 15 km from the property, and Pisa International Airport is 45 km away.

Massa Centro train station and the Nuovo Ospedale Apuano hospital can be reached in 10 minutes by car.
