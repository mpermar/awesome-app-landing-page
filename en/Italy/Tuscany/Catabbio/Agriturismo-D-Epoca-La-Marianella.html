Description

Agriturismo D' Epoca La Marianella is set in 130 hectares of private farmland in Catabbio in rural Tuscany.

It is just 10 minutes' drive from Saturnia and its famous thermal spa, Terme di Saturnia.

Offering a beautiful 2000 mÂ² garden, La Marianella is a working farm that produces its own wine and extra-virgin olive oil as well as fruit, cereals and cheeses.

Dating back to the 1920s, this historic stone building has a classic Tuscan country design.

The rooms and apartments have tiled or parquet floors and some feature wooden ceilings.

Each one offers free Wi-Fi, a TV and private bathroom. Breakfast is a buffet of local produce from nearby farms.

These include honey, cheeses and cold meats. Discounts are available at a horse riding stable 400 metres away.
