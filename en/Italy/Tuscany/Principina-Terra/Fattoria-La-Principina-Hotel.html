Description

Set in 100 hectares of Tuscan countryside, Fattoria La Principina is halfway between Grosseto and Principina a Mare beach.

Its extensive grounds include 3 swimming pools and sports fields. Each room has a balcony.

Rooms feature a classic design, with tiled floors and wood furniture. Air conditioning, a minibar and TV all come as standard.

Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the property. Breakfast is buffet style and includes fresh fruit and Italian cakes.

Tuscan dishes and wines can be enjoyed at dinner. There is also a small kitchen for parents to prepare baby food.

Fattoria La Principina Hotel also has a fitness centre and an indoor wellness centre with a heated swimming pool, Turkish bath, sauna and relax area.

The outdoor sports fields are ideal for football, tennis and volleyball. Set in Principina Terra, this 4-star hotel is around 6 km from the nearest beach.

Grosseto Train Station is also 6 km away for trains to Rome and along the Tuscan coast.
