Description

The Castellare Di Tonda is a farm estate in the heart of the Tuscan countryside. It offers apartments with panoramic views, swimming pools, free WiFi and free parking.

The Castellare’s apartments are located in 12 separate farm buildings across the estate, and each group of buildings has its own pool.

Many apartments offer views of the hills, the estate’s vineyards or its lake. The Castellare Di Tonda Tuscany Country Resort & Spa includes a wellness centre, a golf course and horse-riding stables.

La Cucina del Castellare restaurant is open every day for dinner and breakfast, and also for lunch on Sundays and holidays.

There is also a bar on the estate. The Castellare Country Resort is 58 km from Florence and 65 km from the Tuscan coast.
