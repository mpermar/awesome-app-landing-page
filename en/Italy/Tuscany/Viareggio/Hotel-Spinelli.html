Description

If you want to spend some quiet, relaxing moments in Viareggio, the Spinelli is what you need.

A few steps from Pineta di Levante Park, it offers comfortable family-run accommodation with free WiFi throughout.

The beach, the harbour and the famous Viareggio seaside promenade are all just a 5-minute walk away.

Many sports facilities and free public parking are also nearby. Hotel Spinelli was totally renewed in 2003 and equipped with all modern safety equipment.

It offers a spacious garden where you can relax, while your children play in absolute tranquillity.

In the American bar, skilled bartenders offer a wide variety of cocktails. You will also enjoy the high-quality traditional Tuscan food, served in a pleasant dinning room.

The chef specialises in fish and meat dishes. Gluten-free products are available upon request.
