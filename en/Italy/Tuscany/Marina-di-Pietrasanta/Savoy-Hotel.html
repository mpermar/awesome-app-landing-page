Description

The recently renovated Savoy Hotel is set in the heart of Versilia overlooking the sea and the sandy beach of Marina di Pietrasanta.

It is located just a few steps from the Versilia pine wood and faces the golden sands of this popular seaside resort.

It is close to the park La Versiliana which hosts a number of cultural events and entertainment all year long.

Bikes can be rented on site. With free WiFi, the comfortable guest rooms at the Savoy Hotel feature all modern conveniences and magnificent sea views.
