Description

Surrounded by pine woods in a quiet area of Marina Di Pietrasanta, Hotel Nuova Sabrina is just 2 minutes' walk from the beach.

It offers free parking, and spacious rooms with free Wi-Fi. Nuova Sabrina Hotel's rooftop terrace offers panoramic sea views, and is equipped with a seasonal swimming pool.

The Nuova Sabrina is a family-managed hotel with typical restaurant run by the same owners.

The buffet breakfast includes pastries, fruit juices, honey, jam and eggs. The hotel is situated below the Apuane Alps, and is ideally situated for visiting the beautiful coast of Tuscany.
