Description

Hotel Coluccini is an early 20th-century building located on the seafront of Marina di Pietrasanta.

Featuring a pretty furnished garden, it offers free parking, free Wi-Fi and 1 hour per day of free bike rental.

Rooms all offer views of the Tyrrhenian Sea and are air conditioned. Facilities include a 22” LCD TV with satellite channels, plus a fridge.

Some also feature a balcony. Traditional local recipes and classic Italian cuisine can be enjoyed at Coluccini’s restaurant.

The exit of the A12 motorway is 4 km away. The centre of the famous seaside town of Forte dei Marmi is a 5-minute drive away.
