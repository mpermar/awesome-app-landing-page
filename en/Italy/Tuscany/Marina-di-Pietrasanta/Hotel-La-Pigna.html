Description

Hotel La Pigna is a welcoming establishment situated close to the beach in the enchanting centre of Marina di Pietrasanta, surrounded by a typical cool pinewood on the Versilian coast.

The hotel’s convenient location, near the most attractive artistic and historical sites of Tuscany, including Pisa and Florence, will allow you to spend pleasant day trips.

If you just need to regain your energies, La Pigna is the ideal place to spend a relaxing holiday, sipping a drink by the pool and retreating in the carefully furnished and fully equipped rooms, most boasting wide terraces.
