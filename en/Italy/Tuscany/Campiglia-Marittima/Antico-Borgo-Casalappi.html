Description

Located in Campiglia Marittima’s countryside, just a 10-minute drive from the sea , Antico Borgo Casalappi offers a swimming pool, a spacious garden, free parking on site, and free WiFi in public areas.

All apartments are decorated in a typical Tuscan style, featuring wooden furniture, exposed wood-beamed ceilings and wrought-iron beds.

Each comes with a seating area, an en suite bathroom, and a fully equipped kitchenette.

In the summer, you can relax by the pool, have a swim, or just enjoy the peace and the sun in the property’s garden, equipped with tables, chairs, and deckchairs.

Borgo Casalappi also offers BBQ facilities. Torre Mozza, with its beaches, is just 10 km from the property.
