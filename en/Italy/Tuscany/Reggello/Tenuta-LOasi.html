Description

At the farm stay, each room comes with a closet. All rooms come with a private bathroom with a shower.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property. Guests can dine in the in-house restaurant, which specializes in Italian cuisine.

You can play ping pong at Tenuta L'Oasi.

Florence is 15 miles from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Peretola Airport, 19 miles from the property.
