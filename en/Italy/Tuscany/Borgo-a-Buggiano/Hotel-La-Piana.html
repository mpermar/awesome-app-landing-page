Description

Surrounded by olive groves and fruit trees, Hotel La Piana is a historic 13th-century building.

Just outside Buggiano Castello, it offers an outdoor pool and hot tub and produces extra-virgin olive oil.

The swimming pool is open from March until November and offers beautiful views of the surrounding hills.

In the garden you will also find sun loungers, a football pitch, bowling green and playground.

Indoors, the property features antique furniture, stone walls and ceilings with wood and terracotta panels.

Every room and suite is different but all offer views of the valley. You will have air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, a kettle and minibar.

Breakfast at La Piana Hotel is buffet style with local products, freshly baked cakes and jams.

It is served in the terrace in fine weather. Olive oil is available to taste and purchase on site.

Free parking is provided. Staff will provide maps of the local area and can arrange tickets to local museums, theme parks and the hot springs at Montecatini Terme, 5 km away.
