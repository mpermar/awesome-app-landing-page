Description

Offering a swimming pool, new soccer field and mini golf course, Camping Le Pianacce has something for everyone. Set in Castagneto Carducci, it is 15 minutes' drive from Marina di Castagneto Carducci beach.

The mobile homes feature a fully equipped kitchenette and patio with views of the garden and Tuscan countryside. All units come with a satellite TV.

Le Pianacce Camping has a bar and plenty of sun loungers around the pool. The restaurant serves Tuscan specialties and Italian cuisine.

You can get groceries from the mini market and enjoy the garden where there are some BBQ facilities. There is also a Spray Park with water games. Five hours of free WiFi are provided daily.

Free parking is provided and you are 20 minutes' drive from both Cecina and San Vincenzo.

From mid-June to the end of August a free shuttle bus takes you to Marina di Castagneto beach, 3.7 mi away.
