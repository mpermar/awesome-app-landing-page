Description

Set just outside Peccioli and at the foot of a hill, Hotel Portavaldera provides quiet, simple accommodation with free Wi-Fi.

It is a 5-minute drive from the Firenze-Pisa-Livorno Motorway. Parking is free. Portavaldera's rooms are bright and equipped with independent air conditioning, a TV and a minibar.

Some rooms are suitable for disabled guests. Breakfast is buffet style. The on-site restaurant offers local produce.
