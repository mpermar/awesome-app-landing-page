Description

Park Hotel Chianti is set in the heart of the Chianti Classico area, 15 km from Florence and 40 km from Siena.

This family-managed property offers free parking, an outdoor swimming pool, and modern rooms with free Wi-Fi.

Each room comes air conditioning, satellite TV, and private bathroom with shower.

Most rooms have a balcony, and some overlook the inner garden or the pool. The restaurant serves classic Italian cuisine and Tuscan specialities.

Breakfast is a generous buffet. Chianti Park Hotel is just off the Tavarnelle exit of the Raccordo Autostradale Siena-Firenze motorway.
