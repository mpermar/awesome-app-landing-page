Description

Podere Vigliano offers free parking, a swimming pool and outdoor terraces overlooking the hills and valley.

Just outside the pretty town of Tavarnelle Val Di Pesa, this former farmhouse has a quiet countryside location.

Free WiFi is provided at the property. Surrounded by a large garden of olive groves and woods, Podere Vigliano dates back to the early 1800s.

It now offers Tuscan style apartments, each with a private entrance, living room and fully equipped cooking facilities.

There are 2 outdoor terraces, one with a view of the hills and the other overlooking the swimming pool, which is open between May and October.

Free parking is provided and there is a bus that runs from Tavarnelle to Florence, 30 km away.

Sienna is 40 km away. You will find a supermarket 900 metres away. The surrounding area is famous for its festivals, cultural events, food and wine.
