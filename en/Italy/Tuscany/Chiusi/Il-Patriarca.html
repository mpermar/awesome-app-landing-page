Description

Originally a 19th-century villa belonging to a noble family, Il Patriarca has been carefully restored to its original beauty. It is surrounded by a beautiful pine forest.

Il Patriarca has 2 restaurants, one of which is open during the summer and is recommended by guide books. Try some delicious Tuscan cuisine accompanied by a wide selection of fine wines. The villa has 2 large reception rooms which are ideal for weddings and celebrations.

The villa is located in the heart of Italy, halfway between Rome and Florence, in southern Tuscany. The professional staff will be happy to organize guided tours and wine tasting excursions.
