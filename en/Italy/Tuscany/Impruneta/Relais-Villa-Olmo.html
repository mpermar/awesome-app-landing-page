Description

Surrounded by vineyards and olive groves, the Country Relais Villa L'Olmo features accommodations including villas and a working farm where extra virgin olive oil and wine are produced.

The accommodations are divided into apartments, cottages and farmhouses. They all have a cozy living area with LCD TV, and a full kitchen or kitchenette with dishwasher. All cleaning fees, towels, and bed linens are included.

Villa L’Olmo is ideal for a relaxing break in the Tuscan countryside. You can enjoy large, open spaces, extensive grounds, and a swimming pool. For those who prefer a slightly more active holiday, guests receive pecial rates at nearby golf and tennis clubs.

Get all the tourist information you need from the front desk. The professional staff can also help with reservations and arranging excursions. Florence is just 6 miles away. You can easily reach many other Tuscan cities to see art, which is perfect for an interesting day trip.
