Description

Set among the lush, green Tuscan hills just outside Impruneta city centre, Tenuta Torre Rossa Farm & Apartments offers spacious, comfortable apartments.

Most have splendid views of nearby Florence. Self-catering apartments are bright and colourful, with floral designs, warm colours, elegant white furnishings and terracotta floors.

Each apartment includes bed linen and towels. Free Wi-Fi is provided. A 19th-century country house surrounded by 20 hectares of olive groves, Tenuta Torre Rossa Farm & Apartments produces its own olive oil.

The large garden features a shaded area and a swimming pool open from mid-April until October.

This peaceful countryside setting is just a 10-minute drive from Florence's historic centre.

Tenuta Torre Rossa Farm & Apartments provides free private parking, and public transport into Florence is available nearby.

There are several dining options in the surroundings, including Tuscan restaurants, pizzerias, markets, bars, bakeries and rotisseries.
