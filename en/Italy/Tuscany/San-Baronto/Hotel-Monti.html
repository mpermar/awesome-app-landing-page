Description

Monti is in the Tuscan hills, in the picturesque village of San Baronto, near the birthplace of Leonardo da Vinci.

It features a peaceful garden with swimming pool and playground. This family-run hotel offers personalised service and top quality food in its small restaurant.

a varied buffet breakfast is served each morning. Rooms are spacious, recently restored and have a classic design.

All air conditioned, they also feature a TV, minibar and free Wi-Fi access. Each one has a private bathroom with hairdryer.

Hotel Monti provides free parking and a free bike rental service. Guests can relax in the swimming pool with hydromassage area and use the hotel's free internet point.

The hotel is well connected by the A11 motorway and is ideal for exploring Tuscany.
