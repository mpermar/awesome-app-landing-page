Description

The Restaurant at the Schiatti offers both Tuscan specialties and classic Italian cooking. The Villa serves a continental buffet breakfast and includes homemade cakes.

Villa Schiatti has an outdoor pool and children’s play area. Guests can also enjoy walking or mountain biking in the surrounding hills. Wine tasting tours can be organized.

The Schiatti Villa is 15 mi from the A1 Autostrada del Sole motorway.

Experiences

08/24/2017

Amazing views, great pool, wonderful service.

"Title says it all, doesn't it? This was our first visit here but certainly not our last: we stayed for two weeks with a family of 3, coming from Berlin, Germany. Most people tend to stay 3-5 days, it seems to us. Many families with children and a few couples of all ages. Because of the length of our stay we really got to appreciate what Villa Schiatti has to offer: Angela at reception is an angel; Alan runs the restaurant high above the valley (breathtaking views at night) with charme and knowledge; Laura never tires of breakfast moods - the breakfast is to emphasize as is the beauty of the rooms: they're not very large but tastefully furnished and comfortable. All air-conditioned which was a must as we had > 40 Celsius (> 105 F) on most days. Because of the location there often is a nice breeze and the pool is kept very cool at all times so you don't get a heat stroke. Ample parking. Lovely walks right in the neighborhood. A stone throw to Cortona, a short, beautiful drive to Arezzo. A large outlet mall not far, the lake close, and even excursions to Siena and Florence are doable within a day (though you will regret leaving the countryside if, like us, you live in a city all year round). Schiatti is not a luxury hotel - all the comfort none of the attitude - and great deals. For the asking price you can hardly do better in this neck of the woods. As I said: we'll be back!"
