Description

Rooms at Il Falconiere are in different locations around the property, which is home to vineyards and olive groves. Each room has with flat-screen satellite TV and a private bathroom. Wi-Fi is free in public areas.

Guests can relax by the outdoor pool, with lounge chairs and deck chairs. The Thesan Etruscan Spa offers a sauna, sensory showers and a Turkish bath, as well as beauty treatments and massages.

In a converted orangery, the restaurant serves traditional Tuscan dishes. Guests can sample the estate's own Baracchi wines, or join one of the Cooking under the Tuscan Sun gourmet cooking courses.

Relais & Châteaux Il Falconiere is 1.5 miles from the historic center of Cortona, and a 15-minute drive from Lake Trasimeno. Parking is free.
