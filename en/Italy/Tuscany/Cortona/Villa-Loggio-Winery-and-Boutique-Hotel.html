Description

Offering a garden with an outdoor pool, Villa Loggio Winery and Boutique Hotel is 15 km from Cortona.

Free Wi-Fi access is available throughout. All the rooms here will provide you with a flat-screen TV and air conditioning.

Featuring a hairdryer, private bathrooms also come with free toiletries and towels.

A sweet and savoury breakfast is offered daily. It includes hot drinks, pastries and cold meats.

You can enjoy tasting sessions of the property's award-winning wines. At Villa Loggio you will find a terrace.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling, hiking and table tennis.

The property offers free parking. Lake Trasimeno is a 15-minute drive from the property.
