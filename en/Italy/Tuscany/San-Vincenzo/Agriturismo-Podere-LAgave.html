Description

Located just 2 km from the sandy beaches of San Vincenzo, Agriturismo Podere L'Agave is a farm stay producing organic food and extra virgin olive oil.

Guests enjoy free WiFi and large outdoor areas. The farm stay offers 2 outdoor pools, 2 hot tubs and 2 barbecue areas, as well as gazebos and a playground.

A small fitness area is also available. Apartments at the Podere L'Agave come with wood-and-marble décor, and each has a fully equipped kitchenette and a furnished patio.

Some apartments offer sea views and some are air conditioned. Sweet produce, homemade cakes and bacon, as well as egg dishes and cold cuts are offered for breakfast, together with lactose-free and gluten-free options.

Extra virgin olive oil, Cinta Senese cold cuts and organic jams are produced on site.

Other facilities at this farm stay include table tennis and table football. Guests can relax at the bar, preparing healthy snacks in the afternoon and also drinks for the aperitivo.

L'Agave farm stay is home to many animals, including donkeys, Cinta Senese pigs, peacocks and chickens.
