Description

Right on the beach in San Vincenzo, Hotel il Delfino offers air-conditioned accommodation with free Wi-Fi.

This family-run hotel features a bar and rooms with a balcony. Offering a TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom, all rooms at the Delfino are functionally furnished and have tiled floors, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Just in front of the hotel, at Il Delfino beach it is possible to rent beach umbrellas, sun loungers and deck chairs.

Guests staying a minimum of 7 nights have free use of a parasol, deckchair and sun lounger.

A buffet breakfast with sweet and savoury items is available every morning. San Vincenzo Train Station is a 10-minute walk away.

The Piombino city centre and harbour are both 20 km south, with connections to the Island of Elba and Sardinia.
