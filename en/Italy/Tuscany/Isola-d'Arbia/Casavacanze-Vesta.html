Description

In the hilly area of Crete Senesi, Casavacanze Vesta is set between Val d'Orcia valley and the Chianti wine district.

Only 15 minutes' drive from Siena, it offers a circular pool with countryside views.

Vesta Casavacanze is composed of 4 rural buildings, each one offering a Tuscan country design with tiled floors and wood-beamed ceilings.

Apartments are air conditioned and include satellite TV and cooking facilities. The swimming pool is 20 metres wide and is surrounded by sun loungers and parasols.

You will also find a tennis court and a coin-operated laundry room. There is a BBQ and children's playground out in the garden.

The property is easily reached from Siena's ring road and is 40 minutes' drive from the town of Asciano.
