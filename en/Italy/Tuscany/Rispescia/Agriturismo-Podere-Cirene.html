Description

Agriturismo Podere Cirene is a typical Tuscan house located 10 km from Grosseto. It features a large garden with vineyards and olive trees, an outdoor swimming pool, and free bikes.

Wi-Fi is free throughout. Rooms mix modern and Tuscan-style décor, and come with terracotta floors.

Each room includes a private bathroom and views of the surrounding fields. The outdoor swimming pool is equipped with sun loungers.

Every morning, Podere Cirene serves a breakfast buffet that includes fresh products from the region.

The property also has a snack bar and a restaurant serving typical local products.

Podere Cirene is only 6 km away from the Marina di Alberese Beach. The property is connected with the Natural Park of Maremma, which organises activities such as canoeing, horse riding, and trekking.
