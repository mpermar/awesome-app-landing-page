Description

Set in the Parco dell'Uccellina park, Agriturismo Giuncola & Granaiolo is a 120-hectare farm offering free WiFi, rustic-style rooms and a restaurant.

Cereal grains and Chianina meat are produced on site. Set in 1 of the farmhouses spread over the property's grounds, rooms have a flat-screen TV, patio and fully fitted private bathroom.

A continental buffet breakfast of homemade pies and bread and some local produce is offered daily.

The Giuncola & Granaiolo's restaurant proposes a fixed meat and a fish menu, and has a garden-view dining hall.

Guests can relax in the garden, and buy drinks from the bar. Both the bar and shared lounge with fireplace are set in the main building.

In summer, there a free outdoor pool is available. The village of Rispescia is 1 km from the farm stay, while central Grosseto is 10 km away.
