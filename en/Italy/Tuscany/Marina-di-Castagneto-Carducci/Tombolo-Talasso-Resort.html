Description

Set directly on the unspoilt Tuscan coast, the Tombolo Talasso Resort comes with a large free wellness and thalassotherapy centre.

Enjoy peaceful natural surroundings and relaxing wellness treatments. Tombolo Talasso’s 200-metre, Blue Flag beach is well-equipped and just steps away from the hotel.

Walk through pine and olive groves and arrive at the hotel’s beautiful outdoor pools, one for children and one for adults.

Head down into the grotto to discover Tombolo Talasso’s extensive wellness centre.

Relax in the thalassotherapy saltwater pools, featuring hydromassage and sensory functions, as well as counter-current swimming.

Make the most of the Finnish sauna, Turkish bath, Roman bath and relax area in the wellness centre.

Beauty and massage treatments are available at extra charge, and exercise equipment is also available.

Guests can enjoy free yoga classes from mid-May to mid-September. As well as their own pool, kids have a playroom with games, films and cartoons.

Children’s treatments are available in the spa. Tombolo Talasso’s Corallo restaurant offers regional cuisine made from ingredients sourced from neighbouring estates, dishes with seaweeds and sea salts as well as a kids menu.

The hotel also has a seaside terrace with exceptional views where you can enjoy lunch buffets and drinks.

Almost 300 different wines can be found at the wine bar – enjoy them with local cheeses and meats.
