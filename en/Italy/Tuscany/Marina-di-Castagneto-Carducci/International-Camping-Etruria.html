Description

International Camping Etruria is set in a pine wood area in Marina di Castagneto Carducci, in front of the sandy beach.

The property is equipped with free BBQ facilities and has a free kids’ club. Each air-conditioned accommodation features a kitchenette and 2 dining areas, one indoors and one on the patio.

The private bathroom comes with a shower. Guests can try regional specialties, as well as pizza and fish dishes, at the on-site restaurant.

International Camping Etruria is 20 km from Cecina. Piombino port, with links to Elba Island, is a 30-minute drive away.
