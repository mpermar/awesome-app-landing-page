Description

Offering a 3000-m² garden with swimming pool and sun terrace, Ai Lecci in Centro is a 3-star hotel in the mediaeval centre of Sarteano.

It features a private parking and modern rooms with free Wi-Fi. Homemade cakes, cold cuts and cheese are some of the items included in the breakfast every morning.

Weather permitting, guests can enjoy this buffet on the furnished patio in the garden.

Rooms at the Lecci in Centro hotel are all air conditioned, with a flat-screen TV and en suite bathroom.

The hotel is in the Tuscan Val d'Orcia valley, 19 km from Montepulciano and about a 1-hour drive from both Florence and Perugia.
