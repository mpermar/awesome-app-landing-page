Description

Il Chiostro offers self-catering accommodation in a former friars' convent in the medieval centre of Suvereto.

The apartments have views of the Tuscan hills, covered in vineyards and olive groves.

Casa Vacanze Il Chiostro is a 15-minute drive from the seaside, and just a little more from historic cities of art including Volterra, Siena and San Gimignano.

Il Chiostro's apartments are furnished in Tuscan style, and equipped with air conditioning, electric kitchen, dishwasher and a microwave oven.
