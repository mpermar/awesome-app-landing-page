Description

Residence Reale is set on Reale Bay, 200 metres from the nearest beach and 2 km outside Porto Azzurro.

It offers outdoor pools for adults and children, a playground and free parking. The residence has 2 buildings, each with 2 floors.

Each apartment features a furnished balcony, TV and a kitchenette. Some have sea views.

The Reale Residence offers a terrace with tables, chairs, free WiFi and sea views.

The adult swimming pool features a hydromassage area and you will also find a coin-operated laundry room.

You are just a few steps from a free public beach and a private beach club. In the area you will find cafés, restaurants and pizzerias.

There is also a mini market, newsagents and tobacconist. At the beach you will find a canoeing and scuba diving centre.
