Description

A Tuscan country farmhouse with its own peaceful gardens and pool, Albergo Ristorante Da Vestro is set in the little mediaeval town of Monticiano, 30 minutes' drive from Siena.

Monticiano is surrounded by woods and close to nature reserves and horse riding stables.

Guests of Da Vestro have discounts at a thermal spa at Terme di Petriolo 15 km away.

Rooms at Hotel Da Vestro are simply furnished yet comfortable, with satellite TV and a private bathroom.

You will have free Wi-Fi, free parking and a large garden with children's playground.

A continental breakfast is served each morning at Da Vestro's restaurant, which specialises in traditional Tuscan dishes, including homemade pasta and desserts.
