Description

Located in Pescia in the Tuscany Region, Villa Colle Olivi boasts a seasonal salt water outdoor pool.

A shop with local products and wine is on site. A free parking and free WiFi are offered.

The rooms and suites feature a flat-screen TV and en suite bathroom with and free biological toiletries.

Some units have a seating area where you can relax, others have garden views. Collodi is 4.

5 km from Villa Colle Olivi, while Montecatini Terme is 10 km from the property. The nearest airport is Galileo Galilei Airport, 31 km from the property.
