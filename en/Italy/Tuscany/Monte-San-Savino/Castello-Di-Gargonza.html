Description

Original terracotta floors and wood-beam ceilings are some of the original furnishings of Gargonza's apartments and rooms. To provide a relaxing environment, TVs are not available.

All accommodations are located throughout the village. Some have a fireplace and views of the valley.

An outdoor pool, private gardens and a conference center are all within the walls of this medieval property. St. Tiburzio and St. Susanna Church are also found here.

La Torre di Gargonza restaurant is on site and open every day. Homemade pasta and traditional Tuscan cuisine are served daily, while vegetarian, gluten-free and Jewish options are available upon request.
