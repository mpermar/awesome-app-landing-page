Description

Antico Casale di Scansano is set in the Tuscan countryside just outside of Scansano.

Once a farm, it now offers an outdoor pool, free WiFi throughout and a garden. Guests can dine at the gourmet restaurant, which serves typical Tuscan cuisine and offers vegan and vegetarian options on request.

Other facilities include a full beauty farm with indoor pool, jacuzzi and a variety of treatments.

Access to the indoor pool and all the wellness facilities is available at an additional cost.

A riding school is available 7 km from the property. The Casale di Scansano's gardens have sun loungers, a playground and lovely views of the Maremma hills.

There is an elegant lounge with a real fire, a smoking room, and a restaurant with terrace.

You can also buy local products and cosmetics at the Antico Casale. Your free breakfast contains home-grown, local produce.
