Description

Offering a beachfront location, Hotel La Bussola - Beach & Golf is surrounded by cool pine wood.

Its sea-view restaurant serves traditional local cuisine, with fresh fish and meat specialities.

Rooms offer free WiFi. The rooms of Hotel La Bussola - Beach & Golf have views of the pine wood or the Thyrrenian Sea.

Each room is air-conditioned, and equipped with a tea/coffee maker and a flat-screen TV with pay-per-view channels.

The private bathrooms are complete with bathrobes and slippers. The hotel also offers a fully equipped apartment, with a large panoramic terrace and complete kitchen.

Guests can enjoy a lounge bar, pizza restaurant, tennis courts and football field.

The tourist harbour is 2 km from the hotel, which offers free parking. Punta Ala Golf Club is a 3 minute drive away.
