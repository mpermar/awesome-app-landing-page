Description

Surrounded by the Tuscan countryside, Hotel Le Rotonde offers a large garden with a pool overlooking the natural surroundings.

Its restaurant specialises in Tuscan cuisine and wines. Rooms at Le Rotonde are air conditioned and feature wooden floors and light-coloured walls.

Facilities include a balcony with views of the countryside or the Massaciuccoli Lake.

A varied breakfast including local products is available every morning. This can be served in the garden on sunny days.

The A11 motorway is a 5-minute drive away and leads to Viareggio and the coast in around 20 minutes.
