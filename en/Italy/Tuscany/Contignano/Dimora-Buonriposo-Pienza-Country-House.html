Description

At the resort, the rooms include a balcony with a garden view. Every room has a electric tea pot, while some have a patio. All guest rooms feature a closet.

Saturnia is 26 miles from Dimora Buonriposo Pienza Country House, while Bagno Vignoni is 8 miles away. The nearest airport is Sant'Egidio Airport, 38 miles from the accommodation.
