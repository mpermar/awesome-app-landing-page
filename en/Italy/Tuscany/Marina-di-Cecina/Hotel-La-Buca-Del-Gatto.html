Description

La Buca Del Gatto is a large 3-star resort located on the Tuscan coast of Cecina Mare.

It is separated from the sea by 500 metres of rich pine woods. The rooms are spread over 5 independent villas.

All rooms offer air conditioning, a satellite TV, and bright tiled floors. The rooms on the ground floor feature a furnished patio with pebble garden, and the rooms on the first floor come with 2 balconies.

Rooms for disabled guests are available on request. Hotel La Buca Del Gatto features a new wellness centre, 3 restaurants, a bar, a private beach, and 6 tennis courts.

Breakfast is a generous buffet, and the cuisine is traditional Tuscan. There are junior and mini clubs for children, with access to the private beach and dedicated play areas.

Free Wi-Fi is available in the lobby. Parking is free. Volterra, San Gimignano and Pisa are all easily reachable from this picturesque seaside resort.
