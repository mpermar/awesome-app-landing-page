Description

Set 2 km from the centre of Pitigliano, B&B Maremma nel Tufo is a family-run farmhouse surrounded by vineyards and olive groves.

It offers rooms, self catering apartments and a swimming pool. All themed, rooms and apartments feature country-style furnishings, a satellite TV and private bathroom with hairdryer.

Each of the apartments has a fully equipped kitchenette and dining area. A sweet buffet breakfast is served daily in the breakfast room with wood-beamed ceilings and terracotta floors.

It includes homemade cakes and pastries. Savoury produce is available on request.

The outdoor swimming pool is equipped with chairs, tables and deckchairs. The garden around the pool has a furnished patio and free barbecue facilities.

A shared laundry is also available. A bus leading to Grosseto stops right opposite the B&B while Orvieto can be reached in about 55 minutes by car.
