Description

Just 50 metres from the beach, Hotel Pensione Signorini offers classic accommodation in Castiglioncello.

With a garden, the property is 1. 5 km from Rosignano and CastiglioncelloTrain Station.

Rooms here come with a flat-screen TV. The private bathroom includes a shower. A sweet Italian buffet breakfast with homemade cakes, yoghurt and fruit juice is served daily.

There is also a restaurant serving regional cuisine. A bus which stops 50 metres from Hotel Pensione Signorini takes you to Livorno, 25 km away.
