Description

Hotel Atlantico is a family hotel offering well equipped, comfortable accommodation only 200 metres from the sea and comprehensive amenities including 2 swimming pools and whirlpools.

Hotel Atlantico enjoys a strategic location, immersed in a lush green area in the centre of Castiglioncello.

From there, you may conveniently explore the attractions of Tuscany. The area features cities such as Florence, Lucca, Pisa and Siena, as well as Elba Island and some of the most important wineries in Italy.

The hotel has its own restaurant, serving typical Mediterranean cuisine. Using fresh, natural ingredients, the restaurant is open all summer.
