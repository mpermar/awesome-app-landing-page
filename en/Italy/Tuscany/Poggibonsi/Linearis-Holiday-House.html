Description

Located in Barberino Val D'Elsa, the Linearis Holiday House has an outdoor pool. It offers free Wi-Fi in public areas, and country-style accommodation with garden views.

Apartments at the Linearis come with a kitchen or kitchenette, a bathroom and living area.

Free on-site parking is provided. Poggiponsi Station is 5 km from the property. San Gimignano is a 20-minute drive away.
