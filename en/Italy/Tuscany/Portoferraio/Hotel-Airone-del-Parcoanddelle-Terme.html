Description

The hotel is set in a tranquil area in front of the Rocca Medicea of Portoferraio.

Surrounded by a 30,000 m² garden, it features a saltwater swimming pool. The air-conditioned rooms at Hotel Airone come with TV, hairdryer and a balcony or patio.

The minibar is filled on request. The Airone Hotel features a bowling green, table tennis and a beach volley court.

The restaurant, immersed in the lush garden, offers table service at lunch and dinner.

It specialises in grilled meat, fish and typical dishes. Portoferraio harbour, with ferry links to the mainland, is 3 km from Airone Hotel.
