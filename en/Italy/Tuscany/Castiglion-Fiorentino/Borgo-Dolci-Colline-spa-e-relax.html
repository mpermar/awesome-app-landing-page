Description

The complex consists of beautifully furnished apartments, each with separate entrance.

The Borgo Dolci Colline spa e relax, situated at Castiglion Fiorentino, in the green olive groves of the Valley of Chiana, just 10 minutes from Cortona, 20 minutes from Arezzo and one hour from Florence, is the perfect result of a careful renovation of an old farmhouse dating back to the XVII century.

The perfect solution for a holiday in any season: in summer you can just sit back and relax admiring the rolling hills reading your favourite book, cool down from the summer heat with a long cool swim in the pool, sit back and absorb the warm Tuscan sun, just wander the local villages like Cortona strolling the cobbled streets.
