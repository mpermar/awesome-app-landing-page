Description

Apartments at the Orizzonte are all on the ground floor and feature a cozy living area with kitchenette and TV, and a veranda with table and chairs. Wi-Fi access is available in the large common areas.

The Villaggio has 2 restaurants, one open only in summer, which serve meat and fish dishes. Snacks and drinks are served throughout the day, and the grocery store is open from Easter until September.

Children have their own swimming pool with water slides. During summer, the entertainment team organizes games and activities for children.

Orizzonte Village is a 15-minute drive from the Calidario Thermal Spa. The parks of Sterpaia and Val di Cornia are both nearby.
