Description

Relais Ciavatta is a typical family-owned Tuscan residence surrounded by olive groves and century-old oaks, located 800 m from Montemerano and 5 km from Terme di Saturnia and the Cascate del Mulino waterfalls.

All en suite rooms and apartments are decorated in a typical Tuscan rustic style, and come with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV, some with hydromassage shower.

Cottages come with private garden and fully equipped kitchenette. Surrounded by olive and oak trees, the Ciavatta Relais has its own seasonal outdoor pool with hydromassage corner, as well as sweeping countryside views.

There is plenty of free parking space. You can try delicious Maremma specialities at the on-site restaurant, Osteria La Frasca.

All meals are homemade and prepared with home-grown or local organic ingredients.

The property is surrounded by a spacious park with wild horses. As a guest of the Ciavatta you enjoy a special discount at the Terme di Saturnia spa and golf complex.
