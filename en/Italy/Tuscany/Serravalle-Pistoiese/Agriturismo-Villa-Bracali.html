Description

Set in the countryside, Villa Bracali is 10 minutes’ drive from Pistoia town centre.

It offers air-conditioned accommodation with free WiFi, a free outdoor pool and a garden.

Olive oil is produced and sold on site. Bracali also feature a holiday home with a fully equipped kitchenette and seating area with a flat-screen TV.

Montecatini Terme is less than 5 km away. Florence can be reached by car in 40 minutes.
