Description

Located off the Brindisi-Lecce motorway just outside Lecce's ancient walls, Zenit Hotel is 300 metres from shuttle buses to Brindisi Airport.

It offers air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi and a 32” LCD TV. Balconies, private bathrooms and quality Dorelan mattresses are standard at the Zenit Hotel.

Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the property, and you will find free internet terminals at reception.

A generous Italian breakfast is served daily in the hotel bar. The Angels restaurant serves Italian cuisine and specialities from the Puglia region.

A large parking is available on site. The hotel is 20 minutes' walk from Lecce's cathedral and historic amphitheatre.
