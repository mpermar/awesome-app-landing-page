Description

Offering a sun terrace and views of the city, Attico Barocco is located in Lecce in the Apulia Region, 50 metres from the Basilica of Santa Croce and 200 metres from Villa Comunale.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. Every room has a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Some rooms have a seating area to relax in after a busy day. Enjoy a cup of tea from your terrace or balcony.

Each room comes with a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer.

The daily buffet breakfast, including local products, is served on the terrace in fine weather.

The bed and breakfast also offers bike hire and car hire. Piazza Sant'Oronzo is 100 metres from Attico Barocco, while Roman Amphitheatre is 200 metres away.
