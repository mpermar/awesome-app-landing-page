Description

This eco-friendly property is set in the lower Salento countryside, between 4 and 5 km from the beaches of Torre San Giovanni, Lido Marini, and Pescoluse.

It offers rustic accommodation in 3 restored ancient farmhouses. Hotel Masseria Le Pajare is surrounded by its own 3 hectares of farmland, with centuries-old olive groves, banana trees, and colourful flowers.

The garden features an outdoor swimming pool, and a sun terrace with free sun beds and parasols.

Each room is located on the ground floor and features a small patio. Rooms are equipped with air conditioning, and a satellite TV.

Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas. The restaurant is open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, and offers creative Salento cuisine.

Meals are served in the dining rooms with barrel ceilings, or on the terrace with views of the pool.

Masseria Le Pajare is complete with volleyball, tennis, and 5-a-side football courts.
