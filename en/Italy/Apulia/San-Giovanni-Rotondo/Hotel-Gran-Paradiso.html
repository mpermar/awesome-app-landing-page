Description

Hotel Gran Paradiso is in a quiet traffic-free street that leads to San Pio Basilica, 350 metres away.

You will have free parking, an outdoor pool, and views over San Giovanni Rotondo.

This new 4-star hotel, with internet point and Wi-Fi, is accessible to the disabled, and also offers a garden with playground.

Gran Paradiso's restaurant serves local and international recipes, with vegetarian and special dietary options.
