Description

Located in the village of Borgo Celano on the Gargano peninsula, Hotel Rossemi has free lobby Wi-Fi and a restaurant serving traditional cuisine.

It offers classically furnished rooms with a balcony. Parking is free on site. Rooms at the Rossemi come with a satellite TV and air conditioning, together with free toiletries in the private bathroom.

An Italian breakfast of croissants and cappuccino is provided daily. The restaurant specialises in local fish dishes, together with traditional Puglia cuisine.

The property is just 900 metres from the Dinosaur Museum. The Convent of San Matteo is 1.

8 km away, while San Giovanni Rotondo is 7 km from the hotel. Mattinata Beach is a 50-minute drive away.

A bus stopping right opposite the property takes you to San Giovanni Rotondo, Monte Sant'Angelo and Vieste.
