Description

Featuring air conditioning and a panoramic pool, Palace Hotel San Michele is set on a hill overlooking the Gulf of Manfredonia.

It offers a free spa, a restaurant and a garden just outside Monte Sant‚ÄôAngelo. With a satellite TV, all rooms at the San Michele are elegant and spacious.

Each includes a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer. Some rooms also feature a seating area.

Guests can admire the Gargano landscape when having breakfast, or enjoy the medieval style of the dining hall when at the restaurant.

Special meals can be prepared upon request. There is a private beach 15 km away, which features a free parking for guests.

The property offers 1 free parasol and 2 sun loungers per room. During July and August, the beach can be reached with the property's shuttle service, available at fixed times and at extra charge.

The spa features a wide variety of treatments, saunas and massages. Meeting facilities, a tour desk and gym are also provided.

Free WiFi is provided in common areas. Only 1. 5 km from the hotel, the city centre offers a bus running daily to Foggia and Manfredonia.
