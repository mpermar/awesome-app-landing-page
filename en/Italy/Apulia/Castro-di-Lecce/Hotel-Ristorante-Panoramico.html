Description

Hotel Ristorante Panoramico is set in Castro, a 5-minute walk from the sea. It offers a restaurant with panoramic terrace and an outdoor salt-water swimming pool.

Rooms at Panoramico Hotel all feature balconies overlooking the garden or the sea and come with air conditioning, minibar and satellite TV.

Other services at Panoramico include free parking, a terrace with sunbathing area and massages.

Wi-Fi is provided in the whole building. During summer, Castro is well linked by public bus with nearby towns like Otranto, Santa Cesarea Terme, Santa Maria di Leuca and Gallipoli.
