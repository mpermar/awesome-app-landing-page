Description

The rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Each room includes a private bathroom with free toiletries. Guest rooms at Borgo Canonica feature air conditioning and a closet.

A buffet breakfast is served at the property.

Alberobello is 12 miles from the accommodation, while Taranto is 21 miles away. The nearest airport is Casale Airport, 26 miles from Borgo Canonica.
