Description

La Baronessa is a renovated historic 12-hectare Masseria, a traditional farmhouse, set among Salento's scenic countryside.

Just 5 km from Gallipoli, it features 2 swimming pools and free parking. It consists of 2 buildings, a white and a red one, plus 10 small houses.

Rooms at Tenuta La Baronessa Resort are air conditioned and feature an LCD TV. Classic and superior rooms are in the white building, while deluxe and suite rooms are in the red one.

Some rooms feature stone vaulted ceilings, others are decorated with frescos and stuccoes, others with a fireplace and four-poster bed.

This is an eco-friendly property that uses solar energy. Located in Tuglie, it is 10 minutes' drive from Gallipoli and 45 minutes by car from Otranto, both offering beautiful sandy beaches.
