Description

All units include a fully equipped kitchen, allowing guests to prepare their own meals. An oven and coffee machine are also provided.

The resort village has a seasonal outdoor pool.

Grill facilities are included and guests can also relax in the garden.

Lecce is 18 miles from Tenuta Le Crie. The nearest airport is Casale Airport, 42 miles from the property.
