Description

Featuring sea-view rooms, decorated in a Mediterranean style, the Perla Hotel is located on the promenade at Casalabate.

It features an outdoor pool. The Perla's restaurant serves classic Italian dishes with specialities of the Salento region.

Breakfast is served in the sea-view dining room. Rooms are air conditioned and spacious, and are equipped with a minibar.

At the hotel, there is also a shared lounge area with TV. Hotel Perla is 200 metres from the bus stop to/from historic Lecce.

Brindisi Papola Casale Airport is just 30 minutes away by car. Free parking is available in the surrounding area.
