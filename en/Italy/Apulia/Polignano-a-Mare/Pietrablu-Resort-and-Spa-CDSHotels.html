Description

Set in 12 hectares and set on the beautiful coast of Puglia, Pietrablu Resort & Spa offers a range of leisure activities including 3 swimming pools, gym and sports fields.

Rooms and suites at Pietrablu Resort are air conditioned and feature tiled or wood floors.

Each one has either a terrace or patio overlooking the sea or garden and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Guests can enjoy Italian cuisine at Pietrablu's restaurant, which also serves specialities from the Puglia region.

During the day, you can relax at the small private beach, hire a canoe, or take part in sports tournaments.

A transfer service is available to the centre of Polignano 4 km away. The city of Bari is around 30 minutes away by car.
