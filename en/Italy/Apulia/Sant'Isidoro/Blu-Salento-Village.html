Description

Set right next to its own private beach, Blu Salento Village is 5 km from Porto Cesareo’s centre.

It offers 2 swimming pools, free parking and lots of sports facilities. The air-conditioned rooms have classic Mediterranean-style décor.

They include satellite TV, minibar and bathroom with hairdryer. The meals are buffet style, with freshly grilled fish and meat at lunch and dinner.

The Blu Salento has tennis and volleyball courts, as well as a five-a-side football pitch.

Daytime and evening entertainment is provided for both adults and children. WiFi is free in the public areas.

The hotel is 500 metres from the centre of Sant'Isidoro. Nardò is a 15-minute drive away, while Gallipoli is 20 km away.
