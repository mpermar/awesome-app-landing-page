Description

Offering a private beach area and a free outdoor swimming pool, Hotel Orchidea is 850 metres from the coast.

It features free Wi-Fi in public areas, a gourmet restaurant, and rooms with a private balcony overlooking Peschici or the hills.

All rooms are simply decorated and fitted with classic wooden furnishings and tiled floors.

Each is provided with air conditioning, a satellite TV, and a fully equipped en suite bathroom.

At the Orchidea Hotel you can sit and relax by the pool, which is located in the garden and is surrounded by plenty of sun loungers.

You can also take a short walk to the private beach, located 700 metres away. Breakfast is served daily and is buffet style.

It includes sweet products such as fresh pastries and croissants, and savoury options are available on request.

Peschici Calenella Train Station is just 3 km from the hotel. San Giovanni Rotondo is a 1-hour drive away.
