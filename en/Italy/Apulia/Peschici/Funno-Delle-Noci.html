Description

Set in the countryside 2. 5 km from Peschici, Funno Delle Noci offers an outdoor swimming pool, bar and self-catering bungalows.

There is a children’s playground, and a free shuttle service to the local beach. Featuring a patio, bungalows at Delle Noci all come equipped with a kitchenette, seating area and private bathroom with shower.

Some also include air conditioning. The site is surrounded by olive and orange groves, and offers table tennis.

There is a small supermarket on site, which can also provide take-away meals. The property is a 5-minute drive from Peschici Harbour, where ferries link to the Tremiti Islands.
