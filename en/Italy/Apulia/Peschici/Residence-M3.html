Description

Accommodations are fitted with air conditioning and features a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen with a dining area, and a private bathroom with a hair dryer. Some units have a terrace and/or balcony.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the in-house restaurant, which serves Italian cuisine.

The condo hotel has a grill.

There's a a playground at this property and guests can go cycling nearby.

San Giovanni Rotondo is 24 miles from Residence M3, while Vieste is 7 miles away.
