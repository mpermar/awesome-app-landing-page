Description

All units feature air conditioning and a flat-screen TV. There's a fully equipped private bathroom with bidet and free toiletries.

A continental, buffet or Italian breakfast is available every morning at the property.

The apartment has a grill.

A playground and water sports facilities can be found at Suites Piazza Umberto, along with a terrace.

Lecce is 26 miles from the accommodation, while Gallipoli is 23 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Casale Airport, 50 miles from Suites Piazza Umberto.
