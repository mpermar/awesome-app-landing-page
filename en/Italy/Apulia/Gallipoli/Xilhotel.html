Description

Xilhotel is a unique, historic building 100 metres from Gallipoli Train Station and 5 minutes' walk from the old town and the port.

The rooftop bar and sun deck offer views of the Ionian Sea. Free Wi-Fi is available in all areas.

Rooms at the Xilhotel have air conditioning, a minibar, a TV, and a marble bathroom.

Some rooms overlook the courtyard, and some have a balcony or window with sea views.

The buffet breakfast is served from 07. 30 until 10. 00 and guests can enjoy panoramic views of the port and Gallipoli historic centre from the dining room.

The friendly team of staff are available 24-hours a day and can organise transfers, guided tours, and courses at local scuba diving centres.

Guests have discounts at a beach nearby. The property is a 15-minute walk from the Purit beach and the closest rocky beaches.
