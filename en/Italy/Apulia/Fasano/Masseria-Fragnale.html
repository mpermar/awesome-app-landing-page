Description

The rooms comes with air conditioning, an oven, a toaster, a coffee machine, a bidet, free toiletries and a closet. Every room comes with a electric tea pot, while some have a terrace. All guest rooms will provide guests with a private bathroom with a shower.

Continental and buffet breakfast options are available each morning at Masseria Fragnale.

The accommodation has a playground. The area is popular for cycling, and car rental is available at Masseria Fragnale.

Non-stop guidance is available at the reception. Where staff speak French, Italian and English.

Casale Airport is 32 miles away.
