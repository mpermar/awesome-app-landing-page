Description

Hotel Sierra Silvana is set in the green hills of Selva di Fasano, 12 km from the famous Trulli of Alberobello.

Each room has a private garden or balcony. Rooms at Sierra Silvana feature air conditioning and satellite TV and are set in 5 buildings, scattered around the Mediterranean garden.

Here you will find a swimming pool and 4 Trulli, the stone houses common to this area.

The hotel has a private beach 15 minutes' drive away, where sun loungers and umbrellas are free for guests choosing the half board or full board option.

Sierra Silvana's bar has large windows overlooking the swimming pool. The restaurant serves local specialities.

The hotel is well located for visiting many local attractions, including the town of Ostuni and the caves at Grotte di Castellana, both 24 km away.
