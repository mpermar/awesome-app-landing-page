Description

Offering a summer pool, restaurant and air-conditioned rooms, Basiliani - CDSHotels is located a 5-minute drive both from Otranto Marina and Orte Beach.

This eco-friendly hotel also includes typical stone features and spacious verandas.

Some rooms are in the main building and have a balcony, while others are located in the annex, linked by a flight of 30 steps.

Each stylish room has a flat-screen TV, free wired internet and a private bathroom.

With a large terrace, the restaurant at Basiliani features Puglia specialities and classic Italian cuisine.

A sweet breakfast buffet is served every morning. Sauna, Turkish baths and an indoor pool with hydromassage corner are available at the spa, where guests can enjoy relaxing moments.
