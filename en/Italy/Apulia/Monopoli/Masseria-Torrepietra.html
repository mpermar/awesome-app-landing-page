Description

Offering an outdoor pool and terrace, Masseria Torrepietra is set 3 km from Monopoli in the Apulia Region, and 3 km from the coast.

Guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Free WiFi is featured and free private parking is available on site.

All rooms come with a flat-screen TV. Some units feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

Views of the sea, pool or garden are featured in certain rooms. Every room is equipped with a private bathroom fitted with a bidet and shower.

For your comfort, you will find slippers and free toiletries. Bike hire and car hire are available at this hotel and the area is popular for golfing.

You can play tennis at this hotel, and the area is popular for horse riding and snorkeling.

Alberobello is 17 km from Masseria Torrepietra, while Bari is 45 km away. The nearest airport is Bari Karol Wojtyla Airport, 49 km from the property.
