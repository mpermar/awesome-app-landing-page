Description

Villa Barone is an impressive converted monastery located near San Vito dei Normanni.

Brindisi is a 10-minute drive away, and the beach is even closer. This spacious and well-kept Villa Barone features a beautiful patio and private gardens facing an ancient amphitheatre.

The elegant restaurant and congress room are extremely popular locations for weddings, banquets and business engagements.

Villa Barone's rooms and self-catering apartments are air-conditioned. The staff can provide info for visiting Salento.
