Description

Offering an outdoor swimming pool and a garden, Coppola di Seta is located in the countryside, 4 km from the centre of Locorotondo.

It offers rooms with vaulted ceilings and free Wi-Fi. All air-conditioned rooms come with tiled floors and free heating.

The private bathroom includes a bath tub or shower. A sweet and savoury buffet breakfast, including cakes, croissants, yogurt, cold cuts, cheese and eggs, is served daily in the living room or outside on the patio.

Guests can relax in the garden or by the pool, complete with sun loungers. Discounted packages at a local Spa are also available for guests.

Coppola di Seta is a 30-minute drive from the sandy beach of Capitolo. Alberobello, famous for its unique Trulli dwellings, is 7 km from the property.
