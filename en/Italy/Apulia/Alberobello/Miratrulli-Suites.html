Description

Set in the centre of Alberobello, these air-conditioned units and Trulli come with a kitchenette and views of the UNESCO-protected Trulli houses.

A public car park is located 50 metres away. A washing machine, fridge and dining area are standard in the apartments at the Miratrulli.

The apartment is set on 2 floors, and has a balcony overlooking the historic centre.

The stone walled trullo is located in a separate building. Breakfast products at your disposal in the accommodation include croissants, biscuits, Melba toast and hot and cold drinks.

Restaurants and shops are within a 5-minute walk of the apartments. Bari Airport is 68 km away, and airport transfers can be arranged on request.
