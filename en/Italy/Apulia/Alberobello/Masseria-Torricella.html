Description

Offering a heated outdoor swimming pool, restaurant and set in 40 hectares of private greenery, Masseria Torricella is located in the heart of the Itria Valley, 5 km from Alberobello.

It is surrounded by a number of famous Trulli stone houses. The restaurant serves fresh Apulian cuisine made with local organic products.

Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas. Guests can visit vineyards other than enjoy walks along the property's countryside paths.

The property offers free private parking on site, and the coast and Monopoli are a 20-minute drive.
