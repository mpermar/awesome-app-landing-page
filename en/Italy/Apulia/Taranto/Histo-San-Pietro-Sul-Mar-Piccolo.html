Description

In a small village, surrounded by centuries-old olive trees, the Histò San Pietro features an attractive, striking design. The hotel features its own basilica, which is available for special events and business engagements.

All rooms have stone floors and walls, some with vaulted ceilings. Each room includes air conditioning, private bathroom, and satellite TV.

The lounge rooms and cafe are all in striking white stone.
