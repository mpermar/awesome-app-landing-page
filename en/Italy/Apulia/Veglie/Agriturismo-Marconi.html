Description

Surrounded by a communal garden, Agriturismo Marconi offers rustic-style accommodation in Veglie.

A breakfast with sweet items is served daily. Rooms are equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and safety deposit box.

The private bathroom includes a shower, free toiletries and hairdryer. Agriturismo Marconi is 12 km from Porto Cesareo and the coastline.
