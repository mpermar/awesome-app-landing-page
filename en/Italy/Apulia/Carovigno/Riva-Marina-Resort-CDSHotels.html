Description

Set 900 metres from its private beach, the Riva Marina Resort is surrounded by the Torre Guaceto nature reserve.

This property offers free parking, a wellness centre, and 2 swimming pools. All rooms at the Riva Marina feature an LCD TV, air conditioning, and free Wi-Fi.

Each room offers a hydromassage shower, and a private balcony or terrace. Riva Marina Resort offers plenty of sports opportunities, including tennis, basketball and football.

The wellness centre is complete with sauna and Turkish bath. There is a free shuttle service to the resort's private beach with windsurfing, canoeing, and sailing facilities.

Services for children and teenagers include playgrounds, video games, a theatre and hip hop dance school.

A table is reserved specially for each room in the restaurant each evening. The amphitheatre hosts cabaret evenings.

During Summer, the hotel may only be bookable for weekly stays, from Sunday to Sunday.
