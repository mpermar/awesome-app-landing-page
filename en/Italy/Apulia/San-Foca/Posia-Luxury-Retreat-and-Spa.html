Description

Posia Luxury Retreat & Spa offers a garden, three swimming pools and a fitness centre in San Foca.

Among the facilities of this property are also a restaurant, a bar, a 24-hour front desk and room service, along with free WiFi.

The hotel has newspapers, a fax machine and photocopier and an ATM machine that guests can use.

All guest rooms come with air conditioning, a desk, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fridge and a kettle.

The private bathroom includes a bidet and a hairdryer. A buffet breakfast with continental options is available every morning at Posia Luxury Retreat & Spa.

Wellness facilities at the property include a free outdoor pool, a rooftop infinity pool available upon reservation and at extra charge, and an indoor pool.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike hire is available at the accommodation.

Lecce is 27 km from Posia Luxury Retreat & Spa, while Otranto is 22 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Brindisi - Salento, 71 km from the hotel, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
