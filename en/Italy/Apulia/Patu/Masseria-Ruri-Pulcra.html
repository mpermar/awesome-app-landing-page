Description

Set in a 17th-century farmhouse, Masseria Ruri Pulcra offers air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi.

It features a garden, restaurant and sun terrace, and Patù is a 5-minute drive away.

Rooms at the Ruri Pulcra all feature a flat-screen TV, minibar and a desk. The private bathroom includes free toiletries.

A sweet and savoury continental breakfast is served daily in the restaurant hall.

The restaurant specialises in local seafood and meat dishes. In July and August, a free aperitif is provided in the evenings.

The property is offers free private parking on site, and is 700 metres from the rocky beaches at San Gregorio.
