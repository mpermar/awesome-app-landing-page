Description

Guest rooms at the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV and a kitchen. Rooms come complete with a private bathroom equipped with a bidet and free toiletries, while certain rooms at Ferrocino Resort also provide guests with a seating area. The rooms at the accommodation include air conditioning and a closet.

Ferrocino Resort offers a buffet or Italian breakfast.

The resort has a terrace.

Speaking Spanish, French and German at the 24-hour front desk, staff are always on hand to help.

Lecce is 17 miles from Ferrocino Resort, while Gallipoli is 7 miles from the property. Casale Airport is 37 miles away.
