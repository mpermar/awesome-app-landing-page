Description

Set right on the seafront in Villanova, Residence Club Barbara is just 150 metres from a public sandy beach.

It features 2 swimming pools, sports facilities and a mini club for children. At Barbara Residence Club you have a choice of rooms and apartments.

All come with a TV and a balcony or patio with a sea view. The apartments also feature a fully equipped kitchenette.

You will find a bar and a gazebo where breakfast is available each morning. There are pools for adult and children, plus a tennis court, volleyball pitch and bowling green.

The garden includes table tennis, a children's playground and sun loungers and deckchairs around the pools.

Professional entertainment staff organise a children's club and activities. The reception desk is open from 8:00 to 22:30.

Bikes are available to rent and there is a coin-operated laundry room on site. The residence is within easy reach of services such as restaurants, bars, shops, a fishmongers and pharmacy.
