Description

Masseria Tutosa is set in a 17th-century farmhouse, overlooking the blue Adriatic and the green Murgia Plains.

It offers an outdoor pool surrounded by trees, and parking is free. Hotel Masseria Tutosa is set in extensive grounds with ancient olive groves.

It offers guest rooms and self-catering apartments with free WiFi. In the summer breakfast is served outdoors.

The Masseria also has a church and an underground oil press on site. It is 4 km from the sea and 7 km from Ostuni.

A cycling trail along Via Traiana is located next to the property, and bicycles can be rented on site.
