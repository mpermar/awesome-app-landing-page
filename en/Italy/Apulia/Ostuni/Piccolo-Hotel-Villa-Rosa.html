Description

Villa Rosa is set on a hill filled with olive trees, just 1 km from the centre of Ostuni, and overlooking the Itria Valley.

This intimate hotel features a furnished garden, and functional, en suite rooms with free Wi-Fi.

The rooms of Piccolo Hotel Villa Rosa come with cool tiled floors, air conditioning, and a TV.

The bathrooms are complete with hairdryer. A generous breakfast buffet is served daily in the veranda with views of the hills.

In the garden you will find many relaxation areas where you can admire the panorama.

The hotel offers free guarded parking, and is just a 20-minute drive from the seaside.
