Description

Start your day at Albergo Villa Vittoria with a rich buffet breakfast on the sunny terrace. Take a walk to the sea or to the picturesque center of Rodi Garganico, both only 1,000 feet away.

Your room at Villa Vittoria is comfortable and modern. Most have a sea-view balcony or patio. In the evening, relax with a drink at the bar and enjoy the peace and quiet. Enjoy regional cuisine in the elegant restaurant.
