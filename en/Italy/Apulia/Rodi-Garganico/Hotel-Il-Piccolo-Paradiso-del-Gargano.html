Description

Hotel Il Piccolo Paradiso in Gargano has a restaurant, seasonal outdoor swimming pool, bar and shared lounge in Rodi Garganico.

With a garden, the 3-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom.

The accommodation features entertainment staff and a 24-hour front desk. At the hotel, rooms come with a desk and a flat-screen TV.

The rooms have a wardrobe. Hotel Il Piccolo Paradiso del Gargano offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

The accommodation offers a children's playground. Guests at Hotel Il Piccolo Paradiso del Gargano will be able to enjoy activities in and around Rodi Garganico, like cycling.

San Giovanni Rotondo is 49 km from the hotel, while Vieste is 37 km from the property.
