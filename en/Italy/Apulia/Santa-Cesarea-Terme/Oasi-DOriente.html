Description

The Oasi D'Oriente is exactly what its name suggests: a peaceful oasis in Italy's eastern coast, on the sunny shores of Apulia.

You can choose from different kinds of accommodation. The Oasi D'Oriente offers comfortable studios, and spacious apartments for up to 6 guests.

You will surely find the ideal solution for a memorable stay. Most of the apartments offer views of the sea.

The rate comprises the Club Card, which offers free use of the establishment's entertaining facilities.

The Oasi D'Oriente also features a panoramic terrace. Relaxation is guaranteed in the spacious outdoor pool.
