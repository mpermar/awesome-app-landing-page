Description

All guest rooms in the resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV. The rooms come with a private bathroom and a bath or shower, while some rooms also offer a balcony. Guest rooms include a desk.

A continental, buffet or à la carte breakfast is available each morning at the property. The restaurant at La Petricor serves Italian cuisine.

The accommodation has a seasonal outdoor pool. The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental is available at La Petricor.

Fiera del Levante is 4.2 miles from the resort, while San Nicola Stadium is 1.7 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Karol Wojtyla Airport, 7 miles from La Petricor.
