Description

Masseria Agrituristica Lama S. Giorgio is a rustic farmhouse from the 19th century, set just outside the town of Rutigliano.

It features a good restaurant that uses home-grown produce. The property has been completely renovated with respect to its original design.

You will find a collection of period furniture, and elegant air-conditioned rooms with a private bathroom.

A generous buffet breakfast is served daily from 08:00 until 10:00. At Lama S. Giorgio you can admire the peaceful countryside of Puglia.
