Description

Hotel Ristorante La Masseria is a 3-star property surrounded by the Salento countryside, in the small town of Cocumola and 5 km from the seaside resort of Santa Cesarea Terme.

Parking is free. Rooms at La Masseria hotel and restaurant are all air conditioned and come with a TV, desk and tiled floors.

The bathrooms are complete with hairdryers. You have free Wi-Fi access in public areas.

The restaurant serves à la carte menus of classic Italian cuisine and Salento specialities.

All meals are prepared with organic products. The hotel is a 15-minute drive from the famous seaside resort of Otranto, while Lecce can be reached by car in 35 minutes.
