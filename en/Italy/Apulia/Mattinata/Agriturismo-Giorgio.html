Description

Set at the foot of Monte Saraceno, Agriturismo Giorgio is surrounded by olive groves and is just 2 km from the beach in Porto Mattinata, easily reached by free shuttle.

It features an outdoor pool. The Giorgio Agriturismo produces its own extra-virgin olive oil.

Its restaurant is open for lunch and dinner. Daily breakfast includes coffee and a croissant.

During summer it is served outdoors. The apartments are located around 100 metres from the main farmhouse.

Each one includes a kitchenette and free parking. They also feature a patio, air conditioning and private bathroom with shower.

Guests can relax in the garden with olive trees or take one of the free bikes for a ride.
