Description

Just 200 metres from its own private beach, Villaggio San Matteo offers both rooms and apartments.

Offering a swimming pool and summer entertainment, it is a 5-minute drive from Mattinata.

All accommodation is air conditioned and features satellite TV and a private bathroom.

The apartments include a fully equipped kitchenette. Breakfast is available on request.

The two restaurants, 1 on the beach and 1 inside the property, offer local dishes at lunch.

Entertainment for children and adults is organised in summer. San Matteo Villaggio is 15 km along the coast from Manfredonia.

It is 40 minutes' drive from Vieste port and an hour's drive from Foggia. Free parking is provided.
