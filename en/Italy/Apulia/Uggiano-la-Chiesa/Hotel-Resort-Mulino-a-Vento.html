Description

Located in the Salento countryside, Mulino A Vento is a 4-star hotel set in Uggiano La Chiesa, 6 km from Otranto and 5 minutes' drive from Porto Badisco beach.

It offers independent accommodation, an outdoor pool, and free parking. Each room is on the ground floor, and all rooms have a patio.

Accommodation is air conditioned, spacious, bright and simply furnished. Free Wi-Fi is available in all areas.

Mulino A Vento has plenty of space to enjoy, as well as a large pool with sun deck and parasols where guest can relax and enjoy a deserved break.

The hotel offers a covered veranda where you can enjoy your meals outdoors. The restaurant serves regional cuisine and the extensive breakfast includes homemade cakes.

Guests can rent bikes at reception. The staff can also organise tours and boat trips.
