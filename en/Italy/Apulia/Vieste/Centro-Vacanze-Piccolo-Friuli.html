Description

Offering a summer swimming pool and tennis court, Piccolo Friuli also provides a free shuttle to partner beaches in Molinella.

It is just 5 minutes' drive from the harbour in Vieste. The apartments come with full cooking facilities and covered patios overlooking the sea or swimming pool.

Drinks, snacks and hamburgers are available from the bar and there is a BBQ in the garden.

Piccolo Friuli organises entertainment in July and August. It has karaoke, darts and table tennis.

WiFi is free at reception. Free parking is provided and buses to Calenella Train Station stop right outside the property.
