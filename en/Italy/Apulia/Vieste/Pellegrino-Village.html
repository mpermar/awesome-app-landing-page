Description

Set just opposite a sandy beach and offering a seasonal outdoor pool, the Pellegrino Village is a 5-minute drive from Vieste.

Vieste Harbour is 2. 3 km from the property which provides free private parking and a garden.

The pet-friendly accommodation is air conditioned and features a seating area. There is also a dining area and a kitchenette fitted with a refrigerator.

There is a private bathroom with a bidet and shower in each unit. The holiday park features free WiFi.

The Pellegrino also includes a barbecue. Guests can enjoy a drink at the on-site bar, and in Summer evening entertainment is available.

Spiaggia di Scialmarino is 6 km from the Pellegrino Village, while the long, sandy Pizzomunno Beach is right opposite the property.
