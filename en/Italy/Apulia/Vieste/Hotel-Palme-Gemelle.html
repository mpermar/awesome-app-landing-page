Description

Set in the Gargano region, 700 metres from the beach, the Palme Gemelle offers a restaurant, a summer outdoor pool with sun loungers and parasols, and numerous sports facilities.

Entertainment is provided in summer and parking is free. Wi-Fi is free throughout.

Air-conditioned, spacious rooms at the Hotel Palme Gemelle come with satellite TV, a small fridge, and a large balcony.

Some have a kitchenette. The restaurant is located by the pool and serves classic Italian cuisine and local specialities.

Special menus are available on request. The garden is complete with BBQ facilities, a playground, and a gazebo with tables and chairs.

Sports facilities include a tennis, a grass volleyball court, and 5-a-side football pitch.
