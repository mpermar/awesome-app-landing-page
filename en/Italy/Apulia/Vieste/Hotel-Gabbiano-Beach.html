Description

Offering a sea-view swimming pool and direct access to both private and public beaches, Gabbiano Beach is within the Gargano National Park, 7 km along the coast from Vieste centre.

Rooms at the Gabbiano have an elegant design and are all air conditioned. They also feature satellite TV, a fridge and a balcony.

The restaurant serves Italian cuisine with specialities from the Puglia region. Guests will also find a tennis court, gym and children's playground.

Gabbiano Beach is surrounded by a large floral garden with palm trees and Mediterranean plants.

Staff organise a number of day trips, including to the nearby Tremiti Islands, the pilgrimage site of San Giovanni Rotondo and treks through the Gargano National Park.
