Description

Featuring an outdoor pool and a free tennis court, Village Baia Turchese is located in Vieste on the Gargano Peninsula, 200 metres from a private beach.

It offers air-conditioned rooms with garden views and free WiFi throughout the property.

The rooms at the Turchese come with a fridge and LCD TV. The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries.

A parking space close to each room is included. A buffet breakfast is provided at the resort.

Guests can enjoy special rates at nearby restaurants. Sports lovers will appreciate the free basketball courts, plus the 5-a-side football pitch.

The outdoor pool has a hydromassage area, and entertainment for both children and adults is provided.

The Baia Turchese Resort is 850 metres from the centre of Vieste. Padre Pio’s home in San Giovanni Rotondo is a 1-hour drive away.
