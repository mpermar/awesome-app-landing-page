Description

Gattarella Hotel Residence offers a private beach in a secluded location, 3.7 mi from Vieste. Located in the Gargano National Park, it has a relaxing pool and many sports facilities.

Accommodations at the Gattarella Hotel is set in chalets among Mediterranean gardens. All rooms and apartments have a private bathroom, and some feature Adriatic Sea views.

The Gattarella's staff provide entertainment for children and adults. Guests can enjoy tennis and windsurfing, or a good workout in the gym.

The bar has a panoramic view over the beach. The restaurant is open for lunch and dinner and has a spacious dining hall.

San Giovanni Rotondo, the city of Padre Pio, is a 1-hour drive away. Ferries for the Tremiti Islands leave from Vieste Harbor.
