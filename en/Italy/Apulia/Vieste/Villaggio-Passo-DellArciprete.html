Description

With panoramic views across Vieste and the Adriatic Sea, Villaggio Passo Dell'Arciprete offers a swimming pool, a separate sun terrace, and self-catering apartments with private terraces.

WiFi is free throughout. Each apartment at Passo Dell'Arciprete has a private entrance and a free parking space.

They all feature satellite TV, a lounge and fully equipped kitchenette. Outside you will find a barbecue area with tables, chairs and a wood-burning oven where you can cook pizzas.

Guests have a free private beach area 1. 5 km from Villaggio Passo, reached by free shuttle bus.

Cala Molinella beach is 900 metres from the property, while Vieste centre is 5 minutes away by car.

At Passo Dell'Arciprete Village you will also find ping pong and a tennis court. The peaceful garden has a children's playground and barbecue.

Vieste port is less than 10 minutes' drive away for boats to the Tremiti Islands and Croatia.
