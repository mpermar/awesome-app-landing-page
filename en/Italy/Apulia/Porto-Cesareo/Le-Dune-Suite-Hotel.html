Description

Set directly opposite Porto Cesareo’s Le Dune Beach, the Suite Hotel features a spa, a sun terrace with hydromassage swimming pool, and 2 restaurants.

Its air-conditioned rooms have a balcony, design furniture, and an LCD TV. Wi-Fi is free.

The rooms of Le Dune Suite Hotel are decorated in a minimalist style, with tiled floors, a desk and a minibar.

Some also offer views of the shared garden or partial sea views. The restaurant specialises in local cuisine, including special vegetarian and gluten-free menus.

A breakfast buffet is available every morning. Meals are served in a bright dining hall.

The bar serves aperitifs and cocktails. The Private Spa includes a sauna, a hot tub, and a relaxation area.
