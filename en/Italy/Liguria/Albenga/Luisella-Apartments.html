Description

Offering modern apartments with free WiFi and air conditioning, Luisella Apartments is just 850 metres from the sandy beach in Albenga.

It features free bike rental, and free private parking. With a private garden or a large terrace, each apartment at Luisella Apartments boasts an al-fresco dining area.

They all come with an LCD TV and a modern fully equipped kitchen. Bath towels and bed linen are included.

This family-run property is at the heart of Albenga, a popular seaside town on the Italian Riviera.

You can walk to the historical centre in less than 10 minutes. Staff is happy to suggest restaurants, beaches and attractions in the surroundings.
