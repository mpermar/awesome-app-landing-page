Description

Featuring 2 pools, a free gym, and a restaurant, La Pineta offers rooms, mobile homes and apartments 6 km from Albenga and 5 km from its private beach.

Here you will find a patio, a playground, and a sun terrace. Spacious, air-conditioned rooms at the family-run Pineta include a balcony, a sofa/armchair, and a refrigerator.

Mobile homes and apartments come with a patio, kitchenette and dining area. A coin-operated laundry service is available.

The restaurant serves traditional homemade cuisine from the Liguria region and is open daily for both lunch and dinner.

A bar is available, open until midnight. Entertainment is provided in summer, and guests can also enjoy a game of tennis or relax in the on-site sauna.

The sun terrace is equipped with parasols, tables and chairs. A shuttle bus links with the private beach, where parasols and sun beds are available.

A bus to Albenga centre and Cisano stops 300 metres from the property, and the A10 motorway is 5 minutes' drive away.
