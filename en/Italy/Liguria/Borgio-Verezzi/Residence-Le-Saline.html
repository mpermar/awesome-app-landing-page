Description

This residence is located only 50 metres away from the beach. It features well equipped apartments, an outdoor swimming pool, and a private car park.

Residence Le Saline offers air-conditioned apartments with tiled floors. They come with a bathroom with shower, a kitchenette, and satellite TV.

The residence features a fitness centre, a sauna and a Turkish bath. There is an internet point with WiFi connection available in the lobby.

The property's private beach is available at extra charge. This property offers a central location in Borgio Verezzi.

There are several restaurants located nearby, and the city’s train station is only a 2-minute walk away from the residence.
