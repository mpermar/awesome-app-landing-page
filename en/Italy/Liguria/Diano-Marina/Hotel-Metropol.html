Description

Offering 2 outdoor pools, a private beach and free parking, Hotel Metropol is surrounded by a garden with flowers and olive groves, on a hill overlooking the Gulf of Diano Marina and Cervo.

Rooms at the Metropol Hotel have air conditioning and a satellite TV. Most come with a balcony and/or sea views.

Guests enjoy free access to the beach located 250 metres away, where they will find 1 parasol and 2 sun loungers, plus other beach services.

WiFi and access to the pool are free. The Plumbago restaurant serves Ligurian and national specialities accompanied by quality wines.

The Metropol also offers a bar, featuring a terrace overlooking the Gulf of Diano.

You can relax by the pool, while your children swim in the smaller dedicated pool, or simply borrow 1 of the free property’s bikes.

Cervo city centre is a 10-minute drive from the Hotel Metropol. Diano Marina train station is 1.

5 km away. You can walk to the hotel's private beach in just 5 minutes or take the hotel's free shuttle bus.
