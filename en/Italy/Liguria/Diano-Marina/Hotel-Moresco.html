Description

Surrounded by palm trees and olive groves, Hotel Moresco has its own swimming pool and private beach.

It has a quiet location in Diano Marina, overlooking the Ligurian Sea. All air-conditioned, rooms here come with a balcony or terrace with sea view.

They also feature satellite TV and a private bathroom with hairdryer. Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas and in some rooms.

An Italian breakfast is served each morning, while the chef prepares Ligurian specialities at dinner.

Your meal is accompanied by a wide range of Italian and international wines. The Moresco Hotel has plenty of sun terraces and space in the garden to relax in.

It is just 300 metres from the beach and 3. 5 km from Diano Marina Station for trains along the coast to Genoa and Ventimiglia.
