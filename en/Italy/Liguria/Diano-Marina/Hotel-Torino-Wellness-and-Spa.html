Description

Hotel Torino Wellness & Spa is in Diano Marina's town centre, near the pedestrian shopping area.

It offers a panoramic rooftop restaurant, a wellness centre and free access to its private beach, 150 metres away.

Bikes are provided free at the Torino and the hotel has its own pool with hydromassage area.

There is also a free gym on site and you can sunbathe on the terrace. A sea-view hot tub on the rooftop sun terrace is available.

Each air-conditioned room features free WiFi and an ensuite bathroom with free toiletries, come have a balcony.

It includes a parasol and deckchairs at the private beach. At an extra charge, guests can access the wellness centre, which includes a finnish sauna and turkish bath with chromotherapy and aromatherapy.

You can also enjoy the relaxation area, solarium and a variety of treatments. Torino Hotel's rooms are bright and offer free Wi-Fi access.

The poolside restaurant is open for lunch and dinner, and serves great Italian dishes.
