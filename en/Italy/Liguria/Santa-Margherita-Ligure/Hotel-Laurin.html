Description

The 4-star Hotel Laurin offers a large terrace with pool and panoramic views of the Ligurian Sea.

It is located directly on the sea, in Santa Margherita Ligure. Rooms, located on the first, second and third floor, feature an elegant atmosphere thanks to wooden furniture and mostly wooden floors.

Each one comes with air conditioning, a minibar and satellite TV. Some have a balcony overlooking the sea.

Hotel Laurin features a well equipped fitness centre. Other services include a 24-hour reception and free Wi-Fi access throughout.

At breakfast, you can enjoy a varied buffet that includes homemade cakes and local pastries.

The hotel’s Da Alfredo restaurant specialises in fresh fish and Ligurian dishes. This hotel is a 10-minute walk from Santa Maria Ligure Train Station.
