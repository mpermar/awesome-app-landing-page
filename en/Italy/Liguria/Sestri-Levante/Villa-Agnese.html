Description

Villa Agnese has a peaceful location surrounded by vineyards, 3 km from Sestri Levante city centre and from the beach.

It offers a sun terrace with swimming pool and loungers, and free parking with CCTV video surveillance.

Rooms at Villa Agnese are bright and large, and feature LCD TV and free internet.

Some rooms offer a balcony or a private garden. A standard room suitable for disabled guests is available on request.

Villa Agnese uses geothermal energy for air conditioning and photovoltaic energy for electricity.

Breakfast includes organic products and gluten-free food and is served in an elegant dining hall, overlooking the gardens and swimming pool.

Guests can have access to free bikes on request. The Ligurian Coast, the Cinque Terre and the Tigullio Gulf areas are just a short drive away on the nearby A12 Motorway.
