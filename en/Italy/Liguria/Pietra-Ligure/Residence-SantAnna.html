Description

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool and a free private beach area, Residence Sant’Anna offers self-catering apartments in Pietra Ligure, a 10-minute walk from the beachfront.

The property offers a hydro-massage pool, a small football field, and a free bus shuttle to the town centre.

Free WiFi is available throughout. A bike-washing and bike-repairing area are also available on site.

With a sea-view balcony, each apartment is equipped with a satellite flat-screen TV and a kitchenette with a microwave and a refrigerator.

The private bathroom comes with shower or bath tub. The property features table tennis and a games room.

A bar is also available on site. Guests can use barbecue facilities and a playground for children.

Free private parking is possible on site. Grotte di Toirano Caves are a 20-minute drive from Sant’Anna Residence, while the Pietra Ligure A10 Motorway Exit is 2.
