Description

Residence Holidays is family-managed and is situated in a tranquil area with Mediterranean plants.

It offers a sun terrace with outdoor swimming pool. Residence Holidays features apartments with kitchen, and a balcony with deck chairs and table.

Accommodation includes satellite TV, and some apartments are also air-conditioned.

Holidays Residence is in Pietra Ligure, a short drive from the Ligurian seaside. Parking is free.
