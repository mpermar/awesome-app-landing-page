Description

Located in the historic centre of Varazze, Hotel Spot Varazze offers modern accommodation with air-conditioning.

The property features a terrace and a garden. Rooms here come with free Wi-Fi and a flat-screen TV.

The private bathroom includes free toiletries. A typical Italian breakfast is served daily, together with focaccia bread.

There is also a bar and a restaurant serving local cuisine. Hotel Spot Varazze is 100 metres from the beach, while Varazze Train Station is 1 km away.
