Description

Set in the mountains overlooking Monterosso, the Cuccaro Club offers 2 swimming pools, a rooftop terrace, a children's playground and a quiet environment in Rocchetta di Vara.

The family-run Cuccaro Club features an on-site restaurant using only fresh ingredients like organic beef and local farm produce.

Savour typical Ligurian meals, homemade pasta and fine wines. At 900 metres above sea level, Cuccaro Club is within easy reach of the famous beaches of Cinque Terre.

Go trekking, mountain biking or walking on the many nature paths. In Cuccaro Club's garden you will find a outdoor swimming pool, a football field, and table tennis.

The indoor pool has salt water. Guests can also relax in the wellness area with sauna, steam bath and hot tub.

Choose from cosy single, double, triple and quadruple rooms with en suite bathrooms.
