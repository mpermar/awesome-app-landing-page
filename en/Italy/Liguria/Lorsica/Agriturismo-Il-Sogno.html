Description

Agriturismo Il Sogno is set in a peaceful mountain area in Lorsica and features a restaurant serving local cuisine.

The property has a small farm with animals in the garden, where vegetables are also grown.

Rooms have mountain views and come with free Wi-Fi and a TV. The private bathroom has a shower, free toiletries and hairdryer.

A sweet breakfast is served daily. Savoury dishes are available upon request. Agriturismo Il Sogno is 18 km from the start of Aveto Natural Park.
