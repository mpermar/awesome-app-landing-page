Description

The secluded Castellaro Golf Resort is set in the hills just 9 km from San Remo and 5 km from the Ligurian Sea.

It features spacious accommodation, a pool, and sports and wellness facilities. Rooms at the peaceful Castellaro offer a minibar, LCD TV with SKY satellite channels and a balcony or terrace.

Some have sea views. Apartments and studios also include a fully equipped kitchenette.

The Castellaro Hotel’s Andrea Doria restaurant serves Ligurian cuisine, including locally caught fish.

Guests can also enjoy light meals overlooking the 9-hole golf course at La Buca di Bacco wine bar.

Tennis, football and golf are available for guests’ fitness at the Castellaro Golf Resort.

You will also find a relaxation area. The hills around Pompeiana provide the ideal landscape for hiking and horse riding.
