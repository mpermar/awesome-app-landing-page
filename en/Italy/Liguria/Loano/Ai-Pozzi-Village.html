Description

Boasting a large pool with sun terrace, an international restaurant and wellness centre, Ai Pozzi Village lies 1.

5 km from the Ligurian Sea. It features air-conditioned rooms with free internet connection and a balcony.

Surrounded by a vast garden, the Ai Pozzi includes a tennis court, billiards, a mini market and playground.

A sauna, Turkish bath, indoor pool and hot tub are available at the wellness centre.

Fully soundproofed, guest rooms come with a safe, a 32" LCD TV with satellite channels, and a private bathroom with hairdryer.

Guests enjoy a sweet and savoury buffet breakfast every morning. The Village is 1.

5 km from its own private beach, the Bagni Serena. Loano centre and Train Station are a 15-minute walk away.
