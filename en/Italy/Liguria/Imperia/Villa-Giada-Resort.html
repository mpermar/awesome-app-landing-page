Description

Featuring 2 heated swimming pools and a private olive grove, Villa Giada Resort offers modern accommodation with panoramic views of the sea and Imperia.

The property is about 3 km from the beaches. The apartments come with air conditioning, a fully equipped kitchenette and an outdoor dining area.

The private bathroom includes a shower and free toiletries. There is a bar and a restaurant/pizzeria serving typical Liguria cuisine.

WiFi is available in public areas. On-site facilities also include bike rental, bike storage and a bike-repair point, and other cycling-related services.

Guests can also book the access to a private beach area. Villa Giada Resort is 5 km from Imperia centre, while Sanremo is a 30-minute drive away.
