Description

Formerly a residential home and later a holiday home for the Swiss clergy, Villa San Giuseppe is now a 3-star hotel set in a beautiful park with flowers, palm and citrus trees.

Diano Marina Train Station and Cervo city centre are both 3 km away. Hotel Villa San Giuseppe is comprised of a main villa surrounded by 3 annex buildings.

All rooms are en suite, simply furnished, and most offer views of the garden. Some rooms are complete with air conditioning.

Breakfast includes sweet and savoury food and is served in the restaurant's dining room.

For lunch and dinner you can enjoy Ligurian cuisine, including children's option and gluten-free menus.

A large private area overlooking the sea is equipped with beach-volley court, bowls field, showers and changing cabins.

During summer you have direct access to the sandy beach, where sunbeds and parasols are available to rent There is a church on site, where mass is celebrated every Sunday.
