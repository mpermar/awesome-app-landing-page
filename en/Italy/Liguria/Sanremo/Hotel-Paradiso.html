Description

Just 350 metres from its own private beach and a few steps from Sanremo’s famous Casino, this hotel offers spectacular panoramic views of the sea.

No wonder it is called Paradiso. The Paradiso was built in 1926, and has been run by the same family ever since.

Franco Gaiani continues the family tradition providing wonderful hospitality. At the Paradiso you will enjoy a variety of regional cuisine, a 600 m2 garden, parking for up to 30 cars and use of the large swimming-pool during the summer.

A rich buffet breakfast and a selection of Mediterranean cuisine is available daily.

The hotel's American bar serves drinks on the terrace, around the swimming pool, throughout the day.

The dining rooms overlooking the garden are ideal for ceremonies and business lunches.

On request, the hotel provides a shuttle service from the Nice Côte d'Azur Airport at an additional cost.
