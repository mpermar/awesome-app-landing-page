Description

Residence Dei Due Porti is located in Sanremo city centre, and offers a sun terrace with views of the Ligurian Sea.

You will enjoy discounts at a sandy private beach right in front of the property.

Dei Due Porti Residence offers studios and apartments, some with sea views. Each unit comes with free WiFi, air conditioning and a satellite TV.

Bed linen, towels and daily cleaning are included. The property offers free bikes for you to explore the city.

A cycling trail along the coast starts right on the doorstep. A convenient supermarket is located only 100 metres away.

Children enjoy a dedicated terrace with playground and soft flooring. Staff will be happy to offer suggestions and assistance during your stay.
