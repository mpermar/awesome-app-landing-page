Description

Set in an Art Nouveau-style villa, Lolli Palace Hotel is surrounded by huge palm trees on the famous Promenade Imperatrice in Sanremo.

It offers free WiFi. Positioned on the famous Italian Flower Riviera, this 3-star hotel is just 50 metres from the sea.

Near the Sanremo Casino, main shopping area and historic centre, Lolli Palace Hotel offers panoramic views of the coast from both its spacious rooms and rooftop terrace equipped with sun loungers and sun umbrellas.

Rooms feature parquet floors and a satellite LCD TV. Some have a balcony and sea views.

A restaurant serving local products and Ligurian specialities is available on site.

A buffet breakfast is also prepared daily. From Lolli Palace you will be within easy reach of major attractions.

Next door you will find the winter residence of the former Russian Empress Maria Alexandrovna and, nearby, the Russian Orthodox Church.

Montecarlo and the French Riviera are just 30 km away. Enjoy modern amenities and the hotel’s excellent location in this beautiful city, known for its year-round mild climate.
