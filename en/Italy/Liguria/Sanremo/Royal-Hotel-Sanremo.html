Description

From June to September guests have free entrance, changing huts, lounge chairs and sun umbrellas at a partner beach across from the hotel. Guests can relax year-round in the spa center with sauna, hot tub and Turkish bath. There is also a gym and tennis court for an active holiday.

Rooms at the Royal Hotel combine antique furniture with modern amenities such as air conditioning, satellite TV and a minibar. All rooms feature a marble bathroom with a bathrobe and slippers and some have a sea view.

A buffet breakfast is served each morning and Fiori di Murano restaurant serves fine Italian cuisine and park and sea views. During summer, you also have the Corallina poolside bar and restaurant, plus a gourmet restaurant, Il Giardino, for a candlelight dinner on the terrace.
