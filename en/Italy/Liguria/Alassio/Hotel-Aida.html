Description

Just a 3-minute walk from the beaches of Alassio, Hotel Aida offers classic accommodation with a garden.

Featuring free bike rental, the property is 500 metres from Alassio Train Station.

Guest rooms come with a flat-screen TV, while their private bathrooms have a bath or shower.

A cooling fan is available upon request. A continental breakfast buffet is served daily.

There is also a bar and a restaurant serving Ligurian cuisine as well as international dishes.

Free WiFi is available in public areas. Hotel Aida is 10 km from Albenga, while the Toirano Caves are a 30-minute drive away.
