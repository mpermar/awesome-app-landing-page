Description

Located in the centre of Alassio, 100 metres from the beach, Hotel Corso offers apartments and bright and elegant rooms with free Wi-Fi and satellite TV.

There are a few bikes available for free hire. All rooms at the family-run Corso Hotel have a balcony overlooking the internal courtyard or the street.

They are equipped with air conditioning and a spacious bathroom. The Corso's restaurant serves regional specialities of meat and fish, and a wide variety of homemade desserts.

A buffet breakfast of pastries, fresh fruit and meats is available each day. A number of services for children are available at the Corso Hotel including babysitting, cartoon channels on the TV and even special menus in the restaurant.

Set on the Ligurian coast, the hotel is 50 km from the popular resort of Sanremo, and historic Genova is around an hour's drive away.
