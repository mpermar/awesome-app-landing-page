Description

Offering an outdoor heated pool (at 28 degrees with hydromassage area, cervical waterfall and solarium), restaurant and bar with outdoor terrace, Hotel Adler is just 350 metres from the sandy beach in Alassio.

Guests enjoy free WiFi within the property. Rooms at the Adler Hotel feature a flat-screen TV, safe and air conditioning.

Each has a private bathroom with hairdryer and free toiletries. A sweet and savoury buffet breakfast is served every morning, with a selection of croissants, cold cuts, drinks and more.

Traditional specialities can be tasted at the hotel's restaurant. The hotel is close to Alassio centre, where guests can find restaurants, shops and cafés.
