Description

Set in a lush park a 5-minute drive from Alassio and 200 metres from the beach, Baba Recidence offers apartments with balcony or terrace.

There is free parking, a sun terrace and a restaurant. Boasting views of the Ligurian Sea, apartments offer air conditioning, a satellite flat screen TV and comfortable mattresses.

The kitchenette comes with electric stove and microwave, while the bathroom is complete with a large shower and toilet.

In summer, an à la carte restaurant serving Mediterranean cuisine and pizza is open every day.

A bar and mini market are also open all summer. You can enjoy free basketball and volleyball courts, or book the tennis court at an extra cost.

Both the partner beach area and a free pet-friendly public beach are within 100 metres of the property.

You are advised to bring your own vehicle. Alternatively, a bus stopping nearby provides links to both towns.
