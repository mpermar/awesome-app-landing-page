Description

Set 200 metres above sea level in Moneglia, Hotel La Vigna features an outdoor swimming pool, a garden, and on-site dining.

Free WiFi and air conditioning are available throughout. All guest rooms feature a flat-screen TV, a minibar and a tea/coffee maker.

The private bathroom includes a hairdryer, free toiletries, bathrobes and slippers.

All rooms come with a balcony and some units offer sea views. A buffet breakfast can be enjoyed daily at the property.

Open for lunch and dinner, the on-site restaurant specialises in Italian cuisine.

La Vigna Hotel is a 10-minute drive from Moneglia Train Station, from which you can reach the UNESCO Cinque Terre National Park in 30 minutes.
