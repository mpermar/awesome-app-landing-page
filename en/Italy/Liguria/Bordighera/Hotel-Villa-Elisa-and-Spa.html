Description

Villa Elisa is 500 metres from the centre of Bordighera and offers an outdoor swimming pool, garden sauna and wellness centre with steam bath, hot tub and massage room.

Rooms feature free Wi-Fi, minibar, satellite TV, and air conditioning. The garden at Villa Elisa Hotel features typical plants and lemon trees, and includes a children's playground.

L'Aranceto restaurant serves Mediterranean cuisine including fresh-fish dishes. The breakfast buffet is served in the restaurant or in the garden, during summer.

Friendly staff at Hotel Villa Elisa can organise transfers to the French Riviera and Nice Côte d'Azur International Airport, hiking tours, and mountain-bike excursions.
