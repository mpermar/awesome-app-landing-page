Description

Located in Bordighera, Hotel Lucciola features a bar, shared lounge, garden, and free WiFi throughout the property.

Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a terrace. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk and room service for guests.

Guest rooms are equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kettle, a shower, a hairdryer and a desk.

At the hotel each room is equipped with a wardrobe and a private bathroom. Hotel Lucciola offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

Nice is 45 km from the accommodation, while Menton is 16 km away. The nearest airport is Cuneo International Airport, 116 km from Hotel Lucciola.
