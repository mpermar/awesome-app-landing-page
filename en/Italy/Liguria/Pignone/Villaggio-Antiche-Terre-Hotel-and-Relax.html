Description

Set a 20-minute drive from Cinque Terre National Park, Villaggio Antiche Terre Hotel & Relax offers an outdoor swimming pool and free parking in the quiet village of Faggiona di Pignone.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. Villaggio Antiche Terre’s rooms are decorated in a simple style, have private bathrooms and a TV.

Some have a balcony with views over the countryside. The restaurant serves traditional regional dishes which can be enjoyed out on the sunny dining terrace.

Other facilities include a children’s playground and a tennis court. On site there is a dedicated area to let your dog run freely.

Monterosso al Mare is a 30-minute drive from Hotel & Relax Antiche Terre Village.
