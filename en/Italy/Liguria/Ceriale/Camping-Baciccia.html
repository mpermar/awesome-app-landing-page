Description

Offering a free outdoor pool and free WiFi, Camping Baciccia is a 10-minute walk from its own private beach in Ceriale.

It features a restaurant, snack bar and mini market. All units include a kitchenette, an outdoor dining area and a bathroom with shower.

Some also feature air conditioning and a living room with sofa. On-site facilities at the Baciccia Camping also include a tennis court, table tennis and a children’s playground.

Bikes can be rented on site. Ceriale Train Station is 2. 5 km from the camping. Albenga is a 10-minute drive away.
