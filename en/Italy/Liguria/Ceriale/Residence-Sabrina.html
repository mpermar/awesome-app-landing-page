Description

Set a few steps from the beach, Residence Sabrina offers free Wi-Fi throughout, a free shuttle service to/from Albenga Train Station, and a spacious terrace with tables and chairs.

The property is 400 metres from the beach Spiaggia Bagni Lido and has special rates to access.

You can ask reception for discounts to the Genoa Aquarium, Grotte di Toirano caves, and Parco Acquatico le Caravelle Water Park are provided.

There are 2 supermarkets available less than a 5-minute walk from property. A laundry room is available on site.
