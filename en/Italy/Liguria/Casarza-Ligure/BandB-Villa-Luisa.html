Description

Rooms at this small and cozy B&B are all air conditioned and come with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with hairdryer. Guests also have use of a shared kitchen with fridge, microwave and tea and coffee-making facilities.

Breakfast is buffet style and includes Ligurian specialties. Villa Luisa is eco-friendly and uses solar power. Guests have free parking and free Wi-Fi access throughout the property.

Villa Luisa B&B is 3 miles from the middle of Riva Trigoso. It is easily reached from highway A12 and is a short drive or train ride from the beautiful Cinque Terre.
