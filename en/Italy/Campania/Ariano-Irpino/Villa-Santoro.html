Description

Each unit comes with a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge, a seating area with a sofa, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with bidet and a hair dryer. A patio with mountain views is offered in every unit.

A à la carte or Italian breakfast is available each morning at the property.

The vacation home has a playground.

Guests can also relax in the garden or on the sun terrace.

Foggia is 29 miles from Villa Santoro. The nearest airport is Capodichino Airport, 49 miles from the property.
