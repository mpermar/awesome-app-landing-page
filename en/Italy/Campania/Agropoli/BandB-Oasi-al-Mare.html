Description

At the bed and breakfast, rooms contain a desk and a flat-screen TV. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a bidet and free toiletries, while some accommodations at B&B Oasi al Mare also have a terrace. The rooms include a closet.

A buffet or Italian breakfast is available daily at the property.

Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Agropoli, like cycling.

Salerno is 24 miles from B&B Oasi al Mare, while Amalfi is 27 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Capodichino Airport, 51 miles from the property.
