Description

Located between Paestum and Agropoli, the Insonnia features a restaurant, outdoor pool and terrace.

All rooms come with a flat-screen TV, 5 minutes' drive from the beach. Hotel Insonnia's spacious restaurant specialises in fish and meat dishes from the Cilento region.

In the winter, the Glamour disco offers late night entertainment with guest DJs. All air-conditioned guest rooms provide a mini fridge and private bathroom with hairdryer.

Some rooms feature a balcony. Local buses stop in front of the hotel and go to the seafront and Agropoli-Castellabate Train Station.

The Cilento National Park is best explored by car, and guests have a discount on car rental at a nearby agency.
