Description

Just 200 metres from the nearest beach, Via Trento 30 is situated in Salerno, 5 km from Salerno Harbour and 3 km from Stadio Arechi.

Rooms are equipped with a 28" LCD flat-screen TV and have free WiFi. The private bathroom comes with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

A sweet and savoury breakfast here includes local pastries, croissants, and cakes together with hot drinks and fresh orange juice.

Vegan, vegetarian and gluten-free options are available on request. A free shuttle service to/from Salerno train Station and Salerno Airport is available.

In summer months, staff can assist arranging boat excursions. Naples International Airport is 49 km from the property.
