Description

Set on the edge of Monti Picentini Regional Park, Tenuta D'Amore is 10 km outside Salerno center.

Made entirely of wood and stone, it has a peaceful setting and a large garden with swimming pool.

Tenuta D'Amore has panoramic views of the surrounding green hills. Each room or apartment has its own patio with a table and chairs.

All accommodation is air conditioned and comes with an LCD TV and a private bathroom.

Apartments also have a kitchenette. An Italian breakfast of coffee and croissants is served each morning and can be enjoyed out in the garden during summer.

The restaurant, open at weekends from October to May, specialises in Mediterranean cuisine.

During the rest of the year, it is open every day. Free parking is provided and the San Mango Piemonte exit of the A3 motorway is 2 km away.
