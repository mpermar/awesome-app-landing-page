Description

This family-run bed and breakfast is quietly located in Benevento, surrounded by an area of greenery.

B&B Villa Adriano offers free WiFi as well as a garden with barbecue facilities. Rooms at B&B Villa Adriano are spacious and elegantly furnished.

Each room includes satellite TV, a coffee machine and a private bathroom with a shower and hairdryer.

Guests can also enjoy a view of the garden and the surrounding mountains. B&B Villa Adriano offers free rental bicycles.

The surrounding area is ideal for hiking and horseriding. The property also offers meeting facilities, a shared lounge and laundry facilities.

Free parking is free of charge at B&B Villa Adriano. Naples International Airport is a 1-hour drive away.
