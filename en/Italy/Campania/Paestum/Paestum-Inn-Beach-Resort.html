Description

All rooms in Paestum Inn Beach Resort are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom, while some accommodations at the accommodation also offer a terrace. All guest rooms at Paestum Inn Beach Resort have air conditioning and a desk.

The daily breakfast offers continental and buffet options.

Salerno is 24 miles from the hotel, while Amalfi is 27 miles away. The nearest airport is Capodichino Airport, 50 miles from Paestum Inn Beach Resort.
