Description

A few metres from the entrance to the Herculaneum excavations, this 18th-century villa offers large, modern rooms overlooking the sea or the 25,000 m² grounds surrounding the hotel.

WiFi throughout is free. Breakfast is served in the elegant 18th-century dining room, and you can enjoy a romantic candlelit dinner in the greenery of the park at the restaurant.

The private park of the Miglio d'Oro Park Hotel features marble sculptures, fountains and fish ponds.

There is also a free swimming pool surrounded by sun loungers. The concierge service can provide tourist information.
