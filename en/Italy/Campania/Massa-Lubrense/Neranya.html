Description

Neranya is a self-catering accommodation located in a peaceful area, a 15-minute drive from Massa Lubrense.

Free WiFi access is available throughout. Each apartment will provide you with a TV, air conditioning and a balcony.

Featuring a bath or shower, the private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Bed linen and towels are included. At Neranya you will find a hot tub, a garden and barbecue facilities.

Other facilities like grocery deliveries, an ironing service and laundry facilities are offered.

The property offers free parking. Marina di Puolo is 8 km from the property. Marina di Cantone is a 15-minute drive away.
