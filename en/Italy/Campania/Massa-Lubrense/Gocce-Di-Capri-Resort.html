Description

Overlooking Capri, Ischia and the Bay of Naples, Gocce Di Capri Resort is located in a quiet area 7 km from Massa Lubrense and 12 km from Sorrento.

It features an outdoor pool, sun terrace and garden. All rooms and apartments at Gocce Di Capri Resort are air conditioned and en suite.

Each comes with a satellite TV and tiled floors, while most have views of the sea.

From April to October, the property hosts a restaurant, serving regional specialities prepared from seasonal, organic ingredients.

The café is open all year round for light refreshments and evening cocktails. A 10-minute drive from beaches at Marina del Cantone, the property is close to shops and restaurants.

From nearby Piazza Termini square you can enjoy a very pleasant walk to the marina.
