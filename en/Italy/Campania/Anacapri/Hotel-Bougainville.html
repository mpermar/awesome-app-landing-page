Description

The Bougainville is located in the centre of Anacapri, a charming and romantic Mediterranean location on the Island of Capri.

Wi-Fi is free throughout the hotel which also offers a lounge bar. The hotel is decorated in a typical Capri style, and all rooms offer views of the centre of Anacapri.

The Bouganville is set just a few steps from the famous Villa San Michele previously owned by the Swedish doctor Axel Munthe.

It is also close to the lift to Monte Solaro, and a 5 minutes' drive from the wonderful Blue Grotto.
