Description

Located in the heart of the Cilento National Park, Lido Paradiso Resort offers bungalows in Marina Di Pisciotta.

It features an outdoor pool, bar and a private beach. Each unit comes with a wardrobe and a fan.

The private bathroom is complete with a hairdryer and a shower. When included, a typical Italian breakfast is offered daily with hot drinks, croissants and pastries.

The restaurant serves local cuisine. Guests have free access to the tennis courts, terrace and children’s playground.

Massages and Wi-Fi access in public areas are available on site. The property also offers Hip Hop dance classes, Zumba lessons, and paddle boat rental.

Lido Paradiso Resort is a 15-minute drive from Palinuro, while Marina di Camerota is 20 km away.
