Description

Sweet Sleep features a spa and a hot tub on a rooftop terrace with panoramic views of Naples and Mount Vesuvius.

Located on the 6th floor of a luxurious 19th-century building with coin-operated lift, the B&B is just 120 metres from Napoli Centrale Train Station.

The bright, air-conditioned rooms come with free Wi-Fi access, a fridge and a balcony.

Some rooms have a small kitchenette. Eggs, cheese and cold cuts feature in the sweet and savoury breakfast served daily on the terrace.

The wellness centre includes a hot tub, a sauna, a tub with hydromassage jets and sensory showers.

Sweet Sleep B&B is opposite Piazza Garibaldi and less than 10 minutes’ walk from the Circumvesuviana line to Pompeii and Sorrento, and the Piazza Garibaldi metro station.
