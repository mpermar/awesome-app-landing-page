Description

Every room includes a flat-screen TV with cable channels and DVD player. A balcony or patio are featured in certain rooms. Every room is equipped with a private bathroom with a bidet and shower, with bathrobes and free toiletries provided.

Bike rental and car rental are available at this bed and breakfast and the area is popular for horseback riding.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the area, including snorkeling and diving. Agropoli is 15 miles from Il Grappolo, and Castellabate is 13 miles away.
