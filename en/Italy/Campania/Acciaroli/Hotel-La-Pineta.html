Description

La Pineta is a modern, charming hotel located in the pretty little village of Acciaroli and set just 50 metres from the sea, with its own private, Blue-Flag beach.

Wi-Fi is free throughout and a free internet station is also available. Step out of your bright, spacious room into La Pineta’s charming gardens where you can expect breakfast on the terrace.

Here you will find a swimming pool with hydromassage area and sunbathing area. Acciaroli’s picturesque harbour, where you will find a range of restaurants and bars is located just pleasant walking distance away as are sports facilities and all that the old town has to offer.

Rooms at La Pineta are furnished in a contemporary style and come equipped with LCD satellite television.

On site enjoy drinks in the coffee bar or have a Mediterranean style lunch or dinner in La Pineta’s restaurant.

La Pineta features a play area and a children's menu is available, specially prepared by the chef.

During summer the hotel may only be bookable for weekly stays. From April to October beach service is included in the room rate.
