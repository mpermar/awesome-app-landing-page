Description

Featuring a terrace with an outdoor dining area, La Valle Delle Najadi offers simple accommodation with free WiFi, just a 10-minute walk form the centre of Vietri.

Holiday homes offer a TV, seating area and wooden furnishings. Fully equipped kitchenettes also provide a coffee machine, while the private bathrooms feature a bidet and a hairdryer.

Some apartments have a balcony. La Valle Delle Najadi is a 10-minute drive form Salerno.
