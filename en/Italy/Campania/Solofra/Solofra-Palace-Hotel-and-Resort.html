Description

Solofra Palace Hotel & Resort offers a swimming pool with sun terrace and a range of wellness services.

It is a 15-minute walk from Solofra Station, with connections to Salerno. Parking and internet access are free at the Solofra Palace.

All rooms feature air conditioning, Sky TV, radio, and a minibar. Bathrooms come with hydromassage showers or spa baths.

The hotel's wellness centre, Relais del Nuovo Benessere, offers a wide range of massages and beauty treatments, plus a hot tub and sauna.

Le Cortine restaurant serves specialities from the Campania region, while La Veranda pizzeria offers Neapolitan pizza at the poolside.

The hotel is located next to the hospital, Ospedale Landolfi Solofra. Avellino is 10 minutes' away by car, and Salerno is 30 minutes.
