Description

San Severino Park Hotel & Spa is located in Mercato San Severino. It offers air-conditioned rooms and an outdoor swimming pool.

Wi-Fi throughout and on-site parking are free of charge. The rooms come with a satellite flat-screen TV, minibar, laptop safe box and tiled floors.

Some rooms have a garden or pool view. Breakfast at the San Severino is served buffet style.

It consists of sweet and savoury food. Guests can enjoy Mediterranean specialties in the hotel’s restaurant.

Special menu is available on request. Vietri sul Mare with beaches can be reached in 15 minutes by car.
