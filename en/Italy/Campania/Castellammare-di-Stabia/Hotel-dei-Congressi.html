Description

Hotel dei Congressi combines an exceptional location with comfortable surroundings, high technology equipment, and a conference centre.

The property is conveniently located a few minutes from the centre of Castellammare di Stabia, 7 km from Pompei (ruins), 18 km from Sorrento and 30 km from Naples via a convenient local train, the Circumvesuviana.

Relax around the elegant pool with a palm tree at every corner and enjoy views of majestic Vesuvius.
