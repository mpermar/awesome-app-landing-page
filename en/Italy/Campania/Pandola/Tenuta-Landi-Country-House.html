Description

At Tenuta Landi Country House, all rooms are equipped with a desk. Each room has a private bathroom, while some rooms include a balcony. Guest rooms at the accommodation feature air conditioning and a closet.

Breakfast is available each morning, and includes buffet, American and Italian options.

Tenuta Landi Country House has a playground.

The hotel provides an ironing service, as well as business facilities like fax and photocopying. When guests need guidance on where to visit, the reception will be happy to provide advice.

Naples is 27 miles from Tenuta Landi Country House. The nearest airport is Capodichino Airport, 26 miles from the property.
