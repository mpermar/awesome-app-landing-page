Description

Offering panoramic sea views, Hotel Mary is on the coast of the Sorrentine Peninsula in Vico Equense.

It offers a private deck with direct access the sea, outdoor swimming pool and hot tub, and a restaurant with sea-view terrace.

Rooms have a marine blue colour scheme and come with a patio or balcony offering views of the mountains or Mediterranean Sea.

All air-conditioned, they also feature satellite TV and Wi-Fi access. Breakfast is served buffet style and includes homemade cakes.

Homemade bread and pasta can also be enjoyed in the restaurant offering pizza and Italian specialities.

It is Halal certified. Mary Hotel is 800 metres from Vico Equense Train Station on the Circumvesuviana line for trains to Sorrento, Pompeii and Naples.

Free parking is provided and a shuttle service is available to all major local airports.
