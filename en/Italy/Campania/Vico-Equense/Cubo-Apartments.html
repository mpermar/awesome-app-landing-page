Description

Less than a 5-minute walk from Vico Equense Train Station, Cubo Apartments offers an à la carte restaurant with panoramic views of the Bay of Naples.

Modern-style accommodation comes with air conditioning and free Wi-Fi. With sea views, apartments at the Cubo feature a satellite flat-screen TV with Sky channels, a seating area and washing machine.

The fully equipped kitchenette comes with a dining area and dishwasher. Some apartments have a private balcony.

Suites set in an annex 150 metres away are also available. Homemade pastries and cakes are provided daily for breakfast.

The restaurant specialises in Mediterranean and international cuisine, including salmon, beef and sushi.

The bar provides a free welcome drink, together with made-to-order cocktails. Guests can enjoy discounts at a nearby beach and free electric bike rental.

Staff can arrange tours, make reservations at restaurants and advise guests on local attractions.

The property is located 10 km from Sorrento, while Positano is a 35-minute drive away.
