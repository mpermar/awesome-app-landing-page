Description

Featuring a hill-view pool with sun lounger, Hotel Diecimare is next to the WWF Nature Reserve in the Metelliana Valley, 5 km north of Cava de' Tirreni.

Parking is free at the property. Fitted with free WiFi, rooms here are elegantly furnished with light wood floors and dark wood headboards.

All air conditioned, and they also feature a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom.

Some rooms have a balcony with panoramic countryside views. The restaurant serves an Italian breakfast and Campania specialities.

Meals can be enjoyed in the dining room or out on the terrace while admiring the panorama.

Surrounded by greenery, Hotel Diecimare is 20 minutes' drive from Vietri sul Mare on the coast, reachable by bus or bike.

Paestum is 15 km away. Staff can provide maps, books and information on the Campania region.
