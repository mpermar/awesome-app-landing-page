Description

Set in Ischia town on the island of Ischia, Hotel La Villarosa Terme is 300 metres from its free private beach.

It offers a swimming pool and restaurant on the fourth floor offering panoramic sea views.

All rooms overlook the garden and some have a balcony. Inspired by classic 19th-century styles from Naples, Sicily and France, the rooms feature wrought-iron beds and wooden furniture.

Each one is air conditioned and includes satellite TV and a minibar. A sweet and savoury buffet is served each morning and can be enjoyed out on the terrace in warm weather.

Delicious Mediterranean and international dishes are served at the restaurant. The private beach is open from early May until mid October.

Guests also have free access to the Parco Aurora wellness centre and gym 500 metres away.

A shuttle bus is available to the Castiglione Terme spa, which offers free access to hotel guests, apart from the middle 2 weeks of August.

Free parking is provided and a transfer service is available on request to Naples Central Train Station and Naples Capodichino Airport.
