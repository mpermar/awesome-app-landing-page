Description

Family Spa Hotel Le Canne is in a quiet area, a 5-minute walk from Chiaia and San Francesco beaches. It offers a wellness center, and free parking and Wi-Fi access.

Air-conditioned rooms feature a TV and private bathroom. The hotel provides full facilities for babies and older children.

Extensive gardens, and indoor and outdoor pools are available on site, as are a mini club and cycling services. The wellness center has beauty treatments, massages, a sauna, a thermal pool and a variety of baths.

Hotel Le Canne Family Spa's restaurant serves Neapolitan specialties and regional cuisine. Breakfast is served buffet style indoors or outdoors on the terrace.

Public busses stop outside the property and connect to every major attraction in Ischia, including the city centers of Forio and Ischia Porto and the La Mortella Gardens. Staff can arrange transfers as well as bus and boat tours.

Forio di Ischia is a great choice for travelers interested in sunsets, swimming pools and coastal walks.
