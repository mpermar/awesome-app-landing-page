Description

The Hotel Parco Smeraldo Terme with its outdoor and indoor thermal swimming pools is directly located on Maronti Beach, one of the most beautiful in Ischia.

The hotel is set between Punta della Signora and the picturesque fishing village of Sant’Angelo.

It is surrounded by tropical gardens and features a beautiful palm terrace, an outdoor thermal pool, and a free indoor wellness centre.

The Hotel Parco even has its own private beach area, complete with free use of the sun-loungers and umbrellas.

All rates include access to the wellness centre and the private beach. Guests can use the synthetic-grass tennis courts and leave their car at the free parking facility.

Enjoy typical dishes of Ischia at the hotel restaurant, made using only the finest, natural ingredients.

Close to the hotel you can find restaurants, a small public garden, café, shops, newspaper stand and public transport including a taxi boat to cross the bay to Sant’Angelo.

Check out the new add-on option when booking and guarantee your half-board or full-board service in advance.
