Description

Ischia Blu Resort offers direct access to a private, equipped beach in Ischia Porto, and is near the ferry harbour for connections to the mainland.

It offers bright and spacious studios and apartments, most overlooking the sea. WiFi is free.

The accommodation features air conditioning and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

The style is traditional, with tiled floors and wooden furniture. Each unit has a private terrace or patio, furnished with table and chairs, and some have a parasol.

Blu Resort Ischia is 100 metres from a local bus stop, and a 10-minute walk from the shopping area of Corso Vittoria Colonna.
