Description

Offering panoramic views and both indoor and outdoor pools, Grifo Hotel Charme & Spa is 1.

5 km from Ischia Harbour. The thermal spas of Castiglione Parco Termale are just 500 metres away.

Rooms at Hotel Grifo are air conditioned and elegantly designed. They have either a balcony or patio overlooking the mountains or Mediterranean Sea.

This hotel provides a free shuttle to and from Ischia centre and the harbour in Casamicciola Terme.

Back at the hotel, guests can relax for free in the wellness area with sauna, Turkish bath and hydromassage shower.

The covered pool with hydromassage area is heated to a maximum of 36°C. Breakfast is served out by the pool, while the restaurant serves Mediterranean and international dishes.
