Description

Torre Sant'Angelo offers you a cliff-top location and exceptional views of Sant'Angelo Bay and the Mediterranean Sea.

Even the swimming pool and hot tub offer panoramic views. Set in a green location, Torre Sant'Angelo is peaceful, being surrounded by vineyards.

You can stroll to Ischia's famous Sorgeto beach from here and try out the hot springs.

The hotel's own pool contains thermal water too. Torre Sant'Angelo's rooms are spacious and brightly decorated.

All have satellite TV and superior rooms also have air conditioning, their own balconies and sea views.

Torre Sant'Angelo serves a buffet breakfast on its sea-view terrace, available from 08:00 onwards.
