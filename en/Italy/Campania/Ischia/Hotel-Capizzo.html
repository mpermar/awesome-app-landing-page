Description

Hotel Capizzo is located 500 meters from the beach and the Poseidon Thermal Gardens.

It features a panoramic sun terrace with geothermal swimming pool overlooking the sea.

All rooms at the Capizzo hotel come with private bathroom, satellite TV and a fridge.

Most rooms offer sea views. Located on Citara Bay, the Capizzo is 50 meters from a bus stop to the centre of Forio, 2 km away.

It offers free private parking. The restaurant serves specialities of Campania for lunch and dinner.
