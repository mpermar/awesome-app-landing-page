Description

Located in a peaceful setting, just 400 metres from the beach and a 15-minute walk from Forio town centre, Albergo Parco delle Agavi is surrounded by 2000m² of parkland.

You can really get away from it all in this green hotel, which is full of plants, trees and flowers.

Hotel Parco delle Agavi is made up of 4 buildings, all offering rooms with their own private balconies which overlook the gardens.

In the gardens you will find a large outdoor pool. Hotel Parco delle Agavi also has a covered hot-water pool and a hydromassage area.

A variety of different massage treatments are available on site. The hotel is conveniently situated on a road which links Lacco Ameno to Forio d’Ischia, giving it good bus links.

A bus stop is located just a few metres from Albergo Parco delle Agavi. The hotel’s restaurant offers traditional Italian cuisine as well international dishes.
