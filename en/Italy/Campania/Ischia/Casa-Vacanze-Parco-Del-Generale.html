Description

Casa Vacanze Parco Del Generale is an Aparthotel located about 100 metres from the beach and a 5-minute walk to the centre of Forio d’Ischia.

It features air-conditioned apartments, rooms and 2 swimming pools. The apartments come with an LCD TV with satellite channels, seating area and fully equipped kitchenette.

The bathroom includes a hairdryer. Wi-Fi throughout is free. Vacanze Parco Del Generale Casa also features a 24-hour reception, a garden with loungers and parasols and a sun terrace.

The harbour of Forio is a 5-minute walk away and the harbour of Ischia is 12 km away.

The Giardini Poseidon Terme spa centre can be reached in 5 minutes by car. Parking is free of charge.
