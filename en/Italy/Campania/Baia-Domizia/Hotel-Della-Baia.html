Description

Set in a beautiful English Rose Garden, Hotel Della Baia has its own private beach and quiet setting in Baia Domizia.

Each room is air conditioned and has a balcony with partial sea view. This 4-star hotel has plenty of common areas to relax in, including a TV lounge, reading rooms and games rooms.

The bar features pretty Murano glass lamps and serves drinks and cocktails. Hotel Della Baia's restaurant offers both Calabrian and international dishes, made using only fresh fish and local ingredients.

Rooms here come with an LCD TV with satellite channels, plus a private bathroom with hairdryer and toiletries.

Staff will be happy to share local knowledge and suggest the most popular food and wine tours, museums and archaeological sites.

Next door you will find a horse riding school, windsurfing and scuba diving school, a sports centre and spa.
