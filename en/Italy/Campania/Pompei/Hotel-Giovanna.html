Description

Hotel Giovanna is a 10-minute walk from the Pompeii archaeological site and city centre.

You will be 200 metres from the A3 motorway exit. The hotel is surrounded by gardens.

Rooms at Giovanna Hotel offer private bathrooms, satellite TV, air conditioning and free Wi-Fi access.

Parking is free. Hotel Giovanna has a restaurant, a children's playground, and an internet point.
