Description

La Casa Di Plinio is a 5-minute walk from the archaeological ruins of Pompei, and 1 km from the city centre.

It offers elegant, spacious rooms and apartments with free WiFi and private bathroom, and a free bottle of mineral water on arrival.

All accommodation has tiled floors, wood-beamed ceilings and stone walls. Coloured mosaics and marble are features in honour of the Pompeii ruins.

Each private bathroom is complete with rain shower, hairdryer, and toiletries. A sweet or savoury breakfast is served in the kitchen and gluten-free options are available.

Guided tours of the archaeological ruins, tours of Vesuvius and a shuttle to the Amalfi Coast can be organised on request.

The property also offers free private parking. You will be given both the keys to your room and the main gate so you are totally independent.

B&B La Casa Di Plinio is 1 km from Pompei Train Station, with connections to Sorrento and Naples.

The A3 Autostrada Napoli-Reggio Calabria motorway is 5 minutes’ drive away, and a Carrefour supermarket can be reached within a 2-minute walk.
