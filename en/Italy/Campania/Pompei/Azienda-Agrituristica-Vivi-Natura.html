Description

Set in its own grounds with an outdoor pool, playground, and animal farm, the Vivi Natura offers a restaurant and spacious rooms, all just a 5-minute drive from Pompeii’s ancient ruins.

The nearby Moregina Station on the Circumvesuviana line provides direct links to Naples and Sorrento.

With free WiFi, air-conditioned rooms at the Azienda Agrituristica Vivi Natura include satellite TV, and a private bathroom with free toiletries plus hairdryer.

The restaurant serves traditional cuisine from the Campania region, including the property’s own cheese, salami, and vegetables.

Homemade cakes, croissants and juices, together with cheeses and cold cuts are served for breakfast.
