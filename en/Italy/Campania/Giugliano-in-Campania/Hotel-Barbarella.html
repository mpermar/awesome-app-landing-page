Description

Hotel Barbarella is located in Contrada Fellapane, 4 km from Giugliano in Campania and a 25-minute drive from Naples.

It features an outdoor pool, bicycle rental, and a terrace. On-site parking is free.

The air conditioned rooms all offer free WiFi, a balcony with garden views, and a private bathroom with a shower and complimentary toiletries.

Some have a hot tub. Excursions to the Island of Capri and the Amalfi coast can be booked on site.

Pozzuoli is 21 km from the Barbarella, and Naples International Airport can be reached in 20 minutes by car.
