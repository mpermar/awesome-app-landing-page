Description

Hotel Saline is located in Palinuro on the Cilento Coast. The hotel is next to its private sandy beach and offers a salt-water pool.

Hotel Saline is just 0.6 mi from town center and 3.7 mi from Pisciotta Railway Station. On request the hotel can arrange a shuttle service to Napoli Capodichino Airport.

The property is consisted of of rooms and junior suites, most with a balcony or terrace and some with Tyrrhenian Sea views.

An extensive buffet breakfast is served each morning. For lunch and dinner you can enjoy the flavors and traditional dishes of the local cuisine.
