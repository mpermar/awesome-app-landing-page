Description

Since 1880, Hotel Bonadies has been providing wonderful hospitality in Ravello, one of the most panoramic spots of the Amalfi Coast.

Enjoy comfortable rooms, an outdoor pool and good food. Hotel Bonadies offers an outdoor swimming pool with hydromassage area, where you can enjoy the beautiful sea view.

The establishment also offers Shiatsu and relaxing massages. All rooms are elegant and refined.

Different types of rooms are available, and each with a different, yet amazing view of the sea or the mountains.

The real treat while at Hotel Bonadies is the exquisite food. All dishes are prepared only with the freshest ingredients and following traditional Mediterranean and regional recipes.
