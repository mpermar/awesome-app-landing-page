Description

Overlooking the Tyrrhenian sea, Puteoli Palace Hotel offers rooms with a balcony and air conditioning.

It is located 2. 5 km from the port of Pozzuoli and features a 24-hour front desk and a bar.

Free WiFi is available. Rooms at the Puteoli Palace Hotel include a satellite TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

The hotel offers private parking, while the centre of Naples can be reached by car in 15 minutes.
