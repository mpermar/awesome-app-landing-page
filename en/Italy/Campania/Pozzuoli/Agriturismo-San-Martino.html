Description

Surrounded by a well-kept garden only 3 km from the Cratere degli Astroni Nature Reserve, Agriturismo San Martino offers a restaurant and rustic-style en suite rooms.

This farm stay has an on-site wine cellar. Each room at the San Martino Agriturismo features air conditioning, an outdoor area and views of the shared garden.

All rooms include a flat-screen TV, free toiletries and a hairdryer. Guests are served daily a continental breakfast.

Barbecue facilities are also available at the property. The farm stay is a 10-minute drive from the coastal town of Pozzuoli.
