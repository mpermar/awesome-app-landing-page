Description

Nido Degli Dei is a family-run B&B offering panoramic views of the Amalfi Coast. Just 4 km outside Agerola, its restaurant is exclusively for guests and serves organic dishes.

The B&B is 5 minutes' walk from the main square in San Lazzaro. Conca dei Marini Bay is nearby, as is the Sentiero degli Dei trekking path, which connects Agerola to Positano.

Rooms have an elegant rustic design with wrought-iron beds, Vietri ceramic tiles and decorative frescoes on the walls.

All rooms also offer a view of the surrounding greenery and the Mediterranean Sea.

Featuring organic and local ingredients, breakfast here is a buffet including fresh fruit, pastries and cereal.

It can be enjoyed in the sea-view garden in fine weather. You will find a free internet point in the restaurant and a free car park.

There is a tennis court 300 metres away and a shuttle service available to local airports and train stations.
