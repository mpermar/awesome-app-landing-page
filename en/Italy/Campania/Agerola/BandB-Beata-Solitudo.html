Description

Set in the historic gatehouse of the Avitabile castle, B&B Beata Solitudo offers spacious rooms with antique furnishings in Agerola.

Overlooking the mountains, it features BBQ facilities and free Wi-Fi. Renovated in 2007, air-conditioned rooms at the Beata Solitudo come with a flat-screen satellite TV and a fridge.

Some have a balcony, while the private bathroom offers free toiletries and a hairdryer.

A sweet and savoury continental breakfast is provided daily, served on the terrace in fine weather.

A shared kitchen is also available for guests. Guests can enjoy hiking trails in the Valle delle Ferriere Nature Reserve, 8 km away, or visit the Grotta dello Smeraldo sea cave in Conca dei Marmi, 30 minutes’ drive from the property.
