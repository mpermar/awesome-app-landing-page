Description

Surrounded by extensive gardens offering stunning views of the Bay of Naples, the hotel is located near all Capri’s attractions as well as the island’s largest beach. A 5 minute walk will take you to the cable car, which links the harbor to Capri’s famous Piazzetta, the best place on the island for shops, restaurants and elegant cocktails.

At the hotel you will be offered exclusive facilities and utmost relaxation. ‘Stai’, the spa, offers a huge range of treatments, ranging from the innovative to the traditional...and of course, you will also enjoy relaxing in the lovely outdoor pool.

Rooms are lavish and utterly unique, all individually inspired by different figures from the 19th and 20th centuries who shaped Capri’s cultural and intellectual history.

Ziqù Restaurant presents exquisite dishes combining contemporary cuisine with traditional regional recipes and Mediterranean flavor in beautiful surroundings, perfect for a romantic dinner.
