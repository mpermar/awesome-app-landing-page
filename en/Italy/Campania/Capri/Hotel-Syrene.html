Description

Some rooms offer beautiful sea views and others overlook the pretty gardens. Most rooms have a private balcony.

A massage and hot tub room is available on site to ensure you a truly relaxing stay. If you feel like more exercise you can go for a game of tennis at a nearby hotel of the same ownership.

Enjoy a snack at the poolside bar or try traditional regional cuisine at Hotel Syrene’s restaurant, where you can be served indoors, by the pool or on the terrace. Breakfast is buffet style.
