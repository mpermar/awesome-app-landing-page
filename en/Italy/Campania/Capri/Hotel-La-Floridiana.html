Description

Offering views of the Mediterranean Sea on Capri Island, La Floridiana features a seasonal outdoor pool with mosaic tiles and hydromassage jets.

Via Camerelle shopping street is 3 minutes away on foot. Air-conditioned rooms at the Floridiana include a minibar, and satellite TV with pay-per-view channels.

Many have a sea-view balcony or terrace. Wi-Fi is free in the entire building. A varied buffet breakfast is served daily from 07:30 to 10:30, including pastries, fruit juices and cheeses.

The restaurant features a terrace with sea view and drinks are available from the stylish wine bar.

Relaxing Thai massages are available on request and the property's staff is multilingual, including Russian and Arabic.

The hotel is 5 minutes' drive from the port in Marina Grande for boats to Italy's mainland.
