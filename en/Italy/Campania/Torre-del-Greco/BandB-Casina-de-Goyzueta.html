Description

All guest rooms at the bed and breakfast come with a seating area and a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Each room has a electric tea pot and a private bathroom, while selected rooms come with a terrace. All rooms will provide guests with a microwave.

B&B Casina de Goyzueta offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Torre del Greco, like cycling.

Naples is 8 miles from B&B Casina de Goyzueta, while Sorrento is 10 miles away. The nearest airport is Capodichino Airport, 9 miles from the property.
