Description

Located 3 km outside San Giovanni a Piro, the Caluna Charme offers an outdoor pool and spacious rooms with balcony, free WiFi, and an elegant style.

The restaurant specialises in Cilento cuisine, and breakfast is a sweet buffet. Parking is free.

The rooms come with traditional tiled floors, colourful bedspreads matching with the curtains, and a private bathroom with shower and hairdryer.

The balconies are furnished with table and chairs, and offer views of the garden with swimming pool, and of the sea in the distance.

The restaurant is open for lunch and serves Cilento cuisine including local cheeses and salami, bruschetta and caprese salad.

Breakfast includes jams, juice, milk, biscuits, and homemade cakes. Charme Caluna is a 10-minute drive from the seaside in Scario.
