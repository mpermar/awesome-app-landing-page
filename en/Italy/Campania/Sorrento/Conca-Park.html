Description

Surrounded by Mediterranean greenery, Hotel Conca Park overlooks the historic centre and the ancient walls of Sorrento.

This zero-waste hotel features a wellness centre and an outdoor swimming pool. All air-conditioned rooms at Conca Park are simply furnished and come with free WiFi and a private bathroom.

Some rooms have views of the city of Sorrento and the bay of Naples. The wellness centre is complete with hydromassage pools and showers, as well as a Turkish bath.

A range of relaxing massages and beauty treatments is also available. The international buffet breakfast includes traditional local products and egg dishes, specially prepared by the hotel's bakers and pastry chefs.

Dinner is served in 2 elegant restaurants offering classic Sorrentine dishes. Sorrento Train Station is a 10-minute walk from the property, while Positano is 17 km away.
