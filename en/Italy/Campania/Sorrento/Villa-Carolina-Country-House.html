Description

Villa Carolina Country House offers bright, colourful rooms with free WiFi in a renovated villa, set in Priora into the Sorrento hillside and 4 km from central Sorrento.

Its large sun terrace features tables, chairs, and sun loungers. Villa Carolina enjoys beautiful views of the sea, including Ischia Island.

The flowery garden is filled with orange, lemon, and olive trees. Outdoor parking is free.

Rooms are in a rustic country-style typical of the area, with Majolica tiles, ceramic inserts, and stone floors.

The sea-view rooms are found in the main building. Scooters, bikes, boats and cars are available for rent directly on site.

The property also has a shared outdoor hot tub for 6 people and an outdoor paddling pool.

The staff can arrange trips and excursions to nearby attractions such as Pompeii and the islands of Capri, Ischia, and Procida.
