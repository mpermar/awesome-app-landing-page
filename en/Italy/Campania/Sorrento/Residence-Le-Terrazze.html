Description

Residence Le Terrazze offers memorable views including Sorrento, the Gulf of Naples, and Mount Vesuvius from its panoramic terraces with outdoor swimming pool.

All apartments feature a balcony or terrace with views of the sea. Free parking and a shuttle to Piazza Antiche Mura are available.

Each apartment comes with air conditioning and a satellite TV, and a fully equipped kitchenette.

Some apartments have a raised sleeping area, and guest rooms are also available. The bar is open all day and during summer it also serves snacks.

Ingredients for a self-service breakfast are provided in your accommodation. The bus to Sorrento centre, Positano, and Amalfi stops nearby.
