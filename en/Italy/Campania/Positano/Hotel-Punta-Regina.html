Description

Each room is spacious and all come equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, and a private balcony. Some offer views of the sea.

A buffet breakfast with freshly baked bread and cake but also cold cuts and cheese is served daily. Throughout the day you can enjoy drinks and light snacks from the bar.

Ask at the front desk about the range of organized tours and excursions available and really explore the stunning Amalfi Coast.
