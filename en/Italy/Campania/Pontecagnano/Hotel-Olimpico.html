Description

Set only 50 metres from its own private beach, Hotel Olimpico offers free parking and free WiFi throughout.

This hotel features a seasonal swimming pool in its tropical garden. A free shuttle is offered from/to Salerno Train Station, the city centre and several ports with ferries to the Amalfi Coast and Capri.

Transfers from/to Naples Capodichino Airport can also be arranged on request. The outdoor pool is surrounded by sun loungers and parasols.

The private beach is equally equipped. A free tennis court is also available. All rooms at the Olimpico include a TV with Sky channels and air conditioning.

They also have a balcony. The restaurant serves Italian cuisine and local dishes.

During summer candlelit dinners are served in the garden. A buffet breakfast is offered every morning.
