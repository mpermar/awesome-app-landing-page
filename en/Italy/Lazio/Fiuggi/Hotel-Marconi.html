Description

Hotel Marconi is located in the heart of Fiuggi, in a quiet street, just 150 metres from the Bonifacio VIII thermal springs and the main bus station.

Rooms at Marconi Hotel come with a 22" TV, independent hair conditioning, private bathroom and nice décor.

Most rooms have a balcony. The Marconi's restaurant offers excellent traditional Italian and Ciociaria dishes.
