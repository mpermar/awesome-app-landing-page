Description

Silva Splendid is in Fiuggi, and the hotel has its own wellness centre with Finnish sauna, Turkish bath and Kneipp path, as well as 2 heated indoor pool and 1 heated outdoor pool.

This property also features a restaurant. Rooms come with free WiFi, luxurious slippers and bathrobes, and air conditioning.

Each room includes a tea/coffee maker, a balcony with garden view and a flat-screen TV with Mediaset Premium channels.

Silva Hotel Splendid is surrounded by a private chestnut wood, and shady gardens.

Buffet breakfast and snacks including cakes, pies and fruit salad are served at La Veranda, with park views.

You can also enjoy a free afternoon snack with fresh fruit, yoghurt and sweet products, and enjoy a drink at the bar.

Le Ginger Restaurant offers local and Italian specialities in a simple yet elegant atmosphere.

Gluten-free options are available. The spa comes with a gym, beauty treatments, and diet programmes.

Racing and mountain bikes are available for hire. The property is part of the Italy Bike Hotels Group, with dedicated facilities and services for cyclists.
