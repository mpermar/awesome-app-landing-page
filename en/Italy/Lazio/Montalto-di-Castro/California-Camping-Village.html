Description

Featuring outdoor swimming pools and a private beach area, California Camping Village is set on the beachfront of Maremma.

The Etruscan village of Tarquinia is a 20-minute drive away. Offering air conditioning and a patio, apartments have an outdoor dining area and a fully equipped kitchenette.

The private bathroom comes with shower and a bidet. Tents are also available on site.

The property has an array of services, such as a mini-market on site, restaurant, bar, BBQ facilities and a kids’ club.

A beach volley field and a soccer field are also available. Monte Argentario is a 50-minute drive from Camping Village California, while the Tarot Garden by Niki de Saint Phalle is 25 km from the property.
