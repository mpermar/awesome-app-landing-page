Description

Hotel Maga Circe is set in a green area on the rocky coast of the Circeo hill, a 5-minute walk from the sandy seafront of San Felice Circeo.

It offers contemporary air-conditioned rooms, a restaurant and outdoor pool. Free WiFi is available in public areas.

Rooms at the Maga Circe are well-maintained and include a minibar and a private bathroom with toiletries and hairdryer.

Superior and deluxe double rooms have a sea-view balcony, while standard ones face the internal courtyard.

Family rooms are also available. Set against the backdrop of the Circeo National Park, the Maga Circe is surrounded by lush Mediterranean green.

From May to September you can enjoy a relaxing swim in the salt-water pool, which offers panoramic sea views.

The same views can be enjoyed from the La Veranda Restaurant's terrace, where local and international dishes as well as a sweet Italian breakfast are served.

There is also a small, quiet beach area nearby. Both the bus stop and the shopping area are within a walking distance while the centre of San Felice Circeo is a 3-minute drive away.
