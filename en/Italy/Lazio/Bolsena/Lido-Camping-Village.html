Description

Set right on the shores of Lake Bolsena, Lido Camping Village offers a swimming pool and wide range of sports facilities.

It also features a disco, cinema with surround sound and a bar overlooking the lake.

This holiday park has a choice of different accommodation, from mobile homes to chalets and bungalows.

Each one is air conditioned and has a patio and cooking facilities. Lido Camping Village has its own supermarket and a bar featuring a widescreen TV with sports channels.

You can enjoy traditional Italian dishes and pizzas at the restaurant. Sports lovers will appreciate the tennis courts, football pitch and basketball and volleyball courts.

There are plenty of sun loungers and parasols around the swimming pool. Free parking is provided and a shuttle bus into Bolsena centre is available.
