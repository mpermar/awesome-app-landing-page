Description

Set on a hill overlooking Anagni, Agriturismo Cisogna offers rooms and bungalows in a quiet location in the Lazio countryside.

The farmhouse produces its own olive oil and wine, amongst other things. Rooms and bungalows have a rustic design with natural wood and a private entrance.

They all feature a TV and private bathroom, while the bungalows also have a fully equipped kitchenette.

The Cisogna's restaurant offers a set menu of typical dishes from the Lazio region, all made using homemade ingredients.

The agriturismo is 8 km from the Anagni-Fiuggi exit of the A1 motorway. It is also 15 km from the thermal spas of Ferentino and Fiuggi, and 20 from Valmontone's Rainbow MagicLand amusement park.
