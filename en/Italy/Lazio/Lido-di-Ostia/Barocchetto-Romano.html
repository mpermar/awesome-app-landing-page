Description

Just 2 minutes' walk from the beach in Ostia, Barocchetto Romano offers free Wi-Fi and a large terrace overlooking the sea and town.

The property is surrounded by restaurants and shops. The air-conditioned rooms are decorated in an elegant way, fitted with white furniture and terracotta flooring.

Each comes with a flat-screen TV and a work desk. Breakfast in the Barocchetto B&B is a self-service buffet and includes jams, croissants and cereal, together with coffee, tea and juices.

There is also a shared kitchen where you can prepare your own breakfast. Set in the pedestrian area of town, Barocchetto is a 10-minute drive from the Ostia Antica Roman Ruins.

Rome Fiumicino Airport is 15 minutes' drive away. Lido Centro Train Station, with its connections to Rome’s Metro, can be reached in 5 minutes on foot.
