Description

Set in Terracina, 7 km from Terracina Harbour, Al Canto delle Sirene offers accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a seasonal outdoor swimming pool and a bar.

With a garden, the 3-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom.

The accommodation provides evening entertainment and room service. All guest rooms at the hotel come with a seating area.

At Al Canto delle Sirene all rooms are fitted with a desk and a flat-screen TV. A continental breakfast is available every morning at the accommodation.

Al Canto delle Sirene offers a sun terrace. Temple of Jupiter Anxur is 9 km from the hotel.
