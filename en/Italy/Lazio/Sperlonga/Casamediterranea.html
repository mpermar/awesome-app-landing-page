Description

Sea-view Casamediterranea is a whitewashed building offering a 10000 m² garden with swimming pool and furnished deck.

It is surrounded by the Lazio countryside, just 6 km from Sperlonga's historical centre.

Rooms and studios here are bright and simply furnished, with Vietri ceramic floors.

Each one is air conditioned and comes with an LCD TV and private bathroom. Studios also have a fully equipped kitchenette.

At guest house Casamediterranea you have free Wi-Fi access in public areas, as well as free parking.

You can also enjoy a drink at the bar with terrace, or organise a BBQ. The pool has a hydromassage area and chromotherapy.
