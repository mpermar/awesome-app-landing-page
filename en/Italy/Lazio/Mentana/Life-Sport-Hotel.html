Description

Located 4 km from Monterotondo, the Life Sport Hotel has 2 free pools and sports facilities set in an 8-hectare green area.

It offers a restaurant and modern rooms with LCD TV. Rooms at the Sport Life Hotel are spacious and come with air-conditioning and some of them with a balcony.

The hotel serves a continental buffet breakfast each morning. The restaurant serves both Roman and classic Italian cooking.

Guests can unwind in the spa, which includes a sauna, sensory showers, hot tub, salt cabin and a relaxation area.

Sports lovers will appreciate the hotel’s tennis court, beach volleyball court and volleyball court, water sports lake, free gym and the lake where fishing is possible.

Horse riding is possible in a nearby manege. The property is 9 km away from the golf course Marco Simone Golf & Countryclub.

The centre of Rome is a 30-minute train ride from Monterotondo Station, 6 km away.

A shuttle service can be pre-booked from/to both the station and Fiumicino Airport.
