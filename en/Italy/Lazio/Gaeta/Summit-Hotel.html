Description

Summit Hotel is set directly on its private beach for guests only, equipped with parasols, sun loungers, a beachfront café, and free Wi-Fi.

In summer, a free shuttle to Gaeta centre is provided. Rooms at the Summit are cool and comfortable with air conditioning and decorative tiled floors.

They also feature a laptop safe, minibar and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Guests have free towels for the beach, where the parasols and sea board walk are all made from eco-friendly materials.

In July and August free entertainment is provided for children. Breakfast is buffet style and Summit's restaurant is on the top floor and features a terrace overlooking the Mediterranean Sea.

It serves international cuisine and Italian specialities. You will also find a modern wellness centre with sauna, hot tub and Turkish bath.

Summit Hotel is 10 minutes' drive from the town of Sperlonga and is 5 km from the natural grotto of the Montagna Spaccata.

At reception you can rent cars, organise boat excursions and deep sea fishing, and book taxis and private jet or helicopter transfers.
