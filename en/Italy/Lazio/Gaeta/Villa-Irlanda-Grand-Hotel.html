Description

The spacious rooms have classic décor and wooden furniture. Some feature hand-painted majolica tiles and views of the grounds. They also offer air conditioning and a bathroom with hairdryer.

Villa Irlanda Grand Hotel includes 4 historic buildings, including an Art Nouveau villa and a former convent. The front desk is built among the walls of a Roman villa from the 1st century B.C.

The hotel is 2 miles from Formia Train Station, where trains depart for Rome. Gaeta is 2.5 miles away.
