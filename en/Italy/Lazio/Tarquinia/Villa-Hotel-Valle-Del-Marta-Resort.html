Description

Villa Hotel Valle del Marta Resort is set just out of Tarquinia's historic centre, in the River Marta Valley.

The gardens have a pool and great views. Villa Valle del Marta overlooks Tarquinia's old city walls and fields which lead down to the sea.

It is surrounded by vineyards and liquors are made on site. Free bikes are available at the Valle del Marta Resort.

Buses stop right outside the gate. There is also a beauty farm and wellness centre with sauna and Turkish bath.

Some rooms have a spa bath. Hotel Valle del Marta has a TV room and games are provided.

The restaurant, open mainly in summer, serves local dishes made with ingredients grown at Valle del Marta or nearby.

Gluten-free dishes can be provided. Breakfast is available from 07:30 until 11:30.
