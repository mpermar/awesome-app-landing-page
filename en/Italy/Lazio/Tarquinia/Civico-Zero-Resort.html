Description

At the hotel, rooms have a desk. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with free toiletries, while some rooms also feature a terrace.

A continental, buffet or vegetarian breakfast is served at the property.

CIVICO ZERO RESORT has a playground. Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Tarquinia, like cycling.

Viterbo is 21 miles from Civico Zero Resort, while Civitavecchia is 11 miles away. The nearest airport is Fiumicino Airport, 40 miles from the hotel.
