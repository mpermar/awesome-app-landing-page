Description

Alla Corte delle Terme is set in extensive grounds, and offers a free shuttle to/from the Terme dei Papi thermal baths, 2.

5 km away. The large rooms all have garden views and a flat-screen TV with pay-per-view channels, The private bathroom has a hairdryer and free toiletries.

The Cavaliere restaurant serves both local Tuscia specialities and classic Italian cuisine.

There is also a lounge bar. The Alla Corte Resort is 10 minutes' drive from Viterbo city centre.
