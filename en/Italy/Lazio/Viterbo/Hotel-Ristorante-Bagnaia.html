Description

The Hotel Ristorante Biscetti is a welcoming, family-run establishment set in the heart of Bagnaia.

It is 5 minutes from the beautiful landscaped gardens of Villa Lante. The hotel features a relaxing bar and lounge area and comfortable guest rooms.

Try some regional specialities in the traditional restaurant, including homemade pasta and desserts.

Choose from an extensive wine list of over 100 carefully selected Italian wines. Gluten-free dishes are available.

The Hotel Ristorante Biscetti is in the centre of Bagnaia, just a few steps from the main square.

It is the ideal base for exploring the nearby Lakes of Bolsena and Vico, the Cimini Mountains and the medieval town of Viterbo with its renowned thermal waters.
