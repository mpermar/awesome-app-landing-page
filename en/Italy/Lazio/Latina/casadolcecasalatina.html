Description

Set in Latina, 35 km from Terracina, casadolcecasalatina offers a restaurant and free WiFi.

The bed and breakfast offers a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer, free toiletries and bidet.

Casadolcecasalatina offers a buffet or Italian breakfast. The accommodation offers a children's playground.

Barbecue facilities are included and guests can also relax in the garden or in the shared lounge area.

Frosinone is 49 km from casadolcecasalatina, while Pomezia is 48 km away. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino, 59 km from the bed and breakfast, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
