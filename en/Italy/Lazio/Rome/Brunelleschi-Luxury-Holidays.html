Description

Brunelleschi Luxury Holidays Hostel is set in the Vatican City - Prati district in Rome, 300 metres from Castel Sant'Angelo and 400 metres from St Peter's Square.

Ottaviano Metro Station is a 10-minute walk away. Every room is air conditioned and is equipped with a flat-screen TV, a kettle and a private bathroom.

Extras include free toiletries and a hairdryer. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

There are a vending machine with drinks and snacks, as well as a gym and spa available all day.

The furnished large roof garden offers views of the surrounding area that includes the Apostolic Palace.
