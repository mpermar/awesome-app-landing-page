Description

Featuring free WiFi, Sky Tower offers accommodation in Rome, just a 7-minute walk from Tiburtina Train/Metro Station, which offers direct links to Fiumicino Airport.

Private parking is available on site. Accommodation at Sky Tower comes with a dining area and a seating area with a satellite flat-screen TV.

All include dishwasher, an oven and microwave, as well as a coffee machine and air conditioning.

Some units include a private terrace or a private garden. The Coliseum can be reached in a short metro ride, it just 6 stops away.
