Description

Offering air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi, Hotel Giotto Flavia is set in the historic centre of Rome, 10 minutes' walk from Repubblica Metro.

Termini Station is 1 km away. Classically furnished rooms at the Giotto Flavia include satellite flat-screen TV and a minibar.

The private bathroom has free toiletries and a hairdryer. Breakfast, when booked, can be served in the comfort of your room free of charge.

The hotel is within easy walking distance of Piazza Barberini, Fontana di Trevi, Villa Borghese and other attractions.
