Description

Offering a terrace and views of the city, Rarity Suites is situated in Rome in the Lazio Region, 200 metres from Piazza del Popolo.

Rooms at the Rarity Suites are soundproofed. All rooms have a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, as well as an iPod docking station and a computer.

Each room has a coffee machine and a private bathroom with free toiletries. Rarity Suites features free WiFi throughout the property.

Via Margutta is 300 metres from Rarity Suites, while Via Condotti is 500 metres from the property.
