Description

Le Dune is set right on the beach in Lido Adriano, 7 km outside Ravenna. It features modern rooms with Sky channels and free WiFi.

Rooms at Hotel Le Dune are all air conditioned and feature satellite TV. Each room has a private bathroom with shower box.

An extensive buffet is served daily in the bright breakfast room. During summer the hotel offers free parasols and beach towels.

Discounted rates for the Mirabilandia amusement park, 15 km away, are provided to guests.
