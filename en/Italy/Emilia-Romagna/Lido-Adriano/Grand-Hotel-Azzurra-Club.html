Description

Set on the Lido Adriano in Ravenna and surrounded by gardens, this 3-star hotel offers comfortable accommodation, sport activities, and a wellness centre overlooking the hotel's private beaches.

Grand Hotel Azzurra Club provides comfortable rooms, all with a balcony and side views of the sea.

In-room amenities include air conditioning, a hairdryer, safe and satellite TV. Take advantage of the adult and children swimming pools at Grand Hotel Azzurra.

Play tennis on the clay courts. There are fields or spaces reserved for five-a-side football, table tennis, archery and beach volleyball.

Grand Hotel Azzurra organises entertainment for all ages and includes a miniclub for children from the age of 4 until 12.

There is a private discothèque on site. In the wellness centre, choose from various types of massages and beauty treatments, all available at additional costs.

Rent dressing cabins on the hotel's private beach, accessible at an additional cost.

Here you will find inflatable toys for children and a handy toy-storage room. This comfortable hotel provides private parking, a bar, a sun deck and a refreshing gazebo.

Dine on regional and international meals served in the on-site restaurant. Savour the free continental breakfast.

Public transport connecting to the main railway station is nearby. The hotel provides an airport transfer service at an additional cost.
