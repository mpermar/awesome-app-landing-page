Description

Relais La Colombara Spa & Wellness is set in a medieval hill town with breathtaking views of the countryside.

This unique hotel is made up of a series of restored stone hamlets. La Colombara Relais provides an elegant setting for your stay in Pigazzano.

You will be captivated by the area, so rich in history and nature. Popular activities include hiking, horse riding, canoeing, and golf.

The outdoor swimming pool, open during the summer, is set on the panoramic terrace.

The hotel's Aromi Spa features an indoor pool with hydromassage, a Turkish bath and Kneipp treatment.

The restaurant is open for lunch and dinner and serves a tempting mix of traditional and fusion cuisine.
