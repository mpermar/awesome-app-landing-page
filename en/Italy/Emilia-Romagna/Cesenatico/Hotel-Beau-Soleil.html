Description

Hotel Beau Soleil offers a heated, Olympic-size swimming pool. Next to the main pool you will find a hydro-massage bathtub and a children’s pool. Under the guidance of professional staff, children can enjoy swimming lessons and a variety of water games.

Atheletes will enjoy the water sports offered at Hotel Beau Soleil. Perfect for triathlon training, Hotel Beau Soleil features a bike-maintenance and storage room.

This family-run hotel provides an on-site restaurant. Here you can enjoy classical Italian cooking and regional specialties. Upon request, the restaurant can offer fish-based and vegetarian menus, gluten-free meals and special dietary dishes.

Enjoy a rich breakfast buffet in a friendly, informal atmosphere. Relax in the lounge area and have a drink at the American bar. The hotel offers free use of internet with Wi-Fi access in public areas.

70 rooms at Hotel Beau Soleil are accessible by an elevator. Some rooms offer panoramic balconies with sea views. Enjoy modern bathrooms, spacious accommodations and satellite TV.

The hotel has a free parking lot. You can easily explore the nearby cities of Venice and Ravenna from Hotel Beau Soleil.
