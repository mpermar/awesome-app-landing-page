Description

Offering free WiFi and free bike rental, Hotel Giove is 4 km from the centre of Cesenatico.

It features a children's playground, a traditional restaurant and a bar. The classic-style rooms here come with air conditioning, satellite TV and a safe.

Each has a private bathroom with a shower and hairdryer. A sweet and savoury breakfast is served daily at the Giove.

Open for lunch and dinner, the restaurant specialises in regional cuisine. With free private parking, Giove Hotel is 500 metres from Gatteo a Mare Train Station.
