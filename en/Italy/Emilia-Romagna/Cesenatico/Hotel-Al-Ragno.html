Description

Hotel Al Ragno is located just across the road from Cesenatico’s beaches. It offers air-conditioned accommodation with a sea-view balcony, an outdoor pool and a restaurant with bar.

Classic-style rooms at Al Ragno have a TV, safe and tiled floors. The private bathroom includes a hairdryer.

Free WiFi is available throughout. Breakfast is served buffet style. It includes both sweet and savoury food.

The restaurant features local specialities prepared directly by the owners. A bar is also available.

A bus stop is just opposite the property. Cesenatico Train Station is 15 minutes’ walk away.
