Description

The Kiss Hotel is in a green area located a 10-minute drive from Cesenatico, near the seaside and the pine wood.

All rooms offer a private balcony, LED TV, and air conditioning. Set in a quiet location, next to the tourist harbour and to the many shop and night entertainments, Hotel Kiss offers free parking and free WiFi.

There is an outdoor pool with hydromassage functions in the Kiss's garden. The restaurant on site serves classic dishes from the Romagna area.
