Description

Just a few steps from the sandy beach, Hotel Verona is a family-run property set in Cesenatico in the Emilia-Romagna Region.

The hotel features an outdoor pool, a hot tub and a fitness centre. Free WiFi is offered throughout the property.

Each room has a balcony and comes with a satellite flat-screen TV and a minibar. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Free use of bicycles is available at this hotel and the area is popular for cycling.

Other facilities include free private parking and a shared lounge area. Rimini is a 20-minute drive from the property.
