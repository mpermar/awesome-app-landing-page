Description

Located just 50 metres from the beaches of Cesenatico, Hotel Geminus is a 3-star hotel with a furnished garden and terrace.

It offers free Wi-Fi and air-conditioned accommodation. Modern rooms at the Geminus come with a private terrace and a TV.

A sweet and savoury breakfast is served buffet style in the dining room. A bar is also available.

On request, guests can enjoy discounts at restaurants nearby. Free on-site parking is provided.

A bus stop, 100 metres from the property, takes you to Ravenna, Rimini and Riccione.
