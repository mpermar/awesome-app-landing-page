Description

Hotel Maggiore is set at the foot of San Luca Hill, just 3 kilometres from Guglielmo Marconi Airport.

Take one of the free bikes for a ride around Bologna. The Maggiore offers a personalised service and comfortable accommodation.

Your room is complete with free WiFi. The Maggiore Hotel also has a bar. It has excellent links to the centre, the airport, and the Fiera di Bologna exhibition centre.
