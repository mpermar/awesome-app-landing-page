Description

Savoia Hotel Regency is set in an 18th-century villa at 5 km from Bologna city centre.

Surrounded by a beautiful 10,000 m² park, it features an outdoor swimming pool. The on-site Garganelli Restaurant serves traditional Emilia-Romagna and Italian cuisine.

It boasts retro-style décor from the 1960s, and sometimes offers live music. Guests can stroll and relax in the large garden and sun terrace, equipped with tables, sun loungers and stylish armchairs.

The Savoia Regency Hotel features a spacious hall with Venetian floors and crystal chandeliers.

The reception is open 24 hours a day and provides free Wi-Fi. The elegant rooms are decorated with classic furniture and come with either wooden or carpet floors.

They include a TV with satellite and pay-per-view channels. With free parking, the hotel is 1.

5 km from the A14 motorway and a 5-minute drive from the Bologna Exhibition Centre.
