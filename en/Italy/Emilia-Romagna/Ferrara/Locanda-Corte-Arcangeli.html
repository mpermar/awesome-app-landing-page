Description

One of our top picks in Ferrara.

Corte Arcangeli offers sport facilities, free Wi-Fi and 3 outdoor pools. This Renaissance country villa is located in Ferrara’s suburbs, a 5-minute drive from the city center and hospital.

All rooms at Locanda Corte Arcangeli have a historic atmosphere created by wall stones and wood-beamed ceilings. Each one comes with antique furniture, along with air conditioning, satellite TV and a complete private bathroom.

The property’s restaurant serves traditional Italian cuisine and wines, plus dishes from the Emilia Romagna and Umbria regions. Specialties include fresh fish, Chianina and boar meat, and black truffles from Norcia. It specializes in catering for special events.

Guests can relax on the furnished terrace or use the 2 hot tubs or the free internet point available in the lobby.

Offering free parking, Locanda Arcangeli provides a shuttle service from Ferrara Station. The bus stop next door links with Ferrara and the seaside.
