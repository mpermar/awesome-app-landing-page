Description

Agriturismo-R&B Corte dei Gioghi is just 4 km from the historic centre of Ferrara and the hospital.

It offers spacious, rustic rooms with antique furniture. The garden is complete with outdoor swimming pool.

The rooms are all en suite, and come with air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, and a flat-screen TV.

Breakfast includes organic and local food, and it is served in the Winter Garden, a large hall with a scent of citrus trees.

Agriturismo R & B Corte dei Gioghi is 2.5 km from the South Ferrara exit of the A13 Bologna-Padova Motorway.
