Description

One of our top picks in Ferrara.

The Touring is located in central Ferrara, just 150 feet from the Estense Castle. With attentive service, it offers a spa, and air-conditioned rooms with a mini-bar and free Wi-Fi.

Guests arriving at Hotel Touring are greeted with a welcome homemade pastry. Each morning an extensive continental breakfast is served, including cappuccino, fresh fruit juice, and local cheese. Organic and gluten-free products are also available.

The Touring Hotel’s wellness center features a panoramic view of the city. Guests can relax in the Turkish bath, or re-energize in the ice-fountain.

You can rent bikes on site for day trips to Ferrara’s sites, including the Botanic Gardens and the Palazzo dei Diamanti.

Touring Hotel is 2.8 mi from the Ferrara Nord motorway exit, and Ferrara Train Station is 0.9 mi away.
