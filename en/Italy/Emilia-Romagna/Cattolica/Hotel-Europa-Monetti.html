Description

Hotel Europa Monetti features a restaurant, free bikes, a bar and shared lounge in Cattolica.

With a garden, the 4-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom.

The hotel has an outdoor swimming pool, fitness centre, evening entertainment and a 24-hour front desk.

All rooms in the hotel are fitted with a TV with cable channels. All units have a wardrobe.

Guests at Hotel Europa Monetti can enjoy a buffet or an American breakfast. The accommodation offers 4-star accommodation with a hammam and children's playground.

You can play table tennis at Hotel Europa Monetti, and bike hire is available. Aquarium Le Navi is 1.

5 km from the hotel. The nearest airport is Federico Fellini International, 15 km from Hotel Europa Monetti, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
