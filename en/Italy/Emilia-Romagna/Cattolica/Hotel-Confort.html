Description

Offering free bikes and air-conditioned rooms with a private balcony and free Wi-Fi, Hotel Confort is 200 metres from Cattolica's sandy beach.

The property features a traditional restaurant and a bar. All rooms come with a ceiling fan, a phone, and a satellite TV.

The en suite bathroom is complete with shower, hairdryer and free toiletries. Freshly baked bread and croissants are just some of the items included in the daily breakfast buffet, both sweet and savoury.

Guests can ask for fixed menus and packed lunches at the hotel's restaurant. Cattolica Train Station is 2 km from the hotel, and offers direct links to Riccione.

The Cattolica Aquarium is a 5-minute walk away. Private parking is free at a location nearby.
