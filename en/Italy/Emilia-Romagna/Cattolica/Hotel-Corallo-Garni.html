Description

Hotel Corallo provides bed and breakfast service in the heart of Cattolica, only a few steps from a typical Adriatic beach.

All air-conditioned, rooms have a balcony, a flat-screen TV and a bathroom with a shower.

The Corallo is provided with a fitness centre for you to unwind after a day spent on the seaside.

Guests can also enjoy discounted rates at a partner beach, where they will also find an outdoor swimming pool.

A rich breakfast buffet, comprising salty and sweet foods, is served until 12:00 every day.
