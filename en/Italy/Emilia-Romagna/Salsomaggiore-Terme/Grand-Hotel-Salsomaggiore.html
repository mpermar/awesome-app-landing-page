Description

Situated in Salsomaggiore Terme, Grand Hotel Salsomaggiore features a wellness centre and a terrace.

This 5-star hotel is 10 km from Fidenza. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

All air-conditioned, rooms come with a flat-screen TV, wardrobe and a private bathroom.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property. Grand Hotel Salsomaggiore has a 24-hour front desk, a garden and a luggage storage for guests.

The wellness centre features a Finnish sauna, Kneipp path, Turkish bath and sensory showers.

Parma is 33 km from the property while Parma International Airport is 30 km away.
