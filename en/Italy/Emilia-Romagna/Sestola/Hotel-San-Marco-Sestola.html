Description

Located in Sestola, Hotel San Marco Sestola offers classic-style rooms with free Wi-Fi and satellite flat-screen TV.

Set in the Emilia Romagna countryside, it features a tennis court and a spa area with Turkish bath.

Elegant rooms at Hotel San Marco have either tiled or carpeted floors and antique furnishings, and some feature a balcony.

The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries. Guests can enjoy local and international specialities at the on-site restaurant, and an aperitif on the mountain-view terrace.

When booking the half-board option, free hot drinks and sweet snacks are also served in the afternoon.

This 4-star hotel is 10 km from the foot of Monte Cimone mountain. The property offers free private parking on site.
