Description

With free rental bike, Hotel Meeting is set directly on Ricciones seafront promenade.

All rooms come with a balcony offering front or side views of the Adriatic Sea. All the rooms feature free WiFi, air conditioning and a TV.

The private bathroom is complete with a hairdryer and free toiletries. A continental breakfast, with fruit and homemade cakes, is offered daily.

The restaurant serves local cuisine and includes meat and vegetables. The bar of Meeting Hotel is open until late, and the hotel also has a large terrace where you can enjoy your drinks or sit and chat with friends and family.

With sea-views, the on-site restaurant offers a buffet dinner with typical Italian specialities.
