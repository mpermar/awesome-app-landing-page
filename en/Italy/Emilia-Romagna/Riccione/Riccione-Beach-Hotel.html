Description

Only a 2-minute walk from the sea, Riccione Beach Hotel is 3 km from Riccione town centre.

It boasts rooms with a balcony, a traditional restaurant with bar and free bikes.

Offering free Wi-Fi, rooms at the Beach Hotel come with a TV, safe and tiled floors.

The private bathroom is complete with a hairdryer and free toiletries. Some rooms overlook the sea.

Brunch consists of both sweet, drinks and savoury food. It is served from 8:00 until 14:30 every day.

Guests will have free entrance at the Beach Village water park, located 190 metres away.

The Marano area, famous for its night life and parties on the beach, is nearby. Federico Fellini International Airport can be reached in 5 minutes by car.
