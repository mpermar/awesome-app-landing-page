Description

Only 50 metres from the beach of Riccione, Hotel Darsena boasts air-conditioned rooms with a balcony and free Wi-Fi, a traditional restaurant and a furnished sun terrace.

It also features free bikes. The rooms at the Darsena come with a satellite flat-screen TV, safe and tiled floors.

The bathroom is complete with a hairdryer and free toiletries. A rich buffet breakfast includes both sweet and savoury food such as 4 kinds of homemade cakes, brioches, marmalades, as well as eggs and tomatoes.

Guests can enjoy local specialities in the hotel’s restaurant, which is open daily.

The 24-hour bar is also available. On request, a shuttle service to/from the Aquafan theme park is available on request.
