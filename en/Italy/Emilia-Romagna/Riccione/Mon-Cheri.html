Description

With its beachfront location in Riccione, Mon Cheri offers a restaurant. This hotel has classic-style rooms with air conditioning.

A buffet-style breakfast is offered daily. Rooms at the Mon Cheri have a flat-screen TV, parquet floors and a private bathroom with free toiletries and hairdryer.

Some include view of the sea and a terrace. In the summer months, guests have access to the hotel's private beach at an extra charge, including two swimming pools.

Riccione Train Station is a 10-minute walk from this property. Rimini Federico Fellini Airport is 4.
