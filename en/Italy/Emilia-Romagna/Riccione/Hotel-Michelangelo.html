Description

Hotel Michelangelo is located on Riccione's seafront, opposite the Bagno 94 beach.

It features a spa, a restaurant and rooms with views of the Adriatic Sea. The air-conditioned rooms feature a 32-inch satellite flat-screen TV, minibar and tiled floors.

The bathrooms are complete with a bathrobe, slippers and toiletries. While at Hotel Michelangelo you can relax in the wellness centre, featuring a sauna, Turkish bath and revitalising shower.

The outdoor swimming pool is open in summer. The hotel restaurant serves delicious cuisine and offers an incredible view over the sea.
