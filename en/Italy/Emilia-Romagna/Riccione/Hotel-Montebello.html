Description

Offering a traditional restaurant and rooms with a balcony, Hotel Montebello is 250 metres from Riccione's sandy beach.

Guests enjoy free access at the Beach Village swimming pools. On-site facilities at the Montebello Hotel include bike rental, a bar with outdoor terrace, and a garden with table tennis.

Wi-Fi is free in all public areas, and a free shuttle runs to the Beach Village daily.

Homemade cakes, yoghurt and jams are served every morning in the buffet breakfast, together with more sweet and savoury items.

The Montebello's restaurant serves specialities from the Emilia region. The hotel is about 1.

5 km from Riccione centre and Riccione Train Station, with direct service to Rimini, Cattolica, and other towns along the Adriatic coast.
