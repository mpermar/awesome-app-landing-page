Description

Set a few steps from Riccione’s sandy beaches, Hotel Eliseo offers accommodation with free WiFi throughout, 2 km from Viale Ceccarini and 20-minute drive from the centre of Rimini.

It features a garden and free bicycles. The air-conditioned rooms all come with a garden-view balcony or terrace.

They include a flat-screen TV, a desk, and a safety deposit box. The private bathrooms are complete with a hairdryer and complimentary toiletries.

Italian cuisine can be enjoyed in Hotel Eliseo’s restaurant, which also serves gluten-free dishes.

Guests have access to a 24-hour-reception. Cattolica and the Monte San Bartolo nature protection area are both within 15 km of Eliseo Hotel.
