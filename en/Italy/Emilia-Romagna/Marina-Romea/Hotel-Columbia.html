Description

Hotel Columbia is in the heart of Marina Romea, 400 metres from the beach. It features a swimming pool, free WiFi, and air-conditioned rooms with a LCD TV.

During the summer, guests can benefit from discounted rates at a nearby beach. The hotel’s restaurant serves typical cuisine with fish and meat dishes.

It also offers a conference room with a video projector and DVD player. Rooms at Columbia Hotel include and a private bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer.

Many rooms also have a fridge, and a balcony overlooking the surrounding pinewood.

Bikes can be rented on site for exploring the surroundings. It is 12 km from Ravenna and a 1-hour drive from Bologna.

Tickets to Mirabilandia Amusement Park, 23 km away, can be bought directly at the reception.
