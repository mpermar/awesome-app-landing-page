Description

Featuring a seasonal spa and pool, Hotel La Tavola Rotonda is set in a 13th-century castle in the countryside near Piacenza.

It offers rooms and apartments with modern comforts. The Tavola Rotonda's rooms in the Castello dei Conti Landi have LCD satellite TV, minibars and air conditioning.

You will have free internet. The Tavola Rotonda Hotel has large gardens. The wellness corner includes a hot tub, a sauna, and a corner with teas and infusions.

Staff provide personalised service at La Tavola Rotonda Hotel. There is a restaurant in the castle's old stables.
