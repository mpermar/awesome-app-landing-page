Description

Renovated in May 2011, Locanda Romana is a 2-star hotel located 6 km from Fanano, in the Emilia Romagna region.

It features an à la carte restaurant-bar and serves homemade cakes for breakfast.

Rooms are colourful and fitted with parquet floors. Facilities include a TV and a private bathroom with toiletries and shower.

At the restaurant guests can enjoy specialities from the Modenese cuisine, including the famous homemade tortellini pasta.

Meals can be served outdoors on the veranda during summer. The Locanda is a 40-minute drive from the thermal town of Porretta.
