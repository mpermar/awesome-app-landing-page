Description

Featuring free WiFi access throughout, Hotel Ninfea offers air-conditioned rooms in Cervia, 50 metres from the beachfront.

The property is surrounded by a garden with children’s playground. Each room is equipped with a TV.

The private bathroom comes with a shower, bidet and free toiletries. Breakfast is served daily in the breakfast room.

The restaurant serves regional specialities and Italian classics. Cervia – Milano Marittima Train Station is an 8-minute drive from Ninfea Hotel, while Mirabilandia Theme Park is 15 km away.
