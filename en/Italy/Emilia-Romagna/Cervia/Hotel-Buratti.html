Description

Just 150 metres from a sandy beach in Pinarella di Cervia, Hotel Buratti offers free Wi-Fi and free private garage.

It boasts modern rooms with a balcony, and a free outdoor pool with warm water. Rooms at this 4-star hotel are spacious and air conditioned, and come with a satellite flat-screen TV plus a small fridge.

Each has a private bathroom with free toiletries, a hairdryer and bathrobes. A sweet and savoury breakfast is served daily in the indoor courtyard facing the pool.

The property is complete with its own pastry shop as well as a restaurant specialising in local and international dishes.

Guests can play golf at Adriatic Golf Club Cervia, 7 km away. The large equipped garden and terrace are perfect for quiet moments of relaxation.

Buratti Hotel is a 5-minute drive from the centre of Cervia and 18 km from Mirabilandia fun park.

Buses stopping 300 metres from the hotel link with Cervia, Forli and Milano Marittima.
