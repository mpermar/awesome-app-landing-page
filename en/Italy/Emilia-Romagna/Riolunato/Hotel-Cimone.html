Description

Set in Riolunato, Hotel Cimone offers a restaurant, free hot tub and fitness centre.

Classic-style accommodation comes with a satellite flat-screen TV, and free Wi-Fi is available throughout.

Rooms at the Cimone Hotel all come with a safe, desk and tiled or parquet floors.

The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries. A sweet and savoury breakfast is served daily.

The restaurant specialises in Emilia-Romagna cuisine. The property provides free private parking on site, and is located a 20-minute drive from Abetone.
