Description

Surrounded by the countryside a 10-minute drive from Modena, Ca' del Rio Resort offers free high-speed WiFi and rustic-style rooms with Sky channels.

This property features a restaurant specialising in Emilia cuisine. All air conditioned, rooms come with views of the shared garden, flat-screen TV and a bathroom with slippers and a hairdryer.

At Ca' del Rio Resort you can sample tortellini, tortelloni and tigelle for dinner.

A sweet and savoury buffet-style breakfast is also available daily. There is a children's playground at the property and free rickshaw and bike rental service.

The countryside hotel is 1 km from the village centre of Casinalbo and its train station.

Bologna can be reached in 30 minutes by car. Helicopter tours can be oranised on site and upon request.
