Description

Set 1 km from the centre of Riolo Terme, Hotel Senio offers free bike hire and free WiFi.

There is also a large garden and free private parking. Rooms have a simple style and are equipped with a TV and a safe.

A continental breakfast is served in the sunny dining room, while the restaurant serves regional and international cuisine.

Directly opposite the hotel is the Riolo Terme spa and fitness centre. Riolo Golf and Country Club is also just 2 km away.

Senio Hotel is located less than an hour's drive from the historic cities of Ravenna and Bologna.
