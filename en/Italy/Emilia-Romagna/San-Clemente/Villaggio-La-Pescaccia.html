Description

Villaggio La Pescaccia overlooks its private lake, less than a 15-minute drive from the beach in Riccione.

It offers free Wi-Fi and free parking. La Pescaccia’s rooms also have air conditioning, a TV, and a hydromassage shower.

Some rooms have a balcony, some have a lake view. Guests have use of the outdoor pool with a hot tub and a separate children’s pool.

Surrounding the pool there is a sun terrace with loungers. The Villaggio is situated in the small town of Sant’Andrea in Casale.

It offers fishing in its private lake, and riding stables and a golf course are also available nearby.
