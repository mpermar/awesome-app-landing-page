Description

Featuring 5 outdoor pools and an indoor pool, Palazzo di Varignana Resort and SPA offers modern accommodations in Varignana. With a terrace and a garden, the property also has a fitness center.

The air-conditioned rooms include free Wi-Fi and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. The private bathroom includes a shower.

A sweet and savory breakfast is served daily. There is also a bar and 3 restaurants serving Italian and International cuisine, as well as gourmet food.

Palazzo di Varignana Resort and SPA is a 10-minute drive from Castle San Pietro Terme, while Bologna is 16 mi away.
