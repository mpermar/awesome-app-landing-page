Description

Located in the seaside resort of Lido Di Spina, Michelangelo Resort offers an outdoor swimming pool, a restaurant, and a bar.

Free Wi-Fi and free parking are provided. Resort Michelangelo has apartments, complete with a kitchenette and a dining area.

They also come with a wardrobe and tea/coffee making facilities are available. The restaurant serves traditional food.

Guests can use a nearby private beach area, as well as rent bicycles to explore the surrounding area, which is located in the Delta Del Po Natural Park.

The city of Ravenna is located 30 km from the resort. Bologna Airport is located 100 km away, and Ferrara can be reached in 1 hour by car.
