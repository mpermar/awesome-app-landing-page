Description

The Albergo Hotel Garden Ristorante is situated in a quiet and panoramic position very close to the centre of Tabiano and the thermal baths.

It offers free Wi-Fi and classically furnished rooms with a balcony and air conditioning.

Rooms at the Garden Hotel come with satellite TV, a minibar and a safe. The hotel has a garden with swimming pool and a whirlpool.

The terrace has views of Tabiano Castle and the surrounding hills. A buffet breakfast is provided each morning.
