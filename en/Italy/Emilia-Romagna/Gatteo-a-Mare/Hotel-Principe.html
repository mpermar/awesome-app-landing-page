Description

Just 30 metres from the sea, Hotel Principe offers classic accommodation in Gatteo a Mare.

With a playground, this property is a 5-minute walk from Gatteo a Mare train station.

The air-conditioned rooms include free Wi-Fi and a TV. The private bathroom comes with a shower, hairdryer and free toiletries.

A sweet and savoury breakfast buffet is served daily. There is also a bar and a restaurant serving regional cuisine.

A bus that stops 200 metres from Hotel Principe takes you to Cesena, Rimini and Cesenatico.
