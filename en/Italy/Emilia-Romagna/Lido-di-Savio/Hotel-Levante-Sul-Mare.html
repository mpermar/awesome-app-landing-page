Description

Hotel Levante Sul Mare is located on the seafront in Lido di Savio, 2 km from Milano Marittima.

It offers rooms with balconies, a large, free car park, and a terrace overlooking the garden.

Each air-conditioned room provides satellite TV and a private bathroom with hairdryer.

Some offer views of the Adriatic Sea. The restaurant serves a buffet breakfast, as well as regional and Italian cuisine for lunch and dinner.

A bar is available and packed lunches can be arranged. Wi-Fi is free in public areas.

Mirabilandia Theme Park is 11 km from the Levante Sul Mare Hotel and Le Siepi Race Course is a 5-minute drive.

Cervia Milano Marittima Station is 9 km away and Rimini Airport is 50 minutes by car.
