Description

Set among woods and flowering plants, peaceful Hotel Salus is just 50 metres from the Sant’Andrea Spa.

It offers free Wi-Fi, a traditional restaurant, and air-conditioned rooms with Sky TV.

Rooms at the Salus have views of the piazza or the surrounding woodland. Open in summer, the restaurant at the Salus Hotel serves Italian cuisine and specialties from Parma, such as homemade tortelli and pasta.

The hotel offers a sweet and savoury buffet breakfast with organic produce and fruit.

The nearby Terme Sant’Andrea provides mineral water treatments and massages. Parking spaces are free at the Hotel Salus, and the Fornovo Taro exit of the A15 Motorway is 5 km away.
