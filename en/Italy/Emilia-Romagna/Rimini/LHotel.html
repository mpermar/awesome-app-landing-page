Description

Fully soundproof and air-conditioned, the rooms at the Hotel have a design atmosphere and include a minibar and safe. Served daily from 7 am, breakfast is buffet style.

The property features a spa area with Turkish bath, sauna and a heated outdoor pool with hydro-massage. Sessions in the spa area and massages can be purchased at the front desk. A shared terrace is available.

Rimini Fellini Airport is less than a 10-minute drive from L’Hotel, and the Infermi Hospital is just over half a mile away.

Bellariva is a great choice for travelers interested in fine dining, beach walks and beaches.
