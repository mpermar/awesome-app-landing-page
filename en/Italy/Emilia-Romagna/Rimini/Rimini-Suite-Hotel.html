Description

Located just 90 metres from the coast and its popular beaches, Rimini Suite Hotel features free Wi-Fi throughout.

This hotel set 2 km from Rimini Fiera exhibition centre offers a free swimming pool and contemporary-style rooms.

The stylish rooms have parquet floors and design furnishings. Each comes with a flat-screen TV, air conditioning, and minibar.

Some rooms have disability access, and some have a private hot tub. A continental-style breakfast is provided daily and features sweet and savoury products.

You can relax and unwind in the outdoor pool, and you have discounts at a private beach located nearby.

Rimini Train Station is 4 km from the property, while Riccione is less than a 30-minute drive away.
