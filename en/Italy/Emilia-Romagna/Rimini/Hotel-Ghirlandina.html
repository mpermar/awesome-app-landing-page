Description

Hotel Ghirlandina is a sea-front hotel with free parking and direct access to the beach.

The rooms come with individually controlled air conditioning and a balcony overlooking the sea.

All rooms at the Ghirlandina are en suite and offer a satellite LED TV and a minibar.

The floors are in parquet and the furniture is in white-lacquered wood. The Ghirlandina Hotel has a bar and restaurant with views over the Adriatic Sea.

There is also a 24-hour reception, a garden, a bocce court and a gym. The hotel is a few steps from the nearest thermal baths and a 10-minute walk from the Fiabilandia amusement park.
