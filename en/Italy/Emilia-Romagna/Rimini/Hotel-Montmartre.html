Description

Located opposite Bagno Celso beach in Riminiâ€™s Torre Pedrera area, Hotel Montmartre has a wellness centre and outdoor pool.

Wi-Fi is free, while all rooms feature a balcony. Rooms at the Montmartre are classically furnished and air conditioned.

Each one comes with a minibar and flat-screen TV, and some rooms feature views of the Adriatic Sea.

An extensive buffet breakfast is served until 11:30 and includes bacon, eggs, cereal and much more.

The hotel's bistro is open all day and offers drinks and Emilia Romagna specialities.

After a day along the Romagna Coast, guests can relax with a sauna and Turkish bath.

There is also a fitness centre. The Italy in Miniature theme park is 2 km from the hotel, which provides free outdoor parking upon availability, and guarded parking nearby.
