Description

At the hotel, each room has a terrace with a city view. Rooms include a private bathroom, and certain rooms at Hotel Clerice have a balcony. At the accommodation every room is equipped with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a shared bathroom.

Hotel Clerice offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

Guests at the hotel will be able to enjoy activities in and around Rimini, like cycling.

Rimini/Miramare is 0.9 miles from Hotel Clerice, while Rimini Stadium is 1.4 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Federico Fellini Airport, 1.9 miles from the accommodation.

Bellariva is a great choice for travelers interested in fine dining, beach walks and beaches.
