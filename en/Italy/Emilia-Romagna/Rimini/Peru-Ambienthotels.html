Description

Ambienthotels Peru' is in the Marina Centro area of Rimini, just off the seaside promenade.

It offers modern rooms with free WiFi and a DVD player. All rooms at the Peru' Ambienthotels bed and breakfast feature air conditioning, a minibar, and a radio.

Breakfast is served until 12:00 every day. The Peru' is just 150 metres from the beach in a free-parking area.

A shuttle service connects you to the airport or station, and the Palacongressi Congress Centre is just 2 km away.
