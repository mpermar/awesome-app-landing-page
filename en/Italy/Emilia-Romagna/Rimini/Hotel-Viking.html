Description

A balcony with sea or garden views is standard in every room at the Viking. Each includes satellite TV and a fully equipped private bathroom.

Breakfast is a buffet with cake, croissants and cappuccino coffee. Organic products are available upon request. The restaurant serves traditional Italian dishes and local specialties.

The Italia in Miniatura Theme Park is a 10 minute ride away on the hotel's free bikes. Torre Pedrera Train Station is one mile away, for services to Rimini and Ravenna.

Viserbella is a great choice for travelers interested in kid-friendly beaches, beach walks and the oceanside.
