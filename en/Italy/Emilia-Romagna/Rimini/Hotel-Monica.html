Description

Hotel Monica is in Rimini’s Rivazzurra area, just 150 metres from the beach and a 20-minute walk from Rimini Airport.

It offers free parking, a restaurant specialising in homemade pasta, and free WiFi in public areas.

Set in 2 buildings, rooms at Monica Hotel are air conditioned and come with an LCD TV and a furnished balcony.

They are fitted with parquet floors and wooden furniture. At breakfast, you can enjoy cold cuts and cheese, as well as traditional sweet products and hot drinks.

An American option with eggs and bacon is available upon request. Rimini Miramare Train Station is 800 metres away and links with Rimini’s centre in around 5 minutes.
