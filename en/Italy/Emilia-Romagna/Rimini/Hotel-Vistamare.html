Description

Featuring a seafront location on Rimini Rivazzurra Beach, Hotel Vistamare offers modern rooms with a terrace and a satellite flat-screen TV.

Guests can enjoy a restaurant, free bike rental and free Wi-Fi throughout. The air-conditioned rooms at Hotel Vistamare come with tiled floors and a private bathroom which includes a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Some have a spa bath with hydromassage. Cheeses, pastries and yoghurt are provided daily for breakfast.

The restaurant serves typical Romagnola cuisine. Free private parking is available on site.

The property is located a 6-minute drive from Fiabilandia Theme Park, while Rimini is 6 km away.
