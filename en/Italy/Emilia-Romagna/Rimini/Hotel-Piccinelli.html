Description

This 3-star Hotel Piccinelli is located in Rimini’s centre, just 200 metres from the sandy beaches.

A rich breakfast buffet is served every morning. Free WiFi is available in the public areas.

Rooms come with light-wood furniture. Standard facilities include air conditioning and an LCD TV with satellite channels.

Some rooms also feature a balcony. The hotel’s restaurant serves homemade dishes of Italian and Romagna cuisines.

Guests get discounts at a beach club nearby and at a wellness centre located next door.

Bike rental is free. Bus number 11 stops next to the property and takes you to Rimini Train Station in 5 minutes.
