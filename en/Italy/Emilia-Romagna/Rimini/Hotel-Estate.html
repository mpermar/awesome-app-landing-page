Description

Featuring a private beach area, Hotel Estate is right on the seafront in Rimini's Torre Pedrera area.

You can relax in the outdoor swimming pool and the wellness area with sauna and hot tub on the top floor.

Rooms have bright colour schemes and different furnishings. Each one is air conditioned and includes free Wi-Fi and a view of the promenade or sea.

A sweet Italian breakfast is served each morning. The restaurant on the ground floor is open from mid May until mid September and overlooks the sea.

The Estate Hotel is a family-run property that provides a free shuttle to its car park 600 metres away.

Parasols and deckchairs on the beach are included. You can treat yourself to a massage or take advantage of the transfer to the beach and nearby towns.
