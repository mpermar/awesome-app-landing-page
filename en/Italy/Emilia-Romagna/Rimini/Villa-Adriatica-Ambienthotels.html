Description

Villa Adriatica is an Art Nouveau building on Rimini's Marina Centro seaside promenade, 150 metres from the beach.

The hotel's gardens feature a large swimming pool with hydromassage area. Ambienthotel Villa Adriatica’s air-conditioned rooms reflect the romantic style of the property, being decorated in shades of pink, red and blue.

The rooms in the oldest part of the hotel feature luxurious classical furnishings.

Villa Adriatica’s restaurant is open during summer at both lunch and dinner. It serves traditional local specialities and a choice of meat, fish and vegetarian dishes.

Breakfast buffet includes pastries, cheese, ham and boiled eggs. In summer an entertainment programme, including evening shows and excursions is organised.

Children’s toys and free bikes are available at all times. The hotel also has a library with DVDs available and free Wi-Fi throughout.
