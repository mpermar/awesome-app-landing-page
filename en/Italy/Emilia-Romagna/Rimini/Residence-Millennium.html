Description

Residence Millennium offers modern and bright apartments in Rimini’s Rivazzurra area, 200 metres from the beach and a 5-minute drive from Rimini Airport.

Accommodation is completely air conditioned. Your apartment comes with either a living area or a separate living room, featuring an LCD TV, sofa bed and fully equipped kitchenette.

A communal washing machine is available in the residence. Bed, bath, and kitchen linen is provided and included in the price.

The Millennium is a 10-minute walk from Miramare Station, linking with central Rimini in 5 minutes.
