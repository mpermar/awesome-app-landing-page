Description

One of our top picks in Rimini.Set on the seafront in Rimini Miramare, Hotel Touring features a spa with heated indoor winter pool and an outdoor summer pool. Entertainment is provided for children, and secure garage parking is available on site.

Some rooms are decorated in a classic style, while others have more modern décor. Their LCD TV offers both satellite and pay-per-view channels. Many feature a balcony with sea views.

The Golden restaurant serves both international and regional dishes, with an à-la-carte menu during winter, and a buffet-style formula during summer. Children can enjoy a dedicated menu, and can eat with the entertainment staff members in summer.

Guests can relax at the La Dolce Vita wellness center, which includes a Turkish bath, bio-sauna and chromotherapy treatment, along with a hot tub and relaxation area.

Bicycles can be rented at the 24-hour reception, where free Wi-Fi is also provided. Parking is free during winter, while it comes at extra charge in summer.

The Palacongressi and Palariccione convention centers are both 10 minutes’ drive from the property. Federico Fellini Airport is 1.2 mi away.

Miramare is a great choice for travelers interested in kid-friendly beaches, beach walks and seafood.
