Description

With a beachfront location in Rimini, 2. 1 km from Rimini Fiera, Hotel Madalù features a year-round outdoor pool.

Guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Each room at this hotel is air conditioned and is equipped with a flat-screen TV and free WiFi throughout.

Some units include a seating area for your convenience. A buffet breakfast is offered every morning.

It includes hot drinks, croissants, cold meats and cheese. Marina Centro is 4. 1 km from Hotel Madalù, while Rimini Stadium is 5 km from the property.
