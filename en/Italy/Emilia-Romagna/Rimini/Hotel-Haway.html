Description

Offering a restaurant and free WiFi, Hotel Haway is in Rimini, right opposite the closest sandy beach.

This hotel features functionally furnished en suite rooms. All with a balcony, rooms come with full or partial sea views or views of the shared garden.

Each room includes a desk, air conditioning and a hairdryer. Haway hotel's restaurant proposes 3 options from the menu and a buffet table with vegetables and dessert.

A bar is also available on site. You can rent 1 of the hotel's free bikes to explore the surroundings.
