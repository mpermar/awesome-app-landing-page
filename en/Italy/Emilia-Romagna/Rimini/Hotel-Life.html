Description

Rimini's Hotel Life is set right on the beach and near the exhibition centre. This modern, exclusive, waterfront hotel offers rooms with balcony, free parking, and free Wi-Fi.

The wellness centre is complete with gym and Turkish bath. The rooms of Life Hotel come with parquet floors and are fitted with LCD TV, safe, and minibar.

Some rooms have a modern design. A variety of treatments is available in the wellness centre and also a gym featuring superior equipment.

Breakfast is buffet style. The restaurant serves Italian and international cuisines with homemade pasta and fresh meat and fish.

Children can enjoy safe, attended play areas. The hotel can also accommodate gala events and private parties.
