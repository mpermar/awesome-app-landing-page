Description

Residence Hotel Angeli offers apartments with air conditioning and free Wi-Fi. This modern property is located on Rimini's lively Viale Regina Elena, 50 metres from the beach.

Each apartment offers a fully equipped kitchen, a private balcony, and a living room.

Some apartments offer views of the Adriatic Sea. From mid May until the end of September, rates include 1 parasol and 2 sun loungers on the beach.

Private parking is provided upon availability and is free between October and mid May.
