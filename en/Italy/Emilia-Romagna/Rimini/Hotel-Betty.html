Description

The Betty is surrounded by Rimini's Parco Briolini gardens. This elegant family-run hotel offers sunny rooms and free Wi-Fi.

Rooms are air conditioned and come with a balcony and a private bathroom with a cubicle or wet-room shower.

Breakfast at Hotel Betty includes delicious handmade cakes, yoghurt, a wide selection of cereals, jams, and fresh fruit.

Tasty Mediterranean menus are available for lunch and dinner, with a choice of fish and meat options.
