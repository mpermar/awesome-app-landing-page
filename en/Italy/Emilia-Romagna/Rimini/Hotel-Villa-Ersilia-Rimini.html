Description

Hotel Villa Ersilia Rimini in Rivabella is set only 30 mt from the sandy beach, where guests can enjoy discounted rates.

A sun terrace, traditional restaurant and free private parking are available on site.

Functionally furnished, all rooms at the Villa Ersilia are en suite and come with a safe, desk and tiled floors.

Free Wi-Fi is available in all common areas of the hotel. The property’s restaurant is specialised in regional cuisine and will satisfy any special need.

It hosts daily a sweet buffet breakfast, including homemade cakes and croissants.

Savoury items are available upon request. Rimini Train Station is a 10-minute drive away, and the Federico Fellini airport lies at a distance of 10 km.
