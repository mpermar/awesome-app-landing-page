Description

El Cid enjoys an excellent seafront location on the Torre Pedrera promenade, just North of Rimini. Here you will have free parking, a garden with hot tub, and WiFi access.

El Cid Campeador offers a range of guest rooms and independent apartments. All kinds of accommodations come with a satellite TV and a balcony.

The swimming pool and the apartments are around 500 feet from the main building. Guests have discounts at a local beach.

Rimini's Exhibition Center is just a 10-minute drive from El Cid Campeador.

Torre Pedrera is a great choice for travelers interested in coastal walks, walking and beach walks.
