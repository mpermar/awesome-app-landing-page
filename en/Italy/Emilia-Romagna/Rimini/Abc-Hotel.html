Description

The ABC Hotel is a young and friendly hotel set just 30 metres from the beach in a central but quiet location.

The relaxed atmosphere and personalised service will make you feel immediately at home.

The professional and attentive staff will be delighted to offer recommendations to make sure your stay here in Rivazzurra is unforgettable.

Every morning at the Hotel ABC you can enjoy a rich buffet breakfast. For ldinner, the chef prepares a fine mix of meat and fish dishes accompanied by a buffet of appetizers and vegetables.
