Description

Located on the Miramare seafront, the Due Mari is a hotel with a Romagna restaurant and an outdoor pool.

Wi-Fi, parking and bikes are all free, while rooms have a balcony with Adriatic-Sea or pool views.

Rooms at Hotel Due Mari are classically furnished. Each comes with air conditioning, a minibar and an LCD TV.

A daily sweet and savoury breakfast is provided, including freshly made biscuits, cakes and croissants.

The buffet restaurant serves local and vegetarian dishes. The Fiabilandia theme park is 2 km from the property.
