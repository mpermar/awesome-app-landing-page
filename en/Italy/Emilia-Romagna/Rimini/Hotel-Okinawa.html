Description

The Okinawa is a modern design hotel set in Rimini's peaceful Miramare neighbourhood, 150 metres from the beach.

Rooms come with free WiFi and an LCD TV. All rooms at the Okinawa Hotel are air conditioned and feature a minibar.

They also have a private bathroom with shower cabin. Most feature a balcony. Okinawa's restaurant, Antichi Sapori, specialises in regional, Mediterranean and international cuisine.

It offers a menu of fresh fish and meat, and homemade pasta dishes, which change according to the season.

Rimini Federico Fellini Airport is 1 km away, as is the popular nightclub Altromondo Studios.

Rimini Miramare Train Station is 500 metres from the property. Bus number 11 provides direct links to Rimini Train Station.
