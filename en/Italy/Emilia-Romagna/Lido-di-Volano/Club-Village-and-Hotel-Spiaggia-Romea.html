Description

Club Village is in Lido Delle Nazioni, in the middle of the Po Delta National Park, surrounded by woods, lakes and lagoons.

The Adriatic Sea is a short walk away. Club Village & Hotel Spiaggia Romea offers private beaches on the lake and the sea.

There are numerous sports facilities including 3 outdoor pools and various sport fields, including a tennis court.

Private parking is free at the Spiaggia Romea Hotel Club. This resort village also provides entertainment facilities, a mini club, horse riding facilities, bike rentals, a bar, a minimarket and a restaurant-pizzeria.
