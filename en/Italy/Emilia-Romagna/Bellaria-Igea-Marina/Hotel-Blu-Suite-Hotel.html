Description

One of our top picks in Bellaria-Igea Marina.With its 1148 ft² wellness center, a heated outdoor pool, charming gardens, and well-equipped private beach, Blu Suite Hotel is ideal for a relaxing holiday on Bellaria Igea Marina. Rooms include a parasol, 2 sun loungers at the private beach.

Soak up the sun on the sun loungers provided by Blu Suite's pool, which features warm water and hydromassage corners.

Kids have their own pool area and an outdoor playground, as well as indoor playrooms, a beach playground and a free mini club. A restaurant for kids with special menus is also available. Entertainment activities are organized from June to September.

Offering unlimited access, the wellness center features a sauna, a gym, a hot tub, a Turkish bath, chromotherapy, and relaxation and ayurveda massage rooms or beauty treatments.

All rooms here have modern décor and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Suites also come with large balconies, and the presidential suite provides utter luxury, boasting indoor and outdoor whirlpools, and a huge sea-view terrace. Every room and suite has a kitchenette, which can be used for free to heat up meals for babies.

A generous breakfast complete with organic products and gluten-free items is served. Snacks can be enjoyed by the pool, as well as à-la-carte dinners.

A free shuttle service to Rimini exhibition center, 5 mi away, during major trade fairs is provided. The hotel provides free guarded parking on site. Bikes can be rented on site.
