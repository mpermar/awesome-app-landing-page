Description

All guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Free WiFi is accessible to all guests, while certain rooms are equipped with a balcony. Guest rooms at Hotel Augusta feature air conditioning and a desk.

A buffet breakfast is served at the property.

The accommodation has a sun terrace.

Rimini is 8 miles from Hotel Augusta. The nearest airport is Federico Fellini Airport, 12 miles from the property.
