Description

Just 50 metres from the beach, Hotel Villa Claudia is located in Bellaria-Igea Marina.

It offers free WiFi, free bike rental, and a traditional restaurant also serving vegetarian and vegan options.

Featuring a balcony, the classic-style rooms here come with air conditioning, a TV and a safe.

The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and shower. A sweet buffet breakfast is served daily including homemade cakes.

The on-site restaurant specialises in regional cuisine with a range of local organic products.

With free private parking, Hotel Claudia is 1. 5 km from Bellaria Train Station. Buses stopping 200 metres from the property link with Rimini, 18 km away.
