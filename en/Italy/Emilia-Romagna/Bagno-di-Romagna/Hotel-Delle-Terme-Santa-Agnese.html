Description

Set in the spa town of Bagno Di Romagna, Hotel Delle Terme Santa Agnese offers free indoor and outdoor thermal pools.

Emilia-Romagna specialities are served in its Sapori di Romagna restaurant. All rooms are elegant and spacious with an LCD TV and minibar.

The hotel also includes a TV lounge, mini cinema, and bar. Terme Santa Agnese also features a sun terrace and free bike rental.

The wellness area includes a hot tub and sauna, and offers a wide range of relaxing massages and beauty treatments.

The hotel is surrounded by the greenery of the Apennines. Parco Nazionale delle Foreste Casentinesi nearby is perfect for trekking and cycling.
