Description

Hotel Ville Panazza offers peaceful accommodation in 2 historical buildings, with beautiful gardens and 2 swimming pools.

You will be within easy reach of Ravenna and Bologna by motorway. Rooms at Ville Panazza are furnished with taste and come with a flat-screen TV, minibar and air conditioning.

Free WiFi is available at reception and in guest rooms located in the main building.

Hotel Ville Panazza is set in a former convent, dating back to the 17th century. It is surrounded by beautiful gardens, and a gym and meeting facilities are also available.

The hotel is a 10-minute drive from the Imola exit of the A14 motorway. The Autodromo Enzo e Dino Ferrari car racing track is a 15-minute drive away.
