Description

Hotel Maddalena features free bikes, bar, a shared lounge and garden in Marina di Ravenna.

This 2-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk and luggage storage space. This property offers both free WiFi and private parking free of charge.

At the hotel, every room has a desk. Featuring a private bathroom with a bidet and free toiletries, rooms at Hotel Maddalena also provide guests with a garden view.

The units feature a wardrobe. Continental and buffet breakfast options are available every morning at the accommodation.

Guests at Hotel Maddalena will be able to enjoy activities in and around Marina di Ravenna, like cycling.

Ravenna is 12 km from the hotel, while Cesenatico is 40 km away. The nearest airport is Federico Fellini International Airport, 65 km from Hotel Maddalena.
