Description

Immersed in the Delta del Po's pine woods, Terme Beach Resort is set right on the beach between Marina di Ravenna and Punta Marina.

Private parking and WiFi are free. The spacious rooms have contemporary d√©cor and a flat-screen TV with Sky channels.

Some rooms include a hydromassage bath and pine wood views, while others have panoramic windows and direct beach access.

The large white sandy beach in front of the property is equipped with sun loungers and parasols.

You can book access to the wellness centre on site, which is complete with a thermal pool and sauna.

Breakfast at Terme Beach Resort is a simple buffet with sweet pastries and cereals.

The Marlin Beach Restaurant serves traditional Mediterranean dishes. This property is intended for guests using the Terme di Punta Marina spa.

Ravenna's historic centre is 10 km away, and it is a 25-minute drive to Mirabilandia Theme Park.
