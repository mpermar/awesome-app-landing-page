Description

Immersed in the greenery at 18 km from Rimini, Spa Hotel Villa Leri overlooks Lake Montecolombo.

It offers an outdoor pool, a wellness centre and rooms with classic-style d√©cor. Fitted with a satellite flat-screen TV, rooms include air conditioning, minibar and a bathroom with hairdryer.

Some also offer a balcony with lake views. Villa Leri provides supervised car parking for free.

It also includes a five-a-side football pitch and a tennis court. The restaurant serves Mediterranean cuisine and vegetarian dishes.

A generous buffet breakfast, featuring organic products, homemade desserts and a selection of cheeses, is served in the elegant dining room.

The hotel is 15 km from the A14 motorway and 15 km from the City of San Marino. Federico Fellini Airport is a 20-minute drive away.
