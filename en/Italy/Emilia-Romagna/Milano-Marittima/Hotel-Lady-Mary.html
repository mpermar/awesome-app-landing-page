Description

With a beachfront location, Hotel Lady Mary offers accommodation with free WiFi in Milano Marittima.

Guests enjoy and indoor and outdoor pool, as well as a restaurant. Offering free cinema and football Sky channels, each room and suite features a balcony and sea views.

Some have a spa bath and seating area. Guests enjoy Italian breakfast served daily, an on-site restaurant and bar.

A spa and wellness centre is also featured, massages and beauty treatments can be booked.

Hotel Lady Mary is a 20-minute drive from Cesenatico, while Rimini is 40 km away.
