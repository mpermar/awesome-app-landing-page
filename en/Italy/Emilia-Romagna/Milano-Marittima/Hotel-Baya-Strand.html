Description

At the hotel, every room comes with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Guest rooms have a closet.

A buffet or Full English/Irish breakfast is served at the property. There's an on-site restaurant, which serves Italian cuisine.

Hotel Baya Strand has a seasonal outdoor pool. You can play tennis at this 3-star hotel.

The nearest airport is Federico Fellini Airport, 22 miles from the accommodation.
