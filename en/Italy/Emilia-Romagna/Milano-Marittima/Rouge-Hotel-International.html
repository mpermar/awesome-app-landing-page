Description

Located a few steps from the sandy beach in Milano Marittima, Rouge Hotel International offers an outdoor pool, a free spa and wellness centre, and air-conditioned accommodation with a sea-view balcony.

Free WiFi is available. Rooms at the 4-star Hotel Rouge International all come with a satellite flat-screen TV, minibar and safe.

The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries. A sweet Italian buffet breakfast is served daily.

Guests can relax in the sauna, work out in the fitness centre or rent free bikes.

Guests enjoy discounted rates at partner boutiques nearby. The property provides free private parking.

The hotel is 3 km from Cervia - Milano Marittima Train Station, with links to Ancona and Rimini.
