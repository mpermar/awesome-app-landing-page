Description

Set directly on the seaside in Milano Marittima, Hotel Solemare offers a covered and heated outdoor swimming pool, a mini-club for children, and a garden.

Free WiFi is available. All rooms at the Solemare are elegant, spacious and well furnished.

All of them offer a private balcony, and some overlook the Adriatic Sea. Breakfast is served in the breakfast room or in the garden.

The international buffet includes fresh bread, biscuits and homemade cakes, fruit, eggs, cold cuts, cheeses and bacon.
