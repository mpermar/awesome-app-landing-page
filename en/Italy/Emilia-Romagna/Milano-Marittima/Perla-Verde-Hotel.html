Description

The Perla Verde is just 50 metres from the beach in Milano Marittima. It offers a modern wellness centre, a garden and restaurant, free WiFi is available throughout.

Hotel Perla Verde offers a range of air-conditioned rooms and suites. Most rooms have a balcony, and some also enjoy views of the sea.

The Perla Verde Hotel's wellness centre includes a sauna, hot tub and steam bath.

There is a small pool on the 6th floor with counter-current swimming and hydromassage.

Breakfast is served buffet-style and includes sweet and savoury, organic and gluten-free items.

The Verdepuro restaurant is open at lunch and dinner and offers international and regional dishes, including homemade pasta and desserts.

Free bicycles are available at reception. During summer there is a kids' club and daily entertainment for adults and children on site.
