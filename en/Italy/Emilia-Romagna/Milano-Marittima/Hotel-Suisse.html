Description

Located on Milano Marittima's seafront, the Suisse is a small, intimate hotel offering a Romagna restaurant with a sun terrace.

Parking, Wi-Fi and bike hire are all free. Rooms at Hotel Suisse are air conditioned and come with an LCD TV.

The Suisse Hotel's restaurant serves local cuisine together with a selection of homemade desserts.

The daily breakfast includes organic fruit and yoghurt. The Suisse is just 400 metres from the centre of Milano Marittima.

The Byzantine city of Ravenna is 24 km from the hotel, and Rimini Federico Fellini airport is a 50-minute drive away.
