Description

Near the beach and Milano Marittima, Koko Hotel offers modern rooms with independent entrances, free private parking spaces in front of every room, and an outdoor pool.

Free Wi-Fi is available. Rooms are set in separate buildings on more than 10. 000 m² of parkland at Koko Hotel and Residence.

You will have an electronic key, en suite bathroom, air conditioning and satellite TV.

In the garden you will find a pool with children's area. In addition to an on-site bar, Koko Hotel has a free breakfast buffet served from 07:30 until 11:30.

The property is 200 metres from Le Siepi di Cervia hyppodrome and 500 metres from Cervia Golf Club.
