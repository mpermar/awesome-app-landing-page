Description

Hotel Sahara is surrounded by a quiet centuries-old pine wood. Enjoy the lively Milano Marittima and then retreat at this cosy hotel only a few steps from the Adriatic Sea.

WiFi is free. Hotel Sahara offers a friendly and comfortable environment for a pleasant stay.

Enjoy sociable moments, reading a book or sipping a cocktail in the bright and spacious halls and in the lounges and TV room.

You can also cool off in the spacious garden, or have a swim in the outdoor heated swimming pools with hydromassage.

Guests can also enjoy discounts at a nearby partner beach. All the rooms in the Sahara have been completely renovated.

They come with air conditioning, a satellite TV, and a private balcony. Hotel Sahara is also proud of the genuine cuisine you can enjoy at its restaurant.

You can choose amongst a variety of regional and national dishes for lunch and dinner.
