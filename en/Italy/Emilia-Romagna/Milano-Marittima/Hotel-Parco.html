Description

Offering a restaurant, free bikes and free WiFi, Hotel Parco is located in Milano Marittima.

Rooms here have contemporary-style décor and a flat-screen TV. Rooms at the Parco Hotel are air conditioned.

Each has a minibar and a private bathroom with hairdryer, and most also feature a balcony.

You can enjoy a daily sweet and savoury breakfast in the garden or in the breakfast room.

A shared garden is available, and drinks are sold at the on-site bar. The hotel lies 2 km from Cervia-Milano Marittima Train Station.
