Description

Hotel Montepizzo is set in the centre of Lizzano in Belvedere, 10 km from the ski resort of Corno alle Scale.

It features a wellness centre, a garden, and a restaurant serving typical and international dishes.

Parking is free. With free Wi-Fi, the Montepizzo's rooms have a flat-screen TV, elegant tiled floors and classic furnishings.

Buffet breakfast is available each morning from 07:00 to 10:00. The wellness centre on the 3rd floor is complete with free fitness area.

You can also book the Turkish bath and hydromassage, or a massage session. Porretta Train Station is 12 km away.
