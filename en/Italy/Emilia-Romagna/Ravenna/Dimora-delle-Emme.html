Description

Featuring a garden, Dimme delle Emme is located in Ravenna, 1.2 km from Piazza del Popolo.

The property offers free WiFi, and Ravenna Train Station is 12 minutes away by bus.

With air conditioning, rooms have air conditioning. They come with a flat-screen TV, wardrobe and desk.

Guests at Dimora delle Emme can enjoy a sweet breakfast buffet including coffee and homemade cakes, with cold cuts and cheese on request.

Several restaurants are in Ravenna’s historic centre, within a 10-minute walk of the property.
