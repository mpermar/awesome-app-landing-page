Description

Each room at this bed and breakfast is air conditioned and features a flat-screen TV. Certain units feature a seating area for your convenience. Some rooms include views of the garden or city. Every room comes with a private bathroom. Extras include slippers and free toiletries.

You will find babysitting service at the property. A sweet Italian breakfast including hot drinks, home-made cakes and organic produce is served daily. Gluten-free items are also available.

The bed and breakfast also offers free use of bicycles. Mausoleo di Galla Placidia is 1,300 feet from Villa Noctis, while Basilica of Sant'Apollinare Nuovo is 1,950 feet from the property. The nearest airport is Federico Fellini International Airport, 34 mi from Villa Noctis.
