Description

Set in its 6000-m² park with gazebo, Agriturismo il Brugnolo offers free bikes and classic-style apartments with TV and views of the shared garden.

This country house has a countryside location 13 km from Reggio Emilia. All air-conditioned, the units have a kitchen or kitchenette and a bathroom with hairdryer.

The kitchen/kitchenette can be provided with pre-packaged products for your breakfast upon request.

Agriturismo Il Brugnolo is set in a beatifully renovated stone-walled farmhouse from the 19th century.

You will find shared BBQ and laundry facilities and lounge area here. This property is a 5-minute drive from the village of Scandiano, while Parma is 47 km from the property.
