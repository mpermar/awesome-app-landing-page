Description

Located 3 km from Castelvetro di Modena, Agriturismo Podere Diamante is a working farm surrounded by vineyards.

It offers free Wi-Fi, an Emilia restaurant, and country-style rooms with air conditioning.

Rooms at the Podere Diamante have exposed-beam ceilings and parquet floors. Each comes with a private bathroom.

A buffet breakfast is provided daily. The weekend restaurant specialises in local cuisine, and the chef can cater for special dietary requirements on request.
