Description

Sommer Panzió is located in the small town Balatonföldvár, 400 metres from the shore of Lake Balaton.

Set in a beautiful garden it features a restaurant and a seasonal outdoor pool, both available in the summer season.

Hungarian cuisine is served in the restaurant or on the covered terrace. A children’s menu is also available.

All rooms are comfortably furnished and have a mosquito net and satellite TV. Although not available in the rooms, guests have free access to Wi-Fi in the lobby.

Guests can rent bicycles on site or play table tennis. A children’s playground is available in the garden.

Sommer Panzió is only a few steps away from the centre of Balatonföldvár. Shops and restaurants can be found there.
