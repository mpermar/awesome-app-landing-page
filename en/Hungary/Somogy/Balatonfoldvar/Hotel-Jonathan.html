Description

Hotel Jonathan in Balatonföldvár is situated 400 metres from the beach on the Balaton Lake and offers wellness facilities, a fitness room, free Wi-Fi and free parking.

All rooms and suites at Hotel Jonathan have a balcony and are equipped with a cable TV.

Hungarian and international cuisine can be enjoyed in the restaurant and kids can have fun in the play area.

A sauna, a hot tub, a solarium, fitness facilities and massage services are available.
