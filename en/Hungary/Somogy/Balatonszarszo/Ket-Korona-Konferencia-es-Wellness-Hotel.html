Description

Set 800 metres from the southers shores of Lake Balaton, Két Korona Hotel enjoys a quiet location in the popular resort of Balatonszárszó.

It offers a luxurious spa area. Half-board comprises a breakfast buffet and a dinner with Hungarian and international dishes.

Dietetic meals will be provided on request. All rooms of the Két Korona Konferencia és Wellness Hotel provide a balcony, en-suite bathrooms with bathrobes and cable TV.

Some rooms are air-conditioned. The spa area consists of an indoor and a seasonal outdoor pool, hot tub, steam baths and several saunas.

Access to the spa facilities is free for Két Korona Hotel guests. Massages will be provided on request.

The deckchairs in the surrounding garden can also be used free of charge. Free parking is available.

Complimentary shuttle service is available on request to and from the bus and train stations.

The Két Korona Konferencia és Wellness Hotel is one kilometre from the M7 motorway, the capital Budapest can be reached within one hour car ride.
