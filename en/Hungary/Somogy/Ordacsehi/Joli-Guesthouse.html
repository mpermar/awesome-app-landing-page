Description

Featuring free WiFi throughout the property, Joli Guesthouse offers accommodation in Ordacsehi.

Free private parking is available on site. Enjoy a cup of coffee or tea from your terrace or balcony.

The rooms are fitted with a private bathroom equipped with a shower. A flat-screen TV with satellite channels is provided.

A range of activities is offered in the area, such as horse riding and fishing. Hévíz is 33 km from Joli Guesthouse, while Siófok is 37 km from the property.

Csisztapuszta Thermal Bath is set 16 km away. Joli Guesthouse invites guests who book for at least 1 week for a free traditional Hungarian dinner.
