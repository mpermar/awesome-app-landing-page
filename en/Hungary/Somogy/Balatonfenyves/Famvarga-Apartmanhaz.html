Description

Famvarga Apartmanház is located in Balatonfenyves, 800 metres from the south shore of Lake Balaton and offers free WiFi and a garden with terrace and barbecue facilities.

Each accommodation unit is fitted with a flat-screen TV, a kitchenette, a sofa and a bathroom.

Guests find a grocery store and a supermarket 300 metres away. The Balatonfenyves Train Station is 800 metres away and it is possible to rent bicycles at Famvarga Apartmanház.
