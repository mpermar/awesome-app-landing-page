Description

Situated in the green and peaceful garden suburbs of Budapest, Le Rose Hotel is conveniently located a 15-minute drive from the centre and 6 km from the M0 motorway exit.

It offers a breakfast buffet and free parking. Free WiFi is available. The spacious and bright rooms have either air conditioning or balcony.

All of them features an LCD flat-screen cable TV and a bathroom. Guests can relax at the comfortable bar of the Rose with a drink.

Use of the on-site sauna and swimming pool is free of charge. There is a playground for children in the garden.

A suburban train station and a public bus stop leading into the centre are only a few steps away.
