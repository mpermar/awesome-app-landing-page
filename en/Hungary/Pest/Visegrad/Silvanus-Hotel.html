Description

Panoramic Danube views can be enjoyed at the Silvanus Hotel. It is located on a hillside, just 300 metres from the Visegrád Citadel, and provides rooms with free Wi-Fi.

Guests can relax in the spacious wellness area. Each room comes with a minibar, a TV and a bathroom with amenities and a shower or a tub.

Darts, billiards and nine-pin bowling facilities are provided on site. Guests can work out in the Spa or sunbathe on the large terrace with views of the river.

The wellness area comprises a sauna, steam bath, foot bath, salt chamber, aroma chamber, swimming pools, and a hot tub.

Massages can be requested at Silvanus Hotel. Guests can have lunch and dinner at the award-winning restaurant and enjoy the daily buffet breakfast.

An airport shuttle service is provided on site. For the children’s entertainment, a playground is available.

The hotel also offers facilities for disabled guests. The Royal Palace in Visegrád is 4 km away and the city of Szentendre can be reached within a 25-minute drive.
