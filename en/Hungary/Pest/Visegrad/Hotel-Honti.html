Description

Boasting a wellness area and views of the medieval Upper Castle, Hotel Honti is quietly set in the centre of Visegrád.

It features free WiFi, air-conditioned rooms, a spa area and an à-la-carte restaurant serving Hungarian, international and vegetarian dishes.

Overlooking the hills, all rooms are fitted with wooden furnishings and an LCD TV.

A safe, fridge and a private bathroom with shower are also provided. The wellness area includes a free indoor and seasonal outdoor pool, sauna and hot tub.

Guests can also relax in the garden with a terrace. Various massage treatments can be organised upon request.

Other activities include table tennis. The Danube Riverbank, with numerous fishing, hiking and cycling opportunities, is 300 metres away.

Horseback riding facilities in Gizella-telep are within a 1-km reach. Nagyvillám Ski Slopes are at a distance of 2.

5 km. Esztergom, featuring the biggest basilica in Europe, is 25 km away. Slovakia’s border can be reached within 18 km.
