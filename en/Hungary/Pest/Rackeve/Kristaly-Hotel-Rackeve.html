Description

Right next to the Danube, Hotel Termálkristály Aqualand is set within the Aqualand Thermal and Adventure Bath Complex in Ráckeve and Guests receive a daily entrance ticket to the Aqualand Waterpark.

The property also offers free Wi-Fi and elegant accommodation units. The on-site restaurant with panoramic views serves Hungarian cuisine and international dishes.

All accommodation units comprise an LCD satellite TV and a balcony. Each unit has a private bathroom with a bathtub or a shower.

A garden with a sun terrace is at guests’ disposal. The Aqualand spa and wellness centre consists of indoor and outdoor pools, steam room, saunas and sunbeds.

The healing thermal water is suitable for various treatments. The surrounding area provides numerous fishing, horseback riding and boat trip opportunities.

Quad and go-kart facilities are 8 km away in Kiskunlacháza. The popular market on Danube Bank Corso is open every Wednesday and Saturday.

The Baroque Savoy Castle is within a 1-km reach. A train stop is 350 metres from the Termálkristály Aqualand.
