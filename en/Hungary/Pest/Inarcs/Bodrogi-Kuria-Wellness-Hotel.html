Description

Surrounded by a large pine forest, the castle-style Bodrogi Kúria Wellness Hotel in Inárcs is located close to the M5 motorway, 30 km south of Budapest.

It features indoor and outdoor pools, air-conditioned rooms and free WiFi. The Wellness Hotel Bodrogi Kúria is located close to a bathing lake and offers various spa facilities on site.

These include a Finnish sauna, an infra-red cabin and a hot tub. Fine Hungarian specialities and international cuisine including grilled dishes during the summer months are served in the restaurant.
