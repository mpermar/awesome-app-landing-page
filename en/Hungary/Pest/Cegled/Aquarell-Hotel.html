Description

Offering a free wellness area with an indoor pool and a sauna, the Aquarell Hotel is situated in the southern part of Pest county in Cegléd.

Free parking and WiFi is available in all areas. The hotel offers air-conditioned rooms each come with a flat-screen cable TV as well as a private bathroom.

Aquarell Hotel also features a fine restaurant with 160 seats, a bar and a conference hall with 120 seats, which can be divided into 3 sections.
