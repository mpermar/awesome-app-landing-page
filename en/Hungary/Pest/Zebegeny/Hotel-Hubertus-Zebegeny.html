Description

Hotel Hubertus Zebegeny is situated in a quiet area at the Danube Bend and it offers free WiFi, a garden with free barbecue facilities and free air conditioning.

Free parking spaces are available on site. Zebegeny centre is 800 metres away. The units at the Hotel Hubertus come with a private bathroom and a refrigerator.

They also feature a balcony. Guests can enjoy a daily breakfast buffet and dine at the on-site à-la-carte restaurant with a panoramic terrace.

Massages are available on request. Hiking and cycling tours in the area can be organised directly at the property.

In addition, canoeing and horse riding is also offered. Zebegeny Train Station is located 700 metres from the property and Visegrád Castle is a 14-minute drive away.
