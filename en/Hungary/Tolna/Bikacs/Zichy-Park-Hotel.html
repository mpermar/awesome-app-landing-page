Description

Set within a large park near Bikács, the Zichy Park Hotel offers wine tasting in its own wine cellar, a fishing lake and a restaurant serving Hungarian dishes and regional specialities.

Free Wi-Fi and free parking are provided. The hotel houses wellness facilities such as a sauna, swimming pool and indoor hot tub.

Massages and a sun bed are available as well. Guests can play tennis or billiards on site, and there are horseback riding possibilities.

The Zichy offers en-suite rooms with air conditioning, a minibar and safety deposit box.

Guests can visit the village of Györköny with wine cellars, 9 km away, or the 13th-century castle of Simontornya, 22 km away.
