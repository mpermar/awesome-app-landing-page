Description

Fried Castle Hotel and Restaurant in Simontornya, 40 km east of the famous Balaton Lake, is set amid vineyards and features an own winery, a French park, and outdoor pool, a children's playground and a pet zoo.

The rooms are fitted with original oak parquet floors and offer a TV, air conditioning, a minibar and a bathroom with shower or bath tub, hairdryer, bathrobes and free toiletries.

Guests can enjoy meals in the on-site restaurant and also relax in the spa area with sauna, hot tub and steam bath.
