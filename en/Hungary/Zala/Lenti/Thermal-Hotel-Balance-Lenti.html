Description

Offering direct access to Lenti Thermal Bath and Szent György Energy Parki, Thermal Hotel Balance Lenti is set in Lenti.

The hotel has a restaurant, bar and conference centre. Free private parking is available on site.

Free WiFi access is provided throughout the property. All rooms have a private bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer.

A minibar, a safe and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels are also available in eac room.

You will find a hairdresser's and a beauty salon at the property. Guests can access the Thermal Bath via a covered corridor.

The area is popular for cycling and hiking. The hotel also offers free use of bicycles.

Moravske-Toplice is 25 km from Thermal Hotel Balance Lenti, while Sveti Martin na Muri is 17 km away.
