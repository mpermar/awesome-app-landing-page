Description

In the middle of a 17 hectare estate, close to the Lake Héviz, this 4-story hotel offers traditional Hungarian Hévizi treatments, a beauty and hair salon.

Guests can enjoy treatments, such as mud beauty therapies and massages, as well as Ayurveda, Shiatsu and Yomeiho.

Several pools, a hot tub and various saunas are available. Sport facilities like a fitness center, a driving range or a tennis court are among the many possibilities.

All air-conditioned rooms have a balcony and a minibar. The bathrooms are equipped with bathrobes and a hairdryer.

Upon request guests can book anti-allergy and wheelchair-accessible rooms. The Lotus Therme is located 1.

4 km from the largest thermal lake in Europe. Lake Balaton can be reached within 4 km.
