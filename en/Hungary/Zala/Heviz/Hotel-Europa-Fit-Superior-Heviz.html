Description

Featuring a wide range of pools, saunas, treatments and events, Hotel Europa Fit Superior enjoys a quiet location 750 metres from the Heviz thermal lake.

Free WiFi access is available in all areas. All rooms have a balcony or terrace, air-conditioning, a satellite TV and a private bathroom including a hairdryer.

Guests can enjoy a welcome drink and a healthy buffet breakfast. Typical Hungarian soups are served for lunch (except from June until August).

The Restaurant Citrus offers traditional Hungarian fare, international cuisine and dietetic choices.

In summer, the Mandarin Bar & Café and the Acapulco Barbecue Garden and Coctail Bar awaits guests.

There is a seasonal pool for children, a play area, chairs and baby beds. Baby sitting service is available for an additional charge.

Hotel Europa Fit Superior Hévíz also offers dentist services and beauty treatments for a charge.

Private parking is available on site for a surcharge. Hévíz-Balaton Airport is set 13 km away.
