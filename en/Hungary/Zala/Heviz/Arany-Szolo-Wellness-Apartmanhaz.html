Description

Featuring an outdoor pool, free WiFi and air-conditioning in all apartments, Arany Szőlő Wellness Apartmanház is 1 km from Hévíz's thermal lake and from the bus station.

Private parking is possible on site. All apartments are well-equipped and come with cable TV, a DVD-Player and a terrace or a balcony.

Some unit provide views over the pool. Children can play on the playground in the garden, which also comes with barbecue facilities.

A hot tub and a sauna are also available and a washing machine can be used for free.

The city of Keszthely and its Baroque-style Festetics Castle are 9 km from the Golden Vineyard.
