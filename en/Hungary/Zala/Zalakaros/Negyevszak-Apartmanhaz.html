Description

This cottage-style property is only 1. 2 kilometres away from the thermal bath in Zalakaros.

Rooms here have a well equipped kitchen and a covered terrace by the garden. Accommodation at Négyévszak Apartmanház is bright and includes tiled floors and wooden ceilings.

Each room has a living area with sofas. Guests can dine in the room or on the terrace, where garden furniture is provided.

Lake Balaton is 32 km away. Guests can also visit the Balaton Uplands, a protected national park that extends along the lake.

FlyBalaton Airport is 20 km away and the Négyévszak offers on-demand airport transfers.
