Description

Only 20 metres from the thermal baths, Hotel Forrás Zalakaros features air-conditioned rooms with a balcony, as well as a restaurant and a large garden area.

WiFi in all areas is for free. All accommodation units are equipped with a TV and a fridge.

Each unit contains a private bathroom, while some also have cooking facilities. Daily breakfast and traditional Hungarian cuisine is served at the Forrás hotel.

Other on-site facilities include bicycle rentals and massage treatments, both available for a charge.

Several hiking tracks starts in the vicinity. A bus stop is within 400 metres, while private parking is possible on site for an additional fee.
