Description

The guest-centric and family-friendly hotel is located in the centre of Zalakaros, right next to Zalakaros Thermal and Medicinal Spa.

Guests receive a free entry ticket and they also have direct access to the bath through a covered and heated corridor.

The property welcomes guests with free WiFi, a friendly staff and exceptional gastronomy.

The hotel has standard, connecting rooms and family rooms, plus a superior suite with a hot tub.

All rooms feature air-conditioning, a balcony, minibar, a flat-screen TV, telephone and a safe.

The bathrooms are equipped with a spacious shower or a bath. Free toiletries, bathrobes and bath towels are provided.

Extra bed can be placed in all rooms. Children below 6 years can stay at the property free of charge and a baby cot is provided as well.

Park Kitchen, the restaurant of the hotel, serves rich buffet breakfast in the morning and Hungarian and international dishes in the evening.

Guests can rent bicycles and explore Kis-Balaton, or visit the adventure park and the buffalo reserve.
