Description

The 4-star MenDan Hotel offers guests a perfect atmosphere, next to medical attendance and a lot of therapy and beauty services.

The wellness facilities include several indoor pools, various saunas, a steam bath and a hot tub.

The hotel is located in the very city centre of the Health Resort Zalakaros, only a couple of kilometres away from the famous Lake Balaton.

Hotel MenDan offers elegant rooms with a minibar and a safe. Each comes with a private bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer.

Local specialities are served at the on-site restaurant. There is also a cafe and a pool bar, where you can enjoy snacks and drinks.
