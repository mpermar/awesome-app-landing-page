Description

The Hunguest Hotel Freya is located in a well-groomed park in the centre of Zalakaros, next to the Zalakaros Thermal Bath with various indoor and outdoor pools with water slides.

The property provides number of tickets to the Zalakaros Thermal Bath for the days stayed at the property.

All rooms have a balcony and a minibar. Free WiFi is available in all areas. Hungarian specialities and international cuisine is served in the restaurant.

A wide range of therapeutic treatments based on the medicinal waters are available.

Guest can reach the spa comfortable through a passage. The Zalakaros Bus Station is 50 metres away.
