Description

Surrounded by vineyards, this family-run hotel is situated only 1 km from the M7 highway, near the Csónakázó-tó boating lake in Nagykanizsa.

Vineyard Inn hotel features spa and wellness facilities and a gourmet restaurant recognized by the Gault Millau restaurant guide.

All rooms are air-conditioned and well-equipped and feature a TV, a minibar and a private bathroom.

Vineyard Inn's gourmet restaurant has been awarded the Grand Prix of the Hungarian National Gastronomy Society each year since 1999.

It serves refined international and Hungarian cuisine from only the freshest, locally sourced ingredients.

The spa area offers a Finnish sauna, a steam cabin, a hot tub and tepidarium, all for surcharge.

Take a walk in the scenic surroundings of the nearby lake, rent a boat or enjoy fishing or bathing.
