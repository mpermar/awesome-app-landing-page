Description

Situated 250 metres from Lake Balaton’s north shore, Laroba Hotel offers contemporary rooms with free Wi-Fi and parking.

It has a sauna and a restaurant serving traditional delicacies. Set with simplistic furnishings, each room is air-conditioned and has satellite TV, a mini bar and bathrobes.

Most rooms have a balcony or a large terrace. Relaxation facilities include a solarium and steam baths.

A wide range of local cuisine is served in the hotel’s restaurant. Laroba Hotel is a short walk away from Alsóörs Railway Station.
