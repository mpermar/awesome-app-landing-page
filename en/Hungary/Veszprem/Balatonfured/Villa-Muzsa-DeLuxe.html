Description

Situated in the Old Town of Balatonfüred, the Villa Múzsa DeLuxe is only 200 metres away from the promenade along Lake Balaton.

It offers air-conditioned rooms and apartments, free WiFi, free bike rental, kid´s playing room and free parking.

The accommodation units all come with a private bathroom with bath or shower, while apartments furthermore have a fully equipped kitchen.

You can also make use of the sauna available for free. The city park with its playground is situated right next to the building.

Shops and restaurants are only 50 metres away from the Villa Múzsa DeLuxe. Free transfer is provided on request to and from Balatonfüred Bus and Train Stations.
