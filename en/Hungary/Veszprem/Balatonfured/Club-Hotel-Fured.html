Description

Enjoying a waterfront location in Balatonfüred, on the shore of Lake Balaton, Club Hotel Füred features a private beach area, and a spa area including an indoor pool, a Finnish sauna, a steam cabin and an infrared cabin.

Each air-conditioned room at the Club Hotel has its own balcony or terrace and is fitted with a refrigerator, cable TV and a bathroom.

Some rooms are located in the main building, while others are in an annex where the restaurant and the spa are located.

Free WiFi is provided. A restaurant serving Hungarian cuisine can be found on site and a shopping centre is 300 metres away.

The centre of Balatonfüred and the Tagore Promenade are 2 km away. Guests can relax on the sun terrace in the garden or enjoy the various leisure facilities like table tennis, billiard, mini golf, bowling or tennis.
