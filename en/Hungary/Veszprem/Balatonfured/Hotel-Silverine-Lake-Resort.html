Description

Hotel Silverine Lake Resort in Balatonfüred offers you a restaurant with a lake-view terrace, a seasonal beach bar, a spa area including an indoor pool and its own marina.

All rooms are air-conditioned and offer free WiFi internet access. Typical Hungarian specialities and international cuisine is served on the restaurant and on the terrace with panoramic views over Lake Balaton and the pool.

Every morning you can enjoy a rich buffet breakfast. Wine tastings are offered at an additional cost.

Spa facilities include a Finnish sauna, an infra-red sauna, a pool with a children's section, a hot tub and many more.

Massages and beauty treatments are available as well. You can also rent bicycles on site.

The Tagore Promenade can be found right in front of the Silverine Lake Resort. The Balaton Golf Club is 5 km away and the picturesque Tihany peninsula is 4 km away.
