Description

The family-friendly Hotel Pangea is set in the area of Nyíregyháza Sóstó Zoo, directly next to Sóstó Lake and it offers guests free unlimited zoo access, a restaurant and a coffee house with panoramic views.

The indoor and outdoor play area makes Pangea Hotel an ideal place for families. Hiking, fishing and cycling are among the activities that guests can enjoy near Pangea Hotel.

At the hotel, each thematic room includes a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

Rooms come with air conditioning. A buffet breakfast can be enjoyed daily at the property.

Tokaj is 27 km from the accommodation, while Sárospatak is 37 km from the property.
