Description

Just 30 metres from the Aqua-Palace and a 5-minute walk from Hajdúszoboszló’s thermal bath, Hotel Atlantis Hajdúszoboszló has a modern spa with an indoor pool, a stylish pool terrace, a gym, and a restaurant.

Air conditioning and free WiFi are standard room facilities at Hotel Atlantis Hajdúszoboszló.

Each room has its own balcony. Flooded with natural light, the indoor pool lies in the centre of the wellness centre.

Next to it there is a large hot tub and plenty of lounging space. There are different types of saunas, steam baths and even an ice cave.

Hotel Atlantis Hajdúszoboszló’s restaurant serves national and international dishes.
