Description

Situated in Debrecen's leisure area amidst the Great Forest, the Aquaticum Debrecen Thermal and Wellness Hotel is located above the Debrecen Thermal Spa.

Free Wi-Fi is provided. The spa has an indoor swimming pool, outdoor pools, the indoor Mediterranean pleasure baths, a wellness island, a Thai massage centre, and you can have a check-up in the medical department including a dental care centre.

Hotel guests can use the thermal pools free of charge during opening hours, and discounted tickets are available for accessing the Aquaticum Mediterranean Aqua Park and the Sauna World.

Each of the air-conditioned rooms and apartments in the Aquaticum Debrecen Thermal and Wellness Hotel features a balcony, wireless internet access, bathrobes and many more amenities.

The needs of guests with disabilities and allergies have also been taken into consideration, thus there are special rooms available.

The Natura restaurant offers Hungarian and international specialities and dietary dishes, prepared by the award-winning chefs.
