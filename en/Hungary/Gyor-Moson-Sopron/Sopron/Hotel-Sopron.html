Description

Featuring a free seasonal outdoor pool and a sauna, Hotel Sopron is located just 10 minutes' drive to the Austrian – Hungarian border and offers a unique view of the historic part of Sopron.

Free WiFi access is available in all areas. Each unit comprises a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, as well as a telephone, while there is an en-suite bathroom with a hairdryer.

Some units also come with a balcony. Guests can enjoy the Sky Wellness with unique city panorama free of charge after a registration and deposit.

The services include an infrared cabin, salt chamber, Finnish sauna, steam cabin, whirlpool, relax room and fitness room.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant at Hotel Sopron, as well as relax in the garden or on the terrace.

There is also a free fitness room, while you can play tennis and darts for a surcharge.

The Firewatch Tower and the Medieval Synagogue can be reached in a 10-minute walk.
