Description

The Hotel Kálvária, distinguished with the Hungarian Tourism Quality Award, is situated in 10-minute walk from the baroque city centre of Győr and offers free wireless and wired internet access and paid private parking.

Each en-suite room is equipped with towels, bathrobes, hairdryer, flat-screen TV, a phone and a minibar.

Deluxe rooms and suites are available. Guests can treat themselves to a meal in the restaurant that offers half-board and a la carte menu with Hungarian and international dishes.

Billiards, darts and table football can be enjoyed at the Cross Bar as well. Guests are welcome at the gym, or they can relax at the Magnolia Spa, which includes jacuzzi, steam cabin, finn- and infrasauna.

Property also offers a variety of massages. The hotel is equipped with disabled facilities, including special bathrooms and a lift.

Complete dental treatments are available on site. A recharging station for electric cars can also be found onsite.

The conference rooms can accommodate up to 60 guests and are fully equipped with all audio-visual features.
