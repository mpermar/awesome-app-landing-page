Description

Borostyán Vendégház is located in Kőszeg, 300 metres from a boating and fishing lake, offering an apartment with a terrace and free WiFi.

A free transfer from the train station can be arranged on request. The apartments have private entrance, a living room with TV and a fully equipped kitchen.

Guests get a bottle of wine, mineral water and fruits upon arrival. Borostyán Vendégház also features a garden with barbecue facilities.
