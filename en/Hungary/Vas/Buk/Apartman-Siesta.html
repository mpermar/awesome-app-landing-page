Description

The family-run Apartman Siesta is located in the green area of the village Bük, 1.

8 km from the thermal bath. In addition to a hot tub, Finnish- and infra sauna, satellite TV, it offers free Wi-Fi and a free bike rental.

All apartments decorated in vivid colours come with a fully equipped kitchen, a living room and a bathroom with shower.

They feature cable TV and a balcony or a patio overlooking the garden. The property welcomes guests with a new free wellness centre with a Finnish sauna, infrared sauna and hot tubs.

At Siesta Apartman cosmetic treatments are available and massage can be organised at a surcharge.

Guests can make use of the garden, which includes a barbecue and a children's playground.

A golf course is 3 km away. Restaurants can be reached within a walking distance.

Bus stop is 400 metres away from Siesta. Free private parking is provided. An airport shuttle can be organised at a surcharge.
