Description

Guests of the Hunguest Hotel Répce Gold can directly reach Spa Bük through a closed and heated passage.

Free WiFi is available in all areas and free private parking is possible on site.

The air-conditioned restaurant serves international and Hungarian specialties. The lobby bar provides a wide range of alcoholic and soft drinks.

Breakfast and dinner are served in the buffet room. Free admission to the medicinal spa area and open-air swimming pools in the summer and to the Adventure Bath of Bükfürdő Thermal&Spa is provided per person per night.

Entry to Sauna World in the spa is available for a surcharge and can be bought at the spa.

Services in the thermal bath: medical examinations, complex medical cures, hydro-, electro-, physicotherapy, medical gymnastics, various massages, laser, parafango, mud pack, ultra sound, weight bath.
