Description

The tastefully furnished Hangya Panzió és Étterem in Csepreg offers an outdoor pool, an à la carte restaurant and elegant rooms with satellite TV.

Free WiFi is available in the rooms. The variously decorated rooms have classical design and all comes with a bathroom with shower and a hairdryer.

Some rooms also have a balcony or air conditioning. Breakfast can be enjoyed every morning in the Hangya Panzió and half board is also available.

The restaurant serves international and Hungarian cuisine, as well as pizza specialties.

A sauna is available on site, while the thermal bath of Bükfürdő can be easily reached within a 10-minute drive.

Csepreg Train Station is 2 km from the property and the Fertő Lake is 40 km away.
