Description

Surrounded by greenery, Garda Hotel is quietly located beside a small lake, within 1 km from the centre of Szombathely.

Free Wi-Fi is available. The Indoor Swimming Pool and Thermal Bath is a 4-minute walk from the premises.

Garda Hotel offers free WiFi, free parking and buffet breakfast each morning. The reception is open 24 hours a day.

Each en suite room has a TV. Some rooms also provide a minibar and air conditioning.

The Austrian border is a 15-minute drive away and the historical town of Kőszeg is 20 km from the premises.
