Description

Located in the small spa town of Egerszalók, Mesés Shiraz Wellness & Tréning Hotel Superior features an oriental spa.

It offers free Wi-Fi, a breakfast buffet and free parking. All air-conditioned rooms are very spacious and individually decorated with African patterns.

They provide a private bathroom, and a flat-screen LCD TV with satellite channels.

Some rooms also have balconies. Guests of the Mesés Shiraz Hotel can relax in the Hammam, the hot tub and the indoor and outdoor pools.

All spa facilities are decorated in Moroccan style with beautiful Saracen ornaments.

The hotel’s facilities also include extensive massage programmes. The Mesés Shiraz serves Moroccan mint tea at lunch and dinner, or, on request, throughout the day.

The hotel's restaurants include the Shiraz Restaurant and Casablanca Bar. The dining areas include piano music.

Guests can try Moroccan Shisha water pipe at the bar. Hotel Shiraz is located among the foothills of the mountain Bükk, 6 km from Eger and 120 km from Budapest.
