Description

Herczeg Manor House Poroszló features a restaurant, a seasonal outdoor swimming pool, and a bar and shared lounge in Poroszló.

The property has a garden, as well as a terrace. There is a barbecue and guests can make use of free WiFi and free private parking.

Guests at the hotel can enjoy a continental breakfast. You can play table tennis at Herczeg Udvarház Poroszló, and the area is popular for hiking and cycling.

Around the clock guidance is available at the reception, where staff speak English and Hungarian.

Egerszalók is 40 km from the accommodation, while Eger is 43 km away. The nearest airport is Debrecen International Airport, 91 km from Herczeg Manor House Poroszló.
