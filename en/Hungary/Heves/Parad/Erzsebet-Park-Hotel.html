Description

Situated in the popular forested valley of the Mátra Mountains in Northern Hungary, the Erzsébet Park Hotel hotel in Parádfürdő offers you excellent spa facilities.

It can easily be reached from the M3 motorway. In 2 wellness areas you can enjoy both an indoor and an outdoor swimming pool, a sitting pool and a pool with an underwater massage.

There are also Turkish and Finnish saunas, an aroma sauna with light therapy, a salt sauna, a hot tub and a fitness room.

For a rejuvenating night's rest you can choose from nicely furnished and fully equipped rooms.

The Erzsébet Park Hotel's restaurant with a nice park view offers you tasty local and international cuisine and the the café invites you to spend some relaxing hours enjoying aromatic coffee.

The bar situated in the Zsuzsanna wing has a wide selection of Hungarian palinkas (local vodka), quality wines, cocktails and ice-cream.

Both bars offer free wireless internet access. Conference rooms, bowling alleys, billiard tables and bicycles are at your disposal as well in the Erzsébet Park Hotel.
