Description

Hotel Síkfökút is located at a small lake in Noszvaj, on the border of Bükk National Park.

It offers rooms with free Wi-Fi and a terrace overlooking the lake. Breakfast and dinner are served buffet style, while à la carte lunch is also available.

Meals are also served on the terrace in nice weather. All rooms have a private bathroom, a TV and a refrigerator.

Free private parking comes with all rooms. There are horseback riding facilities nearby.

Hotel Síkfökút is 10 km away from the spa town Eger and 5 km from Bogács. Egerszalók, Demjén and Mezőkövesd thermal baths can be reached within 20 km.
