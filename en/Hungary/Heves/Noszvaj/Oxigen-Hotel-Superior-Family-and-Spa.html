Description

The Oxigén Hotel Superior Family & Spa is located in a picturesque surrounding, 11 km from the town of Eger and provides stylish accommodation units, a wellness area and an on-site restaurant.

WiFi is available for free. Hotel rooms are furnished in English country style and equipped with a flat-screen cable TV, a minibar and a safety deposit box.

The en-suite bathrooms feature a shower cabin and contain a hairdryer and bathrobes as well.

Guests can enjoy traditional Hungarian dishes in the restaurant or participate in organised tasting of wines from the Eger Wine Region.

The services of the wellness area include an indoor swimming pool and a separate pool for children, a hot tub, a Finnish sauna, an infrared cabin, a steam and aroma cabin, a salt chamber, a Kneipp-basin.

The Oxigén Hotel Superior Family & Spa further provides massage treatments, beauty and hairdresser parlours for surcharge.

The property offers all inclusive catering for children under the age of 10 as well as indoor play center, animators, activity park, bouncing castle, amusement park, trampoline, mini traffic park and varied child programs.

Private parking is possible on site. The Egerszalók Thermal Bath can be visited 21 km away.
