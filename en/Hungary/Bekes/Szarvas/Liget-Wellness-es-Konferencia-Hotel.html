Description

The rooms offer views of the river or the Elisabeth Park with old trees.

The Liget Restaurant serves traditional Hungarian cuisine. Special attention is paid to local ingredients and specialties.

The Liget Wellness Hotel features a children's playroom and a spa area. It includes an indoor and outdoor pool as well as an adventure pool. There is a gym, saunas and a massage room available.

Boat and bike rental is offered at the Konferencia Hotel
