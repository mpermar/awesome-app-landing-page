Description

Offering free spa access per person per night, Solaris Apartman&Resort is situated right in front of a thermal bath and has thermal water pumped directly into its pipes.

Each of the air-conditioned apartments comes with a private balcony. Secure parking is provided free of charge.

These self-catering apartments have a separate kitchen and living room. They all feature colourful décor and modern furniture.

Towels and bed linen are also provided. Guests at Solaris Apartman&Resort can visit nearby arboretums, one in Tiszakürt, 5 km away, and the other in Szarvas, 30 km away.

Another popular destination is the National Historical Memorial Park of Opusztaszer, 50 km from the house.
