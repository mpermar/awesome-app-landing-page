Description

The Tisza Balneum Hotel is situated on a beautiful peninsula on the shore of Lake Tisza in Tiszafüred, amid landscaped gardens.

It offers free Wi-Fi and a large spa area. Guests can enjoy the breakfast as well as Hungarian specialities in the on-site restaurant.

You can also try wine made by hotel. Dietetic meals can also be provided on request.

All spacious rooms of the Tisza Balneum provide air conditioning, a bathroom and a terrace or a balcony.

The spa area offers an outdoor swimming pool, as well as 2 indoor pools. A hot tub, a Finnish sauna, a steam bath and a gym can also be used.

Guests can also benefit of the treatment salons where body and facial treatments, special baths and a variety of beauty therapies will be provided.

All pools are filled with mineral rich water from the Tisza Balneum’s own deep thermal spring.

Facilities for the Tisza’s guests include tennis courts and areas set aside for sunbathing or fishing, as well as a car park and moorings for boats.

Nature lovers can enjoy the nearby arboretum, the national park and the bird sanctuary.
