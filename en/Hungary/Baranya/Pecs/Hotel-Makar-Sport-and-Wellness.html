Description

Hotel Makar Sports & Wellness is located in a quiet, green area, a few minutes' walk from the Medical University of Pecs and 2 km from the historic centre.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. It comprises a park, sports fields, playgrounds and a car park.

Apart from an extensive wellness centre with an indoor swimming pool, a hot tub, sauna, steam room, this complex also features tennis and squash courts as well.

The rooms are located in the main building, with direct access to the wellness area and the restaurant.

Daily buffet breakfast is served at the restaurant. Lunches and dinners are available as well.

Hotel Makar is 200 metres away from the bus stop of bus number 30, providing easy connections to the centre.
