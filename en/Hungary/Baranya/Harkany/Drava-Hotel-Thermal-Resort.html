Description

The 4-star Dráva Hotel Thermal Resort in Harkány offers a large wellness centre with indoor pool, hot tub, massages and steam baths, as well as modern rooms fitted with free internet.

Spacious and decorated with contemporary furniture, each unit has cable TV and a minibar.

Some rooms are air-conditioned. The wellness centre includes alternative medicine massages and a sauna.

Guests can also make use of the well-equipped fitness centre or the sunbathing terrace.

The resort has a variety of sports courts, from garden tennis to volleyball fields.

Dráva Hotel Thermal Resort is 25 km from Pécs and 19 km from Villány, region famous for its wine.
