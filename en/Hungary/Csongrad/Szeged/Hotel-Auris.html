Description

Situated in Szeged, 500 metres from the Votive Church, Hotel Auris features a seasonal outdoor pool surrounded by a sun terrace.

Private parking is available on site, while free WiFi is provided throughout the property.

The units face the inner courtyard or the street, and some of them comes with a balcony with pool views, as well as a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

For your comfort, you will find bathrobes, free toiletries and a hairdryer in every bathroom.

The hotel features a Finnish sauna. A kids' corner is available as well. Buffet breakfast is available, while guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

The reception is available 24 hours a day. Dóm Square can be reached in 6 minutes by foot, while the New Synagogue is 1 km away.
