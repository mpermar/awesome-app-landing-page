Description

The 4-star hotel is housed in a reconstructed historical mansion in the middle of a 100-hectare vineyard. The rooms are equipped with Empire furniture and luxurious color-coordinated fabrics. Spend relaxing hours in the noble salon or in the library, dip into the on-site outdoor pool, dine fine in the restaurant and make use of the hotel's exquisite meeting facilities.

Guests can also visit the on-site Theresa Chapel for free, built in 1750 as a tribute to Maria Theresa, then Queen of Hungary.
