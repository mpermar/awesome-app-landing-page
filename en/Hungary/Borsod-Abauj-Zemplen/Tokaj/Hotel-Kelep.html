Description

Opened in June 2012, Hotel Kelep is located in green surroundings 200 metres from the banks of the Bodrog River and 2 km from the centre of Tokaj.

All rooms have a balcony. Free Wi-Fi and a spa area are available. The restaurant servers Hungarian specialities made from local ingredients, as well as home-made cheese.

You can also taste locally made chocolate and a wide range of wines and spirits. You can park your car for free on site, rent bicycles at Hotel Kelep or take advantage of the water sports facilities.

The on-site spa area includes a sauna and a hot tub. The Tisza River for fishing is 1 km away and the ruins of Rakoczi Castle can be found 1.
