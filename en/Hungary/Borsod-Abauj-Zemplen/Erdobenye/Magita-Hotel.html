Description

The Hotel Magita is located in one of the oldest villages of the Tokaj-Hegyalja region called Erdőbénye, and offers you free Wi-fi, a restaurant and an outdoor pool.

All rooms are equipped with a cableTV. Some units also come with air conditioning.

Tasty Hungarian cuisine is served in the restaurant and on the charming summer terrace and the bar in the cellar is a cosy place to end your day.

A sauna, a spa bath and massages are available as well and there is also a skittle alley in the Magita hotel.
