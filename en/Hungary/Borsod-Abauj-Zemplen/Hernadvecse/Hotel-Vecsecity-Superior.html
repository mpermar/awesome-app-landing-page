Description

Set in a calm and green surrounding and offering a sauna, an indoor and a seasonal outdoor pool, Hotel Vécsecity superior is located in Hernádvécse.

Free WiFi access and free private parking are available. All modern furnished and air-conditioned rooms at Hotel Vécsecity superior feature a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a seating area and garden view.

Featuring a bath or shower, private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and bathrobes.

The on-site à la carte restaurant serves traditional Hungarian cuisine and on request, special diet menus are also provided.

For a shorter break, a bar and a vending machine are at guests´ disposal. Guests can relax at the spa and wellness centre, while children can play both inside the building or in the garden.

Horses and other domestic animals are also available on the property. Other facilities, such as babysitting service, tennis court, billiards, massage service, solarium and beauty shop are all available at Hotel Vécsecity superior at an additional surcharge.
