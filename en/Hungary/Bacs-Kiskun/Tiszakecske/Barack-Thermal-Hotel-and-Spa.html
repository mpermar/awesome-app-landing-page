Description

Offering an indoor pool and a spa and wellness centre, Barack Thermal Hotel and Spa is located in Tiszakécske.

Free WiFi access is available. Each room is provided with cable TV, air conditioning and a minibar.

Featuring a shower, the private bathrooms also come with a hairdryer and bathrobes.

At Barack Thermal Hotel and Spa you will find a sauna, a steam cabin and a hot tub.

Other facilities offered at the property include luggage storage and a children's playground.
