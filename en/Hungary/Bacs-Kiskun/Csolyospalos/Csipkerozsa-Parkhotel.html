Description

Situated in Csólyospálos in the Bacs-Kiskun Region, 34 km from Szeged, Csipkerozsa Parkhotel boasts an outdoor pool and an infrared sauna.

The hotel has a barbecue, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

Two types of flight simulators are also available. Free private parking is available on site.

Some units include a seating area where you can relax. A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms.

Csipkerozsa Parkhotel features free WiFi throughout the property. A flat-screen TV is available.

Guests can try a Speed Cruiser and an Airbus A320 flight simulator with the help of a pilot on site.

There is also a shared lounge and hairdresser's, a beautician, a massage parlour at the property.

Bike hire is available. You can engage in various activities, such as golfing and horse riding.

Mórahalom is 27 km from Csipkerozsa Parkhotel, while Palić is 39 km from the property.
