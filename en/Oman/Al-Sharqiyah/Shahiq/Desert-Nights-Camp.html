Description

With an unbeatable setting in the silky dunes of the Omani desert, the 5-star Desert Nights Camp offers 39 luxurious Bedouin-style tents and 14 non tent standard rooms for those seeking the ultimate desert adventure holiday.

The Desert Nights Camp is situated on the Wahiba/Sharqiya sands, a 2-hour drive from Muscat.

This secluded area covers 11 km of beautiful desert landscape. Several free services are offered to the guests, including drive up to the dunes in a 4x4 vehicle to watch the sunset, with light snacks and soft drinks served on the trip.

Also camel rides within the camp premises in the morning are available, and transfers between Al Wasil town and the Desert Nights Camp are offered for those travelling by vehicles not suited for desert driving.
