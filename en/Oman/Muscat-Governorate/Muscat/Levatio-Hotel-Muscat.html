Description

All rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Levatio Hotel Muscat has certain rooms with city views, and every room is equipped with a electric tea pot. At the accommodation, rooms have a private bathroom with free toiletries.

A buffet breakfast is available each morning at the property. Guests can enjoy a meal at the in-house restaurant, which specializes in international cuisine and also offers Halal and Vegetarian options.

At Levatio Hotel Muscat guests are welcome to use a hot tub and a sauna.

The hotel can conveniently provide information at the reception to help guests to get around the area.

Oman Avenues Mall is half a mile from Levatio Hotel Muscat, while Sultan Qaboos Grand Mosque is 1.3 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Muscat International Airport, 8 miles from the accommodation.
