Description

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool, Hilton Garden Inn Muscat Al Khuwair is set in Muscat, 1 km from Oman Avenues Mall.

The hotel offers free WiFi and enjoys city and mountain views. The property is 10 km from Ras Al Hamra Recreation Club.

At the hotel, rooms have a desk and a flat-screen TV. Each room is fitted with a private bathroom equipped with a hot tub and free toiletries, while selected rooms are fitted with a kitchenette fitted with a fridge.

All units feature a wardrobe. The reception can provide advice on the area in order to help guests plan their day.

Sultan Qaboos Grand Mosque, the Royal Opera House and beach are within 15 minutes from the hotel by car.

Qurum Natural Park is 8 km away. The nearest airport is Muscat International Airport, a 25-minute drive from the property.
