Description

All of the accommodations are found in the villa-style units that are scattered through the gardens. All of the suites are large in size, include a sitting area plus a large balcony or terrace. All suites also have a DVD player.

Breakfast is included in the room rate and is served buffet style each morning. The hotel’s restaurant serves a great selection of international food.

The Eram Spa offers many treatments and therapies and yoga classes are also available. For children there is an outdoor playground and kids' pool, plus an indoor play room.
