Description

Radisson Blu Hotel boasts a prime location in Sohar with views of the Gulf of Oman and adjacent to the Silver Jubilee Park.

The property is within close proximity of Sohar Port, Sohar Fort and only 15 km from Sohar International Airport.

Radisson Blu Hotel Sohar offers 179 rooms, including family rooms and suites with fantastic views.

Each room is air conditioned and has a flat-screen TV, a minibar, work desk and tea/coffee maker.

Each room is complete with a private bathroom with a bathtub, bathrobe and slippers.

The hotel features an all-day-dining Al Zafaran restaurant that specializes in local and international cuisines.

THe restaurant hosts themed events and boasts a terrace with pool views. While Amaranthai restaurant serves fusion Thai food with a Chinese Twist and ocean view.

The Al Khaimah Rooftop offers stunning views and dishes. The property also features a temperature-controlled outdoor pool, spa, free gym and sauna access.

Guests can make use of free & high speed Wi-Fi available throughout the property, 24-hours reception, and free on-site public parking.
