Description

Offering a spa centre and a kids club, Fanar Hotel & Residences is set in Salalah directly on the beach.

Guests can enjoy the buffet restaurant or Aubergine a Mediterranean Ã  la carte restaurant Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and features a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Some units include a seating area for your convenience. Views of the sea, mountains or pool are featured in certain rooms.

The rooms come with a private bathroom fitted with a shower. For your comfort, you will find bathrobes and slippers.

The Suite with sea view has a romantic terrace featuring a private jacuzzi. You will find a 24-hour front desk, babysitting service, gift shop and entertainment at the property.

During khareef, bike rental services are available at a surcharge. This hotel has a private beach area and car hire are available.

You can play tennis at the hotel or join water activities. Enjoy nearby Hawana Aqua Park, the largest aquapark in Oman.
