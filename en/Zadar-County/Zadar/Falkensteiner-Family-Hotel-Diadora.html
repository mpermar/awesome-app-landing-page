Description

Family Hotel Diadora's buffet restaurant features a large terrace and front-cooking stations, various seating areas as well as a separate children's restaurant. Taste international and Mediterranean cuisine, with nonalcoholic beverages, beer and wine included in the meals.

You will find 1 bottle of mineral water in your room upon arrival as well as a beach bag with a bath robe, beach towels and slippers available for the entire stay. All rooms are air-conditioned and have satellite TV and a private bathroom.

Falky-Land, (approximately 1.000 m²), provides everything that kids between the ages of 3 and 12 could wish for. There is a play area with a toddler's garden, a cinema and the "Las Vegas" children's amusement arcade.

A children's water world with an indoor pool, a soft ball pit and an amazing outdoor pool complex with spectacular water slides are also on site. Professional child care is offered.

Zadar Old Town is 8.7 mi away, while Zadar Airport can be reached within 15 mi.
