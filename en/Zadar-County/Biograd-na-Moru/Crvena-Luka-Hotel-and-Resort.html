Description

Certain rooms have a sitting area for your convenience. Each room comes with a private bathroom fitted with a shower. For your comfort, you will find bathrobes and a hairdryer. A flat-screen TV with satellite channels is available.

You will find a 24-hour front desk and gift shop at the property.

The hotel also provides bike rental. Šibenik is 24 miles from Resort Crvena Luka Apts, and Vodice is 19 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Zadar Airport, 14 miles from the property.
