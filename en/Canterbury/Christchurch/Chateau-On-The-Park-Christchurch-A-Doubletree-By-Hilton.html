Description

Each room at The Chateau on the Park Christchurch includes heating, air conditioning, satellite TV and free Wi-Fi. The private bathroom features a bath, shower, hairdryer and organic toiletries.

Guests have access to a golf putting area and bicycle hire. Ask the friendly staff about the complimentary shuttle to the CBD as they welcome you with a signature, warm chocolate chip cookie.

Garden Court Brasserie overlooks the garden and offers al fresco dining during the summer months. The Den Bar has an open fire, and serves drinks, snacks and light meals.

It is only 20 minutes’ walk through Hagley Park to Christchurch Cathedral. Christchurch Botanic Gardens, Canterbury Museum and Canterbury University are all within a 15-minute walk of Chateau on the Park.

Riccarton is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, food and botanical gardens.
