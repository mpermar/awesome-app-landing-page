Description

The apartments at BreakFree Grand Pacific are spacious and have lush garden or water views. Each includes a satellite TV, DVD and CD player. Some apartments have a private rooftop or courtyard with a hot tub and BBQ facilities.

Guests can relax in the hotel’s hot tub or sauna. BreakFree Hotel also has a well-equipped fitness center and game room. The tour desk staff can assist guests with arranging excursions to local attractions.

Many of Caloundra’s boutiques and restaurants are within 350 feet of the hotel. Glasshouse Mountains and Blackall Range National Park can be reached in a short drive. Brisbane is a one-hour drive from BreakFree Grand Pacific.
