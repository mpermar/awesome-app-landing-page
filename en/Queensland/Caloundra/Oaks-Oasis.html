Description

The property features a water play park with a splash pool, water-slides and fountains. There is also a giant tipping bucket, along with relaxing sun lounges, picnic areas and party tents.

The Oaks Oasis Resort provides guests with a variety of accommodation styles, with features including air conditioning, cable TV, and kitchens or kitchenettes. It also provides an extensive array of resort facilities, such as a swimming pool, a hot tub, a volleyball court and a tennis court.

Located around a charming waterlily-filled pond, the Reflections restaurant is open for breakfast every day, and dinner on Tuesday through Saturday nights. The Legends Bar is the ideal place to unwind and also features poolside access.

While in the area, guest should be sure to visit Steve Irwin's Australia Zoo, the Titanium Club Pelican Golf Club, Underwater World, The Big Pineapple, Fraser Island and the Queensland Air Museum. Guests can also enjoy scenic drives to Maleny and Montville.

Golden Beach is a great choice for travelers interested in family-friendly trips, scenery and walking.
