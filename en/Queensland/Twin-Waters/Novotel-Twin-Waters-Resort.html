Description

The resort offers an extensive range of activities including complimentary water sports, an outdoor heated swimming pool and spa and a gym. Younger guests can make the most of Busy Bodies Australia Kids Club and enjoy a program of supervised activities.

Novotel Twin Waters offers guests a range of dining options, ranging from buffet breakfasts to a varied dinner menu. Pampering treatments are available in the over-water Day Spa. Golf can be arranged locally on an 18 hole championship golf course.

The Novotel Twin Waters is located just 4 miles from Sunshine Coast Airport.

Up to 2 children (15 years and under) stay free when sharing with parents.
