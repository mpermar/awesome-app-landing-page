Description

The resort is a 15-minute walk from the cafes, restaurants, bars and shops of Macrossan Street.

All accommodations at Pink Flamingo Resort Port Douglas feature kitchen or kitchenette facilities, air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, iPod docking station and a private bathroom.

Guests can relax with a massage in their villa and enjoy a soak in their own private outdoor bath. Facilities include a guest laundry, bike rental and barbecue area. Free parking is available.

The tour desk can arrange Great Barrier Reef cruises, Daintree Rainforest tours, helicopter rides, half-day fishing tours and guided walks of Mossman Gorge.
