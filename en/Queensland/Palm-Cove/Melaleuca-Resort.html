Description

Spacious and set in a minimalist décor, each apartment is equipped with air conditioning, a well-equipped kitchen and a large balcony with views of the tropical garden and swimming pool.

Guests can also relax using any of the 3 in-pool spas at Melaleuca Resort. Other recreational amenities include barbecue facilities and a guest library.

Cairns International Airport is a 25-minute drive from the resort. Paradise Palms Country Club is 4 miles away.
