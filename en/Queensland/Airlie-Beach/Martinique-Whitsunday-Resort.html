Description

Each apartment features a full kitchen and laundry facilities. Each has a lounge area featuring a large flat-screen TV with cable channels and a DVD player. Wi-Fi is available in all public areas of the property and in some rooms. Guests can enjoy an outdoor meal in the shaded BBQ area.

The Martinique Whitsunday Resort is just a 3-minute walk from the center of Airlie Beach, and provides easy access to the Whitsunday Islands and the Great Barrier Reef.
