Description

All rooms at Fitzroy Resort offer modern amenities including air conditioning and cable TV. Each has a private bathroom with shower, fridge and tea/coffee making facilities.

Guests can relax in the outdoor swimming pool with bar or the in-house movie theater. Paths lead through the rainforest and along the white coral beaches.

The Fitzroy’s sea view restaurant offers island fare cooked with fresh Australian produce. Drinks are available at the swim-up bar.

Snorkeling and kayaking equipment is available for rent. Fitzroy Island is located 18 miles off the coast of Cairns.

Great place for a 2-3 day-stay

"The property is directly at the beach. Some good snorkelling possible-ask the staff where and when. Cairns Dive Center has an office at the hotel and at a cost you can hire kayaks, stand-up paddling boards, wetsuits, snorkel gear, book tours to the outer reef. There is a nice outside pool although the water was a little cold for my liking (in July). It is a good place for families; there is a playroom for the little ones, a games room and movies are shown at least twice daily. The Zephyr restaurant offers good breakfast and a la carte dining, which we enjoyed. There is a second restaurant on the island -Foxy's- just a couple of minutes walk away which is more casual and offers good lunches and dinners. The Studio rooms are clean and spacious with a comfortable bed and a marble bathroom with a rain shower. Their window faces to the inside walkway. There are rooms available with balcony and view to the sea at a higher cost. Overall we had pleasant stay."
