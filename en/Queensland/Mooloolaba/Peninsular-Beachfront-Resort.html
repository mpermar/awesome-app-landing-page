Description

The spacious, modern apartments each include a full kitchen, laundry facilities and a lounge area with a flat-screen TV. Guests can dine at one of 3 restaurants, each providing room service.

Centrally located, the Peninsular provides convenient access to the area’s boutiques and theme parks. Many Sunshine Coast attractions are located nearby, including Underwater World and the Mooloolaba Wharf and Esplanade.

The Peninsular is 15 minutes from the Sunshine Coast Airport.
