Description

The modern rooms are fully air-conditioned and feature a private bathroom. Amenities include a fridge, tea/coffee-making facilities and cable TV.

The property provides free bike rental services for guests to explore the area. There is a self-service laundry facility and dry cleaning services can be arranged. There is also a guest lounge with a library.

The Mitoki Modern Japanese Tapas Restaurant serves dinner from Monday to Saturday. Lunch is available from Thursday to Saturday. Guests can also enjoy alcoholic beverages at the restaurant’s bar.

Hotel Il Mondo Boutique is just under a mile from Fortitude Valley and Chinatown. It is just over a mile from the Old Performing Arts Centre and the Brisbane Convention and Exhibition Centre. The Brisbane Airport is 6 miles away.

Kangaroo Point is a great choice for travelers interested in family-friendly trips, convenient public transportation and restaurants.
