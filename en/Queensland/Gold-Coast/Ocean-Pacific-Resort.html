Description

All Ocean Pacific apartments are air-conditioned and feature a full kitchen, laundry facilities and a spacious living area. Guests can enjoy a meal with family and friends in the barbecue area.

Ocean Pacific Resort is a short walk from patrolled beaches, fine restaurants, shopping centers, and casinos. The Gold Coast Airport is less than 12 miles away. Free covered parking is available.

Broadbeach is a great choice for travelers interested in restaurants, food and relaxation.
