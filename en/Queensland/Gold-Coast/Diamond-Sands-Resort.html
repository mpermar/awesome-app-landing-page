Description

The resort offers apartments of various sizes where each modern unit is equipped with air conditioning, cable TV and a well-equipped kitchen.

Diamond Sands Resort has a large lagoon pool, surrounding by a tropical garden and equipped with lounge chairs.

Surfers Paradise is 2.5 miles from the resort. Miami Golf Course is less than 10 minutes’ drive away.

Mermaid Beach is a great choice for travelers interested in theme parks, shopping and family-friendly trips.
