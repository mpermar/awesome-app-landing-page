Description

The accommodations offer a range of self contained 2 & 3 bedroom apartments, with large balconies. Enjoy the independence and privacy of the fully equipped kitchen included.

The infinity-edge pool at the resort peak has spectacular views of Surfers Paradise. There is also a beautiful lagoon pool with its own sandy beach, or a heated waterfall pool.

Energize at the gym. Rejuvenate at 2 spas, and in the sauna. Have fun in the game room, and setting up the barbecue outside. Tours and tickets can easily be organized for trips outside the complex.

Miami is a great choice for travelers interested in family-friendly trips, beach walks and theme parks.
