Description

Located in the centre of Fes, this typical riad features a terrace with panoramic views of the medina.

It offers air-conditioned rooms and suites with free Wi-Fi. Each spacious room and suite at the Riad Boutique Borj Dhab Fez overlooks the medina or the interior courtyard and has a private bathroom.

The Riad Boutique Borj Dhab Fez serves typical Moroccan cuisine and a daily breakfast.

A shuttle service is available between the riad and Fes-Saïss Airport at an extra cost.
