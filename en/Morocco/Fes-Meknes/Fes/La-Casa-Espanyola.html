Description

Situated in Fès and with Royal Palace Fes reachable within 2. 4 km, La Casa Espanyola features express check-in and check-out, non-smoking rooms, a restaurant, free WiFi throughout the property and a shared lounge.

Boasting family rooms, this property also provides guests with a children's playground.

Extra facilities include a 24-hour front desk, meeting rooms, a tour desk and dry cleaning service.

At the guest house rooms come with a private bathroom. La Casa Espanyola offers a continental or halal breakfast.

The accommodation offers a sun terrace. Popular points of interest near La Casa Espanyola include Batha Square, Medersa Bouanania and complexe El batha"EL Morkkab".

The nearest airport is Fès–Saïs, 17 km from the guest house, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
