Description

Dar Victoria is an 18th-century, Moroccan-style house in a central location with modern facilities.

It offers friendly and personalised service Antique furniture decorates the luxurious rooms, which include air conditioning and a private bathroom with a heated towel rail.

Free Wi-Fi access is available throughout the guest house. Dar Victoria offers a lounge with a fireplace, a rooftop terrace with view and a hammam (upon reservation).

Breakfast can be served in the courtyard, in the suites or on the terrace. Dar Victoria is in the Andalou district and offers a peaceful retreat from the liveliness of the nearby Medina.
