Description

Located near the Batha Museum and Bab Boujloud, this Riad Hotel features a rooftop terrace with a view of the city of Fez.

It offers 7 air-conditioned suites and a traditional Hammam and a massage room. Each guest suite at the Riad Ibn Battuta is equipped with satellite TV and free Wi-Fi internet access.

Guests can enjoy regional specialities at the Ibn Battuta Restaurant. Breakfast and lunch are served on the terrace or next to the fountain on the patio.

Free private parking is available on site and the Ibn Battuta Riad Hotel provides a 24-hour reception.
