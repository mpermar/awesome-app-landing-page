Description

Located in the Medina, Riad Tizwa is a 15-minute walk from Madrasa Bou Inania. It offers air-conditioned accommodation decorated in a typical Moroccan style and a terrace with views of the plain.

The rooms feature a seating area and a sofa. There is a private bathroom with free toiletries, hairdryer and shower.

There is access to a traditional lounge with fireplace and patio with seating area.

At breakfast, guest can sample traditional, Moroccan specialties. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout and there is a tour desk on site to arrange excursions in the area.

Dar Batha Museum is just a 2-minute walk away and Al Karaouine Mosque is a 20-minute walk away.
