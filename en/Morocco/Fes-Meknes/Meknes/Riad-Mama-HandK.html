Description

Riad Mama H&K is set in Meknès and features a terrace. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, room service and a shared lounge, along with free WiFi throughout the property.

The guest house features family rooms. The units in the guest house are fitted with a flat-screen TV.

The private bathroom is equipped with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries. Riad Mama H&K offers a continental or vegetarian breakfast.

The nearest airport is Fès–Saïs, 75 km from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
