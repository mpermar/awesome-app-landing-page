Description

The Amina Resort is located in Marrakechs residential lHivernage area. It has an outdoor swimming pool and air-conditioned apartments, each with a balcony, satellite TV and a private bathroom.

Guests of the Amina Resort can relax in the Jacuzzi, sauna and hammam, or enjoy a range of massage treatments in the spa.

The hotels large terrace offers panoramic views of the Atlas Mountains. All apartments at the Amina Resort have lift access and a kitchen equipped with a fridge, dishwasher and an oven/grill.
