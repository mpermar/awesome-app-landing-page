Description

This traditional Moroccan Riad is a 10-minute walk from Jamâa El Fna Square and features an outdoor swimming pool and a terrace with a lounge area and a spa.

Free Wi-Fi is available in the public areas. Decorated with the theme of the 5 natural elements, each of the air-conditioned room at Dar Elma has a patio view and includes a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

A continental breakfast with Moroccan delicacies is offered daily. The restaurant served local specialties that can also be taken in the patio or on the terrace.

Cooking classes of Moroccan cuisine are also provided. A living room with books and a TV is available on site.

At a surcharge, guests can access to the hammam and spa bath, and have massages. The Marrakech Airport is a 15-minute drive and Shuttle service is provided at an extra cost.
