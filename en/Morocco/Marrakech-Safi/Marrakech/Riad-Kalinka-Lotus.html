Description

Featuring an indoor swimming pool, a terrace and a patio, Riad Kalinka Lotus offers typical Moroccan decoration in Marrakesh.

It is just a 5-minute drive from Jamaa El fna and from the Majorelle Garden. The air-conditioned rooms all have free Wi-Fi.

They are complete with a seating area and en suite facilities with hairdryer. The Riad is equipped with a lounge with TV and DVD player.

Guest will enjoy a welcome tea and fruits in the rooms. They can relax at the bar lounge or sample the restaurant’s Moroccan food served by the pool, in the patio or on the terrace.

Breakfast is continental. Ménara Airport is located just 9. 2 km away and Marrakesh Train Station is just a 5.
