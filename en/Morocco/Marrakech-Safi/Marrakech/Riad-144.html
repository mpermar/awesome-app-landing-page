Description

Located in Marrakech, 1. 9 km from Djemaa El Fna, Riad 144 boasts an outdoor pool and hammam.

Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. A flat-screen TV with satellite channels and DVD player, as well as a CD player are offered.

Some units have a seating area for your convenience. A balcony or patio are featured in certain rooms.

Rooms include a private bathroom. Extras include free toiletries and a hair dryer.

Riad 144 features free WiFi throughout the property. There is a shared lounge, babysitting service, concierge service and gift shop at the property.

Souk of the Medina is 400 metres from Riad 144, while Koutoubia is 500 metres away.
