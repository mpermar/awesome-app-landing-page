Description

Offering an indoor pool, Riad Farhan is located in Marrakech. Free WiFi access is available.

Each room here will provide you with air conditioning and a seating area. The private bathroom also comes with a bath or shower.

At Riad Farhan you will find a terrace. Other facilities offered at the property include luggage storage, laundry facilities, massage sessions, body care, hair dressing services, kitchen lessons.

Riad Farhan can organize tours and shuttle service from and to the airport of Marrakech.

The riad is 300 metres from Souk of the Medina and 800 metres from Djemaa El Fna.
