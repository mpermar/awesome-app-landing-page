Description

Riad Alamine offers air-conditioned rooms in a traditional Moroccan setting with an interior courtyard and a top-floor terrace.

It is situated in central Marrakech a 3-minute walk away from Jamaâ el Fna Square.

Each room has a private bathroom with a shower and the more spacious rooms also have a seating area.

Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas. A Moroccan breakfast is served daily, and you can enjoy traditional evening meals on request.

A choice of activites can be organised, such as a visit of the Ourika Valley, various excursions in the area and cooking classes.
