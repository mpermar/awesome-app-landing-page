Description

Situated in Marrakech, 800 metres walk from a shopping area, Be Live Experience Marrakech Palmeraie - All Inclusive boasts a swimming pool, a fitness area and free WiFi throughout the property.

Guests can enjoy the on-site bar. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. Guests can sample local and international dishes in one of the restaurants.

Palais de La Bahia is 7 km from Be Live Experience Marrakech Palmer. The nearest airport is Marrakech-Menara Airport, 13 km from Be Live Experience Marrakech Palmeraie - All Inclusive.
