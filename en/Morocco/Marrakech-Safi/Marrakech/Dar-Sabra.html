Description

Set in a 2-hectare garden at the heart of the palm grove in Marrakech, this design property features Mexican architecture in its rooms and 2-bedroom villa.

It offers a modern art gallery, spa and 6 swimming pools. All air-conditioned rooms are equipped with a minibar and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Each spacious room is either decorated with a contemporary theme or inspired by different countries.

Some rooms feature views of the garden or swimming pool. International cuisine is served for lunch and dinner by the swimming pool or in the restaurant.

The property can also prepare Moroccan meals or special dishes upon request. The buffet breakfast can also be served by the swimming pool or in the restaurant.

Guests can play tennis on site or exercise in the fitness centre. Massages are available at the spa and it also has a cinema with a choice of over 300 films.

Wi-Fi is accessible throughout the hotel. With free private parking on site, this hotel is located 3 km from the Medina.

Menara International Airport is 15 km away. A taxi and shuttle service is available upon request at the reception desk.
