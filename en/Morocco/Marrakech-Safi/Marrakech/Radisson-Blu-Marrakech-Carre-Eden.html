Description

Offering an outdoor pool and a restaurant, Radisson Blu Hotel Marrakech Carré Eden is located in Marrakech.

Free WiFi access is available. Each room here will provide you with air conditioning, a balcony and a patio.

Featuring a bath, private bathroom also comes with a shower and a hairdryer. You can enjoy city view and pool view from the room.

At Radisson Blu Hotel Marrakech Carré Eden you will find a fitness centre. Other facilities offered at the property include a tour desk, luggage storage and a kids' club.

The property offers free parking. The hotel is a 5-minute drive from Marrakesh Train Station and a 5-minute walk from Marrakech Plaza.
