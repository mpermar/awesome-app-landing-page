Description

With a mix of modern and Moroccan design, Riad Origines is located in Marrakech, 1500 metres from the Jamaâ El Fna Square.

It offers a rooftop terrace with views over the Atlas. Each air-conditioned room is in traditional Moroccan décor.

They are equipped with a private patio with panoramic views, free Wi-Fi access and a private bathroom.

Riad Origines’s restaurant serves daily breakfast and traditional Moroccan cuisine in a modern setting.

A drink can be enjoyed at the hotel’s bar. Guests are invited to relax in the spa centre or in the lounge beside the fireplace or sunbath by the outdoor pool.

Riad Origines is 2000 metres away from Jardin Majorelle and an 1500 metres' away from Bus Station Bab Doukkala train station, which can take guests around Marrakech.
