Description

This 16th-century riad is located in Marrakesh, just a 2-minute walk from Jamaâ El Fna Square.

It offers a hot tub, a plunge pool in the patio area and a large garden terrace with both a shaded area and sun beds.

Each air-conditioned room is individually decorated and gives direct access to the patio.

Every room has a bathroom with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries. Free Wi-Fi access is provided in the public areas of the hotel.

Babysitting services are available and the hosts can arrange local activities and tours.

Marrakesh-Menara International Airport is a 15-minute drive from Riad Al Mamoune and airport transfers can be arranged at an extra charge.
