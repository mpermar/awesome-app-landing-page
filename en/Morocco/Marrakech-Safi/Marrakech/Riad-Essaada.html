Description

Located just an 8-minute walk from Jamaâ El Fna Square, the Riad Essaada offers a plunge pool and a sun terrace with views of the Atlas Mountain.

A traditional Moroccan breakfast is served every day. The elegant guest rooms are decorated in bright colours and they include free Wi-Fi access and air conditioning.

Each room features a seating area and an en suite bathroom. Local cuisine can be prepared upon request at this guest house, and you can dine on the patio.

After dinner, you can relax in the lounge with its fireplace. Marrakech-Menara Airport is only a 13-minute drive from this property and the Majorelle Garden is a 7-minute drive away.
