Description

Located in Marrakech, Riad Palais Razala is 2. 1 km from Marrakech Parking Koutoubia.

A hot tub is available for guests. The property comes with an outdoor pool and is set 8 km from Acima Massira Marrakech.

All rooms at the riad are fitted with a seating area. Riad Palais Razala provides certain rooms with mountain views, and all rooms include a patio.

At the accommodation, the rooms have a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hair dryer.

Guests can grab a bite to eat in the on-site restaurant, which serves Moroccan cuisine.

Riad Palais Razala offers a terrace. The riad provides an ironing service, as well as business facilities like fax and photocopying.

For any tips on how to get around or what to do in the area, guests can ask at the reception.

A golf car is available all along the stay. Popular points of interest near Riad Palais Razala include Bahia Palace, Medersa Ben Youssef and Marrakech Museum.
