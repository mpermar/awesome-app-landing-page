Description

Just a 2-minute walk from Jamaâ El Fna Square, this traditional riad offers a rooftop terrace and air-conditioned rooms with a Moroccan-style decor.

It is located a 20-minute walk from Dar Si Saïd Museum and The Bahia Palace. Guest rooms at Riad Abaka are individually decorated and equipped with heating.

Each has an en suite bathroom with a shower and traditional Tadelakt walls. Breakfast is served each morning at the riad and can be enjoyed on the terrace, or in the courtyard.

For other meals, traditional Moroccan dishes are available on site. Free Wi-Fi internet access is provided in the riad’s lounges and in the courtyard.

Staff are available 24 hours a day and for a supplement they can organise an airport transfer or hammam treatments.
