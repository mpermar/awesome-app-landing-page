Description

Located in the Agdal neighborhood, Kech Hotel & Spa overlooks the Oukaimeden Mountains and features a swimming pool and restaurant.

It offers modern accommodation with free Wi-Fi and balcony with views of the pool and sun terrace.

Featuring a LED satelliteTV, all the spacious units at the Kech Hotel & Spa offer air conditioning.

Each has soundproofing. A private bathroom is included. Continental breakfast is served in the dining area.

Guests can taste local recipes at the on-site restaurant. Room service is also possible.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can offer information on nearby attractions such as the Palaces of Bahia and Badii, 5 km away.

Souks are within a 5-minute walk. Marrakesh Menara Airport is at a distance of 8 km.
