Description

Located in the district of Targa-Jawhar, Marrakesh, Palais Dar Ouladna, traditionally decorated guest house is set in a calm, residential area, just a few minutes away from the city centre.

This guest house is composed of 4 air conditioned, en suite rooms and 1 apartment (Douira) with a private terrace.

In addition to this, there is a large swimming pool in a garden, a Hammam, relaxing public areas and a restaurant.

The decoration of the rooms of Dar Ouladna has been chosen with respect to the tradition of the Moroccan style.
