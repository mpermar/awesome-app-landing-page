Description

Boasting a shared lounge, garden and views of garden, Hostel Dream belko is situated in Marrakech, 1.

3 km from Bahia Palace. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a 24-hour front desk and a shared kitchen, along with free WiFi throughout the property.

The accommodation features room service, a concierge service and organising tours for guests.

All air-conditioned units at the hostel come with a shower, a hairdryer, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and DVD Player.

All rooms have a desk. Hostel Dream belko offers a barbecue. The area is popular for hiking, and bike hire and car hire are available at the accommodation.

Koutoubia Mosque is 1. 5 km from Hostel Dream belko, while Djemaa El Fna is 2 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Marrakech-Menara, 6 km from the hostel, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
