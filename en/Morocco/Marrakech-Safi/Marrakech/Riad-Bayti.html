Description

Situated a 5-minute walk from Jamaâ El Fna Square, this Moroccan riad features a rooftop terrace with sun beds, a plunge pool and a lounge with a TV and books.

Car rental is available on site. With a traditional décor, each room at Riad Bayti includes a seating area and a private bathroom with original Tadellakt.

A Moroccan breakfast is served on the terrace, on the patio or in your room. You can sample local specialties in the dining room, which features a fireplace.

Massages can be done on site and a hammam is available next to the riad. A baby-sitting service is provided upon request, and guided tours and excursions are organised by the staff.

Al Bahia Palace and El Badii Palace are a 3-minute walk away. Marrakech Airport and train Station are 7 km away.
