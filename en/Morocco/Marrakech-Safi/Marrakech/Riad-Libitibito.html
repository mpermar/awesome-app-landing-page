Description

Riad Libitibito is a traditional Moroccan house, located in the heart of Marrakech’s Medina, 10 minutes’ from Jamaâ El Fna Square.

It offers a heated pool and free Wi-Fi. The guest rooms are set around an interior garden and patio.

They are all air-conditioned and have private bathrooms. The Libitibito’s restaurant serves traditional Moroccan cuisine, which is prepared with fresh, seasonal produce.

In addition to a 24-hour front desk, Riad Libitibito offers airport shuttle and tour desk services.
