Description

Set in Marrakech, 12 km from Bahia Palace, L'OLIVERAIE DE L'ATLAS offers accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a bar.

With a shared lounge, the 4-star guest house has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom.

The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.

At the guest house, the rooms include a patio with a garden view. At L'Oliveraie de l'Atlas every room has a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Continental and buffet breakfast options are available every morning at the accommodation.

L'OLIVERAIE DE L'ATLAS offers 4-star accommodation with a hot tub and terrace. The area is popular for cycling, and bike hire and car hire are available at the guest house.

Yves Saint Laurent Museum is 12 km from L'Oliveraie de l'Atlas, while Majorelle Gardens is 13 km away.

The nearest airport is Marrakech-Menara, 18 km from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
