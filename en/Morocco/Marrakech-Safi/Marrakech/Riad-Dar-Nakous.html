Description

Offering a plunge pool and table d'hotes, Riad Dar Nakous is located in Marrakech.

Free WiFi access is available. Each room here will provide you with air conditioning, a patio and a seating area.

Featuring a bath or shower, private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and bathrobes.

At Riad Dar Nakous you will find a 24-hour front desk and barbecue facilities. Other facilities offered at the property include a shared lounge, a tour desk and laundry facilities.

The guest house is 400 metres from Bahia Palace, 500 metres from Djemaa El Fna and 500 metres from Marrakech Medina.
