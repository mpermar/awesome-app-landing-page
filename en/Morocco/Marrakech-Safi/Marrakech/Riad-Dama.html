Description

Just a 10-minute walk from JemaÃ¢ El Fna Square in central Marrakech, this 19th-century guest house has a pool, a terrace and a patio with a fountain.

Guests are invited to relax by the fireplace in one of the lounge areas with a hot tub.

The air-conditioned guest rooms at Riad Dama are warmly decorated in an authentic Moroccan style.

All rooms include an en suite bathroom and some open up to a terrace. Riad Dama includes a massage, tradition hammam and treatments salon.

Traditional Moroccan dishes are prepared upon request and a continental breakfast is served daily on the terrace or in the dining room.

Massage treatments and a library are available on site. The guest house proposes car rental and laundry services, and can help organise excursions in the area.

Free Wi-Fi is provided in public areas. Marrakech Train Station is 2. 7 km away and Marrakech Menara Airport is reached by car in 15 minutes.
