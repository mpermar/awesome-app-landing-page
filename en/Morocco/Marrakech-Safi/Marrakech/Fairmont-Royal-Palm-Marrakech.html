Description

Offering an outdoor pool, Fairmont Royal Palm Marrakech is located a 20-minute drive from Marrakech.

Free Wi-Fi access is available. Rooms here will provide you with a balcony. Private bathrooms also come with a bath and shower.

You can enjoy garden, golf or mountains views from all the rooms. At Fairmont Royal Palm Marrakech you will find 4 restaurant and 2 bars.

Other facilities offered include a golf course and a spa. The hotel is 12 km from the Medina, 13 km from Conference Palace and 15 km from Majorelle Gardens.
