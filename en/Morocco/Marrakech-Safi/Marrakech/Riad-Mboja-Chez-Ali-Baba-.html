Description

Located in the heart of Marrakech’s medina, a 15-minute walk from Jamaâ El Fna Square, this traditional riad offers air-conditioned rooms and a rooftop terrace with views of the Koutoubia.

With free Wi-Fi, guest rooms at Riad Mboja are colourful and decorated with traditional Moroccan furnishings.

Each en suite bathroom features tadelakt walls and is equipped with a hairdryer and free toiletries upon arrival.

Breakfast is available daily at the riad. For other meals, guests are invited to sample traditional Moroccan cusine served in the dining room.

During their stay guests can relax in the riad’s Moroccan-style lounge equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels or on the seating area on the terrace when the weather is nice.

The riad is located 200 metres from Bab Doukala and a 15-minute drive from Marrakech Airport.
