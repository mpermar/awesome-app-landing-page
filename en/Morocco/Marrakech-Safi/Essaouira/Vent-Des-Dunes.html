Description

Vent des Dunes is located in a residential neighbourhood a 2-minute walk from the beach.

It offers a garden and free Wi-Fi available in all areas. Rooms at Vent des Dunes are simply decorated and equipped with a private bathroom.

Some rooms have a terrace or balcony. Vent des Dunes serves breakfast on the large terrace that overlooks the city and you can enjoy traditional Moroccan cuisine upon request.

Guests are invited to explore the hotels garden or relax with mint tea in the lounge.

Vent des Dunes is a 15-minute walk from Essaouiras medina. The staff is available to provide information on restaurants and activities in Essaouira.
