Description

Decorated in traditional Moroccan colors, the guest rooms at Semiramis are spacious and have a private balcony. Each is air-conditioned and has free Wi-Fi access and satellite TV.

Le Chellah restaurant serves traditional cuisine. Next to the swimming pool, both La Strada and L’Oasis serve Mediterranean dishes. The hotel’s Bar Aladin opens onto the landscaped grounds and serves a range of drinks.

Additional facilities at Semiramis include a meeting room, fitness center and spa with a steam bath and massages. Guests may also play tennis or volleyball and dance the night away in the nightclub.

The hotel is easily accessed from Marrakesh International Airport, 15 minutes away. The center of the city is a 10 minute drive away and private parking is free on site.
