Description

At the guesthouse, all rooms include a balcony. A private bathroom is provided in all guest rooms, while certain rooms come with a DVD player. Guest rooms at Ksar Salha come with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

A continental breakfast is available every morning at the property.

The accommodation has a playground. The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental and car rental are available at Ksar Salha.

Speaking Arabic and English at the 24-hour front desk, staff are willing to help at any time of the day.

El Badi Palace is 12 miles from the guesthouse, while Saadian Tombs is 12 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Menara Airport, 14 miles from the property.
