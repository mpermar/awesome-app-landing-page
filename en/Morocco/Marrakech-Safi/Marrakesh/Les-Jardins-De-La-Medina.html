Description

Each modern, air-conditioned and soundproof room at Les Jardins De La Medina features a shared balcony with views of the pool and garden. They come with free Wi-Fi, satellite TV and a private bathroom.

Les Jardins’ restaurant and bar has views of the gardens and serves a mixture of Mediterranean and Moroccan cuisine, using fresh, local produce. Moroccan cooking courses are available.

Activities in the area include hiking and horseback riding, and tours are available to book from the 24-hour front desk. Agdal Gardens is less than half a mile from the Riad, and Marrakesh Menara Airport is 4 miles away.

Kasbah is a great choice for travelers interested in markets, old-town exploration and food.
