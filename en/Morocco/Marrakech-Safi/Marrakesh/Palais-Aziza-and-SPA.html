Description

All air-conditioned rooms offer a patio or balcony with views of the pool or grounds. They include a flat-screen TV, mini-bar and spa bath, while others also feature an iPod docking station and fireplace. Egyptian cotton linen and bathrobes are provided in all rooms.

Palais Aziza & SPA offers villas with landscape views and private outdoor pools. The villas includes an equipped kitchen and a living room with a flat-screen TV and satellite channels. All private bathrooms are fitted with a hot tub and free toiletries.

The Maroliano restaurant serves Moroccan and Italian cuisine. There is also La Pergola restaurant, which serves summer-style cuisine.

The Medina is just 5 mi away and Marrakech’s famous luxury golf courses are within 4.3 mi of the hotel. Free Wi-Fi access is available in the entire hotel and free private parking is possible on site.
