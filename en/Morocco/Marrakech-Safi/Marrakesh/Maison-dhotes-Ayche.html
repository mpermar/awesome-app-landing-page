Description

At Maison d'hôtes Ayche, rooms contain a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, and certain rooms at Maison d'hôtes Ayche have a balcony. The rooms feature a closet.

à la carte and halal breakfast options are available each morning at Maison d'hôtes Ayche. At Maison d'hôtes Ayche you'll find a restaurant serving Moroccan cuisine.

Guests at Maison d'hôtes Ayche will be able to enjoy activities in and around Marrakesh, like cycling.

Conference Palace is 10 miles from Maison d'hôtes Ayche, while Saadian Tombs is 11 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Menara Airport, 8 miles from Maison d'hôtes Ayche.
