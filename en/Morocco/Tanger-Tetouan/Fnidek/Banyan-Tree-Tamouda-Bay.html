Description

Offering a beach club, Banyan Tree Tamouda Bay is a luxury all-pool villa resort located in a 10-minute drive from Marina Smir.

The resort has a sun terrace, a main outdoor pool and a private beach area. Banyan Tree Tamouda Bay features free WiFi throughout the property.

Every villa has a private pool. They all include flat-screen TV, a kettle in the room, and Blu-ray player.

Each villa comes with a separate living room, a kitchenette, private bathroom equipped with a hot tub.

Some villas have a direct access to the beach. For your comfort, you will find slippers, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

With 4 restaurants and a 24-hour in-villa dining, guests can also enjoy authentic Thai gastronomy, Moroccan cuisine, Mediterranean-inspired seafood dishes and several international favorites.

Guests can enjoy a drink at the bar. The resort offers modern meeting and event spaces with a 180 square metre ballroom and 2 boardrooms.

Banyan Tree Tamouda Bay includes an Asian-inspired spa using a "high touch, low tech" approach which features six Deluxe Treatment Rooms, two Royal Treatment Rooms, a Hydro and Vitality Pool, Hammam, Gym room and a special Yoga Pavilion.

Tetouan is a 30-minute drive from Banyan Tree Tamouda Bay. The nearest airport is Sania Ramel Airport, is 20 minutes drive from the property.
