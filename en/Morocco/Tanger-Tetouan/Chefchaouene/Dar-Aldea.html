Description

Dar Aldea is set in Chefchaouene, 4. 2 km from Mnt, J. Tissouka. Featuring a terrace, this property is situated a short distance from Outa El Hammam Square and Mohammed 5 Square.

Attractions in the area include Ras Elma Water Source, 600 metres away, or Khandak Semmar, located 1 km from the property.

All guest rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area. With a private bathroom, certain rooms at Dar Aldea also boast a terrace.

A breakfast is available daily at the property. Speaking Arabic, English, Spanish and French, staff are willing to help at any time of the day at the reception.
