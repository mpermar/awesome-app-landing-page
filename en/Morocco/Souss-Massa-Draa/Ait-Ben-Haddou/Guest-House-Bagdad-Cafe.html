Description

Bagdad Café is located just 200 metres from the famous Kasbah of Ait Ben Haddou. It offers traditional Moroccan rooms with free Wi-Fi.

The hotel is in the heart of the village and has a restaurant that serves traditional Moroccan cuisine.

Guests can relax with an evening drink in the bar. Bagdad Café offers excursions in the surrounding area with an official guide, including trips to visit nearby oases and the Kasbah.
