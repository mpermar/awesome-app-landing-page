Description

With a large terrace looking on the garden or the sea, rooms at Paradis Plage have a modern décor with a Moroccan touch. They include an equipped kitchen, a bathroom, and a dining and seating area.

A continental and American buffet-style breakfast is served daily. The restaurant proposes an organic and healthy cuisine with Moroccan and international specialties.

Surf, stand up paddle and yoga are only some of the activities offered by Paradis Plage. There is also an outdoor cinema with a projection every night, and a zen garden with hot tub and hamacs. The kids club organizes beach games and cinema sessions on the beach for children.

Agadir Airport is 50 minutes' drive away and the city center is a 25-minute drive. Essaouira city center is 93 mi away.
