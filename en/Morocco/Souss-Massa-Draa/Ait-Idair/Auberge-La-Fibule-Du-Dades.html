Description

Set in 25 km from Boumalne and 56 km from Tinerhir, Auberge La Fibule Du Dades boasts a sun terrace and views of the mountains.

Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. Certain units feature a seating area where you can relax.

Some units have views of the river or garden. The rooms come with a private bathroom.

Auberge La Fibule Du Dades features free WiFi. You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property.
