Description

Studiotel Afoud’s studio apartments are self-catering and have air-conditioning, cable TV and a private terrace or balcony.

It sits just 900 metres from the beach, and features a small swimming pool. Free Wi-Fi access is available in the studios, which are individually decorated and feature wooden furnishings.

The en suite bathrooms are fitted with a shower. You can enjoy meals and a drink in the restaurant and the coffee shop at Studiotel Afoud, or a coffee in the cafe lounge, Shisha.

There is also a currency exchange service and a tour desk in the 24-hour reception.

The property's guests have free access to a private beach located a 15-minute walk away.

The Royal Tennis club is 150 metres away. Studiotel Afoud is located near the centre of Agadir, close to the commercial area with many bakeries and minimarkets.
