Description

Kenzi Europa is located in Agadir, 2 km from the Kasbah and just 100 metres from a private beach.

the property offers free access to the private beach. Free Wi-Fi access is provided in all rooms.

The air-conditioned guest rooms at the Kenzi Europa are decorated with modern amenities and equipped with satellite TV, a minibar and a fridge.

They each have a private balcony with a view of the pool or the sea. The Kenzi features an outdoor children’s play area, tennis court and a heated outdoor swimming pool.

Hotel Kenzi Europa offers 2 restaurants serving Moroccan and international cuisine.

Guests can also relax with a drink in the English piano bar, which offers entertainment each evening.

This hotel is situated a 10-minute walk from the city centre and Agadir Marina is 1 km away.
