Description

Guest rooms at the Timoulay Hotel are spacious and have a terrace with a view of the hotel’s grounds, pool and the sea. All rooms are air conditioned and have satellite TV.

A full breakfast buffet is served daily at Restaurant Ayour, which also offers traditional and regional dishes.

At the hotel’s Spa, guests have access to a hammam, sauna and fitness room. There is also a range of recreational activities close to the Timoulay, such as scuba diving and horseback riding.
