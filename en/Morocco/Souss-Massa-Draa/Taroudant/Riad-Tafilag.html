Description

This traditional riad is located in Taroudant, between the ramparts and the Medina.

Featuring a swimming pool and panoramic terraces with tents, it offers rooms with private terraces and free Wi-Fi internet access.

The guest rooms are decorated with Moroccan carving and textiles. Each room and suite is air conditioned and some have access to the internal patio.

Breakfast is offered daily and can be taken on one of 4 terraces. The riad also has a restaurant with fireplace that serves traditional Moroccan cuisine.

At Riad Tafilag guests can enjoy a traditional hammam heated by a wood fire and a range of massage treatments.

Guests also have access to a large lounge with satellite TV. The Riad Tafilag is also in close proximity to Place Assarag and Place Talmoklate.
