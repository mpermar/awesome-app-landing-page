Description

Located in the historical centre of Taroudant, Dar Tourkia offers air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi access and views of the Kasbah.

It features a terrace with sun loungers and a garden with an outdoor swimming pool.

Each room includes a private bathroom with a shower. A continental breakfast is served daily, and you can enjoy local specialities at the on-site restaurant.

There is a shared traditional lounge with a fireplace and a TV, as well as a home cinema room for film projections.

Dar Tourkia has a hammam, and you can enjoy beauty treatments and massages at an extra charge.

The property can also provide organisation for transfers and local tours and excursions.
