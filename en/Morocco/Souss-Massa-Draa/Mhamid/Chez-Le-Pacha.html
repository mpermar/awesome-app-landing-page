Description

Chez le Pacha is on a 3-hectares parkland, located in Palm Grove in M'hamid El Ghizlane, close access from Zagora.

It offers an outdoor pool with a furnished terrace. Each air-conditioned room is in a blending traditional style and modern décor.

They are equipped with a private bathroom. The restaurant offers breakfast and traditional Moroccan cuisine served in a tradition setting.

Guests can enjoy a drink at the hotel’s bar while using the free Wi-Fi. There is a 24-hour front desk with concierge service and tour information.

Free private parking is provided. Free bicycle rental is available allowing guests to explore the region.

Activities such as hiking, horse riding and camel excursions are also organised at the Chez le Pacha.
