Description

At Un Thé Au Bout Du Monde, rooms contain a closet and a flat-screen TV. The rooms include a private bathroom equipped with a shower and free toiletries, while selected rooms also feature a kitchen.

A continental or à la carte breakfast is served at the property.

Un Thé Au Bout Du Monde has a terrace. A variety of popular activities are available in the area around the property, including cycling and hiking.

Speaking Arabic, English, Spanish and French at the reception, staff are ready to help around the clock.

Sidi Ifni is 21 miles from Un Thé Au Bout Du Monde.

04/06/2018

Perfect location to find some peace and quiet!

"Perfect location to find some peace and quiet! Higly recommendable for families or couples. The name says it all: Hotel in the middle of nowhere, very calm, surrounded by an amazing landscape:Beautiful beach close by, stunning coastline which invites you for long walks.Hotel owned pool as a nice extra.Amazing hotel staff: very welcoming, genuinely friendly and very helpful with whatever we needed.All hotel facilities are perfectly clean and you can feel that every single detail was designed and arranged with lots of love. Kitchen offers everything you need. Good alternative to the rather expensive hotel restaurant (which is really delicious, though).Very cheap local bus connection to Mirleft, Sidi Ifni or Tiznit once per hour. The small town of Mirleft has a couple of restaurants, very nice artisan/handicraft shops (typical Moroccan lamps, Argan products, pottery, scarfs etc.), surf schools, Moroccan style shops and market to get groceries, toiletries etc.... Very welcoming and relaxed atmosphere, nobody pressures you to buy anything >>>> quite the opposite of Morocco's hectic Medinas (like in Fes or Marrakesh).In a nutshell: Everything you need is close by and easily reachable. If you are searching for the perfect place for a cozy, relaxing and calm getaway, you don't have to look any further... I can tell you: you just found it!"
