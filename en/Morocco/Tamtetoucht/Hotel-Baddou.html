Description

All rooms at the hotel are equipped with a TV and a kitchen. Hotel Baddou has certain units with mountain views, and rooms have a terrace. The rooms at the accommodation come with a seating area.

A à la carte breakfast is served each morning at the property. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of Moroccan dishes and also offers halal and dairy-free options.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental is available at Hotel Baddou.

Marabout - Sidi Mohamed Tmassint is 1.7 miles from the hotel.
