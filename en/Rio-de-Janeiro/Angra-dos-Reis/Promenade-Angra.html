Description

In the Promenade Angra you can do water activities, play tennis, soccer and volleyball.

The family-friendly establishment has an outdoor pool, beach and poolside bar.

The hotel restaurant serves Brazilian and international dishes. There is also a lobby bar.

The Promenade Angra offers free internet, parking, a tour desk and a business center.
