Description

Vila Galé Eco Resort has a 1,500 m² water park with swimming pools for adults and children, an Olympic rowing lake and an outdoor and heated hot tub.

The Satsanga spa has a sauna and Japanese ofurô bath. Guests can also enjoy shiatsu and other treatments and massages.

The Vila Galé Eco Resort has 4 bars and 4 different restaurants offering a selection of regional and international dishes, such as sushi and pasta.

The Vila Galé Eco Resort offers hiking activities in the Atlantic Forest and a variety of water sports.

The hotel is 7 km from Angra dos Reis, which offers connections to Ilha Grande.
