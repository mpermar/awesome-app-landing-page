Description

Some rooms have a seating area. All consist of air conditioning, TV, bathrobes and hairdryer.

The establishment has a 24-hour reception, an ATM and several shops. The beach service includes chairs, umbrellas and towels. There is also a children's club, which opens daily and offers both indoor and outdoor leisure activities.

Various activities are organized for a fee, such as a ferry ride on the Marapendi lagoon, daily physical activities, tasting of caipirinhas, access to the Atiaia spa and the fitness relaxation room, bicycle, 24-hour gym access and welcome drinks at Cantô restaurant.

The Grand Hyatt Rio de Janeiro is located near the Grand Hyatt Rio de Janeiro, 3.2 km from the Arts City and 26 km from the nearest Santos Dumont Airport.

Barra da Tijuca is a great choice for travelers interested in sunsets, sandy beaches and food.
