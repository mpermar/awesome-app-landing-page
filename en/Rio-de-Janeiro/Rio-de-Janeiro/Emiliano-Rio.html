Description

Some rooms have a seating area and all are equipped with a flat-screen TV, a coffee maker and a private bathroom with a bath or shower, a bidet, bathrobes, slippers, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

The Emiliano Rio's restaurant serves modern Brazilian cuisine, made with organic products. Hotel facilities include a spa with fitness center. The roof has a pool and views of the beach.

Emiliano Rio is 1.2 km from Ipanema Beach, 7.5 km from the famous Sugarloaf Mountain and 8.4 km from Christ the Redeemer. The nearest Santos Dumont Airport is 8 km away, while Galeão International Airport is 20 km away.

Copacabana is a great choice for travelers interested in walking along the beach, sightseeing and sandy beaches.
