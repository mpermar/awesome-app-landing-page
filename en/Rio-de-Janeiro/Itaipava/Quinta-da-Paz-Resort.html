Description

Most rooms at Quinta da Paz offer views of the mountainous valley or the garden. They have air conditioning. They are equipped with a fireplace, hot tub and LCD TV. Some rooms are bigger.

The Quinta Imperial restaurant serves a varied menu of international dishes. Drinks and snacks are offered at Varandas da Quinta, next to the garden. A daily buffet breakfast is also offered.

Guests have at their disposal a room with home cinema and an outdoor hot tub. Wine tastings are organized in the complex's cellar. You can organize hiking trails at the property. It also has a games room.

The center of Itaipava is just 12 km away and offers shops and a varied leisure offer. The historic center of Petrópolis is only 25 minutes away by car.
