Description

The establishment has a heated pool with waterfall, sauna, bar, meeting facilities, shared lounge and tour desk.

Some rooms at the Willisau Hotel have a balcony, and all have a flat-screen cable TV, a minibar, a private bathroom with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries and a view of the mountains and the city.

The hotel is 1.2 km from Comary Farm, 2.3 km from the Soberbo Viewpoint and 4.2 km from Casa Portuguesa Club. Galeão International Airport is 49 km away and the center of Teresópolis is 3.5 km away.
