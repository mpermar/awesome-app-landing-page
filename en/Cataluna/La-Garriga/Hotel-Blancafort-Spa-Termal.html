Description

This elegant spa hotel is located in the modern Catalan city of La Garriga and enjoys a perfect environment to enjoy the Mediterranean climate, wonderful landscapes and a variety of relaxing natural treatments.

The hotel spa is large and offers mineral-medicinal water from the springs of La Garriga. There are several thematic areas, such as a Roman bath and an area with an oriental atmosphere. In addition, a variety of massages and beauty treatments are offered. All guests have free access to the spa area and outdoor pools.

This 4-star luxury hotel has an elegant and contemporary style. It offers 2 elegant restaurants, spacious and elegant rooms and convention center.

Rooms at Hotel Blancafort Spa Termal feature air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, minibars, and safes. The private bathroom includes a bathrobe and slippers.

In the immediate vicinity, guests can find several examples of the famous modernist architecture of Catalonia. The property is a 2-minute walk from La Garriga train station, which connects with the lively city of Barcelona in 30 minutes.
