Description

The apartments have air conditioning, a furnished terrace with views of the sea or the pool and a well-equipped kitchen and dining area. The living room has a sofa bed, safe and satellite TV. In the private bathroom there is a hair dryer.

The Voramar complex has a buffet restaurant and a café-bar by the pool. You can order packed lunches and a supermarket is a 2-minute walk away.

A varied entertainment program is offered for children and adults. There is also a games room, a playground and a mini club.

At the reception, which is open 24 hours a day, you can rent a car or a bicycle. You can use the parking for a fee and, in addition, the A7 motorway is well connected.
