Description

Estival Centurión's restaurant serves a selection of traditional Spanish dishes. In addition, there is a bar and a terrace overlooking the sea. An entertainment program is offered at night.

The hotel provides free limited Wi-Fi and there is also high-speed internet connection, available for a fee. At the tour desk you can request information about the surroundings and rent cars and bicycles.

The Estival is just 5 minutes drive from the center of Cambrils and its marina. Buses that go to PortAventura theme park stop in front of the hotel.
