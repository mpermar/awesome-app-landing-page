Description

Rooms at the Torre Laurentii feature classic rustic décor and feature a TV, DVD player, minibar and a private bathroom with free toiletries.

The hotel serves a continental breakfast with cold cuts, cheeses, cakes and bread. The restaurant also serves homemade meals made with local products.

The nearby natural area of the Alta Garrotxa houses multiple hiking trails. In addition, Figueras and its famous Dalí Museum are about 20 minutes away by car.
