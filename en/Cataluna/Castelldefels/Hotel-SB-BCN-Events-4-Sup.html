Description

The SB BCN Events restaurant offers Mediterranean cuisine. It also provides breakfast and a lunch menu with a dish of the day. The lobby bar is elegant and serves cocktails.

The SB BCN Events is located 15 minutes by car from Barcelona airport and 20 minutes from the center of this city. Free parking is available at the property, which is a 25-minute walk from Castelldefels Beach and a 4-minute walk from a large shopping center.
