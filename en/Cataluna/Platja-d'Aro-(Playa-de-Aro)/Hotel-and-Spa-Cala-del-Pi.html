Description

The rooms of the Hotel & Spa Cala del Pi have a hydromassage shower and a private terrace. Many of them offer views of the Mediterranean Sea. All rooms are equipped with satellite TV, pillow menu and Tous toiletries.

Hotel Cala del Pi has a heated indoor pool, waterfalls, ice fountains, Finnish sauna and Turkish bath. It also houses a small gym.

Its Rosa dels Vents restaurant serves Mediterranean cuisine made with seasonal products. The café bar has a summer terrace overlooking the sea.

Hotel & Spa Cala del Pi is located on the Costa Brava, just 27 km from Girona-Costa Brava Airport.
