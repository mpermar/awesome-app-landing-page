Description

The hotel is located next to a 16th-century castle and overlooks the Pyrenees and the Cadí-Moixeró natural park. It is located 500 meters from the old town of La Seu d'Urgell and just 10 km from Andorra. There is free public parking.

Hotel Castell de Ciutat has a wellness center with indoor pool, Turkish bath, gym and outdoor hot tub with mountain views. Treatments and massages are also available, for a fee.

The Castell de Ciutat has 2 restaurants, the Tàpies and the Katia. Tàpies offers creative gourmet dishes and Katia serves traditional specialties made with fresh local ingredients. There is also a cafeteria and a lounge.
