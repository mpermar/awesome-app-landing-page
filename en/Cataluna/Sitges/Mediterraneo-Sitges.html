Description

The Mediterranean apartments are modern and include a flat-screen TV, an equipped kitchen, a marble bathroom and a private terrace, most of them with sea views.

The property has extensive gardens and a solarium.

This establishment is just 5 minutes from the center of Sitges, with its shops, bars and restaurants, and about 40 minutes by train from Barcelona.
