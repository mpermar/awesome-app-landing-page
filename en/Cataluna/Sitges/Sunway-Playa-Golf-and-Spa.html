Description

All rooms at Playa Golf and SPA have air conditioning, tiled floors and satellite TV. The apartments have a modern kitchen with a hob, microwave and washing machine.

The Playa Golf has its own supermarket, a fitness center and a games room. The reception is open 24 hours.

Sunway Playa Golf and Spa has a spa and offers hammam, sauna and massages for a fee, as well as a free indoor pool with hot tub.

The Sunway Playa Golf and SPA complex has 3 restaurants offering a wide variety of Mediterranean dishes. There is also a bar overlooking the Mediterranean.

The hotel provides free bicycles, subject to availability. The center of Sitges is 10 minutes away by bicycle. You can easily reach Barcelona by car, on the C32 road, or by train from Sitges station.

All further older children or adults are charged EUR 35 per night for extra beds.
Maximum number of extra beds in the room: 1.
Extra beds and / or cots are available upon request and must be confirmed by the accommodation.
Supplements are not automatically calculated in the total amount and must be paid separately during the stay.
