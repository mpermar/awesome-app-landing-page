Description

The rooms and apartments have a balcony, satellite TV and a private bathroom.

The Costa Encantada apartments have a lounge area with a flat-screen TV and a sofa bed. The kitchenette has a fridge.

Aparthotel Costa Encantada offers a heated indoor pool. There is also a sauna and gym available to guests.

International dishes are served in the buffet restaurant and light snacks are available at the bar. There is also a pizzeria and a disco bar on site.

Tossa de Mar is 15 minutes drive from the complex and Girona, 25 minutes.
