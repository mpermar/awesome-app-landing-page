Description

All rooms at the Evenia Olympic Palace feature air conditioning, 2 satellite TVs, a seating area, a private balcony, a fridge, a safe and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and toiletries.

The Olympic Resort is surrounded by gardens and features a restaurant, poolside bar, cafeteria and disco. For children there is a very complete entertainment program, a playground and a mini club.

The Resort has a sports club with a water circuit with an indoor pool, sauna, hammam and hot tub. The sports club's fitness center offers a spinning, bodybuilding and aerobics room. Access to all facilities comes at an additional cost.

The reception is open 24 hours a day and offers car rental services. Parking comes at an extra cost. Wi-Fi is available in public areas for a fee.
