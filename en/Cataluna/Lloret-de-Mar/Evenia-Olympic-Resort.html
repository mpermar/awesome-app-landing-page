Description

Rooms at the Evenia Olympic Resort feature air conditioning, a flat-screen satellite TV, a safe, a private bathroom with a hairdryer and toiletries and a private balcony.

The hotel has several buffet restaurants serving local and international cuisine. There is also a poolside bar, a disco and a varied entertainment program for adults and children. The hotel has an internet zone.

For a fee you can access the Sport Club, which has a hot tub, sauna, heated indoor pool and Turkish bath. There are also squash courts and a fitness center with varied classes.

Evenia Olympic Resort is located about 10 minutes walk from the center of Lloret, 10 minutes drive from Tossa and less than 1 hour drive from Barcelona.
