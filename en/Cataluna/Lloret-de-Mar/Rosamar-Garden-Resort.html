Description

The Rosamar Garden Hotel's buffet restaurant serves international cuisine and offers show cooking. There is also a poolside bar where you can have a drink or snack.

The hotel's sports facilities include a basketball court and a ping-pong table. There are also water slides at the hotel's Aquagarden Club.

You can reach the beautiful town of Tossa de Mar in 12 minutes by car from the Rosamar. Barcelona is an hour away by car.
