Description

Rooms at Hotel Marsol have air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, satellite TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer. A safe is available for a fee.

The hotel's buffet restaurant serves a varied breakfast and a selection of international dishes for lunch and dinner. There is also a café-bar with terrace, located on the seafront.

The Marsol hotel is a 5-minute walk from the center of Lloret de Mar, where there is a wide variety of restaurants, bars and nightclubs. The church of Sant Romà is only 100 meters away.
