Description

The Rec de Palau villas and apartment combine traditional design with modern finishes. All units have attractive décor and are equipped with heating and free Wi-Fi.

The villas and the apartment are located on a large estate with gardens, vineyards and fruit trees.

Cadaqués is surrounded by the beautiful park of Cap de Creus, whose coast is ideal for fishing, diving and snorkeling.
