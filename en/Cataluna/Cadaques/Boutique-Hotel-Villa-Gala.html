Description

The rooms have a balcony or terrace, air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fridge and a coffee maker. All have a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries. Most have a rain shower, while others have a bathtub. All have a closet.

A buffet breakfast is served daily.

The reception staff, open 24 hours a day, speaks English, German, Spanish and Catalan.

Girona-Costa Brava Airport is 60 km away.
