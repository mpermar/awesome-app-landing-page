Description

The individually decorated rooms have a lot of charm, and the bedrooms and bathrooms are exterior. You can walk through the interiors of the Aiguaclara and discover the curious facilities that go back to its glorious past. The staircase offers beautiful wrought iron railings and beautiful tile floors. The original facilities contrast with contemporary furniture and modern design details.

The hotel is next to the best beaches and golf courses on the Costa Brava. Make the most of the sun in this region, most of the year. After visiting the tourist attractions, you can enjoy a delicious meal in the hotel restaurant.
