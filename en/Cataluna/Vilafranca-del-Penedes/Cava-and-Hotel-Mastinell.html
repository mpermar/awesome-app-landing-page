Description

The hotel won the Gold Medal of Architecture for the best design in Shanghai in 2011. The rooms have a contemporary style and represent the interior of wine bottles, with circular windows with bubble-shaped meshes overlooking the vineyards.

All rooms have air conditioning, flat-screen satellite TV, Nespresso coffee machine, free Wi-Fi and a private bathroom with free toiletries.

The restaurant offers live cooking and creative Catalan cuisine with local wine and cava. The hotel also has a shop and wine therapy room.

The Penedès region around the hotel is ideal for hiking and cycling or horseback riding. In addition, the beaches of Sitges and Vilanova i la Geltrú are about 25 km away.

The hotel is just 5 minutes from the E15 motorway, which allows access to the coast and the center of Barcelona in less than 45 minutes.
