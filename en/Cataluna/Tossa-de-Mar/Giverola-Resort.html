Description

This accommodation is a 4-minute walk from the beach. The Giverola Resort is located on a hillside, 4 km from Tossa de Mar, and offers magnificent views of the sea. This complex also houses a fitness center, several tennis courts and an outdoor pool with slides.

The apartments at Giverola Resort are bright and have tiled floors. They also have a kitchen and a living area with satellite TV.

The Giverola offers a wide variety of activities and entertainment programs for children and adults, such as snorkeling, aerobics, beach volleyball and live jazz performances on the terrace.

The hotel has 3 restaurants, including a Spanish restaurant and 2 restaurants located in Cala Giverola. There is also a cafeteria and 3 bars, one of them located next to the beach.

The complex provides transfer services to Tossa de Mar. There are also funicular services to the beach. The beautiful town of Girona is 45 minutes away by car.
