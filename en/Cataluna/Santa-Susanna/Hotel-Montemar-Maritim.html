Description

Rooms at Montemar Maritim feature satellite TV, free Wi-Fi, a refrigerator and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The Montemar Maritim's buffet restaurant serves international cuisine. In the evenings there is live cooking. The bar offers drinks and a live entertainment program.

Guests can use the hot tub, sauna and indoor swimming pool for an additional fee. The hotel also offers a games room and free Wi-Fi access throughout.

The hotel is very well connected to the AP7 motorway and is located just in front of the Santa Susanna train station. Girona Airport is 35 km away, while Barcelona Airport is 80 km away.
