Description

The rooms at Tahiti Playa are spacious and simply and joyfully decorated. They have air conditioning and satellite TV.

The restaurant serves a buffet and a variety of Catalan dishes. Guests can enjoy a drink or snack at the hotel's café-bar, overlooking the Mediterranean.

Activities such as water aerobics, soccer and dance classes are offered at the Tahiti Playa Hotel. In addition, there are nightly shows and playground for children.

A regular train service connects Santa Susanna with the center of Barcelona in approximately 1 hour. The Illa Fantasia water park is about 40 km away.
