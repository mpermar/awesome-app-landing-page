Description

The hotel restaurant La Falgarona serves Mediterranean cuisine and special diet menus, such as vegetarian and gluten-free dishes, can be prepared. The property, which offers room service, also has a bar.

The rooms, which have exposed wooden beams, are spacious and have plenty of natural light. All are air-conditioned and have a seating area, satellite TV and radio. The bathroom includes free toiletries and a hairdryer.

The Dalí Museum is a 12-minute drive from Mas Falgarona. It is 55 km from Girona-Costa Brava airport.
