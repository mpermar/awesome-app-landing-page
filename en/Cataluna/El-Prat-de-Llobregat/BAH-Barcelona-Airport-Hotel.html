Description

All rooms at the Barcelona Airport Hotel are soundproofed and have air conditioning, flat-screen TV, internet connection and marble bathroom.

Barcelona Airport has a garden terrace, fitness center and paddle tennis courts.

The Barcelona Airport Hotel also has a bar and a restaurant. The Alabastro Bar offers cocktails and tapas and La Sal Marina serves Mediterranean cuisine.

The hotel also has a 24-hour shuttle service to the airport terminals, upon request and according to the terminal. Mas Blau metro station is a 12-minute walk away.
