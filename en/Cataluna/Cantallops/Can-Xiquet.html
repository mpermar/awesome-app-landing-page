Description

At Hotel Can Xiquet you can enjoy a very quiet stay. Its outdoor pool has spectacular views of the Empordà plain.

All rooms at Can Xiquet enjoy plenty of natural light and have a digital TV with satellite channels and a DVD player. Most include a private terrace.

In addition, the rooms offer windows with views, air conditioning, heating and a bright bathroom.

Can Xiquet's restaurant serves delicious breakfasts and Mediterranean dishes.

You can discover the impressive natural area of the Albera by taking a walk or participating in the quad bike and mountain bike excursions organized by the hotel.
