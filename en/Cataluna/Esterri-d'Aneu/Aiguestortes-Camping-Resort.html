Description

The wooden cabins of the Aiguestortes Camping Resort have a traditional style and can accommodate up to 6 people, ideal for family getaways. Accommodations feature kitchens and outdoor dining areas with awnings. On site there is a restaurant, a poolside bar and a playground for children.

During the summer months, guests can use the property's outdoor swimming pool. Various activities can be done in the surrounding countryside, such as hiking, fishing or rafting along the Noguera River.

During the winter months, guests can spend the day at the Sant Maurici National Park or at the nearby ski resorts. The Espot and Baqueira-Beret ski resorts are about 20 km from the Aiguestortes Camping Resort.
