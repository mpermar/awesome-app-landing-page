Description

The rooms overlook the pool or the garden and are air-conditioned, as well as a private bathroom with a shower and bidet. The suite includes a hot tub.

Hotel Hort De Fortunyo has a games room, luggage storage, tour desk and buffet restaurant.

The property is 79 km from Reus Airport and 35 minutes from MotorLand Aragón.
