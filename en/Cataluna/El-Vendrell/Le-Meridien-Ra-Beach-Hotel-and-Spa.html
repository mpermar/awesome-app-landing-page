Description

The hotel occupies a 1920 building and presents a contemporary interior. The rooms are spacious and elegant and feature high ceilings, bathrobes and flat-screen TVs. Most offer lateral or frontal views of the sea.

The complex has 2 restaurants serving buffet meals and Catalan cuisine. There are also bars by the pool, in the lounge and on the terrace.

The Ra Beach's spacious spa offers hydrotherapy and seawater treatments, as well as massages. The children's club organizes activities and craft workshops for children.

The hotel has a beach club, which has a shaded terrace.

Méridien is located in El Vendrell, less than 30 minutes drive from Barcelona-El Prat airport and 45 minutes drive from the center of Barcelona.
