Description

It is in our selection for Barcelona.The Gran Hotel La Florida is a 5 star hotel located on the Tibidabo mountain and offers spectacular views of Barcelona and the Mediterranean Sea. The establishment has a nice free access spa and a free shuttle service to the center of Barcelona 3 times a day (round trip).

The rooms at the La Florida hotel feature free Wi-Fi, an interactive 37-inch plasma TV and a well-lit bathroom with L'Occitane toiletries, bath and shower. Some also include a private balcony.

The panoramic gardens and terraces of the La Florida hotel have views. In the L'Occitane spa there is a 37-meter stainless steel pool, both indoor and outdoor, hot tub, sauna, pressure showers and a variety of fitness equipment.

The complex houses the Barcelonas restaurant, which serves gourmet Catalan, Spanish and Mediterranean cuisine. There are also 2 elegant bars.

This elegant and representative building retains its legendary appearance from 1925 and has the status of a Special Monument.

Horta-Guinardó is a great choice for travelers interested in monuments, architecture and city walks.
