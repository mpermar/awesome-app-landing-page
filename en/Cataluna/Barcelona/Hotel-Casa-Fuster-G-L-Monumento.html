Description

The luxurious Casa Fuster is a monument in itself. The building was built in 1908 by the modernist architect Lluis Domènech i Montaner and has a monument category. His work, including the hotel, has been declared a World Heritage Site by UNESCO.

The elegant rooms of the Casa Fuster G.L Monumento feature air conditioning, satellite TV and a minibar. The private bathroom is equipped with a hairdryer, bathrobe and slippers. Some rooms have a hot tub.

The Casa Fuster Hotel has an elegant restaurant, which serves Mediterranean cuisine. Room service is available and there is a jazz bar and a rooftop chill-out bar.

Concierge service and 24-hour reception are offered. You can rent a car at the tour desk and you can park in the nearby car park for a fee.

Grace is a great choice for travelers interested in monuments, strolling through the city and architecture.
