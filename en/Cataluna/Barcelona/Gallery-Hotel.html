Description

This elegant hotel offers spacious, air-conditioned rooms with flat-screen satellite TV, CD player and a modern bathroom with toiletries, hairdryer and bathrobe.

The Gallery's restaurant has a beautiful garden terrace and serves Mediterranean dishes. On the weekend, a gourmet menu is offered. There is also a bar and a cafeteria.

The Gallery Hotel is next to the gardens of Palau Robert, a tourist information center and gallery. It is a 3-minute walk from La Pedrera de Gaudí and near the Diagonal metro station, which communicates directly with the Sagrada Familia. It is also 600 meters from Passeig de Gràcia train station, which has regular links to the airport.

Eixample is a great choice for travelers interested in architecture, food and shopping.
