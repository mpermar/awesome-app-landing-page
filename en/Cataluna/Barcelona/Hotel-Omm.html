Description

The rooms at the Omm feature elegant and contemporary décor. They overlook the Paseo de Gracia or the hotel's interior garden. All rooms have satellite TV, free Wi-Fi and a spacious bathroom.

The Omm's spa, the Spaciomm, has a steam bath, fitness room and a selection of treatments, such as facial and body treatments, massages and other specialties from all over the world.

The hotel has a Michelin-starred restaurant, Roca Moo, managed by Celler de Can Roca and specialized in modern Catalan cuisine.

The Roca bar offers a selection of innovative street food and dishes to share. The Omm also has the Omm Club and live music.

Catalonia Square and Las Ramblas are a 15-minute walk away, while Diagonal Metro Station is only 70 meters from the hotel.

Eixample is a great choice for travelers interested in architecture, food and shopping.
