Description

The hotel serves a continental buffet breakfast with show cooking that includes many regional products and homemade cakes. The Contempo restaurant prepares typical Mediterranean dishes with a modern twist.

The Catalonia Plaza Cataluña Hotel has a sun terrace with sun loungers, a gym and a swimming pool. There is also a spa and a cabin for spa treatments and massages.

Hotel Catalonia Plaza is a 5-minute walk from the Gothic Quarter of Barcelona and the Palau de la Música Catalana, a beautiful modernist auditorium. On the other side of Plaza Catalunya, just 100 meters from the hotel, there is a bus stop that communicates regularly with the airport.

Eixample is a great choice for travelers interested in architecture, food and shopping.
