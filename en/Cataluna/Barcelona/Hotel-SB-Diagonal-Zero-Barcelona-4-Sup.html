Description

The gym is free access. The spa is only for people over 14 years old and includes a Turkish bath and a Finnish sauna. Beauty treatments and massages are provided for a fee. Stays of 3 or more nights during January or February include free access to the spa for 1 day.

The rooms at the Hotel SB Diagonal Zero Barcelona are soundproofed and feature air conditioning, flat-screen satellite TV, free Wi-Fi, a laptop safe and an iPod docking station.

They also have ironing facilities, tea and coffee making facilities, kettle, touch screen system to regulate light and temperature and a private bathroom with a hairdryer. Bathrobes and slippers are available upon request.
