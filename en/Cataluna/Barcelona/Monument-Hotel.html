Description

All soundproofed rooms are elegantly decorated and feature a flat-screen TV with pay-per-view channels, a Bluetooth sound system and a safe with a plug for charging portable devices. They also have a private bathroom with a foot bath, velvet bathrobe and Jo Malone toiletries. Most rooms have a balcony overlooking the city.

The 3-star Michelin restaurant Lasarte offers a selection of dishes created by chef Martín Berasategui. The on-site bistro-style restaurant, Oria, serves Mediterranean cuisine with fresh ingredients.

Monument Hotel is a 10-minute walk from Plaza Catalunya and Las Ramblas. From the train station of Paseo de Gracia, located 300 meters away, regular trains leave for Barcelona-El Prat airport. Free high speed WiFi is provided.

The hotel occupies the house of Enric Batlló, an architectural jewel of the nineteenth century, which is one of the characteristic works of the Quadrat d'Or of the Eixample and which has the distinction of Monument.

Eixample is a great choice for travelers interested in architecture, food and shopping.
