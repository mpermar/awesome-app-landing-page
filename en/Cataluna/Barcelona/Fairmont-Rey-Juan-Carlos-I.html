Description

The hotel has 2 seasonal pools and houses the Royal Club Fitness and Spa, which offers a luxurious wellness experience.

There are several restaurants and bars, such as The Terrace and the Pool Restaurant & Lounge.The rooms are spacious and include air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, a minibar and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

The Fairmont Rey Juan Carlos I is a 15-minute walk from the Camp Nou stadium of the Football Club Barcelona and 450 meters from the Zona Universitària metro stop, which links to the city center in less than 20 minutes.

Les Corts is a great choice for travelers interested in city walks, city breaks and monuments.
