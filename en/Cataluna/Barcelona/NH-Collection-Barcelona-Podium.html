Description

The hotel rooms are spacious and include air conditioning, pillow menu, minibar, satellite TV and Barcelona guide.

The NH Collection Barcelona Pódium combines a 19th-century facade with a modern interior design. In addition, the establishment is surrounded by other modernist buildings in the Eixample district of Barcelona.

The hotel is near the Gothic quarter, 800 meters from the Plaza de Cataluña and Las Ramblas. The Citadel park and the Olympic port are also a short walk away.

The NH Collection Pódium Hotel is very well connected to all areas of the city thanks to public transport. The Arc de Triomf metro station is only 200 meters away.

The NH Collection Barcelona Pódium's restaurant offers Mediterranean cuisine and has a lounge bar. In the morning a healthy breakfast is served. The restaurant is closed during the summer months.

Eixample is a great choice for travelers interested in architecture, food and shopping.
