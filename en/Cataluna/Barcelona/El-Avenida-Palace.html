Description

It is in our selection for Barcelona.The Avenida Palace Hotel is located on the Gran Vía of Barcelona, ​​a 5-minute walk from Plaza Catalunya, La Rambla and Paseo de Gracia. The establishment features majestic interiors with an impressive staircase and marble columns.

Many famous people have stayed at the Hotel Avenida Palace, such as the Beatles in 1965.

The rooms are elegant and include air conditioning, satellite TV, carpeted or parquet floors, minibar, desk and safe. In addition, they are soundproofed.

The hotel's panoramic terrace, located on the rooftop, overlooks the city, terrace and pool. There is also a lounge bar, 24-hour reception, free Wi-Fi and room service.
