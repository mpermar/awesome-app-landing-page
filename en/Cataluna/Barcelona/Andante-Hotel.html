Description

The modern rooms include air conditioning, parquet floors, laptop safe, flat-screen satellite TV and a bathroom with full-length mirror and a powerful hair dryer.

The hotel offers a buffet breakfast with show cooking and a gluten-free breakfast, upon request. It has a large terrace and a self-service area with a microwave and vending machines for drinks, snacks and snacks.

A car can be rented at the tour desk and private transport service is provided to and from Barcelona El Prat Airport on request and for a fee. The hotel offers luggage storage, laundry service and ironing service.

Ciutat Vella is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, food and culture.
