Description

All rooms at the Cathedral are elegantly decorated in a modern style and include a flat-screen TV, air conditioning and a minibar.

The Ascent Restaurant by Eboca restaurant serves Mediterranean cuisine and overlooks the Pignatelli Palace art gallery, located nearby. For its part, the lobby bar has free internet terminals and printers.

Catalonia Catedral is within a 10-minute walk of several attractions, such as Las Ramblas and Plaza Catalunya. The establishment is also less than 1 km from the marina and the Old Port of Barcelona. The accommodation is also located a 5-minute walk from Jaume I metro station.

Ciutat Vella is a great choice for travelers interested in shopping, food and culture.

Luxurious stay in ideal Barcelona location

"Gorgeous hotel in a perfect location - right next to the Cathedral in the Gothic Quarter, but also steps away from Portal d'Angel, which leads up to Placa Catalunya. Within minutes you can be in the ancient, labyrinthine streets of El Call; in mainstream shops, or strolling with the crowds along La Rambla. Excellent shops, cafes etc nearby - take your time and wander - also an excellent Vietnamese restaurant behind the hotel itself which offers a great value menu del dia. Short walking distance to some lovely areas such as La Ribera and El Born, which are well worth exploring. We stayed in a superior room with terrace and private pool, which was wonderful - a suntrap space with plunge pool, very welcome after a busy day sightseeing. Sitting out there, feet in the cool water, sipping a cold drink and listening to the sounds of the street entertainers outside the cathedral was blissful. We also had a triple room for our older children, which was spacious and well-equipped. Both rooms had massive bathrooms, robes and slippers, coffee machines, and a safe. The hotel has a rooftop pool as well, which has glimpses of the cathedral as a view, and lots of comfortable loungers. Staff were very pleasant and helpful, and the cleaning staff were never seen but always kept the place spotless. Not the cheapest option but definitely recommended for a touch of luxury!"
