Description

The Blaumar's rooms have a bedroom, a living-dining area, a large private terrace or balcony and a kitchenette that includes an electric cooker and fridge.

The hotel has a playground and a children's pool with a slide, offers a day and night entertainment program for children and adults and has a spa that includes a hot tub, sauna and Turkish baths.

The Blaumar's buffet restaurant offers authentic regional dishes made with local products. It also serves Spanish food and organizes theme nights. The hotel also houses a well-known à la carte restaurant, the Arena.

The PortAventura theme park is just a 5-minute drive from the hotel, while the lively center of Salou is 500 meters away.
