Description

This hotel is a reproduction of a traditional Mediterranean town with gardens and a lake. The rooms are decorated in bright colors and feature satellite TV, a minibar and views of the hotel gardens or the pool. All these rooms include free access to the PortAventura theme park during the total stay, including the dates of check-in and check-out.

For an additional fee, access to the Hotel Caribe Wellness spa is offered. Access to the spa is free for some rooms. Services include sauna, hot tub, steam baths, gym and indoor heated pool.

The Port Regata restaurant serves à la carte dishes for breakfast. The Buffet del Mar, Buffet del Port and La Cascada restaurants offer a buffet breakfast and Mediterranean dishes. Cal Pep prepares drinks and snacks by the pool.

The hotel is a 5-minute drive from the beaches of Salou. PortAventura is 10 minutes by car from Reus airport and 20 minutes by car from the AVE station.

The city of Barcelona is 1 hour away by car, bus or train.
