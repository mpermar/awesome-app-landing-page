Description

Hotel Dorada Palace has air-conditioned rooms with flat-screen satellite TV, a minibar and a private bathroom with a hairdryer. There is free WiFi, but the use of the safe comes at an extra cost.

There is a cafeteria, a children's pool and a children's playground. In addition, the Dorada Palace Salou is surrounded by gardens and has a lounge.

The hotel has a buffet restaurant that serves international and children's dishes. There is also a poolside bar and a lounge bar. The establishment also organizes a disco and shows every night.

The hotel offers massages and beauty treatments on request and for a fee. It also has a children's club with a games room where workshops, crafts, film screenings and Wii and PS3 tournaments are organized.

The 6th floor of the Dorada Hotel Salou has a beauty salon, massage service, a gym and a hot tub. In addition, it has magnificent views of Salou and the surrounding area.

Dorada Palace Hotel is a 5-minute drive from PortAventura theme park and close to several golf courses. The Roman city of Tarragona is 20 minutes away by car.
