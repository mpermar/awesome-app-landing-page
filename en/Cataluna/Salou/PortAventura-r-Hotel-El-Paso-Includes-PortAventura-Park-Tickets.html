Description

The outdoor swimming pool at Hotel El Paso Portaventura is surrounded by sun loungers and has a sunken pirate ship in the center. There are also children's pools.

For an additional fee, guests enjoy access to the Wellness spa at Hotel PortAventura and Hotel Caribe. Access to the spa is free for some rooms. It includes a sauna, hot tub, steam baths, gym and indoor heated pool.

El Paso Hotel Salou, located 1 hour drive from Barcelona, ​​consists of several buildings distributed around a central area with gardens, terraces and swimming pool. It is located 100 meters from the train station through which the Barcelona-Valencia line passes.

The all-inclusive regime offers lunch and dinner menus at the hotel's Mexican restaurant or at the PortAventura park (in certain restaurants, during opening hours).

The PortAventura Hotel El Paso, which has its own train station, is just a 5-minute walk from the main entrance to the PortAventura theme park. It is just a 20-minute drive from the high-speed train station, 3.5 km from the A-7 motorway and a 10-minute drive from Reus Airport.

Barcelona airport is 1 hour away by car or train.
