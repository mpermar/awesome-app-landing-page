Description

This typical mountain hotel is ideally located for visiting the Cerdanya Valley and has beautiful views.

In summer you can stroll through the extensive gardens, forests and meadows that surround the establishment. Hotel Adsera has an outdoor pool and water slide and organizes free excursions and activities daily.

In winter, the Adsera is ideal to enjoy the ski resort of La Molina. The hotel has ski storage.
