Description

Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and is fitted with a flat-screen TV. Certain accommodations feature a sitting area where you can relax. Each room comes with a private bathroom. Extras include free toiletries and a hairdryer.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property.

Free use of bicycles is available at this hotel and the area is popular for biking. Snoqualmie is 24 miles from The Maxwell Hotel, and Everett is 25 miles away. The nearest airport is Sea-Tac Airport, 13 miles from Staypineapple at The Maxwell Hotel.
