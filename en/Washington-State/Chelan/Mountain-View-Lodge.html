Description

Free Wi-Fi access is available in every warmly decorated room at Mountain View Lodge. They are also equipped with a microwave, refrigerator, and hairdryer.

An outdoor pavilion at Mountain View boasts a full kitchen, BBQ grills, picnic tables and a fire pit. The lodge lobby offers free snacks and drinks and is stocked with books and magazines.

Accessible by the free shuttle service, Mill Bay Casino and Ma-8 Golf are both 1.5 miles from the lodge.
