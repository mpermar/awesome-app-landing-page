Description

Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and has a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Each room is fitted with a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Guests can enjoy the on-site steam room or work out in the 24-hour fitness centre. There is also a cash machine, a business centre and gift shop at the property.

Bellingham International Airport is 0.6 miles away.
