Description

All rooms at the hotel come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a bath. At Oxford Suites Bellingham each room comes with bed linen and towels.

A Full English/Irish breakfast is available every morning at the property.

The property has a fitness center and an indoor pool.

A business center is at guests' disposal at the accommodation. Staff are always available to help at the reception.

Western Washington University is 3.4 miles from Oxford Suites Bellingham. The nearest airport is Bellingham International Airport, 2.5 miles from the property.
