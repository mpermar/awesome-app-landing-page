Description

A seating area with over-sized lounge chairs and a flat-screen cable TV is provided in each air-conditioned guest room at Tulalip Resort. A mini-bar, coffee machine and an private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries are also offered.

Casual dining options for breakfast, lunch and dinner include the Cedars Café, Eagles Buffet and The Carvery. Upscale dinner choices include fine dining at Tulalip Bay, Pacific Northwest fare at Blackfish and Asian fusion cuisine at Journeys East. The Draft Sports Bar & Grill offers hearty pub fare and 24-hour room service is also available.

The T Spa features a full menu of massage, skin care, body treatments and salon services. Bridal services are also available.

A heated indoor swimming pool, hot tub, fitness center and sauna are provided for guest relaxation. A guest launderette, shops, and conference center are located on site.

Hibulb Cultural Center and Battle Creek Golf Course are within 10 minutes’ drive of Tulalip Resort Marysville. Future of Flight Aviation Center and Boeing Tour is 17 mi away.
