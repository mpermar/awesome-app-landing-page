Description

The lavish Imperia Spa showcases a Roman-inspired indoor pool, a hot tub and a hammam. There are also 2 types of saunas, beauty and body treatments. A children’s pool can be found outside.

Arena Regia Hotel & Spa - Marina Regia Residence’s rooms and suites have a balcony and are air-conditioned. Vintage furniture and modern bathrooms complete each of the accommodations.

The Arena Restaurant & Bar serves exquisite French and modern European cuisine, in a luxurious setting. Italian cuisine is served at the Romeo & Juliet Restaurant & Bar. Half-board and full-board meal plans are available and guests can also enjoy their meals on the sunny terrace.

Arena Regia Hotel&Spa has a private beach area on the Black Sea, just over a quarter mile from the hotel.

Free amenities include access to Wi-Fi in public areas and private parking on site.
