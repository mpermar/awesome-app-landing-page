Description

Guests can enjoy regional cuisine in the Arganeraie’s restaurant.

L'Arganeraie is situated in a 8.5 acre park. Guests can enjoy hiking and camel rides nearby.

The hotel Arganeraie also has a 24-hour reception and free private parking.

"One could not ask for a more romantic, relaxing, exotic hotel than the Domaine de L'Arganeraie.
