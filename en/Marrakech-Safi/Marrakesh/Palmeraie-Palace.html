Description

The spacious rooms feature quality fabrics and individual balconies with views of the swimming pool, golf course or the Atlas Mountains.

The hotel’s 8 restaurants offer a range of traditional and international cuisine from Moroccan cuisine to exquisite Italian and Asian dishes. Buffets and poolside snacks are also available.

All the guests of the Palmeraie Golf Palace are offered a welcome tea, free shuttle downtown and free access to the fitness center. Children between 4 to 12 years old can enjoy whole day in the Mini Club with multiple activities.
