Description

Decorated in traditional Moroccan colors, rooms at LABRANDA Rose Aqua Parc are set around a central inner courtyard. Each features a private balcony or patio and an LCD TV with satellite channels.

The hotel features 3 restaurants serving International, European and Moroccan cuisine. On sunny days guests can relax on the terrace or in the furnished courtyard with a drink from the bar.

Additional facilities include newspapers at the 24-hour reception. Private parking is free on site.

Hivernage is a great choice for travelers interested in sunshine, friendly locals and ambiance.
