Description

Each air-conditioned guest room has a free Wi-Fi connection and provides a 24-hour room service. A flat-screen TV and a closet are included in the rooms of Hotel Du Golf.

A continental breakfast is available daily and guests can choose to dine at one of 2 restaurants with local and international cuisine. There's a snack bar by the pool or guests can enjoy a drink at the lounge bar.

A free kid’s club is a 3-minute walk from the hotel. Guests also have access to a 27-hole golf course and a 53820 ft² spa with hammam and several beauty treatments at a fee within the resort.

Hotel Du Golf is in the Palmeraie Resort, 5 miles northeast of Marrakech city center and 7.5 miles from Marrakech Airport. It has a business center including a private limousine service.
