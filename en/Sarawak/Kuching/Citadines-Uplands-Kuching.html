Description

Fitted with air conditioning, each unit is decorated with modern furnishings. A flat-screen TV, a seating area and kitchenette are also included. The private bathroom features a hairdryer and shower facilities.

Guests can indulge in a variety of local and international cuisines at the nearby restaurants, a 5-minute walk away.

You can relax by the poolside while the little ones play in the kids' pool. Friendly staff at the 24-hour reception can assist you with airport transfers and laundry request at an additional charge. Other facilities at the property include meeting rooms and babysitting services.

Samajay Commercial Center is 3.7 mi from Citadines Uplands Kuching, while Kuching Waterfront is 3.7 mi away. The nearest airport is Kuching International Airport, 5 mi from the property.
