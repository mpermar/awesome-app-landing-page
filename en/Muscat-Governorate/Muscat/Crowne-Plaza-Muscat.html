Description

All rooms have a minibar, work desk, tea/coffee maker, and a private bathroom with a bathrobe, slippers and hairdryer. The rooms at the Crowne Plaza enjoy views of the sea or the landscaped grounds.

Guests can relax by the pool area and have the tennis and racquetball courts. Various massages, mud wraps, body scrubs and facial treatments are also available.

Arabian, Italian and international dishes are served at the hotel’s 4 restaurants. Guests can enjoy drinks and cocktails at the pool bar or the English pub Duke’s Bar.

Hotel Crowne Plaza Muscat is a 5-minute drive from the shopping area of Qurum and a 20-minute drive from Muscat International Airport. A free shuttle to Grand Mosque, Muttrah Souk and city Centre are available twice daily.
