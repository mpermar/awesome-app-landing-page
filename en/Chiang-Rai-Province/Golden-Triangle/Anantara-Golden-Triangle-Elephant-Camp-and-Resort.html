Description

Spacious rooms at Anantara Golden Triangle Elephant Camp & Resort are furnished with modern Thai decor. Each room features a balcony with views of the Mekong River, a flat-screen cable/satellite TV and a DVD player. An private bathroom comes with a bathtub, a hairdryer and free toiletries. Free mini-bar is included.

For each night stay, guests can enjoy one of the three activities offered by the resort including spa treatment, elephant camp experience or countryside tour. Other recreational activities are yoga classes, cycling and longtail boat rides down the Mekong River.

International breakfast buffet is served at Sala Mae Nam while Italian dishes can be enjoyed at Baan Dahlia. Arrangements for private dining can also be made.

The resort is a 1-hr drive from Chiang Rai International Airport.
