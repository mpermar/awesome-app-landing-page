Description

Combining traditional local designs with modern amenities, each room at Misibis Bay has air conditioning and comes with an LCD cable TV, a DVD player, and a safety deposit box, as well as coffee making facilities. The private bathrooms comes with a hairdryer and free toiletries while others are fitted with a bathtub.

Inspired by local flavors, Spice Market offers a variety of Asian and international favorites. Sula serves a wide selection of wines and spirits as well as snacks while in-room dining is also available.

Misibis Bay offers 2 outdoor swimming pools with views of the sea where guests can relax while sipping their favorite cocktail. Guests can enjoy views of Nag-Aso Lake and the beautiful Mayon Volcano while riding a rented ATV or bicycle. Water activities include parasailing, windsurfing and diving. Other facilities at Misibis Bay include a gym and a movie room.

Tour arrangements and airport shuttle service may be arranged at an additional cost. The property also offers cars for rent, laundry services, and has a gift shop on site.

Mayon Volcano is 37 mi from the property or an hour and 40-minute drive while the nearest airport is Legazpi City Airport which is 23 mi from Misibis Bay.
