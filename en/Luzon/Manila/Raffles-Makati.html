Description

Raffles Makati is conveniently within a 3-minute walk of the Greenbelt Malls and Glorietta Malls. The Ayala Museum and SM Makati is just a 5-minute walk away. Ninoy Aquino International Airport is about a 30-minute drive away. Chargeable airport shuttle service can also be arranged.

Featuring views of the city, spacious air-conditioned suites are furnished with a coffee machine, sitting area with sofa, and a flat-screen cable/satellite TV with DVD player. Luxurious private bathrooms come with a hairdryer, bathrobes, slippers and free bath amenities.

Raffles Makati has a 24-hour front desk that can assist with luggage storage, currency exchange and laundry/ironing services. Guests can enjoy a pampering massage at the spa or approach the tour desk for sightseeing and travel arrangements as well as car rental services.

Spectrum restaurant serves a wide variety of international cuisines, and Café Macaron has tasty pastries. Cocktails are available at the Raffles Long Bar, and room service is provided.

Makati is a great choice for travelers interested in nightlife, friendly locals and shopping.
