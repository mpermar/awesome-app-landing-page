Description

Featuring free WiFi access, spacious air-conditioned suites are equipped with cable TV, an iPod docking station and a kitchenette. Glassware and silverware are provided. The large bathrooms have shower facility, hairdryer and free toiletries.

Facilities and services at Discovery Suites include free WiFi in public areas, 24-hour security system, a car rental service and a business center. Guests also have access to online publications via the PressReader application.

Restaurant 5 serves Asian and Continental dishes. Guests can feast on U.S. Prime steaks at the award winning 22 Prime. Serendipity Lounge features sweeping views of Metro Manila's skyline, lounge entertainment and cocktails. All dining outlets offer free WiFi access and 24-hour room service is available.

Located within walking distance from Asian Development Bank (ADB), Discovery Suites is a 30-minute drive from the Makati business district.

Pasig is a great choice for travelers interested in food, restaurants and budget-friendly traveling.
