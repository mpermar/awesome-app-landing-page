Description

Vivere Hotel and Resorts is a 10-minute walk to Festival Mall. It takes 15 minutes to drive to the center of Alabang. Ninoy Aquino International Airport is a 30-minute drive away.

Each air-conditioned room and suite features a living area, flat-screen cable TV and a kitchenette with a refrigerator and electric kettle. The private bathroom includes a bathtub, shower and hairdryer.

A fitness center allows guests to work out and a spa and health club provides relaxing massage treatments. Other facilities include meeting rooms and a business center.
