Description

The Marco Polo Ortigas Manila is just over half a mile from popular attractions including The Podium and Metrowalk. SM Megamall is just over half a mile away and Ninoy Aquino International Airport is 30 minutes away by car.

Each elegant, air-conditioned room features a flat-screen TV and a Nespresso coffee machine. An iPod dock and carpeted floors are included in all rooms. Spacious bathtubs and premium toiletries are provided in the bathrooms.

Guests enjoy free access to the fitness center. Relaxing spa treatments and soothing massages can be enjoyed at the health club. The property also has a 24-hour front desk and airport shuttle services. The hotel’s signature Continental Club provides guests with exclusive privileges.

A variety of dining options, including a Chinese restaurant and a lobby lounge serve fresh meals daily. Guests can also enjoy a cocktail at the stylish Sky Bar.

Pasig is a great choice for travelers interested in food, restaurants and budget-friendly traveling.
