Description

The rooms at the resort are equipped with a seating area and a flat-screen TV. Free WiFi is accessible to all guests, while some rooms also feature a balcony. Guest rooms at Ponce de Leon Garden Resort feature air conditioning and a desk.

A continental breakfast is served at the property. At the accommodation you'll find a restaurant serving American cuisine.

Ponce de Leon Garden Resort has a playground.

Balinsasayaw Restaurant - Rizal is 1.8 miles from the resort, while City Coliseum is a 15-minute walk away.
