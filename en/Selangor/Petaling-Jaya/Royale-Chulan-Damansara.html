Description

Featuring contemporary decor, all rooms are air-conditioned and equipped with a flat-screen TV. The private bathroom comes with shower facilities and free toiletries. Certain rooms have pool view while others have city view. Newspaper is delivered daily.

Local and international cuisines are served at Royale Coffee House, which opens all day. Coffee/tea and snacks are offered at The Skaters Cafe and Coffee Dreamer. Kopitiam dishes can be enjoyed at The Penguin Cafe, while Minori Restaurant specializes in Japanese delights. Other dining options include fine dining at The Harlequin Room and cocktails at The Camelot.

Guests can have a pampering massage at the spa or workout at the well-equipped gym. Children can join a skating class and play at the kids' club. Extensive meeting facilities and a business center are available. Airport transfers can be arranged at an extra charge.

Various shopping centers are located within a 10-minute walk from Royale Chulan Damansara. Mutiara Damansar MRT Station is 800 feet away, while 1 Utama Shopping Center is 1.7 mi from the property. Kuala Lumpur International Airport is a 35 mi drive.

Mutiara Damansara is a great choice for travelers interested in swimming pools, entertainment and local food.
