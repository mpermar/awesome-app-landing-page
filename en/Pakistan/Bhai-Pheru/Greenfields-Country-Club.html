Description

All air-conditioned units at the resort come with a bidet, free toiletries, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and DVD-Player. Greenfields Country Club has certain units with lake views, and all rooms have a terrace. At the accommodation, the rooms are equipped with a private bathroom with a hair dryer.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at Greenfields Country Club.

The resort has a playground. A variety of popular activities are available in the area around the property, including horse riding and diving.

Languages spoken at the reception include English, Hindi and Urdu.
