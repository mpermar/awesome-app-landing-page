Description

A full-service spa offering massages, body wraps and facials is on site at the CasaBlanca Hotel and Casino. Salon services including cut, color and hair removal are available.

Each room at the Mesquite CasaBlanca Hotel and Casino includes cable TV. Plush bedding and a desk are features in all rooms with private bathrooms. All air-conditioned rooms feature art and unique wood furnishings.

Valley of Fire State Park is 50 miles from this hotel. The excitement of Las Vegas is a 90 minute drive away.
