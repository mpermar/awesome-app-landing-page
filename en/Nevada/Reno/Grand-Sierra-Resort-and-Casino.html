Description

Offering free WiFi, the contemporary rooms at Grand Sierra Resort and Casino offer a flat-screen cable TV with pay-per-view channels and a coffee machine. Select rooms include a work desk, a refrigerator and a microwave.

Guests can play blackjack, craps, roulette as well as video poker and slot machines at the Grand Sierra Casino. Race and sports betting stations are located at Lucky’s, and Pai Gow and Baccarat are also available at the Casino.

The Beach at the Grand Sierra Resort features a modern gym along with go karts, indoor golf and miniature golf. Two movie theaters are also available.

Fine dining is provided at Charlie Palmer Steak restaurant, and Briscola Italian restaurant, located in the Grand Sierra Resort and Casino. Casual dining, bars and lounges are also on site.

The Reno/Tahoe International Airport is 2 miles from this resort and a free airport transfer service is provided to guests. Diamond Peak Ski Resort is a 45 minute drive away.
