Description

A flat-screen TV, a microwave and a mini-refrigerator are standard in each modern room at Marriott's Grand Chateau. All rooms include a seating area and are brightly decorated. Select rooms have panoramic views of the Strip.

Guests can enjoy a variety of drinks and refreshments at the rooftop bar, SkyBar 38 and the bar in the lobby.

Free access to the on-site fitness center and hot tub is provided to all guests of Marriott's Grand Chateau.

The Hard Rock Casino is 20 minutes' walk from Marriott's Grand Chateau.
