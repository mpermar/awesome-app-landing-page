Description

Each spacious room at Sunset Station Hotel Casino offers a flat-screen TV. Suites include an in-room safe and a coffee machine.

Sunset Station Hotel Casino boasts an on-site bowling alley and a 13-screen cinema.

Discover Club Madrid Night Club, located on-site at the Sunset Station, take advantage of live on-site entertainment or dine at the award-winning Sonoma Cellar restaurant. Experience numerous slot machines, card games and bingo or have a quick snack from one of several on-site fast food restaurants.

Sunset Station Hotel Casino is 8 miles away from University of Nevada, Las Vegas. McCarran International Airport is a 20-minute drive away.
