Description

Red Rock Casino Resort Spa features landscaped grounds with an outdoor pool and a beach area with cabanas. Guests can take advantage of the state-of-the-art fitness center, then relax with a massage in the salon.

At the Red Rock, guests can choose from a casual buffet to fine steaks. After enjoying a meal of Japanese or Italian cuisine, guests can visit any of the on-site nightclubs or play a slot machine in the hotel's spacious casino.

Summerlin is a great choice for travelers interested in relaxation, restaurants and food.
