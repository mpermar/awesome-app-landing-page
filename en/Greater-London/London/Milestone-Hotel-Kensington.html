Description

Individually designed, each air-conditioned room offers a romantic elegance, a 24-hour butler service, and modern technology. With antique furnishings, fresh flowers, and fine fabrics, the luxurious rooms include a flat-screen TV with a DVD player, a work desk, sitting area, and a mini-bar. With free toiletries, the private bathroom has a hairdryer. The daily room service includes a complimentary treat, such as sweets, homemade cookies, seasonal fruit, or candles.

Guests at the unique Milestone Hotel Kensington can enjoy a range of traditional food in the on site Cheneston's Restaurant. Dishes are prepared using locally sourced and organic produce whenever possible and the chefs encourage individual requests. The intimate Stables Bar offers a range of over 400 vintage wines. Guests can also sip cocktails in the chic conservatory or take tea by the fire in the lounge.

Dating back to the 19th century, the Milestone Hotel Kensington is in a great location for designer shopping, sightseeing, and enjoying London's superb range of entertainment. The Victoria and Albert Museum is just 1 miles away and the world-famous Harrods Department Store is within an 8-minute walk.

Kensington and Chelsea is a great choice for travelers interested in architecture, monuments and entertainment.
