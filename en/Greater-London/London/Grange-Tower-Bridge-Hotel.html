Description

Each luxurious room at Grange Tower Bridge Hotel offers an iPod docking station, Bluetooth-enabled media hubs, and satellite channels on the 3D-enabled TV. A free daily newspaper is provided, and the sleek, private bathroom includes luxury toiletries.

Tower Bar and Grill restaurant serves a range of grill, individual choice, and authentic tapas dishes. A range of cocktails and wines is available in the sophisticated lounge bar.

Fenchurch Street Station is less than 10 minutes away by foot, and Tower Bridge is a 10 minute walk. London Bridge can be reached in 10 minutes by foot.

A 100 feet swimming pool and a gym feature in the hotel's recreational facilities. There is a business center on site, and there are well-equipped event suites for guests to use.

City of London is a great choice for travelers interested in city trips, city walks and history.
