Description

Each air-conditioned guest room has a flat-screen TV, telephone, desk, wardrobe, minibar and tea and coffee facilities. All have a private bathroom.

Novotel London Brentford provides a range of breakfast options, including a buffet selection, and hot drinks, orange juice and croissants available at the bar. The Elements restaurant specializes in traditional and modern dining throughout the day.

This hotel is 2 miles from Kew Gardens and a 12-minute drive from Richmond Park. South Ealing Tube (subway) Station is 1 miles away and provides direct services to central London in around 30 minutes.
