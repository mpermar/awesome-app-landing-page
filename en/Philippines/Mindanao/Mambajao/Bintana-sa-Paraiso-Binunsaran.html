Description

Bintana sa Paraiso Binunsaran has a restaurant, outdoor swimming pool, a bar and garden in Mambajao.

Boasting family rooms, this property also provides guests with a sun terrace. The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.

At the resort, all rooms include a patio with a garden view. All guest rooms at Bintana sa Paraiso Binunsaran come with a seating area.

The accommodation offers a continental or la carte breakfast. You can play darts at Bintana sa Paraiso Binunsaran, and bike hire is available.
