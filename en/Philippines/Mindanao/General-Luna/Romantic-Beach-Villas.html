Description

Located on the beautiful Siargao Island, Romantic Beach Villas boasts a large, infinity pool and a sun terrace facing the Pacific Ocean.

It offers charming villas fitted with hardwood flooring and free Wi-Fi access. Situated just steps from the best beach area of the island, guests enjoy easy access to world-famous diving, fishing and surfing spots around the island.

The Romantic Villas also provides speed boat trips, wakeboarding, surfing, kayaking amongs other activities.

Massage services and island hopping trips can also be arranged. Villas are air-conditioned and feature private patios with outdoor seating areas.

They are equipped with cable TV and come with either a bathtub or shower facilities.

Guest can enjoy local and international dishes at Mnam-Mnam Beach Restaurant or in the comfort of your room, on your private terrace.

Just metres from the Beach Bar serves a selection of drinks, accompanied with views of the Pacific Ocean.

The villa is situated 800 km southeast of Manila. It is about 250 metres from General Luna, the capital of Philippine surfing.
