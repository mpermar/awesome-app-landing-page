Description

At the hotel, the rooms have a desk. Rooms come with a private bathroom with free toiletries. All guest rooms at Butuan Grand Palace Hotel come with a seating area and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

A buffet breakfast is served each morning at the property.

The accommodation has a terrace.

For guests' convenience, Butuan Grand Palace Hotel has a business center. For any tips on how to get around or what to do in the area, guests can ask at the reception.
