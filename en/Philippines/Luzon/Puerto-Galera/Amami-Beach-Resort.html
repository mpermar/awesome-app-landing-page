Description

Situated along the shores of Talipanan Beach, Amami Beach Resort offers peaceful and comfortable accommodation with free WiFi access in the entire property.

It boasts a private beach area where guests can enjoy activities such as fishing and snorkelling.

The resort is just 700 metres from Aninuan Beach and 1. 6 km from Tamaraw Beach. Sabang Beach is about 14.

2 km away, while Batangas Port is accessible within a 42. 4 km drive. Featuring a balcony with garden/sea views, fan-cooled rooms include a wardrobe, mosquito net, in-room safe, a clothes rack and seating area.

The attached bathroom comes with shower facility and hairdryer. At Amami Beach Resort, guests can enjoy a relaxing massage at the spa or rent a bicycle to explore the area.

Friendly staff can assist guests with luggage storage, laundry services and tour arrangements.

The property houses a restaurant serving a tasty selection of Italian and international cuisines.
