Description

All rooms at the resort are equipped with air conditioning, a 40-inch flat-screen TV, and a wardrobe. Each room come with a terrace and a private bathroom, while some rooms are fitted with a bathtub. Theunits also come with a mini-bar and a seating area.

A continental, full English/Irish or American breakfast is available every morning at the property. Mangroves Restaurant serves locally caught seafood and international cuisine while offering views of the Mangrove bay. Outdoor seating is available while room service my also be arranged.

Guests at Edgewater Dove & Spa Resort will be able to enjoy activities in and around Puerto Galera, like canoeing and diving. The on-site dove shop offers courses for several levels and other inland activities and tours are also available. For guests who wish to relax, the spa offers signature massages for utmost relaxation.

Languages spoken at the 24-hour front desk include Japanese and Dutch, and the staff can assist guests with luggage storage.

Edgewater Dove & Spa Resort is 6.4 mi from White Beach and within 20-minute boat ride from several dove sites. The nearest airport is Manila International Airport, 80 mi from the property via land and boat transfers.
