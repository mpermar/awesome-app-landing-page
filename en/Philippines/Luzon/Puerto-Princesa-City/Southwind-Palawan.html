Description

At the resort, all rooms have a desk, a TV and a private bathroom. Southwind Palawan provides some units with garden views, and rooms are equipped with a terrace.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental breakfast.

Staff at reception can assist around the clock with guidance on the area.

City Coliseum is a 17-minute walk from Southwind Palawan, while Mendoza Park is 1.9 miles away.
