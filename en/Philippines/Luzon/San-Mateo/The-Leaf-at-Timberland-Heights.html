Description

Featuring free WiFi, a swimming pool and free parking, Timberland Sports & Nature Club offers accommodation in the San Mateo Region.

It is a 21 km drive from the heart of Manila city. The Leaf is located in a separate building, a minute away via golf cart.

The accommodation is air conditioned and has a cable flat-screen TV. Some units include a dining area and/or balcony.

There is a private bathroom with bath robes and slippers in each unit. Timberland Sports and Nature Club also includes a spa centre.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant. You can play tennis and billiards at the property, and the area is popular for cycling and hiking.

Makati is 18 km from Timberland Sports and Nature Club. The nearest airport is Manila International Airport, 25 km from Timberland Sports and Nature Club.
