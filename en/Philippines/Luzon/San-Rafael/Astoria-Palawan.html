Description

Astoria Palawan is a beautiful resort located in Puerto Princesa. It boasts a private beach area, a 35 metre infinity pool and a children’s playground.

Free WiFi access is available in this resort. Featuring elegant décor, stylish rooms are equipped with a DVD player, a seating area and a dining area with a microwave.

Bathrooms are private and include free toiletries and shower facilities. Offering panoramic views of the Sulu Sea, the restaurant features a live kitchen where guests can watch their meals being freshly prepared.

Options to dine indoors and outdoors are possible as well. At Astoria Palawan, guests can utilize the meeting facilities and games room.

An array of water-sport activities can be enjoyed on-site. For convenience, a free scheduled airport shuttle service is available for guests of Astoria Palawan, while private airport transfer services can be arranged at a surcharge.

The property offers free parking. Located just a few steps away from the shores of Honda Bay, the accommodation is 62 km away from Puerto Princesa International Airport.
