Description

The property is about 2 miles from SM City Mall of Asia and 2.6 miles from Ninoy Aquino International Airport. Makati City is approximately 4 miles away.

Offering pool and city views, stylish air-conditioned rooms and suites come with carpet floors, in-room safe, a flat-screen cable TV and ironing facilities. Electric kettle, minibar and iPod dock are also included. The private bathroom has a shower, bathrobes and free bath amenities.

At City of Dreams - Nobu Hotel Manila, guests can indulge in a pampering massage at the spa or enjoy a drink at the bar. The friendly staff can assist guests with laundry services, luggage storage and transport arrangements. Business facilities are also available.

The in-house Nobu Restaurant serves a fine selection of Japanese cuisine, and special diet meals can also be prepared upon request.
