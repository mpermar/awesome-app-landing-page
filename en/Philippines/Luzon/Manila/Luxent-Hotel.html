Description

Placed in the heart of the city, Luxent Hotel boasts an outdoor swimming pool overlooking splendid views of Quezon City.

The property is a 1-minute walk to Scout Monument Circle, while luxuriously furnished rooms offer free WiFi.

Luxent Hotel offers 2 dining options - Garden Café, which serves buffet breakfast, lunch and dinner, while Lush Bar & Lounge offers á la carte dishes with alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages.

Both restaurants serve local and international cuisines. Bread Indulgence Corner serves Illy-brand coffee, freshly-baked breads and hand-crafted cakes.

In-room dining is also available for guests' comfort. Guests can relax at the spa or enjoy a traditional massage.

The hotel also houses a fitness centre and meeting/banquet facilities. A 24-hour front desk can assist guests with laundry/ironing services and room service.

Air-conditioned rooms offer a city view and are equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a sofa and a minibar.

Private glass-wall bathrooms include hot/cold shower facilities and free toiletries.

The property is located a 10-minute walk to Robinsons Mall and a 5-minute walk to the historical St Paul Church.
