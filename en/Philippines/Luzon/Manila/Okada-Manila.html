Description

Set in the entertainment city of Manila, 2. 5 km from SMX Convention Centre and 3.

2 km from SM Mall of Asia, Okada Manila is a 5-star city resort that boasts of world-class amenities such as: an outdoor swimming pool overlooking the famous Manila Bay, a casino at the property, and a spa and wellness centre.

There are also several dining options with varying ambiance and international cuisines, as well as an indoor beach club.

Free WiFi is also available throughout the property. The rooms and suites at Okada Manila are all air-conditioned and come with a seating area with a flat-screen cable TV.

Each room is also equipped with a wardrobe, a safety deposit box, and a desk. Some rooms are fitted with a sofa seating along with a dining area.

The rooms feature views of the bay or the swimming pool. Included in the en suite bathroom is a bathtub with free bath amenities, while some rooms have their own private massage room.

Guests can enjoy daily buffet breakfast at the property while 10 dining options are available.

Japanese Kappou Imamura, Ginza Nagaoka, and Enbu serves signature Japanese dishes while Goryeo offers a Korean dining experience.

Red Spice specializes in Chinese Cuisine and La Piazza is an Italian Restaurant that offers a selection of wine while affording views of the fountain.

Kiapo Restaurant serves a tasteful selection of Filipino cuisine. The Medley Buffet, Lobby Lounge, and Okada Lounge are also some of the dining options at the resort.

Room service may also be arranged. The outdoor swimming pools takes guests to a luxurious urban relaxation, just as the The Spa Retreat offers guests signature treatments that help melt the stress away.

The fitness centre is open 24-hours of the day, while the salon and barbershop as well as the kid's club, Play, is open for hotel guests and visitors.

Aside from the casino on-site, guests can also experience day-to-night clubbing at the Cove Manila.

Guests can also find temporary art galleries at the resort. The staff at the 24-hour front desk and concierge can assist guests with any queries at any time of the day.

There is an ATM at the property while currency exchange, laundry, and meeting/banquet facilities as well as shops are also available on-site.

SM By the Bay Amusement Park is 2. 8 km and the World Trade Centre is 5 km. Greenbelt and Glorietta Malls are 12.

8 km away from the resort. The nearest airport is Manila Ninoy Aquino International Airport, 3.
