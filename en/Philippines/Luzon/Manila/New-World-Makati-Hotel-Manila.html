Description

The hotel is just across the street from Greenbelt Malls and a 10-minute walk from the Glorietta shopping complex. Ninoy Aquino International Airport is a 30-minute drive from the hotel.

The elegant rooms include modern décor and large windows that provide plenty of natural light. Each room is fitted with a minibar and TV with cable channels.

Guest can enjoy a massage at Marahai Spa or relax in the sauna. New World Makati provides car rental and currency exchange services. Dry cleaning services are available upon request.

Jasmine Restaurant serves Cantonese dishes and international food is available at Café 1228. Light snacks afternoon tea and nightly musical entertainment are provided at The Lounge. Cocktails, premium whiskeys and Cuban cigars can be enjoyed at Bar Rouge.

Makati is a great choice for travelers interested in nightlife, friendly locals and shopping.
