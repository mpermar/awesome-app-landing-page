Description

With free Wi-Fi, rooms come equipped with air conditioning, a personal refrigerator with minibar and a desk. The private bathroom has free toiletries and shower. A hairdryer, slippers and bathrobes are also provided.

Guests can enjoy a swim at the outdoor swimming pool or relax with a massage or treatment at the spa. It also has a sauna and tour desk where day trips can be arranged.

An International buffet is served at Holiday Inn Suites' all-day dining restaurant, Flavours. Citron Bar serves Asian and Western dishes, and the rooftop bar and restaurant, Oz, serves a delicious Asian menu.

The Holiday Inn & Suites Makati is within 3 miles of the Philippine International Convention Centre and World Trade Centre Manila. Ninoy Aquino Airport is 5 miles away. Free on-site parking is available.

Makati is a great choice for travelers interested in nightlife, friendly locals and shopping.
