Description

Hotel Kimberly Tagaytay is a 4-star property located in Tagaytay, 3. 5 km from Sky Ranch and 13.

9 km from People's Park in the Sky, boasting of an outdoor swimming poll with garden, an on-site mini farm, and an on-site restaurant.

All rooms at Hotel Kimberly Tagaytay are fitted with air conditioning and are equipped with a flat-screen cable TV, a seating area, and a desk.

Other rooms come with a safety deposit box and a wardrobe. The en suite bathrooms have hot and cold shower facility or bath facility along with free toiletries.

Guests may enjoy daily breakfast at the property. Cafe K serves a la carte local and International favorites.

Drinks are served at the bar while in-room dining may be arranged. At the property, guest may lounge by the outdoor swimming pool and kiddie pool.

The hotel also has a garden and a mini-farm where guests can feed and pet domesticated animals.

Other activities such as billiards in the game room and children's playground is also available at the property.

The staff at the 24-hour front desk can assist guests with laundry and massage services.

Airport shuttle is also offered at an additional charge, as well as the business centre and meeting rooms.

Parking is available at the property for free. Museo Orlina is 7. 7 km from the property and Tagaytay Picnic Grove is 10.

1 km away. The nearest airport is Manila International Airport, 57. 9 km away from Hotel Kimberly Tagaytay.
