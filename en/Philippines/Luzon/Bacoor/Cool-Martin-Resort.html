Description

At the resort, rooms include a desk. Rooms come complete with a private bathroom equipped with a shower and free toiletries, while some rooms at Cool Martin Resort also provide guests with a seating area. At the accommodation every room is equipped with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV.

A la carte breakfast is served at the property.

You can play darts at Cool Martin Resort.

Manila is 11 miles from the resort, while Tagaytay is 23 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Ninoy Aquino International Airport, 5 miles from the property.
