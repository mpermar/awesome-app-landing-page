Description

Nestled within the landscaped Mimosa Leisure Estate in Clark, Quest Plus Conference Center, Clark offers access to a 36-hole championship golf course, casino, duty free shopping hub and nature walks.

There is a pool and free WiFi throughout while free private parking is available.

The rooms at Quest Plus Conference Center, Clark are fitted with flat-screen cable TVs and safety deposit boxes.

Minibars and tea/coffee makers are available. Select rooms offer bathrooms with bathtubs.

Guests can maintain their fitness at the hotel’s gym or with a game of table tennis.

Billiard facilities and a games room offering darts are available. A grand ballroom and meeting room facilities are provided.

Offering all-day Eastern and Western dining, Mequeni Restaurant also features an open-concept kitchen.

Rodizio Roof Top Grill has an offering of Mongolian and Brazilian barbeque, coupled with mouth watering Angus beef steaks.

Quest Plus Conference Center is located in Clark Freeport Zone, a 5-minute drive from Clark International Airport.
