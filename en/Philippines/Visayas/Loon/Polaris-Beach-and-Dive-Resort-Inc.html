Description

Offering an outdoor pool, Polaris Beach and Dive Resort Inc is situated in Loon in the Bohol Region, 49 km from Cebu City.

The resort features free WiFi. Certain units feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day.

A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms. The rooms are fitted with a private bathroom.

There is a shared lounge, babysitting service, room service and gift shop at the property.

Polaris Beach and Dive Resort Inc has a children's playground and views of the garden You can play billiards and darts at this resort, and the area is popular for snorkelling and diving.

The resort also offers car hire. Moalboal is 41 km from Polaris Beach and Dive Resort Inc, while Tagbilaran City is 29 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Tagbilaran Airport, 27 km from the property. Airport transfers are available at a surcharge.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant which serves a selection of Asian, American, continental and vegetarian dishes.
