Description

A continental or à la carte breakfast is available daily at the property. There's an in-house restaurant, which specializes in seafood, local and Grill/bbq cuisine.

A variety of popular activities are available in the area around the property, including cycling and hiking.

Magellan's Cross is an 18-minute walk from the resort. The nearest airport is Mactan–Cebu International Airport, 6 miles from the property.
