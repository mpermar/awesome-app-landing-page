Description

Located directly on Anda Beach, Anda White Beach Resort is an hour and 45 minutes’ drive from Tagbilaran City.

It features an outdoor pool and free Wi-Fi in public areas. Air-conditioned rooms have private balconies and DVD players.

Fitted with sliding glass doors and wooden furnishings, they come with a minibar, safe and an attached bathroom.

Guests may snorkel and fish in clear waters bordering the resort. Other leisure options include relaxing poolside massages and games of billiards and darts.

Evening drinks can be enjoyed at Fizz Buzz bar after hearty local and international meals in the restaurant.

Meals can also be served in rooms. Resort Anda White Beach is a 15-minute boat ride from the scenic Basdio Marine Sanctuary and a 30-minute boat ride from Lamanok Island.

It is a 2-hour drive from Tagbilaran Airport, and pick-up service can be arranged.
