Description

The rooms has air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a electric tea pot, a bath, free toiletries and a desk. The rooms include a private bathroom, while certain rooms are equipped with a terrace. At the resort rooms come with a seating area.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property.

Guests at Salaya Beach Houses will be able to enjoy activities in and around Dauin, like hiking.

Dumaguete is 8 miles from the accommodation, while Siquijor is 16 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Sibulan Airport, 9 miles from the property.
