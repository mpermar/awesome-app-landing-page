Description

The rooms at the resort are equipped with a seating area. Each room has air conditioning and a flat-screen TV, and certain units at Calape Forest Resort have a garden view. All guest rooms include a private bathroom with a bidet.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental or a à la carte breakfast.

You can play pool at Calape Forest Resort.

Tagbilaran City is 18 miles from the resort, while Bohol is 18 miles away.
