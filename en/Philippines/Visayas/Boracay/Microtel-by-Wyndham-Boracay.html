Description

Microtel Boracay is a beachfront hotel situated on Diniwid Beach. It features an outdoor swimming pool, restaurant and air-conditioned rooms with a TV.

Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas. Guestrooms at Boracay Microtel come with modern décor.

Each air-conditioned room is fitted with a work desk and TV with cable channels. Hot shower facilities are also provided.

Recreation choices include windsurfing or snorkeling. The hotel provides bicycle rental services while sightseeing arrangements can be made at the tour desk.

The on-site restaurant serves a fine selection of local and international dishes.

Free coffee is offered at the lobby. Microtel Boracay is a 30-minute drive from Cagban Jetty Port.
