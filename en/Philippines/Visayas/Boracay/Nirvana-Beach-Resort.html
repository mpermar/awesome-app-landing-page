Description

Fully furnished, the air-conditioned apartments have large living areas, a well-furnished kitchen and private balcony that overlooks the pool. A cable TV and personal safe are provided.

Guests can snorkel, indulge in a relaxing massage, or go on a horseback riding trip. The staff can also assist with car rental, currency exchange and ticketing services.

Restaurant by the Pool serves a variety of international dishes, local favorites and Scandinavian specialties. In-room dining options are also possible with room service.

Beach Resort Nirvana is a 5-minute walk from a variety of dive shops. It is a 45-minute boat ride and drive from Caticlan Airport.
