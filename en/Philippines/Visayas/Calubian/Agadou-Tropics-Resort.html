Description

All rooms in the resort are equipped with a electric tea pot. Featuring a private bathroom, rooms at Agadou Tropics Resort also provide guests with free WiFi. All guest rooms come with a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

An Asian breakfast is served at the property.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool. Guests at Agadou Tropics Resort will be able to enjoy activities in and around Calubian, like cycling.

Malapascua Island is 22 miles from the resort. The nearest airport is Calbayog Airport, 42 miles from the property.
