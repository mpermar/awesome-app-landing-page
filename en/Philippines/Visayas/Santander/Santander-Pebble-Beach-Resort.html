Description

Santander Pebble Beach Resort has a restaurant, outdoor swimming pool, a shared lounge and garden in Santander.

Boasting room service, this property also provides guests with a children's playground.

The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, a shuttle service, a shared kitchen and free WiFi.

The rooms at the resort are fitted with a seating area. At Santander Pebble Beach Resort rooms have a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, and a patio with a garden view.

The accommodation offers a continental or à la carte breakfast. Santander Pebble Beach Resort offers a terrace.

The area is popular for snorkelling and fishing, and car hire is available at the resort.

Dumaguete is 14 km from Santander Pebble Beach Resort, while Alcoy is 46 km from the property.
