Description

Kasai Village Dive Resort features a restaurant, outdoor swimming pool, a bar and shared lounge in Moalboal.

The accommodation provides a private beach area, as well as a garden and a terrace.

The air-conditioned rooms provide pool view and come with a desk and free WiFi. At the resort, all rooms come with a wardrobe.

Featuring a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries, rooms at Kasai Village Dive Resort also provide guests with a sea view.

At the accommodation every room is fitted with a seating area. Guests at Kasai Village Dive Resort can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

Guests at the resort will be able to enjoy activities in and around Moalboal, like cycling.
