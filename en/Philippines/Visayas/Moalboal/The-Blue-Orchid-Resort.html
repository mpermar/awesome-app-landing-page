Description

The Blue Orchid Resort offers peaceful and relaxing stays, about 8 km from Moalboal.

It features an outdoor pool, several water sports activities and free WiFi access.

The Blue Orchid Resort is a 20-minute boat ride from the popular dive site of Pescador Island.

It offers free parking and is a approximately a 3-hour drive from Mactan International Airport.

Opening out to sea views, rooms are breezy and feature private terraces. Equipped with either a fan or air conditioning, all have a private bathroom and mosquito net.

The on-site dive centre offers diving and snorkeling trips. Alternatively, guests can also explore the surrounding region on a scooter, or relax with a massage.

Adventure activities such as canoeing, and kayaking can also be arranged. Local and international dishes are served at the in-house restaurant.
