Description

Offering an outdoor pool and sun terrace, Pescadores Seaview Suites is set in Moalboal in the Visayas Region, 30 km from Alcoy.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. The nearest airport is Mactan Cebu International Airport, 104 km from Pescadores Seaview Suites.

Airport transfers are provided at an additional charge. The air-conditioned rooms come with a dry rack and a fan.

Views of the sea, pool or garden are featured in certain rooms. The en suite bathroom is equipped with shower facilities and free toiletries.

The 24-hour front desk staff can assist guests with luggage storage. There is a mini-market on-site.

Guests can play table tennis, billiard, and board games at the resort. There is also a KTV room for karaoke sessions and a wellness facility for a massage.
