Description

Featuring garden or ocean views and air conditioning, each room comes with a flat-screen TV with cable channels, DVD player and an iPod dock. The private bathroom comes with a bathtub or shower, free toiletries, bathrobe and slippers.

Guests can taste authentic Chinese cuisine at the Cheon San restaurant. In alternative Café Amiga serves typical Filipino cuisine, and Familia Buffet Restaurant offers Japanese, Western and Oriental breakfast, lunch and dinner buffets.

An array of activities can be enjoyed at JPark Island Resort & Waterpark Cebu, including a spa and health club, evening entertainment and a kids’ club.

Cebu Mactan Airport is a 20-minute drive from JPark Island Resort & Waterpark Cebu, and Ayala Mall Cebu is a 45-minute drive from the property.
