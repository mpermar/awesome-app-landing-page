Description

A plush hideaway in Mactan Island, Abaca Boutique Resort features an infinity pool with views of the sea, an on-site restaurant that offers Mediterranean cuisine, and an indulgent spa that provides in-room spa services.

The expansive rooms at Abaca Boutique Resort offer views of either the pool or the sea with outdoor furniture, where guests can relax.

All rooms have a private bathroom with walk in shower and bathtub while some rooms are fitted with a plunge pool.

Certain rooms include a computer and a flat screen TV with cable channels. Guests can take their breakfast at the on-site restaurant, Abaca.

Abaca Restaurant serves Mediterranean food and allows guests to dine outdoors. Special diet menu is also available upon request and guests may choose to dine in the comfort of their villas and suites.

Apart from the spa and the fitness centre, Abaca Boutique Resort also offers diving and snorkelling activities at an additional cost.

Guests may choose to rest at the garden or at the sun terrace while an indoor play area with children's books is also available, along with babysitting services.

Lapu-Lapu is 8 km away while Ayala Center Cebu is 13. 3 km from the property. The nearest airport is Mactan Cebu International Airport, 5 km.
