Description

Located on Olango Island, Talima Beach Villas & Dive Resort is a beachfront property facing Talima Fish Sanctuary.

Set in a tropical garden, it offers air-conditioned villas, an outdoor pool and restaurant.

A private beach area is available. Talima Fish Sanctuary is a protected area spread over 14 hectares and is just 100 metres from the resort.

A 20-minute boat ride from Mactan Island, the resort is 10 km from Mactan Cebu International Airport.

All villas have a separate living area with sofa, while the suites feature a private balcony.

Private bathroom includes a heated shower. Guests can relax at the resort’s sun terrace and enjoy free WiFi in the common areas.

Diving activities can also be arranged. Filipino cuisine is served at Talima Restaurant.
