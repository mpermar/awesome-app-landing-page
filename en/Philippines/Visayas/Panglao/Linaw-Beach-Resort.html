Description

Situated on the private beach of Danao, this resort boasts an outdoor pool, a fine dining restaurant and an open-air games hall.

It offers rooms with a terrace or balcony and free Wi-Fi access. Fitted with tiled flooring, spacious modern rooms offer a flat-screen TV and DVD player.

They are equipped with tea/coffee making facilities and a minibar. Private bathrooms come with a hairdryer.

Select rooms offer a bathtub and sea views. Located on Panglao Island, Linaw Beach Resort is a 10-minute drive from San Agustin Church and Panglao City Centre.

Tagbilaran Airport is a 30-minute drive away. Guests can play billiards, table tennis or foosball in the games hall.

Alternatively, they can have a relaxing massage, or make travel arrangements at the tour desk.

Gifts are available at Linaw's souvenir shop. Bicycles and motorbikes are available for rent, and an airport shuttle service is provided.

On-site parking is free. Pearl Restaurant serves a selection of international dishes, while the bar offers refreshing drinks by the poolside.
