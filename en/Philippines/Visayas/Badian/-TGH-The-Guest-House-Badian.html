Description

Located 9 km from Santo Nino Church in Badian, "TGH" The Guest House Badian welcomes guests with a restaurant and bar.

Certain rooms feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day. A terrace or patio are featured in certain rooms.

A shared common area with a flat-screen TV is available for guests to unwind. You will find a 24-hour reception and a tour desk at the property.
