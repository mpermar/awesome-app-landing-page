A delightful stay, welcoming & relaxing

"The surroundings & grounds of the property along the lake shore are beautiful & very relaxing. The natural landscape makes no excuses but boasts tranquility & peace. Unfortunately the weather wasn’t on our side but I can’t wait to go back in the summer & next time spend longer! I liked the fact that it was “an island set apart”. Very different to any other location I’ve ever been to; quiet & relaxing. Feels like you are completely removed from the hustle & bustle of the busy world. Beautiful spa facilities & lovely pool. Surprisingly very welcoming of families & young kids. Accommodation very nice, clean & comfortable. Lovely atmosphere in the bar area too!Food isn’t fine dining but tasty, good choice & very satisfying. The Chicken curry is highly recommended. The menu boasts that the chef is Indian and makes a good curry. It didn’t disappoint! Yum! A little ferry takes you & your car for free (only take car if you are a resident on that island for your stay) across the lake. Literally 2mins journey. Little ferry takes two cars at a time & is an on demand shuttle service. This creates a sense of adventure from the second you arrive."
