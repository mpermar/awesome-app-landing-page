Description

The rooms of the Monopol Wrocław feature a stylish interior design with hardwood floors. Each has a flat-screen satellite TV, work desk and minibar. The bathroom includes a shower, bathtub, bathrobe and hairdryer.

A fitness center, sauna and steam bath are among the many spa facilities that the hotel offers. A selection of massages is also offered.

The hotel has 2 restaurants. One focuses on Polish dishes, and the other serves mostly Mediterranean food. In the mornings a breakfast buffet is available.

The Monopol’s is only a 3 minute walk from the Old Town. It features such landmarks as St. Elizabeth’s Church, which has an observation deck that offers scenic views of the area.

Wrocław Główny Train Station is only one mile away. Ostrów Tumski, on which the Wrocław Cathedral is located, is about one mile from the hotel.

Stare Miasto is a great choice for travelers interested in history, nightlife and restaurants.
