Description

The nonsmoking rooms are air-conditioned and feature satellite TV, an electric kettle and a personal safe. The private bathroom comes with hot shower facilities. Hairdryers and ironing facilities are available on request.

The property is located a 15-minute walk from local dining options. It is a 5-minute drive from Cenang Beach and a 10-minute drive from Cenang Mall and the Underwater World. Kuah Town is approximately a 20-minute drive away.
