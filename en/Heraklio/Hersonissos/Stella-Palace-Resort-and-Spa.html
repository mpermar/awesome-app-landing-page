Description

All air-conditioned units feature stylish furnishings in earthy tones and overlook the garden, the pools or the Cretan Sea. They come with a satellite TV, mini fridge, slippers and free toiletries. Some units also have their own pool with wood deck.

The main restaurant serves international and Mediterranean flavors in buffet style and hosts regular themed evenings. Elia Restaurant prepares dishes based on the Cretan cuisine. Guests can listen to live piano music in the indoor bar, while Kohili and Thalassa pool bars offer exotic cocktails.

Guests at Stella Palace have a wide choice of sports activities, such as basketball, badminton and water aerobics. Massages can also be arranged upon request.

The lively town of Hersonissos is 2.5 miles away. The hotel is 12 miles from Nikos Kazantzakis International Airport and 16 miles from Heraklion city. Free private parking is available on site.

Analipsi is a great choice for travelers interested in relaxation, local food and ambiance.
