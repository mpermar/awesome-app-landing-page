Description

All guest rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area. All rooms come with a desk and a flat-screen TV, and certain units at The Thinnai have a terrace. The rooms feature a closet.

A buffet, Full English/Irish or Asian breakfast is served at the property.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool. You can play darts at The Thinnai, and bike rental is available.

Jaffna Public Library is 1.3 miles from the hotel, while Jaffna Fort is 1.6 miles from the property.
