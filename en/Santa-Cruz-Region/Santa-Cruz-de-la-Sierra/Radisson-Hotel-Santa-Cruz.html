Description

At the hotel, the rooms have a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

A buffet breakfast is served at the property. Guests can dine in the in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of local and Peruvian dishes and also offers vegetarian options.

At Radisson Hotel Santa Cruz guests are welcome to use a sauna. You can play ping pong at this 5-star hotel.

Staff at the reception can help guests with any questions that they might have.

Arenal Park is 3 miles from the accommodation, while 24th of September Metropolitan Plaza is 3.2 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Viru Viru International Airport, 9 miles from the property.
