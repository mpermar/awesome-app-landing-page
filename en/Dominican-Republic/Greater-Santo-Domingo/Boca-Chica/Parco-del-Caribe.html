Description

Surrounded by a tranquil garden, Parco del Caribe is just a 1-minute walk from Boca Chica Beach.

It offers a 24-hour front desk. Free WiFi is offered throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

It offers double rooms and apartments with daily cleaning, safe, flat-screen TV with satellite channels, and free air conditioning.

Each Parco del Caribe apartment has bright Caribbean-style décor. The apartments offer a living-dining area and a well-equipped kitchen with a coffee machine and fridge.

A car rental service is available. There is also a patio with a dominoes and a dancing area.

Breakfast can be arranged for a surcharge in a nearby cafe. The Parco del Caribe is situated in central Boca Chica, surrounded by shops, restaurants, bars and lively nightlife.

Las Americas International Airport is 10 km away, while Santo Domingo is 25 km away.
