Description

Across the street from the Caribbean Sea and minutes from the commercial center of Santo Domingo, this hotel offers on-site spa services and a charming restaurant.

Napolitano Hotel & Casino offers an outdoor pool surrounded by lounge chairs and umbrellas.

The spa offers massage services. The hotel's Terrace Restaurant serves international cuisine.

There is also a bar. The Napolitano Hotel is located 35 minutes by car from the international airport of Las Americas.
