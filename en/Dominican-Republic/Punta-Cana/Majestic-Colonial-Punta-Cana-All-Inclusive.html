Description

All suites have a bathroom with a spa bath. The elegant and spacious suites offer tile floors and four-poster beds. Each features a mini-bar and large seating area with a desk. Bathrooms boast a walk-in shower and bathrobes.

Majestic Colonial features 11 bars including a poolside and beach bar, and many feature live music. Seven restaurants provide casual and fine dining. Local Caribbean as well as international food is offered. Majestic Colonial-Punta Cana offers a 1,200 feet outdoor pool with 2 hot tubs.

Guests can workout in the gym or play on the basketball courts. The kid’s club features a large playground with a castle. Guests can relax at Spa Resort Colonial which offers 10 treatment rooms surrounded by gardens, 2 saunas and a large hot tub. Various body and beauty treatments are available.

Bavaro is a great choice for travelers interested in excursions, nightlife and water sports.
