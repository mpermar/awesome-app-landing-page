Description

Meliá Caribe Tropical Resort features a variety of activities with 10 outdoor pools and 8 lighted tennis courts. After spending the day kayaking or snorkeling, guests can relax with a massage at one of the 2 luxurious spas or play in the casino. Kids will enjoy activities at the kids' club, while adults can challenge themselves on the climbing wall.

With several restaurants, the Tropical Meliá Caribe offers delicious authentic Caribbean cuisine, in addition to international dishes. After dinner, guests can have a drink in one of the resort's 10 bars, then enjoy nightly entertainment.

Bavaro is a great choice for travelers interested in excursions, nightlife and water sports.
