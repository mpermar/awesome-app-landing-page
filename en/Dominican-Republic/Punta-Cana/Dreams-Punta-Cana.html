Description

The air-conditioned rooms at Dreams Punta Cana include modern décor, a sitting area, CD player, iPod dock and a flat-screen satellite TV with DVD player. These accommodations also offer a coffee maker and a minibar. The bathrooms are private and provide bathrobe and slippers.

Dreams Punta Cana has 6 international restaurants and 10 lounges. The guests can enjoy Mexican, Asian and Italian cuisine and steaks specialties. The seafood is served in an outdoor thatch-roof palapa.

This complex can arrange activities like horseback riding, windsurfing, diving and snorkeling. There is also a casino, golf course and massage services.

The resort is 19 miles from central Bavaro and 1.5 hours from the dock to go to Catalina Island. Punta Cana International Airport is a 40 minute drive away.

Uvero Alto is a great choice for travelers interested in swimming pools, nature and excursions.
