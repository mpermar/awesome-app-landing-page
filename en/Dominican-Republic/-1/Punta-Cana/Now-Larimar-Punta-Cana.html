Description

A furnished private patio or balcony is available in each room at Now Larimar Punta Cana. A minibar and a flat-screen cable TV are provided. Each room is decorated in bright colors with floral designs.

The water sports facility offers sailing, windsurfing and kayaking. Guests can play on the various courts including basketball and beach volleyball. Daily classes at Punta Cana Now Larimar include dancing and Spanish.

Carnival International Café offers casual dining with outdoor seating. Gourmet á la carte items are served at Capers Italian restaurant. Hamburgers and hotdogs are offered beachside at Barefoot Grill.

Bavaro is a great choice for travelers interested in excursions, nightlife and water sports.
