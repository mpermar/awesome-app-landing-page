Description

The air-conditioned rooms at Memories Splash have cable TV, a minibar, a balcony or terrace. The bathroom includes free toiletries.

The resort has several restaurants that serve Mexican and Italian cuisine, as well as buffet service, an ice cream parlor, a café and 11 different bars.

Memories Splash also features a fitness center, pin-pong and pool tables, sailboats, aqua-aerobics lessons, dance lessons, Spanish lessons, volleyball and water polo, among other activities. The kid's and teen's clubs are open from 9:30 am until 6 pm.

Palma Real Shopping Village is a 20-minute drive from the resort, and Punta Cana International airport is a 30-minute drive away.

Bavaro is a great choice for travelers interested in excursions, nightlife and water sports.
