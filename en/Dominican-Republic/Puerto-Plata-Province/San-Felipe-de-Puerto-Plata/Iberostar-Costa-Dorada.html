Description

Iberostar Costa Dorada is located in Vuelta Larga, 14 km from Sosúa. The hotel has a spa centre and a private beach area, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Each room includes a flat-screen TV, wardrobe, safety and a coffee machine. All rooms have a private bathroom with shower and free toiletries.

You will find a 24-hour front desk and hairdresser's at the property. The on-site restaurant offers buffet and à la carte menu.

This hotel has water sports facilities, tennis court and car hire is available. Guests can enjoy nightclub, evening entertainment and also for the kids there is a playground and kid's club.

Other activities are offered in the area, such as snorkeling and diving. Cabarete is 27 km from Iberostar Costa Dorada, while San Felipe de Puerto Plata is 3.
