Description

Fitted with fine wood furnishings and premium cotton beddings, the air-conditioned villas feature an over-sized bathtub, outdoor dining pavilion and an entertainment system with a flat-screen TV, iPod dock and DVD player. Wine chiller, espresso machine and fully-stocked minibar are also included.

Anantara Kihavah Villas is a scenic 35-minute seaplane ride from Male International Airport. Airport transportation are provided by the hotel for an extra fee.

A range of water sports, including wake-boarding, windsurfing, canoeing, catamaran sailing and snorkeling, is available. This family-friendly resort also provides a variety of complimentary activities for children. For those looking to exercise, there is a tennis court and fitness center on site.

The resort has 5 unique dining options, which includes underwater restaurant and wine cellar at SEA, a Teppanyaki lounge at FIRE and a two-story over-water Sky Bar. Guests can also dine at the private beach or enjoy a romantic dinner on a yacht.
