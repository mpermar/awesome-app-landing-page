Description

Built in 2011, all modern designer rooms at Hotel Lone have free Wi-Fi and a private balcony. They include all modern facilities, including flat-screen satellite TV and laptop safe. Every room has air conditioning.

There is a spa area with a hot tub, steam bath, sauna and massage room. The outdoor pool is shared with another hotel. It is surrounded by a spacious sunbathing area, and guests can also relax in a large indoor pool, which looks out to the lush garden.

There is a sushi bar, a nightclub and several restaurants within the hotel building. Breakfast buffet is served daily at the hotel's restaurant. Pula Airport is just a 40-minute drive away and airport transportation can be arranged upon request.
