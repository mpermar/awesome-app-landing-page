Description

All the rooms in the Hotel Laguna Molindrio are air-conditioned and feature a flat-screen satellite TV, a mini-bar and a safe. They either offer views of the Adriatic Sea or of the park.

A wide range of Istrian specialties and international cuisine is served in the restaurant. During the season guests can also enjoy live music and professional shows. There is also a massage studio, a hair salon and a fitness area available. Free pool towels are provided, as well as free sun loungers on the beach.

Sports activities at the Laguna Molindrio Hotel include tennis, table tennis, beach volley ball, water skiing and many more. Kids can have fun in the children's pool, on the playground or they can take part in animation programs. The Wellness Center features a Finnish and Turkish sauna, whirpools, experience showers and many more and is free of charge for all guests.

The center of Poreč is 2.5 mi away and can be reached within 10 minutes by the boat shuttle service or by bus. You can also take a romantic 45-minute walk to Poreč along the beach. Local bus stops near the hotel. Pula Airport is at a distance of 37 mi.
