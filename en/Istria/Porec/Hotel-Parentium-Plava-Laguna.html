Description

All rooms feature floor-to-ceiling windows and a balcony with a sitting area overlooking the park. They also feature an LCD satellite TV, a minibar and a safe.

The restaurant serves Istrian and seafood dishes, and the other 2 serve a buffet breakfast and dinner. During peak season, guests can enjoy live music, professional programs and other diverse entertainment options.

Hotel Laguna Parentium provides spa facilities, such as Finnish and Turkish saunas, a free fitness area and beauty and hairdresser studios. Tennis, mini golf, volleyball, basketball and other land and sea sports can be enjoyed on site.

The Old Town of Poreč is 3 miles away. A local bus stops right in front of the hotel. Pula Airport is 37 miles away. Secure parking is provided for an additional fee.
