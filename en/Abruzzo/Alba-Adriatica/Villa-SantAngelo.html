Description

At the hotel, every room is equipped with a desk. Each room is equipped with air conditioning, and selected rooms have a balcony. At Villa Sant'Angelo, every room has a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

The daily breakfast offers continental and buffet options.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental is available at the accommodation.

Non-stop assistance is available at the reception, where staff speak German, English, Spanish and Italian.

Pescara is 29 miles from Villa Sant'Angelo, while San Benedetto del Tronto is 9 miles from the property. Abruzzo Airport is 30 miles away.

08/12/2016

A relaxing getaway in the Abruzzo countryside

"This hotel stands a level above anything else we've experienced in Abruzzo. The location and pool are spectacular, the staff always eager to please, and the restaurant (open for all meals) top notch.The included breakfast has a wide range of options that should please everyone. Although technically served from 7:30-9:30, the staff kept track of guests and didn't mind at all if someone came down a bit later. The rooms are on the small side but well laid out and lovingly decorated, so even after a week we didn't feel cramped. The outdoor spaces, including a pool, patio and garden, all have gorgeous views of the surrounding landscape and give the kids plenty of space to run around and play.The beach is just a 10-minute drive away if you want to split your time (we did), but the pool area is so nice you may find yourself happy to just gaze out over the views of the sea but never spend time there!The private parking area adds peace of mind but the area is so tranquil that it feels unnecessary. Overall Villa Sant Angelo is a wonderful getaway in a still mostly-unknown area of Italy and is the perfect spot for a quiet Abruzzo holiday getaway for even the most demanding travelers."
