Description

Opening to a patio with garden views, all units are fitted with a kitchenette with a fridge and a stovetop. There's a seating and/or dining area in some units.

At the resort village you'll find a restaurant serving Italian cuisine.

Riva Nuova Camping Village has a seasonal outdoor pool.

A playground and a private beach area can be found at the accommodation, along with a garden.

San Benedetto del Tronto is 5 miles from Riva Nuova Camping Village, while Ascoli Piceno is 18 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Abruzzo Airport, 34 miles from the resort village.
