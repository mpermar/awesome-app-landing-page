Description

Each air-conditioned room at the Sopot Marriott Resort & Spa includes elegant interiors and a modern bathroom. There is a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, as well as a work desk, a mini-bar and a free bottle of mineral water. Views of the sea or the park can also be enjoyed.

Sopot Marriott Resort & Spa offers 4 swimming pools with mood lighting and waterfalls. One of them is an outdoor pool located on the roof. Visitors are welcome to order a professional massage or relax in the one of the saunas. Children can use the game rooms or the playground.

There are 2 restaurants – Brasserie and Pescatore. The first one serves a breakfast buffet and the latter specializes in fish dishes. The roof bar by the pool is available during summer and offers a wide choice of beverages.

Sopot Marriott Resort & Spa is located 0.9 mi from the pier and runs bicycle rentals. The Sopot Train Station is 1.3 mi away.

Karlikowo is a great choice for travelers interested in beach walks, beaches and the oceanside.
