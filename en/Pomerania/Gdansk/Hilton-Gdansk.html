Description

The Hilton’s rooms have 32-inch LCD TVs with satellite channels. They also feature tea and coffee making facilities and a mini-bar. Most offer views of the Old Town or the river.

The hotel is located just 650 feet from the Medieval Port Crane and about 1,950 feet from Długi Targ Street, the heart of the city’s Old Town. Lech Wałęsa Airport is 9.9 mi from the hotel.

Guests can relax in the Hilton's gym and sauna. The hotel also has spa and wellness facilities, which offer massages and treatments. The hotel’s multilingual staff is available 24/7.

The Mercato Restaurant serves Polish and international dishes. The High 5 Terrace Bar has an open-deck terrace that offers views of Gdańsk. Guests can also get a drink at the F32 bar.
