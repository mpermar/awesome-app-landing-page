Description

The elegant rooms and suites have air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with hairdryer, bathrobes and free toiletries.

Featuring a detailed interior design and limestone walls, Casa Oaxaca showcases original pieces of contemporary art. Guests can pick a book from the library, which has armchairs, a fireplace and a piano.

Casa Oaxaca’s restaurant serves a blend of Mediterranean and typical cuisine. There is also a bar. Additional services like massages, shuttle service or yoga lessons can be requested for an extra cost.

The Contemporary Art Museum is 1,000 feet away. Oaxaca International Airport is 10 miles away.
