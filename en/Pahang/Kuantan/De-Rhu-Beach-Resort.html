Description

The resort is 5 miles from Beserah Fishing Village and 7.5 miles from Cherating Beach and local handicraft at Teluk Cempedak. It is 9 miles from Kuantan and 19 miles from Sultan Ahmad Shah Airport.

With hardwood floors, the air-conditioned rooms feature floor-to-ceiling windows offering sea, pool or garden views. They come equipped with tea/coffee making facilities and a TV. Private bathrooms come with shower facilities.

De Rhu Café serves both local Malaysian delights and Western favorites. Room service is available.

Tour arrangement is possible and barbecue facilities are available on advance request. The staff at the 24-hour front desk can assist with luggage storage, currency exchange and laundry services.

Children and Extra Beds
All children are welcome.
One child under 6 years is charged MYR 53 per night when using existing beds.
All children under 2 years are charged MYR 20 per night in a crib.
All children under 6 years are charged MYR 53 per night for extra beds.
Any additional older children or adults are charged MYR 53 per night for extra beds.
The maximum number of extra beds in a room is 1.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
