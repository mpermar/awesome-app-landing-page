Description

Colmar Tropicale - Berjaya Hills is a 20-minute drive from Bentong Hot Spring and provides free parking. It is 34 miles from the center of Kuala Lumpur and 75 miles from Kuala Lumpur International Airport.

Surrounded by rainforests, air-conditioned rooms at Colmar Tropicale are well-furnished with classic light wood furnishings and floral prints. They have a TV and an attached bathroom with a bathtub.

The award-winning Tatami Spa features relaxing massage therapies. Guests can enjoy the fresh air outdoors while horseback riding or playing paint ball. Other activities include jungle trekking and karaoke.

La Cigogne offers authentic French fine dining while Le Poulet Roti serves roasted chicken. Other dining options include a Japanese restaurant, French bakery, Italian restaurant and 2 cafés.
