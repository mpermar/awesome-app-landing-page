Description

The rooms at the Mendes Plaza Hotel are divided into 2 towers and feature cable TV and a private bathroom with toiletries.

The Mendes houses The Garden Restaurant, which serves a wide variety of local and international dishes and wines.

The Mendes also has a fitness center, a sauna and an outdoor pool surrounded by tables and chairs. Parking is available for a fee.
