Description

The rooms are spacious and bright, with air conditioning, cable TV, a modern kitchenette and a seating area. Some are also equipped with a balcony, LCD TV and a bathroom with a spa bath.

The buffet breakfast is served in the elegant Hillman bistro, carries a supplement and includes varied tropical fruits, artisan breads and pastries. In addition, you can taste exotic cocktails and contemporary gourmet cuisine.

The 5-star George V Residence Casa Branca is 1 block from the select shops on Oscar Freire Street and 2 blocks from the magnificent restaurants on Barão de Capanema Street. Paulista Avenue and Trianon-Masp Metro Station are less than 7 streets away.

Jardim Paulista is a great choice for travelers interested in gourmet cuisine, museums and food.
