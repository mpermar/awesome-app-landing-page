Description

The suites at Tivoli Mofarrej - São Paulo feature modern décor and include a lounge decorated with orchids. Some have stunning panoramic views of the city.

The Anantara spa, one of the winners at the 2015 World Spa Awards, presents a Thai-themed space for restoring balance and harmony between body and mind. It offers several treatments and massages in an oasis of beauty, grace and tranquility.

Guests can enjoy the heated outdoor pool and well-equipped gym.

The Bistrô Tivoli restaurant offers a contemporary menu, while the Seen Restaurant & Bar overlooks the city. The Must bar offers artisan cocktails, especially created by the winner of the best waitress in Brazil, Jessica Sánchez. Guests can also enjoy live music in the evenings.

The Tivoli serves a rich breakfast buffet with croissants, eggs and French pastries. There is also 24-hour room service.

The Tivoli is 500 meters from the Cidade São Paulo shopping center, 2 km from the modern Oscar Freire street and 6.5 km from the Cidade Jardim and JK shopping centers. Congonhas Airport is 7 km away, while Guarulhos Airport is 24 km away.

Jardim Paulista is a great choice for travelers interested in gourmet cuisine, museums and food.
