Description

The rooms at Hyatt São Paulo are modern and bright and feature large windows with panoramic city views, exclusive cleaning service, LCD TV with cable channels, minibar and desk.

The Grand Hyatt, very well valued for the quality of its food, has 2 restaurants serving international dishes. The club lounge is a renovated space that is located on the 20th floor and provides exclusive services to guests. Rates include breakfast and a free cocktail.

The establishment has a sauna, indoor pool, outdoor pool and spa, which offers facial and body treatments. This luxury hotel also houses a recently refurbished and fully equipped fitness center. Your staff is very helpful.

The 5-star Grand Hyatt hotel is just 10 minutes from the Itaim Bibi neighborhood.

Itaim Bibi is a great choice for travelers interested in gourmet cuisine, restaurants and luxury restaurants.
