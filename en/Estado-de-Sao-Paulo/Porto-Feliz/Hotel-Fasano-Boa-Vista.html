Description

The rooms are spacious and luxurious, mix modern decor with colonial elements and include high-quality wooden furniture, exposed brick walls, air conditioning, flat-screen TVs with cable channels and a balcony overlooking the garden and garden. lake.

The dining area has an electric kettle, coffee maker, minibar and dining table, while the private bathroom has a bathtub, hairdryer, bidet and free toiletries.

Hotel Fasano Boa Vista features the famous Fasano restaurant, a spa and wellness center and a tennis court. There are also entertainment staff, meeting facilities and a games room. You can practice various activities, such as golf, cycling and horse riding.

Viracopos International Airport is 56 km away. Private parking is provided.
