Description

Mont Blanc Premium's beautiful suites feature a flat-screen TV with cable channels, air conditioning, a minibar, a dining table and a private bathroom with a shower, hairdryer and bathrobes, as well as views of the city and the pool.

This hotel is 3.8 km from the Municipal Theater, 3.9 km from Pinguim Brewery, 7 km from the bus station, 2 km from Ribeirão Shopping Center and 10 km from the city airport. It offers private parking.

In the morning, in a sophisticated breakfast room, a buffet breakfast is served with a variety of fresh fruit, selected breads and cold meats, and all kinds of hot and cold drinks.
