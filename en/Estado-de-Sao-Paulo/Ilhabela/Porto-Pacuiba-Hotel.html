Description

The rooms at the Porto Pacuíba Hotel feature silent air conditioning and an LCD satellite TV with more than 90 TV and music channels. Some rooms have a private balcony with views of the sea or the garden.

The Portofino restaurant, run by chef Arthur Alves, offers an international menu prepared by chef Michel Darqué. The hotel serves a buffet breakfast with more than 50 options, such as homemade cakes, jams and cheeses. Custom dinners and menus can also be prepared for special occasions.

Guests can relax by the pool with a drink from the hotel bar, enjoy a revitalizing massage in the spa or make use of the sauna. The hotel provides free WiFi connection in all its facilities.
