Description

All rooms at the Bourbon Atibaia Spa Resort have a minibar and a bathroom.

Guests can enjoy a variety of sports in the large sports and leisure area of the Bourbon Atibaia Spa Resort, which includes facilities for archery, soccer field, 2 volleyball courts with sand and 6 tennis courts.

For an additional fee, the Bourbon by L’Occitane SPA offers various health and beauty treatments, such as massages, exfoliations and masks.

The Bourbon Atibaia Spa Resort has several bars and restaurants, serving international and Brazilian dishes.

The hotel is just 40 minutes drive from the Cumbica International Airport.
