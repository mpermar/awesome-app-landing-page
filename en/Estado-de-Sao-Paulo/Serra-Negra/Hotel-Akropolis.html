Description

All rooms and chalets offer mountain views and are equipped with air conditioning, a TV with cable channels, a minibar and a private bathroom with a hot shower and a hairdryer.

A buffet breakfast with a variety of fresh fruits, breads and cold meats is served daily. Guests can also enjoy regional specialties and varied drinks in the hotel restaurant.

Hotel Akropolis offers hiking, horse riding, massage therapies and children's playground with entertainers.

The hotel offers a free shuttle service to the center of Serra Negra and is 2 km from the Serra Negra bus terminal and 70 km from Campinas.
