Description

It also includes a children's club and entertainment staff for adults and for the little ones. For leisure, in addition to the sauna, it houses a games room. There is also a modern gym

The rooms at the Terra Parque Eco Resort are equipped with air conditioning, TV, telephone, minibar and patio with pleasant views of the garden.

This eco resort is 5 km from Ademar de Barros Airport, 20 km from Presidente Prudente Bus Station and a 5-minute drive from Rancho Quarto de Milha Farm.
