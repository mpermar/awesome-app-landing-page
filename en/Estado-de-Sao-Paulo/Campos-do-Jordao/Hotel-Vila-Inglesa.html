Description

The rooms have air conditioning, heating, 32-inch flat-screen TV and DVD player.

Hotel Vila Inglesa has a games room, playground, fitness center, a library and a living room with a fireplace. Bicycle tours and zip line sessions can be booked at the tour desk.

Hotel Vila Inglesa is managed by the Mazzaropi group, is a 2-hour drive from São Paulo and offers free parking.
