Description

The wellness center has saunas, hot tubs and massages. The hotel also has a tennis court and a sports gym. In the surroundings you can go hiking, among many other activities.

All rooms have warm lighting and wooden furniture. Some include a balcony overlooking the garden and a seating area. The private bathrooms have Trousseau toiletries.

There are several places to eat like buffets, pizzerias and a restaurant for children. The Grande Hotel also houses the bar da Lareira, which has an impressive fireplace and offers fondues and an extensive wine list.

Grande Hotel Campos do Jordão is 3 km from the town center and the bus terminal. The entrance is 10 minutes by car from the hotel and São Paulo and Guarulhos International Airport, 2 hours by car.
