Description

Toriba's rooms are decorated in cream tones and feature quality wood fittings, heating, a balcony, cable TV, telephone and minibar. The chalets include a full kitchenette and a seating area.

The rich breakfast buffet consists of homemade bread and pastries and is served in the panoramic breakfast room. The Pennachi restaurant specializes in Brazilian and international cuisine and the Toribinha prepares popular fondues.

The property is 8 km from Capivari, the tourist center of Campos do Jordão, where you will find other dining options and numerous bars and souvenir shops.

You can enjoy massages, a sauna session or a bath in the hot tub, among native pine trees and flower gardens. The 5-star Hotel Toriba also has a games room, mini-orange, private hiking trail and free parking.
