Description

All rooms are non-smoking and have a warm and elegant decoration. They feature flat-screen TVs and minibars. The bathroom is private and includes a hairdryer and bathrobe. Some have a fireplace, DVD player and seating area.

The daily breakfast buffet is served in the Alquimia restaurant, which only offers a variety of vegetarian dishes during lunch and dinner. The famous Baden Baden restaurant is a 5-minute walk away.

The hotel has an outdoor and indoor pool, sauna, fitness center and games room. There is a children's club and a children's playground.

Capivari is a great choice for travelers interested in gourmet cuisine, food and fancy restaurants.
