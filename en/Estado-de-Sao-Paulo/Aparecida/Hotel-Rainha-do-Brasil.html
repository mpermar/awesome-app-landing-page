Description

The rooms at Hotel Rainha do Brasil are elegant and modern and equipped with air conditioning, cable TV, minibar, safe and private bathroom.

In the morning a breakfast is served that includes a variety of fresh fruit, bread and sausages. There is also a restaurant that offers buffets and à la carte specialties, as well as different drinks.

The hotel also has heated swimming pools, sauna, games room and meeting and event rooms, as well as 24-hour reception and free private parking.
