Description

The spacious rooms at the Lindner Park-Hotel Hagenbeck include a flat-screen TV and laptop safe. Wi-Fi is available in all areas.

The arctic-themed Lindner fit & well area on the 5th floor includes 2 saunas, steam room and a relaxation room. The fitness room offers great views of Hamburg.

Creative, international cuisine is served in the Lindner Park-Hotel Hagenbeck’s Augila restaurant with show kitchen. Guests can also relax on the terrace or in the Baobab Bar & Café.

The Hagenbecks Tierpark Underground Station is only 300 feet from the Lindner Park-Hotel. Trains run to the Hamburg Messe Exhibition Center in 20 minutes.

Stellingen is a great choice for travelers interested in convenient public transportation, fish markets and city trips.
