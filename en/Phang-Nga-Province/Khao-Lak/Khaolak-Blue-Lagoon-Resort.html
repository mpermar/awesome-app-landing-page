Description

Khaolak Blue Lagoon Resort is only a 10-minute drive from central Khaolak. It is a 1.5-hour ride from Phuket International Airport.

Stylish villas have beautiful décor with a private balcony. They come equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen cable TV and a refrigerator. Private bathrooms have shower facilities.

Guests can take a relaxing walk on the grounds or laze around the pool. The staff at the tour desk can arrange sightseeing tours for guests.

Enjoy light snacks and refreshing drinks at Breeze Bar. For meals, guests can sample delicious Thai and international cuisine at Shallot Restaurant.

Khuk Khak Beach is a great choice for travelers interested in beach walks, sunsets and sandy beaches.
