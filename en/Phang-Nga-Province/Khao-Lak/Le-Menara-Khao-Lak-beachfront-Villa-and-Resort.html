Description

At the resort, the rooms are equipped with a desk and a flat-screen TV. All rooms come with a private bathroom. All guest rooms include a closet.

Le Menara offers a continental or buffet breakfast. There's an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of Grill/bbq dishes.

The accommodation has a year-round outdoor pool. You can play ping pong, pool and darts at this 5-star resort, and the area is popular for cycling.

PTT Gas Station Khaolak is 10 miles from Le Menara Khao Lak beachfront Villa & Resort. Phuket Airport is 50 miles away.
