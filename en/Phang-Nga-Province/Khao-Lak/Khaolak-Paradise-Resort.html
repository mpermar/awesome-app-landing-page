Description

Khaolak Resort's air conditioned rooms feature hardwood floors, coffee/tea maker, minibar and satellite TV. The private bathroom offers a separate shower and bathtub, bathrobe and slippers.

Guests can relax on the sun deck by the pool or enjoy a variety of treatments and massages at The Paradise Spa. Car rental, laundry and babysitting services are available.

Leelavadee Dining offers indoor and outdoor seating on the beach. It serves Thai and international dishes.

Khaolak Paradise Resort is 56 miles from Phuket and is situated next to the National Park. Central Khao Lak is half a mile away.

Experiences

03/29/2018

We had an amazing stay. Very chilled out and relaxing. No entertainment or children

"Staff were amazing. Very very efficient and friendly. Small boutique hotel with only 30 rooms but really felt welcomed. Nothing was too much trouble. Superb location on the beach. Very pretty. Food was very good and not too expensive. We were initally given a ground floor room which we dont really like, but after two days they were able to move us to a first floor room with a great view of the gardens. None of the rooms apart from the villas have a complete sea view beause of the way the hotel is designed, but it is not a problem because being a small hotel, it is only a short stroll to the bar, dining areas and beach. Loved the beach dining - very romantic!"
