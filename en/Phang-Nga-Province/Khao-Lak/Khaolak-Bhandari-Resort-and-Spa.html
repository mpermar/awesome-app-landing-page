Description

Featuring traditional Thai architecture, accommodations at the Khaolak Bhandari use local materials, fabrics and art for decoration. Spacious and elegantly decorated, chalets and bungalows have polished hardwood floors and several amenities to ensure your comfort.

Unwind by its free-form saltwater swimming pool and a spa tub and and soaking up the sun with a refreshing drink or enjoy the wide range of treatments and facilities provided at the Bhandari Spa. It offers massages, aromatherapy and body treatments, as well as a sauna and hot tub.

For those more adventurous, diving and snorkeling are also arranged by the hotel. For more information on attractions and travel arrangements for a day trip, inquire with the helpful staff at the tour desk.

Dine outdoors at Bhandari Restaurant, which serves delicious Thai and international dishes that will satisfy your appetite.

Khaolak Bhandari Resort and Spa is only a short walk from the beach and is within each reach from attractions, such as the National Park with its rich rainforest and waterfalls.

Just off the resort's coast, Pakarang Reef and the Similan Islands are only a boat ride away.

Nang Thong Beach is a great choice for travelers interested in beach walks, restaurants and sunsets.
