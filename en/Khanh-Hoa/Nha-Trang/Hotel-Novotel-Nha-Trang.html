Description

Adorned in neutral shades, all sea-facing units come with a private balcony offering plenty of natural light. Tastefully furnished, units are also fitted with a cable flat-screen TV and mini-bar. Attached bathroom is equipped with a rain shower and hairdryer.

Other facilities at Hotel Novotel Nha Trang include a stylish spa and gym room. Attentive staff at the 24-hour reception can assist you with luggage storage and other concierge services. Tour arrangements, airport transfer and car hire service can be requested at the tour desk.

Featuring stunning views of Nha Trang Bay, The Square Restaurant serves hearty international buffets. The pool and lobby bars has a selection of light snacks and cocktails.

Hotel Novotel Nha Trang is 650 feet from 2/4 Square, while Nha Trang Center Shopping Mall is 0.7 mi away. The nearest airport is Cam Ranh International Airport, 19 mi away.
