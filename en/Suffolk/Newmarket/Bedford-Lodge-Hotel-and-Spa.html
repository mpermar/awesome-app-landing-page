Description

Bedford Lodge has a first-class health club complete with a 50 feet indoor pool, a hot tub, a sauna and steam rooms. Use of the recreation club is included in the room rate.

The hotel is 20 minutes from Cambridge and has easy access to central London.

The Bedford Lodge Hotel combines classic charm with modern comforts. Bedrooms are stylish and well-furnished, and the conference rooms are spacious.
