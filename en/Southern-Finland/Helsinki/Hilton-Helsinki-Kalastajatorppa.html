Description

Hilton Helsinki Kalastajatorppa’s air-conditioned rooms include an LCD TV, a work desk and a mini-bar. Many rooms have a seating area.

Restaurant Oceana’s large breakfast buffet includes a variety of hot and cold choices. Restaurant Meritorppa has an open kitchen and a terrace with sea views. Guests can choose from a menu of Finnish and international dishes.

Guests can relax in Hilton Kalastajatorppa's large garden or rent bicycles to explore the area.

Free private outdoor parking spaces are found on site. Private indoor parking is provided for an additional fee.

Hilton Helsinki Kalastajatorppa is about 1,300 feet from Saunalahdentie Tram Stop. Helsinki Central Station is about 25 minutes’ tram ride away.

Läntinen Suurpiiri is a great choice for travelers interested in nature, convenient public transportation and tranquillity.

A good place for nature lovers with children

"- The hotel is located in a nice place by the sea, has a lot of parks nearby, and if you have a car then you can access all the sights easily. For children, there is a public playground at Perustie 38 about 2 km from the hotel that has swings, ropes, slides etc for smaller and bigger children. - Swimming in the sea is good even in colder weather, because you can warm up in the sauna. There are bathrobes and slippers in the room, so you don't need to take a lot of things to the pool. Towels are available at the sauna.- The hotel may be old, but everything is clean, the doors are solid, the room is well-planned and comfortable, and the pool is great for children - 0,9 meters deep at one end and 1,8 metres deep at another. We were surprised that so few people visited the pool and sauna, although there were a lot of guests in the hotel.- Breakfast is really good, lots of choices for every taste - I especially liked watermelon, porridge and omelette "muffins". We did not eat in restaurants because prices are high (34 EUR for main course). I can recommend a small place called "Pizza Mix" where an Italian guy makes real good pizza, about 2 km from the hotel. - Free parking places- The price that we paid (about 170 EUR per night) was comparable to other hotels in Helsinki at the time, so we felt it was a good deal, as there were many extras that suited us well, e.g. the king size bed accommodates 2 adults and a child with no problems."
