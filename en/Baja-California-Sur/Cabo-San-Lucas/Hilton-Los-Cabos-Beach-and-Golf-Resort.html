Description

With a full-service spa, a children's club and water sport activities, guests of all ages are sure to have an unforgettable stay at the Hilton Los Cabos Beach and Golf Resort. The resort also boasts a spacious infinity pool overlooking the water, and complete with a swim-up bar. There are also several on-site restaurants available.

The Los Cabos Beach and Golf Resort Hilton is only minutes from top-rated golf courses. Nearby San Jose del Cabo offers charming shops along with a variety of historical sites. Recreational activities, including scuba diving, horseback riding and fishing are also easily accessible.
