Description

The villas at Club Cascadas de Baja feature a rustic Mexican style and include ceiling fans, sitting and dining areas, full kitchens and balconies. Some units may have a hot tub.

Guests staying at Club Cascadas de Baja can enjoy a wide variety of different gourmet tastes: Italian, Mexican, Hawaiian, seafood and buffet services among others. The bar has daily drink specials from 10 am to 6 pm and sushi bar specials from 11 am to 5 pm on Saturdays.

Club Cascadas de Baja has different activities like bingo, Spanish lessons, water aerobics and cooking classes.

The property is a 5-minute drive from Cabo San Lucas Country Club, and Los Cabos International Airport can be reached in a 40-minute drive.
