Description

The spacious, air-conditioned rooms overlook the Mediterranean Sea. Different categories of Suites are available for families; some are overlooking the Sea and others the WaterGate Aqua Park, located near the Hotel.

Le Jardin du Royal serves seafood dishes as well as Italian and Mexican cuisine. French specialities and live piano music are offered at The Titanic and Diwan Shahrayar has belly dance shows and Arabic fare. Ikebana nightclub hosts Arabic comedy evenings.

Tennis and squash courts, an indoor swimming pool and a well-equipped gym are available on the property. The extensive 4-level Royal Spa has face and body treatments and indoor hot tubs.

Le Royal Hotel Beirut, Lebanon is a 10-minute drive from Jbeil Byblos town, Jeita Grotto and a 25-minute drive from Faraya Ski Resorts.
