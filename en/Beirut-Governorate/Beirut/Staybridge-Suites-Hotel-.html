Description

All air-conditioned suites are fitted with tile and carpet floors. Each one has a living area with a flat-screen TV and a balcony overlooking the city. It includes a kitchenette and a dining area. Interconnecting rooms are available.

Guests can enjoy a daily breakfast buffet at the Hub Kitchen. International cuisine for all tastes is available throughout the day.

The hotel is close to the Banking Financial area, downtown Solidere and close to well known universities such as AUB and LAU. Guests can easily reach Raouche, Beirut Souks and the Waterfront Corniche from the property.
