Description

Rooms at Country Lodge Club & Resort are decorated in neutral colors and have satellite TV. Each offers an outdoor balcony, and some rooms have a kitchenette.

Guests can relax with a massage at the spa, or swim in the outdoor or indoor pools. Basketball, rollerblading and aerobics are also available at the Country Lodge & Club.

The Country Lodges clubhouse offers a fireplace, where guests can enjoy a cocktail from the bar.

The hotel is 25 minutes from the Beirut International Airport. Guests can reach the Broumanna tourist district with a short walk.
