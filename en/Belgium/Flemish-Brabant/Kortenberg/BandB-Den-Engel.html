Description

Set in Erps-Kwerps within 10 km from Brussels Airport, B&B Den Engel features an on-site bar and a private garden with a terrace.

Located 18 km from the historic centre of Brussels, this property also includes free WiFi.

The suite at Den Engel comprises a living room with a seating corner and a dining area.

It also offers an open-plan kitchen and a bathroom with a shower. Additional amenities include a minibar and garden view.

Breakfast can be served in the room every morning. Guests can enjoy a drink in the bar or out on the terrace.

Free use of bicycles to explore the surroundings is available. From B&B Den Engel, it is 2 km to the Kortenberg Train Station and 10.

5 km to historical Leuven. A direct bus to Zaventem is stopping right in front of the property.
