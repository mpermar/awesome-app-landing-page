Description

Located along the Lanklaar Canal, this bed and breakfast offers warmly decorated rooms with free Wi-Fi.

There is a lush garden with a Nipa Hut, wood-burning oven, and a terrace. Rooms at Don Genaro come with a seating area and a TV.

Each has a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries. The B&B also has wellness facilities including a sauna and whirlpool.

Bike and Vespa rentals are available for guests to explore the surrounding area. Boat tours to Maastricht village can be arranged on request.

Guests will receive a 10% discount card for Maasmechelen Village Outlet Center upon check-in.

Maasmechelen shopping village is a 5-minute drive. Rekem is 10 km from B&B Don Genaro.
