Description

Surrounded by a green garden, De Dubbelmolen offers spacious suites with free Wi-Fi in a historic watermill.

There is also a playground for younger guests. The bright suites come with a separate seating area and a TV with cable channels.

Each has a private bathroom with a shower or bath and a hairdryer. Some rooms overlook the garden, while others provide a river view.

In the morning, guests can enjoy a buffet breakfast in the breakfast room, or on the garden terrace in summertime.
