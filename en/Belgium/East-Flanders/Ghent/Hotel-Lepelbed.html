Description

Hotel Lepelbed offers air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, just over 10 minutes’ drive from Ghent.

Its restaurant opens onto a furnished terrace, which overlooks the landscaped garden.

A rich breakfast buffet is served daily. In the evening, guests can enjoy regional specialities and traditional Belgian beers in the brightly decorated restaurant.

Packed lunches and room service are also available. The modern rooms come with a flat-screen TV and a minibar.

Some feature a wooden-beamed ceiling. All have a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

The family-run Lepelbed provides easy access to the E40 and E17 highways. A shuttle to Brussels International Airport and Merelbeke Train Station is available on request.
