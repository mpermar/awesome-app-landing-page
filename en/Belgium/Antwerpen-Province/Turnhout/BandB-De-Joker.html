Description

This bed and breakfast is situated in a peaceful residential area, only 5 minutes’ drive from the central market square in Turnhout.

De Joker offers rooms with free Wi-Fi and includes a spacious garden terrace. Bicycles can also be rented on site.

Flat-screen cable TV, work desk, kitchenette and a seating area are provided as standard in the rooms at B&B De Joker A buffet breakfast is served each morning in the cosy dining room.

Lilse Golf Club is a 15-minute car journey from the guest house. The centre of Antwerp is 35 minutes away from De Joker B&B by car.
