Description

Hotel and Chaletpark Fauwater enjoys a peaceful location in the woods, a 5-minute drive from the centre of Kasterlee.

The accommodation offers free access to public parking, a bistro and a bar with game facilities.

Guests can sit out on the hotel's terrace. All rooms at Hotel and Chaletpark Fauwater are equipped with a TV and complimentary WiFi.

They all have a private bathroom with shower. The hotel comprises a bistro which serves various snacks and drinks.

AT the bar, you can play social games such as darts, billiards and table tennis. The surrounding area of the property is ideal for long walks and cycling trips.

Hotel and Chaletpark Fauwater is situated 1,5 km from the city centres of Lichtaart and Kasterlee.

Bobbejaanland Theme Park is 3 km away and a 10-minute drive will take you to the nearest E34 and E313 Motorway Exits.

The Koningsbos bust stop is 100 metres from the hotel and Tielen Train Station is 4 km away.
