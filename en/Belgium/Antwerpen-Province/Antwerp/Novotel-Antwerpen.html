Description

All of the spacious rooms at Novotel Antwerpen feature satellite TV, a minibar and a work desk. They also include tea and coffee-making facilities.

The Foodsquare Brasserie serves international cuisine with Belgian specialties in informal surroundings. Guests can also enjoy a selection of snacks at the bar or on the terrace in warmer weather.

Novotel Antwerpen has a game room with ping-pong. There is also an X-box available for children to use.

Antwerpen Novotel is in the business district and is only a 15-minute drive from Antwerp International Airport. Meir Shopping District and the historic center of Antwerp are a 15-minute drive away. Ghent is a 45-minute car ride away.

Antwerp Center is a great choice for travelers interested in museums, shopping and clothes shopping.
