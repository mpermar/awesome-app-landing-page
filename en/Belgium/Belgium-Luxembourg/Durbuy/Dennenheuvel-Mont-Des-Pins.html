Description

Enjoy a wonderful combination of wellness, gastronomy, natural surroundings and tasteful guest rooms at the friendly resort of the Denneheuvel.

Guests can make use of free WiFi in the bar and lobby. The hotel includes a spa area with sauna, steam bath, Italian shower, relaxation area with starlight ceiling, accessible for an extra fee.

Situated on the edge of Bomal-sur-Ourthe, in the midst of a beautiful park and near Durbuy, this holiday complex offers many recreational facilities.

You can sign up for several outdoor activities at the reception, such as kayaking, rafting or horse riding.

Have a drink and a healthy snack in the restaurant Zen or sit out on its lovely terrace.

Taste the culinary creations, prepared with healthy, regional products, from the buffets at the restaurant Senza.

After exploring the region or participating in some fun activities, you can relax or pamper yourself with a treatment in the wellness centre.
