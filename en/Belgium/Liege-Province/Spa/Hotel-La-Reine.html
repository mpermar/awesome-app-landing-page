Description

Hotel La Reine is situated in tranquil surroundings in the town of Spa, less than a 15-minute walk from the thermal baths.

It benefits from free WiFi and a terrace. Each of the rooms at Hotel La Reine has a private entrance via a covered veranda in the garden.

They also include a minibar, TV and a private bathroom with a bath or a walk-in shower.

Continental breakfast with warm and cold dishes is served daily. When the weather is nice, guests can enjoy the breakfast on the terrace.

The hotel features a Bib Gourmand restaurant 'La Cour de la Reine'. It is using own grown products from the vegetable garden.

The resaurant is specialising in local cuisine, local products, and dry-aged meats.

Hotel La Reine is a 5-minute drive from Royal Golf Club de Fagnes. The Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps is less than 15 minutes away by car.
