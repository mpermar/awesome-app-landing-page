Description

This renovated villa is set in a traditional Ardennes house, located 1 km from the center of Spa and 9 km from the Spa Francorchamps racetrack.

It features a heated swimming pool and accommodation with kitchenettes and flat-screen TVs.

La Villa d'Olne also offers a sauna, bubble bath and massage treatments. For workouts, guests can opt for the on-site gym.

The rooms have parquet floors and wooden ceiling beams. Guests benefit from a kitchenette with microwave, dishwasher and all the necessary utensils.

There is a children’s playground and soccer table for the younger guests. The games room also has a billiards table.

Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel. Spa’s city centre is 10-minutes’ walk away from La Villa d'Olne.
