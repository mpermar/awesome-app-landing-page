Description

Enjoying green surroundings, Chatelet Cremers is housed in an old mansion at the edge of Verviers-Heusy.

This bed and breakfast provides guests with eating facilities, a garden with a terrace and free access to WiFi.

Bicycles can be rented at the property. Fitted with hardwood flooring, each room at Chatelet Cremers comes with an en-suite bathroom with a shower, a toilet and a wash basin.

The superior room features a private balcony whilst the suite is also provided with a seating area and an infrared sauna.

Upon request, you can order packed lunches for your day trips. Guests can enjoy regional meals or a drink at the shared lounge/TV area.

The centre of Spa is a 25-minute drive from Chatelet Cremers. From the bed and breakfast, Francorchamps is 18.

8 km and Aywaille is 21 km. Private parking nearby the accommodation is free of charge.

A bowling alley, a tennis court and a racecourse are within 2 km from the property.
