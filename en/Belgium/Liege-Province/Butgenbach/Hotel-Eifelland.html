Description

Hotel Eifelland is situated near the main market square in the charming town centre of Bütgenbach.

This family hotel has a private garden terrace to relax and offers free access to wireless internet throughout the property.

The hotel’s bar, lounge and winter garden provide an informal setting for a drink and a chat.

Eifelland offers table tennis, a fitness room and a sauna and solarium. A mini-golf course and a playground are situated only 100 metres from the hotel.
