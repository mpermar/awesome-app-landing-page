Description

The rooms at the farm stay come with a seating area. Every room comes with air conditioning, a desk and a flat-screen TV, and some units at Xhawirs Horse Farm have a terrace. All rooms include a closet.

The accommodation has a playground. Guests at Xhawirs Horse Farm will be able to enjoy activities in and around Soumagne, like cycling.

A business center and on-site meeting rooms cater for guests travelling for business purposes.

Maastricht is 16 miles from the farm stay. The nearest airport is Liege Airport, 13 miles from Xhawirs Horse Farm.
