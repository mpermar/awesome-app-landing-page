Description

Hotel Le Val D'arimont offers rooms and cottages at the foot of the High Fens, 3 km from Malmedy.

You can make free use of the many sport and wellness facilities. Guests benefit from free WiFi throughout the hotel.

The rooms and cottages are peacefully located. You can play various sports on-site, such as tennis, ping-pong, squash and minigolf.

If you want to relax, you may use the pools, hammam and spa bath. The café, brasserie and restaurant offer a variety of drinks, meals and snacks from early morning till late in the evening.
