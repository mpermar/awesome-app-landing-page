Description

Domaine de Ronchinne - Gardener's House features a restaurant, bar, shared lounge and garden in Maillen.

Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a terrace. There is a children's playground and guests can make use of free WiFi and free private parking.

At the hotel, every room is equipped with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

A continental breakfast is available daily at Maison du Jardinier. You can play billiards, table tennis, and mini-golf at the accommodation.

Staff speak English and French at the reception. Durbuy is 49 km from Domaine de Ronchinne - Maison du Jardinier, while Namur is 19 km away.
