Description

Offering a barbecue and children's playground, Au Manoir Des Vallees is situated in Maffe.

Free private parking is available on site. Rooms have a flat-screen TV. For your comfort, you will find slippers and free toiletries.

Au Manoir Des Vallees features free WiFi. The accommodation also features a pétanque court.

Free use of bicycles is available at this bed and breakfast and the area is popular for golfing and cycling.

Liège is 37 km from Au Manoir Des Vallees, while Durbuy is 10 km away. Liège Airport is 34 km from the property.
