Description

At the hotel, the rooms come with a closet. FunKey Hotel has certain units with garden views, and each room has a private bathroom.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet or a vegetarian breakfast.

The reception can offer helpful tips for getting around the area.

Rue Neuve is 1.2 miles from FunKey Hotel, while Magritte Museum is 1.3 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Zaventem Airport, 6 miles from the hotel.

Schaarbeek / Schaerbeek is a great choice for travelers interested in monuments, waffles and city walks.
