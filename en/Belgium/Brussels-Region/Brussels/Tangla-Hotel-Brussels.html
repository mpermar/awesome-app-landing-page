Description

Situated in Brussels, 5 km from the European Parliament, Tangla Hotel Brussels boasts an Oriental design respecting Feng-Shui rules.

Free WiFi is offered. Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and is equipped with a flat-screen TV.

Certain rooms feature a seating area for your convenience. You will find a coffee machine and a kettle in the room.

The rooms have a private bathroom. The hotel also features an all-dining restaurant, a bar lounge and several meeting rooms.

Brussels Grand Place is 6 km from Tangla Hotel and the nearest metro station is 700 metres away offering a direct connection to Brussels Central Station.
