Description

The family-run Alfa Inn includes accommodations with cable TV and a work desk. Each room also has a private bathroom with a shower.

Each morning, a continental-style breakfast is served with soft piano music in the bright breakfast room. Guests can choose from 3 types of eggs cooked to order and a buffet including croissants and fresh fruit salad.

The spa center of Alfa Inn includes a sauna, a hammam, a hot tub and a tanning bed. For an additional fee, you can enjoy these facilities.

Blankenberge Train Station and Blankenberge Casino are both only a 5-minute walk away. Wenduine is 5 minutes away by car and the towns of Knokke and Bruges are only a 20-minute car ride away.
