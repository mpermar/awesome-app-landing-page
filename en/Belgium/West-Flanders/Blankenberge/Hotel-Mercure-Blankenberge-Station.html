Description

Guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Hotel Mercure Blankenberge Station provides certain units with city views, and the rooms include a electric tea pot. The rooms feature a desk.

An American breakfast is served daily at the property.

Speaking English and French, staff at the 24-hour front desk can help you plan your stay.

Casino Blankenberge is a 5-minute walk from the accommodation, while Blankenberge Pier is 0.4 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Ostend-Bruges Airport, 14 miles from the property.
