Description

Each room at La Reserve Resort has luxury bedding and a TV with cable channels. The spacious bathroom comes with free toiletries and a separate bathtub.

Offering views of the Zegemeer lake, Restaurant Dinner 160 has a gourmet menu of meals prepared with seasonal ingredients. There is also Bar 160, serving drinks and snacks on its outside terrace.

The resort is less than one mile from the Royal Zoute Golf Club. The Knokke train station offers easy services to the other coastal cities and is just one mile away.

Knokke is a great choice for travelers interested in clothes shopping, restaurants and shopping.
