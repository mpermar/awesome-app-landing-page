Description

Situated in peaceful surroundings in Het Zoute village, Auberge St. Pol offers rooms with free WiFi and cable TV.

The North Sea beach is less than 1. 5 km away and Koningsbos Nature Area is 1100 metres from the hotel.

The rooms of Auberge St. Pol have a modern interior. Each is equipped with a minibar, a work desk, and a telephone.

Some rooms have a seating area. Room service is available. An extensive breakfast is served in Hotel Auberge St.

Pol’s breakfast room daily and the lounge bar offers a variety of drinks. There are restaurants, bistros and bars in the nearby Oosthoek Square.

This hotel's surroundings are ideal for long walks on the North Sea Beach and bike rides.

Guests can also visit the Zwin, Siska's and the Zeno Project. Other coastal cities such as Zeebrugge, Heist Duinberge and Blankenberge are easy to reach by tram from Knokke Station, which is 4 km away.
