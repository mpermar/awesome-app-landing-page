Description

This small holiday park is located within walking distance of the beach and the city centre of Koksijde.

Free WiFi access and parking are available in this camping. The accommodation will provide you with a patio and a seating area.

There is a full a kitchenette with a microwave and a refrigerator. The private bathroom is with a shower, sink and separate toilet.

Other facilities offered at the property include luggage storage and a children's playground.

Amusement park Plopsaland and Plopsaqua are within 5 km of the property. Nearby supermarkets can be reached by foot.

Chalets Eureka & Bloemenduin Koksijde consists out of two attached holiday parks.
