Description

The rooms at Duinhof are decorated in a typical English country style. They are equipped free Wi-Fi, satellite TV and private bathrooms.

Just over a quarter mile from the hotel, you can play golf, ride a horse, tennis, mini-golf and racquetball. There is a coastal tram service going from Knokke to other seaside cities such as Blankenberge and Duinberge.
