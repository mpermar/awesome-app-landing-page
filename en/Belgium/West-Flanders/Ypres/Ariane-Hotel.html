Description

Ariane is a family-run hotel offering modern rooms including a buffet-style breakfast, a 7-minute walk from Ypres' historical centre with the Menin Gate and the In Flanders Fields Museum.

This hotel is surrounded by gardens with a pond, a secluded terrace and a petanque square.

The air-conditioned rooms at this hotel feature classic décor and cable TV. All of the rooms at Ariane Hotel also include a minibar, a Nespresso coffee machine and tea facilities.

The buffet breakfast includes scrambled eggs, bacon and pastries. The restaurant serves modern international dishes prepared with seasonal ingredients.

Guests can enjoy snacks, meals or complete menus in the airy conservatory or outside on the terrace.

Ariane also provides guests with a bicycle rental service and offers packed lunches for day trips.

The Menin Gate, where the Last Post is played each evening, is 750 metres away. Ypres Railway Station is less than 15 minutes’ walk from the hotel.
