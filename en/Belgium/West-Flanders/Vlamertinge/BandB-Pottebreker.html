Description

B&B Pottebreker lies in the village of Vlamertinghe, 5 minutes away from both Ypres and Poperinge.

It offers modern, spacious rooms with flat-screen digital TV, free Wi-Fi and private bathrooms.

The wooden-floored rooms come with a box-spring bed and and free tea and coffee. There are free toiletries provided in the bathroom.

Every morning, guests can enjoy an extensive breakfast with regional products, fresh breads and juices.

Both Vlamertinghe and Poperinge offer a wide range of restaurants. Bellewaerde is 10 minutes away by car.

The Ypres Industry Park is a 5-minute drive from the guest house. The region around B&B Pottebreker features many hiking and cycling routes.
