Description

Surrounded by greenery, D'Hommelbelle offers rooms with private bathroom and free WiFi 3 km from the centre of Poperinge.

On-site private parking is possible free of charge. Rooms at D'Hommelbelle feature wooden floors and a seating area.

The property has also a children's playground and a library, as well as a room for bicycle and motorcycle storage.

Breakfast is served every morning. Packed lunches and special diet menus are available upon request.

Guests can make use of the kitchen and the bar. Ypres is 12 km from D'Hommelbelle and Watou, with its well-known breweries, is 4 km away.
