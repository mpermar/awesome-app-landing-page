Description

The ibis styles Nieuwpoort is located in the city of Nieuwpoort only a few kilometers from the beach of Nieuwpoort.

The hotel features wellness facilities such as a indoor swimming pool, an sauna with infrared lights and a hammam.

Free WiFi is available through the public areas and in all guest rooms. ibis styles Nieuwpoort offers classic and deluxe double and twin room as well as family rooms.

Each room includes a private bathroom with a bath or shower. Extras include free toiletries and a hairdryer.

On the ground floor there is a bar with terrace where guests can enjoy a drink or a quick bite.

Breakfast buffet is also served on the ground floor. For the children there is a digital play zone available.

Guest will find a 24-hour front desk and business facilities at the property. The hotel has a private parking.

The beach is only a 5 minute drive. This hotel has bike hire on site. Bruges is 33 km from ibis Styles Nieuwpoort, while Ostend is 15 km from the property.

Yser Tower and Plopsaland Animation Park is 13 km away from the property. Brussels International Airport is a one-hour drive from ibis Styles Nieuwpoort.
