Description

Whether you’re traveling for business or pleasure, this center allows you to combine both. The well equipped business facilities provide suitable spaces for inspiring meetings and the many sports facilities provide the perfect way to relax. Enjoy a game of tennis, a soak in the spa bathtub or a game of mini golf. The nearby dunes are an especially picturesque location for undisturbed strolls.

Fully equipped studios are the ideal accommodations for families or groups. The multi-level layout creates a spacious atmosphere and the opportunity to retreat to your own room. Enjoy the ease of preparing your own meals, grab something quick at the cafeteria or savor a tasty fish dish in the restaurant, De Kotter – renowned along the Belgian coast.
