Description

Ostend Hotel is set within 100 metres of the Oostende beaches and has fitness and sauna facilities on site.

It offers comfortable, modern accommodation with private bathrooms. Each room at Ostend is colourfully decorated with contemporary touches.

They come equipped with cable TVs, a work desk and box-spring beds. Free Wi-Fi is available in the hotel’s public areas.

Brugge's historic centre, which features the UNESCO World Heritage-listed Beguinage, is 30 minutes' drive away.

De Haan is less than 25 minutes away by car. The hotel offers a wellness area with a sauna and solarium available.

For workouts, the hotel offers an on-site fitness room that comes with cardio equipment and weights.

The younger guests at Ostend Hotel can run wild at a large play area with a trampoline and a bouncy castle.

They can also have a go at billiards and table football. Every morning a self-service breakfast is available in the hotel’s restaurant.

Here, guests can enjoy a 4-course meal for dinner. The Safari Bar serves a wide range of cocktails.
