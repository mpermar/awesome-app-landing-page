Description

Set on the beach promenade in Ostend, a few steps away from the North Sea and the sandy white beaches, Vayamundo Oostende offers modern guest rooms with free WiFi, an on-site restaurant, an indoor swimming pool and a wellness area.

Rooms come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV and a balcony. At Vayamundo Oostende, each room includes an en-suite bathroom with a bath or a shower.

An extensive breakfast buffet is available every morning. The Vayamundo also comprises two brasseries.

Vending machines for drinks and snacks are provided as well. Guests can rent a bicycle to explore the surroundings or take a plunge in the indoor swimming pool.

You can relax in the wellness area which comes with a sauna and a hot tub, or work out in the fitness centre.

The nearest coast tram stop is 50 metres from the hotel and connects to De Panne, Knokke and all other Belgian coastal cities.

Ostend-Bruges International Airport is 1. 1 km and the city centre of Ostend with the Train Station and Casino is 4.
