Description

Hotel De Hofkamers offers Boutique-style rooms and free Wi-Fi only 200 metres from the beach in the centre of Oostende.

It features a 24-hour front desk and bicycle rental service. The rooms at De Hofkamers include a flat-screen satellite TV, seating area and a work desk.

The bathrooms either have a walk-in shower or large bath tub. Each room also includes tea and coffee making facilities.

Guests can enjoy an extensive buffet breakfast daily which includes scrambled eggs, bacon and a large variety of breads.

There are also Danish pastries, cakes and fresh fruit salad. Oostende Railway Station is a 15-minute walk from De Hofkamers Hotel.

The centre of Bruges is 30 minutes’ drive away. Ostend-Bruges International Airport is less than 10 minutes away by car.
