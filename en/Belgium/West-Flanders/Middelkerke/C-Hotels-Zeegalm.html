Description

Peacefully located in the dunes between Middelkerke and Westende at the North Sea, C-Hotels Zeegalm offers private rooms with a sun terrace, as well as a children's playground and a garden.

In July and August, guests can enjoy free access to the outdoor swimming pool. The functional accommodations come with a balcony.

The private bathroom is fitted with a bath and/or a shower. At C-Hotels Zeegalm, a buffet-style breakfast is available every morning.

The nearest restaurants, bars and cafés are within an easy walking distance. A footpath through the dunes connects C-Hotels Zeegalm to the North Sea Beach in a 5-minute walk.

There is a coast tram stop within less than 500 metres. From here, you can travel to all the other Belgian seaside resorts as well as Plopsaland Amusement Park.

Nieuwpoort is 7 km away. Sights including the North Sea Aquarium and Plopsaland are 30-minutes' drive away.

The centre of Bruges is 40 minutes away by car. Medieval Bruges is a 30-minute drive away.

C-Hotels Zeegalm comprises free private parking for bicycles and cars. For a charge, guests can rent a secured garage.
