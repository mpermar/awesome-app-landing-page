Description

Featuring free Wi-Fi, B&B Vis-à-Vis is 550 metres from De Haan Beach. A large sun terrace at the rear has seating and parasols.

Decorated in light colours, all the accommodation includes a flat-screen TV, sofa and dining area.

The studios also have a kitchen area with fridge, microwave and kitchenware. A daily continental breakfast is available and can be taken in the room, lounge or on the terrace.

There is a supermarket 5 minutes’ walk away and a few restaurants within a 10-minute walk.

Every Thursday there is a market in the main Markt square, a 3-minute walk away. Bicycles can be rented and there are cycling routes along the coast and towards the countryside.

Set in a residential area, Vis-à-Vis B&B is 5 km from Blankenberge and 16 km from Bruges.
