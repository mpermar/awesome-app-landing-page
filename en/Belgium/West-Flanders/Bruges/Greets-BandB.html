Description

Set within 3 km from the centre of Bruges, Greets B&B offers its guests a garden with a terrace, free Wi-Fi access and free-of-charge use of bicycles to explore the area around the property.

Free parking facilities are provided. Each room is equipped with a minibar, hardwood floors and a dressing room.

There is a microwave, a refrigerator and coffee and tea facilities. Featuring garden view, the rooms come with a radio and a private bathroom with a bath and free toiletries.

Greets B&B serves a freshly prepared breakfast every morning. Upon request, breakfast can be taken in your room.

The nearest supermarkets and restaurants are within an easy walking distance of 250 to 500 metres.

From Greets B&B, it is a 7-minute drive to ‘T Zand, Steenstraat Shopping Street and the Market Square with the Belfry.

Bruges Railway Station is 2. 3 km and Boudewijn Seapark Amusement Park is 650 metres away.
