Description

Novotel Charleroi Centre is located in the city centre of Charleroi and connected to the Rive Gauche shopping mall.

The train station Charleroi-South is across the river Sambre and just a 5-minute walk from the hotel.

All hotel rooms feature free WiFi. Guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV.

With a private bathroom fitted with a bath or a shower and free toiletries, rooms at Novotel Charleroi Centre also offer a city view.

At the accommodation each room has a seating area. A continental or buffet breakfast can be enjoyed in the breakfast area.

There is an on-site restaurant, which specialises in European and local cuisine. A business centre and vending machines with drinks and snacks are available on site at Novotel Charleroi Centre.

Staff are available to help at the 24-hour front desk. Spiroudome is 1. 8 km from the hotel, while Université Libre De Bruxelles / Campus De Parentville - Charleroi is 3.
