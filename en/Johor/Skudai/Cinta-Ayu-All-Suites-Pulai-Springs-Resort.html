Description

Each air-conditioned suite is equipped with a satellite TV, personal safe and coffee/tea making facilities. Private bathrooms come with shower facilities.

Guests can play golf at the two 18-hole championship golf courses, The Melana Course and The Pulai Course. There is a tennis court and gym. Massages are available at the Pulai Spa.

International, Western, Chinese, Indian and local cuisines are served at the hotel’s 2 restaurants.

Cinta Ayu All Suites is about 7 miles from Sultan Ismail International Airport. It is a 30-minute drive from Singapore’s Tuas checkpoint.
