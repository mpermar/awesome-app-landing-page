Description

The hotel is a 10-minute drive from Alor Setar Tower and Kedah Museum. It's a 15-minute drive from Pekan Rabu shopping center and a 20-minute drive from the Kedah Paddy Museum.

Modern rooms are air-conditioned, and feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, an electric kettle and a personal safe. The private bathroom comes with a shower, toiletries and a hairdryer.

Tepi Bendang Restaurant serves both local specialties and Western favorites, and provides room service.

The staff at the 24-hour front desk can assist with use of meeting/banquet facilities, luggage storage or airport shuttle services. Complimentary parking spaces are available, though reservation is required.
