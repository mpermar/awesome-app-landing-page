Description

All guest rooms are air-conditioned and feature a satellite TV. A safe and a mini-bar are also available. Certain rooms have a balcony and pool view. The private bathroom comes with a shower and free toiletries.

Laguna Restaurant serves local and international dishes, while drinks can be enjoyed at Pool Bar. Many dining options offering Malaysian cuisine are located a 3-minute walk away.

Guests can pamper themselves at at the spa or exercise at the gym. Families can play in the large swimming pool, complete with water slides and fountains. Airport transfers can be arranged at an additional charge and there is a souvenir shop on-site.

Underwater World Langkawi is 2,800 feet from Aseania Resort Langkawi and Cenang Mall is 0.9 mi from the resort. Langkawi International Airport is a 6.2 mi drive.
