Description

Set in Kuala Lumpur, KLCC Serviced Suites offers an outdoor pool. Petronas Twin Towers is 600 metres from the property.

Free WiFi is offered throughout the apartment. All units are air-conditioned and include a seating and dining area.

A flat-screen TV with internet streaming channels and movies are available in all units.

There is also a kitchen, equipped with an oven, microwave and toaster. A fridge and stovetop are also provided, as well as a kettle.

There is a private bathroom with a bath or shower and bidet in every unit. Towels are provided.

Suria KLCC is 600 metres from KLCC Vortex Serviced Suites, while Aquaria KLCC is 700 metres away.

Sultan Abdul Aziz Shah Airport is 30 km from KLCC Vortex Serviced Suites, while Kuala Lumpur International Airport is 57 km away.
