Description

Featuring elegant furnishings, each room is air-conditioned. It comes with a flat-screen TV and a home entertainment system. The private bathroom includes a shower and free toiletries. Selected rooms have access to the Premier Lounge.

Melting Pot Cafe offers Asian and continental specialties, while Chinese dishes are served at Xin Cuisine. Fresh pastries are available at Xroads Xpress. Hard Rock Cafe serves burgers and steaks. Cocktails can be enjoyed at Pool Terrace and Havana Club.

Guests can go to the shopping arcade or relax by the poolside. Secretarial services and travel arrangements can be done in the business center. Other facilities include currency exchange and room service.

Bukit Nanas Monorail station is 550 feet from Concorde Hotel Kuala Lumpur and Petronas Twin Towers is 0.6 mi away. Sultan Abdul Aziz Shah Airport is 14 mi from the property.
