Description

Featuring pool views, THE ROBERTSON BY JOSEPH'S SUITES in Kuala Lumpur features accommodation, a restaurant, an outdoor swimming pool, a fitness centre, a shared lounge and a garden.

The aparthotel features both WiFi and private parking free of charge. Each unit features a terrace, a fully equipped kitchen with a microwave, a seating area with a sofa, a flat-screen TV, a washing machine, and a private bathroom with bidet and a hairdryer.

A fridge, a stovetop and toaster are also available, as well as a kettle. Continental and buffet breakfast options are available each morning at THE ROBERTSON BY JOSEPH'S SUITES.

The accommodation offers a children's playground. Both a bicycle rental service and a car rental service are available at THE ROBERTSON BY JOSEPH'S SUITES.

Berjaya Times Square is 1. 4 km from the aparthotel, while Kuala Lumpur Convention Center is 2.

4 km from the property. The nearest airport is Sultan Abdul Aziz Shah, 30 km from THE ROBERTSON BY JOSEPH'S SUITES, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
