Description

Offering city views, Hyatt House Kuala Lumpur, Mont Kiara in Kuala Lumpur offers accommodation, a restaurant, an outdoor swimming pool, a fitness centre, a bar and a shared lounge.

Complimentary WiFi is featured throughout the property. Every unit features a private bathroom and shower, air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and a microwave.

A buffet breakfast is available every morning at the aparthotel. Guests can enjoy playing billiards, or take advantage of the business centre.

A car rental service is available at Arcoris. Federal Territory Mosque is 3. 5 km from the accommodation, while Putra World Trade Centre is 7 km away.

The nearest airport is Sultan Abdul Aziz Shah, 22 km from Hyatt House Kuala Lumpur, Mont Kiara, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
