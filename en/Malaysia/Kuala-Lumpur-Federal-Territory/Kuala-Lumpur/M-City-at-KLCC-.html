Description

Accommodations are fitted with air conditioning and features a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fully equipped kitchen with a dining area, and a private bathroom with free toiletries. Some units include a seating area and/or a balcony.

The restaurant at the apartment serves local and South african cuisine.

Guests can use the hot tub and sauna.

Guests can swim in the outdoor swimming pool or relax in the garden.

Kuala Lumpur Convention Center is 2.4 miles from M City at KLCC., while Aquaria KLCC is 2.4 miles away. The nearest airport is Sultan Abdul Aziz Shah Airport, 14 miles from the accommodation.
