Description

Set in Kuala Lumpur, 300 metres from Suria KLCC and 400 metres from Aquaria KLCC, Getaway Home Suite KLCC offers accommodation with free WiFi, a garden with an outdoor swimming pool, pool views, and access to a fitness room and a sauna.

Private parking is available on site. All units here are air-conditioned and feature a flat-screen TV, a living room with a sofa, a well-equipped kitchen with a dining area, and a private bathroom with hot tub, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

A microwave, a fridge and stovetop are also featured, as well as a kettle. The apartment offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

Getaway Home Suite KLCC offers a children's playground. A water park and a kids' club are available at the accommodation, while guests can also relax on the sun terrace.

Popular points of interest near Getaway Home Suite KLCC include Kuala Lumpur Convention Center, Petronas Twin Towers and Petrosains, The Discovery Centre.
