Description

Accommodations are comprised of a living room, a kitchen with an oven and a microwave and a private bathroom with hot tub and bath. A fridge and electric tea pot are also offered.

Breakfast is available every morning, and includes buffet and Asian options. There's an on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of Asian dishes and also offers vegetarian options.

Guests can use the fitness center.

The apartment has an outdoor swimming pool and a terrace.

Starhill Gallery is a 9-minute walk from KL holiday suite @ times square, while The Weld is 0.6 miles from the property. Sultan Abdul Aziz Shah Airport is 11 miles away.
