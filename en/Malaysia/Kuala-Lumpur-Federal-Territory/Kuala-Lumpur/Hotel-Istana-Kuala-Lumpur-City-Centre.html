Description

Set 500 metres from the lively Bukit Bintang area, Hotel Istana Kuala Lumpur City Centre offers elegant accommodation with free WiFi throughout.

The property houses 5 dining options and an outdoor swimming pool. Free parking is provided.

Featuring large windows overlooking the city skyline, every unit is air-conditioned and comes with a flat-screen TV.

The spacious suites are equipped with a seating area and some units have Club Lounge access.

The en suite bathroom includes a bath and bath amenities. Fine dining Urban Restaurant specialises in modern European cuisine.

Taman Sari Brasserie serves buffet and a la carte Malaysian dishes. Drinks can be enjoyed at Sports Bar and Songket Lounge.

Fresh pastries and cakes are offered at The Sweet Corner. Guests can choose between swimming, play a game of tennis or exercise at the gym.

Friendly staff at the 24-hour front desk are happy to assist you with luggage storage and room service.

Laundry and car hire are available at a surcharge. Petronas Twin Towers is 1. 5 km from Hotel Istana Kuala Lumpur City Centre, while Raja Chulan Monorail station is a 190-metre walk away.
