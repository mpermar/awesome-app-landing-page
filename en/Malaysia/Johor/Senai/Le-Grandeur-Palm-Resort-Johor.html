Description

Every unit is fitted with a private balcony overlooking the golf course, the pool or the garden. Air conditioning and a cable TV are also included. The private bathroom has a shower and free toiletries. Certain unit comes with a bath.

Buffet and √° la carte meals can be enjoyed at Ishwara Asian Brasserie & Terrace. The Grill at Cocomo serves cakes and specialty teas. Other dining options include Chinese and Japanese restaurants. Drinks are available at The Bar and Turquoise Poolside Bar.

Guests can experience indoor activities such as bowling, billiards and mah-jongg. You can also play various sports at the large lawn court. Adults can pamper themselves in the spa while the kids play in the playground. There is a gift shop on site and staff at the reception can assist you with shuttle service at a surcharge.

Le Grandeur Palm Resort Johor is 6.2 mi from AEON Mall Kulaijaya and is 6.8 mi from Johor Premium Outlets. Senai International Airport is 1.2 mi from the property.
