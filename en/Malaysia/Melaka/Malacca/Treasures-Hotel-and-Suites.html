Description

All guest rooms at the hotel come with a seating area. Each room is equipped with a private bathroom with free toiletries. At Treasures Hotel and Suites rooms include a desk and a flat-screen TV.

Breakfast is available each morning, and includes buffet and à la carte options.

The accommodation has an outdoor pool.

For any tips on how to get around or what to do in the area, guests can ask at the reception.

Popular points of interest near Treasures Hotel and Suites include Porta de Santiago, St John's Fort and St. Peter's Church. Kuala Lumpur International Airport is 54 miles from the property.
