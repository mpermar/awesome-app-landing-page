Description

Located in Melaka, 3. 2 km from Cheng Hoon Teng Temple and 3. 6 km from Jonker Street, Icon Stay Parkland Residence Sungai Melaka Melaka provides accommodation with free WiFi, a garden with an outdoor swimming pool, pool views, and access to a fitness room and a sauna.

All units here are air-conditioned and feature a flat-screen TV, a living room with a sofa, a well-equipped kitchen with a dining area, and a private bathroom with shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

There is also a microwave, fridge and a kettle. The apartment offers a terrace. A water park and a children's playground are available for guests at Icon Stay Parkland Residence Sungai Melaka Melaka.

Baba & Nyonya Heritage Museum is 3. 7 km from the accommodation, while Straits Chinese Jewelry Museum Malacca is 3.

9 km away. The nearest airport is Melaka International Airport, 10 km from Icon Stay Parkland Residence Sungai Melaka Melaka.
