Description

Located in the heart of Melaka, MITC Hotel offers stylish yet comfortable accommodation just 7 km from Melaka Sentral.

The property houses an on-site restaurant where guests can enjoy an array of delicacies.

The property features a welcoming outdoor pool and a terrace for guests' relaxation.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. Each well-appointed room comes decorated in warm colours and lighting.

Each room comes with carpeted flooring and black-out curtains. The rooms feature a flat-screen TV and an electric kettle for tea or coffee-making.

Some rooms have seating areas. All rooms are fitted with a shower facility in the en suite bathroom.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. Guests who arrive by car can benefit from the free public parking on-site.

Night Zoo Melaka is located within a 2. 6 km radius of the property. Sam Po Kong Temple is 9 km from the property.
