Description

Steps from Jonker Street and St. Pauls Hill, Ramada Plaza Melaka features a fitness centre, an outdoor pool and squash courts.

Its spacious rooms offer flat-screen cable TVs and city views. Ramada Plaza Melaka is within 2 km from Melaka Town.

Famous landmarks AFamosa and Christ Church are just 1 km away. Rooms at Ramada Plaza Melaka feature classic wood furnishings and pleasant neutral shades.

They come fitted with minibars and tea/coffee makers. Safety deposit boxes and ironing facilities are included.

High-speed internet is available at an additional charge. The en suite bathrooms come with slippers and free toiletries.

Indulging beauty treatments can be enjoyed at the hotels beauty salon. Staff can assist guests with business and travel needs.

Friendly staff are fluent in English, Chinese and Malay. Dining options include Long Feng Chinese Restaurant and Olio Italian Restaurant.

Award-winning Zest Restaurant features an open kitchen serving international cuisine.
