Description

Elegantly furnished, the air-conditioned rooms come with carpet flooring and a flat-screen satellite TV. For your comfort, the private bathroom comes with a shower and free toiletries.

At Chatterbox Coffeehouse, guests can enjoy daily buffet breakfast with Asian and International options. Lotus Court Chinese Restaurant specializes in Chinese cuisines while The Bakery offers pastries and cakes.

Guests can exercise in the gym or have a pampering treatments in the health center, which include massage and sauna. Friendly staff at the 24/7 reception can assist you with laundry service at an additional charge. For your convenience, daily newspaper and housekeeping is provided for free.

Miri Waterfront is 2,300 feet from Mega Hotel, while Miri City Fan Park is 0.6 mi from the property. Miri International Airport is within a 6.2 mi drive away.
