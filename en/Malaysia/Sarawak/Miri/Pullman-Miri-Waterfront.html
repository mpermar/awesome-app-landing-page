Description

Boasting an infinity outdoor pool, a fitness centre and 5 dining options, Pullman Miri Waterfront overlooks the panoramic views of Baong River in Miri in Sarawak Region.

Bintang Mega Mall is 2. 9 km from the property. Free WiFi is accessible throughout and free parking is provided for guests who drive.

Decorated in grey and blue furnishings, every room is fitted with a TV, an electric kettle and a personal safe.

The en suite bathroom comes with bath and shower facilities. Guests enjoy sea or city views from the room.

Ironing facilities and a hairdryer are also provided. Pullman Kitchen is open for all day dining and serves daily buffet breakfast, while Tree Tops Terrace and Le Cafe serve light meals as well as afternoon tea.

Dietary request can be accommodated and room service is available upon request. You can enjoy a pampering massage at the spa or relax in the sauna.

Outdoor activities such as golfing, cycling and hiking can be arranged at the 24/7 reception.

Shuttle services, bike rentals and airport transfers are provided at an additional charge.

Miri City Fan Recreation Park and Miri Stadium are 2 km from Pullman Miri Waterfront.
