Description

Decorated in earthy tones, the spacious air-conditioned rooms and suites are fitted with a flat- screen TV. Selected units feature a seating area and club access. Premium bath amenities and a hairdryer are provided in the private bathroom.

Buffet breakfast and a la carte menu are served at Cinnamon Coffee House, while Zuan Yuan Chinese Restaurant offers fine dining. Drinks and light meals can be enjoyed at Poolbar and Grill as well as The Sphere Lounge.

You can relax by the poolside or play a game of tennis. A range of treatments are offered at the spa and the gym has separate sauna rooms for men and women. Staff at the 24-hour reception are happy to assist you with any inquiries. There is a scheduled shuttle service to Kidzania and Sunway Lagoon.

One World Hotel is 2.2 mi from Bandar Utama Golf Course and 3.5 mi from Kelab Golf Perkhidmatan Awam. Kuala Lumpur is 8.2 mi away and Kuala Lumpur International Airport is 35 mi from the property

Bandar Utama is a great choice for travelers interested in luxury brand shopping, culturally diverse food and clothes shopping.
