Description

Located in the heart of i-City, i-City I-SOHO offers accommodations in Shah Alam, 4 km from Shah Alam Convention Center.

It features an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre. Free private parking is available on site.

This fully-furnished two-bedroom unit is has 24-hour security. The living room is fitted with a flat-screen TV and a minibar.

Rooms opens up to views of the city. En suite bathrooms include shower facilities.

Bottled water are coffee are provided. Other facilities at the property includes barbecue facilities and a children's pool.

You will find shops at the property. Alternatively, guests can explore the surrounding area for a variety of dining and shopping options.

The homestay also offers car hire. i-City Theme Park is 230 metres from the property.

Stadium Malawati is 7 km from i-City I-SOHO, while Hospital Shah Alam is 800 metres from the property.
