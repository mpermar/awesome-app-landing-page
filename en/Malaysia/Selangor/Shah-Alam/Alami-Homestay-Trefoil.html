Description

Alami Homestay Trefoil is located in Shah Alam in the Selangor Region, 8 km from Shah Alam Convention Center.

The accommodation is air conditioned and is fitted with a sauna. Setia City Convention Centre and Setia City Shopping Mall are only around 100 metres from the property.

Free private parking is available on site. The accommodation features a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Some units include a seating area and/or balcony. There is also a dining area and a kitchen equipped with a toaster and fridge.

There is a private bathroom with slippers in each unit. Towels and bed linen are available.

Alami Homestay Trefoil also includes an outdoor pool. Guests can enjoy a barbecue and a restaurant on site.

You can play tennis at the property, and car hire is available. Hospital Shah Alam is 6 km from Alami Homestay Trefoil.
