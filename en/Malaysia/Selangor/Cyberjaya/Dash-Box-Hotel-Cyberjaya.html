Description

It is 1.7 mi to DPULZE Shopping Center and Shaftsbury Square. Prima Avenue Sport and Recreation Center is is 1.4 mi from Dash Box Hotel Cyberjaya. Kuala Lumpur International Airport is 16 mi away.

At the hotel, all rooms are equipped with a wardrobe. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom, the units at Dash Box Hotel Cyberjaya are equipped with air conditioning, and selected rooms also offer a balcony.

The daily breakfast offers continental, Full English/Irish or American options.

Other facilities at the property include a fitness center, a kids' club and an electric vehicle charging station. Luggage storage is available upon request.
