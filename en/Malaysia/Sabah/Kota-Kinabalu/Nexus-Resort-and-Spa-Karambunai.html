Description

Boasting 3 outdoor pools, 8 dining options and a golf course, Nexus Resort & Spa offers relaxing stays in Karambunai, 30 km from Kota Kinabalu.

Free internet is available throughout and free parking is provided for guests who drive.

The spacious room is tastefully decorated and air-conditioned. It features a flat-screen satellite TV and a private balcony overlooking the sea, the garden or the golf course.

The en suite bathroom comes with a hairdryer and a bathub. Daily buffet breakfast is served at Penyu Restaurant, which also offers all-day dining alongside Kingfisher Restaurant.

Light meals and drinks can be enjoyed at the poolside bar Splashes as well as the semi-outdoor Sunset Bar & Grill.

You can relax with a soothing massage at Borneo Spa or play a friendly game at the 18-hole golf course.

The little ones can also play at the kids' club and the kids' pool. Staff at the 24/7 reception can assist you with outdoor activities such as cycling and horse riding.

Lagoon Park is 4. 2 km away and free hourly shuttle is available. Nexus Resort & Spa Karambunai is 36 km from Kota Kinabalu International Airport.
