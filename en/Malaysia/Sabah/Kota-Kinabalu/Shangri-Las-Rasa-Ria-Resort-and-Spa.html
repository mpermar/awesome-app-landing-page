Description

Set amidst a nature reserve overlooking the South China Sea, Shangri-La’s Rasa Ria Resort & Spa features relaxing stays in elegantly furnished guest rooms.

It boasts an outdoor swimming pool, a golf course and 9 dining options. Free private parking is provided and free WiFi is accessible throughout.

Decorated in earthy tones, every room is tastefully fitted with a flat-screen TV, a minibar and coffee/tea making facilities.

Guests enjoy views of the garden or sea from the private balcony. For your comfort, luxury Shangri-La toiletries and bathrobes are included.

Offering a vast selection of international cuisines, such as Japanese, Italian, Malaysian and Asian favourites at the award-winning restaurants, this resort also offers a beachside bar and a music lounge for a relaxing drink.

Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for tour arrangements and outdoor activities, such as horse riding and jungle trekking.

Alternatively, guests can indulge in a variety of spa treatments at the in-house spa.

Kids can play in the children's club and babysitting service is available at an additional charge.

Other facilities on site include shuttle service and airport transfers. Kota Kinabalu is 35 km from Shangri-La's Rasa Ria Resort & Spa, while Kinabalu Mountain is 72 km away.

The nearest airport is Kota Kinabalu International Airport, a 40 km drive from the property.
