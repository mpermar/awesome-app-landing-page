Description

Boasting 6 dining options, an exclusive Chi, The Spa and an outdoor pool with water jets, Shangri-La's Tanjung Aru Resort & Spa offers luxury beachfront accommodation in Tanjung Aru.

Set 7 km from Kota Kinabalu, it features free WiFi throughout and free parking is provided.

Featuring elegant decor, the spacious rooms are fitted with air-conditioning, a seating area and a flat-screen satellite TV.

Stunning ocean or mountain views can be enjoyed from private balconies with outdoor seating.

For your convenience, the en suite bathroom comes with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Dining options include Shang Palace, which specialises in Chinese cuisines. Live entertainment accompanies light refreshments at Borneo Lounge and Bar.

Guests can enjoy various water sports activities, such as jet ski, surfing and diving.

Children can play in the kids' pool with waterslides. Other amenities include a 9-hole golf course and Chi, The Spa, which offers relaxing massages.

The staff at 24/7 reception are happy to assist you with car rentals and airport transfers at ab additional charge.

The nearest airport is Kota Kinabalu International Airport, 7. 3 km from the property.
