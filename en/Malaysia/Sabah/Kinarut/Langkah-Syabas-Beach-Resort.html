Description

Step Syabas Resort is located in Kota Kinabalu, and offers beachfront accommodation.

The resort has 2 outdoor swimming pools, a mini golf green, a library and a restaurant.

Free Wi-Fi is available at all public areas. Rooms at Langkah Syabas Resort come with a private balcony and air-conditioning.

Standard amenities include a TV, fridge and tea/coffee maker. Various activities including island trips and kayaking can be arranged at the resort.
