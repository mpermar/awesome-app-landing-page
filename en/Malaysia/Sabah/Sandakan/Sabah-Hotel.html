Description

Perched on top of a hill, this resort is surrounded by the tropical rainforests of Borneo.

It features an outdoor swimming pool and guests can enjoy dining options at 4 of the in-house restaurants or have a drink at the bar.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. It is 1. 5 km to Sandakan Palace, while Harbour Mall Sandakan and Sandakan Liberation Monument are 1.

9 km away. Gentingmas Mall is 1. 7 km from the property. Sabah Hotel is 12 km from Sandakan Airport.

Modern air-conditioned rooms feature a TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

Coffee/tea making facilities and a minibar are included. Free newspapers are provided daily.

Guests of Sabah can play a game of tennis or squash or exercise in the fitness centre.

Alternatively, they can sing in the hotel’s karaoke room. Other facilities include a children’s playground and a tour desk.

Overlooking the pool, Plantation Café offers a rich selection of both local and international dishes.

The Ming Restaurant serves authentic Oriental cuisine and dim sum specialities. Amadeus Music Lounge features live performances.
