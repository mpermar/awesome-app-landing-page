Description

Comprising of two buildings connected by a skybridge, Holiday Inn Resort Penang offers accommodations along Batu Ferringhi.

It features an outdoor swimming pool and guests can enjoy meals from the in-house restaurant or have a drink at the bar.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property and there is free parking on site.

Located close to the vibrant night market, the resort is a 30-minute drive from UNESCO World Heritage Site of Georgetown.

Penang International Airport is a 45-minute drive away. Guests can choose a well-appointed room in the Beach Wing, located along the beach or at the Ferringhi Tower, just across the street.

The air-conditioned rooms offer spacious layouts and classic wood furnishings fitted with a satellite TV.

En suite bathrooms includes shower facilities, a hairdryer and free toiletries. Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for tour arrangements, currency exchange, concierge services and luggage storage.

Playlab, an interactive kids' club offers a Mac Station, arcade games and a Lego room.

Enjoy a fun-filled day in the sun with activities such as beach volleyball and parasailing.

Alternatively, guests can enjoy a variety of wellness treatments at the in-house child-friendly SPAcation.

The resort offers a wide spread of local and international dishes at Terrace Cafe, which features outdoor dining.
