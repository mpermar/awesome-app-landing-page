Description

Situated along Batu Ferringhi Beach, PARKROYAL Penang offers 4 dining options and an outdoor pool.

Rooms feature sea or hill views, with free internet access and 24-hour room service.

Free WiFi is available in all rooms. It is 350 metres to Feringghi Night Walk Bazaar, while Batu Feringghi Waterfall is 1.

6 km away. Tropical Spice Garden is 2. 6 km from the property. The hotel is 17 km from Georgetown and 30 km from Penang International Airport.

Rooms at PARKROYAL Penang are decorated in a modern and contemporary style with LED TV featuring local and cable channels.

The rooms come with in-room movies, tea & coffee making facilities, minibar, personal digital safe and DVD player upon request.

Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for currency exchange, tour arrangements, ticketing and concierge services.

Other facilities include Zab Lab gym center, Koko-Nut Kid's Club and Amazonia water theme park.

Alternatively, guests can indulge in a variety of facial and body treatments at St.

Gregory Spa. Guests can indulge in local cuisine at Cinnamon Asian Kitchen or at Gustoso for Italian fare overlooking the sea.

Guests are also able to unwind at Javana Lounge or Sunset Bar with a glass of drinks to the end of the day.
