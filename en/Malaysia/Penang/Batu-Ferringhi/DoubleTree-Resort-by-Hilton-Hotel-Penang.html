Description

Boasting a an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre, DoubleTree Resort by Hilton Hotel Penang offers scenic and comfortable accommodation in Penang.

Guests can enjoy meals at the in-house restaurant. Free private parking is available on-site.

The air-conditioned rooms are fitted with a safety deposit box, refrigerator, and electric kettle.

Some rooms offer a terrace, where guests can unwind after a long today. The private bathroom comes with free toiletries.

Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for currency exchange and luggage storage.

Tickets to TeddyVille Museum can be purchased at a discount from the front desk. An indoor play area is available for children and a kid-friendly buffet is offered at the property.

At DoubleTree Resort by Hilton Hotel Penang, you will find disabled-friendly facilities.

The UNESCO World Heritage Site of George Town is 10 km from DoubleTree Resort by Hilton Hotel Penang, while Tanjung Bungah is 3 km away.
