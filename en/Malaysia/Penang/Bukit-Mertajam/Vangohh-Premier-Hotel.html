Description

Guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a electric tea pot. Rooms come with air conditioning, and some rooms at Vangohh Premier Hotel have a balcony. All rooms have a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

A à la carte or American breakfast is served at the property.

At the accommodation guests are welcome to use a fitness room.

Batu Ferringhi is 19 miles from Vangohh Premier Hotel. The nearest airport is Penang International Airport, 16 miles from the property.
