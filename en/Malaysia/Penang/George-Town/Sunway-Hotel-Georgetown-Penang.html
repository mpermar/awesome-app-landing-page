Description

Sunway Hotel Georgetown Penang offers accommodations in the heart of Georgetown - a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

It features an outdoor swimming pool and guests can enjoy meals from the in-house restaurant.

Free WiFi is available in public areas. Nestled within the old colonial-style shop houses, Sunway Hotel Georgetown Penang is 750 metres from Komtar Shopping Mall.

It is 1. 2 km to Upside Down Museum and 1. 8 km to Sun Yat Sen Museum. Penang International Airport is 18.

2 km away. Airport transfers are available at additional charges. Spacious guestrooms at Sunway Hotel Georgetown Penang offer modern interiors.

In addition to a TV with satellite channels, a minibar and a safe are included. Other facilities at the property include a 24-hour fitness centre.

Guests can approach the 24-hour front desk for currency exchange, tour arrangements, concierge services and luggage storage.
