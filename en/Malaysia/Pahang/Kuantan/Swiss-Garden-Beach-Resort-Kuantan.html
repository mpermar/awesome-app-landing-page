Description

Swiss-Garden Beach Resort, Kuantan provides a free shuttle service to Kuantan Town, located a 15-minute drive away. Its convenient location offers easy access to many tourist attractions and batik souvenir shops.

Rooms at Swiss-Garden Beach Resort have private balconies with panoramic views of the South China Sea or tropical gardens. Facilities include a long bath. Select rooms feature a living area and spa tub.

Guests can relax on the beach or enjoy a game of pool.

Guests can meet friends and family over homemade pastries and hot beverages at Swiss Deli. In the evening, enjoy cocktails with live entertainment at Malibu Club. International and halal-certified meals are available.
