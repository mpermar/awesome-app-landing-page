Description

Via the expressway, the resort is a 20-minute drive from Seremban, the Negeri Sembilan state capital and a 1-hour drive from Kuala Lumpur.

Featuring wooden and stone floors, air-conditioned rooms come with day beds and an open-concept bathroom with bathtub and shower facilities. In addition to a TV and a minibar, room service is available.

Lexis, Port Dickson's Coffee House features all-day dining of both local and international favorites. The Lobby Bar serves a range of beverages including cocktails, fresh juice, and hot drinks.

Guests can relax in the sauna or take a dip in the hot tub. The young ones can swim in the childrenâ€™s pool. Other facilities include a business center and a karaoke room.
