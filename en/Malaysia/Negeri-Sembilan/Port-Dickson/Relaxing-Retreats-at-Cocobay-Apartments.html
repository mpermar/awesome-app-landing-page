Description

Offering a garden and outdoor pool, Relaxing Retreats at Cocobay is set in Port Dickson, 32 km from Seremban.

Sepang is 44 km away. Free WiFi is available throughout the property. Featuring a balcony, the accommodation comes with a dining area and seating area with a flat-screen TV.

All units have a kitchen fitted with an oven and toaster. A fridge and stovetop are also offered, as well as a kettle.

There is a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries in each unit. Towels and bed linen are provided.

Relaxing Retreats at Cocobay also includes a year-round outdoor pool. You can play tennis at the property, and the area is popular for cycling.

Pasir Panjang is 8 km from Relaxing Retreats at Cocobay. The nearest airport is Kuala Lumpur International Airport, 38 km from the property.
