Description

All rooms are feature modern interiors with a private balcony. It includes a flat-screen TV and a fridge. The bathroom is fitted with a bath and a bathrobe.

Sharks Bay features 7 outdoor pools, with plenty of space to relax around the terrace. Guests can keep up with their fitness routines using the high-tech gym facilities, followed by a soothing massage with views of the Red Sea.

The Red Sea coast can be explored with the guidance of the onsite professional diving center. Children will be kept entertained at the kid’s club, their shaded pool area and entertainment from the animation team.

The resort offers a variety of cuisines from 13 restaurant and bars. In the evenings, the Mahfouz and Il Rustico Restaurants serve delicious dishes, while the Sunset Bar features Red Sea views and exotic cocktails.
