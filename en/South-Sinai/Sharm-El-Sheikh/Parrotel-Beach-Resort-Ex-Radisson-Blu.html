Description

The 5-star Radisson Blu Resort, Sharm El Sheikh has rooms decorated with rich fabrics. Each one has a private balcony and satellite TV. Free Wi-Fi is available the lobby and the Marine Club rooms.

The resort has a fully equipped spa with saunas, a hot tub and expert massages available. Guests can also enjoy a swim in the resort’s grand outdoor pool, surrounded by palm trees. For younger guests, the resort features the Abracadabra Club with staff supervision.

A variety of fine dining is available at the resort’s many restaurants, including traditional Egyptian cuisine, up-scale Italian, fine seafood and wood oven pizza. Cocktails and coffees are served at the resort's full-service bars and cafes.

The property is 5 miles from Sharm El-Sheikh International Airport and Soho square and 10.5 miles from Naama Bay. A daily shuttle is available upon request.
