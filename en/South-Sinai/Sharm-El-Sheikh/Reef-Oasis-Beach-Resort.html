Description

Surrounded by the Sinai Mountains, Reef Oasis Beach Resort has spacious, air-conditioned rooms with a satellite TV. Each room is decorated in neutral colors and has modern furnishing.

Reef Oasis has 6 restaurants serving Italian and grilled dishes, among a wide range of specialties. There are 10 bars, some of which serves drinks during the day, and others have entertainment in the evenings.

Guests can relax on the lounge chairs by the pools, or use the fitness center. Younger guests can visit the Nemo’s Kids Club. Reef’s spa offers range of spa facilities, including a hot tub, a sauna, and massage treatments.

Sharm el-Sheik International Airport is 8.5 miles away, and the resort has free parking on site. A free shuttle bus to Na'ama Bay is also available.
