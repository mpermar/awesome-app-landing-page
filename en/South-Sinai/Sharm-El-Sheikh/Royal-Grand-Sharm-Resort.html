Description

The air-conditioned rooms at Royal Grand Sharm have satellite TV, a minibar, and a private bathroom. The rooms feature panoramic views of the pool, the landscaped grounds, or the Red Sea.

The Royal Grand has on-site water sports, such as diving and snorkeling. Guests can get a relaxing massage at the spa, which also features a Turkish bath, a sauna, and a fitness center.

Restaurant Caravanserai has a smart, casual atmosphere and its buffet-style menu serves local delicacies. Fresh salads and seafood specialties are served at the Gulf Stream, the poolside bar.

Royal Grand Sharm is a 10-minute walk from Il Mercato and Phantasia Land. Sharm el Sheikh’s center with its famous traditional bazaars is only a 15-minute drive from the hotel. Free private parking is available on site.
