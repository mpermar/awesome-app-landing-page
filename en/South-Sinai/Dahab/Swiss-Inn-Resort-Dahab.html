Description

The bright and airy rooms and suites at the Swiss Inn Resort Dahab have modern amenities, such as a private balcony and a spacious sitting area with satellite TV. Some rooms have views of the sea or landscaped grounds.

The El Khaima Restaurant and Terrace serves a rich buffet throughout the day, while the Locanda Restaurant and Bar serves traditional Italian dishes and international wines. Cocktails and mocktails prepared with fresh fruit are served at the Beach Bar.

Guests of the Swiss Inn Dahab can work out with cardio equipment at the gym, or relax with a soothing massage in the expansive spa. For children, the resort offers a kids’ club with a playground and professional supervision.

Swiss Inn Resort provides a shuttle service into the center of Dahab and airport transportation to Sharm El Sheikh International Airport can also be arranged. The resort also offers unique safaris across the Sinai by camel, horse, Jeep or quad bike.
