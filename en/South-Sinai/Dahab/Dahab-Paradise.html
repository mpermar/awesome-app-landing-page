Description

All rooms feature touches of traditional architecture including ornate furniture and arched doorways. All rooms are air conditioned and have a private balcony, some of which overlook the ocean.

Situated along Dahab coastline, the hotel has ample diving spots nearby. It features a PADI dive center which offers dive courses and can also organize diving excursions.

Dahab Paradise is a 10-minute drive from the middle of Dahab. The hotel can also organize day trips to Petra.
