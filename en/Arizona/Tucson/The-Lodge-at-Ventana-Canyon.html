Description

A seating area with flat-screen cable TV, spacious work desk, reading chair, leather sofa and dark wood furniture is featured in each suite at The Lodge at Ventana Canyon. A fully-equipped kitchen is included for convenience. A private balcony or terrace provides city or mountain views. Plush bathrobes and a telephone are offered in the private bathroom.

The Catalina Room is open daily for breakfast, lunch and dinner and features Southwestern-style fine cuisine and an eclectic wine list. The Ventana Bar and Grill serves comfort food in a casual pub setting daily for dinner and is also open Friday-Sunday for lunch. Room service is available.

An oversize outdoor pool, hot tub and sauna are provided for guest relaxation. A fitness center includes fitness classes and a personal trainer. The on-site spa offers massage, body treatments and beauty services. A business center is provided. Free parking is available for all guests at the pet-friendly Ventana Canyon Lodge.

Ventana Canyon Trailhead is 1.2 mi away. Shopping at La Encantada is 6.2 mi away. Tucson International Airport is 19 mi away from The Lodge at Ventana Canyon.
