Description

Rooms include free WiFi, a cable TV with a DVD player and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Children can play in the Spray Pool or on the playground and adults work out in the 24-hour fitness center. The Oasis Bar & Grille, the Desert Oasis Sheraton's poolside restaurant, serves appetizers, sandwiches and salads. Guests can also choose from a variety of refreshing beverages such as smoothies and wine.

Desert Botanical Gardens is 13 mi from Sheraton Desert Oasis, while WestWorld of Scottsdale is 1.9 mi from the property. The nearest airport is Scottsdale Airport, 1.9 mi from the property.
