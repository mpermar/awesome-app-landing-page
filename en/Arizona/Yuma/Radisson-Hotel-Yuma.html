Description

Free Wi-Fi, a 32-inch flat-screen TV, a mini-fridge and tea and coffee-making facilities are included in all guest rooms at Radisson Hotel Yuma. The spacious rooms are decorated in soft colors and include a sofa and a work desk.

On-site at Yuma Radisson Hotel, Market Wine Bar Bistro serves breakfast, lunch and dinner and features classic steak and seafood entrees. The bar lounge offers a daily happy hour with drink specialties.

Guests can enjoy a workout at the on-site fitness center or stop by the business center with fax and photocopying services. A guest laundromat and 24-hour front desk are also offered at this hotel.

Yuma Palms Shopping Center is one mile from the Radisson. Desert Hills Golf Course is a 10 minute drive away. The hotel offers a free shuttle service to Yuma International Airport, which is 4 miles away.
