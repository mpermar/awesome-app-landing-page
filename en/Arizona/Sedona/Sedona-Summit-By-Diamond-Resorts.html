Description

The Sedona Summit provides studios, as well as one and 2-bedroom suites. Guests can enjoy full kitchens or kitchenettes, in addition to cable TVs with DVD players.

Guests at the Summit Sedona can workout in the fitness center or go for a swim in the 4 outdoor heated pools and 6 hot bathtubs. The concierge staff can provide information about the best attractions in the area, such as Slide Rock State Park, where guests can spend the day swimming and hiking.

West is a great choice for travelers interested in mountains, restaurants and nature.
