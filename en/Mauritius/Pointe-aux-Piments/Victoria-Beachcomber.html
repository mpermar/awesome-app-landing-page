Description

Facing the sea, the bright, spacious rooms and apartments at the Victoria Beachcomber feature balconies or patios with views of the garden and Indian Ocean. Each has a private bathroom with bathrobes, slippers, and free toiletries.

Offering local and international cuisine, the 4 restaurants include the main restaurant Le Superbe, L'Horizon on the beach, the Mediterranean restaurant La Casa and the casual Morris Beef.

A wide range of activities for guests are available at the Beachcomber. Water-based activities include scuba diving and glass bottom boat tours. Land-based options such as tennis and beach volleyball are also on offer. Guests have privileged access to Mont Choisy Le Golf, an 18-hole golf course located 6.2 mi away.

For younger guests aged 3 to 12 years, the resort has a kids club located on the beach providing a comprehensive program of activities. A fully equipped baby care room is also available to parents.

The Beachcomber Le Victoria Hotel is 8.7 mi from Grand Baie. It is also 39 mi from Sir Seewoosagur Ramgoolam International Airport.
