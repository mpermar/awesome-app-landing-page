Description

Guests can relax on a lounger poolside, and there is a kid’s club for younger guests. Relaxing massage treatments are available in the spa, which also has a modern gym and sauna.

Water-skiing, water surfing, and snorkeling are available at the beach. Guests can also play a game of tennis. Trips to Iles aux Aigrettes nature reserve are offered, as well as glass-bottom boat and kayaking tours.

Beachcomber Shandrani Resort & Spa has 5 in-house restaurants. A variety of snacks and drinks, including pancakes, are served at the pool and beach bars.

The bright rooms and suites include a spacious sitting area with a sofa bed and satellite TV. Each has minibar and a modern bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer. Rooms have free Wi-Fi or wired internet.
