Description

At the resort, the rooms are equipped with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. C Palmar - All Inclusive has some units with sea views, and each room comes with a balcony. Guest rooms at the accommodation have air conditioning and a closet.

A continental breakfast is served each morning at the property.

Golfing and snorkeling are among the activities that guests of C Palmar - All Inclusive can find nearby.

Grand Bay is 19 miles from the resort, while Flic-en-Flac is 28 miles from the property.
