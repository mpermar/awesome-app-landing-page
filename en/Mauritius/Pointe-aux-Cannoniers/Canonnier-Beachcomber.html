Description

The air-conditioned rooms and apartments feature a bright décor. Each comes with a seating area including a satellite TV and a mini-bar. The modern bathrooms are stocked with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

The three restaurants include Le Navigator, which offers stunning views of the northern islands. Buffets are served at the open plan Le Frangipanier Restaurant while Italian cuisine can be enjoyed at La Serenata. The resort also has 2 bars and a weekly entertainment program is available.

Guests can relax on a cushioned lounger poolside or unwind at the spa, which offers 6 massage rooms perched in the branches of a bicentenary banyan tree. For younger guests aged 3 to 12 years, there is a kids club offering a comprehensive program of complimentary activities.

There are 3 flood-lit tennis courts, a volleyball court, and an archery field. Trips, including glass-bottom boat tours, pedal boating, and deep-sea fishing can be arranged by the staff at Canonnier. Bike rentals are also available. Guests have privileged access to four 18-hole par 72 golf courses.

Grand Bay is 3.1 mi away and the resort is 42 mi from Sir Seewoosagur Ramgoolam International Airport.
