Description

At the hotel, every room is equipped with a terrace. Each room includes a private bathroom, and certain accommodations at SeaSense Boutique Hotel have a balcony. The rooms at the accommodation include air conditioning and a desk.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property. Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of international dishes.

A variety of popular activities are available in the area around SeaSense Boutique Hotel, including snorkeling and cycling.

Staff at the reception can provide guidance on what to do in the area.

Grand Bay is 20 miles from the hotel.
