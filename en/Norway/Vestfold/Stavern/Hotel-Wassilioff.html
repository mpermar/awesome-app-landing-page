Description

This charming hotel is located close to Stavern's harbour and offers views of the Skagerrak strait.

Wi-Fi access is free in all rooms. Hotel Wassilioff’s light, airy guest rooms feature classic décor and furnishings.

All rooms at Wassilioff have either a bath or a shower. Guests can relax and browse the satellite TV channels.

Some rooms offer picturesque views of the sea. Hotel Wassilioff’s à la carte restaurant serves à la carte dishes with a focus on sea food.

Guests can relax with a drink in the piano bar by the lobby. Live music events are hosted at the nightclub during weekends.
