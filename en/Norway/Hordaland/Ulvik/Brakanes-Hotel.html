Description

Brakanes Hotel is located on the shores of the Hardanger Fjord in Ulvik. The hotel restaurant features a buffet and a la carte menu of local dishes.

Free WiFi is included. All rooms at Brakanes Hotel include a work desk and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Some rooms include a seating area and some have a balcony with fjord views. The restaurant offers views of the fjord, while the garden lies right next to the water.

Other facilities include a conservatory and a library lounge. An outdoor sauna and hot tub facing the fjord, and an indoor pool are also available at Brakanes Hotel.
