Description

Dating back to 1894, this traditional hillside hotel is just a 5-minute walk from Holmenkollen Ski Jump Tower.

Guests enjoy free access to the spa facilities and panoramic views over Oslo from the lobby.

Holmenkollen metro station is a 15-minute walk away. Each room has a flat-screen TV and private bathroom with shower and free toiletries.

Free WiFi is available. The renowned De Fem Stuer Restaurant offers dining in a unique and historic setting.

Casual bistro dining is offered at Scandic's Park Cafe. Drinks can be enjoyed by the fireplace in the bar.

Additional facilities at Scandic Holmenkollen Park in a fitness room and bicycles rentals.

The surrounding Nordmarka area is ideal for nature walks, skiing and hiking. Oslo city centre is 20-minutes away by metro.
