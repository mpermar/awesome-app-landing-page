Description

Featuring an outdoor Jacuzzi, this hotel lies in southern Nordmarka, between Tyri Fjord and Steins Fjord.

Guests enjoy free access to the fitness centre. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

All of Sundvolden Hotel’s rooms have a flat-screen TV with cable channels. A minibar is available upon request.

Each has a private bathroom with shower. Sundvolden Hotel’s spa offers massages and treatments.

Other leisure and relaxation options include a garden, a terrace and a playground for children.

Free private parking spaces are found next to Sundvolden Hotel, which is just 7 minutes’ drive from Tyri Fjord Golf Club.
