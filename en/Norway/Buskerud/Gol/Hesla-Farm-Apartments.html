Description

Hesla Farm Pensjonat is situated just 500 metres from the centre of Gol. Golsfjellet ski centre is 20 km away while Hemdal ski centre is 25 km away.

Free private parking is available on site. Hesla Farm Pensjonat is located in a historic building, where chambers have been converted into modern guest rooms.

The accommodation is equipped with a satellite flat-screen TV. Some units feature a dining area and/or terrace.

All units include a kitchen equipped with an oven and fridge. Hesla Farm Pensjonat also includes a terrace.

The property also offers packed lunches, free WiFi and a free shuttle service. The property has a ski pass sales point and ski equipment hire is available.

An array of activities are offered in the area, such as fishing, skiing and hiking.
