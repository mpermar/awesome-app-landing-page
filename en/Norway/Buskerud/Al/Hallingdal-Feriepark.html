Description

Located by Strandafjord in Hallingdal, this holiday park is just 2 km from Ål train station.

Ski slopes in Geilo are just 25 km away, while the site boasts its own beach area, a spa and petting zoo.

Each modern cottage at Hallingdal Feriepark comes with free Wi-Fi and a private bathroom.

They also feature a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher, a living area with a fireplace and an outside patio area.

A restaurant is located on site, and serves a variety of dishes with local and international inspiration.

The town of Ål has a selection of shops for buying groceries. Hallingdal Feriepark is just 30 km from the Hallingskarvet National Park, while on site guests can enjoy rock climbing and obstacle courses as well as rent boats.

Fagernes Airport is 75 minutes’ drive from the site. Guests enjoy a 50% discount on the entrance fee for the on-site Hurramegrundt activity house.
