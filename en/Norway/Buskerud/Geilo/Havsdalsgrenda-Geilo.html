Description

This property lies right by the foot of Hallingskarvet National Park, 4 km from central Geilo.

Ski lifts are just 50 metres away from the self-catering apartments. Free access to the gym, pool and sauna are included.

All of Havsdalsgrenda’s ski-to-door apartments feature a living room with flat-screen TV and fireplace.

The fully equipped kitchen includes a dishwasher. Guests can enjoy panoramic mountain views.

Guests arriving by car can make use of Havsdalsgrenda’s free indoor parking spaces.

Bikes and ski equipment can be rented on site. Staff is happy to help arrange ski lessons, guided hiking trips and other activities.

Just 200 metres away is Havsdalen's ski rental, ski school and a cafeteria. Guests can explore Hardangervidda National Park, about 30 minutes’ drive from Havsdalsgrenda.
