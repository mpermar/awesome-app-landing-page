Description

This hotel lies next to Kristiansand Zoo and Kardemomme By Amusement Park. It offers free WiFi, a garden with an outdoor pool and free access to a nearby gym.

Kristiansand city centre is a 15-minute drive away. All guest rooms at Scandic Sørlandet have a satellite TV and a work desk.

Many guest rooms overlook the surrounding garden. Scandic Sørlandet's restaurant offers large buffets as well as à la carte dishes.

The in-house Humørbrygga Bar serves a variety of drinks and snacks. Free tea and coffee is available in the lobby all day.

In summer, Scandic Sørlandet offers a kid's club with a large playroom, an outdoor playground and a kiddie pool.

The hotel also arranges evening entertainment in the summer months. A children’s buffet is served in the restaurant for breakfast and dinner.

The surrounding area offers activities such as hiking and fishing. Kristiansand Airport, Kjevik is a 10-minute drive away.
