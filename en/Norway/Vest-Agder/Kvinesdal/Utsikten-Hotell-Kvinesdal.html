Description

With natural serenity and breathtaking views, centrally situated between Kristiansand and Stavanger and within easy reach of South Norway's most attractive sceneries, is Utsikten Hotel Kvinesdal.

Free WiFi, a flat-screen TV and tea/coffee facilities are offered at Utsikten Hotel Kvinesdal's stylish rooms.

Some have a separate living area, kitchenette and balcony offering Fedafjord views.

The A La Carte Restaurant also boasts stunning views over the Fedafjord. In summertime, meals can be enjoyed on the terrace.

The menu offers everything from traditional Norwegian cuisine to special,locally produced delicacies.

The Utsikten Hotel Kvinesdal also benefits from having an award winning golf course right at its doorstep.
