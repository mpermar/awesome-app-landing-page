Description

Situated in Sauda town centre, Kløver hotel offers free Wi-Fi, free parking and rooms with a seating area, cable TV and minibar.

Svandalen Ski Centre is a 20-minute drive from the hotel. All guest rooms at Hotel Kløver have a private bathroom with bath or shower.

Each is equipped with a work desk and in-room ironing facilities. Guests can enjoy Chinese and Norwegian dishes at the on-site restaurant, featuring a rustic atmosphere with an open fireplace and exposed wooden beams.

Drinks can be enjoyed in the pub. Leisure facilities include a gym. Guests can rent a bicycle on site to explore the surroundings.

Ski storage is also available for guests’ convenience. Sauda Sports and Swimming Centre is 100 metres away.
