Description

Set next to Lake Bjørnheimsvatnet, this hostel is 7 km from the Tau Ferry Terminal and a 1-hour drive from Stavanger.

It offers free WiFi and rooms with a private bathroom. The simply furnished rooms at Hostel Ryfylke Vandrerhjem Vaulali all have a desk, a wardrobe and a bathroom with a shower.

Communal facilities include a kitchen, a lounge and a laundry room. Ryfylke Vandrerhjem Vaulali also has a large lawn with football and volleyball pitches.

The hostel provides canoe rental. There is a swimming area by the lake, a BBQ area and a children's playground.

The Lyse Fjord is 25 km away, while the starting point to the hiking path towards Pulpit Rock is 20 minutes’ drive from the hostel.
