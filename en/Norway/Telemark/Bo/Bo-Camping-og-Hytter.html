Description

Located by Bø Sommerland Water Park, this holiday park offers cottages and apartments with a kitchenette, TV and free Wi-Fi.

The train station in central Bø is 5 km away. Each cottage and apartment at Bø Camping og Hytter has a private patio.

Most options include a private bathroom with toilet and shower. Guests have access to shared laundry facilities.

A mini golf course and a beach volleyball field are found on site. The outdoor swimming pool is open during summer.

Høyt og Lavt rope adventure park offers an active experience next to Bø Camping. Skiing and hiking are popular activities in the area.

Basic groceries can be bought at the on-site kiosk. Hamburgers and other small dishes are served in the bistro during summer.

The Telemark Canal is 15 km from Bø Camping. Sandefjord Airport Torp is 1 hour 40 minutes’ drive away.
