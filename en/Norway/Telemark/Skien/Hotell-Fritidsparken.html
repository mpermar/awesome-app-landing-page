Description

Offering free access to an indoor water park and spa/wellness centre, Hotell Fritidsparken is located in Skien Leisure Park, 3 km from central Skien.

A light evening buffet is offered daily except Sundays. WiFi is free. The brightly decorated rooms at Hotell Fritidsparken include flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with shower, hairdryer and free toiletries.

Each room is equipped with a seating area and work desk. Some rooms have a fridge.

The wellness centre features a hot tub, saunas and aromatherapy showers. A range of activities are available on site, including climbing, tennis and curling.

Two cafés are also located on site. Parking is free on site. Porsgrunn city centre is a 15-minute drive away.
