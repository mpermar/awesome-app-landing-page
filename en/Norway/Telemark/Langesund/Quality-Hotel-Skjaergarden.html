Description

Deep in the heart of the scenic Langesund archipelago lies this unique hotel, which is beautifully situated by the sea and offers a multitude of recreational activities.

At Quality Hotel Skjærgården, families with children can take advantage of the special activity programme during each holiday season.

The area includes Norway's biggest play room, a 81-metre long slide, a children's farm during the summer and a children's buffet every weekend and school holiday.

Skjærgården also provides a 4000 m² swimming park for an extra fee, which includes a large swimming area connected to a heated outdoor pool, a hot tub, sauna and a steam sauna.
