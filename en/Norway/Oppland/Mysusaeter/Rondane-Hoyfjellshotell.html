Description

Rondane Høyfjellshotell is in Mysusæter, 3. 3 km from Rondane National Park and its marked hiking trails.

It offers a gym, sauna and pool access as well as a restaurant and bar. All room have a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

Rondane Høyfjellshotell's restaurant serves international cuisine. Guests can enjoy a drink at the bar or in the fireplace lounge.

Other facilities include a lounge with wide-screen TV and a games room. Ski equipment and bikes are available for rent on site.

Cross-country ski trails are right outside the door. Other activities in the area include rafting and canoeing on the lake.

Shops, restaurants and services are found in Otta, about 20 minutes’ drive from Rondane Høyfjellshotell.
