Description

This 5th-generation family-run hotel dates back to 1877 and is located in Hafjell Mountain, 17.

5 km from central Lillehammer. It offers a private 9-hole golf course. All guest rooms and apartments at Nermo Hotel have cable TV and private bathrooms with showers.

Guest rooms include free WiFi access. Apartments feature modern kitchens, and some have a private sauna.

Traditional dishes based mainly on local produce are served at the in-house restaurant.

In summer, guests can enjoy a barbecue buffet on the terrace. Leisure options include a sauna, table tennis set and a games room.

Activities and attractions include Hunderfossen Family Park and Hafjell Alpinecenter which can be reached in 5 minutes’ by car from the property.
