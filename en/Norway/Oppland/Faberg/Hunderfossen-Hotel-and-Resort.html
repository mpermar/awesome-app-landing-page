Description

Situated by the River Gudbrandsdalslågen, just 5 minutes’ drive from Hafjell Alpine Centre, this hotel offers free in-room WiFi and free private parking.

Lillehammer is about 15 minutes' drive away. All accommodation options at Hunderfossen Hotel & Resort have a private bathroom and satellite TV.

Some include their own fridge. The in-house restaurant, Fossekroa, is open on Friday and Saturday evenings from autumn until spring.

It has a large terrace and serves Norwegian cuisine based on local ingredients. Guests can relax in Hotel Hunderfossen’s fireplace lounge.

Other facilities include bookable private saunas, a playground and a playroom for children and a bar.

Nearby leisure options include golf, skiing and hiking. Hunderfossen Family Park and Hunderfossen Station are both 5 minutes' walk away.
