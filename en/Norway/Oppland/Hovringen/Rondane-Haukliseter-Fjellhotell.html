Description

With panoramic views over Høvringen and the surrounding scenery, this accommodation is set in the mountain landscapes 25 km from Otta.

Guests can enjoy free Wi-Fi, a sauna and easy access to cross-country skiing routes.

Dombås and its ski slopes are just 30 minutes’ drive from Rondane Haukliseter Fjellhotell.

Høvringen is known as the gateway to the Rondane National Park and there are many hiking paths in the area.

Rondane Haukliseter’s accommodation is basic and compact, all featuring a private bathroom.

Apartments and cottages come with kitchen facilities. A restaurant with seasonal opening times can be found at the Fjellhotell, and the closest grocery shop is 1 km away.
