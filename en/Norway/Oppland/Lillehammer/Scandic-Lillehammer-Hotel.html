Description

Featuring spa centre with a sauna and both indoor and outdoor pools, this hotel is only 900 metres from the Lillehammer Train Station.

A large private park surrounds the hotel and guests can dine at 2 restaurants on site.

Lillehammer Hotel’s decorated guest rooms all feature free WiFi, cable TV and a fridge.

The private bathrooms come with toiletries from FACE Stockholm and a hairdryer. International and local cuisine is served in Lillehammer Hotel’s 2 restaurants Salt & Pepper and Tre Diktere.

Guests can also enjoy the hotel bar and outdoor terrace. Other facilities include a large fitness centre, yoga room, children’s playroom, minigolf and a garden.

During the winter season, there is possibility to take a ski shuttle bus towards Hafjell ski centre, 20 km away.

Lysgårdsbakkene ski jump hill is 1. 3 km away, while Maihaugen Open-Air Museum is a 15-minute walk away.
