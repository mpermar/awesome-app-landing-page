Description

Offering Scandinavia’s only indoor surfing wave pool and glass climbing wall, Radisson Blu Resort, Trysil is situated in the Trysil Ski Resort.

It offers free WiFi, free parking and accommodation options with flat-screen TV. All guest rooms and apartments at Radisson Blu Trysil are designed with a rich colour palette and chalet-style furnishings.

Each is equipped with comfortable Jensen beds. The resort offers a bowling alley with 8 modern lanes.

A popular buffet breakfast is served every morning, while in the evening guests can choose from 8 restaurants and bars, including the 1960s-style Chill Bowl & Dine and the Italian-style La Piazza Bar & Restaurant.

The lifts to the ski slopes are within 5 minutes’ walk. Innbygda village centre is 2 km away.
