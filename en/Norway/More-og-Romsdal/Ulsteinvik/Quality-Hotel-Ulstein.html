Description

This seaside hotel is located in central Ulsteinvik. It offers an in-house restaurant, a private pier and modern rooms with a minibar, flat-screen TV and free WiFi access.

Sjøborg Culture House is located on site. A work desk and a private bathroom with shower are standard in all rooms at Quality Hotel Ulstein.

Some rooms have a balcony or terrace, and some offer sea views. Meals are served at Oljepiren Bar & Restaurant, which offers panoramic views of the sea.

Guests can also enjoy a refreshing drink in the bar. Leisure options include a gym, a children's playground and a mini golf course.

Bicycles can be rented on site. A cinema is found at the on-site culture house. A range of activities can be enjoyed in the surroundings, including fishing and hiking.

The property offers free parking. Ålesund city centre is a 1. 5-hour drive and ferry ride away.
