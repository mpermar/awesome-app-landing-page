Description

Rooms at Hotel De L’Isard feature practical design and plenty of natural light. They all have a private bathroom with amenities and a hairdryer.

The De L’Isard’s restaurant serves fresh, gourmet meals and light snacks. The dining room offers lovely views of the mountains. The hotel also has a lounge area with an open fireplace.

The De L’Isard Hotel is within a 5 minute walk of Andorra La Vellas’s tax-free shopping. The famous Caldea Spa is a 10 minute walk away, while the nearest ski resorts are within a 20 minute drive.
