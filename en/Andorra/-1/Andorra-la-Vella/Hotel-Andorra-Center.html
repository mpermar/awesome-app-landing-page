Description

Hotel Andorra Center is in the town’s main shopping district, just 350 feet from Andorra la Vella Conference Center. Andorra Bus Station is a 10-minute walk from the hotel. Secure parking is available on site.

Rooms at the Andorra Center have simple décor and hardwood floors. All rooms have central heating, satellite TV and a minibar. Bathrooms include a hairdryer and amenities.

Hotel Andorra Center has a restaurant, which serves buffet and à la carte meals in a formal setting. The hotel also has a relaxed coffee bar.
