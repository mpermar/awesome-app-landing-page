Description

This elegant hotel is located in the center of Andorra. It is decorated with tapestries by Salvador Dalí. The hotel is less than 5.6 mi from the Grandvalira ski resort. The Vallnord ski runs are 15 minutes away by car and the hotel offers ski storage. Free WiFi is offered throughout the property.

The spacious rooms feature air conditioning, heating, and a flat-screen TV. Tea and coffee making facilities and mini-bar are also provided. Some rooms come with a sofa bed. The bathroom comes with free toiletries, bath, and hairdryer.

The health center includes a sauna, heated indoor pool and gym. Massage services are available, for an extra charge.

The Holiday Inn offers regional and international cuisine at the buffet restaurant, El Gran Café. There is also a cafeteria.
