Description

Park Piolets MountainHotel & Spa is located in the Pyrenees, 1,000 feet from the ski slopes of Grandvalira-Soldeu and 1,950 feet from Vall d'Incles. Guests have free access to the gym, to the kids' club, and to a storage room for bicycles with tools and cleaning area. Free WiFi is offered throughout the property and free public parking is available on site.

Featuring mountain views, each room is spacious and comfortable. They come with a flat-screen TV, a mini-bar, and a safe. Rooms include a balcony or a terrace. The bathroom comes with free toiletries, bath or shower, and hairdryer.

The hotel's buffet restaurant serves international cuisine, 8 show cooking areas and offers views of the surrounding mountains. Guests can enjoy a drink in the lounge bar and make use of the hotel terrace.

Private parking is available for a surcharge and offers an electric vehicle charging station.

Hiking and skiing are popular in the surrounding area. The hotel has ski storage and a shop selling and renting ski equipment. Park Piolets MountainHotel & Spa is a 5-minute drive from El Tarter Ski Resort and 25 minutes’ drive from Pas de La Casa.
