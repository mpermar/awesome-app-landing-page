Description

One of our top picks in Soldeu.Sport Hotel Village is in Soldeu, right next to Grandvalira’s Ski Elevators. It offers a gym, a seasonal outdoor pool and a large children’s section with a soft play area and a ball pit.

The rooms at Sport Hotel Village have smart décor, a TV and a mini-bar. The private bathroom comes with a hairdryer.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning at the on-site restaurant, which organize buffet dinner with show cooking at nights.

Accessible via an underground hall, the Sport Wellness Mountain Spa includes pools of varying temperatures, a large pool with jets and 2 outdoor hydrotherapy baths with views of the slopes. This is available for an extra cost and for over 13s only.

Children have free access to a kids club offering a range of activities. The Sport Hotel Village has also an on-site ski guides and ski storage.
