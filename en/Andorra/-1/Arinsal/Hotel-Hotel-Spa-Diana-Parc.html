Description

Located in Arinsal, a 3-minute walk from Arinsal Ski Elevator, Hotel Spa Diana Parc features a spa and wellness area with a sauna, hammam, and hot tub.

All stylish rooms have heating, and free WiFi. They include a private bathroom with a spa bath, free toiletries, slippers, and hairdryer.

The property includes a bar, restaurant, and a bowling alley. In the summer the hotel offers escorted hiking walks and a free children's mini club.

Comunal de les Valls Nature Reserve is 1.6 mi away, and Andorra la Vella is 8.1 mi from the hotel.
