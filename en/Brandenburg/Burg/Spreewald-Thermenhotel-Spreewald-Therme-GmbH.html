Description

The spacious rooms at Spreewald Thermenhotel all feature a flat-screen TV, and have a private bathroom which includes a hairdryer and complimentary toiletries. A free bottle of water is provided each day.

Guests are welcome to relax in the spa area which includes 8 indoor pools, an outdoor pool, 7 saunas, a spa area and a steam bath. For an extra fee it is possible to use the tanning beds or book a massage.

A rich breakfast buffet is available each morning, and in addition to a restaurant the hotel also has a bar and lounge.

Spreewald Thermenhotel is 5 miles from Vetschau Train Station, and a 15-minute drive from the A15 highway.
