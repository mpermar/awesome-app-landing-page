Description

Guest House Linas is located in the centre of Alytus, only 150 metres from the Town Hall.

It offers rooms with a refrigerator, satellite TV and free Wi-Fi. Free private parking is available.

The rooms of the Linas feature a simple interior design. All rooms have a seating area and carpeted floors, and each has a private bathroom with a shower, as well as a fridge.

The property’s garden has a children’s playground with a trampoline and a designated barbecue area.

In the summer you can relax in the Linas’s outdoor pool surrounded by a flowery garden or find some shade in a gazebo.

There is also a sauna, and property offers beauty treatments. Breakfast is served upon request in the breakfast room.

Locked parking is available. The Nemunas River is within 400 metres, and the Alytaus Arena is a 10 minute walk away.
