Description

Regina, situated a 1-minute walk from the centre of Druskininkai and just 150 metres from the aqua park, offers free Wi-Fi in the public areas.

It features a terrace bar. All of the classically decorated rooms at Regina have a TV and a work desk.

Some rooms feature air-conditioning and balconies. The Snow Arena is 3. 5 km from the property.

Grutas Park is less than 15 minutes away by car. The restaurant at Regina serves European cuisine in the cosy dining room or outside on the terrace.

The hotel has a 24-hour front desk and there is free private parking available nearby.
