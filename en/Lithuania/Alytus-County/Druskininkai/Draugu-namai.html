Description

Friends House offers guest accommodation in the center of Druskininkai, 150 meters from Lake Druskonis.

It is unique for its room layout, and uses solar energy for water and room heating.

It has free Wi-Fi. Each ground floor room has its own, private entrance. Every room is decorated in a simple, modern, Scandinavian style.

All have a private bathroom with a shower and a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Guests can use free, private parking. Bicycle rental is possible at extra charge.

Draugų Namai is located 400 metres from an Aquapark and 900 metres from Druskininkai Bus Station.

Snow Arena, a whole-year skiing and snowboarding resort, is 3. 5 km from Draugų Namai.
