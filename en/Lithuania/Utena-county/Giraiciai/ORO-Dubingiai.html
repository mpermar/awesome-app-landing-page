Description

Situated in a woodland area 100 metres from the Lake Asveja, Dubingiai boasts a private beach.

Its guests can take advantage of a wide range of wellness and leisure facilities, such as a billiards room, a fitness centre or hot tubs.

Free Wi-Fi access is available. Dubingiai offers accommodation in classically decorated rooms and apartments, each of which comes with a TV and a private bathroom with a shower.

Some come with a well-equipped kitchenette. Towels and linen are provided. The resort provides such facilities as a tennis court, a bowling alley, and a complex of spa and steam baths with a heated pool.

Guests can also enjoy karaoke or a game of table tennis. The beach comes with a pier and a rental of boats and other beach equipment.

Dressing cabins are also available. The Dubingė restaurant specialises in traditional Lithuanian cuisine, but also offers a selection of European dishes, as well as snacks.

Picnic baskets can be provided. The restaurant comes with a large terrace and boasts a view of the old pine forest.

Dubingiai is 10 km from the national road number A14. The nearest store is within 3 km, and Vilnius is 45 km away.
