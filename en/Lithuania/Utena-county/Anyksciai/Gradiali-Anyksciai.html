Description

Situated in a hilly area on the shore of the Lake Rubikiai, Gradiali Anykščiai Holiday Park is located in Anykščiai.

Each room here will provide you with a TV and cable channels. Private bathrooms come with a shower and toiletries.

Extras include a desk and bed linen. Gradiali Anykščiai features a seasonal restaurant open in the summer.

The property offers free parking, and WiFi is available in the restaurant and public areas.
