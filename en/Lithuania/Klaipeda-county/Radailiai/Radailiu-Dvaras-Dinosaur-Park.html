Description

Radailiu Dvaras - Dinosaur Park is situated in Radailiai, a quiet town 7 km from Klaipeda and 12 km from the sea.

The hotel offers private parking. Radailių dvaras features the only Dinosaur Park in the Baltics - and the apartment guests can enter it at a discount.

The rooms feature elegant décor. All rooms feature air-conditioning, a flat-screen TV, an electric kettle, a refrigerator, and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

Some of the rooms have a terrace. Radailiu Dvaras - Dinosaur Park offers a football field, as well as basketball and volleyball courts.

Guests have access to barbecue facilities, while children can use the playground.

The apartment’s restaurant, overlooking old oaks and a pond, offers free WiFi and serves European cuisine.

Guests are also welcome to relax in the on-site restaurant. Palanga Inernational Airport is a 45-minute drive away, and Klaipeda International Ferry Terminal is a 30-minute drive from Radailiu Dvaras - Dinosaur Park.
