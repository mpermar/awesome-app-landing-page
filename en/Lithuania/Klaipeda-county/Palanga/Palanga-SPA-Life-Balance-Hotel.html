Description

Situated just 50 metres from the Baltic Sea, this family-owned 5-star hotel offers a luxurious spa centre and water treatments.

The Palanga Spa Life Balance Hotel was awarded the Best SPA Hotel in Eastern Europe in the World Spa and Wellness Awards 2018, the most innovative European SPA Hotel at ESPA awards 2018 and the Perfect Wellness Hotel by Perfect SPA 2018 awards.

Guests can choose their accommodation from 74 rooms which are different in categories and situated in two different buildings: elegant Luxury building and hype and modern Design building, which is the only hotel in the world that has by 7 degrees deviated floorings.

Both buildings have a lounge area with a fireplace. Luxury building has stylish interiors and elegant rooms.

All rooms are bright and decorated with teak-wooden furniture and Brazilian walnut doors and floors.

Each room has a balcony and is equipped with free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, mini-bar, flat-screen TV.

All rooms at Design building are spacious and have a private balcony. Every room is equipped with free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, mini-bar, flat-screen TV.

Located in the Design building, wellness center boasts of four different types of baths, including sauna, steam bath, laconium and outdoor wooden Kelo log bathhouse.

There are several swimming pools – indoor swimming, indoor hot tub, Roman pool and outdoor hot tub.

All pools use mineral water which is extracted from own mineral water bore. Guests can relax in larch therapy room with water beds.

Gym is available free of charge. There is also an outdoor swimming pool with pre-heated water available in the summertime.

Guests can choose from a wide range of beauty and medical treatments as well as individual programmes available upon request.

Rich breakfast and modern European dishes are served at the restaurant located in the Luxury building.

There is also a bar to enjoy snacks and drinks, and a lounge room with a fireplace and views of the surrounding park.

The Palanga Spa Life Balance Hotel is located at Birute Avenue, just 150 metres away from the Botanical Gardens and the Amber Museum.
