Description

Arunes Apartments are located in the centre of Palanga, only 250 metres from the Baltic Sea.

They all feature satellite TV and fully equipped kitchens to cook in. Arunes’ guests are welcome to relax in the air-conditioned living room with a seating area, in front of a flat-screen TV.

There is a green garden with barbecue facilities, as well as free private parking.

Some apartments include terraces and Wi-Fi. Arunes Apartments are situated 700 metres from the Palanga Pier and 6 km from the Palanga International Airport.
