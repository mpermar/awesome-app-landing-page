Description

Nestled next to the pine forest in the quiet part of Palanga resort, Gradiali Wellness and SPA Hotel is 500 metres from the sandy Baltic Sea beach.

Access to spa and sauna is included. A discount for medical procedures is offered.

The fitness centre is free of charge. Decorated with shades of cream and brown colours, all rooms come with a flat-screen TV, a refrigerator and a balcony.

Private bathrooms are equipped with heated floors, a shower, bathrobes and free toiletries.

Some rooms overlook the forest, others the courtyard and the top floor rooms offer a view of the sea.

Guests at Gradiali Wellness and SPA Hotel are welcome to enjoy a variety of massages, herbal bubble baths, underwater massages and other procedures at an extra fee.

Complementary unlimited use of gym facilities is available for all guests. The breakfast is served daily at the hotel's restaurant.

Guests can also enjoy a drink at the on-site bar. The distance from Gradiali Wellness and SPA Hotel to the centre of Palanga and the local bus station is 3 km.
