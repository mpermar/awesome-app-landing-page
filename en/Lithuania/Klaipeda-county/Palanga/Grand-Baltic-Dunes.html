Description

Grand Baltic Dunes offers pet-friendly accommodation in Palanga. Free WiFi is available throughout the property and free private underground parking is available on site.

The property offers various spa and beauty treatments, and massages at a surcharge.

The rooms include a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Certain rooms include a sitting area where you can relax.

You will find a kitchenette in the room. Every room has a private bathroom. Extras include free toiletries and a hairdryer.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. The apartments of Grand Baltic Dunes provide car rental.

Guests can also use the electrical vehicle charging station. Klaipėda is 24. 1 km from Grand Baltic Dunes, and Šventoji is 12.
