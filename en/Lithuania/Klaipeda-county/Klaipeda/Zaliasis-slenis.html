Description

Enjoying a scenic and quiet location, the hotel Zaliasis slenis is situated only 6 km from the Klaipeda city centre.

All rooms at the Zaliasis slenis have a modern decoration with light wooden furniture.

Free WiFi is available in all areas. There are many sports and leisure opportunities in the surrounding area.

A golf course is 5 km away, and horse riding facilities are 8 km away. There is a restaurant specialising in regional and international food.
