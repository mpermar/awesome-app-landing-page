Description

Apartamentai Upė is a self-catering accommodation located in Šventoji, 700 metres from a beach and the River Šventoji.

The apartment will provide you with a TV, a terrace and a seating area. There is a full kitchenette with a microwave and a refrigerator.

Private bathroom comes with a shower. You can enjoy river view and garden view from the rooms.

At Apartamentai Upė you will find a free communal sauna, a garden and barbecue facilities.

Activities in the surrounding area include fishing. The property offers free parking, as well as free water bikes.
