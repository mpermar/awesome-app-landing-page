Description

Surrounded by a beautiful old park, this newly renovated manor is located 700 metres off the Via Baltica highway in Northern Lithuania, 14 km away from Panevežys.

Free WiFi is available. Built in a neo-classical style in the mid-19th century, the Bistrampolis complex consists of the hotel, a restaurant, and palatial halls of various sizes for seminars and banquets.

Each room and suite is very spacious, features a modern bathroom, and is decorated with antiquities from the late 19th and early 20th centuries.
