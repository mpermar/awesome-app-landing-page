Description

Offering a private beach area, Augutės Zurlienės svečių namai is located on the lake of Lake Galve in Trakai, 800 metres from the centre.

Free Wi-Fi access is available. Rooms here will provide you with a TV and a seating area, as well as a picturesque view of the lake and a garden.

Bathroom facilities and kitchen are shared. At Augutės Zurlienės svečių namai you will find a garden with barbecue facilities and a large terrace, as well as a shared kitchen.

Other facilities include bicycle rental, fishing, ski storage and laundry. At extra charge, guests can use a sauna.

Airport shuttle is also available. Trakai Island Castle is located 900 metres from the property.
