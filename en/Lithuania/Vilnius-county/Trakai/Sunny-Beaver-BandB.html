Description

Offering a private forest and beach area and free WiFi, Sunny Beaver B&B is situated in Trakai in the Vilnius county Region, 5 km from Trakai Castle.

Free WiFi is offered and free private parking is available on site. A flat-screen TV, as well as a CD player are available in some rooms.

Some rooms feature a seating area for your convenience. Views of the mountain, lake or garden are featured in certain rooms.

You will find a shared kitchen, a banquet hall, an arbor on the beach, an outdoor fireplace, a grill, an outdoor stove and a smokery, as well as gift shop at the property.

This bed and breakfast has water sports facilities, beach volleyball area and ski storage space and bike, boat, canoe hire is available.

The area is popular for skiing, fishing and horse riding. The nearest airport is Vilnius Airport, 21 km from the property.
