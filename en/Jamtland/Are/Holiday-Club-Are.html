Description

Rooms at the eco-friendly Holiday Club Åre have a flat-screen TV with a Sony PlayStation 3 console. Each room has a seating area and a private bathroom with shower and hairdryer.

The large pool complex offers a 200 feet water slide and 8 pools. For a surcharge, guests can enjoy Season Sauna and the 6 different saunas, including an ice sauna. The spa area has 16 treatment rooms. Other facilities include a bowling alley, mini golf course and the LAB children's club.

Åre Holiday Club offers ground-floor storage for skis, bicycles and golf equipment. Ski equipment can be rented on site.
