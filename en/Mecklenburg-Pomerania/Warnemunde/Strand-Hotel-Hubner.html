Description

Strand-Hotel Hübner’s rooms feature elegant marble bathrooms. Comforts include a minibar, satellite TV and a safe. Some rooms have a balcony with sea views.

The spa is decorated in an attractive, North German style. Guests can relax in the Strand-Hotel’s sauna, book a massage or body treatment, or simply lie on the rooftop terrace overlooking the Baltic Sea.

German and international cuisine is served in Hübner’s restaurant and on the outdoor terrace. Coffee and cake can be enjoyed in the cozy library with open fireplace.

Several shops and restaurants are within a 5-minute walk of the hotel.
