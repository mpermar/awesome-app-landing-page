Description

Hotel Bernstein's spa is 8000 ft² in size. It features a pool, sauna, steam room and solarium. There is also a quiet room with sea views.

All rooms at the Bernstein include a private bathroom, a TV and large windows. They feature soft pastel colors and light wooden furniture.

Fish and meat dishes from West Pomerania are served in the elegant restaurant. During the summer you can dine on the large terrace overlooking the sea. Drinks can be enjoyed in the Bernsteinlounge.

During school holidays, the Bernstein Hotel offers a children's club with free childcare.
