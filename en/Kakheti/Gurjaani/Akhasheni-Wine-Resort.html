Description

The rooms at the resort are equipped with a seating area and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Featuring a private bathroom with a bath and free toiletries, rooms at Akhasheni Wine Resort also offer free WiFi. All rooms at the accommodation have air conditioning and a closet.

Akhasheni Wine Resort offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

The resort has a grill. You can play ping pong and darts at Akhasheni Wine Resort, and bike rental is available.

The nearest airport is Tbilisi International Airport, 42 miles from the property.
