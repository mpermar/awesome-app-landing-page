Description

Offering a restaurant and a spa area, Welness Hotel Harmony is located in Třešť and features free WiFi access in the entire property.

Breakfast is served every morning. Rooms here will provide you with a flat-screen TV, a minibar and a seating area.

Featuring a hairdryer, private bathrooms also come with towels. Extras include satellite channels and pay-per-view channels.

Some rooms are air-conditioned. At Welness Hotel Harmony you will find a terrace and a bar.

Other facilities offered include a games room and ski storage. Wellness and massages are available to guests for a surcharge and upon prior request.

An array of activities in the surroundings, including skiing, cycling and hiking.

The property offers free parking. The hotel can also arrange bike transfer or taxi service upon prior request.

The Roštejn Castle is 20km away from the hotel. The historic city centre of Telč is 13 km away.
