Description

Situated in the Vysočina region near the banks of the Svratka river, the Penzion Zubr features an outdoor swimming pool, horseback riding and free WiFi.

Breakfast is served every morning. The rooms are fitted with cable TV and come with a private bathroom with a shower.

Wired internet is available in the rooms. The on-site restaurant serves Czech cuisine.

Alternatively, barbecue facilities can be used. Guests can relax in a garden and children can roam on a playground.

Tennis courts are available upon request. The Pernštejn Castle lies 3 km from the Zubr Pension, while the Lysice Chateau is 20 km away.

The Šiklův Mlýn Western City with horseback riding is 8 km from the guest house and the Olešnice and Hodonín Ski Areas are 5 km away.

The Ujčov - Na Kopečku Bus Stop is 50 metres from the property. Free parking is possible on site.
