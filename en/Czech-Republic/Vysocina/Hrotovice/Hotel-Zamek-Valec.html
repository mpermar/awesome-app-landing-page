Description

Situated in the Valeč Chateau areal with 2 ponds, a park and a maze, Hotel Zámek Valeč offers a restaurant, a breakfast buffet and a terrace.

Free WiFi is available throughout the hotel. All the rooms in the Zámek Valeč hotel come with a seating area with a flat-screen TV, a refrigerator, a safety deposit box and a private bathroom with a hairdryer.

Some rooms are decorated with antique furniture. The on-site restaurant serves an international cuisine and the nearest grocery shop can be found within 200 metres.

The hotel features a wellness centre with an indoor and outdoor swimming poll, as well as saunas, available free of charge.

Massages are available for a surcharge. The Dalešická Dam is situated 7 km away and the Dalešice Brewery is 4 km away from the property.

The Jaroměřice nad Rokytnou Chateau and the Náměšť nad Oslavou Chateau can be found within a radius of 20 km.
