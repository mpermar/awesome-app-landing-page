Description

Situated 1 km from the center of Nové Město na Moravě, Hotel Horní Dvůr enjoys a quiet location.

Surrounded by garden with a pond, it offers an on-site restaurant and elegant rooms with free Wi-Fi and an LCD satellite TV.

All rooms comprise a private bathroom with a hairdryer. Amenities include a minibar and a safe.

Some come with a seating area with a sofa. The Hotel Horní Dvůr’s restaurant serves international dishes and Czech cuisine.

A selection of hot and cold drinks is also available. The nearest grocery store and a local bus stop are 800 metres away.

Nove Mesto na Morave Castle can be reached within 1 km. Zelena Hora and Sv. Jan Nepomucký Church is 11 km away.

An outdoor swimming pool is 3. 5 km from the Hotel Horní Dvůr. Cross-country skiing, biking and hiking opportunities can be found in the vicinity.
