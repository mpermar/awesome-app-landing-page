Description

Hotelový resort Šikland is located right in the Šikland entertainment park in Vysočina, in the middle of Moravian Highlands.

It consists of 2 hotel buildings - Colorado and Arizona. Rooms have a private bathroom, free WiFi, minibar, TV and adjustable and LED lighting.

Wide range of activities for children, adults and seniors is offered in the Šikland entertainment park which is available to all guests for a surcharge.

The haunted castle Draxmoor is 4 km from the hotel and the town of Zvole is 2 km away.

The Rožná Train Station, Pernštejn Castle and Nové Město na Moravě are both 6 and 14 km away.
