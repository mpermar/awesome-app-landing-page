Description

In Velké Bílovice centre, 3 km from a train station, Hotel Akademie offers free WiFi, a seasonal outdoor pool and a wellness area, featuring a fitness centre, massages and a hot tub.

A bus stop can be found within 50 metres. The rooms at the Akademie all come with a private bathroom with a shower, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a balcony.

The property houses an à la carte restaurant serving Czech and international cuisine.

Guests can rent a wine cellar on site. A bowling club and several conference halls can also be found here.

Chateau Lednice is 10 km away and Mikulov can be reached within a 20-minute drive.
