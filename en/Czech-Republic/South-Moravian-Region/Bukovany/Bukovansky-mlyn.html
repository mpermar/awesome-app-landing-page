Description

Located among the rolling hills of the South Moravian Region, Bukovanský mlýn offers charming accommodation with an outdoor pool and provides hiking and cycling possibilities.

Views can be enjoyed from the look-out tower situated in the areal. Decorated with rustic elements from the Moravian-Slovakian tradition, rooms at Bukovanský mlýn feature oak furnishings and exposed beams.

Each includes free Wi-Fi, a TV set and en suite bathrooms. Hearty dishes from Czech gastronomy are served at the Miller´s House Restaurant, complemented by a range of renowned Moravian wines.

The in-house cellar provides a chance to experience quality-wine tasting. Guests of the Bukovanský mlýn hotel can visit the on-site windmill and discover life in a 19th-century country house.

Brno is less than one-hour drive away and an airport shuttle service can be arranged upon request.
