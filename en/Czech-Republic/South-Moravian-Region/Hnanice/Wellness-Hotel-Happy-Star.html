Description

Located at the Austrian border, on the edge of Podyji National Park, this hotel offers rooms with free Wi-Fi and satellite TV.

Facilities include a wellness centre with an indoor pool. The rooms at Hotel Happy Star are decorated with warm colours and wooden furniture.

All rooms have a work desk, a minibar, and an en suite bathroom. Guests can have a relaxing massage at Happy Star’s wellness centre, which also features a sauna, steam cabin, a hot tub and a fitness centre.

The hotel offers many sports facilities, such as a tennis court, a beach volleyball court, and a petanque court.

Children can have fun at the playground. Happy Star Hotel’s restaurant serves local Moravian specialities.

Guests can enjoy local wine in the hotel’s wine cellar. Znojmo is 8 km from Hotel Happy Star, and the Vranov Castle is 30 km away.
