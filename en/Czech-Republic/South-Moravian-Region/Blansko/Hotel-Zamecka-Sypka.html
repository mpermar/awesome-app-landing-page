Description

Hotel Zámecká Sýpka in Blansko, 100 metres from the castle, provides a summer terrace with a park view and a beer pub with a large variety of different beers types.

There is a restaurant in the building. Free WiFi is available. All rooms in non-smoking Hotel Zámecká Sýpka come with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, and a bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer.

A safety deposit box is available. Some rooms also offer castle and park views. The on-site restaurant serves a traditional Czech cuisine, as well as international dishes.

The town centre with many restaurants and shopping facilities can be reached within a short walk.

Breakfast can be served each morning. Other facilities provided by the property include a bicycle storage room, children´s playground, free monitored parking possible on site and surcharged bowling with 8 alleys and a summer terrace.

The Macocha Abyss is situated within 8 km. The Western Park Boskovice is located within 20 km.
