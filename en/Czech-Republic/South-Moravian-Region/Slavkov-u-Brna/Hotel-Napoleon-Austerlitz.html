Description

Hotel Sokolský Dům Austerlitz is conveniently situated in the centre of Slavkov right on the square, 200 metres from the Slavkov Castle.

Free WiFi is available in the entire hotel. Paid parking is provided in front of the hotel and free parking is available 150 metres away.

Rooms are equipped with solid wooden furniture with a design featuring Napoleonic Wars.

Each room has its own bathroom, toilet and TV. There is a restaurant with a terrace serving also children`s menu.

Nearby guests can find a golf course, tennis courts, beach volleyball courts, swimming pool and stables.
