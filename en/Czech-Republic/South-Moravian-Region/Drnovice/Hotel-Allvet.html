Description

Hotel Allvet in Drnovice is located 1 km from the D1 Highway exit and offers guests a sports and wellness centre with a hot tub and finnish sauna.

Tennis courts, a beach volleyball court and horse riding facilities can also be used on site.

All units are spacious and have a private bathroom. Views of the surroundings fields, garden, courtyard or street can also be enjoyed.

Several rooms are in the attic and Wi-Fi is available free of charge. Hotel Allvet has its own wine cellar and serves international cuisine in the restaurant.

The dining area features a large stone fireplace and rustic furnishings. Hotel Allvet’s garden has a children’s playground with swings, a slide and climbing frame.

An air hockey table can also be used on site. The Drnovska Bioveta Bus Stop is located in front of the hotel.

The bus provides good connections to Vyskov 2 km away and the town’s main bus and train station.

Vyskov’s aquapark and dinosaur-park are just 3 km away. Slavkov u Brna (Austerlitz) can be reached in a 20-minute drive.
