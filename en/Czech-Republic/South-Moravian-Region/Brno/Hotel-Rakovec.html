Description

Hotel Rakovec is located on the banks of the Brno reservoir, 200 metres from the ship pier and 10 km from the city centre.

It offers panoramic lake views, wellness, free parking and free WiFi. Wellness is equipped with a steam sauna, Finnish sauna, swimming pool and whirlpool, available at surcharge.

Also at surcharge, guests of the hotel can also relax at the outdoor salt water swimming pool or go to play bowling in the on-site bowling alley.

All rooms in the property have an en-suite bathroom and cable TV. A restaurant with a large terrace with a view over the lake serves Czech and international cuisine as well as seasonal culinary specialities.
