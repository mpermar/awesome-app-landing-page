Description

Offering views of the Mikulov Castle, Hotel Maroli Mikulov houses an à-la-carte restaurant, bar and 24-hour front desk.

Currency exchange is available on site. Free WiFi is accessible throughout the property.

All rooms at Hotel Maroli feature satellite TV and a bathroom fitted with a bath or shower.

A hairdryer, a hair iron and a curling iron are available in the reception. Guests can relax on a terrace and children can enjoy a playground.

Special diets menu and packed lunches are available at the on-site restaurant. Free parking is possible on site.

Bicycle rental is provided. The castle can be reached within 1. 2 km. Mušovské Lake is 10 km away.
