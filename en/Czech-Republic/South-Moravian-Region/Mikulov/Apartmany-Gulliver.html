Description

Situated in the centre of Mikulov, Apartmány Gulliver offers apartments with free Wi-Fi and modern furniture.

The apartment building comes with tennis and volleyball courts and table tennis facilities.

The local castle is 500 metres away. The air-conditioned apartments are fitted with a TV, wooden floors, a sofa and a bathroom with a shower.

A kitchenette with a fridge is available in all accommodation units and some of them also feature wooden beam ceilings.

Free Wi-Fi is provided throughout the property. A garden with a covered seating area and barbecue facilities is a further feature of Apartmány Gulliver.

You can store your bicycles at the apartment house and request a pedicure. Free private parking is available in front of the building.

Restaurants can be reached within a 3-minute walk and a swimming pool is 50 metres away.

Svatý Kopeček is 3 km from Apartments Gulliver and Nové Mlýny Water Reservoirs.
