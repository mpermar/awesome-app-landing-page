Description

Hotel Kurdějov is located amid the Moravian Wine Region, in the village of Kurdějov, and features an indoor relax pool, tennis hall, sauna and bowling facilities, all available for a surcharge.

The hotel consists of 5 hotel houses. There is also a wine cellar available for wine tasting for group reservations.

Free WiFi is available in all areas. The property consists of 5 buildings, all located within a radius of 200 metres.

The rooms are decorated in different styles, like antique, rustic, modern or oriental.

All units are fitted with a TV, a seating area and a bathroom with bath tub or shower.

The restaurant on site serves Czech and Moravian cuisine. Guests can enjoy free entrance to the wellness centre for 1 hour.

Other facilities offered at Hotel Kurdějov include a hot tub, table tennis, a garden, a barbecue area and a children's playground.

The Lednice–Valtice Cultural Landscape is 29 km away and the Kurdějov Most Bus Stop can be found right in front of the hotel.
