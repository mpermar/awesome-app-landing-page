Description

Offering a seasonal outdoor pool and views of the garden, Apartmány Rose is set in Lednice in the South Moravian Region Region.

Lednice Chateau is 900 metres from the property. Free private parking is available on site.

All units include a flat-screen TV. All units have a kitchenette equipped with a microwave and refrigerator.

A stovetop and kettle are also offered. Every unit is equipped with a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hair dryer.

The apartment features free WiFi throughout the property. Apartmány Rose also includes a sun terrace.

Bike hire is available at the property and the area is popular for cycling. You can engage in various activities, such as fishing and hiking.

Chateau Jan is 1. 9 km from Apartmány Rose, while Minaret is 2. 2 km away. The nearest airport is Brno–Turany Airport, 41 km from the property.
