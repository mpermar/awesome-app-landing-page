Description

Located in Pasohlávky, 20 metres from Aqualand Moravia, Aqualand Inn boasts a restaurant and free WiFi throughout the property.

The hotel has an outdoor pool, year-round outdoor pool and seasonal outdoor pool, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Free private parking is available on site. Every room comes with a flat-screen TV.

Certain rooms feature a seating area for your convenience. Each room is equipped with a private bathroom.

For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer. There is a gift shop at the property.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including cycling and hiking.
