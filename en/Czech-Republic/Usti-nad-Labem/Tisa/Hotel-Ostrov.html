Description

Set in the rocky Tisa Walls area, this 4-star hotel is situated in Ostrov. It offers spacious rooms and suites with tea/coffee makers and balconies.

High-speed WiFi internet is free of charge in whole hotel and free parking is provided.

It also features a spa area with a pool. The bright rooms feature large windows, seating and working areas as well as satellite TV.

Guests have unlimited access to the wellness center with an indoor swimming pool, a Finnish sauna, steam bath and a hot tub.

Massages are available upon prior reservation. Hotel Ostrov houses a restaurant with wooden interiors serving Czech cuisine.

In summer, guests can dine on a terrace. Located in the most western part of Labske piskovce (The Elbe Sandstone Rocks), Tisa Walls provide numerous possibilities to go hiking, cycling, rock climbing and skiing, surrounded by unspoilt nature.
