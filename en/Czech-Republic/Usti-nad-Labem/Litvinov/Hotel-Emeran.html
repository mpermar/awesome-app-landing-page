Description

Just 20 metres from the slopes of the Klíny Ski Area in Krušné hory, Hotel Emeran offers a sauna and a fitness room.

Guests benefit from free Wi-Fi and a 24-hour reception. The bright rooms feature wooden furniture, satellite TV, and a bathroom.

Some have a balcony overlooking the valley. The restaurant at the Emeran Hotel serves Czech and international cuisine.

A snack bar is also on site. Guests can rent bicycles, use a climbing wall, and play table tennis.

In winter, ski passes can be bought on site, and ski equipment is available for rental.

The garden includes a children’s playground and barbecue facilities. Free parking is available on site.
