Description

Surrounded by the Bohemian Switzerland National Park, 6 km from the centre of Hřensko, U Fořta offers an on-site restaurant with a terrace.

Free Wi-Fi is provided in the entire building and free private parking is possible on site.

Breakfast is possible to order every morning. The restaurant offers Czech cuisine and degustation menus.

The rooms of U Fořta include a private bathroom and a TV. Pravčická Brána, the largest natural sandstone arch in Europe, can be reached within a 1-hour walk.

The German Border is 5 km away and the Janov Golf Course is 8 km from the property.
