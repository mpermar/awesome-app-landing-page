Description

Located on the city square of Krásná Lípa in the Bohemian Switzerland region, the Aparthotel Lípa offers accommodation in the Lípa Resort Venue with free WiFi and onsite parking.

There is an onsite restaurant serving Czech cuisine. For your comfort, you can book a table in the restaurant in advance.

The apartments are modernly furnished, with an equipped kitchen and a terrace. They are suitable for families.

All rooms feature a flat-screen satellite TV, a safety deposit box, a sofa and a washing machine.

Featuring a shower, bathrooms come with a hairdryer. Guests can relax in the garden, as well as use the Lípa Resort relax centre with spinning, sauna, gym, solarium and massages upon request.

Kids can have fun on a children's playground. Conference facilities are also available.

Warm buffet dinners or À la carte menu dinners are served at the restaurant. The Bohemian Switzerland Sport Center is 300 metres from the property and offers tennis, beach or hall volleyball, floorball, football, mini golf and a climbing wall.

In the winter, skiing is possible within 6 km in the Jedlová and Horní Podluží ski areas.

The bike and electrical bike rental service is available. The 22-km long Kogler Educational Trail starts on site.

The look-out Tower and Ruins of Tolštejn are 15 km away. There is a bus station in front of the property.

Free shuttle service is provided to the train station, which is located 300 metres away.
