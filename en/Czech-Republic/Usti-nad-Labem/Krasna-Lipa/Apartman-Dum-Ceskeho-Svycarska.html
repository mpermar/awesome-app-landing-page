Description

Apartment - House of Bohemian Switzerland offers accommodation in Krásná Lípa.

Görlitz is 43 km away. A sports area suitable for activities like tennis, volleyball, minigolf or table tennis is 300 metres away.

The attic apartments feature a kitchenette and a bathroom. A regional info centre with services for tourists is located in the same building.

There is also an exposition about the Czech-saxon Switzerland on site. A bus stop is next to the building.

A train station is a 15-minute walk away. Bike and electro bike hire is available at the property and the area is popular for cycling and skiing.
