Description

Offering a barbecue and children's playground, Wellness Penzion Jarmilka is located in Bedřichov, 43 km from Karpacz.

The guest house has a spa centre and hot tub, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Free WiFi is offered throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Every room includes a flat-screen TV. Some rooms include a seating area where you can relax.

The rooms have a private bathroom equipped with a shower. This guest house has ski-to-door access and ski equipment hire is available.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including skiing, golfing and cycling.

Špindlerův Mlýn is 33 km from Wellness Penzion Jarmilka, while Szklarska Poręba is 26 km from the property.
