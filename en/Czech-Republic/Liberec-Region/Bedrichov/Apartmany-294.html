Description

Apartmány 294 enjoys the peaceful surroundings of the forest in Bedřichov, just 500 metres from the ski area, offering self-catered accommodation with free WiFi and 2 children playgrounds.

All units are functionally equipped with modern furniture and , flat-screen satellite TVs and private bathrooms with shower.

Some also have air conditioning, while others have a balcony. Guests at Apartmány 294 can make use of the well-appointed kitchens or kitchenettes.

The closest grocery store and a restaurant are reachable within 500 metres. Barbecue facilities are provided in the garden.

Free ski storage is available on site, and Aqua Park and Zoo Liberec is 15 km away.
