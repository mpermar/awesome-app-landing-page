Description

Offering a golf course, wellness centre and a restaurant, Resort Malevil features traditionally decorated accommodation and numerous possibilities for adrenalin activities.

The green environment provides an abundance of peace and quiet. The golf course has 18 holes, while horseback riding and a kids' zoo are also available on site.

Squash, tennis, pétanque and table tennis can be played at the resort, which also has a rope park, off-road and paintball facilities.

Services of the wellness centre come at an extra charge. It includes sun beds and massage treatments.

The resort disposes of a fully equipped conference room and an outdoor swimming pool and a bowling bar.

There is a bus station 1 km away, and the Jablonné v Podještědí Train Station is 5 km from the hotel.

The town of Oybin featuring an aquapark, spa centre and Gothic-style castle is 6 km away.

Lemberk and Grabštejn castles are both within a 10 km radius. Luž Ski Area is just 6 km away, while Jedlová and Jested ski areas are within 25 km.

Some rooms are located in the annex buildings set within 1 km from the Resort Malevil.
