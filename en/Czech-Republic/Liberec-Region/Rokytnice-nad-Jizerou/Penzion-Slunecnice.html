Description

Situated 1 km from Rokytnice nad Jizerou, Penzion Slunečnice is 3 km from Studenov Ski Center.

It is a good starting point for biking and hiking and offers accommodation with free Wi-Fi.

There is a restaurant and guests can also relax in the common living area with a fireplace and pool table.

Each bedroom comes with a private bathroom with shower and toilet. Ski and bike storage room is provided.

A 200-metre long ski slope for children is just 300 metres away. Horní Domky Ski Centre is 5 km away and Kamenec-Jablonec Ski Centre is at a distance of 7 km.

The nearest grocery store and a restaurant can be reached within 2 km. Studenov is located 3,7 km away.
