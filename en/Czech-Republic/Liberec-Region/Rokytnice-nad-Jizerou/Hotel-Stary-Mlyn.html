Description

Surrounded by nature of the Krkonose mountains and located within 100 metres from the Modrá hvězda and Bahynka Ski Areas, the Hotel Starý Mlýn features a bar and café, a sauna and a games room with billiard and table football.

Free Wi-Fi is provided in the entire building. All rooms are non-smoking and come with satellite TV and a seating area.

They have a private bathroom with free toiletries and a shower. There is a garden with the possibility to have a barbecue.

Boarding is possible in the form of half board, quality homemade food, but it is necessary to order in advance.

Guests can also play billiards or table tennis. The surrounding offers maintained cross-country ski trails starting 300 metres from the Starý Mlýn and the Ski Resort of Horní Domky is 1.

5 km away. Opposite the hotel there is a kart track, which is open from spring to autumn.

You can also rent bikes, skis and snowboard equipment. Parking is available at a parking lot 30 metres from the hotel; it is free in the summer and paid in the winter.
