Description

Penzion Barunka is located in Vysoké nad Jizerou and offers accommodation units with free WiFi and satellite TV.

Guests can relax in a shared lounge, enjoy drinks at the bar and play darts on site.

Free private parking is available on spot. All units come with a fridge and a bathroom with either a shower or a bathtub.

The apartments additionally have a kitchen with a dining area. Guests can also buy ski passes at Penzion Barunka and make use of a ski storage room.
