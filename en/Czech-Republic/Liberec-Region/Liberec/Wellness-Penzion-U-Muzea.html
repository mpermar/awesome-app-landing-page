Description

Providing a spa and wellness centre, The U Muzea is a cosy guest house set in a leafy neighbourhood, 500 metres from the centre of Liberec.

Here, guests can choose between en-suite rooms and a self-catering apartment, all with free Wi-Fi.

The Zoo with its famous white tigers lies 700 metres from the property. Each of the accommodation units at the Penzion U Muzea is decorated with modern wooden furniture.

Breakfast is served each morning, on request. In the secluded garden there is a large trampoline.

The wellness centre is available upon prior request and for a surcharge. Popular nearby attractions include the Babylon Aquapark, found 2.

6 km away. Secured parking is provided free of charge on site. The Jested Ski Resort is located 8.
