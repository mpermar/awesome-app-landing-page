Description

Pytloun Hotel Liberec on the outskirts of Liberec, close to the R-35 expressway and only a 4-minute drive from the city centre.

All rooms come with an LCD satellite TV, free coffee/tea set, as well as a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries.

This comfortable non-smoking hotel offers modern rooms, a friendly staff and its own restaurant.

Because of its central location, the Hotel Pytloun is an ideal starting point to explore the city of Liberec and the surrounding area.
