Description

Set in a peaceful location in Simonovice suburb of Liberec Pension Jitka is located 8 km from the town centre.

It offers comfortable rooms featuring flat-screen satellite TV and furnished with lots of wood.

A rather traditional countryside villa on the outside, Jitka offers views of Jested and comfortable interiors with many modern amenities.

A playground with trampolines and a barbecue terrace are available. The closest bus stop with links to Liberec is 700 metres away.

Babylon Aquapark can be reached within 5 km. The closest restaurant is 1 km away from Jitka.

Jested Ski Area is 6 km away. The highway which connects Liberec with Prague can be reached within 2 km.
