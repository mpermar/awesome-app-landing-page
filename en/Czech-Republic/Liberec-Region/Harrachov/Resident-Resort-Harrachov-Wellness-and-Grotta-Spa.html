Description

The elegant Resident Resort Harrachov - Wellness & Grotta Spa enjoys a quiet location near the centre of Harrachov, only 700 meters from the ski area which can be reached with a free private ski shuttle.

Free WiFi is available. The spacious studios and apartments come with a kitchen, living room, bathroom and garden views.

Czech and international cuisine, as well as a traditional pizza can be enjoyed in the modern restaurant Osteria.

Breakfast is served every morning, and daily room service is provided upon request.

Parking is available in the garage or on the outdoor car park. Guests can relax in a sauna, a hot tub or in a fitness room for 1 hour for free.

Additional procedures, including a massage and a Garra Rufa fish treatment, are available for a surcharge.

Furthermore, new Grotta Spa with a size of 400 m is available for a surcharge and offers a variety of services, such as a salt-herbal sauna, a hammam or a salt tepidarium.

In the picturesque surroundings, you can enjoy skiing, snowboarding, sledging, hiking, or golf.

In the vicinity of Resident Resort Harrachov - Wellness & Grotta Spa, you will find tennis courts and a small ski slope for children.
