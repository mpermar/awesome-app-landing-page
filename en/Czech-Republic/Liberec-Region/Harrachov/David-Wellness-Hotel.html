Description

Located in the centre of Harrachov, David Wellness Hotel is just 100 metres from the ski slopes.

Each room has a balcony overlooking the ski jumps and the slopes. David Wellness Hotel’s rooms include satellite TV, a seating area, and a bathroom.

Guests can enjoy an hour per day at the wellness centre. The restaurant serves Czech and international cuisine, as well as daily breakfast buffet.

Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas. Guests can buy ski passes, hire ski equipment, and play billiards and table tennis.

A ski storage room and free private parking are available on site. In summer, tennis courts, an outdoor pool, and a miniature golf course are within 1 km.
