Description

Situated 300 metres from the Harrachov Ski Resort with night skiing and a 15-minute walk from the town centre, Pytloun Wellness Hotel Harrachov features a restaurant with a summer terrace, serving breakfast and Czech cuisine.

Guests receive the Harrachov Card for free. The Harrachov Card provides various benefits and discounts on attractions in the surroundings.

All rooms come with an LCD satellite TV, free coffee/tea set, ironing facilities, as well as a private bathroom with a bathtub, hairdryer and free toiletries.

All rooms overlook the surrounding countryside or slopes. Guests can relax playing billiard, table tennis, darts, or enjoy an evening drink in the bar.
