Description

In a quiet spot at the edges of a lush green forest, Orea Resort Sklář offers free access to its large wellness centre.

Spa facilities include a swimming pool, steam bath and a hot tub. Harrachov is a famous Czech mountain resort and the hotel is situated just 5 minutes from the centre of town, at an altitude of 700 m above sea level.

The Orea was renovated in 2006 and is ideal for a comfortable, relaxing, restorative break in this beautiful mountain region.
