Description

The units come with hardwood floors and feature a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher, a dining area, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with bath and free toiletries. There's also a oven, microwave and a coffee machine.

A buffet breakfast is served at the property.

The chalet has a playground.

There's a shared lounge at this property and guests can go skiing and cycling nearby.

Ski lift Čertova Hora is an 8-minute walk from Chalet 6 Harrachov, while Glass Museum is 0.7 miles from the property. Pardubice Airport is 54 miles away.
