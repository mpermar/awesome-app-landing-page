Description

A wellness area with a hot tub, a sauna and an indoor swimming pool is provided at Hotel Berg in Staré Splavy, 200 metres from the shore of the Máchovo Jezero Lake.

The hotel’s own restaurant serves international cuisine and features a terrace. All rooms come with classic furniture, a seating area and a flat-screen satellite TV.

A bathroom with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries is also provided in each room.

Free Wi-Fi can be used in all rooms and rooms for disabled guests are available. You can work out in Berg’s fitness centre and read books in the on-site library.

Table tennis and billiards facilities are provided on site and children can romp around on the hotel’s playground.

After a long day, you can request a relaxing massage at Hotel Berg. A breakfast is served daily and can be consumed in the privacy of your own room.

Swimming can be enjoyed in the lake and a miniature golf course can be found 200 metres away.

Fishing is possible in a small lake located 500 metres from the hotel. The Bezděz Castle is 13 km from the property and the Gothic Houska Castle can be reached within 15 minutes by car.

Free private parking is possible in front of the hotel and the closest bus and train station is 300 metres away.
