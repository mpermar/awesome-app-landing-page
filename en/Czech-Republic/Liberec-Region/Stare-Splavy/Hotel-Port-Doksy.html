Description

Offering modern-style rooms and a restaurant, Hotel Port Doksy enjoys a location at the Mácha Lake.

A private sandy lakeshore, 2 swimming pools and several sports facilities are featured.

The wellness centre includes a sauna and a hot tub. Each room is spacious and comes with carpeted floors, satellite TV and a minibar.

The private bathroom has a bath or shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries. A room with a balcony is available upon request and for a surcharge.

Samples of Czech cuisine are served at the hotel’s restaurant, as well as international and fish specialities.

The complex comprises a heated outdoor pool, an indoor pool and 2 bowling lanes, as well as tennis, mini golf and beach volleyball courts.

Front desk offers possibilities for renting bikes, as well as rowboats and pedal boats for use at the lake.

A fitness centre is available at the hotel. There is also a massage centre with professionally trained employees.

Private parking is available on site free of charge. Doksy Train Stop is 700 metres away.

Lemberk Castle is 35 km away, while Bezděz Castle is at a distance of 12 km. The town of Česká Lípa is at a distance of 19 km, while Prague is 80 km to the south from Port Doksy.
