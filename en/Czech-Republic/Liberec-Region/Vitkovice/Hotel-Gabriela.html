Description

This hotel is situated in the highest part of Vitkovice in the Giant Mountains, 800 metres from the Aldrov Ski Area.

It has a restaurant with a fireplace, a garden with a barbecue area and a tennis court.

All of the rooms and suites in the Hotel Gabriela have a private bathroom and satellite TV.

Free Wi-Fi is available in the entire building. Typical Czech cuisine is served in the restaurant or on the garden terrace offering panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.

The Rokytnice nad Jizerou Skia Area is 8 km from the property, while the Horni Misecky ski area is 10 km away.

500 metres from the Gabriela Hotel there is a cross-country skiing track. There are many cycle tracks in the vicinity and horse riding can be arranged on site.
