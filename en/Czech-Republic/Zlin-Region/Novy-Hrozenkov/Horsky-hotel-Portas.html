Description

Located on top of Javorník Mountains, just steps from the slopes in Kohutka’s Ski Area, Horsky hotel Portáš offers peaceful surroundings, panoramic views and a relaxing sauna and outdoor swimming pool.

Ski passes and ski equipment are available on site. In a traditional setting, the à la carte restaurant serves Czech, Valachian and Slovak specialties.

Breakfast is provided and half board and full board can be arranged. Meals can also be enjoyed out in the garden.

Standard rooms offer private bathrooms with a shower. Economy rooms have shared showers and WC.

Free Wi-Fi is provided in the entire hotel. Guests can make use of the tennis court, children’s playground, table football and table tennis.

Learner skiers are given discounts at the nearby ski school. Many hiking, cycling and cross-country skiing trails are in the area.

Nový Hrozenkov is a 15-minute drive away where you can find a grocery store and swimming lake.
