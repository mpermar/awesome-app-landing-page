Description

Offering an à-la-carte restaurant, bowling, a sauna and a hot tub, Hotel Vyhlídka is located in Luhačovice in 2 buildings.

The annex building is located 80 metres from the main building. Free WiFi access is available and the front desk is opened for 24 hours.

Each room here will provide you with a TV, a safety deposit box, a fridge and a minibar.

Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Some units also feature a sofa. The annexes are set 80 metres from the main building.

Guests at Hotel Vyhlídka can make use of meeting facilities, a tour desk and a kids' playroom.

The main building provides disability access. An array of activities can be enjoyed in the surroundings, including cycling and hiking.
