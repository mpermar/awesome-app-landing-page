Description

Housed in a former mill belonging to the 13-th century Cistercian Monastery, Hotel Mlyn Velehrad offers a wine cellar with 60 wine sorts, wellness area with sauna and a playground.

All rooms are equipped with satellite TV. The entire hotel provides free Wi-Fi access.

The restaurant at the Mlyn Velehrad has a summer terrace and serves regional specialities as well as international dishes.

Velehrad is 7 km away from Uherske Hradiste in the Karpaty Mountains in Middle Moravia.

A tennis court is 100 metres away from Hotel Mlyn. During the stay from 1. 10. 2019 to 15.

12. 2019 guests have free access to the sauna. Please contact us for more information.
