Description

Set in Ostrožská Nová Ves, this hotel is 8 km from Uherské Hradiště.

It offers en-suite rooms, free Wi-Fi, a restaurant offering steak and pasta specialities and a courtyard with a pond.

The restaurant features a garden terrace, where also breakfast can be enjoyed when weather is nice.

A grocery store can be found just 150 metres away. You can park your car for free on site, make use of the bicycle storage room and relax in the sauna of the U Racka hotel.

The Jezero Golf Club and a lake for swimming are 1 km away and the Buchlovice Palace is 12 km away.
