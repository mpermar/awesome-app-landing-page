Description

Relax hotel Valaska enjoys a peaceful location and a 10-minute walk from the Horni Becva Water Dam.

This family-run hotel offers functionally furnished rooms with free Wi-Fi. You can taste Czech cuisine at the on-site restaurant.

The garden awaits guests with a summer terrace and a children’s playground. You can take advantage of the billiard as well as table tennis and darts facilities on site.

You can reach the Sachova Studánka Ski Area 5 km away, while the Bílá Ski Area is 15 km from the Valaška Hotel.

Shuttle service to the ski slopes can be arranged on request. Within 15 km you can reach the town of Rožnov pod Radhoštěm with an open-air museum, a beer spa and a golf club.
