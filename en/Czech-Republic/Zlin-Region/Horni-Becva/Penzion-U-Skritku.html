Description

At Penzion U Skřítků, all rooms include a desk and a TV. With a private bathroom, certain accommodations at Penzion U Skřítků also feature a mountain view. All guest rooms include a closet.

Penzion U Skřítků has a playground. A number of activities can be enjoyed in the surrounding areas, including skiing, cycling and hiking.

Wisła is 29 miles from Penzion U Skřítků, while Ustroń is 30 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Ostrava Leos Janacek Airport, 21 miles from the property.
