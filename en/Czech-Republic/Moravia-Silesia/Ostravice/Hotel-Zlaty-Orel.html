Description

Located outside Ostravice, Hotel Zlatý Orel is surrounded by manicured gardens and offers views of Lysa Hora Mountain, a café-bar with summer terrace and a restaurant with wine cellar.

Its spa facilities include a hot tub. The 19th century mansion is located at the foot of the Moravian-Silesian Beskids and its rooms are all equipped with free Wi-Fi, TV sets and telephone.

Guests can find tea and coffee in the room, while the best rooms offer a separate living room and a whirlpool bathtub.

The Castle Restaurant with a fireplace and wine cellar is open daily. Wine-tasting tours are available on request.

The spa centre features a unique massage room with a fireplace and views of the garden, a Finnish sauna and a hot tub.
