Description

Offering a fitness, spa centre, free indoor pool and ski storage, Peras Wellness Hotel is located in Ludvíkov, within 600 metres from the nearest ski lift, and houses a restaurant.

Free WiFi access is available. All units include a TV, a safety deposit box, a minibar, and a private bathroom with a bath or a shower.

Some also feature a kitchenette and a balcony. Guests can relax in the spa centre including a sauna, an indoor pool and a hot tub, all for an additional cost.
