Description

Situated in the village of Čeladná and by a golf resort reachable within 3 km, Horský Hotel Čeladenka offers accommodation with a restaurant, a bar, a games room, a wellness centre, a terrace and a children´s playground.

Free WiFi is available in all areas. The hotel won the award as the best hotel in Moravian-Silesian district in 2015, 2016 and 2017.

Each unit at Horský Hotel Čeladenka comes with a TV with satellite channels, a minibar, a desk, a safety deposit box, a laptop safe and a private bathroom with a shower.

Some unit also feature a disability access and a shared lounge is available for all guests.

The rooms are fitted with modern facilities so that also guests suffering from allergies can be accommodated.

Meals can be enjoyed in the on-site restaurant, packed lunches and special diet menus are also provided.

Other facilities offered for a surcharge include a sauna, massages and tennis court.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including skiing, golfing and cycling.

The property offers free parking. There is also a congress centre with the capacity of 90 people where different events such as business events, parties or weddings can be held.
