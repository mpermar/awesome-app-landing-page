Description

At the hotel, each room has a desk. Featuring a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries, certain units at Hotel Buly Opava also have a balcony. At the accommodation rooms contain a closet and a flat-screen TV.

The daily breakfast offers continental and buffet options. The restaurant at Hotel Buly Opava specializes in international cuisine.

The hotel offers 3-star accommodations with a fitness center. Guests at Hotel Buly Opava will be able to enjoy activities in and around Opava, like cycling.

The nearest airport is Ostrava Leos Janacek Airport, 19 miles from the accommodation.
