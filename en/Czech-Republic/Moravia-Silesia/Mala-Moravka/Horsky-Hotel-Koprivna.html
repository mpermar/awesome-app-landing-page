Description

An own ski area and an outdoor swimming pool are features of Horský Hotel Kopřivná.

The hotel is situated on a secluded hillside, 1 km from the village of Malá Morávka.

A spa area with a sauna and a hot tub is provided and the hotel also comes with a restaurant.

A minibar and a TV are standard in each room. All come with a bathroom which is fitted with a shower and a hairdryer.

Free Wi-Fi is available in every room and some also come with a living area. You can sample Czech and international cuisine on site and enjoy cold and hot drinks at the bar.

Each day, a breakfast is served at Kopřivná Horský Hotel. Barbecue facilities are provided in the surrounding garden.

A small leisure area with trampolines, a playground and a climbing wall is a further feature of the hotel.

You can work out in the on-site gym and play table tennis at the property. Massages can be requested as well.

In good snow conditions, Horský Hotel Kopřivná is directly accessible from the ski slopes.

Ski equipment and bicycles can be rented on site. You can park your car free of charge in front of the building.

The Praděd Mountain is 10 km away and the spa village of Karlova Studánka is 6 km away.
