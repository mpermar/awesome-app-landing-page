Description

The elegant Troyer offers stylish en-suite accommodation in the scenic Beskydy Mountains, 400 metres from the Ski Lift in Pustevny.

Here, guests can enjoy free access to the spa centre. There is also a panoramic restaurant and a nightclub on site.

Free Wi-Fi is provided free of charge. The impressive array of wellness facilities offered free of charge includes a hot tub, a sauna, a gym, and a massage parlour.

Guests can rent bikes, buggies, and skiing equipment directly at the property. The on-site restaurant has a cosy winter garden.

There is also a Wallachian-style pub. Each of the elegant rooms at the Troyer Hotel is equipped with an LCD satellite TV and a hydro-massage shower.

The interior design is sleek and modern. Popular summertime activities include hiking or biking in the surrounding mountains of Nořičí Hora, Radhošť, and Velký Javorník.

Horseback riding is available in Trojanovice, 2. 5 km from the complex. Frenštát Pod Radhoštěm Train Station is located 5 km away.
