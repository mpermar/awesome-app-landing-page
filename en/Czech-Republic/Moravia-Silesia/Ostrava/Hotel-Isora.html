Description

Hotel Isora is situated in a quiet area 4 km from Ostrava’s centre. It offers a beer bar, a restaurant serving Czech cuisine and international dishes, and free Wi-Fi.

All rooms come with satellite TV and a bathroom. Guests can make use of the summer terrace with barbecue facilities.

Darts and table tennis facilities are also available on site, while a playground is 500 metres from the property.

At Isora Hotel a small grocery store can be found. Free private parking is possible on site.

Bicycle repair can be arranged. Koblov Bus Stop is next to the hotel. The D1 motorway is reachable within 1 km, and Ostrava Airport is 15 km away.

An airport pick-up can be organised at a surcharge. Fishing can be done 200 metres away, and Šilheřovice Golf Course is 3 km away.
