Description

Offering an outdoor pool and an indoor pool, Wellness&Restaurant U Fandy is located in Lhotka.

Free WiFi access is available and free private parking is possible on site. At the property guests can also enjoy various meals in the on-site restaurant.

Each room at the property has a seating area, a desk, a dining table and an electric kettle.

Garden views are also available from every room. Featuring a bath or shower, private bathroom also comes with free toiletries.

At Wellness&Restaurant U Fandy a spa and wellness centre is available for guests, featuring a sauna and hot tub.

There are also a bowling alley and table tennis, as well as a garden. Airport shuttle services can be arranged upon request and at a surcharge.

The property is located 34 km away from Ostrava and 25 km away from Leos Janacek Airport.
