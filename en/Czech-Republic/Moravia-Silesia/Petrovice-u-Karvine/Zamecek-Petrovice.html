Description

Enjoying quiet location in a castle park, Zámeček Petrovice provides elegant rooms, 24-hour front desk and a restaurant serving Czech and international cuisine.

Breakfast is served and free WiFi is accessible throughout the property. All rooms are air conditioned and feature a seating area, a flat-screen TV, a minibar, a safety deposit box, and a bathroom fitted with a shower or a bathtub, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Guests at Zámeček Petrovice can have a rest in a garden or on a terrace. Children can enjoy outdoor playground and indoor kid’s room.

A bus stop is 400 metres away. Cycling trails start nearby the property. Borders of Poland are within 2 km.

The castle in Fryštát can be reached within 7 km. The Lipiny Golf Resort is 8 km away.
