Description

Situated by the river of Lužnice and a 10- minute walk from the historical town center of Tábor, Hotel Žižkovy lázně offers en-suite rooms, a bar, a terrace, as well as a garden with river and city views.

Free WiFi is available in all areas and free parking is possible on site. All rooms in Hotel Žižkovy lázně come with a flat-screen TV, a safety deposit box, a minibar and a bathroom with a bath tub or a shower.

Some rooms have a balcony with a view of the river Lužnice and the town of Tábor.

Zizkovy lazne Hotel provides guests with Bed&Breakfast type of accommodation. Guests may use the services of the onsite bar and cafe with an offer of light snacks and sweet cakes.

The hotel restaurant is open only for events like wedding receptions, parties or celebrations.

Reservation in advance is necessary. The nearest bus station is situated 50 metres away.

The Kotnov Castle is reachable within a 10-minute walk and the Hussite Museum and the Catacombs can be found within a 15-minute walk.
