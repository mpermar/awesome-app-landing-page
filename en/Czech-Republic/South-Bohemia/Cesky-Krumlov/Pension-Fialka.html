Description

Set in a peaceful residential part of Cesky Krumlov and only 500 metres from the centre, Pension Fialka is a family guesthouse.

It offers carefully appointed rooms, free Wi-Fi and fenced on-site parking for a small fee.

All of the rooms in Pension Fialka feature hardwood floors and include a private bathroom with shower.

The rooms are all fitted with hand-carved wooden furniture. In summer breakfast can be served in the garden.

In case of cold weather breakfast is served in the room. Guests have a common fridge and microwave at their disposal.

A barbecue terrace set within the large garden can also be used. A restaurant and a grocery shop can be reached within a 5-minute walk.

Massages can be booked against an extra charge. Just across the street there are 2 tennis clubs with outdoor and indoor courts.

A public indoor pool is only 100 metres away, and horseback riding is available within 1 km.

A beach volleyball court is 70 metres away. Cesky Krumlov Castle is 500 metres away and Klet Fortress is 5 km from the Fialka pension.
