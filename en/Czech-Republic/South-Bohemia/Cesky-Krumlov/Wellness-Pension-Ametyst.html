Description

The small family-run Wellness Pension Ametyst offers you a friendly, peaceful atmosphere close to the centre of Český Krumlov.

It is situated in a historical building dated from 1807 which has been fully renovated in 2000 and 2016.

The guest house features a private garden with a terrace offering views of the castle and the castle tower.

One can enjoy here a peaceful atmosphere of the historical heart of Český Krumlov.

Guests can choose from en-suite standard, deluxe or premium hotel-style rooms and apartments, all offering free Wi-Fi access.

Some rooms have a kitchenette and all guests are welcome to use the shared kitchen facilities, complete with a selection of soft drinks.

The premium room on the ground floor features a large terrace of 50 sqm with a direct view of the castle.

Breakfast is prepared according to guests` preferences (continental, vegetarian, macrobiotic or low-calorie) and will be served to their room or - weather permitting - on the sunny terrace of the Pension Ametyst any time guests wish.

Breakfast can be booked directly at the hotel for a surcharge. The property offers private wellness facilities such as a sauna, infra sauna, and a whirlpool among others.

These services are provided for a surcharge. The Spicak bus stop is 200 metres away from the property.
