Description

Located in Černá V Pošumaví, 250 meters from Lipno Nature Water Reservoir, Penzion Stříbrný Větr is a small family-run guest house.

It offers a small private wellness with a finish sauna and an outdoor hot tub. The guesthouse serves home made traditional cuisine.

A 24-hour front desk is available, and there is also a bar with a terrace and outdoor barbeque on site.

The bright rooms all feature a TV and a minibar or a fridge. The bathrooms are fitted with a shower, toilet and free toiletries.

Rooms offer garden views. A cycling path is accessible directly from the guesthouse, and the nearest beach is 250 metres from the property.

In winter, cross-country skiing trails start 100 metres away. An adventure park is 3 km away, while the Frymburk Aquapark is 13 km from the property.
