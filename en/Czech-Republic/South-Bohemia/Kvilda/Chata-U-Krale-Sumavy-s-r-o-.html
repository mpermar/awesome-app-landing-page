Description

Providing ski-to-door access and an à-la-carte restaurant where breakfast is served, Chata U Krále Šumavy s.

r. o. is located in Kvilda, in the vicinity of a ski lift and cross-country skiing tracks.

Free WiFi is available. All units include a TV with cable channels, and a private bathroom fitted with a shower and free toiletries.

Daily maid service is provided. Guests at Chata U Krále Šumavy s. r. o. can have a rest in a garden with a terrace and barbecue facilities.

Children can use a trampoline. Activities such as horse riding, skiing, cycling or hiking can be enjoyed in the surroundings.
