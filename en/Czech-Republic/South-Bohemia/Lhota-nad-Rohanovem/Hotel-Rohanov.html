Description

Providing a restaurant where breakfast is served every morning, Hotel Rohanov is located in Lhota nad Rohanovem and offers table tennis, darts, a garden with barbecue facilities as well as a children’s playground.

Free WiFi is accessible throughout the building. All units include a seating area.

They come with private or shared bathroom facilities. The apartment also features a kitchenette.

Guests can make use of a shared lounge. Packed lunches and special diet menus can be arranged upon request.

Meeting facilities are available for a surcharge. Free parking is possible at the property.

A natural swimming pool can be found within 1 km. The nearest ski lift in Javorník can be reached within 2 km.
