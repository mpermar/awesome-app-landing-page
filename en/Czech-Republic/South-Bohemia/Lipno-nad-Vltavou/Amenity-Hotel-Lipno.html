Description

Amenity Hotel Lipno is located in Lipno nad Vltavou right at the lake shore, with a beach situated in front of the hotel.

Free WiFi access is available. All rooms include satellite flat-screen TV, a safety deposit box, a minibar, an electric kettle, and a bathroom fitted with a bath or shower, a hairdryer, bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries.

Some also provide a balcony, a terrace and lake views. Suites also feature a seating area.

Breakfast is served every morning. Guests can dine at the restaurant in the resort 100 metres away.

The resort also features a spa centre with a sauna, hot tub and steam bath, a fitness centre, a bowling alley, and a sports centre with facilities for tennis, squash, badminton and table tennis, as well as a climbing wall.

All facilities in the resort can be used by hotel guests at a surcharge. In addition, guests can take advantage of a 20 metre-long salt-water swimming pool.

It is accessible free of charge daily until 13:00. Free parking is available on site.

Amenity Hotel Lipno is situated 3 km from Lipno Treetop Walkway and 2 km from the Kramolín Ski Area.
