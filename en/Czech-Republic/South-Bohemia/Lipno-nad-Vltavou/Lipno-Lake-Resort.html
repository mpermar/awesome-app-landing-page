Description

This 4-star resort provides self-catering apartments featuring a private sauna and a furnished balcony overlooking the National Sumava Park.

It offers free outdoor pool and multifunctional sports court, free WiFi and a health club.

Each spacious apartment at Lipno Lake Resort comes with a modern, well-equipped kitchen and heated floors.

The seating area has plush couches, a flat-screen satellite TV, and a fire place.

In the summer, guests can enjoy a game of tennis or sunbathe on the terrace. They can also swim in Lake Lipno or cycle through the nature park.

Animation programme is offered in summer. In the winter, guests can ski or enjoy the bobsleigh track.

Welcome drink is provided. The resort further offers sport and animation centre with sport equipment rental, and a free children's playroom.
