Description

Located in Šejby, 35 km from Český Krumlov, Penzion Šejby features a barbecue, children's playground and sun terrace.

Guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Free private parking is available on site. Rooms have a flat-screen TV.

Some rooms feature a seating area where you can relax. Certain rooms have views of the mountain or garden.

Each room includes a private bathroom. Penzion Šejby features free WiFi throughout the property.

This guest house has ski storage space and bike hire and car hire are available. You can play darts at the guest house.

České Budějovice is 35 km from Penzion Šejby, while Bad Leonfelden is 42 km from the property.
