Description

Located in Zvíkovské Podhradí, this hotel is 20 minutes’ walk from Zvíkov Castle, at the junction of the Vltava and Otava rivers.

It offers a restaurant, an outdoor pool and a sauna. Free Wi-Fi and satellite TV are standard in all of Hotel Zvikov’s rooms.

Each has an en suite bathroom, with toiletries and a hairdryer. National dishes and buffet breakfast are served at the restaurant.

There is also a bar with a terrace overlooking Otava River. Orlik Dam is just 15 km away.
