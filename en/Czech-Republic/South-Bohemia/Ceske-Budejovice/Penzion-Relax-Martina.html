Description

Situated 3 km from the centre of Ceske Budejovice, this hotel offers spacious apartments with free Wi-Fi, private kitchens and bathrooms.

A seasonal outdoor pool and barbecue facilities are available. Penzion Relax Martina is a good starting point to go hiking or cycling in the neighbourhood.

Bicycles can be rented at the reception. The train station is 1. 5 km away from the guest house and 200 metres away is a bus stop with local buses (numbers 2 and 10) going to the city centre.
