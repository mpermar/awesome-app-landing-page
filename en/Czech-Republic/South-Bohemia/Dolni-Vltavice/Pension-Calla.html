Description

Pension Calla is located just 50 metres from a private grassy beach on Lipno Dam.

Guests can go swimming, windsurfing, yachting, fishing, cross-country skiing and ice skating at the dam.

Studios at the Calla have views of Lipno Lake. They also have a kitchenette and bathroom, and some have a balcony.

Wi-Fi is available free of charge. The property has a common room with a TV, bar and board games.

Also on site are a bike storage room and barbecue facilities. 2 bicycles and a boat can be rented from the Calla, and private parking is available on site free of charge.

A restaurant, the local bus stop and a ferry can be reached on foot in 5 minutes.

The Lipno nad Vltavou Ski Resort and the Hochficht Ski Area (in Austria) can both be reached by car in 30 minutes.
