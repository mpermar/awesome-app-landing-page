Description

All rooms and suites are air conditioned and feature a balcony and a private bathroom including bathrobes and slippers.

You can choose from a wide range of spa and recreational facilities, like an indoor pool, a sauna, steam room, cardio equipment, tennis courts and bowling alleys.

Catering services include the Sport Bar, the Bowling Bar and the Tetu Brissy Restaurant serving Czech and international cuisine. You can also treat yourself to a visit in the Beauty Salon.

Průhonice Castle, the Průhonice Dendrological Garden and the Cestlice Water Park Aquapalace are a 5-minute drive away. Prague is easily reachable via exit 6 of the D1 highway.

Underground parking for 200 cars is available on site.

Prague 10 is a great choice for travelers interested in monuments, sightseeing and old-town exploration.
