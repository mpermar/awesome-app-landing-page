Description

Located amidst nature within the venue of the PilsnerGolf Resort Hořehledy and featuring a 9-hole golf course, Hotel Pod Kokšínem offers free WiFi access, a terrace and evening entertainment.

All rooms are fitted with a seating area with a flat-screen TV, a radio and a dining table, a private bathroom with free toiletries.

Views of the town and the golf course can be enjoyed from all the rooms. Breakfast is served in the morning and the hotel's restaurant offers international cuisine.

A bar is to be found on site where guests can go for a drink. A ticket service and a tour desk are also provided.

The Pod Kokšínem Hotel offers bicycle storage and many cycling tracks can be found in the vicinity.

The Pořící and the Kozel Castles can be visited 2 and 5 km away respectively. Swimming is possible in an outdoor pool 7 km away or in the Štěnovice quarry 15 km off.

Vehicles can be parked at the hotel for no fee and shuttle services can be arranged upon request.
