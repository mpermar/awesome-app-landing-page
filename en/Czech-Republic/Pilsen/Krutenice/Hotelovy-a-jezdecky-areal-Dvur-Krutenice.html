Description

Located on a horse farm, the Hotelový a jezdecký areál Dvůr Krutěnice offers horseback riding, a restaurant on site and a wellness centre; free WiFi access is available.

Some room types come with a private bathroom with a shower or a bathtub. Some room types have shared bathroom facilities; a shared kitchen and a shared lounge are available to guests in these rooms.

The on-site restaurant serves Czech cuisine and breakfast is provided every morning.

A grocery shop can be found 500 meters from Hotel and Riding Area Dvůr Krutěnice.

Guests can enjoy an array of activities on site, including a miniature golf, an outdoor pool, a tennis court, a sauna, massages and sport fishing in a private pond.

A garden with a terrace can be relaxed in and children can roam on a playground. Cycling and hiking trails can be found in the vicinity.

The Aquapark Horaždovice can be reached in 20 minutes by car and the Mystic Skatepark Sušice is 16 km away.

The town of Klatovy is located 20 km from the hotel and the Rabí Castle is 19 km away.

The Ústaleč and the Sedlečko Bus Stops are located 500 metres away. Free parking is possible on site.
