Description

Hotel Jitřenka is situated in the spa village of Konstantinovy Lázně and offers rooms with free Wi-Fi.

You can sample Czech cuisine at the on-site restaurant. The local thermal baths are just 50 metres away.

All rooms feature satellite TV and a seating area. Each room has a bathroom with a hairdryer.

Rooms for disabled guests are available. You can read a book in the hotel’s small library.

A playground is provided and Hotel Jitřenka has a 24-hour front desk. You can park your car free of charge in front of the building.

Garage parking is available against an additional surcharge. Many cycling paths and hiking trails can be found in the surrounding area.

Meal plans are available at the on-site restaurant. Restaurants and shops are situated nearby and the closest train station is 500 metres away.
