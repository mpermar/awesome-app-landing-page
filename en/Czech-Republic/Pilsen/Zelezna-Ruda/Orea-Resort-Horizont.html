Description

Orea Resort Horizont is located on the southern slope of Pancíř Hill. It offers an indoor swimming pool and a terrace with panoramic views.

Wellness facilities can be enjoyed free of charge. Free WiFi is available. A satellite TV and telephone are provided in all rooms at Orea Resort Horizont.

Each room has an electronic safe and toiletries are provided in the bathroom. National dishes are served at the restaurant and drinks are available from the bar.

Breakfast is buffet style and Wi-Fi is free in public areas. Spa facilities include a hot tub, sauna, steam bath and solarium.

Guests can exercise in the gym or book a massage treatment. An outdoor mini golf course and table tennis are also available.

The Orea Resort Horizont is located in Železná Ruda, within the Šumava National Park.
