Description

Featuring free WiFi, a restaurant and a children's playground, Hotel SRC Lihovar offers accommodation in Třemošnice.

The hotel has a terrace and views of the mountains, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Free private parking is available on site. Some units feature views of the garden or city.

There is room service, ticket service, a tour desk and hairdresser's at the property.

The area is popular for cycling and fishing. Hradec Králové is 55 km from Hotel SRC Lihovar, while Pardubice is 34 km from the property.
