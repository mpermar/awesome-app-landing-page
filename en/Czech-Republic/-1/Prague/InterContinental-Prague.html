Description

All spacious and air-conditioned rooms come with high-quality furniture and luxurious fabrics. A minibar as well as a safe are at guests' disposal. Some units also feature a sitting area.

The Health Club and Spa is accessible for free and offers a full range of relaxing and energizing massages, a salt-water swimming pool and a gym with the latest Technogym equipment.

Gourmet cuisine is served in the Zlatá Praha restaurant on the top floor. A family brunch is served on Sundays, and the rooftop terrace offers casual fare and cocktails in the summer.

At Duke’s Bar&Café, which looks out to Paris street, guests can have lunch or simply a cup of coffee with a delicious dessert.

Located a quarter mile from the nearest metro station, The InterContinental Prague is in a convenient location; the main places of interest are easily accessible by public transportation and by foot. Old Town Square with the Orloj Astronomical Clock is a quarter mile away, and Wenceslas Square is within a 12-minute walk.

Prague 01 is a great choice for travelers interested in history, architecture and culture.
