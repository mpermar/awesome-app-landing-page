Description

Pension Quest is a newly reconstructed house next to the castle and offers accommodation in Loket town set only a 10-minute drive from the Karlovy Vary Spa Town.

A flat-screen TV with cable channels and blu-ray player, as well as a computer and a CD player are offered.

Some rooms include a seating area for your convenience. Rooms come with a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower and shower.

Air conditioning is available for a surcharge. Guests with little children can also make use of a mobile baby monitor with a camera, available for a surcharge.

Bicycle rental is available at this guest house and the area is popular for cycling.

A range of activities is offered in the area, such as canoeing and hiking. Castle Loket is 100 metres from Pension Quest, while Church of St.

Wenceslas is 100 metres away. The nearest airport is Karlovy Vary Airport, 12 km from the property.
