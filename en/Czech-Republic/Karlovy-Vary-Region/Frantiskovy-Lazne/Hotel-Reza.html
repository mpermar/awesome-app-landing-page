Description

Centrally located in Františkovy Lázně, Hotel Reza is 250 metres away from the Spa Colonnade and provides guests drinking water from its own spring, Erika.

This 180-year-old hotel in typical Art-Nouveau spa-town style is a few minutes from the business centre of Cheb.

Apart from nicely furnished rooms and an inviting restaurant, the Reza features a rehabilitation swimming pool, a sauna, salt cave and a spa centre providing a wide range of procedures.

An in-house hairdresser and pedicure services are available as well. Starting from summer 2013, guests can enjoy a heated outdoor swimming pool with terrace and sun beds.
