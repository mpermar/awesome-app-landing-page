Description

The Carlsbad Plaza is a luxury Medical Spa & Wellness hotel located in the quiet ambience of the spa zone of Karlovy Vary.

The renowned Spring Colonnade is 600 meters away. All rooms feature garden view or a view of the quiet town spa zone.

All spacious rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV, coffee machine, free Wi-Fi, air-conditioning, pillow and cosmetic menu.

The Carlsbad Plaza Medical Spa & Wellness hotel features a 3500 m² medical and wellness area with an indoor pool, whirlpool, 7 different saunas, anatomically shaped massage pool and relaxation zone.

The unique treatment concept takes advantage of traditional procedures as well as of natural thermal mineral water treatments.

More than 250 types of treatments are available at the Medical Spa Center, including the unique concept of medical spa treatment provided in the absolute privacy of the Medical Spa Suites or the Medical Majestic Suite.

A team of doctors is available at the Carlsbad Clinic. The Carlsbad Plaza offers the whole-body cryotherapy, cosmetic procedures at the Beauty Lounge, as well as different kinds of classic and Thai massages.

There are 2 onsite restaurants offering international cuisine. The onsite bar features live music.
