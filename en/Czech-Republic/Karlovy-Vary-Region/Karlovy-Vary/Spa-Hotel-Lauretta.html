Description

Lauretta Hotel is set within 200 metres from the Orthodox Church and the Spa Colonnade in Karlovy Vary.

You can also unwind on the summer terrace, during a sauna visit or a massage. All rooms come with a TV and a private bathroom with a bath or shower.

They have a pastel shades and patterned carpets, while offering views of the surrounding area.

The Lauretta Hotel is close to numerous shops and restaurants. The nearest golf course is 3 km away.

Skiing opportunities are located in Bozi Dar, 25 km away. The Main Train Station is 1.

5 km away, while the Bus Station is 1 km away. Karlovy Vary Airport can be reached within 10 km.
