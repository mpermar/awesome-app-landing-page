Description

Pension Lukas is a family hotel located in a peaceful, green area of Taovice, an 8-minute drive from Karlovy Vary town centre.

A seasonal swimming pool with a terrace is available from spring to autumn for free.

Guests can also relax in a sauna for a surcharge. The rooms are fitted with a minibar.

Breakfast is served on site, including fresh bread rolls, and selection of cheeses, ham, jams and eggs.

Tea or coffee on offer. A fitness centre, a restaurant, a table tennis, a children's playground as well as a spacious garden with barbecue facilities is also available on site.

Guests receive a 15% discount at the Spa Albtiny Lzn in Karlovy Vary. Free parking is available on site as is free WiFi.
