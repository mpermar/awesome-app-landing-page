Description

Featuring a spa centre and free WiFi, Luxury Spa Hotel Olympic Palace, which was opened in August 2015, offers luxury accommodation in Karlovy Vary.

The hotel features a salt cave, wellness centre with mineral water, sun terrace and views of the garden.

Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site restaurant. All rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Certain units have a seating area to relax in after a busy day. A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms.

For your comfort, you will find slippers and free toiletries. A 24-hour front desk is at guests' disposal.

Mill Colonnade is 100 metres from Luxury Spa Hotel Olympic Palace, while Market Colonnade is 200 metres away.
