Description

Located in Karlovy Vary’s Březová region, Hotel St Michael sits next to the river Teplá close to many biking and hiking trails.

A bus stops in front of the property and provides transport to the town’s spa center 3.

4 km away. The hotel was completely renovated in 2015. Rooms at the St. Michael have a bathroom, seating area and TV.

Breakfast is served each morning, and WiFi is available throughout the hotel free of charge.

Bicycles can be rented against a charge, and 2 parking lots are available on site free of charge.

Tennis courts and a water dam can be visited 1. 5 km away, and the Cihelny golf course can be reached by car in 5 minutes.
