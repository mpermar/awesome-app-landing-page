Description

This 4-star hotel is located in Karlovy Vary spa area, less than 10 minutes’ walk from St.

Mary Magdalene Church. It offers varied spa treatments, free WiFi access and al fresco dining.

Spa Hotel Schlosspark’s extensive on-site spa offers a sauna and wide variety of treatments including body wraps and aromatic massages.

Hotel facilities also include a sun terrace. A fitness centre and an indoor pool are available free of charge.

The air-conditioned guest rooms feature cable TV and a seating area. Each includes a work desk and en suite, tiled bathroom with bathrobe.

Al fresco dining is offered on Hotel Schlosspark’s terrace, where international and traditional Czech dishes are available.

Guests can enjoy evening cocktails and music at the lobby bar. Guests can reach the hot springs in Mlynska Kolonada (Mills Colonnade) and many of Karlovy Vary’s shops within a 10 minutes’ walk.
