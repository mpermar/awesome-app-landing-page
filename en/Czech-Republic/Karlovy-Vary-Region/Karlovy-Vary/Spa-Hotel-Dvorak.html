Description

Elegant art nouveau façades adorn the six houses that make up the Spa Hotel Dvořák in Karlovy Vary.

The hotel has a superb location, directly by the Teplá river on the 'drinking colonnades' where the healing mineral spring rises.

The centerpiece of the hotel is the spa and wellness area. All air-conditioned rooms include satellite flat-screen TV with pay-per-view channels, a safety deposit box, a minibar, and a bathroom fitted with a bath or a shower, a hairdryer and bathrobes.

Wired internet is available in all of the traditionally furnished rooms free of charge.

The Czech master chef and his team in Restaurant Dvořák, are accomplished at combining Bohemian with international cuisine.

Should guests have any special requests and want to get into shape during their stay, low-fat and low-calorie dishes made from healthy ingredients are readily available.

In the evening, the Bar & Cafe Opera, concerts and the hotel pianist offer a relaxing atmosphere for guests to enjoy, along with a selection of coffees and the traditional glass of Becherovka.

Spa Hotel Dvořák specializes in preventive medicine. The in-house spa doctor and his team of therapists and masseurs provide guests with an individual spa treatment and diet programme.

The programme is mostly aimed at improving the nutrient supply of the organism and stimulating the metabolism.

A beautiful indoor pool, sauna, steam room, gym and sports programme round off the selection of activities in the hotel.

Paid parking is available on site. St. Mary Magdalene’s Church can be reached within 350 metres.
