Description

Surrounded by botanical gardens and lakes, this spa hotel is in Mariánské Lázně and specializes in wellness treatments.

There is a summer terrace overlooking the gardens. Spacious rooms offer garden views and en-suite facilities.

Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel. The Luna Park restaurant serves Czech cuisine in a traditional setting.

Guests can also enjoy popular dance afternoons in the restaurant. Free sun beds are available and there is a children’s playground.

Karlovy Vary Airport is a 20-minute drive away and free parking is available for guests.
