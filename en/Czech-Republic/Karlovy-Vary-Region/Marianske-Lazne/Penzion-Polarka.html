Description

Set in the spa town of Marianske Lazne close to the Rudolf Spring, this guest house offers en-suite accommodation with satellite TV, free Wi-Fi and hardwood floors.

Penzion Polarka is a good base to visit the Kladska nature reserve, which is 9 km away, or the castles of Kynzvart (9 km) and Becov, 25 km from the Polarka.

In the summertime guests can go cycling on the numerous trails. In the wintertime, a cross-country ski trail is 2 km from Penzion Polarka.

Apart from numerous spa treatments, guests can go swimming at public outdoor pool or play tennis or golf in Marianske Lazne.
