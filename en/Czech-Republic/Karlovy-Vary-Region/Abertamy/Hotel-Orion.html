Description

All rooms at the guesthouse are equipped with a seating area. Featuring a private bathroom, rooms at Hotel Orion also offer free WiFi. Guest rooms will provide guests with a dishwasher.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at the property.

The wellness area at the accommodation is comprised of a sauna and an indoor pool. Guests at Hotel Orion will be able to enjoy activities in and around Abertamy, like skiing and cycling.

Pstruží is one mile from the guesthouse. The nearest airport is Karlovy Vary Airport, 11 miles from Hotel Orion.
