Description

Offering views of the mountains, Apartments Stein is set in Boží Dar. Skiareál Neklid is 400 metres away.

Free WiFi is provided and private parking is available on site. All units have a seating area.

There is also a kitchenette in all units, equipped with a microwave. There is a private bathroom with a shower in every unit.

A ski school and bike hire are available 100 metres from the property. You can play tennis at the property, and the area is popular for skiing and cycling.

Novako Ski Resort is 500 meters from Apartments Stein, while Klement Cable Car is 1.

8 km from the property. The nearest airport is Karlovy Vary International Airport, 23 km from the property.
