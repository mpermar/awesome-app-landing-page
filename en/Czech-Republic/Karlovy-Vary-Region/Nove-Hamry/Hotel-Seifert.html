Description

Hotel Seifert is located in the centre of the peaceful village of Nove Hamry in the Ore Mountains.

It offers free Wi-Fi and free parking. All rooms in the Seifert Hotel have an en-suite bathroom, a satellite TV and panoramic views.

There is an outdoor sauna, garden with a children`s playground, as well as a campsite.

Various Czech specialities, international cuisine and a wide range of wines from the well-stocked cellar are served in the restaurant.

Nove Hamry has 1 ski slope and provides lots of hiking and mountain biking paths.
