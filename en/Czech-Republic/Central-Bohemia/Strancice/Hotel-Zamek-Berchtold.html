Description

The palatial looking Hotel Zamek Berchtold enjoys peaceful surroundings in Strancice.

The property has a hot tub as well as cosmetic, manicure and rehabilitation facilities.

The hotel grounds have a children’s playground with a fairy trail and a petting zoo with goats, rabbits and other animals.

Rooms at Hotel Zamek Berchtold have wood furniture, a private bathroom, satellite TV and views of the surrounding parklands.

Several have elegant furnishings and Wi-Fi is available free of charge in all. The hotel’s restaurant serves traditional Czech and Italian cuisine.

Food and drink can be enjoyed in the elegantly furnished dining area or on the summer terrace.

The hotel also offers massages, and a golf simulator can be used on site. Cycling and hiking are popular activities in the region.

Shops, restaurants, bars and cafes can be visited in Velke Popovice, 4 km away. The city also has a brewery and famous park.

Ricany u Prahy can be reached by car in 15 minutes and Prague is a 30-minute drive away.
