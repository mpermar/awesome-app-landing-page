Description

Hotel Grand is located in the historic town centre and offers an a la carte restaurant and a fitness centre; free WiFi access is provided.

The rooms feature a seating area with a satellite TV, a safe deposit box, a minibar and a private bathroom fitted with a hairdryer.

Guests can start their day with a breakfast served in the hotel and enjoy a drink in an on-site bar.

Within 50 metres, a minimarket can be reached to buy food supplies as well as a café.

Hotel Grand offers luggage storage. The Václav Havel Airport Prague is located 21 km away and the centre of Prague can be reached in 15 minutes by car by the D5 expressway.

Free parking is possible on site and underground parking is available on request at a surcharge.
