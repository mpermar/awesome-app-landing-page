Description

Pension Primus is located a 10-minute walk from Beroun's historic centre and offers panoramic views of the Beroun Valley.

It provides free Wi-Fi and a garden with an outdoor swimming pool and barbecue area.

The spacious rooms at Pension Primus Beroun feature classic décor. All rooms include a flat-screen TV with satellite channels as well as a private bathroom.

A continental breakfast is served each morning in bright breakfast room. Guests receive a 10% discount at the Carino restaurant in the city centre, 5 minutes away by car or 15 minutes on foot.

Pension Primus has a children’s playground and is just 800 metres from an inline skating route.

It is 1 km from Beroun Aqua Park and 2. 5 km from Beroun Golf Course. Secure parking is free at Pension Primus.

It is 300 metres from Beroun Train Station and 500 metres from Beroun Bus Station.
