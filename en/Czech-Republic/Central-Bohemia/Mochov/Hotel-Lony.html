Description

In a small village of Kozovazy, Hotel Lony is a 30-minutes drive from Prague and offers rooms and apartments with satellite TV.

Sauna and hot tub facilities, as well as massage treatments, are available for a charge.

Free Wi-Fi is provided in the entire property. Decorated in warm colours, all units come with a seating area and a refrigerator.

Each private bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries, and the apartment also features a sofa and a fully equipped kitchenette.

The à-la-carte restaurant serves modern Czech cuisine. In warmer weather, guests can also relax on the terrace with barbecue facilities, while children can enjoy their moments on the playground.

Přerov nad Labem, which has one of the oldest open air museums in Central and Eastern Europe, is 8 km away.

Čelákovice and Lysa nad Labem are 7 and 10 km from the Lony Hotel. O2 Arena can be reached within 25 km.
