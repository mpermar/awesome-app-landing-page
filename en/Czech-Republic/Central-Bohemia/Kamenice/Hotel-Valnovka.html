Description

Located near a forest in Kamenice-Ládví, Hotel Valnovka offers free WiFi and free private parking.

A swimming pool is available on prior request. A bus stop can be found within 150 metres.

The rooms at the Valnovka all come with a private bathroom with a shower, a TV and a seating area.

They also feature a forest view. The property also houses an à la carte restaurant serving traditional Czech cuisine.

Breakfast is available to all guests and half and full board can be arranged on request.

Various cycling trails start directly at the hotel. Štiřín Golf Course is a 7-minute drive away and Berchtold Castle can be reached within 13 km.
