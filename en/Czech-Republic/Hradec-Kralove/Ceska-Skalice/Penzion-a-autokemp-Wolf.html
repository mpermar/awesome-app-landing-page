Description

Located 700 metres from the Rozkoš Dam Lake in a quiet area of Kleny, Penzion a autokemp Wolf offers a sauna, a garden with barbecue facilities, free WiFi and free private parking.

A bike rental and a children´s playground can also be found on-site. The colourfully furnished units at the Wolf all feature a fridge and come with a shared or a private bathroom with a shower.

A restaurant can be reached within 30 metres and a grocery shop is 2 km away. At the Penzion, a fully equipped kitchen is available to all guests.

Penzion a autokemp Wolf is situated a 4-minute drive from Ratibořice Castle and the protected area of Babiččino údolí.

The Kudowa Zdroj Spa and Aquapark can be reached within 14 km. A bus stop can be found within 100 metres of the property.
