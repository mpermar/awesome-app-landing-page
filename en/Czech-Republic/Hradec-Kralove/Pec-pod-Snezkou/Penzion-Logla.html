Description

Peacefully situated overlooking Pec pod Snežkou, 800 metres from the ski resort and 200 metres from a smaller ski slope, Penzion Logla serves a buffet breakfast and dinner, and features a garden with small seasonal pool and barbecue facilities.

Guests can also relax in the bar, while children can play outside on the playground.

Wi-Fi is available in the entire building for free. Other on-site facilities include ski and bicycle storage room, as well as free private parking.

Children's pool is available too and Russian bowling can be played for free. All rooms at the family-run Logla offer a view of the surrounding landscape.

They are functionally furnished and come with a TV. Each unit also comprise a private bathroom.

The town centre is 500 metres away. Among all, a grocery store, massage and cosmetic treatments and wellness centre can be found there.
