Description

Featuring an indoor pool, a spa and wellness centre, Hotel Říčky offers suites with free WiFi access, a restaurant on site and a terrace.

Free parking is possible at the hotel. All suites at Hotel Říčky feature a satellite TV in the living room and a terrace.

The bathroom comes with a bathtub or a shower and free toiletries. Mountain views can be taken delight in from all the suites.

There is breakfast served every morning and the hotel's restaurant cooks traditional Czech cuisine.

Drinks can be enjoyed in an on-site bar and packed lunches can also be prepared for guests.

In their leisure time, guests can go horseback riding, play tennis or table tennis, go skiing, swim in the hotel's pool or exercise in the on-site fitness room.

A sauna and a hot tub can be used and massages can be requested. Children can enjoy the playground, have fun in a kids' club or learn skiing in a ski school.
