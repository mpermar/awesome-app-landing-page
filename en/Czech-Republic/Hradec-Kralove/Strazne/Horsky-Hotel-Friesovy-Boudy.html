Description

On the top of a mountain in the heart of the Krkonoše National Park, Horský Hotel Friesovy Boudy is right next to the Friesovy Boudy Ski Resort.

It features a wellness centre, an indoor children’s playground and a games room. Free Wi-Fi is available in the entire hotel.

The wellness facilities include a Finnish sauna and a steam bath, a hot tub, a large pool and a small pool for children, and massage treatments.

In the games room, Playstation 3, Nintendo Wii and other games are provided, as well as 4 computer stations.

Guests can benefit from direct access to the ski slopes and to the marked cross-country ski trails.

There is also a ski school on site. All rooms in the Friesovy Boudy Horský Hotel are decorated in modern style, with bright colours and light wooden furnishings.

Each room comes with a TV and a minibar, and comprises a private bathroom with a shower.

The à-la-carte restaurant serves a buffet breakfast and Czech cuisine. Special diet menus are available upon request.

Guests can also relax in a café and a bar. The Sněžka Mountain and the town of Vrchlabí are both 6 km away.

In summer, private parking is possible on site, and in winter, a transport by a snowmobile is organised.
