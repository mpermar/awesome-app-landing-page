Description

The Orea Resort Horal in the Krkonoše or Giant Mountains is located close to Špindlerův Mlýn; the nearest ski lift is just steps away.

It offers panoramic views, breakfast and wellness center. From the summer terrace you can enjoy the panoramic views of the stunning scenery, which is a beautiful place for hikers, bikers and all nature lovers.

After a day in the mountains you can relax in one of the Orea Resort Horal's restaurants and bars.
