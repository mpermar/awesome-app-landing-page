Description

Hotel Adam features a free indoor pool, well-equipped fitness centre. Sauna and wellness centre are available for an extra charge.

It rents out ski equipment and also offers ski storage. Svatý Petr ski resort is 200 metres away.

All rooms are spacious and decorated with modern furniture. Each one is equipped with a flat-screen TV and minibar.

Bathrobes can be provided at the reception against a deposit. Relaxation facilities at Adam Hotel include a hot tub, steam spa and a games room.

The hotel features an on-site ski school. Guests can try sports activities such as table tennis, cycling and darts.
