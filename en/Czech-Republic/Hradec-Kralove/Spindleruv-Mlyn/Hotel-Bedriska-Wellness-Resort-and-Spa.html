Description

Hotel Bedriska is located in the mountain resort of Spindleruv Mlyn, 400 metres from the Medvědín Ski Resort and a 10-minute walk from the town centre, and provides a wellness area.

It is quietly situated and features a spacious garden with views of the Giant Mountains.

Free WiFi is available. Guests will receive a free return ticket for a cable car from spring until autumn.

The property houses 2 restaurants and a tennis court. Spa area including a pool, hot tub, Finnish sauna, steam sauna, relax zone and cooling program is also provided.

Massages, a solarium and a wellness bar are offered as well. In summer, guests can unwind in the garden restaurant with an outdoor grill and a playground for children.

The restaurant serves various international specialities, mainly prepared on the grill.

A direct ski bus will take you from the hotel to the St. Peter Ski Resort which is located 1 km from the hotel.

Between 1st May and 31st October, guests will daily receive a free return ticket for a cable car.
