Description

This 3-star hotel is located in the centre of Vrchlabí in the Giant Mountains. A shopping centre and a spa area are located in the same building.

All rooms at Hotel Gendorf feature satellite TV, a seating area, and a bathroom with hairdryer.

The Gendorf’s restaurant serves Czech and international cuisine. The spa area includes a sauna, a jacuzzi, and an infrared cabin.

Massages are also available. The Vrchlabí ski area is just 1 km away, and Spindleruv Mlyn is 15 km away.
