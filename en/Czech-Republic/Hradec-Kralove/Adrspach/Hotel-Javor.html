Description

Set in the protected natural reserve of Adrspach-Teplice Rocks in north-eastern Bohemia, Hotel Javor features a summer terrace and en suite accommodation with satellite TV and seating areas.

The air-conditioned restaurant has a cosy open fireplace and a non-smoking bar with a lounge area.

Free Wi-Fi access is provided in the restaurant and the bar. The summer terrace offers views over the Adrspach-Teplice Rocks, which are unusual sandstone formations.

They can be accessed by hiking trails and are popular among rock climbers. The Javor offers a playground for children with a sand pit and a slide.

Guests can spend their time by actively going cycling, horseback riding or playing paint ball and tennis in the vicinity.

Located approximately 400 metres from the Adrspach train station, Javor Hotel also offers free private parking on site.
