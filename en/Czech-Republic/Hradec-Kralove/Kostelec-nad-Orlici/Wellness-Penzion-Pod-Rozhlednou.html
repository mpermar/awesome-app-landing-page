Description

Featuring free WiFi and a spa center and a seasonal outdoor pool, Wellness Penzion Pod Rozhlednou offers pet-friendly accommodation in Vrbice, 4 km from Kostelce nad Orlicí.

The wellness area includes a sauna, a hot tub and a hammam, all for a surcharge. Guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Free private parking is available on site. Rooms have a flat-screen TV and kitchenette.

Some rooms include a seating area to relax in after a busy day. Rooms are fitted with a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower.

This guest house has ski storage space and ski equipment hire is available. Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including horse riding, cycling and fishing.

The guest house also offers bike hire. Kudowa-Zdrój is 39 km from Wellness Penzion Pod Rozhlednou, while Duszniki Zdrój is 36 km from the property.
