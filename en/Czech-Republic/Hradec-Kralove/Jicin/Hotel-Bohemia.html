Description

Hotel Bohemia is situated in a quiet location 500 meters from the center of Jičín and 1 km from the train and bus station Jičín.

The hotel offers free parking and free Wi-Fi. The hotel features garden with children's playground.

The spacious rooms have a minibar, a seating area, cable TV and a private bathroom.

Massages are available on request. Kníže Swimming Pool is located 200m from the hotel, Prachovské Rocks are 5 km away and 13 km from Humprecht Castle, Kost Castle is 16 km away.
