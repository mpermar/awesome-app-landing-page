Description

In a quiet location in Velichovky, 7 km from Jaroměř, the K-Triumf Resort offers a wellness centre, fitness room, sauna, indoor and outdoor tennis courts, a squash court, outdoor beach volleyball court, outdoor pool, bowling.

meeting rooms and restaurant. Free Wi-Fi is available in all areas of the hotel. Rooms at the K-Triumf Resort feature a TV, minibar, seating area and a bathroom with toiletries and a hairdryer.

The restaurant serves traditional Czech cuisine as well as game specialities. Guests can play billiards, table tennis, and darts at the property, and it is possible to see the dressage of parkour horses 100 metres away.

Bicycles can be rented at the property. The garden includes a children’s playground.

Free private parking is available on site. A private shuttle bus is available on request.
