Description

Featuring an outdoor pool and a modern restaurant with international cuisine, Penzion Za Vodou is a guest house set peacefully 1.

5 km away from the centre of Dvůr Králové. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the property and free on-site parking is provided.

All of the rooms at Za Vodou are furnished in modern style and offer a seating area.

Amenities include a private tiled bathroom and a TV. A large garden surrounds the house and includes a barbecue terrace, a playground with kids' pool and an outdoor bar.

Other sports facilities include a mini golf, a football pitch, a basketball court and a putting green.

Many marked cycling paths are available in the immediate surroundings. A local bus stops directly in front of the guest house.

Dvůr Králové Bus Station is 1 km away. Těšnov Dam is 6 km away, and the Baroque Kuks Castle can be reached within 8 km.
