Description

Just 600 metres from the main square of Trutnov, Hotel Alfa enjoys a quiet location in a residential district.

It offers free WiFi, en-suite rooms with satellite TV, common room with soft and alcoholic drinks.

The garden includes barbecue facilities and a seating area. Free private parking is available on site.

The bus station is 500 metres from the Alfa Hotel, and Trutnov Train Station is 600 metres away.

The Černá Hora Ski Area is 15 km away, and it is 21 km to the Pec pod Sněžkou Ski Area.
