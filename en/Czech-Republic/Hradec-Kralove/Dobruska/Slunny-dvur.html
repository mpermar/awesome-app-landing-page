Description

Located in Dobruska, 5 km from the Opočno Chateau, Slunný dvůr offers a restaurant and a garden with barbecue facilities and a children’s playground.

Free Wi-Fi is provided in the entire building and free public parking is possible on site.

The on-site restaurant offers Czech cuisine. Breakfast can be served every morning.

The rooms include a private bathroom and a TV. Lake Rozkos is 10 km away and Nové Město nad Metují with its Chateau is 8 km from the property.
