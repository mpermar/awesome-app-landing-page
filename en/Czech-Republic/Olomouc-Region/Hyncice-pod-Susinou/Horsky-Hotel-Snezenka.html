Description

Horský Hotel Sněženka is located in the Králičák Ski Area, 180 meters from the slopes.

Guests can relax in the property’s wellness centre featuring a sauna, hot tub, heated indoor swimming pool and offers massages.

Rooms contain wooden furniture and brightly coloured walls. Each has a private bathroom with a bath or shower.

The summer is ideal for guests to use the outdoor swimming pool and terrace. Table tennis, barbecue and ski storage facilities are also offered to guests.

Packed lunches, bicycles and laundry services can be arranged on request. Horský Hotel Sněženka has a private bowling alley and a restaurant serving regional cuisine and a buffet breakfast.

Free of charge, guests have access to private parking and Wi-Fi in public areas of the hotel.

Chrastice Train Station is located 4 km away. Stare Mesto can be reached by car in 10 minutes.
