Description

Rezidence u Jezera is set by a lake in Písečná, within a 10-minute drive from Jeseník, and offers an à la carte restaurant with a fireplace, an own winery and a terrace.

Free WiFi is provided in all areas and a free unique Finish sauna next to a lake can be enjoyed.

All rooms at the U Jezera’s come with rustic décor elements. Each unit comprises a balcony with a view of the surrounding landscape, and contains satellite TV and private bathroom.

Some of the rooms include a spa bath. The restaurant offers game specialties, fish dishes and vegetarian cuisine.

Breakfast is served every morning. Guests can enjoy massage treatments at an additional cost.

Billiards can be played for free. On the lake, water scooter rides, fishing and rowing is possible.

Rental of scooters is also available. Guests can make use of discounts on water skiing.

The skiing resorts of Filipovice and Zlaté Hory - Příčná are both reachable in 10 km.
