Description

Situated in Jeseník in the Olomouc Region Region, this hotel is 28 km from Lądek-Zdrój.

The hotel has a barbecue and children's playground, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Some units include a seating area to relax in after a busy day. Rooms come with a private bathroom.

A flat-screen TV is provided An array of activities are offered in the area, such as skiing and hiking.

Ski storage is available at the property. Nysa is 30 km from Hotel STARÝ MLÝN, while Kłodzko is 48 km from the property.
