Description

Located on a south-facing hill right under Jeseník spa and offering panoramic views of Hrubý Jeseník, Hotel Slunný Dvůr offers an on-site restaurant, a wellness area and free WiFi.

The Jeseník Ski Slope is 300 metres away. The accommodation was built in the area of the First Republic.

All accommodation units feature modern furniture and a bathroom with shower. They offer views either of the surrounding mountains or the Priessnitz Spa Resort.

Children’s playground and barbecue facilities are set in the garden, and the restaurant provides local and international dishes every day.

Breakfast is also available. Guests can enjoy a wellness centre and free private parking.

The Spicak Ski Area is 10 km away, while Cervenohorske sedlo Winter Sport Region can be reached in a 20-minute drive.
