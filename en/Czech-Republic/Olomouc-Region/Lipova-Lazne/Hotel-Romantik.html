Description

Offering a children's playground and ski-to-door access, Hotel Romantik is situated in Lipova Lazne in the Olomouc Region Region, 8 km from Ramzová Ski Resort.

The hotel has a sun terrace and ski storage space, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Some units have a seating area for your convenience. Hotel Romantik features free WiFi throughout the property.

A flat-screen TV is offered. You will find a shared lounge at the property. Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including skiing and cycling.

Jeseník Spa is 6 km from the property. The nearest ski slope with a cableway and a ski school for children is located 300 metres from the property.
