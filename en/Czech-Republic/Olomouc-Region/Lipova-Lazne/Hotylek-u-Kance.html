Description

Situated in Lipova Lazne and with Miroslav C-Family reachable within 1. 1 km, Hotýlek u Kance features a garden, non-smoking rooms, free WiFi and a terrace.

Boasting a kids' club, this property also welcomes guests with a restaurant and a children's playground.

Miroslav B-Family is 1. 1 km away and Lazensky vrch - Lipová Lázně is 1. 8 km from the guest house.

At the guest house, rooms come with a wardrobe, a TV and a private bathroom. Hotýlek u Kance offers a continental or à la carte breakfast.

Skiing and cycling are among the activities that guests of the accommodation can find nearby.

Popular points of interest near Hotýlek u Kance include Ski Center Miroslav, Miroslav D-Baby and Miroslav A-Sports.
