Description

Located in Lipova Lazne in the Olomouc Region Region, 700 metres from the nearest ski lift, Hotel Toč features a sauna and ski storage space.

The hotel has a seasonal outdoor pool and children's playground, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Certain units include a seating area where you can relax. Every room comes with a private bathroom.

A flat-screen TV is provided. You will find a shared lounge and gift shop at the property.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including skiing and cycling.
