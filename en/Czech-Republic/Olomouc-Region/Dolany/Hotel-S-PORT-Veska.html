Description

Offering an à-la-carte restaurant, a fitness centre, tennis courts and a playground, the stylish Hotel S-PORT Véska is located next to the golf course, 10 km from the city of Olomouc.

Free Wi-Fi access is available and you can park your car for free on site. Rooms here will provide you with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with a hairdryer and towels. At Hotel S-PORT Véska you will find a garden and a terrace.

The front desk is open form 7 AM till 10 PM. Other facilities offered include luggage storage and a children's playground.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling and hiking.
