Description

Located in a quiet area, 9 km from the centre of Kroměříž, Pivovarský Hotel offers rooms with free Wi-Fi.

The on-site restaurant serves Czech specialties. In summer, you can also dine on the terrace.

All rooms are equipped with a TV and a seating area. Each room has a bathroom with a shower or a tub.

Rooms for disabled guests are available. Each day, a continental breakfast is served at Pivovarský Hotel.

Packed lunches can be requested and bicycles can be rented on site. The hotel features a garden with BBQ facilities.

A small souvenir shop is situated on site. The closest outdoor swimming pool is 600 metres away and a tennis court can be reached within a 5-minute walk.

Within 2 minutes on foot, a fitness and wellness centre can be reached. The Kroměříž Palace with its UNESCO-protected park is 5 km away.

The motorway D1 can be reached within a 3-minute drive and free private parking is possible on site.
