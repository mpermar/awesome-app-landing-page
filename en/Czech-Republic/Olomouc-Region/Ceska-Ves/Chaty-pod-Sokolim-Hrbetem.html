Description

Set in Česká Ves, Chata pod Sokolím Hřbetem offers a garden and a swimming pool. Jeseník is 5 km from the property.

Free private parking is available on site. Some units feature a dining area and/or terrace.

There is also a kitchen fitted with a dishwasher and oven in some units. The chalet features free WiFi in all areas.

A 25-meter long swimming pool Česká Ves is available throughout the year only 1 km from the chalet.

You can play miniature golf or tennis close to the property and the area is popular for skiing and cycling.

Nysa is 25 km from Chaty pod Sokolím Hřbetem. The nearest airport is Leos Janacek Airport, 90 km from the chalet.
