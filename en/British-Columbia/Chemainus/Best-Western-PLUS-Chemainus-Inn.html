Description

Free Wi-Fi along with a refrigerator and coffee maker are provided in the spacious rooms at Best Western PLUS Chemainus Inn. The bright, warm-colored rooms also include a safe, armchair and desk.

This British Columbia hotel serves a breakfast buffet with waffles and scrambled eggs. The lobby features a fireplace, large flat-screen TV and sofas.

Chemainus Tours, Chemainus Theatre Festival and Fuller Lake are within one mile of Best Western Chemainus Inn. Nanaimo Airport is a 25 minute drive away.
