Description

Free Wi-Fi and a flat-screen TV with cable are available in all luxuriously decorated rooms at Prestige Lakeside Resort. They also feature a private balcony with a view of the lake and mountain.

West Coast Grill, the on-site restaurant, serves fresh cuisine for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. There is also an on-site coffee house.

Guests can fish, swim, and sail in the lake at Resort Prestige Lakeside. The on-site Santosha Spa offers a variety of massages and beauty treatments using organic products. Guests can enjoy free use of sporting equipment items such as bikes, balls, beach toys and games, board games, and more.

Chahko-Mika Mall is 1,000 feet from the resort. Granite Pointe Golf Club is 2.1 mi away.
