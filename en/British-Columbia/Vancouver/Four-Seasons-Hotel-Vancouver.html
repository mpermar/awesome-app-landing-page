Description

A 42” flat-screen TV with cable is offered in all rooms at Four Seasons Hotel Vancouver. An iPod docking station and a desk are provided.

Pacific seafood features on the daily menu served at Yew restaurant. Guests can choose from a large selection of wines in the Yew bar. Free tea and coffee with pastries are offered daily from 5:30am-08:00am.

An on-site fitness center and sauna are featured at Vancouver Four Seasons. Massage facilities are available. A business center is provided for guest convenience.

Stanley Park and BC Place are within 20 minutes’ walk from this hotel. Granville Island is 5 minutes’ drive away.

Any additional older children or adults are charged CAD 40 per person per night when using existing beds.
Free!All children under 4 years stay free of charge in a crib.
One older child or adult is charged CAD 40 per person per night in an extra bed.
The maximum number of extra beds/cribs in a room is 1.
Any type of extra bed or crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Additional fees are not calculated automatically in the total cost and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
