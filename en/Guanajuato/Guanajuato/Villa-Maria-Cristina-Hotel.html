Description

Villa Maria Cristina Relais & Chateaux features an outdoor terrace with a large hot tub. Guests can enjoy a cup of afternoon tea or evening cocktail on the terrace, or go for a swim in the indoor pool. After a busy day of sightseeing, visitors can relax with a massage in the hotel's spa.

The suites at the Maria Cristina Villa features amenities such as high ceilings, hardwood floors and French furniture. Guests will appreciate the whirlpool tub in the suite's marble bathroom after a long day.

On the first floor of the hotel, the restaurant serves international and Mexican cuisine. With dinner, guests can enjoy a fine wine from the hotel's cellar.
