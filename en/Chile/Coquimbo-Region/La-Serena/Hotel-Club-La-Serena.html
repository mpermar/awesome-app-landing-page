Description

Boasting an oceanfront location and a large garden, Hotel Club La Serena offers ocean views and a pool surrounded by green areas.

A buffet breakfast with fruits and pastries is available. Rooms at La Serena feature wooden floors and contemporary décor.

All rooms have 32'' plasma cable TVs and free WiFi, while some rooms offer ocean views and a private balcony.

A tennis court is available for guests' use. La Brisa Restaurant serves meat and fresh seafood specialities, and the beach bar offers exotic cocktails and a happy hour to enjoy on the beach.
