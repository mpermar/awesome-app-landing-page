Description

Located in 4 Esquinas beach, this apart hotel features an outdoor swimming pool, garden, a game room, a children’s playground, and BBQ facilities.

Free Wi-Fi is provided and breakfast is available for a fee. The apartments in Apart Hotel Gran Pacifico feature a kitchenette, dining area, LCD TV, and a terrace.

Guests at Apart Hotel Gran Pacifico can request laundry services. Tours to the main attractions can be arranged at the Hotel.

La Serena mall is 3 km away and the bus station is 5 km away. Plaza de Armas square is 6 km from Apart Hotel Gran Pacifico and the casino is a 10-minute walk away.
