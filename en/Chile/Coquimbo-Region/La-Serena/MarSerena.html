Description

MarSerena Hotel & Cabins is a family hotel built upon a dream; to give to our guests quality accommodation along with friendly warmness.

MarSerena is located in La Serena city, only few steps away from the pacific ocean (50 mts), and in the center of the most touristic area of the beach, surrounded by bars and restaurants.

Rooms and Cabins at MarSerna have private bathroom and are completely furnished featuring a small freezer, microwave, flat screen tv with cable, free Wi Fi.

Each unit, also has a terrace and free private parking. Every morning continental breakfast is served at the lounge.

MarSerena is surrounded by gardens, all areas have free Wi Fi, cold water swimming pool and hot water Jacuzzi beside the pool in the garden.

We are located 2 kilometers away from the Casino, 5 minutes from the city center of La Serena and 10 kilometers from La Florida airport.
