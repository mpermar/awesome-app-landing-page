Description

Featuring free WiFi and a spa centre, Spa Cochiguaz offers accommodation in Cochiguaz, 11 km from La Unión.

The hotel has a seasonal outdoor pool and terrace, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

Free private parking is available on site. Certain rooms feature a seating area where you can relax.

A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms. The rooms include a private bathroom equipped with a bath.

A complimentary breakfast is served for guests staying in rooms. The cottage is equipped with a kitchen but does not offer a complimentary breakfast.

You will find a shared lounge at the property. In addition, guests are welcome to order from the vegetarian menu.

Pisco Elqui is 15 km from Spa Cochiguaz, while Vicuña is 54 km away. The nearest airport is La Florida Airport, 101 km from Spa Cochiguaz.
