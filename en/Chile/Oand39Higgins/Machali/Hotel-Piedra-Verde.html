Description

At the hotel, every room has a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Hotel Piedra Verde provides some rooms with garden views, and every room is equipped with a terrace. All rooms at the accommodation feature air conditioning and a closet.

Guests at Hotel Piedra Verde can enjoy a buffet breakfast. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the in-house restaurant, which specializes in local cuisine.

A variety of popular activities are available in the area around the hotel, including skiing and cycling.

Speaking English and Spanish at the reception, staff are willing to help at any time of the day.

Rancagua is 6 miles from Hotel Piedra Verde. Arturo Merino Benitez Airport is 55 miles away.
