Description

Featuring an outdoor and an indoor pool, Termas de Jahuel Hotel & Spa offers accommodations in Santa María.

Free WiFi access is available here. Errazuriz Vineyard is a 10-minute car ride away.

The spacious rooms at Termas de Jahuel Hotel & Spa are equipped with heating, satellite TV, a private bathroom and a view.

Daily breakfast, lunch and dinner are included. At Termas de Jahuel Hotel & Spa guests will find a spa and wellness centre, a restaurant and a shared lounge.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling, hiking and mini golf.

The property offers free parking. Termas de Jahuel Hotel & Spa is a 30-minute car ride from Los Andes Sanctuary and Portillo Ski Centre.
