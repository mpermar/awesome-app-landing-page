Description

Offering a seasonal outdoor pool, a barbecue and a terrace, Cabañas & Hostelling Carilafquen is situated in Laguna Verde, only 7 km from Cerro Alegre hill.

Free WiFi is available in public areas and private parking is available on site. Cabañas & Hostelling Carilafquen offers 6 luxury cabins and a beautiful and cozy hostelling.

The cabins at Cabañas & Hostelling Carilafquen are equipped with a fully-equipped kitchen with an oven and a toaster, private bathroom facilities with free toiletries and a hairdryer, a seating area, a dining area and a terrace offering sea views.

Units are also fitted with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fireplace, a barbecuem, bed linen and towels.

A complimentary water bottle is provided. Cabañas & Hostelling Carilafquen has a restaurant on site, where guests can savour local flavours and cuisine.

It also has a spa centre, where guests can enjoy massages and treatments. Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including snorkeling, cycling and fishing.

Pablo Neruda's La Sebastiana house museum is only 8 km from Cabañas & Hostelling Carilafquen, while Marina Mercante lookout point is only 9 km from the property.

Valparaíso is 10 km away, Viña del Mar is 16 km away and Algarrobo is 29 km away.
