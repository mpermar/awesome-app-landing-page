Description

Offering sea views and a seasonal outdoor swimming pool, En Casa de Patricia offers accommodation in Laguna Verde, only a 10-minute drive from the nearest beach.

Free private parking is available on site. A complimentary breakfast is served daily.

Rooms at En Casa de Patricia are spacious and luminous, and feature a private bathroom, a minibar, a flat-screen cable TV, bed linen and towels.

Guests at En Casa de Patricia can enjoy a traditional Chilean meal at the property's restaurant, which serves lunch and dinner.

Guests can also relax in the property's sauna or request a Reiki session in advance.

In addition, the property also offers tours and excursions, such as horseback rides, fishing trips and guided treks.

En Casa de Patricia also features a game room, complete with table tennis, a foosball table, and other games.

The property also features a terrace and barbecue facilities. En Casa de Patricia is 3 km from Laguna Verde Beach.
