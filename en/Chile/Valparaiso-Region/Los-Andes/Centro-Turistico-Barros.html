Description

Set in Los Andes in the Valparaíso Region Region, 46 km from Portillo, Complejo Turistico Barros features an outdoor pool and ski-to-door access.

Free private parking is available on site. All units are air conditioned and include a TV with cable channels.

Complejo Turistico Barros features free WiFi. Bed linen is provided. Complejo Turistico Barros also includes a seasonal outdoor pool.

Guests can enjoy the on-site bar and restaurant. The property also offers packed lunches.

The property has a ski pass sales point and ski storage space and free use of bicycles and bike hire are available.

You can play table tennis at the property, and the area is popular for skiing. A number of activities are offered in the area, such as cycling and hiking.
