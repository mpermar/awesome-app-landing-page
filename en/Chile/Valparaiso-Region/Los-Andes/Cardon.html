Description

Set in Los Andes, Cardon features an outdoor swimming pool, garden and shared lounge.

Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a shared kitchen and luggage storage space.

WiFi featured at the property. Rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

There is also a private bathroom in each room, with free toiletries. Some rooms here will provide you with mountain views.

American breakfast is served daily at the property. Portillo is 47 km from the accommodation, while San Esteban is 7 km away.
