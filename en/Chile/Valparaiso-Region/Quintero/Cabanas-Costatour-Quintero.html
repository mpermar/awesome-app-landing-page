Description

Featuring a great outdoor pool and a tennis court, Complejo Turistico Urra´s House offers self-catering bungalow accommodations in Quintero.

Free Wi-Fi access is available and guests are offered an optional Continental breakfast daily for an extra fee.

These cosy and fully-furnished bungalows are equipped with heating, a seating area, a private bathroom, a TV with cable channels, a kitchen and a garden and pool view.

Bed linen is provided. At Complejo Turistico Urra´s House guests will find a 24-hour front desk, a large garden and a terrace.

Other facilities offered include meeting facilities, a games room with table tennis and billiards, and a tour desk.

The property offers free parking. Viña del Mar Airport is 19 km away; shuttle services may be arranged for an extra fee.
