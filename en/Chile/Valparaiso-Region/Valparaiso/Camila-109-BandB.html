Description

Featuring a rooftop terrace overlooking the sea and the mountains, Camila 109 B&B offers rooms with free Wi-Fi and breakfast.

It is located a 15-minute drive from Valparaiso historic centre and from Neruda’s House.

Decorated in warm colours, rooms at Camila 109 B&B have large windows and cable TV.

Some of them have private bathrooms and afford sea and hill views. Laundry service is provided.

Breakfast including coffee, milk, fruits, bread, cheese and jam is served daily. Guests can unwind on the terrace or book excursion at the tour desk.

Camila 109 B&B is a 35-minute drive from Viña del Mar, and a 15-minute walk from Alegre and Concepcion Hills.

The Old City centre is a 20-minute walk away, and Valparaiso Cultural Centre is a 5-minute walk away.
