Description

Located in the UNESCO heritage city of Valparaiso, this charming hotel offers a spa facility.

Also featured are a charming garden and lounge area with open fireplace. It is the closest property to La Sebastiana, Pablo Neruda's House Museum, which is 80 metres away.

Hotel Puerto Natura offers neatly appointed en suite rooms, some featuring a balcony with panoramic ocean and mountain views.

Puerto Natura Hotel’s spa facility offers mud, algae, and herbal baths, as well as dry and wet saunas.

Guests can also enjoy relaxation and therapeutic massages, reiki therapy, yoga and meditation.

A daily local breakfast is served in the dining area or out in the hotel’s lush garden.

Plenty of restaurants can be found in the hotel’s direct surroundings. The staff at Puerto Natura’s 24-hour reception desk can assist guests in planning their stay in Valparaiso.

They also offer airport transfer upon request. Puerto Natura Hotel + Spa is one km from Valparaiso Bay, and a 10-minute walk from Muelle Barón.

Beaches are a 10-minute drive away, and free on-site private parking is available.
