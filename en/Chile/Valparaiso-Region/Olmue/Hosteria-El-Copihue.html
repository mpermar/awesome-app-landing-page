Description

Set in the heart of Olmué and only 8 km from Limache's city centre, Hostería El Copihue offers accommodation with free WiFi and breakfast in Olmué.

The property features a restaurant, a garden and a swimming pool on site. Guests are provided with free bicycles throughout their stay.

Providing a tranquil environment, the rooms and suites in El Copihue feature private bathrooms, minibars and TVs.

Room service is available and laundry services can be arranged upon request. Guests at Hostería El Copihue are welcome to relax in the indoor pool and sauna.

Massage sessions can be booked. The property also features a fitness centre, a children’s playground and a game room.

El Copihue is 30 metres from El Patagual park, where the Huaso de Olmué festival is carried out in January and February.

Hostería El Copihue is 8 km from the nearest train station. Viña del Mar and Valparaiso are 50 km away.
