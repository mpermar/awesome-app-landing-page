Description

Located just 1. 5km from Carriel Sur International Airport and opposite to Plaza Trebol Shopping Mall, Ibis Concepcion offers rooms with free WiFi and 40´´ Smart TVs.

Guests are welcome to enjoy the buffet breakfast for an extra fee. Decorated with modern wooden furnishing, rooms at Ibis Concepcion feature heating and air conditioning.

All of them have private bathrooms. Ibis Concepción features a restaurant with a wide variety of international dishes to be enjoyed with the best Chilean wines.

There is also a 24 hours bar serving snacks, drinks and hot beverages around the clock.
