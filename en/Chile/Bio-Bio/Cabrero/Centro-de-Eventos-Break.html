Description

Situated in Cabrero, 36 km from Los Ángeles, Centro de Eventos Break features free WiFi access and free private parking.

The resort has a barbecue and spa centre. Rooms include a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Certain rooms have a seating area for your convenience. Enjoy a cup of tea while looking out at the pool or garden.

Each room is equipped with a private bathroom. You will find a 24-hour front desk and hairdresser's at the property.

You can play table tennis at this resort, and the area is popular for fishing. The nearest airport is Carriel Sur International Airport, 73 km from Centro de Eventos Break.
