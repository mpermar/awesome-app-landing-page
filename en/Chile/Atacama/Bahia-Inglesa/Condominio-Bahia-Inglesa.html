Description

Set in Bahia Inglesa, in the Atacama region, Condominio Bahia Inglesa offers accommodation with free WiFi and outdoor swimming pool.

The beach is just a 500 metre walk away. Complimentary private parking is available on site.

Each unit is fitted with a fully-equipped kitchen. There is a living room and a dinning area, right next to the open plan kitchen.

Units include a terrace with outdoor furniture. Condominio Bahia Inglesa offers an outdoor swimming pool with a slide.

There are barbecue facilities and a terrace are at the accommodation. The city of Caldera is just 5 km from Condominio Bahia Inglesa, and can be reached by taxi or bus.
