Description

Featuring an outdoor and an indoor pool, Hotel Termas de Quinamavida offers accommodations in Linares.

Free WiFi access is available in the common areas. The air-conditioned room feature a private bathroom, cable TV and a safety deposit box.

Breakfast, lunch and dinner are included. At Hotel Termas de Quinamavida guests will find a restaurant, a tennis court and a fitness centre.

Other facilities offered at the property include entertainment staff, meeting facilities and a shared lounge.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including hiking.
