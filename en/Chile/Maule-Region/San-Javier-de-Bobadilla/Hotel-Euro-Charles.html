Description

Surrounded by a large garden and an outdoor swimming pool, Hotel Euro Charles offers comfortable rooms with Free WiFi access.

The on site restaurant combines traditional meals with fresh products from their own organic orchard.

French and Belgium dishes are served as well. Each room at Euro Charles Hotel is fitted with cable TV and central heating.

A daily breakfast with tea, coffee, breads and jams are included. This eco-friendly hotel features a tennis court, a fitness centre and a sun terrace.

A wide array of activities is available in the surroundings, including cycling and hiking.

Guests can also find a variety of locally made crafts at the on site boutique shop.

Hotel Euro Charles is right off Ruta 5 Sur, 3 km from San Javier de Loncomilla train and bus station and 15 km from the city centre of Talca.
