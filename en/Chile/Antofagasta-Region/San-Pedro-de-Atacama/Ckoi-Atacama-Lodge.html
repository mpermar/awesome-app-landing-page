Description

Featuring a garden of 200 trees, Ckoi Atacama Lodge is located in San Pedro de Atacama.

Guests can enjoy free WiFi access in public areas and a complimentary breakfast each morning at this lodge.

The rooms will provide you with a patio and a garden view. There is also an oven.

The private bathrooms come with a bath or shower. At Ckoi Atacama Lodge you will find a shared kitchen, a tour desk, and a concierge.

Laundry and massage services can be arranged. A cleaning service is provided daily.

Free parking is available. Guests can also enjoy the horse stables at the property.

If you feel like visiting the surroundings, check out Saint Peters Church, 600 metres away, and R.

P. Gustavo Le Paige Archeological Museum, 800 metres away. Ckoi Atacama Lodge is 200 metres from San Pedro Main Square.
