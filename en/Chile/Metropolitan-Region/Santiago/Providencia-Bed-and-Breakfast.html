Description

Located in a historical building, only 600 metres from Manuel Montt Metro Station, Providencia Bed & Breakfast offers free Wi-Fi and breakfast.

Rooms feature air conditioning and heating. Luggage storage is provided. Rooms at Providencia feature parquet floors and wooden furnishing.

Common areas are decorated with pictures of indigenous people from Chile. A buffet breakfast can be served in the rooms.

Bikes can be rented to explore the residential areas of Providencia. Laundry and ironing services are provided.

The 24-hour front desk can secure shuttles to Comodoro Arturo Merino Benitez international Airport, which is a 30-minute drive away.
