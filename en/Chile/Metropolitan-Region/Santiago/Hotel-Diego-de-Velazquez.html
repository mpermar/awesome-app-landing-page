Description

Situated in Santiago's elegant Providencia district, Hotel Diego Velazquez offers accommodations with free WiFi access.

There is an indoor swimming pool and free parking is available on site. Los Leones Metro Station is conveniently 2 blocks away.

Rooms at Hotel Diego Velazquez are modern and equipped with a flat-screen TV, air conditioning, a private bathroom and a minibar.

Room service is available and guests can choose to get a traditional hotel service, an apart-hotel service, or both.

Facilities comprise a gym, meeting rooms for up to 120 persons, a 24-hour front desk and business centre with computers and WiFi access.

In addition laundry services can be arranged for an extra fee and there is a gift shop selling souvenirs and local wines.

The hotel also offers a PC rental service for guests. A selection of local specialties, an assortment of Mediterranean classics, pizzas and pastas can all be enjoyed at the recently inaugurated Peztoro Restaurant.

Lighter meals, drinks and a great selection of Chilean wines can be enjoyed at the bar.

Hotel Diego de Velazquez is right next to Panoramico Shopping Centre and a 2-minute walk from Calle Suecia's cafes, restaurants and night clubs.

Pedro de Valdivia Metro Station is a 3-minute walk away. Costanera Shopping Centre, the largest one in South America, is a 7-minute walk away.

Arturo Merino Benitez International Airport can be reached in approximately a 30-minute drive.
