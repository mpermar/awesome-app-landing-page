Description

Courtyard by Marriott Santiago, is located in Las Condes and features indoor swimming pool, restaurant and high speed free WiFi.

Steps away from shopping Centre Parque Arauco, where guest will find a variety of shops and restaurant.

The hotel has great connection to all Ski centres in the region. Fast and easy access to Valle Nevado, El Colorado and La Parva within one hour and 30 minutes driving.

Enjoy skiing at the magnificent Andes mountains during the day and bars and restaurants during the night.

Each room is equipped with air-conditioned, a 42" LCD TV with cable channels, a minibar, private bathroom with a hairdryer and a complimentary coffee machine.

The Suite also has a seating area with a sofa bed. Stunning views of the Andes Mountains can be enjoyed from here.

At Courtyard by Marriott Santiago Las Condes guests will find a fitness centre and laundry facilities.

Three spacious meeting rooms are also available. The property offers free parking and a daily buffet breakfast.

The Hotel is a 5-minute walk from the Las Condes financial district. Santiago International Airport is 25 km away.
