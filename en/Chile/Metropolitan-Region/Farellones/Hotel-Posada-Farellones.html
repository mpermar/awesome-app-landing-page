Description

Located 5 minutes' drive from Farellones Ski Centre, Hotel Posada Farellones offers free Wi-Fi access and an outdoor pool.

Santiago city centre is 56 km away and Arturo Merino Benitez Internatioinal Airport is 80 km from the property.

Providing a tranquil environment, rooms at the boutique hotel are all decorated with a different touch and are fitted with private bathrooms and TVs.

Some rooms offer a magnificent view of the Valley of Santiago while others offer a road view of Camino los Cóndores.

Guests at Hotel Posada Farellones can relax at the on-site spa. The hotel offers free private on-site parking.
