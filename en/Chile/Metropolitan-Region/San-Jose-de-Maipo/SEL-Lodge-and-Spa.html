Description

SEL Lodge & Spa features an outdoor swimming pool and a spa in Cajon del Maipo. It offers free Wi-Fi and Continental breakfast.

Lagunilla ski centre is 25 km away. The rooms in SEL Lodge & Spa feature private bathrooms and garden views.

Suites offer spa baths and air conditioning. Breakfast is served daily. The property has a restaurant that offers local and international dishes.

It is open for dinner every Friday and Saturday from 19:00 to 22:00. Lunch is served every Saturday and Sunday from 13:00 to 16:30.

SEL Lodge & Spa is 8 km from San Jose square and 50 km from Comodoro Arturo Merino Benítez international airport.
