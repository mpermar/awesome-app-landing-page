Description

Located in front of Villa Rica lake and 5 km from Grande beach and Pucon, Cabañas Monte Verde offers self-catering accommodation with heating and views of the lake and mountains.

The bungalows in Cabañas Monte Verde feature a private bathroom, kitchenette, and TV.

Cabañas Monte Verde offers BBQ facilities and tourism information. There is a garden and a game room.
