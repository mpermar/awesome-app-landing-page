Description

Featuring a garden with a heated swimming pool, Cabañas Altos del Lago offers self-catering cottages with free Wi-Fi and barbecue facilities in Villarrica.

Pucón is 10 km away. Decorated with wooden walls and rustic furnishing, cottages at Cabañas Altos del Lago have large windows overlooking the garden.

All of them have central heating, cable TV and fully-equipped kitchens and dining areas.

Parking is provided for a fee of 7 USD per day. Guests can unwind in the garden, by the pool, while their kids enjoy themselves in the playground.

Cabañas Altos del Lago is 24 km from the ski centre and 14 km from Villarrica city centre.
