Description

Featuring 2 seasonal pools, one outdoor and one indoor, and a garden, Complejo Turístico Pucón offers self-catering accommodation in a family-run property in Pucón city, 1,2 km from the city centre.

WiFi access is available in the restaurant and meeting rooms. Providing a tranquil environment, the bungalows in Complejo Tuístico Pucón feature kitchens, balconies, seating areas and dining areas.

The 24-hour front desk can arrange laundry services. Complejo Turístico Pucón also offers a sauna, a massage room, and a fitness centre.

There is a games room with a professional pool table, table tennis, and foosball.

Pucón Tuístico Complex is 14 km from Pucón ski center and 100 km from Temuco airport.
