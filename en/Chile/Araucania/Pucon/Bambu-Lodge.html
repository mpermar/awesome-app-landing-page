Description

Located in over 1. 5 hectares of vegetation and nature, Bambu Lodge offers accommodations in Pucón.

Free Wi-Fi access is available and guests are served a complimentary continental breakfast daily.

There is a 150 square metres swimming pool, a gym and 2 hot tubs. Rooms at Bambu Lodge are cosy and carefully decorated.

They are all fitted with a private bathroom, heating and a great garden and lake view.

Bed linen and towels are provided. At Bambu Lodge guests will find large gardens, a communal terrace and are welcome to use the shared kitchen.

Other facilities offered include a shared lounge, a tour desk and luggage storage.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including horse riding, fishing and hiking.

The property offers free parking. The property, which has a great view of the Villarrica Volcano, is located 5 km from Villarrica Lake and 22 km from Meneteue Hot Springs.
