Description

Featuring a big garden and an indoor swimming pool, Mirador Los Volcanes Cabañas & SPA offers self-catering cabins overlooking the Villarrica Volcano.

Free Wi-Fi and breakfast are provided. Puconn’s hotsprings are 6 km away. Decorated with rustic style wooden furniture, cabins at Mirador Los Volcanes Cabañas & SPA have large windows offering stunning valley and mountain views.

Kitchen facilities include microwaves and refrigerators. Breakfast is served daily.

Guest can unwind in the pool, or book excursions at the tour desk. Villarica Volcano is 30 km away, whereas Ojos Caburgua Park is 20km from the property.

A children’s playground is also available. The 24-hour front desk can secure shuttles to Maquehue Airport, which is 120 km away.

Mirador Los Volcanes Cabañas & SPA is 18 km from Pucon City and 18 km from Vilarica Lake.
