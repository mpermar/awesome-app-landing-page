Description

An outdoor swimming pool with splendid views of the mountains can be enjoyed in these fully furnished cabins in Pucón.

Wi-Fi is free and Los Riscos Beach is 1 km away. Cabañas Alun Kuyen offers bungalows with Alpine-style décor, fireplaces and terraces with lake and mountain views.

There are fully equipped kitchens and washing machines. Pucón City is 5 km away and Manquehue Temuco Airport is 100 km from Alun Kuyen.
