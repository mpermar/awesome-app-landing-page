Description

Featuring an outdoor pool and a tennis court, Complejo Turístico El Conquistador offers bungalow accommodations in Licanray.

Free WiFi access is available in this resort. Bungalows here are all fitted with a private bathroom, a TV, a fully-equipped kitchenette, heating and a garden view.

At Complejo Turístico El Conquistador guests will find a 5-hectare garden, camera surveillance and barbecue facilities.

The pool is built with a 9-metre high water-slide. Other facilities offered at the property include meeting facilities, a shared lounge and a games room.

The property offers free parking. The property is only a 20-minute drive from the city of Coñaripe and a 25-minute drive from Panguipulli.

Geometric Hot Springs are a 45-minute drive away. Pichoy Airport is 80 km from the property.
