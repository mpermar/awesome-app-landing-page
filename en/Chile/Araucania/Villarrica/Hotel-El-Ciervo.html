Description

Featuring a garden with a swimming pool, Hotel El Ciervo offers rooms with free Wi-Fi and cable TV only 20 metres from Villarrica Lake.

Breakfast is provided and there is a restaurant. Downtown Villarrica is 300 metres away.

Decorated in pastel hues, rooms at Hotel El Ciervo are fitted with stylish furnishing.

All of them have heating and some rooms boast fireplaces. A buffet breakfast with fresh ingredients, homemade bread and "kuchen" is served daily.

International dishes can be ordered at the property’s restaurant, and drinks from the bar can be enjoyed in the garden.

In summer, guests can relax by the pool overlooking Villarrica Volcano, or practice water sport at the Nautical Club.

They can also enjoy themselves reading a book from the library in the lobby, which features a fireplace.
