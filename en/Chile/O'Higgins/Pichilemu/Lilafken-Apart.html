Description

Only 200 metres from Infiernillos Beach and Mirador Rapa Nui, Cabaas Lilafken offer rustic wood bungalows and apparments.

Cabaas Lilafken has fully furnished bungalows with TVs. Some of the apartments have WiFi in the whole building.

All units feature kitchen facilities and private bathroom. The property also has BBQ facilities,Hammocks chilling zone and any kind of tourist information is available at the reception (Restaurants info, surf lessons or surf rental, etc).

Guests will also find bicycles and surf boards for rent. La Puntilla Beach is less than 1 km away and the bus terminal is a 10-15 minute walk from the bungalows.

Punta de Lobos is 6 km away and Ross Park is a 10-minute walk. Private parking is free.
