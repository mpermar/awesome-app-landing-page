Description

Surrounded by vineyards and featuring colonial architecture, Hotel Viña La Playa offers 11 elegantly-decorated rooms with large windows overlooking the garden.

The property features a lush garden with a pool and a tennis court, as well as barbecue facilities.

Free WiFi access and parking are available in this property. Arranged in a charming house, rooms at Hotel Viña La Playa are fitted with a private bathroom, heating, and a satellite TV.

Guests are provided with a complimentary American breakfast served daily, including homemade bread, cereals, eggs, cheese and butter.

There is a restaurant on site. The hotel offers accommodation with half board system, where it is possible to choose between lunch and dinner; includes breakfast, 7 hours open bar with Reserve wines and a coffee service in the afternoon; allowing it's guests to benefit from special savings.

Hotel Viña la Playa offers an airstrip of 690 yards long; at no extra cost, for airplanes or helicopters, which meets international standards; plus a large hangar.

Located 180 km south west of Santiago, the hotel is situated right in the heart of the Colchagua Valley.

A variety of additional activities can be arranged for guests, including tours and transportation to the local wineries with wine tasting, hot air balloon rides, as well as cycling excursions and tennis games.

Guests may also enjoy countryside picnics, traditional Patagonian lamb spitfires, and Chilean barbecues.

The 24-hour front desk can secure shuttles to Santiago’s Airport, which is 180 km away.

Hotel Viña La Playa is a 15-minute drive from Colchagua Museum, while Santa Cruz's city centre is 20 minutes away.
