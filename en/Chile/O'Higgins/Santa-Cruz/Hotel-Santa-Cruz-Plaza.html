Description

Right by Colchagua´s Main Square and museum, this hotel offers beautiful colonial rooms with modern amenities.

Casino, acclimatised pool and free Wi-Fi are provided. Santa Cruz Plaza has bright and spacious accommodation, featuring balcony and living area.

The five-star rooms include air conditioning, cable TV and minibar. A varied buffet breakfast is served and free parking is available.

Guests at Santa Cruz Plaza can enjoy a steam bath or make use of the heated pool or gym.

The souvenir shop sells local arts and crafts. Award-winning Los Varietales features contemporary Chilean cuisine and an attractive wine list for guest enjoyment.
