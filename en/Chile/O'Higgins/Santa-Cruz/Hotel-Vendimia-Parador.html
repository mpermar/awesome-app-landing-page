Description

Featuring a seasonal outdoor pool and a garden, Hotel Vendimia Parador offers free WiFi and buffet breakfast in Santa Cruz.

The commercial area and bus terminal are 5 km away. The rooms in Hotel Vendimia Parador feature minibars and private bathrooms.

The Colchagua Museum is 3 km away and Pichilemu is 100 km away. Free private parking is possible on site.
