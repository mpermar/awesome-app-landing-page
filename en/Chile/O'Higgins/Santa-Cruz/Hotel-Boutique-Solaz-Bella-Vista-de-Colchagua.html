Description

Boasting a garden and a swimming pool and a view of the surrounding hills, Hotel Boutique Solaz Bella Vista de Colchagua offers rural accommodation 10 km from Santa Cruz.

Free parking is available. Rooms at Hotel Boutique Solaz Bella Vista de Colchagua feature air conditioning, heating, and satellite TV.

Some of them have hot tubs and private access to the garden. Daily maid service is available.

Breakfast is served daily and drinks from the bar can be enjoyed on the sun loungers and hammocks by the pool.

Additionally, fast food menus and snacks are available, and guests can order homemade dishes in advance.

One crib can be provided free of charge, any extra cribs will have an extra fee. Guests can also request an extra bed or sofa-bed, this will have the cost of an extra bed.

Horseback riding can be organized in advance for an extra cost. Massage sessions can be requested for an extra fee.

There is also luggage storage and a front desk which is open between 08:00 and 22:00.

Laundry and ironing services are provided for an extra fee. The casino and the Colchagua Museum are 10 km away.
