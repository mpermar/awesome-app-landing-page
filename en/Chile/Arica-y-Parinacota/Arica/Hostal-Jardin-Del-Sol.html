Description

Featuring free WiFi access and a variety of guided tours, Jardin Del Sol offers comfortable accommodations in Arica.

Each room here features tasteful parquet floors, dark brown bedding and cable TV.

Private rooms have en suite bathroom and shared bathroom facilities are available for the mixed dormitories.

A daily breakfast buffet is included. Guests at Hostal Jardin Del Sol can inquire about hiking or biking excursions to Lake Chungará National Park or Parinacota Volcano.

They can also visit the Chinchorro Culture Mummies which are located 12 km away. A variety facilities offered at the property include a shared kitchen, laundry facilities and airport shuttle service.

Guests can enjoy the sunshine and garden views from the furnished terrace while reading a book from the library.

Hostal Jardin Del Sol is 30-minute drive from Arica Airport, and 10 blocks from the beach.
