Description

Bungalows and rooms can be booked only 50 metres from Putre station at Pachamama Hostal & Tours.

Guests can book trips to Lauca National Park and Chungara Lake among others. WiFi is free.

Rooms at Pachamama all feature shared bathroom facilities and bungalows are equipped with kitchen facilities, TVs and private bathrooms.

There is a pool table and ping-pong table available for guests to enjoy. Putre’s main square is 300 metres away and there is free public parking nearby.
