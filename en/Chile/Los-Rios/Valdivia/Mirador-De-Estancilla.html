Description

Mirador De Estancilla features a restaurant, free bikes, a fitness centre and bar in Valdivia.

Among the various facilities are a shared lounge, a garden, as well as a terrace.

The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.

The units in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. At Mirador De Estancilla all rooms come with air conditioning and a private bathroom.

Continental and buffet breakfast options are available every morning at the accommodation.

You can play billiards at Mirador De Estancilla, and the area is popular for cycling.

The nearest airport is Pichoy, 42 km from the hotel, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
