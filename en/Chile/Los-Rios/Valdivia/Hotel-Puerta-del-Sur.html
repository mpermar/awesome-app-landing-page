Description

An outdoor swimming pool, elegant rooms can be enjoyed 8 blocks from downtown Valdivia.

Wi-Fi is free. Bicycle rental and babysitting services are available. Rooms at Puerta del Sur are equipped with flat-screen TVs.

They feature pale pink carpeted floors and upholstered chairs in warm shades. All rooms have modern, marbled bathrooms with baths.

Baby cots are available and have no cost. A full buffet breakfast with fresh juices and regional jams can be enjoyed in the restaurant, with plenty of light and outside views.

The Pacific coast is 13 km from the hotel. The tour desk can arrange shuttles to the airport, 28 km away.
