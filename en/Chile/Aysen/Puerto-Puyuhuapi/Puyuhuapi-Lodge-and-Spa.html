Description

Featuring an outdoor thermal swimming pool, a spa and a gourmet restaurant, Puyuhuapi Lodge & Spa offers hot springs in Puerto Puyuhuapi.

The lodge can only be accessed by boat and it features on-site shops and meeting facilities.

Providing a tranquil environment, rooms at Puyuhuapi Lodge & Spa feature private bathrooms and a nice seating area.

Guests at Puyuhuapi Lodge & Spa can work out at the on-site fitness centre, relax at the spa, or arrange massage sessions.

Hiking and canoeing excursions can be booked in the area. Complementary crossing from the Austral Dock to the Lodge is available at 10:00, 13:00, 15:30 and 19:00.
