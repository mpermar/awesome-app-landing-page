Description

Tucked away in the Patagonian forest, these Alpine-style cabins feature rooms with private balconies and free Wi-Fi.

Private parking is also free. The tour desk at Hotel y Cabañas Patagonia Green can arrange bicycle rental and hiking trips.

Chacabuco Port is 15 km away. Patagonia Green has tastefully decorated interiors with chic country style décor.

Rooms have flat-screen satellite TV and bathrooms with baths. Bungalows feature fully furnished kitchen facilities including dishwashers and dining areas with garden views.

A full buffet breakfast with regional jams and hot chocolate can be enjoyed daily.

Guests can also treat themselves to Patagonian lamb. Packed lunches for day trips may be requested.
