Description

Set in a scenic location a 20-minute drive from downtown Puerto Varas, Bahía Celeste has comfortable bungalows with free Wi-Fi and terraces with lake views.

La Burbuja Ski Centre is a 50-minute drive. Cabañas Bahía Celeste feature fully equipped bungalows with cosy wood furnishings, kitchen facilities and TVs.

Some bungalows include 2 bathrooms. Maid services are included. El Tepual Airport in Puerto Montt is a 45-minute drive and private parking is free.
