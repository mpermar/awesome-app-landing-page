Description

Set in a charming house surrounded by a garden with a pool and BBQ facilities, Hotel Casa de la Oma offers rooms with mountain views in Frutillar.

Free Wi-Fi and parking are provided, and there is a restaurant. The city centre is 14 km away.

Decorated with parquet floors and stylish furnishing, rooms at Casa de la Oma are fitted with cable TV.

All of them have private bathrooms. A buffet breakfast is served daily. Regional dishes can be ordered at the property’s restaurant.

Guests can relax by the pool or engage in horseback riding, trekking and canopy activities.

There is also a games room featuring table tennis and billiards. Hotel Casa de la Oma is 14 km from the beaches and 60 km from Puerto Montt Airport.
