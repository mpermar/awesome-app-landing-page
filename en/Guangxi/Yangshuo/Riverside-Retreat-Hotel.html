Description

Riverside Retreat Hotel is a 10-minute drive from central Yangshuo. Guilin Train Station and Liangjiang International Airport are a 1-hour, 30-minute car ride away.

Each guest room comes equipped with a cable TV, work desk and an electric teakettle. The large windows overlooking the mountains, cozy sitting area and works of art create an elegant and gentle atmosphere. Hairdryers and free toiletries are stocked in the attached bathrooms.

Guests can organize day trips at tour desk, or rent a bike or car to explore the surroundings. Alternatively, guests can enjoy the fresh air outdoors, or have a barbecue party. Meeting rooms and printing facilities are available for use.

The restaurant serves a variety of local dishes.

07/07/2017

What an idillic location - the highlight of our trip

"This was an idillic location with superb help from the English speaking manager Jackie and an impromptu sweet traditional song by the owner Shelly beside the stunning nearby river on the first night. I felt I was in some kind of dream nirvana in this place, as after walking from the river in the darkness, amongst the sound of happy locals, whistling frogs and crickets, a whole load of fireflies appeared. This area and its people are just wonderful. Try and stay in a room with a balcony, overlooking the mountain view. They have a comfortable double bed and are well decorated (considering how much you pay for it). The hotel is a little dated and could potentially be tidied here and there, but it oozes charm and has views that are truly outstanding. Jackie arranged for us to visit some of the local sights by taxi and he also hired mopeds (they have a couple of bikes too). The town (which is a reasonable distance by taxi) is a thriving one with laser lights at night and a party atmosphere, but try to get to some of the more remote villages, where the locals are very connected with their families and have an infectious warmth. If you need to take a taxi rather than moped (the roads and driving in general can be a little tricky), don't worry as they are cheap. Overall, you may have the odd niggle with soundproofing, or a less than perfect finish on the decor, but if you want perfection, it will cost three times the price and feel like any other sterile westernised hotel. This place is special because of its cockerel crowing in the morning, the smell of the flowers by the pool, it's truly stunning views and the friendliness of the staff. A very memorable highlight of our three weeks in China..."
