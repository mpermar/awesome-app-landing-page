Description

Li River Resort is a 15-minute drive from Yangshuo Bus Station. The West Street in Yangshuo is a 25-minute walk from this property. Guilin Liangjiang Airport and Guilin Railway Station are a 1.5-hour drive away.

All air-conditioned rooms come with classic dark wood furnishings, fine beddings and a seating area. Private bathrooms include free toiletries, hairdryers and shower facilities.

The tour desk can help guests organize cycling and hiking trips. Added conveniences include a chargeable airport shuttle service, fax and photocopying and a 24-hour front desk. Guests can relax in the outdoor swimming pool or take a walk in the garden. There is a elevator for your convenience.

Guests can enjoy Chinese and Western dishes at the on-site restaurant.
