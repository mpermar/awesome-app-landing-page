Description

The spa area at the Christiania includes a Finnish sauna, a bio sauna, a steam bath, an ice cave, and a 25-yard-long pool. It also offers panoramic views of the Matterhorn. In summer, there is a tennis court and miniature golf available free of charge.

A rich breakfast buffet is served every morning. In the evening, guests can enjoy a 4-course dinner.

Between 08:00 and 20:00, guests can take advantage of the free shuttle service to and from the train station of Zermatt.
