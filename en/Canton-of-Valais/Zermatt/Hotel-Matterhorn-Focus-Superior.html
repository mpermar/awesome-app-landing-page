Description

All rooms and suites were designed by the Swiss artist and designer Heinz Julen. They feature LCD flat screen TVs and a Nespresso coffee machine. Most of them offer views of the Matterhorn, some also have a balcony.

Guests can relax on the beautiful terrace on the ground floor and enjoy the unobstructed view of the Matterhorn.

The spacious and exclusive spa area of the Matterhorn Focus features an indoor and outdoor hot tub, a salt-water bath, a Finnish sauna and a relaxation room with warm deck chairs. Massage and beauty treatments will be provided upon request.

The hotel is reachable through a tunnel from the Kleinmatterhorn valley station of the cable car. A lift will take the guests 100 feet up to the hotel. The largest ski and hiking area can be reached in a few minutes. The village center of Zermatt is only a short walk away.

The hotel provides shuttle service free of charge upon request to/from the Train Station, Taxi Stand or Heliport in Zermatt between 8 am to 7 pm.
