Description

Located in Mautern on the southern bank of the Danube, only 2 km from Krems, the Nikolaihof offers very spacious, classically equipped rooms with panoramic views over the Wachau Valley.

Guests can relax in a sauna and in the large swimming pond with a wooden deck in the garden.

Built in the typical Wachau style, ad vineas Gästehaus Nikolaihof is close to the Nikolaihof Wachau wine-growing estate.

The bright rooms feature beechwood furniture, free WiFi, and either a balcony or a terrace with garden views.

The surrounding garden features a children's playground and a sunbathing area under the shade of apricot trees.

The rich buffet breakfast offers regional organic specialities. Massages are available on request.
