Description

Hotel Schachner Krone und Kaiserhof is located in the pilgrimage town of Maria Taferl, close to the basilica.

It offers panoramic views of the Alps and the Danube River. Consisting of 2 separate buildings, the hotel has a modern penthouse with an indoor pool and a hot tub, a fitness room, and a spacious sauna area.

Culinary pleasures can be enjoyed in the gourmet restaurant Smaragd, which has a garden terrace, and in the traditional restaurant Donauterrasse.

The hotel also has a wine bar, a vinotheque, and a walk-in wine cellar. In summer Hotel Schachner Krone und Kaiserhof features a park with an outdoor pool.

Wi-Fi access is free of charge in Hotel Schachner’s rooms. The business centre offers free wired internet access.
