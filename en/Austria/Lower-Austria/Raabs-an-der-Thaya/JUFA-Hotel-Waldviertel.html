Description

JUFA Hotel Waldviertel is located in Raabs, on the banks of the Thaya River in the Waldviertel region of Lower Austria.

It offers an indoor cinema, a basketball court, and a sauna. The light and airy rooms at JUFA Waldviertel have parquet floors, cable TV, a safe, a work desk, and a modern bathroom.

Guests can enjoy special sports programmes on the football field, as well as hikes and canoe trips on the river.

There is a fitness centre and a free on-site bike rental service for easy access to the region.

The terrace and garden with its playground offers a relaxing setting to use the free WiFi or quietly read a book.

Guests can get a fresh cup of coffee or tea from the café-snack bar. Free parking and luggage storage are available.
