Description

In the centre of Bad Fischau, Hubertushof Fromwald houses a restaurant serving Austrian cuisine and game, and a bar.

Free WiFi is available. The A2 motorway exit is 1. 6 km away. All rooms come with satellite TV and a private bathroom.

Some units are fitted with wall-to-wall carpets, while others have laminated floorings and additional free wired internet connection.

A breakfast buffet is offered in the in-house café every morning (from 06:00 until 10:00 on weekdays and from 08:00 until 11:00 on weekends and public holidays) including home-made spread and freshly baked bread.

The Kristall Therme Bad Fischau, featuring outdoor pools with cold thermal water in both winter and summer, can be reached within a 5-minute walk.

The Linsberg Thermal Spa is a 15-minute drive away. The Lake Arena and riding stables are a 5-minute drive away.

Private parking is possible at Hubertushof Fromwald for free. The city centre of Wiener Neustadt and the MedAustron Research Centre are about 8 km away.

Vienna is reachable in 40 km. Lake Neusiedlersee and the Semmering Ski Area are both around 45 km away.
