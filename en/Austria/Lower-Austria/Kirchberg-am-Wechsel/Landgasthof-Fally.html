Description

Cross-country ski trails at Hochwechsel Panorama, and the ski regions Semmering and Sankt Corona can all be reached by car in 30 minutes from Warth’s Landgasthof Fally.

Guests can use an infrared cabin for free and massages are available at the property upon request.

Rooms at the Landgasthof have views of the surrounding landscape and mountains. The units have wooden furnishings and floors, a table, seating area and a flat-screen TV.

The bathroom is equipped with a shower and towel warmer. WiFi is available free of charge.

Breakfast is served each morning and can be enjoyed on the sun terrace in good weather.

The Landgasthof has a garden, playground, restaurant and ski storage room. Parking is available on site free of charge.

Mountainbiking, hiking and Nordic walking are popular activities in the region. Vienna, Graz and Mörbisch can all be reached by car in 1 hour.
