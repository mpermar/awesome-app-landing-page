Description

Directly connected to the Römertherme Spa via a private covered pathway, Thermenhotel Gutenbrunn is a protected historic building that was once a residence of Beethoven.

Free WiFi is available on site. The spacious, individually decorated rooms have a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

Bathrobes are at guests' disposal. The rooms and the public areas feature Art Nouveau elements.

Guests of the Thermenhotel Gutenbrunn can also relax in the lounge which features an original glass roof.

There are some free parking spaces on site, and these are assigned on a first come first served basis.
