Description

Located in the centre of Annaberg next to the pilgrimage church, Gasthof & Appartementhaus Meyer offers a traditional Austrian restaurant, a sauna, and a children’s playroom.

The slopes of the Annaberg Ski Area are 800 metres away. Each room and apartment features traditional wooden furniture, cable TV, and a bathroom.

The apartments also include a kitchenette. The apartments are located in an annex building, 100 metres from the main house.

Guests of Gasthof Meyer can play table tennis and darts, relax on the sun terrace, and benefit from free WiFi in public areas.

Hiking maps and equipment is provided, and a ski storage room and free private parking are available on site.

The Annaberg-Ortsmitte Bus Stop is 50 metres away, and it is 200 metres to a children’s playground.
