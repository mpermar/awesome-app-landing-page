Description

Set amidst the picturesque countryside of the Weinviertel Region in Stillfried and surrounded by vineyards, Winterhof Küssler offers an inner courtyard hidden from the view, a sun terrace, and wine tastings in the on-site 300-year old historic wine cellar.

Free WiFi is featured. The rooms feature a flat-screen TV and a bathroom, and some of them feature a wine barrel facade.

Another highlight are the the suites consisting of a 9,000-litre wine barrel. A buffet breakfast can be enjoyed every morning.

You can buy home-made wine on site. On request, dinner is available featuring home-made specialities of the Weinviertel region.

Guests can rent bikes at the property and there are many bike routes nearby. Zentrum der Urzeit in Stillfried (museum of Prehistory) is just 1 km away, the Schloss Marchegg 18 km and the Schloss Hof 23 km.

A hiking path and a children's playground are just 200 metres from Winzerhof Küssler.

The March Cycle Path and fishing and bathing possibilities are within 1 km of the property.

Golf courses are situated within 18 km. The property is set next to the March-Thaya-Auen Nature Reserve.

The train station is within 1 km away from the property. Vienna is 30 km away, while Bratislava is 40 km away.
