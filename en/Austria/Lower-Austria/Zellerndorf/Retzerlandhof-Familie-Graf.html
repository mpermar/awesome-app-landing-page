Description

This 4-star hotel in Zellerndorf provides a shuttle service, sauna and massage treatments.

Free Wi-Fi access and flat-screen satellite TV are available in the rooms. Guests can relax in the seating area or take in the Zellerndorf view from the balcony, in some rooms.

Each room of Retzerlandhof Familie Graf is brightly-lit, spacious and fitted with chic furnishings.

Retzerlandhof Familie Graf’s garden can offer a scenic setting for a peaceful stroll.

The tour desk can provide information about excursions in the area and free private parking is available.
