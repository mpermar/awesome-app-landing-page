Description

This former mill in Seefeld’s quiet Weinviertel district features a café with terrace, a children’s play room and a sauna.

Free WiFi is available in public areas. TheJUFA Hotel in der Eselsmühle’s modern café provides a daily breakfast buffet, and a range of snacks and drinks are available.

Guests can dine in the garden, which has a children’s playground. Rooms at the Hotel in der Eselsmühle feature cable TV and a seating area.

The private bathrooms include a hairdryer. Guests can take part in bread-making classes, which involve grinding flour from the mill.

There is also an on-site bowling alley. The hotel is 8 km from the Locatelliwald, ideal for hiking and cycling.
