Description

Surrounded by a 6-hectare park, just 1 km from Lake Ottenstein, this 3-star hotel offers an outdoor pool and a tennis court, and bicycles are provided free of charge.

All rooms have a balcony or terrace and free WiFi is available in all areas. Facilities at Hotel Restaurant Ottenstein Superior include a bowling alley and a fitness room.

After a long day full of activities, guests can unwind in a wellness area featuring a sauna, a bio sauna, an infrared cabin, and a steam bath.

Guests can also play football, volleyball, and table tennis. The spacious rooms at this bungalow complex include a minibar, satellite TV, and a bathroom with hairdryer.

The Ottenstein Superior's restaurant serves Austrian cuisine made from products from the Waldviertel region.

It features a winter garden, a bar, and a sun terrace. A breakfast buffet is available every morning.

Free private parking, including a garage for bicycles and motorcycles, is available on site.
