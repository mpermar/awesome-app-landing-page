Description

Located in Weitenegg on the Danube River, Wirtshaus Gruber offers direct access to the Danube Bicycle Trail and the Way of Saint James.

Guests benefit from free WiFi and traditional Austrian cuisine in the restaurant.

The spacious rooms offer views of the Danube, a flat-screen satellite TV, and a bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Free parking is available on site. Melk Abbey is right across the river and can be reached in a 10-minute drive.
