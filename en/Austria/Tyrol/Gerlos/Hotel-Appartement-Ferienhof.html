Description

Hotel-Appartement Ferienhof offers spacious guestrooms and self catering apartments with private balconies.

Amenities include a wellness area with sauna facilities. The Zillertal Arena cable car service 500 metres away.

Hotel-Appartement Ferienhof offers solarium therapies, a steam room and an outdoor terrace to cool down.

There is a games room available for a game of darts, billiards or chess. Younger guests can use the Playstation game console and a large playground.

Child care is available from Sunday until Friday for children from 5 years. E-mountain bike rent is available at the property.

Guests stay at colourful rooms equipped with free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs. The private bathrooms come with free soap and shampoo products guests can make us of.

Hotel Ferienhof has a bar with a large open fireplace. Guests can relax at a sofa and enjoy a cold drink or light snack.

A breakfast buffet with eggs and fresh juices is served at the hotel’s lounge every morning.

A ski depot at Dorfbahn Gerlos is also available during winter season. Free private parking is available on site and electric bicycles can be rented at the property.

Guests get a 50-% discount on the entrance fee to indoor pool of the neighbouring hotel.

The Durlassboden reservoir for boating is a 5-minute drive away. From 01 May to 01 December the gondola is included in the city tax.
