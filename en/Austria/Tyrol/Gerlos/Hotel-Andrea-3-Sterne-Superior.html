Description

Hotel Andrea is a 3-star superior hotel, located 2 km from the center of the village Gerlos, opposite the tennis and squash centre and the ski bus stop.

This friendly and comfortable hotel features a big parking lot, a sun terrace, conservatory with bar, a farmer's parlour with a tiled stove, and free WiFi throughout the property.

There is also a sauna, a magnetic lounger, a Sanarium and an infrared cabin. All rooms are tastefully furnished in Tyrolean style and offer beautiful views of the surrounding mountains.

The luxurious apartments feature separate bedrooms and living rooms, and a fully equipped kitchen.

All rooms and apartments have a balcony. The rich breakfast buffet at Hotel Andrea comprises organic food and many Tyrolean specialities.

Half-board is available on request in summer. Use of the tennis and squash centre, including rackets, indoor shoes and balls, is included in the price.

The forest playground with 12 stations like stone cave, swings, sandbox and more is located just a short walk from the Hotel Andrea.
