Description

Landhaus Dengg enjoys a quiet location at the entrance to Gerlos, 2 km from the centre and close to a lake.

It offers rooms with balconies and a modern spa area. Within a 5-minute drive or ride on the free ski bus, guests can reach the Zillertal Arena ski area.

The bus stop is only 70 metres away from Landhaus Dengg. There is also an evening and night shuttle service to and from the centre of Gerlos, where you can find many restaurants and bars.

All rooms and apartments have a private bathroom, cable TV, Wi-Fi access, and a balcony or terrace.

Spa facilities include a sauna, an aroma steam bath, an infrared cabin, and rain showers.

There is an Alpine-style breakfast room, a winter garden with a bar, a sunny terrace and a large garden with a pond, a playground and a barbecue area.
