Description

Idyllically located between the mountains of the Lienz and the South Tyrolean Dolomites, the family-friendly Alpenhotel Weitlanbrunn offers a free ski shuttle to the Sexten Dolomites.

Free private parking is offered, and free WiFi is available at the reception. In the morning a varied Austrian-style breakfast buffet is served, while in the afternoon a cake and snack buffet is available.

For dinner the restaurant serves regional and international cuisine. Warm and cold drinks, including soft drinks, beer, wine and schnapps, are included in the rates in the afternoon and evening.

Alpenhotel Weitlanbrunn is located directly next to a bicycle trail. The guided family walks will show you the region's most beautiful spots.

Child care is offered from Sunday to Friday, 8 hours a day, for children from 4 until 12 years.

In winter a cross-country ski run, many winter hiking trails and a toboggan run start directly in front of the hotel.

Toboggan evenings and torch hikes are regularly organised. Ski passes for the Sexten Dolomites ski resort in South Tyrol (4 km away) can be bought at the hotel.
