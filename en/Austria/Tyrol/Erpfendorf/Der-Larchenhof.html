Description

Set amidst the Kitzbühel mountains, the Der Lärchenhof is a luxurious 5-star hotel in an idyllic location in Erpfendorf, only 16 km away from Kitzbühel.

It includes a gourmet restaurant, an indoor pool, an outdoor pool, and a 4. 700 m² spa area.

There is also a modern 300 m² fitness centre and the hotel features its own ski lift and ski school.

Beauty and body treatments with exclusive products by St. Barth, Maria Galland, Environ and Alpienne are available.

Vitality and health checks are also offered and the fitness offer includes Aqua-fit, Pilates, Qi Gong, and Nordic Walking.

The newly-built sauna features an escalator, lifting guests to the panorama sauna garden with impressive views of the surrounding mountains.

The rich breakfast buffet features an organic corner. In the evening you can enjoy a varied selection of delicious dishes, as well as Austrian specialities, a dessert buffet, and a salad buffet.

Guests will find reserved parking spaces at the Steinplatte Ski Area, only 5 km away.

Bike and mountain-bike tours as well as snowshoe hiking and winter hiking tours are offered.

Available leisure options include romantic horse-drawn carriage rides, toboggan evenings, curling, guided cross-country ski tours, biathlon and much more.

Child care is offered for children from 3 years. For tennis players, the Lärchenhof features a tennis academy with 5 clay courts and 3 indoor courts.

For golf players, there is a 9-hole course and a 6-hole academy, while for equestrians, the Hanneshof riding stables are just next door.
