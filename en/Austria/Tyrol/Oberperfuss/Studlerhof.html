Description

The Studlerhof is located 1 km away from the village centre of Oberperfuss and 12 km from Innsbruck.

Hiking and mountain bike trails start right outside, and it is right next to the Rangger Köpfl Ski Area.

The modern apartments at the Studlerhof are equipped with free Wi-Fi and a flat-screen TV.

Each is self-catered with a private kitchen, seating area and balcony with scenic views.

This traditional Tyrolean farm features hares, cows, horses, hens, mini goats and ponies.

Guests can help at the stable and participate in field work, bread baking or tea making.

The Studlerhof also features a playground, games room and barbecue area. Guests can buy drinks and have coffee in the Studlerhof's lounge.
