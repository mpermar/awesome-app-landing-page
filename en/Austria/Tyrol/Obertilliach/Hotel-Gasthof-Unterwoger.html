Description

Occupying a historic building in Obertilliach in East Tyrol's Lesachtal valley, Hotel Gasthof Unterwöger offers views of the Karnischer Kamm Mountain Range.

It features a restaurant and a spa area with an indoor pool. The nearest lift of the Golzentipp Ski Area is just 100 metres away and the Hochpustertal Ski Area can easily reached by ski bus within 20 minutes.

All rooms have a private bathroom equipped with bathrobes, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and offer mountain or garden views.

The rooms are situated in 2 houses next to the main building. Typical Tyrolean specialities can be enjoyed in the à-la-carte restaurant.

A buffet breakfast is offered every morning. The spa area with a sauna, a steam bath, an infra-red cabin and an indoor pool is located in the main building.

Another small sauna and a fitness room are located in the neighbouring guest house.
