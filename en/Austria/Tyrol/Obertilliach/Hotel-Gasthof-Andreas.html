Description

The family-friendly Hotel-Gasthof Andreas can be found in Obertilliach, in Eastern Tyrol's Gail Valley, and offers an indoor pool, cosy, rural-style rooms and tasty regional cuisine.

The spa area features a sauna, a hot tub, a steam bath, and a herbal bath. There is also a sunbathing lawn, a terrace, a children's playground, and a new fitness room.

An indoor games room for children is provided as well. Wired internet is available free of charge in the public areas of Hotel-Gasthof Andreas.
