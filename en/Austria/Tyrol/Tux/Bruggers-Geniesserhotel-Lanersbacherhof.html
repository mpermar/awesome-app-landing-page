Description

The Brugger’s restaurant has won numerous awards in gourmet restaurant guides and offers a rich breakfast buffet with homemade specialties. All half-board guests can select from the rich menu and put together a meal of their own taste. Guests may choose from 480 different wines from the hotel's wine cellar.

Brugger's Genießerhotel Lanersbacherhof offers rustically furnished rooms and apartments with lots of wood furnishings. All rooms have a balcony and are equipped with satellite TV.

The spa area consists of an indoor panorama pool (50 feet) with a counter current system and a waterfall, among many other facilities. Guests can enjoy sunny terraces and tennis courts. A log cabin sauna is available on the grounds.

The Lanersbacherhof is located at the valley station of the Eggalm cable car, taking skiers to Zillertal 3000 ski area. The Hintertux Glacier is 3 miles away and can be reached by a free ski bus, which stops directly at the hotel.

A licensed mountain bike guide and a hiking guide shows guests the most beautiful places of the Tux Mountains. Free Nordic walking lessons are also offered.
