Description

The Sport Bus takes you free of charge in a few minutes to the Hintertux Glacier (2 miles) and ski area Ski Zillertal 3000 (one mile).

The idyllic grounds with natural pond and spacious lawn, as well as the sauna paradise with aroma steam room, Finnish sauna and herbal sauna are an oasis for those seeking tranquility and relaxation.

Bright and tastefully furnished rooms and suites offer a view of the Tuxer mountains from their balcony.

Tyrolean specialties and international cuisine are served in the restaurant. A buffet breakfast can be enjoyed in the morning, snacks, coffee and juices are available in the afternoon and a 4-course dinner is served in the evening.
