Description

In quiet Alpine surroundings in the Tux Valley, ADLER INN Tyrol Mountain Resort features a large spa area with heated outdoor and indoor pools, modern fitness facilities, and a wide range of wellness treatments.

The hotel's own ski shuttle is available free of charge and takes guests to the Hintertux Glacier and Zillertal 3000 Ski Areas every 20 minutes.

Spa facilities include a Finnish sauna, a herbal sauna, a steam bath, a hay bath and a resting room with water beds.

You can also indulge in numerous beauty and spa treatments. The spacious guest rooms are individually furnished in an Alpine, elegant or romantic style.

They feature a big bathroom and offer views of the surrounding mountains. A flat-screen TV with over 70 channels is provided in each room, and free WiFi access is available.

Typical Tyrolean specialities and international cuisine are served in the elegant on-site restaurant.

Vegetarian options and diet menus are offered as well. The lounge with an open fireplace is great for spending the evening hours.

Free private parking is available. You can rent or buy ski equipment including cross-country skis on site.

The stop of the free ski bus can be reached on foot in just 1 minute, and cross-country ski runs pass right next to the Bergland Hotel.

Guided skiing, hiking and mountain biking tours and lots of other activities are offered by the hotel.

Herb picking tours are also available and afterwards you can prepare your own creams and oils under professional guidance.
