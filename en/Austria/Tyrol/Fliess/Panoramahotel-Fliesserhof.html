Description

Set on a hillside in the Naturparkregion Kaunergrat Region, Panoramahotel Fliesserhof is quietly located in Fliess, offering an indoor pool and a restaurant with a terrace, both providing impressive mountain views.

Free WiFi access is available. The Serfaus-Fiss-Ladis Ski Area is a 20-minute drive away.

The Alpine-style rooms boast a balcony overlooking the surrounding mountains and a TV, while the private bathrooms are fitted with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Kitchen facilities are provided in the apartments. Guests of the Fliesserhof can use a spa area with a sauna, a steam bath and a sun bed free of charge.

A ski storage room and luggage storage are further facilities of the hotel. Popular activities in the area are skiing, hiking and cycling.

Free private parking is possible on site. The Venetbahn Cable Car is located 9. 5 km from the Fliesserhof Panoramahotel.
