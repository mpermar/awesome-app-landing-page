Description

Ferienhaus Zillertal is located in Stumm and offers free WiFi and panoramic views of the Zillertal Alps.

The spacious rooms all have a balcony or terrace. There is a free on-site sauna in winter.

All rooms at Ferienhaus Zillertal are furnished in a modern Tyrolean style and feature a seating area and satellite TV.

During the winter season, guest have free access to the spa area including a sauna, an infrared cabin, a relaxation room, hay beds, a saline shower and a hot tub.

Guests can use a ski storage room and relax on the sun terrace and in the garden.

Free private parking is available on site. The ski bus stops in front of the Ferienhaus and takes guests to the ski lift in just 3 minutes.
