Description

Located between the cities of Kirchberg and Kitzbühel, Sport und Familienhotel Klausen offers traditional Tyrolean hospitality, an outside pool and a wellness area with massage treatments.

All rooms have rustic furniture and are equipped with a telephone, radio and a TV.

The balcony provides breathtaking views over the Kitzbühler Horn and the Fleckalmbahn.

In the restaurant you can enjoy homemade Tyrolean and international specialities and wide selection of Austrian wine specialties.

Breakfast and half-board are available. The wellness area includes a Finnish sauna, infrared cabins, steam cabin and a relaxation room.

The massages and the sunbed can be used for surcharge. In winter a ski school is located in front of the hotel, and the Fleckalmbahn Cable Car is 500 metres away which takes you to the slopes.
