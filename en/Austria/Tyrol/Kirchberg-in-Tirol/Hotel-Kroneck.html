Description

Hotel Kroneck is located in Kirchberg. Guests can enjoy a wellness area with heated outdoor pool with indoor access, textile sauna (family sauna), sauna area with steam bath, herbal sauna and 90 ° C sauna.

An infrared cabin is also provided. 4 golf clubs can be reached by car in 20 minutes or less.

All accommodation is spacious and has a balcony or terrace and private bathroom. Several units have a kitchenette and separate bedroom and living room.

Hotel Kroneck’s restaurant serves international and Tyrolean cuisine. There are several dining areas, one with a large open brick fireplace.

The other has a very rustically furnished bar area with large windows overlooking the surrounding ski slopes.

The property also has a large playground and children's playroom. Billiards and table tennis facilities can also be used by guests.

The Kitzbühler Alpen Ski Region is a 10-minute walk away. Kirchberg village centre can be reached in the same time.

Ski passes can be bought on site and ski rental and bicycle rental are also possible.

Hotel Kroneck also has ski storage facilities and the local ski bus stops in front of the property.
