Description

The 4-star superior Hotel Elisabeth amidst the Kitzbühel Alps is only a few minutes' walk from the centre of Kirchberg.

It offers a large spa area with indoor and outdoor pools and fine cuisine. Rooms and suites all have their own charming character and feature a modern bathroom and a cable TV.

Regional and international cuisine is served in the restaurant, based on local fresh produce, including that from the hotel's own farm.

Other facilities include an indoor-pool, various sauna and steam baths, a cosmetic and wellness centre with a huge choice of treatments, a fitness room, a bar and many other facilities.
