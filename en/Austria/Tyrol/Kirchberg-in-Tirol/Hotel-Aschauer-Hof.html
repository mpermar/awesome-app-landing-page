Description

Surrounded by the Kitzbühel Alps, this traditional 3-star hotel is located in Aschau in the scenic Spertental Valley.

It offers a spa area and a heated outdoor pool. The spacious rooms all have a balcony or terrace.

Free WiFi is provided in public areas. A slope for beginners and a small ski lift are right next to the Aschauer Hof.

Free spa facilities include a sauna, a steam bath and an infra red cabin. There is also a sunbathing lawn and a children´s playground.

Furnished in a traditional Tyrolean style, the rooms at Hotel Aschauer Hof provide satellite TV and a bathroom.

The restaurant serves traditional Tyrolean cuisine. In summer, barbecue evenings with live music can be also enjoyed in the garden once per week.

Free private parking is available on site. Hiking and mountain bike trails start just outside.

Kirchberg is 9 km and can be reached by bus. The free ski bus takes guests to the Pengelstein and Wilder Kaiser-Brixental Ski Areas.
