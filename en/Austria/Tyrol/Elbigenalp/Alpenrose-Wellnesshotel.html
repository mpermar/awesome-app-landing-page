Description

Elbigenalp’s Alpenrose Wellnesshotel has a fitness area with a sports shop and different exercise programs.

It has an indoor swimming pool, a hot tub, relaxation room and a sauna. Units at the Alpenrose have a seating area, a tiled stove, free Wi-Fi, a balcony or terrace and a bathroom.

Live music is on offer 4 days a week and guided hikes are offered by the hotel’s entertainment staff.

Also at the Alpenrose are a garden with deck chairs, a children’s playground and play room.

The property has a ski storage room with boot drying facilities, and a ski bus stops on site.

Toboggans, bicycles and e-bikes can be rented at the Alpenrose. Private parking is available on site free of charge.

A beginner’s ski slope is just 150 metres away, and the Warth am Arlberg ski region can be reached by car in 30 minutes.

As from December 2013, the Warth-Schröcken Ski Area offers direct access from the Lech Valley to the well-known Lech Ski Area.

The Auenfeldjet Chair Lift provides a connection by ski between the 2 ski areas. Starting in Winterseason 2013/2014 the hotel offers a free shuttle service to the Auenfeld Jet Cable Car, which connects the Lech Zürs und Warth-Schröcke Ski Area.
