Description

Haus Linn is situated in a quiet location, 300 metres from the centre of Ehrwald, and located next to the Sonnenhang Ski Slope.

The property features a garden and a ski storage room. WiFi is available free of charge.

All rooms and apartments offer panoramic views of the Tyrolean Mountains. They feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, wooden floors, and a private bathroom with a shower.

Guests have free access to the public indoor swimming pool Zugspitzarena, which is just 3 walking minutes from the property.

The Ehrwald Train Station is located 600 metres from the property. Free private parking is possible on site.
