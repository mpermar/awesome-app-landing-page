Description

The Hotel Tirolerhof is situated at the foot of the Zugspitze, amidst the breathtaking backdrop of the Wetterstein Mountains in the Tyrolean Alps.

Combining a wonderful Austrian and international cuisine, exclusive spa and pool facilities and a cosy, traditional interior with a first-class location directly at the slopes and near the ski school, this family-run hotel offers an unforgettable holiday experience for the whole family.

Gourmet board is available on requests and offers a breakfast, a small lunch, a 5-course dinner and free coffee and tea, non alcoholic drinks from the self service station, the whole day, as well as daily from 14:30 till 17:00 a salad buffet, cakes and sweets, a soup buffet and fruits.
