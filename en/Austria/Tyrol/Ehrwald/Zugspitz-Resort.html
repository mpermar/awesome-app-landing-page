Description

Zugspitz Resort is located in the heart of the Tyrolean mountains and the Zugspitze skiing resort.

It offers a heated outdoor pool and a fully equipped spa with infrared cabin and Kneipp water-treading basin.

Children older than 3 years can enjoy a kid's club. Massages are available on request.

The rooms and suites are fitted with a flat-screen TV, a balcony and a private bathroom.

The Zugspitz Resort features several kinds of saunas, Roman and Turkish steam baths as well as various fitness facilities and a quiet garden offering deckchairs and a lawn.

Guests can enjoy Austrian specialities in the hotel's restaurants, in the garden or on the sun terrace framed by a spectacular mountain panorama.

The hotel bar and several lounges offer places for relaxation. The entertainment facilities at Zugspitz Resort include a family-friendly pool area with several features for youngsters such as a pirate ship and various slides.

There is also an indoor kart track, a play area, a boulder wall, a gym and a yoga room.
