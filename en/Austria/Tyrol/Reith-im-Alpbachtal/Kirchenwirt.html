Description

Located in the quiet village of Reith im Alpbachtal, this 4-star superior hotel offers an indoor pool, a spa area, and a fitness room.

Reith Cable Car is only 450 metres away and is part of the Skijuwel Alpachtal Wildschönau Ski Area.

The Kirchenwirt’s spa area features a sauna, a steam bath and a spacious relaxation area.

Massages and various beauty treatments are offered as well. A flat-screen cable TV, a minibar and a work desk are offered in each of the traditionally furnished rooms.

Bathrobes, slippers and a hairdryer are also provided. Guests of Hotel Kirchenwirt can enjoy international and local cuisine in the traditional restaurant or on the terrace in nice weather, and a cocktail bar is also available.

The all inclusive arrangement which is offered in selected periods includes breakfast, dinner, an afternoon snack, alcohol-free beverages as well as beer and wine.

Various activities such as guided hikes, Nordic walking lessons, and air-rifle shooting are offered.

Dance evenings take place 3 times a week. The Kirchenwirt features a heated ski storage room and sells ski passes on site.

Free private parking is offered on site. During summer, guests can swim in the Reither Lake which is only 200 metres away.
