Description

Alpen-Comfort-Hotel Central is located in the centre of Nauders, a 10-minute drive from Bergkastel Nauders Cable Car.

It offers free use of its spa area and indoor pool, as well as free Wi-Fi access.

Each room is modernly furnished and has a flat-screen satellite TV. Bathrobes and slippers are provided for the duration of your stay.

The Alpen-Comfort-Hotel’s spa area includes a sauna, steam bath, infrared cabin, sun bed, and relaxation and massage rooms.

The health bar in the spa area provides a range of herbal teas, juices, fruits, and healthy snacks.

The breakfast buffet includes a muesli corner, fruit juices, a selection of egg dishes, and fresh fruit, bread and pastries.

In the afternoon, snacks and pastries are served in front of the fireplace. The 5-course gourmet dinner includes a large salad bar and a choice of main course.

The children's corner features its own cinema. The Nauderix Club, available for children over 4 years old, provides special meals and childcare services during dinner (in the main season).

A bike cellar is available and guided cycling tours can be booked on site. Free private parking is available on site.
