Description

A spa area with an indoor pool, saunas, a steam bath and an infrared cabin is featured at Alpenhotel Regina in Nauders, 1,500 metres from the Bergkastel Ski Area.

Austrian and international dishes can be enjoyed in the on-site restaurant, and free WiFi is available.

All rooms come with satellite TV, a seating area, wooden furniture, and a bathroom with a hairdryer.

The rooms are either located in the main building Alpenhotel Regina or in the adjacent Pension Regina, located 50 metres away.

Most of the rooms boast a balcony or a terrace. You can relax on Alpenhotel Regina‚Äôs sunbathing terrace and play table tennis and billiards on site.

Bicycles are available free of charge, and a playground is provided for the children‚Äôs entertainment.

Packed lunches can be requested. Half-board includes a breakfast buffet and a dinner with 5-course menu and salad buffet.

Once per week there is a gala dinner, a dinner with a rich buffet selection, ice cream buffet and chocolate buffet.

You can also sample hot and cold drinks at the bar. Alpenhotel Regina features a ski storage room and a ski boot dryer, and free parking is available.

Guided hiking tours are organized in the summer season. A free ski bus stops right in front of the building, and cross-country skiing is possible 1 km from the property.
