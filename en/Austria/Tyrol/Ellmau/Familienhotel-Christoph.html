Description

Situated in Ellmau am Wilden Kaiser enjoying panoramic views, Familienhotel Christoph offers a varied leisure programme in the sports centre and all-day children's care.

The indoor swimming pool for children features a 40-metre-long water slide. Other facilities include a hot tub, a steam bath and a sauna.

Guests can also get active on the squash and tennis courts. Free Wi-Fi and free parking is provided.

Familienhotel Christoph serves a rich breakfast buffet with an organic corner. The dinner consists of 4 courses and there is a gala dinner evening.

Beverages for dinner (wine, beer, soft drinks) and the entertainment in the evening are free of charge.
