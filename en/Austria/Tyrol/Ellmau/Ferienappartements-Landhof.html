Description

Ferienappartements Landhof is located in the heart of the Wilder Kaiser-Brixental ski area, only 800 metres away from the Hartkaiserbahn (mountain railway).

The Landhof Apartments have a balcony or terrace, a fully equipped kitchen, and free wired internet access.

The spa area features a sauna, a steam bath, a solarium, and a winter garden with indoor access into the heated outdoor pool.

In winter, a free ski bus connects the Landhof with the Hartkaiserbahn several times a day.

In summer, an 8,000 m² area with a garden, a natural pond, a sun terrace, a barbecue, and many games such as table tennis, volleyball, and badminton is available.

For children, Ferienappartements Landhof offers a big adventure playground with a children’s pool, a petting zoo, and pony riding.
