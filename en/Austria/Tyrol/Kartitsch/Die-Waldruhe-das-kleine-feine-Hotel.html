Description

Located in the idyllic village of Kartitsch in Tyrol´s Gailtal Valley, Die Waldruhe offers spacious rooms with a furnished balcony and a spacious wardrobe.

Free WiFi is available. All rooms are newly renovated as of May 2017, featuring stone pine wood beds and furniture, natural wood floors, a seating area decorated in warm colours, a spacious wardrobe, a designer table and mood lightning, a flat-screen TV, a bathroom with shower, free toiletries, hairdryer and vanity mirror.

Bathrobes and a safe are provided as well. The restaurant serves a wide range of Austrian and international dishes and fine Austrian wines.

In summer, you can dine in the garden. Die Waldruhe’s spa facilities include a sauna, a steam bath and an infra-red cabin.

Hiking and cycling trails, as well as a cross-country ski run, are very close to Die Waldruhe.
