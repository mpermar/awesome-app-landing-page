Description

Surrounded by hiking and mountain bike trails, Gästehaus Panorama in Biberwier offers panoramic views of the Zugspitze and the Mieminger Mountains.

An infrared cabin is available, and WiFi access and private parking are free of charge.

All rooms have bathroom, a work desk, and satellite TV. Most have a balcony. A varied breakfast buffet with regional products and seasonal specialities is served in the morning.

Massages are available at Gästehaus Panorama, and guided mountain hikes with alpacas are offered.

From the start of the winter season 2013/2014, guests enjoy free admission to the public indoor pool and the ice rink in Ehrwald, 4 km away.
