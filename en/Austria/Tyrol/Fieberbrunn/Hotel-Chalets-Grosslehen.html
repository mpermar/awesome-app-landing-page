Description

Hotel Chalets Grosslehen offers a modern spa area, a zoo, and an 18-hole miniature golf course high above Fieberbrunn.

The property offers free parking and free WiFi access is available in the entire hotel.

Rooms and chalets, newly built in 2018, are offered. All chalets are equipped with 3 bedrooms, a lounge area with sofa bed, a fully equipped kitchen and a terrace offering panoramic views.

The restaurant serves traditional cuisine with products from the hotel’s own farm.

Homemade pastries are served on the sun terrace. The spa area includes a Finnish sauna, a bio sauna with panoramic views, a steam bath, a fitness room, a relaxation room, and a large terrace.

There is a climbing garden, a big playground and a small animal zoo. Ski slopes, cross-country ski runs and hiking trails are very close to Hotel Chalets Grosslehen.

Casino Kitzbuhel and Kitzbüheler Horn can be reached within 21 km from the property.
