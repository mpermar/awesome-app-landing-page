Description

Gasthof Obermair is located in the picturesque village of Fieberbrunn in the Kitzbühel Alps.

A ski bus stop and free parking are just outside, and a toboggan run is nearby. Guests can enjoy a newly designed spa area which includes 2 saunas, an infrared cabin, a steam bath, a solarium, and a relaxation room offering views of the surrounding mountains.

A heated outdoor pool (in summer), a playground and a playroom for children can be found at Gasthof Obermair.

A washing machine and a dryer are also available and Wi-Fi is available against a surcharge.

The restaurant serves regional specialties and Austrian cuisine. In the morning a breakfast buffet is offered.

The free ski bus takes you to many ski lifts and cable cars in Fieberbrunn and the Pillersee Valley.
