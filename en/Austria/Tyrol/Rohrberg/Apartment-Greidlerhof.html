Description

This traditional Tyrolean farmhouse offers panoramic views over the Ziller Valley and the Zillertal Alps.

The Zillertal Arena Ski Area is a 5-minute drive away, with a free ski bus stopping right outside the property.

Free broadband Wi-Fi is available. The apartment at Apartment Greidlerhof has a private entrance, and includes 3 bedrooms, a living room with a flat-screen satellite TV, a kitchen with a dining area, a bathroom, and 2 balconies overlooking the surrounding nature.

On request, fresh bread rolls can be delivered in the morning. Towels are changed twice a week.

A drying room for skiing equipment and free private parking are available at the Greidlerhof.

Hiking and mountain bike trails start right outside, and the centre of Rohrberg is a 9-minute drive away, where shops and good restaurants can be found.

A variety of leisure activities are available at the Leisure Park Zell and the activity Centre Zillertal.
