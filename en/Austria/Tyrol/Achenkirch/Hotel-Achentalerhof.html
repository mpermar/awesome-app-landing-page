Description

Surrounded by a 2,000 m² garden next to the Karwendel Nature Reserve, the Achentalerhof is only 800 metres away from Lake Achen and 100 metres from the Christlum ski lifts.

The indoor pool has direct access to the garden. The spa area features 2 saunas, a steam bath, a solarium, an infrared cabin, waterfall showers, and a large relaxation room.

The Achentalerhof serves traditional Austrian cuisine and international specialities.

Most products come from local farms. Half-board includes a rich breakfast buffet with organic products, afternoon snacks, and a 4-course dinner with a large salad buffet and a choice of 3 main courses.

Barbecue and fondue evenings are hosted regularly, and there is a weekly gala dinners with music.

There are 2 weekly wine tastings in the vinotheque. Books, magazines and games are available in the Achentalerhof’s library.

The south-facing terrace offers panoramic mountain views. In summer and winter, the Achentalerhof offers guided hikes into the Karwendel and Rofan Mountains.

A cross-country ski run is only 150 metres away. The hotel has its own ski school and ski rental.
