Description

Resort Achensee is located in Achenkirch, only a 15-minute walk from the Lake Achen, in the heart of Tyrolean Alps.

It offers an indoor pool, a spa with sauna, steam bath and infrared cabin. Vital Hoteldorf features rooms with a TV and a minibar.

The rooms are decorated in traditional Alpine style and feature light wooden furnishings.

Some units feature a balcony with views of the surrounding mountains, as well. Guests can dine at the on-site restaurant Achensee, which serves traditional specialties from the region or treat themselves to cosmetic procedures at the beauty parlour.

2 tennis courts and a gym complete the sport offer of Hoteldorf Cordial. A children playground and game room are also available.

Achenkirch Golf Club is only 3 km from the property. The closest ski bus stop can be reached within 7 minutes’ walk.
