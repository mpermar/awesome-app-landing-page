Description

Zillertaler Hof is located in Achenkirch, 1 km from Lake Achen, only 200 metres away from the valley station of the Christlum Ski Area.

Free WiFi access is available in the entire building. Bright and friendly rooms with lots of wood, and a spa area with sauna and fitness room can be found at Zillertaler Hof.

The spa area features a sauna, a steam bath and a relaxation room. A fitness room is available, as well.

The restaurant serves traditional regional and international cuisine. Half-board includes a rich breakfast buffet and a choice of 4- or 5-course meals for dinner.

The restaurant's terrace offers beautiful mountain views. Cross-country ski runs, hiking and mountain bike trails, a golf course, tennis courts, and a sailing school are very close to Zillertaler Hof.

Guests can use E-bikes and bicycles in the summer and in winter free snow tubing is available.
