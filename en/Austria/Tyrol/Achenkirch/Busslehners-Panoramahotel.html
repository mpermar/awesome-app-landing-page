Description

Located in the village of Achenkirch, Busslehners Panoramahotel is a 10-minute walk from Lake Achen and the Christlum Cable Car.

Each room has a balcony or terrace. The rooms at the Panoramahotel feature satellite TV, a minibar, a refrigerator, and a bathroom with hairdryer.

There is a sauna, a bar with an open fireplace, and an indoor playroom. Guests can relax in the large garden featuring a sun terrace, a children’s pool and a playground.

A rich breakfast buffet is served each morning. Guided hiking tours into the Karwendel and Rofan Mountains are offered regularly.

A cross-country ski run starts right outside. Free parking is available on site. Transfers from and to Munich Airport are offered on request.
