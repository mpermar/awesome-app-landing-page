Description

Located on a south-facing hillside just 900 metres from the Zettersfeld Ski Area and 1,500 metres from the centre of Lienz, Pension Gaimberg offers a sauna, free WiFi, a fresh bread delivery service and a garden with a seasonal outdoor pool, trampoline and table tennis.

Each apartment has a balcony overlooking the mountains. The spacious apartments come with a bedroom, a living room with satellite TV, a kitchen with a dining area, and a bathroom.

A shared washing machine is available at no additional cost. Free private parking is provided at the Gaimberg Pension.

Hiking and cycling trails start at the doorstep. The closest restaurant is 500 metres away.

A tennis court is 900 metres away, and it is 3 km to the Hochstein Ski Area. A cross-country ski run and a golf course are 8 km away.
