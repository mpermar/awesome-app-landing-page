Description

This luxury 5-star hotel offers an exclusive ambience in the heart of the picturesque town of Lienz in East Tyrol, surrounded by the scenic mountains of the Lienz Dolomites.

Guests benefit from a large spa area and an award-winning restaurant. The spacious, luxuriously furnished rooms and suites at Grandhotel Lienz offer mountain or river views, a private lounge, a flat-screen TV, and an elegant bathroom.

Each of the 5 individually designed restaurants and lounges offers sophisticated gastronomy in exclusive surroundings.
