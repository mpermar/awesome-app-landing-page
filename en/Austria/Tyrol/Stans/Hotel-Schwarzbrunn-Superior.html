Description

Overlooking the Inn Valley and just 3 km from Schwaz, the 4-star superior Hotel Schwarzbrunn offers a 3000 m² spa area with an indoor pool, an outdoor pool, a sauna area with several types of saunas, steam baths, infrared saunas and glacier water.

It also features a 850 m² Fitness & Health Club, a beauty area, several relaxation rooms, and a vitamin bar.

International and traditional Tyrolean specialities are served in the restaurant, and special diet menus are also offered.

The all-inclusive offer includes free non-alcoholic beverages from 10:30 to 21:30 (with a minimum stay of 3 nights).

Hotel Schwarzbrunn Superior also offers a playground and free professional child care for children from the age of 3, combined with various activities.

In summer, bicycles are provided free of charge, and electric bicycles can be used at a surcharge.

The outdoor programme includes hiking tours, mountain biking and Nordic walking. Aqua training, spinal balance and many more courses are offered on site.

Depending on the season and availability, guests can enjoy free entry to the Bergisel Ski Jump in Innsbruck and the Crystal Worlds of Swarvoski in Wattens.

In summer, free admission to the Stans outdoor pool, which is next to the hotel, is also available.

During the winter season, the Hotel Schwarzbrunn also offers bus transfers to the Zillertal, Innsbruck, and other destinations.

Guests can use the Stans Ski Lift, located right next to the hotel or try activities like Nordic walking and snowshoe hiking.
