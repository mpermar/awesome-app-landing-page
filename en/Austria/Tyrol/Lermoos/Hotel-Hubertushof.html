Description

The Hotel Hubertushof in the centre of Lermoos in the Zugspitzarena holiday region offers a panorama terrace, free wellness facilities and free access to public indoor and outdoor pools.

From the sun terrace you can enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding mountains while tasting fine Tyrolean cuisine.

Half board includes a buffet breakfast, a dinner with a choice dishes, a salad buffet and an afternoon snack.

All rooms in the Hotel Hubertushof have a satellite TV. Guests can use the sauna and the infra-red cabin free of charge.

The property offers free access to the public outdoor panorama pool in Lermoos during summer and to the public indoor pool in Ehrwald during winter time.

Electric bicycles can be rented on site. During winter guests can also make use of the ski storage facility at the cable car.
