Description

Haus Montana enjoys a quiet location in the centre of Lermoos, in the heart of the Zugspitz Arena holiday region.

It offers spacious apartments, free Wi-Fi, and free private parking. All apartments have a balcony and a fully equipped kitchen or kitchenette with a coffee maker.

Bed linen and towels are provided. On request and upon a surcharge guests can book fresh daily bread service.

Ski lifts and cross-country ski runs, as well as numerous hiking and biking paths, can be found in the immediate vicinity of the Montana apartment house.

A free ski shuttle takes guests to the Tyrolean Zugspitze Cable Car in Ehrwald, 5 km away.

In summer, all rates include free access to the public outdoor swimming pool of Lermoos, Panoramabad, 1 km from the property.

Bicycles can be stored in a separate lockable room in the cellar. Hiking maps are available free of charge at Haus Montana, and guests enjoy discounted green fees at the Zugspitz golf course, 1.
