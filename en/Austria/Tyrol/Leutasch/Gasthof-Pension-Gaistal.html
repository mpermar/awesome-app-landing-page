Description

The family-run Gasthof Pension Gaistal with free WiFi is situated 3 km from Leutasch’s centre and offers rustic rooms and 2 restaurants serving Tyrolean cuisine.

The guest house features a garden with a terrace with sun loungers. The nearest ski area is within 5 km.

Free WiFi is available. All rooms comprise satellite TV, a seating area and a bathroom with a shower.

A ski storage room is a further feature of Gaistal Pension and guests can park their cars free of charge on site.

The public pool Alpenbad Leutasch can be reached within 5 minutes by car and a bus stop is right outside of the property.

Cross-country skiing tracks can be accessed 150 metres from the property. The centre of Seefeld is a 10-minute drive away.
