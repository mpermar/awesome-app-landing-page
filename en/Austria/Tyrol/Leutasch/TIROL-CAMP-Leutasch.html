Description

Offering a spa area with an indoor pool, TIROL CAMP Leutasch is located a 5-minute drive from the centre of Leutasch and just 10 metres from the next ski bus stop.

It offers you rooms and self-catering units, free WiFi, a restaurant and a supermarket on site.

Breakfast can be booked on site. The on-site restaurant serves Austrian cuisine as well as pizza and pasta, and a minimarket is also located on site.

Children can have fun on the playground and in the games room you can spend some relaxing time in the spa area featuring a sauna and a steam bath.

Laundry facilities area available as well. The Kreithlift Ski Lift is 4 km, the Seefeld Ski Area is 8 km and the Rosshütte Ski Area is 11 km away.

A cross-country skiing track as well as hiking and cycling trails can be accessed right outside of the TIROL.
