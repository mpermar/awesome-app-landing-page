Description

Located next to the Hahnenkamm cable car, the Hotel Kaiserhof Kitzbühel, 4 Sterne Superior is just a 5-minute walk from the centre of Kitzbühel.

It offers free Wi-Fi and a spa with an indoor pool. The Kaiserhof’s rooms are furnished in a traditional Tyrolean style.

They feature a flat-screen cable TV, a work desk, and a bathroom with bathrobes and hairdryer.

Spa facilities at the Hotel Kaiserhof Kitzbühel, 4 Sterne Superior include saunas, a steam bath, massage showers, and a fitness room.

Guests can enjoy traditional Tyrolean cuisine and international dishes at the restaurant.

Half-board includes a rich breakfast buffet, afternoon snacks, and a 4-course dinner with a salad buffet.

One parking space per room in an underground car park is available free of charge at the Hotel Kaiserhof Kitzbühel, 4 Sterne Superior.
