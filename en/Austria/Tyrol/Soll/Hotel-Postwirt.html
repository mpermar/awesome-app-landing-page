Description

The Hotel Postwirt occupies a historic building in the centre of Söll. It includes a Tyrolean restaurant, a heated outdoor pool, and a modern spa area.

Opened in December 2012, the spa area includes an infrared cabin, a salt steam bath, a soft sauna, a Finnish sauna, and 2 relaxation areas.

Bathrobes and towels are provided free of charge. All rooms at the property offer Sky TV Channels and Sky Sport is available at the hotel bar.
