Description

Hotel Alpenpanorama is settled amidst the Tyrolean nature in Söll, next to a forest and 2 km from the SkiWelt Wilder Kaiser - Brixental ski area.

The location allows for panoramic views over the village, the surrounding mountains Pölven, Hohe Salve and Wilder Kaiser, and the valley inbetween.

Free WiFi and complimentary on-site parking facilities are provided. All rooms are fitted with a balcony and a flat-screen TV.

The private bathrooms are equipped with a bath or shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

A buffet breakfast is available every morning, and the hotel restaurant serves regional as well as vegetarian specialities in the evenings.

The spa and wellness centre can be used free of charge, and comes with several saunas, a steam bath and a fitness room.

Massages and treatments can be booked at an additional cost. Guests are also welcome to relax at the bar, in the garden or on the terrace.

Hotel Alpenpanorama offers easy access to many skiing facilities and hiking trails.
