Description

Hotel Garni Tenne is located in the centre of Söll, only 300 metres from the ski lifts.

It offers a spa area and free Wi-Fi. The modern rooms have a balcony with views of the Hohe Salve Mountain.

The spa area offers panoramic mountain views and includes a Finnish sauna, a steam bath, an infrared sauna, massage showers, and a tea bar.

The spa area can be used every day free of charge. Each room at Aktiv- und Saunahotel Garni Tenne features cable TV, a refrigerator, and a bathroom with hairdryer.

The restaurant Bella Vita serves Austrian and international cuisine and features a bar and a sun terrace.
