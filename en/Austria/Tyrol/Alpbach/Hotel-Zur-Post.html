Description

Located in the centre of Alpbach, Hotel Zur Post features a Tyrolean restaurant, a spa area, and an outdoor pool (in summer).

Free private parking is available on site. All rooms at Hotel Zur Post overlook the mountains and offer satellite TV and a bathroom with hairdryer.

The restaurant at Zur Post Hotel serves Tyrolean and Austrian specialities, as well as international cuisine.

There is also a bar. Spa facilities include an outdoor sauna, a eucalyptus steam bath, and an infrared cabin.

Guests can use a heated ski storage room and relax on the sun terrace. A playground and a games room are available for children.

The free ski bus stops right next to Hotel Zur Post. Ski schools, a ski rental, and many shops and restaurants are only steps away.

The Alpbachtal Seenland Card is included in the price. This card comprises many free benefits such as admission to the public indoor pool and use of public buses.
