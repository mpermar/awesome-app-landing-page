Description

Set amidst the beautiful nature of Tyrol’s Upper Inn Valley, the Mozart-Vital offers a varied programme of wellness and leisure activities, as well as excellent healthy cuisine.

The Serfaus-Fiss-Ladis ski area can be reached in only 10 minutes with the hotel’s own shuttle.

After an eventful day on the slopes, you can relax in the 2,200 m² spa area. Here you will find an indoor pool with an open fireplace and a relax lounge, and a wide range of beauty, bath and wellness treatments.

The new adventure pool zone for children features Austria’s longest indoor hotel slide (130 metres).

Children will also enjoy their own sauna and spa area, a 300 m² indoor play zone with a children’s cinema, and the hotel’s professional baby and child care.

For active guests a fitness room, a gymnastics room, 2 squash courts, and a tennis court are available.

The sports and activities programmes comprise Nordic walking, Qi Gong, and guided hiking and cycling tours.

The swimming lake next to the hotel and the public outdoor pool (500 metres away) can be used free of charge.

A sports shop and a ski rental can be found in the hotel. Half-board is included in the price and comprises a welcome cocktail, a rich breakfast buffet with organic dishes, and a 4-course dinner with a choice of dishes and a salad buffet.

Weekly gala dinners, a children’s table with a juice bar and separate meals, and a snack buffet in the afternoon are also included.

The hotel is surrounded by a 5,000 m² garden landscape with a natural pond and sunbathing lawns.

The Ried/Fendels cable car is right next to Hotel Mozart-Vital. The use of the parking garage is included in the price.
