Description

Set peacefully 200 metres from the centre of Kirchdorf in Tirol, the 3-star Hotel Neuwirt offers many sports facilities.

Free ski bus takes guests to Kirchdorf Ski Area, which is 1 km away. This traditional Alpine structure offers rooms decorated with solid wooden furniture.

All of them include a tiled bathroom and satellite TV. Some rooms offer a balcony.

Neuwirt’s restaurant offers large panoramic windows and a tiled stove. It serves Tyrolean and Austrian cuisine.

Special emphasis is put on sourcing only high-quality local produce. Special dietary requirements can be accommodated on request.

The public areas also include a comfortable lounge bar and a games room. A playground is available in the garden.

Sporting facilities include a mini golf pitch, a climbing wall and a ping-pong table.

A ski storage room is available and a small soccer field and a basketball court are also available.

There is a bio sauna and massages can be booked on request and against an extra charge.

Cross-country skiing trails pass by the hotel, and a ski bus stop is located right next to the hotel.

Guests staying at Neuwirt enjoy special discounts at an outdoor pool and a wellness centre which is located 400 metres away.

Wilder Kaiser Climbing Centre is 4 km away. Sankt Johann is 5 km away, and Ellmau can be reached within 15 km.
