Description

Located in Kirchdorf in Tirol, Das Seiwald offers rooms with a balcony. The hotel features a spa area comprising 2 saunas, an infrared cabin and an indoor swimming pool.

The closest ski lift of the Kitzbühel Ski Area is just a 2-minute walk away. All rooms come with cable TV and a bathroom with a hairdryer, amenities and a shower or a tub.

Wi-Fi is available in the entire building. The hotel features a direct access to the slopes and ski passes can be bought on site.

Guests can make use of a ski storage room with a ski boot dryer. Das Seiwald features an on-site tennis centre and a gym.

Austrian and international cuisine is served in the restaurant. Special diet menus are available on request.

Drinks can be enjoyed at the hotel's bar. Kitzbühel is 15 km away and the Kufstein Fortress is a 30-minute drive away.

A ski bus stops right in front of the building and private parking is possible at the hotel.
