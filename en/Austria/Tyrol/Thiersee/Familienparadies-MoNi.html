Description

Set in Landl in the Tyrol region, Familienparadies MoNi offers accommodation with free WiFi.

Free private parking is available on site. Each unit at the apartment comes with a fully equipped kitchen with dishwasher, oven, a coffee machine, as well as a microwave and kettle.

There is also a dining area and a kitchenette equipped with a toaster, a fridge, and a stove top.

There is also a flat-screen TV available. The property offers continental breakfast, while guests can enjoy activities including skiing, cycling or hiking in the surroundings.

The restaurant at Familienparadies MoNi serves Austrian cuisine. The accommodation offers an indoor and outdoor children's playground and a petting zoo.

The area is famous for hiking, biking, cross-country skiing and skiing. A natural bathing lake is a few minutes away, the Sudelfeld Ski Area is 10 minutes away.

On the groundfloor of Familienparadies MoNi there is a restaurant, serving Tyrolian Cuisine.

Mayrhofen is 49 km from the apartment, Kufstein is 20 minutes away. The Thier Lake is 7 minutes from the property.
