Description

The Linserhof offers a quiet location on a sunny plateau on the outskirts of Imst, surrounded by a beautiful mountain landscape.

The spacious, comfortable rooms are decorate in a Tyrolean style. Excellent cuisine is offered at the on-site restaurant.

Half-board includes a rich breakfast buffet and a 3-course dinner with salad buffet, and in winter, additionally an afternoon snack is offered.

The Linserhof features a big sun terrace, a beautiful natural swimming lake with sunbathing area and bar, tennis courts, an indoor pool and a large children’s playground.

In every season the surrounding area offers a wide range of sports and leisure options.

A summer toboggan run is 7 km away. More than 10 different ski areas are between 5 and 60 km away.
