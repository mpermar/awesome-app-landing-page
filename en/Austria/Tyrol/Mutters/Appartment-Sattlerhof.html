Description

Located in a farmhouse, Ferienwohnung Sattlerhof is an apartment with a balcony. It is 10 km from from the centre of Innsbruck and 3.

7 km from the centre of Mutters. The Mutterer Alm Ski Area can also be reached within 5 minutes by car.

The apartment comes with Tyrolean-style furniture and consists of a spacious living room with a sofa, a fully equipped kitchenette, 2 bedrooms and a bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer.

Satellite TV is provided. A garden is surrounding Ferienwohnung Sattlerhof. You can watch and feed farm animals on site.

A heated room, where you can store your ski equipment, is available. The closest outdoor swimming pool is 4 km away and a natural bathing lake is located in Natters, 6 km from the Sattlerhof apartment.

The Schlick 2000 Ski Area can be reached within 25 minutes by car. You can park your car free of charge under the carport.

Within a 4-minute walk, you can reach the closest railway station and the Stubaitalbahn tram.
