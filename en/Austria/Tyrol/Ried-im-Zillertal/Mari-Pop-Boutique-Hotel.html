Description

Reopening in December 2019 after a complete renovation, the Mari Pop Hotel is located in the heart of the skiing region Zillertaler Alps and offers rooms with balcony, satellite TV and free Wifi.

Free parking is also available on site. The on-site spa features an indoor pool, 3 sauna areas indoor and outdoor, a steam room, solarium and infrared cabin.

There is also a rooftop spa (adults only), a family spa and a year-round outdoor pool.

The Mari Pop Hotel consists of a main building and an annex building. Every room in the main building is decorated in a different style and has air conditioning.

The annex building is the quiet part, very suitable for families. There is a ski storage room and a ski boots dryer in every building.

Breakfast is offered not à la carte but with a daily changing menu and service of a waiter.

An afternoon snack is also available for guests. The property offers bars and a restaurant that is located over 3 stories, offering a long table for everybody in the main building.

Guests can enjoy home made and regional products and craft beer. There is an on- site shop where guests can purchase all the home made products.

Guests can take part in various activities just like yoga, meditation classes, art nights and concerts, exhibitions and many more.

A ski bus stops in front of the Mari Pop Hotel, taking guests to the Kaltenbach ski area in 5 minutes.
