Description

Situated in the centre of the picturesque village of Thaur near Innsbruck, the family-run Hotel Purner offers delicious cuisine and comfortable, modern rooms with free Wi-Fi access.

The sauna area with Finnish sauna, steam bath, cold fog cabin, Kneipp pool and fresh-water fountain is available free of charge Monday to Thursday from 17:00 to 21:00.

Massages can also be booked. The Jacuzzi, the massage bed and the solariums are available at an additional cost.

Hotel Purner’s restaurant serves Tyrolean specialities and international dishes. It is open every day throughout the year from 11:00 to 22:00.

The city centre of Innsbruck (4 km away) and the historic town of Hall in Tirol can be reached in a few minutes’ drive.
