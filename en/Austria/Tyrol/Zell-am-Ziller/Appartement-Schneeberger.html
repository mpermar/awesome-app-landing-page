Description

Appartement Schneeberger is located in the centre of Zell am Ziller, just 1 km from the Zillertal Arena Ski Area.

All apartments have a balcony, free WiFi, and a flat-screen satellite TV. Decorated with bright wooden furniture, the spacious apartments also feature a bathroom and a living room with a kitchen and a dining area.

The sauna can be used free of charge 2 times a week. Guests can play table tennis and use a ski storage room with a ski boot dryer.

The garden includes a children’s playground, a sunbathing lawn, and a small stream.

2 bicycles are available at Schneeberger Appartement and can be used free of charge.

Free private parking, including a garage for bicycles and motorcycles, is available on site.

A leisure centre with tennis courts and a heated outdoor pool is 500 metres away.

The ski bus stop and the train station of the Zillertal Railway are 80 metres away.

Hiking and mountain bike trails are right outside, and there are several shops close by.
