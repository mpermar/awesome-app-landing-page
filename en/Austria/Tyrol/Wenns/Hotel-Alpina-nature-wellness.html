Description

This family-run 4-star hotel is set against an impressive mountain scenery in Wenns, at the beginning of the picturesque Pitz Valley.

Hotel Alpina nature-wellness offers a wide range of spa facilities, including an indoor pool, steam baths, saunas, and an infrared cabin.

Massages, beauty treatments, and a fitness room are also available. Half-board comprises a rich breakfast buffet, soups for lunch, an afternoon snack, and a set meal for dinner.

Until 17:00, drinks from the juice and tea bar and the spring water fountain are also available.

The free local ski bus takes you from Hotel Alpina nature-wellness to the local ski areas.
