Description

Hotel Sonnhof is located in the centre of Radfeld in the Inn Valley, just 500 metres from the Old Town of Rattenberg.

It features an indoor pool, a sauna, an infrared cabin, and a bowling alley. Free private parking are available on site.

The spacious, Alpine-style rooms at Hotel Sonnhof feature cable TV and a bathroom with a hairdryer.

All rooms have a balcony overlooking the mountains. Guests can use a ski storage room.

The restaurant features a terrace and serves Tyrolean and international cuisine. Half-board is available upon request and a surcharge.
