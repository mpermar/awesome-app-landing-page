Description

The Eckartauerhof enjoys a quiet location with panoramic views of the Zillertal Alps, 3 km from the centre of Mayrhofen and 20 metres from the stop of the free ski bus.

It offers a modern spa area. The 120 m² spa area includes a Finish sauna, an infrared cabin, a steam bath, and a large relaxation room.

Guests can also relax in the large garden with various terraces. Featuring wooden furniture, all rooms have a safe, and a private bathroom with hairdryer.

Some rooms also feature a balcony. Guests can also dine in the winter garden and on the sun terrace.

The rich breakfast buffet includes regional products. Free private parking is available on site.

There is also covered parking for motorcycles and a lockable bicycle room. The free ski bus takes guests to the Mayrhofen ski area within 5 minutes.

The train station is 500 metres away. In summer, guests have free access to Mayrhofen’s public outdoor pool and the Hippach leisure centre.
