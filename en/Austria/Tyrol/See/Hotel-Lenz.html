Description

Located in See in the Paznaun Valley, right by the ski lift and opposite the free ski bus stop, Hotel Lenz offers an extensive spa area and a sun terrace.

In winter, guests can ski down right to the front door. The ski slopes in Kappl are 3 km away, and Ischgl is 10 km away.

Balconies overlooking the ski slopes are standard in every room. Each includes a seating area, flat-screen satellite TV and work desk.

Bathrobes and slippers are provided in each room. The spa area at the Lenz Hotel includes different types of saunas, a steam bath and infrared cabin.

The relaxation room offers direct access to the surrounding garden. Austrian specialities and a rich buffet breakfast are served at the à la carte restaurant which features a terrace.

Half-board includes a dinner with 4 or 5 courses of your choice, as well as a salad buffet.

For motorcyclists, the hotel offers free use of the Silvretta High Alpine Road, covered parking for motorcycles, a drying room for clothes, a tool and washing corner, and maps showing a range of possible routes in the region.

The Spiel-Sport-Wasserpark See for swimming and relaxing is only 50 metres away from the hotel.

The Alpinarium mountain museum in Galtür can be reached by car in 30 minutes. In summer, the Silvretta All Inclusive Card is included in all rates.

It offers free use of cable cars in the region, of the buses from Landeck to Bielerhöhe, and free access to the indoor and outdoor pools and to the lake in the Paznaun Valley.
