Description

Located in the centre of See, 250 metres from the Bergbahnen See Cable Car, Post Hotel Paznaun offers free access to a sauna and a spa area, free WiFi access, and free parking on site.

The spacious rooms at Post Hotel Paznaun are fitted with a balcony with mountain views, a flat-screen satellite TV, and a private bathroom with a bath or a shower and free toiletries.

Guests can start their day with a buffet breakfast in the restaurant The hotel also features a bar, while the next restaurants and supermarkets are just 100 metres away.

Further facilities at Post Hotel Paznaun include free ski storage and boot dryer.

The ski bus stop is 100 metres from the building. In summer, the Silvretta All Inclusive Card is included in all rates.

It offers free use of cable cars in the region, of the buses from Landeck to Bielerhhe, and free access to the indoor and outdoor pools and to the lake in the Paznaun Valley.
