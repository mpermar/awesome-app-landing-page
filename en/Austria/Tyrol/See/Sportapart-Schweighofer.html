Description

All units come with a seating area, a sofa, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a fully equipped kitchen. A dishwasher, a microwave and toaster are also available, as well as a coffee machine and a electric tea pot.

The apartment has an outdoor pool.

Ski storage space can be found at the property, along with a grill.

6 EUB Medrigjoch is a 5-minute walk from Sportapart Schweighofer, while 6 KSB Zeinisbahn is a 17-minute walk away. The nearest airport is Kranebitten Airport, 43 miles from the property.
