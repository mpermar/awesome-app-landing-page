Description

The 4-star superior Alpenpark Resort is located in the centre of Seefeld in Tyrol and features a family spa with a hot tub, an outdoor pool, and a water park with 3 slides.

The Alpenpark offers a games area, a baby club with child-care services 5 days a week, an activities club for children, children's and baby buffets, a separate dining room for kids, and a garden with playground, bouncing castle and a trampoline.

Prams, baby bathtubs, bottle warmers, high chairs and baby phones are provided. Tyrolean and international cuisine, as well as a varied breakfast buffet, can be enjoyed in the restaurant.

Half-board includes breakfast, an afternoon snack, and dinner. You can park your car for free on site.

An underground car park is available at an additional cost and on prior reservation.
