Description

Located in the pedestrian zone in the centre of Seefeld, DAS Kaltschmid features 2 gourmet restaurants and a large spa area.

The indoor pool offers panoramic mountain views. DAS Kaltschmid offers free private parking.

Spa facilities include saunas, a steam bath, and a hot tub. There is also a modern gym.

Table tennis and billiards can be played at DAS Kaltschmid Since December 2017, The Happy Club kid's club includes extra benefits for children: Breakfast and lunch at the Happy Club, cake and ice cream in the spa park, Happy Club dinner and soft drinks, Child care 6 days a week at least 50 hours and Happy performances 6 times a week, Happy SPA with baby pool and children's pool with water slide, There are 2 discos, live music and dance evenings.
