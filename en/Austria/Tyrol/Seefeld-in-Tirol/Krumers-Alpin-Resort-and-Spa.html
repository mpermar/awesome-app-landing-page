Description

The Krumers' Zirben Alpin Spa includes a large sauna area with a Finnish sauna, a laconium, an aroma-infused steam room, an Alpine herb steam room, an ice room with ice fountain, a tepidarium with heated stone loungers, 2 solariums, and a gym with TechnoGym and Precor equipment and an exercise room. A wide range of massage and beauty treatments is available as well.

The spacious rooms include a flat-screen satellite TV, a mini-bar, and a bathroom with hairdryer. All rooms include a bag with bathrobes, slippers, and bathing towels.

The generous half board includes a breakfast buffet, cakes in the afternoon, a 5-course dinner menu or a choice of dishes or buffet with vegetarian, vegan and allergen-free dishes. The cuisine is a contemporary interpretation of Austrian classics and international favorites.

Guests can relax in the elegant AlpIn bar, in the library, on the sun terrace, and in the winter garden. An afternoon snack with coffee and cakes is served.

In summer, several hiking and biking trails, including theme trails, such as the Mental Power Trail, e-bike and mountain bike trails and a roller ski trail. Several lakes with public bathing beaches and golf clubs are in the vicinity.
