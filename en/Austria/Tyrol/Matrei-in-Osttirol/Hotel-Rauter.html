Description

The traditional Hotel Rauter is located in the centre of Matrei in East Tyrol, close to the cable car.

It offers a modern spa area with indoor pool, and a heated outdoor pool in the garden open during summer months.

The 1,000 m² SPAradies features various saunas, rooms for physiotherapy, massages and fitness, and a large relaxation room overlooking the garden.

Most rooms have a balcony with mountain or garden views, as well as satellite TV.

Tyrolean specialities, international dishes and fine wines are served in the award-winning restaurant.

Hotel Rauter also has an elegant bar, a café, and a breakfast room with a terrace.

The lobby of Hotel Rauter features an open fireplace and a small library. Hotel Rauter offers free parking and a free shuttle service to the cable car for the Großglockner ski area.
