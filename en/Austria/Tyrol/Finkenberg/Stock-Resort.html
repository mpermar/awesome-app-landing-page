Description

Set in the small village of Finkenberg in the Ziller Valley, the Stock Resort enjoys a quiet location amid the Tyrolean Mountains.

It offers a luxurious 5000 m² spa area. All spacious rooms and suites provide stunning views of the surrounding mountain panorama, and come with a cosy seating area, a starry sky, cable TV, a large bathroom and a king-size bed.

Full-board comprises a breakfast buffet with organic products, an afternoon buffet in the fireplace lounge or on the panorama terrace and a 6-course dinner.

The gourmet restaurant of the Stock Resort also provides non-alcoholic beverages, freshly-squeezed fruit and vegetable juices as well as Memon-vitalized water throughout the day.

In the extensive garden, refreshing towels, fruit skewers, ice cream as well as cocktails are provided.

Guests of the Stock Resort can relax in the spacious spa area, featuring several saunas and pools, as well as numerous beauty and health treatments.

A fully equipped and modern 190 m² fitness centre can also be used free of charge.

Children can have fun in the aqua park including a 70 metres long water slide and there is also a children's club with supervision on site.

Guests can get active and join guided themed hikes and ski tours in the Ziller Valley or the adjacent Tux Valley, rent a mountain bike for free or try out the tennis courts nearby.

The Zillertal 3000 ski lifts can be reached within 5 minutes from the Stock Hotel.
