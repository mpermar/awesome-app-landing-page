Description

The Hotel Gletscherblick above Finkenberg in the Tuxertal valley enjoys a quiet location surrounded by meadows and woods.

It offers rooms with balcony and free garage parking. Wi-Fi is available for free.

Each room in the Hotel Gletscherblick has an en-suite bathroom and a satellite TV.

Tyrolean specialities and delicious cakes are served in the restaurant of the Gletscherblick Hotel.

The terrace offers panoramic views over the valley and towards the impressive mountains of the Zillertal Alps.
