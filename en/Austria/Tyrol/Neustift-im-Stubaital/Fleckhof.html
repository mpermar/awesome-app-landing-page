Description

The typical Tyrolean-style Fleckhof in Neustift, 1 km from the Elfer Ski Lifts, is located on a little hill offering panoramic views over the Stubai Valley.

It features a spa area which is accessible free of charge and features a sauna, steam bath, and relaxation room.

All rooms and apartments have a balcony or a terrace with mountain views, solid wooden furniture and a TV with satellite channels.

Free WiFi is available in the entire building. A buffet breakfast is available every morning including a range of regional and home-made products.

The nearest restaurant is just 100 metres away and a supermarket, various other restaurants and shops can be found in the centre of Neustift, a 3-minute drive or 15-minute walk from Fleckhof.

In summer, you can sunbathe outside in the garden and make use of the barbecue facilities.

There is also a playground for children. Within a 10-minute walk you can reach the Kampler See swimming lake.

The ski bus stops just 100 metres from Fleckhof. The Schlick2000 Ski Area is about 8 km away and the Stubaital Glacier can be reached by car in half an hour.

Cross-country skiing tracks can be reached on foot in 5 minutes. From late May to mid-October, the Stubai Super Card is included in the rate.

This card offers many free benefits and discounts, including free use of local cable cars and public transport in the valley and to Innsbruck.
