Description

This 4-star superior hotel is located in Neustift in the Stubai Valley, directly opposite the lift into the Elfer skiing and hiking area.

It offers free Wi-Fi and an indoor pool. Decorated with elegant wooden furniture and floors, the spacious rooms at Sporthotel Neustift feature a minibar, satellite TV, and a bathroom with hairdryer.

Most have a balcony. The Sporthotel’s restaurant serves international cuisine and traditional Austrian and Tyrolean dishes.

The half-board arrangement features a buffet breakfast, an afternoon snack (from 15:00 until 17:00) including coffee, tea and non-alcoholic beverages and cakes, and a 4-course dinner with a vegetarian option.

On Fridays and Sundays, a 6-course gala dinner is served. The spa area includes several saunas, Turkish and Roman steam baths, and indoor and outdoor hot baths.

Massage and beauty treatments are provided. Guests can relax in the large garden and play table tennis.

Free child care is provided for all children over 3 years of age. A children’s pool is also available.

Free private parking is provided on site. Innsbruck is 25 km away, and the Stubai Glacier is 17 km away.

An airport and train shuttle service can be organised on request and for an extra charge.

From late May to mid-October, the Stubai Super Card is included in the rate. This card offers many free benefits and discounts, including free use of local cable cars and public transport in the valley and to Innsbruck.
