Description

Surrounded by meadows and forests with many hiking trails, the 4-star superior hotel Alpeiner - Nature Resort Tirol is located at the foot of the Stubai Alps, a 5-minute walk from Neustift’s village centre and 100 metres from the Elfer Ski Lift.

It offers a spa area with a large indoor pool, as well as a 300 m² natural swimming pond.

The Alpeiner's spa facilities also include a Finnish sauna, an aromatic steam bath, hot tubs, a fitness room, and an infrared cabin.

At an additional cost, guests can enjoy a wide range of massages and beauty treatments.

A sport programme with different activities is offered each week. Free WiFi and wired internet access are available in the entire hotel.

The children’s playroom features a 3D cinema and a PlayStation. A discounted shuttle service is available to Innsbruck Airport and Innsbruck Main Station.

Free early check-in and late check-out are offered (subject to availability). From late May to mid-October, the Stubai Super Card is included in the rate.

This card offers many free benefits and discounts, including free use of local cable cars and public transport in the valley and to Innsbruck.
