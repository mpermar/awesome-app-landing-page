Description

The Activehotel Bergkönig enjoys a central yet quiet location in the heart of Neustift in the Stubai Valley and 18 km from Stubai Glacier.

The hotel features table tennis in summer. The 2000 m² spa area of the Activehotel Bergkönig features an indoor pool, a panoramic bio sauna, a Turkish steam bath, a relaxation room with water beds and Finnish sauna, and a Kneipp fountain.

An indoor pool, an outdoor infinity brine bath with panoramic views of the Stubai Glacier, a big sauna area with biosauna, turkish steam bath, a finnish sauna, a Kneipp bath and a sauna garden as well as various showers and relaxation rooms and a fireplace lounge are available at the property.

The hotel’s exquisite gourmet cuisine pampers you with Tyrolean specialities and refined wines.

If half-board is booked coffee and cake is available in the afternoon. In winter the Stubai Valley’s ski slopes and cross-country ski runs are in the immediate vicinity.

In summer the beautiful surroundings of Activehotel Bergkönig are ideal for hiking and mountain biking (free mountain bike and hiking pole rental at the hotel).

From late May to mid-October, the Stubai Super Card is included in the rate. This card offers many free benefits and discounts, including free use of local cable cars and public transport in the valley and to Innsbruck.
