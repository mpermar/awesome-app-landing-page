Description

Alpenhotel Fernau is located above Neustift in the Stubai Valley, at 1,000 metres above sea level.

It offers free Wi-Fi, rooms and an apartment with balcony, a heated outdoor pool, and free parking.

The free ski bus stops right on the doorstep. Half-board includes a breakfast buffet, a daily cake buffet, and a 4-course dinner with a choice of dishes and a salad buffet.

It is also available on request for the apartments. Alpenhotel Fernau features a spa area with a gym.
