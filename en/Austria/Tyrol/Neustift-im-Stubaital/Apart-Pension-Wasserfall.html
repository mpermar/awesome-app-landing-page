Description

Located a 14-minute drive from the Stubai Glacier and 5 km from the centre of Neustift im Stubaital, Apart-Pension Wasserfall offers a spa area with a Finnish sauna, an infrared cabin, a sun bed and a steam bath.

A ski bus stop is right in front of the property, and the Mischbach Waterfall is next to the apartments.

Decorated with bright wooden furniture, the rooms and apartments feature satellite TV and a bathroom with hairdryer.

The apartments also have a kitchenette. Each unit comes with a balcony with views of the Stubai Glacier.

The Wasserfall Pension features an indoor playroom and a ski storage room with a ski boot dryer.

An outdoor children’s playground with a trampoline is available in summer. Free private parking is available on site.

The Stubai Glacier and Schlick 2000 ski areas are both 11 km away. From end of May to mid-October, the Stubai Super Card is included in the rate.

This card comprises many benefits and discounts, including free use of local cable cars and of buses, and free admission to indoor and outdoor pools.
