Description

Landhaus Pfurtscheller enjoys a quiet location with a large sunbathing lawn in Volderau in the Stubai Valley.

The ski bus stop is 150 metres away. All apartments have a balcony or a terrace and a fully equipped kitchenette including a dishwasher and a microwave.

There is also a flat-screen satellite TV. Guests can make use of the barbecue and relax on the terrace.

Children can have fun on the playground or in the playroom including a Playstation.

Bicycles are available free of charge, and guests can store their own bicycles or skiing equipment in a separate storage room.

Tyrolean specialities and international cuisine are served in the restaurant of the Landhaus Pfurtscheller.

There is also a bar. Guests can use the spa and pool area at Hotel Bergcristall, 50 metres away.

Guided hiking tours are offered during summer, and guests can take part in dance evenings and various activity programmes.

The centre of Neustift im Stubaital with many restaurants, bars and a public outdoor pool is 5 km away.

The Stubaital Glacier and the Elfer Ski Area are 7 km away. The Schlick Ski Area can be reached within a 15-minute drive.

From late May to mid-October, the Stubai Super Card is included in the rate. This card offers many free benefits and discounts, including free use of local cable cars and public transport in the valley and to Innsbruck.
