Description

This exclusive superior family-run 4-star Living & Spa Vitalhotel Edelweiss hotel in Neustift in the Stubai Valley offers panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.

It features a tennis court, an indoor pool and an outdoor pool that is heated all year round.

The Alpine-style rooms feature satellite TV while the private bathrooms are fitted with a hairdryer and bathrobes.

On request, allergy-free rooms are available, which include bed linen suitable for allergy sufferers and wooden floors treated with beeswax.

The 1,200 m² spa area at Living & Spa Vitalhotel Edelweiss includes a Finnish sauna, a salt water steam bath, a sweat bath, a Kneipp basin, and a panoramic relaxation room with water beds.

A wide selection of massages, body and facial treatments, and many beauty treatments with well-known body care products is available.

Living & Spa Vitalhotel Edelweiss features a children’s playroom with a PlayStation, a fitness room, and a leisure room with table tennis, billiards, and tabletop football.

Mountain bikes are available free of charge for all guests. Half-board includes an organic breakfast buffet, afternoon snacks, and a 6-course dinner with a wine recommendation from the hotel's sommelier.

The restaurant uses local products and organic meat and eggs from the private farm.

The sun terrace overlooks the Stubai Alps. Several times a day, the hotel offers a free shuttle service to the Stubai Glacier ski area (about 10 minutes) and to the train station and the airport in Innsbruck (about 20 minutes).
