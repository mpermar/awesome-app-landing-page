Description

Featuring an all-year heated outdoor swimming pool and a modern spa area, the Happy Stubai hotel is located in quiet surroundings, a 5-minute drive from Neustift.

All rooms at the Happy Stubai have a balcony offering panoramic views of Tyrol's mountains and come equipped with a cable TV.

Within a 20-minute ride on the free ski bus you can reach the Stubai Glacier ski area, which also includes cross-country trails.

The Elfer, Serles and Schlick 2000 ski areas, as well a 10 km toboggan run, are close-by as well.

The ski bus stops right outside the Happy Stubai and ski passes are sold at the reception.

Spa facilities include a sauna, a steam bath, a solarium, a Kneipp pool and an inviting relaxation area offering views over the valley.

Gourmet cuisine prepared from locally sourced organic ingredients and a wide range of quality wines can be enjoyed in the restaurant.

There is also a playground and a playroom for kids at the Happy Stubai. From late May to mid-October, the Stubai Super Card is included in the rate.

This card offers many free benefits and discounts, including free use of local cable cars and public transport in the valley and to Innsbruck.
