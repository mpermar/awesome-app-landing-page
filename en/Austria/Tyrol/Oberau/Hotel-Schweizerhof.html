Description

Renovated in May 2014, Hotel Schweizerhof is located in the Wildschönau Valley in the heart of the Kitzbühel Alps.

It offers free Wi-Fi and a sauna. All spacious rooms are equipped with a balcony and satellite TV.

Each room is fitted with modern, wood furnishings. Some accommodation includes a seating area and a kitchen.

Guests can relax with a massage and in a steam bath at Hotel Schweizerhof’s spa. The hotel’s tour desk offers advice on excursions.

Drinks and snacks are provided by Hotel Schweizerhof’s bar. The hotel’s restaurant serves local and international dishes.

Free private parking is available. Kranebitten Airport is 54 km away. Hotel guests receive the Wildschönau Card free of charge.

This card comprises many benefits in winter and summer, such as admission to local museums, guided hikes, use of the cable cars, access to the public swimming pool in summer and much more.
