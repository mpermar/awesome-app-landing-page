Description

Surrounded by the scenic mountains of the Kitzbühel Alps, the family-run Hotel Talhof enjoys a quiet location on the Roggenboden plateau in the Wildschönau Valley.

Guests enjoy free access to the indoor pool and the modern spa area, as well as free WiFi and free use of the underground car park.

Spa facilities include a sauna, a steam bath, an infrared cabin, a relaxation room, and a massage room.

The Talhof Hotel also features an à la carte restaurant and a large children’s playroom with a climbing wall, adventure castle, PlayStation, and many games.

Hotel Talhof is surrounded by green fields in summer and offers direct access to hiking and mountain bike trails.

In winter, it is located directly at the ski slope and next to ski lifts, a ski school, a toboggan run, winter hiking trails, a cross-country ski run, and a stop of the free ski bus.

The Wildschönau Card is included in the rate. This card offers many free benefits, activities and discounts in every season, including free use of local cable cars in summer and the ski bus in winter.
