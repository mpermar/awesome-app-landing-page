Description

The Tirolerhof is a cosy, family-run hotel in the Wildschönau, the romantic high valley in the Kitzbühel Alps, and offers a spa area with an indoor pool, a different saunas and a solarium.

A ski lift is right outside. In addition to a restaurant and a dining room, the hotel features, in winter, the “Stadl-Bar” next to the slopes and the “S’no Blau Bar” with weekly live music.

The cosy and comfortable rooms are furnished in a traditional Tyrolean style. Free WiFi is available.

Landhotel Tirolerhof’s spa area also comprises a steam bath, an aromatic cabin, and offers massages, pedicures and manicures.

A garden with sunbathing area is also available. The Tirolerhof is a perfect choice for every season.

In summer the Wildschönau is an ideal hiking area, while in winter you can ski right in front of the hotel.

A free shuttle transfer from and to Wörgl Train Station can be requested. Innsbruck Airport is located 75 km away.

Hotel guests receive the Wildschönau Card free of charge. This card comprises many benefits in winter and summer, such as admission to local museums, guided hikes, use of the cable cars, access to the public swimming pool in summer and much more.
