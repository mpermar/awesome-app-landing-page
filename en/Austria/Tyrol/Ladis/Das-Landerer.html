Description

This hotel opened in December 2017, offering various saunas and is located next to the Sonnen Cable Car of the Serfaus-Fiss-Ladis Ski Area, Das Landerer offers modern Alpine-style accommodation with views of the surrounding mountains.

Free private parking and free WiFi are provided. The accommodation comprises wooden furnishings and bright décor.

All units come with a flat-screen TV with cable channels, free bathrobes, and a balcony or a terrace.

The property provides daily cleaning service also for the apartments. Daily breakfast offers fresh, home-made products.

Snacks are offered at the hotel bar. Restaurants, souvenir shops and a super market are 5 minutes away in the city centre of Fiss-Ladis.

In winter, guests can ski directly to the property and make use of the sauna and the steam bath on site.

There is also a water world with an indoor swimming pool. Guests can relax on a panoramic terrace, in the garden or as well in a game room.

A massage service or cosmetics treatments provided as well. Ski passes can be purchased at the reception.
