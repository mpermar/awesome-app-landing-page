Description

Located in Götzens, on a sunny plateau of the Western Mittelgebirge mountains, Alp Art Hotel Götzens is close to the ski and hiking areas of the Olympia SkiWorld Innsbruck.

The free ski and hiking bus stops right in front, and free public parking is available on site.

Free WiFi is available throughout the building and guests receive free entrance to the public swimming pool in Axams.

The rooms are decorated with traditional, artistically painted furniture. They have modern, private bathrooms and bedrooms with a spacious seating area and satellite TV.

The Alp Art Hotel's spa area features an infrared cabin and a Finnish sauna. A steam bath is additionally available in winter.

Massages are organised on request. During winter season, half-board is offered, and a winter garden is also on site.

Guests can rent bicycles at the Alp Art Hotel Götzens and participate in guided mountain bike tours.

Mountain tours with certified mountain guides and children entertainment at Lake Natters are offered.

Alp Art Hotel is surrounded by 9 ski areas, including the Axamer Lizum Olympia Ski Region.

Many hiking routes start nearby. There is a bus connection between Götzens and Innsbruck’s main train station, and the bus stop is only 50 metres away.

Innsbruck Airport can be reached within 8 km, and transfers from the airport and the train station are available at an additional cost.

The A13 Brenner Motorway (from and to Italy) is reachable by car in a 10-minutes drive, and the A12 Inntal Motorway (from and to Germany) is a 5-minute drive away.
