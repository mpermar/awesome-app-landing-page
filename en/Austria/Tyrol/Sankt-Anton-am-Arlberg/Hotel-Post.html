Description

Combing modern facilities with a traditional style, this family-run 4-star hotel is right in the centre of St.

Anton, just 200 metres from the Galzigbahn, Gampenbahn and Rendelbahn Cable Cars and the Arlberg Ski Region.

It offers a spacious spa with an indoor pool. Free private parking and free WiFi are available.

Spa facilities at Hotel Post include a bio sauna, a Finnish sauna, a salt water steam bath, an aroma steam bath, an infrared cabin, a hot tub, a sun bed, and a relaxation room with water beds.

Massages are available on request. The Post Hotel’s restaurant serves Austrian and international dishes, as well as a wide selection of fine wines from all over the world recommended by a professional sommelier.

Special diet menus are also available. Guests can enjoy the stylish bar. The à la carte restaurant is open in winter.

Half-board is available at an additional cost and comprises a rich breakfast buffet with healthy dishes, afternoon snacks, and dinner.

Dinner consists of a 4-course dinner in winter and a buffet in summer. A shop is on-site, while guests receive a discount on the Sports and Clothing Shop Alber.

Guests' entertainment is ensured by the property's weekly live music evenings. The popular Piccadilly, a bar with live music, is located underneath the hotel.

Garage parking is possible on request and at a surcharge. Several hiking and cycling tracks start right outside and guided hiking tours and guided electric mountainbike tours with the hotel's guide are offered in summer.

E-bikes can be rented at the property for this tour. From late June to late September, the St.

Anton Summer Card is included in the rate. This card offers many free benefits and discounts, including free use of local cable cars for 1 day and buses.
