Description

Opened in December 2017, Mountain Spa Residences is situated a 20-minute walk from the centre of Sankt Anton am Arlberg in the quiet hamlet of Nasserein, 800 metres from Nassereinbahn ski lift, offering 600 m² spa facilities with indoor and outdoor swimming pools, sauna, steam and wellness zones.

All apartments have large open-plan living space with dining tables, single sofa beds, and large windows.

Some feature views of the mountains or the valley below. The bathrooms have baths or showers.

All apartments have fully fitted kitchens equipped with dishwasher and coffee machine.

In the winter season, guests can enjoy a drink and a light bite at the on-site cafe (only open in the winter.

There is a bread service available for the mornings, on pre-order only and to be collected at reception.

During the winter season, a free hotel shuttle from and to Nassereinbahn ski lift is available, which goes in the morning and late afternoon.

The aparthotel has free on-site covered parking and WiFi is available throughout the property.

Guests can engage in various activities, such as skiing, golfing and cycling. Galzig is 5.

7 km from Mountain Spa Residences. The nearest airport is Innsbruck Airport, 95 km from Mountain Spa Residences.
