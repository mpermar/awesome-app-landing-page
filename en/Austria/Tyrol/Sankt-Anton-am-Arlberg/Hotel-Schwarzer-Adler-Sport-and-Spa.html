Description

The traditional 4-star Hotel Schwarzer Adler - Sport & Spa is located in the pedestrian zone in the heart of Sankt Anton, just a 5-minute walk from the cable cars.

Free WiFi is available. The individually furnished rooms come with cable TV, a seating area, and a bathroom.

Some rooms have a balcony. The Adler Vital Spa features an indoor pool with a waterfall and massage jets, an outdoor pool, indoor and outdoor saunas, steam baths, and a relaxation zone.

A variety of massages and beauty treatments, as well as hay baths, is also available.

In summer, guests can relax on the sunbathing lawn of Hotel Schwarzer Adler - Sport & Spa.

In the evening a wide range of culinary delights, from 4-course meals and speciality buffets to romantic gala dinners, is offered.

The bar and the wine cellar provide a wide choice of fine drinks. Many shops, restaurants and bars are only a few steps away.
