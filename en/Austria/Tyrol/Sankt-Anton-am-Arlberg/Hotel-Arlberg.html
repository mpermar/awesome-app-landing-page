Description

Only 300 metres from the Nasserein cable car base station lies Hotel Arlberg in St.

Anton's town centre. It features a spa area with sauna and indoor swimming pool and free bicycle rental in the warmer months.

Hotel Arlberg is directly located in the Arlberg ski area, the ski bus to the Lech ski area stops 800 metres away.

All other ski areas in the vicinity can be reached by skiing. All spacious rooms have a bathroom and are furnished with warm colours, wooden pieces and local craftsmanship.

In winter, the hotel offers a 24-hour reception service. Half-board comprises a breakfast buffet and a dinner of choice from the daily menu.
