Description

Spectacularly located at an altitude of 2200 metres, this luxurious 4-star hotel boasts stunning views of the surrounding Ötztal peaks and assures its visitors vitality and relaxation.

Hotel Riml offers activities for all its guests – be it skiing, golf, wellness or beauty treatments.

Tee off on one of the worlds most beautiful and technically-advanced indoor golf courses or sample top cuisine at the restaurant.

The hotel radiates a traditional homely atmosphere, complemented by attentive service and staff.

The extensive wellness area, 1000 m² in size, gives you the chance to combine a skiing holiday with wellness and recreation.

Treat yourself to a massage or swim in the pool before having a drink at the hotel bar.

Both the rooms and public areas are stylishly decorated and designed to meet the highest level of comfort.
