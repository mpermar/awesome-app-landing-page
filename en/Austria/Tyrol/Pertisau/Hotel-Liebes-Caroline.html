Description

Situated in Pertisau, 150 metres from the centre and the shore of the Achensee Lake, family-run Hotel Liebes Caroline offers a spacious wellness area and rooms with a balcony.

The Karwendelbahn Cable Car is just 300 metres away. All rooms features rustic, wooden furniture and a flat-screen TV.

A seating area and a bathroom with a hairdryer and a shower or a bathtub are standard in all rooms.

Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas. The on-site wellness area comprises a Finnish sauna, steam baths, different pools and an infrared cabin.

You can work out in Hotel Liebes Caroline’s gym and request a relaxing massage. Children can romp around on the playground or play table tennis and table soccer.

A cosy lounge with a bar and a fireplace is provided on site. A buffet breakfast is available every morning.

Half board includes breakfast, a cake buffet in the afternoon and a 4-course dinner in the evening.

The Liebes Caroline hotel serves Austrian cuisine and a wide range of different wines.

Special diet menus can be requested. A ski storage room with a ski boot dryer is available on site and the closest cross-country skiing trails start right behind the house.

The closest tennis court is 150 metres away and horseback riding is available 400 metres from the hotel.

Wattens can be reached within 25-minutes by car and Wörgl is a 30-minute drive away.
