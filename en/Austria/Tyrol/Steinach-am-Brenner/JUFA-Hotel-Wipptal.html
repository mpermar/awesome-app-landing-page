Description

JUFA Hotel Wipptal is located in Steinach am Brenner, between Innsbruck and the Brenner Pass.

It is located outside the village centre, next to the ski lift. Guests have direct access to the ski slopes.

The modern and spacious rooms come with cable TV and a bathroom with a shower. Half-board as well as full-board meal plans are available on request.
