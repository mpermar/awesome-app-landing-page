Description

The family-run Aktiv Hotel Zur Rose in Steinach am Brenner is the ideal choice for an unforgettable active holiday in winter and in summer, with skiing, cross-country skiing, hiking or mountain biking.

A steam bath and an infra-red cabin is available. The hotel offers single and double rooms, as well as spacious apartments.

Guests can spend pleasant hours by the tiled stove in the lobby, in the library, at the hotel bar or with a drink in the wine and wine tasting cellar, or can enjoy the culinary pleasures served in the stone pine parlour and the non-smoking dining room.

A 4-course dinner can be booked on arrival. On arrival, you can also opt for a basic breakfast including just coffee and croissants for a 50% discounted rate.

A mountain bike and an e-bike rental as well as a cleaning station for mountain bikes is provided.

A underground garage is available free of charge. Because of its central location near the Italian border, the hotel is also ideal for overnight stops.

Excursions to nearby South Tyrol or to Innsbruck, Wattens, the Bavarian Royal Castles and Lake Achen are great options for any holiday at the Aktiv Hotel Zur Rose.
