Description

One of our top picks in Galtür.Only 2,650 feet from the ski elevators and slopes of the Galtür-Silvretta Ski Area, the Alpenresidenz Ballunspitze Wellness- & Kinderhotel features a 1969 ft² spa. The indoor pool offers panoramic views of the Silvretta Mountains.

All rooms feature satellite TV, a safe, and a bathroom with hairdryer.

Guests can enjoy a rich breakfast buffet and a selection of Tyrolean and international cuisine in the restaurant.

The Alpenresidenz Ballunspitze Wellness- & Kinderhotel offers a wide range of facilities and services for children, including skiing and swimming lessons, theater and cinema performances, and indoor and outdoor play areas. Professional baby-sitting and child care is available.

The 1969 ft² Vitalia spa area includes various saunas and steam baths, hot tubs, a fitness center, and a relaxation area on 2 levels. A large selection of massage and beauty treatments is available.

The center of Galtür is 1,300 feet away from the Ballunspitze hotel. Free private parking is available on site.

As of summer 2013, the Silvretta All Inclusive Card will be included in all rates during summer season. It offers free use of cable cars in the region, of the busses from Landeck to Bielerhöhe, and free access to the indoor and outdoor pools and to the lake in the Paznaun Valley.
