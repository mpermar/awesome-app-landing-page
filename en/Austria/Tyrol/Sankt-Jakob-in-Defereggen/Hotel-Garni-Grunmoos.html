Description

Hotel Garni Grünmoos enjoys a quiet location, 1 km from Sankt Jakob’s centre, in East Tyrol’s Defereggen Valley.

It provides views of Austria’s highest mountain, the Großglockner, and free Wi-Fi.

Guests can enjoy a rich breakfast buffet at the Hotel Garni Grünmoos. All rooms and apartments provide balconies, cable TVs and a bathroom.

The property's lounge area features a wood stove. The spa area of the Hotel Garni Grünmoos features a steam bath, several kinds of saunas and a swimming pond.
