Description

Surrounded by the impressive mountains of the Ziller Valley, this 4-star superior hotel features a 4,000 m² wellness area with a heated outdoor pool, a gym, and a natural swimming pond.

It is very close to the Fügen-Spieljoch Ski Area and 2. 5 km from the 18-hole Zillertal Golf Course.

Free WiFi is available in all rooms and in the spa area. Sport- und Wellnesshotel Held offers a 75 m² pool with a waterfall, a hot tub, 7 saunas, and 3 relaxation rooms.

A hot tub and a panoramic relaxation room can be found outdoors. Bathrobes are available free of charge during your stay, and there is also a children’s playroom with a ping pong table.

All rooms have a seating area and a safe and in the modern bathroom you can find free toiletries and a hairdryer.

A champagne breakfast and a dance evening with live music are organised. Half-board comprises a rich breakfast buffet, a lunch buffet, cake in the afternoon, and a 6-course dinner with a choice of dishes and a salad buffet, or a themed buffet.

The free ski bus takes you from the hotel to 3 different ski areas. You can buy ski passes directly at Hotel Held.
