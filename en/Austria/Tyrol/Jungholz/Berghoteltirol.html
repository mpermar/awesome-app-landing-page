Description

Located at 1,050 metres above sea level in Jungholz, Berghoteltirol features a spa area with mountain views and an indoor pool.

It also has a bowling alley. The spa area boasts 3 saunas, a hot tub, and a sun terrace.

Guests can enjoy stunning views through the panoramic windows or relax during a massage or beauty treatment.

The rustically furnished rooms all have a work desk and satellite TV. Most rooms have a balcony.

Hairdryers are available at the reception. The restaurant serves a rich breakfast buffet and a wide range of Tyrolean and international dishes.

Berghoteltirol also has an elegant bar with an open fireplace and panoramic mountain views.
