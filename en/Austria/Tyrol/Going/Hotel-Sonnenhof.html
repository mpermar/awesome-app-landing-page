Description

Hotel Sonnenhof is situated in the Wilder Kaiser Ski area a few-minute walk from the Astberg chair lift.

It features a large wellness area with an indoor pool, several saunas and a fitness room.

The landscaped garden awaits children with a playground, including a trampoline. The Tyrolean-style rooms are fitted with a seating area and a private bathroom with a shower.

Most rooms come with a balcony. Buffet breakfast as well as half-board is available at the Sonnenhof.

The property also extended half-board with a buffet in the afternoon. You can enjoy refreshments at the bar with an own brewery.

Ski equipments can be stored in the ski storage room on site. Free private parking is provided.

You can find golf court, tennis courts as well as hiking and biking paths in the immediate vicinity.
