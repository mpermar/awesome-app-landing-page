Description

Opening in December 2012, this 4-star hotel in the centre of Serfaus is a 3-minute walk from the Serfaus-Fiss-Ladis Ski Area.

It features a spa area with an indoor pool, free Wi-Fi, and a restaurant serving international and traditional Tyrolean cuisine.

The modern and spacious rooms at the ALFA hotel feature a flat-screen cable TV, a seating area, and a bathroom.

Some rooms have a balcony. Spa facilities at the ALFA include a hot tub, a Finnish sauna, a bio sauna, a steam bath, and an infrared cabin.

There is also a terrace and a relaxation room with a tea and soft drink bar. Guests can use a ski storage room with a ski boot dryer.

On-site private parking, including an underground car park, is available for free.

In winter, ski passes can be bought on site. A public outdoor pool is a 10-minute walk away and can be used upon a surcharge.
