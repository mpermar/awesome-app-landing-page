Description

This family-friendly 4-star-superior hotel is located in the heart of Serfaus. All rooms have balconies and it offers an indoor pool, fine cuisine, free Wi-Fi and free parking.

Within a 10-minute walk or a short subway ride, you can reach the Komperdell Cable Car into the Serfaus-Fiss-Ladis Ski Area from the Hotel 3 Sonnen.

The village of Serfaus provides lots of activities and facilities for kids, like the groundhog trail, a playroom and adventure hiking paths for the entire family.

A fitness room, a swimming pool with a counter-current facility and massage jets, a sunny terrace, a sauna and steam bath area are also available on site.

There is a relax area and a tea bar. Children enjoy a games room and the kid's club.

Full board includes a buffet breakfast with organic products and freshly prepared eggs any style, various snacks for lunch, an afternoon buffet with soups, salads and pastries and fine Tyrolean cuisine for dinner.

Children can have their dinner under professional supervision at the kids' table.
