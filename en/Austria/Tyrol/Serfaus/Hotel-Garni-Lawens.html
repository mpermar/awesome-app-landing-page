Description

All rooms have a balcony and are equipped with satellite TV and a work desk. A rich buffet breakfast with organic products from the hotel’s own farm is offered. Free private parking is available on site.

The sunny location of Hotel Lawens invites guests to relax on the terrace or on the grounds. A children’s playground and a ping-pong table are available. During cold weather guests can linger in the cozy lounge bar with a fireplace.

The Garni Lawens is an ideal starting point for hiking, mountain biking or Nordic walking. During winter guests can enjoy one mile of slopes which can be directly accessed from the hotel.
