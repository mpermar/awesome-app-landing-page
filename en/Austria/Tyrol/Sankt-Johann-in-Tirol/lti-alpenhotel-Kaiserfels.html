Description

Newly opened after a full renovation in January 2015, this 4-star hotel is right next to the Eichenhof Ski Lift and provides direct access to the Sankt Johann in Tirol Ski Area.

The modern spa area includes a large indoor pool. The lti alpenhotel Kaiserfels features a restaurant, 2 bars, an après ski hut, 2 large sun terraces, a lounge, and free WiFi access.

A sports shop and a children’s playground are also on site. Spa facilities of the Kaiser beauty&wellness Spa include a Finnish sauna, steam bath, bio sauna and herbal sauna, fitness and aerobics rooms, and a beauty and massage centre.

The modern and spacious rooms and suites all offer mountain views and digital infotainment.

In summer, a wide range of activities, including ballooning, rafting, climbing, guided bicycle and hiking tours, yoga, aqua fitness, skydiving, and paragliding, can be booked on site.

A ski school and ski rental are right next door, and the Fieberbrunn Ski Area can be reached by ski bus in 15 minutes.

There is a connection from the Fieberbrunn Ski Area to the Saalbach Hinterglemm and Leogang Ski Area.
