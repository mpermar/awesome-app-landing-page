Description

With direct access to the slopes of the Kühtai Ski Area, this elegant 4-star hotel next to the Kühtai Cable Car offers a 1,000 m² spa area with an indoor pool and a heated outdoor pool.

Free WiFi and free garage parking are available. The spacious, Alpine-style rooms at Hotel Mooshaus offer mountain views, a flat-screen cable TV, a seating area, and a bathroom.

Some have a balcony. In the spa area, guests of the Mooshaus can enjoy panoramic mountain views and relax in 4 different saunas,a steam cabin, a Turkish steam bath, an infrared cabin, A selection of massages and beauty treatments is offered.

The restaurant serves international cuisine and traditional Tyrolean dishes made from regional products.

The hotel also features a bar and lounge. For children, a separate spa section, an indoor playroom, and an outdoor playground are available.

Child care is available every evening except on Saturdays. Guests can use a ski storage room with a ski boot dryer.
