Description

Sporthotel Kühtai is directly next to the ski slopes and the hiking trails. The rooms and the indoor pool offer panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.

Free open air parking is available on site. Spa facilities at Sporthotel Kühtai include a sauna, a herbal steam bath, a solarium and infrared cabin.

The fitness room offers panoramic views, while the indoor pool has massage jets and waterfalls.

Child care is offered free of charge on 6 days a week in the afternoon. The restaurant offers Tyrolean and international cuisine and a wide range of fine wines.

Innsbruck is 35 km away from Sporthotel Kühtai. A transfer from the main train station or the airport in Innsbruck can be arranged at surcharge.
