Description

Located in the centre of the village of Fiss, in the Serfaus-Fiss-Ladis Ski Area, Hotel Garni Alpendiamant offers a spa area with a sauna, a steam bath, an infrared cabin, and a Kneipp pool.

Free WiFi is available. In the Garni Alpendiamant, all rooms provide a balcony or terrace, cable TV, a seating area, and a bathroom.

Buffet breakfast is served every morning, and the traditionally furnished bar also has a coffee machine and a snacks vending machine.

A swimming pond with a sunbathing terrace is available. The bus stop is only a few steps away, and the cable car to the ski area can be reached within a 5-minute walk from the Garni Alpendiamant.
