Description

Surrounded by high mountains, picturesque castles and idyllic villages, this historic, family-run hotel is located right in the middle of Landeck and offers free WiFi access.

Hotel Schrofenstein features a restaurant serving traditional Austrian and light Mediterranean cuisine.

Half-board is available on request. The Venet ski area is only 1 km from the property, and the free ski bus stops 100 metres away.

It takes guests to many well-known ski regions such as Serfaus, Kaunertal, St. Anton and Ischgl.

In summer the surrounding area offers a wide range of sports and leisure options, from hiking and mountain biking to climbing, rafting, and paragliding.

The Inn Bicycle Trail can be easily reached. 100 metres from the hotel, there is a parking garage and free garage parking for motorcycles and bicycles.

Parking tickets are available at the front desk. Additionally, guests can also park in the public area behind the property at a surcharge from 09:00 to 18:00, available for free after 18:00 in the evenings and all day on weekends.
