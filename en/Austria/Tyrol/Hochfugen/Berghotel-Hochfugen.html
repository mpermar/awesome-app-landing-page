Description

Located in the Hochfügen ski area in the Ziller Valley, the Berghotel is right next to the ski slopes.

It features an indoor pool and a balcony in each room. The modern rooms at Berghotel Hochfügen feature satellite TV and a bathroom with bathrobes and a hairdryer.

Spa facilities include a fitness centre, a sauna, a steam bath, a sunbed, and an infrared cabin.

Massage and beauty treatments are available. The Berghotel features a restaurant serving Tyrolean and international cuisine, an après ski bar, and an evening bar.

Guests can enjoy bowling, billiard and darts. An areal for archery is also closeby.

A ski school and a ski rental can be found on site. A natural toboggan run, a cross-country ski run and a winter hiking trail are only steps away from Berghotel Hochfügen, which is located at 1,500 metres above sea level.
