Description

This traditional hotel in the centre of Oetz offers a spa area and an outdoor tennis court.

All rooms provide free Wi-Fi and mountain views. The Hoch-Oetz Ski Area is 700 metres away, and a free ski bus stops 30 metres away.

Hotel 3 Mohren occupies a historic building from the early 20th century. Spa facilities include a sauna, steam bath, and sun bed.
