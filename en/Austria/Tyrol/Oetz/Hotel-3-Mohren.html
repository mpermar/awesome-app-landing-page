Description

This traditional hotel in the centre of Oetz offers a spa area and an outdoor tennis court.

All rooms provide free Wi-Fi and mountain views. The Hoch-Oetz Ski Area is 700 metres away, and a free ski bus stops 30 metres away.

Hotel 3 Mohren occupies a historic building from the early 20th century. Spa facilities include a sauna, steam bath, and sun bed.

The large garden includes a furnished sun terrace and a children’s playground. Guests of the 3 Mohren Hotel can play billiards and use a ski storage room with a ski boot dryer.

Private parking is available free of charge. In summer, bicycles can be hired free of charge.

A ski school is right next door. The Area47 adventure centre is 5 km away, and it is 18 km to the Aquadome Thermal Spa.
