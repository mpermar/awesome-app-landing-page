Description

Natur & Aktiv Resort Ötztal is a resort in the style of a small Alpine village overlooking scenic mountains at the entrance of the Ötz Valley, only 500 metres from the cable car.

It offers free WiFi and free private parking. The spacious rooms and apartments are located in 14 different buildings made from natural wood.

Natur & Aktiv Resort Ötztal has a courtyard with a restaurant where a large breakfast buffet, an afternoon snack and 4-course evening meals are served.

There is also a 600 m² spa area offering massages, a sunny terrace, and a village square with a small river, a 300 m² pond, meadows, and gardens.

A sports centre with a beach volleyball court, a climbing tower, a mountain bike centre, and an archery course is also part of Natur & Aktiv Resort Ötztal.

From mid-May to mid-October, Natur & Aktiv Resort Ötztal offers a free weekly programme with many activities such as guided hikes and mountain bike tours.

Rafting, canyoning, climbing, archery, and paragliding are offered. In July and August, various activities for children are offered free of charge.

In winter the property has its own ski rental, ski storage facilities and ski school right in the Hochoetz Ski Area.

Snow shoe hiking and sauna infusions are offered free of charge. The village centre of Oetz is only 250 metres away.
