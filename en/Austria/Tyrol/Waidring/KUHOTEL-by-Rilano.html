Description

This hotel is 500 metres from Waidring village centre and 800 metres from Steinplatte Ski Lift.

WiFi, private parking with carports and the 2,000 m² spa and wellness centre are all available free of charge.

The rooms at the 4-star KUHOTEL by Rilano have a balcony, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom.

The KUHOTEL by Rilano's restaurant serves local and international specialities prepared with local ingredients.

A pool, sauna, steam bath and fitness centre belong to the wellness area, where massages can also be booked at a surcharge.
