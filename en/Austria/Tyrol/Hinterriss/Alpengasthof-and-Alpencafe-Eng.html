Description

Located in Eng in the area of Grosser Ahornboden, Alpengasthof & Alpencafé Eng offers a sun terrace with scenic mountain views, a restaurant and a spa area.

Free WiFi is provided. Each room comes with a balcony, a TV and a bathroom with bath or shower and free toiletries.

A rich breakfast buffet is available every morning in the newly renovated breakfast room.

The spa area at Alpengasthof & Alpencafé Eng comprises a sauna, a steam bath and a hot tub.

Other facilities offered at the property include a games room, luggage storage and a children's playground.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling and hiking.
