Description

Alpenresidenz am Mühlbach is an apartment house in an idyllic and quiet location in the centre of Obergurgl, offering an exceptionally pleasant and friendly ambience and breathtaking mountain views.

All apartments are light-flooded and combine modern comfort with traditional cosiness.

They have a fully equipped kitchenette, bedlinen and towels. Fresh bread rolls can be delivered to your apartment from Monday to Saturday on requests.

The sauna can be used free of charge in winter 5 times a week. The sauna is not included during summer.

In summer, numerous hiking trails can be found in the surroundings, while in winter the slopes and the ski school are located right next to Alpenresidenz.

The ski bus stop is only 70 metres away. From late May to mid-October, the Ötztal Premium Card is included in the rate.

This card offers many free benefits and discounts, including free use of local cable cars and buses.
