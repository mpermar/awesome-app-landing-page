Description

Opened in May 2016 and offering a rooftop spa area overlooking the surrounding mountains, Ferienwohnungen Guthof is situated in Schattwald, 150 metres from the Wannenjochbahn Cable Car.

Free WiFi is provided, and 1 underground car park per apartment is available. All apartments feature a south-facing balcony or terrace with mountain views, a private Physiotherm infrared shower, a dining area and a seating area as well as a flat-screen TV in each room.

There is also a well-equipped kitchenette fitted with a dishwasher and Nespresso coffe machine.

The private bathroom is fitted with a spa shower, towel radiator and a hairdryer.

The property has its own children's playground, a bicycle and ski storage room, and guests can also rent bicycles against a surcharge.

Guests can enjoy various activities in the surroundings, including skiing, cycling, cross-country skiing, tobogganing, climbing and hiking in summer as well as in winter.

A bicycle trail and a cross-country ski run are 100 metres away. From May to October, guests enjoy free admission to the public beach of Lake Haldensee and free use of local cable cars.
