Description

Reachable via a beautiful mountain road, the family owned Wohlfühlhotel Berghof offers an award-winning restaurant and a spa area and is specialized in peaceful stays to emerge with nature.

In summer and in winter, the owners who are professional mountain guides offer guided hiking tours 3 times a week and free of charge.

From Wohlfühlhotel Berghof you can reach the Pfundser Tschey high valley within 5 minutes driving or 20 minutes walking.

Guests can reach 5 different ski resorts within 20 km. The Ischgl-Samnaun, Reschenpass-Nauders and Serfaus-Fiss-Ladis Ski Areas are only a few kilometres away.

The Berghof offers free transport service to the ski bus stop. F Some rooms at the Berghof have a balcony or terrace.

To enjoy more calmness, on request the TV can be removed from your room. The restaurant serves traditional Tyrolean cuisine and fine wines.

A dinner is prepared with fresh regional ingredients according to the season. The Berghof’s spa area features a sauna, an infrared cabin, and a relaxation room.

A range of massages and beauty treatments is available. Guests can rent e-bikes. Nordic walking tours and free hiking maps are offered as well.
