Description

Surrounded by the scenic Tyrolean Alps, this 5-star superior hotel in Jochberg, just 7 km south of Kitzbühel, offers indoor and outdoor pools, 2 restaurants, and an award-winning 3,600 m² spa area on 3 floors.

In winter, guests enjoy direct access to the ski slopes. The spacious and elegant rooms at Kempinski Hotel Das Tirol are furnished in a modern Alpine style.

They are air-conditioned and feature a minibar, a tea/coffee maker, satellite TV, CD and DVD players, and an elegant bathroom.

Free WiFi access is available in the entire hotel. In the Sra Bua restaurant, guests can enjoy European and Asian cuisine made from top-quality local products.

The Steinberg restaurant serves 4-course dinners and à la carte dishes as well as lunch.

Guests of Das Tirol Kempinski can enjoy cocktails in the Rubin Bar and a selection of cigars in the cigar lounge and the library.

A fashion boutique, a sports shop with a ski rental, and an underground car park are available.

Limousine service is also offered, and child care is provided in the Kempinski Kids Club.

In summer, guests have direct access to a network of more than 200 km of hiking trails.
