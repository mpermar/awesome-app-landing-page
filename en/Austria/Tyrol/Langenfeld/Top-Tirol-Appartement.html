Description

This family-run apartment building features cozy vacation condos with 2 and 3 rooms, a spa area with an indoor pool, a children’s playroom, a playground, and many free parking spaces.

A bakery, where you can have breakfast, is next door.

A bus stop, restaurants, stores, a ski rental, and a ski school are in the immediate vicinity of Top Tirol Appartements.
