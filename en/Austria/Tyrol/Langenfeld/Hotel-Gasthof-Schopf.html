Description

The family-run Hotel Gasthof Schöpf in Gries close to Längenfeld was completely renovated in autumn 2016 and enjoys a quiet location in the heart of the Ötztal, at 1,573 metres above sea level.

It features a wellness area with a pool and an on-site ski rental and shop. From all rooms you can enjoy panoramic views of the Tyrolean mountains.

Tyroelan specialities and international cuisine are served in the restaurant. An afternoon cake buffet and a fondue evening once a week make the culinary offer complete.

Wellness facilities also include a sauna, a steam bath and an infra-red cabin. For the little guests of the Hotel Gasthof Schöpf there are high chairs and baby monitors available.
