Description

Located in the Ötz Valley, a 5-minute drive from the Aqua Dome Thermal Spa and the centre of Längenfeld, Hotel Bergwelt offers a sun terrace with panoramic mountain views.

The heated indoor pool features a counter-flow swimming system and hot tub. Rooms offer a private balcony and a seating area with a minibar and satellite TV.

Bathrobes and slippers are provided in every room. The apartments are located in the annex, which is 200 metres away.

The self-catering apartments also feature a kitchenette. Set around a stylish lounge room, the spa offers an infrared cabin, steam room and sauna.

Once a week a candle-lit swimming evening is organized at the pool area. The spa has direct access to the garden, where sun loungers are provided in the summer.

Themed buffet dinners are organised at the restaurant, which features elaborate carved-wood ceilings and traditional wooden furniture.

The Sölden ski resort is 15 km away, or 20 minutes on the free ski bus which stops opposite the hotel.

The Bergwelt is 20 km from Ötztal Train Station, and Innsbruck Airport is 65 km away.

From beginning of June to mid-October, the Ötztal Premium Card is included in the rate.

This card offers many free benefits and discounts, including free use of local cable cars and buses.

From 07 June to 31 October 2019, the Thermen Plus Card is included in the rate. This card includes daily admission to the Aqua Dome Thermal Spa (except on the day of check-out and for annex apartments).
