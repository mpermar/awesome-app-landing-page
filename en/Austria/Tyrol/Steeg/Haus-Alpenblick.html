Description

Located in Steeg, a small village in the Lechtal Valley, Haus Alpenblick offers views of the surrounding mountains.

The Warth/Schröcken Ski Area is 11 km away, and a free ski bus stops just 50 metres away.

Free WiFi, satellite TV are provided in each of the modern apartments and rooms at Haus Alpenblick, while only the apartments come with a fully equipped kitchen or kitchenette and a dining and living area separate from the bedroom.

In good weather, guests can relax out on the terrace, use the playground and enjoy a barbecue.

Freshly baked bread rolls are provided every day except Sunday. The nearest supermarket and restaurant are within a 1-minute walk.

One of Europe’s highest rope bridges is a 10-minute drive away. For stays longer than 2 nights, the Lechtal Activ Card is included in room rate.

It offers reduced prices to public swimming pools, mountain railways (also in Lech) and the public bus system.
