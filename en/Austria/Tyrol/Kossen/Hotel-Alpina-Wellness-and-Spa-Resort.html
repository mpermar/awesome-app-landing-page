Description

This 4-star superior hotel, 3 km from Kössen, offers a 3,000 m² spa area with an indoor and an outdoor pool, a tennis court, free Wi-Fi, and 2 restaurants.

The spacious rooms all have a balcony overlooking the Tyrolean and Bavarian Alps.

Hotel Alpina’s spa area includes 8 saunas, 4 steam baths, 2 infrared cabins, a salt-water pool, indoor and outdoor relaxation areas, and a large sun terrace.

A wide range of beauty treatments and massages, including Ayurveda and various Asian treatments, is available.

The rooms at the Alpina Hotel are furnished in an elegant Alpine style and feature a flat-screen satellite TV with free Sky channels, a minibar, a seating area, and a bathroom with a hairdryer and slippers.

The restaurants serve Austrian and international cuisine, as well as Tyrolean specialities.

Full-board includes a breakfast buffet, an afternoon snack, and a dinner buffet or dinner menu.

For children, a playground and an indoor playroom are provided. Free private parking is available on site.

The Hoch-Kössen Ski Area is 2. 5 km away, with a ski bus stopping right outside the hotel.

The access to a large network of cross-country ski runs is right outside as well.

The Kaiserwinkl-Kössen Golf Course is just 200 metres away, and there are 3 other courses within 15 km.
