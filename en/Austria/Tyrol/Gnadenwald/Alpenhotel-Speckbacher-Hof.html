Description

The Alpenhotel Speckbacher Hof is located in Gnadenwald in the Karwendelgebirge Mountains with a view to the Inn Valley and the Glungezer Mountain, a 15-minute drive from Innsbruck.

It offers a restaurant and free WiFi. Guests can enjoy the Speckbacher Hof’s Austrian and international cuisine on the terrace or in its dining rooms furnished in a traditional Tyrolean style.

There is also a modern bar, a lounge with an open fireplace, a sun terrace, and a café.

The Speckbacher Hof's wellness area features a sauna, a steam bath, and a hot tub.

A wide range of beauty and massage treatments is available. A relaxation room with water beds and a foot massage pool are available as well.

The hotel also offers a miniature golf course and a playground with a large trampoline.
