Description

Situated in Fulpmes on a hillside, 2 km from the Schlick 2000 Ski Area, Family Apart Stubai features a terrace with panoramic views of the Stubai Mountains and spa centre featuring an indoor pool and several sauna.

Free WiFi is available and free garage or outdoor parking is offered. The apartments boast a mountain-view balcony and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

There is also a kitchen, equipped with a dishwasher. A microwave, a fridge and stovetop are also offered, as well as a coffee machine and a kettle.

Towels and bed linen are featured. Family Apart Stubai's spa area includes 3 saunas and an infrared cabin.

The indoor pool features massage jets, a counter-current system, and a waterfall.

Guests can enjoy the large garden with a sun lawn and a children's playground. A games room, free bicycles, table tennis and table football facilities are also available.

Child car throughout the day is offered by the Big Family Kids Club, while cots, high chairs, a child carrier and a buggy are available on site.

The Stubai Gletscher Ski Area is 23 km from the Stubai Family Apart, while Innsbruck is 22 km away.

In summer, the Stubai Super Card is included in the rate. This card offers many free benefits and discounts, including free use of local cable cars and public transport in the valley and to Innsbruck.
