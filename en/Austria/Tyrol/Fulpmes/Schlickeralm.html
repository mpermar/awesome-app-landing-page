Description

At the inn, every room includes a closet.

Guests at Schlickeralm can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

The accommodation has a playground. A variety of popular activities are available in the area around Schlickeralm, including skiing, horse riding and cycling.

Kreuzjochbahn 2 is 1.4 miles from the inn. The nearest airport is Kranebitten Airport, 7 miles from Schlickeralm.
