Description

Hotel Donnerhof enjoys a central location in Fulpmes in Tyrol's Stubaital valley and offers you a spa area with indoor and outdoor pools, traditional Tyrolean cuisine, a cosy lounge with an open fireplace and a playroom for children.

You can start every day with a rich buffet breakfast including healthy and organic options with extended breakfast hours on Sundays.

There is also an afternoon buffet and you can enjoy a four-course dinner in the evening.

On-site spa facilities include and indoor/outdoor pool, a sauna, Kneipp baths, a sun bed, steam and herbal baths.

You can also enjoy a massage or cosmetic treatments. Fitness facilities are available as well.
