Description

Located in the heart of the Kleinwalser Valley, Verwöhn-Wellnesshotel Walserhof features a 1,500 m² spa area with 5 new saunas and is an ideal starting point for hiking tours and skiing.

The Hubertuslift Ski Lift is 800 metres away, and free WiFi and free private parking is featured.

Each room includes a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with shower. Some units include a seating area where you can relax.

Guests of the Walserhof can enjoy half-board, including a rich buffet breakfast, a coffee and cake buffet in the afternoon, and a 6-course menu in the evening.

From May to October, the Bergbahnticket Kleinwalsertal/Oberstdorf is included in the rate and offers free use of local cable cars.
