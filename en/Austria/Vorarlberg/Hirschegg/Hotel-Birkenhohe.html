Description

Located right next to the slopes in the Kleinwalsertal ski area, Hotel Birkenhöhe offers a 650 m² large spa area and impressive views of the surrounding mountains.

All units boast a balcony or a terrace. Free private parking is available. The elegantly furnished rooms feature amenities such as a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a seating area.

The private bathroom comes with a hairdryer and bathrobes are provided. The Birkenhöhe Restaurant serves traditional Austrian cuisine and regional specialities from the Kleinwalsertal Valley, with many products coming from nearby farms.

Half board includes an extensive breakfast buffet, soup of the day for lunch, cakes in the afternoon and a 5-course dinner of high quality in the evening.

A barbecue buffet takes place once a week. The 650 m² spa area features an indoor pool, a bio sauna, a Finnish sauna and a steam bath, which can be used free of charge.

The large fitness room, the relaxation rooms, and the terrace offer panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.

A wide range of massage treatments and relaxing baths is also available. In summer, guests can use 8 regional cable cars in Kleinwalsertal and Oberstdorf and public transport in Kleinwalsertal free of charge.
