Description

Surrounded by a scenic mountain landscape in the Montafon Valley and combining modern facilities with a traditional style, Relax- und Vitalhotel Adler offers a 1,000 m² spa area with a large indoor pool and different saunas.

Access to the spa area and WiFi is provided free of charge. The Golm Ski Area is 2 km away.

Relax- und Vitalhotel Adler also features a moonlit bowling lane and a garden where guest can enjoy their breakfast in nice weather.

The non-smoking rooms feature either carpet floors or cherry-wood floors, a flat-screen TV with 35 cable channels, a radio, a telephone, a hairdryer, and a modern bathroom with a hairdryer, and a shower or a bathtub.

Bathrobes and a spa bag are further amenities. An ample breakfast buffet is available every morning and on Sundays guests can enjoy breakfast until 11:00.

The à la carte restaurant serves dishes throughout the day. Guests can enjoy their breakfast or meals in the hotel's garden in nice weather.

Guests can also unwind with a cup of tea in the typical Montafon-style parlour or, in winter, relax at the open fireplace.

The spa area also comprises a steam bath, a sun bed, a free tea bar, and a relaxation area with water beds.

Massages are available on request and a beauty parlour is at guests' disposal. In winter, you can access the cross-country ski run right next to the hotel.

A free ski bus, which stops right outside, takes you to all 5 surrounding ski areas.

The hotel organises guided snow-shoe hikes, cross-country skiing tours and toboggans and sledge are available for guests' use.

A heated ski storage room with ski boot dryer is featured as well. In summer, guided hikes and mountain tours are offered.

Bludenz is a 10-minute drive away, and the S16 motorway can be reached in a 5-minute drive.
