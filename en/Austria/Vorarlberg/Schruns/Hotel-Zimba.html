Description

Hotel Zimba, located in the village of Schruns, offers its guest high-class service and deluxe facilities. You can relax after an active day of hiking or skiing in the wellness area, which includes a sauna, solarium and indoor pool. Spaciously-laid out yet cozy rooms await you after your professional massage.

In colder months, the traditional parlor with its authentic tiled stove is the ideal retreat. Guests can sample excellent regional cuisine or play a round of table tennis. You can enjoy après ski at the bar.
