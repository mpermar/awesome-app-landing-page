Description

Situated directly by the ski slope leading down to the Uga Express Chairlift in Damüls, the 4-star superior Die Mittagspitze offers an extensive spa and an indoor swimming pool.

Free on-site parking is provided. This large and elegant Alpine chalet successfully combines a traditional Alpine atmosphere with modern design furnishings.

Die Mittagspitze features a stylish lounge, a large bar with a winter garden and a stylish restaurant with big panoramic windows.

It serves regional and international dishes made out of local produce. The comfortable rooms all offer a flat-screen satellite TV and a seating area.

They are either furnished in classic or contemporary Alpine style. Some rooms also feature a balcony.

The extensive spa offers different types of saunas and a relaxation rooms. Massages and beauty treatments are also available.

In addition, Alpenhotel provides a free shuttle service to different ski slopes in Damüls - Mellau Ski Area and the Damüls Train Station for its guests.

Special activities for kids, including the babysitting service are also available.

From 01 May to 31 October, the Bregenzerwald Card is included with a minimum stay of 3 nights.

With this card, guests can use all public buses, swimming pools and cable cars for free.
