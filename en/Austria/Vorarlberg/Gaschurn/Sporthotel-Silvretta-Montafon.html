Description

The 4-star Sporthotel Silvretta Montafon in the picturesque village of Gaschurn in the Montafon Valley offers a wide range of sports and leisure options.

WiFi is provided in the public areas for free. Sporthotel Silvretta Montafon features spacious, tastefully furnished rooms comprising a balcony.

Each unit comes with parquet floors or carpet floors and a private bathroom, provided with bathrobes and a hairdryer.

Parking in the underground garage is possible for an additional cost. In the morning, guests can enjoy a rich breakfast buffet.

In the evening, the restaurant offers a 6-course dinner and fine wines. Herbal teas and refreshing drinks are available in the spacious spa area free of charge.

An indoor pool and sauna and fitness areas, as well as guided hikes and outdoor tennis courts are at guests' disposal.

Indoor tennis courts are available at an additional cost. The green fee and the use of the driving range at the Silvretta Golf Course are offered for a surcharge.

It is just a 5-minute walk from the hotel to the cable car station, which provides access to the Silvretta Montafon, a hiking, biking, climbing and skiing area.
