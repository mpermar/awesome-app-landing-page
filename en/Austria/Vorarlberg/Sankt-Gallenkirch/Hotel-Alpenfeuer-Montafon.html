Description

Located in the village of Gortipohl near St. Gallenkirch, 3-star superior Hotel Alpenfeuer Montafon was in 2017 renovated (formerly known as Hotel Alpenrose) is only steps away from the cable cars and ski bus stops of the Montafon-Silvretta ski area.

Free private parking and free WiFi is available. All rooms at Hotel Alpenfeuer Montafon feature traditional wooden furniture, cable TV, and a bathroom with hairdryer.

Half-board includes a rich breakfast buffet and a 4-course dinner in summer (a 5-course dinner in winter) with a choice of dishes.

Hotel Alpenfeuer Montafon features a spa area with 3 saunas and relaxation room, a sun terrace, and a ski storage room with a ski boot dryer.

Guests can play tennis and hire bicycles free of charge. A tennis court and several restaurants and shops and close by.

Ski passes can be bought at the hotel. A backpack with hiking gear is provided in the room and guests can participate in hiking trips.
