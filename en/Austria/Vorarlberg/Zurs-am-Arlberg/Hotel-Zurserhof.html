Description

Individually furnished in an elegant Alpine style, the Zürserhof’s spacious rooms overlook the surrounding mountains. They feature a flat-screen cable TV with a DVD player, a minibar, and a sitting area. Bathrobes and slippers are provided.

The Aureus Spa and the Family Spa offer a combined 34,500 ft² spa area. The Aureus Spa features 10 rooms for beauty and spa treatments, a private spa room, a 3,775 ft² sauna area, a ladies’ spa, and a panoramic hot tub. The new spa will also feature a relaxation room with a library and an open fireplace, a bistro with a bar, and a spa shop. For families, the existing spa will be adapted and will also offer treatments for children and teenagers.

Austrian and international gourmet cuisine are served in the restaurant, with themed evenings offered regularly. Special diet menus are available. Live music is featured every day at the lounge bar.

The on-site sports center includes an indoor tennis court and a fitness center. A ski rental and a sports store are also on site, and guided winter hikes are led. Professional child care for kids of 3 years and older is available. Baby-sitting services can be organized as well.
