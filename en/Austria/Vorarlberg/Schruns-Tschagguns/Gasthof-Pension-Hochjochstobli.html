Description

Offering a sauna, infrared cabin, and restaurant, Gasthof Pension Hochjochstöbli is within a 4-minute walk from the Hochjochbahn Cable Car and a 2-minute walk from the centre of Schruns.

Free WiFi and free private parking are available. The modern, Alpine-style rooms offer views of the Montafon Valley and feature an LED TV with satellite channels and a safe.

The private bathrooms come with a shower and hairdryer. Half-board is available and includes a buffet breakfast and 4-course dinner.

Pension Hochjochstöbli's terrace is furnished with deck chairs. Guests can also relax on the lawn.

Cross-country skiing is possible directly from the house. You can store your ski equipment at the depot next to the cable car for an extra charge.

Entry to the Montafon Theme Park, a 5-minute walk away, is included in the room rate in summer.

Costs may apply for the outdoor pool and tennis courts. Hiking and mountain biking trails are close to the property.
