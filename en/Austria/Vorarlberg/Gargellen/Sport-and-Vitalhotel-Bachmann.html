Description

This 4-star hotel is located in the scenic Gargellen Valley in Vorarlberg, just a 5-minute walk from the cable car.

It features the large Aquarena spa area and an indoor pool. Spa facilities at Hotel Bachmann include various saunas, a waterfall with fresh spring water, 3 steam baths, a and a Kneipp pool.

The indoor pool offers panoramic mountain views and has access to the garden. Massage treatments are available.

The large garden features sunbathing lawns, a pond, a children’s playground, and a sun terrace.

Guests can play open-air chess, as well as billiard, tabletop football, and computer games in the indoor games room.

There is also an in-house cinema for children. The spacious, elegantly furnished rooms at Sport & Vitalhotel Bachmann offer a balcony, satellite TV, a seating area, and a bathroom with hairdryer.

Austrian and international cuisine, as well as a daily breakfast buffet, are offered in the restaurant.

Half board is also available here. The hotel is member of Aktivclub Montafon, Vorarlberg's biggest indoor/outdoor sports and leisure club.

It includes activities such as climbing, football, skatepark, nordic walking, mountain biking, childcare etc.

It is 14 km away and Bachmann's guests have free access. Free private parking and ski storage facilities are available on site.
