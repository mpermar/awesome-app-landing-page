Description

Family-run for over 75 years, the golf hotel Gotthard enjoys a central location in Lech am Arlberg, one of Austria’s most prestigious ski resorts.

The rooms have free WiFi. The newly built 9-hole golf course, Austria's highest place for golfing, offering a 3-hole golf academy, driving range and a practising bunker, is only 200 metres away.

Offering rest and relaxation in a casual atmosphere, the Gotthard features a big indoor pool and a modern gym.

You can enjoy your breakfast with homemade bread and pastries on the new breakfast terrace with morning sun.

The cosy and comfortable rooms are furnished in a colourful country-house style or in a rustic-alpine style.

Guests can rent bicycles, Nordic-walking sticks and backpacks free of charge (subject to availability).

In both summer and winter, the Gotthard in Lech am Arlberg stands for well-being at the highest level.

Locals and guests from all over the world love the comfort and unmistakeable charm of this family-run hotel, which has grown and evolved with each generation.
