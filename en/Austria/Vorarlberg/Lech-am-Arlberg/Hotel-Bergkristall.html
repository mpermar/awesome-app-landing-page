Description

Surrounded by the impressive mountain scenery of the Lechtal Alps, Hotel Bergkristall offers a quiet location next to the ski slopes in Oberlech and features a wide range of spa facilities including various saunas, a hot tub, a solarium and numerous treatments.

Here you will find completely redesigned rooms, a restaurant serving culinary delights and refined wines, and a spa centre with a hot tub, massages and beauty treatments.

The peaceful sun terrace affords beautiful panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.

Your skiing pleasure starts right at Hotel Bergkristall's doorstep. A ski school is in the immediate vicinity, and the nearest ski lift is about 150 metres away.

Oberlech is completely car-free and can only be reached by cable car from the centre of Lech.

At your arrival in Lech tickets for the first ascent will be waiting for you at the valley station.

Your luggage will also be taken care of, letting you start a carefree holiday without lugging suitcases.

If you arrive by car, you will receive a compulsory ticket at a surcharge for the hotel's own car park in the underground car park next to the cable car station.

Reservation for parking is needed, and it is necessary to contact the hotel well in advance to reserve a garage parking space.
