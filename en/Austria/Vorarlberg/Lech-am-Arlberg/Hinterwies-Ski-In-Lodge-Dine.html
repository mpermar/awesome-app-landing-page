Description

Set right at the slope next to the ski lift, Hotel Hinterwies offers a quiet, south-facing location.

The centre of Lech am Arlberg is only a few minutes’ walk away. Free WiFi access is available in the entire hotel.

The restaurant serves Austrian cuisine with a Mediterranean flavour and offers a wide range of fine wines.

From December 2017 the property will offer a new food concept with fresh and healthy compositions, prepared in a frontcooking station.

The winter garden with an open fireplace and the sun terrace with mountain view invite to relax.

A spa area with sauna, solarium and massages, a fitness room, and facilities for table tennis and tabletop football can also be found at Hotel Hinterwies.
