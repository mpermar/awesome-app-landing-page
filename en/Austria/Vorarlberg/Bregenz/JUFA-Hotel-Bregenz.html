Description

JUFA Hotel Bregenz is only 500 metres away from the train station and a 3- to 5-minute walk from Lake Constance, with the lido and the festival stage.

Guests have free access to 3 public pools. All rooms feature a flat-screen TV, free WiFi access, and a private bathroom with shower.

It features lounges and multi-purpose rooms, play rooms, a ski shed and a bicycle shelter.

Leisure options include table tennis, table football and board games. A café features an outdoor dining area and a playground for small children.

Bed linen and towels are included in the price. The reception of the JUFA Hotel Bregenz is open all year from 07:00 to 22:00.

Free access to the public pools include the Strandbad Public Beach and to the Bregenz Public Indoor Pool, both a 3-minute walk away, and to the Mili, a historical bathing jetty, a 15-minute walk away.
