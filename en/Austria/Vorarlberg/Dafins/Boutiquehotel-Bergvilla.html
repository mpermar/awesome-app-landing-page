Description

Featuring free WiFi throughout, Boutiquehotel Bergvilla is situated in Dafins in the Rhine Valley in the Vorarlberg region.

The air-conditioned hotel is 30 km from Lake Constance and 34 km from Vaduz and Liechtenstein, and features an indoor pool on the upper floor and impressive mountain views.

Free private parking is available on site. There is a dining area and a kitchen equipped with dishwasher - the kitchen will only be available if the whole property is booked exclusively.

Flat-screen TVs with with more than 150 channels including free Sky channels are available in every room as well as in the lounge and restaurant.

Other facilities at Boutiquehotel Bergvilla include an outdoor sauna and a breakfast restaurant.

Guided ski safaris are available upon request and a surcharge. Other guided tours, such as hiking tours for beginners, food tours with regional specialties, wine tasting and tours to local spots are also available for a surcharge.
