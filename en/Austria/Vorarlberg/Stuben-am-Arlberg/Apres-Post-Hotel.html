Description

Situated right in the centre of Stuben, Hotel Post features airy rooms with modern, private bathrooms.

For relaxation, the hotel offers a steam bath, 2 saunas and an infra-red cabin. With cable TV and a safe, the rooms at Post Hotel also include a radio and telephone.

Each of the en-suite bathrooms come with a hairdryer. The à la carte restaurant has a wine cellar and an adjoining terrace for bright and sunny afternoons.

Several pubs and bars are situated within a 2-minute walk. The Finnish sauna and bio sauna offer some warmth during a chilly day.

Several body massages are available during winter. Children can enjoy themselves with table football in the children’s room.

Post is situated a 2-minute walk from the Albonabahn and 3 km from Langen’s train station.
