Description

This family-run 4-star hotel enjoys in a quiet location in Bezau in the Bregenz Forest.

"Die Sonnigen" - Hotel und Familienspaß features a sauna and relaxation area, a large children’s playroom with a garden, and professional child care.

Guests of “Die Sonnigen” can use a ski storage room and benefit from free WiFi in public areas.

Pushchairs and baby monitors are provided, and various activities for children, such as barbecuing and archery, are offered.

The breakfast buffet includes homemade bread, fresh butter, regional cheeses, and homemade jams.

The traditionally furnished restaurant features a summer terrace and serves light cuisine and homemade specialities, as well as a separate children’s buffet.

Half-board is available on request. Facilities include a library, an internet corner, an underground car park, and a sun terrace.

Hotel die Sonnigen also offers guided hikes and organises wine tastings. A ski bus stop is right in front of the hotel.

Bregenz is 30 km away. From 01 May to 31 October, the Bregenzerwald Card is included with a minimum stay of 3 nights.

With this card, you can use all public buses, swimming pools and cable cars for free.
