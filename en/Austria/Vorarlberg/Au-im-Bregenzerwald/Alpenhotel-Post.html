Description

Alpenhotel Post offers a quiet location off the main road in the centre of the picturesque village of Au in the Bregenz Forest.

It offers a spa area with an indoor pool, and a restaurant with a bar and a sun terrace.

The rooms feature modern wooden furniture, satellite TV, a seating area, and a bathroom.

Most rooms have a balcony. Free Wi-Fi is available in all rooms. The spa area at the Post Alpenhotel features a children's pool, a Finnish sauna, a herbal steam bath, and a relaxation room.

For children a playground and a playroom with professional child care (40 hours a week) are available.

Free parking is available on site. Hiking trails and a cross-country ski run start right outside.

Guided motorbike tours are offered. From 01 May to 31 October, the Bregenzerwald Card is included with a minimum stay of 3 nights.

With this card, you can use all public buses, swimming pools and cable cars for free.
