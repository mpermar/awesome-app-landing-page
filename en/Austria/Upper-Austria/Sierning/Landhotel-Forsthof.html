Description

Located in Sierning in the Upper Austria Region, 29 km from Linz, a family-run Landhotel Forsthof boasts a sauna and water sports facilities.

The hotel has a fitness centre and a steambath, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.

Free private parking and free WiFi are available on site. All rooms feature a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Some rooms offer a relaxing seating area, while some have views of the pool or garden.

Each room includes a private bathroom. You will find room service at the property.

You can play tennis and squash at the hotel. The hotel also offers bike hire. Wels is 25 km from Landhotel Forsthof, while Bad Schallerbach is 36 km away.
