Description

JUFA Hotel Spital am Pyhrn is located in Spital am Pyhrn, 4 km from the Wurzeralm Cable Car, and offers a spa area, a restaurant, and a ski storage room.

Free WiFi is available. Each room comes with a TV, a seating area, and a bathroom.
