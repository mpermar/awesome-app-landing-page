Description

Located between Hinterstoder and Windischgarsten in southern Upper Austria, Ferienhotel Gut Enghagen is a 3-star hotel surrounded by 75 hectares of meadows and forests.

It offers pony rides and has an outdoor pool. This traditional estate, first mentioned in 1492, is today a hotel with a working farm.

The third generation of owners now maintains old traditions and customs. Gut Enghagen features a large playground with a trampoline, a go-kart track, and a football pitch.

The restaurant serves traditional Austrian cuisine with meat and fish from the farm, many organic products, and water from the private spring.

The spacious rooms are equipped with wooden furniture and offer views of the meadows and mountains.

40 km of slopes await guests in the Hinterstoder ski area, which is 9 km away. It can reached with a free ski bus and offers free skiing to children under 10 years of age.

The A9 motorway is only a short drive away from the Gut Enghagen. Parking is available free of charge.
