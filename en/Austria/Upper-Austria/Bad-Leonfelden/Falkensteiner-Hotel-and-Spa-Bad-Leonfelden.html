Description

The 2,500 m² Acquapura Spa offers a spa and water world on 2 floors with a wide range of wellness options.

Enjoy a heated indoor and outdoor pool (also in winter), with indoor access and plenty of deck chairs.

Public areas include a hall with open fireplace, a fireplace room, a bar, a vinotheque, a library and a cigar lounge.

The spacious Superior and double rooms are very comfortably and elegantly furnished and have balconies with beautiful views of the surrounding area.

Only a 30-minute drive away from Linz, the Falkensteiner Hotel & Spa Bad Leonfelden is the ideal starting point to discover the beauty of the Mühlviertel region and the Bohemian Forest.
