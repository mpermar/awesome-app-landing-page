Description

The Hubertushof is a traditional 4-star hotel in the centre of the spa town of Bad Ischl, next to a park and only steps away from the Imperial Villa.

It offers free WiFi and a spa area with an indoor pool. Spa facilities include a sauna, an infrared cabin, and a thermal salt pool.

Massages are also offered. Landhotel Hubertushof's restaurant serves traditional Salzkammergut cuisine and a wide range of fine Austrian wines.

The Eurotherme spa centre is only a 3-minute walk away from Landhotel Hubertushof.
