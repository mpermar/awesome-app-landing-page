Description

Set in Wiedmais, 2 km from the cross-country ski trail in Oberaschau, Bauernhof Familie Nussbaumer offers a garden with a children’s playground and free Wi-Fi access in some hotel rooms.

Free private parking is possible on site. The accommodation units include a private bathroom and cable TV.

Some have a fully equipped kitchen and a balcony overlooking Lake Attersee. The nearest restaurant is 1 km away and grocery stores are 6 km from the property.

The Nußdorf Golf Course is 7 km away and Lake Attersee can be reached in a 10-minute walk.
