Description

At the farm stay, each room includes a closet. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a hair dryer, while selected rooms have a terrace. The rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

Bauernhof Familie Knoblechner has a playground. Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Nussdorf am Attersee, like cycling.

Salzburg is 21 miles from Bauernhof Familie Knoblechner. The nearest airport is W. A. Mozart Airport, 24 miles from the farm stay.
