Description

Situated 2. 5 km from the centre of Sankt Wolfgang and 400 metres from the beach at Lake Wolfgang, which guests can use for free, the smallest 4-star hotel at Lake Wolfgang offers free WiFi, a spa area, and a restaurant with a beer garden.

Garage parking is possible on site. The modern rooms and suites at Hotel Bürglstein offer lake or mountain views, a flat-screen satellite TV, parquet floors, and a bathroom with a hairdryer.

The Bürglstein Hotel’s restaurant serves Austrian cuisine prepared with seasonal products, as well as a wide range of fine wines, brandies and organic juices.

The 100m² large spa area includes a Finnish sauna, and infrared cabin and a relaxation room.

A storage room for skis and bicycles, as well as free outdoor parking spaces are available on site.

Garage parking spaces are available at a surcharge. Hiking trails start right outside, and a large children's playground is only 50 metres away.

There is a bus stop right in front of the hotel. Bad Ischl is only 12 km away, and it is 40 km to Salzburg.

Both cities can be reached by an hourly bus connection. At a surcharge, the hotel's own taxi and shuttle service takes guest to the train station and to Salzburg Airport.
