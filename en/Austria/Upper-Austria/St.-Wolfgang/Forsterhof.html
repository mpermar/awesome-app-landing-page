Description

The Försterhof offers a private beach with deckchairs and sunshades on Lake Wolfgang, 500 metres away, a restaurant with a garden terrace, a sauna, and free WiFi.

Electric bicycles can be hired at a surcharge. The spacious rooms at Hotel Försterhof have a seating area, satellite TV, a safe, a hairdryer, and a bathroom.

Some rooms have a balcony. A varied selection of free activities such as guided hikes, meditation, yoga and singing bowl therapies is offered every day.

The Schafbergbahn Cable Car and the shipping pier on the lake are both 500 metres away.
