Description

Located in Unterach on the shores of Attersee Lake, Hotel Seegasthof Stadler has a private beach and a large lawn for sunbathing.

Guests can use free WiFi in the lobby. The Seegasthof’s restaurant serves international and regional cuisine in the bright dining room.

Floor-to-ceiling windows open onto the spacious terrace where guests can enjoy their meals in nice weather.

Each room features a spacious, modern bathroom with toiletries. A flat-screen satellite TV, a minibar and a seating area are available as well.

All rooms have a balcony. Guests can relax in the sauna and steam bath or at the lobby’s open fireplace.

A children’s playroom and playground are available on site. Free bicycles can be rented at Hotel Stadler.

Guests can also make use of the water sports facilities at the lake. Seegasthof Stadler is 5 km away from the centre of Nussbach am Attersee.
