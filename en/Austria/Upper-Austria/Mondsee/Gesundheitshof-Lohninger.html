Description

Set on a hillside and surrounded by a well-groomed garden, Gesundheitshof-Lohninger is located 3 km from the centre of Mondsee and offers apartments, an outdoor pool, a sauna, and impressive views of Lake Mondsee and the surrounding mountains.

The accommodation will provide you with a TV and a seating area. There is a well-equipped kitchen with a microwave and an oven.

Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and slippers. You can enjoy mountain views and garden views from the room.

At Gesundheitshof-Lohninger guests can relax in the garden. Other facilities offered at the property include a children's playground.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including cycling and hiking.

The property offers free parking. The Golfclub Drachenwand Golf Course is 6. 3 km away.
