Description

Enjoying a quiet location, 2 km away from the town centre of Mondsee, this guest house offers stunning views of Lake Mondsee from the terrace and features free WiFi.

A spa area with free use of different saunas, an infrared cabin and a steam bath is provided.

A hot tub and massages are offered for a surcharge. All beautifully decorated rooms at the Panorama-Gasthof, feature traditional furniture and modern bathrooms.

Satellite TV, a work desk and a seating area are also available. Panorama-Gasthof-Pension Leidingerhof offers traditional Austrian cuisine in their restaurant and on the sunny terrace.

All dishes are prepared with products from the guest house’s own farm. Guests can relax in the Leidingerhof’s garden where a children’s playground can also be found.

A ping-pong table and free bicycles are available on site. From April until October guests can enjoy a heated outdoor pool.
