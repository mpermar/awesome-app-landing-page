Description

Pension Irlingerhof offers rooms with a balcony, free Wi-Fi access, and free parking in a quiet location on a hill between the Mondsee and Irrsee Lakes in the Salzkammergut region.

The Irlingerhof’s spa area features an infrared cabin and a steam bath. The new fitness centre offers panoramic views and can be used free of charge.

In summer, a covered outdoor pool with a sunbathing lawn is available. The Mondsee is 4 km away from Pension Irlingerhof, and the Irrsee is 2 km away.
