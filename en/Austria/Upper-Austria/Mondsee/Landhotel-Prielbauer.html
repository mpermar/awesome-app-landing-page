Description

Set in a scenic landscape at the foot of the Kolomannsberg Mountain, Landgasthof Prielbauer offers a sauna, 2 sun terraces and a large garden.

It is 3 km from Lake Mondsee and 1. 5 km from Lake Irrsee. Rooms at Landgasthof Prielbauer include cable TV and a tiled bathroom with hairdryer and toiletries.

Most rooms feature a balcony. Open from May to September, the restaurant serves traditional Austrian cuisine and daily buffet breakfast.

On request, special dietary needs can be accommodated. The garden includes a large chess board and a ping pong table for free use.

The Mondsee and Drachenwand Golf Courses are 3 km away. The A1 motorway is a 5-minute drive from Landgasthof Prielbauer.
