Description

Situated just 2 km from Lake Hallstatt, Ferienhof Osl provides the unique opportunity to stay at an organic farm.

It features self-catered apartments and a petting zoo for children. Free private parking is also available at the house.

All apartments at Ferienhof Osl Guest House include a fully equipped kitchen, satellite TV and bathroom with shower.

The spa area includes a sauna and a small relaxation area and is subject to a fee.

Situated in a peaceful spot in Obertraun, the farmhouse is surrounded by beautiful meadows.

There are a number of farm animals such as horses, rabbits, hens, cats and guinea pigs.

The Dachstein-West ski area is about 20 kilometres from the farm house and Planai is 85 km away.

There is a ski storage room with drying facilities. The next restaurant is only a few steps away, the centre of Obertraun as well as supermarkets are 600 metres away.
