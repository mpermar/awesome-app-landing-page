Description

In a scenic location in the Gosau Valley, at the foot of the Dachstein Mountain and 5 km from Lake Gosau, Hotel Sommerhof is a family-run 4-star hotel with a restaurant, spa area, and free WiFi access.

The 1,000 m² garden features an outdoor pool heated throughout the year, a children’s playground with a trampoline, a bouncing castle, and a sunbathing lawn with sun loungers.

For children aged 3 and older, Sommerhof offers free child care and a children’s club.

Babysitting is available at a surcharge. Spa facilities include 2 saunas for adults, a children’s sauna, and a hot tub.

A sun bed and a selection of massages are available at an additional cost. The spacious rooms all offer mountain views, a seating area, a flat-screen cable TV, and a bathroom.

Bathrobes for adults are provided free of charge. In the restaurant, guests can enjoy offers regional and international dishes.

Gluten-free and lactose-free meals are also available. Guided hiking tours are offered during the summer season.

Hiking and mountain bike trails, as well as cross-country ski runs, start at the doorstep.

A free ski bus stops right outside. The Panorama Jet Cable Car is 3 km away, and Lake Hallstatt is 15 km away.
