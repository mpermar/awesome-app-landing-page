Description

Set on a hill above the scenic village of Vorderstoder, the Landhotel Stockerwirt enjoys a quiet location amid the Kalkalpen National Park.

It offers a spa and a breakfast buffet. All rooms provide a private bathroom, satellite TV and a seating area.

Most rooms also have a balcony. Guests can relax in the Finnish Sauna, the herbal steam bath or the heated indoor pool.

Table tennis equipment can be rented at the Landhotel. The Hinterstoder and Wurzeralm ski areas are in the vicinity and can be easily reached with the free ski bus shuttle service, which stops in front of the Stockerwirt.

Rafting, paragliding as well as trips to the high ropes course in the surrounding area can be arranged by the Landhotel Stockerwirt on request.

Many hiking tracks are also nearby. Parking is free of charge. From May until mid October the Pyhrn-Priel Aktiv Card is included in the price.
