Description

Located on the shore of Lake Traunsee, with direct lake access, Seehotel Das Traunsee is situated in the centre of Traunkirchen in the Salzkammergut, 10 km from Gmunden.

It offers an award-winning gourmet restaurant and a spa area on the lake. All rooms all have a balcony with lake view, cable TV, a minibar, a tablet and a bathroom.

The Bootshaus restaurant offers fine cuisine. In summer, the SeeSide restaurant is open during the day.

Each restaurant has a terrace with unique views of Lake Traun and the surrounding mountains.

The See-Spa wellness area features several saunas including one with a panoramic lake view, a steam bath, spa showers and a relaxation area.

Guests have exclusive access to a bathing jetty. Beauty treatments and massages are available.

In winter, the Feuerkogel Family Ski Area is 7 km away, and there are many Christmas markets in the surrounding towns and villages.
