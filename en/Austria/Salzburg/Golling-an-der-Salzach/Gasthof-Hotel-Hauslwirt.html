Description

Gasthof Hotel Hauslwirt is located in the centre of Golling on the Salzach River, 25 km south of Salzburg.

This traditional, family-run hotel offers free parking and free Wi-Fi access. The rooms offer a flat-screen TV with over 100 cable channels.

Some have a balcony. A rich breakfast buffet is available in the morning. The restaurant serves traditional Austrian cuisine.

The Aqua Salza spa centre and the A10 motorway are very close to Gasthof Hotel Hauslwirt.

The surrounding area offers a wide range of sports and leisure activities in every season.
