Description

Located on a south-facing slope above St. Veit, the Hotel Empl provides panoramic views over the Salzach Valley.

It features a heated outdoor pool and a spa area. Surrounded by a charming garden with loungers and a playground, the guest rooms at Landhaus Empl all have a balcony and come equipped with a flat-screen cable TV.

Free Wi-Fi is available in all rooms. Fine Salzburg specialities and international cuisine are served in the Landhaus Empl's restaurant.

Special themed dinner with barbecued food, fondue and other treats are regularly organised.

A tobogganing track can be found right opposite the house and a children's ski lift is 50 metres away.

Within a 2-minute walk you can reach the stop of the free ski shuttle bus to Alpendorf and the lifts of the huge Ski Amadé winter sport region.

A rich breakfast including locally produced butter, cheese and wholemeal bread can be enjoyed every morning.

Spa facilities include a Finnish sauna, a steam bath, an infra-red cabin, heated benches and a spacious relaxation area with a juice bar.

Guided mountain hiking tours are offered twice a week and in winter you can enjoy romantic sleigh rides.

The Goldegg Golf Club is 6 km away, where you will get a 20% discount as a guest of the Landhaus Empl.
