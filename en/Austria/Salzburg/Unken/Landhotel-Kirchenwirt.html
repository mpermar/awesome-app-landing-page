Description

Landhotel Kirchenwirt is located near the Chiemgau Alps, 5 km from the German border, in the town centre of Unken.

It offers free internet access and a breakfast buffet. All rooms of the Landhotel Kirchenwirt provide satellite TV, a bathroom with shower and toilet and most rooms feature a balcony.

The restaurant of the Landhotel serves Austrian and international specialities also on the on side terrace.

Parking is free of charge in the Landhotel Kirchenwirt. In winter, the surrounding area is ideal for skiing, cross-country skiing, ice climbing, and much more.

Several mountain bike trails go past the Landhotel Kirchenwirt. The Berchtesgaden National Park, the Königssee Lake and the Weißbach Nature Resort can be reached within a distance of 10 km.

The spa town of Bad Reichenhall is 13 km away; the city of Salzburg is 40 km from Landhotel Kirchenwirt.
