Description

Surrounded by a majestic mountain landscape, this family-run 4-star hotel is located next to the Asitz Cable Car and the mountain bike park, in the Leogang-Saalbach-Hinterglemm Ski Area and 130 steps from the Saalbach-Hinterglemm ski circus.

It offers panoramic views of the surrounding mountains. The Salzburger Hof's unique spa area on the glass-panelled fifth floor offers panoramic mountain views and features a sauna, a steam bath, a hot tub, and a relaxation room.

There is also a heated outdoor pool with mountain views. In the morning, you can enjoy a rich breakfast buffet with many organic products from surrounding farms.

The restaurant serves international cuisine and regional specialities, as well as fine Austrian and international wines.

Afternoon snacks are also served. The cross-country ski run is only 100 metres away from the Salzburger Hof, and in summer, mountain bikers enjoy the bike park next door.

The nearby golf course offers reduced green fees to hotel guests. Salzburg, Berchtesgaden, Kitzbühel, Kaprun and Zell am See can be reached quickly and easily.

A private parking garage is available at an additional cost. A transfer from the train stations in Leogang, Saalfelden and Hochfilzen is offered free of charge.

Guests receive the Saalfelden Leogang Card all year around, which allows free admission and discounts at several local attractions, as well as for cable cars and public transport.
