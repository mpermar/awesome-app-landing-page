Description

Located right next to the valley station of the Leogang Cable Car, the Hotel Bacher Asitzstubn offers you a spa area, a roof terrace and fine Austrian cuisine.

When weather is nice you can also spend relaxing hours on the rooftop terrace, featuring a heated infinity pool with panoramic views.

All rooms at the Bacher hotel have a balcony, cable TV and a private bathroom. In winter you can ski right down to the front door of the Hotel Bacher from the large Leogang-Saalbach-Hinterglemm ski area.

The kid's ski area can be found right behind the hotel and skiing lessons can be booked on site.

Numerous hiking and mountain biking trails, bathing lakes and public pools in the surroundings offer lots of possibilities for active leisure during summer.

Traditional Austrian cuisine including Salzburg specialities are served in the restaurant, which is furnished in typical Alpine style.
