Description

Ferienhotel Linderhof offers a quiet location close to the centre of Leogang, only a few minutes‘ walk away from Asitzbahn Cable Car, a ski rental, and a ski school.

The big Saalbach-Hinterglemm-Leogang ski area can be reached with a free ski bus directly from the Linderhof.

In summer a 1,500 m² garden with a sunbathing lawn is available. Ferienhotel Linderhof features a restaurant, a café, 3 terraces, a sauna, and a free internet terminal.

Parking is available free of charge. From May until October, half-board includes a cake buffet in the afternoon and juices for children throughout the day.

The infrared cabin can be used free of charge every day, and the use of the sauna is included once a week.

The Löwencard is also included in summer, comprising many free benefits and discounts such as free use of the cable car.
