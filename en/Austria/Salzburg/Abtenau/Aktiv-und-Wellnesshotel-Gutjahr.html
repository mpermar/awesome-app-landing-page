Description

In a quiet location in the centre of the picturesque town of Abtenau, this family-run 4-star superior hotel offers panoramic views of the surrounding mountains, an extensive leisure and fitness area, a indoor pool, and spa facilities such as saunas, a steam bath, and a sun bed.

Massages and beauty treatments are also available. The restaurant at Aktiv- und Wellnesshotel Gutjahr features a terrace and serves Austrian and international cuisine, as well as regional specialities, homemade pastries, and a selection of fine wines.

Half-board is available at an additional cost. The Gutjahr-Alm is the hotel’s own mountain hut, where traditional evenings and dance nights with live music take place regularly.

Many sports facilities can be found in the immediate vicinity of the Gutjahr Hotel.

Guided hikes and individual excursions are available. Free guided cycling tours with the property's owner can be requested and guests can rent different kinds of bicycles on site.

Sights such as Salzburg, the Salzkammergut with its lakes, and the Dachstein region can be reached quickly.
