Description

At the hotel, rooms include a closet and a flat-screen TV. Rooms contain a private bathroom and a shower, and certain units at Enjoy the alps have a balcony.

A continental or buffet breakfast is available every morning at the property.

The property has a sauna. Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Flachau, like skiing, cycling and hiking.

Achter Jet is a 7-minute walk from Enjoy the alps, while Space Jet 2 is 0.9 miles from the property. The nearest airport is W. A. Mozart Airport, 35 miles from the property.
