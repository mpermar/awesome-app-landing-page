Description

Situated 100 metres from Highliner I and providing free WiFi, OFENTÜRL alpine living offers accommodation in Flachau.

Some units also have a kitchen equipped with a dishwasher. Every morning a buffet breakfast is available at the aparthotel.

OFENTÜRL alpine living offers a sun terrace and during winter guests enjoy the ski-to-door access to the property.

Sunshine Shuttle is an 8-minute walk from the accommodation, while Highliner II, Rosskopf is a 19-minute walk from the property.

The nearest airport is Salzburg W. A. Mozart Airport, 63 km from OFENTÜRL alpine living.
