Description

Located in the centre of Russbach, Hotel Kerschbaumer is just 400 metres from the cable car into the Dachstein-West Ski Area.

It features a sauna and a fossil exhibition. Guests can play billiards, and darts.

For children, an indoor play area and a PlayStation are provided. There is also a lounge with books and board games and a ski storage room with a ski boot dryer.

Free WiFi is available in public areas. The Kerschbaumer’s spacious, rustically furnished rooms feature cable TV and a bathroom.

The Weinstube restaurant is 150 metres away. It serves traditional Austrian and international cuisine and features a sauna.

A café with a sun terrace is located in the Kerschbaumer Hotel. Free private parking is available at Hotel Kerschbaumer.

In winter, the free ski bus stops right outside, and guests can use a free ski depot at the cable car station.

In summer, guests can use the cable car and the local swimming lake (only 100 metres away) free of charge with a minimum stay of 2 nights.
