Description

At the hotel, all rooms have a balcony. Each room includes a private bathroom with free toiletries. All rooms have a desk.

A continental breakfast is served daily at the property.

Guests at Hotel Kronenhirsch will be able to enjoy activities in and around Russbach am Pass Gschütt, like skiing and cycling.

The nearest airport is W. A. Mozart Airport, 26 miles from the accommodation.
