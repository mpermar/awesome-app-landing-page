Description

Located in Piesendorf, 8 km from Lake Zell and within 5 km from the Kaprun - Maiskogel Ski Area, Apartments Chalet Sonnentanz offers accommodation units with a balcony and mountain views.

Free WiFi is available in all areas of the property. The apartments also provide a flat-screen cable TV, a DVD and Blu-ray player, a seating area, a fully equipped kitchen with dishwasher, and a bathroom with shower or bath tub.

Apartments Chalet Sonnentanz features a bar. A supermarket and various restaurants are within 250 metres.

Facilities offered on site include a garden, a shared lounge, a games room, a children's playground and a ski storage room.
