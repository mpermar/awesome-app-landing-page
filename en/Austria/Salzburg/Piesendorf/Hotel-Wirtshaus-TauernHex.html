Description

Hotel. Wirtshaus TauernHex is set in Piesendorf, 4 km from Kaprun and 8 km from Zell am See.

This 3-star hotel offers free shuttle service. The hotel features a sauna and luggage storage space.

Free WiFi is accessible to all guests, while selected rooms here will provide you with a balcony.

Hotel. Wirtshaus TauernHex offers a children's playground. During summer guests can rent e-bikes and bikes at the hotel.

Skiing is also among the activities that guests can enjoy near the accommodation.

Guests can purchase their ski ticket for the Zell am See and Kaprun ski area directly at the property.

Saalbach Hinterglemm is 12 km from Hotel. Wirtshaus TauernHex. The nearest airport is Salzburg W.
