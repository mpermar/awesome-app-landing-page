Description

Located in Fusch on the Großglockner High Alpine Road, this 3-star hotel is 13 km from Zell am See.

It offers free Wi-Fi and a balcony in each room. Originally built as a tavern in the 16th century, Hotel Post Fusch offers rooms furnished in a traditional Alpine style.

They feature wooden floors, satellite TV, and a bathroom. The restaurant at Hotel Post serves traditional Austrian cuisine.

Buffet breakfast and half board are available. Guests can relax in the sauna and play billiard and table tennis.

An internet terminal and a ski storage room are available. A playground and an indoor playroom are provided for children.

Hiking and cycling trails into the Hohe Tauern National Park start right outside.

Free private parking is available on site. Guests of Hotel Post receive discounts on admission to the Tauern Spa in Kaprun.

The Hotel’s Guest Card is included in the room rate and offers many benefits and discounts for the attractions in the region.
