Description

The family-run 4-star Hotel Römerhof enjoys a gorgeous location at the foot of the Grossglockner, Austria’s highest mountain, only 10 km away from the Europa Sports Region Zell am See/Kaprun.

A free ski bus is available. Surrounded by the Hohe Tauern National Park, you can relax in a beautiful natural landscape.

In addition to a modern spa centre, the Römerhof has an excellent restaurant serving a rich breakfast buffet and traditional dishes, including many local specialities from the Pinzgau region.
