Description

This family-run 4-star hotel enjoys a scenic, quiet location just a 5-minute walk from the village centre of Kaprun and from the picturesque Kaprun Castle.

In winter, the ski bus stops right at the doorstep. Spa facilities at Verwöhnhotel Vötters Sportkristall include an indoor pool, a sauna, a steam bath, an infrared cabin and a unique outdoor hot tub.

All facílities can be used free of charge. A selection of beauty treatments and massages is available at a surcharge.

In winter, various snacks are offered in the afternoon. From mid-May to mid-October, the Zell am See-Kaprun Card is included in the rate.

This card offers many free benefits and discounts, including free use of local cable cars and buses.
