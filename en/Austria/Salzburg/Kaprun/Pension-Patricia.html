Description

Only 400 metres away from the centre of Kaprun, Pension Patricia was completely renovated in December 2016, offers free WiFi and free parking in a quiet location.

It is only a 10-minute bus ride to the Kitzsteinhorn Glacier and Zell am See ski areas.

The rich breakfast buffet includes homemade jams and bread. The owner of Pension Patricia also owns a taxi company offering tours and excursions in the region.

Guests receive a 10% discount on them. Guests of the property also receive a discount on the entrance to the Tauern Spa thermal bath.

From mid-May to mid-October, the Zell am See-Kaprun Card is included in the rate with a minimum stay of 2 nights.

This card offers many free benefits and discounts, including free use of local cable cars and buses.
