Description

The Dorfkrug Kaprun enjoys an idyllic location in the centre of Kaprun, only a few minutes away from the slopes.

Dorfkrug Kaprun offers free VIP access to the Tauern Spa, which is 2 km away. Guests receive a bag with bathrobes and towels.

The apartments and rooms are equipped with all modern amenities and have a balcony with panoramic views of the mountains.

A restaurant, a ski storage room in the basement with a heated boot rack, a coin-operated washing machine and dryer, and a children's playroom can be found in the same building.

Free professional child care is provided at the Tauern Spa. The half-board arrangement includes a 4-course dinner.

The free ski bus stops right in front of the Dorfkrug Kaprun and takes you to the valley station of the cable car to the Kitzsteinhorn, as well as to the Maiskogel and to Zell am See.
