Description

Just 100 metres from the ski lift in Filzmoos, Familienhotel Filzmooserhof features a heated outdoor pool and free use of its tennis court.

A wide range of activities for children is offered. Activities for children include barbecues, guided hikes, and climbing with mountain guides.

The large garden includes a children’s playground and ponies. The public swimming pool is right next door.

Spa facilities include a sauna, a steam bath, and a fitness room. Guests can play table tennis and tabletop football.

Activities such as rafting trips and mountain bike tours are organised regularly.

The Filzmooserhof’s spacious rooms include a flat-screen satellite TV, a refrigerator, a seating area, and a bathroom.

All rooms have a balcony overlooking the mountains. The restaurant serves Austrian and international cuisine and has a separate children’s table and menu.

For children up to 12 years, all rates comprise an all-inclusive meal plan. Free private parking is available on site.

The centre of Filzmoos is 500 metres away. In winter, a ski school is just 50 metres away.

A children's club and entertainment programme for children from 2. 5 years is arranged from Sunday to Friday, from 10:00 to 16:00 and 18:30 to 21:00.
