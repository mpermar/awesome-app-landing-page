Description

The traditional, family-run Hotel Dachstein is located directly in the centre of Filzmoos, very close to the ski lifts and cable cars of the Ski Amadé region.

Hotel Dachstein’s spa area can be used free of charge. It features a Finnish sauna, a herbal steam bath, a solarium, and much more.

Bathrobes and bathing towels are provided free of charge in the rooms. Half-board includes a varied breakfast buffet with home-made products and a 5-course dinner with a salad buffet.

Gala dinners take place regularly. Facilities also include a lobby with a reading corner, a small library, and a heated storage room for winter and summer sports equipment.

Hotel Dachstein regularly offers various activities such as guided hikes or skiing excursions.
