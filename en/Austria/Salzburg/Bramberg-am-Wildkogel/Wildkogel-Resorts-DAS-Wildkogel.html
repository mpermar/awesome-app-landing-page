Description

Set in a scenic Alpine landscape, just 20 metres from the ski slopes, the Wildkogel Resort in Bramberg features an outdoor pool and a spa area with an indoor pool and sauna.

It offers luxuriously decorated apartments with panoramic mountain views. Mixing contemporary furniture and traditional materials, all the Wildkogel’s apartments and penthouses feature a spacious lounge area with a flat-screen satellite TV, and most of them have a private balcony.

Guests can enjoy their meals in the privacy of their modern kitchenette and dining area.

Restaurants, supermarkets and grocery stores can be found a few minutes’ walk away.

A variety of relaxing massages are available at the spa, while ski lovers can also rent all the necessary equipment on site.

The stop of the ski bus linking to the Kitzbühel Alps Ski Area is just a 3-minute walk away.

Innsbruck Airport is 115 km away, and the property can arrange a shuttle service upon request.
