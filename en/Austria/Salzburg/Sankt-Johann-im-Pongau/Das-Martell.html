Description

Das Martell enjoys a very quiet location on the forest promenade of Sankt Johann im Pongau, only a 5-minute walk away from the centre.

Free WiFi is available throughout the building, and there is a big garden, a playground with a trampoline, and a sunbathing lawn with deck chairs and sunshades.

The quiet, non-smoking rooms feature a flat-screen TV and bright wooden furniture in a traditional style.

Most rooms have a balcony and views of the mountains or the garden. In the morning, Das Martell serves a rich breakfast buffet with homemade jams.

Half-board is available on request and comprises a 3-course dinner with a salad buffet.

The train station and the ski bus stop are only a 5- to 10-minute walk away. The Salzach River and the Tauern Bicycle Trail are right behind the property.
