Description

Verwöhnhotel Berghof is a 4-star-superior hotel right next to the cable car in Alpendorf in the Ski Amadé region, a 5-minute drive from Sankt Johann im Pongau.

It offers a luxurious spa area with various pools, saunas and a spa bath. The indoor pool features massage jets, a counter-current system and special showers.

The Berghof also offers in summer a heated outdoor pool and a children’s pool with a water slide.

Guests can enjoy massages and various beauty treatments. All spacious rooms and suites have free access to WiFi and feature a seating area and a balcony.

Bathrobes and slippers are also provided. The Berghof has a restaurant serving classic Austrian cuisine.

All meals can be enjoyed on the spacious terrace which offers panoramic views. Verwöhnhotel Berghof features a daily children’s entertainment programme throughout summer and from Sunday to Friday during winter.

A children’s playground and a garden with a ping-pong table and swings are provided.

An adventure playground is located opposite the hotel. Several hiking and cycling trails start at the doorstep.
