Description

Offering panoramic views over the Salzach Valley, this elegant, Alpine-style 4-star superior hotel is right next to the ski slopes of Alpendorf.

It has an indoor pool and offers free WiFi and free underground parking. Each room at the Tannenhof has a flat-screen cable TV, and a minibar.

The bathrooms feature bathrobes, slippers, and toiletries. Some also have a balcony.

The spa area at Alpines Lifestyle Hotel Tannenhof includes various saunas and steam baths, a hot tub, and a juice bar.

Guests can choose from a wide range of massages and beauty treatments using products by Alpienne and Thalgo.

The spa and the outdoor terrace offer panoramic mountain views. In the traditionally furnished restaurant, guests can enjoy Austrian cuisine with many regional products and herbs from the Tannenhof’s own garden.

A wide range of fine wines is also available. For children, a playroom, professional child care and many activities are offered.

In summer, the Tannenhof organises guided hikes through the surrounding mountains.

In winter, ski rental and service are available on site. Free transfers from St. Johann Train Station are provided, and transfers from Salzburg Airport are available at an additional cost.
