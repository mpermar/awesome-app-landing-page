Description

Located in the centre of Sankt Johann im Pongau, the Alpenland Sporthotel offers year-round recreational facilities in Alpine surroundings.

The spa area includes a hot tub, a sauna, a steam bath and a sun bed, and there is also a modern fitness area.

The traditionally furnished rooms have a minibar, a flat-screen cable TV, and a bathroom with bathrobes and hairdryer.

Most have a balcony. Free WiFi is available in the entire building. The Alpenland Sporthotel's restaurants serve fine Austrian specialities and international cuisine in an Alpine-style setting.

The tennis court can be used at an additional fee. A shuttle service provides access to the St.

Johann-Alpendorf Ski Area, which is 3 km away and a part of the huge Ski Amadé winter sports region.
