Description

Only 50 metres from the Mühlwinkel Ski Lift, Landhotel Untermüllnergut in Dorfgastein offers a restaurant, free WiFi, and free use of its spa area.

Guided hikes are offered in summer. The spacious, Alpine-style rooms and apartments feature a balcony or terrace with mountain views, cable TV, a minibar, and a bathroom.

In the restaurant, guests of the Untermüllnergut can enjoy traditional Austrian cuisine, as well as homemade products and spirits.

Free private parking is available on site. Horse-riding is available 200 metres away at an additional cost.

The Gastein Card is included in the rate. This card offers many free benefits and discounts in both summer and winter.

Between May and September guests also enjoy free admission to the Solarbad Dorfgastein (an outdoor pool and sauna area).
