Description

The 4-star Hotel Gerl is located in Wals, a 2-minute drive from the A1 motorway and a 15-minute drive from the centre of Salzburg.

It offers free WiFi and free public parking. All rooms at the Hotel Gerl come with cable TV and a bathroom and most units feature a balcony, as well.
