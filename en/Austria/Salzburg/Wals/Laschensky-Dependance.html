Description

Offering a seasonal outdoor pool and sauna, Laschensky Dependance is located in Viehhausen in the Salzburg Region.

The hotel has a children's playground and water sports facilities, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Every room is equipped with a flat-screen TV. Each room is fitted with a private bathroom.

For your comfort, you will find slippers and free toiletries. Breakfast is available every morning at the Laschenskyhof (main building, which is located next door.

Free use of bicycles is available at this hotel and the area is popular for cycling.

Salzburg is 5 km from Laschensky Dependance, while Berchtesgaden is 16 km away. The nearest airport is Salzburg W.
