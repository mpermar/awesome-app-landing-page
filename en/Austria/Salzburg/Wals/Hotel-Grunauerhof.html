Description

Family-run for over 100 years, the hotel features a traditional restaurant, a playground and play room, free underground parking and plenty of covered parking spaces.

Guests of Hotel Grünauerhof can also make use of a sauna, a steam bath, a gym and a solarium.

Its proximity to Salzburg makes the hotel an excellent starting point for visits to the city, but also for excursions to the Salzkammergut, Berchtesgaden and many other attractions. For these tours, bicycles are available for rental at the hotel. The bike path along the Saalach River into Salzburg is in the immediate vicinity of the hotel.
