Description

Just a 10-minute drive from Salzburg Airport, Hotel Königgut enjoys a quiet location on the outskirts of Salzburg.

It offers a large spa area with a sauna and a steam bath, which is available in the evenings, and a fitness room.

The Königgut Hotel’s spacious rooms have a balcony, flat-screen cable TV, seating area, and bathroom.

The restaurant serves Austrian and international cuisine and a selection of Austrian wines.
