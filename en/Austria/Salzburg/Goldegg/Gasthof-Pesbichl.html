Description

In a quiet location 6 km from Goldegg, Gasthof Pesbichl offers panoramic views of the surrounding Alpine landscape.

It features a restaurant serving traditional Salzburg cuisine, a sauna, and free WiFi.

Each of the non-smoking rooms and apartments is decorated with wooden furnishings and equipped with a seating area, satellite TV, a private bathroom and balcony.

Each room provides an electric kettle and different homemade teas. The daily buffet breakfast at the Pesbichl Gasthof includes a wide range of homemade products.

Half-board with a 4-course dinner is available at the restaurant and can be booked on site.

You can watch farm animals like cows, pigs, goats and chickens at the property. Products like jam, cheese, bread and bacon from the on-site farm are offered.

In summer, guests can use the natural swimming lake and the children’s playground.

The garden features deck chairs and umbrellas. The Salzburger Almenweg hiking trail passes directly at the property, and the Böndlsee swimming lake is nearby.

The Ski Amadé Ski Region is a 20-minute drive away, and cross-country ski runs are within easywalking distance.
