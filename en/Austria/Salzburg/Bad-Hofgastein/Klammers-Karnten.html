Description

Klammers Kärnten is an innovative 4-star hotel in Bad Hofgastein offering an ideal combination of health, wellness and sports amidst a breathtaking mountain landscape.

Free WiFi is available. The health pavilion features a beauty department, massages, Fango and mud treatments, and a daily kinetics programme.

Holistic health applications such as acupressure, Shiatsu and Feldenkrais are combined with thermal water.

There are also special spa treatments from Bali, China and Brittany. You will also find a sauna with panoramic views, a steam bath, a hot tub and indoor and outdoor pools with thermal water.

In addition to the healthy cuisine, culinary pleasures include wine tastings and barbecue parties on the terrace in summer.

Cakes are served in the fireplace lounge every afternoon. Breakfast is served until 10:30.

Half-board includes a breakfast buffet, a cake buffet in the afternoon (winter only), an afternoon snack and a 4-course dinner.

The hotel provides free bicycles. Discounts on green fees are offered for hotel guests at the Gastein Golf Club.
