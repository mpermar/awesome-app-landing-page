Description

A wellness area consisting of various saunas, a solarium, an indoor swimming pool and a hot tub is provided in Hotel Zum Stern.

The 4-star superior hotel offers country-style rooms with flat-screen satellite TV.

The Angertal Ski and Hiking Area is an 8-minute drive away, accessible by the free ski bus outside.

All rooms feature a balcony or a terrace with mountain views, and a bathroom with a shower or a bathtub.

Some rooms come with a tile stove. Free Wi-Fi is available in all rooms and at the bar.

A rich breakfast buffet is served at the hotel daily. Hotel Zum Stern comes with 2 restaurants and a cosy lobby with a bar and an open fireplace.

You can sunbathe in the garden or on the large terrace and request beauty- and physiotherapy treatments as well as massages.

A gym, ideal for the daily work out, is provided on site. Bicycles can be borrowed free of charge and hiking tours are organised on a regular basis.

You can buy ski passes on site and make use of the hotel‚Äôs games room. Shuttle services are available on request and free private parking is possible in front of the building.

Zum Stern hotel comes with storage room for your ski, hiking and golf equipment, which is heated during winter.

The 18-hole golf course Gastein is just a 10-minute drive away and offers you greenfee discounts.

The centre of Bad Hofgastein can be reached within a 3-minute drive and Gastein is 7 km away.
