Description

Just a 3-minute walk from Bad Hofgastein’s centre, this 4-star superior hotel offers a large wellness area and rooms with free Wi-Fi.

The award-winning restaurant serves Austrian specialties and international dishes.

Within a 5-minute drive, you can reach the Amadé Ski Area. The wellness area is 1,500 m² in size and includes 4 thermal pools (2 indoor and 2 outdoor), a hot tub, 3 saunas, Kneipp facilities, and much more.

A wide range of massages is available, and you can work out in the on-site gym. The Alpine-style rooms feature a seating area, satellite TV, and a bathroom with a shower.

A freshly prepared breakfast, lunch snacks like soup or salad as well as cake in the afternoon are offered free of charge each day.

Hotel Bismarck features a cosy lounge with a bar and a fireplace. During summer, you can relax and enjoy coffee and cakes on the large terrace.

The hotel provides 2 playrooms for the children’s entertainment. A large park-like garden is surrounding the building.

Cross-country ski runs start right outside, and the free ski bus stops in front of the ski storage room.

Free parking is available on site. A golf course, offering discounts to guests of Hotel Bismarck, can be reached within a 5-minute drive.
