Description

The wellness hotel Das Alpenhaus Gasteinertal is located in the center of Bad Hofgastein, close to the Kurpark, pedestrian area and ski lifts.

Free WiFi is available. Guests are accommodated in alpine style furnished, non-smoking rooms featuring a balcony with mountain views, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a minibar, and a bathroom with bathrobes and a hairdryer.

The Alpenhaus restaurant offers a hotel bar, a sun terrace and serves international, vegetarian and vegan cuisine.

The large spa area features an indoor pool with panoramic views, a whirlpool, and a sauna area.

Massage and wellness treatments are available at a surcharge. A fitness room with high tech equipment and guided fitness and relaxation programs are offered to guests.

During summer the Gastein Card offers many free benefits and discounts throughout the region.

The Schlossalm ski lift is only a 7 minute walk from the Alpenhaus Gasteinertal and the Alpentherme Gastein can be reached within 250 meters.
