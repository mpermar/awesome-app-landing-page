Description

Surrounded by a 7,500 m¬≤ garden in the centre of Bad Hofgastein, this 4-star hotel features a large spa area with spacious thermal indoor and outdoor pools available throughout the year.

Free WiFi and free garage parking are provided. The 2,500 m¬≤ spa area was enlarged in 2016 and includes various saunas and steam baths, including a family sauna and a log cabin sauna in the garden.

A media lounge for relaxation is also featured, and a wide range of health, beauty and massage treatments is available at Thermenhotel Sendlhof.

All rooms at the Sendlhof are elegantly furnished and feature a balcony, satellite TV, and a bathroom with bathrobes and hairdryer.

The restaurant offers panoramic views of the surrounding mountains. Half-board includes a rich breakfast buffet and a 5-course dinner.

After dinner, guests can relax at the bar. The nearest ski lift is 1 km away. The free ski bus stops right in front of Thermenhotel Sendlhof.

Bad Hofgastein Train Station is 3 km away. Guests receive a 25% discount at the Gastein Golf Course.
