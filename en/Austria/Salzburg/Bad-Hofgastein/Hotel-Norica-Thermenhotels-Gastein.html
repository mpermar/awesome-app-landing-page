Description

Located in the pedestrian zone in the centre of Bad Hofgastein, the 4-star superior Hotel Norica is directly connected to the Alpentherme Thermal Spa and offers free and unlimited access to it.

Free WiFi is available. All rooms at the Norica Hotel feature an HD flat-screen cable TV, a seating area, a safe, and a bathroom with a hairdryer, make-up mirror, and free toiletries.

Bathrobes and bathing towels are provided free of charge for use in the spa. Typical Salzburg dishes and international cuisine are served in Hotel Norica’s restaurant.

Live music is played once a week, and the Blue Salon offers homemade Austrian pastries and coffee specialities.

Beverages are available throughout the day at the bar or in the lounge that boasts a fireplace.

Guests can attend a wine tasting in the wine cellar once a week. The in-house 1,500 m² Celtic Spa comprises an indoor pool with massage jets, different saunas, an infrared cabin, a sole steam cabin, showers with ice crushers, radon bath tubs, and relaxation areas.

Special Celtic treatments are also offered. For the length of the stay, guests are also provided free and unlimited entrance to the Alpentherme Thermal Spa.

An air-conditioned congress room with technical equipment can be used free of charge.

Guests can use a ski storage with ski boot dryer. A free ski bus stops right next to the hotel.
