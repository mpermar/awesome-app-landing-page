Description

Hotel Rauscher und Paracelsus is located in Bad Hofgastein, only 1 km from the nearest ski lift.

It features an outdoor pool and a spa area. Free private parking is available. The rooms of the Rauscher und Paracelsus have a classic interior design, with bright colours and wooden furniture.

Each has hardwood floors, cable TV, a safe, and a bathroom. Free Wi-Fi is available.

The spa area features 2 saunas, steam bath, infrared cabin, and a hot tub. There is an additional Finnish sauna in the garden.

The hotel’s restaurant serves Mediterranean and regional dishes. In the morning there is a rich buffet breakfast and in the evening a menu including vegetarian dishes and a salad buffet is served.

There is also a bar. The centre of Bad Hofgastein is within 150 metres. The Alpenkurpark is also 150 metres away from Hotel Rauscher, and the free city bus stops 50 metres away.
