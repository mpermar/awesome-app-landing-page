Description

Situated in Sankt Michael im Lungau, Haus Nohra features barbecue facilities and a garden.

This bed and breakfast offers a terrace, a ski storage space and the free ski bus stops right outside.

All units in the bed and breakfast are equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Rooms include a coffee machine and a private bathroom, while certain rooms include a balcony and some have mountain views.

All rooms include a wardrobe. A continental breakfast is served each morning at the property.

Haus Nohra offers a children's playground. An array of activities are offered in the area, such as skiing and hiking.

The Lungau Card is provided and includes discounts on many activities in the area.

Speiereckbahn is a 15-minute walk from the accommodation. Klagenfurt Airport is 72 km from the property.
