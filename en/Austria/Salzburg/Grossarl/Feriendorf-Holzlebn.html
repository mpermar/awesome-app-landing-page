Description

Situated just 300 metres from the Grossarl cable cars, Feriendorf Holzlebn offers fully furnished chalets with private saunas, spacious living rooms with fireplace and well-equipped kitchens, as well as apartments with kitchen, living room and cable TV.

Some of them have a balcony. All chalets at Feriendorf Holzlebn are traditionally decorated with wooden furniture.

They feature fully equipped kitchens with a microwave, refrigerator and dishwasher.

Guests can relax in their private sauna and steam bath, and the main building at the Feriendorf Holzlebn complex also offers a spa with a hot tub, massage room and sun bed.

Manicure and pedicure treatments can be requested. Other facilities in the complex include table tennis, a playground for children and an animal farm.

The property also organises a number of outdoor activities, such as horse riding and go-karts.
