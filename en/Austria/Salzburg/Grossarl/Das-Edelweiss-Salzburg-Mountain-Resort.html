Description

Awarded with the Austria Quality Seal for excellent quality of service in the hospitality industry, this family-run wellness hotel ensures you and your family a memorable stay.

Friendly staff and a pleasant ambience, combined with unique leisure facilities, await you at Hotel Edelweiss.

Located in a sunny valley near the ski resort Großarl, the hotel features one of the biggest indoor pools in the region.

Relax in the extensive wellness area or treat yourself to breakfast in the room. Children can have fun on the playground or play a round of table tennis while parents enjoy afternoon tea on the terrace.

Treat yourself, after an exciting day exploring the area by bike, to a professional massage or a drink from the bar.
