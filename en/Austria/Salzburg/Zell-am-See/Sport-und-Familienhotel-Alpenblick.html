Description

Located in Zell am See, just a 10-minute walk from the centre and the Lake Zell, the family-run Sport- und Familienhotel Alpenblick features a restaurant and an 1,100 m¬≤ wellness and spa area with a combined indoor and outdoor pool.

The Areitbahn Cable Car and the closest ski slope are a 2-minute drive away. The country-style rooms and apartments provide free WiFi.

A ski bus stops right in front of the hotel, and the Zell am See-Kaprun Golf Course is 3 km away.

From mid-May to mid-October, the Zell am See-Kaprun Card is included in the rate.

This card offers many free benefits and discounts, including free use of local cable cars and buses.
