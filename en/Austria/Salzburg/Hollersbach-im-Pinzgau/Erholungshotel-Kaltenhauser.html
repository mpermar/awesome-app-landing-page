Description

Run by the same family since 1723, Erholungshotel Kaltenhauser in Hollersbach is 1 km from the Panoramabahn Cable Car and the Kitzbühel Alps Ski Area.

It features a spa area with an indoor pool, a restaurant serving Austrian cuisine, and a garden with a pond.

The Kaltenhauser Hotel's rooms are furnished in traditional Alpine or modern style and come with a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom with a hairdryer, and a safe.

In the restaurant you can enjoy typical Austrian cuisine made from organic ingredients provided by farms in the Hohe Tauern National Park.

Spa facilities include a heated indoor pool during winter seasons, a sauna, a sanarium, a tepidarium, and a sun bed.

Massages are offered as well. The Tauernradweg cycle path passes just 100 metres away.

A swimming lake can be found 200 metres away, and the Mittersill-Stuhlfelden Golf Course can be reached by car in 10 minutes.

The city of Kitzbühel is 35 km from the property. From 01 May until 31 October the Nationalpark Sommercard Mobil is included in the rates.
