Description

The family-run Hotel Sonnhof is located on a small hill in the centre of Rauris, 500 metres from the Hochalmbahn Cable Car.

It offers an indoor pool with sauna and a squash court. Massages are also available.

Activities for children and baby equipment are available. Free WiFi is provided in public areas.

Guided ski and mountain tours take place regularly. There are many ski slopes and hiking trails nearby, and some lead into the Hohe Tauern National Park.
