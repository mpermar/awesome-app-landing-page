Description

Just a 30-minute drive south of Salzburg, Landgasthof Reitsamerhof is a 3-minute drive from Werfen, Hohenwerfen Castle and the Eisriesenwelt Ice Caves.

It offers free WiFi access and a restaurant serving traditional Austrian dishes. Rooms at the Reitsamerhof feature a flat-screen satellite TV and a bathroom.

Most also have their own private balcony with views of the surrounding mountains or the secluded garden.

Guests can enjoy their meals in the restaurant or on the garden terrace during the summer season.

There is also a playground in the garden. The Werfenweng Ski Area and the Sportsworld Amadé are a 10-minute drive away.

The public outdoor pools in Pfarrwerfen can be reached in a 5-minute drive, and a swimming lake and a beach volleyball court can be reached in 10 minutes by car.

The Werfen exit of the A10 motorway is just 200 metres away, and the Pfarrwerfen Train Station is a 2-minute walk away.
