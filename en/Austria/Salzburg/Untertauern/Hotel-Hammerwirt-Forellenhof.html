Description

The Hotel Hammerwirkt - Forellenhof is right next to the stop of the free ski bus to Obertauern and provides direct access to the Tauernloipe cross-country ski run.

Hotel Hammerwirkt - Forellenhof offers comfortable rooms with balcony, a children’s playroom, a sun terrace, a ski storage room, and a billiard table.

There is also a sauna, a steam bath, and a solarium. A rich breakfast buffet is offered in the morning and a 3-course dinner with a salad buffet are served in the new dining room in the evening.

The garden of Hotel Hammerwirkt - Forellenhof has a sunbathing lawn and an outdoor pool which is available in summer season.

There is also a children’s playground with a petting zoo with ponies and small animals, as well as an asphalt curling alley.

Hammerwirt - Forellenhof is 5 km away from Radstadt and 12 km from Obertauern. A golf course is 4 km away.
