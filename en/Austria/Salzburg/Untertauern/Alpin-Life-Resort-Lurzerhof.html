Description

Surrounded by the mountains of the Radstädter Tauern, the 4-star superior Alpin Life Resort Lürzerhof offers a restaurant serving Austrian and international cuisine made with local products and a large spa area with an all-year outdoor pool (heated in winter), 6 different saunas and steam baths, numerous relaxation rooms and a wellness garden.

The spacious rooms feature a balcony, a flat-screen TV and a minibar. You will be welcomed with a free drink on arrival and a rich buffet breakfast can be enjoyed every morning until 11:00.

Full board rates additionally include a show-cooking lunch from 12:30 to 13:30 or a late lunch from 13:30 to 15:30 including fresh salads and snacks, a hearty soup and cakes, a tea/coffee time from 15:30 to 17:00 including fine sweet pastry and cakes, free drinks all day until 17:00 plus a 6-course dinner with a choice of dishes and a salad buffet.

Vegetarian options are available every day and there also special offers for guests with food intolerance.
