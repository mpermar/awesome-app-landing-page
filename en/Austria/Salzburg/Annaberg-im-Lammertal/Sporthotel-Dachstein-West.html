Description

In the heart of the small village of Annaberg in the Lammer Valley, the Sporthotel offers 26 rooms for families and individuals.

Situated at the foot of the majestic Dachstein and Tennengebirge mountains and only 150 metres from the nearest ski lift, the hotel offers countless sports and leisure opportunities in summer as well as in winter.
