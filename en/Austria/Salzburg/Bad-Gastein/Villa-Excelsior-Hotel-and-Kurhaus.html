Description

Built in 1897 and once a residence of Sigmund Freud, the villa is today a meeting place of artists, actors, authors and guests from all over the world.

The Villa Excelsior enchants with original furnishings and the flair of a past era. Its beautiful location and wonderful view of Bad Gastein and the Hohe Tauern mountains carries you off to another world and another time.

The excellent food, friendly staff and romantic ambiance will make a stay at the Villa Excelsior Hotel & Kurhaus an unforgettable experience.
