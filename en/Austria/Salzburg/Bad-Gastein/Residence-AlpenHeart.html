Description

In a quiet location near the centre of Bad Gastein, Residence AlpenHeart is only 500 metres from the Stubnerkogel Cable Car and a 5-minute walk from the Felsentherme Thermal Spa.

It offers a sauna and fitness facilities. Free WiFi is available in all areas. The bright, Alpine-style rooms feature a flat-screen cable TV and a bathroom with a hairdryer.

Some have a balcony overlooking the mountains. The leisure room offers a billiards table, tabletop football and a TV.

A ski storage room and free private parking are available on site. Half-board is provided at Residence AlpenHeart.
