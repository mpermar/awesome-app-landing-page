Description

Located at 1,100 metres above sea level at the foot of the Graukogel Mountain, Hotel Helenenburg is right next to the ski slope in Bad Gastein.

In the 19th century, Austrian Empress Sisi stayed there 5 times. All rooms have a balcony.

The elegant rooms at the Kur&Ferien Hotel Helenenburg overlook the Gastein Valley and the mountains.

They feature satellite TV and a bathroom with hairdryer. A rich breakfast buffet is offered daily until 10:00.

The restaurant serves traditional Austrian cuisine and a wide range of fine wines and beers.

It offers buffet and à la carte dining. Kur&Ferien Hotel Helenenburg features a sauna and a fitness room.

Thermal water, massage treatments and physiotherapy are available. Over 1,000 books are available in the library.

The lobby provides magazines and newspapers and offers free Wi-Fi. You can borrow bicycles free of charge on site, and free private parking is available right in front of the hotel.

The centre of Bad Gastein with the casino and the Felsentherme Spa can be reached in a 10-minute walk.

The ski bus stops right in front and takes guests to the Stubnerkogel/Schlossalm Ski Area in 5 minutes.
