Description

Only steps away from the ski lifts, Bad Gastein’s thermal spa and the town centre, the Salzburger Hof offers panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.

It features 2 spa areas with an indoor pool, hot tub, saunas and 3 sun terraces. Massage treatments and aqua gymnastics are available.

The Salzburger Hof also has a playroom, bars and restaurants. Built between 1889 and 1907, Hotel Salzburger Hof provides elegant rooms with Wi-Fi, a flat-screen satellite TV, a DVD player, and a bathroom with bathrobes and hairdryer.

Guests can enjoy traditional Austrian cuisine in the Hofkeller restaurant, while a wide range of cocktails and other drinks is available at the Ritz bar.
