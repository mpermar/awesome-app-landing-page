Description

Located in the centre of Altenmarkt im Pongau, Landhaus Steiner has a spa area with an indoor pool.

The free ski bus to the Ski Amadé region stops right outside. All rooms at Landhaus Steiner have cable TV, a seating area, and a bathroom with bathrobes and a hairdryer.

Some rooms have a balcony. A rich breakfast buffet with organic products is served each morning.

In winter, coffee and cakes are served twice weekly by the open fireplace in the lounge.

Landhaus Steiner has a garden with a playground, a sunbathing lawn, and a sun terrace.

In winter, a heated ski storage room with a ski boot dryer is available. Toboggans are provided for guests’ use free of charge.

In summer, guests can use the storage room for bicycles and hiking gear. The hiking bus stops outside.

Free transfers from and to the train stations in Altenmarkt, Eben and Radstadt are available.

Landhaus Steiner offers free private parking and a lockable garage for motorcycles and bicycles.
