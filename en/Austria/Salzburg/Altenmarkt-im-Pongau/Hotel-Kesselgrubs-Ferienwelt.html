Description

Offering a spa area and a large garden area with a private swimming lake, Kesselgrubs Ferienwelt is a 15-minute walk from the center of Altenmarkt and 7.5 mi from the Zauchensee Ski Area, which is a part of Ski Amadé.

The spa area includes a Finnish sauna, a steam bath, a Kneipp pool, a fitness room, a hot tub, and an outdoor sauna. The hot tub can be used as a children's pool on request. Bathrobes are provided free of charge. Massages and beauty treatments are also available.

The Kesselgrub has 2 restaurants, one modern and one traditional, as well as a wine cellar. Guests can enjoy healthy products from the Kesselgrub’s own farm and from regional suppliers.

In summer, an activity and entertainment program for children is offered on 70 hours per week. The garden features a large play area with a trampoline, as well as a petting zoo and a horse-riding stable on site. Laundry and ironing services are offered for a charge.

Guests of Kesselgrubs Ferienwelt enjoy reductions at the Radstadt Golf Course, a 7-minute drive away. The ski bus stops right outside the premises and takes you to the surrounding slopes of the Ski Amadé region every 10 minutes. In summer, the hiking bus stops right in front.

A shuttle transport from and to Radstadt and Altenmarkt Train Stations is offered.
