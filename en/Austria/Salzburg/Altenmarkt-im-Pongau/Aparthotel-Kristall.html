Description

Surrounded by meadows, forests and mighty mountain scenery, the newly remodelled and generously expanded Aparthotel Kristall is the ideal base for relaxing holidays, only a 10-minute walk from the centre of Altenmarkt.

Kristall Aparthotel offers newly furnished, comfortable and friendly rooms and a rich breakfast buffet with an organic corner.

A sauna, a steam bath, a solarium and a gym offer relaxing moments. Kristall Hotel is a starting point for numerous leisure activities, from hiking and cycling to mountain biking and horseback riding.

In winter, unlimited skiing pleasure in Austria's largest skiing area awaits you.

The closest ski lift is only 900 metres away, with the ski bus stop and the access to the Tauern cross-country ski run right in front of the building.
