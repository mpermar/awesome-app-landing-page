Description

Aparthotel Garni Schranz is only 4 km away from the Krimml Falls in Hohe Tauern National Park.

All rooms have a balcony and a flat-screen cable TV, and there is a heated outdoor pool opened from May to October.

Free WiFi is available. Aparthotel Garni Schranz has a sauna and a sun bed. The garden features a sunbathing area with ping pong table and a children’s playground.

An indoor playroom is also available. The Sky Sports Bar at Aparthotel Garni Schranz serves snacks and drinks.

In winter, the stop of the free ski bus and a cross-country ski run are only 100 metres away from Familiengasthof Schranz.

Parking is available free of charge. From May to October, the Nationalpark Card is included in the rate for the double rooms.

This card offers free access to many different sights and attractions in the region.

The Grossglockner High Alpine Road can be used free of charge from May until October.
