Description

Hotel Almschlössl & Schrotteralm is right next to the slope in the Obertauern ski area.

The Kehrkopf cable car, practice lifts, a ski school, and a ski rental are next door.

Free Wi-Fi is provided throughout the property. The rooms and apartments are furnished in a traditional country-house style.

Schrotteralm & Hotel Almschlössl features a spacious, Mediterranean-style spa area, a fitness room, a ski storage room, and a children’s playroom.

A swimming pool with panoramic views and a separated children's area and a relaxation area is also on site.

Billiard, table tennis and tabletop football can be played. Austrian and international dishes are served in the traditionally furnished restaurant.

The lobby and the sun terrace offer panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.
