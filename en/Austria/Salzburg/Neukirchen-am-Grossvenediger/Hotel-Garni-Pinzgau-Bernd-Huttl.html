Description

Featuring a spa area which is accessible free of charge, Hotel Garni Pinzgau, Bernd Hüttl can be found in the centre of Neukirchen am Großvenediger, a 5-minute walk or a short ski bus ride from the Wildkogel Ski Area.

Free WiFi is available. All rooms have a private bathroom and satellite TV. They feature solid wooden furniture.

A separate ski storage room is available on site. There is a cosy, Alpine-style breakfast room with a tiled stove, a bar with a summer terrace and a conservatory.

Barbecue facilities are available as well at Hotel Garni Pinzgau. Spa facilities include a sauna, an infrared cabin, a hot tub, a sun bed and a steam bath.

There is also a relaxation room with a heated bench. During summer, children can have fun on the playground in the garden.

A 14-km long toboggan trail can be reached within a 5-minute walk. Tennis courts, volley ball and football courts can be reached on foot within 10 minutes.

A swimming lake and the Mittersill Golf Course are a 10- to 15-minute drive away.

From 01 May to 31 October, the Nationalpark Sommercard is included in the rates. This card offers many free benefits and discounts in the region, including free use of local cable cars and free admission to public pools and museums.
