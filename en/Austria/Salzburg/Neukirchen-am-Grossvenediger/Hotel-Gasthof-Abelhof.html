Description

Situated on a hillside with views of the surrounding Alpine panorama, Hotel Gasthof Abelhof is located in Neukirchen am Großvenediger and offers rustic accommodation units with a balcony and wooden furniture.

Guests can sample Austrian cuisine on spot and relax in the hotel’s sauna. The Wildkogelbahn Cable Car is within 2 km.

The rooms at Abelhof Gasthof all come with cable TV and a bathroom with a shower or a bathtub.

A ski storage room can be used by all guests and there’s also a garden with a sun terrace and a children's playground at guests’ disposal.

Active guests can have fun playing table tennis. Free private parking is possible in front of the property.

The public swimming pools Kristallbad Wald are within 5 km and guests can also reach supermarkets within 5 minutes by car.

Between May and October, the Nationalpark Sommercard is included in the price and offers various discounts in the National Park Hohe Tauern.
