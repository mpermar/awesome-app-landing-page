Description

Only 600 metres from Lake Zell and 2 km from Zell am See and the Schmittenhöhe Ski Area, the Hettlerhof features an organic farm, a sauna, and a steam bath.

Each room has a balcony or terrace with mountain or lake views. The Hettlerhof’s country-style rooms feature a flat-screen cable TV, a seating area, and a bathroom with a hairdryer.

The daily breakfast buffet includes organic products from the farm. Guests can rent bicycles, play table tennis, and benefit from free WiFi access in public areas.

A bicycle storage room and a ski storage room with a ski boot dryer are also available.

The garden includes a children’s playground, barbecue facilities, and deck chairs.

Free private parking is available on site. Hiking trails and the Tauernradweg cycling trail can be accessed from the doorstep.

A free ski bus stops 20 metres away. The closest restaurant is 300 metres away, and it is 2 km to the centre of Maishofen and 10 km to Saalbach.
