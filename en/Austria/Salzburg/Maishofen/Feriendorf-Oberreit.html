Description

Feriendorf Oberreit is located in Maishofen, directly at the town limits of Zell am See and offers self-catering wooden chalets featuring a covered terrace with mountain views and free WiFi.

Lake Zell is 1 km away, and the Schmittenhöhe Ski Area is 2 km away. The chalets all come with a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a well-equipped kitchen, a cosy living area, 2 bedrooms and a bathroom with toilet.

Fresh bread rolls are available on request every moroning, and a small grocery store can be found in the main building of Feriendorf Oberreit.

A ski and bicycle rental, as well as a heated and locked ski storage room with a ski boot dryer, are available.

In winter, the free ski bus stops outside and takes guests to the Saalbach-Hinterglemm-Leogang Ski Area.

A dog sitter can be booked on site, and a large, fenced free-range area for dogs is only a 4-minute walk away.

This area also features a private meadow with a playground, barbecue facilities and a football field.
