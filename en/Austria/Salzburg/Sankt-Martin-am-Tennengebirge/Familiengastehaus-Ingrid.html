Description

Familiengästehaus Ingrid is 500 metres from the centre and the ski lift of Sankt Martin am Tennengebirge.

It offers you fully equipped apartments and en-suite rooms, a big garden with pool, tree house, sunbathing lawn, petting zoo and a fish pond.

Spa and fitness facilities can be found on site as well. Within a 5-minute walk you can reach the centre with a supermarket, restaurants and the swimming lake.

Hiking and cross-country skiing trails pass the house. Hiking and cycling trails, as well as a cross-country ski run, start at the doorstep.

The Dachstein West Ski Area is 10 km away, and it is 12 km to the Flachau Ski Area.

Bischofshofen is 20 km away. Within a 30-minute drive you can reach the city of Salzburg, the Eisriesenwelt, the Krimmler waterfalls.
