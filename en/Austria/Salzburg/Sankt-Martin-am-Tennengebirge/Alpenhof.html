Description

In a quiet location at the edge of forest, a 5-minute walk from the centre of Sankt Martin am Tennengebirge, the Alpenhof offers a sauna, a sun terrace, a bar, and free WiFi access.

Hiking and mountain bike trails, as well as cross-country ski runs, start at the doorstep.

All rooms have a balcony overlooking the village, the valley and the surrounding mountains, satellite TV, and a bathroom with a bath or shower.

Breakfast is served in buffet style and includes a choice of freshly prepared eggs, coffee and tea.

A heated ski storage with a ski boot dryer is available at the Alpenhof. A natural swimming lake is 500 metres away.

A snow tube toboggan run can be reached in a 5-minute walk. Ski area Dachstein West can be reached within 10 minutes by skibus or car.
