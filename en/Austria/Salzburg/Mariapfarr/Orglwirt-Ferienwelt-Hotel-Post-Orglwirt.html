Description

Gesund & Fit Hotel Post-Örglwirt lies in the centre of Mariapfarr and offers an extensive buffet breakfast and fitness and wellness area.

Guests can find a swimmin pond at Gesund & Fit Hotel Post-Örglwirt and enjoy the various temperatures in the Finnish sauna, bio sauna, steam bath and infrared cabin or a traditional meal at the cosy restaurant.

All allergy-free rooms feature cable TV and a seating area. Private bathrooms come with a bath or shower, free toiletries, bathrobes and a hairdryer.

At no additional charge in summer, you have access to the Mariapfarrer Schwimmbad, just 20 metres away.

For children the property offers pony rides and features a childrens playground. In winter you can enjoy free access to the Tamsweg Badeinsel, where you will find an indoor pool, a heated outdoor pool and a water slide.

Fanningberg Ski area is 10 minutes by car and there is a toboggan trail in front of the house.

In the price for the winter a Winter Bonus Card is already included. The Lungau Bonus Card is provided free of charge and included in the summer prices.

It includes a lot a various summer activities for the whole family. You can use the card for entrance to pools, sightseeing, out door activities, wellness offers, etc.
