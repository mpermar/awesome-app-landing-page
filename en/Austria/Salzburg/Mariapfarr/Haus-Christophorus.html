Description

Built in a traditional Alpine farm-house style, the Haus Christophorus in Mariapfarr is located right next to a stop of the free ski bus.

Free WiFi is available. All rooms and apartments in the Haus Christoph feature Swiss stone pine furniture and satellite TV.

A private bathroom is fitted with a shower and a hairdryer. The ski bus brings you to the Fanningberg Ski Area in 5 minutes, to Grosseck-Speiereck in 7 minutes to Aineck-Katschberg in 15 minutes and to Obertauern in 20 minutes.

When there is sufficient snow you can ski right down to the front door. Kids can have fun in the petting zoo or in the playroom and you can relax in the infra-red cabin.

There is also a fitness room available on site. You have free access to the local Samsunn Wellness Centre.
