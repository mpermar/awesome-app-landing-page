Description

The Panoramahotel Burgeck enjoys a quiet location above Krimml in the Hohe Tauern National Park.

All rooms have an en-suite bathroom and a balcony. Wi-Fi is available free of charge.

Most rooms offer views of the waterfall and the summits of the Hohe Tauern. The Krimml Waterfalls can be reached on foot within 30 minutes via a panoramic hiking path.

The centre of Krimml is a 10-minute walk away. You can rent bikes and bobsleighs from the Panorama Burgeck.

A toboggan track is located right next to the hotel. From 01 May to 31 October, the Nationalpark Sommercard Mobil is included in the rates.

This card offers many free benefits and discounts in the region, including free use of local cable cars, buses and trains, and free admission to public pools and museums.
