Description

Just a 20-minute walk from the Krimml Waterfalls, this 4-star hotel enjoys a scenic location in the village of Krimml.

It offers a spa area, an indoor pool, and a heated outdoor pool overlooking the Hohe Tauern Mountains.

Free WiFi is available in the lobby. Featuring bright wooden furniture and floors, the spacious, non-smoking rooms at Hotel Krimmlerfälle all have a balcony or terrace, cable TV, and a seating area.

Some rooms overlook the waterfalls. The Krimmlerfälle Hotel’s restaurant serves Austrian and international cuisine, Salzburg specialities, and a selection of organic and vegetarian dishes.

The bar has a summer terrace overlooking the falls. Free private parking is available on site, and a bus stop is right outside.

A transfer service to the falls is available on request. Guests enjoy free admission to the falls throughout their opening season from April to October.

The Zillertal Arena Ski Area can be reached in a 15-minute drive. The nearby Hohe Tauern National Park offers a wide range of hiking and mountain bike trails.
