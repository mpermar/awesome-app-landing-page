Description

Gasthof Wiesen in Uttendorf has a garden and a terrace. Boasting a shared lounge, this property also provides guests with a restaurant.

Guests can make use of a bar. At the hotel, each room is equipped with a wardrobe, a TV and a private bathroom.

Every room has a balcony with mountain view. Guests at Gasthof Wiesen can enjoy a meal at the on site restaurant which also has a sun terrace.

Every morning a buffet breakfast is provided and in the evening a 3-course dinner for half-board.

At the accommodation guests will be able to enjoy activities in and around Uttendorf, like skiing.

Saalbach Hinterglemm is 17 km from Gasthof Wiesen, while Kitzbühel is 27 km away.
