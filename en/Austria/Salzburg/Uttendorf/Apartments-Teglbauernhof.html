Description

Offering self-catered apartments with free WiFi, the Teglbauernhof is located in Uttendorf, with direct access to the hiking trails and an on-site farm.

A Finnish sauna, a children's playground, and an indoor playroom are available. The apartments feature a kitchenette, satellite TV, and a private bathroom.

Most have a balcony. A ski boot dryer, which can also be used for motorbike clothing, is available at Apartments Teglbauernhof.

The Tauernradweg cycling trail and a private fishing and swimming pond are 200 metres away.

The local train station of the Pinzgau Railway, which works as a ski train in winter, can be reached in a 2-minute drive or 15-minute walk.

The closest restaurant is 2 km away, and a swimming lake is 3 km away. The Areitbahn Cable Car in Zell am See is 16 km away.
