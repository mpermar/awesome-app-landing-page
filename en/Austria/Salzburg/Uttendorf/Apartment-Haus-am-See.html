Description

Set in Uttendorf, in the heart of the Hohe Tauern National Park and directly at a small lake, Apartment Haus am See offers accommodation units with a balcony or terrace.

Each apartment features a well equipped kitchen or kitchenette, cable TV and a bathroom with shower.

Towels and baby equipment can be rented on site. A delivery service for fresh bread rolls is possible on request and the restaurant next door also offers breakfast options.

A supermarket is 200 metres away. Apartment Haus am See has a garden with barbecue facilities and outdoor furniture, a sun bathing lawn with sun loungers, and a children's playground.

Guests can enjoy the indoor football hall, the climbing hall or the billiard room at the property.

Guided hiking tours are organised on request. A spa area with sauna and steam bath is available for a surcharge.

The free ski bus stop is in front of the house and leads to the Weißsee Ski Area, 16 km away.

The Paß Thurn Ski Area is 10 km away and the station for the ski train to other ski regions is 200 metres away.

An ice skating rink is 50 metres from the house and the cross country skiing trails start at the doorstep.
