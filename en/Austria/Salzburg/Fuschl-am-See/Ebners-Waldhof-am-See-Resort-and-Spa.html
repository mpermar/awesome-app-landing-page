Description

This elegant, child-friendly and family-run holiday resort offers a fantastic location right at the shore of picturesque Lake Fuschl in the Salzkammergut, only a 20-minute drive from Salzburg.

Ebner's Waldhof am See Resort & Spa features a beautiful spa area, which stretches over more than 4,000 m¬≤, and a wide range of sports and leisure options.

Half board includes a breakfast buffet, a small lunch, a 5-course dinner as well as cakes and a snack in the afternoon.
