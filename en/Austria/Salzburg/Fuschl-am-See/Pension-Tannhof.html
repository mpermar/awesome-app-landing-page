Description

Pension Tannhof is only 100 metres from the shore of Lake Fuschl and the centre of Fuschl am See.

All rooms and apartments have a balcony. Free Wi-Fi is available. Furnished in a traditional wooden style, each room and apartment at the Tannhof features satellite TV and a bathroom.

Guests can play table tennis and relax on the large terrace. The big garden at the Tannhof Pension features barbecue facilities and a children’s playground.

Free admission to Fuschl’s public swimming pool is available. The Gaißau-Hintersee ski area can be reached in a 30-minute drive.

Free private parking, including a storage room for bicycles, is available at Pension Tannhof.
