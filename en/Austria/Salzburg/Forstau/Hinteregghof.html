Description

Offering free WiFi, a sauna and an infrared cabin, this organic farmhouse in Forstau is 1 km from the Reiteralm Ski Area.

Guests of the Hinteregghof can taste homemade farm products, and toboggans are provided free of charge.

The spacious apartments feature a living room with a flat-screen cable TV, a kitchen with a dining area, a bathroom, a balcony, and a terrace.

On request, fresh bread rolls can be delivered every morning. Guests can play table tennis and use a ski storage room with a ski boot dryer.

The Hinteregghof also has a sun terrace with barbecue facilities and a trampoline for children.

Free private parking is available on site. A ski bus stops 700 metres away, and the closest restaurant and a small supermarket are 500 metres away.
