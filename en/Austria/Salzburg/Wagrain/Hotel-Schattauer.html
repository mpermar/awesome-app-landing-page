Description

Situated in the centre of Wagrain, the modern Hotel Schattauer offers a spa area with a sauna, steam bath, and infrared cabin, as well as an outdoor pool in the summer season.

A free ski bus leaves directly from the hotel. The spacious rooms feature cable TV, a safe, and a private bathroom fitted with a shower and a hairdryer.

Free WiFi is available in all areas of Hotel Schattauer. The restaurant serves a rich breakfast buffet and an evening menu featuring Austrian specialties and desserts, along with a selection of wines.

From 01 December 2016, free admission to the Wasserwelt Amadé (public indoor pool) for 3 hours per day is included in the rate.
