Description

Only a few steps away from the Flying Mozart Cable Car in Wagrain, Landhaus & Haus Rustika offers apartments with a balcony and cable TV, free WiFi, and free parking.

The spa area comes with a sauna, hot tub, steam shower, and infrared cabin. The garden at Landhaus & Haus Rustika has an outdoor pool, a sunbathing lawn, a children’s playground, a barbecue site, and a petting zoo.

Haus Rustika is close to many shops and restaurants, several hiking trails, and the Wasserwelt Amadé (public swimming pool).
