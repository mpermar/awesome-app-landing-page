Description

The Wanderhotel Erika is a friendly 3-star hotel in a quiet, central location amidst the Amadé Sports World in Salzburg Province.

Free WiFi is available in all areas, and a computer is provided on site. Enjoy a vacation on the sunny side of Wagrain, personal attention from your hosts and a comfortable house with cosy and bright rooms.

In summer the hotel is an ideal starting point for hikes, while in winter the nearest ski bus stop is only 40 metres away.

As a member of Europa Wanderhotels (European hiking hotels) decorated with 4 mountain crystals, our summer offer includes 3 to 5 guided hikes (Monday to Friday) and Nordic walking offers (including instruction), as well as attractive mountain bike trails, flying fox and an adventure centre in the immediate vicinity.
