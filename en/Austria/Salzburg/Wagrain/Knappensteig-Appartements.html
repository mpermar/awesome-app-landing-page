Description

Located in Wagrain, Knappensteig Appartements is just a 5-minute walk from the Flying Mozart Cable Car and the Ski Amadé Ski Area and offers a spa area and spacious apartments with free WiFi and a balcony or terrace.

Overlooking the garden or the mountains, each apartment is furnished in a modern Alpine style and features a kitchen with a dining area, cable TV, and a bathroom.

Spa facilities at Appartements Knappensteig include a sauna, steam bath, and sun bed.

Guests can play billiards and table tennis, and bicycles are provided free of charge.

There is also a children’s playroom. The garden includes a sun terrace, a children’s playground, and barbecue facilities.

Free private parking spaces and a ski storage room with a ski boot dryer are available on site.

The Wasserwelt Amadé public outdoor pool is a 2-minute walk away and can be used free of charge by guests.
