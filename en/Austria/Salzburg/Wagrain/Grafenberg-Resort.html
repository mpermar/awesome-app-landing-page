Description

Some rooms have a kitchenette with a dishwasher, an oven and a microwave.

A buffet breakfast is available daily at the property.

Guests can use the hot tub and sauna. The area is popular for skiing and hiking.

Grafenberg Express I is 600 feet from the resort, while Wasserwelt Wagrain is 1,500 feet away. W. A. Mozart Airport is 35 miles from the property.
