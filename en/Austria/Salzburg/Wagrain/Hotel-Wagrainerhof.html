Description

The family-run Hotel Wagrainerhof is situated in the centre of Wagrain in the province of Salzburg.

It features a restaurant and a spa area. The breakfast buffet in the morning comprises cereals, freshly cooked egg dishes, fruits and more.

Guests are welcome to relax in the sky bar or in the cafe, and can use the garden terrace in the warmer months.

In winter, half board consists of breakfast, afternoon snacks and a 4-course dinner menu.

During summer, the all-inclusive arrangement includes breakfast, lunch, afternoon snacks, a 4-course dinner and refreshments from 10:00 until 23:00.

A selection of wines is also available. For families the hotel provides a children's playground in summer, and a children's playroom.

Wagrain and its surroundings are suitable for a range of sports and leisure options.

The Wagrainerhof is located in the heart of Ski Amadé, the largest contiguous ski area in Austria, with more than 870 km of slopes and 270 lifts.
