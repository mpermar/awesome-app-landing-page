Description

Located in Grödig at the foot of Untersberg Mountain, the Sallerhof is just 6 km from Salzburg’s city centre.

It offers free wired internet and a furnished balcony in every room. The modern rooms are very spacious and feature wooden floors, a flat-screen cable TV, and a bathroom with hairdryer.

Each room offers views of the garden or Untersberg Mountain. A large breakfast buffet with local products and homemade specialities is served every morning.

Many restaurants are close by. Free private parking is provided directly at the Hotel Garni Sallerhof.

Guests can rent bicycles and book tickets and excursions at the reception. The Sound of Music tour picks up guests at the front door.

A bus stop providing direct connections to Salzburg Main Train Station is just 250 metres away.

The trip takes about 35 minutes. The Grödig exit of the A10 motorway is less than one km away.
