Description

The 4-star superior Familienresort Ellmauhof in Hinterglemm offers a variety of activities for families with children in a quiet location with mountain views. Internet access and parking are available free of charge.

The apartments have a balcony or a terrace. Complete baby equipment is available.

For children various activities such as campfire and barbecue are offered. There is also a petting zoo and professional child care. An adventure playground, an indoor swimming pool with a slide and riding lessons on site are also featured.

The Ellmauhof has a beach volleyball court, a football pitch, a badminton court, an aerobics and fitness center, and a large spa area with massages and beauty treatments.

Guests can enjoy fine wines from the property's own wine cellar in the restaurant or in the cozy winter garden.

In winter Familienresort Ellmauhof - das Feriengut is right next to the slope of the Saalbach-Hinterglemm ski area. In summer it is surrounded by meadows and fields. The surrounding area is ideal for hiking and mountain biking.

In summer, you will receive a free Joker Card. Many excursion destinations in Saalbach, entrance fees, reductions on cable car fees and much more is included in this card.
