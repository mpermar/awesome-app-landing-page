Description

This 4-star hotel is located in the pedestrian zone of Saalbach, only 50 metres from the ski lifts.

It has a large spa area with an indoor pool. Free WiFi is available. The Saalbacher Hof’s rooms are furnished in an elegant country style and feature cable TV and a bathroom with bathrobes and a hairdryer.

Some rooms have a balcony. The spa area features an indoor pool with a waterfall, 3 saunas, and a relaxation room with water beds.

The garden offers panoramic views over Saalbach. The garden offers panoramic views and an outdoor pool.

Each week, different courses like herbal salt production, green smoothie workshop, meditation classes, and more are offered.

The restaurant serves classic Salzburg cuisine and international dishes, as well as fine wines.

The Saalbacher Hof offers daily free admission to its après ski bar and nightclub Castello, with live music (from mid of January) and DJs.

A Silvester Gala Dinner is available on 31 December upon prior reservation and at an additional cost.

Ski passes are available at the reception. Guests of the Saalbacher Hof receive a 10% reduction at all ski rentals in Saalbach.
