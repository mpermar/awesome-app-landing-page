Description

Only a few steps away from the centre of Hinterglemm, Hotel Salzburg enjoys a quiet and sunny location with panoramic mountain views and offers free WiFi access in all areas.

The property has direct access to the Saalbach-Hinterglemm-Leogang Ski Region, as well as to hiking and mountain bike trails.

The Reiterkogel cable car is 80 metres away. Hotel Salzburg’s spa area includes an indoor pool and 2 saunas.

There is a sun terrace and a sunbathing lawn in the garden. Rooms offer a balcony, bathrobes, a minibar, and a safety deposit box.

The restaurant serves classic Austrian cuisine and fine wines. The children’s playroom features a climbing wall, tabletop football, and air hockey.

Parking spaces are available free of charge at Hotel Salzburg. There is also an underground car park which can be used free of charge.

Ski passes are available at the reception. From mid-May to mid-October, the Joker Card is included in price and comprises different discounts and benefits in the surrounding region.
