Description

At the hotel, rooms contain a balcony with a mountain view. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with free toiletries, while certain rooms at Hotel Oberschwarzach also feature a seating area. The rooms have a desk.

A buffet breakfast is served daily at the property. At the accommodation you'll find a restaurant serving Austrian cuisine.

The property has a spa center. Guests at Hotel Oberschwarzach will be able to enjoy activities in and around Saalbach Hinterglemm, like skiing.

Wallehenlift is 2.1 miles from the hotel. The nearest airport is W. A. Mozart Airport, 34 miles from the property.

Hinterglemm is a great choice for travelers interested in pubs, convenient public transportation and cleanliness.
