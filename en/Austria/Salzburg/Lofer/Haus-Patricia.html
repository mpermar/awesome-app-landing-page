Description

Surrounded by a beautiful Alpine panorama, Haus Patricia enjoys a central, south-facing location only a short walk away from the centre of Lofer.

Free WiFi is available in all areas. The rooms and apartments offer mountain views from the balconies.

Guests can enjoy the garden with a terrace and a sun deck. There is also a sauna and a games/bar room with a snooker table.

Hiking and mountain biking tours can start right at the guesthouse’s doorstep. Canoeing is possible close by.

The traffic-free centre of Lofer, with its many shops, restaurants and bars, as well as a cable car and a swimming pool, is just a 3-minute walk away from Haus Patricia.

The cable car takes you to the Loferalm ski area, which features 58 km of perfectly groomed slopes.

At a surcharge, an airport shuttle service is available. From June to September, a local summer card is included in the rate.

It ensures free access to the local swimming pools and the cable car can be used free of charge as well.
