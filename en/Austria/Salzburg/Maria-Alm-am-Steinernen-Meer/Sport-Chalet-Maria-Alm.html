Description

Located in Maria Alm am Steinernen Meer, 200 metres from the Hochkönig Ski Area, Sport Chalet Maria Alm GmbH offers self-catering accommodation units with free WiFi, a free ski storage room and access to a fitness centre.

Private parking spaces are available on site free of charge and the closest ski bus is 20 metres away.

The modern apartments at the Sport Chalet also come with a well-equipped kitchen, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a living area with a sofa.

In addition, they feature a washing machine and a bathroom with a hairdryer, bathrobes and free toiletries.

In some units, a sauna is provided, as well. Upon request and for a surcharge, groceries can be delivered right to the apartments´ doorstep.

Several hiking and cycling trails start directly on the premises and a games room is provided, as well.
