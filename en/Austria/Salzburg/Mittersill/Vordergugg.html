Description

This organic farm is 2 km from the centre of Mittersill and a 10-minute drive from Hollersbach as well as from the Kitzbühel Alps Ski Area.

It offers a Finnish sauna with infrared function, free WiFi, free bicycle hire, and free guided hiking tours.

All rooms and apartments at Vordergugg have a balcony overlooking Mittersill or the Hohe Tauern Mountains.

They feature bright wooden furniture and floors, satellite TV, and at least 1 bathroom.

The apartments include a kitchenette. Breakfast is served in the breakfast room. Guests of Vordergugg can play table tennis and use a ski storage room with a ski boot dryer.

The garden includes a children’s playground and barbecue facilities. Free private parking is available on site.

The Mittersill Golf Course is 2 km away, and Zell am See is 25 km from the property.
