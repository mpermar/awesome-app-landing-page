Description

Situated in the Hohe Tauern National Park, Sporthotel Kogler is located in Mittersill, only 4 km from the Kitzbühel ski area.

It offers free Wi-Fi and a breakfast buffet. Sporthotel Kogler provides a 1. 500 m² spa area, featuring a fully equipped gym, tennis and squash courts as well as a pool with a hot tub and sauna area.

The hotel's entire water has been revitalized by Grander Technology. Guests can enjoy dinner either in the Sporthotel Kogler’s traditionally furnished restaurant or on the terrace, overlooking the mountain panorama.

Drinks are also served at the bar, which features an open fireplace. Sporthotel Kogler also provides a petting zoo with rabbits, guinea pigs, goats and ponies as well as child care and a children’s programme.

The cable car for the Pass Thurn ski area is only a 5-minute drive from Sporthotel Kogler.
