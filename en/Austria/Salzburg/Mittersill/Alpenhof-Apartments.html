Description

The Alpenhof Apartments in Mittersill offer fully-equipped apartments with Hohe Tauern mountain views, infrared cabins, massage showers, satellite TV and free WiFi.

The kitchens feature a fridge, an oven, a coffee machine and all other necessary amenities.

You can start your day with a rich buffet breakfast or order fresh bread rolls. Once a week you will be offered afternoon coffee and cakes for free.

Alpenhof Apartments has a lounge area with panoramic views, an espresso machine and a kid's corner.

You can unwind on the sun terrace or during a massage. In winter, guests can get active at the ski area Paß Thurn - Kitzbühel, which is only 3 km away and can be reached with the free ski bus, which stops 100 metres from Alpenhof Apartments.

Cross-country skiing trails, winter hiking tracks, toboggan runs and a public indoor swimming pool are also nearby.

A heated ski storage is on site. In summer, guided hiking tours are offered and hiking poles and backpacks are available on site.

Zell am See and Lake Zell are a 30-minute drive away. Kitzbühel is 20 km away. From May to late October the Nationalpark Card is included in the rate.
