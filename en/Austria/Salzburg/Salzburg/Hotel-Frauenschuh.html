Description

A 10-minute drive from the centre of Salzburg, Hotel Frauenschuh is located in the quiet and green district of Leopoldskron-Moos.

It has a spa area and offers free outdoor parking. Hotel Frauenschuh’s rooms offer WiFi, satellite TV and a bathroom with hairdryer.

Some have a balcony and a kitchen. The spa area includes a sauna and a steam bath, as well as a small fitness room.

Bathrobes can be rented at the reception. A rich breakfast buffet is served each morning.

2 restaurants are only steps away. Hotel Frauenschuh features a children’s playground and storage facilities for bicycles and motorcycles.

Guests can play table tennis and darts, relax on the terrace and in the garden, or rent bicycles to explore the surroundings.

Garage parking is available upon prior arrangement and at a surcharge. The old town can be reached by bus in 12 minutes.
