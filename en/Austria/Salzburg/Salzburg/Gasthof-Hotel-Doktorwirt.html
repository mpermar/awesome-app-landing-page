Description

The individually furnished rooms all offer free internet access.

The Doktorwirt is a centuries-old inn with an indoor pool, an outdoor pool, and a spa area with sauna, steam bath, solarium, and gym.

The restaurant features a terrace and a beer garden. It serves traditional Austrian cuisine.

A bus stop offering direct services to the center of the city is right in front of Gasthof Hotel Doktorwirt.
