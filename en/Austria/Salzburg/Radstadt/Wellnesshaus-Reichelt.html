Description

Wellnesshaus Reichelt offers rooms and apartments in a quiet location in Radstadt, only a 10-minute walk away from the centre.

There is a spa area with an indoor pool. The breakfast buffet is served in the light-flooded winter garden.

There is a barbecue hut in the garden. Spa facilities at Haus Reichelt include a Finnish sauna, a bio sauna, and an infrared cabin.

The free ski bus to the Ski Amadé lifts stops 100 metres away from Wellnesshaus Reichelt.

A cross-country ski run is right outside. In summer, Haus Reichelt provides electronic bicycles.

It is situated along the Ennsradweg (bicycle trail). Private parking is available free of charge.
