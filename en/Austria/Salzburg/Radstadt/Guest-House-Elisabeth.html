Description

Located on a plateau at 1,143 metres above sea level, this quietly situated bed and breakfast 4 km from Radstadt's centre offers views of the Dachstein Massif and the Radstädter Tauern mountain range.

It features a garden with an outdoor pool and a sun terrace. It is also 8 km from the A10 motorway and 3 km from the toll-free B99 road, the Katschberg federal route.

Close to the Ski Amadé ski area and the Schladming-Dachstein hiking region, Guest House Elisabeth offers a breakfast room and lounge and free Wi-Fi access in public areas.

Take-away breakfast and packed lunches are available on request. The nearest restaurant is 2.

8 km away. Guest House Elisabeth’s garden also includes barbecue facilities and a children’s playground.

Free private parking is available directly in front of the house and garage parking is available at an additional charge.
