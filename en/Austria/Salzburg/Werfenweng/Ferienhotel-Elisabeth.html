Description

Surrounded by a spacious garden, Ferienhotel Elisabeth offers beautiful views of the surrounding mountains, including the Hochkönig and the Tennengebirge and Hagengebirge mountain ranges.

You will find a panoramic restaurant, a hotel bar, a lobby with an open fireplace, a big sunbathing lawn, and the cozy Jagdstube lounge with its open fireplace. The internet terminal and wireless LAN internet in the lobby are both free of charge. All rooms are newly furnished and non-smoking.

The hotel offers modern spa and vitality area, with a sauna, a herbal sauna, an infrared cabin, a brine steam bath, a massage center, a solarium, relaxation rooms, and water beds.

A large, secure parking lot, which you can use free of charge, is right next to Ferienhotel Elisabeth.

The beautiful surrounding landscape offers many recreation options, attractions, and hiking trails. There is a lake suitable for swimming with a park. Hohenwerfen Castle is 4 miles away, the Eisriesenwelt ice caves are 6 miles away. The city of Salzburg is about a 30-minute drive away.

In winter Werfenweng is the perfect place for skiing, cross-country skiing, winter hiking, and snowshoe hiking.
