Description

Located in the centre of the scenic village of Werfenweng, Gut Wenghof - Family Resort Werfenweng offers many activities for children, a health spa with a heated open-air pool, and an indoor pool open from 08:00 to 20:00.

The main building contains a lounge with a bar, a restaurant with show cooking, a conservatory, a self-service bar, and a small wine cellar.

A kids club with child care and excursions for families is also offered at Gut Wenghof.

There is a Austrian and international buffet as well as speciality restaurants. Beverages are served from 07:30 to 22:00 and include beer, wine, soft drinks, coffee, tea and some local spirits.

All rooms in the main building have a balcony. In the annex building, some rooms have a balcony.

Free transfers are offered from the Pfarrwerfen and Bischofshofen train stations.

The hotel owned alpine chalet which offers regional meals and a panoramic view of the surrounding mountains is just a 40 minute hike from the property.

The samo card or samo card+ is included as of December 2019 and allows free admission and discounts at several local attractions as well as for rental cars, cable cars and public transport.
