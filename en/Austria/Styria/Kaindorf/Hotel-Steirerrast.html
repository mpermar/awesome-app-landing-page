Description

Located in the centre of Kaindorf bei Hartberg, Hotel Steirerrast is surrounded by a large park and features an indoor pool renovated in 2015, a spa area and relaxation room, and a restaurant.

The spacious rooms all feature a balcony or terrace, a flat-screen cable TV, free WiFi, and a bathroom with a shower.

The Steirerrast’s programme of activities changes every week and includes Nordic walking, aqua gymnastics, morning exercises, and Smovey exercises.

It is offered from Monday to Friday. Indoor pool and sauna is included in the room rate until 10pm.

Free private parking is available on site. Hartberg, Bad Waltersdorf, the A2 motorway, and Lake Stubenberg can be reached within a 15-minute drive.
