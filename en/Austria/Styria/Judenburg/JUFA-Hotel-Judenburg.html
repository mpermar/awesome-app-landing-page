Description

JUFA Hotel Judenburg occupies a former Jesuit monastery in the centre of the medieval trading town of Judenburg in Upper Styria.

It offers lounges and seminar rooms, a ping pong table, and an open-air theatre in the interior courtyard.

The unique ambience of the JUFA Judenburg is especially evident in the historic corridors, which are decorated with stucco work and frescoes.
