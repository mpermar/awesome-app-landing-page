Description

Offering a restaurant, Radhotel Schischek is located in Oberpurkla in Southern Styria, a 10-minute drive from Bad Radkersburg and the Parktherme Thermal Spa.

Free WiFi and free private parking are available, and guests can relax in the garden or enjoy a drink in the bar.

All rooms have satellite TV, a sofa, a desk, and a bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Some also have a balcony. At Radhotel Schischek there is a children's playground, darts and mini-golf.

Special diet menus are available upon request. Bicycle rentals can be arranged. Free of charge luggage storage and a safety deposit box are also at guests' disposal.
