Description

Ferienwohnung Fam Leuner offers self-catering accommodation units with free WiFi and a balcony, located in Altaussee, within 5 km from the Loser Ski Area and 3 km from the closest ski lifts.

Free private parking is possible on site. Offering mountain views, the apartments also come with a seating area, a flat-screen satellite TV, a fully equipped kitchen with dishwasher, and a bathroom with shower and hairdryer.

Ferienwohnung Fam Leuner has a garden with barbecue facilities and a children's playground.

Supermarkets and restaurants can be found within a distance of 1 km. The Altaussee Lake is 2 km away.
