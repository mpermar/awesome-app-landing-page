Description

Located in Tieschen in the scenic hill country of South-Eastern Styria, JUFA Hotel Tieschen is surrounded by vineyards and offers a sauna and a restaurant serving regional cuisine, with many products coming from surrounding organic farms.

All rooms have wooden floors and solid wooden furniture. They all come equipped with cable TV and have a private bathroom including a hairdryer.

An internet station is available for free in the common room. Children can have fun on the playground, in the playroom, and in the petting zoo.

You can enjoy evenings around the campfire or make use of the barbecue area. Rental bikes are available.

The thermal spas of Bad Gleichenberg and Bad Radkersburg, as well as the border crossing to Slovenia, can be reached within a 20-minute drive.

Maribor is 50 km away from the JUFA Tieschen. The Genuss Card is included from March to October, providing free admission or discounts to over 120 attractions in the region.
