Description

In a quiet countryside location 1 km from the centre of Semriach and a 30-minute drive from Graz, the Trattnerhof offers a spa area with an indoor pool, a restaurant, an indoor tennis centre, and free WiFi.

The indoor pool, sauna and steam bath can be used free of charge. A surcharge applies for the infrared cabin and the sun bed.

Rooms at the Trattnerhof feature cable TV and a private bathroom. Some have a balcony.

A children’s playground and free private parking are available on site. The Trattnerhof also features an indoor archery hall and an outdoor archery course.
