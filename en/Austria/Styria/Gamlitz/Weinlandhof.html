Description

Located in the centre of Gamlitz, a 2-minute walk from the main square, this family-run 4-star hotel is set directly at the South Styrian Wine Road.

It comes with a small spa area featuring a sauna, an infrared cabin, a steam bath and an indoor pool.

The Weinlandhof offers spacious rooms with a private bathroom, a minibar, a flat-screen TV and a balcony overlooking the garden.

Guests can enjoy international and Styrian cuisine with natural products, as well as a wide range of wines, in the restaurant.

The garden features a pond and a seating area. A small vineyard awaits you at the entrance to the Weinlandhof.

In autumn you can harvest your own grapes there. A 2-minute walk away, the Motorikpark is Europe's largest and features several walking and hiking trails.
