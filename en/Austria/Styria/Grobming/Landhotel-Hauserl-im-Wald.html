Description

Set in a green surrounding, Landhotel Häuserl im Wald is 3. 5 km from the centre of Gröbming.

It features a wellness area with a new Finnish sauna, a steam bath, an infra red cabin and a seasonal, heated outdoor pool with a sunbathing terrace.

A buffet breakfast can be enjoyed every morning and WiFi is available free of charge.

The bright rooms are equipped with wooden furnishings and some feature a balcony offering panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.

They are equipped with an LCD TV with satellite channels and there is a private bathroom with a shower.

A bicycle sports shop is 3. 5 km away, providing bicycle and e-bike rental, while 6 golf courses are within 40 km of the hotel.

The hotel is ideally located for motorbike trips, such as a trip on the Stoderzinken Alpenstraße High Alpine Road or the Sölkpass Mountain Pass.

You can ask the owner for tips. From the end May until mid-October is the Schladming Dachstein summer card included in room rate.

The Hauser Kaibling Ski Region can be reached in a 10-minute drive. The Dachstein-Tauern Golf Course is 15 km from the guest house.
