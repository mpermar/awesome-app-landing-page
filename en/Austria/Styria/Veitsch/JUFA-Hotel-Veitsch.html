Description

Located in Veitsch in Upper Styria, JUFA Hotel Veitsch is directly connected to a public indoor pool offering free admission for hotel guests.

Here you will find a big water slide, a sauna and spa area with 4 saunas, a baby pool, and a sun bed.

The rooms are very spacious and equipped with all modern amenities. The rich breakfast buffet comprises many homemade and organic products.

Lunch and dinner are served buffet-style. Salad buffets and food suitable for children are available every day.

Te JUFA Veitsch features a café with WiFi access, dining rooms, seminar rooms, table tennis and billiards tables, a children's play room, a lockable bicycle shelter, a bicycle rental (also for BMX bikes, with helmet), and a children's playground.

Additional leisure options include a brook for children to play in, a beach volleyball court, football and tennis courts, a BMX trail, and a fun court for inline skating.

A nice sunbathing lawn is available for relaxation. There are many hiking and mountain bike trails, as well as riding stables, nearby.

Ballooning, paragliding and carriage rides are also available. The pilgrimage route to Mariazell leads right past the JUFA Veitsch.
