Description

Set in the picturesque natural landscape of the Almenland Region, north of Graz, the 3-star superior Familienhotel Herbst in Fladnitz an der Teichalm offers a café with a large children’s playground, as well as a spa area including an indoor pool with seawater and various saunas.

Featuring a balcony with views of the rural hilly surroundings, each room comes with cable TV and a seating area.

The bathrooms are equipped with bath or shower, a toilet, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

The apartment additionally has a kitchenette. Guests can unwind in the relaxation room of the Familienhotel Herbst, or on the sunbathing lawn with sun loungers.

Massages and beauty treatments are available on request. A rich breakfast buffet with regional products is offered every morning, and the restaurant serves Styrian specialities.

In summer, guests can enjoy home made ice-cream specialities. The surrounding of the Familienhotel Herbst is ideal for hiking and mountain biking tours.

The Teichalm, a ski and hiking area, is just a 15-minute drive away, and the Almenland Golf Course can be reached in a 3-minut drive.
