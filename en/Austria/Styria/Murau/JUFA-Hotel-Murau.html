Description

JUFA Hotel Murau offers a quiet location in the picturesque town of Murau in Upper Styria, only a 5-minute walk away from the centre.

Free WiFi is available in all areas. In addition to spacious rooms, the hotel features cosy lounges, a café, a climbing wall, a children's play corner, a ski storage room with a ski boot warmer, options for table tennis and tabletop football, a large sauna area, a reading and relaxation room, and a public cinema.

Outdoors there is a meadow and a grill. JUFA Hotel Murau's idyllic surroundings offer many sports and leisure opportunities, including the famous ski and snowboard resort Kreischberg, the Frauenalm Ski Area and the Grebenzen Nature Reserve.
