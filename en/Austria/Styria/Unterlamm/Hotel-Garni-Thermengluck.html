Description

Hotel Garni Thermenglück enjoys a sunny location in Unterlamm, 4 km from the spa resort Loipersdorf.

It offers accommodation units with a balcony or a terrace and free WiFi. A breakfast buffet with regional and home made products is served daily and can be consumed on the sun terrace in good weather conditions.

All units are fitted with a flat-screen satellite TV, a Nespresso coffee machine and a bathroom with bathrobes, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

A minibar and coffee making facilities await you in every room. You can sunbathe in the garden while children can have fun in Garni Thermenglück’s games room.

Restaurants and supermarkets are a 3 to 5-minute drive away and free private parking is possible on site.

Bad Waltersdorf and Bad Blumau can both be reached within 20 minutes by car. The Gillersdorf Golf Course is 9 km away and cycling trails start right next to the property.
