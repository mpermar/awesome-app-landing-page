Description

Located in Pichl in the Enns Valley, this 4-star resort hotel offers gourmet cuisine, tennis and squash courts, and a 3,000 m² spa area.

A free shuttle takes guests to the Reiteralm Cable Car, just 500 metres away. The Pichlmayrgut’s spa area includes an outdoor pool, an indoor pool, a hot tub, various saunas and steam baths, and a gym.

A wide range of massage and beauty treatments is offered. Free Wi-Fi is provided in public areas.

The spacious, colourfully decorated rooms at Hotel Pichlmayrgut feature satellite TV, a minibar, and a bathroom with bathrobes and slippers.

Free wired internet access is available in all rooms. A buffet breakfast is offered and half-board additionally includes a 6-course dinner featuring Austrian and international dishes.

Many products come from the hotel’s own organic farm. A large selection of fine wines is on offer.

In summer, guided hikes are offered regularly, and bicycles can be hired for free.

In winter, guests can buy ski passes on site. From May to October, guests can enjoy the grenzen.
