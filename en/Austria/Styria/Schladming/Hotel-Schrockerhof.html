Description

With direct access to the ski slopes, Hotel Schröckerhof enjoys a hillside position, a 5-minute walk from the Planai Chair Lift.

You can enjoy regional cuisine at the restaurant which features a sun terrace and panoramic mountain views.

Wellness facilities include a swimming pond. All rooms come with a balcony and free WiFi.

Facilities include a flat-screen satellite TV and a private bathroom with free toiletries.

The garden includes a playground, barbecue facilities and a football field. The wellness area with garden views features a steam bath and various saunas.

Horseback riding is possible on site. A stop is a free ski bus is a 3-minute walk from the Schröckerhof.

The centre of Schladming can be reached in 5 minutes by car. From late May to mid-October, the Schladming-Dachstein Summer Card is included in the rate.

This card offers many free benefits and discounts, including free use of local cable cars and buses.
