Description

Family-run since 3 generations and renovated in 2017, Vulkanlandhotel Legenstein in South-Eastern Styria Region is only 2 km away from the spa village of Bad Gleichenberg.

The Vulkanium Spa Area comprises a spacious garden with an outdoor pool (heated all year) accessible from inside, an indoor pool, and a sauna area with 5 saunas and 2 relaxation rooms.

A rich and healthy breakfast buffet is available every morning. An extended half-board is offered on request in the air-conditioned conservatory, including a lunch buffet with salads, soups, home-made and regional spread and bread, and a 5-course dinner menu.

An afternoon snack with a cake at the hotel bar is also offered and wine tastings can be enjoyed on site.

Vulkanlandhotel Legenstein's sauna area boasts a Finnish sauna, a steam bath, an infrared cabin, a laconium, and a Bio herbal sauna.

It also has a fitness and gymnastics room with panoramic garden views. Nordic walking is also available.

Hotel guests receive a 20% green fee reduction at the golf course in Bad Gleichenberg.

2 tennis courts with artificial turf, as well as indoor tennis centre and a miniature golf course, are in the immediate vicinity.

The use of the pool, wellness and sports areas, as well as the activity programmes, is included in the price.

You can use all facilities of the hotel on your day of departure for as long as you like.

The in-house beauty and massage studio offers a wide range of relaxing and healthy treatments.
