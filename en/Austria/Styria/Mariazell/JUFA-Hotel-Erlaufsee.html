Description

Just 500 metres from the Bürgeralpe Ski Area, JUFA Hotel Erlaufsee is next to a sports centre 2 km from the centre of Mariazell.

A spa area is available. The spacious rooms at JUFA Erlaufsee include cable TV, wooden floors, a seating area, and a bathroom with hairdryer.

All have access to a terrace. Special discounts are offered at the sports centre, which features an indoor climbing wall, a fitness room, a squash court, and indoor tennis and soccer courts.

Guests can also play tennis, football, basketball, and beach volleyball outdoors.

Spa facilities include a sauna, a steam bath, and a sun bed. The Erlaufsee JUFA features a children’s playground and an internet café.

Free WiFi is provided in public areas, and free parking is available on site. A ski storage room is provided at the ski lift.

The scenic Erlaufsee Lake is 1. 5 km away. Guests can use an indoor pool at a partner hotel 10 minutes’ drive away free of charge.
