Description

JUFA Hotel Sigmundsberg is located 3 km from Mariazell and offers a large indoor pool, bowling alleys, a toboggan run, and a large garden with a terrace.

A breakfast buffet and free parking are available. A public bus stop is 200 metres from the JUFA Sigmundsberg.

Several cable cars and ski lift leading into the Gemeindealpe ski and hiking area can be found in the immediate vicinity.
