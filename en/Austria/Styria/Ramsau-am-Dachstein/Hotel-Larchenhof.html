Description

Set against the scenic backdrop of the Dachstein-Tauern Massif, the 4-star Hotel Lärchenhof in Ramsau offers a large spa area, a sunbathing lawn, and an indoor pool with panoramic views of the surroundings.

A ski lift is right next door. Half-board and free WiFi are available. Rooms at the Lärchenhof include a large private balcony, a seating area and a minibar.

All rooms feature a spacious bathroom. Bathrobes and slippers are also provided in the room.

Spa facilities include different types of sauna, a steam bath, and a fitness room.

There are several treatment rooms where guests can enjoy a massage or a beauty therapy.

Meals are prepared with organic local products and the menu includes fine Austrian wines.

The rich breakfast is buffet style. The Lärchenhof is close to a large network of cross-country ski trails, and offers direct access to the Ramsau's hiking trail network.
