Description

Opened in 2013, Hotel Stierer is 500 metres from the centre of Ramsau and offers a restaurant and access to the slopes of the Ski Amadé Region by car within a few minutes.

Each room has a balcony overlooking the mountains. The rooms at the Stierer Hotel are furnished in a modern Alpine style and come with a flat-screen cable TV and a bathroom with a hairdryer.

Guests benefit from free use of the sauna and infrared cabin, and from free WiFi in public areas.

An indoor playroom for children, a lockable bicycle storage room, and a lockable ski storage room with a ski boot dryer are available.

Free private parking is available on site. Hiking and cycling trails start at the doorstep, and the free ski bus to the Rittisberg and Planai Ski Areas stops right outside.

The Ramsau Beach Leisure Centre is 4 km away, and Schladming can be reached in a 10-minute drive.
