Description

Located right next to the slopes and cross-country ski runs of the Ramsau Ski Area, Hotel Pfeffermühle offers an indoor pool and a spa area.

The spacious rooms all have a balcony overlooking the mountains. Featuring bright wooden furniture and floors, the rooms include a flat-screen satellite TV, a minibar, and a safe.

Bathrooms include bathrobes, slippers, and a hairdryer. Spa facilities include a sauna, a steam bath, an infrared cabin, and a sun bed.

The Pfeffermühle offers free wired internet in the rooms and free Wi-Fi in the entire hotel.

The restaurant serves Styrian specialities, Austrian cuisine, and Italian dishes.

It uses regional products and herbs from the hotel’s own garden. The wine cellar stocks a large selection of Austrian and Italian wines.

Free covered private parking is available on site, and hiking and mountain bike trails start right outside.

A ski school is directly opposite, and the centre of Ramsau is 2 km away. The ski bus takes guests to the Planai, Hauser Kaibling, Hochwurzen, and Reiteralm Ski Areas within 10 minutes.

The bus stop can be reached on skis in 2 minutes. In summer the Schladming-Dachstein Card is included in the rate.
