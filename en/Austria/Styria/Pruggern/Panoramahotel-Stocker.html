Description

This traditional family-run hotel is set in the scenic Dachstein-Tauern region in Austria.

Panoramahotel Stocker includes an infra-red cabin and a sauna. There is children's playground with trampoline, climbing tower and slide as well as a petting zoo with goats, bunnies and the hotel cat.

Each of the rooms at Panoramahotel Stocker includes satellite TV and a balcony overlooking the peaceful scenery.

The rooms also have a private bathroom with a bath or shower. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout.

From 31 May to 31 October the summer card for the area is included in the room rate.

This gives you free or discounted access to a variety of amenities. Panoramahotel Stocker is just over a 10-minute walk to Pruggern.

The Dachstein-Tauern ski resort is 10 km from the guest house. Schladming is less than a 20-minute drive away and numerous hiking trails start nearby.

The pension offers ski storage and sells ski passes on site. There is a ski bus which stops directly outside and there are several ski trails and ski slopes within 2 km.
