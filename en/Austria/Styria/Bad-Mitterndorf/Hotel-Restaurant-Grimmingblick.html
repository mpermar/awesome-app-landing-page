Description

Located in the centre of Bad Mitterndorf in the Salzkammergut, Hotel Grimmingblick features a large spa area with indoor and outdoor pools.

Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas. The spa area includes a hot tub, a Finnish sauna, and various steam baths.

A wide range of massages, beauty and spa treatments is available. The Grimmingblick’s rooms offer cable TV, a safe, and a bathroom with bathrobes and a hairdryer.

The restaurant serves Austrian and international cuisine. It features a winter garden and a terrace with mountain views.

Guests with children will appreciate the children’s pool, the playground, and the professional child care in the summer months.

The playroom features a PlayStation. In summer, guided hikes and Nordic walking tours are offered.
