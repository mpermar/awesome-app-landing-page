Description

Family-run for almost 60 years, Hotel Kogler is located in the centre of Bad Mitterndorf.

It has a restaurant and a large spa area with an indoor pool. The ski bus to the Tauplitz Ski Area stops a 3-minute walk away.

Spa facilities at Hotel Kogler include a hot tub, sauna, sun bed, infrared cabin and relaxation room.

The spa area can be used free of charge throughout the day. Bathrobes are available at the reception.

In the morning, a rich breakfast buffet with an organic corner and juice and tea bars is served.

The restaurant serves Styrian and international cuisine and a wide range of Austrian and international wines.

Barbecue evenings and themed evenings, such as a farmers buffet, are hosted regularly.

Hotel Kogler also features a sun terrace with mountain views, a garden with a big sunbathing lawn, a tennis court (clay) and a mountain bike rental.

The immediate vicinity offers ideal conditions for hiking, mountain biking, fishing, climbing, skiing and cross-country skiing amidst the mountain landscape of the Styrian Salzkammergut.
