Description

Surrounded by the scenic Niedere Tauern mountain range, JUFA Hotel Oberwölz is located on the main square of the small town of Oberwölz in Styria.

It features a café, a children’s playroom, and historic basement vaults. Free WiFi is available throughout.

A sauna and a ski storage room are also available. Guests can play table tennis and rent bicycles.

Free parking is available at the Oberwölz JUFA. The Lachtal Ski Area is a 20-minute drive away.
