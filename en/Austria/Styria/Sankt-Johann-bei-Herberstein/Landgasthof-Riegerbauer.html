Description

Dating from 1652, this historic country hotel in St. Johann is 2 km from Herberstein Castle and Zoo, and 5 km from Lake Stubenberg.

Overlooking the Feistritz Valley, it features a restaurant serving traditional Styrian cuisine.

Free Wi-Fi is available. The suites at Landgasthof Riegerbauer are very spacious and feature a living room, a bedroom with a plasma HD TV, and a large bathroom with a massage steam shower.

Guests of the Riegerbauer Landgasthof can rent bicycles and Nordic walking equipment.

The garden includes a children’s playground. Free private parking is available on site.

Cycling and hiking trails start right outside, and a tennis court is 500 metres away.
