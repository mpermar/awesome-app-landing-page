Description

Completely renovated in 2016, Hotel Vier Jahreszeiten Loipersdorf is located right next to the entrance of the Loipersdorf Thermal Spa in Eastern Styria.

Admission to the spa is included throughout the entire stay, from the day of arrival until the day of departure.

Garage parking is also included in the price. Bathrobes and bathing towels are provided in the spacious and bright rooms, which include a flat-screen satellite TV and a minibar.

The Vier Jahreszeiten Hotel is also connected to the thermal spa through an underground passage.

A private relaxation area is available for hotel guests in the thermal spa. Breakfast includes many organic and regional products and is available until 11:00.

Dinner can be served on request. Participation in the sports programme is included in the rate.
