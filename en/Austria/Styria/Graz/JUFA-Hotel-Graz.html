Description

JUFA Hotel Graz enjoys a quiet location in a park, a 10-minute walk from Graz Main Train Station and a 20-minute walk from the city centre.

Free WiFi is available in all areas. The air-conditioned rooms come with a private bathroom.

Wheelchair-accessible rooms are available on request. For children, indoor and outdoor play areas are available, as well as various board and card games and tabletop football.

The City Adventure Center, the largest climbing hall in Central Europe, is located in an adjacent building and can be reached directly from the hotel.

It features an outdoor climbing area and a skywalk 13 metres above the ground. 3 bus lines stop a 5-minute walk away and take guests directly to the city centre.
