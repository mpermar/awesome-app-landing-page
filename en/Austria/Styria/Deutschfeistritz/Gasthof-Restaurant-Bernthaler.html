Description

Gasthof - Restaurant Bernthaler is within 2 km of Deutschfeistritz town centre, tennis courts, a riding club and a supermarket.

Peggau is 4 km away, and lakes, public pools and Graz are between 10 and 20 km away.

Free WiFi is featured. Rooms at the Bernthaler have a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom.

The property has an à la carte restaurant with a terrace, a playground and table tennis facilities.
