Description

Located in the scenic village of Schöder, 10 km from Murau, Gasthof Hirschenwirt features a traditional restaurant with a sun terrace.

The spa area can be used free of charge, and free horse riding for children is offered twice per stay.

Spa facilities include a sauna, infrared cabin, children’s sauna, relaxation room, and various showers.

Guided hikes to the Hirschenwirt’s own mountain pasture are offered in summer. A flying field for model aircraft is on site, and guests can bring their own aircraft along.

The restaurant offers Styrian cuisine, game dishes, and Austrian wines. Between May and October, guests can catch a trout in the on-site fish pond and have it prepared in the restaurant.

The rustically decorated rooms feature wooden furniture and floors, a TV, and a bathroom.

Some have a balcony. Free parking is available on site, and hiking and mountain bike trails start at the doorstep.

Cross-country ski runs are 200 metres away, and the Kreischberg Ski Area is 18 km away.
