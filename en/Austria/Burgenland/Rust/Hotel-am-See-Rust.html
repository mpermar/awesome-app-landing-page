Description

Just 100 metres from the shore of Lake Neusiedl, Familiengästehaus Seebad Rust offers direct access to the public beach and the Seebad leisure area featuring a 6,000 m² lawn, an adventure pool for children, and a mini golf course.

A restaurant and free WiFi are available. The rooms and apartments come with a flat-screen satellite TV and a bathroom.

Apartments also include a kitchenette. The Familiengästehaus features a children’s playground, a bicycle rental, and a bicycle storage room.

Free private parking is available on site. The centre of Rust is 2 km away. Further amenities include a boat rental, a volleyball court, a trampoline and a ping pong table.

Popular on-site activities include surfing, sailing and swimming. The Mörbisch Seefestspiele and the Festspiele St.

Margarethen can both be reached within 10 km. On request, the property can organise a boat shuttle service to the festivals.

The St. Margarethen Family Park is situated 5 km from the property, while the Eszterhazy Castle in Eisenstadt is 12 km away.

From late March to late October, the Neusiedler See Card is included in the rate.
