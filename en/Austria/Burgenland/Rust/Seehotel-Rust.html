Description

Enjoying a quiet and scenic location between the historic Old Town of Rust and Lake Neusiedl, Seehotel Rust offers a restaurant and a spa area right by the water.

The property has a private beachfront and guests receive a free welcome drink. The restaurant serves regional and international cuisine, as well as fine Burgenland wines.

You can also enjoy your meals on the Mediterranean terrace. A bar and a café are also available.

The comfortable rooms overlook the lake or the old town. Some have a balcony. The minibar is restocked once per day and all non-alcoholic beverages are for free.

Seehotel Rust offers a spacious health and spa area with panoramic views of Lake Neusiedl, and an indoor pool with direct access to the private sunbathing area on the lakeshore.

The hotel also features a Finnish sauna with an outdoor area, a steam bath, an infrared cabin, a big relaxation area, and a fitness room with lake view.

2 indoor tennis courts and a indoor golf court are also available, and bicycles can be rented on site.

The Family Park St. Margarethen is located 3 km from the property, while the Esterházy Palace in Eisenstadt is 15 km away.
