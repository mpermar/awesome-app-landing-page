Description

Vila Vita Pannonia is a 200-hectare nature resort set amidst the impressive landscape of Lake Neusiedl National Park in Burgenland.

It offers air-conditioned rooms with free internet access, and a wide range of sports and spa facilities.

Leisure options include bicycles for guests’ use, a riding stable, 3 outdoor and 2 indoor tennis courts, and a swimming lake with a sandy beach.

Football, beach volleyball, archery, and miniature golf are also possible. Vila Vita Pannonia features an extensive spa area with indoor and outdoor pools, several saunas, and a fitness centre.

Various massages and beauty treatments are also available. Austrian and international dishes are served in the 2 restaurants.

Wine tastings take place in the wine cellar. There is also a cigar lounge. Free private parking is available on site.

Vila Vita Pannonia is 85 km from Vienna and 29 km from the A4 motorway. The nearest beach in Illmitz is 13 km away, and Frauenkirchen Basilica is 16 km from the property.
