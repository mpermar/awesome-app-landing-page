Description

This family-run hotel in the centre of Bad Tatzmannsdorf is located 350 metres from the Avita Thermal Bath, where guests receive a 20% discount on admission.

The property has its own pastry shop. Each room comes with a balcony and a private bathroom with a bath or a shower, a hairdryer and bathrobes.

Extras include a sofa and an outdoor seating area. Half-board includes breakfast and a 3-course dinner, with a choice among 3 main courses.

At Hotel & Konditorei Simon you will find a garden and a terrace. Other facilities offered at the property include a shared lounge, a games room and a tour desk.

An official hiking and cycling path starts 350 metres away. The property offers free parking.
