Description

Peacefully located within 700 metres from the centre of Bad Tatzmannsdorf, this guest house offers free bicycle rental, table tennis and a children's playground.

Free Wi-Fi is available in the entire property. The rooms at Martinihof feature parquet floors, flat-screen cable TV, work desk and bathroom with bathtub or shower.

Guests can start their day in the bright and modern breakfast room with a rich buffet breakfast.

Within 300 metres from the Martinihof there is a restaurant. A cosy reading room is available as well.

Guests benefit from a 20% reduction at the Avita Thermal spa resort, which is 1 km away.

Free parking is offered on site of the property. Oberwart is 6 km from the Martinihof, and A2 Motorway can be accessed in 13 km.
