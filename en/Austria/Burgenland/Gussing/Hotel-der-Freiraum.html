Description

All rooms in the hotel are equipped with a electric tea pot. Rooms are equipped with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV, and selected rooms have a garden view. Guest rooms feature a desk.

Guests at Hotel der Freiraum can enjoy a continental or a buffet breakfast.

Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Güssing, like cycling and hiking.

Güssing Castle is 1.6 miles from Hotel der Freiraum. Thalerhof Airport is 42 miles from the property.
