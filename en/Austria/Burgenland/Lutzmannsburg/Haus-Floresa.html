Description

Offering a 1. 800 m² large garden, Haus Floresa is located in Lutzmannsburg, featruing apartments with a terrace, a roof-top terrace or a garden pavilion.

The Lutzmannsburg Thermal Spa is a 5-minute walk away. Free private parking is available on site.

The accommodation has a dining area and seating area with a flat-screen TV. There is also a kitchen, fitted with a microwave, toaster and fridge.

A stovetop and coffee machine are also featured. There is a private bathroom with a bath or shower in each unit.

Bed linen and towels are offered. Free use of bicycles is available at the property and the area is popular for golfing and cycling.
