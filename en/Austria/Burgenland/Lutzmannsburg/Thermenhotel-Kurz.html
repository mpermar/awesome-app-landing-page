Description

Offering direct underground access to the Sonnentherme Spa Centre in Lutzmannsburg, this 4-star hotel features 5 outdoor and 3 indoor tennis courts.

4 bowling lanes and a large indoor playground are also available. The spacious and elegant rooms at Thermenhotel Kurz feature cable TV, an electric kettle, a refrigerator, and a bathroom with hairdryer.

Free child care is offered in the afternoon and evenings. The spa area includes a Finnish sauna, a hot tub, and a steam bath.

Bathrobes are provided free of charge. A wide range of massage treatments is available at Hotel Kurz.

Guests can rent bicycles and inline skates, play football and table tennis, and take tennis lessons.
