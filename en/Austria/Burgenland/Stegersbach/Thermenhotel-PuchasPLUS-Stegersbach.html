Description

Located in Stegersbach in Southern Burgenland, Thermenhotel PuchasPLUS Stegersbach is directly connected to the Stegersbach Thermal Spa which comprises 14 pools, various saunas and relaxation rooms.

It offers free access to the spa, a wide range of massages and beauty treatments.

Free WiFi is featured throughout the property. The allergy-free rooms at the PuchasPLUS feature Swiss stone pine furniture, a balcony or a terrace, a flat-screen TV, a refrigerator, and a bathroom with a separate toilet.

A bathrobe and a sauna towel per adult is provided. The romantic suites furthermore feature romantic decoration and an open bathroom.

Free wired internet access is available in all rooms. The varied breakfast buffet includes a large selection of healthy, regional and organic products.

Vegetarian and vegan dishes are available as well. The breakfast can be enjoyed in the winter garden or on the garden terrace, and the meals of the half-board arrangement are served at the on-site restaurant or in one of the restaurants nearby, some of which can be reached on foot.

All restaurants are situated within a 7 km radius. On the day of arrival, the Stegersbach Thermal Spa can be used as of 12:00.

For stays longer than 2 nights, guests can use the spa until 16:00 on the day of departure, otherwise until check-out.

Free private parking is available on site while garage parking is possible at an additional cost.

Guests can enjoy e-bike rental for a surcharge. The Stegersbach - Lafnitztal golf area is located directly next to the hotel and comprises 2 18-hole golf courses and 1 9-hole golf course.

A green fee discount is available. Cycling, running and hiking paths are accessibly directly from the hotel, and bikes can be rented on site.

The centre of Stegersbach is 2. 5 km away and an outlet centre can be reached in a few minutes by car.

The A2 motorway is a 15-minute drive away, and Graz can be reached by car in 1 hour.
