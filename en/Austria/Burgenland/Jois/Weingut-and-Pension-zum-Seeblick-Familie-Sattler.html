Description

Surrounded by vineyards, the 3-star superior Weingut & Pension zum Seeblick Familie Sattler is located in Jois, a 30-minute drive from Vienna.

It offers a terrace with panoramic views of the surrounding landscape, air conditioning, buffet breakfast and free WiFi.

Wine tasting in the private wine cellar and vineyard tours are provided on request.

Free private parking and bicycle rental are possible at the property. Free shuttle service from the train station is offered on request.

You can enjoy a relaxing massage at the pension for a surcharge. Weingut & Pension zum Seeblick has a large garden with a sun terrace, a children’s playground and table tennis facilities.

The accommodation units are non-smoking and feature wooden furnishings, a wardrobe, a work desk and satellite TV.

The private bathrooms come with a shower and free toiletries. Restaurants and wine taverns are close to the property.

A train station can be reached within a 10-minute walk. Guests receive a 10% discount on normal rates in selected stores at the Designer Outlet in Parndorf, a 9-minute drive away, and a 20% discount on the admission at St.

Martins Spa, a 30-minute drive from the guest house. From mid-March to October, the Neusiedler See Card is included in the rate.
