Description

Offering a restaurant and access to a public beach, Seehotel Herlinde enjoys a convenient location at Lake Neusiedl in Podersdorf.

The hotel offers rooms with a balcony and lateral lake views and a spa area comprising a sauna and a sun bed.

Relaxing massages can be requested. The rooms at Herlinde Hotel are fitted with a flat-screen cable TV and a minibar.

Each room comes with a private bathroom featuring a hairdryer, free toiletries and a shower or a bathtub.

A breakfast is served daily. Free WiFi is available in public areas. An on-site games room ensures the children’s entertainment, while a playground is 50 metres away and guests can also make use of watersports facilities at the property.

Snacks and drinks are served at the bar and during summer, guests can also dine on a terrace.

The property offers free parking and a lockable bicycle storage room. A surfing school is located next to the premises.

A supermarket is 2 km away and Podersdorf centre with shops and bars is within 300 metres.

The St. Martins Thermal Baths are 8 km away and guests can reach Ilmitz and Neusiedl within 15 minutes by car.
