Description

Landhotel Birkenhof is located in the traditional wine-growing village of Gols in the Burgenland, close by the Lake Neusiedl-Seewinkel National Park and many cycling trails.

The restaurant serves traditional seasonal dishes and a selection of fine Austrian wines.

The Birkenhof features a sauna and a steam bath, as well as a large garden and a terrace.

Parking spaces, bicycles and Nordic walking poles are provided free of charge at Landhotel Birkenhof.

Pick-up from Gols Train Station is available on request free of charge. The Parndorf Designer Outlet Centre, as well as the St.

Martins Therme thermal bath in Frauenkirchen are a 15-minute drive away. Mörbisch can be reached by ferry from Illmitz, 20 km away.

The Neusiedler See Card is included in the rate. This card offers many free benefits and discounts in the region.
