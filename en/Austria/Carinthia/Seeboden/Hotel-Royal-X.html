Description

Hotel Royal X is located right on the shore of Lake Millstatt in Seeboden and offers an indoor pool, indoor and outdoor tennis courts, a sauna, and a surfing and sailing school on site.

Each of the modern rooms at Hotel Royal X comes with free WiFi, satellite TV and a refrigerator.

Typical Carinthian specialities and international cuisine are offered in the buffet-style restaurant.

Additional facilities on site include a volleyball court and a climbing wall. Free WiFi and internet workstations are available as well.
