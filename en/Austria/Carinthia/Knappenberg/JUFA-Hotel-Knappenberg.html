Description

Overlooking the Noric Alps, JUFA Hotel Knappenberg is located 100 metres from the centre of Knappenberg and offers a spa area spread over 2 floors, a restaurant, a café with a sun terrace, and business facilites.

Free WiFi is available. All rooms feature a flat-screen TV with cable channels, wooden floors, and a private bathroom with shower and hairdryer.

Some of the rooms comprise a sofa. Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant and the café with a terrace providing mountain views.

Breakfast is served every morning at JUFA Knappenberg. A grocery shop is 3 km from the property.

JUFA Knappenberg also features a children's playground and games room. Further amenities are vending machines for snacks and beverages, as well as table tennis facilities.

Free private parking is provided. The Klippitztörl Ski Area is 20 km from the property.

The Freibad Klein St. Paul public outdoor pool can be found 14 km from the hotel.
