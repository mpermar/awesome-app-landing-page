Description

All rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area and a TV. The private bathroom is equipped with a shower, free toiletries and a hair dryer. The rooms will provide guests with a desk and a coffee machine.

A buffet breakfast is available every morning at Das kleine Berghotel. There's an in-house restaurant, which specializes in international and European cuisine.

The accommodation has a playground. You can play ping pong at Das kleine Berghotel, and the area is popular for skiing.

Speaking French and German, staff are always on hand to help at the 24-hour front desk.

San Candido is 25 miles from the hotel, while Dobbiaco is 28 miles from the property.
