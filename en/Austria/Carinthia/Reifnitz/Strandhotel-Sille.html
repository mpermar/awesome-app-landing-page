Description

Strandhotel Sille is located right at the shore of Lake Wörth in the village of Reifnitz and comprises a private beach area, a well-known restaurant, a bar, shops, a seminar centre and a spa area with a fitness room.

Massages are offered on site. Free WiFi is available in all rooms of the hotel. The individually furnished and bright rooms and suites offer views of Lake Wörth and feature a balcony, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a safe, a desk and a sofa.

A wide range of international and Carinthian cuisine as well as home-made pastries can be enjoyed at the Seerestaurant which is open daily from 11:00 to 22:00 (September-April 11:00 to 21:00).

It furthermore features a terrace offering scenic views of the lake. A water sports centre is right next to Strandhotel Sille and offers a water ski and boat rental.

A sailing school and tennis courts can be found in Reifnitz. The Strandhotel provides private parking free of charge.

The city of Klagenfurt can be reached in a 10-minute drive, while Klagenfurt Airport is a 20-minute drive away.
