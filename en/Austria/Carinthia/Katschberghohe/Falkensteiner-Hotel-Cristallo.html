Description

Set in a scenic mountain landscape at 1,650 metres above sea level in the Katschberghöhe Ski Area, this elegant 4-star superior hotel offers panoramic mountain views, the large Acquapura spa area, modern fitness rooms, and free WiFi access in all areas.

Guests enjoy direct access to ski slopes and hiking trails. The Falky Land children's area includes indoor play facilties, a children's spa with a family pool, waterslide, baby pool, children's sauna and steam bath, and an outdoor playground with various attractions.

Professional child care for children from 3 to 12 years of age is also available.

In the restaurant, guests can enjoy Austrian and international cuisine, as well as Carinthian specialties.
