Description

Familienhotel Hinteregger is located at 1,650 metres above sea level in Rennweg and the Katschberg skiing and hiking area starts directly on the doorstep.

WiFi access and parking are available free of charge. In winter breakfast, a skiers’ snack, a 5-course dinner with a choice of meals, and a sports programme for children are included.

In summer, the offer includes, breakfast, for lunch soup, main dish, coffee and sweets and a soft drink dispenser.

The restaurant offers a wide selection of Austrian and international dishes and fine wines.

A traditional mountain hut with sun terrace is only a 3-minute walk from the hotel.

It serves Carinthian and Italian cuisine. The 1,500 m² spa area offers panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.

It features a large indoor pool, a modern gym, and many spa facilities. Massages, beauty treatments and Ayurveda are also available.

Bathrobes are provided. A ski storage room is at guests' disposal. Furthermore, guests can book ski school classes on site and purchase ski passes at the hotel.

For children Hotel Hinteregger offers several playrooms, a cinema, a disco with karaoke, and a varied programme of activities.

Professional baby and child care is available. Familienhotel Hinteregger is only a 5-minute drive away from the A10 motorway.
