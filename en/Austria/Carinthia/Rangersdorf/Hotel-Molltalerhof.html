Description

Located in the small village of Lainach in the Möll Valley, in a quiet setting off the main road, the family-run Hotel Mölltalerhof offers a sauna with mountain views, a garden, a herbal garden, and free WiFi.

The restaurant serves classic Carinthian cuisine. Guests of the Mölltalerhof can relax in the lobby and enjoy sunbathing on the terrace.

Parking is available free of charge. In summer, the free usage of bicycles is offered, while in winter, toboggans can be rented on site.

A ski storage room and a sun bed are also provided. During summer, activities include hiking and cycling.

Guided tours can also be organised upon request. Lienz is 20 km away, and Heiligenblut is 27 km away.
