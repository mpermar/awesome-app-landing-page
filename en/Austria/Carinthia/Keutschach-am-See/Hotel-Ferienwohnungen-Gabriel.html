Description

Located on the western shore of Lake Keutschach in Southern Carinthia, Hotel Ferienwohnungen Gabriel features a private beach only 200 metres away.

The Gabriel features a bar and a sun terrace with a fountain. An internet terminal is available at the reception.

Guests can play table tennis and rent bicycles to explore the surroundings. There is a lockable bicycle and motorcycle garage.
