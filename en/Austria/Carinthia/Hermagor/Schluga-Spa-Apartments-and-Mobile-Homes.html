Description

Schluga Spa Apartments & Mobile Homes is located in the village of Obervellach, 1,500 metres from the centre of Hermagor and 4 km from Lake Pressegg.

The Nassfeld ski area is 8 km away and free ski bus stop is in front of the property.

Free private parking is possible on site. The spacious apartments and mobile homes have a kitchenette, satellite TV, and a bathroom.

Wi-Fi is available in all areas for free. The Schluga Spa Apartments & Mobile Homes restaurant serves international and Mediterranean cuisine, as well as Austrian specialities.

In winter, a spa with an indoor pool and 4 saunas is available for an extra charge.

In summer, guests can also enjoy a heated outdoor pool and rent bicycles. Tennis courts are located nearby.
