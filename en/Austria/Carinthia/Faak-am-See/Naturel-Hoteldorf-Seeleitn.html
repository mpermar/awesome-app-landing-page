Description

Located directly on the shore of Lake Faak, Naturel Hoteldorf Seeleitn features its own lake access.

The spacious apartments are located in traditional Carinthian farmhouses. Each apartment at the Seeleitn has a living room with satellite TV, a safe, and a kitchen with a dining area.

The Dorfwirt restaurant serves Carinthian and Austrian cuisine and specialties from the Alpe Adria Region.

Breakfast or half-board is available on request. Half-board includes a rich breakfast buffet and a dinner buffet or 3-course dinner.

Juices, water, coffee, table wine and draught beer are included at dinner. Professional care is available for children from 3 years of age.

Free parking is available on site. There is a large sunbathing area and many water sports opportunities at the lake.

The surrounding area, including the Karawanken Mountains, provides a wide range of sports and leisure options.

Dorf Seeleitn is only open from May to September. Free pick-up is offered from the train station at Faak am See.
