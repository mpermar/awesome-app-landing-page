Description

Surrounded by a 7,000 m² garden with a heated outdoor pool, Forellenhof Waldner offers apartments with a balcony or terrace in Gundersheim in the Gail Valley.

Wi-Fi and parking are free of charge. The Nassfeld Ski Area is 15 km away. Aparthotel Waldner consists of 3 buildings and features a sauna and a sun bed.

Bicycles can be rented. In winter, free ski storage is available on site and a ski lift can be used in Nassfeld.

Aparthotel Waldner provides a breakfast buffet on request and at an additional cost.

Ski passes can be bought on site for discounted prices. The free ski bus for Nassfeld departs right in front of Aparthotel Forellenhof Waldner.

Lake Pressegg is about 25 km away. From mid-June to mid-September, the Nassfeld Card is included in the rate.
