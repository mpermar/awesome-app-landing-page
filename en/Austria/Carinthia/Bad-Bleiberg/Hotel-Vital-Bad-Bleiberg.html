Description

Surrounded by greenery, this lovingly Hotel Vital Bad Bleiberg is located at 920 metres above sea level, only a 3-minute walk from Bad Bleiberg’s thermal spa.

Half-board is available upon request. Spa facilities at Hotel Vital Bad Bleiberg include a wood-fired sauna, an infrared cabin, a relaxation oasis with solarium, a Kneipp track and massages.

For smaller guests, a playground, an Indian wigwam and a sandpit are available in the natural garden.

Hotel Vital Bad Bleiberg also has an illuminated curling lane and a bowling lane.

The swimming beach at Lake Ossiach can be used free of charge. Guided hikes are organised on a regular basis.
