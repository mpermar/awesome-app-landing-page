Description

Situated a 5-minute drive from Techelsberg and a 15-minute walk from the shore of Lake Wörth, Hotel Restaurant Thadeushof offers accommodation units with free WiFi.

An outdoor pool is provided for the guests’ refreshment and Velden’s centre is 7 km away.

The units come with wooden furniture and feature a flat-screen satellite TV and a bathroom with a hairdryer.

Some come with a sofa, a sofa bed, or a balcony, and the apartments have a fully equipped kitchenette.

Guests can enjoy drinks at the on-site bar and Thadeushof also features a restaurant serving Austrian cuisine.

Each day starts with a large buffet breakfast. A bowling alley is a further feature of the property and a playground ensures the children’s entertainment.

Free private parking is possible on site and Pörtschach is 3 km away. Guests of Hotel Restaurant Thadeushof receive the Wörthersee Card free of charge.

This card includes free admission to the lidos and many sights, as well as free or reduced boat rides on Carinthia’s lakes.
