Description

The Werzer’s Seehotel Wallerwirt with its own beach and bathing jetty is located between Pörtschach and Velden right at the Wörthersee Lake, facing the Karawanken mountain range.

The newly renovated rooms have a lake-view balcony and the beds feature high-quality mattresses.

Free Wi-Fi is available. Our exquisite breakfast buffet and a gourmet 4-course set menu is served in the winter garden and on the lakeside terrace.

The hotel's bathing platform includes loungers and parasols. Guests can also relax at the spa which features a sauna, a sun bed, a steam bath, as well as an infrared cabin and an ice grotto.
