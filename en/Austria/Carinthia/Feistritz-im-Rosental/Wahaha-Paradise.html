Description

The Wahaha Paradise is located right at the Drau River, set against the backdrop of the Karawanken Mountains.

It offers various outdoor and indoor leisure facilities and a 500 m² spa area with a natural swimming pond.

The Drauradweg (bicycle trail) leads right through the resort. The Fun & Sports Hall features a 14-m high climbing wall with climbing routes in various difficulties, as well as a boulder wall, a state-of-the-art fitness centre, and an indoor playground.

Outdoor facilities at the Wahaha Paradise include a beach volleyball and a mini football court, miniature golf, a cross-country running trail, and an outdoor pool.

In summer, bicycles can be rented, and guided hiking tours are offered. The spa area includes a panoramic sauna, sanarium, steam bath, Physiotherm cabin and infrared sauna, a relaxation room, indoor access to the swimming pond, and a selection of massages.

The property also offers free of charge sport classes like yoga, spinning or black roll training.

In summer, the panoramic restaurant Fanguan serves seasonal specialities from the region.

Local products can be bought in the shop at the reception. All rooms, apartments and chalets at the Wahaha Paradise feature a kitchenette, cable TV, and a bathroom.

Parking and WIFI are available free of charge. Guests also enjoy direct access to Lake Wörthersee at the partner property Weisses Rössl und Apartments, 21 km away.

Discounts are available for certain attraction such as the Minimundus or the Tscheppa Gorge.

In winter, guests receive reduced tickets for the Gerlitzen Ski Area, 25 km away, and for the Kärnten Therme (thermal spa) in Villach, 28 km away.
