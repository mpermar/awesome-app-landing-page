Description

Featuring free WiFi and a spa center, Familienhotel Post offers accommodations in Millstatt. The hotel has an outdoor pool and hot tub, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar. Free private parking is available on site.

The rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Some rooms include a seating area where you can relax. Every room has a private bathroom.

You will find babysitting service at the property.

This hotel has a private beach area and bike hire is available. You can play table tennis at the hotel. The nearest airport is Klagenfurt Airport, 37 mi from Familienhotel Post.
