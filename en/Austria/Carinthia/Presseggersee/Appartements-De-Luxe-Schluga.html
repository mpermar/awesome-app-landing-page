Description

Appartements De Luxe Schluga offers apartments with a loggia directly on Lake Pressegg in Carinthia’s Gail Valley.

It has a private beach and offers free Wi-Fi. All Schluga Appartements have a flat-screen satellite TV, a fully equipped kitchen, separate bedrooms, and a south-facing balcony or a terrace with views of Lake Pressegg.

A sauna with panoramic views of the lake and the Carnic Alps, an infrared cabin and a relaxation room are located on the third floor.

Furthermore 4 km away you can find an indoor swimming pool with various saunas for a very low surcharge.

A 30,000 m² private beach is right at the Schluga Apartments’ doorstep. It features an artificial, 5-metre-high iceberg, a trampoline, paddleboats and canoes, and a bar.

Bicycle and hiking trails start right at the De Luxe Appartements Schluga. Guests can take part in the activities offered by the camping ground (200 metres away) free of charge.

The Nassfeld ski area is only a short drive away from Appartements De Luxe Schluga.

The free ski bus stops only 150 metres away. Schluga Appartements offers free covered parking spaces.
