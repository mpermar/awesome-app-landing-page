Description

Only a few steps away from the shore of Lake Pressegg, Hotel Kärntnerhof & SeePark Appartements offers rooms with balconies and views of the lake and the Karawanken Mountains.

WiFi is available in the entire property and on the Hotel´s beach. Spa facilities at this 3-star superior hotel include a steam bath, a Finnish sauna, an infrared cabin, a solarium, and a hot tub.

Massages are also available. The Hotel Kärntnerhof & SeePark Appartements serves traditional Carinthian cuisine, fine wines, and a rich breakfast buffet.

Baby monitors and baby equipment are available, and babysitting can be organised on request.

Guests enjoy free admission to the beach. Deck chairs are available free of charge.

Hotel Kärntnerhof provides bicycles for guests’ use. A heated ski storage room is available.

Private parking is available free of charge. In summer, guests get free access to the Kärntner Adventure Park and Pool.
