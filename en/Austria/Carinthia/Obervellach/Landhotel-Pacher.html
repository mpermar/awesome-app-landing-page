Description

Located in the centre of Obervellach in the Möll Valley, Landhotel Pacher offers a spa area with a Finnish sauna, an infrared cabin, and a steam bath.

The restaurant has a sun terrace and serves traditional Carinthian cuisine. Free WiFi is available.

The rooms overlook the Hohe Tauern Mountains and feature wooden furniture, satellite TV, a seating area, and a bathroom with a hairdryer.

Guests of the Pacher Landhotel can relax in the large garden. In summer, bicycles and electric bicycles are provided free of charge.

Free private parking is available on site. Public indoor and outdoor pools, a tennis court, and a cross-country ski run are 300 metres away.

Rafting and canyoning are possible in the Möll River, 500 metres away. The Ankogel Ski Lift is 13 km away, and it is 15 km to the Mölltal Glacier.

A free ski and hiking bus stops 100 metres away. Mallnitz and the Hohe Tauern National Park are 10 km away.
