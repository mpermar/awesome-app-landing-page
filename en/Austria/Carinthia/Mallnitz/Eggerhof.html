Description

Surrounded by the Hohe Tauern National Park, Eggerhof provides rooms with free Wi-Fi in the centre of Mallnitz.

All guests can make use of the wellness area with a sauna and a steam bath. A free ski bus to the Mallnitz Ski Area, which is 3 km away, stops in front of the guest house.

All rooms come with a sofa bed, a seating area, cable TV and a bathroom with a shower.

Eggerhof has a bar and a restaurant. A breakfast buffet is served in the morning and half board is available on request.

Half board is free for children up to the age of 4 and discounts apply for children up to the age of 14.

Guests can relax in the garden or on the terrace. A ski storage room with a ski boot dryer is provided and ski passes can be bought on site.

Bikes can be rent at the guest house. For the children’s entertainment, a trampoline and table tennis facilities are available.

A toboggan run is 3 km away from Eggerhof. The Molltal Glacier is 24 km away, and Lake Millstatt can be reached within a 35-minute drive.

Free private parking is possible on site. From early May to late October, the Nationalpark Kärnten Card is included in the rate with a minimum stay of 2 nights.
