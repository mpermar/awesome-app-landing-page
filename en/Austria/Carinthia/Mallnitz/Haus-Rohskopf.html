Description

Located in the Ankogel ski area, Mallnitz’ Haus Rohskopf has a sauna, a garden, terrace, a small children's playground, a playroom, barbecue facilities, a bar and a ski storage room.

The nearest ski slope can be reached by car in 7 minutes. Rooms at the Rohskopf have a balcony with mountain views, free Wi-Fi, satellite TV and a bathroom.

Additionally, apartments have a kitchen. A bus stop is 50 metres away and cross country ski trail can be accessed within 200 metres.

Mallnitz train station is 1 km away, and toboggan trails are 3 km away. Millstättersee Lake can be reached by car in under 40 minutes.
