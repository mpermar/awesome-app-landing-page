Description

Located 100 metres from the shore of Lake Millstatt in Döbriach, All Inclusive Hotel Zanker offers a wellness area featuring a swimming pool, saunas, a steam bath, and an outdoor relaxation area.

In winter, a free ski bus takes you to Bad Kleinkirchheim, 15 km away. The individually decorated rooms come with a balcony, a flat-screen TV with digital satellite channels, and a bathroom.

The hotel's all-inclusive offer features soft juices, wine, beer, tea and coffee from 8:00 to 23:30, different alcoholic drinks from 16:00 to 23:30, an afternoon snack from 16:00 to 17:00, a buffet breakfast, and a 4-course dinners with a menu of your choice.

The restaurant serves traditionally prepared specialities as well as international dishes.

A themed buffet and live music are offered once a week. All Inclusive Hotel Zanker offers free access to the public Parkbad bathing area in Döbriach.

There you can relax on a 20,000 m² sunbathing area at the 200-metre long sandy lakeshore.

The Kärnten Top Ski Pass is valid for over 30 ski areas in Carinthia and East Tyrol.
