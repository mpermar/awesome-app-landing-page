Description

The Hotel Sonnenalpe Nassfeld is located at 1500 metres above sea level right at the ski slope, next to the lifts.

It offers free childcare and a 1700 m² spa area with indoor and outdoor pools. Parking is available on site free of charge.

Garage parking is available at an additional cost. All rooms in the Falkensteiner Sonnenalpe Nassfeld Hotel are furnished in modern Alpine style and feature a private bathroom, a cable TV and a balcony.

You will find a bottle of mineral water in your room on arrival. Apart from the pools, spa facilities includes various saunas, a relaxation room and a terrace.

All spa facilities are accessible for free. Kids can have fun in the play area and dine in the children's restaurant.

Half board includes a rich buffet breakfast, a snack buffet for lunch and a dinner with a choice of set menus.

Ski schools and sports shops, winter hiking paths and cross-country tracks are located in the immediate vicinity of the Sonnenalpe Nassfeld.

You can also take part in guided hikes and enjoy various themed evenings. In summer guests receive the+Card holiday Nassfeld which offers many free benefits and discounts in the region.

Also in summer all guests receive unlimited free Greenfee for the golf course Nassfeld Golf which is only 15 km away.
