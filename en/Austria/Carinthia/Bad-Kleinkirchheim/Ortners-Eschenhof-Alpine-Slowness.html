Description

Located in Bad Kleinkirchheim’s village centre, 100 metres from the ski lifts, Ortners Eschenhof - Alpine Slowness offers an outdoor heated pool and a spa centre with a steam bath and an indoor pool.

The St. Kathrein Thermal Spa is a 3-minute walk away. This family-run 4-star hotel offers bright, non-smoking rooms and many amenities for families with small children.

These include family tables in the restaurant, baby phones, a kid's club with child care for children from 3 to 12 years, and a children’s playroom.

Guests of Ortners Eschenhof benefit from a massage service, beauty treatments, various baths, and a big sunbathing lawn.

Tennis courts, mountain bikes, e-bikes and guided hiking tours in the Nockberge Mountains are also available.

The hotel offers a private shuttle bus to a ski school and a private ski hut, where guests can enjoy a soup free of charge.

The Kärnten Card is included from the middle of April until the end of October and allows free admission or discounts at several local attractions.
