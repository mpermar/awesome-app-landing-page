Description

Situated in the heart of Bad Kleinkirchheim, Family & Sporthotel Kärntnerhof offers an extensive spa area with an indoor pool and massages, as well as a large children’s play area.

A ski slope and a ski school are located next to the hotel, and the thermal spa is only 50 metres away.

The elegantly furnished rooms all have free access to WiFi, a seating area and private bathrooms with bathrobes and toiletries.

Each room has a balcony and is equipped with cable TV and a minibar. Family & Sporthotel Kärntnerhof’s spa area boasts an indoor pool, a hot tub, various saunas and steam baths.

Various massages are offered on request and children can also access the sauna. Tea and fresh fruit are offered the entire day in the spa area.

The on-site restaurant serves healthy, regional dishes and Mediterranean specialities.

A rich buffet breakfast, a light lunch, coffee and cake in the afternoon and a 5-course dinner are offered.

A 300 m² children's play area with games and many other features is located in the hotel.

There is also a 5,000 m² outdoor area. Professional child care is offered 6 days a week.

Family & Sporthotel Kärntnerhof features an extensive sports programme. 3 outdoor tennis courts and curling courts are available on site.

From 07 March to 29 March 2020, a ski pass is included, also offering a 50% discount on the entry to the Römerbad thermal baths.

From May to October, the Kärnten Card is included. This card offers many free benefits and discounts all across Carinthia.
