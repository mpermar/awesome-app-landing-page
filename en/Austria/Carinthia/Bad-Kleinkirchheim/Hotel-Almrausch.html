Description

Located in the centre of Bad Kleinkirchheim, Hotel Almrausch is 250 metres from the Sonnwiesenbahn Ski Lift and 500 metres from the Sankt Kathrein Thermal Spa.

The spa area features themed relaxation rooms, an outdoor pool and a fitness room.

Free WiFi is available in all areas. Exclusive cuisine with 5-course dinners and a choice of dishes, gala dinners, and traditional evenings with live music can be enjoyed.

On request, special diet menus are available, including gluten-free, lactose-free and vegetarian cuisine.

For younger guests, a children’s playroom is available at Hotel Almrausch. A hut in the ski area, featuring a restaurant open in winter, also belongs to the hotel.

A golf course is 5 km away. The surrounding area of the Nockberge Mountains is ideally suited for hiking, Nordic walking, and mountain biking.
