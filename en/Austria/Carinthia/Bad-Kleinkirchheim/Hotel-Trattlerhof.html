Description

Hotel Trattlerhof enjoys a scenic location on the edge of the forest, close to the centre of Bad Kleinkirchheim.

Many hiking and biking trails pass by the hotel, and in winter the hotel is only 130 metres from the lifts to the Bad Kleinkirchheim and St.

Oswald Ski Areas. It offers a restaurant and an indoor pool. The rooms of the Trattlerhof are furnished in traditional Austrian style.

Some feature a balcony offering panoramic mountain views. Spa facilities include an indoor pool, 2 saunas, a hot tub, and a steam bath.

The Trattlerhof's restaurant serves traditional cuisine as well as creative specialities made of only the freshest local produce, and also offers a wide choice of wines.

Wine tasting is held once a week. During winter guests can enjoy the properties own Aprés ski bar.

The Trattlerhof also offers reduced green fees for golfers during summer. The hotel offers various adventures.

Children can make pizza in a special cooking class or play on a pony farm. E-Cars can be test-driven there.

Guests staying between mid May and end of October receive the Sonnenschein Card and the Kärnten Card for free.

Each year from 9 March until 31 March guests receive a free ski pass and a 50% discount on the entrance to a Thermal Bath.

In case guests buy an adult ski pass for 6 days between 5 January and 6 March 2020, this accommodation offers one ski pass for a child below 12 years for a discounted rate.
