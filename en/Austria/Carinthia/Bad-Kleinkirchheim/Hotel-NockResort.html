Description

Surrounded by a 6,000 m² garden, Hotel NockResort offers a scenic location in Bad Kleinkirchheim, only steps away from the ski slopes.

The modern spa area includes an indoor pool, hot tub, steam bath, and saunas with panoramic views.

The restaurant has won numerous awards by the Gault Millau guide. Half-board includes a breakfast buffet, a soup for lunch, a pie or cake in the afternoon, and dinner Soft drinks from the self-service bar are included.

The NockResort is only 100 metres away from the tennis centre and 300 metres from the village centre and the thermal spa.

In summer, hiking trails start right behind the hotel. The Bad Kleinkirchheim Card and the Kärnten Card are included in the rate in summer.
