Description

Pension & Appartement Steinwender enjoys a quiet location 900 metres from the Römerbad Thermal Spa in Bad Kleinkirchheim.

It offers traditionally furnished rooms and apartments with a balcony and free WiFi.

Each room and apartment is equipped with a seating area, sofa bed and cable TV. Toiletries and a hairdryer are provided in all bathrooms.

A kitchen with kitchenware is available in every apartment, and some also have a dishwasher.

Pension Steinwender features a spa area with a hot tub, a sauna and a steam bath.

The spa area is open 4 times a week and is free of charge for guests. Guests can relax in the garden at the natural pond or enjoy the sun on the terrace.

Barbecue facilities are at guests’ disposal. A children’s playground and a ping-pong table are also on site.

During winter, guests can rent ski equipment at the cable car, which is 900 metres away.

Ski passes can be bought on site, and a ski storage room is at guests’ disposal. A free ski shuttle bus stops directly in front of our house.

Free private parking is offered at our guesthouse. From 09 March to 31 March 2019, a ski pass is included and guests enjoy a 50% discount on admission to the thermal spa.
