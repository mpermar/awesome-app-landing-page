Description

Surrounded by a large park, the Prägant is located in the centre of Bad Kleinkirchheim, opposite the Römerbad Thermal Spa and the Kaiserburg Cable Car.

It features an indoor pool, a sauna area, and a spa and wellness centre. An outdoor pool is available in summer.

The rooms at Hotel Prägant have a balcony and offer views of the park and the Kaiserburg Mountain.

The 5,000 m² park features a pavilion and has many places to relax. A tennis centre and an 18-hole golf course are nearby.

During summer, you have free access to a public beach at nearby Lake Millstatt. The Regionscard is included in the rate, offering discounts on the Römerbad thermal bath, the golf course and the cable cars, as well as free participation in guided hiking and mountain bike tours, sailing and other activities in the area.

From late May to late October, the Kärnten Card can be bought at a 30% discount at the property.

This card offers many free benefits and discounts all across Carinthia. From 09 March to 31 March 2019, a free ski pass and a 50% discount on the admission fee for the thermal baths are included in the rate.
