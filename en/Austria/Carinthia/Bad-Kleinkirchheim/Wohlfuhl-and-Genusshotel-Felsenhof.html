Description

This family-run 4-star hotel in Bad Kleinkirchheim features an indoor pool, a heated outdoor pool, and a spa area on 4 levels.

All rooms have a balcony and cable TV. Free WiFi is available. Wohlfühl- & Genusshotel Felsenhof enjoys a sunny, south-facing location overlooking the Nockberge Mountains, 2 km from the centre.

The Kaiserburg golf course is only 2-minute drive away The Felsenhof’s spa area includes a Finnish sauna, an infrared sauna, and herbal and Roman steam baths.

A wide range of massage and exclusive treatments is available. Guests can also relax on a sun terrace overlooking the mountains.

Half-board, including an afternoon snack with cold and warm dishes and a 5-course dinner, can be enjoyed in the restaurant, which is exclusive for guests of the hotel and providing views of the surrounding mountains.

Guests can either park their cars free of charge in front of the hotel, or in the hotel's parking garage.
