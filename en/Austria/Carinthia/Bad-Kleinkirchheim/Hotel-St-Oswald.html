Description

Nestled amid the gentle landscape of the Nockberge mountains in Carinthia, Hotel St Oswald in Bad Kleinkirchheim at 1,400 metres above sea level offers you a spa area, fine cuisine and spacious rooms.

Bright and spacious rooms and suites with panoramic views of the surrounding mountains await you.

WiFi is provided throughout the building for free. You can enjoy healthy food all day long and visit the vinotheque offering an excellent choice of wines.

Hotel St. Oswald also features a lobby bar with open fireplace. A sun terrace, a spacious swimming and sauna area, a quiet garden with sunbathing area, child care, tennis courts, and a wide range of massage and beauty treatments make the hotel's offer complete.

From mid June till 10th July and from 19th August till 14th October the Kärnten Card is included in all rates for guests staying at least 5 nights.

This card comprises many free benefits and reductions, among them the use of the cable cars in Bad Kleinkirchheim.
