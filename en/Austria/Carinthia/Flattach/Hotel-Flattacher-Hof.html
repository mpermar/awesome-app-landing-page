Description

The Flattacher Hof is located in the centre of Flattach. It offers free use of its spa area and a free ski shuttle service to the Mölltal Glacier ski area, 8 km away.

Run by the same family since 1903, the hotel looks back at 500 years of history. The spacious spa area consists of 4 different saunas, an indoor pool with a sun terrace, a fitness centre, and a café.

Half-board includes a rich buffet breakfast, dinner with salad bar and a choice of main dishes, including a vegetarian option.
