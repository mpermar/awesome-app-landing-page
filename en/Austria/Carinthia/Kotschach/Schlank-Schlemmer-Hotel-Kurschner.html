Description

Surrounded by a large park with a children's playground, Schlank Schlemmer Hortel Kürschner is located in Kötschach-Mauthen in Carinthia’s Gail Valley.

It offers a spa area with an indoor pool, a seasonal outdoor pool, and free WiFi.

Hotel Kürschner's spa area offers relaxing massages, fragrant baths, and beauty treatments.

Features include a gym, several baths, a tea bar, and a sauna area with tepidariums, a hot tub, a steam bath, and a Finnish sauna.

In addition to regional and Italian dishes and wines, the hotel also serves special dishes for allergy-sufferers.

For younger guests, a children’s area and a weekly activity programme are available.

For parents, activities such as guided hikes and Nordic walking are available. The surroundings of the Schlank-Schlemmer Hotel Kürschner are great for hiking or golfing on the Gailtalgolf course.

In winter you can find 152 km of cross-country ski runs. Kötschach has a local family ski area, and the Nassfeld Ski Area is 20 km away.
