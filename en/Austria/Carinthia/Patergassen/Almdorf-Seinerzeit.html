Description

In a scenic location at 1,400 metres above sea level in the Nockberge Mountains in Carinthia, Almdorf Seinerzeit offers traditional, Alpine-style cottages.

Renovated in 2014, it features a restaurant, a village shop, a bath house with 2 saunas and panoramic mountain views, and 2 scenic ponds.

Free WiFi is available. All cottages combine classic Alpine style with modern amenities such as a digital TV and an iPod dock.

They feature a kitchen with a historic stove, a bathroom, a balcony, and a terrace.

The rustic-style restaurant has a terrace and serves traditional Carinthian dishes.

A wide range of fine wines is also available. On request, all meals can also be served to your mountain hut or chalet.

Various massages, as well as unique hay baths, are offered. Activities offered by the Almdorf Seinerzeit include guided hikes, cooking lessons, barbecue and campfire evenings, and, for children, fishing, rafting on the pond, and sleeping in hay.

Child care is available. Hiking trails start right at the doorstep. Bad Kleinkirchheim, featuring a ski area and 2 thermal spas, is 10 km away from Almdorf Seinerzeit.
