Description

Nestled on a crest between Lake Millstatt and Spittal an der Drau in the Naturschutzgebiet Egelsee Nature Reserve, 19 km from Bad Kleinkirchheim, Lug ins Land boasts a barbecue and children's playground.

Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant with a garden, which offers products from the owner's farm.

The rooms come with a TV. Lug ins Land features free WiFi throughout the property.

There is a tour desk at the property. You can play table tennis at the inn. Archery facilities (3D archery targets) are available on site and Lake Egel is a 10-minute walk away, while the Lug ins Land viewpoint is a 15-minute walk away.

Kneipp facilities are located in the surroundings. Bad Gastein is 49 km from Lug ins Land, while Villach is 29 km away.
