Description

The 3-star Hotel Platzer enjoys a quiet location at the entrance to the medieval Old Town of Gmünd in Carinthia.

It features a children’s playroom, a playground, and a spa area. The Platzer’s traditionally furnished, bright rooms provide satellite TV and a bathroom with hairdryer.

Some also have a balcony. The restaurant serves Carinthian and international cuisine, as well as special meals for children.

It features a bar and an outdoor dining area. A free internet terminal is available in the lobby.

The Katschberg and Innerkrems ski areas are within 20 km and can be reached with a free ski bus.

The Platzer Hotel features a ski rental and a ski storage room with a ski boot dryer.

In summer, guests have free access to Gmünd’s public outdoor pool. Lake Millstatt is just 15 km away, and Hotel Platzer organises guided hikes.

Free private parking is available at Hotel Platzer. The A10 motorway is less than 1 km away.
