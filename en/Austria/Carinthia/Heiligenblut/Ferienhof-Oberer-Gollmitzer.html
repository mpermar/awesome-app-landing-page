Description

Situated 1500 metres above the center of Heiligenblut Ferienhof Oberer Gollmitzer provides a garden, non-smoking rooms, free WiFi and balcony.

Boasting a children's playground, this property is located a short distance from Hallenbad, Schareckbahn and Tunnelbahn Fleissalm.

The inn has ski storage space. At the inn, each room is equipped with a desk. With a shared bathroom, some units at Ferienhof Oberer Gollmitzer also provide guests with a terrace.

The rooms feature a wardrobe. A continental breakfast is available each morning at the property.

A number of activities are offered in the area, such as skiing and hiking. The nearest airport is Salzburg W.
