Description

Gasthof-Hotel Winkler is located in the City of Mühldorf, a 10-minute walk from the centre.

It offers rooms with balcony and garden view. The Ski Area Goldegg can be reached within 20 minutes by car.

Free Wi-Fi is available. All rooms have flat-screen TV, telephone and a private bathroom.

Guests can use the hotel sauna free of charge. Solarium is available against charge.

The Gasthof-Hotel Winkler has a restaurant serving traditional Austrian cuisine. Spa baths Kathrein and Bad Kleinkirchheim can be reached within 40 minutes by car, Millstätter Lake in 20 minutes by car.
