Description

Situated in the centre of Weissbriach, Hotel Naggler features a garden with a pond and a sunbathing terrace, as well as a large spa and wellness area and free WiFi in public areas.

Authentic Carinthian cuisine is served in the restaurant. All rooms at the Naggler are spacious and decorated with warm colours.

Each features a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a private bathroom and a balcony with a view of the surroundings.

A tasty buffet breakfast made of fresh produce is served every morning in the on-site restaurant, also offering typical Carinthian specialities.

The Naggler features a Finish sauna, a steam bath and a fitness centre free of charge.

A hot tub and massage services are available on request. Bicycles and billiards are also available.

Hotel Naggler is just 500 metres from the Weissbriach Ski Area and the free ski bus to the Nassfeld Ski Area stops 50 metres away.
