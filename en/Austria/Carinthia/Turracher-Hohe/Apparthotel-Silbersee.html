Description

Apparthotel Silbersee is located in Turracher Höhe and offers ski-to-door access and free WiFi in all areas.

The property is 20 metres from the Wildkopflift Ski Lift and 100 metres from the Kornockbahn Cable Car.

Each apartment features a terrace with mountain views, a flat-screen satellite TV, a DVD player, a fully equipped kitchen, and a bathroom with shower, bathrobes, hairdryer and free toiletries.

Guests can relax in the sauna and in the hammam. Other facilities like a shared lounge, a games room and ski storage room are offered.

Free private parking is possible on site. Klagenfurt Airport is located 46 km from the accommodation.

From early June to mid-October, the Alm Butler Card is included in the rate. This card offers many benefits and discounts, including free guided hiking tours.
