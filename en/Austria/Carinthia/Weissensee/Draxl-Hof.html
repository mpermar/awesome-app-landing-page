Description

Located right at the shore of Lake Weissensee, Draxl-Hof offers a private beach with barbecue facilities, deck chairs and sun shades, as well as a sauna and a children's playroom.

Fishing boats and rowboats are provided free of charge. Weissensee Ski Area is 2.

5 km away. Each of the apartments comes with TV, a fully equipped kitchen and a bathroom with shower or bath and a toilet.

Some apartments have a balcony and some are wheelchair-accessible. A lift is available.

From May to October, guests of the Draxl-Hof can use bicycles free of charge and rent mountain bikes.

200 km of hiking trails and over 100 km of mountain bike trails are available in the surrounding area.

Fishing is possible on site and a diving school is a 15-minute walk away. In winter, a natural ice skating rink is in front of the property and a ski bus takes guests to Nassfeld Ski Area or Weissensee Ski Area.

A delivery service for fresh bread rolls is available upon request at a surcharge, while the next restaurant is a 5-minute walk from the Draxl-Hof and a supermarket is a 15-minute walk away.

Upon request and at a surcharge a shuttle service from the train station and the airport can be booked.
