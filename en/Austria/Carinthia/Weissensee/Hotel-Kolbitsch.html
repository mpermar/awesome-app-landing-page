Description

Hotel Kolbitsch is directly situated at the Weissensee Lake in Oberdorf. It offers rooms with lake views, a spa area, and free Wi-Fi in public areas.

All rooms have satellite TV and a hairdryer. Most have a balcony. Hotel Kolbitsch’s spa area includes various saunas, a solarium, and a winter garden with lake views.

Shiatsu massages and cosmetic treatments are offered as well. The Kolbitsch has a private lakeshore, a sunbathing lawn, and a sun terrace.
