Description

Surrounded by a 6,500 m² park, this elegant 19th-century villa on the shore of Lake Wörth features a private beach and a spa area.

All rooms offer lake views. The spacious and elegant rooms at Schlossvilla Miralago feature wooden floors and a bathroom with bathrobes, slippers and hairdryer.

Free Wi-Fi is available in the entire hotel. The restaurant serves Austrian and international cuisine.

In fine weather, guests can enjoy breakfast in the garden. A historic bath house and a boat dock can be found on the Miralago's beach.

Spa facilities include a sauna and an infrared cabin. Massage and beauty treatments are also offered.

Child care is available in July and August. Free private parking is provided on site.
