Description

The child-friendly Familien- & Wohlfühlhotel Elisabeth in Pörtschach offers rooms and apartments with balcony and views of Lake Wörth.

For children there are big indoor and outdoor play areas. Many activities such as pony riding and guided hikes, is offered at Hotel Elisabeth.

Babysitting can be organised on request. Pushchairs, baby equipment, and family games are provided free of charge.

Hotel Elisabeth features a spa area with sauna, jacuzzi, massage showers, infrared cabin, solarium, and relaxation room with juice bar.

There is also a fitness area. Bathrobes for adults and children are available. Massages and beauty treatments are also offered.

Klagenfurt, Velden and the A2 motorway are only a few minutes‘ drive away from Familien- & Wohlfühlhotel Elisabeth.

Parking is available free of charge. Guests enjoy free access to the Balance Spa area at the Hotel Balance which is only 250 metres away.
