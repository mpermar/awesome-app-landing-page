Description

Panoramic views of Lake Wörthersee are provided at Ferienhotel Wörthersee. It offers accommodation units with free WiFi, a wellness area with an indoor swimming pool, and a private beach.

The centre of Pörtschach is 1. 7 km away. All accommodation units provide satellite TV, a safety deposit box, and a bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

The wellness area comes with a Finnish sauna, a steam sauna, and an infrared cabin.

Different massages and spa treatments are available on request, and Ferienhotel Wörthersee also features a gym.

Guests can use the spa on arrival day from 12:00 and on departure day until 15:00.

Shower facilities and a hairdryer are at guests' disposal. Guests can enjoy a rich breakfast buffet including regional specialities in the breakfast room with a panoramic view and a sun terrace.

Bicycles can be rented on site and the Wörthersee hotel also offers nordic walking equipment.

Free private parking is possible on site and Velden can be reached within 8 minutes by car.

By bus it takes only 3 minutes to reach Pörtschach, the bus stop is only 400 metres away.
