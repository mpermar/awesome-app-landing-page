Description

Located in Maria Luggau in the Lesach Valley, just 400 metres from the basilica, Gasthof Luggau awaits guests with a restaurant serving Austrian cuisine and garden with a small pool, a playground, barbecue facilities and a sunbathing terrace.

Guests can relax in the on-site sauna and in the shared-use TV lounge. The rustic rooms at Luggau Gasthof all come with satellite TV and a bathroom with a bath.

Some rooms have a balcony. WiFi is available free of charge in public areas. Other facilities offered at the inn include a tour desk, a ski storage room, and a billiards table.

Free private parking is available on spot. The closest supermarket is within 300 metres.
