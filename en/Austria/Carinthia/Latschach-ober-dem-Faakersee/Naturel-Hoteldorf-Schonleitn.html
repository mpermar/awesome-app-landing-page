Description

The Naturel Hoteldorf Schönleitn enjoys a scenic location on a high plateau above Lake Faak.

Created as a hotel village, the apartments are located in carefully restored farmhouses.

There is also an indoor pool, an outdoor pool and the free ski bus stops right outside the property.

All apartments feature a living/dining room with a tiled stove, 1 or 2 bedrooms and a kitchen.

Bed linen, dish towels and towels, energy costs and the final cleaning are all included in the rates.

Free Wi-Fi is also available. Half-board is available at the Schönleitn. Traditional cuisine from the border area of Austria, Slovenia and Italy, with an emphasis on regional products, is served at the à-la-carte Dorfwirt restaurant.

At selected times, the ski bus to the Dreiländereck Ski Area and to the Gerlitzen Alpe Ski Area stops right outside the Schönleitn.

The idyllic Aichwald Lake can be reached via an easy hiking trail. Professional care for children of 8 months and up, family and sports programmes are available 6 days a week.
