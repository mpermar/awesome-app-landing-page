Description

Set amidst nature, 5 km from Lake Faak, Gasthof Martinihof offers a garden, pony riding and a restaurant serving home-made products.

Children can pet the animals at the property. Free WiFi is available. Each room offers satellite TV and a bathroom.

Some come with garden views, some with mountain views and there are also anti-allergenic rooms.

The apartments at Martinihof are equipped with a kitchen or kitchenette. Guests can enjoy local meals at the restaurant with terrace, that also offers a selection of home-made liquors.

A supermarket can be found 5 km away. The garden of the house features a children's playground and barbecue facilities.
