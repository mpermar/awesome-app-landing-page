Description

Surrounded by a 7,000 m² park with a private shallow beach, the Barry Memle is located right on the shore of Lake Wörth.

Guests can use the spa area including an indoor pool, a sauna, a sun bed, and an infrared cabin free of charge 24 hours a day.

The rooms at Barry Memle offer views to the resort’s park and some even feature lake views.

They all have satellite TV, electronic safes, a kitchen, and a bathroom with hairdryer and vanity mirrors.

Free WiFi is available all over the resort and in the rooms without password. The daily breakfast buffet is served until noon in the breakfast room overlooking the lake.

A free aqua park with boats and kayaks is offered directly on site, and standup paddling is also offered.

For children there is a playground and a play house. The centre and the casino of Velden are just a 5-minute walk away, and free private parking is possible on site.
