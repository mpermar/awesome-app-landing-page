Description

This property is a 12-minute walk from the beach. Hotel Schönblick - Schneider is located in Velden on Lake Wörthersee, 750 metres from the centre and 850 metres from the beach.

It offers a heated outdoor pool, a hot tub, and free bicycle hire. Free WiFi is available in public areas.

This 4-star hotel features spa facilities including a sauna, steam bath, and infrared cabin.

Guests can also enjoy a relaxing massage and use a fitness room. Carinthian specialities and Austrian and international dishes are served in the restaurant.

Barbecues and themed nights are also organised. Breakfast is served until 11:00. All rooms at the Schönblick - Schneider feature a balcony, a flat-screen satellite TV, a minibar, and a bathroom.

The Wörthersee Plus Card is included in the rate, and the Kärnten Card is available on site for a supplement.
