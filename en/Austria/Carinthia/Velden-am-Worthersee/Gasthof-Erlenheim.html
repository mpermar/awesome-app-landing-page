Description

Only 2. 5 km away from the centre of Velden and 300 metres from the lido, this cosy, family-run hotel enjoys a quiet location at the picturesque southern shore of Lake Wörth.

Gasthof Erlenheim offers a rich breakfast buffet and an à la carte restaurant with a beautiful garden with chestnut trees and a view of the lake.

Half-board is available on request. The spa area features a sauna, an aromatic steam bath, an infrared cabin, and a heated pool.

Many cycling and hiking trails are in the immediate vicinity. Lake Wörth, Austria’s warmest swimming lake, offers drinking water quality and options for water sports of every kind.

Free private parking is offered in front of the property, and garage parking is available at a surcharge.

Guests of Gasthof Erlenheim receive the Wörthersee Card free of charge. This card includes free admission to the lidos and many sights, as well as free or reduced boat rides on Carinthia’s lakes.
