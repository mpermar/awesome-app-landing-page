Description

Situated right at the banks of the Wörthersee lake, the family-run Seehotel Vinzenz features its own beach and an à-la-carte restaurant serving fresh fish from the lake.

All rooms have a balcony, a flat-screen TV and offer free Wi-Fi. From your room at Seehotel Vinzenz you can enjoy views of the lake or of the quiet garden behind the house.

All rooms have a private bathroom equipped with toiletries and a hairdryer. The à-la-carte restaurant offers a wide range of dishes prepared from organic products, fresh fish from the Wörthersee lake and Austrian wines.

You can also dine outside on the lake-view terrace. Half board can be booked on site.

It includes a 4-course set menu and a salad buffet. There is an inviting lounge room with a tiled stove and a selection of books.

In the garden you can find a tennis court, ping-pong tables and barbecue facilities.

Bike rental is available on site as well. The Kärntner Golf Club Dellach is 3 km away from Seehotel Vinzenz.
