Description

Surrounded by a private 30,000 m² park, the 4-star superior Seehotel Europa is only a few steps away from the shore of Lake Wörth and the centre of Velden.

It offers a private beach. The rooms offer panoramic views of the lake and the Karawanken Mountains.

The restaurant serves the Mediterranean cuisine as well as traditional Carinthian dishes.
