Description

This 4-star hotel is located in the Carinthian village of Möllbrücke, 10 km from Lake Millstatt and the A10 motorway.

The spacious rooms all have a balcony. The Landhotel’s elegant rooms feature a flat-screen satellite TV, a seating area, and a bathroom with bathrobes and hairdryer.

Spa facilities include a sauna, a steam bath, an infrared cabin, and a solarium. A wide range of massage treatments is available.

The Kreinerhof also features a large garden with a playground. A bus stop is right in front of the hotel, and free private parking is available at Landhotel Kreinerhof.

2 bicycle trails are right outside, and guests can rent bicycles to explore the scenic surroundings.

In winter, 5 ski areas, including Bad Kleinkirchheim and the Mölltal Glacier, can be reached in just a short drive.
