Description

The airy and air-conditioned villas features state-of-the art technology, modern furnishings and a fully equipped kitchen. A living room opens out to a private plunge pool, sundeck and garden.

Conveniently located opposite BRG Danang Golf Course, The Ocean Villas is 5-minute drive from Marble Mountain and a 15-minute drive from the UNESCO Heritage Site of Hoi An. Da Nang International Airport is a 20-minute drive away.

The on-site restaurant, White Caps Restaurant, serves a range of European and Vietnamese dishes.
