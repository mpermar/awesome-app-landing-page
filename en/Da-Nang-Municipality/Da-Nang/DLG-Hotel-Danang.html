Description

All rooms at the hotel come with a seating area and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Each room includes a private bathroom with a bath, free toiletries and a hair dryer. All guest rooms at DLG Hotel Danang feature air conditioning and a desk.

A buffet or American breakfast is served at the property.

The reception can provide advice on the area in order to help guests plan their day.
