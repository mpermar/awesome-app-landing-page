Description

At the hotel, the rooms come with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Rooms are equipped with air conditioning, and some units at Eco Green Boutique Hotel Da Nang have a balcony. All rooms will provide guests with a closet and a electric tea pot.

A buffet breakfast is served daily at the property. The in-house restaurant serves American and Asian cuisine.

The accommodation offers an indoor pool. The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental is available at Eco Green Boutique Hotel Da Nang.

A business center and vending machines with drinks are available on site at the hotel. Speaking Vietnamese, English, Japanese and Chinese, staff are ready to help around the clock at the reception.

Indochina Riverside Mall is 2.1 miles from Eco Green Boutique Hotel Da Nang. Da Nang International Airport is 3.1 miles away.
