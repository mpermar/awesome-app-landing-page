Description

Set in lush landscaped gardens, Holiday Inn Yanbu offers 2 outdoor pools and 2 restaurants.

It features free Wi-Fi in all areas. Yanbu Airport is 15 minutes by complimentary shuttle from Holiday Inn Yanbu.

Extras include a seating area and satellite channels. At Holiday Inn Yanbu you will find a fitness centre.
