Description

Mira Trio Boutique Hotel - part of Small Luxury Hotels of the World - is located at Al-Tahliah St, One of the most famous streets of Riyadh for shopping and entertainment.

Our Boutique Hotel has outdoor swimming pool, a state of art fitness Center, a Spa and an outdoor garden and water fountains.

Offering a spa and wellness centre and a restaurant, Mira Trio Hotel is located in Tahlia Street.

Free WiFi access is available. Each room here will provide you with air conditioning, a minibar and a seating area.

Complete with a refrigerator, the dining area also has an electric kettle and a dining table.

Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with a bath and a hairdryer. Extras include a sofa, a desk and a safety deposit box.

At Mira Trio Hotel you will find a fitness centre. Other facilities offered at the property include luggage storage, shops (on site) and a vending machine.

The property offers free parking. The hotel is 1. 5 km from Al Faisaliah Tower, 2.

3 km from Kingdom Center and 2. 7 km from Panorama Mall. King Khalid Airport is 29 km away.
