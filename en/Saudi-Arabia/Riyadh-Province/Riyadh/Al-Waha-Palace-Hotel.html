Description

With panoramic views of Riyadh, this 4-star hotel is located in the Olaya district and 1.

2 km from Riyadh Golf Courses. Al Waha Palace also offers free Wi-Fi and complimentary parking.

The stylish rooms are decorated in warm brown shades and come with carpeted floors.

Each has satellite TV and a marble bathroom with a bathtub. Guests can exercise in Al Waha’s fitness centre.

Al Waha Palace Hotel also features on-site shops including a souvenir shop. Al Waha Palace Hotel is situated just minutes away from Riyadh's business district and 30 minutes’ drive from King Khaled Airport.
