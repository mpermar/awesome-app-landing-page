Description

The 36 private villas at the resort have designer furnishings and contemporary artwork. Each one includes a spacious lounge with satellite flat-screen TV, dining area, an equipped kitchenette complete with minibar and microwave. The bathroom includes a shower and complimentary toiletries.

Guests can fully enjoy a workout at the fully equipped gym then head to the wellness spa for a relaxing massage. The Kids Club provides various activities for the younger guests.

Mövenpick Beach Resort Al Khobar is on Half Moon Bay Road in Aziziyah, on the Eastern border of Saudi Arabia. Damman International Airport is a 45-minute drive away and central Al Khobar is a 25-minute drive away.
