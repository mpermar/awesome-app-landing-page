Description

Located in Al Khobar, 4. 1 km from Al Khobar Corniche, Normas Hotel provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a fitness centre and a shared lounge.

This 4-star hotel offers a kids' club and a concierge service. The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and free WiFi.

Guest rooms are equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a microwave, a kettle, a shower, a hairdryer and a desk.

At the hotel each room is equipped with a wardrobe and a private bathroom. Guests at Normas Hotel can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

The accommodation offers 4-star accommodation with a hot tub and terrace. Normas Hotel features amenities such as an on-site business centre, indoor pool and sauna.

Al Rashid Mall is 5 km from the hotel, while Dhahran Expo is 13 km away. The nearest airport is King Fahd International Airport, 58 km from Normas Hotel.
