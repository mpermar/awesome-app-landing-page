Description

Ideally located in the Al Andalus district of Jeddah, Mövenpick Hotel Tahlia Jeddah is set 1.

6 km from Jeddah Mall, 7 km from Al Shallal Theme Park and 7 km from Jeddah Corniche.

Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a 24-hour front desk and room service, along with free WiFi.

The hotel has an outdoor swimming pool, fitness centre and a kids' club. At the hotel, all rooms include a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

All guest rooms at Mövenpick Hotel Tahlia Jeddah feature air conditioning and a wardrobe.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental or a buffet breakfast. Mövenpick Hotel Tahlia Jeddah offers 5-star accommodation with a hot tub and sauna.

King Fahad Fountain is 8 km from the hotel, while Fakieh Aquarium is 11 km from the property.

The nearest airport is King Abdulaziz International Airport, 16 km from Mövenpick Hotel Tahlia Jeddah.
