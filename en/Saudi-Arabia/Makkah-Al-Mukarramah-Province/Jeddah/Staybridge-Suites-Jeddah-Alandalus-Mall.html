Description

Located adjacent to Alandalus Mall, guests at Staybridge Suites – Jeddah can unwind in the state-of-the-art fitness centre or enjoy the outdoor pool, socialize and relax or pop next door to Alandalus Mall or Al Salam Mall.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. Each of the 164 studios and spacious serviced one or two bedroom apartments is equipped with a seating area with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Certain rooms include views of the pool or city. The rooms come with a private bathroom with a bath or shower and bidet, with bathrobes provided.

Extras include slippers and free toiletries. Guests can enjoy the outdoor pool, there is also a children’s pool for the younger guests.

Business guests can make use of the business centre and the meeting convention spaces.

Staybridge Suites - Jeddah Alandalus Mall is located just 20 minutes from King Abdul Aziz International Airport Jeddah and a 10-minute drive from downtown and Jeddah Sea Port.
