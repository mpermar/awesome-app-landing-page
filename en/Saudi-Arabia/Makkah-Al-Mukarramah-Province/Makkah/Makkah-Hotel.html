Description

Overlooking the Holy Haram Mosque and the Kaaba, the Makkah Hotel is set in the heart of Makkah, Saudi Arabia.

Pray with views over the Holy Haram in two air-conditioned and carpeted 10, 000-seat prayer halls.

All rooms at Makkah Hotel have city, mountain, Kaaba or Holy Haram Mosque views. Each offers a desk and a sofa.

The suite features a separate living room with dining area and the executive room gives access to the executive lounge.

EL Etlalah Restaurant, which boasts traditional arched windows, serves Asian dishes as well as regional specialities.

Savor Arabic flavors at Al Fayhaa restaurant or choose authentic international cuisine at Lagenda.

Relax over Arabian coffee at Jasmin Caffe. Guests can enjoy foot massages at the Reflexology Health Centre.

Experience the extensive onsite shopping mall with 450 brand shops and a food court.

Makkah attractions on the doorstep of the Makkah Hotel include the Kaaba and the ZamZam Well, with other important Islamic holy sites less than 5 miles away.
