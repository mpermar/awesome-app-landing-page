Description

The chalet-style rooms and suites offer bright and cozy living spaces. They all have a balcony, individual heating, TV and private bathrooms with bathtub and shower.

Les Grands Montets offers plenty of after-ski facilities including an indoor swimming pool, outdoor hot-tub, sauna, fitness room and steam room. Massages are also available upon request.

In winter warm up in the bar with a drink next to the fireplace, or play a game of pool. In summer, guests can enjoy the large terrace with lounge chairs facing Mont Blanc.

Wi-Fi internet access is available in the public areas of Les Grands Montets and affordable private parking is available on site. Throughout the year, ski lockers, shoe-dryers and multi-passes are available at the hotel.

Argentière is a great choice for travelers interested in backcountry skiing, tranquillity and snowboarding.
