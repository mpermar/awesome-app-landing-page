Description

All units come with a terrace, a kitchenette with a fridge, and a private bathroom.

The restaurant at the campground serves French and pizza cuisine. There is also a bar with a terrace.

At Village Huttopia Dieulefit guests are welcome to take advantage of a hot tub.

A garden and a children's playground are available at the accommodations. Family activities are included and available every weekend and every day during summer season.

Orange is 30 mi from Village Huttopia Dieulefit, while Valence is 29 mi away. The nearest airport is Avignon-Provence Airport, 45 mi from the campground.
