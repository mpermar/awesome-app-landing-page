Description

Guest rooms at the Grandes Rousses are all equipped with satellite TV and private bathrooms. Some rooms also have balconies and views of the mountains and resort.

Les Grandes Rousses has an on-site restaurant serving surf and turf cuisine, a bar and a wine-bar. All-you-can eat buffet is available for breakfast.

There is also free WiFi provided throughout the hotel. External valet parking is available on site for an additional charge and public underground parking is available nearby.
