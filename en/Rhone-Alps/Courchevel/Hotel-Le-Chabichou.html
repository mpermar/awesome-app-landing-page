Description

The rooms at Le Chabichou feature mountain and contemporary styles and come with a telephone, private bathroom and satellite TV.

Le Chabichou’s gourmet restaurant serves traditional French and regional cuisine. Guests can dine in the wood-paneled dining room or on the terrace.

There are a variety of beauty and massage treatments available. Guests have access to the swimming pool, steam room and sauna. Additional facilities include a hot tub, fitness room and sensory rain trail.
