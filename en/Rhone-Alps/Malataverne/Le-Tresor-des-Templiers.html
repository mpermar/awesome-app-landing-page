Description

At Le Trésor des Templiers, every room has a desk. All rooms at Le Trésor des Templiers include air conditioning and a closet.

A continental breakfast is served at the property. The restaurant at Le Trésor des Templiers specializes in French cuisine.

Le Trésor des Templiers has an outdoor pool. Golfing and cycling are among the activities that guests of Le Trésor des Templiers can find nearby.

Orange is 24 miles from The Treasure of the Knights Templar, while Montélimar is 5 miles away. The nearest airport is Caumont Airport, 40 miles from The Templar Treasury.
