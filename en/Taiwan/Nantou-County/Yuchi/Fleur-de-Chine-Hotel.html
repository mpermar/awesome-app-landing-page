Description

Fleur De Chine Hotel is a stylish and Muslim-friendly hotel located at the North Peninsula of Sun Moon Lake.

It offers spacious accommodation with a spa, an outdoor pool and free Wi-Fi. Guestrooms feature beautiful French windows and a private balcony offering views of the lake or the mountain.

Each room is equipped with tea/coffee making facilities and a flat-screen TV. The en suite bathroom has a natural hot spring bathtub.

Guests can enjoy massages and beauty treatments at Qi Shiseido Salon and Spa. Other recreational facilities include an indoor pool and a hydrotherapy hot spring area.

Eco-hiking trips can be arranged. Jade Luminous Restaurant serves a selection of Taiwanese, Cantonese and Szechuan dishes.

Muslim food is served as well. Drinks and a panoramic view of the Sun Moon Lake can be enjoyed at the Sky Lounge.

Hotel Fleur De Chine is a 1-hour drive from Taichung High Speed Railway Station and free parking is provided.
