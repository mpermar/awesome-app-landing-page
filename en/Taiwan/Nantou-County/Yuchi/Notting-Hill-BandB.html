Description

Featuring free WiFi and a sun terrace, Notting Hill B&B offers accommodation in Yuchi, 42 km from Taichung.

Free private parking is available on site. Each room at this bed and breakfast is air conditioned and is equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Enjoy a cup of tea from your terrace or balcony. Every room comes with a private bathroom.

For your comfort, you will find slippers, free toiletries and a hairdryer. There is free shuttle service at the property.

Alishan is 42 km from Notting Hill B&B, while Puli is 19 km from the property. Taichung Ching Chuan Kang Airport is 56 km away.
