Description

About a 2-minute walk from the beach, Howard Beach Resort Kenting is a hotel located a 15-minute drive from the Kenting Forest Recreation Area.

It offers an outdoor pool, 3 dining options and free parking. Air-conditioned guestrooms feature spacious interiors with modern funrihsings.

Each room is equipped with tea/coffee making facilities and a flat-screen TV with cable channels.

Kenting Howard has a well-equipped fitness centre and an outdoor pool with whirlpool.

Other recreational facilities include a games room. Free Wi-Fi is provided on site.

Champs Elysees offers an outdoor garden barbeque experience, coupled with a live jazz band.

Guests can enjoy coffee and beverages at Seven Scholars Lounge bar. A Chinese menu can be found at Formosa restaurant.

Howard Beach Resort Kenting is a 30-minute drive from Hengchun Old Town. Kaohsiung International Airport is a 2.
