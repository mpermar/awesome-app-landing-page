Description

Caesar Park Hotel Kenting is a 5-star resort offering modern accommodation, extensive services and facilities nearby the Hsiaowan.

Guest rooms feature marvellous sea and garden views. Each room is equipped with a flat-screen TV with free film channels, refrigerator, free WiFi access and free mineral water.

Villas with spa baths feature unique décor and private terraces. The resort’s Western and Chinese restaurants, Garden barbecue and Beach Bar serve outstanding dishes indoors or outdoors.

There is also a Recreation Center for children. Many activities and facilities are offered for a family-fun time.

Guests can also relax in the spacious palm tree gardens surrounding the outdoor swimming pool.

Alternatively, guests can spend the day at the beach with Kenting's provision of beach umbrellas, loungers, a beach activities zone, a paddling area, and the Hsiaowan Beach Bar.
