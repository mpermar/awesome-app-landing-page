Description

Located on Dawan seashore, Chateau Beach Resort Kenting is a lovely retreat where you can expect sandy beach with seashells, glorious sunshine and blue sea.

The setting offers beach access, rooms with private balconies, free WiFi and flat-screen cable TVs.

It features outdoor pools, a kid's club, a spa and several dining options. Rooms at the Chateau Beach Resort Kenting include a private bathroom and coffee/tea making facilities.

A safe, refrigerator and work desk are provided. All rooms have views of the mountain or ocean.

Guests can enjoy body treatments at the Azure Spa. The fitness room, which overlooks the beach, offers a place to exercise and relax.

The Aegean Restaurant serves a western-style breakfast buffet. At Mediterranean banquet, Chinese fare awaits you.

At the end of a day, you may find an extensive menu for drinks at The Barbados beach bar.

Both Ocean View and Matisse Cafe are good options to savour delicacies for an afternoon tea.

Chateau Beach Resort Kenting is a 5-minute walk from Kenting Main Street and Dawan Beach.

It is an 8-minute drive from Chuanfanshi (Sail Rock) and a 12-minute drive from Eluanbi Lighthouse.
