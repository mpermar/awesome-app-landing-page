Description

Boasting a fitness centre, a breakfast restaurant and meeting rooms. Some larger rooms have a swimming pool, spa facilities, sauna and steam room, Icloud Luxury Resort & Hotel offers accommodation in Xitun, Taichung.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. Icloud Luxury Resort & Hotel is a 6-minute drive from Fengjia Night Market, an 8-minute drive from National Taichung Theatre and a 15-minute drive from Calligraphy Greenway.

From the property, it takes 15 minutes to drive to Taiwan High Speed Rail - Taichung Station and 24 minutes to drive to Taichung Railway Station.

For guests' convenience, car hire and shuttle service are possible upon request. Featuring air conditioning, a hot bathtub and a seating area with sofa, each luxurious room comes with a flat-screen TV, a minibar and a refrigerator.

The private bathroom has shower facilities, free toiletries and hairdryer. Some units have a private heated pool.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can assist guests with free luggage storage, while computer zone is featured at the ground-floor business centre.

The conference room on the 2nd floor can host a meeting for 20 people. Guests can also visit Vanessa Beauty Salon on the 6th floor to unwind in the professional salon and spa service.

The Chinese and western breakfast is served in the buffet restaurant. The Skyline Lounge bar provides guests overlooking views of Taichung City and relaxing music.
