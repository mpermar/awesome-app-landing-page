Description

The air-conditioned and soundproof rooms feature a flat-screen TV with satellite and cable channels. A safe, minibar and tea/coffee-making facilities are provided. The marble bathroom includes a bathtub, hairdryer and free toiletries.

Taichung Airport is 3 miles from the property. Fullon Hotel Yamay is a 20-minute drive to Taichung Station and 25-minute drive from the center of Taichung.

Guests have access to the hotel’s fitness center. Younger guests will enjoy the children’s pool and playroom.

Chinese and Western dishes are served at the on-site restaurant. End the day with a drink from the Lobby Bar.
