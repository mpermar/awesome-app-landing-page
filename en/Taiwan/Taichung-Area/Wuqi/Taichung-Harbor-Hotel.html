Description

Taichung Harbor Hotel offers modern guestrooms with large windows and free Wi-Fi.

For recreation, guests can enjoy a massage, relax at the sauna or work out at the fitness centre.

Featuring carpeted flooring, all the air-conditioned rooms come with a flat-screen TV, DVD player and free drinking water.

Free daily newspapers are also provided. The en suite bathroom is equipped with a bathtub and free toiletries.

Taichung Harbor Hotel is a 10-minute drive from Gaomei Wetlands and Shalu Railway Station, and a 15-minute drive from Wu-Qi Tourism Fishery Harbour.

The famous Feng Jia Night Market is 30 minutes' drive away. Day trips can be arranged at the tour desk.

The hotel also provides laundry services, a business centre and meeting rooms. Luggage can be stored at the 24-hour front desk.
