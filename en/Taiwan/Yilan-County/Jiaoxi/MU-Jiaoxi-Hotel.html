Description

MU Jiaoxi is composed of 2 buildings, MU Jiaoxi Hotel and MU Jiao Xi Reserve, each building has its own check-in front desk and free parking area.

The property features an outdoor poo and hot springs. Free WiFi is available in all areas.

It is just 300 metres from Tangweigou Hot Spring Park and 500 metres from Jiaoxi Hot Springs Park.

Wufengchi Waterfall is 2. 7 km from MU Jiaoxi Hotel, while Longtan Lake Yilan is 5 km from the property.

The nearest airport is Taipei Songshan Airport, 34 km from the property. All rooms have a TV with satellite channels.

You will find a coffee machine in the room. Some rooms feature a balcony. The rooms come with a private bathroom equipped with a hot spring bath and a bidet toilet.

For your comfort, you will find slippers and free toiletries. The hotel has a children's playground and a fitness centre.

There is free shuttle service and gift shop at the property. MU TABLE, the all-day dining buffet, offers a variety of dishes.
