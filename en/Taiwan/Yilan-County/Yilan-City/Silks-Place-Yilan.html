Description

The luxurious Silks Place Yilan occupies floors 6-11 of Luna Plaza, one of the largest shopping centres in Eastern Taiwan.

Pampering spa treatments, both indoor and outdoor pools, as well as a modern gym are available.

Silks Place Yilan is a 10-minute walk from Yilan Railway Station and a 15-minute walk from Dongmen Night Market.

Taipei can be reached in 60 minutes’ car ride. It is a 2-hour drive from Taoyuan International Airport.

Elegantly decorated, the air-conditioned rooms are fitted with free wired internet and a 40-inch flat-screen TV.

Branded toiletries are provided for your convenience. Overlooking Yilan City, all rooms have a well-stocked minibar and a safe.

The themed guest rooms located on 8th and 9th floor are equipped with tent and a kid electric car.

Wellspring Spa is located within the Sky Garden on the hotel's 6th floor. It provides private treatment rooms where guests can soak in the hot tub or enjoy a massage.

Guests are entitled to unlimited film watching opportunities at LUNA digital cinemax.

The hotel also has a tour desk and concierge services. Red Lantern restaurant serves roasted duck and signature Chinese cuisines.

International buffet meals can be enjoyed at Mix Gourmet. Drinks are available at The Lounge.
