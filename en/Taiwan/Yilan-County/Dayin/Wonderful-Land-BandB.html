Description

Each unit comes with a sofa, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a well-fitted kitchenette and a private bathroom with hot tub. Some units feature a terrace and/or balcony.

A continental breakfast is served at the property.

At the homestay guests are welcome to use a hot tub.

A garden and a playground are featured at Wonderful Land B&B.

Taipei is 29 miles from the accommodation, while Yilan City is 6 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Taipei Songshan Airport, 29 miles from Wonderful Land B&B.
