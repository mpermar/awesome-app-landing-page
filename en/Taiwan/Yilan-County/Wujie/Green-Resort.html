Description

Situated 1. 4 km from Dongshan River Water Park, Green Resort provides accommodation with a shared lounge, a garden and room service for your convenience.

The homestay features both WiFi and private parking free of charge. Featuring a patio, each unit is air conditioned and features a dining area and a seating area with a cable flat-screen TV.

There is a private bathroom with bidet in all units, along with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

Green Resort offers a buffet or la carte breakfast. The accommodation offers a children's playground.

Both a bicycle rental service and a car rental service are available at Green Resort, while cycling and fishing can be enjoyed nearby.

National Center for Traditional Arts is 2. 2 km from the homestay, while Yilan County Government is 10 km from the property.
