Description

Rooms are decorated in soothing shades of pastel browns and fine beddings. Each has a DVD player and flat-screen TV with cable channels. A mini-bar and tea and coffee-making facilities are provided.

Hotel Royal-Nikko Taipei is a 15-minute drive from Songshan Airport, while Taoyuan International Airport is a 45-minute drive away. The hotel provides car rental facilities.

Guests can relax with a massage or enjoy a sauna session. The hotel's gift shop and in-house tailor offer beautiful souvenirs to bring back home.

Five restaurants await guests including Nakayama Japanese restaurant and Ming Court Cantonese restaurant. Sweet treats can be enjoyed at Royal Bakery while drinks are served at Royal Lounge.

Zhongshan District is a great choice for travelers interested in gourmet food, culture and food.
