Description

Spectacular views of the mountain and surrounding greenery can be enjoyed from Gloria Manor. The 4-star hotel provides fitness classes, as well as, free shuttle services for guests who would like to explore the region.

The rooms at Gloria Manor are elegant and well fitted with fine-quality furnishings and amenities. They have a 37-inch LCD TV, a Nespresso coffee maker and an iPod docking station. All rooms have a comfortable sitting area and a bathroom fitted with a bathtub.

The hotel is located a 1.5-hour drive from Kaohsiung Zuoying HSR Station and a 2-hour drive from Kaohsiung Train Station. Kaohsiung International Airport is a 2.5-hour drive away, and National Museum of Marine Biology and Aquarium is a 40-minute drive away.

The restaurant serves a selection of delicious dishes using fresh local produce. Guests can also relax in the lounge bar and enjoy coffee and desserts at the hotel. The hotel also provides a free shuttle service to Kenting Main Street, where guests can enjoy local foods at the local eateries.
