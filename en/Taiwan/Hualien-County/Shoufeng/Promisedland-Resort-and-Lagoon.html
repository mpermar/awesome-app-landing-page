Description

The 5-star Promisedland Resort offers luxurious accommodation with handcrafted bedding, a private balcony, and free Wi-Fi.

A 5-minute drive from Xofen Railway Station, it features a large outdoor pool and 2 restaurants.

Boasting spacious interiors, the air-conditioned room offers a flat-screen cable TV, ironing facilities and a minibar.

They also have a large seating area and a bathtub in the en suite bathroom. Located next to the National Dong Hwa University in Xofen Township, Promisedland Resort & Lagoon is a 30-minute drive from Hualien Railway Station and a 40-minute drive from Hualien Airport.

Taroko and Jade Mountain national parks are approximately a 1-hour drive away. Lira Restaurant serves Spanish delicacies, while Aroma Restaurant provides Chinese cuisine.

Guests can also enjoy refreshing beverages at the Chi-Lai Mountain Bar. Room Service is available.

The resort provides luggage storage services, as well as laundry and car rental facilities for guests’ convenience.
