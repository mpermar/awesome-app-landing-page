Description

Grand Cosmos Resort Ruisui Hualien features a restaurant, fitness centre, a bar and garden in Ruisui.

Featuring room service, this property also provides guests with a children's playground.

The resort provides an indoor pool, hot spring bath and a 24-hour front desk. Guest rooms are equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a fridge, a kettle, a bidet, a hairdryer and a desk.

With a private bathroom equipped with a shower and free toiletries, rooms at the resort also offer free WiFi.

At Grand Cosmos Resort Ruisui Hualien, each room is equipped with a seating area.

A continental breakfast is available daily at the accommodation. Spa and wellness facilities including a sauna and a hot tub are at guests' disposal during their stay at Grand Cosmos Resort Ruisui Hualien.

Guests can enjoy playing billiards and table tennis, or take advantage of the business centre.

Ruisui Farm is 5 km from the resort. The nearest airport is Hualien Airport, 72 km from Grand Cosmos Resort Ruisui Hualien.
