Description

Located in Kaohsiung, 300 metres from Lotus Pond and 3. 2 km from Rueifong Night Market, Kuo Old House provides accommodation with free WiFi, air conditioning, free bikes and a shared lounge.

All units come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and shower.

A fridge and kettle are also provided. The camping offers a children's playground.

Guests at Kuo Old House can enjoy hiking and cycling nearby, or make the most of the garden.

Kaohsiung Museum of Fine Arts is 5 km from the accommodation, while Liuhe Tourist Night Market is 8 km away.

The nearest airport is Kaohsiung International, 21 km from Kuo Old House, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
