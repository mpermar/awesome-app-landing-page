Description

Set in Kaohsiung, within 7 km of Liuhe Tourist Night Market and 8 km of Love Pier, Holiday Garden Hotel Taroko Park offers accommodation with a restaurant and free WiFi throughout the property as well as free private parking for guests who drive.

Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a children's playground.

The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, a concierge service and organising tours for guests.

At the hotel, all rooms are equipped with a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

Holiday Garden Hotel Taroko Park offers a continental or buffet breakfast. Kaohsiung Museum of History is 8 km from the accommodation, while The Pier-2 Art Centre is 9 km away.

The nearest airport is Kaohsiung International Airport, 3. 6 km from Holiday Garden Hotel Taroko Park.
