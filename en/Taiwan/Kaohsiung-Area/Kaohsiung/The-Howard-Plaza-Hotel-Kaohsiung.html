Description

The Howard Plaza Hotel Kaohsiung provides modern accommodation, a 10-minute walk from MRT Formosa Boulevard Station and Hsinshing Night Market.

It features an outdoor pool and spa bath. Free parking and free WiFi are provided.

Rooms are fitted with an electric kettle, minibar and tea bags. A safety deposit box and cable TV channels are provided.

En suite bathrooms come with toiletries and a bathtub. Howard Plaza Hotel is a 20-minute drive from Love River and Taiwan High Speed Rail - Zuoying Station.

Kaohsiung International Airport is a 30-minute drive away. Guests can exercise in the gym or head to the business centre to do work.

Laundry and dry cleaning services are available. Luggage storage is provided at the 24-hour front desk.

The on site restaurant, Pearl River, serves traditional Cantonese specialities while Shanghainese cuisine can be enjoyed at Yangtse River restaurant.
