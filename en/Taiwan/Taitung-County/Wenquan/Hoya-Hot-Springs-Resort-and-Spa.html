Description

Hoya Hot Springs Resort & Spa is located in Taitung, about a 10-minute drive from Zhiben Railway Station and Zhiben National Forest Recreation Area.

Guest can enjoy hot spring bath at the property, or use free bicycles to explore around.

Free WiFi access is available. It is a 8-minute drive from Hoya Hot Springs Resort & Spa to National Museum of Prehistory.

Taitung Airport is a 20-minute away, while it takes half an hour from the hotel to Taitung Railway Station.

Coming with a hot tub, each room here will provide air conditioning, carpeted flooring, a flat-screen TV and an electric kettle.

Soft bathrobes and free toiletries can be found at the private bathroom. At Hoya Hot Springs Resort & Spa you will find a 24-hour front desk.

Ticketing service is possible at the tour desk. Surcharged airport shuttle service is available as well.
