Description

Located close to Taitung City centre, Hotel de Trianon features a French-inspired décor and comfortable rooms with free bottled water, free toiletries and free WiFi.

Guests can enjoy hand-made French desserts at the property. Featuring views of the city, all air-conditioned rooms at Hotel de Trianon are equipped with cosy beds, refrigerator and electric kettle.

The private bathroom has shower facilities, free toiletries and hairdryer. Guests will find newspapers and magazines in the lobby area.

For guests' convenience, water dispenser, and free bike rental are provided. The hotel can also arrange shuttle service or car hire upon request.

Hotel de Trianon is a 13-minute drive from Taitung Railway Station, a 45-minute from Mr.

Brown Avenue and a 1-hour drive from Sanxiantai Scenic Area. It takes 15 minutes to drive to the property from Taitung Airport.

The hotel serves a buffet breakfast at the dining area. For other meals, there are some popular local food stands and restaurants located within a 10-minute drive of the property.
