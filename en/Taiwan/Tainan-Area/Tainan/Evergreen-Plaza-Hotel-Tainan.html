Description

Located just beside Tainan Municipal Cultural Center, Barclay Memorial Park and Durban Department Store, Evergreen Plaza Hotel - Tainan offers modern guestrooms with free WiFi access in Tainan.

It features an outdoor pool, a fitness centre and 4 dining options. The hotel is less than 10 minutes' drive away from Chimei Museum, Ten Drum Culture Village and Dadong Night Market.

It is a 10-minute drive from Tainan Airport and Tainan Railway Station. High Speed Rail Tainan Station is a 20-minute car journey away.

Each guest room here comes with a TV, a working desk and a mini refrigerator. The en suite bathroom has shower facilities and a bathtub.

A hairdryer and free toiletries are stocked in the bathroom. Parents can leave their children in the kid's club.

Guests can relax in the sauna after a long journey or a busy day. The banquet facilities, a 24-hour front desk and a tour desk are provided.

The Chinese restaurant serves a variety of local dishes. Other dining options are a buffet restaurant and a lobby bar, along with a Japanese restaurant featuring Teppanyaki and Japanese dishes.
