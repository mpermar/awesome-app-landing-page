Description

Featuring a seasonal outdoor swimming pool and various activities, Hotel Cham Cham offers accommodation in Tainan.

Free WiFi is available throughout the property. Guests can also use the on-site parking free of charge.

Hotel Cham Cham is a 1-hour drive from Taiwan High Speed Rail - Chiayi Station or Xinying Railway Station, while Zengwun Reservoir is a 10-minute drive away.

For guests’ convenience, hotel can arrange shuttle service based on request. The spacious guest rooms features air conditioning and a balcony with mountain or garden views.

Extras feature a wardrobe, electric kettle, refrigerator and flat-screen TV with cable channels.

The private bathroom comes with bath and shower facilities, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Here at Hotel Cham Cham, guests can join exciting activities such as rock climbing, zip line and bungee jumping.

Renting a bike to explore the neighbourhood or spending time in the garden are also good ideas for relaxing.

Guests will find a fancy DJ bar and games room at the property. For those who need to have a last minute catch-up, meeting and fax/photocopying facilities are available.

Hotel staff are glad to assist guests with luggage storage at the 24-hour front desk.

Extra features an in-house gift shop. Besides going to the DJ bar to grab a drink, Hotel Cham Cham provides poolside barbecue and a restaurant serving exotic cuisines.
