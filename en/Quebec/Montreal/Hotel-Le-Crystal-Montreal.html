Description

The contemporary guest rooms at Hotel Le Crystal Montreal are equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable and pay-per-view channels. The kitchenette offers a microwave, mini-bar, and espresso machine.

The on-site gym features yoga equipment and cardio machines overlooking the Montreal skyline. The hot tub is located on an open hardwood deck on the 12th floor, along with the Amerispa spa and sauna.

A restaurant featuring authentic Thai cuisine will open in June 2018.

The Montreal Museum of Contemporary Art and the Place des Arts Performing Arts Center are within a 20-minute walk of Hotel Le Crystal Montreal. Parc du Mont-Royal is 1.2 mi away.
