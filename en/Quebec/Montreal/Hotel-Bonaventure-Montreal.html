Description

A flat-screen cable TV, sitting area and work desk are provided in each room at this hotel. Rooms are also furnished with a minibar. Guests can enjoy air conditioning during the summer season.

Surrounded by 2.5 acres of beautiful gardens and streams, Montreal Bonaventure Hotel provides stunning views of the city from the year-round rooftop pool and terrace. The hotel also features several gourmet restaurants as well as spacious meeting and banquet facilities.

Guests can easily discover numerous festivals, art galleries, museums and world-renowned restaurants only steps from the hotel's front doors.
