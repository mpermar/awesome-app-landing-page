Description

Hotel Cap-aux-Pierres features an indoor and outdoor pool as well as an outdoor mini-golf course. Adults can play tennis or volleyball, while kids have fun in the game room. After a day of fun, guests can relax with a massage in the full-service spa.

The restaurant at the Cap-aux-Pierres Hotel serves regional dishes made with seasonal ingredients from nearby farms. Serving breakfast and dinner, the restaurant all offers evening entertainment, such as dancing.
