07/30/2017

Great hotel, beautiful beach and perfect location

"The highlight of this hotel for us was the pool and platform beach area. The pool was lovely and warm with large graduated stone steps in which made it perfect for children or those with mobility issues. It had comfortable loungers and was well tended by pool and bar staff. The platform beach area beneath was simply perfect, and located in the most beautiful bay meaning amazing views and a sheltered area for bathing in the sea. Our room was nice, nothing special but was clean and comfortable. Our balcony was nearly as large as our room and had amazing views of the bay. So was lovely to sit out with a drink on the balcony in the evening and watch the sun go down. Breakfast was great but dinner was a little limited. However the views from the dining area at night made it a very special place to have dinner and I would recommend booking one of the tables overlooking the bay if booking dinner. The hotels location was great, about a 2 minute walk to the funicular up to Toarmina where great spots for dinner, drinking and shopping are located and also about a 10 minute walk to the famous Isola Bella."
