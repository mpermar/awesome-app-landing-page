Description

Case di Latomie is in Castelvetrano, in the province of Trapani. The historic cities of Segesta-Calatafimi, Terme Segestane and Erice are a short drive away.

Every large and comfortable room at the Latomie features a terrace with immediate access to the swimming pool.

The restaurant serves traditional Sicilian dishes.
