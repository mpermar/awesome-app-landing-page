Description

Air conditioning and a minibar come standard in Hotel Punta Nord Est’s rooms. Most rooms offer views of the Tyrrhenian Sea from the window or balcony.

A cove beach can be reached down stone steps from the hotel. There is also a larger sandy beach with deck chairs and sun umbrellas for rent.

A buffet breakfast, including freshly baked cake and pastries, is served in a peaceful courtyard.

The staff is on hand to book trips in the local area. Castellammare del Golfo Harbour is a 10-minute walk away, and a shuttle can be arranged to/from Palermo Punta Raisi Airport, 29 miles away.
