Description

The rooms at the Signum are decorated in a traditional Aeolian style with iron beds and whitewashed walls. Each comes with air conditioning and a minibar, and some feature views of the sea and Panarea Island.

The Malfa Signum’s restaurant serves local cuisine, including homemade pasta and fish specialties. Both lunch and dinner can be enjoyed on the terrace, surrounded by citrus and jasmine trees.

The infinity pool at Hotel Signum looks out over the Salina coastline. Guests can also enjoy a herbal treatment in an antique copper bath, or catch up with email for free in the hotel lobby.
