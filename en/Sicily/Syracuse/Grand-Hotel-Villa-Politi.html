Description

Grand Hotel Villa Politi is a truly original establishment, rising on an ancient cliff. The hotel occupies 3 floors and has many original architectural features. From the windows you can enjoy a fairytale view of the Mediterranean Sea.

Guests at the Villa Politi can enjoy special rates at an adjacent sea-front sun terrace, set on the rocks with umbrellas and deck chairs, and available from mid-June until mid-September.
