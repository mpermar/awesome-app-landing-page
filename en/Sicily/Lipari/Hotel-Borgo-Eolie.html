Description

The air conditioned rooms include a flat-screen TV, a minibar and a bathroom with hairdryer. They also have a private terrace furnished with wicker armchairs or sofas.

The hotel features a flowery yard and furnished terraces. The buffet breakfast can be enjoyed in the breakfast room or out on the terraces.

Upon request and at fixed schedule, Borgo Eolie can provide a free shuttle service to/from Lipari habor and the center of town. Bicycles, scooters and cars can be rented on site.

Guests enjoy discounted rates at nearby beaches. The staff can also assist in organizing excursions in and around the archipelago.
