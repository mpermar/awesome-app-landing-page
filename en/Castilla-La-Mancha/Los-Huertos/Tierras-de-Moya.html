Description

The Tierras de Moya is located in a restored 18th-century mansion to recover its original splendor. Here you will find everything you need to make your stay a relaxing and stimulating experience.

The rooms of Tierra de Moya are spacious and retain many of the original elements of the building, such as the beautiful wooden beams of the ceiling. Very bright, the rooms have excellent equipment.

Free internet connection available throughout the hotel. You can relax in the spa and in the garden pool.

If you prefer, you can also discover the beautiful surroundings by renting a quad or riding a horse.
