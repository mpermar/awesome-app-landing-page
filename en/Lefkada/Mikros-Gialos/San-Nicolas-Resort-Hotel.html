Experiences

08/28/2017

The hotel was lovely.

"The hotel was lovely. Very attractive with a clean and large swimming pool. Staff were friendly and professional. The children were made to feel welcome. The breakfast was very nice. Well situated for access into the village which has some excellent restaurants. Great beach though the sun loungers were pretty busy when we were there."

07/11/2017

Truly beautiful resort

"We loved San Nicolas resort. Once we arrived and got into our room we were over the moon with our suite with private pool which overlooked the entire ocean. We straight away headed to the private beach and OH MY it was breathtaking. The water was so clean, the lounges and umbrellas perfectly placed on the rock platforms, there were also several other sitting and relaxing areas. The beach bar (which we made such good use of) had delicious food and fabulous cocktails. We were there for 7 nights and took full advantage of all the facilities. Our kids loved paddle boarding and when they had enough of the beach they headed to the huge pool."
