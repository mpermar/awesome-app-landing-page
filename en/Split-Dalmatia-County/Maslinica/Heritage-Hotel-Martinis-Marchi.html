Description

Every suite at the Heritage Hotel Martinis Marchi has beautiful bay views. The furniture was carefully selected from various European cities and skilfully restored.

Le Château Restaurant serves traditional Dalmatian fish specialties accompanied with fine red wines form the Hotel Martinis Marchi's own wine cellar. Guests can also dine on the sun terrace with occasional live music.

Heritage Hotel is located on over an acre of grounds and features its own heliport. Boat and taxi transportation is offered to Split Airport and nearby islands.

Hotel Marchi's stone façade conceals 2 saunas, a spacious lounge area and a relaxation room with a piano. You can use the heated pool or relax in the large yard.
