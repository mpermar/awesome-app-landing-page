Description

Decorated according to the latest trends in interior design and equipped with modern and luxurious furniture, the hotel is open throughout the year. It is a perfect choice no matter if you are on vacation or a business trip. You can also engage in the training of sports groups.

There are 2 conference rooms and nearby you can visit the sports center with tennis courts, a running track, a swimming pool and a basketball court.

Sample a wide range of traditional Dalmatian and international dishes in a pleasant and elegant atmosphere. Upon request, vegetarian and other specific meals can be prepared.

After a relaxing swim in the sea, sip your favorite drink on the balcony overlooking the sea, and watch the sunset behind Brač Island.

Tradition combined with modern décor and friendly, dedicated staff will make your vacation unforgettable.
