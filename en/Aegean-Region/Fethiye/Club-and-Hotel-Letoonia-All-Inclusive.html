Description

The extensive complex of Club & Hotel Letoonia is located on a wonderful, green peninsula in Fethiye, at a picturesque bay along the Aegean coast. With 3 outdoors and 1 indoor pools, many on-site sports facilities and an entertainment program for the whole family, you will have a fun and relaxing vacation.

With 3 stylish à la carte restaurants and 3 buffet restaurants, you can choose a different cuisine with every meal, at a surcharge. All food and snacks are available as well as all soda drinks, beer, wine and fresh cocktails for an additional fee. At 17:00 you can even break with tea and cakes.

In addition to including all meals and drinks in the rates, this resort also offers many activities and lessons for free. Why not take some windsurfing, sailing or tennis lessons - free of charge. You will also have free entrance to the fitness center.

A desk dedicated to tours is available at Club & Hotel Letoonia. Paragliding is very popular in this area. Babadag where you can do paragliding is only 19 mi from the property. Letoonia's traditional spa center, classically furnished with marble, is also accessible at a surcharge and offers ultimate relaxation. Unwind in the hammam or choose to pamper yourself with a beauty treatment or massage.
