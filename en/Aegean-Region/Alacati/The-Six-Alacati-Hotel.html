Description

All rooms in The Six Alaçatı Hotel are equipped with a coffee machine. The Six Alaçatı Hotel has certain units with pool views, and each room comes with a private bathroom. The rooms include a desk.

Breakfast is available each morning, and includes continental and Full English/Irish options. At The Six Alaçatı Hotel you'll find a restaurant serving Mediterranean cuisine. A halal option can also be requested.

The Six Alaçatı Hotel has a seasonal outdoor pool.

Speaking German and English, staff are willing to help at any time of the day at the reception.

Erythrai Antique City is 2.8 miles from The Six Alaçatı Hotel. The nearest airport is Adnan Menderes Airport, 42 miles from The Six Alaçatı Hotel.
