Description

At the hotel, the rooms have a desk. Featuring a private bathroom, some accommodations at Mehmet Ali Aga Mansion also offer a balcony. All guest rooms include a closet.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property. There's an in-house restaurant, which serves a variety of seafood and international dishes and also offers halal and vegetarian options.

Guests can take part in various activities in the surrounding areas, including snorkeling and cycling.

The accommodation also provides a business center and guests can check the newspapers at Mehmet Ali Aga Mansion. Speaking Arabic and German at the 24-hour front desk, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical information on the area.

The nearest airport is Milas Airport, 34 miles from the property.

"Had the opportunity to stay at Mehmet Ali Ağa mansion after my trip from Istanbul, Göcek and Marmaris.I can easily say that this was a different experience. I have never stayed in a 200 year old Ottoman Landlord’s mansion before :)We had a warm and polite welcome leading me to the well decorated and peaceful room. The room had a patio and sun-lounges to lay down and relax.Stone rooms reminded me of my childhood and is perfect for climate control. Mild during summer and winter.The beautiful green garden was full of Jasmine smell and is filled with different types of regional trees and plants.Laying down around the swimming pool and reading my book whilst I drink a cold beer took all my filled up city stress away.Historical Knidos and the Boat Tour they have arranged for me was very satisfactory."
