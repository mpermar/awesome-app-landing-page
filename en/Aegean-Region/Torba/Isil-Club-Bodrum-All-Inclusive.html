Description

The stylish, air conditioned rooms all offer a balcony with views of the grounds or sea. They come with a modern bathroom with bathrobes, a minibar and satellite flat-screen TV.

Işil Club Bodrum’s large beachfront pool is lined with lounge chairs. A pier stretches into the sea from the private beach, perfect for sunbathing or diving. On-site water sports include windsurfing, canoeing and snorkeling.

For something more relaxing, guests can visit the full-service spa offering an extensive menu of treatments.There is also a sauna and steam room.

There is a kids' club to entertain the little ones, along with a children's pool. Other activities include live music on the terrace, shows in the on-site amphitheater and club nights. The property also has tennis courts.

Işil’s main restaurant offers delightful local and international cuisine, either indoors or outdoor style with sea views. The hotel features no less than 5 bars.

Local transportation services to Bodrum are available just outside the resort.
