Description

Enjoying a scenic location in the town of Einsiedeln, Hotel Allegro is 500 metres from the Benedictine Abbey and Lake Sihl.

The complex consists of a hotel part and a youth and educational centre with an auditorium and a chapel.

Free WiFi is available in public areas. The bright rooms at the Allegro Hotel all feature a private bathroom, a work desk and overlook the abbey or the lake.

Traditional Swiss cuisine and many healthy dishes are served in the Allegro’s restaurant, which features a panoramic roof terrace.

Guests can relax in the large garden, use the bowling facilities, and play billiards and table tennis.

Free private parking is available on site. Einsiedeln Train Station is 2 km away, and the next bus stop can be reached in a 3-minute walk.
