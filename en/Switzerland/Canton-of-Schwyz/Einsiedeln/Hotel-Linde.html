Description

The family-run Hotel Linde in Einsiedeln is located on the abbey square, 200 metres from the town´s abbey, and it offers a restaurant, a roof terrace and free wireless internet access in some rooms.

Public parking spaces are available on site free of charge. The units at the Hotel Linde come with private or shared bathroom facilities.

The junior suites also feature a comfortable seating area. Buffet breakfast is available every morning at the property, and dinner can be enjoyed at the in-house restaurant, serving traditional and local Swiss cuisine.

In summer, guests can savour a selection of drinks and snacks at the bar on the rooftop terrace and the garden bistro.

In addition, a bowling bar is also available on the premises, offering live concerts.
