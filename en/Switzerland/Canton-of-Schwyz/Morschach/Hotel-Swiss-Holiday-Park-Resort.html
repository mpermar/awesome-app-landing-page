Description

Located in the village Morschach above Lake Lucerne, Swiss Holiday Park Resort is the largest holiday and leisure resort in Switzerland and is surrounded by mountains and lush meadows. It offers stunning views of the lake and of the Alps and features a wide range of leisure activities, spa facilities and restaurants.

The resort consists of several buildings with different accommodations options. There are modern hotel rooms with flat-screen TVs, simply furbished hostel rooms with bunk beds and apartments with fully equipped kitchens.

The extensive spa area is free of charge to guests and features an adventure pool, 2 spectacular water slides, an area for toddlers, a hot tub, a white water channel, climbing rocks, various saunas, a salt water pool and a salt water hot tub. Various indoor and outdoor sports and games can be enjoyed and a fitness center is available.

For children, there is an adventure farm with petting animals, a supervised kid's club and a special children's buffet. Baby sitting services can be arranged and equipment for toddlers can be provided upon request.

Three restaurants are on site the Swiss Holiday Park Resort. Different types of cuisine are served at each venue. The Schwiizer Stube offers seasonal Swiss cuisine and wines, while the Restaurant Panorama with its views of the surrounding Alps serves international dishes. The Il Gusto features pizza and pasta specialties.

In the adventure bath, guests can also savor snacks and drinks at the Neptun snack bar and the Travelers Bar and the Fun Factory serve a variety of drinks.

A wide array of mountains, lakes, museums, cities and leisure activities for families can be reached in the surroundings. In winter, a small ski elevator with ski slopes for children and a toboggan run is featured in the resort.

The valley station of the car-free Stoos Ski Area can be reached in a 4-minute drive, and in winter, the resort provides a free shuttle service for guests. A ski storage room is available at the hotel and guests can also buy ski passes there.
