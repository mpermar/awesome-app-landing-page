Description

Built in 1642, the Pension-Restaurant Drei Tannen on the Furka road in the Goms area offers a restaurant with a charming terrace, cosy rooms and free Wi-Fi.

The rustic rooms of the Drei Tannen guest house feature typical Alpine-style wooden furniture.

Seasonal and regional dishes with local specialities are served at the restaurant.

The Niederwald train station is only 100 metres away. Niederwald is also the birth place of Cäsar Ritz, the name giver of the famous hotel, and his tomb can be found in the village.
