Description

Hotel Les Etagnes is right next to the gondola lift in Haute-Nendaz in the Quatres Vallées ski area.

It offers newly renovated rooms with flat-screen TVs and free Wi-Fi access. Rooms at the Les Etagnes have a safe and a large bathroom with bathrobes and a hairdryer.

Many rooms have a balcony. The restaurant serves international cuisine and has a terrace with panoramic views of the Valais Alps.

A varied breakfast buffet is served in the morning. The sauna can be booked for private use free of charge.

Private parking is available free of charge at the hotel. The centre of Haute-Nendaz is only a short walk away and Verbier is an 1-hour drive away.
