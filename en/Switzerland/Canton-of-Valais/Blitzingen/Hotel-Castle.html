Description

Surrounded by the Valais Alps at 1,350 metres above sea level, this 4-star hotel in Blitzingen offers a spa and an award-winning restaurant.

All rooms have a balcony and a kitchenette. The spacious rooms at Hotel Castle feature a flat-screen satellite TV, a seating area, and a bathroom with bathrobes and a hairdryer.

Awarded 16 points by Gault Millau, the restaurant serves Swiss and international cuisine and fine wines.

The Castle Hotel’s spa facilities include a sauna, a hot tub, and a steam bath. A wide range of massage and beauty treatments is offered.

Free public parking is available on site. A private parking garage is available at an additional cost.

Blitzingen Train Station is 2 km away. A free shuttle service is available on request.
