Description

The Turm Hotel & Spa Grächerhof in Grächen bei Zermatt offers Alpine-style rooms, fine cuisine and a spa area in the village centre.

A bus stop is just 50 metres away, and the cable cars can be reached within a 2-minute walk.

Free in-room WiFi is available. The exclusive Private Spa at the Residence Grächerhof, which features a sauna lounge, hot tub, various other spa facilities, and a sun terrace, can be booked for slots of 2.

5 hours. Opened in 2017, the new Alpine Spa Lodge offers a Finnish sauna, a herbal soft sauna, a steam bath, panorama hot tubs, experience showers, a Kneipp foot bubble bath, a relax lodge with a wood-fired oven, a spa-buffet with energized water, juices and teas, as well as a selection of massages.

Guests can enjoy a 4-course dinner (soup or starter at choice or a vegetarian option for main course), or treat themselves to hearty dishes in the Kaminstübli restaurant.

A fine selection of wines from the Valais is stored in the hotel's well-kept wine cellar.

The rustic lobby features an open fireplace, and the bar has access to a terrace.

From Monday to Friday, guests can participate in activities like wine tasting and other regional activities.
