Description

Offering a spa area and a restaurant, Europe Hotel & Spa is located within 700 metres from the Zermatt Train Station, the Zermatt-Furi Ski Lift and the Matterhorn Glacier Paradise Station.

Free WiFi access is available in all areas of the property. The rooms are individually furnished and come with a minibar, a flat-screen TV, and a bathroom with shower, bathrobes, free toiletries and hairdryer.

Most rooms also feature a balcony with views of the Matterhorn Mountain. Some rooms come with an open plan bathroom.

Buffet breakfast is offered every morning and dinner is accompanied by fine wines from the extensive wine card.

Guests can relax in the spa area with 2 saunas, a steam bath, a hot tub and a massage room and relaxation room.
