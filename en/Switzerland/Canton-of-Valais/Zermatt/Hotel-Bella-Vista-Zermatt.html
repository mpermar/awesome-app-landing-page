Description

The Hotel Bella Vista is situated about a 5-minute walk from the centre of Zermatt.

It offers free Wi-Fi, a free internet station and panoramic mountain views from the terrace.

Some rooms offer panoramic Matterhorn views as well. The hotel's owner is a skilled baker and takes great pleasure in making fine croissants and other pastry.

You don't have to be British to enjoy the 35 different kinds of tea. The Bella Vista hotel serves local wines from the Valais at the bar and with your meal.

A shuttle service from and to the Zermatt train station is available on request and for an extra charge.
