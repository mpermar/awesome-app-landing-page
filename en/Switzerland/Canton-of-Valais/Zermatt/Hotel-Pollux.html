Description

The Hotel Pollux in the centre of Zermatt and 200 metres from the train station offers you newly renovated rooms, charmingly combining rustic elements with modern design.

Enjoy tasty Swiss and international cuisine in the 2 restaurants of the Pollux hotel, relax in the hotel bar in front of the fireplace.

The breakfast buffet is included in the room rate. Wireless internet is available in the entire hotel free of charge.

A conference room, a relaxation area with a Finnish sauna, a steam bath, a hot tub, a magic shower and a solarium are at your disposal as well.
