Description

Individually furnished in the style of a different artist, such as Van Gogh or Picasso, all accommodation at Artist Apartments & Hotel Garni features a flat-screen cable TV and balcony.

The balcony in each guestroom provides a stunning view of the Matterhorn. Some rooms offer a well-appointed kitchen and a dining area.

Artist Apartments & Hotel Garni, situated on the forested slopes of the Alps, provides the ideal location for hiking in the summer and skiing in the winter.

The hotel is a 7-minute walk from the Zermatt – Furi ski lift. Artist Apartments also offers free Wi-Fi access and a laundry and ironing service.
