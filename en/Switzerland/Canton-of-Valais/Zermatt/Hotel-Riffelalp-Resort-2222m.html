Description

Offering ski-to-door access and a spa center with an outdoor pool, Riffelalp Resort 7,300 feet is located right within the ski, hiking and biking region above Zermatt. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the property.

Most of the rooms and suites feature a balcony and breathtaking views of the Matterhorn. All rooms have a bathroom with bathtub and/or shower, a Nespresso coffee machine and a Smart-TV. Bathrobes and slippers are provided.

The outdoor pool at Spa 2'750 feet is heated to 35°C, and there are a sun terrace, a selection of saunas, a steam room, a hot tub and a solarium. Massages and other treatments can be booked.

The three restaurants at Riffelalp Resort 7,300 feet offer different culinary delights, from fine dining to Italian and local specialties.

Baby and child care services can be arranged upon request, and there are a 24-hour reception and a concierge service. Ski passes can be purchased at the hotel, and there is a ski and mountain bike hire. In summer, Europe's highest tram operates within the resort.
