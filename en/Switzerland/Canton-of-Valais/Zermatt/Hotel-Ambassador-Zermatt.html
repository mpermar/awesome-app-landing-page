Description

The Hotel Ambassador Zermatt enjoys a central location, only 100 metres from the Zermatt train station and the Gornergrat rack railway station.

It offers a large heated indoor pool, whirlpool, sauna and free high-speed WiFi in all areas.

All rooms and apartments feature a balcony offering panoramic views of the surrounding mountains, including the Matterhorn.

There is a free internet corner next to the lobby, and a ski rental shop is on site as well.

Fine Swiss and international cuisine can be enjoyed in the Chi-Ba-Bou restaurant.

The lounge and the bar are inviting places to spend some relaxing hours with a selection of drinks.

Shuttle services and a taxi to and from the airport or the train station can be arranged.
