Description

Owned by the famous skier Pirmin Zurbriggen, the Suitenhotel in Zermatt can be found just 350 feet from the Matterhorn Express Cable Car and offers you rooms and suites with Matterhorn views, as well as indoor and outdoor pools.

All rooms and suites come with a mountain-view balcony and the suites feature a fully equipped, modern kitchen. Bathrobes and slippers are provided and free Wi-Fi is available in the entire building.

Breakfast is available every morning and will be served to your room. The nearest restaurant is just 350 feet away.

A free shuttle service from the Zermatt Train Station is available. The center of Zermatt is 1,950 feet away from Suitenhotel Zurbriggen.
