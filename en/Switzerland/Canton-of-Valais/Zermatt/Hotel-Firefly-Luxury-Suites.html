Description

One of our best sellers in Zermatt! The 4-star Firefly Luxury Suites at the foot of the Matterhorn in Zermatt is only a 3-minute walk away from the Klein Matterhorn cable car and a short walk from the village center.

The hotel offers modern, spacious suites and an indoor pool and spa area. Most suites feature a balcony and the larger ones an open fireplace.

An à la carte breakfast is served to you in the suite at your desired time.

A transfer from Zermatt's train station or parking areas is offered.Cost may apply
