Description

The stylish Hotel Allalin is set in a quiet and sunny area of the car-free village of Saas-Fee, 200 metres from the Gondola Lift to Hannig.

Most rooms have a balcony that provides excellent views of the surroundings. WiFi is available in the entire hotel free of charge.

The rooms and the ambiance are a mixture of traditional Alpine decorations and modern design elements.

Spend relaxing hours enjoying wonderful local cuisine in the charming restaurant as well as selected beverages in the cosy bar.

The rich buffet breakfast is included in the room rate and half board with a tasty 6-course dinner and a salad and cheese buffet is available as well.

The sauna of the Allalin Hotel invite you to unwind after a great day out in the fresh mountain air.

The terrace also has sweeping views of the surrounding landscape. Heated ski storage facilities are located next to the ski slopes.

From June to October, guests enjoy free use of public transport and the cable cars of the Saas Valley (except the Metro Alpin Cable Car), while from October to April, guests enjoy free use of public transport and several discounts and benefits in the region.
