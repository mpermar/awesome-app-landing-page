Description

Located in the centre of the car-free village of Saas-Fee, Hotel du Glacier - The Dom Collection offers panoramic views of the Swiss Alps and a spa area with 2 saunas and a steam bath.

The rooms at the Hotel du Glacier offer cable TV, a seating area, and a bathroom with hairdryer.

A minibar can be provided on request. Most of the Superior Double Rooms feature a balcony.

The rich breakfast buffet is available from 07:30 to 10:30 at the Mondial restaurant.

At the 4545 Coffee & Lounge or on the terrace you can spend some relaxing time over a cup of aromatic coffee, freshly prepared by the barista.
