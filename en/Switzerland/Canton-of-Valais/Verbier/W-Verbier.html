Description

W Verbier is a luxurious house in the modern centre of Verbier, boasting contemporary alpine design and a spa area with indoor pool and heated outdoor pool.

The hotel is located directly next to the Verbier Cable Car and offers ski-to-door access.

The W Verbier is composed of 6 chalets set around the town centre, the Place Blanche.

Each room features a balcony, a flat-screen TV, a coffee machine and a bathroom with shower or bathtub.

Some rooms provide beautiful views of the mountains or the valley. Free WiFi is available.

Breakfast is served every morning and guests can enjoy dinner at the W Kitchen restaurant, serving modern Swiss and Mediterranean cuisine.

W Verbier also features a tapas bar opened by Sergi Arola, a sushi bar and a cafÃ© on site.

Guests have free access to the Away Spa Area and the gym with modern fitness facilities.

A 24-hour concierge service is offered. The ski lifts leading to the large Verbier Ski Area are just 100 metres away.

A shuttle service to Geneva Airport, a 2-hour drive away, is available on request.
