Description

Offering a spa area and a swimming pool overlooking the mountains, Chalet d'Adrien is located in Verbier, just 30 metres from Savoleyres Ski Lift.

All elegant Alpine-style rooms feature mountain views from the balcony. Guests at Chalet d'Adrien can enjoy gourmet dining, awarded 17 out of 20 points by the Gault Millau guide, with Italian influences and more traditional dishes including fondue, stew pots and old-fashioned pies.

Barbecue facilities are out on the terrace where freshly grilled meat, fish and seafood are served at lunch.

Spectacular mountain views are provided from the terrace. Snacks are also served in the bar.

Spacious rooms include a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and DVD player. They also come with a seating area and a bathroom with a bath, bathrobes, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Further facilities include a large fitness room, free WiFi, free outdoor parking and a games room for children.

Valet and garage parking are available at an additional charge. Some souvenir shops are on site.

It is possible to hire ski equipment and buy ski passes at Chalet d'Adrien. A free ski bus stops right outside.
