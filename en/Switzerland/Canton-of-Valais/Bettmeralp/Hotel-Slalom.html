Description

The friendly Hotel Slalom is situated in car-free Bettmeralp, set on a high plateau in the UNESCO-protected Jungfrau-Aletsch-Bietschhorn area.

It can only be reached by cable car and offers a tranquil atmosphere in the beautiful natural surroundings of the Valais Alps.

The rooms offer satellite TV, a balcony and a bathroom. Some feature panoramic views of the Matterhorn and Weisshorn.

After an active day, you can enjoy a drink in the authentic bar. The hotel is directly accessible from the ski slopes.

The Slalom hotel has a lounge and a breakfast room. Wireless internet is available free of charge.

The Slalom hotel's laptop is available as well. The cable car station is a 10-minute walk away.
