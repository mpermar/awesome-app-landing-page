Description

Hotel Elite’s south-facing rooms feature cable TV and a bathroom with hairdryer.

Guests can enjoy a heated outdoor pool in summer which is free of charge. The restaurant at the Elite Hotel serves traditional Swiss cuisine.

The Jack Nicklaus Golf Course and several cross-country ski runs are right next to Hotel Elite. Golf and ski lessons are available.
