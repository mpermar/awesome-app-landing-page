Description

In a quiet yet central location, 150 metres from the Crans Montana Casino, Hotel Helvetia Intergolf offers a spa area, an indoor pool, and spacious rooms with panoramic views of the Valais Alps.

WiFi and garage parking are free of charge. The Intergolf Helvetia’s rooms feature a balcony, satellite TV, a minibar, and a bathroom with bathrobes and a hairdryer.

The restaurant serves Italian and French cuisine. Swiss specialities such as fondue and raclette are served in the traditional restaurant Carnotzet.

The chalet-style bar offers a wide range of wines and cocktails. Spa facilities include a hot tub, steam bath and a sauna.

There is also a children's play area and a garden with a sun terrace. The Grand Signal Ski Lift is 700 metres away and a free shuttle service is available.

The closest train station is Montana Gare, 700 metres away. The train station Sierre is 17 km from the property.
