Description

Close to Crans Montana's most beautiful boutiques, the sports facilities and the congress centre, the Hotel Etrier offers you up-to-date wellness facilities, completely renovated in 2016, including indoor and outdoor pools.

In winter, the Le fer à Cheval restaurant offers you delicious traditional specialities such as regional cheeses, cheese fondue, Chinese fondue and raclette.

In summer you can enjoy tasty grilled dishes by the outdoor pool. Apart from the pools, also the sauna and the steam bath invite you to unwind after a day out in the fresh mountain air.
