Description

The luxurious and exclusive LeCrans Hotel & Spa welcomes you in an exquisite chalet-style atmosphere high above Crans Montana.

Its fine-dining restaurant by Pierre Crepaud was awarded its first Michelin star and has 17 GaultMillau points Surrounded by mountains and forests, this 5-star hotel offers a breathtaking view of the Alps and the Mont Blanc.

The luxuriously furnished rooms and suites provide an intimate ambience and a wealth of amenities.

Each themed room and suite takes you to a different region or mountain of the world, from the Himalayas to the Kilimanjaro.

Each one is decorated in a unique style, with rare objects and rich wall hangings.

The excellent restaurant serves traditional Swiss as well as international cuisine, combined with fantastic mountain views.

In a unique cellar setting, the nightclub provides a fun atmosphere for an evening out.

LeCrans Hotel & Spa offers a world of well-being at LeSpa, where stone, wood, water and tranquillity bring the nearby mountains to you.

Eastern and Western treatments, massages, a hammam, a sauna and a hot tub pamper body and soul.

The broad balconies, the terraces, the heated outdoor pool and the brasserie afford incomparable views of Europe's highest mountains.
