Description

The Beau Sejour chalet hotel is located about 400 metres from the cable car in Champery amid the Portes du Soleil ski region.

Free Wi-Fi is available. From the balcony of your room you have panoramic views of the Dents du Midi.

All rooms are furnished in elegant Alpine style. During high winter season, an afternoon snack with home-made cakes and tea, which you can enjoy in front of a crackling fire, is optional at an additional cost.

An evening tea is available all year round free of charge. For the little guests of the Art.

Boutique. Hotel Beau-Séjour Champery there is a separate playroom. A camera-controlled storage room is available for your ski equipment.

A limited number of garage parking spaces is available free of charge on a first-come-first-served basis.
