Description

The Berghaus Toni is located in the car-free resort of Riederalp, Valais, which is part of the Aletsch Arena, 200 metres from the Riederalp-West cable car stop.

There is snow from early December to late April and it is possible to access the slopes directly from the ski storage room of the Berghaus Toni.

The ice bar on the terrace invites guests to enjoy a glass of mulled wine next to the open fire, after a day of skiing.

In summer, Berghaus Toni is a convenient base for glacier tours, for biking, paragliding and hiking or for playing golf at 2000 metres above sea level while enjoying Matterhorn views.

All of the rooms have an en-suite bathroom and a TV. Some feature a balcony with panoramic views of the Swiss Alps and the Mörel Valley.

Seasonal and innovative meals prepared from fine local ingredients are served in the restaurant featuring a terrace.
