Description

Situated in quiet surroundings a few steps from the centre of Ovronnaz, the Hostellerie de L'Ardève offers panoramic views of the Alps and the Rhone Valley.

It offers free Wi-Fi. The Thermalp thermal spa and the chair lift are reachable by car within 5 minutes.

Hostellerie de l'Ardève features a brasserie offering Valais specialties, open every day for lunch and dinner.

Free parking spaces are available at Hostellerie de l'Ardève. Cars can also be parked in a garage for surcharge.
