Description

The family-run Hotel Mélèzes in Haudères is located at 1,450 metres above sea level, at the foot of the Dents de Veisivi.

It offers free Wi-Fi and fine cuisine. All of the rustic-style rooms at the Mélèzes Hotel have a private bathroom with a hairdryer and a flat-screen satellite TV.

A buffet breakfast can be enjoyed every morning and specialities from the canton of Valais are served in the restaurant and on the summer terrace.

Special menus for kids are available. A playroom for children is located next to the dining room and a ski storage room can be found in the building, as well.

Private parking spaces are available on site free of charge. On request, a shuttle bus to Evolène can be organised.
