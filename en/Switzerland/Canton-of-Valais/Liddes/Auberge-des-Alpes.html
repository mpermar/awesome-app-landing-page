Description

Offering free Wi-Fi and free parking, this hotel is a 30-minute car drive from Verbier ski resort with 4 valleys.

The restaurant serves traditional French-based cuisine from the Valais region. All rooms at the Auberge des Alpes come with hardwood floors, work desks and private bathrooms with showers.

There is a playground for children and a sports ground to play petanque. Guests can also relax in the TV lounge.

The Vichères-Bavon ski trail is a 10-minute drive from the Auberge. The train station in Liddes and the Route Saint-Bernard (E27 motorway) are within 100 metres from the hotel.
