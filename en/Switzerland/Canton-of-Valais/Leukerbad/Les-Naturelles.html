Description

Only a 10-minute walk from the centre of Leukerbad and the thermal spa, Les Naturelles offers spacious apartments and free ski storage facilities.

The cable car is 50 metres away. Each apartment at Les Naturelles features a digital TV, CD and DVD players, a kitchen with a dining area, and a bathroom with a hairdryer.

Bed linen and towels are included in the rate. Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas, and there is a free internet terminal in the lobby.

On request and at an additional cost, internet access in the apartment can be provided as well.

The residence features a games room where guests can play billiard, table tennis, and tabletop football.

A washing machine and a dryer are also available at Les Naturelles. In summer, a children’s playground and a miniature golf course are just a few steps away.

A bus stop and a cross-country ski run are also close by. The nearby Leukerbad Cable Car takes guests into the Rinderhütte ski area.

Zurich Airport is a 3. 5-hour drive away and Geneva Airport can be reached within 2.

5 hours by car. Bern, Interlaken and Lucerne are reachable within a 3-hour drive.
