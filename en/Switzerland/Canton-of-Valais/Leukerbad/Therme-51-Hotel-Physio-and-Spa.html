Description

This property is located in a central location, 2 minutes from the bus station, between Alpen Thermal Bath and Leukerbad Thermal Bath.

It consists of 2 houses, the guesthouse Volksheilbad and the bathhouse Therme 51.

All rooms at the Kur & Ferienhaus Volksheilbad are equipped with flat-screen cable TV, and most of them feature a balcony and a view of the surrounding mountains.

Some rooms are located in the annex building. Each room features a tablet that functions as a digital concierge where guests are informed about hotel news and can book massages and other hotel offers.

Guests can use the on-site thermal bath for free. There is also a playroom for children, a common room with a library and free WiFi in all areas.

The Burgerbad and the Lindner Alpen Thermal Bath Spas are only a few minutes' walk away.

Guests can enjoy physio, wellness, and cosmetic treatments. A rich buffet breakfast and fine Swiss cuisine for guests having booked half or full board are available in the restaurant of the Kur & Ferienhaus Volksheilbad.

All rates include the Leukerbad Plus Card, entitling to free use of the Sportarena with tennis and squash courts, mini golf, table tennis, fitness facilities and an ice skating rink.

Also, the local bus line can be used for free and electric bikes in summer. The Leukerbad Plus Card offers may vary according to the season.
