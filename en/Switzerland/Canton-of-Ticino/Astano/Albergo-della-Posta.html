Description

Albergo la Posta, operating since 1888 and member of the Swiss Historic Hotels, is located in the quiet little village of Astano in the Malcantone region and offers a seasonal outdoor swimming pool, a restaurant serving gourmet dishes with local products, and a bar.

Both the vintage rooms in the historic building and the modern rooms in the Nicoletta building feature free wired internet and are at least 20 m² in size.

Some units also come with a balcony or a terrace. During summer, you can dine outside in the charming garden and enjoy selected wines provided from regional vineyards.

The local lake for swimming is just 250 metres away. Lugano is 17 km away and is easily accessible by public transport.
