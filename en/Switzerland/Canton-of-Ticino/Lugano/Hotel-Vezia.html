Description

This original hotel in the “Fabulous Fifties” look is located in Vezia, 3 km from Lake Lugano and Lugano’s city centre.

It is 1. 5 km from the motorway exit Lugano-North. The hotel offers a nice restaurant, a large garden with swimming pool, free parking, and free in-room Wi-Fi.

Guests can choose from various set menus or dine à la carte and select from a wide range of wine, beer and other drinks.

Many rooms have air conditioning and a private garage directly under the room. Just across the road there is a modern fitness centre and a beauty farm.

A bus stop is 2 steps from the hotel, and in 12 minutes you can reach Lugano’s city centre and the lake.
