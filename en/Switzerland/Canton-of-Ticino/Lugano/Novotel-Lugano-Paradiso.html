Description

Novotel Lugano Paradiso is a four star hotel, just 200 metres from Lake Lugano and the centre of Lugano can be reached within 5 minutes by bus.

The on-site swimming pool is heated and open from May until the end of September.

The property also offers a pool bar, a fitness centre, and a video game area. Free WiFi is available.

The air-conditioned rooms at the Lugano-Paradiso Novotel feature a flat-screen cable TV, minibar, seating area, and bathroom with a hairdryer.

The air-conditioned restaurant and bar GourmetBar includes a terrace. The private parking garage can be used at an additional cost.
