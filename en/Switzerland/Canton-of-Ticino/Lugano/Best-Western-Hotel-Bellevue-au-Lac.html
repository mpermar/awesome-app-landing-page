Description

Best Western Hotel Bellevue au Lac is situated on the lakeside promenade of Lugano and offers panoramic views.

It features an outdoor pool and all rooms have a balcony and free internet access.

The Bellevue au Lac is only a few minutes' walk away from the historic, financial and shopping centre and the Lugano Sud motorway exit can also be reached quickly.

The 2 restaurants of the Best Western Hotel Bellevue au Lac serve aromatic Italian and international cuisine and in the new Bellvue bar you can enjoy a wide range of drinks and snacks.
