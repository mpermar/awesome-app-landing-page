Description

Swiss Historic & Garten Hotel Villa Carona is a 200-year patrician estate with a lot of history; it is set in an attractive quaint village, where Herrman Hesse spent many precious hours.

The property is managed and owned by the Deuber-Marty family. Each room is individually decorated and furnished with antique pieces.

The garden is a small paradise, in which one may enjoy breakfast outside, lounge around in the sun or shady spots and play table tennis.

It also features a children's pool, grape pergola and a restaurant for healthy meals.
