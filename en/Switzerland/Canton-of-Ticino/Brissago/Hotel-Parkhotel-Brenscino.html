Description

One of our top picks in Brissago.Located just 1,650 feet from the waterfront at Lake Maggiore, Parkhotel Brenscino features a heated outdoor pool, a large terrace with panoramic views and a 125 ft² botanical garden.

Brenscino Hotel is located on a hill and all rooms include a private balcony with beautiful views. They all come with free Wi-Fi and flat-screen satellite TV.

The impressive flower garden that surrounds the hotel includes 2 swimming pools and a playground area.

The restaurant includes a large panorama terrace and it serves Mediterranean specialties.
