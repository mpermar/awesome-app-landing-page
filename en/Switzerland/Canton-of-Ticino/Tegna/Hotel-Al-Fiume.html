Description

The Al Fiume hotel is situated 5 km from Locarno and Ascona in a quiet green garden with tropical plants, directly at the sandy beach of the Maggia river, ideal for bathing and diving.

All south-facing rooms have their own front door, a cosy living room with a seating area, wireless internet access, a fridge, a Nespresso coffee machine and a balcony or a garden area.

Bikes as well as sunshades for the beach are available free of charge at the Al Fiume hotel.

The surroundings offer many opportunities for sports including hiking, (mountain) bike tours, diving, free climbing, paragliding and the like.

There is a tennis court nearby and the new golf course 'Golf Gerre Losone' can be reached on foot in 10 minutes.

Don't miss to visit the Teatro Dimitri in Verscio. You can park your car for free on site.
