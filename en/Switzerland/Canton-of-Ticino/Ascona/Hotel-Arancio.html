Description

Only a few steps from Lake Maggiore, Hotel Arancio is set in a subtropical landscape 2 km from Ascona.

It features a large, heated outdoor pool overlooking the lake. Free bicycles are available for guests.

The rooms have cable TV and a bathroom with hairdryer. Some offer views over the lake or the mountains.

The Arancio’s restaurant serves Mediterranean and international cuisine and features a large terrace overlooking Lake Maggiore.
