Description

Surrounded by a large park, ideally located between Ascona and Locarno and 1 km from Lake Maggiore, Parkhotel Delta offers an indoor pool, a seasonal heated outdoor swimming pool, a wellness area and an -la-carte restaurant.

Free parking spaces and free WiFi are available on site. The Deltas wellness area comprises 2 saunas, a ladies only sauna, a steam bath, a terrace with sun beds, hydro massages and relaxation areas.

A modern and spacious fitness centre is also available. The spacious, classic and air-conditioned rooms and suites at the property come with a marble bathroom, a flat-screen satellite TV and a minibar.

Most also feature a balcony with views of the surrounding mountains. Guests can enjoy dinner at the restaurant Da Jean Pierre, serving seasonal specialities, and have drinks at the lobby bar or the pool bar.

A daily breakfast buffet is available as well. Further facilities at the hotel include an 18-hole miniature golf course, a small football field and a volleyball court.

Free bicycles for adults and children are also provided free of charge. The reception is available 24 hours a day.

On request, a free shuttle service to and from Locarno Train Station can be organised.

The Patriziale Golf Course is reachable within 800 metres and the Gerre Golf Course in Losone is a 4-minute drive away.
