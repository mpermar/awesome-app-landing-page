Description

At the hotel, every room includes a balcony. Every room is equipped with a private bathroom with a bath or shower and free toiletries. All guest rooms in Villa Favorita - Parkhotel Delta are equipped with a flat-screen TV and a hair dryer.

A buffet breakfast is available every morning at the property.

The wellness area at the accommodation is comprised of a fitness center and an indoor pool. You can play ping pong and minigolf at Villa Favorita - Parkhotel Delta, and bike rental is available.

Monte Verità is 1.1 miles from the hotel. The nearest airport is Agno Airport, 12 miles from Villa Favorita - Parkhotel Delta.
