Description

The rooms at the hotel come with a flat-screen TV and a kitchen. The rooms include a private bathroom. Guest rooms at Delta Resort Apartments are equipped with a seating area.

A buffet breakfast is served at the property. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of Italian dishes.

The accommodation has a playground. Guests at Delta Resort Apartments will be able to enjoy activities in and around Ascona, like skiing.

Around the clock advice is available at the reception. Where staff speak German, English, French and Italian.

Monte Verità is a 20-minute walk from the hotel. Agno Airport is 12 miles away.
