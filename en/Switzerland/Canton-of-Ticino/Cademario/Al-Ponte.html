Description

Located on a quiet hilltop in Cademario in the Malcantone region, a 15-minute drive from Lugano, the Al Ponte offers views of Lake Lugano and the mountains from all rooms.

Swimming pool is available from May to September. All rooms at this family-run hotel have their private balcony with sun loungers.

WiFi is provided in all areas for free. Local and international cuisine is served in the Al Ponte Terrazzo Restaurant and Pizzeria, which overlooks Lake Lugano.

Hiking and mountain bike trails are 1 km away. Bioggio can be reached by car in 10 minutes, and Manno is a 20-minute drive away.
