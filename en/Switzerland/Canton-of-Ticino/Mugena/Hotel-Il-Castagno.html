Description

The Hotel Il Castagno is situated between chestnut trees in Mugena the Alto Malcantone area, amid 3000 m² of beautiful gardens.

It offers free Wi-Fi and free parking and it is ideal for those who enjoy leisurely strolls through charming scenery.

Local and seasonal specialities and wines from the Ticino canton and from Italy are served in the restaurant.

The Percorso del Castagno hiking trail leads through the picturesque Alto Malcantone with its villages, churches, art and culture.

33 km long cycling trail starts directly at the hotel. The Il Castagno hotel is situated only 20 minutes from Lugano and 15 minutes' drive from the Lugano North exit on the highway Milan-Basel.
