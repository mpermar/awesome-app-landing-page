Description

This traditional hotel is located in the centre of Engelberg, next to the train station and a bus stop.

It offers free WiFi and a rich buffet breakfast. The recently refurbished rooms at the Bellevue-Terminus offer a contemporary design and have a flat-screen with cable channels.

Creative international cuisine is served in the restaurant, and various bars in the hotel invite you to unwind after a rewarding day.

The cable cars, as well as the pedestrian zone of Engelberg, can be reached in a short walk.

Tickets for the cable cars are sold at the reception of the Hotel Bellevue-Terminus.
