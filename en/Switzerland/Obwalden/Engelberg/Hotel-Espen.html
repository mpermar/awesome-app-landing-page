Description

Located in Engelberg, less than 1 km from Titlis Rotair Cable Car, Hotel Espen provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a bar and a garden.

Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a terrace. This pet-friendly hotel also has free WiFi.

At the hotel, each room is fitted with a wardrobe. Hotel Espen offers a continental or buffet breakfast.

You can play table tennis at the accommodation, and the area is popular for skiing.

Popular points of interest near Hotel Espen include Gerschnialp - Trübsee, Engelberg-Titlis and Brunnibahn.
