Description

In Sarnen, 2 km from the Sarnensee Lake, the self-catered Apartment Suite Chalet Wirz Travel offers an air-conditioned apartment with free WiFi and free private parking on site.

The Wirz Travel apartment features a balcony, a bedroom, and a living area with an open-plan kitchen and a dining table.

The bathroom is fitted with a shower and an Aqua Clean shower toilet. A flat-screen TV and a DVD player are provided in the room, while a computer and a printer are available upon request.

The kitchen comes with a dishwasher and a Nespresso coffee machine. The closest grocery store is within 550 metres, while a restaurant is 700 metres away.

The Sarnen Bizighofen Bus Stop is 250 metres away, and the Sarnen Train Station is reachable in 1.
