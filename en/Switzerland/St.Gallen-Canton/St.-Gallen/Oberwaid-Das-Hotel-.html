Description

Just a 10-minute drive away from Sankt Gallen, Oberwaid - Das Hotel. offers a large wellness area, 3 restaurants serving fine cuisine with regional products, and panoramic views of Lake Constance.

The hotel provides a shuttle service from and to St. Gallen Train Station at a surcharge.

All of the spacious and modern rooms come with a flat-screen satellite TV, wooden floors and a seating area.

A balcony with views of the surrounding area and a bathroom with free toiletries, a hairdryer, slippers and bathrobes are standard in each unit.

Free WiFi access is offered in each room. The spa area comprises a sauna, a hot tub, a Turkish bath, an indoor and an outdoor swimming pool, as well as a steam bath and treatment rooms.

The access to the spa area is free of charge. A private spa for massages is also available, while cardiology treatments are offered by a German and Arabic speaking doctor.

Each day, a freshly prepared buffet breakfast is served on site. You can also request room service.

Upon request, the property serves halal processed foods and products. A gym for your daily workout is a further feature of the Oberwaid.

A park-like garden with a sunbathing terrace surrounds the property. Car rental and concierge services are available on request, and a parking garage can be used against a surcharge.

A bus stops 150 metres away and takes you to St. Gallen’s centre within 10 minutes.

The shore of Lake Constance is 6 km from Oberwaid - Das Hotel, and St. Gallen-Altenrhein Airport can be reached within 20 minutes by car.
