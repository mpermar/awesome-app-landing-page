Description

The Grand Resort Bad Ragaz is a first-class spa hotel situated just an hour’s drive from Zurich, amidst the foothills of the Alps in Eastern Switzerland.

It is surrounded by a spectacular mountain landscape with picturesque hiking trails and stunning ski resorts.

The 5-star luxury resort offers its own thermal spring and a medical health centre.

Room and suites at the Grand Resort Bad Ragaz offer elegance, design and comfort.

There are 7 restaurants with a combined total of 59 GaultMillau points and 1 Michelin star, as well as a sushi takeaway, a bistro and a café, providing exquisite culinary delights.

In addition, the resort also boasts 2 golf courses, a meetings and events centre, the public Tamina Therme thermal spa and its own casino.

The thermal spa features the historic Helena pool, a sports pool, a garden pool, a family spa and the sauna area and 36.

5°C thermal water from the nearby Tamina gorge. This comprehensive spa offer is complemented by the medical expertise of the renowned medical health centre and the Bad Ragaz Clinic, which specialises in the treatment of convalescent patients.
