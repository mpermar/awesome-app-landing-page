Description

Featuring free WiFi throughout the property, Spring Inn offers accommodation in Flums.

Free private parking is available on site. All rooms include a seating area where you can relax and feature views of the mountain or garden.

The rooms come with two shared bathrooms and two additional toilets. You will find a shared kitchen with a microwave, a refrigerator, coffee machine, oven, stovetop and a dishwasher.

This bed and breakfast has ski storage space and bike hire is available. Ski resort Flumserberg is a 15-minute drive from Spring Inn.

The area is popular for skiing and cycling. The Flums Train Station is reachable within 1.

5 km. Davos is 49 km from Spring Inn, while Arosa is 43 km away. The nearest airport is Zurich Airport, 72 km from Spring Inn.
