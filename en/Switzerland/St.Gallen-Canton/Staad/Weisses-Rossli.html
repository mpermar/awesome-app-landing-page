Description

Situated in Staad directly at Lake Constance with its own small marina, Hotel Weisses Rössli is 200 metres from the public swimming pool and a 15-minute drive from St.

Gallen. Free Wi-Fi is provided in the entire hotel and there are sufficient parking places at your disposal.

Pizza prepared in the stone oven is served at the restaurant. In the summertime guests can enjoy their meals in the garden restaurant or the pavilion.

At the Hotel Weisses Rössli there is a post bus stop and the train station is only a few minutes away.

The motorway Rheineck exit is 3 km away and the St. Gallen - Altenrhein Airport is 1.
