Description

Boasting a terrace and free WiFi throughout the property, Berghaus Malbun is set in Buchs.

The accommodation offers ski-to-door access and ski storage space, as well as a restaurant and a garden.

The hotel has family rooms. At the hotel the rooms come with air conditioning and a private bathroom.

Continental and buffet breakfast options are available every morning at Berghaus Malbun.

The accommodation offers a children's playground. The area is popular for hiking and skiing, and bike hire is available at Berghaus Malbun.
