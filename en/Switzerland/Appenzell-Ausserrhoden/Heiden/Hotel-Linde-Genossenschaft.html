Description

Hotel Linde is located in the centre of Heiden, a 15-minute drive from St. Gallen and a 10-minute walk from the Henry Dunant Museum.

It features a sun terrace. The modern rooms at Genossenschaft Hotel Linde all have a seating area, a TV, and a bathroom.

Guests can enjoy classic Swiss cuisine and Appenzell specialities in the restaurant.

Hotel Linde also features a historic ballroom, where various cultural events such as concerts and exhibitions take place.
