Description

A Finnish sauna, a beautiful garden, and Swiss and French cuisine are offered by the 4-star Schloss-Hotel in Merlischachen.

It provides spacious, classic-style rooms with cable TV, a minibar, and a seating area.

Free WiFi is available. Spa facilities at the Schloss-Hotel include a sauna and a sun bed.

Guests can also relax on the spacious terrace. French and Swiss specialties are available in the on-site Swiss-Chalet restaurant.

Cocktails are offered at the Chalet Bar. Paid private parking is available and Merlischachen Train Station is a 5-minute walk away.
