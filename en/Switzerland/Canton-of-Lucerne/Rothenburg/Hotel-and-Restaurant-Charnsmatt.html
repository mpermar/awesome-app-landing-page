Description

Located in Rothenburg, just a 15-minute drive from Lucerne, Hotel & Restaurant Chärnsmatt has a large garden, an indoor children´s play area and a playground that features a 1.

4 km-miniature railway. Private parking is available free of charge in front of the building and the closest bus stop is 40 metres away.

All of the Alpine-style rooms at the Chärnsmatt Hotel & Restaurant are comfortably furnished and have an en suite bathroom and a TV.

Free WiFi is available in the entire building. The à-la-carte restaurant serves traditional Swiss food, featuring fresh local produce.

Meals can be enjoyed on the large garden terrace or in one of the cosy rooms indoors.

Drinks can be savoured at the property´s bar lounge. A cash machine is a 2-minute walk away.
