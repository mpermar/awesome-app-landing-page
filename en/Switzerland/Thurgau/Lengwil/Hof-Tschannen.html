Description

Hof Tschannen enjoys a quiet location on the outskirts of the village of Illighausen, 4 km from the Lake Constanze.

The farm house offers simply furnished rooms with scenic views of the lake and free Wi-Fi.

You can start the day with a rich breakfast prepared from home made farm products.

Half and fully board is also available at Tschannen Hof on request. You can experience farm life by sleeping on straw in the barn.

The garden awaits guests with a barbecue area and a playground. Horseback riding is possible on site.

The next bus stop in Illighausen is 800 metres away, while the Lengwil train station is 2 km from the property.

The town of Kreuzlingen is 5 km from the farm house, and the city of Konstanz can be reached within 8 km.
