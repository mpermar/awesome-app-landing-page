Description

Surrounded by a lush garden, the family-run Haus Erika Am Weg is 100 metres from the shores of the Lake Constance and it offers tastefully furnished rooms and free WiFi connection.

The rooms are equipped with satellite TV and provided with shared bathroom facilities.

Breakfast is served every morning. The city of St. Gallen can be reached within 20 minutes by car.

Parking is possible in front of the building, and a bike storage is also available.
