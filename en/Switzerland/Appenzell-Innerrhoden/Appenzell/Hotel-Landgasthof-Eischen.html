Description

Situated at the foot of the Säntismassiv Mountain Range and the Kronberg Mountain, the Landgasthof Eischen features a traditional Appenzell restaurant with a terrace, a spa area and was renovated in 2012.

The hotel offers guests a wellness area with a sauna area, an outdoor hot tub, a relaxation room with views to the valley, and an indoor swimming pool with a hydro-flow system and views of the Appenzell landscape and the Voralberg Alps.

The family-run Hotel Landgasthof Eischen offers panoramic mountain views, free Wi-Fi, free parking and a playground for kids.

All rooms have a private bathroom and a satellite TV. The centre of Appenzell is a 7-minute drive or bus ride away.
