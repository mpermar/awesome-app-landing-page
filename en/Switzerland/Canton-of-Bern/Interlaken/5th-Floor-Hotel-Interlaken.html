Description

You can choose from rooms with private bathrooms and rooms with shared bathroom facilities. All rooms are non-smoking and equipped with an electric teakettle, a sink and a hairdryer. There is an elevator available which goes up to the 4th floor.

A computer with free internet is available in the hallway.

The Taverne restaurant on the ground floor serves Swiss and international cuisine and is open daily. The Kloster Lounge & Bar is open from Tuesday until Saturday. A hot and cold buffet breakfast can be enjoyed every morning.

A limited number of parking spaces are available on site free of charge, Reservation is not possible. Further parking is available in the nearby public location for an additional fee.
