Description

Family-run for 3 generations, the Stella Swiss Quality Hotel is only a 3-minute walk away from the centre of Interlaken.

It features a heated indoor swimming pool, a restaurant, free Wi-Fi, and free parking on site.

Each room is different in size and layout and comes equipped with bathrobes, bath towels and slippers.

You can enjoy fine Swiss and international cuisine in the Stellambiente restaurant of the Stella Hotel.

Situated in the heart of the Jungfrau region, the Stella Swiss Quality Hotel provides an ideal base for hiking trips and skiing holidays.
