Description

Built in 1845, the Posthotel Rössli is the oldest hotel in Gstaad and is situated right in the pedestrianized centre of the village.

It offers free parking on site and free WiFi in the entire building. Fine Swiss specialities are served in the hotel's 2 restaurants.

Providing a typical Swiss ambience, the Posthotel Rössli's rooms feature Alpine-style wooden furniture.

A summer programme is offered daily from June until September, including guided hiking and mountain bike tours, climbing for beginners and advanced, a visit to the rope park and to the Indiana Jones Trail on Hornberg mountain.
