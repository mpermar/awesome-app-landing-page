Description

The Ermitage Wellness & SPA Hotel in Schönried - Gstaad is located on a sunny terrace above Gstaad in the middle of a park.

It offers panoramic views of the Saanenland and the surrounding mountains. It features a large spa area with a steam bath, different saunas and a tepidarium.

Different indoor and outdoor swimming pools and an outdoor hot tub are also provided.

All rooms in the Wellness & Spa Hotel Ermitage have a seating area, a minibar and a balcony with views.

Fine Swiss cuisine is served in the restaurant. Half-board is available on request and includes breakfast, lunch, cake and coffee in the afternoon and a 5-course dinner menu.

The Rellerli Ski Lift is just 150 metres away. Guided hiking and mountain biking tours are organised on site, and the Gstaad-Saanenland Golf Course is about 10 km away.
