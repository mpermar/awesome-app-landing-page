Description

Located in Gstaad’s pedestrian zone, this 4-star hotel offers 4 restaurants, a spa area with an indoor pool, and free WiFi.

The spacious rooms all have a balcony. Hotel Bernerhof’s elegantly furnished rooms feature a flat-screen TV, a CD player, an iPod docking station, and a seating area.

Bathrooms include a hairdryer, bathrobes, and slippers. Guests of the Bernerhof can dine in the elegant, award-winning restaurant Stafel, as well as in the Italian restaurant Basta, the Chinese restaurant Blun-Chi, and the traditional Swiss Gaststube.

A cigar lounge is also available. When booking the half-board option, guests receive a voucher that can be used on all 4 restaurants for food or drinks during the stay.

Spa facilities include a sauna, a steam bath, and a Kneipp pool. Hiking equipment can be hired free of charge.

During summer season guests can enjoy free bike and e-bike rental to explore the diverse alpine scenery of Gstaad.

Free private parking is available at the Bernerhof Hotel. Gstaad Train Station is a 2-minute walk away.
