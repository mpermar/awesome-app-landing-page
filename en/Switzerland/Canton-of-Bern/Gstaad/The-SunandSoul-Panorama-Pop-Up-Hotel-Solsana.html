Description

Panorama Hotel Solsana is situated in Gstaad, 3. 8 km from Ski Lift Rutti. The property is set 1.

6 km from Saanen-Eggli. All units in the hotel are fitted with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.

Every room comes with a private bathroom and a shower, and some units at Panorama Hotel Solsana have a balcony.

The units will provide guests with a desk and a kettle. A continental or buffet breakfast is served every day until noon.

Additionally, drinks and snacks are offered around the clock in a self-service canteen.

The accommodation offers a terrace, free yoga and fitness classes, as well as personal training (fees apply).

A variety of popular activities are available in the area around Panorama Hotel Solsana, including skiing, cycling and hiking.
