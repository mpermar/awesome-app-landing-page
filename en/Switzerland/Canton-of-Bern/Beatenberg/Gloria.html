Description

The family-run, non-smoking Hotel Gloria in Beatenberg, a 10-minute walk from the Niederhorn cable car, offers spacious rooms with private balconies and views of Lake Thun and the Alps.

Wi-Fi and parking are available for free. The Hotel Gloria includes a panorama restaurant and a beautiful outdoor sun terrace.

In the panorama restaurant chef Bernhard creates a combination of Swiss classics and international specialities using only the finest local produce.

Half board includes breakfast and a lunch or dinner. It is perfectly located as a base for exploring the Bernese Oberland, offering fantastic Alpine hiking trails at the doorstep.
