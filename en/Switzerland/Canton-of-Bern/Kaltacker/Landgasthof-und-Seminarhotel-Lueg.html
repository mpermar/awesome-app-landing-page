Description

Located in Kaltacker, amid the scenic surroundings of the Emmen Valley, Landgasthof Lueg offers a restaurant, individually decorated, themed rooms, and free WiFi access.

Spa access is at a fee. The restaurant serve Swiss and international cuisine, breakfast buffet is offered from Thursday to Sunday, and a wide range of fine wines.

The terrace offers panoramic views over the valley. The scenic surroundings can be explored with the Landgasthof Lueg's own hiking maps.
