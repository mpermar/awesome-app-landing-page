Description

The Seiler au Lac enjoys a peaceful location at the bank of Lake Brienz, with steamers and free buses to Interlaken stopping right in front of it.

It offers free WiFi in the entire property and free parking. The rooms at the Seiler au Lac are spacious and have an en-suite bathroom and a TV.

Fine Swiss and international cuisine is served in the elegant restaurant. Half board is available at a surcharge if you stay 3 nights or more.
