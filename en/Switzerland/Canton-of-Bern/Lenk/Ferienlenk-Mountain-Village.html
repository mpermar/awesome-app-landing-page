Description

Set in several locations in Lenk, the Ferienlenk Mountain Village offers self-catering accommodation units with free WiFi and beautiful views of the surroundings.

Each unit is fitted with design furnishings and comes with a well-equipped kitchen with a dishwasher and a Nespresso coffee machine, and a living area.

Laundry and ironing facilities are also provided. Several scenic hiking and cycling trails can be accessed from the village.

The Betelberg Ski Area is 1 km away, while the Metsch Ski Resort is 2 km from the property.
