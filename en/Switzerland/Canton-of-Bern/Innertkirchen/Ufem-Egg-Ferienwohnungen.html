Description

Set in a quiet location in Wyler at the foot of the Susten Pass, Ufem Egg Ferienwohnungen is a 10-minute drive from the lower terminus of the Meiringen-Hasliberg Cable Car.

Free Wi-Fi is provided in the entire building. The modern accommodation units of Ufem Egg Ferienwohnungen include a private bathroom.

The apartments have their own kitchen and each room also features a flat-screen TV.

A shared washing machine and a dryer are available on site. Barbecue facilities, a lawn for sunbathing and a childrenâ€™s playground can be found in the garden.

Free private parking is possible on site. The nearest restaurant is 150 metres away, while a bus stop 200 metres from the property.
