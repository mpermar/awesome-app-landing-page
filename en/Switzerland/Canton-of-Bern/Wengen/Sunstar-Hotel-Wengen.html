Description

Offering an indoor pool and a sauna, the Sunstar Hotel is situated close to the station in the car-free village of Wengen, which is only reachable by train.

The Männlichen Cable Car is located right across from the property. The lounge bar is equipped with a cosy fireplace.

The Sunstar Hotel Wengen also has a steam bath and a sun bed. During the warmer months, guests can relax in the garden, which also has a trampoline.

A 5-course menu with several choices based on fresh, regional ingredients is offered in the restaurant.

You can select from a delicious range of starters, salads from the buffet, various soups and 3 main courses (meat, fish, vegetarian) plus the chef's seasonal recommendations.

There is also a choice of tasty desserts and twice a week the chef invites you to a special themed dinner.
