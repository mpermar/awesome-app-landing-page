Description

Offering panoramic views of the North Face of the Eiger and the Bernese Alps, this family-run 4-star superior hotel is just a 5-minute walk from the centre of Grindelwald.

It features a gourmet restaurant open in the winter and summer seasons, free WiFi, and a spa area with an indoor pool.

Spa facilities at Belvedere Swiss Quality Hotel include a hot tub, a Finnish sauna, a heat mine gallery, a steam bath, and an outdoor saltwater hot tub in the garden.

Massages and beauty treatments are available. Hotel Belvedere’s rustic or modern rooms all have a balcony overlooking the mountains.

They also feature a flat-screen satellite TV, a minibar, a seating area, and a bathroom with slippers, bathrobes, and a hairdryer.

A selection of pillows is available. A member of the Chaîne des Rôtisseurs association of gourmet restaurants, the non-smoking restaurant serves Swiss and international dishes made from fresh seasonal specialties.

There is also a piano bar. Free parking is available on site. A pick-up service from the train station is available, as well as a private ski bus in the winter season.
