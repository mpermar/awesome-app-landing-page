Description

The Hotel Caprice boasts a perfectly quiet, but very central location in the mountain village of Grindelwald in the Bernese Oberland, and offers you impressive views of the mountains.

Enjoy comfortable rooms with a balcony or a garden terrace and the beautiful views of the majestic mountains.

There is also a wonderful garden with deck chairs, wicker chairs, beautiful floral decorations and a unique view of the famous North Face of the Eiger.

Small snacks are served at the property, while guests get a discount on a 5-course menu at the Hotel Alpina, which is 100 metres away.

The cable cars and buses are only a few steps away. Upon request and availability parking spaces in an underground car park are at your disposal at an additional cost.

The spa offers a hot tub, a sauna, a steam bath, a massage shower, a herbal bath, a solarium and professional massages.
