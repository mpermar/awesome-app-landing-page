Description

Offering modern, tastefully styled rooms in a traditional chalet, this BnB can be found 300 metres from the Wilderswil-Dorf bus stop.

All rooms have a mountain-view balcony or terrace and a flat-screen TV. Free WiFi is available and you can park your car on site for free.

With at least 28 m² in size, the rooms at BnB Chalet Gafri are very spacious. They come with elegant stone floors with underfloor heating, a seating area, a safe and a refrigerator.

Each unit also have a private bathroom with a shower and provided with a hairdryer and a make-up mirror.

There is a shared laundry and ironing facilities, and a shared kitchen can be used on previous notice.

Tea and coffee are available for free at the Gafri chalet. Gluten-free and lactose-free products are available for breakfast.

On request, the property offers a free transfer from the Interlaken or Wilderswil train stations to the hotel.

The centre of Wilderswil and the Wilderswil Train Station can be reached on foot in 10 minutes.
