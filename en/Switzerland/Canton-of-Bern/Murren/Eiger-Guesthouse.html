Description

All rooms are accessible by a spiral stair and renovated and redecorated with a flat-screen TV, a radio free Wi-Fi and a hairdryer.

The Eiger Guest house also offers a great restaurant, a bar/pub and a games room. Free Wi-Fi is available in the entire property.

The tourist office and the Alpine sports center are just 650 feet away.

You have free access to the 100 feet indoor pool (summer and winter) and the ice rink.

I'm the kind of person who does not eat the same dish twice, but I couldn't get enough of the Klettersteig- Rösti (potatoes, ham, bacon, tomatoes, onions, gratinated with cheese and fried egg), so I had it for every meal. he pizzas were very good. The kids schnitzel potion was enough for an adult, but my four-year-old loved it enough that he ate the whole thing. The house red wine was good, and reasonably priced for being in Switzerland (let alone way up in the mountains). Great service and a nice relaxed atmosphere. Our kids enjoyed playing foosball in the game room, and we enjoyed a beer in the relaxed setting. The hotel is in an excellent location and on clear days, the view is amazing."
