Description

The Hotel Restaurant Florida in Studen is located in a quiet area on the edge of the village and offers fine Swiss cuisine in tropical surroundings, a large fitness centre and several wellness facilities.

Free parking spaces are available on site. The property is situated on the banks of a pond with Flamingos and other rare birds.

In-house wellness facilities include various saunas and steam baths and a solarium.

There is also a miniature golf course and a children´s playground. The Hotel Restaurant Florida is only a 20-minute walk away from the Studen Train Station.
