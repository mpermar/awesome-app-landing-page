Description

Only a 1-minute walk from the Old Town of Murten and a 5-minute walk from Lake Murten, Hotel Murten offers a restaurant, a trendy bar, and a large event room.

Free WiFi is available. The restaurant serves local specialities and Mediteranean dishes, as well as a varied breakfast buffet.

In summer, you can dine on the terrace. At the Bar & Lounge, guests can enjoy a wide range of wines, cocktails, and other drinks.

The spacious rooms feature cable TV, a desk, and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and toiletries.
