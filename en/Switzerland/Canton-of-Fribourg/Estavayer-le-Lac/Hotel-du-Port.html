Description

This traditional family-run hotel in the medieval centre of Estavayer-le-Lac is just 200 metres from Lake Neuchatel and the port.

Guests can use a fitness room and benefit from free Wi-Fi and free on-site parking.

Hôtel du Port’s modern rooms feature a flat-screen TV, a minibar, and a bathroom with hairdryer.

The restaurant serves traditional Swiss cuisine and a selection of regional specialities, including fresh fish from the lake.

Electric bicycles can be rented at the Port Hotel. Estavayer-le-Lac Train Station can be reached in a 10-minute walk.
