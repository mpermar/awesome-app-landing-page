Description

The modern rooms and apartments with kitchenettes and sofa beds ensure a relaxing stay for guests.

Delicious Swiss and international cuisine is served in the air-conditioned, non-smoking restaurant, Côté Jardin.

Bus number 30 stops right across the street from the Dorint An der Messe Basel (Riehenring stop) and the tram line 14 stop is a 2-minute walk away. Guests can park their cars in the underground garage.
