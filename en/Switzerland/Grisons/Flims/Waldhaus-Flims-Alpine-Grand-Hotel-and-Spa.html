Description

Located in Flims-Laax, the 5-star hotel complex Waldhaus Flims Alpine Grand Hotel & Spa was built in 1877 and completely renovated in autumn 2016, and combines historical elegance with luxurious modern furnishings.

The hotel complex is located in the largest hotel park of Switzerland and offers a wellness oasis, 6 restaurants and bars, an extensive activity program and supervised child care.

The luxurious and elegant rooms in the 5-star Grand Hotel are equipped with the latest in technology and some of them offer a south- or north-facing balcony with panoramic mountain views.

The hotel complex consists of 4 interconnected buildings, all of which reflect the elegance and grandeur of the Belle Époque.

Guests can enjoy the award-winning 3000 m² Waldhaus Spa with an outdoor- and indoor pool, a natural swimming pond, a steam bath, several saunas, treatment rooms, a luxurious private spa suite and a modern 24-hour fitness centre.

Dining options within the hotel complex include the regional Restaurant Epoca, the classical fondue and raclette callar Il Tschaler, the restaurant The Grand with international cuisine and the family friendly Ristorante Pomodoro with pizza and pasta.

At the end of the day, the alpine bar The Summit with its open fireplace or the legendary Chadafö Bar with its Davidoff Cigar Lounge invite you to relax.

Additional on-site services include a complimentary ski shuttle for hotel guests to and from the cable car station in Flims.
