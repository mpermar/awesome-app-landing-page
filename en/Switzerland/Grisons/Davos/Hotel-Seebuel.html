Description

Located on the bank of the Davos Lake, this hotel is 2. 5 km away from Davos Dorf and the Parsenn Cable Car.

Sledge rental and private parking are offered for free. The Seebüel features a children's playground with a large lawn, slides and swings.

Guests can unwind on the summer terrace with chairs and have a barbecue in the garden.

All rooms come with private bathrooms with showers. They have wooden furniture and some of them have a balcony.

TV can be watched in the communal TV room. The restaurant serves Swiss and international dishes.

The Seebüel features a roof terrace and a children’s playroom. The breakfast room offers panoramic lake views.

Garage parking spaces are available on site, as well, for an extra charge. Skiing, hiking and mountain biking options are possible in the Davos/Klosters area.
