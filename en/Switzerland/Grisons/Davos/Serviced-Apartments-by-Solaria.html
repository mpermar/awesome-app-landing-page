Description

Solaria Serviced Apartments in Davos Dorf offers guests self-catering units with large balconies.

Golf Club Davos and the Bünda cable car station are both just 300 metres away. The local bus stop is in front of the house.

The Parsenn cable car station can be reached on foot in 6 minutes and the Jakobshorn cable car station is 1.

5 km away. Skiing, snowboarding, hiking and mountain biking are popular activities in the region.

Solaria offers free ski shuttles to the Jakobshorn and Parsenn Cable Cars. Solaria Serviced Apartments have modern furnishings, a balcony, a kitchen or kitchenette, a dining area, a washing machine, a seating area, ironing facilities and a bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries.

The units are also fitted with a flat-screen TV with integrated radio. WiFi is available free of charge in all areas of the property.

Bread delivery, daily maid and shuttle services are available upon request. In summer, Solaria has a children’s playground and bikes can be hired there free of charge.

Electric bikes can be hired for an additional charge. A ski storage room and a garden with table tennis facilities can be used at Solaria Serviced Apartments.

The property can help guests organize car rentals, and ski passes can be arranged upon request and against a fee.

Outdoor and garage parking are available at a surcharge. Upon prior request, guests can also purchase tickets for the Spengler Hockey Cup in December.
