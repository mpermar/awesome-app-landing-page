Description

Hotel Bündnerhof enjoys a quiet location in the centre of Davos-Platz, next to the Vaillant Arena Ice Hockey Stadium.

The Bündnerhof offers a rich breakfast buffet with local specialities. In the winter season, the à la carte restaurant with local and international dishes offer many culinary delights, as well as the 4-course half-board menu.

T he Congress Centre and the Eau La La Spa are a 5-minute walk away from the Bündnerhof.

You can reach the Davos Golf Course and the Schatzalpbahn and Jakobshorn cable cars within a 10-minute walk.
