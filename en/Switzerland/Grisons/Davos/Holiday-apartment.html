Description

All units feature a living room with a satellite flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen with a dining area, and a private bathroom with hot tub. A dishwasher and oven are also available, as well as a coffee machine.

A continental or buffet breakfast is served at the property.

At the condo hotel guests are welcome to use a sauna, a Turkish bath and an indoor swimming pool.

A casino can be found at the property, along with a garden.

Popular points of interest near Holiday apartment include Schatzalp, Ski Lift Bolgen and Schatzalpbahn. St. Gallen-Altenrhein Airport is 49 miles away.
