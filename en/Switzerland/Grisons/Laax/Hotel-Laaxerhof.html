Description

Located in the picturesque, mountainous area of Laax-Murschetg, Hotel Laaxerhof offers an indoor pool, sauna and hot tub.

Each spacious room has a seating area and a balcony. Free WiFi is available throughout the building.

All rooms are fitted with wooden furnishings and a private bathroom. Baby and child care is available at a surcharge.

In summer, Hotel Laaxerhof provides hiking and cycling activities. Massage treatments are also available.

Regional and international dishes are served at Laaxerhof Hotel’s restaurant. Dinner buffets are available on Tuesdays and Saturdays, and a lunch buffet is available on Sundays.

The hotel’s bar offers drinks and snacks. A ski school is right next door. St. Gallen-Altenrhein Airport is 77 km from Laaxerhof Hotel.
