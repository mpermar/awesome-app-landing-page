Description

Offering ski-to-door access and a bar in winter and a heated natural swimming pond in the summer season, Pradas Resort Brigels is located in Brigels.

The Breil-Burleun Ski Lift is 200 metres from the 3-star superior property. Free WiFi is provided, and free private parking is available on site.

All serviced apartments feature a seating and dining area. Some units include a terrace and/or balcony.

There is also a kitchen, equipped with a dishwasher. An oven and microwave are also featured, as well as a coffee machine.

Every unit is equipped with a private bathroom with a hair dryer. Towels are offered.

Pradas Resort Brigels also includes a hot tub and sauna. Ski equipment hire is available.

You can play tennis and mini-golf at the property, and the area is popular for skiing and golfing.

You can engage in various activities, such as horse riding, cycling and fishing. From 21 December 2019 until 05 April 2020, ski passes for the Brigels-Waltensburg-Andiast ski region are included.

In summer, the guest card includes free use of the Brigels and Obersaxen-Mundaun Cable Cars and free admission to the Brigels miniature golf course, the Ilanz public outdoor pool, and to several swimming lakes and museums.
