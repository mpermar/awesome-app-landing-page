Description

Situated high above the Rhine valley, in the charming mountain village of Waltensburg, this hotel offers panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.

Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel. Each room of Hotel Ucliva has a different colour, from fresh green to deep red.

All rooms are equipped with heating, a safety deposit box, and a private bathroom.

Some rooms have a private balcony. Ucliva’s restaurant serves regional dishes made with fresh, seasonal products.

It also serves premium organic wines and homemade drinks. Hotel Ucliva rents out electric bikes for guests who want to explore their surroundings.

The hotel also offers a comfortable lounge with a fireplace, a wood-fired sauna, and a children’s playground.

During summer, guests can hike in the surrounding mountains and swim in the clear mountain lakes.

In winter time, guests can ski and snowboard. The Brigels-Waltensburg-Andiast ski station is located next to the hotel.
