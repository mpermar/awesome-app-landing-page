Description

Hotelino Petit Chalet is located on ski slopes and is just a 3-minute walk from Celerina town centre, shops, restaurants and bars.

The Marguns/Celerina cable car stations, and the local bus and train station are within a 5-minute walk.

Units at the Petit Chalet have modern furnishings, a balcony or terrace, free WiFi, a flat-screen TV and DVD player.

Guests staying at the Chalet can purchase discounted ski passes on site. Garage parking, a laundry service and massages are available or can be arranged at the Petit Chalet.

A ski school, children’s fun park and tennis courts are located next to the building, and St.

Mortiz is 3 km away. During winter, guests staying 3 nights or longer can take advantage of several discounts for ski lift tickets.

During summer, the lift ticket is included in the rate when booking for more than 2 nights.
