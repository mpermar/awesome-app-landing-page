Description

This 3-star hotel in Celerina is a 5-minute walk from the Marguns-Corviglia-St. Moritz Cable Car.

It includes a buffet restaurant and an à la carte restaurant. Guests can relax in a sauna, steam bath, and sun bed.

The rooms at Hotel Alte Brauerei feature bright wooden furniture, satellite TV, and a bathroom with hairdryer.

Guests of the Alte Brauerei Hotel can play billiards and use a library and an internet terminal.

Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas. There is also a bar, a fitness room, and a playroom for children.

Private underground parking is available at an additional cost. Celerina Train Station can be reached in a 10-minute walk, and St.

Moritz is just 2 km away. In summer, guests can use the local cable cars free of charge with a minimum stay of 2 nights.

During winter, discounted ski passes are available on site if you stay 2 nights or more.
