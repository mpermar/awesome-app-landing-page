Description

Located in Disentis in the canton of Grisons, the Catrina Hotel was renovated in 2015 and offers spacious rooms.

Right next to the hotel is the skiing and hiking area Bergbahnen Disentis. The base station of the cable car can be easily reached on foot from the Catrina Hotel.

Guests can enjoy pizza, Swiss, Italian and international cuisine in the restaurant, spend pleasant hours at the hotel bar or in front of the fireplace in the lounge.

A wellness area is available on site. Numerous excursion and leisure opportunities for summer or winter are available at the hotel.
