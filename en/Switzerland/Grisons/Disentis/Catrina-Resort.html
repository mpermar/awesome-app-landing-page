Description

Very close to the ski resort DISENTIS BERGBAHNEN, around 50 meters, Catrina Resort features free WiFi.

The cable car to Caischavedra is 200 metres away. All units are furnished in Alpine style and the apartments have an equipped kitchen, bathroom, flatscreen television, and high-speed WiFi.

The units have a balcony with panoramic mountain views. In Catrina Resort there are the Chevrina Steak House and the Stiva Ursus family restaurant.

Guests can access to Catrina Wellness which has an indoor pool, whirlpool, sauna, bio sauna, steam baths and relaxation rooms.

Catrina Resort has direct access to the ski-to-door access and a ski pass sales point Disentis Bergbahnen, Catrina Sportshop and market.

The nearest airport is Lugano Airport, 78 km from Catrina Resort, Zurich Airport is 190 km away.
