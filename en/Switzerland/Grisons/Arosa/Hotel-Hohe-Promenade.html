Description

The Hotel Hohe Promenade, family-run since 1947, enjoys a slightly elevated location on a central yet tranquil spot in Arosa.

It offers a sauna, fine cuisine and wines, and free WiFi. Arosa's centre, the cable cars, the free bus and the starting points for many walks and hiking trails are only a few minutes' walk from the Hohe Promenade Hotel.

All of the non-smoking rooms are en-suite with bath or shower and toilet. Most of the double rooms have a balcony or a patio with a magnificent view to the Arosa mountains.

The lounge area features billiards facilities, a small library and various board games.

In summer, the Arosa Card is included in the rate. This card provides many free benefits in the area, including free use of local cable cars and buses, free boat hire, and free access to the Untersee beach area and the ropes course.
