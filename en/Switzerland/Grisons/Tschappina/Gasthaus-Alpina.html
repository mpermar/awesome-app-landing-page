Description

The family-friendly Gasthaus Alpina enjoys a quiet, secluded location in Tschappina amid the Grisons Alps.

It offers an informal atmosphere, tasty Swiss food, a hot tub with panoramic views of the mountains, a sauna and free parking.

You can ski right down to the front door of the Gasthaus Alpina. Ski lifts and the Thusis train station are easily reachable by bus from the Tschappina Paschget stop, which is only a few steps away.

The simply furnished rooms of the Alpina guest house share bathrooms on the same floor.

Towels and linen are provided. A breakfast including local products is served every morning and for dinner you can enjoy typical Swiss food.

There is also a petting zoo with sheep and goats and a playground for kids. Panoramic views of the Piz Beverin mountain can be enjoyed from the summer terrace.
