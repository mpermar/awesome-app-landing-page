Description

Set on the banks of the Sertig River, Hotel Walserhuus Sertig enjoys a quiet location and offers you panoramic views of the Mittagshorn and Hoch Ducan mountains and a garden terrace.

Rooms at Walserhuus Sertig have wooden furniture, a satellite TV and work desk. Duvets are filled with goose down and a bit of local wood for a relaxing scent.

Most rooms have a balcony or terrace with mountain views. The cosy restaurant is decorated with rustic elements like cowbells and traditional carved-wood furniture.

Local specialities feature strongly on the menu. All guests receive a card that grants them free use of all public transport in the area.

The bus to Davos and Klosters stops next to the Walserhuus and the ride to both resorts takes about 1 hour.
