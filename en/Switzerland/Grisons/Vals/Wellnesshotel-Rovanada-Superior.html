Description

The Hotel Rovanada can be found in a calm and sunny place in Vals, near a little mountain stream, only 50 metres from the famous Therme Vals bath.

The modern, 500 m² spa area features various saunas and an indoor pool. It offers a range of massages and other treatments.

There is also the Devil's Stone aroma steam bath and a sauna garden with breathtaking views.

A beauty salon is available as well. The breakfast buffet includes bread made in the hotel's wood-fired oven.

Guests can enjoy typical Swiss cuisine, Italian specialities, and the Devil's Stone dinner event, which is unique in Grisons.

In total, there are 3 restaurants on site. Wi-Fi access is available in the public areas free of charge.

A bus stop can be found 100 metres from the Rovanda hotel, and covered parking is available free of charge.
