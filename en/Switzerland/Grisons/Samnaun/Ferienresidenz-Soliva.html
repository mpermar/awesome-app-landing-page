Description

The Ferienresidenz Soliva in Samnaun-Compatsch in the Swiss Samnaun Valley is located only 50 metres from the ski bus stop.

It offers rooms with balconies and a free internet corner. All rooms at the Soliva Ferienresidenz have a balcony or a terrace and are equipped with a flat-screen TV and free wired internet access.

After a day out in the Samnaun / Ischgl ski area you can ski right down to the front door of the Ferienresidenz Soliva.

You can help yourself to some coffee or drinks in the lounge, relax in the spa area featuring a sauna and a solarium, or play ping-pong table or table soccer.

Restaurants, cafés, pubs and the first Alpine cheese factory can easily be reached on foot.

The indoor water park Alpenquell is only 100 metres away. In summer the public indoor water park can be used free of charge.
