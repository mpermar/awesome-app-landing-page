Description

Situated in an idyllically calm and natural area in Suvretta, 2 km from the centre of St.

Moritz, the Hotel Chesa Spuondas offers you a unique historic yet friendly atmosphere.

The hotel's rooms have been furnished like private bedrooms rather than hotel rooms.

You can find both antique and modern décor. Some rooms have big original bathrooms and most have oiled parquet floors.

Fine cuisine from organic ingredients of local origin is served for dinner and for lunch you can order snacks or salads.

Guests enjoy dinner at the same time together and are seated on large tables to encourage communication.

Spacious public areas including a library with free internet access invite you to unwind.

Moreover, the Chesa Spuondas provides lots of activities for kids and features a play room.

Forest, meadows, woods and streams surround the hotel and the lakes of Lej Marsch and Lej Nair are only 10 and 20 minutes' walk away.

It is a 5-minute drive to Lake Silvaplana where kite surfing, windsurfing , fishing and sailing are possible.

Samedan Golf Course is a 10-minute drive away. You can park your car for free on site.

For stays of 2 nights or more, guests can use all busses and mountain railways in the region free of charge during summer and receive a discount on ski passes in winter.
