Description

Located next to the Gotschna Cable Car in the centre of Klosters, Hotel Piz Buin features a spa area and offers free WiFi access.

A free shuttle service from Klosters Train Station is available. A balcony, cable TV, and a bathroom with a hairdryer are featured in each of the Alpine-style rooms of the Piz Buin Hotel.

Spa facilities include a swimming pool, a hot tub, 2 saunas, a steam bath, a massage shower, a foot bath, 2 treatment rooms, and a fitness area with Technogym equipment.
