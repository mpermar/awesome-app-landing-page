Description

Located in Klosters, 1. 8 km from Ski Lift Klosters - Gotschnagrat, Hotel Sport Klosters provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a fitness centre and a bar.

The accommodation offers a ski pass sales point and ski storage space, as well as a shared lounge and a garden.

The accommodation features a sauna, evening entertainment and family rooms. At the hotel, each room includes a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.

At Hotel Sport Klosters each room is fitted with a seating area. A continental breakfast is available daily at the accommodation.

Hotel Sport Klosters offers a children's playground. You can play table tennis, darts and mini-golf at the hotel, and the area is popular for hiking and skiing.

Ski Lift Selfranga is 2. 6 km from Hotel Sport Klosters, while Madrisabahn is less than 1 km away.

The nearest airport is St. Gallen-Altenrhein Airport, 104 km from the accommodation.
