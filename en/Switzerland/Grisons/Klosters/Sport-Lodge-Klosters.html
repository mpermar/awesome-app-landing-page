Description

The family-run Sport-Lodge Klosters offers panoramic views of the Gotschna Mountain and features a lounge with a fireplace, a wood sauna, a fitness room, and a beauty studio.

Klosters Free WiFi is available. All rooms at the Klosters Sport-Lodge have a balcony, a flat-screen cable TV, and a bathroom.

The daily breakfast buffet consists of fresh local products. Coffee, tea, homemade cakes, cold drinks and drinks are provided in the lounge or in the garden.

Herbal teas can be provided for hiking tours. A bus stop is right outside, and many shops, restaurants and bars are just a short walk away.

The Gotschna-Parsenn Cable Car can be reached in a 10-minute walk. Facilities such as an ice rink, a tennis court, a public outdoor pool, a cross-country ski run, and hiking and mountain bike trails are within a short walking distance.

Guests can ride the Rhaetian Railway between Davos and Klosters free of charge. In summer, the local cable cars can be used free of charge.
