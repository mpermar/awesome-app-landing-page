Description

Hotel Traube is located in the centre of Scuol, only a 2-minute walk from the Wellnessbad Bogn Engiadina public swimming pool.

It features a sauna and use of the internet corner free of charge. All rooms have satellite TV.

Guests can use a free internet terminal, a fountain with mineral water, and a sunny terrace.

There is also a garage for motorbikes and bicycles for free use. Guests can enjoy the Engadin specialities and various menus with products fresh from the market in the à-la-carte restaurant or relax on the hotel's sun terrace.

Breakfast is provided every morning. On request, half board can be arranged for the full stay.

A bus stop is a 2-minute walk away from the Traube Hotel. Free private parking is available.

Cable car tickets, ski passes and discounted admission to the thermal baths can be purchased on site.
