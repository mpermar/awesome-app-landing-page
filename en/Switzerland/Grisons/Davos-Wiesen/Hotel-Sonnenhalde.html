Description

Set on the outskirts of Davos-Wiesen, 3 km from the Wiesner Alp, Sonnenhalde Hotel & Restaurant offers a restaurant with a terrace.

There is a games room and billiards available for guests. Free public parking is possible on site.

The rooms of the Sonnenhalde Hotel include a private bathroom and a seating area with a flat-screen satellite TV.

Some have a balcony and a kitchen. Guests can start their day with a breakfast buffet.

The restaurant serves Italian cuisine. Barbecue facilities are available on site.

The Post Davos-Wiesen Bus Stop is 500 metres from the hotel. The centre of Wiesen is 700 metres away, while the Davos centre is 17 km from the property.

The Alvaneu Bad Golf Club and Wellness is 13 km away. Many hiking trails are nearby.
