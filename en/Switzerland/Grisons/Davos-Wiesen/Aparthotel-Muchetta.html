Description

Aparthotel Muchetta is located in the Alpine village of Wiesen in Grisons, 18 km from Davos.

Some outdoor parking spaces are available for free, and a garage parking space can be rented for a surcharge.

All apartments in the Aparthotel Muchetta have a balcony or a terrace, a fully equipped kitchenette, and include bathrobes.

Fine Swiss and international cuisine is served in the restaurant, which provides panoramic mountain views.

A terrace is available during summer. The sauna, the steam bath and the children's pool in the spa area are accessible free of charge, while the solarium and massage services area available at an additional cost.

Guests are welcome to rent games or use the library. The Rinerhorn ski area can easily be reached with the free ski bus and there is also a ski lift for children in Wiesen, 400 metres away.

The Wiesen Kirche bus stop is only a 5-minute walk away from the Aparthotel Muchetta.

You can be picked up at the Davos Wiesen Train Station on prior request. During the summer season, guests benefit from the free use of all mountain railways in the Davos/Klosters region.
