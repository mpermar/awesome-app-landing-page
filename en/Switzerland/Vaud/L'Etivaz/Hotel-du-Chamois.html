Description

Set in a wooden chalet from 1871, Hôtel du Chamois offers rustically furbished rooms in the centre L'Etivaz, a 10-minute drive from Les Mosses ski resort.

Free parking and free Wi-Fi are provided. The restaurant serves traditional dishes made from local ingredients, like fresh fish, meat and cheeses.
