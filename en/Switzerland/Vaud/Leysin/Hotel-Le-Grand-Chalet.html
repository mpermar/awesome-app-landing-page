Description

Offering spectacular views of the Swiss and French Alps, Hôtel Le Grand Chalet is a family-run, historic house from 1896, located 50 metres from the ski slopes in Leysin.

It features a restaurant with wooden decor, and an outdoor hot tub overlooking the mountains.

All rooms have a flat-screen satellite TV and a bathroom with hairdryer. Most rooms feature a south-facing balcony.

Every floor can be accessed by wheelchairs. The restaurant Le Carnotzet serves traditional regional specialities.

It offers panoramic mountain views and features a wine bar and a terrace. Le Grand Chalet also features the saloon bar Le Karibou.

Free WiFi is available in the business centre. There is also a children’s room with DVDs.

The Grand Chalet is set in a quiet area near the forest, and a 5-minute walk from the Leysin-Feydey Train Station.

Many restaurants and bars are close by. Free private parking is available on site.
