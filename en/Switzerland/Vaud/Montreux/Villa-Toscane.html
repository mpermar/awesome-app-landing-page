Description

Only a few steps from Lake Geneva, Villa Toscane is an Art Nouveau building with a beautiful interior design.

Free WiFi is available, and guests benefit from free use of local public transport.

The rooms are decorated in a modern style and have a minibar, a flat-screen TV, and a bathroom with a hairdryer.

Check-in and express buffet breakfast consisting of bread, pastries, cereals, fruit juice and hot drinks take place at the 5-star hotel Royal Plaza Montreux & Spa, located across the street, and guests have unlimited free access to the spa area and indoor pool at Royal Plaza.

There are also several offers on boat trips and excursions provided by the hotel.
