Description

The spacious rooms feature satellite TV and a minibar. All rooms offer views of the Alps, Lake Geneva, or the town of Vevey.

The Les Saisons restaurant serves gourmet cuisine prepared by Chef Thomas Neeser with the best regional products available. The breakfast buffet is available daily until 11 am.

The Grand Hotel du Lac’s pool and spa facilities, including the fitness center, can be used free of charge. A wide range of spa and massage treatments is available. Free Wi-Fi is provided at the hotel.

Guests can relax on the grounds and enjoy the views from the terrace. A babysitting service is available.

Local buses can be used free of charge. Vevey Train Station is a 15 minute walk away, and the Entre-deux-Villes Bus Stop is just steps away.
