Description

All the rooms at Holiday Inn Leiden feature a sitting area, box-spring bedding and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. They have a warm colored décor and wood furnishings. Other amenities include a tea/coffee maker and work desk.

Guests can make free use of the heated swimming pool with jet stream, a waterfall and a sauna. Use of the fitness center is also included. Menu dishes and buffets are served in the indoor garden. There is a terrace and a bar for informal meals and drinks.

Naturalis Natural History Museum is just under 1 miles away, and the Keukenhof Gardens are a 15-minute drive from the hotel. The beach of Noordwijk can be reached within 20 minutes by car. From the station, the center of Leiden is a 5-minute walk. A free shuttle service is provided to the train station on weekdays.
