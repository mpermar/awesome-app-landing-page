Description

The property features fully equipped bungalows and villas. They include modern kitchens, sitting areas and spacious dining areas. Many overlook the ocean.

At the Barefoot Cay Resort guests can enjoy a restaurant serving a wide variety of dishes, and the on-site tour desk can help arrange activities like swimming with dolphins, diving and nature tours.

Spa services, such as massages and facials are offered at the resort. Guests can also take advantage of a PADI-certified dive shop on the property.

The area’s beautiful coral reef can be reached in 5 minutes by boat, and Juan Manuel Galvez Airport is just 4 miles from the Barefoot Cay Resort.
