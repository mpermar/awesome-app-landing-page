Description

Each room at the family-run Landhotel has satellite TV channels and a private bathroom.

The Landhotel's spa area includes a Finnish sauna, a infrared cabin, a Turkish steam room and a solarium.

German and regional food is served in the Landhotel's traditional restaurant. A breakfast buffet is provided each morning.

Further facilities offered by the Landhotel Teuteberg include free parking spaces and a children’s playground.
