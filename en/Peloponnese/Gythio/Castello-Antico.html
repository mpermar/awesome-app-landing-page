Description

Castello Antico rooms include wood and stone features following the architectural style of Mani. Each room has a satellite TV, air conditioning and free Wi-Fi access. Their furnished balconies overlook the sea and the surrounding olive groves.

Guests can enjoy their drink by the fireplace at the warm, classically decorated Castello lounge bar. The hotel also has a pool bar serving light meals, cocktails and Mediterranean drinks.

The scenic seaside town of Gythio is 2 miles from Castello Antico. The hotel also serves as an ideal base for exploring the stone-built villages of Mani. Free private parking is available on site.
