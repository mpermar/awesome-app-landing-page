Description

Modernly decorated, all rooms and bungalows open to a furnished balcony or terrace with garden or mountain views. Each has a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, air conditioning and minibar. The bathroom is fitted with a rain-shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

The 1,600-m2 spa centre of Hydra Beach offers an indoor pool with a waterfall and hot tub, Turkish bath and fully equipped gym. Massage and body treatments can be arranged, while a beauty salon is available. Other facilities include a tennis court, mini soccer and a kid's club. Free Wi-Fi is provided in all areas.

Barcelo Hydra Beach lies 6.2 miles from the port of Ermioni connecting to the island of Hydra. Within a 30-minute drive, guests can also reach Galatas Port, where sea taxis depart to the island of Poros. Free, private parking is available on site.
