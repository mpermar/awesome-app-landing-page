Description

Featuring free Wi-Fi, all rooms have a sitting area with a 32'' flat-screen satellite TV. Modernly decorated in warm colors, they include a minibar and hairdryer. The private bathroom is fitted with a shower.

Guests at GMP Bouka Resort Hotel can start their day with a buffet breakfast. Greek dishes are served during lunch and dinner times, at the poolside, seasonal restaurant. There is also an inside bar.

An indoor pool and a hot tub are featured in the spa center and a playground is available for younger guests. A beauty salon and a sauna are also featured.

GMP Bouka Resort is located 3 miles from Messini Town and 6 miles from the seaside Kalamata Town. Kalamata International Airport is around 5 miles from the property. Free parking is available on site.
