Description

All rooms at the hotel come with a flat-screen TV and a kitchen. The private bathroom is equipped with a hot tub and free toiletries. Guest rooms feature a shared bathroom and a hair dryer.

Guests at Imathoessa can enjoy a buffet breakfast.

Rikia is 5 miles from the accommodation, while Kalamata is 23 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Kalamata Captain Vassilis Constantakopoulos Airport, 19 miles from Imathoessa.
