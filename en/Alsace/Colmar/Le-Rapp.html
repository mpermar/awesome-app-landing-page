Description

All rooms have air conditioning and a flat-screen TV. They also have a private bathroom with free toiletries, a hairdryer and a bath or a shower.

The hotel restaurant serves a traditional and seasonal cuisine with the tasty wines of Alsace.

Colmar Train Station is 2,950 feet from Le Rapp and Unterlinden Museum is 1,950 feet away. A private parking is available on site upon reservation and with an extra cost per day.
