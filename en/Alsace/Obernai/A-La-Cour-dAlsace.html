Description

Guest rooms are all equipped with modern private bathrooms and a flat-screen TV with cable channels and some also have a seating area.

A la Cour d’Alsace also features a restaurant. You can try French cuisine in the dining room or, in the vaulted-ceiling wine cellar, indulge in Alsatian wines.

Located 3.7 mi from the A35 motorway, this hotel is 16 mi from Strasbourg. Services include free WiFi and a free parking.
