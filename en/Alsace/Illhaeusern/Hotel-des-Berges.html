Description

At the hotel, each room comes with a desk. Complete with a private bathroom, guest rooms at Hotel des Berges come with a flat-screen TV and air conditioning, and selected rooms will provide you with a seating area. All guest rooms feature a closet.

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental breakfast. Guests can dine in the on-site restaurant, which specializes in French cuisine.

Guests can use the hot tub.

Colmar is 8 miles from Hotel des Berges, while Freiburg im Breisgau is 23 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Entzheim Airport, 27 miles from the hotel.
