Description

Guest rooms features air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fridge, a electric tea pot, a bath, free toiletries and a desk. The rooms include a private bathroom with a shower and a hair dryer. All guest rooms feature a closet.

Guests at the hotel can enjoy a continental or an Asian breakfast.

Islambek Hotel has a terrace. The area is popular for cycling and fishing, and bike rental is available at the accommodation.

For any tips on how to get around or what to do in the area, guests can ask at the reception.
