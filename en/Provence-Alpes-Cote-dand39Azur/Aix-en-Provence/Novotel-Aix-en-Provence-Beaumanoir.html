Description

Rooms are air-conditioned and feature a desk, a kettle, a fridge, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bath.

A buffet breakfast is available daily. The hotel offers a restaurant, a bar, a children's play area and guests can play table tennis. There are also meeting rooms that can accommodate up to 220 people and a free outdoor car park enclosed by a gate.

Aix-en-Provence Old Town is 3.1 mi away. Marseille Provence Airport is 13 mi away.
