Description

Decorated with antique furniture, each air-conditioned room features a mini-bar. All rooms have a private bathroom with a bath or shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries. Certain rooms are provided with a balcony or terrace and a view of the pool.

After a strenuous work-out in the fitness center you can unwind with a sauna and hot tub in the relaxation area. Guests can also explore the garden, which is planted with lemon and orange trees.
