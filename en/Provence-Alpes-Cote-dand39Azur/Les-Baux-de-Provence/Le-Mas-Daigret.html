Description

Each air-conditioned room is individually decorated in a classic décor. They are equipped with double glazed windows, a private bathroom and a flat-screen TV. Some rooms have a balcony or a terrace with views of the countryside.

Le Mas D'aigret’s restaurant has a unique décor with exposed stone walls and a fireplace and serves traditional regional cuisine. Guest can enjoy their meal on the terrace under the mulberry plane trees and panoramic views.

Enjoy a drink at the cozy hotel’s bar while using the free Wi-Fi access. The floral garden allows guests to relax and unwind.

Free private parking is provided. Guests can explore Chateau des baux de Provence and Carrières de Lumières, both within a 15-minute walk of the property.
