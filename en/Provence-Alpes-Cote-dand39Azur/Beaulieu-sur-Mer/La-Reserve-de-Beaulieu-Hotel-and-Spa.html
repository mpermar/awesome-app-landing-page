Description

The hotel has 39 rooms, suites and villas, with a refined decor. There are several room categories. Some are in the tower of the hotel and offer a panoramic view. All the rooms come with air conditioning, a safe, a minibar, internet access, a satellite TV and a radio, a range of beauty care products, dressing gowns, towels, and slippers. Some rooms have balconies. The villa Mistinguett features a hot tub and a hammam.

The hotel has a famous gourmet restaurant with a great sea view, another restaurant open in the summer, and a bar. It also has a spa including a hammam, a hot tub, a fitness center, and several cabins featuring relaxing activities such as massages and beauty care. Guests can find an outdoor swimming pool as well as a small private port for sea excursions. The pink lounge and the billiard room also offer moments of relaxation. Finally, room service 24 hours a day, a laundry, a service of French and foreign newspapers, as well as a shop are available.
