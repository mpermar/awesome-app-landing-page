Description

The friendly proprietors of the Hotel Marc-Hely invite you to their peaceful hotel to discover the beautiful Provence region.

Enjoy breakfast on the patio in the hotel’s pretty garden. You can also have breakfast on the veranda, as you admire the view over St-Paul-de-Vence. Or relax and enjoy breakfast from the comfort of your own room.

Located between Cannes and Nice, the Hotel Marc-Hely is an ideal base for visiting beaches and charming hillside villages.

Free private parking is provided at the hotel, making it easy to explore the surrounding area by car.

Experiences

09/17/2016

Perfect place if you have a car

"A beautiful country house in the hills, a spotless second floor room with a balcony looking out at St. Paul de Vence on the next hill, a small but very pleasant pool, comfortable parking, and a fantastic breakfast in the little garden - or inside, again overlooking St. Paul. The owners are an absolutely wonderful couple (tip: listen to their dinner recommendations - the village has some excellent restaurants, better than the touristy St. Paul which is also just a few minutes' drive away), and everything from Cannes to Nice to Monaco is easily accessible by car. You might even get a hug from one of the owners' sweet kids if you behave yourself well. I'll come again if/when I have a chance."
