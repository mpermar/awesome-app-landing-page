Description

Each guest room at the Sandton Domaine Cocagne has satellite TV, free Wi-Fi and a private sitting area. Some rooms have a private balcony with a view of the pool or the garden.

Additional facilities include a bar and a car rental service. Shiatsu massages are available on site for an additional charge.

With free private parking on site, Nice-Cote d’Azur Airport is 6 miles away. Antibes Train Station is a 25-minute drive away.
