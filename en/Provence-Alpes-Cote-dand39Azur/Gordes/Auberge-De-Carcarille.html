Description

The elegant guest rooms include flat-screen satellite TV, air conditioning and a terrace with direct access to the outdoor pool.

The restaurant serves gourmet French cuisine using local produce. There is also an extensive wine list of regional and French wines.

Guests can relax on lounge chairs by the pool, or use the ping-pong and pool table facilities. Free Wi-Fi internet access is available in public areas.

Auberge De Carcarille is 17 miles from highway A7 and free on-site parking is available arriving by car. Avignon, with its air and high-speed train services, is 19 miles away.
