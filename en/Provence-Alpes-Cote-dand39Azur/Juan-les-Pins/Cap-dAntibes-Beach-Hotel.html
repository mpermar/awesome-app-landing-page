Description

All rooms are soundproofed, air conditioned and have a flat-screen TV. Each room has free WiFi and a terrace or private garden.

The Beach Bar offers panoramic views of the Esterel Mountains, and Restaurant Le Cap is located on the beach. Guests can also enjoy seasonal cuisine at the Les Pêcheurs 1-Michelin-Star restaurant.

The Cap d'Antibes Beach hotel is 1.9 mi from Antibes town center and a 40-minute drive from Nice-Cote d’Azur Airport.
