Description

Guests of La Villa can enjoy champagne, from the hotel’s vineyard, by the swimming pool. Guests can also enjoy numerous water sports at the beach and tennis and riding nearby.

The hotel La Villa also has a bar where guests can relax with a drink and a free newspaper. The hotel is selected in the Michelin Guide for Antibes Juan-les-Pins.

A full buffet breakfast is served on the terrace on the grounds and guests can enjoy a number of room service options.

Nature enthusiasts can go canoeing in the Gorges of Verdon or go hiking in the Colmiane Forest.
