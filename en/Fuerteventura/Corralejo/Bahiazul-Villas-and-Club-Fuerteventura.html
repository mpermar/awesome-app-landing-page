Description

Bahiazul Villas & Club Fuerteventura is located 200 meters from Corralejo Dunes Park and offers luxurious villas with a private pool. This beautiful complex has free Wi-Fi, gym and spa.

The Bahiazul has whitewashed and elegant villas with arches and dim lighting. All rooms include a living room with a flat-screen TV, an iPod docking station and access to a large terrace by the pool.

The kitchenette is equipped with a microwave, fridge and dishwasher. There is also a breakfast bar, dining area and hot tub. The complex has a restaurant where breakfast buffet is served.

The Fuerteventura Club spa has rain showers and Finnish and Turkish saunas. There are also paddle and tennis courts and a children's playground. In addition, several water sports can be practiced nearby.
