Description

The spacious rooms at the Holiday Inn Express Iquique feature pleasant blue and beige décor, air conditioning and cable TV. Some rooms offer panoramic ocean views and separate work desks. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel.

Guests at the Holiday Inn can enjoy the cafeteria, featuring local decoration and serving a selection of salads, sandwiches and regional wines. For additional dining options, the hotel is conveniently located close to several restaurants and bars.

The hotel is located 22.4 miles from the Diego Aracena Airport, and 0.6 miles from Cavancha Beach. Free on site parking is available.
